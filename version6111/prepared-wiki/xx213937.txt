[[Yap flying fox]]

CATEGORIES: Pteropus, Animals described in 1908

The Yap flying fox (Pteropus yapensis) is a species of megabat in the Pteropus genus found on Yap Island.D.E. Wilson & D.M. Reeder, 2005: Mammal Species of the World: A Taxonomic and Geographic Reference. Third Edition. The Johns Hopkins University Press, Baltimore  The species is listed on CITES Appendix I, and is classified as "Vulnerable" due to a major decline due to commercial hunting in the 1970s, and ongoing subsistence harvest.
