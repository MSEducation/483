[[Mike S. Ryan]]

CATEGORIES: American film producers, Living people, Year of birth missing (living people), Place of birth missing (living people)

Mike S. Ryan is an American film producer. He is most known for producing the indie hit feature Junebug, starring Amy Adams. Adams received a nomination for the 2006 Academy Award for Best Performance by an Actress in a Supporting Role, making it the most inexpensive film to be in contention for an Oscar.
