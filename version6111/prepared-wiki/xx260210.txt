[[Night Shot – The Remixes]]

CATEGORIES: 2009 albums, Mark Mallman albums

Night Shot – The Remixes is a 2009 remix album by Ruby Isle. It was released through iTunes on November 24, 2009https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/night-shot-the-remixes/id352489657 and was released as an on-demand CDR through Amazon.com on April 7, 2010 http://www.amazon.com/Night-Shot-Remixes-Ruby-Isle/dp/B003FSTVWM
