[[Michelle van Eimeren]]

CATEGORIES: 1972 births, Living people, Australian beauty pageant winners, Australian female models, Miss Universe 1994 contestants

Michelle van Eimeren (born 15 March 1972) is a beauty queen from Brisbane, Australia.
Van Eimeren represented Australia for the 1994 Miss Universe pageant, which was held in Manila. She placed 11th overall.
After her Miss Universe stint, she starred in numerous Filipino movies. She married Filipino actor/singer Ogie Alcasid in 1996 and had two children with him. She also wrote a children's book called, "Butterfly."
She is also a commercial model and product endorser.
She now lives in Australia with her two children, after her marriage to Alcasid was annulled in October 2007.
