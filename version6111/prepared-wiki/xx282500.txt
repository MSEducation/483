[[Ruby Jessop]]

CATEGORIES: 1987 births, American activists, Former Latter Day Saints, Living people, Mormonism-related controversies, Fundamentalist Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints members

Ruby Jessop (born 1987) is a former member of the Fundamentalist Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints and child bride known for her family connections, her 2013 escape from an  FLDS-controlled polygamous community, and the criminal probe prompted by her escape.[tpl]cite web | last = Ng | first = Christina | title = Arizona Former Child Bride 'Escapes' FLDS Community With Children | url = http://abcnews.go.com/US/arizona-child-bride-escapes-flds-community-children/story?id=18292889 | publisher = ABC News (20/20) | date = January 23, 2013 | accessdate = August 16, 2013[/tpl]
Jessop is the sister of author and ex-FLDS member Flora Jessop.[tpl]cite book | last = Jessop | first = Flora | title = Church of Lies | publisher = Jossey-Bass | year = 2010 | isbn = 0470565462[/tpl]  In Flora Jessop's book, Church of Lies,  she recounts how, as a fourteen-year-old, Ruby called her asking to help her escape after learning that she was to be married off to her second cousin and step-brother, Haven Barlow.[tpl]cite web | last = Lee | first = Ken | title = Former Child Bride, Held Over a Decade, Escapes Polygamist Sect | url = http://www.people.com/people/article/0,,20668005,00.html | publisher = People | date = January 25, 2013 | accessdate = August 5, 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite news | last = Ng | first = Christina | title = Arizona Former Child Bride 'Escapes' FLDS Community With Children | url = http://abcnews.go.com/US/arizona-child-bride-escapes-flds-community-children/story?id=18292889 | newspaper = ABC News | date = January 23, 2013 | accessdate = August 5, 2013[/tpl]   Flora Jessop writes that while the authorities were contacted, they were unable to locate Ruby as she was being moved from place to place to avoid F Jessop's contact.
FLDS Prophet Warren Jeffs officiated at the wedding of 14-year-old Jessop and Barlow, who was then in his mid-20s. When the two were married, this made Barlow her brother-in-law,[tpl]cite news | title = Arizona mother of six escapes 12-year nightmare in Warren Jeffs' polygamous sect | url = http://www.foxnews.com/us/2013/01/23/26-year-old-arizona-mother-six-escapes-jeffs-polygamous-sect/ | newspaper = Fox News | date = January 23, 2013 | accessdate = August 5, 2013[/tpl] second cousin, step-brother and husband. The intermarrying of Fundamentalist Mormon members is very common, as families have many members and only people inside the faith are deemed marriageable.[tpl]cite news | last = Khan | first = Razib | title = Inbreeding among Mormons | url = http://blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/2006/08/inbreeding-among-mormons/#.Uf_aRpKsjK4 | newspaper = Discover | date = August 31, 2006 | accessdate = August 5, 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite news | title = Polygamist Sects: How They Avoid Inbreeding Problems | url = http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2008/04/22/polygamist-sects-how-they_n_97975.html | newspaper = Huffington Post | date = April 30, 2008 | accessdate = August 5, 2013[/tpl]  This can result in inbreeding and can cause genetic defects.[tpl]cite news | last = Johnson | first = Kirk | title = By Accident, Utah Is Proving An Ideal Genetic Laboratory | url = http://www.nytimes.com/2004/07/31/us/by-accident-utah-is-proving-an-ideal-genetic-laboratory.html?pagewanted=all&src=pm | publisher = New York Times | date = July 31, 2004 | accessdate = 16 August 2013[/tpl]
After Jessop's escape, a press conference was held by Arizona Attorney General Tom Horne with Jessop and her sister Flora to announce not only the escape, but a probe into the alleged FLDS practice of holding women against their will and an investigation of the police force in Colorado City.[tpl]cite news | last = Quigley | first = Rachel | title = Revealed: Harrowing ordeal of child bride forced to marry her stepbrother who escaped Warren Jeffs' polygamous sect with her six young children | url = http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2267222/Ruby-Jessop-The-incredible-story-child-bride-escaped-Warren-Jeffs-polygamous-sect.html | newspaper = Daily Mail | date = January 23, 2013 | accessdate = August 5, 2013 | location=London[/tpl] Horne also intervened and prevented Barlow from keeping Jessop's children away from her.[tpl]cite news | last = Keneally | first = Meghan | title = No sex with your husband... just a three-second handshake:  Woman who escaped Warren Jeffs' FDLS reveals bizarre rules cult leader imposed from his prison cell | url = http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2269937/Ruby-Jessop-Warren-Jeffs-banned-members-religious-sect-having-sex-year-married-couples-hold-hands-THREE-SECONDS.html | newspaper = Daily Mail | date = January 29, 2013 | accessdate = August 5, 2013 | location=London[/tpl]
