[[Lompoc High School]]

CATEGORIES: Public high schools in California, High schools in Santa Barbara County, California

Lompoc High School is the public high school serving Lompoc, in northern Santa Barbara County, California, USA.  It was first established in the small farming community in 1892. It is part of the Lompoc Unified School District, as is crosstown rival Cabrillo High.
