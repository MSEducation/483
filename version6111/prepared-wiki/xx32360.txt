[[Bethel Census Area, Alaska]]

CATEGORIES: Bethel Census Area, Alaska, Alaska census areas, Counties of the United States with Native American majority populations

Bethel Census Area is a census area located in the U.S. state of Alaska.  As of the 2010 census, the population is 17,013.[tpl]cite web|last=United States Census Bureau|title=2010 Census Data|url=http://2010.census.gov/2010census/data/|publisher=United States Census Bureau|accessdate=21 May 2012[/tpl]  It is part of the unorganized borough and therefore has no borough seat.  Its largest community by far is the city of Bethel, which also the largest city in the entire Unorganized Borough.

==Geography==

The census area has a total area of [tpl]convert|45,508|sqmi|km2[/tpl], of which, [tpl]convert|40,633|sqmi|km2[/tpl] of it is land and [tpl]convert|4,875|sqmi|km2[/tpl] of it (10.71%) is water.  Its territory includes the large offshore Nunivak Island in the Bering Sea.
