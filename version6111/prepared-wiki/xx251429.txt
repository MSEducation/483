[[Tower 185]]

CATEGORIES: Office buildings completed in 2011, Skyscrapers between 200 and 249 meters, Skyscrapers in Frankfurt

Tower 185 is a 55-storey, [tpl]convert|200|m|abbr=on[/tpl] skyscraper in the Gallus district of Frankfurt, Germany. It is the 4th tallest building in Frankfurt and the 4th tallest in Germany, tied with Main Tower. The anchor tenant of the tower is the German branch office of PricewaterhouseCoopers, which has leased [tpl]convert|60000|m2|abbr=on[/tpl]. 
The tower was initially planned to be [tpl]convert|185|m|abbr=on[/tpl] with 50 stories, however, when plans changed to increase the height by an additional five floors, its name was not changed.[tpl]cite press release | title=Vivico is responsible for one of Frankfurt's new multi-storey office blocks - Tower 185 - in the Europaviertel | url=http://www.skyscrapercity.com/showthread.php?t=505636  | publisher=Vivico Real Estate | date=30 May 2008 | accessdate=29 June 2012[/tpl]
