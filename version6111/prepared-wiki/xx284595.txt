[[Nossa TV]]

CATEGORIES: Brazilian television networks, 2007 establishments in Brazil

Nossa TV (Eng: Our TV) is a Brazilian satellite television service of R.R. Soares' International Church of God's Grace. The service was officially launched on 14 September 2007 in the metropolitan regions of São Paulo and Rio de Janeiro, but now operates in 26 states and the Brazilian Federal District. Nossa TV also operates in Canada and other countries. The service uses the Direct To Home system. In 2010, Nossa TV began to migrate to DVB-S2.Network Broadcast grows in Brazil with Nossa TV and Telefónica NexTV Latam, 18 August 2010. Retrieved 11 August 2013. Archived here.
Soares is one of the richest and most successful televangelists in Brazil with a net worth estimated by Forbes at $125m in 2013.The Richest Pastors In Brazil by Anderson Antunes, forbes.com, 17 January 2013. Archived here. His other media interests include radio, publishing and music.
