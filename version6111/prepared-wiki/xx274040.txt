[[Australia at the 1994 Winter Paralympics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 1994 Winter Paralympics, Australia at the Paralympics, 1994 in Australian sport

Australia competed at the 1994 Winter Paralympics and sent six male skiers.These skiers won three gold, two silver and four bronze medals.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Australian Paralympic Committee|accessdate=26 September 2011|title=Paralympic Games History - Winter|year=2011|url=http://www.paralympic.org.au/games-amp-events/paralympic-games-history-winter/paralympic-games-history-winter[/tpl][tpl]cite web|accessdate=8 November 2011|url=http://rmit.biz/browse;ID=7gv182c2fcx31|title=RMIT graduate makes Paralympics history|publisher=RMIT[/tpl][tpl]cite news|url=http://www.theaustralian.com.au/jessica-gallagher-rejoices-in-paralympic-history/story-fn4vwn8v-1225840781181|accessdate=8 November 2011|newspaper=The Australian|location=Sydney, Australia|date=15 March 2010|title=Jessica Gallagher rejoices in Paralympic history|first=Margie|last=McDonald[/tpl] Australia finished 5th overall in the alpine skiing competition  and 11th in the total medal count.
