[[Viral meningitis]]

CATEGORIES: Meningitis, Viral infections of the central nervous system, Enterovirus-associated diseases

Viral meningitis refers to meningitis caused by a viral infection.[tpl]EMedicine|article|1168529|Viral Meningitis[/tpl] It is sometimes referred to as aseptic meningitis in contrast to meningitis caused by bacteria.
An example is lymphocytic choriomeningitis.
Viral meningitis is most commonly caused by enteroviruses.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.cdc.gov/meningitis/viral.html |title=Viral Meningitis |work=Meningitis |publisher=Centers for Disease Control and Prevention[/tpl]

==Treatment==

Herpes simplex virus, varicella zoster virus and cytomegalovirus have a specific antiviral therapy; most other viruses do not.
