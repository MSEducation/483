[[Zavareh District]]

CATEGORIES: Ardestan County, Districts of Isfahan Province

Zavareh District ([tpl]lang-fa|بخش زواره[/tpl]) is a district (bakhsh) in Ardestan County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 12,747, in 3,629 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|10[/tpl]  The District has one city: Zavareh. The District has two rural districts (dehestan): Rigestan Rural District and Sofla Rural District.
