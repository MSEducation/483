[[Gmina Brok]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Ostrów Mazowiecka County

Gmina Brok is an urban-rural gmina (administrative district) in Ostrów Mazowiecka County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the town of Brok, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|12|km|mi|0[/tpl] south of Ostrów Mazowiecka and [tpl]convert|79|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north-east of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|110.21|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 2,843, of which the population of Brok is 1,859, and the population of the rural part of the gmina is 984.

==Villages==

Apart from the town of Brok, Gmina Brok contains the villages and settlements of Bojany, Laskowizna, Nowe Kaczkowo, Puzdrowizna and Stare Kaczkowo.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Brok is bordered by the gminas of Brańszczyk, Małkinia Górna, Ostrów Mazowiecka and Sadowne.
