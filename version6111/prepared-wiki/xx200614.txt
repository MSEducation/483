[[Kya Aap Paanchvi Fail Champu Hain?]]

CATEGORIES: Indian comedy television series, Filmy television series, 2008 Indian television series debuts

Kya Aap Paanchvi Fail Champu Hain? is a game show on Indian Television and a parody on STAR Plus Show, Kya Aap Paanchvi Pass Se Tez Hain? which in English translates to "Are you Dumber than a 5th Grade Failure?"
