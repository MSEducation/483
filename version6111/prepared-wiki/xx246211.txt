[[K-Rob]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, American rappers, African-American rappers

Since the 1980s, however, aside from providing a verse for "Beat Bop Part 2" on 2004's Bi-Conicals of the Rammellzee, K-Rob has devoted himself more to his Muslim faith.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.cocaineblunts.com/blunts/?p=958 |title=BLUNT RAPS >> Rammellzee Interview |accessdate=8 January 2010 |author= |date=24 April 2008 |work=Cocaine Blunts[/tpl]
