Counterspy is an upcoming side-scrolling stealth video game developed and published by Dynamighty.CounterSpy Sneaking to PS3, PS Vita

==Gameplay==

The game is played as a 2D sidescroller with stealth elements.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ign.com/articles/2013/05/07/counterspy-announced-for-playstation-3-ps-vita |title=CounterSpy Announced For PlayStation 3, PS Vita |publisher=IGN |date=2013-05-07 |accessdate=2013-08-09[/tpl]

==Story==

The game takes place during an alternate history Cold War.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.pocketgamer.co.uk/r/PS+Vita/CounterSpy/news.asp?c=51946 |title=Dynamighty leaks some more information about upcoming espionage thriller CounterSpy | CounterSpy news | PlayStation Vita |publisher=Pocket Gamer |date= |accessdate=2013-08-09[/tpl]

==Development==

The game is being developed with assistance from SCE Foster City Studio, which has previously worked on games in the Uncharted, Infamous, and Resistance series of video games.[tpl]cite web|url=http://origin-www.gameinformer.com/b/features/archive/2013/05/07/race-against-the-doomsday-clock-in-counterspy.aspx |title=Race Against The Doomsday Clock In Counterspy - Features - www.GameInformer.com |publisher=Origin-www.gameinformer.com |date=2013-05-07 |accessdate=2013-08-09[/tpl] While historically known for collaborating with the series' respective large development studios, CounterSpy marks the first time they've teamed up with an indie development team, "Dynamighty". The team, while classified as "indie", comprises past staff from LucasArts and Pixar.[tpl]cite web|last=Hinkle |first=David |url=http://www.joystiq.com/2013/05/07/its-a-sabotage-counterspy-set-to-infiltrate-ps3-ps-vita/ |title=It's a sabotage: CounterSpy set to infiltrate PS3, PS Vita |publisher=Joystiq |date=2013-05-07 |accessdate=2013-08-09[/tpl]
