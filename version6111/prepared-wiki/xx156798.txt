[[International Institute of Information Technology, Pune]]

CATEGORIES: Universities and colleges in Maharashtra, Engineering colleges in Maharashtra, IIIT

The International Institute of Information Technology ([tpl]lang-hi|अंतरराष्ट्रीय सूचना प्रौद्योगिकी संस्थान, पुणे[/tpl]; I2IT, pronounced "I Square IT"), is a higher education institute situated at Rajiv Gandhi Infotech Park, Hinjawadi, in Pune, Maharashtra, India.The institute is affiliated to University of Pune

==History==

The decision to set up I2IT was made in 1999 by P. P. Chhabria, industrialist and philanthropist and Vijay P. Bhatkar, scientist, and the first postgraduate (PG) course took place in 2001–2003. The Indira Gandhi National Open University center of excellence was set up in 2010.[tpl]cite web |url= http://www.isquareit.ac.in/ABOUT+US/I2IT+History/index.aspx |title=Isquareit History |work=isquareit.ac.in |accessdate=15 September 2011[/tpl]
IT started its UG course in 2011.

==Academics==

IIIT (I2IT)-Pune is one of the premier colleges in the country.I2IT offers PG programmes. The technical schools, School of Information Technology and School of Interdisciplinary Science offer  Master of Technology (M.Tech.) and Master of Science (MS) with various specializations, as well as an integrated Ph.D program. The School of Biotechnology offers only MS courses. The School of Management offers Master of Business Administration (MBA) course as well as an Executive MBA.[tpl]cite web |url= http://www.isquareit.ac.in/PROGRAMS/Program+Summary/index.aspx |title=Programs & courses, I2IT |work=isquareit.ac.in  |accessdate=15 September 2011[/tpl]
