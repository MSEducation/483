[[Zeče pri Bučah]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Municipality of Kozje

Zeče pri Bučah is a settlement west of Buče in the Municipality of Kozje in eastern Slovenia. The municipality is included in the Savinja statistical region and the entire area is part of the historical region of Lower Styria.Kozje municipal site

==Name==

The name of the settlement was changed from Zeče to Zeče pri Bučah in 1953.Spremembe naselij 1948–95. 1996. Database. Ljubljana: Geografski inštitut ZRC SAZU, DZS.
