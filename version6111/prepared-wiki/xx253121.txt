[[Lyambirsky District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Mordovia

Lyambirsky District ([tpl]lang-ru|Ля́мбирский райо́н[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-myv|Лямбирьбуе[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-mdf|Лямбирень район[/tpl]) is an administrativeConstitution of the Republic of Mordovia and municipalLaw #122-Z district (raion), one of the twenty-two in the Republic of Mordovia, Russia. It is located in the northern and central parts of the republic. The area of the district is [tpl]convert|852|km2|sp=us[/tpl].[tpl]citation needed|date=October 2012[/tpl] Its administrative center is the rural locality (a selo) of Lyambir.Law #7-Z Population: [tpl]ru-census2010|34,142|;[/tpl] 33,872 (2002 Census);[tpl]ru-pop-ref|2002Census[/tpl] [tpl]su-census1989|34,661|.[/tpl] The population of Lyambir accounts for 24.8% of the district's total population.
