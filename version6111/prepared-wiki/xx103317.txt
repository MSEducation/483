Tiffany Fallon (born May 1, 1974 in Fort Lauderdale, Florida)[tpl]cite web|url=http://wekinglypigs.com/cgi-bin/nand/search/pmstat?browse=%3A%3ACONFIG%3A%3Adatebrowse&key=2004&limit=0|title=Playmate listing|accessdate=October 26, 2009[/tpl] is an American model. In 2004, she was photographed by Stephen Wayda for Playboy and was named Playmate of the Month for December. She was also named Playmate of the Year for 2005.

==Career==

In addition to her work with Playboy, Fallon was a cheerleader for the Atlanta Falcons.[tpl]cite web|last=Staff|title=Tiffany Fallon Miss December 2004|url=http://plus.playboy.com/girls/2010/tiffany-fallon/|publisher=Playboy|accessdate=30 December 2013[/tpl] Prior to that, she worked as a flight attendant.

==Personal life==

Fallon is of Irish-American descent.[tpl]citation needed|date=December 2013[/tpl] She was Homecoming Queen and senior Class President at her high school where she played volleyball, soccer and ran track.[tpl]cite journal|periodical=Playboy|publisher=Playboy Inc.|title=Playmate News|volume=56|date=November 2009|pages=122–123[/tpl] She was Miss Georgia in 2001 and also 2nd-runner up for Miss USA in 2001.[tpl]cite journal|periodical=Playboy|publisher=Playboy Inc.|title=Playmate News|volume=56|date=December 2009|pages=166–167[/tpl] She has a degree in sports management from Florida State University.
Fallon married Joe Don Rooney of the country singing group Rascal Flatts on April 23, 2006, and she lives in Brentwood, Tennessee.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.people.com/people/article/0,,1187752,00.html|title=Rascal Flatts Guitarist Weds in Mexico - Rascal Flatts, Marriage|publisher=People.com|date=2006-04-26|accessdate=2013-12-30[/tpl] On May 31, 2008, Fallon gave birth to their first child, son Jagger Donovan Rooney.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.people.com/people/article/0,,20204177,00.html| title=Rascal Flatts Guitarist Welcomes a Son - Babies|publisher=People.com|date=2013-01-14|accessdate=2013-12-30[/tpl] On September 7, 2010 their daughter, Raquel Blue, was born.[tpl]citation needed|date=December 2013[/tpl]
