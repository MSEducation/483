[[Mie Kotsu]]

CATEGORIES: Bus companies of Japan

[tpl]Nihongo|Mie Kotsu Co., Ltd.|三重交通株式会社|Mie-kōtsū Kabushiki-gaisha[/tpl], also known as [tpl]Nihongo|Sanco|三交|Sankō[/tpl], is a public transportation company which operates local and long-distance buses in Mie prefecture, Japan. The company has other ventures as well, including a taxicab business and real estate.
