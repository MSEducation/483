[[Yorkshire ECB County Premier League]]

CATEGORIES: English domestic cricket competitions, Cricket in Yorkshire, ECB Premier Leagues

The Yorkshire ECB County Premier League is the top level of competition for recreational club cricket in Yorkshire, England, and since 1999 has been a designated ECB Premier League.List of ECB Premier Leagues
The competing teams in 2014 are: Appleby Frodingham, Barnsley, Castleford, Cleethorpes, Doncaster Town, Driffield Town, Harrogate, Hull, Rotherham Town, Scarborough, Sheffield Collegiate, Sheffield United, York, and Yorkshire CCC Academy.
