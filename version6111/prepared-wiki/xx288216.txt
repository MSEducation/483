==Work==

Stockenberg took part in the reconstruction of the Saint Mary's Cathedral in Reval (Tallinn) after it had been severely damaged by fire in 1684.
From 1687 he was employed to work on conversions and improvements to Toompea Castle.
His best known works are several stone sarcophagi that were commissioned for prominent people buried in Saint Mary's Cathedral, notably the grave monuments for the Swedish Field Marshals Fabian von Fersen[tpl]cite web|url=http://register.muinas.ee/?menuID=monument&action=view&id=1344 |title=Estonian National Registry of Cultural Monuments, monument 1344 |publisher=Register.muinas.ee |date= |accessdate=2013-11-23[/tpl] and Otto Wilhelm von Fersen.[tpl]cite web|url=http://register.muinas.ee/?menuID=monument&action=view&id=1367 |title=Estonian National Registry of Cultural Monuments, monument 1367 |publisher=Register.muinas.ee |date= |accessdate=2013-11-23[/tpl]
Stockenberg died in Reval.
