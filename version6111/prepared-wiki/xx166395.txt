[[Werner Mata]]

CATEGORIES: American soccer players, Los Angeles Kickers players, United States men's international soccer players, Living people

Werner Mata is a former U.S. soccer midfielder who earned seven caps with the U.S. national team between 1969 and 1973.  He is listed as Walner Mata in some sources.U.S. Men's National Team: K - O

==National team==

Mata's first two caps came in losses to Haiti in April and May 1969.USA - Details of International Matches 1885-1969  His last game was a 4-0 loss to Poland on March 20, 1973.USA - Details of International Matches 1970-1979

==Club career==

In the mid-1960s, Mata played for Orange County F.C.  He scored a goal in both a 1966 National Challenge Cup quarterfinal and semifinal games as Orange County went to the championship game.1966 U.S. Open Cup Quarterfinals1966 U.S. Open Cup Semifinals  He then moved to the Los Angeles Kickers in the fall of 1966.
