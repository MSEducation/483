[[Qarah Mohammad, Khodabandeh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Qarah Mohammad ([tpl]lang-fa|قره محمد[/tpl], also Romanized as Qarah Moḩammad and Qareh Moḩammad; also known as Kara-Mukhammed and Qarā Moḩammad)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3079719|Qarah Mohammad[/tpl] is a village in Karasf Rural District, in the Central District of Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 618, in 146 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
