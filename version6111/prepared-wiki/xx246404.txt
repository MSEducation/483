[[Rural, Wisconsin]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Wisconsin, Unincorporated communities in Waupaca County, Wisconsin

Rural, Wisconsin is an unincorporated community in the Town of Dayton, Waupaca County, Wisconsin.http://www.wisconsinhistory.org/dictionary/index.asp?action=view&term_id=4999

==History==

Rural was the home town of author and poet Margaret Ashmun (1885-1940.)http://digicoll.library.wisc.edu/cgi-bin/WI/WI-idx?type=article&did=WI.WT1942.JGWales&id=WI.WT1942&isize=M The Ashmun family home still stands in Rural.http://www.mainstreet-marketplace.com/Pages/Waupaca/houses%20of%20waupaca.htm
