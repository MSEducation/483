[[Micrurus diastema]]

CATEGORIES: Micrurus, Animals described in 1854, Reptiles of Mexico, Reptiles of Guatemala, Reptiles of Belize, Reptiles of Honduras

The Variable coral snake (Micrurus diastema) is a species of venomous elapid snake, native to southeastern Mexico and northern Central America. There are seven recognized subspecies.

==Distribution and habitat==

M. diastema occurs in tropical wet, moist and dry forest from near sea level to 1,250 m in Mexico, Belize, Guatemala and Honduras.
