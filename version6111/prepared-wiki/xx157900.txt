[[Belmopan Bandits]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Belize, Super League of Belize

Belmopan Bandits is a Belizean football team which currently competes in the Premier League of Belize.[tpl]cite news|title= Premier League of Belize kicks off this weekend |url=http://amandala.com.bz/news/premier-league-of-belize-kicks-off-this-weekend/|accessdate=4 February 2012|newspaper=Amandala Online|date=3 February 2012[/tpl]  
The team is based in Belmopan.  Their home stadium is Isidoro Beaton Stadium.
