[[Tickera, South Australia]]

CATEGORIES: Yorke Peninsula

Tickera is a settlement on the northern Spencer Gulf coast of Yorke Peninsula. It is located 20 km north of Wallaroo by road. It was surveyed in 1882, but experienced neither significant growth nor stability until the early 1970s. At this time, people started considering Tickera for holiday home purchases and retirement prospects.SA Memory > Tickera State Library of South Australia. Accessed 2014-04-02. It has a thriving football club called the Tickera Taipans.
