[[Covilhã Municipality]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Portugal, Populated places in Castelo Branco District

Covilhã Municipality ([tpl]IPA-pt|kuviˈʎɐ̃|-|Pt-pt Covilhã FF.ogg[/tpl]) is a municipality in the district of Castelo Branco and contains the city of Covilhã. This concelho (municipality) is divided in 31 freguesias (civil parishes).
