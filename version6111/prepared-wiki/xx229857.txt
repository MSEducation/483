[[Kanava]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Solomon Islands, Rennell and Bellona Islands

Kanava is a village in the Solomon Islands, on Rennell Island in the Rennell and Bellona province.

==Location==

Travel easterly for approx 15km or 45min from Tigoa (village located just prior to Tbamagu Village

==Population==

80 people approx.

==Religion==

South Seas Evangelical Church (SSEC)

==Police==

Generally policing is serviced by the Tigoa police station
