[[Claudio Scremin]]

CATEGORIES: 1968 births, Living people, Canadian ice hockey defencemen, Ice hockey people from British Columbia, London Knights (UK) players, HC Varese players, People from Burnaby, San Jose Sharks players

Claudio Francesco Scremin (born May 28, 1968 in Burnaby, British Columbia) is a former National Hockey League player. He played in 17 games for the San Jose Sharks.
