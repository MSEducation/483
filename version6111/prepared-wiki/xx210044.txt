[[Juárez, Chiapas]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Chiapas, Municipalities of Chiapas

 
thumb|right|220px|Centro de Juárez, ChiapasJuárez is a town and one of the 119 municipalities of Chiapas, in southern Mexico.
As of 2005, the municipality had a total population of 19,956.[tpl]cite web|title=Juárez|url=http://www.e-local.gob.mx/work/templates/enciclo/chiapas/municipios/07048a.htm|work=Enciclopedia de los Municipios de México|publisher=Instituto Nacional para el Federalismo y el Desarrollo Municipal / Gobierno del Estado de Chiapas |accessdate=2008-07-25[/tpl] It covers an area of 161.5 km².
