[[Tell the Next Girl]]

CATEGORIES: 2010 debut albums, Hilary Weaver albums

Tell The Next Girl is the first album by Canadian singer-songwriter Hilary Weaver. It was released in Canada on February 24, 2010. Weaver began recording the album in August 2008. The record was written by Hilary Weaver along with co-writer Mark Zubek. All production for the album was done by Mark Zubek.
