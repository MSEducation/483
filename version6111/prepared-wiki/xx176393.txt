[[Billy Alford]]

CATEGORIES: 1981 births, Living people, Vanderbilt Commodores football players, Baltimore Ravens players, Nashville Kats players, Frankfurt Galaxy players, Denver Broncos players, Jacksonville Sharks players

William "Billy" Alford (born October 30, 1981) is a former American football player who is now the defensive backs coach for the Jacksonville Sharks of the Arena Football League (AFL). Alford played college football at Vanderbilt University.
