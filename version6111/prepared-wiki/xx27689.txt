[[Admah]]

CATEGORIES: Torah cities

Admah (or Adamah - earth) was one of the pentapolis of the Vale of Siddim.Bible: Genesis 10:19 It was destroyed along with Sodom and Gomorrah.Bible: Deuteronomy 29:23 It is supposed by some[tpl]Who|date=March 2011[/tpl] to be the same as the "Adam" of Joshua 3:16, the name of which still lingers in Damieh, a ford of the Jordan river.
