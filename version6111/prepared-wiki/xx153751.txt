[[Flattop Island]]

CATEGORIES: San Juan Islands

Flattop Island is an island in the San Juan Islands of the U.S. state of Washington.[tpl]gnis|1531454[/tpl]
The name was given by Charles Wilkes during the Wilkes Expedition of 1838-1842, describing the island's terrain.[tpl]cite book |last= Phillips |first= James W. |title= Washington State Place Names |year= 1971 |publisher= University of Washington Press |isbn= 0-295-95158-3[/tpl]
