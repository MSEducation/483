[[Wanchaq District]]

Wanchaq District is one of eight districts of the province Cusco in Peru.  It includes Avenida de la Cultura, and Avenida de los Incas.  It is home to 2 hospitals, and borders the district of San Sebastian.  [tpl]es icon[/tpl] Instituto Nacional de Estadística e Informática. Banco de Información Distrital. Retrieved April 11, 2008.
