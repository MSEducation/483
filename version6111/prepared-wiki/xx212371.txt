[[Benzylacetone]]

CATEGORIES: Ketones, Flavors, Perfume ingredients

It can be used as an attractant for melon flies (Bactrocera cucurbitae),[tpl]cite web|url=http://entomology.ifas.ufl.edu/creatures/fruit/tropical/melon_fly.htm|title=University of Florida Featured Creatures|accessdate=2008-11-18[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.answers.com/topic/benzylacetone|title=Answers.com webpage|accessdate=2008-11-18[/tpl] in perfume,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.thegoodscentscompany.com/data/rw1024231.html|title=The Goods Company webpage|accessdate=2008-11-18[/tpl] and as an odorant for soap.
