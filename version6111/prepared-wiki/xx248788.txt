[[Denwa Matteimasu]]

CATEGORIES: 2001 singles, Zetima Records singles, Song recordings produced by Tsunku

[tpl]nihongo|"Denwa Matteimasu"|電話待っています|Call Waiting[/tpl] is the third single by Japanese girl group Melon Kinenbi. Its highest position on the Oricon weekly chart was #53.
