[[Queen's Cup]]

CATEGORIES: Queen's Cup, Football cup competitions in Thailand, Recurring sporting events established in 1970, 1970 establishments in Thailand

The Queen's Cup is an annual football cup competition in Thailand. Run by the Football Association of Thailand, it was first contested in 1970. It is named after Queen Sirikit. 
Bangkok Bank and Royal Thai Air Force were the first teams that won this competition by shared.
