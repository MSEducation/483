[[Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Gagnoa]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Ivory Coast

The Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Gagnoa ([tpl]lang-la|Gagnoaën(sis)[/tpl]) is the Metropolitan See for the Ecclesiastical province of Gagnoa in Côte d'Ivoire.
