[[Zulfadhli Mohamed]]

CATEGORIES: 1992 births, Living people, Malaysian footballers, Johor, Association football goalkeepers

Zulfadhli Mohamed (12 April 1992), is a Malaysian footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Malaysia Super League side, Johor Darul Takzim FC.

==Club career==

===Harimau Muda B===

Zulfadhli Mohamed was a product of the Malaysian youth squad or better known as Harimau Muda. He was a team member of the B squad, which is a feeder team for the Harimau Muda A.

===Johor Darul Takzim FC===

Zulfadhli Mohamed joined Johor Darul Takzim FC in 2013 season. He was a reserve goalkeeper behind second-choice goalkeeper Muhammad Zamir Selamat. But in 2014, he will be transfer to hundred-choice goolkeeper
