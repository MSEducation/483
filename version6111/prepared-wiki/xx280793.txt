[[2013 Summer Tour (Toni Braxton)]]

CATEGORIES: Toni Braxton concert tours, 2013 concert tours

The 2013 Summer Tour is a concert tour by American Pop/R&B singer Toni Braxton. The tour kicked off in Las Vegas on August 9, 2013 and ended in Atlanta on August 31, 2013.[tpl]cite web|url=http://singersroom.com/content/2013-07-01/Toni-Braxton-Announces-North-American-Tour-Duet-Album-With-Babyface-Coming-This-Fall/|title=Toni Braxton Announces North American Tour, Duet Album With Babyface Coming This Fall|accessdate=July 1, 2013|work=Singersroom[/tpl] Toni's sisters, Trina and Towanda Braxton, were featured on the tour as background singers.[tpl]Twitter|twitter.com/tonibraxton/status/365697312187617280|date: 8 August 2013[/tpl]
