[[Livadero]]

CATEGORIES: Former municipalities in West Macedonia

Livadero ([tpl]lang-el|Λιβαδερό[/tpl]) is a village and a former community in Kozani regional unit, West Macedonia, Greece. Since the 2011 local government reform it is part of the municipality Servia-Velventos, of which it is a municipal unit.Kallikratis law Greece Ministry of Interior [tpl]el icon[/tpl] The population in 2001 was 1,645.
