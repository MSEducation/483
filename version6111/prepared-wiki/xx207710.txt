[[George Ring]]

CATEGORIES: 1770 births, 1865 deaths, English cricketers, English cricketers of 1787 to 1825, Kent cricketers

George Ring (born 9 April 1770 at Darenth, Dartford, Kent; died 4 May 1865 at Bethersden, Kent) was an English professional cricketer who made 2 known appearances in major cricket matches in 1796.  He was the younger brother of Joey Ring.

==Career==

He was mainly associated with Kent although his two major appearances were both for All-England.Arthur Haygarth, Scores & Biographies, Volume 1 (1744-1826), Lillywhite, 1862
