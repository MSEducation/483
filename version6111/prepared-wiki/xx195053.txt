[[Herbert James Hagerman]]

Herbert James Hagerman (December 15, 1871 â€“ January 28, 1935) was an American attorney, was the 17th Governor of the New Mexico Territory from 1906 to 1907.
Hagerman was born in Milwaukee, Wisconsin to industrialist J. J. Hagerman. He graduated from Cornell University in 1890. From 1898 to 1901, he was the second secretary at the United States Embassy to Russia.
