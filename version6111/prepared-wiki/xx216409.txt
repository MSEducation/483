[[Farmington Municipal Schools]]

CATEGORIES: School districts in New Mexico, Education in San Juan County, New Mexico

Farmington Municipal Schools (also known as the Farmington Municipal School District) is a public school district based in Farmington, New Mexico, United States. 
The district covers a [tpl]convert|807|sqmi|km2|adj=on[/tpl] area[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.ped.state.nm.us/IT/fs/18/district.square.miles.pdf | title = District Size in Square Miles| format = PDF | publisher = Accountability and Information Services, New Mexico Public Education Department | accessdate = 2008-08-18[/tpl] in central San Juan County.  In addition to Farmington, the district also serves the communities of Napi HQ as well as a portion of Flora Vista.
All FMS district schools above elementary school are supplied with MacBook computers in which the teachers and students can use and take home with them.

==Demographics==

There were a total of 10,253 students enrolled in Farmington Municipal Schools during the 2007-2008 school year. The gender makeup of the district was 48.70% female and 51.30% male.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.ped.state.nm.us/IT/fs/15/07.08.gender.pdf | title = Total Enrollment by Gender for School Year 2007-2008| format = PDF | publisher = Information Technology Division, New Mexico Public Education Department  | accessdate = 2008-08-18[/tpl] The racial makeup of the district was 43.00% White, 29.94% Native American, 24.94% Hispanic, 1.44% African American, and 0.67% Asian/Pacific Islander.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.ped.state.nm.us/IT/fs/13/07.08.ethnic.pdf | title = Total Enrollment by Ethnicity for School Year 2007-2008| format = PDF | publisher = Information Technology Division, New Mexico Public Education Department  | accessdate = 2008-08-18[/tpl]
