[[Mizuno Tadashige]]

CATEGORIES: 1541 births, 1600 deaths, Place of birth unknown, Place of death missing, Date of birth unknown

[tpl]nihongo|Mizuno Tadashige|水野 忠重|extra=1541 – August 27, 1600[/tpl] was a retainer of the Japanese clan of Tokugawa following the later years of the Azuchi-Momoyama period of the 16th century. Tadashige was the son of Mizuno Tadamasa and the brother of Mizuno Nobumoto.
He was at one time given his brother's fief of Kariya in Mikawa. He was killed by Kagai Hidemasa following the Sekigahara Campaign of 1600.
