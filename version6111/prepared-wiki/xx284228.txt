[[List of Jacksonville Sharks seasons]]

CATEGORIES: Arena Football League seasons by team, Jacksonville Sharks

This is a list of seasons completed by the Jacksonville Sharks. The Sharks are a professional arena football franchise of the Arena Football League (AFL), based in Jacksonville, Florida, and plays its home games at Jacksonville Veterans Memorial Arena. The team was established in 2010. The Sharks have won their division in every year of their existence, as of 2013. The team reached ArenaBowl XXV in their second year of play, in which they won the league's championship on a game-winning touchdown as time expired.
