[[Invasive Species Forecasting System]]

CATEGORIES: Ecology, Invasive species

The Invasive Species Forecasting System, or simply ISFS, is a modeling environment for creating predictive habitat suitability maps for invasive species. It was developed by the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) in cooperation with various Department of Interior bureaus, including the United States Geological Survey (USGS), the Bureau of Land Management (BLM), and the National Park Service (NPS).
