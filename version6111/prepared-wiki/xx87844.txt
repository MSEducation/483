[[Bosley Butte]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of Oregon, Landforms of Curry County, Oregon

Bosley Butte is a mountain in the Klamath Mountains of southwestern Oregon in the United States. It is located in southern Curry County in the extreme southwest corner of the state. It is approximately [tpl]convert|5|mi|0[/tpl] from the Pacific Ocean and [tpl]convert|5|mi|0[/tpl] north of the California state line.
