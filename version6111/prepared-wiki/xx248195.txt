[[Vitaliy Starukhin]]

CATEGORIES: 1949 births, 2000 deaths, Sportspeople from Minsk, Deaths from pneumonia, Soviet footballers, FC Vorskla Poltava players, FC Shakhtar Donetsk players

Vitaliy Vladimirovich Starukhin ([tpl]lang-uk|Старухін Віталій Володимирович[/tpl]; born June 6, 1949 in Minsk; died August 9, 2000 in Donetsk of pneumonia) was a Soviet professional football player. He is considered by many fans to be the greatest players to ever play for Shakhtar Donetsk.http://sport.segodnya.ua/football/kratkaja-entsiklopedija-pobed-shakhtera.html
In 2011 Vitaly Starukhin, together with Oleg Blohin and Igor Belanov was named as "the legends of Ukrainian football" at the Victory of Football awards.http://www.bulvar.com.ua/arch/2011/51/4ef313ba6efb7/
