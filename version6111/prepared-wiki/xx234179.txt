[[Makinokō Station]]

CATEGORIES: Izuhakone Sunzu Line, Railway stations in Shizuoka Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Makinokō Station|牧之郷駅|Makinokō-eki[/tpl] is a train station of the Izuhakone Railway’s Sunzu Line located in the city of Izu, Shizuoka Prefecture, Japan. It is 18.6 rail kilometers from the line’s terminus at Mishima Station.

==History==

Makinokō Station was opened on August 1, 1924, as part of the final extension of the Sunzu line from Ōhito to its present terminus at Shuzenji.Japanese Wikipedia entry

==Lines==

==Station layout==

Makinokō Station has two opposed side platforms connected to the station building by a level crossing. The station building is unmanned and automatic ticket machines.
