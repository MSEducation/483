[[Eye Spy (EP)]]

CATEGORIES: Bullet for My Valentine albums

 
Eye Spy is a Demo by Welsh metalcore band Jeff Killed John, known today as Bullet for My Valentine. Eye Spy contains two tracks. The first song Eye Spy, is actually a demo of the later, Hit The Floor off of Bullet for My Valentine's album "The Poison". The second song ''Don't Walk Away is a second version of the one released on their debut EP Better Off Alonehttp://jeffkilledjohn.blogspot.com/2011/02/jeff-killed-john-full-discography.html
