[[List of heads of state of Cambodia]]

CATEGORIES: Lists of heads of state, Heads of state of Cambodia, Lists of Cambodian people by occupation

This is a list of heads of state of Cambodia from the death of King Norodom Suramarit on 3 April 1960 until the restoration of the monarchy under Norodom Sihanouk as King on 24 September 1993. It lists various heads of state which served in the meantime, under several different regimes and with several different titles.
