[[Buckeye, Kentucky]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Garrard County, Kentucky, Unincorporated communities in Kentucky

Buckeye is an unincorporated community in Garrard County, Kentucky, United States.  It lies along Route 39 northeast of the city of Lancaster, the county seat of Garrard County.Rand McNally.  The Road Atlas '06. Chicago: Rand McNally, 2006, p. 43.  Its elevation is 955 feet (291 m).[tpl]gnis|507605[/tpl], Geographic Names Information System, 2008-01-03.  Accessed 2007-12-31.
