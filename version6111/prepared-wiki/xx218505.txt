[[Kürkənd, Nakhchivan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Sharur District

Kürkənd (until 2003,Parliament of Azerbaijan - renaming law Kürdkənd, Kyurdkend, and Kyurtkend) is a village and municipality in the Sharur District of Nakhchivan Autonomous Republic, Azerbaijan.  It has a population of 858.
