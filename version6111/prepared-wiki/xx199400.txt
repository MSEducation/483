[[Sakaishi Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Osaka Prefecture, Stations of West Japan Railway Company

[tpl]nihongo|Sakaishi Station|堺市駅|Sakaishi-eki[/tpl] is a railway station on the West Japan Railway Company (JR West) Hanwa Line in Sakai-ku, the city of Sakai, Osaka Prefecture, Japan. 
This station is accessible with ICOCA, Suica, TOICA, SUGOCA and PiTaPa.
