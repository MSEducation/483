[[Zone Music Reporter]]

CATEGORIES: American record charts

Zone Music Reporter (formerly New Age Reporter) is a website tracking New Age, world, and instrumental music.  The site features weekly playlist, monthly airplay charts, and album reviews.  Premium services are also offered to artists and their management compiling custom airplay reports for that artist.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.zonemusic.biz/home/welcome.asp|title=Welcome|publisher=Zone Music Reporter|accessdate=2009-10-17[/tpl]
