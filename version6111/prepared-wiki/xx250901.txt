[[My Cleaner]]

CATEGORIES: 2005 novels, Novels about writers, Novels set in Uganda

My Cleaner is a novel by English author Maggie Gee. It was first published in 2005 by Saqi Books and concerns racism and family life.http://www.guardian.co.uk/books/2005/aug/21/fiction.features1 According to Bernardine Evaristo it was her best novel to that date.Back cover, 1st edition 
Maggie Gee visited Uganda in 2003, sponsored by the Cheltenham Literature Festival's 'Across Continents' commission and a Society of Authors grant,1st edition frontispiece and the novel features many scenes set in that country.  The story is continued in My Driver (2009).http://www.guardian.co.uk/books/2009/mar/28/my-driver

==Plot introduction==

The story is told from the viewpoint of Vanessa Henman, an English writer, and Mary Tendo Ugandan graduate of Makerere University. Vanessa's 22-year-old son Justin refuses to get out of bed with depression.  Justin asks for Mary who looked after him as a child so Vanessa writes to her. Mary returns to London to help Justin, but this time not as a cleaner...
