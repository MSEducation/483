[[Not So Dumb]]

CATEGORIES: 1930 films, American films, Films directed by King Vidor, Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer films, 1930s comedy films

Not So Dumb (1930) is a comedy motion picture starring Marion Davies, directed by King Vidor, and produced for Cosmopolitan Productions for Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer. 
It is based on the stage play Dulcy by George S. Kaufman and Marc Connelly that starred Lynn Fontanne.
The film resulted in a financial loss for the studio of $39,000.Nasaw, David. The Chief: The Life of William Randolph Hearst. Boston: Houghton Mifflin, 2000. pg.411.
