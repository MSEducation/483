[[A City on Fire]]

CATEGORIES: Fightstar songs, 2009 singles, Songs written by Charlie Simpson, Songs written by Alex Westaway, Alternative rock songs, Post-hardcore songs

"A City on Fire" is a song by English alternative rock band Fightstar, released 20 December 2009, on Search and Destroy Records as a digital download only.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.amazon.co.uk/s/ref=nb_ss_0_9?url=search-alias%3Ddigital-music&field-keywords=fightstar+a+city+on+fire&sprefix=Fightstar|title=A City on Fire by Fightstar|publisher=Amazon|accessdate=6 December 2009[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.play.com/Music/MP3-Download-Track/4-/12868030/A-City-On-Fire/Product.html?aid=12866461|title=Fightstar - A City on Fire - MP3 music download|publisher=Play.com|accessdate=6 December 2009[/tpl]
The single is featured on the deluxe edition re-release of the band's third album, Be Human, which includes four recorded tracks.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.newsoftheworld.co.uk/showbiz/xs/454505/Read-my-full-interview-with-singer-Charlie-Simpson.html?OTC-RSSATTR=xs|title=Fightstar: Interview|publisher=News of the World|author=Wootton, Dan|accessdate=15 August 2009[/tpl]
