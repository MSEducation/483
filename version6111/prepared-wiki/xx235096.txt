Denis Podalydès (born 22 April 1963) is a French actor and scriptwriter of Greek descent.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://eu.greekreporter.com/2011/01/15/greek-french-actor-denis-podalydes-stars-as-sarkozy-in-la-conquete/ |title=Greek-French Actor Denis Podalydès Stars as Sarkozy in “La Conquête” |accessdate=2011-01-11|work=greekreporter.com[/tpl] He is a Sociétaire of the Comédie-Française.
Podalydès has appeared in 80 films since 1989. He starred in The Officers' Ward, which was entered into the 2001 Cannes Film Festival.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.festival-cannes.com/en/archives/ficheFilm/id/1100087/year/2001.html |title=Festival de Cannes: The Officers' Ward |accessdate=2009-10-17|work=festival-cannes.com[/tpl]
