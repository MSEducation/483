[[Colonial Theatre (South Hill, Virginia)]]

CATEGORIES: Theatres on the National Register of Historic Places in Virginia, Theatres completed in 1925, Buildings and structures in Mecklenburg County, Virginia, National Register of Historic Places in Mecklenburg County, Virginia

Colonial Theatre, also known as The Colonial Center for the Performing Arts, is a historic movie theater located at South Hill, Mecklenburg County, Virginia. It was built in 1925, and housed in a three story, brick, commercial building.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.dhr.virginia.gov/registers/Counties/Mecklenburg/NR_Mecklenburg_Colonial%20Theatre_301-5054_Text.pdf|title=National Register of Historic Places Inventory/Nomination: Colonial Theatre|author=James S. Sease, Esq. and Joanna Nix| date=May 2009 |publisher=Virginia Department of Historic Resources[/tpl] and Accompanying three photos
It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2003.
