[[Ilișești]]

CATEGORIES: Communes in Suceava County

Ilişeşti is a commune located in Suceava County, Romania. It is composed of two villages, Braşca and Ilişeşti. The commune was called Ciprian Porumbescu (with Ilişeşti village its center) and included Bălăceana and Ciprian Porumbescu villages until 2004, when these were split off to form separate communes.
