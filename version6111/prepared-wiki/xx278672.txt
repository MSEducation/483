[[Jureh Kandi]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Jureh Kandi ([tpl]lang-fa|جوره كندي[/tpl], also Romanized as Jūreh Kandī; also known as Jūrah Khān)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3766526|Jureh Kandi[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 351, in 79 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
