[[Walnut Township, Adair County, Missouri]]

CATEGORIES: Townships in Adair County, Missouri, Kirksville, Missouri micropolitan area, Townships in Missouri

Walnut Township is one of ten townships in Adair County, Missouri, United States. At the 2010 census, its population was 257.[tpl]cite web| url=http://factfinder2.census.gov/bkmk/table/1.0/en/DEC/10_DP/G001/0600000US2900176786| title=Geographic Identifiers: 2010 Demographic Profile Data (G001): Walnut township, Adair County, Missouri| publisher=U.S. Census Bureau, American Factfinder| accessdate=June 20, 2013[/tpl]

==Geography==

Walnut Township covers an area of [tpl]convert|134.9|km2|disp=flip[/tpl] and contains no incorporated settlements. It contains two cemeteries: Cheeseman and Hall.[tpl]citation needed|date=April 2012[/tpl]
The streams of Little Mussel Creek and Little Walnut Creek run through this township.
