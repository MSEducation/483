[[Crystal goby]]

CATEGORIES: Gobiidae, Monotypic fish genera

Crystallogobius linearis, the Crystal goby, is a species of goby native to the Atlantic coasts of Europe and the Mediterranean Sea where it can be found at depths of from [tpl]convert|1|to|400|m|ft[/tpl].  Males of this species grow to a length of [tpl]convert|4.7|cm|in[/tpl] SL while females only reach [tpl]convert|3.9|cm|in[/tpl] SL.  This species is the only known member of its genus.[tpl]FishBase species |genus= Crystallogobius|species= linearis| month = June | year = 2013[/tpl]
