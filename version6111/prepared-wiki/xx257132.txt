[[J.W. Stewart House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses completed in 1865, Italianate architecture in Iowa, Houses in Davenport, Iowa, Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Iowa, National Register of Historic Places in Scott County, Iowa

The J.W. Stewart House is located on the east side of Davenport, Iowa, United States.  The Italianate style residence has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since 1983.
