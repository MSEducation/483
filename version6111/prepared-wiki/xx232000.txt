[[Taravo]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of France, Rivers of Corsica

The Taravo is a river on the island of Corsica, France. Its source is in the mountainous middle of the island, southeast of Monte Renoso. It flows generally southwest, through Palneca, Cozzano and Guitera-les-Bains. It ends in the Mediterranean Sea in Serra-di-Ferro, west of Propriano.
