[[Jonathan Davies (cricketer, born 1980)]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 births, Living people, People from Maesteg, People from Glamorgan, Welsh cricketers, Wales Minor Counties cricketers

Jonathan Davies (born 23 February 1980) is a former Welsh cricketer.  Davies was a right-handed batsman who bowled left-arm medium pace.  He was born at Maesteg, Glamorgan.
Davies made his Minor Counties Championship debut for Wales Minor Counties in 1998 against Wiltshire.  From 1998 to 2001, he represented the team in 14 Championship matches, the last of which came against Herefordshire.Minor Counties Championship Matches played by Jonathan Davies  His MCCA Knockout Trophy debut for the team came in 1998 against the Warwickshire Cricket Board.  From 1998 to 2002, Davies represented the team in 11 Trophy matches, the last of which came against the Worcestershire Cricket Board.Minor Counties Trophy Matches played by Jonathan Davies  
His debut List A appearance for the team came in the 1st round of the 1998 NatWest Trophy against Nottinghamshire.  From 1998 to 2002, he represented the team in 4 List A matches, the last of which came against Durham in the 3rd round of the 2002 Cheltenham & Gloucester Trophy.List A Matches played by Jonathan Davies  In his 4 List A matches, he scored 4 runs at a batting average of 4.00, with a high score of 4*.List A Batting and Fielding For Each Team by Jonathan Davies  With the ball he took 4 wickets at a bowling average of 38.75, with best figures of 1/30.List A Bowling For Each Team by Jonathan Davies
