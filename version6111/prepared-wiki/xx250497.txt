[[Sköndal]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Stockholm

[tpl]lang|sv|Sköndal[/tpl]  or is a district in the Farsta borough of southern Stockholm. The name means beautiful valley in English. It neighbours [tpl]lang|sv|Gubbängen[/tpl] to the northwest, [tpl]lang|sv|Hökarängen[/tpl] to the west, Farsta to the southwest and [tpl]lang|sv|Larsboda[/tpl] to the south. To the east and partially north it neighbours the Skarpnäck borough, in particular the districts of [tpl]lang|sv|Skarpnäcks Gård[/tpl] and Orhem.[tpl]cite web|title=Områdesfakta Stockholm|publisher=Stockholms stads utrednings- och statistikkontor AB|url=http://www.uskab.se/index.php/omradesvis-statistik.html|accessdate=2010-01-09[/tpl] As of December 31, 2008, the total population of [tpl]lang|sv|Sköndal[/tpl] is 7,450.[tpl]cite web|title=Områdesfakta Sköndal|publisher=Stockholms stads utrednings- och statistikkontor AB|url=http://www.usk.stockholm.se/tabellverktyg/tv.aspx?projekt=omradesfakta&omrade=21608|accessdate=2010-07-25[/tpl]
The area was largely developed in the 1950s and 1960, with the building of a number of apartment blocks. In 1969 the shopping centre [tpl]lang|sv|Sköndals Centrum[/tpl] was built, consisting of small number of shops around an open square. The local library is found here, along with a small supermarket, a newsagents and a bar.[tpl]cite web|title=Sköndals Centrum|publisher=Sköndals Centrum Företagförening|url=http://www.skondalscentrum.se/om_skondalscentrum.htm|accessdate=2011-10-19[/tpl]
[tpl]lang|sv|Sköndal[/tpl] is well served by Stockholm's public transport system, with buses running from [tpl]lang|sv|Sköndals[/tpl] Centrum to Farsta, and from the nearby motorway, [tpl]lang|sv|Tyresövägen[/tpl], to [tpl]lang|sv|Gullmarsplan[/tpl]. [tpl]cite web|title=Sköndals Centrum|publisher=Sköndals Centrum Företagförening|url=http://www.skondalscentrum.se/om_skondalscentrum.htm|accessdate=2011-10-19[/tpl]
The area is largely residential, though broken up by green spaces, wooded areas and footpaths and cycleways. Close by is the lake [tpl]lang|sv|Drevviken[/tpl], with a walking trail around the edge, and the [tpl]lang|sv|Flaten[/tpl] conservation area.http://www.stockholm.se/OmStockholm/Forvaltningar-och-bolag/Stadsdelsforvaltningar/Farsta/Om-stadsdelsomradet/Skondal-/
