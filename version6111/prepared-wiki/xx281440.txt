[[Nordre Vartdal]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Møre og Romsdal, Ørsta

Nordre Vartdal is a village in the Vartdal area of Ørsta Municipality in Møre og Romsdal county, Norway.  The village is located along the Vartdalsfjorden about [tpl]convert|4|km[/tpl] northeast of the village of Sætre.  The village sits at the entrance to the Ådalen valley, at the mouth of the Storelva river.  The European route E39 highway runs through Nordre Vartdal, right past Vartdal Church, which sits on the shore of the fjord.
