[[Pima Butte]]

CATEGORIES: Landforms of Pinal County, Arizona

Pima Butte ([tpl]Lang-mrc|Vii Vav[/tpl]) is a mountain summit in Pinal County, Arizona eight miles north of present day Maricopa, Arizona.  Pima Butte is 1,660 feet (505.97 meters) above sea level.[tpl]GNIS|9434|Pima Butte[/tpl]  As a significant landmark in the Gila Valley it was near the site of the 1857 Battle of Pima Butte, to which it gave its name.
