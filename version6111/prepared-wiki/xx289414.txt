[[Tatardeh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Tatardeh ([tpl]lang-fa|تاتارده[/tpl], also Romanized as Tātārdeh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3773412|Tatardeh[/tpl] is a village in Shivanat Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 302, in 67 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
