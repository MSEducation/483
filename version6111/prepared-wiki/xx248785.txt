[[2009 Togolese Championnat National]]

CATEGORIES: Football competitions in Togo, 2009 in Togo, 2009 domestic association football leagues

The 2009 Togolese Championnat National is the forty-eighth season of the Togolese Championnat National since its establishment in 1961. A total of 16 teams are contesting the league.

==League table==

Marantha had points removed for a complication regarding the registration of one of their players and were relegated. They are fighting the decision at the Court of Arbitration for Sport.
