[[Shree (2002 film)]]

CATEGORIES: Tamil-language films, Indian films, Tamil films of 2002, 2002 films

Shree is a 2002 Tamil action film directed by Pushpavasagan which stars Suriya in the title role, with Shrutika and Gayatri Jayaraman in supporting roles.http://www.cinesouth.com/cgi-bin/filmography/newfilmdb.cgi?name=sree The film met with a poor response, critically and made poor business at the box office .
