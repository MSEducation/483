[[Aliabad, Zanjanrud]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Aliabad ([tpl]lang-fa|علي اباد[/tpl], also Romanized as ‘Alīābād)[tpl]IranNCSGN[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 335, in 81 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
