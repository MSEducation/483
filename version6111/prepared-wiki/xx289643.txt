[[Nosratabad, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Nosratabad ([tpl]lang-fa|نصرت اباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Noşratābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|10080891|Nosratabad[/tpl] is a village in Aq Bolagh Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 607, in 112 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
