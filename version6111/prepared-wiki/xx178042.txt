[[George Oliver (golfer)]]

CATEGORIES: American male golfers, Amateur golfers, Olympic golfers of the United States, Golfers at the 1904 Summer Olympics, Olympic medalists in golf, Olympic bronze medalists for the United States, 1883 births, 1965 deaths

George Charles Oliver (January 18, 1883 â€“ August 20, 1965) was an American golfer who competed in the 1904 Summer Olympics.
In 1904 he was part of the American team which won the bronze medal. He finished 30th in this competition.
In the individual competition he finished 50th in the qualification and did not advance to the match play.
