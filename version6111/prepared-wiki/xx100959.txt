[[Giacomo Raffaelli]]

CATEGORIES: 1753 births, 1836 deaths, Artists from Rome, Italian artists, Mosaic artists

Giacomo Raffaelli (February 2, 1753 - October 11, 1836) was an Italian mosaicist from Rome. He is the author of a copy of Leonardo da Vinci's Last Supper commissioned by Napoleon I.[tpl]cite web | title=Church Minoritenkirche – Inside view – Mosaic The last supper | url=http://www.vienna.cc/english/minoriten7.htm | accessdate=2008-12-30[/tpl] The mosaic resides at Vienna in the Minoritenkirche.
