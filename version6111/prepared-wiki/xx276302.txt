[[(37638) 1993 VB]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1993, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Apollo asteroids

(37638) 1993 VB is a near-Earth minor planet in the Apollo group.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on November 6, 1993.  It is listed as a potentially hazardous object.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
