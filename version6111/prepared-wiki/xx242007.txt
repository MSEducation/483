[[The Snake (Wildbirds & Peacedrums album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2007 albums

The Snake is an album by the Swedish duo Wildbirds & Peacedrums, released in 2008. It was recorded by the band and published by The Leaf Label. It was released to 'generally favorable reviews' with an average critic's score of 80/100 and an average user score of 9.6/10 on Metacritic.The Snake reviews at Metacritic.com
