[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Palmas–Francisco Beltrão]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Cascavel, Religious organizations established in 1933, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century, 1933 establishments in Brazil

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Palmas–Francisco Beltrão ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Palmensis–Beltranensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the cities of Palmas & Francisco Beltrão in the Ecclesiastical province of Cascavel in Brazil.
