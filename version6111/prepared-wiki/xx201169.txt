[[Fukiage Station (Saitama)]]

CATEGORIES: Takasaki Line, Stations of East Japan Railway Company

[tpl]nihongo|Fukiage Station|吹上駅|Fukiage-eki[/tpl] is a railway station on the Takasaki Line operated by East Japan Railway Company (JR East) in Kōnosu, Saitama, Japan.

==Layout==

The station has a side platform and an island platform.

==History==

The station opened on 1 March 1885.
