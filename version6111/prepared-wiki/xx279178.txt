[[Churuk-e Sofla]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Churuk-e Sofla ([tpl]lang-fa|چوروك سفلي[/tpl], also Romanized as Chūrūk-e Soflá; also known as Chūrūk-e Pā’īn and Chūzak-e Soflá)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3058919|Churuk-e Sofla[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 274, in 55 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
