[[Jarqavieh Olya District]]

CATEGORIES: Isfahan County, Districts of Isfahan Province

Jarqavieh Olya District ([tpl]lang-fa|بخش جرقویه علیا[/tpl]; Upper Jarqavieh District) is a district (bakhsh) in Isfahan County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 14,099, in 4,053 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|10[/tpl]  The District has one city: Hasanabad. The District has two rural districts (dehestan): Jarqavieh Olya Rural District and Ramsheh Rural District.
