Sir John Huddleton (1517 - 1557) of Sawston, Cambridgeshire, was an English politician.

==Family==

He married Bridget Cotton, and they had one daughter and two sons.

==Career==

He was a Member (MP) of the Parliament of England for Cambridgeshire in October 1553, April 1554 and November 1554.http://www.historyofparliamentonline.org/volume/1509-1558/member/huddleston-sir-john-1517-57
