[[Reubin Munday]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing, Year of death missing, Tennessee State Tigers football coaches

Reubin A. Munday was the ninth head football coach for the Tennessee State University Tigers located in Nashville, Tennessee and he held that position for three seasons, from 1937 until 1939.  His career coaching record at Tennessee State was 13 wins, 5 losses, and 2 ties.  As of the conclusion of the 2007 season, this ranks him ninth at Tennessee State in total wins and fifth at Tennessee State in winning percentage (.700).Tennessee State University coaching records
