[[St. John Lloyd]]

CATEGORIES: Secondary schools in Carmarthenshire, Roman Catholic secondary schools in the Diocese of Menevia

St. John Lloyd Catholic Comprehensive School is a mixed, community comprehensive school of around 500 pupils, catering for all abilities across an age range of 11 to 16 years. 
The school is situated in Llanelli's Dafen suburb, although pupils are drawn from as far afield as Carmarthen, Pembrey, Burry Port, Kidwelly,  and Trimsaran.
