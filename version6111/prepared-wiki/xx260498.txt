[[Stratusfaction (TV series)]]

CATEGORIES: CBC network shows, 1973 Canadian television series debuts, 1973 Canadian television series endings, Canadian music television series

Stratusfaction was a Canadian music variety television series which aired on CBC Television in mid-1973.

==Premise==

Stratusfaction was an 18-member Calgary music group which performed throughout western Canada since 1971. They starred in this mid-year replacement series to perform theatrical and pop songs. Guest artists were featured in each episode except the final: Ed Evanko, Dianne Heatherington, Catherine McKinnon, Pat Rose and Diane Stapley. The series was recorded in Winnipeg on location at the Manitoba Theatre Centre during June 1973.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.broadcasting-history.ca/programming/television/programming_popup.php?id=1284 | first=John | last=Corcelli | date=September 2005 | title=Stratusfaction | publisher=Canadian Communications Foundation | accessdate=7 May 2010 [/tpl]

==Scheduling==

This half-hour series was broadcast Saturdays at 7:00 p.m. from 28 July to 8 September 1973.
