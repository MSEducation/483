[[30th Indian Infantry Brigade]]

CATEGORIES: British Indian Army brigades

The 30th Indian Infantry Brigade was an Infantry formation of the Indian Army during World War II. It was formed on 15 January 1943, by the re designation of HQ Baghdad Line of Communications Sub Area. The brigade was converted into the 2nd Indian Infantry Division in August 1943.
