[[Jystrup]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Region Zealand

Jystrup is a village with a population of 740 (1 January 2014)BEF44: Population 1st January, by urban areas database from Statistics Denmark located in the middle of Zealand, Denmark. It is located in Ringsted municipality in Region Zealand.
Known for its nearby Danish Tramway Museum of Skjoldenæsholm, the town is also close to a golf course and camping site.
The town has the postal code 4174 Jystrup, Midtsjælland.
