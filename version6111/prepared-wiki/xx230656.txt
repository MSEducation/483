[[Irancode]]

CATEGORIES: Economy of Iran, Supply chain management

Irancode is the Iranian national products and services classification and codification system. It enables producers and distributors to identify, classify and codify their products and services. http://www.irancode.ir/ Iran is presently implementing this bar code system across the country in order to facilitate e-commerce and tax collection.[ref]http://www.iran-daily.com/1387/3194/html/economy.htm
