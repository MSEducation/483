[[Hesperides (poetry)]]

CATEGORIES: 1648 books, English poetry collections

Hesperides  is a book of poetry published in 1648 by Robert Herrick. This collection of 1200  of his lyrical poems was published under his direction.http://www.litencyc.com/php/sworks.php?rec=true&UID=4769http://www.luminarium.org/sevenlit/herrick/herribio.htm His reputation rests on the book, which includes "To the Virgins, to Make Much of Time".
