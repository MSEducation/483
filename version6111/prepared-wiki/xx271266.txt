[[Gravesham Council election, 1999]]

CATEGORIES: English local elections, 1999, Gravesham Council elections

The 1999 Gravesham Council election took place on 6 May 1999 to elect members of Gravesham Borough Council in Kent, England. The whole council was up for election and the Labour party stayed in overall control of the council.[tpl]cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/hi/english/static/vote_99/locals_99/html/constituency/107.stm|title=Gravesham|publisher=BBC News Online|accessdate=24 September 2011[/tpl]
