[[Litoměřická wine]]

CATEGORIES: Wine regions of the Czech Republic, Central Bohemian Region, Ústí nad Labem Region, Czech wine

The sub-province Litoměřice (Czech: 'Litoměřická oblast') is one of two wine regions in Bohemia. It is situated on the Elbe river, with its center in the town of Litoměřice. This area contains 29 official wine municipalities.[tpl]cite web|author= |url=http://www.wineofczechrepublic.cz/en/regions-wineries/wine-region-bohemia/3064-litomerice-wine-sub-region.html |title=Litoměřice wine sub-region | Vína z Moravy a vína z Čech |publisher=Wineofczechrepublic.cz |date= |accessdate=2013-11-26[/tpl] The history of winery in this north-Bohemian region is longer than 1,000 years.
