[[A Ship Comes In]]

CATEGORIES: 1928 films, 1920s drama films, American silent feature films, Black-and-white films, American films, American drama films, Films directed by William K. Howard

A Ship Comes In (also known as His Country) is a 1928 silent film which tells the story of immigrants coming to the United States. It stars Rudolph Schildkraut, Louise Dresser, Milton Holmes, Linda Landi, and Fritz Feld. A Ship Comes In at silentera.com
The movie was written by Julien Josephson (story) (adaptation), John W. Krafft (titles) and Sonya Levien. It was directed by William K. Howard.
It was nominated for the Academy Award for Best Actress (Louise Dresser).The AFI Catalog of Feature Films: A Ship Comes In
