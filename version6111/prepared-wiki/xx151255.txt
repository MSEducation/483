[[San Clemente Pier (train station)]]

CATEGORIES: Amtrak stations in Orange County, California, Metrolink stations in Orange County, California, Geography in San Clemente, California

San Clemente Pier is a station on Amtrak's Pacific Surfliner passenger train route and Metrolink's Orange County Line and Inland Empire-Orange County Line located in San Clemente, California, United States. It is very lightly used and only a few trains per day stop at the station.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.trainweb.org/usarail/sanclemente.htm|title=Train Web: San Clemente[/tpl]  Of the 73 California stations served by Amtrak, San Clemente Pier was the 60th-busiest in FY2010, boarding or detraining an average of approximately 25 passengers daily.[tpl]cite web |title= Amtrak Fact Sheet, FY2010, State of California|publisher= Amtrak |date= November 2010 |url= http://www.amtrak.com/pdf/factsheets/CALIFORNIA10.pdf |format= PDF |accessdate= 2011-01-06[/tpl]

==Facilities==

There is a ticket kiosk located at the pier train stop in San Clemente.
Travelers are responsible for bringing their own property on and off the train when leaving or arriving at San Clemente Pier.
Dining, lodging and restroom facilities can be found on or around the pier and it is just minutes to walk to the main part of the Village.
Paid parking can be found on the street or in an adjacent municipal lot.
