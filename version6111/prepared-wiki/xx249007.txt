[[Thomas Doerflinger]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, American historians, Historians of the American Revolution

Thomas M. Doerflinger is an American historian.

==Life==

He is the son of William Main Doerflinger.http://pages.prodigy.net/folkmusic/wmdoerflinger.htm
He was a MCEAS Dissertation Fellow, at Harvard University in 1978-1979.http://www.mceas.org/pastfellows.htm
He trained as a historian at Princeton and Harvard.
