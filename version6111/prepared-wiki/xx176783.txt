[[Hoszów]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Bieszczady County

Hoszów [tpl]IPAc-pl|'|h|o|sz|u|f[/tpl] ([tpl]lang-uk|Гошів Hoshiv[/tpl]) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Ustrzyki Dolne, within Bieszczady County, Subcarpathian Voivodeship, in south-eastern Poland. It lies approximately [tpl]convert|6|km|mi|0[/tpl] south-east of Ustrzyki Dolne and [tpl]convert|85|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-east of the regional capital Rzeszów.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish[/tpl]
On 8 August 1769 in battle against Russian troops during the Bar Confederation was heavily injured and died afterwards in Lesko Franciszek Pułaski cousin of general Kazimierz Pułaski.
