[[Skolye (Hasidic dynasty)]]

CATEGORIES: Hasidic dynasties

The Skolye dynasty is a dynasty of Hasidic rabbis. It is named after the city of Skole ([tpl]lang-yi|Skolye[/tpl]) in Eastern Galicia (currently in the Ukraine) where the founder of this dynasty lived and led his court.
