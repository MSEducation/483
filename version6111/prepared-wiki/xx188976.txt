[[Iakora District]]

CATEGORIES: Ihorombe, Districts of Madagascar

{{Blacklisted-links|1=

Iakora is a district in south-eastern Madagascar. It's a part of Ihorombe Region and borders the districts of Ihosy in northwest, Ivohibe in northeast, Midongy Sud in east, Befotaka in south and Betroka in west. The area is [tpl]convert|4633|km2|sqmi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] and the population was 29,987 in 2001.[ref]
</ref>
The district is further divided into 3 communes.
