[[Gideon Barnard]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 births, Living people, South African cricketers, South Western Districts cricketers

Gideon Barnard (born 8 February 1980) was a South African cricketer. He was a right-handed batsman who played for South Western Districts. He was born in Port Elizabeth.
Barnard made a single appearance for the team, during the 2006-07 season. Batting as an opener, Barnard partnered Sammy-Joe Avontuur to a first-wicket partnership of 149 runs. Although South Western Districts won the game by an innings margin, Barnard was not picked again for the team.
