[[Gmina Turawa]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Opole Voivodeship, Opole County

Gmina Turawa, German Gemeinde Turawa is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Opole County, Opole Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Its seat is the village of Turawa, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|14|km|mi|0[/tpl] north-east of the regional capital Opole.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|171.46|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 31 December 2010 its total population is 9,595.

==Demographics==

As of 31 December 2010, the commune had 9,595 inhabitants. At the time of the census of 2002, the commune had 9,609 inhabitants. Of these, 5,673 (59%) declared the Polish nationality; 2,028 persons (21.1%) declared the German nationality; and 671 (7%) with the non-recognized Silesian nationality. 1,223 inhabitants (12.7%) declared no nationality.

==Neighbouring gminas==
