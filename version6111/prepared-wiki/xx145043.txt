[[MTV Pilipinas for Best Production Design in a Video]]

CATEGORIES: MTV Pilipinas Music Awards

The following is a list of MTV Pilipinas winners for Best Production Design in a Video. The said category is one of the seven new categories added in 2006 along with Favorite Pop Video, Favorite Hip-Hop and R&B Video, Favorite Rock Video, Best Animated Video, Best Cinematography in a Video and Best Editing in a Video.
