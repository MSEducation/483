Eric Regan (born May 20, 1988) is a Canadian professional ice hockey player who is currently playing for the Hannover Scorpions in the Deutsche Eishockey Liga (DEL).  
On September 22, 2008, he was signed as a free agent by the Anaheim Ducks.[tpl]cite book |last=National Hockey League|title=The National Hockey League Official Guide & Record Book/2011|year=2010 |publisher= Triumph Books |page= 327|isbn= 978-1-60078-422-4[/tpl] Following the completion of his entry-level contract within the Ducks affiliations, Regan signed a one-year contract with German DEL team, the Hannover Scorpions, on June 7, 2011.[tpl]cite web| url = http://www.hannoverscorpions.de/index.php | title = Eric Regan joins the Scorpions | publisher = Hannover Scorpions | date = 2011-06-07 | accessdate = 2011-06-07 | language = German[/tpl]
