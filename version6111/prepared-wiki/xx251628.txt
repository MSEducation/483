John McCarthy (born August 9, 1986) is an American professional ice hockey left winger who currently who plays for the San Jose Sharks of the National Hockey League.[tpl]cite web|url=http://sharks.nhl.com/club/news.htm?id=685487|title=Sharks Recall John McCarthy, Assign Matt Pelech|work=San Jose Sharks|date=2013-10-04|accessdate=2013-10-05[/tpl]

==Playing career==

McCarthy earned his first NHL point on October 21, 2010 against the Colorado Avalanche, by assisting Scott Nichol to score a game-tying goal at the end of the first period.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://sharks.nhl.com/club/boxscore.htm?id=2010020090|title=Sharks vs. Avalanche - 10/21/2010 - San Jose Sharks - Boxscore | publisher =San Jose Sharks | date = 2010-10-21 | accessdate = 2010-10-22[/tpl] His first NHL goal was scored the next game, on October 23, against Nikolai Khabibulin of the Edmonton Oilers.
