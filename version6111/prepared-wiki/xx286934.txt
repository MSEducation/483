[[Sefid Kamar, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Sefid Kamar ([tpl]lang-fa|سفيدكمر[/tpl], also Romanized as Sefīd Kamar and Safīd Kamar)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3083333|Sefid Kamar[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 206, in 67 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
