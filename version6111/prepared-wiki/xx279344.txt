[[Talkhab, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Talkhab ([tpl]lang-fa|تلخاب[/tpl], also Romanized as Talkhāb and Talkh Āb)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3766579|Talkhab[/tpl] is a village in Bughda Kandi Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 807, in 187 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
