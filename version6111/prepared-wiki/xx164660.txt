[[Natalie Fox]]

CATEGORIES: British romantic fiction writers, Living people

Natalie Guilar, better known by her pen name Natalie Fox, was a popular writer of 26 romance novels from 1991 to 2002. In 1997 she won the Preston Citizen[tpl]'[/tpl]s book of the year award for Passion With Intent, and since then her books have been translated into many languages.
