[[T. Charles Gaastra]]

CATEGORIES: 1879 births, 1947 deaths, American architects, Dutch emigrants to the United States, People from Santa Fe, New Mexico

Tjalke Charles Gaastra, [tpl]Post-nominals|country=NETHERLANDS[/tpl] (1879 - 1947) was a Dutch-born American architect who worked in the American southwest in the first half of the twentieth century. He won the International Exhibit of Architecture in Berlin for the Gildersleeve house in Santa Fe, New Mexico which he designed for New Mexico Supreme Court justice, David Chavez.[ref]
