[[Caldwell Parrish Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in Louisiana, Transportation in Caldwell Parish, Louisiana, Buildings and structures in Caldwell Parish, Louisiana

Caldwell Parrish Airport [tpl]airport codes|||F86[/tpl] is a public use airport in Caldwell Parish, Louisiana, United States. It is owned by the Caldwell Parish Police Jury and located two nautical miles (4 km) northeast of the central business district of Columbia, Louisiana. It was formerly known as Columbia Airport.

==Facilities and aircraft==

Caldwell Parrish Airport covers an area of 70 acres (28 ha) at an elevation of 67 feet (20 m) above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 1/19 with an asphalt surface measuring 3,501 by 75 feet (1,067 x 23 m).
For the 12-month period ending October 27, 2011, the airport had 13,750 general aviation aircraft operations, an average of 37 per day. At that time there were 12 aircraft based at this airport: 92% single-engine and 8% helicopter.
