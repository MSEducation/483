[[Chhime Rigzing]]

CATEGORIES: Tibetan politicians, Living people, Year of birth missing (living people)

Chhime Rigzing is a Tibetan government official and senior spokesman for Tenzin Gyatso, the 14th Dalai Lama. He functions as a private secretary and is part of the Central Tibetan Administration in exile in Dharamsala in India.
He was Representative of the Dalai Lama and the Government in Exile to Central and Eastern Europe, as head of the Geneva Tibet Bureau, from 27 August 2001 to 15 September 2005.[tpl]cite web |url= http://www.tibetswiss.ch/the-tibet-office.html |title= The Tibet Bureau in Geneva |author= |date= |work= |publisher= Tibetan Community in Switzerland and Liechtenstein | archiveurl  = | archivedate = |accessdate= 2011-05-17 | quote       = [/tpl]
