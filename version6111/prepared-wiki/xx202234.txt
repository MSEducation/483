[[Federico Bricolo]]

CATEGORIES: Venetian politicians, Venetist politicians, Living people, 1966 births

Federico Bricolo (Verona, 13 July 1966) is an Italian politician. He is a Venetist, member of Liga Veneta-Lega Nord.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/world/2004/mar/25/italy.sophiearie|title=Italian MPs plan control of new mosques|last=Arie|first=Sophie|date=25 March 2004|work=The Guardian|accessdate=5 August 2011[/tpl]
He has been member of the Chamber of Deputies from 2001 to 2008, when he was elected to the Senate, where he chairs the parliamentary group of Lega Nord. Between 2005 and 2006 he was Under-Secretary of Infrastructures and Transports in the Berlusconi government.
Bricolo is also deputy secretary of Liga Veneta.
