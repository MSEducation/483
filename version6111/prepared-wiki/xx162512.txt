Paul Healey (born March 20, 1975) is a retired Canadian professional ice hockey winger. He last played for the Florida Everblades in the ECHL.[tpl]cite web |url=http://blogs.naplesnews.com/everblades/2009/12/everblades-lost-mike-hoffman-and-ernie-hartlieb-to-ahl-sign-nhl-veteran-paul-healey.html|title=Everblades lose Mike Hoffman and Ernie Hartlieb to AHL, sign NHL veteran Paul Healey | publisher =naplenews.com | date = 2009-12-10 | accessdate = 2010-06-01[/tpl] He has played 77 games in the National Hockey League with the  Philadelphia Flyers, Toronto Maple Leafs, New York Rangers, and Colorado Avalanche.
