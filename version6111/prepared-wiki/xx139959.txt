[[Greg King (author)]]

CATEGORIES: 1964 births, American biographers, Living people

Greg King (born 1964) is an American author, best known for his biographies of prominent historical figures.

==Biography==

Born in 1964, King first became interested in history at the age of twelve, after watching two films about Imperial Russia's last royal family, the Romanovs: Nicholas and Alexandra (1971), and Anastasia (1956), which  after which he started reading all the books he could find on the subject, including Robert K. Massie's 1967 book, Nicholas and Alexandra.[tpl]cite web|url=http://forum.alexanderpalace.org/index.php?topic=30.30 |title=What got you interested in the Romanovs? |publisher=Forum.alexanderpalace.org |date= |accessdate=November 14, 2012[/tpl]
