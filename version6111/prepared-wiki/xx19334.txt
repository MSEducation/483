[[Dandenong Ranges National Park]]

CATEGORIES: National parks of Victoria (Australia), Protected areas established in 1987, Forests of Victoria (Australia), 1987 establishments in Australia

Dandenong Ranges National Park is in Victoria, Australia, [tpl]convert|38|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] east of Melbourne.
Because the park is located in an urban area, the park has a long history of major problems with feral and roaming animals. A cat-curfew was introduced in the entire Dandenong Ranges area some years ago, and since then the numbers and variety of lyrebirds and other native species have climbed dramatically.[tpl]citation needed|date=December 2013[/tpl]
