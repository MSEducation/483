[[Steven Bishop]]

CATEGORIES: 1970 births, Australian rock drummers, Living people, People from Brisbane, Powderfinger members

Steven Bishop (born on 3 March 1970) is an Australian drummer, formerly of Australian band Powderfinger though he left the band before their rise to prominence because of illness.  Later, while working in the UK for two years he played in London based bands. He has since gone on to record and perform with other Brisbane based bands, including Moonjuice, The Haymakers and The Predators (for which he is also lead vocalist).After finishing at Griffith Uni in 2006, now works in the Film and Television Industry."On the prowl with The Predators", Fasterlouder.com.au,  retrieved 8 June 2007
