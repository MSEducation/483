[[South Bermondsey railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Disused railway stations in Southwark, Former London, Brighton and South Coast Railway stations, Railway stations opened in 1866, Railway stations closed in 1917, Railway stations opened in 1919, Railway stations closed in 1928, Railway stations in Southwark, Former Southern Railway stations, Railway stations opened in 1928, Railway stations served by Southern, DfT Category E stations

South Bermondsey railway station is on the Inner South London Line, between London Bridge and Queens Road Peckham. It is served by trains from London Bridge to West Croydon via [tpl]stnlnk|East Dulwich[/tpl] and [tpl]stnlnk|Norbury[/tpl] (East Croydon on Sundays) and Beckenham Junction via [tpl]stnlnk|Crystal Palace[/tpl] as well as trains returning to London Bridge.

==History==

The original station was situated to the north-west of South Bermondsey Junction, on the northern side of Rotherhithe New Road.[tpl]cite book |last1=Mitchell |first1=Victor E. |last2=Smith |first2=Keith |title=South London Line |series=London Suburban Railways |date=February 1995 |publisher=Middleton Press |location=Midhurst |isbn=1-873793-46-4 |at=map above Figure 15 [/tpl] It opened on 13 August 1866 with the SOUTH LONDON Line, and was originally named Rotherhithe; it was renamed South Bermondsey on 1 December 1869. It closed on 17 June 1928, when the present station, situated south of South Bermondsey Junction, took its place.[tpl]cite book |last=Butt |first=R.V.J. |title=The Directory of Railway Stations |year=1995 |publisher=Patrick Stephens Ltd |location=Yeovil |isbn=1-85260-508-1 |id=R508 |pages=200, 215 [/tpl] The 1928 station is on an embankment, and its platforms and buildings are of wooden construction. After the adjacent New Den Stadium, Millwall F.C.'s home ground, opened in 1993, a direct footpath was built from the station to the ground.
Southern trains from London Bridge to [tpl]stnlnk|London Victoria[/tpl] via [tpl]stnlnk|Denmark Hill[/tpl] called at South Bermondsey until December 2012 when they were replaced by London Overground trains between [tpl]stnlnk|Surrey Quays[/tpl] and [tpl]stnlnk|Clapham Junction[/tpl].
