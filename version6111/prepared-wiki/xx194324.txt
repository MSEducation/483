[[Mutlu Topçu]]

CATEGORIES: 1970 births, Living people, Turkish footballers, Turkey international footballers, Turkey under-21 international footballers, Beşiktaş J.K. footballers, Adanaspor footballers, Association football defenders, People from Bursa

Mutlu Topçu (born 16 November 1970 in Bursa) is a former Turkish footballer.
He played his first game for Turkey against Iceland on 12 October 1994.
