[[Arnoseris]]

CATEGORIES: Asteraceae genera

Arnoseris is a monotypic genus of flowering plants in the daisy family.

==Secondary metabolites==

Arnoseris minima (L.) Schweigg. & Korte is a source of the simple coumarin aesculetin and the flavonoids luteolin, luteolin 7-O-β-D-glucoside, luteolin 4'-O-β-D-glucoside, and 3-O-methylquercetin.[tpl]cite journal | year = 2005 | authors = Zidorn, C; Udovičić, V; Spitaler, R; Ellmerer, E.
