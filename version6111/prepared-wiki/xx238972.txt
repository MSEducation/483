[[Our Lady of Victory Roman Catholic Church]]

CATEGORIES: Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Roman Catholic churches in New York, Roman Catholic churches completed in 1868, 19th-century Roman Catholic church buildings in the United States, Churches in Rochester, New York

Our Lady of Victory Roman Catholic Church, also known as Our Lady of Victory / St. Joseph Roman Catholic Church, is a historic Roman Catholic church located at Rochester in Monroe County, New York.  It was designed by noted Rochester architect Andrew Jackson Warner.
It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1992.
