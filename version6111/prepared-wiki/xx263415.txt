[[1893 Drake Bulldogs football team]]

CATEGORIES: 1893 college football season, Drake Bulldogs football seasons, College football winless seasons, 1893 in Iowa

The 1893 Drake Bulldogs football team represented Drake University during the 1893 college football season. It was the inaugural year for the football program. The season ended with an 0–2–1 record.
