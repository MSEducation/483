[[Jeff Gunther]]

CATEGORIES: Articles created via the Article Wizard, Living people, 1970 births

Jeff Gunther (born March 17, 1975 in Colorado Springs, Colorado) is an American entrepreneur and application architect. He currently resides in Charlottesville, Virginia with his wife and daughter. His first company was launched in the dot-com era in Northern Virginia, and his second venture, Meddius, capitalized on the growing need for integration in the healthcare industry. His newest venture, OpenSpace, launched to create a collaborative workspace for nomadic independents.
