[[Norman Doig]]

CATEGORIES: 1910 births, 2001 deaths, Australian rules footballers from Western Australia, East Fremantle Football Club players, Essendon Football Club players, Doig family, Australian people of Scottish descent

John Norman Doig (born 24 July 1910 â€“ 29 July 2001) was an Australian rules footballer who played for East Fremantle in the West Australian National Football League (WANFL) during the 1930s. He also had a brief stint with Essendon in the Victorian Football League (VFL).
Doig was from a famous footballing family, with his first cousins George Doig and Ron Doig, amongst others, playing league football. His brothers Bill and Edgar also represented East Fremantle in the WANFL. He made a total of 138 senior appearances for East Fremantle, with Doig's two games at Essendon coming when he was in Melbourne on war service.
