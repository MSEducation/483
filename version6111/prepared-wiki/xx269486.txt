[[Narsai Toma]]

CATEGORIES: 1941 births, Living people, 21st-century bishops, Ancient Church of the East, People from Kirkuk, People from Dohuk

Mar Narsai Toma is the Metropolitan of the Ancient Church of the East of the diocese of Kirkuk, Iraq.[tpl]cite news |title=His Holiness Mar Dinkha IV Meets with Ancient Church of the East |url=http://news.assyrianchurch.com/his-holiness-mar-dinkha-iv-meets-with-ancient-church-of-the-east/1140 |publisher=Holy Apostolic Catholic Assyrian Church of the East |date=10 January 2011 |accessdate=25 September 2011 [/tpl]
Narsai Toma was born on 1 July 1941, in the province of Dohuk (Nohadra). He was ordained as a Metropolitan bishop 1968 in Baghdad and he currently resides over the Diocese of Kirkuk in Arrapha district.
