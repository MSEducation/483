[[Little Laver]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Essex, Epping Forest (district), Civil parishes in Essex

Little Laver is a village and a civil parish in the Epping Forest district, in the county of Essex, England. Little Laver has a church called St Mary the Virgin, Little Laver.http://www.achurchnearyou.com/little-laver-st-mary-the-virgin/ At the end of the 19th century it fell just below 100 households and has since remained about this level.http://www.british-history.ac.uk/report.aspx?compid=15580
