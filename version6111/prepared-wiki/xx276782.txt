[[Shelley Patterson]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, Minnesota Lynx coaches, American basketball coaches

Shelley Patterson is an American basketball coach, currently serving as an assistant coach with the Minnesota Lynx of the WNBA.
Patterson began her coaching career as a recruiting coordinator for Indiana State University. She later served as an assistant coach with the Philadelphia Rage of the American Basketball League.
Patterson served as Director of Basketball Operations for the Houston Comets during their 1999 championship season. She then served as an assistant coach for the Indiana Fever from 2000 to 2003, and the Phoenix Mercury in 2004.
In 2005, Patterson worked as head coach of the Chicago Blaze of the National Women's Basketball League. Prior to joining the Lynx in 2010, Patterson worked as an assistant coach with the Seattle Storm for three years.
