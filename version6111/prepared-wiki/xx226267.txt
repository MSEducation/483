[[Vilho Tilkanen]]

CATEGORIES: 1885 births, 1945 deaths, People from Pöytyä, Finnish male cyclists, Olympic cyclists of Finland, Cyclists at the 1912 Summer Olympics

Vilho Oskari Tilkanen (14 April 1885 – 1 August 1945) was a Finnish road racing cyclist who competed in the 1912 Summer Olympics.
He was born and died in Karinainen.
In 1912 he was a member of the Finnish cycling team which finished fifth in the team time trial event.
