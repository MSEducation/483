[[Balingian]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Sarawak

Balingian (also known as Baligian) is a town and province in Sarawak, Malaysia. It lies approximately [tpl]convert|286.1|km|mi|0[/tpl] east-north-east of the state capital Kuching. 
It is located about 60 km from Mukah town, and recent developments have started to transform the area: the coastal road has improved links to other towns; the Sarawak Corridor of Renewal Energy (SCORE); an aluminium factory about 25 km from Balingian. Offshore oil and gas is also a growth area for Balingian.
