[[Combin de Corbassière]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of the Alps, Alpine three-thousanders, Mountains of Valais

The Combin de Corbassière is a mountain in the Pennine Alps, located south of Fionnay in the canton of Valais. It is part of the Grand Combin massif and lies on the west side of the Corbassière Glacier.
