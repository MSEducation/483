[[Shannonside Northern Sound]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in the Republic of Ireland

Shannonside Northern Sound also known as "Shannonside FM" is a dual radio franchise which operates across counties Cavan, Monaghan (Northern Sound Radio), Leitrim, Longford and Roscommon (Shannonside FM) in Ireland. It has a 46.9% market share.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.northernsound.ie/userfiles/file/Newsletter%20Feb%202010.ppt|archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20120305103835/http://www.northernsound.ie/userfiles/file/Newsletter%20Feb%202010.ppt|archivedate=2012-03-05|title=Newsletter Feb 2010[/tpl]

==History==

Shannonside FM and Northern Sound Radio were established in 1989 as separate radio stations. In 1995 both licences and franchise areas were combined to create the first regional station in Ireland.
In 2005, Shannonside Northern Sound was purchased by Radio Kerry Holdings.http://www.shannonside.ie/userfiles/file/Newsletter_May09_SSNS.ppt[tpl]dead link|date=November 2013[/tpl]

==Shows==

The two stations have their own different schedules 7am-7pm weekdays, with the exception of 'The Joe Finnegan Show' (9am - 11am), at other times programmes are networked across both stations.

==Premises==

The franchise has 3 local bases:
Longford - Headquarters
Monaghan - studios, offices and broadcasting school, opened in October 2008
Cavan - studio and sales office, opened in July 2007
