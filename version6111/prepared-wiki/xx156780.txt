Pedro Alejandro García de la Cruz (born 14 March 1974 in Pisco) is a Peruvian footballer who currently plays for Alfonso Ugarte.

==Club career==

García previously played for Universidad San Martín and Universitario de Deportes.
In January 2012, García had his contract with Universitario de Deportes rescinded by the Peruvian Football Federation due to 7 months of unpaid wages.[tpl]cite news |title=FPF declaró libres a seis futbolistas impagos de la ‘U’|url=http://elcomercio.pe/deportes/1360151/noticia-fpf-declaro-libres-seis-futbolistas-impagos-u|work=elcomercio.pe|publisher=El Comercio|language=spanish|date=12 January 2012|accessdate=23 February 2012[/tpl] He joined León de Huánuco along with former Universitario teammate Johan Vásquez for the start of the 2012 Torneo Descentralizado season.[tpl]cite news |title=Dejan la ‘U’: Pedro García y Johan Vásquez ya son de León de Huánuco|url=http://elcomercio.pe/deportes/1350293/noticia-dejan-u-pedro-garcia-johan-vasquez-firmaron-leon-huanuco|publisher=El Comercio|language=spanish|date=19 December 2011|accessdate=23 February 2012[/tpl]
