[[Children's follow-up Clinic (Kindernachsorgeklinik) Berlin-Brandenburg]]

CATEGORIES: Hospitals in Germany, Bernau bei Berlin

The Children's Follow-up Clinic (Kindernachsorgeklinik) Berlin-Brandenburg gGmbH in Bernau bei Berlin is a non-profit family-oriented rehabilitation center for children with cancer and heart defects. This clinic was founded on 13 March 2009.
The Children's Follow-up Clinic (Kindernachsorgeklinik) follows a family-oriented rehabilitation (FOR) concept which has been modeled after the "Nachsorgeklinik Tannheim".Markus Baier. Attorney for the patient's family. In: SÃ¼dkurier 15 November 2010
The Children's Follow-up Clinic consists of four detached two-storey buildings, each with living units and a home therapy. In addition to medical care, Occupational Therapy, Physiotherapy and Psychotherapy, the clinic offers a daycare, a school, a swimming pool, a sports hall and a youth club.
The clinic has capacity for 28 patients and 72 family members.
The clinic is located in the Waldsiedlung, a district of Bernau near Berlin. The buildings belonged to the East German government.
