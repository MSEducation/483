[[Mesodesma]]

CATEGORIES: Mesodesmatidae

Mesodesma is a genus of saltwater clams, marine bivalve mollusks in the family Mesodesmatidae. This is the type genus of the family Mesodesmatidae.Mesodesma Deshayes, 1831, World Register of Marine Species, http://www.marinespecies.org/aphia.php?p=taxdetails&id=156804, accessed 8 August 2013
