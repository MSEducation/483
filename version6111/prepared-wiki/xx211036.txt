[[Eidsvik Skipsbyggeri]]

CATEGORIES: Shipbuilding companies of Norway, Fishing boat builders, Kvinnherad, Companies based in Hordaland

Eidsvik Skipsbyggeri is a small shipyard in Kvinnherad, Norway. The yard has mainly been building fishing vessels, but is now[tpl]when|date=December 2013[/tpl] preparing to build its first offshore vessel, a platform supply vessel.
