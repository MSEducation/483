[[WPMR-LP]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Alabama, Low-power FM radio stations in Alabama, Southern Gospel radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 2003, Franklin County, Alabama

WPMR-LP (95.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve Russellville, Alabama.  The station is owned by Provision Ministry. It airs a Southern Gospel music format.[tpl]cite web |work=Alabama Broadcast Media Page |title=Alabama LPFM & Translators |url=http://bellsouthpwp2.net/c/r/crackedlcd/almediapage/lpfm.html |accessdate=May 21, 2008[/tpl][tpl]cite web |title=Station Information Profile |publisher=Arbitron |url=http://www.arbitron.com/[/tpl]

==History==

This station received its original construction permit from the Federal Communications Commission on April 8, 2003.[tpl]cite web |publisher=FCC Media Bureau |title=Application Search Details (BNPL-20010614AJF) |url=http://licensing.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/app_det.pl?Application_id=569461 |date=April 8, 2003[/tpl]  The new station was assigned the call letters WPMR-LP by the FCC on June 18, 2003.[tpl]cite web |title=Call Sign History |url=http://licensing.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=134788&Callsign=WPMR-LP |publisher=FCC Media Bureau |work=CDBS Public Access Database[/tpl]  WPMR-LP received its license to cover from the FCC on June 18, 2004.[tpl]cite web |publisher=FCC Media Bureau |title=Application Search Details (BLL-20031020AAA) |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/app_det.pl?Application_id=671656 |date=June 18, 2004[/tpl]
Previously the WPMR callsign (for "Pocono Mountain Radio") had been assigned to WPMR (960 AM, now WPLY) and WPMR-FM (107.9 FM, now WKRF) in Mount Pocono/Tobyhanna Township, Pennsylvania.[tpl]cite web |title=REC Broadcast Query |url=http://www.recnet.com/cdbs/fmq.php?facid=134788 |work=REC Networks[/tpl]
