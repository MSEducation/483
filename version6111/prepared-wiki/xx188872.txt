[[Parco District]]

Parco District is one of thirty-four districts of the province Jauja in Peru.[tpl]es icon[/tpl] Instituto Nacional de Estadística e Informática. Banco de Información Distrital. Retrieved April 11, 2008.

==Location==

It is located at 3.435 meters, in an area covering 32.82 km2, exactly at kilometer 60 of the central road on the route from La Oroya to Huancayo in the department of Junin province of Cockaigne.
