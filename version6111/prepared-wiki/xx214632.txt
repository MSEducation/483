[[Compañía Española de Petróleos]]

CATEGORIES: Companies based in the Community of Madrid, Natural gas companies of Spain, Oil companies of Spain, Spanish brands

Compañía Española de Petróleos, S.A.U.  (CEPSA; Spanish Petroleum Company) is a Spanish multinational oil and gas company. It operates in several European countries as well as in Algeria, Canada, Morocco, Brazil, and Panama. The company currently produces around [tpl]convert|260000|oilbbl/d|m3/d[/tpl] and has a refining capacity in three refineries of 21 million tonnes/year.

==Recent news==

In 2011 CEPSA was bought by Abu Dhabi's International Petroleum Investment Company for 267 million euros.[tpl]cite web|last=Cespa|title=Cespa company information|url=http://www.cepsa.com/cepsa/Who_we_are/The_Company/Shareholding/[/tpl]
Since 2012, the company has appeared on Toro Rosso Formula One cars.
In November 2013, the firm agreed to buy Coastal Energy Co for around $2.21 billion.[tpl]cite news| url=http://www.reuters.com/article/2013/11/19/us-coastalenergy-offer-cepsa-idUSBRE9AI04O20131119| title=Spain's Cepsa to buy Coastal Energy in deal worth $2.2 billion | author=Sakthi Prasad | publisher=Reuters | date=19 November 2013[/tpl]
