[[Shiguai District]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Shiguai District or Xiguit District (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠰᠢᠭᠤᠶᠢᠲᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠲᠣᠭᠣᠷᠢᠭ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Siɣuyitu toɣoriɣ[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=石拐区 |t=石拐區 |p=Shíguǎi Qū[/tpl]) is an outlying district of Baotou, the largest city of Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China.
