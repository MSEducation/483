[[Soprano (rapper)]]

CATEGORIES: 1979 births, Living people, French rappers, French people of Comorian descent, French hip hop musicians, People from Marseille

Saïd M'Roubaba better known by his name Soprano ([tpl]IPA-fr|sɔpʁaˈno[/tpl]; born in Marseille on 14 January 1979) is a French rapper of Comorian descent.[tpl]cite web|url=[tpl]Allmusic|class=artist|id=soprano-p862366/biography|pure_url=yes[/tpl]|title=Biography: Soprano|last=Gutierrez|first=Evan C.|publisher=Allmusic|accessdate=16 November 2010[/tpl] He is a part of the rap group Psy 4 de la Rime.[tpl]cite web|url=[tpl]Allmusic|class=artist|id=psy-4-de-la-rime-p748789/biography|pure_url=yes[/tpl]|title=Biography: Psy 4 de la Rime|last=Birchmeier|first=Jason|publisher=Allmusic|accessdate=16 November 2010[/tpl] After leaving the group to make his first solo album he recorded his solo debut Puisqu'il Faut Vivre which made the Billboard European Top 100. 
He returned in 2010 with a new solo album called "La Colombe", which included collaborations with numerous artists such as Amadou & Mariam.
He has continued to work with Psy4 de la Rime: their third album, "Les Cités d'Or", came out in 2008, and their fourth album " 4eme Dimensiom" came out in April 2013.
