[[Jeonggwa]]

CATEGORIES: Hangwa

Jeonggwa, also spelled jeongwa is a variety of hangwa or Korean traditional confectionery made by boiling fruits, plant roots, seeds in honey, mulyeot (물엿, liquid candy) or sugar.
