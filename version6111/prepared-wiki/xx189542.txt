[[Now and Forever...Greatest Hits Live]]

CATEGORIES: Air Supply compilation albums, 1995 live albums, Warner Bros. Records live albums

Now and Forever...Greatest Hits Live is a live album by Australian soft rock band Air Supply released in 1995. The album was a massive success in Asia, where in China it topped the album charts for 16 weeks. It was recorded in Taipei, Taiwan, and later a DVD of the concert was released. The band played live with a 16 piece string section.Biographies: Air Supply 8-12-08 Single "The Way I Feel" became a hit in Asia, and a difficult jewel among the band's songs. The Album sold 8 millions copies.
