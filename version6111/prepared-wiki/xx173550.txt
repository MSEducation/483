[[Duchy of Legnica]]

CATEGORIES: 1675 disestablishments, States and territories established in 1248, Duchies of Silesia, Historical regions in Poland, Former duchies of the Kingdom of Bohemia

The Duchy of Legnica ([tpl]lang-pl|Księstwo Legnickie[/tpl], [tpl]lang-cs|Lehnické knížectví[/tpl])  or Duchy of Liegnitz ([tpl]lang-de|Herzogtum Liegnitz[/tpl]) was one of the Duchies of Silesia. Its capital was Legnica (Liegnitz) in Lower Silesia.
