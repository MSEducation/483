James Gordon "Klepto" Holmes was the sixth head college football coach
for the University of Texas at Arlington Mavericks located in Arlington, Texas
and he held that position for sixteen seasons, from 1935 until 1950.
His career coaching record at UT Arlington was 71 wins, 66 losses, and 5 ties.  This ranks him
