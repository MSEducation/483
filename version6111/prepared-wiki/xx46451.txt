[[Daverdisse]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Luxembourg (Belgium)

Daverdisse is a Walloon municipality of Belgium located in the province of Luxembourg. 
On 1 January 2007 the municipality, which covers 56.4 km², had 1,372 inhabitants, giving a population density of 24.3 inhabitants per km².
