Brad Takairangi is a  rugby league player for the Gold Coast Titans in the National Rugby League. He previously played for the South Sydney Rabbitohs in the Toyota Cup, and the Sydney Roosters in first grade. Takairangi is a utility player, having filled roles at five-eighth, lock, second row, and currently, centre.
Takairangi signed with the Titans on a three-year-deal on 5 February, as part of an attempt by the Sydney Roosters to lure Luke O'Donnell back to the National Rugby League in Bondi colours.
