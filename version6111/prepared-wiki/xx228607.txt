==Description==

The codex contains a small part of the Acts of the Apostles 13:39-46, on one parchment leaf (26 cm by 21 cm). The text is written in two columns per page, 18 lines per page, in large uncial letters. It is a palimpsest, the upper text was written in Georgian, in the 10th century.

==Text==

The Greek text of this codex is mixed, but predominate the Byzantine element. Aland placed it with some hesitation in Category III (Category V?).

==History==

Currently it is dated by the INTF to the 7th-century.[tpl]Cite web |url=http://intf.uni-muenster.de/vmr/NTVMR/ListeHandschriften.php?ObjID=20097 |title=Liste Handschriften |publisher=Institute for New Testament Textual Research |accessdate=21 April 2011 |location=Münster[/tpl]
The manuscript was examined by Constantin von Tischendorf, who published its text in facsimile edition.Constantin von Tischendorf, Monumenta sacra inedita I (Leipzig: 1855), pp. 39-40.  It was again examined by Kurt Treu.
The codex is located now at the Russian National Library (Gr. 18)Uncial 095 has catalogue number Gr. 17, and Uncial 096 has catalogue number Gr. 19 in the same library. in Saint Petersburg.
