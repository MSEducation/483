[[Jarqavieh Sofla District]]

CATEGORIES: Isfahan County, Districts of Isfahan Province

Jarqavieh Sofla District ([tpl]lang-fa|بخش جرقویه سفلی[/tpl]; Lower Jarqavieh District) is a district (bakhsh) in Isfahan County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 20,970, in 5,473 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|10[/tpl]  The District has three cities: Nikabad, Nasrabad, and Mohammadabad. The District has two rural districts (dehestan): Jarqavieh Sofla Rural District and Jarqavieh Vosta Rural District.
