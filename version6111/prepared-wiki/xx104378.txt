[[Shinanomachi Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Tokyo

[tpl]Nihongo|Shinanomachi Station|信濃町駅|Shinanomachi-eki[/tpl] is a railway station in Shinjuku, Tokyo, Japan, operated by the East Japan Railway Company (JR East).

==Lines==

The station is served by the Chūō-Sōbu Line.

==History==

The station opened on 9 October 1894.
