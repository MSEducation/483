[[Mel Whedbee]]

CATEGORIES: 1974 deaths, Kentucky State Thorobreds football coaches, 1904 births

Melville F. Whedbee was the 14th head football coach for the Kentucky State University Thorobreds located in Frankfort, Kentucky and he held that position for five seasons, from 1963 until 1967.  His career coaching record at Kentucky State was 24 wins, 18 losses, and 2 ties.  As of the conclusion of the 2007 season, this ranks him fifth at Kentucky State in total wins and third at Kentucky State in winning percentage (.568).Kentucky State University coaching records
