[[Suffocate (J. Holiday song)]]

CATEGORIES: 2007 singles, J. Holiday songs, Songs written by Tricky Stewart, Songs written by The-Dream, Song recordings produced by Tricky Stewart, Singles certified gold by the Recording Industry Association of America

"Suffocate" is a song by American R&B singer J. Holiday. It is the third single released from his debut album Back of My Lac'. The song was released as a digital download and sent to mainly urban radio stations. "Suffocate" debuted on 106 & Park Live: BET's Top 10 Video Countdown on November 23, 2007 at #10 on the countdown. "Suffocate" has since reached #2 and has been on the countdown for 30 days total.

==Music video==

The music video was directed by Jonathan Mannion and premiered in late 2007. 

==Charts==

"Suffocate" first appeared on the Billboard Hot 100 chart on the charting week of November 7, 2007, debuting at number 96. It has since ascended to number 18.  It reached #2 on the Billboard Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs.
