[[Am Byth]]

CATEGORIES: 2004 compilation albums, Ffa Coffi Pawb albums, Welsh-language albums

[tpl]Unreferenced|date=October 2007[/tpl]

Am Byth is a compilation album by Ffa Coffi Pawb. It was released in 2004 and includes songs recorded by the band between 1986 and 1992.
