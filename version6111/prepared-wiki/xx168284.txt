Aleksandr Aleksandrovich Stepanov ([tpl]lang-ru|Александр Александрович Степанов[/tpl]; born April 26, 1979 in Moscow, Russian SFSR, Soviet Union) is a Russian ice hockey forward who currently plays for Severstal Cherepovets of the Kontinental Hockey League.
