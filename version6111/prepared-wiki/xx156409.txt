[[John Peake]]

CATEGORIES: 1924 births, British field hockey players, Olympic field hockey players of Great Britain, Field hockey players at the 1948 Summer Olympics, Olympic silver medalists for Great Britain, Living people, Olympic medalists in field hockey

John Morris Peake (born 26 August 1924) was a British field hockey player who competed in the 1948 Summer Olympics.
He was a member of the British field hockey team, which won the silver medal. He played all five matches as forward.
