[[Philaeus]]

CATEGORIES: Salticidae

Philaeus is a spider genus of the Salticidae family (jumping spiders).
Philaeus maoniuensis was moved to genus Yllenus in 2003.Platnick 2007

==Distribution==

Philaeus has a wide distribution, with most species coming from the Mediterranean and West Africa. Single species are known from Guatemala and the Galapagos Islands.Murphy & Murphy 2000: 279
