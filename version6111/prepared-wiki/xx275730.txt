[[Investis]]

Investis is an international, digital corporate communications company, working with more than 1,600 public companies in over 30 countries.[tpl]cite web|title=Investis|url=http://www.greshampe.com/investis.htm|publisher=Gresham Private Equity|accessdate=7 March 2013[/tpl]
Services range from designing, building, hosting and managing corporate or investor websites and online annual reports, to Investor Relations app development,[tpl]cite news|title=Kingfisher takes social approach with new IR app|url=http://www.insideinvestorrelations.com/articles/social-media/18396/kingfisher-takes-social-approach-new-ir-app/|publisher=IR Magazine|accessdate=26 September 2011[/tpl] webcasting[tpl]cite web|title=Bloomberg Businessweek; Internet Software & Services|url=http://investing.businessweek.com/research/stocks/private/snapshot.asp?privcapId=184165|publisher=Bloomberg.com|accessdate=26 September 2011[/tpl] and social media solutions.[tpl]cite news|title=Corporate website benchmarking now with extra social media|url=http://www.communicatemagazine.co.uk/news/2271-corporate-website-benchmarking-now-with-extra-social-media|publisher=Communicate Magazine|accessdate=26 September 2011[/tpl]
The company has offices in London, New York, Würzburg, Milan, Helsinki and Vadodara. In October 2012 leading shareholders Dawn Capital and Azini Capital agreed the sale of Investis to Gresham Private Equity. This sale also involved the purchase of the Morningstar Investor Relations business, formerly Hemscott IR.[tpl]cite news|title=Investis bought by management in £30m-plus deal|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/media/2012/oct/01/investis-management-buyout|publisher=The Guardian|accessdate=20 November 2012|location=London|first=Mark|last=Sweney|date=1 October 2012[/tpl]
