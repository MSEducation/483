Bob Nanna (born June 14, 1975) is an American musician best known as the singer and guitarist for the bands Braid[tpl]cite book|last=Greenwald|first=Andy|title=Nothing Feels Good: Punk Rock, Teenagers, and Emo|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=3tJqJ9yDzCAC&pg=PA46|accessdate=3 August 2012|date=2003-11-15|publisher=Macmillan|isbn=9780312308636|pages=46â€“[/tpl] and Hey Mercedes. 
In addition, Nanna also currently performs as part of the bands Lifted Bells and Jack & Ace, as well as a solo project The City on Film.

==Biography==

Born Robert Thomas Nanna on June 14, 1975 in Chicago, Illinois, he attended the University of Illinois in Champaign/Urbana, graduating with a degree in Communications / Advertising. 
Since 2005, Nanna has served as a marketing manager for Chicago-based t-shirt company Threadless
In 2013 he co-founded Downwrite, a songwriting website, with Mark Rose of the band Spitalfield.
