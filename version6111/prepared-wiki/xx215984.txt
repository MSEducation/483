[[Atlantic Air Lift]]

CATEGORIES: Airlines of France

Atlantic Air Lift is a French airline. It provides the only scheduled service to the Île d'Yeu Aerodrome, on the Île d'Yeu off the west coast of France. The service links the Île d'Yeu with Nantes Atlantique Airport.
The airline made its first flight in April 2005.
