[[Nanhai Chao]]

CATEGORIES: Chinese folk songs

"Nanhai Chao" ([tpl]Zh|first=t |t=南海潮 |s=南海潮 |p=Nan2 Hai3 Chao2| cy=Naam4 Hoi2 Chiu4[/tpl]), or "Southern Sea Tides", is a Cantonese song of sung by Overseas Chinese. Its melody is based on the folk songs of the boat people in the Pearl River Delta and its adjacent coasts.
