[[Tongan general election, 1993]]

CATEGORIES: 1993 elections in Oceania, 1993 in Tonga, Elections in Tonga

General elections were held in Tonga on 3 and 4 February 1993 to elect members of the Legislative Assembly of Tonga.  Nine nobles and nine people's representatives were elected.  Six of the latter favoured democratic reform.[tpl]cite book |last=Campbell |first=Ian C.  |title=Elections in Asia and the Pacific: A Data Handbook.  Volume II: South East Asia, East Asia, and the South Pacific |editor1-last=Nohlen |editor1-first=Dieter |editor2-last=Grotz |editor2-first=Florian |editor3-last=Hartmann |editor3-first=Christof |publisher=Oxford University Press |year=2001 |chapter=Tonga |page=820 |ISBN=0-19-924959-8[/tpl] Voter turnout was 59.3%.
