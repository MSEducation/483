[[Yaycı]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Julfa District

Yaycı (also, Yaydzhi, Yaydzhy, and [tpl]lang-fa|یاجی آذربایجان[/tpl] – Yājī-ye Āz̄arbāyjān) is a village and the most populous municipality, except for the capital Julfa, in the Julfa Rayon of Nakhchivan, Azerbaijan.  It has a population of 5,857.
