[[Congolese Amateur Swimming Federation]]

CATEGORIES: National members of the African Swimming Confederation, Sports governing bodies in  the Republic of the Congo, Swimming in  the Republic of the Congo, 1970 establishments in the Republic of the Congo, Organizations established in 1970

The Congolese Amateur Swimming Federation ([tpl]lang-fr|Fédération Congolaise de Natation Amateur[/tpl]), is the national governing body for the sport of swimming in the Republic of the Congo.[tpl]cite web|title=Fédération Congolaise de Natation Amateur|url=http://www.fina.org/H2O/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=447:cgo-republic-of-congo&catid=91:africa&Itemid=638|publisher=FINA|accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl]
