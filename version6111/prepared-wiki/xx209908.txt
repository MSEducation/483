[[Edward Długajczyk]]

CATEGORIES: Jagiellonian University alumni, University of Silesia alumni, Polish historians, 1939 births, Living people

Dr Hab. Edward Stanisław Długajczyk (born 1939) is a Polish historian. He is specializing in history of Upper Silesia in 20th century.
He finished Polish philology at Jagiellonian University. In 1972 he gained a Ph.D. from University of Silesia. In 1982 Długajczyk passed his habilitation.
