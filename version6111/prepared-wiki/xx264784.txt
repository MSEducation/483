[[Tim Shieff]]

CATEGORIES: 1988 births, Living people, Traceurs, Parkour, Sportspeople from Hartford, Connecticut, Freerunners

Tim "Livewire" Shieff (born March 24, 1988 in Hartford, Connecticut) is a professional English Freerunner. He is best known for winning the 2009 Barclaycard World Freerun Championship and participating on the television programme MTV's Ultimate Parkour Challenge.[tpl]cite web|title=Barclaycard World Freerun Championship|url=http://www.worldfreerun.com/[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Tim Shieff - MTV's Ultimate Parkour Challenge|url=http://www.mtv.com/shows/parkour/cast_member.jhtml?personalityId=14085[/tpl] Shieff uses the nickname Livewire while performing freerunning. In 2011 and 2012 he appeared in the televised American Ninja Warrior competition. In terms of Freerunning competitions won, Tim Shieff is currently one of the most successful athletes in the world, achieving victories and runner up's in events such as The Barclaycard World Freerun Championships, and the Red Bull Art Of Motion.  He was a judge in the 2013 Art of Motion in Santorini for the criteria of creativity.[tpl]cite web |title=Red Bull TV - Red Bull Art of Motion 2013 |url=http://live.redbull.tv/events/254/red-bull-art-of-motion-2013/ |publisher=Red Bull |date=2013-09-14 |accessdate=2013-09-15[/tpl]

==Personal life==

Shieff was born in Connecticut and moved to the UK when he was three.[tpl]cite web|title=Tim Shieff|url=http://www.wfpf.com/athletes/tim-shieff[/tpl] As a child, Shieff took breakdance lessons which strongly influenced his style of freerunning. Besides, he is famous for his upper body strength and—therefore—for his (one-armed) handstands and "human flags".
Livewire arouse public interest through performing at Red Bull Art of Motion shows and his videos on YouTube which have gained multiple million views.[tpl]cite web|title=YouTube - 99timshi's Channel|url=https://www.youtube.com/user/99timshi[/tpl] In 2008, he ranked 2nd at the World Freerunning Championship, before he achieved first place the following year.
Prior to the fifth season of American Ninja Warrior, Shieff became a vegan.

==Appearances==

Since becoming famous, Shieff appeared on various late night shows in the United States, such as Late Night with Jimmy Fallon.[tpl]cite web|title=Parkour Athletes - Late Night with Jimmy Fallon|url=http://www.latenightwithjimmyfallon.com/blogs/2009/09/parkour-athletes-flashback-guy-comedian-wayne-federman/[/tpl] He also took part in the filming of the last Harry Potter film entitled Harry Potter and the Deathly Hollows, acting as a Death Eater.[tpl]cite web|title=Freerunner Tim takes to silver screen in new Harry Potter film|url=http://www.thisisderbyshire.co.uk/news/Freerunner-Tim-takes-silver-screen-new-Harry-Potter-film/article-1496910-detail/article.html|work=This is Derbyshire[/tpl]
Tim Shieff appeared on Top Gear, Series 20 Episode 2, racing a motorcycle through the offices of the BBC in London.

==Career==

Tim Shieff is a member of the World Freerunning & Parkour Federation and of the British freerunning project Storm Freerun.[tpl]cite web|title=:: Welcome to World Freerunning & Parkour Federation.com ::|url=http://www.wfpf.com/profiles/view/TimLivewire[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Storm Freerun - Purple|url=http://www.stormfreerun.com/#/purple[/tpl]
Livewire's career with Storm Freerun is shared with other freerunner's such as Paul "Blue" Joseph, Marc Diwan, Kie Willis, Ashley "Spyder" Holland, Phil Doyle, and Jan Barcikowski. Their most viewed video on YouTube—Storm Freerun - Volume 1-- has reached over 4 million views.
Livewire has also recorded a few Drum and Bass tracks in cooperation with a friend.[tpl]cite web|title=Livewires Blog of Everything!|url=http://livewireexplainsitall.tumblr.com/post/454655587/livewire-presents-the-best-of-liquid-drum-n-bass[/tpl]
In April 2013 Shieff appeared in the music video for Disclosure's hit single "You & Me". In the video he is seen traveling across Europe (with Lauren Johns - who also appeared on Delilah's music video "Breathe")
