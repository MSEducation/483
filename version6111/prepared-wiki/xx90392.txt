[[Couto (Santa Cristina)]]

CATEGORIES: Parishes of Santo Tirso Municipality

Couto Santa Cristina is an urban parish located south in the city of Santo Tirso, Portugal. As of the 2001 census, its population was 3,982, making it Santo Tirso's fifth-largest parish in terms of population. It covers 7.8 km² of area in the city, making it Santo Tirso's fourth-largest parish in terms of area.
The area is also home to several industrial commercial facilities.
Amigisc is located there.
