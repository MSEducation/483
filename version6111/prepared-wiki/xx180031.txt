[[Huancané]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Puno Region

Huancané ([tpl]lang-ay|Wankani[/tpl] [tpl]audio|ay_wankani_001.ogg|listen[/tpl]) is the capital of the province of Huancané in Peru. The town is located about north of Lake Titicaca.  The majority of Huancané's 7,000 residents speak the Aymara language.
