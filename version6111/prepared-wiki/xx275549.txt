Rajani Kanta Barman (born 2 February 1979) is a Bangladeshi international footballer who plays as a defender for  Muktijoddha Sangsad KS.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.unbconnect.com/component/news/task-show/id-54719 |title=Abahani meets Muktijoddha in 2nd semis of Super Cup Wednesday |publisher=UNBconnect... |date=2011-08-02 |accessdate=2011-10-17[/tpl] He is former Bangladesh national football team player.[tpl]NFT player|pid=611|Rajani Kanta Barman|accessdate=2011-10-17[/tpl] He plays as a centre back.

==Club career==

Rajani started his football career in Muktijoddha Sangsad KS. He played for Muktijoddha for 12 years before moving to Mohammedan SC.

==International career==

He debuted for national team in 1999 and retired in 2009. He was the captain of 2003 SAFF Championship Bangladesh team. His number in national team was usually number 4.
