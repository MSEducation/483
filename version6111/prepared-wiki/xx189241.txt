[[Orthilia]]

CATEGORIES: Ericaceae, Eudicot genera, Monotypic plant genera, Flora of New Jersey, Flora of Delaware, Flora of North America

Orthilia secunda is a flowering plant of the genus Orthilia in the family Ericaceae. It is the only member of the genus.[tpl]cite web | title = Orthilia secunda L. | work = PLANTS Profile | publisher = United States Department of Agriculture; Natural Resources Conservation Service | url = http://plants.usda.gov/java/profile?symbol=ORSE | accessdate = 2009-07-17 [/tpl] 
[tpl]cite web | title = Orthilia secunda L. | work = GRIN Taxonomy for Plants | publisher = Germplasm Resources Information Network | url = http://www.ars-grin.gov/cgi-bin/npgs/html/taxon.pl?430270 | accessdate = 2009-07-17 [/tpl] 
 Its common name is sidebells wintergreen and it was previously part of genus Pyrola, the wintergreens.
The plant has a circumboreal distribution, growing throughout much of the northern Northern Hemisphere.
