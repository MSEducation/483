[[7831 François-Xavier]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Asteroids named for people, Astronomical objects discovered in 1993

7831 François-Xavier is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1356.5172685 days (3.71 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=7831 |  title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-04-28 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on March 21, 1993 by Eleanor F. Helin at the Palomar Observatory.
