[[Chinese language]]

CATEGORIES: Chinese language, Isolating languages, Sinology, Language versus dialect

Chinese ([tpl]lang|zh|汉语[/tpl] or [tpl]lang|zh|漢語[/tpl], Hànyǔ) is a group of related language varieties, several of which are not mutually intelligible, and is variously described as a language or language family.
