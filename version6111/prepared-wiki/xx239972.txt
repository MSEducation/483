[[Zgornja Ščavnica]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Municipality of Sveta Ana

Zgornja Ščavnica ([tpl]IPA-sl|ˈzɡoːɾnja ˈʃtʃaːu̯nitsa|pron[/tpl]) is a settlement in the Municipality of Sveta Ana in the Slovene Hills in northeastern Slovenia. It lies in the upper Ščavnica Valley.Sveta Ana Municipality site
