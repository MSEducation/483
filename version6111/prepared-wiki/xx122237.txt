[[Mūša]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of Lithuania, Rivers of Latvia, Lielupe river basin, International rivers of Europe, Latvia–Lithuania border

Mūša ([tpl]lang-lv|Mūsa[/tpl]) is a river in Northern Lithuania and Southern Latvia (Zemgale region), having its confluence with river Nemunėlis ([tpl]lang-lv|Mēmele[/tpl]), in Latvia, near city Bauska. Mūša is a tributary of the river Lielupė. Mūša is 164 kilometres (146 km in Lithuania, 18 km in Latvia) long.
