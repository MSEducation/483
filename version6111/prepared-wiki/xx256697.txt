[[Christ the King High School (Antigua)]]

CATEGORIES: Educational institutions with year of establishment missing, Girls' schools in Antigua and Barbuda, Roman Catholic secondary schools in Antigua and Barbuda, St. John's, Antigua and Barbuda

Christ the King High School is a secondary school located on Old Parham Road in St. John's, Antigua and Barbuda. It is the only all-girls Catholic high school located on the island of Antigua. Its brother school is St. Joseph's Academy. If the child is not of age to go to the School there is a school for children named St.John's Catholic and a pre-school named Early Childhood Pre-school.
