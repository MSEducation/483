Ashley James Grimes (born 9 December 1986) is an English footballer who plays for Bury. He normally plays as a striker or attacking midfielder.

==Career==

===Manchester City===

Born in Swinton, Greater Manchester, Grimes began his footballing career with Barr Hill Lads' Club in Salford before signing for Manchester United where he spent two years in the academy before signing for Bolton Wanderers. He was then released by Bolton at the age of 16 and went on trial at Manchester City. ===Rochdale===

Ashley Grimes signed a two-year contract with League One side Rochdale on a free transfer after his contract had expired at Millwall. He expressed excitement at working with Steve Eyre; he said "I have agreed to sign and I'm very pleased to be linking up with Steve Eyre once again".[tpl]cite news |title=Grimes plumps for Rochdale |url=http://www.skysports.com/story/0,19528,12875_6998908,00.html |work=Sky Sports |publisher=BSkyB |date=21 June 2011 |accessdate=2 February 2014 [/tpl] On 9 August 2011, Grimes scored his first goals for Rochdale when he bagged a brace in Rochdale's 3–2 win after extra time against Chesterfield in the League Cup.
