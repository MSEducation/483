[[Gomesh Tappeh, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Gomesh Tappeh ([tpl]lang-fa|گمش تپه[/tpl]; also known as Gol Tappeh, Gomīsh Tappeh, and Qūsh Tepe)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3064769|Gomesh Tappeh[/tpl] is a village in Karasf Rural District, in the Central District of Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 329, in 66 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
