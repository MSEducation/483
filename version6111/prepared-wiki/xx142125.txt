[[Mats Gustafsson (footballer)]]

CATEGORIES: 1975 births, Living people, People from Sund, Åland, Finnish footballers, IFK Mariehamn players

Mats Gustafsson (born 12 December 1975 in Sund, Åland) is a former Finnish footballer. During his career he represented IFK Mariehamn, FC Inter Turku and VG-62.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Guardian.co.uk|title=Stats Centre: Mats Gustafsson Facts|url=http://guardian.touch-line.com/StatsCentre.asp?CTID=57&CPID=120&TEID=1403&PLID=43495&pStr=Player|accessdate=2010-07-16[/tpl]
2007 was his last season of professional football as he wanted to spend more time at home with his family.
