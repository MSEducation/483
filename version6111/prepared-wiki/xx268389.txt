[[Madara Mālmane]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 births, Living people, Latvian female models, People from Riga

Madara Mālmane (born 1989) is Latvian model.
She has been on the cover of Italian magazine Grazia in 2003 and 2005. Mālmane has photos taken for magazines L'Officiel, Vogue, Elle, Marie Claire, Numéro, W, Vanity Fair, and Jalouse.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.apollo.lv/portal/fun/articles/120170|title=Latvijas topmodele Madara Mālmane kļuvusi par «Dior» reklāmas seju|date=11 February 2008|publisher=Apollo|language=Latvian|accessdate=23 September 2011[/tpl] She graduated Rīgas Hanzas vidusskola.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.vipi.lv/1/4/?id_news=31646|title="Christian Dior" reklāmas seja Madara Mālmane absolvējusi 12. klasi|date=3 July 2009|publisher=Vipi|language=Latvian|accessdate=23 September 2011[/tpl]
