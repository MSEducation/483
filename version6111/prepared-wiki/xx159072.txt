[[Citheronia azteca]]

CATEGORIES: Ceratocampinae, Butterflies and moths of North America, Animals described in 1896

Citheronia azteca is a moth of the family Saturniidae that lives in Guatemala, Belize and Mexico.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.mbarnes.force9.co.uk/belizemoths/images3/citazt.htm |title=Citheronia azteca |work=Moths of Belize |author=Matthew J. C. Barnes |date=April 27, 2002 |accessdate=April 7, 2010[/tpl]

==Description==

The species larvae is white with black spots, while the male moth is orange coloured with white spots and gray lines.Description
