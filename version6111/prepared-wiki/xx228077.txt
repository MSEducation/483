[[Hongort]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Inner Mongolia

Hongort ([tpl]lang-mn|comitative case of "soft" or "darling"[/tpl], [tpl]zh|c=红格尔图|p=Hongge'ertu|w=Hung-ko-erh-t'u[/tpl]) is a town (镇) of the Chahar Right Back Banner, which in turn is part of Ulanqab prefecture-level city in Inner Mongolia, China. It is located about 30 km northwest of Shangdu county.  In 2000, when it was still classified as township (乡), it had 11860 inhabitants.www.xzqh.org: 察哈尔右翼后旗—内蒙古自治区—中国—行政区划网 (in Chinese)
In 1936, Hongort was the site of a battle between Chinese and pro-Japanese troops in the Suiyuan Campaign.[ref]*中国抗日战争正面战场作战记 (China's Anti-Japanese War Combat Operations)
