[[Wrestling at the 1992 Summer Olympics – Men's freestyle 82 kg]]

CATEGORIES: Wrestling at the 1992 Summer Olympics

The Men's Freestyle 82 kg at the 1992 Summer Olympics as part of the wrestling program were held at the Instituto Nacional de Educación Física de Cataluña from August 5 to August 7.
The wrestlers are divided into 2 groups. The winner of each group decided by a double-elimination system.
