[[Miloš Marković (footballer born 1992)]]

CATEGORIES: 1992 births, Living people, People from Cetinje, Győri ETO FC players, KF Skënderbeu Korçë players, Montenegrin footballers, Association football midfielders

Miloš Marković (Cyrillic: Милош Марковић, born 28 April 1992) is a Montenegrin football midfielder playing for FK Leotar on loan from FK Rudar Pljevlja.
