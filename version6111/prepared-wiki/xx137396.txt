[[Esmail (Afghan leader)]]

CATEGORIES: Afghan people, Living people, Year of birth missing (living people)

Guantanamo detainee Anwar Khan identified an individual named Esmail as an anti-Taliban militia leader, prior to the ouster of the Taliban.[ref]
detainees ARB|ARB_Transcript_Set_9_21017-21351..pdf}} Summarized transcript (.pdf), from Anwar Khan's Administrative Review Board hearing - page 311[/ref]
According to Khan he and Esmail served under the command of Hazrat Ali, one of the militia commanders who nominated Hamid Karzai for the presidency of the interim Afghan administration.
