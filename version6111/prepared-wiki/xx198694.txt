[[Norman Passmore]]

CATEGORIES: Kentucky State Thorobreds football coaches, 1915 births, 2003 deaths

Norman L. Passmore, Sr. was the seventh head college football coach for the Kentucky State University Thorobreds located in Frankfort, Kentucky and he held that position for the 1944 season.  His career coaching record at Kentucky State was 1 win, 1 loss, and 0 ties.  As of the conclusion of the 2007 season, this ranks him 23rd at Kentucky State in total wins and eighth at Kentucky State in winning percentage (.500).Kentucky State University coaching records
Kentucky State did not field a team in 1943 and only had two games on the schedule in 1944.Kentucky State yearly results He also coached the Lexington Dunbar Bearcats from 1951 to around 1967.
