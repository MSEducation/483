[[Banksia Swamp]]

CATEGORIES: Geography of Victoria (Australia)

Banksia Swamp is a swamp in Victoria, Australia. It is located at [tpl]coord|37|57|S|147|42|E|display=inline,title[/tpl], within the Gippsland Lakes complex.
The name refers to the plant genus Banksia, which grows in the area.
