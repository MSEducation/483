[[Pré-Saint-Didier]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Aosta Valley, Spa towns in Italy

 
Pré-Saint-Didier is a town and comune in the Aosta Valley region of north-western Italy, at 1,004 m above sea level.

==Transportation==

A terminus of a regional railway is located there; direct trains connect the town with Aosta, which is connected to the rest of the Italian network. Regular bus connection to places located further up the mountains (La Thuile, Courmayeur) is available.
