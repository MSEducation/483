[[Kate Boutilier]]

CATEGORIES: American screenwriters, American television producers, American television writers, Women screenwriters, Women television writers, Living people, Place of birth missing (living people), Year of birth missing (living people)

Kate Boutilier is an American screenwriter who is best known for writing Klasky Csupo shows like Rugrats, The Wild Thornberrys and All Grown Up!. She now writes shows aimed for younger viewers like Olivia and The Mr. Men Show.  She is currently working on a show for Nickelodeon UK called Poppy Cat.
