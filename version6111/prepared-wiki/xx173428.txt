[[Mahendranagar Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in Nepal, Mahakali Zone

Mahendranagar Airport [tpl]airport codes|XMG|VNMN[/tpl] is an airport serving Mahendranagar, a city in the Kanchanpur district of the Mahakali zone in western Nepal.

==Facilities==

The airport resides at an elevation of [tpl]convert|650|ft|0[/tpl] above mean sea level. It has one runway which is [tpl]convert|884|m|0[/tpl] in length.
