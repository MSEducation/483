Aleksandr Aleksandrovich Butov ([tpl]lang-ru|Александр Александрович Бутов[/tpl]; born 15 August 1995) is a Russian football defender, who currently playing for FC Energiya Volzhsky.
He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Energiya Volzhsky on 17 July 2013 in a game against FC Chernomorets Novorossiysk.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Russian Football Union|url=http://rfspro.ru/cgi-bin/player/script2.cgi?par1=119591|title=Career Summary|date=14 August 2013[/tpl]
