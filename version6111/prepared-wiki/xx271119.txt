[[(18412) 1993 LX]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1993, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(18412) 1993 LX is a main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on June 13, 1993.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
In 2012, Asteroid 18412 was named in honour of Dr Karl Kruszelnicki, an Australian Science Broadcaster and author.
