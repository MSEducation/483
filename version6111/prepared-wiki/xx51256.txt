[[Canigao Channel]]

CATEGORIES: Straits of the Philippines, Bohol Sea, Central Visayas, Geography of the Visayas

Canigao Channel is a strait in the Central Visayas Region of the Visayas islands group, in the central Philippines.
The strait connects the Bohol Sea with the Camotes Sea. 
The Canigao Channel lies between Bohol Island and Leyte Island.
