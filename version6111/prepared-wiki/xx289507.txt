[[Panbeh Zaban]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Panbeh Zaban ([tpl]lang-fa|پنبه زبان[/tpl], also Romanized as Panbeh Zabān; also known as Panbeh Zanān)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3773305|Panbeh Zaban[/tpl] is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 469, in 103 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
