[[East Branch Baker River]]

CATEGORIES: Tributaries of the Merrimack River, Rivers of New Hampshire

The East Branch of the Baker River is a [tpl]convert|3.1|mi|km|adj=mid|-long[/tpl]New Hampshire GRANIT state geographic information system river located in western New Hampshire in the United States.  It is a tributary of the Baker River, part of the Pemigewasset River and Merrimack River watersheds.
The river rises on the southernmost slopes of Mount Moosilauke in the town of Woodstock.  Flowing south, it quickly enters the town of Warren and crosses twice under Route 118 before joining the main stem of the Baker.
