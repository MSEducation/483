[[Lincolnshire Cricket Board Premier League]]

CATEGORIES: English domestic cricket competitions, Cricket in Lincolnshire, ECB Premier Leagues

The Lincolnshire Cricket Board Premier League is the top level of competition for recreational club cricket in Lincolnshire, England, and since 2000 has been a designated ECB Premier League.List of ECB Premier Leagues
The competing teams in 2014 are: Billingborough, Bourne, Bracebridge Heath, Grimsby Town, Lindum, Louth, Market Deeping, Market Rasen, Skegness, Sleaford, Spalding, and Woodhall Spa.
