[[Odeonsplatz (Munich U-Bahn)]]

CATEGORIES: Munich U-Bahn stations, Railway stations opened in 1971, 1971 establishments in West Germany

Odeonsplatz is an important U-Bahn interchange station on the northern edge of Munich's Old Town. It is serviced by the [tpl]ÖPNV München|U4[/tpl], [tpl]ÖPNV München|U5[/tpl] and [tpl]ÖPNV München|U3[/tpl], [tpl]ÖPNV München|U6[/tpl] lines of the Munich U-Bahn system, with U 4 and U 5 running in an east-west direction and U3, U6 running perpendicular in a north-south direction. It is one of the Old Town's principal U-Bahn interchanges, the others being Sendlinger Tor on the southern periphery, Karlsplatz in the west and Marienplatz in the city centre. 
The station is also serviced by the Museumsline 100, a bus line that calls at several important museums throughout town. Moreover, the night service N40 calls at Odeonsplatz.
