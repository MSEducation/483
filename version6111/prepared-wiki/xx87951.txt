[[Macleod railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Premium Melbourne railway stations, Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1911

Macleod railway station is located on the Hurstbridge line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the north-eastern Melbourne suburb of Macleod opening on 1 March 1911.Macleod Vicsig It was upgraded to a Premium station in 2001.
Macleod was the junction of the short Mont Park branch line from 1911 until 1964. Platform 3 was built on the alignment of the Mont Park line in the late 1970s along with three sidings to the south of the station. A few services terminate at Macleod before proceeding to the sidings to stable.

==Platforms & services==
