[[Amedei]]

CATEGORIES: Companies established in 1990, 1990 establishments in Italy, Italian brands, Chocolate companies, Food companies of Italy, Companies based in Tuscany

Amedei is an artisan chocolate manufacturing company located in Pontedera in Tuscany region of Italy. Their products range from chocolate bars to truffles and pralines.  Their award winning chocolate bars use single origin and single varietal cocoa beans.
