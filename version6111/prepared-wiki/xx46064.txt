[[Somme-Leuze]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Belgium, Municipalities of Namur (province)

Somme-Leuze ([tpl]IPA-fr|sɔm.løz[/tpl] ; [tpl]lang-wa|Some-Leuze[/tpl]) is a Walloon municipality and village in the Belgian province of Namur.
On 1 January 2006 the municipality had 4,656 inhabitants. The total area is 95.09 km², giving a population density of 49 inhabitants per km².
