[[Shweta Munshi]]

CATEGORIES: Indian television actresses

Shweta Munshi is an Indian television actress, played the leading role of Avani in Maayke Se Bandhi Dor on Star Plus TV channel. Previously, she played the role of Arpita in the most famous Zee TV show, Punar Vivah.
