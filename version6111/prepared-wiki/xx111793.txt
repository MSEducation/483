[[Mahershala Ali]]

CATEGORIES: 1974 births, 21st-century American male actors, African-American male actors, African-American Muslims, American Ahmadis, American male film actors, American male television actors, Converts to Islam from Christianity, Living people, Male actors from Oakland, California, New York University alumni, Saint Mary's College of California alumni

Mahershala Ali (born Mahershalalhashbaz Gilmore; February 16, 1974) is an American actor.
His is particularly notable for sharing a name with the Biblical king Maher-shalal-hash-baz.

==Early life==

Ali was born in Oakland, California.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.alislam.org/library/books/Short-Stories-American-Converts-to-Islam.pdf |title=www.alislam.org |format=PDF |date= |accessdate=2010-08-04[/tpl] He is named after Maher-shalal-hash-baz, one of the sons of Isaiah in the Bible. Raised a Christian by his mother, he later converted to Islam and joined the Ahmadiyya Muslim Community

==Career==

Ali is perhaps best known for his portrayal of Richard Tyler on the USA sci-fi series The 4400 and Tizzy in the 2008 film The Curious Case of Benjamin Button. He currently portrays Remy Danton in the Netflix series House of Cards.
