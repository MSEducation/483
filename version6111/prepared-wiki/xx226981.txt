[[Super Chinese Land 1-2-3]]

[tpl]nihongo|Super Chinese Land 1-2-3|スーパーチャイニーズランド１・２・３[/tpl] is a compilation of Super Chinese Land, Super Chinese Land 2, and Super Chinese Land 3 released in 1996 by Culture Brain. Super Chinese Land 1-2-3 was not released in North America, but the first two games on it were, released as Ninja Boy and Ninja Boy II, respectively. The games included are direct ports, as opposed to remakes.
