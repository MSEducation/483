[[Chong'an District]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Jiangsu

Chong'an District (Chinese: 崇安区; Pinyin: Chóng'ān Qū) is a district in Wuxi, Jiangsu Province of China.
It is located in the center of Wuxi City. It has a land area of 17.82 square kilometers, and a population of 186,800 in the end of 2007.
The district government is located on 288 East Xianqian St.
