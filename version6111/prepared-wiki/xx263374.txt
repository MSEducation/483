[[Bell Place-Locust Avenue Historic District]]

CATEGORIES: Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Historic districts in New York, Italianate architecture in New York, Second Empire architecture in New York, Victorian architecture in New York, Buildings and structures in Yonkers, New York, Houses in Westchester County, New York

Bell Place-Locust Avenue Historic District is a national historic district located at Yonkers, Westchester County, New York. It includes 11 contributing buildings.  They are residential structures and outbuildings representative of the High Victorian, Italian Villa, and Second Empire styles.  They were built between 1855 and 1887 and consists of medium sized, single family residences, between two and three stories in height.  Some have extant carriage houses on their properties.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=10776|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration:Bell Place-Locust Avenue Historic District|date=June 1985|accessdate=2011-01-01 |author=Neil G. Larson|publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation[/tpl] See also: [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=10752|title=Accompanying 14 photos[/tpl]
It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1985.
