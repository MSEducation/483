[[Collingwood railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1901

Collingwood railway station is located on the South Morang and Hurstbridge lines in Victoria, Australia. It serves the north-eastern Melbourne suburb of Collingwood opening as Collingwood Town Hall on 21 October 1901, being renamed Collingwood on 1 May 1909.Collingwood Vicsig The station buildings and platforms were rebuilt in 1985/86.[tpl]cite journal|date=September 1986|title=Works|journal=Newsrail|publisher=Australian Railway Historical Society|page=page 283[/tpl]
