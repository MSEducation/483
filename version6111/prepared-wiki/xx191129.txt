[[Polytech'Savoie]]

CATEGORIES: Engineering universities and colleges in France, Annecy, Chambéry, Educational institutions established in 2006, Buildings and structures in Haute-Savoie, Education in Rhône-Alpes

The École Polytechnique d'Ingénieurs de l'Université de Savoie was created in 2006 from the fusion of the École Supérieure d'Ingénieurs - ESIA and the École Supérieure d'Ingénieurs de Chambéry - ESIGEC.
