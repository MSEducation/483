[[J. T. Williams]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing, Year of death missing, Kentucky State Thorobreds football coaches

J. T. Williams was the fifth head football coach for the Kentucky State University Thorobreds located in Frankfort, Kentucky and he held that position for three seasons, from 1928 until 1930.  His career coaching record at Kentucky State was 9 wins, 9 losses, and 3 ties.
