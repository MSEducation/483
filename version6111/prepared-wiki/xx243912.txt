[[Lillian Hansen]]

CATEGORIES: 1957 births, Living people, Members of the Parliament of Norway, Labour Party (Norway) politicians, Mayors of places in Nordland, Women mayors of places in Norway

 Lillian Hansen  (born 1957) is a Norwegian politician who represents the Arbeiderpartiet. She is from Moskenes in Nordland. At the Norwegian parliamentary election, 2009, Hansen was the party's fourth candidate in Nordland.
She was formerly deputy mayor, and mayor of Moskenes municipality 2003-2007. In her working life, Lillian Hansen was a bus driver.
