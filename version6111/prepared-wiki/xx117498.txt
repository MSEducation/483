[[Xenocytaea maddisoni]]

CATEGORIES: Salticidae

Xenocytaea maddisoni is a jumping spider.

==Name==

It is named after Wayne Maddison, a Salticid specialist of the University of British Columbia.

==Appearance==

X. maddisoni is about 3.5mm long, with females about 4mm.

==Distribution==

X. maddisoni is only known from Viti Levu, Fiji.
