Lucy Rahman is a Bangladeshi-born British singer.

==Early life==

Rahman was born in Dhaka, Bangladesh.[tpl]cite web |url= http://www.culturalco-operation.org/artist_network/artist/216/Lucy_Rahman|title=Lucy Rahman|publisher=Cultural Co-operation|date= |accessdate=24 September 2011[/tpl] into a family of famous musicians, artists and poets. From the age of six she was trained in Indian Classical and semi-classical music from her father, Lutfor Rahman,[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.bbwhoswho.co.uk/whoswho_09.pdf|title=Lucy Rahman.|work=British Bangladeshi Who's Who 2009|publisher=British Bangla Media Group|date=October 2009|page=104|accessdate=24 September 2011[/tpl] who was a classical singer and a successful composer. She then studied at the Nazrul Academy where she studied for six years and gained her diploma.
