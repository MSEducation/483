[[2000–01 Premier League of Bosnia and Herzegovina]]

CATEGORIES: Premier League of Bosnia and Herzegovina seasons, 2000–01 domestic association football leagues, 2000–01 in Bosnia and Herzegovina football

Statistics of Premier League of Bosnia and Herzegovina in the 2000–01 season.

==Overview==

It was contested by 22 teams, and FK Željezničar Sarajevo won the championship. The season was the first season all team from Federation of Bosnia and Herzegovina, either Bosniaks and Croats played in the same league, unlike 2 stages league in previous seasons.
