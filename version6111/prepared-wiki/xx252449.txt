[[Mills House (Rome, New York)]]

CATEGORIES: Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Gothic Revival architecture in New York, Houses completed in 1877, Houses in Oneida County, New York, Rome, New York

Mills House is a historic home located at Rome in Oneida County, New York. It is an eclectic High Victorian Gothic style brick residence built in 1877. It has a [tpl]frac|2|1|2[/tpl]-story, gable-roofed main block and hip-roofed, square, brick kitchen wing.  It features a corner tower with pyramidal roof.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=6148|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: Mills House|date=March 1996|accessdate=2010-01-08 |author=Raymond W. Smith|publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation[/tpl] See also: [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=6149|title=Accompanying four photos[/tpl] 
It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1997.
