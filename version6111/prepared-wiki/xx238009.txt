[[Waldemar Nowicki]]

CATEGORIES: 1961 births, Living people, B71 Sandoy players, Expatriate footballers in the Faroe Islands

Waldemar Nowicki (born January 23, 1961) is a Polish football player.

==Career==

Waldemar Nowicki has played a great deal of his career with B71 Sandoy, but has also played football for several Polish teams.[tpl]cite web|publisher=90 Minut|title=Waldemar Nowicki|url=http://www.90minut.pl/kariera.php?id=9775|accessdate=2009-10-18[/tpl] He was very well known on the Faroe Islands for being a part of B71's successful stint in the early 1990s. He played his last game for B71 Sandoy in 2013.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Faroe Soccer|title=TB II-B71 0-0|url=http://www.faroesoccer.com/match.php?matchID=15135&pid=|accessdate=2013-12-18[/tpl] He has assisted several of B71's head-coaches, and has himself taken on the head-coach job, even though it was only for the second part of the 2003 season.
