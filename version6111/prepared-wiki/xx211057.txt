[[The Steel Mirror]]

CATEGORIES: 1948 novels, Spy novels, Novels by Donald Hamilton

The Steel Mirror is a spy novel by Donald Hamilton.

==Plot summary==

Chemist John Emmett hitches a ride with a beautiful woman who might be a murderer.

==Film adaptation==

Filmed as 5 Steps to Danger in 1957 starring Ruth Roman and Sterling Hayden.
