[[Maghull North railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Proposed railway stations in Merseyside, Railway stations in Sefton

Maghull North was a proposed new railway station at the north end  of Maghull, Merseyside, England was planned to be constructed as a modular station. It would have primarily served as the main station for Moss Side, Ashworth Hospital and a planned new prison.
It was announced in December 2010 that the planned prison and the proposed new railway station had been axed by Government spending cutbacks.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.champnews.com/html/newsstory.asp?id=8736 |title=Councillors protest Maghull North railway station being axed |publisher=Champion News |date=18 December 2010 |accessdate=23 August 2011[/tpl]
