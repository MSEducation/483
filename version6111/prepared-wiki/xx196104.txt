[[Palace of Justice (Bucharest)]]

CATEGORIES: Palaces in Bucharest, Historic monuments in Bucharest, Government buildings completed in 1895, Courthouses

The Palace of Justice ([tpl]lang-ro|Palatul Justiţiei[/tpl]), located in Bucharest, Romania, was built between 1890 and 1895. 
Located on the banks of the Dâmboviţa River, it houses the Bucharest Court of Appeal and the Sector 5 Court. Its last major restoration was between 2003 and 2006.
