[[Yoron Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in Kagoshima Prefecture

[tpl]Nihongo|Yoron Airport|与論空港|Yoron Kūkō[/tpl] [tpl]airport codes|RNJ|RORY[/tpl] is a third-class airport located on Yoronjima (Yoron Island) in Kagoshima Prefecture Japan.

==History==

Yoron Airport was opened on 1 May 1976. On 12 May 205, the runway was strengthened to enable operations by the Bombardier Dash 8 aircraft.
