[[Clyde's Bridge]]

CATEGORIES: Buildings and structures in South Lanarkshire, Road bridges in Scotland, Transport in South Lanarkshire

Clyde's Bridge, not to be confused with the similarly named Clyde Bridge further downstream, is the first major road bridge spanning the River Clyde in South Lanarkshire, Scotland. The bridge carries the A702 from Duneaton Roundabout to Wandel.[tpl]cite web|title=Clyde's Bridge|url=http://www.sabre-roads.org.uk/wiki/index.php?title=Clyde%27s_Bridge|publisher=Sabre-Roads|accessdate=11/8/13[/tpl]
