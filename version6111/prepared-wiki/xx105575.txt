[[Quảng Ngãi]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Quang Ngai Province, District capitals in Vietnam, Provincial capitals in Vietnam, Districts of Quang Ngai Province, Cities in Vietnam

Quảng Ngãi ([tpl]Audio|Quang Ngai.ogg|listen|help=no[/tpl]) is a city in central Vietnam. It is the township of Quảng Ngãi Province. Quảng Ngãi City borders Tư Nghĩa District on the south, west and east and borders Sơn Tịnh District on the north. It has an area of 37.12 km² and population of 119,251 inhabitants.

==Tourist destinations==

Quảng Ngãi has benefitted little from the Vietnamese tourist industry. Potential visitors should be aware that English is little spoken and the limited numbers of hotels are used to dealing only with Vietnamese customers.
