[[Gorgopotamos, Ioannina]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ioannina (regional unit)

Gorgopotamos ([tpl]lang-el|Γοργοπόταμος[/tpl]) is a small village located at the foot of Mount Grammos of Epirus (Greece). Is surrounded by forest and the small and beautiful river of Gorgopotamos runs right next to the village.
Gorgopotamos is part of the municipal unit of Mastorochoria of Ioannina regional unit, North-West Greece. Its location is very close to the Greek-Albanian borders. Population 69 inhabitants (2001).
