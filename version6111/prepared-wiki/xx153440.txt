[[Life Outside the Toybox]]

CATEGORIES: 2000 albums, Ardent Records albums

Life Outside the Toybox was Justifide's first album, first put out as an independent album, but later re-released by Ardent Records.

==Track listing==

==Facts about Life Outside the Toybox==

Escucha and Gozo Nuevo are remakes of earlier tracks in Spanish, namely Listen and Sweet New Found Joy.
