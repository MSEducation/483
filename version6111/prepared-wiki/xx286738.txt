[[Ijrud-e Bala Rural District]]

CATEGORIES: Rural Districts of Zanjan Province, Ijrud County

Ijrud-e Bala Rural District ([tpl]lang-fa|دهستان ايجرودبالا[/tpl]) is a rural district (dehestan) in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 10,025, in 2,342 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl] The rural district has 16 villages.
