[[1954 Giro d'Italia]]

CATEGORIES: Giro d'Italia, 1954 in cycle racing, 1954 in Italian sport, 1954 in road cycling

The 1954 Giro d'Italia of cycling was held from 21 May to 13 June 1954, consisting of 22 stages for a total of 4,396 km, ridden at an average speed of 33.563 km/h.
