[[Ollada]]

CATEGORIES: Catalan cuisine, Spanish soups and stews

Ollada ([tpl]IPA-ca|uˈʎaðə[/tpl], also named as putxero or perolada in the Valencian Community) is a traditional dish around the world. Is based in boiling vegetables and meat in a casserole

==Ingredients==

The ingredients can vary, but are based in vegetables (potato, carrot, artichoke etc.), legumes (beans, kidney beans, etc.) and embotit or butchery.
