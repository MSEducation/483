[[São Tomé and Príncipe at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics, 2013 in São Tomé and Príncipe, Athletics in São Tomé and Príncipe

São Tomé and Príncipe competed at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics in Moscow, Russia, from 10–18 August 2013. A team of one athlete, Christopher Lima da Costa, was represented the country in the event.
