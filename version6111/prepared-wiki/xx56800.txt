[[1937 Governor General's Awards]]

CATEGORIES: Governor General's Awards, 1937 in Canada, 1937 awards

The 1937 Governor General's Awards for Literary Merit were the second such awards, and the first to include a category for poetry and drama."Cumulative List of Winners of the Governor General's Literary Awards". Canada Council. March 22, 2011. No monetary prize was yet given; the awards were simply recognition of the authors.
