[[Lozna, Sălaj]]

CATEGORIES: Communes in Sălaj County

Lozna ([tpl]lang-hu|Nagylózna[/tpl]) is a commune located in Sălaj County, Romania. It is composed of five villages: Cormeniş (Ködmönös), Lozna, Preluci (Kőlózna), Valea Leşului (Lesvölgy) and Valea Loznei (Lóznavölgy).
