[[Putinza District]]

Putinza District is one of thirty-three districts of Yauyos Province, Lima Department, Peru.[tpl]es icon[/tpl] Instituto Nacional de Estadística e Informática. Banco de Información Distrital. Retrieved April 11, 2008.
Its capital is San Lorenzo de Putinza and it is the prime apple producer in the Province. Common surnames in this district are: Sandoval, Samaniego, Alcala, Jesusi and Huapaya.
