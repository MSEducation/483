[[Balaclava railway station, Melbourne]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1859

Balaclava railway station is located on the Sandringham line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the south-eastern Melbourne suburb of Balaclava opening on 19 December 1859.Balclava Vicsig
It is an elevated station located above the Carlisle Street rail overpass. Platform 1 has a large pebblemix building, Platform 2 a smaller pebblemix building. In 1981 the station buildings were reconstructed.[tpl]cite book|author=Vincent Adams Winter|title=VR and VicRail: 1962 - 1983|year=1990|isbn=0-9592069-3-0|page=107[/tpl]
In 1996 two men found $200,000 in a drum buried beneath one of the platforms.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.abc.net.au/rn/talks/8.30/lawrpt/lstories/lr060202.htm|title=ABC Radio National - The Law Report Transcript|date=6 February 1996|publisher=www.abc.net.au|accessdate=2008-08-03[/tpl] They handed it into the police and the find was reported in the media. In the same week another man found a second drum under the platform containing a similar quantity of money, which he also turned into the police. As no one claimed the money, both men were granted ownership of the money they found, despite being considered trespassers on land owned by the Public Transport Corporation.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.abc.net.au/rn/talks/8.30/lawrpt/lstories/lr990713.htm|title=ABC Radio National - The Law Report Transcript|date=13 July 1996|publisher=www.abc.net.au|accessdate=2008-08-03|archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20080726111342/http://www.abc.net.au/rn/talks/8.30/lawrpt/lstories/lr990713.htm| archivedate= 26 July 2008 | deadurl= no[/tpl]

==Major station upgrade==

In June 2012 a $13.3 million upgrade involving widening the platforms, upgrades to stair and ramp access, signage, booking office facilities, waiting rooms, provision of new station canopies to improve weather protection, wheelchair shelters and toilets. A new look for Balaclava station St Kilda News 4 June 2012 CCTV cameras will be added as well as provision for future lifts. The project is due for completion in October 2014.Balaclava Station Upgrade Project Public Transport Victoria When completed, the station will become a Premium station.Balaclava Station Upgrade Public Transport Victoria
