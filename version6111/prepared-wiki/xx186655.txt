[[Gimlet, Kentucky]]

Gimlet is an unincorporated community in Elliott County, Kentucky, United States.  It lies along Route 504 north of the city of Sandy Hook, the county seat of Elliott County.Rand McNally.  The Road Atlas '06. Chicago: Rand McNally, 2006, p. 43.  Its elevation is 1,102 feet (336 m).[tpl]gnis|508090[/tpl], Geographic Names Information System, 1979-09-20.  Accessed 2008-01-03.
A post office, established in 1880, closed in 1961. Gimlet is named for the gimlet tool, which is said to have the same crooked shape as the local creek.[tpl]cite book | url=http://books.google.com/books?id=3Lac2FUSj_oC&lpg=PA49&dq=cannon%20ky&pg=PA116#v=onepage&q=cannon%20ky&f=false | title=Kentucky Place Names | publisher=University Press of Kentucky | year=1987 | accessdate=2013-04-28 | author=Rennick, Robert M. | pages=116[/tpl]
