[[Alan Paris]]

Alan Paris (born 15 August 1964) is an English former professional footballer who played as a right back, making over 250 career appearances.

==Career==

Born in Slough, Paris played for Slough Town, Watford, Peterborough United, Leicester City and Notts County.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.neilbrown.newcastlefans.com/player6/alanparis.html|title=Profile|publisher=Post War English & Scottish Football League A - Z Player's Database[/tpl]
