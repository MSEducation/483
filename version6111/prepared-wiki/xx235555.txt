[[1958 Kentucky Wildcats football team]]

CATEGORIES: 1958 Southeastern Conference football season, Kentucky Wildcats football seasons, 1958 in Kentucky

The 1958 Kentucky Wildcats football team represented the University of Kentucky in the 1958 college football season. The Wildcats scored 136 points while allowing 115 points, and finished with a record of 5–4–1.[tpl]cite web|title=1958 Kentucky Wildcats results|url=http://www.jhowell.net/cf/scores/Kentucky.htm#1958|work=|archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/5kJByYIFZ|archivedate=2009-10-05|deadurl=no|accessdate=2009-10-03[/tpl] 

==Season==

Kentucky opened with a 51–0 win over Hawaii at Cardinal Stadium in Louisville.  A 13–0 win in the SEC opener against Georgia Tech followed.  2–0 Kentucky, then ranked #17 in the AP poll,September 22, 1958 AP poll at AP Poll Archive then lost four games in a row: 27–6 to #9 Ole Miss in Memphis, 8–0 to #1 Auburn,  32–7 at #9 LSU and 28–0 at Georgia.  A 33–12 win over Mississippi State was followed by a 0–0 tie against Vanderbilt. After a 20–6 win against Xavier, Kentucky closed the season with a 6–2 victory at Tennessee.[tpl]cite web|title=1958 Kentucky Wildcats scores|url=http://www.jhowell.net/cf/scores/Kentucky.htm#1958|work=|archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/5kJByYIFZ|archivedate=2009-10-05|deadurl=no|accessdate=2009-10-03[/tpl]1997 Kentucky Wildcats Football Media Guide, p.168, 207
The victory over Tennessee was Kentucky's second in a row, and second in a stretch of four games in which Kentucky denied Tennessee a win.1997 Kentucky Wildcats Football Media Guide, p.207  The Wildcats were invited to participate in the 1958 Bluegrass Bowl but declined due to what they considered to be poor treatment during their season opener, a win against Hawaii in the stadium in which the Bluegrass Bowl would be played.Rice, Russell, 'The Wildcats: A Story of Kentucky Football', ISBN 0-87397-075-6  Florida State and Oklahoma State played in the 1958 Bluegrass Bowl instead.
