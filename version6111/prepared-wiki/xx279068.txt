[[Jannat Ulang]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Jannat Ulang ([tpl]lang-fa|جنت اولنگ[/tpl], also Romanized as Jannat Ūlang; also known as Jannat Olang)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795191|Jannat Ulang[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
