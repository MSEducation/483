[[2007 FINA Synchronised Swimming World Trophy]]

CATEGORIES: Synchronized swimming, 2007 in synchronized swimming

The 2nd FINA Synchronized Swimming World Trophy was held 2007 in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. It featured swimmers from 8 nations, swimming in three events: Duet, Team and Free Combination.HistoFINA, volume 9 published by FINA on 2009-07-01; retrieved 2013-08-09.
