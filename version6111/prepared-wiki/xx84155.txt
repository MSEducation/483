[[Ōkuwa, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Ōkuwa|大桑村|Ōkuwa-mura[/tpl] is a village located in Kiso District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the village has an estimated population of 4,559 and a density of 19.45 persons per km². The total area is 234.45 km².
