[[Ole Paulssøn Haagenstad]]

CATEGORIES: 1775 births, 1866 deaths, Members of the Parliament of Norway, Oppland politicians

Ole Paulssøn Haagenstad (17 October 1775 - 1866) was a Norwegian politician.
He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament in 1814, 1815, 1818, 1824, 1827, 1833, 1836, 1839 and 1842, representing the rural constituency of Christians Amt (today named Oppland). He worked as a farmer.Ole Paulssøn Haagenstad — Norwegian Social Science Data Services (NSD)
