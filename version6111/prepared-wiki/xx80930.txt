[[Brouay]]

CATEGORIES: Communes of Calvados, Calvados communes articles needing translation from French Wikipedia

Brouay is a commune in the Calvados department in the Basse-Normandie region in northwestern France. There is a cemetery for war dead in the church yard. There are 377 graves of combatants killed during the Normandy invasions, mostly from the United Kingdom.
