[[Gol Tappeh, Khodabandeh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Gol Tappeh ([tpl]lang-fa|گل تپه[/tpl])[tpl]GEOnet3|-3776430|Gol Tappeh[/tpl] is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 811, in 184 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
