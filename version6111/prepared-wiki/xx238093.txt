[[Belliveau's Cove, Nova Scotia]]

CATEGORIES: Communities in Digby County, Nova Scotia, General Service Areas in  Nova Scotia

thumb|left|Belliveau's Cove Post OfficeBelliveaus Cove (French: Anse-des-Belliveau) is an Acadian community in the Canadian province of Nova Scotia, located in the District of Clare in Digby County. A major centre of wooden shipbuilding in the 19th and early 20th century, Belliveaus Cove built the second largest wooden ship ever constructed in Canada, County of Yarmouth in 1884.
