[[Wolfe Creek Meteorite Crater National Park]]

CATEGORIES: National parks of Western Australia, Kimberley (Western Australia), Protected areas established in 1969, Great Sandy Desert

Wolfe Creek Crater National Park is a national park in Western Australia (Australia), [tpl]convert|1854|km|mi|0[/tpl] northeast of Perth. It contains Wolfe Creek crater.
The park lies about [tpl]convert|145|km|mi|0[/tpl] South from Halls Creek and can be accessed via the Tanami Road. The park is located on the edge of the Great Sandy Desert and is composed mostly of desert plains and spinifex grassland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.outback-australia-travel-secrets.com/wolfe_creek.html|title=Outback Australia - Wolfe Creek Meteorite Crater National Park|year=2006|accessdate=26 September 2010[/tpl]
