[[4408 Zlatá Koruna]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Asteroids named for places, Astronomical objects discovered in 1988

4408 Zlatá Koruna is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1292.3060156 days (3.54 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=4408 | title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-05-24 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on October 4, 1988.
