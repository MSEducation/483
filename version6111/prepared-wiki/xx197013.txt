Sosha Makani ([tpl]lang-fa|سوشا مکانی[/tpl], born 18 November 1986) is an Iranian goalkeeper who plays for Foolad.

==Club Career==

===Foolad===

Makani played a main role in Foolad's success in the 2013-2014 season. Joining them in the winter transfer window, Makani led Foolad to the Iran Pro League title, and helped Foolad reach the knockout stages of the AFC Champions League for the first time ever. Following Makani's stellar performances for Foolad, Feyenoord from the Netherlands and Club Brugge from Belgium have taken interest in Makani.

==International career==

Makani was invited to the Iranian national team by Carlos Queiroz and played in the 2012 WAFF Championship.
