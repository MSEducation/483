[[(10841) 1994 PP1]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1994, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(10841) 1994 PP1 is an inner main-belt minor planet.JPL Small-Body Database Browser  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on August 12, 1994.Discovery Circumstances: Numbered Minor Planets (10001)-(15000)
