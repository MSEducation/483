[[Gościeradów]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Kraśnik County

Gościeradów [tpl]IPAc-pl|g|o|ś|ć|e1|'|r|a|d|u|f[/tpl] is a village in Kraśnik County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It is the seat of the gmina (administrative district) called Gmina Gościeradów. It lies approximately [tpl]convert|17|km|mi|0[/tpl] west of Kraśnik and [tpl]convert|59|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-west of the regional capital Lublin.
The village has a population of 960.
