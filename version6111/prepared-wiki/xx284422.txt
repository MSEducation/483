[[Alan Riach]]

CATEGORIES: 1957 births, Living people, Scottish writers

Alan Riach is a Scottish poet and academic.
He was born in 1957 in Airdrie, Lanarkshire. Riach studied at Cambridge and Glasgow. He worked at the University of Waikato in New Zealand from 1986.
