[[Wildes Veazie]]

CATEGORIES: Maine Black Bears football coaches, 1870 births, 1948 deaths, Harvard University alumni

Wildes Perkins Walker Veazie (April 3, 1870 – June 25, 1948) was the head coach of the University of Maine's football team in 1893 and compiled a 0–5 record. He was an alumnus of Harvard University.
