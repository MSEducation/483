[[Allen Kearns]]

CATEGORIES: 1894 births, 1956 deaths, People from Brockville, Canadian male musical theatre actors

Allen Kearns (14 August 1894 â€“ 20 April 1956) was a Canadian-born singer and actor. He was born in Brockville, Ontario, Canada and died in Albany, New York.  He played the romantic lead role in several Broadway musicals and is especially remembered for introducing two hit songs by George and Ira Gershwin: "'S Wonderful" (from Funny Face, 1927) and "Embraceable You" (from Girl Crazy, 1930).
