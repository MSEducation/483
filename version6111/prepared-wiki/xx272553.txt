[[Hylarana kampeni]]

CATEGORIES: Hylarana, Amphibians of Indonesia, Endemic fauna of Indonesia

Hylarana kampeni is a species of true frog in the genus Hylarana. It is native to Sumatra, Indonesia. It lives in primary rainforest along streams above [tpl]convert|700|m|abbr=on[/tpl].[ref]
