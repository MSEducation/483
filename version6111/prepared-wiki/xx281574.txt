[[Red Roses For Me (song)]]

CATEGORIES: The Dubliners songs, 1994 songs

Red Roses For Me is a single by The Dubliners and Niamh Kavanagh charting at No.13 in the Irish Charts in 1994.http://irishcharts.ie/search/placement This was the last single Ronnie Drew would ever release with The Dubliners.
