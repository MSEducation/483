[[Brazilian blind electric ray]]

CATEGORIES: Narcinidae, Animals described in 2001, Fish of Brazil, Endemic fauna of Brazil

The Brazilian blind electric ray, Benthobatis kreffti, is a species of fish in the Narcinidae family endemic to Brazil. Its natural habitat is open seas.Rincon, G. 2004. Benthobatis kreffti. 2006 IUCN Red List of Threatened Species. Downloaded on 3 August 2007.
