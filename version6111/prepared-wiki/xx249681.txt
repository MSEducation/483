[[SMK St. Anthony]]

CATEGORIES: Schools in Sarawak, 1932 establishments in Sarawak

Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan St. Anthony is a secondary school in Sarikei established in 1932. Currently, there are about 2000 students and more than 100 teachers in the school.[tpl]Citation needed|date=January 2010[/tpl]
