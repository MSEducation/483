[[List of hospitals in Venezuela]]

CATEGORIES: Lists of hospitals by country, Hospitals in Venezuela, Lists of buildings and structures in Venezuela

This is a list of hospitals in Venezuela.
For a complete list, go to:  http://www.paginasamarillascantv.com.ve/.  Where it says "Clasificación", select Clínicas y Hospitales, and where it says "Ciudad", select "Todas las Ciudades".  There are about 2,018 clinics and hospitals in Venezuela, 634 in Caracas, 195 in Maracaibo, 173 in Valencia, and 92 in Barquisimeto.
