[[Croxton railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1889

Croxton railway station is located on the South Morang line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the north-eastern Melbourne suburb of Northcote opening on 8 October 1889.Croxton Vicsig

==Platforms & services==
