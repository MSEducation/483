[[The Birches (Belgrade Lakes, Maine)]]

CATEGORIES: National Register of Historic Places in Maine, Buildings and structures completed in 1916, Buildings and structures in Kennebec County, Maine

The Birches in Belgrade Lakes, Maine is an historic house, built in 1916. The architects were John Calvin Stevens and his son John Howard Stevens.
'The Birches' was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1996. http://nrhp.focus.nps.gov/natregsearchresult.do?fullresult=true&recordid=28
