[[Bułkowizna]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Augustów County

Bułkowizna [tpl]IPAc-pl|b|u|U|k|o|'|w|i|z|n|a[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Bargłów Kościelny, within Augustów County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|8|km|mi|0[/tpl] south of Bargłów Kościelny, [tpl]convert|21|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-west of Augustów, and [tpl]convert|70|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of the regional capital Białystok.
The village has an approximate population of 129.
