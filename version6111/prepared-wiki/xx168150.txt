[[Ælfstan (bishop of London)]]

CATEGORIES: Bishops of London, 10th-century bishops, 995 deaths, Year of birth unknown

[tpl]Other uses|Ælfstan (disambiguation)[tpl]![/tpl]Ælfstan[/tpl]

Ælfstan (or Aelfstan) was a medieval Bishop of London.
Ælfstan was consecrated 959 and 964 and he died between 995 and 996.Fryde, et al. Handbook of British Chronology p 220
