[[3814 Hoshi-no-mura]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Astronomical objects discovered in 1981

3814 Hoshi-no-mura is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 2044.3803165 days (5.60 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=3814 |  title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-04-28 | publisher = NASA[/tpl]
