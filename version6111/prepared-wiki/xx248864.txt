[[Robin Melanie Leacock]]

CATEGORIES: American documentary filmmakers, American film directors, Living people

Robin Melanie Leacock (aka Robin Baker Leacock) is a documentary filmmaker who directed "It Girls", "A Passion For Giving" and "I'll Take Manhattan".
"It Girls" is a documentary film about fashion that aired nationally in the U.S. on April 2 and 7, 2002.
