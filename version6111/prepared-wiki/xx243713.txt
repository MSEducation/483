[[Er Dong]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 films, Chinese films, Mandarin-language films, 2000s drama films

Er Dong ([tpl]zh|c=二冬|p=èr dōng[/tpl]), directed by Yang Jin, is a 2008 narrative independent Chinese film about a rebellious teenager in rural northern China. The film is shot in documentary-style and is the director's second feature. 

==Plot==

Er Dong and his Christian mother lives in a rural Chinese village. She sends him to a Christian boarding school because of his inappropriate behavior, hoping that God will give him a new direction in life. At the school, Er meets a girl, Chang'e, and they are almost expelled because of their misconduct.
