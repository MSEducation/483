[[Bonab Industrial Complex]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County, Company towns in Iran

Bonab Industrial Complex ([tpl]lang-fa|مجتمع صنعتي بناب[/tpl] – Mojtame`-ye Şanʿatī-ye Bonāb) is a village and company town in Bonab Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 9, in 4 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
