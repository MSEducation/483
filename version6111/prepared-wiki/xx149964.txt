[[Borodino, New York]]

CATEGORIES: Hamlets in New York, Syracuse metropolitan area, Hamlets in Onondaga County, New York

Borodino is a hamlet located at the intersection of New York State Route 41 and New York State Route 174 in the Town of Spafford in Onondaga County, New York, near Skaneateles Lake.      There is a boat launch into Skaneateles Lake for residents.   There is a monument for veterans in the center of Borodino, designed by Gianfranco Fritelli.  There is also a little-altered 1830 Methodist Church—known as "The Church"—in the Federal Style.
The Spafford town offices are located in Borodino.
The Borodino District School#8 and Borodino Hall are listed on the National Register of Historic Places.[tpl]NRISref|version=2009a[/tpl]
