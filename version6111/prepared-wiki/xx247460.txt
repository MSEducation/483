[[Mykhaylo Sokolovsky]]

CATEGORIES: 1951 births, Living people, Soviet footballers, FC CSKA Kyiv players, FC Metalurh Zaporizhya players, FC Shakhtar Donetsk players, FC Krystal Kherson players, FC Metalurh Donetsk managers, People from Slovyansk

Mykhaylo Ivanovich Sokolovsky ([tpl]lang-uk|Михайло Іванович Соколовский[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-ru|Михаил Иванович Соколовский[/tpl]; born November 15, 1951) is a Ukrainian professional football coach and a former player. Currently, he works as a scout for FC Metalurh Donetsk.
For the big number of games played for Shakhtar Donetsk he received the Club Loyalty Award in 1987. He was the most titled player of Shakhtar Donetsk during the Soviet Union, and he is considered to be one of the most legendary players in the history of the club.http://sport.segodnya.ua/football/kratkaja-entsiklopedija-pobed-shakhtera.html
