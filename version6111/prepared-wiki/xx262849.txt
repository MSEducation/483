[[1963 USAC Championship Car season]]

CATEGORIES: 1963 USAC Championship Car season, USAC Championship Car, 1963 in American motorsport

The 1963 USAC Championship Car season consisted of 12 races, beginning in Trenton, New Jersey on April 21 and concluding in Phoenix, Arizona on November 17. There was also one non-championship event at Pikes Peak, Colorado.  The USAC National Champion  was A. J. Foyt, and the Indianapolis 500 winner was Parnelli Jones.
