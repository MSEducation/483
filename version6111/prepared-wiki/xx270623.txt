[[Hylarana everetti]]

CATEGORIES: Hylarana, Amphibians of the Philippines, Endemic fauna of the Philippines

Hylarana everetti is a species of true frog in the genus Hylarana. It is endemic to the island of Mindanao in the Philippines.[ref]Arvin Diesmos, Angel Alcala, Rafe Brown, Leticia Afuang, Cynthia Dolino, Genevieve Gee, Katie Hampson, Mae Leonida Diesmos, Aldrin Mallari, Perry Ong, Liza Paguntalan, Marisol Pedregosa, Dondi Ubaldo, Baldwin Gutierrez, Djoko Iskandar 2004. Hylarana everetti. In: IUCN 2011. IUCN Red List of Threatened Species. Version 2011.1. 
[/ref]
