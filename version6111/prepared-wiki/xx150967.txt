[[Selby's Secret]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 novels, Australian novels, Selby Series

Selby’s Secret is the first children's novel in the Selby series by Australian writer Duncan Ball, and was first published in 1985. It was reissued in 2004.

==Awards and nominations==

Years after publication, the book won its first award, the 1987 Primary section in the Waybra Award.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.wayrba.iinet.net.au/winners.htm |title=WAYRBA - West Australian Young Reader's Book Award |author= |authorlink= |coauthors= |date= |format= |work= |publisher= |pages= |language= |archiveurl= http://www.webcitation.org/5c23wFFeK|archivedate= 2008-11-02|quote= |accessdate=2008-11-02[/tpl] It also came fourth place in the 1987  Primary section of the Koala Awards. The book was also shortlisted many other awards including the  1993 Yabba Awards for young readers. It has been shortlisted for the Koala Awards for 1988-1991 as well as 1996, 1999, and 2000. In 2001 it was finally voted into the Koala Awards’ Hall of Fame.
