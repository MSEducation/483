[[R705 road (Ireland)]]

CATEGORIES: Regional roads in the Republic of Ireland, Roads in County Kilkenny, Roads in County Carlow

The R705 road is a regional road in Ireland which runs north-south from the M9 at Leighlinbridge in County Carlow and through Muine Bheag before crossing into County Kilkenny. 
It continues to Graiguenamanagh, passes Brandon Hill (516m) and continues south along the western side of the valley of the River Barrow
until it terminates at a junction with the R700.
The route is [tpl]convert|41|km|mi|abbr=on[/tpl] long.
