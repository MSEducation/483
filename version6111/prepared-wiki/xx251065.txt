[[Anne Cohen]]

CATEGORIES: 1941 births, Living people, New South Wales Liberal politicians, Members of the New South Wales Legislative Assembly

Anne Margaret Cohen (born 2 January 1941) is a former Australian politician. She was the Liberal member for Minchinbury and then Badgerys Creek from 1988 to 1995.
Cohen was born in Inverell in New South Wales, and was an editor and small business owner before entering politics. Married to Richard, she has two children.[ref]
