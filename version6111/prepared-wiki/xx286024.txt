[[Monica Mehta (author)]]

CATEGORIES: American television personalities, American women writers, American business writers, Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania alumni, University of Pennsylvania alumni, 1974 births, Living people

Monica Mehta (born 1974) is an American author, finance commentator and investor.  She is the author of The Entrepreneurial Instinct: How Everyone Has the Innate Ability to Start a Successful Small Business.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.entrepreneur.com/article/227768|website = Entrepreneur|title = Look at Problems From an Outsider's POV and 4 More Business Tips From the Week|last = Eha|first = Brian Patrick[/tpl]  Mehta writes small business and finance columns for Inc. and Entrepreneur and appears regularly on national cable networks including Fox News, Fox Business, CNN, Bloomberg Television, MSNBC and HBO.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.shesource.org/experts/profile/monica-mehta|title = Monica Mehta Profile|website = Shesource[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://kmmsam.com/monica-metha-on-entrepreneurs-open-for-business-saturday-922/|website = AM1450KMMS|title = Monica Mehta On Entrepreneurs – Open for Business Saturday 9/22|last = Egelhoff|first = Tom[/tpl]  She is a managing principal at Seventh Capital, a New York based investment firm.[tpl]cite web|url=https://www.openforum.com/videos/small-biz-startup/|website = American Express Open Forum|title = Small Biz Startup[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.entrepreneur.com/article/227768|title = Look at Problems From an Outsider's POV and 4 More Business Tips From the Week|last = Eha|first = Brian Patrick|website = Entrepreneur[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.forbes.com/sites/danschawbel/2012/09/30/how-to-train-your-mind-to-become-entrepreneurial/|website = Forbes|title = How to Train Your Mind to Become Entrepreneurial|last = Schawbel|first = Dan[/tpl]

==Early life==

Monica is South Asian-American.[tpl]cite web|url=http://houston.netip.org/events/view/507d74af-a068-4d42-8e09-3461c665e11f|website = NetIP Houston|title = Meet Monica Mehta author of "The Entrepreneurial Instinct"[/tpl] Mehta’s father was a merchant marine ship’s captain and she spent her early childhood living at sea until her family immigrated to Texas in 1980.  Her parents faced initial struggles as new immigrants before becoming successful small business owners.[tpl]cite web|url=http://adamcarolla.com/monica-mehta-and-david-wild/|website = The Adam Carolla Show|title = Episodes: Monica Mehta and David Wild[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.blogher.com/why-rhetoric-against-successful-must-end|title = Why the Rhetoric Against the Successful Must End|website = BlogHer|last = Mehta|first = Monica[/tpl]  In 1997, Mehta graduated from the University of Pennsylvania with a Bachelor of Science in Economics from the Wharton School.[tpl]cite web|url=http://whartonmagazine.com/issues/fall-2012/the-bookshelf/|website = Wharton Magazine|title = The Bookshelf[/tpl]
While at Penn, she was a member of the award winning jazz a cappella group Counterparts, performing alongside Grammy award winning singer John Legend.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.dolphin.upenn.edu/cparts/alumni.html|website = Counterparts|title = Counterparts Alumni[/tpl] Mehta also worked as a small business consultant at the Wharton Small Business Development Center where she assisted local West Philadelphia businesses write business plans and prepare loan applications.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.mcgraw-hill.com.sg/html/9780071797429.html|website = McGraw-Hill Education|title = "The Entrepreneurial Instinct" description[/tpl] Mehta was inducted into The Lantern Society, a senior society for outstanding Wharton undergraduates[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.linkedin.com/groups?viewMembers=&gid=2944922&sik=1376424844946|website = LinkedIn|title = The Lantern Society Alumni Association[/tpl]

==Career, Media Appearances and Book==

Following graduation, Mehta joined Marakon Associates as a strategy consultant and then worked as a brand manager for L’Oreal and an investor for a seed fund backed by the Texas Pacific Group.  She is currently an investor for Seventh Capital, an investment firm with offices in New York and Texas.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.forbes.com/sites/danschawbel/2012/09/30/how-to-train-your-mind-to-become-entrepreneurial/|website = Forbes|title = How to Train Your Mind to Become Entrepreneurial|last = Schawbel|first = Dan[/tpl]
Mehta started appearing on cable television as a business and finance expert in 2006.
