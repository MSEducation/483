[[McCartys, New Mexico]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Cibola County, New Mexico, Unincorporated communities in New Mexico

McCartys is an unincorporated community in Cibola County, New Mexico, United States. McCartys is located near Interstate 40, [tpl]convert|11.8|mi|km[/tpl] east-southeast of Grants.
