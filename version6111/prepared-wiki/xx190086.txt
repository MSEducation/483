[[The Four Musketeers (musical)]]

CATEGORIES: 1967 musicals, West End musicals, Musicals based on novels

The Four Musketeers is a musical with a score by Laurie Johnson and lyrics by Herbert Kretzmera. It was developed from a book by Michael Pertwee loosely based on The Three Musketeers.  
The musical premiered at the Theatre Royal, Drury Lane, London on 5 December 1967.  It was directed by Peter Coe. The sets were designed by Sean Kenny.
Harry Secombe played D'Artagnan and Aubrey Wood Richelieu. Also in the original London cast were: Elizabeth Larner, Glyn Owen, John Junkin, Stephanie Voss, Jeremy Lloyd, Sheena Marshe and Kenneth Connor.
The show ran for 462 performances.
