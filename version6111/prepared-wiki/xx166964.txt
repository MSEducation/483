[[Newbarn]]

CATEGORIES: Hamlets in Kent

Newbarn is a hamlet around a crossroads northwest of Etchinghill in Kent, England. Etchinghill Golf Club is on the road between the two.http://www.uk-golfguide.com/england/53717.html Tolsford Hill BT Tower can clearly be seen on Tolsford Hill to the south.
