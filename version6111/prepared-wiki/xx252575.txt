[[Cyprien Richard]]

CATEGORIES: French alpine skiers, Living people, 1979 births, Alpine skiers at the 2010 Winter Olympics, Olympic alpine skiers of France, Alpine skiers at the 2014 Winter Olympics

Cyprien Richard (born 27 January 1979 in Thonon-les-Bains, Haute Savoie)http://www.fis-ski.com/uk/604/613.html?sector=AL&competitorid=50789&type=result is a French alpine skier. 
Richard has attained four podiums in Alpine Skiing World Cup, all in giant slalom. He has won one World Cup race at Adelboden, Switzerland, tying Norwegian Aksel Lund Svindal for first place.
