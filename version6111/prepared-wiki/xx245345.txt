[[List of tallest buildings in Albany, New York]]

CATEGORIES: Skyscrapers in Albany, New York, Buildings and structures in Albany, New York, Lists of tallest buildings in New York

The city of Albany, New York is the site of 56 high-rises, 19 of which stand taller than [tpl]convert|150|ft|m|0|abbr=on[/tpl]. The tallest building in the city is the 44-story Erastus Corning Tower, which is [tpl]convert|589|ft|m|0|abbr=on[/tpl] tall. Completed in 1966, it is the tallest building in the U.S. state of New York outside of the city of New York.[tpl]cite web|title=Erastus Corning Tower|url=http://www.emporis.com/building/erastuscorningtower-albany-ny-usa|publisher=Emporis GmbH|accessdate=13 March 2012[/tpl] The second-tallest building is the Alfred E. Smith State Office Building, which stands [tpl]convert|387|ft|m|0|abbr=on[/tpl].[tpl]cite web|title=Alfred E. Smith State Office Building|url=http://www.emporis.com/building/alfred-e-smith-state-office-building-albany-ny-usa|publisher=Emporis GmbH|accessdate=13 March 2012[/tpl] Four buildings tie for third tallest in Albany at [tpl]convert|310|ft|m|0|abbr=on[/tpl], all of which belong to the Empire State Plaza.[tpl]cite web|title=Albany's tallest buildings - Top 20|url=http://www.emporis.com/statistics/tallest-buildings-albany-ny-usa|publisher=Emporis GmbH|accessdate=13 March 2012[/tpl]
