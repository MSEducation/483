[[Noonday Creek Structure Number 4]]

CATEGORIES: Buildings and structures in Cobb County, Georgia, Dams in Georgia (U.S. state)

Noonday Creek Structure Number 4 is an earthen dam on Noonday Creek regulated by the Georgia Safe Dams Program,Georgia Safe Dams Program located west of Noonday Creek Park. Construction was completed in 1954.

==Dimensions==

The dam is [tpl]convert|34|ft|m[/tpl] high, and [tpl]convert|765|ft|m[/tpl] long. The maximum discharge is 2600 cubic feet  per second.Findlakes
