[[Keo Puth Rasmey]]

CATEGORIES: Cambodian people of Chinese descent, Funcinpec politicians, People from Kampong Cham Province, Living people, 1952 births

Keo Puth Rasmey ([tpl]lang-km|កែវ ពុទ្ឋរស្មី[/tpl], born in Kampong Cham Province on October 1, 1952Keo Puth Rasmey: president of Cambodia's co-ruling Funcinpec Party, People's Daily Online) is a Cambodian politician. On 19 October 2006 he was appointed to lead Funcinpec, the royalist party of Cambodia, replacing former leader Norodom Ranariddh. He is the son-in-law of Norodom Sihanouk, former king of Cambodia, and is of Chinese descent.第二届世界华商(香港)论坛, 姜文卉; 西哈努克親王女兒夫婦: 中柬友誼源遠流長, 2007-8-12, Ta Kung Newspaper
