[[Scalado]]

CATEGORIES: Software companies of Sweden

Scalado is a provider of imaging technologies, applications and engineering services for the camera (phone) industry. The company is a Swedish imaging software company that focuses on the wireless device industry. Scalado's CAPS is embedded in more than a billion cellphone camera devices.[tpl]cite web | url=http://venturebeat.com/2011/12/20/scalado-mobile-photo-1b-devices |title=Scalado’s innovative mobile photo tech powers 1 billion devices |publisher=Venturebeat.com |date=2011-12-20 |accessdate=2012-06-07[/tpl][tpl]cite web | url=http://www.scalado.com/display/en/2011-12-20+Scalado%27s+advanced+imaging+software+now+in+more+than+1+billion+devices |title=Scalado's advanced imaging software now in more than 1 billion devices |publisher=scalado.com |date=2011-12-20 |accessdate=2012-06-07[/tpl] Scalado was acquired by Nokia in 2012.[tpl]cite web | url=http://tech2.in.com/news/smartphones/nokia-acquires-developers-and-ip-from-scalado/331812 |title=Nokia acquires developers and IP from Scalado |date=2012-07-24 |accessdate=2012-07-25[/tpl]

==History==

Scalado was founded in 2000 at Ideon Science Park in Lund, Sweden by former students from Lund's University, Sami Niemi, Fadi Abbas, Maziar Jahanshahi and Pierre Elzouki (active until 2004).[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.scalado.com/display/en/founders |title=Scalado Founders |publisher=scalado.com |date=2000-10-13 |accessdate=2012-06-07[/tpl] Scalado employs imaging specialists worldwide and is headquartered in Lund, Sweden.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.sydsvenskan.se/ekonomi/exportsucce-fran-lund-prisas |title=Exportsuccé från Lund prisas |publisher=sydsvenskan.se |date=2010-08-25 |accessdate=2011-01-05[/tpl] The company has offices in Sweden (HQ), Korea, China, Hong Kong, Taiwan, Singapore and the United States.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.ideon.se/pressrum/pressmeddelanden/artiklar/artikel/scalado-moves-into-asia-and-chooses-south-korea-as-its-first-operational-office-in-the-region |title=Scalado moves into Asia |publisher=ideon.se |date=2007-03-27 |accessdate=2012-06-07[/tpl][tpl]cite web | url=http://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20110425006525/en/Cybercom-Group-Cybercom-Building-Product-Development-Department |title=Scalado Development Department in China |publisher=computersweden.idg.se |date=2011-04-26 |accessdate=2012-06-07[/tpl][tpl]cite web | url=http://computersweden.idg.se/2.2683/1.228602 |title=Scalado Inc (USA) |publisher=computersweden.idg.se |date=2009-05-08 |accessdate=2012-06-07[/tpl]

==Technology==

In 2010 Scalado technology was embedded in more than 930 millions devices with a predicted annual growth of 500 million devices per year.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.visionmobile.com/rsc/researchreports/VisionMobile-100-Million-Club-%282H10%29.pdf |title=Mobile Industry Atlas 3rd edition |format=PDF |publisher=visionmobile.com |year=2010 |accessdate=2011-01-05[/tpl] Examples of its technologies is Scalado RAJpeg, Scalado SpeedTags and Scalado SpeedView.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.scalado.com/display/en/Technologies |title=Scalado Technologies |publisher=scalado.com |accessdate=2012-06-07[/tpl]
During Blackberry World 2012 in Florida, Research in Motion revealed a demo of its upcoming new mobile os, BlackBerry 10, incorporating Scalado's time warp feature.

==Products==

Scalado's software offers products in capturing, viewing and editing images. Examples of some of the imaging products Scalado provides its customers with: Scalado CAPS, Scalado PhotoFlow, Scalado Rewind, Scalado TimeWarp, Scalado Camera SDK, Scalado Album SDK, and Scalado Editor SDK.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.scalado.com/display/en/Products |title=Scalado Products |publisher=scalado.com |accessdate=2012-06-07[/tpl] In 2012 Scalado also started producing mobile apps for the consumer market.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.scalado.com/display/en/Apps |title=Scalado Apps |publisher=scalado.com |accessdate=2012-06-07[/tpl]
