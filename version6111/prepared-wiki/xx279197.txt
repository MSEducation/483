[[Gomishabad]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ahar County

Gomishabad ([tpl]lang-fa|گميش اباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Gomishābād; also known as Gomeshābād, Gomoshābād, Gyumishabad, Gyumishili, and Qāsem ‘Alī Kandī)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3064761|Gomishabad[/tpl] is a village in Goyjah Bel Rural District, in the Central District of Ahar County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 158, in 28 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|03[/tpl]
