[[Ged Maybury]]

CATEGORIES: 1953 births, Living people, Australian science fiction writers, New Zealand science fiction writers, New Zealand children's writers, Steampunk writers

Ged Maybury is a children's book author.  He was born in Christchurch, New Zealand, in 1953, spent his childhood in Dunedin and has been based in Australia since 2002. He has been writing books for children and young adults since 1984. He was a finalist in the AIM New Zealand Children's Book Awards 1994 with The Triggerstone, and in the NZ Post Children's Book Awards 2001;Christchurch City Library - New Zealand Post Children's book awards with Crab Apples. He writes science fiction, in particular of the steampunk sub-genre, and humour.
