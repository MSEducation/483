[[El mundo frio]]

CATEGORIES: Corrupted (band) albums, 2005 albums

El mundo frio is the fourth full-length studio album by Japanese doom metal band Corrupted.  The album consists of a single, continuous piece of music. The instrumentation is notable for inclusion of the Western harp.
