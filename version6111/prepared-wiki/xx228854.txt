[[Shadow WX]]

CATEGORIES: Japanese male professional wrestlers, Living people, 1969 births

Satoru Shiga is a professional wrestler currently competing in Big Japan Pro Wrestling by his ring name Shadow WX. He debuted in 1995 in IWA Japan and proceeded to move to Big Japan Pro Wrestling in 1996, where he became a protege of Mr. Pogo, and won the BJW Deathmatch Heavyweight Championship 4 times[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.wrestling-titles.com/japan/bigjapan/bj-dm-h.html|title=BJW Death Match Heavyweight Title|publisher=www.wrestling-titles.com[/tpl] and has been one of the most successful wrestlers in Big Japan Pro Wrestling history.
