 Minja Popović  (Serbian Cyrillic: Mињa Пoпoвић ; born January 23, 1983 in Belgrade) is a Serbian footballer currently playing with FK Voždovac.
Popović scored 3 goals for FK BASK Beograd in the Serbian First League during the 2006-07 season.Prva liga Srbija 2006-2007 - Lista strelaca  He transferred to Ukrainian Premier League club FC Zakarpattia Uzhhorod for the following season, where he only appeared in two league matches.Zakarpattia's Ukrainian Premier League 2007 season statistics
