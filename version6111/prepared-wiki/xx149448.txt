[[Mohamed Al-Bishi]]

CATEGORIES: 1987 births, Living people, Saudi Arabian footballers, Saudi Arabia international footballers, 2006 FIFA World Cup players, Al-Nassr FC players

Mohamed Al-Bishi ([tpl]lang-ar|محمد البيشي[/tpl]) (born 3 May 1987) is a Saudi Arabian football (soccer) defender who currently, as of January 2010, plays for Al Nassr.
He is a member of the national team, and was called up to the 2006 FIFA World Cup[tpl]FIFA player|253509|Mohamed Al Bishi[/tpl] after Mohammed Al-Anbar was injured in a training session.
