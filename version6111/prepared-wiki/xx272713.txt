[[Jennie Simpson]]

CATEGORIES: 1987 births, Living people, Waterford camogie players

Jennifer ‘Jennie’ Simpson is a camogie player, winner of  Soaring Star awards in 2010 and 2011 and an All Ireland junior medal in 2011.

==Other awards==

National Camogie League Division 2 medal 2011, Munster Inter-provisional title, County Championship 2009, Ashbourne Cup 2008.[tpl]cite book | last = Moran | first =Mary | title = A Game of Our Own: The History of Camogie | publisher = Cumann Camógaíochta | year = 2011 | location = Dublin, Ireland | pages = 460[/tpl]
