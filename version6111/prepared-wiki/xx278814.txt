[[Jazimaq]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Jazimaq ([tpl]lang-fa|جزيمق[/tpl], also Romanized as Jazīmaq; also known as Jazīman (Persian: جزيمن), Gizmeh, Kharīmaq, and Kiz’ma)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3067891|Jazimaq[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Pain Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 250, in 51 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
