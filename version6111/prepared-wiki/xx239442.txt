[[Patria submachine gun]]

CATEGORIES: 9mm Parabellum firearms, Machine pistols, Submachine guns, Submachine guns of Argentina, Telescoping bolt submachine guns

The Patria submachine gun (Pistola Ametralladora Patria is a submachine gun of Argentine origin.

==Developmemt==

It was developed by Major Luis Ricardo Dávila of the Argentine Air Force, and is protected by national Patent n° 220494/5/6/7 on 20/08/1980. It uses 9x19mm Parabellum rounds.

==Overview==

The weapon is a close copy of the Ingram MAC-10 and features a cooling jacket/barrel extension much like the South African BXP.[tpl]cite web|author=08:26 |url=http://www.zonamilitar.com.ar/foros/showthread.php?t=1010 |title=Armas "made In Argentina" - Foros Zona Militar |publisher=Zonamilitar.com.ar |date= |accessdate=2009-11-05[/tpl]
