[[Plain and Bordered White Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Plain and Bordered White Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠰᠢᠯᠤᠭᠤᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠥᠪᠡᠭᠡᠲᠦ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠴᠠᠭᠠᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Siluɣun Köbegetü Čaɣan qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=正镶白旗|t=正鑲白旗|p=Zhèngxiāngbái Qí[/tpl]) is a banner (administrative division) of Inner Mongolia, China. It is under the administration of Xilin Gol League.
