[[WAGP]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in South Carolina, Christian radio stations in the United States, Talk radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 1986, 1986 establishments in South Carolina

WAGP (88.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Christian talk format.Licensed to Beaufort, South Carolina, USA.  The station is currently owned by Community Broadcasting Corp. of Beaufort, Inc.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=WAGP |title=WAGP Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division[/tpl]
WAGP features programing from Salem Communications and from the Moody Broadcating Network.  The station also produces local call-in shows, an advice show aimed at women, daily bible study and a rebroadcast of the weekly sermon at a local church.
