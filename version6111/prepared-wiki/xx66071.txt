[[Read Music/Speak Spanish]]

CATEGORIES: 2002 albums, Desaparecidos (band) albums, Saddle Creek Records albums

Read Music/Speak Spanish was the only record by the American emo band Desaparecidos, and was released in February 2002. This album is the forty-second release of Saddle Creek Records.
