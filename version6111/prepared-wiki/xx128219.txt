[[George T. Bagby State Park]]

CATEGORIES: State parks of Georgia (U.S. state), Protected areas of Clay County, Georgia

George T. Bagby State Park is a [tpl]convert|700|acre|ha|-1|adj=on[/tpl] state park located in southwestern Georgia on the shore of Walter F. George Lake. The park offers a 60 room lodge, conference center, restaurant, cottages, and features the 18 hole Meadow Links Golf Course, as well as a marina and boat ramp. Visitors may search for wildlife on a three mile (5Â km) long nature trail, or fish or boat in Lake Walter F. George.
