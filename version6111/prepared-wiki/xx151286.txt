</ref> Following its European premiere, the Macedonian daily ''[[Utrinski vesnik (daily newspaper)|Utrinski Vesnik]]'' praised the film for its realism and imagery, but concluded by criticizing its emphasis on misery for being one-sided.<ref name="vesnik-23-nov-2004"/>
Critics have singled out Marko Kovacevic in his lead role for praise. His performance was lauded as "remarkable" by Variety and "superb" by The Hollywood Reporter, while The Village Voice commended him for "channeling gentle and ferocious with equal ease" in his depiction of the protagonist.
