[[Brandy for the Parson]]

CATEGORIES: 1952 films, 1950s romantic comedy films, British films, British criminal comedy films, British romantic comedy films, English-language films

Brandy for the Parson is a 1952 British comedy film directed by John Eldridge and starring Kenneth More, Charles Hawtrey, James Donald and Jean Lodge. A young couple get mixed up in a smuggling ring.http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0044442/ It was based on a novel by Geoffrey Household. The title is a reference to A Smugglers Song by Rudyard Kipling.
