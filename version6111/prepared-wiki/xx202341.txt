[[Remixes (Coldplay EP)]]

CATEGORIES: Coldplay EPs, 2003 remix albums, 2003 EPs, Remix EPs, Parlophone remix albums, Parlophone EPs, English-language EPs, English-language remix albums

Remixes was a 2003 remix EP released on 12 inch vinyl by English rock band Coldplay. It consists of three tracks recorded by the band with the Parlophone label. The original songs are all taken from their 2002 album A Rush of Blood to the Head. The release was limited to approximately 1000 copies and was released exclusively through the band's website.
