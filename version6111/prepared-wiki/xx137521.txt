[[KKIQ]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in the San Francisco Bay Area, Adult contemporary radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 1969, 1969 establishments in California

KKIQ is a commercial radio station located in Pleasanton, California, broadcasting to the Tri-Valley area on 101.7 FM.  KKIQ airs an adult contemporary music format.
KKIQ is owned by the Coast Radio group, which also owns KKDV 92.1 FM in Walnut Creek and KUIC 95.3 FM in Vacaville.
