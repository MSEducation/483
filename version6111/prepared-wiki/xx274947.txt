[[Elachista dispunctella]]

CATEGORIES: Elachistidae, Animals described in 1843, Butterflies and moths of Europe, Moths of Europe, Insects of Germany, Insects of Romania, Insects of Poland, Insects of Latvia, Insects of Hungary, Insects of Italy

Elachista dispunctella is a moth of the Elachistidae family. It is found in most of Europe, except most of the Balkan Peninsula, Fennoscandia, the Benelux, Portugal, Great Britain, Ireland and Iceland.
Adults are on wing from April to May and again from July to August.
The larvae feed on Festuca species,Lepidoptera.pl including Festuca ovina and Festuca duriuscula. They mine the blades of their host plant. Larvae can be found from April to May and again from June to July.[tpl]cite web|url= http://www.lepiforum.de/hist_lit/schuetze/Schuetze_1931_Seite_8-222.pdf|title=Lepiforum.de|format=PDF[/tpl]
