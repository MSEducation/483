Alistair Smith (born 8 August 1990) is an Australian rules footballer who played for the St Kilda Football Club in the Australian Football League (AFL). He currently plays for Perth in the West Australian Football League (WAFL).

==AFL career==

Alistair Smith was recruited from Perth in the 2008 AFL Draft by St Kilda, with their fourth round selection (number 62 overall).[tpl]cite web|title=Selection 62: Alistair Smith|url=http://www.afl.com.au/news/newsarticle/tabid/208/default.aspx?newsid=70074|publisher=Australian Football League|accessdate=31 August 2011|date=29 November 2008[/tpl]
Smith plays regularly with St Kilda's Victorian Football League (VFL) affiliate team Sandringham and spent 2009 and 2010 developing his career with the Zebras in the VFL.[tpl]cite web|last=George|first=Felicity|title=Smith working hard in VFL|url=http://www.saints.com.au/tabid/5315/default.aspx?newsid=94661|publisher=St Kilda Football Club|accessdate=31 August 2011|date=20 May 2010[/tpl]
