[[Jason Ross (writer)]]

CATEGORIES: American television writers, Living people, People from Chico, California, University of California, Santa Barbara alumni, Writers from California, Writers from New York City, People from Manhattan, 21st-century American writers

Jason Ross is an American writer. From 2002 to 2013[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.thedailyshow.com/watch/thu-june-27-2013/moment-of-zen---jason-ross |title=Moment of Zen, June 27, 2013 |work=The Daily Show with John Stewart[/tpl]  he was a staff writer at The Daily Show with Jon Stewart. As part of the writing team, he has received seven Primetime Emmy Awards.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/name/nm1413217/awards |title=Jason Ross (v) - Awards |work=Internet Movie Database[/tpl] He has occasionally performed on-camera as Muppet John McCain.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/name/nm1413217/ |title=Jason Ross (v) |work=Internet Movie Database[/tpl] He also helped to write that show's two best-selling books, America: The Book, (2004) and Earth: The Book (2010).  He attended University of California, Santa Barbara, where he served as editor of the student newspaper, The Daily Nexus.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.theawl.com/2013/03/daily-show-writer-jason-ross |title="Daily Show" Writer Jason Ross On Writing For Free and Breaking Into Comedy |work=The Awl[/tpl] He lives in Manhattan with his family.
