[[Eslamabad, Khodabandeh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Eslamabad ([tpl]lang-fa|اسلام آباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Eslāmābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|10153450|Eslamabad[/tpl] is a village in Sojas Rud Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 156, in 38 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
