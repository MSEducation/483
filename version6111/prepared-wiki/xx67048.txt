[[United Kingdom in the Eurovision Song Contest 1974]]

CATEGORIES: United Kingdom in the Eurovision Song Contest, 1974 in the United Kingdom, Countries in the Eurovision Song Contest 1974

The United Kingdom held a national preselection to choose the song that would go to the Eurovision Song Contest 1974. It was held on 23 February 1974 and presented by Jimmy Savile.[tpl]citation needed|date=October 2012[/tpl]

==Results==

"Long Live Love" won the national and went on to tie for 4th in the contest.
