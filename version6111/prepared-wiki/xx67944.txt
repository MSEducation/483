[[Jacqueline Harpman]]

CATEGORIES: 1929 births, 2012 deaths, Belgian psychologists, Prix Médicis winners

Jacqueline Harpman (5 July 1929 – 24 May 2012Jacqueline Harpman est décédée, LaLibre, 24 May 2012) was a Belgian writer who wrote in French.  She also worked as a psychoanalyst and lived in Etterbeek, Brussels.http://www.lamediatheque.be/the/auteurs_belges/auteurs/harpman_jacqueline_.html

==Personal Life==

Harpman was born on 5 July 1929, in Brussels, Belgium, and was later well known for her books written in French.

==Death==

Harpman died on 24 May 2012, in Brussels, Belgium, after having been severely ill for a long time.  She was 82.
