[[Oak Park railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1956

Oak Park railway station is located on the Craigieburn line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the northern Melbourne suburb of Oak Park opening on 13 August 1956.Oak Park Vicsig
The railway past the site of Oak Vale station was opened in 1872 as part of the North East railway to Wodonga.[tpl]cite book|last=Turton|first=Keith W|title=Six And A Half Inches From Destiny. The first hundred years of the Melbourne-Wodonga Railway 1873-1973|publisher=Australian Railway Historical Society|year=1973|isbn=0-85849-012-9|page=87[/tpl]
Boom barriers replaced hand gates at Devon Road in 1962.VR History by Andrew Waugh
