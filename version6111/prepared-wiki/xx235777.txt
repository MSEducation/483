[[Echecrates of Thessaly]]

CATEGORIES: Hellenistic Thessalians, Ptolemaic generals, Hellenistic generals, Ancient Thessalian generals

Echecrates was a Thessalian military officer of Ptolemy Philopator in the Fourth Syrian War with Antiochus the Great in 219 BC.  Echecrates was employed in the levying of troops and their arrangement into separate companies. He was entrusted with the command of the Greek forces in Ptolemy's pay, and of all the mercenary cavalry. According to Polybius, he did good service in the war, especially at the Battle of Raphia in 217 BC.Polybius. v. 63, 65, 82, 85
