[[Rohel Briceño]]

Rohel Briceño (born 15 March 1984) is an Venezuelan international footballer who plays for Caracas FC, as a defender.

==Career==

Briceño has played for Zamora FC and Caracas FC.[tpl]NFT player|pid=44511[/tpl]
He made his international debut for Venezuela in 2011.
