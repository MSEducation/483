[[Rafael E. Núñez]]

CATEGORIES: Cognitive scientists, Mathematical cognition researchers, American mathematicians, Living people, University of California, San Diego faculty, Year of birth missing (living people), 20th-century American writers, 21st-century American writers

Rafael E. Núñez is a professor of cognitive science at the University of California, San Diego and a proponent of embodied cognition.  He co-authored Where Mathematics Comes From with George Lakoff.
