[[Wath, Harrogate]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in North Yorkshire, Civil parishes in North Yorkshire

Wath is a village and civil parish  about 4 miles north of Ripon in the Harrogate district of North Yorkshire, England and has a population of 186.
