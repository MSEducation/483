[[Radon difluoride]]

CATEGORIES: Radon compounds, Fluorides

Radon difluoride ([tpl]chem|Rn||F|2[/tpl]) is a compound of radon, a noble gas.  Radon reacts readily with fluorine to form a solid compound, but this decomposes on attempted vaporization and its exact composition is uncertain.[tpl]cite journal|doi=10.1021/ja00880a048|year=1962|last1=Fields|first1=Paul R.|last2=Stein|first2=Lawrence|last3=Zirin|first3=Moshe H.|journal=Journal of the American Chemical Society|volume=84|issue=21|pages=4164[/tpl][ref]
