[[RJ Hadley]]

CATEGORIES: 1969 births, Living people, Dartmouth College alumni, People from Conyers, Georgia

RJ Hadley (born Rakeim Jerhal Hadley on January 14, 1969) is a Democratic Party activist and current Tax Commissioner of Rockdale County, Georgia.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.rockdalecounty.org/main.cfm?id=2795 |title=Rockdale County - Tax Commissioner's Office |accessdate=February 13, 2013 [/tpl] In 2008 he was a delegate to the Democratic National Convention representing Georgia.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.democraticpartyofgeorgia.org/election08/georgiadelegation |title=Georgia's Delegation To The Democratic National Convention |accessdate=December 31, 2010 [/tpl] He has been a member of the Rockdale County Democratic Party since 2006 and was elected to its executive board in 2010.
Hadley is a graduate of Dartmouth College. He has worked in the public sector as juvenile justice social worker for the State of New Jersey. He has also held software technology positions in the United States and abroad with the Federal Aviation Administration, Motorola, and Logica.[tpl]cite web |url=http://thedartmouth.com/2010/04/01/news/senate |title=Hadley seeks to oust Georgia Senate incumbent |date=April 1, 2010 |accessdate=December 31, 2010 [/tpl] In 2009, he was appointed Chief of Staff for Rockdale County.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.rockdalecounty.org/docs/News010609.pdf |title=RJ Hadley Tapped for Chief of Staff Position |accessdate=December 31, 2010 [/tpl] He served as the Chief Operating Officer for Affordable Housing CDC, a non-profit housing and economic development organization, until his election as Tax Commissioner in 2012.
In 2010, Hadley sought the Democratic Party's nomination for Senate in Georgia, losing to Labor Commissioner Mike Thurmond.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.ajc.com/news/georgia-politics-elections/democrat-could-make-history-552767.html |title=Democrat could make history in U.S. Senate race |date=June 18, 2010 |accessdate=December 31, 2010 [/tpl] On January 29, 2011, he was elected Vice-Chair of County Parties with the Democratic Party of Georgia.[tpl]cite web|title=Rockdale Dem wins state party seat|url=http://www.rockdalecitizen.com/home/headlines/Rockdale_Dem_wins_state_party_seat_114941879.html|accessdate=10 May 2011[/tpl]

==Personal life==

He is married to noted bird veterinarian Dr. Tarah Hadley of the Atlanta Hospital for Birds and Exotics. They met at Dartmouth, have 5 children, and live in Conyers, Georgia.

==Political Activism==

Hadley was an early volunteer for the Barack Obama presidential campaign when an Obama campaign office opened in Atlanta in 2007. He went on to volunteer for the Obama campaign throughout Georgia, South Carolina, North Carolina, and Texas.[tpl]cite web |url=http://timesenterprise.com/local/x966813550/Hadley-eager-to-serve |title=Hadley eager to serve |date=February 27, 2010 |accessdate=December 31, 2010 [/tpl] In 2008, he was chosen by the Obama campaign to serve as an At-Large delegate at the Democratic National Convention in Denver. He was also the campaign manager for Richard Oden who became Rockdale County’s first African-American Chairman in 2009.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.neighbornewspapers.com/stories/Local-residents-take-part-in-party-conventions,139954 |title=Local residents take part in party conventions |date=August 28, 2010 |accessdate=December 31, 2010 [/tpl]

==2010 U.S. Senate campaign==

In 2009, Hadley announced his campaign to seek the Democratic Party of Georgia’s nomination for United States Senate to replace Johnny Isakson, a Republican. For nearly a year he ran unopposed until Labor Commissioner Michael Thurmond entered the race in the last week of qualifying.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.romenews-tribune.com/view/full_story/7260526/article-Last-minute-qualifying-rounds-out-races |title=Last Minute Qualifying Rounds Out Races |accessdate=December 31, 2010 [/tpl] In the primary election held on July 20, 2010, Hadley garnered 15.7% of the popular vote.[tpl]cite web |url=http://sos.georgia.gov/elections/election_results/2010_0720/0010102.htm |title=Official Results of the Tuesday, July 20, 2010 General Primary Election |date=August 4, 2010 |accessdate=December 31, 2010 [/tpl]

==Rockdale County Tax Commissioner==

In 2012, Hadley was elected Tax Commissioner in Rockdale County. He defeated Republican incumbent Dan Ray with 51.73% of the vote.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.11alive.com/news/article/263302/1/Rockdale-County-Election-Results- |title=Rockdale County Election Results |accessdate=February 13, 2013 [/tpl]
