[[Brick Fleagle]]

CATEGORIES: 1906 births, Year of death unknown, African-American jazz guitarists

Brick Fleagle or Jacob Roger FleagleBrick Fleagle, AllMusic, accessed January 2011 (born 22 August 1906) was a jazz guitarist. He played during the Golden Age of jazz, performing with leading musicians including Miles Davis. At one time Fleagle had his own 16-piece band.Time Magazine, 1945.
