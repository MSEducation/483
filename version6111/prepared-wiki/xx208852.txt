[[Kiyonobu Murakami]]

CATEGORIES: 1907 births, Japanese boxers, Flyweight boxers, Olympic boxers of Japan, Boxers at the 1932 Summer Olympics, Possibly living people

[tpl]nihongo|Kiyonobu Murakami|村上清信[/tpl] (born December 24, 1907) was a Japanese boxer who competed in the 1932 Summer Olympics.
He was the national champion in the flyweight division for two years running in 1930–31, winning at the Japan Amateur Boxing Association's Japanese Amateur Boxing Championships."Amateur Record - All Japan Championship". Boxing Yearbook 2005 (Japan). Boxing Magazine Editor's Guide, Japan Boxing Commission / Japan Pro Boxing Association, April 30, 2005 ISBN 4-583 - 03849-6 , 201 pages.
