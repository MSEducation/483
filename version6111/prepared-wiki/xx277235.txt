[[Sowley Green]]

CATEGORIES: Hamlets in Suffolk

Sowley Green is a hamlet in the civil parish of Great Thurlow, in the St Edmundsbury District of the county of Suffolk, England. It is about two miles to the east of the village of Great Thurlow and about five miles to the north east of the market town of Haverhill. The hamlet sits just off the A134 road on 'Bury Road' which leads to the village of Great Thurlow.
