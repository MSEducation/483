[[Gunnar Helén]]

CATEGORIES: 1918 births, 2002 deaths, People from Vingåker Municipality, Liberal People's Party (Sweden) politicians, Swedish party leaders, County governors of Sweden, Uppsala University alumni

Gunnar Helén, (5 June 1918 Vingåker – 8 December 2002 Nacka) was a Swedish liberal politician. He was party leader of Liberal People's Party 1969-1975, Governor of Kronoberg County 1965-1970 and of Stockholm County 1977-1984.
