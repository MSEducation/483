[[47th Wisconsin Volunteer Infantry Regiment]]

CATEGORIES: Wisconsin Civil War regiments

The 47th Regiment Wisconsin Volunteer Infantry was an infantry regiment that served in the Union Army during the American Civil War. 

==Service==

The 47th Wisconsin was organized at Madison, Wisconsin and mustered into Federal service on February 27, 1865. 
The regiment was mustered out on September 4, 1865.

==Casualties==

The 47th Wisconsin suffered  39 enlisted men who died of disease, for a total of 39 fatalities.http://www.civilwararchive.com/Unreghst/unwiinf2.htm#47th
