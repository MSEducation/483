[[Thomas Maxwell Harris]]

CATEGORIES: English botanists, Fellows of the Royal Society, 1903 births, 1983 deaths, Members of the Royal Swedish Academy of Sciences, Alumni of the University of Cambridge, People educated at Bootham School

Thomas Maxwell Harris FRS[tpl]cite doi|10.1098/rsbm.1985.0009[/tpl] (born 8 January 1903; died 1983) was a British botanist. 
He was educated at Bootham School[tpl]cite book|first=Bootham Old Scholars Association |title=Bootham School Register|place=York, England|publisher=BOSA|year=2011|ref=harv[/tpl], York.
He received his doctorate from the University of Cambridge and was a Fellow of the Royal Society. He served as President of the Linnean Society of London from 1961 to 1964.
