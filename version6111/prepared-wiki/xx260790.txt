[[Indonesian Aerospace N-219]]

CATEGORIES: Proposed aircraft of Indonesia, Indonesian Aerospace aircraft

The Indonesian Aerospace N-219 is a new light transport aircraft designed by Indonesian Aerospace for multi-purpose missions in remote areas.[tpl]Cite web|url = http://www.airframer.com/aircraft_detail.html?program=1156|title = Indonesian Aerospace N-219|accessdate = 1 January 2011|last = Airframer|authorlink = |date=January 2011[/tpl]

==Design and development==

The N-219 was developed from the CASA C-212 Aviocar and, like that design, is also of all metal construction. It is claimed that it will have the largest cabin volume in its class, along with a flexible door system to allow a multi-purpose missions for transporting passengers and cargo. N-219 has been design to comply with FAR 23 (commuter category aircraft) and will be certified in 2013, with the first deliveries scheduled for 2014. Foreign certification will be pursued later. The initial price is forecast to be US$4 million each,[tpl]Cite news|url = http://finance.detik.com/read/2011/06/23/184046/1667341/1036/pesawat-baru-n-219-garapan-pt-di-dibandrol-us--4-juta|title = Pesawat Baru N-219 Garapan PT DI Dibandrol US$ 4 Juta |accessdate = 27 June 2011|last = Irawan|first = Ade|authorlink = |date=June 2011| work = detikFinance[/tpl] while the predicted development budget was about $30 million for  15 aircraft.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.mediaindonesia.com/read/2012/01/12/290822/21/2/PT-DI-Kembangkan-Pesawat-N-219- |title=PT DI Kembangkan Pesawat N-219 |date=January 12, 2012[/tpl]
Due to funding shortages loans for US$65M to develop the N-219 were procured from the $1B Qatar-Indonesia Joint Investment Fund in 2006.[tpl]Cite news|url = http://www.menafn.com/qn_news_story_s.asp?StoryId=1093116895|title = IAe to bid for finance from Qatar-Indonesia Fund|accessdate = 1 January 2011|last = Menafn |authorlink = |date=December 2010| work = The Peninsula[/tpl]
In June 2011, the Industry Minister requested that the Indonesian government provide Rp.59 billion (US$6.9M) to complete the N-219 prototype. Budi Darmadi, Director General of the Industry Ministry's Advanced Technology-Based Seed Industry indicated he believed that the aircraft has good market potential, due to the age of most other aircraft in its class.[tpl]Cite news|url = http://en.indonesiafinancetoday.com/read/6423/Government-Establishes-N-219-Development-Fund|title = Government Establishes N-219 Development Fund|accessdate = 9 June 2011|last = Hapsari|first =  Rukmi and Adi Teguh|authorlink = |date=June 2011| work = Indonesia Finance Today[/tpl]PT DI Siap Ciptakan Pesawat N-219, 'Burung Besi' Asli RI
The company has received an order of 20 units N-219 from Merpati Nusantara Airlines to replace its older aircraft such as Twin Otter, with deliveries expected in 2015.
