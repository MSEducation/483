[[Khun Wichitmatra]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing, Year of death missing, Thai composers

Khun Wichitmatra (Sanga Kanchanakphan) ([tpl]lang-th|ขุนวิจิตรมาตรา (สง่า กาญจนาคพันธุ์)[/tpl]; [tpl]RTGS|Khun Wichitmattra (Sa-nga Kanchanakhaphan[/tpl]) wrote the lyrics of the Thai National Anthem which was played for the first time in July, 1932.  The lyrics were subsequently rewritten two years later by Chan Kamwilai.
