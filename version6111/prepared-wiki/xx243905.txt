[[Allerton Junction]]

CATEGORIES: Rail transport in Merseyside, Rail junctions in Great Britain

Allerton Junction is an at grade junction just east of Liverpool South Parkway, Allerton, Merseyside. It takes its name from the former station Allerton.
The junction is where the Liverpool to Manchester line branches off the Liverpool branch of the West Coast Main Line. Services operating over the junction are East Midlands Trains, First TransPennine Express, London Midland, Northern Rail and Virgin Trains.
