[[Fen (name)]]

CATEGORIES: Feminine given names, Chinese given names

Fen may be either a Chinese female given name or family name.  As a given name, it is represented in simplified Chinese as 芬.
Fen may also be a surname in countries other than China, such as Uzbekistan where it is represented in Cyrilic as Фен.
Fen or Fenna is also an unrelated Frisian name.http://www.behindthename.com/submit/name/fen
