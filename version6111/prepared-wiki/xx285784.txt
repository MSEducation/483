[[Witan Pacific Investment Trust]]

CATEGORIES: Investment trusts of the United Kingdom

Witan Pacific Investment Trust plc (Witan Pacific) is a multi-managed investment trust offering a portfolio of equity investments in the Asia Pacific region including Japan. Witan Pacific is listed on the London Stock Exchange.

==Operations==

Witan Pacific invests in companies operating across the Pan-Asian region, including Japan, China, Australia and India. Its portfolio is actively managed by three managers, with Witan Investment Services acting as executive manager to manage and monitor the outsourced structure and relationships and to assist the board on investment strategy and marketing.
