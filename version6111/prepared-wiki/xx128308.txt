[[Brought Back to Life]]

CATEGORIES: Nekromantix albums, 1994 albums

Brought Back to Life is the third album by the Danish psychobilly band the Nekromantix, released in 1994 by Intermusic Records. It was the band's first album to include drummer Tim Kristensen (credited as Grim Tim Handsome) and only album to include guitarist Jan Daggry (credited as Ian Dawn), replacing original members Peter Sandorff and Peek who had left the band. Brought Back to Life earned a Grammy Award nomination for "Best Heavy Metal Album."[ref]
