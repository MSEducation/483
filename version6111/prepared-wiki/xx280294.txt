[[Bedroom Community]]

CATEGORIES: Icelandic record labels, Record labels established in 2006

Bedroom Community is an Icelandic record label/collective which was created in 2006 by producer Valgeir Sigurðsson,http://www.cyclicdefrost.com/blog/2012/08/a-bedroom-community-in-reykjavik-interview-with-ben-frost-and-valgeir-sigurdsson/ alongside fellow artists Nico Muhlyhttp://www.newyorker.com/online/blogs/culture/2013/10/nico-muhlys-new-ideas.html and Ben Frost,http://markfisherreblog.tumblr.com/post/17036871044/ben-frost-interview-full-textlater adding more artists to the roster. Based on the outskirts of Reykjavík, Iceland, Bedroom Community is also the home of Greenhouse Studios.
