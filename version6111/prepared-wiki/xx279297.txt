[[Lolitawork Libretto]]

CATEGORIES: 2010 albums, Neoclassical albums

[tpl]nihongo|"Lolitawork Libretto"|少女仕掛けのリブレット|Shoujo Jikake no Riburetto[/tpl] is the second studio album by singer and cellist Kanon Wakeshima.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.sonymusic.co.jp/Music/Arch/DF/kanonwakeshima/m_disco.html |title=ディスコグラフィ |publisher=Sony Music Online |language=Japanese |accessdate=2012-01-05 [/tpl] The song "Toumei no Kagi" was released as a digital download prior to the release of the album on September 16, 2009. The song was used as the theme song of the online game Avalon no Kagi. A promotional music video for the song "Lolitawork Libretto ~Storytelling by Solita~" was also released prior to the album.
The official full title of the album is "Lolitawork Libretto: A Libretto on What Makes a Girl Work".
