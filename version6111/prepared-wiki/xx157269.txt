[[Super Cup of Bosnia and Herzegovina]]

CATEGORIES: Football cup competitions in Bosnia and Herzegovina, National association football supercups

The Super cup of Bosnia-Hezegovina was a former football competition. It was contested between the league champion and the cup winner of the previous season. It was first contested in the 1996–97 season and has not been played since the 2000–01 season.
