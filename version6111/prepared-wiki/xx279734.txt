A.S.D. Romagna Centro is an Italian football club based in Martorano, a frazione of Cesena, Emilia-Romagna. Currently it plays in Italy's Serie D.

==History==

===Foundation===

The club was founded in 2002.http://www.polisportivamartorano.it/

===Serie D===

In the season 2012-13 the team was promoted for the first time, from Eccellenza Emilia-Romagna/B to Serie D.

==Colors and badge==

The team's colors are lightblue and black.
