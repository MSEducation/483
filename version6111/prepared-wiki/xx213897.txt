[[Kei flying fox]]

CATEGORIES: Pteropus, Animals described in 1867

The Kei flying fox (Pteropus keyensis) is a species of megabat in the Pteropus genus found in the Kai Islands of Indonesia. It was formerly considered a subspecies of the black-bearded flying fox.D.E. Wilson & D.M. Reeder, 2005: Mammal Species of the World: A Taxonomic and Geographic Reference. Third Edition. The Johns Hopkins University Press, Baltimore The species is sometimes classified as a subspecies of Pteropus melanopogon. Very little is known about the species, its habitat, or threats to it.[tpl]IUCN|id = 136528 | taxon = Pteropus keyensis | assessors = Bates, P. & Bumrungsri, S. | assessment_year = 2008 | version = 3.1 | accessdate = 24 December 2013[/tpl]
