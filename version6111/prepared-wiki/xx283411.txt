[[The Legend of Buddha]]

CATEGORIES: English-language films, 2004 films, Indian animated films

The Legend of Buddha is an Indian animated film directed by Shamboo Falke, produced by Stalin Saravanan and written by Caarthick Raju and Ramesh Karthikeyan.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.buddhistchannel.tv/index.php?id=12,447,0,0,1,0#.UgcrkaxlOpo|title=Movie: "The Legend of Buddha"|publisher=Buddhist Channel|date=2011-05-30|accessdate=4 June 2011[/tpl] The United States dub was released by Walt Disney Pictures and stars the voices of Dwayne Tan and Bridgit Mendler.
