[[1st Indian Cavalry Brigade]]

CATEGORIES: Cavalry brigades of the British Indian Army, Brigades of India in World War I, Brigades of India in World War II

The 1st Indian Cavalry Brigade or the 1st (Risalpur) Cavalry Brigade was a cavalry formation of the Indian Army during World War I and during World War II.
It was formed on the North West Frontier in September 1939, and converted to the 1st Indian Motor Brigade in January 1940.[tpl]cite web|accessdate=2009-10-19|publisher=Order of Battle|title=1 Indian Cavalry Brigade|url=http://www.ordersofbattle.com/UnitData.aspx?UniX=10075&Tab=Uhi[/tpl]
