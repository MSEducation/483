[[Bodine Koehler]]

CATEGORIES: 1992 births, Living people, Dutch emigrants to Puerto Rico, Puerto Rican beauty pageant winners, Puerto Rican people of Dutch descent, Puerto Rican people of Dominican Republic descent, Dutch people of Dominican Republic descent

Bodine Koehler Peña (born September 30, 1992 in Amsterdam) is a Dutch-born Puerto Rican model and beauty pageant titleholder.  She represented Río Grande municipality at Miss Universe Puerto Rico 2012.

==Early life==

Bodine was on born September 30, 1992 in Amsterdam, Netherlands to a Dutch father and a Dominican mother. Koehler moved to Puerto Rico when she was 8 years old and was raised in San Juan. She speaks fleunt Spanish, Dutch, and English and is one of the most well-known models in the Island.
She is the second cousin of Miss World 1959, Corine Rottschäfer.http://www.elnuevodia.com/bodinekoehlerseguradesimisma-1124630.html

==Miss Universe Puerto Rico 2012==

On November 7, 2011 Bodine represented Río Grande at Miss Universe Puerto Rico 2012 and beat out 38 other contestants for the title, gaining the right to represent Puerto Rico at Miss Universe. 

===Controversy===

During her reign, Koehler and the Miss Universe Puerto Rico Organization were ridiculed for the fact that Bodine was of Dominican Republic and Dutch descent, not Puerto Rican. Pageant rules state that in order to compete participants must be citizens of Puerto Rico who have lived there for at least six months. ==Miss Universe 2012==

Koehler represented Puerto Rico in the Miss Universe 2012 competition. Although considered  a big favorite and a front runner, she failed to place in the Top 16.
