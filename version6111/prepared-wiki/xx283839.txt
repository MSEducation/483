[[Christos Stergioglou]]

CATEGORIES: 1952 births, Living people, Greek male film actors, People from Didymoteicho

Christos Stergioglou (born 1952) is a Greek actor. He won the award for Best Actor at the 2002 Thessaloniki International Film Festival.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.athenscasting.gr/card.php?id=20 |title=Athens Casting |accessdate=2013-08-11 |work=athenscasting[/tpl] He has appeared in more than 20 films since 1981.
