[[Tateshina, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Tateshina|立科町|Tateshina-machi[/tpl] is a town located in Kitasaku District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the town has an estimated population of 8,492 and a density of 127.09 persons per km². The total area is 66.82 km².
Tateshina is famous for its apple orchards.
Tateshina is sister city to Oregon City, Oregon, in the United States.
