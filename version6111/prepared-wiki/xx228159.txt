[[Shangdu County]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia, Ulanqab

Shangdu County (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠱᠠᠩᠳᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠰᠢᠶᠠᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Šaŋdu siyan[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=商都县 |t=商都縣 |p=Shāngdū Xiàn[/tpl]) is a county of south-central  Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China. It is under the administration of Ulanqab City and has an area of [tpl]convert|4353|km2[/tpl], and in 2004 had about 330,000 inhabitants.
