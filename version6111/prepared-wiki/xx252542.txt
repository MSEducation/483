[[Taïna Barioz]]

CATEGORIES: French alpine skiers, Living people, 1988 births, Alpine skiers at the 2010 Winter Olympics, Olympic alpine skiers of France

Taïna Barioz (born 2 June 1988 in Papeete, Tahiti)FIS-Ski - biographie is a French alpine skier. 
Barioz won one podium in Alpine Skiing World Cup, 3rd place in giant slalom in Lienz, Austria (December 2009).
Barioz also placed 1st in the 2014 FIS NorAms.
