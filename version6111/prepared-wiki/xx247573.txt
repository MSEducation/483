[[1993 European Figure Skating Championships]]

CATEGORIES: 1993 in Finnish sport, 1993 in figure skating, European Figure Skating Championships by year, Figure skating in Finland

The 1993 European Figure Skating Championships was a senior-level international competition held in Helsinki, Finland. Elite skaters from European ISU member nations competed in the disciplines of men's singles, ladies' singles, pair skating, and ice dancing.
