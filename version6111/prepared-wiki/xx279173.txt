[[Giovanni Conti (painter)]]

CATEGORIES: 1909 deaths, 19th-century Italian painters, 20th-century Italian painters, Year of birth unknown

Giovanni Conti (died April 9, 1909) was an Italian painter, mainly of sacred subjects.
He was a pupil of Luigi Pastore in Aversa. He painted Trumpet of the Last Judgement, (1865), Resurrection of Lazarus (1869), and Death of Abel (1869).Exhibition of Artisti dell'agro aversano tra Ottocento e primo Novecento (1790-1922), Biographies by Franco Pezzella. He participated in Neapolitan Promotrice exhibitions.
