[[Crown Game Preserve]]

CATEGORIES: Natural areas in Ontario

A Crown Game Preserve is an officially designated natural area in Ontario, Canada, "primarily for the purpose of sustaining populations of game animals".[tpl]cite web|url=http://nhic.mnr.gov.on.ca/MNR/nhic/glossary/area_types.cfm|title=Ontario Natural Area Types|publisher=Government of Ontario Ministry of Natural Resources|accessdate=2007-08-01[/tpl] Hunting is not permitted or strictly regulated, making it akin to a wildlife refuge. Crown Game Preserves are managed by the Government of Ontario Ministry of Natural Resources.
