[[FC Dornbirn 1913]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Austria, Association football clubs established in 1913, 1913 establishments in Austria

FC Dornbirn 1913 is an Austrian association football club based in Dornbirn, Vorarlberg.
He is currently playing in the Regionalliga West,which is the 3rd level in Austrian football. For many years FC Dornbirn played in the second level of professional football.1970/71 they played in the Nationalliga, the highest class in football at this time.

==History==

The club was founded on 12 March in an inn in Dornbirn (Gasthaus zur Flur).

Currently there are 230 players in 17 teams.
