[[Faith and The Law]]

CATEGORIES: Local talk shows in the United States, Religious television series, Christian television, Legal television series, 2009 American television series debuts

Faith and The Law is a television series hosted by Tim Rowe airing Wednesdays at 12 noon ET on WHMB-TV Channel 40 in Indianapolis, Indiana.  Faith and The Law debuted on Wednesday October 7, 2009.
Faith and The Law is about legal issues and Christians. It reaches out to help all people get through the maze of the American legal system. Jill Savage is the co-host. 
The hosts discuss issues of importance to Christians and also offers a helping hand to those who may need some assistance with a legal matter.
