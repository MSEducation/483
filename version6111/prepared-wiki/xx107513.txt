[[Amha Records]]

CATEGORIES: Companies of Ethiopia, Ethiopian record labels, Record labels established in 1969, Record labels disestablished in 1975

Amha Records was an Ethiopian record label founded by Amha Mulatu. The company released 103 singles and 12 albums between 1969 and 1975. 
Prominent singers and musicians who recorded for the label included Alemayehu Eshete, Mahmoud Ahmed, Mulatu Astatke and Tilahun Gessesse.
