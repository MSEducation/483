[[Alfredo Moser]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Brazilian inventors, People from Minas Gerais, Year of birth missing (living people)

Alfredo Moser (Uberaba) is a Brazilian mechanic and inventor.

==Biography==

Born in Uberaba, Minas Gerais in Brazil. He married Carmelinda in 1978. He is known for the Lamp Moser.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.cromo.com.uy/2013/08/el-thomas-edison-de-los-pobres/|title =El Thomas Edison de los pobres |author =Cromo | date=20 August 2013 |accessdate = 5 September 2013|language = Spanish[/tpl]

===Lamp Moser===

Moser invented a lamp in 2002 with a plastic bottle, water and bleach, which works by refraction of sunlight. The intensity of the Lamp Moser is approximately 60 voltios (watts) and emits no CO2.[tpl]cite web |url=http://spanish.china.org.cn/photos/txt/2013-08/15/content_29727469.htm|title = Gran invento de bombilla que triunfa en suburbios de todo el planeta |author =Spanish China | date= 15 August 2013|accessdate =5 September 2013|language = Spanish[/tpl] Are easily constructed and installed.[tpl]cite web |url= http://www.concienciaeco.com/2013/08/18/diy-como-hacer-luz-con-una-botella-de-plastico/|title =Cómo hacer luz con una botella de plástico |author = Conciencia Eco | date= 18 de August 2013|accessdate =5 September 2013|language = Spanish[/tpl]
