[[Mordacia lapicida]]

CATEGORIES: Mordacia, Fish of the Pacific Ocean, Fish of Chile, Endemic fauna of Chile

[tpl]italic title[/tpl]

Mordacia lapicida, also known as the Chilean Lamprey, is a species of southern topeyed lamprey endemic to southern Chile where it inhabits rivers.  This species is anadromous.
