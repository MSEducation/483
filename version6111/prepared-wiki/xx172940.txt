[[FC Pocheon]]

CATEGORIES: Challengers League clubs, Sport in Gyeonggi Province, Pocheon, Association football clubs established in 2007, 2007 establishments in South Korea

Football Club Pocheon is a South Korean football club based in the city of Pocheon. It is a member of the Challengers League, an amateur league and the third tier of league football in South Korea.
2008 will be its first season of participation in the Korean football league setup.
