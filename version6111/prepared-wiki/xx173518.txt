[[Energy Economics (journal)]]

CATEGORIES: Economics journals, Elsevier academic journals, Energy and fuel journals, Energy economics, Publications established in 1979

Energy Economics is a scientific journal published by Elsevier under its "North Holland" imprint. The journal publishes research papers concerned with the economic and econometric modelling and analysis of energy systems and issues. The Journal of Energy Finance and Development was incorporated into Energy Economics in 1999.JEFD at ScienceDirect
