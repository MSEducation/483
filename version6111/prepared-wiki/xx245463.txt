[[Normand D'Amour]]

CATEGORIES: 1962 births, Living people, Canadian male film actors, Canadian male television actors

Normand D'Amour (born September 22, 1962) is an award winning Canadian actor. D'Amour won a Jutra Award for his supporting role in Tout est parfait as well as a Genie Award nomination for the same role. D'Amour has also appeared in other works including Cheech, Maman Last Call and Looking for Alexander.[tpl]IMDb name|id=0195197[/tpl]
