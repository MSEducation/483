[[Panasonic Lumix DMC-GX7]]

CATEGORIES: Panasonic Lumix cameras, Micro Four Thirds system, Live-preview digital cameras

The Panasonic Lumix DMC-GX7 announced in August 2013, is a Micro Four Thirds compact mirrorless interchangeable lens camera. 
It was Panasonic's first Micro Four Thirds camera with a built-in in body stabilization system (IBIS) and has built-in EVF (not optional anymore).[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.trustedreviews.com/panasonic-lumix-gx7_Digital-Camera_review |title=Panasonic Lumix GX7 review |accessdate=January 9, 2014[/tpl] Panasonic only uses 2 -axis stabilization and behind 5-axis stabilization, resulting in blurriness starting at 1/5 of a second.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.bestmirrorlesscamerareviews.com/2013/10/06/panasonic-lumix-gx7-vs-olympus-pen-e-p5-how-does-low-light-performance-compare/ |title=Panasonic Lumix GX7 vs. Olympus Pen E-P5: How does low-light performance compare? |author=Mathieu |accessdate=February 8, 2014[/tpl]
