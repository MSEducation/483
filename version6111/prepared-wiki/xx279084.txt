[[Mian Darreh, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Mian Darreh ([tpl]lang-fa|مياندره[/tpl], also Romanized as Mīān Darreh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795193|Mian Darreh[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 116, in 24 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
