[[Italian Formula Renault Championship]]

CATEGORIES: Formula Renault 2.0 series, Auto racing series in Italy, Recurring sporting events established in 2000, Recurring events disestablished in 2012, Defunct auto racing series

Formula Renault 2.0 Italia is a Formula Renault 2.0 racing series that is based in Italy.  The series races mostly on Italian races but it has regular races at Belgium's Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps and in Spain.
Since the year 2000, several Formula One drivers have raced in this series, including Felipe Massa, Kamui Kobayashi, Robert Kubica and Pastor Maldonado.
