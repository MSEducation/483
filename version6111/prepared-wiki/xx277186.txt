[[Thomas Chippenham (fl.1388-1402)]]

CATEGORIES: 14th-century births, 15th-century deaths, 14th-century English people, People from Hereford, Members of the Parliament of England (pre-1707)

Thomas Chippenham of Hereford, (fl. 1388-1402) was an English politician.

==Family==

Chippenham had three sons who were all MPs, Henry, Nicholas and Thomas.

==Career==

He was a Member (MP) of the Parliament of England for Hereford in February 1388 and 1402.http://www.historyofparliamentonline.org/volume/1386-1421/member/chippenham-thomas-i
