[[Tolkunbek Hudaybergenov]]

Tolkunbek Hudaybergenov (born January 4, 1986) is a Turkmen weightlifter. He was born in the Gurbansoltan Eje district, Daşoguz Province.[tpl]cite web|title=Turkmen sportsmen drop out of Olympic Games in Beijing|publisher=TURKMENISTAN.RU|url=http://www.turkmenistan.ru/?page_id=3&lang_id=en&elem_id=13431&type=event&sort=date_desc|date=2008-08-18|accessdate=2008-08-22[/tpl]
At the 2008 Asian Championships he won the bronze medal in the clean and jerk in the 62 kg category, overall ranking 4th with a total of [tpl]convert|284|kg[/tpl].[tpl]cite web|title=HUDAYBERGENOV Tolkunbek|publisher=International Weightlifting Federation|url=http://www.iwf.net/results/results_athlete_e.php?compid=2225[/tpl]
He competed in Weightlifting at the 2008 Summer Olympics in the 62 kg division finishing seventh, with [tpl]convert|288|kg[/tpl], beating his previous personal best by [tpl]convert|4|kg[/tpl].
He is [tpl]convert|5|ft|3|in|sigfig=3[/tpl] tall and weighs [tpl]convert|143|lb[/tpl].
