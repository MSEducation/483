[[WBCP]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Illinois, Rantoul, Illinois

WBCP (1580 AM) is a radio station broadcasting an Urban Adult Contemporary music format. It is licensed to  Urbana, Illinois and broadcasts to the Champaign, Illinois area. The station established in 1989 and is currently owned by P & C Enterprises, Inc. Despite its solid signal throughout the Campaign area, WBCP has the distinction of being the radio station with the lowest daytime power of any licensed AM station in the USA.
