[[Zettabyte]]

CATEGORIES: Units of information

The zettabyte is a multiple of the unit byte for digital information. The prefix zetta indicates multiplication by the seventh power of 1000 or 1021 in the International System of Units (SI). Therefore one zettabyte is one sextillion (one long scale trilliard) bytes.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fierceenterprisecommunications.com/story/zettabyte-flood-predicted-2015/2008-01-31|title=Zettabyte flood predicted for 2015|author=Tom Burton|date=2008-01-31[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=https://www.computerworld.com/s/article/9012364/A_zettabyte_by_2010_Corporate_data_grows_fiftyfold_in_three_years|title=A zettabyte by 2010: Corporate data grows fiftyfold in three years|date=2007-03-06|author=Lucas Mearian|publisher=Computerworld[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=https://www.computerworld.com/s/article/9067639/Study_Digital_universe_and_its_impact_bigger_than_we_thought|title=Study: Digital universe and its impact bigger than we thought|author=Lucas Mearian|date=2008-03-11|publisher=Computerworld[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.circleid.com/posts/813110_internet_traffic_graph_zettabyte/|title=Internet Traffic to Reach a Zettabyte by 2015, Says Study|date=2008-01-31[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.discovery.org/a/4428|title=The Impact of Video and Rich Media on the Internet – A ‘zettabyte’ by 2015?|date=2008-01-29|author=Bret Swanson & George Gilder|publisher=Discovery Institute[/tpl] The unit symbol is ZB.
A related unit, the zebibyte (ZiB), using a binary prefix, means  [tpl]gaps|10247|bytes[/tpl].

==Comparisons for scale==

The combined space of all computer hard drives in the world was estimated at approximately 160 exabytes in 2006.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.emc.com/collateral/analyst-reports/expanding-digital-idc-white-paper.pdf|title=An IDC White Paper: The Expanding Digital Universe|publisher=EMC|date=March 2007|author=John F. Gantz|format=PDF[/tpl] As of 2009, the entire World Wide Web was estimated to contain close to 500 exabytes.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/business/2009/may/18/digital-content-expansion|title=Internet data heads for 500bn gigabytes|date=2009-05-18|author=Richard Wray|publisher=The Guardian[/tpl] This is one half zettabyte. This has increased rapidly however, as Seagate Technology reported selling 330 exabytes worth of hard drives during the 2011 Fiscal Year.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.tomshardware.com/news/seagate-hdd-gigabyte-terabyte-quarter-result,13118.html|title=The Average HDD is Now 590 GB in Capacity|author=Douglas Perry|date=2011-07-22|publisher=Tom's Hardware[/tpl]
As of 2013, the World Wide Web is estimated to have reached 4 Zettabytes.[tpl]cite web|url=http://vsatglobalseriesblog.wordpress.com/2013/06/21/in-2013-the-amount-of-data-generated-worldwide-will-reach-four-zettabytes/|title=In 2013 the amount of data generated worldwide will reach four zettabytes|date=2013-06-21|author=Richard Currier[/tpl]
