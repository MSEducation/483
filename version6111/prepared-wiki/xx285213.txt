[[The Surface]]

CATEGORIES: Upcoming films, Films shot in Wisconsin, 2010s drama films, 2010s thriller films, American drama films, American thriller films, Films directed by Gil Cates Jr., American films

The Surface is an upcoming American drama thriller film directed by Gil Cates Jr. and written by Jeff Gendelman. The film stars Sean Astin, Mimi Rogers and Chris Mulkey.

==Cast==

==Production==

On July 22, 2013 Sean Astin and Mimi Rogers joined the cast of the film, Gil Cates Jr. is directing the film and Jeff Gendelman wrote the script for thriller film.[tpl]cite news|title=Sean Astin, Chris Mulkey to Star in ‘The Surface’|url=http://variety.com/2013/film/news/sean-astin-chris-mulkey-to-star-in-the-surface-exclusive-1200566397/|accessdate=12 August 2013|newspaper=variety.com|date=22 July 2013[/tpl] Mimi Rogers also joined the cast on 7 August in the Good Notes Productions financing film.[tpl]cite news|title=Mimi Rogers Set For Thriller ‘The Surface’|url=http://www.deadline.com/2013/08/mimi-rogers-set-for-thriller-the-surface/|accessdate=12 August 2013|newspaper=deadline.com|date=7 August 2013[/tpl]
