[[62]]

CATEGORIES: 62

Year 62 (LXII) was a common year starting on Friday (link will display the full calendar) of the Julian calendar. At the time, it was known as the Year of the Consulship of Marius and Asinius[tpl]dn|date=December 2013[/tpl] (or, less frequently, year 815 ''Ab urbe condita''). The denomination 62 for this year has been used since the early medieval period, when the Anno Domini calendar era became the prevalent method in Europe for naming years.
