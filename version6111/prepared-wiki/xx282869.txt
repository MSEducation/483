[[Harramiz Soares]]

CATEGORIES: 1990 births, Living people, São Tomé and Príncipe footballers, Association football forwards, S.L. Benfica footballers, Segunda Liga players, São Tomé and Príncipe expatriate footballers, São Tomé and Príncipe expatriates in Portugal, Expatriate footballers in Portugal

Harramiz Quieta Ferreira Soares (born 3 August 1990) is a Santoméan footballer who plays for Benfica B, as a forward.

==Football career==

After playing most of his senior career in lower divisions, Harramiz joined Benfica in June 2013 for a fee of 15.000€.[tpl]cite web|title=Médio Harramiz custou 15 mil euros|url=http://www.abola.pt/nnh/ver.aspx?id=405957|work=abola.pt|date=2 June 2013[/tpl] 
On 10 August 2013, Harramiz made his professional debut with Benfica B in a 2013–14 Segunda Liga game against Trofense.[tpl]cite web|title=Trofense 0 - 0 Benfica B|url=http://www.lpfp.pt/segunda_liga/pages/jogo.aspx?epoca=20132014&jornada=1&jogo=8342|work=LPFP|date=10 August 2013[/tpl]
