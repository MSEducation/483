[[Matteo Trini]]

CATEGORIES: Italian footballers, Juventus F.C. players, S.S. Juve Stabia players, F.C. Südtirol players, A.C. Lumezzane players, Association football goalkeepers, People from Sassari, 1987 births, Living people

Matteo Trini (born 18 May 1987) is an Italian footballer who plays for Carpi.

==Biography==

===Juventus and early career===

Born in Sassari, Sardinia, Trini started his career at Piedmont team Juventus. He played for Berretti under-18 team in 2004–05 season.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.lega-pro.com/it/Comunicati/Comunic2005/Berretti/29%20TB.pdf|title=Comunicato Ufficiale n.29/TB (2004–05)|date=22 December 2004|accessdate=25 September 2011|work=Campionato Berretti, Lega Serie C|language=Italian|format=[tpl]PDFlink|​|45.51 KB[/tpl] [/tpl] That season Juventus was the champion of wild card group (Berretti team of Serie A, B & D clubs). Trini also played a few games in Primavera under-20 team that season, as understudy of Nicola Avitabile. In 2005–06 season he played for hometown club Alghero at Serie D (located in the Province of Sassari). He returned to Juventus after the team relegated to Serie B, wearing no.45 of the first team.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.legaseriea.it/c/document_library/get_file?uuid=211368c7-617f-4ebd-b1a6-ed91da401f4b&groupId=10192|title=Comunicato Stampa n.50 (2006–07)|date=26 November 2006|accessdate=25 September 2011|work=Lega Calcio|language=Italian|format=[tpl]PDFlink|​|109.07 KB[/tpl] [/tpl] Juve promoted back to Serie A at the end of season.
Trini also became the starting keeperfullsoccer stats. of Primavera under-20 team, ahead Giorgio Merlano and Andrea Pozzato.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.juventus.com/it/news/detail.aspx?lml_language_id=0&trs_id=1202000&ID=4310|title=Primavera|date=7 October 2006|accessdate=25 September 2011|work=Juventus FC|language=Italian|archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20070305164337/http://www.juventus.com/it/news/detail.aspx?lml_language_id=0&trs_id=1202000&ID=4310|archivedate=5 March 2007[/tpl] He was the starting keeper in the playoffs, losing to Sampdoria in quarter-finals (eventually Samp was the runner-up).[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.pescaracalcio.com/giovanili/archivio-news/1481-alla-primavera-non-riesce-limpresa.html|title=Alla Primavera non riesce l'impresa|date=25 April 2007|accessdate=13 December 2011|work=Pescara Calcio|language=Italian[/tpl]Juventus Sampdoria

===Lega Pro clubs===

Trini then left for Juve Stabia in co-ownership deal, along with Stefano Di Berardino and Giuseppe Rizza. He played 13 times in Serie C1 and in June 2008 returned to Turin for €5,000,[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.juventus.com/site/eng/NEWS_newseventi_C99B7E4DD6914260A814121EB8F68271.asp|title=Transfer market: joint-ownerships settled yesterday |date=26 June 2008|accessdate=25 September 2011|work=Juventus FC|archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20080708134106/http://www.juventus.com/site/eng/NEWS_newseventi_C99B7E4DD6914260A814121EB8F68271.asp|archivedate=8 July 2008[/tpl] signing a 2-year contract.
On 16 July he left for South Tyrol,[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.fc-suedtirol.com/it/informazione/news-detail.asp?lProductID=129489|title=Matteo Trini nuovo portiere dell´FC Südtirol-Alto Adige|date=16 July 2008|accessdate=25 September 2011|work=FC Südtirol|language=Italian[/tpl] which he played 33 out of 34 games. He also played in the relegation "play-out", made the club remained in Seconda Divisione after winning Valenzana in 4–2.
In July 2009 he left for Lumezzane in another co-ownership deal (also extended his contract with Juve before the transfer).[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.tuttomercatoweb.com/juventus/?action=read&idnet=dHV0dG9qdXZlLmNvbS02Njc2|title=JUVE, il promettente Trini in comproprietà al Lumezzane|date=10 July 2009|accessdate=25 September 2011|work=Tutto Mercato Web|language=Italian[/tpl] In the first season he was the understudy of Massimo Gazzoli and in the next season played 30 out of possible 34 games. The rest of the game was played by Gianluca Di Gennaro. In June 2011 Juventus bought back Trini again.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.aclumezzane.it/news.php?id=694|title=Sevieri rinnova. Trini torna alla Juventus|date=24 June 2011|accessdate=4 July 2011|work=AC Lumezzane|language=Italian[/tpl] However, he failed to find a new club.
In July 2012 he was trained with Livorno but failed to sign a contract.
In November 2012 Trini left Juventus after his contract was terminated by mutual consent.[tpl]cite news|url = http://www.tuttojuve.com/?action=read&idnotizia=122164[/tpl] On 1 December 2012 he joined Carpi.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.carpifc1909.it/wp/?p=23830|title=TESSERATO TRINI|date=1 December 2012|accessdate=11 February 2013|publisher=Carpi FC 1909|language=Italian[/tpl]
