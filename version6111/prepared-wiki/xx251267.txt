[[Tony Chévez]]

CATEGORIES: 1954 births, Living people, Baltimore Orioles players, Charlotte O's players, Major League Baseball players from Nicaragua, Miami Orioles players, Rochester Red Wings players

Silvio Antonio Aguilera Chévez (born June 20, 1954, in Telica, Nicaragua) is a former Nicaraguan professional baseball player. He began his career in 1974 when he played for the Miami Orioles in the minors. In 1976, he signed with the Charlotte O's. In 1977, Chévez was signed by the Major League Baseball's Baltimore Orioles.http://www.thebaseballcube.com/players/C/Tony-Chevez.shtml During his short career in the MLB, he played in 4 games. After his career in the majors, Chévez went back to play in the minors.
