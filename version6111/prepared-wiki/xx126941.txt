[[Lakereia]]

Lakereia ([tpl]lang-el|Λακέρεια[/tpl]) is a former municipality in the Larissa regional unit, Thessaly, Greece. Since the 2011 local government reform it is part of the municipality Agia, of which it is a municipal unit.Kallikratis law Greece Ministry of Interior [tpl]el icon[/tpl] Population 1,481 (2011). The seat of the municipality was in Dimitra.
