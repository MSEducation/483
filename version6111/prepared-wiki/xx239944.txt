[[Club One (bar)]]

CATEGORIES: Buildings and structures in Savannah, Georgia, Theatre in Georgia (U.S. state)

Club One is a night club located in Savannah, Georgia, famous for its drag shows featuring The Lady Chablis.http://www.nytimes.com/2005/09/25/books/review/25goodheart.html?_r=1&pagewanted=print

==History==

The building was originally built in 1893, by the estate of George Wymberly Jones Derenne, a descendent of Noble Jones, as a warehouse.

==Events==

Club One hosts a variety of events including drag shows, pageants, standup comedy, live theater, and karaoke.
The weekend drag shows, including those featuring The Lady Chablis, as well as theater events are held in the upstairs cabaret level.http://www.clubone-online.com/cabaret.html  There is a dance floor on the main level.http://www.clubone-online.com/dance.html  The basement level (known as The Bay Cafe) hosts Thursday and Sunday drag shows, as well as karaoke.http://www.clubone-online.com/cafe.html
