[[Berardo Carboni]]

CATEGORIES: 1975 births, Living people, Italian screenwriters, Italian film directors, Place of birth missing (living people)

Graduate in jurisprudence, Berardo Carboni, born in 1975, is PhD in “civil probles of the human being”. A scriptwriter and director, has made shorts and documentaries, including Roma in Quel Niente, a documentary about Federico Caffe with Giorgio Ruffolo, Valentino Parlato and Carlo Azeglio Ciampi. Since 2000 he has been working with Lara Favaretto on the making of videos for the art circuit that have been awarded in contexts as “Furla Prize” “MomaP.S. 1 studio program”. Together with Favaretto, he also worked in 2003 on the TV movie Buco Nell'Acqua, a docudrama with Sandra Milo, produced by Kublakhan for Mediatrade.
From 2005 until 2008 plans, he produced and directed the project-movie Shooting Silvio. Shooting Silvio film was produced by popular subscription and selected and awarded in international festivals,(between them: Roma, Annecy, Tiburon[tpl]disambiguation needed|date=June 2012[/tpl], Kiev, and won the best director prize at the Gallio Film Festival) currently distributed by Martha Production.Actually is working on two version of the movie Vola vola, one experimental version entirely shot in machinima, and realised in festival, art and research contest in different places of the world and the other, thought for a wider audience, that will be shot in mixed reality during the next months.[tpl]When|date=January 2012[/tpl]
