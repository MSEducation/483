[[1999 RTHK Top 10 Gold Songs Awards]]

CATEGORIES: RTHK Top 10 Gold Songs Awards, 1999 in Hong Kong, 1999 music awards

The 1999 RTHK Top 10 Gold Songs Awards (Chinese: 1999年度十大中文金曲得獎) was held in 1999 for the 1998 music season.

==Top 10 song awards==

The top 10 songs (十大中文金曲) was expanded to top 13.

==Other awards==

The top 10 outstanding artist was also extended to 11 artists.
