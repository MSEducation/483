[[List of spiders of Texas]]

CATEGORIES: Lists of spider species, Lists of fauna of Texas

This is a list of all spider species that have been found in Texas, USA, as of July 17, 2006. It is taken from the Catalogue of Texas Spiders by D. Allen Dean, which was started in 1940. The list contains 980 species in 52 families.
