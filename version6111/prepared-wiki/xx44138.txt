[[Board of European Students of Technology]]

CATEGORIES: Educational organizations, International nongovernmental organizations, International student societies

The Board of European Students of Technology (BEST) is a non-governmental, non-political and non-profit student organisation. The Board is supported by around 3,600 volunteers, who are members of local BEST groups in 95 technical universities in 33 countries.

==Local BEST Groups==

A Local BEST Group is an association of BEST members at the same university. These groups are responsible for promoting and organising the activities of BEST at their university.
