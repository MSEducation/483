[[Gary Wolf (journalist)]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Writers from the San Francisco Bay Area, American journalists, Wired (magazine) people

This article refers to the journalist and contributing editor for Wired magazine. For the novelist and creator of the Roger Rabbit universe, see Gary K. Wolf.
Gary Wolf is a writer and contributing editor at America's Wired magazine. Among his other Wired stories, Wolf is the author of "The Curse of Xanadu," about Ted Nelson and Project Xanadu,[tpl]cite news|title=The Curse of Xanadu|author=Gary Wolf|work=Wired magazine|date=June 1995|url=http://www.wired.com/wired/archive/3.06/xanadu.html|accessdate=July 3, 2011[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://ted.hyperland.com/whatsay|title=What they say|publisher=Ted.hyperland.com|accessdate=2011-05-26[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.wired.com/wired/archive/3.09/rants.html|title=Letters about "The Curse of Xanadu"|publisher=Wired.com|date=2009-01-04|accessdate=2011-05-26[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://vinci.org/rlv/c/xanadu2/index.html|title=Errors in "The Curse of Xanadu," by Gary Wolf|work=vinci.org|year=2010|quote=Errors in 'The Curse of Xanadu', by Gary Wolf|accessdate=May 25, 2011[/tpl] and "The World According to Woz", about Steve Wozniak.
In 2007, with Kevin Kelly,[tpl]cite web|last=Wolf|first=Gary|title=Quantified Self|url=http://aether.com/quantifiedself|publisher=Gary Wolf|accessdate=2012-03-26|archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/66TEHdz4d|archivedate=2012-03-26[/tpl] Wolf co-founded the Quantified Self,[tpl]cite web|last=Singer|first=Emily|title=The Measured Life|url=http://www.technologyreview.com/biomedicine/37784/|publisher=Massachusetts Institute of Technology[tpl]![/tpl]MIT|accessdate=2011-07-05[/tpl] a collaboration of users and tool makers who share an interest in self-knowledge through self-tracking. In 2010, he spoke about the movement at TED.[tpl]cite web|last=Wolf|first=Gary|title=The quantified self|url=http://www.ted.com/talks/gary_wolf_the_quantified_self.html|publisher=TED (conference)|accessdate=2012-03-26[/tpl]
Wolf lives in Berkeley, California.[tpl]citation needed|date=July 2011[/tpl]
