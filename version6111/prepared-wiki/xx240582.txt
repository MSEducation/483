[[Péter Almási]]

CATEGORIES: 1975 births, Canoeists at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Hungarian canoeists, Living people, Olympic canoeists of Hungary

Péter Almási (born May 17, 1975) is a Hungarian sprint canoer who competed in the mid-1990s. He finished ninth in the K-2 1000 m event at the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta.
