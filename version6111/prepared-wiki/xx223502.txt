Princess ''Maria de la Esperanza'' Amalia Raniera Maria Rosario Luisa Gonzaga of Bourbon-Two Sicilies[tpl]cite web | url=http://thepeerage.com/p10430.htm#i104291| title=Maria de la Esperanza di Borbone, Principessa di Borbone delle Due Sicilie| date=20 October 2005 | accessdate=2008-10-05 | publisher=thePeerage.com | author=Darryl Lundy[/tpl][tpl]cite web | url=http://www.angelfire.com/realm/gotha/gotha/2sicilies.html| title=TWO SICILIES | date= | accessdate=2008-10-05 | publisher=Paul Theroff's Royal Genealogy Site | author=Paul Theroff[/tpl] [tpl]Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot[/tpl] (14 June 1914, Madrid, Spain – 8 August 2005, Villamanrique de la Condesa, Spain) was the youngest daughter of Prince Carlos of Bourbon-Two Sicilies and his wife Princess Louise of Orléans. Maria Esperanza was titular Empress of Brazil through her husband Prince Pedro Gastão of Orléans-Braganza, who was one of two claimants to the Brazilian throne as titular Emperor of Brazil, and head of the Petrópolis branch of the Brazilian Imperial House.[tpl]cite journal|author=Handler, Bruce|date=  5 March 1989|title=Brazil to Decide on Return of Monarchy|journal=Los Angeles Times|pages=34[/tpl] Maria Esperanza was also an aunt of Juan Carlos I of Spain, son of her sister Princess Maria Mercedes of Bourbon-Two Sicilies.
