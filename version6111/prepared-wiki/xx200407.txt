[[2008 Internationaux de Strasbourg – Doubles]]

CATEGORIES: Internationaux de Strasbourg, 2008 WTA Tour

Yan Zi and Jie Zheng were the defending champions, but Zheng chose not to participate, and only Yan competed that year.

Yan partnered with Tatiana Perebiynis, and won in the final 6–4, 6–7(3), 10–6, against Yung-jan Chan and Chia-jung Chuang.
