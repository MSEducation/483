[[Uzzah]]

CATEGORIES: Hebrew Bible people, Books of Samuel

According to the Tanakh, UzzahMeaning strength (died 1010 BC[tpl]Citation needed|date=April 2013[/tpl]) was an Israelite whose death is associated with touching the Ark of the Covenant. He was the son of Abinadab, in whose house the men of Kirjath-jearim placed the Ark when it was brought back from the land of the Philistines.[tpl]bibleverse|1|Samuel|7:1|131[/tpl] With his brother Ahio, he drove the cart on which the ark was placed when David sought to bring it up to Jerusalem. When the oxen stumbled, Uzzah, in direct violation of the divine law,[tpl]bibleverse||Numbers|4:15|131[/tpl] steadied the ark with his hand, and was immediately killed. David, displeased because Yahweh had broken forth upon Uzzah,[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.bibler.org/glossary/uzza.html |title=www.Bibler.org - Dictionary - Uzzah |date=2012-06-06[/tpl] called the place where this occurred Perez-uzzah.[tpl]bibleverse|1|Chronicles|13:11|131[/tpl] David feared to proceed further, and placed the ark in the house of Obed-edom the Gittite[tpl]bibleverse|2|Samuel|6:2-11|131[/tpl]; [tpl]bibleverse|1|Chronicles|13:6-13|131[/tpl] for three months.  Yahweh then blessed Obed-edom and David went and brought up the ark of God into the city of David.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.bibler.org/glossary/uzza.html |title=www.Bibler.org - Dictionary - Uzzah |date=2012-06-06[/tpl]
