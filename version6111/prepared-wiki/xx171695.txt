[[Mikhail Yunkov]]

CATEGORIES: 1986 births, Living people, Russian ice hockey centres, Ak Bars Kazan players, Krylya Sovetov Moscow players, HC Spartak Moscow players, Washington Capitals draft picks

Mikhail Yunkov ([tpl]lang-ru|Михаил Юньков[/tpl]; born February 16, 1986 in Voskresensk, USSR) is a Russian ice hockey forward who is currently playing for HC Spartak Moscow team. He is the brother of Alexander Yunkov, who is also a professional ice hockey player.
In the 2004 NHL Entry Draft, Mikhail was drafted 62nd overall in the 2nd round by the Washington Capitals.http://www.eurohockey.net/players/show_player.cgi?serial=63349
