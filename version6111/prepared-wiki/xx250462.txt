[[Buskerud Folk High School]]

CATEGORIES: Education in Buskerud, Folk high schools in Norway, Øvre Eiker, Educational institutions established in 1909, 1909 establishments in Norway

Buskerud Folk High School ([tpl]lang-no|Buskerud Folkehøgskole[/tpl]) is a secular folk high school located in Darbu, Øvre Eiker municipality, Buskerud county, Norway. It identifies itself as an art and culture school, and admits about 90 students every year. It is owned by the Buskerud school association, and its current principal is Arild Mikkelsen.
It was founded on 8 October 1909.http://buskerud.fhs.no/?page_id=463&paged=26 Former principals include Aslak Torjusson from 1936 to 1952.[tpl]cite encyclopedia|year=1973|title=Torjusson, Aslak|encyclopedia=Hvem er hvem?|editor=Steenstrup, Bjørn|publisher=Aschehoug|location=Oslo|url=http://runeberg.org/hvemerhvem/1973/0567.html|page=567|language=Norwegian|accessdate=27 July 2013[/tpl]
