[[Jimenez Novia]]

CATEGORIES: Vehicles introduced in 1995, Sports cars, Concept automobiles

The Novia W16 was a supercar built only in a single model in 1995 by Jimenez, a French maker, with a central engine and rear wheel transmission.
 Its W16 engine is made of 4 Yamaha 1100 engines.
