[[Fuglesongen]]

CATEGORIES: Islands of Svalbard

Fuglesongen (The Bird Song) is an island in Albert I Land at Spitsbergen, Svalbard. It has an area of about 4.1 km², and is located in the archipelago of Nordvestøyane. Its highest peak is 387 m.a.s.l.
