[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Crateús]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1963, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Fortaleza, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Crateús ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Crateopolitanus[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Crateús in the Ecclesiastical province of Fortaleza in Brazil.
