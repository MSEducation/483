[[Dvorana Župa Tivat]]

CATEGORIES: Sports venues in Montenegro

Župa Hall (Montenegrin, Дворана Жупа, Dvorana Župa) is a sports arena in Tivat, Montenegro, located in the Tivat's settlement Župa, by which it got its name. Capacity of the hall is 700 seats. Župa Sports Hall is one kilometer away from the center of Tivat.

==Facilities==

==History==

The hall was home of the European Championship in Bocce which was held from 27 September to 3 October in Tivat.
In 2011 it was home of the Montenegro Basketball Cup final tournament for women.
Montenegro women's national handball team played against Slovenia in this hall.
