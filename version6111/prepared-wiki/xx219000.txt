[[Lysva River]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of Perm Krai

Lysva ([tpl]lang-ru|Лысьва[/tpl]) is a river in Perm Krai, Russia. It is a left tributary of the Chusovaya, and is [tpl]convert|112|km|mi|sp=us[/tpl] long, with a drainage basin of [tpl]convert|1010|km2|sqmi|sp=us[/tpl]. By the river lies the town of Lysva.
