[[Minister of State – Agriculture]]

CATEGORIES: Canadian ministers

The Minister of State – Agriculture (French: Ministre d'État - Agriculture) is a  minister in the Canadian Cabinet responsible for National revenue. The Minister of State – Agriculture reports to the Minister of Agriculture. 
The [tpl]As of|2008|alt=current[/tpl] Minister of State – Agriculture for Canada is Jean-Pierre Blackburn (Riding: Jonquière-Alma, Québec).
