[[Povl Wøldike]]

CATEGORIES: 1899 births, 1975 deaths, Danish male film actors, People from Copenhagen

Povl Wøldike (13 August 1899 – 25 July 1975) was a Danish film actor. He appeared in 32 films between 1938 and 1970.
He was born in Copenhagen, Denmark and died in Denmark.
