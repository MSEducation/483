[[1978 Wimbledon Championships – Men's Singles]]

CATEGORIES: Wimbledon Championship by year – Gentlemen's Singles, 1978 Wimbledon Championships

Björn Borg defeated Jimmy Connors 6–2, 6–2, 6–3 in the final to win the Gentlemen's Singles tennis title at the 1978 Wimbledon Championships.
