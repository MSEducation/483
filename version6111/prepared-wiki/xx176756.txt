[[Zaga Pyaw Thaw Athe-Hnalon]]

CATEGORIES: 1968 films, Burmese films, 1960s drama films

Zaga Pyaw Thaw Athè-Hnalon ([tpl]lang-my|စကားပြောသောအသည်းနှလုံး[/tpl]) is a 1968 Burmese drama film directed by Thukha. The film won three Myanmar Academy Awards including Best Film, Best Director and Best Supporting Actor.
