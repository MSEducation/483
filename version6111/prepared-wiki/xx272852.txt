[[Ken Kiff]]

CATEGORIES: 1935 births, 2001 deaths, People from Dagenham, Alumni of Middlesex University, 20th-century English painters, Royal Academicians, Figurative art

Ken Kiff, RA (29 May 1935, Dagenham, Essex, England – 15 February 2001, London) was a 20th-century British painter.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.independent.co.uk/news/obituaries/ken-kiff-728812.html | title=Obituaries: Ken Kiff| work=The Independent | first=Martha | last=Kapis | date=17 February 2001 | accessdate=September 25, 2011[/tpl][tpl]cite news | url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/news/2001/feb/16/guardianobituaries |title=Obituaries: Ken Kiff — Painter on a poetic journey into colour |work=The Guardian | date=16 February 2001 | first=Norbert | last=Lynton | accessdate=September 25, 2011[/tpl]
Kenneth Kiff studied at the Hornsey School of Art (later to become part of Middlesex University)
during 1955–61. Kiff was a figurative artist at a time when abstract art held sway. He became a Royal Academician in 1991. During 1991–93, he was Associate Artist at the National Gallery in London.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.nationalgallery.org.uk/learning/associate-artist-scheme/ken-kiff/ken-kiff | title=Ken Kiff: Associate Artist 1991–3 |publisher=National Gallery, London, UK |accessdate=September 25, 2011[/tpl]
Kiff's dealer was Marlborough Fine Art[tpl]cite web| url=http://www.marlboroughfineart.com/biography-Ken-Kiff-39.html | title=Ken Kiff (b. 1935 – d.2001) |publisher=Marlborough Fine Art |accessdate=September 25, 2011[/tpl] in London and he exhibited there amongst other galleries. A mini retrospective exhibition of Kiff's paintings was held at the Marlborough Fine Art gallery in 2008.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/art/features/ken-kiff-and-peter-doig-psychodrama-on-canvas-781718.html |title=Ken Kiff and Peter Doig: Psychodrama, on canvas| work=The Independent | first=Tom | last=Lubbock |date=14 February 2008 | accessdate=September 25, 2011[/tpl]
Ken Kiff was married with one son and one daughter.
