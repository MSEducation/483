[[Keith Alexander (guitarist)]]

CATEGORIES: 1963 births, 2005 deaths, Road accident deaths in New York, Cycling road accident victims, People from Brooklyn, American heavy metal guitarists, Musicians from New York City

Keith Alexander (November 23, 1963 - July 11, 2005) born in Brooklyn, New York was a guitarist, cyclist and body modification enthusiast.

==Career==

Keith Alexander was the guitarist for the thrash metal band Carnivore, whose first album he played on. He then joined Brooklyn-based band Primal Scream.http://www.brooklynvegan.com/archives/2005/07/keith_alexander.html During the late 1990s he was a member of Dee Snider's SMFs. Keith also worked as a talent finder, though technically credited as "Piercing consultant"http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0124102/fullcredits#cast/ on Dee Sniders movie 1998 movie Strangeland.http://news.bme.com/2005/01/10/rtfm-keith-alexander/ For reasons of both cost savings with special effects and sense of reality in appearance, Keith went within the body modification community for extra's who were modded for some roles. In the 2000s, he became a somewhat well known blogger, being associated with blogs such as Boingboing.http://boingboing.net/2005/07/12/in-memoriam-keith-al.html

==Death==

Keith Alexander died in a bicycle accident on July 11, 2005.  He lost control of his bike while passing a child and crashed into a fence on Shore Road, Bay Ridge Brooklyn.
