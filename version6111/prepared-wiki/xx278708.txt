[[List of Places of Scenic Beauty of Japan (Shizuoka)]]

CATEGORIES: Visitor attractions in Shizuoka Prefecture, Places of Scenic Beauty

This list is of the Places of Scenic Beauty of Japan located within the Prefecture of Shizuoka.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.bunka.go.jp/english/pdf/h24_chapter_06.pdf |title=Preservation and Utilization of Cultural Properties |publisher=Agency for Cultural Affairs |accessdate=15 September 2013[/tpl]

==National Places of Scenic Beauty==

As of 1 September 2013, ten Places have been designated at a national level (including one *Special Place of Scenic Beauty); Mount Fuji spans the prefectural borders with Yamanashi.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.bunka.go.jp/bunkazai/shoukai/shitei.html |title=史跡名勝天然記念物 |trans_title=Count of Monuments of Japan by Prefecture |language=Japanese |publisher=Agency for Cultural Affairs |date=1 September 2013 |accessdate=15 September 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web |url=http://kunishitei.bunka.go.jp/bsys/index_pc.asp |title=Database of National Cultural Properties: 史跡名勝天然記念物 (名勝, 特別名勝 静岡県) |language=Japanese |publisher=Agency for Cultural Affairs |accessdate=15 September 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web |url=http://www.pref.shizuoka.jp/kyouiku/kk-100/shiteibunkai_kensu.html |title=指定文化財の件数 |trans_title=Counts of Designated Cultural Properties |language=Japanese |publisher=Shizuoka Prefecture |date=1 May 2013 |accessdate=15 September 2013[/tpl]

==Prefectural Places of Scenic Beauty==

As of 1 May 2013, seven Places have been designated at a prefectural level.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.bunka.go.jp/bunkazai/shiteibunkazai/todoufuken.html |title=都道府県別指定等文化財件数（都道府県分）|trans_title=Count of Prefectural Cultural Properties by Prefecture |language=Japanese |publisher=Agency for Cultural Affairs |date=1 May 2013 |accessdate=15 September 2013[/tpl]

==Municipal Places of Scenic Beauty==

As of 1 May 2013, sixteen Places have been designated at a municipal level.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.bunka.go.jp/bunkazai/shiteibunkazai/sichouson.html |title=都道府県別指定等文化財件数（市町村分）|trans_title=Count of Municipal Cultural Properties by Prefecture |language=Japanese |publisher=Agency for Cultural Affairs |date=1 May 2013 |accessdate=15 September 2013[/tpl]
