[[Khomasdal]]

CATEGORIES: Geography of Windhoek, Populated places in the Khomas Region, Suburbs of Windhoek

Khomasdal is a suburb of Namibia's capital of Windhoek in the Khomas Region. Founded as Windhoek's residential area for Coloured people, Khomasdal still is primarily composed of Coloured people.Community dreams of a new Khomasdal by Denver Isaacs, The Namibian, 4 March 2008 
In October 2006, the City of Windhoek announced the construction of an informal market in Khomasdal. The market will give the Khomasdal community the opportunity to generate their own income and even create more jobs in the process. Since then the Market has been finished and is situated on the corner of Mahatma Gandhi and Hans-Dietrich Genscher streets in Khomasdal. 
Informal market for Khomasdal, extension of SME centre, The Namibian, Nampa, 26 September 2010 A similar SME Market can also be found in Katutura.
It is also home to the Windhoek College of Education, one of four national colleges of education.Windhoek College of Education
