[[Gardiner railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1890

Gardiner railway station is located on the Glen Waverley line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the eastern Melbourne suburb of Glen Iris opening on 24 March 1890.Glen Iris Vicsig
A signal box is situated on Platform 2 at the eastern end of the station, to control the Burke Road tramway crossing.
The station buildings were reconstructed in 1975.[tpl]cite book|author=Vincent Adams Winter|title=VR and VicRail: 1962-1983|year=1990|isbn=0-9592069-3-0|page=106[/tpl] The station is among sixteen others that are the site of eleven storey high communication towers aimed to improve identification of trains around the network and emergency management.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.theage.com.au/victoria/rail-station-towers-enrage-residents-20100330-rbip.html|title=Rail station towers enrage residents|author=Lucas, Clay|date=31 March 2010|publisher=The Age[/tpl]
