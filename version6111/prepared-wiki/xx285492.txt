[[Dhauli Nag Temple]]

CATEGORIES: Hindu temples in Uttarakhand

Dhauli Nag Temple (hi:धौली नाग मंदिर ) is a temple located, near Vijaipur, Bageshwar in Uttarakhand state of India.

==Detail==

Temple is located approximate 1 kilometer from Vijaipur, which is 25 kilometer from Bageshwar.
