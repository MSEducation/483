Bastien Charles Patrick Hery (born 23 March 1992) is a French footballer who plays for Rochdale.

==Career==

Hery began his career with Paris Saint-Germain before joining English club Sheffield Wednesday in the summer of 2012. After failing to make an appearance for Wednesday he joined League Two side Rochdale on 8 May 2013 on a free transfer.[tpl]cite web|title=Rochdale: Bastien Hery to join from Sheffield Wednesday|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/22448542|work=BBC Sport|accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl] He made his professional debut on 6 August 2013 in a Football League Cup match at Doncaster Rovers.[tpl]cite web|title=Doncaster 1-0 Rochdale|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/23499365|work=BBC Sport|accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl]
