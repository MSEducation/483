[[Justin Channing]]

Justin Andrew Channing (born 19 November 1968 in Reading, Berkshire) is an English former footballer who played as a defender or midfielder in the Football League for Queens Park Rangers, Bristol Rovers and Leyton Orient.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.neilbrown.newcastlefans.com/player2/justinchanning.html |title=Justin Channing |work=UK A–Z Transfers |publisher=Neil Brown |accessdate=9 October 2009[/tpl]
Channing signed professional forms with QPR in August 1986 and made his debut in November that year against Luton Town.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.qprnet.com/history/seasons/excels/QPR1986.xls |format=Excel spreadsheet |title=Queens Park Rangers 1986–87 |publisher=QPRnet |first=Kenneth |last=Westerberg |accessdate=9 October 2009[/tpl] He played 55 league games for QPR scoring 5 goals before transferring to Bristol Rovers in 1993 for a fee of £275,000,[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.qprnet.com/history/seasons/excels/QPR1992.xls |format=Excel spreadsheet |title=Queens Park Rangers 1992–93 |publisher=QPRnet |first=Kenneth |last=Westerberg |accessdate=9 October 2009[/tpl] and later spent two seasons at Leyton Orient before moving into non-league football with Slough Town.
