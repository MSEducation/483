[[Arto Laatikainen]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 births, Living people, Espoo Blues players, New York Rangers draft picks, Södertälje SK players

Arto Laatikainen (born May 24, 1980) is a Finnish professional ice hockey defenceman who currently plays for Espoo Blues of the SM-liiga.Espoo Blues 2013-14 Roster He was selected by the New York Rangers in the 7th round (197th overall) of the 1999 NHL Entry Draft.
