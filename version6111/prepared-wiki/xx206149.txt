[[Tim Armstead]]

Tim Armstead, of Elkview, West Virginia (born February 26, 1965) is a Republican politician from West Virginia, currently representing 32nd District (Kanawha County) in the West Virginia House of Delegates.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.legis.state.wv.us/House/Members/delmemview1.cfm?input=Delegate%20Armstead|title=Tim Armstead (R - Kanawha)|publisher=West Virginia House of Delegates|accessdate=3 May 2011[/tpl]  Armstead is originally from Clendenin, West Virginia.  He is a graduate of the University of Charleston, West Virginia and West Virginia University College of Law.
Armstead is a House Minority Leader, which makes him the highest ranking Republican in the body.
