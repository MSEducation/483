McLeod was born in Zorra, Ontario. He was elected to a term in the Alberta Legislature in the 1905 Alberta general election with a 110 vote plurality over Conservative candidate John Jackson. He would serve as a backbencher with the government and serve on the Committee on Privileges and Elections.
