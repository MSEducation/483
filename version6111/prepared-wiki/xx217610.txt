[[Vənənd]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ordubad District

Vənənd ([tpl]lang-hy|Թրունյանց Ձոր T'runyants Dzor or Վանանդ Ձոր Vanand Dzor[/tpl]; also, Venend) is a village and municipality in the Ordubad Rayon of Nakhchivan, Azerbaijan.  It has a population of 2,337.  The municipality consists of the villages of Vənənd and Çapayevka.Belediyye Informasiya Sistemi [tpl]dead link|date=January 2013[/tpl]

==History==

The canyon has settles by Armenian population since immemorial times. There settles about 14-15 large and small villages. Ruins of numerous architectural structures prove the fact of developed life yet in the period before Christ.
