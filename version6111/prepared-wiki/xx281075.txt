[[2008 World University Cycling Championship – Women's road race]]

CATEGORIES: World University Cycling Championships, 2008 in road cycling

The Women's U23 road race at the 2008 World University Cycling Championship took place on 25 May 2008 in Nijmegen, Netherlands. The race was 96.3 km long.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cyclingarchives.com/ritfiche.php?ritid=111227|title=World Championship, Road, Univ. (F) 2008|publisher=cyclingarchives.com|accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl]
Ellen van Dijk, who won the title in 2006 (and was still a student) did not defend her title.
