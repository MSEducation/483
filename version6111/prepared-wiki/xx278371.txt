[[Badri Nath Prasad]]

CATEGORIES: Nominated members of the Rajya Sabha, 1899 births, 1966 deaths, Recipients of the Padma Bhushan

Dr. Badri Nath Prasad (1899-1966) was an Indian parliamentarian. He wrote many books on mathematics and was awarded the Padma Bhushan in 1963. He was a nominated member of the Rajya Sabha from 1964 till his death in 1966.
