[[Dion Lewis]]

CATEGORIES: 1990 births, Living people, Sportspeople from Albany, New York, Players of American football from New York, Blair Academy alumni, American football running backs, Pittsburgh Panthers football players, Philadelphia Eagles players, Cleveland Browns players

Dion John Lewis (born September 27, 1990) is an American football running back for the Cleveland Browns of the National Football League (NFL). He was drafted by the Philadelphia Eagles in the fifth round of the 2011 NFL Draft. He played college football at Pittsburgh.

==Early years==
