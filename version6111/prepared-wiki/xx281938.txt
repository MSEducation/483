[[Brazil at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics, 2013 in Brazilian sport, Athletics in Brazil

Brazil competed at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics in Moscow, Russia, from 10–18 August 2013.
A team of 32 athletes were announced to represent the country in the event.[ref]
