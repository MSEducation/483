[[Holy Cross Cemetery, Colma]]

CATEGORIES: Cemeteries in San Mateo County, California, Protected areas of San Mateo County, California, Roman Catholic cemeteries in the United States, Roman Catholic Archdiocese of San Francisco

Holy Cross Catholic Cemetery in Colma, California is an American Roman Catholic cemetery operated by the Archdiocese of San Francisco. Established in 1887 on [tpl]convert|300|acre|km2[/tpl] of a former potato farm, it is the oldest and largest cemetery established in Colma to serve the needs of San Francisco. Several notable historical figures are interred at Holy Cross. One of the cemetery sequences in the film Harold and Maude was filmed here.
