[[Sligo Racecourse]]

CATEGORIES: Horse racing venues in the Republic of Ireland, Sports venues in County Sligo

Sligo Racecourse is a horse racing venue in Sligo, Ireland which stages both National Hunt and Flat racing. The course is a right- handed track on a one mile circuit. Racing has taken place at the course since 1955 although racing has taken place locally since 1781.[tpl]citeweb|url = http://www.goracing.ie/HRI/Whats-On/Racecourses/Sligo/|title=Profile|date=|work=Go Racing Ireland|accessdate=12 August 2013 [/tpl]
The racecourse is located in Cleveragh about 1 km from the town and there are around eight race days per year at the course.
