[[Claude Chirac]]

CATEGORIES: 1962 births, Living people, People from Paris, PPR (company) people, Jacques Chirac, Panthéon-Assas University alumni, Officers of the Order of Merit of the Italian Republic, Recipients of the Order of the Cross of Terra Mariana, 3rd Class

Claude Chirac (born 6 December 1962),France, Vème République, présidence, Claude Chirac the younger daughter of French president Jacques Chirac, has been her father's personal advisor since 1994.

==Biography and occupation==

She has been involved with French judoka Thierry Rey, who is the father of her son Martin Rey-Chirac.
She's also the Director of PPR, the Luxury Goods Group.
Since 11 February 2011, Claude Chirac is married to former general secretary of the Elysée, Frédéric Salat-Baroux.
