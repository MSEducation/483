[[Owrganjeh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Owrganjeh ([tpl]lang-fa|اورگنجه[/tpl], also Romanized as Ūrganjeh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3773301|Owrganjeh[/tpl] is a village in Bizineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 142, in 31 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
