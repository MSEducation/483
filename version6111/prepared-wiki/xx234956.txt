[[Ballader och grimascher]]

CATEGORIES: Cornelis Vreeswijk albums, 1965 albums, Swedish-language albums

Ballader och grimascher (English: Ballads and grimaces) is a second studio album by the Swedish-Dutch folk singer-songwriter Cornelis Vreeswijk.Recorded in Metronome Studio with producer Anders Burman.Album info on www.answers.comAlbum info on www.cornelis.se

==Track listing==

Music and lyrics by Cornelis Vreeswijk.
