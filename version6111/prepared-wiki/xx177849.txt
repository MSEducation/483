[[Edward Cummins]]

CATEGORIES: American male golfers, Amateur golfers, Olympic golfers of the United States, Golfers at the 1904 Summer Olympics, Olympic medalists in golf, Olympic gold medalists for the United States, 1886 births, 1926 deaths

 
Edward McClellan "Ned" Cummins (July 25, 1886 â€“ November 21, 1926) was an American golfer who competed in the 1904 Summer Olympics.
In 1904 he was part of the American team which won the gold medal. He finished 25th in this competition.
In the individual competition he finished 25th in the qualification and was eliminated in the first round of the match play.
