[[Oerlikon 30 mm twin cannon]]

CATEGORIES: Naval anti-aircraft guns, 30 mm artillery

The Oerlikon 30 mm twin cannon is an anti aircraft gun used by the Royal Navy. They were fitted to Type 42 destroyers after the Falklands War to improve defence against air attack.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.globalsecurity.org/military/world/europe/type42.htm|title=Type 42|publisher=www.globalsecurity.org|accessdate=2008-04-05[/tpl]
