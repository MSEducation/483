[[Martin Otáhal]]

CATEGORIES: 1971 births, Canoeists at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Czech canoeists, Living people, Olympic canoeists of the Czech Republic

Martin Otáhal (born March 28, 1971) is a Czech sprint canoer who competed in the mid-1990s. He was eliminated in the semifinals of the K-4 1000 m event at the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta.
