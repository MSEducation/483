[[Kyle Jones (ice hockey)]]

CATEGORIES: 1983 births, Living people, British Columbia Hockey League players, Canadian ice hockey goaltenders, Colorado Eagles players, Phoenix RoadRunners players, Quad City Mallards (IHL) players, Worcester Sharks players

Kyle Jones (born August 22, 1983) is a Canadian ice hockey goaltender. He is currently playing with the Braehead Clan of the British Elite Ice Hockey League (EIHL).
Jones began his professional career in 2008, playing the 2008–09 season in both the American Hockey League with the Worcester Sharks and in the ECHL with the Phoenix RoadRunners. He then played the 2009–10 season in the International Hockey League with the Quad City Mallards, before joining the Colorado Eagles where he played three seasons from 2010-2013. 
On August 13, 2013, the Braehead Clan of the British Elite Ice Hockey League announced that they had signed Jones for the 2013–14 EIHL season.http://www.braeheadclan.com/it-was-worth-the-wait-net-minder-announcement-p185598
