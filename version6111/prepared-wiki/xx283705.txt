[[Fortuna Elementary School District]]

CATEGORIES: School districts in Humboldt County, California

Fortuna Elementary School District  is a public school district based in Humboldt County, California, United States, created from the consolidation of Rohnerville and Fortuna Union Elementary School Districts effective July 1, 2012.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.cde.ca.gov/ds/si/ds/reorg1213.asp |title=District Reorganizations and Name Changes 2012-13 |publisher=California Department of Education |accessdate=August 11, 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web |url=http://www.rohnervillesd.org/index.html |title=District Consolidation |publisher=www.rohnervillesd.org |accessdate=August 11, 2013[/tpl]
