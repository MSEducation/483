[[Hasanabad, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Hasanabad ([tpl]lang-fa|حسن اباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Hasanābād; also known as Ḩasanābād-e Tāzeh Kand, Tāzeh Kand, Tāzeh Kand-e Ḩasanābād, and Tazekhkend)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3066110|Hasanabad[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 79, in 16 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
