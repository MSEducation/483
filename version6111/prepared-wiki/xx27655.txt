[[Abez]]

CATEGORIES: Hebrew Bible places

Abez ([tpl]lang-he|אבץ[/tpl], meaning "tin", "white", or alternatively "academic") was a town in the allotment of the tribe of Issachar ([tpl]bibleverse||Joshua|19:20|HE[/tpl]), at the north of the Jezreel Valley, or plain of Esdraelon. It is probably identified with the ruins of el-Beida.
