[[Ashwini Kalsekar]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Indian television actresses, Indian film actresses, 1970 births, Actresses in Hindi cinema, Indian actresses, Indian soap opera actors

Ashwini Kalsekar is an Indian soap opera actress. She is best known to television audiences for her role as the scheming aunt Jigyaasa in Kasamh Se.
Ashwini Kalsekar was born in Jan 22 1970 in Mumbai in Maharashtra. Her father Anil Kalsekar was a Bank employee. Ashwini studied her B.A. from Bombay and graduated in 1991. She pursued theatre from 1991-1994 as an understudy. From 1992-1993 she was an understudy to actress Neena Gupta. She also trained under Muzammeel Vakil, a theatre acting coach from 1992-1995.
