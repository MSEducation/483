[[Kings Peak (British Columbia)]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of British Columbia, Vancouver Island Ranges

[tpl]About|the mountain in British Columbia||Kings Peak (disambiguation)[tpl]![/tpl]Kings Peak[/tpl]

Kings Peak is a mountain located on Vancouver Island, British Columbia.  The mountain is located in Strathcona Provincial Park [tpl]convert|25|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] northeast of Gold River and [tpl]convert|2|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of Elkhorn Mountain.
Kings Peak is close to Highway 28 along the Elk River.  It is one of the most frequented mountains on Vancouver Island.
