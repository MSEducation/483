[[Frost Island]]

CATEGORIES: San Juan Islands

Frost Island is an island in the San Juan Islands of the U.S. state of Washington.[tpl]gnis|1505056[/tpl]  The approximately 70-acre (28 ha) island has no public electric service or land-line telephone service.  There is a water-taxi that runs between the island and Anacortes.http://frostisland.com/
The name was given by Charles Wilkes during the Wilkes Expedition of 1838-1842, in honor of John Frost, the boatswain of the expedition's Porpoise.[tpl]cite book |last= Phillips |first= James W. |title= Washington State Place Names |year= 1971 |publisher= University of Washington Press |isbn= 0-295-95158-3[/tpl]
