[[National Alopecia Areata Foundation]]

CATEGORIES: Medical and health organizations based in the United States, Organizations based in California, San Rafael, California

National Alopecia Areata Foundation based in San Rafael, California is dedicated to the discovery of a cure or treatment for alopecia areata and supporting the conditions victims. It was founded in 1981."Bald and Beautiful". Martinez Record, 7/22/1999.
