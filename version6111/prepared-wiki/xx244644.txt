[[A Painting of Roses]]

CATEGORIES: Portuguese films, 2004 films

A Painting of Roses ([tpl]lang-pt|Um quadro de rosas[/tpl]) is a short documentary by Miguel Ribeiro.
It was produced in 2003 by Bookcase, an independent Portuguese film company.
