[[2535 Hämeenlinna]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Asteroids named for places, Astronomical objects discovered in 1939

2535 Hämeenlinna is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1223.9388569 days (3.35 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=2535 | title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-04-28 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on February 17, 1939.
