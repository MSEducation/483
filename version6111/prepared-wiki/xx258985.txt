[[Viscumitol]]

CATEGORIES: Cyclitols

Viscumitol is a cyclitol. It is a dimethyl-ether of muco-inositol that can be isolated from Viscum album.[tpl]cite journal | last1 = Richter | first1 = Andreas | title = Viscumitol, a dimethyl-ether of muco-inositol from Viscum album | journal = Phytochemistry | volume = 31 | pages = 3925 | year = 1992 | doi = 10.1016/S0031-9422(00)97555-1[/tpl]
