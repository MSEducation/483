[[Paul Peter Vilhelm Breder]]

CATEGORIES: 1816 births, 1890 deaths, Members of the Parliament of Norway, County Governors of Norway, Nordland politicians

Paul Peter Vilhelm Breder (1816–1890) was a Norwegian politician.
From 1854 to 1858 he was County Governor of Nordlands Amt (today named Nordland).
He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament in 1857, representing Nordlands Amt. He sat only one term.Paul Peter Vilhelm Breder — Norwegian Social Science Data Services (NSD)
