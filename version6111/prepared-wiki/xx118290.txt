Akshay Kumar Baral (1860 â€“ 1919) was a Bengali poet and writer. He was born in Kolkata. 

==Career==

Boral was a fan of the poets, Biharilal Chakraborty and Rabindranath Tagore.  However, he wrote with a unique style and he was not influenced by either one.
His literary works were included in the curriculum of school level, secondary and higher secondary Bengali Literature in Bangladesh.
