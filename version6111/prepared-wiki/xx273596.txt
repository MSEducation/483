[[Lakewood, Mason County, Illinois]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Mason County, Illinois, Unincorporated communities in Illinois

Lakewood is an unincorporated community in Havana Township, Mason County, Illinois, United States. Lakewood is located on County Route 1 [tpl]convert|4|mi|km[/tpl] south-southwest of Havana.
