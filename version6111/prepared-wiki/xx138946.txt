[tpl]Nihongo|Shigeyoshi Mochizuki|望月重良|| born 9 July 1973 in Shizuoka Prefecture, Japan[/tpl] is a retired Japanese football player. He was capped 14 times for the Japanese national team and scored the winning goal in Asian Cup 2000 Final, Japan vs Saudi Arabia.
Mochizuki played for Vissel Kobe and JEF United Ichihara in the J. League.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Guardian.co.uk|title=Stats Centre: Shigeyoshi Mochizuki Facts|url=http://guardian.touch-line.com/StatsCentre.asp?CTID=57&CPID=120&TEID=1403&PLID=65759&pStr=Player|accessdate=2009-11-30[/tpl]
He is currently the chairman of the football club S.C. Sagamihara.
