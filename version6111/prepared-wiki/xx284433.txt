[[Beverly Fort]]

CATEGORIES: Forts in Massachusetts, Buildings and structures in Beverly, Massachusetts

Beverly Fort was a fort that existed from 1775 to 1776 and in 1814 on Hospital Point in Beverly, Massachusetts. In 1801, a smallpox hospital was built there, and was used in 1814 as a barracks. The J-shaped earthwork of the fort still exists today.[tpl]cite web|title=Massachusetts|url=http://www.northamericanforts.com/East/ma.html#bev|publisher=American Forts Network|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl]
