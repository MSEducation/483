[[Volgograd Oblast Police]]

CATEGORIES: Volgograd Oblast

Directorate of the Ministry for Internal Affairs in Volgograd Oblast (ГУ МВД России по Волгоградской области) or the Police of Volgograd Oblast (Полиция Волгоградской области) is the main law enforcement agency in Government of Volgograd Oblast in Russia. It is subordinate to the regional MVD and the Governor of Volgograd Oblast.
The current chief of police in Volgograd Oblast is Aleksander Kravchenko (since 2011).
