[[Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Conakry]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Guinea, Religion in Guinea

The Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Conakry ([tpl]lang-la|Konakrien(sis)[/tpl]) is the Metropolitan See for the Ecclesiastical province of Conakry in Guinea.

==History==

1920.04.18: Promoted as Apostolic Vicariate of French Guinea
1949.05.12: Renamed as Apostolic Vicariate of Conakry
1955.09.14: Promoted as Metropolitan Archdiocese of Conakry
