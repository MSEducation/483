[[Tváre pred zrkadlom]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 singles, Marika Gombitová songs, Songs written by Marika Gombitová, Songs written by Kamil Peteraj, 1989 songs

"Tváre pred zrkadlom" ([tpl]lang-en|Faces in Front of the Mirror[/tpl]) is a song by Marika Gombitová released on OPUS in 1989.[tpl]harvnb|Graclík|Nekvapil|2008|p=393.[/tpl]For Marika Gombitová's discography, see [tpl]harvnb|Lehotský|2008a|pp=54–56.[/tpl].
The composition wrote Gombitová in collaboration with Kamil Peteraj. B-side featured "Ave Mária" (music by Gabo Dušík), and both tracks were available on CD in 2004 as bonus tracks on the re-release of album Kam idú ľudia? (from 1989).[tpl]harvnb|Graclík|Nekvapil|2008|p=406.[/tpl]
