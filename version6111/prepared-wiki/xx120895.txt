[[Chen Shanshan]]

CATEGORIES: 1983 births, Living people, Chinese classical guitarists, People's Republic of China musicians, Place of birth missing (living people)

Chen Shanshan ([tpl]zh|s=陈姗姗|t=陳姍姍|p=Chén Shānshān[/tpl]; born November 21, 1983) is a Chinese classical guitarist.  Some sources give her birth year as 1982.
Chen Shanshan has studied with Chen Zhi and toured internationally.
She has also performed in a quartet formation (Four Angels) with Wang Yameng, Li Jie, Su Meng.
