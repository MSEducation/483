[[Boones Hill, North Carolina]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Surry County, North Carolina, Unincorporated communities in North Carolina

Boones Hill is a small Unincorporated community in the Mount Airy Township of northern Surry County, North Carolina, on the outskirts of the city of Mount Airy near Bannertown.  The community is centered around the intersection of Business U.S. Highway 52 (South Main Street) and U.S. Highway 52 Bypass (Andy Griffith Parkway) south of Bannertown.
