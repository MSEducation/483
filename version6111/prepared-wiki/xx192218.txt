[[IMS-1]]

CATEGORIES: Space programme of India, Artificial satellites orbiting Earth, Satellites, Earth observation satellites of India, Satellites of India, Mini satellites of India

IMS-1 is an Earth observation satellite in a sun-synchronous orbit. The satellite which is the fourteenth satellite in the Indian Remote Sensing (IRS) satellite series has been built, launched and maintained by the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO). IMS-1 is the first satellite to use ISRO's Indian Mini Satellite bus.http://www.isro.org/satellites/ims-1.aspx
It was launched by the Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle - C9 on April 28, 2008 along with the Cartosat-2A and eight nano research satellites belonging to research facilities in Canada, Denmark, Germany, Japan and the Netherlands.India in multi-satellite launch
