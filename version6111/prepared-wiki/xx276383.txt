[[Aiden O'Brien]]

CATEGORIES: 1993 births, Living people, People from Islington, English footballers, Republic of Ireland association footballers, Republic of Ireland youth international footballers, Republic of Ireland under-21 international footballers, Association football strikers, Millwall F.C. players, Staines Town F.C. players, Hayes & Yeading United F.C. players, Crawley Town F.C. players, Football Conference players

Aiden Anthony O'Brien (born 4 October 1993) is a footballer who plays for Millwall as a striker.

==Career==

Born in Islington, London,[tpl]cite book|editor1-first=Glenda|editor1-last=Rollin|editor2-first=Jack|editor2-last=Rollin|title=Sky Sports Football Yearbook 2012–2013|year=2012|publisher=Headline|location=London|isbn=978-0-7553-6356-8|page=497|edition=43rd[/tpl] O'Brien started his career in the youth system at Millwall and signed his first professional contract in 2010 on his 17th birthday.[tpl]Cite news |url=http://www.millwallfc.co.uk/page/NewsDetail/0,,10367~2175941,00.html |title=Pro Contract for O'Brien |date=6 August 2010 |accessdate=2011-09-26[/tpl] He made his debut for the club in the League Cup third round 5–0 defeat to Wolverhampton Wanderers, coming on as a substitute for Dany N'Guessan.[tpl]Cite news |url=http://www.lfe.org.uk/news/night-to-remember-for-millwall-s-o-brien |title=Night To Remember For Millwall's O'Brie |date=23 September 2011 |accessdate=2011-09-26[/tpl] On 1 January 2012, O'Brien signed for Conference South side Staines Town on a one-month loan.[tpl]Cite news |url=http://nonleague.pitchero.com/news/young-lion-loaned-to-staines-9471/ |title=Young Lion Loaned to Staines |date=30 December 2011 |accessdate=2012-02-25[/tpl]
A month later, Aiden signed for Conference Premier side Hayes and Yeading on a one-month loan and immediately found himself playing games.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/17321143/ |title=Hayes & Yeading sign Millwall striker Aiden O'Brien on loan |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20120310160032/http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/17321143 |archivedate=2012-03-10 |accessdate=21 May 2013[/tpl]

==International career==

On 6 February 2013 O'Brien scored on his debut for the Republic of Ireland U21s against Netherlands in a 3–0 win at Tallaght Stadium.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.fai.ie/international/under-21/103017-o-brien-double-helps-u21s-beat-dutch.html|title=O'Brien double helps U21s beat Dutch|date= 6 Feb 13[/tpl]
O'Brien then scored his first competitive goal and his third goal for Ireland in 4–1 away win against the Faroe Islands.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.uefa.com/under21/season=2015/matches/round=2000412/match=2011505/postmatch/report/index.html|title= Faroe Islands-Republic of Ireland report|date=14 August 13[/tpl]
On 15 November 2013, O'Brien bagged another international brace against Faroe Islands in a high scoring 5–2 win at The Showgrounds.[tpl]cite news|url= http://www.uefa.com/under21/season=2015/matches/round=2000412/match=2011495/postmatch/report/index.html|title= Republic of Ireland-Faroe Islands report |date= 15 Nov 13[/tpl]
O'Brien has scored five times for the 21s and is two off Robbie Brady's all time goal scoring record with seven.
