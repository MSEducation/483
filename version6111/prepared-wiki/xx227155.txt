[[Maiden Holdings]]

CATEGORIES: Economy of Bermuda, Reinsurance companies

Maiden Holdings Ltd., ([tpl]NASDAQ|MHLD[/tpl]) is a Bermuda based holding company with insurance subsidiarieshttp://www.maiden.bm/ that provides specialty reinsurance products for the global property and casualty market.  The company has operating subsidiaries in the United States (Maiden Re,http://www.maidenre.com/ Maiden Specialtyhttp://www.maidenspecialty.com/), Europe (Maiden Internationalhttp://www.maideniis.com/), and Bermuda (Maiden Insurancehttp://www.maideninsurance.bm/).
