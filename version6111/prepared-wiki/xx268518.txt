[[Isaak "Tjaak" Pattiwael]]

CATEGORIES: 1914 births, 1987 deaths, Indonesian footballers, Indonesia international footballers, Association football forwards, 1938 FIFA World Cup players

Isaak "Tjaak" Pattiwael (23 February 1914 – 16 March 1987) was an Indonesian football forward who played for the Dutch East Indies in the 1938 FIFA World Cup.Copa do Mundo da FIFA França 1938 He also played for VV Jong Ambon Batavia.
