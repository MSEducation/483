[[Kraśniczyn]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Krasnystaw County

Kraśniczyn [tpl]IPAc-pl|k|r|a|ś|'|n|i|cz|y|n[/tpl] is a village in Krasnystaw County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It is the seat of the gmina (administrative district) called Gmina Kraśniczyn. It lies approximately [tpl]convert|16|km|mi|0[/tpl] south-east of Krasnystaw and [tpl]convert|66|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-east of the regional capital Lublin.
The village has a population of 447.
