[[62nd Indian Infantry Brigade]]

CATEGORIES: British Indian Army brigades

The 62nd Indian Infantry Brigade was an Infantry formation of the Indian Army during World War II. It was formed in November 1943, and assigned to the 19th Indian Infantry Division The brigade fought in the Burma Campaign and remained with the 19th Division until the end of the war.[tpl]cite web|accessdate=2009-10-23|publisher=Order of Battle|title=62 Indian Brigade|url=http://www.ordersofbattle.com/UnitData.aspx?UniX=6201&Tab=Sup[/tpl]
