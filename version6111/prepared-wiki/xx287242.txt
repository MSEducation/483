[[Chet Baker in New York]]

CATEGORIES: 1959 albums, Chet Baker albums, Riverside Records albums, Albums produced by Orrin Keepnews

Chet Baker in New York is an album by trumpeter Chet Baker recorded in 1958 and released on the Riverside label early the following year.Chet Baker discography accessed August 13 2013 

==Reception==

Allmusic awarded the album with 4 stars stating "Chet Baker in New York is a highly recommended entry into Baker's catalog".Planer, L. Allmusic listing accessed August 13, 2013
