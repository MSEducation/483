[[Fire in the Sky (Quasar album)]]

CATEGORIES: 1981 albums, Neo-progressive rock albums

Fire In The Sky, released in 1981, was the first studio album by neo-progressive rock group Quasar. It was released on vinyl 1981, but released on CD in 1989 with a new cover.
The track Fire In The Sky, was re-recorded for the EMI Fire in Harmony Progressive Rock compilation with Susan Robinson on vocals. The USA CD release has this recording as a bonus track.
