[[Changuri]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Changuri ([tpl]lang-fa|چنگوري[/tpl], also Romanized as Changūrī; also known as Chinguru)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3058052|Changuri[/tpl] is a village in Sojas Rud Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 851, in 174 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
