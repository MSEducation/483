[[Andrei Mendel]]

CATEGORIES: 1995 births, Living people, Russian footballers

Andrei Semyonovich Mendel ([tpl]lang-ru|Андрей Семёнович Мендель[/tpl]; born April 17, 1995) is a Russian football midfielder, who currently playing for  FC Biolog-Novokubansk Progress.
He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Biolog-Novokubansk Progress on August 4, 2013 in a game against FC Mashuk-KMV Pyatigorsk.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Russian Football Union|url=http://rfspro.ru/cgi-bin/player/script2.cgi?par1=119879|title=Career Summary|date=11 August 2013[/tpl]
