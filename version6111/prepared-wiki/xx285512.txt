[[Gedko II (bishop of Płock)]]

CATEGORIES: Bishops of Płock, Year of birth unknown, 13th-century births, 1296 deaths, 13th-century Roman Catholic bishops

Bishop Gedko II was Bishop of Płock, Poland from 1294 until his death in 1296AD.Gedko at Diocese of Plock on GCatholic.org He is also known as Gosław.
Little is know of his life, career or short episcopacy, that lasted from 1294 to 1296AD. Although according to Jan Długosz, Gedko participated in the coronation of Przemysł II in Gniezno in 1295.Piotr Nitecki, Biskupi Kościoła w Polsce. w latach 965-1999. Słownik biograficzny, Warszawa, Instytut Wydawniczy PAX, 2000, ISBN 83-211-1311-7.
