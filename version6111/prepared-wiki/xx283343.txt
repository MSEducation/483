[[Memorial for Premier Zhou's inspection of the Loukou Yellow River Railway Bridge]]

CATEGORIES: Visitor attractions in Shandong

The Memorial for Premier Zhou's inspection of the Loukou Yellow River Railway Bridge ([tpl]zh|c=[tpl]linktext|周|总|理|视|察|泺|口|黄|河|铁|路|桥|纪|念|地[/tpl]|p=[tpl]linktext|Zhōu |zǒng|lǐ |shì|chá |luò|kǒu |huáng|hé |tiě|lù|qiáo |jì|niàn|de[/tpl][/tpl]) is a major historical site protect by Shandong Province in Tianqiao District of Jinan, Shandong, China. It commemorates the visit by Premier Zhou Enlai to the Luokou Yellow River Railway Bridge during the 1958 Yellow River flood on August 6th, 1958. The bridge was defended against the flood successfully. The site has been listed as a protected by Shandong province since December 23, 1977 (site number 1-03).
