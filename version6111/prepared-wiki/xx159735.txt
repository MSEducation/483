[[Zlatka Reljić]]

CATEGORIES: 1929 births, 2007 deaths, People from Krupanj, League of Communists of Serbia politicians, Government ministers of Serbia

Zlatka Reljić ([tpl]lang-sr|Златка Рељић[/tpl]) (born Krupanj, Kingdom of Yugoslavia, 1929, died in Šabac, Serbia, August 10, 2007) was a Serbian communist and humanitarian labourer. She also was a minister in Serbian government from 1982 until 1990.
