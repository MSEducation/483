[[Don't Say Good-Bye]]

CATEGORIES: 2009 singles

"Don't Say Good-Bye" is the first in the five-single series of indies collaboration singles by Japanese girl group Melon Kinenbi, in a collaboration with Beat Crusaders. It was released as limited distribution on June 24, 2009. People that purchased the single from the Tower Records online store received a free original computer wallpaper.
