[[Bernardino José de Campos Júnior]]

CATEGORIES: Governors of São Paulo (state), Presidents of the Chamber of Deputies of Brazil, University of São Paulo alumni, 1915 deaths, Government ministers of Brazil, Members of the Federal Senate, 1841 births

Bernardino José de Campos Júnior (September 6, 1841 — January 18, 1915) was a Brazilian politician, second and sixth governor of the State of São Paulo (August 23, 1892 to April 15, 1896 and July 1902 to May 1904).
Campos was born in Pouso Alegre.  He was also Treasury Minister from 1896 to 1898 and a senator.
The city of Bernardino de Campos is named after him
