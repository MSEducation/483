[[Dick Smith (defensive back)]]

CATEGORIES: 1944 births, Year of death missing, People from Hamilton, Ohio, American football defensive backs, Northwestern Wildcats football players, Washington Redskins players

Richard Henry Smith (born June 18, 1944 in Hamilton, Ohio) was an American football defensive back in the National Football League for the Washington Redskins.  He played college football at Northwestern University and was selected in the ninth round of the 1966 AFL Draft by the Kansas City Chiefs.
