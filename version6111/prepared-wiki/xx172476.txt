[[Andrei Kalimullin]]

CATEGORIES: 1977 births, People from Kohtla-Järve, Living people, FC Levadia Tallinn players, FC Lantana Tallinn players, Estonian footballers

Andrei Kalimullin (born 6 October 1977 in Kohtla-Järve) is an Estonian professional footballer. He plays the position of defender and is 1.82 m tall and weighs 79 kg.
