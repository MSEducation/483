[[Paul Barnett (producer)]]

CATEGORIES: British television directors, People from Kingswinford, Living people, 1968 births

Paul Barnett was born in Kingswinford, Staffordshire, England in September 1968 and is a Television Producer and Director. After spending his early career creating and directing computer generated imagery sequences, Paul moved on to directing special effects sequences for film and television, before working as a freelance television director and producer for the BBC, ITV,Channel Four and Channel Five.
