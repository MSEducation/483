[[Ina Bauer (figure skater)]]

CATEGORIES: German female single skaters, German film actresses, 1941 births, Living people, 20th-century German actresses

Ina Bauer (born 31 January 1941) is a German retired competitive figure skater. She is the 1957-1959 German national champion. She twice placed fourth at the World Figure Skating Championships, once in 1958 and once in 1959. During this time she was training in Colorado Springs, Colorado."Foreign News", Skating magazine, March 1959
Bauer is the inventor of the eponymous skating element.
She starred in two movies with Austrian alpine skier Toni Sailer.
