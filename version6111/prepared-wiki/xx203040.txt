[[James R. Peterson]]

CATEGORIES: South Dakota State Senators, Members of the South Dakota House of Representatives, 1943 births, Living people

James R. "Jim" Peterson is a Democratic member of the South Dakota Senate, representing the 4th district since 2005. He is currently Minority Whip. Earlier he was a member of the South Dakota House of Representatives from 2001 through 2004.
