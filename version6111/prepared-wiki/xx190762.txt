[[Per Henrik Wallin]]

CATEGORIES: Swedish jazz pianists, Swedish composers, 1946 births, 2005 deaths

Per Henrik Wallin (July 17, 1946; June 15, 2005) was a Swedish jazz pianist and composer. He was acclaimed in Continental Europe, but relatively obscure in the United States.All About Jazz He received the Swedish variant of the Golden Django two years before his death.
