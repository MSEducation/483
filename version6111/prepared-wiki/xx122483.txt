[[Debbie Dickinson]]

CATEGORIES: American people of Belarusian descent, American people of Polish descent, 1957 births, Living people, American female models

Debbie Dickinson (born December 30, 1957 in Hollywood, Florida) is an American actress and fashion model. The youngest of three sisters, Dickinson is of Belarusian and Polish descent.
