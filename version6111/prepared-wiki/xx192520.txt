[[William Gray (cricketer)]]

CATEGORIES: 1864 births, 1898 deaths, English cricketers, Essex cricketers

William Gray (November 26, 1864 â€” December 18, 1898) was an English cricketer who played first-class cricket for Essex. Gray was born and died in Chelmsford.
Gray made just one first-class appearance for the team, during the 1894 season, against Yorkshire. Playing as an opening batsman, he scored just four runs in two innings.
Gray died at the age of 34.
