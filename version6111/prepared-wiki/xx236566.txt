[[We're All Doomed]]

CATEGORIES: 2002 albums, Go-Kart Records albums

We're All Doomed is an album by Boston crust punk band Toxic Narcotic. It was released in 2002 by Go-Kart Records.Skratch Magazine - Interview Lyrically, the album deals with politic and environmental issues. The songs on this album, like most Crust Punk, are short and only a small number are over 3 minutes long.Exclaim - review
