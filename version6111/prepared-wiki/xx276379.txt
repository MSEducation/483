[[Martialia hyadesii]]

CATEGORIES: Squid

Martialia hyadesii is a species of squid commonly known as the sevenstar flying squid.  These large squid are known to prey upon deep-sea Ridgeheads, and to be preyed upon in turn by King Penguins.[tpl]cite book|last=Williams|first=Tony D.|title=The Penguins: Spheniscidae|year=1995|publisher=Oxford University Press|location=Oxford, UK|isbn=978-0-19-854667-2|page=147[/tpl]
