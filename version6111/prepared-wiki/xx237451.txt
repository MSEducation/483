[[Taurus (film)]]

CATEGORIES: 2001 films, 2000s drama films, Biographical films, Films directed by Alexander Sokurov, Russian films, Russian-language films, Works about Vladimir Lenin

Taurus ([tpl]lang-ru|Телец[/tpl], translit. Telets) is a 2001 Russian biographical drama film directed by Alexander Sokurov, portraying Vladimir Lenin. It was entered into the 2001 Cannes Film Festival.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.festival-cannes.com/en/archives/ficheFilm/id/2001813/year/2001.html |title=Festival de Cannes: Taurus |accessdate=2009-10-18|work=festival-cannes.com[/tpl]
