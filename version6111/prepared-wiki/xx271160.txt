[[Acacia notabilis]]

CATEGORIES: Acacia, Fabales of Australia, Flora of New South Wales, Plants described in 1858

Acacia notabilis, known colloquially as mallee golden wattle or Flinders wattle, is a species of Acacia native to Australia. A shrub anywhere from one to five metres high, it is native to the Broken Hill district of arid far western New South Wales as well as Victoria and South Australia.
