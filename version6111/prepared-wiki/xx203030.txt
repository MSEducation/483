[[Temple Place Historic District]]

CATEGORIES: Historic districts in Massachusetts

Temple Place Historic District is a national historic district at 11-55, 26-58 Temple Place in Boston, Massachusetts. The district features Greek Revival and late-Victorian architecture and a building by Nathaniel J. Bradlee.  The district was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1988. Among the former tenants: Ritz & Hastings, photographers (1860s-1880s).Illustrated Boston, the metropolis of New England. NY: American Publishing and Engraving Co., 1889
