Luke Bradley Bombardir (born May 5, 1972) is a retired Canadian professional ice hockey player who played for the New Jersey Devils, Minnesota Wild and the Nashville Predators in the NHL. He currently serves as the Minnesota Wild's Director of Player Development and Senior director of Community relations.[tpl]cite web| url = http://wild.nhl.com/club/news.htm?id=532618 | title = Brad Bombardir named Director of Player Development |  publisher = Minnesota Wild | date = 2010-06-24 | accessdate = 2010-08-18[/tpl]

==Playing career==

Bombardir was selected in the third round, 56th overall, in the 1990 NHL Entry Draft by the New Jersey Devils. He won a Stanley Cup with the Devils in 2000.
