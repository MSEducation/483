[[(13110) 1993 LS1]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1993, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(13110) 1993 LS1 is a main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on June 15, 1993.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
