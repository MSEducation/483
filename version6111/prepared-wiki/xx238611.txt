[[Trout River (British Columbia)]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of British Columbia, Liard Country, Rivers of the Canadian Rockies

The Trout River is a tributary of the Liard River in far northern British Columbia, Canada, flowing northwest from headwaters at [tpl]coord|58|44|N|126|14|W[/tpl], near Muncho Lake, to meet the Liard at the community of Liard River.[tpl]BCGNIS|22995|Trout River[/tpl]   It is at the upper end of the Grand Canyon of the Liard,[tpl]BCGNIS|11637|Grand Canyon of the Liard[/tpl] which is part of Liard River Corridor Provincial Park and Protected Area.
