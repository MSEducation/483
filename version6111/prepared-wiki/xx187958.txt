[[Special Life!]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 singles, Kotoko songs, Song recordings produced by I've Sound, Songs with lyrics by Kotoko (singer)

"Special Life!" is Kotoko's twelfth maxi single produced by I've Sound and Geneon Entertainment label. This single was released on May 21, 2008.[tpl]cite web | title = KOTOKO OFFICIAL GENEON WEB SITE | url = http://www.geneon-ent.co.jp/rondorobe/music/kotoko/news.html | accessdate = 28 April 2008 | language = Japanese |archiveurl = http://web.archive.org/web/20080407222804/http://www.geneon-ent.co.jp/rondorobe/music/kotoko/news.html  |archivedate = 7 April 2008[/tpl] The title track was used as the opening theme for the anime Kamen no Maid Guy. The single was sold as a regular edition (CD only) and a limited edition (CD+DVD) which contained the video for the title track.
