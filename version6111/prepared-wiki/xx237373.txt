==Afro-Asiatic languages (formerly known as hamito-semitic)==

==French==

Due to colonialism, European languages are often present in the African continent. Mauritania is no exception to this rule. Mauritania is a member of Francophonie. Most of the population speak French.[tpl]Citation needed|date=December 2010[/tpl] The historic ties with France are still present, although Mauritania has also become more Arabised.
