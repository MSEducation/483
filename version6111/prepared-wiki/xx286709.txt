[[Nakatu, Iran]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Nakatu ([tpl]lang-fa|نكتو[/tpl], also Romanized as Nakatū; also known as Lakatu, Nagatū, Nagtū, and Taktu)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3076202|Nakatu[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 652, in 146 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
