[[Party Around the World]]

CATEGORIES: Smile.dk albums, 2008 albums

Party Around the World is the fourth studio album from Swedish bubblegum pop group Smile.dk. It was released in 2008. It contains twelve new tracks and a bonus remix of "Heal My Broken Heart", a song from their album Future Girls. Some versions include the bonus track, "Butterfly 09 (United Forces Airplay Edit)".
