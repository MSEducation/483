[[Carcase for Hounds]]

CATEGORIES: 1974 novels, Kenyan novels, Mau Mau Uprising, Novels set in Colonial Africa, Novels set in Kenya

Carcase for Hounds is a novel by Kenyan writer Meja Mwangi first published in 1974. The novel concerns the Mau Mau liberation struggle during the latter days of British colonial rule and attempts, by the actions of the main protagonists, to show how Mau Mau was organized and why it took so long for the colonial government to defeat them.Amazon Carecase for HoundsEncyclopedia of African Literature published by Routledge of London in 2003 ISBN 0-415-23019-5]
