[[Hexarthrius parryi]]

CATEGORIES: Lucaninae

Hexarthrius parryi is a species of large stag beetles. It belongs to the genus Hexarthrius of the tribe Lucanini. It is classified under the subfamily Lucaninae of the stag beetle family Lucanidae.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.coleoptera.org/p1760.htm|title=Coleoptera: Lucanidae |author=J.M. Maes|publisher=Coleoptera.org|date=January 11, 2004[/tpl] It contains several subspecies:[tpl]cite web|url=http://data.gbif.org/species/browse/provider/1/taxon/14734710/|title=Classification of Genus: Hexarthrius|author=Global Biodiversity Information Facility[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.biolib.cz/en/taxon/id325459/|title=Hexarthrius parryi Hope, 1842 |publisher=BioLib[/tpl]
