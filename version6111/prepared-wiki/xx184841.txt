[[Óscar Serrano (tennis)]]

CATEGORIES: 1978 births, Living people, Spanish male tennis players, Catalan tennis players, Sportspeople from Barcelona

Óscar Serrano Gámez (born 25 May 1978, in Barcelona, Catalonia) is a former Spanish professional male tennis player. Currently, as June 2010, Oscar Serrano is the coach of Fabio Fognini.
Serrano retired in 2005, after playing a total of 19 matches during his ATP career. He became professional in 1996, and achieved his career-high ATP ranking on 27 November 2000, reaching #128 in the world.
