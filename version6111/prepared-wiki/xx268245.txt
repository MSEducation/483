Gianfranco Roberto Espinoza Andrade (born 28 August 1986 in Lima) is an Peruvian international footballer who plays for León de Huánuco, as a defender.

==Career==

Espinoza has played for Coronel Bolognesi, Sport Boys, Alianza Atlético and León de Huánuco.[tpl]NFT player|pid=41820[/tpl]
He made his international debut for Peru in 2011.
