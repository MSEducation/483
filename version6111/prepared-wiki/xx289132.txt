[[Mehdilu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Mehdilu ([tpl]lang-fa|مهديلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Mehdīlū)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3074617|Mehdilu[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
