[[Uitgeest railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in North Holland, Railway stations opened in 1867, Railway stations on the Staatslijn K

Uitgeest is a railway station located in Uitgeest and the village of  Assum, the Netherlands. It is on the Den Helder–Amsterdam railway and the branch Haarlem–Uitgeest railway. The station opened 1 May 1867. A new station building was opened in 2006. The station has 3 platforms.
