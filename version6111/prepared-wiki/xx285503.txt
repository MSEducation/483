[[Ahmed Borhane]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1995 births, Djiboutian swimmers

Borhane Abro, (born 1995) is a Djiboutian swimmer.http://www.bcn2013.com/en/swimming/detail/member/borhane-ahmed-mohamed-abro He competed in the 50, 100 m freestyle, 50 m butterfly and 100 m butterfly events at the 2012 FINA World Swimming Championships (25 m). Borhane also took part in the 50 m freestyle and 50 m butterfly events at the 2013 World Aquatics Championships.
