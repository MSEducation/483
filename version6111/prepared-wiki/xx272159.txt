[[Cappsia]]

CATEGORIES: Chrysauginae, Monotypic lepidoptera genera

Cappsia is a genus of snout moths. It was described by Pastrana in 1953 and contains the species C. bourquini. It is found in Argentina.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.nic.funet.fi/pub/sci/bio/life/insecta/lepidoptera/ditrysia/pyraloidea/pyralidae/chrysauginae/cappsia/index.html |title=Cappsia at funet |publisher=Nic.funet.fi |date= |accessdate=2011-09-29[/tpl]
