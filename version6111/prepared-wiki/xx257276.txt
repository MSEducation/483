[[Brussels International 1910]]

CATEGORIES: World's Fairs in Brussels, 1910 in Belgium, Colonial exhibitions

Exposition Universelle et Internationale was a world's fair held in Brussels in 1910 from 23 April to 1 November.Findling and Pelle, Encyclopedia of World's Fairs and Expositions, 9780786434169 p206 This was just thirteen years after the previous Brussels world's fair.
It received 13 million visitors, covered 220 acres and lost 100,000 Belgian Francs.Findling and Pelle, Encyclopedia of World's Fairs and Expositions, 9780786434169 p415
A major site for the exhibition was the Mont des Arts, although the site was largely demolished during the post-war construction process of Brusselization.

==Legacy==

The Hotel Astoria, Brussels was built for the fair, and is now a protected monument.
