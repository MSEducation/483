Kana Oyama (大山 加奈 Ōyama Kana , born June 19, 1984) is a Japanese volleyball player who plays for Toray Arrows.
She competed at the 2004 Summer Olympics in Athens, Greece, wearing the #13 jersey. There she and the Japan women's national team took fifth place. Oyama played as a wing-spiker.　Toray announced her retirement on 29 June 2010.Toray website(ja)
