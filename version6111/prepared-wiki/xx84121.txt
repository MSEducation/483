[[Achi, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Achi|阿智村|Achi-mura[/tpl] is a village located in Shimoina District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
Achi was the birthplace of the photographer and illustrator Motoichi Kumagai, who worked as a teacher in the village and photographed its life; the village has a permanent gallery of Kumagai's work, Kumagai Motoichi Shashin Dōgakan.Kumagai Motoichi Shashin Dōgakan [tpl]ja icon[/tpl]
On March 31, 2009 the village of Seinaiji, also in Shimoina District, was merged into Achi.http://www.kokudo.or.jp/new/cities/sub/chubu/20.htm [tpl]Dead link|date=September 2011[/tpl] With the merger, the population became 7,075. The total area of the village is 214.47 km².[tpl]cite web|title=阿智村の紹介 > 阿智村の概要|url=http://www.vill.achi.nagano.jp/2009/06/post-51/|work=Achi official website|publisher=Achi Village|accessdate=September 19, 2011|language=Japanese[/tpl]
