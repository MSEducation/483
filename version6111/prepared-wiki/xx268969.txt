Ellina Anissimova (born 7 July 1992 in Narva, Estonia) is an Estonian hammer thrower. 
On 29 July 2011, she won the Estonian championship.Молотом по всей спортивной Европе, DzD.ee, 20.08.2011 On 16 September 2011, she achieved her personal best throw, [tpl]convert|60.23|m|ft[/tpl], an Estonian record.Sündis uus Eesti rekord juunioride vasaraheites, Õhtuleht, 16.09.2011
Her coaches are Sergei Anissimov and Vadim Devyatovskiy.
