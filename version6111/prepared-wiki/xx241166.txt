[[1st Indian Infantry Brigade]]

CATEGORIES: British Indian Army brigades

The 1st Indian Infantry Brigade was an Infantry formation of the Indian Army during World War II. It was formed in September 1939, in Abbottabad in India. It was assigned to HQ Rawalpindi District until May 1942, when it joined the 23rd Indian Infantry Division until the end of the war.[tpl]cite web|accessdate=2009-10-22|publisher=Order of Battle|title=75 Indian Brigade|url=http://www.ordersofbattle.com/UnitData.aspx?UniX=63309&Tab=Uhi[/tpl]
