[[Šibenik-Knin County]]

CATEGORIES: Counties of Croatia, Dalmatia, Šibenik-Knin County

Šibenik-Knin County ([tpl]lang-hr|Šibensko-kninska županija[/tpl]) is a county in Croatia, located in north-central Dalmatia. Its center is Šibenik; other notable towns are Knin, Drniš and Skradin.
The county covers 3000 km2. It includes 242 islands and national parks Krka and Kornati.

==Population==

According to the 2011 census, Šibenik-Knin County has a population of 109,375. Croats make up a majority with 87.39% of the population.[tpl]Croatian Census 2011|E|15[/tpl]
