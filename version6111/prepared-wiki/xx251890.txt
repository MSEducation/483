[[Shimo-Goshiro Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Tochigi Prefecture, Stations of Tobu Railway, Railway stations opened in 1929, 1929 establishments in Japan

[tpl]Nihongo|Shimo-goshiro Station|下小代駅|Shimo-goshiro-eki[/tpl] is a railway station on the Tobu Nikko Line in Nikkō, Tochigi, Japan, operated by the private railway operator Tobu Railway.

==Lines==

Shimo-goshiro Station is served by the Tobu Nikko Line, and is 78.5 km from the starting point of the line at [tpl]Ja-stalink|Tōbu-Dōbutsu-Kōen[/tpl].

==Station layout==

The station is unmanned with a single island platform connected to the station entrance by a footbridge.

In fiscal 2011, the station was used by an average of 215 passengers daily.
