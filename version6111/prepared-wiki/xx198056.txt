[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Jequié]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1978, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Vitória da Conquista, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Jequié ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Iequieanus[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Jequié in the Ecclesiastical province of Vitória da Conquista in Brazil.
