[[Gaujiena]]

CATEGORIES: Towns and villages in Latvia, Castles of the Teutonic Knights, Kreis Walk

Gaujiena is a village along the Gauja River in Gaujiena parish, Ape municipality, Latvia. It was the seat of a Komtur of the Teutonic Knights. A castle was erected in the 13th century, but fell into disrepair in the 18th century.
