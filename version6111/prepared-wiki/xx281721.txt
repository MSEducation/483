[[Robert B. Hauser]]

CATEGORIES: American cinematographers

Robert B. Hauser was an American cinematographer that worked on more than 70 television and film projects from 1958 to 1994. He died in 1994 aged 75.

==Work==

===Film and Television===

For a period of 10 years from 1958 to 1968 he worked mainly in television as director of photography. From 1958 to 1959 he worked on 12 episodes of Man with a Camera that starred Charles Bronson.IMDB Full cast and crew for 'Man with a Camera' In 1960 he did 4 episodes of The Untouchables IMDB Full cast and crew for 'The Untouchables' Others he worked on were Combat!, Breaking Point, Peyton Place,  The Man From Uncle and Trapper John MD'' etc. 
From 1968 to 1981 he worked on many films including The Odd Couple, Riot, Mean Dog Blues and Killjoy IMDB Robert B. Hauser

===Nominations===

He was nominated twice for Primetime Emmy awards. Once in 1963 for TV series Combat and again in 1978 for Roll of Thunder, Hear My Cry.IMDB Robert B. Hauser
