[[Opglabbeek]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Limburg (Belgium)

Opglabbeek is a municipality located in the Belgian province of Limburg. On January 1, 2012 Opglabbeek had a total population of 10,162. The total area is 24.98 km² which gives a population density of 385 inhabitants per km².
