[[Tobias Welz]]

CATEGORIES: 1977 births, Living people, German football referees

Tobias Welz (born July 11, 1977) is a German football referee. He has officiated in the German Football Association (DFB) since 1999. His made his Bundesliga debut at 28 August 2010 in a game between 1. FC Nuremberg and SC Freiburg.
