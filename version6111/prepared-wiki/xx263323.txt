[[PS Kathleen Mavourneen (1885)]]

CATEGORIES: 1885 ships, Passenger ships of the United Kingdom, Steamships, Mersey-built ships, Ships of the London and North Western Railway, Ships of the Lancashire and Yorkshire Railway

PS Kathleen Mavourneen was a paddle steamer passenger vessel operated by the Drogheda Steam Packet Company from 1855 to 1902 and the  Lancashire and Yorkshire Railway from 1902 to 1903.Railway and Other Steamers, Duckworth. 1962

==History==

She was built by A. Jack and Co of Seacombe for the Drogheda Steam Packet Company and transferred to the Lancashire and Yorkshire Railway in 1902 when they took over the business of the Drogheda company.
In 1903 she was sold and scrapped in the Netherlands.
