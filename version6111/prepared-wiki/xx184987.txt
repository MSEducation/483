[[Saatlen]]

CATEGORIES: District 12 of Zürich

Saatlen is a quarter in the district 12 of Zurich, located in the Glatt Valley (German: Glattal).
It was formerly a part of Schwamendingen municipality, which was incorporated into Zurich in 1934.
The quarter has a population of 6,727 distributed on an area of 1.13 km².
