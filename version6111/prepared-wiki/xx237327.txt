[[Momo division]]

CATEGORIES: Subdivisions of Cameroon

Momo is one of the Divisions of the North West Province of Cameroon, now North West Region according to the constitutional changes brought about by the Biya Regime. The other Divisions include Boyo, Bui, Donga-Mantung, Menchum, Ngoh-Kehtunja and Mezam which hosts the Regional Headquarters in Bamenda. Momo Division is peopled by the Widikum tribe, five of whose clans are represented in the Division: the Menemo, the Moghamo, the Ngie, the Ngwaw,and the Oshie. Mbengwi, a small administrative town created by the British, is the Divisional headquarters.
