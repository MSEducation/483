[[Virola venosa]]

CATEGORIES: Virola, Medicinal plants

Virola venosa is a species of tree in the Myristicaceae family.  It is found in Colombia, Venezuela and Brazil (in Amapá, Amazonas, Pará and Rondônia).  It grows 5-30 m tall.
The fruits are ellipsoidal to subglobular, 19-22 mm long and 16-18mm in diameter.
