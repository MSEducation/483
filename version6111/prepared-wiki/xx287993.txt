[[Oceans (EP)]]

CATEGORIES: 2012 EPs, 2012 live albums, Hillsong Music live albums, English-language EPs

Oceans is the third EP by the Australian worship band Hillsong United.http://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=10151761635225813&set=a.168505175812.153990.8800570812&type=1 Released on September 10, 2013, the EP comprises several different versions of the song Oceans (Where Feet May Fail) from the album Zion.http://www.allmusic.com/album/zion-mw0002469943 The song hit number one on the Billboard Christian Songs chart at the end of 2013.[tpl]cite web|title=Christian Songs chart|url=http://www.billboard.com/charts/christian-songs|publisher=Billboard magazine|accessdate=January 1, 2014[/tpl] Billboard referred to the band's name as "United" on the chart.
