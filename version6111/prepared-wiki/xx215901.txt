[[Daniel Pavlov]]

CATEGORIES: 1967 births, Living people, Bulgarian archers, Archers at the 2008 Summer Olympics, Olympic archers of Bulgaria

Daniel Pavlov (born November 6, 1967) is an athlete from Bulgaria, who competes in archery.

==2008 Summer Olympics==

At the 2008 Summer Olympics in Beijing Pavlov finished his ranking round with a total of 618 points, which gave him the 59th seed for the final competition bracket in which he faced Balzhinima Tsyrempilov in the first round. Tsyrempilov won the match by 112-102 and Pavlov was eliminated. Tsyrempilov would lose in the third round against Ryuichi Moriya.Athlete biography: Daniel Pavlov, beijing2008.cn, ret: Aug, 19 2008
