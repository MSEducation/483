[[Harmanjot Khabra]]

CATEGORIES: 1988 births, Living people, People from Punjab, India, I-League players, Association football midfielders, Footballers from Punjab, India international footballers, Indian footballers, Sporting Clube de Goa players, East Bengal F.C. players

Harmanjot Singh Khabra (born 18 December 1988 in Punjab) is an Indian footballer who plays as a midfielder for East Bengal F.C. in the I-League.

==Career==

===Sporting Goa===

After spending his youth career at the Tata Football Academy[tpl]cite web|title=East Bengal Snap Subhash Singh  And Harmanjot Khabra|url=http://www.goal.com/en-india/news/222/transfer-zone/2009/04/30/1237581/east-bengal-snap-subhash-singh-and-harmanjot-khabra|work=Goal.com|accessdate=29 March 2013[/tpl] Khabra joined Sporting Clube de Goa in the I-League for which he scored one goal for on 15 November 2008 against East Bengal which would turn out to be Sporting's only goal of the game as the club lost 3–1 on the day.[tpl]cite web|title=Easy for East Bengal|url=http://www.hindu.com/2008/11/16/stories/2008111657651600.htm|work=Hindu|accessdate=29 March 2013[/tpl]

===East Bengal===

On 30 April 2009 it was announced that Khabra had signed with fellow I-League side East Bengal. On 10 November 2012 Khabra scored his first league goal for East Bengal against Air India in which he also scored a second goal as East Bengal won 3–0.[tpl]cite web|title=Air India 0-3 East Bengal – Toothless Airmen floored by Trevor Morgan’s clinical unit|url=http://www.goal.com/en-india/match/93691/air-india-vs-east-bengal/report|work=Goal.com|accessdate=29 March 2013[/tpl] East Bengal's vice captain is targeting to score more goals regularly and hopes that it will help him to earn a place in Indian Football Team.[tpl]cite web|last=George|first=Joseph|title= Harmanjot Khabra – "I Have To Prove Many Things In Indian Football"|url=http://www.thehardtackle.com/2013/exclusive-harmanjot-khabra-i-have-to-prove-many-things-in-indian-football/|work=The Hard Tackle|accessdate=7 December 2013[/tpl]
