[[Sombreffe]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Belgium, Municipalities of Namur (province)

Sombreffe ([tpl]IPA-fr|sɔ̃.bʁɛf[/tpl]) is a Walloon municipality located in the Belgian province of Namur. On 1 January 2006 the municipality had 7,615 inhabitants. The total area is 35.78 km², giving a population density of 213 inhabitants per km².
