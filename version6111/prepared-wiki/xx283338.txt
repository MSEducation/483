==Biography==

Walter Matthew Gallichan was born in St. Helier, Jersey, the son of John Gallichan, an antiques dealer, and his wife, Elizabeth, a music teacher. Gallichan made his name as a journalist, being one of the earliest members of the staff of the Daily Mail, and was later a pioneer in sex education in Britain. He died on 27 November 1946 in Weston-super-Mare, Somerset.
