Kelly Brannigan (born September 18, 1982 in Torrance, California, U.S.A.[tpl]cite web|title="Deal or No Deal" â€“ Kelly Brannigan bio|url=http://www.nbc.com/Deal_or_No_Deal/models/kelly.shtml|publisher=NBC|accessdate=25 September 2011[/tpl]) is an American model. She is best known as the model holding case #24 on the hit U.S. game show Deal or No Deal from 2005 to 2009.

==Early life==

Kelly Brannigan was born in Torrance California September 18, 1982. She moved to Auburn, New York, after the death of her father Mark in 1993.
She is a 2000 graduate of Auburn High School.[tpl]cite news|last=Wilcox|first=David|title=She thrives to be No. 1|url=http://auburnpub.com/lifestyles/article_d7b46154-2fc6-5491-b8a7-fdf36c75e46a.html|accessdate=25 September 2011|newspaper=The Citizen (Auburn, New York)|date=23 October 2006[/tpl] She earned an associate's degree in hotel management from Monroe Community College in Rochester, New York, and also studied at the Culinary Institute of America.

==Career==

Kelly returned to her Southern California in 2004 to pursue a career in modeling. Less than a year later, she was hired by the prime-time TV game show Deal or No Deal to replace Meghan Markle as the model holding case #24. Brannigan remained on the show, which was hosted by Howie Mandel and aired on NBC, until its cancellation in 2009.
Kelly Brannigan has appeared on TV shows including CSI: Miami, Entourage, and My Fair Brady.  She also has appeared in National TV commercials, including a 2010 AXE Hair ad featuring NFL quarterback Matthew Stafford.
Brannigan was the "Lovely Lady of the Day" at SportsIllustrated.com on July 8, 2011.[tpl]cite news|last=Traina|first=Jimmy|title=Hot Clicks|url=http://sportsillustrated.cnn.com/2011/extramustard/hotclicks/07/08/kelly-brannigan-derek-jeters-top-pop-culture-moments/index.html|accessdate=25 September 2011|publisher=SI.com|date=8 July 2011[/tpl]
