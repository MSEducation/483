[[Moscow Peak]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of Tajikistan

Moscow Peak, also Moskva Peak or Pik Moskva ([tpl]lang-tg|Қуллаи Москва[/tpl], [tpl]lang-ru|Пик Москва[/tpl]), is a 6,785 m peak in western Pamir, located in the south-east of Jirgatol district in Tajikistan's Region of Republican Subordination, about 10 km west of Ismail Samani Peak, Tajikistan's highest mountain.
