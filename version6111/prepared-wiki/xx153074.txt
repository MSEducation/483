[[Lerotholi Polytechnic]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Lesotho, Maseru, Association football clubs established in 1906

Lerotholi Polytechnic is a Lesotho football club based in Maseru. 
Lerotholi Polytechnic Football Club is named after a tertiary institution in Maseru called Lerotholi Polytechnic. This institution was founded in about 1906 during the Life of King Lerotholi 2 of Lesotho after whom it was named.
