[[Focus Management Consultants Limited]]

CATEGORIES: Employment agencies of the United Kingdom

Focus Management is a recruitment company that finds people jobs in the food industry. Focus Management, the food industry recruitment company set up by Stephen Jones and Michael Staniland in 1990 in Manchester.
Formerly known as Carrus, the company was later rebranded and given the name Focus Graduates. Focus Graduates forms part of Focus Management Consultants which is made up of a number of specialist divisions all recruiting into the food and drink industry.http://www.focusmanagementconsultants.co.uk/about-us
