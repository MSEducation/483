[[The Uprising]]

CATEGORIES: 2012 albums, Foreign Beggars albums, Hip hop albums by British artists

The Uprising is the sixth studio album by British rap group Foreign Beggars. It was released on 1 October 2012 through mau5trap.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.beatport.com/release/the-uprising/968613 |title=The Uprising mau5trap :: Beatport |publisher=Beatport |date=2012-10-01[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/gb/album/the-uprising/id561674399 |title=iTunes - Music - The Uprising by Foreign Beggars |publisher=iTunes |date=2012-10-01[/tpl] Most of the mixing was done by Wez Clarke.[tpl]cite web|url=https://twitter.com/WezClarke/status/252863755115970560 |title=Twitter / WezClarke: Foreign Beggars album ... |publisher=Twitter |date=2012-10-01[/tpl]
The group completed their nine-date headline Uprising Tour in 2013, off the back of the album release, supported by True Tiger and Æ±Z. The original dates were from 9 to 19 October 2012, but the tour was postponed due to unforeseen reasons.[tpl]cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/foreignbeggars/posts/10151069615046301 |title=Hi All. Due to... |publisher=Facebook |date=2012-10-06[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=10151042194231301|title=Cover photos |publisher=Facebook|date=2012-09-12[/tpl]
A remix of "Crep Hype" by MRK1 was released as a free download on 22 December 2012.[tpl]cite web|url=https://soundcloud.com/foreignbeggars/kreps-mrk-1-rmx-limited-16bit|title=Foreign Beggars Crep Hype - MRK 1 Remix by ForeignBeggars on SoundCloud - Hear the world's sounds|publisher=SoundCloud|date=2012-12-22[/tpl] "See the Light" features on the FIFA 13 soundtrack as well as the Ministry of Sound compilation Addicted To Bass 2013.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.amazon.co.uk/Addicted-Bass-2013-Various-Artists/dp/B00BIPYGT2|title=Addicted To Bass 2013: Amazon.co.uk: Music|publisher=Amazon|date=2013-03-25[/tpl]
