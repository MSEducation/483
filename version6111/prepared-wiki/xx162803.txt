[[Royal Oak Mall]]

CATEGORIES: Buildings and structures in Auckland, Shopping centres in the Auckland Region

Royal Oak Mall is a shopping centre in Auckland, New Zealand which was expanded in the mid-1980s.[tpl]Cite web | title =Parish, Royal Oak | work =Parish Listings | publisher =The Anglican Diocese of Auckland | url =http://www.auckanglican.org.nz/?sid=188&do=detail&type=parish&id=3406 | accessdate =28 March 2011 [/tpl] It is on the corner of Manukau and Mt Albert Roads in Royal Oak, Auckland City, and has 80 shops, 18 of which are national brands. 
There are 778 of carparks available which are covered and uncovered.
