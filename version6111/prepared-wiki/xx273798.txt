[[Finest Hour (album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2011 albums, Patton Oswalt albums, English-language albums, Albums recorded at the Moore Theatre

Finest Hour is the fourth comedy album by Patton Oswalt. It was released in 2011 under Comedy Central Records.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.amazon.com/Finest-Hour-Explicit/dp/B005LAZK8G/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&qid=1316988783&sr=8-1 |title=Finest Hour Explicit|publisher=Amazon.com |date= |accessdate=2011-09-25[/tpl] It followed the 2009 release My Weakness Is Strong.
