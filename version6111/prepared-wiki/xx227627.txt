[[Jay Hoffman (rugby league)]]

CATEGORIES: 1958 births, Australian rugby league players, Canberra Raiders players, Living people

Jay Hoffman (born 20 January 1958[tpl]cite book|author=Gary Lester (editor)|title=The Sun Book of Rugby League - 1983|year=1983|publisher= John Fairfax Marketing|location=Sydney, New South Wales|isbn=0-909558-83-3|pages=page [/tpl]) is an Australian former professional rugby league player for the 
Canberra Raiders in the New South Wales Rugby League (NSWRL) competition. Hoffman primarily played at hooker. He is the father of Melbourne Storm second-rower, Ryan Hoffman.[tpl]cite news|publisher=The Sydney Morning Herald|title=So it is a family game, after all |url=http://www.smh.com.au/articles/2004/04/16/1082055656494.html?from=storyrhs |accessdate=2008-10-06|date=2004-04-17[/tpl]
Prior to his move to Canberra, Hoffman played for Brisbane's Past Brothers in the Brisbane Rugby League. While playing for Brothers, Hoffman was selected to represent Queensland for three matches. In 1979, he played for the state against New South Wales and Great Britain, and in 1980 against New South Wales in one of the non-State of Origin matches.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Queensland Rugby League|title=Queensland Representatives - H |url=http://www.qrl.com.au/qrl_history/qld_rep_h.php |accessdate=2008-10-06 |archiveurl = http://web.archive.org/web/20080720070015/http://www.qrl.com.au/qrl_history/qld_rep_h.php  |archivedate = 2008-07-20[/tpl]
In 1983 and 1985, Hoffman was awarded the Clubman of the Year at Canberra.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Canberra Raiders|title=Canberra Raiders History - Award Winners |url=http://www.raiders.com.au/www/history/awards/|accessdate=2008-10-06 |archiveurl = http://web.archive.org/web/20080722083752/http://www.raiders.com.au/www/history/awards/  |archivedate = 2008-07-22[/tpl] 
Hoffman left the Raiders in 1987 and finished his playing career in France.
