[[Citra Methodist Episcopal Church-South]]

CATEGORIES: United Methodist churches in Florida, National Register of Historic Places in Marion County, Florida, Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in Florida, Carpenter Gothic churches in Florida, Churches in Marion County, Florida

The Citra Methodist Episcopal Church-South (also known as Citra United Methodist Church) is a historic church in Citra, Florida. It is located at 2010 Northeast 180th Street. On March 5, 1998, it was added to the U.S. National Register of Historic Places.
