[[Nat and Alex Wolff discography]]

CATEGORIES: Film and television discographies, Pop music group discographies, Discographies of American artists

This is a discography of albums made by Nat & Alex Wolff.
==Throwbacks ==	
Throwbacks is the third soundtrack album by The Naked Brothers Band. Season 3 of the Show aired from October 18, 2008 to June 13, 2009. After the band's split in 2009 it was originally cancelled, until it was announced on September 19, 2013 to be released as a Nat & Alex Wolff album instead of a Naked Brothers Band album on October 15, 2013.[tpl]cite web|title=Nat & Alex Wolff announce release of Season 3 music|url=https://gumroad.com/l/throwbacks|work=Gumroad|accessdate=September 20, 2013[/tpl]

==Black Sheep==

Black Sheep is the debut studio album by the sibling duo, Nat & Alex Wolff. This is the brothers' first album since the departure of their Nickelodeon series, The Naked Brothers Band.
