[[WKDK]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in South Carolina, Adult contemporary radio stations in the United States

WKDK (1240 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Variety format and licensed to Newberry, South Carolina, USA.  The station is currently owned by Newberry Broadcasting Company, Inc. and features programing from ABC Radio .[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=WKDK |title=WKDK Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
