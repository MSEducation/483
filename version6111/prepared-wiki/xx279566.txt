[[Holmes à Court Gallery]]

CATEGORIES: Art museums and galleries in Australia, Margaret River, Western Australia

The Holmes à Court Gallery is an art gallery located in the original Vasse Felix winery at Cowaramup (near Margaret River) in Western Australia. It is owned by Janet Holmes à Court.[tpl]cite web|title=Holmes à Court Gallery - Home|url=http://www.holmesacourtgallery.com.au/index.html|publisher=Holmes à Court Gallery|accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Art Gallery|url=http://www.vassefelix.com.au/visit/art-gallery/|publisher=Vasse Felix|accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl]

==Exhibits==

The gallery displays works from the Holmes à Court Collection through a series of seasonal exhibitions. There is a sculpture garden extending into the grounds of the Vasse Felix winery, where sculptures by local artists are displayed in a garden setting. In 2013, the gallery installed The Art of Sound exhibition in collaboration with the National Film and Sound Archive of Australia.[tpl]cite web|title=The Art of Sound : 9 June - 15 September 2013|url=http://www.holmesacourtgallery.com.au/article/the-art-of-sound--9-june-15-september-2013|publisher=Holmes à Court Gallery|accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl]

==History==

The gallery was previously located in East Perth but relocated in October 2010 to the Vasse Felix site.[tpl]cite web|last=Contributor: pentacles22|title=This Gallery has closed in East Perth|url=http://www.tripadvisor.com.au/ShowUserReviews-g255103-d256588-r133563677-Holmes_a_Court_Gallery-Perth_Greater_Perth_Western_Australia.html|publisher=TripAdvisor|accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl]

==Holmes à Court Collection==

The Holmes à Court family was interested in Australian art and commenced a collection in the early 1970s. The collection is now internationally renowned as it has over 4000 artworks, many of them being culturally significant works.
