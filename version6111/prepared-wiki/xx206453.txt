[[Joey Jayne]]

CATEGORIES: Members of the Montana House of Representatives, 1957 births, Living people, Women state legislators in Montana, University of Montana alumni

Joey Jayne is a Democratic Party member of the Montana House of Representatives, representing District 15 since 2000.
