[[Jholabibi]]

CATEGORIES: Hinduism in Bangladesh, Islam in Bangladesh, Regional Hindu gods, Hindu folk deities

Jholabibi is a Hindu goddess and folk deity in Bengal, worshipped in conjunction with the Goddesses Oladevi (the Goddess of Cholera), Ajgaibibi, Chandbibi, Bahadabibi, Jhetunebibi and Asanbibi.Oladevi - Banglapedia
