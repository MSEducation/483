[[Saroda]]

CATEGORIES: Dungarpur district, Villages in Dungarpur district

Saroda is famous village of Dungarpur District. Situated in Sagwara & Sabla line. The population of this village is around 5000. There are all type of cast people living. Recently this village has developed in many area like in : road, education, hospital, well developed homes, Internet, computers.There are SHIV temple(NILKANTHA MANDIR),KRISHNA temple(GOPAL MANDIR),HANUMAN temple and many small temple. main profession is agriculture,teaching,smithwork and kirana shops.
