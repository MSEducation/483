[[Space Cadet (Kid Koala)]]

CATEGORIES: Kid Koala albums, Graphic novels, Articles created via the Article Wizard

Space Cadet is a graphic novel written by Kid Koala. Like his previous graphic novel, Nufonia Must Fall, Space Cadet comes with a soundtrack CD. Drawn completely on etchboards, and is due to be released on September 19 in the UK and Europe, and October 25 in North America.[tpl]cite web |url=http://kidkoala.com/visual/space-cadet-3/ |title=Space Cadet |accessdate=25 September 2011 [/tpl][tpl]cite web |url=http://kidkoala.com/store/product-info.php?pid109.html |title=Space Cadet store page |accessdate=25 September 2011 [/tpl] The story is about a girl astronaut and was inspired by birth of Kid Koala's daughter Maple.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.babelgum.com/series/180209/clips/6003398 |title=Space Cadet interview on Babelgum |accessdate=25 September 2011 [/tpl]
