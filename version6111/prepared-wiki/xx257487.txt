[[1982 Winnipeg Blue Bombers season]]

CATEGORIES: Winnipeg Blue Bombers seasons

The 1982 Winnipeg Blue Bombers finished in 2nd place in the West Division with a 11–5 record. They appeared in the West Final but lost 24–21 to the Edmonton Eskimos.
