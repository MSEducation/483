[[The Inugamis (1976 film)]]

CATEGORIES: 1976 films, Japanese films, Films directed by Kon Ichikawa, Japanese-language films, Toho films

[tpl]Nihongo|The Inugamis|犬神家の一族 |Inugami-ke no ichizoku[/tpl] is a 1976 Japanese film directed by Kon Ichikawa. Ichikawa would remake the film in 2006.
