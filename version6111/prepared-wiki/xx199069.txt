[[Call Me a Mack]]

CATEGORIES: 1993 singles, Usher (entertainer) songs, Debut singles, Music videos directed by F. Gary Gray, Songs written by Usher (entertainer)

"Call Me a Mack" was the debut single released by R&B artist Usher for the soundtrack album Poetic Justice. "Call Me a Mack" was released in 1993 for Epic Records and was produced by Tim Thomas and Teddy Bishop. The song peaked at #56 on the Billboard's Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs chart.
