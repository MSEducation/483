[[Ashburton (Jersey) Limited]]

CATEGORIES: Investment companies, Companies of Jersey, Companies established in 1982, 1982 establishments in Jersey

Ashburton (Jersey) Limited is an investment company located in Jersey, Channel Islands.
Founded in 1982, it is now a subsidiary of The First Rand Group based in South Africa, which is one of the top 10 listed companies of the JSE Securities Exchange. The group employs 39,738 people and has a retail franchise footprint of 680 branches, 4,185 ATMs and has Assets Under Administration of $120b.
