[[Pop Negro]]

CATEGORIES: 2010 albums

Pop Negro is the 4th album from El Guincho. This album was composed by Pablo Díaz-Reixa and recorded in 2010.  Pop Negro was named best Spanish album of 2010 by Rockdelux, while the single "Bombay" and its music video, directed by Nicolás Méndez, were named best song and best music video, respectively.[tpl]cite web|title=El Guincho arrasa en los mejores de 2010 de ‘Rockdelux’|url=http://www.rollingstone.es/noticias/view/el-guincho-arrasa-en-los-mejores-de-2010-de-rockdelux/|work=RollingStone- El Guincho arrasa en los mejores de 2010 de ‘Rockdelux’|publisher=RollingStone|accessdate=27 July 2011[/tpl] The music video for "Bombay" also serves as part of the trailer for a larger film by Méndez, with music by El Guincho.[tpl]cite web|last=Kim|first=Brandon|title=Video Premiere: El Guincho "Bombay" (warning NSFW)|url=http://www.ifc.com/news/2010/09/video-premiere-el-guincho-bomb.php/|work=IFC-"Bombay" Music Video Premiere|publisher=AMC Networks|accessdate=27 July 2011[/tpl]
