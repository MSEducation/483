==Geography==

It separates the island provinces of Cebu and Bohol.[tpl]cite web|title=Cebu Strait: Philippines|url=http://www.geographic.org/geographic_names/name.php?uni=-3340260&fid=4981&c=philippines|work=Geographical Names|publisher=Geographic.org|accessdate=28 October 2013[/tpl] 
The Cebu Strait connects the western part of the Bohol Sea with the Camotes Sea. 

==Transport==

It is a major sea-lane connecting Cebu City on its northern end with port cities in the south such as Dumaguete City and Cagayan de Oro City. 
Mactan Island, on the northern end of the strait, has Mactan International Airport, one of the major airports in the country.
