[[Wales Airport (Alaska)]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in the Nome Census Area, Alaska

Wales Airport [tpl]airport codes|WAA|PAIW|IWK[/tpl] is a state-owned public-use airport located one nautical mile (2 km) northwest of the central business district of Wales, a city in the Nome Census Area of the U.S. state of Alaska.

==Facilities==

Wales Airport has one runway designated 18/36 with a 4,000 x 75 ft (1,219 x 23 m) gravel surface.
