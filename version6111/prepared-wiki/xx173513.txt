[[Chadwell, Leicestershire]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Leicestershire

Chadwell is a small village approximately [tpl]convert|4|mi|km[/tpl] northwest of Melton Mowbray in Leicestershire. 
Chadwell is very close to Wycomb and they share the same church of St.Mary in the same parish. The hamlets are part of Scalford and share the same parish council. The village was originally named Cauldwell, and was once twice the size it is now.
