[[North Staffordshire and South Cheshire League]]

CATEGORIES: English domestic cricket competitions, Football in Cheshire, Football in Staffordshire, ECB Premier Leagues

The North Staffordshire and South Cheshire League is the top level of competition for recreational club cricket in the North Staffordshire and South Cheshire area of England. Since 2001 it has been a designated ECB Premier League.List of ECB Premier Leagues
The competing teams for 2014 are: Burslem, Cheadle (Staffordshire), Hem Heath, Knypersley, Leek, Little Stoke, Longton, J & G Meakin, Porthill Park, Stone, Whitmore, and Wood Lane.
