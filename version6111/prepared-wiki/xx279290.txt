[[Seyfabad, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Seyfabad ([tpl]lang-fa|سيف اباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Seyfābād; also known as Safiābād and Seyfiabad)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3083598|Seyfabad[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 141, in 28 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
