[[Suleh, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Suleh ([tpl]lang-fa|سوله[/tpl], also Romanized as Sūleh and Sooleh; also known as Sallāh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3086401|Suleh[/tpl] is a village in Shivanat Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 514, in 109 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
