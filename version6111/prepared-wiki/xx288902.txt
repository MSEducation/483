[[Sharur, Iran]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Sharur ([tpl]lang-fa|شرور[/tpl], also Romanized as Sharūr, Sharoor, and Shorūr; also known as Sharūn)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3084642|Sharur[/tpl] is a village in Bizineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 926, in 204 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
