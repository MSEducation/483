[[Aghol Beyk-e Sofla]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Aghol Beyk-e Sofla ([tpl]lang-fa|اغلبيك سفلي[/tpl], also Romanized as Āghol Beyk-e Soflá; also known as Aghalbak, Aqalbak Pāin, Ash Oghulbeyn, Ogholbeyg, Oghol Beyg-e Pā’īn, Owghlī Beyg-e Soflá, and Owghol Beyg-e Pā’īn)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3077508|Aghol Beyk-e Sofla[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Bala Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 930, in 229 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
