[[Shimo-Imaichi Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Tochigi Prefecture, Stations of Tobu Railway, Railway stations opened in 1929, 1929 establishments in Japan

[tpl]Nihongo|Shimo-Imaichi Station|下今市駅|Shimo-Imaichi-eki[/tpl] is a railway station in Nikkō, Tochigi, Japan, operated by the private railway operator Tobu Railway.

==Lines==

Shimo-Imaichi Station is served by the 94.5 km Tobu Nikko Line, and also forms the starting point of the 16.2 km Tobu Kinugawa Line to [tpl]Ja-stalink|Shin-Fujiwara[/tpl]. It is 87.4 km from the starting point of the line at [tpl]Ja-stalink|Tōbu-Dōbutsu-Kōen[/tpl].
