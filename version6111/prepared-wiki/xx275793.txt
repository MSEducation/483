[[2011 Telavi Open – Doubles]]

CATEGORIES: 2011 ITF Women's Circuit, 2011 in Georgia (country), Telavi Open

Veronika Kapshay and Agnes Szatmari were the defending champions, but Szatmari chose not to participate. Kapshay partnered up with Julia Cohen but lost in the first round to Tatia Mikadze and Sofia Shapatava. 
Elena Bogdan and Mihaela Buzărnescu won the title, defeating Ekaterine Gorgodze and Anastasia Grymalska, 1–6, 6–1, 10–3 in the final.
