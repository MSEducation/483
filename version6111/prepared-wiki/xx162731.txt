[[Crooked Oak, North Carolina]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Surry County, North Carolina, Unincorporated communities in North Carolina

[tpl]Unreferenced|date=September 2009[/tpl]Crooked Oak is a small unincorporated community in the Stewarts Creek Township of northern Surry County, North Carolina.  The community is centered around the intersection of Old Lowgap Road and Pine Ridge Road.  Prominent landmarks in the community include Zion Hill Primitive Baptist Church and cemetery.
