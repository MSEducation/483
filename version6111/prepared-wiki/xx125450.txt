[[M. R. D. Dattan]]

CATEGORIES: People from Kerala, People from Kochi, 1935 births, 2006 deaths, Indian sculptors, Government College of Fine Arts, Chennai alumni

M R D Dattan (7 July 1935 - 1 August 2006) was a famous artist and sculptor in the state of Kerala, India. Dattan's works include statues of Mahatma Gandhi, Swami Vivekananda and Sree Narayana Guru.Artist and sculptor M R D Dattan passes away, The Hindu, August 1, 2006 via webarchive He had created more than 200 statues of Sree Narayana Guru alone.[tpl]citation needed|date=April 2013[/tpl]
He was the son of Mr Raman (who was the Palace painter of Kochi royal family) and Kavootti. After his studies at Madras School of Art, he returned to Kerala to become the director of Cochin School of Art that his father established.[tpl]citation needed|date=April 2013[/tpl]
His other works include statues of C. Kesavan, Vallathol Narayanamenon, Dr. Ambedkar, Panambilli Govindamenon, R. Venkata Raman and "Guruvayur Kesavan".[tpl]citation needed|date=April 2013[/tpl] He was also the recipient of the Lalith Kala Akademi fellowship.
