[[Jim McCaffrey]]

CATEGORIES: 1951 births, Living people, English footballers, People from Luton, People educated at St Columba's College, St Albans, Association football midfielders, The Football League players, Nottingham Forest F.C. players, Mansfield Town F.C. players, Huddersfield Town F.C. players, Portsmouth F.C. players, Northampton Town F.C. players

James "Jim" McCaffrey (born October 12, 1951 in Luton) was a professional footballer, who played for Nottingham Forest, Mansfield Town, Huddersfield Town, Portsmouth and Northampton Town.
