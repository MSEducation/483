[[Luigi Ferrando]]

CATEGORIES: 1941 births, Living people, People from the Province of Piacenza, Italian bishops, Brazilian bishops, 20th-century Roman Catholic priests, 20th-century Roman Catholic bishops

Luigi Ferrando (Dom Luís) (born January 22, 1941) is the current bishop of the Roman Catholic Diocese of Bragança do Pará.

==Biography==

Ferrando was born in Agazzano, in the Province of Piacenza. He was ordained priest on May 1, 1965 by the auxiliary bishop of the Roman Catholic Diocese of San Miniato Paolo Ghizzoni.
On May 5, 1996 he received episcopal ordination of Bragança do Pará from cardinal Ersilio Tonini. He replaces the previous bishop of Bragança Miguel Maria Giambelli.

==Awards==

Mons. Ferrando received the "Antonino d'oro" of the Province of Piacenza.[tpl]it[/tpl]www.liberta.it
