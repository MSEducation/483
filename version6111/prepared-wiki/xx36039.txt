[[Greg Howe (album)]]

CATEGORIES: Greg Howe albums, 1988 debut albums, Shrapnel Records albums, Albums produced by Mike Varney

Greg Howe is the self-titled first studio album by guitarist Greg Howe, released in 1988 through Shrapnel Records. In a 2009 article by Guitar World magazine, the album was ranked tenth on the all-time top ten list of shred albums.Guitar World Staff (2009-07-09). "Top 10 Shred Albums of All Time". Guitar World. Future US. Archived from the original on 2011-05-28. Retrieved 2014-03-04. Prior to its recording, Howe had sent a demo tape to Shrapnel founder Mike Varney in 1987, upon which he was signed to the label. As of 2006 it remains Howe's best-selling album.Malusardi, Guglielmo (October 2006). "Interview with Greg Howe". Guitar Nine Records. Retrieved 2014-03-04.
