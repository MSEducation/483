[[Isidra Vega]]

CATEGORIES: 1979 births, Living people, American actresses

Isidra Vega (born January 12, 1979) is an American actress of Hispanic descent, born and raised in the Morningside Heights section of New York City'.  
Vega acted in the New York Undercover television series, and she debuted as a movie actress in 1997, as 'Melena', in the critically acclaimed movie Hurricane Streets. In Hurricane Streets, her character was that of an abused child who wanted to run away from her father.
Vega has gone on to play more adult characters in other movies, but her career has been made up, so far, of low budget movie jobs only.
