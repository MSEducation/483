[tpl]Nihongo|Yoshu Fukushu|予襲復讐|Yoshū Fukushū[/tpl] is the sixth studio album by Japanese rock band Maximum the Hormone. It was released on July 31, 2013. It marks the longest gap between the band albums, excluding the 2011 Maxi Single Greatest the Hits 2011–2011, having been six years since the band's last full-length album.
