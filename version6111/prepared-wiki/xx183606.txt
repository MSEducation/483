[[John Bonk]]

CATEGORIES: 1950 births, Living people, Hamilton Tiger-Cats players, Winnipeg Blue Bombers players, Grey Cup champions, Players of Canadian football from Ontario, Canadian football offensive linemen, Canadian Football Hall of Fame inductees, Sportspeople from Hamilton, Ontario

John Bonk (born August 27, 1950, in Stoney Creek, Ontario) is a former all-star offensive lineman in the Canadian Football League. The four-time All-Star played from 1973 to 1985 for the Winnipeg Blue Bombers. Bonk won the CFL's Most Outstanding Offensive Lineman Award in 1984. He also won the 72nd Grey Cup with Winnipeg in 1984.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.cfl.ca/article/john_bonk_hof_bio | title=John Bonk HOF Bio | publisher=Canadian Football League[/tpl] 
In 2008, John was inducted into the Canadian Football Hall of Fame.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.cfhof.ca/page/bonkjohn | title=John Bonk[/tpl]
