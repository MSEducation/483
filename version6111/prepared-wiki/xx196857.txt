[[House of Hungarian Wines]]

CATEGORIES: Visitor attractions in Budapest, Tourism in Hungary, Alcohol distribution retailers

The House of Hungarian Wines ([tpl]lang-hu|Magyar Borok Háza[/tpl]) is a wine shop in Budapest, near the Buda Castle.[tpl]cite news|title=House of Hungarian Wines|publisher=The New York Times|url=http://travel.nytimes.com/travel/guides/europe/hungary/budapest/attraction-detail.html?vid=1154684782228&inline=nyt-classifier|accessdate=2008-05-21[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Open Cellars’ Days - 10–12 May|publisher=The Budapest Times|url=http://www.budapesttimes.hu/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=7295&Itemid=29|date=2008-05-07|accessdate=2008-05-21[/tpl] This is one of the largest wine houses of the country.
The entry fee enables one unlimited sampling for two hours.[tpl]cite news|title=36 Hours in Budapest|publisher=The New York Times|url=http://travel.nytimes.com/2007/08/12/travel/12hours.html?scp=10&sq=Budapest&st=nyt|date=2007-08-12|accessdate=2008-05-21 | first=Evan | last=Rail[/tpl]  The House has over 700 wines on display from Hungary’s 22 wine regions.[tpl]cite web|title=Hungary for More: Two Days in Budapest|publisher=Lonely Planet|url=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/cities/pdf_downloads/09-hungary-for-more.pdf|accessdate=2008-05-21[/tpl] The visitors can try another Hungaricum, the pálinka (different types of fruit spirits) specialities also. It is possible to taste a collection of handmade cheeses.
During the wine tasting one can get acquinted with the history, type of wine regions and international and local grape selections.. The House guarantees secure delivery throughout Europe.  
The collection can be visited every day 12:00-20:00.thumb|House of Hungarian Wines
