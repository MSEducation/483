Gastón Filgueira Méndez (born 8 January 1986 in Montevideo) is a Uruguayan football player who plays for Liverpool in the Uruguayan Primera División.

==International career==

Filgueira made his Uruguay debut against Venezuela in 2006. He was recalled to the team for a match against Australia, on 2 June 2007.
