[[Luopioinen]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places disestablished in 2007, Former municipalities of Finland

Luopioinen is a former municipality of Finland, situated about [tpl]convert|60|km|-1[/tpl] from Tampere. It is part of the Pirkanmaa region and in 2007 it was joined to the municipality of Pälkäne. It is a rural town containing a few lakes, most famous being lake Kukkia.
