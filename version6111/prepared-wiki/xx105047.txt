CATEGORIES: Political parties in Ukraine

Solidarity ([tpl]lang-uk|Солідарність[/tpl]) is a political party in Ukraine registered in March 2001. The party is a spin-off of the Social Democratic Party of Ukraine (united).Ukrainian Political Update by Taras Kuzio and Alex Frishberg, Frishberg & Partners, 21 February 2008 (page 22) The party has been always linked to Petro Poroshenko.

==History==

The party started as a parliamentary faction in 2000 created by Social Democratic Party of Ukraine (united) faction member Petro Poroshenko. Taras Kuzio claims with the help of then President Kuchma who allegedly wanted to limit the influence of Social Democratic Party of Ukraine (united). Many deputies elected in 1998 for the Peasant Party of Ukraine joined the new party.
