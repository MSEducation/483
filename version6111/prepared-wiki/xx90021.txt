[[List of hospitals in Thailand]]

CATEGORIES: Lists of buildings and structures in Thailand, Lists of hospitals by country, Hospitals in Thailand

This is a comprehensive list of hospitals in Thailand. As of 2010, there are 1,002 public hospitals, and 316 private hospitals are registered with the Ministry of Public Health's Medical Registration Division.
