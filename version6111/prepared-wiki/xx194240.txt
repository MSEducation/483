Suresh Kumar Alemagar is a Nepalese politician, belonging to the Communist Party of Nepal (Maoist). He was previously working as a lecturer in English at the Nepal Law Campus, Tribhuvan University, Kathmandu.Nepal: Suresh Ale Magar and Pawan Shrestha - Amnesty International
Ale was the founding General Secretary of the Nepal Federation of Indigenous Nationalities and the Nepal Magar Association.Nepal Federation of Indigenous Nationalities (NEFIN) - NEFIN's General Secretary Dr. Om Gurung met Maoist Leaderwww.magar.org: The web portal of Magar Indigenous Peoples of Nepal He has also been the president of the All Nepal Nationalities Organisation.
In 1999 Ale was arrested three times under the Public Security Act. In late 1999, Amnesty International expressed concern over the possibility that Ale had 'disappeared'. However after the visit of an AI official to Nepal, Ale was released by the state authorities in early 2000.AIUK : Two 'disappeared' prisoners released at end of AI visit to Nepal
In 2004 Ale was arrested by Indian authorities, and handed over the regime in Nepal. Ale was at the time active in the International Department of CPN(Maoist) and the World People's Resistance Movement.News &Views Matrika Prasad Yadav and Upendra Yadav were arrested along with Ale.ANFREL
In the 2008 Constituent Assembly election he was elected from the Tanahu-1 constituency, winning 17744 votes.Election Commission of Nepal
