[[Eugene Youth Symphony]]

CATEGORIES: Education in Eugene, Oregon, American youth orchestras, Musical groups from Eugene, Oregon, 1934 establishments in Oregon, Musical groups established in 1934, Youth organizations based in Oregon

The Eugene Youth Symphony is the major youth orchestra serving Eugene, Oregon, United States and its outlying areas. Founded in 1934, the Eugene Youth Symphony is the second-oldest youth orchestra west of the Mississippi. The Eugene Youth Symphony is funded by Arts Umbrella (now known as the Eugene Springfield Youth Orchestras), a Eugene-based non-profit organization that provides arts education opportunities for children in Lane County, OR. The Eugene Youth Symphony performs several concerts annually and is conducted by Dr. Jason Duckles.
