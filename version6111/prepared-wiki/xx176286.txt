[[WTOD]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in South Carolina, Cumulus Media radio stations, Radio stations established in 1968, CBS Sports Radio stations

WTOD (1450 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a sports format.Licensed to Hartsville, South Carolina, USA.  The station is currently owned by Cumulus Media and features programing from CBS Sports Radio.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=WHSC |title=WHSC Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl] It is simulcast with WWFN-FM in Lake City, South Carolina.http://www.krgspec.com/MSAOverview.cfm?AreaID=554, Retrieved on 2009-04-14.
Formerly known as WHSC, the station took the WTOD callsign from a former sister station in Toledo (spun off by Cumulus and now known as WWYC).
