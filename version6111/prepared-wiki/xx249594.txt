[[Shell Cove, New South Wales]]

CATEGORIES: Suburbs of Wollongong, New South Wales

Shell Cove is a seaside suburb in the  Shellharbour area located just south of Wollongong, New South Wales, Australia. Shell Cove adjoins Bass Point and Killalea State Park and includes a variety of facilities and amenities such as: parks and bush reserves, The Links Golf Course, Shell Cove Primary School & pre-schools, Family Health Medical Centre and a general store.
