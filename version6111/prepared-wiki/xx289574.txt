Tom Hughes-Croucher is a British Internet programmer, entrepreneur, author, and public speaker.[tpl]cite web|url=http://velocityconf.com/velocity2012/public/schedule/speaker/6981 |title=Speaker: Tom Hughes-Croucher: Velocity 2012 - O'Reilly Conferences, June 25 - 27, 2012, Santa Clara |publisher=Velocityconf.com |date= |accessdate=2013-12-11[/tpl] He's worked for and with numerous well known brands, including Walmart, Yahoo!, NASA, MySpace, Joyent, Tesco, Three Telecom, and UK Channel 4. He has been the acting Chief Technology Officer for Change.org since November 2012.

==Early life==

Tom Hughes-Croucher was born on October 29, 1981 to David Crocher and Hazel Croucher. He grew up in Worksop, UK.

==Education and Career==

Tom earned his Bachelor of Science in 2005 from the University of Sunderland and was ranked first in his class.[tpl]dead link|date=December 2013[/tpl] After Graduating from the University of Sutherland joined Yahoo as a front end engineer. In 2009 he joined Joyent as a Technical Evanglist.
