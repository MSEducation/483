[[Harry L. Watson]]

CATEGORIES: American historians, University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill faculty, Brown University alumni, Year of birth missing (living people), Living people

Harry L. Watson is an American historian of the antebellum American South, Jacksonian America, and the history of North Carolina. He is Director of the Center for the Study of the American South at the University of North Carolina.
Watson is an alumnus of Brown University and received a Ph.D. from Northwestern University in 1976.
