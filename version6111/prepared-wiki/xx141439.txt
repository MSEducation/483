[[Allphones]]

CATEGORIES: Mobile phone companies of Australia, Privately held companies of Australia

Allphones is an Australian telecommunications retailer which specialises in offering a wide range of mobile and broadband related services across a range of carriers.
Allphones was started in 1989 with the first store in Mile End, South Australia.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.amtgrp.com.au/allphones.html |title=Allphones Rental |publisher=AMT Group |accessdate=31 August 2013[/tpl] The company franchised a further 14 stores in South Australia before it was sold in 2000 by Brian and Helen Warner.http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1G1-121534803.html Allphones has stores in every state and territory with over 170 stores nationally. 
Their products and services include mobile phones, mobile plans, broadband plans, pre-paid services and mobile phone accessories.

==Class action==

In 2008, the ACCC on behalf of 74 Allphones Retail franchisees, launched proceedings against Allphones Retail Pty Ltd as well as then chief executive Matthew Donnellan, director and chief operating officer Tony Baker, and former national franchising manager Ian Harkin as respondents, alleging they were "knowingly concerned in or party to the contravening conduct".[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.accc.gov.au/content/index.phtml/itemId/888657 |title=takes class action on behalf of Allphones franchisees |publisher=ACCC |date=2009-08-18 |accessdate=2013-10-14[/tpl] Matthew Donnellan was removed from his position by the board in April 2011.http://www.theaustralian.com.au/business/allphones-board-terminates-chiefs-contract/story-e6frg8zx-1226039385518
The case was settled outside of court for 3 million to be shared amongst 55 franchisees represented in the action.http://www.theaustralian.com.au/australian-it/punishment-not-enough-for-allphones-bullying-tactics/story-e6frgakx-1225861764812
