[[Fernán Sánchez de Badajoz]]

CATEGORIES: Businesspeople in real estate, Year of birth missing, Year of death missing

Fernán Sánchez de Badajoz was a member of the prominent Sánchez de Badajoz family. In 1369, he took over multitudes of property in Barcarrota, near Badajoz, granted by Enrique II.
