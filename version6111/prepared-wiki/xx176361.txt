[[Martín Stefanonni Mazzocco]]

Martín Stefanonni Mazzocco (born April 30, 1964) is a Mexican politician[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.vanguardia.com.mx/viable,_implantar_la_pena_de_muerte:_jesus_torres_charles_fiscal_de_coahuila-338662.html|title=Viable la pena de muerte: Jesús Torres Charles|date=22 April 2009|publisher=Vanguardia|language=Spanish|accessdate=15 March 2011[/tpl] from San Miguel de Allende, Guanajuato.
A member of the National Action Party (PAN) Stefanonni serves as a deputy in the Chamber of Deputies, to which he was elected in 2006.
