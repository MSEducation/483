Daniel Leo (born 2 October 1982) is a Samoan rugby union footballer.

==Career==

Leo can play in the second row or as a back row forward. Since 2010, he has played for Union Bordeaux Bègles in the Top 14 having played several seasons for London Wasps in the Premiership. He also plays for Samoa.
Before joining London Wasps, Leo, who was born in New Zealand but moved to Australia when he was 18, played for Sunnybank in the Queensland Competition, as well as the Queensland Reds. He made his international debut for Samoa at the Telstra Stadium in Sydney against Australia in June 2005. He was included in the Pacific Islanders squad that toured Europe in late 2006. He was also selected in the Samoa squad for the 2007 Rugby World Cup and the 2011 Rugby World Cup.
