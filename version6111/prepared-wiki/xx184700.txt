[[2008 Sport Club Corinthians Paulista season]]

CATEGORIES: Sport Club Corinthians Paulista seasons, Brazilian football clubs 2008 season

The 2008 season is the 98th season in Sport Club Corinthians Paulista's existence, and their first in Brazil's second division.
