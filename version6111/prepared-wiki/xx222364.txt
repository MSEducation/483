Janusz Gol (born November 11, 1985 in Świdnica) is a Polish footballer who plays as a midfielder for Amkar Perm in the Russian Premier League.

==Career==

===Club===

In February 2011, he joined Legia Warsaw on a three and a half year contract.Felix Ogbuke i Janusz Gol zagrają wiosną w Legii 24.02.2011, legia.com
In July 2013, he joined Amkar Perm on a two years contract.http://fc-amkar.org/news/?id=28132
