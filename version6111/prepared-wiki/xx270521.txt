[[Anyay Abichar]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 films, Bengali-language films, Indian films, Indian action films

Anyay Abichar is a 1985 Bengali-language Indian feature film directed by Shakti Samanta, starring Mithun Chakraborty, Rozina and Utpal Dutt

==Plot==

Anyay Abichar is an action film starring Mithun Chakraborty in the lead role, supported by Utpal Dutt.
