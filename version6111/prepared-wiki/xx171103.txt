[[Atopochilus]]

CATEGORIES: Mochokidae, Fish of Africa, Fauna of Cameroon, Fauna of Equatorial Guinea

Atopochilus is a genus of upside-down catfishes native to Africa.

==Species==

There are currently seven recognized species in this genus: [tpl]FishBase genus | genus = Atopochilus| month = December | year = 2011[/tpl]

==Description==

Atopochilus species have their lips and barbels modified into a suckermouth.[tpl]cite book|title=Fishes of the World|last=Nelson|first=Joseph S.|publisher=John Wiley & Sons, Inc|year=2006|isbn=0-471-25031-7[/tpl] Atopochilus species range in size from 6.0–14.1 centimetres (2.4–5.6 in) in length.
