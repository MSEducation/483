[[A.S.D. Thermal Abano Teolo]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Italy, Football clubs in Veneto

A.S.D. Thermal Abano Teolo is an Italian football club based in Abano Terme and Teolo, Veneto. Currently it plays in Italy's Serie D.

==History==

===Serie D===

In the season 2012-13 the team was promoted for the first time, from Eccellenza Veneto/A to Serie D.

==Colors and badge==

The team's colors are red and green.
