[[Andrii Toptun]]

CATEGORIES: Ukrainian marathon runners, Ukrainian long-distance runners, Year of birth missing (living people), Living people

Andrii Toptun ([tpl]lang-uk|Андрій Топтун[/tpl]) is a Ukrainian marathon runner. He won the 2008 Cellcom Green Bay Marathon, finishing with the time of 2:22:51.
