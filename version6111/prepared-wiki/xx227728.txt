[[Lee Tae-Woo]]

CATEGORIES: 1984 births, Living people, Association football midfielders, South Korean footballers, Daegu FC players, K League players, Korea National League players

Lee Tae-Woo ([tpl]lang-ko|이태우[/tpl]; born 8 January 1984) is a South Korean football player who currently plays for Korea National League side Suwon City FC. He has spent his career at Daegu FC in the K-League.
