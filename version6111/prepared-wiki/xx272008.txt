[[Jang Ik-jae]]

CATEGORIES: South Korean golfers, Japan Golf Tour golfers, 1973 births, Living people

Jang Ik-jae (born 14 February 1973), also known as I.J. Jang, is a South Korean professional golfer.
Jang plays on the Japan Golf Tour, where he has won twice.
