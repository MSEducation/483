[[Synurus]]

Synurus is a genus of flowering plants in the daisy family.
It is often regarded as a monotypic genus containing the single species Synurus deltoides.Synurus. Flora of China. Some authorities accept a second species, S. excelsus.Synurus. The Plant List. 2010. Other species names, such as S. atriplicifolius, S. hondae, S. palmatopinnatifidus, and S. pungens, are synonyms of S. deltoides.Synurus deltoides. Flora of China.
Synurus deltoides is native to Asia. It has a thick stem up to 1.5 meters tall. It produces large, nodding, spherical flower heads up to 6 centimeters wide with purple florets.
