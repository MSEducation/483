[[Pine Mountain Ridge (California)]]

CATEGORIES: Transverse Ranges, Mountain ranges of Ventura County, California, Los Padres National Forest, Mountain ranges of Southern California

Pine Mountain Ridge is a Transverse Range located in northern Ventura County, and entirely within the Los Padres National Forest.http://www.magney.org/photofiles/PineMtnRegion1.htm
Major peaks on the ridge include, from west to east, Reyes Peak, Haddock Peak, and Thorn Point.
