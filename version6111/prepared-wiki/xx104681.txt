[[Stade du 26 Mars]]

CATEGORIES: Football venues in Mali, Athletics (track and field) venues in Mali, National stadiums, Buildings and structures in Bamako

Stade du 26-Mars is located in the southern neighborhoods of Bamako, Mali. It serves as a home ground for domestic football club Stade Malien and is the national stadium. It has a capacity of 50,000 as an all-seater stadium.
