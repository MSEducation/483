[[Gjaerumiaceae]]

CATEGORIES: Ustilaginomycotina, Plant pathogens and diseases, Fungi families

The Gjaerumiaceae are a family of smut fungi in the Basidiomycota, class Exobasidiomycetes. The family is monotypic, and contains the single genus Gjaerumia.
