[[Bank Saint Petersburg]]

CATEGORIES: Banks of Russia, Companies based in Saint Petersburg, Companies established in 1990, Companies listed on the Moscow Exchange

Bank Saint Petersburg is a Russian bank created in 1990 in Saint Petersburg. According to the bank official website, Saint Petersburg Bank is mostly implemented in Northwest Russia.Saint Petersburg Bank, official website The bank is listed on Moscow Exchange.
Alexander Savelyev is the chairman of the bank's management board.
