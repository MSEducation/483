[[Tan-Tar-A Resort Seaplane Base]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in Missouri, Seaplane bases in the United States, Buildings and structures in Camden County, Missouri

Tan-Tar-A Resort Seaplane Base [tpl]airport codes|||19T[/tpl] is a privately owned, public-use seaplane base located six nautical miles (11 km) north of the central business district of Osage Beach, in Camden County, Missouri, United States. It is serves the Tan-Tar-A Resort, located on the Lake of the Ozarks.Tan-Tar-A Resort, official site

==Facilities==

Tan-Tar-A Resort Seaplane Base has one seaplane landing area designated NW/SE which measures 13,000 x 1,200 feet (3,962 x 366 m).
