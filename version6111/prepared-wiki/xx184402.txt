[[Trisector]]

CATEGORIES: Van der Graaf Generator albums, 2008 albums, Virgin Records albums

Trisector is a studio album by the British rock group Van der Graaf Generator. It was released on Virgin/EMI Records in March 2008. It is an important release for Van der Graaf Generator because it is the first album the band recorded as a trio. Saxophonist David Jackson departed the band following the 2005 tour.

==Track listing==

All songs by Hugh Banton, Guy Evans and Peter Hammill except where noted.
