[[Faku County]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Liaoning

Faku County ([tpl]zh|s=法库县 |t=法庫縣 |p=Fǎkù Xiàn[/tpl]) is a county in the north of Liaoning province, China, under the administration of the provincial capital of Shenyang, the downtown of which is [tpl]convert|77|km|abbr=on[/tpl] to the south. [tpl]asof|2010[/tpl], it has a population of 449,996 residing in an area of [tpl]convert|2320|km2|abbr=on[/tpl]. It lies on China National Highway 203.

==Administrative divisions==

There are 8 towns, 11 townships, and 1 ethnic township within the county.[ref]
