[[Gmina Zembrzyce]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Lesser Poland Voivodeship, Sucha County

Gmina Zembrzyce is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Sucha County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. Its seat is the village of Zembrzyce, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|5|km|mi|0[/tpl] north of Sucha Beskidzka and [tpl]convert|40|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-west of the regional capital Kraków.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|39.9|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 5,533.

==Villages==

Gmina Zembrzyce contains the villages and settlements of Marcówka, Śleszowice, Tarnawa Dolna, Tarnawa Górna and Zembrzyce.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Zembrzyce is bordered by the town of Sucha Beskidzka and by the gminas of Budzów, Maków Podhalański, Mucharz, Stryszawa, Stryszów and Wadowice.
