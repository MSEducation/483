[[Paul Dennert]]

CATEGORIES: Members of the South Dakota House of Representatives, South Dakota State Senators, 1937 births, Living people, South Dakota Democrats, American farmers, People from Brown County, South Dakota, American cattlemen

H. Paul Dennert is a Democratic member of the South Dakota House of Representatives, representing District 2 since 2005. He earlier served in the House from 1993 through 1996, and the South Dakota Senate from 1997 through 2004.
