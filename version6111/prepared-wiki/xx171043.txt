[[Bronco McLoughlin]]

CATEGORIES: Irish male film actors, Living people, Stunt performers, Year of birth missing (living people)

Bronco McLoughlin is an Irish actor, stuntman and animal trainer with more than 30 years experience in Hollywood movies.  
He has performed and co-ordinated stunts in more than 40 movies including
In the horror movie by Clive Barker, Hellbound: Hellraiser II, because of a neck accident, Kenneth Cranham only wore the Channard Cenobite makeup for one day, McLoughlin played the Channard Cenobite for the rest of the shoot. He also played the part of the assassin in Martin Scorsese's Gangs of New York.

==Awards==
