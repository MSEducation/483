[[Avaş]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Yardymli District

Avaş is a village and municipality in the Yardymli Rayon of Azerbaijan.  It has a population of 962.  The municipality consists of the villages of Avaş, Qaravuldaş, and Deman.Belediyye Informasiya Sistemi [tpl]dead link|date=January 2013[/tpl]
