[[Pilatovica]]

CATEGORIES: Kopaonik

Pilatovica ([tpl]lang-sq|Pilatovicë[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-sr|Pilatovica[/tpl], Cyrillic: Пилатовица) is a mountain peak in the southern part of the Kopaonik range, in northern Kosovo[tpl]ref label|status|a|[/tpl]. It is 1,703m high and just a few kilometres north-west of the Šatorica peak, which is 1,750m high.
