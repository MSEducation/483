[[Septet (album)]]

CATEGORIES: ECM Records albums, Chick Corea albums, 1985 albums

Septet is an album by pianist Chick Corea featuring music for string quartet, piano, flute and French horn recorded in 1984 and released on the ECM label.ECM discography accessed September 23, 2011  

==Reception==

The Allmusic review awarded the album 2 stars.Allmusic Review accessed September 23, 2011
