[[Italian Team of the Century]]

CATEGORIES: Lists of players of Australian rules football, Australian Football League awards, Australian rules football awards, Australian rules football representative teams, Best of century awards, Italian-Australian culture

The Victorian Football League-Australian Football League (VFL/AFL) Italian Team of the Century was announced in June 2007. It recognises the role of Italian Australian players in the sport.Italian Team of the Century
Players and/or at least one of the player's parents or grandparents had to have been born in Italy.  They also had to have played at least one game at VFL/AFL level.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.italianteamofthecentury.com.au/criteria.php |title=Selection Criteria |work=Italian Team of the Century[/tpl]

==The team==

The final selected team was announced at the Palladium Room at the Crown Casino in Melbourne on 31 May 2007.[tpl]cite web |url=http://afl.com.au/Season2007/News/NewsArticle/tabid/208/Default.aspx?newsId=44390 |title=Italian Team of the Century named |work=AFL.com |publisher=BigPond |date=1 June 2007|archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20110810221348/http://www.afl.com.au/Season2007/News/NewsArticle/tabid/208/Default.aspx?newsId=44390|archivedate=10 August 2011[/tpl]

A team President was also named: Frank Costa.
