[[29477 Zdíkšíma]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Astronomical objects discovered in 1997

29477 Zdíkšíma is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1463.7470713 days (4.01 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=29477 | title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-05-24 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on October 31, 1997.
