[[Teleflorist]]

CATEGORIES: Companies established in 1947, Companies based in Hampshire, Florist companies, Distribution, retailing, and wholesaling, 1947 establishments in the United Kingdom

Teleflorist is the trading name for British Teleflower Service Limited, and has about 1100 members. Teleflorist was established in 1947 in Hampshire, England, and has remained privately owned.
Teleflorist is part of Teleflora and provides same day local flower delivery in 140 countries.
As of October 2009, Teleflorist was rebranded as "eFlorist". It was acquired by the larger Euroflorist group in 2010
