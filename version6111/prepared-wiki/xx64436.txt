Berry's Canal, the small canal that was constructed under direction of Alexander Berry at the Coolangatta Estate to link the Shoalhaven River and the Crookhaven River now forms the main Shoalhaven River estuary, with the former entrance to the Shoalhaven River at Shoalhaven Heads usually closed to the ocean, except during floods.[tpl]cite web | title = Estuaries in NSW | author = NSW Department of Natural Resources | url = http://www.naturalresources.nsw.gov.au/estuaries/inventory/shoalhaven.shtml | accessdate = 19 October 2009 | archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20091013095211/http://www.naturalresources.nsw.gov.au/estuaries/inventory/shoalhaven.shtml| archivedate= 13 October 2009 | deadurl= no[/tpl]
