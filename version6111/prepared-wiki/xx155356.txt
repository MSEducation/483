[[Protea welwitschii]]

CATEGORIES: Protea, Flora of South Africa

Protea welwitschii (cluster-head sugarbush) is a multistemmed shrub or small gnarled tree occurring in grassland and bushveld. Leaves are bluish green and hairy at the base when mature. Flowers usually in clusters of 3 or 4. Fruit is a densely hairy nut.
