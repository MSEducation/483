[[Chuzak]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Chuzak ([tpl]lang-fa|چوزك[/tpl], also Romanized as Chūzak; also known as Chizuk, Chūzok, Chūzūk, Jūrak, and Jūrīk)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3058921|Chuzak[/tpl] is a village in Sojas Rud Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,558, in 363 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
