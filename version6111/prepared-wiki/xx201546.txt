[[1998 Men's World Floorball Championships]]

CATEGORIES: 1998 in sports, 1998 in floorball, International sports competitions hosted by the Czech Republic, Floorball World Championships

The 1998 Men's Floorball Championships were the second world championships in men's floorball. It was held in May 1998 in the Czech Republic, and won by Sweden.
