[[Kiewit airfield]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in Belgium, Limburg (Belgium)

Kiewit Airfield [tpl]Airport codes||EBZH[/tpl] is a recreational airfield located near Hasselt, Limburg, Belgium. It has a single grass runway of 600 m length.Belgian official AIP It is operated by Aeroclub Kiewit, and is also home to gliding club Albatros.
Like most recreational fields in Belgium, its use is subject to prior permission, but in practice that is only a formality of a single phone call.
