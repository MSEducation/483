[[Ayumi Beppu]]

CATEGORIES: Japanese actresses, 1983 births, Living people, People from Sakai, Osaka

[tpl]nihongo|Ayumi Beppu|別府 あゆみ|Beppu Ayumi|born June 14, 1983[/tpl] is a Japanese actress and tarento from Sakai, Osaka Prefecture. She is chiefly known for her role as [tpl]nihongo|Houka Ozu/Magi Pink|小津 芳香／マジピンク|Ozu Hōka/Maji Pinku[/tpl] in Mahou Sentai Magiranger.
