Gian-Marco Crameri (born December 13, 1972) is a Swiss professional ice hockey defenceman who currently plays for EHC Olten in Switzerland's National League B.
Crameri has participated as a member of the Swiss national team in numerous international tournaments, including the 2002 Winter Olympics.
