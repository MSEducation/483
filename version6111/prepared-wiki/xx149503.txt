[[Collie Buddz]]

CATEGORIES: 1981 births, Living people, Reggae fusion artists, Dancehall musicians, Soca musicians, Bermudian reggae musicians, Musicians from New Orleans, Louisiana, American reggae musicians, American hip hop singers

Collie Buddz (born Colin Patrick Harper, August 21, 1984) is a reggae artist best known for his single "Come Around".[tpl]cite web|url=[tpl]Allmusic|class=artist|id=p843767/biography|pure_url=yes[/tpl]|title=Biography: Collie Buddz|last=Jeffries|first=David|publisher=AMG|accessdate=15 May 2010[/tpl] Although born in New Orleans, Louisiana, he was raised in Bermuda. He studied audio engineering at Full Sail University in Orlando, FL.http://www.miaminewtimes.com/2007-05-24/music/a-star-is-buddz/ He performed on Shaggy's 2007 album Intoxication on the track "Mad Mad World". In 2008, he performed the song "SOS" on WWE The Music, Vol. 8 as the entrance theme for wrestler Kofi Kingston. He also appeared in a 2009 remix of Kid Cudi's single "Day 'n' Nite". His name is based on slang for cannabis, and he has been photographedhttp://mflb.tumblr.com/post/21861979350/collie-buddz-and-his-new-mflb flaunting a device commonly used for its consumption.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.marijuana.com/threads/question-about-magic-flight-launch-box.278571/|title=Question about magic flight launch box.[/tpl] He launched his own record label 'Harper Digital.' Buddz has built an extensive touring machine, selling out venues across the US and worldwide. He is currently on tour in Amphitheaters with Rebelution, Matisyahu and Zion I on the Good Vibes tour. Buddz' festival appearances include Lollapalooza, Outside Lands, Summer Jam, Reggae on the Rocks, California Roots Festival, UCLA JazzReggae, Seattle City Arts festival, and the Marley Family's 9 Mile Music Festival, among others.
