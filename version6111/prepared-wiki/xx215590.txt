[[Jaminy]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Augustów County

Jaminy [tpl]IPAc-pl|j|a|'|m|i|n|y[/tpl], ([tpl]lang-lt|Jominai[/tpl]), is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Sztabin, within Augustów County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|7|km|mi|0[/tpl] west of Sztabin, [tpl]convert|21|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south of Augustów, and [tpl]convert|62|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of the regional capital Białystok.
