[[Liechtensteiner National Badminton Championships]]

CATEGORIES: Badminton in Liechtenstein, National badminton championships, Recurring sporting events established in 1986, Sports competitions in Liechtenstein

The Liechtensteiner National Badminton Championships is a tournament organized to crown the best badminton players in Liechtenstein. They are held since 1986.
