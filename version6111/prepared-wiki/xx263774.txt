[[Juho Mielonen]]

CATEGORIES: 1987 births, Living people, Ässät players, Detroit Red Wings draft picks

Juho Mielonen (born March 1, 1987) is a Finnish professional ice hockey defenceman who currently plays for HPK of the SM-liiga.Assat Pori's 2010-11 Roster Mielonen was drafted 175th overall by the Detroit Red Wings in the 2005 NHL Entry Draft.
