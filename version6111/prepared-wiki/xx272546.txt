[[Sağlıklı]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Mersin Province, Mediterranean Region, Turkey, Villages in Turkey, Tarsus District

 
Sağlılı is a village in Tarsus district of Mersin Province, Turkey. At [tpl]coord|37|02|N|34|55|E[/tpl] it is [tpl]convert|10|km[/tpl] to Tarsus and [tpl]convert|37|km[/tpl] to Mersin. Sağlıklı  is famed  for the [tpl]convert|3|km[/tpl] Roman road starting point of which is near the village. The population of village is 1455 Statistical Institute as of 2011.
