[[Coluria]]

CATEGORIES: Rosoideae

Coluria is a plant genus in the sub family Rosoideae native of Asia, Siberia and Altai.
This herbaceous perennial plant reaches 30 cm in height and 30 cm wide. The flowers are hermaphrodite and are pollinated by insects. It grows on the banks of mountains rivers.
