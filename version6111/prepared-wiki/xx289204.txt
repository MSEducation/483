[[Khalifeh Qeshlaq]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Khalifeh Qeshlaq ([tpl]lang-fa|خليفه قشلاق[/tpl], also Romanized as Khalīfeh Qeshlāq, and Khalīfeh-ye Qeshlāq; also known as Khalfak Qishlāq and Khalifeh Gheshlagh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3070443|Khalifeh Qeshlaq[/tpl] is a village in Shivanat Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 334, in 67 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
