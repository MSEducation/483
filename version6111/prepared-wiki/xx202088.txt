[[Vancouver Bay, British Columbia]]

CATEGORIES: Sunshine Coast (British Columbia), Company towns in Canada, Ghost towns in British Columbia, Unincorporated settlements in British Columbia

Vancouver Bay was a cannery town on the South Coast of British Columbia, Canada, located northwest of Stuart Island on the west side of Prince of Wales Reach of lower Jervis Inlet, at the bay of the same name, which is the mouth of the Vancouver River.
