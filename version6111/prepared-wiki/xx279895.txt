[[Derek Dainard]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1998 births, Micronesian people

Derek Dainard (born 1998) is a Micronesian swimmer.http://www.bcn2013.com/en/swimming/detail/member/derek-dainard He competed in the 50 m freestyle and 50 m butterfly events at the 2013 World Aquatics Championships.
