[[Shirakamidaketozanguchi Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Aomori Prefecture, Gonō Line

[tpl]nihongo|  Shikakamidaketozanguchi Station|白神岳登山口駅|Shikakamidake-Tozanguchi-eki[/tpl] is a railway station on the JR East Gonō Line located in the town of Fukaura, Aomori Prefecture, Japan. It is 42.3 rail kilometers from the southern terminus of the Gonō Line at Higashi-Noshiro Station.

==History==

Shikakamidaketozanguchi Station was opened on June 1, 1952 as [tpl]nihongo|Mutsu-Kurosaki Station|陸奥黒崎駅|Mutsu-Kurosaki-eki[/tpl] on the Japanese National Railways (JNR).  With the privatization of the JNR on April 1, 1987, it came under the operational control of JR East. The station was renamed to its present name of December 2, 2000.

==Lines==

==Station layout==

Shikakamidaketozanguchi Station has a single side platform serving bidirectional traffic use. The station is unattended, and is managed from Fukaura Station.
