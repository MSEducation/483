[[Egyptian constitutional referendum, 1980]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 in Egypt, Referendums in Egypt, 1980 referendums

A constitutional referendum was held in Egypt on 22 May 1980.Establishment Egyptian State Information Service The amendments would create the Shura Council, an upper house for the country's Parliament, and were approved by 99% of voters.Nohlen, D, Krennerich, M & Thibaut, B (1999) Elections in Africa: A data handbook, p341 ISBN 0-19-829645-2
