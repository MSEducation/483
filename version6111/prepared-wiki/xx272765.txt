[[Madonna of the Streets (film)]]

CATEGORIES: 1930 films, 1930s drama films, American films, American drama films, Black-and-white films, Sound film remakes of silent films, Films directed by John S. Robertson, Columbia Pictures films

Madonna of the Streets is a 1930 American drama film directed by John S. Robertson and starring Evelyn Brent.[tpl]cite web |url=http://movies.nytimes.com/movie/100895/Madonna-of-the-Streets/overview |title=Madonna of the Streets |accessdate=2011-09-25 |work=NY Times[/tpl]
This film is a sound remake of the 1924 silent film Madonna of the Streets starring Alla Nazimova.
