[[Marco Perella]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, American male film actors, American male television actors

Marco Perella is a Texan character actor and author, who has played a variety of bit part characters in films shot in Texas over the last twenty years, as well as a recurring role in Walker, Texas Ranger, playing Cobalt.

==Career==

Perella is best known for his bestselling book, 'Adventures of A No Name Actor', a humorous biography recounting his struggles in the acting world. He portrayed also Duane in the Horror film Spirit Camp.Welcome to SPIRIT CAMP; first pics and info
