[[British West Indies at the 1960 Summer Olympics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 1960 Summer Olympics, Barbados at the Olympics, Jamaica at the Olympics, Trinidad and Tobago at the Olympics, 1960 in Barbados, 1960 in Jamaica, 1960 in Trinidad and Tobago, Sport in the West Indies, 1960 in the Caribbean, West Indies Federation, 1960 in Caribbean sport

Athletes from the West Indies Federation competed under the name Antilles (ANT)http://www.la84foundation.org/6oic/OfficialReports/1960/OR1960v1.pdf page 230 at the 1960 Summer Olympics in Rome, Italy.  The short-lived nation only participated at these single Games, as Jamaica and Trinidad and Tobago competed independently again in 1964, and Barbados started competing at the 1968 Games.
