[[Yagisawa Dam]]

CATEGORIES: Dams in Gunma Prefecture, Dams completed in 1967

[tpl]Nihongo|Yagisawa Dam|矢木沢ダム[/tpl] is a dam in the Gunma Prefecture of Japan. It supports a 240 MW hydroelectric power station.[tpl]cite web|title=Hydropower & Dams in South and East Asia|url=http://www.hydropower-dams.com/d/d_S&East_Asia_Map.pdf|publisher=Hydropower and Dams|accessdate=3 October 2010|page=9[/tpl]
