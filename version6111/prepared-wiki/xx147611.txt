[[Geoffrey Lumsden]]

CATEGORIES: British male television actors, 1914 births, 1984 deaths

Geoffrey Lumsden (26 December 1914 - 4 March 1984) was a British character actor who had a lengthy career on television.
By some way his best known role was Captain Square in Dad's Army, the pompous commander of the Eastgate platoon of the Home Guard who was a rival of Captain Mainwaring.
