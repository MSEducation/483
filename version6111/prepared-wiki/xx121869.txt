[[West Scrafton]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in North Yorkshire, Civil parishes in North Yorkshire

West Scrafton is a village and civil parish within the Yorkshire Dales in the Richmondshire district of North Yorkshire, England. It is located [tpl]convert|4|mi|km[/tpl] south west of Leyburn. There is also an East Scrafton but it is only a hamlet.
West Scrafton is featured several times in the British television series All Creatures Great and Small, in the episodes "A New Chapter" and "Alarms & Excursions".
