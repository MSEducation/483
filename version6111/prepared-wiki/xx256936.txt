Kämpfer is a 12-episode Japanese anime series by Nomad, based on the series of light novels written by Toshihiko Tsukiji. The series follows a boy named Natsuru Senō, who is chosen to participate in the Kämpfer battles, giving him the ability to transform into a girl. The anime series aired on TBS between October 2 and December 17, 2009. The opening theme was [tpl]nihongo|"Unreal Paradise"|あんりある♥パラダイス|Anriaru Paradaisu[/tpl] by Minami Kuribayashi, while the ending theme is [tpl]nihongo|"One Way Ryō Omoi"|ワンウェイ両想い|Wan Wei Ryō Omoi[/tpl] by Marina Inoue and Megumi Nakajima. The series has been licensed for release in North America by Sentai Filmworks and will be released in a complete collection by Section23 Films on January 18, 2011.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/news/2010-10-21/section23-films-adds-koihime-muso-11eyes-kampfer|title=Section23 Films Adds Koihime†Musō, 11Eyes, Kampfer|publisher=Anime News Network|date=October 21, 2010|accessdate=October 21, 2010[/tpl] Two additional episodes, titled Kämpfer für die Liebe, were released on Blu-ray Disc and DVD on May 25, 2011, with one of the episodes also airing on April 8, 2011.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/news/2010-12-30/kampfer-fur-die-liebe-promo-video-streamed|title=Kämpfer für die Liebe Promo Video Streamed|publisher=Anime News Network|date=December 30, 2010|accessdate=December 30, 2010[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.starchild.co.jp/special/kenpu/new/|title=けんぷファー für die Liebe|trans_title=Kämpfer für die Liebe|publisher=StarChild|accessdate=December 30, 2010|language=Japanese[/tpl] The opening theme is "Choose my love!" by Minami Kuribayashi while the ending theme is [tpl]nihongo|"Battle Girl A"|妄想少女A|Musō Shōjo A[/tpl] by Yui Horie and Yukari Tamura.
