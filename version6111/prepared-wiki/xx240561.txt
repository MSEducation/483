[[Miyanoshita Station]]

CATEGORIES: Stations of Hakone Tozan Railway, Railway stations in Kanagawa Prefecture, Hakone, Kanagawa, 1919 establishments in Japan

[tpl]nihongo|Miyanoshita Station|宮ノ下駅|Miyanoshita-eki[/tpl] is a railway station on the Hakone Tozan Line located in Hakone, Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan. It is 12.1 rail kilometers from the line’s terminus at Odawara Station.

==History==

Miyanoshita Station was opened on June 1, 1919.
