[[Anders Danielsson]]

CATEGORIES: Swedish police officers, 1953 births, Living people

Sven Anders Herman Danielsson (born 27 December 1953) is the current Director-General of the Swedish Migration Board, succeeding Dan Eliasson in 2012. He was Director-General and Chief of the Swedish Security Service from 2007 to 2012.[tpl]cite press release |title=Ny Säpo-chef utsedd |url=http://www.regeringen.se/sb/d/9213/a/83562 |publisher=Government of Sweden |date=4 June 2007 |language=Swedish |accessdate=1 January 2011[/tpl] Anders Danielsson lives in Stockholm and Skåne. He is married and has two children.
