[[Sébastien Charlier]]

CATEGORIES: 1971 births, Living people, People from Beaumont-sur-Oise, Jazz harmonica players, Blues harmonica players, Rock harmonica players, French musicians, Jazz fusion musicians, Bebop musicians

Sébastien Charlier (Beaumont-sur-Oise, 1971) is a French diatonic harmonica player. He plays chromatically, in all keys, on a single blues harp. His music is inspired by many different influences, from Blues to Jazz, from Fusion to Electro-Pop.Sebastien CharlierDelphi'Muz - Sébastien Charlier pour la sortie de "Precious Time"  2 déc. 2009 – L'harmoniciste Sébastien Charlier nous fait l'amitié de nous faire découvrir en exclusivité son nouvel album Precious Time" He is also author of two textbooks: Je débute l'harmonicaJe débute l'harmonica (Editions Hit Diffusion) and Méthode en poche harmonica.Méthode en poche harmonica (Editions Hit Diffusion)
