[[Jacinto Villalba]]

CATEGORIES: Paraguayan footballers, Paraguay international footballers, Association football forwards, Cerro Porteño footballers, 1930 FIFA World Cup players

Jacinto Villalba was a Paraguayan football forward who played for Paraguay in the 1930 FIFA World Cup.1930 FIFA World Cup Uruguay He also played for Cerro Porteño. In Argentina, he was played for San Lorenzo de Almagro, in 1932 to 1935, and obtain the championship in 1933 with this club.
