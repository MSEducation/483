[[Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Onitsha]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic Church in Nigeria, Roman Catholic dioceses in Nigeria, Onitsha, Religious organizations established in 1889

The Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Onitsha ([tpl]lang-la|Onitshan(us)[/tpl]) is the Metropolitan See for the ecclesiastical province of Onitsha in Anambra State, Nigeria.

==History==

==Special churches==

The seat of the archbishop is the Holy Trinity Basilica (the only Basilica in Nigeria) in Onitsha.
