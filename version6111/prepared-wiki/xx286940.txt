[[Ich, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Tarom County

Ich ([tpl]lang-fa|ايچ[/tpl], also Romanized as Īch; also known as Aiye, Ayeh, Eiy, and Īj)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3067181|Ich[/tpl] is a village in Chavarzaq Rural District, Chavarzaq District, Tarom County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 609, in 145 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
