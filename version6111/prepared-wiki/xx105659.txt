[[International Day for Tolerance]]

CATEGORIES: November observances, Recurring events established in 1995, United Nations days

The International Day for Tolerance is an annual observance declared by UNESCO in 1995 to generate public awareness of the dangers of intolerance. It is observed on 16 November.  The entire text of the Declaration of Principles on Tolerance"Declaration of Principles on Tolerance", UNESCO, 1995. (PDF:) is included below.
