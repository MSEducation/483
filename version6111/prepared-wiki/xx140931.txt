[[Harry Perry III]]

CATEGORIES: 1977 births, Living people, American male television actors, Place of birth missing (living people)

Harry Perry III (born December 7, 1977) is an actor best known for his recurring role on the Nickelodeon television show Unfabulous as Manager Mike, who runs a smoothie bar named The Juice. In addition to his role, he serves as the Post-Production Supervisor on the show. He holds a similar position on the show Ned's Declassified School Survival Guide.
