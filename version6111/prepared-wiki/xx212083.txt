[[George G. Bruce]]

CATEGORIES: American non-fiction writers, Year of death missing, Year of birth missing

George G. Bruce was an American drum major during the Civil War, best known for co-writing The Drummer's and Fifer's Guide with Daniel Decatur Emmett.Snell and Kelley, pg. 30 The Guide was published in 1862, and remains well known as a source for music for the fife and drum. Bruce was a player of the drums, banjo, violin and mandolin.Music for Fife and Field Drums
His work influenced Sanford A. Moeller of the Moeller Method of drumming.
