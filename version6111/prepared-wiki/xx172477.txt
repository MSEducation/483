[[Hub Bechtol]]

CATEGORIES: 1926 births, 2004 deaths, All-American college football players, People from Amarillo, Texas, Players of American football from Texas, American football tight ends, Texas Tech Red Raiders football players, Texas Longhorns football players, Baltimore Colts (1947–50) players, College Football Hall of Fame inductees

Hubert "Hub" Edwin Bechtol (April 20, 1926 – October 22, 2004) was a college football player for the Texas Tech Red Raiders and the Texas Longhorns. He was elected to the College Football Hall of Fame in 1991.
