[[Profondeville]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Namur (province)

Profondeville ([tpl]IPA-fr|pʁɔ.fɔ̃d.vil|lang[/tpl]) is a Walloon municipality located in the Belgian province of Namur. On January 1, 2006, Profondeville had a total population of 11,367. The total area of the municipality is 50.34 km² and the population density is 226 inhabitants per km².
