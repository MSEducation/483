[[Salvadoran legislative election, 1956]]

CATEGORIES: 1956 elections in North America, Elections in El Salvador, 1956 in El Salvador

Legislative elections were held in El Salvador on 15 May 1956.Nohlen, D (2005) Elections in the Americas: A data handbook, Volume I, p276 ISBN 978-0-19-928357-6 The result was a victory for the Revolutionary Party of Democratic Unification, which won all 54 seats.
