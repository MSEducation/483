Tana Ramsay (born Cayetana Elizabeth Hutcheson in 1974)"Tana Ramsay". imdb.com, accessed November 15, 2011. is a British author of bestselling books on cookery and a TV broadcaster.

==Life==

Born in Croydon, Ramsay, a Montessori-trained former schoolteacher, married celebrity chef Gordon Ramsay in London on 21 December 1996.  They have four children: daughters Megan and Matilda, and fraternal male-female twins Jack and Holly.
