[[Veterinary Disciplinary Board (Sweden)]]

CATEGORIES: Government agencies of Sweden, Veterinary organizations

The Swedish Veterinary Disciplinary Board (Veterinära ansvarsnämnden) is a Swedish government agency that answers to the Ministry of Agriculture, Food and Consumer Affairs. The agency tries cases of professional misconduct among veterinarians. It is located in Jönköping.
