[[Mike Yaschuk]]

CATEGORIES: 1922 births, Ice hockey people from Saskatchewan, St. Boniface Athletics players, Streatham Royals players, Year of death missing

Mike Yaschuk (born November 5, 1922) was a Canadian ice hockey right winger who played junior with the St. Boniface Athletics who lost two successive provincial playoffs to the 1941-42 Portage Terriers, and the 1942-43 Winnipeg Rangers (who both went on to win the Memorial Cup).
Yaschuk was born in Ituna, Saskatchewan.
