[[Nikita Chaly]]

Nikita Ilyich Chaly ([tpl]lang-ru|Никита Ильич Чалый[/tpl]; born May 7, 1996) is a Russian football forward, who currently playing for FC Chernomorets Novorossiysk.
He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Chernomorets Novorossiysk on July 12, 2013 in a game against FC Krasnodar-2.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Russian Football Union|url=http://rfspro.ru/cgi-bin/player/script2.cgi?par1=119568|title=Career Summary|date=11 August 2013[/tpl]
