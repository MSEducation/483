[[Bagh, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Bagh ([tpl]lang-fa|باغ[/tpl], also Romanized as Bāgh; also known as Bag)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3054486|Bagh[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 321, in 69 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
