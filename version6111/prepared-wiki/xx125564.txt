[[A Sword from Red Ice]]

CATEGORIES: Fantasy novels, 2007 novels

A Sword from Red Ice is the third book in the Sword of Shadows fantasy series by J. V. Jones. The first two books in the series are A Cavern of Black Ice and A Fortress of Grey Ice.[tpl]cite journal|last=Murray|first=Frieda|title=A Sword from Red Ice (book review)|journal=Booklist|date=October 1, 2007|volume=104|issue=3|page=41[/tpl]  It was published in the United States and the United Kingdom on October 16, 2007.[tpl]cite web|title=A Sword from Red Ice|url=http://us.macmillan.com/aswordfromredice/JJones#buy-the-book|work=Macmillan Publishers|accessdate=11 December 2013[/tpl]

==Plot summary==

From OCLC Worldcat's summary, "As Ash March pursues her destiny with the legendary Sull people, Raif Sevrance seeks a place where he belongs, in a tale set in the wake of deadly clan battles and a darker force from an evil city that threatens their world."[tpl]cite web|title=A Sword from Red Ice|url=http://www.worldcat.org/title/sword-from-red-ice/oclc/124036349&referer=brief_results|work=OCLC Worldcat|accessdate=11 December 2013[/tpl]
