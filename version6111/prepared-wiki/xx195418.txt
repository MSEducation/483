[[Osamu Takizawa]]

CATEGORIES: 1906 births, 2000 deaths, Japanese actors

[tpl]Nihongo|Osamu Takizawa|滝沢 修|Takizawa Osamu|13 November 1906 – 22 June 2000[tpl]cite web|url=http://kotobank.jp/word/%E6%BB%9D%E6%B2%A2%E4%BF%AE|title=Takizawa Osamu|work=Nihon jinmei daijiten|publisher=Kōdansha|language=Japanese|accessdate=5 December 2010[/tpl][/tpl] was a Japanese actor. He was born in Ushigome, Tokyo, Japan. Starting at the Tsukiji Little Theater, Takizawa participated in a number of theatrical troupes before forming Gekidan Mingei with Jūkichi Uno. His was praised for his performance in Death of a Salesman and also directed a version of The Diary of Anne Frank. Perhaps his most notable film role was in Fires on the Plain.Osamu Takizawa (I)
