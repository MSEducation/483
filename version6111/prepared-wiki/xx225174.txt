[[Kazumi Kawai]]

CATEGORIES: 1964 births, 1997 deaths, Japanese actresses, Japanese female singers, People from Tokyo, Suicides by jumping in Japan, Singers from Tokyo

Kazumi Kawai (可愛 かずみ Kawai Kazumi) (July 9, 1964 – May 9, 1997 in Tokyo, Japan), was a Japanese actress. She committed suicide by jumping from an apartment buildingIMDb on May 9, 1997.
