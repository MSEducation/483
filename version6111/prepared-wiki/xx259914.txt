[[St. Stanislaus' Church (Pleasant Valley, New York)]]

CATEGORIES: Religious organizations established in 1903, Roman Catholic churches in New York, Churches in Dutchess County, New York

The Church of St. Stanislaus is a Roman Catholic parish church under the authority of the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of New York, located in Pleasant Valley, Dutchess County, New York City. It was established as a parish in 1903 and was formerly staffed by the Jesuits. The first rector, Rev. J. Havens Richards, S.J. was succeeded by Rev.
