Metro Aquiles Serdán ([tpl]Lang-es|Estación Aquiles Serdán[/tpl]) is a station along Line 7 of the Mexico City Metro.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.metro.df.gob.mx/red/estacion.html?id=90|title=Aquiles Serdán|language=Spanish|accessdate=2 August 2011[/tpl][tpl]Cite web|last=Archambault|first=Richard|url=http://mexicometro.org/metro/line7/aquiles-serdan/|title=Aquiles Serdán »  Mexico City Metro System|accessdate=2 August 2011[/tpl]  It is located in the Colonia La Preciosa neighborhood of the Azcapotzalco borough of northwestern Mexico City.
Its logo represents the bust of Aquiles Serdán, a martyr in the Mexican Revolution.
The station opened on 29 November 1988.[tpl]Cite web|last=Schwandl|first=Robert|url=http://www.urbanrail.net/am/mexi/mex-history.htm|title=Opening Dates for Mexico City's Subway|accessdate=1 August 2011[/tpl]
