[[Susannah Johnson]]

CATEGORIES: 1990 births, Living people, American trampolinists

Susannah Johnson (born September 30, 1990) is an American junior-level gymnast.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.usa-gymnastics.org/athletes/newbio/athletebios.php?id=319585 |title=USA Gymnastics National Team Athlete Biography | accessdate=2007-09-17 | publisher=USA Gymnastics | pages=1 [/tpl]
Johnson won the bronze medal in tumbling at the 2007 World Cup for tumbling and trampolining with a score of 64.80.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.usoc.org/11831_51954.htm | title=Ludvigson and Johnson win bronze medals at Trampoline and Tumbling World Cup | accessdate=2007-09-17 | last=Saladyga | first=Karen | date=2007-04-03 | publisher=U.S. Olympic Team | pages=1 |archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20070713154903/http://www.usoc.org/11831_51954.htm  |archivedate = 2007-07-13[/tpl]
At the 2007 U.S. Trampoline and Tumbling Championships, Johnson again placed third, with a score of 125.70.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.usoc.org/11831_52768.htm | title=Ludvigson and Estrada defend U.S. titles at 2007 U.S. Trampoline and Tumbling Championships | accessdate=2007-09-17 | last=Saladyga | first=Karen | date=2007-06-25 | publisher=U.S. Olympic Team | pages=1 |archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20070712151343/http://www.usoc.org/11831_52768.htm  |archivedate = 2007-07-12[/tpl]
Johnson lives in Roanoke, Virginia.
