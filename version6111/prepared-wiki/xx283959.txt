[[Bhuj railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Kutch district

Bhuj railway station is a railway station in Bhuj, Gujarat, India, on the Western line of the Western railway network.
It is the last station on the western railway line in the area.
It is not a busy station & handles only 7 trains.
