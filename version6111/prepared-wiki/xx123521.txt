[[Town Ground, Coalville]]

CATEGORIES: Cricket grounds in Leicestershire

The Town Ground is a cricket stadium based on Broom Leys Road, Coalville, Leicestershire, England.
There was only ever one first class county cricket game here, which was held in 1950. The game involved Leicestershire and Warwickshire. Warwickshire won the game by 6 wickets.
This is the home ground to Broomleys Cricket Club.
