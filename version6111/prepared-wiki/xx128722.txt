[[ARC in Karolinska Institutet]]

CATEGORIES: Karolinska Institutet, Stockholm University, Ageing, Gerontology, Educational organizations, Organizations based in Sweden, Organizations established in 2000, 2000 establishments in Sweden

The Aging Research Centre (ARC) in Karolinska Institutet was established in the year 2000, is a multidisciplinary center in which both the medical university Karolinska Institutet (KI) and Stockholm University (SU) are collaborating. The administration of the center is placed at the Division of Geriatric Epidemiology, Department of Neurotec, KI.
