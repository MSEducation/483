[[Kopsia dasyrachis]]

CATEGORIES: Kopsia, Plants described in 1934, Flora of Malaysia, Endemic flora of Borneo

Kopsia dasyrachis grows as a tree up to [tpl]Convert|10|m|-1[/tpl] tall, with a trunk diameter of up to [tpl]Convert|15|cm|0[/tpl]. The bark is grey or yellow. Its flowers feature a white corolla. The tree is found in lowland forests from sea-level to [tpl]convert|900|m|-2[/tpl] altitude. K. dasyrachis is endemic to Borneo and known only from Sabah.
