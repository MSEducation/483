[[732d Air Expeditionary Group]]

CATEGORIES: Military units and formations established in 1940, Air expeditionary groups of the United States Air Force

The 732d Air Expeditionary Group (732 AEG) provided oversight and advocacy for roughly 1,100 Air Force personnel spread across four squadrons who were tactically assigned to U.S. Army and Marine units throughout Iraq. These Airmen include security forces, civil engineers, lawyers, truck drivers, interrogators, military working dog teams, intelligence specialists, and airfield managers.  These Airmen were under tactical, or day to day, control of Army and Marine units, while administratively and operationally controlled by the 732d AEG. http://www.balad.afnews.af.mil/library/factsheets/factsheet.asp?id=4032
