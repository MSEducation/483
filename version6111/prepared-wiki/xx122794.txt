[[Guilty by Affiliation]]

CATEGORIES: WC (rapper) albums, 2007 albums, Albums produced by Denaun Porter, Albums produced by Jelly Roll, Albums produced by Nottz, Albums produced by Rick Rock, Albums produced by The Legendary Traxster, Albums produced by Emile Haynie, Gangsta rap albums

Guilty by Affiliation is the third studio album by American rapper WC. It was released on August 14, 2007 by Ice Cube's independent label, Lench Mob Records. Ice Cube, The Game, Snoop Dogg and Butch Cassidy make guest appearances.
