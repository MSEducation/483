[[Podgorje pri Pišecah]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Municipality of Brežice

Podgorje pri Pišecah is a settlement in the hills west of Bizeljsko in the Municipality of Brežice in eastern Slovenia. The area was traditionally part of Styria. It is now included with the rest of the municipality in the Lower Sava statistical region.Brežice municipal site

==Name==

The name of the settlement was changed from Podgorje to Podgorje pri Pišecah in 1953.Spremembe naselij 1948–95. 1996. Database. Ljubljana: Geografski inštitut ZRC SAZU, DZS.
