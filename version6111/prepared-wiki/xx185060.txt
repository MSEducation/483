[[Gmina Miedzna]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Węgrów County

Gmina Miedzna is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Węgrów County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Miedzna, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|9|km|mi|0[/tpl] north-east of Węgrów and [tpl]convert|80|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] east of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|115.78|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 4,102.

==Villages==

Gmina Miedzna contains the villages and settlements of Glina, Miedzna, Międzyleś, Orzeszówka, Poszewka, Rostki, Tchórzowa, Ugoszcz, Warchoły, Wola Orzeszowska, Wrotnów, Wrzoski, Żeleźniki and Zuzułka.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Miedzna is bordered by the town of Węgrów and by the gminas of Kosów Lacki, Liw, Sokołów Podlaski and Stoczek.
