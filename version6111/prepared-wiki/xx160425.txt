[[Seale and Sands]]

CATEGORIES: Borough of Guildford, Civil parishes in Surrey

Seale and Sands is a civil parish in the  Guildford District Council area of Surrey, England with a population of 887.Census data  Mr WA Nelson is the chair.Guildford Borough Council
The principal settlement is Seale; other places include Sandy Cross and The Sands.
