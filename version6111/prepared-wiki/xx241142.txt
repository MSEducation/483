[[Tumed Right Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Tumed Right Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠲᠦᠮᠡᠳ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠪᠠᠷᠠᠭᠤᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Tümed Baraɣun qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|c=土默特右旗 |p=Tǔmòtè Yòuqí[/tpl]) is a banner of western Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China. It is under the administration of Baotou City, [tpl]convert|59|km[/tpl] to the west, and is located along on the Jingzang Expressway, running from Beijing to Tibet.
