[[FC Slobozia Mare]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Moldova, Association football clubs established in 1958, 1958 establishments in Moldova

 FC Slobozia Mare  is a Moldovan football club based in Slobozia Mare, Moldova. 

They play in the Moldovan "A" Division, the second division in Moldovan football in season 2013-2014.
