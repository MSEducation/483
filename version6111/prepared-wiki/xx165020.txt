[[Bottom Liners]]

CATEGORIES: American comic strips, Articles to be expanded from January 2012

Bottom Liners is a comic strip devised by cartoonists Eric and Bill Teitelbaum. The themes of the strip are the worlds of business and finance.

==External links==

The Teitelbaum brothers also are co-creators of the internationally syndicated "Pink Panther" comic strip. Their drawings have appeared in most leading publications including The New Yorker, Forbes, Saturday Review and Family Circle.
