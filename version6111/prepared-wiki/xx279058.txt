[[Ebrahimabad, Zanjanrud]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Ebrahimabad ([tpl]lang-fa|ابراهيم اباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Ebrāhīmābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795476|Ebrahimabad[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 138, in 30 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
