[[Mohammad Shahlu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Mohammad Shahlu ([tpl]lang-fa|محمدشاه لو[/tpl], also Romanized as Moḩammad Shāhlū; also known as Moḩammadlū, Moḩammad Şāleḩ, Moḩammad Shālū, and Muhammad Sālih)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3075541|Mohammad Shahlu[/tpl] is a village in Shivanat Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 101, in 31 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
