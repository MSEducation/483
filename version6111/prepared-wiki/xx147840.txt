[[Yaoi Hentai]]

CATEGORIES: Yaoi anime and manga

Yaoi Hentai is a yaoi original English-language manga anthology series by various creators. This manga is considered extreme for yaoi and is reserved for mature and explicit content. 
In 2007, Wal-Mart removed Yaoi Hentai from its online shop after receiving a complaint.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.icv2.com/articles/news/9968.html|title=ICv2 - Wal-Mart Yanks Yaoi Titles[/tpl]
In 2008, it was replaced with the manga anthology Happy Yaoi Yum Yum.[tpl]cite web|url=http://comipress.com/news/2008/02/11/3256|title=Yaoi Hentai to be Replaced by Happy Yaoi Yum Yum [tpl]pipe[/tpl] ComiPress[/tpl]
