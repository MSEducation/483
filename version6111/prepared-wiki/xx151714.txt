[[Artiluc]]

CATEGORIES: Republic of Ragusa, Coins, Medieval currencies

Artiluc ([tpl]lang-hr|artiluk[/tpl], [tpl]lang-it|artilucchi[/tpl]) is the name of a type of silver coin forged and used in the 17th century in the Republic of Ragusa that had its capital city in Dubrovnik, modern-day Croatia.
