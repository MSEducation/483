====The fiber oil sorbent boom====

The most known technology of the company is the fiber oil sorbent boom. The essence of the technology is that the special fibers saturated with oil eating microbes are fastened on the floating booms. The fibers hook up the oil patches on themselves and microbes biodegrade it during several days, oil is decomposed into carbonic acid and water. The fiber attachments do not hinder the water flow. Also it has the largest absorbing surface area: 5-10 thous. m2 per 1 cubic metre of the construction. The tests on the river [tpl]link-interwiki|en= Var-Egan|lang= ru|lang_title= Большой Варь-Еган[/tpl] show the reduction of the petroleum product concentration of water column in 4 times, the surface pellicular oil reduced in 43 times.
The project became the finalist of the «[tpl]link-interwiki|en= Russian innovations competition|lang= ru|lang_title= Конкурс русских инноваций[/tpl]–2009»[tpl]cite news |title= Проекты бизнес-инкубатора: нефтесорбирующий бон|author= |first= |last= |authorlink= |authorlink2= |author2= |author3= |author4= |author5= |author6= |author7= |url= http://www.vsluh.ru/news/oilgas/168891.html|agency= |newspaper= Вслух.ру|publisher= |location= Tyumen|isbn= |issn= |oclc= |pmid= |pmc= |bibcode= |doi= |id= |date= September 24, 2009|page= |pages= |at= |accessdate= 2013-08-09|language= ru|trans_title= The business incubator projects: oil sorbent boom|quote= |archiveurl= |archivedate= |deadurl= |ref=[/tpl] and the «[tpl]link-interwiki|en= Zworykin Award|lang= ru|lang_title= Зворыкинский инновационный проект[/tpl]-2011». ====The pyrolysis plant by TransGlobal Remediation Group====

In the result of the partnership with TransGlobal Remediation Group in the time of the aftermath’s destruction of the Gulf of Mexico accident, RSI got the exclusive right to distribute the American pyrolysis plants into Russia. The plants allow the processing of any organic compound with at a rate of from 115 kg to 8 tons per hour with the performance 99%. On the downstream the synthetic fuel, biogas, biofuel and electric energy are generated.
