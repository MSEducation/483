[[Seif Daoud]]

CATEGORIES: 1977 births, Living people, Masry players, Egyptian footballers, Egypt international footballers

 Seif Daoud  [tpl]lang-ar|سيف داود[/tpl] (born 28 August 1977, Port Said, Egypt), is a former Egyptian footballer.

==Career==

Daoud played for Al-Masry Club in the Egyptian Premier League between 1995 and 2003. He was recently appointed as the assistant coach of the team.

==Clubs==

==National teams==

He played once for the Egyptian national team.
