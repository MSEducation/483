[[Gavin Purcell]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1974 births, People from Seattle, Washington

Gavin Purcell (born in Seattle, Washington on May 26, 1974) is currently the co-producer of Late Night with Jimmy Fallon,Gavin Purcell joins Jimmy Fallon and former Executive Producer of G4-TV's Attack of the Show!. Prior to working on that show, he was an executive producer for other shows on G4, a network that primarily focuses on video game-based and technology shows.[tpl]citation needed|date=May 2010[/tpl]
