[[1981 Peach Bowl (December)]]

CATEGORIES: 1981–82 NCAA football bowl games, Chick-fil-A Bowl, Florida Gators football bowl games, West Virginia Mountaineers football bowl games

The 1981 Peach Bowl was a post-season college football bowl game between the West Virginia Mountaineers and the Florida Gators.  The game took place on December 31, 1981, resulting in a West Virginia win over Florida 26-6.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cstv.com/sports/m-footbl/stories/120106aco.html|title=Chick-Fil-A Bowl Results Formerly Peach Bowl|year=2007|publisher=CSTV Networks, Inc.|accessdate=2008-10-05[/tpl]  The offensive MVP was Mickey Walczack of West Virginia and the defensive MVP was West Virginia's Don Stemple.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.rauzulusstreet.com/football/college/peach.htm|title=NCAA Football Bowl Games -Peach Bowl|date=1996–2007|publisher=Rauzulu's Street|accessdate=2008-10-05| archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20080907182104/http://www.rauzulusstreet.com/football/college/peach.htm| archivedate= 7 September 2008 | deadurl= no[/tpl]
