[[Data East Arcade Classics]]

CATEGORIES: 2010 video games, Data East video games, Majesco Entertainment games, Wii-only games, Wii games, Video game compilations

Data East Arcade Classics is a compilation of video games created by the now-defunct Japanese video game company Data East. The collection disc is developed by G1M2 and published and released by Majesco Entertainment for the Wii on February 19, 2010. The compilation was first announced by GameSpot on September 10, 2009 after the ESRB rated the game.
