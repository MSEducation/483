[[Natalie Hunter (canoeist)]]

CATEGORIES: 1967 births, Australian female canoeists, Canoeists at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Living people, Olympic canoeists of Australia

Natalie Hunter (born 13 February 1967) is an Australian sprint canoer who competed in the mid-1990s. She finished eighth in the K-4 500 m event at the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta.
