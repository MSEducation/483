[[Roberto "Cyborg" Abreu]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Brazilian practitioners of Brazilian jiu-jitsu, 1980 births

Roberto Abreu (also known as Cyborg) is a super heavy weight Brazilian jiu-jitsu (BJJ) fighter currently living in the United States.[tpl]cite web|url=http://adccbarcelona.com/2009/08/06/roberto-cyborg-abreu/ |title=Roberto “Cyborg” Abreu « ADCC WORLD CHAMPIONSHIP BARCELONA 2009 |publisher=Adccbarcelona.com |date=2009-08-06 |accessdate=2011-11-17[/tpl]  He grew up in rural Brazil and is affiliated with Nova Geracao.[tpl]cite web|url=http://thejiujitsulab.wordpress.com/2011/07/16/roberto-cyborg-abreu/ |title=Roberto "Cyborg" Abreu | The Jiu Jitsu Laboratory |publisher=Thejiujitsulab.wordpress.com |date= |accessdate=2011-11-17[/tpl] Abreu is active and highly competitive in BJJ, with multiple titles including gold medals at the 2010, 2011 and 2012 IBJJF No-Gi World Championships. He also won the coveted ADCC Absolute title in 2013. http://www.graciemag.com/2013/10/adcc-2013-cyborg-champion-of-absolute-gabi-wins-again-and-more/
