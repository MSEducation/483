[[Willie Mands]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Scottish darts players, 1966 births, People from Thurso, British Darts Organisation players

Willie Mands is a Scottish former darts player.
Mands played in the 1986 World Professional Darts Championship losing 3-1 in sets to Fred McMullan.  Mands was previously a winner of the Scottish Masters in 1985.
