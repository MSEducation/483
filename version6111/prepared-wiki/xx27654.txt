[[Abel-mizraim]]

CATEGORIES: Torah places

Abel-mizraim (the "meadow of Egypt", or "mourning of Egypt")Meadow of Egypt, would come from Abel (Meadow) and Mizraim (Egypt).  "Mourning of Egypt" would come from Ebel (Mourning). is a place "beyond," or west, of the Jordan river, at the "threshing-floor of Atad." Here the Egyptians mourned seven days for Jacob (Genesis [tpl]bibleref2-nb|Genesis|50:4-11[/tpl]). Its site is unknown.
