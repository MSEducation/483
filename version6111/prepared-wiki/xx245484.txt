[[La Viuda Negra (1977 film)]]

CATEGORIES: 1977 films, Mexican films, Spanish-language films, Films based on novels, Films directed by Arturo Ripstein

La Viuda Negra (The Black Widow) is a 1977 film directed by Arturo Ripstein, produced in Mexico and based on the novel Debiera haber Obispas written by  Rafael Solana.

==Cast==

==Plot==

A young woman called Matea (Isela Vega) come to work in a church of the Father Feliciano (Mario Almada). The relationship between a very attractive woman with a sorcerer caused the rage of the people.
