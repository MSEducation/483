[[John Amen]]

CATEGORIES: United States Army officers, Nuremberg trials, American military personnel of World War II

Colonel John Harlan Amen is a US Army Intelligence officer who served as Nuremberg Prison Chief Interrogator during the Nuremberg War Trials.
He was the second husband of Marion Cleveland Dell, the daughter of former President Grover Cleveland.
