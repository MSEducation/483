[[Reynolds' pentad]]

CATEGORIES: Diagnostic gastroenterology

Reynolds pentad is a collection of signs and symptoms suggesting the diagnosis obstructive ascending cholangitis, a serious infection of the biliary system. ==Eponym==

It was named after the surgeon, B.M. Reynolds, who described it in 1959 along with Dr. Everett L. Dargan.
