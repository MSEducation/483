[[6 Minutes of Pleasure]]

CATEGORIES: 1991 singles, LL Cool J songs, Songs written by LL Cool J, 1990 songs, Def Jam Recordings singles

"6 Minutes of Pleasure" is the fifth and final single from LL Cool J's fourth album, Mama Said Knock You Out. It was released in 1991 for Def Jam Recordings and featured production from Marley Marl. "6 Minutes of Pleasure" was a mild success for LL Cool J, it made it to 95 on the Billboard Hot 100 and 26 on the Hot R&B/Hip-Hop songs.[tpl]Cite web|url=[tpl]Allmusic|class=artist|id=p95754/charts-awards/billboard-singles|pure_url=yes[/tpl]|title=Billboard chart positions|publisher=Billboard / All Media Guide|accessdate=2010-02-20[/tpl] On the B-side was "Eat Em Up L Chill".
