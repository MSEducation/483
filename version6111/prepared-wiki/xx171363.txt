[[Robert Loren Fleming]]

CATEGORIES: American comics writers, Living people, 1956 births

Robert Loren Fleming (born 1956) is an American comic book writer.

==Biography==

Fleming worked for DC Comics initially as a proofreader and later writer, creating Thriller, and was writing an Aquaman limited series and one-shot special in 1989 with plotter/breakdown artist Keith Giffen and artist Curt Swan.
He is also known for co-writing (with Giffen) DC's popular comedic character, Ambush Bug in a handful of mini-series in the 1980s. The duo reunited for 2008's Ambush Bug: Year None.
