[[1994 Kansas State Wildcats football team]]

CATEGORIES: 1994 Big Eight Conference football season, Kansas State Wildcats football seasons

The 1994 Kansas State Wildcats football team represented Kansas State University in the college football season of 1994.  The team's head football coach was Bill Snyder.  The Wildcats played their home games in KSU Stadium.  1994 saw the wildcats finish with a record of 9–3, and a 5–2 record in Big 8 play.  The season ended with a loss against [tpl]cfb link|year=1994|team=Boston College Eagles|title=Boston College[/tpl] in the 1994 Aloha Bowl.
