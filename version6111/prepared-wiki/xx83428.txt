[[Dona Flor and Her Two Husbands (novel)]]

CATEGORIES: 1966 novels, Modernist novels, Novels by Jorge Amado, Portuguese-language novels, Novels adapted into films, Novels set in Salvador, Bahia

Dona Flor and Her Two Husbands (Portuguese: Dona Flor e Seus Dois Maridos) is a Brazilian novel, written by Jorge Amado in 1966 and published in English in 1969.
Amado's  tale is of a woman's unlikely path to happiness. It is a vast panorama of life in the town of Salvador, Bahia, with dozens of characters.
The novel has been adapted into a 1976 film.
