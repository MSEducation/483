[[Asociación de Periodistas de la Televisión y Radiofonía Argentina]]

CATEGORIES: Journalism in Argentina, Organisations based in Argentina, 1959 establishments in Argentina

The Asociación de Periodistas de la Televisión y Radiofonía Argentina (APTRA) ([tpl]lang-es|Association of journalists of Argentine television and radio[/tpl]) is an Argentine association of journalists, established in 1959. They give the Martín Fierro Awards each year, except in the 1976-1988 period.Un premio con una extensa historia [tpl]es icon[/tpl]
