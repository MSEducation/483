[[Martin Strege]]

Martin Strege (born 21 February 1966 in Kassel) is a retired German runner who specialized in the 3000 metres steeplechase.
He finished fourteenth at the 1993 World Championships, sixth at the 1994 European Championships1994 European Championships, men's results (Sporting Heroes) and eighth at the 1995 World Championships.
His personal best time is 8:18.57 minutes, achieved at the 1995 World Championships in Gothenburg.
