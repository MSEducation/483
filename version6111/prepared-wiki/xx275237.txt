[[All-Ireland Sevens Camogie]]

CATEGORIES: Camogie competitions, Inter-county camogie competitions

All-Ireland Sevens Camogie Competitions are seven-a-side national inter-club or inter-college Camogie tournaments held at senior, junior, legends/master, colleges and inter-varsity level. 
The main competition is held at the Kilmacud Crokes GAA club in South County Dublin, played off in one day annually the day before the All-Ireland Camogie Final in close proximity to Croke Park as part of the All-Ireland festivities. It is known as the Daintree sevens for sponsorship purposes. The current (2011) holders are Toomevara.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.camogie.ie/Home/tabid/54/mid/411/newsid412/1803/Daintree-Paper-All-Ireland-Camogie-7s/Default.aspx |title=The Camogie Association |publisher=Camogie.ie |date= |accessdate=2011-10-17[/tpl]
The secondary school colleges competitions are held in November and the inter-varsity competition, the Fr Meachair Cup, in March.
