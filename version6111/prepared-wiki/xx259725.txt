Jean-Claude Sawyer (born August 12, 1986) is a Canadian professional ice hockey player who is currently playing for the Cincinnati Cyclones in the ECHL.  He was selected by the Minnesota Wild in the 5th round (161st overall) of the 2004 NHL Entry Draft.
On July 2, 2007, he was signed as a free agent by the Chicago Blackhawks.http://forecaster.thehockeynews.com/hockeynews/hockey/player.cgi?4782&showAllMoves=1
