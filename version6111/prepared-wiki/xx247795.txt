[[Drongan United]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Scotland, Sport in East Ayrshire

Drongan United are an amateur football team from Drongan, Ayrshire.
They play their home games at the local football park by the Mill Of Shield Road situated in the very near centre of Drongan. They currently play in the Ayrshire Amateur Football League's Third Division.
Drongan's biggest achievement is having lifted the Scottish Amateur Cup in 1985. Drongan's main rivals are Mauchline United.
