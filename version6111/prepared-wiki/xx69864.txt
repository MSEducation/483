[[De Luxe Records]]

CATEGORIES: Defunct record labels of the United States

De Luxe Records (later DeLuxe Records) was a record label formed in 1944 by brothers David and Jules Braun in Linden, New Jersey, United States.  The label flourished in the 1940s and 1950s. In 1947, Syd Nathan, the founder and owner of King Records, purchased a majority interest in DeLuxe.  In 1949, Nathan moved the label to Cincinnati, Ohio and in 1951 he bought out the Braun brothers completely.
