[[Texas State Highway Spur 501]]

CATEGORIES: State highway spurs in Texas, Greater Houston

Texas State Highway Spur 501 (Spur 501), also known as the La Porte Spur or South Broadway Street is a short Texas State Spur in Harris County, Texas. It runs north-south through La Porte and Shoreacres.

==History==

Spur 501 was established in 1971 from its current southern terminus north to Ave. J in La Porte.  In 1977, the northern 1.1 miles of the route was transferred to Loop 410 (present-day Bus. SH 146).
