[[Cannonball Takes Charge]]

CATEGORIES: 1959 albums, Riverside Records albums, Cannonball Adderley albums, Albums produced by Orrin Keepnews

Cannonball Takes Charge is an album by jazz saxophonist Cannonball Adderley released on the Riverside label featuring performances by Adderley with Wynton Kelly, Paul Chambers, and Jimmy Cobb with Percy and Albert Heath replacing Chambers and Cobb on two selections.Cannonball Adderley discography accessed 19 October 2009 The Allmusic review by Ken Dryden awarded the album 4 stars and states "Cannonball Adderley is in top form on this 1959 release".Dryden, K. Allmusic Review accessed 19 October 2009
