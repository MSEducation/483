[[Aq Qui, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Aq Qui ([tpl]lang-fa|آق قوئي[/tpl], also Romanized as Āq Qū’ī; also known as Aghoo’i, Aq Qūeh, and Āqū’ī)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3053469|Aq Qui[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 45, in 7 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
