[[Gongona]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Solomon Islands, Rennell and Bellona Islands

Gongona  is a small village in the Solomon Islands, on Rennell Island in the Rennell and Bellona province, with a population of only about 150 people[tpl]Citation needed|date=April 2011[/tpl]. It is located at east of Tigoa, on the southern side of Copperhead road, and features a helicopter lading site. It is best known for its Sporting events, which occur every Thursday, with over 300 people attending.[tpl]Citation needed|date=April 2011[/tpl].
