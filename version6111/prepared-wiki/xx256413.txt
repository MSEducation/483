[[Bezovje pri Šentjurju]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Municipality of Šentjur

Bezovje pri Šentjurju is a small settlement in the Municipality of Šentjur in Eastern Slovenia. It lies just outside Šentjur north of the regional road leading east to Šmarje pri Jelšah. The area is part of the historical region of Styria. The municipality is now included in the Savinja statistical region.Šentjur municipal site
