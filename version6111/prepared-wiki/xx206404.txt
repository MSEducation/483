[[Ronna Reeves]]

CATEGORIES: 1966 births, American country singers, Living people, Mercury Records artists, Musicians from Texas, People from Big Spring, Texas

Ronna Renee Reeves (born September 21, 1966 in Big Spring, Texas) is a female country music singer. Between 1990 and 1998, she released five studio albums, including three on Mercury Records; she has also charted five singles on the Hot Country Songs charts. Her highest-charting single was "The More I Learn (The Less I Understand About Love)", which reached number 49 in 1992.[tpl]cite book|last=Whitburn|first=Joel|title=Hot Country Songs 1944 to 2008|publisher=Record Research, Inc|year=2008|page=346|isbn=0-89820-177-2[/tpl]
After leaving Mercury in 1995, Reeves signed to River North Records and released two more albums. She also recorded a duet with Peter Cetera, a cover of ABBA's "SOS", on his album One Clear Voice.[tpl]cite web|url=http://allmusic.com/artist/ronna-reeves-p1785/biography|title=Ronna Reeves biography|last=Loftus|first=Johnny|work=Allmusic|accessdate=23 May 2011[/tpl]
She is perhaps best remembered as a regular on The Statler Brothers Show during the mid-1990s, as well as opening act on many of their tours.
