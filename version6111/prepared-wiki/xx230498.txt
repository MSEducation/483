[[Yousif Aljamal]]

CATEGORIES: 1983 births, Living people, Filipino basketball players, People from Pampanga, Power forwards (basketball), San Beda College alumni, Small forwards, Talk 'N Text Tropang Texters players

Yousif B. Aljamal Jr. (born in Pampanga on June 14, 1983) is a Filipino professional basketball player in the Philippine Basketball Association. He currently plays for the GlobalPort Batang Pier. He is also a former NCAA Most Valuable Player for its 82nd season with the San Beda Red Lions.
