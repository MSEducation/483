[[Illya Aleksiyevich]]

Illya Leanidavich Aleksiyevich ([tpl]lang-be|Ілля Леанідавіч Алексіевіч[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-ru|Илья Алексиевич[/tpl] (Ilya Aleksiyevich); born 10 February 1991) is a Belarusian professional football player currently playing for BATE Borisov.
Aleksiyevich made his debut for the senior national team of his country on 14 November 2012, in the 2:1 away win over Israel in a friendly match.
