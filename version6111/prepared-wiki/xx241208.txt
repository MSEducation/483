[[Arun Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Arun Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠠᠷᠤᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Arun qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=阿荣旗 |t=阿榮旗 |p=Āróng Qí[/tpl]) is a banner of northeastern Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China, bordering Heilongjiang province to the south and east. It is under the administration of Hulunbuir City, and is [tpl]convert|92|km[/tpl] north-northwest of the city of Qiqihar in Heilongjiang province.
