[[Lyudmila Karachkina]]

CATEGORIES: Russian astronomers, Soviet astronomers, Ukrainian astronomers, 20th-century astronomers, Women astronomers, Discoverers of asteroids, 1948 births, Living people

Lyudmila Georgievna Karachkina ([tpl]lang-uk|Людмила Георгіївна Карачкіна[/tpl], [tpl]lang-ru|Людмила Георгиевна Карачкина[/tpl]) (born 3 September 1948, Rostov-on-Don) is a former Soviet/Ukrainian astronomer.
Working at the Crimean Astrophysical Observatory since 1978, she discovered a number of asteroids, including the Amor asteroid 5324 Lyapunov and the Trojan asteroid 3063 Makhaon. She has received a Ph.D. in astronomy from I. I. Mechnikov Odessa National University in 2004.
She has two daughters, Maria and Renata.
