[[WFBU-LP]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the United States, Low-power FM radio stations in Florida, Radio stations in Florida, Moody Radio affiliate stations

WFBU-LP is a Christian radio station licensed to Graceville, Florida, broadcasting on 94.7 MHz FM.  The station is owned and operated by the Baptist College of Florida.WFBU-LP fcc.gov. Accessed November 23, 2012
