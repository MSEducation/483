[[WALW-LP]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Alabama, Low-power FM radio stations in Alabama, Adult album alternative radio stations in the United States, Country radio stations in the United States, Lawrence County, Alabama, Radio stations established in 2002, 2002 establishments in Alabama

WALW-LP (98.3 FM, "Southern Radio 98.3") is a radio station licensed to serve Moulton, Alabama.  The station is owned by Community Information And Education Radio Inc. It airs an Americana music format.[tpl]cite web |work=Alabama Broadcast Media Page |url=http://bellsouthpwp2.net/c/r/crackedlcd/almediapage/lpfm.html |title=Alabama LPFM & Translators |accessdate=2008-05-21[/tpl][tpl]cite web |title=Station Information Profile |work=Arbitron |url=http://www.arbitron.com/[/tpl]
The station was assigned the WALW-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on December 12, 2002.[tpl]cite web |title=Call Sign History |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=133800&Callsign=WALW-LP |work=FCC Media Bureau CDBS Public Access Database[/tpl] WALW-LP is the oldest LPFM station in Alabama.[tpl]cite web |url=http://bellsouthpwp2.net/c/r/crackedlcd/almediapage/profiles/wqjj.html |title=FM History Profile: WQJJ |work=Alabama Broadcast Media Page |accessdate=2008-05-21[/tpl]
