[[F. Jacob Schmidt House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses completed in 1890, Queen Anne architecture in Iowa, Houses in Davenport, Iowa, Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Iowa, National Register of Historic Places in Scott County, Iowa

The F. Jacob Schmidt House is located in the West End of Davenport, Iowa, United States.  The Queen Anne style house has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since 1983.
