[[Barik Ab, Khodabandeh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Barik Ab ([tpl]lang-fa|باريك اب[/tpl], also Romanized as Bārīk Āb; also known as Barkab)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3055562|Barik Ab[/tpl] is a village in Aq Bolagh Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 190, in 40 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
