[[Guiyuan Temple]]

CATEGORIES: Buddhist temples in Hubei, Buildings and structures in Wuhan, Religious buildings completed in 1658

Guiyuan Temple ([tpl]zh|s=归元寺|t=歸元寺|p=Guīyuán Sì[/tpl]) is a Buddhist temple located on Cuiwei Rd., Wuhan City, Hubei Province of China. This part of Wuhan is the former Hanyang city.
It was built in Shunzhi 15th year (1658), Qing Dynasty. It has a land area of [tpl]convert|4.67|acre[/tpl]. The New Pavilion built in 1922 is the treasury of the temple.
