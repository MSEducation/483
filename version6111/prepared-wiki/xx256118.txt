[[Richard Lowe Fashion Group]]

CATEGORIES: Public relations companies of the United States, Companies based in New York City, Advertising agencies of the United States

Richard Lowe Fashion Group (RLFG) is headquartered in New York City. RLFG is a  full-service, global fashion design and public relations firm. RLFG is best known for designing and marketing mass and luxury fashion lines for internationally recognized brands and department stores.

==About==

RLFG is responsible for design, technical design, product placement and PR of high profile brands of accessories,  clothing and celebrities in such brands as Nicole Miller, Vera Wang, Oscar de la Renta, Juicy Couture and Rebecca Minkoff.
