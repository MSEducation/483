[[Htangsan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Naga Self-Administered Zone, Lahe Township

 
Htangsan, also Htangsan Naga,[tpl]cite book|last=Klose|first=Albrecht|title=Sprachen der Welt|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=LXxiAAAAMAAJ|accessdate=2 January 2011|year=2001|publisher=De Gruyter|isbn=978-3-598-11404-5|page=239[/tpl] is a village in Lahe Township, Naga Self-Administered Zone, in the Sagaing Region of northwestern Burma. It is located in the Naga Hills, to the northwest of Hwekum and to the southwest of Lahe.[tpl]cite map|title=Bing Maps|publisher= Microsoft and Harris Corporation Earthstar Geographics LLC|accessdate=2 January 2010[/tpl] At the centre of the village is a circle with a grey roofed building.[tpl]cite map|title=Google Maps|publisher=Google|accessdate=2 January 2010[/tpl] Human sacrifice and headhunting has been documented in the Lahe Township.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1G1-68273454.html|title=Human Sacrifice and Slavery in the "Unadministered" Areas of Upper Burma During the Colonial Era.|author=Menas, Gordon P.|publisher=Journal of Social Issues in Southeast Asia |date=October 1, 2000|accessdate=2 January 2010[/tpl]
