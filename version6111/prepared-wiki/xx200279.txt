[[Eric Fitzgibbon]]

CATEGORIES: Australian Labor Party politicians, Members of the Australian House of Representatives for Hunter, Members of the Australian House of Representatives, 1936 births, Living people

Eric John Fitzgibbon (born 27 August 1936) was an Australian politician. Born in Taree, New South Wales, he attended the University of New England and became a teacher. He served on Cessnock City Council, and was mayor from 1981-83. In 1984, he entered the Australian House of Representatives as the Labor member for Hunter. He held the seat until 1996, when he retired.
