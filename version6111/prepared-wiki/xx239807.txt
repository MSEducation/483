[[Alchichica Silverside]]

CATEGORIES: Poblana, Endemic fauna of Mexico, Central Mexico, Animals described in 1945, Critically endangered animals

The Alchichica Silverside (Poblana alchichica) is a species of neotropical silverside endemic to Lake Alchichica in Mexico.
Poblana achichica measures up to 10 in. (25 cm). According to a 2001 book, information on its breeding habits are not widely known and its primary food source is small aquatic invertebrates.
