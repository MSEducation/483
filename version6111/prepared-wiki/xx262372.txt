[[Derek Dinger]]

CATEGORIES: 1987 births, Living people, DEG Metro Stars players, Eisbären Berlin players, ERC Ingolstadt players, Kassel Huskies players

Derek Dinger (born March 28, 1987) is a German professional ice hockey defenceman who currently plays for ERC Ingolstadt of the Deutsche Eishockey Liga (DEL). Dinger originally joined the DEG Metro Stars for the 2010–11 season following the folding of hometown team the Kassel Huskies on September 2, 2010.[tpl]cite web| url = http://www.deg-metro-stars.de/servlet/PB/menu/1015785/index.html | title = Dinger comes to Dusseldorf | publisher = DEG Metro Stars | date = 2010-09-02 | accessdate = 2010-09-02 | language = German [/tpl] After two seasons with the Stars, Dinger left to join ERC Ingolstadt on a one-year contract on May 11, 2012.[tpl]citeweb| url = http://www.erc-ingolstadt.de/html/index.php?nav=news&id=1822 | title = Defender Derek Dinger moves to Ingolstadt | publisher = ERC Ingolstadt | date = 2012-05-11 | accessdate = 2012-05-11 | language = German[/tpl]
