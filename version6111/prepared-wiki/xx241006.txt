[[Chengqu, Jincheng]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Shanxi

Chengqu ([tpl]zh|s=城区 |t=城區 |p=Chéngqū |l=urban district[/tpl]), or Cheng District is a district of Jincheng, Shanxi, China. As of 2010, it had a population of 470,000, an increase of 56.78% from 2000, living in an area of [tpl]convert|141|km2|abbr=on[/tpl].
