[[Patrick Monckeberg]]

CATEGORIES: Male actors from London, English male television actors, German expatriates in England, Guatemalan actors, Living people

Patrick Mönckeberg (Patrick Mönckeberg) is a London-based German actor, born in Guatemala City. He trained at the Academy of Live and Recorded Arts  (ALRA) in London.
He has appeared in many roles in the theatre, on television and in films in England and Germany including Love Soup (BBC One)  and Bonekickers (BBC One) and Captain America: The First Avenger.
