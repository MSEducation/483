[[Companies Court]]

CATEGORIES: High Court of Justice, English civil law, Welsh law, Economy of England, Economy of Wales

The Companies Court is a specialist court within the Chancery Division of the High Court of Justice of England and Wales, which deals with certain matters relating to companies. These include matters which are regulated by the Companies Acts, the Financial Services and Markets Act 2000, the Insolvency Acts in relation to companies registered in England and Wales, or the Company Directors Disqualification Act 1986, which must be commenced in the Companies Court.CHAPTER 20 THE COMPANIES COURT
