[[Aslan Kalmanov]]

CATEGORIES: 1994 births, Living people, Russian footballers

Aslan Khazbiyevich Kalmanov ([tpl]lang-ru|Аслан Хазбиевич Калманов[/tpl]; born January 5, 1994) is a Russian football forward, who currently playing for FC Alania-d Vladikavkaz.
He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Alania-d Vladikavkaz on July 22, 2013 in a game against FC MITOS Novocherkassk.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Russian Football Union|url=http://rfspro.ru/cgi-bin/player/script2.cgi?par1=119862|title=Career Summary|date=10 August 2013[/tpl]
