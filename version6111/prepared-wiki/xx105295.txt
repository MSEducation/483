[[National Biodiversity Conservation Areas]]

CATEGORIES: National Biodiversity Conservation Areas, Lists of protected areas by country, Laos-related lists, Geography of Laos

A National Biodiversity Conservation Area (NBCA) is an environmentally protected area in Laos. There are all together 21 different NBCAs in Laos, protecting 29,775 square kilometers.National parks and nature reserves in Laos Another 10 NBCAs have been proposed, many of them being treated by authorities as though they were already officially protected.World Database on Protected Areas
