[[Soharin]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Soharin ([tpl]lang-fa|سهرين[/tpl], also Romanized as Soharīn and Sohrīn; also known as Mehrīn, Zokhrun, Z̄ūkarā’īn, and Zukrāīn)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3085976|Soharin[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 2,309, in 602 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
