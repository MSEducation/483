[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Yola]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic Church in Nigeria, Roman Catholic dioceses in Nigeria, Religious organizations established in 1950, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century, Adamawa State, 1950 establishments in Nigeria

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Yola ([tpl]lang-la|Yolaën(sis)[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Yola in Plateau State and in the Ecclesiastical province of Jos in Nigeria.
