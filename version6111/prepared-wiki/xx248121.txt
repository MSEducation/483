[[Noritada Saneyoshi]]

CATEGORIES: 1972 births, Living people, Japanese footballers, Gamba Osaka players, J. League players, People from Ehime Prefecture

Noritada Saneyoshi (born 19 October 1972 in Minamiuwa District, Ehime) is a Japanese former professional football player who spent his entire senior career at Gamba Osaka. He was a member of the Japan national football team at the 1993 Summer Universiade.
