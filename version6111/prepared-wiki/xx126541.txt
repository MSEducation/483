[[Armenio]]

Armenio ([tpl]lang-el|Αρμένιο[/tpl]) is a village, a former municipality and an ancient city in the Larissa regional unit, Thessaly, Greece. Since the 2011 local government reform it is part of the municipality Kileler, of which it is a municipal unit.Kallikratis law Greece Ministry of Interior [tpl]el icon[/tpl] Population 2,273 (2001).
