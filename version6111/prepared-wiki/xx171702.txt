[[Quarry Falls (Macon County)]]

CATEGORIES: Protected areas of Macon County, North Carolina, Waterfalls of North Carolina

Quarry Falls is a small waterfall (or perhaps large rapid in high water) located beside US Hwy. 64 west of Highlands, North Carolina. It is best known for the large, deep pool at the bottom and is a popular place for swimming during warm weather.  Quarry Falls is known to the locals as "Bust-Your-Butt-Falls" or "Sliding Rock".
