The Bronx Times is an online newspaper, founded in March, 2005[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bronx.com/about.html|title=The Bronx Times & Bronx.com - About Us|last=Milosheff|first=Peter|year=2013|publisher=The Bronx Times|accessdate=11 August 2013|quote=The Bronx Times was born in March, 2005 as a full-fledged online newspaper upon completing the strategic acquisition of the Bronx.com domain name.[/tpl][tpl]Primary-source-inline|date=August 2013[/tpl] by Peter Milosheff,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.doubleknot.com/openrosters/DocDownload.aspx?id=112136|title=Community Newspapers Contact List|publisher=Greater New York Councils - Boy Scouts of America|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] in the Financial District of Manhattan, NY.
The newspaper came into existence upon completing the strategic acquisition of the Bronx.com domain name, which has since served as the newspaper's website and primary outlet.
The Bronx Times developed as an informational outlet on everything related to New York City's borough of Bronx, along with other local, national and international events, free of charge.
The content of The Bronx Times is updated continuously with current news and editorials by over 40 reporters, with a readership mass of more than 250,000.
