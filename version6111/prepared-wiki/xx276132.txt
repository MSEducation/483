[[Critodemus]]

CATEGORIES: Ancient Greek astrologers, Year of birth unknown, Year of death unknown

Critodemus was a Greek astrologer. He was used as a source by Pliny and Vettius Valens among others. Dates uncertain but 1st century AD at the latest.O. Neugebauer, H. B. Van Hoesen. Greek Horoscopes. p, 185. http://books.google.com/books?id=kEgnLpm06zQC&pg=PA185
