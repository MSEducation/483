[[KWFH]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Arizona, Radio stations established in 1975

KWFH is a radio station playing Christian Contemporary Music licensed to Parker, Arizona, broadcasting on 90.3 MHz FM.   The station serves the areas of Lake Havasu City, Arizona, Needles, California, and Parker, Arizona. KWFH is owned by Advance Ministries, Inc. KWFH fcc.gov. Accessed August 2, 2012
