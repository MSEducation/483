[[Epicoccum nigrum]]

CATEGORIES: Plant pathogens and diseases, Ascomycota incertae sedis, Fungi described in 1816

Epicoccum nigrum is a species of fungus. A plant pathogen, it is widespread fungus which produces colored pigments that can be used as antifungal agents against other pathogenic fungi. Fluorescent stain Epicocconone is extracted from it.
