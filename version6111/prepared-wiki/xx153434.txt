[[Paul Miller (defensive end)]]

CATEGORIES: 1930 births, 2007 deaths, Sportspeople from New Orleans, Louisiana, Players of American football from Louisiana, American football defensive ends, LSU Tigers football players, Los Angeles Rams players, Dallas Texans (AFL) players, San Diego Chargers (AFL) players, Western Conference Pro Bowl players

Paul William Miller, Jr. (November 8, 1930[tpl]spaced ndash[/tpl]January 24, 2007) was an American football defensive end in the National Football League (NFL) and the American Football League (AFL). He played for the NFL's Los Angeles Rams (1954–1957) and the AFL's Dallas Texans (1960–1961) and San Diego Chargers (1962). He played college football at Louisiana State University.
