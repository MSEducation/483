[[Vibe Australia]]

CATEGORIES: Indigenous Australian musical groups, Indigenous Australian musicians, Indigenous Australian media, Organisations serving Indigenous Australians

Vibe Australia Pty Ltd (Vibe) is an Aboriginal media, communications and events management agency. Located in Darlinghurst, Sydney, New South Wales, they work with Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander people throughout Australia.
