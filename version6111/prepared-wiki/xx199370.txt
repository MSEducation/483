[[Iaslovăț]]

CATEGORIES: Communes in Suceava County

Iaslovăț is a commune located in Suceava County, Romania. It is composed of a single village, Iaslovăț. This became part of the then-commune of Milișăuți in 1968. It remained so until 2002, when it was split off.
The village is the birthplace of Communist politician Emil Bodnăraș.
