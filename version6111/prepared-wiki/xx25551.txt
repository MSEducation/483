[[Saskatoon Yellow Jackets]]

CATEGORIES: Sport in Saskatoon, Baseball teams in Saskatchewan

The Saskatoon Yellow Jackets are a baseball team which plays in Saskatoon, Saskatchewan.  The team is a member of the Western Major Baseball League, a collegiate summer baseball league operating in the prairie provinces of Canada. Their head coach is Bryn Biancalana, assistant coach is Rye Pothakos and pitching coach is Logan Clewes.
