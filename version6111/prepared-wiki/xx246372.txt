[[Famous Impostors]]

CATEGORIES: 1910 books, Works by Bram Stoker

Famous Impostors is the fourth and final book of nonfiction by Bram Stoker (the author of Dracula), published in 1910.http://www.fantasticfiction.co.uk/s/bram-stoker/ Stoker's Biography at Fantastic Fiction.  It is a book that deals with exposing various impostors and hoaxes.
