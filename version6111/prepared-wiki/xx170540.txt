[[HMS Pique]]

CATEGORIES: Royal Navy ship names

Seven ships of the Royal Navy have borne the name HMS ''Pique'':
[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.nmm.ac.uk/upload/pdf/Warship_Histories_Vessels_i.pdf|title=NMM, vessel ID 379457|work=Warship Histories, vol i|publisher=National Maritime Museum|accessdate=30 July 2011[/tpl][/ref] Because Pique served in the navy's Egyptian campaign (2 March to 8 September 1801), her officers and crew qualified for the clasp "Egypt" to the  Naval General Service Medal, which the Admiralty issued in 1847 to all surviving claimants.[tpl]London Gazette|issue=21077|startpage=791|endpage=792|date=15 March 1850[/tpl] She was sold for breaking up in 1819.
