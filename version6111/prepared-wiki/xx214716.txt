[[Robert H. Mollohan-Jefferson Street Bridge]]

CATEGORIES: Buildings and structures in Marion County, West Virginia, Bridges over the Monongahela River, Transportation in Marion County, West Virginia, Bridges on the National Register of Historic Places in West Virginia, Road bridges in West Virginia, National Register of Historic Places in Marion County, West Virginia, Bridges completed in 1921, 1921 establishments in West Virginia, Individually listed contributing properties to historic districts on the National Register, Historic district contributing properties in West Virginia

The Robert H. Mollohan-Jefferson Street Bridge, also known as the Million Dollar Bridge, or the High Level Bridge is located in Fairmont, Marion County, West Virginia.  It was dedicated on May 30, 1921. This bridge connects east and west Fairmont, and crosses the Monongahela River. After falling into poor repair over the years, it was preserved and repaired and reopened in October 2000. The bridge is now a significant historic landmark in the city.Koon, Thomas and Oce Smith.
