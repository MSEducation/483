[[Yengijeh, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Yengijeh ([tpl]lang-fa|ينگيجه[/tpl], also Romanized as Yengījeh; also known as Yangija, Yantidzhe, Yengejeh, and Yenkejeh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3088633|Yengijeh[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Bala Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,116, in 310 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
