[[Ganju]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Chenaran County

Ganju ([tpl]lang-fa|گنجو[/tpl], also Romanized as Ganjū) is a village in Bizaki Rural District, Golbajar District, Chenaran County, Razavi Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 19, in 4 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|09[/tpl]
