[[Buzzword]]

CATEGORIES: Buzzwords, Propaganda techniques using words, Rhetorical techniques

A buzzword is a word or phrase used to impress, or one that is fashionable. Buzzwords often originate in jargon or are neologisms.Grammar.About.com - definition of buzzword
The term was first used in 1946 as student slang.Online Etymology Dictionary. Douglas Harper, Historian.
