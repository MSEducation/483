[[Qoli Kandi, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Qoli Kandi ([tpl]lang-fa|قلي كندي[/tpl], also Romanized as Qolī Kandī; also known as Gulkandi)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3080332|Qoli Kandi[/tpl] is a village in Mojezat Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 210, in 54 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
