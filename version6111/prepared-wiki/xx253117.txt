[[David Terrien]]

CATEGORIES: 1976 births, Living people, Sportspeople from Nantes, French racing drivers, Formula Ford drivers, French Formula Three Championship drivers, 24 Hours of Le Mans drivers, International Formula 3000 drivers, FIA GT Championship drivers, Speedcar Series drivers

David Terrien (born October 27, 1976 in Nantes) is a French racing driver. He has competed in such series as International Formula 3000 and the French Formula Three Championship.
