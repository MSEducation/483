[[Hand Me Down My Bible]]

CATEGORIES: 1971 songs, The Dubliners songs, Columbia Graphophone Company singles, 1971 singles, EMI Records singles

Hand Me Down My Bible is a single by The Dubliners charting at No.7 in the Irish Charts in 1971. http://irishcharts.ie/search/placement
The song was written in protest against the political and religious views of Ulster Unionist politician, Ian Paisley.
