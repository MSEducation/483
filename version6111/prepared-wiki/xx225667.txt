[[Kawachi Dam]]

CATEGORIES: Dams in Fukuoka Prefecture, Buildings and structures in Fukuoka Prefecture, Dams completed in 1927

Kawachi Dam is a dam in Yahata Higashi, KitaKyushu City, Fukuoka Prefecture, Japan.[tpl]cite web|title=Kawachi Dam|url=http://en.structurae.de/structures/data/index.cfm?ID=s0041395|publisher=Structurae|accessdate=3 October 2010[/tpl] It has been built from stone and mortarhttp://damnet.or.jp/cgi-bin/binranA/All.cgi?db4=3616 to sustain water flow for local steel millshttp://www.travelvisionweekly.com/article.php?id=2308 between 1919 and 1927. It was once to be said as "Orient's best dam". As for Minamikawachi bridge, completed in 2006,http://www.bunka.go.jp/1osirase/pdf/houdou_20061027_kenzoubutu_sitei it is a part of Kitakyushu Seminational Park and is a national industrial heritage since 2007. A mountain hike begins from these sites.
