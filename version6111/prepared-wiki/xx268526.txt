[[Central District (Khvansar County)]]

CATEGORIES: Khvansar County, Districts of Isfahan Province

The Central District of Khvansar County ([tpl]lang-fa|بخش مرکزی شهرستان خوانسار[/tpl]) is a district (bakhsh) in Khvansar County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 31,542, in 9,394 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|10[/tpl]  The District has one city: Khvansar. The District has three rural districts (dehestan): Cheshmeh Sar Rural District, Kuhsar Rural District, and Poshtkuh Rural District.
