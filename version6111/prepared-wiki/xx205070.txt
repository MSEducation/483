[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Leopoldina]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1942, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Juiz de Fora, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Leopoldina ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Leopoldinensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Leopoldina in the Ecclesiastical province of Juiz de Fora in Brazil.
