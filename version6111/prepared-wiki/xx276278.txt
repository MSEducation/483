[[Cathedral of Our Lady of the Rosary (Duluth, Minnesota)]]

CATEGORIES: Religious organizations established in 1923, Roman Catholic cathedrals in the United States, Roman Catholic churches in Minnesota, Buildings and structures in Duluth, Minnesota, Churches in St. Louis County, Minnesota, Italianate architecture in Minnesota

The Cathedral of Our Lady of the Rosary, also known simply as the Cathedral of Our Lady, is a Roman Catholic cathedral located in Duluth, Minnesota, United States.  It is the seat of the Diocese of Duluth.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.gcatholic.org/churches/northamerica/2097.htm|title=Cathedral of Our Lady|publisher=GCatholic.org|accessdate=2011-09-26|last=|first=[/tpl]  The present cathedral replaced the former Sacred Heart Cathedral, which is now a music center.[tpl]cite web|url=http://sacredheartmusic.net/about/history|title=History|publisher=Sacred Heart Music Center|accessdate=2013-11-07|last=|first=[/tpl]
