[[Mary Lee Taylor Kinosian]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, American classical violinists

Mary Lee Taylor Kinosian is the concertmaster of the South Carolina Philharmonic and assistant concertmaster of the Greenville Symphony. She is also the violinist and composer of the Upton Trio. She lives in Columbia, SC, with her husband, Paul Kinosian, and her daughters Catherine, Jessica, and Cristina.
