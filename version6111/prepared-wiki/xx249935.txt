[[Vasquez Peak Wilderness]]

CATEGORIES: Wilderness Areas of Colorado, Protected areas established in 1993, Protected areas of Grand County, Colorado, Arapaho National Forest, Roosevelt National Forest

The Vasquez Peak Wilderness is a U.S. Wilderness Area located southwest of Winter Park, Colorado. The [tpl]convert|12300|acre|km2|adj=on[/tpl] wilderness established in 1993 in the Arapaho and Roosevelt national forests has [tpl]convert|15|mi|km[/tpl] of trails.[tpl]cite web |title=Vasquez Peak Wilderness |publisher=Wilderness.net |url=http://www.wilderness.net/index.cfm?fuse=NWPS&sec=wildView&WID=624 |accessdate=August 12, 2012[/tpl][tpl]cite web |title=Vasquez Peak Wilderness |publisher=U.S. Forest Service |url=http://www.fs.usda.gov/detail/arp/specialplaces/?cid=fsm91_058258 |accessdate=August 12, 2012[/tpl][tpl]cite web |title=Vasquez Peak Wilderness Area |publisher=Colorado Wilderness |url=http://www.coloradowilderness.com/wildpages/vasquez.html |accessdate=August 12, 2012[/tpl][tpl]cite web |title=Vasquez Peak Wilderness |publisher=Public Lands Information Center |url=http://publiclands.org/explore/site.php?id=6245 |accessdate=August 12, 2012[/tpl]
