[[Islwyn John]]

CATEGORIES: 1933 births, 2009 deaths, Alumni of the University of Wales, Lampeter, Archdeacons of Carmarthen

Islwyn David John (1933-2009) was Archdeacon of Carmarthen from 1993 until 1999.Crockford’s on-line accessed by subscription 14:08 GMT 12 August 2013
John was educated at St David's College, Lampeter and ordained in 1959.Crockford's Clerical Directory 1980-82 London, OUP, 1983 ISBN 0192000101 p 532 After curacies in Brynamman and Carmarthen he held incumbencies in Penbryn and Llandysul until his Archdeacon’s appointment.
