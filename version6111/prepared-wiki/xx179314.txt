[[Elena Fanchini]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 births, Living people, People from the Province of Bergamo, Italian alpine skiers, Alpine skiers at the 2006 Winter Olympics, Alpine skiers at the 2010 Winter Olympics, Olympic alpine skiers of Italy, Athletes of Fiamme Gialle, Alpine skiers at the 2014 Winter Olympics

Elena Fanchini (born 30 April 1985) is an alpine ski racer from Italy. She was born and lives in Val Camonica, she is the sister of Nadia Fanchini, also a racer on the Italian team. Fanchini has represented Italy at two Winter Olympics and four World Championships.
