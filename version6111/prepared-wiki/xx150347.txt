[[Music for Grass Bars]]

CATEGORIES: 1997 albums

Music For Grass Bars is an album by Plateau, released in 1997.

==Track listing==

==Personnel==

==Notes==

This is a "benefit album for the legalization of Ganja Culture".
The track "Purple Passion", while credited to Plateau, is actually a solo track by Philth originally titled "Mourning" (in an albeit longer version). It is taken from his debut album The Escapist.
