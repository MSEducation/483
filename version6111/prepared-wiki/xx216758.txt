[[Wiley-VCH]]

CATEGORIES: John Wiley & Sons, Publishing companies of Germany, Companies established in 1921, Publishing companies established in the 1920s

Wiley-VCH is a German publisher owned by John Wiley & Sons. It was founded in 1921 by two German "learned societies". Later, it was merged into the German Chemical Society (GDCh). It has been owned by John Wiley & Sons since 1996.About Wiley-VCH on the Wiley website
