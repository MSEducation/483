[[Mohammed Sadiq (diplomat)]]

CATEGORIES: Pakistani diplomats, Ambassadors of Pakistan to Afghanistan, Living people, Columbia University alumni, University of Peshawar alumni, National Defence University, Pakistan alumni

Mohammed Sadiq is a Pakistani diplomat.
He was appointed Pakistan's ambassador to Afghanistan in December 2008.
He has held a number of other senior posts within the Pakistan Ministry of Foreign Affairs, including deputy Chief of Mission in Washington DC in 1999-2000.
