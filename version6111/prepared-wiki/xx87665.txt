[[Keilor Plains railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 2002

Keilor Plains railway station is located on the Sunbury line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the western Melbourne suburb of Keilor opening on 27 January 2002 as part of the extension of the electrified network from St Albans to Sydenham.Keilor Plains Vicsig
Boom barriers were provided at Taylors Road level crossing in 1986.VR History by Andrew Waugh The level crossing was recently grade separated.Taylors Road Project VicRoads
