[[Rester la même]]

CATEGORIES: 2005 albums, Lorie (singer) albums, Sony Music France albums

Rester la même is French pop singer Lorie's fourth studio album, and her seventh album overall. It was released on October 28, 2005 and contains the hit single "Parti pour zouker". The album went straight to number one on the French Albums Chart, and also achieved success in Belgium (Wallonia).
