[[Flémalle]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Liège (province)

Flémalle ([tpl]lang-wa|Flémåle[/tpl]) is a Walloon municipality located in the Belgian province of Liége. On January 1, 2006 Flémalle had a total population of 25,140. The total area is 36.68 km² which gives a population density of 685 inhabitants per km².
