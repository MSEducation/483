[[Last Call at the Blue Note]]

CATEGORIES: Oscar Peterson live albums, 1990 live albums, Telarc Records live albums

Last Call at the Blue Note is a 1990 live album by Oscar Peterson; it is the third CD of the Telarc 4-CD set.[tpl]Allmusic|class=album|id=r145475|label=Last Call[/tpl] The performance includes three of Oscar Peterson's famous originals: "Blues Etude"; "March Past"; and "Wheatland".
