[[São Vicente (Braga)]]

CATEGORIES: Parishes of Braga Municipality

São Vicente is a Portuguese parish, located in the municipality of Braga. It has a population of 12 162 inhabitants and a total area of 1.42 km².
In São Vicente is located the Faculty of Philosophy part of Catholic University of Portugal, the Escola Secundária Sá de Miranda and the Colégio D. Diogo de Sousa.
