[[Something Good Can Work]]

CATEGORIES: Kitsuné Music singles, 2009 singles, Debut singles, Two Door Cinema Club songs

"Something Good Can Work" is the first single by the Northern Irish rock band Two Door Cinema Club. The single was first released for promotion, but was released again on 7 April 2009[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.amazon.com/dp/B0022Y5QRE|title=Kitsuné : Something Good Can Work: Two Door Cinema Club|work=Amazon.com|accessdate=8 January 2010[/tpl] after signing with Kitsuné Music.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.ponystep.com/music/article/SomethingGoodCanWork_415.aspx|title=Something Good Can Work.|work=PonyStep|date=6 January 2010|accessdate=8 January 2010|author=Hynam Kendall[/tpl] The music video has amassed over fourteen million views on YouTube.TWO DOOR CINEMA CLUB | SOMETHING GOOD CAN WORK on YouTube (retrieved 13 January 2013)
"Something Good Can Work" appeared on Kitsuné's compilation Kitsuné Maison Compilation 7 as the first track.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.musicomh.com/albums/kitsune-maison-7_0509.htm|title=Various - Kitsuné Maison 7|work=musicOMH|accessdate=8 January 2010|author=Darren Harvey|authorlink=[/tpl] The version on this compilation is different from the final album version, featuring slightly different vocals and a more guitar-oriented sound. There is also a different music video for this version.Two Door Cinema Club - Something Good Can Work on YouTube (retrieved 13 January 2013)
Both "Something Good Can Work" and "Do You Want It All?" appear on Two Door Cinema Club's  album Tourist History.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.nme.com/news/two-door-cinema-club/49082|title=Two Door Cinema Club announce debut album plans|work=NME.COM News|date=5 January 2010|accessdate=5 January 2010[/tpl] The song was used in episodes of the popular British sitcom The Inbetweeners as an instrumental version. The 2011 film Chalet Girl[tpl]cite web|title=Chalet Girl Soundtrack on Spotify|url=http://chaletgirlfilm.wordpress.com/2011/03/21/chalet-girl-soundtrack-on-spotify/|accessdate=15 September 2011|date=21 March 2011[/tpl]http://chaletgirl-movie.com/ and in the pilot of Covert Affairs.[tpl]cite web|title=Covert Affairs Episode Music Guide|url=http://www.usanetwork.com/series/covertaffairs/theshow/blogs/musiclist.html|publisher=USA Network|accessdate=15 September 2011[/tpl]
The Twelves Remix version of "Something Good Can Work" was featured in the 2012 racing video game, Forza Horizon.
