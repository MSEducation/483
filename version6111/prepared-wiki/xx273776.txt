[[Robjohn, Alabama]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Choctaw County, Alabama, Unincorporated communities in Alabama

Robjohn is an unincorporated community in Choctaw County, Alabama, United States. Robjohn is located on Alabama State Route 156 [tpl]convert|4.5|mi|km[/tpl] west of Pennington. Robjohn had a post office, which opened on July 14, 1888, and closed on January 31, 1955.[tpl]cite web|title=Postmaster Finder - Post Offices by Discontinued Date|url=http://webpmt.usps.gov/pmt014.cfm|publisher=United States Postal Service|accessdate=September 25, 2011[/tpl]
