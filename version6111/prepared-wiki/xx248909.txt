[[Alan Boston]]

CATEGORIES: American poker players, Living people

Alan "Boston" Dvorkis is an online poker professional playing predominantly at Full Tilt Poker. He is a specialist at Seven-card stud. He has over a decade of experience at the WSOP, where he has achieved many money finishes, including a runner-up finish in a Seven-card stud event.
