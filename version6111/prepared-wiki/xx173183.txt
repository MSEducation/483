[[Joe Lynn]]

CATEGORIES: 1925 births, Living people, English footballers, People from Northumberland, Association football midfielders, The Football League players, Huddersfield Town F.C. players, Exeter City F.C. players, Rochdale A.F.C. players

Joseph "Joe" Lynn (born 31 January 1925) is a former professional footballer, who played for Huddersfield Town, Exeter City and Rochdale. He was born in Seaton Sluice, Northumberland.
