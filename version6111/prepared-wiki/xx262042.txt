[[Rocks TV]]

CATEGORIES: Television channels and stations established in 2012, American television networks, Shopping networks

Rocks TV is an American reverse auction jewelry shopping channel. The channel currently broadcasts 24 hours a day and is available on DirecTV, Dish Network and also online via the channel's website. 
Rocks TV operates from The Genuine Gemstone Company studios in Redditch, United Kingdom.  The channel is the sister network to Gems TV in the UK.
