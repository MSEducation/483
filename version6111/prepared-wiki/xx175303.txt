[[KPRD]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Kansas

KPRD is a Christian radio station licensed to Hays, Kansas, broadcasting on 88.9 MHz FM, and is owned by The Praise Network, Inc.KPRD fcc.gov. Accessed August 12, 2012
KPRD airs Christian Contemporary Music, as well as a variety of Christian Talk and Teaching programs including; Back to the Bible, Focus on the Family, Insight for Living with Chuck Swindoll, Revive Our Hearts with Nancy Leigh DeMoss, Turning Point with David Jeremiah, Joni & Friends, and Unshackled!.Good News Radio - Monday-Friday Good News Radio. Accessed August 12, 2012
