[[Up All Night (Unwritten Law song)]]

CATEGORIES: 2001 songs, Unwritten Law songs

"Up All Night" is a song by the San Diego-based rock band Unwritten Law, released as the first single from the band's 2001 album Elva. It was written by singer Scott Russo and produced by Michael "Miguel" Happoldt. It was their second song to chart, reaching #14 on Billboard's Modern Rock Tracks chart.[tpl]cite web| url=[tpl]BillboardURLbyName|artist=unwritten law|chart=Alternative Songs[/tpl] |title=Unwritten Law Album & Song Chart History: Alternative Songs |publisher=Billboard charts |accessdate=2009-10-06[/tpl]
