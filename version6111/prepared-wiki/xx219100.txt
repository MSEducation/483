[[Beijing Street Church]]

CATEGORIES: Christianity in China, Churches in Dalian

Beijing Street Church ([tpl]zh|北京街礼拜堂[/tpl]) is a Protestant church located in Dalian, China  It is the former Dalian Lutheran Church ([tpl]zh|大连路德教堂[/tpl]) and its church building is now a Historical Building Protected by Dalian City.  It was recently renamed as Dalian City Cheng-en Church  ([tpl]zh|大连市承恩堂[/tpl], literally "Dalian City Receiving Grace Church"), but the local people still call it "Beijing Street Church."
