[[Black River Canal Warehouse]]

CATEGORIES: Commercial buildings on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Commercial buildings completed in 1850, Warehouses on the National Register of Historic Places, Buildings and structures in Oneida County, New York

Black River Canal Warehouse is a historic canal warehouse building located at Boonville in Oneida County, New York. It was built in 1850 and is a [tpl]frac|1|1|2[/tpl]-story, rectangular, wood-frame building, 28 feet by 40 feet with a gable roof. It was built as a warehouse on the Black River Canal and used as such until the canal was abandoned in 1924.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=14096|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: Black River Canal Warehouse|date=December 2002|accessdate=2010-01-08 |author=Raymond W. Smith|publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation[/tpl] See also: [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=14094|title=Accompanying six photos[/tpl] 
It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2003.
