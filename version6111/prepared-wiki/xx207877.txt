[[Søren Jørgensen Aandahl]]

CATEGORIES: Members of the Parliament of Norway, Møre og Romsdal politicians, 1802 births, 1886 deaths, 19th-century Norwegian people

Søren Jørgensen Aandahl (1802–1886) was a Norwegian politician.
He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament in 1842, 1851, 1854 and 1857, representing the rural constituency of Romsdals Amt (today named Møre og Romsdal). He worked as a farmer.Søren Jørgensen Aandahl — Norwegian Social Science Data Services (NSD)
