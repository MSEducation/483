[[Makuru FC]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in the Solomon Islands, Honiara

Makuru FC is a Solomon Islands football club, playing in the Honiara FA League.
They won the 2004 and 2006/2007 Honiara FA League title.Solomon Islands List of Champions - RSSSF The team is also known as JP Su'uria.
They declined to join the newly formed Telekom S-League due to religious reasons since they did not want to play on Saturdays.Solomon Islands 2011 - RSSSF
