[[(26118) 1991 TH]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1991, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(26118) 1991 TH is a main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on October 1, 1991.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
