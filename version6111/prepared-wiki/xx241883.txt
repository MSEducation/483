[[Anders Björner]]

CATEGORIES: 1947 births, Living people, Swedish mathematicians, Royal Institute of Technology academics, Members of the Royal Swedish Academy of Sciences, Directors of the Mittag-Leffler Institute

Anders Björner (born 17 December 1947"Festive Combinatorics in Honor of Anders Björner's 60th Birthday") received his Ph.D. from the Stockholm University, in 1979, under Bernt Lindström.
Anders Björner is a Swedish professor of mathematics, Department of Mathematics, Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm, Sweden. His research interest is combinatorics, as well as the related areas of algebra, geometry, topology, and computer science.Anders Björner's profile at the KTH website
His other positions include director of the Mittag-Leffler InstituteStaff of the Mittag-Leffler Institute (retrieved 19 October 2009) and editor-in-chief of Acta Mathematica Acta Mathematica profile at the Springer Verlag website (retrieved 19 October 2009)
Anders Björner is a recognized expert in algebraic and topological combinatorics.Abstract of the CBMS Regional Conference in the Mathematical Sciences - Algebraic and Topological Combinatorics of Ordered Sets - 18 - 22 July 2005
Anders Björner is a 1983 recipient of the Pólya Prize, and is a member of the Royal Swedish Academy of Sciences since 1999.
