[[Bayuquan District]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Liaoning

Bayuquan District ([tpl]zh|s=鲅鱼圈区 |t=鮁魚圈區 |p=Bàyúquān Qū[/tpl]) is a district of the city of Yingkou, Liaoning province, People's Republic of China. Located on the northeast coast of the Bohai Sea and in the northwest part of the Liaodong Peninsula, it is [tpl]convert|46|km[/tpl] south-southwest of Yingkou city centre.
