[[Botričnica]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Municipality of Šentjur

Botričnica is a settlement north of Šentjur in eastern Slovenia. The area is part of the historical Styria region. The Municipality of Šentjur is now included in the Savinja statistical region.Šentjur municipal site
The local church is dedicated to Mary of the Seven Sorrows and belongs to the Parish of Šentjur. It was built in 1736.Slovenian Ministry of Culture register of national heritage reference number ešd 3043
