[[Seishun on the Road]]

CATEGORIES: 2009 singles

[tpl]nihongo|"Seishun On The Road"|青春・オン・ザ・ロード|Youth On The Road[/tpl] is the fourth in the series of five indies collaboration singles by Japanese girl group Melon Kinenbi, in a collaboration with The Collectors. It was released as limited distribution on October 10, 2009. People that purchased the single from the Tower Records online store received a free original computer wallpaper.
