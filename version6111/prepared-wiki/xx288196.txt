[[Qezeljeh, Ijrud]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Qezeljeh ([tpl]lang-fa|قزلجه[/tpl], also Romanized as Qezeljah; also known as Kizildzhakh and Qiziljah)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3080239|Qezeljeh[/tpl] is a village in Saidabad Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 200, in 66 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
