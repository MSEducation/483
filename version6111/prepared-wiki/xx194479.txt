[[Basil Wellicome]]

CATEGORIES: 1926 births, Bobsledders at the 1948 Winter Olympics, British bobsledders, Living people

Basil William Wellicome (born December 28, 1926) was a British bobsledder who competed in the late 1940s. At the 1948 Winter Olympics in St. Moritz, he competed in the two-man event, but fell during the third run and did not finish.
