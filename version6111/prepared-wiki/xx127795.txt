[[Gen. Robert Bullock House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Florida, National Register of Historic Places in Marion County, Florida, Houses in Marion County, Florida

The Gen. Robert Bullock House is a historic home in Oklawaha, Florida, United States. It is located at the junction of Southeast 119th Court and Southeast 128 Place. On July 13, 1993, it was added to the U.S. National Register of Historic Places.
