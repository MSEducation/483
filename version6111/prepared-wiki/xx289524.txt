[[Qarah Mohammad, Bizineh Rud]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Qarah Mohammad ([tpl]lang-fa|قره محمد[/tpl], also Romanized as Qarah Moḩammad and Qareh Moḩammad; also known as Ghareh Mohammad, Moḩammadābād, Qarāmadh, and Qarā Moḩammad)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3079718|Qarah Mohammad[/tpl] is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 283, in 50 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
