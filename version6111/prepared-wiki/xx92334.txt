[[Union College (Nebraska)]]

CATEGORIES: Universities and colleges in Nebraska, Educational institutions established in 1891, Universities and colleges affiliated with the Seventh-day Adventist Church, Council of Independent Colleges, National Association of Independent Colleges and Universities members, Education in Lincoln, Nebraska, North Central Association of Colleges and Schools, Clock towers in the United States, Buildings and structures in Lincoln, Nebraska, 1891 establishments in Nebraska

Union College in Lincoln, Nebraska  is a four-year coeducational college owned and operated by the Seventh-day Adventist Church in the Midwest. Founded in 1891, the College is located in Lincoln, Nebraska.
T. R. M. Howard and Sandra Pierantozzi are Union alumni.
