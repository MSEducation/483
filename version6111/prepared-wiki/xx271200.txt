[[Amanda Fink]]

CATEGORIES: 1986 births, Living people, People from Tarzana, Los Angeles, American female tennis players

Amanda Fink (born December 4, 1986 ) is a professional American tennis player. On November 21, 2011, she reached her highest WTA singles ranking of 260 whilst her best doubles ranking was 228 on September 27, 2010.[ref]
