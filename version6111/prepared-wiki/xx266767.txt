[[Great America Rifle Conference]]

CATEGORIES: Shooting in the United States, Rifle shooting sports, NCAA conferences

The Great America Rifle Conference (GARC) is a National Collegiate Athletic Association (NCAA) rifle-only conference. The GARC was established for schools that sponsor rifle teams, but do not have rifle sponsored in their respective conferences.http://www.garcrifle.com/
