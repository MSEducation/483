Aleksei Pavlovich Prudnikov ([tpl]lang-ru|Алексей Павлович Прудников[/tpl]; born March 20, 1960) is former USSR and Russian football player who played as a goalkeeper.
His previous clubs were Spartak Moscow, Dynamo Moscow, Torpedo Moscow, Baltika Kaliningrad, Kolos Krasnodar, and Velež Mostar, Sarajevo in Yugoslavia, Chonbuk Hyundai Dinos in South Korea, Jaro in Finland.Aleksey Prudnikov at KLISF.
