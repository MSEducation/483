[[Jon-Jon Smuts]]

CATEGORIES: 1988 births, Living people, South African cricketers

Jon-Jon Smuts (born 21 August 1988) is a South African cricketer who plays for the Chevrolet Warriors in the South African domestic competitions.http://www.cricket.co.za/chevrolet-warriors.html He is a right-handed opening batsman and a slow left-arm bowler. He attended Graeme College in Grahamstown.[tpl]Citation needed|date=November 2009[/tpl] In 2010 he was selected for the South African Emerging Players' squad to tour Australia. In the 2010/11 South African domestic season he was named the Chevrolet Warriors cricketer of the season.
He is the brother of cricket player Kelly Smuts.
