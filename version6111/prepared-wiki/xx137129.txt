[[Archives of Sexual Behavior]]

CATEGORIES: Sexual orientation and medicine, Sexology journals, Bimonthly journals, Publications established in 1971, Springer Science+Business Media academic journals, English-language journals

The Archives of Sexual Behavior is a peer-reviewed academic journal in sexology. It is the official publication of the International Academy of Sex Research.

==History==

The journal was established in 1971 by Richard Green, who served as its editor-in-chief until 2001.[tpl]Citation |title=Editorial Board |work=Archives of Sexual Behavior |year=2008 |url=http://www.springer.com/public+health/journal/10508?detailsPage=editorialBoard |accessdate=2013-06-03[/tpl] He was succeeded by Kenneth J. Zucker. It is published by Springer Science+Business Media[tpl]Citation |title=Home page |work=Archives of Sexual Behavior |year=2008 |url=http://www.springer.com/public+health/journal/10508 |accessdate=2013-06-03[/tpl] and has become a leading journal in its field.[tpl]cite news |first=Daniel |last=Bergner |title=What Do Women Want? |date=2009-01-22 |publisher=New York Times |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2009/01/25/magazine/25desire-t.html?_r=2& |work=New York Times Magazine |pages=MM26 |accessdate=2013-06-03[/tpl]
