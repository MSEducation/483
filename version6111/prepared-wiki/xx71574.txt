[[Puruándiro]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Michoacán

The city of Puruándiro is in the north of the Mexican state of Michoacán. 
It is located at [tpl]coord|20|3|18|N|101|18|46|W|type:city(90,000)[/tpl]. 
The population is 90,000.
The name of the city means "place of hot water" in the Purépecha language. Puruandiro is one of the most outstanding cities in the state of Michoacan.
