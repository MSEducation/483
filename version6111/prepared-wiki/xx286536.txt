[[AM & Shawn Lee]]

CATEGORIES: Musical groups from Los Angeles, California, Musical groups from London, England, Musical duos

AM & Shawn Lee is a Los Angeles and London indie musical duo.Lipshutz, Jason, "AM & Shawn Lee: Creating Music On Different Continents", Billboard,  April 4, 2013  Its members are  producer, DJ, songwriter, artist AM and producer, multi-instrumentalist, artist Shawn Lee.  La Musique Numerique (French for Digital Music) was released May 13, 2013.[tpl]cite web|last=Collar |first=Matt |url=http://www.allmusic.com/album/la-musique-numerique-mw0002499832 |title=La Musique Numerique - AM & Shawn Lee : Songs, Reviews, Credits, Awards |publisher=AllMusic |date= |accessdate=2013-05-07[/tpl]

==Awards and achievements==

AM & Shawn Lee's song "Two Times" from their album La Musique Numerique was named “Song Of The Day” by KEXP on 23 August 2013.[tpl]cite web|url=http://blog.kexp.org/2013/04/23/song-of-the-day-am-shawn-lee-two-times/|title=KEXP Song Of The Day|publisher=KEXP|date=April 23, 2013[/tpl]
AM & Shawn Lee's song "Dark Into Light" from their album Celestial Electric was named “Song Of The Day” by KEXP on 8 August 2011.[tpl]cite web|url=http://blog.kexp.org/2011/08/08/song-of-the-day-am-shawn-lee-dark-into-light/|title=KEXP Song Of The Day|publisher=KEXP|date=August 8, 2011[/tpl]
