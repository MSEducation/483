[[Benjamin Smith House (New Bern, North Carolina)]]

CATEGORIES: Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in North Carolina, Georgian architecture in North Carolina, Federal architecture in North Carolina, Houses completed in 1790, Buildings and structures in Craven County, North Carolina, New Bern, North Carolina

The Benjamin Smith House in New Bern, North Carolina was built in 1790.  It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1972.
