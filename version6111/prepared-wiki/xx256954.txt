[[Joachim Plambeck House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses completed in 1888, Victorian architecture in Iowa, Houses in Davenport, Iowa, Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Iowa, National Register of Historic Places in Scott County, Iowa

The Joachim Plambeck House is located in the West End of Davenport, Iowa, United States.  The Late Victorian style residence has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since 1984.
