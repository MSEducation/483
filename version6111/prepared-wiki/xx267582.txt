[[Khadra Haji Ismail Geid]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Somaliland politicians, Peace, Unity, and Development Party politicians

Khadra Haji Ismail Geid ([tpl]lang-so|Khadra Xaaji Ismaaciil Geydh[/tpl], [tpl]lang-so|خضرة الحاج اسماعيل غيد[/tpl]) is a Somali politician. She is the Vice Mayor of Gabiley.

==Career==

Geid joined the ruling Peace, Unity, and Development Party Party in 2002. In May 2011, she was approved by the Interior Minister as Mayor of Gabiley District. Geid is the district's seventh Mayor since 1991 and the first woman to serve in this capacity in the Somaliland region.
