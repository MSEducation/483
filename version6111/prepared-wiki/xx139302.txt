[[Vitali Bezrukov]]

CATEGORIES: 1942 births, Living people, Russian male actors, Russian and Soviet theatre directors

Vitali Sergeyevich Bezrukov ([tpl]lang-ru|Вита́лий Сергее́вич Безру́ков[/tpl]; born 1 January 1942), is a Russian actor and theatre director.
He has made appearances with his son, Sergei Bezrukov, in two TV miniseries, Brigada (2002) and Yesenin (2005), as well as in the 2002 production of the play Aleksandr Pushkin, which Vitali directed, at the Yermolova Theatre.
