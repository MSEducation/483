[[Nairobi Marathon]]

CATEGORIES: Marathons in Kenya, Sport in Nairobi, Recurring sporting events established in 2003

Nairobi Marathon is an annual road running competition over the marathon distance which held in October in Nairobi, Kenya. First held in 2003, the competition expanded and now includes a half marathon race along with the main race.Kibet breaks Nairobi Marathon record
It was part of the "The Greatest Race on Earth", fully sponsored by Standard Chartered Bank. The other three legs of this four-marathon race were the Hong Kong Marathon, the Mumbai Marathon and the Singapore Marathon.
