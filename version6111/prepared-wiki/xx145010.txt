Valery Yesipov ([tpl]lang-ru|Валерий Вячеславович Есипов[/tpl]; born 4 October 1971 in Shchigry, Kursk Oblast) is a retired association footballer and a current manager, who is currently managing FC Sever Murmansk. He holds the record for most appearances in the Russian Premier League (390). Yesipov has also been capped five times for the Russia national football team and is among the top ten goalscorers of the Premier League (third with 88 goals [tpl]As of|2006|alt=as of 30 August 2006[/tpl]).
