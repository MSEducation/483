[[Kiyotake, Miyazaki]]

CATEGORIES: Dissolved municipalities of Miyazaki Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Kiyotake|清武町|Kiyotake-chō[/tpl] was a town located in Miyazaki District, Miyazaki Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the town had an estimated population of 28,937 and the density of 605.25 persons per km². The total area was 47.81 km².
On March 23, 2010, Kiyotake was merged into the expanded city of Miyazaki and no longer exists as an independent municipality. Miyazaki District was dissolved as a result of this merger.http://www.kokudo.or.jp/new/cities/sub/kyusyu/45.htm
