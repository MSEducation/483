[[Rehden–Hamburg gas pipeline]]

CATEGORIES: Buildings and structures completed in 1994, Natural gas pipelines in Germany

Rehden–Hamburg gas pipeline (RHG) is a [tpl]convert|132|km[/tpl] long branch of MIDAL pipeline with diameter of [tpl]convert|800|mm|in[/tpl] to supply Hamburg area with natural gas.  The pipeline was constructed in 1994 by Wingas and E.ON Hanse (Hamburger Gaswerke).
