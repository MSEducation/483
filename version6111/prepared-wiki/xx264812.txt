[[Nicolas van Houy]]

CATEGORIES: 1550s births, 1611 deaths, Dutch Golden Age painters, People from Leiden

Nicolas van Houy or de Hoey (c.1550– 1611) was a Dutch Golden Age painter active in France.

==Biography==

Van Houy was born in Leiden.  According to the RKD he was court painter to the French king Henry IV of France.Nicolas van Houy Biography in the RKD  He died in Paris.
He was the son of Jan de Hoey, active in Dijon (Palace of the Dukes of Burgundy), and in Paris from 1590–1611, where he is registered in the payment records of the French court.  He is also thought to have been the grandfather of Nikolaus van Hoy.Biography portal of the Netherlands
