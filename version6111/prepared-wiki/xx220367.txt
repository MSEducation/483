[[Bakindick Mandinka]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Gambia

 
Bakindick Mandinka   is a town in western Gambia. It is located in Lower Niumi District in the North Bank Division.  As of 2008, it has an estimated population of 1,801.World Gazetteer, Retrieved on August 20, 2008
