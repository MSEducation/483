[[Ichalkovsky District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Mordovia

Ichalkovsky District ([tpl]lang-ru|Ича́лковский райо́н[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-myv|Ицял буе[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-mdf|Ичалконь район[/tpl]) is an administrativeConstitution of the Republic of Mordovia and municipalLaw #98-Z district (raion), one of the twenty-two in the Republic of Mordovia, Russia. It is located in the northeast of the republic. The area of the district is [tpl]convert|1265|km2|sp=us[/tpl].[tpl]citation needed|date=October 2012[/tpl] Its administrative center is the rural locality (a selo) of Kemlya.Law #7-Z Population: [tpl]ru-census2010|20,582|;[/tpl] 22,835 (2002 Census);[tpl]ru-pop-ref|2002Census[/tpl] [tpl]su-census1989|26,578|.[/tpl] The population of Kemlya accounts for 23.5% of the district's total population.
