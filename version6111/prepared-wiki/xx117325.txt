Karl Dennis Perssonhttp://www.democratandchronicle.com/article/20120223/SPORTS02/302230051/Rochester-Amerks-T-J-Brennan-David-Leggio (born June 2, 1988) is a Swedish professional ice hockey defenceman. He is currently playing with the Brynäs IF of the Brynäs IF Swedish Elite League.

==Playing career==

Persson was drafted by the Buffalo Sabres in the first round, 24th overall, in the 2006 NHL Entry Draft. The Buffalo Sabres signed Persson to a three-year entry level contract on June 3, 2008.
On March 25, 2009, Persson was reassigned to the Buffalo Sabres' American Hockey League (AHL) affiliate, the Portland Pirates.
