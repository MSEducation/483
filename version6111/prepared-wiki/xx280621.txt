[[Tomáš Komara]]

CATEGORIES: 1994 births, Living people, Slovak footballers, Association football midfielders, FK Senica players, Slovak First League players

Tomáš Komara (born 23 May 1994) is a Slovak football midfielder who currently plays for the Corgoň Liga club FK Senica. 

==Career==
