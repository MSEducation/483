[[KOVE (AM)]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Wyoming, Country radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 1950, 1950 establishments in Wyoming

KOVE (1330 AM, "Country 1330") is a radio station broadcasting a country format. Licensed to Lander, Wyoming, USA, the station serves the Riverton area.  The station is currently owned by Fremont Broadcasting, Inc.[tpl]cite web |title=KOVE Facility Record |url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=KOVE |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division[/tpl]
