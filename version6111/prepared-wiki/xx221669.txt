Robert "Bobby" Lemieux (born December 16, 1944) is a retired professional ice hockey defenceman who played 19 games for the Oakland Seals of the National Hockey League (NHL) in 1967–68.  He was a First Team All-Star and was named the top defenceman of the International Hockey League in 1965–66 while playing with the Muskegon Zephyrs.
