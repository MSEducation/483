[[Yamaha Y135LC]]

CATEGORIES: Yamaha motorcycles

The Yamaha T135[tpl]Clarify|date=May 2010[/tpl] is an underbone motorcycle manufactured by Yamaha Motor Company.  It is also known as the Spark 135 in Thailand, Sniper in the Philippines, Jupiter MX in Indonesia, 135LC in Malaysia, Exciter in Vietnam, and T 135 Crypton X in Greece. It is powered by a liquid-cooled [tpl]convert|134.4|cc|cuin|abbr=on[/tpl] 4-stroke engine. It is considered a better fit for circuit racing than its rival Honda Sonic 125 and the Suzuki Raider 150 and is one of the fastest 4-stroke underbone motorcycles in Southeast Asia.
In 2008, the fuel-injected version of the Yamaha Spark 135 was launched for the Thailand market, making it the second underbone motorcycle using fuel injection after the Honda Wave 125i.  There are 3 main Yamaha factories that assembles T135. These are Indonesia, Thailand and newest is Philippines.[tpl]Citation needed|date=December 2009[/tpl]
In 2011, the Yamaha Y135LC was facelifted with a new instrument panel. The transmission of the Malaysian manual clutch variant was revised to 5-speed transmission from the previous 4-speed.[tpl]cite journal |title = Yamaha 135LC Baru Meningkatkan Populariti [tpl]ms icon[/tpl] |journal = Roda-Roda |issue = May 2011 |pages = 28â€“30 |publisher = Bayu Enterprise |location = Sungai Buloh, Malaysia |issn = 0127-9149 [/tpl]

==Model history==

The Yamaha T135 debuted in 2005 for the Thai and Indonesian markets, and then the Malaysian market in February 2006.  The model was very successful in Southeast Asia especially in Malaysia.[tpl]Citation needed|date=December 2009[/tpl]
To promote the T135, Yamaha organized a [tpl]convert|9000|km|abbr=on[/tpl] road tour of ASEAN (Association of Southeast Asian Nations) countries in November 2005 through May 2006, passing through Thailand, Malaysia, Singapore, and the Philippines.http://www.yamaha-motor.co.jp/global/asean-touring/index.html
