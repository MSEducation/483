[[Clavelina sabbadini]]

CATEGORIES: Ascidiacea, Animals described in 1987

[tpl]italic title[/tpl][tpl]italictitle[/tpl]

Clavelina sabbadini is a species of tunicate (sea squirt), in the genus Clavelina (the "little bottles"). Like all ascidians, these sessile animals are filter feeders.

==Distribution==

This species is found in European waters and in the Mediterranean Sea.Ascidiacea World Database - Clavelina sabbadini Brunetti, 1987
