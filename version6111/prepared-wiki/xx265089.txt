[[Farm to Market Road 477]]

CATEGORIES: Farm to Market Roads in Texas, Transportation in Guadalupe County, Texas

Farm to Market Road 477 (FM 477) is a [tpl]convert|2.462|mi|km|abbr=on[/tpl] farm-to-market road in Guadalupe County, Texas.

==Route description==

The western terminus of FM 477 is at SH 123 south of Seguin. The route travels east before ending at an intersection with FM 466.

==History==

The current routing of FM 477 was designated in 1955. A previous FM 477 was designated in Atascosa County in 1945, from SH 173 to Christine; that route was cancelled in 1952, when it became part of FM 140.
