[[Hirokazu Kobayashi (figure skater)]]

CATEGORIES: Japanese male single skaters, 1985 births, Living people

[tpl]nihongo|Hirokazu Kobayashi|小林 宏一|Kobayashi Hirokazu|extra=born June 29, 1985 in Tokyo, Japan[/tpl] is a Japanese figure skater. He placed 22nd at the 2003 World Junior Figure Skating Championships and 12th at the 2004 NHK Trophy. He is coached by Nobuo Satō.
