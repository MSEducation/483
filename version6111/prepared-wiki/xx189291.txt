[[Columbus Historic Riverfront Industrial District]]

CATEGORIES: National Historic Landmarks in Georgia (U.S. state), Historic districts in Georgia (U.S. state), Historic districts in Columbus, Georgia

Columbus Historic Riverfront Industrial District is a historic district in Columbus, Georgia. It is located in the main district of Downtown.  It consists of four separated areas along the Chattahoochee River from 8th Street North to 38th Street.
It was declared a National Historic Landmark in 1978.[tpl]Cite document|title=[tpl]PDFlink|National Register of Historic Places Inventory-Nomination: Columbus Historic Riverfront Industrial District|32 KB[/tpl]|date=February 10, 1978 |author=John S. Lupold |publisher=National Park Service|postscript=[/tpl] and [tpl]PDFlink|Accompanying 16 photos, exteriors and interiors, from c. 1910, 1924, 1940, 1966, and 1977|32 KB[/tpl]
It includes Columbus Iron Works, also listed on the National Register.
