[[Dixieland Jubilee Records]]

CATEGORIES: Defunct record labels of the United States, Jazz record labels

[tpl]unreferenced|date=December 2006[/tpl]right
Dixieland Jubilee Records was a United States record label of the 1950s that featured Dixieland and jug band music.  The label was owned by Gene Norman and Frank Bull.
Artists featured included Teddy Buckner, Kid Ory and Wild Bill Davison.
In the 1960s it was folded into GNP Crescendo Records also owned by Norman.
In the first decade of the 2000s the repertoire has become available for downloading on iTunes and Emusic.
