Ádám Hrepka (born 15 April 1987 in Szeged) is an Hungarian football player who plays for Szombathelyi Haladás He is a striker and has also played for the Hungary national team.[tpl]cite web|url=http://hlsz.hu/index.php?tid=7&Jatekos=hPLf_74e23ed|title=HREPKA ÁDÁM (magyar) részletes adatlapja|language=Hungarian|publisher=Hivatasos Labdarugok Szervezete|accessdate=20 January 2010[/tpl]

==Club career==

Born in Szeged, Csongrád in Southern Hungary, Hrepka was with local side Szeged, before joining Újpest where he stayed until 2004 as a youth player. In 2004 he joined Hungarian National Championship I side MTK Hungária, making nine appearances and scoring two goals in his first season. In 2005–06 he scored twelve goals in 24 appearances, and a further 11 goals in 27 appearances the following season as MTK finished as runners-up in the league.[tpl]NFT player|pid=17299|Hrepka, Ádám|accessdate=25 January 2010[/tpl]
He spent a short time in 2007–08 on loan at Dutch Eredivisie club, NEC, making seven appearances. He also made 17 appearances back at MTK, scoring twice, as the club won the league title. He then made 27 appearances, scoring five goals in 2008–09. On 8 October 2008 he scored all four goals in MTK's 4–2 victory over Kaposvári Rákóczi in the 1st leg and added two more in the 6–1 win in the 2nd leg of their Hungarian Cup, Round 5 tie.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.goalzz.com/main.aspx?c=4043&stage=2|title=Hungary Cup 2008/2009|publisher=goalzz.com|accessdate=25 January 2010[/tpl]
In the 2009–10 season Hrepka was loaned to Budapest Honvéd. In late-January 2010, during the league's winter break, he was linked with a move to English Championship side Blackpool.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.pepsifoci.hu/node/52709|title=Fordulat Hrepka ügyében: Angliába utazik próbajátékra|date=24 January 2010|publisher=pepsifoci.hu|language=Hungarian|accessdate=25 January 2010[/tpl][tpl]cite news|url=http://www.skysports.com/story/0,19528,11728_5885345,00.html|title=Tangerines hungary for striker, Blackpool eye Hungarian|last=Pal|first=Juha|date=25 January 2010|publisher=Sky Sports|accessdate=31 January 2010[/tpl] MTK confirmed on 24 January on the club's official website that Hrepka had travelled to Blackpool to discuss a possible move.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.mtkhungaria.hu/index.php?action=main&nid=9053&sact=news|title=Hrepka Ádám Blackpoolba tart|date=24 January 2010|publisher=MTK Hungária|language=Hungarian|accessdate=25 January 2010[/tpl]
