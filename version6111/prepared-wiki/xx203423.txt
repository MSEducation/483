[[Santa Elena Province]]

CATEGORIES: Santa Elena Province, Provinces of Ecuador, States and territories established in 2007

The Province of Santa Elena is a province of Ecuador in the coastal region. Created in 2007 from territory that belonged to the Guayas Province, it is one of the two newest provinces of Ecuador, along with Santo Domingo de Los Tsáchilas. Its capital city is Santa Elena, from which the province derives its name.Santa Elena Info
