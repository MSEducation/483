Ilian Yordanov (Bulgarian: Илиян Йорданов; born 3 April 1989) is a Bulgarian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Denizlispor.[tpl]cite web|publisher=footballdatabase.eu|title=Ilian Yordanov Facts|url=http://www.footballdatabase.eu/football.joueurs.iliyan.yordanov.49561.en.html|accessdate=[/tpl]

==Career==

Yordanov started his career in Botev Plovdiv but left when the club went bankrupt in the early 2010 and joined Lyubimets 2007.
