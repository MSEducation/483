[[Mississippi Highway 702]]

CATEGORIES: Benton County, Mississippi, State highways in Mississippi

Mississippi Highway 702 is a short unsigned highway located in Michigan City, Mississippi, connecting Mississippi Highway 7 to Jackson Avenue.

==Route description==

The entire span of MS 702 is only 0.3 miles. It begins as a spur off of Mississippi Highway 7 heading east, also known as Main Street. Then, MS 702 crosses two tracks of the Mississippi Central Railroad. MS 702 then turns abruptly south and becomes Fort Street.
