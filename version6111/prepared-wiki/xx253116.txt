[[Kochkurovsky District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Mordovia

Kochkurovsky District ([tpl]lang-ru|Кочку́ровский райо́н[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-myv|Кочкурбуе[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-mdf|Кочкуровань район[/tpl]) is an administrativeConstitution of the Republic of Mordovia and municipalLaw #121-Z district (raion), one of the twenty-two in the Republic of Mordovia, Russia. It is located in the south of the republic. The area of the district is [tpl]convert|816.46|km2|sp=us[/tpl].[tpl]citation needed|date=October 2012[/tpl] Its administrative center is the rural locality (a selo) of Kochkurovo.Law #7-Z Population: [tpl]ru-census2010|10,794|;[/tpl] 11,829 (2002 Census);[tpl]ru-pop-ref|2002Census[/tpl] [tpl]su-census1989|13,863|.[/tpl] The population of Kochkurovo accounts for 29.6% of the district's total population.
