[[Martin Buchwieser]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 births, Living people, Adler Mannheim players, Augsburger Panther players, EHC München players

Martin Buchwieser (born. May 28, 1989) is a German professional ice hockey forward who currently plays for Adler Mannheim of the Deutsche Eishockey Liga (DEL). After his third season in the DEL with EHC München, his first as captain in 2012–13, Buchwieser was released as a free agent and signed by Adler Mannheim on a two-year contract on April 12, 2013.[tpl]citeweb| url =  http://adler-mannheim.de/news.php?d=3727&more=yes&naid= | title = Eagles commit to Buchwieser, Hospelt and Fischer | publisher = Adler Mannheim | date = 2013-04-12 | accessdate = 2013-04-12 | language = German[/tpl]
