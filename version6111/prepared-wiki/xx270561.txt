[[Big Foot (Nico Touches the Walls song)]]

CATEGORIES: 2009 singles, Nico Touches the Walls songs

"Big Foot" (ビッグフット) is the fourth major single by the Japanese band Nico Touches the Walls from their second studio album Aurora.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.jame-world.com/us/news-45404-latest-from-nico-touches-the-walls.html|title=Latest from NICO Touches The Walls|accessdate=2011-11-20 |publisher=jame-world.com |date=March  1, 2009[/tpl] 
"Big Foot" contains five live tracks from their previous successes, all performed during the last show of their tour called Tour 2008 Bon Voyage, Etranger.

==Chart position==

The single was released on May 13, 2009, and reached number 24 on the Oricon Chart in Japan, that year.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.oricon.co.jp/prof/artist/386109/products/music/812987/1/|title=ＮＩＣＯ　Ｔｏｕｃｈｅｓ　ｔｈｅ　Ｗａｌｌｓの作品(CD)|accessdate=2011-11-20 |publisher=oricon.com |date=March  13, 2009[/tpl]
