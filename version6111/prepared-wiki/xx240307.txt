[[Setúbal Municipality]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Portugal, Populated places in Setúbal District, Setúbal Municipality

Setúbal Municipality is located in Setúbal District. The main city is Setúbal.
The municipality of Setúbal is composed of 8 parishes, and is the seat of the Setúbal District on the subregion of Península de Setúbal. It is located on the northern bank of the Sado River estuary, approximately 40 km south of Portugal's capital, Lisbon.
