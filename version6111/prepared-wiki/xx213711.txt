[[CASEY Award]]

CATEGORIES: Baseball trophies and awards, Baseball books, Baseball writers, Sports writing awards, Awards established in 1983

The CASEY Award has been given to the best baseball book of the year since [tpl]by|1983[/tpl]. The honor was begun by Mike Shannon and W.J. Harrison, editors and co-founders of “Spitball: The Literary Baseball Magazine.”
