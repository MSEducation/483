[[Owli Beyk]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Owli Beyk ([tpl]lang-fa|اولي بيك[/tpl], also Romanized as Owlī Beyk; also known as Olībeyg and Owlī Beyg)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3077520|Owli Beyk[/tpl] is a village in Shivanat Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 762, in 156 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
