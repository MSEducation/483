[[Victor Jones]]

CATEGORIES: 1966 births, Living people, American football linebackers, Virginia Tech Hokies football players, Detroit Lions players, Tampa Bay Buccaneers players

Victor Purnell Jones (born October 19, 1966 in Rockville, Maryland) is a former professional American football linebacker in the National Football League for the Tampa Bay Buccaneers, Detroit Lions, He played college football at Virginia Tech and was drafted in the twelfth round of the 1988 NFL Draft.
