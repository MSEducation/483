[[Jerry Simmons (American football)]]

CATEGORIES: 1942 births, Living people, Sportspeople from Bartow, Florida, American football wide receivers, Bethune-Cookman Wildcats football players, Undrafted National Football League players, Pittsburgh Steelers players, New Orleans Saints players, Atlanta Falcons players, Chicago Bears players, Denver Broncos players, New England Patriots coaches, Players of American football from Florida

Jerry Bernard Simmons (born November 14, 1942 in Nichols, Florida) is a former American football wide receiver. He played college football at Bethune-Cookman University, and professionally for ten seasons in the National Football League for the Pittsburgh Steelers, the New Orleans Saints, the Atlanta Falcons, the Chicago Bears, and the Denver Broncos.
