[[Danielle Foote]]

CATEGORIES: 1987 births, Australian female singers, Australian musicians, Big Brother Australia, Big Brother Australia contestants, Living people

Danielle Foote, born 8 June 1987 in Adelaide, Australia, is a singer and former Big Brother Australia 2006 housemate. She is signed to Secret Days, an independent record label based in Brisbane, Australia, and her debut single, a cover of Underworld's Underneath the Radar, was released on 7 August 2006. It debuted at No.52 on the ARIA charts, and peaked at No.41.http://www.australian-charts.com/showitem.asp?interpret=Danielle&titel=Underneath+The+Radar&cat=s Danielle Foote attended Oakbank Area School which is near where she lived in woodside, South Australia.
