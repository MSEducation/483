[[Motherboard Monitor]]

CATEGORIES: Motherboard, Computer performance

Motherboard Monitor is a utility designed by Alexander Van Kaam for Microsoft Windows that provides live information about the state of a motherboard and other hardware, including temperatures, voltages, fan speeds, and more.
On July 6, 2004, it was announced that development on Motherboard Monitor has been stopped.The End of Motherboard Monitor http://www.tomshardware.co.uk/forum/105811-12-motherboard-monitor However, on September 13, 2006, a new update (Update 2) was released adding slightly more compatibility with new motherboards.
On December 8, 2006 a new update (Update 3) was released to add compatibility with Intel's Core/Core2/Core i7(preliminary) Digital Thermal Sensors via a plugin.MBM5 Update 3 with Core(TM) Temp plugin http://www.thetechrepository.com/showthread.php?t=74
Current alternatives to Motherboard Monitor include Open Hardware Monitor, SpeedFan and RealTemp.
