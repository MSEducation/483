Niki Bettendorf (born 20 December 1936 in Belvaux) is a Luxembourgish politician.  He served as Mayor of Bertrange from 1982 until 2001.
He was a member of the Chamber of Deputies for sixteen years, from 19 June 1990 until he resigned on 10 October 2006,[tpl]fr icon[/tpl] [tpl]cite web |url=http://www.chd.lu/fr/organisation/membres/membres.jsp?ID=56 |title=Niki Bettendorf |accessdate=2008-01-02 |date= |publisher=Chamber of Deputies [/tpl] [tpl]Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot[/tpl] when he resigned in favour of the younger Alex Krieps.
