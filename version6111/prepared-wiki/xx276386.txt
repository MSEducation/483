[[Arturo Fernández Meyzán]]

CATEGORIES: 1906 births, 1999 deaths, Peruvian footballers, Peru international footballers, Association football defenders, Universitario de Deportes footballers, 1930 FIFA World Cup players, Universitario de Deportes managers

Arturo Fernández Meyzán (3 February 1906 - 27 November 1999) was a Peruvian football defender who played for Peru in the 1930 FIFA World Cup.1930 FIFA World Cup Uruguay He also played for Universitario de Deportes.
His brother was the also footballer Teodoro Fernández Meyzán.
