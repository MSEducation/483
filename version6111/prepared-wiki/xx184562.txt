[[Now That's What I Call Music! 69 (UK series)]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 compilation albums, Now That's What I Call Music! albums (British series), EMI Records compilation albums, Universal Music Group compilation albums, Virgin Records compilation albums

Now That's What I Call Music! 69 or Now 69 is a compilation album that was released on 17 March 2008.Album listing at play.com. The album is the 69th edition of the original UK Now! series. It was released on compact disc and to digital music retailers. In its first week of release, it sold 382,759 copies, at the time the biggest ever first week sale of any Now album beating the previous record of Now 57 in 2004.  Now 69 is the second fastest selling Now after Now 70.Now 69 record sales musicweek.com It was the first in the series to include bonus music videos from iTunes.
Now 69 features four songs which reached number one on the UK Singles Chart: "Mercy", "Now You're Gone", "When You Believe", and "What a Wonderful World".
The original version of the Utah Saints song Something Good previously appeared on Now! 22.
