[[Whole Hog Café]]

CATEGORIES: Companies based in Little Rock, Arkansas, Restaurant chains in the United States, Companies based in Arkansas

Whole Hog Café is a restaurant chain based in Little Rock, Arkansas.
It offers barbecue along with side dishes including potato salad, beans, coleslaw, salad, and dinner rolls.  Other specialties include barbecue nachos and loaded baked potatoes.[tpl]cite news |title=Whole Hog Café Converts Non-Believers |newspaper=Arkansas Business Publishing Group |date=2001-04-09 |url=http://findarticles.com/p/articles/mi_hb5248/is_15_18/ai_n28832951/ |accessdate=2010-01-09 |archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/5meMk1ISr |archivedate=2010-01-09 [/tpl] Currently there are twelves locations with restaurants in Arkansas, Louisiana, Missouri, New Mexico, and New Jersey.[tpl]cite news |title=Whole Hog Café set to open in August |author=Jacobs, Dee Dee |newspaper=Springfield Business Journal |publisher=American City Business Journals |date=2009-05-26 |url=http://sbj.net/main.asp?SectionID=18&SubSectionID=23&ArticleID=84827&TM=43890.04 |accessdate=2010-01-09 |archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/5meMtELuI |archivedate=2010-01-09 [/tpl]
