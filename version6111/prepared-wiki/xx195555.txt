[[Regular Lovers]]

CATEGORIES: 2005 films, Films directed by Philippe Garrel, French films, French-language films, May 1968 in France

Regular Lovers ([tpl]lang-fr|Les amants réguliers[/tpl]) is a 2005 film directed by Philippe Garrel and starring his son, actor Louis Garrel. The director's father, actor Maurice Garrel, has a supporting role. 
The film won the Louis Delluc Prize. The director won a Silver Lion. Actors William Lubtchansky won a Golden Osella, and Louis Garrel won a César Award for Most Promising Actor for his performance.
