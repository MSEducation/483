[[Ezee-On]]

CATEGORIES: Manufacturing companies of Canada, Companies established in 1949, Companies based in Alberta

Ezee-On Manufacturing, a Vegreville, Alberta company is an operating company of TerraVest Income Fund. Ezee-On Manufacturing develops and manufactures farm equipment for the agriculture sector.

==Profile==

Ezee-On Manufacturing is company that serves the agricultural sector, both domestically and abroad, for over 50 years. Ezee-On Manufacturing develops and manufactures farm equipment for the agriculture sector. Products include Heavy-duty Offset and Tandem Discs, Air Drills, Coulter Drills & Air Seeder Carts, Tillage Equipment: Chisel Plows, Field Cultivators, Front-end Loaders, and Post Drivers.
The company has operations in Canada, the United States, and Australia.

==Corporate governance==

Current members of the board of directors of TerraVest Income Fund are: John Zaozirny, Bob Phillips, Jim Hall, Murray Wallace, Dale Laniuk, Tom Kileen, and Tim Zosel.
