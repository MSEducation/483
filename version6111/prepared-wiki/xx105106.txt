thumb|KPU(o) election posterThe Communist Party of Ukraine (renewed) ([tpl]lang-uk|Комуністична партія України (оновлена)[/tpl]; Komunistyčna Partija Ukraïny (onovlena)) is a political party in Ukraine, formed in November 2000[tpl]uk icon[/tpl] Комуністична партія України (оновлена), sd.net following a split from the Communist Party of Ukraine (KPU). KPU(o) is led by Mykhaylo Savenko, previously a member of Parliament of the pro-president KuchmaThe European Union and Democratization: Reluctant States (Europe and the Nation State) by Paul Kubicek, Routledge, ISBN 978-0-415-31136-6, page 171 Labour Ukraine.Ukraine Political Parties, GlobalSecurity.org
KPU claimed that the formation of KPU(o) was instigated by the political establishment, in order to steal votes from KPU.http://www.bhhrg.org/CountryReport.asp?ChapterID=9&CountryID=22&ReportID=2&keyword=[tpl]Dead link|date=November 2009[/tpl]
At the legislative elections on 30 March 2002, the party won 1.4% of the popular vote and no seats. despite the fact that only six other parties spent more on their election campaign.Virtual Politics - Faking Democracy in the Post-Soviet World, Andrew Wilson, Yale University Press, 2005, ISBN 0-300-09545-7 page 256 In the 30 September 2007 elections, the party garnered only 0.29% of the popular vote.
