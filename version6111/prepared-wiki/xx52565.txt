[[Imfundo]]

CATEGORIES: Public bodies and task forces of the United Kingdom government

Imfundo is the team within the United Kingdom Department for International Development that creates partnerships to deliver information technology-based activities supporting gender equality and universal primary education in sub-Saharan Africa. 
The name Imfundo is from the Nguni languages of southern Africa. It means "The acquisition of knowledge; the process of becoming educated."
