[[1966 USAC Championship Car season]]

CATEGORIES: 1966 in motorsport, USAC Championship Car, 1966 in American motorsport

The 1966 USAC Championship Car season consisted of 16 races, beginning in Avondale, Arizona on March 20 and concluding at the same location on November 20.  There was also one non-championship event at Fuji Speedway in Japan. The USAC National Champion was Mario Andretti, and the Indianapolis 500 winner was Graham Hill. At Indianapolis in the Qualifying of the 500 miles race death Chuck Rodee. He was 39 years old.
