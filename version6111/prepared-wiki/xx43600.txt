[[Funk metal]]

CATEGORIES: Funk metal, Heavy metal subgenres, Fusion music genres

Funk metal (also known as thrash funk,[tpl]cite web|url=http://news.google.com/newspapers?id=mCEyAAAAIBAJ&sjid=3uUFAAAAIBAJ&pg=6991,2564797&dq=thrash-funk&hl=en|title=Roll Over Manilow: Thrash funk is here|first=Elisabeth|last=Dunham|publisher=Lawrence Journal-World|accessdate=2012-11-16[/tpl] funkcore,  or punk-funk) is a subgenre of funk rock and alternative metal that fuses elements of heavy metal and funk.
