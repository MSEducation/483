[[KTRY]]

CATEGORIES: Media in Sonoma County, California, Radio stations in the San Francisco Bay Area, Media in Santa Rosa, California

KTRY (106.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a country music format.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.arbitron.com |date=Spring 2010 |title=Station Information Profile |publisher=Arbitron |accessdate=2011-07-01[/tpl] Licensed to Cazadero, California, USA, the station serves the Santa Rosa area.  The station is currently owned by Redwood Empire Stereocasters.
