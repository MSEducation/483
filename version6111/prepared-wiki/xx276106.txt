[[(27743) 1990 VM]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1990, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(27743) 1990 VM is a main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on November 8, 1990.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
