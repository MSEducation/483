[[Aries Trikala B.C.]]

CATEGORIES: Basketball teams in Greece, Sports clubs established in 1993

Aries Trikala B.C. (Greek:Τρίκαλα B.C KAE) is a Greek professional basketball club that is based in Trikala, Greece. The club was previously known as Aeolus Trikalon (Greek: Αίολος Τρικάλων).

==History==

Aries Trikala B.C. was founded in 1993, under the name Aeolus Trikalon. In 2012, the club changed its name to Trikala B.C., and it competed in the Greek Second Division for the first time, during the 2012-13 season.OnSports.gr Αίολος Τρικάλων: Μετονομάστηκε σε Τρίκαλα BC [tpl]el icon[/tpl]. They competed in the top Greek League for the first time, in the 2013-14 season.
