[[Emessa - Homs]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 debut albums, Albums by Syrian artists, Emessa in popular culture

Emessa - Homs includes original music compositions for piano and orchestra composed and orchestrated by Malek Jandali and recorded with The Russian Philharmonic Orchestra with Seregey Kondrashev as a conductor. The music was inspired by and dedicated to the people of Syria in their noble quest for freedom and human rights. The release of “Freedom Qashoush Symphony”  was accompanied by a hugely successful tour of the USA, Europe and the Middle East.
