[[Things Are Getting Better]]

CATEGORIES: 1959 albums, Riverside Records albums, Cannonball Adderley albums, Milt Jackson albums, Albums produced by Orrin Keepnews

Things Are Getting Better is the eleventh album by jazz saxophonist Cannonball Adderley, and his second release on the Riverside label, featuring performances with Milt Jackson, Wynton Kelly, Percy Heath and Art Blakey.Cannonball Adderley discography accessed 19 October 2009 

==Reception==
