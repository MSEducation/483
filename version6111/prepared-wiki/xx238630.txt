[[Chaetosphaeriaceae]]

CATEGORIES: Sordariomycetes, Fungi families

The Chaetosphaeriaceae are a family of fungi in the Ascomycota, class Sordariomycetes.[tpl]cite journal |last=Lumbsch TH, Huhndorf SM. |date=December 2007 |title=Outline of Ascomycota – 2007 |journal=Myconet |volume=13 |issue= |pages=1–58 |id= |url=http://www.fieldmuseum.org/myconet/outline.asp |accessdate= |publisher=The Field Museum, Department of Botany, Chicago, USA [/tpl] The family was named by M. Réblová in 1999.[tpl]cite journal |author=Réblová M, Barr ME, Samuels GJ. |year=1999 |title=Chaetosphaeriaceae, a new family for Chaetosphaeria and its relatives |journal=Sydowia |volume=51 |issue= |pages=49–70[/tpl] Species in the family have a cosmopolitan distribution, and are found in both temperate and tropical climates.[tpl]cite book |author=Cannon PF, Kirk PM. |title=Fungal Families of the World |publisher=CABI|location=Wallingford |year=2007 |pages=65–66 |isbn=0-85199-827-5[/tpl]
