[[Relentless (Natalie Grant album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 albums, Natalie Grant albums, Curb Records albums

Relentless is the second album and seventh release by Christian singer Natalie Grant. It was released February 12, 2008.[tpl]cite web |url=[tpl]BillboardURLbyName|artist=natalie grant|chart=all[/tpl] |title=Relentless - Natalie Grant|work=Billboard|accessdate=2009-09-03[/tpl] Four singles have been released from the album: "In Better Hands", "I Will Not Be Moved", "Perfect People", and "Our Hope Endures".
