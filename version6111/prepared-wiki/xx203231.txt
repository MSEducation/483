[[Canby School District]]

CATEGORIES: School districts in Oregon, Education in Clackamas County, Oregon, Canby, Oregon, Wilsonville, Oregon

Canby School District is an [tpl]convert|85|sqmi|km2|adj=on[/tpl] public school district based in Canby, Oregon, United States, and serving students in Canby and the surrounding rural area of Clackamas County, including the community of Carus, parts of the city of Wilsonville, and as far south as the Ninety-one School near Hubbard.[tpl]cite news |title= Equity issue colors name debate |last= Green |first= Aimee |date= March 14, 2002 |work= The Oregonian[/tpl][tpl]cite web |url= http://www.charbonneauneighbors.com/schools/ |title= Local Community Schools |publisher= Charbonneau Neighbors |accessdate= 2011-03-12[/tpl][tpl]cite web |url= http://www.canby.k12.or.us/entries/1 |title= Our Schools and Information |publisher= Canby School District |accessdate= 2011-03-12[/tpl] There are approximately 5,000 students enrolled in the district's eight schools, which include five elementary schools, one middle schools, one K-8 school, and one high school. The superintendent is Jeff Rose.Canby School District

==Demographics==

In the 2009 school year, the district had 332 students classified as homeless by the Department of Education, or 6.6% of students in the district.[tpl]cite news|url=http://blog.oregonlive.com/education_impact/2009/09/homelessbydistrict.pdf|title=Count of homeless students in Oregon school districts, 2008-2009|publisher=The Oregonian|pages=6|accessdate=2009-09-18[/tpl]
