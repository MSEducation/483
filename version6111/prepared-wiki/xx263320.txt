[[Devla, India]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Rajkot district

Devla (also known as Devda, 21.781922,70.811934) is a small village in the Rajkot District, Gujarat, India near Gondal, with postal code - 364485.  It is 31 km from the taluka city Gondal and 69 km from the district capital Rajkot.  It has the population of 2000 people and is situated at the banks of Bhadar River.  Devla is one of the highest producer of cotton, garlic and onion in the Gujarat.  Devla is the clean village of the rajkot district.  The structure of devla is very best in a rajkot district.  Its people are very rich.
