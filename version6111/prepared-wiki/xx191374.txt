[[Guam national baseball team]]

CATEGORIES: National baseball teams, National sports teams of Guam

The Guam national baseball team is the national baseball team of Guam. The team represents Guam in international competitions. Guam has competed in the Asian Baseball Championship. 

==Asian Baseball Championship==

Guam have competed in the Asian Baseball Championship, and in [tpl]by|1987[/tpl] finished 4th.
