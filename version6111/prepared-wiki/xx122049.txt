[[Spiral fracture]]

CATEGORIES: Bone fractures, Traumatology

A spiral fracture (a.k.a. torsion fracture) is a bone fracture occurring when torque (a rotating force) is applied along the axis of a bone.[tpl]cite web|title=Spiral Fracture|url=http://www.fas.harvard.edu/~scdiroff/lds/NewtonianMechanics/SpiralFracture/SpiralFracture.html|publisher=Harvard University|accessdate=25 November 2010[/tpl]
