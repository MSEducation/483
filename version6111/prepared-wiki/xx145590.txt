[[Maškovce]]

CATEGORIES: Villages and municipalities in Humenné District

Maškovce (Hungarian: Maskóc) is a village and municipality in Humenné District in the Prešov Region of north-east Slovakia.

==History==

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1574.

==Geography==

The municipality lies at an altitude of 253 metres and covers an area of 6.995 km².
It has a population of about 62 people.
