[[Albert Torrens]]

CATEGORIES: 1976 births, Living people, Australian rugby league players, Huddersfield Giants players, Manly-Warringah Sea Eagles players, Northern Eagles players, St. George Illawarra Dragons players, Indigenous Australian rugby league players, Bundjalung people

Albert Torrens (born 1 July 1976) is an Australian former rugby league footballer of the 1990s and 2000s. He played for the Manly-Warringah Sea Eagles, Northern Eagles and St. George Illawarra Dragons in the NRL and in England for the Huddersfield Giants of Super League as a centre and on the wing.Torrens signs for Huddersfield
Since retiring from competitive rugby, Torrens has worked as an Indigenous outreach worker for Mission Australia.Spotlight on Albert Torrens In 2009 and 2010, he studied indigenous community health at Curtin University in Western Australia.2009 Australian Rotary Health2010 Australian Rotary Health
