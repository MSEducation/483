[[Pyrsos Grevena F.C.]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Greece, Football clubs in West Macedonia, 1928 establishments in Greece

Pyrsos Grevena F.C. is a Greek football club, based in Grevena.
The association was founded in 1928. In 2008, they were promoted to Gamma Ethniki. Previously they have played as high as the Greek second category (Beta Ethniki) in the '70s.
