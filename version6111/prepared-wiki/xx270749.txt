[[Brežđe]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Kolubara District

Brežđe is a village situated in Mionica municipality, Kolubara District in Serbia.Institut national d'études démographique (INED) A cave (Šalitrena pećina) in the village was used for living in the 4th century and then in the 12th- to 15th century.
