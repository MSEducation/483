[[Running Water (The Moody Blues song)]]

CATEGORIES: 1984 singles, The Moody Blues songs, Songs written by Justin Hayward

"Running Water" is a 1984 single by The Moody Blues guitarist Justin Hayward, and it was the final of three singles released from their 1983 album The Present.  The two previous single from The Present were "Blue World" and "Sitting at the Wheel."  Running Water was later released as a single in March 1984, with "Under My Feet," another song from The Present, on the B-side.
