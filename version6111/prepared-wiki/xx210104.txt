Issawa Singthong (Thai อิศวะ สิงห์ทอง), (born October 10, 1980) is a Thai footballer. He was the former Midfield of Thailand national team since 2002-2004. He has played for Thai Premier League side Air Force Central.[tpl]NFT player|pid=6607|Issawa Singthong[/tpl]
