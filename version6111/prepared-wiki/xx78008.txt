[[Saint-Germain-lès-Arpajon]]

CATEGORIES: Communes of Essonne

Saint-Germain-lès-Arpajon is a commune in the Essonne department.  It is a suburb of Paris located [tpl]convert|30|km|mi|abbr=on[/tpl] south of Paris via the N20, and [tpl]convert|20|km|mi|abbr=on[/tpl] north of Étampes.
Its inhabitants are called Germinois.
