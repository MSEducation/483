[[Old Dixie Seafood]]

CATEGORIES: Companies based in Boca Raton, Florida, Food retailers, Seafood companies, Retail companies established in 1995

Old Dixie Seafood is a popular seafood market and deli in Boca Raton, Florida.[tpl]cite news| last=Hartz-Seeley|first=Deborah| title=The Dish on our Fish| pages=1G| work=The South Florida Sun Sentinel | date=August 2, 2007|url=http://www.accessmylibrary.com/coms2/summary_0286-32525705_ITM [/tpl] Established in 1995 by brothers Larry and Kerry Siemsen, the market is famous locally for its marlin dip,  seafoood mustard sauce, and wide variety of fresh and frozen Floridian and Alaskan seafood. The market has been featured in local newspapers and magazines who consult the owners on recipe ideas and seafood handling procedures which are well known in Florida except for tourists.
Old Dixie was featured as the "Best of Boca" in Best of Boca Magazine in 2008 http://www.bocamag.com/Boca-Raton-Magazine/May-June-2008/Best-of-Boca/index.php?cparticle=1&siarticle=0#artanc Best of Boca Magazine The market is a retail seafood store that derives a small part of their income from shipping orders for seafood around the country however shipping prices can be quite high.http://www.olddixieseafood.com/_shipping Old Dixie Seafood website, shipping
