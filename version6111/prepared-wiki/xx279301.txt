[[Sardehat-e Bayat Jafar]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Sardehat-e Bayat Jafar ([tpl]lang-fa|سردهات بيات جعفر[/tpl], also Romanized as Sardehāt-e Bayāt Ja‘far; also known as Sardehāt, Sardehāt-e Ja‘far, Sardehāt-e Pā’īn, Sardehāt Ja‘far, and Sardekhat)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3082695|Sardehat-e Bayat Jafar[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 234, in 52 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
