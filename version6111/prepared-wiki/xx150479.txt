[[Ittaqullah]]

CATEGORIES: Arabic words and phrases

Ittaqullah ([tpl]lang-ar|اتقوا الله[/tpl]) is an Arabic phrase meaning "fear God". It is composed of the words "Ittaqu" and "Allah", and is often found in Muslim literature.
The phrase comes from the Arabic word for piety: Taqwa Muslim Scholars - Religion Islam
The phrase in itself has been subjected to several different translations, as "fear" does not adequately translate the connotation of the phrase. Other examples include "Be aware of Allah" Tasw 3-Spiritual Unveilings and "Return to God" [tpl]Citation needed|date=February 2007[/tpl].
