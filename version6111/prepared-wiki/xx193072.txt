[[Sonic Jihad (Snake River Conspiracy album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2000 debut albums, Albums produced by David Kahne, Albums produced by Jason Slater, Albums produced by Eric Valentine, Albums produced by Charlie Clouser

Sonic Jihad is Snake River Conspiracy's only full-length album, released in 2000. It includes three cover songs: T-Ride's “You and Your Friend” (former band of co-producer Eric Valentine), The Cure's “Lovesong” and The Smiths' “How Soon Is Now.”  [ref]
