[[Bijiang District]]

CATEGORIES: Geography of Guizhou

Bijiang District ([tpl]zh|s=碧江区 |t=碧江區 |p=Tóngrén Shì[/tpl]), is the seat of the city of Tongren, Guizhou province, People's Republic of China. The area was known as Tongren City (铜仁市) until November 2011, when it was renamed Bijiang District. The district has an area of [tpl]convert|1514|km2[/tpl] and has a population of 340,000.[tpl]when|date=November 2011[/tpl]
