</ref> a [[3D computer graphics|3D animated]] interactive CD incorporating images (predominantly [[Crowdsourcing|crowdsourced]]), an instrumental version of "Lifeline", the "seeds and solos only" version and "heap speaks seeds and solos" (an 18-minute Heap commentary on how sounds and solos were used). The song is a tribute to [[2011 Tōhoku earthquake#Casualties|victims of the 2011 Tōhoku earthquake, Pacific Ocean]] ([[Japanese language|Japanese]]: [[:ja:東北地方太平洋沖地震|[tpl]nowrap|東日本大震災[/tpl] [tpl]nowrap|太平洋[/tpl]]]).
"Propeller Seeds" was recorded using 3D audio effects (headphone listening recommended) and was released 8 July 2011, on Megaphonic Records.[ref]
