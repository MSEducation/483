[[Athy (Parliament of Ireland constituency)]]

CATEGORIES: Constituencies of the Parliament of Ireland (pre-1801), Historic constituencies in County Kildare, Athy, 1614 establishments in Ireland, 1800 disestablishments in Ireland, Constituencies established in 1614, Constituencies disestablished in 1800

Athy was a constituency represented in the Irish House of Commons to 1800. Following the Act of Union 1800 the borough was disenfranchised.

==History==

In the Patriot Parliament of 1689 summoned by King James II, Athy was represented with two members. O'Hart (2007), p. 502
