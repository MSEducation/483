[[Val Doonican Rocks, But Gently]]

CATEGORIES: 1967 albums, English-language albums, Val Doonican albums

Val Doonican Rocks, But Gently is the first and only number one album in the UK for Irish singer Val Doonican. It spent 3 weeks at the top of the chart in 1968.Virgin Book of Hit Albums
