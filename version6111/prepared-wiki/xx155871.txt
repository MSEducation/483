[[Holy Rosary High School (New Orleans)]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic secondary schools in New Orleans, Louisiana, Private middle schools in Louisiana, Schools in New Orleans, Louisiana

Holy Rosary High School is a private, Roman Catholic high school in New Orleans, Louisiana.  It is located in the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of New Orleans.

==Background==

Holy Rosary High School also educates students with learning disabilities such as dyslexia, dysgraphia, language delay, academic anxiety, and/or Attention Deficit Disorder.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.hra-hrhs.org|title=HRHS Admissions|accessdate=2007-05-11|author=HRHS[/tpl]

==History==

Holy Rosary High School was established in 2005 when the residents of the surrounding neighborhoods were growing older. The school opened their doors to 33 students, and when Hurricane Katrina hit, 28 students returned to the school. As of 2010 the school now has up to 150 students.
