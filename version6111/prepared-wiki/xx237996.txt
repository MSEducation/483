[[Oliveira de Azeméis (parish)]]

CATEGORIES: Cities in Portugal, Populated places in Aveiro District

Oliveira de Azeméis is a city in Oliveira de Azeméis Municipality in Porto Metropolitan Area (North of Portugal), counting more than 20,000 inhabitants.

==Sports==

Its most important sports club is the U.D.O.União Desportiva Oliveirense which fields teams in football, basketball and ring hockey.
One other club is Futebol Clube Pinheirense (FCP), it is a club dedicated mostly to football. They have a kids school in partnership with Benfica.
