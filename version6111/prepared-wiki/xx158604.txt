[[Troy Barnett]]

CATEGORIES: 1971 births, Living people, American football defensive linemen, Washington Redskins players, New England Patriots players, People from Jacksonville, North Carolina

Troy Anthony Barnett (born May 24, 1971 in Jacksonville, North Carolina) is a former American football defensive lineman in the National Football League for the New England Patriots and Washington Redskins.  He played college football at the University of North Carolina.
