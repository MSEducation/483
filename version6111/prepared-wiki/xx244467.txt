[[Maguen David Synagogue]]

CATEGORIES: Synagogues in Honduras

The Maguen David Synagogue ([tpl]lang-he|בית הכנסת מגן דוד[/tpl]) in San Pedro Sula, Honduras is one of only two synagogues in the entire country.[tpl]Cite web |url=http://www.jewishvirtuallibrary.org/jsource/vjw/Honduras.html |title=Honduras |last1=Rosen |first1=Rachel |publisher=Jewish Virtual Library |accessdate=20 October 2009 [/tpl] Built in 1997, it serves as the central gathering place of the tiny Jewish community of San Pedro Sula.
