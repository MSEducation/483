[[Nadja Kamer]]

CATEGORIES: Swiss alpine skiers, Living people, 1986 births, Alpine skiers at the 2010 Winter Olympics, Olympic alpine skiers of Switzerland, Alpine skiers at the 2014 Winter Olympics

Nadja Kamer (born 23 July 1986) is a World Cup alpine ski racer from Switzerland. From Schwyz, Kamer specializes in the speed events of Downhill and Super G and has made five World Cup podiums, all in downhill.http://www.fis-ski.com/uk/604/613.html?type=biog&competitorid=70230&sector=AL
