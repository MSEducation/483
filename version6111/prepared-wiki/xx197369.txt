Barry Mitchell Dallas [tpl]post-nominals|country=NZL|OBE|JP[/tpl] (1 December 1926 – 21 April 1992) was a New Zealand politician and doctor. He was twice mayor of Greymouth on the West Coast of New Zealand.

==Biography==

Dallas, a general practitioner, served as mayor of Greymouth from 1966 to 1968, and later from 1980 until his unexpected death in 1992.[tpl]cite news | title=Mayor adds grave-tending to his duties | date=11 July 2003 | work=The Press | page=4 [/tpl][tpl]cite news | title=City flood wall highlight, says retiring mayor | date=24 September 1998 | work=The Press | first=Peter | last=Christian | page=7[/tpl] He was appointed an Officer of the Order of the British Empire for services to local government and the community in the 1990 New Year Honours,[tpl]London Gazette |issue=51982 |date=30 December 1989 |startpage=30 |supp=yes |accessdate=20 November 2013[/tpl] and was also awarded the New Zealand 1990 Commemoration Medal.[tpl]cite book |last1=Taylor |first1=Alister | last2=Coddington |first2=Deborah |title=Honoured by the Queen — New Zealand: recipients of honours 1953–1993 |year=1994 |publisher=New Zealand Who's Who Aotearoa |location=Auckland |isbn= 0908578342 |page=115[/tpl]
Dallas stood as an independent candidate for the Westland electorate in 1969; and in 1972 and 1975 for the replacement West Coast electorate. He finished second behind the incumbent Paddy Blanchfield in all three elections; standing as an independent candidate in 1972, and as the National candidate in 1975. In 1969 he trailed Blanchfield by only 1879 votes.[tpl]cite book |last1=Templeton |first1=Ian | last2=Eunson | first2=Keith |title=In the balance: election '72 |year=1972 |publisher=John McIndoe |location=Dunedin |isbn= |page=21[/tpl] In 1972 the majority for Blanchfield was 4242, and in 1975 it was 2401.[tpl]cite book |ref = harv |last = Norton |first = Clifford |authorlink =  |title = New Zealand Parliamentary Election Results 1946-1987: Occasional Publications No 1, Department of Political Science |year = 1988 |publisher = Victoria University of Wellington |location = Wellington |isbn = 0-475-11200-8 [/tpl]
