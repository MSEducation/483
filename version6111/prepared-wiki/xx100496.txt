[[Husqvarna]]

Husqvarna ([tpl]IPA-sv|ˈhʉːskvɑːrna|pron[/tpl]) was originally a military arsenal founded in 1689 to produce muskets for the Swedish Army. The company has since grown and its production has changed from weapons to sewing machines, kitchen equipment, bicycles, motorcycles, lawn mowers, chainsaws and construction products.
