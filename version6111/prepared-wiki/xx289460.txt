[[Aghuzlu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Aghuzlu ([tpl]lang-fa|اغوزلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Āghūzlū and Aghooz Loo; also known as Āqowzlū and Auqūzlu)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3051888|Aghuzlu[/tpl] is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 627, in 139 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
