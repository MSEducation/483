Vítor Manuel Andrade Gomes da Costa (born 15 June 1977 in Lisbon), aka Toy, is a Cape Verdean footballer who plays for Doxa Katokopias F.C. in Cyprus, as a forward.

==Club career==

Toy spent the bulk of his professional career in his adopted country, Portugal, first starting in amateur football. In 1999 he signed with first division giants S.L. Benfica, but played mainly with its reserves during his two-year spell.
Toy represented mainly S.C. Olhanense, first arriving at the Algarve club in 2004 then returning in the 2007 summer after a spell in Saudi Arabia. In the 2008–09 season, in the second level, he contributed with six goals as it returned to the top flight after a 34-year absence.
In the following three first division campaigns, Toy was relatively used by Olhanense, mainly as a substitute.

==International career==

Toy opted to represent Cape Verde internationally, receiving his first callup in May 2008 at nearly 31 years of age.Foreign contingent boost Cape Verde; FIFA.com, 21 May 2008
