[[High Steward of Westminster Abbey]]

CATEGORIES: Westminster Abbey

The High Steward of Westminster Abbey is an honorary role at Westminster Abbey, London.  He is appointed by the Dean and Chapter, and holds the office for life.  Past holders have included Robert Cecil (16th century http://journals.cambridge.org/action/displayAbstract?fromPage=online&aid=93220) and Douglas Hurd (fl. 2004 http://www.westminster-abbey.org/press/archives/040209_recommendations.htm), and the present holder is Lord Luce.
The official costume is an orange cape and white ruff.http://www.guardian.co.uk/queenmother/article/0,,681682,00.html
The deputy High Steward is an ex officio role of the Lord Mayor of Westminster.
