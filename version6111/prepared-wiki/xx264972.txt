[[Marc Bélanger (ice hockey)]]

CATEGORIES: 1983 births, Arizona Sundogs players, Canadian ice hockey centres, Living people, St. Norbert College alumni, Wichita Thunder players

Marc Bélanger (born October 19, 1983, Nanaimo[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oursportscentral.com/services/releases/?id=3949593|title=Sundogs Acquire Forward Marc Belanger, Deal Rookie Jason Reese to Wichita|work=oursportscentral.com|date=January 9, 2010[/tpl]) is a Canadian ice hockey player. He played for two seasons in the British Columbia Hockey League as a member of the Penticton Panthers from 2002-2004. He then played for four years for St. Norbert College in the NCAA from 2004-2008. In the 2008-2009 season he played 43 games for the Texas Brahmas in the Central Hockey League. He then spent the next two seasons as a member of the Wichita Thunder where he played in 50 games, and in the 2009-2010 season he played 30 games for the Arizona Sundogs.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.hockeydb.com/ihdb/stats/pdisplay.php?pid=76320|title=Marc Bélanger|work=www.hockeydb.com[/tpl]
