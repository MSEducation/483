[[Glensboro, Kentucky]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Anderson County, Kentucky, Unincorporated communities in Kentucky, Frankfort, Kentucky micropolitan area

Glensboro is an unincorporated community in Anderson County, Kentucky, United States.  It lies along Route 44 west of the city of Lawrenceburg, the county seat of Anderson County.Rand McNally.  The Road Atlas '06. Chicago: Rand McNally, 2006, p. 43.  Its elevation is 610 feet (186 m).[tpl]gnis|492908[/tpl], Geographic Names Information System, 1979-09-20.  Accessed 2008-01-03.
The community is part of the Frankfort Micropolitan Statistical Area.
