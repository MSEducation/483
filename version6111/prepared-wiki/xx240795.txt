[[Tai'an County]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Liaoning

Tai'an ([tpl]zh|c=台安 |p=Tái'ān[/tpl]) is a county of central Liaoning province, People's Republic of China. It is located in the northern administrative area of Anshan City, and has a population of 370,000[tpl]when|date=July 2012[/tpl] residing in an area of [tpl]convert|1393|km2|abbr=on[/tpl].
