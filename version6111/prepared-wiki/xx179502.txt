[[Gmina Korczew]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Siedlce County

Gmina Korczew is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Siedlce County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Korczew, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|32|km|mi|0[/tpl] north-east of Siedlce and [tpl]convert|111|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] east of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|105.14|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 3,022.

==Villages==

Gmina Korczew contains the villages and settlements of Bartków, Bużyska, Czaple Górne, Drażniew, Góry, Józefin, Juhana, Knychówek, Korczew, Laskowice, Mogielnica, Mokrany-Gajówka, Nowy Bartków, Ruda, Sarnowiec, Starczewice, Stary Bartków, Szczeglacin, Tokary, Tokary-Gajówka, Zacisze and Zaleś.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Korczew is bordered by the gminas of Drohiczyn, Paprotnia, Platerów, Przesmyki and Repki.
