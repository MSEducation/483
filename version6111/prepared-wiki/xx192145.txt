CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1910, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Cuiabá, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of São Luíz de Cáceres ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Sancti Aloisii de Caceres[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Cáceres in the Ecclesiastical province of Cuiabá in Brazil.
