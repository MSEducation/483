[[Oslonett]]

CATEGORIES: Internet service providers of Norway, Companies based in Oslo, 1991 establishments in Norway, Companies established in 1991, 1995 disestablishments in Norway, Companies disestablished in 1995

Oslonett was an Internet service provider based in Oslo, Norway, and the first ISP to serve Norway. It was established 12 of December 1991, co-founded by amongst other Gisle Hannemyr. The company was bought by Schibsted in 1995.
