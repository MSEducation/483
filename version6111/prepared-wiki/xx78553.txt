[[Tsuno, Miyazaki]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Miyazaki Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Tsuno|都農町|Tsuno-chō[/tpl] is a town located in Koyu District, Miyazaki Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the town has an estimated population of 12,201 and the density of 119.23 persons per km². The total area is 102.33 km².
