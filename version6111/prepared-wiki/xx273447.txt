[[Luz María Aguilar]]

CATEGORIES: 1936 births, Living people, 20th-century Mexican actresses, 21st-century Mexican actresses

Luz María Aguilar Torres (Ojinaga, Chihuahua, March 26, 1936) is a Mexican actress.
