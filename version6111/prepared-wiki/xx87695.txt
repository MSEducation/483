[[Batman railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1889

Batman railway station is located on the Upfield line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the northern Melbourne suburb of Coburg North opening on 8 October 1889 as Bell Park. It closed on 13 July 1903, reopening on 1 October 1914 as Batman, named after the founder of Melbourne, John Batman.Batman Vicsig
