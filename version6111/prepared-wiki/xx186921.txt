[[31650 Frýdek-Místek]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Astronomical objects discovered in 1999, Frýdek-Místek

31650 Frýdek-Místek is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1657.8527234 days (4.54 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=31650 |  title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-04-28 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on April 18, 1999.  The discoverer named this asteroid for the cities of Frydek (Silesia) and Mistek (Moravia), located on opposite sides of the Ostrovice River.  The discoverer spent his childhood in these twin cities.
