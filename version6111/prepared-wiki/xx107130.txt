[[My Funny Valentine]]

CATEGORIES: 1937 songs, 1930s jazz standards, Songs with music by Richard Rodgers, Songs with lyrics by Lorenz Hart, Ella Fitzgerald songs, Frank Sinatra songs, Johnny Mathis songs, Ronnie Milsap songs, Eartha Kitt songs, Andy Williams songs, Songs from Babes in Arms, Songs from Pal Joey (film), Jazz compositions in C minor, American songs

"My Funny Valentine" is a show tune from the 1937 Richard Rodgers and Lorenz Hart musical Babes in Arms in which it was introduced by former child star Mitzi Green. Recorded by Chet Baker, Frank Sinatra, Andy Williams, Miles Davis and Chaka Khan, the song is a popular jazz standard, appearing on over 1300 albums performed by over 600 artists.
