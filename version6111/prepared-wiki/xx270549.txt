[[Join the Bar]]

CATEGORIES: 2011 novels, Novels set in India, Bangalore culture

Join the Bar is a 2011 novel written by Brajesh Rajak, a final year student of National Law School of India University, Bangalore.http://www.nls.ac.in/resources/studentsachivement/brajesh.htm It is a fictional account of the lives of 3 law students in a premier law institute in India.http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2011-01-14/bangalore/28366214_1_national-law-school-chetan-bhagat-india-university This novel is considered to be the first novel based on legal education system in India.http://www.dnaindia.com/bangalore/report_bar-none-tales-from-a-law-school_1384416 Brajesh Rajak has also written a law book titled "XXX Must not be Tolerated."http://www.bangaloremirror.com/article/10/20100506201005060521349704e5ef39b/Everything-u-don%E2%80%99t-know-about-XXX.html

==Reception==

The novel received mixed reviews from readers. Lawyer's update described the novel as early blossom of Brajesh Rajak's talent.http://www.lawyersupdate.co.in/LU/24/602.asp  Rainmaker's Mylaw.com criticised it for too many twists in the plot but also praised it for the hard work that the author has put in to give voice to issues of concern at a certain law school.http://www.mylaw.net/Article/Join_the_Bar_and_grab_a_drink/ Some readers criticized it for being exceedingly honest while dealing with the problems of a certain law school. An agreement of love published in the book is appreciated and quoted by many bloggers including legallyindia.com.http://www.lawyersclubindia.com/forum/Agreement-of-love-44885.asp
