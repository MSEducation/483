[[Carmenta laurelae]]

CATEGORIES: Sesiidae

Carmenta laurelae is a moth of the Sesiidae family.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.nic.funet.fi/pub/sci/bio/life/insecta/lepidoptera/ditrysia/sesioidea/sesiidae/sesiinae/carmenta/index.html |title=Carmenta|publisher=Nic.funet.fi |date= |accessdate=2011-12-16[/tpl] It was described by Brown, Eichlin and Snow in 1985, and is known from Florida, U.S.A.
The length of the forewings is 9–10 mm.[tpl]cite web|url=http://peabody.research.yale.edu//jls/pdfs/1980s/1985/1985-39(4)262-%20Brown.pdf |title=A New Species Of Clearwing Moth, Carmenta laurelae |format=PDF |date= |accessdate=2011-12-16[/tpl]
