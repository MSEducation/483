[[Yagya Bahadur Basnyat]]

CATEGORIES: Government ministers of Nepal, Possibly living people, Year of birth missing

Yagya Bahadur Basnyat was a Nepalese politician. He was appointed Minister of Health & Local Self Development in the government formed by Sir Mohan Shamsher Jang Bahadur Rana after King Tribhuvan's return to the throne on February 18, 1951. In a cabinet reshuffle on June 10, 1951, he was named Minister of Health. This cabinet lasted until November 1951.http://www.healthnet.org.np/users/dixit/ANNEX-III.PDF
