[[SAGEM Sigma 30]]

CATEGORIES: Avionics, Missile guidance, Navigational equipment

The Sigma 30 is an inertial navigation system produced by SAGEM for use with artillery applications including howitzers, multiple rocket launchers, mortars and light guns.  It is currently produced for more than 25 international programs,[tpl]Citation needed|date=May 2008[/tpl] including France (CAESAR,[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.army-technology.com/projects/caesar/ | title = Caesar 155mm Self-Propelled Artillery System | accessdate = 2008-05-26 | publisher = Army Technology [/tpl] 2R2M, MLRS), Sweden (FH77 BD, Archer), India (Pinaka MBRL),[tpl]cite web | url = http://frontierindia.net/india-orders-sigma-95-and-sigma-30-systems-from-sagem-defense-securite | title = India orders SIGMA 95 and SIGMA 30 systems from Sagem Défense Sécurité | accessdate = 2008-05-23 | date = 2008-02-22 | publisher = Frontier India Defence and Strategic News Service [/tpl] Polish PT-91M tank (build for Malaysia)  and the United States (topographic survey).[tpl]cite web | url =http://www.zupt.com/Overview.pdf | title = Integrated Inertial Positioning Systems, some facts, some editorial and some biased opinions | accessdate = 2008-05-26 | publisher = Zupt LLC [/tpl]
