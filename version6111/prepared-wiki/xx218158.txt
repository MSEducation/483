[[Rahsaan Bahati]]

CATEGORIES: American cyclists, People from Lynwood, California, Living people, 1982 births

Rahsaan Bahati (born 13 February 1982 in Lynwood, California) is an American racing cyclist who currently rides for his own cycling team, Bahati Foundation Elite Team.
