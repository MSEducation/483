[[Ihosy District]]

CATEGORIES: Ihorombe, Districts of Madagascar

{{Blacklisted-links|1=

Ihosy is a district in south-eastern Madagascar. It's a part of Ihorombe Region and borders the districts of Ikalamavony in north, Ambalavao in northeast, Ivohibe in east, Iakora in southeast, Betroka in south, Benenitra in southwest, Sakaraha and Ankazoabo Sud in west and Beroraha in northwest. The area is [tpl]convert|17358|km2|sqmi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] and the population was estimated to be 106,591 in 2001.[ref]
</ref>
The district is further divided into 17 communes.
