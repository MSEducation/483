==Military background==

General de Sousa is, along with fellow "top generals" Francisco Higino Carneiro, Hélder Vieira Dias, Roberto Leal Monteiro, and Kundi Paihama, one of the military leaders holding top ministerial posts for the Popular Movement for the Liberation of Angola, the political party that has ruled Angola since it gained its independence from Portugal in 1975.http://www.africafiles.org/article.asp?ID=16918 "Private security companies and a parallel State in Angola" (africafiles.org)
