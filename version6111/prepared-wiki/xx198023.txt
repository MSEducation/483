[[Grzegorz Piekarski]]

CATEGORIES: 1986 births, Living people, Polish lugers, People from Gorzów Wielkopolski, Sportspeople from Lubusz Voivodeship

Grzegorz Piekarski (born December 23, 1986 in Gorzów Wielkopolski) is a Polish luger who has competed since 2001. His best Luge World Cup finish was 18th in men's doubles in 2007-08.
Piekarski also finished 19th in the men's doubles event at the 2008 FIL World Luge Championships in Oberhof.
