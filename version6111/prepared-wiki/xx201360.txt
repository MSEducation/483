[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Barra]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1913, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Feira de Santana, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Barra ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Barrensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Barra in the Ecclesiastical province of Feira de Santana in Brazil.
