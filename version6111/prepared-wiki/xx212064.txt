[[DYFR]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Metro Cebu

98.7 DYFR-FM, is an FM radio station owned and operated by Far East Broadcasting Company in the Philippines. It is a sister station to DZFE 98.7 in Manila. The station's studio and transmitter are located at the Immanuel Bible College Compound Banawa Hills, Cebu City, the station operates daily from 5:00 AM to 9:30 PM.
