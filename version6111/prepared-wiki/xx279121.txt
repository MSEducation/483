[[Idahlu, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Idahlu ([tpl]lang-fa|ايده لو[/tpl], also Romanized as Idahlū and Idehloo; also known as Aikdulu, Aindulu, Īdalū, and Īdelū)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3067189|Idahlu[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
