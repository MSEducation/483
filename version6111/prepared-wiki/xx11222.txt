[[Svenska Dagbladet]]

CATEGORIES: Media in Stockholm, Newspapers published in Sweden, Publications established in 1884, 1884 establishments in Sweden, Swedish-language newspapers

Svenska Dagbladet (common abbreviation SvD; the title translates as "the Swedish daily paper") is a daily newspaper in Sweden. 

==Overview==

200px|thumb|left|Front page of the first issue of Svenska Dagbladet (18 December 1884)The first issue appeared on 18 December 1884. Svenska Dagbladet is published in Stockholm and provides coverage of national and international news as well as local coverage of the Greater Stockholm region. Its subscribers are concentrated in the capital, but it is distributed in most of Sweden.

The stated position of the editorial page is "independently moderate" (oberoende moderat), which means it is independent but adheres to the liberal conservatism of the Moderate Party.
Since 1925, Svenska Dagbladet has awarded an individual sportsperson or a team the Svenska Dagbladet Gold Medal at the end of each year.
