[[2008–09 Djurgårdens IF Hockey season]]

CATEGORIES: Djurgårdens IF Hockey seasons, 2008–09 in Swedish ice hockey

The 2008–09 Djurgårdens IF Hockey season will be the club's 33rd season in the Swedish elite league Elitserien. The signing of David Schneider during late July 2008 is the first American player in the club's history to play for the club.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.difhockey.se/nyhet.php?news=4753 |title=David Schneider klar för Djurgården |publisher=Djurgården Hockey |work=difhockey.se |last=Dammbro |first=Jerker |language=Swedish |date=2008-07-28 |accessdate=2008-08-11[/tpl] The regular season started on home ice on September 18, 2008 against Luleå HF and will be concluded on February 28, 2009 away against Frölunda HC.[tpl]cite web |url=http://difhockey.se/season.php?sasong=2009 |title=Säsongen 2008/2009 |publisher=Djurgården Hockey |work=difhockey.se |language=Swedish |accessdate=2009-01-23| archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20090215093811/http://difhockey.se/season.php?sasong=2009| archivedate= 15 February 2009 | deadurl= no[/tpl]

==Offseason==

March 20: Team captain Jimmie Ölvestad re-signed a one year contract with Djurgården.[tpl]cite web |url=http://difhockey.se/nyhet.php?news=4648 |title=Glad påsk: Ölvestad har förlängt |publisher=Djurgården Hockey |work=difhockey.se |last=Peterson |first=Jimmy |language=Swedish |date=2008-03-20 |accessdate=2009-01-24[/tpl]
April 15: Former assistant coaches Mikael Johansson and Tomas Montén signed a two-year contract as the new coaches of the team.[tpl]cite web |url=http://difhockey.se/nyhet.php?news=4693 |title=Johansson och Montén leder DIF |publisher=Djurgården Hockey |work=difhockey.se |last=Peterson |first=Jimmy |language=Swedish |date=2008-04-15 |accessdate=2009-01-23[/tpl]
