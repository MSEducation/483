[[Josh Duinker]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 births, Living people, Australian basketball players, Dutch Basketball League players, Dutch basketball players, Richmond Spiders men's basketball players, Sportspeople from Sydney, Zorg en Zekerheid Leiden players

Joshua Duinker (born April 18, 1989) is an Australian-Dutch professional basketball player who currently plays for Zorg en Zekerheid Leiden in the Dutch Basketball League. Duinker is also a part of the Dutch national basketball team.

==Career==

Duinker played college basketball at the University of Richmond. With the Richmond Spiders, Duinker won the Atlantic 10 conference and reached the Last 16 in the NCAA tournament.[tpl]cite news | url = http://basketballplus.nl/joshua-duinker-onbekend-gezicht-in-oranje/ | title = Joshua Duinker unknown face in Orange | language = [tpl]du icon[/tpl] | publisher = Basketball+ [/tpl]
After his year at Richmond, Duinker signed with the Spanish club Cáceres Ciudad del Baloncesto in the LEB Gold league. Duinker noted 7.3 points and 5.1 rebounds per game for Cáceres. In the summer of 2013, Duinker signed with Zorg en Zekerheid Leiden in the Netherlands.[tpl]Cite news | url = http://www.omroepwest.nl/sport/30-07-2013/zz-leiden-trekt-210-meter-lange-josh-duinker-aan | title = ZZ Leiden signs 2.11 m tall Josh Duinker | publisher = Omroep West | date = 2013-07-30 | accessdate= 2014-03-16 | language = [tpl]du icon[/tpl] [/tpl]
