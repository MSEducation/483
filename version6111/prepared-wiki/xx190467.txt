[[Community Harvest Charter School]]

CATEGORIES: Charter schools in California, Schools in Los Angeles, California, Public high schools in California, Public middle schools in California

Community Harvest Charter School is a school located in Los Angeles, California. It was founded in 2002 by Charletta Johnson, Jackie Gichohi, Viviana Trujillo, and Kweli Umoja. Community Harvest Charter School is a joint middle and high school.
The school has a 100-percent graduation rate and 87 percent college and or vocational enrollment. Typical enrollment is in the range of 300-350 students.
