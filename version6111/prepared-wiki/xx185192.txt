[[Pine Ridge, Kentucky]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Wolfe County, Kentucky, Unincorporated communities in Kentucky

Pine Ridge is an unincorporated community in Wolfe County, Kentucky, United States.  It lies along Route 15 northwest of the city of Campton, the county seat of Wolfe County.Rand McNally.  The Road Atlas '06. Chicago: Rand McNally, 2006, p. 43.  Its elevation is 1,266 feet (386 m).[tpl]gnis|514598[/tpl], Geographic Names Information System, 1979-09-20.  Accessed 2008-01-03.  The community has a ZIP code of 41360.Zip Code Lookup
