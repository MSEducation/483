Terry Evans (born 12 April 1965) is an English former professional footballer who played as a central defender, making over 350 career appearances.

==Career==

Born in Hammersmith, Evans played for Hillingdon Borough, Brentford, Wycombe Wanderers and Kingstonian.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.neilbrown.newcastlefans.com/player2/terryevans.html|title=Profile|publisher=Post War English & Scottish Football League A - Z Player's Database[/tpl]
