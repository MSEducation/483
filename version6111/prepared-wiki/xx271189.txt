[[Mohammed Mahroufi]]

Mohammed Mahroufi (born 1947) is a Moroccan football midfielder who played for Morocco in the 1970 FIFA World Cup.1970 FIFA World Cup Mexico He also played for Difaa El Jadida. Also, Mahroufi was a business student.
