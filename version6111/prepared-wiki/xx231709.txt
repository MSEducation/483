Orlando M Baker (born 15 September 1979) is a Jamaican-born American cricketer.Orlando Baker at Cricinfo A right-handed batsman and right-arm medium pace bowler,Orlando Baker at CricketArchive he made his debut for the United States national cricket team in 2008, having previously played first-class cricket for his native Jamaica.Teams played for by Orlando Baker at CricketArchive

==Biography==

Born in St Catherine in 1979, Orlando Baker first represented Jamaica at Under-19 level, playing in the Nortel Youth Tournament in 1996 and 1997, also playing for them in matches against a Nortel All Star XI and Pakistan Under-19s in 1996.Other matches played by Orlando Baker at CricketArchive He played his only first-class match for Jamaica in 2001, a Busta Cup match against England A.First-class matches played by Orlando Baker at CricketArchive He scored a total of five runs in the match.
He first played for the USA in Division Five of the World Cricket League in Jersey in 2008, playing eight matches in the tournament and finishing as the USA's second highest run scorer in the tournament.USA stats at 2008 WCL Division Five official site
In 2012 Baker was selected as to be a part of the United States national cricket team at the 2012 ICC World Twenty20 Qualifier in the UAE in March 2012.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.espncricinfo.com/ci/content/squad/552747.html|title=United States of America Squad, ICC World Twenty20 Qualifier, 2011/12|publisher=ESPNcricinfo |date=2012-03-16 |accessdate=2012-08-06[/tpl] Later in the same year he was selected for the 2012 ICC World Cricket League Division Four which takes place from 3 to 10 September 2012 in Malaysia.
