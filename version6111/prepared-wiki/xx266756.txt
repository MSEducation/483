[[Dun, Norway]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Nord-Trøndelag, Fosnes

Dun is the administrative centre of the municipality of Fosnes in Nord-Trøndelag county, Norway.  It is located in the central part of the island of Jøa, and it is the location of Dun Church.
