[[Blue Seven]]

CATEGORIES: 1965 albums, Albums produced by Esmond Edwards, Albums recorded at Van Gelder Studio, Prestige Records albums, Shirley Scott albums

Blue Seven is a studio album by organist Shirley Scott recorded in 1961 for Prestige and issued in late 1965 as PRLP 7376. It features performances by Oliver Nelson and Joe Newman, among the others.

==Track listing==

The CD reissue includes the bonus track "How Sweet".
