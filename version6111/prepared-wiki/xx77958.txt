[[Kavajë District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Albania

The District of Kavajë ([tpl]Lang-sq|Rrethi i Kavajës[/tpl]) is one of the 36 Districts of Albania. An administrative division of Tirana County, it is centrally located in the Western Lowlands region of the country. Its capital is Kavajë. The district has a total surface area of 414 km2 and it consists of 2 municipalities, 8 communes and 65 localities. Its population as of 2011[tpl]cite web|url=http://census.al/Resources/Data/Census2011/Instat_print%20.pdf|title=Censusi i Popullsisë[/tpl] was estimated at 88,000 inhabitants.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bashkiakavaje.gov.al|title=Bashkia Kavajë[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.aac-al.org/qarku.php?idq=11&lang=1&idr=23|title=Shoqata e Komunave të Shqipërisë[/tpl]
