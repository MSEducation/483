[[Tyrone Pau]]

CATEGORIES: Cook Islands national rugby league team players, Cook Island rugby league players, Place of birth missing (living people), Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, Limoux Grizzlies players, Hibiscus Coast Raiders players, Auckland rugby league team players, Leigh Centurions players

Tyrone Pau is a Cook Islander professional rugby league footballer playing at representative level for Cook Islands, as a [tpl]rlp|CE[/tpl], or [tpl]rlp|SR[/tpl].

==Playing career==

Pau played for the Hibiscus Coast Raiders in 2001 and 2002, being selected for the 2001 Auckland side and 2002 and 2003 New Zealand Residents sides.Hibiscus Coast Raiders Rugby League and Sports Club Inc Auckland Rugby League
Pau then spent five years in France, playing for the Limoux Grizzlies. In 2006 Pau signed with the Leigh Centurions.Smith treating Wigan showdown 'like a cup final' The Independent, 7 July 2006

==Representative career==

Tyrone Pau won caps for Cook Islands in the 2000 Rugby League World Cup.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.rugbyleagueproject.org/players/Tyrone-Pau/summary.html|title=Statistics at rugbyleagueproject.org|publisher=rugbyleagueproject.org|date=31 December 2011|accessdate=1 January 2012[/tpl]
