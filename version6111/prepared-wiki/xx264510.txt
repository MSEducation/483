[[Belfatto & Pavarini]]

CATEGORIES: Companies based in Manhattan, Defunct architecture firms based in New York City, Architects of Roman Catholic churches, American ecclesiastical architects

Belfatto & Pavarini, was an American architect firm based in New York City practicing in the mid twentieth century. The firm was notable for its Modernist style of design, principally of St. Brendan's Church (Bronx, New York) (1966) and the Church of the Epiphany in New York City (1965).
