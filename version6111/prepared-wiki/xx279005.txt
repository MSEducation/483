[[Sari Kand-e Kabali]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Sari Kand-e Kabali ([tpl]lang-fa|ساري كندكابلي[/tpl], also Romanized as Sārī Kand-e Kābalī; also known as Sārī Kand, Sārī Kand-e Pā’īn, and Sārī Kand-e Soflá)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3082829|Sari Kand-e Kabali[/tpl] is a village in Chaypareh-ye Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 101, in 23 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
