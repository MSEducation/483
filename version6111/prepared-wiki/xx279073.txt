[[Habash, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Habash ([tpl]lang-fa|حبش[/tpl], also Romanized as Ḩabash)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3065298|Habash[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 200, in 51 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
