[[Ari Greenberg]]

CATEGORIES: 1981 births, American bridge players, Living people, People from Malibu, California

Ari David Greenberg (born April 1, 1981 in Malibu, California) is an American world junior champion in contract bridge. A Stanford computer science graduate, Greenberg is employed at Facebook. Previously, he has also been employed at Bridge Base and Google. He currently resides in Menlo Park, California.
