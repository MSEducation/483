Dan Doyle (born 1864, died 1918) was a Scottish footballer who played for among others, East Stirlingshire, Hibernian, Sunderland, Grimsby Town, Bolton Wanderers, Everton, Celtic and the Scotland national team. 
He won a total of 8 caps for Scotland between 1892 and 1898.
