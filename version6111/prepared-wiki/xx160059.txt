[[Belgian general election, 1958]]

CATEGORIES: 1958 elections in Europe, Elections in Belgium, 1958 in Belgium

General elections were held in Belgium on 1 June 1958.Nohlen, D & Stöver, P (2010) Elections in Europe: A data handbook, p289 ISBN 978-3-8329-5609-7 The result was a victory for the Christian Social Party, which won 104 of the 212 seats in the Chamber of Representatives and 53 of the 106 seats in the Senate.Nohlen & Stöver, pp309-311 Voter turnout was 93.6% in the Chamber election and 93.7% in the Senate election.Nohlen & Stöver, p291
