[[Hanford Community Medical Center]]

CATEGORIES: Hospital buildings completed in 1965, Hospitals in California, Hospitals affiliated with the Seventh-day Adventist Church, Adventist Health, Buildings and structures in Kings County, California

Hanford Community Medical Center is located in the city of Hanford, California. Hanford Community Medical Center is a part of the Seventh-day Adventist hospital system. Hanford Community Medical Center is part of a division of Adventist Health known as the "Adventist Health/Central Valley Network," consisting of Hanford Community Medical Center, Central Valley General Hospital, Selma Community Hospital, and seventeen Adventist Health/Community Care clinics throughout a [tpl]convert|2500|sqmi|km2|adj=on[/tpl] region in the Central Valley.
