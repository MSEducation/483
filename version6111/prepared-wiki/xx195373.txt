[[KGNB]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in San Antonio, Texas, Sports radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 1963, 1963 establishments in Texas

KGNB (1420 AM) is a radio station  broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to New Braunfels, Texas, USA, the station serves the San Antonio area.  The station is currently owned by New Braunfels Communications, Inc. and features programing from CNN Radio, Motor Racing Network and Sporting News Radio.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KGNB |title=KGNB Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division[/tpl]
