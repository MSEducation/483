[[Double je]]

CATEGORIES: 2007 singles, Christophe Willem songs, Ultratop 50 Singles (Wallonia) number-one singles, Number-one singles in France, Singles certified platinum by the Syndicat National de l'Édition Phonographique, Songs written by Zazie

"Double je" is a 2007 song recorded by the French singer Christophe Willem with lyrics by Zazie. It was his third single and the second one from his debut album, Inventaire. Released on 25 May 2007, the single was a hit in France and Belgium, topping the singles charts for about two months and thus becoming the singer's best-selling single.

==Background, lyrics, music and video==

"Double je" was composed by the French artist Zazie. The song, with its "very funky melody and background vocals", was considered as being "much more effective" than the previous single, "Élu produit de l'année", released some weeks earlier digitally in France and as a single in Belgium (Wallonia).[tpl]cite web |title=Déjà un nouveau single pour Christophe Willem ? |first=Thierry |last=Cadet |date=7 April 2007 |url=http://www.chartsinfrance.net/actualite/news-64523.html |publisher=Chartsinfrance |language=French |accessdate=28 April 2008[/tpl]
Willem chose the "self-mockery" to illustrate the song, which has also electronic sonorities. Produced by the director Sylvain Fusée who also wrote the screenplay with Willem,[tpl]cite web |title=Christophe Willem surfe sur la vague du succès avec "Double je" & "Inventaire" |date=6 June 2007 |url=http://www.musiqueradio.com/article_news3960_christophe-willem-surfe-sur-la-vague-du-succes-avec-double-je--et--inventaire.php |publisher=musiqueradio |language=French |accessdate=28 April 2008[/tpl] the music video shows the singer participating in a group therapy, to express his dual personality.[tpl]cite web |title=Le Double Je de Christophe Willem |url=http://musique.aol.fr/christophe-willem-le-double-je-de-christophe-willem/PTFR_52507/p-p_p/article_id/article.html |publisher=musique.aol |language=French |accessdate=28 April 2008[/tpl] It was nominated for the 2008 Victoires de la Musique in the category 'best videoclip of the year'.[tpl]cite web |title=Victoire de la musique 2008: tous les nommés! |first=Julien |last=Bellver |date=31 January 2008 |url=http://www.ozap.com/actu/victoire-musique-2008-nommes/131328 |publisher=Ozap |language=French |accessdate=28 April 2008[/tpl] It did not win, but the song was awarded 'original song of the year'.[tpl]cite web |title=Les Victoires de la Musique 2008: le palmarès |first=Julien |last=Lalande |date=9 March 2008 |url=http://www.ozap.com/actu/victoires-musique-2008-palmares/132996 |publisher=Ozap |language=French |accessdate=28 April 2008[/tpl]

==Chart performances==

In France, the single entered at number-one on the French Singles Chart, on 2 June 2007, selling 38,002 units this week.[tpl]cite web |title=Disques: Dion, Willem et Lemarchal en tête des ventes |first=Charles |last=Decant |date=5 June 2007 |url=http://www.ozap.com/actu/disques-dion-willem-lemarchal-tete-ventes/117322 |publisher=Ozap |language=French |accessdate=28 April 2008[/tpl] It remained for seven non consecutive weeks at the top, then dropped slowly almost every week. The single stayed for 18 weeks in the top ten, 24 weeks in the top 50 and 35 weeks in the top 100.[tpl]cite web |title="Double je", French Singles Chart |url=http://lescharts.com/showitem.asp?key=306761&cat=s |publisher=Lescharts |language=French |accessdate=25 April 2008[/tpl] Certified Platinum disc by the SNEP,[tpl]cite web |title=Certifications Singles Platine - année 2007 |url=http://www.disqueenfrance.com/fr/monopage.xml?id=259165&year=2007&type=9 |publisher=Disqueenfrance |language=French |accessdate=28 April 2008[/tpl] the song was the best-selling single of the year,[tpl]cite web |title=2007 French Singles Chart |url=http://www.disqueenfrance.com/fr/page-259376.xml?year=2007 |publisher=Disqueenfrance |language=French |accessdate=28 April 2008[/tpl] with 309,481 sales.[tpl]cite web |title=Disques 2007: Mika a fait bouger la France |first=Charles |last=Decant |date=12 March 2008 |url=http://www.ozap.com/actu/disques-2007-mika-bouger-france/133196 |publisher=Ozap |language=French |accessdate=28 April 2008[/tpl]  
The single was also a success on the digital chart : entered at number 17 on 14 April 2007, it jumped to number one the next week,[tpl]cite web |title=French Digital Chart, from April 15 to April 21, 2007 |url=http://www.ifop.com/europe/sondages/topteletitres/tlt0716.stm |publisher=Ifop |language=French |accessdate=25 April 2008[/tpl] [tpl]Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot[/tpl] and remained on the chart (top 40) for one year. It was ranked at number two on the End of the Year Chart[tpl]cite web |title=2007 French Digital Chart |url=http://www.disqueenfrance.com/fr/pag-259376-Classements-Annuels.html?year=2007&type=7 |publisher=Disqueenfrance |language=French |accessdate=28 April 2008[/tpl] with about 62,690 digitally sales (2007 only).[tpl]cite web |title=2007 digital downloads in France |url=http://www.infodisc.fr/DownLoad_T2007.php |publisher=Infodisc |language=French |accessdate=28 April 2008[/tpl]
In Belgium (Wallonia), the single charted for 38 weeks on the Ultratop 40, from 12 May 2007. It debuted at number 27 and reached the top ten in the fifth week. It was number one four weeks later and remained there for three consecutive weeks, then dropped to number two for six weeks. It totaled 19 weeks in the top ten,[tpl]cite web |title="Double je", Belgian (Wallonia) Singles Chart |url=http://www.ultratop.be/fr/showitem.asp?key=306761&cat=s |publisher=Ultratop |language=French |accessdate=25 April 2008[/tpl] achieved Gold status[tpl]cite web |title=2007 certifications in Belgium |url=http://www.ultratop.be/fr/certifications.asp?year=2007 |publisher=Ultratop |language=French |accessdate=28 April 2008[/tpl] and became the fourth best-selling single of the year.[tpl]cite web |title=2007 Belgian (Wallonia) Singles Chart |url=http://www.ultratop.be/fr/annual.asp?year=2007 |publisher=Ultratop |language=French |accessdate=28 April 2008[/tpl]
In Switzerland, the song achieved a minor success: after a debut at number 69 on 20 May, it peaked at number 21 four weeks later, and stayed in the top 100 for 25 weeks.[tpl]cite web |title="Double je", Swiss Singles Chart |url=http://hitparade.ch/showitem.asp?key=306761&cat=s |publisher=Hitparade |language=German |accessdate=25 April 2008[/tpl]
