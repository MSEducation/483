[[Marie Van Tassell]]

CATEGORIES: People from New York, American silent film actresses, 1871 births, 1946 deaths, 20th-century American actresses

Marie Van Tassell (6 April 1871, Little Falls, New York  - 22 January 1946, Oakland, California) was an American silent film actress. 
She starred in 15 films between 1915 and 1920.
