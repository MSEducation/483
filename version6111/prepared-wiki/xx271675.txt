[[İnsu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Mersin Province, Mediterranean Region, Turkey, Villages in Turkey, Populated places in Yenişehir, Mersin

 
Insu is a small village in Yenişehir district, which is an intracity district within Greater Mersin, Turkey. The village which is at [tpl]coord|36|52|N|34|30|E[/tpl] is [tpl]convert|20|km[/tpl] north of Mersin city center and south of a canyon about [tpl]convert|400|m[/tpl] deep.  The population of the village is 163 [tpl]cite web|url= http://rapor.tuik.gov.tr/reports/rwservlet?adnksdb2&ENVID=adnksdb2Env&report=wa_idari_yapi_10sonrasi.RDF&p_il1=33&p_yil=2011&p_dil=2&desformat=html |title=Turkstat|publisher=Turkish Statistical Institute|accessdate=2012[/tpl] as of 2011.
