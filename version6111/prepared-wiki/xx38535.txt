[[Johan Paulik]]

CATEGORIES: 1975 births, Living people, People from Bratislava, Slovak actors in gay pornographic films, Slovak male pornographic film actors

Johan Paulik (born 14 March 1975) is the stage name of a Slovak former gay pornographic model. In 2000, he was inducted into the GayVN Hall of Fame with Lukas Ridgeston and George Duroy, owner of Bel Ami, the studio Paulik worked for his entire career. In 2002, Paulik became general manager for Bel Ami in Europe.
