[[Cactus Range]]

CATEGORIES: Mountain ranges of Nevada, Landforms of Nye County, Nevada, Mountain ranges of the Great Basin

The Cactus Range is a small mountain range in Nye County, Nevada. The range lies southwest of Cactus Flat and north of Pahute Mesa. Goldfield lies [tpl]convert|23|mi|abbr=on[/tpl] to the west in Esmeralda County. The range lies within the restricted area of the Tonopah Test Range.
Named peaks in the range include:Cactus Flat, Nevada 1:100,000 scale topographic map, 37116-EM-TM-100, USGS, 1988
