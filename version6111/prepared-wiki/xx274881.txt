[[Satadree]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of India

Other name for river Sutlej. Mahabharat describes, once sage vashishta, being determined on suicide flung himself afterwards into the Sutlej; but the pious water of the river divided into a hundred shallow channel and left the disappointed sage on dry land; from which the river was ever afterwards called satadree"the hundred channeled" from Sanskrit words sata, a hundred, and, dree, to flow
