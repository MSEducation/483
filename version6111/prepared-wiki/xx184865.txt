Jackson Henrique Gonçalves Pereira, commonly known as Jackson (born June 3, 1988), is a Brazilian footballer currently playing for Toronto FC of Major League Soccer. He is known for his physical prowess and utility player abilities.  

==Career==

Jackson made his professional debut for São Paulo against Fluminense in a 1-1 draw at the Maracanã in the Campeonato Brasileiro on October 13, 2007. He was then taken off at half-time and replaced by Fernando.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.zerozero.pt/pt/jogo.php?id=245260|title=Fluminense 1-1 São Paulo Campeonato Brasileirão 2007 Jornada 31 |publisher=zerozero.pt|accessdate=January 4, 2008|date=October 13, 2007[/tpl] He also made his Copa Sudamericana debut for São Paulo in 0-1 home defeat to Millonarios on October 10, 2007.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.sportsya.com/futbol/ficha_partido.php/Copa_Sudamericana_2007_Sao_Paulo_FC_0_-_Millonarios_1.html?id=20633|title=Copa Sudamericana debut|publisher=sportsya.com|accessdate=January 4, 2008|date=October 10, 2007[/tpl]
On August 13, 2010, FC Dallas of Major League Soccer signed Jackson on loan from Brazilian first division side São Paulo FC. He made his debut for his new club the following day, coming on in the 82nd minute for Brek Shea against D.C. United at RFK Stadium in Washington, D.C.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcdallas.com/news/2010/08/match-recap-fc-dallas-3-dc-united-1|title=Match Recap: FC Dallas 3, DC United, 1|publisher=fcdallas.com|accessdate=August 16, 2010|date=August 14, 2010[/tpl] He started in the MLS Cup 2010 loss to Colorado Rapids, playing 34 minutes. Jackson was also named to the MLS Team of Week 15 and 16 in the 2011 MLS season for his play against Portland Timbers and Columbus Crew.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.mlssoccer.com/node/154913 |title=Team of the Week: Week 15 |publisher=MLSsoccer.com |date=June 28, 2011 |accessdate=January 5, 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.mlssoccer.com/news/article/2011/07/06/mlssoccercom-team-week-week-16 |title=Team of the Week: Week 16 |publisher=MLSsoccer.com |date=July 6, 2011 |accessdate=January 5, 2013[/tpl] Jackson was loaned for one year by FC Dallas to Cruzeiro on January 18, 2012.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcdallas.com/news/2012/01/fc-dallas-loans-jackson-brazilian-first-division-side-cruzeiro-0 |title=loans Jackson to Brazilian first division side Cruzeiro |publisher=FC Dallas |date=January 18, 2012 |accessdate=January 5, 2013[/tpl] On April 13, 2012, FC Dallas recalled Jackson from Cruzeiro, cutting short the loan deal due to injuries in the FC Dallas midfield.[tpl]cite web|author=EJ Holland |url=http://www.mlssoccer.com/news/article/2012/04/13/injuries-force-dallas-recall-jackson-cruzeiro |title=Injuries force Dallas to recall Jackson from Cruzeiro |publisher=MLSsoccer.com |date=April 13, 2012 |accessdate=January 5, 2013[/tpl]
On December 9, 2013, Jackson was traded to Toronto FC in exchange for a conditional second-round draft pick in the 2015 MLS SuperDraft, and allocation money.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.torontofc.ca/news/2013/12/tfc-acquires-jackson-fc-dallas |title=TFC Acquires Jackson From FC Dallas |publisher=TorontoFC.ca |date=December 9, 2013 |accessdate=December 9, 2013[/tpl]
