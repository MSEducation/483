[[Nagatoro Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Saitama Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Nagatoro Station|長瀞駅|Nagatoro-eki[/tpl] is a railway station on the Chichibu Main Line in Nagatoro, Saitama, Japan, operated by the Chichibu Railway.

==Lines==

Nagatoro Station is served by the Chichibu Main Line from [tpl]ja-stalink|Hanyū[/tpl] to [tpl]ja-stalink|Mitsumineguchi[/tpl], and is located 46.5 km from Hanyū.[tpl]cite book | last = Terada | first = Hirokazu |title = データブック日本の私鉄 |trans_title= Databook: Japan's Private Railways | publisher = Neko Publishing | date = July 2002 | location = Japan | page = 192| isbn = 4-87366-874-3[/tpl]

==Station layout==

The station is staffed and consists of one side platform and one island platform serving three tracks in total. Track 3 is a bidirectional line used by the Paleo Express, freight services and through-running services to and from the Seibu Chichibu Line.[tpl]cite book | last = Kawashima| first = Ryozo |title = 日本の鉄道　中部ライン　全線・全駅・全配線　第１０巻　上越・秩父エリア |trans_title= Railways of Japan - Chubu Line - Lines/Stations/Track plans - Vol 10 Joetsu & Chichibu Area | publisher = Kodansha | date = January 2011 | location = Japan | page = 30/85| isbn = 978-4-06-270070-2[/tpl]

==History==

The station opened on September 14, 1911 as [tpl]Nihongo|Hodosan Station|宝登山[/tpl]. It was renamed Nagatoro from 7 July 1923.

==Passenger statistics==

In fiscal 2000, the station was used by an average of 1,051 passengers daily.
