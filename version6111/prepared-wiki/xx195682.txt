[[Holocausto de la Morte]]

CATEGORIES: Necrophagia albums, 1998 albums

Holocausto de la Morte is the third full-length studio album by the American death metal band Necrophagia. It was released in 1998 on Red Stream Records. This is also the first release with Anton Crowley (a.k.a. Phil Anselmo) on guitars.
