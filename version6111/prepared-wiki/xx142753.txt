[[Journal of Chemical Education]]

CATEGORIES: Chemical education journals, American Chemical Society academic journals, Monthly journals, Publications established in 1924, English-language journals

The Journal of Chemical Education is a monthly peer-reviewed academic journal available in both print and electronic versions. It is published by the Division of Chemical Education of the American Chemical Society and was established in 1924. The journal covers research on chemical education.
According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal has a 2012 impact factor of 0.817.[tpl]cite web |url=http://isiwebofknowledge.com |title=Web of Science |year=2012 |accessdate=[/tpl]
