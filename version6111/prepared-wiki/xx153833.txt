[[Ladonia, North Carolina]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Surry County, North Carolina, Unincorporated communities in North Carolina

Ladonia is an unincorporated community located in the Franklin Township of northwest Surry County, North Carolina.  The community generally lies on the Fisher River and is centered around the intersection of Blevins Store Road and Ladonia Church Road [tpl]Harvard citation|Powell|1968|p=267[/tpl].  Area landmarks include Ladonia Baptist Church and the nearby Raven Knob Scout Reservation.
