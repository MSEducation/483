[[SBACH]]

CATEGORIES: Suicide Squeeze artists, Kill Rock Stars, American indie rock groups, Musical groups from Sacramento, California

[tpl]lowercase[/tpl]

sBACH is an American band from Nevada City, California, led by Spencer Seim of the band Hella and drummer of The Advantage. Although Seim plays all instruments on recorded material, he plays drums live, and has a live band which includes Ben Milner (of The Advantage), Justin Hunt, and Keith Dailey all playing guitar and synthesizer.http://onemicrophone.com/reviews/sBACH-Interview
