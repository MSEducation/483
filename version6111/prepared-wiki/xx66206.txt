[[Nicole Markotic]]

CATEGORIES: Canadian women poets, Writers from Windsor, Ontario, Living people, 20th-century Canadian poets, 21st-century Canadian poets, University of Windsor faculty

Nicole Markotic is a Canadian poet[tpl]cite book|last=Calder|first=edited by Alison|title=History, literature, and the writing of the Canadian Prairies|year=2005|publisher=University of Manitoba Press|location=Winnipeg|isbn=978-0-88755-682-1|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=CANH1WxF-dYC&pg=PA236&dq=%22Nicole+Markotic%22#v=onepage&q=%22Nicole%20Markotic%22&f=false|coauthors=Wardhaugh, Robert|accessdate=25 January 2011|page=236[/tpl]  and novelist who lives in Windsor, Ontario. She teaches creative writing at the University of Windsor. Markotic specializes in the subjects of Canadian literature, poetry, children's literature, disability in film and disability in literature; she is currently working on a critical book about disability in film.
