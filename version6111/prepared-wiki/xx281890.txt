[[Tamerlan Bazayev]]

CATEGORIES: 1995 births, Living people, Russian footballers

Tamerlan Olegovich Bazayev ([tpl]lang-ru|Тамерлан Олегович Базаев[/tpl]; born 29 January 1995) is a Russian football forward, who currently playing for FC Alania-d Vladikavkaz.
He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Alania-d Vladikavkaz on 17 July 2013 in a game against FC SKVO Rostov-on-Don.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Russian Football Union|url=http://rfspro.ru/cgi-bin/player/script2.cgi?par1=119057|title=Career Summary|date=10 August 2013[/tpl]
