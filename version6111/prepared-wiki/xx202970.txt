[[Rus, Sălaj]]

CATEGORIES: Communes in Sălaj County

Rus ([tpl]lang-hu|Oroszmező[/tpl]) is a commune located in Sălaj County, Romania. It is composed of three villages: Buzaş (Buzamező), Fântânele-Rus (Kabalapatak) and Rus. It also included two other villages until 2002, when they were split off to form Șimișna Commune.
