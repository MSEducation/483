[[Marathwada Neta]]

CATEGORIES: Marathi-language newspapers

Marathwada Neta is a popular marathi newspaper daily with wide reach in Marathwada region of Maharashtra. Marathwada Neta was the first Marathi language daily to take the internet route to reach the worldwide Marathi audience with its internet edition epaper which is an exact copy of its print edition circulated locally. 
News Reach: Marathwada Neta reports specific news from districts in Marathwada and features news and articles from Latur, Nanded, Beed, Parbhani, Osmanabad, Hingoli apart from the rest of the Maharashtra State news and National headlines.
