[[Donald Smith (American football, born 1967)]]

CATEGORIES: 1967 births, Living people, Kentucky State Thorobreds football coaches

Donald Wayne Smith (born October 6, 1967) was the 26th head football coach for the Kentucky State University Thorobreds located in Frankfort, Kentucky and he held that position for three seasons, from 2001 until 2003.  His career coaching record at Kentucky State was 13 wins, 20 losses, and 0 ties.  As of the conclusion of the 2007 season, this ranks him ninth at Kentucky State in total wins and 15th at Kentucky State in winning percentage (.394).Kentucky State University coaching records
