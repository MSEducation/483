[[Y City, Arkansas]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Scott County, Arkansas, Unincorporated communities in Arkansas

Y City is an unincorporated community in Scott County, Arkansas, United States. It is located at the junction of U.S. Routes 71 and 270 in the southern part of the county.[tpl]cite map|publisher=Arkansas State Highway and Transportation Department|title=Arkansas State Highway Map|edition=2007|section=E2[/tpl]
