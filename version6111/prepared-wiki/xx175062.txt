[[McGill Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in Clackamas County, Oregon

McGill Airport [tpl]airport codes|||OR67[/tpl] is a privately owned, private-use airport located two miles (3 km) northwest of the central business district of Estacada, in Clackamas County, Oregon, United States. It has one runway designated 16/34 with a 1,400 x 50 ft. (427 x 15 m) gravel surface.
