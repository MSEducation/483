[[Rowson's Reserve]]

CATEGORIES: Indian whisky, 2011 introductions, Brand name alcohol products

Rowson's Reserve is a brand of Indian whisky, owned by Diageo, and launched on 3 October 2011.[tpl]cite web|url=http://ibnlive.in.com/generalnewsfeed/news/diageo-enters-imfl-space-with-rowsons-reserve-whisky/844657.html |title=Diageo enters IMFL space with Rowson's Reserve whisky |publisher=IBN Live |date=2011-10-03 |accessdate=2013-08-10[/tpl] It is a blend of Indian grain whisky combined with aged Scotch whisky matured in American Oak casks.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.drinksint.com/news/fullstory.php/aid/3102/Indian_whisky.html |title=Indian whisky |publisher=Drinks International |date=2012-07-09 |accessdate=2013-08-10[/tpl] According to Diageo, the whisky is a tribute to the wares sold by R. Rowsons & Sons dating back to 1894, when R. Rowsons & Sons traded wines and spirits from their well known store in Watergate Street, Chester, United Kingdom. The whisky is sold in a tall bottle, which gently tapers from bottom to top.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.diageo.com/en-ie/newsmedia/Pages/resource.aspx?resourceid=1080 |title=Diageo launches Rowson's Reserve - a premium IMFL whisky |publisher=Diageo.com |date=2011-10-03 |accessdate=2013-08-10[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.afaqs.com/news/company_briefs/?id=51691_Diageo+India+launches+Rowson%27s+Reserve |title=Diageo India launches Rowson's Reserve |publisher=afaqs! |date=2011-10-03 |accessdate=2013-08-10[/tpl]
Rowson's Reserve's main competitors in the premium segment of the Indian whisky market are Pernod Ricard's Blenders Pride and United Spirits Limited's Signature.[tpl]cite web|url=http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2011-09-07/india-business/30122699_1_gilbert-ghostine-largest-spirits-maker-whiskeys |title=Diageo brews local whiskies for larger peg |publisher=The Times Of India |date=2011-09-07 |accessdate=2013-08-10[/tpl]

==History==

Rowson's Reserve was launched on 3 October 2011 in Maharashtra, Haryana, Punjab and Karnataka.
