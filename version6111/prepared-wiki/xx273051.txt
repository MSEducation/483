[[Kaori Kato]]

CATEGORIES: 1977 births, Living people, Japanese women cricketers

Kaori Kato (born 26 July 1977) is a former Japanese cricketer who played five Women's One Day International cricket matches for Japan national women's cricket team in 2003.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.espncricinfo.com/ci/content/player/55213.html |title=Player Profile: Kaori Kato |publisher=ESPNcricinfo |accessdate=25 September 2011[/tpl] She captained the side in their matches during the 2003 IWCC Trophy, which served as a qualification competition for the 2005 World Cup.[tpl]cite web|url=http://stats.espncricinfo.com/ci/engine/records/team/match_results.html?class=9;id=2003;team=2331;type=year |title=Records / 2003 - Japan Women / Women's One-Day Internationals / Match results |publisher=ESPNcricinfo |accessdate=11 September 2011[/tpl]
