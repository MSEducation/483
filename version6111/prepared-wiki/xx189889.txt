[[KFHL]]

CATEGORIES: Religious radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Bakersfield, California, Radio stations established in 2003

KFHL (91.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to Wasco, California, USA, the station serves the Bakersfield area.  The station is currently owned by Mary V. Harris Foundation.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KFHL |title=KFHL Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==History==

The station was assigned the KFHL call letters on 2003-02-07.[tpl]cite web |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=89679&Callsign=KFHL |title=KFHL Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division[/tpl]
