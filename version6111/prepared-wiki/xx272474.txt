[[Hylarana florensis]]

CATEGORIES: Hylarana, Amphibians of Indonesia, Endemic fauna of Indonesia

Hylarana florensis, commonly known as the Floresian frog or Flores frog, is a species of true frog in the genus Hylarana. It is native to the islands of Lombok, Sumbawa, and Flores in Indonesia. It is a lowland species that lives in wet areas of lowland dry forests and savannahs.[ref]
Djoko Iskandar, Mumpuni 2004. Hylarana florensis. In: IUCN 2011. IUCN Red List of Threatened Species. Version 2011.1.
