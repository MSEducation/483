[[Wola Siennicka]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Krasnystaw County

Wola Siennicka [tpl]IPAc-pl|'|w|o|l|a|-|ś|e1|n|'|n|i|c|k|a[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Siennica Różana, within Krasnystaw County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) – TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|12|km|mi|0[/tpl] east of Krasnystaw and [tpl]convert|60|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-east of the regional capital Lublin.
