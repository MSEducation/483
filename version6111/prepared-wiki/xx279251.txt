[[Qalaychi, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Qalaychi ([tpl]lang-fa|قلايچي[/tpl], also Romanized as Qalāychī; also known as Kalāchī, Kalahchi, and Kalakhchi)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3078859|Qalaychi[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 89, in 19 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
