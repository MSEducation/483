CATEGORIES: 2009 World Taekwondo Championships

The Men's flyweight competition was the lightest class featured  at the 2009 World Taekwondo Championships, and was held at the Ballerup Super Arena in Copenhagen, Denmark on October 15. Flyweights were limited to a maximum of 58 kilograms in body mass.
