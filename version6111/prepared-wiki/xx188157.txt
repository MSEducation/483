[[1945–46 Segunda División]]

CATEGORIES: Segunda División seasons, 1945–46 in Spanish football leagues

The 1945–46 Segunda División season saw 14 teams participate in the second flight Spanish league. Sabadell and Deportivo were promoted to the Primera División. Ceuta, Salamanca and Jerez were relegated to the Tercera División.
