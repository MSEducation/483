[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Bragança Paulista]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Campinas, Religious organizations established in 1925, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Bragança Paulista ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Brigantiensis in Brasilia[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Bragança Paulista in the Ecclesiastical province of Campinas in Brazil.
