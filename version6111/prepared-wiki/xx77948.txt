[[Dibër District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Albania, Dibër County

The District of Dibër ([tpl]lang-sq|Rrethi i Dibrës[/tpl]) is one of the 36 districts of Albania, part of Dibër County. It has a total population of 62,825,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.instat.gov.al/graphics/doc/tabelat/Treguesit%20Sociale/Popullsia/POP%202009/t2.xls|title=POPULLSIA SIPAS RRETHEVE, 2001-2010|publisher=Albanian Institute of Statistics|accessdate=2010-09-09[/tpl] and an area of 1088 km². It is in the northeast of the country, and its capital is Peshkopi.
