[[Valsavarenche]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Aosta Valley

Valsavarenche (known as Valsavara under fascist rule from 1939 to 1946, and as Valsavaranche from 1946 to 1976) is an Italian comune with a population of 186[tpl]Citation needed|date=January 2009[/tpl]. It is located in the Aosta Valley, and is part of the Grand-Paradis Mountain Community.
