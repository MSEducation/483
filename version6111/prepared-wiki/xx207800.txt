[[Michael Blix]]

CATEGORIES: 1786 births, 1870 deaths, Members of the Parliament of Norway, Finnmark politicians

Michael Blix (23 May 1786 – 1 November 1870) was a Norwegian politician.
He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament in 1842, representing the rural constituency of Finmarkens Amt (today named Finnmark). He worked as a farmer.Michael Blix — Norwegian Social Science Data Services (NSD) He served only one term.
