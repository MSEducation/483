[[Douglas Thomas (politician)]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, Maine State Senators, Members of the Maine House of Representatives, Maine Republicans, People from Somerset County, Maine, Businesspeople from Maine

Douglas A. Thomas is an American politician and businessperson. Thomas is a Republican State Senator from Maine's 27th District, representing parts of Piscataquis, Somerset, and Penobscot counties, including the population centers of Dover-Foxcroft and Millinocket. He was first elected to the Maine State Senate in 2010, when he replaced fellow Republican Douglas Smith, who chose not to run for re-election. He represented the towns of Athens, Harmony, Ripley, Dexter, Garland and Charleston in the Maine House of Representatives.Senator Douglas A. Thomas Maine.gov
