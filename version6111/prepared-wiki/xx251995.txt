[[Frank McCoy]]

CATEGORIES: Amherst College alumni, Yale Law School alumni, Maine Black Bears football coaches, 1881 births, Year of death missing

Francis James "Frank" McCoy was the head coach of the University of Maine's football team from 1905 through 1908. He compiled a 12–15–5 record.
He was born in Holyoke, Massachusetts in 1881 to Owen and Annie (née Donahue) McCoy.
