[[Jarbidge Lake]]

CATEGORIES: Landforms of Elko County, Nevada, Lakes of Nevada

Jarbidge Lake is a glacial tarn in the Jarbidge Mountains of Elko County in northeastern Nevada.  [tpl]cite gnis|title=Jarbidge Lake|id=862368|entrydate=December 1, 1989|accessdate=August 10, 2013[/tpl] Its is located within the Jarbidge Wilderness, which is administered by the Jarbidge Ranger District of the Humboldt-Toiyabe National Forest.  It is near the terminus of the Jarbidge River Trail where it joins the West Marys River Trail and Emerald Lake Trail and is just below Emerald Lake Pass. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.nvtrailmaps.com/pdfs/maps/jarbidge_nw.pdf|title=Jarbidge Ranger District Northwest - Nevada Trail Maps.com|accessdate=August 10, 2013[/tpl] Jarbidge Lake is the principal source of the Jarbidge River.
