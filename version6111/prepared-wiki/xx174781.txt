[[Gmina Lisia Góra]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Lesser Poland Voivodeship, Tarnów County

Gmina Lisia Góra is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Tarnów County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. Its seat is the village of Lisia Góra, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|7|km|mi|0[/tpl] north of Tarnów and [tpl]convert|78|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] east of the regional capital Kraków.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|105.4|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 13,714.

==Villages==

Gmina Lisia Góra contains the villages and settlements of Breń, Brzozówka, Kobierzyn, Lisia Góra, Łukowa, Nowa Jastrząbka, Nowe Żukowice, Pawęzów, Śmigno and Stare Żukowice.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Lisia Góra is bordered by the city of Tarnów and by the gminas of Czarna, Dąbrowa Tarnowska, Radgoszcz, Tarnów and Żabno.
