[[WJWC-LP]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Alabama, Low-power FM radio stations in Alabama, Urban adult contemporary radio stations, Radio stations established in 2003, Hale County, Alabama, Radio stations disestablished in 2011, Defunct radio stations in the United States

WJWC-LP (101.9 FM) was a radio station formerly licensed to serve Gallion, Alabama. The station was owned by the Gallion Urban Broadcast Association. It aired an Urban Oldies music format.[tpl]cite web |accessdate=2008-05-21 |title=Alabama LPFM & Translators |url=http://bellsouthpwp2.net/c/r/crackedlcd/almediapage/lpfm.html |work=Alabama Broadcast Media Page[/tpl][tpl]cite web |title=Station Information Profile |work=Arbitron |url=http://www.arbitron.com/[/tpl]

==History==

This station received its original construction permit from the Federal Communications Commission on April 24, 2003.[tpl]cite web |publisher=FCC Media Bureau |title=Application Search Details (BNPL-20010611ADP) |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/app_det.pl?Application_id=657290 |date=April 24, 2003[/tpl] The new station was assigned the call letters WJWC-LP by the FCC on October 9, 2003.[tpl]cite web |title=Call Sign History |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=134041&Callsign=WJWC-LP |publisher=FCC Media Bureau CDBS Public Access Database[/tpl] WJWC-LP received its license to cover from the FCC on March 3, 2005.[tpl]cite web |publisher=FCC Media Bureau |title=Application Search Details (BLL-20031009AGA) |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/app_det.pl?Application_id=980995 |date=March 3, 2005[/tpl] On November 17, 2011, the station's license was cancelled and its call sign deleted from the FCC's database per the licensee's request.[tpl]cite web |publisher=FCC Media Bureau |title=Broadcast Actions |url=http://hraunfoss.fcc.gov/edocs_public/attachmatch/DOC-311108A1.pdf |date=November 22, 2011[/tpl]
