[[Shivah, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Shivah ([tpl]lang-fa|شيوه[/tpl], also Romanized as Shīvah and Shīveh; also known as Shivekh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3085128|Shivah[/tpl] is a village in Golabar Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,388, in 324 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
