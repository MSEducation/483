[[Gmina Lanckorona]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Lesser Poland Voivodeship, Wadowice County

Gmina Lanckorona is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Wadowice County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. Its seat is the village of Lanckorona, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|16|km|mi|0[/tpl] east of Wadowice and [tpl]convert|27|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-west of the regional capital Kraków.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|40.61|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 5,819.

==Villages==

Gmina Lanckorona contains the villages and settlements of Izdebnik, Jastrzębia, Lanckorona, Podchybie and Skawinki.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Lanckorona is bordered by the gminas of Budzów, Kalwaria Zebrzydowska, Skawina, Stryszów and Sułkowice.
