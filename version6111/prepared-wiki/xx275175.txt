[[Work the Walls]]

CATEGORIES: 1992 songs, Go-go songs

"Work the Walls" is a song by Rare Essence, which was released as a single in 1992. A sample of the theme song that was used in The Oprah Winfrey Show (which made its debut in 1986) from 1989-93 is played at the beginning of "Work the Walls" before the rapping begins.
