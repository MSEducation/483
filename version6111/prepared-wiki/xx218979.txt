[[AT&T Building (Kingman, Arizona)]]

CATEGORIES: Commercial buildings completed in 1930, AT&T buildings, Spanish Colonial Revival architecture in Arizona, Buildings and structures in Kingman, Arizona, Commercial buildings on the National Register of Historic Places in Arizona, Telecommunications buildings on the National Register of Historic Places

The AT&T Building is a historic building located at the corner of Pine and Third Streets in Kingman, Arizona. The building was built in 1930 by contractors Mead & Mount. Architects Fisher & Fisher designed the building in the Spanish Colonial Revival style. Built in a residential area, the building is still used by AT&T.[tpl]cite web|title=Arizona State Historic Property Inventory: A.T. & T. Building|url=http://pdfhost.focus.nps.gov/docs/NRHP/Photos/86001114.pdf|publisher=National Park Service|accessdate=January 21, 2014|date=July 25, 1984[/tpl] The building was placed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1986.
