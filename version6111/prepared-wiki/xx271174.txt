[[Abdallah Lamrani]]

CATEGORIES: 1946 births, Moroccan footballers, Morocco international footballers, Association football defenders, FAR Rabat players, 1970 FIFA World Cup players, Living people

Abdallah Lamrani (born 1946) is a Moroccan football defender who played for the Morocco in the 1970 FIFA World Cup.1970 FIFA World Cup Mexico He also played for FAR Rabat.
Also, Lamrani was a sergeant of the Moroccan Army.
