[[Qandi Bolagh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Qandi Bolagh ([tpl]lang-fa|قندي بلاغ[/tpl], also Romanized as Qandī Bolāgh; also known as Kand Bulag, Kandbulāq, and Qand Bolāgh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3079515|Qandi Bolagh[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 72, in 15 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
