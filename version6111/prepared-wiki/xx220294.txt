Soslan Tigiev (born October 12, 1983) is an Uzbekistani wrestler who competed in the Men's Freestyle 74 kg at the 2008 Summer Olympics and won the silver medal.Olympic results He was also bronze medalist at 2006 Asian Games.
He originally won the bronze medal at the 2012 Summer Olympics in the men's 74 kg category but his medal was stripped on November 7, 2012 after testing positive for the banned stimulant methylhexaneamine.IOC strips Uzbek wrestler of London bronze, IOC, November 7, 2012.
