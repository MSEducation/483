[[Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Bouaké]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Ivory Coast

The Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Bouaké ([tpl]lang-la|Buaken(sis)[/tpl]) is the Metropolitan See for the Ecclesiastical province of Bouaké in Côte d'Ivoire.

==Special churches==

The seat of the archbishop is Cathédrale Sainte Thérèse in Bouaké.
