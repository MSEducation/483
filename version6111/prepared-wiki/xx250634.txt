[[Grendel R31]]

CATEGORIES: Rifles of the United States

The Grendel R31 is a semi-automatic carbine that is chambered in .22 Winchester Magnum cartridge and is fed via 30-round, detachable box magazine. The is housed in the pistol grip. The carbine has a skeleton butt that can be extended using the two tubes that are housed on either side of the receiver. Most are equipped with a muzzle brake and telescopic sight.
