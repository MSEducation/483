[[Josef Hellensteiner]]

CATEGORIES: 1889 births, 1980 deaths, Austrian male cyclists, Olympic cyclists of Austria, Cyclists at the 1912 Summer Olympics, Sportspeople from Tyrol (state)

Josef Hellensteiner (26 October 1889 – 7 December 1980) was an Austrian road racing cyclist who competed in the 1912 Summer Olympics.
He was born in Tyrol, Austria–Hungary and died in St. Johann in Tirol, Austria.
In 1912 he was a member of the Austrian cycling team which finished seventh in the team time trial event.
