[[Princess Sara bint Al Faisal]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1997 births, House of Hashim, Twin people from Jordan, Jordanian princesses

 
 

Princess Sara bint Al Faisal of Jordan (born  27 March 1997) is the daughter Prince Faisal bin Al Hussein and Princess Alia, and a niece of King Abdullah II of Jordan. She is the younger sister of Princess Ayah and Prince Omar, and also twin to Princess Aisha.
