[[Vallée de l'Ahzar]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of Mali, Rivers of Niger, International rivers of Africa, Wadis, Mali–Niger border, Gao Region, Tahoua Region, Tillabéri Region

Vallée de l'Ahzar is a wadi in West Africa. For most of its course, the wadi forms part of the international boundary between Mali and Niger. It separates the Gao Region of Mali from the Tahoua and Tillabéri Regions of Niger.
