[[Yann Clairay]]

CATEGORIES: 1983 births, Living people, People from Laval, Mayenne, French racing drivers, Eurocup Formula Renault 2.0 drivers, French Formula Renault 2.0 drivers, Formula 3 Euro Series drivers, 24 Hours of Le Mans drivers, European Le Mans Series drivers, FIA GT1 World Championship drivers, Blancpain Endurance Series drivers

Yann Clairay (born December 2, 1983 in Laval, Mayenne) is a French racing driver. He has competed in such series as Eurocup Formula Renault 2.0 and the Formula Three Euroseries. Clairay won the GT1 class of the 2009 Le Mans Series along with Patrice Goueslard.
