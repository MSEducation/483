[[Gaetan Boucher (ice hockey)]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Canadian expatriate sportspeople in Switzerland, 1956 births, Ice hockey players at the 1988 Winter Olympics, Olympic ice hockey players of Switzerland, EHC Biel players, SC Bern players, Lausanne HC players

Gaetan Boucher (born May 5, 1956) is a former Canadian-born Swiss professional ice hockey centre who last played for HC Villars in Switzerland's 1. Liga.
Boucher has participated as a member of the Swiss national team in numerous international tournaments, including the 1988 Winter Olympics.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.eliteprospects.com/player.php?player=31891|title=Eliteprospects.com - Gaetan Boucher|publisher=EliteProspects.com|accessdate=2013-08-12[/tpl]
