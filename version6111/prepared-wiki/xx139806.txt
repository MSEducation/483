[[Loeks Theatres, Inc.]]

CATEGORIES: Movie theatre chains in the United States, Companies based in Grand Rapids, Michigan, IMAX venues

right|thumb|200px|Celebration! Cinema SouthLoeks Theatres, Inc., (also known as Celebration! Cinema) is a movie theater chain with headquarters in Grand Rapids, Michigan, USA. Its theatres serve the cities and surrounding areas of Grand Rapids, Lansing, Muskegon, Benton Harbor/St. Joseph, Portage/Kalamazoo, and Mount Pleasant.  An average of 5.5 million customers will see movies annually via LTI's Celebration! Cinemas locations.http://www.marketwatch.com/story/national-cinemedia-adds-celebration-cinema-as-a-network-affiliate-2012-06-26, MarketWatch
The Grand Rapids North, Lansing, and Portage locations feature IMAX theatres.  RiverTown Crossings and Lansing feature MOPIX Rear Window Technology for the hearing impaired, and most locations feature DLP Digital cinema by Christie.
Recently, Loeks Theatres, Inc. purchased two theaters from Plano, TX based Cinemark.  The theaters are located in RiverTown Crossings in Grandville, MI and the Woodland Mall in Kentwood, MI.  The 20-screen theater has been renamed Celebration! Rivertown and the 14-screen theater has been renamed Celebration! Woodland after their respective malls.  The purchase was an effort of Loeks Theatres to continue expansion in the West Michigan area.

==IMAX==

IMAX is a motion picture film format and a set of proprietary cinema projection standards created by the Canadian company IMAX Corporation. IMAX has the capacity to record and display images of far greater size and resolution than conventional film systems. Since 2002 some feature films have been converted (or upgraded) into IMAX format for display in IMAX theatres and some have also been partially shot in IMAX.
Celebration! theaters North Grand Rapids, Lansing, and Portage have been built with IMAX theaters.
