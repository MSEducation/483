[[Union, Gaffney City and Rutherfordton Railroad]]

CATEGORIES: Defunct South Carolina railroads, Predecessors of the Southern Railway (U.S.), Railway companies established in 1878, Railway companies disestablished in 1885

The Union, Gaffney City and Rutherfordton Railroad was a railroad chartered by the South Carolina General Assembly shortly after the end of Reconstruction.
The Union, Gaffney City and Rutherfordton Railroad was chartered by the South Carolina General Assembly in 1878.Wikipedia, WikiProject Trains, ICC valuations, Southern Railway, Carolina Division The line changed its name to the Atlantic and Northwestern Railroad in 1885.Southern Railway Company, Atlantic and Northwestern Railroad
In 1887, the Atlantic and Northwestern Railroad merged with the Augusta, Edgefield and Newberry Railroad to create the Georgia and Carolina Midland Railroad.Wikipedia, WikiProject Trains, ICC valuations, Southern Railway, Carolina Division
