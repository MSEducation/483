[[Tenpyō-kanpō]]

CATEGORIES: Japanese eras

[tpl]nihongo|Tenpyō-kanpō|天平感宝[/tpl] was a [tpl]nihongo|Japanese era name|年号,|nengō,|lit. "year name"[/tpl] after Tenpyō and before Tenpyō-shōhō.  This period spanned mere months, April through July 749.Nussbaum, Louis-Frédéric. (2005). "Tenpyō-kanpō" in [tpl]Google books|p2QnPijAEmEC|Japan Encyclopedia, p. 957|page=957[/tpl]; n.b., Louis-Frédéric is pseudonym of Louis-Frédéric Nussbaum, see Deutsche Nationalbibliothek Authority File.  The reigning emperor was [tpl]nihongo|Shōmu-tennō|聖武天皇[/tpl].Titsingh, Isaac. (1834). Annales des empereurs du Japon, p. 73-74.
