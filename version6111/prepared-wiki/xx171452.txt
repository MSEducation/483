[[Lost (T)apes]]

CATEGORIES: 2006 albums, Guano Apes albums, GUN Records compilation albums

Lost (T)apes is a compilation album by Guano Apes. It contains previously unreleased demos, originally recorded in 1994 and 1995.
A deluxe edition, titled The Best & The Lost (T)apes also exists.  Disc one is simply the standard edition of the Planet of the Apes compilation, and disc two is the Lost (T)apes album.
