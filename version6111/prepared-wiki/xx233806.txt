[[Kain O'Keeffe]]

CATEGORIES: Australian male television actors, Living people, 1987 births, Australian male child actors

Kain O'Keeffe (born 22 October 1987) is an Australian actor, known for his appearances in Australian television dramas and minor roles in films.

==Career==

O'Keeffe was born in Sydney, Australia on 22 October 1987.[tpl]cite web|title=ABC Blue Water High series 3|url=http://www.abc.net.au/rollercoaster/bluewaterhigh/|work=ABC Online|publisher=(Australian Broadcasting Corporation)|accessdate=2 November 2011[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.sjmanagement.com.au/actor/Kain-O'Keeffe|title=Kain O'Keeffe|publisher=Showcast|accessdate=11 July 2012[/tpl] Between 2003-05 O'Keefe filmed two guest appearances in the Seven Network television series All Saints. In 2005, O'Keeffe made his film debut aged fourteen in Swimming Upstream, starring as a Harold Jr.[tpl]cite web|last=Holden|first=Stephen|title=Film review; Struggling to Stay Afloat in a Sea of Dysfunction, and Winning|url=http://query.nytimes.com/gst/fullpage.html?res=9B06E4D9103BF937A35751C0A9639C8B63|work=The New York Times|publisher=(The New York Times Company)|accessdate=2 November 2011|date=4 February 2005[/tpl] O'Keeffe then secured a regular role Blue Water High as Guy Spender, after previously appearing in a guest role as another character called Ryan. O'Keeffe said that he did not mind filming more than one role in the same series, because he felt the industry was "quite small". He said that he hoped to gain more acting roles from starring in the programme.[tpl]cite web|title=Growing up on camera|url=http://www.theage.com.au/articles/2008/04/02/1206850985614.html|work=The Age|publisher=(Fairfax Media)|first=Erik |last=Jensen|accessdate=2 November 2011|date=3 April 2008[/tpl] O'Keeffe also starred in the television pilot of television series Resistance, working alondside Nicholas Hope and Jane Badler. In 2009, it was announced that O'Keeffe had joined the cast of Home and Away playing the role of Brendan Austin as part of the serial's new family. O'Keeffe was working alongside his former Blue Water High colleague, Rebecca Breeds.[tpl]cite journal|last=Stewert|first=Carolyn|title=Meet the Austins|journal=TV Week|date=10–16 January 2009|pages=10, 11|accessdate=2 November 2011|publisher=ACP Magazines[/tpl] He also starred in the film I Wish I Were Stephanie V, which went onto win "Best Feature Film" at INDIE FEST USA and gained further distribution in 2011.[tpl]cite web|title="I Wish I Were Stephanie V" wins IndieFest and gets a New York City Premiere|url=http://www.prlog.org/11621927-wish-were-stephanie-wins-indiefest-and-gets-new-york-city-premiere.html|work=Press Release|publisher=(PRLOG)|accessdate=2 November 2011|date=12 August 2011[/tpl] 
ABC later picked up the rights to Resistance and went the programme went into pre-production in 2011, with a planned airdate of late 2012.[tpl]cite web|last=Knox|first=David|title=ABC announces $14m children’s series|url=http://www.darwindaily.net.au/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=1466:abc-announces-14m-childrens-series&catid=41:radio-and-television&Itemid=186|work=The DarwinDaily|accessdate=2 November 2011|date=16 August 2011[/tpl]
