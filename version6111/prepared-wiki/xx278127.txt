[[Solomon Islands Ports Authority]]

CATEGORIES: Honiara, Transport in the Solomon Islands

The Solomon Islands Ports Authority (SIPA) in Honiara operates the Port of Honiara and Port of Noro. Established on June 4 1956, it has the primary responsibility of operating and maintaining all the facilities at the ports in public interest. It is a commercially viable organization able to meet all its expenses and also generate surplus revenue.
