[[Bootsauce]]

CATEGORIES: Musical groups established in 1989, Musical groups disestablished in 1996, Musical groups from Montreal, Canadian dance music groups, Canadian alternative metal musical groups, Funk metal musical groups, Juno Award winners, English-language musical groups from Quebec

Bootsauce was a Canadian rock band formed in Montreal in 1989.  The band received a Juno Award for their 1991 single "Everyone's a Winner".  The band was  composed of Drew Ling (real name Drew Thorpe) (vocals), Pere Fume (real name Perry Johnson) (guitar), Sonny Greenwich Jr. (guitar), Alan Baculis (bass guitar), and John “Fatboy” Lalley (drums).  Combining soul, funk and metal sounds, the band is best known for their 1990 cover of Hot Chocolate's "Everyone's a Winner".
