[[Jinzhou, Ningxiang]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Ningxiang County

Jinzhou Town ([tpl]zh|s=金洲镇|t=金洲鎮|p=Jīnzhōu Zhèn[/tpl]) is an urban town in Ningxiang County, Changsha City, Hunan Province, People's Republic of China. It is surrounded by Shuangjiangkou Town to the northwest, Wushan Town to the east, Lijingpu Township to the west, and Xiaduopu Town to the south. As of the 2000 census it had a population of 27,223 and an area of 62.1 square kilometers.

==Transportation==

The Jinzhou Highway (金洲大道) runs from Yutan Town through Chengjiao Town, Shuangjiangkou Town and Jinzhou Town to Changsha City in the Yuelu District.
