[[Phaisan Hansawong]]

CATEGORIES: Thai weightlifters, 1989 births, Living people, Weightlifters at the 2008 Summer Olympics, Olympic weightlifters of Thailand

Phaisan Hansawong ([tpl]lang-th|ไพศาล หรรษาวงศ์[/tpl], born October 21, 1989) is a Thai weightlifter.
At the 2007 World Championships he ranked 15th in the 56 kg category, with a total of 247 kg.[tpl]cite web|title=HANSAWONG Phaisan|publisher=International Weightlifting Federation|url=http://www.iwf.net/results/results_athlete_e.php?compid=1641[/tpl]
He competed in Weightlifting at the 2008 Summer Olympics in the 62 kg division finishing fifth, with 294 kg, beating his previous personal best by a considerable 47 kg.
He is 5 ft 5 inches tall and weighs 130 lb.
