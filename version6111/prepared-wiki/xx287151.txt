[[Incheh Rahbari]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Incheh Rahbari ([tpl]lang-fa|اينچه رهبري[/tpl], also Romanized as Īncheh Rahbarī; also known as Īncheh')[tpl]GEOnet3|-3766547|Incheh Rahbari[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Bala Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 718, in 166 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
