[[Moldova 1]]

CATEGORIES: Television channels in Moldova, Television channels and stations established in 1958, TeleRadio-Moldova

Moldova 1 is the national Moldovan television channel, operated by the national public broadcaster, TeleRadio-Moldova. 

==History==

Moldova 1 was launched on April 30, 1958, at 7:00 pm.
