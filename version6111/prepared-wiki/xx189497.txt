[[Tula, Tamaulipas]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Tamaulipas

Tula is a town located in Tula Municipality in the state of Tamaulipas.
In August 2013, archeologists discovered 30 skeletons estimated to be over 2,000-years-old.
