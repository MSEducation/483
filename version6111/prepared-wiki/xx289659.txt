[[Zarand, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Zarand ([tpl]lang-fa|زرند[/tpl])[tpl]GEOnet3|-3089010|Zarand[/tpl] is a village in Aq Bolagh Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 901, in 208 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
