[[Prospect (Topping, Virginia)]]

CATEGORIES: Plantations in Virginia, Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Virginia, Federal architecture in Virginia, Houses completed in 1849, Houses in Middlesex County, Virginia, National Register of Historic Places in Middlesex County, Virginia

Prospect is a historic plantation home located near Topping, Middlesex County, Virginia.  The house was constructed between 1820 and 1850, and is a 2 1/2-story, five-bay, frame dwelling with a gable roof in the Federal style. Two 38-foot chimneys abut each end of the house and the front and rear facades have identical gable-roofed porticos.  Also on the property are the contributing a 19th-century carriage house, an early 1900's farm shed, and the original brick-lined well.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.dhr.virginia.gov/registers/Counties/Middlesex/059-0025_Prospect_2004_Final_Nomination.pdf|title=National Register of Historic Places Inventory/Nomination: Prospect |author=Patricia R. Perhson and Mary C. Taylor| date=December 2003|publisher=Virginia Department of Historic Resources[/tpl] and Accompanying four photos
It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2004.
