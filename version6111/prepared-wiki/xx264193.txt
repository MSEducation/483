[[Trans-3-Methyl-4-decanolide]]

CATEGORIES: Lactones

''trans''-3-Methyl-4-decanolide is a chemical compound with formula [tpl]chem|C|11|H|20|O|2[/tpl], found in clary sage,B. Maurer and A. Mauser (1983), New constituents of clary sage oil. IXth Int. Congr. Essent. Oils, Book 3, pages 69-76 in the juice of the blood orange, and in the extract of mandarin peel.[tpl]cite journal | author = Regula Naef and Alain Velluz | year = 2005 | title = Identification of trans-3-Methyl-4-Decanolide in Blood Orange Juice and Mandarin Peel | journal = Journal of Essential Oil Research[/tpl] It is a lactone with coconut-like odor.
The cis isomer is found in an African orchid.R. Kaiser (1993), The Scent of Orchids,page 191.  Elsevier
