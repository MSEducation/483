[[Nevio de Zordo]]

CATEGORIES: 1943 births, 2014 deaths, Bobsledders at the 1972 Winter Olympics, Italian bobsledders, Olympic bobsledders of Italy, Olympic silver medalists for Italy, Olympic medalists in bobsleigh

Nevio de Zordo (sometimes listed as Nevio De Zordo, 11 March 1943 – 27 March 2014) was an Italian bobsledder who competed from the mid-1960s until the early 1970s. He won the silver medal in the four-man event at the 1972 Winter Olympics in Sapporo.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fisi.org/node/54126 |title=Lutto nel mondo del bob, è scomparso Nevio De Zordo |publisher=www.fisi.org |date= |accessdate=2014-03-28[/tpl]
de Zordo also won four medals at the FIBT World Championships with two golds (Two-man: 1969, Four-man: 1970) and two silvers (Two-man: 1967, Four-man: 1965).
