[[Haldi River]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of West Bengal

Haldi River is a tributary of Hooghly River flowing through Purba Medinipur district . The Keleghai joins the Kansai at Tangrakhali under Mahisadal police station in Tamluk subdivision. The combined stream is called Haldi River. It is [tpl]convert|24|km[/tpl] long.[ref]
