[[21257 Jižní Čechy]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Asteroids named for places, Astronomical objects discovered in 1996

21257 Jižní Čechy is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1848.2459658 days (5.06 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=21257 |  title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-04-30 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on February 26, 1996. It was named after South Bohemia, a region of the Czech Republic.
