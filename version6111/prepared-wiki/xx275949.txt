[[Sarah Hollis Andrews]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, English child actresses, English television actresses

Sarah Hollis Andrews was an English child actress from London, who starred in the 1975 drama adaptation of Frances Hodgson Burnett's The Secret Garden.

==Career==

Born in London, Andrews' breakthrough role came in 1972, when she was cast as Mary Lennox in the BBC children's drama The Secret Garden, an adaptation of Frances Hodgson Burnett's novel of the same name. This serial was first shown in 1975. Her only other role was in The Man from Nowhere (1975).
