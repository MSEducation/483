[[Otto Schläpfer]]

CATEGORIES: 1931 births, Swiss ice hockey players, Olympic ice hockey players of Switzerland, Living people, Ice hockey players at the 1952 Winter Olympics, Ice hockey players at the 1956 Winter Olympics, ZSC Lions players

Otto Schläpfer (born March 11, 1931 in Switzerland) is a former Swiss ice hockey player who played for the Switzerland men's national ice hockey team at the 1952 and 1956 Olympics. He also played for the Zürcher SC.
