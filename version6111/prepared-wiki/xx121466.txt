[[Ellispontos]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Kozani (regional unit), Former municipalities in West Macedonia

Ellispontos ([tpl]lang-el|Ελλήσποντος[/tpl]) is a former municipality in Kozani regional unit, West Macedonia, Greece. Since the 2011 local government reform it is part of the municipality Kozani, of which it is a municipal unit.Kallikratis law Greece Ministry of Interior [tpl]el icon[/tpl] The 2011 census recorded 5,834 residents in the municipal unit. The seat of the municipality was in Koilada.
