[[Ardumanish]]

CATEGORIES: Achaemenid people

Ardumaniš was a Persian nobleman and son of Vakauka. He was one of the seven conspirators who killed the Magian Gaumāta, who had attempted to usurp the throne of the Achaemenids, and helped Darius the Great become king.Lendering, Jona. "Ardumanish". Livius.org date 1 June 2007
