[[100 Miles]]

CATEGORIES: 2007 films, 2000s crime thriller films, Comedy thriller films, 2000s short films, Independent films, Emirati films

100 miles is a 2007 United Arab Emirates comedy short thriller crime film written and directed by Mustafa Abbas[tpl]cite news|last=Crane|first=Kelly|title=Out in the rain: Mustafa Abbas|url=http://gulfnews.com/arts-entertainment/film/out-in-the-rain-mustafa-abbas-1.63834|accessdate=June 17, 2012|newspaper=Gulf News|date=April 13, 2009[/tpl][tpl]cite news|last=Chaudhary,|first=Shuchitra|title=Emirati filmmaker is making the cut|url=http://gulfnews.com/life-style/people/emirati-filmmaker-is-making-the-cut-1.969813|accessdate=June 17, 2012|date=January 27, 2012|newspaper=Gulf News[/tpl]  and stars Abbas, Mahmood Arjumand, Kurt Neyberg, and Salma Othman. Considered Abbass' seventh film,[tpl]cite news|last=Bazaar staff|title=When film defines life|url=http://www.bazaar-magazine.com/baz/bazaar/index.php?show=eIndex&show_filter=view&action=article&art_id=ART00000001377|accessdate=June 17, 2012|newspaper=Bazaar Magazine|date=April 2012 [/tpl]  The film won 'Best Non-Documentary' at the 2007 Emirates Film Competition[tpl]cite web|title=A Night of Emirati Short Films|url=http://www.thethirdline.com/event_details.php?id=134&cbo=0&year=2006|publisher=The Third Line|accessdate=June 17, 2012[/tpl][tpl]cite news|last=Chubb|first=Laura|title=Mustafa Abbas|url=http://www.timeoutdubai.com/films/features/8768-mustafa-abbas|accessdate=June 17, 2012|newspaper=Time Out Dubai|date=May 11, 2009[/tpl] and was screened at the Dubai International Film Festival in 2008 as part of the 'Emirati Voices' category.[tpl]cite news|last=Ross|first=Matt|title=Mustafa Abbas Debuts First Feature at GFF|url=http://www.rollingstoneme.com/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=1356|accessdate=June 17, 2012|newspaper=Rolling Stone|date=April 5, 2012[/tpl]
