[[Håkon Jacobsen]]

CATEGORIES: 1962 births, Living people, Norwegian businesspeople, Norwegian engineers, Norwegian Institute of Technology alumni

Håkon Jacobsen (born 1962) is a Norwegian engineer and executive officer. Since 2008 he has been CEO of Blom.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.dn.no/forsiden/naringsliv/article1374936.ece?WT.mc_id=dn_rss |title=Blaauw vil gi plass |author=Dagens Næringsliv |date=2008-04-07 |language=Norwegian[/tpl]

==Biography==

Jacobsen is educated with a MSc in Engineering from the Norwegian Institute of Technology. He worked for 20 years as a consultant, and later partner in Accenture before he was hired by Blom. In Accenture he worked within telecom and high tech, and lived six years in Beijing, China.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.blomasa.com/en/about-blom/corporate-management |author=Blom |title=Corporate Management |accessdate=2008-05-22[/tpl] [tpl]Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot[/tpl]
