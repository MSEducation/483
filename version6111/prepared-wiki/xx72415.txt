Guadalupe Victoria (29 September 1786 – 21 March 1843),[tpl]Cite web |title=Famous People of Mexico|url=http://www.buzzle.com/articles/famous-people-of-mexico.html|publisher=Buzzle |accessdate=October 3, 2010[/tpl][tpl]Cite web |title=Today in History for 29th September 1786 |url=http://www.historyorb.com/date/1786/september/29 |publisher=History Orb |accessdate=October 3, 2010[/tpl] born José Miguel Ramón Adaucto Fernández y Félix,[tpl]Cite web |title=Victoria, Guadalupe (José Miguel Ramón Adaucto Fernández y Félix |url=http://bicentenario.com.mx/?p=831&cp=2#comment-9061|publisher=Tu Bicentenario |accessdate=October 3, 2010 |language=Spanish[/tpl] was a Mexican politician and military officer who fought for independence against the Spanish Empire in the Mexican War of Independence.
