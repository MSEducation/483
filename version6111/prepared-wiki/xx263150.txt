[[Sparta Janowiec Wielkopolski]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Poland, Association football clubs established in 1925, 1925 establishments in Poland, Żnin County, Sport in Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship

Ludowy Klub Sportowy Sparta Janowiec Wielkopolski is a football club in Janowiec Wielkopolski, Poland, founded in 1925. They play in Liga Okręgowa (VI level)
