[[Paint It Golden]]

CATEGORIES: Lydia (band) albums

Paint It Golden is the fourth album by Lydia. It is their first album after coming back from hiatus and was released on October 4, 2011.Lydia announce new album

==History==

On May 17, 2011 it was announced that after a year long hiatus, Leighton and Craig would bring back the band to record a new album under the same name.Leighton Antelman InterviewAP.net - Leighton Announces New Lydia Album And New Project 'The Cinema'
They released the song and music video for "Dragging Your Feet in the Mud" on September 26, 2011.Alt Press: New Lydia video The music video for "Best Nights" was released on July 7, 2012.Lydia Post Video for "Best Nights"
