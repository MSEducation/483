[[Poštena Vas]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Municipality of Brežice

Poštena Vas ([tpl]lang-sl|Poštena vas[/tpl]) is a small village in the Municipality of Brežice in eastern Slovenia. The area was traditionally part of Lower Carniola. It is now included with the rest of the municipality in the Lower Sava statistical region.Brežice municipal site
