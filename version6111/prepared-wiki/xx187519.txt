[[Maleta Roberts]]

CATEGORIES: Papua New Guinean netball players, Commonwealth Games competitors for Papua New Guinea, Netball players at the 2010 Commonwealth Games, People from the Autonomous Region of Bougainville, 1985 births, Living people

Maleta Roberts (born 25 April 1985) is a Papua New Guinean international netball player. Roberts was a member of the Papua New Guinea national team that played in the 2007 Arafura Games and the 2007 Netball World Championships,Queensland Academy of Sport: December 2006 results and subsequently at the 2010 Commonwealth Games. In 2013 was appointed Co-Captain of the PNG Pepes for the Pacific Series in Samoa. She also played with the Queensland Firebirds in Australia's Commonwealth Bank Trophy,NetballOnline: 2006 Commonwealth Bank Trophy and played with the Queensland Fusion in the inaugural Australian Netball League.Queensland Fusion playing roster at Netball Australia At State League level, Roberts played in the Holden Astra Cup (Tier 1) and was Co-Captain of Suncoast Lynx and later played for the Gold Coast Jaguars. Roberts is now living and playing netball in Bendigo, Victoria.
