[[UnionBanCal Corporation]]

CATEGORIES: Companies based in San Francisco, California, Mitsubishi, Banks based in California

UnionBanCal Corporation (stylized as UnionBanCal) is a bank holding company in San Francisco, California. UnionBanCal has assets of $105 billion at December 31, 2013.https://www.unionbank.com/Images/fd_q1_09_earnings_releasev2_tcm9-19146.pdf UnionBanCal has the largest presence in the U.S. among all other Japanese Banks and in 2013 stated that its aim is to grow to the tenth largest financial operating institution in the U.S. through aggressive acquisition(s).[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.realtytoday.com/articles/4107/20130408/mitsubishi-ufj-acquire-property-loans-deutsche-bank.htm |title=Mitsubishi UFJ to Acquire US Property Loans of Deutsche Bank worth $3.7 Billion |date=April 8, 2013[/tpl]
It is a member of the Mitsubishi UFJ Financial Group.
