[[2011 Rakuten Japan Open Tennis Championships]]

CATEGORIES: 2011 ATP World Tour, Japan Open Tennis Championships, 2011 in Japanese sport

The 2011 Rakuten Japan Open Tennis Championships was a tennis tournament played on outdoor hard courts. It was the 39th edition of the event known that year as the Rakuten Japan Open Tennis Championships, and was part of the 500 Series of the 2011 ATP World Tour. It was held at the Ariake Coliseum in Tokyo, Japan, from October 3 through October 9, 2011. Andy Murray won both the singles and doubles titles, the latter with his brother Jamie.
