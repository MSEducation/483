[[Anarta mendax]]

CATEGORIES: Hadenini, Insects of Greece, Insects of Bulgaria, Moths of Israel

Anarta mendax is a species of moth of the Noctuidae family. It is found in the south-eastern Balkans, Turkey, Israel, Lebanon and Transcaucasia.
Adults are on wing from April to May. There is one generation per year.
