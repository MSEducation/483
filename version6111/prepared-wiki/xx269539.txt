[[August 1991 (film)]]

CATEGORIES: 2000s drama films, 2005 television films, Estonian-language films, Films based on actual events

August 1991 is a 2005 television dramatization of a failed Soviet attempt to suppress the Singing Revolution independence movement in Estonia.
