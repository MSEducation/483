[[Thomas Evans (Archdeacon of Carmarthen)]]

CATEGORIES: 1914 births, 1982 deaths, Alumni of the University of Wales, Lampeter, Archdeacons of Carmarthen

Thomas John Evans was Archdeacon of Carmarthen from 1974 until his death in 1982.Crockford’s on-line accessed by subscription 14:21 GMT 8 August 2013
Evans was born in 1914,Crockford's Clerical Directory 1980-82 London, OUP, 1983 ISBN 0192000101 p 320 educated at St David's College, Lampeter and ordained in 1944. After curacies in Dafen and Llanelly he was Vicar of Llangunnor until his Archdeacon’s appointment.
