[[Murrumbeena railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1879

Murrumbeena railway station is located on the Cranbourne and Pakenham lines in Victoria, Australia. It serves the south-eastern Melbourne suburb of Murrumbeena opening on 14 May 1879.Murrumbeena Vicsig The station once had a signal box at the northern end of Platform 1 and two sidings at the southern end of Platform 2.
In March 2014, the State Government announced a grade separation project to replace the Murrumbeena Road level crossing immediately to the west of the station. This will include rebuilding the the station.Massive rail investment to benefit millions of Victorians Premier of Victoria 6 March 2014
