[[Peñafiel Castle]]

CATEGORIES: Castles in Castile and León

Peñafiel Castle is located in Valladolid Province, Spain. The castle is more than [tpl]convert|150|m[/tpl] long and encloses an area about [tpl]convert|10|m|abbr=on[/tpl] wide.
