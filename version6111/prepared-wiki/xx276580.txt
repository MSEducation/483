[[Hamilton, Ontario municipal election, 1944]]

CATEGORIES: Ontario municipal elections, 1944, Municipal elections in Hamilton, Ontario

The 1944 Hamilton municipal election was held on December 4, 1944 to select one Mayor, four Controllers, and sixteen members of the Hamilton, Ontario City Council, as well as members of the local School Board."City Council, 1945," Hamilton Spectator, News, Dec. 5, 1944.
