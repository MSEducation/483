[[Bøverbru]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Oppland, Vestre Toten

Bøverbru is a village in Vestre Toten municipality, Norway. Its population is 691. [tpl]cite paper | author=Statistics Norway | title=Urban settlements. Population and area, by municipality. 1 January 2006 | date=2006 | url=http://www.ssb.no/english/subjects/02/01/10/beftett_en/tab-2006-07-05-01-en.html[/tpl] It is located between Reinsvoll and Kolbu. A local meat-producer, Totenkjøtt, is located here.
