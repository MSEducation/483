[[Peter Giles (canoeist)]]

CATEGORIES: 1970 births, Canadian canoeists, Canoeists at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Living people, Olympic canoeists of Canada

Peter Giles (born April 18, 1970) is a Canadian sprint kayaker who competed in the mid-1990s. He finished seventh in the K-4 1000 m event at the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta.
