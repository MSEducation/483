==Early life and education==

Born in Jerusalem, Serna attended Cheltenham Girls' High School and graduated from the University of Technology, Sydney with a Bachelor of Mathematics and Finance and a Bachelor of International Studies in 2009. In August 2010, she began co-presenting Letters and Numbers as the show's arithmetical guru while completing honours studying part-time in mathematics with applications in biology looking at effects of flooding on the Fitzroy River and Great Barrier Reef.[tpl]cite web|last=Tapp|first=Jessica|url=http://newsroom.uts.edu.au/news/2010/10/letters-and-numbers|title=Letters and Numbers|work=UTS News Room|date=3 October 2010|archivedate=30 March 2012|deadurl=no|archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/66ZYLkktu[/tpl] Serna intends to complete a Ph.D. in environmental science.[tpl]cite web|url=http://newsroom.uts.edu.au/news/2010/10/letters-and-numbers|title=Letters and numbers|publisher=University of Technology, Sydney|date=3 October 2010[/tpl]
