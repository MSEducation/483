[[Sardar Hasanov]]

CATEGORIES: Azerbaijani weightlifters, 1985 births, Living people, Weightlifters at the 2008 Summer Olympics, Weightlifters at the 2012 Summer Olympics, Olympic weightlifters of Azerbaijan, People from Baku

Sardar Hasanov (born May 12, 1985, Baku) is an Azerbaijani weightlifter. His personal best is 280 kg.
At the 2007 World Championships he ranked 14th in the 62 kg category, with a total of 280 kg.[tpl]cite web|title=HASANOV Sardar|publisher=International Weightlifting Federation|url=http://www.iwf.net/results/results_athlete_e.php?compid=1649[/tpl]
At the 2008 European Weightlifting Championships he ranked fifth in the 62 kg category, with a total of 275 kg.
He competed in Weightlifting at the 2008 Summer Olympics in the 62 kg division but did not finish.[tpl]cite web|title=HASANOV Sardar|publisher=International Weightlifting Federation|url=http://www.iwf.net/results/results_athlete_e.php?compid=2580[/tpl]
He is 5 ft 3 inches tall and weighs 137 lb.
