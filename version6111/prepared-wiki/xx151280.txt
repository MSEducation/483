==Reception==

===Box office===

In the United States, the film opened in a single theatre on March 17, 2006 and grossed $1,511 during its one week of screening.[ref]
</ref> Overall, the film has grossed $2,241 in the United States.<ref name="mojo"/>

===Critical reception===

The film was well-received by most film critics. On Rotten Tomatoes, it earned an aggregate review score of 82%, deeming it "Fresh" based on 11 reviews.[ref]
