[[Dendrocalamus]]

CATEGORIES: Bamboo genera

Dendrocalamus is a tropical genus of giant clumping bamboo, which are similar to the genus Bambusa. With about 29 species, this genus is found from the China throughout Southeast Asia. Dendrocalamus giganteus is one of the tallest of bamboos, capable of reaching heights up to 46 m.Bamboos Of India, http://www.inbar.int/publication/txt/tr17/. Accessed 8 June 2007Bioone
