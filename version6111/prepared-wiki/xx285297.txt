[[Keasden]]

CATEGORIES: Hamlets in North Yorkshire, Populated places in North Yorkshire

Keasden is a hamlet in the civil parish of Clapham-cum-Newby, in the Craven district of the county of North Yorkshire, in the north-eastern England.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.explorebritain.info/locality-north-yorkshire-keasden-sd7266 | title=Keasden, North Yorkshire | work=Explore Britain | accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl] It is about two miles to the south west of the village of Clapham and about five miles to the east of Bentham. The hamlet sits on an unclassified road and is surrounded to the north, south and west by woodland. Keasden has a church but no schools.[tpl]cite web | url=http://home.rm.com/SchoolFinder/ShowSchools.aspx?l=Keasden,%20North%20Yorkshire&t=pri | title=Primary schools in Keasden,Devon | work=RM Education | accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl] The postcode is LA2.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.postcode-info.co.uk/find-a-postcode.html?submitted=1&areaname=keasden&countyid=0 | title=Information on Keasden| work=Postcode Info | accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl]
