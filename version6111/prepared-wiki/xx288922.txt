[[Shin, Iran]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Shin ([tpl]lang-fa|شين[/tpl], also Romanized as Shīn; also known as Sen)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3084969|Shin[/tpl] is a village in Aq Bolagh Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 84, in 20 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
