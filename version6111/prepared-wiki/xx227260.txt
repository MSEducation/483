[[Jin Kyung-sun]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 births, Living people, Association football midfielders, South Korean footballers, Jeju United FC players, Daegu FC players, Jeonbuk Hyundai Motors players, Gangwon FC players, K League players, Ajou University alumni

Jin Kyung-Sun ([tpl]lang-ko|진경선[/tpl]; born April 10, 1980) is a South Korean football player who currently plays for Gangwon FC in the K-League.
His previous club is Jeonbuk Hyundai Motors, Daegu FC, Bucheon SK and Ulsan Hyundai Mipo Dolphin in Korea National League.
