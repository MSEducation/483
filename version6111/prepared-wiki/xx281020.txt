[[Ruslan Imayev]]

CATEGORIES: 1994 births, Living people, Russian footballers

Ruslan Ravilyevich Imayev ([tpl]lang-ru|Руслан Равильевич Имаев[/tpl]; born February 1, 1994) is a Russian football defender who plays for FC Podolye Podolsky district.
He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Kaluga on July 22, 2013 in a game against FC Dynamo Bryansk.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Russian Football Union|url=http://rfspro.ru/cgi-bin/player/script2.cgi?par1=119709|title=Career Summary|date=9 August 2013[/tpl]
