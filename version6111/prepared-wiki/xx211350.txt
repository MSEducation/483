[[Lost Worlds (TV series)]]

CATEGORIES: History (TV channel) original programs, American documentary television series

Lost Worlds is a documentary television series by The History Channel that explores a variety of "lost" locations from ancient to modern times. These "great feats of engineering, technology, and culture"[tpl]cite web|title=About the Series|url=http://www.history.com/content/lostworlds/about-the-series|work=history.com|publisher=The History Channel|accessdate=12 June 2012|archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20100209172830/http://www.history.com/content/lostworlds/about-the-series|archivedate=9 February 2010|quote=A team of field investigators and engineering experts went out into the world in search of clues about these great feats of engineering, technology, and culture in order to bring them back to life with modern-day graphic technology.[/tpl] are revealed through the use of archaeological evidence, interviews with relevant experts while examining the sites, and CGI reproductions.[tpl]cite web|title=About Lost Worlds|url=http://www.history.com/videos/lost-worlds-about-lost-worlds#lost-worlds-about-lost-worlds|work=history.com|publisher=The History Channel|accessdate=12 June 2012|format=video[/tpl] These visual re-creations take the form of rendered 3D environments and photo manipulated overlays, allowing the "lost world" to be seen over its present-day state.
The pilot episode "Palenque: Metropolis of the Maya" was first aired on April 4, 2005. It was followed by 12 more episodes in 2006, and a further 19 episodes in 2007.
