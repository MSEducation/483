[[Not Animal]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 albums, Epic Records albums, Margot and the Nuclear So and So's albums

Not Animal is the second full-length album released by Margot and the Nuclear So and So's, the first on Epic Records. It is being co-released with the band-selected version, Animal!. It was recorded at Engine Studios in Chicago, IL. Brian Deck produced the album and it was engineered by Neil Strauch.

==Track listing==

The track "Hello Vagina" was used on the CW's One Tree Hill (TV Series), November 10, 2008, episode "Even Fairy Tale Characters Would Be Jealous".
