[[Madison County Airport (Ohio)]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in Ohio

Madison County Airport [tpl]airport codes||KUYF|UYF[/tpl] is a public airport located three miles (5 km) north of the central business district of London, a city in Madison County, Ohio, United States. It is owned by the Madison County Airport Authority.
Although most U.S. airports use the same three-letter location identifier for the FAA and IATA, Madison County Airport is assigned UYF by the FAA but has no designation from the IATA.Great Circle Mapper: KUYF - London, Ohio (Madison County Airport)

==Facilities and aircraft==

Madison County Airport covers an area of [tpl]convert|148|acre|ha[/tpl] which contains one runway designated 9/27 with a 4,001 x 75 ft (1,220 x 23 m) asphalt pavement.
