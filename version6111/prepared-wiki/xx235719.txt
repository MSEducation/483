[[Caroline Manzo]]

CATEGORIES: 1961 births, Living people, American people of Italian descent, American television personalities, People from Brooklyn, The Real Housewives cast members

Caroline Laurita-Manzo (born August 23, 1961) is an American television personality, entrepreneur and radio show host. She is most known for her role on The Real Housewives of New Jersey.

==Career==

Manzo first appeared on the first season of Bravo's The Real Housewives of New Jersey in 2009. Her sister, Dina, and sister-in-law, Jaqueline, were also cast members when the show first aired. Her sister Dina, was married to Albert's brother, Tommy Manzo. Manzo left The Real Housewives after its fifth season and has a spin-off, Manzo'd With Children, in development with Bravo.[tpl]cite web|last=Mullins|first=Jenna|title=Caroline Manzo Leaving Real Housewives of New Jersey|url=http://www.eonline.com/news/463881/caroline-manzo-leaving-real-housewives-of-new-jersey|work=E!|date=September 26, 2013[/tpl]

==Personal life==

Manzo is married to Albert Manzo, co-owner of The Brownstone, an event and catering facility in Paterson, New Jersey.
