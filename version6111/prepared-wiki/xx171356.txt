[[Bristol, Ohio]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Perry County, Ohio, Unincorporated communities in Ohio

Bristol is an unincorporated community in southern Pike Township, Perry County, Ohio, United States.  It lies along State Route 93 at its intersection with Marietta Road and Township Road 223.  It is located 4 miles (6 kilometers) east of New Lexington, the county seat of Perry County.DeLorme. Ohio Atlas & Gazetteer.  7th ed.  Yarmouth: DeLorme, 2004, p. 70.  ISBN 0-89933-281-1.
