[[Promontorium Agarum]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains on the Moon

Promontorium Agarum is a raised mountainous cape protruding into the southeast of Mare Crisium on the near side of the Moon. Its selenographic coordinates are 14.0° N, 66.0° E and it is 70 km in diameter.
Promontorium Agarum is named for a cape in the Sea of Azov.
