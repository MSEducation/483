[[Cataluña]]

CATEGORIES: Compositions by Isaac Albéniz, 1899 compositions, Solo piano pieces, Compositions for guitar

Cataluña, Op. 47, No. 2, is a composition by Isaac Albéniz. It premiered as a piano performance in Paris in January 1899.[tpl]cite book|last=Clark|first=Walter Aaron|title=Isaac Albéniz: Portrait of a Romantic|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=dg60ZjqZLfkC&pg=PA196|accessdate=24 March 2012|date=15 May 2002|publisher=Oxford University Press|isbn=978-0-19-925052-3|page=196[/tpl] Since it has been transcribed for classical guitar by Miguel Llobet, it has become one of the staples of classical guitar music. It has been performed and recorded by guitarists such as John Williams, Milos Janjic, Charles Mokotoff and many others. It is usually played in the key of G minor.
