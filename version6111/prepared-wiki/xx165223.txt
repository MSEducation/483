[[Herb and Jamaal]]

CATEGORIES: Comic strips missing date information

Herb and Jamaal is a comic strip by Stephen Bentley syndicated by Creators Syndicate. It is published daily and centers on the eponymous friends who run a diner together, inspired by the illustrator's experience at a high school reunion.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.creators.com/comics/herb-and-jamaal-about.html |title=About Stephen Bentley |accessdate=2013-09-09 |publisher=Creators Syndicate[/tpl] and a desire to provide increased representation for black people in comics. According to Bentley, "Some of the characters in 'Herb & Jamaal' partially come come from myself and people that I know but the full story is just a fantasy..."[tpl]cite news |title='Herb & Jamaal' added to comics |url=http://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=1368&dat=19890807&id=tItQAAAAIBAJ&sjid=yxIEAAAAIBAJ&pg=6511,2093652 |author=Jerry Resler |newspaper=The Milwaukee Sentinel |date=1989-08-07 |accessdate=2013-09-09[/tpl]

==Story and Setting==

The strip centres around the title characters and the diner they run together.
