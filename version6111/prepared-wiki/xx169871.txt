[[Weird Western Tales]]

CATEGORIES: 1972 comic debuts, 2001 comic debuts, Comics anthologies, DC Comics titles, Fantasy comics, Horror comics, Jonah Hex, Western (genre) comics

Weird Western Tales is a Western genre comic book title published by DC Comics which ran from June–July 1972 to August 1980. It is perhaps best known for featuring the adventures of Jonah Hex until #38 (1977) when the character was promoted to his own eponymous series. Scalphunter then took Hex's place as the featured character in Weird Western Tales.

===Revival===

Weird Western Tales was revived in 2001 as a four-issue limited series.Weird Western Tales vol. 2 at the Grand Comics Database The mini-series had no relation to the earlier title, instead featuring a series of one-shot Western-based stories.
