[[Sooriya Arana]]

CATEGORIES: 2004 films, 2000s drama films, Sri Lankan films

Sooriya Arana ([tpl]lang-en|Fire Fighters[/tpl]) is a Sri Lankan 2004 drama film directed by Somaratne Dissanayake. The winner of 10 Presidential Film Awards, it was also Sri Lanka's highest-grossing local release of all time (with Rs. 121 million).
