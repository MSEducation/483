[[AMEN Missionary]]

CATEGORIES: Non-profit organizations based in California, International nongovernmental organizations, Organizations established in 2009, Online communities for social change, Social networks for social change

AMEN Missionary is an international 501(c)3 Christian nonprofit organization founded in 2009 based in California, USA and Mae Sot, Thailand. The organization's purpose is to provide emergency aid and long-term development to Burmese migrants and orphans on Thai-Burma border. As of 2012, the AMEN Missionary is focusing its efforts in Mae Sot  (see Mae Sot District#Burmese refugees). The organization strives to meet the fundamental needs of these people through such activities as providing food and donated educational materials. The AMEN Missionary has partnered with the Mingalar Church in their efforts.
