[[The Romsey School]]

CATEGORIES: Secondary schools in Hampshire, Academies in Hampshire

The Romsey School is a mixed community academy in Romsey, Hampshire, England. The school was a secondary modern, called Romsey County Secondary School,[tpl]cite book | url=http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=kdsVAQAAIAAJ&q=%22romsey+county+secondary%22&dq=%22romsey+county+secondary%22&hl=en&ei=wGwgTPi0CcOPcZnrtT0&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&redir_esc=y | title=The Education Authorities Directory and Annual | publisher=School Government Publishing, Company Limited | year=1968[/tpl] until the 1970s when it became a comprehensive. In 2000 it became a Specialist Language College jointly with The Mountbatten School.[tpl]cite web | url=http://teachweb.co.uk/secondary-schools/Hampshire-B47/The-Romsey-School-SC906.html | title=The Romsey School, Hampshire | publisher=teachweb[/tpl] In 2005 the school's specialisation changed to a Mathematics and Computing College.[tpl]cite web | url=http://rlp.romsey.hants.sch.uk/rlp/course/view.php?id=23 | title=Vacancies | publisher=Romsey School[/tpl] In August 2011 The School became an academy after a decision by Governors.[tpl]cite web | url=http://rlp.romsey.hants.sch.uk/rlp/mod/resource/view.php?id=23726 | title=Romsey School | publisher=Romsey School[/tpl] [tpl]dead link|date=October 2013[/tpl] The school has approximately 1100 children aged 11â€“16 and 100 teachers and the catchment area includes the villages of Ampfield, Braishfield, Sherfield English, Michelmersh, Timsbury and Awbridge.
In 2010 81% of pupils achieved 5 or more A*-C grades in their GCSEs. 94% of pupils achieved at least a pass on A*-C. The School also provides Adult Education classes that makes the School open to the community.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.romsey.hants.sch.uk/aboutus.html | title=About us, The Romsey School | publisher=Romsey School[/tpl]

==ASD Support Base==

The School runs an ASD Support Base to support pupils in lessons who find it difficult as a result of Special needs and Autistic spectrum disorder. The ASD Base was established in 2002 and is directly run by 2 teachers and 9 support staff.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.romsey.hants.sch.uk/asd/index.htm | title=Autistic Spectrum Disorder (ASD) Resourced Provision | publisher=Romsey School[/tpl]
