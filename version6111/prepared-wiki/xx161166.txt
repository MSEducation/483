[[Ritual of Love]]

CATEGORIES: 1991 albums, Warner Bros. Records albums, Karyn White albums, Albums produced by Laney Stewart

Ritual of Love is Karyn White's second album, released in 1991. It contains her biggest hit "Romantic", as well as other R&B hits "The Way I Feel About You," "Walkin' the Dog," and "Do Unto Me."
