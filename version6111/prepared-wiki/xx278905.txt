[[Varmazyar-e Olya]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Varmazyar-e Olya ([tpl]lang-fa|ورمزيارعليا[/tpl], also Romanized as Varmazyār-e ‘Olyā; also known as Varmaziar and Varmazyār)[tpl]GEOnet3|10081942|Varmazyar-e Olya[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Pain Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 263, in 49 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
