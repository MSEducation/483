Athena Air Services was an airline based in Johor Bahru, Malaysia. It operated scheduled, domestic and international passenger services.[tpl]cite web | title=Athena Air Services | work=Flight International, Directory: World Airlines, 16â€“22 March 2004| url=http://www.flightglobal.com/pdfarchive/view/2004/2004-09%20-%200092.html?search=Athena%20Air%20Services | accessdate=26 October 2010[/tpl]

==History==

The airline was established in 2003 and started operations on 18 November 2003. It was owned jointly by Athena Sdn Bhd and PT Avipatria. In 2004 it was considered by analysts as a small budget airline worth watching, with plans for expansion, but restricted by lack of a website for bookings.[tpl]cite news | title=Rise of the budget airline | work=The Guardian, 6 March 2004| url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/travel/2004/mar/06/cheapflights.guardiansaturdaytravelsection | accessdate=26 October 2010 | location=London | first=John | last=Aglionby | date=22 November 2006[/tpl]

==Services==

Athena Air Services operated flights to Jakarta and Surabaya from Senai International Airport.

==Fleet==

The Athena Air Services fleet consisted of 1 Boeing 727-200.
