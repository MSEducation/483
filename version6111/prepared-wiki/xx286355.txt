[[Damaris Phillips]]

CATEGORIES: 1980s births, American television chefs, Date of birth missing (living people), Jefferson Community and Technical College faculty, Living people, People from Louisville, Kentucky

Damaris Phillips is an American chef who won the ninth season of the Food Network television series Food Network Star.

==Early life==

Phillips was born in Fayette County, Kentucky. She has four brothers and sisters.

==Education==

Phillips graduated from Jefferson Community and Technical College, where she earned a degree in Culinary Arts. She states to have attended culinary school because she “didn’t know what to do in life”. Phillips later went on to become a culinary food instructor.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.foodnetwork.com/damaris-phillips/index.html|title=Damaris Phillips, Season 9 Food Network Star Finalist|accessdate=August 12, 2013[/tpl]

==Food Network==

===Food Network Star===

In early 2013, Phillips became a contestant on the ninth season of the Food Network series Food Network Star.[tpl]cite web|url=http://videos.huffingtonpost.com/entertainment/food-network-star-hopeful-damaris-phillips-517785890|title='Food Network Star' Hopeful: Damaris Phillips (VIDEO) - Huffington Post|accessdate=August 12, 2013[/tpl] She eventually became one of the final three contestants, and she filmed a pilot for a potential series called Eat, Date, Love.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=C0PZITqLZ5w|title=Eat Date Love – YouTube|accessdate=August 12, 2013[/tpl] Phillips was announced as the winner of the competition on August 11, 2013. 

===Southern at Heart===

Phillips' series on Food Network is called Southern at Heart. The show premiered on October 27, 2013.[tpl]cite web|url=http://blog.foodnetwork.com/fn-dish/2013/09/damaris-phillips-southern-at-heart/ |title=Food and Love: Better Together on Damaris Phillips' Southern at Heart |publisher=Food Network |date= |accessdate=2013-10-26[/tpl] The first season aired on Sundays at 10:30 am/9:30c and consisted of six episodes.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.foodnetworkgossip.com/2013/09/damaris-phillips-show-southern-at-heart/ |title=Damaris Phillips Show - Southern at Heart |publisher=Food Network |date= |accessdate=2013-11-03[/tpl] Phillips posts regular show reviews and updates to her Facebook page.[tpl]cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/pages/Damaris-Phillips/132256863631165/ |title=Damaris Phillips Facebook Page |publisher=Facebook |date= |accessdate=2013-11-03[/tpl]
