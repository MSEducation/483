[[115950 Kocherpeter]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Astronomical objects discovered in 2003

115950 Kocherpeter is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1522.6803878 days (4.17 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=115950 |  title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-04-28 | publisher = NASA[/tpl]
