[[Pawhuska Osages]]

CATEGORIES: Sports clubs established in 1922, Defunct minor league baseball teams, Professional baseball teams in Oklahoma

The Pawhuska Osages were a minor league baseball team that played in the Western Association in 1922. They were based in Pawhuska, Oklahoma, and they posted a record of 29-93 - in part because they disbanded on August 16 of that year, and all remaining games were considered forfeits.Baseball Reference Bullpen They were managed by Clyde Wren, John Wuffli and Otis Stucker.
