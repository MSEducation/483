[[KVNE]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Texas, Christian contemporary radio stations, Radio stations established in 1983

KVNE (89.5 FM) is a radio station  broadcasting a Christian radio format. Licensed to Tyler, Texas, USA, the station serves the Tyler-Longview area.  The station is currently owned by Educational Radio Foundation of East Texas, Inc.. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KVNE |title=KVNE Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==Translators==

In addition to the main station, KVNE is relayed by an additional 4 translators to widen its broadcast area. KVNE was formerly heard on co-owned translator K214BE licensed to Shreveport, Louisiana. K214BE has since been sold.
