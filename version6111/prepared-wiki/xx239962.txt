[[Zgornja Ročica]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Municipality of Sveta Ana

Zgornja Ročica ([tpl]IPA-sl|ˈzɡoːɾnja ɾɔˈtʃiːtsa|pron[/tpl]) is a settlement in the Municipality of Sveta Ana in the Slovene Hills in northeastern Slovenia.Sveta Ana municipal site
There is a small chapel in the settlement. It was built at the beginning of the 20th century in a Neo-Gothic style.Slovenian Ministry of Culture register of national heritage reference number 23212
