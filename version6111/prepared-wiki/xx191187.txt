Silvio José del Rosario Parodi Rojas (born 30 August 1932 in Luque – died 22 August 2006) was a football player and coach from Paraguay.  
He played for Club Sportivo Luqueño, Calcio Padova, Genoa C.F.C., UD Las Palmas, Nîmes Olympique and FC Mulhouse. After retiring from football as a player he became a coach and his first managerial job was for the French side FC Mulhouse.A dos años de la partida
He died on August 2006 and was buried in his native town of Luque.
