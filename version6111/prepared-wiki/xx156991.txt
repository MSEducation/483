[[Battle of Kara Killisse (1915)]]

CATEGORIES: Battles of the Caucasus Campaign, Battles of World War I involving the Ottoman Empire, Battles of World War I involving Russia, Conflicts in 1915, History of Ağrı Province, 1915 in the Ottoman Empire, 1915 in Russia

The Battle of Kara Killisse (Lit. Black church, Turkish: Karakilise Muharebesi) was a battle  on the Caucasus front in July 1915 after the Battle of Manzikert.[tpl]cite book|last=Clodfelter|first=Micheal|authorlink=|title= Warfare and Armed Conflicts: A Statistical Reference to Casualty and Other Figures, 1500-2000| publisher=McFarland|date=2002|location=|pages=460|isbn=0-7864-1204-6[/tpl] In Russian historical literature, this engagement is considered as a part of "Alashkert defensive operation" (9 July-3 August).
