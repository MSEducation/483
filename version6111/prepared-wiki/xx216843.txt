[[Uzunoba, Khachmaz]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khachmaz District

Uzunoba is a village and municipality in the Khachmaz Rayon of Azerbaijan.  It has a population of 1,260.  The municipality consists of the villages of Uzunoba, Baraxum, Xəzərli, and Şərifoba.Belediyye Informasiya Sistemi [tpl]dead link|date=January 2013[/tpl]
