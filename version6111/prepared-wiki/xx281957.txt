Nick Hengelman (born 15 November 1989, Glanerbrug) is a footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Achilles '29 in the Dutch Eerste Divisie.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.worldfootball.net/player_summary/nick-hengelman/ | title = Nick Hengelman | publisher = worldfootball.net | accessdate = 13 January 2014[/tpl]

==Career==

Hengelman is a youth product from the FC Twente/Heracles Almelo football academy. He formerly played for AGOVV Apeldoorn. When the club was declared bankrupt in January 2013, he joined Vitesse to maintain his fitness.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.omroepgelderland.nl/web/Sport/Betaald-voetbal/1993188/Hengelman-traint-bij-Jong-Vitesse.htm#.UgbjXdK-3To | title = Hengelman traint bij Jong Vitesse (Dutch) | publisher = Omroep Gelderland | accessdate = 11 August 2013[/tpl] When the club didn't offer him a new contract in the summer of 2013, Hengelman found a new club in Achilles '29.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.telegraaf.nl/telesport/voetbal/nationaal/21751097/__Hengelman_naar_Achilles_29__.html | title = Hengelman naar Achilles '29 (Dutch) | publisher = De Telegraaf | accessdate = 11 August 2013[/tpl] He made his début for the team in the first match in professional football for Achilles on 3 August 2013. The game in Emmen ended in 2–2.
