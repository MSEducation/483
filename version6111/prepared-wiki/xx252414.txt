[[Susana Freyre]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, Argentine film actresses, People from Rosario, Santa Fe, Argentine television actresses, 20th-century Argentine actresses

Susana Freyre is an Argentine actress. She appeared in 30 films and television shows between 1945 and 1984. She starred in the film Three Loves in Rio, which was entered into the 9th Berlin International Film Festival.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0161803/awards |title=IMDB.com: Awards for Three Loves in Rio |accessdate=2010-01-10 |work=imdb.com[/tpl]
