Matthew Ronald "Matt" Fry (born 26 September 1990) is an English professional footballer who plays as a defender. He has previously played for West Ham United, Gillingham, Charlton Athletic, Bradford City and Concord Rangers.

==Career==

===Gillingham (Loan)===

He signed for Gillingham on 15 October 2009, on a one month loan.[tpl]cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/g/gillingham/8306754.stm|title=Gillingham snap up Vernon and Fry |publisher=BBC Sport |date=15 October 2009 |accessdate=17 October 2009[/tpl] He made his professional, Gillingham and Football League debut on 17 October 2009, in the 2–0 away defeat against Milton Keynes Dons.[tpl]cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/eng_div_2/8295499.stm |title=MK Dons 2–0 Gillingham |publisher=BBC News |date=17 October 2009 |accessdate=20 May 2010[/tpl] In November his loan with Gillingham was extended for a further month,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.gillinghamfootballclub.com/page/NewsDetail/0,,10416~1875127,00.html |title=Fry Extends Loan Deal |publisher=Gillinghamfootballclub.com |date= |accessdate=20 May 2010[/tpl] and in December extended again for another month.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.gillinghamfootballclub.com/page/NewsDetail/0,,10416~1900900,00.html |title=Fry agrees one month extension |publisher=Gillinghamfootballclub.com |date= |accessdate=20 May 2010[/tpl] Fry suffered a knee injury in Gillingham's away game against Exeter City on 28 December 2009 ruling him out of action for six weeks. ===Charlton Athletic (Loan)===

On 25 March 2010 he signed for League One side Charlton Athletic until the end of the season but returned to West Ham without having played for Charlton due to a persisting knee problem suffered earlier in the year.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.whufc.com/articles/20100520/fry-feeling-good_2236884_2055588 |title=Fry feeling good |publisher=Whufc.com |date= |accessdate=20 May 2010[/tpl] In August 2010 Fry again joined Charlton on loan, this time until January 2011.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.whufc.com/articles/20100805/fry-joins-addicks_2236884_2111860 |title=Fry joins Addicks |publisher=Whufc.com |date= |accessdate=5 August 2010[/tpl] On 28 August 2010 Fry made his first appearance for Charlton and scored his first goal, in a 3–1 away defeat to Huddersfield Town.[tpl]cite news|author= |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/eng_div_2/8922364.stm |title=BBC Sport – Football – Huddersfield 3–1 Charlton |publisher=BBC News |date= 28 August 2010|accessdate=29 August 2010[/tpl]
In March 2011 Fry returned to West Ham having made 32 appearances scoring one goal, in all competitions, for Charlton Athletic.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cafctv.co.uk/page/NewsDetail/0,,10267~2321672,00.html|title=Fry returns to Upton Park |publisher=cafctv.co.uk |date=22 March 2011 |accessdate=23 March 2011[/tpl]

===Bradford City===

In February 2012, Fry joined Bradford City on a free transfer following the termination of his contract by mutual consent from West Ham in January.[tpl]cite web|title=Fry signs up.|url=http://www.bradfordcityfc.co.uk/page/News/0,,10266~2605966,00.html|publisher=www.bradfordcityfc.co.uk|accessdate=10 February 2012[/tpl] He made his debut in a 1–1 draw at home to Port Vale on 14 February, in which he played 79 minutes before being substituted."Bradford 1–1 Port Vale" BBC Sport. 14 February 2012. ===Concord Rangers===

After leaving Bradford City, Fry played for Concord Rangers helping them win promotion from the Isthmian League to the Conference South in season 2012–13 via the play-offs.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/22951289 | title=Dartford sign former West Ham man Matt Fry | publisher=BBC Sport | accessdate=15 July 2013[/tpl] In May 2013, Fry played for Concord against Lowestoft Town in the play-off final at Crown Meadow with Concord winning 2–1 to gain promotion.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.edp24.co.uk/sport/lowestoft-town-fc/heartbreak_again_as_lowestoft_town_lose_out_to_concord_rangers_1_2182284 | title=Heartbreak again as Lowestoft Town lose out to Concord Rangers | publisher=www.edp24.co.uk | date=7 May 2013 | accessdate=15 July 2013 | author=Bailey, Michael[/tpl]

===Dartford===

In June 2013 Fry signed for Dartford. but returned to Concord in March 2014 having made 15 appearances.
