[[Slobodan Inić]]

CATEGORIES: People from Zrenjanin, 1946 births, 2000 deaths, Democratic Party (Serbia) politicians, University of Belgrade faculty

Slobodan Inić (born 1946 in Zrenjanin, Serbia, DF Yugoslavia - died June 22, 2000) was a political commentator and professor of sociology at the University of Belgrade.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.vreme.com/arhiva_html/495/06.html |title=In memoriam |publisher=Vreme.com |date= |accessdate=2013-06-27[/tpl]  He was one of the founders of the Democratic Party in Serbia, Yugoslavia in December 1989.
His published books include "Portreti".
