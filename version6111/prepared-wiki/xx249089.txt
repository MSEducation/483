[[Martin Laumann Ylven]]

CATEGORIES: 1988 births, Living people, Ice hockey players at the 2010 Winter Olympics, Linköpings HC players, Norwegian ice hockey players, Olympic ice hockey players of Norway, People from Oslo, Stjernen Hockey players

Martin Laumann Ylven (born December 22, 1988 in Oslo, Norway) is a Norwegian professional ice hockey forward. He currently plays for Linköpings HC of the Swedish Elitserien. Ylven joined the Linköpings organization in 2004 and played on their junior teams for three seasons. He made his professional debut in the Elitserien during the 2006–07 season. He returned to Norway in 2007, playing the most games (25) with Stjernen Hockey of the top-level GET-ligaen. He rejoined Linköpings in 2008. 
Ylven was selected to play for the Norway men's national ice hockey team at the 2010 Winter Olympics. He previously represented Norway at the 2005 and 2006 IIHF World U18 Championships, the 2007 and 2008 IIHF World U20 Championship, and the 2009 World Ice Hockey Championships.
