[[Noji, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Noji ([tpl]lang-fa|نجي[/tpl], also Romanized as Nojī and Naji; also known as Nuji, and Nujun)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3077071|Noji[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
