[[Quli Qaseh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Quli Qaseh ([tpl]lang-fa|قو لي قصه[/tpl], also Romanized as Qūlī Qaṣeh; also known as Solţānābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|10138556|Quli Qaseh[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 779, in 193 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
