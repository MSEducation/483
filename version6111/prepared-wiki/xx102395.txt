[[Venta River]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of Lithuania, Rivers of Latvia, Venta River basin, International rivers of Europe

The Venta River (Latvian pronunciation [tpl]IPA-lv|ˈvænta|[/tpl], Lithuanian [tpl]IPA-lt|venˈta||venta.ogg[/tpl], [tpl]Lang-de|Windau[/tpl], [tpl]Lang-pl|Windawa[/tpl], Livonian Vǟnta joug) is a river in north-western Lithuania and western Latvia. Its source is near Kuršėnai in the Lithuanian Šiauliai County. It flows into the Baltic Sea at Ventspils in Latvia. 
Cities along the Venta include Mažeikiai (Lithuania), Kuldīga and Ventspils (Latvia). It has only one tributary longer than 100 km, the Abava. The tributary Virvyčia is 99.7 km long. Another tributary Varduva is 96 km long and flows into Venta at the Lithuanian-Latvian border. 
Smaller tributaries include the Avižlys, which runs for 20 kilometers and flows into the Venta River[tpl]lt icon[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=LIETUVOS RESPUBLIKOS UPIŲ IR TVENKINIŲ KLASIFIKATORIUS (Republic of Lithuania- River and Pond Classifications)|url=http://gamta.lt/files/LR_upiu_klasifikatorius.pdf|publisher=Ministry of Environment (Lithuania)|accessdate=2011-11-03[/tpl] at Akmenė district municipality, Šiauliai County, northern Lithuania.
