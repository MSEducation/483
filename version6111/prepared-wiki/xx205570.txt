[[Autumn Ringlet]]

CATEGORIES: Erebia, Insects of Italy

The Autumn Ringlet (Erebia neoridas) is a member of the Satyridae subfamily of Nymphalidae.It is a high mountain butterfly found in the Pyrenees, Alps and in Italy.
