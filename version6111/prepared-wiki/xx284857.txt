[[Jay Gordon (doctor)]]

CATEGORIES: 1948 births, Living people, American pediatricians, University of California, Los Angeles faculty

Jay N. Gordon (born 1948) is an American author and celebrity pediatrician.

==Biography==

Gordon is an author and celebrity pediatrician. He became a member of the International Health Advisory Council of the La Leche League in 2005[tpl]cite web|url=http://web.archive.org/web/20051222103744/http://www.llli.org/hac.html |title=LLLI Health Advisory Counci |publisher=Web.archive.org |date= |accessdate=2013-08-18[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.llli.org/hac.html |title=Health Advisory Council |publisher=LLLI |date=2011-05-20 |accessdate=2013-08-18[/tpl] and is a UCLA Associate Professor of Pediatrics.[tpl]cite web | url=http://newsroom.ucla.edu/portal/ucla/PRN-ucla-headlines-july-22-2008-53792.aspx | title=UCLA Headlines July 22, 2008 | publisher=UCLA | date=July 22, 2008 | accessdate=August 29, 2013 | author=Staff Writer[/tpl]
Gordon is the author and co-author of several books on children including: "Brighter Baby" (1999)[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.regnery.com/books/brighter-baby/ |title=Brighter Baby |publisher=Regnery.com |date= |accessdate=2013-08-18[/tpl]  Listening to your baby : a new approach to parenting your newborn (2002), Good nights : the happy parents' guide to the family bed, and a peaceful night's sleep! (2002), The ADD and ADHD cure : the natural way to treat hyperactivity and refocus your child (2008), 
and "Preventing Autism: What You Can Do to Protect Your Children Before and After Birth" in 2013.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.worldcat.org/search?qt=worldcat_org_all&q=jay+Gordon | title=Search results for Jay Gordon | publisher=World Cat | accessdate=August 31, 2013 | author=Unknown author[/tpl] In 2008 he published a DVD called Vaccinations? : assessing the risks and benefits.
Gordon's patients include Jenny McCarthy's son Evan[tpl]cite web | url=http://transcripts.cnn.com/TRANSCRIPTS/0804/02/lkl.01.html | title=Jenny McCarthy's Autism Fight | publisher=CNN | date=2 April 2008 | accessdate=3 September 2013[/tpl] and Gordon appeared with McCarthy on an episode of Larry King Live in July 2008  to discuss the issue of children's vaccinations. He also appeared with McCarthy at the "Green Our Vaccines," rally[tpl]cite web | url=http://blogs.plos.org/thepanicvirus/2013/07/15/a-jenny-mccarthy-reader-pt-3-jenny-legitimizes-the-scientific-fringe/ | title=A Jenny McCarthy reader, Pt. 3: Jenny legitimizes the scientific fringe | publisher=Public Library of Science | date=15 July 2013 | accessdate=14 August 2013 | author=Mnookin, Seth[/tpl]
