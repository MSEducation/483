[[Contracorriente]]

CATEGORIES: Eiza González albums, 2009 debut albums

Contracorriente ("Countercurrent" in English) is the debut solo album by Mexican singer and actress, Eiza González.http://www.terra.com.mx/articulo.aspx?articuloId=777944&ref=1http://mundo52.com/musica/eiza-gonzalez-tras-los-pasos-de-miley-cyrus It was released on November 24, 2009, through EMI Televisa Music. The album was recorded in Los Angeles, California, Texas, and Mexico City during 2008 and early 2009.
