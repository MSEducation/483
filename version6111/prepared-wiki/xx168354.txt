[[Miloslav Konopka]]

CATEGORIES: 1979 births, Living people, Slovak hammer throwers, Athletes (track and field) at the 2000 Summer Olympics, Athletes (track and field) at the 2004 Summer Olympics, Athletes (track and field) at the 2008 Summer Olympics, Olympic athletes of Slovakia, People from Rimavská Sobota

Miloslav Konopka (born 23 January 1979 in Rimavská Sobota) is a male hammer thrower from Slovakia. His personal best throw is 81.33 metres, achieved in May 2004 in Banská Bystrica.
