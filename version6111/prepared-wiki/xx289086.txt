[[Gol Bolagh, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Gol Bolagh ([tpl]lang-fa|گل بلاغ[/tpl], also Romanized as Gol Bolāgh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795511|Gol Bolagh[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 59, in 13 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
