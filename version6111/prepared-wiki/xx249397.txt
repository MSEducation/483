[[2010 Chonburi F.C. season]]

CATEGORIES: Chonburi F.C. seasons, Thai football clubs 2010 season

The 2010 season was Chonburi's 5th season consecutive season in the top division of Thai football. This article shows statistics of the club's players in the season, and also lists all matches that the club played in the season.

==Team kit==

The team kit for the 2010 season is produced by FBT and Chang Beer remain as the main sponsor.
