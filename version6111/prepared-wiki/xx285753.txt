[[Bertram Hughes]]

CATEGORIES: 1925 births, 2012 deaths, Alumni of the University of Wales, Lampeter, Archdeacons of Carmarthen

Evan Arthur Bertram Hughes (1925Crockford's Clerical Directory 1980-82 London, OUP, 1983 ISBN 0192000101 p 499 -2012Church Times) was Archdeacon of Carmarthen from 1985 until 1991.
Hughes was educated at St David's College, Lampeter and ordained in 1950. After curacies in Abergwili and Llanelly he became a CMS missionary in  India. He was Archdeacon of Bhagalpur from 1965 to 1966; and then of Patna from 1966 to 1969.
