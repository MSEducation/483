[[Sucha Rzeczka]]

Sucha Rzeczka [tpl]IPAc-pl|'|s|u|h|a|-|'|ż|e|cz|k|a[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Płaska, within Augustów County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland, close to the border with Belarus.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) – TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|3|km|mi|0[/tpl] west of Płaska, [tpl]convert|18|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] east of Augustów, and [tpl]convert|87|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of the regional capital Białystok.
