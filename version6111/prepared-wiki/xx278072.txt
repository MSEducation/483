[[1987–88 Welsh Cup]]

CATEGORIES: Welsh Cup seasons, 1987–88 domestic association football cups, 1987–88 in Welsh football, Cardiff City A.F.C. matches

The 1987-88 Welsh Cup winners were Cardiff City. The final was played at the Vetch Field in Swansea in front of an attendance of 5,465.
