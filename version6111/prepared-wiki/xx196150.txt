[[Natalya Strunnikova]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Swimmers at the 1980 Summer Olympics, Olympic swimmers of the Soviet Union, Olympic bronze medalists for the Soviet Union, Olympic medalists in swimming, European Aquatics Championships medalists in swimming

[tpl]MedalCountry | the [tpl]URS[/tpl] [/tpl]
Natalya Strunnikova is a bronze medalist in the 1980 Summer Olympics in the women's 4 x 100 meter medley relay. Natalya was born on March 14, 1964, in Sverdlovsk, Russia.
