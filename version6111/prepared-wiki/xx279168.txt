[[Chavor]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Chavor ([tpl]lang-fa|چاور[/tpl], also Romanized as Chāvor; also known as Chāveh, Chīr, Chūvīr, Chuwir, and Jāvor)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3058210|Chavor[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 16, in 5 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
