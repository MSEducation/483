[[Journal of Immunology]]

CATEGORIES: Immunology journals, Publications established in 1915, Biweekly journals, English-language journals

The Journal of Immunology is a peer-reviewed academic journal that publishes basic and clinical studies in all aspects of immunology. It was founded by Arthur Fernandez Coca in 1915 (in cooperation with Edward B. Passano).In Memoriam of Arthur F. Coca, Founder of the Journal of Immunology, J.Immun., 85/1960 (page 331) It is the official journal of The American Association of Immunologists and published by The American Association of Immunologists.
