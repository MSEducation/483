[[Commodity price shocks]]

CATEGORIES: International economics, Commodity markets

Commodity price shocks are times when the prices for commodities have increased suddenly. http://scholar.google.com/scholar?hl=en&q=commodity%20price%20shocks&um=1&ie=UTF-8&sa=N&tab=ws  

==1971–1973==

At the time of the 1973 oil crisis the price of corn and wheat went up by a factor of three.

==2005–2008==

The 2007–2008 world food price crisis has seen corn, wheat, and rice go up by a factor of three when measured in US dollars.
