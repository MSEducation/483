[[Knarrlagsund]]

CATEGORIES: Hitra, Villages in Sør-Trøndelag

Knarrlagsund or Knarrlaget is a village on the island of Ulvøya in the municipality of Hitra in Sør-Trøndelag county, Norway.  It is located on the southern part of the island about [tpl]convert|2.5|km[/tpl] east of Ulvan.
The village lies along the Knarrlagsundet strait which separates the islands of Ulvøya and Fjellværsøya.  There is a bridge across the strait at Knarrlagsund.
