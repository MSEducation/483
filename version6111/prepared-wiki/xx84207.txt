[[Ogawa, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Ogawa|小川村|Ogawa-mura[/tpl] is a village located in Kamiminochi District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
[tpl]As of|2003[/tpl], the village has an estimated population of 3,480 and a density of 59.93 persons per km². The total area is 58.07 km².
