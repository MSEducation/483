[[Adam Donovan]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Year of birth missing (living people), People from Victoria (Australia), Augie March, Australian indie pop musicians, Australian indie rock musicians

Adam Donovan comes from Shepparton, Victoria, and is one of the original members in the Australian band, Augie March. He attended St Brendans Primary school Shepparton and Notre Dame High school, Shepparton.
He attended a music course at Northern Melbourne Institute of TAFE along with fellow Augie March founding members Edmondo Ammendola and David Williams.NMIT Music Department, Music Performance Alumni, Accessed 11 October 2008
