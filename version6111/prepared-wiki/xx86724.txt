[[Fallacy of distribution]]

CATEGORIES: Logical fallacies

A fallacy of distribution is an informal fallacy occurring when an argument assumes there is no difference between a term in the distributive (referring to every member of a class) and collective (referring to the class itself as a whole) sense.
