[[Haruhiko Mikimoto]]

CATEGORIES: 1959 births, Living people, Anime character designers, Japanese illustrators, Manga artists, Macross, Japanese animators, People from Tokyo, Manga artists from Tokyo, Keio University alumni

[tpl]nihongo|Haruhiko Mikimoto|美樹本 晴彦|Mikimoto Haruhiko|born [tpl]nihongo|Haruhiko Satō||佐藤晴彦|Satō Haruhiko[/tpl], August 28, 1959 in Tokyo, Japan[/tpl] is a Japanese anime character designer, illustrator and manga artist. Mostly active during the 1980s, during that decade he rose to prominence and is considered one of the top character designers of his time.
He graduated from Keio University, and attended the university in the same years as Macross creator Shōji Kawamori and screenwriter Hiroshi Ōnogi. He joined the animation studio Artland while attending school, and was character designer in The Super Dimension Fortress Macross.
