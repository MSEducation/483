[[South Branch Mad River]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of Maine, Rivers of New Hampshire, Saco River

The South Branch of the Mad River is a 1.1 mile long (1.7 km)New Hampshire GRANIT state geographic information system mountain brook on the Maine-New Hampshire border in the United States, within the eastern White Mountains.  It is a tributary of the Mad River, a short feeder of the Cold River, part of the Saco River watershed.
The South Branch, beginning in New Hampshire and finishing in Maine, flows east off the slopes of West Royce Mountain.  It joins the Mad River less than 0.l mile above that river's end at the Cold River in the floor of Evans Notch.
