[[Rick Parashar]]

CATEGORIES: American record producers, Living people

Rick Parashar is a music producer who came to prominence in the early 1990s through his work with grunge bands such as Pearl Jam,[tpl]cite book|last=Williams|first=Paul|title=Back to the Miracle Factory: Rock Etc. 1990s|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=COfUbzOvmmkC&pg=PT96|accessdate=29 January 2011|year=2003|publisher=Macmillan|isbn=978-0-7653-0353-0|page=96[/tpl] Alice in Chains, and Blind Melon.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.mtv.com/music/artist/blind_melon/artist.jhtml|title=Blind Melon|last=Prato|first=Greg|publisher=MTV|accessdate=29 January 2011[/tpl] Parashar co-founded London Bridge Studio with his brother, Raj.[tpl]cite web|url=http://mixonline.com/recording/business/audio_year_london_bridge/|title=A Year at London Bridge|last=Schultz|first=Barbara|date=1 February 2007|work=Mix|accessdate=29 January 2011[/tpl] Parashar currently resides in a house in Seattle's upper Queen Anne neighborhood.
