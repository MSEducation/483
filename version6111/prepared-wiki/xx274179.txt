[[Trio Music Live in Europe]]

CATEGORIES: ECM Records albums, Chick Corea albums, 1986 albums

Trio Music Live in Europe is a live album by pianist Chick Corea with bassist Miroslav Vitous and drummer Roy Haynes recorded in Switzerland in 1984 and released on the ECM label.ECM discography accessed September 26, 2011 

==Reception==

The Allmusic review by Michael G. Nastos awarded the album 4 stars stating "Close to perfect, undeniably strong, willful, and musical beyond compare, Trio Music, Live in Europe belongs in your collection, and is simply as good as modern progressive mainstream jazz gets".Nastos, M. G. Allmusic Review accessed September 26, 2011
