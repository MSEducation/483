[[Gerry Hurley]]

CATEGORIES: Irish rugby union players, Rugby union scrum-halves, Garryowen Football Club players, Munster Rugby players, 1984 births, Living people, Sportspeople from County Cork

Gerry Hurley (born 16 May 1984 in Midleton, County Cork, Ireland) is an Irish rugby union player for Munster. He plays as a Scrum-half. Hurley won a Munster Schools Rugby Senior Cup title with Rockwell College.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.munsterrugby.ie/news/1903.php#.UpDSml9FCM8|title=Introducing Gerry Hurley|date=6 December 2007|work=munsterrugby.ie|accessdate=23 November 2013[/tpl] He plays his club rugby with Cork Constitution.

==Garryowen==

Hurley won the AIB League and Cup with Garryowen Football Club.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.munsterrugby.ie/news/1903.php#.UpDSml9FCM8|title=Introducing Gerry Hurley|date=6 December 2007|work=munsterrugby.ie|accessdate=23 November 2013[/tpl]

==Cork Constitution==

Hurley captained his club side Cork Constitution to the Ulster Bank Bateman Cup on 27 April 2013.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.munsterrugby.ie/domestic/news/11886.php|title=Bateman Cup Final Victory For Cork Constitution|date=27 April 2013|work=munsterrugby.ie|accessdate=23 November 2013[/tpl]

==Munster==

Whilst still in the Munster Academy, Hurley made his first senior start for Munster in a pre-season friendly against the USA on 26 August 2007.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.munsterrugby.ie/news/2143.php#.UpDPc19FCM8|title=Hurley Among The Munster New Caps|date=24 August 2007|work=munsterrugby.ie|accessdate=23 November 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.munsterrugby.ie/news/2161.php#.UpDP9l9FCM8|title=Final Score Toyota Park|date=26 August 2013|work=munsterrugby.ie|accessdate=23 November 2013[/tpl] Hurley made his Magners League debut as a substitute in Munster's 26-26 win against Scarlets on 22 September 2007.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.munsterrugby.ie/rugby/286.php|title=Munster See Off Scarlets|date=22 September 2007|work=munsterrugby.ie|accessdate=23 November 2013[/tpl] His Heineken Cup debut came against Clermont Auvergne on 18 November 2007, with Hurley coming on as a substitute in the 36-13 win in Thomond Park.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.munsterrugby.ie/rugby/268.php|title=Bonus Point Win For Munster|date=18 November 2007|work=munsterrugby.ie|accessdate=23 November 2013[/tpl] Hurley graduated from the Munster Academy at the end of the 2007â€“08 season, but did not receive a contract.
Hurley started for Munster in an uncapped friendly against La Rochelle on 12 August 2011.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.munsterrugby.ie/rugby/9256.php|title=Munster Leave Themselves Too Much To Do|date=12 August 2013|work=munsterrugby.ie|accessdate=23 November 2013[/tpl]
Despite not earning a contract when he graduated from the Munster Academy in 2008, Hurley signed a one-year training contract with Munster in July 2013.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.munsterrugby.ie/news/12081.php#.UpDRx19FCM9|title=Hurley Back On Board|date=19 July 2013|work=munsterrugby.ie|accessdate=23 November 2013[/tpl] He came off the bench during Munster's Pro 12 game against Connacht on 27 December 2013, his first appearance for Munster in 6 years.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.munsterrugby.ie/rugby/12562.php|title=Hanrahan Stars As Munster 8 Points Clear|date=27 December 2013|work=munsterrugby.ie|accessdate=28 December 2013[/tpl] He was added to Munster's 2013-14 Heineken Cup squad on 20 March 2014.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.munsterrugby.ie/news/18138.php|title=Trio Nominated For Heineken Cup Squad|date=20 March 2014|work=munsterrugby.ie|accessdate=21 March 2014[/tpl]

==Ireland==

Hurley captained the Ireland Club XV side against England Counties XV to a 30-20 victory on 8 February 2013.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.irishrugby.ie/rugby/28329.php|title=Ireland Club Team Triumphant In Cork|date=8 February 2013|work=irishrugby.ie|accessdate=23 November 2013[/tpl] He also captained the side during their 30-18 defeat to Scotland Clubs on 8 March 2013.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.irishrugby.ie/rugby/28618.php|title=Second Half Comeback Not Enough For Ireland Club Team|date=8 March 2013|work=munsterrugby.ie|accessdate=23 November 2013[/tpl]
