[[Oberstrass]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Zürich, Former municipalities of the canton of Zürich

Oberstrass is a quarter in the district 6 in Zurich.
It was formerly a municipality of its own, having been incorporated into Zurich in 1893.
The quarter has a population of 9,494 distributed on an area of 2.64 km².
