[[Mount Olivet Cemetery (Halifax)]]

CATEGORIES: Cemeteries in the Halifax Regional Municipality, Roman Catholic cemeteries in Canada

Mount Olivet Cemetery is a Roman Catholic cemetery located in Halifax, Nova Scotia, Canada at which 19 bodies recovered from the RMS Titanic are buried.
Many of the dead from the 1917 Halifax Explosion are also buried here, including Vincent Coleman, the heroric railway dispatcher who sent warning of the explosion.

==War Graves==

The Commonwealth War Graves Commission maintains 78 war graves in this cemetery. There are 64 Commonwealth service personnel buried here from World War I and 10 from World War II.CWGC Cemetery Report[tpl]dead link|date=May 2013[/tpl]
