[[Le Lys]]

CATEGORIES: Russian ballet, Ballets by Ludwig Minkus, Ballets by Arthur Saint-Léon, 1869 ballet premieres

Le Lys (The Lily), also known as Liliya, is a ballet in 3 acts/4 scenes, with choreography by Arthur Saint-Léon and music by Ludwig (Léon) Minkus. 
The ballet was first presented by the Imperial Ballet on [tpl]OldStyleDate|2 November|1869|21 October[/tpl], at the Imperial Bolshoi Kamenny Theatre in St. Petersburg, Russia.
Principal dancers at the première included Adèle Grantzow.
