[[3975 Verdi]]

CATEGORIES: Koronis asteroids, Asteroids named for people, Discoveries by Freimut Börngen, Astronomical objects discovered in 1982, Main Belt asteroids, Giuseppe Verdi

3975 Verdi is a small main belt asteroid. It was discovered by Freimut Börngen in 1982 and is named after the Italian composer Giuseppe Verdi.
