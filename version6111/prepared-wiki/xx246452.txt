[[Ed, Edd n Eddy (season 5)]]

CATEGORIES: 2005 television seasons, 2006 television seasons, 2007 television seasons, Ed, Edd n Eddy seasons

The fifth season of the animated comedy series Ed, Edd n Eddy aired on Cartoon Network from October 1, 2005, to March 11, 2007, and consists of 12 episodes. The series revolves around three adolescent boys collectively known as "the Eds," who hang around in a suburban cul-de-sac. Unofficially led by Eddy, the Eds constantly scheme to make money off their peers in order to purchase their favorite confectionery; jawbreakers. However, their plans usually fail, leaving them in various predicaments.
The voice cast remained the same as in season four, featuring Matt Hill, Samuel Vincent and Tony Sampson as Ed, Double D and Eddy, David Paul Grove as Jonny 2x4, Keenan Christenson as Jimmy, Janyse Jaud as Sarah and Lee Kanker, Kathleen Barr as Kevin and Marie, Peter Kelamis as Rolf, and Erin Fitzgerald as Nazz and May Kanker. Unlike the first four seasons, none of season five's episodes were released on home video. However, the season is available for purchase on the iTunes Store.

==Episodes==

Note: All episodes directed by Danny Antonucci, except "Smile for the Ed", which was directed by Scott Underwood.
