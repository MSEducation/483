[[Lolita Go Home]]

CATEGORIES: Jane Birkin albums, 1975 albums, Fontana Records albums

Lolita Go Home is an album by Jane Birkin, released in 1975. About half of the songs were written by Serge Gainsbourg (music) and Philippe Labro (words). The other half are old musical tunes.

==Track listing==

Music by Serge Gainsbourg, words by Philippe Labro; except where indicated
Arranged and conducted by Jean-Pierre Sabar
