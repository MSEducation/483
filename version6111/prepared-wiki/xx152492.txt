[[Leira, Oppland]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Oppland

Leira is a village in Nord-Aurdal municipality, Norway. Its population is 792.[tpl]cite paper | author=Statistics Norway | title=Urban settlements. Population and area, by municipality. 1 January 2006 | date=2006 | url=http://www.ssb.no/english/subjects/02/01/10/beftett_en/tab-2006-07-05-01-en.html[/tpl]

==The name==

The place is named after the river Leira. The name of the river is derived from leire 'clay'. Leira is a common name of rivers many places in Norway.
