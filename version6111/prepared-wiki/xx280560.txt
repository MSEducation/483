[[N. Siki Reddy]]

CATEGORIES: Indian badminton players, Indian Badminton Champions, Living people, 1993 births

N. Siki Reddy (born 17 October 1990) is an Indian badminton player who currently plays doubles and mixed doubles.  She partners Aparna Balan for women's doubles events and previously partnered with P. C. Thulasi.Doubles Parners
For mixed doubles events she partners with Manu Attri, and previously, Pranaav Jerry Chopra.Mixed Doubles Partners
