[[Janiya]]

CATEGORIES: Hebrew-language names

Janiya or Jania is an uncommon female given name, of Hebrew origin, and its meaning is from Jana. The name may be Arabic, unsure, and could possible be related to name Jana, Jane, Janie. Names that sound similar are Tania and Taniya.
Janiya is also a village in Arabia. See Al-Janiya. It can be pronounced JAH-NIY-AH or Jah-nee-ah.
