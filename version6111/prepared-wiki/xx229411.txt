Mark Owen Lee (born July 20, 1964) is a former Major League Baseball pitcher. Lee pitched in all or part of four seasons between [tpl]By|1988[/tpl] and [tpl]By|1995[/tpl].  As a minor leaguer he was traded with Rey Palacios from the Detroit Tigers to the Kansas City Royals for Ted Power during the 1988 season.[tpl]cite news|url=http://news.google.com/newspapers?id=0WoWAAAAIBAJ&sjid=uRIEAAAAIBAJ&pg=6182,63584&dq=baseball+ted-power&hl=en|title=Sports in brief|date=1 September 1988|work=Milwaukee Sentinel|publisher=AP|page=4|accessdate=7 June 2010[/tpl]
