[[Moscow, Virginia]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Virginia

Moscow is a small village about [tpl]convert|8|mi|0[/tpl] south of Bridgewater, Virginia located on route 42 with only a few residents left. It consists of a Brethren church, elementary school named North River Elementary (for surrounding area), cabinet shop, a couple houses, and a gas and grocery store. This is mostly a business area for the Mount Solon, Virginia area.
North River High School was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1985.[tpl]NRISref|version=2010a[/tpl]
