Frank Hubert Copp (February 2, 1881 â€“ 1959) was a manufacturer and political figure in New Brunswick, Canada. He represented Westmorland County in the Legislative Assembly of New Brunswick as a Liberal member from 1935 to 1948.
He was born in Bristol, New Brunswick, the son of Adam Bliss Copp and Sarah Jane Oulton. He was educated at Mount Allison University. In 1903, he married Bertha Copp. Copp owned woollen mills and was also involved in fox ranching.
