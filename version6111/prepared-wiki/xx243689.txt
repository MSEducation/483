[[Samuel Muturi Mugo]]

CATEGORIES: 1986 births, Living people, Male long-distance runners, Kenyan long-distance runners, Kenyan marathon runners

Samuel Muturi Mugo (born 2 May 1986) is a Kenyan athlete who specialises in long distance running, particularly the marathon. He also competes under the name Sammy Mugo.
Mugo moved to Japan to become a professional athlete and began participating in senior competitions in 2003. He started out as a 5000 and 10,000 metres track runner and finished in the top three in the Shizuoka International 10,000 m from 2003–2005, improving his best time every year.10,000 Metres 2003.
