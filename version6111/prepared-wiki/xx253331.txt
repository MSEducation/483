[[Edi Expedition]]

CATEGORIES: Aceh, Dutch conquest of Indonesia, History of Sumatra

The Edi-expedition (Dutch:Edi-expeditie) was a Dutch expedition in 1890 to Edi, a small state on the east coast of Aceh .
