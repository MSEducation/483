[[Whiz Kids (album)]]

CATEGORIES: ECM Records albums, Gary Burton albums, Albums produced by Manfred Eicher, 1987 albums

Whiz Kids is an album by vibraphonist Gary Burton's Quintet recorded in 1986 and released on the ECM label.ECM discography accessed September 27, 2011 

==Reception==

The Allmusic review by Scott Yanow awarded the album 4 stars stating "The repertoire (all obscurities) and post-bop solos have more fire than one would normally expect on a Gary Burton record, and there are plenty of colorful moments on this subtle but adventurous set".Yanow, S. Allmusic Review accessed September 19, 2011
