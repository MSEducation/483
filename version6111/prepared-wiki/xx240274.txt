[[Michel Cury Neto]]

Michel Cury Neto (born 24 July 1981) is a Brazilian football midfielder who currently plays for Araxá.
He started his career in Cruzeiro Esporte Clube, where he played together with Maicon and Luisão.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Denník Šport|author=Róbert Čapla|title=Imponuje mu Jánošík!|url=http://www.denniksport.sk/article/111128/|date=2009-10-31[/tpl] He played one season for FC Spartak Trnava in the Slovak Super Liga.
