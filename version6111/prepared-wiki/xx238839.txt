[[Sovnya]]

CATEGORIES: Pole weapons, Blade weapons, Russian inventions

A sovnya is a traditional weapon used in Russia, much like the Japanese Naginata, resembling a curved sword on the end of a long pole. This was a popular weapon with late-mediaeval Muscovite cavalry and retained use until mid-17th century.
