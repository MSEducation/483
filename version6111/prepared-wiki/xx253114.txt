[[Insarsky District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Mordovia

Insarsky District ([tpl]lang-ru|Инса́рский райо́н[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-mdf|Инзаронь район[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-myv|Инесаро буе[/tpl]) is an administrativeConstitution of the Republic of Mordovia and municipalLaw #119-Z district (raion), one of the twenty-two in the Republic of Mordovia, Russia. It is located in the south of the republic. The area of the district is [tpl]convert|968.6|km2|sp=us[/tpl].[tpl]citation needed|date=October 2012[/tpl] Its administrative center is the town of Insar.Law #7-Z Population: [tpl]ru-census2010|14,098|;[/tpl] 15,909 (2002 Census);[tpl]ru-pop-ref|2002Census[/tpl] [tpl]su-census1989|29,638|.[/tpl] The population of Insar accounts for 61.6% of the district's total population.
