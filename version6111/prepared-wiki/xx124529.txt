[[Tugu Keris]]

CATEGORIES: Buildings and structures in Selangor, Monuments and memorials in Malaysia, Visitor attractions in Selangor

Tugu Keris (Kris Monument) is a huge monument in the shape of a kris in Klang, Selangor, Malaysia. It is located along the Federal Highway (Federal route [tpl]JKR|2[/tpl]) near Taman Kris and Sungai Rasau toll plaza. Its construction was ordered by the late Sultan of Selangor, Almarhum Sultan Salahuddin Abdul Aziz Shah to commemorate the Silver Jubilee of his reign on 22 September 1985.
