==Design==

The exterior of the building was designed by Lyndon Stromberg who also worked on the The Venetian and the MGM Grand in Las Vegas as well as the Atlantis Paradise Island Resort in the Bahamas and Planet Hollywood in New York City. The long façade exterior of WinStar World Casino features replicas of London's Parliament Building, Rome's Coliseum and the Arc de Triomphe from Paris.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.strombergarchitectural.com/projects/winstar-world-casino|title=Stromberg Architectural Project Profile: WinStar World Casino[/tpl]

==Attractions==

WinStar World Casino is home to the 12-story, 395-room WinStar World Casino Hotel that’s adjacent to the gaming floor. The hotel which opened on August 6, 2009, held a grand opening celebration that featured performances by American Idol Winner Kelly Clarkson and American rock band Lynyrd Skynyrd. Also available are meetings rooms that can accommodate groups of 10 to 200, a resort-style pool with a custom-built waterfall grotto, an award winning spa, and Cloverleaf Boutique gift shop.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.tripadvisor.com/Hotel_Review-g51690-d1508149-Reviews-Winstar_World_Casino_Hotel-Thackerville_Oklahoma.html |title=TripAdvisor:WinStar World Casino Hotel, Thackerville[/tpl] WinStar World Casino Hotel Tower Two is expected to open in Fall 2013. Tower Three is expected to open 6 months later.

==WinStar Golf Club==

The WinStar Golf Club features a 225-acre, 27-hole golf course designed by former PGA Tour Pro, D.A. Weibring which holds elite golf tournaments each year.  In addition, the WinStar Golf Academy, a [tpl]convert|4850|sqft|m2|adj=on[/tpl] facility, offers private golf lessons and clinics by world class instructors. Practice areas include an outdoor grass putting range, 2 indoor/outdoor heated hitting bays, 8 covered outdoor hitting bays, and 2 PGA Tour AboutGolf simulating studios. The facility is also home to a Science and Motion putter fitting center and practice studio, custom club fitting center, and a golf ball fitting studio. Additional amenities include a fitness room, mindfulness and stretching rooms, the Academy lounge and recreation room, and locker rooms and showers. Visitors are also able to shop at a brand-sponsored pro shop.[tpl]cite web|url=http://winstar.rexacademies.com/pages/golf-academy/facilities |title=Facilities - Winstar Golf Club |publisher=Winstar.rexacademies.com |date= |accessdate=2013-11-23[/tpl]

==WinStar RV Park==

WinStar RV park is a 200-space RV Park that is available to people visiting the casino. A shuttle service to the casino is available to guests in the RV Park.

==Dining==

==Bull riding==

In 2011, the Professional Bull Riders brought their Built Ford Tough Series bull riding tour to the WinStar World Casino with an event at an outdoor arena constructed near the casino itself. The event was sponsored by WinStar World Casino and called the WinStar World Casino Invitational. It was the first outdoor event on the BFTS since 2005.
