[[Dmytro Oleksandrovych Voloshyn]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Ukrainian footballers, 1986 births

Dmytro Oleksandrovych Voloshyn ([tpl]lang-uk|Дмитро Олександрович Волошин[/tpl]; born 29 April 1986) is a Ukrainian football defender currently playing for Veikkausliiga club IFK Mariehamn.

==Club history==

Dmytro Voloshyn began his football career in Olimpik-UOR youth club in Donetsk.
