[[Saidabad-e Sofla, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Saidabad-e Sofla ([tpl]lang-fa|سعيدابادسفلي[/tpl], also Romanized as Sa‘īdābād-e Soflá and Sa‘id Abad Sofla; also known as Safīdābād-e Soflá and Sa‘īdābād-e Pā’īn)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3081924|Saidabad-e Sofla[/tpl] is a village in Saidabad Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 222, in 74 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
