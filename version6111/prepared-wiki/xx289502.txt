[[Amu Kandi]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Amu Kandi ([tpl]lang-fa|عموكندي[/tpl], also Romanized as ‘Amū Kandī)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3773067|Amu Kandi[/tpl] is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 268, in 51 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
