[[Hallam railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1880

Hallam railway station is located on the Pakenham line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the south-eastern Melbourne suburb of Hallam opening on 1 December 1880 as Hallam's Road being renamed Hallam on 2 May 1904.Hallam Vicsig

==Platforms & services==

Hallam has two side platforms. It is serviced by Metro Trains' Pakenham line services.Pakenham Line timetable Public Transport Victoria
