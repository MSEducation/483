José Augusto Santana dos Santos (born 14 March 1977 in Rosário do Catete, Sergipe), aka Guga, is a Brazilian retired footballer who played as an attacking midfielder.
Spent most of his professional career in Portugal, representing five clubs over the course of eight top division seasons (11 years in total).
