[[Luiz Trainini]]

CATEGORIES: 1978 births, Living people, Brazilian archers, Archers at the 2008 Summer Olympics, Archers at the 2011 Pan American Games, Olympic archers of Brazil, People from Rio Grande do Sul

Luiz Gustavo Trainini (born January 14, 1978 in Canoas) is an athlete from Brazil, who competes in archery.

==2008 Summer Olympics==

At the 2008 Summer Olympics in Beijing Trainini finished his ranking round with a total of 610 points, which gave him the 61st seed for the final competition bracket in which he faced Park Kyung-Mo in the first round. Park won the match by 116-99 and Trainini was eliminated. Park would go on to win the silver medal in the tournament.Athlete biography: Luiz Trainini, beijing2008.cn, ret: Aug, 19 2008
