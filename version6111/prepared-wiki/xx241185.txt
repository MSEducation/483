[[Horqin District]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Horqin District (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠷᠴᠢᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠲᠣᠭᠣᠷᠢᠭ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Qorčin toɣoriɣ[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=科尔沁区 |t=科爾沁區 |p=Kē'ěrqìn Qū[/tpl]) is a district of the city of Tongliao, Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China. The local Mongolian dialect is Khorchin Mongolian.
