Stara Kamionka [tpl]IPAc-pl|'|s|t|a|r|a|-|k|a|'|m|j|o|N|k|a[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Bargłów Kościelny, within Augustów County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) – TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|4|km|mi|0[/tpl] north of Bargłów Kościelny, [tpl]convert|13|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-west of Augustów, and [tpl]convert|80|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of the regional capital Białystok.
The village has a population of 280.
