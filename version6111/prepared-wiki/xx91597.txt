The Faroe Islands, or Faroe(s), ([tpl]lang-fo|Føroyar[/tpl], [tpl]lang-da|Færøerne[/tpl]) are an island group situated between the Norwegian Sea and the North Atlantic Ocean, approximately halfway between Scotland and Iceland. The Faroe Islands are an autonomous country of the Danish Realm.[tpl]cite web |url=https://www.cia.gov/library/publications/the-world-factbook/geos/fo.html |title=CIA World Factbook, Faroe Island |publisher=Central Intelligence Agency |accessdate=2010-07-15[/tpl]
