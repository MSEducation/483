[[Acer sterculiaceum]]

CATEGORIES: Acer, Plants described in 1830

Acer sterculiaceum (Franchet’s maple, Himalayan maple, 苹婆枫) is a species of maple tree indigenous to Bhutan, China and India that grows at altitudes of [tpl]convert|1800|-|3100|m[/tpl].
