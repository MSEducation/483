[[San Nicolás de Carretas]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Chihuahua, Populated places established in 1688

 
San Nicolás de Carretas is a town in the Mexican state of Chihuahua. It serves as the municipal seat for the surrounding municipality of Gran Morelos.
San Nicolás de Carretas was founded as a Franciscan mission in 1688.[tpl]cite web|title=Gran Morelos|url=http://www.e-local.gob.mx/work/templates/enciclo/chihuahua/Mpios/08026a.htm|work=Enciclopedia de los Municipios de México|publisher=Instituto Nacional para el Federalismo y el Desarrollo Municipal |accessdate=2008-10-05[/tpl]
