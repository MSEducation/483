[[2012 Pacific-12 Conference Men's Basketball Tournament]]

CATEGORIES: Pacific-12 Conference Men's Basketball Tournament, 2011–12 Pacific-12 Conference men's basketball season

The 2012 Pacific Life Pacific-12 Conference Men's Basketball Tournament was played on March 7–10, 2012 at the Staples Center in Los Angeles, California.[tpl]cite web|title=Men's Basketball TV Schedule Announced|url=http://www.pac-12.org/SPORTS/BasketballM/Tabid/1449/Article/136942/Mens-Basketball-TV-Schedule-Announced.aspx|work=pac12.org|publisher=Pacific 12 Conference|accessdate=October 13, 2011|date=September 16, 2011[/tpl] The tournament champion became the NCAA Tournament automatic qualifier from the conference. The pairings will be announced following the completion of the regular season on March 4, 2012. The first three rounds was all broadcast on FSN with the championship game on CBS.   The Pac-12 announced, on March 1, that Men's and Women's tournament games that were not televised would be streamed on YouTube.Pacific Life Pac-12 Basketball Tournament To Be Streamed On YouTube. March 1, 2012, Pac-12 Also streamed live on YouTube was a post-game press conferences for the semifinals and championship games. In its first season in the Pac-12, No. 6 seeded Colorado defeated No. 4 seeded Arizona 53–51 for the title and the automatic bid to the NCAA National Championship Tournament. Colorado (along with USC in 2009) has been the lowest seeded team ever to win in this tournament's history. Colorado also was the first team ever to win four games to become the champion of this tournament (because the 6th seed plays in the first of four rounds).

==Seeds==

Teams was seeded by conference record, with a tiebreaker system used to seed teams with identical conference records.[tpl]cite web|title=2012 PACIFIC LIFE PAC-12 BASKETBALL TOURNAMENT Bracket|url=http://pac-12.org/portals/7/images/pac-12/tournament/downloads/combined-bracket.pdf|publisher=pac 12|accessdate=February 9, 2012[/tpl]
