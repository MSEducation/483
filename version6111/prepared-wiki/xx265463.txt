[[Farm to Market Road 2576]]

CATEGORIES: Farm to Market Roads in Texas, Transportation in Crosby County, Texas

Farm to Market Road 2576 (FM 2576) is a farm-to-market road in Crosby County, Texas.

==Route description==

The southern terminus of FM 2576 is at an intersection with [tpl]jct|state=TX|FM|40|noshield=yes[/tpl] near the unincorporated community of Savage. The northern terminus is [tpl]convert|5.210|mi|km[/tpl] to the north, at an intersection with [tpl]jct|state=TX|US|62|US|82|SH|114|noshield=yes[/tpl] west of Ralls.[tpl]Texas Mapbook|216|accessdate=2001-01-05[/tpl]

==History==

A route with the designation FM 2576 previously existed in Jasper County, from 1959 to 1961. This route became a part of [tpl]jct|state=TX|FM|1131|noshield=yes[/tpl].[tpl]TxDOT|FM|1131|accessdate=2011-01-05[/tpl] The current designation was established in 1962.
