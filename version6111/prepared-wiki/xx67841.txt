[[Carson Range]]

CATEGORIES: Sierra Nevada (U.S.), Mountain ranges of Nevada, Mountain ranges of Northern California, Humboldt-Toiyabe National Forest, Mountain ranges of El Dorado County, California, Landforms of Douglas County, Nevada, Landforms of Washoe County, Nevada

The Carson Range is a spur of the Sierra Nevada in eastern California and western Nevada that starts at Carson Pass and goes to the Truckee River.  The Tahoe Rim Trail is in the range.
