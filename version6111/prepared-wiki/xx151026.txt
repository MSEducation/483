[[Union Sportive des Forces Armées]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Burkina Faso, Association football clubs established in 1962, Sport in Ouagadougou, 1962 establishments in Upper Volta

Union Sportive des Forces Armées is a Burkinabé football club based in Ouagadougou. They play their home games at the Stade de l'USFA. The club's colors are blue and red.
