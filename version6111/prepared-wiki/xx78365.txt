[[Tarnówka, Złotów County]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Złotów County

Tarnówka [tpl]IPAc-pl|t|a|r|'|n|u|f|k|a[/tpl] is a village in Złotów County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It is the seat of the gmina (administrative district) called Gmina Tarnówka.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|13|km|mi|0[/tpl] west of Złotów and [tpl]convert|105|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of the regional capital Poznań.
Before 1772 the area was part of Kingdom of Poland, 1772-1945 Prussia and Germany. For more on its history, see Złotów County.
The village has a population of 1,300.
