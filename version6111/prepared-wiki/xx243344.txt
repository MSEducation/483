[[Mattias Oscarsson]]

CATEGORIES: 1975 births, Canoeists at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Living people, Olympic canoeists of Sweden, Swedish canoeists

Peter Mattias Oscarsson (born April 12, 1975 in Västerås) is a Swedish sprint canoer who competed in the mid-1990s. He finished sixth in the K-4 1000 m event at the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta.
