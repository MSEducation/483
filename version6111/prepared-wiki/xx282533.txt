[[Kadhal Dhandapani]]

CATEGORIES: Indian male film actors, Living people

Kadhal Dhandapani is an Indian film actor who appears in Tamil, Telugu and Malayalam films. He rose to fame portraying the antagonist in Balaji Sakthivel's Kaadhal (2004), and is credited with the film's name as a prefix.[tpl]cite web|first=The Hindu|title=Rajendran|url=http://www.thehindu.com/todays-paper/tp-features/tp-cinemaplus/article932200.ece|publisher=The Hindu|accessdate=15 February 2012[/tpl][tpl]cite web|first=Joint Scene|title=Rajendran Biography|url=http://www.jointscene.com/artists/Kollywood/Rajendran/14529|publisher=Joint Scene|accessdate=15 February 2012[/tpl]
