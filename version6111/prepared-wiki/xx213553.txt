[[DXMA (Davao)]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the Philippines, Radio stations in Davao City, Radio stations established in 2006

104.3 The Edge Radio is a music FM radio station owned and operated by United Christian Broadcasters in the Philippines. Its studios are located at the 3rd Floor, NB Mercado Bldg., McArthur Highway cor. Sandawa Rd., Matina, Davao City.
