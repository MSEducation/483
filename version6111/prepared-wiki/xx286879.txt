[[Najm Sheykhan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Najm Sheykhan ([tpl]lang-fa|نجم الشيخان[/tpl], also Romanized as Najm Sheykhān; also known as Nāderchekhān, Nadirchikhan, Nadirchkhān, Najm esh Sheykhān, Najm osh Sheykhān, Naz̧m-e Sheykhān, and Naz̧m Sheykhān)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3076337|Najm Sheykhan[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 336, in 77 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
