[[EMPI B-school]]

CATEGORIES: Schools in Delhi, Business schools in Delhi

EMPI is an AICTE approved B-school situated in Delhi, India. It is located at CSKM Educational Institute, Satbari, Chattarpur, New Delhi.
It offers dual specialization in nine courses. It has completed years in 2010. It has been awarded Dainik Bhaskar Leadership Award for Best curriculum in Advertising to EMPI- AdCom.

==Facilities==

===Courses===

Advertising Communication, Finance, Human Resources and Organisation Behavior, Global Business, Integrated Marketing and Communication, Media Planning and Buying, Manufacturing and Quality managemnet,Research and Business Analytics
