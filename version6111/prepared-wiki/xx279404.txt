[[Colonial Creek Falls]]

CATEGORIES: Landforms of Whatcom County, Washington, North Cascades National Park, Waterfalls of Washington (state)

Colonial Creek Falls is the tallest waterfall in the continental United States.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.worldwaterfalldatabase.com/waterfall/Colonial-Creek-Falls-4738/ |title=Colonial Creek Falls |publisher=World Waterfall Database |date=September 18, 2011 |accessdate=August 11, 2013[/tpl] It is  [tpl]convert|2,584|ft|m|0[/tpl] tall. The falls traverse a total of over [tpl]convert|4,200|ft|m|-2[/tpl] in 13 distinct drops with an average incline of 65 degrees.
