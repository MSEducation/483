[[Afropsipyla pictella]]

CATEGORIES: Animals described in 1994, Phycitinae

Afropsipyla pictella is a species of snout moths in the genus Afropsipyla. It was described by Balinsky, in 1994, and is known from Namibia (it was described from Abachaus) and South Africa.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.afromoths.net/species/show/40394 |title=Afro Moths |publisher=Afro Moths |date= |accessdate=2011-09-29[/tpl]
