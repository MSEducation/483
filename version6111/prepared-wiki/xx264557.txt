[[Texas State Highway Spur 53]]

CATEGORIES: State highway spurs in Texas, Transportation in Bexar County, Texas

Spur 53 is a state highway spur in San Antonio, Texas.

==Route description==

Spur 53 provides access from Interstate 10 to the southern side of the University of Texas at San Antonio. State maintenance begins along UTSA Boulevard at a point approximately [tpl]convert|200|ft|m|abbr=on[/tpl] west of the main southern entrance of UTSA at Edward Ximenes Avenue. The route travels east, passing the headquarters of Valero Energy Corporation, before the Spur 53 designation ends at Interstate 10 Exit #557. UTSA Boulevard continues to the east under local jurisdiction.[tpl]Texas Mapbook|404|accessdate=March 13, 2011[/tpl]

==History==

A previous route known as Spur 53 was designated in 1939, connecting with US 81 in the town of West. This designation was canceled in 1953, when the mileage for that route was transferred to [tpl]roadlink|state=TX|FM|2114[/tpl].[tpl]TxDOT|FM|2114|accessdate=February 1, 2014[/tpl]
The current route was designated in 1980.
