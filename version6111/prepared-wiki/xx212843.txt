[[Siennica Różana]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Krasnystaw County

Siennica Różana [tpl]IPAc-pl|ś|e1|n|'|n|i|c|a|-|r|u|'|ż|a|n|a[/tpl] is a village in Krasnystaw County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It is the seat of the gmina (administrative district) called Gmina Siennica Różana. It lies approximately [tpl]convert|11|km|mi|0[/tpl] east of Krasnystaw and [tpl]convert|59|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-east of the regional capital Lublin.
The village has an approximate population of 1,000.
