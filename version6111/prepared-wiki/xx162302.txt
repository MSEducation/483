Cristian Alberto Tula (born 28 January 1978 in a trailer in Rawson, Chubut) is an Argentine football defender who plays for Independiente in the Primera B Nacional in Argentina.

==Career==

Tula started his career with Ferro Carril Oeste in 1998 but in 2000 the club were relegated from the Primera, and the season after they were relegated again to the 3rd division. In 2003 they won the 3rd division title and Tula was signed by Argentine giants River Plate. In 2004 he was part of the River Plate squad that won the Clausura title.
In 2005 Tula had a loan spell with Arsenal de Sarandí before returning to River. In 2006 River sold Tula to San Lorenzo de Almagro where he has established himself as a first team regular.
