[[Odin Sivertsen]]

CATEGORIES: 1914 births, 2008 deaths, Deputy members of the Parliament of Norway, Progress Party (Norway) politicians, Rogaland politicians

Odin Sivertsen (1 August 1914 – 24 March 2008) was a Norwegian politician for the Anders Lange Party.
He served as a deputy representative to the Parliament of Norway from Rogaland during the term 1973–1977. In total he met during 20 days of parliamentary session.[tpl]Stortingetbio|ODSI[/tpl]
