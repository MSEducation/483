[[Downtown Journal]]

CATEGORIES: Newspapers published in Minnesota, News websites, Media in Minneapolis, Minnesota

Downtown Journal, founded during the 1970s as a print newspaper, is an online and print newspaper serving Minneapolis, Minnesota in the United States. The paper covers the Minneapolis Mill District, Loring Park, North Loop, Elliot Park and the Mississippi riverfront.[tpl]cite news|title=Skyway News to become Downtown Journal|url=http://www.bizjournals.com/twincities/stories/2005/07/11/daily14.html|date=July 12, 2005|work=Minneapolis / St. Paul Business Journal|publisher=American City Business Journals|accessdate=2008-10-07[/tpl]
Minnesota Premier Publications Inc., its parent company, acquired the paper as the Skyway News in 2001 and renamed it Downtown Journal in September 2005. MPP also publishes Southwest Journal, Minnesota Parent and Minnesota Good Age.  In April 2007, MPP redesigned its Web sites to concentrate more on online news.[tpl]cite press release|author=|title=MPP announces revamped dtjournal.com|url=http://www.mnpubs.com/index.php?publication=mpp&section=45&page=50&readmore=70|publisher=Minnesota Premier Publications|date=2007-04-30|accessdate=2007-08-27[/tpl] and in July of that year the Downtown Journal became a biweekly in print.[tpl]cite news|author=Wyant, Carissa|title=Downtown Journal scales back publishing schedule|url=http://www.bizjournals.com/twincities/stories/2007/07/09/daily42.html|work=Minneapolis / St. Paul Business Journal|publisher=American City Business Journals|date=2007-07-13|accessdate=2007-08-29[/tpl]
