[[Swimming at the 1984 Summer Olympics – Men's 4 × 100 metre medley relay]]

CATEGORIES: Swimming at the 1984 Summer Olympics

The final of the Men's 4x100 metres Medley Relay event at the 1984 Summer Olympics was held in Los Angeles, California, on August 4, 1984. The first eight qualified for the final.
