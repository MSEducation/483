[[Raúl Araiza]]

CATEGORIES: 1964 births, Living people, Mexican male film actors, Mexican male television actors, Mexican television presenters, Male actors from Mexico City

Raúl Araiza Herrera ([tpl]IPA-es|raˈul aˈɾaisa[/tpl]; born November 14, 1964) is a Mexican actor and television presenter.
He was born in Mexico City. He studied drama in the Centro de Educación Artística of Televisa. He has two daughters with his wife. His brother, his mother and his father are also actors.http://www2.esmas.com/entretenimiento/biografias/009319/raul-araiza/
