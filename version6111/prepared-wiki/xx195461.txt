[[Luis Enrique Porozo]]

Luis Enrique Porozo Mina (born June 30, 1990 in Santo Domingo de Los Colorados, Santo Domingo de los Ts√°chilas) is a male amateur boxer from Ecuador who qualified in the men's featherweight division for the 2008 Summer Olympics as a seventeen-year old.
Porozo, from Pichincha, came in second at his qualifier when he beat Roberto Navarro. He lost the meaningless final to Idel Torriente but qualified anyway. In his first Olympic fight he beat Navarro in a rematch 3:3+, jury decision.
