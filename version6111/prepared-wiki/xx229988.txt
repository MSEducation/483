[[Winter Haven Area Transit]]

CATEGORIES: Bus transportation in Florida, Transportation in Polk County, Florida, Winter Haven, Florida

Winter Haven Area Transit is a bus system based in Winter Haven, Florida.  The system operates 9 of its own buses, and another 4 buses are provided for WHAT by the Citrus Connection.  There are 8 routes serving Winter Haven, Auburndale, Lakeland, Lake Alfred, Haines City, Lake Wales, Frostproof, Bartow and Fort Meade.
Winter Haven Area Transit was started in 1999.Citrus Connection website  It is a joint venture between the Board of County Commissioners of Polk County and the City of Winter Haven.
