[[Pegasus (Mt. Olympus Water and Theme Park)]]

CATEGORIES: Roller coasters in Wisconsin, Roller coasters introduced in 1996

Pegasus is the name of a wooden roller coaster located at Mt. Olympus Water & Theme Park in Wisconsin Dells, Wisconsin.  The trains were built by the Philadelphia Toboggan Company.

==Track layout==

Pegasus travels over and around Medusa's drop.  It covers [tpl]convert|1600|ft|m[/tpl] of track, reaching [tpl]convert|60|ft|m[/tpl] above the ground, and includes a [tpl]convert|45|ft|m|adj=on[/tpl] drop.
