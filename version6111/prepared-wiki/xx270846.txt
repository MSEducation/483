[[(16657) 1993 UB]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1993, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Amor asteroids

(16657) 1993 UB is a near-Earth minor planet in the Amor group.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on October 23, 1993.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
