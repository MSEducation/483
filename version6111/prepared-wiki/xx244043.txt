[[KidsWorld Sports]]

CATEGORIES: Canadian children's television series

Kids World Sports is a 26 episode series for young teens. Each episode features 2 kids from across the globe, who strive for greatness in their chosen sport. The show profiles kids who have the potential to become tomorrow's sport heroes.
The show was produced by Breakthrough Films and Television in Toronto Ontario Canada.
The show was first produced with Discovery Kids UK and BBC Kids Canada, and has since aired throughout the world on various channels.
