[[1990 Australian Open – Men's Singles]]

CATEGORIES: 1990 Australian Open

Ivan Lendl was the defending champion,[tpl]cite news|agency=Associated Press (AP)|work=The New York Times|title=Tennis; Lendl Takes Title and No. 1|url=http://www.nytimes.com/1989/01/30/sports/tennis-lendl-takes-title-and-no-1.html|date=1989-01-30|accessdate=2012-01-13[/tpl] and retained his title defeating Stefan Edberg 4–6, 7–6(7–3), 5–2 in the final – leading in the third set when Edberg was forced to retire because of a torn stomach muscle injury. The Swede's retirement marked the first time since the 1911 Wimbledon Championships that a male player abandoned during the singles' title match of a Grand Slam tournament.[tpl]cite news|agency=Associated Press (AP)|work=The Tuscaloosa News|title=Lendl claims Australian title after Edberg injury|url=http://news.google.com/newspapers?id=WUkgAAAAIBAJ&sjid=eaUEAAAAIBAJ&pg=2984,6798703&dq=edberg+injury&hl=en|date=1990-01-28|accessdate=2011-12-24[/tpl]
