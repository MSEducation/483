Jacob Wayne Young (born 10 September 1979) is an American actor and singer. He is perhaps best known for his role as JR Chandler on the ABC daytime soap opera All My Children and Rick Forrester on CBS's The Bold and the Beautiful. He was the second actor to portray Lucky Spencer on ABC's General Hospital.

==Early life and career==

His parents divorced and his mother remarried to edward vasquez. He has one older brother, Michael, Jr., and two sisters, Tiffany and Charity.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.people.com/people/archive/article/0,,20135833,00.html | title=Jacob Young: Sexiest Soap Star | publisher=people.com, 2001-11-2Young portrayed Rick Forrester on the CBS soap The Bold and the Beautiful from December 31, 1997 to September 15, 1999. Young later portrayed Lucky Spencer on General Hospital for three years from February 25, 2000 until February 10, 2003. In 2005, he was again nominated for a Daytime Emmy Award as Outstanding Younger Actor, and in 2009 he was nominated for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Drama Series.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.emmyonline.org/mediacenter/daytime_36th_nominations.html | title=Daytime Entertainment Emmy Awards - Nominations Announcement | accessdate=2009-09-03[/tpl][tpl]cite web | url=http://www.soapoperadigest.com/daytime_emmy_nominations_2009/ | title=2009 Daytime Emmy Nominations | accessdate=2009-09-03[/tpl]
