[[(46597) 1993 DK2]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1993, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(46597) 1993 DK2 is a main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on February 24, 1993.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
