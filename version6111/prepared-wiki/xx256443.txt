[[Cao Thang Eye Hospital]]

CATEGORIES: Hospitals in Vietnam, Buildings and structures in Ho Chi Minh City, Eye hospitals

Cao Thang Eye Hospital (CT Eye Hospital, Bệnh viện mắt Cao Thắng), incorporated in 2001, is the leading private ophthalmology hospital of Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, that integrates clinical and hospital care services with medical research and institutional education. Cao Thang is recognized as one of the very few hospitals in Vietnam, achieving international standard by Joint Commission on Accreditation of Healthcare Organizations.

==Overview==

Cao Thang has served 80,000 patients and performed around 10,000 surgeries each year of cataract surgery by Phacoemulsification and laser vision correction by Lasik, Lasek and PRK.
In 2003, the Hospital received certification in accordance with standard management system ISO 9001:2000 quality certification by international organizations DNV (Norway) level. In 2009, Cao Thang was certified by Joint Commission on Accreditation of Healthcare Organizations international standards.Accredited Organizations Joint Commission International
In 2011, Cao Thang Eye Hospital became the first member from Vietnam, of the World Association of Eye Hospitals.New member Vietnam! WAEH Official Website In the same year, it became the first member of Vietnam, joining the ASEAN Association of Eye Hospitals (AAEH)
Since 2012, the hospital has opened admission program for Elective (medical). MBBS and Doctor of Medicine students from various parts of the world has sought Cao Thang for their internship. Many of them are from Utrecht University, Imperial College London, University of Warwick, University of California, Los Angeles, University of California, Irvine, University of Western Australia, University of New South Wales, Monash University, National University of Singapore.

==Services==

Besides providing the general ophthalmology clinical services, Cao Thang does offer a well-organised blend of specialists and technicians working together to generate techniques and prevent the threat of blindness. The services including LASIK/LASEK/PRK surgery, Cataract surgery, lens implantation, plastic surgery of the eyelids (blepharoplasty, ptosis repair, pterygia).
