[[Nanoro Department]]

CATEGORIES: Departments of Burkina Faso, Boulkiemdé Province

Nanoro is a department or commune of Boulkiemdé Province in central Burkina Faso. As of 2005 it has a population of 33,511. Its capital lies at the town of Nanoro.Burkinabé government inforoute communale
