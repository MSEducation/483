[[Kevin Luckassen]]

CATEGORIES: 1993 births, Living people, Dutch footballers, Dutch people of Ghanaian descent, Sportspeople from Eindhoven, Dutch expatriate footballers, Expatriate footballers in Scotland, Ross County F.C. players, Scottish Professional Football League players

 Kevin Luckassen (born July 27 1993 in Eindhoven) is a Dutch-Ghanaian footballer who plays for Gambrinus Liga club Slovan Liberec. He previously played for AZ but never got a game for the first team.

==Career==

Luckassen began playing youth football for Amstelveen Heemrad and AVV Zeeburgia. He later played for AFC Amsterdam and AZ Alkmaar. He was promoted to the AZ first team squad as an 18 year old at the start of the 2011–12 season, Luckassen did not make an appearance this whole season. In the 2012–13 season he did not make an appearance and at the end of his contract he was released.[tpl]cite news|title=Wijnaldum looking for lack of guarantees salvation elsewhere|trans_title=Wijnaldum zoekt bij gebrek aan garanties heil elders|url=http://www.vi.nl/nieuws/wijnaldum-zoekt-bij-gebrek-aan-garanties-heil-elders.htm|publisher=Voetbal International|language=Dutch|date=31 March 2013|accessdate=21 February 2014[/tpl]
In July 2013, he signed a three-year deal with Scottish Premiership club Ross County.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www1.skysports.com/football/news/25704/8794256/ross-county-confirm-signings-of-kevin-luckassen-and-marc-klok |title=Ross County confirm signings of Kevin Luckassen and Marc Klok|work=Sky Sports |publisher=BSkyB |date=26 June 2013 |accessdate=20 August 2013[/tpl] He made his debut in a 2–1 against Celtic at Celtic Park. Since then, he was would be often used in first team ins and out in the first half of the season.
After six months at Ross County, Luckassen left Ross County to join Gambrinus Liga side Slovan Liberec after the club accepted their bid.[tpl]cite news|title=Ross County's Kevin Luckassen poised to seal move to Slovan Liberec|url=http://sport.stv.tv/football/264835-ross-countys-kevin-luckassen-poised-to-seal-move-to-slovan-liberec/|publisher=STV Sport|date=20 February 2014|accessdate=21 February 2014[/tpl] Days later, the move was confirmed.[tpl]cite news|title=Slovan Liberec incorporates former AZ'er Luck in Assen|trans_title=Slovan Liberec lijft ex-AZ'er Luckassen in|url=https://www.vi.nl/nieuws/slovan-liberec-lijft-exazer-luckassen-in.htm|publisher=Voetbal International|language=Dutch|date=19 February 2014|accessdate=3 April 2014[/tpl] Having made two appearance so far, Luckassen scored his first goal on 7 March 2014, as Slovan Liberec win 1-0 against Zbrojovka Brno.[tpl]cite news|title=Liberec, Brno 1:0 in football předehrávce guests sank goalkeeper howler|trans_title=Liberec- Brno 1:0, ve fotbalové předehrávce hosty potopil kiks gólmana|url=http://fotbal.idnes.cz/19-kolo-prvni-ligy-liberec-brno-dka-/fotbal.aspx?c=A140307_195817_fotbal_min|publisher=Indes|language=Czech|date=7 March 2014|accessdate=3 April 2014[/tpl] He followed up his second goal two weeks later on 21 March 2014, in a 3-0 win over Jablonec.[tpl]cite news|title=FC Slovan Liberec 3-0 FK Baumit Jablonec|trans_title=FC Slovan Liberec 3-0 FK Baumit Jablonec|url=http://fotbal.idnes.cz/fotbal-online.aspx?online=7080958|publisher=Indes|language=Czech|date=21 March 2014|accessdate=3 April 2014[/tpl]
