[[Have Another Ball]]

CATEGORIES: Me First and the Gimme Gimmes albums, Covers albums, 2008 compilation albums, Fat Wreck Chords compilation albums

Have Another Ball is a compilation album by the punk rock cover band Me First and the Gimme Gimmes. It is their seventh album overall, and was released on July 8, 2008 by Fat Wreck Chords.
The album features a-sides dating back to before their first album, Have a Ball."Me First and the Gimme Gimmes detail Have Another Ball!."  Punknews.org.  May 22, 2008.  Many of the tracks were previously released on now out-of-print 7" vinyl singles, while others were featured on compilation albums.
