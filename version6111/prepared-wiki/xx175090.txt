[[KTRZ]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Wyoming

KTRZ (93.1 FM) is a radio station broadcasting an Adult Contemporary format. Licensed to Riverton, Wyoming, USA.  The station is currently owned by the estate of Jimmy Ray Carroll and features programing from Dial Global's Adult Contemporary network and AP Radio. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KTRZ |title=KTRZ Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==Translators==

In addition to the main station, KTRZ is relayed by an additional translator to widen its broadcast area.
