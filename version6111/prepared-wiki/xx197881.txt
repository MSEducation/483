[[Orient Group]]

CATEGORIES: Companies based in Heilongjiang, Conglomerate companies of China, Privately held companies of China, Companies established in 1978

Orient Group Incorporation ([tpl]SSE|600811[/tpl]) is a private-owned conglomerate engaged in finance, trading, construction, port, industry, tourism and property development. It is headquartered in Harbin, Heilongjiang, China.Orient Group IncorporationOrient Group Incorporated - Company Profile Snapshot It was listed on the Shanghai Stock Exchange in 1994 and it is the first Heilongjiang-based A share listed in Shanghai.
