[[Nicosia Turkish Municipality]]

CATEGORIES: Nicosia, Northern Cyprus

Nicosia Turkish Municipality ([tpl]lang-tr|Lefkoşa Türk Belediyesi[/tpl]) is the governing body of North Nicosia. It was established in 1958 and was recognized by Article 173 of the Constitution of the Republic of Cyprus.The Constitution - Appendix D: Part 12 - Miscellaneous Provisions After 1974, it became the municipality of North Nicosia. The current mayor is Cemal Metin Bulutoğluları.Cemal Metin Bulutoğluları: Biography, Nicosia Turkish Municipality, Retrieved on 21 July 2012.
