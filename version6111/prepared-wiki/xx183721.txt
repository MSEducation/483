[[Liberty Township, Adair County, Missouri]]

CATEGORIES: Townships in Adair County, Missouri, Kirksville, Missouri micropolitan area, Townships in Missouri

Liberty Township is one of ten townships in Adair County, Missouri, United States. As of the 2010 census, its population was 324.[tpl]cite web| url=http://factfinder2.census.gov/bkmk/table/1.0/en/DEC/10_DP/G001/0600000US2900141924| title=Geographic Identifiers: 2010 Demographic Profile Data (G001): Liberty township, Adair County, Missouri| publisher=U.S. Census Bureau, American Factfinder| accessdate=June 20, 2013[/tpl]

==Geography==

Liberty Township covers an area of [tpl]convert|52.94|sqmi|km2[/tpl] and contains no incorporated settlements.  It contains seven cemeteries: Baden Springs, Lutz, Mount Moriah, Parcell Salisbury, Pinkerton, Pratt and Ringo Point.
