==History==

Skinner & Co. was started in 1880, and began supplying jewelry to the English royal family during Queen Victoria's reign.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bentley-skinner.co.uk/history/|title=History:  Bentley & Skinner (Bond Street Jewellers) Ltd|work=Bentley & Skinner|accessdate=8 January 2010[/tpl]
Bentley & Co. was founded in 1934. The two companies joined together in 1997 creating Bentley & Skinner, Jewellers by Royal Appointment.
