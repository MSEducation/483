[[Minan, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Minan ([tpl]lang-fa|مينان[/tpl], also Romanized as Mīnān)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795710|Minan[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 139, in 29 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
