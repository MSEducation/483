[[MixBit]]

CATEGORIES: Video software

Mixbit is a video-sharing service, which is created by the founders of YouTube, Steve Chen and Chad Hurley. It was started on 8 of August 2013. This website gives users a chance to create dynamic shared videos. It competes with Vine (owned by Twitter) and Instagram (owned by Facebook) on the video sharing website market.[tpl]cite web|title=YouTube founders take on Vine and Instagram with MixBit video app|url=http://www.theguardian.com/technology/appsblog/2013/aug/08/mixbit-iphone-app-youtube-founders[/tpl]

==Details==

The iPhone app was released in August of 2013, with the Android app following that October.
