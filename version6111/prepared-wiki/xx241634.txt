[[Jonathan Wilkins]]

CATEGORIES: British ballroom dancers, Living people, English dancers

Jonathan Wilkins is an English ballroom dancer and dance teacher, born in London. He holds many titles in Professional Ballroom Dancing including being 10 times Professional Ballroom Champion, 1997 - 2007 ( in a partnership with Katusha Demidova which lasted eleven years, and brought considerable success). http://www.dancesportinfo.net/Couple/Jonathan_Wilkins_and_Katusha_Demidova_2754/Results.aspx In 2004 they won the World Classic Showdance Championship,http://sports123.com/dan/wcsd.html which is one of the subsidiary World Dance Council Dance Sport events. Their final result was to win the WDC Open Professional Ballroom title at the USA Open in Orlando, Florida 2007.
From November 2007, Wilkins danced with Hazel Newberry. Together they became European Profession Ballroom Champions (2009) and British Professional Ballroom Champions (2009). Their best results were first in the WDC Professional Ballroom, Moscow 2009 and second in the Professional World Ballroom Dance Championship in Tokyo 2009.http://www.dancesportinfo.net/Couple/Jonathan_Wilkins_and_Hazel_Newberry_80903/Results.aspx After this championship the two partners retired from competitive dancing. Both currently making a brilliant Adjudicator careers judging World's most prestigious Ballroom Dance Championships.
In 2010 Jonathan got married to Anastasia Kazmina, Canadian Professional Ballroom vice-Champion and currently teaching students in the US and England.
