[[1990]]

CATEGORIES: 1990

Important events of 1990 include the Reunification of Germany and Yemeni unification,http://www.britannica.com/EBchecked/topic/652831/Yemen/273073/Unification-of-Yemen the separation of Namibia from South Africa, Desert Shield, the formal beginning of the Human Genome Project (finished in 2003), the launch of the Hubble Space Telescope, and the Baltic states declaring independence from the Soviet Union amidst perestroika. Also in this year, Nelson Mandela was released from prison, and Margaret Thatcher resigned as Prime Minister of the United Kingdom after over 11 years.
1990 was an important year in the Internet's early history. In the fall of 1990, Tim Berners-Lee created the first web server and the foundation for the World Wide Web; it would be released to the public in 1991.http://www.w3.org/History/19921103-hypertext/hypertext/WWW/History.html 1990 also saw the official decommissioning of the ARPANET, the forerunner of the Internet system and the introduction of the first true search engine, Archie.http://people.lis.illinois.edu/~chip/projects/timeline/1990archie.htm
Al Qaeda's predecessors were present on American soil as far back as the mid-1980s, and in 1990 members of this network committed  assassinations; including the killing of liberal Muslim Rashad Khalifa in Arizona, and the killing of Jewish nationalist Meir Kahane in New York City.http://s3.amazonaws.com/911timeline/2001/arizonarepublic092801.htmlhttp://articles.philly.com/2011-02-03/news/27098992_1_father-el-sayyid-nosair-shooting-range
Encyclopedia Britannica, which ceased printing in 2012, saw its highest all time sales in 1990; 120,000 volumes were sold that year.http://www.nooga.com/154314/encyclopaedia-britannica-ceases-publication-after-244-years/ The number of librarians in the United States also peaked around 1990.[tpl]cite web|title=Librarians in the U.S. from 1880-2009 | OUPblog |url=http://blog.oup.com/2011/06/librarian-census/|work=|archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/6Hth6iZuM|archivedate=2013-07-05|deadurl=no|accessdate=2013-07-03[/tpl]
