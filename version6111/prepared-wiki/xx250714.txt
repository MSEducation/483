[[Alma Seidler]]

CATEGORIES: Austrian film actresses, Austrian untitled nobility, People from Leoben, 1899 births, 1977 deaths, Austrian television actresses

Alma Seidler (born Leoben, June 1, 1899 - died Vienna, December 8, 1977) was an Austrian actress. She was the daughter of Ernst Seidler von Feuchtenegg, and had an extensive stage and screen career.
