[[Daniel Maderner]]

CATEGORIES: Austria footballers, 1995 births, Living people

Daniel Maderner (born 12 October 1995) is an Austrian footballer who currently plays for Austrian side SC Wiener Neustadt. He plays as striker. He made his Bundesliga debut at 10 August 2013 against SV Ried.
