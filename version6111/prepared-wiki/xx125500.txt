[[Book M]]

CATEGORIES: 2001 albums, Secret Chiefs 3 albums, Web of Mimicry albums, Albums produced by Trey Spruance

Book M is the third studio album by Secret Chiefs 3, released in 2001.  The album is centered around the theme of the Holy Grail, and the artwork consists of several layers of ciphers and alchemical symbols.
