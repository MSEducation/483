[[Gare de Pas-des-Lanciers]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur, Buildings and structures in Bouches-du-Rhône, Railway stations opened in 1847

thumb|right|Gare de Pas-des-LanciersGare de Pas-des-Lanciers is a railway station in southern France. It is located in the hamlet of Pas-des-Lanciers, on the territory of the commune of Saint-Victoret. It is situated on the Paris–Marseille railway.
