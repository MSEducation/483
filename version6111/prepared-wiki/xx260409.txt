[[Baij Nath Puri]]

CATEGORIES: Indian historians, Historians of India, Indian Indologists, Indian academics, 1916 births, Possibly living people, Alumni of the University of Oxford, University of Lucknow faculty, Emeritus Professors in India

Prof. Baij Nath Puri (B. N. Puri) (born January 25, 1916) is a historian from India. He completed M. Lit. and D. Phil from Oxford University. He is vice-president and member of the International Board of Editors for the history of Civilizations in Central Asia at the UNESCO, and remained President of Indian History Congress (Guwahati Session).
