[[Longling County]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Baoshan, Yunnan

Longling County ([tpl]zh|s=龙陵县|p=Lónglíng Xiàn[/tpl]) is a county in Baoshan Prefecture, Yunnan Province, China.
Its capital is the large community Longshan (龙山镇).
The former site of the Songshan-Battleground ([tpl]zh|s=松山战役旧址|p=Sōngshān zhànyì jiùzhǐ[/tpl]) (1944, the Anti-Japanese War) since 2006 is on the list of monuments of the People's Republic of China.
