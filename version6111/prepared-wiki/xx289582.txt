[[Sarab, Khodabandeh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Sarab ([tpl]lang-fa|سراب[/tpl], also Romanized as Sarāb; also known as Sarab Bin Rineh Rood)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3082400|Sarab[/tpl] is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 342, in 67 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
