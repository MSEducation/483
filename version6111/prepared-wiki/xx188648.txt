[[Roman Catholic Diocese of São Carlos]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Campinas, Religious organizations established in 1908, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of São Carlos ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Sancti Caroli in Brasilia[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of São Carlos in the Ecclesiastical province of Campinas in Brazil.
