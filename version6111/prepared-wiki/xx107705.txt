[[Tramway line between Liberec and Jablonec]]

CATEGORIES: Tram transport in the Czech Republic, Railway lines in the Czech Republic, Metre gauge railways in the Czech Republic, Liberec, Liberec District, Jablonec nad Nisou District

The [tpl]convert|12|km|abbr=on[/tpl] long tramway line between cities Liberec and Jablonec nad Nisou in the Czech Republic is an intercity transportation system connecting these cities.

==Overview==

Its construction started in 1947 and the line became operational on January 1, 1955. Its gauge is [tpl]RailGauge|1000mm|allk=on[/tpl], while most of other tramway lines built in Czechoslovakia at this time had [tpl]RailGauge|sg|allk=on[/tpl]. The line is single track with some places upgraded to double track. Over the time the line was also upgraded for higher safety.
Future plans intend to replace this line with Tram-train.[tpl]cs icon[/tpl] Infos at doprava.unas.cz
The only similar intercity tramway line in the Czech Republic exists between Most and Litvínov. Tramvajová trať Most - Litvínov
