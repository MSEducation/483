[[Savoonga Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in the Nome Census Area, Alaska

Savoonga Airport [tpl]airport codes|SVA|PASA|SVA[/tpl] is a state-owned public-use airport located two nautical miles (4 km) south of the central business district of Savoonga, a city in the Nome Census Area of the U.S. state of Alaska. Savoonga is located on St. Lawrence Island in the Bering Sea.

==Facilities==

Savoonga Airport covers an area of [tpl]convert|834|acre|ha|lk=on[/tpl] at an elevation of 53 feet (16 m) above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 5/23 with a 4,400 × 100 ft (1,341 × 30 m) gravel surface.
