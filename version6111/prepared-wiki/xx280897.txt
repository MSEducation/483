[[Dizaj-e Bala]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Dizaj-e Bala ([tpl]lang-fa|ديزج بالا[/tpl], also Romanized as Dīzaj-e Bālā)[tpl]IranNCSGN[/tpl] is a village in Mojezat Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 569, in 166 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
