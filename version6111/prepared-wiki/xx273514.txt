[[September (2011 film)]]

CATEGORIES: 2010s drama films, Turkish films, Films set in Turkey, Films set in Istanbul, 2011 films

September ([tpl]lang-tr|Eylül[/tpl]) is a 2011 Turkish drama film, written and directed by Cemil Ağacıkoğlu, starring Turgay Aydın and Görkem Yeltan as an introverted couple who encounter a maltreated young woman.
