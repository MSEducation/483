[[Valeri Dikarev]]

CATEGORIES: Russian footballers, Soviet footballers, Soviet Union international footballers, PFC Neftchi players, FC Spartak Moscow players, FC Lokomotiv Moscow players, 1939 births, 2001 deaths, FC Torpedo NN Nizhny Novgorod players

Valeri Pavlovich Dikarev ([tpl]lang-ru|Валерий Павлович Дикарев[/tpl]) (born September 10, 1939 in Baku, died in 2001) is a retired Soviet football player.

==Honours==

==International career==

Dikaryov made his debut for USSR on October 11, 1964 in a friendly against Austria. He played in a 1966 FIFA World Cup qualifier, but was not selected for the final tournament squad.
