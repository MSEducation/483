Aleksandr Sergeyevich Sheshukov ([tpl]lang-ru|Александр Сергеевич Шешуков[/tpl]) (born April 15, 1983 in Omsk) is an association footballer currently playing for FC Lokomotiv Moscow.

==International career==

Sheshukov made his debut for the Russia national football team on June 7, 2011 in a friendly against Cameroon.
