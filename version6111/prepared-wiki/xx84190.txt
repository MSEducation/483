[[Hanishina District, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Districts in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Hanishina|埴科郡|Hanishina-gun[/tpl] is a district located in Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of December 1, 2005, the district has an estimated population of 16,742. The total area is 53.64 km².
Currently, there is only one town in this district.
