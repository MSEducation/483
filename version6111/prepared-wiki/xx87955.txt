[[Montmorency railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1923

Montmorency railway station is located on the Hurstbridge line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the north-eastern Melbourne suburb of Montmorency opening on 5 September 1923.Montmorency Vicsig

==Platforms & services==

Montmorency has one platform. It is serviced by Metro Trains' Hurstbridge line services.Hurstbridge Line timetable Public Transport Victoria
