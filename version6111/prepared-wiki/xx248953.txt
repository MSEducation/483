[[Emily Carter]]

CATEGORIES: American women writers, 1960 births, People from New York City, Living people, New York University alumni

Emily Carter (born December 1960 in New York City) is an American writer.http://www.nndb.com/people/985/000047844/  She has been married to ex-punk rock guitarist (Christian Death, The Joneses, The Mau-Mau's) Johnnie Sage Ammentorp since 1999.  They divide their time between New Haven, CT and New York City.

==Life==

High School: Robert Louis Stevenson School for Gifted Underachievers.
College: New York University.  
Her work appeared in The New Yorker,http://www.newyorker.com/search/query?query=Emily+Carter+&queryType=nonparsed&submitbtn.x=42&submitbtn.y=11&submitbtn=Submit Story, Gathering of the Tribes, Between C & D, Artforum, Open City, Great River Review, and Poz.
