Sarita Phongsri ([tpl]lang-th|สริตา ผ่องศรี[/tpl]; born October 12, 1991) is a female Thai taekwondo practitioner. She's been nicknamed 'Yin'([tpl]lang-th|หยิน[/tpl]).
