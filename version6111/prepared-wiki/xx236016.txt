[[Zacatonal]]

CATEGORIES: Montane grasslands and shrublands, Ecoregions of Mexico, Ecoregions of Guatemala, Grasslands of Mexico, Flora of Central Mexico, Flora of Guatemala, Trans-Mexican Volcanic Belt, Neotropic ecoregions

The Zacatonal is a montane grassland and shrubland ecoregion of central Mexico, and Guatemala[tpl]citation required|date=August 2013[/tpl].

==Description==

The Zacatonal consists of several enclaves of grassland and shrubland occupying the highest peaks of the Trans-Mexican Volcanic Belt, covering about [tpl]convert|306|km2|sqmi|sp=us[/tpl]. 
They are surrounded by the Trans-Mexican Volcanic Belt pine-oak forests ecoregion at lower elevations.
