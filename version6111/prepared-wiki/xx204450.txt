[[Mieux qu'ici-bas]]

CATEGORIES: 2000 albums, Isabelle Boulay albums, V2 Records albums

Mieux qu'ici-bas is francophone Canadian pop singer Isabelle Boulay's third studio album, released in September 2000.Isabelle Boulay, biographie, Radio France Internationale.  Accessed on line 9 Dec 2013.  It was led by the hit single "Parle-moi" which was released about the same time. The album achieved a great success in Belgium (Wallonia) and France, where it reached the top ten and stayed on the chart for about two years.
