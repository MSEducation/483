[[Sergio Arboleda University]]

CATEGORIES: Universities and colleges in Colombia

Sergio Arboleda University ([tpl]lang-es| Universidad Sergio Arboleda[/tpl]) is a Roman Catholic university located in Bogotá, Colombia, with a sectional located in Santa Marta, Colombia. 
Pedro Carmona former coupster against the government of Hugo Chávez in Venezuela holds a post as university professor.
