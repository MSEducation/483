[[Slam Creepers]]

CATEGORIES: Swedish rock music groups, Musical groups established in 1963, Beat groups

Slam Creepers were a rock band from Vansbro, Sweden formed by Björn Skifs in 1962 and broke up in 1969. Björn Skifs then went on a solo career.Answers.com Björn Skifs They had some moderate hits including a cover of a Foundations song "We Are Happy People". Skifs would later find success with a number 1 hit "Hooked On A Feeling as a member of Blue Swede.
 
The name is a humorous anglicisation of Swedish 'slamkrypare', 'mud crawlers', a slang name for a kind of shoes.
