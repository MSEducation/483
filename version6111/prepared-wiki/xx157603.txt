[[Alliance des jeunes patriotes pour le sursaut national]]

CATEGORIES: Politics of Ivory Coast

The Alliance des jeunes patriotes pour le sursaut national ([tpl]fr icon[/tpl] Alliance of Young Patriots for National Survival)  commonly known as Young Patriots, of Côte d'Ivoire.

==Organisation==

A youth movement supportive of the President of Côte d'Ivoire, Laurent Gbagbo and his ruling Ivorian Popular Front (FPI) party.[tpl]Clarify|date=November 2010[/tpl] Its founder, and current leader, is Charles Blé Goudé.
The group formed in 2002, as a wider, and more militant coalition of nationalist groups, led by Blé Goudé's Congrès Panafricain des Jeunes et des Patriotes (COJEP), which still is the dominant organization.
