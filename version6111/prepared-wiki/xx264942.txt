Lukáš Krenželok (born 30 June 1983 in Ostrava) is a Czech professional ice hockey forward who currently plays for HC Slavia Praha of the Czech Extraliga.Slavia Praha HC's 2010-11 Roster
He previously played for HC Havířov and HC Vítkovice.Profile at HC Slavia Praha official website
