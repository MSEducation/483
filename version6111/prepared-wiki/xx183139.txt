[[KDDI Designing Studio]]

CATEGORIES: KDDI

KDDI DESIGNING STUDIO, also known as [tpl]nihongo|"K Sta"|Kスタ[/tpl], is KDDI's consumer showroom in Harajuku.

==Overview==

KDDI Designing Studio was built on March 4, 2005 in Harajuku.[tpl]cite web |url=http://k-tai.impress.co.jp/cda/article/news_toppage/22830.html |title=KDDI DESIGNING STUDIO, Open 4th |publisher=Impress |accessdate=2008-01-02 |language=Japanese[/tpl] The showroom is 8 minutes walk from Harajuku Station Takeshita Street exit. In 2008, KDDI Designing Studio created [tpl]nihongo|"au Garden"|auの庭|au no niwa[/tpl], like the one in their commercial, opened to the public from April 26, 2008 to June 3, 2008.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oricon.co.jp/pj/k-stu/longevent/au_niwa.html|title=auの庭＠KDDIデザイニングスタジオ|accessdate=2008-05-27|language=Japanese[/tpl]
