[[Ljiljana Vukajlović]]

CATEGORIES: Serbian classical pianists, University of Arts in Belgrade faculty, University of Pristina faculty, Living people, University of Arts in Belgrade alumni, Accompanists

Ljiljana Vukajlović is a Serbian pianist and accompanist.

==Education==

She graduated from the Belgrade Music Academy, where also completed her postgraduate studies in Piano Performance (1966) as a student of Professor Olga Mihajlović.

==Career==

In addition to giving concerts as a soloist and chamber musician, she served for many years as an accompanist at the Faculty of Music in Belgrade and Faculty of Arts of Priština.
