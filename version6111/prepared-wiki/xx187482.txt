[[Josie Janz]]

CATEGORIES: Australian netball players, West Coast Fever players, 1988 births, Living people

Josie Janz (born 24 February 1988) is an Australian netball player in the ANZ Championship, playing for the West Coast Fever. 
Works at the David Wirrpanda Foundation in the Dare to Dream program as the project officer. 
Josie began working at the foundation as a mentor for young Indigenous children. 
Josie was born in Thursday Island. And was raised in a small town in the Kimberly known as Derby. 
Josie played for the Australian under 21's in Jamaica in 2009.
