[[Niclas Andersén]]

CATEGORIES: 1988 births, Living people, Brynäs IF players, Los Angeles Kings draft picks, Swedish ice hockey players

Niclas Andersén (born 28 April 1988 in Grums) is a Swedish ice hockey player. He is currently playing with the Brynäs IF in the Elitserien. 
He was drafted by the Los Angeles Kings in the fourth round of the 2006 NHL Entry Draft, 114th overall.
