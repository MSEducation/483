[[Jörgen Sundqvist (ice hockey)]]

CATEGORIES: 1982 births, Living people, Brynäs IF players, People from Härnösand, Swedish ice hockey players

Ulf Erik Jörgen Sundqvist (born September 24, 1982) is a Swedish professional ice hockey defenceman. He currently plays for Brynäs IF of the Elitserien.
Sundqvist has played with Brynäs IF since 2005 and, in March 2014, he signed an extension to keep him with the club until 2018.http://www.expressen.se/sport/hockey/shl/avslojar-brynas-back-stannar-till-2018/
