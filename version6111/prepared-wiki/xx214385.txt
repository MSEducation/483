[[Raciu]]

CATEGORIES: Communes in Dâmboviţa County

Raciu is a commune in Romania.  It is located in Dâmboviţa County. It is composed of three villages: Raciu, Siliştea and Şuţa Seacă. These were part of Lucieni Commune from 1968 to 2004, when they were split off to re-establish a separate commune.
