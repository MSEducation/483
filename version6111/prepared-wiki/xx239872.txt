[[Peekskill Hollow Creek]]

CATEGORIES: Geography of Putnam County, New York, Tributaries of the Hudson River

The headwaters of Peekskill Hollow Creek originate as the outflow of Lake Tibet in the town of Carmel in Putnam County, New York. It flows in a south westerly direction for approximately [tpl]convert|17|mi|km[/tpl] before joining Sprout Brook and then Annsville Creek.
The creek's drainage area is slightly more than [tpl]convert|47|sqmi|km2[/tpl]. The creek is dammed [tpl]convert|0.8|mi|km[/tpl] upstream of the mouth at Van Cortlandtville.
