[[Muhammad Al-Munajjid]]

CATEGORIES: 1960 births, Living people, Islamic studies scholars, Muslim scholars, Salafis, Hanbalis, Saudi Arabian imams, Saudi Arabian Muslims, People from Aleppo, 20th-century imams, 21st-century imams

Muhammad Saalih Al-Munajjid is an Islamic lecturer who was born in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, in 1960. He was raised in and currently lives in Saudi Arabia.[tpl]fact|date=April 2014[/tpl]

==Education==

Al-Munajjid graduated from King Fahd University of Petroleum and Minerals (KFUPM) in Dhahran, Saudi Arabia, with a Bachelor's degree in Industrial Management.[tpl]fact|date=April 2014[/tpl]
Al-Munajjid studied Islamic law under the scholars 'Abd al-'Aziz ibn Baaz, Muhammad ibn al Uthaymeen, Abdullah Ibn Jibreen, Saleh Al-Fawzan, and Abd ar-Rahmaan al-Baraak.[tpl]fact|date=April 2014[/tpl]
