[[Slavcho Boychev]]

Slavcho Boychev ([tpl]lang-bg|Славчо Бойчев[/tpl]; born 2 May 1983) is a Bulgarian footballer, currently playing for FC Kyustendil as a defender.
Boychev previously played for Marek Dupnitsa and Montana in the A PFG.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Guardian.co.uk|title=Stats Centre: Slavcho Boychev Facts|url=http://guardian.touch-line.com/StatsCentre.asp?CTID=7&CPID=411&TEID=6438&PLID=174260&pStr=Player|accessdate=2010-01-15[/tpl]
