[[Aspidosperma tomentosum]]

CATEGORIES: Aspidosperma, Trees of Brazil

Aspidosperma tomentosum is a timber tree native to Brazil, which is typical of Cerrado vegetation. It was first described by Carl Friedrich Philipp von Martius.[tpl]IPNI|id=77467-1|accessdate=14 November 2010[/tpl]
