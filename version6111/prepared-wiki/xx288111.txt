[[Judy Dan]]

CATEGORIES: 1931 births, Living people, Miss Universe 1952 contestants, 20th-century Chinese actresses, Film actresses from Shanghai, Hong Kong film actresses

Judy Dan (Chinese: 但茱迪) (born 1931) is a Chinese-born American actress.

==Biography==

Judy Dan was born in Shanghai, the daughter of film director Dan Duyu (但杜宇) and actress Yin Mingzhu, and was raised in Hong Kong. In 1952, she won the Miss Hong Kong Pageant, then known as the Miss Hong Kong Beauty Contest,Article about Judy Dan and was 3rd Runner-Up at the Miss Universe 1952 contest.Judy Dan, Miss Hong Kong 1952
