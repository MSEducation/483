[[Stare Leśne Bohatery]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Augustów County

Stare Leśne Bohatery [tpl]IPAc-pl|'|s|t|a|r|e|-|'|l|e|ś|n|e|-|b|o|h|a|'|t|e|r|y[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Lipsk, within Augustów County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland, close to the border with Belarus.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) – TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|12|km|mi|0[/tpl] north-east of Lipsk, [tpl]convert|38|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] east of Augustów, and [tpl]convert|80|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of the regional capital Białystok.

==Name==

The name of the settlement originates from [tpl]lang-be|"багатыр"[/tpl], which means "rich man". There is also a settlement Polnyja Bahatyry on the Belarusian side of the border.
