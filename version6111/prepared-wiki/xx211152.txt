[[Gillespie Corners, Oregon]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Lane County, Oregon, Unincorporated communities in Oregon

Gillespie Corners is a road junction and unincorporated community in Lane County, Oregon, United States.[tpl]cite web |url=http://geonames.usgs.gov/pls/gnispublic/f?p=gnispq:3:::NO::P3_FID:1121135 |title=Feature Query Results |publisher= United States Board on Geographic Names, United States Geological Survey |accessdate=2008-07-29[/tpl][tpl]cite web |url=http://geonames.usgs.gov/features.html#l |title=Feature Class Types |publisher= Geographic Names Information System, United States Geological Survey |accessdate=2008-07-29 |archiveurl = http://web.archive.org/web/20080602160459/http://geonames.usgs.gov/features.html#l  |archivedate = 2008-06-02[/tpl]  Gillespie Corners lies at the intersection of Territorial Highway and Lorane Highway, southeast of Eugene.
