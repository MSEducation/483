==Ethnic groups==

The people in the province are mainly indigenous citizens of Aymara descent. Aymara is the language which the majority of the population (85.42%) learnt to speak in childhood, 13.70% of the residents started speaking using the Spanish language and  0.55% using Quechua (2007 Peru Census).inei.gob.pe INEI, Peru, Censos Nacionales 2007
