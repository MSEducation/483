[[Novoulyanovsk]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Ulyanovsk Oblast, Populated places on the Volga, Cities and towns built in the Soviet Union, Populated places established in 1960

Novoulyanovsk ([tpl]lang-ru|Новоулья́новск[/tpl]) is a town in Ulyanovsk Oblast, Russia, located on the right bank of the Volga River, [tpl]convert|19|km|sp=us[/tpl] south of Ulyanovsk. Population: [tpl]ru-census|p2010=16,033|p2002=17,595|p1989=16,757[/tpl]

==History==

It was founded in 1960 as a settlement around a cement factory.[tpl]citation needed|date=March 2012[/tpl] Urban-type settlement status was granted to it in 1961 and a town status—in 1967.[tpl]citation needed|date=June 2010[/tpl]

==Administrative and municipal status==

Within the framework of administrative divisions, it is, together with five rural localities, incorporated as the town of oblast significance of Novoulyanovsk—an administrative unit with the status equal to that of the districts.Law #126-ZO As a municipal division, the town of oblast significance of Novoulyanovsk is incorporated as Novoulyanovsk Urban Okrug.Law #043-ZO

==Economy==

Today there are significant reserves of raw materials for cement production, cement factory here working  Eurocement group , factories asbestos cement products and concrete structures, soft roof.
