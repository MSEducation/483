[[Qarrah Dagh, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Qarrah Dagh ([tpl]lang-fa|قره داغ[/tpl], also Romanized as Qarrah Dāgh and Qareh Dāgh; also known as Karadag and Qara Dāgh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3079671|Qarrah Dagh[/tpl] is a village in Golabar Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 131, in 32 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
