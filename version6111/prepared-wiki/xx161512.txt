[[James Wray Williams]]

CATEGORIES: 1792 births, 1842 deaths, Members of the United States House of Representatives from Maryland, Speakers of the Maryland House of Delegates

James Wray Williams (October 8, 1792 â€“ December 2, 1842) was a U.S. Representative from Maryland.
Born in that State, Williams completed preparatory studies.  He served as member of the Maryland House of Delegates, and was speaker in 1830.
Williams was elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-seventh Congress and served from March 4, 1841, until his death on Prieshford farm, Deer Creek, Maryland, December 2, 1842.
He was interred in the family cemetery on Prieshford farm.
