[[Sari Kand-e Mohammad Reza]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Sari Kand-e Mohammad Reza ([tpl]lang-fa|ساري كندمحمدرضا[/tpl], also Romanized as Sārī Kand-e Moḩammad Rez̤ā) is a village in Chaypareh-ye Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
