[[Mauro Ribeiro]]

CATEGORIES: Brazilian male cyclists, 1964 births, Living people, Brazilian Tour de France stage winners, Olympic cyclists of Brazil, Cyclists at the 1996 Summer Olympics, People from Curitiba

Mauro Ribeiro (Curitiba, 19 July 1964) was a Brazilian professional road bicycle racer. In the 1991 Tour de France, he became the first Brazilian cyclist to win a stage in the Tour de France.
