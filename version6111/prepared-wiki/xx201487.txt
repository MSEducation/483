[[Scott Mathie]]

CATEGORIES: 1983 births, Living people, African people of British descent, South African rugby union players, Rugby union scrum-halves, Eastern Province Kings players, Sharks (rugby union) players, Natal Sharks players, Blue Bulls players, People from Durban

Scott Mathie (born 1 February 1983 in Durban, South Africa) is a former rugby union player that played as a scrum-half. Mathie played for the Blue Bulls, Natal Sharks, Leeds Carnegie, Sale Sharks and [tpl]Rut Eastern Province Kings[/tpl].[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/playerprofile.aspx?id=25607 | title=Player Profile: Scott Mathie | publisher=South African Rugby Union | accessdate=8 October 2013[/tpl] He retired in October 2013.
He joined the [tpl]Rut Eastern Province Kings[/tpl] in 2012.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.worldartists.co.za/page/news/1279944-Mathie-signs-with-Kings | title=Mathie signs with Kings | date=2012-05-29 | publisher=worldartists.co.za | accessdate=2012-05-29 [/tpl] He made 23 appearances for them in 2012 and 2013. He was initially named in the [tpl]Rut Kings[/tpl] squad for the 2013 Super Rugby season, but was later released to the 2013 Vodacom Cup squad.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.rugby15.co.za/2013/02/southern-kings-name-reduced-35-man-super-rugby-squad/ | title=Southern Kings name reduced 35 man Super Rugby squad | publisher=Rugby15 | work=Rugby15 | date=12 February 2013 | accessdate=12 February 2013[/tpl]
