[[Veikko Sinisalo]]

CATEGORIES: 1926 births, 2003 deaths, People from Riihimäki, Finnish male film actors, 20th-century Finnish male actors

Veikko Sinisalo (30 September 1926 – 16 December 2003) was a Finnish actor. He appeared in 24 films and television shows between 1954 and 2002.
