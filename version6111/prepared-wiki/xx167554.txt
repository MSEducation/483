[[DVDs to the Desert]]

CATEGORIES: Charities based in the United States, Concord, North Carolina

DVDs to the Desert is a charity founded on July 25, 2007 by Jesse L. Medford, the President of Challenge Coin Association based in Concord, North Carolina.
The purpose of DVDs to the Desert is to donate movies to the United States service members deployed overseas.
