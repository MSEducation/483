[[New Zealand at the 1972 Winter Olympics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 1972 Winter Olympics, New Zealand at the Olympics, 1972 in New Zealand sport

New Zealand competed at the 1972 Winter Olympics in Sapporo, Japan.  The country was represented by 2 athletes, Ross Ewington and Chris Womersley, both in the Alpine Skiing events.  Womersley finished 41st, and Ewington 49th (out of 54 competitors) in Downhill.
