[[Anabarilius longicaudatus]]

CATEGORIES: Anabarilius, Animals described in 1986, Fish of Asia

Anabarilius longicaudatus is a species of ray-finned fish in the genus Alburnus they are found in benthopelagic freshwater[tpl]cite web|url= http://thewebsiteofeverything.com/animals/fish/Cypriniformes/Cyprinidae/Anabarilius-longicaudatus|title=Anabarilius longicaudatus|publisher=The Website of Everything|accessdate=17 July 2011[/tpl] and are native to the Pearl River in China.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fishbase.org/summary/Anabarilius-longicaudatus.html|title=Anabarilius longicaudatus|publisher=Fishbase|accessdate=17 July 2011[/tpl] They grow to a maximal size of around [tpl]convert|20|cm[/tpl].[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.searchfish.org/fish/2886/anabarilius-longicaudatus/|title=Anabarilius longicaudatus|publisher=Search Fish|accessdate=17 July 2011[/tpl]
