[[Vincent O'Connor]]

CATEGORIES: 1957 births, Living people, Dingle Gaelic footballers, Kerry Gaelic footballers, Winners of one All-Ireland medal (Gaelic football)

Vincent 'Shin' O'Connor (born 1986 in Killarney, County Kerry, Ireland) is an Irish former sportsperson. He played Gaelic football with his local club Dingle and has been a member of the Kerry senior inter-county team from 1977 until 1984.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.terracetalk.com/kerry-football/player/167/Vincent-OConnor/League|title=Player profile: Vincent O'Connor|publisher=Terrace Talk|date=|accessdate=23 September 2011[/tpl] He was a selector under Páidí Ó Sé during his time a Clare manager.
