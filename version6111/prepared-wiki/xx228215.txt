[[Ove Liavaag]]

CATEGORIES: 1938 births, 2007 deaths, Directors of government agencies of Norway, Avinor people

Ove Liavaag (1938 - 2007) was a Norwegian civil servant.
He was the director of the Norwegian Civil Aviation Administration from 1989 to 2000. Prior to this he was technical director of the same organization from 1981.
