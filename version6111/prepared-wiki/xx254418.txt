[[Picture Perfect Hair Salon]]

CATEGORIES: 2009 video games, Nintendo DSi games, Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection games, Video games developed in Italy

Picture Perfect Hair Salon (Hair Salon in Europe) is a video game released solely for the Nintendo DSi. The game was released on November 13, 2009 in Europe and November 23, 2009 in the United States.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.gamespot.com/ds/sim/pictureperfecthair/tech_info.html?tag=tabs;summary|title=Picture Perfect Hair Salon Tech Info|publisher=CBS Interactive Inc. ==Gameplay==

The game utilizes the Nintendo DSi console's camera feature to be able to recognise and take pictures of the player's face to allow them to customize their hair in the game.http://uk.ds.ign.com/articles/103/1034535p1.html
