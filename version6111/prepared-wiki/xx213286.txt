[[PressOnline]]

CATEGORIES: Macedonian-language media, News websites, Macedonian media

Press OnLine is a news internet site providing news and information in Macedonian, Albanian and English.
For now, in Republic of Macedonia, PressOnline is the only trilingual daily online media outlet whose contents are changed two to three times a day. Larger articles and analyses are carried from a political magazine in the Albanian language, LOBI but you also have daily news, and pages are updated two to three times a day. The archive on this site is also trilingual.
