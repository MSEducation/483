[[Afsharlu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Afsharlu ([tpl]lang-fa|افشارلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Afshārlū and Afshār Loo; also known as Afshār and Aūshārle)[tpl]GEOnet3|10080677|Afsharlu[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 33, in 7 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
