[[Iași County]]

CATEGORIES: Iaşi County, Counties of Romania

Iași ([tpl]IPA-ro|ˈjaʃʲ[/tpl]) is a county (județ) of Romania, in Moldavia, with the administrative seat at Iași.

==Demographics==

As of 20 October 2011 census, Iași County had a population of 772,348, making it the second most populous county in Romania, after Bucharest.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.recensamantromania.ro/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/sR_TAB_3.xlsx | title = Population at 20 October 2011 | date = 5 July 2013 | accessdate = 5 July 2013 | publisher = INSSE | language = Romanian[/tpl] On the other hand, according to the 2012 data provided by the County Population Register Service, the total registered population of the county is as high as 873,662 people.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.ziarulevenimentul.ro/stiri/Moldova/zeci-de-mii-de-a-fantomea-bantuie-judetul--64002.html |title= Unul din 25 de ieșeni nu dțţine acte de identitate |publisher= ziarulevenimentul.ro | language = Romanian| date= 2013-02-14 |accessdate= 2013-02-15[/tpl]
The population of Iași County today is nearly double what it was sixty years ago.

==Geography==

This county has a total area of 5,476 km². It lies on a plain between the Siret River and the Prut River. Two other rivers run through the county: the Bahlui River (on the banks of which lies the city of Iași) and the Jijia River.
