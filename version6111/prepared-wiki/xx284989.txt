[[Lanthanosuchidae]]

CATEGORIES: Permian life

Lanthanosuchidae is a family of procolophonomorph parareptiles that lived 268-255 million years ago. The group was named in 1946 by Ivan Antonovich Efremov.Ivakhnenko, M.F. (1980). "Lanthanosuchids from the Permian of the East European Platform". Paleontologica Journal 1980: 80-90.
