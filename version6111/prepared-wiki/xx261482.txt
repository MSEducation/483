[[Liber Quiñones]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 births, Living people, Uruguayan footballers, Uruguayan expatriate footballers, Racing Club de Montevideo players, Cobreloa footballers, Danubio F.C. players, Tiburones Rojos de Veracruz footballers, Liga MX footballers, Expatriate footballers in Chile, Expatriate footballers in Mexico

Liber Daniel Quiñónes Prieto (born February 11, 1985 in Montevideo, Uruguay) is a Uruguayan footballer currently playing for Tiburones Rojos de Veracruz of the Liga MX.
