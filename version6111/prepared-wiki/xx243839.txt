[[Juha Tapio]]

CATEGORIES: 1974 births, Living people, Finnish musicians, Finnish songwriters

Juha Leevi Antero Tapio (born February 5, 1974) is a Finnish pop singer, lyricist, composer and guitarist. Tapio began with the first band to play rock in Ylistaro the age of 13. He was influenced by the Red Song Book. His first two albums were gospel, three more secular pop music.
