[[Chamzinsky District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Mordovia

Chamzinsky District ([tpl]lang-ru|Чамзинский райо́н[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-myv|Чаунзабуе[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-mdf|Чамзинкань район[/tpl]) is an administrativeConstitution of the Republic of Mordovia and municipalLaw #128-Z district (raion), one of the twenty-two in the Republic of Mordovia, Russia. It is located in the east of the republic. The area of the district is [tpl]convert|1009.5|km2|sp=us[/tpl].[tpl]citation needed|date=October 2012[/tpl] Its administrative center is the urban locality (an urban-type settlement) of Chamzinka.Law #7-Z Population: [tpl]ru-census2010|31,639|;[/tpl] 33,871 (2002 Census);[tpl]ru-pop-ref|2002Census[/tpl] [tpl]su-census1989|36,077|.[/tpl] The population of Chamzinka accounts for 29.9% of the district's total population.
