[[Turkish Journal of Biology]]

CATEGORIES: Biology journals, English-language journals, Publications established in 1977, Monthly journals, Open access journals, Academic journals published by learned societies

The Turkish Journal of Biology is a peer-reviewed scientific journal covering all aspects of biology. It was established in 1977 as Doğa: Turkish Journal of Biology before obtaining its current name in 1994. Since 2001, the journal is published in English, earlier it also contained articles in Turkish. It is published by Scientific and Technological Research Council of Turkey and the editor-in-chief is Ekrem Gürel (Abant Izzet Baysal University).
