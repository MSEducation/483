[[Codex Regius (New Testament)]]

CATEGORIES: Greek New Testament uncials, 8th-century biblical manuscripts, Bibliothèque nationale de France collections

Codex Regius designated by siglum Le or 019 (in the Gregory-Aland numbering), ε 56 (von Soden), is a Greek uncial manuscript of the New Testament, dated paleographically to the 8th century.[ref]
