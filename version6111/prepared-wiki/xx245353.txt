[[Beverly Hills Cop III (soundtrack)]]

CATEGORIES: Film soundtracks, 1994 soundtracks, MCA Records soundtracks, Hip hop soundtracks, Rock soundtracks, Contemporary R&B soundtracks, Beverly Hills Cop

Beverly Hills Cop III is the soundtrack to the 1994 film Beverly Hills Cop III. It was released on May 10, 1994 through MCA Records and consisted mostly of R&B music with some rock and hip hop. Like the film, the soundtrack was not well received and only made it to 158 on the Billboard 200 and 66 on the Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums. Three singles found minor success on the charts, "The Right Kinda Lover" by Patti LaBelle, "Luv 4 Dem Gangsta'z" by Eazy-E and "The Place Where You Belong" by Shai.
