[[Löcknitz (river)]]

CATEGORIES: Löcknitz basin, Rivers of Brandenburg, Rivers of Lower Saxony, Rivers of Mecklenburg-Vorpommern

The Löcknitz is a river in northern Germany (Mecklenburg-Vorpommern, Brandenburg and a few km in Lower Saxony). It is a right tributary of the Elbe and its total length is 66 km. The Löcknitz originates south of Parchim, and flows through Karstädt, Lenzen, and Dömitz. The Löcknitz joins the Elbe in Wehningen, a few km downstream from Dömitz.
