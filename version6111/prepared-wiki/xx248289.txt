[[Marianne Berndt]]

CATEGORIES: 1978 births, Living people, Chilean discus throwers, Chilean shot putters, Athletes (track and field) at the 1999 Pan American Games, Athletes (track and field) at the 2003 Pan American Games, Chilean people of German descent, Pan American Games competitors for Chile

Marianne Berndt (born December 24, 1978) is a female shot putter and discus thrower from Chile. She set her personal best (16.39m) in the women's shot put at the 2003 South American Championships.
