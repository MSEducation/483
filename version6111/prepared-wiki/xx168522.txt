As a co-writer with James Blunt, he is responsible for James Blunt's 2007 single 1973 (song).http://itunes.apple.com/us/artist/james-blunt/id27496674 He has also worked with Dave Matthews Band,http://www.rollingstone.com/music/albumreviews/stand-up-20090507 Alicia Keys, Anthony Hamilton,http://www.bmi.com/affiliate/C132 Nas, Maroon 5, Beyonc√©, and Grace Potter and the Nocturnalshttp://www.popmatters.com/pm/review/128495-grace-potter-the-nocturnals-grace-potter-the-nocturnals/ among others.http://www.bmi.com/musicworld/entry/20041202mark_batson_mark_batson/20041202mark_batson_mark_batson
Batson was formally trained in jazz piano at Howard Universityhttp://www.coas.howard.edu/music/huje/members.htm before mixing hip hop sampling and drum programming with live instrumentation as part of the group Get Set V.O.P. This blend is characteristic of much of his later production work. He has composed music for the feature films Beauty Shop (MGM, 2005), Miami Vice (film) (Universal Pictures, 2006),  and War (Lion's Gate, 2007).
