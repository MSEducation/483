[[Kevin MacCormack]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1981 births

Kevin McCormack is one of the first dancers selected for Riverdance and currently runs one of Ireland's most successful workshops in the centre of the town on Tuesday and Thursday and on Saturday for juniors.
