CATEGORIES: Swimming at the 1984 Summer Olympics

The final of the Men's 200 metres Backstroke event at the 1984 Summer Olympics was held in the McDonald's Olympic Swim Stadium in Los Angeles, California, on July 31, 1984. The first eight qualified for the final, the next for the B-final.
