[[Joyce Aboussie]]

CATEGORIES: American campaign managers, American political consultants, Living people, 1957 births

 Joyce Aboussie (born 1957) is the President and Owner of Aboussie & Associates, a public relations firm in St. Louis, Missouri. She is founder of Telephone Contact Inc., a voter contact, telemarketing and direct mail firm.
Aboussie was the National Vice Chair of Congressman Dick Gephardt’s Presidential Campaign and previously served as his National Political Director.[tpl]cite news |title=Joyce Aboussie |url=http://www.bizjournals.com/stlouis/stories/2000/01/31/focus2.html |work=St. Louis Business Journal |date=2000-01-28 |accessdate=2007-08-03[/tpl]  She worked with Congressman Dick Gephardt at the top of his political organization for nearly 30 years.
