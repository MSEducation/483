[[Kand-e Tatar]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Kand-e Tatar ([tpl]lang-fa|کند تاتار[/tpl], also Romanized as Kand-e Tātār and Kand Tātār; also known as Chashmeh Thāthār, Chashmeh-ye Tāteh, Cheshmeh Tātā, Cheshmeh Tātār (Persian: چشمه تاتار), Cheshmeh-ye Tāteh, and Kand Tātā)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3069507|Kand-e Tatar[/tpl] is a village in Shivanat Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 78, in 20 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
