[[Räni]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Tartu County

Räni is a village in Ülenurme Parish, Tartu County in southern Estonia, it's located on the southwestern side of the city of Tartu. It has a population of 466 (as of 1 September 2010).[tpl]cite web|title=Ülenurme valla arengukava aastateks 2010-2014|url=http://www.ylenurme.ee/client/common-docs/_igusaktid_2010/Arengukava_2010.doc|publisher=Ülenurme vald|pages=2[/tpl]
