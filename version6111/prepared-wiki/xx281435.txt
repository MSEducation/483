[[Ray Guell]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, American freestyle musicians, American male singers, People from Miami, Florida

Raymond Guell, better known as Ray Guell (Miami,http://freestylemania.com/ray-guell-2008-interview-exclusive/ Florida, 1 February) is a singer and composer of Freestyle music. His biggest hits in music is "Love Will Come" and "Love Is the Answer", which reached positions # 22 and # 32 respectively in Hot Dance Club Songs.
