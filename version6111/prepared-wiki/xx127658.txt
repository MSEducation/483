[[Belleview School]]

CATEGORIES: Edward Columbus Hosford buildings, National Register of Historic Places in Marion County, Florida, Mission Revival architecture in Florida, Spanish Colonial Revival architecture in Florida, School buildings on the National Register of Historic Places in Florida

The Belleview School (also known as Belleview City Hall) is a historic building in Belleview, Florida, United States. Built in 1928, it was designed by noted architect Edward Columbus Hosford in the Mission Revival style. 
The Belleview School is located at 5343 Southeast Abshier Boulevard. On March 25, 1999, it was added to the U.S. National Register of Historic Places.
