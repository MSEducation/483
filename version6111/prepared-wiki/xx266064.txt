==Background==

Matfors Idrottsförening is a Swedish sports club situated in Matfors in Sundsvall Municipality, Västernorrland County. It was founded in 1901 under the name of Thule skidklubb (Thule Skiing club). In 1916 the name was changed to Matfors IF. The club is currently active in football (men's and women's soccer), skiing and tennis.
Matfors IF currently plays in Division 4 Medelpad which is the sixth tier of Swedish football.[tpl]cite web |url= http://www.everysport.com/sport/fotboll/fotbollsserier-2011/division-4-herr/medelpad/44637|title= Medelpad - Division 4 – Fotbollsserier 2011 – Fotboll – everysport.com |format= |work= |accessdate=2011-09-22[/tpl] They play their home matches at the Thulevallen in Matfors.[tpl]cite web |url= http://svenskfotboll.se/svensk-fotboll/om-svff/distrikten/?scr=table&ftid=32412|title= Tabell och resultat - Division 4 Herrar - Svenskfotboll.se |format= |work= |accessdate=2011-09-22[/tpl]
The club is affiliated to Medelpads Fotbollförbund.[tpl]cite web |url= http://svenskfotboll.se/svensk-fotboll/om-svff/distrikten/?flid=25731 |title= Kontaktuppgifter och tävlingar – Matfors IF - Svenskfotboll.se |format= |work= |accessdate=2011-09-22[/tpl]  Matfors IF played in the 2010 Svenska Cupen but lost 0-2 at home to Friska Viljor FC in the preliminary round.
