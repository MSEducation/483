[[PGA European Tour (video game)]]

CATEGORIES: Golf video games, 2000 video games, Amiga games, Amiga 1200 games, CD32 games, Nintendo 64 games

PGA European Tour is a golf game for the Amiga (AGA), Nintendo 64 and Amiga CD32 consoles; it was released in 2000 and 1995 respectively.
