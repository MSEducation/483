[[Padarth]]

CATEGORIES: Technical festivals in India

Padarth, (Sanskrit word meaning "materials") is the annual materials festival of the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay organized by the Department of Metallurgical Engineering and Materials Science. It is India's largest college festival in the Materials Science field. Its events are held all through the year, culminating in a 2-day long festival of events held usually in the beginning of March. Padarth was started in 2006 by Arunabh Sinha, Sumeet Malik and Varun Verma (Class of 2008), with an aim of bringing forth the various phenomenon related to materials science and spread awareness of the scientific advancements in the field. To realize its goals a broad outline of Padarth in the form of competitions, lectures, workshops, exhibitions and on-the-spot events was created.[tpl]cn|date=February 2013[/tpl]

==Organization==

Padarth is an entirely student run non-profit organization.
