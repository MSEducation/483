[[Elizabeth S. Wiskemann]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Year of birth missing (living people), Swiss billionaires

Elizabeth S. Wiskemann is the widow of Martin Wiskemann, a Swiss businessman who made his fortune managing a precious metal fund. Elizabeth Wiskemann closely guards her privacy, and as a result is the only member of the Forbes 400 who is of unknown age.http://www.forbes.com/lists/2006/54/biz_06rich400_Elizabeth-S-Wiskemann_UUG3.html. Wiskemann owns 6% of Franklin Resources.http://www.forbes.com/lists/2005/54/UUG3.html
