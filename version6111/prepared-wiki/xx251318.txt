[[W.H. Dorrance House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Queen Anne architecture in New York, Houses completed in 1900, Houses in Oneida County, New York

W.H. Dorrance House is a historic home located at Camden in Oneida County, New York. It was built in 1900 and is an irregularly massed, [tpl]frac|2|1|2[/tpl]-story wood-frame structure in the Queen Anne style. It features an engaged circular tower with conical roof.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=6163|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: W.H. Dorrance House|date=March 1999|accessdate=2010-01-08 |author=Raymond W. Smith|publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation[/tpl] See also: [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=6165|title=Accompanying six photos[/tpl] 
It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1999.
