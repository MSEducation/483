[[Thrypti]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of Crete, Mountain ranges of Greece, Lasithi

Thrypti ([tpl]lang-el|Θρυπτή[/tpl], also Θρυπτής Ορη - Thryptis Ori) is a mountain range in Lasithi in eastern Crete, Greece. It stretches between Ierapetra in the southwest and Siteia in the northeast. Its highest summit, Afentis, is 1,476 m amsl.
