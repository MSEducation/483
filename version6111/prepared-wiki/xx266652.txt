[[Left, Right, Left]]

CATEGORIES: 1999 singles

"Left, Right, Left" (also known as "Left/Right") was the lead single released from Drama's debut album, Causin' Drama. The song was produced by Shawty Redd, with Drama both co-producing and writing it. 
The song became a breakout hit for Drama, becoming only a minor hit on the Billboard Hot 100, where it reached number 73, while becoming a major hit on the R&B and rap charts, peaking at number 18 and number 2 respectively. "Left, Right, Left" became one of 2000's biggest rap hits, reaching number nine on the Year-End Hot Rap Singles chart.
