[[KWRE]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in St. Louis, Missouri, Country radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 1993, 1993 establishments in Missouri

KWRE (730 AM) is a commercial radio station that is licensed to Warrenton, Missouri, serving mainly the western portion of the Greater St. Louis metropolitan area, including Warren, St. Charles, Lincoln, Franklin, and parts of St. Louis counties.  The radio station is the sister station of KFAV-FM 99.9.  Both KFAV and KWRE play country music.
