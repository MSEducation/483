[[Szastarka]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Kraśnik County

Szastarka [tpl]IPAc-pl|sz|a|s|'|t|a|r|k|a[/tpl] is a village in Kraśnik County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It is the seat of the gmina (administrative district) called Gmina Szastarka. It lies approximately [tpl]convert|12|km|mi|0[/tpl] south-east of Kraśnik and [tpl]convert|48|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south of the regional capital Lublin.
