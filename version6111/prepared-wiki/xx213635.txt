[[CCD Cerceda]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Spain, Galician football clubs, Association football clubs established in 1968, 1968 establishments in Spain

Centro Cultural e Deportivo CercedaHistory of the club is a Spanish football club based in Cerceda, in the autonomous community of Galicia. Founded in 1968, it plays in Tercera División – Group 1, holding home games at Estadio O Roxo, which has a capacity of 2,500.
