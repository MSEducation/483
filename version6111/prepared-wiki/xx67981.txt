[[Abergavenny Thursdays F.C.]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Wales, Association football clubs established in 1927, Sport in Monmouthshire, Abergavenny, 1927 establishments in Wales

Abergavenny Thursdays Football Club is a Welsh football team, a founder member of the League of Wales, but now playing in the Gwent County League Division 3.
The club was founded in 1927 in Abergavenny, though several defunct clubs had existed in the town since the mid 19th century.
