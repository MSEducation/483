[[Malleco Unido]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Chile, Association football clubs established in 1974, 1974 establishments in Chile

Club de Deportes Malleco Unido Joven is a Chilean football club from Angol, Chile. The club was founded on March 25, 1974 and currently play in the Chilean Segunda División, the third tier of the Chilean football league system.
