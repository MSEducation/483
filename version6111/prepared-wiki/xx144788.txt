[[Nerežišća]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Croatia, Brač, Split-Dalmatia County

Nerežišća is a village on the island Brač in Croatia. It has a population of 868 (2001 census), 98% which are Croats.Croatian Census 2001 (Popis stanovništva 2001)
In the past, Nerežišća was the capital of the island.Footprint Croatia by Jane Foster It was founded in the middle of the island because of fear of pirates marauding the Adriatic Sea. Once these pirates were eradicated by the Venetians, the locals started populating the settlements by the sea, especially Supetar and Sutivan, across the Brač Straits facing Split.
