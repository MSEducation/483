[[Willy Trenk-Trebitsch]]

CATEGORIES: 1902 births, 1983 deaths, Austrian male stage actors, Austrian male film actors, Austrian expatriates in Germany, Jewish Austrian male actors, Male actors from Vienna

Willy Trenk-Trebitsch (born March 11, 1902, in Vienna - died September 21, 1983, in Berlin) was an Austrian actor.  He was especially famous for his performances as Mack the Knife in The Threepenny Opera; he also had a film career.
