[[Samhita (organisation)]]

CATEGORIES: Organisations based in India

Samhita is an offshoot of the Nadathur Trust,[tpl]Cite web|url=http://articles.economictimes.indiatimes.com/2011-06-30/news/29721620_1_indian-philanthropy-social-sector-ngos|title=NS Raghavan on way to change Indian philanthropy|author=Karunakaran,Naren|publisher=The Economic Times|date=Jun 30, 2011|accessdate=Oct 13, 2011[/tpl] a private investment firm founded by N. S. Raghavan, co-founder of Infosys. The group initially focused on Non Governmental Organisations to give them clarity, resources and a support group.
