[[Darreh Lik]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Darreh Lik ([tpl]lang-fa|دره ليك[/tpl], also Romanized as Darreh Līk and Darehlīk; also known as Darrekhlik)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3059694|Darreh Lik[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 419, in 103 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
