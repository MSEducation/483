[[The Difference Between Hell and Home]]

CATEGORIES: Heavy metal albums by Canadian artists, 2013 albums

The Difference Between Hell and Home is the third studio album by Canadian melodic hardcore/metalcore band Counterparts. It was released on July 24, 2013 through Victory Records. The album received positive critical reception, generating an aggregated score of 79/100 on Metacritic, indicating "Generally favorable reviews".http://www.metacritic.com/music/the-difference-between-hell-and-home/counterparts. Retrieved on 2013-08-13.
This album also marks the first appearance by drummer Kelly Bilan as well as the last appearance by bass guitarist Eric Bazinet.
