[[Battle of Negapatam (1758)]]

CATEGORIES: Naval battles involving France, Naval battles involving Great Britain, Naval battles of the East Indies Campaign (1757–1763), Conflicts in 1758, 1758 in France, 1758 in India, Third Carnatic War

The Battle of Negapatam was an indecisive battle between a British squadron under Vice-Admiral George Pocock and French squadron under Comte d'Aché off the Carnatic coast of India near Negapatam during the Seven Years' War. The battle took place on 3 August 1758.Heritage History - List of Battles, retrieved 30 September 2008
