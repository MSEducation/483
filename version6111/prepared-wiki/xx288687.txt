[[Leon Koudelak "best of"]]

CATEGORIES: 2005 albums

Leon Koudelak "best of" is a 2005 album by the classical guitarist Leon Koudelak.
The album was released by the record label RS Promotion in Bangkok and available only in Asia, by permission of Tyrolis Music. The album also contains  some of Koudelak's unpublished recordings from Tyrolis studios.
