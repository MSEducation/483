[[2014 Palarong Pambansa]]

CATEGORIES: Palarong Pambansa, 2014 in the Philippines, 2014 in multi-sport events

The 2014 Palarong Pambansa (also known as the 2014 PALARO) is the 57th edition of the annual multi-sport for Filipino student-athletes. It is to be held in Sta. Cruz, Laguna usually in summer month of April. Since the Philippines will host the 6th ASEAN School Games 2014 probably in June 2014, 2014 Palarong Pambansa might schedule earlier. The Department of Education will give the rights of hosting to the bidding province. According to initial reports, five provinces are bidding for 2014 Palarong Pambansa.
Expected to participate on this year's edition are the student-athletes from 17 regions of the Philippines who will compete in various sports.

==Bidding==

Five provinces bid for 2014 Palarong Pambansa. These are Laguna, Batangas, Mindoro, Palawan at Marikina.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.ndb-online.com/051113/sports-news/sports-5-provinces-vying-host-2014-palaro |title=Sports: 5 Provinces Vying to Host 2014 Palaro |date=May 11, 2013[/tpl] The province of Laguna is the strongest bidder among the five provinces.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.philstar.com/sports/2013/07/24/1006031/laguna-bids-palaro |title=Laguna bids for Palaro  |date=July 24, 2013[/tpl]
Last 23 October 2013, the DepEd or Department of Education held a meeting to announce the host of 2014 Palaro. It was the province of Laguna that was awarded for the hosting of 2014 Palarong Pambansa by the Department of Education regional directors.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.sunstar.com.ph/cebu/sports/2013/10/24/laguna-wins-palaro-hosting-bid-310400 |title=Laguna wins Palaro hosting bid  |date=October 24, 2013[/tpl] It was the first time of Laguna to host the Palarong Pambansa and expected to have a grand opening ceremony.
On the other hand of the same meeting, the city of Marikina was selected to host the 6th ASEAN School Games.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.philstar.com/sports/2013/10/26/1249634/laguna-named-host-2014-palaro |title=Laguna named host of 2014 Palaro   |date=October 26, 2013[/tpl]

==Sports==

The 2014 Palarong Pambansa will feature 17 sports plus the 3 demonstration sports from last year . The 57th edition of the games may introduce wrestling as part of the grassroot program of Philippine Sports Commission. THE Wrestling Association of the Philippines (WAP) will promote the sport in schools nationwide after the Department of Education (DepEd) included wrestling in the 2014 Palarong Pambansa program. President Aquino signed Republic Act 10588, an act institutionalizing the conduct of the Palarong Pambansa, with wrestling as one of the medal sports.[tpl]cite web |url= http://www.businessmirror.com.ph/index.php/en/sports-news/17380-palaro-adopts-wrestling-in-2014-edition |title=Palaro adopts wrestling in 2014 edition  |date=August 3, 2013[/tpl]
