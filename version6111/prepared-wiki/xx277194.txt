[[James Nash (MP)]]

CATEGORIES: 14th-century births, 1400 deaths, 14th-century English people, People from Hereford, Members of the Parliament of England (pre-1707)

James Nash or Ash (died 1400), of Hereford, was an English politician.

==Family==

He was the illegitimate son of Richard Nash, MP.

==Career==

He was a Member (MP) of the Parliament of England for Hereford in January 1390, November 1390, January 1397 and 1399.http://www.historyofparliamentonline.org/volume/1386-1421/member/nash-%28ash%29-james-1400
