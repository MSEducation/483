[[John O'Dempsey]]

CATEGORIES: League of Ireland players, Bohemian F.C. players

John O'Dempsey was an Irish soccer player during the 1930s.
O'Dempsey played for Bohemians amongst others during his career in the League of Ireland.Bohemian FC match programme, Vol. 44, no. 20 King played alongside such legendary names as Johnny McMahon and Jimmy White during his time at Bohs.
