[[Bergamo–Albino light rail]]

CATEGORIES: Bergamo, Railway lines in Lombardy, Light rail in Italy, Railway lines opened in 2009

The Bergamo–Albino light rail is a light rail line that connects the city of Bergamo, Italy, with the town of Albino, in the lower part of the Val Seriana. It was built on the right-of-way of the former Valle Seriana railway, closed in 1967.
