Alphanso Cunningham (born 29 August 1980) is a Paralympian athlete from Jamaica competing mainly in category F53 throwing events.[tpl]cite web|url=http://ipc.infostradasports.com/asp/lib/theasp.asp?pageid=8937&sportid=-1&personid=681111&refreshauto=1|title=Alphanso Cunningham|accessdate=9 August 2013|publisher=infostradasports.com[/tpl]

==Career history==

Cunningham competed in the 2004 Summer Paralympics in the javelin and won gold in the F53 discus throw.
profile on paralympic.org He competed in the 2008 Beijing Paralympics without winning a medal.
He won gold in the F52/53 javelin at the London 2012 Paralympics.[tpl]cite web|title=London 2012 Paralympic Games Athletics Men's Javelin F52/53|url=http://www.paralympic.org/Athletes/Results?sport=all&games=all&Medal=all&npc=all&name=Cunningham&fname=Alphonso&gender=all|publisher=International Paralympic Committee[/tpl]
