[[Roman Catholic Diocese of São João del Rei]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1960, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Juiz de Fora, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century, 1960 establishments in Brazil

The Roman Catholic Diocese of São João del Rei ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Sancti Ioannis a Rege[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of São João del-Rei in the Ecclesiastical province of Juiz de Fora in Brazil.
