[[Kilbirnie railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Disused railway stations in North Ayrshire, Railway stations opened in 1905, Railway stations closed in 1966, Beeching closures in Scotland, Former Glasgow and South Western Railway stations

Kilbirnie railway station was a railway station serving the town of Kilbirnie, North Ayrshire, Scotland. The station was part of the Dalry and North Johnstone Line on the Glasgow and South Western Railway.

==History==

The station opened on 1 June 1905,Butt, p. 131 and closed to passengers on 27 June 1966. The station's island platform remains in place and intact, however the trackbed is now part of National Cycle Route 7.
