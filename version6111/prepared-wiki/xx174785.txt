[[1912 Cleveland Naps season]]

CATEGORIES: Cleveland Indians seasons, 1912 Major League Baseball season

The [tpl]by|1912[/tpl] Cleveland Naps season was a season in American baseball. The Naps had two of the best hitters in the majors in Shoeless Joe Jackson and Nap Lajoie. Despite this, they ended up back in the second division, finishing in fifth place with a record of 75-78.
