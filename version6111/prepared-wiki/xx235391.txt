[[Chambersburg, Indiana]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Indiana, Unincorporated communities in Orange County, Indiana

Chambersburg is an unincorporated community in Paoli Township, Orange County, Indiana, United States.

==History==

The town was laid out in 1840 by Samuel Chambers, who named it for himself.

==Geography==

Chambersburg is located at [tpl]coord|38|31|05|N|86|23|32|W|type:city[/tpl] at an elevation of 659 feet.
