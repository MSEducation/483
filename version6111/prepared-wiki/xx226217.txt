[[Kirk House (Narrowsburg, New York)]]

CATEGORIES: Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Houses completed in 1876

Kirk House is a historic home located at Narrowsburg in Sullivan County, New York.  The original section was built in the 1840s as a one story frame schoolhouse.  It was moved to its present site in 1875 and a second story was added the following year.  It measures [tpl]convert|32|by|20|ft[/tpl], three bays in length and two bays wide.  It was coated with stucco in the 1920s.  It features a center entrance and foll width, one story porch with Italianate style detailing.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=9116|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: Kirk House|date=September 1983|accessdate=2010-06-26 |author=Mark L. Peckham|publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation[/tpl] See also: [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=9126|title=Accompanying three photos[/tpl] 
It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1984.
