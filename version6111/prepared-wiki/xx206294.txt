[[Lenie 't Hart]]

CATEGORIES: 1941 births, Living people, Animal rights advocates, Dutch activists, People from Delfzijl

Lenie 't Hart (born September 16, 1941 in Farmsum) is a Dutch animal rights activist. In 1971, she founded the Seal Rehabilitation and Research Centre in Pieterburen, Groningen. She treats ill and weak seals for a certain period and then releases them back into the wild.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.allegoededoelen.nl/index.php?gd=22&t=oo|title= Zeehondencr√®che Lenie 't Hart|publisher=Allegoededoelen.nl|accessdate=2008-05-24|language=Dutch[/tpl]
