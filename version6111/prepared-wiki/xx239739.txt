[[Sladki Vrh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Municipality of Šentilj

Sladki Vrh  is a settlement in the Municipality of Šentilj in northeastern Slovenia, right next to the border with Austria. The main part of the settlement is concentrated on the right bank of the Mura River with the remainder dispersed in the Slovene Hills ([tpl]lang-sl|Slovenske gorice[/tpl]) to the south.Šentilj municipal site
