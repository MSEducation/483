[[You're the Best Break This Old Heart Ever Had]]

CATEGORIES: Ed Bruce songs, 1981 singles, Billboard Hot Country Songs number-one singles, Songs written by Wayland Holyfield, Song recordings produced by Tommy West (producer), MCA Records singles

"You're the Best Break This Old Heart Ever Had" is a song written by Wayland Holyfield and Randy Hatch, and recorded by American country music artist Ed Bruce.  It was released in 1981 as the third single from the album One to One.  "You're the Best Break This Old Heart Ever Had" would be Ed Bruce's twenty-second country hit and his only number one.  The single went to number one for one week in March 1982, and spent twenty-one weeks on the charts.[tpl]cite book|last=Whitburn|first=Joel|title=Hot Country Songs 1944 to 2008|publisher=Record Research, Inc|date=2008|page=67|isbn=0-89820-177-2[/tpl]
