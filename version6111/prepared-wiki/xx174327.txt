[[Sanchaung Township]]

CATEGORIES: Townships of Yangon

Sanchaung Township ([tpl]lang-my|စမ်းချောင်း မြို့နယ်[/tpl] [tpl]IPA-my|sáɴdʑáʊɴ mjo̰nɛ̀|[/tpl]) is located in the north central part of Yangon. The township comprises 18 wards, and shares borders with Kamayut township in the north, Kamayut township and Bahan township in the east, Kyimyindaing township in the west, and Dagon township and Ahlon township in the south. The township has 19 primary schools, two middle schools and four high schools.
