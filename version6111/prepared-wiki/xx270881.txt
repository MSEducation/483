[[Moore Hall, Cheshire]]

CATEGORIES: Grade II* listed buildings in Cheshire, Country houses in Cheshire, Grade II* listed houses

Moore Hall is a country house located in the village of Moore, Cheshire.  It was built in the early 18th century, and is constructed in roughcast brick with a slate roof.  The house has three storeys, and is in five bays.  The porch is a more modern, and is fronted by a Venetian window.  The windows are sashes.  The house is designated by English Heritage as a Grade II* listed building.[tpl]NHLE |num= 1330357|desc= Moore Hall|accessdate= 24 September 2011[/tpl]
