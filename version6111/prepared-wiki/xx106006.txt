[[Brendan Smyth (politician)]]

CATEGORIES: 1959 births, Living people, Deputy Chief Ministers of the Australian Capital Territory, Australian Capital Territory Liberal politicians, Liberal Party of Australia politicians, Members of the Australian House of Representatives, Members of the Australian House of Representatives for Canberra, Members of the Australian Capital Territory Legislative Assembly, Leaders of the Opposition in the Australian Capital Territory

Brendan Michael Smyth (born 27 July 1959) is a Liberal Party of Australia politician. From 2002 to 2006 he was  the Leader of the Opposition in the Australian Capital Territory.   He retains his shadow previous portfolios of Health, Ageing, Business and Tourism at present.
He also served briefly as Deputy Chief Minister in 2000.
