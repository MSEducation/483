[[The Peel Sessions (1986 Joy Division EP)]]

CATEGORIES: Joy Division live albums, 1986 EPs, Peel Sessions recordings, Live EPs, 1986 live albums, English-language EPs, English-language live albums, Joy Division EPs, Strange Fruit Records EPs, Strange Fruit Records live albums

The Peel Sessions is an EP by Joy Division. It was released in 1986 on the Strange Fruit label. 
It features the recordings made for the for John Peel's BBC Radio 1 show transmitted on 14 February 1979. The recordings were made at the BBC Studios, Maida Vale, London, on 31 January 1979.http://www.gerpotze.com/joydivision/jddisco1.htm Joy Division Discography None of the songs had been released before the transmission.
The EP spent thirteen weeks on the UK Indie Chart, peaking at number 4.Lazell, Barry (1998) Indie Hits 1980-1989, Cherry Red Books, ISBN 0-95172-069-4, p. 124
