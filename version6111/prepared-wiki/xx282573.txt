[[Nicholas Huish]]

CATEGORIES: 14th-century births, 15th-century deaths, 15th-century English people, People from Essex, People from Cambridgeshire, Members of the Parliament of England (pre-1707)

Nicholas Huish (fl. 1415), of Balsham, Cambridgeshire and Stansted Mountfitchet, Essex, was an English politician.

==Family==

Huish was married and had one daughter.

==Career==

He was a Member (MP) of the Parliament of England for Cambridgeshire in 1415.http://www.historyofparliamentonline.org/volume/1386-1421/member/huish-nicholas
