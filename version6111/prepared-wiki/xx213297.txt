[[WKSY-LD]]

CATEGORIES: Retro Television Network affiliates, Television stations in Georgia (U.S. state), Channel 21 low-power TV stations in the United States, Channel 21 digital TV stations in the United States, Digital low-power stations, Television channels and stations established in 1987

WKSY-LD ("Sky 21") is a low-power digital television station licensed and operating in Summerville, Georgia broadcasting over the air on UHF digital channel 21. The station is owned by Audio Graphics, Inc. From its transmitter atop the Mack White Gap east of Summerville, in addition to cable coverage, WKSY-LD covers northwestern Georgia and northeastern Alabama, including Rome, Dalton and Ringgold, Georgia; as well as Fort Payne, Alabama.
