Mario Brodmann (born April 24, 1966) is a former Swiss professional ice hockey forward who last played for EHC Chur in Switzerland's National League B.
Brodmann has participated as a member of the Swiss national team in numerous international tournaments, including the 1992 Winter Olympics.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.eliteprospects.com/player.php?player=28334|title=Eliteprospects.com - Mario Brodmann|publisher=EliteProspects.com|accessdate=2013-08-12[/tpl]
