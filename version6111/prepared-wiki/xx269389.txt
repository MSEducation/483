[[Santa Maria Consolatrice al Tiburtino]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic churches completed in 1945, Titular churches in Rome, Roman Catholic congregations established in the 20th century

St Mary the Consoler at Tiburtino ([tpl]lang-it|Santa Maria Consolatrice al Tiburtino[/tpl]) is a Roman Catholic titular parish church in Rome, located at Casal Bertone square.
