[[Ali Ansari]]

CATEGORIES: 1967 births, Living people, Alumni of University College London, Alumni of King's College London, Alumni of SOAS, University of London, British historians, Academics of the University of St Andrews, Iranologists, British people of Iranian descent

Ali Massoud Ansari ([tpl]lang-fa|علی مسعود انصاری[/tpl], born 24 November 1967 in Romehttp://www.encyclopedia.com/article-1G2-3034400017/ansari-ali-m-1967.html) is the Professor in Modern History with reference to the Middle East at St. Andrews University in Scotland, where he is also the founding director of the Institute for Iranian Studies.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.iranheritage.org/sixthbiennial/biographies.htm |title=Sixth Biennial Conference of Iranian Studies |publisher=Iran Heritage Foundation |accessdate=2008-05-02[/tpl]  He was educated at University College London (BA), King's College London (MA), and obtained his PhD from the University of London's School of Oriental and African Studies (SOAS).
He is also an Associate Fellow at Chatham House and sits on the Governing Council of the British Institute of Persian Studies (BIPS). He is a regular speaker at conferences and events regarding Iran, including "Iran's New Parliament" at the New America Foundation.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.newamerica.net/events/2008/irans_new_parliament |title=Iran's New Parliament |publisher=New America Foundation |accessdate=2008-05-02| archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20080517065116/http://www.newamerica.net/events/2008/irans_new_parliament| archivedate= 17 May 2008 | deadurl= no[/tpl] His work appears in The Guardian,[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/commentisfree/2007/jan/30/comment.usa1 |title=Only the US hawks can save the Iranian president now |work=The Guardian |date=January 30, 2007 |author=Ali M. Ansari |accessdate=2008-05-02[/tpl] The Independent,[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.independent.co.uk/opinion/commentators/ali-ansari-they-are-marching-as-to-war-523029.html |title=They are marching as to war |work=The Independent |date=15 January 2006 |author=Ali M. Ansari |accessdate=2008-05-02[/tpl] and the New Statesman,[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.newstatesman.com/200609040032 |title=Dictators: Reform and the mullahs |work=New Statesman |date=4 September 2006 |author=Ali M. Ansari |accessdate=2008-05-02[/tpl] among other publications.
Ansari is the son of Mohammad Ali Massoud Ansari, cousin of Farah Pahlavi and Mariam Dariabegi.  He married Marjon Esfandiary in 2010, after which he had a celebration held at Chatham House.http://www.royalrussell.co.uk/announcements/Marriages_printer.php
