[[Dover Kosashvili]]

CATEGORIES: 1966 births, Living people, Israeli film directors, Israeli screenwriters, Film directors from Georgia (country), Screenwriters from Georgia (country), Ophir Award winners: Directors, Ophir Award winners: Screenwriters

Dover Kosashvili (born 8 December 1966) is an Israeli film director and screenwriter of Georgian descent. He has directed five films since 1998. His film Late Marriage was screened in the Un Certain Regard section at the 2001 Cannes Film Festival.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.festival-cannes.com/en/archives/ficheFilm/id/2000106/year/2001.html |title=Festival de Cannes: Late Marriage |accessdate=2009-10-19|work=festival-cannes.com[/tpl]
