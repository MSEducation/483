[[Germany at the 1936 Summer Olympics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 1936 Summer Olympics, Germany at the Summer Olympics, 1936 in German sport

Nazi Germany was the host nation and top medal recipient for the 1936 Summer Olympics in Berlin. 433 competitors, 389 men and 44 women, took part in 143 events in 22 sports.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/countries/GER/summer/1936/ |title=Germany at the 1936 Berlin Summer Games |accessdate=2010-05-31 |work=sports-reference.com[/tpl]
