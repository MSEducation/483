[[Flower/Halloween]]

CATEGORIES: Sonic Youth songs, 1985 singles

Flower/Halloween is a single by the alternative rock band Sonic Youth. It was released in 1985 on Homestead Records.

==Track listing==

The tracks were recorded and mixed at Radio Tokyo in Venice, California in January 1985.

==Personnel==

==Releases==

Some Blast First releases are in translucent orange vinyl.
The CD release of the Bad Moon Rising album from Geffen Records also contains this single.

==Vinyl Etchings==

==Cover Versions==

Halloween was covered by Mudhoney, originally recorded in 1988 for the split single Touch Me I'm Sick/Halloween, on which each band covered each other. The song was also released on Superfuzz Bigmuff Plus Early Singles in 1990.
