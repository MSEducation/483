[[David Khurtsidze]]

CATEGORIES: 1993 births, Living people, Russian footballers

David Khvichayevich Khurtsidze ([tpl]lang-ru|Давид Хвичаевич Хурцидзе[/tpl]; born July 4, 1993) is a Russian football midfielder, who last played for  Zenit Penza on loan from CSKA Moscow.
In June 2013 Khurtsidze moved on a year long loan to Zenit Penza.[tpl]cite web|publisher=PFC CSKA Moscow|title=Трансферные новости|url=http://pfc-cska.com/ru/news/?id=6778|date=20 November 2013|language=Russian[/tpl] He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Zenit Penza on July 16, 2013 in a game against FC Metallurg-Oskol Stary Oskol.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Russian Football Union|url=http://rfspro.ru/cgi-bin/player/script2.cgi?par1=117990|title=Career Summary|date=10 August 2013[/tpl]
