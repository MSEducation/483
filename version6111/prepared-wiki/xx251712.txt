[[Lower Marsh Creek Presbyterian Church]]

CATEGORIES: Presbyterian churches in Pennsylvania, Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in Pennsylvania, Religious buildings completed in 1790, Churches in Adams County, Pennsylvania

Lower Marsh Creek Presbyterian Church is a historic Presbyterian church located near Orrtanna, Highland Township, Adams County, Pennsylvania. It was built in 1790, and is a three bay wide and six bay deep rectangular rubble fieldstone building.  It features a semi-circular opening set in the front gable over the main entrance.[tpl]cite web| url = https://www.dot7.state.pa.us/ce/SelectWelcome.asp| title = "National Historic Landmarks & National Register of Historic Places in Pennsylvania"| publisher = CRGIS: Cultural Resources Geographic Information System| format = Searchable database[/tpl] Note: This includes [tpl]cite web| url = https://www.dot7.state.pa.us/ce_imagery/phmc_scans/H001262_01H.pdf | title = National Register of Historic Places Inventory Nomination Form: Lower Marsh Creek Presbyterian Church| accessdate = 2011-12-08| author = Arthur M. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1980.
