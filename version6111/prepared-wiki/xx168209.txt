[[Daisuke Nakamura]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 births, Japanese voice actors, Living people

[tpl]nihongo|Daisuke Nakamura |中村太亮|Nakamura Daisuke|born 21 September 1985 in Chiba prefecture, Japan[/tpl] is a Japanese voice actor and actor. Daisuke is probably most known for the roles as: Colonello (Reborn!) and Yamato Akitsuki (Suzuka).
