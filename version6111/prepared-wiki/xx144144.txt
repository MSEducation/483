[[Hotel Casa del Mar]]

CATEGORIES: Hotel buildings completed in 1926, Hotel buildings on the National Register of Historic Places in California, Buildings and structures in Santa Monica, California, National Register of Historic Places listings in Los Angeles County, California, Hotels established in 1998, History of Santa Monica, California, Italian Renaissance Revival architecture in the United States, Renaissance Revival architecture in California, Visitor attractions in Santa Monica, California

The Hotel Casa del Mar is a historic luxury hotel located on the beach in Santa Monica, California. 

==History==

Club Casa del Mar was constructed by brothers Jack and Til Harter, owners of the H & H Holding Company.http://www.hotelcasadelmar.com/sites/default/files/press/casa_historical_retro_0.pdf It opened on May 1, 1926 as a beach club. The $2 million building, was designed in the Italian Renaissance Revival style by noted architect Charles F. Plummerhttp://www.hotelcasadelmar.com/sites/default/files/press/casa_del_mar_hotel_fact_sheet_0.pdf and contained 129 guest rooms and lavish facilities.
It became a hotel and recreation center for U.S. service personnel during World War II and resumed its role as a beach club and hotel through the 1950s. In 1959 it became the headquarters of the Synanon drug treatment program.http://www.hotelcasadelmar.com/sites/default/files/press/casa_del_mar_hotel_fact_sheet_0.pdf It served as a Pritikin Longevity Center from 1978-1997.http://www.hotelcasadelmar.com/sites/default/files/press/casa_del_mar_hotel_fact_sheet_0.pdf In 1998, The Edward Thomas Hospitality Corporation, owners of the adjacent Shutters on the Beach Hotel, acquired the propertyin November 1997 and spent $50 million converting it into a luxury hotel. It reopened in October 1999.
The hotel is a member of The Leading Hotels of the World and  is on the National Register of Historic Places.
