[[Blackwater, North Carolina]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Surry County, North Carolina, Unincorporated communities in North Carolina

Blackwater (sometimes written as Black Water) is a small Unincorporated community in the Eldora Township of Surry County, North Carolina.  The community is centered around the intersections of Ararat Road, Crotts Road and Siloam Road.  Prominent landmarks include Blackwater Community Church, Blackwater United Methodist Church and the now defunct Blackwater Grocery.
