[[Beizhen]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Liaoning

Beizhen ([tpl]zh|s=北镇 |t=北鎮 |p=Běizhèn |l=north town[/tpl]) is a city in west-central Liaoning province of Northeast China. It is under the administration of Jinzhou City.
