[[Lorentz Reitan]]

CATEGORIES: 1946 births, Living people, Norwegian musicologists, Bergen University College faculty

Lorentz Reitan (born 5 January 1946) is a Norwegian musicologist.
He was born in Haugesund. He was an associate professor at Bergen Teacher's College from 1976 to 1989, then director of the Bergen International Festival from 1990 to 1995.
