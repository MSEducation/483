[[Min Orkizesai]]

CATEGORIES: 2007 singles, Eleftheria Arvanitaki songs, Greek-language songs

"Min Orkizesai" ([tpl]lang-el|"Μην Ορκίζεσαι"[/tpl]; English: "Don't take an oath") is a pop song performed by Greek singer/songwriter Eleftheria Arvanitaki with lyrics by Nikos Moraitis.[tpl]cite web|url=http://beta.mad.tv/news/new.php?id=29739|title=Συλλεκτική έκδοση για Ελευθερία Αρβανιτάκη|date=2008-04-04|publisher=MAD TV|language=Greek|accessdate=2008-10-06[/tpl] The song was produced for Arvanitaki's greatest hits compilation Dinata 1986-2007 and was released as the album's first single.[tpl]cite web|url=http://beta.mad.tv/news/new.php?id=27797|title=Ελευθερία Αρβανιτάκη: best of|date=2007-12-04|publisher=MAD TV|language=Greek|accessdate=2008-10-06[/tpl] "Min Orkizesai" is a cover version of "Come Mona Lisa" by the Italian singer Mango.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ekathimerini.com/4dcgi/_w_articles_civ_1_11/01/2008_92025|title=Italian flavor to music hits|last=Rigopoulos|first=Dimitris|date=2008-01-11|publisher=Kathimerini|accessdate=2008-10-06[/tpl]
