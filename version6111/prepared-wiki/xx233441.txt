Facundo Sebastián Roncaglia ([tpl]IPA-es|roŋˈkaɣlja|local[/tpl], [tpl]IPA-it|roŋˈkaʎʎa|lang[/tpl]; born 10 February 1987) is an Argentine professional footballer who plays for ACF Fiorentina in Serie A. Mainly a central defender, he can also play as a right back.

==Football career==

Born in Chajarí, Entre Ríos, Roncaglia made his professional debuts with Boca Juniors, his first top flight match being on 21 October 2007 in a 1–1 draw against Estudiantes de La Plata. He helped the capital team win the 2008 Apertura.
On 27 July 2009, Roncaglia signed with La Liga side RCD Espanyol on loan,[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.goal.com/es-la/news/1546/argentina/2009/07/27/1407224/boca-confirmado-facundo-roncaglia-se-va-cedido-al-espanyol|title=Boca: Confirmado, Facundo Roncaglia se va cedido al Espanyol|trans_title=Boca: Confirmed, Facundo Roncaglia loaned to Espanyol|publisher=Goal.com|language=Spanish|date=27 July 2009|accessdate=5 July 2012[/tpl] being part of a squad that also featured five countrymen – including manager Mauricio Pochettino and former Boca teammate Juan Forlín. He returned to his previous team at the end of the season, only to move to Estudiantes immediately, in the same predicament.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.ole.com.ar/estudiantes/Rorro-Torito_0_287371326.html|title=Con Rorro y el Torito|trans_title=With Rorro and Torito|publisher=Olé|language=Spanish|date=26 June 2010|accessdate=27 June 2010[/tpl]
Roncaglia left Boca in July 2012 as a free agent,[tpl]cite news|url=http://muyboca.com.ar/16996/roncaglia-y-una-renovacion-que-se-complica-cada-dia-mas|title=Roncaglia y una renovación que se complica cada día más|trans_title=Roncaglia and an increasingly difficult renewal|publisher=Muy Boca|language=Spanish|date=4 May 2012|accessdate=5 July 2012[/tpl] having scored in the first leg of the 2012 Copa Libertadores final against Sport Club Corinthians Paulista late in the previous month (1–1 home draw, 1–3 aggregate loss).[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.as.com/futbol/articulo/final-sigue-abierta/20120628dasdasftb_2/Tes|title=La final sigue abierta|trans_title=Still an open final|publisher=Diario AS|language=Spanish|date=28 June 2012|accessdate=5 July 2012[/tpl] He joined ACF Fiorentina in Italy immediately after.
