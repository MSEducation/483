Julien Darui (16 February 1916 – 13 December 1987) was a French  football goalkeeper, who had stints as a coach after his playing career. 
Born in Luxembourg during World War I, Darui was capped 25 times for France. In 1999, he was elected best French goalkeeper of the century by L'Équipe.Galerie des grands joueurs du Red Star: D
