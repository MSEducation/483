[[Carmenta guyanensis]]

CATEGORIES: Sesiidae

Carmenta guyanensis is a moth of the Sesiidae family.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.nic.funet.fi/pub/sci/bio/life/insecta/lepidoptera/ditrysia/sesioidea/sesiidae/sesiinae/carmenta/index.html |title=Carmenta at funet.fi |publisher=Nic.funet.fi |date= |accessdate=2011-12-16[/tpl] It was described by Le Cerf in 1917, and is known from Brazil, French Guiana, Bolivia, and Peru.
