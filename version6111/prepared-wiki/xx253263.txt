[[Kyriakos Kyritsis]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing, Year of death missing, People from Lunxhëri, Northern Epirotes, Greek lawyers, 19th-century Greek people, Greek MPs 1915–17

Kyriakos Kyritsis ([tpl]lang-el|Κυριάκος Κυρίτσης[/tpl]) was a Greek lawyer and politician from the village of Stegopoli (Lunxhëri), in modern southern Albania (Northern Epirus among Greeks). He supported financially the local Greek revolt of 1878 against Ottoman rule. Because of this involvement his possessions in Butrint-Saranda area were confiscated.[tpl]cite book | last = Ruches | first = Pyrrhus J.| title = Albania's captives | publisher = Argonaut | year = 1965 | location = Chicago | pages = 54, 97 |ref= | isbn = | url= http://books.google.com/books?id=01eQAAAAIAAJ&q=&pgis=1[/tpl]
