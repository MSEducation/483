[[Söråkers FF]]

CATEGORIES: Sport in Medelpad, Football clubs in Sweden, 2001 establishments in Sweden

Söråkers FF is a Swedish football club located in Söråker.[tpl]cite web |url= http://www.svenskalag.se/sorakersff |title= Söråkers FF – Fotboll Klubb |format= |work= |accessdate=2011-09-23[/tpl]

==Background==

Söråkers FF currently plays in Division 4 Medelpad which is the sixth tier of Swedish football.[tpl]cite web |url= http://www.everysport.com/sport/fotboll/fotbollsserier-2011/division-4-herr/medelpad/44637|title= Medelpad - Division 4 – Fotbollsserier 2011 – Fotboll – everysport.com |format= |work= |accessdate=2011-09-23[/tpl] They play their home matches at the Söråkers IP in Söråker.[tpl]cite web |url= http://svenskfotboll.se/svensk-fotboll/om-svff/distrikten/?scr=table&ftid=32412|title= Tabell och resultat - Division 4 Herrar - Svenskfotboll.se |format= |work= |accessdate=2011-09-23[/tpl]
The club is affiliated to Medelpads Fotbollförbund.[tpl]cite web |url= http://svenskfotboll.se/svensk-fotboll/om-svff/distrikten/?flid=32449 |title= Kontaktuppgifter och tävlingar – Söråkers FF - Svenskfotboll.se |format= |work= |accessdate=2011-09-23[/tpl] On Saturday 14 August 2010 Söråkers FF's attendance record was set when 726 people watched the derby match between Söråkers FF and Ljustorps IF.
