[[Yagyū Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Saitama Prefecture, Stations of Tobu Railway, Railway stations opened in 1929, 1929 establishments in Japan

[tpl]Nihongo|Yagyū Station|柳生駅|Yagyū-eki[/tpl] is a railway station on the Tobu Nikko Line in Kazo, Saitama, Japan, operated by the private railway operator Tobu Railway.

==Lines==

Yagyū Station is served by the Tobu Nikko Line, and is 23.6 km from the starting point of the line at [tpl]Ja-stalink|Tōbu-Dōbutsu-Kōen[/tpl].

In fiscal 2011, the station was used by an average of 1,539 passengers daily.
