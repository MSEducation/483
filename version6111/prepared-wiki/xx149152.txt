[[Card Mondor]]

CATEGORIES: 1922 births, 2001 deaths, Australian magicians, People from Melbourne

Card Mondor (1922-2001) was an Australian magician and stage performer. A one time assistant for the Great Virgil,[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.geniimagazine.com/wiki/index.php/Card_Mondor |title=Card Mondor |work=MagicPedia Listing|publisher=Genii Magazine |accessdate=2009-12-10 [/tpl] he gained fame as a performer in the United States, most notably for entertaining troops during World War II. He was featured on the cover of Genii (magazine) in April 1947.[ref]
