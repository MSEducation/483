[[Talk-Action=0]]

CATEGORIES: 2010 albums, D.O.A. (band) albums, Sudden Death Records albums

Talk - Action = 0 is the 17th studio album by Canadian punk rock band D.O.A.. Notably, the title had already appeared as the slogan on the back of their 2002 album Win The Battle.
All songs are written by Joe Keithley except where noted.
