[[Arne Lindtner Næss]]

CATEGORIES: 1944 births, Living people, Norwegian male film actors, Norwegian film directors, Norwegian writers, Olsen Gang, Norwegian television producers, Norwegian male television actors

Arne Lindtner Næss (born 19 December 1944, [tpl]age|1944|12|12[/tpl]) is a Norwegian actor, director, television producer, and writer. He has had many film and television roles. The best known may be Frode Birkeland in the television series Familiesagaen De syv søstre on TV2.
His father is the director and writer Peder Hamdahl Næss.
