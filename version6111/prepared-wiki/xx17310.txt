===Beaches and parks===

The old corporation limit of Chennai has a total coast length of about 19 km, which has more than doubled with the expanded corporation limits. Marina Beach runs for [tpl]convert|6|km|mi|abbr=on[/tpl], spanning along the shoreline of the city between the deltas of Cooum and Adyar, and is the second longest urban beach in the world.[tpl]cite book|url=http://books.google.com/?id=ZJfu5vbPbA0C&pg=PA81&dq=Chennai+World's+second+largest+beach#v=onepage&q&f=false|title=Observing our environment from space: new solutions for a new millennium ... |author=EARSeL|year=2002|isbn=90-5809-254-2|publisher=A. A. Balakema|accessdate=28 December 2012[/tpl] Elliot's Beach lies south of the Adyar delta.
Chennai is one of the few cities in the world that accommodates a national park, the Guindy National Park, within its limits.[tpl]cite web|title=Guindy National Park|publisher=Tamil Nadu Forest Department|url=http://www.forests.tn.nic.in/WildBiodiversity/np_gnp.html|accessdate=28 December 2012[/tpl] The city has an estimated 4.5 percent of its area under green cover.[tpl]cite news|url=http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2011-05-09/chennai/29524468_1_saplings-d-narasimhan-neem-tree|title=Massive afforestation drive to begin in Chennai|work=The Times of India|date=9 May 2011|accessdate=28 December 2012|last=Janardhanan|first=Arun[/tpl] This enables Chennai residents to go birding in Chennai. The seven zones of the old corporation limits has about 260 parks, many of which suffer poor maintenance.[tpl]cite news|url=http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2012-08-24/chennai/33365243_1_new-parks-open-space-reservation-osr|title=More parks, but Chennai needs green thumb|work=The Times of India|date=24 August 2012|accessdate=28 December 2012|last=Philip|first=Christin Mathew[/tpl] The city has a per capita park space of 0.41 sq m, which is the least among all metros in India. The eight zones in the newly added areas of the city have about 265 locations that have been identified for development of new parks.[ref]
