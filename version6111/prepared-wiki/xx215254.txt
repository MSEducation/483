[[Burns Junction, Oregon]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Malheur County, Oregon, Unincorporated communities in Oregon

Burns Junction is a highway junction and unincorporated community in Malheur County, Oregon, United States. Burns Junction lies at the intersection of U.S. Route 95 and Oregon Route 78, about [tpl]convert|80|mi|km[/tpl] southeast of the Harney County city of Burns.
