[[Andrijana Videnović]]

CATEGORIES: Serbian actresses, Serbian film actresses, Serbian stage actresses, Serbian television actresses, University of Pristina faculty, Living people, 1964 births, University of Arts in Belgrade alumni

Andrijana Videnović (born 1 September 1964) is a Serbian theater and movie actress and Associate Professor of Diction at the University of Pristina Faculty of Arts, in North Kosovo.
