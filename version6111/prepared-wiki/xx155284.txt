[[Live 05]]

CATEGORIES: 2005 EPs, Astralwerks EPs, Astralwerks live albums, 2005 live albums, Live EPs, Virgin Records live albums, Virgin Records EPs

Live 05 is a digital-only EP by The Chemical Brothers released in 2005.  It contains live tracks recorded during the band's 2005 tour, promoting their album, Push the Button.
