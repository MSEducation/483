[[Nicktoons Nitro]]

CATEGORIES: 2009 video games, Nicktoons video games, Karting video games, Racing video games, Arcade games, Video games based on SpongeBob SquarePants, Danny Phantom video games, Invader Zim video games, The Adventures of Jimmy Neutron: Boy Genius video games, Avatar: The Last Airbender games, Video games based on The Fairly OddParents

Not to be confused with Nicktoons Racing or Nicktoons Winners Cup Racing.
Nicktoons Nitro is a racing game which is a sequel to Nicktoons Racing and Nicktoons Winners Cup Racing. It features characters from SpongeBob SquarePants, Danny Phantom, The Adventures of Jimmy Neutron: Boy Genius, The Fairly OddParents, Invader Zim, Avatar: The Last Airbender. The game was released in 2009 as an arcade game by developer Raw Thrills.

==Story==

An average day in Bikini Bottom turns sour for SpongeBob SquarePants when he gets sucked into a mysterious portal. Several other Nicktoons are also snatched away from their own worlds and all are soon stranded in a mysterious laboratory.
Soon, they see the being responsible for their kidnapping: Evil Nitro, who desires that the Nicktoons race through his planet solely for his amusement. Given unique vehicles and sent into racetracks based on their homeworlds, the Nicktoons compete not just for victory, but to escape from their mysterious captor.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.rawthrills.com/game.php?GameID=11 |title=Games | Raw Thrills, Inc |publisher=Rawthrills.com |date=2012-02-16 |accessdate=2012-02-23[/tpl]
