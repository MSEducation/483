[[India at the 1964 Winter Olympics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 1964 Winter Olympics, India at the Olympics

India competed at the Winter Olympic Games for the first time at the 1964 Winter Olympics in Innsbruck, Austria.
The sole athlete representing India was Jeremy Bujakowski, who competed in the Men's Downhill event in Alpine Skiing.
