[[KRQU]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Wyoming

KRQU (98.7 FM) is an American radio station licensed to Laramie, Wyoming, USA. The station serves the Cheyenne area.  The station is currently owned by Murray Grey Broadcasting, Inc.

==History==

Prior to switching frequencies, KRQU was located at 104.5 FM.  The station was assigned the call sign KHIH on May 2, 2005.  On December 29, 2005, the station changed its call sign to KUSZ, and on March 5, 2008, to KRQU. On April 6, 2011, changed its call sign to KAAZ. On November 26, 2012, changed its call sign to the current KRQU.[tpl]cite web|url=http://licensing.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=82198&Callsign=KRQU |title=KRQU Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division |accessdate=April 8, 2011[/tpl]
