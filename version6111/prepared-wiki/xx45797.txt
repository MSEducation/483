[[Photographic and Imaging Manufacturers Association]]

CATEGORIES: Photography organizations

The Photographic and Imaging Manufacturers Association, or PIMA, was founded in 1946 under the name National Association of Photographic Manufacturers (NAPM). In 1997 the name was changed to PIMA.[tpl]Citation needed|date=July 2009[/tpl]
In 2001 PIMA merged with the Digital Imaging Group to form the International Imaging Industry Association.[tpl]Citation needed|date=July 2009[/tpl]
