[[Mark Cameron (cricketer)]]

CATEGORIES: 1981 births, Living people, Australian cricketers, New South Wales cricketers, Cricketers from New South Wales

Mark Cameron (born 31 January 1981) is an Australian former cricketer played Australian domestic cricket for New South Wales. He is a right-handed batsman and a right-arm fast bowler. Cameron was drafted into the squad for the Australia A tour of India in September 2008. He later left New South Wales to play for Western Australia and the Perth Scorchers, but retired without playing a game for either team due to injury.
