[[(13929) 1988 PL]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1988, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(13929) 1988 PL is a main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on August 13, 1988.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
