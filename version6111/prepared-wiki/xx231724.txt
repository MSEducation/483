[[The Reign Goes On]]

CATEGORIES: Whitecross video albums, 1992 video albums, Music video compilation albums, 1992 compilation albums

The Reign Goes On is the only music video recording from the Christian metal band Whitecross. It was only available on the VHS video tape format and has been out of print for several years. The video album includes with concert, concept, behind-the-scenes and interview footage. Released in 1992 by Star Song Communications, 45 minutes.

==Contents==

During the end credits there is a live-recording from the In the Kingdom concert at Cornerstone '91 with a chorus of Christian artists present at the festival. Among these are John Schlitt & Louie Weaver from Petra.
