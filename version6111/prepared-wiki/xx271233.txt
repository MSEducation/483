Artyom Igorevich Popov ([tpl]lang-ru|Артём Игоревич Попов[/tpl]; born August 30, 1992) is a Russian professional football player. Currently, he plays for FC Zenit Saint Petersburg.[tpl]cite web|publisher=PFC CSKA Moscow|title=Переходы|url=http://pfc-cska.com/ru/news/?id=6752|date=10 June 2013|language=Russian[/tpl]
 He made his Russian Premier League debut for PFC CSKA Moscow on 24 September 2011 in a game against FC Volga Nizhny Novgorod.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Russian Premier League|url=http://rfpl.org/reports/id7662|title=Game report by RFPL|date=24 September 2011[/tpl]
