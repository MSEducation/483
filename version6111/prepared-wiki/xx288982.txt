[[Zarrineh Rud Rural District (Zanjan Province)]]

CATEGORIES: Rural Districts of Zanjan Province, Khodabandeh County

Zarrineh Rud Rural District ([tpl]lang-fa|دهستان زرينه رود[/tpl]) is a rural district (dehestan) in Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 16,067, in 3,258 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl] The rural district has 28 villages.
