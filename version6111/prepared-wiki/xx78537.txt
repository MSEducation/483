[[Kitamorokata District, Miyazaki]]

CATEGORIES: Districts in Miyazaki Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Kitamorokata|北諸県郡|Kitamorokata-gun[/tpl] is a district located in Miyazaki Prefecture, Japan.
As of the Miyakonojō merger (but with 2003 population statistics), the district has an estimated population of 26,752 and a density of 243 persons per km². The total area is 110.01 km².
