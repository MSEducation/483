[[Jolimont railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1901

Jolimont railway station is located on the South Morang and Hurstbridge lines in Victoria, Australia. It serves the inner Melbourne suburb of East Melbourne opening on 21 October 1901.Jolimont Vicsig
Jolimont is one of two closest stations to the Melbourne Cricket Ground, the other being Richmond. During events at the MCG extra exit gates are open to reduce crowding.Public Transport Melbourne Cricket Ground

==Platforms & services==

Jolimont has two side platforms. It is serviced by Metro Trains' South Morang and Hurstbridge line services.South Morang Line timetable Public Transport VictoriaHurstbridge Line timetable Public Transport Victoria
