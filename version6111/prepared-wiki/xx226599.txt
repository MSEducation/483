[[Chinna Mapillai]]

CATEGORIES: 1993 films, Indian films, Tamil films of 1993, Tamil-language films, Film scores by Ilaiyaraaja

Chinna Mapillai (English: Younger Son-in-law) is a 1993 Indian Tamil-language film starring Prabhu Ganesan and Sukanya in lead roles. The film was a commercial success upon release in January 1993.http://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=P9oYG7HA76QC&dat=19930117&printsec=frontpage&hl=en It was remade in Telugu as Chinna Alludu (1993), in Hindi as Coolie No.1 (1995)  and in Kannada as Coolie Raja (1999).

==Plot==

Prabhu is a kind-hearted poor man who works as a coolie in bus stand. Visu, a marriage broker wants him to pose as a rich man from Singapore and marry Sukanya to break the arrogance and her money-minded father (Radha Ravi). Her father finds out that he is a coolie. But Prabhu brilliantly lies that it is his younger brother. How he reforms both of them forms the story.
