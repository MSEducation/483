[[Daylaqlı]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Shakhbuz District

Daylaqlı (until 2003,Azerbaijan Parllament: the act that renamed the village and municipality Əzızbəyov and Azizbekov) is a village and municipality in the Shakhbuz Rayon of Nakhchivan, Azerbaijan.  It has a population of 395.
