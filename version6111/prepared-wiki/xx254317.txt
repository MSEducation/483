[[Deorwulf]]

CATEGORIES: Bishops of London, 800s deaths, Year of birth unknown

Deorwulf (died between 867 and 896) was a medieval Bishop of London.
Deorwulf was consecrated between 845 and 860. He died between 867 and 896.Fryde, et al. Handbook of British Chronology p. 220
