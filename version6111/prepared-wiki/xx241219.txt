[[Jungar Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Jungar Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠵᠡᠭᠦᠨᠭᠠᠷ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Jegünɣar qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=准格尔旗 |t=准格爾旗 |p=Zhǔngé'ěr Qí[/tpl]) is a banner of western Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China, lying on the western (right) bank of the Yellow River and bordering the provinces of Shanxi to the southeast and Shaanxi to the southwest. It is under the administration of Ordos City.
