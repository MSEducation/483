[[List of Mexican records in swimming]]

CATEGORIES: Swimming records, Swimming in Mexico

The Mexican Records in Swimming are the fastest times ever swum by a swimmer from Mexico. These records are kept/maintained by Mexico's national swimming federation: la Federación Mexicana de Natación (FMN).
