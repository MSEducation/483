[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Lages]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1927, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Florianópolis, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Lages ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Lagensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Lages in the Ecclesiastical province of Florianópolis in Brazil.
