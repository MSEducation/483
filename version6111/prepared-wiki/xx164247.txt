[[Dickerson Middle School]]

CATEGORIES: Schools in Cobb County, Georgia, Public middle schools in Georgia (U.S. state), Educational institutions established in 1980, Marietta, Georgia

Dickerson Middle School (established 1980) is a Blue Ribbon middle school in Marietta, Georgia, United States, as part of the Cobb County School District. Its feeder schools are Mt. Bethel, Sope Creek, Timber Ridge, and East Side Elementary Schools. The principal is Dr. Carole Brink who replaced Dr. Daniels in September 2007.
As of the 2005-06 school year, the school had an enrollment of 1,410 students and 89.1 classroom teachers (on an FTE basis), for a student-teacher ratio of 15.8.Dickerson Middle School, National Center for Education Statistics. Accessed November 21, 2007.

==Music==

Dickerson has a respected music program headed by Mr. Palmer and assisted by Mr. Brown (also the percussion director) along with Ms. Laux who is the orchestra conductor. The Dickerson orchestra, though mostly the Dickerson band and from the band, the percussion, have been recognized with highly praised recognitions. Dickerson Middle School also has a school choir led by Ms. Dobbins and Ms. Smith.
