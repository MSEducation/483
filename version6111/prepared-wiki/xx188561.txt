[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Uruaçu]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1956, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Brasília, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Uruaçu ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Uruassuensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Uruaçu in the Ecclesiastical province of Brasília in Brazil.
