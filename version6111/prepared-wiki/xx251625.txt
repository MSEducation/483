[[Guilford Center Presbyterian Church]]

CATEGORIES: Presbyterian churches in New York, Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Religious buildings completed in 1817, 19th-century Presbyterian church buildings, Churches in Chenango County, New York

Guilford Center Presbyterian Church is a historic Presbyterian church on County Road 36 in Guilford Center, Chenango County, New York. It was built in 1817 and is a large [tpl]frac|1|1|2[/tpl]-story rectangular wood-frame building, five bays long and three bays wide.  It is built into the side of a hill on a cut-stone foundation.  It features a three-stage tower with a spire.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=100744|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: Guilford Center Presbyterian Church|date=November 2003|accessdate=2010-02-20 |author=Kathleen LaFrank|publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation[/tpl] See also: [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=100746|title=Accompanying seven photos[/tpl] 
It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 2004.
