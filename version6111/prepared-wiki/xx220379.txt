[[Bob Correll]]

CATEGORIES: American stunt performers, 1948 births, Living people, American motorcycle racers

Bob "Crazy" Correll (born January 7, 1942) is a former daredevil and stuntman from Long Beach, California. According to his official biography from Balls, Unlimited, Inc., Correll has enjoyed a varied career racing motorcycles, drag cars, stock cars, and go-carts. He has also flown hang gliders, sail planes, powered planes, and hot-air balloons.Official biography of Bob Correll, available directly from Aerovisions, Inc. or Balls, Unlimited, Inc.
