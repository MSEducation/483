[[Helmi (given name)]]

CATEGORIES: Feminine given names, Finnish feminine given names, Estonian feminine given names

Helmi is a feminine given name. In Finnish the name means pearl. It is also used as a short form of the name Vilhelmiina or Vilhelmina.Behind the Name The name was the 10th most popular name for baby girls born in Finland in 2007.Helsingin Sanomat, October 16, 2007.Aino and Eetu top list of most popular children's names.
