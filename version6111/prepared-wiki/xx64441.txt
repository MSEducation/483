[[Esta É a Nossa Pátria Bem Amada]]

CATEGORIES: National anthems, Guinea-Bissauan music, National symbols of Guinea-Bissau, Portuguese-language songs

"[tpl]lang|pt|Esta É a Nossa Pátria Bem Amada[/tpl]" ("This Is Our Beloved Country") is the national anthem  of Guinea-Bissau. Written by Amílcar Cabral and composed by Xiao He, it was adopted upon independence in 1974. It was also the national anthem of Cape Verde until 1996, when a new anthem ([tpl]lang|pt|Cântico da Liberdade[/tpl]) was chosen by the latter country.
