Junior Sa'u (born 18 April 1987) is a New Zealand professional rugby league player for the Salford Red Devils of the English Super League. He primarily plays centre but can fill in at wing and second-row.

==Playing career==

Born in Auckland, New Zealand, Sa'u played his junior football for Logan Brothers in Queensland, Australia before being signed by the Canberra Raiders. He played for the Raiders' Premier League team in 2006, scoring 7 trieshttp://www.rleague.com//db/player/s/sau_junior/index.php before signing a 4-year contract starting in 2008 to be a part of the newly cleaned out Newcastle Knights.
In Round 13 of the 2008 NRL season he made his NRL debut for the Knights against the Canterbury-Bankstown Bulldogs. He scored 2 tries on debut. In June 2011, Sa'u re-signed with the Knights for a further 2 years.[tpl]cite news| url=http://www.smh.com.au/rugby-league/league-news/cowboys-lifeline-for-paterson-20110601-1fgpm.html | work=The Sydney Morning Herald | title=Cowboys' lifeline for Paterson[/tpl]
On 18 August 2012, Sa'u was released from the final year of his Knights contract and signed with the Melbourne Storm on a 1-year contract starting in 2013.http://www.newcastleknights.com.au/news-display/Sau-signs-with-the-Storm/61793http://www.melbournestorm.com.au/news-display/Sau-signs-with-Storm/61963
In June 2013, it was announced that Sa'u had signed a two-year deal with Salford Red Devils in the English Super League starting in 2014.

==Representative career==

In 2008, Sa'u was named in the Samoa training squad for the 2008 Rugby League World Cup but did not make the final squad.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.leagueunlimited.com/article.php?newsid=16750|title=Samoa name World Cup Squad|publisher=League Unlimited|date=2008-08-05|accessdate=2008-08-06[/tpl] 
In 2009, Sa'u was selected in the New Zealand Kiwis Four Nations squad and made his international debut for the Kiwis against Tonga.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.nzherald.co.nz/sport/news/article.cfm?c_id=4&objectid=10602715&ref=rss |title=League: Chance for rookie Kiwis |date=12 October 2009 |agency=NZPA |work=The New Zealand Herald |accessdate=8 October 2011[/tpl]
In 2010, Sa'u was selected for New Zealand in the 2010 ANZAC Test. He was also again selected in the New Zealand squad for the 2010 Four Nations.
In 2013 Junior played for Samoa in the Pacific Rugby League International against Tonga.

==Personal life==

He is a cousin to Manly-Warringah Sea Eagles player Steve Matai.
In 2009, Sa'u was praised for coming to the aid of a 15-year old girl who was being assaulted in Waratah, New South Wales.[tpl]cite news|author = Nick Walshaw|publisher=Fox Sports News (Australia)|title=Newcastle Knights' Junior Sau hailed for saving girl's life |url=http://www.foxsports.com.au/story/0,8659,25715216-5018866,00.html |accessdate=2009-08-07|date=2009-07-01[/tpl]
