[[1984 Japan Open Tennis Championships]]

CATEGORIES: 1984 Grand Prix (tennis), 1984 Virginia Slims World Championship Series, Japan Open Tennis Championships

The 1984 Japan Open Tennis Championships  was a tennis tournament played on outdoor hard courts in Orlando, Florida in the United States that was part of the 1984 Volvo Grand Prix and the 1984 Virginia Slims World Championship Series. The tournament was held from October 8 through October 14, 1984.
