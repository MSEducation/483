[[Jack Samuels]]

CATEGORIES: 1912 births, 1970 deaths, Indonesian footballers, Indonesia international footballers, Association football defenders, 1938 FIFA World Cup players

Jack Samuels (1912 – 1970) was an Indonesian football defender who played for the Dutch East Indies in the 1938 FIFA World Cup.Copa do Mundo da FIFA França 1938 He also played for Excelsior Soerabaja.
