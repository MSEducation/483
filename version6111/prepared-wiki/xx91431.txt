[[Tagea Brandt Rejselegat]]

CATEGORIES: Danish culture, Danish awards, Awards and prizes honoring women, Art awards, Writing awards, Awards established in 1924

The Tagea Brandt ''Rejselegat'' (Travel Scholarship) is a Danish award, which is awarded annually, on the 17 March,Litteraturpriser.dk Tagea Brandts Rejselegat which was his wife's birthday,Gravsted.dk to women who have made a significance in either science, literature or art.Denstoredanske.dk The Grand Danish Encyclopedia, Tagea Brandts Rejselegat (in Danish) The grant is given without application.
The scholarship was created and endowed by Danish industrialist Vilhelm Brandt (1854–1921) in 1905 in honor of his wife, Tagea Brandt (born Rovsing in Copenghagen in 1847, died in 1882 in Odense only one year after they got married. Tagea was a feminist and fought for women's rights as well as her mother and her sister. She was a board member and secretary of Kvindelig Læseforening (Women's reading club) 1877-80.Gravsted.dk The charter of 1922 provides that it shall be given to outstanding women in science, art, music, literature and theater arts (particularly in this case to actresses at the Royal Danish Theatre). The first scholarships were given in 1924; the first time the amount was DKK 10.000, in 1958 it was increased to DKK 15.000, in 1967 to 25.000, later to 50.000 and currently it is DKK 75.000, which usually is given to 2-3 women annually.Denstoredanske.dk
The intent is for the awardee to both broaden her horizons while promoting Danish society abroad, and to benefit from vacation and rest time.[tpl]Citation needed|date=September 2010[/tpl]
