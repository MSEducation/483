[[Carl Madrick]]

CATEGORIES: 1968 births, Living people, English footballers, People from Bolton, Association football midfielders, The Football League players, Huddersfield Town F.C. players, Peterborough United F.C. players

Carl James Madrick (born September 20, 1968 in Bolton) is a former professional footballer, who played for Huddersfield Town, Peterborough United and Chorley.
