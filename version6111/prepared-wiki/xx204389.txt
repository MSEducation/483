[[Dungloe railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Disused railway stations in County Donegal, Railway stations opened in 1903, Railway stations closed in 1940

Dungloe railway station served the town of Dungloe in County Donegal, Ireland.
The station opened on 9 March 1903 when the Londonderry and Lough Swilly Railway opened their Letterkenny and Burtonport Extension Railway, from Letterkenny to Burtonport. It closed on 3 June 1940 when the LLSR closed the line from Tooban Junction to Burtonport in an effort to save money.[tpl]cite web | title=Dungloe station | work=Railscot - Irish Railways | url=http://www.railscot.co.uk/Ireland/Irish_railways.pdf | accessdate=2008-05-24[/tpl]
