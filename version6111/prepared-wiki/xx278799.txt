[[Birundeh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Birundeh ([tpl]lang-fa|بيرونده[/tpl], also Romanized as Bīrūndeh; also known as Berūndeh, Birundekh, Birundoh, and Borūndeh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3056460|Birundeh[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Pain Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 365, in 80 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
