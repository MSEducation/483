[[The Ghost of the Mountain]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 albums, English-language albums, Fiction Records albums, Polydor Records albums, Tired Pony albums

The Ghost of the Mountain is the second album by Tired Pony, released on August 19, 2013 in the United Kingdom and on October 1, 2013 in the United States. The album was recorded between February and March, 2013, at Jacknife Lee's studio in Topanga Canyon.[tpl]cite news | first = Carly | last = Bush | title = Where | date = 2013-08-01 | publisher = mxdwn | url = http://www.mxdwn.com/2013/08/01/news/tired-pony-gary-lightbody-peter-buck-scott-mccaughey-announce-new-album-the-ghost-of-the-mountain-set-for-october-2013-release/ | work = http://www.mxdwn.com/author/carly-bush/ | accessdate = 2013-08-11[/tpl]

==Recording==

"It feels like a fully formed band in full flight", says  Lightbody about the album. This album features harmony vocals from Minnie Driver, Bronagh Gallagher and Kim Topper.[tpl]cite news | title = who | date = 2013-07-30 | publisher = direct | url = http://www.mxdwn.com/2013/08/01/news/tired-pony-gary-lightbody-peter-buck-scott-mccaughey-announce-new-album-the-ghost-of-the-mountain-set-for-october-2013-release/ | work = http://www.directcurrentmusic.com/music-news-new-music/tired-pony-the-ghost-of-the-mountain.html | accessdate = 2013-08-11[/tpl]
