[[I'll Wait for You]]

CATEGORIES: 2006 singles, Joe Nichols songs, Songs written by Bill Anderson (singer), Songs written by Harley Allen, Song recordings produced by Buddy Cannon, Show Dog-Universal Music singles, Music videos directed by Chris Hicky

"I'll Wait for You" is a song written by Bill Anderson and Harley Allen, and recorded by American country music artist Joe Nichols.  It was released in July 2006 as the third single from the album III.  The song reached a peak position of #7 on the U.S. Billoard Hot Country Songs chart, and a peak of #71 on the U.S. Billboard Hot 100''.

==Music videos==

Two videos were made for the song. In the first video, Nichols plays the song's protagonist at various ages; makeup was used to make him appear thirty years older.[ref]
