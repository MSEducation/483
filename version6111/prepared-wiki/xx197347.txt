[[2008 Singapore Armed Forces FC season]]

CATEGORIES: Singapore Armed Forces FC seasons, Singaporean football clubs 2008 season

Singapore Armed Forces FC will compete as one of the 12 teams in the 2008 NTUC Income Yeo's S-League season. The club will also take part in the 2008 editions of the RHB Singapore Cup and the Singapore League Cup, as well as the regional AFC Cup.
