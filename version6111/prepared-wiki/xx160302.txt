[[PDA College of Engineering]]

CATEGORIES: Engineering colleges in Karnataka, Education in Gulbarga

The PDA College of Engineering or Poojya Dodappa Appa College of Engineering is located at Gulbarga in the state of Karnataka, India. The college was established in 1958 by the Hyderabad Karnataka Education Society. The college offers courses in ceramic and cement technology, one of the few in India.

==Courses==

Bachelor of Engineering (aided)
Bachelor of Engineering (non-aided)
