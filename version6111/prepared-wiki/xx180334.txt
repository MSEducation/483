[[Shackleton Icefalls]]

CATEGORIES: Icefalls of Antarctica, Landforms of the Ross Dependency, Dufek Coast

Shackleton Icefalls ([tpl]coord|85|8|S|164|0|E|type:glacier_region:AQ[/tpl]) is extensive icefalls of the upper Beardmore Glacier, southward of Mount Darwin and Mount Mills. Named by the British Antarctic Expedition (1910–13) for Sir Ernest Shackleton, leader of the British Antarctic Expedition (1907–09), who first penetrated this region.
