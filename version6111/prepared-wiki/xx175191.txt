[[KRMC]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Arizona, Spanish-language radio stations in Arizona, Radio stations established in 1997

KRMC is a Spanish language Christian radio station licensed to Douglas, Arizona, broadcasting on 91.7 MHz FM. KRMC is owned by World Radio Network, Inc.KRMC fcc.gov. Accessed October 14, 2012
