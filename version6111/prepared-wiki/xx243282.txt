[[Pavel Mráz]]

CATEGORIES: 1968 births, Canoeists at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Czech canoeists, Living people, Olympic canoeists of the Czech Republic, People from Ústí nad Labem

Pavel Mráz (born June 18, 1974 in Ústí nad Labem) is a Czech sprint canoer who competed in the mid-1990s. He was eliminated in the semifinals of the K-4 1000 m event at the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta.
