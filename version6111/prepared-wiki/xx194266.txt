[[Jennifer Lin]]

CATEGORIES: 1990 births, Living people, American pianists, People from Florida

Jennifer Lin (born 1990,[tpl]Citation needed|date=August 2011[/tpl] Florida) is an American pianist. She is a student of the Yamaha Music Education System (YMES) and has been since she was 4. She is also a student in the Junior Original Concert program.Yamaha
She found her major introduction to the public at large when a recording of her inspirational improv performance at the TED conference in Oldsmar, Florida, on February 27, 2004, was posted on TED.com on Aug 2006. She was 14 years old at the time.TED: Jennifer Lin Improves Piano Magic
She has performed for Oprah and The Daily Show, among others. On The Oprah Winfrey Show, she composed a piece on the spot with 5 random music notes that Oprah chose.
