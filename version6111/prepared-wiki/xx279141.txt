[[Alvarlu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Alvarlu ([tpl]lang-fa|الوارلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Alvārlū and Ālvārlū; also known as Alwari and Gūzī Raj‘īn)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3052872|Alvarlu[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 123, in 21 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
