[[Seykhan Aliyev]]

CATEGORIES: 1991 births, Living people, Ukrainian footballers, FC Stal Dniprodzerzhynsk players, Association football midfielders

Seykhan Aliyev ([tpl]lang-ua|Сейхан Фамалович Алієв[/tpl]; born 22 September 1991 in Urozhayne, Sovetskyi Raion, Crimea, Ukraine) is a professional Ukrainian football midfielder who plays for FC Stal Dniprodzerzhynsk in the Ukrainian Second League.
Aliyev was the product of FC Avanhard Yevpatoria sportive school. His first trainer was A. Babenko. In 2010 he signed contract with FC Stal Dniprodzerzhynsk in the Ukrainian Second League, and made debut in Second League together with his team against FC Hirnyk Kryvyi Rih on 19 March 2010.[tpl]cite web|publisher=ukfootball.in.ua|title=Сейхан Алиев — из "Сталкера" в "Сталь"|url=http://ukrfootball.in.ua/second-league-b/1134-----lr--lr-|language=Russian|date=2011-08-14[/tpl]
