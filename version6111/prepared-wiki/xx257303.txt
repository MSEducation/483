[[Kids for Character]]

CATEGORIES: Children's television, Yogi Bear, The Flintstones, Scooby-Doo, Woody Woodpecker, Thomas & Friends, Garfield, Crossover animation, Films with live action and animation, Crossover films, Preschool education television series

Kids for Character was a program that was released on VHS in April 23, 1996. It was a charity special about moral character that featured many different children's characters. It was produced by The Character Counts! Coalition and was distributed by Lyrick Studios. The host of the show is Tom Selleck. The sequel, Kids for Character: Choices Count is released a year later.
