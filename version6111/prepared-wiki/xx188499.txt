[[Ángel Jara Saguier]]

Ángel Críspulo Jara Saguier (born 1 October 1936 in Asunción) is a former football player from Paraguay. Angel is one of the seven Jara Saguier brothers that played professional football in Paraguay.La dinastía de los Jara se renueva en el fútbol paraguayo [tpl]Es icon[/tpl]
He played for Cerro Porteño, before joining France, where he played for Toulouse (Ligue 1), Red Star (Ligue 1 and Ligue 2) and RC Besançon (Ligue 2).
