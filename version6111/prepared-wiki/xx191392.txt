[[Marcel Leclef]]

CATEGORIES: 1909 births, Year of death missing, Belgian bobsledders, Bobsledders at the 1948 Winter Olympics, Bobsledders at the 1952 Winter Olympics, Bobsledders at the 1956 Winter Olympics, Sportspeople from Antwerp, Recipients of the Olympic Order

Marcel Leclef (born 25 July 1909, date of death unknown) was a Belgian bobsledder who competed from the late 1940s to the mid-1950s. Competing in three Winter Olympics, he earned his best finish of sixth in the two-man event at Oslo in 1952.
He was born in Antwerp.
