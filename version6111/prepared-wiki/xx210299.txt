[[Air Power (TV series)]]

CATEGORIES: 1950s American television series, 1956 American television series debuts, 1957 American television series endings, Aviation television series, CBS News, English-language television programming, Historical television series, Television series by CBS Paramount Television

Air Power is a historical educational television series broadcast in the 1950s over the CBS television network dealing with the rise of aviation as a military weapon. It starred Walter Cronkite as the narrator and featured a musical score by Norman Dello Joio.
