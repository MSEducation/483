[[Notre Dame Fieldhouse]]

CATEGORIES: Sports venues completed in 1900, Demolished sports venues in Indiana, Defunct college basketball venues in the United States, Notre Dame Fighting Irish basketball venues, Defunct indoor arenas in Indiana, University of Notre Dame buildings and structures, Basketball venues in Indiana

Notre Dame Fieldhouse was a 4,000 seat multi-purpose arena in Notre Dame, Indiana.  It opened in 1898.
It was home to the University of Notre Dame Fighting Irish basketball team.  It was replaced when the Edmund P. Joyce Center opened in 1968. [tpl]fact|date=February 2011[/tpl]
