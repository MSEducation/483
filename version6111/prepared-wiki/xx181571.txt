[[Joaquim Pinto de Andrade]]

CATEGORIES: Angolan clergy, 1926 births, 2008 deaths, MPLA politicians

Joaquim Pinto de Andrade (1926 – February 23, 2008) served as the first honorary President of the Popular Movement for the Liberation of Angola (MPLA),[tpl]cite book|last=Lloyd-Jones|first=Stewart|coauthors=António Costa Pinto|year=2003|title=The Last Empire: Thirty Years of Portuguese Decolonization|pages=27[/tpl] Chancellor of the Luanda Archdiocese, and as a member of the African Society of Culture.[tpl]cite book|last=Sellström|first=Tor|year=1999|title=Sweden and National Liberation in Southern Africa|pages=378[/tpl] He died on February 23, 2008 following a long illness, the same day as fellow MPLA politician Gentil Ferreira Viana.http://ww1.rtp.pt/noticias/index.php?article=328578&visual=26 (Portuguese)
