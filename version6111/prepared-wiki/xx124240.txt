[[Murderers & Robbers]]

CATEGORIES: 2000 albums, Project Pat albums, Gangsta rap compilation albums

Murderers & Robbers is the first compilation album by rapper Project Pat.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.amazon.com/Murderers-Robbers-Project-Pat/dp/B00004U5DC/ |title=Murderers & Robbers: Project Pat: Music |publisher=Amazon.com |date= |accessdate=2012-03-02[/tpl] It was released on July 25, 2000. Labeled as an "underground album", it is mainly a compilation of the rapper's mixtape material from the early 1990s before a prison sentence suspended his career.
