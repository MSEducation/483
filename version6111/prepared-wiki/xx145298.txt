Peter Kevin Dineen (born November 19, 1960) is a Canadian-born and Seattle, Washington-raised[tpl]citation needed|date=February 2011[/tpl] former professional ice hockey defenceman who played in the National Hockey League (NHL) for the Los Angeles Kings and Detroit Red Wings. He is the second oldest of the Dineen brothers (Kevin and Gord) who also played in the NHL. He is currently a scout for the Columbus Blue Jackets.[tpl]citation needed|date=February 2011[/tpl]

==Playing career==

Dineen was drafted in the 9th round of the 1980 NHL Entry Draft by the Philadelphia Flyers after he had a fairly successful junior career. He would go onto a brief NHL career, playing in a total of 13 games for the Los Angeles Kings and Detroit Red Wings. He got 2 assists in those 13 games.
