[[Benaleka]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Democratic Republic of the Congo

Benaleka or Bena-Leka is a settlement in Kasai-Occidental, Democratic Republic of the Congo. It is located 220 km (137 mi) northwest of Kananga. The elevation is 467 m (1,532 ft).

==Transport==

Benaleka is served by a station on the national railway system.
On August 1, 2007, a train derailed near Benaleka, killing more than 100 people.[tpl]cite news |first=Joe |last=Bavier |title= Congo train crash kills at least 100|url= http://africa.reuters.com/world/news/usnL02743533.html|work=Reuters Africa |publisher=Reuters Group PLC |date= 2007-08-02|accessdate=2007-08-03 [/tpl] Brake failure appears to be the cause.
