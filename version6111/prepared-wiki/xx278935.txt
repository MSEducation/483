[[Guglar]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Guglar ([tpl]lang-fa|گوگلر[/tpl], also Romanized as Gūglar; also known as Gūlar)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795528|Guglar[/tpl] is a village in Chaypareh-ye Bala Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 48, in 9 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
