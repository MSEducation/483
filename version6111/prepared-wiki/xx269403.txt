[[Brachiolodes]]

CATEGORIES: Phycitinae, Monotypic lepidoptera genera

Brachiolodes is a genus of snout moths. It was described by Amsel, in 1953, and contains the species B. ziczac. It is found in Mauretania.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.archive.org/stream/bulletinofbritis25entolond/bulletinofbritis25entolond_djvu.txt |title=Bulletin of the British Museum (Natural History) |publisher=Archive.org |date= |accessdate=2011-09-29[/tpl]
