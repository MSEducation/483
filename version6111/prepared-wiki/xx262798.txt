[[Ville Varakas]]

CATEGORIES: 1984 births, Living people, Espoo Blues players, Finnish ice hockey players, HIFK players, Växjö Lakers players

Ville Varakas (born February 13, 1984) is a Finnish ice hockey defenceman. He is currently playing with Växjö Lakers of the Swedish Hockey League (SHL).
Varakas made his SM-liiga debut playing with  HIFK during the 2003–04 SM-liiga season.HIFK 2003–04 player statistics
