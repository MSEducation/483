[[Marion Ashmore]]

CATEGORIES: Players of American football from Illinois, Milwaukee Badgers players, Duluth Eskimos players, Green Bay Packers players, Gonzaga Bulldogs football players, 1899 births, 1948 deaths

Marion Ashmore (born Roger Marion Ashmore[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/A/AshmRo20.htm|title=Roger Ashmore|publisher=Pro-Football-Reference.com|accessdate=2011-01-02[/tpl]) is a former player in the National Football League. He played his first two seasons with the Milwaukee Badgers and the Duluth Eskimos before playing his final two with the Green Bay Packers.
