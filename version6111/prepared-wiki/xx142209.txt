[[Camp Colbern]]

CATEGORIES: Closed facilities of the United States Army, United States military in South Korea

Camp Colbern, in Hanam-shi near Seoul, Republic of Korea, was a U.S. Army installation  and the home of the 304th Signal Battalion. It closed in late 2005, with the move of the 304th to Camp Stanley, Republic of Korea. Also, the land was returned in 2006 to ROK. Camp Stanley existed for 41 years, with the 304th occupying the Camp for 3 of those decades.
