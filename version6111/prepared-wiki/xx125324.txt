[[The Forum (shopping mall)]]

CATEGORIES: Shopping malls in Bangalore

The Forum  is a popular shopping mall located on Hosur Road in Koramangala, Bangalore, Karnataka, India developed by Prestige Group.
The Forum was Bangalore's first full-fledged mall with a multi-storey book store, 12 cinema halls and other entertainment facilities. Mall is popular among the youth in Bangalore and an attraction to those coming to Bangalore. The shopping mall houses [tpl]convert|72000|m2|sqft|abbr=on[/tpl] of shops over five levels.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.theforumexperience.com/mallinfo.htm|title=Forum Mall: Overview|publisher=Prestige Estates Projects Pvt. Ltd|accessdate=2009-02-28[/tpl] A major attraction at the mall is the multiplex, PVR.

==Location==

The Forum Mall is located on Hosur Road, koramangala which has several multinational corporations such as, Robert Bosch GmbH  and Hewlett Packard and large number of shoppers are from nearby Christ University and Christ Junior College.
