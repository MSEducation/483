==Early life==

Rattan Mohan Sharma was born in Rajasthan to Padma and Mohan Lal Sharma. He is the nephew of classical vocalist, Pandit Jasraj. It was found that young Rattan had an affinity towards percussion instruments so till the age of 15, he also learned tabla.

==Career==

Rattan Mohan Sharma hails from the illustrious Mewaati Gharana and belongs to the family of vocalist like Pandit Motiram, Pandit Maniram, Pandit Pratapnarayan, and Pandit Jasraj. He has performed solos in many concerts and festivals in India and abroad. 
He performs regularly at the classical music festivals, Pandit Motiram Pandit Maniram Sangeet Samaroh organized by Pandit Jasraj and Harivallabh Sangeet Sammelan, Sawai Gandharva Bhimsen Festival. 

===Music director===

Also a successful music director and composer giving music to a number of tele-serials and private albums.
