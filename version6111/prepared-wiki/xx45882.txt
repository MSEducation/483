[[Mont-Saint-Guibert]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Walloon Brabant

Mont-Saint-Guibert is a Walloon municipality located in the Belgian province of Walloon Brabant. On January 1, 2012 Mont-Saint-Guibert had a total population of 7000. The total area is 18.63 km² which gives a population density of 344 inhabitants per km².
