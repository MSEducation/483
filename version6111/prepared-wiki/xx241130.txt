[[Togtoh County]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Togtoh County (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠲᠣᠭᠲᠠᠬᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠰᠢᠶᠠᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Toɣtaqu siyan[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=托克托县 |t=托克托縣 |p=Tuōkètuō Xiàn[/tpl]) is a county in west-central Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China, located on the north bank of the Yellow River at which point that great river turns out of the Ordos Loop toward the south. It is under the administration of the regional capital of Hohhot, [tpl]convert|78|km[/tpl] to the northeast, with a population of around 200,000.
