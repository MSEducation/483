[[Dino Marino]]

CATEGORIES: Italian footballers, Serie A footballers, U.S. Lecce players, Inter Milan players, U.S. Arezzo players, Aurora Pro Patria 1919 players, F.C. Südtirol players, Association football midfielders, Sportspeople from Naples, 1985 births, Living people

Dino Marino (born 23 May 1985 ) is a former Italian footballer.

==Football career==

He started his career at Internazionale. After played 3 games for Inter, including a match in 2004–05 Coppa Italia, Marino was graduated from the under-20 youth team and farmed to Serie B team Arezzo[tpl]Cite news|url=http://www.inter.it/aas/news/reader?N=19371&L=en|title=DINO MARINO JOINS AREZZO |date=20 July 2005|accessdate=19 July 2011|work=FC Internazionale Milano|publisher=www.inter.it[/tpl] along with Nicola Beati in a joint ownership bid. But Marino was then loaned to Serie C1 team Pro Patria after just 1 appearance,[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.inter.it/aas/news/reader?N=21666&L=en|title=TRANFERS: SIX MORE DEALS DONE |date=31 January 2006|accessdate=19 July 2011|work=FC Internazionale Milano|publisher=www.inter.it[/tpl] joining Carlo Raffaele Trezzi, also from the youth rank of Inter. In June 2006 Inter bought back Marino.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.inter.it/aas/news/reader?N=23198&L=en|title=TRANSFER MARKET: CO-OWNERSHIP DEALS |date=20 June 2006|accessdate=19 July 2011|work=FC Internazionale Milano|publisher=www.inter.it[/tpl]
In August 2006, Pro Patria bought Marino in another co-ownership deal,[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.inter.it/aas/news/reader?N=23485&L=en|title=NEW DEALS FOR SIX YOUNGSTERS |date=4 August 2006|accessdate=19 July 2011|work=FC Internazionale Milano|publisher=www.inter.it[/tpl] and Inter sold remain half to Pro Patria in June 2007.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.inter.it/aas/news/reader?N=26755&L=en|title=Transfer market: co-ownership deals |date=20 June 2007|accessdate=19 July 2011|work=FC Internazionale Milano|publisher=www.inter.it[/tpl] In July 2008 he was signed by South Tyrol.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.fc-suedtirol.com/it/informazione/news-detail.asp?lProductID=129292|title=Dino Marino, ex centrocampista dell´Inter, firma per l`FC Südtirol|date=11 July 2008|accessdate=19 July 2011|work=F.C. Südtirol – Alto Adige|language=Italian[/tpl] However in November he broke his leg (fibula & tibia) and missed the rest of the season.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.fc-suedtirol.com/it/informazione/news-detail.asp?lProductID=138574|title=Stagione finita per Dino Marino|date=16 November 2008|accessdate=19 July 2011|work=F.C. Südtirol – Alto Adige|language=Italian[/tpl] At the end of season he joined Serie D team Ostuni.
