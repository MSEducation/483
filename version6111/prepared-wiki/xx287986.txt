[[Donna Sheldon]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Georgia (U.S. state) Republicans

Donna Sheldon is an American politician from the state of Georgia and Republican candidate for [tpl]ushr|GA|10[/tpl] to replace Paul Broun.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.peachpundit.com/2013/04/09/its-official-donna-sheldon-is-in-georgias-10th-cd-race/[/tpl] She was a member of the Georgia House of Representatives from 2002-2012.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.house.ga.gov/representatives/en-US/member.aspx?Member=205&Session=23|publisher=Georgia House of Representatives[/tpl]
Sheldon announced her resignation from the state House on August 26, 2013 to focus on her campaign for U.S. Congress.[tpl]cite web|publisher=ZPolitics.com|url=http://zpolitics.com/donna-sheldon-resigns-from-ga-house/|accessdate=7 January 2014[/tpl]
