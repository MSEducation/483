[[Wilcze Bagno]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Augustów County

Wilcze Bagno [tpl]IPAc-pl|'|w|i|l|cz|e|-|'|b|a|g|n|o[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Sztabin, within Augustów County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) – TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] The name literally translates as "wolves' swamp".
