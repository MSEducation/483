[[Blair County Convention Center]]

CATEGORIES: Buildings and structures in Blair County, Pennsylvania, Convention centers in Pennsylvania

Blair County Convention Center is a convention center located just south of Altoona, Pennsylvania. It has two floor levels with the exhibit floor and ballroom on separate levels.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.blairconventioncenter.com/facility/specifications|title=Specifications|accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl] Barry Kumpf is the President and Chief Executive Officer.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.blairconventioncenter.com/hidden/contact|title=Contact|accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl] The facility is in proximity to Interstate 99 and Route 36.
