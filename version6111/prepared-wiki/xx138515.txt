[[Napsbury railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Disused railway stations in Hertfordshire, Railway stations opened in 1905, Railway stations closed in 1959

Napsbury railway station was built by the Midland Railway in 1905 on its line to St Pancras station. 
It was never more than an island platform between the slow lines, with a siding serving the Middlesex County Asylum at Napsbury, and closed in 1959.Radford, B., (1983) Midland Line Memories: a Pictorial History of the Midland Railway Main Line Between London (St Pancras) & Derby London: Bloomsbury Books
