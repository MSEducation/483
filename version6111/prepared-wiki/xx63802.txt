[[Rat (zodiac)]]

CATEGORIES: Astrological signs, Chinese astrology, Vietnamese astrology, Rats, Mythological rodents

The Rat (wiktionary:大鼠) is one of the 12-year cycle of animals which appear in the Chinese zodiac related to the Chinese calendar, the Year of the Rat is associated with the Earthly Branch symbol 子.

==Years and the Five Elements==

Persons born within these date ranges can be said to have been born in the "year of the Rat", while also bearing the following elemental sign:http://pinyin.info/chinese_new_year/
