[[Ukrainian Catholic Eparchy of Saint Josaphat in Parma]]

CATEGORIES: Eastern Catholicism in Ohio, Ukrainian Catholic Metropolia of Philadelphia, Ukrainian Greek Catholic eparchies, Ukrainian-American history, Religious organizations established in 1983, Eastern Catholic dioceses in the United States, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century, Ukrainian-American culture in Ohio, Parma, Ohio

Ukrainian Catholic Eparchy of Saint Josaphat in Parma is a diocese of the Ukrainian Greek Catholic Church in the United States, with its seat in Parma, Ohio. It was established in 1983 by Pope John Paul II. The first bishop was Robert M. Moskal.
