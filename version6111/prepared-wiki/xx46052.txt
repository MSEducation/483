[[Bièvre]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Belgium, Municipalities of Namur (province)

Bièvre ([tpl]IPA-fr|bjɛvʁ[/tpl]) is a Walloon municipality located in the Belgian province of Namur. On 1 January 2006 the municipality had 3,151 inhabitants. The total area is 109.59 km², giving a population density of 29 inhabitants per km².
