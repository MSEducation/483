[[30 Days (2006 film)]]

CATEGORIES: 2006 films, 2000s action thriller films, Nigerian films, Nigerian action thriller films, English-language films

30 Days is a 2006 Nigerian action thriller film written and directed by Mildred Okwo. The film received 10 nominations at the African Movie Academy Awards in 2008.[tpl]cite web|url=http://ama-awards.com/amaa-nominees-and-winners-2008|title=AMAA Nominees and Winners 2008|publisher=African Movie Academy Award|accessdate=15 February 2011[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.screenafrica.com/news/industry/363428.htm|title=List of Nominees for AMAA 2008|publisher=ScreenAfrica.com|accessdate=2009-10-20[/tpl]
