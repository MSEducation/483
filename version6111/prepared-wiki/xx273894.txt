[[Vicente Arraya]]

CATEGORIES: Bolivian footballers, Bolivia international footballers, Association football goalkeepers, 1950 FIFA World Cup players, 1922 births, 1992 deaths

Vicente Arraya Castro (25 January 1922 - 21 November 1992) is a Bolivian football goalkeeper who played for Bolivia in the 1950 FIFA World Cup.1950 FIFA World Cup Brazil He also played for Ferroviario La Paz, and 8 matches in Atlanta of Argentina, in 1944-45. He was the first Bolivian player in the Argentina First Division.
