[[Owen Jenkins]]

CATEGORIES: 1906 births, 1988 deaths, Alumni of the University of Wales, Lampeter, Alumni of Jesus College, Oxford, Archdeacons of Cardigan, Archdeacons of Carmarthen

(John) Owen Jenkins (13 June 1906-9 August 1988) was an eminent Welsh Anglican priest in the Twentieth century.‘JENKINS, Ven. (John) Owen’, Who Was Who, A & C Black, an imprint of Bloomsbury Publishing plc, 1920–2008; online edn, Oxford University Press, Dec 2012 ; online edn, Nov 2012 accessed 8 Aug 2013
Jenkins was educated at St David's College, Lampeter and Jesus College, Oxford; and ordained in 1930.Crockford's Clerical Directory 1947-48 London, OUP, 1947 p 692 After curacies in Cwmamman and Carmarthen he held incumbencies at Spittal, Llangadog, Newport  and Llanfihangel Aberbythych. He was Archdeacon of Cardigan from 1962 until 1967; and then of Carmarthen from 1967‘New Archdeacon Of Carmarthen’ The Times  (London, England), Monday,  Jun 12, 1967; pg. 10; Issue 56965 until 1974.
