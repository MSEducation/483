[[Hwekum]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Naga Self-Administered Zone, Lahe Township

 
 Hwekum is a village in Lahe Township, Naga Self-Administered Zone, in the Sagaing Region of northwestern Burma. It is located in a mountainous forest area to the northwest of Singkaling Hkamti.[tpl]cite map|title=Google Maps|publisher=Google|accessdate=2 January, 2010[/tpl][tpl]cite map|title=Bing Maps|publisher= Microsoft and Harris Corporation Earthstar Geographics LLC|accessdate=2 January, 2010[/tpl]The village, close to the Indian border, has been subject to raids by the Ponyo Nagars,[tpl]cite book|last=Barua|first=Surendra Nath|title=Tribes of Indo-Burma border: a socio-cultural history of the inhabitants of the Patkai Range|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=OAZuAAAAMAAJ|accessdate=2 January 2011|year=1991|publisher=Mittal Publications|isbn=978-81-7099-308-7[/tpl]and since 2010 has been part of the new Naga-self-administered zone.
