[[Kristof Maes]]

CATEGORIES: 1988 births, Living people, Belgian footballers, Association football goalkeepers, Beerschot AC players, K.A.A. Gent players

Kristof Maes (born 19 April 1988) is a Belgian goalkeeper currently playing for Belgian Pro League side K.A.A. Gent. He has represented the Belgium national football team at Under-18 level. He has played for Belgian Pro League side Beerschot AC before moving to K.A.A. Gent during the 2012 summer transfer window.
He started his career at Belgian club KFCO Wilrijk.
