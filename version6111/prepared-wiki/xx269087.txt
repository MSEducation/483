[[Erin Burnett OutFront]]

CATEGORIES: CNN shows, 2011 American television series debuts, 2010s American television series, American news television series, American television talk shows, English-language television programming

Erin Burnett OutFront is an hour-long television news program hosted by Erin Burnett on CNN. The show premiered on October 3, 2011 in the 7PM and 11PM time slot to replace John King, USA.[tpl]cite web|last=Hall|first=Colby|title=CNN Reveals New 7PM Show Title: Erin Burnett: OutFront|url=http://www.mediaite.com/tv/cnn-reveals-erin-burnetts-new-show-title-outfront/|work=Mediaite|publisher=Mediaite, LLC|accessdate= September 23, 2011[/tpl]  CNN hopes Burnett's expected popularity will provide an "attractive" opening to an evening of "talk shows and news analysis."[tpl]cite web|last=Friedman|first=Jon|title=CNN's Erin Burnett needs your respect|url=http://www.marketwatch.com/story/cnns-erin-burnett-needs-your-respect-2011-09-28?siteid=rss&rss=1|work=MarketWatch|publisher=MarketWatch, Inc|accessdate= September 30, 2011[/tpl]
Erin Burnett, OutFront is broadcast live from CNN's Time Warner Center studios in New York City or on location from the site of a breaking news event, airing from 7:00 pm ET to 8:00 pm ET. The program covers a number of news stories of the day in a Count-up format, starting at #1 -- most important and making it to #7 by the end of the hour. Burnett' program features taped packages from CNN Correspondents along with analysis and panel discussion.
