[[Leben (surname)]]

Leben, and its variant Leban, is a surname with different origins.
As a habitation surname, it is derived from the village of Leybourne: ancestors of the Leben surname lived in that village in ancient Anglo-Saxon England.http://www.houseofnames.com/leben-family-crest
