[[The Magic Machines]]

CATEGORIES: 1969 films, American films, English-language films, Short documentary films, American documentary films, Live Action Short Film Academy Award winners, Independent films, 1960s short films, 1960s documentary films

The Magic Machines is a 1969 American short documentary film directed by Bob Curtis. It won an Academy Award in 1970 for Best Short Subject and was nominated for Best Documentary Short.[tpl]cite web |url=http://movies.nytimes.com/movie/430708/The-Magic-Machines/details |title=New York Times: The Magic Machines |accessdate=2008-05-23|work=NY Times[/tpl]
