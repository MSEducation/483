[[Estienne du Tertre]]

CATEGORIES: French composers, 16th-century French people, 16th-century composers

Estienne du Tertre (fl. mid-16th century) was a French composer. He spent most of his life in Paris and worked as an editor for the publisher Attaingnant. Many of his chansons were published.[tpl]By whom|date=January 2013[/tpl]
Du Tertre published suyttes de bransles in 1557, giving us the first use of the term "suite", although the usual form of the time was as pairs of dances.
