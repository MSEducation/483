[[Suniti Namjoshi]]

Suniti Namjoshi (born 1941) is an Indian writer and poet, many of whose works explore issues of gender and sexual orientation.Spinifex Press - CyberFeminism - Suniti Namjoshi She has written several collections of fables, poetry and fantasy fiction. She has also written some children's fiction.

==Biography==

She was born in Mumbai. She has worked as an officer in the IAS.http://www.penguinbooksindia.com/AuthorLounge/AuthorDetail.asp?aid=952 She received a master's degree in business administration from the University of Missouri, and a Ph.D. from McGill University.'Suniti Namjoshi (1941-  )', Harveen Sachdeva Mann in South Asian Novelists in English: An A-to-z Guide, Jaina C. Sanga (ed.), Greenwood Press, 2003 http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=qlpKOzsOc-IC&pg=PA181&lpg=PA181&dq=%22Suniti+Namjoshi%22+lesbian&source=bl&ots=842h-D9FX1&sig=cBVPgNRnos7fQy49ZA1lWP-Vp58&hl=en&ei=EMm_SdSYIuS1jAe3n5U5&sa=X&oi=book_result&resnum=1&ct=result#PPA181,M1 After lecturing at the University of Toronto, she settled in England, where she serves as a research fellow at Exeter University.
