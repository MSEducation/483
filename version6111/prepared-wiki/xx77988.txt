[[Pukë District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Albania, Shkodër County, Pukë District

The District of Pukë ([tpl]Lang-sq|Rrethi i Pukës[/tpl]) is one of the thirty-six districts of Albania, part of Shkodër County.
