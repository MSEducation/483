[[Bhargavram Vitthal Warerkar]]

CATEGORIES: Nominated members of the Rajya Sabha, 1883 births, 1964 deaths, Recipients of the Padma Bhushan, Marathi-language writers

Bhargavram Vitthal Warerkar (1883-1964) also known as Mama Warerkar was an India|Indian author and parliamentarian. He wrote a large number of books consisting of plays, novels, short stories in Marathi. He was nominated twice as a member of the Rajya Sabha in 1956 and 1962 and served till his death in 1964. He was also a recipient of the Padma Bhushan in 1959.
