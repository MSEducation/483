[[Technology Incubation Scheme]]

CATEGORIES: Science and technology in Singapore

The Technology Incubation Scheme (TIS) is a government funding program of the National Research Foundation (NRF) under the Singapore Government.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.nrf.gov.sg/nrf/otherprogrammes.aspx?id=1490 |title=Technology Incubation Scheme Call for Proposal |website=Singapore National Research Foundation |accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl]

==Details of the Program==

The TIS program selected funds that matched the following criteria:[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.nrf.gov.sg/nrf/otherprogrammes.aspx?id=1206 |title=National Framework for Innovation and Enterprise |website=Singapore National Research Foundation |accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl]

==Incubators accepted into the Program==

As of August 1, 2013, there are currently 14 approved funds to work with the Singapore Government as part of the program:[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.nrf.gov.sg/nrf/uploadedFiles/20130319%20List%20of%20Approved%20TIS%20Incubators.pdf |title=List of Approved TIS Incubators |publisher=Singapore National Research Foundation |accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl]
