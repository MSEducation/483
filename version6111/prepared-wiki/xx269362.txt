Abdelaziz Barrada (born 19 June 1989), sometimes known as just Abdel, is a Moroccan professional footballer who plays for Al Jazira Club in the United Arab Emirates, as a central midfielder.

==Club career==

===Getafe===

Barrada was born in Provins, France. After playing three years with Paris Saint-Germain FC's reserves, he moved to Spain in 2010 and joined Getafe CF, initially being assigned to the reserves which were playing for the first time ever in Segunda División B. On 14 March 2011 he signed his first professional contract with the Madrid outskirts team,Abdelaziz Barrada passe pro à Getafe (Abdelaziz Barrada turns pro at Getafe); Maroc Football, 14 March 2011 [tpl]fr icon[/tpl] and proved essential as the B's eventually retained their league status at the end of the season.
On 28 August 2011 Barrada made his La Liga debut with Getafe, starting and playing 60 minutes in a 1–1 home draw against Levante UD.Juanlu saves Levante; ESPN Soccernet, 28 August 2011 He was immediately propelled into manager Luís García's starting XI.Abdel Barrada, la joya de la cantera del Getafe (Abdel Barrada, Getafe youth system's gem); ABC, 7 November 2011 [tpl]es icon[/tpl]
On 6 November 2011 Barrada scored his first goal for Getafe's main squad, helping the hosts – who played more than 60 minutes with ten players – to a 3–2 home win against Atlético Madrid.Getafe 3–2 Atlético Madrid; ESPN Soccernet, 6 November 2011 In the following month, on the 17th, he netted twice in a 2–1 success at RCD Mallorca.Getafe fight back for vital win; ESPN Soccernet, 17 December 2011
Barrada scored four goals in 32 games in both of his seasons with the club, helping it to consecutive midtable positions (11th and 10th).
