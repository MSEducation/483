[[Central District (Isfahan County)]]

CATEGORIES: Isfahan County, Districts of Isfahan Province

The Central District of Isfahan County ([tpl]lang-fa|بخش مرکزی شهرستان اصفهان[/tpl]) is a district (bakhsh) in Isfahan County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,860,674, in 515,487 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|10[/tpl]  The District has three cities: Isfahan, Baharestan, and Khvorasgan. The District has seven rural districts (dehestan): Baraan-e Jonubi Rural District, Baraan-e Shomali Rural District, Jey Rural District, Keraj Rural District, Mahmudabad Rural District, Qahab-e Jonubi Rural District, and Qahab-e Shomali Rural District.
