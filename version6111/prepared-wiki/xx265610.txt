[[Ke Seung-woon]]

Ke Seung-Woon (Hangul: 계승운 or 기승운; Hanja:?; born 26 December 1943) is a North Korean football midfielder who played for North Korea in the 1966 FIFA World Cup.Copa do Mundo da FIFA Inglaterra 1966 He also played for Rodongja Sports Club.
