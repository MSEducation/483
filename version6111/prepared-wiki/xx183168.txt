[[MSC Splendida]]

CATEGORIES: Cruise ships, Ships of MSC Cruises, Ships built in France

MSC Splendida is [tpl]sclass|Fantasia|cruise ship[/tpl] owned and operated by MSC Cruises. She entered service in March 2009, as a sister ship to MSC Fantasia. She was to be named MSC Serenata, but the name was changed in May 2007 to MSC Splendida[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.msccruises.com.au/default.asp?action=article&ID=40903  |archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20070829015959/http://www.msccruises.com.au/default.asp?action=article&ID=40903 |title=MSC Serenata is renamed MSC Splendida |date=2007-05-31 |publisher=MSC Cruises |archivedate=2007-08-29 |accessdate=2008-01-02[/tpl][tpl]cite web |title=MSC Serenata is Renamed MSC Splendida |url=http://www.yourcruiseyourway.com/2007/06/01/msc-serenata-is-renamed-msc-splendida/ |date=2007-06-01 |work=yourcruiseyourway|accessdate=2009-07-09[/tpl] "to reflect the ship's beauty and elegance".[tpl]cite web |title=MSC Serenata Becomes MSC Splenida |url=http://thecruisepeople.ca/cruiseblog/?p=70 |date=2007-05-31 |work=thecruisepeople.ca |accessdate=2009-07-09[/tpl]
MSC Splendida's itineraries cover Mediterranean ports including Marseille; Heraklion; Katakolon; Piraeus; Civitavecchia;  Genoa; Messina; Naples; Palermo; Valletta; Barcelona; Malaga; Gibraltar; Palma De Mallorca; Valencia; La Goulette; Izmir and Marmaris and Atlantic Ocean ports including Casablanca; Santa Cruz De Tenerife and Funchal.
MSC Splendida was delivered to MSC Cruises on July 4, 2009.[tpl]cite web |title=MSC Splendida completes trials |url=http://www.shipmanagementinternational.com/?p=1115 |date=2009-05-27 |work=Ship Management International |accessdate=2009-07-10[/tpl] She embarked on her maiden voyage on July 4, 2009, to the Mediterranean and returned July 11, to be officially named on July 12, 2009, in Barcelona by Sophia Loren.
