[[K T Jaleel]]

CATEGORIES: 1967 births, Living people, Indian politicians

Dr. K T Jaleel (born 30 May 1967) is an Indian politician, social worker and an author from Kerala, India. He is the Member of Legistative assembly from Thavanoor constituency in Malappuram district.[tpl]cite web|author= |url=http://niyamasabha.org/codes/13kla/mem/dr_k_t_jaleel.htm |title=Members - Kerala Legislature |publisher=Niyamasabha.org |date= |accessdate=2013-09-08[/tpl] He started his political carrier through Indian Union Muslim League, Later he came out of IUML protesting against the IUML leadership. Afterwards he contested independently with LDF support. He is also author of book “Oru Kodunkattaya neonapaksha Rashtreeyam” and published many articles in periodicals and News papers.
He was elected as an MLA for the first time in 2006, from Kuttipuram constituency as LDF supported Independent candidate, defeating then Muslim league General Secretary, P. K. Kunhalikutty,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.hindu.com/2006/05/14/stories/2006051400530500.htm |title=Kerala News : IUML citadel no more invincible |publisher=The Hindu |date=2006-05-14 |accessdate=2013-09-08[/tpl] after his expulsion from IUML, in 2005 because of his rebellious stand against the party leadership.[tpl]cite web|author= |url=http://www.milligazette.com/news/4847-kerala-peoples-front-against-iuml |title=Kerala: People’s front against IUML |publisher=Milligazette.com |date= |accessdate=2013-09-08[/tpl] In 2011, he was again elected from Thavanoor constituency, defeating Adv. V.V. Prakash (I.N.C) for a margin of 6854 votes.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.hindu.com/2011/04/11/stories/2011041155680700.htm |title=Kerala News : A hard fight for UDF, LDF in Thavanur |publisher=The Hindu |date=2011-04-11 |accessdate=2013-09-08[/tpl]

==Early life and education==

Jaleel was born the son of Shri K.T. Kunhi Muhammed and Smt. Parayil Nafeesa at Tirur, Malappuram. He completed his Primary education  from GUPS-Paikanur and GUPS-Velimukku. His Secondary education was from GHS-Kuttippuram. He did his Predegree from Islahiya College Chenamangalure and his Graduation and Post graduation from PSMO-College Tirurangadi.He owns an M.Phil-degree from Calicut university a Ph.D  from Kerala University Thiruvananthapuram on topic "Roll of Variyam Kunjahammed haji and Ali Musliyar in the Malabar Rebellation of 1921". He is currently Associate Professor in history, in PSMO-College Tirurangadi.[tpl]cite web|url=http://psmocollege.ac.in/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=49&Itemid=92 |title=Department of History |publisher=Psmocollege.ac.in |date= |accessdate=2013-09-08[/tpl]
