[[Red Brush, North Carolina]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Surry County, North Carolina, Unincorporated communities in North Carolina

Red Brush is a small unincorporated community in Surry County, North Carolina.  The community is roughly centered around the junction of Interstate 74 and Red Brush Road.  Prominent landmarks include Meadowview Middle School.
