[[Kiki Bosio]]

CATEGORIES: 1987 births, Living people, Santa Clara Broncos women's soccer players, United States women's international soccer players

Kiara "Kiki" Bosio (born November 3, 1987) is an American former soccer player from Mission Viejo, California. She played as a forward for the Women's Professional Soccer (WPS) club FC Gold Pride and the United States U-23 women's national soccer team.
Bosio played college soccer for Santa Clara Broncos, where she became known for her acrobatic flip throw-ins.[tpl]cite web|title=Broncos soccer team ready for flip side|publisher=San Francisco Chronicle|date=12 November 2009|accessdate=2 October 2013|first=Tom|last=Fitzgerald|url=http://www.sfgate.com/sports/article/Broncos-soccer-team-ready-for-flip-side-3281322.php[/tpl] While at University she also occasionally turned out for Ajax America Women of the Women's Premier Soccer League (WPSL).[tpl]cite web|title=The Bosio Sisters lead a Ajax family affair as they win their first WPSL Championship since 2001|publisher=Women's Premier Soccer League|accessdate=2 October 2013|url=http://www.wpsl.info/news/index.php/?cat=1&id=572|date=8 March 2008[/tpl]
In 2011 Bosio played for Orange County Waves alongside her sister Dani.[tpl]cite web|title=LOCALS: Waves make waves in women's game|publisher=ESPN|date=22 July 2011|accessdate=25 September 2013|first=Scott|last=French|url=http://espn.go.com/blog/los-angeles/soccer/post/_/id/9849/locals-waves-make-waves-in-womens-game[/tpl] She scored three goals in nine regular season appearances as the team won the 2011 WPSL Championship.[tpl]cite web|title=2011 Statistics - Games Played|publisher=Women's Premier Soccer League|accessdate=26 September 2013|url=http://www.wpsl.info/stats/index2011b.php?category=played[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=2011 Statistics - Goals|publisher=Women's Premier Soccer League|accessdate=26 September 2013|url=http://www.wpsl.info/stats/index2011b.php?category=goals[/tpl]
