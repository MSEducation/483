[[V.N. Tiwari]]

CATEGORIES: Nominated members of the Rajya Sabha, 1936 births, 1984 deaths, People from Punjab, India

Dr. V. N. Tiwari (1936-1984) is an Indian author and parliamentarian. He wrote books in Punjabi, English and Hindi. He was nominated as member of the Rajya Sabha in 1982 and served till 1984 shorty before his death.
