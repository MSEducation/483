Kevin Roderick Feely (born 30 August 1992) is an Irish professional footballer who plays for Newport County on loan from Charlton Athletic.

==Career==

Feely began his career with Bohemians in 2010, signed by Pat Fenlon, however it was under Aaron Callaghan that he became a regular first-team member in 2012. His performances attracted the attention of a number of overseas clubs, with both Peterborough United  and Everton amongst his rumoured suitors.
