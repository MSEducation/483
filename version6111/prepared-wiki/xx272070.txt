[[47th General Assembly of Newfoundland and Labrador]]

CATEGORIES: Terms of the General Assembly of Newfoundland and Labrador

The 47th Newfoundland and Labrador House of Assembly was elected on October 11, 2011. Members of the House of Assembly were sworn in on October 27, 2011, and former cabinet minister Ross Wiseman was named Speaker of the House of Assembly the same day.[tpl]cite news|title=Ross Wiseman named Speaker|url=http://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/newfoundland-labrador/story/2011/10/27/nl-speaker-house-assembly-1027.html|accessdate=14 April 2012|newspaper=Canadian Broadcasting Corporation|date=27 October 2011[/tpl]
