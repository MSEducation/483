[[Gorriti Island]]

CATEGORIES: Islands of the Río de la Plata, Punta del Este, River islands of Uruguay

Gorriti Island ([tpl]lang-es|Isla Gorriti[/tpl]) is a small island near the shores of Punta del Este, Uruguay.Isla Gorriti [tpl]es icon[/tpl]

==History==

Discovered in 1516 by Juan Díaz de Solís,Gorriti Island, past and present [tpl]es icon[/tpl] it was settled from the 18th century, when in the face of Portuguese influence it was of some military significance.
Gorriti Island forms part of Maldonado department.

==Declared a National Heritage==

The island has been declared to be a National Heritage. Many tourists visit its shores yearly.
