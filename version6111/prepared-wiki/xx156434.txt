[[Bundelkhand University]]

CATEGORIES: Universities and colleges in Uttar Pradesh, Education in Jhansi, 1975 establishments in India, Educational institutions established in 1975

Bundelkhand University is a public university based in Jhansi, Uttar Pradesh, India. Founded in 1975, it has professional, technical and vocational study programmes along with facilities for research.
