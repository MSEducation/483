[[S. Shankar Sastry]]

CATEGORIES: Control theorists, Indian roboticists, University of California, Berkeley faculty, Living people, Indian Institute of Technology Bombay alumni, Indian emigrants to the United States, University of California, Berkeley alumni, Massachusetts Institute of Technology faculty

S. Shankar Sastry is the Dean of the College of Engineering at the University of California, Berkeley. He currently holds the Nippon Electronics Corporation (NEC) Distinguished Professorship in the College of Engineering and the Walter A. Haas School of Business. He was elected to the National Academy of Engineering in 2001 and is a recipient of the Donald P. Eckman Award in 1990.

==Biography==

Sastry obtained his bachelor degree from Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay (1977), his master and PhD degrees from University of California, Berkeley (1979, 1980, 1981). His PhD advisor was Professor Charles Desoer.
