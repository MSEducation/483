[[Jenni Screen]]

Jenni Screen (born 26 February 1982 in Newcastle, New South Wales, Australia) is an Australian women's basketball player who plays for Australia and the Italian club team Basket Parma. She was a member of Australia women's national basketball team, "The Opals", that won a gold medal at the 2006 Commonwealth Games.
Screen is married to fellow basketballer Neil Mottram, brother of middle-distance runner Craig Mottram.
