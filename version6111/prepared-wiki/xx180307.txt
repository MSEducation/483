[[PAF Base Minhas]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in Pakistan, Pakistan Air Force bases, Attock District, Military installations in Punjab, Pakistan

Minhas Airbase or Kamra Airbase is a PAF Airbase located at Kamra, Attock District, Punjab, Pakistan. It was named in the honour of Pilot Officer Rashid Minhas, who was awarded the Nishan-e-Haider for valor in the Indo-Pakistani War of 1971.Active Bases PAF Official website. Retrieved 8 August 2010.
