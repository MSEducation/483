[[Kevin Leahy (Gaelic footballer)]]

Kevin Leahy is a current Gaelic football player for the Ballymun Kickhams. He is a former player with the Dublin Junior team.

==Playing career==

Kevin opened his 2007 Junior campaign with Dublin by scoring a total of 0-10 (0-9f) against Louth in the quarter final of the Leinster Junior Football Championship.http://www.hill16.ie/viewstoryhill16.asp?mainheading=Word+on+The+Hill&id=8385&viewstory=yes Dublin then progressed to the semi final against neighbours Wicklow. Leahy continued with his impressive scoring for Dublin by getting 0-8 (0-3f, 0-1 '45').http://www.hill16.ie/viewstoryhill16.asp?id=8443&mainheading=Word+on+The+Hill&viewstory=yes This leaves him with a total of 0-18 (0-12f, 0-1 '45') in the 2007 Junior championship. Dublin meet Wexford in the final as they beat last years champions Meath by 2-14 to 2-09.http://www.hoganstand.com/Meath/ArticleForm.aspx?ID=78781 Kevin continued with his scoring but his four points weren't enough to seal the victory against Wexford in the final. Wexford beat Dublin by 1-10 to 1-08 at Wexford Park.http://www.hill16.ie/viewstoryhill16.asp?id=8533&mainheading=Word+on+The+Hill&viewstory=yes
Kevin won the 2012 Dublin Senior Football Championship with Ballymun Kickhams against Kilmacud Crokes at Parnell Park.
