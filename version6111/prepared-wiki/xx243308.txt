[[Namgyal Tsemo Monastery]]

CATEGORIES: Buddhist monasteries in Ladakh, Tibetan Buddhist monasteries, 1430 establishments

Namgyal Tsemo Monastery or Namgyal Tsemo Gompa is a Buddhist monastery in Leh district, Ladakh, northern India.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.buddhist-temples.com/buddhist-monastery/ladakh/namgyal-tsemo.html|title=Namgyal Tsemo Gompa|publisher=Buddhist-temples.com|accessdate=October 19, 2009[/tpl]Founded in 1430 by King Tashi Namgyal of Ladakh, it has a three-story high gold idol of Maitreya Buddha and ancient manuscripts and frescoes.
The Monastery was featured in a television commercial for WorldSoccerShop.com in 2011.
