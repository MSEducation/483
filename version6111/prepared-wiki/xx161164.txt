[[1975 St. Louis Cardinals season]]

CATEGORIES: St. Louis Cardinals seasons, 1975 Major League Baseball season

The 1975 St. Louis Cardinals season was the team's 94th season in St. Louis, Missouri and its 84th season in the National League. The Cardinals went 82-80 during the season and finished in a tie for third (with the New York Mets) in the National League East, 10Â½ games behind the Pittsburgh Pirates.
