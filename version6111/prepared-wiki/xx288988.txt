[[Aq Bolagh Rural District]]

CATEGORIES: Rural Districts of Zanjan Province, Khodabandeh County

Aq Bolagh Rural District ([tpl]lang-fa|دهستان آق بلاغ[/tpl]) is a rural district (dehestan) in Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 5,996, in 1,226 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl] The rural district has 18 villages.
