[[Little Canfield]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Essex, Uttlesford, Civil parishes in Essex

Little Canfield is a village and a civil parish on the B1256 road, near the A120 road and the village of Takeley, in the Uttlesford district, in the county of Essex, England. It is in the Takeley and the Canfields ward of Uttlesford; and the  Dunmow division of Essex County Council. 
Little Canfield has a pub called the Lion and Lamb. The Lion and Lamb, Little Canfield in Essex near Stansted Airport
The village is currently expanding as housing developments are being built around the area.
