[[Fujimi, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Fujimi|富士見町|Fujimi-machi[/tpl] is a town located in Suwa District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the town has an estimated population of 15,525 and a density of 107.32 persons per km². The total area is 144.66 km².
