[[Türkmennebit]]

CATEGORIES: Oil and gas companies of Turkmenistan, National oil and gas companies

Türkmennebit (also spelled Turkmennebit, also known as Turkmenoil or Turkmenneft) is the national oil company of Turkmenistan. It has its headquarters in Ashgabat.  The chairman of the company is Annaguly Deryayev, the former minister of oil and gas industry and mineral resources of Turkmenistan.
Main oil fields operated by Türkmennebit are Koturdepe, Barsagelmes, Nebitdag, Korpeje, Kamyshlyja, Chekelen, and Kemer, mainly in the Balkan Province near the Caspian Sea.
