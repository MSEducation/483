[[Lajos Jekelfalussy]]

CATEGORIES: Hungarian politicians, Hungarian soldiers, 1848 births, 1911 deaths, Defence ministers of Hungary

Lajos Jekelfalussy (1 October 1848–1911) was a Hungarian military officer and politician, who served as Minister of Defence between 1906 and 1910. His grandfather was József Jekelfalussy royal chamberlain.
