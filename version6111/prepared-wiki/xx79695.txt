[[Rubyvale, Queensland]]

CATEGORIES: Mining towns in Queensland, Towns in Queensland, Central Queensland

Rubyvale is a town in the Australian state of Queensland, approximately [tpl]convert|61|km|mi[/tpl] west of Emerald.  Situated in a region called the Gemfields, sapphires are mined extensively in the area. At the 2006 census, Rubyvale had a population of 510.[tpl]Census 2006 AUS|id=UCL349600|name=Rubyvale (L) (Urban Centre/Locality)|accessdate=2009-07-21|quick=on[/tpl]
Rubyvale is home to a small library, a convenience store/news agents, post office, hotel and take-away bottle shop, as well as a variety of accommodation and fossicking areas.
