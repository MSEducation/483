[[Satsuki Muramoto]]

CATEGORIES: Japanese female single skaters, 1990 births, Living people

[tpl]nihongo|Satsuki Muramoto|村元 小月|Muramoto Satsuki|born May 15, 1990[/tpl] is a Japanese figure skater. She is the 2010 Triglav Trophy champion and 2009 Merano Cup silver medalist. Muramoto is coached by Mie Hamada and Yamato Tamura.
