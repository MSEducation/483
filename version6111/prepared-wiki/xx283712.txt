[[Mário Teixeira Costa]]

CATEGORIES: 1970 births, Living people, Portuguese footballers, Association football defenders, Primeira Liga players, Sporting Clube de Portugal footballers, S.L. Benfica footballers, FC Alverca players

Mário Teixeira Costa (born 24 November 1970 in Singen, Germany) is a retired Portuguese professional footballer who played as a right back

==Club career==

Born in the Germany, Marinho joined Sporting at 14 years old, climbing through the youth ranks, and playing nearly 50 league games in his 6-year spell al Alvalade.
In 1995, 24-year-old Marinho joined long time rivals Benfica as a replacement for Veloso, appearing sporadically over the course of two seasons.[tpl]cite web|title=Marinho|url=http://serbenfiquista.com/forum/index.php?topic=38467.0|work=Serbenfiquista.com[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Vedeta ou Marreta? n.º 161 Marinho|url=http://vedetaoumarreta.blogspot.pt/2009/09/n161-marinho.html|work=vedetaoumarreta.blogspot.pt[/tpl]  He then played for Campomaiorense, Alverca and ending his career in 2004 after 4 seasons at Estrela da Amadora with a hiatus from 1999 to 2001.
