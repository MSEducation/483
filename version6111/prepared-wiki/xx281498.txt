[[Do You Love Me (2NE1 song)]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 singles, 2NE1 songs, YG Entertainment singles, 2013 songs, Korean-language songs

"Do You Love Me" is a song by South Korean girl group 2NE1. The single was digitally released on August 7, 2013 and its music video was released the following day.[tpl]cite web|title=2NE1 asks 'Do You Love Me' with choreography video release|url=http://www.allkpop.com/article/2013/08/2ne1-asks-do-you-love-me-with-choreography-video-release|publisher=Allkpop|accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=2NE1's 'Do You Love Me' MV released!|url=http://www.allkpop.com/article/2013/08/2ne1s-do-you-love-me-mv-released|publisher=Allkpop|accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl]
The song, produced by YG Entertainment's main producer Teddy Park, is said to be very representative of 2NE1's electro-pop style.[tpl]cite web|title=NEWS ABOUT ‘2NE1 AND SEUNGRI’|url=http://www.yg-life.com/archives/20263?lang=en|publisher=YG-LIFE|accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl] The song's musical qualities parallel those of 2NE1's most well-known track "I Am the Best", released two years prior.

==Promotion==

2NE1 appeared on Korean music shows M! Countdown, Music Bank, and Inkigayo to perform the song live. Their live performance of "Do You Love Me" on KBS's Music Bank was their first on the show in three years since its entertainment agency ceased relationships with KBS following a dispute.[tpl]cite web|title=2NE1 finally performs on Music Bank after 3 long years|url=http://www.allkpop.com/article/2013/08/2ne1-finally-performs-on-music-bank-after-3-long-years|publisher=Allkpop|accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl]
