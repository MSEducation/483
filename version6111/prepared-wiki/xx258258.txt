[[Pontus Petterström]]

CATEGORIES: 1982 births, Living people, Linköpings HC players, New York Rangers draft picks, Skellefteå AIK players, Swedish ice hockey players

Pontus Petterström (born April 21, 1982) is a Swedish professional ice hockey winger. He currently plays for Skellefteå AIK of the Swedish Hockey League (SHL). He was selected by the New York Rangers in the 7th round (226th overall) of the 2001 NHL Entry Draft.
He previously played with Linköpings HC.Linkoping HC's 2010-11 Roster
