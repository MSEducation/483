[[Travis Ismaiel]]

CATEGORIES: South African rugby union players, Living people, 1992 births, Sportspeople from Cape Town, Blue Bulls players

Travis Ismaiel (born 2 June 1992 in Cape Town) is a South African rugby union player, currently playing with the [tpl]Rut Blue Bulls[/tpl].[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/playerprofile.aspx?id=53239 | title=SARU Player Profile Travis Ismaiel | work=South African Rugby Union | accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl] His regular position is winger.

==Career==

===Youth===

He played for the [tpl]Rut Blue Bulls U19[/tpl] team in the 2011 Under-19 Currie Cup competition and for the [tpl]Rut Blue Bulls U21[/tpl] team in 2012 and 2013.
He was included in the S.A. Under-20 squad for the 2012 IRB Junior World Championship competition and made one appearance for them, in their pool stage match against Italy.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/matchbreakdown.aspx?id=109261&category=&leagueid=1171&homeid=13091&awayid=13041&fullview=true | title=Match Breakdown: South Africa vs Italy | work=South African Rugby Union | date=8 June 2012 | accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl] He was also an unused substitute in the final against New Zealand.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/matchbreakdown.aspx?id=109391&category=&leagueid=1171&homeid=12981&awayid=13091&fullview=true | title=Match Breakdown: New Zealand vs South Africa | work=South African Rugby Union | date=22 June 2012 | accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl]

===Blue Bulls===

He made his first class debut for the [tpl]Rut Blue Bulls[/tpl] in their opening match of the 2013 Vodacom Cup competition, starting their match against [tpl]Rut Griquas[/tpl].[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/matchbreakdown.aspx?id=103764&category=&leagueid=1164&homeid=11664&awayid=11754&fullview=true | title=Match Breakdown: GWK Griquas vs Vodacom Blue Bulls | work=South African Rugby Union | date=9 March 2013 | accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl] He scored his first senior try a week later, when he dotted down in the 78th minute against the [tpl]Rut Pumas[/tpl].[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/matchbreakdown.aspx?id=103824&category=&leagueid=1164&homeid=11754&awayid=11644&fullview=true | title=Match Breakdown: Vodacom Blue Bulls vs Steval Pumas | work=South African Rugby Union | date=16 March 2013 | accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl] He made seven appearances in total in that competition, scoring six tries.
His Currie Cup debut came later in the same year, when he was included in the Blue Bulls starting team to face [tpl]Rut Western Province[/tpl] in the opening match of the 2013 Currie Cup Premier Division season.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.supersport.com/Rugby/currie-cup/news/130807/Bulls_now_take_their_orders_in_English | title=Bulls now take their orders in English | work=SuperSport | date=7 August 2013 | accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl]
