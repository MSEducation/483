[[1995–96 A PFG]]

CATEGORIES: Bulgarian A Professional Football Group seasons, 1995–96 domestic association football leagues, 1995 in Bulgarian sport, 1996 in Bulgarian sport

Statistics of Bulgarian A Professional Football Group in the 1995/1996 season.

==Overview==

It was contested by 16 teams, and PFC Slavia Sofia won the championship. Lovech, renamed from Lex Lovech in the previous season, were relegated along with FC Spartak Plovdiv and Shumen.
