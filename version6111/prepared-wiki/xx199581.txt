[[2008 Premios Juventud]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 awards, 2008 music awards, 2008 in sports, 2008 film awards, Latin American music awards, Latin American television awards, Hispanic and Latino American culture, 2008 in Florida

The fifth annual edition of the Premios Juventud youth awards show was held on July 17, 2008 at the BankUnited Center from the University of Miami in Coral Gables, Florida.[tpl]cite web |title="From peopleenespanol.com "Premiosjuventud|url=http://www.peopleenespanol.com/pespanol/en/articles/0,22490,1737256,00.html|accessdate=2008-05-20[/tpl] 
The event, presented and broadcast live by Univision,[tpl]cite web |title="Univision.com "Premiosjuventud|url=http://www.univision.com/content/channel.jhtml?chid=6&schid=10416| accessdate=2008-05-20| archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20080506085128/http://www.univision.com/content/channel.jhtml?chid=6&schid=10416| archivedate= 6 May 2008 | deadurl= no[/tpl] recognized the preferences of young Hispanic and Latino Americans with awards in over two dozen untraditional categories.Hispanic Youth Rules Airwaves on Premios Juventud 2008, a Business Wire July 2008 press release via MarketWatch Nearly half the awards were for music, with the rest spanning other areas of Hispanic popular culture including film, sports, and fashion (but not television).  Special awards were given to  Pedro Fernández for Career Achievement (Trayectoria Artística) and Thalía as Premiere Diva (Premio Diva).
The ceremony was hosted by Eduardo Santamarina, Mayrín Villanueva, and the Mexican pop group RBD.

==Audience==

The broadcast attracted an average audience of 5.2 million viewers and was the top-rated program of the evening among adults 18-34, attracting more viewers than all of its English-language competitors, including Are You Smarter Than a 5th Grader?, So You Think You Can Dance, Greatest American Dog, CSI, Swingtown, Last Comic Standing, Fear Itself, Ugly Betty, Grey's Anatomy, and Hopkins''.
