[[KZAL]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Washington (state), Country radio stations in the United States

KZAL (94.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a country music format.Licensed to Manson, Washington, USA, the station serves the Wenatchee  area.  The station is currently owned by Icicle Broadcasting Inc..
