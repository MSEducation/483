[[Hamid Nater]]

Hamid Nater ([tpl]lang-ar|حامد ناطر[/tpl]; born 30 December 1980) is a Moroccan football Midfielder who plays for Raja Casablanca.
Nater played for Raja at the 2000 FIFA Club World Cup.[tpl]FIFA player|176586[/tpl]
