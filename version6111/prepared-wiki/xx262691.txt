[[MicroFalcon]]

CATEGORIES: Unmanned aerial vehicles of Israel

The MicroFalcon is a small UAV produced by Innocon in Israel.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.flightglobal.com/articles/2009/10/27/334030/innocon-cites-market-interest-in-micro-falcon-uav.html|accessdate=2011-01-02|title=Innocon cites market interest in Micro Falcon UAV[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.defpro.com/news/details/14052/|accessdate=2011-01-02|title=Innocon’s Micro-Falcon UAV Highly Successful with Controp’s New Stamp Payload Onboard [/tpl]
The MicroFalcon typically operates at altitudes of 500-2000 feet, and speeds of 20-40 knots. It uses the a lightweight, compact sensor package,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.satnews.com/cgi-bin/story.cgi?number=1518554023|accessdate=2011-01-02|title=MicroBat'ing Success With Micro Falcon UAV : Satnews Publishers[/tpl] similar to that used by the Elbit Skylark.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.aviationweek.com/aw/blogs/defense/index.jsp?plckController=Blog&plckScript=blogScript&plckElementId=blogDest&plckBlogPage=BlogViewPost&plckPostId=Blog:27ec4a53-dcc8-42d0-bd3a-01329aef79a7Post:28b814d1-4ec0-42a9-979e-6ae842cfec65|accessdate=2011-01-02|title=Micro-Falcon MAV Tests Onboard STAMP Payload[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.asdnews.com/news/26870/Innocon_s_Micro-Falcon_UAV_Highly_Successful_With_STAMP_Payload_Onboard.htm|accessdate=2011-01-02|title=Innocon's Micro-Falcon UAV Highly Successful With STAMP Payload Onboard - ASDNews[/tpl]
MicroFalcon weighs 6-10kg and has 2-3 hour endurance.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.innoconltd.com/?CategoryID=179&ArticleID=104|accessdate=15 August 2011|title=Micro Falcon|publisher=Innocon[/tpl]
