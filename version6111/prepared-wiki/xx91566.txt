[[Gudrun Boysen]]

CATEGORIES: 1939 births, Living people, Danish physicians

Gudrun Margrethe Boysen (b. April 5, 1939 in Lille Utterslev, Denmark) is a Danish physician (MD and DM.Sc.) and conducts research into the causes and effects of strokes.http://www.skolamed.pl/img/panel/files/eso2010/Boysen.pdfhttp://strokeforum.infill.com/include/biographie.php?speakerID=99 She conducts her research at the Department of Neurology, Bispebjerg Hospital, Copenhagen. In 1994, Boysen won a Tagea Brandt Rejselegat.[tpl]Citation needed|date=January 2009[/tpl] She is married to Troels Johan Dahler Kardel and has two children, Maria (1972) and Sakina (1974).
