Metro Vallejo ([tpl]Lang-es|Estación Vallejo[/tpl]) is a station along Line 6 of the Mexico City Metro.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.metro.df.gob.mx/red/estacion.html?id=87|title=Vallejo|language=Spanish|accessdate=2 August 2011[/tpl][tpl]Cite web|last=Archambault|first=Richard|url=http://mexicometro.org/metro/line6/vallejo/|title=Vallejo »  Mexico City Metro System|accessdate=2 August 2011[/tpl] It is located in the Colonia Vallejo neighborhood of the Azcapotzalco borough of northwestern Mexico City.
Its logo represents a silhouette of a factory. The station opened on 21 December 1983.[tpl]Cite web|last=Monroy|first=Marco|editor-last=Schwandl|editor-first=Robert|url=http://www.urbanrail.net/am/mexi/mex-history.htm|title=Opening Dates for Mexico City's Subway|accessdate=2 August 2011[/tpl]
