[[(17555) 1993 VC5]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1993, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Mars-crossing asteroids

(17555) 1993 VC5 is a Mars-crossing minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on November 4, 1993.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
