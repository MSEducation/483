[[Judo at the 2011 Pan American Games – Women's 70 kg]]

CATEGORIES: Judo at the 2011 Pan American Games

The women's 70 kg competition of the judo events at the 2011 Pan American Games in Guadalajara, Mexico, was held on October 27 at the CODE II Gymanasium.[tpl]cite web|title=Judo technical manual|url=http://www.guadalajara2011.org.mx/documentos/manuales/eng/technical/tm_judo.pdf|publisher=Guadalajara 2011|accessdate=October 21, 2011[/tpl]  The defending champion was Ronda Rousey of the United States.
