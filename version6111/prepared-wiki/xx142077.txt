[[Desborough railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Disused railway stations in Northamptonshire, Beeching closures in England

Desborough railway station was built by the Midland Railway in 1857 on its extension from Leicester to Bedford and Hitchin. 
Later called Desborough and Rothwell it closed in 1968.Radford, B., (1983) Midland Line Memories: a Pictorial History of the Midland Railway Main Line Between London (St Pancras) & Derby London: Bloomsbury Books
