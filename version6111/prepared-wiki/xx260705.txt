[[Qataniaria]]

CATEGORIES: Prehistoric plants, Fossil taxa described in 2011

[tpl]italic title[/tpl]

Qataniaria is an extinct plant genus which existed in what is now Israel during the Albian period. It was described by Valentin Krassilov and Eckart Schrank in 2011, and the type species is Qataniaria noae.[tpl]cite journal |last1=Krassilov |first1=Valentin |last2=Schrank |first2=Eckart |year=2011 |title=New Albian macro- and palynoflora from the Negev (Israel) with description of a new gymnosperm morphotaxon |url=http://paleobotany.ru/PDF/2011/Krassilov_2011_New%20Albian%20macro.pdf |journal=Cretaceous Research |volume=32 |issue=1 |pages=13â€“29 |doi=10.1016/j.cretres.2010.10.001[/tpl]
