[[Paul Jelfs]]

CATEGORIES: Australian rugby league players, Sydney Roosters players, Living people

Paul Jelfs is an Australian former professional rugby league player for Sydney's Eastern Suburbs and South Sydney clubs in the New South Wales Rugby League(NSWRL) competition.
Jelfs played in 18 matches for the Roosters before joining South Sydney in 1981 playing in a further 6 matches. Jelfs formerly played rugby union with the Eastern Suburbs rugby union club before switching codes to play league in 1978.
