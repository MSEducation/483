[[Office Mayhem]]

CATEGORIES: 2007 books, Comedy books, Business books, Practical jokes, Abrams Books books

Office Mayhem: A Handbook to Practical Anarchy is a book published by Abrams Books on September 1, 2007. It covers techniques  to "bring down the post-industrial office complex and unite the worlds workers." To do this, it gives you a list of pranks to pull in the office, each one softening the office's tyrannical rule.Link text
