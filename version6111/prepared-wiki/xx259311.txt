Richard Piakura (born [tpl]birth date and age|df=yes|1976|07|01[/tpl]) is a Cook Islander athlete and professional rugby league and rugby union footballer of the 1990s and 2000s, playing representative level rugby league (RL) for the Cook Islands, and playing representative level rugby union (RU) for the Cook Islands at rugby sevens.

==Athletics career==

Piakura represented the Cook Islands in the high jump, setting his personal best of 1.83m in Tereora in 1998.Piakura, Richard iaaf.org This is the Cook Islands record in high jump.
