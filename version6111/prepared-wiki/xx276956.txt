[[When I Feel Like It]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 singles, Fabolous songs, 2 Chainz songs, Songs written by 2 Chainz, Def Jam Recordings singles, 2013 songs

"When I Feel Like It" is a song by American hip hop recording artist Fabolous featuring fellow American rapper 2 Chainz. The song serves as the second single from Fabolous' sixth studio album Loso's Way 2: Rise to Power (2014). The song was released on July 9, 2013 and was produced by David D.A. Doman. The song has since peaked at number five on the US Billboard Bubbling Under R&B/Hip-Hop Singles chart.
