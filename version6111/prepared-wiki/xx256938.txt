[[J.C. Peters House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses completed in 1865, Greek Revival architecture in Iowa, Houses in Davenport, Iowa, Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Iowa, National Register of Historic Places in Scott County, Iowa

The J.C. Peters House is located in the West End of Davenport, Iowa, United States.  The Greek Revival residence has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since 1984.
