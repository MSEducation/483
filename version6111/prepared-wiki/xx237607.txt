[[Dino Urbani]]

CATEGORIES: 1882 births, 1958 deaths, Italian fencers, Olympic fencers of Italy, Fencers at the 1920 Summer Olympics, Olympic gold medalists for Italy, Olympic medalists in fencing, People from Livorno, Medalists at the 1920 Summer Olympics

Dino Urbani (born 8 March 1882 in Livorno, died 9 May 1958 in Varese) was an Italian fencer who took part in the 1920 Olympics in Antwerp. 
Urbani was Olympic champion in fencing twice.
