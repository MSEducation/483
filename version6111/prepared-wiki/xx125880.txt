[[Frode Glesnes]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Norwegian black metal musicians, Norwegian rock singers, Norwegian male singers, Norwegian rock guitarists, Year of birth missing (living people), Norwegian heavy metal singers, Norwegian heavy metal guitarists, Norwegian multi-instrumentalists, Place of birth missing (living people)

Frode Glesnes is one of the founders of the Norwegian Viking metal band Einherjer. He started as guitarist/vocalist, but decided to concentrate on the guitar duties. He did the lead vocals on  Einherjer's  last album, Blot.[tpl]cite web|url=[tpl]Allmusic|class=artist|id=p302817/biography|pure_url=yes[/tpl]|title=Biography-Einherjer|last=DaRonco|first=Mike|publisher=Allmusic|accessdate=20 February 2010[/tpl] He started a new band with the other Einherjer members called  Battered.[tpl]cite web|url=[tpl]Allmusic|class=album|id=r821276|pure_url=yes[/tpl]|title=Review Battered|last=Henderson|first=Alex|publisher=Allmusic|accessdate=20 February 2010[/tpl] He did the lead vocals on their first demo recording, but they chose a new singer. He also had a couple of projects with Einherjer drummer Gerhard Storesund called Angelgrinder and Beelzebub.
