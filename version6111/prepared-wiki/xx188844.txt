[[Curicaca District]]

Curicaca (in hispanicized spelling) or Quriqaqa (Quechua quri gold, qaqa rock, "gold rock") is one of thirty-four districts of the province Jauja in Peru.[tpl]es icon[/tpl] Instituto Nacional de Estadística e Informática. Banco de Información Distrital. Retrieved April 11, 2008.
