[[2006 AF2 season]]

CATEGORIES: AF2 seasons, 2006 Arena Football League season

The 2006 AF2 season was the seventh season of the AF2, the Arena Football League's minor league.  The league champions were the Spokane Shock who capped off an impressive inaugural season with a win over the Green Bay Blizzard in Arena Cup VII.
