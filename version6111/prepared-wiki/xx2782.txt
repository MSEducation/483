[[Ecuador]]

CATEGORIES: Ecuador, Andean Community of Nations, Countries in South America, Former Spanish colonies, Member states of OPEC, Member states of the Union of South American Nations, Member states of the United Nations, Republics, Spanish-speaking countries and territories, States and territories established in 1830

Ecuador ([tpl]IPAc-en|audio=en-us-Ecuador.ogg|ˈ|ɛ|k|w|ə|d|ɔr[/tpl] [tpl]respell|E|kwə-dawr[/tpl]), officially the Republic of Ecuador ([tpl]lang-es|República del Ecuador|links=no[/tpl] [tpl]IPA-es|reˈpuβlika ðel ekwaˈðor|[/tpl], which literally translates as "Republic of the Equator") is a representative democratic republic in northwestern South America, bordered by Colombia on the north, Peru on the east and south, and the Pacific Ocean to the west. Ecuador also includes the Galápagos Islands in the Pacific, about [tpl]convert|1000|km[/tpl] west of the mainland.
The main spoken language in Ecuador is Spanish (94% of the population). Languages of official use in native communities include Quichua, Shuar, and eleven other languages. Ecuador has a land area of 283,520 km2. Its capital city is Quito, which was declared a World Heritage Site by UNESCO in the 1970s for having the best preserved and least altered historic center in Latin America.
