[[15963 Koeberl]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Astronomical objects discovered in 1998

15963 Koeberl is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1600.6681545 days (4.38 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=15963  |  title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-04-29 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on February 6, 1998 and was named after Christian Koeberl.
