[[Cambarellus shufeldtii]]

CATEGORIES: Cambaridae, Freshwater crustaceans of North America, Animals described in 1884

[tpl]Italic title[/tpl]

Cambarellus shufeldtii is a small species of crayfish native to the Southern United States. The males reach a length of about [tpl]convert|1.5|in|mm[/tpl], while the females are somewhat smaller. They are easily bred in a tank, and kept by freshwater aquarists, who commonly refer to them as Cajun dwarf crayfish.
