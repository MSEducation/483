[[Bozusha]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Bozusha ([tpl]lang-fa|بزوشا[/tpl], also Romanized as Bozūshā; also known as Bazoo Shah, Bazūshāh, Bazushakh, Bozowshā, and Bozūsheh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3057038|Bozusha[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 768, in 172 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
