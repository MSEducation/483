[[B+H Architects]]

CATEGORIES: Architecture firms of Canada, Companies established in 1953

B+H Architects (formerly known as Bregman + Hamann Architects), founded in 1953, is an international architecture, interior design and urban planning firm working from offices in Toronto, Vancouver, Calgary, Seattle, Shanghai, Beijing, Hong Kong, Singapore, Ho Chi Minh City, Delhi, Doha, and Dubai.
With over 450 architects, designers, planners, and practice support teams working in 12 offices around the world.
