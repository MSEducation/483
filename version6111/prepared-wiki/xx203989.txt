[[Onda Station]]

[tpl]nihongo|Onda Station|恩田駅|Onda-eki[/tpl] is a railway station operated by Yokohama Minatomirai Railway's Kodomonokuni Line located in Aoba-ku, Yokohama, Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan. It is 1.8 kilometers from the terminus of the Kodomonokuni Line at Nagatsuta Station.

==History==

Onda Station was opened on March 29, 2000.

==Lines==

==Station layout==

Onda Station has an elevated island platform serving two tracks.  The station building is normally unattended.
