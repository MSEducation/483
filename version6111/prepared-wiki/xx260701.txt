[[1984 WTA Swiss Open]]

CATEGORIES: 1984 Virginia Slims World Championship Series, European Open (tennis)

The 1984 WTA Swiss Open  was a tennis tournament played on outdoor clay courts in Lugano, Switzerland that was part of the 1984 Virginia Slims World Championship Series. The tournament was held from May 7 through May 13, 1984.
