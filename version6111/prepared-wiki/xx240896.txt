[[Malangali, Ileje]]

CATEGORIES: Wards of Tanzania, Populated places in the Mbeya Region

Malangali, Ileje is an administrative ward in the Ileje District of the Mbeya Region of Tanzania, East Africa. [tpl]As of|2002[/tpl], the ward had a total population of 10,066.[tpl]Cite web|title=2002 Population and Housing General Report: Mbeya: Ileje|url=http://www.tanzania.go.tz/census/census/districts/ileje.htm|archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20040318054624/http://www.tanzania.go.tz/census/census/districts/ileje.htm|archivedate=18 March 2004|deadurl=yes[/tpl]
