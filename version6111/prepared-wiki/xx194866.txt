[[Salis of the Maldives]]

CATEGORIES: Maldivian nobility, 1302 deaths, 14th-century monarchs in Asia, 13th-century monarchs in Asia, Year of birth unknown

Al-Sultan Salis Kalaminjaa Siri Meesuvara Mahaa Radun (Dhivehi: އައްސުލްޠާން ސަލިސް ކަލަމިންޖާ ސިރީ މީސުވަރަ މަހާރަދުން) was the Sultan of Maldives from 1293 to 1302. He succeeded to the throne after the death of his father Sultan Yoosuf I, by becoming the 15th sultan to ascend the throne of Maldives from the Lunar dynasty.
