[[Linda Emond]]

CATEGORIES: 1959 births, American film actresses, American stage actresses, American musical theatre actresses, American television actresses, Living people, Actresses from New Jersey, People from New Brunswick, New Jersey, University of Washington alumni

Linda Emond (born May 22, 1959) is an American stage, film and television actress.

==Life and career==

Emond was born in New Brunswick, New Jersey. She graduated with a BA in Theatre Arts from California State University, Fullerton in 1982.  She earned an MFA from the Professional Actor Training Program at the University of Washington, Seattle.University of Washington Alumni, washington.edu; accessed April 17, 2014.
