[[Jibon Singh]]

CATEGORIES: Indian footballers, 1990 births, Living people, Kingfisher East Bengal FC players, I-League players, India international footballers, Pailan Arrows players, People from Manipur, Footballers from Manipur

Jibon Singh is an Indian footballer who plays for Shillong Lajong in the I-League. He plays in Midfield position. Jibon Singh represented the India U23 in the SAFF Cup 2009 & 2012 AFC Pre-Olympic Tournament in 2011.

==Career==

===Pailan Arrows===

For the 2010-11 I-League season Singh played for I-League newcomers Indian Arrows. Indian Arrows is only made up of Indian Under-19 players. On December 3, 2010 Singh took part in Indian Arrows first I-League game ever against Chirag United, the final score was 2-1 in Chirag United's favor.

====2011-12====

Singh started off the 2011-12 footballing season in the 2011 Indian Federation Cup with Pailan Arrows (Indian Arrows changed name). He was selected as the captain of Pailan Arrows for 2011-12 season. In Pailan's first match against HAL S.C., Singh scored the only goal of the game in the 89th minute from the penalty spot which left Pailan the 1-0 winners.http://the-aiff.com/pages/news/index.php?N_Id=2733
