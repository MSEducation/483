Fraser Aird (born 2 February 1995) is a Canadian-born Scottish footballer currently playing for Scottish League One side Rangers.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.rangers.co.uk/teams/first-team/item/420-fraser-aird | title=Fraser Aird Profile | publisher=Rangers FC | accessdate=28 October 2012[/tpl][tpl]cite web | url=http://www.uefa.com/under17/season=2012/teams/player=250042539/index.html | title=Fraser Aird profile | publisher=UEFA | accessdate=28 October 2012[/tpl]

==Personal life==

Aird is a boyhood fan of his current club Rangers due to his Scottish, Rangers supporting, parents immigrating to Canada in 1987.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.scotsman.com/sport/football/sfl-division-three/family-pride-at-stake-for-rangers-starlet-fraser-aird-as-dad-flies-in-1-2602810 | title=Family pride at stake for Rangers’ starlet Fraser Aird as dad flies in | publisher=The Scotsman | accessdate=28 October 2012[/tpl][tpl]cite web | url=http://torontoobserver.ca/2012/10/20/born-to-be-a-ranger/ | title=Born to be a Rangers | publisher=Toronto Observer | accessdate=1 February 2013[/tpl]

==Club career==

===Early career===

Aird signed as a youth for Rangers at the age of sixteen.[tpl]cite news | url=http://www.thestar.com/sports/soccer/article/936640--scarborough-teen-signed-by-scottish-giant-rangers-fc | title=Scarborough teen signed by Scottish giant Rangers FC | publisher=Toronto Star | accessdate=28 October 2012 | date=10 February 2011[/tpl]

===Rangers===

====2012-13 season====

Within a year at the club he worked his way from the under-17 squad to the under-19 squad, eventually making his first team debut during a 4–1 victory over Montrose in the Scottish Third Division on 23 September 2012.[tpl]cite news | url=http://www.thestar.com/sports/soccer/article/1262194--scarborough-teen-debuts-with-glasgow-rangers | title=Scarborough teen debuts with Glasgow Rangers | publisher=Toronto Star | accessdate=28 October 2012 | date=25 September 2012[/tpl] He scored his first goal for the club against Queens Park at Hampden on 29 December 2012.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/20857984 | title=Queen's Park 0–1 Rangers| publisher=BBC Sport | date=29 December 2012[/tpl]

====2013-14 season====

In the first competitive match of the 2013–14 season he scored the first goal for Rangers as a half-time substitute against Forfar Athletic in the League Cup, Rangers went on to lose 2–1.

==International career==

Having played at youth international level for the Scotland under-17's, Aird was called up to the preliminary Canadian national team squad for the 2013 Gold Cup in the USA.[tpl]cite press release |title=Canada announces preliminary roster for Gold Cup |date=31 May 2013 |publisher=Canada Soccer |url=http://www.canadasoccer.com/canada-announces-preliminary-roster-for-gold-cup-p154099 |accessdate=31 May 2013[/tpl] Aird has since been called up to the Scotland under-19's, but is still not tied to one nation until he makes a first team appearance.[tpl]cite news |title=Decision time for Gers kid Fraser Aird as Scotland tussle with Canada |first=Chris |last=Jack |url=http://www.eveningtimes.co.uk/rangers/decision-time-for-gers-kid-fraser-aird-as-scotland-tussle-with-canada-130970n.21642918 |newspaper=Evening Times |date=19 July 2013 |accessdate=23 August 2013[/tpl] 
In April 2014 it was reported that Aird was set to pledge his international allegiance to Canada and join the senior squad for friendlies against Bulgaria and Moldova in Austria the following month.[tpl]cite web|title=Rumour Mill: Fraser Forster [tpl]![/tpl] Rangers [tpl]![/tpl] World Cup|url=http://www.scotsman.com/sport/football/spfl/rumour-mill-fraser-forster-rangers-world-cup-1-3386697|publisher=The Scotsman|accessdate=25 April 2014[/tpl]
