[[Kumamoto Prefectural Gymnasium]]

CATEGORIES: Indoor arenas in Japan, Volleyball venues, Sports venues in Kumamoto Prefecture, Buildings and structures completed in 1982

[tpl]nihongo|Kumamoto Prefectural Gymnasium|熊本県立総合体育館|Kumamoto-kenritsu Sōgō Taiikukan[/tpl] is an indoor sports arena, located in Kumamoto, Japan. 
The arena holds 1,480 people.
The hall hosted some of the group games for the 2007 FIVB Women's World Cup. It will host some of the group games for the 2011 FIVB Men's World Cup.
