Sandali Sinha is an Indian Bollywood actress and model. Her acting in Tum Bin brought her attention.Sandali sinha-Times of India

==Early life==

She comes from an Air Force background. Her father died in a road accident. Her mother raised three children, including Sandali, in Delhi. She did her schooling from Air Force Bal Bharati School and graduated from Jesus and Mary College, Delhi. Sinha went to Kishore Namit Kapoor Acting Institute. Born in a family of pilots and doctors, Sinha was keen on becoming a doctor. But a brief experience on an amateur fashion show made her shift to the world of modelling.Sandali Sinha Personality

==Career==

A graduate in commerce, Sinha shifted to Mumbai for better prospects. In a short period, Sinha became a model.Sandali's interview Fame first came to her with a Sonu Nigam music video of video called "Deewana" which was directed by Anubhav Sinha. This music video took Sinha to her debut film Tum Bin, directed by Anubhav Sinha and produced by Super Cassettes Industries Limited. In the film, Sinha plays Pia, an innocent woman who becomes a victim of circumstances. Paired opposite her are three famous models: Priyanshu Chatterjee, Himanshu Malik and Rakesh Bapat.
