[[Quzan, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Quzan ([tpl]lang-fa|قوزان[/tpl], also Romanized as Qūzān; also known as Ozan and Uzūn)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3080551|Quzan[/tpl] is a village in Golabar Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 520, in 134 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
