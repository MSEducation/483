[[It All Starts Here...]]

CATEGORIES: Jem (singer) albums, 2004 EPs, Debut EPs, English-language EPs, ATO Records albums, RCA Records EPs

It All Starts Here... is the first EP by Jem, released in 2003 (see 2003 in music). It was followed in 2004 by her full-length LP, Finally Woken, which features six new tracks.
The cover features a picture of her older sister, Chloe, that was taken by her mother.
