Ginny Aiken is an author of inspirational fiction and mystery novels. Ginny was born in Havana, Cuba, and grew up in Valencia and Caracas, Venezuela.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.goodreads.com/author/show/168021.Ginny_Aiken|title=Ginny Aiken|publisher=Goodreads profile|accessdate=14 May 2013[/tpl]
The former newspaper reporter discovered books early on and wrote her first novel at age fifteen. That effort was followed years later by award-winning and best-selling titles in the secular and Christian markets.[tpl]fact|date=May 2013[/tpl] Aiken holds certification in French literature and culture from the University of Nancy, France, as well as a B. A. from Allegheny College in Pennsylvania. She lives in South-Central Pennsylvania.
