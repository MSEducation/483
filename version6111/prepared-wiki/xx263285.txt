[[Instinkt Obrechyonnykh]]

CATEGORIES: Heavy metal albums by Russian artists

Instinkt Obrechyonnykh ([tpl]lang-ru|Инстинкт обречённых[/tpl], lit. "Instinct of the Doomed") is the fifth and final studio album by the Russian nu-metal band Amatory. The album was produced by Tue Madsen.
