[[Twin Forks, New Mexico]]

CATEGORIES: Census-designated places in New Mexico, Census-designated places in Otero County, New Mexico

Twin Forks is a census-designated place in Otero County, New Mexico, United States. Its population was 196 as of the 2010 census.[tpl]GR|8[/tpl] U.S. Route 82 passes through the community.
