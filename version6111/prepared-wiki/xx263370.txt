[[Volpriehausen station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Lower Saxony

Volpriehausen is a railway station located in Volpriehausen, Germany.

==History==

The station is located on the Sollingbahn (Ottbergen to Northeim). The train services are operated by Deutsche Bahn.
