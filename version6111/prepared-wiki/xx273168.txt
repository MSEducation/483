Jeremiah D. O'Connor (born 1940) is an Irish former Gaelic footballer who played as a midfielder for the Kerry senior team.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.terracetalk.com/kerry-football/player/504/JD-OConnor|title=Player profile: Jer D. O'Connor|date=|publisher=Terrace Talk|accessdate=25 September 2011[/tpl] 
O'Connor made his first appearance for the team during the 1960 championship and established himself as a regular member of the starting fifteen between 1964 and 1967. During that time an All-Ireland medal eluded him, however, he won three Munster winner's medals and one National League winner's medal.
At club level, O'Dowd played with the Ballydonoghue club and the Shannon Rangers divisional team.
