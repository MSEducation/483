Jewel Belair Staite (born June 2, 1982) is a Canadian actress. Her most known works include Catalina in Space Cases (1996–1997),  Kaylee Frye in Firefly (2002–2003) and its spin-off film Serenity (2005), and Dr. Jennifer Keller on Stargate Atlantis (2007–2009). Staite has also starred as Raquel Westbrook in the Canadian drama The L.A. Complex (2012).

==Personal life==

Staite was born in White Rock, British Columbia.  The youngest of seven children, she modeled as a child and has acted since age six. She has stated that she is of British, Irish, French Canadian, and Iroquois ancestry.[tpl]cite web | title = Jewel Staite Interview | first = Michael J.| last = Lee | work = Radio Free Entertainment | date = September 15, 2005 | url = http://www.radiofree.com/profiles/jewel_staite/interview02.shtml [/tpl]
Staite attended the Vancouver Film School and worked at the Vancouver Youth Theatre.
She was married to actor Matt Anderson from 2003 to 2011.[tpl]cite web | date = 13 December 2011 | first = Jewel | last = Staite | url = http://happyopu.net/happier-than-a-pig-in | title = Happier Than a Pig In...: |work=Happy Opu |accessdate=April 16, 2013[/tpl]

==Career==

Staite played Kaylee Frye in the short-lived space western television series Firefly and its subsequent 2005 film, Serenity. She wrote a chapter in the book Finding Serenity, called "Kaylee Speaks: Jewel Staite On Firefly".[tpl]cite web | last = Foy | first = Scott | date = November 10, 2009 | url = http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/34485/firefly-star-encounters-mothman-syfy | title = Firefly Star Encounters Mothman on Syfy | work = Dread Central | accessdate = December 31, 2013 [/tpl]
She played Heidi Gotts in the television series Wonderfalls, and appeared in a Season Three episode of The X-Files.
Staite played the role of head medical doctor Jennifer Keller in the Stargate SG-1 spinoff Stargate Atlantis,[ref]

 [tpl]Dead link|date=September 2010|bot=H3llBot[/tpl][/ref] making her the third actor from the Firefly series to move on to appear in a Stargate series (the first being Adam Baldwin, who had a brief role as a colonel in the Stargate SG-1 Season Seven episode "Heroes", and the second being Morena Baccarin, who played Adria, the main villain in the tenth season of SG-1), and the second to take a recurring role. She replaced Paul McGillion (Dr. Carson Beckett). In the fifth season her character was changed from recurring status to part of the main cast. Before taking on the role as Dr. Keller she  previously played the Wraith child Ellia in the Stargate Atlantis episode "Instinct".[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/37091/syfys-mothman-spreads-its-wings-new-clip |title=Syfy's Mothman Spreads Its Wings in New Clip|last=Barton|first=Steve|work=Dread Central|date=April 21, 2010|accessdate=April 16, 2013[/tpl] She also appeared in the 2010 Sci-Fi film, Mothman, under direction from Sheldon Wilson.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.bloody-disgusting.com/news/19914 |title=TV: First Look at 'Mothman' Creature in Clip|last=Miska|first=Brad|work=Bloody Disgusting|date=April 21, 2010|accessdate=April 16, 2013[/tpl]
Staite guest starred in an episode of Warehouse 13 as the love interest of Sheldon (played by Sean Maher, who played her love interest in Firefly).
She also did voices in the animated TV series Mummies Alive! and Sabrina: The Animated Series for DIC Entertainment. As of 2012, Staite was a series regular on The L.A. Complex, in which she portrayed Raquel Westbrook, a struggling actress. The show aired in Canada on MuchMusic and in the U.S. on The CW.  She also appeared recently in an episode of The Listener.
