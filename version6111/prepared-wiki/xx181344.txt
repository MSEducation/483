[[KPQ (AM)]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Washington (state), Radio stations established in 1929, News and talk radio stations in the United States

KPQ (560 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a News Talk Information format. Licensed to Wenatchee, Washington, USA, the station serves the Wenatchee area. The station is currently owned by Ccr-Wescoast Iv, LLC and features programing from ABC Radio and Premiere Radio Networks.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=KPQ |title=KPQ Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
KPQ began broadcasting on May 2, 1928.KPQ-AM in Wenatchee WA begins radio transmissions May 2 in History
