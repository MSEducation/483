[[Onhaye]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Namur (province)

Onhaye is a Walloon municipality located in the Belgian province of Namur. On January 1, 2006 Onhaye had a total population of 3,120. The total area is 65.53 km² which gives a population density of 48 inhabitants per km².
The region is home to several cultural heritage monuments, such as the fortified farmhouse Falaën Castle.
