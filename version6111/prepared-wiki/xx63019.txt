[[South Cariboo Regional Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Registered aerodromes in British Columbia, Cariboo Regional District

South Cariboo Regional Airport or 108 Mile Ranch Airport [tpl]Airport codes|ZMH|CZML[/tpl] is a registered aerodrome located [tpl]convert|5.3|NM|lk=in[/tpl] northwest of 108 Mile Ranch, British Columbia, Canada. The airport is the regional aerodrome for the South Cariboo Regional District, handling over 2,000 movements a year. It provides facilities for commercial passenger service, MEDEVAC, flight training and corporate traffic.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.airport-data.com/world-airports/CZML-ZMH/|title=Information of South Cariboo Regional Airport (108 Mile Ranch Airport)|publisher=Airport-Data.com[/tpl]

==Facilities and aircraft==

South Cariboo Regional Airport contains one Asphalt paved runway.
In 2011, the airport had a total of 963 plane arrivals and departures; 72 of these were medevacs.
The airport is located within walking distance of two resorts and an 18 hole PGA golf course.[tpl]cite web|url=http://southcaribooregionalairport.com/|title=South Cariboo Regional Airport|publisher=South Cariboo Regional Airport|accessdate=17 January 2013[/tpl]

==Events==

Each year, the airport hosts Airport Day. This event was created for locals to enjoy aviation activities and learn more about the aviation industry and, in particular, the airport as a valuable south Cariboo asset.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cariboord.bc.ca/Services/Airports/SouthCaribooRegionalAirport.aspx|title=South Cariboo Regional Airport|publisher=Cariboo Regional District[/tpl]
