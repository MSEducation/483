[[Pinpollitol]]

CATEGORIES: Cyclitols

Pinpollitol is a cyclitol. It is a di-O-methyl-(+)-chiro-inositol that can be isolated from Pinus radiata.[tpl]cite journal | last1 = Gallagher | first1 = R | title = (+)-Pinpollitol: A di-O-methyl D-(+)-chiro-inositol from Pinus radiata | journal = Phytochemistry | volume = 14 | pages = 755 | year = 1975 | doi = 10.1016/0031-9422(75)83029-9[/tpl]
