[[Michael Horace Miller]]

CATEGORIES: Royal Air Force officers, People of the Royal Observer Corps, Commanders of the Order of the British Empire, Recipients of the Air Force Cross (United Kingdom), 1928 births, Living people

Air Commodore Michael Horace Miller CBE, AFC was a senior Royal Air Force officer in the post-war years and a Commandant Royal Observer Corps.http://www.rafweb.org/Grp09.htm  From 1971 to 1973, Miller was Station Commander of RAF Gutersloh.http://www.rafweb.org/Stations/Station%20OCs-Germany.htm
