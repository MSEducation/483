[[Carl Hall (mountaineer)]]

CATEGORIES: 1848 births, 1908 deaths, People from Frederiksberg, Danish mountain climbers, Knights of the Order of St. Olav

Carl Hall (1848 – 1908) was a Danish mountaineering pioneer. 
He was born in Frederiksberg as a son of Prime Minister Carl Christian Hall. He made 46 first ascents of Norwegian mountains.
