[[OpenDyslexic]]

CATEGORIES: Dyslexia, Sans-serif typefaces, Open-source typefaces

OpenDyslexic is a free typeface/font designed to mitigate some of the common reading errors caused by dyslexia. The typeface was created by Abelardo Gonzalez, who released it through an open-source license.[tpl]cite web|last=Jason|first=Mick|title=First Free Digital Font Optimized for Dyslexics Arrives|url=http://www.dailytech.com/First+Free+Digital+Font+Optimized+for+Dyslexics+Arrives/article27801.htm|publisher=DailyTech.com|accessdate=August 13, 2013[/tpl] 
Like many dyslexia-intervention typefaces, most notably Dyslexie, OpenDyslexic adds to dyslexia research and is a reading aid, but it is not a cure for dyslexia.[tpl]cite web|last=Ross|first=Selena|title=New Font Helps Dyslexics Read Clearly|url=http://thebottomline.as.ucsb.edu/2012/10/new-font-helps-dyslexics-read-clearly|publisher=Associated Students, UC Santa Barbara|accessdate=August 13, 2013[/tpl] The typeface includes regular, bold, italic, bold-italic, and monospaced font styles. In 2012, Gonzalez explained his motivation to the BBC, "I had seen similar fonts, but at the time they were completely unaffordable and so impractical as far as costs go."[tpl]cite web|last=Kelion|first=Leo|title=OpenDyslexic font gains ground with help of Instapaper|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/technology-19734341|publisher=BBC News.|accessdate=August 13, 2013[/tpl] 
The typeface is currently an optional choice on many websites and formats, including Wikipedia,[tpl]cite web|last=Bhattacharjee|first=Runa|title=Universal Language Selector (ULS) deployed on more than 150 wikis|url=http://blog.wikimedia.org/2013/07/01/universal-language-selector-uls-deployed-on-more-than-150-wikis/|publisher=Wikimedia|accessdate=August 13, 2013[/tpl] Instapaper,[tpl]cite web|last=Kelion|first=Leo|title=OpenDyslexic font gains ground with help of Instapaper|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/technology-19734341|publisher=BBC News.|accessdate=August 13, 2013[/tpl] the Kobo eReader,[tpl]cite web|last=Gonzalez|first=Abelardo|title=Kobo Mini, Glo, Aura HD|url=http://opendyslexic.org/2013/03/kobo-mini-and-glo/|publisher=OpenDyslexic|accessdate=August 13, 2012[/tpl] a few children's books,[tpl]cite web|last=Gonzalez|first=Abelardo|title=Zack & Zoeyâ€™s Alien Apocalypse: Alien Busting Ninja Adventure|url=http://opendyslexic.org/2013/08/zack-zoeys-alien-apocalypse-alien-busting-ninja-adventure/|publisher=OpenDyslexic|accessdate=August 13, 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web|last=Gonzalez|first=Abelardo|title=The Swamp Man, by Shoo Rayner|url=http://opendyslexic.org/2013/07/the-swamp-man-by-shoo-rayner/|publisher=OpenDyslexic|accessdate=August 13, 2013[/tpl] and several imprints of classic literature.[tpl]cite web|title=http://www.folktal.es/strawberry-classics.html|url=http://www.folktal.es/strawberry-classics.html|publisher=Strawberry Classics|accessdate=August 13, 2013[/tpl]
