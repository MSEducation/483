[[Gourmet Burger Kitchen]]

CATEGORIES: Restaurants in London, Hamburger restaurants, New Zealand cuisine

Gourmet Burger Kitchen (GBK) is a restaurant business in the United Kingdom and Ireland specialising in gourmet burgers from a fully traceable West Country herd. 
The company was established by owner, Peter Gordon in 2001 when the first restaurant opened in Battersea, London. Since 2001 the company has spread around the United Kingdom to a current store count of approximately 50 restaurants. They have a New Zealand theme in their store design and food and the most recently opened store was in Bath, England.
