[[Mohamed Younis]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Egyptian footballers, Association football forwards, 1979 births

Mohamed Younis (Arabic: محمد يونس; 30 October 1979) is an Egyptian footballer. He currently plays as a defender for the Egyptian Premier League club Al-Ittihad Al-Sakndary.[tpl]cite web|author=Maher, Hatem|publisher=FilBalad|title=Zaki's wonder strike gives Zamalek Cup title|url=http://www.filgoal.com/english/News.asp?NewsID=43215|date=2008-05-25|accessdate=2009-01-30[/tpl] [tpl]Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot[/tpl]
After spending 5 years in ENPPI and becoming the team's captain, Younis left the club in 2007 and went to El-Masria Lalettesalat.
Mohamed joined Zamalek from El-Masria Lalettesalat in July 2010 as a free agent.
After failing to make an impression with Zamalek, Younis was sold to Petrojet during the 2011 Summer transfers.
