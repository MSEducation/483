[[General Roberto Fierro Villalobos International Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in Mexico

General Roberto Fierro Villalobos International Airport [tpl]Airport codes|CUU|MMCU[/tpl] is an international airport located in Chihuahua, Chihuahua, Mexico. It handles national and international air traffic of the city of Chihuahua. In 2012, Chihuahua Airport handled 855,129 passengers, and in 2013 it handled 885,659 passengers.
