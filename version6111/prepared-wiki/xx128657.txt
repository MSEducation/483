[[Silvies River]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of Oregon, Landforms of Harney County, Oregon, Landforms of Grant County, Oregon

The Silvies River flows for about [tpl]convert|95|mi|km[/tpl] through Grant and Harney counties in the U.S. state of Oregon. The river drains [tpl]convert|1273|mi2|km2[/tpl] of the northern Harney Basin.[tpl]cite web|title = Silvies River and Tributaries |publisher = U.S. Army Corps of Engineers|url = http://www.nww.usace.army.mil/dpn/images/silvies-map1.htm|year = 2005| format = map| accessdate = October 24, 2010[/tpl]
The headwaters are on the southern flank of the Aldrich Mountains, about [tpl]convert|10|mi[/tpl] south of Mount Vernon in Grant County. Named tributaries include Bear Creek and Emigrant Creek.  The Silvies runs generally southward and passes near Seneca and Burns.  Southeast of Burns, in Harney County, the river splits into two distributaries, the East Fork Silvies River and the West Fork Silvies River. Both terminate at Malheur Lake about [tpl]convert|25|mi[/tpl] southeast of Burns.[tpl]cite map|title = Oregon Atlas & Gazetteer | publisher = DeLorme| year = 2008|section = 77–78; 81–82|isbn= 978-0-89933-347-2[/tpl]
