[[Micronesian presidential election, 2011]]

CATEGORIES: Politics of the Federated States of Micronesia, 2011 elections in Oceania

Indirect presidential elections were held in the Federated States of Micronesia on 11 May 2011, following the parliamentary election on 8 March 2011.http://www.saipantribune.com/newsstory.aspx?cat=1&newsID=109312 Incumbent president Manny Mori was reelected without opposition.http://rulers.org/2011-05.html
