[[Ozzie Cobb Lake]]

CATEGORIES: Lakes of Oklahoma, Protected areas of Pushmataha County, Oklahoma

Ozzie Cobb Lake is a small recreational lake in Pushmataha County, Oklahoma.  It is located [tpl]convert|6|mi|km[/tpl] northeast of Rattan, Oklahoma.
The lake, which was built in 1958, impounds the waters of Rock Creek.  It is named for J. Ozzie Cobb (1898-1965), an area resident.  It is managed by the Oklahoma Department of Wildlife Conservation.
Ozzie Cobb Lake comprises [tpl]convert|116|acre|km2[/tpl] of area and four miles (6 km) of shoreline.  Normal pool elevation is [tpl]convert|513|ft|m[/tpl] above sea level.  Its normal holding capacity is [tpl]convert|833|acre.ft|m3|lk=in[/tpl]. The maximum depth is 22 ft. The average depth is 7.2 ft. Information on Ozzie Cobb is obtained from Pushmataha County Cemeteries.  Information on the lake is obtained from lake’s entry in the Oklahoma Water Atlas, available online from the Oklahoma Water Resources Board.
