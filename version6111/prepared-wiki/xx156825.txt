[[Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Dakar]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Senegal, Dakar

The Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Dakar ([tpl]lang-la|Dakaren(sis)[/tpl]) is the Metropolitan See for the Ecclesiastical province of Dakar in Senegal.

==History==

==Special churches==

The seat of the archbishop is Cathédrale Notre Dame des Victoires in Dakar.  There is a Minor Basilica at  Basilique Notre-Dame de la Délivrance in Poponguine.
