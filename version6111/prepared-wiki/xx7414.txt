[[Miocene]]

CATEGORIES: Miocene

The Miocene [tpl]IPAc-en|ˈ|m|aɪ|ə|s|iː|n[/tpl] (symbol MI[tpl]cite web |url=http://pubs.usgs.gov/of/1999/of99-430/of99-430_sec38.pdf |accessdate=2011-06-22 |title=Geologic Age Symbol Font (StratagemAge) |publisher=USGS |id=99-430[/tpl]) is the first geological epoch of the Neogene period and extends from about [tpl]mya|23.03|5.332[/tpl] (Ma). The Miocene was named by Sir Charles Lyell. Its name comes from the Greek words [tpl]lang|grc|μείων[/tpl] ([tpl]lang|grc-Latn|meiōn[/tpl], “less”) and [tpl]lang|grc|καινός[/tpl] ([tpl]lang|grc-Latn|kainos[/tpl], “new”)[ref]
