[[Lindheimera]]

CATEGORIES: Asteraceae genera

Lindheimera is a genus of flowering plants in the daisy family.

==Description==

Lindheimera is a plant that is [tpl]convert|6|to|24|in|cm[/tpl] tall. Leaves on the lower half on the plant are “alternate and coarsely toothed”, but on the upper half are “opposite and smooth on the edges.”("NPIN: Lindheimera texana (Texas yellowstar)." Lady Bird Johnson Wildflower Center - The University of Texas at Austin. N.p., n.d. Web. 10 Mar. 2010. .) “Each flower head has five bright yellow ray flowers, each with two prominent veins and indented at the tip”.("NPIN: Lindheimera texana (Texas yellowstar)." Lady Bird Johnson Wildflower Center - The University of Texas at Austin. N.p., n.d. Web. 10 Mar. 2010. .) The flowers will be at least [tpl]convert|1|-|1.25|in|cm[/tpl] in diameter. The flowers will grow within [tpl]convert|2|in|cm[/tpl] of each other.
