[[St. Karen's High School]]

CATEGORIES: Schools in Patna

St. Karen's High School is a private Christian high school located in Patna, Bihar, India. It was established by Donald Patrick Galstaun and his wife Lesley Barbara Galstaun (Robert n√©e Webb) in 1965.
The school is located at Gola Road, Danapur, Patna (0.3km north-east from Gyan Niketan). It is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education. Previously it was affiliated to the Indian Certificate of Secondary Education. The current principal is Mrs. Seema Singh.
The patron saint of the school is St. Katherine of Siena.
St. Karen's Secondary School is also a private Christian high school located in Patna, Bihar, India was established in 1990 with Phillip Vincente D'Souza as the first principal. P.V. D'Souza held his position until December 2001. Then Llewellyn Galstaun, son of Donald Patrick Galstaun, took the charge. 
The school is located on the Khagaul Road, Danapur, Patna (1.3 km south from Saguna More, Danapur). The current principal is K K Kuriyakose.
