Serhiy Viktorovich Kovalenko ([tpl]lang-uk|Сергій Вікторович Коваленко[/tpl], born 10 May 1984) is a Ukrainian footballer who is currently playing for Naftan Novopolotsk.

==Biography==

Kovalenko started his career at Juventus youth team. He was offered a contract after a trial along with Ilyos Zeytulayev and Viktor Budyanskiy. In February 2002, he joined Juve permanently. The Turin club paid US$400,000 (about €454,000) to buy Kovalenko and Zeytulayev's image and registration rights from Sportakademklub Moscow.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.juventus.com/site/filesite/finance/bilanci_relazioni/22_apr_2002_eng_.pdf|title=Six-Monthly Report at 31 December 2001|date=2002-04-22|accessdate=2010-02-17|work=Juventus FC[/tpl] In January 2004, he was loaned to Lodigiani before joining Standard Liège in August 2004.[tpl]cite news|url=http://en.archive.uefa.com/competitions/uefacup/news/kind=1/newsid=220986.html|title=Runje commits future to Standard|date=2004-08-31|accessdate=2010-06-23|publisher=UEFA.com[/tpl] Juve received €5,000 for the player.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.juventus.com/site/filesite/finance/bilanci_relazioni/25_ott_2005_eng.pdf|title=Reports and Financial Statements at 30 June 2005|date=2005-10-25|accessdate=2010-02-18|work=Juventus FC[/tpl]
He then joined Lokeren in January 2007[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.sporting.be/content/html/newsd.asp?id=3779|title=Kovalenko tot 30 juni bij Sporting |date=2007-01-25|accessdate=2010-02-18|publisher=Sporting Lokeren|language=Dutch[/tpl] and in August 2007 signed a 2-year contract with Roeselare.[tpl]cite news|url=http://archief.ksvroeselare.be/a-kern/default.php?pagina=transfers07-08|title=Transfers voor het seizoen 2007-2008|accessdate=2010-02-18|publisher=KSV Roeselare|language=Dutch[/tpl] In March 2008 he terminated his contract with Roeselare.[tpl]cite news|url=http://archief.ksvroeselare.be/start/default.php?pagina=0803/nieuwtjes0803|title=Een pak zondagsnieuws|date=2008-03-16|accessdate=2010-02-18|publisher=KSV Roeselare|language=Dutch[/tpl]
Kovalenko returned to Ukraine for FC Volyn Lutsk on 12 August 2008.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fc.volyn.net/volin-pdpisala-vikhovancja-juventusavwf.html |title=‘Волинь’ підписала вихованця ‘Ювентуса’ |publisher=FC Volyn Official site |accessdate=2010-02-18|date=2008-08-12|language=Ukrainian[/tpl]
In March 2009, he was signed by Torpedo Zhodino of Belarus.[tpl]cite news|url=http://tarpeda.zhodzina.info/index.php?mod=news&id=151|title=Коваленко С.В.:"Очень хочется обыграть Муслина".|publisher=FC Torpedo Zhodino|date=2009-03-09|accessdate=2010-02-18|language=Russian[/tpl][tpl]cite news|url=http://tarpeda.zhodzina.info/index.php?mod=news&id=149|title=В Жодино прибавление|publisher=FC Torpedo Zhodino|date=2009-03-09|accessdate=2010-02-18|language=Russian[/tpl]
