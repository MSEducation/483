[[Ilisha africana]]

CATEGORIES: Pristigasteridae, Edible fish, Commercial fish, Fish of the Atlantic Ocean

Ilisha africana, called the West African ilisha, is a species of longfin herring native to the coasts, lagoons and estuaries of western Africa from Senegal to Angola. It prefers shallow waters, and is unlikely to be found below 25 m. Some individuals can reach 30 cm, with the average closer to 18 cm. 
The species is commercially fished, with 32,815 t landed in 2008.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.fao.org/fishery/species/2112/en |title= Ilisha africana |author= |date= |work=Fisheries Global Information System |publisher=Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations |accessdate=31 December 2010[/tpl]
