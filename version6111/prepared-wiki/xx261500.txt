[[Ian Capon]]

CATEGORIES: 1977 births, Living people, People from Haverfordwest, People from Pembrokeshire, Welsh cricketers, Wales Minor Counties cricketers

Ian James Capon (born 23 January 1977) is a Welsh cricketer.  Capon is a right-handed batsman who bowls right-arm fast-medium.  He was born at Haverfordwest, Pembrokeshire.
Capon made his Minor Counties Championship debut for Wales Minor Counties in 2003 against Cornwall.
