[[Osen Church]]

CATEGORIES: Osen, Churches in Sør-Trøndelag

Osen church ([tpl]lang-no|Osen kirke[/tpl]) is located in the village of Osen in the municipality of Osen in Sør-Trøndelag county, Norway.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.yr.no/place/Norway/S%C3%B8r-Tr%C3%B8ndelag/Osen/Osen~529908/|title=Osen|publisher=yr.no|language=Norwegian|accessdate=2010-12-31[/tpl]  The church was built in 1877 and can hold 350 people.  It is part of the Church of Norway in the Fosen deanery in the Diocese of Nidaros.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.osen.kommune.no/file.axd?fileID=9646|title=Osen Kirke|format=PDF|publisher=Osen kommune|accessdate=2010-12-31[/tpl]
