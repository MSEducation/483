[[Geo TV]]

CATEGORIES: Pakistani television networks, Television stations in Pakistan, Geo TV, Television channels and stations established in 2002, 2002 establishments in Pakistan, A&B Entertainment

Geo TV (trademarked GEO TV) or Geo Television is a Pakistani television channel established in May 2002 and owned by Independent Media Corporation. The channel began test transmission on 14 August 2002, with regular transmission beginning 1 October 2002.Geo News Geo is an Urdu word meaning live on.

Geo Dost (meaning Geo Friends) is a Geo TV portal that offers ordinary citizens the chance to report the news, whether it be funny or serious.

==Technical==

Geo TV's broadcast facilities are based at Dubai Media City in the United Arab Emirates. Its uplink teleport station is Samacom, the uplink provider in the UAE. Geo TV is currently being aired on Asiasat 3s.

==Hacking==

On 16 Feb 2003, Geo TV's website was hacked by 'Z Hacking Crew' (ZHC), a hacktivist group that has defaced large scale Indian websites in the past. ZHC protested against a lack of coverage of the week long curfew imposed by Indian authorities in Kashmir and what they saw as India-oriented programming.http://www.saach.tv/2013/02/16/geo-gets-hacked-for-ignoring-kashmir
