[[Party of Industrialists and Entrepreneurs of Ukraine]]

CATEGORIES: Political parties in Ukraine

The Party of Industrialists and Entrepreneurs of Ukraine ([tpl]lang-uk|Партія промисловців i підприємців України[/tpl]) is a political party in Ukraine registered in April 2000. Former Ukrainian Prime Minister Anatoliy Kinakh is one of its most noticeable members.[tpl]uk icon[/tpl] Позиція ПППУ, Official party website (March 2, 2010)

==History==

At the parliamentary elections on 30 March 2002, the party was part of the For United Ukraine alliance.[tpl]uk icon[/tpl] Партія промисловців і підприємців України, Database DATA Party leader Anatoliy Kinakh was First Vice Prime Minister in the first Tymoshenko GovernmentFirst Vice Premier Anatoli Kinakh introduces Yuri Artemenko, newly appointed Zaporizhia Governor, to regional administration staffers , Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine (February 12, 2005)PRESIDENT YUSHCHENKO APPOINTS THE NEW PRIME MINISTER AND MEMBERS OF HER CABINET, European Parliament (February 4, 2005)
At the parliamentary elections on 26 March 2006, the party took part in the new Our Ukraine bloc.
At the early parliamentary elections parliamentary  on September 30, 2007, party-members participated in the elections on the Party of Regions election-list.[tpl]uk icon[/tpl] Історія ПППУ, Official party website
In the 2010 local elections the party won 1 representatives in 3 regional parliaments and 6 in the Lviv Oblast.[tpl]uk icon[/tpl] Results of the elections, preliminary data, on interactive maps by Ukrayinska Pravda (November 8, 2010)
The party did not participate in the 2012 parliamentary elections.[tpl]uk icon[/tpl] Results of voting in single constituencies in 2012 & Nation-wide list, Central Election Commission of Ukraine
