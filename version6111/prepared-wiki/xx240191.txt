[[Deer Park Mosque]]

CATEGORIES: Mosques in Australia, Buildings and structures in Melbourne, Places of worship in Melbourne

The Deer Park Mosque, an Islamic mosque supervised by the Australian Bosnian Islamic Centre, is located in the Melbourne north-western suburb of [tpl]VICcity|Albanvale[/tpl] (adjacent to the suburb of Deer Park), in Victoria, Australia.
In 1961 the first Bosnian muslims gathered and established a multi-ethnic Islamic community in [tpl]VICcity|Carlton[/tpl], [tpl]VICcity|Footscray[/tpl] and Deer Park. The Deer Park Mosque is among one of the largest mosques in Australia.
