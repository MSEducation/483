[[Sculptor Wall]]

CATEGORIES: Large-scale structure of the cosmos, Galaxy filaments

The Sculptor Wall is a superstructure of galaxies ("wall of galaxies") relatively near to the Milky Way Galaxy (redshift of approximately z=0.03), also known as the Sculptor superclusters.
