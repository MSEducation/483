[[Hot Dixie Chick]]

CATEGORIES: 2007 racehorse births, Thoroughbred racehorses, Northern Dancer bloodline, Racehorses bred in Kentucky, Racehorses trained in the United States, American racehorses

Hot Dixie Chick (foaled February, 2007 in Kentucky) is an American Thoroughbred racehorse.
Out of the mare Above Perfection, a descendant of the very influential sire, Caro, Hot Dixie Chick was sired by Dixie Union, a son of the stakes winner, Dixieland Band who is a son of the most successful sire of the 20th Century, Northern Dancer.Hot Dixie Chick's pedigree

==2009 racing season==

Trained by Steve Asmussen, at age two Hot Dixie Chick won her first three starts. A frontrunner, after winning her debut in a sprint race at Saratoga Race Course, Hot Dixie Chick won the 6-furlong Grade III Schuylerville Stakes at Saratoga.ESPN - July 29, 2009 In her next outing, she won the Grade I 7-furlongs Spinaway Stakes in stakes-record time of 1:22.28.The Saratogian -0 September 7, 2009
