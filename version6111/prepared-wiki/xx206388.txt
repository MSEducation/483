[[Eliot Congregational Church]]

CATEGORIES: Churches in Boston, Massachusetts, United Church of Christ churches in Massachusetts, Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in Massachusetts, Stone churches in Massachusetts

Eliot Congregational Church ("Walnut Avenue Congregational Church" or "Eliot United Church of Christ") is an historic Congregational church at 56 Dale Street, at the corner of 118-120 Walnut Street in Boston, Massachusetts.
The church building was built in 1873 and added to the National Historic Register in 1994.
