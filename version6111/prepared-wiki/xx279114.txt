[[Qezeljeh-ye Sofla, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Qezeljeh-ye Sofla ([tpl]lang-fa|قزلجه سفلي[/tpl], also Romanized as Qezeljeh-ye Soflá)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795920|Qezeljeh-ye Sofla[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 82, in 18 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
