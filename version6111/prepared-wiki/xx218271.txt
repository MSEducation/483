[[Dəmirçi, Nakhchivan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Sharur District

Dəmirçi (also, Damirchi and Demirchi) is a village and the most populous municipality, except for the capital Şərur, in the Sharur District of Nakhchivan Autonomous Republic, Azerbaijan.  It has a population of 4,980.
