==Rolling stock==

As Mongolia's railroads are not electrified, UBTZ relies entirely on Diesel traction. Most common locomotives are M62 variants, including five rebuilt 2Zagal ([tpl]lang-mn|two white horses[/tpl]) double engines.2Zagal-006 and -007 are rebuilt TE10s. Other engines include TEM2 and TE116 variants, DASH-7 and one Evolution locomotive on lease from GE.GE press release In October 2010, Ulaanbaatar Railway ordered 35 2TE116UM diesel freight locomotives from Transmash.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.railwaygazette.com/nc/news/single-view/view/ulaanbaatar-railway-orders-transmash-locomotives.html|accessdate=2010-11-01|title=Railway Gazette: Ulaanbaatar Railway orders Transmash locomotives[/tpl]
