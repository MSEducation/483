[[Henashi Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Aomori Prefecture, Gonō Line, 1936 establishments in Japan

[tpl]nihongo| Henashi Station|艫作駅|Henashi-eki[/tpl] is a railway station on the JR East Gonō Line located in the town of Fukaura, Aomori Prefecture, Japan. It is 57.9 rail kilometers from the southern terminus of the Gonō Line at Higashi-Noshiro Station.

==History==

Henashi Station was opened on July 30, 1936 as a station on the Japanese Government Railways (JGR). With the privatization of the Japanese National Railways (successor of JGR) on April 1, 1987, it came under the operational control of JR East.

==Lines==

==Station layout==

Henshi Station was built with double opposed side platforms, but only  a single side platform serving bidirectional traffic is currently in use. The station is unattended, and is managed from Fukaura Station.
