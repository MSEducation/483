[[Gmina Czarnia]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Ostrołęka County

Gmina Czarnia is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Ostrołęka County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Czarnia, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|40|km|mi|0[/tpl] north-west of Ostrołęka and [tpl]convert|127|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|92.53|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 2,620.

==Villages==

Gmina Czarnia contains the villages and settlements of Bandysie, Brzozowy Kąt, Cupel, Cyk, Czarnia, Długie, Michałowo, Rutkowo and Surowe.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Czarnia is bordered by the gminas of Baranowo, Chorzele, Myszyniec, Rozogi and Wielbark.
