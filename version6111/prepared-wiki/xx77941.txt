[[Berat District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Albania, Berat County, Berat District

The District of Berat ([tpl]Lang-sq|Rrethi i Beratit[/tpl]) is one of the thirty-six districts of Albania, part of Berat County. It has a population of 117,066[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.instat.gov.al/graphics/doc/tabelat/Treguesit%20Sociale/Popullsia/POP%202009/t2.xls|title=POPULLSIA SIPAS RRETHEVE, 2001-2010|publisher=Albanian Institute of Statistics|accessdate=2010-09-09[/tpl] (2010 estimate), and an area of 939 km². It is in the centre of the country, and its capital is Berat. An other large population centre in this district is Ura Vajgurore. This is also the birthplace of Sejfi Protopapa, a Balli Kombëtar leader.
