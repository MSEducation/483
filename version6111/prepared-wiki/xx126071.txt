[[Viking Voyage]]

CATEGORIES: Roller coasters in Georgia (U.S. state), Roller coasters introduced in 2010, Wild Adventures, Roller coasters operated by Herschend Family Entertainment

Viking Voyage is a steel roller coaster located at Wild Adventures in Valdosta, Georgia. It opened on March 5, 2010. The ride was previously located at Celebration City where it was known as Jack Rabbit. It operated at that location from 2003 until October 25, 2008. 
It is a E&F Miler Industries/Hi-Miler/52ft.
Length:,1300ft.
Hight:52ft.
Inversions:0
Trains:3 cars per train. Riders are aranged 2 across in a single row for a total of 6 riders per train.
