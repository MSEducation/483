[[Neville Broderick]]

CATEGORIES: 1927 births, Collingwood Football Club players, Fitzroy Football Club players, Fitzroy Best and Fairest winners, Living people

Neville Broderick (born 10 April 1927) was an Australian rules footballer who played with Collingwood and Fitzroy in the VFL.
Broderick was a defender and spent three seasons at Collingwood. He crossed to Fitzroy in 1952 and won their Best and fairest in his debut season with the club.
