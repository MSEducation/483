[[United Africa Tigers]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Namibia, Sport in Windhoek, 1942 establishments in South-West Africa

The United Africa Tigers are a Namibian football club. They are based in the Namibian capital Windhoek, and play in the country's top league, the Namibia Premier League. In the 1990's, they were sponsored by Mukorob Pelagic and referred to as the MP Tigers.
