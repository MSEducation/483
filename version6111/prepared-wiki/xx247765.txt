[[What If Someone Is Watching Their T.V.?]]

CATEGORIES: Screaming Females albums, Don Giovanni Records albums, 2007 albums

What If Someone Is Watching Their T.V.? is the second full-length album by the Screaming Females. Originally self released in 2007, it was re-released on Don Giovanni Records in 2009 and later re-released again on October 4, 2011, this time on vinyl.[tpl]cite web | url=http://consequenceofsound.net/2011/08/screaming-females-reissue-first-two-lps-tour-this-fall/ | title=Screaming Females reissue first two LPs, tour this fall | work=Consequence of Sound | date=3 August 2011 | accessdate=16 March 2014 | author=Coplan, Chris[/tpl]

==Track listing==

All songs written by Screaming Females (Jarrett Dougherty, King Mike, Marissa Paternoster).
