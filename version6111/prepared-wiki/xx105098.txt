[[Ukrainian Peasant Democratic Party]]

CATEGORIES: Agrarian parties in Ukraine, Political parties established in 1991, Political parties disestablished in 2011, Defunct political parties in Ukraine

The Ukrainian Peasant Democratic Party ([tpl]lang-uk|Українська селянська демократична партія[/tpl]) is a former political party in Ukraine. It was registered with the Ministry of Justice on 15 January 1991. The party merged into the (then) new party United Left and Peasants in December 2011.[tpl]uk icon[/tpl] Ніколаєнко очолив "об'єднаних лівих", Ukrayinska Pravda (18 December  2011)

==History[tpl]uk icon[/tpl] Українська селянська демократична партія, DATAAbout the Party, Party official website==

The party ran for the parliamentary election 1998 as the part of “European Choice of Ukraine” block winning 0,13% of the votes and no seats. At the parliamentary elections on 30 March 2002, the party was part of the Team of Winter Generation alliance,Electoral list of candidates for deputy from Election bloc of Political Parties "Team of Winter Generation", Central Election Commission of Ukraine which won 2.0% of the popular vote and no seats. At the parliamentary elections on 26 March 2006 the party was part of the electoral Lytvyn's People's Bloc, which won 2.44% of the popular vote and no seats.
In the 30 September 2007 elections, the party was a part of the Peasants' Bloc "Agrarian Ukraine" which again failed to win parliamentary representation.http://www.useldp.org/index.php?page=page&article_id=9, Official website
