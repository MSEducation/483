[[Darlin' I]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 singles, Vanessa L. Williams songs, Rhythm and blues ballads, 1988 songs, Songs written by Rex Salas, Wing Records singles

Darlin' I is a song written by Kenny Harris and Salas. In 1987, Vanessa Williams recorded the song and released it as a single from her debut album, The Right Stuff.

==Music video==

The music video for the song is filmed in black and white based on the I Love Lucy show.
