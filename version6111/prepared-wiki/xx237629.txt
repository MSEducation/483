[[Johnny Love (footballer)]]

CATEGORIES: 1937 births, English footballers, Association football defenders, Telford United F.C. players, Oxford United F.C. players, The Football League players, 2010 deaths, Place of death missing

Johnny Love (born 11 March 1937) is a former English footballer who played for Oxford United and Wellington Town. Love represented England twice at youth level. During his spell at Oxford, he played 279 league games. Between 1955 and 1957, Love was called up to the National Service in the West Indies. Love played on the left-wing during his years at Headington United and Oxford United.[ref]
