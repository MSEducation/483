[[Tomomi Itano]]

CATEGORIES: 1991 births, Living people, Japanese idols, Japanese female singers, Japanese child actresses, Japanese female pop singers, AKB48 members, King Records (Japan) artists, Musicians from Kanagawa Prefecture

[tpl]Nihongo|Tomomi Itano|板野 友美|Itano Tomomi|born July 3, 1991[/tpl] is a former member of the Japanese idol girl group AKB48. Her talent agent is Horipro. She was the first member of AKB48 to get a solo debut . Her solo debut is Dear J follow by Fui ni , 10nen go no kimi e and 1% . After graduating from AKB48 , her single debut "little" attracted a lot of fans to respective venues for her solo concerts.

==Career==

In 2005, Itano joined the Japanese idol girl group AKB48[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.oricon.co.jp/prof/artist/444095/profile/history/ | title = 板野友美のプロフィール・ヒストリーならオリコン芸能人事典-ORICON STYLE | language = Japanese  [/tpl] as part of the first team, Team A. She also modeled for the women's fashion magazine Cawaii!, and released her first photo book titled T.O.M.O.row in April 2009.[tpl]cite web | url = http://ascii.jp/elem/000/000/407/407693/| title =  AKB48メンバーでCawaii!専属モデルの板野友美、編集会議まで開いた写真集を発売| language = Japanese[/tpl] In early 2010, she played Shibuya in AKB48's TV Tokyo drama Majisuka Gakuen.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.tv-tokyo.co.jp/majisuka/index.html|title = Majisuka | language = Japanese[/tpl][tpl]better source|date=August 2013|reason=existing source is just a ref to the general site, does not point to itano[/tpl] She had a recurring role on Kamen Rider W as Queen, alongside fellow AKB48 performer Tomomi Kasai, until the show ended in late 2010. Together, they made up the sub-unit Queen & Elizabeth.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.oricon.co.jp/news/deview/72690/ | title = 最強アイドルと最強ヒーローが合体！　AKB48板野友美＆河西智美と仮面ライダーＷがコラボシングルリリース | language = Japanese [/tpl] In AKB48's 2010 general election, she placed fourth overall.
Itano released her first single, "Dear J", on January 26, 2011. It reached number 1 on the Oricon daily ranking and number 2 on the Oricon weekly ranking, selling 204,981 copies. In April, she reprised her role as Shibuya in Majisuka Gakuen 2.[tpl]cite web|author=現在の時刻 |language = Japanese | url=http://www.tv-tokyo.co.jp/majisuka2/cast/index.html |title=ドラマ24「マジすか学園２」：テレビ東京 公式サイト |publisher=Tv-tokyo.co.jp |date= |accessdate=2013-08-25[/tpl] Her first digital single "Wanna Be Now" reached number 2 on Recochoku's daily charts and number 6 on the weekly charts.[tpl]cite web|url=http://generasia.com/wiki/RIAJ_Digital_Track_Weekly_for_2011_week_20 |title=RIAJ Digital Track Weekly for 2011 week 20 |publisher=generasia |date= |accessdate=2013-08-25[/tpl][tpl]better source|date=January 2014|reason=generasia is a wiki-like source, need better one, also need oricon chart[/tpl] She placed eighth overall in AKB48's 2011 general election held in June. Her second single, "Fui ni", was released on July 13 and sold 90,103 copies, and reached number one on the Oricon weekly chart.
In 2012, Itano was cited as the "queen of TV commercials" for having more contracts with companies than any other female tarento (although her count of 20 was technically a tie with those of fellow AKB48 member Mariko Shinoda).[tpl]cite web|title=今年のＣＭ女王は、麻里子さま＆ともちん ともに２０社ずつ|url=http://sankei.jp.msn.com/entertainments/news/121212/ent12121207570003-n1.htm|work=Sankei Nyūsu|accessdate=14 June 2013|language=Japanese[/tpl]
