[[Alfred Ayer House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Florida, National Register of Historic Places in Marion County, Florida, Houses in Marion County, Florida

The Alfred Ayer House (also known as the James Eagleton House) is a historic building located on US Alternate 27/441 west of Oklawaha, Florida. On July 13, 1993, it was added to the U.S. National Register of Historic Places.
