[[2011 Internationaux de Nouvelle-Calédonie]]

CATEGORIES: 2011 ATP Challenger Tour, 2011 in French sport, Internationaux de Nouvelle-Calédonie

The 2011 Internationaux de Nouvelle-Calédonie was a professional tennis tournament played on hard courts. It was the seventh edition of the tournament which is part of the 2011 ATP Challenger Tour. It took place in Nouméa, New Caledonia between 3 and 9 January 2011.
