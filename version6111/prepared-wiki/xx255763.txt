[[Lesično]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Municipality of Kozje

Lesično is a village in the Municipality of Kozje in eastern Slovenia. The area is part of the historical Lower Styria region. The municipality is included in the Savinja statistical region.Kozje municipal site
The local church is dedicated to Saint Ulrich ([tpl]lang-sl|sveti Urh[/tpl]) and belongs to the Parish of Pilštanj. It was first mentioned in written documents dating to 1394.Slovenian Ministry of Culture register of national heritage reference number ešd 3243
