[[Botafogo de Futebol e Regatas (basketball)]]

CATEGORIES: Basketball teams in Brazil

Botafogo de Futebol e Regatas, or Botafogo F.R., is a Brazilian men's professional basketball club that is based in Botafogo, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.

==History==

Botafogo won the Brazilian League championship in 1967. They also competed at the 1968 edition of the Intercontinental Cup.
