[[Amadeo Ortega]]

CATEGORIES: Paraguayan footballers, Paraguay international footballers, Association football forwards, 1930 FIFA World Cup players, 1983 deaths, Year of birth missing

Amadeo Ortega (date of birth unknown, died 17 October 1983) was a Paraguayan football forward who played for Paraguay in the 1930 FIFA World Cup.1930 FIFA World Cup Uruguay He also played for Club River Plate.
