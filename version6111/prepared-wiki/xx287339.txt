[[The Pretty One]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 films, Films shot in Los Angeles, California, Directorial debut films, 2010s comedy-drama films, American comedy-drama films

The Pretty One is a 2013 comedy drama film directed and written by Jenée LaMarque. The film stars Zoe Kazan, Jake Johnson, Ron Livingston, Sterling Beaumon and John Carroll Lynch.[tpl]cite news|title=The Pretty One: Tribeca Review|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/review/pretty-one-tribeca-review-444000|accessdate=13 August 2013|newspaper=hollywoodreporter.com|date=22 April 2013[/tpl]

==Cast==

==Production==

On March 30, 2012, Zoe Kazan and Jake Johnson joined the cast of The Pretty One to play lead roles, Jenée LaMarque has written the script and she will make her directorial debut of her own 2011 black listed script. The filming started on June 1, 2012.[tpl]cite news|title=Zoe Kazan, Jake Johnson Star In ‘The Pretty One’|url=http://www.deadline.com/2012/03/zoe-kazan-jake-johnson-star-in-the-pretty-one/|accessdate=13 August 2013|newspaper=deadline.com|date=30 March 2012[/tpl] Sterling Beaumon also joined the cast in the middle of June to play as Hunter, a womanizer who is sleeping with his former babysitter.[tpl]cite news|title=Sterling Beaumon has joined the indie film The Pretty One|url=http://www.deadline.com/2012/06/movie-castings-roundup-lost%E2%80%99s-pellegrino-in-cate-mccall-actors-join-pretty-one-interns-cbgb/|accessdate=13 August 2013|newspaper=deadline.com|date=21 June 2012[/tpl] Later on 22 June, Ron Livingston joined the cast to play Charles, Robin  Schorr and Steven Berger are producing the film.[tpl]cite news|title=Ron Livingston Joins ‘The Pretty One’|url=http://www.deadline.com/2012/06/ron-livingston-joins-the-pretty-one/|accessdate=13 August 2013|newspaper=deadline.com|date=22 June 2012[/tpl]

===Filming===

The filming began in early June 2012 in Los Angeles.
