[[Ava Muhammad]]

In 1975 she received her Juris Doctorate from Georgetown University Law Center. She later became a member of the New York Bar Association.
She married Darius Muhammad in 1988; she is a lawyer, minister, and also the National Spokesperson for the Minister Louis Farrakhan and the Nation of Islam. She is also a researcher and author of several books. She also hosts a weekly radio talk show titled "Elevated Places" which airs every Sunday on WVON 1690AM in Chicago.
