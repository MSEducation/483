[[Battle of Şarköy]]

CATEGORIES: Battles of the First Balkan War, Conflicts in 1913, Battles involving Bulgaria, Battles involving the Ottoman Empire, History of Tekirdağ Province, 1913 in the Ottoman Empire

The Battle of Şarköy or Sarkoy operation ([tpl]lang-bg|Битка при Шаркьой[/tpl], [tpl]lang-tr|Şarköy Çıkarması[/tpl]) took place between 9 and 11 February 1913 during the First Balkan War between Bulgaria and the Ottoman Empire. The Ottomans attempted a counter-attack, but were defeated by the Bulgarians at the Battles of Bulair and Şarköy.
