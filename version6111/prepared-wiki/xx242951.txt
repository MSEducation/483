[[Slatinski Dol]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Municipality of Kungota

Slatinski Dol is a dispersed settlement in the western Slovene Hills ([tpl]lang-sl|Slovenske gorice[/tpl]) north and west of Zgornja Kungota in the Municipality of Kungota in northeastern Slovenia.Kungota municipal site
