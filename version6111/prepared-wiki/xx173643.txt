[[Fiona Dourif]]

CATEGORIES: 1981 births, Actresses from New York, American film actresses, American people of French descent, American television actresses, Living people, People from Ulster County, New York

Fiona Dourif (born October 30, 1981) is an American film and television actress, and the daughter of actor Brad Dourif.

==Early life==

Fiona Dourif was born in Woodstock, New York. Her father is actor Brad Dourif, and her mother is Jonina "Joni" Dourif. She has one sister, Kristina Dourif, born in 1976.[tpl]fact|date=October 2013[/tpl]
