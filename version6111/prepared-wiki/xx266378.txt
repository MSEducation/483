[[Swarna Trishna]]

CATEGORIES: 1990 films, Bengali-language films, Indian films, Indian action films

Swarna Trisha is a 1990 Bengali-language Indian feature film directed by Mangal Chakraborty, starring Mithun Chakraborty, Prem Chopra, Anil Chatterjee, Tom Alter and Yogeeta Bali. The film also stars Hiro Kaju Kana Zowa, Hisa-Co-Kate and Sagiri Suzuki.

==Plot==

Swarna Trisha is an action film starring Mithun Chakraborty in the lead role.
