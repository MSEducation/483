[[Maklok, Voronezh Oblast]]

Maklok ([tpl]lang-ru|Маклок[/tpl]) is a rural locality (a settlement) in Shuberskoye Rural Settlement of Novousmansky District of Voronezh Oblast, Russia,Law #87-OZ located in a forest [tpl]convert|20|km|sp=us[/tpl] north from the district's administrative center of Novaya Usman.
