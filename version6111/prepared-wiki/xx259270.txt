[[Peoples National Bank and Trust Company Building]]

CATEGORIES: Bank buildings on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Neoclassical architecture in New York, Office buildings completed in 1929, Buildings and structures in Westchester County, New York

Peoples National Bank and Trust Company Building is a historic bank building located at White Plains, Westchester County, New York. It was built in 1929 and is a nine story commercial building in the Classical Revival style.  It has an "L" shaped plan and features highly finished limestone and brick materials and terra cotta ornament at the street level.  Above the two story base, the building remaining stories are built of brick with limestone trim.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=10857|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration:Peoples National Bank and Trust Company Building|date=August 1999|accessdate=2010-12-30 |author=Neil Larson and Peter D.
