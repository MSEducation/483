[[Jiul de Vest River]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of Romania, Rivers of the Jiu subbasin, Rivers of Hunedoara County

 
The Jiul de Vest River, (previously also known as Jiul Românesc River) is a headwater of the Jiu River in Romania. The upper course of the river is also known locally as Câmpușelu River.
