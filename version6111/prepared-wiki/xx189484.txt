[[Legislative Assembly of the Republic of Karelia]]

CATEGORIES: Politics of the Republic of Karelia, Legislatures of the federal subjects of Russia, Unicameral legislatures, Defunct bicameral legislatures

The Legislative Assembly ([tpl]lang-ru|Законода́тельное Собра́ние Респу́блики Каре́лия[/tpl], Zakonodatelnoye sobraniye Respubliki Kareliya; [tpl]lang-fi|Karjalan tasavallan lakiasäätävä kokous[/tpl]) is the Regional parliament of the Republic of Karelia, Russia. It comprises fifty deputies. Chairman of the Assembly is Alexander Pereplesnin (United Russia). He succeeded Nikolay Ivanovich Levin (United Russia)  [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.karelia-zs.ru/eng-index.html|title=Legislative Assembly of the Republic of Karelia|accessdate=2007-04-28|work=Legislative Assembly of the Republic of Karelia[/tpl]
