[[Chiara Ottaviano]]

CATEGORIES: 1955 births, Italian historians, Historians of Italy, People from Ragusa, Sicily, Living people

Chiara Ottaviano, (Ragusa, 1955) is an Italian historian, writer and film director.

==Biography==

After obtaining a degree in philosophy, at the Università di Catania, thanks to scholarships of Einaudi Foundation of Torino, has done research on social and cultural history in Italy, England and South Africa.
She taught from 1994 at Università di Torino and then History and sociology of mass communication, from 1996 to 2012, at the Politecnico di Torino[tpl]cite web|title=Chiara Ottaviano|url=http://www.italiandoc.it/area/public/wid/TUZE/scheda.htm|publisher=Italiandoc|accessdate=10 August 2013|language=it[/tpl]
With Peppino Ortoleva founded the Cliomedia Officina, a company that operates in the cultural industry in order to combine historical research at the old and new media and directing it since 1985. In addition to several audiovisual history, Cliomedia produced the film documentary Terramatta,[tpl]cite journal|title=Giorno & notte|journal=La Repubblica|date=4 December 2012|url=http://ricerca.repubblica.it/repubblica/archivio/repubblica/2012/12/04/giorno-notte.fi_031.html?ref=search|accessdate=26 July 2013|language=it[/tpl] a story on the writer illiterate Vincenzo Rabito, presented at the 69th Venice International Film Festival and winner of the Nastro d'Argento for 2013 Best documentary.[tpl]cite journal|title=Nastro a Terramatta miglior documentario|journal=La Repubblica|date=8 May 2013|url=http://palermo.repubblica.it/cronaca/2013/05/08/news/nastro_a_terramatta_miglior_documentario-58374779/|accessdate=28 July 2013|language=it[/tpl]
Since 2000, she directed the Historical Archives of Telecom Italia.
In 2013 she founded the Archivio degli Iblei,[tpl]cite web|title=Archivio degli Iblei|url=http://www.archiviodegliiblei.it/index.php?it/1/home|accessdate=10 August 2013|language=it[/tpl][tpl]cite journal|last=Ragusa|first=Raffaele|title=Le fonti e la memoria degli Iblei in un archivio|journal=La Sicilia|date=24 March 2013|url=http://www.progettoterramatta.it/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/La-sicilia-24-marzo-2013.pdf|accessdate=10 August 2013|language=it[/tpl][tpl]cite journal|title=Le memorie di Rabito ispirano la nascita dell'Archivio degli Iblei|journal=Gazzetta del Sud|date=23 March 2013|url=http://www.progettoterramatta.it/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/gazzetta-del-sud-23-marzo-2013.pdf|accessdate=10 August 2013|language=it[/tpl][tpl]cite journal|last=Burderi|first=Marcella|title=Scampoli di storia nella rete|journal=La Sicilia|date=6 August 2013|url=http://giornaleonline.lasicilia.it/page=24|accessdate=10 August 2013|location=Ragusa|language=it[/tpl] along the lines of Archivio Diaristico Nazionale, and the latter associated in order to enable the digitization of diaries to make them available to scholars, historians and anthropologists.
She has written numerous essays on the history, curatorial essays and prefaces to other historians.[tpl]cite web|title=Chiara Ottaviano|url=http://www.istituticulturalidiroma.it/result.php?dove=breve&useq=1&nf=va&vf=CFIV068789&startp=semplice|accessdate=10 August 2013|language=it[/tpl]
