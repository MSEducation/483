[[Chap Chap]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Chap Chap ([tpl]lang-fa|چپ چپ[/tpl], also Romanized as Chepchep and Chop Chop)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3058062|Chap Chap[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 677, in 143 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
