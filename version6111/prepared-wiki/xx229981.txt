Yulong Naxi Autonomous County ([tpl]zh|s=玉龙纳西族自治县|p=Yùlóng Nàxīzú Zìzhìxiàn[/tpl]) is located in Lijiang City Prefecture, Yunnan, China. Wenhai village and lake along with Jade Dragon Snow Mountain are some of the famous scenic spots found here.
