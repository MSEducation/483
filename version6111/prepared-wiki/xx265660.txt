[[Texas State Highway Spur 148]]

CATEGORIES: State highway spurs in Texas, Transportation in Hudspeth County, Texas

State Highway Spur 148 (Spur 148) is a state highway spur route near Fort Hancock in Hudspeth County, Texas.

==Route description==

Spur 148 begins at [tpl]jct|state=TX|SH|20|noshield=yes[/tpl], travels northeastward through Fort Hancock as Knox Ave., and ends at exit 72 of [tpl]jct|state=TX|I|10|noshield=yes[/tpl].[tpl]Texas Mapbook|76|accessdate=2011-09-22[/tpl]

==History==

Spur 148 was assigned in 1944 to the "Fort Hancock Spur," running northward from [tpl]jct|state=TX|US|80|noshield=yes[/tpl] (present-day SH 20) to Fort Hancock. In 1963, Spur 148 was extended northward by 0.4 miles to I-10.
