Nico-Jan Hoogma (born 26 October 1968 in Heerenveen, Netherlands) is a former football defender.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.kicker.de/news/fussball/bundesliga/vereine/1-bundesliga/1999-00/hamburger-sv-12/22641/spieler_nico-jan-hoogma.html | title = Hoogma, Nico Jan | language = German | publisher = kicker.de | accessdate = 12 February 2012[/tpl] He started his career with SC Cambuur and after three years moved to FC Twente. In 1998 he moved to Germany, where he played for Hamburger SV, before seeing out his career with Heracles Almelo, aged 38.
