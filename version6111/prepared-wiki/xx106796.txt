[[Korman Stadium]]

CATEGORIES: Football venues in Vanuatu, Athletics (track and field) venues in Vanuatu, Port Vila

Korman Stadium is a sports stadium located in Port Vila, Vanuatu. It is the national stadium and the home of Vanuatu's national football team. The stadium's capacity is 6,500.http://goal.sportal.com.au/venues/vanuatu/korman-stadium Korman stadium was named for one of Vanuatu's politicians, the head of the Vanuatu Republican Party, Maxime Carlot Korman. Amical FC are tenants of the stadium.
