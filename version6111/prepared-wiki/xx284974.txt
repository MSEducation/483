[[2013 AMJ Campbell Shorty Jenkins Classic]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 in Canadian curling, AMJ Campbell Shorty Jenkins Classic

The 2013 AMJ Campbell Shorty Jenkins Classic was held from September 19 to 22 at the Brockville Country Club in Brockville, Ontario as part of the 2013–14 World Curling Tour. Both the men's and women's events was held in a round robin format. The purse for the men's event was CAD$45,400,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.worldcurl.com/events.php?task=Event&eventid=3048&view=Contact|title=2013 AMJ Campbell Shorty Jenkins Classic – Men's event – Event/Contact information|publisher=World Curling Tour|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] while the purse for the women's event was CAD$18,000.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.worldcurl.com/events.php?task=Event&eventid=3049&view=Contact|title=2013 AMJ Campbell Shorty Jenkins Classic – Women's event – Event/Contact information|publisher=World Curling Tour|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl]
In the men's final, Brad Jacobs of Northern Ontario defeated Jeff Stoughton of Manitoba with a score of 7–5 after stealing a point in the last end of an otherwise tight game. In the women's final, Mirjam Ott of Switzerland defeated Ontario's Rachel Homan with a score of 8–5 in seven ends.
