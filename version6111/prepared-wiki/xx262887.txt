[[B Is for Bob]]

CATEGORIES: Bob Marley and the Wailers compilation albums, 2009 remix albums, 2009 compilation albums, Island Records remix albums, Island Records compilation albums

B is for Bob is a children's music remix album by reggae band Bob Marley & The Wailers, released on June 23, 2009 and is a posthumous album released after Marley's death. The album is a collection of old songs from their previous albums Legend, Rastaman Vibration, and Burnin'.
