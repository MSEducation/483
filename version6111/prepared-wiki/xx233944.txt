 Beecham described her portrayal of Desdemona as "the best I have seen on any stage."<ref name=Steane1992/> Her other roles in London included Olympia in Offenbach's ''[[The Tales of Hoffmann]]'', Zerlina in Mozart's ''[[Don Giovanni]]'', and Marguerite in Gounod's [[Faust (opera)|''Faust'']]. Her last appearance there was in 1922.

Other venues included  the Opéra-Comique in Paris in 1920 (in the title role of Lakmé by Delibes and as Mimì in Puccini's La bohème),
