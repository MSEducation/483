Until broken by Pablo Sandoval in [tpl]by|2012[/tpl], Rucker's 17-game hitting streak to start the [tpl]by|1945[/tpl] campaign was the Giants' franchise record for a hitting streak to begin a season. The skein lasted from April 17 through May 9, and Rucker batted .333 with 28 hits in 84 at bats, with six doubles and two home runs. It was halted on May 11, 1945, by Frank Dasso of the Cincinnati Reds.Retrosheet During his MLB career, Rucker amassed 711 hits, including 105 doubles and 39 triples.
