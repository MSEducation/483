[[Sai Wan]]

CATEGORIES: Sai Wan

Sai Wan ([tpl]Zh|t=西環[/tpl], literally means west ring, west borough or west district), or Western District, or simply Western,  is an area in Hong Kong that corresponds to Sai Ying Pun, Shek Tong Tsui, Belcher Bay and Kennedy Town.
West Point, a former cape where major government structures in Sai Wan are close to, also used to refer to Sai Wan[tpl]Citation needed|date=February 2007[/tpl].
