[[27th Indian Infantry Brigade]]

CATEGORIES: British Indian Army brigades

The 27th Indian Infantry Brigade was an Infantry formation of the Indian Army during World War II. It was formed in March 1941, at Secundarabad in India and assigned to the 6th Indian Infantry Division. 
The brigade was used as Line of Communication troops in Iran and Iraq. [tpl]cite web|accessdate=2009-10-23|publisher=Order of Battle|title=27 Indian Brigade|url=http://www.ordersofbattle.com/UnitData.aspx?UniX=1423&Tab=Sup[/tpl]
