[[Ulster Under-21 Football Championship]]

CATEGORIES: Under 21 inter-county Gaelic football competitions, Ulster inter-county football competitions

The Ulster Under-21 Football Championship, currently known for sponsorship reasons as the Cadbury GAA Football Under-21 Ulster Championship, is a Gaelic football tournament for players under the age of 21, contested by the nine counties of Ulster in Ireland. The winners of the Ulster championship go on to qualify for the All-Ireland Under-21 Football Championship.
The trophy for the winning team is called the Irish News Cup and the current holders are Cavan.
