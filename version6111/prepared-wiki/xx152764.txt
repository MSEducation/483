[[People's Court Daily]]

CATEGORIES: Newspapers published in the People's Republic of China, Chinese-language newspapers (Simplified Chinese), Publications established in 1992, Media in Beijing, 1992 establishments in China, Supreme People's Court of the People's Republic of China

People's Court Daily (Chinese: 人民法院报; Pinyin: Rénmín Fǎyuàn Bào) is a daily newspaper in China owned by the PRC Supreme People's Court. It was established on October 1, 1992, and is headquartered in Beijing.
