[[Østensjø]]

CATEGORIES: Boroughs of Oslo

Østensjø is a district of the city of Oslo, Norway.
The district is in the southeastern part of Oslo. It is well known for its proximity to the forested area of Østmarka, a popular resort and hiking area for the citizens of Oslo and Lørenskog. Østensjø consists of the suburbs of Bøler, Oppsal and Manglerud, all located around Lake Østensjøvannet. The Østensjøvannet area has been a protected wildlife reserve since 1992.
The club IL Manglerud Star which is known for ice hockey and football is located here.
