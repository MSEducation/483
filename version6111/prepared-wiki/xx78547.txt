[[Higashimorokata District, Miyazaki]]

CATEGORIES: Districts in Miyazaki Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Higashimorokata|東諸県郡|Higashimorokata-gun[/tpl] is a district located in Miyazaki Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the district has an estimated population of 42,283 and the density of 114.12 persons per km². The total area is 370.50 km².
