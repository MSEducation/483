[[Kadri, Mangalore]]

CATEGORIES: Localities in Mangalore

Kadri ( Tulu/Kannada:ಕದ್ರಿ ) is a location in Mangalore city within the Coproration limits. Kadri is home to the famous Kadri Manjunath Temple.
Kadri is also famous for its annual buffalo race known as Kambala. A Famous personality from Kadri is Kadri Gopalnath.
Nearby localities are Balebail and Derebail.
