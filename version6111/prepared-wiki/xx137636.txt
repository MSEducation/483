Metro Polanco  is a  station along Line 7 of the Mexico City Metro.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.metro.df.gob.mx/red/estacion.html?id=94|title=Polanco|language=Spanish|publisher=Sistema de Transporte Colectivo|accessdate=1 August 2011[/tpl][tpl]Cite web|last=Archambault|first=Richard|url=http://mexicometro.org/metro/line7/polanco/|title=Polanco »  Mexico City Metro System|accessdate=1 August 2011[/tpl] The station opened on 20 December 1984.[tpl]Cite web|last=Monroy|first=Marco|editor-last=Schwandl|editor-first=Robert|url=http://www.urbanrail.net/am/mexi/mex-history.htm|title=Opening Dates for Mexico City's Subway|accessdate=21 August 2011[/tpl]  It serves the Polanco neighborhood.  The logo for the station features a silhouette of a building typical of the architecture of Polanco.
