[[Sangha Department (Burkina Faso)]]

CATEGORIES: Departments of Burkina Faso, Koulpélogo Province

Sangha  is a department or commune of Koulpélogo Province in eastern Burkina Faso. Its capital lies at the town of Sangha. According to the 1996 census the department has a total population of 53,621.Burkinabé government inforoute communale
