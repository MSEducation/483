[[Donna M. Walsh]]

CATEGORIES: Members of the Rhode Island House of Representatives, 1943 births, Living people, Rhode Island Democrats, People from Washington County, Rhode Island, Women state legislators in Rhode Island

Donna M. Walsh (born 1943) is an American politician who is a Democratic member of the Rhode Island House of Representatives, representing the 36th District since elected 7 Nov 2006. She currently serves on the House Committee on Corporations, and the House Committee on Environment and Natural Resources.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.rilin.state.ri.us/Walsh/Biography.html | title= Representative Donna M.
