==Life and career==

Katharine Isabelle was born on November 2, 1981 in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada as Katharine Isobel Murray.
Isabelle is the daughter of Graeme and Gail Murray. Graeme Murray is an art director & production designer who has won two Emmys for his work on The X-Files. Her mother, Gail Murray is an amateur Vancouver writer/producer. Katharine is the sister of journalist and former child actor Joshua Murray.
Graeme created special effects for the TV series The X Files.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ginger-snaps.com/unleashed/presskit.htm |title=GINGER SNAPS 2: UNLEASHED - the Sequel |publisher=Ginger-snaps.com |date=2001-05-11 |accessdate=2014-01-23[/tpl] Isabelle's brother is actor, writer and director Joshua Murray.[tpl]cite web|author=Flashback Weekend 2009 Recap |url=http://www.horrorsociety.com/2009/11/16/flashback-weekend-2009-recap/ |title=Flashback Weekend 2009 Recap |publisher=Horror Society |date=2009-11-16 |accessdate=2014-01-23[/tpl] Isabelle broke into acting at an early age with parts in the films Cousins and Cold Front and an episode of MacGyver. Isabelle later had the lead role in Ginger Snaps.
