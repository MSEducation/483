[[Ivan Cesar Asenjo]]

CATEGORIES: 1982 births, Chilean footballers, Puerto Montt footballers, S.League players, Living people

Ivan Cesar Asenjo (born January 16, 1982) is a Chilean footballer who plays as a midfielder or forward.

==Football career==

He started his career in then-Chilean second division team Deportes Puerto Montt, before moving to PSDS Deli Serdang in Indonesia in 2003. He played for several different poor clubs in Indonesia for four years before he was signed by Malaysian Premier League,second division side Johor Pasir Gudang in 2007.He is nowhere to be seen.
