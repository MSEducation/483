[[Siegel upper half-space]]

CATEGORIES: Complex analysis, Automorphic forms, Differential geometry

In mathematics, the Siegel upper half-space of degree ''g (or 'genus g) (also called the Siegel upper half-plane') is the set of g × g'' symmetric matrices over the complex numbers whose imaginary part is positive definite. It was introduced by [tpl]harvs|txt|authorlink=Carl Ludwig Siegel|last=Siegel|year=1939[/tpl].
In the case g = 1, the Siegel upper half-space is the well-known upper half-plane.
