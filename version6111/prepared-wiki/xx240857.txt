[[Longgang District, Huludao]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Liaoning, Huludao

Longgang District ([tpl]zh|s=龙港区 |t=龍港區 |p=Lónggǎng Qū |l=Dragon Port[/tpl]) is a district of Huludao, Liaoning, People's Republic of China. It is by far the smallest division of Huludao City with an area of just [tpl]convert|138|km2[/tpl],[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.hld.gov.cn/government/ShowArticle.asp?ArticleID=1570|title=Huludao Administrative Districts|publisher=Huludao Municipal Government|accessdate=2010-01-08[/tpl] and along with Lianshan District is one of the two districts within which Huludao city itself is situated.
Longgang district encompasses the new district of Huludao city, and includes Longbeishan Park, Longwan Beach, Wanghai Temple and the Huludao shipyard within its boundaries.
