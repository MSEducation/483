[[Columbia Sertoma Basketball Club]]

CATEGORIES: Basketball teams in the United States

The Columbia Sertoma Basketball Club was a professional basketball team. They were a part of the National Alliance of Basketball Leagues.

==History==

Columbia Sertoma Basketball Club competed at the 1970 edition of the Intercontinental Cup.Linguasport.com V Intercontinental Cup (Varese 1970).
