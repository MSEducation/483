[[Kunisaki, Ōita (town)]]

CATEGORIES: Dissolved municipalities of Ōita Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Kunisaki|国東町|Kunisaki-machi[/tpl] was a town located in Higashikunisaki District, Ōita Prefecture, Japan.
As of October 1, 2004, the town had an estimated population of 13,106 and the density of 116.73 persons per km². The total area was 112.28 km².
On March 31, 2006, Kunisaki absorbed the towns of Aki, Kunimi and Musashi (all from Higashikunisaki District) to create the city of Kunisaki.
