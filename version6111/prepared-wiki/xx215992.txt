[[Cayman Islands at the Commonwealth Games]]

CATEGORIES: Cayman Islands at the Commonwealth Games

The Cayman Islands have attended eight Commonwealth Games, beginning in 1978. They have won two medal during this time, a bronze from Kareem Streete-Thompson in the 2002 long jump and a gold by Cydonie Mothersille at the 2010 games for the 200 metres.
