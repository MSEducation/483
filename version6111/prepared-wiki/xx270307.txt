[[Château de Fénelon]]

CATEGORIES: Châteaux in Dordogne, Historic house museums in Aquitaine, Museums in Dordogne

Château de Fénelon is a château in Dordogne, Aquitane, France. The château's exterior served as the home of Danielle de Barbarac (Drew Barrymore) in the 1998 film Ever After.
The information above is incorrect. The chateau de la roussie was used in the movie as the home of Danielle De Barbarac.
