[[WBJW]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Illinois

WBJW is a Christian radio station licensed to Albion, Illinois, broadcasting on 91.7 MHz FM.  The station is owned by Music Ministries Inc.,WBJW fcc.gov. Accessed August 6, 2012 and is an affiliate of Thy Word Network.Tune In: Our Stations www.thywordnetwork.org. Accessed August 6, 2012
For the station in Orlando, Florida formerly known as WBJW (BJ105), see WOMX.
