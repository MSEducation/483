[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Almenara]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1981, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Diamantina, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Almenara ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Almenarensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Almenara, Minas Gerais, in the Ecclesiastical province of Diamantina in Brazil.
