[[Paul Pawlak]]

CATEGORIES: 1940 births, Living people, The Citadel Bulldogs football coaches, Cornell Big Red football coaches, Tufts Jumbos football coaches, Northeastern Huskies football coaches, Southern Connecticut State University alumni, UMass Minutemen football coaches, Washington & Jefferson Presidents football coaches, World League of American Football coaches

Paul Pawlak, Jr. (born July 25, 1940) is a former American football coach.  He served as the head football coach at Tufts University from 1974 to 1977 and at Northeastern University from 1981 to 1990, compiling a career college football record of 49–85–1.
