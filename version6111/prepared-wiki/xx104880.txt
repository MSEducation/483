[[Chesley Awards]]

CATEGORIES: Chesley Awards, Science fiction awards, Art awards, Lists of speculative fiction-related award winners and nominees, 1985 establishments in the United States

The Chesley Awards were established in 1985 by the Association of Science Fiction and Fantasy Artists to recognize individual artistic works and achievements during a given year. The Chesleys were initially called the ASFA Awards, but were later renamed to honor famed astronomical artist Chesley Bonestell following his death in 1986.[tpl]cite news |date=December 1999 |title=Chesley Awards |work=Science Fiction Chronicle |volume=21 |issue=1 |page=4[/tpl] The awards are presented annually, typically at the World Science Fiction Convention (Worldcon).[tpl]cite news |work=The Tennessean |location=Nashville, TN |title=People in Business |page=E5 |url=http://pqasb.pqarchiver.com/tennessean/access/1753929081.html?FMT=CITE&FMTS=CITE:FT |date=September 24, 2006 |accessdate=August 28, 2012 |quote=The Chesley Awards, established in 1985 by the Association of Science Fiction and Fantasy Artists, are annual international awards presented during the World Science Fiction Convention and on some occasions when WSFC is on another continent, the North American Science Fiction Convention.[/tpl]
