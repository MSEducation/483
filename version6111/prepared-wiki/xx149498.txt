[[K17FA-D]]

CATEGORIES: Channel 17 low-power TV stations in the United States, Television channels and stations established in 1990, Channel 17 digital TV stations in the United States, Channel 41 virtual TV stations in the United States

K17FA-D is a low-powered station in Willmar, Minnesota affiliated with Ion. This station is owned and operated by UHF-TV Inc., which offers Ion as part of a limited line-up of cable and broadcast channels to Willmar-area viewers.
Prior to 2010, K17FA relayed an over-the-air signal of the cable channel TV Land, making it the only broadcast affiliate of the network.
