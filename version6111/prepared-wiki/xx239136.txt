[[David Taboureau]]

CATEGORIES: 1968 births, Belgian canoeists, Canoeists at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Living people, Olympic canoeists of Belgium

David Taboureau (born 24 April 1968) is a Belgian sprint canoer who has competed in the mid-1990s. At the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta, he was eliminated in the semifinals of the K-2 500 m event and the repechages of the K-2 1000 m event.
