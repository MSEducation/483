[[Towzlu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Towzlu ([tpl]lang-fa|توزلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Towzlū, Toozloo, Tūzlū; also known as Tozlū)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3087755|Towzlu[/tpl] is a village in Bizineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 2,085, in 450 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
