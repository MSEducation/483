[[KOPY-FM]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Texas

KOPY-FM (92.1 FM) is a radio station licensed to Alice, Texas, USA, the station serves the Kingsville-Alice-Falfurrias area.  The station is currently owned by Claro Communications, Ltd. ==History==

The station was assigned the call letters KDSI on January 13, 1982.  On June 3, 1985, the station changed its call sign to the current kopy. On August 5, 1991 to KQNN, on April 1, 1996 to KOPY, on August 23, 1996 to the current kopy, [tpl]cite web |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=982&Callsign=kopy |title=kopy Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division |accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl] [tpl]cite web|url= |title=FCC Application  |publisher=Federal Communications Commission |accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl]
