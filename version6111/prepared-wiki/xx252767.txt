[[Vopreki]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 albums, Albums by Russian artists

Vopreki ([tpl]lang-ru|Вопреки[/tpl], [tpl]lang-en|Despite[/tpl]) is sixth studio album of Russian pop singer Valery Meladze released in December 2008 on label "Misteria Zvuka".

==Track listing==

All songs written and composed by Konstantin Meladze.
