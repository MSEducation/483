[[Hasan Al]]

CATEGORIES: 1972 births, Living people, People from Sivas, Danish boxers, Turkish boxers, Welterweight boxers, Turkish emigrants to Denmark

Hasan Al (born June 18, 1972 in Sivas, Sivas Province) is a Turkish-born boxer from Denmark, best known for winning the European welterweight title in 1996 as an amateur.

==Amateur==

In his adopted country he became European Champ in 1996 against Marian Simion. At the Olympics 1996 he defeated Sergiy Dzindziruk then lost to Simion.http://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/al/hasan-al-1.html sports-reference

==Professional career==

Al turned professional at middleweight in 1996 but was not successful. He beat Meldrick Taylor on points in 1998. In 2001, he first drew then was outpointed in a rematch by Carlos Baldomir in his only loss. He retired in 2004.[tpl]fact|date=December 2012[/tpl]
