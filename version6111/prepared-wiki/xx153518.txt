[[James Packman]]

CATEGORIES: 1979 births, Living people, Australian cricketers, New South Wales cricketers

James Russell Packman (born 21 August 1979 in Paddington) is an Australian cricketer who is currently a New South Wales Blues squad member. He has scored a first class hundred, an innings of 107 against Western Australia and has played in a winning Pura Cup final side.
At grade cricket level, he plays for Gordon.
