[[KMEN]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in California, Radio stations established in 1965, 1965 establishments in California, Spanish-language radio stations in California

La Maquina MusicalKMQA/KMEN (100.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Regional Mexican format. One Transmitter is Licensed to East Porterville, California, USA, it serves the Tulare, California area, while KMEN is Licensed to Mendota, California. Both KMQA and KMEN  is currently owned by Mbp Licensee, LLC.
