Brianne Theisen-Eaton (born 23 February 1958) is a retired Canadian track and field athlete who competed in the heptathlon and women's pentathlon. She represented Canada in the heptathlon at the 1984 Summer Olympics. She was the former Canadian national record holder in the pentathlon before that record was broken by Brianne Theisen-Eaton.
