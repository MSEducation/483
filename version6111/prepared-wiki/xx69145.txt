[[Operation Shfifon]]

CATEGORIES: Battles and operations of the 1948 Arab–Israeli War

On May 13, 1948, a day before the state of Israel was proclaimed, the Haganah launched Operation Shfifon ([tpl]Lang-he|מבצע שפיפון[/tpl], lit. Operation Cerastes Cerastes) with the aim of capturing the British outposts in the Old City of Jerusalem and preventing the Arab forces from taking control of them first.
