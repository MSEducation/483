[[Joshua Adams]]

CATEGORIES: 1986 births, Living people, Australian dancers, Queensland University of Technology alumni

Joshua K. Adams (born 10 December 1986) is an Australian Dancesport competitor. Adams also has two older brothers, Paul and Michael who were successful Ballroom Dancers.

==Career==

Adams is a two time National Dancesport Champion dancing with long time dance partner Jessica Reid, both who were trained by successful Dancesport instructor Michael Bishop in Brisbane Queensland.http://www.bishopdancesport.com.au/results/results2002.htm
