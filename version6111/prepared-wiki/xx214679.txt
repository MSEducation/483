[[Lifecapture Interactive]]

CATEGORIES: Advertising agencies of Canada, Privately held companies of Canada

Lifecapture Interactive is Toronto, Ontario based online marketing agency.  Specializing in building Interactive websites and Social Marketing the company has done work for a large number blue chip Canadian brands.
Lifecapture placed on PROFIT magazine's prestigious 2006 and 2007 HOT 50 lists, which ranks Canada's fastest-growing companies.
