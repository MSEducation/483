[[Fjerritslev]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in North Denmark Region, Jammerbugt Municipality

Fjerritslev is a Danish town in Jammerbugt Municipality, Region Nordjylland. The town's population is 3,379 (1 January 2013).BEF44: Population 1st January, by urban areas database from Statistics Denmark
Until 1 January 2007, Fjerritslev was the municipal seat of the former Fjerritslev Municipality.
