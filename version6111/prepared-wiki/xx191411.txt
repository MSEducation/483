[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Barra do Garças]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1982, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Cuiabá, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Barra do Garças ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Barragartiensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Barra do Garças in the Ecclesiastical province of Cuiabá in Brazil.
