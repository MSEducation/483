[[Wayne O'Gorman]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Dual players, Wicklow inter-county Gaelic footballers, Wicklow inter-county hurlers, Ashford Gaelic footballers, Glenealy hurlers, Year of birth missing (living people)

Wayne O'Gorman is a dual player[tpl]cite web|url=http://hoganstand.com/wicklow/ArticleForm.aspx?ID=48912|title=O’Gorman’s tough weekend call|publisher=Hoganstand.com|date=2005-07-15|accessdate=2011-01-05[/tpl] from County Wicklow, Ireland. He played both Gaelic football and hurling with Wicklow[tpl]cite web|url=http://hoganstand.com/wicklow/ArticleForm.aspx?ID=33159|title=O’Gorman hat-trick sinks Longford|publisher=Hoganstand.com|date=2003-03-30|accessdate=2011-01-05[/tpl] during the 2000s. He was also part of the Wicklow team that won the All-Ireland Junior Football Championship Title in 2002 where he scored 3-02 in the final win over Kerry.[tpl]cite web|url=http://hoganstand.com/wicklow/ArticleForm.aspx?ID=33409|title=Junior All-Ireland for Wicklow|publisher=Hoganstand.com|date=2002-08-04|accessdate=2011-01-05[/tpl] He plays his club football with Ashford[tpl]cite web|url=http://hoganstand.com/wicklow/ArticleForm.aspx?ID=123155|title=O'Gorman on comeback trail|publisher=Hoganstand.com|date=2010-01-29|accessdate=2011-01-05[/tpl] and hurling with Glenealy with whom he has won County Championship medals.
