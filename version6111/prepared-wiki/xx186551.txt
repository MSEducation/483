==Model organisms==

Model organisms have been used in the study of NIPA1 function. A conditional knockout mouse line, called Nipa1tm1a(KOMP)Wtsi[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.knockoutmouse.org/martsearch/search?query=Nipa1 |title=International Knockout Mouse Consortium[/tpl][tpl]cite web |url=http://www.informatics.jax.org/searchtool/Search.do?query=MGI:4363744 |title=Mouse Genome Informatics[/tpl] was generated as part of the International Knockout Mouse Consortium program — a high-throughput mutagenesis project to generate and distribute animal models of disease to interested scientists — at the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute.[tpl]Cite pmid|21677750[/tpl][tpl]cite journal |author=Dolgin E |title=Mouse library set to be knockout |journal=Nature |volume=474 |issue=7351 |pages=262–3 |date=June 2011 |pmid=21677718 |doi=10.1038/474262a [/tpl][tpl]cite journal |author=Collins FS, Rossant J, Wurst W |title=A mouse for all reasons |journal=Cell |volume=128 |issue=1 |pages=9–13 |date=January 2007 |pmid=17218247 |doi=10.1016/j.cell.2006.12.018 [/tpl]
Male and female animals underwent a standardized phenotypic screen to determine the effects of deletion.[tpl]cite journal| author=van der Weyden L, White JK, Adams DJ, Logan DW| title=The mouse genetics toolkit: revealing function and mechanism. | journal=Genome Biol | year= 2011 | volume= 12 | issue= 6 | pages= 224 | pmid=21722353 | doi=10.1186/gb-2011-12-6-224 | url=http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/entrez/eutils/elink.fcgi?dbfrom=pubmed&tool=sumsearch.org/cite&retmode=ref&cmd=prlinks&id=21722353 | pmc=3218837[/tpl]  Twenty four tests were carried out on mutant mice but no significant abnormalities were observed.
