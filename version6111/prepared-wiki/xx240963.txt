[[Youyu County]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Shanxi

Youyu ([tpl]zh|c=右玉 |p=Yòuyù[/tpl]) is a county of northwestern Shanxi, People's Republic of China, bordering Inner Mongolia to the north and west. It is under the administration of Shuozhou City.

==Geography and climate==

Elevations are generally higher in the south of the county, in which flows the Cangtou River ([tpl]lang|zh-hans|苍头河[/tpl]); Youyu reaches a north-south extent of [tpl]convert|67.7|km|abbr=on[/tpl] and east-west width of [tpl]convert|45.7|km|abbr=on[/tpl]. The Great Wall demarcates the northwestern border with Inner Mongolia's Liangcheng and Horinger counties; within the province, Youyu borders Zuoyun County to the east, Shanyin County and Pinglu District to the south.
Youyu has a monsoon-influenced, humid continental climate (Köppen Dwb), with cold and very dry winters, and warm, humid summers. The monthly 24-hour average temperature ranges from [tpl]convert|−14.4|°C|1[/tpl] in January to [tpl]convert|19.5|°C|1[/tpl] in July, and the annual mean is [tpl]convert|3.93|°C|1[/tpl]. June thru September accounts for over three-fourths of the [tpl]convert|410|mm[/tpl] of annual precipitation. Due to the high elevation and dry climate, the diurnal temperature variation averages [tpl]convert|16|C-change[/tpl] annually.
