[[Steven St. Croix]]

CATEGORIES: 1968 births, Male actors from Los Angeles, California, American male pornographic film actors, American pornographic film directors, Living people, Pornographic film actors from California

 
Steven St. Croix (b. 1968, Los Angeles, California) is a pornographic actor and director.
He has been in more than 1000 films, and has won multiple AVN awards. He was inducted into the AVN Hall of Fame in 2005.
In 1996, St. Croix became the first man to sign a contract with one of the adult industry's largest video production companies, Vivid Video.
