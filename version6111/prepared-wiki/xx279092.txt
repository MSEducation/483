[[Mirjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Mirjan ([tpl]lang-fa|ميرجان[/tpl], also Romanized as Mīrjān, Mīr Jān, and Merījān; also known as Mirdzhan)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3075134|Mirjan[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,359, in 325 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
