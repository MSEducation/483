[[Horrid Henry (TV series)]]

CATEGORIES: British animated television series, Horrid Henry, ITV children's television programmes, Television series by Nelvana, Television programs based on children's books, 2006 British television programme debuts, Cartoon Network programs

Horrid Henry is a British animated television series based on the book series by Francesca Simon produced by Novel Entertainment  and Nelvana Limited (season 1), broadcast from late 2006 on Children's ITV in the UK. It airs on Cartoon Network (India) from April 2012 and its third season started airing on Cartoon Network (India) from second half of 2013 at 7-30pm Mon-Fri.  The animation style differs from the Tony Ross illustrations in the books.  Series Producer of the series is Lucinda Whiteley, Animation Director is Dave Unwin.   The series has been sold to more than a dozen countries including France, Germany, South Africa, South Korea, and the Philippines. The series also airs on Cartoon Network (India)
There is a music album Horrid Henry's Most Horrid Album.[tpl]cite news |title=Record deal for Horrid Henry|publisher=Licensing.biz|date=17 November 2008|url= http://www.licensing.biz/news/2225/Record-deal-for-Horrid-Henry|accessdate=2011-06-19[/tpl] The incidental music is composed by Lester Barnes and additional songs are composed by Lockdown Media.
Three Series have been aired so far with 52 episodes each. A Fourth Series is scheduled to be aired on CiTV in UK in 2014.Thus the total number of episodes will be 208.

==Awards==

Horrid Henry was nominated for "Best Cartoon" award at the Nickelodeon Kids Choice Awards in 2008, and for "Best Animation" at the British Academy Children's Awards in both 2009 and 2010.
