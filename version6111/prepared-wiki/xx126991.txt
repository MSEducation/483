[[Clark High School (Plano, Texas)]]

CATEGORIES: High schools in Plano, Texas, Public high schools in Texas

R. C. Clark High School is a secondary school in Plano, Texas (USA) serving grades nine and ten. It is part of the Plano Independent School District. In Plano ISD, high school freshmen and sophomores attend one of two high schools; Clark was one of 3 junior high schools, Clark, Williams and Vines (circa 1980+), juniors and seniors attend separate "senior high schools" Plano West and Plano East. Clark High School feeds into Plano East Senior High School, and is one of two high schools in Plano that offer the IB (International Baccalaureate) courses.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.pisd.edu/students/ib/index.shtml|title=International Baccalaureate: Plano ISD|publisher=Plano Independent School District|accessdate = 2006-07-10[/tpl]
The High School has recently added a new wing to the building, which was much needed due to overcrowding in the old building, as well as a new band hall. The new building houses science, art, and foreign language classes. It also boasts new listening labs in some of the foreign language rooms.
Clark High School maintains a population of over 1,300. The school's current principal is Janis Williams.http://www.pisd.edu/schools/secondary/clark/staff.shtml

==Courses==

Clark High School currently offers 3 AP courses: Human Geography AP, World History AP, and European History AP.
