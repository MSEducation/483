==Life==

Yoder was educated at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, graduating in English in 1956.  He then won a Rhodes Scholarship to Jesus College, Oxford, and studied PPE from 1956 to 1958.Civil Rights Greensboro: Edwin M.
