[[No Name Lake]]

CATEGORIES: Lakes of Glacier National Park (U.S.), Lakes of Glacier County, Montana

No Name Lake is located in Glacier National Park, in the U. S. state of Montana. The lake is just north of Pumpelly Pillar in the Two Medicine region of Glacier National Park.[tpl]cite web| title =Mount Rockwell, MT|publisher =TopoQuest| url =http://www.topoquest.com/map.php?lat=48.47941&lon=-113.44760&datum=nad83&zoom=4| format =Map (USGS Quad)| accessdate = 2010-12-31[/tpl] No Name Lake is a [tpl]convert|5|mi|km[/tpl] hike from the Two Medicine Store.[tpl]Cite web| title = Day Hikes | work = Plan Your Visit | publisher = National Park Service | url = http://www.nps.gov/glac/planyourvisit/upload/dayhikes_inside_09.pdf | format = pdf | accessdate = 2011-01-22[/tpl]
