[[Edmund Roche]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing, Year of death missing, Irish Sinn Féin politicians, Teachtaí Dála, Members of the 2nd Dáil, Members of the 3rd Dáil, Politicians from County Kerry

Edmund Roche was an Irish Sinn Féin politician. He was elected unopposed as a Sinn Féin Teachta Dála (TD) to the Second Dáil for the Kerry–Limerick West constituency at the 1921 elections. He opposed the Anglo-Irish Treaty and voted against it. He was re-elected unopposed at the 1922 general election to the Third Dáil as an anti-Treaty Sinn Féin TD but did not take his seat. He did not contest the 1923 general election.[tpl]cite web|url=http://electionsireland.org/candidate.cfm?ID=1137|title=Edmund Roche|work=ElectionsIreland.org|accessdate=10 April 2009[/tpl]
