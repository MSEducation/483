Matteo Vanetta (born 6 August 1978) is a Swiss football player. He currently plays for FC Chiasso.
Vanetta has previously played for AC Lugano, FC Sion, FC Servette and FC Aarau in the Swiss Super League.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Guardian.co.uk|title=Stats Centre: Matteo Vanetta Facts|url=http://guardian.touch-line.com/StatsCentre.asp?CTID=25&CPID=124&TEID=6387&PLID=46670&pStr=Player|accessdate=2010-04-07[/tpl]
