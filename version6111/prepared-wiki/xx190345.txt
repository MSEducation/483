[[Robert Nunn]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Green Bay Packers coaches, New York Giants coaches

Robert Nunn is the defensive line coach for the New York Giants. He played linebacker at Oklahoma State.
Nunn joined the Green Bay Packers on February 8, 2005 as a defensive tackles coach, but was let go along with the majority of the defensive coaching staff after a disappointing 2008 season.Green Bay Packers Fire Six Coaches January 5, 2009 Nunn was hired several weeks later by the Buccaneers as one of two defensive line coaches.Bucs Promote Williams, Hire Several New Assistant Coaches Yahoo Sports, February 3, 2009
On Wednesday January 27, 2010 Robert Nunn was hired as the New York Giants Defensive Line Coach.
