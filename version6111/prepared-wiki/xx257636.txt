[[AERO Specialties]]

CATEGORIES: Aircraft ground handling, Aviation ground support equipment companies

AERO Specialties, Inc. is a manufacturer and international distributor of new and refurbished aircraft ground support equipment (GSE). AERO Specialties was founded in 1987 by President Matt Sheehan to supply GSE products to corporate, fixed-base operator (FBO), commercial, military, and general aviation customers globally.[tpl]cite web | url = http://investing.businessweek.com/research/stocks/private/snapshot.asp?privcapId=51820889 | title = Company Overview | publisher = Bloomberg Businessweek | accessdate = 28 December 2010[/tpl] AERO Specialties manufactures aircraft diesel-electric hybrid ground power units (GPUs), lavatory, potable water, cleaning, and oxygen/nitrogen servicing carts, as well as aircraft towbars and heads. AERO Specialties is an authorized U.S. distributor for Eagle Tugs, Tug Technologies, TLD, Unitron, Clyde Machines, Wasp, and Stinar.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.amtonline.com/article/article.jsp?id=5185 | title = AERO Specialties Signs Agreements with TLD, Unitron | publisher = Aircraft Maintenance Technology | accessdate = 27 December 2010[/tpl]
AERO Specialties has experienced significant international growth due to the expansion of its CE marked product line for the European Economic Area, which was introduced in 2006.[tpl]cite web | url = http://commerce.idaho.gov/news/2009/05/idaho-export-council-honors-fours-idaho-companies-for-export-achievement.aspx | title = Idaho Export Council Honors Four Idaho Companies for Export Achievement | publisher = Idaho Department of Commerce | accessdate = 27 December 2010| archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20101216215246/http://commerce.idaho.gov/news/2009/05/idaho-export-council-honors-fours-idaho-companies-for-export-achievement.aspx| archivedate= 16 December 2010 | deadurl= no[/tpl] Exclusive international distribution agreements with AeroStock of France, Franke Care Systems of Germany, TDH Visions of Saudi Arabia, Movac Group of Mexico, and GSE Services Pty Ltd. of Australia have contributed to an increased global distribution network for AERO Specialties.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.ainonline.com/news/single-news-page/article/aero-specialties-expands-its-global-reach-26919/ | title = Aero Specialties Expands Its Global Reach | publisher = Aviation International News | accessdate = 27 December 2010[/tpl][tpl]cite web | url = http://commerce.idaho.gov/news/2011/05/aero-specialties-secures-australia-oceania-region-distribution-partnership-.aspx | title = AERO Specialties Secures Australia Oceania Region Distribution Partnership  | publisher = Idaho Department of Commerce | accessdate = 22 August 2011[/tpl] An estimated 30 to 40 percent of AERO's business is related to exporting.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.idahostatesman.com/2010/04/15/1153583/local-and-small-but-global-and.html | title = Idaho businesses are local and small, but global and profitable | publisher = Idaho Statesman | accessdate = 28 December 2010[/tpl]
AERO Specialties has partnered with Wainbee International to produce its own line of hydraulic power units (HPU) or hydraulic mules.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.ainonline.com/airshow-convention-news/nbaa-convention-news/single-publication-story/browse/0/article/aero-specialties-highlights-new-ground-support-offerings-22323/?no_cache=1&tx_ttnews | title = Aero Specialties highlights new ground-support offerings | publisher = Aviation International News | accessdate = 28 December 2010[/tpl]  In December 2010, AERO Specialties acquired Australia-based JetGo International, a diesel-electric hybrid GPU manufacturer.  JetGo is now manufactured and sold under the AERO brand.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.idahostatesman.com/2011/01/19/1494272/boise-company-purchases-australian.html | title = Boise company purchases Australian firm and will move jobs to Idaho | publisher = Idaho Statesman | accessdate = 2 February 2011[/tpl]

==Services==

In addition to supplying new and used GSE, AERO Specialties maintains and repairs GSE for clients. Equipment can be refurbished or zero-timed.
AERO Specialties regularly rents and leases GSE for specific purposes or special events.

==Events==

AERO Specialties provided GSE to all FBOs (Avitat, Landmark Aviation, and Million Air) at Vancouver International Airport (YVR) during the 2010 Vancouver Winter Olympics to meet increased aircraft traffic demands from athletes, fans and organizers.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.wingsmagazine.com/content/view/3798/132/ | title = AERO Specialties supplies GSE for the 2010 Olympics  | publisher = Wings Magazine | accessdate = 28 December 2010[/tpl] AERO Specialties supplied approximately 40 pieces of equipment including Eagle TT-series pushback tractors, TLD and JetGo GPUs, and AERO brand towbars, towbar heads, lavatory and potable water carts.
AERO Specialties supplied GSE to FBOs for the First inauguration of Barack Obama and 2009 Super Bowl.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.aero-news.net/index.cfm?ContentBlockID=516ba113-7310-45f7-917a-dfa44cb201df&Dynamic=1&Range=NOW&FromDate=01/14/2009&ToDate=01/14/2009&Category=/index.cfm | title = AERO Specialities Will Be On The Ground At Inauguration, Super Bowl | publisher = Aero-News Network | accessdate = 28 December 2010[/tpl]

==Awards==

AERO Specialties received the U.S. Department of Commerce Small Business Association (SBA) Northwest regional exporter award on May 4, 2011.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.businessair.com/aircraft-press-releases/aero-specialties-named-nw-region-exporter-year | title = AERO Specialties Named N.W. Region Exporter of the Year  | publisher = Business Air | accessdate = 22 August 2011[/tpl]  This honor came in addition to being recognized as the SBA exporter of the year in Idaho.
On May 6, 2009, in honor of World Trade Day, AERO Specialties was awarded the U.S. Department of Commerce Commercial Service Export Achievement Certificate for recent accomplishments in the global marketplace, presented by the Idaho Export Council.
The Boise Metro Chamber of Commerce awarded AERO Specialties its Small Business of the Year award in June 2002.
