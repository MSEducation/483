[[2011 Prime Cup Aberto de São Paulo – Singles]]

CATEGORIES: 2011 ATP Challenger Tour, Aberto de São Paulo

1st seed Ricardo Mello successfully defended his last year's title after defeating qualifier Rafael Camilo 6–2, 6–1 in the final. It was Mello's fourth overall and third consecutive win in São Paulo.
