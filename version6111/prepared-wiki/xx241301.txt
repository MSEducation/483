[[74th Indian Infantry Brigade]]

CATEGORIES: British Indian Army brigades

The 74th Indian Infantry Brigade was an Infantry formation of the Indian Army during World War II. It was formed in July 1942, in India. The brigade was assigned to the 25th Indian Infantry Division and fought in the Burma Campaign.[tpl]cite web|accessdate=2009-10-23|publisher=Order of Battle|title=74 Indian Brigade|url=http://www.ordersofbattle.com/UnitData.aspx?UniX=6197&Tab=Sup[/tpl]
