[[WALI]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in South Carolina

WALI (93.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a country music format and licensed to Walterboro, South Carolina, USA. The station is currently[tpl]When|date=February 2011[/tpl] owned by Hess Communications LLC.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=WALI |title=WALI Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==History==

The station went on the air as WONO on April 19, 1991. On May 15, 1996, the station changed its call sign to WALI.[tpl]cite web|url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=25206&Callsign=WALI |title=WALI Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl] WALI is also the home of Colleton County High School football.
