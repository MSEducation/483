[[Lispe]]

CATEGORIES: Muscidae, Diptera of Europe, Articles containing video clips

The Lispe is a genus of true flies, belonging to the family Muscidae.[tpl]cite book|author=D'Assis Fonseca, E.C.M|year=1968 |title=Diptera Cyclorrhapha Calyptrata: Muscidae |pages=118|publisher=Royal Entomological Society of London|series= Handbooks for the Identification of British Insects|volume= 10|month=|location= London.[/tpl]
The adult are predators of small insects. Larvae are also predators, living in damp sand and mud.[tpl]cite book|author=Gregor, F.; Rozkosny, R.; Bartak, M.; Vanhara, J.|year=2002 |title=The Muscidae (Diptera) of Central Europe|pages=280|publisher=Masaryk University|series= Scientiarum Naturalium Universitatis Masarykianae Brunensis|volume= 107|month=|location= Masaryk.[/tpl]
