[[Mohammed Amin (boxer)]]

CATEGORIES: 1913 births, Possibly living people, Egyptian boxers, Light-heavyweight boxers, Olympic boxers of Egypt, Boxers at the 1936 Summer Olympics

Mohammed Amin (born November 15, 1913) is an Egyptian boxer who competed in the 1936 Summer Olympics.
In 1936 he was eliminated in the second round of the light heavyweight class after losing his fight to Robey Leibbrandt.
