[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Criciúma]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1998, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Florianópolis, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Criciúma ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Criciumensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Criciúma in the Ecclesiastical province of Florianópolis in Brazil.
