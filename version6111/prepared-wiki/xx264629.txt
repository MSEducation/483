[[Janne Kolehmainen]]

CATEGORIES: 1986 births, Living people, Finnish ice hockey players, HPK players, KalPa players

Janne Kolehmainen (born March 22, 1986) is a Finnish professional ice hockey forward. He is currently playing with KalPa in the Finnish Liiga. Kolehmainen was originally drafted by the Ottawa Senators 115th overall in the 2005 NHL Draft but did not sign.[tpl]cite web|title=Prospect Janne Kolehmainen Player Profile|url=http://www.hockeysfuture.com/prospects/janne_kolehmainen|work=Hockey's Future|accessdate=January 6, 2014[/tpl]
