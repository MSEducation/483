[[Mesrabad, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Mesrabad ([tpl]lang-fa|مصراباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Meşrābād; also known as Misrābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3074768|Mesrabad[/tpl] is a village in Shivanat Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 499, in 100 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
