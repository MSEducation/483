[[Hasanlu, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Hasanlu ([tpl]lang-fa|حسن لو[/tpl], also Romanized as Ḩasanlū)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3849030|Hasanlu[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 150, in 27 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
