[[Nilson Cortes]]

CATEGORIES: 1977 births, Living people, Colombian footballers, Colombia international footballers, Independiente Santa Fe footballers, Millonarios Fútbol Club footballers, Boyacá Chicó footballers, Once Caldas footballers

Nilson Armes Cortes (born March 29, 1977 in Barranquilla) is a Colombian football striker.
At club level he currently plays for Once Caldas in the Copa Mustang.
Having had a great season with Once Caldas has recently got him a recent call up for a friendly game against Venezuela on April 30.
