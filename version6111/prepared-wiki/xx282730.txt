The anime series Space Cobra is based on the manga series of the same name written by Buichi Terasawa. The series is directed by Osamu Dezaki and produced by TMS Entertainment.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.moviesquare.jp/channel/catalog?c=cobra | title=アニメの殿堂ムービースクエア - スペースコブラ | publisher=Movie Square | accessdate=August 10, 2013 | language=Japanese[/tpl] The episodes follows Cobra, a spatial pirate, who enjoys an adventurous life along with his gynoid partner Armaroid Lady.
Space Cobra broadcast between October 7, 1982 and May 19, 1983 on Fuji Television. The 31 episodes were later released in a DVD box set on October 25, 2000.[tpl]cite web |last=Terasawa |first=Buichi |authorlink=Buichi Terasawa |url=http://www.buichi.com/works/cobra/cobra_03.html |title=HISTORY |publisher=Buichi.com |language=Japanese |archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20080121045329/www.buichi.com/works/cobra/cobra_03.html |archivedate=January 21, 2008 |accessdate=August 10, 2013 [/tpl][tpl]cite web | url=http://www.amazon.co.jp/dp/B00005HNGH | title=スペースアドベンチャー コブラ DVD-BOX | publisher=Amazon.com | accessdate=August 10, 2012[/tpl]
On January 15, 2013, Right Stuf announced they licensed the series to it release in North America. Nozomi Entertainment, a Right Stuf's publishing division, plan to release the anime in two DVD sets, along with digital distribution in late 2013.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.rightstuf.com/rsstatic/nozomiEntertainment/pressReleases/nozomi2013/Right-Stuf-Acquires-Space-Adventure-Cobra-Original-TV_2013-01-15.pdf | title=Right Stuf, Inc. Acquires Space Adventure Cobra: The Original TV Series | date=January 15, 2013 | publisher=Right Stuf | accessdate=August 10, 2013[/tpl]
The series use two pieces of theme music, a opening theme and ending theme: [tpl]nihongo|"Cobra"|コブラ|Kobura[/tpl] and [tpl]nihongo|"Secret Desire"|シークレット・デザイアー|Shīkuretto Dezaiā[/tpl] both by Yoko Maeno.
