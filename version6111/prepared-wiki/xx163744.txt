Anzour Nadimovich Nafash ([tpl]lang-ru|Анзор Надимович Нафаш[/tpl]; born 2 November 1978) is an Jordanian international football midfielder who last played for FC Kavkaztransgaz-2005 Ryzdvyany.
Between 2002 and 2003 he has played 4 matches for the Jordanian national team.
