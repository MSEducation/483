[[Rudolf Baier]]

CATEGORIES: 1892 births, Year of death missing, German male cyclists, Olympic cyclists of Germany, Cyclists at the 1912 Summer Olympics, Sportspeople from Dresden

Rudolf Baier (born 22 March 1892, date of death unknown) was a German road racing cyclist who competed in the 1912 Summer Olympics.
In 1912, he was a member of the German cycling team which finished sixth in the team time trial event.
