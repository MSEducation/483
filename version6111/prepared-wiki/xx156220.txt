Richards signed with the Cubs in 1946 and he had finished his sixth season in their farm system when he was called to the Majors; ironically, 1951 was his worst pro season, as he had batted only .223 in 120 games split between the Class A Des Moines Bruins and the Triple-A Springfield Cubs.Minor league record from Baseball Reference In his first at bat on September 15, facing Sheldon Jones, he flied out to center fielder Willie Mays of the New York Giants,1951-9-15 box score from Retrosheet but overall he collected eight hits (including two doubles) in 27 at bats during his Major League audition. Richards would split 1952 between Des Moines and Springfield again, but he never returned to the Major Leagues.
