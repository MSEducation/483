[[1962 FIFA World Cup qualification (UEFA – Group 4)]]

CATEGORIES: 1962 FIFA World Cup qualification (UEFA)

The three teams in this group played against each other on a home-and-away basis. The group winner qualified for the seventh FIFA World Cup held in Chile.

==Table==

[tpl]fb|NED[/tpl] vs. [tpl]fb|GDR[/tpl] was not played as due to visa problems, the East German players were not allowed to enter the Netherlands. Hungary was already qualified with three wins, and no other date was arranged.
