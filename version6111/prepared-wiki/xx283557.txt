[[The Titan EP]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 EPs, Oh, Sleeper albums

The Titan EP is the latest EP by metal band Oh, Sleeper. It was released in 2013 independently after a successful crowd-funding effort on the website Indiegogo.[tpl]cite web|title=OH SLEEPER [tpl]![/tpl] Indiegogo|url=http://www.indiegogo.com/projects/oh-sleeper|work=Oh, Sleeper|publisher=Indiegogo|accessdate=August 11, 2013[/tpl]
