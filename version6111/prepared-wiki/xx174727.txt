[[KDLY]]

CATEGORIES: Classic hits radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Wyoming, Radio stations established in 1978, 1978 establishments in the United States

KDLY (97.5 FM, "Classic Hits 97.5") is a radio station broadcasting a Classic Hits music format. Licensed to Lander, Wyoming, USA, the station serves the Riverton area. The station is currently owned by Fremont Broadcasting, Inc.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KDLY |title=KDLY Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division[/tpl]
