[[Remo Sernagiotto]]

CATEGORIES: 1955 births, Living people, Venetian politicians, People from Montebelluna, Forza Italia politicians

Remo Sernagiotto (1 September 1955, in Montebelluna) is an Italian politician from Veneto.
A long-time Christian Democrat, he was first elected to the Regional Council of Veneto in 2000 for Forza Italia. In 2005 he  and became floor leader of his party, often being a vocal critic of President Giancarlo Galan.
