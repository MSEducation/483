[[29th Indian Infantry Brigade]]

CATEGORIES: British Indian Army brigades

The 29th Indian Infantry Brigade was an Infantry formation of the Indian Army during World War II. It was formed in October 1940, by the renumbering of the 21st Indian Infantry Brigade. It was assigned to the 5th Indian Infantry Division.  They took part in the East African Campaign and the Western Desert Campaign and were destroyed 28 June during the fighting at Fuka during the First Battle of El Alamein.[tpl]cite web|accessdate=2009-10-23|publisher=Order of Battle|title=29 Indian Brigade|url=http://www.ordersofbattle.com/UnitData.aspx?UniX=1406&Tab=Sup[/tpl]
