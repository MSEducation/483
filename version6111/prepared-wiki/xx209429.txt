[[Shengang District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Taichung

Shengang District ([tpl]zh|t=神岡區|p=Shéngāng Qū[/tpl]) is a rural district in northwest Taichung City, Taiwan.

==Transportation==

National Highway 1 and National Highway 4 run through Shengang.
