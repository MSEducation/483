Baron Samu Hazai ([tpl]lang-de|Samuel Freiherr von Hazai[/tpl], 26 December 1851 – 10 February 1942) was a Hungarian military officer and politician of Jewish origin, who served as Minister of Defence of Hungary between 1910 and 1917.
