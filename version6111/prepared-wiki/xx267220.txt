[[(10773) 1991 AK2]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1991, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(10773) 1991 AK2 is an outer main-belt minor planet.JPL Small-Body Database Browser  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on January 7, 1991.Discovery Circumstances: Numbered Minor Planets (10001)-(15000)
