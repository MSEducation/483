[[Jing Boran]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 births, Living people, Chinese people of Russian descent, Musicians from Shenyang, Chinese Mandopop singers, Reality singing competition winners

Jing Boran ([tpl]zh|c=井柏然|p=Jǐng Bórán[/tpl]; born April 19, 1989 in Shenyang, Liaoning) is a Chinese pop singer who became famous after winning first place in the 2007 My Hero contest.[tpl]cite news|last=Morrison|first=Doriah|title=Reunions and wrecks on the rollercoaster of life|url=http://life.globaltimes.cn/entertainment/2010-07/555088.html|accessdate=31 July 2010|newspaper=Global Times|date=23 July 2010[/tpl][tpl]cite news|title=The fan phenomenon - Is it out of control?|url=http://www.china.org.cn/english/entertainment/226049.htm|accessdate=31 July 2010|newspaper=Shanghai Daily|date=September 27, 2007[/tpl] Along with third place winner, Fu Xinbo, he then formed a group called BoBo. The two has since released their first album. He is 1/8 (12.5%) Russian.
