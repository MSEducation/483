[[Henry S. Clark Stakes top three finishers and starters]]

CATEGORIES: Turf races in the United States, Ungraded stakes races in the United States, Sports in Baltimore, Maryland, Pimlico Race Course, Horse races in Maryland, Recurring sporting events established in 2001

This is a listing of the horses that finished in either first, second, or third place and the number of starters in the Henry S. Clark Stakes, an American stakes race for three year-olds & up at one mile on the turf held at Pimlico Race Course in Baltimore, Maryland.Stakes Histories, The Original Racing Almanac 2009, page 288 on June 26, 2008.
