[[Piers Jacobs]]

CATEGORIES: 1936 births, 1999 deaths, Financial Secretaries of Hong Kong, Government officials of Hong Kong, Hong Kong people of British descent, Knights Commander of the Order of the British Empire, HK LegCo Members 1985–88

Sir Piers Jacobs  (翟克誠爵士 1936 - 23 September 1999) KBE, JP was Financial Secretary of Hong Kong from 1986 to 1991. He also was the Secretary for Economic Services from 1982 to 1986. He died in Hong Kong on 23 September 1999.
