Tigran Petrosyants ([tpl]lang-hy|Տիգրան Պետրոսյանց[/tpl], born on 23 December 1973 in Yerevan, Soviet Union) is a former Armenian football midfielder. He was a member of the Armenia national team, participated in 8 international matches since his debut on 3 March 1999 in away UEFA Euro 2000 qualifying match against Poland.
