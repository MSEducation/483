[[Detachment Brandenstein]]

CATEGORIES: Military units and formations of Germany, Finnish Civil War

Detachment Brandenstein was a unit commanded by German Baron Otto von Brandenstein. The 3,000 man unit which fought in the Finnish Civil War by landing at Loviisa April 7, 1918. Its assigned mission was to cut the Reds' railway connections by attacking to the East, thus cutting the railway between Helsinki and Viipuri.
Later the unit was attached to the Baltic Sea Division.
