Takia Nicole Starks (born January 16, 1986) is a shooting guard for the Perth Lynx of the Women's National Basketball League.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.wnbl.com.au/index.php?id=164|title=Lynx roster[/tpl] Born in Houston, Texas, she played college basketball at Texas A&M.[tpl]cite news|url=http://media.www.thebatt.com/media/storage/paper657/news/2008/11/13/News/Aggie.Basketball.Tips.Off.With.Fresh.Start-3541136.shtml|title=Aggie basketball tips off with fresh start[/tpl] She is A&M's all-time leading scorer.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.google.com/hostednews/ap/article/ALeqM5isYcHZ-gQbyxLH3qh9e59PLAZqhgD9738PTG0|title=Defense leads Texas A&M past Evansville 80-45[/tpl]
