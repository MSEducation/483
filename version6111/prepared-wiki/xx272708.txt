[[Rhona Torney]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Antrim camogie players, Year of birth missing (living people)

Rhona Torney is a camogie player, winner of a Soaring Star award in 2010 and an All Ireland Intermediate championship medal in 2011.

==Other awards==

One Junior and one Intermediate County Titles, two Under-16 Ulster Championships, two Ulster U18 Championships, three Senior Ulster Championships, Purcell Cup, Junior National League Division 3.[tpl]cite book | first = Mary | last = Moran | authorlink = Mary Moran (camogie) | title = A Game of Our Own: The History of Camogie | publisher = Cumann Camógaíochta | year = 2011 | location = Dublin, Ireland | pages = 460[/tpl]
