[[KKBN]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in California

KKBN (93.5 FM) is a radio station  broadcasting a Country music format. Licensed to Twain Harte, California, USA, the station serves the Modesto area.  The station is currently owned by Clarke Broadcasting Corporation.
