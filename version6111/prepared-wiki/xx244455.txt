[[World Finance]]

CATEGORIES: British magazines, Business magazines

World Finance is a financial magazine produced every two months by World News Media, based  in the UK and covering finance, economics major companies.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.newsstand.co.uk/196-Business-and-Finance-Magazines/7342-Subscribe-to-WORLD-FINANCE-Magazine-Subscription.aspx|title=World Finance Magazine Subscription|publisher=Newsstand|accessdate=15 January 2013[/tpl] The magazine has included contributions from personalities such as Nouriel Roubini and Robert Shiller.[tpl]citation needed|date=January 2013[/tpl] It is available by subscription and at newsstands.
The magazine hosts a series of annual awards for companies and organisations in the banking sector.  As at 2012 it had worldwide circulation of  27,453.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.ifslearning.ac.uk/Alumni/AlumniMembershipBenefits/FinancialWorlddetail.aspx |title=Financial World and Financial World Online |publisher=IFS University College |accessdate=10 January 2014[/tpl]
