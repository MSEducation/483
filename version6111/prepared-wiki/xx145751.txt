[[Aake Kalliala]]

CATEGORIES: 1950 births, Living people, People from Heinola, Finnish actors, Finnish television actors, Finnish Freemasons

Aarne "Aake" Kalliala (born 5 October 1950 in Heinola) is a Finnish actor. He is most famous for appearing on comedy-sketch shows such as Velipuolikuu and Pulttibois (both with Pirkka-Pekka Petelius).
Since 1995 Kalliala has starred the drama series Kotikatu in which he plays sexton Mauri Alho. In 1999, he appeared on the cooking show Aaken ja Sakun kesäkeittiö on MTV3.
Kalliala has also written for several TV series including Manitbois in 1992 and Tsa tsa tsaa in 1993.
