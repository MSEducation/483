[[Emamzadeh Shah Zeyd]]

CATEGORIES: Iranian architecture, Buildings and structures in Isfahan

Emamzadeh Shah Zeyd ([tpl]lang-fa|امامزاده شاه زید[/tpl]) is an imamzadeh in Isfahan, Iran. It belongs to the early Safavid era. It is well known for paintings on its walls. These paintings are about the Battle of Karbala. Cavalries are in in the most parts of the paintings, but there are also other elements such as veiled women, dead bodies, birds, mosques and even flowers and nightingales.[tpl]cite book|title=Rāhnamā ye Safar be Ostān e Esfāhān(Travel Guide for the Province Isfahan)|publisher=Rouzane|isbn=964-334-218-2|author=Hosseyn Yaghoubi|editor=Arash Beheshti|page=115|language=Persian|year=2004[/tpl]
