[[Guatemalan general election, 2015]]

CATEGORIES: Elections in Guatemala, 2015 in Guatemala, 2015 elections in Central America

General elections will be held in Guatemala on 13 September 2015 to elect the President and Vice President, as well as members of Congress, the Central American Parliament, and mayors and councils for all Municipalities in the country. All positions will be elected for a four-year period. Current incumbent Otto Pérez Molina is not eligible due to constitutional term limits.
