[[Good Ikuze!]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 albums, Avex Group albums, Albums certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of Japan

[tpl]Nihongo|Good Ikuze!|Goodいくぜ！|Good Let's Go![/tpl] is the second album by Japanese boy band Kis-My-Ft2. It was released on March 27, 2013 in Japan under the record label Avex Trax.

==Overview==

Approximately one year after their first album Kis-My-1st, Good Ikuze! was released with 2 limited editions: with the DVD [tpl]Nihongo|Kis-My-History~Kakezan~!|Kis-My-History~かけざん~！|Kis-My-History~Multiplied~![/tpl] as well as with the special CD Kis-My-Zero 3.
On the weekly Oricon charts published April 8, 2013, the simultaneous release of their single キ・ス・ウ・マ・イ 〜KISS YOUR MIND〜／S.O.S (Smile On Smile), their DVD YOSHIO -NEW MEMBER-, and their album Good Ikuze! granted them all the number one spot in their respective categories, with sales totalling over 2,140,000. The feat of taking top spots in DVD, single, and album sales at the same time had previously only been accomplished by Ayumi Hamasaki and KAT-TUN.[tpl]Cite web|date=2013-04-02|url=http://www.oricon.co.jp/news/rankmusic/2023224/full/|title=【オリコン】キスマイ3冠で7年ぶり快挙　あゆ、KAT-TUNに続く史上3組目|work=|author=|publisher=Oricon|accessdate=2013-04-11[/tpl]
