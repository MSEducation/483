[[Sergei Bezukhov]]

CATEGORIES: 1992 births, Living people, Russian footballers

Sergei Anatolyevich Bezukhov ([tpl]lang-ru|Серге́й Анатольевич Безухов[/tpl]; born 6 July 1992) is a Russian football defender, who last played for FC Druzhba Maykop.
He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Druzhba Maykop on 30 July 2013 in a game against FC Terek-2 Grozny.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Russian Football Union|url=http://rfspro.ru/cgi-bin/player/script2.cgi?par1=119596|title=Career Summary|date=14 August 2013[/tpl]
