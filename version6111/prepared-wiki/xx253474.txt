[[Royal Air Freight]]

CATEGORIES: Airlines of the United States, Companies based in Oakland County, Michigan

Royal Air Freight (also known as Royal Air Charter) is an American passenger and cargo airline based in Waterford, Michigan, adjacent to the Oakland County International Airport. 
The airline is licensed and certified to serve the US, Canada and Mexico with charter passenger and air cargo services.Air Charter Guide, http://aircharterguide.com/Operator_Info/ROYAL_AIR_FREIGHT,_INC./854/PONTIAC/927  
Its main base is Oakland County International Airport located 6 miles west of Pontiac, Michigan.
