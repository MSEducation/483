[[You Bought It – You Name It]]

CATEGORIES: Joe Walsh albums, 1983 albums, Albums produced by Bill Szymczyk, Warner Bros. Records albums

You Bought It – You Name It is the sixth studio album by American rock musician Joe Walsh, released in 1983 (see 1983 in music).

==Reception==

Writing for Allmusic, critic James Chrispell wrote of the album "Rather retro in feel, like the title, it harkens back to a wackier time. Good, but flawed."

All songs by Joe Walsh, except where noted.
