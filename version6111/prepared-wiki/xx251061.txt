[[Gregersen]]

CATEGORIES: Danish-language surnames

Gregersen [tpl]IPA-da|ˈɡʁɛːˀɡɐsn̩[/tpl]  is a North German, Danish and Norwegian patronymic surname, literally meaning "son of Gregers" (equivalent of "Γρηγόριος", cf. English Gregory). It is an unusual surname in Denmark, shared by little more than 5,800 personsStatistics Denmark
