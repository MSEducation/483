Craig Martin Wilson (born February 5, 1957 in Beeville, Texas) is a former water polo player from the United States, who won two Olympic silver medals during his career: in 1984 and 1988.
Wilson was a college water polo player at the University of California, Santa Barbara; he was the  starting goalie for UCSB's 1979 NCAA national championship team.
