[[William Washington Cole]]

CATEGORIES: 1915 deaths, Ringling Bros. and Barnum & Bailey Circus, Circus proprietors, 1847 births

William Washington Cole (1847–1915), was part owner of the Barnum & Bailey Circus.
In 1884, he established  "W.W. Cole’s New Colossal Shows", which survives to this day, as the Cole Bros. Circus.[tpl]cite wdl|10699[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Cole Bros. History: W.W. Cole's Legacy Lives On|url=http://www.gotothecircus.com/index.php/cole-bros-history-sp-1020991886|work=Cole Bros. Circus of Stars|accessdate=22 January 2014[/tpl]
When he died he left an estate in excess of $5,000,000.[tpl]cite news |first= |last= |authorlink= |coauthors= |title=W.W. Cole, will receive $200,000, all household and personal effects, pictures, books, jewelry, and one-fourth of the residuary estate. |work=New York Times |date=March 20, 1915 |accessdate=2009-10-17 [/tpl]
