[[Trooz]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Liège (province)

Trooz is a Walloon municipality of Belgium in Province of Liege. 
On July 1, 2012 Trooz had 8,432 registered inhabitants, of whom 4,199 were male and 4,233 female. The total area is 24.00 km² which gives a population density of 351 inhabitants per km².[tpl]weblink|url=http://www.ibz.rrn.fgov.be/fileadmin/user_upload/Registre/fr/statistiques_population/stat_1_f.pdf |title=Chiffre global de la population par commune, statistiques fédérales issue du registre national (SPF intérieur) |auteur= |année= |éditeur= |accessdate=29 august 2012 [/tpl]
Trooz is situated on the Vesdre River, and is part of the GREOA (Groupement Régional Économique des vallées de l'Ourthe et de l'Amblève) regional grouping for economic purposes.

==Industrial heritage==

The municipality includes several surrounding hamlets including Nessonvaux which is where, between 1904 and 1958, the Imperia company produced automobiles.   The factory building survives.   On the roof cam be seen the test track.   This is one of just two test tracks on the roof of a car factory, the other (better known) one being on top of the old Fiat factory in Turin.
