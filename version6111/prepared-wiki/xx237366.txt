Ray Ternent (born 9 September 1948) is an English-born former football defender, who played in the Football League in the 1960s and 1970s.Football League career stats at Neil Brown
He was spotted by prolific scout Jack Hixon in his native north-east and Ternent joined Burnley from school in April 1964 and made his first team debut against Southampton on 8 April 1967.Jack Hixon - Soccer's golden nursery [tpl]Dead link|date=December 2012[/tpl]
However, Ternent only managed 13 league appearances for Burnley before moving to Southend United in June 1971, where he was part of the 1971-72 team who were promoted from Division Four.Southend Utd Classic Campaigns After two seasons he moved back north with Doncaster Rovers and also made over 80 league appearances for them before moving into non-league football with Frickley Athletic.
Despite being at Burnley at the same time as Stan Ternent, Stan and Ray were not brothers, contrary to some reports.
As of November 2002 he was living in Burnley.Where are they now? at Bob's 70-71 Footballers
