[[Şəfəqli]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Yardymli District

Şəfəqli is a village and the most populous municipality, except for the capital Yardımlı, in the Yardymli Rayon of Azerbaijan.  It has a population of 1,865.  The municipality consists of the villages of Şəfəqli, Ciribul, and Gilar.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.belediyye.org/index.php?con=qanun&bkod=3637 |title=Belediyye Informasiya Sistemi |archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20080924083213/http://www.belediyye.org/index.php?con=qanun&bkod=3637 |archivedate=24 September 2008[/tpl]
