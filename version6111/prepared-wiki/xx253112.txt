Dubyonsky District ([tpl]lang-ru|Дубёнский райо́н[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-myv|Тумобуе[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-mdf|Дубёнкань район[/tpl]) is an administrativeConstitution of the Republic of Mordovia and municipalLaw #118-Z district (raion), one of the twenty-two in the Republic of Mordovia, Russia. It is located in the east of the republic. The area of the district is [tpl]convert|896.9|km2|sp=us[/tpl].[tpl]citation needed|date=October 2012[/tpl] Its administrative center is the rural locality (a selo) of Dubyonki.Law #7-Z Population: [tpl]ru-census2010|13,851|;[/tpl] 16,366 (2002 Census);[tpl]ru-pop-ref|2002Census[/tpl] [tpl]su-census1989|18,571|.[/tpl] The population of Dubyonki accounts for 24.0% of the district's total population.
