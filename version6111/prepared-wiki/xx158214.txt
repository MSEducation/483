[[Bill Briggs (American football)]]

CATEGORIES: 1943 births, Living people, American football defensive linemen, Washington Redskins players, People from Lee County, North Carolina, Iowa Hawkeyes football players

William John Briggs (born December 25, 1943 in Sanford, North Carolina) is a former American football defensive end in the National Football League for the Washington Redskins.  He played college football at the University of Iowa and was drafted in the fifth round of the 1966 NFL Draft.
