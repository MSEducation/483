[[Back to Sherwood]]

CATEGORIES: 1998 Canadian television series debuts, 1998 Canadian television series endings, CBC network shows, Canadian children's television series

Back to Sherwood is a Canadian television series that originally aired on CBC Television in 1998. Created by Ellis Iddon and Phil Meagher of Winklemania Productions (UK). The theme music was also written and performed by Ellis Iddon and Phil Meagher.  A UK/Canada Production.

==Plot Overview==

When Robyn Hood finds a magical amulet, she finds that she can go back in time to the medieval age in England when she puts it on.  In the past, she learns that Robin Hood and Maid Marian have been captured and trapped by magical means by a sorceress in league with the Sheriff of Nottingham.  As their descendant, Robyn and her own group of merry teens must save her ancestors and protect Sherwood.
