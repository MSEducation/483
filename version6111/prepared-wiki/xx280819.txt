[[Gol Bagh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Sari County

Gol Bagh ([tpl]lang-fa|گل باغ[/tpl], also Romanized as Gol Bāgh)[tpl]GEOnet3|48761|Gol Bagh[/tpl] is a village in Chahardangeh Rural District, Chahardangeh District, Sari County, Mazandaran Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 11, in 6 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|02[/tpl]
