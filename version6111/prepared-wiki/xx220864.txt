[[ESN Anadolu]]

CATEGORIES: Student exchange

ESN Anadolu is one of the sections of Erasmus Student Network and it is situated in Anadolu University. ESN Anadolu is officially working as a Student Club in Anadolu University and which is the responsible of preparing activities for exchange students in Eskisehir and helping to exchange students. The ESN Anadolu members adopted in principle Students Helping Students.

==The six principles of ESN Anadolu==

These principles are the same as Erasmus Student Network.
