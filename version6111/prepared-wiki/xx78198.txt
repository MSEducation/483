[[Ōsaki, Kagoshima]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Kagoshima Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Ōsaki|大崎町|Ōsaki-chō[/tpl] is a town located in Soo District, Kagoshima Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2008, the town has an estimated population of 15,406. The total area is 100.82 km².
Osaki is the birthplace of star baseball player Kosuke Fukudome.
