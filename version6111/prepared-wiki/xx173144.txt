[[WWBD]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in South Carolina

WWBD (94.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a mainstream rock music format.[tpl]cite web |work=Arbitron |url=http://www.arbitron.com/radio_stations/station_information.htm |title=Station Information Profile[/tpl] Licensed to Sumter, South Carolina, USA, the station is currently owned by Miller Communications, Inc.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=WWBD |title=WWBD Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division[/tpl]

==History==

The station was assigned the call letters WICI-FM on January 31, 1995.  On February 17, 1995, the station changed its call sign to WICI. On January 21, 2009, the station changed its callsign to the current WWBD.[tpl]cite web |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=29140&Callsign=WWBD |title=WWBD Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division[/tpl] 
The "Bad Dog" classic rock format moved from the station that is now WMXZ to WGFG early in 2009. The former WICI also began airing the music, but a few months later added more newer rock.
