[[Sant Pere de Ribes]]

CATEGORIES: Garraf, Municipalities in the Province of Barcelona

Sant Pere de Ribes is a town in the center of the Garraf comarca, in Barcelona province, Catalonia, Spain. It boasts the remains of a 12th-century castle once ruled by the troubadour Guillem de Ribes.
