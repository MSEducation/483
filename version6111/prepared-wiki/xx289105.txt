[[Yasavol, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Yasavol ([tpl]lang-fa|يساول[/tpl], also Romanized as Yasāvol)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3796132|Yasavol[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 64, in 12 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
