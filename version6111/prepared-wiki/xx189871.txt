[[Anthony Cotrone]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 births, Living people, Players of American football from New York, American football running backs, American football fullbacks, Maine Black Bears football players, Undrafted National Football League players, Jacksonville Jaguars players

Anthony Cotrone (born on September 14, 1985 in Valley Stream, New York) is an American football fullback who is currently a free agent. He was signed by the Jacksonville Jaguars as an undrafted free agent in 2008. He played college football at Maine.

==Early years==

He played high school football at Holy Trinity High School.[tpl]cite web |url=http://goblackbears.cstv.com/sports/m-footbl/mtt/cotrone_anthony00.html |title=Maine Black Bears player page |accessdate=2008-05-21 |work=goblackbears.cstv.com [/tpl] He then played college football for the University of Maine Black Bears. There he played several positions: fullback, H-back, quarterback, and holder on extra point and field goal attempts.[tpl]cite web |url=http://goblackbears.cstv.com/sports/m-footbl/mtt/cotrone_anthony00.html |title= Main Black Bears player page|accessdate=2008-05-21 |work=goblackbears.cstv.com [/tpl]

==Professional career==

===Jacksonville Jaguars===

He was a prospect of the 2008 NFL Draft, but was not selected. He signed an undrafted rookie free agent contract with the Jacksonville Jaguars.[tpl]cite web |url=http://goblackbears.cstv.com/sports/m-footbl/spec-rel/042808aaa.html |title=Anthony Cotrone Signs Rookie Free Agent Contract with the Jacksonville Jaguars |accessdate=2008-05-21 |work=goblackbears.cstv.com [/tpl] He plays alongside former University of Maine teammate, Montell Owens.
After suffering a season-ending injury during the preseason, Cotrone was waived by the Jaguars on August 4, 2008. He was added to the injured reserve list the following day. He was later re-signed to the team, only to be waived on April 29, 2009.

===New York Giants===

Cotrone was asked to try out for the 2010 New York Giants. He participated at their rookie mini-camp.
