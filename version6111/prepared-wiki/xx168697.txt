[[Yekaterina Khoroshikh]]

CATEGORIES: 1983 births, Living people, Russian hammer throwers, Doping cases in athletics, Russian sportspeople in doping cases, People from Rostov Oblast

Yekaterina Khoroshikh ([tpl]lang-ru|Екатерина Хороших[/tpl]; born 21 January 1983 in Rostov Oblast) is a female hammer thrower from Russia. Her personal best is 76.63 metres, achieved in June 2006 in Zhukovsky.
