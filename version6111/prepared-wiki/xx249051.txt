[[Alfred McCoy (American football)]]

CATEGORIES: 1899 births, 1990 deaths, Boston Yanks coaches, Penn State Nittany Lions football players, Northeastern Huskies men's basketball coaches, Northeastern Huskies football coaches, Northeastern Huskies baseball coaches

Alfred Mudge McCoy (October 7, 1899 – January 28, 1990) was the first head coach of Northeastern University's football team from 1933 to 1936. He compiled a 17–8–5 record. McCoy also coached the Huskies' men's basketball program.
