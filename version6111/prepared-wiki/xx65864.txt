[[Didier Van Cauwelaert]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1960 births, French novelists, People from Nice, Prix Goncourt winners, French people of Belgian descent

Didier Van Cauwelaert (born July 29, 1960) is a French author of Belgian descent who was born in Nice. In 1994 his novel Un Aller simple won the Prix Goncourt.
