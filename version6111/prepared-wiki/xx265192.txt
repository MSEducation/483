[[Eighth Area Army (Japan)]]

CATEGORIES: Military units and formations established in 1942, Military units and formations disestablished in 1945

The Eighth Area Army was an area army of the Imperial Japanese Army. The army was formed on 9 November 1942, becoming effective on 26 November at Rabaul as part of the Southern Army.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.axishistory.com/index.php?id=6743|title=8. Area Army (Japan)|publisher=Axis History Factbook|accessdate=3 January 2011[/tpl] The army was disbanded in September 1945.
