La Villa-Basílica ([tpl]Lang-es|Estación La Villa-Basílica[/tpl]) is station along Line 6 of the Mexico City Metro.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.metro.df.gob.mx/red/estacion.html?id=89|title=La Villa-Basílica|language=Spanish|accessdate=2 August 2011[/tpl][tpl]Cite web|last=Archambault|first=Richard|url=http://mexicometro.org/metro/line6/la-villa-basilica/|title=La Villa-Basílica »  Mexico City Metro System|accessdate=2 August 2011[/tpl]
Its logo is the  façade of the nearby Basilica of Our Lady of Guadalupe.
The station serves the Colonia Aragón and Colonia La Villa neighborhoods.  It opened on 8 July 1986.[tpl]Cite web|last=Schwandl|first=Robert|url=http://www.urbanrail.net/am/mexi/mex-history.htm|title=Opening Dates for Mexico City's Subway|accessdate=1 August 2011[/tpl]
