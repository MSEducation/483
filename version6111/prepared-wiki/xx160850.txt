[[MTV Spain]]

CATEGORIES: MTV Networks, Television stations in Spain, Spanish-language television stations, Television channels and stations established in 2000

MTV España (MTV Spain) is the Spanish-speaking version of the popular 24-hour music and youth entertainment channel. MTV España is available on digital satellite Pay-TV offer Digital+, IPTV, many cable networks and from September 2010 on digital terrestrial television (DTT - TDT in Spanish) for free (the second version to do so after MTV Italia).
