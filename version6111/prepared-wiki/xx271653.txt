[[Olivet, Wisconsin]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Pierce County, Wisconsin, Unincorporated communities in Wisconsin

Olivet is an unincorporated community located in the towns of Gilman and Spring Lake, Pierce County, Wisconsin, United States.
