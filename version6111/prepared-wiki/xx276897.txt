Maika Monroe (born May 29, 1993) is an American actress and professional kiteboarder,[tpl]cite web|title=Maika Monroe balances acting with kiteboarding|url=http://espn.go.com/espn/page2/index?id=7392313|publisher=go.com|accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl] who made her acting debut in 2012 in the film At Any Price co-starring with Zac Efron.

==Career==

On April 3, 2012 Monroe signed up for Jason Reitman's drama film Labor Day starring Josh Brolin and Kate Winslet, an adaptation of the novel of the same name.[tpl]cite news|title=Monroe working on ‘Labor Day’|url=http://variety.com/2012/film/news/monroe-working-on-labor-day-1118052270/|accessdate=8 August 2013|newspaper=variety.com|date=3 April 2012[/tpl] She played the role of Mandy, a farm girl who romances with a young man and eventually becomes his wife and the mother of his child.[tpl]cite news|title=Maika Monroe, Tom Lipinski join Kate Winslet in 'Labour Day'|url=http://www.digitalspy.co.uk/movies/news/a374788/maika-monroe-tom-lipinski-join-kate-winslet-in-labour-day.html|accessdate=8 August 2013|newspaper=digitalspy.co.uk|date=4 April 2012[/tpl]
On June 26, 2013 Monroe joined the cast of the psychological thriller film The Guest as a lead actress opposite Dan Stevens.[tpl]cite news|title=Rising Star Maika Monroe Joins 'Downton Abbey's' Dan Stevens in 'The Guest'|url=http://www.thewrap.com/movies/column-post/rising-star-maika-monroe-joins-dan-stevens-thriller-guest-exclusive-99926|accessdate=8 August 2013|newspaper=thewrap.com|date=26 June 2013[/tpl] The film is directed by Adam Wingard and the filming began in mid-July 2013 in New Mexico.[tpl]cite news|title=‘Downton Abbey’ star filming thriller in N.M.|url=http://www.abqjournal.com/222475/blogs/downton-abbey-star-filming-thriller-in-nm.html|accessdate=8 August 2013|newspaper=abqjournal.com|date=17 July 2013[/tpl]
