[[Huilongpu, Ningxiang]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Ningxiang County

Huilongpu Town ([tpl]zh|s=回龙铺镇|t=回龍鋪鎮|p=Huílóngpù Zhèn[/tpl]) is an urban town in Ningxiang County, Changsha City, Hunan Province, People's Republic of China. It's surrounded by Dachengqiao Town on the west, Meitanba Town and Lijingpu Township on the north, Baimaqiao Township on the east, and Batang Town on the south. As of the 2007 census it had a population of 37,000 and an area of 71.8 square kilometers.

==Cityscape==

The Town is divided into thirteen villages and one community, the following areas: Wanshoushan Community (万寿山社区), Huilongpu Village (回龙铺村), Duanjiaqiao Village (段家桥村), Baiyunxin Village (白云新村), Huangshan Village (黄山村), Yuanjiahe Village (袁家河村), Senjin Village (森金村), Tian'e Village (天鹅村), Quanfu Village (泉福村), Huatian Village (华田村), Zuojiahe Village (左家河村), Weihe Village (沩河村), Dongche Village (东车村) and Xinkaihe Village (新开河村).
