[[Gwangju Gwangsan FC]]

CATEGORIES: Challengers League clubs, Sport in Gwangju, 2007 establishments in South Korea

Gwangju Gwangsan FC is a South Korean football club based in the city of Gwangju. It is a member of the Challengers League, an amateur league and the third tier of league football in South Korea.
2008 will be its first season of participation in the Korean football league setup.
The players are mainly a member of scrub team of Honam University's football team.
