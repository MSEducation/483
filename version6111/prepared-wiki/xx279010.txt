[[Sari Kand-e Olya]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Sari Kand-e Olya ([tpl]lang-fa|ساري كندعليا[/tpl], also Romanized as Sārī Kand-e ‘Olyā)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795978|Sari Kand-e Olya[/tpl] is a village in Chaypareh-ye Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 166, in 33 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
