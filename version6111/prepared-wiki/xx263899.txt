[[Ganyo]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Naga Self-Administered Zone, Lahe Township

 
 Ganyo is a village in Lahe Township, Naga Self-Administered Zone, in the Sagaing Region of northwestern Burma. It is located in the Naga Hills near the border with India.[tpl]cite map|title=Google Maps|publisher=Google|accessdate=2 January, 2010[/tpl][tpl]cite map|title=Bing Maps|publisher= Microsoft and Harris Corporation Earthstar Geographics LLC|accessdate=2 January, 2010[/tpl]
