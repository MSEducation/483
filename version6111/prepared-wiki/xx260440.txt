[[Quelque Show]]

CATEGORIES: CBC network shows, 1975 Canadian television series debuts, 1975 Canadian television series endings

Quelque Show is a Canadian talk show television series which aired on CBC Television in 1975.

==Premise==

Episodes featured commentary on various topics by members of the public as recorded on location in Montreal.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.broadcasting-history.ca/programming/television/programming_popup.php?id=1124 | first=John | last=Corcelli | date=September 2005 | title=Quelque Show | publisher=Canadian Communications Foundation | accessdate=7 May 2010 [/tpl]

==Scheduling==

This series was broadcast Sundays at 3:30 p.m. from 5 January to 23 March 1975.
