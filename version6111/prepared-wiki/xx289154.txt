[[Tazeh Qeshlaq, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Tazeh Qeshlaq ([tpl]lang-fa|تازه قشلاق[/tpl], also Romanized as Tāzeh Qeshlāq)[tpl]GEOnet3|351178|Tazeh Qeshlaq[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 39, in 10 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
