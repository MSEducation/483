[[List of best-selling music downloads in the United Kingdom]]

CATEGORIES: Lists of best-selling singles in the United Kingdom

A music download is a transferral of music from an Internet-facing computer or website to a user's local desktop computer; legal sales of music downloads have been monitored and charted in the United Kingdom since 2004. [tpl]As of|2014|4|21[/tpl], the UK's most downloaded song of all time is "Blurred Lines" by Robin Thicke featuring T.I. and Pharrell Williams, which topped both the UK Official Download Chart and UK Singles Chart for five weeks. The first single to sell over one million downloads in the UK was "I Gotta Feeling" by The Black Eyed Peas.
"Blurred Lines" is the best-selling download by a male singer, while "Someone Like You" by Adele is the best-selling download by a female artist. The best-selling download not to top the UK Official Download Chart is "Love the Way You Lie" by Eminem featuring Rihanna, which peaked at number two.
[tpl]As of|2012|8|3[/tpl], the UK's most downloaded album of all time is 21 by Adele, which sold over 320,000 downloads in its first two months of sale.[tpl]Citation needed|date=July 2013[/tpl] The first album to sell over 300,000 downloads in the UK was The Fame by Lady Gaga.http://www.officialcharts.com/chart-news/lady-gaga-sets-platinum-record-for-digital/ The fastest-selling download album is Mylo Xyloto by Coldplay, which sold over 80,000 downloads in its first week of sale, beating the record previously held by Take That with Progress.http://www.officialcharts.com/chart-news/digital-album-sales-smash-2010s-total/
