[[Afon Ddu (Drum)]]

CATEGORIES: Caerhun, Rivers of Conwy County Borough, Rivers of Snowdonia

Afon Ddu is a stream in Snowdonia, in north-west Wales.  It is a tributary of Afon Dulyn which itself is a tributary of the river Conwy.
Afon Ddu drains the south-eastern slopes of Drum and Foel-fras, mountains in the Carneddau mountain range.
(NB - There is another river of the same name, also a tributary of the River Conwy, some 3 miles to the south-east.
