[[Fight with Tools]]

CATEGORIES: 2007 albums, Flobots albums, Universal Republic Records albums

Fight with Tools is the debut album by Flobots originally released in October 2007 and re-released on May 20, 2008. The album was recorded in 2007 around the band's hometown of Colorado. The album features the single "Handlebars" which became a popular hit on Modern Rock radio the following April, also becoming a popular hit in the UK (#14[tpl]cite journal|title = The Official UK Singles Chart for the week ending August 16, 2008|journal = ChartsPlus|issue = 364|pages= 1–4|accessdate = August 12, 2008|publisher = IQ Ware Ltd|location = Milton Keynes[/tpl]), New Zealand (#26[tpl]cite web|url=http://charts.org.nz/search.asp?cat=s&search=Handlebars|title=New Zealand Singles Chart|publisher=charts.org.nz|accessdate=August 9, 2008[/tpl]) and Canada (#63[tpl]cite web| url=[tpl]BillboardURLbyName|artist=flobots|chart=Canadian Hot 100[/tpl]| title=Flobots Album & Chart History – Canadian Hot 100|work=Billboard|publisher=Prometheus Global Media|accessdate=April 22, 2014[/tpl]). The album also features the single "Rise" which also became a popular hit on Modern Rock radio after its release.
In the week of June 7, 2008, the album jumped on the Billboard 200 chart 168 places and peaked at number 15, selling over 265,000 copies. The album also peaked at #52 on the UK Albums Chart.
