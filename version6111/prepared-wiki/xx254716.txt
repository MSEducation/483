[[Gillis Pieter de Neve]]

CATEGORIES: People from Bergen op Zoom, Royal Netherlands East Indies Army generals, Royal Netherlands East Indies Army personnel, Aceh War, 1823 births, 1883 deaths, Knights Third Class of the Military William Order

Gillis Pieter de Neve (Bergen-op-Zoom, 30 December 1823 - Leiden, 19 September 1883) was a Dutch Lieutenant General, commander of the Royal Netherlands East Indies Army and knight and officer in the Military William Order.
