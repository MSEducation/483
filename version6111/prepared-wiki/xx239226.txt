[[Délivrance]]

CATEGORIES: 2009 albums, A Hawk and a Hacksaw albums

Délivrance is the fourth full-length studio album by A Hawk and a Hacksaw, released in 2009 on The Leaf Label.

==Singles==

"Foni Tu Argile", a traditional Greek Rembetika song, is the first single from the album. It was released as a 500 copy, limited edition, hand-numbered 10” single, cut at 78rpm. It was also released digitally.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.theleaflabel.com/en/releases/view/153/A%20Hawk%20And%20A%20Hacksaw/Foni%20Tu%20Argile/DOCK%2053|title=Foni Tu Argile|date=27 April 2009|publisher=The Leaf Label Ltd.|accessdate=27 October 2009[/tpl]
The song was featured on NPRs All Things Considered as the 'Song of the Day' in August 2009.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.npr.org/templates/transcript/transcript.php?storyId=112352947|title=Highlights From 'Song Of The Day'|last=Raz|first=Guy|coauthors=Thompson, Stephen|date=29 August 2009|work=All Things Considered|publisher=NPR|accessdate=27 October 2009[/tpl]
