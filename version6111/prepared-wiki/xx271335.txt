[[Baladeh, Fars]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Kazerun County, Cities in Iran, Cities in Fars Province

Baladeh ([tpl]lang-fa|بالاده[/tpl], also Romanized as Bālādeh and Bālā Deh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3054925[/tpl] is a city in and the capital of Jereh and Baladeh District, in Kazerun County, Fars Province, Iran.  At the 2006 census, its population was 3,936, in 856 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|07[/tpl]
