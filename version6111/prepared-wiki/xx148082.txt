[[El Piveto Mountain]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of British Columbia, Vancouver Island Ranges

El Piveto Mountain  is a mountain located lies between Wolf River and Cervus Creek on Vancouver Island, British Columbia.  It is located [tpl]convert|2|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] southeast of Rambler Peak and [tpl]convert|24|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] east of Gold River.

==History==

The first ascent of El Piveto Mountain was completed in 1966 by Mike Walsh, Pat and Elizabeth Guilbride, Bob Tustin, Ray Paine, Syd Watts and John and Doreen Cowlin.Stone, p 249
