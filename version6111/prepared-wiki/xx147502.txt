[[Guam Men's Soccer League]]

CATEGORIES: Football competitions in Guam, National association football premier leagues

Guam Men's Soccer League is the top league of the Guam Football Association in the United States territory of Guam and is referred to as the BGMSL.
Since 2004, the BGMSL has had two levels:  Division I and Division II.  Since 2007, the league has adopted a longer season that starts in the fall and ends in the spring. Previously, the BGMSL was divided into two seasons: Spring and Fall. When the champions of the Spring and Fall seasons were different, a one-off match was played to decide the year's overall champion.
