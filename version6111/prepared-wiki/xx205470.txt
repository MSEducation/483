CATEGORIES: Irish-American culture, Gaelic games in Canada, Gaelic games in the United States

Gaelic games in North America  in North America (United States and Canada) are governed by three  Gaelic Athletic Association (GAA) County boards: 

==History==

The New York Board is the oldest county board of the GAA in North America, however Gaelic games were played in many parts of the continent before the foundation of the GAA, and the organisation of county boards.
