[[Animal Collective discography]]

CATEGORIES: Discographies of American artists, Rock music group discographies

The discography of Animal Collective, an American experimental psychedelic band, consists of nine studio albums, two live albums, one video album ("visual album"), six extended plays and eleven singles.
The group consists of musicians Avey Tare (David Portner), Panda Bear (Noah Lennox), Deakin (Josh Dibb), and Geologist (Brian Weitz). The band straddles a wide range of genres, most prominently psych folk and noise rock. The group runs the record label Paw Tracks, on which they have released much of their own material.
