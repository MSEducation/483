[[Bruin, Kentucky]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Elliott County, Kentucky, Unincorporated communities in Kentucky

Bruin is an unincorporated community in Elliott County, Kentucky, United States.  It lies along Routes 7 and 409 just south of Grayson Lake State Park. Bruin is northeast of the county seat, Sandy Hook.Rennick, Robert M. (1987) Kentucky Place Names  University Press of Kentucky, Lexington, Kentucky, p. 39, ISBN 0-8131-0179-4  Its elevation is 689 feet (210 m).[tpl]gnis|507598[/tpl], Geographic Names Information System, 1979-09-20.  Accessed 2008-01-03.
The post office was established in 28 December 1869 by Samuel MobleyThe community was named after Bruin Creek which flows parallel to Route 7 on the east side.Bruin Quadrangle, Kentucky, (1971) Topographic Map 1:24,000, United States Geological Survey
