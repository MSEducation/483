[[1999 NCAA Division I Baseball Tournament]]

CATEGORIES: NCAA Division I Baseball Championship, 1999 NCAA Division I baseball season

The 1999 NCAA Division I Baseball Tournament was held from May 28 through June 19, 1999. 64 NCAA Division I college baseball teams met after having played their way through a regular season, and for some, a conference tournament, to play in the NCAA Tournament. The tournament culminated with eight teams competing in the 1999 College World Series at historic Rosenblatt Stadium in Omaha, Nebraska.
