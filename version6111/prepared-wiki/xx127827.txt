[[E. C. Smith House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Florida, Buildings and structures in Ocala, Florida, National Register of Historic Places in Marion County, Florida, Houses in Marion County, Florida

The E. C. Smith House (also known as the James Hall House) is a historic home in Ocala, Florida. It is located at 507 Northeast 8th Avenue. On May 24, 1990, it was added to the U.S. National Register of Historic Places.
