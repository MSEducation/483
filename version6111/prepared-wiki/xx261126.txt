[[Inti Pestoni]]

CATEGORIES: 1991 births, Living people, HC Ambri-Piotta players, Nationalliga A players, Swiss ice hockey players

Inti Pestoni (born August 8, 1991) is a Swiss ice hockey player who is currently playing for HC Ambrì-Piotta of National League A. Brought up as a youth within the Ambri-Piotta club, Pestoni was re-signed to a further two-year contract extension on February 27, 2012, to remain under contract with Ambri until 2016.[tpl]cite web| url = http://www.hcap.ch/it/article/1399/pestoni-in-biancoblu-fino-al-2016 | language = Italian | title = Pestoni to remain in Biancoblu until 2016 | publisher = HC Ambrì-Piotta | date = 2012-02-27 | accessdate = 2012-10-02[/tpl]
