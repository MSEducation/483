[[Mourad Amara]]

CATEGORIES: 1959 births, Living people, Algerian footballers, Algeria international footballers, 1982 FIFA World Cup players, 1986 FIFA World Cup players, 1982 African Cup of Nations players, Olympic footballers of Algeria, Footballers at the 1980 Summer Olympics, JS Kabylie players

Mourad Sadegh Amara (born 19 February 1959) is a retired Algerian international football goalkeeper. He represented Algeria in several tournaments those being the 1980 Summer Olympics,
1982 FIFA World Cup, 1986 FIFA World Cup, and the 1991 Afro-Asian Cup of Nations.Player statistics: Mourad AMARA. FIFA.
