[[Marcelo Roberto Lima de Mattos]]

CATEGORIES: 1986 births, Living people, Brazilian footballers, Clube Atlético Paranaense players, Clube Atlético Sorocaba players, Figueirense Futebol Clube players, Clube Náutico Capibaribe players

Marcelo Roberto Lima de Mattos or simply Marcelinho (born October 24, 1986 in Palmital), is a Brazilian attacking midfielder. He currently plays for Náutico on loan from Atlético-PR.
