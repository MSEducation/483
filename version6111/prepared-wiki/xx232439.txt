[[WIHG]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Tennessee, Classic hits radio stations in the United States

WIHG (105.7 FM, "The Hog") is a classic hits radio station licensed to Rockwood, Tennessee, USA.  The station is currently owned by Southern Media Group. The station has obtained a construction permit from the FCC for a power increase to 2,400 watts.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=WIHG |title=WIHG Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www1.arbitron.com/sip/displaySip.do?surveyID=SU08&band=fm&callLetter=WIHG|title=WIHG Station Information Profile|publisher=Arbitron[/tpl]

==History==

The station went on the air as WOFE-FM on 1990-03-19. On 2003-06-11, the station changed its call sign to WWSR and on 2007-02-26 to WLSQ and to its current WIHG on 2007-07-16.[tpl]cite web|url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=51113&Callsign=WIHG |title=WIHG Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
