[[World Mission Sunday]]

CATEGORIES: Catholic holy days, October observances

World Mission Sunday is a day set aside for the Catholic Church throughout the world to publicly renew its commitment to the missionary movement. It is celebrated on the third
Sunday of October every year.World Mission Sunday: October 18, 2009 It was created by Pope Pius XI in 1926 as the day of prayer and propaganda of missions.Speech from John Paul II
