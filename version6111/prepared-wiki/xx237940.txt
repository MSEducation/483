[[Mannen som älskade träd]]

CATEGORIES: Cornelis Vreeswijk albums, 1985 albums, Swedish folk music

Mannen som älskade träd,(English: The Man, who loved trees) is a music album recorded by the Swedish-Dutch folk 
singer-songwriter Cornelis Vreeswijk in 1985. Recorded in Tromsø, Norway, four years after his previous album.
