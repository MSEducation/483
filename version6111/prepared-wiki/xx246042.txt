[[Bleach (season 14)]]

CATEGORIES: 2010 television seasons, 2011 television seasons, Bleach episode lists

The fourteenth season of the Bleach anime series, which is known as the [tpl]nihongo|Arrancar: Downfall arc|破面・滅亡篇|Arankaru Metsubō Hen[/tpl],[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.amazon.co.jp/dp/B004EWFWLM/|title = BLEACH 破面・滅亡篇 1|publisher=Amazon.com|language=Japanese|accessdate=December 6, 2010[/tpl] is directed by Noriyuki Abe and produced by TV Tokyo, Dentsu, and Studio Pierrot.[tpl]cite web |url=http://pierrot.jp/title/bleach/staff.html |title=ぴえろ BLEACH =ブリーチ= |publisher=Studio Pierrot |language=Japanese |accessdate=April 8, 2009[/tpl] The story will continue the fight between Soul Society's Soul Reapers and Sōsuke Aizen's arrancar army as the former defends Karakura Town from the latter's invasion, while Ichigo and his group fight the arrancars in Hueco Mundo to rescue Orihime Inoue.
The season started airing on April 13, 2010 in Japan on TV Tokyo.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.tv-tokyo.co.jp/program/detail/10155_201004131800.html |title=ＢＬＥＡＣＨ　第２６６話「一護ＶＳウルキオラ、再開」 番組情報 テレビ東京 |publisher=TV Tokyo |language=Japanese |accessdate=April 8, 2010[/tpl] Aniplex collected it in eleven DVD volumes between February 23 and December 14, 2011.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.amazon.co.jp/dp/B005TL0V4A/|title=BLEACH 破面・滅亡篇 11 |publisher=Amazon.com |language=Japanese |accessdate=December 27, 2011[/tpl] The English adaptation of the Bleach anime is licensed by Viz Media, and this season started airing on August 11, 2012.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/news/2006-03-15/viz-officially-announces-bleach-anime |title=Viz Officially Announces Bleach Anime |date=March 15, 2006 |publisher=Anime News Network|accessdate=April 13, 2009[/tpl]
The episodes uses six pieces of theme music: two opening themes and four closing themes. The first opening theme, "chAngE" by Miwa is used for episodes 266 to 291, while the second opening theme, [tpl]nihongo|"Ranbu no Melody"|乱舞のメロディ|Ranbu no Merodi|"Melody of the Wild Dance"[/tpl] by Sid is used from episode 292 to 316. The first ending theme, "Stay Beautiful" by Diggy-Mo is used for episodes 266 to 278, the second ending theme, "echoes" by Universe is used from episode 279 to 291, the third ending theme, "Last Moment" by SPYAIR is used from episode 292 to 303, and the fourth ending theme "Song For..." by ROOKiEZ is PUNK'D is used from episode 304 to 316.
