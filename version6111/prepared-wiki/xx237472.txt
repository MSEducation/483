[[York Student Television]]

CATEGORIES: British student media groups, Student television stations in the United Kingdom, University of York, Media in York

York Student Television (abbreviated YSTV) is England’s oldest student television station. Founded in 1967, the station is based at the University of York, with its studio in James College. YSTV once held the world record for longest continuous television broadcast under a single director,[tpl]cite book | last = McWhirter | first = Norris | title = Guinness Book of World Records | year = 1990 | pages = 233 | isbn = [/tpl] and is a member of the National Student Television Association (NaSTA). YSTV creates and produces a wide range of shows, both independently and in collaboration with other university societies.
Current shows include Monday Evening Live, a magazine show; The York Sport Show, with updates on college and varsity sporting events; The Ultimate Challenge, a game show; Past to Present, a history documentary series; Front Row, a film review show; Student Kitchen, a cookery series; and irregular reports on cultural events under the umbrella of YSTV Reports.YSTV Current Productions

==History==

YSTV first broadcast on 21 November 1967, from the university’s Chemistry department.[tpl]cite news | first=Mark | last=Dowdney | title=York TV students take to the air | date=22 November 1967 | url = | work =The Northern Echo | pages =7 | accessdate =30 December 2009[/tpl] At the start of the 2004/05 academic year on 10 October 2004, the station started to broadcast live on the campus intranet to student bedrooms, with on demand content available worldwide.[tpl]cite web|url=http://ystv.co.uk/wiki/index.php/YSTV_Timeline |title=YSTV Timeline |accessdate=18 October 2009 |date=14 February 2009 |publisher=YSTV [/tpl] On 13 January 2006, the station opened up its live stream to the whole world, releasing their content under a Creative Commons license.[tpl]cite news | title=YSTV goes worldwide | date=5 January 2006 | publisher=BBC Yorkshire | url =http://www.bbc.co.uk/northyorkshire/content/articles/2006/01/05/ystv_web_launch_feature.shtml | accessdate =18 October 2009 [/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://ystv.york.ac.uk/streamlaunch/pressrelease.php |title=Internet Stream Launch |accessdate=30 December 2009 |date=10 December 2005 |publisher=YSTV |archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20060910070153/http://ystv.york.ac.uk/streamlaunch/pressrelease.php |archivedate=10 September 2006 |deadurl=yes[/tpl] The station celebrated its 45th birthday in November 2012.
