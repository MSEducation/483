[[Mike Hawthorne]]

CATEGORIES: American comics artists, Living people, American webcomic creators

Mike Hawthorne is an American comic book artist.
He is the creator of the comic book series Hysteria and has provided the artwork for various other comics, including Deadpool,The Un-men, Fear Agent, Umbra, G.I. JOE: Origins, Whiskey Dickel, Three Days in Europe, one story arc of Queen & Country, and Conan: Road of Kings.
He also writes and draws a webcomic titled "Raising Crazy" about his experiences raising his son.
