[[Tarma District]]

Tarma District is one of nine districts of the province Tarma in Peru.[tpl]es icon[/tpl] Instituto Nacional de Estadística e Informática. Banco de Información Distrital. Retrieved April 11, 2008.
The archaeological site Tarmatampu is situated in the district.
