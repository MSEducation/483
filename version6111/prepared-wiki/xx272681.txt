==School and College==

Born in Clonfert, Co Galway near where the Shannon forms the border between Galway and Offaly, she attended Rynagh’s Banagher, captaining the school team to the All Ireland in 2004, and NUI Galway from where she graduated in 2008. More recently, Ms. Earner has graduated from University College Cork with her Higher Diploma in Education. She is now a qualified secondary school teacher in French and Geography and teaching locally. She is currently learning spoken and written Welsh.https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=u0IDGmWZMLc
