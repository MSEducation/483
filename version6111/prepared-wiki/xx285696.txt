[[DBL Coach of the Year]]

CATEGORIES: European basketball awards, Dutch Basketball League awards

The Dutch Basketball League Coach of the Year is an award given to the best coach in the Dutch highest professional basketball league. The award is given after the regular season and was first handed out in 1975 to Bill Sheridan.Coach of the Year at j-dus.com (History of Dutch basketball)
