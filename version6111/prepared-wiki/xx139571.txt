[[Grand Junction, Birmingham]]

CATEGORIES: Rail junctions in England

Grand Junction is a railway junction in Birmingham, England. It is situated less than one mile east of Birmingham New StreetBaker, S. K., Rail Atlas of Great Britain & Ireland, page 48, Oxford Publishing Company, 1992, ISBN 0860935027 and is thus one of the busiest railway junctions in the UK . It is the junction between the former London and North Western Railway line and the Midland Railway's lines to Bristol and Derby.
