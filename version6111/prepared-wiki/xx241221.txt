[[Otog Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Otog Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠣᠲᠣᠭ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Otoɣ qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|c=鄂托克旗 |p=Ètuōkè Qí[/tpl]) is a banner of southwestern Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China. It is under the administration of Ordos City, and borders Otog Front Banner to the southwest and Uxin Banner to the southeast.

==Geography and climate==

Otog Banner is located in the southwest of Inner Mongolia and the west of Ordos City's administration, with a latitude range of 38° 18' to 40° 11' N and a longitude range of 106° 41' to 108° 54', spanning [tpl]convert|209|km[/tpl] north to south and [tpl]convert|188|km[/tpl] west to east. Its total area is [tpl]convert|20064|km2[/tpl].
Otog Banner has a monsoon-influenced, continental cold desert climate (Köppen BWk), with long, cold and very dry winters, and hot, slightly wetter summers. The monthly daily average temperature ranges from [tpl]convert|−9.9|°C|1[/tpl] in January to [tpl]convert|24.1|°C|1[/tpl] in July, and the annual mean is [tpl]convert|8.08|°C|1[/tpl]. Only during the summer months does significant rainfall occur, and there is an average of [tpl]convert|146|mm[/tpl] of precipitation annually. There are 3,000 hours of sunshine and 122 frost-free days per year.
