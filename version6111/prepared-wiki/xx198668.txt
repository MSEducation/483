[[Louis J. Harper]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing, Year of death missing, Kentucky State Thorobreds football coaches

Louis J. Harper was the second head football coach for the Kentucky State University Thorobreds located in Frankfort, Kentucky and he held that position for two seasons, from 1924 until 1925.  His career coaching record at Kentucky State was 1 wins, 5 losses, and 0 ties.  As of the conclusion of the 2007 season, this ranks him 23rd at Kentucky State in total wins and 24th at Kentucky State in winning percentage (.167).Kentucky State University coaching records
