[[KERM]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Wyoming, Country radio stations in the United States

KERM (98.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Country music format. It is licensed to Torrington, Wyoming, USA.  The station is currently owned by Kath Broadcasting, and features programing from ABC Radio .[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KERM |title=KERM Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
