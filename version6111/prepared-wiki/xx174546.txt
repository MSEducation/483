[[KJUA]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Wyoming, Radio stations established in 1978, Hip hop radio stations

KJUA (1380 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a  ClassicHip-Hop format. Licensed to Cheyenne, Wyoming, USA, the station is currently owned by La Familia Broadcasting, LLC. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=KJUA |title=KJUA Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==History==

The station was assigned the calls KSHY on 1978-06-09. On 1996-09-16, the station changed its call sign to KJJL and on 2005-04-10 to the current KJUA.
