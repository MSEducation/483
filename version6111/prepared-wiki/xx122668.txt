[[Cypriot Young Scientists]]

CATEGORIES: Organizations based in Cyprus, Non-governmental organizations based in Cyprus

Cypriot Young Scientists Organisation (ISCHYS) (Greek: Οργανισμός Κυπρίων Νέων Επιστημόνων "ΙΣΧΥΣ") constitutes the first non-governmental youth organisation in Cyprus. Focusing on the promotion of the most capable new scientists and the essential and long-term resolution of problems and concerns of amounting students of Cyprus.
Maintaining organised local delegations across the world, ISCHYS takes initiatives that lead to thorough proposals, studies, suggestions, completed deposits from capable and exempted from clusters youth, rendering young Cypriot scientists eminently active citizens.
