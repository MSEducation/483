[[Ambalamedu High School]]

CATEGORIES: Schools in Kerala

Ambalamedu High School (AHS) is a school in Kerala, India.  AHS provides co-curricular activities including Nature Club, Maths Club, Science Club, School band, Youth Festival, Scouts, Guides and NCC.

==Formation==

The school was inaugurated on 18 May 1970, the first SSLC batch in 1973 had just four students. It has maintained a continuous 100% pass in the SSLC board examinations. It was run by the Cochin Division of the Fertilizers and Chemicals Travancore Ltd (FACT). The school enjoyed an all-pass status till 2004, the last year of its run under FACT before it was leased out to a private management.http://www.deccanchronicle.com/130425/news-current-affairs/article/dashed-hopes-reviving-school%E2%80%99s-30-year-glorious-run

==Tenure under TocH Residential Public School==

Ambalamedu High School was handed over to TocH Residential Public School (Prabhat Residential Public School) in 2004. The private firm run the school for five years and handed in back to FACT in 2009. A trust formed under a society in FACT managed the school for another two years just for the sake of the students who were in class nine and ten. It eventually closed down in 2011.http://www.deccanchronicle.com/130425/news-current-affairs/article/dashed-hopes-reviving-school%E2%80%99s-30-year-glorious-run

==Closure==

The school has been in the limelight among state schools, producing a handful of gems for the world. Sadly, the lights all went out since 2004 and eventually it was closed down at the end of 2011, as the company refused to run it any longer. The school today exists only in the realm of memory. Mr. V.K. Gopalakrishnan was the last headmaster of the school.http://www.deccanchronicle.com/130425/news-current-affairs/article/dashed-hopes-reviving-school%E2%80%99s-30-year-glorious-run
