[[Piqua, Kentucky]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Robertson County, Kentucky, Unincorporated communities in Kentucky

Piqua (pronounced locally as "PICK-way") is an unincorporated community in Robertson County, Kentucky, United States.  It lies along Routes 165 and 617 south of the city of Mount Olivet, the county seat of Robertson County.Rand McNally.  The Road Atlas '06. Chicago: Rand McNally, 2006, p. 43.  Its elevation is 705 feet (215 m).[tpl]gnis|508828[/tpl], Geographic Names Information System, 1979-09-20.  Accessed 2008-01-03.  Johnson Creek, a tributary of the Licking River, flows through the community.
