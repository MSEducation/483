[[Little Leviathan]]

CATEGORIES: 1998 albums, Giant Records (Warner) albums, Michelle Lewis albums

Little Leviathan is an album by singer Michelle Lewis. The album was released in 1998, and the lead single "Nowhere and Everywhere" reached number #40 on the US Hot Adult Top 40 chart,"Nowhere And Everywhere" at the Billboard Hot Adult Top 40 Chart and #21 on the Bubbling Under Hot 100 Singles chart.lewis|chart=Bubbling Under Hot 100}} "Nowhere And Everywhere" at the Billboard Bubbling Under Hot 100 Chart  The album was produced by Steve Fisk.
