[[Aur Bhi Gham Hain Zamane Mein]]

CATEGORIES: Doordarshan television series

Aur Bhi Gham Hain Zamane Mein (Hindi: और भी ग़म हैं ज़माने में) was an Indian television serial broadcast fortnightly on Doordarshan in the early 1980s.[tpl]Citation | title=Lok Sabha Debates | author= | publisher=Parliament of India, Government of India, 1983 | isbn= | url=http://books.google.com/books?id=NV43AAAAIAAJ | quote=... 'Aur Bhi Gham Hain Zamane Mein' which fulfil the twin objectives of education and entertainment ...[/tpl]  It spanned 32 episodes and combined entertainment with education, as each episode highlighted a social problem.[tpl]Citation | title=Parliamentary debates: official report, Volume 120, Issues 9-13 | author= | publisher=Rajya Sabha, Parliament of India, Government of India, 1981 | isbn= | url=http://books.google.com/books?id=yyg3AAAAIAAJ | quote=... 'Aur Bhi Gam Hain Zamane Mein' a serial is telecast on fortnightly basis highlighting social problems, behaviour and attitudes ...[/tpl] The name of the serial was an allusion to Faiz Ahmed Faiz's famous Urdu poem with a slightly different title, "Aur Bhi Dukh Hain Zamane Mein," in which a man explains to his beloved that he is distracted from being focused on her completely by the social injustice and pain he sees around him.
