[[Seboyeta, New Mexico]]

CATEGORIES: Census-designated places in New Mexico, Census-designated places in Cibola County, New Mexico, Populated places established in 1749

Seboyeta is a census-designated place  in Cibola County, New Mexico, United States. Its population was 179 as of the 2010 census. Seboyeta had a post office from February 5, 1885, to January 7, 1995.[tpl]cite web|title=Postmaster Finder - Post Offices by ZIP Code|url=http://webpmt.usps.gov/pmt008.cfm|publisher=United States Postal Service|accessdate=August 10, 2013[/tpl] The community was established in 1749.
