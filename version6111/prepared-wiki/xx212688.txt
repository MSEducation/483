[[2006–07 2. Bundesliga (women)]]

CATEGORIES: 2. Bundesliga (women) seasons, 2006–07 domestic women's association football leagues, 2006–07 in German women's football, 2006–07 in German football leagues

The 2nd Fußball-Bundesliga (women) 2006–07 was the 3rd season of the 2. Fußball-Bundesliga (women), Germany's second football league. It began on 10 September 2006 and ended on 28 May 2007.[tpl]cite web | title = 2. ==Relegation Play-offs==

For the first time a five teams were relegated after the season. The 11th and 12th place finishers of each division were automatically relegated while the 10th place finishers of each division determined the fifth team to be relegated in a match over two legs.
