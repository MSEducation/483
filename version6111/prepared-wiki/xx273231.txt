[[Jamal Al-Qabendi]]

CATEGORIES: 1959 births, Kuwaiti footballers, Kuwait international footballers, Association football defenders, Olympic footballers of Kuwait, Footballers at the 1980 Summer Olympics, 1982 FIFA World Cup players, Living people

Jamal Yaqoub Al-Qabendi (born 7 April 1959 in Kazmi) is a Kuwaiti football defender who played for Kuwait in the 1982 FIFA World Cup.1982 FIFA World Cup Spain He also played for Kazma Sporting Club.
