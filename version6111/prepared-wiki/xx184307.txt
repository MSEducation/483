[[Albisrieden]]

CATEGORIES: District 9 of Zürich, Former municipalities of the canton of Zürich

Albisrieden is a quarter in the district 9 in Zurich.
It was formerly a municipality of its own, having been incorporated into Zurich in 1934.
The quarter has a population of 17,226 distributed on an area of 4.6 km².
