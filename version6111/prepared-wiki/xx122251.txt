[[Dracula Society]]

CATEGORIES: Gothic fiction, Literary societies, Science fiction organizations, Dracula, Learned societies of the United Kingdom

The Dracula Society is a London-based literature and travel group with an interest in supernatural and macabre works of fiction, as exemplified by Bram Stoker's Dracula.

==Children of the Night Award==

An annual prize given by the Society to a novel, short story or poem in the gothic genre.

==Voices from the Vaults==

The society's quarterly journal which contains reviews, articles and fiction of direct or related interest to members.
