[[WJSD-LP]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Alabama, Low-power FM radio stations in Alabama, Religious radio stations in the United States, DeKalb County, Alabama, 3ABN radio stations

WJSD-LP (94.5 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve Henagar, Alabama.  The station is owned by the Henagar Educational Foundation. It airs a Religious radio format[tpl]cite web |work=Alabama Broadcast Media Page |title=Alabama LPFM & Translators |url=http://bellsouthpwp2.net/c/r/crackedlcd/almediapage/lpfm.html |accessdate=May 21, 2008[/tpl][tpl]cite web |title=Station Information Profile |work=Arbitron |url=http://www.arbitron.com/[/tpl] as a member of the Three Angels Broadcasting Network.[tpl]cite web |publisher=3BN Radio Network |title=Radio Stations Listing  |url=http://www.3abnradio.org/radio_stations.cfm |accessdate=December 19, 2008[/tpl]

==History==

This station received its original construction permit from the Federal Communications Commission on November 15, 2002.[tpl]cite web |publisher=FCC Media Bureau |title=Application Search Details (BNPL-20010615AMA) |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/app_det.pl?Application_id=569354 |date=November 15, 2002[/tpl]  The new station was assigned the call letters WJSD-LP by the FCC on December 20, 2002.[tpl]cite web |title=Call Sign History |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=134704&Callsign=WJSD-LP |publisher=FCC Media Bureau CDBS Public Access Database[/tpl]  WJSD-LP received its license to cover from the FCC on April 29, 2004.[tpl]cite web |publisher=FCC Media Bureau |title=Application Search Details (BLL-20040428ABL) |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/app_det.pl?Application_id=991008 |date=April 29, 2004[/tpl]
