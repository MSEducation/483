[[Ciel Satellite Group]]

CATEGORIES: Companies based in Ottawa, Companies established in 2004, Privately held companies of Canada, Telecommunications companies of Canada

Ciel Satellite Group is a private Canadian satellite operator, established in 2004, providing services throughout the Americas as a result of Borealis Infrastructure, a financial institution, partnered with satellite operator SES. The headquarters are located in Ottawa, Ontario.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cielsatellite.ca/company.php|title=Homepage|publisher=Ciel Satellite Group|accessdate=2010-12-31[/tpl] Ciel was founded to develop Canadian spectrum opportunities and meet the demand for domestic competitive satellite services. In 2006, Ciel requested Thales Alenia Space to manufacture the Ciel-2 satellite, which was launched on an ILS Proton Breeze M vehicle from the Baikonur Cosmodrome on December 10, 2008.
