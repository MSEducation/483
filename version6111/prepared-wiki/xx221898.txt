Archduchess Maria Isabella of Austria, Princess of Tuscany[tpl]cite web | url=http://thepeerage.com/p11216.htm#i112157| title=Marie Isabelle Erzherzogin von Österreich| date=10 May 2003 | accessdate=2008-10-04 | publisher=thePeerage.com | author=Darryl Lundy| archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20080907050626/http://thepeerage.com/p11216.htm| archivedate= 7 September 2008 | deadurl= no[/tpl] (21 May 1834 – 14 July 1901) was a princess of Bourbon-Two Sicilies and later Archduchess of Austria.
Maria Isabella was born in Florence, Grand Duchy of Tuscany, the daughter of Leopold II, Grand Duke of Tuscany and his second wife Princess Maria Antonia of Bourbon-Two Sicilies.
