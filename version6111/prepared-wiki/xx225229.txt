[[Northamptonshire Cricket League]]

CATEGORIES: English domestic cricket competitions, Cricket in Northamptonshire, ECB Premier Leagues

The Northamptonshire Cricket League is the top level of competition for recreational club cricket in Northamptonshire, England, and since 1999 has been a designated ECB Premier League.List of ECB Premier Leagues
Although the league primarily serves Northamptonshire, it has member clubs from the surrounding counties of Bedfordshire, Buckinghamshire, Cambridgeshire and Leicestershire.
The competing teams in 2014 are: Brixworth, Burton Latimer, Finedon Dolben, Horton House, Northampton Saints, Old Northamptonians, Oundle Town, Peterborough Town, Rushden Town, Rushton, Stony Stratford, and Wollaston.
