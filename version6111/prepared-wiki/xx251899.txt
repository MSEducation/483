[[Big Brother Brasil 1]]

CATEGORIES: 2002 television seasons, Big Brother Brasil, Big Brother (TV series) seasons

Big Brother Brasil 1, known at the time as Big Brother Brasil was the debut season of the Brazilian version of the international reality show Big Brother, which premiered January 29, 2002 with the season finale airing April 2, 2002 on the Rede Globo television network.
The show was produced by Endemol Globo. It was presented by news reporter Pedro Bial and actress Marisa Orth. The prize award was R$500,000 without tax allowances.
The winner was 24-year-old dancer Kléber de Paula, from Campinas, São Paulo.
Kléber returned to compete in Big Brother Brasil 13.

==Overview==

There were twelve housemates competing for the grand prize. The season lasted 64 days nearly a two-three weeks shorter than most seasons, making this the shortest season of the Brazilian series of Big Brother.

===Reunion Show===

The reunion was hosted by Pedro Bial and aired on April 7, 2002. All the former housemates attended.
