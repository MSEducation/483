[[Norfolk County Courthouse]]

CATEGORIES: National Historic Landmarks in Massachusetts, Government buildings completed in 1827, Buildings and structures in Dedham, Massachusetts, Courthouses on the National Register of Historic Places in Massachusetts, County courthouses in Massachusetts

The Norfolk County Courthouse is a National Historic Landmark at 650 High Street in Dedham, Massachusetts.
The Greek Revival courthouse was built in 1827 with modifications by Solomon Willard. It was the site of the famous Sacco-Vanzetti trial. The courthouse was added to the National Historic Register in 1972.NHL listing info
