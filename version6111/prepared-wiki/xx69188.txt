[[Aprasia]]

CATEGORIES: Legless lizards, Reptiles of Australia

Aprasia is a genus of lizards in the family Pygopodidae from Australia.[tpl]NRDB genus|genus=Aprasia|accessdate=9 March 2014[/tpl] They are worm-like, burrowing lizards. At least four of the species are oviparous.[tpl]cite jstor|1446986[/tpl]
