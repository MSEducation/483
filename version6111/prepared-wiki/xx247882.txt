[[Paradise (1931 song)]]

CATEGORIES: 1931 songs, Songs with music by Nacio Herb Brown

"Paradise" is a 1931 song by Nacio Herb Brown, with lyrics by Nacio Herb Brown and Gordon Clifford. It was first sung by Pola Negri in RKO Pictures' 1932  film A Woman Commands, and has since been heard in many other films, including a memorable performance by Gloria Grahame (dubbed by Kaye Lorraine), in the 1949 Nicholas Ray film A Woman's Secret. The song has been recorded by many artists, including well-known recordings by Nat King Cole, Frank Sinatra, and Bing Crosby.
