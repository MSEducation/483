[[Łopiennik Górny]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Krasnystaw County

Łopiennik Górny [tpl]IPAc-pl|ł|o|'|p|j|e|n|n|i|k|-|'|g|u|r|n|y[/tpl] is a village in Krasnystaw County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It is the seat of the gmina (administrative district) called Gmina Łopiennik Górny. It lies approximately [tpl]convert|11|km|mi|0[/tpl] north-west of Krasnystaw and [tpl]convert|40|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-east of the regional capital Lublin.
The village has a population of 563.
