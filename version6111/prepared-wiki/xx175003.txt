[[KWOR]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Wyoming, Radio stations established in 1946, 1946 establishments in Wyoming

KWOR (1340 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a talk radio format. Licensed to Worland, Wyoming, USA. The station is currently owned by the Big Horn Radio Network, a division of Legend Communications of Wyoming, LLC and features programing from News Radio,Westwood One and CBS Radio.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=KWOR |title=KWOR Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
KWOR KYTS and KKLX studios are located at 1340 Radio Drive, Worland. The KWOR transmitter and tower are at the studios.
