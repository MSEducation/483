[[Young Communist League of Western Ukraine]]

CATEGORIES: Youth wings of political parties in Poland, Youth wings of communist parties

Young Communist League of Western Ukraine ([tpl]lang-pl|Komunistycznego Związku Młodzieży Zachodniej Ukrainy[/tpl], KZMZU) was the youth league of the Communist Party of Western Ukraine.
