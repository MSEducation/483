[[Quyjuq-e Olya]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Quyjuq-e Olya ([tpl]lang-fa|قويجوق عليا[/tpl], also Romanized as Qūyjūq-e ‘Olyā and Qūy Jūq-e ‘Olyā)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3849029|Quyjuq-e Olya[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 312, in 63 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
