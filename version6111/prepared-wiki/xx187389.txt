[[Kabisthalam]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Thanjavur district

Kabisthalam is a village under Papanasam taluk in Thanjavur district in Tamil Nadu.
It is located on the river Cauvery.
Major crops are: Paddy, Sugarcane, and Vegetables. The place derives its name from the Gajendra Varadha Temple located in it.
