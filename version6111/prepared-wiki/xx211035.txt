[[1976 All-Ireland Senior Football Championship]]

The 1976 All-Ireland Senior Football Championship was played from May to September, 1976. The final was a repeat of the previous year's encounter, between Kerry and Dublin. Dublin exacted revenge for their 8 point defeat in '75, beating Kerry by 7 points, 3-8 to 0-10.
