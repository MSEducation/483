[[KF Vëllaznimi]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Kosovo, Association football clubs established in 1927, Football clubs in Balcan

Klubi Futbollistik Vëllaznimi (KF Vëllaznimi) is a football club based in Gjakova, in western Kosovo[tpl]ref label|status|a|[/tpl]. The club was founded in 1927 and currently plays in the second division of football in Kosovo, the Liga e Parë.Official Raiffeisen Superliga Table
KF Vëllaznimi are nine times champions of Kosovo and together with KF Prishtina they are one of the most successful football clubs in Kosovo.
