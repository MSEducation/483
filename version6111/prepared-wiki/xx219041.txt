Efioanwan Ekpo (born 25 January 1984) is a Nigerian female footballer. A member of the Nigeria women's national football team, Ekpo competed at the 2003 World Cup, 2004 Summer Olympics, 2006 African Championship, 2007 World Cup and 2008 Summer Olympics.Efioanwan Ekpo at sports-reference.comFIFA.com profile
