[[2013–14 Burton Albion F.C. season]]

CATEGORIES: Burton Albion F.C. seasons, English football clubs 2013–14 season

The 2013-14 season is Burton Albion's fifth consecutive season in League Two.[tpl]cite web|title=Burton Albion History|url=http://www.burtonalbionfc.co.uk/club/history/|publisher=Burton Albion F.C.|accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl] They finished 4th in the previous season but failed to get promotion via the playoffs.[tpl]cite web|title=Burton 1-3 Bradford (agg 4-5)|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/22328159|publisher=BBC|accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl]  It is Gary Rowett's second season as manager of the club.[tpl]cite web|title=Gary Rowett Manager Profile|url=http://www.leaguemanagers.com/managers/profile-1795.html|publisher=League Managers Association|accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl]

==Overview==

===League===

Burton Albion's league campaign began well with a 2-2 away draw against Cheltenham Town on the opening day of the season,[tpl]cite web|title=Cheltenham 2-2 Burton|url=http://www.burtonalbionfc.co.uk/fixtures-results/match-report/index.aspx?matchid=3630533|publisher=Burton Albion F.C|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl]  which was followed by a 1-0 victory versus Rochdale in their first home game of the season.[tpl]cite web|title=Burton 1-0 Rochdale|url=http://www.burtonalbionfc.co.uk/fixtures-results/match-report/index.aspx?matchid=3630576|publisher=Burton Albion F.C.|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] In the following match they held off a Fleetwood Town comeback to beat the Lancashire side 3–2, to give them their first league away win since March 2013 (excluding play-offs).[tpl]cite web|title=Fleetwood 2–3 Burton|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/23644557|publisher=BBC Sport|accessdate=17 August 2013[/tpl]

===Cup Competitions===

====League Cup====

In the League Cup, Burton were drawn against Sheffield United away in the first round.[tpl]cite web|title=Capital One Cup - Round 1 Draw|url=http://www.capitalonecup.co.uk/news/article/round-1-draw-1314-869070.aspx|publisher=Capital One Cup|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl]  They won 2-1.[tpl]cite web|title=Sheffield United 1-2 Burton Albion|url=http://www.burtonalbionfc.co.uk/fixtures-results/match-report/index.aspx?matchid=3629997|publisher=Burton Albion F.C.|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl]  On the 8 August 2013, they were drawn a home tie against Premier League side Fulham in the second round.[tpl]cite web|title=Capital One Cup - Round 2 Draw|url=http://www.capitalonecup.co.uk/news/article/round-2-draw-1314-977479.aspx|publisher=Capital One Cup|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] Against Fulham, Albion equallised with five minutes remaining through Jack Dyer before taking the lead 12 minutes into extra-time, before conceding through Hugo Rodallega. After the 2–2 draw Phil Edwards was the only player to miss a penalty and Albion crashed out 4–5 in the shoot-out. After the match Burton manager Gary Rowett said he was incredibly proud to nearly knock the Premier League side out.[tpl]cite web|title=Burton 2–2 Fulham (4–5 pens)|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/23764151|publisher=BBC|accessdate=30 August 2013[/tpl]

====Football League Trophy====

On the 17 August 2013, Burton were drawn away to Notts County in the first round of the Football League Trophy.[tpl]cite web|title=The Football League Trophy - Round 1 Draw|url=http://www.football-league.co.uk/johnstonespainttrophy/news/20130817/trophy-round-1-draw_2293332_3349604|publisher=The Football League|accessdate=17 August 2013[/tpl]
