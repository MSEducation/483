==Career==

Bowyer is a professor at University of California at Berkeley. He was also affiliated with the United States Naval Research Laboratory.  He worked in a group directed by Herbert Friedman. He is generally given credit for starting the field of extreme ultraviolet astronomy.
He is credited in shepherding the launch of the EUVE satellite and subsequent research activities.Sanders, Robert. "Lewis satellite to carry UC Berkeley experiment into orbit for atmospheric imaging and mapping of the interstellar medium," University of California, Berkeley (press release). May 27, 1997.
