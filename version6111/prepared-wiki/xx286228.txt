[[Ollatrim]]

CATEGORIES: Townlands of North Tipperary

Ollatrim (Calatroim in Irish)http://www.logainm.ie/46580.aspx is a townland in the historical Barony of Ormond Upper, North Tipperary, Ireland. It is located in North Tipperary between the settlements of Toomevara and Moneygall. The Ollatrim River flows through Ollatrim and the R445 road passes over the river within the townland.

==Structure of note==

Allatrim House (as spelled on National Inventory of Architectural Heritage) is a three bay private house built around 1820. The house is listed as being of architectural and artistic interest.http://www.buildingsofireland.ie/niah/search.jsp?type=record&county=TN&regno=22402205
