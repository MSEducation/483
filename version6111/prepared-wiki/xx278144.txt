[[Young World Quiz]]

CATEGORIES: Student quiz competitions

Young World Quiz is a live Indian quiz contest held for students of class 7, 8 and 9 in India. It is a part of The Hindu's 'Newspapers in Education' initiative. It is, according to The Hindu Young World, " arguably the biggest live quiz show for middle and high school students in India." It is hosted by noted quiz master V.V. Ramanan, and has now completed 13 years. In 2002, it was held in eleven cities, with 3000 teams from over 1200 schools participating. The grand finale of The Hindu Young World Quiz 2012 was held at Chennai in Tamil Nadu, on December 7, 2012.
