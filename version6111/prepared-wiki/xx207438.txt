[[Mount Aetna, Pennsylvania]]

CATEGORIES: Census-designated places in Berks County, Pennsylvania, Census-designated places in Pennsylvania

Mount Aetna is a census-designated placehttp://www.census.gov/2010census/ in Tulpehocken Township, Berks County, Pennsylvania.  It is located along Pennsylvania Route 501 near the border of Lebanon County.  The community is named after the active volcano, Mount Etna in Sicily.  As of the 2010 census, the population was 354 residents.http://www.census.gov/#
