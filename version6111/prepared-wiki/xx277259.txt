[[The Biologist]]

CATEGORIES: English-language magazines, Professional and trade magazines, Bi-monthly magazines, Magazines established in 1953

The Biologist is a bimonthly British professional magazine published by the Society of Biology. The magazine was established in 1953[tpl]cite web |url=http://lccn.loc.gov/75640041 |title=Biologist, Journal of the Institute of Biology |publisher=Library of Congress |work=Catalogue |accessdate=2013-08-18[/tpl] and the editor-in-chief is Sue Nelson. The magazine is abstracted and indexed in several EBSCO databases, including full-text access through Academic Search Complete.[tpl]cite web |url=http://ebscohost.com/titleLists/a9h-journals.htm |title=Magazines and Journals |publisher=EBSCO Publishing |work= Academic Search Complete |accessdate=2013-08-18[/tpl]
