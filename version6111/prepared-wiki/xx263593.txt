[[Aro Kangan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Sarawak

Aro Kangan is a settlement in the remote mountainous interior of Sarawak, Malaysia. It lies approximately [tpl]convert|581.4|km|mi|0[/tpl] east-north-east of the state capital Kuching. 
To reach Aro Kangan from the coastal city of Miri the journey involves a one-hour flight to Long Lellang Airport, followed by a four-hour walk through the jungle from Long Lellang to Aro Kangan.
