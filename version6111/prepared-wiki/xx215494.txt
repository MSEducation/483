Nowinka [tpl]IPAc-pl|n|o|'|w|i|n|k|a[/tpl], ([tpl]lang-lt|Naujinė[/tpl]), is a village in Augustów County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It is the seat of the gmina (administrative district) called Gmina Nowinka. It lies approximately [tpl]convert|10|km|mi|0[/tpl] north of Augustów and [tpl]convert|92|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of the regional capital Białystok.
The village has a population of 300.
