[[Pavol Pronaj]]

CATEGORIES: 1982 births, Living people, Slovak footballers, Gambrinus liga players, SK Dynamo České Budějovice players, Odra Opole players, Motor Lublin players, Znicz Pruszków players, Expatriate footballers in Poland, Slovak expatriates in Poland

 Pavol Pronaj  (born 30 May 1982, in Martin) is a Slovak professional footballer, who plays as a goalkeaper for MŠK Fomat Martin in the Slovak Third League.
