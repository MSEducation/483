[[Kalletumkara]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Thrissur district

Kallettumkara is located in the Thrissur district of Kerala, India, near Irinjalakuda. The Irinjalakuda railway station is situated here. It is a small village where people of multiple religious backgrounds live. Kalettumkara comes under Aloor Panchayat and the panchayat office is situated in Kallettumkara. It is an important junction between Irinjalakuda and Chalakudy.Church: Infant Jesus Church
