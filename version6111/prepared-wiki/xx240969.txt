[[Jiaoqu, Yangquan]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Shanxi

Jiaoqu ([tpl]zh|s=郊区 |t=郊區 |p=Jiāoqū |l=suburban district[/tpl]), or Jiao District is a district of Yangquan, Shanxi province, China. As of 2002, it had a population of 220,000 residing in an area of [tpl]convert|633|km2|abbr=on[/tpl].
