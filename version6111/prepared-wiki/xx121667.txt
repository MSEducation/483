[[Chapel-le-Dale]]

CATEGORIES: Hamlets in North Yorkshire

Chapel-le-Dale is a hamlet in the civil parish of Ingleton, North Yorkshire, England. It is in the Yorkshire Dales.
The hamlet is situated on the B6255 road between Ingleton and Ribblehead near to the Ribblehead Viaduct. The hamlet is in close proximity to Ingleborough and there are several potholes in the vicinity, the best known being Great Douk Cave. The source of the River Doe is nearby.
