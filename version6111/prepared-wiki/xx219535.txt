[[Operation Clambake]]

CATEGORIES: Critics of Scientology, Digital Millennium Copyright Act takedown incidents, Internet properties established in 1996, Non-profit organisations based in Norway, Organizations established in 1996, Scientology and the Internet

Operation Clambake, also referred to by its Web address, Xenu.net, is a Web site and Norway-based non-profit organization, launched in 1996, founded by Andreas Heldal-Lund, that publishes criticism of the Church of Scientology.
