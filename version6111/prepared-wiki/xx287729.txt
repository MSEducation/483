[[Sunil Alagh]]

CATEGORIES: Indian Institute of Management Calcutta alumni, Indian businesspeople, Indian chief executives, Living people

Sunil Alagh is an Indian business executive. He is a former Managing Director and Chief Executive Officer of Britannia Industries, an Indian food products corporation. He was asked to quit by the Board of Britannia in June 2003, for alleged misuse of company funds for his personal use.[tpl]Cite news |url=http://www.business-standard.com/article/companies/sunil-alagh-asked-to-quit-immediately-103060501012_1.html |title=Sunil Alagh Asked To Quit Immediately |author= |accessdate=13 August 2013 |publisher=Business Standard |date=June 5, 2003 |location=[/tpl][tpl]Cite news |url=http://archives.digitaltoday.in/businesstoday/20030706/cover1.html |title=Perform Or Perish |author=Brian Carvalho |accessdate=13 August 2013 |publisher=Business Today (business magazine) |date= |location=[/tpl][tpl]Cite web |url=http://www.rediff.com/money/2003/jul/17alagh.htm |title=Britannia picks Alagh's Rs 1 crore tab |author=Reeba Zachariah |accessdate=13 August 2013 |publisher=Rediff.com |date=June 17, 2003[/tpl]
Alagh is an alumnus of Indian Institute of Management Calcutta. He joined Britannia in December 1974 from Jagatjit Industries, where he was product manager (marketing services). He is credited with Britannia's rise to being India's largest biscuit company.[tpl]Cite web |url=http://www.domain-b.com/companies/companies_b/britannia/20030502_md.html |title=Sunil Alagh to step down as Britannia MD, CEO next year |author=Mohini Bhatnagar |accessdate=13 August 2013 |publisher=domain-b.com |date=2 May 2003[/tpl]
After his exit from Britannia, Alagh founded a consulting firm, SKA Advisors, which advises clients on marketing strategies.[tpl]Cite news |url=http://www.business-standard.com/article/companies/alagh-set-for-second-innings-in-fmcg-104111701023_1.html |title=Alagh set for second innings in FMCG |author=S Kalyana Ramanathan |accessdate=13 August 2013 |publisher=Business Standard |date=November 17, 2004 |location=New Delhi[/tpl] He was appointed as the President of the All India Management Association (AIMA) in 2004.[tpl]Cite news |url=http://www.thehindubusinessline.in/2004/10/13/stories/2004101301110500.htm |title=Sunil Alagh takes over as AIMA President |author=Brian Carvalho |accessdate=13 August 2013 |publisher=Business Line |date=Oct 12, 2004 |location=New Delhi[/tpl]
