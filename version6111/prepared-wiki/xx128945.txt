[[Feuer]]

Feuer is the German language word for fire. From its origins, the word "feuer" has made its way into contemporary use as a family name. 
According to Israel's Diaspora Museum,http://www.israel-mfa.gov.il/MFA/Israel%20beyond%20the%20conflict/The%20Computer%20that%20Finds%20Your%20Family the surname, Feuer, indicates that the ancestors of those bearing this name were the "shedders of light and wisdom" on their villages.
