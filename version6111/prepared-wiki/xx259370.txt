[[Tiri Toa]]

CATEGORIES: Cook Islands national rugby league team players, Cook Island rugby league players, Place of birth missing (living people), Year of birth missing (living people), Living people

Tiri Toa is a Cook Islander professional rugby league footballer of the 2000s, playing at representative level for Cook Islands, as a [tpl]rlp|WG[/tpl].

==International honours==

Tiri Toa won a cap for Cook Islands in the 2000 Rugby League World Cup.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.rugbyleagueproject.org/players/Tiri-Toa/summary.html|title=Statistics at rugbyleagueproject.org|publisher=rugbyleagueproject.org|date=31 December 2011|accessdate=1 January 2012[/tpl]
