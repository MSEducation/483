[[Kusu District, Ōita]]

CATEGORIES: Districts in Ōita Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Kusu|玖珠郡|Kusu-gun[/tpl] is a district located in Ōita Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the district has an estimated population of 29,807 and the density of 53.43 persons per km². The total area is 557.85 km².
