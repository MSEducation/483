CATEGORIES: 1985 births, Living people, Alumni of the University of Wales Institute, Cardiff, English athletes, People from Skegness

Gary White (born 16 June 1985 in Skegness, England) is a triple jumper. He was the 2007 European under-23 champion.[tpl]cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport2/hi/athletics/6900016.stm|title=White wins European triple jump|date=15 July 2007|publisher=BBC Sport|accessdate=2008-11-21[/tpl] He is also an under-23 AAA Champion.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.northamptonchron.co.uk/sport/White39s-delight-as-he-takes.1655423.jp|title=White's delight as he takes triple jump title|date=27 July 2006|publisher=Northampton Chronicle & Echo|accessdate=2008-11-21[/tpl] White attended University of Wales Institute, Cardiff. He competes for Cardiff AAC, his personal best triple jump is 16.33 metres.
