[[KWRK]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Arizona, Media in Apache County, Arizona, Native American radio, Navajo mass media

KWRK (96.1 FM) is a radio station with a city of license of Window Rock, Arizona, USA.  The station is owned by The Navajo Nation.  It airs a Country music format.[tpl]cite web |title=Station Information Profile |work=Arbitron |url=http://www.arbitron.com/[/tpl][tpl]page needed|date=April 2011[/tpl][tpl]page needed|date=April 2011[/tpl]
The station was assigned the KWRK call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on February 1, 1991.[tpl]cite web |title=Call Sign History |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=66147&Callsign=KWRK |work=FCC Media Bureau CDBS Public Access Database[/tpl]
