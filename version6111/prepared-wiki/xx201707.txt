[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Joinville]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Joinville, Religious organizations established in 1927, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Florianópolis, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Joinville ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Ioinvillensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Joinville in the Ecclesiastical province of Florianópolis in Brazil.
