[[Thomas E. Watson House]]

CATEGORIES: National Historic Landmarks in Georgia (U.S. state), Buildings and structures in McDuffie County, Georgia, Houses in Georgia (U.S. state), Houses completed in 1864

Thomas E. Watson House, also known as Hickory Hill was a home of Georgia Populist Party co-founder Thomas E. Watson.
It was declared a National Historic Landmark in 1976.[tpl]Cite document|title=[tpl]PDFlink|National Register of Historic Places Inventory-Nomination: Hickory Hill; Thomas E. Watson House|32 KB[/tpl]|date=January, 1976 |author=Mary Jane Gregory and Ralph Christian |publisher=National Park Service|postscript=[/tpl] and [tpl]PDFlink|Accompanying three photos, exterior, from 1975|32 KB[/tpl]
It is located on Hickory Hill Drive at Lee Street.
