[[David Meunier]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1973 births, American male television actors, American male film actors, American male video game actors, 20th-century American male actors, 21st-century American male actors, Male actors from Oregon

David Meunier is an American actor, known for his role as Johnny Crowder on the television series Justified. He has also had substantial roles in other series such as Revolution and Jericho.
He played football in high school, and a teammate convinced him to try out for a school production of "Guys and Dolls." He was cast as "Nathan Detroit," and the experience had him "hooked" on acting.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.jericho4kids.com/davidm.html |title=Jericho4Kids Jericho Season 2 |publisher=Jericho4kids.com |date= |accessdate=2013-08-25[/tpl]
