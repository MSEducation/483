[[St Anne's Roman Catholic High School]]

CATEGORIES: Secondary schools in Stockport, Roman Catholic secondary schools in the Diocese of Shrewsbury, Educational institutions established in 1959, 1959 establishments in England, Voluntary aided schools in England

St Anne's Roman Catholic High School is a secondary school in Heaton Chapel, Stockport, England. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stockport.gov.uk/services/educationlearning/localeducationandtrainingproviders/secondaryschools/stannesrchighsch|title=St Anne's R.C. High School|publisher=SMBC|accessdate=15 December 2009[/tpl]
In 2009, it achieved media specialist school status. It is fed by St Winifred's and St.Josephs's R.C Primary School,Reddish, and feeds, in turn, Aquinas College, Stockport. At the end of each year all students visit Alton Towers rewarding progress, this has been the school's tradition for many years.
