[[Carolyn Jakobsen]]

CATEGORIES: Australian Labor Party politicians, Members of the Australian House of Representatives for Cowan, Members of the Australian House of Representatives, 1947 births, Living people, Women members of the Australian House of Representatives

Carolyn Anne Jakobsen (born 11 September 1947) is a retired Australian politician. Born in Auckland, New Zealand, she was an electorate secretary and ministerial officer before entering politics. In 1984, she was elected to the Australian House of Representatives as the Labor member for the new seat of Cowan. Her election was the second time a woman had been elected to the House from Western Australia (the first was Wendy Fatin in 1983).
