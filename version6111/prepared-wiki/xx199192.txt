[[Lamiplast]]

CATEGORIES: Companies established in 1962, Companies of Spain, Furniture retailers

Lamiplast is a Spanish home-improvement discount retailer . Founded in 1962, the company is based in Valencia, Spain. The company owns and operates 6 stores and one warehouse and distribution center located in Valencian Community and  Madrid. The company operates it´s stores under the commercial brands Lamiplast (Entre Profesionales) and Diseño y Colección Lamiplast.
In 2006, the company became one of the official sponsors of the Valencia CF Spanish football team.
