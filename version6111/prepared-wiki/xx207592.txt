[[WKOR (AM)]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Mississippi, Radio stations disestablished in 2013, Defunct radio stations in the United States

WKOR (980 AM) was a radio station licensed to Starkville, Mississippi, USA.  The station was owned by Cumulus Media. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=WKOR |title=WKOR Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
The station's broadcast license was surrendered to the Federal Communications Commission for cancellation by trustee Volt Radio, LLC on January 14, 2013.Application Search Details fcc.gov. Accessed January 3, 2014
