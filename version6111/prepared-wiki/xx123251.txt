[[Terry Slater]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Alumni of the University of Hull, British academics, English Anglicans, Academics of the University of Birmingham, Alumni of the University of Birmingham

Canon Doctor Terry Slater is an honorary senior research fellow in Historical Geography at the University of Birmingham, UK. Born in Bromley but raised in Charlton he was educated at Hull University, University College, London and the University of Birmingham.
He joined the University of Birmingham as a lecturer in 1972 and continued until retirement. He devoted a lot of his spare time to the churches of Birmingham that led to him being made a lay preacher and later a Canon in 2005.
