[[Chris Bravacos]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, American University alumni, Pennsylvania lobbyists, Pennsylvania political consultants

Christopher Bravacos is a prominent communications consultant and lobbyist in Pennsylvania. He is the President and CEO of Bravo Group, a lobbying and communications consulting firm.
He worked as executive director of the Republican State Committee of Pennsylvania 1992 to 1994.[tpl]cite news | last = Bravacos| first = Chris| title = What Now for the GOP?
