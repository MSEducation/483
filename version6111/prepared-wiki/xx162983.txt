[[Michael Morton (American football)]]

CATEGORIES: 1960 births, Living people, American football running backs, Seattle Seahawks players, Tampa Bay Buccaneers players, UNLV Rebels football players, Washington Redskins players, Sportspeople from Birmingham, Alabama

Michael Da'mond Morton (born February 6, 1960) is a former American football running back in the National Football League for the Tampa Bay Buccaneers, the Washington Redskins, and the Seattle Seahawks.  He played college football at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas.
