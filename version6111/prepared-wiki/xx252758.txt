[[Acrossocheilus fasciatus]]

CATEGORIES: Acrossocheilus, Fish of China, Endemic fauna of China

Acrossocheilus fasciatus is a species of ray-finned fish in the genus Acrossocheilus. It inhabits mountain streams of southeast China. It matures at an age of 2–3 years, has a lifespan of about 4–5 years, and maximum length of about [tpl]convert|20|cm|abbr=on[/tpl].[tpl]cite doi |10.1007/s10228-012-0271-5[/tpl]
