[[Chandigarh Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in India, Buildings and structures in Chandigarh, Transport in Chandigarh

Chandigarh Airport [tpl]airport codes|IXC|VICG[/tpl]  is located in the Union Territory of Chandigarh (Capital city of Haryana and Punjab, India. It is listed as one of the major airports in North India. Work is underway for an International terminal. A total of 26 domestic flights connect Chandigarh to different destinations in India.

==International terminal==

A new International Terminal is being built at Mohali. Work on the international terminal, upgrading of the domestic terminal, construction of three link taxiways, parallel taxi track, cargo complex and hangars of flying club activities and runway extension have been completed. The terminal will have 4 aero-bridges, construction of apron for parking three in contact and eight remote parking stands for wide-bodied jet aircraft. The project has both Punjab and Haryana as partners.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.tribuneindia.com/2008/20080105/main3.htm|title=Haryana forces stake in Mohali airport|last=Garewal|first=Naveen|date=2008-01-04|publisher=The Tribune|accessdate=21 April 2012|location=Chandigarh[/tpl] The International terminal is located on [tpl]convert|306|acre|km2[/tpl] land in Jheorehri village, near Mohali. The Greater Mohali Area Development Authority is spending Rs 1,700 crore to connect this airport with Punjab.Mera Chandigarh http://merachandigarh.in/mohali-news/gmada-chandigarh-international-airport-roads.html

==Naming Controversy==

Originally, it was proposed to name the airport as Mohali International Airport due to its proximity to the town of Mohali in Punjab. However, in 2008, Haryana demanded a stake in the airport,[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.tribuneindia.com/2008/20080105/main3.htm|title=Haryana forces stake in Mohali airport|last=Garewal|first=Naveen|date=2008-01-04|publisher=The Tribune|accessdate=21 April 2012|location=Chandigarh[/tpl] thus resulting in Punjab and Harayana reaching an agreement to name it Chandigarh International Airport.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.newsreporter.in/no-dispute-over-naming-of-chandigarh-airport-haryana-cm-hooda|title=No dispute over naming of Chandigarh airport: Haryana CM Hooda|date=2011-05-26|publisher=News Reporter |accessdate=21 April 2012|location=Chandigarh[/tpl]
This airport would not be greenfield but the present Indian air force base would be used.http://www.punjabnewsline.com/content/view/11643/93/

==Project plan==

The project includes building an international terminal near the existing Chandigarh Indian Air Force base and further expansion of the domestic terminal. Also, the existing runway will be expanded to handle large passenger aircraft.
