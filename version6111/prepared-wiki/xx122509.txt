[[Districts of the Turks and Caicos Islands]]

CATEGORIES: Lists of country subdivisions, Geography of the Turks and Caicos Islands, Turks and Caicos Islands-related lists

The Turks and Caicos Islands are divided into six administrative districts (two in the Turks Islands and four in the Caicos Islands), headed by District Commissioners (the area figures were determined from information in "TCI Physical Characteristics")
