[[Palatov D1]]

CATEGORIES: Sports cars

The Palatov D1 was designed by Dennis Palatov and is being readied for production by Palatov Motorsport.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.palatov.com |title=palatov.com |publisher=palatov.com |date= |accessdate=2013-07-25[/tpl]  A proof-of-concept prototype (dp1) was constructed over a period of several years using a 4-cylinder engine from Suzuki Hayabusa motorcycle.  There are detailed blogs on the design,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.dpcars.net/dp1dsn/ |title=dp vehicle home |publisher=Dpcars.net |date= |accessdate=2013-07-25[/tpl] build[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.dpcars.net/dp1bld/ |title=dp vehicle home |publisher=Dpcars.net |date= |accessdate=2013-07-25[/tpl] and testing[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.dpcars.net/dp1test/ |title=dp vehicle home |publisher=Dpcars.net |date= |accessdate=2013-07-25[/tpl] of the prototype.  After extensive testing the prototype was eventually retired and has since found a second life as the electric racecar.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.dpcars.net/dp1e/ |title=dp vehicle home |publisher=Dpcars.net |date= |accessdate=2013-07-25[/tpl]
Taking everything learned from the dp1 prototype, Dennis formed Palatov Motorsport in 2008 to manufacture lightweight track and racing cars.  The first car released was the D4.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.palatov.com/cars/d4.html |title=Palatov Motorsport - D4 |publisher=Palatov.com |date= |accessdate=2013-07-25[/tpl]  Its development is documented in a separate blog.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.dpcars.net/dplite/ |title=dp vehicle home |publisher=Dpcars.net |date= |accessdate=2013-07-25[/tpl]  Now the D1[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.palatov.com/cars/d1.html |title=Palatov Motorsport - D1 |publisher=Palatov.com |date= |accessdate=2013-07-25[/tpl] development continues with a 430 hp Hartley V8 engine and redesigned bodywork.  First production D1 is expected to be released in 2011[tpl]update after|2011|12|31[/tpl] and the development is being documented in its own bolg.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.dpcars.net/dp1v8/ |title=dp vehicle home |publisher=Dpcars.net |date= |accessdate=2013-07-25[/tpl]
With a proprietary AWD system, extreme light weight and 1,000 hp per metric ton the D1 will be the flagship of the Palatov Motorsport product lineup. The D1 is designed to use the Hartley V8[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.h1v8.com |title=h1v8.com |publisher=h1v8.com |date= |accessdate=2013-07-25[/tpl] exclusively.
The production V8 version is intended for skilled and experienced track drivers who can make use of its potential. The D1 will only be sold as a complete (turnkey) car exclusively for track use with no streetable option.
