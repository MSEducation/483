[[Thomas Belcher]]

CATEGORIES: 1834 births, 1898 deaths, People of Maine in the American Civil War, Union Army soldiers, United States Army Medal of Honor recipients, American Civil War recipients of the Medal of Honor, People from Bangor, Maine

Private Thomas Belcher (1834 to May 22, 1898) was an American soldier who fought in the American Civil War. Belcher was awarded the country's highest award for bravery during combat, the Medal of Honor,[tpl]Cite web |url=http://valor.defense.gov/Recipients/ArmyMedalofHonorRecipients.aspx |title=U.S. Army Medal of Honor Recipients |accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl] for his action during the Battle of Chaffin's Farm in Virginia on 29 September 1864. He was honored with the award on 6 April 1865.[tpl]Cite web |url=http://www.maine.gov/sos/kids/cmoh/recipients.htm |title=Maine Congressional Medal of Honor Recipients |accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl]
Belcher died on 22 May 1898 and is believed to be buried in Togus, Maine.
