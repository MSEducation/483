[[Hurstbridge railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1912

Hurtsbridge railway station is the terminus of the Hurstbridge line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the north-eastern Melbourne suburb of Hurstbridge opening on as Hurtst's Bridge on 25 June 1912 being renamed Hurstbridge on 9 December 1912.Hurstbridge Vicsig

==Platforms & services==

Hurstbridge has one platform. It is serviced by Metro Trains' Hurstbridge line services.Hurstbridge Line timetable Public Transport Victoria
