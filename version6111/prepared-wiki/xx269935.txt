[[Istanbul Men's Volleyball League]]

CATEGORIES: Volleyball leagues in Turkey, National volleyball leagues, Defunct sports leagues in Turkey, 1921 establishments in Turkey, 1970 disestablishments in Turkey

The Istanbul Volleyball League (Turkish: İstanbul Erkekler Voleybol Şampiyonası) is a defunct volleyball league competition in Turkish volleyball, run by the Turkish Volleyball Federation from 1921 to 1970.
