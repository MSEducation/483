[[Theodore W. Richards House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses completed in 1900, National Historic Landmarks in Massachusetts, Houses in Cambridge, Massachusetts, Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Massachusetts, Harvard Square, National Register of Historic Places in Middlesex County, Massachusetts

The Theodore W. Richards House is a National Historic Landmark at 15 Follen Street in Cambridge, Massachusetts.
The house was built in 1900 by Warren, Smith & Biscoe and was the home of Harvard University chemist and 1914 Nobel Prize winner, Theodore William Richards. The house was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1976,NHL Listing information and included in the Follen Street Historic District in 1986.[tpl]cite web|url=http://mhc-macris.net/Details.aspx?MhcId=CAM.151|title=MACRIS inventory record for Theodore W.
