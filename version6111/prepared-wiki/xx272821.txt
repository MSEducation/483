[[Hylarana spinulosa]]

CATEGORIES: Hylarana, Amphibians of China, Endemic fauna of Hainan

Hylarana spinulosa (common names: spiny frog, fine-spined frog) is a species of true frog in the genus Hylarana. It is native to Hainan, southern China.[tpl]cite web |url=http://research.amnh.org/vz/herpetology/amphibia/Amphibia/Anura/Ranidae/Hylarana/Hylarana-spinulosa |title=Hylarana spinulosa (Smith, 1923) |author=Frost, Darrel R. |year=2014 |work=Amphibian Species of the World: an Online Reference. Version 6.0 |publisher=American Museum of Natural History |accessdate=26 March 2014[/tpl] Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical moist lowland forests, rivers, freshwater marshes, and intermittent freshwater marshes.
