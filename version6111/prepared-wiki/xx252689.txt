[[Line 14 (Stockholm metro)]]

CATEGORIES: Stockholm metro

[tpl]unreferenced|date=January 2014[/tpl]Line 14 (T14) is one of the two red lines of the Stockholm Metro connecting Fruängen and Mörby centrum. It is a 19.5 km (12.1 mi) long line with a 33 min travel time and 19 stations.
