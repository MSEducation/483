[[Metro Constitución de 1917]]

CATEGORIES: Mexico City metro stations, Railway stations opened in 1994

Constitución de 1917 is a terminal station at the southeastern end of line 8 of the Mexico City Metro in Mexico City, Mexico.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.metro.df.gob.mx/red/estacion.html?id=115|title=Constitución de 1917|language=Spanish|accessdate=26 July 2011[/tpl]
[tpl]Cite web|last=Archambault|first=Richard|url=http://mexicometro.org/metro/line8/constitucion-de-1917/|title=Constitución de 1917 »  Mexico City Metro System|accessdate=26 July 2011[/tpl]
The logo of the station depicts a quill above a document dated 1917 and is intended to represent the Constitution of Mexico, which was adopted 5 February 1917.  The station was opened on 20 July 1994.
