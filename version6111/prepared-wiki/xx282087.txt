[[Hit Trésor SC (basketball)]]

CATEGORIES: Basketball teams in the Central African Republic

Hit Trésor is a professional basketball team that is based in the Central African Republic. The compete in the Central African League.

==History==

Hit Trésor won the FIBA Africa Clubs Champions Cup twice, in the years 1973 and 1976, and they competed at the 1975 edition of the Intercontinental Cup.
