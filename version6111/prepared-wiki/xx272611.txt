[[Hylarana montivaga]]

CATEGORIES: Hylarana, Amphibians of Thailand, Amphibians of Vietnam

Hylarana montivaga (sometimes known as Langbian Plateau frog or Chantaburi stream frog) is a species of true frog in the genus Hylarana. It is native to Thailand and Vietnam, and is likely to be present in Laos and Cambodia.[tpl]cite web |url=http://research.amnh.org/vz/herpetology/amphibia/?action=names&taxon=Hylarana+montivaga |title= Hylarana montivaga (Smith, 1921)|author=Frost, Darrel R. |date=2013 |work=Amphibian Species of the World 5.6, an Online Reference |publisher=American Museum of Natural History |accessdate=25 May 2013[/tpl] It is known from streams in evergreen forests.
