[[Andi Mappanyukki]]

CATEGORIES: Indonesian revolutionaries, National Heroes of Indonesia

Andi Mappanyukki, also spelt as Andi Mappanjukki,[tpl]cite book|author=Audrey Kahin|title=Regional dynamics of the Indonesian Revolution: unity from diversity|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=r3xuAAAAMAAJ|year=1985|publisher=University of Hawaii Press|isbn=978-0-8248-0982-9|page=235[/tpl] was an Indonesian rebel who fought against the Dutch from the 1920s to the 1930s. A National Hero of Indonesia, he was the father of Andi Abdullah Bau Massepe.
