[[Brandy Pond Seaplane Base]]

CATEGORIES: Seaplane bases in the United States, Airports in Cumberland County, Maine, Naples, Maine

Brandy Pond Seaplane Base [tpl]airport codes|||5ME[tpl]nobold|, formerly[/tpl] 06ME[/tpl] is a privately owned, public-use seaplane base located one nautical mile (2 km) southeast of the central business district of Naples, in Cumberland County, Maine, United States. It is on Brandy Pond, south of Long Lake and U.S. Route 302.Naples Seaplane Service: Find us

==Facilities and aircraft==

Brandy Pond Seaplane Base has one landing area designated 17W/35W and measuring 7,200 x 600 feet (2,195 x 183 m). For the 12-month period ending August 13, 2007, the airport had 2,000 general aviation aircraft operations, an average of 167 per month.
