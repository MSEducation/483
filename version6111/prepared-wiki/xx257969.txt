[[Nate Potter]]

CATEGORIES: 1988 births, Living people, All-American college football players, American football offensive tackles, American football offensive guards, Arizona Cardinals players, Boise State Broncos football players, Sportspeople from Boise, Idaho, Players of American football from Idaho

Nate Potter (born May 16, 1988) is an American football offensive tackle for the Arizona Cardinals of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football for Boise State University and was recognized as an All-American.  The Cardinals drafted him in the seventh round of the 2012 NFL Draft.

==Early years==

Potter was born in Denver, Colorado.  He attended Timberline High School in Boise and was a standout lineman for the Timberline Wolves high school football team.[tpl]cite news |title=Kellen Moore and Nate Potter Named Second-Team Academic All-Americans |url=http://www.broncosports.com/ViewArticle.dbml?DB_OEM_ID=9900&ATCLID=205036223 |work=broncosports.com |date=November 23, 2010 |accessdate=2010-12-30[/tpl]

==College career==

Potter attended Boise State University, where he played for the Boise State Broncos football team from 2008 to 2011.  He grayshirted and later redshirted in 2007.
