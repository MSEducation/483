[[Shimominochi District, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Districts in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Shimominochi|下水内郡|Shimominochi-gun[/tpl] is a district located in Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the district has an estimated population of 7,643 and a density of 24.96 persons per km². The total area is 306.25 km².
