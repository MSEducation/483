Ben Kamin
Ben Kamin is a nationally-known rabbi, teacher, counselor, and the prize-winning author of ten books on human values. www.benkamin.comHe is a scholar on Rev. Martin Luther King, Jr.  He has led congregations in Toronto, New York, Cleveland, and San Diego since his ordination in 1978. He has published over 1000 articles about community life in newspapers around the world, ranging from The New York Times to The International Herald Tribune.
