Alexander Vladimirovich Popov ([tpl]lang-ru|Александр Владимирович Попов[/tpl]; born February 22, 1965 in Tobolsk) is a Russian biathlete who competed for the USSR, the Unified Team and Belarus. Since 1999 he has been the Head Coach of the Belarus National Biathlon Team.
