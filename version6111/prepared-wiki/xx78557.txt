[[Kadogawa, Miyazaki]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Miyazaki Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Kadogawa|門川町|Kadogawa-chō[/tpl] is a town located in Higashiusuki District, Miyazaki Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the town has an estimated population of 19,293 and the density of 160.13 persons per km². The total area is 120.48 km².
