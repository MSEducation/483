[[Dave Freisleben]]

CATEGORIES: Major League Baseball pitchers, San Diego Padres players, Cleveland Indians players, Toronto Blue Jays players, Tri-City Padres players, Alexandria Aces players, Hawaii Islanders players, Baseball players from Pennsylvania, 1951 births, Living people

David James Freisleben (born October 31, 1951) is a retired professional baseball pitcher. He played all or part of six seasons in Major League Baseball, from 1974 to 1979, for the San Diego Padres, Cleveland Indians, and Toronto Blue Jays.
