[[Stephens, Kentucky]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Elliott County, Kentucky, Unincorporated communities in Kentucky

Stephens is an unincorporated community in Elliott County, Kentucky, United States.  It lies along Routes 409 and 486 northeast of the city of Sandy Hook, the county seat of Elliott County.Rand McNally.  The Road Atlas '06. Chicago: Rand McNally, 2006, p. 43.  Its elevation is 628 feet (208 m).[tpl]gnis|509125[/tpl], Geographic Names Information System, 1979-09-20.  Accessed 2008-01-03.
