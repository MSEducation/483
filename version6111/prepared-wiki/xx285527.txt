[[Viper Recordings]]

CATEGORIES: English record labels, Electronic music record labels

Viper Recordings is a Liverpool drum and bass record label created by Futurebound and Jaquan in 2003. Currently Viper Recordings roster of artists includes: Matrix & Futurebound,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.viperrecordings.co.uk/artists|title=Artists|publisher=Viper Recordings|accessdate=15 August 2013[/tpl] ShockOne, Metrik, DC Breaks, Camo & Krooked, Furlonge (whose song, "Drifter", made #56 on the UK Singles Chart[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.officialcharts.com/artist/_/brookes%20brothers/|title=BROOKES BROTHERS|publisher=The Official Charts Company|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] after being as the B-Side to the Brookes Brothers song Tear You Down[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.discogs.com/Brookes-Brothers-The-Tear-You-Down-Drifter/release/1516529|title=Brookes Brothers, The* - Tear You Down / Drifter (Vinyl)|publisher=Discogs|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl]) and more recently the Brookes Brothers, whose single Carry Me On debuted on the UK Singles Chart at #71 on Sunday, 11 August 2013[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.officialcharts.com/singles-chart/|title=Official UK Singles Top 100|publisher=The Official Charts Company|accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl] after being touted as Zane Lowe's Hottest Record in the World.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/p019gjv0|title=Zane Lowe, Zane Lowe Vs Wretch 32, Brookes Brothers - Carry Me On Ft.
