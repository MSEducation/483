[[Ouffet]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Liège (province)

Ouffet is a municipality of Belgium. It lies in the country's Walloon Region and Province of Liege. On January 1, 2006 Ouffet had a total population of 2,529. The total area is 40.22 km² which gives a population density of 63 inhabitants per km².
