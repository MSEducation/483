[[David Payton]]

CATEGORIES: 1952 births, Living people, Administrators of Tokelau, New Zealand diplomats, Ambassadors of New Zealand to Saudi Arabia, Ambassadors of New Zealand to the Netherlands

David Bruce Payton (born 1952) was the Administrator of Tokelau from 17 October 2006 to 2009.[tpl]cite web|title=Pacific |url=http://www.mfat.govt.nz/Foreign-Relations/Pacific/Tokelau/index.php |publisher=New Zealand Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Trade |accessdate=7 September 2010 [/tpl] 
He was previously Ambassador to Saudi Arabia (1997–2000) and then Ambassador to the Netherlands (2002–2006)
