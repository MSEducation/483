[[Saint-Paul-de-Fenouillet]]

CATEGORIES: Communes of Pyrénées-Orientales

Saint-Paul-de-Fenouillet ([tpl]Lang-oc|Sant Pau de Fenolhet[/tpl]) is a commune in the Pyrénées-Orientales department in southern France.

==Twin towns==

It is twinned with the town of Ennis in the Republic of Ireland.Section 15 Freedom of Information Manual for Ennis Town Council (pdf format)
