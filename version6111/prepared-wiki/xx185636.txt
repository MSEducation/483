==Biography==

Davis was the son of stockbroker John E. Davis and Maude Bouvier, younger sister of John Vernou Bouvier III and therefore first cousin of Jacqueline Kennedy Onassis and Lee Radziwill. He was a graduate of Princeton University. He also studied in Italy and served as a naval officer with the Sixth Fleet in the Mediterranean.
