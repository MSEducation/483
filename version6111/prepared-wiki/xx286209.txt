[[Sarah Wylie]]

CATEGORIES: Canadian women novelists, Canadian children's writers, 1989 births, Living people, 21st-century Canadian novelists

Sarah Wylie (born 1989) is a Canadian writer of young adult novels. Her first novel, All These Lives, was published in 2012. Her next novel, another stand-alone contemporary young adult novel, will be published in 2014.[tpl]cite web|last=McAndrews|first=Jennifer|title=Welcome Wednesday: ALL THESE LIVES author Sarah Wylie!|url=http://www.honestlyya.com/2013/08/welcome-wednesday-all-these-lives.html|work=Honestly YA|accessdate=12 August 2013|date=7 August 2013[/tpl]

==Biography==

Sarah Wylie is the Canadian author of young-adult novels, best known for her debut novel All These Lives. Having lived in Africa and Australia as a child, Wylie graduated college in 2011 with a degree in Neuroscience.[tpl]cite web|title=About|url=http://sarahwyliebooks.com/about/|accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Sarah Wylie|url=http://us.macmillan.com/author/sarahwylie|accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl] She currently resides in Alberta, Canada.[tpl]cite web|last=Schick|first=Michal|title=Hypable Exclusive Author Interview: Sarah Wylie|url=http://www.hypable.com/2013/01/11/hypable-exclusive-author-interview-sarah-wylie/|work=Hypable|accessdate=12 August 2013|date=11 January 2013[/tpl]
