[[Rudolf Cranz]]

CATEGORIES: 1918 births, 1941 deaths, Olympic alpine skiers of Germany, Alpine skiers at the 1936 Winter Olympics, German military personnel killed in World War II

Heinz-Rudolf "Rudi" Cranz (September 2, 1918 – June 22, 1941) was a German alpine skier who competed in the 1936 Winter Olympics.
He was born in Uccle, Belgium and died in Różaniec, Przemyśl Voivodeship. He was the younger brother of Christl Cranz.
In 1936 he finished sixth in the alpine skiing combined event.
He died on his first day of battle on the Eastern Front, where he served as a corporal in an Alpine division.[tpl]cite news|title=Rudi Cranz falt|date=1 July 2011|work=Morgenbladet|page=44|language=Norwegian|accessdate=8 July 2011[/tpl]
