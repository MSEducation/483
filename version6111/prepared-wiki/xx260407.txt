[[Edward Coke, Viscount Coke]]

CATEGORIES: 1719 births, 1753 deaths, Members of the Parliament of Great Britain for English constituencies, Heirs apparent who never acceded, Coke family

Edward Coke, Viscount Coke (2 February 1719 – 31 August 1753), styled The Hon. Edward Coke from 1728 to 1744, was a British Member of Parliament.
He was the only child of Thomas Coke, 1st Earl of Leicester and his wife Margaret Coke, 19th Baroness de Clifford. He was married on 1 April 1747 to Lady Mary Campbell (6 February 1727 – 30 September 1811), daughter of John Campbell, 2nd Duke of Argyll; they had no children. 
He represented Norfolk in Parliament from 1741 to 1747 and Harwich from 1747 to his death.
