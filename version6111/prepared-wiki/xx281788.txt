[[M. V. P. Peiris]]

CATEGORIES: Sri Lankan politicians, Sri Lankan surgeons, Members of the Senate of Ceylon, Ambassadors of Sri Lanka to the Soviet Union, High Commissioners of Sri Lanka to the United Kingdom, Officers of the Order of the British Empire

M. V. P. Peiris, OBE was a Ceylonese surgeon and politician. He was Minster of Commerce and Trade and a member of the Senate of Ceylon. He also served as Ceylon's Ambassador to Soviet Union and High Commissioner to United Kingdom.The last hours of a great statesman
