[[Potter-Williams House (Davenport, Iowa)]]

CATEGORIES: Houses completed in 1872, Greek Revival architecture in Iowa, Houses in Davenport, Iowa, Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Iowa, National Register of Historic Places in Scott County, Iowa

The Potter-Williams House was located on the east side of Davenport, Iowa, United States.  The Greek Revival style residence was built in 1872.  It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places since 1984, an has subsequently been torn own.
