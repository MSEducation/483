[[Lulwoana]]

CATEGORIES: Fungi described in 2005, Sordariomycetes, Monotypic Sordariomycetes genera

Lulwoana is a genus of fungi within the Lulworthiaceae family.[tpl]cite journal |last=Lumbsch TH, Huhndorf SM. |date=December 2007 |title=Outline of Ascomycota – 2007 |journal=Myconet |volume=13 |issue= |pages=1–58 |id= |url=http://www.fieldmuseum.org/myconet/outline.asp |accessdate= |publisher=The Field Museum, Department of Botany, Chicago, USA [/tpl] This is a monotypic genus, containing the single species Lulwoana uniseptata.
