[[The Best of L'Arc-en-Ciel C/W]]

CATEGORIES: L'Arc-en-Ciel albums, B-side compilation albums, 2003 compilation albums, Albums certified gold by the Recording Industry Association of Japan, Ki/oon Records albums

The Best of L'Arc-en-Ciel C/W is a compilation album released by L'Arc-en-Ciel on March 19, 2003, simultaneously alongside The Best of L'Arc-en-Ciel 1994–1998 and The Best of L'Arc-en-Ciel 1998–2000. It collects previously released B-sides.
