[[Paolo Coloni]]

CATEGORIES: 1969 births, Living people, Italian racing drivers, Italian Formula Three Championship drivers, German Formula Three Championship drivers

Paolo Coloni (born May 24, 1969 in ??) is an Italian racing driver. He is currently team principal at Coloni Motorsport, a motor racing team founded in 1981 by his father, Enzo Coloni. He has competed in such series as the German Formula Three Championship and the Italian Formula Three Championship. He finished in second-place in the Masters of Formula 3 race of 1993.
