[[Crime Over London]]

CATEGORIES: 1936 films, 1930s crime films, British films, British crime films, English-language films, Films set in London, Films directed by Alfred Zeisler, Isleworth Studios films, Films based on novels

Crime Over London is a 1936 British crime film directed by Alfred Zeisler and starring Margot Grahame, Paul Cavanagh and David Burns. It was made at Isleworth Studios,Wood p.89 based on the novel House of a Thousand Windows by Ludwig von Wohl. The film's sets were designed by art director Edward Carrick.

==Plot==

With the police on their tail, a gang of New York criminals decided to relocate to London where they plan a major robbery on a department store.
