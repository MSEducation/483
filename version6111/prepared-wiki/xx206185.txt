[[Premier's Cup]]

CATEGORIES: Horse races in Australia, Open middle distance horse races, Sport in Brisbane

The Premier's Cup is a Brisbane Racing Club Group 3 Australian Thoroughbred horse race for horses more than aged three years old, over a distance of 2200 metres under handicap conditions held at Doomben Racecourse in Brisbane, Queensland in May.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.progroupracing.com.au/group-races/brisbane-racing-club/premiers-cup|title=Winners and Past Results for the Premier's Cup|accessdate=18 May 2013|publisher=Progroup Racing  (Australia)|date = 2012[/tpl] Total prizemoney is A$125,000 and a $1,100 trophy.

==Race history==

The inaugural running of the race was in 2006. The race is considered a major prep leadup race to the Brisbane Cup which is held several weeks after this race in June.
