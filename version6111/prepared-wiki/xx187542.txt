[[Harry Kuhl]]

CATEGORIES: 1903 births, Australian rules footballers from Victoria (Australia), St Kilda Football Club players, Dimboola Football Club players, Year of death missing

Harry Kuhl (born 7 March 1903, date of death unknown) was an Australian rules footballer who played with St Kilda in the Victorian Football League (VFL).
