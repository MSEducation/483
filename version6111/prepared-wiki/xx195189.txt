[[Laura Ritchie]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Academics of the University of Chichester, American cellists, 1973 births

Laura Ritchie (born 11 November 1973 in Roanoke[tpl]dn|date=March 2012[/tpl], USA) is a famous cellist and a senior lecturer in music at the University of Chichester, and coordinates the Instrumental and Vocal Teaching Programmes and leads the annual Cello Weekend.http://www.chiuni.ac.uk/music/LauraRitchie.cfm She is a member of the Brighton-based band The Mummers.http://www.myspace.com/themummers
