[[Rezaabad, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Rezaabad ([tpl]lang-fa|رضااباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Reẕāābād and Rez̧āābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795960|Rezaabad[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 543, in 125 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
