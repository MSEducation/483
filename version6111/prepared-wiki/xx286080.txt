[[Ernst Plener]]

CATEGORIES: 1919 births, 2007 deaths, Association football forwards, German footballers, Germany international footballers, People from Katowice, People from the Province of Upper Silesia

Ernst Plener (21 February 1919 – 16 March 2007) was a German international footballer who played for Vorwärts Gleiwitz and HSV Groß Born.
