[[Shunem]]

CATEGORIES: Hebrew Bible places

Shunem ([tpl]lang-he-n|שׁוּנֵם[/tpl]; Douay-Rheims: Sunam) is a small village mentioned in the Bible. It was located in the tribe of Issachar, to the north of Jezreel and south of Mount Gilboa [tpl]bibleverse||Joshua|19,18|JP[/tpl].  
Shunem is where:
Shunem is listed on the towns conquered by the Egyptian pharaohs Thutmose III and Shoshenk I.
Shunem is identified with the modern village of Sulam.John L. McKenzie, Dictionary of the Bible, Touchstone Press, 1965
