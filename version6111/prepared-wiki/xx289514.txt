[[Chet Baker Plays the Best of Lerner and Loewe]]

CATEGORIES: 1959 albums, Chet Baker albums, Riverside Records albums, Albums produced by Orrin Keepnews

Chet Baker Plays the Best of Lerner and Loewe is an album by trumpeter Chet Baker featuring show tunes by Lerner and Loewe which was recorded in 1959 and released on the Riverside label.Chet Baker discography accessed August 14, 2013 

==Reception==

Allmusic awarded the album with 3 stars stating "these readings are comparatively understated. That said, the timelessness of the melodies, coupled with the assembled backing aggregate, make Chet Baker Plays the Best of Lerner and Loewe (1959) a memorable concept album".Planer, L. Allmusic listing accessed August 14, 2013 

==Track listing==

All compositions by Alan Jay Lerner and Frederick Loewe
