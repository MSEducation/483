Gordon McCauley (born March 9, 1972) is a cycling competitor for New Zealand. The five time winner of the New Zealand National Road Championship, competed at the 2006 Commonwealth Games in Melbourne where he won a bronze medal in the Individual Time Trial.Profile at the New Zealand Olympic Committee website
