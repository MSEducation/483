[[Józef Pułaski]]

CATEGORIES: 1704 births, 1769 deaths, Polish nobility, Bar confederates, Radom confederates, Members of the Sejm of the Polish–Lithuanian Commonwealth, Pułaski family

Józef Pułaski of the house of Ślepowron (February 17, 1704 – February 1769) was a Polish noble, starost of Warka, deputy to Sejm, one of the creators and members of the Bar Confederation.http://www.muzeumpulaski.pl/html/kazimierz_pu3aski.htmlAntoni Lenkiewicz: Kazimierz Pułaski (1745-1779). Wrocław: Wydawnictwo Biuro Tłumaczeń, 2004, s. 147. ISBN 83-88826-26-3.
Father of Kazimierz Pułaski, Franciszek Ksawery Pułaski and Antoni Pułaski.
