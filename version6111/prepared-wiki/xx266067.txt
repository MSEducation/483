[[Virginia's Husband (1928 film)]]

CATEGORIES: 1928 films, 1920s comedy films, British films, British silent films, British comedy films, English-language films, Films directed by Harry Hughes, Films based on plays, Nettlefold Studios films

Virginia's Husband is a 1928 British silent comedy film directed by Harry Hughes and starring Mabel Poulton, Lilian Oldland and Patrick Aherne.http://ftvdb.bfi.org.uk/sift/title/56769 It was based on the play Virginia's Husband by Florence Kilpatrick. A woman enlists a man to pose as her husband to trick her aunt.
