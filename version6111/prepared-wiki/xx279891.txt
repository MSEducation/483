[[Porsa (river)]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of Finnmark, Kvalsund

Porsa is a river in the municipality of Kvalsund in Finnmark, Norway. The river drains and area of 85 square kilometers and debouches into the strait of Vargsundet.
