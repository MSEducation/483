[[Ingoldiomyces]]

CATEGORIES: Ustilaginomycotina, Monotypic Basidiomycota genera

Ingoldiomyces is a genus of fungi in the Tilletiaceae family. This is a monotypic genus, containing the single species Ingoldiomyces hyalosporus.[tpl]cite journal |author=Vánky K, Bauer R. |year=1996 |title=Ingoldiomyces, a new genus of Ustilaginales  |journal=Mycotaxon |volume=59 |issue= |pages=277–87[/tpl]
