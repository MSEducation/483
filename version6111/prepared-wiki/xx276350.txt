[[(41504) 2000 QY148]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 2000, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(41504) 2000 QY148 is an inner main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on August 29, 2000.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
