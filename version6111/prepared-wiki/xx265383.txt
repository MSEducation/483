[[Chácara Klabin (São Paulo Metro)]]

CATEGORIES: São Paulo Metro stations

Chácara Klabin is a station on Line 2 (Green) of the São Paulo Metro in the Vila Mariana district of São Paulo, Brazil.Information about Chácara Klabin on the São Paulo Metro website (Portuguese) The expansion of Line 5 (Lilac) will ultimately result in Chácara Klabin serving as the terminus for Line 5 (Lilac) (scheduled to open in 2013).
