[[Albert Achard]]

CATEGORIES: 1894 births, 1972 deaths, People from Briançon, French World War I flying aces, Recipients of the Croix de Guerre 1914–1918 (France)

Albert Achard (March 26, 1894 – August 21, 1972) was a World War I flying ace. Born in Briançon in France, Achard scored 1 victory as an observer and 4 victories as a pilot before being wounded in action on July 24, 1918.http://www.theaerodrome.com/aces/france/achard.html Retrieved August 1, 2006
