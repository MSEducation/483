[[NAIA independent schools]]

CATEGORIES: National Association of Intercollegiate Athletics

NAIA independent schools are 4-year institutional members of the NAIA that play independent of any formal conference affiliation.[tpl]cite web |title = NAIA Member Schools |url = http://www.naia.org/ViewArticle.dbml?DB_OEM_ID=27900&ATCLID=205322922 |publisher = NAIA |accessdate = 2012-08-24[/tpl]  Many NAIA independent schools are members of the Association of Independent Institutions (AII), which allows members to have a formal route to NAIA tournaments.
