[[WAGL]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in South Carolina, Lancaster County, South Carolina

WAGL (1560 AM) is a radio station broadcasting an oldies format. Licensed to Lancaster, South Carolina, USA.  The station is currently owned by B.L. "Len" Phillips, under the name Palmetto Broadcasting System, Inc.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=WAGL |title=WAGL Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
Because it shares the same frequency as "clear channel"station WQEW in New York City, WAGL only operates during the daytime hours.
Its transmitter site is located near the Catawba River, nearly 10 miles from its studio site at 101 N. Woodland Drive.
The call letters of WAGL stands for Weaving A Greater Lancaster.
