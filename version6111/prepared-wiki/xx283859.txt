[[Sacha Prechal]]

Alexandra 'Sacha' Prechal (born 15 March 1959 in Prague, Czechoslovakia) is a Dutch law professor and judge. She has been a judge at the European Court of Justice since 10 June 2010.

==Biography==

Prechal was born in 1959 in Prague.[tpl]cite web|author= |url=http://www.europa-nu.nl/id/vigtg42tisyw/a_sacha_prechal |title=Dr. A. (Sacha) Prechal |language=Dutch |publisher=Europa Nu |date= |accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] She studied law at the University of Groningen between 1977 and 1983 and received her title of Doctor of Laws at the University of Amsterdam in 1995. After receiving her initial law degree she started working as an academic lecturer of Law at Maastricht University until 1987. She then worked as a legal secretary at the Court of Justice of the European Communities between 1987 and 1991. She returned to the Netherlands to work as an academic lecturer at the Europe Institute of the Law faculty of the University of Amsterdam. After receiving her promotion in the same university in 1995 she moved to work as professor of European Law at Tilburg University. She held a position there from 1995 until 2003, when she moved to Utrecht University where she occupies the same position and is still currently employed.[tpl]cite web|author= |url=http://curia.europa.eu/jcms/jcms/Jo2_7026/ |title=Presentation of the Members |publisher=Court of Justice |date= |accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] In 2008 Prechal was made member of the Royal Netherlands Academy of Arts and Sciences.[tpl]cite web|author=|url=https://www.knaw.nl/nl/leden/leden/7969 |title=Sacha Prechal |publisher=Koninklijke Nederlandse Akademie van Wetenschappen |date= |accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] In 2010 Prechal was appointed as a judge of the European Court of Justice. She succeeded Christiaan Timmermans as the Dutch judge. Prechal was named as the most powerful Dutch woman in the field of Justice and Public Order in 2012 by Opzij magazine.[tpl]cite web|author= |url=http://www.opzij.nl/Nieuws-Opinie/Nieuws-Opinie-Artikel/Top100-2012-Justitie-openbare-orde.htm |title=Top-100 2012 Justitie & openbare orde |language=Dutch |publisher=Opzij Magazine |date=25 March 2013 |accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl]
