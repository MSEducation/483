[[Solar eclipse of August 29, 1867]]

CATEGORIES: 1867 in science, Solar eclipses

A total solar eclipse occurred on August 29, 1867. [tpl]Total solar eclipse summary[/tpl]
Totality occurred across central Argentina and the southern Atlantic ocean.

==Observations==

José J. Vergara and Luis Grosch observed the eclipse from a small hill close to Santiago.[tpl]cite journal | title = Beobachtung der Sonnenfinsterniss am 29. August 1867.| author = L. Grosch | journal = Astronomische Nachrichten | volume =73 | pages = 137–138 | bibcode      =  1869AN.....73..137G[/tpl]

==Related eclipses==
