[[Amadou Ali]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1943 births, Government ministers of Cameroon

Amadou Ali (born 1943) is a Cameroonian politician. Born in Kolofata, he was Minister of Justice since 2001 and has been a vice Prime Minister since 2004.
From 1974 to 1982 he served as Secretary General of the Ministry of Public Service. From August 24, 1985 to 1997 he served three terms as Secretary of State Defense.
