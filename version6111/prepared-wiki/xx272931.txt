[[Veterans League of Joint Council of Municipalities]]

CATEGORIES: Joint Council of Municipalities, Serbian minority institutions and organizations in Croatia, Football in Croatia, Football competitions in Croatia

The Veterans League of Joint Council of Municipalities ([tpl]lang-hr|Liga veterana ZVO-a[/tpl], [tpl]lang-sr-Cyrl|Лига ветерана ЗВО-а[/tpl]) is an amateur competitive football league based in eastern Croatia in the areas of the Joint Council of Municipalities in eastern Slavonia, Baranja and western Syrmia. League is not part of the Croatian football league system. The involved teams are those whose players are members of the Serbian minority or teams from the Serbian communities from this region.
