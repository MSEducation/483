[[Robert Thomas Miller]]

CATEGORIES: 1962 deaths, Mayors of Austin, Texas, Year of birth missing

Robert Thomas "Tom" Miller (September 21, 1893-April 30, 1962) was mayor of Austin, Texas for 22 years, from 1933 to 1949 and again from 1955 to 1961.Floylee Hunter Hemphill Goldberger, "MILLER, ROBERT THOMAS," Handbook of Texas Online, accessed May 01, 2014. Uploaded on June 15, 2010. Published by the Texas State Historical Association. The Tom Miller Dam is named after him.
