Teemu Lassila (born March 26, 1983) is a Finnish professional ice hockey goaltender who currently a free agent. He is participating at the 2011 IIHF World Championship as a member of the Finland men's national ice hockey team.
Lassila was selected by the Nashville Predators in the 4th round (117th overall) of the 2003 NHL Entry Draft, but he has never played in the National Hockey League. Lassila made his Avangard debut in a 5-1 defeat to SKA St. Petersburg.
