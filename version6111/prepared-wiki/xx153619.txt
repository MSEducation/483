[[Cute Princess]]

CATEGORIES: Malaysian musical groups, Musical groups established in 2005, Mandopop musical groups, Children's musical groups

Cute Princess ([tpl]zh|c= 巧千金[/tpl]) (formerly known as 小千金) is a 4 member girl group formed in 2005 by a Malaysian company called Wayang Tinggi(威扬)Their ages ranged from 4–5 years.  Their first public appearance was in Four Golden Princess's 11th Year Concert.  They released their first album 送你一个大年糕 (English Translation:Give You A Big New Year Cake) under the group name 小千金.  A fortune teller recommended their record company to change the group's name to 巧千金 for better luck and success.

==Career==

===2005-2011===

Their English name was tentatively called "Little Princess" or "Qiao Qian Jin" by fans until their company officially published their English group name as "Cute Princess" in their album.

===2013-present===

Another album in production by Cute Princesses was announced on June 24th by their official company Star Media. This album titled Beautiful Day (清新美丽) was later released https://www.facebook.com/StarmediaDistribution/posts/10151666898272179 On November 28th, their New 2014 Chinese New Year Album was released for pre-order. This album is titled Zhao Cai Jin Bao (招财进宝) and marks the first time only three members participate in a Chinese New Year Album, with Vicki not participating in this album, instead Angel Baby makes another guest appearance. The Pre-Release consists of the DVD+CD, signed poster, horse bookmark and horse red packet. http://starmedia.com.my/product_info.php?products_id=76
