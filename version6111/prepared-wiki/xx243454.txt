[[Andrew Farrance]]

CATEGORIES: 1972 births, Australian canoeists, Canoeists at the 2000 Summer Olympics, Living people, Olympic canoeists of Australia

Andrew Farrance (born 14 September 1972 in Melbourne) is an Australian slalom canoer who competed from the early 1990s to the mid-2000s. He finished 11th in the C-2 event at the 2000 Summer Olympics in Sydney.
