[[Eddie Ahern]]

CATEGORIES: 1977 births, Living people, Irish jockeys

Eddie Ahern (born 6 December 1977) is an Irish professional jockey.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.eddieahern.co.uk/|publisher=Eddieahern.co.uk|title=Profile of Eddie Ahern|accessdate=9 January 2010|year=2010[/tpl]
In 1997 he became champion apprentice in Ireland.
[tpl]asof|2009|January|9[/tpl], Ahern has won 1084 races. In 2007 Ahern won the Hayes Golden Button Challenge.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.goldenbutton.co.uk/previouswinners.html|title=Golden Button Challenge 2009: Previous winners 2006/07|publisher=Goldenbutton.co.uk|accessdate=9 January 2010[/tpl] In December 2007 Ahern was banned from racing for 3 months for a whip offence.[tpl]cite web|last=Muscat|first=Julian|url=http://www.timesonline.co.uk/tol/sport/racing/article3080260.ece|title=Eddie Ahern Gets Three-Month Riding Suspension for ‘Deliberate’ Whip Offence|publisher=Times Online|date=21 December 2007|accessdate=9 January 2010[/tpl] In a race on 16 October 2008, Ahern was still in the corporate hospitality box in the stadium when the riders were due in for weighing and consequently he missed the race, and a chance to win £1,500.[tpl]cite web|last=Townend|first=Marcus|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/sport/othersports/article-1078610/Eddie-Aherns-right-Charlie-gets-boxed-Newmarket.html|title=Eddie Ahern's a Right Charlie as He Gets Boxed in at Newmarket|publisher=Daily Mail|date=17 October 2008|title=Ahern Misses Race|accessdate=9 January 2010[/tpl]

==Nickname==

Ahern was very popular with users of the betting exchange Betfair. In recent years his popularity gained him the nickname "Betfair Eddie" [tpl]http://community.betfair.com/horse_racing/go/thread/view/94102/29620437/betfair-eddie[/tpl] This was a tribute to the unique riding style and specialised tactics he had developed. He was particularly adept at deploying his skills on {Synthetic racetrack surfaces for horse racing}

==Ban==

In May, 2013, Ahern received a ten-year ban from the British Horseracing Authority for race fixing  between September 2010 and February 2011. He reportedly intends to appeal against both the Authority's findings and the ban's severity.[tpl]cite news|title=Eddie Ahern and Neil Clement receive lengthy BHA bans|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/horse-racing/22624365|accessdate=23 May 2013|newspaper=BBC Sport|date=23 May 2013[/tpl]
