[[Wattle Glen railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1912

Wattle Glen railway station is located on the Hurstbridge line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the north-eastern Melbourne suburb of Wattle Glen opening as Balee on 25 June 1912 being renamed Wattle Glen on 14 August 1922.Wattle Glen Vicsig

==Platforms & services==

Montmorency has one platform. It is serviced by Metro Trains' Hurstbridge line services.Hurstbridge Line timetable Public Transport Victoria
