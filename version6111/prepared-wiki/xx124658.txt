[[Brazos Country, Texas]]

CATEGORIES: Cities in Texas, Cities in Austin County, Texas, Greater Houston

Brazos Country is a city in Austin County, Texas. It is located south of Interstate 10 and [tpl]convert|7|mi|0[/tpl] southeast of Sealy. Downtown Houston is [tpl]convert|43|mi[/tpl] to the east. The eastern city limits of Brazos Country run along the west bank of the Brazos River. The only entrances to the city are from Chew Road. As of the 2010 census the population was 469.[tpl]cite web| url=http://factfinder2.census.gov/bkmk/table/1.0/en/DEC/10_DP/G001/1600000US4810090| title=Geographic Identifiers: 2010 Demographic Profile Data (G001): Brazos Country city, Texas| publisher=U.S. Census Bureau, American Factfinder| accessdate=August 26, 2013[/tpl]
Development of the area began in the late 1970s, and the city was incorporated in May 2000.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.census.gov/popest/geographic/boundary_changes/index.html | title = Boundary Changes | work = Geographic Change Notes: Texas | publisher = Population Division, United States Census Bureau | date = 2006-05-19 | accessdate = 2008-05-27[/tpl]Brazos Country, Texas - Handbook of Texas.
City government is by mayor and city council. The Sealy Independent School District serves area students.
Brazos Country encompasses the unique and popular River Ridge Golf Club which occupies the west bank of the Brazos River.
