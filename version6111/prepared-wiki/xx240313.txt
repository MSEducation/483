[[Welcome 2 Tahland]]

CATEGORIES: 2009 debut albums

Welcome 2 Tahland is the debut solo album by Tah Mac released on 30 March 2009 under his own label, "Tahmac Entertainment".

==Overview==

The album includes collaborations with some established as well as new names in music, including former Sugababes Mutya Buena, singer Nyah, the duo Shay & Trix as well as Tia Myrie.link BBC Album Review - Welcome 2 Tahland

==Critical reception==

Reception from the album has been mainly positive.
