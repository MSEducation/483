[[Alex Hughes (cricketer)]]

CATEGORIES: 1991 births, Living people, People from Wordsley, English cricketers, Staffordshire cricketers, Derbyshire cricketers

Alex Lloyd Hughes (born 29 September 1991) is an English cricketer. Hughes is a right-handed batsman who bowls right-arm medium pace. He was born in Wordsley, Staffordshire.
Hughes played two matches for Staffordshire in the 2009 Minor Counties Championship against Norfolk and Cumberland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/316/316408/Minor_Counties_Championship_Matches.html|title=Minor Counties Championship Matches played by Alex Hughes|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=26 September 2011[/tpl]  Having played for the Derbyshire Second XI since 2009,[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/316/316408/all_teams.html|title=Teams Alex Hughes played for|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=26 September 2011[/tpl] Hughes made his full debut for Derbyshire in a Twenty20 match against Yorkshire in the 2011 Friends Provident t20,.[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/316/316408/Twenty20_Matches.html|title=Twenty20 Matches played by Alex Hughes|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=26 September 2011[/tpl] scoring an unbeaten 11 runs and bowling three wicket-less overs.[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Scorecards/316/316273.html|title=Derbyshire v Yorkshire, 2011 Friends Provident t20|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=26 September 2011[/tpl] Hughes did not feature in any further matches for Derbyshire in the 2011 season.
