[[Cruise & Maritime Voyages]]

CATEGORIES: Cruise ships, Cruise ships of the United Kingdom

Cruise & Maritime Voyages is a passenger shipping company headquartered in Essex, United Kingdom.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cruiseandmaritime.com/contact-us/index.aspx |title=Cruise Line Website - Contact Us |publisher=Cruiseandmaritime.com |date= |accessdate=2011-01-24[/tpl]

==History==

Cruise & Maritime Voyages was formed in 2009, by parent organisation Cruise & Maritime Services International, after German based Transocean Tours (for whom they were the UK representative) filed for bankruptcy.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cruisecritic.co.uk/news/news.cfm?ID=3420 |title=Trans Ocean Files for Bankruptcy |publisher=Cruisecritic.co.uk |date=2009-09-04 |accessdate=2011-01-24[/tpl] 
Cruise & Maritime Voyages has positioned itself as "Providing ex-UK 'no fly' cruising holidays aboard smaller and medium sized classic and more traditional style ships".[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cruiseandmaritime.com/our-ship/introduction.aspx |title=About Us (Company Website) |publisher=Cruiseandmaritime.com |date= |accessdate=2011-01-24[/tpl] Cruise & Maritime Voyages serves an adult market, with an on board style of traditional entertainment, dining and rooms using a fleet of older tonnage.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.comparecarhire.co.uk/news/new-uk-cruise-liner-launched-5388069.html |title=New UK Cruise Liner Launched | Travel News |publisher=Comparecarhire.co.uk |date=2009-08-15 |accessdate=2011-01-24[/tpl]

==Fleet==

===MS Marco Polo===

In 2009 the veteran liner MS Marco Polo was chartered for cruising as the line's first ship.
The ship is operated as an adults only (16 and above) vessel, and is marketed at cash short consumers, with multiple departure points in the UK along with exotic itineraries.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.comparecarhire.co.uk/news/new-uk-cruise-liner-launched-5388069.html |title=New UK Liner |publisher=Comparecarhire.co.uk |date=2009-08-15 |accessdate=2011-01-24[/tpl] She is under a five-year charter [tpl]cite web|last=Ind |first=Cruise |url=http://cruiseind.wordpress.com/2009/08/13/new-cruise-line-formed/ |title=New Cruise Line Formed |publisher=Cruiseind.wordpress.com |date=2009-08-13 |accessdate=2011-01-24[/tpl] with the line, and was transferred in 2009 after previous operator, Transocean Tours filed for bankruptcy.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cruisecritic.co.uk/news/news.cfm?ID=3420 |title=Trans Ocean Files for Closure |publisher=Cruisecritic.co.uk |date=2009-09-04 |accessdate=2011-01-24[/tpl]

===MV Discovery===

Started in February 2013, the MV Discovery will sail for Cruise & Maritime Voyages in blocks of 249 days, running from May to October, following a joint agreement with All Leisure Group, she will also be replacing the semi-smaller Ocean Countess.http://www.cruiseindustrynews.com/cruise-news/7774-all-leisure-group-enters-agreement-to-jointly-market-discovery-with-cmv.htmlhttp://www.cruiseindustrynews.com/cruise-news/7821-discovery-to-join-cruise-and-maritime-after-drydock.html

===MS Astor===

Joining in November 2013, the MS Astor is sailing for Cruise & Maritime Voyages on a three-year charter, and will sail both northbound and southbound voyages, by way of South Africa, in addition to round-Australia http://maritimematters.com/2013/02/astor-heading-down-under-for-cmv/ and Asian itineraries.http://www.cruisecritic.co.uk/news/news.cfm?ID=5186
