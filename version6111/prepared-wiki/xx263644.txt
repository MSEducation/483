[[Christianity in Shandong]]

CATEGORIES: Shandong, Christianity in China

Christianity is a minority religion in Shandong province of China. Christianity in Henan, Christianity in Anhui and Christianity in Jiangsu are other major provincial Christian populations.Atlas of Global Christianity. 1910-2010. Edinburgh University Press, 2009, p. 137 
Cheeloo University is defunct. St. Michael's Cathedral, Qingdao and Sacred Heart Cathedral (Jinan) are churches of European style. Another European-style church is due to be built in Qufu, where Confucius originated.http://www.chinapost.com.tw/china/national-news/2010/12/26/285130/Planned-church.htm This idea was or is opposed by Confucianist groups.http://www.chinapost.com.tw/china/national-news/2010/12/26/285130/Planned-church.htm 
Shandong has persecution of Christians.http://www.refdag.nl/nieuws/china_arresteert_nog_steeds_christenen_1_301734
In 2007, 270 Christian leaders were arrested in Shandong at a raid.http://www.asianews.it/index.php?l=en&art=11008&size=A
