[[Nyasia]]

CATEGORIES: American female musicians, American freestyle musicians, American pop singers

Blanca Santiago Batista, known as Nyasia (born in West Palm Beach, Florida, USA) is an American singer of Freestyle music and dance-pop. Nyasia is best remembered for her single "Who's Got Your Love", which reached # 95 position on  Billboard Hot 100.http://www.allmusic.com/album/nyasia-mw0000236545/awards
