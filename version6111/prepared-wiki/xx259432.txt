[[Diwan-e-Ghalib]]

CATEGORIES: Poetry collections, Poetry by Mirza Ghalib

Diwan-e-Ghalib is famous poetry book written by a famous Persian and Urdu poet Mirza Asadullah Khan Ghalib. It is a collection of the Ghazals of Ghalib.http://books.google.co.in/books?id=wsxjAAAAMAAJ&q=biography+of+ghalib&dq=biography+of+ghalib&hl=en&sa=X&ei=5Kz0Ue-hDcWArgfNvYHwDg&ved=0CDcQ6AEwAQ Though it does not include all his Ghazals as he was too choosy to include all of his Ghazals, still in many other copies of diwan e Ghalib the Urdu scholars have tried to collect all his precious works. A lot of authentic copies of diwan e ghalib exist like Nuskha e Nizami, Nuskha e Arshi, Nuskha e Hamidiya(Bhopal), Nuskha az Ghulam rasool Mehr.http://karwan-e-adab.website.org/downloads/Deewan%20e%20Ghalib.pdf

==Synopsis==

Diwan-e-Ghalib includes around 200 Ghazals and the original copy had fewer Ghazals than this. The researchers included his other Ghazals, as and when found, after the death of Ghalib. The Ghazals are written in Reekhta the then spoken Urdu language.http://books.google.co.in/books?id=fcvxUKpliocC&printsec=frontcover&dq=biography+of+ghalib&hl=en&sa=X&ei=5Kz0Ue-hDcWArgfNvYHwDg&ved=0CDsQ6AEwAg#v=onepage&q&f=false
