[[Creditinform]]

CATEGORIES: Business services companies of Norway, Credit rating agencies, Companies based in Oslo

Creditinform AS is a Norwegian credit rating agency, and subsidiary of Experian. 

==History==

The company was started in Brønnøysund, as a commercial venture related to the establishment of the Brønnøysund Register Centre. In 1997 the company was bought by Blom, who sold it to Experian in 2000. this is provided by Capital one credit cards for a fee of $8.99
