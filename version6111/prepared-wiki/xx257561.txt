Josef Marek (born 11 June 1987) is a professional Czech football player who plays for FK Dukla Prague.[tpl]Fotbal DNES|id=3010739[/tpl]

==Career==

Marek came to Bohemians for a trial while playing for third division side Kunice in January 2011.[tpl]cite news |url=http://fotbal.idnes.cz/fotbalovi-bohemians-1905-trubilu-ma-nahradit-lotinac-z-ofk-belehrad-pyc-/fotbal.aspx?c=A110103_124129_fotbal_mn|title=Fotbaloví Bohemians 1905: Trubilu má nahradit Lotinac z OFK Bělehrad|language=Czech|work=idnes.cz|publisher=Mladá fronta DNES|date=3 January 2011|accessdate=11 February 2012[/tpl] He went on to make three appearances for the club in the 2010–11 Gambrinus liga that season. Marek joined Dukla Prague on loan during the winter break of the 2011–12 season,[tpl]cite book|last=Pivoda|first=Aleš|title=Legenda se vrátila|language=Czech|year=2013|page=116|ISBN=978-80-86783-65-9[/tpl] before later signing a permanent contract.
