[[Itakura Tōyōdai-mae Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Gunma Prefecture, Stations of Tobu Railway, Railway stations opened in 1997, 1997 establishments in Japan

[tpl]Nihongo|Itakura Tōyōdai-mae Station|板倉東洋大前駅|Itakura Tōyōdai-mae-eki[/tpl] is a railway station on the Tobu Nikko Line in Itakura, Gunma, Japan, operated by the private railway operator Tobu Railway.

==Lines==

Itakura Tōyōdai-mae Station is served by Tobu Nikko Line, and is 25.6 km from the starting point of the line at [tpl]Ja-stalink|Tōbu-Dōbutsu-Kōen[/tpl].

==Passenger statistics==

In fiscal 2011, the station was used by an average of 3,337 passengers daily.
