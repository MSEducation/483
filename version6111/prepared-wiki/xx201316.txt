[[Cláudio Ulpiano]]

CATEGORIES: Brazilian philosophers, 1932 births, 1999 deaths

Cláudio Ulpiano (14 November 1932 – 4 January 1999) was a Brazilian philosopher and expert on the work of Gilles Deleuze.
