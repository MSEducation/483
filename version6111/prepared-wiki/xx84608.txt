[[Xenos Books]]

CATEGORIES: Book publishing companies based in California, Companies based in Riverside, California, Media in Riverside, California, Publishing companies established in 1985

Xenos Books is a publishing company in Riverside, California that was founded in 1985 by Karl Kvitko and Verona Weiss. The company is known for publishing bilingual books, and modern American and foreign writers in translation.http://portal.unesco.org/culture/en/ev.php-URL_ID=3898&URL_DO=DO_PRINTPAGE&URL_SECTION=201.html
