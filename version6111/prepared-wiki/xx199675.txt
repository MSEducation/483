[[Isle of Wight Coastal Path]]

CATEGORIES: Long-distance footpaths in England, Geography of the Isle of Wight, Coastal paths in England, Transport on the Isle of Wight

The Isle of Wight Coastal Path (or Coastal Footpath) is a circular long-distance footpath of 67 miles (107 km) around the Isle of Wight, UK. It follows public footpaths and minor lanes, with some sections along roads.

==History==

==Route==

The path is waymarked in both directions and can be started at any point, but is described here clockwise from the pier at Ryde ([tpl]gbmapping|SZ594929[/tpl]).
