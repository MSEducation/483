Kisean Anderson (born February 3, 1990), better known by his stage name Sean Kingston, is a Jamaican-American rapper, singer and songwriter. He pursued a music career and debuted in 2007 with the album Sean Kingston.

==Early life and education==

Kingston was born on February 3, 1990, in Miami, Florida, and moved to Kingston, Jamaica, when he was six. ==Career==

===2007–08: Sean Kingston===

Sean Kingston was discovered on Myspace by Tommy Rotem at Beluga Heights, and signed to the label in a partnership deal with Sony.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.hitquarters.com/index.php3?page=intrview/opar/intrview_JR_Rotem_Interview.html |title=Interview With JR Rotem |publisher=HitQuarters|date=April 4, 2010 |accessdate=April 8, 2010[/tpl] In an interview with HitQuarters label head and producer J.R. Rotem described this process with regards to Kingston:
 "Sean Kingston was a rapper when we found him and it was a development process to get him more melodic. At Beluga we essentially refine the talent so that it's more of a marketable product." 
In a venture between Epic Records and Koch Records, Kingston recorded and released the single "Beautiful Girls" in May 2007.[tpl]cite news|last=Garrity|first=Brian|url=http://www.nypost.com/seven/08122007/business/no_rap_turf_war_here_business_.htm|title=No Rap Turf War Here|work=New York Post|date=August 12, 2007|accessdate=September 24, 2007[/tpl] The single, based on the bass line and lyrical "association" of the 1961 hit "Stand by Me" by Ben E. King, reached number one on the US Billboard Hot 100 for three weeks and topped the UK Singles Chart along with many other international charts. Similarly, the song "Me Love" samples the main hook out Led Zeppelin's "D'yer Mak'er", from their 1973 album Houses of the Holy. The song also debuted at number one in Australia, where it knocked off Fergie's "Big Girls Don't Cry", which had been at number one for nine weeks. The song stayed on the Canadian Hot 100 number one spot for over six weeks before getting knocked down to third place by the Plain White T's. "Beautiful Girls" remained at number one in Canada longer than any other song in 2007. In 2007, Kingston was the opening act for Gwen Stefani's The Sweet Escape Tour and for select dates on Beyoncé's The Beyoncé Experience Tour. In 2008, he was one of the opening acts for the Australian leg of Kelly Clarkson's My December Tour.[tpl]cite web|author= |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6j98t4ZXw54 |title=Sean Kingston At Rod Laver Arena Melbourne |publisher=YouTube |date= |accessdate=2012-01-03[/tpl] Since Sean is a Jamaican singer, he got his stage name "Kingston" from the capital of Jamaica called Kingston, Jamaica.

==Personal life==

===Activism===

Kingston filmed a public service announcement with Do Something to encourage teens to become active in their communities by forming a Do Something club.[tpl]cite web|title=Start a Do Something Club - Sean Kingston|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sVXiPpOsZco|publisher=Youtube[/tpl] In 2010, he appeared with one of his dogs in an ad for PETA, encouraging people not to chain their dogs outside."Sean Kingston: Chill With Your Dogs," ABS-CBNnews.com, 15 September 2010.

===Jet skiing accident===

On May 29, 2011, Kingston was involved in a jet skiing accident in Miami Beach, Florida. He was with a female passenger, who was severely injured when their jet ski struck the bridge connecting Palm Island with the MacArthur Causeway at approximately 6 pm. Kingston was taken to Jackson Memorial Hospital in Miami, where he was placed on the standard medical/surgical care unit, but had to be transferred to the intensive care unit.Sean Kingston Hospitalized After Jet Ski Accident – ABC NewsSean Kingston in Jet Ski Accident : People.com He suffered a fractured wrist and broken jaw along with water in the lungs, but he later made a full recovery.
