[[List of books banned in India]]

CATEGORIES: Censorship in India, Book censorship by country, India-related lists

This is a list of books or any specific textual material that have been or are banned in India or parts of India.

==Nationwide==

This section lists books that are banned or once faced a nationwide ban in India (including in British India). 

==Regional==

This section lists books that were banned by a state government. The Section 95 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 allows the state governments to declare any publication as forfeit.[tpl]cite web|title=Criminal Procedure Code, 1973|url=http://www.mha.nic.in/pdfs/ccp1973.pdf|publisher=Ministry of Home Affairs (India)|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl]   

==Other books challenged but not banned==

This section lists books that have been legally challenged to impose a ban or to exclude from a syllabus but have not been banned.
