[[Swaziland National Trust Commission]]

CATEGORIES: Government ministries of Swaziland

The Swaziland National Trust Commission (SNTC) is the custodian of Swaziland's cultural and natural heritage. It is governed by The National Trust Commission Act of 1972.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.sntc.org.sz/legislation/sntcact5.asp |title=The National Trust Commission Act, 1972 |publisher=Swaziland National Trust Commission |accessdate=5 May 2012[/tpl] It is a parastatal of the Ministry of Tourism and Environmental Affairs.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.gov.sz/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=260&Itemid=278 |title=Ministry of Tourism and Environmental Affairs - Departments and Parastatals |publisher=The Government of the Kingdom of Swaziland |accessdate=5 May 2012[/tpl]

==Nature==

It is responsible for Hawane Nature Reserve, Malolotja Nature Reserve, Mantenga Nature Reserve and Mlawula Nature Reserve.

==Culture==

It operates the Swaziland National Museum and Mantenga Swazi Cultural Village.

==National Monuments==

Three sites have been proclaimed National Monuments and a number of further sites are being investigated with a view to future proclamation.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.sntc.org.sz/cultural/monuments.asp |title=Cultural Resources: National Monuments |publisher=Swaziland National Trust Commission |accessdate=5 May 2012[/tpl]
