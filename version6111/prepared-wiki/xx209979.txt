[[Norway at the 2008 Summer Paralympics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 2008 Summer Paralympics, Norway at the Paralympics, 2008 in Norwegian sport

Norway sent a delegation of 24 competitors, (14 men and 10 women,) to compete at the 2008 Summer Paralympics in Beijing, from September 6 to September 17, 2008.[tpl]no icon[/tpl] De siste Paralympics ut√∏verne tatt ut, Olympiatoppen, retrieved 1 August 2008
