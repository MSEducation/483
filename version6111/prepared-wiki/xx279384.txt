[[Calendula Requiem]]

CATEGORIES: Debut singles, 2010 singles

[tpl]Nihongo|"Calendula Requiem"|カレンデュラ レクイエム[/tpl] is the first single from singer and cellist Kanon Wakeshima and An Cafe bassist Kanon's duo group Kanon x Kanon, and Wakeshima's third overall single.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.sonymusic.co.jp/Music/Arch/DF/kanon_kanon/index.html |title=kanonxkanon |publisher=Sony Music Online |language=Japanese |accessdate=2010-09-06 [/tpl] The song was used as the second theme song for the anime adaptation of Shiki.
