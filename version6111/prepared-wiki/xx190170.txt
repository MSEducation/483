[[Alton Bay Seaplane Base]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in New Hampshire, Seaplane bases in the United States, Transportation in Belknap County, New Hampshire, Buildings and structures in Belknap County, New Hampshire

Alton Bay Seaplane Base [tpl]airport codes|||B18[/tpl] is a state-owned, public-use seaplane base located two nautical miles (4 km) north of the central business district of Alton Bay, in Belknap County, New Hampshire, United States.

==Facilities and aircraft==

Alton Bay Seaplane Base has one seaplane landing area designated 1/19 and measuring 2,600 x 100 feet (792 x 30 m). For the 12-month period ending December 31, 2006, the airport had 500 general aviation aircraft operations, an average of 42 per month.
