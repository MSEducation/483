[[Sture Allén]]

CATEGORIES: 1928 births, Living people, People from Gothenburg, Members of the Swedish Academy, Linguists from Sweden, University of Gothenburg faculty, Members of the Norwegian Academy of Science and Letters, Computational linguistics researchers

Sture Allén (born 31 December 1928 in Gothenburg) is a Swedish retired professor of computational linguistics at the University of Gothenburg, who was the permanent secretary of the Swedish Academy between 1986 and 1999. He was elected to chair 3 of the Swedish Academy in 1980.[tpl]cite web | last = Arnald | first = Jan | title = Chair no. 3 - Sture Allén | work = Svenska Akadmien | url = http://www.svenskaakademien.se/web/938e01b1-b318-4c23-ba05-954127697d2a.aspx | accessdate =21 January 2010 [/tpl] He is a member of the Norwegian Academy of Science and Letters.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.dnva.no/c26849/artikkel/vis.html?tid=40108|title=Gruppe 5: Filologi og språkvitenskap|publisher=Norwegian Academy of Science and Letters|language=Norwegian|accessdate=10 January 2011[/tpl]
