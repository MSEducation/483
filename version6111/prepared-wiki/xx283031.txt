[[Clevelandia ios]]

CATEGORIES: Gobiidae, Monotypic fish genera

Clevelandia ios, the Arrow goby, is a species of goby native to marine and brackish waters of the Pacific coast of North America from British Columbia to Baja California.  This species grows to a length of [tpl]convert|6.4|cm|in[/tpl] SL though most do not exceed [tpl]convert|4.2|cm|in[/tpl] TL.  This fish can also be found displayed in public aquaria.  This species is the only known member of its genus.[tpl]FishBase species |genus= Clevelandia|species= ios| month = June | year = 2013[/tpl]
