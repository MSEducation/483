[[Sanzhar Sultanov]]

CATEGORIES: 1988 births, Kazakhstani film directors, Kazakhstani film producers, Living people

Sanzhar Sultanov (born December 7, 1988) is a Kazakhstan-born film director, producer and screenwriter.http://www.imdb.com/name/nm3343487/

==Early life==

Sultanov was born in the small village of Korday, Kazakhstan. He grew up and studied in England, Canada and the USA.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.caravan.kz/article/274 |title=Газета "Караван" / Глазами казаха |publisher=Caravan.kz |date= |accessdate=2011-10-15[/tpl] In 2007, Sultanov moved to New York to study at the Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.liter.kz/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=4550&Itemid=6 |title=Общественно - политическая газета "Литер" - Не теряя корни |publisher=Liter.kz |date=2010-12-14 |accessdate=2011-10-15[/tpl]
