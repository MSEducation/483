[[H.L. Kapur]]

CATEGORIES: Nominated members of the Rajya Sabha, Lieutenant Governors of Delhi, Indian Air Force officers, People from Dehradun

H. L. Kapur is former Air Vice Marshal from the Indian Air Force and Lt. Governor of Delhi. He is a recipient of PVSM and AVSM. He was nominated as a member of Rajya Sabha in 1985.
