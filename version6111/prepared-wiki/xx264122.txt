[[United States Senate election in Delaware, 2012]]

CATEGORIES: United States Senate elections in Delaware, United States Senate elections, 2012, Delaware elections, 2012

The 2012 United States Senate election in Delaware took place on November 6, 2012, concurrently with the 2012 U.S. presidential election as well as other elections to the United States Senate and House of Representatives and various state and local elections. Incumbent Democratic U.S. Senator Tom Carper won re-election to a third term.
