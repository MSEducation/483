[[Astrid Gunhilde Karlsen]]

CATEGORIES: 1920 births, 2008 deaths, Deputy members of the Parliament of Norway, Labour Party (Norway) politicians, Aust-Agder politicians, Norwegian women in politics

Astrid Gunhilde Karlsen (17 October 1920 – 5 May 2008) was a Norwegian politician for the Labour Party.
She served as a deputy representative to the Parliament of Norway from Aust-Agder during the terms 1954–1957, 1973–1977 and 1977–1981. In total she met during 4 days of parliamentary session.[tpl]Stortingetbio|ASKA[/tpl]
