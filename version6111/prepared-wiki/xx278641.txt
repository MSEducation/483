[[Do Saran]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Do Saran ([tpl]lang-fa|دوسران[/tpl], also Romanized as Do Sarān, Dowsarān, and Dusaran)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3061568|Do Saran[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 146, in 35 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
