[[Kim Young-Mu]]

CATEGORIES: 1984 births, Living people, Association football goalkeepers, South Korean footballers, Daegu FC players, K League players

Kim Young-Mu (born September 3, 1984) is a South Korean football player who played for Daegu FC as a Goalkeeper.

==Club career==

Kim was drafted from Soongsil University for Daegu FC's 2007 season.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.kleague.com/record/record_player.aspx?team_id=K09&player_id=2007085 |title=K-League player profile |publisher=kleague.com[/tpl][tpl]cite news |url=http://www.footballsquads.co.uk/sthkorea/2007/kleague/daegu.htm |title=Daegu FC 2007 squad |publisher=footballsquads.co.uk[/tpl]   Although Baek Min-Cheol was the first choice keeper for Daegu FC, Kim would play three games in 2007, conceding 11 goals.  Kim remained on the roster for the 2008 season,[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.footballsquads.co.uk/sthkorea/2008/kleague/daegu.htm |title=Daegu FC 2008 squad |publisher=footballsquads.co.uk[/tpl]   but left the club without playing a game that season.
