[[Marco Aurelio Dias Ferreira]]

CATEGORIES: Mayors of places in Brazil, Living people

Marco Aurélio Dias Ferreira is a Brazilian politician in the states of Paraná and Minas Gerais.  Aurelio was mayor of Rio Novo, Minas Gerais[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.santosdumont.mg.gov.br/portal1/municipio/noticia.asp?iIdMun=100131708&iIdNoticia=93132|title=Equipes sandumonenses participam dos Jogos Escolares de Minas Gerais –JEMG/2008|work=Site Oficial de Santos Dumont|language=Portuguese|archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/60N6DG1NQ|archivedate=22 July 2011[/tpl] three times, in the years 1982 to 1988, 1992 to 1996 and 2004 to 2008.
