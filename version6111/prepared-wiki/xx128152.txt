[[Night Patrol]]

CATEGORIES: 1984 films, American films, 1980s comedy films, Films shot in Los Angeles, California

Night Patrol is a 1984 movie starring Linda Blair, Pat Paulsen, Andrew Dice Clay, Billy Barty, and Murray Langston.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.nytimes.com/1985/03/09/movies/the-screen-night-patrol.html|work=The New York Times|title=The Screen: 'Night Patrol'|author=Vincent Canby|date=March 9, 1985[/tpl]

==Plot==

A policeman who wishes to make it in show business moonlights as a stand-up comedian comic at night called The Unknown Comic who does his act with a paper bag over his head.
