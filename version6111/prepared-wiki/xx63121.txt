[[Wezep]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Gelderland

Wezep is a town in the Dutch province of Gelderland and it has about 12.000 inhabitants. It is located in the municipality of Oldebroek.ANWB Topografische Atlas Nederland, Topografische Dienst and ANWB, 2005.
Wezep has a railway station and a Municipal Website.
