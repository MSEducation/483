[[Kangasniemi]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Southern Savonia, Populated places established in 1867

Kangasniemi is a municipality of Finland. 
It is located in the province of Eastern Finland and is part of the Southern Savonia region. The municipality has a population of [tpl]Formatnum: [tpl]Infobox Finnish Municipality/population count|Kangasniemi[/tpl][/tpl] ([tpl]#time: j F Y|[tpl]Infobox Finnish Municipality/population count|sourcedate[/tpl] [/tpl]) and covers an area of [tpl]convert|[tpl]Infobox Finnish Municipality/total area|Kangasniemi[/tpl]|km2[/tpl] of which [tpl]convert|[tpl]Infobox Finnish Municipality/waters area|Kangasniemi[/tpl]|km2|abbr=on[/tpl] is water. The population density is [tpl]convert|[tpl]Infobox Finnish Municipality/population density|Kangasniemi|round=2[/tpl]|PD/km2[/tpl].
There are 3467 summer houses, and summer guests redouble the number of inhabitants during the summer months. There are more than [tpl]convert|1550|km[/tpl] of shoreline. The largest lakes are Puulavesi and Kyyvesi.
Kangasniemi is located on the Finnish national road 13, [tpl]convert|50|km|abbr=on[/tpl] northwest of Mikkeli and [tpl]convert|63|km|abbr=on[/tpl] southeast of Jyväskylä. Distance to the national capital, Helsinki is [tpl]convert|250|km|abbr=on[/tpl].
Kangasniemi has large forest resources, approximately [tpl]convert|12000000|m3[/tpl], and the yearly growth is approximately [tpl]convert|500000|m3|abbr=on[/tpl].
The municipality is unilingually Finnish.
The municipality was founded in 1867.
