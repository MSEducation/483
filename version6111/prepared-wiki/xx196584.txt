[[Super Show (album)]]

CATEGORIES: Super Junior albums, 2008 live albums, S.M. Entertainment live albums, Avex Taiwan albums

Super Show - Super Junior The 1st Asia Tour Concert Album is the first live album of  South Korean boy band Super Junior. It was released on 19 May 2008 by SM Entertainment. The songs in this album are direct recordings from their concerts at the Olympic Fencing Gymnasium in Seoul, South Korea from 22 to 24 February, the first stop of their Super Show. It was also released in Taiwan by Avex Taiwan on 27 June 2008."Super Show - Super Junior The 1st Asia Tour Concert Album info" Avex Taiwan. 27 June 2008. Retrieved 2012-03-20 [tpl]zh icon[/tpl]  It debuted at number one on Taiwan's G-Music Weekly G-Music J-Pop/K-Pop Chart with a percentage sales of 27.58%.
