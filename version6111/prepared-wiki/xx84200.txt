[[Shinano, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Shinano|信濃町|Shinano-machi[/tpl] is a town located in Kamiminochi District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2005, the town has an estimated population of 9,851 and a density of 65.99 persons per km². The total area is 149.27 km².
Lake Nojiri and Mount Kurohime are in Shinano.  Kobayashi Issa, a Haiku poet, was born and died here.

==Climate==

Hirosaki has a hot humid continental climate characterized by short warm to hot (and often humid) summers and cold winters with heavy snowfall.
