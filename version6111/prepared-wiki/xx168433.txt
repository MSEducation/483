Roman Rozna (born 25 March 1976 in Grigoriopol, Moldavian SSR) is a male hammer thrower from Moldova. His personal best throw is 76.62 metres, achieved in June 2003 in Minsk.
He finished twelfth at the 2001 Summer Universiade and seventh at the 2003 Summer Universiade. He also competed at the 2005 World Championships, the 2006 European Championships as well as the Olympic Games in 2000, 2004 and 2008 without reaching the final.[tpl]iaaf name|id=176701[/tpl]
