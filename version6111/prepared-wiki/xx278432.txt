[[Hayatullah Ansari]]

CATEGORIES: Nominated members of the Rajya Sabha, Uttar Pradesh MLCs, 1912 births, 1999 deaths, Recipients of the Sahitya Akademi Award

Dr. Hayatullah Ansari (1912-1999) was an Indian author and politician from Uttar Pradesh. 
As an author he wrote novels and short stories and was awarded the Sahitya Akademi Award in 1970. He was a member of the Uttar Pradesh Legislative Council (1952-66) and was nominated as member of the Rajya Sabha in 1966 and served till 1972.
