[[Sebastian Enterfeldt]]

CATEGORIES: 1988 births, Living people, Brynäs IF players, Luleå HF players, Swedish ice hockey players, People from Gävle

Sebastian Enterfeldt (born May 22, 1988) is a Swedish ice hockey player. He is currently playing with Brynäs IF of the Swedish Hockey League (SHL).
Enterfeldt made his Elitserien debut playing with Luleå HF during the 2010–11 Elitserien season.Lulea HF's 2010-11 Roster
