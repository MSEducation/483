[[Bar Building]]

CATEGORIES: Commercial buildings on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Commercial buildings completed in 1926, Buildings and structures in White Plains, New York

Bar Building is a historic commercial building located at White Plains, Westchester County, New York. It was built in 1926 and is a 10 story, 125 foot tall building.  Above the tenth floor is a two story, recessed penthouse.  The penthouse floors are enclosed with a crenellated parapet.  It is a steel frame building clad in face brick and cement with Neo Gothic / Art Deco terra cotta ornamentation.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=101968|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration:Bar Building|date=November 2006|accessdate=2010-12-30 |author=Peter D. Shaver|publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation[/tpl] See also: [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=101969|title=Accompanying eight photos[/tpl]
It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 2007.
