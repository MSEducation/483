[[Silent Descent]]

CATEGORIES: English melodic death metal musical groups, Industrial metal musical groups, Dartford (borough), Musical septets, Musical groups established in 2006

Silent Descent are a seven-piece trance metal band from Dartford, England.
Combining metal with trance and synth, they are viewed by many as an innovative band. Their sound can be described as a mixture of melodic death metal and trance music.
In 2007 the band entered the Radio global unsigned band awards, winning the "Symphonic rock or Metal" category, and selected by Judges to receive the "Supreme Silver" award (4th overall in the contest).[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.totalrock.com/awards_winners.php|title=Award Winners|publisher=TotalRock|accessdate=2008-05-22[/tpl]  The following year the band won the Kerrang!/MCN Unsigned Live competition .[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.kerrangradio.co.uk/article.asp?id=453821&spid=9697|title=Kerrang! Unsigned Live|publisher=Kerrang!|accessdate=2008-05-22[/tpl] In August 2008, Silent Descent were featured in metal and alternative music magazine Metal Hammer who proclaimed them "...the trance metal juggernaught the world has been waiting for." In September 2009 Silent Descent sold out the Camden Underworld along with Alestorm.
The band performed at the Bloodstock Open Air music festival  on 17 August 2008[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bloodstock.uk.com/bands-line-up.php|title=Bands Line Up (Open Air Festival) for the year 2008|publisher=Bloodstock Open Air|accessdate=2008-08-08[/tpl] and headlined Thursday night's Boardie Takeover at Download festival in 2009[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.downloadfestival.co.uk/news/article.aspx?AID=0ec279ee-3eef-48d3-893d-cf934d9c8640|title=Boardie Takeover â€“ the FULL review|publisher=Download Festival|accessdate=2009-10-05[/tpl] and 2010.
Mind Games was officially released in the UK on 7th May and is due to be released in Japan on 23rd May featuring 2 bonus tracks Call Me When You Get There & Wipe Your Chin And Walk Away. The album was tracked at Legacy London, featuring guest vocals from Official Sarah Jezebel Deva (Cradle of Filth), and mixed by Pontus Hjelm (Dead by April) with tracks 1 & 8 mixed by Hard Trance producer Alf Bamford (Technikal).
Silent Descent has been announced to play the Pepsi Max stage on Friday 8th June at the 2012 Download Festival
In 2013 their long-term musician Kipster left the band, they played with him at a farewell show at Leo's Red Lion in Gravesend, Kent.
