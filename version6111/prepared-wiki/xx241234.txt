[[Chahar Right Front Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia, Ulanqab

 
Chahar Right Front Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠴᠠᠬᠠᠷ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠪᠠᠷᠠᠭᠤᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠭᠠᠷᠤᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠡᠮᠦᠨᠡᠳᠦ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Čaqar Baraɣun Ɣarun Emünedü qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=察哈尔右翼前旗 |t=察哈爾右翼前旗 |p=Cháhā'ěr Yòuyì Qiánqí[/tpl]) is a Banner of Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China, surrounding Jining District and bordering Xinghe County to the east, Fengzhen City to the south, Zhuozi County to the west, and Chahar Right Back Banner to the north. It is under the administration of Ulaan Chab City.
