[[World of Wishes]]

CATEGORIES: Children's fiction books

World of Wishes is a children's book series, written by Carol Barton. The series revolves around a young girl named Madeleine "Maddie" O'Neill, who discovers the land of Zavania, a land where wishes come true. But wishes don't just happen; they have to be granted by somebody. Usually, two books are released per year.
