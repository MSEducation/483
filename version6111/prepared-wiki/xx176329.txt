[[WKSC (AM)]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in South Carolina, News and talk radio stations in the United States

WKSC (1300 AM) is a radio station licensed to Kershaw, South Carolina, USA.  The station is currently owned by Lucy Radio LLC.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=WKSC |title=WKSC Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division[/tpl]
The station was an affiliate of Scott Shannon's The True Oldies Channel from ABC Radio.[tpl]cite web |publisher=Scott Shannon's True Oldies Channel |title=Radio Stations |url=http://www.trueoldieschannel.com/html/stations.html |accessdate=December 19, 2008 |archiveurl = http://web.archive.org/web/20080728203304/http://www.trueoldieschannel.com/html/stations.html  |archivedate = July 28, 2008[/tpl]  It is no longer listed on the website. Affiliate list, retrieved November 26, 2013.
On May 17, 2010 WKSC returned to the air with talk.  On January 22, 2013, WKSC went silent.List of silent A.M. stations-November 15, 2013.  Retrieved November 26, 2013
