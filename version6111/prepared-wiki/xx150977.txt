[[Andrew Collins (actor)]]

Andrew Collins is a British actor, best known for playing recurring character Jarrett Maxwell in the CBS soap opera The Bold and the Beautiful as well as appearing on the first episode of Gilmore Girls.
He is a regular performer in Big Finish Productions, where he starred as Barnabas Collins in the Dark Shadows audio dramas,http://bigfinish.com/releases/v/kingdom-of-the-dead-box-set-75 various characters in Doctor Who,http://www.bigfinish.com/releases/v/live-34-240 Gallifrey, Earthsearch, and Stargate SG-1.http://www.bigfinish.com/releases/v/stargate-sg-1-series-3-part-two-772
In the UK, his stage name is Andy Coleman.http://www.hundredwattfilms.com/resident-artists/andrew-collins/

==Personal life==

Collins was born in the West Midlands, England and moved to America in the early 2000s. He is currently living in LA, and is married with one child.

==Career==

Andrew Collins graduated from Guildford School of Acting and has worked extensively in theatre, film and television both in the United States and the United Kingdom. He was the star of a Spanish national commercial, as well as several commercials for the UK, and also works as a voice-over artist.
Collins currently plays Jarrett Maxwell on the CBS soap opera The Bold and the Beautiful and has been seen on Gilmore Girls, Passions, The Young and the Restless, All My Children and the BBC’s Dangerfield, among others. He is a regular performer for Big Finish audio dramas, and in such capacity has played characters in Doctor Who, Gallifrey, Earthsearch, and Dark Shadows, from which he has earned a following as the new incarnation of Barnabas Collins.
As a writer/performer he was a leading light of the cult London satire show, Newsrevue, as well as several other shows including The Smiling Assassinshttp://www.uktw.co.uk/biog/Andy+Coleman and Lounge Lizards in Love.
