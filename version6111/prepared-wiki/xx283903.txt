[[Charles Henzen]]

CATEGORIES: 1945 births, Living people, Ice hockey players at the 1972 Winter Olympics, Ice hockey players at the 1976 Winter Olympics, Olympic ice hockey players of Switzerland, Swiss ice hockey players

Charles Henzen (born October 4, 1945 in Switzerland) is a former Swiss ice hockey player who played for the Switzerland men's national ice hockey team at the 1972 and 1976 Olympics.
