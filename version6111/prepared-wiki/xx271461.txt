[[Jim Nagle]]

Jim Nagle (born July 4, 1977) is an American professional ice hockey player who is currently playing in Germany with the EV Lindau in the Bayernliga.
In addition to playing professional ice hockey in Germany, Nagle has also played professionally in the United States in the ECHL and the United Hockey League, and also in The Netherlands where he played in the Dutch Eredivisie, the highest-level hockey division in the Netherlands.Landesverbände - Süd - Indians verpflichten Ausländer - Hockeyweb
