[[Jeff Staggs]]

CATEGORIES: San Diego Chargers (AFL) players, San Diego Chargers players, St. Louis Cardinals (football) players, San Diego State Aztecs football players, 1944 births, Living people, Point Loma High School alumni

Jeffrey Hugh Staggs was an American college and professional football player.  He played college football at San Diego State University, where he was a linebacker.  He played professionally in the American Football League and the National Football League for the San Diego Chargers and the St. Louis Cardinals.
