[[5762 Wänke]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Astronomical objects discovered in 1981

5762 Wänke is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1306.1161465 days (3.58 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=5762 | title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-05-23 | publisher = NASA[/tpl]
