[[Nur-Adad]]

CATEGORIES: Sumerian rulers, Amorite kings, 18th-century BC deaths, Year of birth unknown

Nur-Adad ruled the ancient Near East city-state
of Larsa from 1801 BC to 1785 BC. He was a contemporary of Sumu-la-El of Babylon.
