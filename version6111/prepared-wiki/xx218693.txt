[[Rocky Coppinger]]

CATEGORIES: 1974 births, Major League Baseball pitchers, Baseball players from Texas, Baltimore Orioles players, Milwaukee Brewers players, Living people, El Paso Diablos players, Sportspeople from El Paso, Texas

John Thomas "Rocky" Coppinger (March 19, 1974 in El Paso, Texas), is a retired Major League Baseball player who played pitcher from [tpl]By|1993[/tpl]-[tpl]By|2001[/tpl]. He played for the Baltimore Orioles and Milwaukee Brewers.  He attended high school at Coronado High School in El Paso, Texas and graduated in 1993.  He is also known for surrendering the final home run of Mark McGwire's career, #583.
