[[Stephen Hopgood]]

CATEGORIES: British political scientists, Academics of SOAS, University of London, 1965 births, Living people

Stephen Hopgood (born 1965) is a British political scientist and Reader in International Relations at the School of Oriental and African Studies in London. He is also the Co-Director of the Centre for the International Politics of Conflict, Rights and Justice.Centre for the International Politics of Conflict, Rights and Justice.
