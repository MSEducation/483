[[2009 Anugerah Bintang Popular]]

CATEGORIES: Malaysian music awards

2009 Bintang Popular Berita Harian Awards (2009 Most Popular Artiste Berita Harian Awards) was held on April 4, 2010 on Arena of Stars, Genting Highlands as an appreciation for artists and most entertaining artists in 2009. The show was broadcast live by TV3.
