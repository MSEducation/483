[[William Nichol (athlete)]]

CATEGORIES: 1901 births, Athletes (track and field) at the 1924 Summer Olympics, British athletes, British sprinters, Olympic athletes of Great Britain, Olympic silver medalists for Great Britain, Year of death missing, Olympic medalists in athletics (track and field), Male sprinters

William Peter Nichol (born 1901, date of death unknown) was a British athlete who competed mainly in the 100 metres.
