[[1939 Cincinnati Reds season]]

CATEGORIES: Cincinnati Reds seasons, 1939 Major League Baseball season, National League champion seasons

The 1939 Cincinnati Reds season was a season in American baseball. The team finished first in the National League, winning the pennant by 4Â½ games over the St. Louis Cardinals with a record of 97-57. The team went on to the 1939 World Series, which it lost in four straight games to the New York Yankees.
