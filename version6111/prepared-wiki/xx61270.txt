[[Pilanesberg International Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in South Africa, Transport in North West (South African province), Buildings and structures in North West (South African province)

Pilanesberg International Airport [tpl]Airport codes|NTY|FAPN[/tpl] is an airport serving Sun City in the North West province of South Africa. It is located adjacent to the Pilanesberg Game Reserve.

==Facilities==

The airport resides at an elevation of [tpl]convert|3412|ft|0[/tpl] above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 05/23 with an asphalt surface measuring [tpl]convert|2750|x|30|m|0[/tpl].

==Communication==

The Communication Frequency for Pilanesberg International Airport is 118.40 MHz.
Runway lights can be activated by making 3-7 clicks on 118.40 MHz.AVIMAP Frequency Chart September 2011, AVIMAP Frequency Chart link retrieved 23 December 2011
The control tower can be contacted on +27 (0)14 55 22154.
