[[Universidad Autónoma de Santa Ana]]

CATEGORIES: Universities in El Salvador, Educational institutions established in 1982

The Universidad Autónoma de Santa Ana is a university in Santa Ana, El Salvador.
The university was founded on April 26, 1982 by lawyer Mauricio Gutiérrez Castro and the doctor Hector Abel Tobar. 
The authority of the university lies with the Assembly of Governors (Asamblea de Gobernadores).
The university has 2 faculties, the Faculty of Health, and the Faculty of Science and Humanities.
