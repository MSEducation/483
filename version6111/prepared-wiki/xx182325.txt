[[KOPJ]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Minnesota

KOPJ is a Christian radio station licensed to Sebeka, Minnesota, broadcasting on 89.3 MHz FM.  The station serves the areas of Brainerd, Minnesota, Park Rapids, Minnesota, and Wadena, Minnesota.  KOPJ is an affiliate of LifeTalk Radio and is owned by We Have This Hope Christian Radio, Inc. KOPJ fcc.gov. Accessed November 23, 2012
KOPJ's programming includes Christian talk and teaching shows such as Back to Genesis by the Institute for Creation Research, Family Life Today with Dennis Rainey and Bob Lepine, Joni & Friends, and Unshackled!.  KOPJ also airs a variety of Christian music, as well as children's programs Adventures in Odyssey and Jonathan Park.Program Schedule Accessed November 23, 2012
We Have This Hope Christian Radio is also heard in Bemidji, Minnesota through a translator on 93.5 MHz FM.Local Christian Radio Stations in Northern Minnesota fcc.gov. Accessed November 23, 2012
