[[Hylarana novaeguineae]]

CATEGORIES: Hylarana, Amphibians of New Guinea, Amphibians of Indonesia, Amphibians of Papua New Guinea

Hylarana novaeguineae is a species of true frog in the genus Hylarana.[tpl]cite web |url=http://research.amnh.org/vz/herpetology/amphibia/?action=names&taxon=Hylarana+novaeguineae |title= Hylarana novaeguineae (van Kampen, 1909)|author=Frost, Darrel R. |date=2013 |work=Amphibian Species of the World 5.6, an Online Reference |publisher=American Museum of Natural History |accessdate=25 May 2013[/tpl] It is native to New Guinea where it is widespread in the lowland savannahs. It is also found in foothill rainforests.
