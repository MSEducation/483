[[Gmina Tułowice]]

CATEGORIES: Communes in Opole Voivodeship, Opole County

Commune Tułowice is a rural commune (administrative district) in Opole County, Opole Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Its seat is the village of Tułowice, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|22|km|mi|0[/tpl] south-west of the regional capital Opole.
The commune covers an area of [tpl]convert|81.13|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 5,504.

==Villages==

Commune Tułowice contains the villages and settlements of Goszczowice, Ligota Tułowicka, Skarbiszowice, Szydłów and Tułowice.

==Neighbouring communes==

Commune Tułowice is bordered by the communes of Dąbrowa, Komprachcice, Korfantów, Łambinowice, Niemodlin and Prószków.
