[[Connie Rasinski]]

CATEGORIES: 1907 births, 1965 deaths, Terrytoons, American film directors, American animators, Animation directors, Women animators

J Conrad (Connie) Rasinski (January 28, 1907 in Torrington, Connecticut - October 13, 1965 in Larchmont, New York) was an animation director who did the 1952 animated short "Hansel and Gretel"The enchanted screen: the unknown history of fairy-tale films by Jack Zipes, pgs 395-36 among others. Rasinski's "House of Hashimoto" was in competition at the 1961 Cannes Film Festival.Festival de Cannes page for 1961
