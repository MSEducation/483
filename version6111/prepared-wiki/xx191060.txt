[[Lincoln Council election, 2003]]

CATEGORIES: English local elections, 2003, Lincoln Council elections

Elections to Lincoln Council in Lincolnshire, England, were held on 1 May 2003. One third of the council was up for election and the Labour party stayed in overall control of the Council.
A trial took place for all postal voting leading to turnout increasing from 26% in 2002 to 47% in this election.[tpl]cite web| url = http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/england/lincolnshire/2994919.stm | title = Postal voting is election's big winner | accessdate = 2008-04-29 | date = 2003-05-02| publisher = BBC Online[/tpl]
