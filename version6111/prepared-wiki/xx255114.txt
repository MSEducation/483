[[Public Archives and Records Office (Prince Edward Island)]]

CATEGORIES: Prince Edward Island government departments and agencies, Archives in Canada, Official document archives

The Public Archives and Records Office is the official government archive of the Canadian province of Prince Edward Island. It is located in the Hon. George Coles Building, 175 Richmond Street, Charlottetown, Prince Edward Island, Canada.  
It includes resources for genealogy and archival collections. It is part of the Department of Communities, Cultural Affairs and Labour.
