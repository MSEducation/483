[[Barajally]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Gambia

 
Barajally   is a small town in central Gambia. It is located in Niani District in the Central River Division.  As of 2008, it has an estimated population of 878.World Gazetteer, Retrieved on August 20, 2008
It is the birthplace of President Dawda Jawara.
