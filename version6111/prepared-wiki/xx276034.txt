[[EDGE Tech]]

CATEGORIES: Personal computers, Personal computing, Companies established in 1986, Electronics companies of the United States, Manufacturing companies based in Oklahoma, Computer memory companies, Privately held companies based in Oklahoma, Articles created via the Article Wizard, Ada, Oklahoma, 1986 establishments in Oklahoma

EDGE Tech Corporation, is a US-based manufacturer and supplier of  computer memory  upgrades, portable external storage and flash devices. Edge Tech is headquartered in Ada, Oklahoma and maintains sales, marketing and eCommerce facilities in Addison, Texas.

==History==

Peripheral Outlet was formed in 1986 by 14 year-old, Jeff Thompson, with $2,500 he had saved from a newspaper route in Ada, Oklahoma. Thompson was named Young Entrepreneur of the Year in 1994 by the U.S. Small Business Administration, and profiled in Fortune magazine.[tpl]Cite news |title= Don't call me SLACKER! Other family members were employed in the business, including brother Ryan Thompson who later founded another company.[tpl]Cite news |title= Aria: Size is no limit on ideas |date= September 2, 2011 |author= Jessica Huseman |work= Dallas Business Journal |url= http://www.bizjournals.com/dallas/print-edition/2011/09/02/enterprisezone-aria.html |accessdate= October 10, 2011 [/tpl]
By 1996 the company's sales had increased by 650% and was named to the Inc. magazine 500 list of fastest growing private companies.[tpl]Cite news |title= The Inc. 500 Index: A ranking of the fastest-growing private companies in America |date= October 15, 1996 |work= Inc. Peripheral Outlet incorporated and became Peripheral Enhancements Corporation (PECO).
In 1998 PECO acquired the NewerRam division of Newer Technology and opened offices in Dallas, Texas.[tpl]Cite news |title= Peripheral Enhancements Corporation Announces Opening of Dallas Office |work= News release |publisher= Peripheral Enhancements Corporation |date= August 20, 1998 |url= http://www.thefreelibrary.com/Peripheral+Enhancements+Corporation+Announces+Opening+of+Dallas+Office-a021039772 |accessdate= October 10, 2011 [/tpl]
In 1999 PECO obtained ISO 9001 certification.
Peripheral Enhancements Corporation became EDGE Tech Corporation in 2004.
It moved to new office space in Ada in 2006.[tpl]Cite news |title= EDGE Tech relocates within Ada |work= The Ada News |location= Ada, Oklahoma |date= October 23, 2006 |url= http://theadanews.com/local/x212579033/EDGE-Tech-relocates-within-Ada/ |accessdate= October 10, 2011 [/tpl]
