[[Kremlin]]

CATEGORIES: Fortifications in Russia, Kremlins

A kremlin ([tpl]lang-rus|кремль|kreml|ˈkrʲemlʲ[/tpl], fortress; same root as in kremen ([tpl]lang-rus|кремень|kremen|krʲɪˈmenʲ[/tpl], flint)) is a major fortified central complex found in historic Russian cities. This word is often used to refer to the most famous one, the Moscow Kremlin, or metonymically to the government that is based there.
