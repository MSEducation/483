[[Matsuura Aya Best 1]]

CATEGORIES: 2005 greatest hits albums, Aya Matsuura albums, Albums certified gold by the Recording Industry Association of Japan

[tpl]Nihongo|Matsuura Aya Best 1|松浦亜弥ベスト１[/tpl] is Aya Matsuura's 'best of' album. It was released on March 24, 2005, and has sold 110,367 copies. Fans were invited to vote on which songs would appear on the album.  12 out of the 16 tracks are former singles. The album was certified gold by the Recording Industry Association of Japan.[tpl]Cite certification|region=Japan|certyear=2005|certmonth=03|title=松浦亜弥ベスト1|accessdate=2012-01-02[/tpl]
