[[Wise Group]]

CATEGORIES: Companies of Sweden, Service companies, High tech recruitment companies, Companies established in 1991

Wise Group AB (publ) is a Swedish corporation which owns, starts and develops companies specializing in recruitment, consultancy and other services within human resources. Wise subsidiaries services include recruitment, outplacement, career coaching, labor law, HR consulting, HR education, HR networking, employee and customer surveys and web-based HR tools. Wise has offices in Stockholm, Gothenburg, Malmoe, Lindkoping, Boras and Copenhagen.
The Wise share trades on the stock OMX First North Premier with Mangold Fondkommission as Cetified Adviser.
