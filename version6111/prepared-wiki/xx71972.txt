[[Secretary of State for Culture, Media and Sport]]

CATEGORIES: Lists of government ministers of the United Kingdom, British culture, Culture ministers, Current ministerial offices in the United Kingdom, Department for Culture, Media and Sport

Her Majesty's Principal Secretary of State for Culture, Media and Sport, informally known as the Culture Secretary, is a United Kingdom cabinet position with responsibility for the Department for Culture, Media and Sport. The role was created in 1992 by John Major as Secretary of State for National Heritage.
