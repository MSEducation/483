[[Baseball America Major League Player of the Year]]

CATEGORIES: Major League Baseball trophies and awards, Most Valuable Player awards, Awards established in 1998

The Baseball America Major League Player of the Year award is given each year by Baseball America to the best player—at any position—in Major League Baseball. (Baseball America does not have a Pitcher of the Year award.Baseball America 2009 Award Winners webpage. Baseball America, Inc. Retrieved 2010-12-30.) The award was first presented in 1998.
