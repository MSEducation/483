[[Darko Vukašinović]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 births, Living people, Sportspeople from Podgorica, Montenegrin footballers, FK Budućnost Podgorica players, PFC Slavia Sofia players, Bulgarian A Professional Football Group players, Expatriate footballers in Bulgaria, FK Zeta players, Association football defenders, Montenegrin expatriates in Bulgaria

Darko Vukašinović (Serbian Cyrillic: Дарко Вукашиновић, born 5 March 1985 in Titograd, now Podgorica) is a Montenegrin football defender. He currently plays for FK Lovćen.
He is a FK Budućnost Podgorica trainee; prior to his transfer to Slavia Sofia, he played for FK Zeta of Podgorica.  He moved to Slavia on a free transfer, as he had a contract with the FK Zeta, helping them to win the league.
He was part of the Serbia and Montenegro team that played at the 2002 UEFA European Under-17 Football Championship.
