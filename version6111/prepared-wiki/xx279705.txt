[[Ntala Skinner]]

CATEGORIES: Olympic biathletes of the United States, Biathletes at the 1998 Winter Olympics, Female biathletes, People from Sun Valley, Idaho, 1973 births, Living people

Ntala Skinner (born 22 February 1973) is a retired American biathlete from Sun Valley, Idaho.Biathlon World bio She competed for the United States at the 1998 Winter Olympics.AP via the Washington Post of February 18, 1998 In 1993 she was the only junior team member to make the U.S. squad for the 1993 Worlds.Chicago Tribune for February 2, 1993
