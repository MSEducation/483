[[Kaloneri]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Kozani (regional unit)

Kaloneri (previously Vrongista) is a village and the seat of the former Askio municipality in the Kozani regional unit, Greece. It is situated at an altitude of [tpl]convert|630|m|ft|abbr=on[/tpl] above sea level.  The 1991 population was 617.
