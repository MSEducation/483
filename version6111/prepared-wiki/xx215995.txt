[[List of Welsh Highland Railway Ltd rolling stock]]

CATEGORIES: Welsh Highland Railway, United Kingdom narrow gauge rolling stock

This is a list of the rolling stock of the Welsh Highland Railway Limited which is used to operate the Welsh Highland Heritage Railway, a [tpl]RailGauge|600mm[/tpl] narrow gauge heritage railway line in Porthmadog, North Wales.  The line opened in 1980.  The railway owns Russell - the only remaining original Welsh Highland locomotive, and a number of historically important coaches.
