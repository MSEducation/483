Gedalia Suchowolski ([tpl]lang-he|גדליה סוצ'וולסקי[/tpl], born 1928) is an Israeli painter and sculptor known by his nickname Sucho ([tpl]lang-he|סוצ'ו[/tpl]).

==Biography==

Gedalia Suchowolski was born in Białystok, Poland. He immigrated to Mandate Palestine in 1941 and studied construction engineering at Technion – Israel Institute of Technology in Haifa, Israel.
