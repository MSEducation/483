[[Lygodesmia]]

CATEGORIES: Asteraceae genera

Lygodesmia is a genus of flowering plants in the daisy family. The name derives from the Greek lygos, a pliant twig, and
desme, a bundle, because of the fascicled twiggy or rush-like stems of the plants.Saskatchewan Conservation Data Centre: Shinnersoseris rostrata, Plant Characterization Abstract.
