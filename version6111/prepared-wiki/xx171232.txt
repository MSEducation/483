László Zsidai (born 16 July 1986 in Budapest) is a Hungarian footballer playing for Debreceni VSC.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Hivatasos Labdarugok Szervezete|title=ZSIDAI LÁSZLÓ (magyar) részletes adatlapja|url=http://hlsz.hu/index.php?tid=7&Jatekos=hPLf_1bab35a|accessdate=2010-01-20[/tpl]
On 19th June, 2013 he signed for DVSC. He commended that he wants to win the Hungarian Championship and he didn't see chance for that with MTK. Szombathelyi Haladás also wanted to sign the centre defensive midfilder but he chose DVSC to reach his goal.
