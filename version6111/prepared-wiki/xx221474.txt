[[Toshi Hiruma]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, American film producers

Toshi Hiruma is a film producer, who has produced Batman: Gotham Knight, The Batman (animated series), Kangaroo Jack: G'Day, U.S.A.!, Daffy Duck for President, Justice League, What's New, Scooby-Doo?, and What's New, Scooby-Doo?
