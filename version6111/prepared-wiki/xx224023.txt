[[Château de Ranrouët]]

CATEGORIES: Castles in Pays de la Loire, Loire-Atlantique, Official historical monuments of France, Ruins in Pays de la Loire

The Château du Ranroët is a castle existing since the 12th century, located in Herbignac in the Loire-Atlantique department of France.
Since 1925, the Château du Ranroët has been listed as a monument historique by the French Ministry of Culture.Base Mérimée: Château de Ranrouët [tpl]fr icon[/tpl]
