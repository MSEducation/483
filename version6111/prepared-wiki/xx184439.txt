[[Podbucze]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Wodzisław County

Podbucze [tpl]IPAc-pl|p|o|d|'|b|u|cz|e[/tpl] is a village in Gmina Godów, Wodzisław County, Silesian Voivodeship, southern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) – TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It has a population of 204 and lies close to the border with the Czech Republic.
