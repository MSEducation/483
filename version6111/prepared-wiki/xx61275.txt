[[Kimberley Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in South Africa, Buildings and structures in Kimberley, Northern Cape, Transport in the Northern Cape, Airports of the British Commonwealth Air Training Plan

Kimberley Airport [tpl]airport codes|KIM|FAKM[/tpl] is an airport serving Kimberley, the capital city of the province of Northern Cape in South Africa.

==Facilities==

The airport is at an elevation of [tpl]convert|3950|ft|0[/tpl] above mean sea level. It has two asphalt paved runways: 02/20 measuring [tpl]convert|3000|x|46|m|0[/tpl] and [tpl]convert|2439|x|46|m|0[/tpl].
