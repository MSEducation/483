[[Améleté Abalo]]

CATEGORIES: 1962 births, 2010 deaths, Deaths by firearm in Angola, Murdered sportspeople, Togolese people murdered abroad, Terrorism victims, Togolese football managers, People murdered in Angola

Améleté Abalo (March 7, 1962 – January 9, 2010) was the assistant coach of Togo national football team and manager of ASKO Kara. He was killed in the Togo national football team bus attack by the terrorist group Front for the Liberation of the Enclave of Cabinda when travelling to the 2010 Africa Cup of Nations.[tpl]cite web | language = German | url = http://www.1asport.de/sport/fussball/Zwei_Tote_bei_Anschlag__OK_erhebt_Vorwuerfe_sl_133182N.html | title = Zwei Tote bei Anschlag, OK erhebt Vorwürfe | publisher = 1asport.de[/tpl]
