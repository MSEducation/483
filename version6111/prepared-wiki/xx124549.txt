[[Bosniak Institute]]

CATEGORIES: Museums in Sarajevo, Museums established in 2001, 16th-century architecture, Ottoman architecture in Bosnia and Herzegovina, 2001 establishments in Bosnia and Herzegovina, Ottoman baths

The Bosniak Institute is a cultural center in Sarajevo focusing on Bosniak culture. It was established by former Bosnian Vice President Adil Zulfikarpašić. The institute is housed in a renovated sixteenth century Turkish bath and includes a library and an art center.[ref][tpl]cite web |url=http://www.arhivsa.ba/ica2004/robert.htm |title= Archives and Cultural Memory under Fire: Destruction and the Post-war Nationalist Transformation |author=Donia, Robert J. |publisher=University of Michigan |accessdate=10 May 2010[/tpl]
