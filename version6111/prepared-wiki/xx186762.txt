[[Dolores River Bridge]]

CATEGORIES: Road bridges in Colorado, Buildings and structures in Montrose County, Colorado, Bridges on the National Register of Historic Places in Colorado, Truss bridges, Bridges completed in 1952, Transportation in Montrose County, Colorado

The Dolores River Bridge is a through truss bridge spanning the Dolores River near Bedrock, Colorado. It carries State Highway 90 and is listed on the National Register of Historic Places (reference number 02001150[tpl]NRISref|2006a[/tpl]).
