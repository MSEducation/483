Leandro Javier Díaz Borzani ([tpl]IPA-es|leˈandro xaˈβjer ˈðiaθ[/tpl], born 26 June 1986) is an Argentine footballer that currently plays for Argentine Primera División side Quilmes as a Central midfielder.

==Club career==

===Boca Juniors===

Díaz played for the youth teams of Boca Juniors from 2004 and in 2005 he spent time in Spain with Ciudad de Murcia and Villarreal. He returned to Boca Juniors in 2006.

===Huracán===

In 2007 he joined Huracán on a one-year loan. At the beginning of the 2008-09 season his loan was renewed.
On October 5, 2008, he scored a bicycle kick to give Huracán a victory over Independiente.Díaz de gloria at Diario Olé [tpl]es icon[/tpl]
