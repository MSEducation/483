[[Miguel Murillo (Bolivian footballer)]]

CATEGORIES: Bolivian footballers, Bolivia international footballers, Association football goalkeepers, Club Bolívar players, 1930 FIFA World Cup players

Miguel Murillo was a Bolivian football goalkeeper who played for Bolivia in the 1930 FIFA World Cup.1930 FIFA World Cup Uruguay He also played for Club Bolívar.
