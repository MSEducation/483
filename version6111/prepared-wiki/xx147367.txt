[[Deane, Hampshire]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Hampshire, Civil parishes in Basingstoke and Deane

Deane is a village and civil parish in the county of Hampshire, England. Its name appears in the name of the borough in which it is placed, Basingstoke and Deane.

==Governance==

The village is a civil parish[tpl]cite web |url=http://mapit.mysociety.org/area/63408.html |title=Deane |year=2010 |accessdate=28 November 2011[/tpl] and part of the Oakley and North Waltham ward of Basingstoke and Deane borough council.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.basingstoke.gov.uk/council/councillors/wardsinfo.htm |title=Basingstoke and Deane Wards info |year=2010 |accessdate=28 November 2011[/tpl] The borough council is a Non-metropolitan district of Hampshire County Council.

==Geography==

The parish is surrounded by other Hampshire parishes, with Kingsclere north, Hannington north east, Oakley east, Dummer south east, North Waltham south, Steventon south west and Overton north west.
