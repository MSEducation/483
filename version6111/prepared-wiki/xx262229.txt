Tyler Beechey (born. June 5, 1981) is a Canadian professional ice hockey forward who currently with the Schwenninger Wild Wings of the Deutsche Eishockey Liga (DEL). He previously played for the Straubing Tigers after joining on June 29, 2012, from the DEG Metro Stars.[tpl]citeweb| url = http://www.tigershockey.de/page/news/article.php?article_file=1340977320.txt | title = Tigers get Canadian striker Beechey | publisher = Straubing Tigers | date = 2012-06-29 | accessdate = 2012-06-29 | language = German[/tpl]
On July 31, 2013, Beechey agreed to a one-year contract with his fifth German club, the newly promoted Schwenninger Wild Wings.[tpl]citeweb| url =  http://www.wildwings.de/web/aktuell/detailansicht/wild-wings-schlagen-doppelt-zu-4203/ | title = Tyler Beechey and Elias Granath come to Schwenninger | publisher = Schwenninger Wild Wings | date = 2013-07-31 | accessdate = 2013-07-31 | language = German[/tpl]
