[[Karel Leština]]

CATEGORIES: 1973 births, Canoeists at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Canoeists at the 2000 Summer Olympics, Czech canoeists, Living people, Olympic canoeists of the Czech Republic

Karel Sango Leština (born March 23, 1973 in Kadaň) is a Czech sprint canoer who competed from the mid-1990s to the early 2000s (decade). At the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta, he was eliminated in the semifinals of the K-2 500 m and K-4 1000 m events. Four years later in Sydney, Leština was eliminated in the semifinals of the K-4 1000 m event.
