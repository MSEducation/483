[[The Beauty Around]]

CATEGORIES: 2006 albums, Pop albums by Dutch artists

The Beauty Around is the first full length music album by Dutch singer/songwriter Marike Jager and her band.

==Track listing==

All songs written by Marike Jager.

===Info===

Recorded in: Studio 150 in Amsterdam. 
Artwork by: Mia Maria.
