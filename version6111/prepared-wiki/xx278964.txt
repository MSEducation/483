[[Iljaq]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Iljaq ([tpl]lang-fa|ايلجاق[/tpl], also Romanized as Īljāq)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3067219|Iljaq[/tpl] is a village in Chaypareh-ye Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 576, in 121 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
