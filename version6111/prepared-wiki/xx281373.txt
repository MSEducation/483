[[Certificate in Quantitative Finance]]

CATEGORIES: Educational programs, Professional certification in finance

The Certificate in Quantitative Finance (CQF; site: cqf.com) is a finance designation intended "to provide in-depth, practical training" in mathematical finance, financial modelling, derivatives and risk management. It is half a year in duration and is offered class based through Fitch Learning -  a London-based provider of training for the financial services industry, owned by the Fitch Group (fitchlearning.com/uk) - and via E-learning. The CQF was founded in 2003 by Course Directors Paul Wilmott and Paul Shaw.
