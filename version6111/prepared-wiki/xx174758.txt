[[Damien Boisseau]]

CATEGORIES: French male voice actors, Living people, Year of birth missing (living people)

Damien Boisseau is a French voice actor who specializes in dubbing. He is the official voice-over artist of Matt Damon, Jared Padalecki and Edward Norton.
