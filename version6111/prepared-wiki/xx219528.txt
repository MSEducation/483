[[Aniq]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Jolfa County

Aniq ([tpl]lang-fa|انيق[/tpl], also Romanized as Anīq and Annīq; also known as Anekh, Aneq, Anigh, Anīkh, Kochev’ye Anekho, and Maḩall-e-Chādor Neshīnī)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3053315|Aniq[/tpl] is a village in Nowjeh Mehr Rural District, Siah Rud District, Jolfa County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 170, in 41 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|03[/tpl]
