[[Martin Bowles]]

CATEGORIES: Australian public servants, Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, Secretaries of the Australian Government Immigration Department

Martin Bowles is a senior Australian public servant. He is Secretary of the Secretary in the Department of Immigration and Border Protection, a position to which he was appointed in 2013.

==Education==

Bowles has a Bachelor of Business from the Capricornia Institute of Advanced Education and a Graduate Certificate of Public Sector Management from Griffith University.[tpl]citation|url=http://www.immi.gov.au/about/department/senior-staff/_pdf/biography-martin-bowles-psm.pdf|archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20131110122154/http://www.immi.gov.au/about/department/senior-staff/_pdf/biography-martin-bowles-psm.pdf|archivedate=10 November 2013|title=Martin Bowles PSM, Secretary|publisher=Department of Immigration and Border Protection|date=2013[/tpl] He is also a Fellow of the Australian Society of Certified Practising Accountants.

==Career==

Bowles has held various senior corporate services and general management positions across Queensland and New South Wales.[tpl]citation|url=http://www.climatechange.gov.au/about/accountability/annual-reports/annual-report-1011/overview/departmental.aspx|archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20120229110351/http://www.climatechange.gov.au/about/accountability/annual-reports/annual-report-1011/overview/departmental.aspx|archivedate=29 February 2012|publisher=Department of Climate Change and Energy Efficiency|date=2011|title=Annual report 2010-11[/tpl] He worked in Health across a number of Area Health Services including his appointment in June 2002 as the Chief Executive Officer of Wentworth Area Health Services. 
In 2006, Bowles joined the ranks of the Australian Public Service, Australia's federal civil service, when he was appointed as Deputy Secretary of the Department of Defence.[tpl]citation|url=http://www.immi.gov.au/about/department/senior-staff/_pdf/biography-martin-bowles-psm.pdf|archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20140122222332/http://www.immi.gov.au/about/department/senior-staff/_pdf/biography-martin-bowles-psm.pdf|archivedate=22 January 2014|title=Martin Bowles PSM, Secretary|date= 2013|publisher=Department of Immigration and Border Protection[/tpl] 
Following his time at Defence, Bowles was appointed as Deputy Secretary of the Energy, Safety and Corporate Group at the Department of Climate Change and Energy Efficiency, where he had executive oversight of the Corporate Support Division, the Energy and Safety Programs Division and the Renewable and Energy and Efficiency Division.
At the end of 2012, Prime Minister Julia Gillard appointed Bowles Secretary of the Department of Immigration and Citizenship after he had acted in the position since March 2012.[tpl]cite press release|url=http://pmtranscripts.dpmc.gov.au/browse.php?did=18818|archivedate=19 December 2013|archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20131219061003/http://pmtranscripts.dpmc.gov.au/browse.php?did=18818|first=Julia|last=Gillard|author-link=Julia Gillard|date=28 September 2012|title=Appointment of Secretaries[/tpl] Bowles stayed in the role of immigration head as the department transitioned to the Department of Immigration and Border Protection.
