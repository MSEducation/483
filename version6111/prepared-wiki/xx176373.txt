[[Delirious (band)]]

CATEGORIES: Musical groups established in 1990, German thrash metal musical groups

Delirious is a thrash metal band from Hamm, Germany,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.metal-archives.com/band.php?id=1983|title=Delirious[/tpl] formed in 1990. They performed a cover of fellow Germans Blind Guardian's song "Majesty" that was included on the Blind Guardian tribute album entitled "Tales From the Underworld."[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.blind-guardian.cz/tribute/uk/bands-and-samples.htm|title=Bands & Samples[/tpl]
