[[Robert Stoltz]]

CATEGORIES: 1976 births, Bodens BK players, Kalmar FF players, Djurgårdens IF Fotboll players, Living people, Swedish footballers, Association football defenders

Robert Stoltz (born 10 August 1976) is a Swedish football defender, currently representing Enköpings SK. 
He joined the Djurgårdens IF for the 2006 season, leaving Kalmar FF. After two seasons with the club he was put on loan to Enköpings SK for the rest of the 2008 season.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.dif.se/sidor/nyheter/nyheter.asp?action=view&id=8400&typ=1 |title=Robert Stoltz till Enköping |accessdate=2008-08-20 |last=Riedel |first=Jonas |publisher=Djurgårdens IF Fotboll |date=2008-02-25 [/tpl] Stoltz got little time on the pitch being capped only 27 times. His only goal was scored against IF Elfsborg during the 2007 season. He started his career at Lira Luleå BK in Luleå and has also represented Bodens BK.
