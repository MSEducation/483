[[India national baseball team]]

CATEGORIES: National baseball teams in Asia, National sports teams of India, Baseball in India

The India national baseball team is the national baseball team of India. The team represents India in international competitions. India now also boasts of two International players Rinku Singh and Dinesh Patel, both of whom were in the Pittsburgh Pirates organization.[tpl]cite web|url=http://sports.espn.go.com/mlb/news/story?id=4298148|title=Indian pitchers to make pro debuts|date=July 13, 2009|work=ESPN.com|publisher=ESPN Inc.|accessdate=5 May 2012[/tpl]
