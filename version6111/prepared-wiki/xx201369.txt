[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Barreiras]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1979, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Feira de Santana, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century, Barreiras

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Barreiras ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Barreriensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Barreiras in the Ecclesiastical province of Feira de Santana in Brazil.
