[[Larráinzar]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Chiapas

 
Larráinzar is  one of the 119 municipalities of Chiapas, in southern Mexico. The municipal seat is the town of San Andrés Larráinzar.
As of 2005, the municipality had a total population of 16,538.[tpl]cite web|title=Larráinzar|url=http://www.e-local.gob.mx/work/templates/enciclo/chiapas/municipios/07049a.htm|work=Enciclopedia de los Municipios de México|publisher=Instituto Nacional para el Federalismo y el Desarrollo Municipal / Gobierno del Estado de Chiapas |accessdate=July 27, 2008[/tpl] 
It covers an area of 171.04 km².
