[[DigiProces]]

CATEGORIES: Electronics companies, Electronics companies of Spain, Companies established in 1990, Companies of Spain, Companies based in Catalonia, Spanish brands

DigiProces is an Electronics Manufacturing Services (EMS) company headquartered in Barcelona, Spain. DigiProces provides a range of integrated services and solutions to OEMs (original equipment manufacturers). 

==History==

The company was created on 1987 to cater Electronics Manufacturing Services (EMS) to Sistel group companies. It was pioneer in Surface-mount technology in Spain.
