[[Francisco Lupica]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, American musicians, Musicians from California, Year of birth missing (living people), Place of birth missing (living people)

Francesco Lupica is a California based musician. Once part of the band Shanti, he moved into a solo career, releasing one album, the Cosmic Beam Experience. Lupica is the first musician to widely make use of the Cosmic Beam Experience, first in live shows, but then in film scores such as The Thin Red Line, The New World, Tree of Life, To the Wonder and Last Hours''.
