[[Roman Catholic Diocese of União da Vitória]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Curitiba, Religious organizations established in 1976, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century, 1976 establishments in Brazil

The Roman Catholic Diocese of União da Vitória ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Unionensis a Victoria[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of União da Vitória in the Ecclesiastical province of Curitiba in Brazil.
