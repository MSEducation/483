[[Australia at the 2002 Winter Paralympics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 2002 Winter Paralympics, Australia at the Paralympics, 2002 in Australian sport

Australia competed at the 2002 Winter Paralympics. Australia was represented by seven male alpine skiers. Michael Milton won four gold medals  and Bart Bunting with guide Nathan Chivers two gold medals.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Australian Paralympic Committee|accessdate=26 September 2011|title=Paralympic Games History - Winter|year=2011|url=http://www.paralympic.org.au/games-amp-events/paralympic-games-history-winter/paralympic-games-history-winter[/tpl] It was Australia's most successful Winter Games in terms of gold medals.
