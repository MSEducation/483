[[Konstantin Sakaev]]

CATEGORIES: Chess grandmasters, Chess Olympiad competitors, Russian chess players, Sportspeople from Saint Petersburg, 1974 births, Living people, Russian chess writers, World Youth Chess Champions

Konstantin Sakaev ([tpl]lang-ru|Константин Сакаев[/tpl]; born in a Tatar family 13 April 1974) is a Russian chess Grandmaster (1992),"Lessons Learned", Chessville, February 2004 from St Petersburg and Russian Champion in 1999. He is also a chess writer. Sakaev is on the staff of the Grandmaster Chess School in St. Petersburg and has assisted Vladimir Kramnik and Nana Ioseliani while preparing for World Championship Candidates' Matches.GMchess
