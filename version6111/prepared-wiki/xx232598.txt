[[Sphincterochilidae]]

CATEGORIES: Sphincterochilidae

Sphincterochilidae is a taxonomic family of medium-sized air-breathing land snails, terrestrial pulmonate gastropod mollusks in the superfamily Helicoidea (according to the taxonomy of the Gastropoda by Bouchet & Rocroi, 2005).[tpl]Bouchet 2005[/tpl]
In the fossil record, Sphincterochilidae are known from the upper Eocene to the Holocene."Family summary for Sphincterochilidae". AnimalBase, last modified 27 August 2005, accessed 10 April 2009.
