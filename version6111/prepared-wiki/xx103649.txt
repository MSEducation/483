[[Pincher Creek-Macleod]]

CATEGORIES: Former Alberta provincial electoral districts

Pincher Creek-Macleod was a provincial electoral district in south west Alberta, Canada.
The riding was created in 1993, when Pincher Creek-Crowsnest merged with Macleod. In 1997 the riding changed names to Livingstone-Macleod.
The riding was situated in the rocky mountains and foothills of south west Alberta and along the Alberta, British Columbia border.
