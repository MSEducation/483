[[S. Devapriya]]

CATEGORIES: Sri Lankan cricketers, Kalutara Physical Culture Centre cricketers, Living people

S. Devapriya (first name and birthdate unknown) was a Sri Lankan cricketer who played for Kalutara Physical Culture Centre.
Devapriya made four first-class appearances for the team, during the Saravanamuttu Trophy competition of 1991-92. Devapriya scored a pair on his debut, partnering former Test cricketer Jayantha Silva in the tailend.
Devapriya made a highest score of just four runs in the six innings in which he batted. Devapriya bowled 23 overs in the four first-class matches he played, securing innings-best figures of 3-33.
