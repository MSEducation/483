[[Kindi Department]]

CATEGORIES: Departments of Burkina Faso, Boulkiemdé Province

Kindi is a department or commune of Boulkiemdé Province in central Burkina Faso. As of 2005 it had a population of 13,033. Its capital lies at the town of Kindi.Burkinabé government inforoute communale
