[[Sebastian MacLean]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people

Sebastian MacLean is a Canadian, born in Toronto, Ontario. He is best known for his appearance in, and producing/co-directing of, the documentary film Facing Goliath, which both covers his natural bodybuilding career and his efforts to transform a visually impaired, middle aged, friend from couch potato to competitive bodybuilder.[tpl]imdb title|0779266|Facing Goliath[/tpl] The film has been seen across Canada on CBC's Country Canada network and New Zealand's Sky TV network. 
As an actor he has been seen in a number of Canadian independent film productions.[tpl]imdb name|0572650[/tpl] and has been both host and producer for a province wide, New Brunswick, television series, called Body Quest.
