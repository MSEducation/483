[[Johann Gottfried Auerbach]]

CATEGORIES: 1697 births, 1753 deaths, Austrian artists, 18th-century Austrian painters

Johann Gottfried Auerbach (October 28, 1697 - August 5, 1753), was an Austrian painter and etcher.
Auerbach painted primarily portraits and battle genre works. Some of his works can be found at the Kunsthistorisches Museum.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.answers.com/topic/johann-gottfried-auerbach|title=Brief Biography Johann Gottfried Auerbach|accessdate=2010-02-03[/tpl]
