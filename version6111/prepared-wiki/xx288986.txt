[[Bizineh Rud Rural District]]

CATEGORIES: Rural Districts of Zanjan Province, Khodabandeh County

BizinehRud Rural District ([tpl]lang-fa|دهستان بزينه رود[/tpl]) is a rural district (dehestan) in Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 12,664, in 2,865 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl] The rural district has 12 villages.
