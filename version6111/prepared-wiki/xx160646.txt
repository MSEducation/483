[[Providence High School (Burbank, California)]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic secondary schools in Los Angeles County, California, Educational institutions established in 1955, High schools in the San Fernando Valley, Burbank, California, 1955 establishments in California

Providence High School is a private, Roman Catholic high school in Burbank, California.  It is located in the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Los Angeles. It was established in 1955 by the Sisters of Providence.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.providencehigh.org/history.jsp?rn=362929|title=School History|accessdate=2007-05-11|author=PHS[/tpl]  
Providence High School offers three focus programs in Media, Medical, and Technology along with visual and performing arts and competitive athletics.   
Providence High School enrolls approx. 425 students from all over the San Fernando Valley and surrounding areas. [tpl]Citation needed|date=November 2009[/tpl] 
In 2011 PHS was awarded the CIF Champion for Character Award.  From 1997-1998, it received The Blue Ribbon Award for Academic Excellence in the fields of math and science.
