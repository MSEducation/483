[[Sammy Moore]]

CATEGORIES: 1987 births, Living people, People from Dover, Kent, English footballers, Association football midfielders, Ipswich Town F.C. players, Brentford F.C. players, Stevenage F.C. players, Dover Athletic F.C. players, AFC Wimbledon players, The Football League players, Football Conference players

Sammy Leslie Moore (born 7 September 1987) is an English professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for League Two side AFC Wimbledon. He made his league debut for Ipswich Town at the age of 19. He went on to play for both Brentford and Stevenage for short spells before signing for non–league side Dover Athletic with whom he remained for two seasons. Dover Athletic proved unable to offer Moore his demands for full–time football however, hence he moved to Conference side AFC Wimbledon. At the end of AFC Wimbledon's first season in League Two, Moore was voted as Player of the Year 2011–12 by "Dons" fans.

===Stevenage Borough===

On 28 January 2008, Moore signed for Conference side Stevenage Borough on a non-contractual basis until the end of the 2007–08 season.[tpl]cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/mobile/football/teams/s/stevenage/7213981.stm|title=Stevenage sign Ipswich youngster|publisher=BBC Football|accessdate=28 January 2008[/tpl] The 20-year–old midfielder made his debut for "The Boro" on 2 February 2008 in a 4–0 defeat by Exeter City.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.soccerbase.com/players/player.sd?player_id=44732&season_id=137|title=Exeter City 4–0 Stevenage Borough|publisher=soccerbase.com|accessdate=2 February 2008[/tpl] Moore scored his only goal for Stevenage in a 2–1 defeat by Northwich Victoria on 22 April 2008.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.soccerbase.com/players/player.sd?player_id=44732&season_id=137|title=Stevenage Borough 1–2 Northwich Victoria|publisher=soccerbase.com|accessdate=22 April 2008[/tpl] Moore made his final appearance for "The Boro" in a 2–1 win over Halifax Town on 26 April 2008.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.soccerbase.com/players/player.sd?player_id=44732&season_id=137|title=Halifax Town 1–2 Stevenage Borough|publisher=soccerbase.com|accessdate=26 April 2008[/tpl] Moore was not offered a new deal with Stevenage and was subsequently released by the club on 20 May 2008.

===Dover Athletic===

On 5 June 2008, the 20-year-old midfielder signed a two-year contract with his local club, Dover Athletic, who had just won promotion to the Isthmian League Premier Division.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.doverathletic.com/index.php?p=news&id=1114&link=|title=Sammy seals the deal|publisher=Dover Athletic|accessdate=5 June 2008[/tpl] He remained with the club for two years but following his demands for full–time football, which could not be met by Dover, Moore left at the close of the 2009–10 season.

===AFC Wimbledon===

On 16 July 2010, Moore signed for Conference side AFC Wimbledon following strong performances as a trialist. On 21 August 2010, Moore opened his scoring account for The Dons with a goal in a 3–0 victory over Tamworth.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.afcwimbledon.co.uk/-/reports/blue-square-bet-premier/16656/tamworth |title=AFC Wimbledon 3–0 Tamworth |publisher=afcwimbledon.co.uk |date=16 July 2010 |accessdate=16 July 2010[/tpl] On 18 November 2010, Moore scored a goal late in injury time against Ebbsfleet United to advance AFC Wimbledon to the second round of the 2010–11 FA Cup. On 5 May 2012, Moore was named AFC Wimbledon Player of the Year for the 2011–12 season. He made his 100th league appearance for the club, with only 5 of them being from the substitutes bench, in a 2–0 win over York City at Bootham Crescent on 7 September 2013.[tpl]cite web|title=York City 0–2 AFC Wimbledon|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/23909813|publisher=BBC Sport|accessdate=7 September 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Sammy Moore career statistics|url=http://www.soccerbase.com/players/player.sd?player_id=44732|publisher=Soccerbase.com|accessdate=7 September 2013[/tpl]
