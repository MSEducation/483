[[Castelo de Lindoso]]

CATEGORIES: Castles in Portugal, National monuments in Portugal

Castelo de Lindoso is a castle in Portugal. It is classified as a National Monument. Specifically, a military monument.Portuguese government site
The castle is located on the River Lee near the Portuguese/Spanish border.http://www.igespar.pt/pt/patrimonio/pesquisa/geral/patrimonioimovel/detail/70164/
The style and technology displayed in the castle's construction were introduced to Portugal during the medieval period.http://www.igespar.pt/pt/patrimonio/pesquisa/geral/patrimonioimovel/detail/70164/ The architecture displays both Romanesque and Gothic qualities.http://www.igespar.pt/pt/patrimonio/pesquisa/geral/patrimonioimovel/detail/70164/ During the mid 17th century, the castle was militarily upgraded, with the upgrades finishing in 1666, roughly three years after the Spanish conquered the castle in 1663; the Protuguese subsequently retook the castle and finished the upgrades.http://www.igespar.pt/pt/patrimonio/pesquisa/geral/patrimonioimovel/detail/70164/
