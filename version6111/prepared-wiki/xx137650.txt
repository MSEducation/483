Metro San Pedro de los Pinos ([tpl]Lang-es|Estación San Pedro de los Pinos[/tpl]) is a station on Line 7 of the Mexico City Metro system. It is located in the Benito Juárez borough of Mexico City, west of the city centre.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.metro.df.gob.mx/red/estacion.html?id=97|title=San Pedro de los Pinos|language=Spanish|accessdate=1 August 2011[/tpl][tpl]Cite web|last=Archambault|first=Richard|url=http://mexicometro.org/metro/line7/san-pedro-de-los-pinos/|title=San Pedro de los Pinos »  Mexico City Metro System|accessdate=1 August 2011[/tpl]  The station opened on 19 December 1985.[tpl]Cite web|last=Schwandl|first=Robert|url=http://www.urbanrail.net/am/mexi/mex-history.htm|title=Opening Dates for Mexico City's Subway|accessdate=1 August 2011[/tpl]
The logo of the station features a silhouette of two pine trees.
