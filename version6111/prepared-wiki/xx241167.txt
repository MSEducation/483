[[Ongniud Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Ongniud Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠣᠩᠨᠢᠭᠤᠳ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Oŋniɣud qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|c=翁牛特旗  |p=Wēngniútè Qí[/tpl]) is a banner of eastern Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China. It is under the administration of Chifeng City, [tpl]convert|76|km[/tpl] to the south-southwest.
