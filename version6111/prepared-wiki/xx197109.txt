[[Shanghai Oriental Pearl (Group)]]

CATEGORIES: Companies based in Shanghai, Culture in Shanghai, Government-owned companies of China, Companies established in 1992, 1992 establishments in China

Shanghai Oriental Pearl Group ([tpl]SSE|600832[/tpl]) is a cultural company engaged in touring (including operations and management of Oriental Pearl Tower), radio and TV transmission service, media investments & advertising operations, and real estate investments. It is listed as one of the 50 pivotal large-scale enterprises by Shanghai government.Company Intro
