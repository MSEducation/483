[[Instituto Regiomontano]]

CATEGORIES: Lasallian educational institutions, Schools in Mexico

Instituto Regiomontano is a private institution serving students from primary school through preparatory school in Monterrey, Nuevo Leon. It is part of the Institute of the Brothers of the Christian Schools. It has tree units: Chepe Vera and Cumbres, that offers pre-kindergarten education to preparatory school and Santa Catarina that offers preparatory school. The actual principal of the school is the FSC Tarsicio Felix Larios. In 2012 the school turn 70.
