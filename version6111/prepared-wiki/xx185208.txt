[[Colin McKay (footballer)]]

CATEGORIES: 1896 births, Year of death missing, Scottish footballers, People from Kilmarnock, Association football midfielders, The Football League players, Sheffield Wednesday F.C. players, Huddersfield Town F.C. players, Bradford City A.F.C. players

Colin McKay (born 1896) was a Scottish professional footballer, who played in England for Sheffield Wednesday, Huddersfield Town and Bradford City. He was born in Kilmarnock, Scotland.
