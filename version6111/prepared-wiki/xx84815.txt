[[Child Find Ontario]]

CATEGORIES: Charities based in Canada, Child-related organizations, Missing people organizations, Organizations established in 1984

Child Find Ontario is a non-profit community service founded in 1984 dedicated to advocating missing children. As a branch of Child Find, it oversees 20 chapters in the province of Ontario. Each year, Child Find Ontario receives reports of 40 to 50 missing children.
