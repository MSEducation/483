[[Qarah Gozlu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Sepidan County

Qarah Gozlu ([tpl]lang-fa|قره گزلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Qarah Gozlū; also known as Qaraguzlu, Qareh Gowzlū, Shamsābād-e Qareh Gozlū, and Shamsābād-e Qoroq)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3084470|Qarah Gozlu[/tpl] is a village in Beyza Rural District, Beyza District, Sepidan County, Fars Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 366, in 83 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|07[/tpl]
