[[Deeba]]

CATEGORIES: 1947 births, Living people, Pakistani people, People from Ranchi, Pakistani film actresses, Nigar Award winners, 20th-century Pakistani actresses, 21st-century Pakistani actresses

Deeba ([tpl]lang-ur| دیبا[/tpl]) is a Pakistani film actress. Her real name is Raheela.she was born on 1 august 1947 inRanchi district of Bihar  in India . She was one of the leading film actresses during the 1960s and 1970s, well known for her romantic and tragic roles in Urdu and Punjabi films.http://www.mazhar.dk/film/stars/heroines/60s.htm [tpl]Dead link|date=March 2012[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://anisshakur.tripod.com/id68.html |title=Deeba |publisher=Anisshakur.tripod.com |date= |accessdate=2012-07-26[/tpl] She started her career as an actress in Charagh jalta raha. Her performances in several films, such as Milan (1964), Aina (1966), Payal ki jhankar (1966), Sangdil (1968), Dard (1969), Sajna door diya (1970), Neend hamarey khuwab tumharey (1971), Ansoo (1971) and Perdes (1972), have won critical acclaim.[tpl]Citation needed|date=March 2012[/tpl] She received a Nigar Best Actress award for the Punjabi film Sajna door diya in 1970. Her smiling face and innocent look have given rise to the description "Pakistani Mona Lisa"http://www.dawn.com/weekly/dmag/archive/070311/dmag7.htm [tpl]Dead link|date=March 2012[/tpl]
Deeba married camera man Naeem Rizvi in 1971 and left silver-screen for 10 years. However, financial hardships made her join film industry again in 1987 as character actress. It was on TV where she appeared on the longest run Geo TV SOP, Tere Pehlu Mein as Dadi of Sarosh , Jabran Shahid, Laiba, Sara Chaudhry, and Rachel Gill.
