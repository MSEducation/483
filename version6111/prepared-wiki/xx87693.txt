[[Moreland railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1884

Moreland railway station is located on the Upfield line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the northern Melbourne suburb of Coburg opening on 9 September 1884.Moreland Vicsig
A footbridge is located at the northern end of the station, whilst a disused signal box is located at the southern end of Platform 2.
Manually controlled boom barriers replaced interlocked gates on Moreland Road in 1986.VR History by Andrew Waugh
