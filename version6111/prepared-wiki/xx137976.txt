[[Asaka, Uzbekistan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Andijan Province

Asaka ([tpl]lang-uz|Asaka[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-ky|Асаке[/tpl]) is a city in Andijan Province, Uzbekistan, to the east of Andijan in the Ferghana Valley."Asaka, Andijon, Uzbekistan," travelingduck.com website It was formerly known as Leninsk during the communist era. The first automobile assembly plant in Central Asia was opened there by an Uzbekistani-South Korean joint venture, UzDaewoo.
