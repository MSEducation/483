[[Helen McGregor]]

CATEGORIES: English novelists, English stage actresses, Living people, Year of birth missing (living people)

Helen McGregor is a writer, actor and lecturer.  Her first novel Schrodinger's Baby was published in the UK, Germany and the US.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.oxfordmail.co.uk/archive/1998/05/25/Oxfordshire+Archive/6639910.Meet_maverick_author_who_lives_on_Oxford_canal/ | title=Meet maverick author who lives on Oxford canal | publisher=Oxford Mail | date=May 25, 1998 | accessdate=September 28, 2011[/tpl] She teaches film history and theory at the National Film and Television School (NFTS) and the Met Film School, based at Ealing Studios in London.  In 2010 and 2011 she performed a one woman version of Richard III called Now is the Winter by Kate Saffin to critical acclaim at the Edinburgh Fringe Festival.
