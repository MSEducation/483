[[Louis Rostollan]]

CATEGORIES: French male cyclists, 1936 births, Living people, Sportspeople from Marseille

Louis Rostollan (born January 1, 1936 in Château Gombert) is a French former professional road bicycle racer. He was a professional from 1958 until 1967, winning the Critérium du Dauphiné Libéré in 1958 and the Tour de Romandie in 1960 and 1961.
