[[András Benkei]]

CATEGORIES: 1923 births, 1991 deaths, People from Nyíregyháza, Members of the Hungarian Working People's Party, Members of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, Hungarian Interior Ministers

András Benkei (September 11, 1923 – August 8, 1991) was a Hungarian communist politician, who served as Interior Minister between 1963 and 1980.
