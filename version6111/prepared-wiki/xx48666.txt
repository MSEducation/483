[[Bjerke]]

CATEGORIES: Boroughs of Oslo

Bjerke is a borough of the city of Oslo, Norway.
The most densely populated residential areas are located along the Trondheimsveien, including the high-rise apartment blocks of Linderud. The shopping centre at Linderud is also home to the borough council's administration. The northern areas of the borough are bordered by the vast woodlands of Oslomarka.
