[[2009 Seongnam Ilhwa Chunma season]]

CATEGORIES: South Korean football clubs 2009 season, Seongnam Ilhwa Chunma seasons

The 2009 season was Seongnam Ilhwa Chunma's twenty-first season in the K-League in South Korea. Seongnam Ilhwa Chunma was competed in K-League, League Cup and Korean FA Cup.
