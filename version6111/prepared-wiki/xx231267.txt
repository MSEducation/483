[[Draper House (Lima, New York)]]

CATEGORIES: Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Houses completed in 1842, Houses in Livingston County, New York

Draper House is a historic home located at Lima in Livingston County, New York. It was built about 1842 and remodeled in the 1860s.  It is a two story, three bay, side hall frame residence with vernacular Italianate style design features.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=3747|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: Draper House |date=October 1988|accessdate=2009-09-01 |author=Frances Gotcsik|publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation[/tpl] 
It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1989.
