[[Bráz]]

CATEGORIES: 1920 births, Brazilian basketball players, Basketball players at the 1948 Summer Olympics, Basketball players at the 1952 Summer Olympics, Olympic bronze medalists for Brazil, Olympic basketball players of Brazil, Year of death missing, Olympic medalists in basketball

João Francisco Bráz (November 25, 1920 in São Paulo – September 11, 1996) was a Brazilian basketball player. At his Olympic debut he won the bronze medal with Brazilian basketball team under the guidance of head coach Moacyr Daiuto.
