[[Katherine McNamara]]

CATEGORIES: American actresses, Living people

Katherine McNamara  is an American actress and singer. She is best known for playing the role of Myra Santelli in the Disney Channel original movie Girl vs. Monster.

==Life and career==

McNamara guest starred on shows including Drop Dead Diva and 30 Rock. She was also in some movies such as New Years Eve. She is also set to star in the "Tom Sawyer and Huckleberry Finn" movie remake.http://variety.com/2011/film/news/players-29-1118039601/
She appeared on the Disney XD show Kickin' It, in which she had a recurring role as Claire, Kim's rival, as well as appearing as Myra Santinelli in the Disney Channel movie Girl vs. Monster.
She also appeared in two episodes of Jessie, playing Bryn Brietbart, as well as an episode of the new Nickelodeon series The Thundermans. She also appeared in the independent movie Contest.
