[[Cilvēka bērns]]

CATEGORIES: 1991 films, Latvian films

Cilvēka bērns is a 1991 Latvian film directed by Jānis Streičs, starring Akvelīna Līvmane, Jānis Paukštello, and others.
The film was awarded the Latvian National Film Prize Lielais Kristaps in 1991 for the best film of the year.Lielajam Kristapam - 50! In 1994, along with Karakum, it received the "Rights of the Child Award" in the Chicago International Children's Film Festival.
