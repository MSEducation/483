[[(46544) 1988 QO]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1988, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(46544) 1988 QO is a main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on August 19, 1988.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
