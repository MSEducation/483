[[PS Princess of Wales (1870)]]

CATEGORIES: 1870 ships, Passenger ships of the United Kingdom, Steamships, Tyne-built ships, Ships of the London and North Western Railway, Ships of the Lancashire and Yorkshire Railway

PS Princess of Wales was a paddle steamer passenger vessel operated by the London and North Western Railway and the Lancashire and Yorkshire Railway from 1870 to 1896.Railway and Other Steamers, Duckworth. 1962 

==History==

She was built by A. Leslie and Company on the River Tyne as one of the first new ships commissioned by the London and North Western Railway and the Lancashire and Yorkshire Railway for their services from Fleetwood.
