[[Mohammadabad, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Mohammadabad ([tpl]lang-fa|محمداباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Moḩammadābād; also known as Muhammadābād and Mukhammabad)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3075438|Mohammadabad[/tpl] is a village in Sojas Rud Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 475, in 92 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
