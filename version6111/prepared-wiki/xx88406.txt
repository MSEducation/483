[[Sociology of Rulership and Religion]]

CATEGORIES: Sociology books, Works by Max Weber

Sociology of Rulership and Religion is a book written by Maximilian Weber, a German economist and sociologist. The original edition was in German. The book examined the hierarchal structures in religion and how these related to political theories of governance and economics.
