[[Emma Talley]]

CATEGORIES: American female golfers, Alabama Crimson Tide women's golfers, Golfers from Kentucky, People from Paducah, Kentucky, People from Caldwell County, Kentucky, 1994 births, Living people

Emma Talley (born March 24, 1994) is an American amateur golfer currently playing at the University of Alabama, and is entering her sophomore year in 2013-14. Talley won the 2013 U.S. Women's Amateur by defeating Yueer Cindy Feng in the 36-hole final match, 2 & 1.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.usatoday.com/story/sports/golf/2013/08/11/emma-talley-us-womens-amateur-alabama-crimson-tide/2641323/ |title=Alabama's Emma Talley wins US Women's Amateur |agency=AP |date=August 11, 2013 |newspaper=USA Today |accessdate=August 12, 2013[/tpl]

==College==

Talley is a student at the University of Alabama. She is entering her sophomore year in 2013–14, and is a part of the class of 2016.

==Amateur==

In 2008, Talley was on the victorious U.S. Junior Ryder Cup team, which defeated Europe 22–2. On August 11, 2013, Talley won the U.S. Women's Amateur in a 36-hole finale by defeating Yueer Cindy Feng, 2 & 1.
