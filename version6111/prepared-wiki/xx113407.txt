[[Adyar, Karnataka]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Dakshina Kannada district

[tpl]About|High class residential area, Karnataka, India||Adyar (disambiguation)[tpl]![/tpl]Adyar[/tpl]
Adyar is a residential neighborhood in Karnataka, India.

==Demographics==

[tpl]As of|2001[/tpl] India census,[tpl]GR|India[/tpl] Adyar had a population of 6501. Males constitute 50% of the population and females 50%. Adyar has an average literacy rate of 73%, higher than the national average of 59.5%; with 53% of the males and 47% of females literate. 13% of the population is under 6 years of age.
