[[Shin-Koga Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Saitama Prefecture, Stations of Tobu Railway, Railway stations opened in 1935, 1935 establishments in Japan

[tpl]Nihongo|Shin-Koga Station|新古河駅|Shin-Koga-eki[/tpl] is a railway station on the Tobu Nikko Line in Kazo, Saitama, Japan, operated by the private railway operator Tobu Railway.

==Lines==

Shin-Koga Station is served by the Tobu Nikko Line, and is 20.6 km from the starting point of the line at [tpl]Ja-stalink|Tōbu-Dōbutsu-Kōen[/tpl].

==Passenger statistics==

In fiscal 2011, the station was used by an average of 2,288 passengers daily.
