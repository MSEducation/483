[[Václav Cílek]]

CATEGORIES: 1955 births, Living people, Czech geologists, Czech writers

Václav Cílek (born 1955 in Brno) is Czech geologist, climatologist, writer, philosopher,  science popularizer and translator of Tao and Zen texts.[tpl]cite web|url=http://zpravy.idnes.cz/vaclav-cilek-moc-neutracejme-a-vracejme-lidem-i-prirode-to-co-jsme-dostali-1qn-/kavarna.asp?c=A090810_172616_kavarna_bos|title=Václav Cílek: Moc neutrácejme a vracejme lidem i přírodě to, co jsme dostali|date=2009-08-12|work=Mladá fronta DNES|publisher=iDnes|language=Czech|accessdate=28 April 2011[/tpl]

==Biography==

In his youth, he moved to Tanzania, where his father worked as a geologist. From 1969 to 1970, he studied at a high school in Tanzania.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.czechlit.cz/autori/cilek-vaclav/?allInMonth=1&month=3&day=1&year=2011|title=Václav Cílek|publisher=Portál české literatury|language=Czech|accessdate=28 April 2011[/tpl] Later he continued his studies at the Mining Technical School in Příbram (1970–1974). In 1979 he graduated in geology from the Charles University in Prague. Since 1980, he works in Geologic Institute of Czech Academy of Sciences and specializes on geology of Cenozoic Era. He also explores interactions between environment and civilization. Between 2004 to 2012, he was the director of the Geologic Institute of Czech Academy of Science.
Cílek also published several popularization books and many articles in journals and newspapers. During his career, he collaborated with Hospodářské noviny, Lidové noviny, Mladá fronta DNES, Mladý svět, Právo, Reflex, Respekt, Czech Television, Czech Radio etc.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.rozhlas.cz/strednicechy/tandem/_zprava/vaclav-cilek-geolog-klimatolog-a-spisovatel--871964|title=Václav Cílek - geolog, klimatolog a spisovatel (interview)|publisher=Czech Radio|language=Czech|accessdate=28 April 2011[/tpl] He was a co-creator of the TV series about caves of the Czech Republic, called Podzemní Čechy (The Underground Czechia, 2001).
In 2004, Cílek was awarded Tom Stoppard Prize for his books Krajiny vnitřní a vnější (2002) and Makom: Kniha míst (2004).[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.czechlit.cz/authors/cilek-vaclav/|title=Václav Cílek|publisher=Czech Literature Portal|accessdate=28 April 2011[/tpl] Additionally, he was named Laureate by the Minister of Ecology for "valuable contribution to the popularization of Czech science, especially geology and climatology" (2007). In 2009, he was awarded the  Václav Havel Foundation VIZE 97 Prize.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ct24.cz/domaci/68738-cenu-nadace-manzelu-havlovych-letos-ziskal-geolog-vaclav-cilek/video/1/|title=Cenu nadace manželů Havlových letos získal geolog Václav Cílek|work=Czech Television|publisher=ČT 24|language=Czech|accessdate=28 April 2011[/tpl]
