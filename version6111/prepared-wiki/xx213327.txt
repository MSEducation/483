[[Frederick Hawkes]]

CATEGORIES: 1878 births, Alumni of Magdalen College, Oxford, Archdeacons of Southwark, Bishops of Kingston, 20th-century Anglican bishops, 1966 deaths

 Frederick Ochterloney Taylor Hawkes was the fourth Bishop of Kingston.
Born on 22 November 1878 and educated at Magdalen College, Oxford,“Who was Who” 1897-1990 London, A & C Black, 1991 ISBN 0-7136-3457-X he was ordained in 1903.  He then became a curate at St Mary's Portsea thumb|left|100px|St Mary's Portsea following which he was Vicar of Aldershot, Rector of Lambeth and Archdeacon of Southwark before his ordination to the episcopate,A New Bishop. (Official Appointments and Notices) The Times Saturday, Dec 18, 1926; pg. 12; Issue 44458; col F a post he held until retirement to Oxted in 1952. He died fourteen years later on 26 January 1966.Rt.
