[[FV Northern Belle]]

CATEGORIES: 1979 ships, 2010 in Alaska, Fishing ships of the United States, Maritime incidents in 2010, Shipwrecks of the Alaska coast

The Northern Belle was a fishing vessel that sank in the Gulf of Alaska on April 20, 2010. Three of her four crew were rescued alive; her captain, Robert Royer, died before rescue teams arrived.[tpl]cite news |url=http://abcnews.go.com/US/wireStory?id=10432203 |first=Dan |last=Joling |title=Fisherman Recounts Rescue as Boat Sinks off Alaska |publisher=ABC News |date=April 21, 2010 |accessdate=January 1, 2011[/tpl]
