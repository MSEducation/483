[[Philippine local election, 1899]]

CATEGORIES: Local elections in the Philippines, 1899 elections in Asia, 1899 in the Philippines

Local elections were held for provincial and municipal posts throughout the Philippine Archipelago from May 7, 1899. The first local elections under the American occupation were held in Baliuag, Bulacan, supervised by US General Henry W. Lawton.
