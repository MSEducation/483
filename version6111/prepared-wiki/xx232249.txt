Andy Silvester (born 16 June 1947 in, Kidderminster, Worcestershire)http://www.bbc.co.uk/music/artists/ed5c7479-2b40-4c82-807d-5537eb570b32 is a bassist and multi-instrumentalist.  Silvester has played in various bands during his career, most notably as co-founder of both Chicken Shack and Big Town Playboys as well as a tenure in the British blues band Savoy Brown[tpl]cite web|url=[tpl]Allmusic|class=artist|id=p125296|pure_url=yes[/tpl]|title=Biography: Andy Silvester|last=Eder|first=Bruce|publisher=Allmusic|accessdate=20 May 2010[/tpl] and Los Angeles based soft rock ensemble, Wha-Koo. He has also performed with The Steve Gibbons Band, ex-Fleetwood Mac guitarist Danny Kirwan, and Savoy Brown vocalist Chris Youlden.
