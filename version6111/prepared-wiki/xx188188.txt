[[The Sign on the Door]]

CATEGORIES: 1921 films, 1920s drama films, American drama films, American films, American silent feature films, Black-and-white films, Films based on plays, Films directed by Herbert Brenon, First National Pictures films

The Sign on the Door is a 1921 American silent drama film starring Norma Talmadge and Lew Cody. The film was directed and written by Herbert Brenon, and based upon the play of the same name by Channing Pollock.[tpl]cite book|editor=White Munden, Kenneth|title=The American Film Institute Catalog of Motion Pictures Produced in the United States: Feature Films, 1921-1930|year=1997|publisher=University of California Press|isbn=0-520-20969-9|page=715-716[/tpl] A copy of this film is in the Library of Congress film archive.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.silentera.com/PSFL/data/S/SignOnTheDoor1921.html |title=Progressive Silent Film List: The Sign on the Door |accessdate=2014-01-23 |work=Silent Era[/tpl]
A second adaptation of the play was filmed in 1929 as The Locked Door, starring Barbara Stanwyck.White Munden 1997 p. 445
