[[Taham]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 births, Living people, Iranian hip hop musicians, Iranian rappers

Taham (born March 13, 1989) is an Iranian singer, rapper, songwriter and producer.

==Biography==

===Life and career===

Taham was born in Tehran, Iran. He made his recording debut in late 2003 in Tehran and joined paydar later on in 2010.

===Work to date===

He released his debut album Taranehaye bazargani (Commercial songs) in 2011. On December 2, 2012 Taham released the album, 'Behtarin Rahe Mordan(EP)" Featuring Erfan (rapper), Paya, Layla .[tpl] iTunes web|accessdate=2012-12-02|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/behtarin-rahe-mordan-ep/id586042211|title=Taham|Best Way to Die; iTunes[/tpl]

===Album: Taranehaye Bazargani Songs (2010)===

On September 16, 2011 Taham released Commercial Songs.

===Album(EP): Behtarin Rahe Mordan Way To Die (2012)===

On December 2, 2012 Taham released Best Way To Die (EP).

===Musical style and influence===

His early style of G-rap has now evolved into a very different genre of production and singing unique to himself which has separated him from all other Iranian Music Producers. He is also known for his Taste and creativity in other areas such as fashion and visual arts.
