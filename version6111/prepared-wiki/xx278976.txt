[[Hesar, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Hesar ([tpl]lang-fa|حصار[/tpl], also Romanized as Ḩeşār and Zanjānrūd)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3066432|Hesar[/tpl] is a village in Chaypareh-ye Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 731, in 193 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
