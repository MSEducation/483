[[David Stevenson (cyclist)]]

CATEGORIES: 1882 births, Year of death missing, Scottish male cyclists, Olympic cyclists of Great Britain, Cyclists at the 1912 Summer Olympics, Scottish Olympic competitors, Sportspeople from County Down, Ulster Scots people

David M. Stevenson (born 17 January 1882, date of death unknown) was a Scottish road racing cyclist who competed in the 1912 Summer Olympics.
He was born in Clough, Ireland.
In 1912 he was a member of the Scotland cycling team which finished fourth in the team time trial event.
