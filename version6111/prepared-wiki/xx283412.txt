[[Cubero, New Mexico]]

CATEGORIES: Census-designated places in New Mexico, Census-designated places in Cibola County, New Mexico

Cubero is a census-designated place  in Cibola County, New Mexico, United States. Its population was 289 as of the 2010 census.
