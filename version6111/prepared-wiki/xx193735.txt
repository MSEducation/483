==Limitations==

mapy has few limitations. Since it is only a command sender (And not a plug-in) it can't solve problems occurred by Maya (software). Also mapy is not multi platform.
The mac version of mapy is not available yet and the linux version has been discontinued since version 1.4.0  (but available for download).
