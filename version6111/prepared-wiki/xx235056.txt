[[Nicifor Ranjina]]

CATEGORIES: Croatian architects, Year of death unknown, Year of birth unknown

Nicifor Ranjina or Nićifor Ranjina was a Croatian local builder active in the Republic of Ragusa (today Dubrovnik). He built Minčeta Tower in 1319, originally as a strong four-sided fort.Dubrovnik City walls Dubrovnik Guide
