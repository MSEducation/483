[[Stephen van Haestregt]]

CATEGORIES: 1974 births, Living people, Dutch heavy metal drummers, People from Terneuzen, Within Temptation members

Stephen van Haestregt (born September 12, 1974, in Westdorpe, Zeeland) is a Dutch drummer, best known as the drummer of the Dutch symphonic metal band Within Temptation.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.trouw.nl/cultuur/film/article2047536.ece/Jan_Smit_overdonderd_door_Gouden_Harp.html|title= Jan Smit overdonderd door Gouden Harp|date=6 March 2009|publisher=Trouw|language=Dutch|accessdate=16 February 2010[/tpl][tpl]cite news|url=http://www.bndestem.nl/regio/oosterhout/4944653/Studenten-strijden-om-studiotijd.ece|title=Studenten strijden om studiotijd|date=9 May 2009|publisher=BN/De Stem|accessdate=16 February 2010[/tpl]
Van Haestregt first band was Sacrament, and then he joined Paralysis. In 2000 he worked with Within Temptation, as sound engineer of their album Mother Earth. When the band's drummer, Ivar de Graaf, left in 2002, he joined them and five days later made his first performance with the band on the German festival Rock im Park. Stephen has performed on Arjen Anthony Lucassen's album Fate of a Dreamer, under the Ambeon moniker, and has also produced several albums such as The Rebel in You by Yellow Pearl.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.regiohoogeveen.nl/agenda/2138/Yellow-Pearl.html|title=Yellow Pearl|date=11 October 2006|publisher=Regio Hoogeveen|language=Dutch|accessdate=16 February 2010[/tpl]
Also Stephen produced several albums, such as Middles EP and Tsumi from the Dutch Nu-Metal band Dreadlock Pussy.
On March 30, 2010, Stephen announced via Within Temptation's official websitehttp://en.within-temptation.com/ that he would be leaving the band after the Theatretour planned for April in order to concentrate on his new band My Favorite Scar.
A press release was announced by the band My Favorite Scar on December 18, 2012 stating that Stephen Van Haestregt was leaving the band. It was also in the press release that "Stephen would continue his work as a drummer and producer at Swampmusicstudio."[tpl]cite web|title=My Favorite Scar Press Release|url=http://www.myfavoritescar.com/press-release-december-2012/[/tpl]
