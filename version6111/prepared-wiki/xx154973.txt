[[Kavalerovo]]

CATEGORIES: Urban-type settlements in Primorsky Krai, Populated places established in the 1910s

Kavalerovo ([tpl]lang-ru|Кавале́рово[/tpl]) is an urban locality (an urban-type settlement) and the administrative center of Kavalerovsky District of Primorsky Krai, Russia. Population: [tpl]ru-census|p2010=15,381|p2002=17,358|p1989=19,336[/tpl]

==History==

It was founded in 1910 by Fyodor Popolitov as the village of Kavalerovka ([tpl]lang|ru|Кавалеровка[/tpl]).
