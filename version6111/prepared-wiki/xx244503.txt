[[Isobel Varley]]

CATEGORIES: Body modification, British women

Isobel Varley (born 1937, Yorkshire, United Kingdom) is a Guinness Book of World Records-recognized tattooed senior.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-1092407/Body-ink--record-holding-tattooed-British-pensioner-takes-Berlin-storm.html|newspaper=Daily Mail UK|title=Body ink.. record-holding tattooed British pensioner takes Berlin show by storm|date=12-08-2008[/tpl][tpl]cite book|author=Glenday, Craig|url=http://books.google.ca/books?id=hLYzvUvPL3MC&pg=PA98&dq=isobel+varley&hl=en&ei=4w30TYOCC8jOgAfj5ZTZCw&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=4&ved=0CDoQ6AEwAw#v=onepage&q=isobel%20varley&f=false |title=Guinness World Records 2010: Thousands of New Records in The Book of the Decade!|publisher=Random House of Canada|page=98|year=2010[/tpl]
She was first named "the world's most-tattooed senior woman" in 2000.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Wired.com|url=http://www.wired.com/culture/lifestyle/news/2001/06/44628|date=2001-06-19|title=Tattooed Matron[/tpl]
Varley got her first tattoo in her late 40s at a tattoo convention at the Hammersmith Palais.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/threecounties/content/articles/2008/03/18/people_herts_isobel_varley_feature.shtml | title=Isobel Varley - she's a work of art!|publisher=BBC - Beds Herts and Bucks|year=2008[/tpl]
According to Guinness over a ten-year period, Varley had over 200 designs inked, covering at least 76% of her body in tattoos. She reports that "the only areas not completely tattooed is my face, the soles of my feet my ears and some area on my hands."[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.isobelvarley.com/h7/my_story_7.htm|author=Varley, Isabel|title=My Story Part 8|accessdate=2011-06-11[/tpl]
