[[KvLQT3]]

CATEGORIES: Ion channels, Proteins

Kv7.3 (KvLQT3) is a potassium channel protein coded for by the gene KCNQ3.[tpl]cite web | title = Entrez Gene: KCNQ3 potassium voltage-gated channel, KQT-like subfamily, member 3| url = http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/sites/entrez?Db=gene&Cmd=ShowDetailView&TermToSearch=3786| accessdate = [/tpl]
It is associated with benign familial neonatal epilepsy.
