[[Eigil Nansen]]

CATEGORIES: 1931 births, Living people, Olympic cauldron lighters

Eigil Nansen (born 18 June 1931) is the son of architect and humanist Odd Nansen and the grandson of explorer and humanist Fridtjof Nansen.
In 1991, he won The Lisl and Leo Eitinger Prize for his work with refugees and human rights,http://www.uio.no/om_uio/priser/eitinger/previous.html The Lisl and Leo Eitinger Prize @ The University of Oslo Eigil Nansen is also known for lighting the first Winter Olympic Flame in 1952.
