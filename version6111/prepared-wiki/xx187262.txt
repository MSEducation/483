[[Aquarium ferry wharf]]

CATEGORIES: Ferry wharves in Sydney

Aquarium Ferry Wharf (or Darling Harbour Aquarium Ferry Wharf) was part of the  part of the Sydney Ferries network serving the locality of Darling Harbour in Sydney, Australia. 
As part of the timetable changes of 10 October 2010, all Sydney Ferries services were moved to  King St No 3 ferry wharf  a few minutes' walk away.
