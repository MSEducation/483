[[Barysh]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Ulyanovsk Oblast

Barysh ([tpl]lang-ru|Барыш[/tpl]) is a town and the administrative center of Baryshsky District of Ulyanovsk Oblast, Russia, located on the Barysh River (Volga's basin) [tpl]convert|139|km|sp=us[/tpl] southwest of Ulyanovsk. Population: [tpl]ru-census|p2010=17,149|p2002=18,902|p1989=20,213[/tpl]

==History==

It was founded in 1954 via merger of urban-type settlements of Barysh and Guryevka.[tpl]citation needed|date=March 2012[/tpl]

==Economy==

In Barysh located Baryshsky meat plant, which produces canned meat stew for the Ministry of Defence, Ministry of Internal Affairs, Ministry of Emergency Situations, Federal Security Service, Federal Protective Servicehttp://bmk-russia.ru/.
