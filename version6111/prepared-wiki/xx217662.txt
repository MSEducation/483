[[Babek (city)]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Babek District

Babək (also, Babek, Babekskiy, and Tazakend) is a city and municipality in and the capital of Babek Rayon, in the Nakhchivan Autonomous Republic of Azerbaijan.

==History==

Before the 1978, settlement was known as Tazakand. However, then was renamed in honor of the Persian revolutionary leader Babak Khorramdin.[tpl]cite web|title=Описание курорта Бабек, Азербайджан|url=http://www.tury.ru/resort/info.php?cid=6373|publisher=tury.ru|accessdate=8 January 2011[/tpl]

==Demography==

As of 2010, population of Babək contains 3252 people.
