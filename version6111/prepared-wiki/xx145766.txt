[[Zaraki]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Rudsar County

Zaraki ([tpl]lang-fa|زراكي[/tpl], also Romanized as Zarakī and Zarākī)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3839195|Zaraki[/tpl] is a village in Shuil Rural District, Rahimabad District, Rudsar County, Gilan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 95, in 25 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|01[/tpl]
