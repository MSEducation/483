[[C.D. Audaz]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in El Salvador

Club Deportivo Audaz  is a Salvadoran professional football club based in Apastepeque, San Vicente,  El Salvador.
The club currently plays in the Tercera Division de Fútbol Salvadoreño.

==Stadium==

The club's home venue is the Estadio La Coyotera in Apastepeque.
