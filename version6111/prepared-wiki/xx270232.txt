[[Katwa I (community development block)]]

CATEGORIES: Community development blocks in Bardhaman district

Katwa I (community development block) ([tpl]lang-bn|কাটোয়া I সমষ্টি উন্নয়ন ব্লক[/tpl]) is an administrative division in Katwa subdivision of Bardhaman district in the Indian state of West Bengal. Katwa police station serves this block. Headquarter of this block is at Panchghara under Khajurdihi Gram Panchayat. Panuhat is an urban area in this block.[tpl]cite web| url = http://wbprd.gov.in/html/asp/bdo_contact.asp?cd=DF |title = Contact details of Block Development Officers |work = Burdwan  district | publisher = West Bengal Government |accessdate = 2011-09-23 | [/tpl][tpl]cite web | url = http://web.cmc.net.in/wbcensus/DataTables/02/Table4_9.htm | title = Provisional population totals, West Bengal, Table 4, Barddhaman District | accessdate =  2011-09-23 | last = | first = | work = Census of India 2001 | publisher = Census Commission of India[/tpl]
==Geography== 
Srikhanda, a constituent gram panchayat of Katwa I block, is located at [tpl]coord|23.596|N|88.084|E|format=dms|[/tpl].
Katwa I community development block has an area of 169.91  km2. 

===Gram panchayats===

Gram panchayats of Katwa I block/panchayat samiti are:Alampur, Gidhagram, Goai, Karajkgram, Khajurdihi, Koshigram, Saragram, Srikhanda and Sudpur.[tpl]cite web| url = http://wbprd.nic.in/html/asp/writereaddata/Notifications/01120055.doc | title = No. 229 (Sanction)-PN/P/II/1G-5/2005(Pt.II) dated 02.02.09 |work= Allotment No. 5 for five districts – Cooch Behar, Burdwan, Uttar Dinajpur, Hooghly and Bankura| publisher = Government of West Bengal - Department of Panchayats & Rural Development |accessdate = 2011-09-23 | [/tpl]

==Demographics==

As per 2001 census, Katwa I block had a total population of 152,066, out of which 78,638 were males and 73,428 were females. Katwa I block registered a population growth of 16.27 per cent during the 1991-2001 decade. Decadal growth for Bardhaman district was 14.36 per cent.Decadal growth in West Bengal was 17.84 per cent.[tpl]cite web | url = http://web.cmc.net.in/wbcensus/DataTables/02/FrameTable4_1.htm | title = Provisional Population Totals, West Bengal. Table 4 | accessdate =  2011-09-23 | last = | first = | work = Census of India 2001 | publisher = Census Commission of India [/tpl]
Scheduled castes at 48,852 formed around one-third the population. Scheduled tribes numbered 1,676.

==Literacy==

As per 2001 census, Katwa I block had a total literacy of 61.56 per cent for the 6+ age group. While male literacy was 69.84 per cent female literacy was 52.66 per cent. Bardhaman district had a total literacy of 70.18 per cent, male literacy being 78.63 per cent and female literacy being 60.95 per cent.[tpl]cite web | url = http://web.cmc.net.in/wbcensus/DataTables/02/Table5_9.htm | title = Provisional population totals, West Bengal, Table 5, Bardhaman District | accessdate =  2011-09-23 | last = | first = | work = Census of India 2001 | publisher = Census Commission of India[/tpl]
