[[Jarchi]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Jarchi ([tpl]lang-fa|جارچي[/tpl], also Romanized as Jārchī)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3766504|Jarchi[/tpl] is a village in Bonab Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 39, in 7 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
