[[Bughda Kandi]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Bughda Kandi ([tpl]lang-fa|بوغداكندي[/tpl], also Romanized as Būghdā Kandī; also known as Bugda-Kend and Bukda Kandi)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3057059|Bughda Kandi[/tpl] is a village in Bughda Kandi Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,036, in 258 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
