Mark Linden Lee is a former Major League Baseball pitcher. Lee pitched in all or part of four seasons from [tpl]By|1978[/tpl] until [tpl]By|1981[/tpl], the first two for the San Diego Padres and the last two for the Pittsburgh Pirates. He is currently the manager for the Amarillo Sox of the American Association of Independent Professional Baseball.
