[[Ouagadougou Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in Burkina Faso, Buildings and structures in Ouagadougou

Ouagadougou Airport [tpl]airport codes|OUA|DFFD[/tpl] is an airport located in the centre of the city of Ouagadougou in Burkina Faso.
In 2004, the airport served 241,466 passengers (+18.5% vs. '03).
