[[Rome Cee]]

CATEGORIES: 1982 births, Living people, People from Baltimore, Maryland, American rappers

Jerome Carrington (born December 25, 1982), better known by his stage name Rome Cee, is an American rapper from Baltimore, MD. ==Early life==

Rome's father served in the military and moved the family frequently until finally returning to the family's hometown in Baltimore.
