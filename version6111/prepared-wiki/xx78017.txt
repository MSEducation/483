[[Virovitica-Podravina County]]

CATEGORIES: Slavonia, Counties of Croatia, Virovitica-Podravina County

Virovitica-Podravina County ([tpl]lang-hr|Virovitičko-podravska županija[/tpl]) is a northern Slavonian county in Croatia. Its county seat is in Virovitica and it includes the area around the Drava river, hence the name Podravina. Other notable towns are Slatina and Orahovica.
