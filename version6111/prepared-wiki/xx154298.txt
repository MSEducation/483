Oshri Gita ([tpl]lang-he|אושרי גיטא[/tpl]; born 2 July 1985) is an Israeli retired football player. During the 2007–08 season, with Maccabi Haifa Oshri's goal against Hapoel Petah Tikva during the 2006–07 season,  was ranked in the top ten goals of the season by Sport5.
