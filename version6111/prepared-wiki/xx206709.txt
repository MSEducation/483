[[Clearlake Highlands, California]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Lake County, California, Neighborhoods in California, Unincorporated communities in California

Clearlake Highlands is a former unincorporated community now incorporated in Clearlake, in Lake County, California. It is located on the north shore of the southeast end of Clear Lake, just south of the village of Clearlake Park, 15 miles (24Â km) east-southeast of Lakeport, at an elevation of 1348 feet (411 m).
