[[Gordon Stratton]]

CATEGORIES: Canadian ice hockey right wingers, Ice hockey people from Manitoba, Living people, 1934 births, Winnipeg Barons players

Gordon Stratton (Born - October 4, 1934  in Winnipeg, Manitoba) was a Canadian ice hockey right winger who recording 474 goals and 502 assists for 976 points as a pro.
