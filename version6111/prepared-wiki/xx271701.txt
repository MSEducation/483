[[Dindayalpur]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Siwan district, Siwan district

Dindayalpur is a village situated in Siwan district of Bihar state, India. The same district where 1st President of India Dr. Rajendra Prasad was born. Dindayalpur has village code 0021 in subdistrict 008.[tpl]cite web|url=http://censusindia.gov.in/2011-VillageDirectory/Directory/short_code_rural_20.pdf|title=Village directory|publisher=India 2011 census|accessdate=25 September 2011[/tpl]

==Geography==

The global location of Dindayalpur is between 26°22'3"N and 84°49'11"E longitudes. It is situated 14 km to the district headquarters and 12 km to another historical place called Hathwa.

==Population==

It has a population of 11234 as per census 2011.

==History==

It is a historical place and has produced number of freedom fighters, I.A.S. officers, doctors, engineers and software developers.[tpl]or|date=June 2013[/tpl]

==Education==

A government high school, inter college, a middle school, 6 primary schools, 3 coaching institutes and a library are in the village.

==Financial and public institutions==

Punjab National Bank has a branch situated in Dindayalpur.[tpl]cite web|url=http://banksifsccode.com/punjab-national-bank-micr-code/bihar/siwan/dindayal-pur-branch/|title=PNB|publisher=Banks IFSC Code|accessdate=26 September 2011[/tpl]

==Tourism==

Ram Janki Mandir in Dindayalpur is one of the biggest temple in the area which attracts crowd from surrounding villages during Mela seasons. [tpl]citation needed|date=June 2013[/tpl]
