==Route description==

SR 595 begins at Tropicana Avenue and proceeds north along Rainbow Boulevard for [tpl]convert|5.495|mi|km[/tpl] to U.S. Route 95 (at the interchange locally known as the "Rainbow Curve").  The state-maintained portion of Rainbow Boulevard is a designated portion of the National Highway System.

===Rainbow Boulevard description===

Rainbow Boulevard begins south of Blue Diamond Road (SR 160) in Clark County and continues north to Horse Drive in northern Las Vegas.AAA map Las Vegas Map 2006  Prior to the completion of Durango Drive north of Blue Diamond Road, Rainbow Boulevard was one of only two roads west of I-15 that connected Blue Diamond road with the developed areas to the north. The other road was Industrial Road, and now Dean Martin Drive,Decatur Blvd and Fort Apache Road.AAA map Las Vegas Map 1996

==History==

In January 2003, the southern terminus of SR 595 was at State Route 160 (Blue Diamond Road, Pahrump Valley Road), giving the highway a total distance of [tpl]convert|10.846|mi|km[/tpl].  By January 2006, the highway had been truncated to its current terminus at Tropicana Avenue.
