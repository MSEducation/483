[[First Old School Baptist Church of Roxbury and Vega Cemetery]]

CATEGORIES: Baptist churches in New York, Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Cemeteries on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, National Register of Historic Places in Delaware County, New York, Religious buildings completed in 1856, 19th-century Baptist church buildings, Churches in Delaware County, New York, Cemeteries in Delaware County, New York, Baptist cemeteries

First Old School Baptist Church of Roxbury and Vega Cemetery is a historic Baptist church and cemetery located at Roxbury in Delaware County, New York. The church was built in 1856 and is a rectangular, front gabled frame building in the meeting house form.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=1701|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: First Old School Baptist Church of Roxbury and Vega Cemetery|date=February 1996|accessdate=2010-01-08 |author=Neil Larson|publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation[/tpl] See also: [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=1700|title=Accompanying 12 photos[/tpl]
It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1996.
