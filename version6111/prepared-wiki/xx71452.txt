[[Battle of Mikagehama]]

CATEGORIES: 1351 in Japan, Battles involving Japan, Sieges involving Japan, Battles of the Middle Ages, Conflicts in 1351

[tpl]Unreferenced|date=January 2008[/tpl]The Battle of Mikagehama took place in 1351 in Japan between Ashikaga Takauji and Ko Morona on one side and Ishido Yorifusa on the other. Ishido won.
