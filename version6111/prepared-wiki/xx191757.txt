[[The Kingsway School]]

CATEGORIES: Secondary schools in Stockport, Community schools in Stockport

The Kingsway School is a coeducational comprehensive state secondary school in Stockport, Greater Manchester.
The school has sites on each side of Kingsway; on Foxland Road in Gatley to the west and Broadway in Cheadle to the east. 
In 2011, a house system was introduced (Rylands, Pankhurst, Whitworth, Gaskell, Turing and Lowry). 

==History==

The school was formed in 1983 by the merger of Broadway Boys School and Kingsway Girls School.
