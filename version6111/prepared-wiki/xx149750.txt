[[Allamanda blanchetii]]

CATEGORIES: Apocynaceae, Flora of Brazil

Allamanda blanchetii (purple allamanda, violet allamanda; syn. Allamanda violacea) is a species of flowering plant in the family Apocynaceae. It is native to Brazil.Allamanda blanchetii. Germplasm Resources Information Network (GRIN). It is cultivated as an ornamental plant.
