[[Tongan general election, 1990]]

CATEGORIES: 1990 elections in Oceania, 1990 in Tonga, Elections in Tonga

General elections were held in Tonga on 14 and 15 May 1990 to elect members of the Legislative Assembly of Tonga.  Nine nobles and nine people's representatives were elected.  Seven of the latter favoured democratic reform.[tpl]cite book |last=Campbell |first=Ian C.  |title=Elections in Asia and the Pacific: A Data Handbook.  Volume II: South East Asia, East Asia, and the South Pacific |editor1-last=Nohlen |editor1-first=Dieter |editor2-last=Grotz |editor2-first=Florian |editor3-last=Hartmann |editor3-first=Christof |publisher=Oxford University Press |year=2001 |chapter=Tonga |page=820 |ISBN=0-19-924959-8[/tpl] Voter turnout was 65.4%.
