[[Bank of America Building (Midland)]]

CATEGORIES: Bank of America buildings, Skyscrapers in Midland, Texas, Skyscrapers between 100 and 149 meters

The Bank of America Building is a highrise located in downtown Midland, Texas.  Built in 1978, at 24 stories and [tpl]convert|332|ft|m[/tpl], it is the tallest building in the city.  The building is located at 303 W. Wall St. and houses various oil and gas companies as well as Bank of America, Midland.
