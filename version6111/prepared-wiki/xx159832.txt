[[Aikala]]

CATEGORIES: Localities in Mangalore, Villages in Dakshina Kannada district

Aikala is a village near the town of Kinnigoli. It is situated in Mangalore taluk of Dakshina Kannada district. It is commonly known as Pompei in the Konkani language.

==Education==

There is a college called Pompei College which offers courses in commerce and arts at Aikala.

==Religious Places==

The Kirem Church, which is officially known as Our Lady of Remedies Church is located in Aikala.http://www.mangalorean.com/ekata/cdetail.php?chid=71
