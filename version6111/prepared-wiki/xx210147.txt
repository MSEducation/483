[[Sumiko Sakamoto]]

CATEGORIES: 1936 births, Living people, Japanese film actresses

[tpl]Nihongo|Sumiko Sakamoto|坂本スミ子|Sakamoto Sumiko[/tpl] was born on November 26, 1936 in Osaka, Japan.  Sakamoto is a singer and award-winning actress whose heartfelt performances made her a favorite of the late film director Shohei Imamura.
