[[Cape Verde at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics, 2013 in Cape Verde, Athletics in Cape Verde

Cape Verde competed at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics in Moscow, Russia, from 10–18 August 2013. A team of one athlete was announced to represent the country in the event.
