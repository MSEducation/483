[[The African Beat]]

CATEGORIES: 1962 albums, Art Blakey albums, Blue Note Records albums, Albums produced by Alfred Lion

The African Beat is a jazz album released by Art Blakey and The Afro-Drum Ensemble in November 1962 on Blue Note Records.Billboard, 1 Dec 1962, p. 20. He described it as the first opportunity he had to work with drummers from Africa; as a blend of American jazz with the traditional rhythms and tonal colors in the percussion of that continent. The album features compositions by African and American musicians, all based on aspects of West African (especially Nigerian) music.Original liner notes by Nat Hentoff
