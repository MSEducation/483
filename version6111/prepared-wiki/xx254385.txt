[[Identity (BoA album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2010 albums, BoA albums, Avex Group albums

Identity is Korean singer BoA's seventh Japanese studio album, released on February 10, 2010, nearly two years since The Face.

==Promotion==

The album was preceded in release by the singles "Bump Bump!," a collaboration with Verbal from the hip-hop group M-flo, and "Mamoritai: White Wishes." The singles peaked at #5 and #2 on the Oricon charts, respectively.[tpl]ja icon[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://blog.oricon.co.jp/nextstage-rarara/archive/19/0|title=11月9日付 シングル 週間ランキング：NEXT STAGE - スタ☆ブロ -|accessdate=2010-11-03|date=2010-03-04|work=Oricon[/tpl][tpl]ja icon[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://blog.oricon.co.jp/clare/archive/1767/0|title=【結果】シングルデイリー推移 (2009/12/21付け)：Clareのチャートブログ - スタ☆ブロ -|accessdate=2010-12-19|date=2010-03-04|work=Oricon[/tpl] Also, there was an official music video for "Possibility". However, it was not released as a single.

==Chart performance==

Identity debuted at #2 on the daily Oricon albums chart with 14,023 units sold in its first day[tpl]ja icon[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://blog.oricon.co.jp/nextstage-rarara/archive/104/0|title=2月9日付 アルバム デイリーランキング：NEXT STAGE - スタ☆ブロ -|accessdate=2010-02-10|date=2010-03-04|work=Oricon[/tpl] and at #4 on the weekly chart with 37,606 copies sold making it her first Japanese album that did not chart to number 1, ending her streak of studio albums debuting at #1 on the Oricon weekly charts.  "Identity" also became BoA's only studio album to not reach over 100,000 units sold and not receive certification.[tpl]ja icon[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://blog.oricon.co.jp/nextstage-rarara/archive/116/0|title=2月22日付 アルバム 週間ランキング：NEXT STAGE - スタ☆ブロ -|accessdate=2010-02-16|date=2010-03-04|work=Oricon[/tpl]
