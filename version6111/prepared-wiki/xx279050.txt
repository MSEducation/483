[[Chehrehabad]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Chehrehabad ([tpl]lang-fa|چهره اباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Chehrehābād; also known as Chehrābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3058293|Chehrehabad[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 378, in 107 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
