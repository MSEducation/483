[[Gudimalkapur]]

CATEGORIES: Neighbourhoods of Hyderabad, India

Gudimalkapur is a major suburb in Hyderabad, India. It is part of the old city of Hyderabad.
There is a very old huge Lord Venkateswara Temple called "Jhamsing Venkateswara Temple" just besides the market.

==Commercial area==

There are many shops in this area catering to all budgets.
It is home to a big vegetable market called as Gudimalkapur market, where the vegetables prices are very cheap.
There are some 8 function halls located here which is popular during weddings and parties season.

==Transport==

Gudimalkapur has an APSRTC bus depot, but popularly called as Mehdipatnam Bus Depot - It is just beside the famous Amba Theater, making it a hub of many buses bound to all parts of the city.
The closest MMTS Train station is at Nampally.

==Schools==

There are many schools here catering to all economic classes of the society.
