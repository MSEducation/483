[[István Zsolt]]

CATEGORIES: 1921 births, 1991 deaths, Hungarian football referees, FIFA World Cup Final match officials, 1954 FIFA World Cup referees, 1958 FIFA World Cup referees, 1966 FIFA World Cup referees, Olympic football referees

Istvan Zsolt (28 June 1921, Budapest – 7 May 1991) was a Hungarian international football referee. He officiated at the 1954, 1958 and 1966 World Cup tournaments and the Olympic Games of 1952, 1960, 1964 and 1968.
