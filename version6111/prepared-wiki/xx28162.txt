[[KAvZ]]

CATEGORIES: Bus manufacturers, Motor vehicle manufacturers of Russia, Motor vehicle manufacturers of the Soviet Union, GAZ

KAvZ ([tpl]lang-ru|Курга́нский авто́бусный заво́д, КАвЗ[/tpl], Kurgansky Avtobusny Zavod) is a bus manufacturer in Kurgan, Russia. The factory started producing buses in 1958, based on trucks from GAZ. During the 1990s, it assembled Ikarus buses for the Russian market. Now a subsidiary of GAZ, it specialises in producing small buses, in particular school buses.
KaVZ is a subsidiary of Russian Buses which is a subsidiary of GAZ Group .
