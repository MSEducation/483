[[Stonnington City SC]]

CATEGORIES: Association football clubs established in 1976, Greek-Australian culture in Melbourne, Soccer clubs in Melbourne, Soccer clubs in Victoria (Australia), 1976 establishments in Australia

Stonnington City is an Australian soccer club from Malvern, a suburb of Melbourne, Victoria, Australia. The club was formed in 1976 as Malvern City, but changed to Stonnington City in 1995.
