[[The Plant Genome]]

CATEGORIES: Genetics journals, Open access journals, Botany journals

The Plant Genome is an international open-access electronic Academic journal published by the Crop Science Society of America. It provides the readership with advances in plant genomics research.
The journal publishes original research investigating aspects of plant genomics from a diverse array of authors. It publishes reports that use innovative genomic applications that advance understanding of plant biology and may have applications to crop improvement.
