[[Strained tendon]]

CATEGORIES: German musical groups, Musical groups established in 1995

Strained tendon is a German band whose music and artwork is experimental. It was founded in 1995 in the town of Winterberg, Germany.
The members are:
Tobias Basedow (piano,keyboard,voice,arrangement) 
Thomas Hetzler (guitar,voice,producer,artwork)
