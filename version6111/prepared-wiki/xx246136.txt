[[2007 Internationaux de Strasbourg]]

CATEGORIES: 2007 WTA Tour, Internationaux de Strasbourg

The 2007 Internationaux de Strasbourg was a tennis tournament played on outdoor clay courts. It was the 21st edition of the Internationaux de Strasbourg, and was part of the Tier III Series of the 2007 WTA Tour. It took place at the Centre Sportif de Hautepierre in Strasbourg, France, from May 21 through May 26, 2008.
