[[(31017) 1996 EH2]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1996, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(31017) 1996 EH2 is a main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on March 15, 1996.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
