[[1925 Bulgarian State Football Championship]]

CATEGORIES: Bulgarian State Football Championship seasons, 1925 domestic association football leagues, 1925 in Bulgaria

The 1925 Bulgarian State Football Championship was the 2nd season of the Bulgarian State Football Championship.
It was contested by 6 teams and Vladislav Varna was the first champion of Bulgaria.

==Teams==

The teams that participated in the competition were the six winners of their local sport federations.
