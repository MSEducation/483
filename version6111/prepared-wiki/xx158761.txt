[[Functional Plant Biology]]

CATEGORIES: Botany journals, CSIRO Publishing academic journals, Monthly journals, English-language journals

Functional Plant Biology is a monthly scientific journal published by CSIRO Publishing. It covers all aspects of plant physiology, including biochemistry, biophysics, developmental biology, cell and molecular biology, and plant-environment and plant-microbe interactions, and the integration of all of these areas. The journal publishes full papers on experimental or theoretical work, review articles, Viewpoints, Research Notes, Methodological papers, and Comments.
The current editor-in-chief is Rana Munns.
