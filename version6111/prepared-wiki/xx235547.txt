[[David Selvas]]

CATEGORIES: 1971 births, Living people, Spanish male film actors, Male actors from Barcelona

David Selvas (born 21 December 1971) is a Spanish actor. He has appeared in 27 films and television shows since 1996. He starred in the film Pau and His Brother, which was entered into the 2001 Cannes Film Festival.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.festival-cannes.com/en/archives/ficheFilm/id/2001142/year/2001.html |title=Festival de Cannes: Pau and His Brother |accessdate=2009-10-17|work=festival-cannes.com[/tpl]
