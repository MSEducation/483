[[Department of Public Enterprises]]

CATEGORIES: Government departments of South Africa

The Department of Public Enterprises is one of the ministries of the South African government. It is the governments shareholder representative with oversight responsibility for a number of state-owned enterprises (SoEs).

==Enterprises==
