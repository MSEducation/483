[[KSPD]]

CATEGORIES: Religious radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Idaho

KSPD (790 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Christian talk and teaching format, licensed to Boise, Idaho, USA. The station is currently owned by Inspirational Family Radio.
