[[Na Gbewa]]

CATEGORIES: Ghanaian people, Ghanaian royalty

Na Gbewa (also known as Nedega or Kulu Gbagha) is, traditionally, the father of the Dagomba-Mossi states in what is now northern Ghana. His sons and his daughters are credited with founding several states.[tpl]sfn|Lipschutz|Rasmussen|1989|p=168[/tpl][tpl]sfn|Ade Ajayi|Crowder|1976|p=417[/tpl]
