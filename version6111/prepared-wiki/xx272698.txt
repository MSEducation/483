[[KK Škofja Loka]]

CATEGORIES: Basketball clubs established in 1954, Basketball teams in Slovenia, 1954 establishments in Yugoslavia

Košarkarski Klub Škofja Loka ([tpl]lang-en|Škofja Loka Basketball Club[/tpl]), commonly referred to as KK Škofja Loka or simply Škofja Loka, is a professional basketball team that is based in Škofja Loka, Slovenia. The club was founded in 1954.
Its women's team made four appearances in the Ronchetti Cup through the first half of the 1990s.Profile in FIBA Europe's website Its best results in the 1. SKLŽ are 3rd (1992, 1995) and 4th (1993, 1994, 1997, 2004) positions. In 2011 it withdrew from the championship.Profile in eurobasket.com
