[[Brian Irvine (composer)]]

CATEGORIES: 1965 births, Living people, Musicians from Belfast, Alumni of the University of Ulster, Members of the Order of the British Empire, British film score composers, Composers from Northern Ireland, 21st-century classical composers

Brian Irvine, [tpl]post-nominals|country=GBR|MBE[/tpl] (born 1965) is a composer from Northern Ireland. He has written several film scores and his piece Interrupting Cutler, partly based on the work of Ivor Cutler, was a winner in the 2003 BBC Jazz Awards. Irvine was appointed Member of the Order of the British Empire in the 2009 Birthday Honours.[tpl]London Gazette |issue=59090 |date=13 June 2009 |startpage=19 |supp=yes [/tpl]
Irvine's students include Ed Bennett, Dave Kane, Steve Davis and Brian Robinson.
