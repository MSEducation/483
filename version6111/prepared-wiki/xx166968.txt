[[Give it Back to the Indians]]

CATEGORIES: Songs about New York, Songs with music by Richard Rodgers, Songs with lyrics by Lorenz Hart, 1939 songs

"Give it Back to the Indians" is a show tune from the 1939 Rodgers and Hart musical Too Many Girls (1939), where it was introduced by Mary Jane Walsh.
The song is about the island of Manhattan.

==Notable recordings==

Rodgers, Richard; The Rodgers and Hart Song Book; Simon and Schuster, 1951
