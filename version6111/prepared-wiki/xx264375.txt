[[1985 Swedish Open]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 Grand Prix (tennis), Swedish Open

The 1985 Swedish Open was a men's tennis tournament played on outdoor clay courts held in Båstad, Sweden and was part of the Grand Prix circuit of the 1985 Tour. It was the 38th edition of the tournament and was held from July 15 through July 21, 1985. Mats Wilander won the singles title.
