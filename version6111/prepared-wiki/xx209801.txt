[[Hope (Harem Scarem album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 albums, Harem Scarem albums, Frontiers Records albums

Hope is a 2008 album by the Canadian hard rock band Harem Scarem. This is the bands twelfth and final studio album.Harem Scarem | Discography - Hope The European version of the album contains an acoustic version of "Higher", which was originally from their 2003 album of the same name.
