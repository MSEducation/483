[[Chris Baillie]]

CATEGORIES: Scottish athletes, British hurdlers, Commonwealth Games competitors for Scotland, Athletes (track and field) at the 2006 Commonwealth Games, Commonwealth Games silver medallists for Scotland, TeamBath, People from Clydebank, People educated at Clydebank High School, Living people, 1981 births

Christopher ("Chris") Baillie (born 21 April 1981) is a Scottish hurdler from Old Kilpatrick, most noted for his silver medal at the 2006 Commonwealth Games in the 110 metre hurdles.  He is the younger brother of the late Ross Baillie who was also a hurdler.
