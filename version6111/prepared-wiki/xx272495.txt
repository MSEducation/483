[[Woman Trap]]

CATEGORIES: 1929 films, 1920s drama films, American films, American drama films, Black-and-white films, Films directed by William A. Wellman

Woman Trap is a 1929 American drama film directed by William A. Wellman and starring Evelyn Brent.[tpl]cite web |url=http://movies.nytimes.com/movie/117541/Woman-Trap/overview |title=Woman Trap |accessdate=2011-09-25 |work=NY Times[/tpl] This action thriller was Wellman's second sound picture. Woman Trap is focused in a four cornered love between captain Hal Skelly (Clarence Wilson, Kitty Evans(Evelyn Brent) and his good for nothing brother Ray Malone (Chester Morris)
