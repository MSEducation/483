[[Squaw Creek Southern Railroad]]

CATEGORIES: Georgia (U.S. state) railroads, Indiana railroads

The Squaw Creek Southern Railroad is a subsidiary of the Respondek Railroad Corporation. http://www.respondekrailroad.com/ The name comes from an Indiana coal hauling line that Respondek purchased. The name is also used for Respondek's Georgia operation which leases the former Central of Georgia Athens to Macon main line between Madison and Shady Dale, Georgia (Machen, GA). Squaw Creek Southern Railroad is a member of the Indiana Railroad Transportation Group (IRTG). http://www.irtg.org/members.htm
