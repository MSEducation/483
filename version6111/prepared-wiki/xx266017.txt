[[Tomás Bartomeus]]

CATEGORIES: 1982 births, Living people, Paraguayan footballers, Paraguay international footballers

Tomás Bartomeus (born 27 October 1982) is an Paraguayan international footballer who plays for Guaraní, as a defender.

==Career==

Bartomeus has played for Club Universal, Rubio Ñu and Guaraní.[tpl]NFT player|pid=40691[/tpl]
He made his international debut for Paraguay in 2010.
