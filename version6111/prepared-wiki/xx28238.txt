[[Seir]]

Seir ([tpl]Hebrew name|שֵׂעִיר|Se'ir|Śēʻîr[/tpl], "Rough, hairy"). It is sometimes used as an alternative term for a goat, as in Seir La'Azazel (שעיר לעזאזל - scapegoat).
