[[Horqin Left Middle Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Horqin Left Middle Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠷᠴᠢᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠵᠡᠭᠦᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠭᠠᠷᠤᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠳᠤᠮᠳᠠᠳᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Qorčin Jegün Ɣarun Dumdadu qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=科尔沁左翼中旗 |t=科爾沁左翼中旗 |p=Kē'ěrqìn Zuǒyì Zhōngqí[/tpl]), formerly known as Darhan hoshuu, is a banner of eastern Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China, bordering Jilin province to the east. It is under the administration of Tongliao City, [tpl]convert|101|km[/tpl] to the southwest. The local Mongolian dialect is Khorchin Mongolian.
