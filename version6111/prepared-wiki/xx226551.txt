[[I Was A Teenage Serial Killer]]

CATEGORIES: 1993 films, 1990s short films, Black-and-white films, American independent films

I Was a Teenage Serial Killer is an underground no budget film written and directed by "The Queen of Underground Film",http://www.nypress.com/17/7/nyc/ Sarah Jacobson. It is a short black-and-white film of a 19-year-old girl who is sick of sexist men and kills them.http://www.indiewire.com/people/people_040218sarah.html It was Jacobson's first filmhttp://www.avclub.com/content/node/23210 and it was released through her own company, Station Wagon Productions. She made the film under the guidance of her teacher, George Kuchar.http://www.nypress.com/17/7/nyc/ The film featured songs by Heavens to Betsy.
