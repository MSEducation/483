[[Live Session EP (Cat Power album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2006 EPs, Cat Power albums, Live EPs, 2006 live albums, ITunes-exclusive releases

Live Session EP is an iTunes Exclusive album by American singer-songwriter Chan Marshall published in 2006 by Matador Records. It includes an acoustic version of the song "Love & Communication" from Marshall's earlier 2006 album, The Greatest, as well as three previously unreleased cover songs.[tpl]cite web |url= http://drownedinsound.com/news/1129457 |title= Cat Power's new iTunes EP available |last= Diver |first= Mike |date= 2006-09-07 |work= Drowned in Sound |publisher= Silentway |accessdate=2010-01-10[/tpl]
