[[Pangyo Station]]

CATEGORIES: Seoul subway stations, Bundang, Metro stations in Gyeonggi Province, Railway stations opened in 2011

Pangyo Station is a station on the Shinbundang Line, serving the planned community of Pangyo in the city of Seongnam. It began operations on October 28, 2011, with the opening of the Shinbundang Line.
In 2015, it will become the western terminus of the Yeoju Line.
