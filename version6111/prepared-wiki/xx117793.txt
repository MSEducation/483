[[Chilka]]

CATEGORIES: Jat clans of Rajasthan

Chilka or Chalka is a  gotra of Jats found in Jaipur, Sikar and Jhunjhunu districts of Rajasthan, India. 'Chilka' is a Rajasthani language word, which means 'Shining'. These people were fair in colour with a typical signing on their faces. So these people were known as Chilka in Rajasthan.Dr Mahendra Singh Arya, Dharmpal Singh Dudi, Kishan Singh Faujdar & Vijendra Singh Narwar: Ä€dhunik Jat Itihasa (The modern history of Jats), Agra 1998 (Page 243)
