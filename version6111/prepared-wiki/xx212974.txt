[[Ludmiłówka]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Kraśnik County

Ludmiłówka [tpl]IPAc-pl|l|u|d|m|i|'|ł|u|f|k|a[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Dzierzkowice, within Kraśnik County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) – TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|15|km|mi|0[/tpl] west of Kraśnik and [tpl]convert|53|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-west of the regional capital Lublin.
The village has a population of 530.
