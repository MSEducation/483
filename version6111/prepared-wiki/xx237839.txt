[[Anata ni Muchū]]

CATEGORIES: 1973 singles, Candies (group) songs

[tpl]nihongo|"Anata ni Muchū"|あなたに夢中||"Crazy For You"[/tpl] is the debut single of Japanese girl group Candies, released on September 1, 1973. It sold over 81,000 copies.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www7a.biglobe.ne.jp/~yamag/single/candys.html|title= Yamachan Land (Archives of the Japanese record charts) - Singles Chart Daijiten - Candies|language=Japanese|accessdate=2011-05-16[/tpl]
The song features Yoshiko Tanaka (at that time known by her nickname, 'Sue') as the main vocalist from the three.
The song was featured on their album Anata ni Muchū ~Uchiki na Candies~
