[[Timm's Point and Landing]]

CATEGORIES: California Historical Landmarks, Los Angeles Historic-Cultural Monuments, San Pedro, Los Angeles

Timm's Point and Landing is a California Historical Landmark at Los Angeles harbor in the San Pedro neighborhood of Los Angeles.  It is a Los Angeles Historic-Cultural Monument, listed in 1977 as Site of Timm's Landing.
The area was a shipping center in mid-19th century.  No original structures remain.  It is located at northwest end of fish slip and includes a landscaped park in front of Fishermen's Co-op building, east of the harbor bel railroad tracks[tpl]Cite book|title=Historic-Cultural Monuments 1-588|publisher=City of Los Angeles Cultural Affairs Department|date=July 1994[/tpl]
