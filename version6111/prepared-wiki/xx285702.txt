[[Gavin Devine]]

CATEGORIES: Alumni of the University of York

Gavin Devine is a public relations consultant and the CEO of MHP Communications.

==Early life and education==

Devine was born in Barnet, London in 1970. His father, John Hunter Devine was a former footballer, who represented Great Britain in the 1960 Olympic Games.
He was educated at the Royal Grammar School, Guildford. He later studied Politics at the University of York.

==Work==

Devine became a clerk in the House of Commons in 1992, working on the Home Affairs Committee before moving to work on the Transport Committee and Environment, Food and Rural Affairs Committee before becoming deputy principal clerk in 2002.
In 2004, Devine joined AS Biss and Co and progressed to become Deputy MD in 2005.[tpl]clarify|date=November 2013|reason=What's a Deputy MD?  We should be writing for an international audience.[/tpl] Following their merger with Republic PR, he became the Joint MD of Mandate Communications before becoming COO of MHP Communications when that entity was created in 2010. In 2012, he was appointed as CEO of MHP.
In September 2013, Devine was invited to join the Public Relations Consultants Association Board of Management.http://news.prca.org.uk/prca-announces-matt-neale-as-vice-chairman-at-agm/
