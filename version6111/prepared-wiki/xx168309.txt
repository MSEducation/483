Ali Barraud N'Goni (born January 31, 1918)Détail sur la personnalité sélectionnée - BARRAUD N’GONI Ali [tpl]fr icon[/tpl] Retrieved on April 12, 2008 was a Burkinabé politician who served as Minister of Public Health and Population for Upper Volta.[tpl]cite book |author=United Nations |title=Treaty Series |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=o3syAAAAIAAJ&q=%22Ali+Barraud%22&dq=%22Ali+Barraud%22&pgis=1 |accessdate=2007-08-02 |year=1947 |publisher=United Nations |location= |pages=215[/tpl] He resigned his position on January 22, 1974.[tpl]cite news |title=Burkina Faso : la petite histoire des coups d'Etat |url=http://www.mediaf.org/fr/sources/fiche.php?itm=339&md=&thm=&sc=32 |work=L'Observateur Paalga |date=2003-10-08 |accessdate=2007-08-02 |language=French[/tpl]
He was involved in the 1948 founding of the Voltaic Democratic Union (PDV),[tpl]cite book |last=Hodgkin |first=Thomas Lionel |author-link=Thomas Lionel Hodgkin|title=African Political Parties: An Introductory Guide |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=AuEUAAAAIAAJ&q=%22Ali+Barraud%22&dq=%22Ali+Barraud%22&pgis=1 |accessdate=2007-08-02 |year=1971 |publisher=Peter Smith |location= |pages=208[/tpl] which joined with the Social Party for the Emancipation of the African Masses (PSEMA) in 1956 to form the Unified Democratic Party (PDU) electoral alliance.[tpl]cite book |last=Janda |first=Kenneth |title=Political Parties: A Cross-National Survey |year=1980 |publisher=Free Press |location=New York |isbn=0-02-916120-7 |pages=933–34[/tpl]
From 1957 to 1959, he served as a member of the delegation of Upper Volta to the Grand Council of French Western Africa.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.petiteacademie.gov.bf/AutreRepere/AutreRepere.asp?CodeAutreRepere=8922 |title=Détail sur le repère sélectionné |accessdate=2007-08-02 |year=2004 |work=La Petite académie |language=French[/tpl] In 1971, he was elected Vice-Chairman of the Executive Board of the World Health Organization.[tpl]cite book |author=World Health Organization |title=WHO Chronicle |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=N0wgAAAAMAAJ&q=%22Ali+Barraud%22&dq=%22Ali+Barraud%22&pgis=1 |accessdate=2007-08-02 |year=1959 |publisher=World Health Organization |location= |pages=373[/tpl]
