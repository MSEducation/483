[[Perfect (Darin song)]]

CATEGORIES: 2006 singles, Darin songs, Dance-pop songs, Songs written by Robbie Nevil, Songs written by Fredrik Thomander

"Perfect" is a song recorded by Swedish singer Darin. It was released as the first single from Darin's third studio album Break the News in Sweden on October 18, 2006.

==Formats and track listings==

==Charts==

"Perfect" debuted and peaked at number 3 in Sweden, becoming his sixth consecutive top 10 hit and third top 3 hit there. It also landed at number 4 in Finland, becoming his first top 5 single in Finland.
