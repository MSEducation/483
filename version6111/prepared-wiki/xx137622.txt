[[Snowman (album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2006 debut albums, Snowman albums

Snowman is the self-titled debut album, released by the Australian band Snowman on 23 September 2006.APRA database at the Australasian Performing Right Association website
The 13 tracks on the album highlight many of Snowman's signature sounds, including haunting and falsetto vocals, rock and roll guitars and rockabilly drums.
The first single from the album is Smoke & Mirrors.
