[[Qarah Buteh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Qarah Buteh ([tpl]lang-fa|قره بو ته[/tpl], also Romanized as Qarah Būţeh and Qareh Būţeh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3079654|Qarah Buteh[/tpl] is a village in Chaypareh-ye Bala Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 2,747, in 701 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
