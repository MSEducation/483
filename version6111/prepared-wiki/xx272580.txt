[[Hylarana margariana]]

CATEGORIES: Hylarana, Amphibians of Burma, Endemic fauna of Burma

[tpl]italic title[/tpl]

Irrawaddy frog, Hylarana margariana, is a species of true frog in the genus Hylarana. It is native Myanmar and may be present in China.[ref]
