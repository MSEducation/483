[[Mehtar, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Mehtar ([tpl]lang-fa|مهتر[/tpl], also known as Mehmar and Metar)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3074697|Mehtar[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Bala Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 246, in 67 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
