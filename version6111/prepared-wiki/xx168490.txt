[[Giordano Cottur]]

CATEGORIES: 1914 births, 2006 deaths, People from Trieste, Italian male cyclists, Sportspeople from Friuli-Venezia Giulia

Giordano Cottur (May 24, 1914 - March 8, 2006) was an Italian cyclist. He was born in Trieste.
His palmarés include three 3rd places overall at the Giro d'Italia (1940, 1948 and 1949, plus five stage wins) and an 8th overall at the 1947 Tour de France.
