[[Continuum (design consultancy)]]

CATEGORIES: Industrial design firms, Design companies of the United States, Companies based in Massachusetts

Continuum is a design and innovation consultancy based in Newton, Massachusetts, with other offices in Los Angeles, Milan, Shanghai and Seoul.  The company’s core disciplines include Industrial Design, Design strategy, Service Design, Human Factors, Interaction Design, Sustainable Design, Mechanical Engineering and Electrical Engineering.Continuum fact sheet
The company was founded in Boston as Design Continuum in 1983.Continuum Private Company info  Their goal was simple: to design products for businesses. Since then, the mission has grown to include strategy and brand services along with experience and product design.[tpl]cite news |last=Hamm |first=Steve |coauthors=Walters, Helen |url=http://www.businessweek.com/innovate/content/jan2008/id20080116_412812.htm?chan=search |title=Masters of Collaboration |publisher=Business Week |date=2008-01-16 |accessdate=2008-02-16[/tpl]
