[[Charles Kesteven]]

CATEGORIES: 1923 deaths, British solicitors, Indian solicitors, Knights Bachelor, Year of birth missing

Sir Charles Henry Kesteven (died 18 January 1923) was Solicitor to the Government of India from 1904 to 1919, and Solicitor to the Government of Bengal from 1919 to 1923. He was knighted in the 1920 New Year Honours.[tpl]LondonGazette |issue=31712 |date=30 December 1919 |startpage=3 |supp=yes[/tpl]
