[[Kolo Kapanui]]

CATEGORIES: 1983 births, Living people, Native Hawaiian people, Sportspeople from Hawaii, American football tight ends, West Texas A&M Buffaloes football players, Undrafted National Football League players, Cleveland Browns players, New Orleans Saints players, Cincinnati Bengals players, Florida Tuskers players

Kolo Kapanui (born November 13, 1983 in Palolo, Hawaii) is an American football tight end free agent for the Florida Tuskers of the United Football League. He was signed by the Cleveland Browns as an undrafted free agent in 2008. He played college football at West Texas A&M. He was cut by the Bengals on September 6, 2009.
