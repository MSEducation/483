[[13025 Zürich]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Astronomical objects discovered in 1989

13025 Zürich is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1343.3353105 days (3.68 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=13025 | title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-05-24 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on January 28, 1989.
