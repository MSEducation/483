[[Martin Jurkemik]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 births, Living people, Slovak footballers, Association football defenders, FK Slovan Duslo Šaľa players, MFK Ružomberok players, ŠK SFM Senec players, Slovak First League players, Sportspeople from Bratislava

Martin Jurkemik (born 14 November 1989 in Bratislava) is a Slovak football defender who last played for Slovak 2. liga club ŠK SFM Senec.

==MFK Ružomberok==

In summer 2011, he joined Slovak club MFK Ružomberok on two-year contract. He made his debut for MFK RužomberokMasaryk dvomi gólmi rozhodol o triumfe Ruže v Trnave 24.09.2011, profutbal.sk against FC Spartak Trnava on 30 April 2011.
