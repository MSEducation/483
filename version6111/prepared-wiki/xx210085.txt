[[Petri Mór]]

CATEGORIES: Hungarian academics, People from Tăşnad, Hungarian journalists, Hungarian schoolteachers, Hungarian historians, Hungarian anthropologists, Political analysts, Hungarian sociologists, Hungarian educators, 1863 births, 1945 deaths

Petri Mór (b. Tăşnad, July 11, 1863 - Budapest, March 2, 1945) was a teacher, school inspector and author in Transylvania. 
His masterwork is Szilágy vármegye monographiája (I-IV, Budapest, 1901-1904).
