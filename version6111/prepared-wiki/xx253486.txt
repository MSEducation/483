[[No Time for Flowers]]

CATEGORIES: Films directed by Don Siegel, 1952 films, American films, 1950s romantic comedy films

No Time for Flowers is a 1952 film directed by Don Siegel. 

==Plot==

A young girl's loyalty to the Communist Party is tested in Prague when she falls in love with an attache who has just arrived from the United States.
