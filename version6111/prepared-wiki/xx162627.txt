[[Crystal Kay discography]]

CATEGORIES: Pop music discographies, Discographies of Japanese artists, Rhythm and blues discographies, Articles containing Japanese-language text

The discography of Crystal Kay, a Japanese pop singer. Kay debuted in 1999 with the single "Eternal Memories" at the age of thirteen. Her 2007 album "All Yours" became her first number-one release in June of that year. Her best selling single is [tpl]nihongo|"Koi ni Ochitara"|恋におちたら|If I Fall in Love[/tpl].
