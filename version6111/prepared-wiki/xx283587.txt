[[Srishty Rode]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Indian television actresses, Indian soap opera actresses

Srishty Rode is an Indian soap opera television actress. 
She started her career with the role of Manjari in Star One's television series Yeh Ishq Haaye. She later played Shobha in Shobha Somnath Ki  on Zee TV and Sarita in Punar Vivah - Ek Nayi Umeed.
