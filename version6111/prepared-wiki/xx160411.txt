[[Acanthosicyos horridus]]

CATEGORIES: Cucurbitaceae, Flora of Namibia, Desert fruit, Drought-tolerant plants

Acanthosicyos horridus is an unusual melon that occurs only in Namibia; it is locally called nara.
The nara plant is leafless, the modified stems and spines serve as the photosynthetic "organs" of the plant.
The edible seeds are known locally as butterpips.Grubben, G.J.H. & Denton, O.A. (2004) Plant Resources of Tropical Africa 2. Vegetables. PROTA Foundation, Wageningen; Backhuys, Leiden; CTA, Wageningen.
