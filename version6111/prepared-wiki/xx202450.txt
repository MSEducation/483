[[UEFA Euro 1980 qualifying Group 3]]

CATEGORIES: UEFA Euro 1980 qualifying, 1978–79 in Spanish football, 1979–80 in Spanish football

Standings and results for Group 3 of the 1980 UEFA European Football Championship qualifying tournament.
Group 3 consisted of Cyprus, Romania, Spain and Yugoslavia. Group winners were Spain, who pipped Yugoslavia by a single point.
