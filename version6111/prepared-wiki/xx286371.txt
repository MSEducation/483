[[Aqbolagh-e Humeh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Aqbolagh-e Humeh ([tpl]lang-fa|اقبلاغ حومه[/tpl], also Romanized as Āqbolāgh-e Ḩūmeh; also known as Ak-Bulag, Āq Bolāgh,  Āqbulāq, Āt Bolāgh, and Kharābeh Āghbolāgh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3053392|Aqbolagh-e Humeh[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Bala Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 718, in 159 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
