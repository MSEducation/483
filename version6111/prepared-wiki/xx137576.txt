Norte 45 ([tpl]Lang-es|Estación Norte 45[/tpl]) is a station on Line 6 of the Mexico City Metro.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.metro.df.gob.mx/red/estacion.html?id=86|title=Norte 45|language=Spanish|accessdate=2 August 2011[/tpl][tpl]Cite web|last=Archambault|first=Richard|url=http://mexicometro.org/metro/line6/norte-45/|title=Norte 45 »  Mexico City Metro System|accessdate=2 August 2011[/tpl] It is located in the Colonia Pueblo Salinas neighborhood.
The logo for this station is a compass rose and the station takes its name from a nearby street that runs from northeast to southwest (somewhat unusual for streets in Mexico City that typically run either north-south or east-west). The station opened on 21 December 1983.[tpl]Cite web|last=Schwandl|first=Robert|url=http://www.urbanrail.net/am/mexi/mex-history.htm|title=Opening Dates for Mexico City's Subway|accessdate=2 August 2011[/tpl]
