[[Gouranga Biswas]]

CATEGORIES: Indian footballers, 1987 births, Living people, People from Kolkata, Footballers from West Bengal, I-League players, East Bengal F.C. players, Air India FC players, Prayag United S.C. players

Gouranga Biswas (born 17 December 1987 in Kolkata) is an Indian footballer who is currently playing for Eagles F.C. on loan from IMG RELIANCE as a midfielder.
