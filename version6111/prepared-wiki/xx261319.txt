[[2011 Internationaux de Nouvelle-Calédonie – Doubles]]

CATEGORIES: 2011 ATP Challenger Tour, Internationaux de Nouvelle-Calédonie

Édouard Roger-Vasselin and Nicolas Devilder were the defending champions but did not participate. No 2 seeds Dominik Meffert and Frederik Nielsen won the final against the No. 1 seeds Flavio Cipolla and Simone Vagnozzi, 7–6(4), 5–7, 10–5.
