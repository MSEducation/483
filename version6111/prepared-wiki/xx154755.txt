[[Rudy White]]

Rudolph (Rudy) White (born June 23, 1953, in Silver City, New Mexico) is a retired American basketball player. He was a 6'2" (1.88 m), 195 lb (88.5 kg) guard and attended Arizona State University and played in the NBA from 1975 to 1981 with three teams.
White was selected in the third round of the 1975 NBA Draft by the Houston Rockets and in the second round of the 1975 ABA Draft by the Spirits of St. Louis.BasketballReference.com Rudy White page
