[[Basarak]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Basarak ([tpl]lang-fa|بصرك[/tpl], also Romanized as Başarak; also known as Basazak, Basrag, and Bastarak)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3055683|Basarak[/tpl] is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 271, in 54 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
