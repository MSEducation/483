[[Lordsburg (Amtrak station)]]

CATEGORIES: Amtrak stations in New Mexico, Buildings and structures in Hidalgo County, New Mexico, Transportation in Hidalgo County, New Mexico, Southern Pacific Railroad stations

The Lordsburg, New Mexico (Amtrak Station) (LDB) is located at Center Street and S.P. Blvd. in Lordsburg, New Mexico.
The former station has been demolished. There is presently no shelter or platform for passengers.
