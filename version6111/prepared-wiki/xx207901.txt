[[Robert Kasprzak]]

CATEGORIES: 1987 births, Living people, Polish speedway riders, People from Leszno, Sportspeople from Greater Poland Voivodeship

Robert Kasprzak (born April 8, 1987 in Leszno, PolandŚwiat Żużla, No 1 (73) / 2008, pages 40-41, [tpl]ISSN|1429-3285[/tpl]) is an international speedway rider who was a member of Poland U-21 national team.
He is a son of former Polish national speedway team member Zenon Kasprzak. His brother Krzysztof (born 1984) is also a speedway rider who was a 2008 Speedway Grand Prix rider and who won the 2005 Individual U-21 World Champion title.
