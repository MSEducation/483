[[KAECHS]]

CATEGORIES: Neighbourhoods of Karachi

KAECHS (Karachi Administration Employees Co-operative Housing Society) ([tpl]lang-ur|  کے اے سی ایچ ایس   [/tpl]) is a suburb of Jamshed Town in Karachi, Sindh, Pakistan.  
There are several ethnic groups living in KAECHS and they including Muhajirs, Punjabis, Sindhis, Kashmiris, Seraikis, Pakhtuns, Balochis, Memons, Bohras, Ismailis, etc. Over 99% of the population is Muslim. The population of Jamshed Town is estimated to be nearly one million.
