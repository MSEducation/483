[[Talmi-Teshub]]

CATEGORIES: Kings of Carchemish, 12th-century BC rulers

Talmi-Teshub was "the great-great-great-grandson of Suppiluliuma I" and a viceroy at Carchemish in Syria under Suppiluliuma II.Trevor R. Bryce, The Kingdom of the Hittites, Oxford University Press, 1998. p.384 According to royal seal impressions found at Lidar Höyük found in 1985 on the east bank of the Euphrates river, Talmi-Teshub was succeeded by his own son, Kuzi-Teshub.Bryce, op. cit., p.384
