[[Barák Workers Association]]

CATEGORIES: Organisations based in Austria, Czech educationists, Czechs in Austria, Interwar period

The Barák Workers Association ([tpl]lang-de|Arbeitervereinigung Barák[/tpl]) was a Czech educational organization in Austria during the interbellum years. The association was linked to the Czech National Socialist Party, and was an important pillar of the party in Austria.Brousek, Karl M. Wien und seine Tschechen: Integration und Assimilation einer Minderheit im 20. Jahrhundert. Schriftenreihe des Österreichischen Ost- und Südosteuropa-Instituts, Bd. 7. München: Oldenbourg, 1980. p. 75
