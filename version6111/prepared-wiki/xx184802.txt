[[Valley Medical Center Heliport]]

CATEGORIES: Hospital heliports in Oregon, McMinnville, Oregon, Transportation in Yamhill County, Oregon, Buildings and structures in Yamhill County, Oregon

Valley Medical Center Heliport [tpl]Airport codes|||OG38[/tpl] is a private heliport located at Willamette Valley Medical Center, two miles southeast of McMinnville in Yamhill County, Oregon, United States.
