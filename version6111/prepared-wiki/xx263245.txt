[[San Antone / The Great Divide]]

CATEGORIES: 2009 singles, Whiskeytown songs, Record Store Day releases, Songs written by Ryan Adams, Songs written by Phil Wandscher, Geffen Records singles

"San Antone" / "The Great Divide" is a limited edition 7" vinyl single by alternative country band Whiskeytown, released by Geffen Records in 2009 for Record Store Day.  Both tracks are previously unreleased recordings from the band's 1996 "Baseball Park" sessions.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.recordstoreday.com/Page/642|title=Record Store Day Releases - 2009|date=2009|accessdate=2011-02-01|publisher=Record Store Day[/tpl]
A live version of "The Great Divide" was previously issued on the Power of Tower: Live from the WXDU Lounge benefit compilation CD in 1996.[tpl]cite AV media notes |title=Power of Tower - Live from the WXDU Lounge |others=Various Artists |year=1996 |publisher=Cred Factory Records |id=CRED 008 |location=Chapel Hill, NC [/tpl]
