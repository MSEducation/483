[[Almalu, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Almalu ([tpl]lang-fa|المالو[/tpl], also Romanized as Ālmālū; also known as Almali)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3052821|Almalu[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 67, in 16 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
