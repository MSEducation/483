[[Flüela Schwarzhorn]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of the Alps, Alpine three-thousanders, Mountains of Switzerland, Mountains of Graubünden

The Flüela Schwarzhorn is a mountain of the Albula Alps, overlooking the Flüela Pass, in the canton of Graubünden. With a height of 3,146 metres above sea level, it is the highest point of the Albula Alps north of the Fuorcla da Grialetsch (2,537 m). From the Flüela Pass a trail lead to the summit.
