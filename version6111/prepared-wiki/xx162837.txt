[[Tony Settles]]

CATEGORIES: 1964 births, Living people, People from Laurinburg, North Carolina, American football linebackers, Elon Phoenix football players, Washington Redskins players

Anthony C. Settles (born August 29, 1964) is a former American football linebacker in the National Football League for the Washington Redskins.  He played college football at Elon University.  He played high school football at Scotland High School in Laurinburg, North Carolina.
