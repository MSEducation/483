[[École élémentaire catholique Élisabeth-Bruyère]]

CATEGORIES: Educational institutions established in 1998, French-language elementary schools in Ontario, Roman Catholic elementary schools in Ontario, 1998 establishments in Canada

 
École élémentaire catholique Élisabeth-Bruyère is an elementary school situated in the Western region of Ottawa, Ontario, Canada.
