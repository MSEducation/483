[[Jukka Laamanen]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1976 births, Finnish ice hockey players, Ässät players, HPK players, Mikkelin Jukurit players, Oulun Kärpät players, SaPKo players, Tappara players

Jukka Laamanen (born October 4, 1976) is a Finnish professional ice hockey player who currently plays for HPK in the SM-liiga.
