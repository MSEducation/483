[[Myron Allen]]

CATEGORIES: 1854 births, 1924 deaths, Major League Baseball outfielders, Baseball players from New York, People from Kingston, New York, New York Gothams players, Boston Beaneaters players, Cleveland Blues (1887–1888) players, Kansas City Cowboys players, Minor league baseball managers, Richmond Virginias players, Albany Governors players, Olean (minor league baseball) players, Kingston Patriarchs players, Kingston Colonels players, Binghamton Crickets players, 19th-century baseball players

Myron Smith Allen (March 22, 1854 – March 8, 1924) nicknamed "Zeke", was a Major League Baseball outfielder who played for four seasons. He played for the New York Gothams in 1883, the Boston Beaneaters in 1886, the Cleveland Blues in 1887, and the Kansas City Cowboys in 1888.
