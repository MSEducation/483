[[Vrenska Gorca]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Municipality of Kozje

Vrenska Gorca ([tpl]IPA-sl|ˈwreːnska ˈɡoːrtsa|pron[/tpl]) is a settlement south of Buče in the Municipality of Kozje in eastern Slovenia. The municipality is included in the Savinja Statistical Region. The area is part of the historical region of Styria.Kozje municipal site
