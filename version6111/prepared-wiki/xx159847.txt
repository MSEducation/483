[[Police Mutual]]

CATEGORIES: Insurance companies of the United Kingdom, Friendly societies, Mutual insurance companies

Police Mutual is a mutual society, dedicated to helping Serving and Retired Police Officers, Police Staff and their families with their financial wellbeing.
Set up by the Police in 1922 for the financial welfare of Police Officers, Police Mutual is a leading provider of Financial Services and welfare support to the Police Service. Today, with over 210,000 members trusting them with investments, insurance and savings, Police Mutual is the UKâ€™s largest affinity friendly society. Open to both Officers and Staff, serving and retired, Police Mutual offers a full range of financial planning help and services and shares its unique insight into the Police market by collaborating with forces across the UK to promote financial health and wellbeing.
