[[Central Electronics Engineering Research Institute]]

CATEGORIES: Research institutes in India, Engineering research institutes, Science and technology in Tamil Nadu, Science and technology in Rajasthan

Central Electronics Engineering Research Institute (CEERI), located at Pilani, Rajasthan and Chennai, Tamil Nadu is a pioneer research institute in India and a constituent laboratory of Council of Scientific and Industrial Research (CSIR India), New Delhi. It was established in 1953 for advanced research and development in the field of Electronics.[ref]
