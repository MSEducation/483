[[Kanako Mitsuhashi]]

CATEGORIES: 1978 births, Living people, Japanese voice actresses, People from Yokohama

[tpl]nihongo|Kanako Mitsuhashi|三橋 加奈子|Mitsuhashi Kanako|January 10, 1978[/tpl] is a Japanese voice actress from the Hodogaya-ku ward of Yokohama, Japan. Mitsuhashi is probably most known for her roles as Killua Zoldyck from Hunter × Hunter, Rico from Gunslinger Girl, and Suzuka Asahina from Suzuka. She was married to fellow voice actor Kōji Yusa, but recently got divorced.
