Gerry Hundt (born May 29, 1977, Appleton, Wisconsin) is an American Chicago blues musician and multi-instrumentalist. His favoured instrument is the mandolin. Hundt released his debut solo album, Since Way Back, in early 2007.

==Biography==

Born in Appleton, Wisconsin, Hundt's family relocated to Rockford, Illinois when he was aged three.[tpl]cite web |url=[tpl]Allmusic|class=artist|id=p859077/biography|pure_url=yes[/tpl]|title=Gerry Hundt |author=Poet, J |publisher=Allmusic |accessdate=February 3, 2010[/tpl]
He has garnered respect for his work with other musicians such as John-Alex Mason and Nick Moss. This led to his 2007 solo album, Since Way Back,Myspace.comCdbaby.com(on Blue Bella Records), which earned him nominations for the Blues Foundation Blues Music Award for Best Instrumentalist, Other (mandolin)Blues.org in 2008, 2009, 2010, and 2011.
