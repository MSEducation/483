[[3826 Handel]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Asteroids named for people, Astronomical objects discovered in 1973

3826 Handel is a small main belt asteroid. It was discovered by F. Borngen in 1973. It is named after George Frideric Handel, the eighteenth century German composer.
