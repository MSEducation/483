[[Marriott, Saskatchewan]]

CATEGORIES: Marriott No. 317, Saskatchewan, Unincorporated communities in Saskatchewan

[tpl]Other uses|Marriott (disambiguation)[tpl]![/tpl]Marriott[/tpl]
Marriott is a hamlet in Marriott Rural Municipality 317, Saskatchewan, Canada. The hamlet is located south of the Town of Biggar and north of the Town of Rosetown at the intersection of Highway 4 & Highway 768.
