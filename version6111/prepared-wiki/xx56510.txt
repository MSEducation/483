[[Day for Night (The Tragically Hip album)]]

CATEGORIES: 1994 albums, The Tragically Hip albums, MCA Records albums

Day for Night is the fourth full-length album by the Canadian rock band The Tragically Hip. It was released in September 1994. It is the band's first album to debut at #1 on the Canadian Albums Chart.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.collectionscanada.gc.ca/rpm/028020-119.01-e.php?&file_num=nlc008388.2612&type=1&interval=50&PHPSESSID=c6btf3r8hs459qqt5ln3o3dcv5|title=Top Albums/CDs - Volume 60, No. 11, October 03 1994|publisher=RPM|accessdate=2011-12-03[/tpl]

==Charts==

The album has been certified 6x platinum  in Canada.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.musiccanada.com/GPSearchResult.aspx?st=Day%20For%20Night&ica=False&sa=The%20Tragically%20Hip&sl=&smt=0&sat=-1&ssb=Artist|title=Gold Platinum Database: The Tragically Hip - Day For Night|accessdate=2011-12-03[/tpl]

==Production==

The album was produced by Mark Howard, Mark Vreeken and The Tragically Hip.

==Saturday Night Live==

The band also appeared on Saturday Night Live in 1995, performing "Grace, Too" and "Nautical Disaster" in an episode hosted by John Goodman, in order to promote the album.

==Track listing==

All songs were written by The Tragically Hip.
