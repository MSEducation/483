[[Jamie Kennedy (ice hockey)]]

CATEGORIES: 1946 births, Canadian ice hockey centres, Ice hockey people from Prince Edward Island, Living people, New York Raiders players, People from Charlottetown

Jamie Kennedy (born September 7, 1946 in Charlottetown, Prince Edward Island) is a retired World Hockey Association player. He played 54 games for the New York Raiders. He scored 4 goals and 6 assists for 10 points.
