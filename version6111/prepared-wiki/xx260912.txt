[[Saudi Arabia–United Arab Emirates relations]]

CATEGORIES: Saudi Arabia–United Arab Emirates relations, Bilateral relations of Saudi Arabia, Bilateral relations of the United Arab Emirates

Saudi Arabia – United Arab Emirates relations refers to the current and historical relationship between Saudi Arabia and the United Arab Emirates. Saudi Arabia maintains an embassy in Abu Dhabi and a consulate in Dubai while the U.A.E. has an embassy in Riyadh and consulate in Jeddah. Both countries are neighbours and as part of the Middle East Gulf region, share extensive political and cultural ties. The two countries are also known for having a border dispute.
