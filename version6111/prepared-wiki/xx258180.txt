John Cootes is an Australian former professional rugby league footballer and Roman Catholic priest.Paddy McAteer (22 December 2010) "Whole World in their Hands" North West Evening Mail An Australian international representative three-quarter back and adept goal-kicker, he played club football in the Newcastle Rugby League for Western Suburbs and also later worked as a television commentator and presenter.
Trained during boyhood by Clive Churchill, Cootes came from Newcastle and later played for the Lazio rugby union club in 1966 while on scholarship to study theology at Rome's Propaganda College.[ref]
