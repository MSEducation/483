[[Look Alive (EP)]]

CATEGORIES: We Are the Ocean albums, 2009 EPs

Look Alive is the second EP by post-hardcore band We Are the Ocean. The EP was released on November 16, 2009, and the songs were used for their full album release in January 2010, Cutting Our Teeth. The song 'Cutting Our Teeth' was used as the album title, although was not actually included in the original album. It was, however, included in the deluxe re-issue of the album. A promotional video was released for the song 'Look Alive'.
