[[Gorsty Knoll]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Gloucestershire, Forest of Dean

Gorsty Knoll is a small hamlet, located in the Forest of Dean, Gloucestershire, England. The internationally important remains of Darkhill Ironworks and the Titanic Steelworks, are located on the edge of the hamlet.www.way-mark.co.uk Gorsty Knoll is also famed for its Glow worms.Forestry Commission website
