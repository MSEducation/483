[[Dronten railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations on the Hanzelijn, Railway stations in Flevoland, Railway stations opened in 2012

Dronten is a railway station, on the Hanzelijn from Lelystad to Zwolle. The station is located in the north of the town of Dronten.
The station opened on 9 December 2012, with there being 2 platforms and 4 tracks, for passing Intercities from The Hague and Schiphol to Groningen and Leeuwarden. The station is located on a viaduct station at 7 m above street level.
