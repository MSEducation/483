[[Virginia's Husband (1934 film)]]

CATEGORIES: 1934 films, 1930s comedy films, British films, British comedy films, English-language films, Films directed by Maclean Rogers, Films based on plays

Virginia's Husband is a 1934 British comedy film directed by Maclean Rogers and starring Dorothy Boyd, Reginald Gardiner and Enid Stamp-Taylor.http://ftvdb.bfi.org.uk/sift/title/56770 It was based on the play Virginia's Husband by Florence Kilpatrick. A woman enlists a man to pose as her husband to trick her aunt.
