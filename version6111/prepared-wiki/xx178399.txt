[[Gmina Boguty-Pianki]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Ostrów Mazowiecka County

Gmina Boguty-Pianki is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Ostrów Mazowiecka County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Boguty-Pianki, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|36|km|mi|0[/tpl] east of Ostrów Mazowiecka and [tpl]convert|112|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north-east of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|89.13|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 2,900.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Boguty-Pianki is bordered by the gminas of Ciechanowiec, Czyżew-Osada, Klukowo and Nur.
