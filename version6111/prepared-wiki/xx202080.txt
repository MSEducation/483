[[Jerold Promes]]

CATEGORIES: 1984 births, Living people, Dutch footballers, Dutch people of Surinamese descent, Eredivisie players, Eerste Divisie players, RKC Waalwijk players, Sparta Rotterdam players, SC Telstar players, VVV-Venlo players, Sportspeople from Amsterdam

Jerold Promes (born 9 March 1984) is a Dutch footballer who currently plays for Dutch Eerste Divisie side VVV-Venlo.

==Club career==

Promes, born in Amsterdam, came through the youth ranks of Ajax but did not make the grade at his hometown club and was sold to RKC Waalwijk in 2004. After a long injury lay-off he was signed by AZ to play in their youth team and then Danny Blind lined him up at Sparta Rotterdam in summer 2007. He left the club in 2009, joining SC Telstar in the Dutch second division. After playing four seasons for Telstar, he left the club as a free agent and signed with VVV-Venlo in July 2013.
