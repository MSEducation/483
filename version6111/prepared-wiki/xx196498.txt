[[Dejiang County]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Guizhou

Dejiang County (Chinese: 德江县; Pinyin: Déjiāng Xiàn) is a county belonging to Tongren Prefecture, Guizhou Province of China.
Area: 2072 km2.
Population: 420,000 in 2002.
Postal Code: 565200.
The government is located in Qinlong town.
