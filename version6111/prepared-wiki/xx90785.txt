[[University of Lyon]]

CATEGORIES: University of Lyon, Educational institutions established in 2007, 2007 establishments in France

The University of Lyon (Université de Lyon), located in Lyon and Saint-Étienne, France, is a center for higher education and research comprising 16 institutions of higher education. The three main universities in this center are: Claude Bernard University Lyon 1, which focuses upon health and science studies and has approximately 27,000 students; Lumière University Lyon 2, which focuses upon the social sciences and has about 30,000 students; Jean Moulin University Lyon 3, which focuses upon the humanities and law with about 20,000 students.http://lyon-university.org/
The other member institutions are:http://lyon-university.org/about-universite-de-lyon/members-128616.kjsp?RH=PENudl&RF=PENmembers

==Research==

A team from the university conducted archaeology in Cyprus at Salamis and (from 1976) Kition (in present-day Larnaca).Kition
