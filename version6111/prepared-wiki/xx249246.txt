[[Perry Local School District (Stark County)]]

CATEGORIES: Educational institutions established in 1853, School districts in Stark County, Ohio

Perry Local School District is a public school district serving students in Perry Township, Stark County, Ohio and portions of the city of Massillon, Ohio.
