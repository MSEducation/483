[[Aleksei Sergeyevich Kovalyov]]

CATEGORIES: 1995 births, Living people, Russian footballers, FC Energiya Volzhsky players

Aleksei Sergeyevich Kovalyov ([tpl]lang-ru|Алексей Серге́евич Ковалёв[/tpl]; born June 8, 1995) is a Russian football defender, who currently playing for FC Energiya Volzhsky.
He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Energiya Volzhsky on July 17, 2013 in a game against FC Chernomorets Novorossiysk.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Russian Football Union|url=http://rfspro.ru/cgi-bin/player/script2.cgi?par1=119592|title=Career Summary|date=14 August 2013[/tpl]
