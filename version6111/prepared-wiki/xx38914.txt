[[Glabbeek]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Flemish Brabant, Cities and towns in Flanders, Populated places in Belgium

Glabbeek ([tpl]IPA-nl|ˈɣ̟l̪ɑbeːk[/tpl]) is a municipality located in the Belgian province of Flemish Brabant. The municipality comprises the towns of Attenrode, Bunsbeek, Glabbeek proper, Kapellen, Wever and Zuurbemde. On January 1, 2006, Glabbeek had a total population of 5,189. The total area is 26.78 km² which gives a population density of 194 inhabitants per km².
