[[Tarrah Harvey]]

CATEGORIES: Canadian female ice dancers, 1989 births, Sportspeople from Vancouver, Living people

Tarrah Harvey (born December 20, 1989 in Vancouver) is a Canadian ice dancer who competes with partner Keith Gagnon. They are the 2009 Canadian junior silver medalists 2011/2012 Senior National Team Members and Senior Grand Prix competitors. They began skating together in June 1998.
