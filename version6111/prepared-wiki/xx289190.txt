[[Bakhti]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Bakhti ([tpl]lang-fa|بختي[/tpl], also Romanized as Bakhtī)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3054901|Bakhti[/tpl] is a village in Shivanat Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 231, in 56 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
