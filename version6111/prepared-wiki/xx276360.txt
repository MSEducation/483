[[(42930) 1999 TM11]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1999, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(42930) 1999 TM11 is a main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on October 6, 1999.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
