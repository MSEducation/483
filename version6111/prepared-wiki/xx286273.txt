[[Sheykh Jaber]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Sheykh Jaber ([tpl]lang-fa|شيخ جابر[/tpl], also Romanized as Sheykh Jāber)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3796030|Sheykh Jaber[/tpl] is a village in Qoltuq Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 311, in 67 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
