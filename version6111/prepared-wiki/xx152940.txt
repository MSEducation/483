Youssouf Hersi (born 20 August 1982) is an Ethiopian-Dutch professional footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder for A-League side Western Sydney Wanderers.[tpl]soccerway|youssouf-hersi/2127[/tpl]

==Career==

===Club===

===International===

Hersi played with the Dutch under-21 team at the 2001 FIFA World Youth Championship.[tpl]FIFA player|180812[/tpl]
He was never capped for the Dutch full national side, and although he had expressed an interest in representing the nation of his birth Ethiopia at the international level, he was unable to do so due to FIFA regulations.[tpl]cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport2/hi/football/africa/4237185.stm|title=Hersi keen on Ethiopia|date=4 February 2005|author=Nicolas White|publisher=BBC Sport[/tpl]
