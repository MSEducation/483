[[South Side Park (Winston-Salem)]]

CATEGORIES: Defunct baseball venues, Baseball venues in North Carolina, Sports venues in Winston-Salem, North Carolina

South Side Park aka Southside Park was a baseball park in Winston-Salem, North Carolina. It was the home field of the Winston-Salem minor league ball clubs prior to the opening of Ernie Shore Field in 1956, which was constructed after fire destroyed the old South Side venue.
The wooden structure was located at South Main Street and Waughtown Street. The site is now occupied by various commercial buildings and athletic facilities.
