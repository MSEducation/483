[[Boguchar]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Voronezh Oblast, Voronezh Governorate

Boguchar ([tpl]lang-ru|Богуча́р[/tpl]) is a town and the administrative center of Bogucharsky District in Voronezh Oblast, Russia, located on the Boguchar River (a tributary of the Don), [tpl]convert|243|km|sp=us[/tpl] south of Voronezh, the administrative center of the oblast. Population: [tpl]ru-census|p2010=11,811|p2002=13,756|p1989=8,499[/tpl]

==History==

Town status was granted to it in 1779.[tpl]citation needed|date=December 2013[/tpl]

==Administrative and municipal status==

Within the framework of administrative divisions, Boguchar serves as the administrative center of Bogucharsky District.Law #87-OZ As an administrative division, it is incorporated within Bogucharsky District as Boguchar Urban Settlement. As a municipal division, this administrative unit also has urban settlement status and is a part of Bogucharsky Municipal District.Law #63-OZ

==Military==

The 10th Guards Uralsko-Lvovskaya Tank Division of the Russian Ground Forces is based there.
