[[Sourgou Department]]

CATEGORIES: Departments of Burkina Faso, Boulkiemdé Province

Sourgou is a department or commune of Boulkiemdé Province in central Burkina Faso. As of 2005 it has a population of 13,878. Its capital lies at the town of Sourgou.Burkinabé government inforoute communale
