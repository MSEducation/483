[[Qushchi, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Qushchi ([tpl]lang-fa|قوشچي[/tpl], also Romanized as Qūshchī; also known as Qūshcheh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3080531|Qushchi[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 179, in 43 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
