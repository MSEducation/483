[[Zażółkiew]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Krasnystaw County

Zażółkiew [tpl]IPAc-pl|z|a|'|ż|u|U|k|J|e|f[/tpl] ([tpl]lang-uk|Зажолків[/tpl], Zazholkiv) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Krasnystaw, within Krasnystaw County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) – TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|5|km|mi|0[/tpl] south-west of Krasnystaw and [tpl]convert|50|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-east of the regional capital Lublin.
