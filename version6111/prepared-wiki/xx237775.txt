[[Ezrat Torah]]

CATEGORIES: Neighbourhoods of Jerusalem

Ezrat Torah ([tpl]lang-he|עזרת תורה|[/tpl], in Ashkenazi Hebrew pronunciation: Ezras Torah)  is a Haredi neighborhood in northern Jerusalem. It is bordered by Kiryat Sanz on the west, Golda Meir Blvd. on the north and east, and Tel Arza on the south.

==History==

Founded around 1970,[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.eifermanrealty.com/ShowNb.aspx?id=25 |title=Ezrat Torah |publisher=Eiferman Realty |accessdate=27 August 2011[/tpl] Ezrat Torah is named for the Ezras Torah Fund, a Jewish American charitable organization.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.jerusalem.muni.il/jer_sys/pro/rehovot/rehovot.asp?STREET_KOD=390 |title=Jerusalem Municipality street name information |language=Hebrew |publisher=Jerusalem Municipality[/tpl][tpl]cite book |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=yqXXD_74yAEC&pg=PA104&dq=ezras+torah+jerusalem+who+what+eisenberg&hl=en#v=onepage&q&f=false |title=The streets of Jerusalem: who, what, why |last=Eisenberg |first=Ronald L. |page=104 |publisher=Devora Publishing |year=2006|isbn=1-932687-54-8[/tpl]

==Rabbinic presence==

Rabbi Yechiel Michel Stern, the author of 84 published Torah works who is considered an expert on the halakhot of the Four Species, has served as the Rav of Ezrat Torah since the 1970s.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.haaretz.com/print-edition/news/the-species-master-1.314527 |title=The Species Master: Rabbi Yechiel Stern is considered the supreme authority in his Jerusalem neighborhood and beyond on everything related to the Four Species of Sukkot |last=Ettinger |first=Yair |date=19 September 2010 |work=Haaretz|accessdate=27 August 2011[/tpl][tpl]cite web |url=http://www.mishpacha.com/Browse/Article/700/A-Kindness-Repaid |last=Shulman |first=Eliezer |title=A Kindness Repaid: How Rav Yechiel Michel Stern's forgotten deed saved his life |work=Mishpacha |date=2 February 2011|page=45[/tpl] The Kapishnitzer Rebbe, Rabbi Yitzchak Meir Palintenstein, brought his Hasidut here in 1975.[tpl]cite book |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=zhdp9btWGWQC&pg=PA591&dq=ezras+torah+jerusalem&hl=en#v=onepage&q=ezras%20torah%20jerusalem&f=false |title=Where Heaven Touches Earth: Jewish life in Jerusalem from Medieval times to the present  |page=591 |last=Rossoff |first=Dovid |publisher=Guardian Press |year=2004 |isbn=0-87306-879-3[/tpl][tpl]cite book |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=_D1RExQx_LcC&pg=PA192&dq=ezrat+torah+jerusalem&hl=en#v=onepage&q=ezrat%20torah%20jerusalem&f=false |title=Israguide 2006 |publisher=Heimishkeit Eru'im Vanofesh Ltd. |page=192 |isbn=1-58330-294-8[/tpl] Rabbi Shmuel Rabinovitch, Rabbi of the Western Wall and the Holy Sites of Israel, also resides in Ezrat Torah.Israguide 2006, p. 194
