[[Ol-molog]]

CATEGORIES: Monduli District, Wards of Tanzania, Populated places in the Arusha Region

Ol-molog is an administrative ward in the Monduli district of the Arusha Region of Tanzania. According to the 2002 census, the ward has a total population of 12,209.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.tanzania.go.tz/census/census/districts/monduli.htm|title=2002 Population and Housing Census General Report|publisher=Government of Tanzania|language=en|accessdate=2008-08-19[/tpl] [tpl]Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot[/tpl]
