[[Milan Metro Line 4]]

CATEGORIES: Milan metro lines

The Line 4 is a new underground rapid transit line under construction[tpl]cite web|title=Metrò 4, aprono i cantieri|url=http://milano.corriere.it/milano/notizie/cronaca/11_maggio_10/metropolitana-4-linea-blu-aprono-cantieri-190606819302.shtml|publisher=Corriere della Sera Milano|accessdate=26 September 2011[/tpl]  in Milan, Italy, part of the Milan Metro system. The line was planned to be completed in 2015, as part of the works for the upcoming Milan Expo. However, delays in planning, financing[tpl]cite web|title=Tabacci: a rischio il secondo lotto della M4|url=http://milano.corriere.it/milano/notizie/cronaca/11_luglio_29/tabacci-m4-rischio-finanziamenti-expo-1901201418693.shtml|publisher=Corriere della Sera Milano|accessdate=26 September 2011[/tpl] and construction works will probably force to postpone the complete opening, and only the first part of the line is now expected to open by 2015.[tpl]cite web|title=Metrò 4 "dimezzato". Per l'Expo  arriverà solo a Dateo o a San Babila|url=http://milano.corriere.it/milano/notizie/cronaca/11_luglio_6/expo-metro-dimezzata-1901028755296.shtml|publisher=Corriere della Sera Milano|accessdate=26 September 2011[/tpl] 
The line, when complete, will be [tpl]convert|15|km|abbr=on[/tpl] long with 21 stations. The expected annual ridership is 87 million. The total estimated cost is about €1.7 billion. 786 million will be granted by the government, 512 million will come by private investors and 400 from the city of Milan.[tpl]cite web|title=M4, lavori assegnati a Impregilo e Atm|url=http://milano.corriere.it/milano/notizie/cronaca/11_maggio_27/metropolitana-quattro-190741492144.shtml|publisher=Corriere della Sera Milano|accessdate=26 September 2011[/tpl] The line will feature completely automatic driverless trains[tpl]cite web|title=Metropolitana M4 approvata, realizzazione Linate – Lorenteggio al via|url=http://www.cronacamilano.it/cronaca/3268-metropolitana-m4-approvata-realizzazione-linate-lorenteggio-al-via.html|publisher=Cronaca Milano|accessdate=26 September 2011[/tpl]  and is designed for a capacity of 24-28,000 passengers per hour in each direction.[tpl]cite web|title=Milano M4|url=http://87.248.52.211/pub/page/it/MM/metropolitane_milano?contentId=924|publisher=Metropolitana Milanese|accessdate=28 December 2011[/tpl] 
The consortium ATM - Impregilo will be responsible for the construction works.[tpl]cite news|title=Tar: "Metrò 4, gare regolari". Via ai cantieri|url=http://milano.corriere.it/milano/notizie/cronaca/12_gennaio_21/metro-quattro-gare-regolari-cantieri-al-via-santucci-stella-1902954594007.shtml|accessdate=22 January 2012|newspaper=Corriere della Sera|date=21 January 2012[/tpl] 
Initially, blue was chosen as the color of the line. However, since then, the suburban railway has been identified by color blue on the maps and, in order not to create misunderstandings, the color of Line 4 will be probably changed. The most likely color will be orange.[tpl]cite news|title=Milano M4: la tratta Linate-Forlanini FS si attiverà in tempo per l'Expo 2015|url=http://www.cityrailways.it/home/2012/4/24/milano-m4-la-tratta-linate-forlanini-fs-si-attivera-in-tempo.html|accessdate=24 April 2012|newspaper=Cityrailways|date=24 April 2012[/tpl]

==Route==

The line is planned to connect the south-western neighborhood of Lorenteggio with the Linate Airport on the east, passing through the city center. Works were planned to begin at Lorenteggio; however, since only one part could be finished in time for the Expo, the one connecting Linate Airport to the city was chosen to be the most important and therefore construction works will proceed in the opposite direction.
