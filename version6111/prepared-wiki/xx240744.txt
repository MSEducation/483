==Climate==

Jianping has a monsoon-influenced humid continental climate (Köppen Dwa) that barely avoids semi-arid designation (Köppen BSk), with hot and humid summers and rather long, cold, and very dry winters. More than two-thirds of the annual rainfall occurs from June thru August. Monthly 24-hour average temperatures range from [tpl]convert|−10.0|°C|1[/tpl] in January to [tpl]convert|24.1|°C|1[/tpl] in July, for an annual average of [tpl]convert|8.37|°C|1[/tpl]. The frost-free period lasts 120 to 155 days.
