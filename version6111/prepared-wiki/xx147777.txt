[[Warped Tour 2006 Tour Compilation]]

CATEGORIES: Warped Tour compilation albums, 2006 compilation albums

The Warped Tour 2006 Tour Compilation is the eleventh installment in the annual compilation series accompanying the Warped Tour. The compilation's cover is a photograph of MxPx bassist and singer Mike Herrera playing on the 2005 Warped Tour.
