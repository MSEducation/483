[[Cymophora]]

CATEGORIES: Asteraceae genera

Cymophora is a genus of flowering plants in the aster family, Asteraceae. Most are native to Mexico.Turner, B. L. (1992). A new species of Cymophora (Asteraceae, Heliantheae) from Guerrero, Mexico. Phytologia 73(3) 242-46.
Some authors have included these plants in the genus Tridax because they are similar species with the same chromosome count. Others find that they have sufficient morphological differences to be kept separate.
