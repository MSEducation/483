[[Avtar Gill (darts player)]]

CATEGORIES: Canadian darts players, Living people, Year of birth missing (living people), British Darts Organisation players

Avtar Gill is a Canadian darts player.
Gill was runner up in the 1986 Canada National Championships losing to Bob Sinnaeve in the final.  Gill played in the 1987 World Professional Darts Championship, losing in the first round 3-1 in sets to Belgium's Frans Devooght.
