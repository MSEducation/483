[[Qareh Said, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Qareh Said ([tpl]lang-fa|قره سعيد[/tpl], also Romanized as Qareh Saʿīd; also known as Qarah Seyyed and Qareh Seyyed)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3766546|Qareh Said[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Bala Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 954, in 191 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
