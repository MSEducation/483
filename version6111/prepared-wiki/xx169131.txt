[[Robert McCollum]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, American male voice actors, Funimation voice actors

Robert McCollum is an American voice actor who works for anime series at Funimation. He has provided voices for a number of English-language versions of Japanese anime films. McCollum is also the host of WFAA-TV's Good Morning Texas.http://ndkdenver.org/guests/robert-mccollum
