[[Notre Dame OpenCourseWare]]

CATEGORIES: Creative Commons-licensed works, University of Notre Dame, OpenCourseWare

Notre Dame OpenCourseWare is an initiative by the University of Notre Dame to make materials from various courses freely available on the web.  Launched September 30, 2006,About Notre Dame OCW  the site currently hosts materials from 45 courses in 7 subjects.  The project was initially funded by a grant from the William and Flora Hewlett Foundation, and, unless indicated otherwise, all materials are released with a Creative Commons license.
Notre Dame OCW was recognized along with other universities as part of Readers Digest's America's 100 Best Award in 2007.Notre Dame Release on Readers Digest Award
