[[Mount Shattuck]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of Ellsworth Land

Mount Shattuck ([tpl]Coord|80|26|S|81|28|W||display=inline,title[/tpl]) is a peak, 1,430 m, located at the south end of Independence Hills, about 3 miles (4.8Â km) northwest of Redpath Peaks, in the Heritage Range. It was named by the Advisory Committee on Antarctic Names (US-ACAN) for aviation machinist Wayne M. Shattuck of the U.S. Navy, who was an air crewman on LC-47 aircraft, who perished in a crash on the Ross Ice Shelf on February 2, 1966.
