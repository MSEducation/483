[[Elisabetta Tona]]

CATEGORIES: 1984 births, Living people, Italian women's footballers, Italy women's international footballers, Italian expatriate footballers, Expatriate soccer players in the United States, People from Lecco, Serie A (women's football) players, Torres Calcio Femminile players

Elisabetta Tona (born 22 January 1984) is an Italian football defender who plays for Torres CF, where she has won four Italian leagues, four nationals cups and two Italy Women's Cups in eleven seasons. She has also won the 2007 WPSL, playing for FC Indiana.http://www.wpsl.info/news/indexa.php?cat=1&id=154 WPSL As a member of the Italian national team she played at the 2005 and 2009 UEFA Women's Championships.http://www.uefa.com/womenseuro/news/newsid=881533.html UEFA
A commanding centre back, Tona's powerful heading ability makes her an important attacking player for her teams.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.uefa.com/womenseuro/news/newsid=881533.html|title=Tona eyes upset for Germany|accessdate=8 December 2013|date=4 September 2009|work=uefa.com|publisher=UEFA|first=Roberta|last=Radaelli[/tpl]

==International career==

Tona made her senior debut for the Italy women's national football team in June 2002, in a 6–0 friendly win over Yugoslavia.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.figc.it/other/Brochure2013/ITALIA_EuropeoFemminile2013_Bassa.pdf|publisher=Italian Football Federation|language=Italian|page=12|accessdate=8 December 2013|title=Italia Campionato Europeo Femminile Svezia 10 - 28 Luglio 2013|format=PDF[/tpl] She competed at UEFA Women's Euro 2005 in North West England and conceded a penalty kick in the Italians' 4–0 defeat to perennial champions Germany.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.theguardian.com/football/euro2005/2005/jun/09/holderstighten|title=Holders tighten their grip|accessdate=8 December 2013|date=9 June 2005|publisher=The Guardian|first=Georgina|last=Turner[/tpl]
At UEFA Women's Euro 2009 in Finland, Tona played in all four games as the Italians reached the quarter-finals. She had scored a hat-trick against Hungary during the qualification round.[tpl]cite web|url=http://en.archive.uefa.com/competitions/woco/players/Player=63523/index.html|title=Elisabetta Tona|accessdate=8 December 2013|work=uefa.com|publisher=UEFA[/tpl] National coach Antonio Cabrini named Tona in his selection for UEFA Women's Euro 2013 in Sweden,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.uefa.com/womenseuro/news/newsid=1968336.html|title=Cabrini finalises Italy's Women's EURO squad|date=1 July 2013|accessdate=7 December 2013|work=uefa.com|publisher=UEFA[/tpl] but replaced her with Federica Di Criscio when she was injured on the eve of the tournament.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.figc.it/en/204/37013/2013/07/News.shtml | title=Europeo: domani l’esordio con la Finlandia.
