[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Joaçaba]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1975, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Florianópolis, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Joaçaba ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Ioassabensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Joaçaba in the Ecclesiastical province of Florianópolis in Brazil.
