[[Leigh Vial]]

CATEGORIES: 1909 births, 1943 deaths, History of Papua New Guinea, Papua New Guinea in World War II, Explorers of Papua New Guinea, Australian explorers, Recipients of the Distinguished Service Cross (United States)

Leigh Grant Vial DSC (28 February 1909 - 30 April 1943) was an Australian patrol officer and coastwatcher in Papua New Guinea during World War II. While a patrol officer he became the first white person to climb Mount Wilhelm the highest mountain in Papua New Guinea.
