[[KBXL]]

CATEGORIES: Religious radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Idaho, Radio stations established in 1970, 1970 establishments in Idaho

KBXL (94.1 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Christian talk and teaching format. Licensed to Caldwell, Idaho, USA, the station serves the Boise metropolitan area, and is currently owned by Inspirational Family Radio.
