[[Alternanthera littoralis]]

CATEGORIES: Alternanthera

Alternanthera littoralis is an African species in the botanical family Amaranthaceae. The leaves are eaten as a vegetable.Grubben, G.J.H. & Denton, O.A. (2004) Plant Resources of Tropical Africa 2. Vegetables. PROTA Foundation, Wageningen; Backhuys, Leiden; CTA, Wageningen.
