[[Franklin American Mortgage Company]]

CATEGORIES: Companies established in 1993, Companies based in Nashville, Tennessee, Mortgage lenders

Franklin American Mortgage Company (FAMC) is a privately held mortgage banking firm headquartered in Nashville suburb Franklin, Tennessee.
FAMC was founded in 1993.FAMC site (accessed 2011-01-01).  Franklin American Mortgage Company was listed as an Inc. 500 honoree from 1999-2004.[tpl]cite web|title=Franklin American Mortgage - Franklin, TN. FAMC sponsored professional golf's Franklin American Mortgage Championship and as of 2010 sponsors United States college football's Franklin American Mortgage Music City Bowl.
