[[Estuary stargazer]]

CATEGORIES: Leptoscopidae, Endemic marine fish of New Zealand

Leptoscopus macropygus, the estuary stargazer, is a species of southern sandfish endemic to the Pacific waters around New Zealand.[tpl]FishBase species |genus= Leptoscopus|species= macropygus| month = December | year = 2013[/tpl]  It occurs at depths between a few and 60 m.  This species can reach a length of [tpl]convert|17|cm|in[/tpl] FL.  It is currently the only known member of its genus.
