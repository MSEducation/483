[[Kerry Ketter]]

Kerry Kenneth Ketter (born September 20, 1947 in Prince George, British Columbia) is a retired ice hockey player. He played in the National Hockey League with the Atlanta Flames and the World Hockey Association with the Edmonton Oilers.
