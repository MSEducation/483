[[Caenurgia escondida]]

CATEGORIES: Ectypina

Caenurgia escondida Poole, R. W. (1989). Lepidopterorum Catalogus (New Series) Fascicle 118, Noctuidae. CRC Press. ISBN 0-916846-45-8, ISBN 978-0-916846-45-9. is a species of moth of the Noctuidae family. It is found in South America, including the Brazil.
