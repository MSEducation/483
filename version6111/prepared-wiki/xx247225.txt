[[Saumik Dey]]

CATEGORIES: Indian footballers, 1980 births, Living people, East Bengal F.C. players, I-League players

Saumik Dey  (born 20 August 1984) is an Indian footballer who is captain and plays as a defender for Kingfisher East Bengal Club. He had captained the red and gold brigade and led them to win the 32nd Federation Cup and 2010 Calcutta Football League.
