[[Dibrugarh Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in  Assam, Dibrugarh

Dibrugarh Airport ([tpl]lang-as|ডিব্ৰুগড় বিমানবন্দৰ[/tpl]) ([tpl]lang-hi|डिब्रूगढ़ एअरपोर्ट[/tpl]) [tpl]Airport codes|DIB|VEMN[/tpl], known as Mohanbari Airport, is located at Mohanbari, distance about 15 KM North East from Dibrugarh town, Assam, India.

==Airport specifications==

==Terminals==

Dibrugarh Airport, New Terminal building opened in February, 2009,it occupies an area of [tpl]convert|10536|m2|sqft|abbr=on[/tpl],a state-of-the-art and integrated terminal. New Terminal building has 500 passenger seating capacity (250 Arrival/250 Departure),10 Check-in Counter,2 security check-in, two aerobridges and 5 remote parking bays.

==Runways==

Dibrugarh airport has runway 05/23, with length [tpl]convert|1829|m|ft|abbr=on[/tpl]. DVOR,DME,NDB navigation aids available in Dibrugarh Airport. Landing Aids and Visual Instruments available in Dibrugarh airport on Runway 05 are ILS CAT I, PAPI-3°,VASI-NIL
,On Runway 23 ILS -NIL, PAPI-NIL, VASI-NIL
