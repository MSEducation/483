[[ELM Denmark]]

CATEGORIES: Sustainability by country, Environment of Denmark

ELM Denmark was an environmental and sustainable development committee formed in August 1991 by a group of Danish corporations.[tpl]sfn|Callenbach|1993|p=53[/tpl] The formation of the committee was led by Brødrene Hartmann A/S and involved the Danish National Railways, InvestMiljø, Danapak, and other key Danish companies.[tpl]sfn|Callenbach|1993|p=53[/tpl]
