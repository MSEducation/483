[[List of diplomatic missions of Guinea-Bissau]]

CATEGORIES: Diplomatic missions of Guinea-Bissau, Lists of diplomatic missions by sending country, Guinea-Bissau-related lists

This is a list of diplomatic missions of Guinea-Bissau, excluding honorary consulates. The Republic of Guinea-Bissau follows a nonaligned foreign policy and seeks friendly and cooperative relations with a wide variety of states and organizations. France, Portugal, Angola, Brazil, China, Egypt, Nigeria, Libya, Cuba, the Palestine Liberation Organization, and Russia have diplomatic offices in Bissau.
