Vučina Šćepanović (Serbian Cyrillic: Bучинa Шћeпaнoвић; born November 17, 1982 in Priština) is a Serbian footballer currently playing for FK Sarajevo in the Premier League of Bosnia and Herzegovina.
He had previously played with Serbian clubs FK Priština, FK Bečej, FK Sartid and FK Hajduk Beograd, Montenegrin FK Mogren, Bosnian FK Slavija, Ukrainian FC Kryvbas Kryvyi Rih and Macedonian FK Makedonija Gjorče Petrov.
