[[Wolonkoto Department]]

CATEGORIES: Departments of Burkina Faso, Léraba Province

 Wolonkoto   is a small department or commune of Léraba Province in south-western  Burkina Faso. Its capital lies at the town of Wolonkoto. According to the 1996 census the department has a total population of 3,687.Burkinabé government inforoute communale
