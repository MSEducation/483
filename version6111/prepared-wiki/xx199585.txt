[[English Amateur Championship]]

CATEGORIES: Snooker competitions in the United Kingdom, Snooker amateur competitions, Recurring sporting events established in 1916, Snooker in England

The English Amateur Championship is a snooker competition that has been played since 1916.
The first two tournaments were won by Charles Nathaniel Jaques, who was subsequently shot through the wrist in World War I and unable to defend his title again.[tpl]cite web |url= http://pqasb.pqarchiver.com/csmonitor_historic/access/298784772.html?dids=298784772:298784772&FMT=ABS&FMTS=ABS:AI&date=Mar+26%2C+1920&author=Special+to+The+Christian+Science+Monitor&pub=Christian+Science+Monitor&desc=A.R.+WISDOM+WINS+THE+SNOOKER+TITLE&pqatl=google|title= A.R.
