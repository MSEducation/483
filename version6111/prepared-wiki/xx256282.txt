[[Manasantha Nuvve]]

CATEGORIES: 2001 films, Telugu-language films, Indian films, Directorial debut films, Telugu films remade in other languages

Manasantha Nuvve is a 2001 blockbuster Telugu film written and directed by V.N. Aditya. This film stars Uday Kiran and Reema Sen in the lead roles. The film is produced by M. S. Raju and has music composed by R. P. Patnaik.
