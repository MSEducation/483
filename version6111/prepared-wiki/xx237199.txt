[[D. L. Geary Brewing Company]]

CATEGORIES: Beer brewing companies based in Maine, Companies based in Maine

D. L. Geary Brewing Company is a brewery in Portland, Maine, USA, incorporated in October 1983 by David and Karen Geary.
Geary's, as it is more commonly known, was one of the first new microbreweries during America’s late 20th century brewing renaissance.[tpl]Citation needed|date=October 2009[/tpl] In 2006 Geary’s London Porter won a New York Times blind taste test.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2006/10/18/dining/18wine.html?pagewanted=1&_r=2&ei=5070&en=e7a480f42a808634&ex=1165899600|title=A Journey to the Dark Side|last=Asimov|first=Eric|date=October 18, 2006|publisher=New York Times|accessdate=2009-09-23[/tpl]
