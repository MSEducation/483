[[Hiushinai Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Hokkaido Prefecture

[tpl]Nihongo|Hiushinai Station|緋牛内駅|Hiushinai-eki[/tpl] is a railway station located in Hiushinai in the Tanno-chō district of Kitami-shi city in Hokkaidō, Japan and services the Sekihoku Main Line operated by JR Hokkaido.

==Station structure==

Hiushinai is an unmanned station with two side platforms alongside two railway tracks.
