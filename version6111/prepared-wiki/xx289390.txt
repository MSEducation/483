[[Rostamabad, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Rostamabad ([tpl]lang-fa|رستم اباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Rostamābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3773372|Rostamabad[/tpl] is a village in Shivanat Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
