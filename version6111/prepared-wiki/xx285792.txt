[[Beat Forster]]

CATEGORIES: 1983 births, Swiss ice hockey players, Ice hockey players at the 2006 Winter Olympics, Living people, Phoenix Coyotes draft picks, Olympic ice hockey players of Switzerland, HC Davos players, ZSC Lions players

Beat Forster (born February 2, 1983) is a Swiss professional ice hockey player currently playing for HC Davos. He plays defence. He played for the Swiss national ice hockey team at the 2006 Winter Olympics.
