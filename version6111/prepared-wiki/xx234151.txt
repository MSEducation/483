[[Takyō Station]]

CATEGORIES: Izuhakone Sunzu Line, Railway stations in Shizuoka Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Takyō Station|田京駅|Takyō-eki[/tpl] is a train station of the Izuhakone Railway’s Sunzu Line located in the city of Izunokuni, Shizuoka Prefecture, Japan. It is 14.2 rail kilometers from the line’s terminus at Mishima Station.

==History==

Takyō Station was opened on July 17, 1899 as part of the extension of the Sunzu line from Nanjō Station (present-day Izu-Nagaoka to Ōhito.Japanese Wikipedia entry

==Lines==

==Station layout==

Takyō Station has an island platform and a side platforms connected to the station building by a level crossing. The station building has both a manned service counter and automatic ticket machines.
