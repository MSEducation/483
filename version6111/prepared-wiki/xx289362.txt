[[List of Butuan city officials]]

CATEGORIES: Philippines politics-related lists, Butuan

Butuan City Council, commonly known as, Sangguniang Panlungsod consists of Thirteen (13) Members - One (1) Vice Mayor, Ten (10) Elected Councilors, One (1) ABC-Liga Federation President, and One (1) Sangguniang Kabataan President. http://www.butuan.gov.ph/home/city-government/city-officials.html
