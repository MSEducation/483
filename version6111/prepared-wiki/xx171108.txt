[[Rico Freiermuth]]

CATEGORIES: 1958 births, Bobsledders at the 1984 Winter Olympics, Living people, Swiss bobsledders, Olympic bobsledders of Switzerland, Olympic bronze medalists for Switzerland, Olympic medalists in bobsleigh, People from Liestal, Medalists at the 1984 Winter Olympics

Rico Freiermuth (born January 1, 1958 in Liestal, Basel-Land) is a Swiss bobsledder who competed during the early to mid-1980s. He won a bronze medal in the four-man event at the 1984 Winter Olympics in Sarajevo.
Freiermuth also won two medals in the four-man event at the FIBT World Championships with a gold in 1982 and a bronze in 1985.
