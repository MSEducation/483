[[Elite League Riders' Championship]]

CATEGORIES: Speedway competitions in the United Kingdom

The Elite League Riders Championship was a motorcycle speedway contest between the top riders (or two riders) with the highest average points total from each club competing in the Elite League in the UK.[tpl]cite web | title = SCB Speedway Regulations | publisher = Speedway Control Board | year = 2008 | url = http://www.acu.org.uk/uploaded/documents/The%20SPEEDWAY%20REGULATIONS%202008.pdf | format = pdf | accessdate = 2008-04-02 [/tpl] The winner of the 2009 championship was Australian rider Leigh Adams of the Swindon Robins.
The competition ran until 2013, after which it was replaced with the Elite Riders Championship, which is not limited to riders of Elite League teams.
