[[Jens Pieper]]

Jens Pieper (born February 9, 1968 in Braunschweig) is an athlete from Germany, who competes in archery.

==2008 Summer Olympics==

At the 2008 Summer Olympics in Beijing Pieper finished his ranking round with a total of 648 points, which gave him the 45th seed for the final competition bracket in which he faced Oleksandr Serdyuk in the first round.
