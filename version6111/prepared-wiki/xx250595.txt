[[Saqi Books]]

CATEGORIES: Book publishing companies of the United Kingdom, Publishing companies established in 1984

Saqi Books is an independenthttp://www.literaturetraining.com/metadot/index.pl?id=37982&isa=DBRow&op=show&dbview_id=2300 UK publisher co-founded in 1984 by author and feminist Mai Ghoussoubhttp://thylemma.blogspot.com/2008/07/al-saqi-books-25th-anniversary.html to "print quality academic and general interest books on the Middle East". It now claims to be "the UK's largest publisher of Middle Eastern and Arabic titles". Its titles include cookery and travel books.http://www.visitlondon.com/attractions/detail/264581
On its 21st Anniversary in 2004, The Independent praised it for 'issuing titles that stimulate and challenge - books with "editorial audacity"'http://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/books/features/cover-stories-saqi-books-jonathan-aitkens-book-deal-alistair-cookes-letters-559345.html
It has bookshops in London and Beirut and in 2008 held its 25th anniversary celebration at Kensington Town Hall featuring musician Brian Eno, Turkish author Moris Farhi and British author Maggie Gee.
