[[Koyu District, Miyazaki]]

CATEGORIES: Districts in Miyazaki Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Koyu|児湯郡|Koyu-gun[/tpl] is a district located in Miyazaki Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the district has an estimated population of 78,392 and the density of 109.52 persons per km². The total area is 715.80 km².
