[[Queen of the Valley Medical Center (Napa, California)]]

CATEGORIES: Hospital buildings completed in 1958, Hospitals in the San Francisco Bay Area, Buildings and structures in Napa County, California, Hospitals established in 1958, Napa, California

Queen of the Valley Medical Center is a medical center located in Napa, California operated by St. Joseph Health System. The hospital first opened its doors in 1958 when it replaced Parks Victory Memorial Hospital.  It is a Level III adult trauma center.
