[[The West, Christians and Jews in Saudi Arabian Schoolbooks]]

CATEGORIES: 2003 books, Cultural studies books

The West, Christians and Jews in Saudi Arabian Schoolbooks is a January 2003 publication by the Institute for Monitoring Peace and Cultural Tolerance in School Education (IMPACT-SE), which was known as CMIP at the time of publication.The West, Christians and Jews in Saudi Arabian Schoolbooks by IMPACT-SE The publication analyzes how Saudi Arabian school textbooks portray the West, Christians, and Jews.
