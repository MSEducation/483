[[Golabar Rural District]]

CATEGORIES: Rural Districts of Zanjan Province, Ijrud County

Golabar Rural District ([tpl]lang-fa|دهستان گلابر[/tpl]) is a rural district (dehestan) in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 13,644, in 3,433 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl] The rural district has 23 villages.
