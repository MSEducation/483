[[Fred Makimare]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 births, Australian people of Cook Island descent, Cook Islands national rugby league team players, Melbourne Storm under 20's players, Living people

Fred Makimare (born 17 June 1989) is a  rugby league player for the Melbourne Storm in the National Rugby League. His position is at prop or in the back row. He played in Melbourne Storms Under 20s grand final win over the Wests Tigers.http://www.rleague.com/db/content/92000/92188.php

==Representative career==

He made his international debut for the Cook Islands 22-20 defeat of Samoa, scoring 2 tries.http://www.nrl.com/newsviews/latestnews/newsarticle/tabid/10874/newsid/57129/cook-islands-progress-to-pacific-cup/default.aspx
