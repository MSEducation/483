[[Kazbar]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Kazbar ([tpl]lang-fa|كزبر[/tpl]; also known as Karzbar, Karzabar, Karzebar, Karzebū, Kazabar, and Kazzebar)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3069935|Kazbar[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 662, in 172 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
