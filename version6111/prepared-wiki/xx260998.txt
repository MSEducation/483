[[Bye Bye Bangkok]]

CATEGORIES: Indian films, Bengali-language films, 2011 films

Bye Bye BangkokBye Bye Bangkok Shooting StillsTelegraphIndia retrieved 30th Sept 2010 is a 2011 Bengali comedy film, directed by Aniket Chattopadhaya. It featured Swastika Mukherjee, Rajesh Sharma, Shilajit Majumdar, Rudranil Ghosh, Kharaj Mukherjee, Anjana Basu, Locket Chatterjee, Sonali Choudhury, Kanchan Mullick, Kanchana Maitra, Neel Mukherjee, Rajatava Dutta, Biswajit Chakrabarty, Phalguni Chatterjee, Kunal Padhi, Sudipa Basu and others.
The film has been shot in Bangkok and Kolkata.
