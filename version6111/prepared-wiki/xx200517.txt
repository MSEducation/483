[[Fufeng County]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Shaanxi, Baoji

Fufeng County ([tpl]zh|c=扶风县|p=Fúfēng Xiàn[/tpl]) is a county in Baoji, Shaanxi Province of China. The county lies on the north bank of the Wei River between Xi'an, 110 kilometers to the east, and Baoji, 95 kilometers to the west.
It has a land area of 751 square kilometers, and a population of 460,000.
The township of Famen contains the Famen Temple and Zhouyuan Museum (in Zhaochen Village).
