[[Archivolt]]

CATEGORIES: Arches and vaults, Architectural elements

An archivolt (or voussure) is an ornamental molding or band following the curve on the underside of an arch.[ref]
[tpl]cite book| last =Ching| first =Francis D.K.| title =A Visual Dictionary of Architecture| publisher =John Wiley & Sons, Inc.| year =1995| location =New York| page =12| isbn = 0-471-28451-3 [/tpl][/ref]  It is  composed of bands of ornamental moldings (or other architectural elements) surrounding an arched opening, corresponding to the architrave in the case of a rectangular opening. The word is sometimes used to refer to the under-side or inner curve of the arch itself (more properly, the intrados).
The word originates in the Italian (or French) equivalents of the English words arch and vault.
