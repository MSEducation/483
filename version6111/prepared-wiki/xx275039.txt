Luan de Burgh is the founder and head of the de Burgh Group,deBurghGroup Website: http://www.deburghgroup.com an organisation specialising in providing high end corporate training and bespoke conferences and seminars for leading professionals. A professional public speaker and presentation coach, Luan coaches politicians, lawyers and businesspeople in how to deliver dynamic messages with confidence, competence and credibility.Luan de Burgh on Twitter: http://twitter.com/#!/deburghgroup

==Career==

Luan has worked as a coach and lecturer for over ten years and previously as a voice-over artist, recording commercials, talking books,YouTube - Received Pronunciation: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Qjnt9rNvNR0 audio descriptions, documentaries, language aids and film dubs for the companies including the BBC, LBC, CBC and Visiontext.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.centrecoaching.de/page8/page8.html |title=Luan De Burgh |publisher=Centrecoaching.de |date= |accessdate=2011-10-17[/tpl] He is regular contributor in the media.
Since the de Burgh Group was founded, the number of clients including front-bench politicians, directors and CEOs of leading multinational organisations and business graduates in some of Europe's top international firms has continued to grow. These include the DLA Piper, Rabobank, Oriel Securities, Lloyds Banking Group, BBC, UBS, Citigroup, Allen & Overy, Herbert Smith, BUPA, Shell UK, GSK, National Health Service, Aviva and the University of Surrey.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.deburghgroup.com/about/ |title=About |publisher=de Burgh Group |date= |accessdate=2011-10-17[/tpl] He has also lectured at London's Central School of Speech and Drama,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cw.com/assets/content/pdfs/resource/MVC-press-release-singapore.pdf |title=Press Release |publisher= |date= |accessdate=2011-10-17[/tpl] The Royal Academy of Dramatic Art and Berlin University.
