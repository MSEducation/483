[[Patriotic Union (Panama)]]

CATEGORIES: Liberal parties in Panama

The Patriotic Union (Unión Patriótica) was a political party in Panama. It was formed when the National Liberal Party (Partido Nacional Liberal) and the Solidarity Party (Partido Solidaridad) merged.
The party's president was Guillermo Ford. After the party did not reach the minimum votes, it was merged with the Democratic Change Party.[tpl]fact|date=August 2012[/tpl]
