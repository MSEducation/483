[[76th Division (People's Republic of China)]]

CATEGORIES: Infantry divisions of the People's Volunteer Army, Infantry divisions of the People's Liberation Army

The 76th Division was a military formation of the People's Volunteer Army (aka Chinese People's Volunteers (CPV) or Chinese Communist Forces (CCF)) during the Korean War. They were a component of the 26th Army, consisting of the 226th, 227th, and the 228th Regiments.[tpl]cite book|url = http://www.history.army.mil/books/korea/ebb/ch3.htm| last = Mossman| first = Billy C.| title = Ebb and Flow November 1950-July 1951| page = 55| publisher = United States Army Center of Military History| year = 1990[/tpl]
