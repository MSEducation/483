[[Qinghe District, Tieling]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Liaoning, Tieling

Qinghe District ([tpl]zh|s=清河区 |t=清河區 |p=Qīnghé Qū[/tpl]) is a district of Tieling, Liaoning province, People's Republic of China. However, it is located closer to Kaiyuan City than it is to downtown Tieling, which lies [tpl]convert|39|km[/tpl] to the southwest.
