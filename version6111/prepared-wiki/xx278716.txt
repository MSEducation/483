[[Qarluq, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Qarluq ([tpl]lang-fa|قارلوق[/tpl], also Romanized as Qārlūq and Qarlūq; also known as Karluk, Qārloq, and Qārlūk)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3079816|Qarluq[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 152, in 27 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
