[[5751]]

CATEGORIES: Vacuum tubes, Guitar amplification tubes

5751 is the designation for a low-voltage, low-noise avionics vacuum tube popular as a pre-amplification tube in guitar amplifiers. The 5751 is a modified version of the 12AX7 and is used to "soften up a harsh modern amp".[tpl]cite book|last=Pittman|first=Aspen|title=The Tube Amp Book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=JT1I7Ld76YsC&pg=PA132|year=2003|publisher=Hal Leonard|isbn=978-0-87930-767-7|page=132[/tpl]
