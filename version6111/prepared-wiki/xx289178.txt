[[Aghcheh Gonbad]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Bijar County

Aghcheh Gonbad ([tpl]lang-fa|آغچه گنبد[/tpl], also Romanized as Āghcheh Gonbad; also known as Āghjeh Gonbad and Āghjeh Kand)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795251|Aghcheh Gonbad[/tpl] is a village in Gorgin Rural District, Korani District, Bijar County, Kurdistan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 157, in 31 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|12[/tpl]
