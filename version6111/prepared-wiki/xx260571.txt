[[LaBrassBanda]]

CATEGORIES: German musical groups, Bavarian-language singers

LaBrassBanda is a Bavarian Brass Band originating from Übersee (Chiemgau), near Chiemsee, Bavaria, Germany. The name of the band is a portmanteau of the Italian word "la banda" and the English term "brass band", both meaning the same.BR-online about LaBrassBanda (in German)

==History of the band==

The band started in 2007. It became well known thanks to their tour from home town to the final match of the UEFA 2008 in Vienna.
