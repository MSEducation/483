[[KCGS]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Arkansas, Southern Gospel radio stations in the United States, 3ABN radio stations, Radio stations established in 1988

KCGS (960 AM) is a radio station serving the Marshall, Arkansas, area with a southern gospel format. The station was under the ownership of Southland Broadcasting Corporation from 2002-2008. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KCGS |title=KCGS Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl] In late 2008, the station was sold to the Word and Faith Christian Church Inc. for $300,000. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.allaccess.com/net-news/archive/story/50439/station-sales-arkansas-am-2-translators?ref=search |title=Station Sales: Arkansas AM [/tpl] Effective December 21, 2012, the station was sold to Steven & Alice Kiefer, through licensee Kiefer Retirement Services, Inc., for $150,000.
