[[21564 Widmanstätten]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Astronomical objects discovered in 1998

21564 Widmanstätten is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1986.8059528 days (5.44 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=21564 | title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-05-23 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on August 26, 1998.
