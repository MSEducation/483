Hunter J. Gorskie (born June 27, 1991) is an American soccer player who currently plays for New York Cosmos in the North American Soccer League.

==Career==

===Youth, College and Amateur===

Gorskie played club soccer for the Player Development Academy of New Jersey before committing to Stanford University where he played for four years.  In 2009, Gorskie started all 20 games for the Cardinal and helped them to nine clean sheets, including three in the Pac-10 tournament and two in the NCAA Tournament.  In 2010, Gorskie made 16 appearances, including four starts, and helped the Cardinal defense to a 1.14 goals against average. ===Professional===

After going undrafted in the 2013 MLS SuperDraft, Gorskie went on trial with San Jose Earthquakes and made two reserve league appearances for them before signing his first professional contract with North American Soccer League club New York Cosmos on April 26, 2013.[tpl]cite web|title=Cosmos Sign Three More Players|url=http://nycosmos.com/news/cosmos-sign-three-more-players|publisher=New York Cosmos|accessdate=11 August 2013|date=26 April 2013[/tpl]  He made his professional debut on August 3 in a 2-1 victory over Fort Lauderdale Strikers in the opening match of the NASL fall season.[tpl]cite web|title=Match Report: New York Cosmos 2-1 Fort Lauderdale Strikers|url=http://nycosmos.com/news/ft-new-york-cosmos-2-1-fort-lauderdale-strikers|publisher=New York Cosmos|accessdate=11 August 2013|date=3 August 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Gorskie Makes Pro Debut With Cosmos|url=http://www.gostanford.com/ViewArticle.dbml?DB_OEM_ID=30600&ATCLID=208992560|publisher=Stanford Athletics|accessdate=11 August 2013|date=8 August 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web|last=Harris|first=David|title=New York Cosmos reboot the Ft.
