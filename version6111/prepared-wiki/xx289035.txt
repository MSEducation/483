[[Aslanlu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Aslanlu ([tpl]lang-fa|اصلانلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Aşlānlū; also known as Isīlānlu)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3053984|Aslanlu[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 138, in 38 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
