[[Dragonborn]]

CATEGORIES: Dungeons & Dragons dragons, Dungeons & Dragons humanoids, Fictional dragon species and races, Forgotten Realms creatures

The dragonborn are a fictional race in the Dungeons & Dragons fantasy tabletop role-playing game.

==Publication history==

The dragonborn was originally introduced in the Dungeons & Dragons 3.5 supplement book Races of the Dragon, published by Wizards of the Coast in 2006.Races of the Dragon Excerpt In that book, dragonborn are presented as a transitive race, members of other humanoid races who, to show devotion to Bahamut, willingly took on draconic traits in place of their original biology.[tpl]Cite book| last = Kestrel | first = Gwendolyn F.M. | coauthors = Wilkes, Jennifer Clarke; Liquette, Kolja Raven | title = Races of the Dragon | publisher = Wizards of the Coast | year=2006[/tpl]
The dragonborn race is a true draconic race in Dungeons & Dragons 4th Edition.  They are descendants of the greater Dragon God, Io.[tpl]Cite book| author=Heinsoo, Rob; Collin, Andy; Wyatt, James | title=Player's Handbook | publisher=Wizards of the Coast | year=2008[/tpl]
The dragonborn appears in the fourth edition Monster Manual (2008), including the dragonborn soldier, the dragonborn gladiator, the dragonborn raider, and the dragonborn champion.[tpl]Cite book| author=Mearls, Mike, Stephen Schubert, and James Wyatt | title=Monster Manual | publisher=Wizards of the Coast | year=2008[/tpl]
The dragonborn is detailed in Dragon #365 in the "Ecology of the Dragonborn",[tpl]Cite journal| author=Sim, Chris | url=http://www.wizards.com/default.asp?x=dnd/drfe/20080709 | title=Ecology of the Dragonborn | journal=Dragon Magazine | volume=365 | publisher=Wizards of the Coast |date=July 2008[/tpl] and in the book Player's Handbook Races: Dragonborn.[tpl]Cite book| author=Wyatt, James | title=Player's Handbook Races: Dragonborn | publisher=Wizards of the Coast | year=2010[/tpl]

===Forgotten Realms===

===Eberron===

In the Eberron campaign setting, the Dragonborn are descendants of the dragons of the lost continent of Argonessen, where they have lived in isolation since before Khorvaire's founding.[tpl]citation needed|date=January 2011[/tpl]

===Dark Sun===

In the Dark Sun campaign setting, the Dragonborn are created from humans by the sorcerer-king Dregoth, and refer to themselves as Dray.''Dark Sun Campaign Setting' (4th edition)
