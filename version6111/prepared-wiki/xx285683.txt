[[Yousef Al-Nehmi]]

CATEGORIES: 1993 births, Living people, Yemeni swimmers

Yousef Al-Nehmi, (born 1993) is a Yemeni swimmer.http://www.bcn2013.com/en/swimming/detail/member/yousef-al-nehmi He competed in the 50 m freestyle and 50 m butterfly events at the 2010 Asian Games and in the 50 m butterfly event at the 2013 Asian Indoor and Martial Arts Games. Al-Nehmi also took part in the 50 m freestyle and 50 m butterfly events at the 2013 World Aquatics Championships.
