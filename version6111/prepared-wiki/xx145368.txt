==Transport==

As the centre connects with major pedestrian malls there is no direct vehicular transport to the location. However, the area is well serviced by bus links along St Georges Terrace, as well as services from the Wellington Street Bus Station and the Esplanade Busport. Rail services are available from the nearby Perth railway station.
