[[Faris]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Laconia

Faris ([tpl]lang-el|Φάρις[/tpl]) is a former municipality in Laconia, Peloponnese, Greece. Since the 2011 local government reform it is part of the municipality Sparti, of which it is a municipal unit.Kallikratis law Greece Ministry of Interior [tpl]el icon[/tpl] Population 5,269 (2001). The seat of the municipality was in Xirokampi.
