[[Barry Banks (rugby league)]]

CATEGORIES: England national rugby league team players, English rugby league players, Hull F.C. players, Hunslet Hawks players, Living people, Place of birth missing (living people), Rugby league centres, Year of birth missing (living people), York City Knights players

Barry Banks is an English professional rugby league footballer of the 1970s and '80s, playing at representative level for England, and at club level for York, Hull, and Hunslet, as a [tpl]rlp|CE[/tpl], i.e. number 3 or 4.

==Playing career==

===International honours===
