[[KZAR]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in San Antonio, Texas

KZAR (97.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to McQueeney, Texas, USA. The station serves the San Antonio area with a Christian rock format. The station is currently owned by Educational Media Foundation.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KZAR |title=KZAR Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division[/tpl]

==History==

The station broadcast a Reggaeton format until 2008, then changed to rock. It broadcast the rock format branded as "977 Rock" until 2011.
