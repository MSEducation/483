[[Malek Baghi, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Malek Baghi ([tpl]lang-fa|ملك باغي[/tpl], also Romanized as Malek Bāghī)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795728|Malek Baghi[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 269, in 65 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
