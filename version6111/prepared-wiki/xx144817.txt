[[Selca, Croatia]]

CATEGORIES: Brač, Populated places in Split-Dalmatia County, Municipalities of Croatia

Selca is a municipality on the island of Brač in Croatia in the Split-Dalmatia County. It has a population of 1,804 (2011 census),[tpl]Croatian Census 2011|S|17|3832[/tpl] 97.17% of which are Croats.[tpl]Croatian Census 2011|E|17[/tpl]
