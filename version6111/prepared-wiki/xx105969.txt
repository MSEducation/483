[[Kankan Region]]

CATEGORIES: Regions of Guinea, Kankan Region

The Kankan Region is located in eastern Guinea. It is bordered by the countries of Mali and Côte d'Ivoire and the Guinean regions of Nzérékoré and Faranah.
