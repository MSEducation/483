[[Stephen Brenner]]

CATEGORIES: 1974 births, Living people, Hurling goalkeepers, De La Salle hurlers, Waterford hurlers

 
Stephen Brenner (born 1974 in Waterford, Ireland) is an Irish sportsperson.  He plays hurling as a goalkeeper with his local club De La SalleDelighted Brenner beams: ‘it’s raining trophies now!’ and was a member of the Waterford senior inter-county team from the 1990s until the 2000s.
