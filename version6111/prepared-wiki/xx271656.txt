[[Uzunkaş]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Mersin Province, Mediterranean Region, Turkey, Villages in Turkey, Populated places in Yenişehir, Mersin

 
Uzunkaş (former Apsun) is a village in Yenişehir district, which is an intracity district within Greater Mersin, Turkey. The village which is at [tpl]coord|36|52|N|34|27|E[/tpl] is [tpl]convert|22|km[/tpl] north west of Mersin city center. The village is situated in Toros Mountains. The population of the village is 363 [tpl]cite web|url= http://rapor.tuik.gov.tr/reports/rwservlet?adnksdb2&ENVID=adnksdb2Env&report=wa_idari_yapi_10sonrasi.RDF&p_il1=33&p_yil=2011&p_dil=2&desformat=html |title=Turkstat|publisher=Turkish Statistical Institute|accessdate=2012[/tpl] as of 2011. The main agricultural products are citrus and other fruits.[tpl]cite web|url= http://uzunkas.webnode.com.tr/koy%20hakk%c4%b1nda/ |title=Uzunkaş village|publisher=Village muhtar|accessdate=2011[/tpl] [tpl]tr icon[/tpl]
