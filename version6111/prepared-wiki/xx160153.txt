[[Belgian general election, 1977]]

CATEGORIES: 1977 elections in Europe, Elections in Belgium, 1977 in Belgium

General elections were held in Belgium on 17 April 1977.Nohlen, D & Stöver, P (2010) Elections in Europe: A data handbook, p289 ISBN 978-3-8329-5609-7 The result was a victory for the Christian People's Party, which won 56 of the 212 seats in the Chamber of Representatives and 28 of the 106 seats in the Senate. Voter turnout was 95.1%.Nohlen & Stöver, p291
