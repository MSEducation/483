[[Petr Kadlec]]

Petr Kadlec (born 5 January 1977 in Prague) is a Czech professional ice hockey defenceman who currently plays for HC Slavia Praha of the Czech Extraliga.Slavia Praha HC's 2010-11 Roster
Kadlec previously played for HC Berounští Medvědi and HC Keramika Plzeň.Profile at HC Slavia Praha official website
