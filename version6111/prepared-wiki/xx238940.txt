[[John Louis Clarke]]

CATEGORIES: 1881 births, 1970 deaths, Blackfoot people, Deaf artists

John Louis Clarke (May 10, 1881 - November 20, 1970) was a Blackfoot wood carver from Montana.

==Biography==

His Blackfoot name was Cutapuis.
