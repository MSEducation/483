[[Out to the World]]

CATEGORIES: 1994 films, Korean-language films, South Korean films

Out to the World ([tpl]ko-hhrm|세상밖으로||Saesang bakkeuro[/tpl]) is an award-winning 1994 South Korean black-comedy film.

==Synopsis==

A satirical allegory of contemporary South Korean politics, the film follows the exploits of two convicts who accidentally escape while being transferred to another prison. Before they can turn themselves back into custody, they meet a female outlaw. She persuades the pair of prisoners to rob a bank in Seoul.
