[[Thompson Mann]]

CATEGORIES: 1942 births, Living people, American male backstroke swimmers, Former world record holders in swimming, North Carolina Tar Heels swimmers, Olympic gold medalists for the United States, Olympic medalists in swimming, Olympic swimmers of the United States, Swimmers at the 1964 Summer Olympics

Harold Thompson Mann (born December 1, 1942) is an American former competition swimmer, Olympic champion, and former world record-holder.  He competed at the 1964 Summer Olympics in Tokyo, Japan, where he received a gold medal as a member of the winning U.S. team in the men's 4x100-meter medley relay.  He also broke the world record in his leg of the relay, the 100-meter backstroke."1964 Summer Olympics – Tokyo, Japan – Swimming" – databaseOlympics.com (Retrieved on April 29, 2008)
