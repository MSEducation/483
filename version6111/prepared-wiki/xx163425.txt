[[Hystricella turricula]]

CATEGORIES: Hygromiidae

Hystricella turricula is a species of small, air-breathing land snail, a terrestrial pulmonate gastropod mollusk in the family Hygromiidae. This species is indigenous to Madeira.

==Shell description==

The shell of these snails is shaped like a discus, or a lens, with a sharp edge around the periphery of the whorls.

==Conservation status==

This species is mentioned in annex IV of the Habitats Directive.
