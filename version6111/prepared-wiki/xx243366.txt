[[Alcobaça River (Portugal)]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of Portugal

Alcoa River is a river in Portugal. It flows into the Atlantic Ocean near Nazaré. The two rivers, Alcoa and Baça confluent in the centre of Alcobaça passing the earlier (now arable land) sea of Pederneira by later seeping out just south of the fishing-harbour of Nazaré.
