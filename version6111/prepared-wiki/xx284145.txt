[[Felicity Passon]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 2000 births, Seychellois swimmers

Felicity Passon (born 1999 or 2000) is a Seychellois swimmer.http://www.bcn2013.com/en/swimming/detail/member/felicity-elizabeth-passon 

==Swimming Career==

Passon is a participant in competitions in the Seychelles. In a recent Ironwoman competition, she and two other swimmers Aurelie Fanchette and Clara Omath won their age group with a time of 20 minutes 40.3 seconds. The competition involved swimming, canoeing and running.
She competed in the 50 m, 100 m and 200 m freestyle, 50 m and 100 m butterfly and 100 m backstroke events at the 2012 FINA World Swimming Championships (25 m). Passon also competed in the 50 m freestyle and 50 m butterfly events at the 2013 World Aquatics Championships.
