[[Webster Baptist Church (Webster, New York)]]

CATEGORIES: Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Baptist churches in New York, Cobblestone architecture, Religious buildings completed in 1856, 19th-century Baptist church buildings, Churches in Monroe County, New York

Webster Baptist Church is a historic Baptist church located at Webster in Monroe County, New York.  It is a large Greek Revival style cobblestone church building built in 1856. The building features an octagonal open domed belfry supported by eight Ionic columns. It has a veneer of small, rounded and uniform lake-washed cobbles. It is one of only 14 cobblestone church buildings in New York state, and of 106 cobblestone structures in Monroe County.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=4224|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: Webster Baptist Church|date=September 1991|accessdate=2009-10-01 |author=Nancy L. Todd |publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation[/tpl] See also: [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=4223|title=Accompanying eight photos[/tpl]
It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1991.
