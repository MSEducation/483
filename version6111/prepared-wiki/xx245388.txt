Nolan Gould (born October 28, 1998)Modern Family Nation. Post at Twitter October 28, 2013. is an American child actor. He is best known for his role as youngest sibling Luke Dunphy on the ABC sitcom Modern Family. 

==Early life and education==

Gould was born in New York City, New York.[tpl]cite web| last= Harvey| first= Alec| title='Modern Family' star Nolan Gould has ties to Alabama| url= http://www.al.com/entertainment/index.ssf/2012/01/modern_family_star_nolan_gould.html| publisher= Al.com| accessdate=January 22, 2012[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Nolan Gould Biography|url=http://www.tvguide.com/celebrities/nolan-gould/bio/293440|publisher=TV Guide[/tpl] Shortly after his birth, because of his father's military career, he moved with his parents, Angela and Edwin, to Phenix City, Alabama, and when Gould was five years old, the family moved to California.http://www.deardoctor.com/inside-the-magazine/issue-19/nolan-gould/ His brother is actor Aidan Gould.
Gould is a member of Mensa,"It's Evening in America", Vanity Fair. May 2012. Page 158. and as of 2012, had accelerated ten grades in school. He stated on The Ellen Degeneres Show that he has an IQ of 150. During the 2012 summer, at age 13, he graduated from high school by taking a General Educational Development (GED) Test and said he hopes to take online college courses.[tpl]cite news|first=John|last=Johnson|title=Modern Family Star Nolan Gould Graduated High School Early at Age 13!| url= http://www.usmagazine.com/celebrity-news/news/modern-family-star-nolan-gould-graduated-high-school-early-at-age-13-20121110|newspaper=Us Weekly|date=October 11, 2012[/tpl] 
Gould has trained with Patrick Malone and Lisa Picotte for years, focusing on scene study, cold reading and improv.

==Career==

Gould began his career at the age of three doing commercials.[tpl]cite web|title=Nolan Gould Biography| url=http://web.poptower.com/nolan-gould.htm|publisher=Pop Tower[/tpl] Recent films include leads in the features Ghoul (based on the novel by Brian Keene) and Space Buddies. He is part of the ensemble cast on the TV show Modern Family which has twice won a Screen Actor's Guild Award for Best Ensemble in a Comedy Series.
Other television credits for him include Eleventh Hour and the movie of the week, Sweet Nothing in My Ear. During 2009, Gould appeared in commercials for The Hartford.

==Personal life and interests==

Gould plays the double bass and banjo. He received his first banjo after going on The Ellen Degeneres Show, and as a result, started playing instruments such as the didgeridoo and mandolin.
