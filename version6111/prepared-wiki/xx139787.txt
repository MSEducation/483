[[The Paralegal Institute]]

CATEGORIES: Distance Education and Training Council, Online colleges, Distance education institutions

The Paralegal Institute is a nationally accredited two-year college based in Phoenix, Arizona. The Paralegal Institute (TPI) offers programs specializing in paralegal, criminal justice, medical transcription and legal nurse consultation. 

==History==

The Paralegal Institute was founded in 1974 by John Morrison and is now run by Kathleen Mirabile. It was a paraprofessional residential school until 1979 when it became a distance learning institution. Since then, The Paralegal Institute received accreditation from the Distance Education and Training Council and was approved to grant degrees and diplomas by the State Board for Private Postsecondary Education.
The Paralegal Institute may issue two-year associate degree's in paralegal studies and criminal justice that are recognized by the U.S. Department of Education and Council for Higher Education Accreditation (CHEA).http://chea.org/search/actionInst.asp It has been accredited by Distance Education and Training Council since 1979.[tpl]cite news | url=http://www.detc.org/degree.html#PI | title=The Paralegal Institute, Inc. | publisher=Distance Education and Training Council |year= 2009 | first= | last=  | accessdate =2009-01-17[/tpl]
