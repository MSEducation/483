[[Pic de Médécourbe]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of the Pyrenees, Mountains of Andorra, Mountains of France, Mountains of Catalonia, International mountains of Europe, Andorra–France border, Andorra–Spain border, France–Spain border, Border tripoints

Pic de Médécourbe (Catalan: Pic de Medacorba) is a mountain in Europe that sits on the western tripoint boundary of Andorra, France and Spain. It is [tpl]convert|2914|m|ft|0[/tpl] tall. It overlooks Étang de Soulcem, a large artificial lake used for hydroelectricity, and the Vicdessos valley.Govern d'Andorra (1991). Atlas d'Andorra, map of northwestern Andorra, p.20. [tpl]ca icon[/tpl]
There is also an eastern tripoint of Andorra, France, and Spain approximately 6 km south of Pas de la Casa.
