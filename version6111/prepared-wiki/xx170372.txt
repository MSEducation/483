[[Belharra]]

CATEGORIES: Big wave surfing, Surfing locations in France, Labourd, Geography of Pyrénées-Atlantiques, Geography of Aquitaine

Belharra, ([tpl]lang-eu|Belarra[/tpl], literally "grass, weed") is a reef and a surf spot in France located off Saint-Jean-de-Luz in the Northern Basque Country.
