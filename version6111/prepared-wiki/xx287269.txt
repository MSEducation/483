[[Fort at Salisbury Point]]

CATEGORIES: Forts in Massachusetts, Salisbury, Massachusetts, Demolished buildings and structures in the United States

The Fort at Salisbury Point was a fort that existed between 1863 and 1865 in Salisbury, Massachusetts. It was located at the mouth of the Merrimack River, where eventual erosion washed it away.[tpl]cite web|title=Massachusetts|url=http://www.northamericanforts.com/East/ma.html#bev|publisher=American Forts Network|accessdate=13 August 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite book|last=Heitman|first=Francis B.|title=Historical Register And Dictionary Of The United States: 1789-1908|year=1994|publisher=Genealogical Pub.
