 Djamel Amani  ([tpl]lang-ar|أماني جمال[/tpl]) (born 17 June 1962 in Blida, Algeria) is a retired Algerian professional footballer.

==Club career==

He played for USM Alger in Algeria, Royal Antwerp FC in BelgiumStats - FC Antwerp and Aydınspor in Turkey.

==International career==

Amani was also part of the Algeria national football team, scoring 8 international goals.
