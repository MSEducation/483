[[Do Asb]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Do Asb ([tpl]lang-fa|دواسب[/tpl], also Romanized as Dow Asb and Duasb; also known as Duvast)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3061330|Do Asb[/tpl] is a village in Bonab Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 708, in 154 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
