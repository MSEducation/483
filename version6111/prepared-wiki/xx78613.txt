[[Ōita District, Ōita]]

CATEGORIES: Former districts of Ōita Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Ōita|大分郡|Ōita-gun[/tpl] was a district located in Ōita Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the district had an estimated population of 40,723 and density of 99.35 persons per km². The total area was 409.90 km².

==Dissolution==

As of January 1, 2005 the district had 4 towns.
