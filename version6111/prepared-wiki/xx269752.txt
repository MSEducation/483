[[Common Dead]]

CATEGORIES: Melodic death metal musical groups, American thrash metal musical groups, Musical groups established in 2009

Common Dead is a melodic thrash/groove metal band from Chicago, United States.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/artist/common-dead/discography/albums/1525535#/artist/common-dead/discography/albums/1525535 |title=Billboard: Common Dead |publisher=Billboard Music[/tpl] The band is also considered one of the background players in the new wave of American heavy metal.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.metal-rules.com/zine/content/view/1876/86/ |title=Interview with Common Dead |publisher=Metal Rules |date=February 20, 2010[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://metalbuzz.net/site/2011/06/03/common-dead-animalism/ |title=Common Dead: Animalism |publisher=MetalBuzz[/tpl]

==History==

Common Dead is classified as melodic death metal[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.spirit-of-metal.com/album-groupe-Common_Dead-nom_album-Common_Dead-l-en.html |title=Common Dead Band Info |publisher=Spirt of Metal Guide[/tpl]Decibel Magazine. 76. Feb. 2011 by various sources, although the band touches on melodic thrash metal, groove metal, and alternative metal influences. Andrew Laurenson is the band's sole proprietor and has performed all instrumentation for all recorded efforts thus far. He plays lead/rhythm guitar and vocals for live occasions.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bravewords.com/hardwares/1002011 |publisher=Brave Words & Bloody Knuckles |author=Greg Pratt |title=Review of Diatribe[/tpl]
Common Dead debuted with a self-titled album in 2009[tpl]Allmusic|class=artist|id=p2226152|pure_url=yes[/tpl] AllMusic Guide that earned favorable reviews from press.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.themetalforge.com/modules.php?name=Reviews&rop=showcontent&id=2966 |publisher=The Metal Forge |title=Third party review of Common Dead |date=August 11, 2010 |author=Robinson, Kyall[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.metal-rules.com/review/viewreview.php?month=February&year=2010&pos=12 |publisher=Metal Rules |title=Review of Common Dead (self-titled) |date=February 2010 |author=Williams, Robert[/tpl] In an interview with Metal-Rules.com, Laurenson commented that the band revolves around a melodic guitar influence and raw, forthright vocals. A four-track EP was released in January 2011 entitled Interim Flesh, which experimented with industrial metal influences[tpl]cite web|url=http://metalbuzz.net/site/news/common-dead-release-interim-flesh-ep/ |title=Common Dead Release Interim Flesh EP |publisher=MetalBuzz |date=January 20, 2011 |author=Ken Kopija (Editor in Chief)[/tpl] not typical of the band's style. The EP scored warm praise from critics.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.metal-rules.com/review/viewreview.php?month=January&year=2011&pos=4 |publisher=Metal Rules |title=Review of Interim Flesh |date=January 2011 |author=Williams, Robert[/tpl]
The band released an album Diatribe in February 2012. It was met with generally positive reviews from numerous sources.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.metalunderground.com/reviews/details.cfm?releaseid=5731 |publisher=Metal Underground |title=Diatribe Review |date=February 24, 2012 |author=Firuath [/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.brutalism.com/content/common-dead-diatribe |publisher=Brutalism |title=Third party review of Diatribe |date=March 10, 2012 |author=Brutalism[/tpl] The band has so far released three music videos for songs from this album. One titled "Critical Mass" was broadcast on Underground Video TV.[tpl]Cite episode |title= Underground Video TV |url= http://itunes.apple.com/ca/podcast/uvtv-heavy-metal-hardcore/id168075141 |credits= Underground Video TV |city= Chicago, IL|date= December 11, 2011 |episode=16[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.undergroundvideotelevision.com/2011/music-videos/common-dead-critical-mass/ |publisher=Underground Video TV |title=Common Dead Critical  Mass |date=Nov 25, 2011[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://itunes.apple.com/ca/podcast/uvtv-heavy-metal-hardcore/id168075141 |title=Underground Video Television Episode Listing (#16)|publisher=Underground Video TV[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.podfeed.net/episodes.asp?p=1&id=7692&ct=2 |title=Underground Video TV: Aired Episodes & Podcasts |publisher=Underground Video TV[/tpl] The following year, the album Allegorize was released.
Metal Underground interviewed the band after a raw mix for a Fear Factory cover came out on YouTube. Common Dead recorded a cover for "Flashpoint" as one instrumentalist. They also announced that new members would be joining the band for live performances.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.metalunderground.com/news/details.cfm?newsid=79384 |title=Metal Underground Exclusive with Common Dead |publisher=Metal Underground |date=April 9, 2012 [/tpl]
Laurenson was born in Chicago and is a former film studio intern at Sony Pictures in Culver City, California. His father Phil Laurenson was also an actor in extreme grindhouse horror films including the original The Wizard of Gore and Gore Gore Girls.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.metal-rules.com/zine/content/view/2038/91/ |publisher=Metal Rules |title=A Metal Halloween Interviews Feature |date=October 6, 2010 |author=Sandra Torres[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0491192/ |title=Phil Laurenson (IMDb) |publisher=IMBb[/tpl]
