The first motorway in the country was the Kumanovo-Petrovec section of the A-1, opened for traffic in 1979
In 2008 the country had [tpl]convert|228|km|mi|sp=us[/tpl] of motorways, with additional [tpl]convert|21|km|mi|sp=us[/tpl] under construction and the beginning of works on [tpl]convert|28|km|mi|sp=us[/tpl] (the Demir Kapija - Smokvica section of A-1) being postponed for 2009.
In 2008 the government also carried out an ambitious public tender for giving concessions for [tpl]convert|550|km|mi|sp=us[/tpl] of motorways. The outcome will be known in 2009.

===Road rules===

The traffic signs adhere to the Vienna Convention on Road Signs and Signals.
Lights must be on all the time, seatbelts are mandatory for passengers in the front and drivers are forbidden to speak on a mobile phone while driving.
