[[First Grand Constitution and Bylaws]]

CATEGORIES: 1996 albums, Secret Chiefs 3 albums, Web of Mimicry albums, Albums produced by Trey Spruance

First Grand Constitution and Bylaws is the debut studio album by American experimental rock band Secret Chiefs 3. It was released on September 30, 1996, through Amarillo Records.

==Track listing==

 Bonus tracks, only on remastered CD release.

==Critical reception==

AllMusic praised the album, noting that "the trio somehow manage to concoct even more mysterious and demented compositions than Mr. Bungle."
