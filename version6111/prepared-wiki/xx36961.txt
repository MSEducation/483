[[Vojens Municipality]]

CATEGORIES: Former municipalities of Denmark

Vojens Municipality ([tpl]lang-de|Woyens[/tpl]), was a municipality (Danish, kommune) in the former South Jutland County on the Jutland peninsula in south Denmark. The municipality, which existed from 1970 to 2006, covered an area of [tpl]convert|298|kmÂ²|0|abbr=on[/tpl], and had a total population of 16,792 (2005).  Its last mayor was Nis Mikkelsen, a member of the Venstre (Liberal Party) political party.
The site of its municipal council was the town of Vojens.

It merged with existing Gram and Haderslev municipalities, as well as Bjerning, Hjerndrup, Fjelstrup and Bevtoft parishes of Christiansfeld municipality to form the new Haderslev municipality.
