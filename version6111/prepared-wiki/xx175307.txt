[[KREJ]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Kansas, Christian radio stations in the United States

KREJ (101.7 FM) is a Christian radio station licensed to Medicine Lodge, Kansas.  The station is owned by Florida Public Radio, Inc.
KREJ fcc.gov. Accessed August 23, 2012
KREJ's programming includes Christian Talk and Teaching programs including; Revive our Hearts with Nancy Leigh DeMoss, Turning Point with David Jeremiah, Focus On The Family, The Phyllis Schlafly Report, Unshackled!, Enjoying Everyday Life with Joyce Meyer, In the Market with Janet Parshall, Love Worth Finding with Adrian Rogers, and Insight for Living with Chuck Swindoll. KREJ, KRTT, KNJT, K300 Program Schedule KREJ. Accessed August 23, 2012

==Translators==

KREJ is also heard on full powered stations 88.1 KRTT in Great Bend, Kansas, and 90.3 KNJT in Coldwater, Kansas, as well as a low powered translator on 97.1 in Woodward, Oklahoma.
