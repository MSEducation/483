[[CSM Dunărea Galaţi]]

CATEGORIES: Ice hockey teams in Romania, Ice hockey in Romania

CSM Dunărea Galaţi is an ice hockey club in Galați, Romania that plays in the Romanian Hockey League. It was established in 1932 under the name "Gloria C.S.U." Their home rink is the Galați Skating Rink. In the 2010-2011 season, SCM Dunărea was ranked fourth in the Romanian Hockey league.
