[[(32171) 2000 ND10]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 2000, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(32171) 2000 ND10 is a main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on July 1, 2000.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
