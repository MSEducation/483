[[Monte Real]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Portugal, Leiria, Populated places in Leiria District

Monte Real is a town (vila in Portuguese) and a parish (Pt: freguesia) in the municipality and District of Leiria. It covers an area of 12.23 km² and has a population of 2,778 people. Monte Real, which in English means Royal Mount, was once a municipality (pt: concelho), before being anexed to Leiria municipality.
Monte Real hosts an air base (base aérea), Monte Real Air Base for the Portuguese Air Force.[tpl]cite web|title=Base Aérea Nº 5|url=http://www.emfa.pt/www/unidade-18-ba5|accessdate=16 October 2012|language=Portuguese[/tpl]
Monte Real is also known for its hot springs, (Termas de Monte Real).[tpl]cite web|title=Termas Monte Real|url=http://www.termasdemontereal.pt/|accessdate=16 October 2012[/tpl]
