[[Frans Tuohimaa]]

Frans Tuohimaa (born August 19, 1991) is a Finnish professional ice hockey goaltender. He is currently playing for the Oklahoma City Barons of the American Hockey League (AHL). Jokerit in the SM-liiga. Tuohimaa was selected by the Edmonton Oilers in the 7th round (182nd overall) of the 2011 NHL Entry Draft.
Oilers make Dillon Simpson, son of ex-Oiler Craig Simpson, first pick in Round 4
