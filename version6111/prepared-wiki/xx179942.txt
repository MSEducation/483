[[Van Hise Rock]]

CATEGORIES: Geography of Sauk County, Wisconsin, National Historic Landmarks in Wisconsin, National Register of Historic Places in Wisconsin, Natural features on the National Register of Historic Places, Rock formations in Wisconsin

Van Hise Rock is a rock outcropping significant for its association with geologist Charles Van Hise.http://www.wisconsinhistory.org/hp/register/viewSummary.asp?refnum=97001267
It was declared a National Historic Landmark in 1997.[tpl]Cite document|title=[tpl]PDFlink|National Historic Landmark Nomination: Van Hise Rock|185 KB[/tpl]|date=July 24, 1994 |author=Joanne Kluessendorf |publisher=National Park Service|postscript=[/tpl] and [tpl]PDFlink|Accompanying 4 photos, of Van Hise himself, of explanatory plaque, and of outcropping, from 1991 and other dates.|670 KB[/tpl]
It is located on Wisconsin Highway 136, about 3/4 mile north of intersection with Wisconsin Highway 154.
