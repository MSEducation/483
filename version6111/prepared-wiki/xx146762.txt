[[Reagan's In]]

CATEGORIES: 1981 albums, Wasted Youth albums

Reagan's In is the 1981 debut album by the Los Angeles punk band Wasted Youth. The cover art was drawn by Pushead.

==Trivia==

Pushead's original drawing for the record was of Charles Manson with the swastika on the forehead. It was changed to Reagan without Pushead's consent.[tpl]citation needed|date=November 2011[/tpl]
