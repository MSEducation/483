[[Boutersem]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Flemish Brabant

Boutersem ([tpl]IPA-nl|ˈbɔ̞u̯t̪ərs̪əm[/tpl]) is a municipality located in the Belgian province of Flemish Brabant. The municipality comprises the towns of Boutersem proper, Kerkom, Neervelp, Roosbeek, Vertrijk and Willebringen. On January 1, 2006 Boutersem had a total population of 7,532. The total area is 30.75 km² which gives a population density of 245 inhabitants per km².
