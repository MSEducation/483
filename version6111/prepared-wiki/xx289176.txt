[[Varmanlu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Varmanlu ([tpl]lang-fa|ورمانلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Varmānlū)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3766558|Varmanlu[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 21, in 6 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
