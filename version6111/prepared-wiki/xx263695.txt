[[Sebeliau]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Sarawak

Sebeliau is a riverside village in the Lubok Antu division of Sarawak, Malaysia. It lies approximately [tpl]convert|161|km|mi|0[/tpl] east-south-east of the state capital Kuching.
Sebeliau jetty is the embarkation point for tourists travelling up the Lemanak River to visit an Iban longhouse.
