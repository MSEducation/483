[[Jiangkou County]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Guizhou

Jiangkou County (Chinese: 江口县; Pinyin: Jiāngkǒu Xiàn) is a county in Tongren Prefecture, Guizhou Province of China. 
Area: 1869 km2.
Population: 220,000 in 2002.
Postal Code: 554400.
The county government is located in Shuangjiang town.
