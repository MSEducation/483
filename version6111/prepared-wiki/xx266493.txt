[[Jack Manuel]]

CATEGORIES: 1991 births, Living people, People from Sutton Coldfield, English cricketers, Staffordshire cricketers, Worcestershire cricketers

Jack Kenneth Manuel (born 13 February 1991) is an English cricketer. Manuel is a left-handed batsman who bowls right-arm off break. He was born in Sutton Coldfield, Warwickshire and educated at Wilnecote High School in Tamworth.[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/305/305867/305867.html|title=Player profile: Jack Manuel|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=22 September 2011[/tpl]
Manuel made his debut in county cricket for Staffordshire in the 2009 MCCA Knockout Trophy against Suffolk. He made six appearances in the competition in 2009, with three further appearances in 2010.[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/305/305867/Minor_Counties_Trophy_Matches.html|title=Minor Counties Trophy Matches played by Jack Manuel|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=22 September 2011[/tpl] He made his debut for England Under-19s in 2009, playing Youth Test, One Day International and Twenty20 International matches in 2009 and 2010.[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/305/305867/Youth_Test_Matches.html|title=Youth Test Matches played by Jack Manuel|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=22 September 2011[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/305/305867/Youth_One-Day_International_Matches.html|title=Youth One-Day International Matches played by Jack Manuel|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=22 September 2011[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/305/305867/Youth_Twenty20_International_Matches.html|title=Youth Twenty20 International Matches played by Jack Manuel|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=22 September 2011[/tpl]
The 2010 season saw him make his debut for Worcestershire in a Twenty20 match in the Friends Provident t20 against Northamptonshire.[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/305/305867/Twenty20_Matches.html|title=Twenty20 Matches played by Jack Manuel|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=22 September 2011[/tpl] That same season also saw him make his List A debut against Sussex in the Clydesdale Bank 40.[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/305/305867/List_A_Matches.html|title=List A Matches played by Jack Manuel|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=22 September 2011[/tpl] The following season he made his first-class debut against Somerset in the 2011 County Championship.[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/305/305867/First-Class_Matches.html|title=First-Class Matches played by Jack Manuel|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=22 September 2011[/tpl] In his only first-class appearance to date, he scored 5 runs in Worcestershire's first-innings of the match, before being dismissed by Alfonso Thomas, while in their second-innings he was dismissed for a duck by Murali Kartik.[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Scorecards/316/316311.html|title=Worcestershire v Somerset, 2011 County Championship|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=22 September 2011[/tpl] To date he has played seven List A matches and thirteen Twenty20 matches.
