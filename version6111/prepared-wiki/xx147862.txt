[[Trish Doan]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 births, Canadian rock bass guitarists, Canadian heavy metal bass guitarists, Canadian people of Korean descent, Female bass guitarists, Living people, People from Gwangju, Musicians from London, Ontario, Canadian female guitarists, Sheridan College alumni

Trish Doan (born May 9, 1985 in Gwangju, South Korea) is the bassist of the all-woman band Kittie.  She joined in August 2005 along with Tara McLeod.  Doan is a niece of Sudbury, Ontario MPP Rick Bartolucci.  In addition to bass, she has also played guitar since the age of 13.  She has several body modifications:  Piercings include a 14g tongue stud, two 16g lip rings, 18g nose stud, 18g nose ring, 00g stretched ears, 2x 12g ear lobes, 4x 16g lobes, and 14g helix, and several tattoos, including one on her left wrist of a formation of the symbol Gwangju, the city in which she was born.  In 2006, Doan graduated from Sheridan College, earning a diploma in Media Arts: Film and Television Production.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.atvf.ca/ma/index.php |title=Sheridan's Media Arts Program |publisher=Atvf.ca |date= |accessdate=2012-02-19[/tpl]Sheridan Institute - Media Arts /[tpl]dead link|date=February 2012[/tpl]
On June 28, 2007 Doan announced that she would be taking a hiatus from Kittie, "due to pressing personal health matters".  Her stand-in replacement for the Sweet Revenge tour would be Jeff Phillips from Thine Eyes Bleed.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bravewords.com/news/69043 |title=BW&BK - KITTIE Bassist Sidelined For Sweet Revenge Tour; THINE EYES BLEED's Jeff Phillips To Stand In |publisher=Bravewords.com |date= |accessdate=2012-02-19[/tpl]  On March 4, 2008 it was officially announced that Doan had left the band Kittie due to the eating disorder Anorexia-Athletica-Nervosa that she had been struggling with for almost two years.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.roadrunnerrecords.com/blabbermouth.net/news.aspx?mode=Article&newsitemID=92189 |title=KITTIE Parts Ways With Bassist TRISH DOAN |publisher=Roadrunnerrecords.com |date= |accessdate=2012-02-19[/tpl]
On February 13, 2012 Kittie announced that Ivy Vuijic was resigning the band and that Trish Doan was back.
