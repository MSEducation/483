[[Liberty Professionals F.C.]]

CATEGORIES: Association football clubs established in 1996, Football clubs in Ghana, Liberty Professionals F.C., Sport in Accra, 1996 establishments in Ghana, Sports clubs in Ghana

Liberty Professionals Football Club is a Ghanaian professional football club based in Dansoman, Accra. The club are currently competing in the Glo Premier League.

==History==

Liberty Professionals, founded in 1996, have tried to use a scientific approach to the management of a football club throughout their history. The club started their first season in the Poly Tank Division One League, but were promoted to the Ghana Telecom Premier League within their second season. Since then the club has consistently maintained respectable league match performances. They are affiliated with Kenyan Premier League club Thika United.
