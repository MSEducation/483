[[Donghai Island]]

CATEGORIES: Islands of Guangdong

Donghai Island ([tpl]zh|s=东海岛|t=東海島|p=Dōnghǎi Dǎo[/tpl]) is an island in the southeast part of the urban area of Zhanjiang, Guangdong, People's Republic of China. It is part of Mazhang District. It has a coastline of [tpl]convert|159.48|km|2[/tpl], with a total area of [tpl]convert|286|sqkm[/tpl], making it the largest island of Guangdong and China's [tpl]Specify|date=March 2011[/tpl] fifth largest island. In 2008, it had a permanent population of around 211,000.[tpl]Citation needed|date=January 2011[/tpl]
