[[Golabar-e Sofla]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Golabar-e Sofla ([tpl]lang-fa|گلابرسفلي[/tpl], also Romanized as Golābar-e Soflá; also known as Ghalabar Sofla, Kulabar, Qolābar-e Pā’īn, Qolābar-e Soflá, Qolābar-e Soflā, Qulabīr Buzurg, and Qulabīr-e Buzurq)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3080320|Golabar-e Sofla[/tpl] is a village in Golabar Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,883, in 505 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
