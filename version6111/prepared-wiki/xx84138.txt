[[Kyōbashi Station (Tokyo)]]

CATEGORIES: Stations of Tokyo Metro, Railway stations in Tokyo, Tokyo Metro Ginza Line, Railway stations opened in 1932, 1932 establishments in Japan

[tpl]Nihongo|Kyōbashi Station|京橋駅|Kyōbashi-eki[/tpl] is a subway station on the Tokyo Metro Ginza Line in Chūō, Tokyo, Japan, operated by the Tokyo subway operator Tokyo Metro. It is numbered "G-10".

==Lines==

Kyōbashi Station is served by the Tokyo Metro Ginza Line from [tpl]Ja-stalink|Shibuya[/tpl] to [tpl]ja-stalink|Asakusa||Tokyo Metro, Toei, Tobu[/tpl].

==Station layout==

The station has one underground island platform, located on the 2nd basement (B2F) level, serving by two tracks.
