[[Bolsheignatovsky District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Mordovia

Bolsheignatovsky District ([tpl]lang-ru|Большеигна́товский райо́н[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-myv|Покш Игнадбуе[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-mdf|Оцю Игнатовань район[/tpl]) is an administrativeConstitution of the Republic of Mordovia and municipalLaw #96-Z district (raion), one of the twenty-two in the Republic of Mordovia, Russia. It is located in the northeast of the republic. The area of the district is [tpl]convert|834.2|km2|sp=us[/tpl].[tpl]citation needed|date=October 2012[/tpl] Its administrative center is the rural locality (a selo) of Bolshoye Ignatovo.Law #7-Z Population: [tpl]ru-census2010|8,313|;[/tpl] 9,483 (2002 Census);[tpl]ru-pop-ref|2002Census[/tpl] [tpl]su-census1989|10,615|.[/tpl] The population of Bolshoye Ignatovo accounts for 34.2% of the district's total population.
