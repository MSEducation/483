[[Wim Thoelke]]

CATEGORIES: German television personalities, German television presenters, German sports journalists, German game show hosts, 1927 births, 1995 deaths, People from Mülheim

Georg Heinrich Willem (Wim) Thoelke (May 9, 1927 in Mülheim an der Ruhr -  November 26, 1995 in Engenhahn) was a German TV entertainer. He worked during the 1960s and 1970s for TV sport serie Aktuelles Sportstudio on German channel ZDF. Thoelke was showmaster of the TV shows Drei mal Neun and Der große Preis in 1970s and 1980s.
Thoelke was married and had two children.
