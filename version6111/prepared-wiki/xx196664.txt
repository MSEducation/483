[[KIFG (AM)]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Iowa, Radio stations established in 1962

KIFG (1510 AM) is a commercial radio station serving the Iowa Falls, Iowa area.  The station primarily broadcasts an classic hits format.  KIFG is licensed to Times-Citizen Communications, Inc and has a daytime-only license.

==History==

On June 30, 2010 KIFG changed their format from adult contemporary to classic hits.
