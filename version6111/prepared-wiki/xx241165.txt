[[Bairin Right Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Bairin Right Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠪᠠᠭᠠᠷᠢᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠪᠠᠷᠠᠭᠤᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Baɣarin Baraɣun qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|c=巴林右旗 |p=Bālín Yòu Qí[/tpl]) is a banner of eastern Inner Mongolia, China. It is under the administration of Chifeng City, [tpl]convert|143|km[/tpl] to the south-southeast. Baarin Mongols live here. The distinct Mongolian dialect of this region is Baarin Mongolian.
