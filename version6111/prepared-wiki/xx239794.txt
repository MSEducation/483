[[Stara Gora pri Šentilju]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Municipality of Šentilj

Stara Gora pri Šentilju is a small dispersed settlement in the Slovene Hills ([tpl]lang-sl|Slovenske gorice[/tpl]) south of Šentilj v Slovenskih Goricah in the Municipality of Šentilj in northeastern Slovenia.Šentilj municipal site

==Name==

The name of the settlement was changed from Stara Gora to Stara Gora pri Šentilju in 1953.Spremembe naselij 1948–95. 1996. Database. Ljubljana: Geografski inštitut ZRC SAZU, DZS.
