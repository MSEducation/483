[[KRSV-FM]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Wyoming, Country radio stations in the United States, Lincoln County, Wyoming, Radio stations established in 1975

KRSV-FM (98.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Country music format. Licensed to Afton, Wyoming, USA.  The station is currently owned by Western Wyoming Radio, Inc. and features programing from ABC Radio .[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KRSV-FM |title=KRSV-FM Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
