[[Zenobi]]

CATEGORIES: Companies established in 1982, Interactive fiction, Video game companies of the United Kingdom, Video game development companies, Video game publishers

Zenobi is a video game company that is known for its interactive fiction. The company was started by John Wilson in late 1982.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.ifwiki.org/index.php/John_Wilson |title=John Wilson - IFWiki[/tpl]  Originally formed as a part-time operation it became full-time in early 1986. The company produced and published adventure-games for the ZX Spectrum (along with the Atari) range of home computer from 1982 to 1997. Since then it has concentrated on the "emulation" side of things and still produces numerous "compilation" CDs and DVDs.
