[[Gastric rugae]]

CATEGORIES: Digestive system

The gastric rugae (or gastric folds) may be seen during esophagogastroduodenoscopy or in radiological studies. They provide the stomach with increased surface area for nutrient absorption.[tpl]fact|date=November 2013[/tpl] When food enters the stomach, these rugae fold and become stretched in. The purpose is to allow it to expand. This therefore allows expansion in volume of the stomach without increased pressure.
