[[KAML-FM]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Wyoming, Radio stations established in 1980, Contemporary hit radio stations in the United States

KAML-FM (97.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Top 40 format. Licensed to Gillette, Wyoming, USA.  The station is currently owned by the Basin Radio Network, a division of Legend Communications of Wyoming, LLC and features programing from AP Radio and ABC Radio .[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KAML-FM |title=KAML-FM Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
KAML-FM and its three sister stations, KIML, KGWY, and KDDV are located at 2810 Southern Drive, Gillette. KAML-FM and KDDV share a transmitter site south of Gillette.

==History==

The station was assigned the call sign KOLL-FM on 1980-06-02.  On 1988-12-26, the station changed its call sign to the current KAML.[tpl]cite web|url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=24211&Callsign=KAML |title=KAML Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
KAML-FM has now become KAML 97.3, and it is a Top 40 radio station.
