[[Andrea Covi]]

CATEGORIES: 1968 births, Canoeists at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Italian canoeists, Living people, Olympic canoeists of Italy

Andrea Covi (born June 16, 1968) is an Italian sprint canoer who competed in the mid-1990s. He was eliminated in the semifinals of the K-4 1000 m event at the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta.
