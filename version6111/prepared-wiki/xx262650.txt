[[December 2010 Abuja attack]]

CATEGORIES: Mass murder in 2010, Terrorist incidents in Nigeria in 2010, Abuja, Improvised explosive device bombings in Nigeria

The December 2010 Abuja attack was a bomb attack on a barracks on the outskirts of Abuja, Nigeria, on 31 December 2010. Four people were killed, including a pregnant woman, and 26 were injured; according to defence minister Adetokunbo Kayode, all of the dead were civilians, as were most of the injured.[tpl]cite news|title=Barracks bomb claims 4 on New Year's Eve|url=http://www.timeslive.co.za/sundaytimes/article832114.ece/Barracks-bomb-claims-4-on-New-Years-Eve|accessdate=27 June 2012|newspaper=Times Live|date=1 January 2011[/tpl] The attack was the second in Abuja in three months, and was the first near a barracks in the country since its return to democracy in 1999.
