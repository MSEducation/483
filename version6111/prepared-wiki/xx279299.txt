[[Sarcham-e Olya]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Sarcham-e Olya ([tpl]lang-fa|سر چم عليا[/tpl], also Romanized as Sarcham-e ‘Olyā; also known as Sarcham-e Bālā)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3804217|Sarcham-e Olya[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 103, in 26 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
