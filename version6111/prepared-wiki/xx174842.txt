[[Gmina Skrzyszów]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Lesser Poland Voivodeship, Tarnów County

Gmina Skrzyszów is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Tarnów County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. Its seat is the village of Skrzyszów, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|7|km|mi|0[/tpl] south-east of Tarnów and [tpl]convert|81|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] east of the regional capital Kraków.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|86.23|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 13,022.

==Villages==

Gmina Skrzyszów contains the villages and settlements of Ładna, Łękawica, Pogórska Wola, Skrzyszów and Szynwałd.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Skrzyszów is bordered by the city of Tarnów and by the gminas of Czarna, Pilzno, Ryglice, Tarnów and Tuchów.
