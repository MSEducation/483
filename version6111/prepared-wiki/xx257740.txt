[[Hmawbi Township]]

CATEGORIES: Townships of Yangon Region

Hmawbi Township is a township  in the Yangon Region of Burma (Myanmar). It is located northwest of the city of Yangon. The principal town and administrative seat is Hmawbi. The Hmawbi airport is at Indan (Inntan), four kilometres northeast of the town of Hmawbi.
Hmawbi Township is home to the Myaung Dagar Industrial Zone, which is a [tpl]convert|1016|acre|km2[/tpl] zone constructed in 2006-2008 and is intended to house all of Yangon's steel factories.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.mmtimes.com/no407/b_brief.htm|title=Myaung Dagar Industrial Zone 80pc done|date=25 February 2008|work=Myanmar Times|accessdate=5 March 2012[/tpl] Operating licenses for steel smelters that do not relocate to the Industrial Zone are not renewed.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.mmtimes.com/no410/b008.htm|title=YCDC forces smelters out of Yangon|author=Htar Htar Khin|coauthors=Yi Yi Htwe|date=17 March 2008|work=Myanmar Times|accessdate=5 March 2012[/tpl]
