[[Malik Ek]]

CATEGORIES: Hindi-language films, Indian biographical films, Indian films

Malik Ek is a Hindi spiritual film on Sai Baba of Shirdi featuring Jackie Shroff, Manoj Kumar and Divya Dutta.[tpl]cite web|title=Malik Ek|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/movie-reviews/hindi/Malik-Ek/movie-review/6836820.cms|publisher=THE TIMES OF INDIA|accessdate=15 February 2013[/tpl] Shroff will be playing  in the title role. The film is directed by Deepak Balraj Vij and the music is scored by Anup Jalota, who also plays the role of Das Ganu. The film was released on 29 October 2010.[tpl]cite web|title=Jackie Shroff to play Shirdi Sai Baba|url=http://www.rediff.com/movies/2006/aug/03jackie.htm|publisher=www.rediff.com|accessdate=15 February 2013[/tpl]
