[[Niuean general election, 1993]]

CATEGORIES: Elections in Niue, 1993 elections in Oceania, 1993 in Niue

General elections were held in Niue on 27 February 1993.[tpl]cite journal |title=Niue in Review: Issues and Events, 1 July 1992 to 30 June 1993 |author=Stephen Levine |journal=The Contemporary Pacific |volume=6 |issue=1 |pages=185–189 |year=1994[/tpl] Turnout was 91%.
Following the election Frank Lui was elected Premier of Niue, defeating incumbent Young Vivian 11–9. John Tofo Funaki was elected Speaker.
