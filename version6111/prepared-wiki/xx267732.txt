[[Quintero Unido]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Chile, Association football clubs established in 1962, 1962 establishments in Chile

Club de Deportes Quintero Unido is a Chilean Football club, their home town is Quintero, Chile.
The club were founded on June 10, 1962 and participated for 5 years in Primera B, 12 years in Tercera División A and 2 seasons in Tercera División B.
