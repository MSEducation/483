[[List of mayors of Alexandria, Virginia]]

CATEGORIES: Mayors of Alexandria, Virginia, Lists of mayors of places in Virginia

Mayors of Alexandria, Virginia serve as the Chief Executive of the independent City of Alexandria, Virginia.  Elected at large by the voters of the city, the Mayor serves a three-year term.  The current mayor is William D. Euille, who is the first African-American mayor of Alexandria.Political Graveyard
