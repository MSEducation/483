Ulrich "Ueli" Bächli (born January 5, 1950 in Zürich)[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/ba/ueli-bachli-1.html | title = Ulrich "Ueli" Bächli | publisher = Sports Reference | accessdate = November 1, 2013[/tpl] is a Swiss bobsledder who competed from the mid-1970s to the early 1980s. Competing in two Winter Olympics, he won two silver medals  in the four-man event (1976, 1980).
Bächli also won four medals at the FIBT World Championships with three silvers (Two-man: 1982; Four-man: 1977, 1978) and one bronze (Four-man: 1979).
