[[Phil Von Stefenelli]]

CATEGORIES: 1969 births, Boston Bruins players, Canadian ice hockey defencemen, Ottawa Senators players, Hamilton Canucks players, Ice hockey people from British Columbia, Sportspeople from Vancouver, Living people

Phil Von Stefenelli (born April 10, 1969 in Vancouver, British Columbia) is a former ice hockey player. He played for the Boston Bruins and Ottawa Senators.
