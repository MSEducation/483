[[Dobrin, Sălaj]]

CATEGORIES: Communes in Sălaj County

Dobrin ([tpl]lang-hu|Debren[/tpl]) is a commune located in Sălaj County, Romania. It is composed of six villages: Deleni (Nagymonújfalu), Doba (Nagydoba), Dobrin, Naimon (Nagymon), Sâncraiu Silvaniei (Szilágyszentkirály) and Verveghiu (Vérvölgy).
Doba Mică (Kisdoba) is a hamlet in Doba village and a former fief of the Dobai family. It features a 17th-century wooden church.
