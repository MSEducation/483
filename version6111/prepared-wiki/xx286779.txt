[[OneScreen]]

CATEGORIES: Establishments in the United States, Companies based in Irvine, California

OneScreen is an Irvine, California-based online video technology and services company.http://www.crunchbase.com/company/onescreenhttp://www.inc.com/profile/onescreen OneScreen’s clients include producers, publishers, and advertisers in the digital video, television, film production, publishing, and advertising industries.

==Overview==

OneScreen is a privately held company headquartered in Irvine, CA and is currently ranked #14 in the Orange County Business Journal’s 2013 Best Places to Work in Orange County.http://bestplacestoworkoc.com/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=59&Itemid=1

==History==

OneScreen was founded in 2009 by CEO Atul Patel, who was also a founding member of Optimal Inc, LeadiD, and LeadROI. OneScreen’s clients range from large broadcasters and magazine conglomerates to bloggers and YouTube Channel owners and Multi-Channel Networks. On October 3, 2011, the Wall Street Journal and OneScreen announced a partnership to develop applications for several device platforms including Yahoo!, Samsung, Sony, Vizio, Boxee, and others.http://www.tvexchanger.com/platforms/onescreen-wsj-live/ On February 2, 2013 Wochit Inc. announced a platform that gives web publishers access to video news through partnerships with Grab Media, OneScreen and Rightster.http://www.multichannel.com/technology/wochit-lands-475-million-round/143273 On July 30, 2013, OneScreen and The Young Turks Network announced the TYT Network Roku Channel.http://www.videonuze.com/article/case-study-the-young-turks-expands-beyond-youtube-to-roku-and-beyond/?print=truehttp://newmediarockstars.com/2013/07/the-young-turks-network-launches-roku-channel-smartphone-apps/http://www.thevideoink.com/news/the-young-turks-network-roku-channel-expands-to-android-and-ios-devices/

==Technology==

OneScreen is used by video producers, digital publishers, and advertisers to establish networking profiles, access a digital video marketplace, and utilize a complete video platform that includes a content management system, with video publishing tools, online video analytics, custom HTML5, and Flash video players and applications. Digital publishers can access a sell side ad platform to manage ad inventory and revenue from video advertising as well as a video ad platform to serve and deliver IAB[tpl]Disambiguation needed|date=October 2013[/tpl] compliant video ads.
