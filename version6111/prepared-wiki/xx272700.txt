[[Your Best Friend]]

CATEGORIES: 2011 singles, Mai Kuraki songs, Japanese-language songs

"Your Best Friend" is the 37th single by Japanese singer Mai Kuraki. It was  released on October 19, 2011 on the Northern Music record label.[tpl]cite web|url=http://mai-kuraki.com/information/index.html#newrelease |title=INFORMATION｜倉木麻衣 Official Website |publisher=Mai Kuraki Official Website (Northern Music) |date=2011-09-27 |accessdate=2011-10-15[/tpl]

==Information==

"Your Best Friend" is the 40th ending theme for the Japanese animation series Case Closed (20th Season). "Step by Step" is the official theme song for the Mie Prefecture-based Nemu no Sato Hotel and Resort.[tpl]YouTube|id = W_R1GJKebf8|title = Nemu no Sato Hotel and Resort 2011 CM[/tpl]New TVCM for 2011 Nemu no Sato Hotel and Resort
Both songs were written and arranged by Kuraki and Giorgio "Giorgio 13" Cancemi from the R&B duo So' Fly. Cancemi, a Japanese-Italian, is also behind the song "Doshite Sukinandaro", a song by Cancemi's solo project Nerdhead (on Universal Music Japan label), which featured Kuraki on vocals.
The two songs are both included in Kuraki's 10th studio album, Over the Rainbow.

===Music video and radio airplay===

This song was launched on Space Shower TV and bayfm Chiba two weeks before its official release.[tpl]Citation needed|date=March 2012[/tpl]
