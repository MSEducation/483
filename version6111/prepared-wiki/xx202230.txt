[[Kokologho Department]]

CATEGORIES: Departments of Burkina Faso, Boulkiemdé Province

Kokologho is a department or commune of Boulkiemdé Province in central Burkina Faso. As of 2005 it had a population of 40,621. Its capital lies at the town of Kokologho.Burkinabé government inforoute communale
