[[Simon Watson Taylor (politician)]]

CATEGORIES: 1811 births, 1902 deaths, Members of the United Kingdom Parliament for English constituencies, UK MPs 1857–59

Simon Watson Taylor (1811 – 25 December 1902) was a politician in the United Kingdom. He was Member of Parliament (MP) for Devizes in Wiltshire from 1857 to 1859.Historical list of Members of Parliament: D
On 30 June 1843 Watson Taylor married Lady Hannah Charlotte Hay (1818–1887), one of the daughters of Field Marshal George Hay, 8th Marquess of Tweeddale (1787–1876), thus becoming a brother-in-law of the Duke of Wellington, the Marquess of Dalhousie, the 9th Marquess of Tweeddale, George Hay, Earl of Gifford, Admiral of the Fleet Lord John Hay, General Sir Richard Taylor, and Sir Robert Peel, 3rd Baronet.Lady Hannah Charlotte Hay at thepeerage.com
The marriage resulted in two daughters, Rose Edith (died 1933) and Violet Emily (died 1937). By each daughter there were grandchildren.
