[[Dana MacDuff]]

CATEGORIES: 1955 births, Living people, American film producers, American male film actors, United States Navy sailors, Male actors from Los Angeles, California, Male actors from Pasadena, California, People from Reno, Nevada, People from Belfast

Dana John MacDuff (born December 26, 1955) is a film producer and actor in Hollywood, California, and Reno, Nevada.
He was born in Pasadena, California, to the actor Tyler MacDuff and the former Beverlie May Anderson (born 1930), who divorced in 1961. He is named for his father's close friend, the actor Dana Andrews.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0532057/bio|title=Biography for Tyler MacDuff|publisher=Internet Movie Data Base|accessdate=January 9, 2010[/tpl] He graduated in 1974 from Blair International Baccalaureate School in Pasasdena.[tpl]cite web|url=http://namesdatabase.com/people/MACDUFF/DANA/19579|title=NamesDatabase: Dana MacDuff|publisher=namesdatabase.com|accessdate=January 9, 2010[/tpl] Like his father, Dana MacDuff served in the United States Navy, having been on active duty from 1974–1977. He then worked as a page at the American Broadcasting Company. After working in features for a time, he accepted a position as a floor and stage manager with the BBC in Belfast, Northern Ireland. He returned to Hollywood in 1995 to produce films.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0002689/bio|title=Biography for Dana MacDuff|publisher=Internet Movie Data Base|accessdate=January 9, 2010[/tpl]
MacDuff has worked in such pictures as Bad Blood (2006), An American Reunion (2003), and The Remnant (2001). He has acted in Alligator (1980) and Far from Bismarck (1999).[tpl]cite web|url=http://people.theiapolis.com/producer-39QZ/dana-macduff/|title=Dana MacDuff|publisher=theiapolis.com|accessdate=January 9, 2010[/tpl]
He and his older brother, Brandon R. MacDuff (born 1954), a 1972 graduate of Blair High School, have formed Oakdale Pictures, a production company in Reno.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oakdalepictures.us/contact.htm|title=Oakdale Pictures|publisher=oakdalepictures.us|accessdate=January 9, 2010[/tpl]
MacDuff was married from 1986–1995 to the former Anne Pope. He is divorced from Laurie Howell MacDuff, to whom he was married from 2001–2005.
