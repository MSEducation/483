[[Bəhmətli]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zaqatala District

Bəhmətli (known as Verxiyan until 1992)http://e-qanun.az/print.php?internal=view&target=1&docid=7976&doctype=0 is a village and municipality in the Zaqatala Rayon of Azerbaijan.  It has a population of 4,273.  The municipality consists of the villages of Bəhmətli and Kürdəmir.Belediyye Informasiya Sistemi [tpl]dead link|date=January 2013[/tpl]
