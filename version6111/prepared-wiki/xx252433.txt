[[Alfred Massey]]

CATEGORIES: English footballers, Stoke City F.C. players, The Football League players, 1918 births, 1916 births, 2006 deaths, Congleton Town F.C. players, English cricketers, Staffordshire cricketers

Alfred Massey (16 October 1918 – 24 January 2006) was an English footballer who played in the Football League for Stoke City. He also played for Congleton Town and Stafford Rangers.[tpl]cite book|last=Matthews|first=Tony|title=The Encyclopaedia of Stoke City|year=1994|publisher=Lion Press|isbn=0-9524151-0-0[/tpl]

==Career==

Massey was born in Stoke-on-Trent and played amateur football with Caversall Old Boys and Congleton Town before joining Stoke City in 1938. He played twice in 1938–39 before his career was interrupted by World War II. He later played for Stafford Rangers.
Outside of football, Massey played cricket for Staffordshire in the Minor Counties Championship between 1937 and 1949.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/122/122528/122528.html|title=Player profile: Alfred Massey|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=18 June 2011[/tpl]
