[[Narre Warren railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Premium Melbourne railway stations, Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1882

Narre Warren railway station is located on the Pakenham line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the south-eastern Melbourne suburb of Narre Warren opening on 10 Match 1882 on the western side of the level crossing. A new station opened east of the level crossing on 3 June 1995.Narre Warren Vicsig

==Platforms & services==

Narre Warren has one island platform with two faces. It is serviced by Metro Trains' Pakenham line services.Pakenham Line timetable Public Transport Victoria
