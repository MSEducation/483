[[3-Hydroxyacetophenone]]

CATEGORIES: Natural phenols

3-Hydroxyacetophenone is a chemical compound. It is a component of castoreum, the exudate from the castor sacs of the mature beaver.[tpl]cite journal|doi=10.1007/BF00994195|title=Pheromonal activity of single castoreum constituents in beaver,Castor canadensis|year=1991|last1=Müller-Schwarze|first1=D.|last2=Houlihan|first2=Peter W.|journal=Journal of Chemical Ecology|volume=17|issue=4|pages=715–34|pmid=24258917[/tpl]

==Related compounds==
