[[Westchester Wildkatz]]

CATEGORIES: Atlantic Coast Professional Basketball League teams, Basketball teams in New York, Port Chester, New York, Basketball teams in the New York metropolitan area

The Westchester Wildkatz are a charter team of the Atlantic Coast Professional Basketball League scheduled to play in its inaugural 2010-11 season.  Based in Westchester, New York, the Wildkatz play their home games at the Carver Center in nearby Port Chester.
