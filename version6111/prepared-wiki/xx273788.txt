[[Jachin, Alabama]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Choctaw County, Alabama, Unincorporated communities in Alabama

Jachin is an unincorporated community in Choctaw County, Alabama, United States. Jachin is located at the junction of Alabama State Route 17 and Alabama State Route 156 [tpl]convert|10.2|mi|km[/tpl] north-northeast of Butler. Jachin had a post office, which opened on March 19, 1892, and closed on February 27, 1993.[tpl]cite web|title=Postmaster Finder - Post Offices by ZIP Code|url=http://webpmt.usps.gov/pmt008.cfm|publisher=United States Postal Service|accessdate=September 25, 2011[/tpl]
