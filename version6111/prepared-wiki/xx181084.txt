[[KGTS]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Washington (state), Walla Walla County, Washington, Walla Walla University

KGTS (91.3 FM) is a radio station in College Place, Washington, broadcasting to the Walla Walla Valley, including Walla Walla, Washington, and the Tri-Cities. The station oversees the programming for the Positive Life Radio network. It is owned by Walla Walla University.
