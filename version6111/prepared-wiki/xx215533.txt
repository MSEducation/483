[[Kielmin]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Augustów County

Kielmin [tpl]IPAc-pl|'|k|J|e|l|m|i|n[/tpl] is a settlement in the administrative district of Gmina Płaska, within Augustów County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland, close to the border with Belarus.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) – TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|17|km|mi|0[/tpl] east of Płaska, [tpl]convert|36|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] east of Augustów, and [tpl]convert|88|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of the regional capital Białystok.
