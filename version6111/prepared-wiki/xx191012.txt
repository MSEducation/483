[[Mountain Center, California]]

CATEGORIES: Census-designated places in Riverside County, California, Census-designated places in California

Mountain Center is a census-designated place[tpl]gnis|2629133[/tpl] in west central Riverside County, California.  It lies along State Route 74 in the southern division of San Bernardino National Forest, just north of Lake Hemet.  Mountain Center lies midway between Hemet and Palm Desert, and it is southeast of the city of Riverside, the county seat of Riverside County.Rand McNally.  The Road Atlas '08. Chicago: Rand McNally, 2008, p. 15.  Its elevation is 4,518 feet (1,377 m).[tpl]cite gnis|id=1661073|name=Mountain Center|date = 1981-01-19|accessdate = 2008-04-29[/tpl]  Although Mountain Center is unincorporated, it has a post office, with the ZIP code of 92561.Zip Code Lookup
