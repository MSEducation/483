==Personal life==

Brant was born and raised in Mason, Ohio, the son of David Daugherty, an art teacher, and his wife, Mary Beth (Hirt) Daugherty, head of rehabilitation services for Shriners Hospitals for Children, Cincinnati. He grew up along with his brother, Adam, and sister, Caitey. Brant's father, David, was an art teacher at Mason Middle School when he died of cancer on February 19, 2009 at age 57.http://news.cincinnati.com/article/20130916/ENT11/309160031/Mason-actor-starts-Dancing-Stars-journeyhttp://hosting-25437.tributes.com/show/David-Brant-Daugherty-85358547http://www.legacy.com/obituaries/dayton/obituary.aspx?n=David-Daugherty&pid=124387346#sthash.kJl38oQ1.dpbs 
Brant attended William Mason High School, where he played football until his sophomore year and later tried out for the school play. He continued to perform in school plays until his graduation in 2004.http://news.cincinnati.com/article/20130916/ENT11/309160031/Mason-actor-starts-Dancing-Stars-journeyhttp://masonbuzz.com/tag/brant-daugherty/ He moved to Los Angeles in 2008 after earning a film degree from Columbia College Chicago.
