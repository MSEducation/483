[[Angriffsführer England]]

CATEGORIES: Luftwaffe

Angriffsführer England (Attack Leader England) was a Luftwaffe combat unit created in April 1943 in Le Coudray. The Stab was directly subordinated to the Oberkommando der Luftwaffe. The unit was integrated into the IX. Fliegerkorps in July 1943.
