[[Kate Crossan]]

CATEGORIES: Irish female singers, Irish folk singers, Irish pop singers, Living people, New Age musicians, Celtic fusion musicians

Kate Crossan (also has performed under the name Cait agus Sean, meaning Kate and John) is an Irish singer and composer.
