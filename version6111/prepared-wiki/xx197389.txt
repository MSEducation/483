Pascal Vanderveeren (born on 16 March 1946, in Brussels) is a Belgian lawyer.  He was the president of the International Criminal Bar (the bar association of the International Criminal Court) during 2007-2010.  He was president of the bar in Belgium during 1998-2000.  He also founded his own law firm, Vanderveeren & Associes, in Brussels, which specializes in business crime.
Vanderveeren received his Dr. Jur. from Université catholique de Louvain in 1968, and Lic. Econ. Law from Université Libre de Bruxelles in 1969.  He is married to Ana da Silveira.
