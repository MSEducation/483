[[2013 US Open – Boys' Singles]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 US Open (tennis)

Filip Peliwo was the defending champion, but could not defend his title as he was no longer eligible to compete in junior tennis. 
Borna Ćorić won the title, defeating Thanasi Kokkinakis in the final, 3–6, 6–3, 6–1.
