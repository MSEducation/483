[[WTUS-LP]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Alabama, Low-power FM radio stations in Alabama, Talk radio stations in the United States, Tuscaloosa County, Alabama

WTUS-LP (103.3 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve Tuscaloosa, Alabama.  The station is owned by the City of Tuscaloosa Travel and Convention Bureau. It airs a Traveller's Information Station format.[tpl]cite web |work=Alabama Broadcast Media Page |url=http://bellsouthpwp2.net/c/r/crackedlcd/almediapage/lpfm.html |accessdate=2008-05-21 |title=Alabama LPFM & Translators[/tpl]
The station was assigned the WTUS-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on October 19, 2004.[tpl]cite web |title=Call Sign History |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=134739&Callsign=WTUS-LP |work=FCC Media Bureau CDBS Public Access Database[/tpl]  WTUS shares time on the channel with WUAC-LP.  WTUS broadcasts from 6:00 AM to 6:00 PM daily, with WUAC broadcasting the other twelve hours of each day.
