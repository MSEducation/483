[[KJAX]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Wyoming, Country radio stations in the United States

KJAX (93.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to Jackson, Wyoming, USA.  The station is currently owned by Chaparral Broadcasting, Inc. and features programing from CBS Radio. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KJAX |title=KJAX Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==History==

The station was assigned the KBHJ call letters on 1998-07-17. On 2000-05-26, the station changed its call sign to the current KJAX. [tpl]cite web |title=KJAX Call Sign History |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=82088&Callsign=KJAX   |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
On January 31, 2013, KJAX moved from 93.3 FM to 93.5 FM.

==Ownership==

In March 2007, Denver-based Blue Point Media announced that it was set to merge with KJAX owner Chaparral Broadcasting Inc.[tpl]cite news |work=Idaho Mountain Express |title=Chaparral set to merge with Denver company; Blue Point Media buying stations throughout West |url=http://www.mtexpress.com/index2.php?issue_date=03-09-2007&ID=2005114463 |first=Jody |last=Zarkos |date=2007-03-09[/tpl] Chaparral Broadcasting also owns three other radio stations in Wyoming as well as three stations in Idaho.
