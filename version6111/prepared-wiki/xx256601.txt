Nikolaj Misiuk (born 4 January 1987) is a Lithuanian footballer who plays as a forward. Born in Vilnius, he made 58 appearances in the A Lyga for Žalgiris, FBK Kaunas and Tauras Tauragė.[tpl]cite web|title=Nikolaj Misiuk|url=http://www.footballdatabase.eu/football.joueurs.nikolaj.misiuk.58607.en.html|publisher=Football Database|accessdate=19 March 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Nikolaj Misiuk|url=http://www.90minut.pl/kariera.php?id=13305|publisher=90minut|accessdate=19 March 2013[/tpl] Misiuk also played in the Meistriliiga for Narva Trans and the Belarusian Premier League for Neman Grodno. In 2011, he made one appearance in the second qualifying round of the UEFA Europa League,[tpl]cite web|title=Den Haag 2–0 Tauras|url=http://www.uefa.com/uefaeuropaleague/season=2012/matches/round=2000269/match=2007100/index.html|publisher=UEFA|accessdate=19 March 2013[/tpl] and spent time on trial with Plymouth Argyle.[tpl]cite news|title=No contracts for trialists after they help Argyle to a 3–1 victory at Tavistock|url=http://www.thisisdevon.co.uk/story-13048840-detail/story.html|accessdate=19 March 2013|newspaper=Western Morning News|date=1 August 2011|location=Plymouth[/tpl]
