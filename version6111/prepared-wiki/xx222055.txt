[[Spare Time Machine]]

CATEGORIES: Pepe Deluxé albums, 2007 albums, Neo-psychedelia albums, English-language albums, Art rock albums

Spare Time Machine is the third album by Pepe Deluxé, released in 2007. The first Pepe Deluxé album entirely without samples, it features dozens of musicians including several members of the band Husky Rescue.  Though sales were slow, the group's third album resonated with music journalists, earning an Emma (the Finnish version of Grammy) under the "critic's choice" category.Tiny Mix Tapes interview Spare Time Machine was ranked as one of PopMatters' Slipped Discs 2007.PopMatters Slipped Discs 2007
