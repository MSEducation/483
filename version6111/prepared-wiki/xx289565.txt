[[Seqerchin, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Seqerchin ([tpl]lang-fa|سقرچين[/tpl], also Romanized as Seqerchīn and Saqarchīn)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3082362|Seqerchin[/tpl] is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 736, in 172 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
