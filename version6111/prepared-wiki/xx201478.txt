[[Rory Kockott]]

CATEGORIES: 1986 births, Living people, South African rugby union players, Sharks (rugby union) players, Natal Sharks players, Lions (Super Rugby) players, People from East London, Eastern Cape, Rugby union scrum-halves, Castres Olympique players

Rory Kockott born 25 June 1986 in East London, South Africa is a rugby union player and attended Selborne College. He currently plays for Castres Olympique after signing from the Sharks in the Super Rugby competition. He plays as a scrumhalf.
