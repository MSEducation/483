[[Saidabad-e Olya, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Saidabad-e Olya ([tpl]lang-fa|سعيدابادعليا[/tpl], also Romanized as Sa‘īdābād-e ‘Olyā and Sa‘id Abad Olya; also known as Sa‘īdābād, Sa‘īdābād-e Bālā, Saiyidābād, and Seydābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3081923|Saidabad-e Olya[/tpl] is a village in Saidabad Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 913, in 266 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
