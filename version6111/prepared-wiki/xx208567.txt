[[Buzava]]

CATEGORIES: Ethnic groups in Russia, Kalmyk people, Cossacks, Oirats, Kalmykia

The Buzava or Buzava Kalmyks are the ethnic Kalmyk people centered in the western Republic of Kalmykia, in the present day Southern Federal District of Russia. 
In 1699 group of the Oirat DÃ¶rbets, a Choros clan within the Mongol tribe, migrated from the Buddhist Oirat Kalmyks people in the Volga River area to join the Don Cossacks people. They eventually became called the Buzava Kalmyks.  
They resettled with the Don Cossacks along the middle and lower Don River in Kalmykia.
