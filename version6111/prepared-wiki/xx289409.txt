[[Shurja]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Shurja ([tpl]lang-fa|شورجا[/tpl], also Romanized as Shūrjā; also known as Sarjāh, Shoorcheh, and Shūrjeh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3085458|Shurja[/tpl] is a village in Shivanat Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 241, in 45 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
