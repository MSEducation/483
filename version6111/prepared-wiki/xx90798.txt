[[Henry Saari]]

CATEGORIES: 1964 births, Living people, People from Pori, Finnish male pornographic film actors

Henry Saari (born 14 January 1964, in Pori, Finland), also known as Henry the Great (Henry Suuri) is a Finnish actor, director and porn star. In 1993, Saari won the title of Mr. Finland and placed 3rd in the Mr. Europe contest.Interview [tpl]fi[/tpl]
Saari is best known for the five eponymous Henry the Great movies he directed and starred in between 1998 and 2001.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.city.fi/artikkeli/Henry+Saari/88/ |title=Henry Saari |publisher=City.fi |date= |accessdate=2013-12-06[/tpl]  Aside from his pornographic work, Saari had parts in the Hollywood movie History Is Made at Night (1999) and the mainstream Finnish movie Young Gods (2003).
