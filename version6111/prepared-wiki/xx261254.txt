[[Talkhan]]

CATEGORIES: Afghan cuisine

Talkhan is an Afghan local sweet, made from walnut and red or white mulberry. Talkhan are said to resemble chocolate, only they are lighter and coarser.[tpl]cite book|author=Bell|title=American Engineer in Afghanis|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=V57cz7xlcZgC&pg=PA267|accessdate=3 January 2011|date=1 January 1999|publisher=U of Minnesota Press|isbn=978-0-8166-0046-5|page=267[/tpl] Talkhan is mainly produced in the Hindukush mountain valleys, specially Panjshir Province.
