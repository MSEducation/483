[[Trazana Beverley]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, New York University alumni, American film actresses, Tony Award winners, 20th-century American actresses, American stage actresses

Trazana Beverley is an American stage and film actress. She received an MFA in Acting from New York University's Graduate Acting Program.http://gradacting.tisch.nyu.edu/object/ga_alumbios.html
She won a Tony Award for "Best Featured Actress in a Play" for the 1977 Broadway play For Colored Girls Who Have Considered Suicide When the Rainbow Is Enuf,[tpl]IBDB name|74369[/tpl] having previously won a Theatre World Award for the Off-Broadway production.Trazana Beverley at the Internet Off-Broadway Database Beverley also shared a cast Obie Award for the latter. In addition, Beverley earned 1977 Theatre World and Drama Desk Awards for the Broadway version.
