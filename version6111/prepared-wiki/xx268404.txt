[[Stefan Sinovec]]

CATEGORIES: 1988 births, Living people, Basketball League of Serbia players, KK Metalac Valjevo players, KK Partizan players, KK Radnički Kragujevac players, Serbian basketball players, Shooting guards, Sportspeople from Belgrade

Stefan Sinovec (born June 20, 1988) is a Serbian professional basketball player. He currently plays for MZT Skopje.

==Pro career==

Sinovec grew up with Usce Vizura Belgrade juniors, made his debut with Vizura during the 2005-06 season and played there till February ' 09, moved to Ukraine, signed for the remainder of the season by Khimik. He back to Serbia for the 2009-10 season, signed by Partizan. For 2010–2011 season he moved to Metalac Valjevo. In 2011 he goes to KK Radnički Kragujevac. In July 2013 he signed for the Macedonian basketball champion MZT Skopje.
