[[Morotsuka, Miyazaki]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Miyazaki Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Morotsuka|諸塚村|Morotsuka-son[/tpl] is a village located in Higashiusuki District, Miyazaki Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the village has an estimated population of 2,202 and the density of 11.74 persons per km². The total area is 187.59 km². Its industry is forestry and farming, and its local products include shiitake mushrooms, tea, and miso. Despite the small size of the village, it has a strong community and many popular festivals steeped in Japanese traditional culture.  
To get to Morotsuka, travel northwest from Hyuga up route 327.
