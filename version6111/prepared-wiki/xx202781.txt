[[50 West Street]]

CATEGORIES: Residential skyscrapers in Manhattan, Skyscrapers between 200 and 249 meters, Buildings and structures under construction in the United States

50 West Street is a new 63-story, mixed-use retail and residential condominium tower developed by Time Equities Inc. rising in Lower Manhattan, New York City. Renowned architect Helmut Jahn has designed an iconic tower of rare curved glass that will provide breathtaking views of the New York Harbor and waterways.
