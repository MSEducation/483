[[Yaddi Bolagh, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Yaddi Bolagh ([tpl]lang-fa|يدي بلاغ[/tpl], also Romanized as Yaddī Bolāgh, Yeddī Bolāgh, and Yadī Bolāgh; also known as Yadibulak and Yadibulāq)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3088413|Yaddi Bolagh[/tpl] is a village in Golabar Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 79, in 23 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
