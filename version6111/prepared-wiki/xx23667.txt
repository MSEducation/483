[[Lee Armstrong]]

CATEGORIES: 1970 births, American film actresses, Living people

Lee Armstrong (born January 2, 1970) is an American actress. She starred in two movies in 1995, Magic Island and Leprechaun 3. She had an uncredited role in the 40 minute family short "Classic Stories for Children" in 1992. According to IMDB she appeared in a commercial for Ocean Spray. She also served as an intern on the Howard Stern radio show.
