[[Republican Christian Party]]

CATEGORIES: Christian political parties in Ukraine, Political parties in Ukraine, Political parties established in 1997

The Republican Christian Party ([tpl]Lang-uk|Республiканська Християнська партiя[/tpl]) is a political party in Ukraine registered July 1997.[tpl]uk icon[/tpl] Республіканська Християнська партія, Database DATA

==History[tpl]Uk icon[/tpl] Databases ASD: Political parties in Ukraine==

The party ran independently at the Ukrainian parliamentary election, 1998 winning 0.54% and no seats.
At the legislative elections of 30 March 2002, the party was part of the Viktor Yushchenko Bloc Our Ukraine. It did not participate in the Ukrainian parliamentary election, 2006. In the 30 September 2007 elections, the party failed as part of the Ukrainian Regional Asset to win parliamentary representation.
During the 2010 Ukrainian local elections the party won 1 representatives in the Lviv Oblast Council (regional parliament).[tpl]uk icon[/tpl] Results of the elections, preliminary data, on interactive maps by Ukrayinska Pravda (November 8, 2010) In the council of Lviv the party won 3 seats.
The party did not participate in the 2012 parliamentary elections.[tpl]uk icon[/tpl] Results of voting in single constituencies in 2012 & Nation-wide list, Central Election Commission of Ukraine
