[[Prime Minister of Bulgaria]]

CATEGORIES: Prime Ministers of Bulgaria

The Prime Minister of Bulgaria ([tpl]lang-bg|Министър-председател, Ministar-predsedatel[/tpl]) is the head of government of Bulgaria. He or she is the leader of a political coalition in the Bulgarian parliament – known as the National Assembly of Bulgaria ([tpl]lang-bg|Народно събрание, Narodno sabranie[/tpl]) – and the leader of the cabinet. 
The current Prime Minister is Plamen Oresharski, who has served since 29 May 2013.
