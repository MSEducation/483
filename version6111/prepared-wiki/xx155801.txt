[[Ralf Pönitzsch]]

CATEGORIES: 1957 births, Living people, East German long-distance runners

Ralf Pönitzsch (born 20 October 1957) is a former East German runner who specialized in the 3000 metres steeplechase.
He won the silver medal in this event at the 1979 IAAF World Cup behind Kip Rono.IAAF World Cup in Athletics (GBR Athletics)
He won four East German national titles (1976, 1977, 1979, 1981) in his special distanceEast German championships, men's 3000 m steeplechase as well as the 5000 metres title in 1979.East German championships, men's 5000 m He competed for the sports club SC Karl-Marx-Stadt during his active career.
