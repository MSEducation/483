[[Reg Krezanski]]

CATEGORIES: 1948 births, Canadian ice hockey defencemen, Ice hockey people from British Columbia, People from New Westminster, Rochester Americans players, San Diego Mariners players, San Diego Mariners (PHL) players, Living people

Reg Krezanski (born January 1, 1948 in New Westminster, British Columbia) is a retired World Hockey Association player. He played 2 games for the San Diego Mariners in the 1974-75 season.
