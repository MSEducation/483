[[Qareh Goni]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Qareh Goni ([tpl]lang-fa|قره گني[/tpl], also Romanized as Qareh Gonī; also known as Qarah Gownī)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795866|Qareh Goni[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
