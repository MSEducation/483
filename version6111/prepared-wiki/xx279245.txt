[[Qaheran]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Qaheran ([tpl]lang-fa|قاهران[/tpl], also Romanized as Qāherān and Qāharān; also known as Kaharān and Kakharan)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3078764|Qaheran[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 267, in 70 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
