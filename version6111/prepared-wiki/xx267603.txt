[[Krunk UAV]]

CATEGORIES: Unmanned aerial vehicles of Armenia

Krunk (Armenian: Կռունկ) is an Armenian unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) in service with the Armed Forces of Armenia.[tpl]cite news|title=Armenia Claims Its Military Produces Drones|url=http://www.rferl.org/content/article/24241481.html|accessdate=9 May 2012|newspaper=Radio Free Europe[/tpl]  It is intended for close reconnaissance, transmitting real-time video data (visual or infrared) or taking higher resolution still images.
The Krunk was demonstrated for the first time on September 21, 2011 during a military parade dedicated to the 20th anniversary of the independence of Armenia. http://www.suasnews.com/2011/09/8336/armenian-made-uavs-on-parade/[tpl]cite news|title=UAV’s Used in Separatist Nagorno Karabakh during Military Exercise|url=http://www.unmanned.co.uk/unmanned-vehicles-news/unmanned-aerial-vehicles-uav-news/uavs-used-in-separatist-nagorno-karabakh-during-military-exercise/|accessdate=9 May 2012[/tpl] 
There are currently 15 Krunk UAVs in the Armenian Armed Forces.
