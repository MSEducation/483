[[Barnes Island]]

CATEGORIES: San Juan Islands

Barnes Island is a small island in the San Juan Islands of the U.S. state of Washington. It is located near Clark Island off the northeast coast of Orcas Island.
The name was given by Charles Wilkes during the Wilkes Expedition of 1838-1842, to honor a sailor who was killed during the Battle of Lake Erie of the War of 1812.
