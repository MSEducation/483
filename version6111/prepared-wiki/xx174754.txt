[[KGWY]]

CATEGORIES: Country radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Wyoming, Radio stations established in 1983

KGWY (100.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to Gillette, Wyoming, USA, the station is currently owned by Basin Radio Network, a division of Legend Communications of Wyoming, LLC.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KGWY |title=KGWY Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
KGWY and its three sister stations, KIML, KAML-FM, and KDDV are located at 2810 Southern Drive, Gillette. The KGWY transmitter site is on Rain Dancer Court, close to the radio studios.

==History==

The station was assigned the call sign KGWY on 1983-01-18.[tpl]cite web|url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=54044&Callsign=KGWY |title=KGWY Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
