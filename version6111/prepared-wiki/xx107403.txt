[[Bellingham Theatre Guild]]

CATEGORIES: Theatre companies in Washington (state), Bellingham, Washington, Performing groups established in 1929, Visitor attractions in Bellingham, Washington

The Bellingham Theatre Guild is a community theater located in Bellingham, Washington.  Founded in 1929, the guild has been housed in its current location - the old Congregational Church converted for live theater use - since 1944.[tpl]cite web | title = Bellingham Theatre Guild - A Short History | url = http://www.bellinghamtheatreguild.com/history/history.html | accessdate = 2008-11-13| archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20081012011342/http://www.bellinghamtheatreguild.com/history/history.html| archivedate= 12 October 2008 | deadurl= no[/tpl]
Oscar winner Hilary Swank spent some of her early years acting at the guild and in the surrounding community.[tpl]cite web | title = Hilary Swank Biography | url = http://www.tiscali.co.uk/entertainment/film/biographies/hilary_swank_biog/2 | accessdate = 2008-11-13| archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20081226081711/http://www.tiscali.co.uk/entertainment/film/biographies/hilary_swank_biog/2| archivedate= 26 December 2008 | deadurl= no[/tpl]
