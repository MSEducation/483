[[Wonder World (Texas)]]

CATEGORIES: Buildings and structures in San Marcos, Texas, Recorded Texas Historic Landmarks, San Marcos, Texas, Visitor attractions in Hays County, Texas

Wonder World is an amusement park located in San Marcos, Texas.  The park's primary attraction is Wonder Cave, an ancient earthquake cave and Recorded Texas Historic Landmark.Texas Historical Commission Other attractions include an anti-gravity house, a trackless motor train,Image of the Wonder World trackless motor train and a wildlife park.
