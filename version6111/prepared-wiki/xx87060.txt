David Eugene Price (born August 17, 1940) is a professor and the U.S. Representative for [tpl]ushr|NC|4[/tpl], serving since 1997 and previously from 1987 to 1995. He is a member of the Democratic Party. The district includes portions of, among other cities, Burlington, Raleigh, Durham, and all of Chapel Hill.

==Early life, education, and academic career==

Born in Erwin, Tennessee, Price attended Mars Hill College in Mars Hill, North Carolina when it was a junior college.[ref]
