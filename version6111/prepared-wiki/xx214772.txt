[[Giorgos Poniros]]

Giorgos Poniros ([tpl]lang-gr|Γιώργος Πονηρός[/tpl]; born 8 October 1987 in Agrinio, Greece) is a professional football goalkeeper. He currently plays for Diagoras F.C..[tpl]cite web|publisher=Hellenic League|title=Β Εθνική / ΠΑΝΑΙΤΩΛΙΚΟΣ ΓΦΣ Π.Α.Ε. / ΠΟΝΗΡΟΣ ΓΕΩΡΓΙΟΣ|url=http://www.epae.org/player.fds?categ=2&team=46&player=11449&pagecode=04.02.02&langid=1|language=Greek|accessdate=2009-11-24[/tpl]  
Poniros started his career at Akarnanikos. In the summer of 2008, he signed with Panetolikos F.C.. On 23 August 2010, he signed a 1-year loan contract with Gamma Ethniki team Aias Salamina F.C..http://www.panaitolikos.gr/main_page/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=660:2010-08-22-09-38-38&catid=1:latest-news&Itemid=131  In the summer of 2011, he signed with Diagoras F.C..
