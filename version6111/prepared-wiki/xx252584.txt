Johan Clarey (born 8 January 1981)http://www.fis-ski.com/uk/604/613.html?sector=AL&competitorid=10394&type=result is a World Cup alpine ski racer from France. He specializes in the speed events of downhill and super-G.
Born in Annecy, Haute-Savoie, Clarey made his World Cup debut in November 2003 and has three World Cup podiums through March 2014.
