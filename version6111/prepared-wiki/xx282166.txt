[[HIF Akademi]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Sweden

HIF Akademi is a Swedish football club located in Helsingborg.[tpl]cite web |url= http://www.hif.se/StandardPage.aspx?id=bb340d5c-c81e-4c44-9d0d-6896d5dd227e |title= Helsingborgs IF Akademi |format= |publisher=Helsingborgs IF |accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl] The club works as a feeder team to Helsingborgs IF.

==Background==

HIF Akademi was created on 2 March 2013 after Helsingborgs IF took over the club Ramlösa Södra FF and renamed it Helsingborgs IF Akademi.[tpl]cite web |url= http://hd.se/sport/2013/03/05/detta-hander-pa-heden/ |title= Detta händer med Södra |format= |publisher=Helsingborgs Dagblad |accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl] They currently play in Division 2 Västra Götaland which is the fourth tier of Swedish football.[tpl]cite web |url= http://www.everysport.com/sport/fotboll/fotbollsserier-2013/division-2-herr/vstra-gtaland/57978|title= Division 2 Västra Götaland |format= |publisher=Everysport|accessdate=10 August 201[/tpl] They play their home matches at the Olympia in Helsingborg.
The club is affiliated to Skånes Fotbollförbund.[tpl]cite web |url= http://svenskfotboll.se/cuper-och-serier/information/?feid=9333 |title= Kontaktuppgifter och tävlingar 2013 – Helsingborgs IF Akademi|format= |publisher=SvFF |accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl]
