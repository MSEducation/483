[[Qashqa Tappeh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Qashqa Tappeh ([tpl]lang-fa|قاشقاتپه[/tpl], also Romanized as Qāshqā Tappeh; also known as Qashqeh Tappeh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3766373|Qashqa Tappeh[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 65, in 16 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
