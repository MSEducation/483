[[Hiller Building]]

CATEGORIES: Residential buildings completed in 1859, Federal architecture in Iowa, Apartment buildings on the National Register of Historic Places in Iowa, Apartment buildings in Davenport, Iowa, National Register of Historic Places in Scott County, Iowa

The Hiller Building is located on the edge of downtown Davenport, Iowa, United States.  It has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since 1974.  The Federal style building is a row house.
