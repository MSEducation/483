[[Luis Fonseca (athlete)]]

CATEGORIES: 1977 births, Athletes (track and field) at the 2003 Pan American Games, Athletes (track and field) at the 2004 Summer Olympics, Athletes (track and field) at the 2008 Summer Olympics, Living people, Olympic athletes of Venezuela, Venezuelan long-distance runners, Venezuelan marathon runners

Luis Heberto Fonseca (born June 3, 1977) is a Venezuelan long-distance runner, who twice represented Venezuela in the men's marathon race at the Summer Olympics: in 2004 and 2008. He set his personal best in the men's marathon (2:11:49) on April 15, 2002 in the Boston Marathon.
