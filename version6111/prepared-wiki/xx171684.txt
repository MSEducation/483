[[Mike Hancock (American football)]]

CATEGORIES: 1950 births, Living people, People from Tulare County, California, American football tight ends, Idaho State Bengals football players, Washington Redskins players

Carl Michael Hancock (born February 25, 1950 in Woodlake, California) is a former American football tight end in the National Football League for the Washington Redskins.  He played college football at Idaho State University and was drafted in the eighth round of the 1973 NFL Draft.
