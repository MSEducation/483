[[Abby Sargent]]

Abby Marie Sargent (née Teare; born 25 May 1977 in Melbourne, Australia) is a retired Australian netball player. Sargent was also a member of the England national team that played in the 2002 and 2006 Commonwealth Games.England Netball past players profile. Retrieved on 2009-03-29. She also played for the Melbourne Kestrels (1999–2000) and the Melbourne Phoenix (2007) in the Commonwealth Bank Trophy.[tpl]cite news |last=Pearce |first=Linda |title=Phoenix recruit's unstoppable rise |publisher=The Age |url=http://www.theage.com.au/news/sport/phoenix-recruits-unstoppable-rise/2007/04/21/1176697155476.html |date=2007-04-22 |accessdate=2009-03-29[/tpl] With the start of the ANZ Championship, Sargent played for the Melbourne Vixens in the 2008 season, after which she announced her retirement from netball.[tpl]cite web |title=Abby Sargent announces retirement |publisher=Melbourne Vixens |url=http://www.melbournevixens.com.au/newsitem.asp?id=7507&orgid=2422 |date=2008-08-29 |accessdate=2009-03-29[/tpl] She held an Australian Institute of Sport scholarship from 1997-1998.
