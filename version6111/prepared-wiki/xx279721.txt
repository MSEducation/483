[[Pigmentiphaga daeguensis]]

CATEGORIES: Burkholderiales

Pigmentiphaga daeguensis is a gram-negative, non-spore-forming, rod-shaped, non-motile bacterium from the genus Pigmentiphaga, which was isolated from wastewater of a dye works in Daegu in South Korea[tpl]cite doi|10.1099/ijs.0.64901-0[/tpl]EzBioCloud
