[[Orus Jones]]

CATEGORIES: American male golfers, Amateur golfers, Olympic golfers of the United States, Golfers at the 1904 Summer Olympics, Olympic medalists in golf, Olympic bronze medalists for the United States, Golfers from Ohio, People from Jackson County, Ohio, 1867 births, 1963 deaths

Orus W. Jones (March 1867 â€“ August 10, 1963) was an American golfer who competed in the 1904 Summer Olympics.
He was born in Jackson, Ohio. In 1904 he was part of the American team which won the bronze medal. He finished 25th in this competition. In the individual competition he finished 19th in the qualification and was eliminated in the first round of the match play.
