[[Major League Lacrosse Defensive Player of the Year Award]]

CATEGORIES: Major League Lacrosse awards

Warrior Major League Lacrosse Defensive Player of the Year Award is given annually to the individual judged to be, from amongst all MLL players, the best all-around defensively across a given season. Brodie Merrill has largely dominated this award, winning it a record six consecutive times.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oursportscentral.com/services/releases/?id=4281918|work=OurSportsCentral.com|title=Nationals Brodie Merrill Repeats as Warrior MLL Defensive Player of the Year|date=August 24, 2011|accessdate=2011-11-05[/tpl]
