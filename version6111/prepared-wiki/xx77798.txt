[[Cramer]]

CATEGORIES: Occupational surnames

Cramer [tpl]IPAc-en|ˈ|k|r|eɪ|m|ər[/tpl] is a surname. It is a variation of the German surname Krämer (pronounced [tpl]IPA-de|ˈkʁɛːmɐ|[/tpl]) which is an old term for the profession of traveling merchants in the Late Middle Ages. The meaning later changed to "merchants trading with different, rather small things".see http://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Krämer See also: Kramer (disambiguation).
