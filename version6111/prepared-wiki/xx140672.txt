[[Northridge (Metrolink station)]]

CATEGORIES: Metrolink stations in Los Angeles County, California, Northridge, Los Angeles, Public transportation in the San Fernando Valley

The Northridge Metrolink Station is a Metrolink rail station in the community of Northridge of the northern San Fernando Valley, in Los Angeles.
It is served by the Metrolink Ventura County Line from Union Station in Downtown Los Angeles  to Montalvoâ€”East Ventura (city of Ventura).  Twenty Metrolink trains serve the station each weekday.
The station has 290 parking spaces, 8 handicapped spaces, and 2 parking spaces which have electric vehicle charging stations. Parking is free and overnight parking is allowed.
