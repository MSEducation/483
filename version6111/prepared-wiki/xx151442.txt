[[I'll Always Love You (Taylor Dayne song)]]

CATEGORIES: 1988 singles, Taylor Dayne songs, Tito Nieves songs, Song recordings produced by Ric Wake

"I'll Always Love You" is a song by Taylor Dayne and is one of her best-known songs. The third single from her debut album Tell It to My Heart, the song was a romantic ballad, showcasing Dayne's softer side after the first two singles released were mainly high energy dance tracks.

==Charts==

This single gave Dayne her first adult contemporary hit and her first (and to date) only song to chart on the R&B chart.  It reached number three on the Billboard Hot 100 on September 24, 1988.

==Covers==

In 1997, Tito Nieves covered the song in salsa from his album, I Like It Like That.
