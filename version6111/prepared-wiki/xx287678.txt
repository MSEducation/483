[[M. R. Madhavan]]

CATEGORIES: Indian Institute of Management Calcutta alumni, Indian Institute of Technology Madras alumni, Indian businesspeople, Indian chief executives, Living people, 1968 births

M. R. Madhavan is the President and co-founder of PRS Legislative Research,[tpl]Cite web |url=http://www.prsindia.org/aboutus/team/ |title=Team |author= |accessdate=13 August 2013 |publisher=PRS Legislative Research |date=[/tpl][tpl]Cite news |url=http://www.outlookindia.com/article.aspx?236223 |title=Hammurabi's Reading List |author=Anjali Puri |accessdate=13 August 2013 |publisher=Outlook |date=Dec 10, 2007 |location=[/tpl] a public policy research institution that focuses on making the legislative process in India better informed, more transparent and participatory. PRS publishes "legislative briefs" (short commented summaries) of Bills in Lok Sabha and Rajya Sabha and other articles related to legislation in India.
Madhavan holds an undergraduate degree in engineering from IIT Madras and an MBA and PhD from IIM Calcutta. He started his career in ICICI Securities in the equity research group and later headed interest rates research at ICICI. He also worked for Bank of America as a Principal and Senior Strategist for the Asia region.[tpl]Cite web |url=http://info.shine.com/Career-Advice-Articles/Career-Building/3-success-stories-of-successful-career-transitions/5670/cid28.aspx |title=3 success stories of successful career transitions |author= |accessdate=13 August 2013 |publisher=shine.com |date=February 13, 2012[/tpl][tpl]Cite news |url=http://www.livemint.com/Leisure/ZlRKhG5truaOOKfSJBVecJ/To-the-nation-with-love.html |title=To the nation, with love |author=Sonya Dutta Choudhury |accessdate=13 August 2013 |publisher=Mint |date=Aug 11, 2013 |location=[/tpl]
Madhavan quit Bank of America in September 2005 and joined forces with C. V. Madhukar to open PRS Legislative Research. At PRS, Madhavan set up and led the Research team, before becoming the President of PRS.
