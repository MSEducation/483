[[Shinil High School]]

CATEGORIES: High schools in South Korea, Education in Seoul

[tpl]multiple issues|[tpl]Notability|Geography|date=December 2010[/tpl]|unreferenced=December 2010|orphan = January 2011[/tpl]

Shinil High School is a secondary school in Seoul,  South Korea. It was founded in 1966, and has been designated as an autonomous private school by the Ministry of Education since 2009.
