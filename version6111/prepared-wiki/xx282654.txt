[[William Freville]]

CATEGORIES: 1396 births, 1460 deaths, 15th-century English people, People from Cambridgeshire, Members of the Parliament of England (pre-1707)

William Freville (c.1396-1460), of Little Shelford, Cambridgeshire, was an English politician.
He was a Member (MP) of the Parliament of England for Cambridgeshire in May 1421.http://www.historyofparliamentonline.org/volume/1386-1421/member/freville-william-1396-1460
