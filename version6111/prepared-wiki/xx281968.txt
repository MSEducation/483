[[Jamaica at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics, 2013 in Jamaica, Athletics in Jamaica

Jamaica competed at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics in Moscow, Russia, from 10–18 August 2013.
A team of 45 athletes was announced to represent the country in the event.
