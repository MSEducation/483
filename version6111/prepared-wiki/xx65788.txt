[[Lytchett Minster]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Dorset

Lytchett Minster is a village in the English county of Dorset. It lies in the Purbeck local government district, about 4 miles northwest of Poole town centre. The village forms part of the civil parish of Lytchett Minster and Upton, Upton now being a suburb of Poole. The village is within the Mid Dorset and North Poole constituency of the House of Commons and the South West England constituency of the European Parliament.[tpl]cite book | title = OS Explorer Map OL15 - Purbeck & South Dorset | publisher = Ordnance Survey | year = 2006 | isbn = 978-0-319-23865-3 [/tpl]
Lytchett Minster is home to a number of manor houses, one of which now hosts the local Secondary School Lytchett Minster School.
