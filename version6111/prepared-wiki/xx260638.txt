Jonathan W. Rheault (born August 1, 1986) is an American professional ice hockey player who is currently playing for Adler Mannheim in the Deutsche Eishockey Liga (DEL). Rheault was born in Arlington, Texas, but grew up in Deering, New Hampshire.

==Playing career==

He was selected by the Philadelphia Flyers in the 5th round (145th overall) of the 2006 NHL Entry Draft. On June 16, 2010, he was signed as a free agent by the Abbotsford Heat.[tpl]cite book |last=National Hockey League|title=The National Hockey League Official Guide & Record Book/2011|year=2010 |publisher= Triumph Books |page= 327|isbn= 978-1-60078-422-4[/tpl] After two successful seasons with the Heat in the AHL, Rheault was signed to a one-year NHL contract with the Florida Panthers of the NHL on July 2, 2012.[tpl]citeweb| url = http://panthers.nhl.com/club/news.htm?id=636791 | title = Panthers agree to one-year, two way contract with Jon Rheault | publisher = Florida Panthers | date = 2012-07-02 | accessdate = 2012-07-02[/tpl]
