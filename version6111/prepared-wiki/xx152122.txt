[[Şilki]]

CATEGORIES: History of Tatarstan, Volga Bulgaria

Şilki (pronounced [tpl]IPA-tt|ʃilˈki|[/tpl]) or Shilki (Islamized as Abdallah; mid 9th - beginning of the 10th century) grandson of Tat-Ugek (descendant of Attila and his son Irnik, through Kubrat and his son Bat-Bayan Bezmer) was a Bolgar ruler (iltäbär). Shilki provided a policy of unification of Bolgar tribes. His son Almış was also a Bolgar ruler.
