[[Hylarana moluccana]]

CATEGORIES: Hylarana, Amphibians of Indonesia, Endemic fauna of Indonesia

Hylarana moluccana (Moluccas frog) is a species of true frog in the genus Hylarana.[tpl]cite web |url=http://research.amnh.org/vz/herpetology/amphibia/?action=names&taxon=Hylarana+moluccana|title= Hylarana moluccana (Boettger, 1895)|author=Frost, Darrel R. |date=2013 |work=Amphibian Species of the World 5.6, an Online Reference |publisher=American Museum of Natural History |accessdate=30 May 2013[/tpl] It is native to the Maluku Islands (=the Moluccas) of Indonesia. This presumably common species has been found in water-filled pits in a village.
