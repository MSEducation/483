[[Suryakaanti]]

CATEGORIES: Kannada-language films, 2010 films, Indian films, Film scores by Ilaiyaraaja

Suryakanthi is a 2010 Indian Kannada language movie directed by K.M. Chaitanya of Aa Dinagalu fame. It stars Chetan Kumar, Regina Cassandra, Nassar. The music is scored by  maestro Illayaraja.http://www.kannadaaudio.com/Songs/Moviewise/home/Suryakaanti.php[tpl]dead link|date=September 2011[/tpl]

==Plot==

The film is a story of an international assassin played by Chetan.
