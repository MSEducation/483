[[Finn Valley Post]]

CATEGORIES: Newspapers published in the Republic of Ireland, Media in County Donegal

The Finn Valley Post is a local newspaper in County Donegal, Ireland, based in the town of Ballybofey. The paper was first published on 18 July 2007 by the River Media group, which produces other local titles such as the Donegal Post and Letterkenny Post. The paper covers much of east and central County Donegal, including Ballybofey, Stranorlar, Raphoe and Lifford.
The publication's name comes from the River Finn which flows through the area.
