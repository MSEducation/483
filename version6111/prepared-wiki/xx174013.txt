[[What Do You Want?]]

CATEGORIES: 1959 singles, Parlophone singles, UK Singles Chart number-one singles, Bobby Vee songs, Songs written by Les Vandyke, 1959 songs

"What Do You Want?" was a 1959 song, which became a number one hit in the United Kingdom for Adam Faith. It was written by Les Vandyke and produced by John Burgess and arranged by John Barry.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.45cat.com/record/r4591 |title=Adam Faith - What Do You Want? / From Now Until Forever - Parlophone - UK - 45-R 4591 |publisher=45cat |date= |accessdate=2014-01-31[/tpl] It first appeared on the UK Singles Chart on 20 November 1959 and spent 19 weeks there. It was at number one for three weeks, sharing the position for the final week with "What Do You Want to Make Those Eyes at Me For?" by Emile Ford & The Checkmates. It is the shortest song to reach number one in the UK Singles Chart.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.everyhit.com/record7.html |title=Record-Breakers and Trivia |publisher=EveryHit.com |date= |accessdate=2014-01-31[/tpl]
"What Do You Want?" was covered by American singers Ersel Hickey in 1959 on Epic Records and Bobby Vee in 1960 on Liberty Records.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.45cat.com/record/f55234 |title=Bobby Vee - What Do You Want? / My Love Loves Me - Liberty - USA - F-55234 |publisher=45cat |date= |accessdate=2014-01-31[/tpl]
