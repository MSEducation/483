[[Coordinating Council of Political Parties and Public Organisations]]

CATEGORIES: Politics of Abkhazia

The Coordinating Council of Political Parties and Public Organisations unites the major opposition movements of Abkhazia.

==History==

The Council was founded on 10 July 2013 uniting the political parties United Abkhazia, the Forum for the National Unity of Abkhazia, the People's Party of Abkhazia and the Party for the Economic Development of Abkhazia, the public movements Akhatsa, Aruaa and Abaash, the Civil Union For the Rule of Law, Stability and Democracy and the Patriotic Movement Young Abkhazia.[tpl]cite news|title=Ряд политических партий и общественных движений Абхазии подписали Соглашение о сотрудничестве и совместную декларацию|url=http://apsnypress.info/news/9506.html|accessdate=29 July 2013|newspaper=Apsnypress|date=10 July 2013[/tpl] On 9 August, the Council was joined by two more movements, the Congress of Russian Communities of Russian Compatriots in Abkhazia, and the Coordinating Council of Russian Compatriots in Abkhazia.[tpl]cite news|title=В состав Координационного совета политических партий и общественных организаций вошли еще две организации|url=http://apsnypress.info/news/9709.html|accessdate=9 August 2013|newspaper=Apsnypress|date=6 August 2013[/tpl]
