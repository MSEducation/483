[[Fountain Lake Farm]]

CATEGORIES: National Historic Landmarks in Wisconsin, Houses in Wisconsin, Houses in Marquette County, Wisconsin, Houses completed in 1864, Visitor attractions in Marquette County, Wisconsin, Farms on the National Register of Historic Places in Wisconsin

The Fountain Lake Farm, also known as Wisconsin Farm Home of John Muir, was the home of John Muir from 1849 to 1856 and 1860 to 1862. The landmark site is located at County Highway F and Gillette Road in rural Montello, Wisconsin, United States, North America and includes an area of [tpl]convert|80|acre|ha[/tpl]. Muir attributed much of his philosophy about conservation to ideas he conceived while living at Fountain Lake Farm.
