[[Kürçülü]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Sharur District

Kürçülü (until 2003,Azerbaijan Parliament - renaming law Kürd Çullu, Kyurdchullu, Kyurdchulu, and Kyurtchulu) is a village and municipality in the Sharur District of Nakhchivan Autonomous Republic, Azerbaijan.  It has a population of 812.
