[[Asako Watanabe]]

CATEGORIES: 1969 births, Canoeists at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Japanese canoeists, Living people, Olympic canoeists of Japan, Asian Games medalists in canoeing, Canoeists at the 1994 Asian Games

[tpl]Nihongo|Asako Watanabe|渡辺 麻子|Watanabe Asako[/tpl] (born December 5, 1959) is a Japanese sprint canoer who competed in the mid-1990s. At the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta, she was eliminated in the repechages of the K-2 500 m event and the semifinals of the K-4 500 m event.
