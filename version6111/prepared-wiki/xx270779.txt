[[(16460) 1989 YF1]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1989, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(16460) 1989 YF1 is a main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on December 30, 1989.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
