[[Charles Hansen]]

CATEGORIES: 1891 births, Year of death missing, Danish male cyclists, Olympic cyclists of Denmark, Cyclists at the 1912 Summer Olympics, Sportspeople from Copenhagen

Charles Carl Hans Hansen (23 April 1891, date of death unknown) was a Danish road racing cyclist who competed in the 1912 Summer Olympics. He was born in Copenhagen.
In 1912 he was a member of the Danish cycling team which finished eighth in the team time trial event.
