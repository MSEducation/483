[[Ennio Foppiani]]

CATEGORIES: Psychodynamics, Psychology writers, 1959 births, Living people

Ennio Foppiani (born 13 May 1959) is an Italian writer, psychiatrist and psychotherapist and the founder and editor of the Radure ("Clearings") series of magazines, published in Italy by Moretti & Vitali.http://www.morettievitali.it/radure/
Foppiani is also the author of several publications and essays.
