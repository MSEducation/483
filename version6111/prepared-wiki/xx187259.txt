[[Chun Keng Hong]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 births, Living people, Malaysian Chinese people, Malaysian footballers, Malaysia international footballers, Kuala Muda Naza FC players, Penang FA players, Perak FA players

Chun Keng Hong (born on October 25, 1985 in Alor Setar, Kedah) is a Malaysian footballer who is currently playing as a defender for Indonesian Premier League club Bontang FC. He was a member of the Malaysian national team.

==Career==

Keng Hong played for Penang FA for the 2007/08 Malaysian League season.  He also have played for Sarawak, Suria NTFA, Kuala Muda NAZA, Perak and ATM FA.
For the 2012 season, he joined Thai Division 1 club Chanthaburi F.C..http://www.thaipremierleague.co.th/en/playerslist.php?clubID=29
He was part of Malaysia Under-23 squad for 2007 SEA Games. Keng Hong also was part of the Malaysian national senior team; he was selected for the friendly match against Indonesia but did not play the match.
