[[Erica Rose]]

CATEGORIES: 1982 births, People from Cleveland, Ohio, Living people, American female swimmers, American long-distance swimmers

Erica Rose (born July 6, 1982 in Cleveland, Ohio) is an American long-distance swimmer and former World Champion, a feat she achieved at age 15.
At the 1997 Pan Pacific Swimming Championships, she finished 3rd in the 1500-metre freestyle, but she didn't win a medal due to meet rule that one country can only win two medals per event. ISHOF list with all medalists in Pan Pacific Championships history
