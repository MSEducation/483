[[Terenozek]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Kyzylorda Province

 
Terenozek ([tpl]lang-kk|Тереңөзек[/tpl]) is an urban-type settlement and the administrative centre of Syrdariya District of the Kyzylorda Province of southern-central Kazakhstan. It lies on the northern bank of the Syr Darya. Population: [tpl]Kz-census2009|9,132|punct=;[/tpl] [tpl]Kz-census1999|9,408|punct=.[/tpl]
