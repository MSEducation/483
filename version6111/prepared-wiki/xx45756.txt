[[Aiseau-Presles]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Hainaut (province), Populated places in Belgium

Aiseau-Presles ([tpl]lang-wa|Åjhô-Préle[/tpl]) is a Walloon municipality located in the Belgian province of Hainaut. On January 1, 2006, Aiseau-Presles had a total population of 10,754. The total area is 22.19 km² which gives a population density of 485 inhabitants per km². It is located in the neighbourhood of Charleroi. Its postal area is: 6250.
Cities and communes bordering: Châtelet - Farciennes - Fosses-la-Ville - Gerpinnes - Sambreville.
