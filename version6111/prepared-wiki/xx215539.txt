[[Lubinowo, Gmina Płaska]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Augustów County

Lubinowo [tpl]IPAc-pl|l|u|b|i|'|n|o|w|o[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Płaska, within Augustów County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland, close to the border with Belarus.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) – TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|19|km|mi|0[/tpl] east of Płaska, [tpl]convert|36|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] east of Augustów, and [tpl]convert|84|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of the regional capital Białystok.
The village has a population of 20.
