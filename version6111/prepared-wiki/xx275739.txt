[[Elachista lugdunensis]]

CATEGORIES: Elachistidae, Animals described in 1859, Butterflies and moths of Europe, Moths of Europe, Insects of Germany, Insects of Sweden

Elachista lugdunensis is a moth of the Elachistidae family that can be found in Portugal, Spain, France, Germany, Austria and Sweden.
