[[Thomas Raffl]]

Thomas Raffl (born June 19, 1986) is an Austrian professional ice hockey winger who currently plays for EC Red Bull Salzburg of the Austrian Hockey League (EBEL). He previously played in the Swedish with Luleå HF of the Elitserien.Lulea HF's 2010-11 Roster He is the older brother of Michael Raffl.
