[[DBL MVP Under 23]]

CATEGORIES: Dutch Basketball League awards

The Dutch Basketball League Most Valuable Player Under Age 23 is an award that is yearly given to the best player under age 23 in the DBL, the highest professional basketball league in the Netherlands. The award is handed out after the regular season since 2004.MVP Under 23 award at j-dus.com (history of Dutch basketball) The award is handed out by the FEB (Federatie Eredivisie Basketbal).
