[[Lee Sang-yi]]

CATEGORIES: 1922 births, South Korean footballers, South Korea international footballers, Association football midfielders, 1954 FIFA World Cup players, Living people, Asian Games medalists in football, Footballers at the 1954 Asian Games

Lee Sang-Yi (Hangul: 이상의, born 1922) is a South Korean football midfielder who played for the South Korea in the 1954 FIFA World Cup.1954 FIFA World Cup Switzerland He also played for Seoul Army Club.
