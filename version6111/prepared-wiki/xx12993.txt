[[0s]]

CATEGORIES: 0s

The 0s is usually considered the first decade of the 1st century and the 1st millennium. However, this decade has only 9 years.  Note that there is no year zero (0) in either the proleptic Gregorian calendar or the Julian calendar. Hence, AD 1 was preceded by the year 1 BC.
