[[146268 Jennipolakis]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Discoveries by David Healy (astronomer), Asteroids named for people

146268 Jennipolakis is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1275.3675928 days (3.49 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=146268  |  title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-04-29 | publisher = NASA[/tpl]
