[[Nakajō, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Dissolved municipalities of Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Nakajō|中条村|Nakajō-mura[/tpl] was a village located in Kamiminochi District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the village had an estimated population of 2,742 and a population density of 82.37 persons per km². The total area was 33.29 km². This village lies about 20 km west of Nagano City along route 19. Its landscape is surrounded by dense forests and mountains.  
On January 1, 2010, Nakajō, along with the town of Shinshūshinmachi (also from Kamiminochi District), was merged into the expanded city of Nagano.[tpl]cite web|title=長野市・信州新町・中条村合併後調査報告について|url=http://www.city.nagano.nagano.jp/pcp_portal/PortalServlet?DISPLAY_ID=DIRECT&NEXT_DISPLAY_ID=U000004&CONTENTS_ID=13354|work=Nagano official website|publisher=Nagano City|accessdate=18 September 2011|language=Japanese[/tpl]
The village's landmark, Mt. Mushikura (虫倉山), is the centerpiece of local lore.
