Mohammed Diallo (born 22 May 1983 in Belley, France) is a Côte d'Ivoire football player, who last played for French club FC Nantes.

==Career==

Diallo started his European career at Beveren, where he played with Ivorian like Emmanuel Eboué, Boubacar Barry and Yaya Touré.
In summer 2006, likes many youth products, Diallo was sold to FC Sion, signed a three year contract,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.uefa.com/footballeurope/news/kind=2/newsid=431663.html|title=Various title|publisher=UEFA.com|date=2006-06-22[/tpl] he was loaned to Maccabi Petah Tikva and FC Chiasso. In July 2008 left Sion and signed a contract with FC Champagne Sports and signed just one year later with Ajax Cape Town on 7 August 2009.Ferreira told to 'get his act together'
