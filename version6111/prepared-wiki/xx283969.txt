[[Manhunt International 1997]]

CATEGORIES: Manhunt International

The 1997 Manhunt International World Final was held in Singapore on May 24, 1997. About 38 participants compete in this edition. Jason Erceg from New Zealand was crowned as the winner of the pageant.
