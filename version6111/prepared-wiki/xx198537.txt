[[Blankenese station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Hamburg, Hamburg S-Bahn stations, Railway stations opened in 1867, Heritage sites in Hamburg, Buildings and structures in Altona, Hamburg

Blankenese is a railway station on the Altona-Blankenese line, part of the Hamburg S-Bahn in Germany, located in the suburb of Blankenese in the borough of Altona, Hamburg. It opened in 1867, and is a heritage site.[tpl]Cite web | title =Bahnhof unter Denkmalschutz - Der Blankeneser Bahnhof ist als "bedeutendes Zeugnis der Verkehrs- und Ortsgeschichte Altonas und Blankeneses" unter Denkmalschutz gestellt worden. | publisher =Hamburger Abendblatt | date =20 October 2008 | url =http://www.abendblatt.de/hamburg/article569965/Bahnhof-unter-Denkmalschutz.html | accessdate =13 January 2011 [/tpl][tpl]Cite web | title =S-Bahnhof Blankenese | work =Blankenese Bahnhofsplatz | publisher =Prelios Asset Management Deutschland GmbH and ING Real Estate Germany GmbH | url =http://www.blankenese-bahnhofsplatz.de/Vermietung_DGAG_Blankenese_Bahnhofsplatz/Bahnhof_Blankenese_Bahnhofsplatz.html | accessdate =13 January 2011 [/tpl]
It is a terminus, trains of the city train line S11 end here and the other line S1 change their direction.
