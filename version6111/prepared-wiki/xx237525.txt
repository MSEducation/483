[[Jānis Paukštello]]

CATEGORIES: 1951 births, Living people, Latvian male stage actors, Latvian male film actors

Jānis Paukštello (born 26 March 1951) is a Latvian actor. In the theater, since the 1970s he has worked for Dailes teātris. He has also took part in several films.
