[[Andrzej Rewilak]]

CATEGORIES: 1942 births, Living people, Polish footballers, Poland international footballers, Sportspeople from Kraków

Andrzej Rewilak (born 4 September 1942) is a retired Polish international footballer. He played for Cracovia from 1960–1971.[tpl]cite web|publisher=90 Minut|title=Andrzej Rewilak|url=http://www.90minut.pl/kariera.php?id=8400|language=Polish|accessdate=2010-02-23[/tpl]
He was capped for Poland only once. This was in the 1960s where he played against England in a 1-1 draw.
