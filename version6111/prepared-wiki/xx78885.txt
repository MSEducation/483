[[Airy (Martian crater)]]

CATEGORIES: Impact craters on Mars

Airy is an impact crater on Mars, named in honor of the British Astronomer, Royal Sir George Biddell Airy (1801–1892). The crater is approximately [tpl]convert|40|km[/tpl] in diameter and is located at 0.1°E 5.1°S in the Meridiani Planum region. The much smaller crater Airy-0, which defines the location of Mars' prime meridian, lies within it.PIA03207: The Martian Prime Meridian -- Longitude "Zero"
