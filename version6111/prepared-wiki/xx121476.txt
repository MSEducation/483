[[Mouriki]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Kozani (regional unit), Former municipalities in West Macedonia

Mouriki ([tpl]lang-el|Μουρίκι[/tpl]) is a former municipality in Kozani regional unit, West Macedonia, Greece. Since the 2011 local government reform it is part of the municipality Eordaia, of which it is a municipal unit.Kallikratis law Greece Ministry of Interior [tpl]el icon[/tpl] The population in 2001 was 4,896. The seat of the municipality was in Emporio.
