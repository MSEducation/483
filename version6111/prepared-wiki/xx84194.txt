[[Yamanouchi, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Yamanouchi|山ノ内町|Yamanouchi-machi[/tpl] is a town located in Shimotakai District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the town has an estimated population of 15,326 and a density of 57.63 persons per km². The total area is 265.93 km².
The Shiga Kōgen ski resort is located in Yamanouchi.
