[[King Water]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of Cumbria

King Water is a river in the north of Cumbria, England. The Brampton Angling Association has a long term let from the Earl of Carlisle for fishing rights on a portion of the River Irthing and part of the King Water.1891 River Agreement between the Earl of Carlisle and the Bramption Angling Association  Hadrian's Wall crossed the King Water to the east of the village of Walton
