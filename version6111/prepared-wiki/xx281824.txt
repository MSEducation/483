[[Manhunt International 1995]]

CATEGORIES: Manhunt International

Manhunt International 1995 was the 3rd edition of the international male pageant. The final was held in Singapore. About 35 countries were represented in the pageant. Nikos Papadakis of Greece crowned Albe Geldenhuys of South Africa at the end of the event.
