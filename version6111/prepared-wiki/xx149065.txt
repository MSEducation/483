[[Niclas Rasck]]

Niclas Rasck (born 10 March 1969) is a retired Swedish football defender who last represented Djurgårdens IF. He was capped 152 times for Djurgården.[tpl]cite book |last=Gänger |first=Hasse |title=Djurgårdens IF Fotboll 1899-2006 |edition= |page=163 |publisher=Djurgårdens IF Fotboll |location=Stockholm |language=Swedish |isbn=978-91-633-0992-2 [/tpl]
Prior to joining the Stockholm club, he has played for Örebro SK and Ludvika FK,[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.dif.se/sidor/nyheter/nyheter.asp?action=view&id=1575&typ=2 |title=Rasck siktar mot toppen |author=Johanna Lundeberg |date=2002-03-19 |work=dif.se |publisher=Djurgårdens IF Fotboll |language=Swedish |accessdate=2010-01-23[/tpl] where his career began.
