[[Gennady Mikhaylov]]

CATEGORIES: Russian male cyclists, 1974 births, Living people, People from Cheboksary

Gennady Mikhaylov ([tpl]lang-ru|Геннадий Михайлов[/tpl], born 8 February 1974 in Cheboksary, Chuvash ASSR), is a former professional road bicycle racer from Russia. He last rode for UCI ProTour [tpl]ct|KAT|2009[/tpl]. He was part of the successful team [tpl]ct|SIL|2001[/tpl], in which he assisted Robbie McEwen to numerous sprint victories. He won his first professional race in 2002. Before joining [tpl]ct|AST|2007[/tpl] for the 2007 season, he spent four years with [tpl]ct|DSC|2003[/tpl], from 2003 until 2006.
