[[Those Sessions]]

CATEGORIES: 1997 EPs, Peel Sessions recordings, The Olivia Tremor Control albums, Live EPs, 1997 live albums

"Those Sessions" is a 1997 EP by The Olivia Tremor Control. The album is also known as Peel Sessions for BBC radio jock, John Peel, who worked on it with The Olivia Tremor Control.
