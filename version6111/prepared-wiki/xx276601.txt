[[Arecleoch Wind Farm]]

CATEGORIES: Wind farms in Scotland

Arecleoch Wind Farm is a 60 turbine wind farm in South Ayrshire, Scotland with a total capacity of 120 megawatts (MW), enough to power over 67,000 homes.UK Operational Wind Farms – Renewable UK Accessed 27 September 2011 Construction started in 2009 and it was commissioned in June 2011.Arecleoch Wind Farm – Renewables Map Accessed 27 September 2011
