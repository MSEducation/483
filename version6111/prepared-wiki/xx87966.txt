[[Thornbury railway station, Melbourne]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1889

Thornbury railway station is located on the South Morang line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the north-eastern Melbourne suburb of Thornbury opening on 8 October 1889.Thornbury Vicsig
