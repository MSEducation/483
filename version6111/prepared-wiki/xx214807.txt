[[Dwight Ferguson]]

CATEGORIES: Bahamian sprinters, Athletes (track and field) at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Olympic athletes of the Bahamas, 1970 births, Living people

Dwight K. Ferguson (born May 16, 1970) is a sprint athlete from the Bahamas. He won the silver medal in the men's 100 metres at the 1998 Central American and Caribbean Games, and competed professionally for his native country at the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta, Georgia, and the Senior Pan Am Games.
