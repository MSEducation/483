[[Harqin Left Wing Mongol Autonomous County]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Liaoning, Chaoyang, Liaoning

Harqin Left Wing Mongolian Autonomous County ([tpl]zh|s=喀喇沁左翼蒙古族自治县 |t=喀喇沁左翼蒙古族自治縣 |p=Kèlǎqìn Zuǒyì Měnggǔzú Zìzhìxiàn[/tpl]) is a county in the west of Liaoning province, China.
