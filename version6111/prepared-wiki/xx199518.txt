[[Erick Mombaerts]]

CATEGORIES: 1955 births, Living people, French footballers, RC Lens players, French football managers, Paris Saint-Germain F.C. managers, EA Guingamp managers, Toulouse FC managers, Ligue 1 managers

Erick Mombaerts (born 21 April 1955 in Chantecoq) is a French football coach and retired player. He has been involved with the youth sections of the French national side.

==Career==

===Playing career===

As a player, Mombaerts played for Montargis, Nemours, INF Vichy, RC Lens, US Noeux-lès-Mines and ÉDS Montluçon.

===Coaching career===

Mombaerts began his coaching career with the reserve side of Paris Saint-Germain, coaching the senior side briefly in 1988, before leaving in 1989 to manage Guingamp, AS Cannes and Toulouse FC.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.rsssf.com/players/trainers-fran-clubs.html|title=France - Trainers of First and Second Division Clubs|accessdate=2008-05-21|publisher=RSSSF[/tpl] He left Toulouse in 2006 to manage the French under-18 youth side. He became head coach of the French under-21 side in August 2008.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.uefa.com/competitions/under21/news/kind=1/newsid=689947.html|title=France U21s turn to Mombaerts|accessdate=2008-05-21|date=2008-04-29|publisher=UEFA |archiveurl = http://web.archive.org/web/20080503095959/http://www.uefa.com/competitions/under21/news/kind=1/newsid=689947.html  |archivedate = 2008-05-03[/tpl]
On 18 October 2012, Mombaerts was sacked as France coach after his team lost in the play-offs for next summer's UEFA European Under-21 Championship.http://uk.uefa.com/under21/news/newsid=1878315.html
Mombaerts became manager at Le Havre 22 December 2012.
