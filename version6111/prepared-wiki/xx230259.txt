[[Dōtoku Station]]

CATEGORIES: Stations of Nagoya Railroad

[tpl]nihongo|Dōtoku Station|道徳駅|Dōtoku-eki[/tpl] is a railway station operated by Meitetsu's  Tokoname Line located in Minami Ward, Nagoya, Aichi Prefecture,  Japan. It is located 2.4 rail kilometers from the terminus of the line at Jingū-mae Station. [tpl]cite web |url=http://www.meitetsu.co.jp/rosenzu/line06/eki/1173711_1178.asp|title=道徳|trans_title=Dotoku|publisher=Nagoya Railroad |language=Japanese |archiveurl= |archivedate= |accessdate=26 November 2010[/tpl]

==History==

Dōtoku Station was opened on February 18, 1912 as a station on the Aichi Electric Railway Company. The Aichi Electric Railway became part of the Meitetsu group on August 1, 1935. The station was closed in 1944, and reopened in October 1949. From 1983-1984, the tracks were elevated. On January 15, 2005, the Tranpass system of magnetic fare cards with automatic turnstiles was implemented.
