[[Isobutyl formate]]

CATEGORIES: Formate esters

Isobutyl formate (2-methylpropyl methanoate) is an organic ester with the chemical formula C5H10O2. It is formed by diluting isobutanol with formic acid; sulfuric acid can serve as a catalyst. It is used as a flavor and fragrance ingredient because of its odor which is sweet, ethereal, and slightly fruity.Isobutyl formate, The Good Scents Company[tpl]cite journal | author = Mosciano, Gerard | journal = Perfumer & Flavorist | volume = 14 | issue = 6 | pages = 47 | year = 1989[/tpl]
