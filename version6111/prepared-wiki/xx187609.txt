[[List of Hong Kong football champions]]

CATEGORIES: National association football champions, Hong Kong First Division League

The Hong Kong football champions are the winners of the Hong Kong First Division League.
South China is the most successful club in Hong Kong. The club has won 40 league titles until 2009–10 season. A dissolved club Seiko is the record holders of winning longest consecutive titles. In 1979–1985, they had won 7 league titles.
Former South China player Ho Cheung Yau scored 40 league goals in 1957–58 season, is the record holders of the goal number of top scorers.
