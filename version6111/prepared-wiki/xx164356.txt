Julio Cesar DePaula (born December 31, 1982 in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic) is a former Major League Baseball relief pitcher. He last played for the York Revolution of the Atlantic League of Professional Baseball.
He appeared in 16 games for the Minnesota Twins in the [tpl]mlby|2007[/tpl] season, pitching 20 innings total. He became a free agent at the end of the [tpl]by|2008[/tpl] season and signed a minor league contract with the Tampa Bay Rays on Jan. 6, [tpl]by|2009[/tpl].
He signed with Hanwha Eagles in South Korea on Dec. 10, [tpl]by|2009[/tpl]. He appeared in 41 games for Eagles that season. He was released from Eagles on June 4, [tpl]by|2011[/tpl], due to poor results.
