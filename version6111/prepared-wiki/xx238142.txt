[[Liz Wardley]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 births, Living people, Papua New Guinean yacht racers, People from Finistère

 Liz Wardley  is a Papua New Guinean skipper, born in 1980.PNG girl loves sailing

==Biography==

She lives in La Forêt-Fouesnant in Finistère, and has also lived in Sydney, Australia.

==Honours==

She was named the Papua New Guinean Sportswomen of the Year in 1999 and 2000.
