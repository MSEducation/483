Matt Cohen (born November 8, 1985) is an American professional ice hockey defenceman who is  currently playing for Fife Flyers. He most recently played for the Hamburg Freezers of the Deutsche Eishockey Liga, posting 5 assists in 40 games during his one season with the Freezers in 2010–11.[tpl]cite web| url = http://www.hamburg-freezers.de/index.php?link=http://www.hamburg-freezers.de/html/news_05090e5a-efb7-47ef-afb8-78aa608f73df.html | title = Personnel decisions continue, Traverse re-signed | publisher = Hamburg Freezers | date = March 20, 2011 | accessdate = March 29, 2011 | language = German[/tpl]
