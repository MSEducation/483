[[Le Monde Person of the Year]]

CATEGORIES: Awards

In 2009 the French newspaper Le Monde initiated a Person of the Year designation for the man or woman of that year who had affected the world most significantly, either positively or negatively.Bloomberg newswire, 24 December 2009  To date only two men have been selected, who have been referred to Man of Year for their respective years (2009 and 2010).
