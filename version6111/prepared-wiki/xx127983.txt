[[MSR Customs]]

CATEGORIES: Software companies based in Toronto, Companies based in Toronto

MSR Customs is a provider of customs duty recovery and customs brokerage services in Canada and the United States. The company is based in Toronto, Canada. MSR Customs is C-TPAT and Partners in Protection (PIP) Certified by the U.S. and Canadian governments respectively. It is also a member of customs-related organizations including the Canadian Society of Customs Brokers (CSCB), American Association of Exporters and Importers (AAEI), I.E. Canada, and others.
