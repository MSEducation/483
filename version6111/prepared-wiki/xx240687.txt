[[KOOK (FM)]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Texas

KOOK (93.5 FM) is a country music radio station licensed to Junction, Texas, USA.  The station is currently owned by Foster Charitable Foundation.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KOOK |title=KOOK Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division |accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl]

==History==

The station was assigned the call letters KAHO on April 7, 1994. On March 26, 1997, the station changed its call sign to KAHO, on September 8, 1998 to the current KOOK,[tpl]cite web |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=57035&Callsign=KOOK |title=KOOK Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division |accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl] On June 12, 2007 the station was sold to Foster Charitable Foundation.[tpl]cite web|url= |title=FCC Application  |publisher=Federal Communications Commission |accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl]
