[[Art Pepper Today]]

CATEGORIES: 1978 albums, Art Pepper albums, Galaxy Records albums

Art Pepper Today is a 1978 jazz album by saxophonist Art Pepper playing with Stanley Cowell, Cecil McBee, Roy Haynes and Kenneth Nash.
