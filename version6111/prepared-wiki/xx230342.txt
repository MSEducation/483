Finding Rin Tin Tin is a 2007 Bulgarian–American drama film directed by Israeli filmmaker Danny Lerner.

==Plot==

The film tells the story of the original Rin Tin Tin, the legendary German Shepherd, found shortly before the end of World War I by American serviceman Lee Duncan as a shell-shocked puppy in a bombed-out dog kennel in Lorraine, France. The dog was taken to America and became the hero of several films made in the 1920s and 1930s.[tpl]cite web |title=Finding Rin Tin Tin |url=http://www.hometheaterinfo.com/finding_rin_tin_tin.htm |publisher=Hometheaterinfo.com |format=DVD review |date=August 17, 2008 |accessdate=March 4, 2011[/tpl]
