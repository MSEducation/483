[[Saul Dibb]]

CATEGORIES: 1968 births, Living people, Alumni of the University of East Anglia, English film directors, English screenwriters

Saul Dibb (b. 1968BBC - Movies - interview - Saul Dibb) is an English director and screenwriter, best known for co-writing and directing Bullet Boy, for which he was nominated for the Douglas Hickox Award,Saul Dibb - BIFA The Line of Beauty, and The Duchess. He is a graduate of the University of East Anglia. His father is the documentary maker Mike Dibb.
