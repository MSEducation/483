[[Humay District]]

Humay District is one of eight districts of the province Pisco in Peru.[tpl]es icon[/tpl] Instituto Nacional de Estadística e Informática. Banco de Información Distrital. Retrieved April 11, 2008.
The Lily Mine, about [tpl]convert|40|km|abbr=on[/tpl] east of Pisco, Peru, is a well-known producer of mineral specimens for collectors, and rough material for decorative stone-carving.
