[[Rural Municipality of Park]]

CATEGORIES: Rural municipalities in Manitoba

Park is a rural municipality in the province of Manitoba in Western Canada. Until 2006, the Rural Municipality of Park consisted of two disjoint parts, called Park (South) and Park (North). On January 1, 2007, Park (North) was transferred to the Rural Municipality of Shell River.[tpl]Cite web |url=http://www.amm.mb.ca/PDF/Tools%20for%20Change/Shell%20River%20Park%20North.doc |title=Working Regionally With New Partners: Annexation. Rural Municipality of Shell River/Rural Municipality of Park North |accessdate=2 January 2009 |work= |publisher=Association of Manitoba Municipalities |date=[/tpl] The surviving part, Park (South), is now simply called Park. It is located adjacent to the southern part of Riding Mountain National Park, hence its name.
