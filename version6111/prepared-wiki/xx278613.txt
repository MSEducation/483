[[Owrta Bolagh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Owrta Bolagh ([tpl]lang-fa|اورتا بلاغ[/tpl], also Romanized as ' Owrtā Bolāgh and Ūrtā Bolāgh; also known as Arooteh Bolagh, Owrtah Bolāgh, Owrteh Bolāgh,  Urtahbulāq, and Urtakh-Bulag)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3077536|Owrta Bolagh[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 246, in 61 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
