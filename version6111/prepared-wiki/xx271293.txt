[[Andi Knoll]]

CATEGORIES: 1972 births, Living people, Austrian radio personalities, Austrian television presenters, Austria in the Eurovision Song Contest, People from Innsbruck

Andreas (Andi) Knoll (born 11 June 1972 in Innsbruck) is an Austrian radio and television presenter.
Since the 1999 Contest, Knoll following Ernst Grissemann has been the Austrian commentator for the Eurovision Song Contest and since 2002 Knoll has hosted the Austrian Heat for the Eurovision. Knoll has also commentated for Austria at the Eurovision Dance Contest since the 2007 Contest.
