[[Mykyta Kamenyuka]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 births, Living people, Ukrainian footballers, FC Illichivets Mariupol players, FC Zorya Luhansk players, Ukrainian Premier League players, People from Luhansk

Mykyta Kamenyuka ([tpl]lang-uk|Микита Каменюка[/tpl]; born 3 June 1985, Ukraine) is a professional Ukrainian football midfielder who plays for Zorya in the Ukrainian Premier League. He moved from Illichivets to Zorya during the 2008–09 summer transfer season.
