[[Chanayethazan Township]]

CATEGORIES: Townships of Mandalay, Townships of Mandalay Region, Mandalay

Chanayethazan Township ([tpl]lang-my|ချမ်းအေးသာဇံ မြို့နယ်[/tpl], [tpl]IPA-my|tɕʰáɴ ʔé θà zàɴ mjo̰nɛ̀|pron[/tpl]) is a township located in downtown Mandalay, Myanmar. The township is bounded by Aungmyethazan township and the Mandalay Palace in the north, and Patheingyi township in the east, Mahaaungmye township in the south, and the Ayeyarwady river in the west. Chanayethazan is the main business district of the city. It is home to the city's biggest shopping center, the Zegyo Market and most international standard hotels.[tpl]Cite web| url=http://www.dpsmap.com/mandalay | title=Map of Mandalay | publisher=DPS | accessdate=2009-04-04[/tpl]
