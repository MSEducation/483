[[Baljevac]]

CATEGORIES: Suburbs of Belgrade

Baljevac (Serbian Cyrillic: Баљевац) is a village in Belgrade region, in the municipality of Obrenovac. According to the 2002 census, Baljevac had 532 inhabitants.
