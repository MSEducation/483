[[Archie Alemania]]

CATEGORIES: Filipino male actors, Filipino comedians, Filipino male television actors, 1978 births, Living people, Star Magic

Archie Alemania (born 1978) is a Filipino actor, host, comedian and dancer.

==Early life==

Alemania has appeared in several television commercials such as Chippy, San Miguel, Globe and Pop Cola. He is a talent of ABS-CBN and a member of Big Brother's Dance Group, Big Men Dance Group, and the Big Men Singers. At present, Archie is into sitcom, honing his talents as an actor.
Alemania has also been a host from 2004-2007 for Studio23's weekly info-tainment show and gag showWazzup Wazzup with fellow hosts Toni Gonzaga and Vhong Navarro
Alemania is former QTV 11 mainstay for his defunct gag-show, "I Laugh Sabado".
