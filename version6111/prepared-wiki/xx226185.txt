[[Yukiko Fujisawa]]

[tpl]Nihongo|Yukiko Fujisawa|藤澤 亮子|Fujisawa Yukiko|extra=born April 14, 1995 in Chiba[/tpl] is a Japanese figure skater. She is the 2009 Junior Grand Prix Final silver medalist and the 2010 Japanese junior bronze medalist.
