[[Mount Hopkins (California)]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of Fresno County, California, Mountains of the John Muir Wilderness

Mount Hopkins is in the John Muir Wilderness north of Kings Canyon National Park. It is one of four peaks that surround Pioneer Basin, [tpl]convert|1.4|mi[/tpl]  south-southeast of Mount Crocker, [tpl]convert|2|mi[/tpl] west-southwest of Mount Huntington and [tpl]convert|2|mi[/tpl] southwest of Mount Stanford.  The mountain was named for Mark Hopkins, one of the builders of the Central Pacific Railroad.[tpl]cite Farquhar |index=H |accessdate=2009-08-11[/tpl]
