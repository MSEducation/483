[[Hildegard Werner]]

CATEGORIES: 1834 births, Swedish conductors (music), Swedish journalists, Women conductors (music), 19th-century conductors (music), Women of the Victorian era, English people of Swedish descent, 1911 deaths

Hildegard Charlotta Aurora Werner (1834–1911) was a Swedish musician, musical conductor and a journalist active in Great Britain. 
Werner was born in Stockholm and was a student at Royal College of Music, Stockholm in 1856. In 1871 she became the principal of a music school and a conductor in music in Newcastle-upon-Tyne. she was a journalist for several British papers from 1880, and also a correspondent for some Swedish papers.  
She was an associée of the Royal Swedish Academy of Music in 1895.
