[[Belarusians]]

CATEGORIES: Belarusian people, Ethnic groups in Kazakhstan, Ethnic groups in Europe, Slavic ethnic groups, Rus

Belarusians ([tpl]lang-be|беларусы[/tpl], belarusy) are an East Slavic ethnic group who populate the majority of the Republic of Belarus. There are over 8 million people who associate themselves with the Belarusian nationality today.
