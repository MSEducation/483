[[Izhak Graziani]]

CATEGORIES: 1924 births, 2003 deaths, Israeli conductors (music), Israeli musicians, People from Ruse, Bulgaria, Eurovision Song Contest conductors, Bulgarian people of Jewish descent, Israeli people of Bulgarian descent

Izhak "Ziko" Graziani ([tpl]Lang-he|יצחק גרציאני[/tpl]; August 4, 1924 in Ruse – July 7, 2003) was born in Bulgaria and studied music and conducting.
In 1948 he moved to Israel where he became the conductor of the IDF Orchestra. He eventually became conductor of the IBA Radio Orchestra (eventually renamed IBA Radio and TV Orchestra).
