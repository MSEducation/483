[[Rožengrunt]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Municipality of Sveta Ana

Rožengrunt ([tpl]IPA-sl|ˈɾoːʒənɡɾunt|pron[/tpl]) is a dispersed settlement in the Municipality of Sveta Ana in the Slovene Hills in northeastern Slovenia.Sveta Ana municipal site
There is a large chapel in the settlement. It is dedicated to The Visitation of the Virgin Mary. It has a sanctuary with a two storey belfry and dates to the early 20th century.Slovenian Ministry of Culture register of national heritage reference number 2848
