[[Armaki]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Bandar Lengeh County

Armaki ([tpl]lang-fa|ارمكي[/tpl], also Romanized as Armakī)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3053672|Armaki[/tpl] is a village in Moqam Rural District, Shibkaveh District, Bandar Lengeh County, Hormozgan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 68, in 11 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|22[/tpl]
