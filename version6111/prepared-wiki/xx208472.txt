[[Patrick McGinley]]

CATEGORIES: 1937 births, Living people, Irish novelists

Patrick McGinley (born 1977) is an Irish novelist, born in Glencolumbkille, Ireland.
After teaching in Ireland, McGinley moved to England where he pursued a career as a publisher and author.  Among his strongest literary influences is his Irish predecessor, author Flann O'Brien, who McGinley emulates most noticeably in his novel The Devil's Diary.Shea, Thomas F. "Patrick McGinley's Impressions of Flann O'Brien: The Devil's Diary and At Swim-Two-Birds."  Twentieth Century Literature, Vol. 40, No. 2 (Summer, 1994), pp. 272-281.
