[[Alexis Sarei]]

CATEGORIES: 1934 births, Papua New Guinean politicians, People from the Autonomous Region of Bougainville, Living people, High Commissioners of Papua New Guinea to the United Kingdom, Heads of state in Oceania

Alexis Holyweek Sarei CBE (b. 25 March 1934) is a Papua New Guinean politician from Bougainville. He governed North Solomons Province (Bougainville) as district commissioner from 1973 to 1975, as president of the secessionist Republic of the North Solomons that existed from 1975 to 1976,[tpl]cite book|last1=Howe|first1=K. R.|last2=Kiste|first2=Robert C.|last3=Lal|first3=Brij V.|title=Tides of history: the Pacific Islands in the twentieth century|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=P-ybBDLbKJIC&pg=PA217|accessdate=22 March 2011|year=1994|publisher=University of Hawaii Press|isbn=978-0-8248-1597-4|page=217[/tpl] and as premier twice, from 1976 to 1980 and again from 1984 to 1987. Between premierships, he also served as Papua New Guinean High Commissioner to the United Kingdom.
