Pavel Kováč (born 12 August 1974) is a Slovak goalkeeper who currently plays for Slovak club Ružomberok. He is 2.02 m tall and weighs 96 kg. His international debut came in the second half of the Slovakia vs. Hungary match on 6 February 2008.
Kováč previously played for FC Slovácko in the Gambrinus liga, Apollon Kalamarias F.C. in the Greek Super League and DAC Dunajská Streda.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Guardian.co.uk|title=Stats Centre: Pavel Kováč Facts|url=http://guardian.touch-line.com/StatsCentre.asp?CTID=31&CPID=76&TEID=3460&PLID=183316&pStr=Player|accessdate=2009-10-23[/tpl]
