[[2007 AF2 season]]

CATEGORIES: AF2 seasons, 2007 Arena Football League season

The 2007 AF2 season was the eighth season of the AF2.  The regular season began on Friday, March 30 and ended on July 28.  The league champions were the Tulsa Talons, who defeated the Wilkes-Barre/Scranton Pioneers in ArenaCup VIII.
