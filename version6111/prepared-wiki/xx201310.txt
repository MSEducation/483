[[Hans Leitert]]

CATEGORIES: 1973 births, Living people, Austrian footballers, Sportspeople from Vienna

Hans Leitert (born 15 January 1973) is an Austrian goalkeeping coach. He has coached at VfB Mödling, SK Rapid Vienna, FK Austria Vienna, the Austria national under-21 team, Panathinaikos and Tottenham Hotspur.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.hansleitert.com/en/personal_facts.php|title=Hans Leitert personal facts|publisher=Hans Leitert - The Art of Goalkeeping|accessdate=2008-05-22[/tpl] He joined Tottenham in May 2008 from Recreativo de Huelva, replacing Hans Segers, who left the club in October 2007 following Juande Ramos's appointment as manager.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.thisislondon.co.uk/sport/article-23486451-details/One+safe+Hans+replaces+another+as+Spurs+appoint+new+goalkeeping+coach/article.do|title=One safe Hans replaces another as Spurs appoint new goalkeeping coach|date=2008-05-21|publisher=Evening Standard|accessdate=2008-05-22[/tpl] He left when Ramos himself was sacked in November 2008.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.tottenhamhotspur.com/news/articles/clubannouncement181108.html |title=Club announcement |accessdate=18 November 2008 |publisher=Tottenham Hotspur FC |date=2008-11-18[/tpl] 
In July 2010 Hans Leitert takes over his new position as head of goalkeeping development at FC Red Bull for all Red Bull’s football clubs (Salzburg/Austria, New York/USA, Leipzig/Germany) and the academies in Brazil and Ghana.
Leitert was born in Vienna. He was formerly a goalkeeper for the Austrian under-21 and under-18 teams, before having to retire early due to a wrist injury.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.tottenhamhotspur.com/news/articles/coachingappointment210508.html|title=Coaching appointment|publisher=Tottenham Hotspur|date=2008-05-21|accessdate=2008-05-22[/tpl] [tpl]Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot[/tpl] He holds a Master's degree in Sports Science and speaks at goalkeeping lectures on behalf of UEFA and FIFA.
