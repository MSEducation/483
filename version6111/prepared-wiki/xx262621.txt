[[Swiss-Belhotel International]]

CATEGORIES: Hotel chains

Swiss-Belhotel International is a hotel management chain. It was founded since 1987 by Mr. Peter Gautschi. It entered the European market in 1997 and today it has 40 operating hotels and several hotels planned to open in 2013.[tpl]Citation needed|date=January 2011[/tpl]
