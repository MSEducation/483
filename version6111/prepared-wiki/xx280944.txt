[[Qinarjeh, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Qinarjeh ([tpl]lang-fa|قينرجه[/tpl], also Romanized as Qīnarjeh; also known as Qīnarcheh and Qīzjeh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3766375|Qinarjeh[/tpl] is a village in Mojezat Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 439, in 100 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
