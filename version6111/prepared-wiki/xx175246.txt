[[WOAK]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Georgia (U.S. state)

WOAK is a Christian radio station licensed to LaGrange, Georgia, broadcasting on 90.9 MHz FM.  The station is owned by Oakside Christian School. WOAK fcc.gov. Accessed August 21, 2012
WOAK's programming includes; Thru the Bible with J. Vernon McGee, Back to the Bible, In Touch with Charles Stanley, The Family Altar Program with Lester Roloff, Love Worth Finding with Adrian Rogers, Turning Point with David Jeremiah, Truths that Transform with D. James Kennedy, and Adventures in Odyssey. WOAK Program Schedule WOAK Accessed August 21, 2012
