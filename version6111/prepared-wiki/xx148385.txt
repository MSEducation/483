[[Torunn Janbu]]

CATEGORIES: 1954 births, Living people, Norwegian physicians, Norwegian women physicians, University of Oslo alumni, Date of birth missing (living people), Place of birth missing (living people)

Torunn Janbu (born 26 November 1954) is a Norwegian physician. The president of the Norwegian Medical Association, she became the first female president in the history of the organization upon election in 2005.[tpl]cite book|title=Hvem er hvem?|last=Kvalshaug|first=Vidar|year=2008|editor=Knut Olav Åmås|publisher=Aschehoug|location=Oslo|isbn=978-82-03-23561-0|pages=272–73[/tpl] She attained her cand.med. degree at the University of Oslo in 1979.

==Personal life==

She is married to politician and physician Kjell Maartmann-Moe.
