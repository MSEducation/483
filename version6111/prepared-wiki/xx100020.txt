[[Hitler's Priestess]]

CATEGORIES: 1998 books

''Hitler's Priestess: Savitri Devi, the Hindu-Aryan Myth and Neo-Nazism'' is a book by Nicholas Goodrick-Clarke.  It is a biography of Savitri Devi.
It was published by New York University Press http://www.nyupress.org/product_info.php?products_id=2497 in June 1998 (hardcover, ISBN 0-8147-3110-4), and in paperback in October, 2000 (ISBN 0-8147-3111-2)
