Thomas Myron Braatz (born May 12, 1933) is a former American football linebacker in the National Football League for the Washington Redskins, Los Angeles Rams, and the Dallas Cowboys, and a former general manager of the Atlanta Falcons and Green Bay Packers.  He was selected by the Dallas Cowboys in the 1960 NFL Expansion Draft.  He played college football at Marquette University and was drafted in the fourteenth round of the 1955 NFL Draft.
