[[Ma Bourgogne]]

CATEGORIES: Le Marais, Restaurants in Paris, 4th arrondissement of Paris

Ma Bourgogne ([tpl]IPA-fr|ma buʁɡɔɲ[/tpl]) is a bistro in Place des Vosges in the Le Marais district of Paris. It is on the North-West point and is a café in the traditional French style. It has been around for many years and it has been spoken of as one of the best bistros in Paris.
Jean-Paul Sartre and Simone de Beauvoir came here after escaping from a dangerous protest about Algeria.
