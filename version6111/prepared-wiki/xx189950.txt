[[Sin-Iddinam]]

CATEGORIES: Sumerian rulers, Amorite kings, 18th-century BC people

Sin-Iddinam ruled the ancient Near East city-state
of Larsa from 1785 BC to 1778 BC. He was the son of Nur-Adad, with
whom there may have been a short co-regency overlap.
[ref]http://cuneiform.ucla.edu/staff/fitz/dissertation.pdf The Rulers
of Larsa, M. Fitzgerald, Yale University Disertation, 2002[/ref]
[ref]Larsa Year Names, Marcel Segrist, Andrews University Press, 1990,
ISBN 0-943872-54-5[/ref]
Chronology of the Larsa Dynasty, E.M. Grice , C.E. Keiser, M. Jastrow, AMS Press, 1979, ISBN 0-404-60274-6
The annals for his 7-year reign record that he campaigned against Babylon in year 4, Ibrat and Malgium in year 5, and Eshnunna in year 6.
