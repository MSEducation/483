[[Bruce Lee, the Man and the Legend]]

Bruce Lee,The Man and The Legend ([tpl]zh|t= 李小龍的生與死[/tpl]) (aka "The Legend is Born of Bruce Lee" and "Year of the Dragon") is a 1973 Hong Kong Documentary produced by Raymond Chow, and starring Bruce Lee. A Follow-up/reworking of this Documentary was released in 1984 under the title Bruce Lee,The Legend
