[[Alexius of Nicaea]]

CATEGORIES: Metropolitan bishops, Byzantine writers, Bishops of Nicaea

Alexius ([tpl]lang-el|[tpl]lang|grc|Ἀλέξιος[/tpl][/tpl]) was a metropolitan bishop of Nicaea who composed a Canon or Hymn about Saint Demetrius of Thessaloniki. It is uncertain when he lived.  The canon is in manuscript.Peter Lambeck, Biblioth. Vindobon. vol. v. p. 599, ed.
