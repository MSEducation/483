[[Meelad Air]]

CATEGORIES: Defunct airlines of Jordan, Airlines established in 2006, Airlines disestablished in 2008

Meelad Air was a private aviation company and charter airline based in Amman, Jordan, specializing on short and long-term aircraft charter and lease, especially during Hajj and Umra season. It became operational in 2006 and was shut down again in 2008.Meeöad Air at airlineupdate.com
