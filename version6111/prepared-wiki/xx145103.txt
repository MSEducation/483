[[Michał Lubomirski]]

CATEGORIES: Polish generals, Generals of the Polish–Lithuanian Commonwealth, 1752 births, 1825 deaths

Michał Lubomirski (1752–1825) was a Polish military commander and a Lieutenant General of the Polish Army. A son to Stanisław Lubomirski (1704–1793), he should not be confused with his ancestor Aleksander Michał Lubomirski.
