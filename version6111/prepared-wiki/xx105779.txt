[[Glossopteridales]]

CATEGORIES: Prehistoric plants, Permian life, Triassic life

Glossopteridales is an extinct order of plants belonging to Pteridospermatophyta, or Seed Ferns. They arose at the beginning of the Permian ([tpl]Ma|[tpl]Period start|Permian[/tpl][/tpl]) on the southern continent of Gondwana, but dwindled to extinction by the end of the Permian period ([tpl]Ma|[tpl]Period end|Permian[/tpl][/tpl]). The best known genus is Glossopteris.
