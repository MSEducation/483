[[Vardø Tunnel]]

CATEGORIES: Road tunnels in Finnmark, Subsea tunnels in Norway, Vardø, 1982 establishments in Norway, Tunnels completed in 1982

The Vardø Tunnel ([tpl]lang-no|Vardøtunnelen[/tpl]) is a subsea road tunnel in Vardø Municipality in Finnmark county, Norway.  The [tpl]convert|2890|m|adj=on[/tpl] long two-lane tunnel under the Bussesundet strait and it connects the island of Vardøya to the village of Svartnes on the Varanger Peninsula on the mainland. The tunnel is part of the European Route E75 highway and it reaches a depth of [tpl]convert|88|m[/tpl] below sea level. The tunnel opened in 1982 and was the first subsea tunnel in Norway.
