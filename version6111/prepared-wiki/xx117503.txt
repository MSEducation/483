[[Xenocytaea anomala]]

CATEGORIES: Salticidae

Xenocytaea anomala is a jumping spider.

==Name==

The epitheton anomala indicates the divergence of some characters of the species with others of the genus (e.g. the epigynum).

==Appearance==

X. anomala is about 3mm long, with females slightly larger.

==Distribution==

X. anomala is only known from the Palau District on the western Caroline Islands.
