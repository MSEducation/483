[[Pete List]]

CATEGORIES: American animators, Living people, Musicians from New York City

Pete List is a New York City-based musician and animator.

==Animation==

List has done animation work for Celebrity Deathmatch, Gary & Mike, and Disney's Doug.[tpl]cite web|url=http://movies.nytimes.com/person/961341/Pete-List |title=Pete List - About This Person - Movies & TV - NYTimes.com |publisher=Movies.nytimes.com |date=2007-01-18 |accessdate=2011-03-12[/tpl] Also included on his resume are ID's and promos for companies like MTV, VH1 and Nickelodeon. He directed the Marilyn Manson music video for "Astonishing Panorama of the Endtimes".[tpl]cite web|url=http://marilyn-manson.net/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=76 |title=Music Videos (Click titles to watch videos) |publisher=Marilyn-manson.net |date= |accessdate=2011-03-12[/tpl] His latest work has focused on children's videos for Scholastic.[tpl]cite web|url=http://store.scholastic.com/webapp/wcs/stores/servlet/ProductDisplay_null_47886_-1_10052_10051 |title=Scholastic Storybook Treasures: Don?t Let the Pigeon Drive the Bus DVD | The Scholastic Store |publisher=Store.scholastic.com |date= |accessdate=2011-03-12[/tpl]

==Music==

List is a multi-instrumentalist, primarily known for his beatboxing for Middle Eastern-influenced music.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.petelist.com/cds/ |title=Pete List |publisher=Pete List |date= |accessdate=2011-03-12[/tpl] He is a member of Beatbox Guitar with Rob Mastrianni, and Djinn with Carmine Guida,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.hagalla.de/hagalla%20com/Texts/E%20Interview%20Texts/engl%20DJAM%20Interview,%201.html |title=hagalla, interview DJAM, page 1 |publisher=Hagalla.de |date= |accessdate=2011-03-12[/tpl] and has toured various countries with these acts. His music with Djinn has featured on the Istanbul episode of Anthony Bourdain's No Reservations.
