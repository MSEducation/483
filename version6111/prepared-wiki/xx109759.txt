[[Cyanolyca]]

CATEGORIES: Cyanolyca, Bird genera

Cyanolyca is a genus of small jays found in humid highland forests in southern Mexico, Central America and the Andes in South America. All are largely blue and have a black mask. They also possess black bills and legs and are skulking birds.
