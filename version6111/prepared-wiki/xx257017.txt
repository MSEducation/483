[[Louis C. and Amelia L. Schmidt House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses completed in 1895, Queen Anne architecture in Iowa, Houses in Davenport, Iowa, Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Iowa, National Register of Historic Places in Scott County, Iowa

The Louis C. and Amelia L. Schmidt House is located on the east side of Davenport, Iowa, United States.  The Queen Anne style residence has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since 2007.
