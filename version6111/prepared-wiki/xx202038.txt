[[2008 Miller Superbike World Championship round]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 Superbike World Championship season, 2008 in American motorsport

The 2008 Miller Superbike World Championship round was the sixth round of the 2008 Superbike World Championship season. It took place on the weekend of May 30-June 1, 2008, at the Salt Lake City circuit.
