[[Kärla Parish]]

CATEGORIES: Kärla Parish

Kärla Parish is in Saare County, Estonia.
The parish consists of one small borough, Kärla,  and 22 villages: Anepesa, Arandi, Hirmuste, Jõempa, Käesla, Kandla, Karida, Kirikuküla, Kogula, Kulli, Kuuse, Kõrkküla, Mätasselja, Mõnnuste, Nõmpa, Paevere, Paiküla, Sauvere, Sõmera, Ulje, Vendise and Vennati. The municipality has a population of 1,803 (as of 1 January 2007) and covers an area of 217.87 km² (84.12 mi²).
