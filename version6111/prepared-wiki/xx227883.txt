[[Princeton Alumni Weekly]]

CATEGORIES: Princeton University, Princeton University publications, Alumni magazines, American magazines, Publications established in 1900

The Princeton Alumni Weekly (PAW) is a magazine published for the alumni of Princeton University.  It was founded in 1900 and until 1977, it was the only weekly college alumni magazine in the United States.The Princeton Alumni Weekly (PAW), section from Alexander Leitch, A Princeton Companion, Princeton University Press (1978). Upon changing to biweekly publication in 1977, the number of issues per year decreased from 28 to 21, and then later decreased to 17. It still remains the most frequently published alumni magazine in the world, currently publishing 15 times per year.Princeton Alumni Weekly: About PAW
