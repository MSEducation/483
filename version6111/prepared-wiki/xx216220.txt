[[Czech Centres]]

CATEGORIES: Organizations established in 1986, Cultural promotion organizations, Czech culture, Czech language, Organizations based in the Czech Republic, 1986 establishments in Czechoslovakia

The Czech Centres ([tpl]lang-cs|Česká centra[/tpl]) comprise an organisation funded by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Czech Republic. Its goal is to promote the Czech language and Czech culture abroad. It was created in 1986, and is based in Prague. There are currently 21 Czech Centres in twenty countries around the world.[tpl]cite web|title=The Czech centers|url=http://www.czechcentres.cz|publisher=Czech Republic|accessdate=1 February 2012[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.radio.cz/en/section/talking/czech-centres |title=Radio Prague - Czech Centres |publisher=Radio.cz |date= |accessdate=2012-07-26[/tpl]
