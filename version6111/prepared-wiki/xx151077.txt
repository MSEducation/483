[[Theta Orionis]]

CATEGORIES: Bayer objects, Orion (constellation)

Theta Orionis (tet Ori, Theta Ori, θ Orionis, θ Ori) is the name of several astronomical objects, located at RA [tpl]RA|05|35.3[/tpl] DEC [tpl]DEC|-05|24[/tpl] :
The Theta Orionis set of objects are located within the NGC 1977 nebula.
