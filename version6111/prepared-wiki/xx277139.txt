[[Tønne Huitfeldt]]

CATEGORIES: 1625 births, 1677 deaths, People from Buskerud, Norwegian Army personnel, Norwegian landowners

Tønne Huitfeldt (20 November 1625 – 12 September 1677) was a Norwegian landowner and military officer.
He was born in Hurum, and was the father of Iver Huitfeldt. He is renowned for leading the defence of Halden in 1659 and 1660, during the Second Northern War.
