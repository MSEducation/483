[[KNBT]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in San Antonio, Texas

KNBT (92.1 FM) is a radio station broadcasting an Americana music format. Licensed to New Braunfels, Texas, USA, the station serves the San Antonio area.  The station is currently owned by New Braunfels Communications, Inc.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KNBT |title=KNBT Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division[/tpl]
