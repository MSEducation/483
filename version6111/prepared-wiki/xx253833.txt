[[Bi-Conicals of the Rammellzee]]

CATEGORIES: 2004 albums, Rammellzee albums

Bi-Conicals of the Rammellzee is an album by American rapper Rammellzee. Released in 2004, it is his first full-length record, released over twenty years after the single "Beat Bop" with K-Rob.
