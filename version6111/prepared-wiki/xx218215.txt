[[Mike Grace (pitcher)]]

CATEGORIES: 1970 births, Living people, Bradley Braves baseball players, Major League Baseball pitchers, Baseball players from Illinois, Philadelphia Phillies players, Sportspeople from Joliet, Illinois

Michael James Grace (June 20, 1970 in Joliet, Illinois), is a retired Major League Baseball pitcher. He played all or part of five seasons in the majors, from [tpl]By|1995[/tpl] until [tpl]By|1999[/tpl], all for the Philadelphia Phillies.
