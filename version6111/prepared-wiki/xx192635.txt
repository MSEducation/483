[[Continent (album)]]

CATEGORIES: The Acacia Strain albums, 2008 albums

Continent is the fourth studio album of the American band The Acacia Strain. This is the first album with bassist Jack Strong and second with drummer Kevin Boutot. Recording began on April 13, 2008 and it was released August 19, 2008 via Prosthetic Records.BLABBERMOUTH.NET - THE ACACIA STRAIN: 'Continent' Release Date Set Frontman Vincent Bennett also spoke to MTV News about the band's forthcoming LP, saying it was the band's "darkest" effort to date.
