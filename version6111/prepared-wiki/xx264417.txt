[[TSS Cambria (1920)]]

CATEGORIES: 1920 ships, Passenger ships of the United Kingdom, Steamships, Clyde-built ships, Ships of the London and North Western Railway

TSS ''Cambria'' was a twin screw steamer passenger vessel operated by the London and North Western Railway from 1921 to 1923, and the London, Midland and Scottish Railway from 1923 to 1948.Railway and Other Steamers, Duckworth. 1962

==History==

She was built by William Denny and Brothers of Dumbarton and launched in 1920, but she did not come into service with the London and North Western Railway until 1921.
In 1948 she was renamed TSS ''Cambria II in preparation for a new vessel of the same name, the motor vessel Cambria'' and the following year she was scrapped by the British Transport Commission.
