William Michael Harry Rhodes (known as Will) (born 2 March 1995) is a cricketer who plays for Yorkshire and for the England Under-19 cricket team.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.espncricinfo.com/ci/content/current/player/570421.html |title=Will Rhodes | Cricket Players and Officials |publisher=ESPN Cricinfo |date= |accessdate=2014-04-10[/tpl] He is an all-rounder.[tpl]cite web|author=Jon Culley |url=http://www.independent.co.uk/sport/cricket/yorkshire-rose-in-full-bloom-again-8735137.html |title=Yorkshire rose in full bloom again - Cricket - Sport |publisher=The Independent |date=2013-07-28 |accessdate=2014-04-10[/tpl]
Rhodes made both his List A and Twenty20 debuts for Yorkshire during the 2013 English cricket season. In the same year, he also made 102 for England Under 19s against the Pakistan Under-19 cricket team.
Rhodes was the captain of the England Under 19s at the 2014 ICC Under-19 Cricket World Cup held in the United Arab Emirates. He lead the England U19s to victory over the tournament favorites, the India U19s, in the quarterfinals. In the semifinals, he rescued his side with an unbeaten 76 n.o. after the team was reduced to 119 for 6 in the 37th over. However, the England U19s lost the semifinal match to the Pakistan U19s in a tight contest,  Rhodes nonetheless received praise for his captaincy in that match.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.espncricinfo.com/icc-under-19-world-cup-2014/content/story/722579.html |title=England v Pakistan, Under-19 World Cup, 1st semi-final, Dubai : We bettered our expectations - Rhodes | Cricket News | ICC Under-19 World Cup |publisher=ESPN Cricinfo |date= |accessdate=2014-04-10[/tpl]
