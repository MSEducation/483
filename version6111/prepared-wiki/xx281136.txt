[[Legal View with Ashleigh Banfield]]

CATEGORIES: CNN shows, 2013 American television series debuts

Legal View with Ashleigh Banfield is a daytime news program on CNN/US, focusing on legal stories. Hosted by the Canadian-American journalist Ashleigh Banfield, the show airs weekdays 12 pm ET.[tpl]cite news | url=http://www.mediabistro.com/tvnewser/cnn-rebranding-11-am-hour-as-legal-view-with-ashleigh-banfield_b191026 | title=CNN Rebranding 11 AM Hour As ‘Legal View with Ashleigh Banfield’ | work=TVNewser | date=Auggust 6, 2013 | accessdate=October 23, 2013[/tpl]
Legal View is based at the Time Warner Center in New York City.
