Ian MacDonald (born 30 August 1953) is a Scottish former professional footballer who played as a central defender, making over 300 career appearances.

==Career==

Born in Elgin, MacDonald played for Elgin City, St. Johnstone, Carlisle United, Dundee and Arbroath.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.neilbrown.newcastlefans.com/player1/iainmacdonald.html|title=Profile|publisher=Post War English & Scottish Football League A - Z Player's Database[/tpl]
