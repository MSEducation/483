[[Sebastian Ottosson]]

CATEGORIES: 1992 births, Living people, Linköpings HC players

Sebastian Ottosson (born February 7, 1992) is a Swedish professional ice hockey player. He is currently playing in Sweden's Division 1 with Kallinge/Ronneby.
Ottosson made his Elitserien debut playing with Linköpings HC during the 2009–10 Elitserien season.Eliteprospects.com - 2009-10 Linköpings HC player statistics
