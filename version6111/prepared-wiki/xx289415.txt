[[Araghul]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Araghul ([tpl]lang-fa|اراغول[/tpl], also Romanized as Arāghūl; also known as ‘Arqūl)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3773068|Araghul[/tpl] is a village in Bizineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
