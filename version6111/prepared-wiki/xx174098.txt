[[Qionglai City]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Chengdu, County-level divisions of Sichuan

Qionglai City ([tpl]zh|s=邛崃市 |t=邛崍市 |p=Qiónglái Shì[/tpl]) is a county-level city in the west of Sichuan Province, China. It is located around [tpl]convert|60|km[/tpl] from and under the administration of Chengdu City, the provincial capital. The city is located on the western edge of the Sichuan Basin and in the foothills of the Qionglai Mountains that bound the basin from the west.
Qionglai is also near P'ing-lo Town to West, Pujiang County and Chengdu Shuangliu International Airport that maintaining connection traffic route and if going from Chengdu to Datang Town there are bus services interchangeable in Qionglai Highway gates then can go to the countryside Bus but Bus are normally off services for evening time. It also has Taxi outbound call to Country at outside of Bus gate and also in no Bus operation times.

==Transport==

There are many rivers around in Qionglai City,so it has abundant water power.South River,Zhuojiang River,Xiejiang River and Pujiang River flow through this city,and they are 217.15 kilometers long in total.The quantity of runoff of surface water may reach 991 million cubic meters,including 532.8 million cubic meters which can be made use of by humans.  Underground water can reanch 106 million cubic meters.Surface and underground water create many opportunities to support  agriculture and manufacture.
