[[Nasirabad, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Nasirabad ([tpl]lang-fa|نصيراباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Naşīrābād; also known as Naşrābād-e Torpākhlū)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795760|Nasirabad[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 698, in 164 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
