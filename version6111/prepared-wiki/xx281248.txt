[[The Kingslayer Saga]]

CATEGORIES: Musical groups established in 1996

==Biography==

While a member of Rhapsody of Fire where he played guitar, Taca Heumemarerion formed The Kingslayer Saga in 1996 as a side-project. After the successful release of their first album Aletheia Crosses of God, Towers saw the potential of Dungeon and left Dragons.[tpl]clarify|date=November 2013[/tpl] The name of the band was changed to The Kingslayer Saga and they signed to Vendetta Records.
