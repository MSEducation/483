[[Carol Weymuller Open 2013]]

CATEGORIES: Squash in the United States, 2013 in squash, 2013 in American sports, 2013 in New York

The Carol Weymuller Open 2013 is the women's edition of the 2013 Carol Weymuller Open, which is a tournament of the WSA World Tour event Gold (Prize money : 50 000 $). The event took place at The Heights Casino in Brooklyn, New York in the United States from the 3rd of October to the 6th October. Nicol David won her second Carol Weymuller Open trophy, beating Camile Serme in the final.
