[[Sourgoubila Department]]

CATEGORIES: Departments of Burkina Faso, Kourwéogo Province

Sourgoubila is a department or commune of Kourwéogo Province in central  Burkina Faso. Its capital lies at the town of Sourgoubila. According to the 1996 census the department has a total population of 39,226.Burkinabé government inforoute communale
