[[Seikkan Township]]

CATEGORIES: Townships of Yangon

Seikkan Township ([tpl]lang-my|ဆိပ်ကမ်း မြို့နယ်[/tpl], [tpl]IPA-my|sʰeiʔkáɴ mjo̰nɛ̀|pron[/tpl]) is a township located in the western part of Yangon, Myanmar. One of the smallest townships, Seikkan consists of just three wards. It has a primary school and a hospital.
