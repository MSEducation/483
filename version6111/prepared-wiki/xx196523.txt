[[Shiqian County]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Guizhou

Shiqian County ([tpl]zh|s=石阡县|p=Shíqiān Xiàn[/tpl]) is a county belonging to Tongren Prefecture, Guizhou Province of China.
Area: 2172 km2.
Population: 370,000 in 2002.
Postal Code: 555100.
The government is located in Tangshan town.
