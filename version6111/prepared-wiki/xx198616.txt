[[Liri Ballabani Stadium]]

CATEGORIES: Football venues in Albania, Multi-purpose stadiums in Albania

Liri Ballabani Stadium (aka Burreli Stadium, [tpl]lang-sq|Stadiumi Liri Ballabani or Stadiumi i Burrelit[/tpl]) is a multi-purpose stadium in Burrel, Albania. The stadium is the home ground of KS Burreli and has a capacity of 2,500.Worldstadiums.com
