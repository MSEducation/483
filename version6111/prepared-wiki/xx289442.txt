[[Dash Tappeh, Khodabandeh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Dash Tappeh ([tpl]lang-fa|داش تپه[/tpl], also Romanized as Dāsh Tappeh; also known as Tūsh Tepe)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3059978|Dash Tappeh[/tpl] is a village in Bizineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 120, in 27 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
