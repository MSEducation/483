[[Güneli Gün]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, Turkish novelists, Turkish–English translators, Translators to English

Güneli Gün is a Turkish translator and author. She is best known for her novels On the Road to Baghdad and Book of Trances as well as her English translations of some of the novels of Nobel laureate author Orhan Pamuk.[tpl]cite web|url=http://germany.usembassy.gov/germany/turkey08.html|title=The U.S. Embassy Literature Series:|year=2008|publisher=United States Diplomatic Mission to Germany|accessdate=29 November 2010[/tpl]
