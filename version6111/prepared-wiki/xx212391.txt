[[Anna Vladimirovna Dybo]]

CATEGORIES: 1959 births, Living people, Linguists from Russia, Corresponding Members of the Russian Academy of Sciences

Anna Vladimirovna Dybo (born June 4, 1959) is a Russian linguist, member of the Russian Academy of Sciences and co-author of the Etymological Dictionary of the Altaic Languages (2003).
