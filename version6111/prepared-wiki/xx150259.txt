[[Hunter MacKenzie Austin]]

CATEGORIES: 1966 births, Actresses from San Diego, California, American voice actresses, Living people, American video game actresses, American magazine journalists, American women journalists, American Christians

Hunter MacKenzie Austin (born March 22, 1966) is an American voice actress. In teen life, she was a magazine journalist of Tiger Beat, Glamour and LA Weekly.
