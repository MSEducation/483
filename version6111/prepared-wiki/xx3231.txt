[[Europe of Democracies and Diversities]]

CATEGORIES: Former European Parliament party groups, Euroscepticism

Europe of Democracies and Diversities (EDD) was a Eurosceptic[tpl]cite book|author1=David Phinnemore|author2=Lee McGowan|title=A Dictionary of the European Union|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=m9EB7e8pgywC&pg=PA276|accessdate=7 August 2013|date=26 June 2013|publisher=Routledge|isbn=978-1-135-08127-0|pages=276–[/tpl][tpl]cite book|author=Alexander H. Trechsel|title=Towards a Federal Europe|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=mTndAAAAQBAJ&pg=PT72|date=13 September 2013|publisher=Taylor & Francis|isbn=978-1-317-99818-1|pages=72–[/tpl] political group with seats in the European Parliament between 1999 and 2004. Following the elections in 2004, the former MEPs of the group took MEPs from the new European Union member nations on board and together registered the new group Independence/Democracy (IND/DEM).
