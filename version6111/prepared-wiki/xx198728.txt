[[John K. Wright]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, Johnson C. Smith Golden Bulls football coaches, Kentucky State Thorobreds football coaches

John K. Wright was the 21st head football coach for the Kentucky State University Thorobreds located in Frankfort, Kentucky and he held that position for the 1986 season.  His career coaching record at Kentucky State was 1 wins, 6 losses, and 3 ties.  As of the conclusion of the 2007 season, this ranks him 23rd at Kentucky State in total wins and 18th at Kentucky State in winning percentage (.250).Kentucky State University coaching records
