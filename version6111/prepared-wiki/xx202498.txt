[[Dunaway House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Arkansas, Houses completed in 1915, Houses in Little Rock, Arkansas, Terracotta

The Dunaway House is an historical home in Little Rock, Arkansas.  An  Craftsman Style home located in a picturesque boulevard on Battery Street, the two-story brick house is in the Central High School Neighborhood Historic District[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.arkansaspreservation.org/pdf/publications/Central_High.pdf|title=Little Rockâ€™s Central High School Neighborhood Historic District|authors=Sandra Taylor Smith and Anne Wagner Speed|publisher=Arkansas Historic Preservation Program|pages=24|accessdate=2008-05-22[/tpl] It was designed by Charles L. Thompson in 1915. The Dunaway House features a terra-cotta gable roof with a portico over an arched entrance as well as a south-facing two-story wing with a hip roof.
