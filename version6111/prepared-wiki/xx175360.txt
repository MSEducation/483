[[KJYL]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Iowa, Radio stations established in 2004

KJYL is a Christian radio station licensed to Eagle Grove, Iowa, broadcasting on 100.7 MHz FM. KJYL serves the Fort Dodge, Iowa area. The station is owned by Minn-Iowa Christian Broadcasting, Inc. KJYL fcc.gov. Accessed July 26, 2012

==Translators==

KJYL is also heard locally in Newell, Iowa, through a translator on 96.3 FM.
