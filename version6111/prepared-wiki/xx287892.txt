[[Pantacordis pallida]]

CATEGORIES: Animals described in 1876, Pantacordis, Insects of Italy

Pantacordis pallida is a moth of the Symmocidae family. It is found in mainland Italy and on Malta and Sicily.Fauna Europaea
The wingspan is 10–12 mm. Adults are dirty yellowish with a very light darker sprinkling on the forewings.lepiforum.de
