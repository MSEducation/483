Kathryn Mead (born 14 February 1977 in Nelson, New Zealand) is a shooting competitor for New Zealand. 
At the 2006 Commonwealth Games she won a bronze medal in the 50m Rifle Prone Pairs event with Juliet Etherington.Profile at the New Zealand Olympic Committee website
