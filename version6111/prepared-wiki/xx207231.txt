[[Kyneton Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in Victoria (Australia)

Kyneton Airport [tpl]airport codes||YKTN[/tpl] is located in Kyneton, Victoria, Australia. The airfield is located [tpl]Convert|1.5|NM|lk=in[/tpl] northwest of the town.
Home of the Kyneton Aero Club and the Sports Aircraft Association of Australia Chapter 20, the airfield is managed by the Kyneton Aero Club on behalf of the Shire of Macedon Ranges.
