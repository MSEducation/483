[[Rookwood School]]

CATEGORIES: Andover, Hampshire, Educational institutions established in 1934, Independent schools in Hampshire, 1934 establishments in England, Boarding schools in Hampshire, Member schools of the Independent Schools Association (UK)

Rookwood School is a coeducational non-selective independent day and boarding school for children aged 3 to 16, located on a rural campus off Weyhill Road in Andover, Hampshire, England.[tpl]citenews|title=See for yourself|url=http://www.andoveradvertiser.co.uk/archive/2009/01/30/learning+for+life/4088939.See_for_yourself/|publisher=Andover Advertiser|date=30 January 2009[/tpl] Due to its proximity to the military garrison at Tidworth, Wiltshire, a substantial number of its pupils are children of military personnel or MoD employees.2006 ISI Inspection Reports

==History==

The school was founded in 1934 as a girls' school and moved to its current site in 1946. Boys were admitted in 1997.

==Organisation==

The school has three departments: the Rookery (Pre-School and Pre-Preparatory), Paddocks (Prep School) and Highfield (Senior School).

==Academics==

Rookwood does not have a sixth form; most pupils will continue on to independent schools or sixth form colleges in the county or in neighbouring Wiltshire.[tpl]cn|date=July 2013[/tpl]
Although relatively small compared to many independent schools in the county, Rookwood has a strong academic reputation in the GCSEs and is among the top performing schools in the ISA.[tpl]citenews|title=Former Rookwood pupils awarded for UK's highest grades|url=http://www.hampshirechronicle.co.uk/archive/2009/11/21/News+%28aa_news%29/4752120.Former_Rookwood_pupils_awarded_for_UK_s_highest_grades/|publisher=Hampshire Chronicle|date=21 November 2009[/tpl][tpl]citenews|title=After the results, celebrations|url=http://www.hampshirechronicle.co.uk/archive/2002/08/29/Hampshire+Archive/5605228.After_the_results__celebrations/|publisher=Hampshire Chronicle|date=29 August 2002[/tpl][tpl]Failed verification|date=July 2013[/tpl]

==Boarding==

Although most of its pupils are day pupils Rookwood has boarding provisions for a small number of boarders. There are two boarding houses: Beechwood (girls) and Oakwood (boys).Boarding Houses
