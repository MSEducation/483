[[Howlin' (Jagwar Ma album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 albums, Mom + Pop Music albums

Howlin' is the debut album from Australian dance rock duo Jagwar Ma. The album was written and performed by Jono Ma and Gabriel Winterfield. Recorded and produced by Jono Ma. It was released in June 2013 under Mom + Pop Music in the USA and Marathon in the UK and Europe.
