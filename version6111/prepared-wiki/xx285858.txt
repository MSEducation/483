[[Presbyterian Church in Korea (GaeHyukHapDong II)]]

CATEGORIES: Presbyterian denominations in South Korea, Presbyterian denominations in Asia

The Presbyterian Church in Korea (GaeHyukHapDong II) is a Reformed denomination in South Korea. In 2004 it had 13,272 members in 125 congregations and 171 ordained ministers. It adheres to the Apostles Creed and Westminster Confession.http://www.reformiert-online.net/adressen/detail.php?id=13166&lg=eng
