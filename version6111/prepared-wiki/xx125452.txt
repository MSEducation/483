[[Hunton, North Yorkshire]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in North Yorkshire, Civil parishes in North Yorkshire

Hunton is a village and civil parish about 3 miles south of Catterick Garrison, in England.  It is part of the Richmondshire district of North Yorkshire, and as of the 2001 census has a population of 420.[tpl]cite web | url=http://neighbourhood.statistics.gov.uk/dissemination/LeadTableView.do?a=7&b=798016&c=Hunton&d=16&e=15&g=476372&i=1001x1003x1004&m=0&r=1&s=1216559715238&enc=1&dsFamilyId=779 | title=2001 Census: Key Statistics: Parish Headcounts: Area: Hunton CP (Parish) | publisher=Office for National Statistics | accessdate=2008-07-20 [/tpl]
The small village's local amenities include a combined post office/village shop and The Countryman's Inn, a welcoming pub and restaurant.
