[[Nestucca Bay]]

CATEGORIES: Bays of Oregon, Oregon Coast, Landforms of Tillamook County, Oregon

Nestucca Bay is a bay formed by the confluence of the Nestucca River and the Pacific Ocean located in northwest Oregon in the United States. [tpl]cite gnis |id=1163171 |name=Nestucca Bay |links=off |accessdate=2013-03-24[/tpl] It is located near the town of Pacific City in southwestern Tillamook County, approximately 12 mi (19 km) south of Cape Lookout. The bay is a bar-built estuary and totals [tpl]convert|1000|acre|km2[/tpl] in area.  The Nestucca and Little Nestucca rivers enter the bay. The bay is separated from the Pacific Ocean by the Nestucca Spit which includes Bob Straub State Park. A part of Nestucca Bay is included in the Nestucca Bay National Wildlife Refuge.
