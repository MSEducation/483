[[Saint-Pierre, Aosta Valley]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Aosta Valley

Saint-Pierre is a town and comune in the Aosta Valley region of north-western Italy. There are about 150 medieval castles, tours and fortified houses in the Aosta Valley.Massetti, E. "Aosta Valley Castles" http://www.naturaosta.it/castles.htm accessed on 15 March, 2014. Nearby, the Sarriod family built the Sarriod de la Tour Castle.
