[[Army of Lovers (song)]]

CATEGORIES: 2005 singles, Lee Ryan songs, Number-one singles in Italy, 2005 songs, Sony BMG singles

"Army of Lovers" is the debut single from Blue member Lee Ryan's debut solo album, Lee Ryan. The single was released on 24 July 2005. The song peaked at #3 on the UK Singles Chart. The song also achieved success worldwide, reaching #1 in Italy and staying there for four weeks. Although his highest selling single to date, it is his least critically acclaimed.
