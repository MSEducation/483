CATEGORIES: Gymnastics at the 2011 Pan American Games

The women's artistic team all-around competition of the wrestling events at the 2011 Pan American Games in Guadalajara, Mexico, was held on October 24 at the Nissan Gymnastics Stadium.[tpl]cite web|title=Wrestling technical manual|url=http://www.guadalajara2011.org.mx/documentos/manuales/eng/technical/tm_wrestling.pdf|publisher=Guadalajara 2011|accessdate=21 October 2011[/tpl]  The defending champion was Ivana Hong, Shawn Johnson, Anastasia Liukin, Samantha Peszek and Amber Trani from the United States.
