[[Stéfano de Gregorio]]

CATEGORIES: 1994 births, Living people, Argentine male film actors, Argentine male television actors, Male actors from Buenos Aires

Stéfano de Gregorio (born September 16, 1994) is an Argentine actor and model. Stéfano is also known by his nickname- Yeyo, which is the first word he said when he was a child.

==Biography==

Stéfano became famous when he was only eighteen months old. He participated in a lot of commercial activities, acted in the movie Valentine. In 2003, he acted the role of Mateo in Rincón de Luz (Light Place) produced by Cris Morena. In 2004 and 2005, he play the role of Thomas in the novel Floricienta (parody of Cinderella). In 2006, he acted in Chiquititas Sin Fin. From 2007 until 2010, he acts in the novel Casi Ángeles (Almost Angels). He is emphasized in his physical attraction and intelligence.
