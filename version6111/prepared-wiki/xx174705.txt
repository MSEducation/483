CATEGORIES: Lists of tallest buildings in Europe, Skyscrapers in Croatia, Lists of buildings and structures in Croatia

This list of tallest buildings in Croatia ranks buildings in Croatia by official height. The tallest building in Croatia is currently the [tpl]convert|105|m|adj=on[/tpl] tall Zagreb Cathedral. The tallest structure in Croatia is the [tpl]convert|340|m|adj=on[/tpl] chimney of the Plomin Power Station in Plomin, Istria.
