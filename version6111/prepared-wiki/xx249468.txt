[[Murray Taylor]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1956 births, New Zealand international rugby union players, New Zealand rugby union players

Murray Barton Taylor (born 25 August 1956) is a retired rugby union player from New Zealand. He played thirty matches for the All Blacks in the late 1970s and early 1980s; seven of these were test matches.[tpl]cite web|url=http://stats.allblacks.com/asp/profile.asp?ABID=875|title=Profile of Murray Barton Taylor|publisher=allblacks.com|accessdate=2011-02-07[/tpl] He is now living in the Waikato in New Zealand.
