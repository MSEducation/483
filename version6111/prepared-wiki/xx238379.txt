[[NWA Arizona Heavyweight Championship]]

CATEGORIES: National Wrestling Alliance championships, Regional professional wrestling championships, National Wrestling Alliance state heavyweight wrestling championships, State heavyweight wrestling championships

The NWA Arizona Heavyweight Championship is a National Wrestling Alliance-controlled championship for the state of Arizona.  It has been primarily defended in Rising Phoenix Wrestling and Impact Zone Wrestling. The current champion is Brandon Gatson, who won the title on July 31, 2011.
