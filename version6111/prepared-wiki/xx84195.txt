[[Kijimadaira, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Kijimadaira|木島平村|Kijimadaira-mura[/tpl] is a village located in Shimotakai District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the village has an estimated population of 5,416 and a density of 54.54 persons per km². The total area is 99.31 km².
