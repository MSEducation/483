Michael Jon O'Brien (born October 23, 1965) is an American former competition swimmer who won the gold medal in the men's 1,500-meter freestyle event in a time of 15:05.20 at the 1984 Summer Olympics in Los Angeles, California.Sports-Reference.com, Olympic Sports, Athletes, Michael O'Brien.
