[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Coimbatore]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in India, Religious organizations established in 1850, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 19th century, 1850 establishments in India

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Coimbatore ([tpl]lang-la| Coimbaturen(sis)[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Coimbatore in the Ecclesiastical province of Madras and Mylapore in India.
