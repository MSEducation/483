[[Diógenes Domínguez]]

Diógenes Domínguez (born 1902, date of death unknown) was a Paraguayan football forward who played for Paraguay in the 1930 FIFA World Cup.1930 FIFA World Cup Uruguay He also played for Club Sportivo Luqueño.
