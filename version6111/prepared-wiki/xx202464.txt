[[Bredwardine]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Herefordshire

Bredwardine is a village in Herefordshire, England, located off the B4352 road in the west of the county.
Features include a brick bridge over the River Wye, a historic late 17th century coaching innHerefordshire - Nikolaus Pevsner 1963 named the Red Lion, St Andrews parish church and the site of Bredwardine Castle. 
The Wye Valley Walk passes through the village.
