Michael Meaker (born 18 August 1971) is a Welsh footballer who plays for Yate Town in the Southern League South and West Division.

==Club career==

He began his career at Queens Park Rangers, where he played in the FA Premier League. While at QPR, he had a loan spell at Plymouth Argyle.
