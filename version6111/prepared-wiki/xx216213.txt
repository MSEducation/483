[[Walnut Street Historic District (Chaska, Minnesota)]]

CATEGORIES: Greek Revival architecture in Minnesota, Geography of Carver County, Minnesota

Walnut Street Historic District is a historic district in Chaska, Minnesota listed on the National Register of Historic Places.  The district is roughly bounded by Walnut, Second, Chestnut, and Sixth Streets.  It contains burial mounds representing the area's prehistory, and the buildings and structures in the district represent Chaska's period of commercial, industrial, religious, and residential development.[tpl]cite book|title=The National Register of Historic Places in Minnesota|last=Nord|first=Mary Ann|publisher=Minnesota Historical Society|year=2003|isbn=0-87351-448-3[/tpl]  Houses within this district include the Greek Revival style and other neoclassical styles.[tpl]cite web|url=http://pdfhost.focus.nps.gov/docs/NRHP/Text/64000345.pdf|title=National Register of Historic Places nomination form: Carver County MRA|pages=65, 68|accessdate=2008-08-19[/tpl]
