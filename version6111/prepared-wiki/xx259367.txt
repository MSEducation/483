[[Lasha Dekanosidze]]

CATEGORIES: 1987 births, Living people, Footballers from Georgia (country), Georgian expatriate footballers, Expatriate footballers in Belarus, FC Torpedo-BelAZ Zhodino players

Lasha Dekanosidze (born 9 July 1987 in Tbilisi) is a Georgian professional football player. As of 2013 he is playing for Finnish club ORPa.
