[[Raman Ghosh]]

CATEGORIES: Indian badminton players, Indian Badminton Champions, Recipients of the Arjuna Award, Living people, Year of birth missing (living people), Asian Games medalists in badminton

Raman Ghosh is an Indian sportsman who represented India in several badminton tournaments in the 1960s and 1970s. He was a member of the Indian badminton team, which won the 1975 Thomas Cup qualification by defeating Pakistan at Lahore.
He received the Arjuna award for badminton in 1974.
