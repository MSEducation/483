[[The Yardbirds Greatest Hits]]

CATEGORIES: The Yardbirds compilation albums, 1967 greatest hits albums, Epic Records compilation albums, English-language compilation albums

The Yardbirds Greatest Hits was the first compilation album of songs recorded by The Yardbirds. It was released in March, 1967 by Epic Records, LN 24246/BN 26246, and was their highest charting U.S. album, peaking at #28 on the Billboard 200.[tpl]Allmusic|class=artist|id=p5888/charts-awards|pure_url=yes[/tpl]
