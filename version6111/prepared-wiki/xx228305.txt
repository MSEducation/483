[[Palaestinus]]

CATEGORIES: Greek mythology, Offspring of Poseidon

Palaestinus (Ancient Greek: [tpl]lang|grc|Παλαιστῖνος[/tpl]) was in Greek mythology a son of Poseidon and father of Haliacmon.
