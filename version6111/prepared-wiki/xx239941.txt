[[Américo Brasiliense de Almeida Melo]]

CATEGORIES: 1833 births, 1896 deaths, People from São Paulo (city), Governors of Paraíba, Governors of Rio de Janeiro (state), Governors of São Paulo (state), University of São Paulo alumni, Government ministers of Brazil

Américo Brasiliense de Almeida Melo (São Paulo, August 8, 1833 -Rio de Janeiro, March 25, 1896) was the third governor of the State of São Paulo, from March to December 1891, and first president of the State of São Paulo. 
Died in Rio de Janeiro, as Minister of the Supreme Federal Tribunal. The city of Américo Brasiliense in the State of São Paulo is named after him, as is a school in the city of Santo André.
