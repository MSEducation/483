[[Gilat Satellite Networks]]

CATEGORIES: Companies listed on NASDAQ, Companies listed on TASE, Companies established in 1987, Telecommunications companies of Israel

Gilat Satellite Networks is a public company headquartered in IsraelGilat Company Profiles - Israel that develops and sells VSAT satellite ground stations and related equipment. Its shares are traded on the NASDAQ Global Select Market and on the Tel Aviv Stock Exchange.
