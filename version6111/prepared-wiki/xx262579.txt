[[1984 Borden Classic]]

CATEGORIES: 1984 Virginia Slims World Championship Series, Tennis tournaments in Japan

The 1984 Borden Classic  was a tennis tournament played on outdoor hard courts in Tokyo, Japan that was part of the 1984 Virginia Slims World Championship Series. The tournament was held from October 1 through October 7, 1984.
