[[Galinthias amoena]]

CATEGORIES: Hymenopodidae, Mantodea of Africa, Fauna of Zimbabwe, Insects of Tanzania, Insects of Cameroon, Insects of Malawi, Insects of Sierra Leone

Galinthias amoena is a species of praying mantis found in Angola, Cameroon, Kenya, Malawi, Mozambique, Natal, Sierra Leone, Tanzania, Zanzibar, Transvaal, Zimbabwe and the Congo River region. http://insects.tamu.edu/research/collection/hallan/Arthropoda/Insects/Mantodea/Family/Hymenopodidae.txt Texas A&M University
