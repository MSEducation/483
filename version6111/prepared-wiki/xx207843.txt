[[Peder Rasmus Lyng]]

Peder Rasmus Lyng (1799–1863) was a Norwegian politician.
He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament in 1842, representing the rural constituency of Nordre Trondhjems Amt (today named Nord-Trøndelag). He worked as a farmer.Peder Rasmus Lyng — Norwegian Social Science Data Services (NSD) He served only one term.
