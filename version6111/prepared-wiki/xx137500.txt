[[Martyn Griffiths]]

CATEGORIES: British hillclimb drivers, Living people, 1946 births

Martyn Griffiths (born 18 March 1946) is a British racing driver, whose greatest success has been in hillclimbing. He has won the British Hill Climb Championship on five occasions (1979, 1986, 1987, 1990, 1991).[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.motorsportcircuitguide.com/circuit-news1.php?id=432|title=Speed Hillclimbing|publisher=Motor Sport Circuit Guide|accessdate=2010-12-26[/tpl]
