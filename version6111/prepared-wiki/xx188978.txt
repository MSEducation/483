[[Ivohibe District]]

CATEGORIES: Ihorombe, Districts of Madagascar

{{Blacklisted-links|1=

Ivohibe is a district in south-eastern Madagascar. It's a part of Ihorombe Region and borders the districts of Ambalavao in north, Vondrozo in east, Midongy Sud in south, Iakora in southwest and Ihosy in west. The area is [tpl]convert|4119|km2|sqmi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] and the population was estimated to be 34,289 in 2001.[ref]
</ref>
The district is further divided into 4 communes.
