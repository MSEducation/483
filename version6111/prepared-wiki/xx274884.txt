[[Over the Air]]

CATEGORIES: BBC New Media, 2008 conferences, 2009 conferences, 2010 conferences, 2011 conferences, 2012 conferences, Hacking (programmer subculture), Meetings, Mobile phone culture, Mobile software development, Mobile telecommunications, Software development events, Telephony, Unconferences, Vodafone

Over the Air is an annual mobile technology-focused overnight hack day event held in London since 2008.[tpl]Cite journal|last=Kiss |first=Jemima |date=[tpl]Date|2008-04-03[/tpl] |title=Mobile developers gather for London festival |url=http://www.theguardian.com/media/2008/apr/03/digitalmedia.mobilephones |journal=The Guardian[/tpl]  It developed from previous hack day events in 2007 and 2008.  The 2011 version of Over the Air was part of the 2011 London Mobile Week.[tpl]cite web|author=LondonMobileWeek |url=http://londonmobileweek.com/ |title=Mobile Content for Everybody |publisher=LondonMobileWeek |date= |accessdate=2011-10-05[/tpl][tpl]cite web|author=BlueVia |url=http://www.theguardian.com/technology/appsblog/2011/sep/22/london-mobile-week-blue-via |title=London to become the mobile centre of the world – for a week |publisher=the Guardian |date=2011-09-22 |accessdate=2011-10-05[/tpl]
From 2008 to 2010, the event was held at Imperial College in South Kensington.[tpl]cite web|last=Smith |first=Colin |date=[tpl]Date|2008-04-10[/tpl] |title=Over the Air: Imperial College hosts a leading technology festival |url=http://www3.imperial.ac.uk/newsandeventspggrp/imperialcollege/newssummary/news_10-4-2008-9-9-19 |publisher=Imperial College London[/tpl][tpl]cite web|author=Matthew Postgate |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/blogs/legacy/bbcinternet/2008/04/over_the_air_mobile_going_main_1.html |title=Over The Air: Mobile Going Mainstream |publisher=BBC |date=2008-04-10 |accessdate=2011-10-05[/tpl][tpl]cite news|last=Spence |first=Ewan |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/8332665.stm |title=How to hack a handset |publisher=BBC |date=2009-10-30 |accessdate=2011-10-05[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://thefonecast.com/Home/tabid/61/ArticleID/3248/ArtMID/538/Default.aspx |title=Over The Air 2010 – one man's summary for the wider mobile industry |publisher=thefonecast.com |date= |accessdate=2011-10-05[/tpl] From 2011 to 2013, it was held at Bletchley Park. There was no Over the Air event in 2014.
The ethos of Over the Air is simple[tpl]cite web|author= |url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oQ-maSNfzgA |title=What is Over The Air |publisher=YouTube |date=2010-09-01 |accessdate=2011-10-05[/tpl] — a 2 day event, including practical and educational talks and a hacking competition.  Sponsors of the event have included the BBC, Bluevia,[tpl]cite web|url=http://blog.bluevia.com/tag/over-the-air/ |title=Over the Air |publisher=Blog.bluevia.com |date=2011-09-22 |accessdate=2011-10-05[/tpl][tpl]dead link|date=April 2014[/tpl] Nokia,[tpl]cite web|url=http://overtheair.org/blog/tag/nokia/ |title=nokia « Over the Air |publisher=Overtheair.org |date=2008-04-14 |accessdate=2011-10-05[/tpl] PayPal,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ewanspence.com/blog/category/conferences/2010-conferences/over-the-air-2010-conferences-conferences/ |title=Over the Air : All New Musings |publisher=Ewanspence.com |date=2010-10-21 |accessdate=2011-10-05[/tpl] and Vodafone.[tpl]cite web|url=http://overtheair.org/blog/2011/08/27/vodafone-are-a-sponsor-of-ota11/ |title=Vodafone are a Sponsor of OTA11 « Over the Air |publisher=Overtheair.org |date= |accessdate=2011-10-05[/tpl] Some of the ideas developed at Over the Air have been turned into commercial services.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.co.uk/2011/09/16/hackers-get-hired-at-blet_n_966246.html |title=Hackers Get Hired At Bletchley Park |publisher=Huffingtonpost.co.uk |date=2011-09-16 |accessdate=2011-10-05[/tpl]
