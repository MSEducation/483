[[Kumna]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Harju County, Kreis Harrien

Kumna is a village in Harku Parish, Harju County in northern Estonia. It has a population of 290 (as of 1 June 2010).[tpl]cite web|title=Üldinfo|url=http://www.harku.ee/index.php|publisher=Harku vald[/tpl]
Kumna has been the site of a manor house since the 17th century. The current manor house was built in 1913 in a neoclassical style.[tpl]cite book | last = Sakk | first = Ivar | authorlink = | title = Estonian Manors - A Travelogue | publisher = Sakk & Sakk OÜ | series = | volume = | edition = | date = 2004 | location = Tallinn | page = 32 | language = | url = | doi = | id = | isbn = 9949-10-117-4 | mr = | zbl = | jfm = [/tpl]
In 1865 a silver hoard from the 13th century B.C. was found in Kumna.[tpl]Citation needed|date=December 2012[/tpl]
