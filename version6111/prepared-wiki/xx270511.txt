[[Nenápadná]]

CATEGORIES: 1986 singles, Marika Gombitová songs, Songs written by Ján Lehotský, Songs written by Kamil Peteraj

"Nenápadná" ([tpl]lang-en|Inconspicuous[/tpl]) is a song by Marika Gombitová released on OPUS in 1986.[tpl]harvnb|Graclík|Nekvapil|2008|p=393.[/tpl]For Marika Gombitová's discography, see [tpl]harvnb|Lehotský|2008a|pp=54–56.[/tpl].
The composition, written by Gombitová with Kamil Peteraj, being released with "Mami, mami" on B-side. For the first time on CD, both songs were attached to the 96's re-release of the singer's album Ateliér duše as bonus tracks.[tpl]harvnb|Graclík|Nekvapil|2008|p=401.[/tpl]
