[[SlideRocket]]

CATEGORIES: Web software, Web applications, Rich Internet applications, Cloud applications, Cloud storage, Presentation software, VMware

SlideRocket is an online presentation platform that lets users create, manage, share and measure presentations. SlideRocket is provided via a SaaS model. The company was acquired by ClearSlide, a similar SaaS application, in March 2013.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.sliderocket.com/blog/2013/03/acquired-by-clearslide/|title=Big News, SlideRocket Has Been Acquired by ClearSlide![/tpl] They are no longer offering independent signups, as the platform is being integrated into ClearSlide.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.sliderocket.com/blog/2013/06/integration-in-progress/|title=No New Signups: Integration In Progress[/tpl]

==History==

SlideRocket was founded in Jan 2006, and launched as a private beta[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.techcrunch.com/2008/03/19/sliderocket-presentation-web-app-enters-private-beta-get-your-invite-here-2/|title=TechCrunch article on SlideRocket - SlideRocket Presentation Web App Enters Private Beta[/tpl][tpl]cite news|url=http://scobleizer.com/2008/03/20/vc-admits-he-hates-boring-powerpoints/|title=Scobeleizer article on SlideRocket - VC admits he hates boring PowerPoints[/tpl] in March 2008 at the Under The Radar Spring event. A public beta was announced in September 2008[tpl]cite news|url=http://blogs.zdnet.com/Stewart/?p=951|title=ZDNET article on SlideRocket - SlideRocket opens up to the public[/tpl] followed shortly by public release[tpl]cite news|url=http://news.cnet.com/8301-10787_3-10045587-60.html|title=CNET article on SlideRocket - SlideRocket isn't yet PowerPoint's undoing. But it might be.[/tpl][tpl]cite news|url=http://www.techcrunch.com/2008/10/28/sliderockets-impressive-online-presentation-app-hits-general-release/|title=TechCrunch article on SlideRocket - SlideRocket’s Impressive Online Presentation App Hits General Release[/tpl] on October 28, 2008. SlideRocket is most commonly credited with inventing the PResuMÉ or Presentation Résumé in early 2009.
On April 26, 2011 SlideRocket was acquired by VMware.[tpl]cite web|title=VMware Buys Another Slice of the Cloud With SlideRocket|url=http://www.pcworld.com/businesscenter/article/226552/vmware_buys_another_slice_of_the_cloud_with_sliderocket.html[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=VMware acquires SlideRocket|url=http://www.sliderocket.com/launch/vmware_sliderocket.html[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=VMware Acquires SlideRocket Collaborative Presentation Provider|url=http://www.vmware.com/company/news/releases/vmw-sliderocket-042611.html[/tpl]  On March 5, 2013 VMware sold SlideRocket to ClearSlide.[tpl]cite web|title=ClearSlide Acquires SlideRocket|url=http://blogs.vmware.com/vmware/2013/03/clearslide-acquires-sliderocket.html[/tpl]
SlideRocket is based in San Francisco.
