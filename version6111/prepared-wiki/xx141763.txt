[[Mosdeng]]

CATEGORIES: Tripuri cuisine

Mosdeng is a very popular Tripuri food Cultural Exchange between North East and South East Asia: East meet East made of chillies.[tpl]citation needed|date=January 2011[/tpl]
Moso means Chille and deng is to mix by pressing. Mosodeng is prepared by mixing raw chillies with a dried fish called Berama and meats and vegetables.[tpl]citation needed|date=January 2011[/tpl]
