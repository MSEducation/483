[[The Monarch (production team)]]

CATEGORIES: American record producers, People from Orlando, Florida

[tpl]refimprove|date=December 2013[/tpl]

The Monarch is a grammy nominated music production duo from Orlando, Florida, consisting of Andre "Dre" Davidson and Sean "Sean D" Davidson.  They are best known for producing "Ready" and "Take It to the Head" by Fabolous and DJ Khaled respectively, as well as How We Do (Party) by Rita Ora, and Cher Lloyd's debut single Swagger Jagger which both debuted at #1 on the UK Singles Chart[tpl]Cite web|url=http://stupiddope.com/2011/08/08/cher-lloyd-tops-the-uk-singles-chart-with-swagger-jagger/ |title=Cher Lloyd Tops The UK Singles Chart with "Swagger Jagger" |publisher=http://stupiddope.com/ |date= |accessdate=2011-09-23[/tpl]http://www.allmusic.com/album/ready-mw0002483826/credits  The Monarch has also produced several other songs including "Kiss and Tell" by Justin Bieber, "What I Do" by Chris Brown featuring Plies, "Thinkin' About You" by Mario, "A Million Lights" by DJ Khaled featuring Kevin Rudolf, Tyga, Mack Maine, Jae Millz & Cory Gunz, "We On" by Ace Hood, "Biggest Fan" by Chris Brown, "Him" by Tamia, "Take It to the Head" by DJ Khaled featuring Chris Brown, Rick Ross, Nicki Minaj & Lil Wayne, "Piss Em Off" by Ace Hood and "Lay Back" by Rick Ross featuring Robin Thicke [tpl]Cite web|url=http://firmmagazine.net/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=143:official-introduction-the-monarch&catid=37:music&Itemid=56 |title=Official Introduction: The Monarch |publisher=http://firmmagazine.net/ |date= |accessdate=2011-09-23[/tpl]http://www.allmusic.com/artist/sean-davidson-mn0002157911http://www.allmusic.com/artist/andre-davidson-mn0002610506
