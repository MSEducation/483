[[Pampas XV]]

CATEGORIES: Argentine rugby union teams, South African rugby union teams

Pampas XV is an Argentine rugby union team that was based in South Africa when they participated in the South African second domestic competition, the Vodacom Cup between 2010 and 2013.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.uar.com.ar/equipos_uar/default.asp?idcategoria=9 | title=Equipos UAR / Pampas XV | publisher=uar.com.ar | accessdate=23 September 2011[/tpl] The team was established in 2010 and was composed almost entirely by members of the High Performance Plan of the UAR. It was an unofficial third national team, below the Pumas and Jaguars,[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.26noticias.com.ar/pampas-xv-el-equipo-nacional-que-nos-representa-en-un-torneo-sudafricano-168060.html | title=Pampas XV, el equipo nacional que nos representa en un torneo sudáfricano | publisher=26 Noticias | accessdate=19 November 2013 | language=Spanish[/tpl] although it has been described as the second team as well.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.rugbynews.com.uy/divisiones/seleccion/frutos-del-plan/ | title=Frutos del Plan | publisher=Rugby News | accessdate=19 November 2013 | author=Astesiano, Eugenio | language=Spanish[/tpl]
In November 2013, Pampas XV withdrew from the Vodacom Cup thus not competing in the 2014 Vodacom Cup competition due to financial considerations.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sport24.co.za/Rugby/Pampas-out-of-Vodacom-Cup-20131119 | title=Pampas out of Vodacom Cup | publisher=Sport24 | date=19 November 2013 | accessdate=19 November 2013[/tpl] However, in December 2013, it was annoucned that the Argentine third team would join the Pacific Rugby Cup, where they would face the likes of Fiji Warriors, Samoa A, Tonga A, Junior Japan plus Australian Super Rugby Academy Teams.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.uar.com.ar/noticias/noticias.asp?idinfo=2455 | title=Plantel de Seniors para la temporada 2014 | publisher=UAR.com | date=27 December 2013 | accessdate=27 December 2013[/tpl]

==Vodacom Cup==

The team played in the Southern Section of the Vodacom Cup. In 2010, these teams had to play against other teams in the Southern Section, so the Pampas XV were based in the Stellenbosch area. However, in 2011 all the teams in the Southern Section had to play against teams in the Northern Section, so they relocated to [tpl]Rus Olën Park[/tpl]. They played their home mtaches during the 2012 and 2013 competitions at the A.F. Markötter Stadium at Paul Roos Gymnasium in Stellenbosch.

===Seasons===

====2010 season====

The Pampas XV were based in Stellenbosch for their first season, their first season was average though, winning 3 matches and losing as many whilst drawing one match. They finished fifth in their conference and failed to qualify for the knockout rounds.

====2011 season====

For the Pampas XV's second season, they moved to Potchefstroom. The Pampas XV went through the season undefeated and won the Vodacom Cup. They passed 40 points in 5 of their 11 matches. Juan Imhoff finished the season as the tournament's top try scorer.

==Pacific Rugby Cup==

In December 2013 under the new coach of Martín Gaitán (formally Daniel Hourcade), it was annoucned that the Argentine third team would join the Pacific Rugby Cup, where they would face the likes of Fiji Warriors, Samoa A, Tonga A, Junior Japan plus Australian Super Rugby academy team. The team won the tournament winning all matches.
