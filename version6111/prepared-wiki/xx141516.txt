[[Dagdi Chawl]]

CATEGORIES: Neighbourhoods in Mumbai

Dagdi Chawl is an erstwhile slum neighbourhood in Byculla, Mumbai, used to house mill workers from the nearby industries.  Now it is known for the fortified home of mafia don turned politician Arun Gawli.[tpl]cite web | url=http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2012-09-01/mumbai/33534848_1_arun-gawli-life-imprisonment-dagdi-chawl | title=Ex-MLA Arun Gawli goes from Dagdi Chawl to life in jail | publisher=Times of India | date=1 September 2012 | accessdate=7 June 2013 | author=Samervel, Rebecca[/tpl]
