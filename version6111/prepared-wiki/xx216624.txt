[[Headstrong Games]]

CATEGORIES: Companies based in London, British companies established in 2008, Video game companies of the United Kingdom, Video game development companies, Technology companies established in 2008

Headstrong Games is a subsidiary of Kuju Entertainment based in London that creates video games. It is most well known for its work on Battalion Wars, The House of the Dead: OVERKILL and the Art Academy series.

==History==

The studio was formed in 2000 as Kuju London. In 2008 it was rebranded as Headstrong Games,[tpl]cite web|author=July 29, 2008 3:21PM BST |url=http://www.mcvuk.com/press-releases/read/kuju-london-renamed-headstrong/060836 |title=Kuju London renamed Headstrong. |publisher=mcvuk.com |date=2008-07-29 |accessdate=2013-10-28[/tpl] with a focus on continuing to create character action titles. Ian Baverstock, CEO at the time, said "Headstrong is the final piece in the puzzle for Kuju's offering in the UK. As action specialists they complement the other studios that have already rebranded and together offer publishers a full service solution to their development needs." 
In 2013 Headstrong games made the Develop 100, a list of the top 100 developers within the UK based upon their commercial and critical success, reputation, and potential.[tpl]cite web|author=July 26, 2013 1:00PM BST |url=http://content.yudu.com/A28qw0/Dev1002013/resources/index.htm?referrerUrl= |title= Develop 100 |publisher=Develop |date=2013-07-26 |accessdate=2013-11-11[/tpl]

==Work==

While Headstrong Games has a large catalogue of games, in recent years it has focused on games for the Nintendo family of consoles including The House of the Dead: OVERKILL and  The Lord of the Rings: Aragorn's Quest for the Nintendo Wii and Art Academy, New Art Academy and  Rabbids Rumble for the DS and 3DS respectively.
The studio's latest release, Art Academy: Sketchpad, was announced at E3 2013 during 'Nintendo Direct' as an exclusively downloadable title for the WiiU. Art Academy: Sketchpad launched on 9 August 2013.
