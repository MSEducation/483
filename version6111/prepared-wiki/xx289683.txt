[[Chalakhvor]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Chalakhvor ([tpl]lang-fa|چلاخور[/tpl], also Romanized as Chalākhvor and Chalakhur; also known as Chīlākhowr, Chīlākhvor, and Galakhur)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3057763|Chalakhvor[/tpl] is a village in Sojas Rud Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 288, in 66 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
