[[Qezlar Bolaghi]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Qezlar Bolaghi ([tpl]lang-fa|قزلاربلاغي[/tpl], also Romanized as Qezlār Bolāghī)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795921|Qezlar Bolaghi[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 68, in 13 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
