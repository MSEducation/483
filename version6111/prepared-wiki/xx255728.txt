[[Ješovec pri Kozjem]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Municipality of Kozje

Ješovec pri Kozjem is a dispersed settlement in the hills east of Kozje in eastern Slovenia. The area is part of the historical Lower Styria region. The Municipality of Kozje is now included in the Savinja statistical region.Kozje municipal site

==Name==

The name of the settlement was changed from Ješovec to Ješovec pri Kozjem in 1953.Spremembe naselij 1948–95. 1996. Database. Ljubljana: Geografski inštitut ZRC SAZU, DZS.

==Cultural heritage==

A small chapel-shrine in the settlement dates to 1820.Slovenian Ministry of Culture register of national heritage reference number ešd 22442
