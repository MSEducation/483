[[Kitaaiki, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Kitaaiki|北相木村|Kitaaiki-mura[/tpl] is a village located in Minamisaku District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of October 1, 2007, the village has an estimated population of 869 and a density of 15.4 persons per km². The total area is 56.26 km².
