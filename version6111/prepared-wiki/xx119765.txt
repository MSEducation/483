[[Andrew Moss]]

CATEGORIES: 1984 births, Living people, English male television actors

Andrew "Andy" Moss (born 1 June 1984) is an English television actor.  He is currently best known for playing Rhys Ashworth in the Channel 4  soap opera Hollyoaks from 2005 to 2012.

==TV credits==

Hollyoaks is Moss's first regular television role, though he had previously played Craig Gutteridge in the BBC drama series Cutting It, in addition to minor roles in Merseybeat, Doctors and two episodes of Coronation Street. In 2013 he starred in one episode of Casualty
Moss trained at the North Cheshire Theatre College, where he earned a Diploma in Drama.  He also attended Sheena SimonB College, where he also obtained a National Diploma in Musical Theatre.

==Music==
