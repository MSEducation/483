Gustav "Guzzi" Lantschner (August 12, 1910 â€“ March 19, 2011) was an Austrian-born German alpine skier turned actor. He competed in the 1936 Winter Olympics. 
He was born in Innsbruck, Austria and is the younger brother of Hellmut Lantschner. Competing for Austria, he won the downhill world championship in 1932. At the 1936 Winter Olympics, he won the silver medal in the alpine skiing combined event.
