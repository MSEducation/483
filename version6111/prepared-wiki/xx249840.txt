[[Antz Racing]]

CATEGORIES: 2001 video games, Game Boy Color games, Karting video games, Racing video games, Antz video games

Antz Racing is a kart racing game developed by RFX Interactive and published by Electronic Arts for the Game Boy Colorhttp://uk.gamespot.com/gbc/driving/antzracing/index.html in 2001. The gameplay is similar to Mario Kart. The game has 3 racing modes, Quick Race, 4 Seasons, and Head to Head.
