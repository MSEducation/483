[[Carlo de Reuver]]

CATEGORIES: 1995 births, Living people, Dutch footballers, SBV Excelsior players, Sportspeople from Rotterdam

Carlo de Reuver (born 29 January 1995 in Rotterdam) is a Dutch professional footballer who plays as striker for Eerste Divisie side SBV Excelsior. He is also known for his nickname, El Dromedario, the Spanish word for dromedary. He has yet to score his first official goal.Carlo de Reuver profile on the official Excelsior website
