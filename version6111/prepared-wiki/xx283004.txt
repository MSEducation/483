[[Take One (song)]]

CATEGORIES: 1998 songs

"Take One" is a song by Korean musician Seo Taiji, the second track from his solo debut, Seo Tai Ji. While only four minutes in length, a video was made for the song that is approximately eight minutes. The video become very popular and was used as the opening in Taiji's return to Korea concert. The song appeared on the 2012 Taiji compilation Seotaiji & 20.
