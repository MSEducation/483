[[Joey Massey]]

CATEGORIES: 2000 births, Australian male child actors, Australian male film actors, Australian male television actors, Living people, People from the Gold Coast, Queensland

Joseph "Joey" Massey (born 25 February 2000) is an Australian actor.
Massey was born in Gold Coast, Australia. His first role was in the 2006 film Aquamarine. He also appeared in 2007's The Condemned. In 2006 and 2007, he appeared in the television series H2O: Just Add Water.
He is the son of Anna Waters Massey and the brother of Cleo Massey.
