[[Mikkel Johannesen Borge]]

CATEGORIES: 1791 births, Year of death missing, Members of the Parliament of Norway, Hordaland politicians

Mikkel Johannesen Borge (1791 - ??) was a Norwegian politician.
He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament in 1836, 1839 and 1842, representing the rural constituency of Søndre Bergenhus Amt (today named Hordaland). He worked as a farmer and bailiff.Mikkel Johannesen Borge — Norwegian Social Science Data Services (NSD)
