[[Andrew Gunn (film producer)]]

CATEGORIES: Canadian film producers, Living people, 1969 births, People from Toronto

Andrew Gunn (born July 15, 1967 in Toronto, Ontario, Canada)http://www.imdb.com is a film producer. His production company, Gunn Films, was started in 2001. He has worked with Walt Disney Pictures to produce movies such as Freaky Friday and Sky High.
Gunn Films' latest release is Disney's Race to Witch Mountain, a re-imagining of the classic 1975 film Escape to Witch Mountain, starring Dwayne Johnson.
