[[Lake Charles Transit]]

CATEGORIES: Bus transportation in Louisiana, Transit agencies in Louisiana, Lake Charles, Louisiana

Lake Charles Transit is the operator of public transportation in Lake Charles, Calcasieu Parish, Louisiana. Five routes operate 5 days per week, each consisting of an approximately 55 minute journey in full. Currently, the city's downtown bus terminal is under re-construction, and the main hub as at the Amtrak station.
