[[Enforce (game engine)]]

CATEGORIES: 2012 software, Proprietary software, Video game engines, Video gaming in the Czech Republic

Enforce is a proprietary computer game engine developed by Black Element Software and now Bohemia Interactive. Where it was used first is unknown, however Enforce was used for Alpha Prime. Bohemia Interactive restarted the use of this game engine with Carrier Command: Gaea Mission in 2012. Later in 2013 it appeared again to develop Take On Mars;[tpl]cite web|url=http://forums.bistudio.com/showthread.php?157061-Take-On-Mars-Announced-at-E3&p=2417308&viewfull=1#post2417308|title=Engine being used.|date=June 18, 2013|accessdate=August 9, 2013[/tpl] which was started around the time Carrier Command was released.
