[[Breakfast at Fatboys]]

CATEGORIES: 2004 debut albums, Butterfingers (Australian band) albums

Breakfast at Fatboys is the debut album by Australian hip hop group Butterfingers, released on 3 May 2004 on the group's own Valley Trash label and distributed by MGM.ARIA releases (May 2004)
The album reached #61 on the ARIA album charts,ARIA Chart, 21 June 2004. #15 on the ARIA Australian Release charts[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.accessallareas.net.au/data/Brisvegas_rock_outfit_Butterfingers_signs_with_Festival_Mushroom.php |title=Brisvegas rock outfit, Butterfingers, signs with Festival Mushroom!|publisher=Access All Areas.net.au|date=2005-03-15|accessdate=2008-12-11[/tpl] [tpl]Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot[/tpl] and #2 on the AIR (Australian Independent Record Labels Association) Charts  where it remained in the top twenty of the AIR charts for over a year following its release.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.warnermusic.com.au/artist,w_artist,115378 |title=Artist Profile: Butterfingers|publisher=Warner Music Australia |accessdate=2008-12-11[/tpl] [tpl]Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot[/tpl] Triple J named it 'Album of the Week' upon its release and added the new single, "Yo Mama" to high rotation at the station.rage guest programmer profile - Butterfingers The album was also nominated for the Best Independent Release ARIA Award.Butterfingers at the ARIA Music Awards website
