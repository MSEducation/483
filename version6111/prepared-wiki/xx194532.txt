[[San Jerónimo District, Cusco]]

San Jerónimo District is one of eight districts of the province Cusco in Peru.
[tpl]es icon[/tpl] Instituto Nacional de Estadística e Informática. Banco de Información Distrital. Retrieved April 11, 2008.  It is home to the Univerisidad Andina del Cusco.  According to the 2007 census, there are 31,687 residents in the district.  A large part of the population communicates in Quechua.
