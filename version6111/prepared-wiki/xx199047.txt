[[Murray Criddle]]

CATEGORIES: 1943 births, Living people, People from Geraldton, Western Australian National politicians, Members of the Western Australian Legislative Council

Murray John Criddle (born 11 December 1943) is an Australian politician. He was a Nationals member of the Western Australian Legislative Council from 1993 to 2008, representing the region of Agricultural.
Criddle was born in Geraldton, Western Australia.
