[[Yoshio Inaba]]

CATEGORIES: 1920 births, 1998 deaths, Deaths from myocardial infarction, People from Chiba Prefecture, Japanese actors

[tpl]nihongo|Yoshio Inaba|稲葉 義男|Inaba Yoshio|July 15, 1920–April 20, 1998[/tpl] was a Japanese actor best known for his role as one of the title characters (the good-natured, second-in-command Gorobei) in Akira Kurosawa's Seven Samurai. He died of a heart attack at the age of 77.
