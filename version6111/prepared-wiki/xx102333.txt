[[Soulphonic Soundsystem]]

CATEGORIES: Electronica music groups

Soulphonic Soundsystem is a U.S.-based bicoastal production team of DJ Santo and Chauncey Canfield.  Their broken beat style incorporates jazz and Brazilian influences with dance-oriented downtempo and nu-jazz elements.  Their improvised live sets utilize both real and electronic instruments with DJ. Contemporary influences include Fila Brazillia, Kruder and Dorfmeister, Dzihan & Kamien, and Bugz in the Attic.
