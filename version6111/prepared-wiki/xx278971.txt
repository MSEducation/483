[[Chapar, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Chapar ([tpl]lang-fa|چپر[/tpl])[tpl]GEOnet3|-3803208|Chapar[/tpl] is a village in Chaypareh-ye Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 91, in 18 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
