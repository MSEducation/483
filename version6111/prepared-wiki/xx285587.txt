Robert de Bruyn (born 26 February 1991 in Johannesburg) is a South African rugby union player, currently playing with the [tpl]Rut Golden Lions[/tpl].[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/playerprofile.aspx?id=43348 | title=SARU Player Profile Robert de Bruyn | work=South African Rugby Union | accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl] His regular position is centre.

==Career==

===Youth===

He played for the [tpl]Rut Natal Sharks[/tpl] at the 2007 Grant Khomo Week and 2008 Craven Week tournaments and was also included in the 2008 Under-18 Elite squad.
He then returned to Johannesburg and played for the [tpl]Rut Golden Lions U19[/tpl] team in 2009 and 2010 and for the [tpl]Rut Golden Lions U21[/tpl] team in 2011 and 2012.
