[[Mike Kenny (writer)]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, British dramatists and playwrights

Mike Kenny is a British playwright, most noted for his works in young people's theatre.  His works include the 2008 adaptation of E Nesbit's The Railway Children at the National Railway Museum in York. The first production, directed by Damian Cruden, starred Sarah Quintrell, Colin Tarrant, Marshall Lancaster, Elizabeth Keates, Marcus Brigstocke and featured a working Stirling Single (GNR 4-2-2 No.1) steam locomotive on a real rail track.
