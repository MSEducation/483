[[William Lake Provincial Park]]

CATEGORIES: Parks in Manitoba

William Lake Provincial Park is a provincial park located in the Rural Municipality of Morton, in southeast Manitoba on Turtle Mountain. Its sister park, the much larger Turtle Mountain Provincial Park, lies to the west.
William Lake has a campground, a beach, and hiking trails. William Lake is home to the Turtle's Back trail, which is known for The Turtle's Back (from the Turtle and the Snake stories).
