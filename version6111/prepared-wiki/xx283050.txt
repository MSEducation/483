[[LEVEL 5 -judgelight-]]

CATEGORIES: 2010 singles, A Certain Magical Index, Television theme songs

"LEVEL 5 -judgelight-" is the 2nd single released by the second generation of fripSide. It was released on February 17, 2010 under the label Geneon. The track is used as is the second opening theme for the TV anime A Certain Scientific Railgun. This is fripSide's second tie-in with the said anime series.
