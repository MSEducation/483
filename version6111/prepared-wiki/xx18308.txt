[[FilmLight]]

CATEGORIES: Film and video technology

Established in 2002, FilmLight design, create and manufacture unique colour grading systems, image processing applications and workflow tools for the film and television industry.
The company is headquartered in London with offices around the world.

==Products==

FilmLight’s first product was the Northlight film scanner,Northlight film scanner a motion picture film scanner capable of scanning 16, 35 or 65 mm film formats. It is commonly used for feature film effects, Digital Intermediate and archiving. It then followed with the BaselightBaselight colour grading system and the TruelightTruelight colour management system.
Today, the product range includes:
Deskside grading system with 2TB SSD cache only or 24TB with 2TB SSD cache.
Turnkey grading system.
Multi GPU/multi-node grading systems.
End-to-end dailies system (coming soon).
Control surface for Baselight systems.
Compact grading panel for FilmLight systems based on the same technology as Blackboard 2.
On-set preview system.
Streaming media storage and data management.
Same core toolset as the full Baselight systems inside Final Cut Pro 7, Avid and NUKE.
