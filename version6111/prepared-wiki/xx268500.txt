[[Central District (Khomeyni Shahr County)]]

CATEGORIES: Khomeyni Shahr County, Districts of Isfahan Province

The Central District of Khomeyni Shahr County ([tpl]lang-fa|بخش مرکزی شهرستان خمینی‌شهر[/tpl]) is a district (bakhsh) in Khomeyni Shahr County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 282,888, in 75,305 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|10[/tpl]  The District has three cities: Khomeyni Shahr, Kushk, and Dorcheh Piaz. The District has two rural districts (dehestan): Marbin-e Olya Rural District and Marbin-e Vosta Rural District.
