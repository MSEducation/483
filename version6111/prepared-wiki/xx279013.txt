[[Shokurchi]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Shokurchi ([tpl]lang-fa|شكورچي[/tpl], also Romanized as Shokūrchī; also known as Shokorchī)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3804254|Shokurchi[/tpl] is a village in Chaypareh-ye Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 251, in 59 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
