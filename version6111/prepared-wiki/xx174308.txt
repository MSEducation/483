[[WGAC (AM)]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Augusta, Georgia, News and talk radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 1981

WGAC (580 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a news radio format. Licensed to Augusta, Georgia, United States, the station is currently owned by Beasley Broadcast Group. WGAC broadcasts games of the Augusta GreenJackets, the local minor league team.
