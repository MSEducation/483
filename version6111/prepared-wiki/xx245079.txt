[[Forum 66]]

CATEGORIES: Buildings and structures in Shenyang, Skyscrapers over 350 meters, Buildings and structures under construction in China, Hang Lung Group, Skyscrapers in China, Kohn Pedersen Fox buildings

Forum 66 ([tpl]zh|s=市府恒隆广场[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|t=市府恆隆廣場[/tpl]) is a twin tower complex currently under construction in Shenyang, China. The complex consists of two supertall skyscrapers; Tower 1 will be [tpl]convert|384|m|ft|abbr=on[/tpl] with 76 floors and Tower 2 will be [tpl]convert|351|m|ft|abbr=on[/tpl] with 68 floors.[tpl]cite web|url=http://skyscraperpage.com/cities/?buildingID=69152|title=Forum 66|publisher=SkyscraperPage|accessdate=8 January 2010[/tpl] The towers are expected to be completed by 2013.
The buildings are developed by Hang Lung Properties and share their Chinese name with other projects of Hang Lung, like Plaza 66.
