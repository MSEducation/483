[[Little Black River (Saint John River)]]

CATEGORIES: Tributaries of the Saint John River (Bay of Fundy), Rivers of Aroostook County, Maine, Geography of Bas-Saint-Laurent, International rivers of North America, Rivers of Maine

The Little Black River (French: Rivière Noire) is a river in Quebec and northern Maine. From its source ([tpl]coord|47.413|-69.517 |format=dms |type:river_region:CA-QC |name= Rivière Noire source[/tpl]), in Témiscouata RCM, Quebec, the river runs east and southeast across the Canada – United States border in Maine Township 19, Range 12, WELS, to the Saint John River at Dickey, Maine.
