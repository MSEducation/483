[[Challace McMillin]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, James Madison Dukes football coaches, James Madison University faculty, Rhodes Lynx football players, 1942 births

Challace Joe McMillin (born 1942) was the first head coach of James Madison University's football program. He coached from 1972 to 1984 and compiled a 67–60–2 record. McMillin continues to work for the Dukes as a Sport Psychologist.
