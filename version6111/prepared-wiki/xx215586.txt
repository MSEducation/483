[[Huta, Augustów County]]

Huta [tpl]IPAc-pl|'|h|u|t|a[/tpl], ([tpl]lang-lt|Ūta[/tpl]), is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Sztabin, within Augustów County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|7|km|mi|0[/tpl] north-west of Sztabin, [tpl]convert|16|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-east of Augustów, and [tpl]convert|68|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of the regional capital Białystok.
