[[Mount Kurlak]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of the Ross Dependency, Shackleton Coast

Mount Kurlak is an ice-covered mountain [tpl]convert|3|mi|km|1[/tpl] south-east of Mount Bell in the Queen Alexandra Range of Antarctica. It was named by the Advisory Committee on Antarctic Names for Lt. Cdr. William B. Kurlak, USN, aircraft commander during USN Operation Deep Freeze, 1964.
