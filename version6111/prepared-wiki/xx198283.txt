[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Uberlândia]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1961, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Uberaba, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century, 1961 establishments in Brazil

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Uberlândia ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Fertiliensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Uberlândia in the Ecclesiastical province of Uberaba in Brazil.
