[[2011 World Amateur Boxing Championships – Light welterweight]]

CATEGORIES: 2011 World Amateur Boxing Championships

The Light welterweight competition was the fifth lightest class featured at the 2011 World Amateur Boxing Championships, held at the Heydar Aliyev Sports and Exhibition Complex. Boxers were limited to a maximum of 64 kilograms in body mass.
