[[Mount Morappu]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of Hokkaido

[tpl]nihongo|Mount Morappu|モラップ山|Morappu-san[/tpl] is a mountain located in Shikotsu-Toya National Park in Hokkaidō, Japan. It sits on the shore of Lake Shikotsu, a caldera lake. It also hosts a nationally established ski slope.Geographical Survey Institute website, 25000:1 maps  樽前山 and 支笏湖温泉
