[[Lerum Municipality]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Västra Götaland County, Municipalities of Sweden, North Älvsborg, Metropolitan Gothenburg

Lerum Municipality (Lerums kommun) is a municipality in Västra Götaland County in western Sweden, situated just east of Gothenburg. Its seat is located in the town of Lerum.
In 1969 the municipality got its present size when "old" Lerum was amalgamated with Skallsjö and Stora Lundby.
In 2007, Lerum Municipality launched a new logotype to used instead of its official municipal coat of arms which features the head of an oxen and three oak leaves.Lerum Municipality: Lerums logotyp, accessed on February 16, 2009 [tpl]sv icon[/tpl] Municipal officials and PR staff felt the coat "outdated".
