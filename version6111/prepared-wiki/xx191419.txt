[[Yargatenga Department]]

CATEGORIES: Departments of Burkina Faso, Koulpélogo Province

Yargatenga is a department (or commune) of Koulpélogo Province in eastern Burkina Faso. Its capital lies at the town of Yargatenga. According to the 1996 census, the department has a total population of 40,660.Burkinabé government inforoute communale
