[[Ram Chandra Poudel]]

CATEGORIES: Members of the Nepalese Constituent Assembly, Nepali Congress politicians, Speakers of the Nepal House of Representatives, Government ministers of Nepal, Living people

Ram Chandra Poudel or Ram Chandra Paudel is a Nepalese politician, belonging to the Nepali Congress. In the 2008 Constituent Assembly election he was elected from the Tanahu-2 constituency, winning 18970 votes.[ref]Election Commission of Nepal 
Interview with Ram Chandra Poudel[/ref] 
He was elected as the Leader of Parliamentary Party of the Nepali Congress on June 20, 2009 by securing 61 votes against Sher Bahadur Deuba, a former Prime Minister who secured 48 votes.
Poudel is also Vice President of Nepali Congress Party.
