[[Julia Mena]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Vice Presidents of Nicaragua, Members of the National Assembly of Nicaragua, Independent Liberal Party (Nicaragua) politicians, Nicaraguan women in politics

Julia de la Cruz Mena Rivera is a Nicaraguan politician.

==Background and earlier career==

Her profession was as a school teacher.
She was a deputy in National Assembly of Nicaragua and also served as first vice president of National Assembly.

===Political affiliation===

She is a member of the Independent Liberal Party party.

==Vice President of Nicaragua==

In 1995 National Assembly elected her as Vice President following resignation of Virgilio Godoy for the remainder of the term until January 1997.History of Vicepresidency
