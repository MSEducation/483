[[Zoltán Káldy]]

CATEGORIES: 1969 births, Living people, Hungarian long-distance runners, Athletes (track and field) at the 1992 Summer Olympics, Athletes (track and field) at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Olympic athletes of Hungary, People from Győr

Zoltán Káldy (born 7 January 1969 in Győr, Győr-Moson-Sopron) is a retired Hungarian long-distance runner who specialized in the 10,000 metres.
