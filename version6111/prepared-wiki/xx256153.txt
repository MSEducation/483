[[Ottran]]

CATEGORIES: 2003 films, Tamil films of 2003, Indian films, Tamil-language films

Ottran ([tpl]lang-en|A Spy[/tpl]) is a 2003 Tamil film starring Arjun Sarja, Simran, Manorama, Vadivelu, Tejashree, Sarath Babu and directed by Ilankannan. The score and soundtrack was composed by Pravin Mani. The film becomes hit at box office.[tpl]cite news|last=Pillai|first=Sreedhar|url=http://www.hindu.com/mp/2003/12/29/stories/2003122900170100.htm|title= Reel of fortune |accessdate=2013-09-06|date=2003-12-29|newspaper=The Hindu|location=Chennai, India[/tpl]

==Production==

After the Arjun-directed Ezhumalai, the Arjun-Simran pair come together in yet another film titled ‘Ottran’. Directing the film is first-timer Ilankannan, who had apprenticed with director Shanker. Arjun plays a secret agent in the film, which gives him scope for enough of action scenes. Shooting commenced in Chennai in a forty-day schedule.http://web.archive.org/web/20030210121457/http://www.chennaionline.com/location/index.asp
