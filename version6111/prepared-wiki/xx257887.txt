[[Prem Lata Sharma]]

CATEGORIES: Articles created via the Article Wizard, Indian musicologists, Year of birth missing, Year of death missing

Prem Lata Sharma was a musicologist, scholar and educator. She was born in Punjab and completed her doctorate in the Banaras Hindu University.
Her teachers included Prof. P.L. Vaidya, Pt. Omkarnath Thakur, Pt. Mahadev Shastri, M.M.Pt. Gopinath Kaviraj, Prof. V.S. Agrawal and Pt. Hajari Prasad Dwivedi, Pt. Brahmadatta Jijnasu, Pt.T.V. Ramachandra Dixitar. She was a polyglot who knew eight languages. She headed the Department of Musicology at the Banaras Hindu University.
The various seminars she organized and the translation projects she undertook represent seminal and unique contributions to Indian musicology.
