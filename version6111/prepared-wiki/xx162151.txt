[[Rock'n'Roll-Übermensch]]

CATEGORIES: 2001 singles, Die Ärzte songs, Songs written by Bela B.

"Rock'n'Roll-Übermensch" (also known as "Rock'n'Roll Übermensch") (Rock'n'roll overman) is a song by the German punk band Die Ärzte. It's the eighteenth track and the fourth single from their 2000 album Runter mit den Spendierhosen, Unsichtbarer!.

==The video==

The band is performing in a department store. Throughout the song, Bela slowly grows larger and larger until he becomes too large for the stage and eventually pops through the store's ceiling.  The video is shot in a cinema verite style.  Directed by Philipp Stölzl and Johannes Grebert.
