[[C. N. Jayadevan]]

CATEGORIES: Indian politicians, Malayali people, Malayali politicians, People from Thrissur, Living people, Candidates in the 2014 Indian general election, 1950 births, Government Victoria College, Palakkad alumni

C.N. Jayadevan  is an Indian politician of the Communist Party of India (CPI) from Kerala-Thrissur, and native of Manalur.   Mr. Jayadevan is CPI Thrissur district secretary and a member of the party’s State executive  council and he served as Member of the Legislative Assembly (MLA) of  Kerala from 1996 to 2001 representing the Ollur Assembly Constituency .[tpl]Citation needed|date=March 2014[/tpl]
He was formerly All-India Students Federation State president (1982–85), Chairman of the Block Development Council in Anthicaud (1987–91) and District Council vice-president (1990–92).[tpl]Citation needed|date=March 2014[/tpl]
He has been declared CPI Candidate for 16th Parliament Election 2014 from Thrissur Constituency.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.thehindu.com/news/national/kerala/cpi-names-4-candidates/article5774758.ece |title=CPI names 4 candidates |publisher=www.thehindu.com |date= |accessdate= March 12, 2014  [/tpl]
