[[Duberty Aráoz]]

CATEGORIES: Bolivian footballers, Bolivia international footballers, Association football midfielders, Club Litoral players, 1950 FIFA World Cup players

Duberty Aráoz (dates of birth and death unknown) is a Bolivian football midfielder who played for Bolivia in the 1950 FIFA World Cup.1950 FIFA World Cup Brazil He also played for Club Litoral.
