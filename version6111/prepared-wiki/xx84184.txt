[[Kitaazumi District, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Districts in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]Nihongo|Kitaazumi|北安曇郡|Kita-Azumi-gun[/tpl] is a district in Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the district has an estimated population of 36,547 and a density of 57.31 persons per km².[tpl]Citation needed|date=November 2009[/tpl] The total area is 637.66 km².
