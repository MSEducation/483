[[Land Force Atlantic Area]]

CATEGORIES: Divisions of Canada, Land Force Areas in Canada, Military units and formations established in 1992, 1992 establishments in Canada

The 5th Canadian Division, formerly Land Force Atlantic Area (LFAA),  is responsible for Canadian Army operations in the Canadian provinces of New Brunswick, Newfoundland and Labrador, Nova Scotia, and Prince Edward Island. The 5th Canadian Division is headquartered at Canadian Forces Base Halifax.
