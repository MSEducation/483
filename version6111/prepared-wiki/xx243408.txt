[[Graphiolaceae]]

CATEGORIES: Ustilaginomycotina, Fungal plant pathogens and diseases, Fungi families

The Graphiolaceae are a family of fungi in the Basidiomycota, Exobasidiales order. Species in the family have a widespread distribution, especially in warm temperate and tropical areas. Members of the Graphiolaceae are plant pathogens that grow biotrophically on the leaves of plants in the Palmae family.[tpl]cite book |author=Cannon PF, Kirk PM. |title=Fungal Families of the World |publisher=CABI|location=Wallingford |year=2007 |page=146 |isbn=0-85199-827-5[/tpl]
