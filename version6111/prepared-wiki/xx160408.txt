[[HMS C20]]

CATEGORIES: British C-class submarines, Royal Navy ship names, 1909 ships

HMS ''C20'' was a British C class submarine built by HM Dockyard, Chatham. She was laid down on 1 June 1908 and was commissioned on 31 January 1910.
HMS C20 was sold on 26 May 1921 in Sunderland.
