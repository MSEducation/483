[[Seattle Giants]]

CATEGORIES: Sports clubs established in 1910, Defunct minor league baseball teams, Professional baseball teams in Washington (state)

The Seattle Giants were a minor league baseball team that played in various leagues from 1910 to 1920. Based in Seattle, Washington, USA, they played in the Northwestern League from 1910 to 1917, the Pacific Coast International League in 1918 and 1920, and the Northwest International League in 1919. Two of their ballparks were Yesler Way Park and Dugdale Field.Seattle Washington BR Minors page In 1919, they were also known as the Seattle Drydockers.
