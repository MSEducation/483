[[Trondheim District Court]]

CATEGORIES: District courts of Norway, Organisations based in Trondheim

Trondheim District Court ([tpl]lang-no|Trondheim tingrett[/tpl], previously [tpl]lang|no|Trondheim byrett[/tpl]) was a district court located in and serving Trondheim, Norway. From 1 January 2010, it was merged with Sør-Trøndelag District Court, which was located in the same building. It was subordinate Frostating Court of Appeal.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.regjeringen.no/nb/dep/jd/dok/regpubl/stmeld/20002001/stmeld-nr-23-2000-2001-/18.html?id=323993 |title=Oversikt over dagens domstolstruktur |author=Norwegian Ministry of Justice and the Police |accessdate=2 January 2011 |archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/5vR8w6TJ6 |year=2001 |archivedate=2 January 2011[/tpl]
