Adrien Zeller (2 April 1940, Saverne, Bas-Rhin - 22 August 2009, Haguenau, Bas-Rhin

« Décès d'Adrien Zeller, président de la Région Alsace », Le Figaro, 22 August 2009.) was State Secretary of the Social Security in the second Jacques Chirac government from 1986 to 1988. He was the president of the regional council of Alsace from 1996 until 2009. He was a member of the Union for a Popular Movement. He was born in Saverne, and died aged 69 of a heart attack in Haguenau.
He was a graduate of the College of Europe in Bruges (promotion of 1965-1966).
