Danny Sleath (born 14 December 1986) is an English footballer who plays for Nuneaton Town. Sleath made 14 appearances in Football League Two for Mansfield Town in the 2006–07 and 2007–08 seasons.

Career

Born in Matlock, Derbyshire, Sleath is a graduate of the Mansfield Town youth academy and signed his first professional contract in the summer of 2006.
He played in the final of the 2011 Summer Universiade for Great Britain.
In June 2012 he joined Nuneaton Town.
