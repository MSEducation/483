[[Abner Lloveras]]

CATEGORIES: 1982 births, Living people, Spanish mixed martial artists, Welterweight mixed martial artists, Spanish practitioners of Brazilian jiu-jitsu, Sportspeople from Barcelona, Catalan sportspeople, Spanish boxers

Abner Lloveras (born September 4, 1982 in Barcelona) is a Spanish-Catalan professional mixed martial artist in the welterweight division. He has fought for Shooto and M-1 Global, Abner is a member of M-1 Mixfight Team Spain.m-1global.com/lang/en/teams/spain/ 

Mixed martial arts career

Lloveras most notable bout is with former UFC fighter Phillipe Nover. Abner recently received his Gold Medal in the Spanish Olympic boxing tournament in 2010.
M-1 European Welterweight Champion.
Shooto European Welterweight Champion.
