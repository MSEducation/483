[[2008–09 Eastbourne Borough F.C. season]]

CATEGORIES: Eastbourne Borough F.C. seasons, English football clubs 2008–09 season

During the 2008–09 season, Eastbourne Borough are participating in the Conference National. This is the first time the East Sussex side have reached their highest league of non-league football. Eastbourne Borough's home games were all played at Priory Lane. Setanta Sports hosted their first live televised home league game against Stevenage Borough on 12 October 2008. Eastbourne finished the season 13th in the Table.  This article covers a period from 1 July 2008 to 30 June 2009.

Team kit

The team kit for the 2008-09 season is produced by Macron and the shirt sponsors are Hailsham Roadway,
for home shirts, and Health Plus for away shirts.
