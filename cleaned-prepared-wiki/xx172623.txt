[[Hikaru Ijūin]]

CATEGORIES: 1967 births, Japanese comedians, Japanese radio personalities, Living people, People from Tokyo

, real name Ken Shinooka (篠岡 建), formerly Tanaka (田中 建), born 11 Nov 1967, is a Japanese celebrity, radio personality, computer game reviewer and commentator. He was born in the Tokyo Metropolitan area of Arakawa-ku Nishiogu. He is married to former idol Mika Shinooka.
