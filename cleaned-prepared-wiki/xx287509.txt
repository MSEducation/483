[[Michael Anaba]]

CATEGORIES: 1993 births, Living people, Ghanaian footballers, 2013 African U-20 Championship players, 2013 FIFA U-20 World Cup players, Asante Kotoko F.C. players, Elche CF players

Michael Anaba is a Ghanaian professional footballer and currently plays for Elche CF in the Spanish La Liga.

Club career

Michael Anaba started his professional football career with Windy Professionals, a division two football team based in Winneba.

International Championships

CAF U-20 Championship, Algeria 2013

Anaba was a member of the Ghana Under-20 national team during the qualification rounds for the 2013 African U-20 Championship. In 2013, coach Sellas Tetteh called him up for the Ghana Under-20 national team for the 2013 tournament in Algeria.

FIFA U-20 Championship, Turkey 2013

Anaba was a member of the Ghana Under-20 national team that took part in the 2013 FIFA U-20 tournament in Turkey.
