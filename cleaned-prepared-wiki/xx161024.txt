[[Edwards Wildman Palmer]]

CATEGORIES: Law firms established in 2005, Foreign law firms with offices in Hong Kong, Law firms based in Boston, Massachusetts

Edwards Wildman is an AmLaw 100 law firm. It was formed from the 2011 merger of Edwards Angell Palmer & Dodge and Wildman, Harrold, Allen & Dixon. Edwards Angell Palmer & Dodge had been formed by the 2005 merger of Edwards & Angell LLP and Palmer & Dodge LLP.   
Edwards Wildman has 15 offices in the U.S., Europe, and Asia.Latham, Wachtell, Covington, Edwards Wildman: Business of Law - Bloomberg  

Leadership

Alan Levin is the chairman of Edwards Wildman; Robert Shuftan serves as managing partner and Laurence Harris is the firm's deputy managing partner.Boston's Edwards Angell law firm merging | HartfordBusiness.com
