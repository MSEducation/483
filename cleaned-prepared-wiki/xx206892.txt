Chen Zhizhao (; pinyin: Chén Zhìzhāo; born 14 March 1988), known as Zizao in Brazil, is a Chinese footballer who currently plays for Beijing Guoan in the Chinese Super League.

Club career

Chen Zhizhao originally began his football career playing for the Shanghai Shenhua youth team, however he was unable to break into their senior team. He would move to Hong Kong football team Citizen AA at the beginning of the 2007-08 league season to start his professional football career where he would make his competitive debut against Lanwa Redbull in the first game of the season in a 0-0 draw.com Retrieved 2012-07-17 He quickly established himself within the team and would play in 13 league games and see Citizen come second within the league. Throughout the season he would also play in all of Citizen's cup games and would score his first goal against Eastern AA on 24 November 2007 in a  Hong Kong Senior Shield game that Citizen lose 3-1.com Retrieved 2012-07-17 His following goals would come in the Hong Kong FA Cup where he scored against Eastern AA on 10 May 2008 in a semifinal game that saw them win 1-0.com Retrieved 2012-07-17 This was then followed by a goal in the final of the FA Cup win against Wofoo Tai Po on 18 May 2008 that saw Citizen win 2-0.com Retrieved 2012-07-17
Chen moved back to mainland China and signed a five-year contract with Nanchang Hengyuan on 26 February 2009.陈志钊身陷与南昌队"合同监" 想转会葡甲球队 at chinanews. Chen's first Super League season was fantasy. He scored 10 goals and assisted 8 times in 30 appearances, which secured Nanchang's stay in the top flight for the next season. He was voted by fans as the most valuable player of the club in the 2010 league season.
Chen was linked with Liga de Honra side C.D. Trofense in January 2011. Trofense was interested with Chen, but Nanchang Hengyuan blocked this transfer. According to Chen's contract with Nanchang Hengyuan on 26 February 2009, if a non-Chinese football club provided a bid of around €200,000 for him before 26 February 2011, then Nanchang would accept the transfer without any condition.
