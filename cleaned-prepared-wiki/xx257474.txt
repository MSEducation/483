[[Scalado]]

CATEGORIES: Software companies of Sweden

Scalado is a provider of imaging technologies, applications and engineering services for the camera (phone) industry. The company is a Swedish imaging software company that focuses on the wireless device industry. Scalado's CAPS is embedded in more than a billion cellphone camera devices.

History

Scalado was founded in 2000 at Ideon Science Park in Lund, Sweden by former students from Lund's University, Sami Niemi, Fadi Abbas, Maziar Jahanshahi and Pierre Elzouki (active until 2004).

Technology

In 2010 Scalado technology was embedded in more than 930 millions devices with a predicted annual growth of 500 million devices per year.
During Blackberry World 2012 in Florida, Research in Motion revealed a demo of its upcoming new mobile os, BlackBerry 10, incorporating Scalado's time warp feature.

Products

Scalado's software offers products in capturing, viewing and editing images. Examples of some of the imaging products Scalado provides its customers with: Scalado CAPS, Scalado PhotoFlow, Scalado Rewind, Scalado TimeWarp, Scalado Camera SDK, Scalado Album SDK, and Scalado Editor SDK.
