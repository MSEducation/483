[[Tomomi Itano]]

CATEGORIES: 1991 births, Living people, Japanese idols, Japanese female singers, Japanese child actresses, Japanese female pop singers, AKB48 members, King Records (Japan) artists, Musicians from Kanagawa Prefecture

 is a former member of the Japanese idol girl group AKB48. Her talent agent is Horipro. She was the first member of AKB48 to get a solo debut . Her solo debut is Dear J follow by Fui ni , 10nen go no kimi e and 1% . After graduating from AKB48 , her single debut "little" attracted a lot of fans to respective venues for her solo concerts.

Career

In 2005, Itano joined the Japanese idol girl group AKB48 In AKB48's 2010 general election, she placed fourth overall.
Itano released her first single, "Dear J", on January 26, 2011. It reached number 1 on the Oricon daily ranking and number 2 on the Oricon weekly ranking, selling 204,981 copies. In April, she reprised her role as Shibuya in Majisuka Gakuen 2. She placed eighth overall in AKB48's 2011 general election held in June. Her second single, "Fui ni", was released on July 13 and sold 90,103 copies, and reached number one on the Oricon weekly chart.
In 2012, Itano was cited as the "queen of TV commercials" for having more contracts with companies than any other female tarento (although her count of 20 was technically a tie with those of fellow AKB48 member Mariko Shinoda).
On February 1, 2013, during an AKB48 stage greeting, Itano announced she was leaving the group. Her graduation song is "Saigo no Door", which was released as a B-side on one of the editions of AKB48's single "Koisuru Fortune Cookie".
