[[List of places in Hampshire]]

CATEGORIES: Geography of Hampshire, Lists of places in English counties, Hampshire-related lists

This is a list of settlements in the county of Hampshire, England. Places highlighted in bold type are towns.
A relatively small far western area, four large parishes, were in Hampshire until joining the ceremonial and administrative county of Dorset in 1974, listed in footnotes.  The Isle of Wight was in Hampshire until 1890, for which see List of places in the Isle of Wight.
