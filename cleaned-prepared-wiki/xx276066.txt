Hot Entertainment AV Visual Arts Academy

In the Fall of 2007, Hot Entertainment set up a series of courses for people wanting to enter the adult video industry under the name of Hot Entertainment AV Visual Arts Academy. Separate series of courses consisting of seven or eight 90-minute classes were available for a 200,000 yen (about $2000) fee for aspiring producers, directors and actors.
