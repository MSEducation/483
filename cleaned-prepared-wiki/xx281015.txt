[[Mickey van der Hart]]

CATEGORIES: 1994 births, Living people, Dutch footballers, AFC Ajax players, Eredivisie players, Eerste Divisie players, People from Amstelveen

Mickey van der Hart (born 13 June 1994 in Amstelveen, Netherlands) is a Dutch professional football player who plays as goalkeeper in the Eredivisie for AFC Ajax.

Career

AFC Ajax

Acquired into the Ajax Youth Academy from local amateur side Amsterdamsche FC in 2007, Mickey van der Hart worked his way up the youth ranks, and had an outstanding 2011–12 season with the Ajax A1 youth squad.
The 2012–13 season was Mickey van der Hart's first with the Ajax first team since the departure of former third keeper Jeroen Verhoeven,
The 2013–14 season saw Mickey maintain his position as Frank de Boer's third choice behind Kenneth Vermeer and Jasper Cillessen,

International career

Mickey van der Hart has represented Netherlands U-18 and Netherlands U-19. On 11 November 2011, Mickey made his debut for Netherlands U-18 in a friendly encounter against Romania U-18.

Personal life

Mickey van der Hart is the grandson of former AFC Ajax central defender Cor van der Hart.
