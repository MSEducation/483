[[List of international cricket centuries by Jacques Kallis]]

CATEGORIES: South African cricket lists, Lists of international cricket centuries by player

Jacques Kallis is a South African cricketer who plays as an all-rounder. He has been described as "the greatest cricketer ever",
Kallis made his Test debut against England in December 1995. He reached his second double century, and his highest score, in 2012 against Sri Lanka, making 224.
In ODI cricket, Kallis scored his maiden century in January 1998, totalling 111 against New Zealand.
