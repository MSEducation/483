[[Tony Craig]]

Tony Andrew Craig (born 20 April 1985) is an English football defender who plays for Brentford in League One. He began his career at Millwall and had three spells with the club.

Career

Millwall

Born in Greenwich, London, Craig came through the youth ranks at Millwall. Adept as a centre back or a left back, Craig received his maiden call into the first team squad for a Division One league match against Derby County on 16 April 2003. He was an unused substitute during the 2–1 win. Craig was an unused substitute for three of the following four games and made his professional debut with a starting appearance in a 3–3 draw with Nottingham Forest on 26 April 2003.

=Wycombe Wanderers (loan)=

Craig joined League Two side Wycombe Wanderers on a "short term" loan on 22 October 2004 and made his debut with a starting appearance in a 1–1 home draw with Mansfield Town. The loan was later extended to 22 January 2005, but a failure to extend the loan further saw Craig make his final appearance for the club on the final day of his loan in a 1–1 draw with Macclesfield Town. Craig made 18 appearances for the Chairboys and scored no goals.

Crystal Palace

Craig signed for Championship side Crystal Palace on 27 June 2007 on a three year deal for an undisclosed fee.

=Millwall (loan)=

Craig returned to Millwall on a one month loan on 27 March 2008. He made five appearances and scored against Carlisle United in the final match of his spell, before returning to Crystal Palace on 27 April.

Third spell with Millwall

Craig signed permanently for the second time with Millwall on 11 July 2008, on a three-year deal. Manager Kenny Jackett said he "was very impressed with Tony during his loan spell here last season and I think he'll be a good asset for us". Craig made a personal-best 52 appearances during the 2008–09 season, scoring two goals and helping Millwall to a place at Wembley Stadium for the 2009 League One play-off Final versus Scunthorpe United. Craig left the club in July 2012, having made 141 appearances and scored four goals in his third stint with the club.

=Leyton Orient (loan)=

Craig signed for League One side Leyton Orient on a one month loan on 24 November 2011, Craig made four appearances for Orient and was recalled By Millwall on 28 December.

Brentford

Craig joined League One side Brentford on 13 July 2012 for an undisclosed fee on a three-year contract.
