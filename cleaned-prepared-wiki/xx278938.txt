[[1997 in Pancrase]]

CATEGORIES: Pancrase events, 1997 in mixed martial arts

The year 1997 is the 5th year in the history of Pancrase, a mixed martial arts promotion based in Japan. In 1997 Pancrase held 14 events beginning with Pancrase: Alive 1.
