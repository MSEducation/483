[[As Daylight Dies]]

CATEGORIES: Killswitch Engage albums, 2006 albums, Roadrunner Records albums, Albums produced by Adam Dutkiewicz

As Daylight Dies is the fourth studio album by American metalcore band Killswitch Engage. It was released on November 21, 2006 through Roadrunner Records. It was produced by Adam Dutkiewicz and Joel Stroetzel.

Background and release

The album was released on November 13, 2006 through Roadrunner Records. It peaked at number 32 on the Billboard 200,
The album's first single, "My Curse", was released in 2006, and managed to peak at number 21 on the Billboard Rock Songs Chart.
A special edition was released on August 28, 2007, including four B-sides and a DVD containing three music videos and behind-the-scenes footage.23.2007, Roadrun.

Critical reception

The album has received generally positive reviews from contemporary music critics. Thom Jurek of Allmusic praised the album, scoring it a four and a half out five, positively commenting, "If the cynics don't get it by now, they never will. For the rest, this is the album to come into the tent with."
