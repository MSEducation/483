[[Ruby Jessop]]

CATEGORIES: 1987 births, American activists, Former Latter Day Saints, Living people, Mormonism-related controversies, Fundamentalist Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints members

Ruby Jessop (born 1987) is a former member of the Fundamentalist Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints and child bride known for her family connections, her 2013 escape from an  FLDS-controlled polygamous community, and the criminal probe prompted by her escape.
Jessop is the sister of author and ex-FLDS member Flora Jessop.   Flora Jessop writes that while the authorities were contacted, they were unable to locate Ruby as she was being moved from place to place to avoid F Jessop's contact.
