Benjamin Neil Reeves (born 19 November 1991) is an English-born Northern Irish professional footballer who plays for Milton Keynes Dons as a midfielder.

Club career

Having started out in the youth team as a left back, he has since been converted into an attacking midfielder. On 5 April 2011, Reeves signed his first professional contract on a two year deal.
On 21 September 2011, he made his debut for Southampton, playing the full 90 minutes in the League Cup third round match against Preston North End. He set up the first goal in a 2–1 win.
He joined Dagenham & Redbridge on a month's loan on 23 February 2012,
He scored his first goal for Southampton against Stevenage in the League Cup on 28 August 2012.
After six first-team appearances for the Saints in 2012–13, on 31 January 2013 Reeves joined Southend United on loan for a month.
On 15 March 2013, it announced that Reeves had re-joined Southend United on loan, until the end of the season, in his second spell at the club. Reeves played in the final of the Football League Trophy against Crewe, where he came on as a substitute for Bilel Mohsni in the 57th minutes, as Southend lost 2–0.
On 4 June 2013, he was released by Southampton.
