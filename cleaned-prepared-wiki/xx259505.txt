[[List of Maltese football transfers winter 2010–11]]

CATEGORIES: Football transfers summer 2010, Football transfers winter 2010–11, 2010–11 in Maltese football, Lists of Maltese football transfers

This is a list of Maltese football transfers for the 2010–11 winter transfer window by club. Only transfers of clubs in the Maltese Premier League and Maltese First Division are included.
The winter transfer window will open on 1 January 2011, although a few transfers may take place prior to that date. The window will close at midnight on 31 January 2011. Players without a club may join one at any time, either during or in between transfer windows.
