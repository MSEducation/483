[[Dmitry Gurevich]]

CATEGORIES: 1956 births, Living people, Russian Jews, American people of Russian-Jewish descent, American Jews, Chess grandmasters, Russian chess players, American chess players, Jewish chess players

Dmitry Gurevich (born 11 September 1956, Moscow, Russia) is a Russian-American chess grandmaster. 
Gurevich emigrated to New York in 1980 and earned the grandmaster title three years later. Dmitry has tied or won the U.S. Open four times (in 1988, 1994, 2009, and 2012). Also, Gurevich has had especially good results at the National Open in Las Vegas, sharing first place on numerous occasions, e.g. 1985, 1986, 1990, 1991, 1996, 1997, and 2005. He has been a regular finisher at the top of North American events as well as a regular participant in the U.S. Invitational Championship.US Chess Federation
