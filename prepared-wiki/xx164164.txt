[[Cyllogenes suradeva]]

CATEGORIES: Melanitini, Butterflies of India

Branded Evening Brown Cyllogenes suradeva  is a Brown butterfly that is found in the Himalayas.Marrku Savela's Website on Lepidoptera Page on Cyllogenes genus.

==Range==

The butterfly is found in the Himalayas in Sikkim and Bhutan.

==Status==

As per Evans it is "Rare".Evans,W.H.(1932) The Identification of Indian Butterflies, (2nd edition) ser no D23.1, pp 127

==Description==

The Branded Evening Brown is 75 to 85Â mm in wingspan and broadly resembles the Melanitis Evening Browns, with a strong purple tinge above. The upper forewing has a narrow yellow apical band which does not reach the termen. The male has a large black brand on the upper forewing at the bases of 2 to 5 and the end cell.Wynter-Blyth, M.A. (1957) Butterflies of the Indian Region, pg 125.

==Habits==

It is a low elevation butterfly.
