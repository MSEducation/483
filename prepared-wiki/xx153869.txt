[[Second Army (France)]]

CATEGORIES: Field armies of France, Military units and formations of France in World War I, Military units and formations of France in World War II, Military units and formations established in 1914, 1914 establishments in France

The Second Army ([tpl]lang-fr|IIe Armée[/tpl]) was a Field army of the French Army during World War I and World War II. The Army became famous for fighting the Battle of Verdun in 1916 under Philippe Pétain.
