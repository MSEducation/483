[[KKRT]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Washington (state), Morris Communications radio stations

KKRT is an AM sports radio station broadcasting at 900 kHZ. Licensed to Wenatchee, Washington, USA, the station serves the Wenatchee  area.  The station is currently owned by Mcc Radio, LLC and features programing from ESPN Radio. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=KKRT |title=KKRT Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==History==

KKRT has changed its name various times since its debut. KKRT first went on the air as KSGA on the first of July 1985, but changed its call sign to KEYK in early January of 1987. On the thirteenth of October 1988, the call sing was changed again to its current name of KKRT.
