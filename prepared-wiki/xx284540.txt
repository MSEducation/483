[[Joachim Eickmayer]]

Joachim Eickmayer (born 11 January 1993 in Bully-les-Mines) is a French footballer who currently plays as a midfielder for Ligue 1 side FC Sochaux-Montbéliard. He is a youth exponent from the club.
