[[Þrettándinn]]

CATEGORIES: Icelandic culture

Þrettándinn, also known as Twelfth Night, is an Icelandic holiday celebrated on January 6. It is the last day of Christmas, and is celebrated with elf bonfires and elf dances.[tpl]cite web | url = http://simnet.is/gardarj/folk/days.htm | title = Of Time and Tide | accessdate = 2010-12-19 | work = Virtually Virtual Iceland[/tpl] On this day, families get together, have dinner and light fireworks.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.iceland.org/us/the-embassy/curiosities/nr/757#Threttandinn_–_Last_Day_of_Christmas | title = Christmas in Iceland | accessdate = 2010-12-31 | work = Embassy of Iceland[/tpl]
