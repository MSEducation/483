[[John Kelly (footballer)]]

CATEGORIES: League of Ireland players, Bohemian F.C. players, Year of birth missing, Possibly living people

John Kelly was an Irish soccer player during the 1960s.
Kelly was a goalkeeper who played for Bohemians amongst others during his career in the League of Ireland.Bohemian FC match programme, Vol. 44, no. 20 Kelly played alongside the likes of Billy Young and Willie Browne during his time at the then amateur Bohs.
