[[Elachista metella]]

CATEGORIES: Elachistidae, Moths described in 2002, Butterflies and moths of Europe, Moths of Europe, Insects of Hungary, Insects of Bulgaria, Insects of Italy

Elachista metella is a moth of the Elachistidae family that is found in France, Switzerland, Austria, the Czech Republic, Slovakia, Hungary, Slovenia, Croatia and Bulgaria.
