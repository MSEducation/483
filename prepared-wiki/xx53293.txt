[[Aamon]]

CATEGORIES: Demons in Judaism, Demons in Christianity, Goetic demons

Aamon (also Amon and Nahum), in demonology, is a Marquis of Hell who governs forty infernal legions.

==Description==

The names Aamon and Amon come from the god Amun or from the god Baal-hamon. Nahum means who induces to eagerness.
According to the tradition, Aamon has little owl's head, lion's or wolf's fore part of the body, including legs, and worm's or snake's tail.  He knows all past and future events.
