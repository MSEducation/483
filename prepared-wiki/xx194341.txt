[[Diego Renan]]

CATEGORIES: 1990 births, Living people, Brazilian footballers, Cruzeiro Esporte Clube players, Criciúma Esporte Clube players, Club de Regatas Vasco da Gama players, Campeonato Brasileiro Série A footballers, Campeonato Brasileiro Série B footballers

Diego Renan de Lima Ferreira or simply Diego Renan  (born January 26, 1990 in Surubim-PE), is a Brazilian left back. He currently plays for Vasco da Gama.
He made his professional debut for Cruzeiro in a 1-1 away draw to Ituiutaba-MG in the Minas Gerais State Championship on March 27, 2008.
