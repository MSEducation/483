[[Querida, Colorado]]

CATEGORIES: Former populated places in  Custer County, Colorado, Ghost towns in Colorado

Querida (also called Bassick City) is a ghost town in Custer County, Colorado, United States.[tpl]GR|3[/tpl]  The town was built to serve the surrounding silver mines, the most important of which was the Bassick mine.  Querida is Spanish for "beloved."
