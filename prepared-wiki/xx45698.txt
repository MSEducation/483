[[Bree, Belgium]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Limburg (Belgium)

''For other pages named Bree, see Bree

Bree is a city located in the Belgian province of Limburg. On January 1, 2006, Bree had a total population of 14,503. The total area is 64.96 km² which gives it a population density of 223 inhabitants per square km. The mayor of Bree is Liesbeth Van der Auwera.

==Climate==

Climate in this area has mild differences  between highs and lows, and there is adequate rainfall year round.  The Köppen Climate Classification subtype for this climate is "Cfb" (Marine West Coast Climate/Oceanic climate).Climate Summary for Bree, Belgium
