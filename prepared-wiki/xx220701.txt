[[Cyclirius webbianus]]

CATEGORIES: Polyommatinae, Animals described in 1840

The Canary Blue, Cyclirius webbianus, is a butterfly of the family Lycaenidae. It is endemic to the Canary Islands.
The length of the forewings is [tpl]convert|14|-|15|mm[/tpl]. The species flies from March to August on heights of up to [tpl]convert|3000|m[/tpl]. 
The larvae feed on Fabaceae (Lotus, Cytisus, Spartocytisus and Ononis species).
