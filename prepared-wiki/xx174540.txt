[[Gmina Polanka Wielka]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Lesser Poland Voivodeship, Oświęcim County

Gmina Polanka Wielka is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Oświęcim County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. Its seat is the village of Polanka Wielka, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|10|km|mi|0[/tpl] south-east of Oświęcim and [tpl]convert|46|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] west of the regional capital Kraków.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|24.08|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 4,136.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Polanka Wielka is bordered by the gminas of Osiek, Oświęcim, Przeciszów and Wieprz.
