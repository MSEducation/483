Susie Feldman (birth name Sussanah Sprague; born June 7, 1982) is a former participant in the celeb-reality television series The Two Coreys and the former wife of Corey Feldman. She is also a model, and has posed for Stuff, Playboy magazines and PETA.

==Early life==

Sprague's parents, Sue Egberts and Scott Sprague, separated when Susie was two years old.  She subsequently lived with her father until his death. Her father was murdered, according to her website. While living with her father, they lived  in Northern and Southern California, Hawaii, and Spain.Meet The Two Coreys - A&E TV[tpl]citation broken|date=October 2011[/tpl]
Upon the death of her father, Sprague moved in with her mother. During this time, Sprague attended junior high and high school in Corona, California, and graduated from Santiago High School in 2000.[tpl]citation broken|date=October 2011[/tpl]

==Reality TV==

Feldman participated in the celebrity reality series The Two Coreys (2007-2008) on A&E. In the series, she is seen sharing her home with her husband and Corey Haim. They started filming season two in the beginning of 2008.
During season two of the show, Susie and Corey Feldman posed for a PETA ad campaign promoting vegetarianism. The photo was inspired by John Lennon and Yoko Ono "Give Peace a Chance" photo.
Also while on the show she posed for Stuff magazine. Additionally, Susie posed nude for the August 2008 edition of Playboy.[tpl]cite journal|journal=Playboy|title=Sprague posted nude for Playboy|date=July 2008|issue=140|url=http://members.i.playboy.com/Playboy-Magazine/Jul-2008-Issue/140[/tpl][tpl]subscription required[/tpl]
