[[Jantzen Derrick]]

CATEGORIES: 1943 births, Living people, English footballers, English expatriate footballers, Bristol City F.C. players, Mansfield Town F.C. players, Paris Saint-Germain F.C. players, Ligue 1 players, Expatriate footballers in France, Bath City F.C. players, Association football forwards

Jantzen Derrick (born 10 January 1943 in Bristol) is a former English association football striker.
He started his career with Bristol City F.C., playing 259 matches and scoring 31 goals. He was loaned to Mansfield Town F.C. in the 1970-71 season. He then joined Paris SG in France for one year and became the first ever English player to do so, and came back to England at Bath City F.C..
