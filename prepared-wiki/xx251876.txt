[[Solar eclipse of June 26, 1824]]

CATEGORIES: 1824 in science, Solar eclipses

A total solar eclipse occurred on June 26, 1824. [tpl]Total solar eclipse summary[/tpl]
Totality was visible across parts of China and Japan, with a partial eclipse across much of North America near sunset.

==Related eclipses==

It is a part of solar Saros 124.
