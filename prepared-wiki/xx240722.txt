[[Heping District, Shenyang]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Liaoning

Heping District ([tpl]zh|s=和平区 |t=和平區 |p=Hépíng Qū |l=peace[/tpl]) is an urban district of Shenyang, the capital of Liaoning province, People's Republic of China. The district contains 13 subdistricts of the city proper of Shenyang, and 8 other subdistricts.
