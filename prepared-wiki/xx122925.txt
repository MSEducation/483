Buena Television (also known as Buena TV) is a Venezuelan regional television station that can be seen by those who live in the Tachira State.

==History==

Buena TV was inaugurated on August 1, 2006, by its founder, the then governor of the Tachira State Ronald Blanco La Cruz.  It is expected that it will have a cultural, entertainment, educational, and information and opinion programming.  1
