[[Shamrock Hill]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains and hills of South Georgia and the South Sandwich Islands, Volcanoes of South Georgia and the South Sandwich Islands

Shamrock Hill ([tpl]Coord|56|42|S|27|5|W||display=inline,title[/tpl]) is a prominent volcanic cone located northwest of Irving Point in the east part of Visokoi Island, South Sandwich Islands. So named by the survey party from HMS Protector because they occupied this feature as a survey station on St. Patrick's Day, March 17, 1964.
