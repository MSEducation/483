[[Sereyn]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Sereyn ([tpl]lang-fa|سرئين[/tpl], also Romanized as Sareyen, Sarīn, and Serīn; also known as Sūzīn)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3082833|Sereyn[/tpl] is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 447, in 79 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
