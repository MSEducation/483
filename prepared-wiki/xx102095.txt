[[Left Alliance (Scotland)]]

CATEGORIES: Socialist parties in Scotland, Politics of Fife

The Left Alliance are a small political group in Fife, Scotland.
They currently have two seats on Fife Council, out of a total of 78 councillors.  They are William L. Clarke, who represents Ballingry and Lochore, and Alexander Maxwell, who represents Cowdenbeath Central. Clarke is a member of the Communist Party of Scotland and Maxwell is a member of Democratic Left Scotland.[tpl]cite news|url=http://findarticles.com/p/articles/mi_qn4156/is_20020310/ai_n12574353/|title=In Marx We Trusted|last=Abrahams|first=Tim|date=10 March 2002|work=The Sunday Herald|location=Scotland|accessdate=15 February 2010[/tpl]
They represent areas that are part of the former Fife coalfield and have in the past been held by members of the Communist Party of Great Britain and are themselves descended from that political tradition.
