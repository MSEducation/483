[[Rebarthy]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Warangal district

Rebarthy' is a village in the Warangal district in Andhra Pradesh, India.
The village population is 5,032.

==Media==

Telugu newspapers, such as Eenadu, Vaartha and Andhra Jyothy, are available. TV Channels available are the Telugu cable channels ETV Telugu, Gemini TV and Teja TV.

==People==

The village is largely middle class. Rebarthy is home to teachers, businessmen, software engineers, and mathematicians.

==Weather==

Summers are hot, but winters are cold; this is one of the coldest places in Andhra Pradesh.
