[[Lobilo Boba]]

CATEGORIES: 1950 births, Democratic Republic of the Congo footballers, Democratic Republic of the Congo international footballers, Association football defenders, AS Vita Club players, 1974 FIFA World Cup players, 1974 African Cup of Nations players, 1976 African Cup of Nations players, Living people

Florian Lobilo Boba (born 10 April 1950) is a Congolese football defender who played for Zaire in the 1974 FIFA World Cup.1974 FIFA World Cup Germany

==Career==

Lobilo played for AS Vita Club, where he would win the 1973 African Cup of Champions Clubs.[tpl]cite web|publisher=La Conscience|author=Ligodi, Patient|title=Lobilo : un héros à qui le pays a volé sa jeunesse!|url=http://www.laconscience.com/Lobilo-un-heros-a-qui-le-pays-a-vole-sa-jeunesse.html|language=French|date=31 January 2006[/tpl]
Lobilo made several appearances for Zaire and participated in the 1974 African Cup of Nations finals.
In 2006, he was selected by CAF as one of the best 200 African football players of the last 50 years.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Sport24.gr|title=Οι 200 κορυφαίοι Αφρικανοί|url=http://www.sport24.gr/football/article273152.ece?service=print|date=25 October 2006[/tpl]
