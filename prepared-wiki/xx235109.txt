[[Imperial Japanese Army Air Force Academy]]

CATEGORIES: Military history of Japan, Imperial Japanese Army, Japanese Air Force, Defunct Japanese military academies, 1945 disestablishments

The [tpl]nihongo|Imperial Japanese Army Air Force Academy|陸軍航空士官学校|Rikugun Kōkū Shikan Gakkō[/tpl] was the principal officers' training school for the Imperial Japanese Army Air Force. The classrooms of the academy were located in the city of Sayama, Saitama Prefecture, northwest of Tokyo. The former airfield of the academy is now  Iruma Air Base of the Japan Air Self-Defense Force.
