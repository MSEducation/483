[[Stop Sign]]

CATEGORIES: 2003 singles, Abz Love songs, Song recordings produced by Richard Stannard, Songs written by Abz Love

"Stop Sign" is the third single released from former Five member Abs Breen's debut solo album, Abstract Theory. The single found fair success, peaking at No. 10 in the UK and No. 53 in Australia. The song samples the Stop Sign by Mel Wynn.
