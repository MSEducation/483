[[1461 Jean-Jacques]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Asteroids named for people, Astronomical objects discovered in 1937

1461 Jean-Jacques is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 2018.2528546 days (5.53 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=1461  |  title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-04-29 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on December 30, 1937 by Marguerite Laugier and named after her son.
