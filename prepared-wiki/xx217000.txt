[[Manghopir Hills]]

CATEGORIES: Hills of Karachi

Manghopir Hills are located in between Karachi District of Sindh and Lasbela District of Balochistan in Pakistan. The City District Government of Karachi is building a resort and recreational area in Manghopir Hills in Gadap Town. 
There are several ethnic groups including Muhajirs, Sindhis, Punjabis, Kashmiris, Seraikis, Pakhtuns, Balochis, Memons, Bohras, Ismailis, etc. Over 99% of the population is Muslim.
