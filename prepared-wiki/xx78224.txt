[[Kumage District, Kagoshima]]

CATEGORIES: Districts in Kagoshima Prefecture

[tpl]Nihongo|Kumage|熊毛郡|Kumage-gun[/tpl] is a district located in Kagoshima Prefecture, Japan. The district covers two islands from the Ōsumi Islands, Tanegashima and Yakushima.
The district has an estimated population of 29,706 with a total area of 789.16 km², according to 2005 Census.
