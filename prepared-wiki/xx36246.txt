[[Sæby]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in North Denmark Region, Frederikshavn Municipality

Sæby is a Danish town, with a population of 8,770 (1 January 2013),BEF44: Population 1st January, by urban areas database from Statistics Denmark in Frederikshavn municipality, Region Nordjylland on the northeast coast of Vendsyssel-Thy, a part of the Jutland peninsula in northern Denmark.
