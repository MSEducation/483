[[Salvadoran legislative election, 1952]]

CATEGORIES: 1952 elections in North America, Elections in El Salvador, 1952 in El Salvador, Single-party elections

Legislative elections were held in El Salvador in March 1952.Nohlen, D (2005) Elections in the Americas: A data handbook, Volume I, p276 ISBN 978-0-19-928357-6 The result was a victory for the Revolutionary Party of Democratic Unification, which was the only party to contest the elections as the opposition Renovating Action Party claimed that they were rigged.Webre, Stephen (1979) José Napoleón Duarte and the Christian Democratic Party in Salvadoran Politics 1960-1972 Baton Rouge: Louisiana State University Press, p18
