Zena Lotus Grey[tpl]Cite news|last=Tudor|first=Silke|coauthors=|title=Going to the Chapel|pages=|publisher=The Village Voice|date=2006-09-06|url=http://www.villagevoice.com/2006-09-05/nyc-life/going-to-the-chapel/|accessdate=2010-10-20[/tpl] (born November 15, 1988) is an American actress. She is known for her roles in the films Snow Day, Max Keeble's Big Move, and The Shaggy Dog.

==Life and career==

Grey was born in Brooklyn, New York, the daughter of artists Allyson and Alex Grey.[tpl]Cite news|last=Stephens|first=Michael|coauthors=|title=High Art: Alex Grey and the Chapel of Sacred Mirrors|pages=|publisher=PopMatters|date=2002-05-08|url=http://www.popmatters.com/columns/stephens/020508.shtml|accessdate=2010-10-20[/tpl] Her first role was in the Broadway play The Herbal Bed. In 1999, she had a role in The Bone Collector, before starring in Snow Day (2000) and Max Keeble's Big Move (2001). She also appeared on the TV show All That. In December 2004, Grey appeared in In Good Company. After that, she starred as Tim Allen's daughter in The Shaggy Dog (2006). Her latest role was that of Penelope Bryte in 2010's My Soul to Take. She also had a brief recurring role in 2011 as Ruby on the television series House.
