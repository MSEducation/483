[[2008 ADAC Formel Masters season]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 in German motorsport, ADAC Formel Masters seasons

The 2008 ADAC Formel Masters season was the first season of the open wheel racing series from Germany. The ADAC Formel Masters is a new continental series for junior drivers powered by Volkswagen,[tpl]cite news|title=New ADAC Formula Series "powered by Volkswagen"|url=http://www.volkswagen-motorsport.com/web/news/index.php?PHPSESSID=296090484898f26986a54945b93cf0e2&flash=&lg=e&st=239&id=1568|work=volkswagen-motorsport.com|publisher=Volkswagen|date=2007-11-30|accessdate=2008-05-21[/tpl] a 1.6-litre FSI engine delivering [tpl]convert|145|hp|abbr=on[/tpl] will power the single-seaters made by the Italian manufacturer Dallara.
The season started on May 9, 2008 at the Oschersleben and ended on October 26 at Hockenheim with the win of the Portuguese driver Armando Parente.[tpl]cite news|title=Armando Parente erster Champion der "Formel ADAC"|language=German|url=http://www.automobilsport.com/adac-fomel-masters-armando-parente-champion-abt-vollmair-dennis-kug-racing---46611.html|work=automobilsport.com|publisher=automobilsport|date=2008-10-26|accessdate=2009-08-14|author=Maggy Parries |archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/5j68akn74|archivedate=2009-08-17|deadurl=no[/tpl]
