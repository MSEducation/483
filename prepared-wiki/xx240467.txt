[[WZHD]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in New York

WZHD (97.1 FM) is a radio station licensed to Canaseraga, New York, USA, and serving the Hornell/Dansville area. The station is currently owned by Equinox Broadcasting Corp.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=WZHD |title=WZHD Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division |accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl][tpl]cite news|url=http://www.fybush.com/NERW/2009/091019/nerw.html#ny|title=NorthEast Radio Watch report mentioning the station|accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl]
The station is currently running a classic hits format similar to that found on sister station WPHD-FM.
