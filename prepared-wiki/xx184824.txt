[[Mountfield Halt railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Disused railway stations in East Sussex, Former Southern Railway stations, Railway stations opened in 1923, Railway stations closed in 1969, Beeching closures in England, Rother

Mountfield Halt (TQ 746 197 [tpl]Coord|50.950140|0.486702|display=inline|format=dms[/tpl]) was situated on the Hastings Line between Robertsbridge and Battle. It opened in 1923 and was closed on 6 October 1969. Both platforms were built of sleepers. The station stood just south of the level crossing.Southern Railway Halts, p52  
There is no remains of the halt today.
