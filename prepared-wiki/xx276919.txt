[[Manele, Hawaii]]

CATEGORIES: Census-designated places in Maui County, Hawaii

Manele is a census-designated place (CDP) located in Maui County, Hawaii, United States, on the island of Lanai. As of the 2010 Census, the CDP had a population of 29.http://dbedt.hawaii.gov/census/census_2010/ Manele is one of only two census-designated places on Lanai, the other being the much larger Lanai City. Manele is the site of Four Seasons Resort Lanai.
