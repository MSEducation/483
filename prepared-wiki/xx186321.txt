===Relegation===

The two worst placed teams in the first stage, which are [tpl]brazil fc|Grêmio[/tpl] and [tpl]brazil fc|Vitória[/tpl], were relegated to the following year's second level.

===Promotion===

The champion and runner-up, which are [tpl]brazil fc|Paysandu[/tpl] and [tpl]brazil fc|Guarani[/tpl], were promoted to the following year's first level.

==Copa do Brasil==

The Copa do Brasil final was played between [tpl]brazil fc|Criciúma[/tpl] and [tpl]brazil fc|Grêmio[/tpl].

[tpl]brazil fc|Criciúma[/tpl] declared as the cup champions on the away goal rule by aggregate score of 1-1.
