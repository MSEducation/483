[[River Guard]]

CATEGORIES: Upcoming films, English-language films, American films, 2010s crime films, American crime films, Films shot in North Carolina

River Guard is an upcoming American independent crime film directed by Jim Klock and written by Patrick Jennings. The film stars Eric Ladin and Stacia Crawford.
