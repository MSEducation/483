[[Marcello de Ridder]]

CATEGORIES: Argentine bobsledders, Olympic bobsledders of Argentina, Bobsledders at the 1948 Winter Olympics, Possibly living people, 1921 births, Argentine people of Dutch descent

Marcello de Ridder (born June 6, 1921) was an Argentinian bobsledder who competed in the late 1940s. At the 1948 Winter Olympics in St. Moritz, he finished 12th in the four-man and 15th in the two-man events.
