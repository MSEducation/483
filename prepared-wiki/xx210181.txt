Roy Oscar "Doc" Miller (February 4, 1883 in Chatham, Ontario - July 31, 1938 in Jersey City, New Jersey) was a Major League Baseball outfielder from [tpl]by|1910[/tpl]-[tpl]by|1914[/tpl]. He played for the Chicago Cubs, Boston Braves, Cincinnati Reds, and Philadelphia Phillies. He was interred in North Bergen's Garden State Crematory.
