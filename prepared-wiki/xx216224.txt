[[Anshas]]

CATEGORIES: Cities in Egypt, Arab League, Sharqia Governorate

Anshas is a city East of Cairo located in the Sharkiya Governorate in the Nile Delta of Egypt.
It holds the first experimental nuclear reactor to be operated in Egypt, the Egyptian Atomic Energy Authority Experimental Farm, unauthorized IAEA.http://www-pub.iaea.org/MTCD/publications/PDF/te_1317_web.pdf (search word "Anshas")http://www.arabicnews.com/ansub/Daily/Day/980130/1998013017.html
The City also holds the First Arab League Summit in 1949, which declared the creation of the League.
