[[Donita Sparks and the Stellar Moments]]

CATEGORIES: American rock music groups, Musical groups established in 2007, Musical groups disestablished in 2012

Donita Sparks and The Stellar Moments was best known L7 member Donita Sparks' solo project. The band also featured former L7 drummer Demetra Plakas. In an interview with former guitarist Alan "the Italian", he has said that the Stellar Moments are no more. Former bassist Dat has joined him to form the Shiteland Ponies, while he is unsure of what Dee and Donita are doing.
Their only album Transmiticate was released on February 19, 2008 through SparksFly Records and produced by Ethan Allen and Donita Sparks.
