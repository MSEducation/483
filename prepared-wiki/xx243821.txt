==Biography==

Greg Selkoe was born in Boston, Massachusetts and raised in the Jamaica Plain neighborhood.http://www.vaporsmagazine.com/2007/11/greg-karamaloop/
Selkoe began Karmaloop from his parents' basement in 2000.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.entrepreneur.com/worklife/successstories/youngmillionaires/article196556.html |title=From Basement Boostrapper to Prominent Retailer |publisher=Entrepreneur.com |date=2008-08-21 |accessdate=2013-07-22[/tpl]
He went to the The Winchendon School, a boarding school in Winchendon, Massachusetts. Selkoe received his undergraduate degree from Rollins College[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.boston.com/lifestyle/fashion/articles/2008/03/20/in_the_loop/ |title=Greg Selkoe keeps hipsters in the loop - The Boston Globe |publisher=Boston.com |date=2008-03-20 |accessdate=2013-07-22 |deadurl=yes[/tpl] [tpl]Dead link|date=April 2014|bot=RjwilmsiBot[/tpl]  in Winter Park, Florida. After graduation from Rollins and before starting Karmaloop, Selkoe worked for the City of Boston for three years at the Boston Redevelopment Authority.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.bizjournals.com/boston/stories/2010/05/03/story5.html |title=Online streetwear retailer Greg Selkoe finds his groove - Boston Business Journal |publisher=Bizjournals.com |date= |accessdate=2013-07-22[/tpl]  After starting Karmaloop, Selkoe continued his education at The Kennedy School of Government at Harvard University and completed a Masters in Public Policy in 2005. Selkoe also suffers from ADHD, but cites the disorder as one of the reasons he's been successful.[tpl]cite web|url=http://nextshark.com/karmaloop-ceo-greg-selkoe-interview/|title=Karmaloop CEO Greg Selkoe: Why Having Creative People Makes Successful Businesses INTERVIEW |publisher=NextShark.com |date= |accessdate=2013-10-29[/tpl]
Greg Selkoe currently lives in Boston, Massachusetts with his wife and Creative Director at Karmaloop, Dina Selkoe.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.boston.com/lifestyle/fashion/articles/2009/11/05/25_most_stylish_bostonians_of_2009dina_selkoe/ |title=25 Most Stylish Bostonians of 2009 - Dina Selkoe - The Boston Globe |publisher=Boston.com |date=2009-11-05 |accessdate=2013-07-22 |deadurl=yes[/tpl] [tpl]Dead link|date=April 2014|bot=RjwilmsiBot[/tpl]

==About Karmaloop.com==

Karmaloop.com was founded in and is currently headquartered in Boston, Massachusetts, with an office and TV studio in New York City. It is one of the world’s largest online retailers of streetwear (including footwear, apparel, accessories, housewares and more).http://www.internetretailer.com/top500/list/?page=2[tpl]cite web|author=/ |url=http://radioboston.wbur.org/2010/12/21/karmaloop |title=Karmaloop: An Underground Fashion Empire | Radio Boston |publisher=Radioboston.wbur.org |date=2010-12-21 |accessdate=2013-07-22[/tpl] The company targets and sells to the "Verge Culture” demographic, a multicultural group referred to as the first generation “raised on the internet" typically between the ages of 18 to 34 years-old.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.boston.com/business/articles/2011/07/10/up_and_coming_sites_may_make_boston_a_leader_in_e_commerce/ |title=Up-and-coming sites may make Boston a leader in e-commerce - The Boston Globe |publisher=Boston.com |date=2011-07-10 |accessdate=2013-07-22 |deadurl=yes[/tpl] [tpl]Dead link|date=April 2014|bot=RjwilmsiBot[/tpl]
The company is anchored by its retail website and lifestyle network – www.Karmaloop.com – which attracts millions of unique monthly visitors and includes both e-commerce and original content through internet television and blogs. Karmaloop.com sells over 500 men’s and women’s brands - including upstart lines via its Kazbah entrepreneurial marketplace - to customers in more than 40 countries.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.boston.com/yourtown/boston/backbay/articles/2010/12/12/fashion_forward/ |title=Fashion forward - The Boston Globe |publisher=Boston.com |date=2010-12-12 |accessdate=2013-07-22 |first=Johnny |last=Diaz[/tpl] The company utilizes its global Karmaloop Rep Program – a street team – to drive online sales and build brand loyalty with exclusive word-of-mouth discounts.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.internetretailer.com/2012/01/03/karmaloops-peer-peer-marketing-pays |title=Social Media - Karmaloop’s peer-to-peer marketing pays off |publisher=Internet Retailer |date=2013-03-15 |accessdate=2013-07-22[/tpl]
The Karmaloop family also includes Karmaloop TV, a multi-platform broadband programming network which was launched in 2008. Planning to expand and launch as a 24-hour cable television channel in 2012, Karmaloop TV is helmed by President Katie McEnroe (formerly President of Television & Digital Networks at Lionsgate Entertainment and President of AMC (TV channel)) and has an executive roster which includes Grammy-winning musician and entrepreneur, Pharrell Williams, who serves as Creative Director.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/pharrell-williams-named-creative-director-186878 |title=Pharrell Williams Named Creative Director at Karmaloop TV |publisher=The Hollywood Reporter |date= 2011-05-09|accessdate=2013-07-22[/tpl]
