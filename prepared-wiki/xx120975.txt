[[Saint-Vincent, Aosta Valley]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Aosta Valley

Saint-Vincent is a town and comune in the Aosta Valley region of north-western Italy. Saint-Vincent (575m/1,898 ft), is a popular summer holiday resort with mineral springs (recommended for liver and stomach disorders).

==Geography==

The town is bounded by Ayas, Brusson, Châtillon, Emarèse and Montjovet.
