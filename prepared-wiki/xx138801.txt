[[The Amazing Mr. Bickford]]

CATEGORIES: 1987 films, Direct-to-video animated films, Films directed by Frank Zappa, Stop-motion animated films

The Amazing Mr. Bickford is a video released by Frank Zappa in 1987, containing orchestral pieces by Zappa set to the clay animation of Bruce Bickford. As of December 2011, the video has not yet been released on DVD.
