[[First Baptist Church (Raleigh, North Carolina)]]

CATEGORIES: Baptist churches in North Carolina, Churches in Raleigh, North Carolina, Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in North Carolina

First Baptist Church of Raleigh is a Cooperative Baptist Fellowship (CBF) church located in downtown Raleigh, North Carolina. The church was the first Baptist church in the city of Raleigh and is one of the oldest churches in the area. The church is located on 99 North Salisbury Street, directly across the street from the Capitol. You may contact First Baptist at (919) 832-4485.
