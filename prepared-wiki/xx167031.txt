[[Advanced Technology Development Center]]

CATEGORIES: Georgia Institute of Technology, Organizations established in 1980, Science parks in the United States, Business incubators of the United States

The Advanced Technology Development Center (ATDC) is a science and business incubator in Georgia. It is part of the Enterprise Innovation Institute (EI2) at the Georgia Institute of Technology, and is headquartered in Technology Square.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.catalog.gatech.edu/supportfac/atdc.php|title=Advanced Technology Development Center|publisher=Georgia Tech Research Support Facilities|accessdate=2007-08-08| archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20070817191102/http://www.catalog.gatech.edu/supportfac/atdc.php| archivedate= 17 August 2007 | deadurl= no[/tpl] ATDC was formed in 1980 to stimulate growth in Georgia's technology business base, and admitted its first member company in 1981.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.atdc.org/history.asp|title=History|publisher=Advanced Technology Development Center|accessdate=2007-08-08 |archiveurl = http://web.archive.org/web/20070607203832/http://www.atdc.org/history.asp  |archivedate = 2007-06-07[/tpl] It now has locations in Atlanta and Savannah. In 2011, ATDC expanded its mission by merging with Georgia Tech’s VentureLab and with the Georgia SBIR Assistance Program. ATDC has opened its membership to all technology entrepreneurs in Georgia, from those at the earliest conception stage to the well-established, venture-fundable companies.[tpl]cite web|url=http://atdc.org/about/about|title=About ATDC|publisher=Advanced Technology Development Center|accessdate=2011-10-12[/tpl]
More than 120 companies started there, including firms such as MindSpring (now part of EarthLink) and TransNexus.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.atdc.org/graduates.asp|title=Graduates|publisher=Advanced Technology Development Center|accessdate=2007-08-08 |archiveurl = http://web.archive.org/web/20070626091719/http://www.atdc.org/graduates.asp  |archivedate = 2007-06-26[/tpl] Sponsored companies have created almost 51,000 man-years of employment, generated over $12.7 billion revenue, generated over $100 million in profit to Georgia, and raised over $1 billion in venture capital since 1999.[tpl]cite web|url=http://atdc.org/success.asp|title=Success|publisher=Advanced Technology Development Center|accessdate=2007-08-08 |archiveurl = http://web.archive.org/web/20070608112119/http://www.atdc.org/success.asp  |archivedate = 2007-06-08[/tpl] ATDC has been recognized by Inc. Magazine and Business Week as one of the nation's top incubators, and won several other awards.[tpl]cite news|first=Rifka|last=Rosenwein|url=http://www.inc.com/magazine/20001101/20906.html|title=The Idea Factories|work=Inc.
