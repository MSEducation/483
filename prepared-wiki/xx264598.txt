[[Yegyibauk]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Mandalay District, Amarapura Township

 
Yegyibauk is a river village on the Myitnge River in Amarapura Township, Mandalay District, in the Mandalay Region of central Burma. It is located just to the southwest of Myitnge.[tpl]cite map|title=Google Maps|publisher=Google|accessdate=2 January, 2011[/tpl] [tpl]cite map|title=Bing Maps|publisher= Microsoft and Harris Corporation Earthstar Geographics LLC|accessdate=2 January, 2011[/tpl]
The village was mentioned in the Agricultural Journal of India by the Indian Council of Agricultural Research in 1914.[tpl]cite book|author1=Imperial Council of Agricultural Research (India)|author2=Indian Council of Agricultural Research|author3=India.
