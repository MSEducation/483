Hans Richter (born September 14, 1959 in East Germany) is a retired football forward.
During his club career, Richter played for FC Karl-Marx-Stadt, Lokomotive Leipzig and Kickers Offenbach. He played 15 times for the East Germany national team, scoring 4 goals.
