[[O.H.P. Tanner House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Virginia, Georgian architecture in Virginia, Houses completed in 1769, Houses in Mecklenburg County, Virginia, National Register of Historic Places in Mecklenburg County, Virginia

O.H.P. Tanner House, locally known as the Old Courthouse, is a historic home located at La Crosse, Mecklenburg County, Virginia. It was built about 1769, and is a 1 1/2-story, 28-foot square, gambrel-roofed, double-pile Georgian style frame dwelling.  The house was remodeled about 1820, and a rear ell added about 1923.  It was moved to its present location in 2006, and a substantial rehabilitation was completed in 2009.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.dhr.virginia.gov/registers/Counties/Mecklenburg/058-5108_OHP_Tanner_House_2011_NR_FINAL.pdf|title=National Register of Historic Places Inventory/Nomination: O.H.P. Tanner House|author=Mary Harding Sadler and Llewellyn Hensley; Susan Bracey Sheppard| date=May 2011 |publisher=Virginia Department of Historic Resources[/tpl] and Accompanying six photos
It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2011.
