[[HTMS Phutthaloetla Naphalai]]

CATEGORIES: Ships of the Royal Thai Navy, Phutthayotfa Chulalok-class frigates, 1970 ships

HTMS ''Phutthaloetla Naphalai'' (FFG 462) ([tpl]lang-th|พุทธเลิศหล้านภาลัย [/tpl]) is the former [tpl]USS|Ouellet|FF-1077[/tpl], a [tpl]Sclass|Knox|frigate[/tpl].
The Royal Thai Navy purchased the ship from the US Navy after she was decommissioned on August 6, 1993. The ship subsequently underwent a US $14M refit at the Cascade General Shipyard, Portland, Oregon, and arrived in Thailand in 1998.
As a [tpl]Sclass|Phutthayotfa Chulalok|frigate|1[/tpl], she has a sister ship, the HTMS Phutthayotfa Chulalok (FFG 461).
