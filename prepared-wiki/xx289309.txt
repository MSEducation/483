[[Russell Mills (architect)]]

CATEGORIES: American architects, People from Nevada, NRHP architects, 1892 births, 1959 deaths

Russell Mills (1892-1959) was an American architect based in Reno, Nevada.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.livingplaces.com/people/russell-mills.html |title=Russell Mills[/tpl]  A number of his works are listed on the U.S. National Register of Historic Places.[tpl]NRISref|version=2010a[/tpl]
He "spent early years" in the Philippines.[tpl]cite web|url=http://museums.nevadaculture.org/dmdocuments/dcbee10_09.pdf |title=lecture |publisher=DC Bee[/tpl]
He worked as a draftsman for noted architect Frederic DeLongchamps.
He opened his own practice in about 1936.
He was one of the first members of the Nevada State Board of Architecture.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.aianevada.org/displaycommon.cfm?an=1&subarticlenbr=1 |title=AIA NSB[/tpl]
