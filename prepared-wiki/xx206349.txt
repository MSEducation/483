[[Brighton Evangelical Congregational Church]]

CATEGORIES: Religious buildings completed in 1922, 20th-century church buildings, Churches in Boston, Massachusetts, Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in Massachusetts

Brighton Evangelical Congregational Church is a historic church at 404-410 Washington Street in Brighton, a neighborhood of Boston, Massachusetts.
The church was designed in 1922 by Blackall, Clapp and Whittemore and C.S. Allen and Company and added to the National Historic Register in 1997.
