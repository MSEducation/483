[[Kaspars Dubra]]

CATEGORIES: 1990 births, Living people, Sportspeople from Riga, Latvian footballers, Skonto FC players, FK Ventspils players, Latvia international footballers, Latvian expatriate footballers, Polonia Bytom players, Expatriate footballers in Poland, Latvian expatriates in Poland, JFK Olimps players

Kaspars Dubra (born 20 December 1990) is a Latvian professional football defender, currently playing for FK Ventspils in the Latvian Higher League. He plays as a centre-back and as a defensive midfielder. Attack starter and good header.[tpl]citation needed|date=February 2014[/tpl]

==Club career==

===Skonto Riga===

Dubra started playing football at the age of seven, as a youth player he played for Skonto Riga academy JFC Skonto. In 2007 he was taken to the first team by that time manager Paul Ashworth, but didn't appear on the pitch that season.
Before the start of the 2008 season Dubra was loaned out to another Latvian Higher League club located in Riga JFK Olimps. He played 25 matches through the season and managed to score twice. In 2009 Dubra was taken back to Skonto, but, playing only one match, was once again loaned to Olimps, playing 9 games and scoring once during this loan spell.
In 2010, Dubra was one of several Olimps players to be taken to Skonto by the club's new manager Aleksandrs Starkovs. Dubra made a surprisingly good impact and earned himself a place in the starting line-up right after joining. Being one of the best defenders in the league during the season, all in all he made 19 appearances and scored 5 goals for Skonto and helped his team become the champions of Latvian Higher League. In July 2010 he was named the best player of the month, being the only defender to receive this award during the season.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.lff.lv/lv/news/2010/augusts/20/ |title=LFF: LMT Virslīga: Kaspars Dubra atzīts par labāko spēlētāju JŪLIJĀ |publisher=Lff.lv |date=1990-12-20 |accessdate=2014-02-15[/tpl] At the end of the season he was included in the team of the tournament. In November 2010 Dubra went on trial with the English Premier League club Blackpool.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.kasjauns.lv/lv/zinas/33532/skonto-aizsargs-dubra-stazesies-blackpool |title="Skonto" aizsargs Dubra stažēsies "Blackpool" |publisher=Kasjauns.lv |date= |accessdate=2014-02-15[/tpl]
In 2011, Dubra suffered from a serious injury and managed to return to the pitch only at the end of the season, playing only 6 league matches.[tpl]NFT player|pid=40591[/tpl] On January 1 his contract with Skonto Riga expired, and Kaspars went on trial with the English Premier League club Wolverhampton Wanderers, Kaspars has left a good impression on Mick McCarthy coach of Wolverhampton Wanderers, but his agent couldn't agree on deal.[tpl]cite web|author= |url=http://sports.delfi.lv/news/football/news/kaspars-dubra-uz-parbaudes-laiku-pievienojas-anglijas-premjerligas-klubam-wanderers.d?id=42039050 |title=Kaspars Dubra uz pārbaudes laiku pievienojas Anglijas premjerlīgas klubam 'Wanderers' - DELFI |publisher=Sports.delfi.lv |date=2012-01-09 |accessdate=2014-02-15[/tpl]

===Polonia Bytom===

Being linked with several Polish, Russian and Ukrainian clubs, Dubra signed with Polish I liga club Polonia Bytom for one and a half year contract with them in February 2012. After playing just 4 league games in July 2012 he terminated contract with Polonia Bytom.[tpl]cite web|url=http://sportacentrs.com/futbols/legionari/21022012-dubra_polijas_1_ligas_kluba_bitomas_polon |title=Oficiāli: Dubram līgums ar Polijas 1. līgas klubu Bitomas "Polonia" – Leģionāri – Futbols – |publisher=Sportacentrs.com |date= |accessdate=2014-02-15[/tpl]

===FK Ventspils===

In July 2012, he joined the Latvian Higher League club FK Ventspils. He signed contract till June 2015.[tpl]citation needed|date=February 2014[/tpl]
In May 2013, Dubra scored an amazing goal against FK Liepājas Metalurgs and brought victory in the cup final in Latvia.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.uefa.com/memberassociations/association=lva/news/newsid=1953640.html | title = Ventspils victorious in Latvian Cup final | publisher = UEFA.com | date = 18 May 2013 | accessdate = 6 December 2013 | first = Mikhail | last = Korolev[/tpl]

==International career==

Dubra is a current member of Latvia U-21. He also played for Latvia U-19. Kaspars was firstly called up to Latvia national football team by coach Aleksandrs Starkovs for a friendly match in Kunming against China on November 17, 2010.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.lff.lv/lv/news/2010/novembris/23/ |title=LFF: Starkovs izsauc 18 kandidātus Ķīnas mačam |publisher=Lff.lv |date= |accessdate=2014-02-15[/tpl] He made his debut, being just 19 years old, coming on as a substitute in the 90th minute.http://www.lff.lv/lv/aizlase/fixtures/2010/friendlies/china-latvia/ [tpl]dead link|date=February 2014[/tpl] So far that is his only full international appearance.http://www.lff.lv/lv/aizlase/speletaji/ [tpl]dead link|date=February 2014[/tpl]
