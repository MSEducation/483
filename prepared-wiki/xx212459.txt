[[Demond Greene]]

Demond Greene (born 15 June 1979) is a dual German-American citizen and professional basketball player. He plays both the point guard and shooting guard positions. He is 1.86 m (6 ft 1 ¼ in) in height and he weighs 86 kg (190 pounds). His father is from the USA and his mother is German. 
Greene currently plays with the number 24 for Bayern Munich. Before transferring to Munich he played with the  number 6 for the Greek club GS Olympia Larissa BC of the A1 Ethniki, which is the highest Greek basketball division. Before that he played two years for Brose Baskets from Bamberg and one year at Alba Berlin. During his time at Bayer Giants Leverkusen (2002-2005), he arised to one of the best German thrower. From 1997 to 2002 Greene played with DJK Würzburg where he was a teammate of Dirk Nowitzki.

==German national team==

Greene is also a member of the Germany national basketball team.
