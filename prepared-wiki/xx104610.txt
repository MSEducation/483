[[National Board of Trade (Sweden)]]

CATEGORIES: Government agencies of Sweden, Economy of Sweden, 17th-century establishments in Sweden, Organizations established in 1651, Trade

Swedish National Board of Trade (Kommerskollegium) is a Swedish government agency that answers to the Ministry for Foreign Affairs. The agency is located in Stockholm.
It is the central administrative body dealing with foreign trade and trade policy and provides the Swedish government with analyses and recommendations. It was founded already in 1651, so it is one of the oldest government agencies in Sweden.
