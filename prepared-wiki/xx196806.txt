[[Kim Gillan]]

CATEGORIES: Montana State Senators, Members of the Montana House of Representatives, Cornell University alumni, 1951 births, Living people, Women state legislators in Montana, Montana Democrats, People from Contra Costa County, California, People from Billings, Montana, University of California, Los Angeles alumni

Kim J. Gillan is a former Democratic Party member of the Montana Senate.  She represented District 24 from 2004 to 2012. She was unable to run for reelection in 2012 due to Montana's term limits. Earlier she was a member of the Montana House of Representatives from 1996 through 2004.
