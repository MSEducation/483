[[Elachista littoricola]]

CATEGORIES: Elachistidae, Animals described in 1938, Moths of Europe, Insects of Germany, Insects of Estonia, Insects of Latvia, Insects of Finland, Insects of Italy

Elachista littoricola is a moth of the Elachistidae family. It is found in Great Britain, France, Germany, Denmark, the Czech Republic, Italy, Latvia, Estonia, Finland and Russia.
The wingspan is about 7–8 mm.Hants Moths
The larvae feed on Elytrigia repens. They mine the leaves of their host plant.
