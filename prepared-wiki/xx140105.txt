[[Harkujärve]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Harju County

Harkujärve ([tpl]lang-de|Harkojärw[/tpl]) is a village in Harku Parish, Harju County in northern Estonia. It has a population of 564 (as of 1 June 2010).[tpl]cite web|title=Üldinfo|url=http://www.harku.ee/index.php|publisher=Harku vald[/tpl] The village is located on the western and southwestern side of Lake Harku.
