[[News from Nowhere (album)]]

CATEGORIES: 1995 albums, Air Supply albums

News from Nowhere is the thirteenth studio album by Australian soft rock band Air Supply released in 1995. The album marks a turn for the band from pop to more adult contemporary centered themes. Single "Someone" reached #60 in US Adult Contemporary charts.Biographies: Air Supply 8-12-08 The interpretation of the song "Unchained Melody" and single "Always" both have become favourites in the band's repertoire.
