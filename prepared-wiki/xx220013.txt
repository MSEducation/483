[[Georgina Póta]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 births, Hungarian table tennis players, Table tennis players at the 2008 Summer Olympics, Olympic table tennis players of Hungary, Living people, Table tennis players at the 2012 Summer Olympics

Georgina Póta (born 13 January 1985 in Budapest) is a Hungarian table tennis player.
She won gold medal in the team competition at the 2007 Table Tennis European Championships in Belgrade, silver medal in women's doubles and bronze medal in mixed doubles.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ittf.com/ittf_stats/All_events3.asp?ID=5699 |title=ITTF_Database |publisher=Ittf.com |date= [/tpl][tpl]Wayback |date=20080907182251 |url=http://results.beijing2008.cn/WRM/ENG/BIO/Athlete/4/232104.shtml |title=Biography at the 2008 Beijing official website[/tpl]
She also competed at the 2008 Olympic Games in Beijing, where she was knocked out in the third round by Wang Nan of China who later managed to win the silver medal.
