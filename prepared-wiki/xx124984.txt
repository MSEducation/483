[[List of airports in Ukraine]]

CATEGORIES: Lists of airports by country, Airports in Ukraine, Ukraine transport-related lists, Lists of buildings and structures in Ukraine, Lists of busiest airports

This is a list of airports in Ukraine, grouped by type and sorted by location.
Ukraine is a country in Eastern Europe. It borders the Russian Federation to the east and northeast, Belarus to the northwest, Poland, Slovakia and Hungary to the west, Romania and Moldova to the southwest, and the Black Sea and Sea of Azov to the south and southeast, respectively.
Ukraine is a unitary state composed of 24 oblasts (provinces), one autonomous republic (Crimea), and two cities with special status: Kiev, its capital and largest city, and Sevastopol.
