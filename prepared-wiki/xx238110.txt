[[Liberation Front of the State of Cabinda]]

CATEGORIES: Secessionist organizations, Political parties established in 1996, History of Angola, National liberation movements, Cabinda Province, Rebel groups in Angola

The Front for the Liberation of the State of Cabinda (Frente de Libertação do Estado de Cabinda (FLEC(Lopes))) is a separatist group created by Cabindese ex-patriates in the Netherlands in 1996. This group adopted a blue, yellow and black flag with the Silambuco monument in the center.
