[[Jomehlu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Jomehlu ([tpl]lang-fa|جمعه لو[/tpl], also Romanized as Jom‘ehlū; also known as Dzhamlu and Jum‘alu)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3068056|Jomehlu[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 117, in 24 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
