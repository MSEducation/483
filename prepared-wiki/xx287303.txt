[[Innocence (2013 film)]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 films, Films based on horror novels, Films based on American novels, Vampires in film, 2013 horror films, 2010s drama films, American horror films, American drama films, Killer Films films, American films

Innocence is a 2013 American horror drama film directed by Hilary Brougher and co-written with Tristine Skyler, based on a 2000 novel of same name written by Jane Mendelsohn. The film stars Sophie Curtis, Kelly Reilly, Graham Phillips, Linus Roache, Sarah Sutherland and Stephanie March.

==Plot==
