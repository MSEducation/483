Marc Allen Wilkins (born October 21, 1970 in Mansfield, Ohio) is an American former Major League Baseball player. A pitcher, Wilkins played for the Pittsburgh Pirates from [tpl]mlby|1996[/tpl]-[tpl]mlby|2001[/tpl]. Wilkins attended high school at Ontario High School in Ontario, Ohio and college at the University of Toledo.
