[[Overstrand railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Disused railway stations in Norfolk, Former Norfolk and Suffolk Joint Railway stations, Railway stations closed in 1953

Overstrand railway station was a station in North Norfolk on the Norfolk and Suffolk Joint Railway, serving the settlement of Overstrand. It was much used in the summer months by holidaymakers. It closed along with the rest of the line in April 1953.British Railways Atlas.1947. p.18
