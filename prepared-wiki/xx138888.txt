[[Haiti at the 1988 Summer Olympics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 1988 Summer Olympics, Haiti at the Olympics, 1988 in Haiti

Haiti competed at the 1988 Summer Olympics in Seoul, Korea. Haiti's delegation consisted of four competitors and one official.

Men's 100 meters
Men's 200 meters
