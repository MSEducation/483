[[Eye Green]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Cambridgeshire, Geography of Peterborough

[tpl]Unreferenced|date=February 2009[/tpl]

Eye Green is a village in the unitary authority of Peterborough in England. It lies north of Eye and south of Crowland.
Eye Green Nature Reserve abounds the A47 Trunk Road which separated Eye Green from its sister village of Eye.
Eye Green Nature Reserve used to be the site of a brickworks.
