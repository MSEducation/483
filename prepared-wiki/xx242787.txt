[[Kozjak nad Pesnico]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Municipality of Kungota

Kozjak nad Pesnico is a dispersed settlement in the Municipality of Kungota in the western part of the Slovenian Hills ([tpl]lang-sl|Slovenske gorice[/tpl]) in northeastern Slovenia.Kungota municipal site

==Name==

The name of the settlement was changed from Kozjak to Kozjak nad Pesnico in 1955.Spremembe naselij 1948–95. 1996. Database. Ljubljana: Geografski inštitut ZRC SAZU, DZS.

==Cultural heritage==

There are two mansions in the settlement. Pahta Mansion ([tpl]lang-sl|Graščina Pahta[/tpl] or Pahtejev Grad) was built in 1870 in the hills south of Zgornja Kungota. Lepi Dol Mansion, built in the Pesnica Valley, is an early 18th-century Baroque mansion on the site of an earlier building. It was renovated in a Neoclassical style in the 19th century.Slovenian Ministry of Culture register of national heritage reference numbers 7768 and 7769
