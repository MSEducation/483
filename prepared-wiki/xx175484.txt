[[KCBK]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Oklahoma

KCBK is a Christian radio station licensed to Frederick, Oklahoma, broadcasting on 91.5 MHz FM.  The station serves the areas of Lawton, Oklahoma and Wichita Falls, Texas and is owned by First Dallas Media, Inc.KCBK fcc.gov. Accessed August 22, 2012
KCBK programming includes Christian music and Christian talk and teaching.  Christian talk and teaching shows heard on KCBK include; Grace to You with John MacArthur, Back to the Bible with Woodrow Kroll, Focus on the Family,  In Touch with Charles Stanley, Love Worth Finding with Adrian Rogers, The Alternative with Tony Evans, and Revive Our Hearts with Nancy Leigh DeMoss.
