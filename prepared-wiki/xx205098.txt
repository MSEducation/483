Roman Wörndle (October 4, 1913 – February 2, 1942) was a German alpine skier who competed in the 1936 Winter Olympics.
He was born in Partenkirchen, where he was member of the Skiclub Partenkirchen (SCP).Zehn Sportler vertraten die Heimat bei den Olympischen Spielen 1936 [tpl]de icon[/tpl], Merkur online, August 17, 2004.
In 1936 he finished fifth in the alpine skiing combined event.
