[[Ye with breve]]

Ye with breve ([tpl]Unicode|Ӗ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|ӗ[/tpl]; italics: [tpl]Unicode|Ӗ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|ӗ[/tpl]) is a letter of the Cyrillic script. In Unicode, this letter is called "Ie with breve"."Cyrillic: Range: 0400–04FF". pp 38–43 of The Unicode Standard, Version 6.0 (2010). p. 43. Retrieved 2011-04-25.
