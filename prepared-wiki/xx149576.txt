[[Jinling College]]

CATEGORIES: Education in Nanjing

Jinling College (Chinese: 金陵学院) is a private college in Nanjing, China. It was founded in 1998 and is located on the Pukou Campus of Nanjing University. It was dedicated as Jinling Institute in 2001. 
The school has a 93% employment rate for graduating students.[tpl]Citation needed|reason=reliable source needed for the whole sentence|date=March 2014[/tpl]
