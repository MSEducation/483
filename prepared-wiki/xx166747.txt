[[1957 Giro d'Italia]]

CATEGORIES: Giro d'Italia, 1957 in cycle racing, 1957 in Italian sport, 1957 in road cycling

The 1957 Giro d'Italia of cycling was held from 18 May to 9 June 1957, consisting of 21 stages.
