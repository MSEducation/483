[[Minamiamabe District, Ōita]]

CATEGORIES: Former districts of Ōita Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Minamiamabe|南海部郡|Minamiamabe-gun[/tpl] was a district located in Ōita Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the district had an estimated population of 33,239 and the density of 47.09 persons per km². The total area was 705.90 km².
