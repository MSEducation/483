[[Shuzenji Station]]

CATEGORIES: Izuhakone Sunzu Line, Railway stations in Shizuoka Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Shuzenji Station|修善寺駅|Shuzenji-eki[/tpl] is the southern terminal train station of the Izuhakone Railway’s Sunzu Line located in the city of Izu, Shizuoka Prefecture, Japan. It is 19.8 rail kilometers from the line’s northern terminus at Mishima Station.

==History==

Shuzenji Station was opened on August 1, 1924 as part of the final extension of the Sunzu line from Ōhito Japanese Wikipedia entry

==Lines==

==Station layout==

Shuzenji Station has two island platforms serving four tracks. Tracks 1 and 2 are used for local service only; Tracks 3 and 4 are used for both local and limited express Odoriko services. The station building has both a manned service counter and automatic ticket machines.
