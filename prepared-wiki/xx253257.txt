[[New Jersey Museum of Agriculture]]

CATEGORIES: University museums in New Jersey, Agriculture museums in the United States, Museums in Middlesex County, New Jersey, History museums in New Jersey, Buildings and structures in New Brunswick, New Jersey, Rutgers University, Visitor attractions in New Brunswick, New Jersey

The New Jersey Museum of Agriculture, located in New Brunswick, New Jersey, focuses on the evolution of agriculture in New Jersey.  The museum's exhibits include farm tools and machinery, household implements, scientific instruments, trade tools, farm vehicles, early electrical appliances and a general store.  Other displays tell the story of important agricultural crops in New Jersey, including apples, cranberries, tomatoes, blueberries, corn and potatoes.
The museum opened in 1990 and located on the campus of the School of Environmental and Biological Sciences  of Rutgers University.
