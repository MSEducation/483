[[Fun Radio (France)]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in France, RTL Group, Radio stations established in 1985

Fun Radio is a French network of FM radio stations created on 2 October 1985 and dedicated to R&B, Dance music, Electro music and House music, operating on 250 different frequencies across France. The station belongs to RTL Group as do its sisters stations RTL and RTL2. These three radio stations share the same headquarters located in the 8th arrondissement of Paris.
Fun Radio claims approximately 3.8 million listeners every day (January 2012).
