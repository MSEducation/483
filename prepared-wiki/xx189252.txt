[[Murder Madness]]

CATEGORIES: 1931 novels, 1930s science fiction novels, American science fiction novels, Novels first published in serial form, Works originally published in Analog Science Fiction and Fact

Murder Madness is a science fiction novel by author Murray Leinster.  It was first published in book form in 1931 by Brewer and Warren.  It was Leinster's first book.  The novel was originally serialized in four parts in the magazine Astounding beginning in May 1930.

==Plot introduction==

The novel concerns a tyrant who attempts to control civilization by using a madness-inducing drug.
