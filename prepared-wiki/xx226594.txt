[[Screenslaves]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 albums, Paragon albums, Massacre Records albums

Screenslaves is the ninth studio album by Paragon. It is their first album released under Massacre Records, and also the first album to feature Dirk Seifert on bass. The digipak version of the album includes the video for "Screenslaves".
