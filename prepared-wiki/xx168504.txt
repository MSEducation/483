Fakhraddin Manaf oglu Manafov ([tpl]lang-az| Fəxrəddin Manafov[/tpl]) (born 2 August 1955, Stepanakert, NKAO, Soviet Azerbaijan) is an Azerbaijani and Soviet actor.
He was born in Stepanakert (Khankandi), capital of Soviet Azerbaijan's Nagorno-Karabakh Autonomous Oblast. His family moved to Baku when he was around 5. He made his first movie appearance in 1978. In 1980, Manafov graduated from the Azerbaijan State University of Culture and Arts and started working with Azerbaijanfilm.[tpl]ru icon[/tpl] Fakhraddin Manafov. Kino-teatr.ru
