[[Paungbyin]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Sagaing Region, Township capitals of Burma

Paungbyin,"Paungbyin" Geonames database, United States National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency Pyaungbin"Paungbin" Geonames database, United States National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency or Phaungbyin is a town in Mawlaik District, Sagaing Division, of Burma (Myanmar), on the Chindwin River. It is the principal town of Paungbyin Township.
