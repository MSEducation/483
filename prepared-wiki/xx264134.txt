Christian Kauter (born 6 May 1947) is a Swiss fencer. He won a silver medal in the team épée event at the 1972 Summer Olympics and a bronze in the same event at the 1976 Summer Olympics.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.databaseolympics.com/players/playerpage.htm?ilkid=KAUTECHR01 |title=Olympics Statistics: Christian Kauter |accessdate=2011-01-02 |work=databaseolympics.com[/tpl][tpl]cite web |url=http://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/ka/christian-kauter-1.html |title=Christian Kauter Olympic Results |accessdate=2011-01-02 |work=sports-reference.com[/tpl]
