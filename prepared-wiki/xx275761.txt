[[Lucy Blake]]

CATEGORIES: American conservationists, Living people

Lucy Blake is an American conservationist, President of the Northern Sierra Partnership.[tpl]citation needed|date=April 2013[/tpl] She was a 2000 MacArthur Fellow. 
She founded the Sierra Business Council and won the Pat Brown Award.[tpl]cite web|title=Mary Nichols wins the 2013 Pat Brown Award for leadership on cap-and-trade.|url=http://www.cceeb.org/events/pba.html|work=PAT BROWN AWARD|publisher=California Council for Environmental and Economic Balance|accessdate=10 April 2013[/tpl]
Blake works for the Department of Energy on Energy and Natural Resources.[tpl]cite web|title=Obama Administration: Key Positions|url=http://livableworld.org/news/obama_key_positions/|work=Obama Administration positions as of December 2010|publisher=A Council for a Livable World|accessdate=10 April 2013[/tpl]
