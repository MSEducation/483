[[Yegor Shamov]]

CATEGORIES: 1994 births, Living people, Russian footballers

Yegor Valentinovich Shamov ([tpl]lang-ru|Егор Валентинович Шамов[/tpl]; born June 2, 1994) is a Russian football goalkeeper, who currently playing for FC Biolog-Novokubansk Progress.
He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Biolog-Novokubansk Progress on August 9, 2013 in a game against FC Vityaz Krymsk.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Russian Football Union|url=http://rfspro.ru/cgi-bin/player/script2.cgi?par1=119573|title=Career Summary|date=11 August 2013[/tpl]
