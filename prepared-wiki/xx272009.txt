[[Blue Ridge Rollergirls]]

CATEGORIES: Roller derby leagues in North Carolina, Women's Flat Track Derby Association Division 2, Non-profit organizations based in North Carolina, Roller derby leagues established in 2006

The Blue Ridge Rollergirls (BRRG) is a roller derby league based in Asheville, North Carolina, a member of the Women's Flat Track Derby Association (WFTDA). The league was established in 2006,[tpl]cite web|title=About the Blue Ridge Rollergirls|url=http://www.blueridgerollergirls.com/index.php/home/about/|publisher=Blue Ridge Rollergirls[/tpl] held its first competitive season in 2007,[tpl]cite web|last=O’Connor|first=Aislinn|title=BLUE RIDGE ROLLERGIRLS: ASHEVILLE’S DERBY DIVAS|url=http://www.marqueemagazine.com/2008/04/08/blue-ridge-rollergirls-asheville%E2%80%99s-derby-divas/|publisher=Marquee Magazine[/tpl] became a WFTDA apprentice team in 2010,[tpl]cite web|title=WFTDA APPRENTICE PROGRAM WELCOMES 10 NEW LEAGUES|url=http://wftda.com/news/apprentice-program-welcomes-10-new-leagues-october-2010|publisher=WFTDA[/tpl] and a full WFTDA member in March 2012.[tpl]cite web|title=WFTDA WELCOMES 14 NEW MEMBERS|url=http://wftda.com/news/new-members-march-2012|publisher=WFTDA[/tpl][tpl]cite web|last=Rhodes|first=Bill|title=Blue Ridge Rollergirls accepted to international association|url=http://www.mountainx.com/article/40883/Blue-Ridge-Rollergirls-accepted-to-international-association|publisher=Mountain Xpress Newspaper|accessdate=1 March 2012[/tpl] It is composed of two teams, the All-Stars travel team, and the French Broads, the BRRG B team[tpl]cite web|title=Roller Derby Action In Boone|url=http://imageasheville.com/2011/06/roller-derby-action-in-boone/|publisher=Image Asheville|accessdate=7 October 2011[/tpl] (named for the French Broad River that flows through Asheville). Members of the BRRG recently formed the Mad Divas Junior Derby for girls 12-17.[tpl]cite web|last=Hyworth|first=Tracy|title=Smac and Smithereen|url=http://www.mountainx.com/article/826/Smac-and-Smithereen|publisher=Mountain Xpress|accessdate=7 October 2011[/tpl]
thumb|left|Blue Ridge Rollergirls warming up for a bout doing yoga on skates. Community support of the league is strong with bout attendance typically ranging from 2000 to more than 3000 fans.[tpl]cite web|title=All the Blue Ridge Roller Girls want for Christmas is dedicated space for a training facility|url=http://ashvegas.squarespace.com/journal/tag/blue-ridge-roller-girls|publisher=Ashevegas|accessdate=7 October 2011[/tpl] The league fosters community outreach[tpl]cite web|title=Fly People: Blue Ridge Rollergirls|url=http://www.flyjba.com/people/158/blue-ridge-rollergirls|publisher=Fly JBA[/tpl] and gives a portion of the proceeds of each bout to a western North Carolina charity.[tpl]cite web|title=Blue Ridge Rollergirls|url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lxvDbvqKzrs|publisher=YouTube|accessdate=7 October 2011[/tpl] Charities supported include The Animal Compassion Network, Care Partners Children's Fund, Skye, RAINN, and Interlace.[tpl]cite web|title=Fly People: Blue Ridge Rollergirls|url=http://www.flyjba.com/people/158/blue-ridge-rollergirls|publisher=Fly JBA[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Blue Ridge Rollergirls|url=http://www.animalcompassionnetwork.org/node/283|publisher=Animal Compassion Network|accessdate=7 October 2011[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Special Events: Roller Derby supports our fund for children|url=http://www.carepartnersfoundation.org/pagedisplay.cfm?mainpage=10&subpage=25|publisher=Care Partners Foundation|accessdate=7 October 2011[/tpl]
The league notably prepares for bouts with acupuncture sessions,[tpl]cite web|title=Blue Ridge Roller Girls use acupuncture|url=http://whitepineacupuncture.com/blog_files/category-asheville-acupuncture.html|publisher=Asheville Acupuncture|accessdate=7 October 2011[/tpl] and features synchronized yoga on skates during its warmup laps.[tpl]cite web|title=Blue Ridge Roller Girls Report: Roller Girls dominate; beer taps dry up; ejection for fighting; promise of free popcorn from the Asheville Civic Center; and much more|url=http://ashvegas.squarespace.com/journal/tag/blue-ridge-roller-girls|publisher=Ashevegas|accessdate=7 October 2011[/tpl]
On 2 November 2012, the WFTDA announced that the BRRG would host one of its four national Division 1 playoff tournaments, in Asheville on 20–22 September 2013.[tpl]cite web|title=WFTDA Announces 2013 Tournament Locations|url=http://wftda.com/news/2013-tournament-locations|publisher=WFTDA|accessdate=3 November 2012[/tpl]
