Princess Maria Ludwiga Theresia of Bavaria (6 July 1872[tpl]spaced ndash[/tpl]10 June 1954) was a daughter of the last King of Bavaria, Ludwig III, and his wife, Maria Theresia of Austria-Este.[tpl]cite web | url=http://thepeerage.com/p10565.htm#i105646| title=Marie Prinzessin von Bayern | date=24 May 2004 | accessdate=2008-10-05 | publisher=thePeerage.com | author=Darryl Lundy[/tpl][tpl]cite web | url=http://www.angelfire.com/realm/gotha/gotha/bavaria.html| title=BAVARIA | date= | accessdate=2008-10-05 | publisher=Paul Theroff's Royal Genealogy Site | author=Paul Theroff[/tpl] Her husband, Prince Ferdinand Pius, Duke of Calabria, became the head of the House of Bourbon-Two Sicilies in 26 May 1934, thus she was titular queen consort of the Two Sicilies.
