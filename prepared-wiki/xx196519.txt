[[Yu Nakajima]]

CATEGORIES: 1991 births, Living people

[tpl]nihongo|Yu Nakajima|中島 悠|extra=born February 15, 1991 in Ebetsu, Hokkaidō[/tpl] is a Japanese Rubik's Cube solver.
Yu held the former world record for Rubik's Cube average (11.28 seconds) and single (8.72 seconds).[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.worldcubeassociation.org/results/p.php?i=2007NAKA03|title=Official results of Yu Nakajima. |accessdate=2008-05-21|work=worldcubeassociation.org[/tpl] He beat the previous world record holder Edouard Chambon, who had a single solve record of 9.18 seconds.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.worldcubeassociation.org/results/p.php?i=2004CHAM01|title=Official results of Edouard Chambon.|accessdate=2008-05-21|work=worldcubeassociation.org[/tpl] Both records were set on May 5, 2008, at the Kashiwa Open 2008. He set the World Record for the 5x5x5 cube at 54.86 seconds in April.
On May 23, 2008 Yu posted a video on YouTube where he completed the cube in 6.57 seconds.Rubik's Cube: 6.57 seconds - nakaji1084 May 23, 2008 He also solved the Rubik's magic in 1.05 seconds.
