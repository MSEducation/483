[[Wild Child (band)]]

CATEGORIES: American indie rock groups

Wild Child is an American indie-folk pop band from Austin, Texas. Members are: primary singers/songwriters Kelsey Wilson and Alexander Beggins,http://www.popmatters.com/feature/175631-now-hear-this-wild-child-the-runaround-album-premiere/ drummer Carey McGraw, cellist Sadie Wolfe, keyboardist Evan Magers and bassist Chris D'Annunzio.http://eyeinthesky.org/wildchild/about/ They are managed by Pat Cassidy.http://www.popmatters.com/feature/175631-now-hear-this-wild-child-the-runaround-album-premiere/
Their first album, the 15-track Pillow Talk,Mongillo, Peter (14 November 2011). "Local CDs: Wild Child, Scott Biram, Will Sexton, and Sons of Fathers: Austin sounds from new faces, old friends", Austin American-Statesman. Retrieved 10 September 2013. was released on October 25, 2011 http://schedule.sxsw.com/2012/events/event_MS17237 (with help from San Francisco producers Evan Magers and Alex Peterson, and mastered by Erik Wofford).http://eyeinthesky.org/wildchild/about/ Their second album, The Runaround, was released on October 8, 2013 via The Noise Company and produced by Ben Kweller. http://www.popmatters.com/feature/175631-now-hear-this-wild-child-the-runaround-album-premiere/
The band was named by the Austin Chronicle as the best indie, hipster band and the best folk band of the 2013 South by Southwest (SXSW) music festival.[tpl]Cite web | url = http://www.austinchronicle.com/Awards/MusicAwards/?category=Best%20Performing%20Bands | title = Best Performing Bands | publisher = Austin Chronicle | accessdate = 12 August 2013 [/tpl] In August 2013, the band performed as the opening act for the John Butler Trio.
