[[Sam Adkins]]

CATEGORIES: 1955 births, Living people, Sportspeople from Los Angeles, California, American football quarterbacks, Wichita State Shockers football players, Seattle Seahawks players

Samuel Adam Adkins (born May 21, 1955 in Van Nuys, California) is a former professional American football player. He attended Cleveland High School in Reseda, CA and Wichita State University. He played for the NFL Seattle Seahawks from 1977â€“1983, and is the only member of the Seahawks to wear the number 12. Following his final year, the number 12 was retired for the Seattle Seahawks fans (aka the "12th man"). He and his wife Dayle have 4 children.
Since his retirement, he has worked in broadcasting in the Seattle area, as the color commentator for the University of Washington football radio broadcasts and more extensively as a host and correspondent on the Seahawks post-game show.
