[[Geometric Centre of the Republic of Slovenia]]

CATEGORIES: Geography of Slovenia, Visitor attractions in Slovenia, Geographical centres, Municipality of Litija

The Geometric Centre of the Republic of Slovenia ([tpl]lang-sl|Geometrično središče Republike Slovenije[/tpl], GEOSS) is the geometric centre of the country. Its geographic coordinates are  [tpl]coord|46|07|11.8|N|14|48|55.2|E|type:landmark_region:SL|display=inline,title|[/tpl] and its elevation is 644.842 m.Inscription on the GEOSS monument. It lies in the hamlet of Spodnja Slivna near Vače in the municipality of Litija. Since 4 July 1982,Društvo za razvoj in varovanje Geossa. GEOSS. Information sign posted in Slivna. it has been marked with a memorial stone designed by the architect Marjan Božič. A plaque reading Živimo in gospodarimo na svoji zemlji ('We live and prosper upon our land') was added on 14 September 1989. In 2003, Slovenia adopted an act on GEOSS.
