[[Anthurium andreanum 'Royal Flush']]

CATEGORIES: Anthurium

'Anthurium andreanum'' 'Royal Flush is a cultivar from the species Anthurium andraeanum. It has hot pink spathes with a dark spadix that has tones of pink. The leaves are a dark green.
