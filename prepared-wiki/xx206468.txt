[[Mingjian, Nantou]]

CATEGORIES: Townships in Nantou County

Mingjian Township ([tpl]zh|t=名間鄉|p=Míngjiān Xiāng|w=Ming2-chien1 Xiang1|poj=Bêng-kan Hiong[/tpl]) is a rural township in west central of Nantou County, Taiwan.

==Naming==

Its name originates from a Japanese transliteration of the Taiwanese word, Lama ([tpl]zh|t=湳仔|p=nǎnzǎi|poj=Lâm-á[/tpl]), meaning a very wet place.[ref]

==History==

Mingjian was a hunting ground for the Taiwanese aborigines. 
In 2008, the 100th congregation of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in Taiwan was organized in Mingjian.
