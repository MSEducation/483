[[Collapsing Opposites]]

CATEGORIES: Musical groups established in 2002, Musical groups from Vancouver, Canadian indie rock groups, 2002 establishments in Canada

Collapsing Opposites is an indie rock group and music project from Vancouver, BC. It was founded in 2002 and has subsequently included over 18 different members.[tpl]Citation needed|date=February 2011[/tpl]
