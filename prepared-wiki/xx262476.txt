[[Conor McLaughlin]]

CATEGORIES: 1991 births, Living people, Association footballers from Northern Ireland, Northern Ireland under-21 international footballers, Northern Ireland international footballers, Association football defenders, Preston North End F.C. players, Shrewsbury Town F.C. players, Fleetwood Town F.C. players, The Football League players

Conor Gerard McLaughlin (born 26 July 1991) is a Northern Irish international footballer who plays professionally for Fleetwood Town, as a right back.

==Career==

===Club career===

====Preston North End====

Born in Belfast, McLaughlin turned professional with Preston North End in January 2010.[tpl]cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/p/preston/8467970.stm|title=Preston hand Conor McLaughlin professional contract|accessdate=2 January 2011|date=19 January 2010|publisher=BBC Sport[/tpl] He made his début for Preston on 12 November 2010, in a 0–2 loss against Hull City.[tpl]cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/eng_div_1/9176033.stm|title=Preston 0 – 2 Hull|accessdate=2 January 2011|date=12 November 2010|publisher=BBC Sport[/tpl] In December 2010, McLaughlin signed a contract extension, keeping him with Preston until 2013.[tpl]cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/p/preston/9310849.stm|title=Conor McLaughlin signs Preston contract extension|accessdate=2 January 2011|date=21 December 2010|publisher=BBC Sport[/tpl] He signed a two-month loan deal with Shrewsbury Town in March 2011.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/17232132|title=Shrewsbury Town sign Preston defender Conor McLaughlin|date=2 March 2012|accessdate=2 March 2012|publisher=BBC Sport[/tpl]
He was transfer listed by the club in May 2012.[tpl]cite news|title=Graham Westley wields the axe at Preston North End|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/18024194|accessdate=11 May 2012|newspaper=BBC Sport|date=10 May 2012[/tpl]
