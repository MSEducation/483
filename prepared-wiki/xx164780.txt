Sami Ristilä (born 15 August 1974, in Valkeakoski) is a Finnish football manager and former player who has been managing FC Haka since September 2010 in the Veikkausliiga.http://www.fchaka.fi/etusivu/667-sami-ristilaestae-hakan-uusi-paeaevalmentaja

==Career==

Ristilä played an important role in the Drogheda midfield and sometimes played up front. He has represented the Finland national side on two occasions. Before he joined Drogheda Ristilä played for MyPa, SC Neukirchen, FC Zwolle and FC Haka. He played three seasons with Zwolle in the Dutch Eerste divisie.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Voetbal International|title=Sami Ristilä|url=http://www.vi.nl/Spelers/Speler/Sami-Ristil.htm|language=Dutch|accessdate=2009-09-23[/tpl] In December 2008 he returned to FC Haka and signed a contract to 30 December 2011.
He has played 247 games in the Veikkausliiga.
