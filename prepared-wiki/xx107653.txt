[[Lymington Pier railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Buildings and structures in Lymington, Railway stations in Hampshire, Former London and South Western Railway stations, Railway stations opened in 1884, Railway stations served by South West Trains, DfT Category F1 stations, Railway stations serving harbours and ports in the United Kingdom

Lymington Pier railway station is a railway station serving the harbour area of Lymington in Hampshire, England. It the terminus of the Lymington Branch Line from Brockenhurst, and provides a connection with the ferry service to Yarmouth on the Isle of Wight. It has only one platform.
The station is served by South West Trains, who operate two trains an hour during the week.
