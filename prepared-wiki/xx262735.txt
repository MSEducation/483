[[Petri Lammassaari]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 births, Living people, Espoo Blues players, Finnish ice hockey players, KalPa players

Petri Lammassaari (born August 6, 1985) is a Finnish professional ice hockey forward. He is currently playing with KalPa in the Finnish Liiga.
