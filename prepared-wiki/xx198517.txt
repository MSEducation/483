[[KIQQ (AM)]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in California, Spanish-language radio stations in California

KIQQ (1310 AM) is a radio station  broadcasting a Regional Mexican format. Located in Barstow, California, USA, the station serves the High Desert/ Eastern Sierra area.  The station is currently owned by MBR Licensee LLC.
