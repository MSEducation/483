[[Barney Mellows]]

Barney (Herbert) Charles MellowsRecorded erroneously in Oireachtas Members Database as George Mellows. (c. 1896–1942) was an Irish Sinn Féin politician, and the brother of Liam Mellows.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oireachtas.ie/members-hist/default.asp?housetype=0&HouseNum=4&MemberID=2086&ConstID=102|title=Mr. Barney Mellows|work=Oireachtas Members Database|accessdate=9 May 2012[/tpl]
He was elected at the 1923 general election as Teachta Dála (TD) for the Galway constituency, but in accordance with Sinn Féin's abstentionist policy, he did not take his seat.[tpl]cite web|url=http://electionsireland.org/candidate.cfm?id=1280|title=Barney Mellows|work=ElectionsIreland.org|accessdate=9 May 2012[/tpl] He was defeated in the June 1927 general election, and did not stand again.
