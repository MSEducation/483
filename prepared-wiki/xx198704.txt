[[C. Randy Taylor]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing, Year of death missing, Kentucky State Thorobreds football coaches

C. Randolph "Randy" Taylor was the tenth head football coach for the Kentucky State University Thorobreds located in Frankfort, Kentucky and he held that position for four seasons, from 1947 until 1950.  His career coaching record at Kentucky State was 21 wins, 17 losses, and 2 ties.  As of the conclusion of the 2007 season, this ranks him sixth at Kentucky State in total wins and sixth at Kentucky State in winning percentage (.550).Kentucky State University coaching records
