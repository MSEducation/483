[[János Pap]]

CATEGORIES: 1925 births, 1994 deaths, Hungarian communists, Hungarian politicians, Hungarian Interior Ministers, Hungarian politicians who committed suicide, Suicides in Hungary

János Pap (1925–1994) was a Hungarian communist politician, who served as Interior Minister between 1961–1963. Member of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party (1959–1985). First secretary of the HSWP in Veszprém county (1956–1961, 1965–1985). Pap committed suicide.
