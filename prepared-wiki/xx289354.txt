[[Watermark (film)]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 films, 2010s documentary films, Canadian films, Canadian documentary films, Documentary films about water and the environment, Films directed by Jennifer Baichwal

[tpl]Use dmy dates|date=August 2013[/tpl]
Watermark is a 2013 Canadian documentary film by Jennifer Baichwal and Edward Burtynsky. It concerns the history and use of water. Burtynsky was previously the subject of Baichwal's 2006 documentary, Manufactured Landscapes.
The film was recorded in various international locations using ultra high definition equipment.[tpl]cite news | url=http://www.cbc.ca/news/arts/story/2013/08/07/tiff-cdn-lineup-announcement.html?cmp=rss | title=Talented veterans, emerging directors make TIFF's Canadian lineup | date=7 August 2013 | publisher=CBC News | accessdate=13 August 2013 [/tpl]
The film won the Rogers Best Canadian Film Award at the 2013 Toronto Film Critics Association Awards, over The Dirties and Gabrielle"Critics name Baichwal documentary 'Watermark' best Canadian film". CTV News, 7 January 2014. and was named Best Feature Length Documentary at the Canadian Screen Awards."Canadian Screen Awards: Orphan Black, Less Than Kind, Enemy nominated". CBC News, 13 January 2014.
