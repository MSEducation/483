[[Ole Johannesen Staff]]

CATEGORIES: 1789 births, Year of death missing, Members of the Parliament of Norway, Oppland politicians

Ole Johannesen Staff (1789–??) was a Norwegian politician.
He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament in 1836, 1839 and 1842, representing the rural constituency of Christians Amt (today named Oppland). He worked as a farmer.Ole Johannesen Staff — Norwegian Social Science Data Services (NSD)
