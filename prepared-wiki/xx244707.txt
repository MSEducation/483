[[The Little Snob]]

CATEGORIES: 1928 films, Films directed by John G. Adolfi, Warner Bros. films, Lost films

 
The Little Snob is a 1928 part-talkie comedy from Warner Bros. Pictures. It had Vitaphone sound effects and music.
