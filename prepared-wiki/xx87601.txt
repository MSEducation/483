[[Sandringham railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Premium Melbourne railway stations, Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1887

Sandringham railway station is located on the Sandringham line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the south-eastern Melbourne suburb of Sandringham opening on 2 September 1887.Sandringham Vicsig
A signal box is located at the Flinders Street end of the station, whilst a stabling yard is directly opposite to the station, stabling up to four trains overnight.
It was upgraded to a Premium Station on 30 October 1995.[tpl]cite journal|date=October 1997|title=Upgrading Eltham to a Premium Station|journal=Newsrail|publisher=Australian Railway Historical Society|page=page 310[/tpl]
A tram service, operated by the Victorian Railways  ran from Sandringham station to Black Rock from 1919 until 1956.
On 9 March 2011 a Siemens Nexas train overshot one of the sidings and crashed into a Bendigo Bank branch.Metro train ploughs into Bendigo Bank in Sandringham Herald Sun 10 March 2011
