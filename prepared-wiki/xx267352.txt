[[Haplochrois albanica]]

CATEGORIES: Animals described in 1932, Elachistidae, Insects of Albania, Insects of Hungary, Insects of Greece, Insects of Italy

Haplochrois albanica is a species of moth of the Elachistidae family. It is found in southern and central Europe.
The wingspan is 11–12 mm. Adults have been recorded from June to August.
The foodplant is unknown.
