[[Havelange]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Belgium, Municipalities of Namur (province)

Havelange ([tpl]lang-wa|Havlondje[/tpl]) is a Walloon municipality and village in the Belgian province of Namur. 
On 1 January 2006 the municipality had 4,844 inhabitants. The total area is 104.73 km², giving a population density of 45 inhabitants per km².
