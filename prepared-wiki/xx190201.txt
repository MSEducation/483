[[KXKQ]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Arizona, Country radio stations in the United States, Media in Graham County, Arizona

KXKQ (94.5 FM, "Kat Kountry 94") is a radio station licensed to serve Safford, Arizona, USA.  The station is owned by McMurray Communications, Inc. It airs a Country music format.[tpl]cite web |title=Station Information Profile |work=Arbitron |url=http://www.arbitron.com/[/tpl] Home of "Reed in the Morning", hosted by Reed Richins. The top morning show in the Gila Valley for 20 years. 
The station was assigned the KXKQ call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on March 13, 1979.[tpl]cite web |title=Call Sign History |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=40916&Callsign=KXKQ |work=FCC Media Bureau CDBS Public Access Database[/tpl]
In 2000, KXKQ was named "Best Country Music Station" by the editors of the Phoenix New Times.[tpl]cite news |work=Phoenix New Times |title=2000 Best of Phoenix |url=http://www.phoenixnewtimes.com/bestof/2000/award/best-country-music-station-25206[/tpl] In 2001, the same newspaper named KXKQ "Best Country Radio Station".[tpl]cite news |work=Phoenix New Times |url=http://www.phoenixnewtimes.com/bestof/2001/award/best-country-radio-station-25538 |title=2001 Best of Phoenix[/tpl]
