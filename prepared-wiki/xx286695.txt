[[Saha, Iran]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Saha ([tpl]lang-fa|سها[/tpl], also Romanized as Sahā and Sohā; also known as Saḩeh and Sakha)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3085971|Saha[/tpl] is a village in Golabar Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 601, in 159 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
