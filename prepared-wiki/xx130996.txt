[[Robbie Williams (footballer born 1984)]]

CATEGORIES: 1984 births, Living people, People from Pontefract, English footballers, Association football fullbacks, Barnsley F.C. players, Blackpool F.C. players, Huddersfield Town F.C. players, Stockport County F.C. players, Rochdale A.F.C. players, Plymouth Argyle F.C. players, Limerick F.C. players, The Football League players, League of Ireland players

Robert Ian "Robbie" Williams (born 2 October 1984) is an English footballer who plays as a left back for Limerick. Born in Pontefract, he has played in the Football League for Barnsley, Blackpool, Huddersfield Town, Stockport County, Rochdale and Plymouth Argyle.

==Career==

===Barnsley===

Williams came through the youth ranks with Barnsley, playing over fifty first-team games. He made his debut on 5 October 2002, against Brentford. On 22 March 2007, Williams joined Blackpool on loan until the end of the season.[tpl]cite web|title=Williams set for Blackpool switch|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/b/blackpool/6475919.stm|work=BBC Sport|publisher=BBC|accessdate=31 January 2013|date=21 March 2007[/tpl] He scored on his debut for the Seasiders, in a 3–1 win at Bradford City two days later.[tpl]cite web|title=Bradford 1–3 Blackpool|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/eng_div_2/6461523.stm|work=BBC Sport|publisher=BBC|accessdate=31 January 2013|date=24 March 2007[/tpl] Williams scored the first goal in the 2007 Football League One play-off Final against Yeovil Town at Wembley Stadium.[tpl]cite web|last=Hughes|first=Ian|title=Yeovil 0–2 Blackpool|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/eng_div_2/6686991.stm|work=BBC Sport|publisher=BBC|accessdate=31 January 2013|date=27 May 2007[/tpl] The goal was part of a 2–0 win for Blackpool and saw them promoted to the Championship.

===Huddersfield Town===

He was set to join Huddersfield Town in July 2007 before sustaining a hairline fracture to his shin.[tpl]cite web|title=Williams move to Terriers on hold|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/h/huddersfield_town/6292600.stm|work=BBC Sport|publisher=BBC|accessdate=31 January 2013|date=18 July 2007[/tpl] The transfer was completed the following month, with Williams signing a three-year contract for an undisclosed fee.[tpl]cite web|title=Williams completes Terriers move|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/h/huddersfield_town/6961829.stm|work=BBC Sport|publisher=BBC|accessdate=31 January 2013|date=24 August 2007[/tpl] He made his debut for the club in December at Leeds United, and scored his first goal against Brighton & Hove Albion in January 2008. He made 28 appearances during his first season with Huddersfield,[tpl]cite web|title=Robbie Williams|url=http://www.htafc.com/page/ProfilesDetail/0,,10312~25440,00.html|publisher=Huddersfield Town F.C|accessdate=31 January 2013|archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20120214194742/http://www.htafc.com/page/ProfilesDetail/0,,10312~25440,00.html|archivedate=14 February 2012[/tpl] and 39 in the 2008–09 campaign. The following season, he played in both legs of the League One play-off semi-final defeat to Millwall. Williams was released in the summer of 2010 when his contract expired and spent time on trial at Aberdeen.[tpl]cite web|title=Five more trialists out to impress Aberdeen|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/a/aberdeen/8831291.stm|work=BBC Sport|publisher=BBC|accessdate=31 January 2013|date=17 July 2010[/tpl]

===Rochdale===

Having joined Rochdale on a free transfer, Williams made his debut in March against Notts County. He was released at the end of the season after the club decided against offering him a new contract.[tpl]cite web|title=Flynn, Williams and Fon Williams released by Rochdale|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/r/rochdale/9481984.stm|work=BBC Sport|publisher=BBC|accessdate=31 January 2013|date=10 May 2011[/tpl] He made nine appearances for Rochdale.
