[[Hakone-Itabashi Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Kanagawa Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Hakone-Itabashi Station|箱根板橋駅|Hakone-Itabashi-eki[/tpl] is a railway station on the Hakone Tozan Line located in Odawara, Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan. It is 1.7 rail kilometers from the line’s terminus at Odawara Station.

==History==

Hakone-Itabashi Station was opened on October 10, 1935, when the Hakone Tozan Railway (founded 1928) changed its Odawara - Hakone-Yumoto tram line to a railway.

==Lines==

==Building==

Hakone-Itabashi station has an island platform and a side platform serving three tracks; however, the side platform is not in use.
