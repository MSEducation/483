[[El Bruc]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities in Anoia, Populated places in Anoia

El Bruc ([tpl]IPA-ca|əɫ ˈβɾuk[/tpl], [tpl]lang-es|Bruch[/tpl]) is a municipality in the comarca of the Anoia in Catalonia, Spain. It is situated on the side of Montserrat, of which the north-western third is within the municipality. A local road connects the village with the main N-II road from Barcelona to Lleida, and to
Monistrol de Montserrat via the monastery.
