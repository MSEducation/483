[[Hans von Mžik]]

CATEGORIES: Austrian geographers, Austrian orientalists, 1876 births, 1961 deaths

Hans von Mžik (1876 – 1961) was an Austrian orientalist and geographer. Since 1921 he was head of the map collection at the Austrian National Library and co-founder of the series Museion.
