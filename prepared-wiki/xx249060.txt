[[Alexander Bonsaksen]]

CATEGORIES: 1987 births, Living people, Ice hockey players at the 2010 Winter Olympics, Modo Hockey players, Norwegian ice hockey players, Olympic ice hockey players of Norway, Ice hockey players at the 2014 Winter Olympics, Sportspeople from Oslo, Vålerenga Ishockey players

Alexander Bonsaksen (born January 24, 1987 in Oslo, Norway) is a Norwegian professional ice hockey defenceman. He currently plays with Rögle BKIn Swedish second division HockeyAllsvenskan. 
Bonsaksen joined the Vålerenga Ishockey organization in 2003, playing with their junior team in parts of three seasons. He made his professional debut in the GET-ligaen during the 2005–06 season. He played for Vålerenga until 2009. He was playing in Modo Hockey for the 2008–09 Elitserien season. 

==International career==

Bonsaksen was selected to play for the Norway men's national ice hockey team at the 2010 Winter Olympics. He has previously represented Norway at the 2005 IIHF World U18 Championships, the 2006 and 2007 World Junior Ice Hockey Championships, and the 2008 and 2009 World Ice Hockey Championships.
