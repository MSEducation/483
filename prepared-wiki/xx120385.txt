Ernst Melchior (26 June 1920 in Villach – 5 August 1978 in Rouen, France) was an Austrian football player.

==Club career==

He played seven years for Austrian giants Austria Wien before moving to France where he played for FC Rouen and FC Nantes.

==International career==

Melchior made his debut for Austria in an April 1946 friendly match against Hungary and was a participant at the 1948 Summer OlympicsRecord at FIFA Tournaments - FIFA He earned 36 caps, scoring 16 goals.Goalscoring for Austrian National Team - RSSSF His last international was a November 1953 World Cup qualification match against Portugal.

==Death and legacy==

Melchior died, aged 58, after a long illness in Rouen. In his honour, a gasse (street) in Vienna was named after him.
