[[Mildenhall Cricket Club]]

CATEGORIES: English club cricket teams, Cricket in Suffolk, Forest Heath, 1876 establishments in England

Mildenhall Cricket Club was established in 1876. The club currently[tpl]When|date=March 2012[/tpl] has four teams competing on Saturdays in the Two Counties (Essex and Suffolk) Cricket league. The club also has two teams playing in the Hunts County Bats league on Sundays. They also have a team competing in the La Hogue Fordham midweek league. The club also has a youth section with players as young as eight. The Mildenhall club also runs a ladies' team.
