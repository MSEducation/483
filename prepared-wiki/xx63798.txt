[[Goat (zodiac)]]

CATEGORIES: Goat (zodiac), Chinese astrology, Mythological caprids, Astrological signs, Vietnamese astrology

The Goat ( 羊 ) is the eighth sign of the 12-year cycle of animals that appear in the Chinese zodiac related to the Chinese calendar. The Year of the Goat is associated with the Earthly Branch symbol 未.
