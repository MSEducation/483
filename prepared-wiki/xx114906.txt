[[Holoplatys]]

CATEGORIES: Salticidae, Spiders of Australia

Holoplatys is a genus of the spider family Salticidae (jumping spiders).

==Description==

Holoplatys have a long, flat, oval cephalothorax, which is truncated anteriorly, and a long, flat, oval abdomen. They are often found under the bark of trees, especially eucalyptus. They have a resemblance to Marpissa.Murphy & Murphy 2000: 274
