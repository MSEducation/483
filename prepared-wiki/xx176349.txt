[[Nymphaea odorata subsp. odorata]]

CATEGORIES: Nymphaea, Plant subspecies, Flora of North America

Nymphaea odorata'' subsp. ''odorata is a subspecies of Nymphaea odorata that is found in the United States. It has a very round leaf. The family is also called the "water-lily family[tpl]cite web|title=American Water Lily Wildflower|url=http://www.wildflowers-guide.com/28-american-waterlily.html[/tpl]".
