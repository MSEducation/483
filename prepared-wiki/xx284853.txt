[[2013 Swiss Cup Basel]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 in curling

The 2013 Swiss Cup Basel was held from October 3 to 6 at the Curlingzentrum Region Basel in Arlesheim, Switzerland as part of the 2013–14 World Curling Tour. The event was held in a round robin format, and the purse for the event was CHF 43,200, of which the winner, Thomas Ulsrud, received CHF 14,000.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.swisscupbasel.ch/images/swisscup/pdf/FactsDaten.pdf|title=Swiss Cup Basel – Facts|publisher=swisscupbasel.ch|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] Ulsrud defeated Oskar Eriksson in the final with a score of 6–3.
