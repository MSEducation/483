[[Babin Potok (Prokuplje)]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Toplica District

Babin Potok is a village in the municipality of Prokuplje, Serbia, near the town of Prokuplje. According to the 2002 census, the village has a population of 674 people.Popis stanovništva, domaćinstava i Stanova 2002. Knjiga 1: Nacionalna ili etnička pripadnost po naseljima.
