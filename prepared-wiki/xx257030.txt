[[John Schricker House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses completed in 1910, Houses in Davenport, Iowa, Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Iowa, National Register of Historic Places in Scott County, Iowa

The John Schricker House is located in the far West End of Davenport, Iowa, United States.  The house has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since 1985.
