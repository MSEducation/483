[[Kunitomi, Miyazaki]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Miyazaki Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Kunitomi|国富町|Kunitomi-chō[/tpl] is a town located in Higashimorokata District, Miyazaki Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003,[tpl]Update inline|date=June 2013[/tpl] the town has an estimated population of 22,058 and the density of 168.76 persons per km². The total area is 130.71 km².[tpl]fact|date=June 2013[/tpl]
