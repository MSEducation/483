[[Ryan Pitylak]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Email spammers, 1982 births

Ryan Pitylak (born 1982) is an American entrepreneur and chief executive officer of Unique Influence, a digital marketing consulting firm based in Austin, Texas.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bizjournals.com/austin/print-edition/2012/09/07/texas-venture-labs-set-to-help.html?page=all|title=Texas Venture Labs set to help 12 startups this school year|author=Garza, Vicky|work=Austin Business Journal|accessdate=8 April 2014[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bizjournals.com/austin/blog/at-the-watercooler/2014/02/whats-better-for-entrepreneur-instinct-experience.html|title=What's better for an entrepreneur: Gut instinct or experience? ABJ experts react|author=Barr, Greg|work=Austin Business Journal|accessdate=8 April 2014[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://socialmediatoday.com/jennifer-oreilly/2325976/listen-old-school-advertisers-social-media-and-digital-advertising-are-here|title=Listen Up Old School Advertisers: Social Media and Digital Advertising Are Here To Stay|author=Jennifer O'Reilly|work=Social Media Today|accessdate=8 April 2014[/tpl] He is former internet spammer who, in civil settlements with the state of Texas and Microsoft, admitted to "sending 25 million e-mails every day at the height of his spamming operation in 2004" and paid out over $1 million.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.nbcnews.com/id/13138246/ns/technology_and_science-tech_and_gadgets/t/spam-king-settles-texas-microsoft|title=Spam king settles with Texas, Microsoft|publisher=NBC News|date=June 4, 2006|accessdate=June 4, 2006[/tpl] He was listed as the fourth-worst spammer by Spamhaus Project at one time.
Pitylak is a graduate of the University of Texas, where he received undergraduate degrees in economics and philosophy.[tpl]cite news|title=Million-dollar penalty reforms spammer: Now ready to sell his anti-spam expertise|work=Chicago Tribune|date=4 June 2006|publisher=Edmonton Journal[/tpl] In June 2006, he launched Pitylak Security, an Internet security company that focused on anti-spam consulting.[tpl]cite news|title=I was a teenage spammer; Before even leaving high school, Ryan Pitylak was sending out millions of spam e-mails -- and making big bucks|author=Gantert, Tom|date=12 July 2006|work=Times-Picayune[/tpl]
