[[Wilkownia]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Augustów County

Wilkownia [tpl]IPAc-pl|w|i|l|'|k|o|w|ń|a[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Sztabin, within Augustów County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) – TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|16|km|mi|0[/tpl] north-east of Sztabin, [tpl]convert|22|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] east of Augustów, and [tpl]convert|75|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of the regional capital Białystok.
The village has a population of 20.
