[[2005–06 Liga Leumit]]

CATEGORIES: Liga Leumit seasons, 2005–06 domestic association football leagues, 2005–06 in Israeli football

The 2005–06 Liga Leumit season saw Maccabi Herzliya win the title and promotion to the Premier League. Runners-up Hakoah Amidar Ramat Gan were also promoted.
Ironi Rishon LeZion and Maccabi Be'er Sheva (playing in their first, and to date only, season at the second level) were relegated to Liga Artzit.
