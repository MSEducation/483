[[Allan Morris]]

CATEGORIES: Australian Labor Party politicians, Members of the Australian House of Representatives for Newcastle, Members of the Australian House of Representatives, 1940 births, Living people

Allan Agapitos Morris (born 26 July 1940) is a former Australian politician. Born in Waratah, New South Wales, he was educated at the University of Newcastle and became a computer programmer. He served on Newcastle City Council and Shortland County Council; his brother, Peter Morris, was a Labor member of the Australian House of Representatives, representing the seat of Shortland. In 1983, Allan too was elected to the House as the Labor member for Newcastle.
