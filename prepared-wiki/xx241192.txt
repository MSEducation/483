[[Naiman Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

Naiman Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠨᠠᠢᠮᠠᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Naiman qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|c=奈曼旗 |p=Nàimàn Qí[/tpl]) is a banner of Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China. It is under the administration of Tongliao City, [tpl]convert|156|km[/tpl] to the northeast, and lies on the China National Highway 111.Naiman Mongols live here.
