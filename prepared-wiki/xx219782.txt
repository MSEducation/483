[[Maria Tsiartsiani]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 births, Greek beach volleyball players, Beach volleyball players at the 2008 Summer Olympics, Beach volleyball players at the 2012 Summer Olympics, Olympic beach volleyball players of Greece, Living people, Greek sportswomen, Sportspeople from Thessaloniki

Maria Tsiartsiani ([tpl]lang-el|Μαρία Τσιαρτσανη[/tpl]; born 21 October 1980 in Thessaloniki) is a Greek Olympic beach volleyballer.   She competed at the 2008 Summer Olympics, partnering with Efthalia Koutroumanidou.http://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/ts/maria-tsiartsiani-1.html at Sports Reference  At the 2012 Summer Olympics, she competed with Vassiliki Arvaniti.
