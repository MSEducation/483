[[Avesta AIK]]

CATEGORIES: Sport in Dalarna, Football clubs in Sweden, Association football clubs established in 1905, Bandy clubs established in 1905, Defunct bandy clubs in Sweden, 1905 establishments in Sweden

Avesta AIK is a Swedish football club located in Avesta, Sweden.[tpl]cite web |url= http://www.avestaaik.se/ |title= Avesta AIK – Fotboll Klubb |format= |work= |accessdate=2011-03-03[/tpl]

==Background==

The club was founded in February 1905 as Avesta Absolutisters Idrottsklubb. The name was changed to Avesta Allmänna Idrottsklubb in 1916. The club now only participates in football but over the years has undertaken many different sports such as bandy, table tennis, boxing, athletics, gymnastics, skiing and bowling. The club has grown from 24 members at its inception to over 800 members at the present day.[tpl]cite web |url= http://www1.idrottonline.se/AvestaAIK-Fotboll/Info/Historia/Historia/ |title= Avesta AIK – Historia |format= |work= |accessdate=2011-03-03[/tpl]
Since their foundation Avesta AIK has participated mainly in the upper and middle divisions of the Swedish football league system. The club currently plays in Division 3 Södra Norrland which is the fifth tier of Swedish football.[tpl]cite web |url= http://www.everysport.com/sport/fotboll/fotbollsserier-2011/division-3-herr/soedra-norrland/44188|title= Södra Norrland - Division 3 – Fotbollsserier 2011 – Fotboll – everysport.com |format= |work= |accessdate=2011-03-03[/tpl] They play their home matches at the Avestavallen in Avesta.[tpl]cite web |url= http://svenskfotboll.se/svensk-fotboll/om-svff/distrikten/?scr=table&ftid=31373 |title= Tabell och resultat – Div 3 Södra Norrland, herrar – Svenskfotboll.se |format= |work= |accessdate=2011-03-03[/tpl]
Avesta AIK are affiliated to Dalarnas Fotbollförbund.[tpl]cite web |url= http://svenskfotboll.se/svensk-fotboll/om-svff/distrikten/?flid=11610 |title= Kontaktuppgifter och tävlingar – Avesta AIK – Svenskfotboll.se |format= |work= |accessdate=2011-03-03[/tpl]
