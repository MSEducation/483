[[Charlie Luke (English footballer)]]

CATEGORIES: 1909 births, English footballers, Sportspeople from County Durham, Association football midfielders, The Football League players, Huddersfield Town F.C. players, Sheffield Wednesday F.C. players, 1983 deaths

Charles "Charlie" E. Luke (born 16 March 1909 in Esh Winning, County Durham) was a professional footballer, who played for Bishop Auckland, Huddersfield Town and Sheffield Wednesday.
