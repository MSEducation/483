[[Shila Khodadad]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 births, Iranian actresses, Iranian people, Living people

Shila Khodadad (in Persian: شیلا خداداد) (born on November 5, 1980) is an Iranian actress. She has played in some films such as Marriage, Iranian Style, Saint Petersburg (film) and in a famous Iranian TV series, A Traveler from India (مسافری از هند).
