[[Cardiff Marriott Hotel]]

CATEGORIES: Hotels in Cardiff, Marriott International

Cardiff Marriott Hotel is a four star[tpl]cite book|last1=Godfrey-Faussett|first1=Charles|last2=Murphy|first2=Alan|last3=Ford|first3=Rebecca|title=Britain|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=G-hjFhCvkigC&pg=PA542|accessdate=25 September 2011|date=1 April 2004|publisher=Footprint Travel Guides|isbn=978-1-903471-89-0|page=542[/tpl] Marriott hotel in the southern part of The Hayes[tpl]cite book|last1=Nevez|first1=Catherine Le|last2=Parker|first2=Mike|last3=Whitfield|first3=Paul|title=The Rough Guide to Wales|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=I6z234p43ggC&pg=PA117|accessdate=25 September 2011|date=28 April 2009|publisher=Rough Guides|isbn=978-1-84836-050-1|page=117[/tpl] in the centre of Cardiff, capital of Wales. It is located in close proximity to Cardiff Central Library and St. David's Hall.[tpl]cite book|author=BBC|title=BBC music magazine|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=gJsJAQAAMAAJ|accessdate=25 September 2011|year=1999|publisher=BBC Magazines[/tpl] 
thumb|left|Marriott Hotel including the multi-storey car park.
The hotel opened in 1986 as a Holiday Inn hotel,[tpl]cite web|url= http://www.visitcardiff.com/meet/latest-news/2011/6/9/cardiff-marriott-hotel-celebrates-25th-anniversary-a887|title= Cardiff Marriott Hotel celebrates 25th anniversary|publisher= Whycardiff.com |accessdate=29 January 2012[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url= http://www.cardiffians.co.uk/timeline.shtml#20th|title= Cardiff Timeline|publisher= cardiffians.co.uk |accessdate=29 January 2012[/tpl] the hotel was later sold in 1990 to InterContinental Hotels Group to become a Marriott hotel. The hotel underwent refurbishment in 2008, which included a new multi-storey car park, because part of the hotel car part was sold to make way for a new Cardiff Central Library as part of the St. David's 2 development.
