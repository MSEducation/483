[[Gmina Kamiennik]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Opole Voivodeship, Nysa County

Gmina Kamiennik is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Nysa County, Opole Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Its seat is the village of Kamiennik, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|18|km|mi|0[/tpl] north-west of Nysa and [tpl]convert|57|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] west of the regional capital Opole.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|89.23|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 3,737.

==Villages==

Gmina Kamiennik contains the villages and settlements of Białowieża, Chociebórz, Cieszanowice, Goworowice, Kamiennik, Karłowice Małe, Karłowice Wielkie, Kłodobok, Lipniki, Ogonów, Siodłary, Suliszów, Szklary, Tarnów, Wilemowice and Zurzyce.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Kamiennik is bordered by the gminas of Grodków, Otmuchów, Pakosławice, Przeworno and Ziębice.
