[[Jørgen Langhelle]]

CATEGORIES: 1965 births, Living people, Norwegian male film actors

Jørgen Langhelle (born 18 August 1965) is an Award-winning leading Norwegian actor of stage, screen and television.
Langhelle has starred in the two successful Norwegian mini-series Deadline Torp (2005) and Torpedo (2007), as well as starring in such movies as Kristin Lavransdatter (1995), Elling (2001), I Am Dina (2002), Tyven, tyven (2002), Ulvesommer (2003) and En folkefiende (a modernized version of Henrik Ibsen's play An Enemy of the People) (2005).Jørgen Langhelle - FILMWEB.pl In March 2010, Langhelle was cast in The Thing, the 2011 prequel to John Carpenter's 1982 horror classic.So There Will Be Norwegians in The Thing
