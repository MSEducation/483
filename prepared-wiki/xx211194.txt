[[Rainbow, Oregon]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Lane County, Oregon, 1924 establishments in Oregon, Unincorporated communities in Oregon

Rainbow is an unincorporated community in Lane County, Oregon, United States. It lies off Oregon Route 126, northeast of Eugene. Rainbow's elevation is [tpl]convert|1211|ft|m[/tpl].[tpl]cite gnis|id=1125779|name=Rainbow|entrydate=November 28, 1980|accessdate=August 3, 2011[/tpl] A post office was established on July 1, 1924, and closed August 31, 1937. The post office got its name from the rainbow trout that swim in the McKenzie River.[tpl]Cite OGN|7th|page=796[/tpl]
