[[Andrei Chasovskikh]]

CATEGORIES: 1991 births, Living people, Russian footballers

Andrei Yuryevich Chasovskikh ([tpl]lang-ru|Андрей Юрьевич Часовских[/tpl]; born May 31, 1991) is a Russian football forward, who currently playing for FC Tambov.
He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Tambov on July 16, 2013 in a game against FC Oryol.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Russian Football Union|url=http://rfspro.ru/cgi-bin/player/script2.cgi?par1=116817|title=Career Summary|date=10 August 2013[/tpl]
