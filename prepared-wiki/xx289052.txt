[[Choqluy-e Sofla]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Choqluy-e Sofla ([tpl]lang-fa|چقلوي سفلي[/tpl], also Romanized as Choqlūy-e Soflá; also known as Chaghalū-ye Pā’īn, Chaghalū-ye Soflá, Chaghlooy Sofla, Chonglu Pāīn, Choqlū-ye Soflá, Chūqlū-ye Soflá, and Jaghalū-ye Soflá)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3057226|Choqluy-e Sofla[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 225, in 48 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
