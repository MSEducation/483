[[50th Wisconsin Volunteer Infantry Regiment]]

CATEGORIES: Wisconsin Civil War regiments

The 50th Regiment Wisconsin Volunteer Infantry was an infantry regiment that served in the Union Army during the American Civil War.

==Service==

The 50th Wisconsin was organized at Madison, Wisconsin and mustered into Federal service between March and April 1865. 
The regiment was mustered out between April 19 and June 12, 1866.

==Casualties==

The 50th Wisconsin suffered 1 enlisted man killed in action and 1 officer and 43 enlisted men who died of disease, for a total of 45 fatalities.http://www.civilwararchive.com/Unreghst/unwiinf2.htm#50th
