[[Juryzdyka]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Augustów County

Juryzdyka [tpl]IPAc-pl|j|u|r|y|z|'|d|y|k|a[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Nowinka, within Augustów County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) – TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|5|km|mi|0[/tpl] north of Nowinka, [tpl]convert|14|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of Augustów, and [tpl]convert|97|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of the regional capital Białystok.
