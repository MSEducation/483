[[Interstate Aviation Committee]]

CATEGORIES: Commonwealth of Independent States, Civil aviation authorities, Organizations investigating aviation accidents and incidents, Aviation in Russia, 2010 Polish Air Force Tu-154 crash

The Interstate Aviation Committee (IAC; [tpl]lang-ru|Межгосударственный авиационный комитет, МАК[/tpl]) is a supervising body overseeing the use and management of civil aviation in the Commonwealth of Independent States (CIS). The IAC is the official civil aviation authority in Russia while some other countries recognize it as a certification and expert body to a varying extent.
