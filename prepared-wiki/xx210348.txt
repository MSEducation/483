[[Jog Meher Shrestha]]

CATEGORIES: Government ministers of Nepal, Living people, Rastriya Prajatantra Party politicians

Jog Meher Shrestha is a Nepalese politician, belonging to the Rastriya Prajatantra Party. He was politically active during the Panchayat period.http://www.nepalnews.com.np/contents/englishweekly/spotlight/2004/nov/nov05/offtherecord.htm Shrestha was appointed Land Reform and Management Minister in the cabinet of Sher Bahadur Deuba in 2004.http://www.nepalhomepage.com/politics/cabinet/050704.html 
After the 2005 royal take-over, Shrestha was arrested accused of misuse of the Prime Ministers Fund. In March 2005, he was released on bail.http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2005-03-22/rest-of-world/27864691_1_deuba-prime-minister-nepali In June 2005, he was acquitted.http://www.hindu.com/2005/06/28/stories/2005062802331400.htm
In 2006, he was acting chairman of the RPP.http://www.peoplesreview.com.np/2006/280906/update29.html
