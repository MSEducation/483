[[The Eternal Waltz]]

CATEGORIES: 1954 films, West German films, German-language films, Films about classical music and musicians

The Eternal Waltz ([tpl]lang-de|Ewiger Walzer[/tpl]) is a 1954 West German film dramatizing the life of Johann Strauss II. The initial story was written by Hanns Marschall and Ruth Charlotte Silbermann, and the film itself was written by Alexander Lix; the adaptation was by Paul Verhoeven who also directed the film.
