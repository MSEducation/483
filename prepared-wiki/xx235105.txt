[[University of Defence (Czech Republic)]]

CATEGORIES: Military of the Czech Republic

The University of Defence (Czech: Univerzita obrany, UO) is an institution of higher education of the Czech Armed Forces located in Brno and Hradec Králové. The university is responsible for education of military professionals and experts engaged in national security.
