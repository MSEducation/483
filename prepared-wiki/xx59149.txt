</ref> In 1930, the route was extended southward to [[Cape May, New Jersey]], and the alignment of current-day Route 157 was bypassed to the west.<ref name="1930take">New Jersey State Legislature: Takeover: Beesley's Point Bridge to Shore Road in Absecon, June 24, 1930.</ref> The former alignment of Route 4 was left unnumbered.<ref name="usgs">[tpl]cite map|url=http://www.lib.utexas.edu/maps/topo/250k/txu-pclmaps-topo-us-wilmington-1946.jpg|title=Wilmington, Delaware 1:250,000 Quadrangle|publisher=[[United States Geological Survey]]|year=1946|accessdate=November 9, 2009[/tpl]</ref> Route 157 was designated to a previously unnumbered roadway<ref name="nj1953">[tpl]citation|title=1953 renumbering|url=http://en.wikisource.org/wiki/1953_New_Jersey_state_highway_renumbering|publisher=New Jersey Department of Highways|accessdate=July 31, 2009[/tpl]</ref> and has remained unchanged since.<ref name="2007map">[tpl]cite map|title=New Jersey Official Road Map|year=2007|publisher=New Jersey Department of Transportation|cartography=New Jersey Department of Transportation[/tpl]</ref>
