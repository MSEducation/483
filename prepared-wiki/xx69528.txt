[[Schönebeck]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Saxony-Anhalt, Salzlandkreis

Schönebeck ([tpl]IPA-de|ˈʃøːnəbɛk|lang|De-Schönebeck.ogg[/tpl]) is a town in the district of Salzlandkreis, in Saxony-Anhalt, Germany. It is situated on the left bank of the Elbe, approx. [tpl]convert|14|km|0|abbr=off[/tpl] southeast of Magdeburg.
