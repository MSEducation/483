[[The Grand Garden EP]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 EPs, Aslyn albums, Album series

The Grand Garden EP is an EP recorded by singer-songwriter Aslyn. The EP features 28 songs, most of which were recorded between Aslyn's debut album, Lemon Love and her second album, The Dandelion Sessions. It is divided up into four parts, or 'phases', each one having seven different songs.
