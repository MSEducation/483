[[Mogambo (horse)]]

CATEGORIES: 1983 racehorse births, Thoroughbred racehorses, Racehorses bred in Kentucky, Racehorses trained in the United States, American racehorses, American Grade 1 Stakes winners

Mogambo (foaled 1983 in Kentucky) is a retired American Thoroughbred racehorse whose wins included a nine-length victory in the Grade I Champagne Stakes at Belmont Park in Elmont, New York.Newsday - October 20, 1985 Bred and raced by Peter M. Brant, Mogambo was sired by the outstanding Champion stallion, Mr. Prospector. His dam was Lakeville Miss, the American Champion Two-Year-Old Filly of 1977.Mogambo's pedigree
Trained by National Museum of Racing and Hall of Fame inductee, Leroy Jolley, Mogambo retired having won four of his nineteen starts with earnings of $958,176.
