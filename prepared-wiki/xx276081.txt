[[Anthony Nigro (singer-songwriter)]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, American guitarists, American singer-songwriters

Anthony Nigro is an American singer-songwriter. He is a founding member of Osgoods, the rock band formed in Tucson, Arizona with school-mate and drummer Colin Wyatt. In 1998, Nigro relocated to Los Angeles.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/album/osgoods/automatic-do-over/538534#/artist/osgoods/487149 |title=Automatic Do-Over - Osgoods |publisher=Billboard.com |date= |accessdate=2011-10-17[/tpl]
