[[Hylarana grisea]]

CATEGORIES: Hylarana, Amphibians of Indonesia, Amphibians of New Guinea, Endemic fauna of Indonesia

Hylarana grisea is a species of true frog in the genus Hylarana. It is native to Irian Jaya, Indonesia.[ref]
