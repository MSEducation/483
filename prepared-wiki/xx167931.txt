[[Gierałtowice, Silesian Voivodeship]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Gliwice County

Gierałtowice [tpl]IPAc-pl|g|J|e|r|a|U|t|o|'|w|i|c|e[/tpl] (German Gieraltowitz) is a village in Gliwice County, Silesian Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It is the seat of the gmina (administrative district) called Gmina Gierałtowice.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|9|km|mi|0[/tpl] south-east of Gliwice and [tpl]convert|21|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] west of the regional capital Katowice.
The village has a population of 3,752. It is first recorded in the 13th century.
