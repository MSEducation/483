He is also the author of the 2005 book, Rather Dumb: A Top Tabloid Reporter Tells CBS How to Do News. Between April 11, 1996 and December 2010, Walker was a guest every week on The Howard Stern Show to play "The Gossip Game." He would read four gossip stories, and the Stern crew guesses which one is false.  During a 2006 Stern show appearance, Stern staff members Richard Christy and Sal Governale recorded audio which allegedly was from Walker's flatuence.  Dubbed the "Mike Walker Fart," the audio clip was played often on the show.
