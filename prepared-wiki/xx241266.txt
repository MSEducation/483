[[60th Indian Infantry Brigade]]

CATEGORIES: British Indian Army brigades

The 60th Indian Infantry Brigade was an Infantry formation of the Indian Army during World War II. It was formed in July 1943, by the re designation of the 10th Indian Motor Brigade. The brigade served in Lebanon, Iraq and Persia with the 12th Indian Infantry Division.[tpl]cite web|accessdate=2009-10-23|publisher=Order of Battle|title=60 Indian Brigade|url=http://www.ordersofbattle.com/UnitData.aspx?UniX=154019&Tab=Sup[/tpl]
