[[Jesse Owens Memorial Stadium]]

CATEGORIES: Sports venues in Columbus, Ohio, Ohio State Buckeyes sports venues, Soccer venues in Ohio, College soccer venues in the United States, Sports venues completed in 2001, 2001 establishments in Ohio

Jesse Owens Memorial Stadium is a 10,000-capacity stadium located in Columbus, Ohio, United States. The stadium is home to the Ohio State Buckeyes men's and women's lacrosse teams as well as the soccer and track and field teams. The stadium opened for soccer in the fall of 2001. It also hosts the OHSAA boys and girls track and field State Tournament. It is named after former OSU athlete, Jesse Owens.
