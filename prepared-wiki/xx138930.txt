[[China Merchants Holdings (International)]]

CATEGORIES: Companies listed on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange, Government-owned companies of China, Hang Seng Index Constituent Stocks, Warrants issued in Hong Kong Stock Exchange, Port operating companies, China Merchants

China Merchants Holdings (International) Company Limited ([tpl]hkex|0144[/tpl]) is a major conglomerate based in Hong Kong and is involved in a range of businesses such as port operations, general and bulk cargo transportation, container and shipping business, air cargo, logistics park operation and paint products. The predecessor of the parent company was established in 1872 and was the preeminent industrial and commercial group in mainland China.China Merchants Holdings Internationalhttp://www.reuters.com/finance/stocks/overview?symbol=0144.HKhttp://www.google.com/finance?q=HKG%3A0144

==Corporate structure==

The corporate structure of China Merchant Holdings (International) is organised into two divisions. The first division is organised around port services such as logistics, bulk cargo and container port management.http://www.alacrastore.com/company-snapshot/China_Merchants_Holdings_International_Company_Limited-2015458
The second division is involved with the manufacturing of paint and containers.

==Head office==

China Merchant Holdings (International) originally conducted its business in Shanghai as its headquarters. Over the years, the head office has been located in Hong Kong. The corporate address is 38/F East, China Merchants Tower, Shun Tak Centre, 168-200 Connaught Road Central, Central, Hong Kong
