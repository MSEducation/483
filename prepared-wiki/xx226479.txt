[[Robert Hagelin]]

Robert Hagelin (2 August 1884 – after 1938) was a Norwegian politician for the Conservative Party.
Born in Bergen, he moved to Ålesund in 1915 to become the owner and manager of the factory Aalesunds Margarinfabrikk.Robert Hagelin — Norwegian Social Science Data Services (NSD)
He was a member of Ålesund city council from 1925 to 1928, and served as a deputy representative to the Norwegian Parliament during the term 1931–1933, representing the Market towns of Møre og Romsdal county.
