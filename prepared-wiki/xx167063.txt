[[BAM B26]]

CATEGORIES: Air guns

The BAM XS-B26 is a Chinese copy of the Beeman R9. It is available in 2 calibres: .177, and .22. It is a break barrel type air rifle.
The 26 in B26 is for 26 mm chamber, but it never went into production at 26 mm.
It is a 25 mm chamber. The Beeman R9 has 26 mm, where the B26 does NOT not have the 26 mm chamber.
Now Bam b26 is 26mm chamber
