[[Niobichthys ferrarisi]]

CATEGORIES: Hypoptopomatini, Monotypic fish genera, Fish of South America, Fauna of Venezuela

Niobichthys ferrarisi is the only species of the monotypic genus Niobichthys of loricariid catfish (order Siluriformes).[tpl]ITIS |id=679940 |taxon=Niobichthys ferrarisi |accessdate=June 2007[/tpl] N. ferrarisi is classified in the tribe Hypoptopomatini within the subfamily Hypoptopomatinae.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.auburn.edu/academic/science_math/res_area/loricariid/fish_key/loricariid_species.htm|title=Loricariid species|last=Armbruster|first=Jonathan W.|accessdate=2007-06-01[/tpl] This fish reaches a length of [tpl]convert|7.0|cm|in[/tpl] SL.  This species is endemic to Venezuela and occurs in the upper Baria River and the Rio Negro basin.[tpl]FishBase species|genus=Niobichthys|species=ferrarisi|year=2011|month=December[/tpl]
