[[Feel My Heart]]

CATEGORIES: 1996 singles, Every Little Thing songs, Songs written by Mitsuru Igarashi, 1996 songs, Avex Trax singles

"Feel My Heart" is a song by the Japanese J-pop group Every Little Thing, released on August 7, 1996. The track was remixed by the Swiss trance producer, Moogwai, in 2002.Moogwai
