[[St. Michael Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in the Nome Census Area, Alaska

St. Michael Airport [tpl]airport codes|SMK|PAMK|SMK[tpl]nobold|, formerly [/tpl]5S8[/tpl] is a state-owned, public-use airport located two nautical miles (4 km) west of the central business district of St. Michael, a city in the Nome Census Area of the U.S. state of Alaska.

==Facilities==

St. Michael Airport covers an area of [tpl]convert|355|acre|ha|lk=on[/tpl] at an elevation of 98 feet (30 m) above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 2/20 with a 4,001 x 75 ft (1,220 x 23 m) gravel surface.
