[[Saint-André-de-Corcy]]

CATEGORIES: Communes of Ain

Saint-André-de-Corcy is a commune in the Ain département in eastern France.

==Population==

==Sites and monuments==

The commune has several châteaux and poypes (fortified mounds, or mottes). The motte castrale Poype Les Roussière has been listed as monument historique by the French Ministry of Culture since 1989.Ministry of Culture: Saint-André-de-Corcy: Edifice fortifié, dit poype de la Roussière  [tpl]fr[/tpl] The Château de Montribloud a castle converted to a residence, dates from the 14th century.
