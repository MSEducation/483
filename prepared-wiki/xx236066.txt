[[Blumeopsis]]

CATEGORIES: Asteraceae genera

Blumeopsis is a genus of flowering plants in the aster family, Asteraceae.
It is sometimes treated as monotypic, including only Blumeopsis flava.Pornpongrungrueng, P., et al. (2007). Phylogenetic relationships in Blumea (Asteraceae: Inuleae) as evidenced by molecular and morphological data. Plant Systematics and Evolution 269(3-4), 223-43.Blumeopsis. Flora of Pakistan. Some sources accept a second species, Blumeopsis falcata.Blumeopsis. The Plant List.
