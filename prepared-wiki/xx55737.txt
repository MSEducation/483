[[Linati schema for Ulysses]]

CATEGORIES: Ulysses (novel)

This schema for the novel Ulysses was produced by Joyce in 1920 to help a friend (Carlo Linati) understand the fundamental structure of the book.Ellmann, Richard, Ulysses on the Liffey, Oxford University Press, 1986, ISBN 978-0-19-501663-5, pp. 186-190. The schema has been split into two subtables for better ease of reading.
