[[Dave Hull (rugby league)]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 births, People from St Helens, Merseyside, Living people, English rugby league players, St Helens RLFC players, Swinton Lions players

Dave Hull (1 January 1985) is a rugby league footballer who plays for Swinton, having previously played for St Helens in the Super League. Dave Hull's preferred position is [tpl]rlp|FB[/tpl].
Dave Hull played his junior career alongside James Graham at the Thatto Heath club.
In 2007, Dave Hull was promoted to train with the St Helens first team squad.
