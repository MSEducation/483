[[Adam Thirlwell]]

CATEGORIES: Fellows of All Souls College, Oxford, Alumni of New College, Oxford, English novelists, People educated at Haberdashers' Aske's Boys' School, English Jews, British Jewish writers, 1978 births, Living people

Adam Thirlwell (born 22 August 1978) is a British novelist. His work has been translated into thirty languages. He has twice been named as one of Granta's Best of Young British Novelists.

==Life==

Thirlwell was educated at the independent Haberdashers' Aske's Boys' School, Elstree. He read English at New College, Oxford, where he got the top first.[ref]
