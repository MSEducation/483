[[St. Dominic's Church (Bronx, New York)]]

CATEGORIES: Religious organizations established in 1927, Romanesque Revival architecture in New York, Anthony J. DePace buildings, Religious buildings completed in 1925, 20th-century Roman Catholic church buildings, Roman Catholic churches in New York, Churches in the Bronx

The Church of St. Dominic is a Roman Catholic parish church under the authority of the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of New York, located at 1739 Unionport Road, Van Nest, Bronx, New York City.Official Website of the Parish of Our Lady of Solace - St. Dominic. http://www.ourladyofsolace--stdominic.org/ The parish was established in 1927. The Romanesque Revival was built 1925 to the designs of Bronx-native architect Anthony J. DePace of the firm DePace & Juster.[tpl]cite news |title=Do You Remember? St. Dominic's Church was established as a National Italian Parish |newspaper=The Bronx Times Reporter |first=Bill |last=Twomey |date=January 21, 2010 |page=48[/tpl] In May 2011, two of the three bells were removed in order to service the wooden supports which were waterlogged and had seepage problems. The goal is to replace the wooden supports and return the bells to their original location.[tpl]cite news |last=O'Shaughnessy |first=Patrice |title=Bells toll for school, but ring for church |newspaper=The New York Daily News |date=May 19, 2011 |page=31 |url=http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/bronx/bells-toll-school-ring-bronx-church-article-1.142026 |accessdate=2011-05-19[/tpl] The expected restoration cost of the bells is estimated at $30,000.[tpl]cite news |last=Weisbrod |first=Bill |title=St. Dominic's Church Set To Recieve(sic) Bell Repairs |newspaper=The Bronx Times Reporter |date=May 31, 2011 |page=27 |url= http://www.bxtimes.com/stories/2011/22/bronxtimes-yn_bronx_front_page-21-bells.html |accessdate=2013-04-24[/tpl]

==School==

The parish school was among one of 27 slated to be closed by Archbishop Dolan in the Archdiocese of New York as announced on 11 January 2011.Alice McQuillan, "New York Archdiocese to Close 27 Schools," NBC New York, 11 January 2011 (Accessed 7 February 2011)Archdiocese of New York, "RECONFIGURATION COMMITTEE RECOMMENDATIONS REGARDING “AT-RISK” SCHOOLS ACCEPTED BY ARCHDIOCESE OF NEW YORK," Official Press Release, 11 January 2011 (Accessed 7 February 2011) The school, after 59 years was closed at the end of June 2011 resulting in its student body having to transfer to other local Catholic schools.[tpl]cite news |last=Lestch |first=Corinne |title=St. Dominic school staff and students bid farewell to Bronx parish school |newspaper=New York Daily News |date=July 1, 2011 |page=42 |url=http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/bronx/st-dominic-school-staff-students-bid-farewell-bronx-parish-school-article-1.160635 |accessdate=2011-07-02[/tpl]
