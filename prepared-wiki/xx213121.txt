[[Dubrovnik Avenue]]

CATEGORIES: Roads in Zagreb, Novi Zagreb

Dubrovnik Avenue ([tpl]lang-hr|Avenija Dubrovnik[/tpl]) is an avenue located in the Novi Zagreb part of Zagreb, Croatia. It is mostly six or eight lanes wide. It runs between the roundabout beneath the southward extension of the Youth Bridge ([tpl]lang|hr|Most mladosti[/tpl]) in the east and the Remetinec Roundabout in the west of Novi Zagreb.
