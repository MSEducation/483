[[Parnleka Wanmuang]]

CATEGORIES: 1963 births, Living people, Thai beauty pageant winners, Miss World 1985 delegates, People from Bangkok

Parnleka Wanmuang ([tpl]lang-th|ปานเลขา ว่านม่วง[/tpl]), nicknamed Por ([tpl]lang-th|ปอ[/tpl]) (born September 8, 1963 in Bangkok, Thailand) is Miss Thailand World 1985.[tpl]cite news|url=http://news.hunsa.com/detail.php?id=19324|title="น้องวิว"เทควันโดทีมชาติ-ซิวมิสไทยฯ|work=Hunsa News|language=Thai|accessdate=26 June 2011[/tpl] she was completed in Miss World 1985 pageant competition held in UK.
