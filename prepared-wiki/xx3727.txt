[[Hemoglobin]]

CATEGORIES: Hemoglobins, Equilibrium chemistry, Respiratory physiology

Hemoglobin ([tpl]IPAc-en|ˈ|h|iː|m|ə|ˌ|ɡ|l|oʊ|b|ɪ|n[/tpl]); also spelled haemoglobin and abbreviated Hb or Hgb, is the iron-containing oxygen-transport metalloprotein in the red blood cells of all vertebrates[ref]
