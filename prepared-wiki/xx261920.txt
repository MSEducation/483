[[New Zealand cricket team in Zimbabwe in 1992–93]]

CATEGORIES: 1992 in New Zealand sport, 1992 in Zimbabwean sport, International cricket competitions from 1991–92 to 1994, New Zealand cricket tours of Zimbabwe, 1992 in cricket, Zimbabwean cricket seasons from 1980–81 to 1999–2000

The New Zealand cricket team toured Zimbabwe for a two-match Test series and a two-match One Day International (ODI) series between 31 October and 12 November 1992. New Zealand won the Test series, which was the first played between the two teams, 1–0[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.espncricinfo.com/zimbabwe/engine/series/60584.html|title=New Zealand in Zimbabwe Test Series 1992/93 / Results | publisher= ESPN| work = Cricinfo| accessdate= 31 December 2010[/tpl] and the ODI series 2–0.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.espncricinfo.com/zimbabwe/engine/series/60929.html|title=New Zealand in Zimbabwe ODI Series 1992/93 / Results | publisher= ESPN| work = Cricinfo| accessdate= 31 December 2010[/tpl]
