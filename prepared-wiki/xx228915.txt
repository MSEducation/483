[[Liga Nacional de Ascenso de Honduras 2008–09 Apertura]]

CATEGORIES: Liga Nacional de Ascenso de Honduras, 2008–09 domestic association football leagues, 2008 in Honduras

The Liga Nacional de Ascenso de Honduras 2008–09 Apertura is the first part of the 2008-09 season in the Honduran second division of football. The champion of this tournament will face the winner of the Clausura tournament to decide who will be promoted to the first division.
