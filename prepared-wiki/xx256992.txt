[[Rowhouses at 702-712 Kirkwood Boulevard]]

CATEGORIES: Houses completed in 1905, Tudor Revival architecture in Iowa, Residential buildings in Davenport, Iowa, Residential buildings on the National Register of Historic Places in Iowa, National Register of Historic Places in Scott County, Iowa

The Rowhouses at 702-712 Kirkwood Boulevard are located on the east side of Davenport, Iowa, United States.  The Tudor Revival structure has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since 1984.
