[[Pierre Thiolon]]

CATEGORIES: 1927 births, Living people, French basketball players, Olympic basketball players of France, Basketball players at the 1948 Summer Olympics, Olympic silver medalists for France, Olympic medalists in basketball

Pierre Thiolon (born January 17, 1927) was a French basketball player who competed in the 1948 Summer Olympics.databaseOlympics profile
He was part of the French basketball team, which won the silver medal.
