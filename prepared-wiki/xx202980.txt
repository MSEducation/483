[[Mirșid]]

CATEGORIES: Communes in Sălaj County

Mirşid ([tpl]lang-hu|Nyirsid[/tpl]) is a commune located in Sălaj County, Romania. It is composed of four villages: Firminiş (Fürményes), Mirşid, Moigrad-Porolissum (until 1996 Moigrad; Mojgrád) and Popeni (Szilágypaptelek).
