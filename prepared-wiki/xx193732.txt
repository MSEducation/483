[[Ivan Šantek]]

CATEGORIES: 1932 births, Sportspeople from Zagreb, Living people, Croatian footballers, Yugoslav footballers, Yugoslavia international footballers, Croatian expatriate footballers, Expatriate footballers in Austria, Yugoslav First League players, NK Zagreb players, GNK Dinamo Zagreb players, FC Wacker Innsbruck players, Olympic footballers of Yugoslavia, Olympic silver medalists for Yugoslavia, Footballers at the 1956 Summer Olympics, 1958 FIFA World Cup players, Olympic medalists in football

Ivan Šantek (born 23 April 1932 in Zagreb) is a former Croatian football player. On the national level he played for Yugoslavia national team (6 matches), and was a participant at the 1958 FIFA World Cup.
