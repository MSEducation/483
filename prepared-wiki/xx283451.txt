[[2013 Maserati Challenger]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 ATP Challenger Tour, Maserati Challenger, 2013 in German sport

The 2013 Maserati Challenger is a professional tennis tournament played on clay courts. It is the first edition of the tournament which is part of the 2013 ATP Challenger Tour. It is taking place in Meerbusch, Germany, between 10 and 18 August 2013.
