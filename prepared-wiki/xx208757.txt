[[Vozha River]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of Ryazan Oblast

The Vozha ([tpl]lang-ru|Во́жа[/tpl]) is a river in Ryazan Oblast, Russia. It is a right tributary of the Oka River, and is [tpl]convert|103|km|sp=us[/tpl] long.
The town of Rybnoye is situated by the Vozha.
In August 1378, Muscovite prince Dmitry Donskoy defeated a Mongol army in the Battle of the Vozha River.
