[[To the Limit (2007 film)]]

CATEGORIES: 2007 films, German films, Austrian films, German documentary films, 2000s documentary films, Films by German directors, Documentary films about sportspeople, Austrian documentary films, Documentary films about climbing

To the Limit ([tpl]lang-de|Am Limit[/tpl]) is a 2007 German documentary film written and directed by German film director Pepe Danquart, about brothers Alexander Huber and Thomas Huber climbing the El Capitan rock formation in Yosemite National Park.Catsoulis, J. Movie Review: To the Limit, The New York Times, June 13, 2008
