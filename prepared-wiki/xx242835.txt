[[Boloria pales]]

CATEGORIES: Boloria, Insects of Germany, Insects of Romania, Insects of Poland, Insects of Albania, Insects of Andorra, Insects of Bosnia and Herzegovina, Insects of Bulgaria, Insects of Italy

Shepherd's Fritillary (Boloria pales) is a butterfly of the Nymphalidae family. It is found from the Cantabrian Mountains and the Pyrenees through the Alps and Apennine Mountains east to the Balkan, Carpathian Mountains, the Caucasus and central Asia up to western China.
The wingspan is 25–30 mm. Adults are on wing from June to August depending on the location. There is one generation per year.
The larvae feed on various low growing plants, but prefer Viola species.
