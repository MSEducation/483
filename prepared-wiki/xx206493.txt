[[Marcos Martín]]

CATEGORIES: 1972 births, Living people, Spanish comics artists

Marcos Martín is a critically acclaimed, Eisner Award-winning Spanish comic book artist who has worked in American comics.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.elpais.com/articulo/portada/Rivales/intimos/elpepisupep3/20100326elptenpor_4/Tes|title=Rivales íntimos|last=CORCUERA|first=ÁLVARO|date=26 March 2010|work=El País|language=Spanish|accessdate=27 September 2011[/tpl] His notable works include Batgirl: Year One, Doctor Strange: The Oath, Breach, The Amazing Spider-Man, and Daredevil. He has also produced numerous covers for Marvel Comics and DC Comics.
