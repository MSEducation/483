[[Methodist-Episcopal Church of Norwich]]

CATEGORIES: Methodist churches in New York, Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Religious buildings completed in 1873, 19th-century Methodist church buildings, Churches in Chenango County, New York

Methodist-Episcopal Church of Norwich is a historic Methodist Episcopal church located at 74 N. Broad Street in Norwich, Chenango County, New York.  It was designed by noted architect Isaac G. Perry and built 1873-1875.  It is a large, two story brick structure, generally rectangular in shape (110 feet long by 64 feet wide) with a cross gabled transept (76 feet wide by 12 feet deep).  The front facade features two engaged towers with a large, central round arched entrance.  The north tower is surmounted by a 115 foot octagonal spire.  The south tower includes a belfry and 190  foot spire.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=11524|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: Methodist-Episcopal Church of Norwich|date=February 2003|accessdate=2010-02-20 |author=Kathleen LaFrank|publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation[/tpl] See also: [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=11535|title=Accompanying 10 photos[/tpl] 
It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 2003.
