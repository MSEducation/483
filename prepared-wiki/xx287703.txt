[[Budget Host]]

CATEGORIES: Hotels established in 1975, Motels in the United States, Companies based in Fort Worth, Texas

Budget Host is an American lodging chain. It was founded in 1975 in Fort Worth, Texas by Ray Sawyer and Ed Semmler.[tpl]cite journal|title=Ed Semmler Dies|journal=Motel/Motor Inn Journal|volume=42|issue=1-3|page=11|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=HoTwAAAAMAAJ&q=%22budget+host%22+%22ray+sawyer%22&dq=%22budget+host%22+%22ray+sawyer%22&hl=en&sa=X&ei=zxcKUpvDH6P8yAGF2IHwDQ&ved=0CE4Q6AEwBw[/tpl]
