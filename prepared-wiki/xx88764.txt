[[Lewis Henry Lapham]]

Lewis Henry Laphamhttp://freepages.genealogy.rootsweb.ancestry.com/~battle/celeb/lloyd.htm (March 13, 1858 – June 10, 1934)[tpl]cite news| url=http://select.nytimes.com/gst/abstract.html?res=F00E16FE3958177A93C3A8178DD85F408385F9 | work=The New York Times | title=LEWIS H. LAPHAM, FINANCIER, 76, DIES; Retired Leather Merchant Was a Founder of Texas Corporation, an Oil Concern, HELD MANY DIRECTORATES Began Career at 19, Later Joining His Father's Company-Interested in Ship Line | date=1934-06-11[/tpl] was an American entrepreneur who made a fortune consolidating smaller business in the leather industry. He was also one of the founders of Texaco Oil Company.
Lapham was born in Brooklyn, New York, the son of Samantha Lapham (née Vail) and Henry Griffith Lapham, who were second cousins.http://www.abebooks.fr/servlet/BookDetailsPL?bi=185489044&searchurl=bi%3Dh%26vcat%3DAutographs___Manuscripts%26vcatn%3DAutographs-Manuscripts%26vci%3D35834 He built Waveny House[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.mofflymedia.com/Moffly-Publications/New-Canaan-Darien-Magazine/July-2008/The-Magic-of-Waveny/|title=The Magic of Waveny|accessdate=2009-05-19[/tpl] in New Canaan, Connecticut as a summer residence for his family to escape the heat of New York City. The mansion and property are now a public park and arts center in New Canaan.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.nytimes.com/1987/05/10/realestate/if-you-re-thinking-of-living-in-new-canaan.html|title=If You're Thinking of Living in:; NEW CANAAN|publisher=New York Times|accessdate=2009-05-19 | first=Eleanor | last=Charles | date=1987-05-10[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://laphamcenter.com/welcome_history.html|title=Lapham Center|accessdate=2009-05-19[/tpl]
Lapham was the father of San Francisco mayor Roger Lapham, the grandfather of actor Christopher Lloyd, and the great-grandfather of Lewis H. Lapham, of Harper's Magazine.
