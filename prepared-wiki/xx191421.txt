[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Diamantino]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1929, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Cuiabá, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Diamantino ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Adamanteae[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Diamantino in the Ecclesiastical province of Cuiabá in Brazil.
