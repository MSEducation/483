[[Slovenia at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics, Athletics in Slovenia, 2013 in Slovenian sport

Slovenia is scheduled to compete at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics in Moscow, Russia, from 10–18 August 2013.
A team of 9 athletes was announced to represent the country in the event.
