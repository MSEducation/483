==Biography==

George was born in Manchester, New Hampshire. At school she was known as a beauty and she went to New York where she sang with big bands including Glen Gray. She worked for over 16 years with Milton Berle.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.lasvegassun.com/news/2000/aug/25/joe-delaney-remembers-1957-milton-berle-at-origina/ |title=Milton Berle at original El Rancho Vegas casino |first=Joe |last=Delaney |newspaper=Las Vegas Sun |date=25 August 2000[/tpl]
In the 1970s and 1980s she revived her career when she became a regular guest on local TV in Albany.
George died in Albany, New York, where she was living, but was buried in New Hampshire.
