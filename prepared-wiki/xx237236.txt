[[Thierry IV]]

CATEGORIES: 720s births, 780s deaths, Counts of Autun, Counts of Toulouse, Frankish people

Thierry IV or Theoderic IV ([tpl]circa[/tpl] 720[tpl]spaced ndash[/tpl]c. 782) was a Frankish noble. Count of Autun and Toulouse; he was thought to be a son of Sigebert V, and grandson of Sigebert IV of Raze. It is now well documented that it supposed Davidic blood was a hoax (see Priory of Sion). Thierry married Auda, daughter of Charles Martel, sister of Pepin III.
