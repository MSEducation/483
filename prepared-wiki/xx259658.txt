Kaelan Joyce (born 20 August), is an amateur boxer from the British Overseas Territory, Gibraltar.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.chronicle.gi/headlines_details.php?id=16114|title=BOXING - ‘STRAIGHT SIX’ FOR GIB. BOXERS|last=Gaiviso|first=Gino|date=18 May 2009|work=Gibraltar Chronicle|publisher=Gibraltar Chronicle|accessdate=29 January 2011[/tpl]  He started his boxing career in January 2000 as a Middleweight and competed exclusively in Gibraltar at first, but then mostly abroad thereafter.

==University Days==

Kaelan was awarded a place at Southampton Solent University (UK) in September 2002, where he went on to earn an honours degree in a Computer Networking related subject.  Whilst at University he trained at the Golden Ring ABC[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.goldenringabc.co.uk/Honours.htm |title=Statistics |publisher=Goldenringabc.co.uk |date= |accessdate=2011-02-25[/tpl] (Southampton, UK) where he gained valuable experience and subsequently went on to win gold[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.dailyecho.co.uk/archive/2003/12/06/Hampshire+Archive/5582206.Indoor_Cricket__Shirazi_bats_for_Institute_cause/ |title=Indoor Cricket: Shirazi bats for Institute cause (From Daily Echo) |publisher=Dailyecho.co.uk |date= |accessdate=2011-02-25[/tpl] at the BUSA (British Universities Sports Association) Championships,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bucs.org.uk/sport.asp?section=980&sectionTitle=Boxing |title=British Universities and Colleges Sport Boxing |publisher=Bucs.org.uk |date= |accessdate=2011-02-25[/tpl] thus becoming champion of his respective weight class. He remains the only Gibraltarian to date to accomplish such a feat.

==Boxing Career and Background==

Joyce started off as a brawler with limited boxing skills but with time developed into a decent boxer.  Known for his aggressive style and sturdy defence, he improved considerably under the watchful eye of Ernest Victory (Head coach - Gibraltar Amateur Boxing Association).  
Kaelan has sparred with several big names including Tom "Kong" Watson and former World Boxing Foundation (WBFo) Super Featherweight Champion Carl Greaves.[tpl]cite web|url=http://boxrec.com/list_bouts.php?human_id=14801&cat=boxer |title=Carl Greaves - Boxer |publisher=Boxrec.com |date= |accessdate=2011-02-25[/tpl]  He has also fought and beaten renowned British Martial Artist, Jason “Daddy Cool” Ball in an amateur boxing bout that took place on 3 July 2008 at the Victoria Stadium, Gibraltar.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccsqy4amQH4 |title=Kaelan Joyce (Red) Vs Jason Ball (Blue) |publisher=YouTube |date= |accessdate=2011-02-25[/tpl]
Currently competing in the light heavyweight division (having moved up a weight class) Joyce lost his last bout on points, which took place on April the 17th 2010 at the Don Principe Pabellon (Spain).  The Spanish media had Joyce winning.[tpl]cite web|author=Club Boxeo Sevillano dice: |url=http://www.lalineadigital.es/2010/04/muy-buena-acogida-a-la-ultima-velada-de-boxeo-en-el-don-principe/ |title=Muy buena acogida a la última Velada de Boxeo en el Don Príncipe | La Linea Digital |publisher=Lalineadigital.es |date= |accessdate=2011-02-25[/tpl]
