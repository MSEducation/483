[[Zebina L. Raymond]]

CATEGORIES: 1804 births, 1872 deaths, Mayors of Cambridge, Massachusetts, Massachusetts State Senators, Massachusetts city council members

Zebina L. Raymond  (August 23, 1804-January 5, 1872) was a Massachusetts politician who served as a member of the Massachusetts State Senate and the Mayor of Cambridge, Massachusetts.
Raymond was born to Asa and Huldah (Rice) Raymond in  Shutesbury, Massachusetts on August 23, 1804.
Raymond married Rhoda C. Hildreth on July 5, 1828.
