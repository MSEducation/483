[[Hesamabad, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Hesamabad ([tpl]lang-fa|حسام اباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Ḩesāmābād; also known as Asmawa and Hīssamābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3066414|Hesamabad[/tpl] is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,817, in 350 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
