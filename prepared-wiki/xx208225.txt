[[Jean De Schryver]]

CATEGORIES: 1916 births, Possibly living people, Belgian boxers, Middleweight boxers, Olympic boxers of Belgium, Boxers at the 1936 Summer Olympics

Jean Albert De Schryver (born 7 March 1916) is a Belgian boxer who competed in the 1936 Summer Olympics.
In 1936 he was eliminated in the second round of the middleweight class after losing his fight to Henryk Chmielewski.
