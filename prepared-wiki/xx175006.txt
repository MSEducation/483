[[Gmina Kościelisko]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Lesser Poland Voivodeship, Tatra County

Gmina Kościelisko is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Tatra County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland, on the Slovak border. Its seat is the village of Kościelisko, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|5|km|mi|0[/tpl] west of Zakopane and [tpl]convert|86|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south of the regional capital Kraków.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|136.37|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 8,035.

==Villages==

The gmina contains the villages of Dzianisz, Kościelisko and Witów.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Kościelisko is bordered by the town of Zakopane and by the gminas of Czarny Dunajec and Poronin. It also borders Slovakia.
