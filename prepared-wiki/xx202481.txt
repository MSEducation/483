[[Renato Chisso]]

CATEGORIES: 1954 births, Living people, Venetian politicians, People from the Province of Venice, The People of Freedom politicians, Forza Italia politicians, Italian Socialist Party politicians

Renato Chisso (Quarto d'Altino, 28 July 1954) is an Italian politician from Veneto.
A long-time member of the Italian Socialist Party, he joined Forza Italia in 1994. Elected to the Regional Council of Veneto in 1995 and re-elected in 2000, 2005 and 2010, he was regional minister of Environment and Transports in Galan II Government between 2000 and 2005.
