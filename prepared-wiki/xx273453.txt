[[Plainview, Illinois]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Macoupin County, Illinois, Unincorporated communities in Illinois

Plainview is an unincorporated community in Hillyard Township, Macoupin County, Illinois, United States. Plainview is [tpl]convert|4|mi|km[/tpl] northeast of Shipman. Plainview had a post office, which closed on October 3, 1998.[tpl]cite web|title=Postmaster Finder - Post Offices by ZIP Code|url=http://webpmt.usps.gov/pmt008.cfm|publisher=United States Postal Service|accessdate=September 25, 2011[/tpl]
