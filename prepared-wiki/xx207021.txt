[[95th National Guard Higher Command (Greece)]]

CATEGORIES: Brigades of Greece, Rhodes

The 95th National Guard Higher Command ([tpl]lang-el|95η Ανώτερη Διοίκηση Ταγμάτων Εθνοφυλακής, 95η ΑΔΤΕ[/tpl]), is a Greek Army formation responsible for the defence of the island of Rhodes in Dodecanese Islands.
