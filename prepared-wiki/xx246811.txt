[[ADVA Optical Networking]]

CATEGORIES: International organizations, Telecommunications companies of Germany

ADVA Optical Networking SE is a telecommunications vendor[tpl]cite web | url=http://fibresystems.org/cws/article/magazine/30359 | title=Fibre Systems Europe[/tpl] that provides network equipment for data, storage, voice and video services. ADVA Optical Networking has a global workforce of over 1,300 employees and its Fiber Service Platform (FSP) has been deployed in more than 250 carriers and 10,000 enterprises.[tpl]cite web | url=http://fibresystems.org/cws/company/B400011001 | title=Fibre Systems Europe[/tpl]
