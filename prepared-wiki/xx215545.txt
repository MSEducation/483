[[KBHC]]

CATEGORIES: Oldies radio stations in the United States, Adult standards radio stations in the United States, Spanish-language radio stations in Arkansas, Radio stations established in 1974, Radio stations in Arkansas

KBHC (1260 AM) is a radio station serving the Texarkana area with a Hispanic format.  The station is currently under ownership of Arklatex Radio, Inc.
