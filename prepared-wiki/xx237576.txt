[[Michael Jones (New Zealand footballer)]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, New Zealand association footballers, New Zealand international footballers

Michael Jones is a former association football player who represented New Zealand at international level.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ultimatenzsoccer.com/NZRepSoccer/id1708.htm|title=A-International Appearances - Overall|author=|publisher=The Ultimate New Zealand Soccer Website|date=|accessdate=17 Oct 2009[/tpl]
Jones made his full All Whites debut in a 0-0 draw with New Caledonia on 25 July 1969[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ultimatenzsoccer.com/NZRepSoccer/id1559.htm|title=A-International Lineups|author=|publisher=The Ultimate New Zealand Soccer Website|date=|accessdate=17 Oct 2009[/tpl] and ended his international playing career with four official caps to his credit, his fourth and final cap an appearance in a 0-2 loss to Israel on 1 October 1969.
