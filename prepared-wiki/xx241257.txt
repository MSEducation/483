[[Sonid Left Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Sonid Left Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠰᠥᠨᠡᠳ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠵᠡᠭᠦᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Söned Jegün qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=苏尼特左旗 |t=蘇尼特左旗 |p=Sūnítè Zuǒqí[/tpl])) is a banner of north-central Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China. It is under the administration of Xilin Gol League.Sunud
Mongols live here.

==Climate==

Sonid Left Banner features a cold semi-arid climate (Köppen BSk), marked by long, cold and very dry winters, hot, somewhat humid summers, and strong winds, especially in spring. The monthly 24-hour mean temperature in January, the coldest month, is [tpl]convert|−18.7|°C|1[/tpl], and in July, the warmest month, [tpl]convert|22.0|°C|1[/tpl], with the annual mean at [tpl]convert|3.07|°C|1[/tpl]. The annual precipitation is [tpl]convert|185|mm|in|2[/tpl], with more than half of it falling in July and August alone.
