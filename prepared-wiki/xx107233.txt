[[I'll Walk with God]]

CATEGORIES: Songs with music by Nicholas Brodzsky, Songs with lyrics by Paul Francis Webster, 1954 songs, Mario Lanza songs

"I'll Walk with God" is a popular song with music by Nicholas Brodzsky and lyrics by Paul Francis Webster, published in 1954.    This song is best known from the movie The Student Prince, in which the title character, played by Edmund Purdom, but sung by tenor Mario Lanza, performs this song at the coffin of his grandfather, the king of Carlsburg.
