[[SM City Cagayan de Oro]]

CATEGORIES: Shopping malls in the Philippines, Shopping malls established in 2002, SM Prime Holdings, Cagayan de Oro, 2002 establishments in the Philippines

SM City Cagayan de Oro is a large shopping mall owned and operated by SM Prime Holdings, the largest mall operator in the Philippines. It is the first SM Supermall in Northern Mindanao area. The mall is located at Fr. Masterson Avenue Cor. Gran Via St., Pueblo de Oro Township, Uptown Carmen, Cagayan de Oro City, Misamis Oriental.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.smprime.com/smprime/index.php?p=671&mall=30|title=SM City CDO|publisher=SM Prime[/tpl] It opened on November 2002 with a total  gross floor area of 87,000+ sqm. on a 5.2 hectares land.

==Physical details==

The mall opened in November 15, 2002 with a 56,010.55 sqm. area offering more shopping and dining options. It also has a 30,000+ sqm. car park building.  
The mall consists of a rectangle-shaped building and an outdoor and indoor parking. The mall features anchors like SM Department Store, SM Supermarket and SM Cinemas. The mall has undergone a major renovation which was finished by first quarter of 2012.
