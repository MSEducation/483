[[D.J. Stapleton]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing, Year of death missing, Erin's Own hurlers (Kilkenny), Kilkenny hurlers, Winners of three All-Ireland medals (hurling)

D.J. Stapleton was an Irish sportsperson.  He played hurling with his local club Erin's Own and with the Kilkenny senior inter-county team in the early years of the 20th century.
