[[Bolugh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Bolugh ([tpl]lang-fa|بلوغ[/tpl], also Romanized as Bolūgh; also known as Bolūk and Buluk)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3056595|Bolugh[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 36, in 7 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
