Erich Brabec (born 24 February 1977) is a Czech football player who is currently playing for Bohemians 1905.
He has played in one international match for the Czech Republic in the 2000 Olympic Games.
Brabec joined FC Dynamo Moscow in January 2002, and left for FC Alania Vladikavkaz in July 2003. Now, he plays for Sparta Prague.
