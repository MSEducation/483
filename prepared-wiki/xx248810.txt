[[The New Daisy Theatre]]

CATEGORIES: Buildings and structures in Memphis, Tennessee, Culture of Memphis, Tennessee, Visitor attractions in Memphis, Tennessee, Music venues in Tennessee, Cinemas and movie theaters in Tennessee, Theatres completed in 1936, Historic district contributing properties in Tennessee

The New Daisy Theatre is an all-ages venue located at 330 Beale Street in Memphis, Tennessee. It plays host to both local and national acts as well as being available for rental events.
The venue opened in 1936 and has featured artists such as John Lee Hooker, Gatemouth Brown, Jerry Lee Lewis, Al Green, Sam and Dave, Bob Dylan, Alex Chilton, The Cramps, Stevie Ray Vaughan, Nirvana, Alice in Chains, Justin Timberlake, Nelly, The Cult and Oasis, among others.
