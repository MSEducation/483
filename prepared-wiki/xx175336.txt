[[KGLE]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Montana, Radio stations established in 1962, Christian radio stations in the United States, Dawson County, Montana, Moody Radio affiliate stations

KGLE (590 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve Glendive, Montana.  The station is owned by Friends of Christian Radio, Inc. It airs a Religious radio format including programming from Moody Broadcasting Network.[tpl]cite web |title=Station Information Profile |work=Arbitron |url=http://www.arbitron.com/[/tpl]
The station was assigned the KGLE call letters by the Federal Communications Commission.[tpl]cite web |work=FCC Media Bureau CDBS Public Access Database |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=11016&Callsign=KGLE |title=Call Sign History[/tpl]
