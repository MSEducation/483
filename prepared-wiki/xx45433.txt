[[High/Low]]

CATEGORIES: Nada Surf albums, 1996 debut albums, Albums produced by Ric Ocasek, Elektra Records albums

High/Low was Nada Surf's first full-length album. The album is best known for its single "Popular."

==Track listing==

All tracks written by Matthew Caws and Daniel Lorca.
