[[Almeida, Portugal]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Portugal

Almeida is a town in Almeida Municipality, Portugal.  The fortress around the town guards an important cross-border road from Spain, and underwent several sieges.  The siege of 1810 ended spectacularly when a chance shell ignited the main gunpowder magazine, which exploded, killing 500 defenders and destroying most of the town http://www.historyofwar.org/articles/siege_almeida_1810.html.
