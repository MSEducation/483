[[Ford, Virginia]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Dinwiddie County, Virginia, Unincorporated communities in Virginia

Ford is an unincorporated community in Dinwiddie County, Virginia, United States. Ford is located on U.S. Route 460 [tpl]convert|19.2|mi|km[/tpl] west-southwest of Petersburg. Ford has a post office with ZIP code 23850.[tpl]GR|7[/tpl]
