Marc Andre Manga Epresse Priso (born 16 January 1988) is French professional footballer who plays as a striker.

==Career==

After trialling with Hereford United in the previous summer, Manga signed on non-contract terms with Rochdale in October 2009.[tpl]cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/r/rochdale/8297894.stm|title=Rochdale snap up Frenchman Manga|date=8 October 2009|publisher=BBC Sport[/tpl] Manga made three appearances for Rochdale, two in the Football League and one in the FA Cup,[tpl]soccerbase|id=52543[/tpl] before being released in November 2009.[tpl]cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/b/barrow/8380542.stm|title=Dale loan pair head for Gateshead|date=26 November 2009|publisher=BBC Sport[/tpl]
