[[Aboriginal Protection Board]]

CATEGORIES: History of Indigenous Australians, Organisations serving Indigenous Australians, Stolen Generations

Aboriginal Protection Board refers to a number of historical Australian State run institutions with the function of regulating the lives of Indigenous Australians. They were also responsible for administering the various Half-caste acts where these existed and had a key role in the Stolen generations. The Board had nearly ultimate control over Aborigines' lives.
