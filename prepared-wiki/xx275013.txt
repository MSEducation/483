[[A. M. Harun-ar-Rashid]]

CATEGORIES: 1933 births, Living people, Bangladeshi writers, Bengali writers, Bengali-language writers, University of Dhaka alumni, Bangladeshi scientists, Bangladeshi physicists, University of Dhaka faculty, Recipients of the Ekushey Padak (Bangladesh)

A. M. Harun-ar-Rashid is a Bangladeshi physicist and Bose Professor of Physics at the University of Dhaka. He is the nephew of Shamsuddin Abul Kalam.
