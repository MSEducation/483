[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Juazeiro]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1962, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Feira de Santana, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Juazeiro ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Iuazeiriensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Juazeiro in the Ecclesiastical province of Feira de Santana in Brazil.
