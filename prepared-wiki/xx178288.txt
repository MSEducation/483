[[Gmina Kadzidło]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Ostrołęka County

Gmina Kadzidło is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Ostrołęka County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Kadzidło, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|20|km|mi|0[/tpl] north of Ostrołęka and [tpl]convert|118|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|258.94|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 11,030.

==Villages==

Gmina Kadzidło contains the villages and settlements of Brzozowa, Brzozówka, Chudek, Czarnia, Dylewo, Dylewo Nowe, Gleba, Golanka, Grale, Jazgarka, Jeglijowiec, Kadzidło, Karaska, Kierzek, Klimki, Krobia, Kuczyńskie, Piasecznia, Podgórze, Rososz, Siarcza Łąka, Sól, Strzałki, Tatary, Todzia and Wach.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Kadzidło is bordered by the gminas of Baranowo, Lelis, Łyse, Myszyniec and Zbójna.
