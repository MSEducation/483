[[Thirumangalam, Tiruchi]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Tiruchirappalli district

Thirumangalam is a village near Lalgudi in Tiruchi district in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. It has a famous temple by the same name

==Geography==

Thirumangalam is located near Lalgudi, Tiruchirappalli. The town is situated on the banks of a distributary of river Cauvery near Trichy.
