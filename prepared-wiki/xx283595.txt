[[Cuervo, New Mexico]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Guadalupe County, New Mexico, Unincorporated communities in New Mexico, Ghost towns in New Mexico

Cuervo is an unincorporated community in Guadalupe County, New Mexico, United States. Cuervo is located along Interstate 40 [tpl]convert|16.8|mi|km[/tpl] north-northeast of Santa Rosa. Cuervo had a post office until September 10, 2011; it still has its own ZIP code, 88417.[tpl]GR|7[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Postmaster Finder - Post Offices by ZIP Code|url=http://webpmt.usps.gov/pmt008.cfm|publisher=United States Postal Service|accessdate=August 11, 2013[/tpl]
