[[1973–74 DFB-Pokal]]

CATEGORIES: DFB-Pokal seasons, 1973–74 in German football cups

The DFB-Pokal 1973–74 was the 31st season of the competition. It began on 1 December 1973 and ended on 17 August 1974. 32 teams competed in the tournament of five rounds. In the final Eintracht Frankfurt defeated Hamburg 3–1 after extra time.[tpl]cite web | title = DFB-Pokal 1973-74 | publisher = fussballdaten.de | accessdate = 2008-10-07 | year = 2008 | url = http://www.fussballdaten.de/dfb/1974/ | language = German| archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20080929212654/http://www.fussballdaten.de/dfb/1974/| archivedate= 29 September 2008 | deadurl= no[/tpl]

==Mode==

The tournament consisted of five single elimination rounds. In case a game ended with a draw 30 minutes of extra time were played. If the score was still level the game was replayed with 30 minutes of extra time in case of another draw. If still no winner could be determined, a penalty shooutout decided which team advanced to the next round.
