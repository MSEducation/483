[[KYCN]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Wyoming, Country radio stations in the United States

KYCN (1340 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Country music format. Licensed to Wheatland, Wyoming, USA.  The station is currently owned by Smith Broadcasting, Incorporated and features programing from CBS Radio  and Dial Global.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=KYCN |title=KYCN Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
