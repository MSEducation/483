[[Trippple Nippples]]

CATEGORIES: Japanese pop music groups, Musical groups from Tokyo

thumb|Performing in April 2011.Trippple Nippples is an electropop band from Tokyo, Japan. Famous for their unique performances, the band was formed in 2005. The artists going by the stage names Qrea Nippple, Yuka Nippple and Nabe Nippple do the singing and dancing. The Australians Joseph No  and Jimi Mased perform on keyboards, synthesizers, guitar, bass and laptop.  American Eddie Clay plays the drums.[tpl]cite web |url=http://search.japantimes.co.jp/cgi-bin/fm20100716a1.html |title=Trippple Nippples serve up a hot mess |author=Jody Godoy |date=July 16, 2010 |publisher=The Japan Times |accessdate=September 23, 2011[/tpl] Several of their more wild performances resulted in being banned from those venues.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.thecmuwebsite.com/article/approved-trippple-nippples/ |title=Approved: Trippple Nippples |author=CMU |date=September 22, 2011 |publisher=UnLimited Publishing |accessdate=September 23, 2011[/tpl] In an endorsement deal with the footwear brand Palladium Boots, Pharrell Williams hosted a documentary that featured Trippple Nippples' live show.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.palladiumboots.com/video/tokyo-rising#part5|title=Trippple Nippples Live at Trump Room |accessdate=September 26, 2011[/tpl][tpl]cite web |url=http://www.thecmuwebsite.com/article/approved-trippple-nippples/ |title=Pharrell Williams endorses Palladium footwear brand |author=Giles Fitzgerald  |date=September 2, 2011 |publisher=Music Week |accessdate=September 23, 2011[/tpl]
The band have never officially released a CD, but have amassed an international following.  They have played shows in Australia, China, France, Holland, Korea, Mexico, Taiwan, the UK and USA. In Japan they have performed at the Summer Sonic Festival, with Mademoiselle Yulia and Die Antwoord. In America they opened four shows for Devo.
