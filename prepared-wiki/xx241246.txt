==Tumor removal surgeries==

In 2000, McKinnon treated a woman with a large tumor disorder in Michigan. He successfully removed a 200-pound tumor in an 18-hour operation that drew worldwide attention, according to USA Today.[tpl]cite web|url=http://us.imdb.com/title/tt0785057/ |title=Super Surgery: 200 Pound Tumor (TV 2002) - IMDb |publisher=Us.imdb.com |date=2009-05-01 |accessdate=2011-08-06[/tpl]http://television.aol.com/show/surviving-a-super-tumor/11515979/main
In 2004, McKinnon offered his services and traveled on a special mission to Romania. Along with 12 other doctors, he performed a 10-hour operation and removed an 80-pound tumor from a 47-year-old woman that suffers from a genetic disorder causing tumors to grow on her body, according to BBC news.[tpl]cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/health/3419373.stm |title=Health | Giant tumor removed from woman |publisher=BBC News |date=2004-01-22 |accessdate=2011-08-06[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cbsnews.com/stories/2004/01/21/health/main594805.shtml |title=Giant Tumor Successfully Removed |publisher=CBS News |date= |accessdate=2011-08-06[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.mymultiplesclerosis.co.uk/misc/nf-tumour.html |title=Megatumour - 11 stone parasite |publisher=Mymultiplesclerosis.co.uk |date=2008-07-15 |accessdate=2011-08-06[/tpl]
He has led a mission to Choluteca, Honduras where he performs cleft lip and palate surgery. Over the years, more than 500 children have received treatment at no cost. McKinnon was asked to lead the service and says the children are grateful to be given the health treatment.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.extranews.net/news.php?nid=3907 |title=Cirujano de Clase Mundial Cambia Vidas |publisher=Extra News |date= |accessdate=2011-08-06[/tpl]
In January 2012, in a 12 hour operation a team led by McKinnon removed a 198-pound (90kg) non-cancerous tumor which was growing on the right leg of the patient, Nguyen Duy Hai, in Vietnam.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-asia-16446239 |title=Vietnam man Nguyen Duy Hai has 90kg tumor removed |publisher=BBC |date= |accessdate=2012-01-07[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cnn.com/2012/01/06/health/vietnam-tumor/index.html?hpt=hp_t3|title=Vietnam surgery removes tumor twice man's weight|publisher=CNN|date=2012-01-06 |accessdate=2012-01-06[/tpl] McKinnon waived his fee and other costs were funded through donations.
In October 2012, McKinnon was featured in the television program My Giant Face Tumor on the TLC Network. In the program, he successfully removed large facial tumors from two patients, one in Ohio and the other in Thailand, who suffer from neurofibromatosis.[tpl]cite web |url=http://blogs.discovery.com/tlc-new-now/2012/10/my-giant-face-tumor-makes-neurofibromatosis-personal.html |title="My Giant Face Tumor" Makes Neurofibromatosis Personal |author= |date=October 9, 2012 |work=TLC NewNow |publisher=Discovery.com |accessdate=October 17, 2012[/tpl]
