[[Tinmaung]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Naga Self-Administered Zone, Lahe Township

 
Tinmaung is a village in Lahe Township, Naga Self-Administered Zone, in the Sagaing Region of northwestern Burma. It is located in the Naga Hills,[tpl]cite book|last1=Saul|first1=Jamie|author2=Dominique Viallard|title=The Naga of Burma: their festivals, customs, and way of life|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=MGtuAAAAMAAJ|accessdate=2 January 2011|year=2005|publisher=Orchid Press|isbn=978-974-524-065-0[/tpl] near the Indian border, to the southwest of Htinpakhwe and northeast of Htun.[tpl]cite map|title=Bing Maps|publisher= Microsoft and Harris Corporation Earthstar Geographics LLC|accessdate=2 January 2011[/tpl] At the centre of village life is a circle with a grey roofed building, located in its western part.[tpl]cite map|title=Google Maps|publisher=Google|accessdate=2 January 2011[/tpl] Human sacrifice and headhunting has been documented in the Lahe Township.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1G1-68273454.html|title=Human Sacrifice and Slavery in the "Unadministered" Areas of Upper Burma During the Colonial Era.|author=Menas, Gordon P.|publisher=Journal of Social Issues in Southeast Asia |date=October 1, 2000|accessdate=2 January 2011[/tpl]
