Her first performance upon signing with London Records was when she invited to join the 1998 Lilith Fair at the Jones Beach venue in New York, USA.
Kevin Bacon and Jonathan Quarmby (Bacon & Quarmby) produced much of her work as did Ian Stanley formally of Tears For Fears.
Singles were released but despite critical acclaim, the single did not reach a significant chart position, but the song "Fall" received airplay on alternative UK radio stations and frequently on BBC Radio 2.
