[[Pascal Plovie]]

CATEGORIES: 1965 births, Belgian footballers, Belgium international footballers, Belgian Pro League players, Club Brugge K.V. players, 1990 FIFA World Cup players, Living people, Sportspeople from Bruges, Royal Antwerp F.C. players

Pascal Yvan Plovie (born 7 May 1965 in Bruges) is a Belgian former footballer. Playing as a defender he represented Club Brugge K.V. and Royal Antwerp FC as well as the Belgium national football team.
