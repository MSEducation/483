[[M. H. Sims]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing, Year of death missing, Northwest Missouri State Bearcats football coaches, Northwest Missouri State Bearcats men's basketball coaches

M. H. Sims was the third head football coach for the Northwest Missouri State University Bearcats. He held that position for two games during the 1918 season.  His career coaching record at Northwest Missouri State was 1 win, 1 loss, and 0 ties.  This ranks him 16th at Northwest Missouri State in total wins and sixth at Northwest Missouri State in winning percentage.http://www.nwmissouri.edu/sports/football/mediaguide/2007/07fbmg14.pdf
