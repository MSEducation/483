[[This is Unmei]]

CATEGORIES: 2001 singles, Zetima Records singles, Song recordings produced by Tsunku

[tpl]nihongo|"This is Unmei"|This is 運命|This is Fate[/tpl] is the fourth single by Japanese girl group Melon Kinenbi. It was used as the ending theme for TV Tokyo's show "Mr. Marick's Magic Time". It was released on October 11, 2001, and its highest position on the Oricon weekly chart was #28.
