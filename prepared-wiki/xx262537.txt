[[Wisdom (TV series)]]

CATEGORIES: NBC network shows, English-language television programming, 1957 American television series debuts, 1965 American television series endings, 1950s American television series, 1960s American television series, Non-fiction television series

Wisdom was a television series of half-hour interviews conducted by the NBC between 1957 and 1965.[tpl]cite web|title=Wisdom Records finding aid|url=http://hdl.handle.net/1903.1/20693|work=ArchivesUM|publisher=University of Maryland Libraries|accessdate=5 September 2013[/tpl] The series featured interviews with distinguished guests who were notable experts in the arts, physical and social sciences and in politics at the time. The series has not aired in the last 50 years and appears to have not been released for home video, but does remain accessible through a multitude of databases including the Library of American Broadcasting through several universities in the United States and around the world.
