[[(18496) 1996 JN1]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1996, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(18496) 1996 JN1 is a main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on May 9, 1996.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
