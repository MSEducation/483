[[15 Anos (Pólo Norte album)]]

CATEGORIES: Pólo Norte albums, 2008 albums, Portuguese-language albums, Farol Música albums

15 Anos is the seventh album of the Portuguese band Pólo Norte. It was published in April 2008 by Farol Música. This album was produced by  Steve Lyon. It was released to celebrate the 15 years of the band and it was composed by their most importants hits and by two new songs ("Asa Livre" and "Jeito de Ser").
