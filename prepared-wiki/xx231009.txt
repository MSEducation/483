[[Fengqing County]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Lincang City

Fengqing County (凤庆县; pinyin: Fèngqìng Xiàn) is located in Lincang Prefecture, Yunnan, China. During the Ming Dynasty it was a frontier known as Shunning Prefecture (顺宁府; Shùnníng Fǔ).[tpl]cite web|title=Southeast Asia in the Ming Shi-lu: An Open Access Resource|url=http://www.epress.nus.edu.sg/msl/place/1557[/tpl]
