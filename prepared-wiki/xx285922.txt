[[Don't Walk Away (EP)]]

CATEGORIES: Bullet for My Valentine albums

 
Don't Walk Away is an EP by Welsh metalcore band Jeff Killed John, known today as Bullet for My Valentine. Don't Walk Away contains five studio tracks and one live track. This EP contains a song titled "Nation2Nation", which is a Demo of Turn To Despair, later on the Hand of Blood EP. Also, there is a Phil Collins cover for the song In the Air Tonight.http://jeffkilledjohn.blogspot.com/2011/02/jeff-killed-john-full-discography.html
