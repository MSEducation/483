[[117240 Zhytomyr]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Astronomical objects discovered in 2004

117240 Zhytomyr is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1573.7649191 days (4.31 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=117240 | title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-05-24 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on September 19, 2004 at Andrushivka Astronomical Observatory, Ukraine.
Named after Zhytomyr city.
