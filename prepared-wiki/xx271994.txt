[[Idun Pharmaceuticals]]

CATEGORIES: Biotechnology companies, Pfizer

IDUN Pharmaceuticals is a private biotechnology firm focused on developing drugs that target apoptosis, mediated cell death in diseases related to cancer and inflammation.
IDUN was founded by Larry Bock and Drs. Larry Fritz, John Reed and Robert Horvitz.
IDUN was acquired by Pfizer in 2005 for an undisclosed amount of money, although news sources put the purchase price around $200m.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.outsourcing-pharma.com/Preclinical-Research/Pfizer-acquires-Idun-Pharmaceuticals |title=Pfizer acquires Idun Pharmaceuticals |publisher=Outsourcing-pharma.com |date= |accessdate=2012-08-03[/tpl]
