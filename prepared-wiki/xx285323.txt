[[Pusillimonas ginsengisoli]]

CATEGORIES: Burkholderiales

Pusillimonas ginsengisoli is a gram-negative, oxidase- and catalase-positive, aerobic, non-spore-forming, short rod-shaped, motile bacterium from the genus Pusillimonas, which was isolated from the soil of a ginseng field in South Korea.PubMedEzBioCloudInternational Journal of Systematic and Evolutionary Microbiology http://ijs.sgmjournals.org/content/60/8/1783.full.pdf Colonies of Pusillimonas ginsengisoli are pale yellow in color.International Journal of Systematic and Evolutionary Microbiology
