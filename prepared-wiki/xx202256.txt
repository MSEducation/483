[[Siglé Department]]

CATEGORIES: Departments of Burkina Faso, Boulkiemdé Province

Siglé is a department or commune of Boulkiemdé Province in central Burkina Faso. As of 2005 it has a population of 31,279. Its capital lies at the town of Siglé.Burkinabé government inforoute communale
