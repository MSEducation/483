[[Bumbești-Jiu]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Romania, Populated places in Gorj County, Monotowns in Romania

Bumbești-Jiu ([tpl]IPA-ro|bumˌbeʃtʲ ˈʒiw|-|Ro-Bumbești-Jiu.ogg[/tpl]) is a town in Gorj County, western Wallachia, Romania, on the river Jiu with a population of 10,617 (2002 census). It administers four villages: Curtișoara, Lăzărești, Pleșa and Tetila.

The town features the Lainici Monastery, the Vișina Monastery (built in 1418), and a "Village Museum".
