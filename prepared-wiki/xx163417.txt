[[Leshnicë e Poshtme]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Vlorë County

Leshnica e Poshtme ([tpl]Lang-el|Κάτω Λεσινίτσα[/tpl] Kato Lesinitsa) is a village in the District of Sarandë in Albania, adjacent to the Greek-Albanian borders. It is located less than 1 km north-west of the Greek border. It consists of ethnic Greek population.Report by the Albanian Government, ''Framework Convention for the protection of national minorities; Albanian Telegraphic Agency , 98-08-18.

==Geography==

The village is situated on an altitude of 648 meters (2129 ft) above the sea-level.
