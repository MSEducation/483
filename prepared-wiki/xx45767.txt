[[Gerpinnes]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Belgium, Municipalities of Hainaut (province)

Gerpinnes ([tpl]IPA-fr|ʒɛʁ.pin[/tpl]) is a Walloon municipality located in the Belgian province of Hainaut. On 1 January 2006 the municipality had 12,030 inhabitants. The total area is 47.10 km², giving a population density of 255 inhabitants per km².
Every year at Whitsun, a large procession is organised in honour of Saint Rolendis (Sainte Rolende). Participants are dressed as Napoleonic soldiers, and walk about 40 km around the whole municipality.
