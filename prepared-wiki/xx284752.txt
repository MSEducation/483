==History==

Wilddesign founded by Markus Wild in 1990, derived its name from Markus’s last name Wild. Markus Wild, an industrial designer by profession, believes  in simple, creative and functional designs.http://www.frp.nrw.de/frp2/_dld/va/v619/S4_Health_2_Wild%20-%20Design%20and%20Usability.pdf
Diagnostic systems as lifesavers and gifts – Life between science and styleMarkus Wild Profile
Wilddesign was first established in Northrhine Westphalia, Germany in 1990 and later relocated to Science Park, Gelsenkirchen, Germany in the year 1995. The specialization in the design of medical products and devices including drug delivery devices began in the year 1996, nearly 6 years after the company was founded.
Shanghai Wilddesign Co. Ltd., the first German industrial design company in China was started in the year 2006. Wilddesign Hong Kong Ltd. was also started in the year 2006.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.techmankanata.com/ar-35-pg-1/What-is-a-European-design-office.htm |title=What is a European design office doing in China? |publisher=TechmanKanata.com |date=2006-05-01 |accessdate=2013-08-11[/tpl] The newest spin-off of Wilddesign was established in Gummersbach, Germany in the year 2012.

==Locations==

Wilddesign currently has a total of 7 locations and approximately 30 employees. The Director, CEO Markus Wild leads the team in the Ruhr area of Germany. The Shanghai office was launched together with Gerhard Seizer, the initial CEO of the Shanghai office.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.designer-profile.de/profiles/418-wilddesign-group |title=Designerprofile |publisher=Designer-profile.de |date= |accessdate=2013-08-11[/tpl] Currently Helena Albrechtsson, a Swedish industrial engineer is running the operations in Shanghai.[tpl]cite web|url=http://businessforum-china.com/writer_detail.html?authorid=265 |title=Writer Profile |publisher=Business Forum China |date= |accessdate=2013-08-11[/tpl]
