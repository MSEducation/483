[[Edward Goldman (professor)]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Talmudists, Academic journal editors, Hebrew Union College alumni, Hebrew Union College faculty, Harvard University alumni, Hebrew University of Jerusalem alumni

Edward A. Goldman is a Talmudic scholar. He is Professor Emeritus Israel and Ida Bettan Professor of Midrash and Homiletics at the Hebrew Union College. He is also editor of the Hebrew Union College Annual.
Goldman studied at Harvard College, the Hebrew University of Jerusalem, and the Hebrew Union College.[tpl]cite web|title=Edward A. Goldman|url=http://huc.edu/faculty/faculty/goldmane.shtml|publisher=Hebrew Union College|accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl]
