[[Sara bint Faisal Al Saud]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, House of Saud, Saudi Arabian princesses, Saudi Arabian women in politics, Members of the Consultative Assembly of Saudi Arabia, Recipients of the Order of Abdulaziz al Saud

Sara bint Faisal Al Saud is a Saudi Arabian activist for women and children welfare and a member of the House of Saud.

==Early life==

Sara bint Faisal is a daughter of King Faisal and Iffat Al Thunayan.[tpl]cite web|title=Family Tree of Faisal bin Abdulaziz bin Abdul Rahman Al Saud|url=http://www.datarabia.com/royals/famtree.do?id=176247|work=Datarabia|accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl] Her full-siblings include Prince Mohammad, Prince Saud, Prince Turki, Princess Lolowah and Princess Haifa.

==Career and activities==

Princess Sara established one of the first charitable organizations in Saudi Arabia, Al Nahda, which was awarded the first Chaillot prize for human rights organisations in the Gulf in 2009.[tpl]cite web|title=‘Strong Foundations’? The Imperative for Reform in Saudi Arabia|url=http://edoc.bibliothek.uni-halle.de/servlets/MCRFileNodeServlet/HALCoRe_derivate_00003652/Strong%20Foundations.pdf|work=FRIDE|accessdate=10 August 2013|author=Ana Echagüe|coauthors=Edward Burke|pages=1–23|date=June 2009[/tpl] She has been the chair of the organization.[tpl]cite web|title=Board of Directors|url=http://www.alnahda-ksa.org/About/members_board_directors.aspx?lang=en-US|work=Al Nahda|accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl] She also established the private Al Tarbeya Al Islamiya Schools in 1964. She is the chair of Effat University's board of founders and board of trustees.[tpl]cite web|title=Board of Founders|url=http://www.effatuniversity.edu.sa/About/Pages/Board_of_Founders.aspx|work=Effat University|accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=10th Anniversary of Effat University|url=http://www.effatuniversity.edu.sa/About/SiteAssets/Pages/President%20office%20Speeches/2009-16%20november%20_10th%20anniversary%20of%20effat%20university.pdf|work=Effat University|accessdate=6 December 2013|date=10 November 2009[/tpl] She also serves as member of the various organizations, including Maharat Center.[tpl]cite web|title=Board of Trustees|url=http://www.maharat.org/board_en.htm|work=Maharat Center|accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl]
She was named as a member of the Consultative Assembly on 11 January 2013.[tpl]cite news|title=Two royal orders issued|url=http://www.thefreelibrary.com/Two+royal+orders,+amending+the+Shura+Council+system+and+forming+the...-a0314659905|accessdate=10 August 2013|work=Saudi Press Agency|date=11 January 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite news|title=Breakthrough in Saudi Arabia: women allowed in parliament|url=http://www.alarabiya.net/articles/2013/01/11/259881.html|accessdate=10 August 2013|work=Al Arabiya|date=11 January 2013[/tpl] She is one of the two royal women appointed to the assembly along with Moudi bint Khalid, daughter of King Khalid.[tpl]cite journal|last=Friedman|first=Brandon|title=The Saudi Kingdom in Transition:  Women Appointed to the Majlis|journal=Telaviv Notes|date=28 January 2013|volume=7|issue=2|url=http://www.dayan.org/sites/default/files/Friedman_Brandon_CHANGE_IN_SAUDI_ARABIA_TANOTES_280113.pdf|accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Royal orders amend Shura Council system and form new chamber|url=http://www.saudiembassy.net/latest_news/news01111301.aspx|work=Royal Embassy, Washington DC|accessdate=4 September 2013[/tpl]

==Personal life==

Sara bint Faisal married Mohammad bin Saud, son of King Saud.[tpl]cite journal|last=Stenslie|first=Stig|title=Power Behind the Veil: Princesses of the House of Saud|journal=Journal of Arabian Studies: Arabia, the Gulf, and the Red Sea|year=2011|volume=1|issue=1|pages=69–79|doi=10.1080/21534764.2011.576050[/tpl][tpl]cite book|author=Sharaf Sabri|title=The House of Saud in Commerce: A Study of Royal Entrepreneurship in Saudi Arabia|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=51Bb8Ix7xw8C&pg=PA305|accessdate=10 August 2013|year=2001|publisher=Sharaf Sabri|isbn=978-81-901254-0-6|pages=72[/tpl] She has four children, a daughter and three sons.[tpl]cite web|title=Family Tree of Sara bint Faisal bin Abdulaziz Al Saud|url=http://www.datarabia.com/royals/famtree.do?id=176289|work=Datarabia|accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl]

==Honors==
