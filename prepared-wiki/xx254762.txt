[[Take My Own Advice]]

CATEGORIES: 1993 albums, Willie P. Bennett albums

Take My Own Advice was the fifth album released by Canadian singer-songwriter Willie P. Bennett and was released on compact disc by Dark Light Records in 1993 (DL 12003), the same label that had previously issued his compilation album Collectibles Vol 1.
The title track is a re-recording of a song from Bennett's 1979 album Blackie and the Rodeo King.

==Track listing==

All words and music by Willie P. Bennett, all songs published by Eiffel Dog Publishing.
