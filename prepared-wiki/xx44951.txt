[[Hurdegaryp]]

CATEGORIES: Tytsjerksteradiel, Populated places in Friesland

Hurdegaryp ([tpl]lang-nl|Hardegarijp[/tpl]) is a village in the northern part of the Netherlands, in the municipality of Tytsjerksteradiel. Its history dates back at least to the 13th century. It has around the 5,000 citizens (2008).Municipality of Tytsjerksteradiel - population per village
In Dutch the village is called Hardegarijp, Hurdegaryp is its West Frisian and official name.
