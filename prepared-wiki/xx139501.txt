[[Sacha Cosmetics]]

CATEGORIES: Cosmetics companies of Trinidad and Tobago

Sacha Cosmetics Ltd. was founded in Trinidad and Tobago in 1979 by Kama Maharaj. The company manufactures makeup for women with exotic skin tones - African, Indian, Latin, Asian, Mediterranean, etc.  It is the first and only line of makeup in the world that is Haalal certified. Sacha is distributed in 23 countries, including Puerto Rico, Cuba, Senegal, New Zealand, Australia, South Africa and the Dominican Republic. In North America, it is sold online and distributed by Sacha USA, Artesia, California. Sacha was the Official Cosmetics of the Miss Universe, Miss USA and the Miss Jamaica Pageants.
