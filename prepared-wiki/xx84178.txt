[[Minamiazumi District, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Former districts of Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Minamiazumi|南安曇郡|Minamiazumi-gun[/tpl] was a district located in Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the district had an estimated population of 99,785 and a density of 117.22 persons per km². The total area was 851.25 km².

On October 1, 2005, the towns of Hotaka and Toyoshina, and the villages of Horigane and Misato, along with the town of Akashina (from Higashichikuma District) were merged to create the city of Azumino. Minamiazumi District was dissolved as a result of this merger.
