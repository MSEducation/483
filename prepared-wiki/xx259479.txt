[[Annemarie Spilker]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 births, Living people, Dutch photographers, People from Hillegom

Annemarie Spilker (born 1980, Hillegom) is a Dutch photographer best known for her self-portraits and landscapes. Spilker attended The Photography Academy in Amsterdam where she graduated Cum Laude in 2003,[tpl]cite web|author= |url=http://www.artmajeur.com/?go=user_pages/bio_section&artist_id=174067&subsec=biography&article_id=416515&login=annemariespilker |title=Annemarie Spilker - Biography Article #416515 - Artist #174067 |publisher=Artmajeur.com |date= |accessdate=2012-11-02[/tpl] with the series self-portraits "Searching to fill the emptiness..." [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.blurb.com/bookstore/detail/746700 |title=Searching to fill the emptiness... | By Annemarie Spilker | Category: Fine Art Photography |publisher=Blurb |date= |accessdate=2012-11-02[/tpl]
Spilker's photography focuses on penetrating self-portraits with an uncomfortable and confronting atmosphere, in which she searched for the hidden places in her own personality. Her landscapes appear almost unreal due to the unprecedented silence and timelessness. Since college, Spilker has worked mainly with a medium format Hasselblad,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.hasselblad.com/hoc/photographers/annemarie-spilker.aspx |title=Portfolio |publisher=Hasselblad.com |date= |accessdate=2012-11-02[/tpl] the reason most of her images are square. Sometimes she works with antique medium format cameras from around 1930-1940.
She is a member of the World Photography Organization.[tpl]cite web|author=Netherlands |url=http://www.worldphoto.org/profile/20369/ |title=Annemarie Spilker - World Photography Organisation |publisher=Worldphoto.org |date=2010-11-05 |accessdate=2012-11-02[/tpl]
