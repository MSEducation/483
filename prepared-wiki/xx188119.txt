[[Mario Vitali]]

CATEGORIES: 1914 births, Bobsledders at the 1948 Winter Olympics, Italian bobsledders, Year of death missing

Mario Vitali (born August 2, 1914, date of death unknown) was an Italian bobsledder who competed in the late 1940s. At the 1948 Winter Olympics in St. Moritz, he finished sixth in the two-man event.
