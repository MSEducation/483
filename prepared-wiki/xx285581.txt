[[College SA]]

CATEGORIES: Universities and colleges in South Africa by type, Distance education in South Africa

College SA is a distance learning college located in Bellville, South Africa and serving students throughout South Africa. thumb|The College SA head office in Cape Town, South Africa.

==History==

College SA was initially established by the current owners, in partnership with the Curro School Group, as Curro College in 2007. In 2008, the Curro School Partners sold their shares in the college to the current owners, and Curro College became College SA. Since its inception, College SA has been offering education via distance learning to students all over South Africa.

==Accreditation and registration==

College SA is registered with the South African Department of Higher Education and Training (DHET) as a private FET college. Further Education and Training (FET) refers to education at the level of Grade 10-12, and is usually focused on skills development and on preparing students for entry into the workplace.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.dhet.gov.za/LinkClick.aspx?fileticket=pDGJiq0c2Ho=&tabid=405&mid=1219 |title=The List of Registered Private FET Colleges |publisher=Dhet.gov.za |accessdate=2014-02-23[/tpl]
College SA has been accredited by FASSET and the Services SETA to offer a variety of national qualifications in bookkeeping, office administration, and management.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.westerncape.gov.za/general-publication/fet-college-information |title=FET College Information | Western Cape Government |publisher=Westerncape.gov.za |date=2013-09-04 |accessdate=2014-02-23[/tpl] Sector Education and Training Authorities (SETAs) such as FASSET and the Services SETA are responsible for overseeing skills development and training in their respective industry sectors, as well as for implementing quality assurance measures to ensure that certain standards are maintained on a national level.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.courses4africa.co.za/courses/hair-beauty-and-personal-services-courses/beauty-therapist/college-sa-diploma-in-beauty-therapy.html |title=College SA Diploma in Beauty Therapy |publisher=Courses4africa.co.za |date= |accessdate=2014-02-23[/tpl]

==Mode of delivery==

College SA is a distance learning provider. All of their courses are offered via correspondence, and are available to students all over South Africa,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.vocational.co.za/ |title=Skills Education Training Authorities. SETA Offices in South Africa |publisher=Vocational.co.za |date= |accessdate=2014-02-23[/tpl] as well as to students in a number of other African countries.  Academic support is provided to students by means of telephone, e-mail, and instant messaging programmes such as Skype.
