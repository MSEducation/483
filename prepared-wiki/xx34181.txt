[[Berney Marshes RSPB reserve]]

CATEGORIES: Royal Society for the Protection of Birds reserves in England, Nature reserves in Norfolk

Berney Marshes RSPB reserve is a nature reserve in the care [tpl]Clarify me|date=March 2008[/tpl] of the Royal Society for the Protection of Birds (RSPB). It is situated south of the River Bure in Norfolk, England, within The Broads National Park.
It is part of the Halvergate Marshes.

Berney Arms village and the impressive landmark of Berney Arms windpump are nearby.
