[[Mississippi Highway 53]]

CATEGORIES: State highways in Mississippi

Mississippi Highway 53 (MS 53) is a state highway in Mississippi. The highway runs approximately [tpl]convert|40|mi|km[/tpl] from Gulfport to Poplarville and is part of the Mississippi Highway System.  It serves a total of 3 counties (Pearl River, Hancock and Harrison).  The highway begins at the intersection of U.S. Highway 49 in Gulfport and ends at the intersection of Mississippi Highway 26 in Poplarville.
