[[Urocyclidae]]

CATEGORIES: Urocyclidae

 

Urocyclidae is a family of air-breathing land snails and semi-slugs, terrestrial pulmonate gastropod mollusks in the superfamily Helicarionoidea, (according to the taxonomy of the Gastropoda by Bouchet & Rocroi, 2005).[tpl]Bouchet 2005[/tpl]
Some species within this family make and use love darts before mating.

==Distribution==

The Urocyclidae are distributed in the Afrotropical Region, including Madagascar, the Comores and the islands of the Gulf of Guinea.[tpl]cite book|last=Herbert|first=Dai|title=Field guide to the land snails and slugs of eastern south Africa|year=2004|publisher=Natal Museum|location=Pietermaritzburg :Natal Museum|isbn=0-620-32415-5|edition=1st|coauthors=Kilburn, Dick[/tpl] 

==Taxonomy==

The family Urocyclidae consists of 2 subfamilies (according to the taxonomy of the Gastropoda by Bouchet & Rocroi, 2005), that follows classification of Urocyclinae by Van Goethem (1977):Van Goethem J. (1977). "Révision systématique des Urocyclinae (Mollusca, Pulmonata, Urocyclidae)". Musée Royal de l'Afrique Centrale , Annales, Sciences zoologiques 218: 355 pp., 4 plates.
