[[Tongan general election, 1987]]

CATEGORIES: 1987 elections in Oceania, 1987 in Tonga, Elections in Tonga

General elections were held in Tonga on 18 and 19 February 1987 to elect members of the Legislative Assembly of Tonga. Nine nobles and nine people's representatives were elected.
