[[KCWC-FM]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Wyoming, College radio stations in Wyoming, Radio stations established in 1984

KCWC-FM (88.1 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Top 40/Contemporary Hits format. Licensed to Riverton, Wyoming, USA, the station is currently owned by Central Wyoming College.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KCWC-FM |title=KCWC-FM Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==History==

The station went on the air as KCWC-FM on January 7, 1984. In January 2009, the name of the station, as well as its format, were changed. The station is now "Rustler Radio" and instead of a Jazz/Adult Alternative format, a fresher Top 40/Contemporary Hits sound.[tpl]cite web|url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=10030&Callsign=KCWC |title=KCWC Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
