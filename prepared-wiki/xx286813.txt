[[Daydar-e Sofla]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Daydar-e Sofla ([tpl]lang-fa|دايدارسفلي[/tpl], also Romanized as Dāydār-e Soflá, Daydar Sofla, and Dāidār Sufla; also known as Daidar, Dāydār, Daydar, Dāydār-e Pā’īn, and Dāyedār-e Pā’īn)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3060186|Daydar-e Sofla[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 69, in 18 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
