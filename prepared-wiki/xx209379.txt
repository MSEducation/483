[[Wild Geese Calling]]

CATEGORIES: American films, 1941 films, Films directed by John Brahm, 1940s drama films

Wild Geese Calling is a 1941 film made by 20th Century-Fox, directed by John Brahm and starring Henry Fonda and Joan Bennett. The screenplay was written by Horace McCoy, based on a novel by Stewart Edward White. The music score is by Alfred Newman.

==Plot==

left|thumb|Joan Bennett and Henry FondaA lumberjack, John, travels to Alaska from Seattle in search of gold. He marries a dance-hall girl named Sally, but soon finds that she was once in love with his best friend, Blackie.
