[[WSIM]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in South Carolina

WSIM (93.7 FM) is a radio station  broadcasting a hot adult contemporary format. Licensed to Lamar, South Carolina, USA, the station serves the Florence area.  The station is currently owned by Miller Communications, Inc. and features programing from ABC Radio .[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=WSIM |title=WSIM Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www1.arbitron.com/sip/displaySip.do?surveyID=SU08&band=fm&callLetter=WSIM|title=WSIM Station Information Profile|publisher=Arbitron[/tpl]  The programming was also simulcast on WDKD, which now airs W246BX, or "97.1 Frank FM", the HD-2 channel.

==Translators==

In addition to the main station, WSIM has been relayed by a translator to widen its broadcast area.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.radio-locator.com/cgi-bin/info?call=W246AW&service=FX|title=W246AW Radio Station Information |accessdate=2009-08-25[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.radio-locator.com/cgi-bin/info?call=W255BD&service=FX|title=W255BD Radio Station Information|accessdate=2009-08-25[/tpl] W246AW is now "97.1 Frank FM", while W255BD has been a translator for WOLH.http://www.miller.fm/news/archives.asp, Retrieved on 2009-08-25.

==History==

This station played oldies for several years with the slogan "Good Time Rock and Roll".http://www.radio-locator.com/cgi-bin/info?call=WDKD&service=AM, Retrieved on 2009-08-25.
More recently, it was adult contemporary. In September 2012, WSIM changed to hot adult contemporary.
