[[Brandon Smith (ice hockey)]]

Brandon Smith (born February 25, 1973) is a Canadian professional ice hockey defenceman who most recently played for the Straubing Tigers of the German Deutsche Eishockey Liga (DEL). He previously played parts of four seasons in the National Hockey League (NHL) for the Boston Bruins and New York Islanders. He played in a total of 33 NHL games and registered 3 goals and 4 assists.
