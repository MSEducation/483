[[Château de Belpuig]]

CATEGORIES: Castles in Languedoc-Roussillon, Pyrénées-Orientales, Ruins in Languedoc-Roussillon, Ruined castles in France

The Château de Belpuig is a ruined castle in the commune of Prunet-et-Belpuig in the Pyrénées-Orientales département of France.
The castle occupies a strategic position on a rocky spur overlooking the surrounding countryside that is only a 15 minute walk from the village. The extensive panorama takes in the mountain ranges of Canigou, the Albères, the Corbières and the coast of Languedoc and Roussillon.Green Guide: Languedoc, Roussillon, Tarn Gorges p.94, Michelin Tyre plc, 1998, ISBN 2-06-136602-3
