[[Kovlands IF]]

CATEGORIES: Sport in Medelpad, Football clubs in Sweden, 1903 establishments in Sweden

Kovlands IF is a Swedish football club located in Kovland.[tpl]cite web |url= http://www8.idrottonline.se/default.aspx?id=225764 |title= Kovlands IF – Fotboll Klubb |format= |work= |accessdate=2011-09-22[/tpl]

==Background==

Kovlands IF currently plays in Division 4 Medelpad which is the sixth tier of Swedish football.[tpl]cite web |url= http://www.everysport.com/sport/fotboll/fotbollsserier-2011/division-4-herr/medelpad/44637|title= Medelpad - Division 4 – Fotbollsserier 2011 – Fotboll – everysport.com |format= |work= |accessdate=2011-09-22[/tpl] They play their home matches at the Ånäsvallen in Kovland.[tpl]cite web |url= http://svenskfotboll.se/svensk-fotboll/om-svff/distrikten/?scr=table&ftid=32412|title= Tabell och resultat - Division 4 Herrar - Svenskfotboll.se |format= |work= |accessdate=2011-09-22[/tpl]
The club is affiliated to Medelpads Fotbollförbund.[tpl]cite web |url= http://svenskfotboll.se/svensk-fotboll/om-svff/distrikten/?flid=32407 |title= Kontaktuppgifter och tävlingar – Kovlands IF - Svenskfotboll.se |format= |work= |accessdate=2011-09-22[/tpl]
