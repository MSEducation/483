[[Long Datih]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Sarawak

Long Datih is a Christian Kelabit longhouse settlement in the mountainous interior of Sarawak, Malaysia. It lies approximately [tpl]convert|576.1|km|mi|0[/tpl] east-north-east of the state capital Kuching. 
The village converted to Christianity in the 1940s; a visitor in 1960 described how, as well as Sunday services in the village chapel, prayers were held in each longhouse at the start of each day.
