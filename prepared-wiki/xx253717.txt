[[Milt Welch]]

CATEGORIES: 1924 births, Living people, Detroit Tigers players, Major League Baseball catchers, Baseball players from Illinois

Milton Edward Welch (born July 26, 1924 in Farmersville, Illinois) is a former Major League Baseball catcher who played for one season. He played in one game for the Detroit Tigers on June 5 during the 1945 Detroit Tigers season.
