[[Friends 4 Ever]]

CATEGORIES: 2009 albums, BeFour albums

 Friends 4 Ever  is the fourth studio album by pop group beFour via Universal Records. It was  released on February 6, 2009. The lead single from the album, "No Limit" was officially released on January, 2009. The second single was Ding-A-Dong.
