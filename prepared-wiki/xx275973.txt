[[2011–12 Olympique Lyonnais season]]

CATEGORIES: Olympique Lyonnais seasons, French football clubs 2011–12 season, Articles created via the Article Wizard

The 2011–12 season of Olympique Lyonnais is the clubs 53rd season in Ligue 1. After a 3rd place finish last season, the club will play in the UEFA Champions League for the 12th consecutive season. The club will be looking to win the Ligue 1 after missing out on the title for the past 3 seasons.

==News==

At the end of the 2010–11 season, fans had become dissatisfied with then manager Claude Puel,[tpl]cite news |title=Tags et menaces au domicile de Claude Puel |url=http://www.foot01.com/equipe/lyon/tags-et-menaces-au-domicile-de-claude-puel,72061|work=Foot01.com |date=23 May 2011 |accessdate=28 September 2011[/tpl] despite the club managing to finish in 3rd place, synonymous with qualification to the UEFA Champions League Play-off round. However, due to poor results and a falling out with the clubs hierarchy, he was sacked.[tpl]cite news |title=Lyon-Puel: Pas de conciliation |url=http://www.eurosport.fr/football/ligue-1/2011-2012/lyon-puel-pas-d-accord_sto2834866/story-lci.shtml|work=Eurosport.fr |date=15 June 2011 |accessdate=28 September 2011[/tpl][tpl]cite news |title=Communiqué du club |url=http://www.olweb.fr/fr/Accueil/100007/Article/56463/Communique-du-club|work=OLWeb |date=20 June 2011 |accessdate=28 September 2011[/tpl] He was replaced by former technical coach Rémi Garde,[tpl]cite news |title=Former Arsenal midfielder Rémi Garde appointed coach of Lyon |url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/football/2011/jun/22/arsenal-remi-garde-coach-lyon|work=Guardian.co.uk |date=22 June 2011 |accessdate=28 September 2011[/tpl] who announced his intentions to compete for the league title.[tpl]cite news |title=OL: Rémi Garde veut jouer le titre |url=http://www.footmercato.net/ol-remi-garde-veut-jouer-le-titre_69073|work=FootMercato |date=30 June 2011 |accessdate=28 September 2011[/tpl]
On 17 June, Lyon announced the departure of midfielder Jérémy Toulalan to Málaga for €10M.[tpl]cite news |title=Lyon et Málaga OK pour Toulalan |url=http://www.lequipe.fr/Football/breves2011/20110617_113628_lyon-et-malaga-ok-pour-toulalan.html|work=L'Equipe.fr |date=17 June 2011 |accessdate=28 September 2011[/tpl][tpl]cite news |title=Toulalan ya es del Málaga por 10 millones de euros |url=http://www.as.com/futbol/articulo/toulalan-malaga-millones-euros/20110617dasdasftb_15/Tes|work=as.com |date=17 June 2011 |accessdate=28 September 2011[/tpl]
During pre-season training, Yoann Gourcuff suffered another injury blow, this time to his left ankle, which required an operation keeping him out for at least a month.[tpl]cite news |title=Gourcuff, une cheville qui fait peur |url=http://www.eurosport.fr/football/ligue-1/2011-2012/gourcuff-deja-un-coup-dur_sto2869785/story.shtml|work=Eurosport.fr |date=13 July 2011 |accessdate=28 September 2011[/tpl]
On 16 July, Brazilian midfielder Ederson was injured to the right knee during a pre-season match against Genk after a collision with team-mate Dejan Lovren.[tpl]cite news |title=Ederson blessé au genou |url=http://www.goal.com/fr/news/1764/matches-amicaux/2011/07/16/2577547/amical-lyon-ederson-bless%C3%A9-au-genou|work=goal.com |date=16 July 2011 |accessdate=29 September 2011[/tpl]
On 12 August Lyon announce the signing of Burkina Faso international defender Bakary Koné from Guingamp for €2M.[tpl]cite news |title=Communiqué de presse |url=http://www.olweb.fr/fr/Accueil/100007/Article/56819/Communique-de-presse|work=OLWeb |date=12 August 2011 |accessdate=29 September 2011[/tpl]
On 27 August, during a match against Montpellier, forward Lisandro López injured his ankle. It was originally feared that he had broken his ankle, as the striker believed he had heard a crack,[tpl]cite news |title=Lisandro, blessé, sort en larmes sur une civière |url=http://www.leparisien.fr/lyon-69000/l1-lyon-lisandro-blesse-sort-en-larmes-sur-une-civiere-27-08-2011-1581956.php|work=Leparisien.fr |date=27 August 2011 |accessdate=29 September 2011[/tpl] but scans later alayed these fears.[tpl]cite news |title=Lisandro évite le pire|url=http://www.football365.fr/france/infos-clubs/lyon/lisandro-evite-pire-736161.shtml|work=Football365.fr |date=28 August 2011 |accessdate=29 September 2011[/tpl] Nevertheless, the Argentine will be out for several weeks.[tpl]cite news |title=Lyon's Lisandro López escapes ankle break but is out for several weeks after injury in 2–1 win over Montpellier |url=http://www.goal.com/en-gb/news/3274/ligue-1/2011/08/28/2639914/lyons-lisandro-lopez-escapes-ankle-break-but-is-out-for|work=goal.com |date=28 August 2011 |accessdate=29 September 2011[/tpl]
On 30 August Lyon signed defender Mouhamadou Dabo from Sevilla for a modest €1M that could rise to €1.8M if certain playing criteria are met.[tpl]cite news |title=Dabo returns to France with Lyon |url=http://soccernet.espn.go.com/news/story/_/id/951050/mouhamadou-dabo-returns-to-france-with-lyon?cc=5739|work=ESPNSoccernet |date=30 August 2011 |accessdate=29 September 2011[/tpl]
On summer transfer deadline day, Lyon announced the sale of Bosnian international midfielder Miralem Pjanić to Roma for €11M,[tpl]cite news |title=Official Club Statement: Sale of Miralem Pjanić |url=http://www.olweb.fr/en/home/100007/Article/57000/Official-Club-Statement|work=OLWeb |date=31 August 2011 |accessdate=29 September 2011[/tpl] and the arrival of midfielder Gueïda Fofana from Le Havre for €1.8M possibly rising to €2.6M.[tpl]cite news |title=Communiqué du club: Arrival of Gueïda Fofana |url=http://www.olweb.fr/fr/Accueil/100007/Article/57001/Communique-du-club|work=OLWeb |date=31 August 2011 |accessdate=29 September 2011[/tpl]
On 28 September, the club announced that Michel Bastos had signed a two-year contract extension keeping him at the club until 2015.[tpl]cite news |title=Bastos prolonge son contrat |url=http://www.olweb.fr/fr/Accueil/100007/Article/57296/Bastos-prolonge-son-contrat|work=OL Web |date=28 September 2011 |accessdate=28 September 2011[/tpl]
On 30 March, the club issued a press release confirming that forward Alexandre Lacazette had signed a contract extension until 2016, and that defender Samuel Umtiti had signed his first professional contract at the club. The latter deal comes after the young central defenders successful promotion into the clubs first team in recent months.[tpl]cite news |title=Umtiti professionnel, Lacazette prolonge |url=http://www.olweb.fr/fr/Accueil/100007/Article/59024/Umtiti-professionnel-Lacazette-prolonge|work=OL Web |date=30 March 2012|accessdate=2 April 2012[/tpl]
