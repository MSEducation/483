[[Soma Moritane]]

CATEGORIES: 1529 births, 1601 deaths, Samurai, Daimyo

[tpl]nihongo|Sōma Moritane|相馬盛胤[/tpl] (1529–1601) was a Japanese samurai of the Sengoku period. 15th generation head of the Soma clan. Key figure in the fighting that took place in northern Japan, especially with the Date clan. Father of Soma Yōshitane.
