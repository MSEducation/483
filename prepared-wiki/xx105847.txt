[[Actua Ice Hockey]]

CATEGORIES: 1998 video games, Actua Sports video games, Gremlin Interactive games, Ice hockey video games, PlayStation games, Sports video games, Video games developed in the United Kingdom, Windows games, Winter Olympic video games

Actua Ice Hockey is a sports video game developed by Gremlin Interactive for the PlayStation and Microsoft Windows. It is part of the Actua Sports series.

==Sequel==

Actua Ice Hockey was followed by its sequel Actua Ice Hockey 2.
