[[Sister Sadie]]

CATEGORIES: 1950s jazz standards

"Sister Sadie" is a jazz standardhttp://www.jazzstandards.com/compositions-5/sistersadie.htm JazzStandards.com. Retrieved 9 August 2013. written in 1959 by Horace Silver, and first recorded for his 1959 Blue Note album, Blowin' the Blues Away.
