[[Bagh Kandi]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Bagh Kandi ([tpl]lang-fa|باغ كندي[/tpl], also Romanized as Bāgh Kandī, Bāgh-i-Kandi, and Bag-Kendi)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3054689|Bagh Kandi[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Bala Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 265, in 75 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
