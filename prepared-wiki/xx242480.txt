[[Matty van Toorn]]

CATEGORIES: 1950 births, 2007 deaths, Dutch footballers, Belgian footballers, Belgian football managers, Feyenoord players, SBV Excelsior players, R. Charleroi S.C. players, R.S.C. Anderlecht players, RFC Liège players, RFC Liège managers, Sports agents, Sportspeople from Rotterdam

Matthijs 'Matty' van Toorn (29 December 1950, Rotterdam – 22 November 2007) was a Dutch football player, manager and player's agent.
Van Toorn began his playing career with Feyenoord. He played for SBV Excelsior in the 1970–71 Eerste Divisie season.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Voetbal International|title=Matty Van Toorn|url=http://www.vi.nl/Spelers/Speler.htm?dbid=40542&typeofpage=84137|language=Dutch|accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl] He moved to Belgium where he would spend six seasons with Sporting Charleroi before joining R.S.C. Anderlecht in 1978.[tpl]cite news|work=El Mundo Deportivo|title=Van Toorn, un Belga a prueba en el Malaga|url=http://hemeroteca.elmundodeportivo.es/preview/1980/12/27/pagina-39/1067062/pdf.html|language=Spanish|date=1980-12-27[/tpl] He would later play for R.F.C. de Liège and K. Sint-Niklase S.K.E.
Following his playing career, Van Toorn managed K.S.K. Tongeren, Union Royale Namur, Olympic and R.F.C. de Liège and later became a player's agent, discovering Taye Taiwo.
