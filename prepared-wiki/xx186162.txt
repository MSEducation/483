[[Christmas in Concert]]

CATEGORIES: 2001 live albums, Live Christmas albums, Christmas albums by American artists, John Denver albums, RCA Records live albums

Christmas In Concert  is a Christmas-themed album of music performed live by American singer-songwriter John Denver. It was released in September 2001 by RCA Records. It features the World Children's Choir (directed by Sondra Harnes) and the National Symphony Orchestra (conducted by Richard Kaufman).
