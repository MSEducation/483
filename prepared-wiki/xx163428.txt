Michael G. Green (born October 23, 1979) is a Canadian former professional ice hockey player. 
During the 2003–04 season he played 24 games in the National Hockey League (NHL) with the Florida Panthers and New York Rangers, scoring one goal and three assists, and collecting four penalty minutes. 
Green moved to Germany in 2004, signing with the Nürnberg Ice Tigers and then joining the Hannover Scorpions the next season before returning to the United States to play in the American Hockey League with the Iowa Stars. He rejoined the Hannover Scorpions for the 2007–08 season. He has joined the Wolfsburg Grizzly Adams for the 2008-09 season.
