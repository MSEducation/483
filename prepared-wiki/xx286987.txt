[[Halilabad]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Halilabad ([tpl]lang-fa|هليل اباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Halīlābād; also known as ‘Alīlābād, Jalīlābād, and Khillabad)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3065672|Halilabad[/tpl] is a village in Golabar Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 674, in 171 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
