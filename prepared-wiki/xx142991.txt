[[Bodegas Pomar, C.A.]]

CATEGORIES: Companies of Venezuela, Companies established in 1985, 1985 establishments in Venezuela

Bodegas Pomar is an Empresas Polar subsidiary that makes still and sparkling wine. Its headquarters are located in Barquisimeto, Lara, Venezuela. Their vineyards are located in Altagracia, Lara and enclose 80 hectares.
