[[Ardhin]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Ardhin ([tpl]lang-fa|اردهين[/tpl], also Romanized as Ardhīn and Ardahīn; also known as Ardehen and Ūrd-ī-Hūn)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3053568|Ardhin[/tpl] is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 809, in 151 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
