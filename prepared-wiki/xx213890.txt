[[Pożarów, Lublin Voivodeship]]

Pożarów [tpl]IPAc-pl|p|o|'|ż|a|r|u|f[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Firlej, within Lubartów County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) – TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|7|km|mi|0[/tpl] north of Firlej, [tpl]convert|19|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north-west of Lubartów, and [tpl]convert|42|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of the regional capital Lublin.
