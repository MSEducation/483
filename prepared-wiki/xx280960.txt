[[Richard the Lionheart (2013 film)]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 films, American films, Italian films, Richard I of England

Richard The Lionheart is a 2013 film, starring Chandler Maness as Richard I of England, Malcolm McDowell as Henry II of England and Andrea Zirio as Henry the Young King.
