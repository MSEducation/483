[[Joe Spence (footballer born 1925)]]

CATEGORIES: 1925 births, People from Salford, Greater Manchester, English footballers, Association football central defenders, Chesterfield F.C. players, York City F.C. players, Gainsborough Trinity F.C. players, 2009 deaths

Joseph Lown "Joe" Spence (born 13 October 1925 in Salford, England) was an English former footballer.

==Career==

A former England Schools' player, Spence started his football career with  Chesterfield http://www.chesterfield-fc.co.uk/page/ClubAnnouncements/0,,10435~1916453,00.html in January 1948. He moved to Buxton in July 1949, after not making a single appearance at Chesterfield. Spence moved on to York City in July 1950. He was a virtual ever-present at the club during the 1951–52 and 1952–53 seasons. He left the club in the summer of 1954 to move to Burton Albion.[ref]
