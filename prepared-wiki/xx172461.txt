[[Aguja skate]]

CATEGORIES: Bathyraja, Fish of Peru, Animals described in 1912

The aguja skate (Bathyraja aguja) is a species of skate, a fish in the Rajidae subfamily. Little is known about this fish. It has not been collected since its discovery in the open seas off the coast of Peru in 1904.Ishihara, H. & Huveneers, C. Bathyraja aguja.   2006 IUCN Red List of Threatened Species.
