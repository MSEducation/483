[[Bank of America Plaza (Atlanta)]]

CATEGORIES: Office buildings completed in 1992, Bank of America buildings, Skyscrapers between 300 and 349 meters, Skyscrapers in Atlanta, Georgia, Office buildings in Atlanta, Georgia, Roche-Dinkeloo buildings

Bank of America Plaza is a skyscraper located in between Midtown Atlanta and Downtown Atlanta, otherwise known as the SoNo district of Atlanta, Georgia. At [tpl]convert|317|m|abbr=on[/tpl] the tower is the 60th-tallest building in the world. It is the 10th tallest building in the U.S.[tpl]cite news|last=Fernandez|first=Bob|title=Tallest between Manhattan and Chicago? An Atlanta peach|accessdate=21 March 2014|newspaper=The Philadelphia Inquirer|date=21 January 2014[/tpl]  and the tallest building in Georgia.[tpl]cite web|title=Bank of America Plaza Atlanta|url=http://www.emporis.com/building/bankofamericaplaza-atlanta-ga-usa|publisher=Emporis|accessdate=21 March 2014[/tpl] It is also the tallest building in any U.S. state capital, and the tallest building in the United States outside of Chicago and New York City; however, the Comcast Innovation and Technology Center in Philadelphia would surpass the Bank of America Plaza as the tallest building outside of New York and Chicago if built as proposed at [tpl]convert|342|m|abbr=on[/tpl]. It has 55 stories of office space and was completed in 1992, when it was called NationsBank Plaza.[ref]
