[[Hylarana tipanan]]

CATEGORIES: Hylarana, Amphibians of the Philippines, Endemic fauna of the Philippines

Hylarana tipanan is a species of true frog in the genus Hylarana. It is native to the Sierra Madre mountains of the island of Luzon, Philippines. It inhabits cool streams and rivers in lower montane and lowland forests. It is threatened by logging and habitat loss.
