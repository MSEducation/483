[[2012 UEFA Women's U-19 Championship Second qualifying round]]

CATEGORIES: 2012 UEFA Women's U-19 Championship, 2012 in women's football

The 2012 UEFA Women's U-19 Championship Second qualifying round will determine the participating teams at the 2012 UEFA Women's U-19 Championship held in Turkey.

==Qualified teams==

Seeded nations Germany, France and England were joined by 21 teams from the first qualifying round. Northern Ireland qualified as the best third placed team.[tpl]cite web|title=Norway, Denmark among magnificent seven|url=http://www.uefa.com/womensunder19/news/newsid=1683068.html|publisher=UEFA|accessdate=27 September 2011|date=22 September 2011[/tpl]

==Group stage==

The draw was held on 15 November 2011 at Nyon, Switzerland. Teams will be drawn in six groups of four. The winners and best runners-up will qualify for the final tournament.[tpl]cite web|title=Germany face Sweden, Poland, Northern Ireland|url=http://www.uefa.com/womensunder19/news/newsid=1715133.html|publisher=UEFA|accessdate=15 November 2011[/tpl]
