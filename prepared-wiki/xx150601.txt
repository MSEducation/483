[[K43DV]]

CATEGORIES: Television stations in Texas, Trinity Broadcasting network affiliates, Defunct terrestrial television stations in the United States, Television channels and stations established in 1991

K43DV was a low-power television station licensed to Victoria, Texas. It was a repeater that broadcast programming from the Trinity Broadcasting Network, via satellite.  The station broadcast on UHF channel 43, with no digital channel.
K43DV left the air July 27, 2010.
