[[Asia Muhammad]]

CATEGORIES: 1991 births, Living people, Sportspeople from Long Beach, California, African-American tennis players, American female tennis players

Asia Muhammad (born April 4, 1991) is a professional American tennis player. On March 31, 2014, she reached her highest WTA singles ranking of 295 while her best doubles ranking was 103 on September 16, 2013.[ref]
