[[Astorga FS]]

CATEGORIES: Spanish futsal clubs, Futsal teams in Castile and León, Sports clubs established in 1977, Sports clubs disestablished in 2001, 1977 establishments in Spain, 2001 disestablishments in Spain

Astorga Fútbol Sala was a futsal club based in Astorga, Castile and León.
The team played on pavilion Felipe Miñambres with capacity of 3,000 seats.
