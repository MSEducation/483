[[ArtCyclopedia]]

CATEGORIES: Art websites, Virtual art museums and galleries, Domain-specific search engines, Arts databases, Online databases

Artcyclopedia is an online database of museum-quality fine art founded by Canadian John Malyon. 

==Information==

The Artcyclopedia only deals with art that can be viewed online, and indexes 2,300 art sites (from museums and galleries), with links to around 180,000 artworks by 8,500 renowned artists.Artcyclopedia home page, November 2006. The site has also started to compile a list of art galleries and auction houses.
