[[G. C. Sekhar]]

CATEGORIES: Telugu people, Living people, Telugu film directors

G.C. Sekhar is a Telugu and Tamil film director between the 1970s and 1980s. He directed movies with prominent actors, including Krishna, Suman, Mohan Babu, Krishnam Raju and others.
