[[Good as Gold (Red Rockers album)]]

CATEGORIES: 1983 albums, Red Rockers albums, Albums produced by David Kahne, 415 Records albums

Good As Gold is the second full length album from the Red Rockers, released in 1983 on Columbia/415. It contained the single "China," which peaked at number 53 on the Billboard 200.[ref]
 [tpl]Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot[/tpl][/ref] Other singles from the album were "Til It All Falls Down" and the title track, "Good As Gold." The music video for the song Good As Gold was filmed at the Alamo Village; the same location where John Wayne shot the film, The Alamo.
