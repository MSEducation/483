[[Martin Baehr]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, German entomologists, Date of birth missing (living people)

Martin Baehr is a German entomologist, currently a research associate of the Zoologische Staatssammlung München.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.zsm.mwn.de/col/e/staff.htm|title=Staff of the Coleoptera Section|publisher=Zoologische Staatssammlung München|accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl]
In addition to scholarly works, Baehr has written a number of popular books including Welcher Käfer ist das? ("What Beetle is This?") published by Stuttgart-based publishing company Kosmos.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.kosmos.de/personendetail-199-199/martin_baehr-756/|title=Martin Baehr |publisher=Kosmos (publisher)|accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl]
