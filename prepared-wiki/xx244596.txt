[[Save Me (Wake Up Call)]]

CATEGORIES: 2005 singles, Unwritten Law songs, Songs written by Linda Perry, 2005 songs

"Save Me (Wake Up Call)" is a song by the San Diego-based rock band Unwritten Law, released as the first single from the band's 2005 album Here's to the Mourning. It was written by singer Scott Russo along with Aimee Allen and Linda Perry, and was produced by Perry and Sean Beavan. It became the second highest-charting single of the band's career, reaching #5 on Billboard's Modern Rock Tracks chart.[tpl]cite web| url=[tpl]BillboardURLbyName|artist=unwritten law|chart=Alternative Songs[/tpl] |title=Unwritten Law Album & Song Chart History: Alternative Songs |publisher=Billboard charts |accessdate=2009-10-06[/tpl]
