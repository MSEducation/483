[[5904 Württemberg]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Astronomical objects discovered in 1989

5904 Württemberg is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1747.5573759 days (4.78 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=5904 | title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-05-23 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on January 10, 1989.
