[[Jere Sallinen]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Espoo Blues players, 1990 births

Jere Sallinen (born October 26, 1990) is a Finnish professional ice hockey winger who currently plays for Espoo Blues of the SM-liiga.Blues' 2010-11 Roster He was selected by the Minnesota Wild in the 6th round (163rd overall) of the 2009 NHL Entry Draft.
His older brother Tomi also plays in Blues.
