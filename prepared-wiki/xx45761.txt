[[Courcelles, Belgium]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Hainaut (province)

Courcelles ([tpl]IPA-fr|kuʁ.sɛl[/tpl]) is a Walloon municipality located in the Belgian province of Hainaut. As of January 1, 2006 Courcelles had a total population of 29,626. The total area is 44.24 km² which gives a population density of 670 inhabitants per km².
In 1944, the town was the site of the Courcelles Massacre of 20 civilians by Belgian collaborators. The event is commemorated by a plaque at the site.
