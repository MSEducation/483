[[Ole Arntzen Lützow]]

CATEGORIES: 1801 births, 1871 deaths, Members of the Parliament of Norway, Hedmark politicians

Ole Arntzen Lützow (14 November 1801 – 2 November 1871) was a Norwegian politician.
He was elected to the Parliament of Norway in 1839, 1842 and 1845, representing the rural constituency of Hedemarkens Amt.
