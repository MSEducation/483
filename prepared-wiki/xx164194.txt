[[Akron Symphony Orchestra]]

CATEGORIES: Musical groups from Akron, Ohio, American orchestras, Visitor attractions in Akron, Ohio, Musical groups established in 1949, 1949 establishments in Ohio

The Akron Symphony Orchestra is a professional orchestra based in Akron, Ohio. The ASO was founded in 1949.http://www.akronsymphony.org/about/aboutUs.asp In 2006, the ASO appointed Christopher Wilkins music director. 
The Akron Symphony Orchestra is operated by the Greater Akron Musical Association (GAMA)
