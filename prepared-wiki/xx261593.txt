[[Akaimia]]

CATEGORIES: Fossil taxa described in 2010, Extinct sharks, Jurassic fish

Akaimia is an extinct genus of carpet sharks which existed in Ogrodzieniec near Zawiercie, southern Poland, during the middle or late Jurassic (Callovian or Oxfordian age).[tpl]Cite journal|author=Jan Rees |year=2010 |title=Neoselachian sharks from the Callovian–Oxfordian (Jurassic) of Ogrodzieniec, Zawiercie Region, southern Poland |journal=Palaeontology |volume=53 |issue=4 |pages=887–902 |doi=10.1111/j.1475-4983.2010.00967.x [/tpl] It was first named by Jan Rees in 2010 and the type species is Akaimia altucuspis.
