[[Edgar Jiménez]]

CATEGORIES: 1984 births, Living people, Venezuelan footballers, Venezuela international footballers, Caracas F.C. players

Edgar Hernán Jiménez (born 19 October 1984) is an Venezuelan international footballer who plays for Caracas FC, as a midfielder.

==Career==

Jiménez has played for Caracas FC since 2003,[tpl]NFT player|pid=15004[/tpl] winning the Venezuelan Primera División twice and the Copa Venezuela once.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.soccerway.com/players/edgar-hernan-jimenez/23308/|title=Profile|publisher=Soccerway[/tpl]
He made his international debut for Venezuela in 2006.
