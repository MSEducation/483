[[Gmina Wieprz]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Lesser Poland Voivodeship, Wadowice County

Gmina Wieprz is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Wadowice County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. Its seat is the village of Wieprz, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|10|km|mi|0[/tpl] west of Wadowice and [tpl]convert|46|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-west of the regional capital Kraków.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|74.51|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 11,493.

==Villages==

Gmina Wieprz contains the villages and settlements of Frydrychowice, Gierałtowice, Gierałtowiczki, Nidek, Przybradz and Wieprz.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Wieprz is bordered by the gminas of Andrychów, Kęty, Osiek, Polanka Wielka, Przeciszów, Tomice, Wadowice and Zator.
