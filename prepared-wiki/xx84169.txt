[[Asahi, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Asahi|朝日村|Asahi-mura[/tpl] is a village located in Higashichikuma District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the village has an estimated population of 4,941 and a density of 69.96 persons per km². The total area is 70.63 km².
