[[Yarrow Park]]

CATEGORIES: Football venues in Scotland, Sports venues in the Scottish Borders

Yarrow Park is a football ground located in the town of Selkirk in Scotland. The ground is the home of Scottish Lowland Football League club Selkirk F.C.. It has a capacity of 1,000 with 100 seats.Selkirk, nonleaguescotland.org.uk. Retrieved 11 August 2013.
