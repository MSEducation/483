[[Mount Alice (British Columbia)]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of British Columbia, Pacific Ranges

[tpl]Other uses|Mount Alice (disambiguation)[tpl]![/tpl]Mount Alice[/tpl]

Mount Alice is a mountain located at the Queens Reach arm of the Jervis Inlet within the Pacific Ranges of the Coast Mountains in British Columbia Canada.  The mountain was named during the 1860 survey by the HMS Plumper who charted all of the known area and named the mountain after HRH Alice Maud Mary who was the third child of Queen Victoria and Prince Albert.Hitz, Charles W.(2003). Through the Rapids - The History of Princess Louisa Inlet, p.54. Sikta 2 Publishing., Kirkland, WA. ISBN 0-9720255-0-2.
