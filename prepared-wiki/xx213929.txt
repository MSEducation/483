[[Kosrae flying fox]]

CATEGORIES: Pteropus, Fauna of Micronesia, Animals described in 1883

The Kosrae flying fox (Pteropus ualanus) is a species of megabat in the Pteropus genus found on the island of Kosrae, Micronesia.D. E. Wilson & D. M. Reeder, 2005: Mammal Species of the World: A Taxonomic and Geographic Reference. Third Edition. The Johns Hopkins University Press, Baltimore
