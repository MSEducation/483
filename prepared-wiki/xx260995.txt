[[West Kirk, Helensburgh]]

CATEGORIES: Category B listed buildings in Argyll and Bute, Church of Scotland churches in Scotland, Listed churches in Scotland, Churches in Argyll and Bute, Religious buildings completed in 1853, 19th century in Scotland, Helensburgh

The West Kirk is a Church of Scotland parish church on Colquhoun Square in Helensburgh, Argyll, Scotland. Designed by James Hay, in 1853, it is designated as a Category B listed building by Historic Scotland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://hsewsf.sedsh.gov.uk/hslive/hsstart?P_HBNUM=34766|title=Listing|publisher=Historic Scotland|accessdate=1 January 2011[/tpl]
