[[Pashed]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Masal County

Pashed ([tpl]lang-fa|پشد[/tpl]; also known as Pashed Nīlāzh)[tpl]GEOnet3|6003260|Pashed[/tpl] is a village in Shanderman Rural District, Shanderman District, Masal County, Gilan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 189, in 49 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|01[/tpl]
