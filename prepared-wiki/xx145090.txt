[[Itabashi-honchō Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Tokyo

[tpl]Nihongo|Itabashi-honchō Station|板橋本町駅|Itabashi-honchō-eki[/tpl] is a subway station on the Toei Mita Line in Itabashi, Tokyo, Japan, operated by Toei Subway.
It is located in the northern part of Tokyo, under the intersection of Nakasendo and Kannana-dori.
