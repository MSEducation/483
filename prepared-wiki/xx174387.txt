[[Dawbon Township]]

CATEGORIES: Townships of Yangon

Dawbon Township ([tpl]lang-my|ဒေါပုံ မြို့နယ်[/tpl] [tpl]IPA-my|dɔ́bòʊɴ mjo̰nɛ̀|[/tpl]) is located in the southeastern part of Yangon, Myanmar. The township comprises 14 wards, and shares borders with Thingangyun township in the north, Mingala Taungnyunt township in the west, Thaketa township in the east, and Pazundaung township in the south across the Pazundaung Creek. Dawbon is connected to downtown Yangon via the Maha Bandula Bridge.
The township has twelve primary schools, four middle schools and one high school.
