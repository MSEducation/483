[[Bothriocline]]

CATEGORIES: Asteraceae genera

Bothriocline is a genus of flowering plants in the aster family, Asteraceae. They are native to tropical Africa. They are hairy annual and perennial herbs with purple or mauve flowers.Hyde, M. A., et al. (2013). Bothriocline. Flora of Zimbabwe.
There are about 62 accepted species names in the genus, including:Bothriocline. The Plant List.
