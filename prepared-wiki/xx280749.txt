[[Yahyaabad, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Yahyaabad ([tpl]lang-fa|يحيي اباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Yaḩyáābād; also known as Yahiābād and Yakhiabad)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3838333|Yahyaabad[/tpl] is a village in Bonab Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 64, in 15 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
