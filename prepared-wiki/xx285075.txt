[[Yen Teh-fa]]

CATEGORIES: Political office-holders in the Republic of China on Taiwan, Living people, Defense Ministers of the Republic of China

Yen Teh-fa ([tpl]zh|t=嚴德發|p=Yán Défā[/tpl]) is an armed forced personnel of the Republic of China. He currently serves as the Chief of the Republic of China Army since 16 January 2014.
