[[Usa District, Ōita]]

CATEGORIES: Former districts of Ōita Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Usa|宇佐郡|Usa-gun[/tpl] was a district located in Ōita Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the district had an estimated population of 12,739 and the density of 48.85 persons per km². The total area was 260.79 km².

==Merger==

On March 31, 2005 - the towns of Ajimu and Innai were merged into the expanded city of Usa. Therefore, Usa District was dissolved as a result of this merger.
