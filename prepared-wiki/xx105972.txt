[[Faranah Region]]

CATEGORIES: Regions of Guinea, Faranah Region

The Faranah Region is located in east-central Guinea. It is bordered by the countries of Sierra Leone and Mali and the Guinean regions of Kankan, Mamou, Nzérékoré, and Labé.
