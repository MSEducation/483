[[Delores Taylor]]

CATEGORIES: American film actresses, People from Tripp County, South Dakota, Living people, Actresses from South Dakota, University of South Dakota alumni, 1932 births

Delores Taylor is a former American film actress, writer and producer, known for her roles in the Billy Jack films of the 1970s.

==Life and career==

Taylor was born in 1932.  She was raised in Winner, South Dakota.  She met actor Tom Laughlin while a student at the University of South Dakota in 1953, and married him on October 15, 1954.[tpl]cite news|last=Spiro|first=J.D.|title=Milwaukee's Actin' Tom Laughlin|url=http://news.google.com/newspapers?id=eTEaAAAAIBAJ&sjid=5iUEAAAAIBAJ&pg=5732,2727998&dq=tom+laughlin&hl=en|work=Milwaukee Journal|date=August 26, 1956|accessdate=February 19, 2010[/tpl]  Together the couple developed the character of "Billy Jack", who first appeared in the 1967 film The Born Losers. They followed that with Billy Jack in 1971, then The Trial of Billy Jack in 1974, and Billy Jack Goes to Washington in 1976. Taylor and Laughlin played the starring roles in the latter three films.
