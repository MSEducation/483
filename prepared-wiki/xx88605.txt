[[FleishmanHillard]]

CATEGORIES: Public relations companies of the United States, Companies based in St. Louis, Missouri, Companies established in 1946, 1946 establishments in Missouri

Fleishman-Hillard Inc. is a public relations and marketing agency founded and based in St. Louis, Missouri.  It was acquired by Omnicom Group in 1997,[tpl]cite book| last = Devlin | first = Edward S.| title = Crisis Management Planning and Execution| accessdate = 2 February 2014| year = 2006| publisher = Taylor & Francis| isbn = 9780203485897| page = 372[/tpl] becoming part of the Diversfied Agency Services (DAS) division.[tpl]cite news| last = Elliott| first = Stuart.| title = An Agency Division Chronicles Its Own I.T. Overhaul | accessdate = 2 February 2014| date = 18 June 2012| newspaper = The New York Times[/tpl]  The company was founded in 1946 by Alfred Fleishman and Bob Hillard.[tpl]Cite web |title= FleishmanHillard Milestones |url= http://fleishmanhillard.com/about/milestones/ |publisher= FleishmanHillard |accessdate= May 3, 2013[/tpl] For more than twenty years, its customer base was largely limited to regional clients.  
In 1994, the company expanded its operations to the Asia Pacific region with an office in Beijing.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.holmesreport.com/agencyreport-info/2022/FleishmanHillard.aspx |title=Agency Report: Fleishman-Hillard |publisher=The Holmes Report |date=2010-03-29 |accessdate=2013-08-05[/tpl]  In May 2013, the company rebranded its name to FleishmanHillard and launched the slogan "the Power of True".[tpl]cite news | url =  http://www.nytimes.com/2013/04/29/business/media/fleishmanhillard-rebrands-itself-with-a-21st-century-focus.html?_r=0| title= The New Look of Public Relations| first1=Stuart | last1=Elliott | work=The New York Times | date=April 29, 2013[/tpl]  [tpl]As of|2013|8[/tpl], the company had 111 offices in 29 countries across the Americas, Asia Pacific, Europe, Middle East, and Africa.[tpl]cite web|url=http://fleishmanhillard.com/offices/ |title=FleishmanHillard Offices |publisher=FleishmanHillard |date=N/A |accessdate=2013-08-05[/tpl]
