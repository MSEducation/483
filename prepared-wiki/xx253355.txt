[[Pleasant Valley Grange Hall]]

CATEGORIES: Grange buildings on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Houses completed in 1830, Buildings and structures in Oneida County, New York

Pleasant Valley Grange Hall is a historic Grange Hall  located at Pleasant Valley in Oneida County, New York. It was built about 1830 as a farmhouse.  It consists of a rectangular, [tpl]frac|2|1|2[/tpl]-story, gable-roofed limestone main block with a 1-story service wing.  There is also a 1-story gable-roofed frame wing.  It has been used as a Grange Hall since 1922.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=6160|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: Pleasant Valley Grange Hall|date=September 1998|accessdate=2010-01-08 |author=Raymond W. Smith|publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation[/tpl] See also: [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=6161|title=Accompanying four photos[/tpl] 
It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1999.
