[[Gabriella Pession]]

CATEGORIES: 1977 births, Italian actresses, American actresses, Living people, People from Daytona Beach, Florida

Gabriella Pession is an Italian actress.

==Biography==

She was born on 2 November 1977 and lived until the age of seven years in the United States, before embarking on a career as an actress. She has practiced figure skating at a competitive level.
