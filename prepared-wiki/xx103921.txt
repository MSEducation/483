[[Moritz Nähr]]

CATEGORIES: 1859 births, 1945 deaths, Austrian photographers

Moritz Nähr (August 4, 1859 – June 29, 1945) was an Austrian photographer. Nähr was a friend of the members of the Vienna Secession art group. He is best known for his portraits of Gustav Klimt and Ludwig Wittgenstein.
