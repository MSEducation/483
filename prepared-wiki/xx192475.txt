[[Rural Municipality of Lac du Bonnet]]

CATEGORIES: Rural municipalities in Manitoba

Lac du Bonnet is a rural municipality in the province of Manitoba in Western Canada. The separately administered town of Lac du Bonnet lies within the borders of the municipality. It also mostly encircles the Pinawa local government district in the municipality's southeast part. The RM contains the southern part of Manitoba's Brightstone Sand Hills Provincial Forest and the northern part of Agassiz Provincial Forest, although most of these forests lie in other RMs.
