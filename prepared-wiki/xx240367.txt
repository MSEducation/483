[[4th Indian Cavalry Brigade]]

CATEGORIES: Cavalry brigades of the British Indian Army, Brigades of India in World War I

The 4th Indian Cavalry Brigade was a cavalry formation of the Indian Army during World War II.
It was formed in Bolarum in September 1939, and disbanded in July 1940.[tpl]cite web|accessdate=2009-10-19|publisher=Order of Battle|title=4 Indian Cavalry Brigade|url=http://www.ordersofbattle.com/UnitData.aspx?UniX=10073&Tab=Uhi[/tpl]
