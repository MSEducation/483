[[Francisco Antequera]]

CATEGORIES: 1964 births, Living people, Sportspeople from Valencia, Valencian cyclists, Cyclists at the 1984 Summer Olympics, Olympic cyclists of Spain, Spanish male cyclists

Francisco Antequera (born 9 March 1964 in Valencia, Spain) was a Spanish racing cyclist. Professional from 1985 to 1993, he won the Vuelta a La Rioja and the Tour de Burgos. He was selector of the Spanish men's road racing team from 1997 to 2008.
