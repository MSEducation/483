[[Mikhael Jaimez-Ruiz]]

Mikhael Aimar Jaimez-Ruiz (born 12 July 1984) is a Venezuelan professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Conference South side Hayes and Yeading United.
He started his senior career at Olimpia Gherla in 2003, and had spells at CFR Cluj, FC Arieşul Turda and Barnet before joining Northwood in 2007. He made eleven appearances at Northwood, but soon moved to Aldershot Town in 2007. During his three-year spell at Aldershot, he made 49 league appearances, before returning to his native Venezuela, when he joined Yaracuyanos FC in 2010, where he made 9 league appearances. He rejoined Aldershot Town in February 2011, but did not make a single appearance, and, after a one-off game for Hemel Hempstead Town, he joined Conference South side Dover Athletic on a free transfer in August 2011. He made 38 league appearances for the club, before switching to AFC Wimbledon in August 2012. However, he made just two appearances for AFC Wimbledon, and left the club in June 2013.

==Career==

Jaimez-Ruiz finished his education in Venezuela and moved to the United States. He played university soccer for Graceland University and St. Gregory's University. In 2004, Jamiez-Ruiz moved to Romania and played for Olimpia Gherla, CFR Cluj and Arieşul Turda.
Jamiez-Ruiz moved to England and signed for Barnet on non-contract terms in 2006. He never played for Barnet and joined Northwood in early 2007.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.northwoodfc.com/AZ/J/Jaimez-Ruiz%20Mikhael.htm | title= Mikhael Jaimez-Ruiz – Northwood Record | publisher=Northwood F.C.[/tpl]
He then joined Aldershot Town on non-contract terms in April 2007 and was an unused substitute goalkeeper in the last two Conference National league games of that season. In July 2007, Jaimez-Ruiz signed a full contract at Aldershot. He made three league appearances near the end of a season. He also played in the Conference League Cup Final, when Aldershot beat Rushden & Diamonds. He saved a penalty in extra time and again in the penalty shootout.[tpl]cite news | url=http://newsimg.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/eng_conf/6903365.stm | title= Setanta Shield results 2007/08 | publisher=BBC Sport | accessdate=7 May 2011[/tpl] He signed a new contract with Aldershot Town in the summer of 2008 and he made his Football League debut in October 2008 at home against Bury.[tpl]cite news | url=http://www.theshots.co.uk/newspage.ink?nid=30319&newstype=m&matchid=8265|title=Aldershot Town 3–3 Bury| publisher=Aldershot Town F.C. | accessdate=4 October 2009[/tpl] Jamiez-Ruiz signed another one-year contract with Aldershot in May 2009 and following the departure of Nikki Bull in the summer of 2009 he became the first choice goalkeeper.[tpl]cite news | url=http://www.theshots.co.uk/newspage.ink?nid=31262&newstype=p&matchid=| title= Scott Donnelly And Mikhael Jaimez Ruiz Sign| publisher=Aldershot Town F.C. | accessdate=14 May 2009[/tpl] In November 2009, he was awarded the Aldershot Player of the Month award for October.[tpl]cite news | url= http://newsimg.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/a/aldershot/8368862.stm| title= Ruiz delight at Shots club award | publisher=BBC Sport | accessdate=7 May 2011 | date=19 November 2009[/tpl]
He was released at the end of the 2009–10 season and joined Venezuelan club Yaracuyanos FC. In February 2011, Jaimez-Ruiz returned to Aldershot Town, but was released at the end of the season without making an appearance.[tpl]cite news | url=http://newsimg.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/a/aldershot/9397492.stm | title= Mikhael Jaimez Ruiz welcomes Aldershot return | publisher=BBC Sport | accessdate=7 May 2011 | date=15 February 2011[/tpl] He made a one-off appearance for Hemel Hempstead Town in a 1–1 draw against Leamington.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.leamingtonfc.co.uk/index.php/match-reports/820-hemel-hempstead-town-v-leamington- |title=Hemel Hempstead Town 1 Leamington 1 |publisher=Leamington FC |date=13 August 2011 |accessdate=27 June 2013[/tpl]

===AFC Wimbledon===

Jaimez-Ruiz signed for AFC Wimbledon in League Two on 8 August 2012.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/19187299|title=AFC Wimbledon sign keeper Mikhael Jaimez-Ruiz|publisher=BBC Sport|accessdate=8 August 2012[/tpl] He made his first appearance for the Dons in the First Round of the Football League Trophy on 4 September 2012, in a 2–1 loss to Southend United. Jaimez-Ruiz made his league début for AFC Wimbledon on 8 September 2012 against Northampton Town. On 21 June 2013, it was confirmed that Jaimez-Ruiz had been released by AFC Wimbledon due to the club's new goalkeeping structure for next season.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.afcwimbledon.co.uk/news/article/ross-worner-875790.aspx|title=Dons sign goalkeeper|publisher=AFC Wimbledon|accessdate=21 June 2013[/tpl]
