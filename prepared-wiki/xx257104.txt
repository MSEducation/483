[[Position Among the Stars]]

CATEGORIES: 2010 films, Dutch films, Indonesian-language films, Dutch documentary films, 2010s documentary films, Documentary films about families

Position Among the Stars ([tpl]lang-nl|Stand van de Sterren[/tpl]) is a 2010 documentary directed by Leonard Retel Helmrich. It was released on 17 November 2010 as the opening film of the IDFA (International Documentary Festival Amsterdam).
The documentary is the continuation of Eye of the Day and Shape of the Moon and follows again the Sjamsuddin family, consisting of three generations, living in the slums of Jakarta, Indonesia.

==Writers==

==Plot==

Bakti quarrels with his wife. He uses holy water for his fighting fish, she takes revenge by cooking them.
He is upset after his mother has taught his little nephew a Christian prayer, since the boy is raised as a Muslim.
Bakti's stepdaughter passes her final examination. With a mortgage on the home they finance her university study.
