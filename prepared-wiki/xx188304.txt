[[Pyree, New South Wales]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in New South Wales, Towns in the South Coast (New South Wales), City of Shoalhaven

Pyree is a farming locality in the Shoalhaven district of New South Wales, Australia. It consists of a community hall and grounds which host a local farmers and craft market on the fourth Sunday of each month.Pyree, South Coast.Checkin.com.au
