[[Aaron Jeavons]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 births, Living people, People from Stafford, Alumni of Oxford Brookes University, English cricketers, Oxford UCCE cricketers, Staffordshire cricketers

Aaron Francis Jeavons (born 23 April 1989) is an English cricketer.  Jeavons is a left-handed batsman who bowls right-arm off break.  He was born in Stafford, Staffordshire and educated at Walton High School in Stafford.
While studying for his degree at Oxford Brookes University, Jeavons made a single first-class appearance for Oxford MCCU against Middlesex in 2010.[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/375/375108/First-Class_Matches.html|title=First-Class Matches played by Aaron Jeavons|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=23 September 2011[/tpl]  In this match, he scored 62 runs in the Oxford MCCU first-innings, before being dismissed by Ravi Patel.  In their second-innings, he was dismissed for 4 runs by Thomas Hampton.[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Scorecards/262/262699.html|title=Oxford MCCU v Middlesex, 2010|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=23 September 2011[/tpl]  During the 2010 season, Jeavons also made his debut for Staffordshire in the Minor Counties Championship against Suffolk.  To date he has made ten Minor Counties Championship appearances[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/375/375108/Minor_Counties_Championship_Matches.html|title=Minor Counties Championship Matches played by Aaron Jeavons|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=23 September 2011[/tpl] and a single MCCA Knockout Trophy appearance, which was made against Northumberland in 2011.[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/375/375108/Minor_Counties_Trophy_Matches.html|title=Minor Counties Trophy Matches played by Aaron Jeavons|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=23 September 2011[/tpl]
