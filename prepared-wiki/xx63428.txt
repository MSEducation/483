==Early life==

Bellucci was born in Città di Castello, Umbria, Italy[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.elsalvador.com/noticias/2003/10/30/escenarios/escen1.html | title= Persephone | first=Claudio | last=Martínez |work=El Diario de Hoy | language=Spanish | date=30 October 2003 | accessdate=2009-11-09[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://movies.msn.com/celebs/celeb.aspx?c=254848&mp=b|title=Monica Bellucci: Biography|accessdate=2008-01-23|publisher=MSN[/tpl] as the only child of Brunella Briganti and Pasquale Bellucci, and she grew up in San Giustino.

==Career==

===Modeling===

Monica Bellucci began modelling at age 13 by posing for a local photo enthusiast.[tpl]cite web|title='Monica Bellucci' by Monica Bellucci with Introduction by Giuseppe Tornatore – Book Review|url=http://glamourphotography.co/?p=3190[/tpl] In 1988, Bellucci moved to one of Europe's fashion centres, Milan, where she signed with Elite Model Management. By 1989, she was becoming prominent as a fashion model in Paris and across the Atlantic, in New York City. She posed for Dolce & Gabbana and French Elle, among others. In that year, Bellucci made the transition to acting and began taking acting classes. The February 2001 Esquire's feature on Desire featured Bellucci on the cover and in an article on the five senses. In 2003, she was featured in Maxim.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.maximonline.com/girls_of_maxim/pictures_and_bio/977/MonicaBellucci.girl|title=Monica Bellucci Photos|accessdate=2007-01-15|work=Maxim[/tpl] Men's Health also named her one of the "100 Hottest Women of All-Time", ranking her at No. 21.[tpl]cite web|title=The 100 Hottest Women of All-Time|url=http://www.menshealth.com/sex-women/hottest-women-all-time|work=Men's Health|accessdate=2012-01-03|year=2011[/tpl] AskMen named her the number one most desirable woman in 2002.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.askmen.com/specials/2002_top_99/1.html |title=Monica Bellucci |publisher=AskMen.com |date= |accessdate=2012-07-06[/tpl]

===Acting===

Bellucci's film career began in the early 1990s. She played some minor roles in La Riffa (1991) and Bram Stoker's Dracula (1992). In 1996 she was nominated for a César Award for best supporting actress for her portrayal of Lisa in The Apartment[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/culture/3588732/Fantasy-made-flesh.html|title=Fantasy made flesh|date=5 October 2006|accessdate=2009-04-17|work=The Daily Telegraph|last=Campion|first=Chris[/tpl] and strengthened her position as an actress. She became known and popular with worldwide audiences, following her roles in Malèna (2000), Brotherhood of the Wolf, "Under Suspicion",Film Credits for movie Under Suspicion and Irréversible (2002). She has since played in many films from Europe and Hollywood like Tears of the Sun (2003), The Matrix Reloaded (2003), The Passion of the Christ (2004), The Brothers Grimm (2005), Le Deuxième souffle (2007), Don't Look Back (2009), and The Sorcerer's Apprentice (2010).
She was supposed to be seen portraying Indian politician Sonia Gandhi in the biopic Sonia, originally planned for release in 2007, but it has been shelved.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.contactmusic.com/news.nsf/story/indian-government-demands-halt-to-gandhi-biopic_1003058|title=Monica Bellucci – Indian Government Demands Halt To Gandhi Biopic|accessdate=2010-10-29[/tpl] Bellucci dubbed her own voice for the French and Italian releases of the film Shoot 'Em Up (2007).[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.craveonline.com/entertainment/film/article/monica-belluccis-balancing-act-64993|title=Monica Bellucci's balancing act|date=5 September 2007|accessdate=2007-10-21|work=CraveOnline [/tpl] She also voiced Kaileena in the video game Prince of Persia: Warrior Within, and the French voice of Cappy for the French version of the 2005 animated film Robots.
Empire magazine selected her as twenty-first on their list of "The Sexiest 25 Characters in Cinema - The Women" for her role of "Persephone" in the Matrix series.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.empireonline.com/features/sexiest-movie-characters/women/default.asp?character=21 |title=The Sexiest Characters In Cinema - The Women | 21. ==Personal life==

Bellucci met French actor Vincent Cassel on the set of their 1996 film The Apartment.[tpl]cite news| url = http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2013/08/26/monica-bellucci-vincent-cassel-divorce-splitting_n_3817219.html| title=Monica Bellucci, Vincent Cassel To Divorce After 14 Years| work=Huffington Post| date= 26 August 2013|accessdate =2013-08-26[/tpl] They married in 1999, and have two daughters, Deva (born 2004) and Léonie (born 2010).[tpl]cite news| url = http://www.people.com/people/article/0,,696499,00.html| title="Passion" actress bellucci has baby girl| work=People| date= 14 September 2004| first= Stephen M.| last= Silverman| accessdate =2010-08-08[/tpl][tpl]cite news| url= http://www.google.com/hostednews/afp/article/ALeqM5gGo-agIP6DA24A6pMUPXT6ELdShw| title = Actress Monica Bellucci has baby girl| date=  25 May 2010|agency=Agence France-Presse| accessdate =2010-08-08[/tpl] Bellucci and Cassel announced their separation in August 2013.
In the documentary movie The Big Question, about the film The Passion of the Christ, she stated: "I am an agnostic, even though I respect and am interested in all religions. If there's something I believe in, it's a mysterious energy; the one that fills the oceans during tides, the one that unites nature and beings."[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.monica-bellucci.net/quotes.php|title=Monica-Bellucci.net|accessdate=2006-10-08|publisher=Monica bellucci[/tpl]
