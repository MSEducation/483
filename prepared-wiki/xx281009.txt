[[Ukraine at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics, 2013 in Ukrainian sport, Ukraine at the World Championships in Athletics

Ukraine is scheduled to compete at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics in Moscow, Russia, from 10–18 August 2013.
