[[Robert Porco]]

CATEGORIES: Indiana University faculty, Year of birth missing (living people), Living people

Robert Porco is a noted American director of large choral groups performing orchestral works. He has directed the Cincinnati May Festival Chorus with the Cincinnati Symphony Orchestra since 1989 along with the Cleveland Orchestra's Blossom Festival Chorus.rider.edu, Conducting Residency: Robert Porco, retrieved 25 May 2008
Porco was born in Steubenville, Ohio. From 1980 to 1998 he was a professor of music and chairman of the choral department at Indiana University, Bloomington.
