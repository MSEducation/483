[[John Shaw (field hockey)]]

CATEGORIES: 1962 births, Living people, British field hockey players, Field hockey players at the 1992 Summer Olympics, Field hockey players at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Olympic field hockey players of Great Britain

John David Shaw (born April 24, 1962 in Taiping, Perak, Malaysia) is a former field hockey player, who represented Great Britain in the 1992 Summer Olympics in Barcelona and the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta.British Olympic Association > Athletes > John Shaw He is currently coach of Southgate HC Men's 1st XI, and also club coach for Oxford University Hockey Club.
He won a silver medal at the 1986 Hockey World Cup.
John is now also working as a Hockey Coach at Bradfield College, and coaches at Gerrards Cross Hockey Club.
