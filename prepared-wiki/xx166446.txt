[[Lynbrook Union Free School District]]

CATEGORIES: Town of Hempstead, New York, School districts in New York, Education in Nassau County, New York

The Lynbrook Union Free School District is a public school district serving portions of the southwestern part of the Town of Hempstead. The school district includes most of the Village of Lynbrook and parts of the unincorporated areas of Lynbrook as well as portions of East Rockaway, Hewlett, Hewlett Harbor, and Valley Stream.Lynbrook Public Schools: 2007-2008 Directory & Calendar, Boundaries, p. 27. Accessed August 1, 2007.
