[[HMS C17]]

CATEGORIES: British C-class submarines, Royal Navy ship names, British submarine accidents, Ships sunk in collisions, Maritime incidents in 1917, 1908 ships

HMS ''C17'' was a C-class submarine built by HM Dockyard, Chatham for the Royal Navy. She was laid down on 11 March 1907 and was commissioned on 13 May 1909.
C17 collided with [tpl]HMS|C16||2[/tpl] in the North Sea, south of Cromer, Norfolk on 14 July 1909, and with destroyer [tpl]HMS|Lurcher|1912|2[/tpl] in May 1917.[tpl]cite web|url=http://submariners.co.uk/Boats/Losses/index.php|title=Submariners Association (Barrow in Furness Branch) - Losses|accessdate=2008-08-19[/tpl] [tpl]Dead link|date=November 2010|bot=H3llBot[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.battleships-cruisers.co.uk/c_class1.htm|title=C17 at battleships-cruisers.co.uk website|accessdate=2008-08-19[/tpl]
C17 was sold on 20 November 1919.
