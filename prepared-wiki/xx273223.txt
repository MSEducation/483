[[Abdulaziz Al-Buloushi]]

CATEGORIES: 1962 births, Kuwaiti footballers, Kuwait international footballers, Association football forwards, 1982 FIFA World Cup players, Living people

Abdulaziz Mohammed Al-Buloushi (born 4 December 1962) is a Kuwaiti football forward who played for Kuwait in the 1982 FIFA World Cup.1982 FIFA World Cup Spain He also played for Qadsia SC.
