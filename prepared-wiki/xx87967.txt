[[Bell railway station, Melbourne]]

CATEGORIES: Premium Melbourne railway stations, Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1889

Bell railway station is located on the South Morang line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the north-eastern Melbourne suburb of Preston opening as Preston-Bell Street on 8 October 1889 being renamed Bell on 1 August 1905.Preston Vicsig A signal box is located on Platform 1, which controls level crossings located at both ends of the station, as well as a siding.
On 25 August 2008 it was upgraded to a Premium station.
