[[John Glenn (screenwriter)]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people

John Glenn is an American screenwriter, producer, and director.
Glenn was born in Tuscaloosa, Alabama and worked as a musician prior to moving to Los Angeles.  Glenn began his screenwriting career 2000, selling the spec script for the film Red World to Jerry Bruckheimer and Walt Disney Pictures.[tpl]cite web|last=Lyons|first=Charles|title=Glenn, Wright to update 'Warriors'|url=http://variety.com/2002/film/news/glenn-wright-to-update-warriors-1117865115/|work=April 8, 2002|publisher=Variety|accessdate=3 September 2013[/tpl]  Subsequently, Glenn worked on films including Journey to the Center of the Earth with producer Richard Zanuck, Clash of the Titans with producer Adam Schroeder and Law Abiding Citizen with director F. Gary Gray.[tpl]cite web|last=Fleming Jr.|first=Mike|title=Paramount Hires John Glenn For Sci-Fi Thriller 'Abducted'|url=http://www.deadline.com/2011/10/paramount-hires-john-glenn-for-sci-fi-thriller-abducted/|work=March 31, 2011|accessdate=3 September 2013[/tpl] In 2008, he wrote the original draft for the Steven Spielberg produced feature Eagle Eye which Spielberg had originally planned to direct.[tpl]cite web|last=Koehler|first=Robert|title=Review: "Eagle Eye"|url=http://variety.com/2008/film/reviews/eagle-eye-1200470290/|work=September 25, 2008|publisher=Variety|accessdate=3 September 2013[/tpl] Also in 2008 - The Lazarus Project.
