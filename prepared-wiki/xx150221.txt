[[Kristian Kvakland]]

CATEGORIES: Norwegian sculptors, 1927 births, Living people

Kristian Kvakland (born February 5, 1927, Orkdal) is a Norwegian sculptor and artist. Among his most famous works is the bronze Amanda-statue of the annual Amanda awards. The statuette originally weighed [tpl]convert|4.5|kg[/tpl], but has since been slimmed down to [tpl]convert|2.5|kg[/tpl].[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.nfi.no/english/_nyheter/vis.html?id=3319|title=Kleveland returns for the 25th Amandas|publisher=Norwegian Film Institute|accessdate=28 January 2010[/tpl] [tpl]dead link|date=December 2011[/tpl] His works are also represented in a number of public art collections in Norway. Kvakland is based at Blylaget, Nesodden in southeast Norway.
