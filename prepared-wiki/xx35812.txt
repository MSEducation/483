[[Ludham Bridge]]

CATEGORIES: Hamlets in Norfolk

Ludham Bridge is a hamlet on the River Ant on the Norfolk Broads in Norfolk, England.Ordnance Survey (2005). OS Explorer Map OL40 - The Broads. ISBN 0-319-23769-9. It carries road traffic from Wroxham to Potter Heigham on the A1062.  The bridge has 2.59m (8' 6")[tpl]cite web  |url=http://www.broads-authority.gov.uk/boating/navigating/bridge-heights.html  |title=Bridge Clearances  |publisher=Broads Authority[/tpl] mean headroom for vessels and to the north has 130 metres of free 24 hours public mooring available to river craft.[tpl]cite web  |url=http://www.broads-authority.gov.uk/boating/facilities-and-access/24-hour-moorings.html  |title=Moorings |publisher=Broads Authority[/tpl]
