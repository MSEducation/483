[[Martin Thelander]]

CATEGORIES: 1981 births, Living people, Swedish ice hockey players, Färjestad BK players, Storhamar Dragons players, Swedish expatriates in Norway

Martin Thelander (born 15 May 1981, in Sweden) is a professional Swedish ice hockey player. He is currently playing for the Storhamar Dragons in the Norwegian GET-ligaen. He joined the club in 2007. His former teams are Färjestad, Bofors and Södertälje.
Thelander was a member of the 2008 Swedish national inline hockey team that competed at the 2008 Men's World Inline Hockey Championships in Bratislava.
