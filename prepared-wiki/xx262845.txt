[[1958 USAC Championship Car season]]

CATEGORIES: 1958 in motorsport, USAC Championship Car, 1958 in American motorsport

The 1958 USAC Championship Car season consisted of 13 races, beginning in Trenton, New Jersey on March 30 and concluding in Phoenix, Arizona on November 11. There were also five non-championship events.  The USAC National Champion was Tony Bettenhausen and the Indianapolis 500 winner was Jimmy Bryan. At Indianapolis in the 500 miles race death Pat O'Connor, he was 29 years old. At Atlanta in the Atlanta 100 death Art Bisch, he was 31 years old. At Trenton in the Trenton 100 death Jimmy Reece, he was 28 years old.
