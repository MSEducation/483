[[Scholars Sing Cayabyab]]

CATEGORIES: Compilation albums by Filipino artists, 2008 compilation albums, Pop compilation albums, Rhythm and blues compilation albums, Star Records compilation albums, English-language compilation albums, Tagalog-language compilation albums

Scholars Sing Cayabyab is a compilation album of Ryan Cayabyab-composed songs performed by the Pinoy Dream Academy Season 2 Top 10 scholars and the PDA Season 1 Grand Star Dreamer Yeng Constantino.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.abs-cbn.com/headlines/Article/975/Top-PDA-6-scholars-present-the-Dream-BIGayan.aspx|title=Top PDA 6 scholars present the Dream BIGayan|last=Quintos|first=Napoleon|date=2008-09-05|publisher=ABS-CBN|accessdate=2008-10-30[/tpl] The album was released on 2008 under Star Records.[tpl]cite news|url=http://showbizandstyle.inquirer.net/entertainment/entertainment/view/20080906-158960/Optimism-youthful-vibe-in-PDA-scholars-Cayabyab-album|title=Optimism, youthful vibe in ‘PDA scholars’ Cayabyab album |last=Asilo|first=Rito|date=2008-09-06|publisher=Philippine Daily Inquirer|accessdate=2008-10-30[/tpl]
The album is composed of eleven tracks that includes the songs "Paano Na Kaya", "Manalig Ka", "Limang Dipang Tao" and "Awit Ng Pangarap".
