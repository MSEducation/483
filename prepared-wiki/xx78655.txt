[[Hita District, Ōita]]

CATEGORIES: Former districts of Ōita Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Hita|日田郡|Hita-gun[/tpl] was a district located in Ōita Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the district had an estimated population of 14,180 and the density of 35.72 persons per km². The total area was 396.98 km².
