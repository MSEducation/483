[[Soul Discharge]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 albums, Boredoms albums

Soul Discharge ([tpl]nihongo2|ソウル・ディスチャージ[/tpl]) is a 1989 album by rock band Boredoms.  It was named the 89th greatest album of the 1980s by Pitchfork Media.Top 100 Albums of the 1980s on Pitchforkmedia.com
