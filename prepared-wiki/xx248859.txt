[[Mi Da Ra Matenrō]]

CATEGORIES: 2003 singles, Zetima Records singles, Song recordings produced by Tsunku

[tpl]nihongo|"Mi Da Ra Matenrō"|MI DA RA 摩天楼|Mi Da Ra Skyscraper[/tpl] is the tenth single by Japanese girl group Melon Kinenbi. It was released on September 10, 2003, and its highest position on the Oricon weekly chart was #15.
