[[Brian Marshall (footballer)]]

CATEGORIES: 1954 births, Living people, English footballers, People from Bolton-on-Dearne, Association football defenders, The Football League players, Huddersfield Town F.C. players, Scunthorpe United F.C. players

Brian Marshall (born September 20, 1954 in Bolton on Dearne, South Yorkshire) is a former professional footballer, who played for Huddersfield Town and Scunthorpe United.
