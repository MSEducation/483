[[John O'Sullivan (rugby union)]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 births, Living people, Irish rugby union players, Connacht Rugby players, Munster Rugby players, Garryowen Football Club players, Rugby union number eights

John O'Sullivan (born 12 July 1980) is a rugby union player who currently plays for Agen in the French Top 14.http://www.irishtimes.com/newspaper/sport/2009/0619/1224249120204.html He plays as a back row forward, preferably at number eight. He is a native of Tralee.
He formally played for Munster in the Magners League. Before being recruited by his native province, O'Sullivan played for two seasons with Connacht. In the 2003–04 season he was voted Connacht's senior player of the year.http://findarticles.com/p/articles/mi_qn4161/is_20040905/ai_n12904450
He left Munster at the end of the 2008–09 season to join Agen.
