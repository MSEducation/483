[[Kenyon Smith]]

CATEGORIES: American synchronized swimmers, 1990 births, Living people

Kenyon Smith (born 1990) is a male synchronized swimmer. He became interested in the sport due to a sister and ultimately joined the Santa Clara Aquamaids. He had hopes to compete in the Olympics, but this was not possible for various reasons including Synchronized swimming at the Summer Olympics being an all-female sport."This Swimmer Is in Deep End Of Gender Wars Kenyon Smith Ventures Where Few Men Go" in The Wall Street JournalABC News He is currently a Jr. Coach for the Santa Clara Aquamaids.Aquamaids coaches page
