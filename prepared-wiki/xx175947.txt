[[Riverdale (MARC station)]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Maryland, Maryland Area Regional Commuter stations, Stations along Baltimore and Ohio Railroad lines, Transportation in Prince George's County, Maryland

Riverdale is a passenger rail station on the MARC Camden Line between Washington, DC and Baltimore's Camden Station.MARC station list (includes Riverdale) MARC official website The station is part of the Riverdale Park Historic District, which has been part of the National Register of Historic Places since 2002.[tpl]NRISref|2008a[/tpl]
