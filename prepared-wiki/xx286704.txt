[[Armutlu, Iran]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Armutlu ([tpl]lang-fa|ارموتلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Ārmūtlū)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795291|Armutlu[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 76, in 19 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
