[[Bangor Children's Home]]

CATEGORIES: Buildings and structures completed in 1869, 1839 establishments in the United States, Buildings and structures in Bangor, Maine

The Bangor Children's Home was an asylum for orphans in Bangor, Maine. It was established in 1839 by the Maine Legislature as the Bangor Female Orphan Asylum.Bangor Daily News, October 4, 1989 In 1869, a new building was completed, costing $25,000, and the Children's Home was rechartered to accept both boys and girls.http://www.mainememory.net/artifact/33338/zoom The building currently houses the Hilltop School, a preschool.
It was placed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1975.
