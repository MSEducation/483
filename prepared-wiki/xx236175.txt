[[Cancriniella]]

CATEGORIES: Asteraceae genera, Anthemideae, Monotypic plant genera, Endemic flora of Kazakhstan

Cancriniella is a monotypic genus of flowering plants in the aster family, Asteraceae, containing the single species Cancriniella krascheninnikovii. It is endemic to Kazakhstan.Cancriniella krascheninnikovii. Global Compositae Checklist.
