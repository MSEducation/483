[[Maggie Lawson]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 births, 20th-century American actresses, 21st-century American actresses, Actresses from Kentucky, American child actresses, American film actresses, American television actresses, Living people, People from Louisville, Kentucky

Margaret Cassidy "Maggie" Lawson (born August 12, 1980) is an American actress who is best known for her role as Detective Juliet "Jules" O'Hara in the TV show Psych. She has also starred in the sitcoms Inside Schwartz, It's All Relative, and Crumbs, as well as the television movie Nancy Drew.

==Life and career==

Lawson was born in Louisville, Kentucky, the daughter of Judy, a homemaker, and Mike Lawson, a hotel manager.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.filmreference.com/film/6/Maggie-Lawson.html |title=Maggie Lawson Biography (1980-) |publisher=Filmreference.com |date= |accessdate=2012-05-22[/tpl] She attended Assumption High School, an All-Girls Catholic school. Early on, she co-hosted WDRB Fox 41 Kid's Club in Louisville. In 2000, she starred in Model Behavior, an ABC-TV movie featuring Justin Timberlake. She has also made guest appearances on television series such as Smallville and Fear Itself. She was recently the star of a play called Greedy, directed by James Roday, which played for a few weekends in Los Angeles.[tpl]cite web|url=http://articles.latimes.com/2011/jan/12/entertainment/la-et-james-roday-20110112 |title=The Spotlight: James Roday - Los Angeles Times |publisher=Articles.latimes.com |date=2011-01-12 |accessdate=2013-03-15[/tpl]
She co-stars in the USA Network original series, Psych, as Detective Juliet O'Hara. She has been in a relationship with Psych co-star James Roday since 2006.
On February 5, 2014 USA Network announced that Psych would finish its run on March 26 after 8 seasons.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.chicagotribune.com/entertainment/tv/chi-psych-canceled-20140205,0,2446744.story|title=USA cancels 'Psych' after 8 seasons|date=February 5, 2014|first=Tribune Support Staff|accessdate=February 8, 2014[/tpl]
Lawson starred in the ABC sitcom Back In The Game, which was picked up for the 2013 fall season. She played Terry Gannon, a single mother and former college softball player who reluctantly agrees to coach her son's little league team.[tpl]cite news|last=West|first=Kelly|title=Maggie Lawson And James Caan Get Back In The Game In New ABC Comedy|url=http://www.cinemablend.com/television/Maggie-Lawson-James-Caan-Get-Back-Game-ABC-Comedy-55687.html|accessdate=15 May 2013|newspaper=Cinema Blend|date=14 May 2013[/tpl] On November 1, 2013, ABC canceled Back in the Game.[tpl]cite web|url=http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2013/11/01/back-in-the-game-canceled-by-abc-trophy-wife-more-episodes-for-super-fun-night/213090|title='Back In The Game' Canceled by ABC; 'Trophy Wife' & 'The Goldbergs' Get Full Seasons; More Episodes for 'Super Fun Night'|last=Seidman|first=Robert|work=TV by the Numbers|date=November 1, 2013|accessdate=November 2, 2013[/tpl] The tenth episode was aired on December 11, 2013.  ABC will not air the remaining three episodes of its original 13-episode order, but may do so at a later date.[tpl]cite web|url=http://blog.sitcomsonline.com/2013/12/back-in-game-not-finishing-its-run-on.html|title=Back in the Game Not Finishing Its Run on ABC; Sitcom Stars on Talk Shows (Week of December 9, 2013)|work=Sitcoms Online|date=December 6, 2013|accessdate=December 6, 2013[/tpl]
