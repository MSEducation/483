[[(37650) 1994 FP]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1994, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(37650) 1994 FP is a main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on March 21, 1994.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
