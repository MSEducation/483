[[Qareh Aghaj, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Qareh Aghaj ([tpl]lang-fa|قره اغاج[/tpl], also Romanized as Qareh Āghāj; also known as Kara-Agach, Qara Aghach, Qarah Āqāj, Qareh Āqāch, and Qareh Āqāj)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3079611|Qareh Aghaj[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 384, in 99 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
