[[Tooloom National Park]]

CATEGORIES: National parks of New South Wales, Protected areas established in 1995, Gondwana Rainforests of Australia, 1995 establishments in Australia

Tooloom is a national park in New South Wales, Australia, 616Â km north of Sydney. The name Tooloom is derived from Bundjalung Duluhm.[tpl]cite book |last=Sharpe |first=Margaret |chapter=Bundjalung |title=Macquarie Aboriginal Words |location=Sydney |publisher=Macquarie Library |pages=21[/tpl]
The Park is part of the Focal Peak Group World Heritage Site Gondwana Rainforests of Australia inscribed in 1986 and added to the Australian National Heritage List in 2007.
