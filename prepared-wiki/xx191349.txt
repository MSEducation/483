[[Comin-Yanga Department]]

CATEGORIES: Departments of Burkina Faso, Koulpélogo Province

Comin-Yanga  is a department or commune of Koulpélogo Province in eastern Burkina Faso. Its capital lies at the town of Comin-Yanga. According to the 1996 census the department has a total population of 36,432.Burkinabé government inforoute communale
