[[Blue Sky Green Field Wind Energy Center]]

CATEGORIES: Energy infrastructure completed in 2008, Buildings and structures in Fond du Lac County, Wisconsin, Wind farms in Wisconsin

Blue Sky Green Field Wind Energy Center is a wind farm in Fond du Lac County, Wisconsin. The [tpl]convert|10600|acre|sing=on[/tpl] farm is located in the towns of Calumet and Marshfield in northeast Fond du Lac County.[tpl]cite news |first=Sherry |last=Mark |title=Johnsburg are turbines tested, We Energies says |work=Tri-County News |publisher=Delta Publishing |location=Kiel, Wisconsin |page=16 |date=2008-04-10 |accessdate=2008-05-29[/tpl] Owned by We Energies, it is the largest operating wind farm in Wisconsin.[tpl]cite news |title=Blue Sky Green Field wind farm starts spinning |work=Fond du Lac Reporter |publisher=Gannett |location=Fond du Lac, Wisconsin |date=2008-05-19 |accessdate=2008-05-29[/tpl]

==History==

An application for authorization to construct the Blue Sky Green Field Wind Farm was filed with the Public Service Commission of Wisconsin (PCSW) on March 17, 2006. On February 1, 2007, the PSCW issued the Certificate of Public Convenience and Necessity authorizing the project.Blue Sky Green Field Wind Energy Center
Construction on the farm began in June 2007 by Alliant Wind Connect at a cost of $300 million. Testing began in 2008, and the farm was placed in service on May 19, 2008.[tpl]cite news |title=Johnsburg wind farm officially in operation |work=Tri-County News |publisher=Delta Publishing |location=Kiel, Wisconsin |page=18 |date=2008-05-22 |accessdate=2008-05-29[/tpl]

==Capacity==

The wind farm consists of 88 Vestas Wind Systems V82 turbines, which have a capacity to produce 145 megawatts of power. They are expected to generate 328 million kilowatt hours annually, which is enough to power 36,000 homes. Each turbine is capable of production 1.65 megawatts of electricity. The turbines are approximately [tpl]convert|262|ft|m[/tpl] tall, and reach [tpl]convert|397|ft|m[/tpl] tall when the [tpl]convert|134|ft|m|sing=on[/tpl] blade is faced upwards.
