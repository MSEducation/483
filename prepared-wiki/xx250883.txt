[[Those Lips, Those Eyes]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 films, 1980s romantic comedy films, American romantic comedy films, Films about actors, Films directed by Michael Pressman, Films set in Ohio, Films shot in Ohio, United Artists films

Those Lips, Those Eyes is a 1980 romantic comedy film directed by Michael Pressman, starring Frank Langella, Glynnis O'Connor, and Tom Hulce.
