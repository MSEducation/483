[[Chieko Akagi]]

CATEGORIES: 1966 births, Canoeists at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Japanese canoeists, Living people, Olympic canoeists of Japan, Asian Games medalists in canoeing, Canoeists at the 1990 Asian Games, Canoeists at the 1994 Asian Games

Chieko Akagi ([tpl]lang-ja|千恵子赤木[/tpl]; born December 19, 1966) is a Japanese sprint canoer who competed in the mid-1990s. At the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta, she was eliminated in the repechages of the K-2 500 m event.
