[[Unquillo]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Córdoba Province, Argentina

Unquillo is a city in the province of Córdoba, Argentina. It has 15,369 inhabitants as per the [tpl]census-ar|2001[/tpl]. It is located about 22 km north-northwest from the center of the provincial capital, Córdoba City, and 14 km east of Cosquín.
Unquillo is the birthplace of professional tennis player David Nalbandian.
