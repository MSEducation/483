[[Molly Kiely]]

CATEGORIES: 1969 births, American comics artists, Living people, Female comics artists, People from Bracebridge, Ontario

Molly Maud Kiely (born October 1969)Kiely entry, Lambiek's Comiclopedia. Accessed Mar. 6, 2014. is an American alternative cartoonist best known for erotica. Her work is published by Fantagraphics/Eros Comix.
Kiely was born in Bracebridge, Ontario, to parents from Saint Louis, Missouri, who left the United States in resistance to the Vietnam War. She was raised in Kitchener, and attended the University of Waterloo. She left Canada for San Francisco in 1992.
While still in Canada, Kiely became involved in 'zines and underground comic books.
Kiely now resides in Tucson, Arizona, is married, and has a child, Perla Fiore Kiely. (The child carries Molly Kiely's surname because of Kiely's feminist convictions.)[tpl]cn|date=March 2014[/tpl]
