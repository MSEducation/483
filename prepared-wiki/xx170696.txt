[[Stephanie Wittels]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, American voice actresses, Funimation voice actors, 1981 births

Stephanie Rose Wittels is an American voice actress who has been working for ADV Films, Funimation, and Seraphim Digital.  Wittels also is a teacher in the theater department at the High School for the Performing and Visual Arts in Houston, Texas.
