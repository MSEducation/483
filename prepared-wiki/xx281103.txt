[[What Turns You On?]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 albums, Japanese-language albums, CNBLUE albums, Warner Music Group albums

What Turns You On? (stylized What turns you on?) is a Japanese studio album by the South Korean rock band CNBLUE. It was released in August 28, 2013 as the group's fourth Japanese album[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oricon.co.jp/prof/artist/488826/products/music/1036453/1/|title=「Ｗｈａｔ　ｔｕｒｎｓ　ｙｏｕ　ｏｎ？」 ＣＮＢＬＵＥ / オリコン芸能人事典-ORICON STYLE|date=|work=|publisher=Oricon|accessdate=2013-08-09|language=Japanese[/tpl] and their second major album under Warner Music Japan.[tpl]cite web|url=http://wmg.jp/artist/cnblue/news_49895.html|title=JAPAN MAJOR 2nd ALBUM発売決定！！|date=2013-07-12|work=|publisher=Warner Music Group|accessdate=2013-08-09|language=Japanese[/tpl]

==Background==

The album was announced in July 12, on the group's official website, along with the first details about it. In July 23, jacket covers of all editions of the album were revealed, along with a promotional photo. Tracklisting of the album was revealed in July 31, containing, in total, eleven songs.[tpl]cite web|url=http://natalie.mu/music/news/96236|title=CNBLUE、シングルに続いてロックなニューアルバム発表|date=2013-07-31|work=|publisher=Natalie.mu|accessdate=2013-08-09|language=Japanese[/tpl]

==Composition==

The album comes with eleven songs, three singles, three songs already released on the singles and five new songs.
Most part of the songs included on the album were composed and written by the members Jung Yong-hwa and Lee Jong-hyun. Yong-hwa wrote and composed the songs "Lady", "One More Time", "Change", "Don't Care", "Greedy Man", "Robot" (both Japanese and English versions) and "Let Me Know", while Jong-hyun wrote and composed the songs "Blind Love", "Crying Out", "I Can't Believe" and "Starlit Night".

==Singles==

Three songs from the album were released as singles:
The first single, "Robot", was released in December 19, 2012. The song was originally written by the member Yong-hwa, with Japanese translation by Kosuke Oba. The physical single ranked number 2 on Oricon[tpl]'[/tpl]s singles chart, selling 42,563 in its first week and charted for seven weeks.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oricon.co.jp/prof/artist/488826/products/music/994847/1/|title=「Ｒｏｂｏｔ」 ＣＮＢＬＵＥ / オリコン芸能人事典-ORICON STYLE|date=|work=|publisher=Oricon|accessdate=2013-08-09|language=Japanese[/tpl] The song "Starlit Night", one of the b-sides included of the single, was included on the album as track #10. An English version of the song was included as bonus track of the limited edition type A.
The second single, "Blind Love", was released in April 24, 2013. The song was written by Jong-hyun and composed by Jong-hyun and Vinyl House. The physical single ranked number 4 on Oricon[tpl]'[/tpl]s singles chart, selling 47,577 copies in its first week, charting for nine weeks.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oricon.co.jp/prof/artist/488826/products/music/1015520/1/|title=「Ｂｌｉｎｄ　Ｌｏｖｅ」 ＣＮＢＬＵＥ / オリコン芸能人事典-ORICON STYLE|date=|work=|publisher=Oricon|accessdate=2013-08-09|language=Japanese[/tpl] The song "Greedy Man", one of the b-sides of the single, was included on the album as track #6.
The third and final single of the album, "Lady", was released on July 31, 2013. The song was written by Yong-hwa with Kosube Oba and composed by Yong-hwa and Park Hyun-woo. The physical single ranked at number 4 on Oricon[tpl]'[/tpl]s singles chart, selling 42,169 in its first week.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oricon.co.jp/prof/artist/488826/products/music/1031828/1/|title=「Ｌａｄｙ」 ＣＮＢＬＵＥ / オリコン芸能人事典-ORICON STYLE|date=|work=|publisher=Oricon|accessdate=2013-08-09|language=Japanese[/tpl] The song "Don't Care", first b-side of the single, was included on the album as track #5.
