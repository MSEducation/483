[[Brian Morton (canoeist)]]

CATEGORIES: 1970 births, Australian canoeists, Canoeists at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Canoeists at the 2000 Summer Olympics, Living people, Olympic canoeists of Australia

Brian Morton (born 1 January 1970) is an Australian sprint canoer who competed from the mid-1990s to the early 2000s (decade).
