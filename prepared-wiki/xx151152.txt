[[Mitchell Secondary College]]

CATEGORIES: High schools in Victoria (Australia), Public schools in Victoria (Australia)

Formally located on Thomas Mitchell Drive Wodonga, Victoria, Australia; Mitchell Secondary College now know as Wodonga Middle Years College specialised in educating student from multicultural diversities  from all over Australia. The Higher education school provided students with tailored learning curriculums suited to each students needs.  [tpl]Refimprove|date=November 2008[/tpl]Mitchell Secondary College was one of three public secondary schools educating years 7-12 in Wodonga, Victoria. In 2005-2006 it formally merged and pooled resources with the other two public secondary schools in Wodonga, Wodonga High School and Wodonga West Secondary College.http://www.dpc.vic.gov.au/domino/Web_Notes/newmedia.nsf/798c8b072d117a01ca256c8c0019bb01/52955dd4b7469c39ca2570900000d367!OpenDocument Mitchell and Wodonga West Secondary College became a combined college over two campuses, specialising in Years 7, 8 and 9, known as Wodonga Middle Years College, which is loosely associated with Wodonga Senior Secondary College. As of 2006, the former Mitchell facility has become Wodonga Middle Years College Huon Campus.
