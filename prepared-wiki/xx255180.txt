Asahi Shimbun. October 19, 2009. Retrieved on January 11, 2010. Its headquarters are in Tsu, Mie Prefecture."Corporate profile." Imuraya Confectionery. March 30, 2008. Retrieved on January 11, 2010. The company plans to sell Japanese confectionery to US consumers."Imuraya to expand into U.S., sell ice cream, Japan-style confectionery." Breitbart. March 3, 2009. Retrieved on January 11, 2010.
