[[The Boomin' System]]

CATEGORIES: 1990 singles, LL Cool J songs, Songs written by LL Cool J, Billboard Rap Songs number-one singles

"The Boomin' System" was the first single released from LL Cool J's fourth album, Mama Said Knock You Out.  It was released on August 2, 1990, for Def Jam Recordings, produced by Marley Marl and co-produced by LL Cool J.[tpl]cite web|url=[tpl]Allmusic|id=r28046|class=album|tab=credits|pure_url=yes[/tpl]|title=Album credits|publisher=Rovi Corporation|accessdate=2011-12-30[/tpl]  "The Boomin' System" was the first of the six singles released from the album and reached No. 48 on the Billboard Hot 100 and No. 1 on the Hot Rap Singles chart.[tpl]Cite web|url=[tpl]Allmusic|id=p95754|class=Artist|tab=charts-awards/billboard-singles|pure_url=yes[/tpl]|title=LL Cool J's Billboard singles history|publisher=Rovi Corporation / Billboard|accessdate=2011-12-30[/tpl] The song also contains a guitar sample from "The Payback" by James Brown.
