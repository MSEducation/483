[[Jerry Rollins]]

CATEGORIES: 1955 births, Birmingham Bulls players, Canadian ice hockey defencemen, Detroit Red Wings draft picks, Ice hockey people from British Columbia, Indianapolis Racers players, People from New Westminster, Philadelphia Firebirds (AHL) players, Phoenix Roadrunners (WHA) players, Toronto Toros draft picks, Toronto Toros players, Living people

Jerry Allan Rollins (born March 22, 1955 in New Westminster, British Columbia) is a retired WHA player. He played for the Toronto Toros, Birmingham Bulls, Phoenix Roadrunners, and the Indianapolis Racers.  Rollins is the son of former NHL, WHA and WHL player and coach, Al Rollins.
