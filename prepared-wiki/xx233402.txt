[[Bitnje, Bohinj]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Municipality of Bohinj

Bitnje ([tpl]IPA-sl|ˈbiːtnjɛ|pron[/tpl]) is a settlement on the left bank of the Sava Bohinjka River in the Municipality of Bohinj in the Upper Carniola region of Slovenia.

==Church==

The originally Gothic Medieval village church that has been redesigned in the Baroque style is dedicated to the Assumption of the Virgin Mary.[tpl]cite web |url=http://giskd2s.situla.org/rkd/Opis.asp?Esd=17 |title=17: Bitnje v Bohinju - Cerkev Marije vnebovzete |trans_title=17: Bitnje in Bohinj – Assumption of the Virgin Mary Church |work=Register nepremične kulturne dediščine of Immovable Cultural Heritage |language=Slovene |publisher=Ministry of Culture, Republic of Slovenia |accessdate=6 June 2012[/tpl] It features a beautiful Renaissance portal.Bohinj municipal site
