[[1996 Florida Marlins season]]

CATEGORIES: Miami Marlins seasons, 1996 Major League Baseball season

The 1996 Florida Marlins season began with the team trying to improve on their record from 1995. Their managers were Rene Lachemann, Cookie Rojas, and John Boles. They played home games at Joe Robbie Stadium. They finished with a record of 80-82, 3rd in the National League East.
