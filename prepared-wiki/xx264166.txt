[[Sid Bennett]]

CATEGORIES: People from Kane County, Illinois, Chicago Tigers players, Milwaukee Badgers players, Northwestern Wildcats football players, 1895 births, Year of death missing

Sydney Chisholm "Sid" Bennett (born 2 February 1895) was an American football player in the National Football League. He first played with the Chicago Tigers during the 1920 NFL season. After a year away from the NFL, he played with the Milwaukee Badgers during the 1922 NFL season.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/B/BennSi20.htm|title=Sid Bennett|publisher=Pro-Football-Reference.com|accessdate=2011-01-02[/tpl]
