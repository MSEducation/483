[[Euclid Kyurdzidis]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Russian actors, Pontic Greeks, Russian people of Greek descent

Euclid Kiriakovich Kyurdzidis, a.k.a. Euclid Gurdjieff (Gr. Ευκλείδης Κιουρτζίδης του Κυριάκου, b.22 February 1968 in Yessentuki) is a Russian actor, best known for his role as Ruslan Shamayev in Voyna. He was born to ethnic Greek parents in Yessentuki, Stavropol Krai. He has two brothers and an older sister.
