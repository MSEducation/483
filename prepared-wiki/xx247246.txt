[[Ábal]]

CATEGORIES: Masculine given names

Ábal ([tpl]IPA-fo|ˈɔabal|[/tpl]) is a faroese (Germanic ethnic group) masculine name. It is the faroese form of the name Abel. Its name-day is 2 January.
