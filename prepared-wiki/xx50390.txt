[[Eight Immortals from Sichuan]]

CATEGORIES: Cultural lists, Articles about multiple people, Sichuan

Eight Immortals from Sichuan ([tpl]zh|c=蜀中八仙|p=Shǔ zhōng bāxiān[/tpl]) are eight Sichuanese who supposedly became  xian ("immortals; transcendents; fairies"). The term is first used by Qiao Xiu (譙秀 qiáo xiù) in Record of Shu (《蜀紀》 shǔ zì) written in Jin Dynasty.
