[[WAFC (AM)]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Florida, Oldies radio stations in the United States

WAFC (590 AM) is an oldies radio station licensed to Clewiston, Florida, USA. The station serves the Clewiston area.  The station is currently owned by Glades Media Company Llp.
