Ousmane Berthé (born 5 February 1987) is a Malian footballer who currently plays for CS Constantine in the Algerian Ligue Professionnelle 1.

==Club career==

In July 2008, Berthé joined Jomo Cosmos in the South African Premier Soccer League.
