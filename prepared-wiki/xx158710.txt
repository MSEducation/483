[[Olivia Sanchez]]

CATEGORIES: 1982 births, Living people, French female tennis players, French people of Spanish descent, Sportspeople from Paris

Olivia Sanchez (born 17 November 1982 in Paris) is a professional tennis player from France.

==Biography==

She received a wildcard into the main draw of the 2007 French Open, but lost in 6–3, 4–6, 3–6, to Shenay Perry in the first round. As of 9 June 2008, Sanchez reached her highest singles ranking of World Number 90 on 9 June 2008, and is ranked 203rd in the WTA rankings. Her highest doubles ranking was World Number 464, of which she achieved on 12 August 2002. During her career, she has won 5 ITF singles titles and 1 ITF doubles title, but no singles or doubles titles on the WTA Tour.
