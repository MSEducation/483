[[HighEnd (magazine)]]

CATEGORIES: Indonesian magazines, Monthly magazines, Publications established in 2008, Media in Jakarta

HighEnd is an Indonesian lifestyle magazine based in Jakarta. It was first published in 2008. With tagline "First Class Lifestyle", HighEnd magazine caters to the affluent society with sophisticated look and content and regular exclusive events.
The magazine is published by PT. MNI Entertainment, a subsidiary of Indonesia's biggest media conglomerate Media Nusantara Citra.
