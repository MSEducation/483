[[Veterans Park (Charlotte, North Carolina)]]

CATEGORIES: Parks in Charlotte, North Carolina, Disc golf courses in Charlotte, North Carolina

Veterans Park is a nineteen acre urban park at 2136 Central Avenue in the Plaza-Midwood region of Charlotte, North Carolina.[tpl]cite web|url=http://charmeck.org/mecklenburg/county/parkandrec/parks/parksbyregion/centralregion/pages/veterans.aspx |title=Veterans |publisher=Charmeck.org |date= |accessdate=2013-08-17[/tpl] It features baseball fields, tennis courts, a basketball court, indoor and outdoor shelters, a playground, trails, and a sprayground for kids.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.charlotteobserver.com/2008/08/03/98728/kids-and-their-elders-keep-cool.html |title=Kids (and their elders) keep cool at spraygrounds |publisher=CharlotteObserver.com |date=2008-08-03 |accessdate=2013-08-17[/tpl]  
A disc golf course was built in Veterans Park in 1998.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.dgcoursereview.com/course.php?id=906 |title=Veterans Park in Charlotte, NC - Disc Golf Course Review |publisher=Dgcoursereview.com |date= |accessdate=2013-08-17[/tpl]
