[[1976 Wimbledon Championships – Men's Singles]]

CATEGORIES: Wimbledon Championship by year – Gentlemen's Singles, 1976 Wimbledon Championships

Björn Borg defeated Ilie Năstase 6–4, 6–2, 9–7 in the final to win the Gentlemen's Singles tennis title at the 1976 Wimbledon Championships.
