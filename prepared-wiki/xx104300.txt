[[Suncoast Lions Football Club]]

CATEGORIES: Sport in the Sunshine Coast, Queensland, Queensland State Football League clubs, Australian rules football clubs in Queensland

The Suncoast Lions Football Club is the former name of the reserve team of the Brisbane Lions Australian Football Club which competes in the Queensland State League Australian rules football competition.  The team plays home matches at the Brisbane Cricket Ground (as a curtain raiser game for Brisbane Lions matches) and, formerly, at the Fishermans Road football complex on the Sunshine Coast.
