[[Hesarlu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Miandoab County

Hesarlu ([tpl]lang-fa|حصارلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Ḩeşārlū)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3803627|Hesarlu[/tpl] is a village in Ajorluy-ye Sharqi Rural District, Baruq District, Miandoab County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 229, in 47 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|04[/tpl]
