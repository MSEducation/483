[[Sivan]]

CATEGORIES: Months of the Hebrew calendar

Sivan (Hebrew: סִיוָן, Standard Sivan Tiberian [tpl]Unicode|Sîwān[/tpl] ; from Akkadian [tpl]unicode|simānu[/tpl], meaning "Season; time") is the ninth month of the civil year and the third month of the ecclesiastical year on the Hebrew calendar. It is a spring month of 30 days. Sivan usually falls in May–June on the Gregorian calendar.

==Holidays in Sivan==

6 Sivan - Shavuot
