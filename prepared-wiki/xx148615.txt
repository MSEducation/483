[[Divandu albimarginatus]]

CATEGORIES: Pseudocrenilabrinae, Fish of Africa, Freshwater fish of Africa, Monotypic fish genera

Divandu albimarginatus is a species of cichlid native to the Republic of Congo and Gabon in Africa.  This species reaches a length of [tpl]convert|12.1|cm|in[/tpl] SL.  It is currently the only known member of its genus.[tpl]FishBase species |genus= Divandu|species= albimarginatus| month = February | year = 2013[/tpl]
