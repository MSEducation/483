[[Osman II of the Maldives]]

CATEGORIES: Sultans of the Maldives, 15th-century monarchs in Asia

Sultan Osman II Sri dhanmaru loka mahaa radun was the Sultan of Maldives from 1420 to 1421. He was the son of His Highness Sultan Hassan I and also a brother of HH Sultan Ibraahim I. Sultan Osman is famous for his kindness to his subjects.[tpl]Fact|date=February 2009[/tpl]
