[[Shag Rock (Antarctica)]]

CATEGORIES: Rock formations in Graham Land, Graham Coast

Shag Rock ([tpl]Coord|66|0|S|65|38|W||display=inline,title[/tpl]) is a rock 0.1 miles (0.2 km) east of Cliff Island and 8 miles (13 km) west of Prospect Point, off the west coast of Graham Land. Charted and named by the British Graham Land Expedition (BGLE), 1934–37, under Rymill.
