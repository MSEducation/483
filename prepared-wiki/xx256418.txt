[[PS Duke of Connaught]]

CATEGORIES: 1875 ships, Passenger ships of the United Kingdom, Steamships, Barrow-built ships, Ships of the Lancashire and Yorkshire Railway, Ships of the London and North Western Railway

PS Duke of Connaught was a paddle steamer passenger vessel operated by the London and North Western Railway and the Lancashire and Yorkshire Railway from 1875 to 1893.Railway and Other Steamers, Duckworth. 1962
