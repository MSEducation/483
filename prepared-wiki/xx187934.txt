[[Neal, Kansas]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Greenwood County, Kansas, Unincorporated communities in Kansas

Neal is an unincorporated community in eastern Greenwood County, Kansas, United States.  It lies along U.S. Route 54 east of the city of Eureka, the county seat of Greenwood County.Rand McNally.
