[[Rudy Hobbs]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Members of the Michigan House of Representatives, Michigan Democrats, American Christians

Rudy Hobbs is an American politician from the state of Michigan. He is a member of Michigan House of Representatives and the Democratic Party. 
Hobbs is from the east side of Detroit. He later moved to Southfield, Michigan. He taught first grade in the Detroit Public School System. He began to volunteer in politics, and was hired by Sander Levin's office.
