[[Alice Long]]

CATEGORIES: 1967 songs, Songs written by Boyce and Hart

Alice Long (You're Still My Favorite Girlfriend) was written, produced & sung by Tommy Boyce & Bobby Harthttp://www.tommyboyce.com/http://www.bobbyhart.net/ in 1968. The song was arranged by Artie Butler and reached #16 on the Cash Box chart and #27 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart.
