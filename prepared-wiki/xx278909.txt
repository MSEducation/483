[[United States Senate election in Idaho, 1990]]

CATEGORIES: Idaho elections, 1990, United States Senate elections, 1990, United States Senate elections in Idaho

The 1990 United States Senate election in Idaho was held on November 6, 1990. Republican Rep. Larry Craig defeated Democratic former state legislator Ron Twilegar for the seat of Senator Jim McClure, who did not seek reelection.
