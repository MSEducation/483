[[Caitlin Wachs]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 births, American child actresses, American film actresses, American soap opera actresses, American television actresses, Living people, Actresses from Eugene, Oregon, 20th-century American actresses, 21st-century American actresses

Caitlin Elizabeth Wachs (pronounced wax; born March 15, 1989) is an American actress who has acted in several films and TV series. She appeared alongside Ally Walker and Robert Davi in Profiler, and played the president's daughter, Rebecca Calloway, on the series Commander in Chief.Caitlin Wachs: Daughter in Chief, Film Reference  
Wachs was born in Eugene, Oregon. Her parents are Patrice Wachs, a graphic designer, and Allan Wachs, a producer of music videos.Caitlin Wachs Biography (1989-), Film Reference
