[[Ommabad, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Ommabad ([tpl]lang-fa|ام اباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Ommābād and Amābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3771551|Ommabad[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Pain Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 17, in 10 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
