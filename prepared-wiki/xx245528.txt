[[Lake Aktaş]]

CATEGORIES: Lakes of Georgia (country), Lakes of Turkey, International lakes of Europe, International lakes of Asia, Georgia (country)–Turkey border

Lake Aktaş ([tpl]lang-tr|Aktaş Gölü[/tpl]), Lake Khozapini ([tpl]lang-ka|ხოზაფინის ტბა[/tpl], khozap'inis tba; Turkish: Hazapin Gölü), or Kartsakhi Lake (Georgian: კარწახის ტბა, karts'akhis tba) is a lake the Caucasus Mountains. It sits on the international border between Georgia and Turkey.
