[[William W. McDonald]]

CATEGORIES: American ranchers, American conservationists, MacArthur Fellows, Arizona State University alumni, Living people

William W. McDonald is an American rancher, and conservationist. He is executive director of the Malpai Borderlands Group.http://www.terrain.org/essays/18/sayre.htm
He was a 1998 MacArthur Fellow.

==Life==

He graduated from Arizona State University. His Sycamore Ranch is in Arizona.http://www.swwings.org/staff.html
