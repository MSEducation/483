[[Centrostephanus coronatus]]

CATEGORIES: Echinoidea, Animals described in 1867

Centrostephanus coronatus, as known as Crowned Sea Urchin, is a sea urchin in the family Diadematidae. It lives in holes and crevices is shallow rocky waters. Individuals forage within [tpl]convert|1|m[/tpl] of their shelters, to which they return at night .[tpl]cite journal |author=B. V. Nelson & R. R.
