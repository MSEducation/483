[[Associação Maga Esporte Clube]]

CATEGORIES: Association football clubs established in 1993, Football clubs in Santa Catarina (state), 1993 establishments in Brazil

Associação Maga Esporte Clube. commonly known as Maga, is a Brazilian football club based in Indaial, a city in Santa Catarina. It currently plays at the third level of its state's league system. The club's colors are red and white.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.timesdelbrasil.com.br/SC/Associação%20Maga%20EC-indaial-sc-htm|title=Maga|publisher=Times del Brasil|accessdate=January 21, 2011|language=Portuguese[/tpl]

==History==

The club was founded on May 12, 1993.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.esportesnaweb.com/equipe.asp?id=3219|title=Associação Maga Esporte Clube|publisher=Esportes na Web|accessdate=January 21, 2011|language=Portuguese[/tpl] The club competed in the Campeonato Catarinese Third Level in 2010, finishing in the last position in the competition.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.rsssfbrasil.com/tablesrz/sc2010l3.htm|title=Santa Catarina 2010 - Third Level|publisher=RSSSF|date=December 19, 2010|accessdate=January 21, 2011[/tpl] The club received two instalment from BVI company Stadium Management in 2011.http://www.bcb.gov.br/rex/registrosCE/2011/rce022011.pdfhttp://www.bcb.gov.br/rex/registrosCE/2011/RCE201108.pdf
