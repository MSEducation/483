[[WCRS-LP]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Columbus, Ohio, Community radio stations in the United States, Low-power FM radio stations in Ohio, Radio stations established in 2007

WCRS-LP is a North American community radio station in the Columbus, Ohio, area. It broadcasts from 3pm-3am seven days a week, sharing the frequency with WCRX-LP, and has an on-line stream available 24/7 at wcrsfm.org. WCRS-LP broadcasts on 102.1 and on translator station W252AY 98.3 FM in most of Franklin County, licensed to Marble Cliff. WCRS is owned by The Neighborhood Network and is affiliated to the Pacifica Radio Network. 
The station is a not-for-profit, volunteer, non-commercial community radio station.[tpl]cite news |first=Jennifer |last=Hambrick |work=Short North Gazette |title=Columbus Community Radio Takes Off in '08 |url=http://www.shortnorth.com/WCRSRadio.html |date=January 2008[/tpl]
The station was assigned the WCRS-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on March 30, 2006.[tpl]cite web |title=Call Sign History |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=132329&Callsign=WCRS-LP |work=FCC Media Bureau CDBS Public Access Database[/tpl]
Among the programs on WCRS are Democracy Now!, Free Speech Radio News, and English, French, Somali, and Spanish-language public affairs programs.[tpl]cite news |first=Eric |last=Pacella |work=The Lantern |title=Local radio station a Columbus niche |url=http://media.www.thelantern.com/media/storage/paper333/news/2009/03/12/Arts/Local.Radio.Station.A.Columbus.Niche-3669139.shtml |date=March 12, 2009[/tpl]
