[[Diego Cánepa (canoeist)]]

CATEGORIES: 1976 births, Argentine canoeists, Canoeists at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Living people, Olympic canoeists of Argentina

Diego Cánepa (born May 31, 1976) is a Belgian sprint canoer who has competed in the mid-1990s. At the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta, he was eliminated in the semifinals of the K-2 500 m event.
