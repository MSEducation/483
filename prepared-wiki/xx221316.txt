[[Football Under Cover]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 films, German documentary films, Iranian documentary films, English-language films, German films, German-language films, Persian-language films, 2000s documentary films, Documentary films about association football, Documentary films about women's sports

Football Under Cover is a 2008 German/Iranian documentary film directed by David Assmann and Ayat Najafi. It follows the attempts of German Marlene Assmann and Iranian Ayat Najafi to organise a football match between Assman's team BSV Aldersimspor and the Iranian national woman's team.[ref]
</ref>

==Reception==

At the 2008 Berlin International Film Festival, Football Under Cover won the Teddy Award for Best Documentary Film and the Teddy Audience Award.
