Plaza Servicios Aéreos was a passenger and domestic cargo airline based in Zaragoza, Spain.[tpl]cite news |first= |last= |authorlink= |coauthors= |title=CH_Aviation |url= http://www.ch-aviation.ch/airlinepage.php?code1=PLC |publisher= |accessdate=2008-05-24 [/tpl]

==History==

The airline was established in 2006 and was wholly owned by Swiftair. It ceased operations in 2008.
