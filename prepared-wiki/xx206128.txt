[[10626 Zajíc]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Astronomical objects discovered in 1998

10626 Zajíc is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1501.3959229 days (4.11 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=10626 | title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-05-24 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on January 10, 1998 by L. Sarounová at Ondrejov.  It is named after Jan Zajíc (b. 1910), founder and long-time director of the Vlasim observatory in the Czech Republic.
