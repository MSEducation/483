[[Gmina Lasowice Wielkie]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Opole Voivodeship, Kluczbork County

Gmina Lasowice Wielkie, German Gemeinde Gross Lassowitz is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Kluczbork County, Opole Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Its seat is the village of Lasowice Wielkie, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|13|km|mi|0[/tpl] south of Kluczbork and [tpl]convert|31|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north-east of the regional capital Opole.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|210.84|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 7,192. Since 2006 the commune, like much of the area, has been bilingual in German and Polish.
The gmina contains part of the protected area called Stobrawa Landscape Park.
