[[Sidi Bel Abbès Province]]

CATEGORIES: Sidi Bel Abbès Province, Provinces of Algeria

Sidi Bel Abbès ([tpl]lang-ar|ولاية سيدي بلعباس[/tpl]) is one of the provinces (wilayas) of Algeria. Its name is derived from the name of its capital, the city of Sidi Bel Abbès. It is situated in the northwestern part of the country.

==Administrative divisions==

The province is divided into 15 districts (daïras), which are further divided into 52 communes or municipalities.
