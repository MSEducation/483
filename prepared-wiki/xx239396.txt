[[Everblack Industries]]

CATEGORIES: American record labels

Everblack Industries is the record imprint created by American heavy metal band Shadows Fall in conjunction with Warner Music Group's ILG, Ferret Music and ChannelZERO Entertainment. The label's first release was Shadows Fall's album Retribution on September 15, 2009."Everblack Industries". Tartarean Desire. Retrieved on March 31, 2012.
