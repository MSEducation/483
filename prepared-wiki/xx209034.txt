[[Nimbus Mono L]]

CATEGORIES: Monospaced typefaces, Open-source typefaces, 1984 introductions

Although the characters are not exactly the same, Nimbus Mono has metrics that are very similar to Courier and Courier New. 
It is one of the Ghostscript fonts, free alternatives to 35 basic PostScript fonts (which include Courier).
