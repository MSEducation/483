[[Sookmyung Girls' High School]]

CATEGORIES: Education in Seoul, High schools in South Korea, Girls' schools in South Korea, Educational institutions established in 1905, Gangnam District

Sookmyung Girls' High School is a private girls' school located in Dogok-dong, Gangnam-gu, Seoul, South Korea. It was founded in April 1905 under the name Myeongsin Girls' School (hangul: 명신여학교; hanja: 明新女學校), and was originally located in Yongdonggung palace.
