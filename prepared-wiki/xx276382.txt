[[Lizardo Rodríguez Nue]]

Lizardo Rodríguez Nue (born 30 August 1910, date of death unknown) was a Peruvian footballer who played for Peru at the 1930 FIFA World Cup.1930 FIFA World Cup Uruguay He also played for Sport Progreso.
