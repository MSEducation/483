[[1966 in film]]

CATEGORIES: Years in film, 1966 in film

The year 1966 in film involved some significant events.

==Events==

Entertainment pioneer Walt Disney, best known for his creation of Mickey Mouse, breakthroughs in the field of animation and theme park design and other extraordinary achievements, died on 15 December 1966 at the age of sixty-six of acute circulatory collapse following a diagnosis of, and surgery for, lung cancer.  He had been a smoker since the 1940s. He died while he was producing The Jungle Book, The Happiest Millionaire, and Winnie the Pooh and the Blustery Day; the last three films under his personal supervision.
