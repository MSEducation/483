[[Behrud]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ordubad District

Behrud ([tpl]lang-hy|Բողրութ, Boghrut[/tpl]; also, Bakhrud and Begryut) is a village and municipality in the Ordubad Rayon of Nakhchivan, Azerbaijan.  It has a population of 520. 

==History==

It was located on trade routes and prospered in Middle-Ages. Currently only the ruins of a church and a bridge has remained.
