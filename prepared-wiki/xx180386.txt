[[Shark Peak]]

CATEGORIES: Nunataks of Mac. Robertson Land

Shark Peak ([tpl]Coord|68|3|S|62|41|E||display=inline,title[/tpl]) is an isolated nunatak 3.5 miles (6 km) south-southwest of Van Hulssen Nunatak in the Framnes Mountains of Mac. Robertson Land. Mapped by Norwegian cartographers from air photos taken by the Lars Christensen Expedition, 1936–37, and named Hanuten (the shark peak). The translated form of the name recommended by Antarctic Names Committee of Australia (ANCA) has been adopted.
