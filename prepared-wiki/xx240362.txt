[[Arūnas Bižokas]]

CATEGORIES: Ballroom dancers, Lithuanian dancers, Living people

Arūnas Bižokas is a professional ballroom dancer, representing USA.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.dancesportinfo.net/Couple/Arunas_Bizokas_and_Katusha_Demidova_78324/Details |title=Arunas Bizokas (Lithuania) & Katusha Demidova (USA) |accessdate=2013-09-29[/tpl] He was born in Vilnius, Lithuania.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.arunasandkatusha.com/about-us |title=About Us |year=2011[/tpl] His current partner is Katusha Demidova. As a couple they have won the 2009, 2010, 2011, 2012 and 2013 Professional World Ballroom Dance Championships in Standard Ballroom.[tpl]cite web |publisher=Dancebeat World |url=http://www.dancebeatworld.com/members.php |accessdate=2013-09-29 |title=Doing a "Katusha"[/tpl]  In 2013, Bižokas and his partner were ranked number one on the World Dance Council world ranking in the Professional Ballroom category.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.wdcdance.com/index.php?set_ActivMenu=163 |title=WDC World Ranking – Professional Ballroom |publisher=World Dance Council |date=September 2013[/tpl]
His previous partner was Edita Daniute of Lithuania.
