[[Chatsmore Catholic High School]]

CATEGORIES: Comprehensive schools in West Sussex, Arts Colleges in England, Worthing, Roman Catholic secondary schools in the Diocese of Arundel and Brighton

Chatsmore Catholic High School is an 11–16, mixed comprehensive school located in Goring By Sea, Worthing, West Sussex. The school holds specialist Arts College status.
The head teacher is Mr Michael Madden, NPQH. As of 2013, 632 students are enrolled.[tpl]Citation needed|date=July 2010[/tpl]

==Facilities==

Chatsmore has a canteen, the Learning Resource Centre (library) more commonly known as the LRC by the pupils, a food technology room, an Apple suite, which has many iMacs and Macbooks inside for more creative lessons, a wood tech room, graphics rooms, a drama department, an average-sized[tpl]What|date=February 2013[/tpl] gym, a large sports hall, tennis courts, football pitch and a very large[tpl]What|date=February 2013[/tpl] field.
