[[Zhuxinzhuang Station]]

Zhuxinzhuang Station is a station on the Changping Line of the Beijing Subway. It is a cross-platform interchange with Line 8, which extended to here by the end of 2013.[tpl]cite web|title=朱辛庄|url=http://www.beijing.cn/rcpage/ditie/page/100740.shtml|language=Chinese|publisher=Beijing Municipal Government|accessdate=12 October 2012[/tpl] The Line 8 tracks are the inner tracks, while the Changping Line tracks are the outer tracks. It is the current northern terminus of Line 8.
