[[Santo Tirso Municipality]]

CATEGORIES: Cities in Portugal, Municipalities of Porto District

Santo Tirso ([tpl]IPA-pt|ˈsɐ̃tu ˈtiɾsu[/tpl]) is a city and municipality located in the north of Porto Metropolitan Area, Porto District, Portugal. In the region, the Ave Valley, there is a large center of textile industry.
The city population is 14000 (last 2001 census), the urban area of the city with 8 parishes has 28407. Total municipality (Concelho) population is 71623 in 24 parishes.
Another important center in the municipality is Vila das Aves.
