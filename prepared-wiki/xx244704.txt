[[Hillside Methodist Church]]

CATEGORIES: Methodist churches in New York, Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Religious buildings completed in 1855, 19th-century Methodist church buildings, Churches in Dutchess County, New York

Hillside Methodist Church is a historic Methodist church on US 9 in Rhinebeck, Dutchess County, New York. It was built about 1855 and is a small, one story, rectangular stone building in a rural, picturesque style. It features elaborate scroll-sawn bargeboards and a steeply pitched gable roof.  It has an open-frame bell tower.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=7052|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration:Hillside Methodist Church|date=March 1987|accessdate=2011-01-08 |author=Nancy Todd, Neil Larson|publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation[/tpl]
It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1987.
