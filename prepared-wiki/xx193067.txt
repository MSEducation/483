[[Fernand Jaccard]]

CATEGORIES: 1907 births, 2008 deaths, Swiss centenarians, Swiss footballers, Switzerland international footballers, 1934 FIFA World Cup players, Swiss football managers, FC Basel managers, FC Basel players, Servette FC managers, Neuchâtel Xamax managers, FC Lausanne-Sport managers, FC Chiasso managers, FC Locarno managers

Fernand Alfred Jaccard (8 October 1907 – 15 April 2008) was a Swiss football midfielder and manager. He was born in La Chaux-de-Fonds and died in Lutry.

==Career==

Jaccard played in the 1934 FIFA World Cup.[tpl]FIFA player|51368[/tpl][tpl]cite web | url = http://www.tagesanzeiger.ch/dyn/news/fussball/861915.html | title = Obituary at Tages Anzeiger | language = German[/tpl][tpl]cite news|title = Fernand Jaccard s'est éteint|publisher = 20 Minutes|date = 16 April 2008 |url=http://www.20min.ch/ro/sports/football/story/27078764|accessdate = 29 April 2008 | language = French[/tpl][tpl]cite web | url = http://www.hommages.ch/detail/6180__Fernand_Jaccard | title = Notice of death at Hommages | language = French[/tpl] He went on to manage FC Basel from 1937 to 1939 as player manager. After retiring as a player, he coached FC Locarno, Servette FC, Cantonal Neuchâtel FC, FC Chiasso and FC Lausanne-Sport.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.rsssf.com/players/trainers-zwit-clubs.html | title = Switzerland – Trainers of First and Second Division Clubs | publisher = RSSF[/tpl]
