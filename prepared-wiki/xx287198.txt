[[Román Gastaldi]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 births, Living people, Argentine decathletes, People from Buenos Aires, Athletes (track and field) at the 2011 Pan American Games

Román Andrés Gastaldi (born 25 September 1989 in Villa Devoto, Buenos Aires) is a Argentine decathlete. He competed at the 2011 Pan American Games in Guadalajara, Mexico and 2013 World Championships in Moscow, Russia.
