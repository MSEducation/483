James Arthur Robinson (born 1932) is an American academic who in 1974-87 served as the second president of the University of West Florida (UWF).[tpl]Cite web|title=about UWF|publisher=University of West Florida|location=Pensacola, Florida|url=http://www.uwf.edu/uwfmain/about.cfm|archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20040405123928/http://www.uwf.edu/uwfMain/about.cfm|archivedate=5 April 2004|deadurl=no[/tpl]
Robinson received his doctorate in political science from Northwestern University in 1958.
