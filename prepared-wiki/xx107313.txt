[[Zuzanna Szwed]]

CATEGORIES: Figure skaters at the 1992 Winter Olympics, Olympic figure skaters of Poland, Polish female single skaters, 1977 births, Living people, Sportspeople from Warsaw

Zuzanna Szwed (born July 19, 1977 in Warsaw, Poland) is a Polish retired figure skater.  She was the first woman from her country to compete in the Olympics, and she did it in the 1992 Winter Olympics and is a multiple Polish national champion.
After turning pro, she began coaching at the Detroit Skating Club in Detroit, Michigan. She married Aaron Parchem in July, 2004. In addition to coaching, she also works as a choreographer.
