[[Hydrophylax]]

CATEGORIES: Rubiaceae, Monotypic plant genera, Flora of India, Flora of Sri Lanka, Flora of Thailand

Hydrophylax is a monotypic genus of flowering plants in the Rubiaceae family.[tpl]ITIS|id=35170 |taxon=Hydrophylax[/tpl] It was described by Carl Linnaeus the Younger in 1782. The genus contains only one species,  Hydrophylax maritima, which is found in India, Sri Lanka and Thailand.[tpl]cite web |url=http://apps.kew.org/wcsp/qsearch.do?plantName=Hydrophylax |title=Hydrophylax maritima L.f., Suppl.
