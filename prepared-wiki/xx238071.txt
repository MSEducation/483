[[KFAS-LP]]

CATEGORIES: Low-power FM radio stations in Montana, Radio stations in Montana

KFAS-LP (95.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to Shelby, Montana, USA.  The station is currently owned by Shelby Educational Association.
