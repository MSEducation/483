[[Trinity Evangelical Lutheran Church (Abilene, Kansas)]]

CATEGORIES: Lutheran churches in Kansas, Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in Kansas, Buildings and structures in Dickinson County, Kansas

Trinity Evangelical Lutheran Church is a church in Abilene, Kansas affiliated with the Evangelical Lutheran Church in America (ELCA). It is listed on the National Register of Historic Places.
