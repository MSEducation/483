[[Elena Quinteros]]

CATEGORIES: 1945 births, 1976 deaths, Uruguayan murder victims, Uruguayan torture victims

Elena Quinteros (Montevideo, Uruguay, on September 9, 1945 - missing from 1976), school teacher arrested and killed during the civic - military Uruguayan dictatorship.
On June 26, 1976, Elena Quinteros is detained and taken to the center of tortures " 300 Carlos " of the Division of Army I.
She tried to escape asking for help in the Venezuelan embassy, but she was taken by force.
A few days later she was tortured and killed.
