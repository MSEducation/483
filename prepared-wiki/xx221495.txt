[[Karen White]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, People from Tulsa, Oklahoma, American romantic fiction writers, Tulane University alumni, Writers from Georgia (U.S. state)

Karen White is an American author of more than fifteen  fiction  novels.

==Life and career==

White was born in Tulsa, Oklahoma and during her childhood lived in numerous states and also in Venezula and London, England, where she graduated from The American School in London.[tpl]citation needed|date=January 2013[/tpl]  She attended college at Tulane University in New Orleans, Louisiana, where she obtained a Bachelor of Science in Management.[tpl]cite web|title=About Me |url=http://www.karen-white.com/about.shtml |publisher=Karen-White.com |accessdate=2010-06-04[/tpl]
Her first book, In the Shadow of the Moon was a double finalist for the Romance Writers of America RITA Award.[tpl]cite web|date=May 16, 2002 |title=2001 RITA Winners |url=http://www.deewhybooks.com.au/link/2001%20RITA%20Winners.htm |publisher=Dee Why Books |accessdate=2010-06-04[/tpl] The Girl on Legare Street hit The New York Times Best Seller list in November 2009,[tpl]cite news|date=November 12, 2009 |title=Paperback Trade Fiction |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2009/11/22/books/bestseller/bestpapertradefiction.html?_r=1&scp=5&sq=%22Karen%20White%22&st=cse |work=The New York Times |accessdate=2010-06-04[/tpl][tpl]cite web|date=February 2, 2010 |title=Lunch with ‘The Girl on Legare Street’ Author  |url=http://www.lcweekly.com/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=1476:lunch-with-the-girl-on-legare-street-author&catid=24&Itemid=109 |work=Lowcountry Weekly |accessdate=2010-06-04[/tpl] and On Folly Beach in May 2010, which was also a NYT bestseller.[tpl]cite web|date=May 21, 2010 |title=Paperback Trade Fiction|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2010/05/30/books/bestseller/bestpapertradefiction.html?scp=1&sq=%22Karen%20White%22&st=cse |work=The New York Times |accessdate=2010-06-04[/tpl] Most of White's novels are based in the low-country of the southeastern United States. White currently has 15 novels published with her current book, "The Time Between" hitting the shelves in June 2013 and debuting on the NYT Bestseller list at number 25 for Hardcover fiction.
White is married with two children. White is currently published by a division of Penguin Books. She is currently contracted with them for four more books.[tpl]citation needed|date=January 2013[/tpl]
