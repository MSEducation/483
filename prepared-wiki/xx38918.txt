[[Holsbeek]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Flemish Brabant

Holsbeek ([tpl]IPA-nl|ˈɦɔl̪z̪beːk[/tpl]) is a municipality located in the Belgian province of Flemish Brabant. The municipality comprises the towns of Holsbeek proper, [tpl]Ill|nl|Kortrijk-Dutsel[/tpl], [tpl]Ill|nl|Nieuwrode[/tpl] and Sint-Pieters-Rode. On January 1, 2006 Holsbeek had a total population of 9,205. The total area is 38.50 km² which gives a population density of 239 inhabitants per km².
Most known historical landmark is the medieval castle of Horst.
