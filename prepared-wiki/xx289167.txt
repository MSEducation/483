[[Tuturqan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Tuturqan ([tpl]lang-fa|توتورقان[/tpl], also Romanized as Tūtūrqān; also known as Tūtūrqānlu)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3087905|Tuturqan[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 445, in 84 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
