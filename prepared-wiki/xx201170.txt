[[Michael Poghosyan]]

CATEGORIES: Armenian film actors, People from Yerevan, Living people, 1965 births, Laureates of the Prince Claus Award

Michael Movsesi Poghosyan (sometimes credited as Mikael Pogosyan, Michael Poghosian, Mikael Poghosyan, [tpl]lang-hy|Միքայել Պողոսյան[/tpl], born 31 May 1956, Yerevan, Armenian SSR) is an Armenian film and theatre actor and judge on the Armenian version of Pop Idol.

==Professional practice==

In 1978 he was graduated from the Acting Department of Yerevan Fine Arts and Theatre Institute, 1976-91 - worked in the Yerevan Chamber Theatre, 1978-92 - actor of Armenfilm Studio, 1992-94 - worked in the Hamazgayin Yerevan Drama Theatre.Mikael Pogosyan - Biography
