[[Movies4Men 2]]

CATEGORIES: Movie channels in the United Kingdom, Sony Pictures Television, Defunct British television channels, Television channels and stations disestablished in 2012, Television channels and stations established in 2006

Movies4Men 2 was a satellite television film channel in the United Kingdom and Ireland, available as a free-to-air service on Freesat, Sky and as in the basic package on Smallworld Cable. Prior to October 9, 2006, Movies4Men 2 was previously known as ACTIONMAX. Between November 2011 and January 2012, Movies4Men 2 and Movies4Men 2 +1 were also available on Freeview in Manchester.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.digitalspy.co.uk/digitaltv/news/a319733/first-local-freeview-carriage-deal-agreed.html|title=First local Freeview carriage deal agreed|publisher=Digital Spy|date=16 May 2011[/tpl]
Different from Movies4Men; the channel was aimed at the young to middle aged male viewer, focusing mainly on action and thriller film genres. As of September 2008, the channels transmuted films in 16:9 widescreen where available. Movies4Men 2 +1 launched on Sky 326 on Monday October 27, 2008.
The channel closed on May 2, 2012 ahead of the launch of Sony Movie Channel and Sony Movie Channel +1 the following day.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.facebook.com/sonytvuk/posts/317176308351154|title=Hi guys, for...|publisher=Facebook|date=30 April 2012[/tpl]
