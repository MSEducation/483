[[Vakil Qeshlaq]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Vakil Qeshlaq ([tpl]lang-fa|وكيل قشلاق[/tpl], also Romanized as Vakīl Qeshlāq; also known as Qishlaqs, Tāzeh Qeshlāq, Vakil-Kishlaki, Vakīl Qeshlāqī, and Vakīl Qeshlāqī)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3088020|Vakil Qeshlaq[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 32, in 5 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
