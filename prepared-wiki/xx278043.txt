[[Jegurupadu]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in East Godavari district, Mandals in East Godavari district

Jegurupadu ([tpl]Lang-te|జేగురుపాడు[/tpl]) is a village near mandal headquarters Kadiam, Rajahmundry in East Godavari district of Andhra Pradesh, India.
