[[Boonton Electronics]]

CATEGORIES: Companies based in Morris County, New Jersey, Morris Plains, New Jersey

Boonton Electronics is an American manufacturer of electronic test equipment. Originally founded in 1947 in Morris Plains, New Jersey, they are now located nearby in Parsippany. Boonton became a wholly owned subsidiary of the Wireless Telecom Group in 2000. 
Boonton is well known as a manufacturer of wideband power meters, having developed the first power sensors using full-wave rectification and the first two-channel power meter.
