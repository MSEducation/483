[[Oraba]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Uganda, South Sudan–Uganda border crossings, Koboko District, Northern Region, Uganda

Oraba is a town in Uganda.

==Location==

The town is located in the northwestern corner of Uganda, close to the International borders with the Republic of South Sudan and the Democratic Republic of the Congo (DRC). It is located a few kilometres (miles), northeast of the precise South Sudan–Uganda–DRC tripoint. Oraba lies in Koboko District, West Nile sub-region, in Northern Uganda. It is situated directly across the border from the city of Kaya, South Sudan.

==Overview==

Oraba is a small border town in Koboko District, West Nile sub-region, in Northern Uganda. It sits directly across the border from Kaya, South Sudan, which by looking at the satellite map, is about 10 times bigger. There are plans in the pipeline to tarmac the road from Vura through Arua and Koboko to Oraba, to ease the flow of people and goods through this busy trade corridor.
