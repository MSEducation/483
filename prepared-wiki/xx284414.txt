Aleksandr Stanislavovich Rudenko ([tpl]lang-ru|Александр Станиславович Руденко[/tpl]; born March 4, 1993) is a Russian football goalkeeper, who currently playing for FC Chernomorets Novorossiysk.
He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Chernomorets Novorossiysk on August 4, 2013 in a game against FC Olimpia Volgograd.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Russian Football Union|url=http://rfspro.ru/cgi-bin/player/script2.cgi?par1=117949|title=Career Summary|date=11 August 2013[/tpl]
