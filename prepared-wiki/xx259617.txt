[[Akis Agiomamitis]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Cypriot footballers, Cyprus international footballers, OFI Crete players, Apollon Limassol FC players, Expatriate footballers in Greece, Cypriot expatriate footballers, Cypriot football managers, Aris Limassol managers

Akis Agiomamitis is a retired Cypriot international footballer who played as a defender for OFI Crete and is now a football manager.

==Club career==

Agiomamitis moved to Greece in July 1979, joining Greek first division side OFI Crete.[tpl]cite web|publisher=RSSSF|author=Mastrogiannopoulos, Alexander|title=Greece 1979/80|url=http://www.rsssf.com/tablesg/grk80.html|date=24 April 2003[/tpl] He would stay with OFI for seven seasons in the Greek first division.[tpl]cite web|publisher=RSSSF|author=Mastrogiannopoulos, Alexander|title=Foreign Players in Greece since 1959/60|url=http://www.rsssf.com/players/foreign-players-in-grk6080.html|date=24 April 2003[/tpl]

==International career==

Agiomamitis made one appearance for the Cyprus national football team during 1987.[tpl]cite web|publisher=RSSSF|author=Cazal, Jean-Michel|title=International Matches 1987 - Intercontinental|url=http://www.rsssf.com/intldetails/1987ic.html|date=2 February 2005[/tpl]

==Managerial career==

After he retired from playing, Agiomamitis become a manager. He led Aris Limassol F.C. in 2008[tpl]cite web|publisher=sport.gr|title=Ο Αγιομαμίτης στο τιμόνι του Αρη Λεμεσού|url=http://www.sport.gr/default.asp?pid=4&aid=9524|language=Greek|date=7 April 2008[/tpl] and 2009, before managing South African club Mpumalanga Black Aces F.C. in 2009[tpl]cite web|publisher=politis-sports.com|author=Christofi, Christoforos|title=ΠΡΟΚΛΗΣΗ ΓΙΑ ΤΟΝ ΚΥΠΡΙΟ ΠΡΟΠΟΝΗΤΗ: Ο Αγιομαμίτης στους Black Aces!|url=http://www.politis-sports.com/cgibin/hweb?-A=56097&-V=onlinenews|language=Greek|date=11 November 2009[/tpl] and 2010.[tpl]cite web|publisher=absapremiership.co.za|title=Aces look to end losing streak|url=http://absapremiership.ensight.co.za/live/content.php?Item_ID=3565|date=13 October 2010[/tpl]
