[[Xurs]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ordubad District, Populated places in Azerbaijan

Xurs ([tpl]Lang-hy|Խուրս[/tpl], [tpl]Lang-ru|Хурс[/tpl])(also, Khurs and Khurst) is a village and municipality in the Ordubad Rayon of Nakhchivan, Azerbaijan.  It has a population of 155.
The village historically was a part of the Goghtn Region of Armenia, and had an Armenian population until the beginning of the 20th century. Now only ruins of ancient church remain.
