[[Hans Dobbertin]]

CATEGORIES: Modern cryptographers, 1952 births, 2006 deaths

Hans Dobbertin (April 17, 1952 - February 2, 2006) was a German cryptographer who is best known for his work on cryptanalysis of the MD4, MD5, and original RIPEMD hash functions, and for his part in the design of the new version of the RIPEMD hash function.
