[[Seattle Tower]]

CATEGORIES: Skyscrapers in Seattle, Washington, Landmarks in Seattle, Washington, Office buildings completed in 1929, Office buildings on the National Register of Historic Places in Washington (state), Skyscrapers between 100 and 149 meters, Office buildings in Seattle, Washington, Art Deco architecture in Washington (state)

The Seattle Tower, originally known as the Northern Life Tower, is a 27-story skyscraper in downtown Seattle, Washington.  The building is located on 1218 Third Avenue and is known as Seattle's first art-deco tower.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.emporis.com/en/wm/bu/?id=seattletower-seattle-wa-usa|title=Emporis.com page[/tpl] Its distinctive, ziggurat exterior is clad in 33 shades of brick designed to effect a gradient which lightens from the bottom to the top of the building.  This is said to have been inspired by local rock formations.
