[[Tomur Barnes]]

CATEGORIES: 1970 births, Living people, Players of American football from Texas, People from Harris County, Texas, American football cornerbacks, Washington Redskins players, Houston Oilers players, Minnesota Vikings players, Tennessee Oilers players, North Texas Mean Green football players

Tomur Barnes (born September 8, 1970 in McNair, Texas) is a former American football cornerback in the National Football League for the Houston Oilers, Minnesota Vikings, and the Washington Redskins.  He played college football at the University of North Texas.
