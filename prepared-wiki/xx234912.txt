[[Wamba Hospital]]

CATEGORIES: Hospitals affiliated with the Roman Catholic Church, Hospitals in Kenya, Hospitals established in the 1960s

Wamba Hospital, also known as Wamba Catholic Hospital, is a hospital in Wamba, Samburu District, Kenya.http://www.ehealth.or.ke/facilities/facility.aspx?fas=15769 It was established in the 1960s.
Money is donated to Wamba Hospital by Aiglon College, a school located in Switzerland, via the S.H.E.P. Foundation. This foundation has recently helped build a school building for the children who are taken care of at the hospital.
