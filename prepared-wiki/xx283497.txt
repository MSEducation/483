Samuel Hammington (born July 31, 1977) is a New Zealand-born Australian comedian, television personality, radio presenter and actor based in South Korea. Hammington hosted radio show Drivetime with Annabelle Ambrose from 2008 to 2012. He is currently a cast member in the TV show Real Men together with Seo Kyung-suk, Kim Su-ro, Ryu Soo-young, Son Jin-young, Jang Hyuk, Park Hyung-sik.
