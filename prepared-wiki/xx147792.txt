[[Solitaire (film)]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 films, English-language films, 2000s drama films

Solitaire (2008) is an independent coming of age drama film in pre-production from director Victor Franko (Frank D'Agostino) and Rand Alan Sabatini of Rand Alan Studios with a planned cast of 10. This was late pornstar/actress Marilyn Chambers' final film.
