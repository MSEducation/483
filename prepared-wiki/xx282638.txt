[[United States elections, 1894]]

CATEGORIES: 1894 elections in the United States, United States general elections

The 1894 United States general election was held on November 6. These were mid-term elections during Democratic President Grover Clevelands second term. 
The Democrats suffered a landslide defeat in the House, losing 107 seats to the Republicans. The Democrats also lost four seats in the Senate.
A number of gubernatorial and municipal elections were also held.
