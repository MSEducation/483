[[Frank Jackson State Park]]

CATEGORIES: State parks of Alabama, Protected areas of Covington County, Alabama

Frank Jackson State Park is a state park on the northwest side of Opp, Alabama. The [tpl]convert|2050|acre|ha|adj=on[/tpl] park wraps around Lake Frank Jackson, a [tpl]convert|1000|acre|ha|adj=on[/tpl] lake that sits within Opp city limits. The park offers facilities for fishing, swimming, boating, picnicking and camping.[tpl]cite web |title=Frank Jackson State Park |work=Alabama State Parks |publisher=Alabama Department of Conservation and Natural Resources |url=http://www.alapark.com/FrankJackson/ |accessdate=2012-11-06[/tpl]
