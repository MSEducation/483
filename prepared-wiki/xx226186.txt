Benjamin Morgan Cowie was Dean of Manchester and then Exeter in the last quarter of the 19th Century. 
Born on 8 June 1816,“Who was Who”1897-1990 London, A & C Black, 1991 ISBN 0-7136-3457-X he was educated at St John's College, Cambridge and graduated Senior Wrangler in 1839.“Mr Hopkins’ Men: Cambridge Reform and British Mathematics in the 19th Century” Craik,A.D.D: London, Springer, 2007 ISBN 978-1-84628-790-9 Ordained in 1841 he was successively Tutor, Lecturer and Fellow at his old college.[tpl]acad|id=CWY833BM|name=Cowie, Benjamin Morgan[/tpl] Afterwards he was  Vicar of St Lawrence Jewry followed by a 11 year spell in Manchester,Victoria County History A History of the County of Lancaster: Volume 4 J. Brownbill;, William Farrer,W (ed) 1911 followed by a further 17 at Exeter. He died on 3 May 1900.The Times, Friday, May 04, 1900; pg. 6; Issue 36133; col G Obituary-The Dean Of Exeter
