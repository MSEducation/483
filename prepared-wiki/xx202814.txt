[[Veronika Dytrtová]]

CATEGORIES: Czech female single skaters, 1980 births, Living people, Sportspeople from Prague

Veronika Dytrtová (born June 18, 1980 in Prague) is a Czech figure skater. She is a four-time medalist at the  Czech Figure Skating Championships, winning two silvers and two bronzes between 1999 and 2002. She placed 27th at the 1999 World Figure Skating Championships and competed twice at the European Figure Skating Championships and the World Junior Figure Skating Championships. She competed domestically in Germany under the surname "Dytrt" and won the bronze medal at the 1998 German Figure Skating Championships.
She is the sister of Anette Dytrt.
