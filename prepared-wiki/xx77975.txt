[[Lushnjë District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Albania, Fier County

The District of Lushnjë ([tpl]Lang-sq|Rrethi i Lushnjës[/tpl]) is one of the thirty-six districts of Albania, part of Fier County. It has a population of 143,276[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.instat.gov.al/graphics/doc/tabelat/Treguesit%20Sociale/Popullsia/POP%202009/t2.xls|title=POPULLSIA SIPAS RRETHEVE, 2001-2010|publisher=Albanian Institute of Statistics|accessdate=2010-09-09[/tpl] (2010 estimate), and an area of 712 km².
