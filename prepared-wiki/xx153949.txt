[[Paul Keenan]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Scottish police officers, Scottish record producers, Scottish songwriters, 1976 births

Paul Keenan (born 5 November 1976) is a songwriter and producer based in Dumbarton, near Glasgow in Scotland. He is best known as being part of the successful UK dance act Uniting Nations along with Daz Sampson,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.musicomh.com/albums/uniting-nations_1105.htm|title=Uniting Nations - One World (Gusto)|last=Hogwood|first=Ben|date=14 November 2005|publisher=musicOMH|accessdate=4 November 2010[/tpl]  who have had four hits singles to date "Out of Touch", "You and Me", "Ai No Corrida" and "Do It Yourself".
