[[Croker]]

CATEGORIES: Rowing equipment manufacturers

Croker Oars is an Australian manufacturer of Rowing oars that was started by Howard Croker OAM in Sydney, Australia. During the 1950s, Croker and his two brothers were students at Newington CollegeNewington College Register of Past Students 1863-1998 (Syd, 1999) pp 45  and their father was a rowing coach at the school.Interview with Howard Croker He was also a successful rower in the 1960s, winning both State and National rowing titles. Croker rowed for the then Haberfield Rowing Club at Dobroyd Point and was a coach in the years 1975 and 1976 at The Scots College. Croker Oars currently produce sculling and sweep oars for the Australian and international market. Croker also manufactures surf boat oars. Many elite rowers use Croker oars and together with Concept2 oars make up the majority of oars used in international competition. The business continues to grow and the Croker family are still involved in the day-to-day running of the firm.
