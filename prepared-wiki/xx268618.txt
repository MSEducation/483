[[Equestrian at the 2011 Pan American Games – Individual eventing]]

CATEGORIES: Equestrian at the 2011 Pan American Games

The individual eventing competition of the equestrian events at the 2011 Pan American Games took place between October 21–23 at the Guadalajara Country Club and the Hipica Club.  The defending Pan American champion was Karen O'Connor of the United States of America.
