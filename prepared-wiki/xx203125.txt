[[Renaissance Recordings]]

CATEGORIES: British record labels, Trance record labels, Record labels established in 1994, Electronic music record labels

Renaissance Recordings was a British record label, founded by the club Renaissance in 1994. In went into administration in September 2010 and the back catalogue was sold to Phoenix Music International Ltd.
After a year of inactivity, in July 2011 it was announced that Renaissance had been reborn, with founder and original owner Geoff Oakes once again guiding the brand.http://renaissance.dj/about/
