[[Hannspeter Winter]]

CATEGORIES: Austrian physicists, 1941 births, 2006 deaths, People from Wels, Recipients of the Austrian Cross of Honour for Science and Art, 1st class

Hannspeter Winter (born in Wels on August 22, 1941; died in Vienna on November 8, 2006) was an Austrian plasma physicist who did research on hollow atoms.IOP He won the Austrian Cross of Honour for Science and Art, 1st class in 2001 and the prestigious German Alexander von Humboldt Research Prize in 2003.
