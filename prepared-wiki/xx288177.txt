[[Molla Piri]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Molla Piri ([tpl]lang-fa|ملاپيري[/tpl], also Romanized as Mollā Pīrī; also known as Mollā Parī and Mullah Pīri)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3075673|Molla Piri[/tpl] is a village in Saidabad Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,107, in 281 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
