[[Lilli Ann]]

CATEGORIES: Clothing brands

Lilli Ann was a clothing company that was started in San Francisco, California in 1934 http://query.nytimes.com/gst/fullpage.html?res=9D00E0D91239F931A35753C1A963948260 by Adolph Schuman, and named for his wife Lillian. Throughout the 1940s and 1950s, the company was known for its good workmanship and high-quality fabrics.
