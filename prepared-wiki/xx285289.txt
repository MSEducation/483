Borislav Nikolov ([tpl]lang-bg|Борислав Николов[/tpl]; born 3 February 1992) is a Bulgarian footballer, who currently plays as a midfielder for Marek Dupnitsa.[tpl]cite web|publisher=footballdatabase.eu|title=Borislav Nikolov Facts|url=http://www.footballdatabase.eu/football.joueurs.borislav.nikolov.107655.en.html|accessdate=[/tpl]

==Career==

Nikolov began his club career with CSKA Sofia, but made only one appearance for the senior squad. He played 56 minutes in a 2–0 away league loss against Chernomorets Burgas on 16 May 2010.
In June 2011, Nikolov was loaned out to Akademik Sofia.[tpl]cite web|publisher=akademik-sofia.com|title=Академик София, статистика сезон 2011/12|url=http://www.akademik-sofia.com/page.php?777|language=Bulgarian[/tpl]
