[[Roberts Center]]

CATEGORIES: Sports venues completed in 1958, Boston College Eagles basketball venues, Defunct sports venues in Massachusetts, Defunct indoor arenas in the United States, Defunct college basketball venues in the United States, Buildings and structures in Suffolk County, Massachusetts

Roberts Center was a 4,400-seat multi-purpose arena in Chestnut Hill, Massachusetts.  It opened in 1958.  It was home to the Boston College Eagles basketball teams until the Conte Forum opened in 1988.
