[[Hjalmar Väre]]

CATEGORIES: 1892 births, 1952 deaths, People from Vihti, Finnish male cyclists, Olympic cyclists of Finland, Cyclists at the 1912 Summer Olympics

Frans Albert Hjalmar Väre (22 July 1892 – 20 March 1952) was a Finnish road racing cyclist who competed in the 1912 Summer Olympics.
He was born in Vihti and died in Turku.
In 1912 he was a member of the Finnish cycling team which finished fifth in the team time trial event.
