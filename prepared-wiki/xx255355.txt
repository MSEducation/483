[[Nordbotn, Sør-Trøndelag]]

CATEGORIES: Hitra, Villages in Sør-Trøndelag

[tpl]About|the village in Hitra, Norway||Nordbotn (disambiguation)[tpl]![/tpl]Nordbotn[/tpl]

Nordbotn is a village in the municipality of Hitra in Sør-Trøndelag county, Norway.  It is located on the east side of the island of Fjellværsøya.  It is the location of the Nordbotn church.  The village was once a trading post with a post office, bakery, and store.
