[[David Townsend (Devon cricketer)]]

CATEGORIES: 1965 births, Living people, People from Tiverton, Devon, People from Devon, English cricketers, Devon cricketers

David Edward John Townsend (born 26 October 1965) is a former English cricketer.  Townsend was a right-handed batsman who bowled right-arm medium pace.  He was born at Tiverton, Devon.
Townsend made his debut for Devon in the 1993 Minor Counties Championship against Wiltshire.  From 1993 to 2002, he represented the county in 10 Championship matches, the last of which came against Oxfordshire.Minor Counties Championship Matches played by David Townsend  His MCCA Knockout Trophy debut for the county came against Dorset in 1993.  He played one further Trophy match for Devon, which came in 2002 against Wiltshire.Minor Counties Trophy Matches played by David Townsend  
He also represented Devon in a single List A match against Derbyshire in the 1993 NatWest Trophy.List A Matches played by David Townsend  In this match, he scored 29 runs before being bowled by Matthew Vandrau.List A Batting and Fielding For Each Team by David Townsend
His younger brother, Gareth, played first-class cricket for Somerset.  He also played List A cricket for Surrey, Devon and a combined Minor Counties team.
