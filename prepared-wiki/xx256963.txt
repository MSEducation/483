[[Elizabeth Pohlmann House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses completed in 1896, Queen Anne architecture in Iowa, Houses in Davenport, Iowa, Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Iowa, National Register of Historic Places in Scott County, Iowa

The Elizabeth Pohlmann House is located in the West End of Davenport, Iowa, United States.  The Queen Anne style residence has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since 1984.
