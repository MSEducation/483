[[Alan Richardson (priest)]]

CATEGORIES: 1905 births, 1975 deaths, Deans of York, Alumni of the University of Liverpool, Alumni of Exeter College, Oxford, Academics of the University of Nottingham, Knights Commander of the Order of the British Empire

Alan Richardson KBE (1905-1975University of Durham) was educated at Liverpool University, Exeter College, Oxford and  Ridley Hall, Cambridge. “Who was Who” 1897-2007 London, A & C Black, 2007  ISBN 978-0-19-954087-7  Ordained in 1928Crockford's Clerical Directory1947-48 Oxford, OUP,1947 his first post was as a curate at St Saviour's Liverpool. He was Vicar of Cambo and then Secretary of the Student Christian Movement. Later he was a canon of Durham Cathedral then Professor of Christian Theology at the University of Nottingham from 1953 until 1964 when he accepted the position as Dean of York, a post he held until his death.Church TimesUniversity of Nottingham, Papers of Rev.
