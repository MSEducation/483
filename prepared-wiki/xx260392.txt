[[Bruni Glass]]

CATEGORIES: Glass makers, Companies based in Milan

Bruni Glass is a glass manufacturing company based in Milan, Italy and was founded in 1974.  Bruni Glass specializes in the making of wine, liquor and food container bottles.Wine County MinuteWines and VinesBottless.orgItalian Chamber of Commerce in Canada
