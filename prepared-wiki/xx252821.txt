[[Yala Technical College]]

CATEGORIES: Educational institutions established in 1957, Institutions of higher education in Thailand

[tpl]Portal|Yala Technical College[/tpl]
Yala Technical College is a higher education institution in Yala, Thailand offering 2 years (full-time) post-secondary diplomas in Information technology, Computer engineering, Architecture, Mechanical Engineering, Construction engineering, Rubber Technology, Electrical Engineering, Electronics Engineering, Automotive Engineering, and Business Information Systems.
The College also offers a 2+2 years programme in Technical Education.
