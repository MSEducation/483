[[King Tom]]

CATEGORIES: Temne people

King Tom was the Temne ruler of the land where the Province of Freedom, Sierra Leone was first located.Braidwood S. (1994) p. 181
He was described by John C. Lettsome as "a very fine fellow, so far as dress goes, for he generally has variegated bright colours; his hat is immenesely large, and the whole flap covered with gold lace, of which he is very proud. His palace is, however equal to a hut."Braidwood S. (1994) p. 7
Lettsome also stated that he was at one time father-in-law of Henry Smeathman.Braidwood S. (1994) p. 7
