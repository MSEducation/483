[[Omi, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Omi|麻績村|Omi-mura[/tpl] is a village located in Higashichikuma District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the village had an estimated population of 3,287 and a density of 95.61 people per km². The total area is 34.38 km².
