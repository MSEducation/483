[[Yoshiyuki Nishi]]

CATEGORIES: 1976 births, Manga artists, Manga artists from Tokyo, People from Western Tokyo, Living people

[tpl]nihongo|Yoshiyuki Nishi|西 義之|Nishi Yoshiyuki|born December 27, 1976[/tpl] is a Japanese manga artist best known for his work, Muhyo and Roji's Bureau of Supernatural Investigation. He is the former assistant of Takeshi Obata. Born and raised in Hachioji, Tokyo.
