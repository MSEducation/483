[[Historic Landmarks of Exceptional Importance]]

CATEGORIES: Historic Landmarks of Exceptional Importance, Cultural Heritage of Serbia, Landmarks in Serbia, History of Serbia by theme, Serbian culture, Serb people

Historic Landmarks of Exceptional Importance ([tpl]lang-sr|Знаменита места од изузетног значаjа/Znamenita mesta od izuzetnog značaja[/tpl]) are historical places in the Republic of Serbia that have the highest level of the State protection.
Those are part of the Cultural Property of Great Importance protection list.
