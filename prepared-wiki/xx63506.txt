Kathleen Marie "Kathy" Ireland (born March 20, 1963) is an American model, actress, and entrepreneur.[tpl]cite news|last=Forbes|first=Moira|title=Kathy Ireland: Swimsuit Cover Girl Turned $2 Billion Business Model?|url=http://www.forbes.com/sites/moiraforbes/2012/02/13/kathy-ireland-swimsuit-covergirl-turned-model-entrepreneur/|accessdate=7 July 2013|newspaper=Forbes|date=2012-02-13[/tpl] Ireland was a supermodel in the 1980s and 1990s, best known for appearing in 13 consecutive Sports Illustrated swimsuit issues. In 1993, she founded a brand marketing company, "kathy ireland Worldwide" (kiWW), which has made her one of the wealthiest former models in the world.[tpl]cite news |last=Pomerantz |first=Dorothy |title=How Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Model Kathy Ireland Became a $350 Million Mogul|url=http://www.forbes.com/sites/dorothypomerantz/2012/02/08/how-sports-illustrated-swimsuit-model-kathy-ireland-became-a-350-million-mogul/ |work=Forbes |date=February 8, 2012 |deadurl=no |accessdate=September 2, 2012[/tpl][tpl]cite news|last=Welch|first=Liz|title=reland graced the cover of Sports Illustrated's|url=http://www.inc.com/magazine/20091201/the-way-i-work-kathy-ireland.html|accessdate=7 July 2013|newspaper=Inc.|date=December 1, 2009[/tpl][tpl]cite web|last=Rizzo|first=Monica|title=Kathy Ireland's Shocking Weight Gain (and Loss)|url=http://www.people.com/people/article/0,,20269449,00.html|work=People|accessdate=July 6, 2012[/tpl]

==Early life==

Kathy Ireland was born in 1963 in Glendale, California, the daughter of John Ireland, a labor union organizer, and Barbara Ireland, who worked in charity.Kathy Ireland Biography (1963–) Her first job was a paper route when she was 11; she landed the job by writing the paper and stating she was the girl for the job.

==Modeling==

At age 16, Ireland was discovered by an agent for the Elite Model Management company. She appeared on covers for Vogue and Cosmopolitan.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.eonline.com/celebrities/profile/index.jsp?uuid=67c3c82c-7d5a-4e66-8d20-7cfc12989337 |title=Kathy Ireland – Profile |publisher=E! |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20080423113319/http://www.eonline.com/celebrities/profile/index.jsp?uuid=67c3c82c-7d5a-4e66-8d20-7cfc12989337 |archivedate=April 23, 2008 |accessdate=September 2, 2012[/tpl]

Starting in 1984, Ireland was featured in the annual Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue for 13 consecutive years, with her final appearance in 1996.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.colossuscollectibles.com/calendars/siswimsuit/index.html|title=Collosus Collectibles Sports Illustrated Swimsuit|accessdate=July 30, 2008[/tpl] She appeared on the cover of the annual issue three times, a feat tied by Christie Brinkley and Cheryl Tiegs, and surpassed only by Elle Macpherson (with five appearances). Ireland was the cover model for the first time on the 25th Anniversary Swimsuit Edition, which became SI 's best selling swimsuit issue to date.[tpl]cite news|url=http://sportsillustrated.cnn.com/swimsuit/collection/issues/ultimate_swimsuit/ult_kireland_01.html|title=SI Swimsuit Collection – Kathy Ireland|author=John G. Zimmerman|publisher=Sports Illustrated|accessdate=July 30, 2008[/tpl][tpl]cite news|url=http://sportsillustrated.cnn.com/swimsuit/collection/issues/ultimate_swimsuit/ult_kireland_01.html|title=Sports Illustrated 50: The Anniversary Book, Rob Fleder, 2005, p.286, ISBN 1-932273-49-2 | publisher=CNN | accessdate=May 7, 2010[/tpl] She returned to the cover in 1992 and in 1994. In her third appearance, she was accompanied by Rachel Hunter and Elle Macpherson, who were both pregnant at the time.
During SI Swimsuit's 50th Anniversary event, Ireland's 1989 cover was awarded "The Greatest Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Cover Of All Time." [tpl]cite web|url=http://sportsillustrated.cnn.com/video/si-video/20140203/si-swimsuit-50-no-1-cover-kathy-ireland.sportsillustrated/|title=SI Swimsuit reveals its No. 1 cover of the last 50 years| accessdate=February 5, 2014[/tpl]

==Business ventures==

===Kathy Ireland Worldwide===

In 1993, Ireland put her name on a line of socks for Kmart, which became a bestseller. The retailer subsequently gave Ireland her own clothing line.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.forbes.com/celebrities/forbes/2004/0705/116.html|title=The Model Mogul |last=Blakeley|first=Kiri|date=May 7, 2004 |work=Forbes|accessdate=August 1, 2008|archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20110604012658/http://www.forbes.com/forbes/2004/0705/116.html|archivedate=May 24, 2011[/tpl] She then founded kathy ireland Worldwide, initially a brand marketing firm, which she and her related trusts own in its entirety.[tpl]cite book | title=Contemporary Business 12th Edition |work=Thomson South-Western | author=Boone & Kurtz | year=2007[/tpl] Since 1994, Ireland has published several fitness videos that led to her design of athletic apparel and launched her brand licensing of fitness equipment.[tpl]Citation needed|date=July 2013[/tpl]
In 2004, Ireland's annual take is thought to be around $10 million. Forbes described Ireland as a "prototype for model-turned-mogul" in a 2006 article about the trend of modelpreneurs.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.forbes.com/2008/07/17/supermodel-entrepreneur-gisele-ent-sales-cx_kb_0717modelpreneurs_slide_11.html?thisSpeed=20000 | title=Models Turned Moguls | last=Blakeley | first=Kiri|date=December 6, 2006|work=Forbes|accessdate=August 1, 2008|archiveurl=http://archive.is/nmICX|archivedate=January 2, 2013[/tpl] By 2004, kathy ireland Worldwide was marketing products from 16 manufacturers, including those of Shaw Industries and Pacific Coast Lighting.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.forbes.com/lifestyle/2006/02/02/fashion-supermodels-licensing-cz_kb_0203fashion2_ls.html |title=Most Entrepreneurial Supermodels |last=Blakeley |first=Kiri |date=December 6, 2006 |work=Forbes |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20080923071255/http://www.forbes.com/lifestyle/2006/02/02/fashion-supermodels-licensing-cz_kb_0203fashion2_ls.html |archivedate=September 23, 2008 |accessdate=September 2, 2012[/tpl] Between 2005-2012, Ireland's company grew to about $2 billion in annual sales and became a leader in its industry.
[tpl]cite web|title=About kiWW|url=http://www.kathyireland.com/aboutkathy.html|accessdate=April 2, 2012[/tpl] In a May 2012 British Vogue article, Ireland was called the world's richest model.[tpl]cite news| url=http://www.vogue.co.uk/news/2012/05/24/anne-v---anne-vyalitsyna-model-victorias-secret-maroon-5 | work=British Vogue | title=Meet Anne V | accessdate=May 25, 2012 | date=May 24, 2012[/tpl]"Supermodel meets supermogul as Warren Buffet teams up with former swimsuit model Kathy Ireland, now a CEO and worth $2 billion", "Daily Mail", May 4, 2013

==Acting==

Ireland has appeared in a number of television and movie roles. Her first movie, 1988's Alien from L.A., was later skewered during the fifth season of Mystery Science Theater 3000.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.tv.com/mystery-science-theater-3000/alien-from-l.a./episode/46964/summary.html|title=Mystery Science Theater 3000|date=November 20, 1993|publisher=TV.com|accessdate=July 31, 2008[/tpl] Ireland's other appearances include Melrose Place, Boy Meets World, The Larry Sanders Show, Muppets Tonight, Side Out, Mr. Destiny, Necessary Roughness, Mom and Dad Save the World, National Lampoon's Loaded Weapon 1, Once Upon A Christmas, and its sequel Twice Upon A Christmas. She also appeared in the ninth season of Dancing with the Stars, where her couple was the third eliminated in the second week of competition.

==Philanthropy==

Ireland's sister, Cynthia, has a child with Down syndrome. Ireland wrote an article about her niece and the need for an increase in research.[tpl]cite web|url=http://abilitymagazine.com/Kathy-Ireland.html|title=Ability Magazine: Kathy Ireland Interview with Chet Cooper and Molly Mackin" (2011)|accessdate=April 3, 2012[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.aolnews.com/2010/10/11/kathy-ireland-the-tragic-shortfall-in-down-syndrome-research/|title=AOL News: Kathy Ireland: The Tragic Shortfall in Down Syndrome Research" (2010)|accessdate=April 3, 2012[/tpl] Other Ireland-supported causes include Big Brothers Big Sisters, St. Jude Children's Research Hospital, the Alliance for Global Education, the Alliance for Christian Education and the Make-a-Wish Foundation.[tpl]Citation needed|date=July 2013[/tpl]
Ireland was presented with an honorary Doctorate of Humane Letters in 2012 by the California State University System, which said she "generously uses her power and influence to benefit others, supporting social causes including empowering women, supporting young girls through mentoring, and providing opportunities for girls and women at risk." http://www.calstate.edu/pa/News/2012/Story/honorary2012.shtml
Ireland has lent her estates to charitable organizations such as Toys For Tots to generate funds for charity.[tpl]cite web|url=http://archives.midweek.com/content/story/midweek_coverstory/Kathy_Ireland_Toys_For_Tots/P1/|title=Kathy Ireland From Model to Mogul| accessdate=December 15, 2010[/tpl] According to Maj. Bill Grein, USMC (Ret,) Ireland’s Kathy Ireland Worldwide company and her book business partner Bendon Publishing International, the world’s largest publisher of children's books donated $10 million to supplement the 2013 annual Marine Corps’ Toys For Tots gathering and distribution of Christmas gifts for millions of at-risk children and youths.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.sacbee.com/2013/12/02/5962314/a-real-life-santa-role-for-kathy.html/|title=A Real-Life Santa Role for Kathy Ireland|date=December 2, 2013| accessdate=December 2, 2013[/tpl]

Ireland is a Christian. She is a pro-life advocate.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.cbn.com/700club/features/kathy_ireland-interview.aspx|title=Kathy Ireland: Designing Woman|last=Blim|first=Julie|date=August 20, 2002|publisher=CBN|accessdate=April 21, 2009[/tpl]

===Friendship with Elizabeth Taylor===

Elizabeth Taylor and Ireland had a close friendship and attended public events together. After Ireland received outspoken criticism for her uneven performance as an on-air host of ABC's 2010 pre-Oscar special, Taylor publicly defended Ireland; "I have never seen anyone epitomize glamour and grace and professionalism like she did." "Elizabeth Taylor ‘Boiling’ Over Criticism Of Kathy Ireland At Oscars", "Access Hollywood", 03/11/2010"Elizabeth Taylor and Kathy Ireland", "Zimbio", September 26, 2008
Ireland publicly credits Taylor as her mentor and for part of her success in life, business, design and philanthropy."Kathy Ireland on Her Mentor Elizabeth Taylor", "ET Online", March 26, 2013 Taylor bequeathed her Jean Hersholt Humanitarian Oscar to Ireland.[tpl]cite news |url= http://www.forbes.com/sites/dorothypomerantz/2012/02/08/the-surprising-link-between-kathy-ireland-and-janet-jackson/ |title=The Surprising Link Between Kathy Ireland and Janet Jackson |author=Dorothy Pomerantz |work= Forbes |date=February 8, 2013 |accessdate= December 2, 2013[/tpl]
