[[Ismail Muttalib]]

CATEGORIES: Malaysian politicians, Living people, Members of the Dewan Rakyat, United Malays National Organisation politicians

Datuk Ismail Abd Muttalib is a Malaysian politician and the Member of the Parliament of Malaysia for the Maran constituency in Pahang. He is a member of the United Malays National Organisation (UMNO) party in the governing Barisan Nasional coalition.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.parlimen.gov.my/index.php?modload=ahlidewan&uweb=dr&action=details&id=74|title=Ismail bin Haji Abd Muttalib, Y.B. Dato' Haji|publisher=Parliament of Malaysia|language=Malay|accessdate=12 July 2010[/tpl]
Muttalib was elected to Parliament in the 2004 election and was re-elected in 2008.[ref]
