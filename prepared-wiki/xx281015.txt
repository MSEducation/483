[[Mickey van der Hart]]

CATEGORIES: 1994 births, Living people, Dutch footballers, AFC Ajax players, Eredivisie players, Eerste Divisie players, People from Amstelveen

Mickey van der Hart (born 13 June 1994 in Amstelveen, Netherlands) is a Dutch professional football player who plays as goalkeeper in the Eredivisie for AFC Ajax.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.transfermarkt.de/de/mickey-van-der-hart/profil/spieler_207048.html|title=Mickey van der Hart profile|date=2 March 2012|publisher=Transfermarkt.com|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl]

==Career==

===AFC Ajax===

Acquired into the Ajax Youth Academy from local amateur side Amsterdamsche FC in 2007, Mickey van der Hart worked his way up the youth ranks, and had an outstanding 2011–12 season with the Ajax A1 youth squad.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.afc.nl/syscontent/pdfs/12.pdf|title=Amsterdamsche Football Club Persmap|date=25 March 2012|publisher=AFC.nl|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] That year, the under-20 team where he was first choice keeper at the time, had won the national title (Nike Eredivisie),[tpl]cite news|url=http://bin617-02.website-voetbal.nl/sites/voetbal.nl/files/04%20A%20-%20Beste%20spelers%20A-jun%20eredivisie.pdf|title=Beste Spelers Nike A-junioren Eredivisie seizoen 2011/'12 |date=26 January 2012|publisher=Nikefootball.com|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] as well as finished as runners-up in that seasons NextGen Series (the Champions League equivalent for youth teams) when they lost to Inter Milan on penalties.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.voetbalcentraal.nl/nieuws/75887/ajax-a1-verliest-finale-van-nextgen-series-van-inter-milan|title=Ajax A1 verliest finale van NextGen Series van Inter Milan|date=25 March 2012|publisher=Voetbal Centraal|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] Mickey was awarded his first professional contract when he was pronounced as the first teams third choice goalkeeper, as well as receiving the award for Talent van De Toekomst (Talent of the Future).[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.ajax.nl/Ajax-Nieuws/Ajax-nieuwsarchief/Ajax-nieuwsartikel/Van-der-Hart-talent-van-de-Toekomst.htm|title=Van der Hart talent van de Toekomst|date=15 June 2012|publisher=Ajax.nl|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] His first contract was a three-year deal, binding him to the club until June 2015.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.ajaxlife.nl/updates/keeper-van-der-hart-tekent-contract-tot-2015|title=Keeper Van der Hart tekent contract tot 2015|date=2 March 2012|publisher=Ajaxlife.nl|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl]
The 2012–13 season was Mickey van der Hart's first with the Ajax first team since the departure of former third keeper Jeroen Verhoeven,[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.ajaxshowtime.com/hoofdnieuws/61586/van-der-hart-wordt-derde-keeper.html&page=2|title=Van der Hart wordt derde keeper|date=2 March 2012|publisher=Ajax Showtime|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] however with then first and second choice keepers Kenneth Vermeer and Jasper Cillessen each having an injury free season, Mickey was unable to make his debut for the first team, meanwhile still playing in the Ajax A1 youth squad, where he was still eligible to participate due to his young age.[tpl]cite news|url=http://ajax1.nl/archief/van-der-hart-veel-te-danken-aan-fred-grim/|title=Van der Hart veel te danken aan Fred Grim|date=18 June 2013|publisher=Ajax1.nl|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] On 8 April 2013 an opportunity presented itself for Mickey to appear as second choice keeper behind Jasper Cillessen, following a red card suspension to Kenneth Vermeer. That void would however be filled by Chiel Kramer, first keeper of the reserves team Jong Ajax, since Mickey was sidelined due to a groin injury at the time.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.sport1.nl/nieuws/49482-de-boer-kiest-jeugd-boven-van-der-sar.html|title=De Boer kiest jeugd boven Van der Sar|date=8 April 2013|publisher=Sport1.nl|accessdate=6 June 2013[/tpl] Ajax would eventually go on to win the national title in what would be Mickey's first season as a professional.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.ad.nl/ad/nl/1441/Ajax/article/detail/3436672/2013/05/05/Ajax-freewheelend-naar-32ste-landstitel.dhtml|title=Ajax freewheelend naar 32ste landstitel|date=5 June 2013|publisher=AD.nl|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl]
The 2013–14 season saw Mickey maintain his position as Frank de Boer's third choice behind Kenneth Vermeer and Jasper Cillessen,[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.ajaxlife.nl/updates/ajax-maakt-alle-rugnummers-a-selectie-bekend|title=Ajax maakt alle rugnummers A-selectie bekend|date=18 July 2013|publisher=Ajaxlife.nl|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] as the season opened with Ajax winning the Dutch Super Cup (Johan Cruijff Shield).[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.vi.nl/nieuws/ajax-verovert-pas-na-verlenging-de-johan-cruijff-schaal.htm|title=Ajax verovert pas na verlenging de Johan Cruijff Schaal|date=27 July 2013|publisher=Voetbal International|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] Mickey made his professional debut on 9 August 2013 in the Jupiler League keeping for Jong Ajax, where he is still eligible to play, having made less than 15 appearances for Ajax first team in the Eredivisie.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.at5.nl/artikelen/108609/jong-ajax-ten-onder-tegen-fc-oss|title=Jong Ajax ten onder tegen FC Oss|date=9 August 2013|publisher=AT5|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] The match against FC Oss in which he played the full 90' minutes ended in a 2–0 loss for the Amsterdammers, in what would be only Jong Ajax second match ever in professional football, with the team having moved up from the Beloften Eredivisie before the start of the season.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.nu.nl/sport/3547096/fc-oss-in-eigen-huis-sterk-jong-ajax.html|title=FC Oss in eigen huis te sterk voor Jong Ajax|date=9 August 2013|publisher=NU.nl|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl]

==International career==

Mickey van der Hart has represented Netherlands U-18 and Netherlands U-19. On 11 November 2011, Mickey made his debut for Netherlands U-18 in a friendly encounter against Romania U-18.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.worldfootball.net/teams/niederlande-u18-h-team/2011/3/|title=Netherlands U-18 friendlies 2011|date=1 January 2012|publisher=Worldfootball.net|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] On 7 September 2012, Mickey made his debut for the under-19 team in a friendly match against Greece U-19.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.worldfootball.net/alle_spiele/u19-h-freundschaft-2012/|title=All U-19 friendlies 2012|date=1 January 2012|publisher=Worldfootball.net|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] He was selected by head coach Wim van Zwam to participate in the 2013 UEFA European Under-19 Football Championship in Lithuania.[tpl]cite news|url=http://ajaxonline.org/18-ajaxtotaal-nl/13350-drie-ajacieden-geselecteerd-voor-ek-19|title=Drie Ajacieden geselecteerd voor EK-19|date=14 July 2013|publisher=Ajaxonline.org|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] Mickey played in all the group matches, of which 1 was won, and 2 were lost, failing to advance past the group stage.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.onsoranje.nl/nieuws/artikel/16557/o-19-uitgeschakeld-op-ek|title=O-19 uitgeschakeld op EK|date=26 July 2013|publisher=OnsOranje.nl|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] On 2 August 2013 head coach of the Netherlands U-21 team Albert Stuivenberg selected Mickey van der Hart for the 32 member provisional squad, ahead of the 2013 UEFA European Under-21 Football Championship. On 9 August 2013 however it was announced that Mickey had not made the final 22-member squad selection for the tournament.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.knvb.nl/nieuws/30714/voorlopige-selectie-jong-oranje-telt-32-spelers|title=Voorlopige selectie Jong Oranje telt 32 spelers|date=31 July 2013|publisher=KNVB.nl|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl]

==Personal life==

Mickey van der Hart is the grandson of former AFC Ajax central defender Cor van der Hart.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.ajax.nl/Ajax-nieuws/Ajax-nieuwsarchief/Ajax-nieuwsartikel/Van-der-Hart-kent-zijn-rol.htm|title=Van der Hart kent zijn rol|date=11 January 2013|publisher=Ajax.nl|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl]
