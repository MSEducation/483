[[Archery at the 2010 Commonwealth Games]]

CATEGORIES: 2010 Commonwealth Games events, 2010 in archery, Archery at the Commonwealth Games

The archery competition at the 2010 Commonwealth Games was held at the Yamuna Sports Complex between 4 and 10 October. This was the second games appearance of the optional Commonwealth Games sport, almost thirty years after its debut at the 1982 Commonwealth Games.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cwgdelhi2010.org/dcwg/?q=node/617|title=Archery|publisher=Organising Committee Commonwealth Games 2010 Delhi|accessdate=23 September 2010[/tpl] Twenty-three nations entered archers into the tournament.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.archery.org/UserFiles/Document/Miscellaneous%20Events/10_CG_Delhi/10_CG_Participants.pdf|title=Provisional List of Participants|publisher=World Archery|accessdate=23 September 2010| archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20100922062042/http://archery.org/UserFiles/Document/Miscellaneous%20Events/10_CG_Delhi/10_CG_Participants.pdf| archivedate= 22 September 2010 | deadurl= no[/tpl]
