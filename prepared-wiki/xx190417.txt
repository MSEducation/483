[[Kōrakuen Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Tokyo, Tokyo Metro Marunouchi Line, Tokyo Metro Namboku Line, 1954 establishments in Japan

[tpl]nihongo|Kōrakuen Station|後楽園駅|Kōrakuen-eki[/tpl] is a subway train station in Bunkyō, Tokyo, Japan, operated by Tokyo Metro. It is directly connected by an underground pedestrian passage to the Toei-operated Kasuga Station. It is integrated with the Tokyo Dome City complex and the Bunkyō ward capitol building.
Due to the terrain, the Marunouchi Line platforms are elevated, and are [tpl]convert|40|m[/tpl] above the underground, deep-level Namboku Line platforms.
