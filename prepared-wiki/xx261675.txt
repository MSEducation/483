[[USS Talofa (SP-1016)]]

CATEGORIES: Patrol vessels of the United States Navy, World War I patrol vessels of the United States, Ships built in Massachusetts, 1910 ships

USS ''Talofa'' (SP-1016) was a United States Navy patrol vessel in commission from 1917 to 1919.
Talofa was built in 1910 as a private steam yacht of the same name by George Lawley & Son at Neponset, Massachusetts.
