[[Pete's Treats]]

CATEGORIES: Pete Rock albums, 1999 albums

Pete's Treats is the name of a compilation record of songs that have been sampled by the hip hop producer Pete Rock.  Following in the tradition of the Ultimate Breaks and Beats series, Pete's Treats contains an eclectic range of musical styles: mainly funk, jazz and soul. Most are relatively obscure tracks treasured by music conoisseurs and DJs. All of the songs (or elements thereof) on Pete's Treats have been sampled into new musical compositions by Pete Rock.
