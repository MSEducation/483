[[Needham Heights (MBTA station)]]

CATEGORIES: Needham, Massachusetts, Stations along Old Colony Railroad lines, MBTA Commuter Rail stations in Norfolk County, Massachusetts

Needham Heights is a  MBTA Commuter Rail station serving the Needham Line. It  is the terminus of the line, although this wasn't always the case. Needham Heights Station was built on a line that originally ran between Medfield Junction and what is today Newton Highlands station on the "D" Branch of the MBTA Green Line.
Daily inbound boardings: 509 (2009)
