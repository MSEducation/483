[[Valasai]]

CATEGORIES: Social groups of Pakistan, Sindhi tribes, Sindhi people

Valasai is a Sindhi clan of Meghwar tribe in Pakistan. There are many Muslim Valasai in Sindh but most are scheduled caste Hindus. Most Valasai live in the Thar Desert of Pakistan and many in Mithi, capital of Tharparkar District. Their main villages are  Kaloi, Diplo, Guriri Verhar, Tooh, Parkash villages in Mithi Tehsil.
