[[Azergues]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of France, Geography of Rhône (department), Rivers of Rhône-Alpes

The Azergues is a river in the Rhône department, in eastern France. It is a right tributary of the Saône, which it joins in Anse. Its source is in the Beaujolais hills, near Chénelette. The Azergues flows through the following towns: Lamure-sur-Azergues,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.geoportail.fr |title=Le portail des territoires et des citoyens - Géoportail |publisher=Geoportail.fr |date= |accessdate=2013-02-09[/tpl] Le Bois-d'Oingt, Chessy, Châtillon, Chazay-d'Azergues and Anse.http://sandre.eaufrance.fr/app/chainage/courdo/htm/U46-0400.php?cg=U46-0400
