[[Chiisagata District, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Districts in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Chiisagata|小県郡|Chiisagata-gun[/tpl] is a district located in Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of February 1, 2006, the district has an estimated population of 12,039. The total area is now down to 241.04 km².
There are only one town and one village left in the district
