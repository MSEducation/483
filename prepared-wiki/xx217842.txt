[[Tom Joyner Morning Show]]

CATEGORIES: American variety radio programs, TV One (US TV network) shows, 1994 American television series debuts, 2000s American television series, 2010s American television series

The Tom Joyner Morning Show is a nationally syndicated program, featuring host Tom Joyner and a team of commentators reporting and discussing the latest news and sports of the day, playing popular R&B songs from the 1970s through the 1990s as well as contemporary R&B hits, and an on-air soap opera, Itâ€™s Your World..
Southwest Airlines is a prominent sponsor of the radio show, especially Joyner's on-site remotes (called "Sky Shows"), and offers free round-trip airfare to any destination that Southwest flies as a recurring giveaway on the show.
It can be heard on mobile devices and on the Xbox 360 console via iHeartRadio.

==History==

In 1994, Joyner was signed by ABC Radio Networks (now Cumulus Media Networks) to host the show.
