[[Kuangqu, Datong]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Shanxi

Kuangqu ([tpl]zh|s=矿区 |t=礦區 |p=Kuàngqū |l=mining district[/tpl]), or Kuang District is a district of Datong, Shanxi province, China. As of 2002, it has a population of 430,000 residing in an area of [tpl]convert|62|km2|abbr=on[/tpl].
