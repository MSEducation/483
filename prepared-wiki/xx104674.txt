[[Swedish Board of Agriculture]]

CATEGORIES: Government agencies of Sweden, Government agencies established in 1991, Agricultural organizations based in Sweden, Jönköping County

The Swedish Board of Agriculture ([tpl]lang-sv|Statens jordbruksverk[/tpl], commonly known as Jordbruksverket) is a government agency in Sweden that answers to the Ministry of Agriculture. The agency headquarters is located in Jönköping.
It is responsible for agriculture, horticulture and reindeer husbandry, and functions as the Swedish government's expert authority in the field of agricultural and food policy.
