[[USS Doloma (SP-1062)]]

CATEGORIES: Patrol vessels of the United States Navy, World War I patrol vessels of the United States, 1907 ships, Great Lakes ships

USS ''Doloma'' (SP-1062) was a United States Navy patrol vessel in commission from 1917 until 1918 or 1919.
Doloma was built as a private motorboat of the same name in 1907.
