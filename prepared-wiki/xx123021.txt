[[Uncanned! The Best of Canned Heat]]

CATEGORIES: Canned Heat albums, 1994 compilation albums, EMI Records compilation albums

Uncanned! The Best of Canned Heat is two disc CD set issued in 1994, features various tracks from previous albums and some previously unreleased tracks.  Highlights include an alternate, longer take of "On the Road Again," and "Let's Work Together" in stereo for the first time ever.
