[[Kexbrough]]

CATEGORIES: Geography of Barnsley, Villages in South Yorkshire

 
Kexbrough is a village in the metropolitan borough of Barnsley (part of South Yorkshire, England), on the border with West Yorkshire.
Kexbrough lies directly to the west of Darton off the A637 road. It is located at approximately 53° 35' North, 1° 32' 30" West, at an elevation of around 110 metres above sea level.

==Main roads==

The main road that runs through the village is the A637 Huddersfield Road, although the other main roads that run through it are Churchfield Lane, which runs across the top of the M1 and Bence Lane, which also runs directly overhead on the motorway, except that this road is a housing estate.
