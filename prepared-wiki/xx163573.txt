Álvaro Alejandro Melo Silvera (born May 13, 1979, Montevideo, Uruguay) is a Uruguayan footballer currently playing for El Tanque Sisley of the Primera Division in Uruguay. Also played for Uruguayan clubs and Ukrainian club FC Chornomorets Odessa.
