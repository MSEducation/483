[[Bergen District Court]]

CATEGORIES: District courts of Norway, Organisations based in Bergen

Bergen District Court ([tpl]lang-no|Bergen tingrett[/tpl]) is a district court serving Bergen, Norway. It is co-located with Nordhordland District Court in Bergen Courthouse. Cases can be appealed to Gulating Court of Appeal.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.domstol.no/no/DOMSTOLER/Domstoloversikt-per-lagdomme/Gulating-lagdomme/Bergen-tingrett/ |title=Bergen tingrett |author=Norwegian National Courts Administration |accessdate=2 January 2011[/tpl]
