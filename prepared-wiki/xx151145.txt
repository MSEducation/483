[[Golden Star (Fort-de-France)]]

CATEGORIES: Association football clubs established in 1905, Football clubs in Martinique, 1905 establishments in Martinique

Golden Star de Fort-de-France is a football club of Martinique, playing in the town of Fort-de-France.
They play in the Martinique's first division, the Martinique Championnat National.
