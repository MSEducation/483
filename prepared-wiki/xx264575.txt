Vladem Lázaro Ruiz Quevedo, "Delém" (born April 15, 1935 in Sao Paulo, Brazil; died March 28, 2007 in Buenos Aires, Argentina) was a former Brazilian footballer that played for clubs in Brazil, Argentina and Chile.
