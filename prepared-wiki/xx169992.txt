[[Ohlone/Chynoweth (VTA)]]

CATEGORIES: Santa Clara Valley Transportation Authority light rail stations, Santa Clara Valley Transportation Authority bus stations, Railway stations in San Jose, California

Ohlone/Chynoweth is a light rail station operated by Santa Clara Valley Transportation Authority (VTA).  This station is served by  VTA's Alum Rock–Santa Teresa line and serves as the terminus of the little-used, stub Ohlone/Chynoweth–Almaden line, popularly known as the Almaden Shuttle. It was part of the original Guadalupé Line, the first segment of light rail from Santa Teresa to Tasman. Although the Almaden Shuttle also has a stop near it, Ohlone/Chynoweth is very close to the Westfield Oakridge shopping mall.

==Location==

Ohlone/Chynoweth station is located near the intersection of State Route 87 and State Route 85 in southern San Jose, California. Nearby Gunderson High School is served by this station.
