Sasha Pieterse (born February 17, 1996)[tpl]cite web | url=http://prettylittleliars.alloyentertainment.com/sasha-pieterse-birthday-wishes-sweet-sixteen | title=Sweet Sixteen: Happy Birthday Wishes for Pretty Little Liars Star Sasha Pieterse | publisher=Alloy Entertainment | date=February 17, 2012[/tpl] is an American actress and singer. She is known for her role as Alison DiLaurentis on ABC Family's series Pretty Little Liars.

==Early life==

Sasha Pieterse was born in Johannesburg, South Africa on February 17, 1996. Immigrating to the United States when she was three years old, she was raised in Las Vegas before moving to Los Angeles.[tpl]cite web|last=Pieterse|first=Sasha|title=Alison Dilaurentis by Sasah Pieterse - ABC Family|url=http://abcfamily.go.com/shows/pretty-little-liars/cast/alison-dilaurentis|publisher=ABCFamily|accessdate=20 March 2014[/tpl] She became accustomed to a career in entertainment at an early age, as her parents were a professional acrobatic dance team who performed all over the world. She soon met with an agent who quickly signed her to an acting and modeling contract. She started in the business at the age of 4 1/2 working on commercials and print work.[tpl]cn|date=April 2014[/tpl]
In 2004, she appeared in Stargate SG-1.  Pieterse has done numerous television commercials including long-running national spots for “Nesquick” and “Firestone Tires.” She was featured on a billboard for “Supercuts” that appeared throughout the country then she sang and modeled in the Macy’s Passport Fashion Show, performing throughout California.[tpl]cn|date=April 2014[/tpl]

==Career==

===Acting===

At 6 years old, Pieterse got her start in television, co-starring in 2002 as Buffy on the Warner Bros. comedy series Family Affair. She started to rise to fame as an actress when she played in House portraying a young girl named Andie with terminal cancer. Her performance in House M.D was later considered for a daytime Emmy in 2005.[tpl]cn|date=April 2014[/tpl] In 2005, she appeared in her first film The Adventures of Shark Boy and Lava Girl cast as Marissa the "Ice Princess" who is also the daughter of Mr. Electricidad.[tpl]cn|date=April 2014[/tpl] During 2006 she filmed for a feature starring Sarah Michelle Gellar called The Air I Breathe and she also landed a lead in a Hallmark Hall of Fame Movie as Maggie in Claire, both of which were released in 2007.[tpl]cn|date=April 2014[/tpl]
In 2006, Pieterse appeared in Good Luck Chuck as a young goth girl named Anisha who, after having her sexual advances rejected by Chuck, places a curse on him so that every single woman he sleeps with will break up with him and marry the next man who asks her out.[tpl]cn|date=April 2014[/tpl] Around this time, Pieterse also appeared as Millie Rose for two episodes of a short-lived series called Wanted with her former Family Affair co-star, Gary Cole. [tpl]cn|date=April 2014[/tpl]
In 2009, Pieterse was cast as a series regular on Pretty Little Liars as Alison DiLaurentis, the former "queen bee" of her high school clique and the most popular and beautiful girl in her school before her disappearance, a role she still plays today.[tpl]cn|date=April 2014[/tpl] Being only 18, she is the youngest actress on the show, as all of her co-stars are in their early to late 20s or older. [tpl]cn|date=April 2014[/tpl] On March 26, 2013, ABC Family renewed the show for a fifth season that will premiere on June 10, 2014.[tpl]cite web |url=https://twitter.com/TVGuideMagazine/status/316644179352645632 |title=Twitter / TVGuideMagazine: This just in from @ABCFamily: ... |date=March 26, 2013 |work=Twitter |accessdate=April 16, 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web |url=https://twitter.com/ABCFpll/status/316640833661190145 |title=Twitter / ABCFpll: #Ravenswood premieres this ... |work=Twitter |accessdate=April 16, 2013 |date=March 26, 2013[/tpl]
Other roles Pieterse has had include a 2009 appearance in Without a Trace as Daphne Stevens in the episode "Wanted" and a recurring role as Amanda Strazzulla, an abandoned daughter, in Heroes.[tpl]cn|date=April 2014[/tpl] In 2011, she appeared as Amy Loubalu in the Disney Channel Original Movie Geek Charming and also as a teenage girl in the movie X-Men: First Class. More recently, Pieterse has appeared in the film G.B.F. with one of the lead roles of Fawcett Brooks and in an episode of Hawaii Five-0 as a terrorist pupil named Dawn Hatfield.

Pieterse was signed to record label Dan Franklin Music.[tpl]cn|date=April 2014[/tpl] Her debut single "This Country is Bad Ass" was released on April 12, 2013.
