[[Palm Beach Central High School]]

CATEGORIES: High schools in Palm Beach County, Florida, Public high schools in Florida, Wellington, Florida

Palm Beach Central High School is a high school located in Wellington, Florida in the Palm Beach County School District. Opened in 2003, Palm Beach Central has more than 2990 enrolled students for the 2011-2012 school years.
