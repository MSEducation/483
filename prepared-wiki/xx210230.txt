Beijing, sometimes romanized as Peking,Loaned earlier via French Pékin. is the capital of the People's Republic of China and one of the most populous cities in the world. Beijing is the second largest Chinese city by urban population after Shanghai and is the nation's political, cultural, and educational center.[ref]
