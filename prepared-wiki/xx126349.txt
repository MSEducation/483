[[Cuttin' Capers (album)]]

CATEGORIES: 1959 albums, Doris Day albums, Columbia Records albums, English-language albums

Cuttin' Capers is a Doris Day album issued by Columbia Records, as catalog # CL-1232 in monaural and CS-8078 in stereo, on March 9, 1959. 
The album was combined with Day's 1961 album, Bright and Shiny, on a compact disc, issued on November 13, 2001 by Collectables Records.
