[[Beaumont, Belgium]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Hainaut (province)

Beaumont ([tpl]IPA-fr|bo.mɔ̃[/tpl]) is a Walloon municipality located in the Belgian province of Hainaut, on the border with France. On January 1, 2012 Beaumont had a total population of 7,060. The total area is 92.97 km². The Tour Salamandre, a 11th-century donjon, is the most remarkable remains of the ancient fortifications of Beaumont.
