[[Ahmadabad, Qareh Poshtelu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Ahmadabad ([tpl]lang-fa|احمداباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Aḩmadābād; also known as Akhmedabad)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3052013|Ahmadabad[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Pain Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 25, in 7 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
