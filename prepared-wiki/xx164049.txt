[[Begone Dull Care]]

CATEGORIES: 1949 films, Canadian films, Quebec films, Films without speech, Canadian animated films, Canadian short films, Films directed by Norman McLaren, Visual music, Jazz films, Genie Award winning films, Drawn-on-film animated films, National Film Board of Canada animated shorts, 1940s animated short films, 1949 animated films

Begone Dull Care (1949) is a visual music animated film directed by Norman McLaren and Evelyn Lambart.
Using drawn on film animation, McLaren and Lambart paint and scratch directly onto film stock to create a visual representation of Oscar Peterson's jazz music. The film is produced by the National Film Board of Canada.

==Honours and awards==

Designated and preserved as a "masterwork" by the Audio-Visual Preservation Trust of Canada, a charitable non-profit organization dedicated to promoting the preservation of Canada’s audio-visual heritage.[tpl]cite web |url=http://avtrust.ca/masterworks/2005/en_film_2.htm |title=Begone Dull Care  |accessdate=2008-02-29 |work=Audio-Visual Preservation Trust of Canada Web site[/tpl][tpl]dead|date=May 2013[/tpl] Winner of a Special Genie Award for experimental filmmaking.
At the 1st Berlin International Film Festival it won the Silver Medal (Culture Films and Documentaries) award.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.berlinale.de/en/archiv/jahresarchive/1951/03_preistr_ger_1951/03_Preistraeger_1951.html |title=1st Berlin International Film Festival: Prize Winners |work=berlinale.de[/tpl]
The Canadian electronic music duo Junior Boys named their 2009 album Begone Dull Care for the film, which was reported to influenced its conception and creation."Junior Boys to Release Begone Dull Care in April", Paste, January 20, 2009.

==Opening titles==

McLaren opened the film with titles in seven different languages: English, French (Caprice en Couleurs), Spanish (Fantasía en Colores), Hindi (रंग बाहार; Raṅga Bāhāra), Italian (Capriccio a Colori), Russian (Красочная Фантазия; Krasočnaja Fanatzija), and German (Trübsal Ade!).http://www.nfb.ca/film/begone_dull_care/
