[[Jamie Drummond]]

CATEGORIES: 1971 births, Living people, People from Edinburgh, Sommeliers, People educated at George Watson's College, Alumni of the University of East London, Scottish emigrants to Canada

Jamie Drummond (born November 7, 1971) is a Canadian sommelier, wine consultant, and writer who acts as Senior Editor and Director of Programs for Toronto not-for-profit Good Food Revolution.[tpl]cite web|last=Aronovitch |first=Davida |url=http://www.torontolife.com/daily/daily-dish/rumours-rumblings/2009/09/21/closing-in-on-the-sale-of-the-jamie-kennedy-wine-bar/ |title=Closing in on the sale of the Jamie Kennedy Wine Bar | Rumours & Rumblings |publisher=torontolife.com |date=September 21, 2009 |accessdate=October 18, 2011[/tpl]  Drummond was born in Edinburgh, Scotland, and is a former pupil of George Watson's College.[tpl]dead link|date=October 2011[/tpl]  Following this Drummond went on to read Cultural Studies at the University of East London, UK.[tpl]Citation needed|date=November 2009[/tpl] 
Drummond began working as a sommelier for UK Chef Andrew Radford's Michelin Guide recognised Atrium restaurant in Edinburgh, Scotland.[tpl]cite web|author=Photo by Chloé Dulude |url=http://enroute.aircanada.com/en/articles/spin-the-bottle |title=enRoute | Spin the Bottle |publisher=Enroute.aircanada.com |date=March 1, 2009 |accessdate=October 18, 2011[/tpl][tpl]dead link|date=October 2011[/tpl] During this time he studied with the United Kingdom's Wine & Spirit Education Trust (WSET).[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.sommelierservice.com/show_info.php?page_id=7&osCsid=cb0661d386cf22474e8e4c98de31c588 |title=Sommelier Service |publisher=Sommelier Service |accessdate=October 18, 2011[/tpl]http://www.royalfair.org/files/Wine%20Competition%20Judges%20List.pdf In 1997 Drummond emigrated to Canada and began working as Sommelier at Toronto's Granite Club.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.torontolife.com/features/nose-job/ |title=Nose Job |publisher=torontolife.com |date=March 30, 2011 |accessdate=October 18, 2011[/tpl]  In 2004 he was hired by Jamie Kennedy and was in control of the selection and purchasing of wines for Kennedy's restaurants[tpl]dead link|date=October 2011[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.citybites.ca/archives/CityBites_Nov08.pdf |title=CityBItes TEMP (Page 1) |format=PDF |accessdate=October 18, 2011[/tpl] until late 2009.  For some time it was rumoured that he was the chef behind Charlie's Burgers in Toronto,[tpl]cite web|author=Susan Sampson Food Writer |url=http://www.thestar.com/living/article/608942 |title=Delicious, mysterious, covert cuisine |work=Toronto Star  |date=March 27, 2009 |accessdate=October 18, 2011[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www2.macleans.ca/tag/charlies-burgers/ |title=Charlie’s Burgers – Topics |publisher=Macleans.ca |date=March 30, 2009 |accessdate=October 18, 2011[/tpl] which was not the case.[tpl]cite web|author=james chatto |url=http://www.theglobeandmail.com/life/food-and-wine/trends/trends-features/revealed-charlie-burgers-true-identity/article2107039/ |title=Revealed: Charlie Burger's true identity |work=Globe and Mail |location=Canada  |accessdate=October 18, 2011[/tpl] Drummond has served as a wine judge for Intervin, BioVino, Ontario Wine Awards, Royal Agricultural Winter Fair.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.intervin.ca/sitepages/?cid=323&cn=MEET%20THE%20JUDGES&an=MEET%20THE%20JUDGES |title=Intervin |publisher=Intervin.ca |accessdate=October 18, 2011[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.tastecelebration.ca/judges.html |title=Taste!
