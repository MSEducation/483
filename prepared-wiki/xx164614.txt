[[Mick McCauley]]

CATEGORIES: League of Ireland players, Bohemian F.C. players

Mick McCauley was an Irish soccer player during the 1960s.
McCauley was a defender who played for Bohemians amongst others during his career in the League of Ireland.Bohemian FC match programme, Vol. 44, no. 20 McCauley played alongside the likes of Billy Young and Willie Browne during his time at the then amateur Bohs.
