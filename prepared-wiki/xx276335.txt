[[(39636) 1995 BQ2]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1995, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(39636) 1995 BQ2 is a main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on January 29, 1995.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
