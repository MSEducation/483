[[Natalya Melekhina]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Soviet female cyclists, UCI World Champions

Natalya Melekhina (also written as Natalia Melikhina) is a retired Soviet Union female road cyclist. She became World Champion in the women's team time trial at the UCI Road World Championships in 1987 and finished second in 1990.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.memoire-du-cyclisme.eu/feminines/mondial_clmpe_1987.php|work=memoire-du-cyclisme.eu|accessdate=10 August 2013|title= Championnat du Monde féminin CLM par équipes 1989[/tpl] [tpl]fr icon[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.memoire-du-cyclisme.eu/feminines/mondial_clmpe_1987.php|work=memoire-du-cyclisme.eu|accessdate=10 August 2013|title= Championnat du Monde féminin CLM par équipes 1990[/tpl] [tpl]fr icon[/tpl] In 1990 she also won stage 5 in Tour Cycliste Féminin de la Drôme and finished second in the general classiciation.[tpl]cite book|title=Ljubow Pogowitschnikowa|url=http://www.cyclingarchives.com/coureurfiche.php?coureurid=45610|publisher=cyclingarchives.com|accessdate=26 July 2013[/tpl]
