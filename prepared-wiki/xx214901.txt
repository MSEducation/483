[[Demofest]]

CATEGORIES: Music festivals in Europe, Festivals in Bosnia and Herzegovina, Bosnia and Herzegovina music

Demofest is a music festival held in Banja Luka, Bosnia and Herzegovina, where pop/rock demo bands from the Balkan region compete for prizes. The first Demofest was organized in 2008, between July 19 and 26, achieving notable popularity among the media and general public. Demofest is to continue on annual basis.
