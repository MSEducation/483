[[Amy Stiller]]

CATEGORIES: 1961 births, Actresses from New York City, American film actresses, American stand-up comedians, American television actresses, Jewish comedians, Jewish American actresses, Living people, People from Manhattan, Circle in the Square Theatre School alumni, American women comedians

Amy Stiller (born August 9, 1961) is an American actress, stand-up comedian and Bikram Yoga instructor. She was born in New York City, New York, the daughter of the comedy duo Jerry Stiller and Anne Meara (Stiller and Meara), and the older sister of actor Ben Stiller. She married Rodger Bumpass, the voice of Squidward Tentacles from the Nickelodeon animated series SpongeBob SquarePants, in 2008.
