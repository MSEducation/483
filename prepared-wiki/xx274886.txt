[[Pablo Centurión]]

CATEGORIES: Possibly living people, Year of birth missing, Paraguayan footballers, Paraguay international footballers, Association football goalkeepers, Cerro Porteño footballers, Millonarios Fútbol Club footballers, Atlético Nacional footballers, Independiente Santa Fe footballers, 1950 FIFA World Cup players

Pablo Centurión is a Paraguayan football goalkeeper who played for Paraguay in the 1950 FIFA World Cup.1950 FIFA World Cup Brazil He also played for Cerro Porteño.
In 1951, Centurión joined Fútbol Profesional Colombiano side Boca Juniors de Cali. He played for Boca Juniors for seven years, before moving to Millonarios, Atlético Nacional and Santa Fe.[tpl]cite web|publisher=El Colombiano|author=Carreno Suarez, Alfredo|title=Cápsulas: Rincón del recuerdo|trans_title=Capsules: Corner of memories|url=http://www.elcolombiano.com/proyectos/capsulas/historico/2002/abril/13042002.htm|language=Spanish|date=13 April 2002[/tpl]
