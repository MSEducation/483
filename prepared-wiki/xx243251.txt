[[Paul Lynch (canoeist)]]

CATEGORIES: 1967 births, Australian canoeists, Canoeists at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Living people, Olympic canoeists of Australia

Paul Lynch (born 18 December 1967) is an Australian sprint canoer who competed in the mid-1990s. He finished ninth in the K-4 1000 m event at the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta.
