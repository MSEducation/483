[tpl]nihongo|Katsutomo Kaneishi|金石 勝智|Kaneishi Katsutomo|born November 21, 1968 in Osaka Prefecture, Japan[/tpl] is a Japanese racing driver. He has competed in such series as the Japanese Touring Car Championship, Deutsche Tourenwagen Masters and Formula Nippon.
