[[Laboulaye, Córdoba]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Córdoba Province, Argentina, Populated places established in 1886, Cities in Argentina

Laboulaye is a city in the southeast of the province of Córdoba, Argentina. It has 19,908 inhabitants as per the [tpl]census-ar|2001[/tpl]. It lies on National Route 7, near the provincial borders of Santa Fe and Buenos Aires, about 315 km south from Córdoba City and 285 km west from Rosario.
