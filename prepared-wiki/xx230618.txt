[[2008–09 UEFA Cup group stage]]

CATEGORIES: 2008–09 UEFA Cup

The group stage of the 2008–09 UEFA Cup is the second stage of the competition proper.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.uefa.com/multimediafiles/download/regulations/uefa/others/70/22/70/702270_download.pdf |title=Regulations of the UEFA Cup 2008/09 |accessdate=24 October 2008 |format=PDF |publisher=UEFA |pages=8–9 | archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20081031154353/http://www.uefa.com/multimediafiles/download/regulations/uefa/others/70/22/70/702270_download.pdf| archivedate= 31 October 2008 | deadurl= no[/tpl] The draw took place on 7 October 2008 at UEFA headquarters in Nyon, Switzerland.[tpl]cite news |title=Tough groups for former champions |url=http://www.uefa.com/competitions/uefacup/news/kind=1/newsid=759113.html |publisher=UEFA |date=7 October 2008 |accessdate=24 October 2008 | archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20081029024543/http://www.uefa.com/competitions/uefacup/news/kind=1/newsid=759113.html| archivedate= 29 October 2008 | deadurl= no[/tpl] Group stage matches began on 23 October 2008 and concluded on 18 December 2008.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.uefa.com/uefa/keytopics/kind=64/newsid=551134.html |title=2008/09 Draw and match calendar |accessdate=24 October 2008 |date=1 June 2008 |publisher=UEFA |archiveurl = http://web.archive.org/web/20080520110807/http://www.uefa.com/uefa/keytopics/kind=64/newsid=551134.html  |archivedate = 20 May 2008[/tpl] The top three teams in each group progressed to the Round of 32, to be joined by the eight third-place finishers from the Champions League group stage.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.uefa.com/competitions/uefacup/format/index.html |title=Competition format |accessdate=24 October 2008 |date=13 July 2005 |publisher=UEFA | archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20081029211926/http://www.uefa.com/competitions/uefacup/format/index.html| archivedate= 29 October 2008 | deadurl= no[/tpl]

==Seedings==

The following teams qualified for the group stage:
Pot 1 contained teams ranked between 2 and 39: Pot 2 held teams ranked 40 to 71, Pot 3 held teams ranked 72 to 88, and Pot 4 teams ranked 91 to 121 together with unranked teams from England and Spain. Pot 5 held the remaining teams.
Dinamo Zagreb, and all the Pot 5 teams, had beaten higher-ranked teams in the First Round. Lech Poznań and Žilina had also beaten higher-ranked teams in the Second Qualifying Round. Seven of the eight teams in Pot 5 qualified for the Round of 32, Žilina being the only exception. In contrast, only five of the eight teams in Pot 1 managed to do the same, as Sevilla, Benfica and Schalke 04 were eliminated.
