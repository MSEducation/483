Bernhard Lehmann (born January 11, 1948 in Großräschen, Brandenburg) is an East German bobsledder who competed from the late 1970s to the late 1980s. Competing in three Winter Olympics, he won four medals with one gold (Four-man: 1976), two silvers (Two-man and four-man: both 1984), and one bronze (Two-man: 1988).
Lehmann also won two medals in the four-man event at the FIBT World Championships with a gold in 1985 and a silver in 1982.
