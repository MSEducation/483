[[Silichy]]

CATEGORIES: Ski areas and resorts in Belarus, Geography of Minsk Region, Visitor attractions in Minsk Region

Silichy or Silichi ([tpl]lang-ru|Силичи[/tpl], [tpl]lang-be|Сілічы[/tpl]) is a ski resort situated in Lahojsk (or Logoysk), Belarus, located 30 km (18.64 mi) away from Minsk.
Silichy was officially open by the President of Belarus Aleksandr Lukashenko on January 29, 2005. The length of 3 routes for skiing is 2.5 km (1.55 mi), its total capacity is 1000 skiers per day and it has a 4-chair elevator. There are a parking lot for 500 cars, hotel accommodations and hire of skiing equipment.
The mountain skiing season starts in December and lasts until March (until there is no snow).[tpl]en icon[/tpl] Postcard from Belarus Radiostation "Belarus", April 24, 2008
