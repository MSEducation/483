Stuart Abbot (born 21 June 1986) is a Scottish former professional footballer who currently plays in midfield for junior side Lochee United.

==Career==

Abbot, born in Dundee, began his career with hometown club Dundee United, making his senior debut in April 2006 as a late substitute in an SPL match at Inverness CT. Offered a new one-year contract shortly afterwards,[tpl]cite news|url=http://sport.scotsman.com/Register.aspx?ReturnURL=http%3A%2F%2Fsport.scotsman.com%2Fdundeeunitedfc%2FTannadice-deals.2770511.jp|title=Tannadice deals|date=26 April 2006|publisher=The Scotsman|accessdate=14 April 2009[/tpl] Abbot made his full debut at home the following week, although his third appearance ended early due to injury. It proved to be Abbot's last appearance in senior football, quitting professional football in September to pursue full-time university studies and he was released from his United contract,[tpl]cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/d/dundee_utd/5314250.stm|title=Dundee Utd consider African duo |date=4 September 2006|publisher=BBC Sport|accessdate=14 April 2009[/tpl] subsequently joining local side Carnoustie Panmure. Abbot moved to current club Lochee United in July 2008.
