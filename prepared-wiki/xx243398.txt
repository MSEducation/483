[[Egira tibori]]

CATEGORIES: Orthosiini, Insects of Romania, Insects of Greece, Insects of Bulgaria, Insects of Turkey, Moths of Israel

Egira tibori is a species of moth of the Noctuidae family. It is endemic to the eastern part of the Mediterranean, the Balkans, Turkey, Israel, Lebanon and Jordan.
Adults are on wing from February to May. There is one generation per year.
The larvae probably feed on various herbaceous plants and trees.
