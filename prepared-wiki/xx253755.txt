[[Catriona Power]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, Dublin camogie players

Catríona Power is a camogie player and Occupational Therapist who won Junior All-Ireland championship medals with Dublin in 2005 and 2006.  She also captained Naomh Mearnóg from Portmarnock to their first Dublin senior championship title in 2009, the first senior title in any code for the club.
