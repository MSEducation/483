[[Richard Nash (MP died 1395)]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing, 1395 deaths, 14th-century English people, People from Hereford, Members of the Parliament of England (pre-1707)

Richard Nash or Ash (died 1394/1395), of Hereford, was an English politician.
He was a Member (MP) of the Parliament of England for Hereford in January 1377, 1379, January 1380, 1381 and October 1383 and for Herefordshire November 1384, February 1388 and November 1390.http://www.historyofparliamentonline.org/volume/1386-1421/member/nash-%28ash%29-richard-13945
