[[Ariophantidae]]

CATEGORIES: Ariophantidae

Ariophantidae is a taxonomic family of air-breathing land snails and semi-slugs, terrestrial pulmonate gastropod mollusks in the superfamily Helicarionoidea (according to the taxonomy of the Gastropoda by Bouchet & Rocroi, 2005).[tpl]Bouchet 2005[/tpl]

==Distribution==

Distribution of Ariophantidae include India and south-eastern Asia.

==Anatomy==

Snails within this family make and use love darts made of chitin.
