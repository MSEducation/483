[[Gmina Goszczyn]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Grójec County

Gmina Goszczyn is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Grójec County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Goszczyn, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|16|km|mi|0[/tpl] south of Grójec and [tpl]convert|55|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|56.99|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 2,943.

==Villages==

Gmina Goszczyn contains the villages and settlements of Bądków, Bądków-Kolonia, Długowola, Goszczyn, Jakubów, Józefów, Modrzewina, Nowa Długowola, Olszew, Romanów and Sielec.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Goszczyn is bordered by the gminas of Belsk Duży, Jasieniec, Mogielnica and Promna.
