[[Gmina Murów]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Opole Voivodeship, Opole County

Gmina Murów, German Gemeinde Murow is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Opole County, Opole Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Its seat is the village of Murów (Murow), which lies approximately [tpl]convert|23|km|mi|0[/tpl] north of the regional capital Opole.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|159.7|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 5,912. Since 2009 the commune, like much of the region, has been bilingual in German and Polish.
The gmina contains part of the protected area called Stobrawa Landscape Park.
