Let's Stay Together is an American romantic comedy television series created by Jacque Edmonds Cofer. It premiered on BET on January 11, 2011. The title of the series refers to the Al Green landmark 1972 song of the same name.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.thefutoncritic.com/news/2011/01/06/bet-presents-lets-stay-together-a-new-original-series-that-gives-an-updated-urban-perspective-on-love-and-marriage-premiering-tuesday-january-11-at-1100-pm-323113/20110106bet01/|title=BET Presents "Let's Stay Together," a New Original Series that Gives An Updated, Urban Perspective on Love and Marriage Premiering Tuesday, January 11 at 11:00 P.M.|date=January 6, 2011|work=The Futon Critic|accessdate=January 7, 2011[/tpl] The series premiere drew 4.4 million viewers.[tpl]cite web|last=Seidman |first=Robert |url=http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2011/01/12/cable-debut-of-the-game-ranks-as-1-ad-supported-scriptes-series-premiere-in-cable-history/78387 |title=Cable Debut of ‘The Game’ Ranks as #1 Ad-Supported Scriptes Series Premiere in Cable History |publisher=Tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com |date=January 12, 2011 |accessdate=April 3, 2011[/tpl][tpl]cite news|last=Stelter|first=Brian|title=‘The Game’ Is a Winner, Helped by BET Loyalists|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2011/01/17/business/media/17bet.html|accessdate=April 3, 2011|newspaper=The New York Times|date=January 16, 2011[/tpl] Initially, Soul Food star Malinda Williams was cast in the lead role of Stacy.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.tvsquad.com/2010/04/07/bet-picks-up-lets-stay-together/ |title=BET Commits to 'Let's Stay Together' |publisher=Tvsquad.com |date= |accessdate=April 3, 2011[/tpl] For undisclosed reasons, she was recast with Nadine Ellis. On April 20, 2013, BET announced that the series was renewed for a second season which aired 22 episodes starting in January 2012.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bet.com/shows/lets-stay-together/news/let-s-stay-together-returning-in-january-2012.html |title="Let's Stay Together" Returns in January|publisher=BET-Staff |date=April 20, 2011 |accessdate=April 20, 2011[/tpl] For its second season, Erica Hubbard appeared infrequently due to her pregnancy. New cast member Kyla Pratt (formerly of UPN's One on One) joined the cast portraying Crystal, Charles and Kita's cousin. At the 2012 BET Upfront on April 13, 2012, it was revealed that the show has been renewed for a third season.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.deadline.com/2012/04/bet-renews-comedy-series-the-game-and-lets-stay-together/|title=BET Renews Comedy Series ‘The Game’ And ‘Let’s Stay Together’|work=Deadline |date=April 10, 2012 |accessdate=April 21, 2012[/tpl] The third season premiered on March 26, 2013. In April 2013, BET Networks announced the show had been renewed for a fourth season which premiered on March 4, 2014.[tpl]cite web|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|title=BET Renews ‘The Game’, Officially Picks Up ‘Being Mary Jane’ To Series, Revives ‘ComicView’, Orders T.D. Jakes Talk Show|url=http://www.deadline.com/2013/04/bet-officially-picks-up-being-mary-jane-to-series-revives-comicview-orders-t-d-jakes-talk-show-at-upfront/|work=Deadline Hollywood|accessdate=September 15, 2013|date=April 2, 2013[/tpl]
