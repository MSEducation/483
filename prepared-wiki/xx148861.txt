Ljubica Christina Vukicevic (Serbian: Ljubica Kristina Vukičević, Љубица Кристина Вукићевић; born 18 June 1987 in Lørenskog) is a Norwegian hurdler of Serbian descent. She represents Ski IL, and is coached by her father Petar Vukićević, who participated for Yugoslavia in the 1980 Summer Olympics in Moscow.Men 110m Hurdles Olympic Games Moscow 1980 
Her gold medal in the 2003 European Youth Summer Olympic Festival in Paris was her first international medal.
