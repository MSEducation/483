[[DeviceLogics]]

CATEGORIES: Computer companies of the United States, Companies based in Utah, 2002 establishments in Utah

DeviceLogics is a startup company in Lindon, Utah, USA, founded in November 2002. Bryan Sparks co-founded DeviceLogics, and acquired DR-DOS from the Canopy Group, a Utah technology venture group. Copies of DR DOS 7.03 have been licensed and distributed by this company.[tpl]cite journal|last=Conner|first=Deni|title=Start-up revives once-vaunted DR-DOS|magazine=Network World|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=SBkEAAAAMBAJ&pg=PT9|date=December 16, 2002[/tpl]

==History of DR-DOS==

In 1994, Bryan Sparks, with help from Novell's Ray Noorda, founded Caldera, Incorporated. Caldera bought DR DOS from Novell in 1996.
