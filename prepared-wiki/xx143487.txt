[[HMS Sulham (M2732)]]

CATEGORIES: Ham-class minesweepers, Ships built in Scotland, 1955 ships, Cold War minesweepers of the United Kingdom, Royal Navy ship names, Ham-class minesweepers of the French Navy

HMS ''Sulham'' was a [tpl]sclass2|Ham|minesweeper||ship[/tpl] of the Royal Navy.
Their names were all chosen from villages ending in -ham. The minesweeper was named after Sulham in Berkshire.
