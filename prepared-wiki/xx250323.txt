[[John Helmer (journalist)]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, American people of Australian descent, American journalists, 1946 births

John Helmer (born 1946) is a Moscow-based blogger and journalist, known for his controversial investigative work on Russian business sector.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.mineweb.com/mineweb/view/mineweb/en/page675?oid=38098&sn=Detail |title=An interview with Mineweb Russian correspondent John Helmer |publisher=Mineweb |date=9 Oct 2007 |accessdate=9 January 2010[/tpl] (First published in Norilsk Nickel Magazine.)
On November 16, 2001 John Helmer delivered a lecture “Stealing The Truth – How To Read, And Not To Read, The Press In Russia” at the ELE public forum in Moscow.[tpl]cite web |url= http://www.elemoscow.net/Speakers-List.html|title= ELE Speakers List[/tpl]
