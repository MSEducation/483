[[Charlton Halt railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Disused railway stations in Oxfordshire, Former London and North Western Railway stations, Railway stations opened in 1905, Railway stations closed in 1926

This article is about the disused railway station in Oxfordshire. For the disused railway station in Bristol, see Charlton Halt railway station (Bristol).

Charlton Halt was a railway station on the Varsity Line [tpl]convert|1|mi|km[/tpl] north of the village of Charlton-on-Otmoor. The London and North Western Railway opened the halt in 1905 and the London, Midland and Scottish Railway closed it in 1926.
