[[Leonid A. Umansky]]

CATEGORIES: IEEE Edison Medal recipients, 1891 births, 1957 deaths

Leonid Alexandrovich Umansky was an American electrical engineer and a recipient of the IEEE Edison Medal for "outstanding contribution to the electrification of industry through the application of electrical machines, devices, and systems to automatic process machinery; and for his inspiration, leadership, and teaching of men in this work".
