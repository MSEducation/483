[[Cell Cycle (journal)]]

CATEGORIES: Molecular and cellular biology journals, Monthly journals, Publications established in 2002, English-language journals, Landes Bioscience academic journals

Cell Cycle is a peer-reviewed scientific journal covering cell biology. The editor-in-chief is Mikhail V. Blagosklonny (Roswell Park Cancer Institute). Cell Cycle is abstracted and indexed in Medline/PubMed and the Science Citation Index Expanded.[tpl]Cite web |url=http://www.landesbioscience.com/journals/cc/ |title=About Cell Cycle |publisher=Landes Biosciences |accessdate=25 April 2011[/tpl]
