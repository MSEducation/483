Artem Savin ([tpl]lang-ua|Артем Сергійович Савін[/tpl]; born 20 January 1981 in Voroshylovhrad, Ukrainian SSR) is a professional Ukrainian football defender who plays for FC Olimpik Donetsk in the Ukrainian First League. He was acquired from Zorya Luhanks in during the 2008–09 transfer season.
