[[Wokingham Council election, 2000]]

CATEGORIES: English local elections, 2000, Wokingham Council elections

The 2000 Wokingham Council election took place on 4 May 2000 to elect members of Wokingham Unitary Council in Berkshire, England. One third of the council was up for election and the Conservative party lost overall control of the council to no overall control.[tpl]cite web| url = http://news.bbc.co.uk/hi/english/static/uk_politics/vote2000/locals/148.stm | title = Wokingham | accessdate = 9 January 2010 | work = BBC News Online[/tpl]
