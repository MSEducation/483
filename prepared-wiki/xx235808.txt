[[Allan L. Drash]]

CATEGORIES: 1931 births, 2009 deaths, American endocrinologists, Vanderbilt University alumni, University of Virginia alumni

Allan Drash was a pediatric endocrinologist and former president of the American Diabetes Association and was the second president of ISGD (now the International Society for Pediatric and Adolescent Diabetes for two terms from 1981-1984-1987.
