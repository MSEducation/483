[[Daniel Lloyd (cyclist)]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 births, Living people, English cyclists, People from Christchurch, Dorset

Daniel Lloyd (born August 11, 1980[tpl]cite web|url=http://autobus.cyclingnews.com/results/2008/teams2008/SKT.shtml|title=Team's database, Cyclingnews|publisher=Cyclingnews.com|accessdate=2008-04-28[/tpl] is a retired English[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.anpost.ie/AnPost/content/Cycling/Team/Team/Daniel+Lloyd.htm|title=Daniel Lloyd (profile)|publisher=An Post[/tpl] professional road racing cyclist from Christchurch, Dorset.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cyclingweekly.co.uk/news/latest/497664/hammond-and-lloyd-join-garmin-cervelo-for-2011.html|title=Cycling Weekly News|date=1 September 2010|publisher=IPC Media Ltd.[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.dailypeloton.com/displayarticle.asp?pk=12632|title=Cycling News Roundup|date=7 May 2008|publisher=Daily Peloton[/tpl] He was a member of the Irish [tpl]ct|SKT|2008[/tpl] team in 2008. In 2009 he joined the [tpl]ct|CTT|2009[/tpl]. After his contract expired at the end of 2011, he signed for the British [tpl]ct|SGS|2012[/tpl].[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.cyclingnews.com/news/daniel-lloyd-to-team-ig-sigma-sport-for-2012|title=Daniel Lloyd to Team IG–Sigma Sport for 2012|date=27 January 2012|work=Cycling News|publisher=Future Publishing Limited|accessdate=27 January 2012[/tpl] In November 2012, Lloyd announced his retirement from professional cycling, and he became assistant directeur sportif at IG-Sigma Sport.[tpl]cite web|url=http://roadcyclinguk.com/news/racing-news/daniel-lloyd-moves-into-team-management.html|title=Daniel Lloyd moves into team management|publisher=RoadCyclingUK|first=George|last=Scott|date=22 November 2012|accessdate=27 November 2012[/tpl]
Lloyd won the Vuelta a Extremadura overall in 2008 and stages of Tour of Siam and Tour of Qinghai Lake in 2006.
Lloyd also works as a broadcaster for Eurosport's cycling coverage and the Global Cycling Network.[tpl]cite web |url=https://www.anpost.ie/AnPost/IrishCycling/News_Events/Events/Hard+road+and+stepping+stones.htm |title=Hard roads and stepping stones |last=Kelly |first=Cillian |website=An Post |accessdate=17 April 2014[/tpl]
