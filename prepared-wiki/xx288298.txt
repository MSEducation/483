[[Phil Goodlad]]

CATEGORIES: BBC sports presenters and reporters, People from Shetland, Living people, Scottish people

Phil Goodlad is a Scottish television and radio sports reporter currently working for the BBC. He is from Yell in the Shetland Islands. He is usually on heard on Good Morning Scotland and also appears on Reporting Scotland as sports correspondent. He previously worked for local Orkney and Shetland radio stations.[tpl]cite web|url= http://www.shetlandtimes.co.uk/2008/10/24/isles-views-18 |title=Isles views 24.10.08 |date=24 October 2008|work=Shetland Times|accessdate=13 August 2013[/tpl]
During his time as golf correspondent, he was once cut off mid-sentence which later led to Reporting Scotland presenter Sally Magnusson flipping out on air.
