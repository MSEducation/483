[[Cortifoam]]

CATEGORIES: Drugs

Cortifoam (Hydrocortisone Acetate USP) is a topical corticosteroid commonly used to treat Inflammatory Bowel Disease, especially Ulcerative Colitis.http://www.medbroadcast.com/drug_info_details.asp?brand_name_id=736 The drug is in the form of a foam that is sprayed from a canister into an applicator syringe, which is then administered through the anus. The drug is targeted to relieve inflammation of the rectum, but may also be used for inflammation elsewhere in the colon.http://www.medicinenet.com/hydrocortisone-rectal_foam/article.htmDosage Dosage is usually determined according to the severity of the disease.
