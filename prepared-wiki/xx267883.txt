[[Elachista anserinella]]

CATEGORIES: Elachistidae, Animals described in 1839, Moths of Europe

Elachista anserinella is a moth of the Elachistidae family. It is found from Scandinavia and the Baltic region to the Pyrenees, Italy and Greece and from France to Russia.
The wingspan is [tpl]convert|10|-|11|mm[/tpl]. Adults are on wing from May to June.Swedish Moths
The larvae feed on Brachypodium pinnatum. They mine the leaves of their host plant.[tpl]cite web|url= http://www.bladmineerders.nl/minersf/lepidopteramin/elachista/argentella/argentella.htm|title=Elachista anserinella Zeller, 1839|publisher=Bladmineerders.nl |accessdate=September 23, 2011[/tpl] Larvae can be found in late spring.
