Karen Elizabeth Stives (born November 3, 1950) is an American eventing competitor and olympic champion.
At the 1984 Olympic Games in Los Angeles, Stives anchored the United States team that received a gold medal in Team eventing, and also an individual silver medal."1984 Summer Olympics – Los Angeles, United States – Equestrian" – databaseOlympics.com (Retrieved on April 28, 2008)
