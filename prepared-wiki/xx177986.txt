[[Fast Stories... from Kid Coma]]

CATEGORIES: 1995 debut albums, Truly albums, Albums produced by Adam Kasper, Sub Pop albums

Fast Stories... from Kid Coma is the 1995 debut album of the grunge band Truly.  "Hot Summer 1991" was featured in the 1997 compilation album Subject to Change: Artists for a Hate-Free America.
