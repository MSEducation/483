[[KSKB]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Iowa

KSKB is a Christian radio station licensed to Brooklyn, Iowa, broadcasting on 99.1 MHz FM.  KSKB serves East Central Iowa including; Cedar Rapids, Iowa City, Marshalltown, Pella, Newton and Oskaloosa.  The station is owned by Florida Public Radio, Inc. KSKB fcc.gov. Accessed July 27, 2012

==Translators==

KSKB is also heard in Waterloo, Iowa through a translator on 99.7 FM.
