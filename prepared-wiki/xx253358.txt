Horace Robert Milan (April 7, 1894 in Linden, Tennessee – June 29, 1955 in Texarkana, Arkansas), was a Major League Baseball player who played outfielder from [tpl]by|1915[/tpl] to [tpl]by|1917[/tpl]. He would play for the Washington Senators but spent far more time in the minors leagues, where he would play until 1927. His brother, Clyde Milan, was his teammate with the Senators.
