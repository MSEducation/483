[[Lagoon-A-Beach]]

CATEGORIES: Buildings and structures in Davis County, Utah, Visitor attractions in Davis County, Utah, Water parks in the United States

Lagoon-A-Beach is a water park that is situated inside the Lagoon Amusement Park located in Farmington, Utah.  Lagoon-A-Beach currently has ten water slides that fall into three main categories: tube slides, body slides, and drop slides.  Its name is a play on Laguna Beach

==Tube Slides==

These slides must be ridden with an inner tube. The inner tubes available at Ruby's tubes can be rented for five dollars a day and when returned, will be given a coupon for a 32 oz. drink.
