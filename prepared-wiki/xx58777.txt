IDEO was formed in 1991 by a merger of four established design firms: David Kelley Design (founded by  Stanford University professor David Kelley), London-based Moggridge Associates and San Francisco's ID Two (both founded by British-born Bill Moggridge), and Matrix Product Design (founded by Mike Nuttall).[ref]
