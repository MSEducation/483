[[Yasuko Matsuyuki]]

CATEGORIES: 1972 births, Living people, Japanese film actresses, Japanese television actors, People from Saga Prefecture, Stardust Promotion artists

[tpl]Nihongo|Yasuko Matsuyuki|松雪 泰子|Yasuko Matsuyuki|born November 28, 1972 in Saga Prefecture[/tpl] is a Japanese actress and singer signed to Stardust Promotion.

==Biography==

Yasuko Matsuyuki was born in 1972 in Tosu, Saga. In 1989, while she was in high school, Matsuyuki won a Non-no fashion magazine contest. After graduating from Tosu Commercial High School, she moved to Tokyo and started her career as a model.
In 1991, Matsuyuki made her film debut in the TV drama series "Nekketsu Shinnyū Shain Sengen". In 1993, she played an outgoing leading role in the manga based drama series "Shiratori Reiko de Gozaimasu!". That role made a big impact on the drama scene and established her career as an actress.[tpl]cite web|title = Yasuko Matsuyuki's Profile|publisher = Yasuko Matsuyuki official website|url = http://official.stardust.co.jp/matsuyuki/profile/english_intro.html|accessdate=October 5, 2008[/tpl] [tpl]Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title = Tarento Meikan: Yasuko Matsuyuki|publisher = Sponichi Annex|url = http://www.sponichi.co.jp/entertainment/meikan/ma/matsuyukiyasuko.html|language = Japanese|accessdate=October 5, 2008[/tpl]
In 1998, after a 2 year relationship, Matsuyuki married guitarist [tpl]Nihongo|Gaku Kadowaki|門脇 学[/tpl]. She had a baby boy in 2004, but got divorced in 2005, having separated the previous year.[tpl]cite web|title = Yasuko Matsuyuki got divorced|publisher = Sponichi Annex|url = http://www.sponichi.co.jp/entertainment/special/2005hakyoku/KFullNormal20050116018.html|language = Japanese|accessdate=October 5, 2008[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title = Matsuyuki, Gaku Split|publisher = Japan Zone |url = http://www.japan-zone.com/news/2005/01/17/matsuyuki_gaku_split.shtml|accessdate=October 5, 2008[/tpl]
Matsuyuki has two younger brothers. One is [tpl]Nihongo|Kōhei Takamura|高村 晃平[/tpl], who is also an actor, and the other is [tpl]Nihongo|Yūna Katsuki|香月 優奈[/tpl] who is a rock singer.
