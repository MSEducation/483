[[Qezel Bolagh, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Qezel Bolagh ([tpl]lang-fa|قزل بلاغ[/tpl], also Romanized as Qezel Bolāgh; also known as Ghezel Bolagh and Qizil Bulāq)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3080218|Qezel Bolagh[/tpl] is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 682, in 143 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
