[[Nirmala Devi]]

CATEGORIES: Hindustani singers, Living people, Year of birth missing (living people), Place of birth missing (living people)

Nirmala Devi, also known as Nirmala Arun (died 1996), was a Punjabi Hindustani classical vocalist of the Patiala Gharana, in far northern India. She is the mother of Bollywood actor Govinda.

==Personal life==

Nirmala Devi was the wife of a popular actor, of the 1940s, Arun Ahuja. She has five children, including actor Govinda and director Kirti Kumar. She died in 1996. Her original name was Nazeem who was Muslim and had converted to Hinduism and adopted the name Nirmala Devi after marriage.
