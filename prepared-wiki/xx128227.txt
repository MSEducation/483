[[Richard B. Russell State Park]]

CATEGORIES: State parks of Georgia (U.S. state), Disc golf courses, Protected areas of Elbert County, Georgia

Richard B. Russell State Park is a 2,508 acre (10.15 km2) state park located on the shore of Russell Lake in Elbert County, Georgia. The park features the 18 hole Arrowhead Golf Course, as well as picnic shelters and a swimming beach.
