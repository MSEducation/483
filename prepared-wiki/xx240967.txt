[[Chengqu, Yangquan]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Shanxi

Chengqu ([tpl]zh|s=城区 |t=城區 |p=Chéngqū |l=urban district[/tpl]), or Cheng District is a district of Yangquan, Shanxi, China. As of 2002, it had a population of 200,000 living in an area of [tpl]convert|19.28|km2|abbr=on[/tpl].
