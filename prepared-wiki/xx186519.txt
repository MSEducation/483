[[Gmina Dobrzeń Wielki]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Opole Voivodeship, Opole County

Gmina Dobrzeń Wielki, German Gemeinde Groß Döbern is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Opole County, Opole Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Its seat is the village of Dobrzeń Wielki, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|13|km|mi|0[/tpl] north-west of the regional capital Opole.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|91.42|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 14,049. Since 2009 the commune, like much of the surrounding area, has been bilingual in German and Polish.
The gmina contains part of the protected area called Stobrawa Landscape Park.
