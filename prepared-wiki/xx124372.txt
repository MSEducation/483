[[Anaiyur, Virudhunagar]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Virudhunagar district

Anaiyur is a panchayat town in Sivakasi block of  Virudhunagar district  in the state of Tamil Nadu, India.

==Demographics==

[tpl]As of|2001[/tpl] India census,[tpl]GR|India[/tpl] Anaiyur had a population of 19,847. Males constitute 50% of the population and females 50%.
