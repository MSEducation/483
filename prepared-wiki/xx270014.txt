[[Annanagar Mudhal Theru]]

CATEGORIES: 1980s comedy films, 1988 films, Indian films, Tamil films of 1988, Indian comedy films, Tamil-language films, Indian film remakes

Annanagar Mudhal Theru is a 1988 Tamil film starring Sathyaraj, Radha, Ambika, Janagaraj, Raghuvaran and Manorama.[tpl]cite book|title=Encyclopaedia of Indian cinema|publisher=British Film Institute|author=Ashish Rajadhyaksha, Paul Willemen|pages=658[/tpl]  Prabhu makes an extended cameo appearance. 
The film was a remake of the 1986 Malayalam film Gandhinagar 2nd Street starring Mohanlal and Karthika directed by Sathyan Anthikad. The film is a mixture of comedy, romance, sentiments and affection. It was a profitable venture for Vivekananda Film Circuit K.Padmavathi.

==Reception==

The film was a blockbuster and completed 100 day run in theaters. It earned a good reputation for its director Balu Anand who proved his competence via this project.
