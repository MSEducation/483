CATEGORIES: Cycling at the 2011 Pan American Games

The women's team pursuit competition of the cycling events at the 2011 Pan American Games will be held on October 18 at the Pan American Velodrome in Guadalajara.Guadalajara 2011 sessions This will be the first time this event is held at the Pan American Games.

==Schedule==

All times are Central Standard Time (UTC-6).
