[[Nataliya Zolotukhina]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 births, Living people, Ukrainian hammer throwers

Nataliya Zolotukhina ([tpl]lang-uk|Наталія Золотухіна[/tpl]; born 4 January 1985 in Kharkiv, in the Ukrainian SSR of the Soviet Union) is a female hammer thrower from Ukraine. Her personal best throw is 70.30 metres, achieved in May 2010 in Yalta.
