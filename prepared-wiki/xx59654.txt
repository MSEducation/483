[[Owen Roberts International Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in the Cayman Islands, 1952 establishments in the Cayman Islands, Airports established in 1952, George Town, Cayman Islands

Owen Roberts International Airport [tpl]Airport codes|GCM|MWCR[/tpl] is an airport located in Grand Cayman, Cayman Islands. It is the main international airport for the Cayman Islands as well as the main base for Cayman Airways. The airport is named after Owen Roberts, a pioneer of commercial aviation in the country. Owen Roberts is also the last International airport in the Caribbean with an "open-air" passenger waving gallery. Owen Roberts International airport is one of the two entrance ports to the Cayman Islands. There is a bar at the departing terminal, and they sell rum cakes at the shops here. http://www.caymanislands.co.uk/planatrip.aspx

==Renovations==

In 2007, the Cayman Islands Government announced plans to expand and upgrade the existing airport. Plans include the expansion of the check-in area, the purchase of a new X-ray machine and baggage screening machine as well as the employment of additional passenger screening staff.
