[[Serbia at the 2008 Summer Paralympics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 2008 Summer Paralympics, Serbia at the Paralympics, 2008 in Serbian sport

Serbia competed at the 2008 Summer Paralympics in Beijing  for the first time as independent country. Serbian competitors took part in table tennis and athletics events."9 Nations preparing for the Paralympics in Lasko", official website of the International Paralympic Table Tennis Federation They won 2 silver medals, Borislava Perić in table tennis event and Draženko Mitrović in athletics.
