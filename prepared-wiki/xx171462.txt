[[Setmana Catalana de Ciclisme]]

CATEGORIES: UCI Europe Tour, Cycle races in Catalonia, Recurring sporting events established in 1963, 1963 establishments in Spain, Recurring events disestablished in 2005, Defunct cycling races

The Catalan Cycling Week (Setmana Catalana de Ciclisme in Catalan) was an important multi-stage road bicycle race held in Catalonia, Spain. It was last organised in 2005 as a 2.HC race on the UCI Europe Tour. Setmana Catalana was cancelled in 2006 and it is no longer part of the UCI Calendar.
The first winner of the race was José Pérez-Francés in 1963.
