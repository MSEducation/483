[[Anjoman-e Sofla]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Anjoman-e Sofla ([tpl]lang-fa|انجمن سفلي[/tpl], also Romanized as Anjoman-e Soflá; also known as Anjoman and Anjoman-Pā’īn)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3053309|Anjoman-e Sofla[/tpl] is a village in Chaypareh-ye Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 345, in 74 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
