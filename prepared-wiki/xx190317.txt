[[Kremlin pool]]

CATEGORIES: Russian media, Russian journalists

Kremlin pool ([tpl]lang-ru|Кремлевский пул[/tpl]), also President's pool is a common reference to a group of Russian journalists accredited to regularly cover the activities of the President of Russia and  the top Russian government.
Yelena Tregubova in her controversial"RUSSIA: Book prompts angry reaction from Kremlin" book The tales of a Kremlin digger speaks about the behind-the-scenes of the "Kremlin pool" of late Boris Yeltsin and early Vladimir Putin.Yelena Tregubova, The tales of a Kremlin digger (Russian edition), ISBN 5-93321-073-0
