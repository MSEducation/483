[[Barney Smith]]

Lloyd Barnaby "Barney" Smith CMG (born 21 July 1945) is a retired British ambassador, now chairman of Coastal Energy.

==Career==

Smith was educated at Merchant Taylors' School and Brasenose College, Oxford. He joined the Diplomatic Service in 1968 and served in Bangkok, Paris, Dublin, Brussels and Bangkok (with a year at the École nationale d'administration in Paris). He was ambassador to Nepal 1995–99 and ambassador to Thailand (concurrently non-resident ambassador to Laos) 2000–03. He has been editor of Asian Affairs since 2005. He has been a non-executive director since 2006, and chairman since 2011, of Coastal Energy, an oil and gas company working in Thailand and Malaysia.Coastal Energy: Board of Directors
