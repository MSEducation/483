[[Waipu River]]

CATEGORIES: Whangarei District, Rivers of the Northland Region

The Waipu River is a river in the Northland Region of the North Island of New Zealand. It runs close to the town of Waipu. The river is popular with birdwatchers as species such as the New Zealand Dotterel, oyster catchers and fairy terns live near it.
