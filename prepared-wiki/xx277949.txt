[[Alistair Vermaak]]

CATEGORIES: South African rugby union players, Living people, 1989 births, People from Port Elizabeth, Boland Cavaliers players, Western Province (rugby team) players

Alistair Vermaak (born 28 April 1989) is a South African rugby union player, currently playing with [tpl]Rut Western Province[/tpl].[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/playerprofile.aspx?id=37667 | title=SARU Player Profile Alistair Vermaak | work=South African Rugby Union | accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl] His regular position is prop.

==Career==

===Youth===

At youth level, he played for [tpl]Rut Eastern Province[/tpl] at the 2005 Under-16 Grant Khomo Week and for [tpl]Rut Griffons[/tpl] at the Under-18 Academy Week in 2006, as well as the 2007 Craven Week. He also played for [tpl]Rut Griffons U19[/tpl] in the 2007 Under-19 Currie Cup competition.
In 2008, he moved to Cape Town to join Western Province. He played for them in the 2008 Under-19 Currie Cup and 2010 Under-21 Currie Cup competitions.

===Western Province===

In 2011, he was included in the [tpl]Rut Western Province[/tpl] squad for the 2011 Vodacom Cup competition[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/teams.aspx?id=8462&category=&leagueid=802 | title=2011 Western Province Vodacom Cup season | work=South African Rugby Union | accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl] and made his first class debut in that competition, coming on as a substitute in their match against the [tpl]Rut Welwitschias[/tpl] and promptly scored a try within a minute of coming on.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/matchbreakdown.aspx?id=20056&category=&leagueid=802&homeid=8462&awayid=8476&fullview=true | title=Match Breakdown: DHL Western Province vs Welwitschias | work=South African Rugby Union | date=11 March 2011 | accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl] He made five appearances in total in the competition. One more appearance followed in the 2012 Vodacom Cup competition.
His Currie Cup debut came in 2013, when he was included in the Western Province team to face the [tpl]Rut Blue Bulls[/tpl] in the opening match of the 2013 Currie Cup Premier Division season.[tpl]cite news | url=http://www.sport24.co.za/Rugby/CurrieCup/WP-name-strong-team-for-Bulls-20130807 | title=WP name strong team for Bulls | work=Sport24 | date=7 August 2013 | agency=Media24 | accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl]

===Boland Cavaliers===

He had a short loan spell at the [tpl]Rut Boland Cavaliers[/tpl] during the 2013 Vodacom Cup competition,[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/teams.aspx?id=11704&category=&leagueid=1164 | title=2013 Boland Cavaliers Vodacom Cup season | work=South African Rugby Union | accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl] making three appearances in total.

===Varsity Cup===

He also represented the [tpl]Rut Maties[/tpl] in the Varsity Cup competition in 2011, 2012 and 2013.
