[[Jay Sonza]]

CATEGORIES: 1955 births, ABS-CBN Corporation, De La Salle University alumni, Filipino television journalists, GMA Network, Living people, Kapampangan people, People from Pampanga, Reporters and correspondents, Radio Philippines Network

Jay Sonza (born Jose Yumang Sonza on September 20, 1955 in Macabebe, Pampanga, Philippines) is a veteran newscaster and talk show host. He is currently the station manager of the terrestrial television station UNTV 37, which is owned by his former co-host, Daniel Razon.[tpl]cite web|url=http://showbizandstyle.inquirer.net/entertainment/entertainment/view/20090919-225929/AnakTV-bares-temporary-changes-in-leadership |title=AnakTV bares temporary changes in leadership|publisher=Showbizandstyle.inquirer.net |date=2009-09-19 |accessdate=2010-12-06[/tpl]
