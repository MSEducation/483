[[Tekmeh Dash, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Tekmeh Dash ([tpl]lang-fa|تكمه داش[/tpl], also Romanized as Tekmeh Dāsh; also known as Nīkmeh Dāsh, Tīkmeh Dāsh, Tukmahdāsh, Tukmakhdash, and Tūkmeh Dāsh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3087449|Tekmeh Dash[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 497, in 110 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
