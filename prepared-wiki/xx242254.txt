John Edward Marquis (born 16 May 1992) is an English professional footballer who plays for Northampton Town, on loan from Millwall, as a striker.

==Career==

===Millwalll===

Born in Lewisham, London, Marquis made his professional debut for Millwall in September 2009.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.millwallfc.co.uk/team/player-profile/index.aspx?playerid=434897&tcmuri=25107|title=Profile|publisher=Millwall F.C.[/tpl] Marquis signed a three-year contract with Millwall in May 2011.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/13471791|title=John Marquis agrees three-year Millwall contract|date=20 May 2011|publisher=BBC Sport[/tpl] In September 2012 he was injured and was ruled out until 2013.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/19666371|title=Millwall hit by John Marquis injury blow|date=20 September 2012|publisher=BBC Sport[/tpl]
