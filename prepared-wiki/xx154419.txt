[[Jay (given name)]]

Jay is a common given name or nickname. It may be a nickname for James, Jamie, Jacob, Jared, Joaquin, Jeremy, John, Jonathan, Joseph, or Jason. In Hindu cultures, Jay or Jai is a common first name derived from the Sanskrit for "win" or "victory".
