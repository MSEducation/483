[[Echoes (1950 song)]]

CATEGORIES: 1950 songs, Songs written by Bennie Benjamin, Songs written by George David Weiss, Jo Stafford songs

"Echoes" is a popular song.
It was written by Bennie Benjamin and George David Weiss and published in 1950.
It was first recorded in 1950 by Bill Kenny who's recording reached #24 on the US. Pop charts. In 1950, Jo Stafford and Gordon MacRae also recorded the composition. The Stafford/MacRae recording, released by Capitol Records as catalog number 782, was backed with "Bibbidi-Bobbidi-Boo (The Magic Song)" on the flip side. The song reached #18 on the Billboard charts and #13 on the Cash Box charts.
