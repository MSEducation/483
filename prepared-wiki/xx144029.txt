[[Nakamita Station]]

CATEGORIES: Geibi Line, Stations of West Japan Railway Company in Hiroshima city, Railway stations opened in 1915

[tpl]nihongo|Nakamita Station|中三田駅|Naka-Mita-eki[/tpl] is a JR West Geibi Line station located in Mita, Shiraki-chō, Asakita-ku, Hiroshima, Hiroshima Prefecture, Japan. 

==History==

==Station building and platforms==

Nakamita Station features one island-style platform capable of handling two lines simultaneously. The station, while privately operated under contract from the railroad, is unmanned. The Nakamita Station building is a simple concrete structure.
