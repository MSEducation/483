==About YSW==

Yellow Studio Works is a team of young film-makers passionate about films.http://www.channel6.in/articles/5309-2/ Yellow Studio Works was the first independent film-making group in Hyderabad, India to release their short film exclusively on Facebook.[tpl]cite news| url=http://www.hindu.com/edu/2011/06/06/stories/2011060650730400.htm | location=Chennai, India | work=The Hindu | title=Transforming their passion into a career | date=6 June 2011[/tpl] Although, none of the founders had any professional experience they have been successful in the field of film making and have been providing workshops for the cinema enthusiasts of Hyderabad.[tpl]cite news| url=http://www.hindu.com/2011/04/03/stories/2011040362600700.htm | location=Chennai, India | work=The Hindu | title=A medium of expression for amateur film-makers | date=3 April 2011[/tpl]
