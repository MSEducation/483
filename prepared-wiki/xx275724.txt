==Career==

Bendall started out at Orange Studios, moving on to Steinway & Sons as a piano tuner.From Steinway to Abbey Road article He joined Ken Townsend's Abbey Road team in 1973 as a part-time assistant, taking on a full-time position a year later.
Initially partnering with Tony Clarke, Bendall worked on albums by Herbie Flowers, BeBop Deluxe, Steve Harley, Alan Parsons, Cliff Richard and Camel,Case Study of Producer / Engineer – Haydn Bendall by Peter H A Jackson, SAE Institute developing a close working relationship with Sky. In the 1980-90s, he worked with Kate Bush on five albums, including Hounds of Love in 1985. Further Abbey Road work included engineering or producing for XTC, Bonnie Tyler, Tina Turner and Massive Attack. Film soundtrack work included The Long Good Friday[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/album/the-long-good-friday-soundtrack-r85038/credits |title=Allmusic |publisher=Allmusic |accessdate=26 January 2012[/tpl] and Ryuichi Sakamoto's pieces on the Oscar- and Grammy-winning[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.abbeyroad.com/visit/history-of-abbey-road/1980s/ |title=Abbey Road history |publisher=Abbeyroad.com |accessdate=26 January 2012[/tpl] soundtrack to The Last Emperor.Last Emperor Soundtrack credits at Discogs
Leaving Abbey Road in 1991, Bendall became an independent producer/engineer.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.alchemea.com/index.php/audio/engineering/haydn-bendall/ |title=Alchemea Biography |publisher=Alchemea.com |date=30 November 2011 |accessdate=26 January 2012[/tpl] As an independent, Bendall has worked with The Pet Shop Boys, Everything but the Girl, Pat Metheny, Chris Botti, Leona Lewis, The Priests, Mike Batt and Katie Melua.Mike Batt's Twitter stream[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/haydn-bendall-p55764/credits/date-asc/ |title=Allmusic |publisher=Allmusic |accessdate=26 January 2012[/tpl] In 1992, Bendall engineered[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.riotsquadpublicity.com/images/management/Secret%20Story%20(Expanded%20%20remastered).pdf |title=Secret Story production |format=PDF |accessdate=26 January 2012[/tpl] the Grammy-award winning album Secret StoryDetails at Artist Direct for Pat Metheny. He also worked with Eric Woolfson on the development of three musicals: Gaudi, Gambler and Poe.Alchemea biography
He is currently based in Strongroom Studios, Shoreditch, London.[tpl]cite web|url=http://blog.strongroom.com/2010/03/22/strongroom-resident-haydn-bendall-in-focus/ |title=Haydn Bendall : Strongroom Resident |publisher=Blog.strongroom.com |accessdate=26 January 2012[/tpl]
Recently Bendall mixed and engineered La Voce for Russell Watson and Noël for The Priests. Other projects include engineering and mixing the Vatican album Alma Mater featuring the voice of Pope Benedict XVI,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/haydn-bendall-q12557/credits/all |title=AllMusic |publisher=AllMusic |accessdate=26 January 2012[/tpl] recording orchestral tracks for Andrea Bocelli and mixing tracks for Siphiwo Ntshebe.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.duncanmanagement.com/haydnbendall.cfm |title=biography |publisher=Duncanmanagement.com |accessdate=26 January 2012[/tpl]
