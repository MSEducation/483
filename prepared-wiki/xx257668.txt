[[Peggy Michel]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1949 births, American female tennis players, Sportspeople from Santa Monica, California, Tennis people from California, Grand Slam (tennis) champions in women's doubles

Peggy Michel (born February 2, 1949) was an American professional tennis player. She was a doubles specialist who won three Grand Slam titles, all with Evonne Goolagong.
She was born in Santa Monica, California.
