[[1998 Heineken Open – Singles]]

CATEGORIES: 1998 ATP Tour, Heineken Open (tennis)

Marcelo Ríos defeated Richard Fromberg 4-6, 6-4, 7-6 (7-3) to win the 1998 Heineken Open singles competition. Jonas Björkman was the champion but did not defend his title.
