[[Pyrgomantis simillima]]

CATEGORIES: Pyrgomantis, Mantodea of Africa, Animals described in 1954

[tpl]Italic title[/tpl]

Pyrgomantis simillima is a species of praying mantis found in Tanzania, Transvaal, and Zimbabwe.  It was originally identified as two separate species: Pyrgomantis curta (Beier, 1954) and Pyrgomantis simillima (also Beier, 1954). http://tolweb.org/tree?group=Amorphoscelis Tree of Life Web Project. 2005http://insects.tamu.edu/research/collection/hallan/Arthropoda/Insects/Mantodea/Family/Tarachodidae.txt Texas A&M University
