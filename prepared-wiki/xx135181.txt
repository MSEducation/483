[[Bath High School (Lima, Ohio)]]

CATEGORIES: High schools in Allen County, Ohio, Public high schools in Ohio

Bath High School is a public high school located near Lima, Ohio in Allen County.  It is the only high school in the Bath Local School District.

==Organizations==

School organizations at Bath include: Band, Symphonic Band, Choir, FCA, National Honors Society, Quiz Bowl, Science Fair, Math Counts, SADD, PawPrint Newspaper, Science Olympiad, Student Council, and Yearbook editors.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bathwildcats.org/HS/organization.html|title=Organizations[/tpl]

==Athletics==

The school's mascot is a wildcat for boys' sports, and a wildkitten for girls'.  They are members of the Western Buckeye League.
