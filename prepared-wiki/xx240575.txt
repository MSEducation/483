[[Grant Leury]]

CATEGORIES: 1967 births, Australian canoeists, Canoeists at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Living people, Olympic canoeists of Australia

Grant Leury (born 5 November 1967) is an Australian sprint canoer who competed in the mid-1990s. He finished seventh in the K-2 1000 m event at the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta.
