[[Ebola River]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of the Democratic Republic of the Congo

The Ebola River in northern Democratic Republic of the Congo is the headstream of the Mongala River, a tributary of the Congo River.
Ebola virus (EBOV) and the taxonomic ranks associated with it and its relatives (genus Ebolavirus, and species Bundibugyo ebolavirus, Reston ebolavirus, Sudan ebolavirus, Ta√Ø Forest ebolavirus, and Zaire ebolavirus) are named after this river.
