[[Rare Birds]]

CATEGORIES: 2001 films, Canadian films, English-language films, Canadian comedy films, Canadian drama films, 2000s comedy-drama films, Films based on novels, Films directed by Sturla Gunnarsson, Films set in Newfoundland and Labrador

Rare Birds is a 2001 Canadian comedy/drama film. It was directed by Sturla Gunnarsson and written by Edward Riche based on his novel. This movie features spectacular scenery from Cape Spear, Newfoundland, Canada. It also features music by The Pogues and characteristic Canadian Maritime musicians such as Ashley MacIsaac.
