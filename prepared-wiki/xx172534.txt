Igor Morozov (born 27 May 1989 in Tallinn) is an Estonian professional footballer, who plays in Hungarian NB I, for Debreceni VSC. He plays the position of defender and is 1.93 m tall and weighs 82 kg. He is also a member of Estonia national football team.

==Club career==

===Levadia===

Morozov is a product of Levadia football academy. He made his debut for the first team in 2005 and is currently the club captain.
