[[Roane State Community College]]

CATEGORIES: Two-year colleges in the United States, Universities and colleges in Tennessee, Education in Roane County, Tennessee, Education in Campbell County, Tennessee, Education in Scott County, Tennessee, Education in Cumberland County, Tennessee, Education in Loudon County, Tennessee, Education in Morgan County, Tennessee, Education in Anderson County, Tennessee, Oak Ridge, Tennessee, Buildings and structures in Roane County, Tennessee, Educational institutions established in 1971, Universities and colleges accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools

Roane State Community College is a two-year college located in eastern Tennessee. It was authorized by the Tennessee General Assembly in 1969, along with two other community colleges, and operates under the authority of the Tennessee Board of Regents.
