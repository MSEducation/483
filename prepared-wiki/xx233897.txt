[[St. Thomas English Medium School]]

CATEGORIES: Schools in Uttar Pradesh, Education in Meerut, Educational institutions established in 1972

St. Thomas' English Medium School, also known as STEMS, is a school located in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, India, established in 1972 by the late (Ms)K. Narhona. Starting as very small school with little funds, it is now an eminent educational institution producing 1st class students for several years. STEMS is affiliated to the ICSE Board.
STEMS has been turned to +2 I.S.C. since 2006.

Address : St. Thomas' English Medium School School address Jyoti Niketan Chippi Tank City Meerut.
