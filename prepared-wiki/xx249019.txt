[[Maximiliano Scapparoni]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1989 births, People from Buenos Aires Province, Association football goalkeepers, Argentine footballers, Argentine expatriate footballers, Boca Juniors footballers, Ñublense footballers, Expatriate footballers in Chile

Maximiliano Scapparoni ([tpl]IPA-es|maksimiˈljano skapaˈroni[/tpl], born 13 January 1989) is an Argentine footballer that currently plays for Primera B Nacional side Independiente Rivadavia as a goalkeeper.
