[[Black Horse Tavern (Gettysburg, Pennsylvania)]]

CATEGORIES: Commercial buildings on the National Register of Historic Places in Pennsylvania, Drinking establishments in Pennsylvania, Houses completed in 1812, Drinking establishments on the National Register of Historic Places in Pennsylvania, Historic American Buildings Survey in Pennsylvania
