[[Ashby Road]]

CATEGORIES: Cricket grounds in Leicestershire, 1911 establishments in England, 1937 disestablishments in England

The Ashby Road ground was a cricket stadium based in the town of Hinckley, Leicestershire, England. It operated between 1911 and 1937. It is now a housing estate.
Ashby Road had been used by Leicestershire as an out-ground, and held 19 games. The first game took place in 1911 against Warwickshire and the last in 1937 against Worcestershire.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.cricketarchive.com/Leicestershire/Grounds/526.html |title=Ashby Road, Hinckley |publisher=CricketArchive |accessdate=31 October 2011[/tpl]
