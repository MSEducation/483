==Mail==

The postal service in the Netherlands is performed by PostNL in most cases—which has, as of 2008, a monopoly on letters lighter than 50 g.NOSJOURNAAL - Reorganisatie bij postbedrijf The monopoly is planned to expire in 2009. PostNL's competitors include Selekt MailSelekt Mail is a DHL Global Mail company. and Sandd."Royal TNT Post to seek cuts as its Dutch monopoly ends" The New York Times (3 April 2007) Post offices that are owned by Postbank and TNT Post have been earmarked for closure between 2008 and 2013.http://www.parool.nl/nieuws/2008/MRT/05/p1.html"Postkantoren dicht uit protest tegen sluiting" Elsevier.nl (25 March 2008)
Postal codes in the Netherlands are formed of four digits then two letters (in capitals), separated by a space—1234 AB, for example.

general assessment: highly developed and well maintained
domestic: extensive fixed-line fiber-optic network; cellular telephone system is one of the largest in Europe with three major network operators utilizing the third generation of the Global System for Mobile Communications (GSM).
 international:
9 submarine cables; satellite earth stations - 3 Intelsat (1 Indian Ocean and 2 Atlantic Ocean), 1 Eutelsat, and 1 Inmarsat (Atlantic and Indian Ocean regions) (2004)
