[[Dalad Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Dalad Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠳᠠᠯᠠᠳ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Dalad qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=达拉特旗 |t=達拉特旗 |p=Dálātè Qí[/tpl]) is a banner of western Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China, lying on the southern (right) bank of the Yellow River. It is under the administration of Ordos City, although it is closer to the city of Baotou, [tpl]convert|33|km[/tpl] to the north-northwest.
