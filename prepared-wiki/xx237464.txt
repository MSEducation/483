[[Cornelis sjunger Povel]]

CATEGORIES: Cornelis Vreeswijk albums, 1981 albums, Swedish folk music

Cornelis sjunger Povel, (English: Cornelis sings Povel) is a music album recorded by the Swedish-Dutch folk 
singer-songwriter Cornelis Vreeswijk in 1981. It's a homage to composer and lyricist Povel Ramel. 
Issued as double LP in 1981 titled as "Alla vi har varit små" och "The Grönsäkling blues", it was re-issued on CD 
in 2007 under title Diamanter.
