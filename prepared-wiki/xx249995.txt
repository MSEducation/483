[[West Elk Wilderness]]

CATEGORIES: Wilderness Areas of Colorado, Protected areas established in 1964, Protected areas of Gunnison County, Colorado, Gunnison National Forest

The West Elk Wilderness is a U.S. Wilderness Area located northwest of Gunnison, Colorado in the Gunnison National Forest. The area supports large elk and deer populations and is busiest during the fall hunting season.[tpl]cite web |title=West Elk Wilderness |publisher=Wilderness.net |url=http://www.wilderness.net/index.cfm?fuse=NWPS&sec=wildView&WID=641 |accessdate=August 12, 2012[/tpl][tpl]cite web |title=West Elk Wilderness |publisher=U.S. Forest Service |url=http://www.fs.fed.us/air/technical/class_1/wilds.php?recordID=85 |accessdate=August 12, 2012[/tpl][tpl]cite web |title=West Elk Wilderness Area |publisher=Colorado Wilderness |url=http://www.coloradowilderness.com/wildpages/westelk.html |accessdate=August 12, 2012[/tpl]
