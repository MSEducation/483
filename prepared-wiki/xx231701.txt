[[Jørgen Beck]]

CATEGORIES: 1914 births, 1991 deaths, Danish male film actors, People from Roskilde, 20th-century Danish male actors

Jørgen Beck (13 December 1914 – 5 October 1991) was a Danish film actor. He appeared in 32 films between 1945 and 1978.
He was born in Roskilde, Denmark and died in Denmark.
