[[Koi no Doutei]]

CATEGORIES: Debut singles, 2010 singles

[tpl]Nihongo|"Koi no Doutei"|恋の道程[/tpl] is the second single from singer and cellist Kanon Wakeshima and An Cafe bassist Kanon's duo group Kanon x Kanon, and Wakeshima's fourth overall single.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.cdjapan.co.jp/detailview.html?KEY=DFCL-1774 |title=koi no dotei [/tpl] The song was used as the opening song for the anime adaption of 30-sai no Hoken Taiiku.
