[[2004–05 2. Bundesliga (women)]]

CATEGORIES: 2. Bundesliga (women) seasons, 2004–05 domestic women's association football leagues, 2004–05 in German women's football, 2004–05 in German football leagues

The 2nd Fußball-Bundesliga (women) 2004–05 was the 1st season of the 2. Fußball-Bundesliga (women), Germany's second football league. It began on 5 September 2004 and ended on 22 May 2005.[tpl]cite web | title = 2.
