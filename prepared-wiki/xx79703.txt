[[Willows Gemfields, Queensland]]

CATEGORIES: Mining towns in Queensland, Towns in Queensland, Central Queensland

The Willows is a town in Queensland, Australia. Situated in a region called the Gemfields, sapphires are mined extensively in the area. At the census, Willows Gemfields had a population of 103.[tpl]Census 2006 AUS|id=UCL360300|name=Willows Gemfields (L) (Urban Centre/Locality) |accessdate=25 June 2011|quick=on[/tpl]
