[[Gmina Łubniany]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Opole Voivodeship, Opole County

Gmina Łubniany, German Gemeinde Lugnian is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Opole County, Opole Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Its seat is the village of Łubniany (Lugnian), which lies approximately [tpl]convert|14|km|mi|0[/tpl] north of the regional capital Opole.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|125.41|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 9,156. Since 2010 the village, like much of the area, has been officially bilingual in German and Polish.
The gmina contains part of the protected area called Stobrawa Landscape Park.
