[[Eduardo Guadamud]]

CATEGORIES: 1986 births, Living people, Ecuadorian weightlifters, Olympic weightlifters of Ecuador, Weightlifters at the 2007 Pan American Games, Weightlifters at the 2008 Summer Olympics

Eduardo Francisco Guadamud Braulio (born June 11, 1986) is an Ecuadorian weightlifter.
At the 2008 Pan American Weightlifting Championships he won silver in the 94 kg category, with a total of 355 kg.[tpl]cite web|title=GUADAMUD Eduardo|publisher=International Weightlifting Federation|url= http://www.iwf.net/results/results_athlete_e.php?compid=737[/tpl]
He competed in the 94 kg class at the 2008 Summer Olympics, but did not finish.[tpl]cite web|title=GUADAMUD Eduardo|publisher=International Weightlifting Federation|url= http://www.iwf.net/results/results_athlete_e.php?compid=2625[/tpl]
