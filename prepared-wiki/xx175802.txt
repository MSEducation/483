[[WDSC (AM)]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in South Carolina

WDSC (800 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Gospel format. Licensed to Dillon, South Carolina, USA, the station is currently owned by Qantum Broadcasting. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=WDSC |title=WDSC Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl] 

==History==

In 1993, WDSC had a sports talk format when it went off the air.Paul Woolverton, "WRCQ Owners to Purchase Competitors," The Fayetteville Observer, November 2, 1993.
As of 1997, WDSC was owned by Root Communications, Ltd.[tpl]cite web|url= http://transition.fcc.gov/Bureaus/Mass_Media/Public_Notices/Brdcst_Applications/ap971117.txt |title=Report No. 24119 Broadcast Applications |publisher=FCC |date=1997-11-17 |accessdate=2011-11-01 [/tpl] Qantum Communications Inc. purchased Florence's Root Communications Group LP stations in 2003.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.broadcastingcable.com/article/101197-Changing_Hands.php|title=Changing Hands|work=Broadcasting & Cable|date=2003-03-16|accessdate=2011-10-13[/tpl]
The station was simulcast on WWRK.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.krgspec.com/MSAOverview.cfm?AreaID=554|title=Florence, SC Metro Survey Area Overview|accessdate=2009-04-14[/tpl]
