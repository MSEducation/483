[[Tubulariidae]]

CATEGORIES: Hydrozoa

Tubulariidae is a family of hydroid cnidarians. For long placed in a presumed superfamily or infraorder Tubulariida of suborder Capitata, they are actually close relatives of the Hydridae and are now united with these and a number of relatives in a newly-recognized suborder Aplanulata.http://www.marinespecies.org/aphia.php?p=taxdetails&id=1603 accessed 9 August 2013
