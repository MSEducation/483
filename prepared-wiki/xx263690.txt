"Elementary introduction to the theory of functions of complex variables with particular regard to integral representations" (English translation of the title reads) is a textbook, published by the Accademia Nazionale dei Lincei, taken from the notes from a course held by Martinelli when he was in charge to the academy as a "Professore Linceo".
