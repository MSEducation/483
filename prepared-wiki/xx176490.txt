[[Alberto Joaquim Vinama]]

CATEGORIES: 1986 deaths, Angolan anti-communists, Angolan rebels, Angolan revolutionaries, Members of UNITA, Year of birth missing, Warlords

Brigadier Alberto Joaquim Vinama (died October 10, 1986), also known as Chendovava, served as the Chief of Staff of the Armed Forces for the Liberation of Angola, the armed wing of UNITA, from January 1985 until his death in October 1986. He replaced Demosthenes Amos Chilingutila, demoted to Chief of Operations, who regained his office upon Vinama's death.[tpl]cite book|last=Kukkuk|first=Leon|year=2005|title=Letters to Gabriella|pages=160[/tpl]
