[[Lefki, Lasithi]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Lasithi

Lefki ([tpl]lang-el|Λεύκη[/tpl]) is a former municipality in the Lasithi regional unit, eastern Crete, Greece. Since the 2011 local government reform it is part of the municipality Siteia, of which it is a municipal unit.Kallikratis law Greece Ministry of Interior [tpl]el icon[/tpl] Population 2,117 (2001). The seat of the municipality was in Ziros.
