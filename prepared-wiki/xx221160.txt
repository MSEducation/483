[[Mir Bandeh Ali Khan Talpur]]

CATEGORIES: Chief Ministers of Sindh, Sindhi people, Sindh politicians, Pakistani politicians, Possibly living people

Mir Bandeh Ali Khan Talpur ([tpl]lang-ur| میر بندہ علی خان تالپور [/tpl]) was a politician from Sindh, Pakistan. 
Mir Bandeh Ali Khan Talpur was Chief Minister of Sindh from  April 14, 1940 to March 7, 1941.
