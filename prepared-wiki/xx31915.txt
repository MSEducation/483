[[Amykos]]

CATEGORIES: Greek mythology, Trojans, Offspring of Poseidon

In Greek mythology, Amykos ([tpl]lang-grc|Ἄμυκος[/tpl]), latinized as Amycus, was the son of Poseidon and Melia. He was a boxer and King of the Bebryces, a mythical people in Bithynia. Polydeuces beat him in a boxing match when the Argonauts passed through Bithynia.Apollonius Rhodius, Argonautica, 2. 1.ff & 2. 94 ff with scholiaBibliotheca 1. 9. 20Hyginus, Fabulae, 17Argonautica Orphica, 657 ff He was also a prominent Trojan during the Trojan War. He married Theona and had one son named Mimas.Virgil, Aeneid, 10. 702 He followed Aeneas to Italy, where he was killed by Turnus.Virgil, Aeneid, 12. 509
