[[Let's Dance 2010]]

CATEGORIES: Let's Dance (Swedish TV series), TV4 (Sweden) television programmes

Let's Dance 2010 was the fifth and current season of the Swedish version of Strictly Come Dancing, and is broadcasting on the Swedish television channel TV4 starting on January 8, 2010 and ended on March 26, 2010.
