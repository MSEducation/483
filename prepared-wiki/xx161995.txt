[[Smokin' the Dummy/Bloodlines]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 albums, 1983 albums, Terry Allen (artist) albums, Sugar Hill Records (bluegrass) albums

Smokin' the Dummy and Bloodlines are two albums released by Terry Allen in 1980 and 1983, respectively. The albums have been recently reissued on a single compact disc on Sugar Hill Records.http://www.sugarhillrecords.com/catalog/pagemaker.cgi?1057 The album features The Panhandle Mystery Band, Terry's backing band.
