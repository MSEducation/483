[[WBHW]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Indiana

WBHW is a Christian radio station licensed to Loogootee, Indiana, broadcasting on 88.7 MHz FM.  The station is owned by Music Ministries Inc.,WBHW fcc.gov. Accessed August 6, 2012 and is an affiliate of Thy Word Network.Tune In: Our Stations www.thywordnetwork.org. Accessed August 6, 2012
