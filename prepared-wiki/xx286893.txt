[[Pasha Chay]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Pasha Chay ([tpl]lang-fa|پاشاچاي[/tpl], also Romanized as Pāshā Chāy and Pāshā Chā’ī)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795779|Pasha Chay[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 159, in 42 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
