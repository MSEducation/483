Joseph "Joe" Joyce (born 18 March 1961 in Consett, County Durham) is an English former footballer who made 479 appearances in the Football League playing for Barnsley, Scunthorpe United, Carlisle United and Darlington. He played as a right back. He was appointed Academy Director at Newcastle United in 2007.
