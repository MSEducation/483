Kent McDonell (born March 1, 1979) is a Canadian professional ice hockey player, currently playing for Linköpings HC in the Swedish Hockey League (SHL). He has played 32 games in the NHL with the Columbus Blue Jackets. McDonell was born in Williamstown, Ontario, but grew up in Cornwall, Ontario.
