[[Charles Harris (American football)]]

CATEGORIES: Tennessee Titans players, Washington State Cougars football players, American football offensive linemen, Living people, 1982 births, Place of birth missing (living people)

Charles Harris (born in 1982) is an American football offensive lineman.

==Pro career==

Harris signed a free agent contract with the National Football League Tennessee Titans in May 2007.

==College career==

Harris played college football at Washington State University.
