[[Long Tebangan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Sarawak

Long Tebangan is a longhouse settlement in the interior of the Marudi division of Sarawak, Malaysia, on the upper Baram River. It lies approximately [tpl]convert|541.7|km|mi|0[/tpl] east-north-east of the state capital Kuching.
