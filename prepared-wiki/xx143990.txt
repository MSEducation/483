[[Lusaka Dynamos F.C.]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Zambia, Sport in Lusaka

Lusaka Dynamos Football Club is a Zambian football club based in Lusaka.They play in the top division in Zambian football. It is renowned for its ability to nurture upcoming footballers into full-fledged players. The club is owned by Hanif Adams, a businessman and experienced administrator. At the moment, Hanif is seeking election for President of the Football Association of Zambia.
