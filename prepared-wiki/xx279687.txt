[[The Trials of Cate McCall]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 films, English-language films, Films shot in Los Angeles, California, 2010s drama films, American drama films, American films, Films directed by Karen Moncrieff

The Trials of Cate McCall is an American drama film directed and written by Karen Moncrieff. The film stars Kate Beckinsale, Nick Nolte and Clancy Brown.
