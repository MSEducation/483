[[Def Jux Teaser 2005]]

CATEGORIES: 2005 compilation albums, Albums produced by Aesop Rock, Definitive Jux compilation albums, Hip hop compilation albums, English-language compilation albums, Record label compilation albums, Albums produced by El-P, East Coast hip hop compilation albums

Def Jux Teaser 2005 is a compilation album that served as a sample of what was to come from the independent hip hop label Definitive Jux for the year 2005. 

==Criticism==

This teaser has been criticised[tpl]who|date=January 2014[/tpl] for not being long enough to encapsulate all the artists from the label.[tpl]cn|date=January 2014[/tpl]
