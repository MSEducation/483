[[Xenia Stad-de Jong]]

CATEGORIES: 1922 births, 2012 deaths, Dutch sprinters, Female sprinters, People from Semarang, Olympic athletes of the Netherlands, Athletes (track and field) at the 1948 Summer Olympics, Olympic gold medalists for the Netherlands, Olympic medalists in athletics (track and field), European Athletics Championships medalists

Xenia Stad-de Jong (4 March 1922 – 3 April 2012) was a Dutch track and field athlete who competed in sprinting events.
Born in Semarang in the former Dutch East Indies (Indonesia), her greatest success was winning the gold medal as the first runner in the 4 x 100 metres relay at the 1948 Summer Olympics, together with Netty Witziers-Timmer, Gerda van der Kade-Koudijs and Fanny Blankers-Koen.databaseolympics.com She also took part in the individual 100 metres event, where she was eliminated in the semi-finals.Xenia Stade de Jong.
