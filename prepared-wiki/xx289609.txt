[[Aqbolagh-e Sofla]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Aqbolagh-e Sofla ([tpl]lang-fa|اقبلاغ سفلي[/tpl], also Romanized as Āqbolāgh-e Soflá; also known as Āq Bolāgh, Āq Bolāgh-e Pā’īn, and Aq Bulāq)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3053389|Aqbolagh-e Sofla[/tpl] is a village in Aq Bolagh Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 723, in 144 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
