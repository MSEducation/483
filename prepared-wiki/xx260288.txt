[[Jeff Moturi]]

CATEGORIES: 1986 births, Living people, American football wide receivers, UTEP Miners football players, Dallas Cowboys players

Zuriel Jeffrey Moturi (born April 4, 1986) is an American football wide receiver for the Dallas Cowboys of the National Football League. He played college football at UTEP.
He has also played for the Green Bay Packers and Chicago Bears.

==Professional career==

===Green Bay Packers===

After going undrafted in the 2010 NFL Draft, Moturi was signed as a free agent by the Green Bay Packers on April 24, 2010. He was placed on injured reserve on August 5, 2010, and released with an injury settlement on August 14, 2010.

===Chicago Bears===

Moturi signed to the Chicago Bears' practice squad on December 7, 2010. He was released on December 21, 2010.

===Dallas Cowboys===

Moturi signed to the Dallas Cowboys' practice squad on December 29, 2010.
