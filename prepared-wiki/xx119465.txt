[[Glomel]]

CATEGORIES: Communes of Côtes-d'Armor

Glomel ([tpl]Lang-br|Groñvel[/tpl]) is a commune in the Côtes-d'Armor department in Brittany in northwestern France.

==Population==

Inhabitants of Glomel are called glomelois in French.

==Breton language==

In 2008, 25.47% of primary school children attended bilingual schools.[tpl]Fr icon[/tpl] Ofis ar Brezhoneg: Enseignement bilingue
