[[Get Your Phil]]

CATEGORIES: Disappear Fear albums, 2011 albums, Self-released albums, Phil Ochs tribute albums

Get Your Phil is the sixth studio album released by Disappear Fear.[tpl]cite web | url = http://allmusic.com/album/get-your-phil-r2250889 | title = Get Your Phil - Album | accessdate = 22 September 2011 | work = Allmusic.com[/tpl] This release marks the return of the harmonizing vocals of sisters: Sonia Rutstein (SONiA) and Cindy Frank (CiNDY); which have not been featured since the group's 1996 album, Seed in the Sahara. The album has been described as, "100% made in America, folk music."[tpl]cite web | url = http://thedailysonia.wordpress.com/2011/06/15/new-cd-by-disappear-fear-get-your-phil/ | title =New CD by disappear fear - "Get Your Phil" | accessdate=22 September 2011 | work = Thedailysonia.wordpress.com[/tpl] All tracks are songs written by Phil Ochs, except for "Because the Night" which was penned by Patti Smith and Bruce Springsteen.[tpl]cite web | url = http://allmusic.com/album/get-your-phil-r2250889/credits | title = Get Your Phil > Album > Credits | accessdate = September 22, 2011 | work = Allmusic.com[/tpl] The album was released on July 4, 2011. Cindy said of the release date, "We think a new anti-war album released on Independence Day is a great idea and we hope that Phil would be proud."[tpl]cite web | url = http://thedailysonia.wordpress.com/2011/06/15/new-cd-by-disappear-fear-get-your-phil/ | title = New CD by disappear fear - "Get Your Phil" | accessdate = September 22, 2011 | work = Thedailysonia.wordpress.com[/tpl] The majority of the instrumentation on the album is done by Sonia. She plays the acoustic guitar, piano and harmonica. The album was engineered and produced by Grammy nominee, John Jacob.
