Tyler Shattock (born February 10, 1990) is a Canadian professional ice hockey player who is currently playing for the Chicago Wolves in the American Hockey League (AHL). He was selected by the St. Louis Blues in the 4th round (108th overall) of the 2009 NHL Entry Draft.Blues look to add size on second day of draft - St. Louis Blues - bnd.com
