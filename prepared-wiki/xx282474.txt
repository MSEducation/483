[[Seán Hartney]]

CATEGORIES: 1902 births, 1974 deaths, Fianna Fáil politicians, Members of the 7th Seanad, Members of the 8th Seanad, People from County Limerick

Seán Hartney (1902 – 11 May 1974) was an Irish Fianna Fáil politician. He was a member of Seanad Éireann from 1951 to 1957. He was elected to the 7th Seanad in 1951 by the Industrial and Commercial Panel.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oireachtas.ie/members-hist/default.asp?housetype=1&HouseNum=7&MemberID=1357&ConstID=207|title=Mr. Seán Hartney|work=Oireachtas Members Database|accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl] He was re-elected at the 1954 Seanad election but lost his seat at the 1957 Seanad election.
He had previously stood unsuccessfully for Dáil Éireann as a Fianna Fáil candidate for the Limerick constituency at the 1943 general election.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.electionsireland.org/candidate.cfm?ID=2088|title=Seán Hartney|work=ElectionsIreland.org|accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl]
