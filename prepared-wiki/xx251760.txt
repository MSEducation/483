[[Small Dam, Naryab]]

CATEGORIES: Buildings and structures in Hangu District, Pakistan, Dams in Pakistan

Small Dam is located in Naryab, Hangu District of Khyber-Pakhtunkhwa . It is one of a number of small water reservoir constructed in Khyber-Pakhtunkhwa to provide water for irrigation and drinking in times of drought.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.brecorder.com/index.php?id=216557&currPageNo=1&query=&search=&term=&supDate=|title=SDO identifies 78 small dam sites in Khyber-Pakhtunkhwa|date=23 March 2005|work=Business Recorder|accessdate=10 January 2010|location=Karachi [/tpl] There are no other dam within 55 km2. This dam was inaugurated by Syed Iftikhar Hussain Shah Gilani.
