[[KTON (AM)]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Texas, News and talk radio stations in the United States

KTON (1330 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a News/Talk format. Licensed to Cameron, Texas, USA, the station is currently owned by Milam Broadcasting Company. [tpl]cite web |url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=KTON |title=KTON Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division |accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl] The station is programmed similarly to sister station KCAA in Southern California, with programs including Brother Stair, Alex Jones, Free Talk Live, Joyce Riley and Barry W. Lynn.
Previously licensed as "KMIL", the station changed its call sign to "KTAE" on August 1, 2007, and again to "KTON" on December 17, 2012.
