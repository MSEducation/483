[[Lake Albert, New South Wales]]

CATEGORIES: Suburbs of Wagga Wagga

Lake Albert is a suburb of the city of Wagga Wagga, New South Wales, Australia, on the shores of Lake Albert from which it is named. The suburb has three schools; Lake Albert Primary School, Mater Dei Primary School and Mater Dei Catholic College. The Lake Village Shopping Centre, built in 1980, is located within the suburb and houses Foodworks supermarket and specialty shops.
