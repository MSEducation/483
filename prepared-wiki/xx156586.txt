WZTF (102.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Urban Adult Contemporary format. Licensed to Scranton, South Carolina, USA, the station serves the Florence, South Carolina area.  The station is currently owned by Qantum of Florence License Company, LLC.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=WZTF |title=WZTF Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==History==

WSQN was adult contemporary before becoming a soft AC station airing a syndicated format from Broadcast Programming called AC45+.[tpl]cite news|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=1g0EAAAAMBAJ&pg=PA116&lpg=PA116&dq=wsqn+billboard&source=bl&ots=gR7tqlGPjg&sig=suNxuz8cRnv8CcFxD5-MQdeAOqA&hl=en&ei=-eidTpLbOYfy0gH9n-yACQ&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=1&ved=0CB8Q6AEwAA#v=onepage&q=wsqn%20billboard&f=false |title=45-Plus AC 'Specially Created for Mom'|work=Billboard|date=Sep 9, 1995|accessdate=2011-10-18[/tpl] Its later formats included oldies[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.broadcastingcable.com/article/101197-Changing_Hands.php|title=Changing Hands|work=Broadcasting & Cable|date=2003-03-16|accessdate=2009-09-15[/tpl] and, under Root Communications, "Old school" R & B oldies.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.radioblack.com/South_Carolina.html|title=Darnell's Black Radio Guide - South Carolina Stations|accessdate=2009-09-15[/tpl]Toby Eddings, "Active rock finds an Asylum at 93.5," The Sun News, Jan. 31, 1999. As WURV ("The River") it was classic rock,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ontheradio.net/radiostations/wurvfm.aspx|title=WURV 102.9 FM|publisher=OnTheRadio.net|accessdate=2009-09-15[/tpl] and it was modern rock "Rock 102.9"http://www.grayboxx.com/c/b/5107066.html, Retrieved on 2009-09-15. and then classic hits "102.9 the Point" using the letters WWRK.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.allbusiness.com/services/motion-pictures/4482906-1.html|title=WWRK Flips To Urban AC|first=Dana|last=Hall|work=Radio Monitor|date=2006-09-18|accessdate=2009-09-15[/tpl]
In a deal announced in February 1997, Root Communications Ltd. announced plans to buy eight radio stations owned by Florence-based Atlantic Broadcasting, including WSQN. Qantum Communications Inc. purchased Florence's Root Communications Group LP stations in 2003.

==Translators==

In addition to the main station, WZTF has an additional translator to widen its broadcast area.
