Lloyd Joseph Rigby (born 27 February 1989) is a professional English footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Salford City.

==Career==

Rigby came through Rochdale's youth system, signing a one-year contract in 2007. He was loaned out to Conference North club Vauxhall Motors in January 2008.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.ellesmereportpioneer.co.uk/ellesmere-port-sport/other-sports/2008/08/13/football-rigby-steps-up-to-league-one-after-stockport-seal-deal-with-motors-55940-21521164/|publisher=Ellesmere Port Pioneer|first=Alec|last=Doyle|title=Football: Rigby steps up to League One after Stockport seal deal with Motors|date=13 August 2008|accessdate=19 October 2009[/tpl] He made his debut in their 2–0 defeat away at Redditch United.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.rochdaleafc.co.uk/page/NewsDetail/0,,10441~1209406,00.html|publisher=Rochdale A.F.C.|title=Keeper on Vauxhall|date=8 January 2008|accessdate=19 October 2009[/tpl] After a successful loan period with Vauxhall, Rigby signed a permanent deal with the club at the end of the 2007–08 season, before being taken on trial by League One club Stockport County and eventually signing a permanent contract.
He made his debut on 1 September for Stockport in their 4–1 away victory over Crewe Alexandra in the Football League Trophy.[tpl]cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/8230370.stm|publisher=BBC Sport|title=Crewe 1 – 4 Stockport|date=1 September 2009|accessdate=19 October 2009[/tpl] Rigby spent most of the season on loan at Radcliffe Borough before returning to Stockport, to make a couple of appearances at the end of the 2009–10 season.
He returned to pre-season training at Stockport County at the start of the 2010–11 season, after the club came out of administration. and appeared in a couple of their friendlies, but was released before the season started. Rigby spent some time playing in Belgium, but became homesick and went on to sign for Chorley.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.chorley-guardian.co.uk/sport/Flitcroft-strengthens-his-squad.6648377.jp|publisher=Chorley Guardian|title=Flitcroft strengthens his squad|date=2 December 2010|accessdate=3 December 2010[/tpl]
He played for Leigh Genesis before signing for Salford City in January 2012.
