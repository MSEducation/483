[[Muayad Al-Haddad]]

CATEGORIES: 1960 births, Kuwaiti footballers, Kuwait international footballers, Association football forwards, Khaitan Sporting Club players, 1982 FIFA World Cup players, Living people, Olympic footballers of Kuwait, Footballers at the 1980 Summer Olympics

Muayad Rehayyem Gamal Al-Haddad (born 1 January 1960) is a Kuwaiti former football forward who played for Kuwait in the 1982 FIFA World Cup.1982 FIFA World Cup Spain He also played for Khaitan Sporting Club.
