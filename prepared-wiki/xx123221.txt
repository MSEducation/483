[[Ennenda]]

CATEGORIES: Former municipalities of the canton of Glarus

Ennenda is a former municipality in the canton of Glarus in Switzerland. Effective from 1 January 2011, Ennenda is part of the municipality of Glarus.

==History==

Ennenda is first mentioned around 1303-07 as Obront-Ennant-A and Nydern-Ennant-A.

==Geography==

Ennenda has an area, [tpl]as of|2006|lc=on[/tpl], of [tpl]convert|22.2|km2|sqmi|abbr=on[/tpl].  Of this area, 35.5% is used for agricultural purposes, while 37.7% is forested.  Of the rest of the land, 3.8% is settled (buildings or roads) and the remainder (23%) is non-productive (rivers, glaciers or mountains).

The entire Swiss population is generally well educated.  In Ennenda about 67.5% of the population (between age 25-64) have completed either non-mandatory upper secondary education or additional higher education (either University or a Fachhochschule).

==Transport==

Ennenda railway station is on the Weesen to Linthal railway line. It is served by the Glarner Sprinter express train to Zurich, and by a regional train between Rapperswil and Linthal.
