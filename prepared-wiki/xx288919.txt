[[Khanlar, Iran]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Khanlar ([tpl]lang-fa|خانلار[/tpl], also Romanized as Khānlār)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3773238|Khanlar[/tpl] is a village in Bizineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 230, in 49 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
