[[Sebastian Meijer]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, HV71 players, Leksands IF players, Luleå HF players, VIK Västerås HK players, 1984 births

Sebastian Meijer (born September 1, 1984) is a Swedish professional ice hockey winger who currently plays for Malmö Redhawks of the HockeyAllsvenskan (Swe-1). He has played six seasons in the Swedish top-tier league Elitserien (SEL), as well as six seasons in the second-tier league HockeyAllsvenskan (Swe-1). Internationally Meijer represented the Swedish junior national team at the 2004 World Junior Ice Hockey Championships.
