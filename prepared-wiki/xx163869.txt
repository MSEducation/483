[[Leandro Henrique de Oliveira Bravin]]

CATEGORIES: 1986 births, Living people, Brazilian footballers, Clube Atlético Paranaense players

Leandro Henrique de Oliveira Bravin or simply Leandro Bravin (born May 13, 1986 in Maringá), is a Brazilian attacking midfielder. He is currently a free agent after his Atlético-PR contract expired in September 2007.
