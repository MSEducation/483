[[Roman Catholic Diocese of São Tomé and Príncipe]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in São Tomé and Príncipe, 1534 establishments, Religious organizations established in the 1530s, Religion in São Tomé and Príncipe, Christianity in São Tomé and Príncipe, Roman Catholic Church in São Tomé and Príncipe, Roman Catholic dioceses established in the 16th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of São Tomé and Príncipe  ([tpl]lang-la|Sancti Thomae in Insula[/tpl]) is a diocese, immediately subject to the Holy See, with its seat in the city of São Tomé in São Tomé and Príncipe.

==Special churches==
