[[Chumalu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Chumalu ([tpl]lang-fa|چومالو[/tpl], also Romanized as Chūmālū; also known as Chomalu, Chormāl, Chormālū, and Chūmānlū)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3058816|Chumalu[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Pain Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 382, in 70 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
