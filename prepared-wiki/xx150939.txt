[[Central African Republic League]]

CATEGORIES: Football competitions in the Central African Republic, National association football premier leagues

Central African Republic League is the top football league in the Central African Republic It was created in 1968. The league is sanctioned by the Fédération Centrafricaine de Football, the governing body of football in the CAR.
