[[Tep Sodachan]]

CATEGORIES: Cambodian films, Khmer-language films, 1968 films

Tep Sodachan ([tpl]lang-km|រឿងទិព្វសូដាច័ន្ទ[/tpl]) is a widely acclaimed Cambodian film released in 1968 by Van Chan Pheap Yun. It was directed by Lay Nguon Heng and stars Kong Sam Oeurn, Vichara Dany, and Saksi Sbong. It has become one of the more enduring creations from the nation's pre-communist era and copies are still sold today.
