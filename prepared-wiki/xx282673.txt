[[William Fulbourn]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing, 1441 deaths, 15th-century English people, People from Cambridgeshire, Members of the Parliament of England (pre-1707)

William Fulbourn (died c.1441), of Fulbourn St. Vigors, Cambridgeshire, was an English politician.

==Family==

Fulbourn may have been the illegitimate son of William Fulbourn and Alice Whiting of Fulbourn. William married before 1396.

==Career==

He was a Member (MP) of the Parliament of England for Cambridgeshire in December 1421.http://www.historyofparliamentonline.org/volume/1386-1421/member/fulbourn-william-1441
