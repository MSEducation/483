[[Gary Banks]]

Gary Rashad Banks (born November 4, 1981 in Meridian, Mississippi) is a Free Agent American football wide receiver who most recently played for the San Diego Chargers of the National Football League. He was signed by the Chargers as an undrafted free agent in 2008. He played college football at Troy.

==Early years==

Banks attended Southern Choctaw High School, leading the Indians to back-to-back state titles in 1998 and 1999 while playing quarterback.

==Baseball career==

After high school he was selected in the fifth round of the 2000 Major League Baseball Draft by the Chicago Cubs and played right field for three years as part of the Cubs' minor league system. Banks never played beyond class A, playing two years for the rookie league Arizona League Cubs and two years for the class A Boise Hawks.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.troytrojans.com/ViewArticle.dbml?SPSID=87291&SPID=10377&DB_OEM_ID=17200&ATCLID=1065038&Q_SEASON=2007 |title=Troy Trojans player page |accessdate=2008-07-31 |format= |work=troytrojans.com[/tpl]

==College career==

After baseball, Banks went back to school and played college football at Troy University, switching positions from quarterback to wide receiver. While playing for the Trojans, Gary was used periodically in trick play situations and accumulated a career passing record with 8-of-9 passes completed for 110 yards and two touchdowns.[tpl]cite web |url=http://sports.espn.go.com/ncf/player/profile?playerId=158129 |title=ESPN.com player page |accessdate=2008-07-31 |format= |work=ESPN.com[/tpl]
