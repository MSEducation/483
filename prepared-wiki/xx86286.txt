[[Sabryn Genet]]

CATEGORIES: 1971 births, Living people, American soap opera actresses

Sabryn Genet (born May 14, 1971) as Sabryn Genet Ertle in Orange County, California is an American actress.  
She is best known for her role as Tricia Dennison McNeil in The Young and the Restless
from 1997 to 2001.  She received the Outstanding Female Newcomer Award at the 14th Soap Opera Digest Awards in 1998 for her role as Tricia.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/event/ev0000616/1998|title=Soap Opera Digest Awards (1998)|accessdate=11 October 2011[/tpl]  She has since married and has left show business.http://www.soaptownusa.com/06-04-05AshleyJonesFan15.html
