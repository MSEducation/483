[[Cullen, County Cork]]

CATEGORIES: Towns and villages in County Cork, Civil parishes of County Cork

Cullen ([tpl]Irish place name|Cuillinn[/tpl])Placenames Database of Ireland. Cuillinn Verified 2011-03-27. is a small village in County Cork, Ireland, situated north west of Millstreet town. It is about four miles east of the Kerry border and Rathmore village. St. Laitiaran's Well can be found in the village.
