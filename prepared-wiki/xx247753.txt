[[Paul Clarkson]]

CATEGORIES: English male musical theatre actors, People from Worcester, Living people, Laurence Olivier Award winners, Year of birth missing (living people)

Paul Clarkson is an English actor, theatre director and teacher. He was born and educated in Worcester. 
He won the 1984 Laurence Olivier Award for Best Actor in a Musical for his performance in Howard Goodall's The Hired Man and starred in the European premiere of A Man of No Importance at The Union Theatre Southwark and The Arts Theatre London.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.playbill.com/news/article/133932-Olivier-Winner-Paul-Clarkson-to-Star-in-London-Premiere-of-A-Man-of-No-Importance-|title=Olivier Winner Paul Clarkson to Star in London Premiere of A Man of No Importance|last=Shenton|first=Mark|date=17 October 2009|publisher=Playbill|accessdate=26 June 2011[/tpl]
