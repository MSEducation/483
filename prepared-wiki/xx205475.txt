Henrik Blakskjær (born July 8, 1971) is a Danish sailor and Olympic champion. He won a gold medal in the Soling Class at the 2000 Summer Olympics in Sydney, together with Jesper Bank and Thomas Jacobsen.2000 Summer Olympics – Sydney, Australia – Sailing – databaseOlympics.com (Retrieved on May 23, 2008)
