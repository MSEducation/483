[[New American Music Union]]

CATEGORIES: Music of Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, Festivals in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania

New American Music Union was a two-day summer music festival scheduled on August 8 and 9, 2008, at the SouthSide Works in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.[tpl]cite news | last =McCaffrey  | first =Kate | title =Music festival draws crowd to South Side | newspaper =Pittsburgh Post-Gazette | date =August 9, 2008  | url =http://www.post-gazette.com/ae/2008/08/09/Music-festival-draws-crowd-to-South-Side.html | accessdate = [/tpl][tpl]cite news | last =Mervis | first =Scott | title =Cast of American Eagle's New American Music Union festival set to soar on the South Side | newspaper =Pittsburgh Post-Gazette | date =August 7, 2008  | url =http://www.post-gazette.com/ae/music/2008/08/07/Cast-of-American-Eagle-s-New-American-Music-Union-festival-set-to-soar-on-the-South-Side.html | accessdate = [/tpl] Lineups on two different stages were curated by Anthony Kiedis of the Red Hot Chili Peppers.[tpl]cite news | last =Mervis | first =Scott | title =NAMU: The Chili Pepper's hot bill | newspaper =Pittsburgh Post-Gazette | date =August 7, 2008  | url =http://www.post-gazette.com/ae/music/2008/08/07/NAMU-The-Chili-Pepper-s-hot-bill.html | accessdate = [/tpl]  It featured established music acts along with college bands vying for a recording contract. ==College band competition==

The college bands on the second stage were judged by a panel including industry and media professionals as well as other musicians. The winner received a full-day recording session in a recording studio in Los Angeles and promotion by American Eagle. The Black Fortys from University of Southern Illinois were announced the winners of the best college band by Anthony Kiedis on Saturday August 9.http://www.ae.com/web/77e/music/festival-info/index.html[tpl]cite news | last =Mervis | first =Scott | title =Local Scene: The NAMU winner | newspaper =Pittsburgh Post-Gazette | date =August 14, 2008  | url =http://www.post-gazette.com/ae/2008/08/14/Local-Scene-The-NAMU-winner.html | accessdate = [/tpl]
