[[2813 Zappalà]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Asteroids named for people, Astronomical objects discovered in 1981

2813 Zappalà is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 2030.3075284 days (5.56 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=2813 | title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-05-24 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on November 24, 1981.
