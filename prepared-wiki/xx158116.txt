[[Friedrich August Ukert]]

CATEGORIES: 1780 births, 1851 deaths, German historians, People from Eutin, University of Halle alumni, University of Jena alumni

Friedrich August Ukert (28 October 1780 – 18 May 1851) was a German history scholar, teacher and humanitarian. He was born in Eutin, Bishopric of Lübeck.
He helped Arnold Heeren to found the historical collection, Geschichte der Europaischen Staaten (History of the States of Europe).
The crater Ukert on the Moon is named after him.
