[[Symbion Power]]

CATEGORIES: Engineering companies of the United States, Power station technology, Electric power in Iraq

Symbion Power is a US power engineering and construction firm that has extensive operations in Iraq and Afghanistan. Since 2005 Symbion has been responsible for the construction of transmission and distribution facilities throughout Iraq. In 2005/2006 the company was awarded $250m of US funded competitively bid reconstruction work. Symbion is rated in the top 10 US firms using IRRF funds.
