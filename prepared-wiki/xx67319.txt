[[Audion]]

CATEGORIES: Vacuum tubes

The Audion was an electronic amplifying vacuum tube invented by American electrical engineer Lee De Forest in 1906.De Forest patented a number of variations of his detector tubes starting in 1906. The patent that most clearly covers the Audion is [tpl]US patent|879532[/tpl], Space Telegraphy, filed January 29, 1907, issued February 18, 1908[ref]
