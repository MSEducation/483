[[Middle Brighton railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1861

Middle Brighton railway station is located on the Sandringham line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the south-eastern Melbourne suburb of Brighton opening on 21 September 1861 being renamed Middle Brighton on 1 January 1867.Brighton Beach Vicsig

==Platforms & services==

Middle Brighton has two side platforms. It is serviced by Metro Trains Sandringham line services traveliing between Flinders Street and Sandringham.Sandringham Line timetable Public Transport Victoria
