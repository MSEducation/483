[[King of Hearts (1936 film)]]

CATEGORIES: 1936 films, 1930s comedy films, 1930s romance films, Black-and-white films, British films, English-language films, Films directed by Oswald Mitchell, Films directed by Walter Tennyson, British romance films, British comedy films, Cricklewood Studios films

King of Hearts is a 1936 British romance film directed by Oswald Mitchell and Walter Tennyson and starring Will Fyffe, Richard Dolman and Googie Withers. It was produced by Butcher's Film Service, and made at Cricklewood Studios in London.Wood p.91

==Synopsis==

A working-class boy falls in love with a working-class girl.
