[[Yeovil and District League]]

CATEGORIES: Yeovil, Football leagues in England, Sports leagues established in 1903, 1903 establishments in England, Football in Somerset

The Yeovil and District Football League is a football competition based in England. It has a total of four divisions including the Yeovil and District League Premier Division which sits at level 14 of the English football league system. It is a feeder to the Somerset County League[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.thepyramid.info/sections/Sb4.htm | title=Somerset County League and feeders | publisher=The Pyramid.info | accessdate=25 November 2010[/tpl] and is affiliated to the Somerset County FA.
In 1974 Westland-Yeovil progressed from the Y&DFL to the Western Football League where they spent 6 seasons.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.fchd.info/WESTLANY.HTM | title=Football Club History Database – Westland-Yeovil | publisher= | accessdate=2011-05-23[/tpl]
