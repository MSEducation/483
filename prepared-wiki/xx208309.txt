[[Naradmuni Thulung]]

CATEGORIES: Government ministers of Nepal, Nepali Congress politicians, Year of birth missing, Possibly living people

Naradmuni Thulung was a Nepalese politician, belonging to the Nepali Congress. In November 1951 he became Minister of Local Self-Development in the cabinet of Matrika Prasad Koirala. He held the position until August 1952.http://www.healthnet.org.np/users/dixit/ANNEX-III.PDF He also served as Defence minister and acted as Prime Minister during Matrika P Koirala's premiership.[tpl]Citation needed|date=October 2012[/tpl] Thulung became the chairman of the Royal Land Reform Commission. Under Thulung's chairmanship the commission suggested introduction of land limits, tenancy and agriculture loans. These reforms were, however, not implemented.http://www2.army.mod.uk/linkedfiles/bgn/reset_brief/bgn_reset_brief_land_tenancypdf.pdf
