[[Ramongo Department]]

CATEGORIES: Departments of Burkina Faso, Boulkiemdé Province

Ramongo is a department or commune of Boulkiemdé Province in central Burkina Faso. As of 2005 it has a population of 28,326. Its capital lies at the town of Ramongo.Burkinabé government inforoute communale
