[[Ashton, Maryland]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Maryland, Unincorporated communities in Montgomery County, Maryland

Ashton is an unincorporated community in Montgomery County, Maryland.[tpl]gnis|582943[/tpl] The commercial center of Ashton lies at the junction of Route 108 (Ashton Road) and New Hampshire Avenue (Route 650).
The United States Census Bureau combines Ashton with the nearby community of Sandy Spring to form the census-designated place of Ashton-Sandy Spring, and all census data are tabulated for this combined entity. The Sandy Spring Museum, located in the heart of Sandy Spring, is a historical museum offering educational displays and programs.

==Development==

A new village shopping center was planned to begin construction in 2006  at the corner of Route 108 and New Hampshire Avenue, where the current Sandy Spring Bank offices are located, consisting of about a 7 to [tpl]convert|10|acre|m2|sing=on[/tpl] plot. It would possibly be anchored with a grocery store, as yet undetermined. Opinions vary on the effects of the plan on Ashton. As of 2013, construction has not begun.
