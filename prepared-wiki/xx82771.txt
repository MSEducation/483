[[Idiosepius]]

CATEGORIES: Bobtail squid

Idiosepius is a genus of bobtail squid encompassing around eight species. It is the sole genus of the family Idiosepiidae.
Members of this genus represent no interest to commercial fisheries.Reid, A. 2005. Family Idiosepiidae. In: P. Jereb & C.F.E. Roper, eds. Cephalopods of the world.
