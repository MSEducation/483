[[Muddy Creek (Colorado)]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of Colorado, Geography of Grand County, Colorado, Tributaries of the Colorado River in Colorado

Muddy Creek is a tributary of the Colorado River, approximately [tpl]convert|60.5|mi[/tpl] long,U.S. Geological Survey. National Hydrography Dataset high-resolution flowline data. The National Map, accessed March 18, 2011 in north central Colorado in the United States.
It rises in northwestern Grand County, in the Routt National Forest west of Rabbit Ears Pass at the continental divide. It flows south, east, then southwest, and joins the Colorado near Kremmling.

==Wolford Mountain Reservoir==

The creek was dammed in 1996 to create the Wolford Mountain Reservoir, which forms part of the Wolford Mountain Recreation Area.Colorado River District. Wolford Mountain Reservoir
