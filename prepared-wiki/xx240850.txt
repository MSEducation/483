[[Linghai]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Liaoning

Linghai ([tpl]zh|c=凌海 |p=Línghǎi[/tpl]) is a city in the west of Liaoning province, Northeast China. Lying on the west (right) bank of the Daling River (大凌河), which flows into the Liaodong Bay near the city, it is under the administration of Jinzhou City, the seat of which is [tpl]convert|21|km[/tpl].

==Administrative Divisions锦州市==

There are 3 subdistricts, 11 towns, 7 townships, and 1 ethnic township under the city's administration.
