[[5 x Deutschland]]

CATEGORIES: 2007 films, German films, German-language films, German short films

5 x Deutschland is a German short film directed by Thierry Bruehl. It was released in 2007.
In the form of five video clips are shown from the so-called socially disadvantaged young people. This answered the young people of Aachen, Bremen, Dortmund, Munich and Magdeburg questions about the origins, goals and perspectives.
Shown at various festivals, the film attracted some attention and thus achieved over 60,000 spectators.
