[[Guran, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Guran ([tpl]lang-fa|گوران[/tpl], also Romanized as Gūrān)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3065142|Guran[/tpl] is a village in Aq Bolagh Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 375, in 68 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
