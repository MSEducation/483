[[Sat Paul Mittal]]

CATEGORIES: Nominated members of the Rajya Sabha, Punjabi politicians, Indian National Congress politicians, Rajya Sabha members from Punjab, 1931 births, 1992 deaths

Sat Paul Mittal (1931-1992) was an Indian politician from Punjab. He was a member of the Punjab Legislative Council (1964-70) and a Deputy Minister in the Government of Punjab. He was elected as a member of the Rajya Sabha for two terms from 1976 to 1982 and again from 1982 to 1988. He was nominated as a member of the same house in 1988 and served till 1992. Mittal was a member of the Indian National Congress and served as the Secretary of Punjab P.C.C. in 1972.
His son Sunil Mittal is a telecom mogul, philanthropist and the founder, chairman and Group CEO of Bharti Enterprises.
