[[Alyson Hannigan]]

CATEGORIES: 1974 births, 20th-century American actresses, 21st-century American actresses, Actresses from Atlanta, Georgia, Actresses from Los Angeles, California, Actresses from Washington, D.C., American child actresses, American film actresses, American people of Irish descent, American stage actresses, American television actresses, Jewish American actresses, California State University, Northridge alumni, Living people

Alyson Lee Hannigan (born March 24, 1974) is an American actress. She is best known for her roles as Willow Rosenberg in the television series Buffy the Vampire Slayer (1997–2003), Lily Aldrin on the CBS sitcom How I Met Your Mother (2005–2014) and Michelle Flaherty in the American Pie film series (1999–present).[tpl]cite web |title=How I Met Your Mother Cast: Alyson Hannigan |work=CBS|url=http://www.cbs.com/shows/how_i_met_your_mother/cast/61492 |accessdate=2012-06-12[/tpl]

==Early life==

Hannigan was born in Washington, D.C., the only child of Emilie (Posner) Haas, a real estate agent, and Al Hannigan, a truck driver.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.filmreference.com/film/58/Alyson-Hannigan.html |title=Film Reference Alyson Hannigan Bio |publisher=Filmreference.com |date= |accessdate=2010-03-07| archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20100210084454/http://www.filmreference.com/film/58/Alyson-Hannigan.html| archivedate= 10 February 2010 | deadurl= no[/tpl] Her father is of Irish descent and her mother is Jewish.[tpl]cite web |title=Celebrity Jews |work=the Jewish News Weekly of Northern California |url=http://www.jweekly.com/article/full/23122/celebrity-jews/ |date=2004-07-09 |author=Bloom, Nate |accessdate=2006-07-16[/tpl]http://www.interfaithfamily.com/arts_and_entertainment/popular_culture/Hollywood_Now_Alyson_Hannigan_Jewish_Male_Actors_Fall_Features.shtml Her parents divorced when she was two and she was raised mostly by her mother in Atlanta, Georgia.[tpl]IMDb name|4989|section=bio[/tpl]

==Career==

===1978–1996===

At age four, Hannigan began appearing in commercials.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.scotsman.com/scotland-on-sunday/scotland/interview-alyson-hannigan-on-american-pie-1-2248522 |title=Interview: Alyson Hannigan on American Pie |publisher=Scotland on Sunday |date=April 22, 2012 |accessdate=2012-06-12[/tpl] She moved to Hollywood at age 11.[tpl]cite web|url=http://blogs.ajc.com/radio-tv-talk/2009/09/21/former-atlantan-alyson-hannigan-interview-for-how-i-met-your-mother/ |title= Former Atlantan Alyson Hannigan interview for ‘How I Met Your Mother’ |publisher=The Atlanta Journal-Constitution |date=September 21, 2009 |accessdate=2012-06-12[/tpl] Living with her mother and attending North Hollywood High School, she successfully auditioned for agents while visiting her father in Santa Barbara. After attending North Hollywood High School, she attended California State University, Northridge where she earned a degree in psychology.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.superiorpics.com/alyson_hannigan/ |title=Alyson Hannigan |publisher=SuperiorPics.com |date= |accessdate=2010-04-18[/tpl]
Hannigan's first major film role was in My Stepmother Is an Alien, a science fiction comedy released in 1988; one of her co-stars in the film was actor Seth Green, who later joined her in the regular cast of Buffy as her on-screen boyfriend. Then in 1989, her first regular role on a TV series came when she was cast in the short-lived ABC sitcom Free Spirit. As a teenager, Hannigan babysat for the kids of Bob Saget, who later narrated her TV series, How I Met Your Mother."It's Evening in America". Vanity Fair. May 2012. Page 158.

===1997–present===

In 1997, Hannigan was cast to play Willow Rosenberg, Buffy Summers' best friend, on the television series Buffy the Vampire Slayer.[tpl]cite web |title=Where Are They Now? Buffy the Vampire Slayer|work=People|url=http://www.people.com/people/article/0,,20170817,00.html|date=December 3, 2007 |accessdate=2012-06-12[/tpl] The show became a success, and Hannigan gained recognition, subsequently appearing in several notable films aimed at teenage audiences, including American Pie, American Pie 2, Boys and Girls and American Wedding. She also had a guest spot on the Buffy spin-off, Angel, reprising her role of Willow in a few episodes (including most notably "Orpheus," during the fourth season of Angel and the seventh season of Buffy), but none after Buffy finished production.
In early 2004, Hannigan made her West End debut, starring in a stage adaptation of When Harry Met Sally... at the Theatre Royal Haymarket, opposite Luke Perry.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.britishtheatreguide.info/reviews/harrysally-rev.htm |title=When Harry Met Sally, Review by Philip Fisher (2004) |publisher=British Theatre Guide |date= |accessdate=2010-04-18[/tpl]
In 2005, Hannigan returned to starring in a regular television series, appearing in the hit comedy How I Met Your Mother as Lily, and also playing a recurring guest role on Veronica Mars as Trina Echolls.[tpl]cite web|url=http://featuresblogs.chicagotribune.com/entertainment_tv/2006/01/veronica_mars_s.html |title='Veronica Mars' scoop  |publisher=Chicago Tribune |date= January 20, 2006|accessdate=2012-06-12[/tpl]
In February 2006, Hannigan starred as Julia Jones in Date Movie, a parody of romantic comedies. She was also a guest star on the ABC animated sitcom The Goode Family in 2009.[tpl]IMDb name|4989[/tpl]
Hannigan joined forces with Emily Deschanel, Jaime King, Minka Kelly, and Katharine McPhee in a "video slumber party" featured on FunnyorDie.com to promote regular breast cancer screenings for the organization Stand Up 2 Cancer.[tpl]cite news | author=Kate Stanhope | title=Deschanel, Hannigan and Others Cop a Feel for a Cause | url=http://www.tvguide.com/News/Deschanel-Hannigan-Cop-1010367.aspx | work=TVGuide.com | date=2009-10-01 | accessdate=2009-10-01| archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20091001221130/http://www.tvguide.com/News/Deschanel-Hannigan-Cop-1010367.aspx| archivedate= 1 October 2009 | deadurl= no[/tpl]
In 2012, Hannigan reprised the role of Michelle in American Reunion.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.usatoday.com/life/television/news/story/2012-04-04/how-i-met-your-mother-alyson-hannigan-cobie-smulders/54301680/1 |title=Alyson Hannigan, Cobie Smulders and 'Mother'-hood|publisher=USA Today |date=April 15, 2012|accessdate=2012-06-12[/tpl]

==Personal life==

Hannigan married fellow Buffy the Vampire Slayer actor Alexis Denisof at Two Bunch Palms Resort in Desert Hot Springs, California, on October 11, 2003. The couple have two daughters, Satyana Marie Denisof, born March 24, 2009,[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.people.com/people/article/0,,20268972,00.html |title=Alyson Hannigan Welcomes a Daughter! |accessdate= 2009-03-31 |first=Ulrica |last=Wihlborg |date=2009-03-31 |publisher=People[/tpl] and Keeva Jane Denisof, born May 23, 2012.[tpl]cite web|url=http://celebritybabies.people.com/2012/06/12/alyson-hannigan-alexis-denisof-welcome-daughter-keeva-jane/ |title=Alyson Hannigan and Alexis Denisof Welcome Daughter Keeva Jane |publisher=People |date=June 12, 2012|accessdate=2012-06-12[/tpl]
Hannigan and her husband are godparents to Joss Whedon's son, Arden.Hannigan is godmother of Joss Whedon's son, Alyson Hannigan.

===Stalking===

On February 13, 2013, Hannigan was granted a temporary restraining order against New Hampshire resident John Hobbs after he began sending her threatening and obsessive messages via the Internet, wishing to meet and possibly kill her.
