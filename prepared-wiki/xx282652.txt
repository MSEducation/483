[[Liubova Pogovitchaikova]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Soviet female cyclists, UCI World Champions

Liubova Pogovitchaikova (also written as Ljubow Pogowitschnikowa) is a retired Soviet Union female road cyclist. She became World Champion in the women's team time trial at the UCI Road World Championships in 1987.[tpl]cite book|title=Ljubow Pogowitschnikowa|url=http://www.cyclingarchives.com/coureurfiche.php?coureurid=62494|publisher=cyclingarchives.com|accessdate=26 July 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.memoire-du-cyclisme.eu/feminines/mondial_clmpe_1987.php|work=memoire-du-cyclisme.eu|accessdate=10 August 2013|title= 1er Championnat du Monde féminin CLM par équipes 1987[/tpl] [tpl]fr icon[/tpl]
