[[Alhard Freiherr von der Borch]]

CATEGORIES: 1916 births, 1975 deaths, People from Berlin, German military personnel of World War II, Recipients of the Knight's Cross, Barons of Germany, People from the Province of Brandenburg

Alhard Freiherr von der Borch[tpl]ref label|Note1|a|a[/tpl] (10 February 1916 – 2 November 1975) was a highly decorated Oberstleutnant in the Wehrmacht during World War II and a recipient of the Knight's Cross of the Iron Cross. The Knight's Cross of the Iron Cross was awarded to recognise extreme battlefield bravery or successful military leadership.
