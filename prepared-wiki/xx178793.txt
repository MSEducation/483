[[Gmina Nowy Duninów]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Płock County

Gmina Nowy Duninów is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Płock County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Nowy Duninów, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|16|km|mi|0[/tpl] west of Płock and [tpl]convert|112|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] west of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|144.79|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 3,884.
The gmina contains part of the protected area called Gostynin-Włocławek Landscape Park.

==Villages==

Gmina Nowy Duninów contains the villages and settlements of Brwilno, Brwilno Dolne, Brzezinna Góra, Duninów Duży, Dzierzązna, Grodziska, Jeżewo, Kamion, Karolewo, Lipianki, Nowa Wieś, Nowy Duninów, Popłacin, Soczewka, Środoń, Stary Duninów, Trzcianno and Wola Brwileńska.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Nowy Duninów is bordered by the city of Płock and by the gminas of Baruchowo, Brudzeń Duży, Gostynin, Łąck, Stara Biała and Włocławek.
