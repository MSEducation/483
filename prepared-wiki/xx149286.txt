[[Jerzy Rudlicki]]

CATEGORIES: 1893 births, 1977 deaths, Polish aerospace engineers

Rudlicki Jerzy (1893-1977) was a Polish aerospace engineer who invented and in 1930 patented  the V-tail (Polish patent# 15938) configuration for aircraft combining the rudder and elevators in one system. He both worked in the World War I and World War II on aircraft. He was the main designer of the Polish Plage i La≈õkiewicz works, designing aircraft under Lublin brand.
