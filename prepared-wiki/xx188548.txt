[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Luziânia]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1989, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Brasília, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Luziânia ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Lucianiensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Luziânia in the Ecclesiastical province of Brasília in Brazil.
