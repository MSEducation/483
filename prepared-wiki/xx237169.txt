[[The Catholic Historical Review]]

CATEGORIES: Publications established in 1915, Quarterly journals, English-language journals, Religion history journals, The Catholic University of America

The Catholic Historical Review is the official organ of the American Catholic Historical Association. It was founded at The Catholic University of America in 1915 and is published by The Catholic University of America Press. The journal publishes articles and book reviews received in all areas of ecclesiastical history.
