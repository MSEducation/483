Mary Beth Evans (born March 7, 1961) is an American television actress, best known for her role as Kayla Brady on Days of our Lives.

==Career==

Evans is most famous for her role as Kayla Brady in the soap opera Days of our Lives from 1986 to 1992, 2006 to 2009, and 2010 to present.
