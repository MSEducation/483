Timo Heinze (born 23 February 1986 in Rosenheim, Bavaria) is a German retired footballer who last played for Fortuna Köln.

==Career==

Heinze began his career with SC Westerndorf before joining  TSV 1860 Rosenheim. In 1998, After six years with TSV 1860 Rosenheim he signed a youth contract with FC Bayern Munich and was promoted to the reserve team in 2004.Ein Bayer für die SpVgg In 2009, Heinze left FC Bayern Munich the club and signed for local rivals SpVgg Unterhaching on a three year contract.Unterhaching holt Außenverteidiger Heinze He left the club after one year. After a year without a club, he signed for Fortuna Köln in 2011.
