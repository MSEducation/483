[[Arnaud Chaffanjon]]

CATEGORIES: French writers, 1929 births, 1992 deaths

Arnaud Chaffanjon (born on 23 April 1929 and deceased on 22 November 1992) was a French specialist in heraldry and aristocratic genealogy. He was journalist at Point de Vue.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.elpais.com/articulo/agenda/Arnaud/Chaffanjon/periodista/reyes/elpepigen/19921128elpepiage_3/Tes?print=1 |title=Arnaud Chaffanjon, el periodista de los reyes |language=Spanish |publisher=El País[/tpl]  He is known for his seminal works on the history of the European aristocratic dynasties, such as Le Petit Gotha Illustré (1968), Les Grands Ordres de Chevalerie (1969), Les Grandes familles de l'Histoire de France (1980), L'Année Princière dans le Monde (1985).
