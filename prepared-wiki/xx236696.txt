[[Nathanael Greene Middle School]]

CATEGORIES: Public middle schools in Rhode Island, Schools in Providence County, Rhode Island, School districts in Rhode Island, Education in Providence, Rhode Island

Nathanael Greene Middle School is located in Providence, Rhode Island and is a part of the Providence Public School District. It has a capacity of 1000 students and serves grades 6-8. Built in 1929 the school is named after Continental Army general, Nathanael Greene.
