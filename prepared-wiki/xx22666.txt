U-238's third and last patrol began in January 1944, and lasted a fruitless month, until on 9 February, she was caught by convoy escorts of [[List of World War II convoys|
SL-147]] and MKS-38 [tpl]convert|270|nmi|km[/tpl] off Cape Clear. She counter-attacked, unsuccessfully, and was sunk by the sloops, [tpl]HMS|Kite|U87|6[/tpl], [tpl]HMS|Magpie|U82|2[/tpl] and [tpl]HMS|Starling|U66|2[/tpl]. There were no survivors.Kemp, pp. 167-168.
