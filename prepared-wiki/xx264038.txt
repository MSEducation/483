[[Villa Loretto]]

CATEGORIES: Residential buildings on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Residential buildings completed in 1928, Houses in Westchester County, New York

Villa Loretto is a historic institutional building located at Peekskill, Westchester County, New York. It was built in 1928 by the Sisters of the Good Shepherd for the housing and treatment of young delinquent women.  It is a three to four story, "H" shaped brick building containing more than 175,000 square feet. The facility closed in June 1975.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=10664|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: Villa Loretto|date=December 1987|accessdate=2011-01-01 |author=Neil Larson|publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation[/tpl] See also: [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=10642|title=Accompanying 11 photos[/tpl]
It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1989.
