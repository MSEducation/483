[[Antoinette Spolar]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, American actresses

Antoinette Spolar is an American actress who is best known for playing the role of Larry David's receptionist on the hit HBO series Curb Your Enthusiasm. She has made many guest appearances on shows such as Friends and Cow and Chicken. She was also an impersonator on Invader Zim. The celebrities she impersonated were Haylie Duff, Sheryl Crow, and Edie McClurg.
