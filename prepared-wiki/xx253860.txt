[[W34DV]]

CATEGORIES: Television stations in Mississippi, Television channels and stations established in 1984, Channel 34 low-power TV stations in the United States

W34DV Channel 34 TV is a Class A television station located in Booneville, Mississippi.http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/tvq?list=0&facid=68905  The station is owned and operated by the Unity Broadcasting Network.  The station is currently broadcasting an analog signal and hopes to go digital in the near future.http://ubntv.org/ubntv/index.html
