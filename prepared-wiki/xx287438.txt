[[Daniyar Kairov]]

CATEGORIES: 1994 births, Living people, Kazakhstani ice hockey players, Nomad Astana players, Snezhnye Barsy players, Barys Astana players

Daniyar Zhandosuly Kairov ([tpl]lang-kk|Данияр Жандосұлы Қайыров[/tpl]; born April 4, 1994) is a Kazakhstani professional ice hockey defenceman currently playing for the Barys Astana in the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). He was drafted in the first round 19th overall in the 2011 KHL Junior Draft by SKA Saint Petersburg.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.eliteprospects.com/draft_other2.php?year=2011&type=KHL%20Draft |title=2011 KHL Junior Draft|publisher=Elite Prospects|date= |accessdate=August 13, 2013[/tpl] On June 6, 2011, his rights were traded to Barys Astana for 2nd and 3rd round draft picks in 2012.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Gazeta.ru|title=SKA traded Kairov for two draft picks in 2012 |url=http://www.gazeta.ru/news/sport/2011/06/08/n_1874969.shtml|language=ru |date=June 6, 2011|accessdate=August 13, 2013[/tpl]
