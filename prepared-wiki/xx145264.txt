[[1978 English cricket season]]

CATEGORIES: English cricket seasons from 1969 to 2000, 1978 in cricket, 1978 in English sport, 1978 in English cricket

The 1978 English cricket season was played amidst growing concerns about the impact of World Series Cricket.  On the domestic front, Kent was the most outstanding county team despite Alan Knott and Derek Underwood having joined WSC.
