[[Charles Montague Bakewell]]

CATEGORIES: 1867 births, 1957 deaths, Burials at Grove Street Cemetery, Connecticut State Senators, Members of the United States House of Representatives from Connecticut, University of California, Berkeley alumni, Harvard University alumni

Charles Montague Bakewell (April 24, 1867 – September 19, 1957) was a university professor and Republican politician. He was born in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. He became a university professor, then a member of the Connecticut state senate, 1920–24; United States Representative from Connecticut at-large, 1933–35; delegate to the Republican National Convention from Connecticut, 1936. He died in New Haven, Connecticut, and was interred at Grove Street Cemetery.http://bioguide.congress.gov/scripts/biodisplay.pl?index=B000079
