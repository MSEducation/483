[[Etienne van der Linde]]

CATEGORIES: 1978 births, Living people, Sportspeople from Johannesburg, South African racing drivers, White South African people, Afrikaner people, South African people of Dutch descent, British Formula Renault 2.0 drivers, German Formula Three Championship drivers, Auto GP World Series drivers

Etienne van der Linde (born 25 February 1978 in Johannesburg) is a South African racing driver. He has competed in such series as Euro Formula 3000 and the German Formula Three Championship.
