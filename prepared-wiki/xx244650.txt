Rob Haworth (born 21 November 1975) is a footballer who plays for St Albans City, and who started his career playing in the Football League, before playing for a significant number of non-league clubs in London and the South East.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.dartfordfc.co.uk/teams.htm|title=Rob Haworth|work=The Teams 2009–2010|publisher=Dartford FC|accessdate=6 April 2012[/tpl]
He started his career in the Football League with Fulham,Fulham Players Football League Stats at Neil Brown and he made 29 senior appearances for them,Dartford – player profiles with his last League appearance for Fulham being in Football League Division 3 on 8 January 1995, when he came on as a substitute against Chesterfield.[tpl]cite book |title=Rothmans Football Yearbook 1995–96 |last=Rollin (ed) |first=Jack |year=1995 |publisher=Headline |isbn=0-7472-7823-7 [/tpl] He then moved to Kettering Town, and he made a total of 13 appearances for them in the 1995–96 season, having made his debut in December 1995.Stats at poppiesfans.com – Kettering Town Supporters [tpl]Dead link|date=March 2013[/tpl] As of April 1996 he was playing for Aylesbury United in the Isthmian League.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.aylesburyunitedfc.co.uk/match/1996-04-27/worthing/away/ |title=Match Reports –  Worthing 1 Aylesbury Utd 3 |author= |date=27 April 1996 |work= |publisher=Aylesburyunitedfc.co.uk |accessdate=15 May 2010[/tpl] In the summer of 1996 he played four league matches for TPS in the Finnish Veikkausliiga, scoring one goal, on 6 June 1996 against Mikkelin Palloilijat.[tpl]Cite book |title=The European Football Yearbook 1997/98 |last=Hammond (ed) |first=Mike |year=1997 |publisher=Sports Projects Ltd |isbn=0-946866-42-2 [/tpl]
