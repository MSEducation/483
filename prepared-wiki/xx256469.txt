[[LIDAX]]

CATEGORIES: Aerospace companies

LIDAX is a Spain space engineering company, founded at the beginning of the year 2000. It designs and manufactures advance mechanical systems for on-ground (OGSE & MGSE) and space scientific instrumentation from conceptual design to integration and testing. 
It employs a group of engineers and technicians representing practically the 90% of the company staff.
The company is headquartered in Torrejón de Ardoz, near Madrid, Spain. It operates next to the Spain's space agency Instituto Nacional de Técnica Aeroespacial (INTA, [tpl]lang-en|National Institute of Aerospace Technology[/tpl]) and other known space industries such as EADS CASA Espacio part of EADS Astrium.
