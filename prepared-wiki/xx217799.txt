[[Ağbulaq, Nakhchivan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Shakhbuz District

Ağbulaq (until 2003,Azerbaijan Parliament - law renaming the village and municipality Geçəzur, Gecəzur, Gedzhazur, and Gidzhadzur) is a village and municipality in the Shakhbuz Rayon of Nakhchivan, Azerbaijan.  It has a population of 436.
