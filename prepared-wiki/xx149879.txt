[[Padraic Kenney]]

CATEGORIES: American historians, History teachers, Indiana University faculty, Living people, 1963 births, University of Colorado faculty, American political writers

Padraic Jeremiah Kenney (born 1963) is a professor of history at Indiana University.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.indiana.edu/~histweb/faculty/Display.php?Faculty_ID=15|title=Faculty and Research: Padraic Kenney|publisher=Indiana University Bloomington|accessdate=24 April 2011[/tpl] 
He is the author of several books on East European (particularly Polish) history and politics; his area of specialization is social change and political change. He was awarded a grant under the Fulbright Program in 2005.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www2.ed.gov/programs/iegpsfra/fragrantees2005.xls|title=FY 2005 Grantees by World Area for the Faculty Research Abroad Program (Excel)|publisher=U.S. Department of Education|accessdate=24 April 2011[/tpl]
