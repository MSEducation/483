[[Pitumarca District]]

Pitumarca District is one of eight districts of the province Canchis in Peru.[tpl]es icon[/tpl] Instituto Nacional de Estadística e Informática. Banco de Información Distrital. Retrieved April 11, 2008.

==Geography==

The Willkanuta mountain range lies in the district. Some of the highest mountains situated in  the district are Chumpi, Hatunuma, Hatun Ñañu Punta, Kuntur Ikiña, Wayruru Punku, Wila Jaqhi, Yanaqaqa and Yayamari.

==Ethnic groups==

The people in the district are mainly indigenous citizens of Quechua descent.
