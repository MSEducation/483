[[Guyang County]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Guyang County (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠭᠦᠶᠠᠩ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠰᠢᠶᠠᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Güyaŋ siyan[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=固阳县 |t=固陽縣 |p=Gùyáng Xiàn[/tpl]) is a county in western Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China. It is under the administration of Baotou City, [tpl]convert|44|km[/tpl] to the south-southwest.
