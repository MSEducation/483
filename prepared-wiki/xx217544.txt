[[Woman with bicycle]]

CATEGORIES: Murals in New York City, New York City cultural history, Cycling media

Woman with Bicycle is a six-story wall painting created by the American painter, sculptor and muralist Knox Martin in 1979 under the sponsorship of the Public Art Fund. A well-known New York art landmark, Woman with Bicycle was located at Houston and MacDougal Streets in Manhattan.Art - Knox Martin - Angry But More Human - Nytimes.Com In 2002 Woman with Bicycle was covered over with an underwear ad.
