[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Patos de Minas]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1955, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Uberaba, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Patos de Minas ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Patensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Patos de Minas in the Ecclesiastical province of Uberaba in Brazil.
