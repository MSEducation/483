[[Ikusaka, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Ikusaka|生坂村|Ikusaka-mura[/tpl] is a village located in Higashichikuma District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the village has an estimated population of 2,286 and a density of 58.66 persons per km². The total area is 38.97 km². It contains the Ikusaka Dam.
