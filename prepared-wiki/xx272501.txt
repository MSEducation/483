[[Peklo milencov]]

CATEGORIES: 1994 singles, Marika Gombitová songs, Songs written by Marika Gombitová, Songs written by Kamil Peteraj

"Peklo milencov" ([tpl]lang-en|Lovers' Hell[/tpl]) is a song by Marika Gombitová released on Jumbo Records in 1994.[tpl]harvnb|Graclík|Nekvapil|2008|p=393.[/tpl]For Marika Gombitová's discography, see [tpl]harvnb|Lehotský|2008a|pp=54–56.[/tpl].
The composition wrote Gombitová in common with Kamil Peteraj. B-side featured "Paradiso", also written by themselves. Both songs were released on the singer's last studio album to date Zostaň (1994).[tpl]harvnb|Graclík|Nekvapil|2008|p=399.[/tpl]
