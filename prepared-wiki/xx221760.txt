[[Perigeo]]

CATEGORIES: Italian progressive rock groups, Jazz fusion ensembles, RCA Records artists

Perigeo are an Italian progressive rock group that released a group of albums for RCA Italiana in the 1970s.  Several of the members went on to have long careers in jazz.Allmusic
The group had subsequent editions under the names New Perigeo and Perigeo Special.Allmusic
