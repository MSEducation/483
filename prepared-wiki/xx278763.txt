[[Valiyaran]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Valiyaran ([tpl]lang-fa|ولياران[/tpl], also Romanized as Valīyārān and Valyārān; also known as Valgaran and Walgaran)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3088077|Valiyaran[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 605, in 132 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
