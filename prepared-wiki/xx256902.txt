[[St. Bursouma's Orthodox Church]]

CATEGORIES: Churches in Kerala, Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church

St. Bursouma's Orthodox Syrian Church, Attuva is a parish under the  Malankara Orthodox Church. Mar Bursouma is the patron saint of the parish. The parish is coming under Metropolitan of the Diocese of Chengannur in Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church. The Church celebrated its centenary in February 2012.

==Location==

Parish is situated in Attuva,(between Pandalam and Mavelikkara) in Alappuzha District in the State of Kerala.

==History==

Established in 1912, it is one of the first churches established in the name of Mar Bursouma in Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church.
