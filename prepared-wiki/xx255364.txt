[[Kõiv]]

CATEGORIES: Estonian-language surnames

Kõiv is an Estonian surname. The word means "birch" in Võro. 
According to the Estonian Population Register, there lived 1,242 people with the surname Kõiv in Estonina, as of 8 May 2012.https://www.eesti.ee/portaal/rrteenus.nimede_stat
