[[Bank of America Fifth Avenue Plaza]]

CATEGORIES: Bank of America buildings, Office buildings completed in 1981, Office buildings in Seattle, Washington, Skyscrapers in Seattle, Washington, Skyscrapers between 150 and 199 meters, Bank buildings in Washington (state)

Bank of America Fifth Avenue Plaza is a [tpl]convert|166|m|abbr=on[/tpl] skyscraper in Seattle, Washington. It was constructed from 1979 to 1981 and has 42 floors. It is the 9th tallest building in Seattle and was designed by 3D/International. The building has been able to retain a minimum of 98% occupancy since it was opened.
