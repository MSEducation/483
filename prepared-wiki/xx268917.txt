[[Never Ending Tour 2005]]

CATEGORIES: Bob Dylan concert tours, 2005 concert tours

The Never Ending Tour is the popular name for Bob Dylan's endless touring schedule since June 7, 1988.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/music/2011/jun/12/bob-dylan-never-ending-tour |title=Bob Dylan begins his 'never-ending' tour |publisher=The Guardian |date=2011-06-12 |accessdate=2011-11-26[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.rollingstone.com/music/photos/the-evolution-of-bob-dylan-20110509/the-never-ending-tour-begins-1988-1996-0242530 |title=The Evolution of Bob Dylan |publisher=Rolling Stone Magazine |date= |accessdate=2011-11-26[/tpl]

==Tour==

Dylan started to 2005 tour with a tour of the United States with Merle Haggard.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.glidemagazine.com/articles/46883/bob-dylan-merle-haggard-3-19-2005.html |title=Bob Dylan & Merle Haggard 3/19/2005 |publisher=Glide Magazine |date= |accessdate=2011-12-26[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://studioclub.com/events/Dylan%20Haggard%20Tour/DylanHaggardJournal.htm |title=Concert Review |publisher=Studio Club |date= |accessdate=2011-12-26[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://boblinks.com/dates23.html |title=Bob Dylan 2005 Spring & Summer Tour Guide |publisher=BobLinks |date= |accessdate=2011-12-26[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://bjorner.com/DSN26830%20-%202005%20US%20Spring%20Tour.htm |title= Still On The Road: 2005 US Spring Tour with Merle Haggard |publisher=Bjorner |date= |accessdate=2011-12-13[/tpl] The tour included several residencies at various venues. These included three nights in Seattle, two nights in Portland, three nights in Oakland, five nights in Los Angeles, two nights in Denver, five nights in Chicago, two nights in Milwaukee, three nights in Boston and five nights in New York City.
After completing the first leg of the tour Dylan started out on a US ballpark tour with Willie Nelson starting in South Fort Myers, Florida on May 25 and ended Saint Paul, Minnesota on July 12.[tpl]cite web|url=http://bjorner.com/DSN27220%20-%202005%20US%20Summer%20Tour.htm |title= Still On The Road: 2005 US Spring Tour with Willie Nelson |publisher=Bjorner |date= |accessdate=2011-12-13[/tpl]
Five days after the end of the previous leg Dylan performed six shows in Canada without any co-headliners and five shows in the United States.[tpl]cite web|url=http://bjorner.com/DSN27540%20-%202005%20Canada%20Summer%20Tour.htm |title= Still On The Road: 2005 Canada & US North West Summer Tour |publisher=Bjorner |date= |accessdate=2011-12-13[/tpl]
Dylan returned to Europe after 15 months in the fall of 2005. He performed 31 shows over the space of two months covering 13 countries and 27 cities. The tour started in Stockholm, Sweden on October 17 and finished on November 27 in Dublin, Ireland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://bjorner.com/DSN27650%20-%202005%20Europe%20Fall%20Tour.htm |title= Still On The Road: 2005 Europe Fall Tour |publisher=Bjorner |date= |accessdate=2011-12-13[/tpl]
