[[André Künzi]]

CATEGORIES: 1967 births, Swiss ice hockey players, Olympic ice hockey players of Switzerland, Living people, Ice hockey players at the 1988 Winter Olympics, Ice hockey players at the 1992 Winter Olympics, EV Zug players

André Künzi (born January 10, 1967 in Zäziwil, Switzerland) is a former Swiss ice hockey player who played for EV Zug in the National League A. He also played for the Switzerland men's national ice hockey team at the 1988 and 1992 Olympics.
