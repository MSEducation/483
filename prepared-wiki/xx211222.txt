Kevin Nolan is a Gaelic footballer for Dublin and Kilmacud Crokes. He has made his Championship debut for Dublin against Wexford in the Leinster Senior Football Championship final.http://www.hill16.ie/viewstoryhill16.asp?mainheading=Word+on+The+Hill&id=10014&viewstory=yes

==Playing career==

Nolan won the Dublin Senior Football Championship with Kilmacud Crokes in 2008. He then won a Leinster Senior Club Football Championship, Dublin Division One AFL and the All-Ireland Senior Club Football Championship.http://www.hill16.ie/viewstoryhill16.asp?mainheading=Word+on+The+Hill&id=10880&viewstory=yes Kevin also has medals for winning the Dublin Minor Football Championship, the U-17 International rules and two Leinster Senior Football Championship with Dublin in 2009 and 2011.
