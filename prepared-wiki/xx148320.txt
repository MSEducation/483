[[Jibbs]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, African-American rappers, Midwest hip hop musicians, 1990 births

Jovan Campbell better known by his stage name Jibbs (born November 13, 1990) is an American rapper. He had one top ten single on the Billboard Hot 100, which was his debut single "Chain Hang Low". Jibbs' only other song that charted on the Billboard Hot 100 is "King Kong" (featuring Chamillionaire).
