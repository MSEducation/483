[[WJAY]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in South Carolina

WJAY (1280 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Gospel format, licensed to Mullins, South Carolina, USA.  The station is currently owned by The Greater Hwy Church of Christ and features programing from ABC Radio .[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=WJAY |title=WJAY Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
