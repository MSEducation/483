[[Eddie Yagin]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1979 births, American practitioners of Brazilian jiu-jitsu, American people of Filipino descent, Featherweight mixed martial artists, People from Hilo, Hawaii, American sportspeople of Asian descent

Eddie Yagin (born March 23, 1979) is a Filipino-American mixed martial artist who currently fights as a Featherweight for the Ultimate Fighting Championship.

==Mixed martial arts career==

===Early career===

Yagin was scheduled to face off against undefeated prospect Brandon Visher on September 12, 2009 for the Up-N-Up Featherweight Championship, but was forced off the card due to injuries sustained during training. He was replaced by Stockton, California's Issac de Jesus.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.mmalinker.com/wiki/index.php/Eddie_Yagin |title=Eddie Yagin - FightWiki |publisher=Mmalinker.com |date= |accessdate=2011-10-15[/tpl]
Yagin fought former Bellator Champion Joe Soto at Tachi Palace Fights 10 on August 5, 2011. At the time, Soto was scheduled to compete in the Bellator Season Five Bantamweight Tournament. Yagin defeated Soto with a guillotine choke submission in the first round.

===Ultimate Fighting Championship===

Yagin was signed by the UFC after his win over Soto.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fightline.com/fl/news/2011/0829/543596/keith-jardine/ |title=Quick Shots: Jardine Moving To Middleweight, Eddie Yagin Signs With UFC, Daley Gets An Opponent |publisher=FightLine.com |date=2011-08-29 |accessdate=2011-10-15[/tpl] His octagon debut was against Junior Assunção at UFC 135 on September 24, 2011. Yagin lost via unanimous decision.[tpl]cite web|last=Stupp|first=Dann|title=UFC 135 preliminary-card results: Ferguson breaks Riley's jaw, Boetsch cruises|url=http://mmajunkie.com/news/25389/ufc-135-preliminary-card-results-ferguson-breaks-rileys-jaw-boetsch-cruises.mma|work=MMAJunkie.com|accessdate=25 September 2011[/tpl]

Yagin was expected to face Dennis Siver on September 1, 2012 at UFC 151.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.thefightlounge.co.uk/dennis-siver-vs-eddie-yagin-ufc-151/|title=Dennis Siver vs. Eddie Yagin – UFC 151 |publisher=thefightlounge.co.uk |last=Rimmer |first=Joe |date=June 5, 2012[/tpl] However, after UFC 151 was cancelled, Yagin/Siver was rescheduled and will take place on December 8, 2012 at UFC on Fox 5.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.bloodyelbow.com/2012/8/30/3279787/mma-news-ufc-151-rescheduled-eddie-yagin-vs-dennis-siver-moved-ufc-on-fox-5|title=Dennis Siver Vs.
