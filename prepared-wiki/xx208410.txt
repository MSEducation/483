[[František Havelka]]

CATEGORIES: 1908 births, Year of death missing, Czech boxers, Czechoslovak boxers, Light-heavyweight boxers, Olympic boxers of Czechoslovakia, Boxers at the 1936 Summer Olympics

František Havelka (born July 8, 1908, date of death unknown) was a Czech boxer who competed in the 1936 Summer Olympics for Czechoslovakia.  In 1936, he was eliminated in the quarter-finals of the light heavyweight class after losing his fight to Robey Leibbrandt.
