[[Steve Dansiger]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, American rock drummers, King Missile members

Steve Dansiger is an American drummer and member of the first two incarnations of avant-garde band King Missile and of the band Pianosaurus.
Dansiger is now a therapist with a Doctorate in Clinical Psychology with ONE80CENTER.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.one80center.com/bios/stephen-dansiger-psyd-executive-director-at-one80center/ |title=Stephen Dansiger, PsyD- Executive director at ONE80CENTER |publisher=One80center.com |date=2010-07-22 |accessdate=2011-11-26[/tpl] Dansiger has appeared on comedian Marc Maron's WTF podcasts multiple times.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.wtfpod.com/podcast/episodes/episode_230_-_dr._stephen_dansiger |title=WTF with Marc Maron Podcast - Episode 230 - Dr. Stephen Dansiger |publisher=Wtfpod.com |date= |accessdate=2011-11-26[/tpl]
