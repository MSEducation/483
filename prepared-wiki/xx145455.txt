[[Sind Division]]

CATEGORIES: Divisions of British India, Historical Indian regions, History of Sindh, Bombay Presidency

The Sind Division was the name of Sindh after being annexed by the Bombay Presidency of British India in 1843, following a British Indian conquest led by then Major-General Charles Napier. The region separated from the Bombay Presidency to become the Sind Province on 1936-04-01.
