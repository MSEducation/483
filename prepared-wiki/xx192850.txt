Noah Dalton Danby (born April 24, 1974) is a male Canadian film and television actor.  He is known for portraying Connor King on the series Painkiller Jane.

==Life==

Danby was born in Guelph, Ontario, Canada, the son of Canadian painter Ken Danby. He became engaged to fellow Painkiller Jane star Kristanna Loken on January 17, 2008.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.kristannaloken.net/fan_letter2.html |title=Kristanna Loken |publisher=Kristanna Loken |date=2008-01-17 |accessdate=2010-09-17[/tpl] The two married at Loken's family farm in Ghent, New York on May 10, 2008.Kristanna Loken[tpl]dead link|date=September 2010[/tpl]  In an interview published on November 16, 2009, Kristanna Loken announced that she had separated from husband Noah Danby and was now in a relationship with a woman.Kriegh, LeeAnn.
