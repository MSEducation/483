[[KDXU]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Utah

KDXU (890 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a talk radio format. Licensed to St. George, Utah, USA, the station is currently owned by Cherry Creek Radio.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=KDXU |title=KDXU Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl] KDXU is also an affiliate of the BYU Cougars Sports Network.
KDXU signed on in 1957 on 1450 kHz. It moved to 890 kHz in September 1985; a directional antenna is used from sunset to sunrise to protect the nighttime skywave signal of WLS (AM) (class A) in Chicago.
