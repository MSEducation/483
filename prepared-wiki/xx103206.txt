[[Guess What?]]

CATEGORIES: 1990 books, Children's picture books, Books by Mem Fox, Australian books

Guess What? is a 1990 picture book for children, written by Mem Fox and illustrated by Vivienne Goodman, about an old woman, with various witchlike qualities.
The book has a steady phrasing, along the lines of:
The book's final twist reveals the old woman as a witch. Guess What? is on the American Library Association list of the 100 Most Frequently Challenged Books of 1990-2000 at number 66.http://www.librarycareers.org/Template.cfm?Section=bbwlinks&Template=/ContentManagement/ContentDisplay.cfm&ContentID=85714 The challenges to the book are generated because of the supposed occult connection since the book is leading the reader to develop a positive impression of a witch.
