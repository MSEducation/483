[[César Povolny]]

CATEGORIES: 1914 births, French footballers, Le Havre AC players, 1938 FIFA World Cup players, French people of German descent, Living people

César Povolny (born 19 July 1914 in Recklinghausen; also known as Czeslaw Povolny and listed as Martin Povolny) was a German-French association footballer. He played for Le Havre AC, and was selected for the 1938 FIFA World Cup,1938 FIFA World Cup France but did not earn any caps for the France national football team.
