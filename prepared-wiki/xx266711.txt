[[If You Want It (2nd II None song)]]

CATEGORIES: 1992 singles, 2nd II None songs, Song recordings produced by DJ Quik

"If You Want It" is the third and final single released off 2nd II None's debut self-titled studio album, 2nd II None. The song is produced by DJ Quik and samples Isaac Hayes's Hung Up on My Baby.http://www.whosampled.com/sample/view/7201/2nd%20II%20None-If%20You%20Want%20It_Isaac%20Hayes-Hung%20Up%20on%20My%20Baby/
