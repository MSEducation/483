[[Queen of Songs]]

CATEGORIES: Nora Aunor albums, 1971 albums

Queen of Songs is a studio album by Filipino singer-actress Nora Aunor, released in 1972 by Alpha Records Corporation in the Philippines in LP format[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.superstarnoraaunor.com/queenofsongs.html|title=Queen of Songs Album|accessdate=2013-08-09[/tpl] and later released in 1999 in a compilation/ cd format.[tpl]cite web |url= http://www.alphamusic.ph/opm_all5.html|title=Alpha Records OPM Hits|accessdate=2013-08-04[/tpl]
