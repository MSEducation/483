[[William G. Blakeley House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Arizona, Houses completed in 1887, Houses in Mohave County, Arizona, Buildings and structures in Kingman, Arizona

The William G. Blakeley House is at 503 Spring Street in Kingman, Arizona. The house was built in 1887. It is a Colonial Revival. This is one of the oldest houses in Kingman. From 1887 to 1911, Mr. Blakeley was a miner, lawyer, probate judge, on the school board, and the pastor of St. Johnâ€™s Methodist Church. This house is on the National Register of Historic Place and the number is 86001115.
