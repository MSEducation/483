[[Kazuo Hara]]

CATEGORIES: 1945 births, Living people, Japanese film directors, People from Yamaguchi Prefecture

[tpl]Nihongo|Kazuo Hara|原一男|Hara Kazuo[/tpl] is a Japanese documentary film director. He won the award for Best Director at the 12th Hochi Film Award[tpl]Cite web |url=http://cinemahochi.yomiuri.co.jp/h_award/1987/ |title= 報知映画賞ヒストリー |accessdate= 2010-01-10|language= Japanese|publisher= Cinema Hochi[/tpl]  and at the 9th Yokohama Film Festival for The Emperor's Naked Army Marches On.[tpl]Cite web |url=http://homepage3.nifty.com/yokohama-eigasai/09-1987/09_1987_shou.html |title=第９回ヨコハマ映画祭 １９８７年日本映画個人賞|accessdate= 2010-01-10|language= Japanese |publisher= Yokohama Film Festival[/tpl] That film also earned him the Directors Guild of Japan New Directors Award.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.dgj.or.jp/award_g/|title=Nihon Eiga Kantoku Kyōkai Shinjinshō|publisher=Directors Guild of Japan|language=Japanese|accessdate=11 December 2010[/tpl]
