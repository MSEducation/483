[[Kusahlar]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Kusahlar ([tpl]lang-fa|كوسه لر[/tpl], also Romanized as Kūsahlar and Kūsehlar; also known as Kossehlar, Kusahiar, Kusalar, and Kūzehlar)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3072553|Kusahlar[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Bala Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 634, in 126 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
