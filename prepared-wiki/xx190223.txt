[[Grand Lake St. Marys Seaplane Base]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in Ohio, Seaplane bases in the United States, Transportation in Auglaize County, Ohio, Buildings and structures in Auglaize County, Ohio

Grand Lake St. Marys Seaplane Base [tpl]airport codes|||O12[/tpl] is a public use seaplane base located five nautical miles (9 km) southwest of the central business district of St. Marys, a city in Auglaize County, Ohio, United States. It is owned by the Ohio Division of Parks & Recreation and is located on Grand Lake St. Marys, a lake and state park in Ohio.

==Facilities and aircraft==

Grand Lake St. Marys Seaplane Base covers an area of [tpl]convert|643|acre|ha[/tpl] at an elevation of 871 feet (265 m) above mean sea level. It has one seaplane landing area designated 9/27 and measuring 7,000 x 4,000 ft (2,134 x 1,219 m).
