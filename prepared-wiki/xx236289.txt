[[Evening (magazine)]]

CATEGORIES: Japanese weekly manga magazines, Kodansha magazines, Seinen manga magazines

[tpl]nihongo|Evening|イブニング|Ibuningu[/tpl] is a bi-weekly Japanese seinen manga magazine published by Kodansha, aimed at adult men. It is printed in black and white on newsprint and saddle-stapled in B5 format, and retails for 330 yen. Circulation was reported by the Japan Magazine Publishers Association at 166,000 copies in late 2008.
