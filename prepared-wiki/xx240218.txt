==Biography==

Andrew Goldberg was born and raised in White Plains, New York, of Jewish descent, earned his BA at Columbia University (2000) and his MFA at the UCLA School of Film & Television (2002).
In 2007, Goldberg wrote a half-hour pilot for Fox Broadcasting Company with 20th Century Fox Television and Watson Pond Productions. In 2008 he wrote for "Seth MacFarlane's Cavalcade of Cartoon Comedy," an internet series produced by MRC and Fuzzy Door Productions and distributed by Google.
