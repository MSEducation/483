[[Namida no Taiyō]]

CATEGORIES: 2004 singles, Zetima Records singles, Song recordings produced by Tsunku

[tpl]nihongo|"Namida no Taiyō"|涙の太陽|Crying In A Storm[/tpl] is the twelfth single by Japanese girl group Melon Kinenbi. It was released on June 9, 2004, and its highest position on the Oricon weekly chart was #15. It is a cover of "Namida no Taiyō (Crying in a Storm)" by Emy Jackson, originally released on April 20, 1965.http://www.emyjackson.com/ (Japanese)
