[[5910 Zátopek]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Astronomical objects discovered in 1989

5910 Zátopek is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1257.4626479 days (3.44 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=5910 | title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-05-24 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on November 29, 1989.
