[[Gmina Gromnik]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Lesser Poland Voivodeship, Tarnów County

Gmina Gromnik is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Tarnów County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. Its seat is the village of Gromnik, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|23|km|mi|0[/tpl] south of Tarnów and [tpl]convert|77|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] east of the regional capital Kraków.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|69.81|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 8,348.
The gmina contains part of the protected area called Ciężkowice-Rożnów Landscape Park.

==Villages==

Gmina Gromnik contains the villages and settlements of Brzozowa, Chojnik, Golanka, Gromnik, Polichty, Rzepiennik Marciszewski and Siemiechów.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Gromnik is bordered by the gminas of Ciężkowice, Pleśna, Rzepiennik Strzyżewski, Tuchów and Zakliczyn.
