[[Coup de Boule]]

CATEGORIES: 2006 singles, Football songs and chants, La Plage songs, Number-one debut singles, Ultratop 50 Singles (Wallonia) number-one singles, Number-one singles in France

"Coup de Boule" (French for "headbutt") is a French song written and performed by brothers SÃ©bastien and Emmanuel Lipszyc, who run a firm called La Plage that produces television jingles, and Franck Lascombes. The soca-style song is about the France national football team's appearance at the 2006 FIFA World Cup Final, especially Zinedine Zidane's headbutt on Marco Materazzi. After its release, the song spread throughout the world via the Internet and topped the French and Belgian (Wallonia) charts.
