[[Oleg Berdos]]

CATEGORIES: Moldovan cyclists, 1987 births, Living people, Cyclists at the 2012 Summer Olympics, Olympic cyclists of Moldova

Oleg Berdos (born June 9, 1987 in Chişinău) is a Moldovan road bicycle racer who rides for the UCI Continental Tusnad Cycling Team.
