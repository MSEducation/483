[[Leukoma]]

CATEGORIES: Edible molluscs, Seafood in Native American cuisine, Veneridae

Leukoma is a genus of saltwater clams,  marine bivalve mollusks in the family Veneridae, the Venus clams.Veneridae, 2001 This genus of bivalves has been exploited by humans since prehistory; for example, the Chumash peoples of California harvested this genus from Morro Bay in approximately 1000 AD.C.M.Hogan, 2008
