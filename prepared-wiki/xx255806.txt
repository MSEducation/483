[[Gil's Guests]]

CATEGORIES: Prestige Records albums, Gil Mellé albums, 1956 albums, Albums recorded at Van Gelder Studio

Gil's Guests is an album by American saxophonist Gil Mellé recorded in 1956 and released on the Prestige label.Prestige Records discography accessed December 30, 2010

==Reception==

The Allmusic review by Scott Yanow awarded the album 3 stars and stated "Baritonist Gil Melle's recordings are usually a bit unusual and this CD reissue is no exception... The charts are unpredictable and often dramatic, looking ahead toward a musical future that never occurred".Yanow, S. Allmusic Review accessed December 30, 2010
