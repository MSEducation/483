[[Bořanovice]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in the Czech Republic, Prague-East District

Bořanovice is a village in the Central Bohemian Region of the Czech Republic. On the date July 3, 2006 it has  612 inhabitants. First written notice about village is from year 1227. The village consists of two parts - Bořanovice and older part Pakoměřice, where is situated pond, gothic church and old fortress. Its a member of Mikroregion Povodí Mratínského potoka.
