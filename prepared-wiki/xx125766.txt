[[What Are You Waiting For? (FM Static album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2003 debut albums, FM Static albums, Tooth & Nail Records albums

What Are You Waiting For? is the first album by the pop punk band FM Static. As of the end of 2006, it has sold more than 60,000 copies.[tpl]Citation needed|date=December 2008[/tpl]
