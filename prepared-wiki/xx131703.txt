[[Kelly Greenwood]]

CATEGORIES: 1982 births, Living people, English television actresses

Kelly Condron, (born in Salford, 1982) is a British actress better known by her previous stage name Kelly Greenwood. Greenwood played Zara Morgan in the soap opera Hollyoaks for six years.

==Career==

Greenwood has secured acting roles in Seven Deadly Sins, Casualty, Heartbeat and Children's Ward.
She played Zara Morgan in the soap opera Hollyoaks from 1999 until her departure in 2005.

==Personal life==

Greenwood married Adam Condron on 25 February 2006.
