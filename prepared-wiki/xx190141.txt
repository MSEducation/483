==Career==

Spiesel has for many years operated a busy pediatrics practice in New Haven, Connecticut.[tpl]Cite web|author=Mary Jane Minkin|url=http://yalemedicine.yale.edu/spring2010/people/reunion/reunionetcetera/51858|title=Class of 1975: 35th reunion[/tpl]

==Education==

Spiesel received his M.D. and Ph.D. from Yale University in 1975.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://yalemedicine.yale.edu/spring2010/people/faculty/sydney_spiesel-1.profile|title=Sydney Spiesel|work=Yale.edu|author=Yale School of Medicine|accessdate=2012-10-18[/tpl]
For his undergraduate work, Spiesel attended Shimer College, then located in Mount Carroll, Illinois.  He enrolled through the school's early entrance program, which since 1950 has allowed students to matriculate who have not received a high school diploma.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.shimer.edu/academicprograms/undergraduate/earlyentrantprogram.cfm|title=Early Entrant Program|author=Shimer College|accessdate=2012-04-08[/tpl]  He graduated from Shimer in 1961.  In 2012, Spiesel was named one of the first three Shimer alumni to receive the school's Alumni Service Award.[tpl]Cite web|title=The Inaugural Alumni Service Award Winners|url=http://alumni.shimer.edu/s/1028/index.aspx?sid=1028&gid=1&pgid=252&cid=1494&ecid=1494&ciid=4357&crid=0|work=Alumni.shimer.edu|author=Shimer College|accessdate=2012-04-08[/tpl]
