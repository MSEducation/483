[[Ardalan Shoja Kaveh]]

CATEGORIES: 1963 births, Living people, Iranian actors

Ardalan Shoja Kaveh, born on 1963 in Birjand, Iran, is an Iranian actor. He has worked with Mohsen Makhmalbaf in the film Boycott (originally Baykot). Shoja Kaveh is also a famous television actor and has starred in various sit-coms such as Char Khooneh, a very successful Iranian comedy.
