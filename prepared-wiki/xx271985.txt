Sergio Díaz Castilla (born 2 July 1991 in Málaga, Andalusia) is a Spanish footballer who plays for Real Oviedo on loan from Mallorca B, as a left defender.

==Football career==

Díaz played his first season as a senior with local Atlético Malagueño, in Tercera División. In July 2011 he was loaned to Hércules CF, with the Segunda División team having an option to buy at the end of the season.Sergio Díaz ceded out to Hércules Club de Fútbol; Málaga's official website, 12 July 2011
Díaz made his debut for Hércules on 7 September 2011, in a Copa del Rey match against CD Alcoyano,Hércules vs. Alcoyano 3–2; Soccerway, 7 September 2011 and he made his league debut on the 16th, coming on as a substitute for Adrián Sardinero in a 2–1 home win against FC Barcelona B.Hércules vs. Barcelona II 2–1; Soccerway, 16 September 2011
