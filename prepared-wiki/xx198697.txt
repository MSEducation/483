[[Gunnar Grendstad]]

CATEGORIES: 1960 births, University of Bergen faculty, People from Kristiansand, Living people

Gunnar Grendstad (born 1 May 1960 in Kristiansand, Norway) is a Norwegian political scientist and Professor at the University of Bergen, Norway. He has researched on methodological aspects of political science and specialises in American politics. He has been a Visiting Fellow at the University of California, Berkeley and Purdue University.
