[[WHHH]]

CATEGORIES: Radio One stations, Radio stations in Indianapolis, Indiana, Rhythmic contemporary radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 1991, 1991 establishments in the United States

WHHH, 96.3 FM, is a rhythmic contemporary formatted radio station in Indianapolis which primarily focuses on mainstream urban music consisting of hip-hop and R&B music.  Along with four other Indy stations, WTLC (AM), WTLC-FM, WNOU and TV station WDNI they are owned and operated by Radio One.  All four stations and TV outlet share studios on Meridian Street in downtown Indianapolis and its transmitter tower is atop Riley Towers II nearby.
"Hot 96.3" is the home to Russ Parr in the Morning.
