[[Ministry of Trade and Shipping]]

CATEGORIES: Government ministries of Norway, 1947 establishments in Norway, 1987 disestablishments, Ministries established in 1947

The Royal Norwegian Ministry of Trade and Shipping ([tpl]lang-no|Handels- og skipsfartsdepartementet[/tpl]) was a Norwegian ministry that existed from 1947 to 1987.
It was established on 6 December 1947. It ceased to exist on 31 December 1987. Its tasks were mainly transferred to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Ministries since 1814 - Government.no
For the heads of the Ministry of Trade and Shipping, see the list of Ministers of Trade and Shipping.
An unrelated Ministry of Shipping had existed from 1942 to 1945.
