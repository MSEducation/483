[[Dehradun Cantonment]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Dehradun district, Dehradun, Cantonments of British India, Cantonments of India

Dehradun Cantonment is a cantonment town in Dehradun district  in the state of Uttarakhand, India.

==Demographics==

[tpl]As of|2001[/tpl] India census,[tpl]GR|India[/tpl] Dehradun Cantonment had a population of 30,102. Males constitute 52% of the population and females 48%. Dehradun Cantonment has an average literacy rate of 79%, higher than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 82% and, female literacy is 76%. In Dehradun Cantonment, 10% of the population is under 6 years of age.
