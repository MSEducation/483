[[Raised on Country Music]]

CATEGORIES: 1982 albums, Family Brown albums, RCA Records albums

Raised on Country Music is the eighth studio album by Canadian country music group Family Brown. It was released in 1982 by RCA Records and includes the singles, "Some Never Stand a Chance," "Raised on Country Music," and "Memorized by Heart," which all charted on the RPM Country Tracks chart in Canada. The album won the award for Album of the Year at the 1982 and 1983 Canadian Country Music Association Awards.[tpl]cite web | title=Family Brown | url=http://www.thecanadianencyclopedia.com/index.cfm?PgNm=TCE&Params=U1ARTU0001167 | work=The Canadian Encyclopedia | accessdate=August 10, 2013[/tpl]
