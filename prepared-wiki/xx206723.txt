[[The Song of Songs (1933 film)]]

CATEGORIES: 1933 films, American films, Black-and-white films, English-language films, Films based on novels, Films based on plays, Films directed by Rouben Mamoulian, Paramount Pictures films, 1930s romantic drama films

The Song of Songs (1933) is a romantic drama film starring Marlene Dietrich as a naive, German peasant who moves to Berlin and suffers much heartache. It was based on the 1908 novel Das Hohe Lied by Hermann Sudermann and the subsequent 1914 play, The Song of Songs by Edward Sheldon. It is a remake of the 1918 silent The Song of Songs starring Elsie Ferguson and the 1924 Lily of the Dust with Pola Negri.The American Film Institute Catalog Feature Films: 1931-40 by The American Film Institute, c.1993
