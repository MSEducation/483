[[Oiketerion]]

CATEGORIES: New Testament Greek words and phrases

Oiketerion (οἰκητήριον) is a Greek word meaning "habitation".Strong's Greek DictionaryNETBibleheartlight's
It is used in two places in the Bible: [tpl]bibleverse|2|Corinthians|5:2[/tpl] and [tpl]bibleverse||Jude|1:6[/tpl]

==Usages==

According to the King James Version translation of the Greek, the specific scriptural usages of the word are:
 And the angels which kept not their first estate, but left their own habitation, he hath reserved in everlasting chains under darkness unto the judgment of the great day.2 Corinthians 5:2 
and
 For in this we groan earnestly desiring to be clothed upon with our house which is from heaven:Jude 1:6
