[[Andi Abdullah Bau Massepe]]

CATEGORIES: National Heroes of Indonesia

Andi Abdullah Bau Massepe (1918[tpl]spaced ndash[/tpl]February 2, 1947) was an Indonesian nobleman who initiated attacks against Dutch forces during the National Revolution. He was the son of Andi Mappanyukki. On November 9, 2005, he was conferred the title of National Hero of Indonesia posthumously.

==Early life==

Andi Abdullah Bau Massepe was born in 1918.[tpl]Cite book|last=Mirnawati| title=Kumpulan Pahlawan Indonesia Terlengkap|trans_title=Most Complete Collection of Indonesian Heroes|language=Indonesian| year=2012|location=Jakarta| publisher=CIF| isbn=9789797883430| pages=229—230[/tpl] His father was rebel warrior Andi Mappanyukki. Massepe had one brother named Andi Pangerang Petta Rani.
