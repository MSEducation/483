[[2002–03 First Macedonian Football League]]

CATEGORIES: 2002–03 domestic association football leagues, 2002–03 in Republic of Macedonia football, First Macedonian Football League seasons

The 2002-03 First Macedonian Football League was the 11th since its establishment. The first matches of the season were played on 10 August 2002 and the last on 1 June 2003. FK Vardar defended their championship title, having won their fifth title, second in a row.
