[[Red Field]]

CATEGORIES: 1946 novels, Novels by Jorge Amado, Brazilian novels, Portuguese-language novels

Red Field (Portuguese: Seara Vermelha) is a Brazilian Modernist novel. It was written by Jorge Amado in 1946.
A movie [tpl]imdb title|0353987|Seara Vermelha[/tpl] of the same name based upon the novel was released in 1964.
