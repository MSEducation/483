[[Bjarni Haukur Thorsson]]

CATEGORIES: 1971 births, Living people, Icelandic television actresses, Tisch School of the Arts alumni

Bjarni Haukur Thorsson was born April 20, 1971. He is a famous Icelandic director, writer, producer, and actor. He is the owner of Thorsson Productions Ltd. which is an intellectual property based operating company and live entertainment production studio. Thorson Productions develops, produces and manages broadcast and licensing around the world. Thorsson Productions was founded by Bjarni Haukur Thorsson and he has developed and produced more than 40 theatrical and broadcasting productions around the world since 1996.[tpl]cite web | title = Thorsson productions | url = http://www.thorsson.is [/tpl]

==Career==

Bjarni Haukur Thorsson is a well established director in Scandinavia and has since he started out in 1996 directed numerous productions in several countries for both stage and screen. His productions have been nominated for 12 major theatre and television awards in Scandinavia.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.loftkastalinn.is/?pageid=39 |title=Loftkastalinn – Listrænir stjórnendur |publisher=Loftkastalinn.is |date= |accessdate=October 17, 2011[/tpl] Mr. Thorsson graduated from the distinguished American Academy of Dramatic Arts in New York City[tpl]cite web | title = American Academy of Dramatic Arts in New York City | url = http://www.aada.org/home/home.html| accessdate = September 26, 2011[/tpl] in 1995, having prior attended New York University – Tisch School of the Arts.[tpl]cite web | title = Tisch School of the Arts – Bjarni Segment | url = http://www.tisch.nyu.edu/page/home.html | accessdate = September 26, 2011[/tpl]
Bjarni Haukur is the creator and co-star of the plays The DAD[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.visir.is/fraskilinn-bjarni-haukur-lelegur-ad-skreyta/article/2008521715418 |title=Vísir – Fráskilinn Bjarni Haukur lélegur að skreyta |publisher=Visir.is |date= |accessdate=October 17, 2011[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.borgarleikhus.is/media/leikskrar/leikskrar-1011/Afinn-web.pdf |title=Afinn |publisher=www.borgarleikhus.is |date= |accessdate=October 17, 2011[/tpl] and The GRAND-DAD .
He is also a well established producer in Scandinavia who has produced around 40 major theatrical and television productions in 8 countries since 1995.[tpl]cite web | title = BJARNI HAUKUR THORSSON – PRODUCERS CV – Bjarni Segment | url = http://thorsson.is/cv/Bjarni_Thorsson_Producer_CV.pdf| accessdate = September 26, 2011[/tpl] He completed his MBA degree in May 2011 from Reykjavik University.
