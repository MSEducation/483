[[Amirlu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Amirlu ([tpl]lang-fa|اميرلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Amīrlū and Amir Loo)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3053067|Amirlu[/tpl] is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 547, in 97 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
