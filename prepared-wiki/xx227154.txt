[[Alfred Dancey]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing, Year of death missing, English murderers of children, Convicts transported to Australia

A British schoolchild, Alfred Dancey was convicted of murder at the age of 14 and exiled to Australia in 1850, after he shot two classmates with a pistol he brought to the school.Wilson, Colin. "The Mammoth Book of True Crime", 1998
Dancey grew up in Bedminster, Bristol.
