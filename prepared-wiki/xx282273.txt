[[Trickstar Games]]

CATEGORIES: Companies based in Melbourne, Companies established in 2009, Video game development companies, Video game companies of Australia

Trickstar Games is a computer game developer founded in 2009, developing content for Nintendo and Sony-based platforms as well as Microsoft desktop-based computers. Based in Melbourne, Australia, Trickstar Games is led by Mike Fegan and Tony Parkes, and titles include International Cricket 2010, JASF: Jane's Advanced Strike Fighters and the ill-fated Ashes Cricket 2013http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/technology-25137093.
