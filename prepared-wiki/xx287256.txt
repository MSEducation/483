[[Živim po svome]]

CATEGORIES: 2009 albums, Pop albums by Croatian artists

Živim po svome (I live my way) is the 21st album of Croatian singer Josipa Lisac,http://www.josipalisac.com/ released by Croatia Records in 2009.
Album is dedicated to Karlo Metikoš.http://hr.wikipedia.org/wiki/Karlo_Metiko%C5%A1
