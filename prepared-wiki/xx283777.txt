[[Kvalsundet (Tromsø)]]

CATEGORIES: Sounds of Norway, Geography of Troms, Tromsø

Kvalsundet is a strait in the municipality of Tromsø in Troms, Norway. The strait has a length of about twenty kilometers and separates the island of Kvaløya from Ringvassøya.
