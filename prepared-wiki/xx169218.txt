[[Baldwinsville Central School District]]

CATEGORIES: School districts in New York, Education in Onondaga County, New York

Baldwinsville Central School District is a public school district which serves the community of Baldwinsville, New York.  It consists of 6,036 students in eight schools in this grade span (five K-5 elementary schools, one 6-7 middle school, one 8-9 junior high school, and one 10-12 senior high school).
