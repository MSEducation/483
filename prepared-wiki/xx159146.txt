Robert Wayne Turner II (born August 20, 1984)[tpl]cite web|title=Robert Turner|url=http://www.cbssports.com/nfl/draft/players/historical/1226377|publisher=CBS Sports|archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/5uRH5V7U9|archivedate=November 22, 2010|accessdate=November 22, 2010[/tpl] is an American football offensive lineman for the Tennessee Titans of the National Football League. He was signed by the New York Jets as an undrafted free agent in 2007. Turner played college football in New Mexico.
Turner has also played for the St. Louis Rams.

==College career==

Over four years, Turner started 45 games for the Lobos as a right guard and right tackle.[tpl]cite web|title=Jets sign Turner and Oldenburg to roster|url=http://nyj.scout.com/2/712970.html|publisher=Green and White Report|author=Thomas, Craig|date=December 19, 2007|archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/5uRFZrncH|archivedate=November 22, 2010|accessdate=November 22, 2010[/tpl] A wrist injury plagued him during his final two years which caused teams to shy away from drafting him.[tpl]cite web|title=Jets sign guard Robert Turner|url=http://nwe.scout.com/a.z?s=121&p=2&c=644598|publisher=Patriots Insider|author=Leberfeld, Dan|date=May 17, 2007|archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/5uRFmu79D|archivedate=November 22, 2010|accessdate=November 22, 2010[/tpl]

==Professional career==

===New York Jets===

Turner was signed by the New York Jets as an undrafted free agent out of New Mexico on May 16, 2007. Turner was waived during the final roster cuts on September 1, 2007 before signing on to the team's practice squad on September 3. Turner was promoted to the active roster on December 19, 2007. Turner made his professional debut against the Tennessee Titans on December 23, 2007.[tpl]cite web|title=Robert Turner|url=http://www.newyorkjets.com/team/roster/Robert-Turner/ea37c44c-cd37-4a2f-bbde-97e3d149d154|publisher=New York Jets|archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/5uRGFSqVP|archivedate=November 22, 2010|accessdate=November 22, 2010[/tpl] Turner was well known for his versatility and was utilized on offense, defense and special teams with the Jets.[tpl]cite web|title=Turner Made the Rounds in Win over Browns|url=http://blog.newyorkjets.com/2010/11/16/turner-made-the-rounds-in-the-cleveland-win/|publisher=New York Jets|author=Allen, Eric|date=November 16, 2010|archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/5uRGeWjMu|archivedate=November 22, 2010|accessdate=November 22, 2010[/tpl]
Turner was placed on the injured reserve list on September 27, 2011 after suffering a broken ankle in the preseason.[tpl]cite web|title=Baker to Active Roster, Cumberland, Turner to IR|url=http://www.newyorkjets.com/news/article-1/Baker-to-Active-Roster-Cumberland-Turner-to-IR/23ba5d6e-af4c-4f01-970c-ac21ff106962|publisher=New York Jets|author=Jets Media Relations Department|date=September 27, 2011|archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/622hco1kM|archivedate=September 28, 2011|accessdate=September 28, 2011[/tpl]
