[[Kibatalia maingayi]]

CATEGORIES: Kibatalia, Plants described in 1936, Trees of Thailand, Trees of Malaysia, Trees of Indonesia, Flora of Borneo, Trees of the Philippines

Kibatalia maingayi grows as a tree up to [tpl]Convert|40|m[/tpl] tall, with a trunk diameter of up to [tpl]Convert|120|cm|-1[/tpl]. The bark is pale brown, dark grey or whitish. Inflorescences bear up to 25 flowers. The flowers feature a white or pale yellow corolla. The fruits are cylindrical, up to [tpl]convert|0.6|cm|in|1|abbr=on[/tpl] long. The tree is found in lowland and lower montane forests. K. maingayi is native to Thailand, Malaysia, Indonesia and the Philippines.
