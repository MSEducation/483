[[Meshkin, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Meshkin ([tpl]lang-fa|مشكين[/tpl], also Romanized as Meshkīn; also known as Meshgīn and Mushkin)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3074760|Meshkin[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,321, in 285 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
