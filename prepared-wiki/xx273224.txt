[[Vladimir Sharpatov]]

CATEGORIES: 1940 births, Living people, People from Zvenigovsky District, Heroes of the Russian Federation

Vladimir Ilyich Sharpatov ([tpl]lang-ru|Влади́мир Ильи́ч Шарпа́тов[/tpl], born March 21, 1940, Krasnogorkiy village of Zvenigovsky District, Mari El) — airplane pilot, Hero of the Russian Federation (1996). An Ilyushin Il-76 cargo plane under his command made a forced landing at the airfield near Kandahar, Afghanistan. On August 1996 crew successfully escaped from captivity on that plane. This episode was filmed in 2010, Alexander Baluyev played his role.

==Biography==

Studied in Kazan Aviation Institute on evening classes. Attended local flying club, where he became a glider pilot. After he completed the third year of studies at KAI, he entered the Krasny Kut civil aviation flight school.
