[[Anti-Imperialist Cell]]

CATEGORIES: Terrorism in Germany, Terrorism stubs

The Anti-Imperialist Cell (German: Antiimperialistische Zelle, AIZ) was a leftist militant group responsible for bombing attacks in Germany in 1995.
The two members of the group, Bernhard Falk and Michael Steinau, were arrested in February, 1996.  After these arrests, the AIZ actions ceased.
