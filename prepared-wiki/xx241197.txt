[[Horqin Left Back Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

Horqin Left Back Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠷᠴᠢᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠵᠡᠭᠦᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠭᠠᠷᠤᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠶᠢᠲᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Qorčin Jegün Ɣarun Qoyitu qosiɣu[/tpl]; Mongolian: Qorcin jegün garun qoyidu qosigu 'Northern banner of the Khorchin east wing'; [tpl]zh|s=科尔沁左翼后旗 |t=科爾沁左翼後旗 |p=Kē'ěrqìn Zuǒyì Hòuqí[/tpl], original Mongolian name Büve vang qosigu) is a banner of southeastern Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China, bordering Liaoning province to the south. It is under the administration of Tongliao City, [tpl]convert|75|km[/tpl] to the north. The local Mongolian dialect is Khorchin Mongolian.
