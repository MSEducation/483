[[Newellton Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in Louisiana, Transportation in Tensas Parish, Louisiana, Buildings and structures in Tensas Parish, Louisiana

Newellton Airport [tpl]airport codes|||9M2[/tpl] is a public use airport in Tensas Parish, Louisiana, United States. It is owned by the Town of Newellton and located one mile (2 km) southwest of its central business district.

==Facilities and aircraft==

Newellton Airport covers an area of 20 acres (8 ha) at an elevation of 77 feet (23 m) above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 6/24 with an asphalt surface measuring 2,750 by 75 feet (838 x 23 m).
For the 12-month period ending August 15, 2000, the airport had 7,000 general aviation aircraft operations, an average of 19 per day. At that time there were eight single-engine based at this airport.
