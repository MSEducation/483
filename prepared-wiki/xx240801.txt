Jean-Christian Lang (born 22 August 1950 in Roubaix) is a French football manager and former player.
Lang began his playing career with FC Sochaux-Montbéliard, playing three Ligue 1 matches, before leaving to play in Ligue 2 with CS Louhans-Cuiseaux, LB Châteauroux, CS Thonon and FC Annecy.[tpl]cite web|publisher=ElJadida.ma|title=Interview avec Jean Christian Lang, coach du DHJ|url=http://www.eljadida.ma/actualite_news_el_jadida/interview-avec-jean-christian-lang-coach-du-dhj-a1551.html|language=French|date=2007-12-29[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.footballdatabase.eu/football.joueurs.jean-christian.lang.60265.en.html|title=Footballdatabase.eu - Jean-Christian Lang|publisher=footballdatabase.eu|accessdate=6 May 2013[/tpl]
Lang was a player-manager with Annecy, and went on to manage USF Le Puy and FC Montceau Bourgogne in Ligue 2,[tpl]cite web|publisher=RSSSF|author=Garin, Erik|title=France - Trainers of First and Second Division Clubs|url=http://www.rsssf.com/players/trainers-fran-clubs.html|date=2007-07-12[/tpl] before moving to Swiss club FC Servette. He has also managed clubs in North Africa, including Difaa El Jadida and Maghreb Fez[tpl]cite web|publisher=MTN Football|title=Maghreb Fes Fire Coach|url=http://worldcup.mtnfootball.com/live/content.php?Item_ID=17091&Revision=en/0&Start=0|date=2008-11-10[/tpl] in Morocco, as well as JS Kabylie in Algeria.[tpl]cite web|publisher=D’après Le Jeune Indépendant|title=Jean-Christian Lang de retour en Kabylie|url=http://www.algerie-dz.com/article17248.html|language=French|date=2009-06-24[/tpl]
Lang has Polish ancestry, and became a naturalised French citizen in 1964.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Le Buteur|author=Ait Ali, Achour|title=Lang, côté homme : "J’aurais bien aimé être un magicien !"|url=http://www.lebuteur.com/division-1/actualites/lang-cote-homme-jaurais-bien-aime-etre-un-magicien|language=French|date=2009-05-06[/tpl] He speaks fluent Polish, French and English.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.qslm-license.com/ClubsFolder/Lekhwiya/lekhwiya%20coaching%20staff3.pdf|title=Jean-Christian Lang C.V.|accessdate=6 May 2013[/tpl]
