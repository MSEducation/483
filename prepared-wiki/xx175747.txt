[[WFCO]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Ohio

WFCO is a Christian radio station licensed to Lancaster, Ohio, broadcasting on 90.9 MHz FM, and is owned by Lancaster Educational Broadcasting Foundation.WFCO fcc.gov. Accessed August 11, 2012
WFCO airs a variety of classic Christian music from the 70's, 80's, and 90's,  and also airs Christian Talk and Teaching programs including; Revive our Hearts with Nancy Leigh DeMoss, Insight for Living with Chuck Swindoll, Turning Point with David Jeremiah, Breakpoint with Chuck Colson, Focus on the Family, and Unshackled!. Refreshing 90.9 fm Program Schedule: wfcofm.com. Accessed August 11, 2012  
WFCO began broadcasting in 1988, and initially operated only 12 hours a day, running 200 watts.  WFCO was originally owned by Fairfield Christian Academy, and the station was known as "Fairfield's Christian Outreach".  WFCO began 24 hour operations in the early 1990s.  In 2002 WFCO added more local and musical programming and became known as "Peaceful 90.9 FM".  In 2006 WFCO increased its power to 1,200 watts. In 2007 WFCO adopted its current branding of "Refreshing 90.9 FM".
