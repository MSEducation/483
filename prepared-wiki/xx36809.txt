==Life==

He was educated by a certain Tigernach, and having become a monk in 1052[tpl]CathEncy|wstitle=Marianus Scotus[/tpl] he crossed over to the continent of Europe in 1056, and his subsequent life was passed in the abbeys of St Martin at Cologne and of Fulda, and at Mainz. 
He died at Mainz, on December 22, 1082 or 1083,[tpl]cite DNB|wstitle=Marianus Scotus|first=Charles Lethbridge|last=Kingsford|volume=36[/tpl] and was buried in Mainz Cathedral.
