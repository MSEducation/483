[[Kiso, Nagano (village)]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]Nihongo|Kiso|木祖村|Kiso-mura[/tpl] is a village located in Kiso District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the village has an estimated population of 3,438 and a density of 24.48 persons per km². The total area is 140.46 km².
