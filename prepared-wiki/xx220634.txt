[[KCOM]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Texas, Country radio stations in the United States, Southern Gospel radio stations in the United States, Comanche County, Texas, Radio stations established in 1998

KCOM (1550 AM) is a radio station licensed to serve Comanche, Texas, USA.  The station is owned by Cherry Creek Radio, LLC and the license is held by CCR-Stephenville III, LLC.
KCOM broadcasts a Country Gospel music format that blends inspirational country music, Southern Gospel music, and bluegrass music.[tpl]cite web |title=Station Information Profile |work=Arbitron |url=http://www.arbitron.com/[/tpl]

==History==

In October 1998, Arrowhead Broadcasting, Inc., reached an agreement to sell this station to Texas West Media, Inc.  The deal was approved by the FCC on December 10, 1998, and the transaction was consummated on February 1, 1999.[tpl]cite web |publisher=FCC Media Bureau |title=Application Search Details (BAL-19981026GH) |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/app_det.pl?Application_id=276098 |date=February 1, 1999[/tpl]
In February 2005, Texas West Media, Inc. (David B. Bacon, president/director) reached an agreement to sell this station to Cherry Creek Radio (Joseph D. Schwartz, CEO/president), through their CCR-Stephenville III, LLC, holding company, for a reported sale price of $164,000.[tpl]cite news |date=April 11, 2005 |url=http://www.broadcastingcable.com/article/CA516252.html |work=Broadcasting & Cable |title=Deals - 4/11/2005[/tpl]  The deal was approved by the FCC on August 2, 2005, and the transaction was consummated on September 30, 2005.[tpl]cite web |publisher=FCC Media Bureau |title=Application Search Details (BAL-20050218ACF) |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/app_det.pl?Application_id=1045998 |date=September 30, 2005[/tpl] At the time of the sale, the station aired a country music format.
