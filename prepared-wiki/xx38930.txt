[[Tielt-Winge]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Flemish Brabant

Tielt-Winge ([tpl]IPA-nl|ˌt̪iːl̪t̪ ˈβ̞ɪŋə[/tpl]) is a municipality located in the Belgian province of Flemish Brabant. The municipality comprises the towns of Houwaart, Meensel-Kiezegem, Sint-Joris-Winge and Tielt. On January 1, 2006 Tielt-Winge had a total population of 10,009. The total area is 44.16 km² which gives a population density of 227 inhabitants per km².
