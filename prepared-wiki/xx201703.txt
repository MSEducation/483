[[Pilot Station Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in the Wade Hampton Census Area, Alaska

Pilot Station Airport [tpl]airport codes|PQS||0AK[/tpl] is a state-owned public-use airport located one nautical mile (2 km) southwest of the central business district of Pilot Station, a city in the Wade Hampton Census Area of the U.S. state of Alaska.

==Facilities and aircraft==

Pilot Station Airport covers an area of [tpl]convert|103|acre|ha|lk=on[/tpl] at an elevation of 305 feet (93 m) above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 7/25 with a 2,541 x 55 ft (774 x 17 m) gravel surface. For the 12-month period ending August 31, 2005, the airport had 700 aircraft operations, an average of 58 per month: 71% air taxi and 29% general aviation.
