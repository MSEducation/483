[[William Caffrey]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing, Year of death missing, Fine Gael politicians, Members of the 2nd Seanad, People from County Sligo

William John Caffrey was an Irish Fine Gael politician. He was a member of Seanad Éireann from April to August 1938. He was elected to the 2nd Seanad in April 1938 by the Agricultural Panel.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oireachtas.ie/members-hist/default.asp?housetype=1&HouseNum=2&MemberID=1188&ConstID=205|title=Mr. William Caffrey|work=Oireachtas Members Database|accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl] He did not contest the August 1938 Seanad election.
He stood unsuccessfully for Dáil Éireann as a Fine Gael candidate for the Sligo constituency at the 1937 general election.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.electionsireland.org/candidate.cfm?ID=1939|title=William Caffrey|work=ElectionsIreland.org|accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl] He was a member of Sligo County Council from 1934 to 1945.
