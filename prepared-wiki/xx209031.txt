[[Velma, Illinois]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Christian County, Illinois, Unincorporated communities in Illinois

Velma is an unincorporated community in Christian County, Illinois, United States.  It lies at [tpl]coord|39|30|53|N|89|13|57|W|type:city[/tpl].http://www.genealogytrails.com/ill/christian/index.html
A post office was established in 1894.
