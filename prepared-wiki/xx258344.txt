[[Olga Sandberg]]

CATEGORIES: 1844 births, Swedish ballerinas, Year of death missing, 1926 deaths

Olga Sandberg (born in Stockholm 1844- 1926) was a Swedish ballerina. 
Sandberg was a student at the Royal Swedish Ballet in 1854 and second dancer in 1865-1870. She was a student of Gasperini. She performed at Victoria-Theater in Berlin, in Rostock and Lemberg. She was active at the Wiener Staatsoper in Vienna in 1873—1878. 
Her best known parts was La magniola, Polka coquette, Valse, Jalousie de metier, Polka mazurka and the abbess in »Robert».
