[[North Yemen at the 1988 Summer Olympics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 1988 Summer Olympics, Yemen at the Olympics, North Yemen, 1988 in North Yemen

North Yemen competed as the Yemen Arab Republic at the 1988 Summer Olympics in Seoul, Korea.  After unification with South Yemen, the nation would return as Yemen at the 1992 Summer Olympics.
