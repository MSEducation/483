[[Catherine Loyola]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1986 births, Filipino beauty pageant winners, Filipino female models

Catherine Loyola (born 1986) is a fashion model and beauty queen from the Philippines. She competed in the ninth edition of the national Miss Philippines Earth beauty pageant and was crowned Miss Philippines Water 2009
