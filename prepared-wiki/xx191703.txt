[[Virola peruviana]]

CATEGORIES: Virola, Medicinal plants

Virola peruviana is a species of tree in the Myristicaceae family.  It is found in Brazil (Amazonas, Pará), Colombia, Ecuador and Peru.  It grows to a height of about 35 m (100 ft).  The fruit is ellipsoidal, 14-24 mm long and 11-23 mm in diameter, forming groups of about 5 to 15.
