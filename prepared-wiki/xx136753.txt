[[University of Health Sciences Antigua]]

CATEGORIES: Schools of medicine in Antigua and Barbuda

The University of Health Sciences Antigua (UHSA) is a private, for-profit medical school located in Dowhill near Falmouth, Antigua, in the Caribbean. UHSA confers upon its graduates the Doctor of Medicine (MD) degree.https://imed.faimer.org/details.asp?country=130&school=&currpage=1&cname=ANTIGUA+AND+BARBUDA&city=&region=CA&rname=Central+America%2FCaribbean&mcode=130010&psize=25

==History==

UHSA was established in 1982 and began instruction in 1983. The Dowhill campus is located about [tpl]convert|12|mi|km[/tpl] from the capital city of St. John's. Formerly a NASA tracking station, the university is located on a [tpl]convert|50|acre|m2|adj=on[/tpl] area within the Historical National Park area of English Harbour.[tpl]cite web|title=Bat's Cave, Antigua|url=http://travel.aol.com/travel-guide/mexico-and-caribbean/antigua-and-barbuda/antigua/bat-s-cave-thingstodo-detail-227014/|accessdate=17 August 2011[/tpl]

==Curriculum==

The Doctor of Medicine program at UHSA is a four year course of study that consists of three semesters per calendar year. The remainder of the program consists of clinical clerkships at affiliated hospitals in the United States, Puerto Rico, the United Kingdom, and China.[tpl]cite web|title=UHSA Affiliated Hospitals|url=http://www.uhsa.ag/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=237&Itemid=259|publisher=UHSA School of Medicine|accessdate=18 August 2012[/tpl]
