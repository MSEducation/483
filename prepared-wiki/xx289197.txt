[[Fereydun, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Fereydun ([tpl]lang-fa|فريدون[/tpl], also Romanized as Fereydūn, Farīdūn, and Fereidoon)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3062935|Fereydun[/tpl] is a village in Shivanat Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 348, in 86 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
