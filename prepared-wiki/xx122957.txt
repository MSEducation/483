[[Sebhat Ephrem]]

CATEGORIES: People from Asmara, Eritrean soldiers, Living people, 1951 births, Rebels, People's Front for Democracy and Justice politicians, Government ministers of Eritrea

Sebhat Ephrem (Tigrinya: ሰብሓት ኤፍረም; b. 1951, Asmara, Eritrea) is the Minister of Defence for Eritrea, and former Eritrean People's Liberation Front commander during the Eritrean War of Independence.
Sebhat attended high school at the Evangelical Lutheran High School in Asmara, Eritrea. Sebhat Ephrem served from June 1992 to March 1994 as the Governor of Asmara. From March 1994 to May 1995 he served as the Minister of Health. In both positions he brought structure and reorganization to these offices. After leaving the military in June 1992, he returned to the army in May 1995 with the rank of General and was appointed and confirmed as Minister of Defense.
Sebhat is a Protestant whose church is the Lutheran Church.
