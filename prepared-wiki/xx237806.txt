[[Great Asby]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Cumbria

Great Asby is a village in Cumbria, England. It is located approximately [tpl]convert|15|mi|0[/tpl] south east of Penrith and approximately [tpl]convert|5|mi[/tpl] south of Appleby-in-Westmorland. Its name is said to be derived from the [tpl]lang-non|askr[/tpl], meaning ash and by, meaning farm.Great Asby info on Cumbria Directory 
In present times the village is used mainly by the farming community.
