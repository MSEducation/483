[[James Wilson (Australian rules footballer)]]

CATEGORIES: Geelong Football Club players, Australian jockeys, Year of birth uncertain, 1935 deaths

James Wilson (born c 1855 - died 16 November 1935) was an Australian rules footballer who played with Geelong in the Victorian Football Association (VFA), captaining the side for a few seasons during the 1870s and 1880s. This was an era when the club won a number of premierships prior to the inception of the Victorian Football League (VFL).
