[[Earl (mixtape)]]

CATEGORIES: 2010 mixtape albums, OFWGKTA, Albums produced by Tyler, The Creator, Rap operas

Earl is the debut mixtape by Odd Future member Earl Sweatshirt. It was released as a free digital download on March 31, 2010, on the Odd Future website.[tpl]cite web|url=http://oddfuture.tumblr.com/post/486144881/earl-sweatshirt-earl |title=OFWGKTA: Earl Sweatshirt - EARL |publisher=Oddfuture.tumblr.com |date=2010-03-31 |accessdate=2011-02-27[/tpl]

==Background and lyrics==

The album features production by BeatBoy and fellow Odd Future members Tyler, The Creator and Left Brain. The album features vocals from Tyler, The Creator, Vince Staples, Hodgy Beats, and some backing vocals from fellow Odd Future members Syd Tha Kyd and Taco Bennett. The album has drug references, themes of rape and murder, and sexual lyrics.

==Reception==

Upon its release, the album was met with much interest and general acclaim on the web.
