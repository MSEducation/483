[[Siege of Adrianople (378)]]

CATEGORIES: 4th-century conflicts, Sieges involving the Roman Empire, Battles involving the Goths, 4th century in the Roman Empire, 378 in Europe, History of Edirne

The Siege of Adrianople took place in 378 following the Gothic victory at the Battle of Adrianople. Gothic forces were unable to breach the city walls and retreated. It was followed by an unsuccessful Gothic attempt to breach the walls of Constantinople.Alessandro Barbero, The Day of the Barbarians, Walker & Co, 2008 p 120-125
