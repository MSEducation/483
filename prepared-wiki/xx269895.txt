[[Step (Kara song)]]

CATEGORIES: 2011 songs, Korean-language songs, Kara (band) songs

"Step" is a song by Korean pop girl group Kara.  It is the group's title track from their third full-length album Step and was released to online music sites on September 6, 2011.

==Background==

On August 4, 2011, the group confirmed that they will be releasing their third official album on mid-September and will resume their activities in South Korea for the first time in over six months. It was also reported by industry representatives that the group has been recording songs for the album during their spare time while they were busy promoting their fourth Japanese single, "Go Go Summer!".  DSP Media responded to the news by stating that the group are in the process of reviewing which song is to be used as the lead song from the album.[tpl]ko icon[/tpl] [tpl]cite web|url=http://news.nate.com/view/20110805n03700|title=카라 ‘너무 바빠’ 9월 국내 컴백 ‘3주만 활동’|date=2011-08-05|work=|publisher=Nate|accessdate=2011-08-25|language=[/tpl]

==Composition==

The song was written and composed by Han Jae-ho and Kim Seung-su, also known as "Sweetune", the producers behind many of the group's hit songs including "Mister".  The song is a dance pop song, with some rock-electronica theme. In the song, the group sings about encouraging people who have been put down by sorrow and pain to stand up and "Step it up".

==Music video==

The teaser for the music video was released on September 1, 2011.  The full music video officially premiered on September 6. The video features the group dancing in front of microphones while dressed in deep blue clothes with some colorful patterns around it. In the background, there are bright lighting signs that show the names of the group's past hit songs. In other scenes the group can be seen individually in a bright room with colored walling dressed in colored neon clothing while jumping up. During the bridge of the song the girls are seen dressed in glittering dresses and holding onto microphone stands in a plain white room.  This scene was initially from another teaser video for the Step album that was uploaded on the group's YouTube page on August 29, 2011 before revealing the teaser for the music video, which featured the "Kara 4 U (Outro)" playing in the background.

===Release and reception===

The teaser for the music video was released on September 1, 2011.  The full music video officially premiered on September 6.
The music video has managed to gain over 3 million views within two days after its release. Jen Erenza from Ryan Seacrest Blog described the video as “colorful” with “out-of-this-world scenes“, and asserted, “With their super cute styles and head-bopping music, there is no doubt their popularity will rise stupendously!”.http://ryanseacrest.com/2011/09/14/crazy-colorful-south-korean-band-k-pop-gets-2-million-views-in-three-days-video/

==Live performances==

The group began performing the song on TV music starting in September 15, on Mnet's M! Countdown. They also performed "Date (My Boy)" and "With My Heart (Dear Kamilia)" as a part of the group's comeback stage special. They won 1st place on that show on the same day and they won another award on the following week. They won another award during the comeback week on KBS's show Music Bank and on the following week. The group made their comeback on SBS' Inkigayo on September 18, 2011 and won a mutizen in the show on September 25. Due to the group's commitments with their schedule in Japan they only performed the song on several music shows for three weeks.
