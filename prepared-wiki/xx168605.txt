[[McCormack Building]]

CATEGORIES: Skyscrapers in Boston, Massachusetts, Buildings and structures completed in 1975, Office buildings in Boston, Massachusetts, Skyscrapers between 100 and 149 meters, Beacon Hill, Boston

The McCormack Building is a high-rise building adjacent to the Beacon Hill neighborhood in Boston, Massachusetts. The building stands at 401 feet (122 m) with 22 floors, and was completed in 1975. It is currently the 25th-tallest building in Boston. The architectural firm who designed the building was Hoyle, Doran and Berry Architects.
