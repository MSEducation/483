[[Harish Hande]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Indian businesspeople, People from Bangalore, Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur alumni, University of Massachusetts Lowell alumni, Ramon Magsaysay Award winners, Social entrepreneurship, People associated with solar power, Solar power in India, People from Kosala

Harish Hande ([tpl]lang-kn|ಹರೀಶ್ ಹಂದೆ[/tpl]) is an Indian social entrepreneur, who co-founded SELCO India in 1995. SELCO India is a social enterprise that provides sustainable energy services to the poor in India.[tpl]cite news |title=Nileema Mishra, Harish Hande win Magsaysay award|url=http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2011-07-27/india/29820340_1_ramon-magsaysay-magsaysay-award-solar-lights |publisher=The Times of India |date=Jul 27, 2011  [/tpl] He was awarded with Asia's prestigious Ramon Magsaysay Award for 2011, also sometimes referred to as Asia's Nobel Prize,  for “his pragmatic efforts to put solar power technology in the hands of the poor, through his social enterprise SELCO India”.[tpl]cite news |title= Bangalorean gets Ramon Magsaysay Award|url= http://www.thehindu.com/news/cities/Bangalore/article2300127.ece  |publisher=The Hindu |date=July 28, 2011  [/tpl] In 2013 the Trustees of University of Massachusetts conferred on Harish Hande their highest honour by awarding him the Doctorate of Humane Letters. http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mG0CAXhGXl0

==Early life and education==

He was born in  Handattu (ಹಂದಟ್ಟು), Kundapura Taluk (ಕುಂದಾಪುರ), Udupi District, Karnataka and raised in Rourkela, Orissa, India.
After completing his basic schooling in Orissa in Ispat English Medium School, he went to  IIT Kharagpur for his undergraduate studies in Energy Engineering.A toast to IIT Kharagpur He then went to the U.S. to do his Master’s and later PhD. in Energy Engineering at the University of Massachusetts Lowell.[tpl]cite web |title=Social Entrepreneur:Harish Hande|url=http://sramanamitra.com/blog/913 [/tpl]

==Career==

Hande co-founded SELCO INDIA (in 1995), a social enterprise, to eradicate poverty by promoting sustainable technologies in rural India. With its headquarters in Bangalore, 38 energy service centers in Karnataka and Gujarat, Maharashtra, Bihar and Tamil Nadu in India  
SELCO INDIA under the leadership of Hande has won the Ashden Award for Sustainable Energy 2005 and Accenture Economic Development Award for 2005.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.techawards.org/laureates/stories/index.php?id=104 Tech Museum Award 2005|title=The Tech Awards Laureates 2005|accessdate=July 16, 2011[/tpl] In 2007 SELCO INDIA again won the Ashden Award, this time for "Outstanding Achievement" in energy sector.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ashdenawards.org/finalists_2007 Outstanding Achievement Award from Ashden Awards|title=International winners 2007[/tpl] The award was presented by Al Gore, former Vice President of the United States of America. Hande was named the Social Entrepreneur of the Year 2007[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.khemkafoundation.org/images/pdf/SEY07_booklet.pdf|title=Social Entrepreneur of the Year 2007[/tpl] by the Schwab Foundation for Social Entrepreneurship[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.schwabfound.org/sf/SocialEntrepreneurs/Profiles/index.htm?sname=173458&sorganization=0&sarea=0&ssector=0&stype=0|title=Hande Harish[/tpl] and the Nand & Jeet Khemka Foundation.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.khemkafoundation.org/|title=The NAND & KHEMKA Foundation[/tpl] In 2008, Hande was chosen by Business Today as one of the "21 young leaders for India’s 21st century".[tpl]cite web|url=https://boston.tie.org/speaker/9/dr-harish-hande|title=Dr. Harish Hande[/tpl] Later, in June 2008, India Today named him one of the 50 pioneers of change in India.[tpl]cite web|url=http://indiatoday.digitaltoday.in/index.php?option=com_content&issueid=60&task=view&id=10476&Itemid=1|title=Let there be light|publisher=India Today|date=June 26, 2008|accessdate=July 16, 2012[/tpl]
Harish Hande was also awarded the Karnataka Rajyotsava Prashasti in 2011 by the Government of Karnataka.Karnataka: Harish Hande among 50 state awardees - South India - Karnataka - ibnlive
