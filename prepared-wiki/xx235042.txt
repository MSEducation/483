[[Lots of Luck]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 television films, 1985 films, American films, 1980s comedy films

Lots of Luck is a 1985 Walt Disney television film directed by Peter Baldwin.

==Plot==

The film focuses on a blue-collar family who wins the lottery. Their lives are changed forever, sometimes for the good, sometimes for the bad.Review Summary The New York Times
