[[Batagay]]

CATEGORIES: Settlements of urban type in the Sakha Republic, Populated places of Arctic Russia

Batagay ([tpl]lang-ru|Батагай[/tpl]) is an urban locality (a settlement) and the administrative center of Verkhoyansky District in the Sakha Republic, Russia, located on the Yana River. Population: [tpl]ru-census|p2010=4,369|p2002=4,589|p1989=8,385[/tpl]

==Transportation==

Batagay is served by the Batagay Airport.
