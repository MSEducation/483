</ref> [[Andrew Sarris]] in the ''[[New York Observer]]'' wrote, "the main incentive to see this movie is its witty, pungent and idiomatic dialogue, such as you never hear on the screen anymore in this age of special-effects illiteracy".<ref name= "sarris">

===Legacy===

More than half a century after its release, Sweet Smell of Success is commonly listed among the greatest films of all time. In 2012, the movie was ranked #171 on Sight & Sound's list of the top 250 films, as selected by the British Film Institute.The Greatest Films Poll. Sight & Sound. Retrieved December 13, 2013. The film was among the 400 nominated movies for the American Film Institute's 100 Years ... 100 Movies (10th Anniversary Edition),[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.afi.com/Docs/100Years/Movies_ballot_06.pdf|title=AFI's 100 Years... 100 Movies (10th Anniversary Edition): Official Ballot|publisher=American Film Institute|accessdate=December 13, 2013[/tpl] AFI's list of the best films in American cinema. In 2008, Empire magazine ranked the film on their list of The 500 Greatest Movies of All Time.The 500 Greatest Movies of All Time. Empire. Retrieved December 13, 2013. It was placed on a similar list of 1000 films by The New York Times.The Best 1000 Movies Ever Made. The New York Times. Retrieved December 13, 2013. In 2010, Total Film selected Sweet Smell of Success as one of The 100 Greatest Movies of All Time.[tpl]cite web| url=http://www.totalfilm.com/features/100-greatest-movies-of-all-time/page:8 |title=Film Features: 100 Greatest Movies of All Time |publisher=Total Film |date= |accessdate=December 13, 2013[/tpl] Time magazine ranked the film as one of the All-TIME 100 Movies.[tpl]cite news|url=http://web.archive.org/web/20110628234513/http://www.time.com/time/specials/packages/completelist/0,29569,1953094,00.html |title=Time Magazine's All-Time 100 Movies |publisher=Internet Archive |work=Time |date= February 12, 2005|accessdate= December 13, 2013[/tpl] The film was 49th on Entertainment Weekly's list of the 100 Greatest Movies of All Time.[tpl]cite web|work=Entertainment Weekly |url=http://www.filmsite.org/ew100.html |title=Entertainment Weekly's 100 Greatest Movies of All Time |publisher=Published by AMC FilmSite.org |date= |accessdate= December 13, 2013[/tpl] Additionally, Films101.com ranked the film as the 132nd best movie of all time (a list of the 10,059 most notable).[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.films101.com/filmsr.htm#132 |title=The Best Movies of All Time (10,059 Most Notable) |publisher=Films101.com |date= |accessdate= December 13, 2013[/tpl] In 1993, Sweet Smell of Success was selected for preservation in the United States National Film Registry by the Library of Congress as being "culturally, historically, or aesthetically significant".[ref]
