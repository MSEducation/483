[[The Tony Rock Project]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 American television series debuts, 2000s American television series, 2009 American television series endings

The Tony Rock Project is a sketch comedy television series that premiered on October 8, 2008 on MyNetworkTV. The show stars Tony Rock, John Heffron and Whitney Cummings.MyNetworkTV Prepares to Rock Out, Broadcasting & Cable, June 5, 2008. It served as the lead-in for Flavor Flav's sitcom, Under One Roof.
