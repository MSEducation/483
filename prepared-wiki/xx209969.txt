[[Croatia at the 2008 Summer Paralympics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 2008 Summer Paralympics, Croatia at the Paralympics, 2008 in Croatian sport

Croatia competed at the 2008 Summer Paralympics in Beijing. The country's delegation consisted of 25 competitors.
Croatian competitors took part in table tennis,"9 Nations preparing for the Paralympics in Lasko", official website of the International Paralympic Table Tennis Federation athletics, equestrian, and swimming.
