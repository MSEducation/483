[[Robert Nyholm]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, HIFK players, Kingston Frontenacs players, 1988 births, Espoo Blues players

Robert Nyholm (born March 7, 1988) is a Finnish professional ice hockey forward who currently plays for HIFK of the SM-liiga.HIFK Helsinki's 2010-11 Roster He was selected by the  Columbus Blue Jackets in the 5th round (129th overall) of the 2006 NHL Entry Draft.
