[[Geovane]]

Geovane (born 5 August 1985) is a Brazilian football forward, who plays for São Caetano.
His previous club is Daegu FC in South Korea, América Futebol Clube (RN) and Salgueiro Atlético Clube. During the 2010 season, he also played for Grêmio Prudente and Guarani Futebol Clube in the Brazilian Série A.
