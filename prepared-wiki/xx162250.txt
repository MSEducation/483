John Dunne is an American college basketball coach and current head men's basketball coach at Saint Peter's College.[tpl]cite news|last=Brennan|first=Sean|title=PEACOCKS HIRE HALL ASSISTANT|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/archives/sports/2006/05/25/2006-05-25_peacocks_hire_hall_assistant.html|accessdate=30 October 2010|newspaper=New York Daily News|date=25 May 2006[/tpl]
He was previously an assistant coach at Seton Hall University under Louis Orr. He has also served as an assistant coach at Siena College.
Dunne was born in Queens, New York and attended Archbishop Molloy High School, where he also played basketball. He played at the collegiate level at Ithaca College.
