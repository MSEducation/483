[[Tammara Webber]]

CATEGORIES: American fiction writers

Tammara Webber is an American New York Times,http://www.nytimes.com/best-sellers-books/2012-07-29/e-book-fiction/list.html USA Today,http://books.usatoday.com/book/tammara-webber-easy/l47311 and Amazon Bestseller novelist. Her new-adult fiction title Easy was released on eBook and paperback in June 2012 then republished through Penguin/Razorbill UK Publishinghttp://www.thebookseller.com/news/razorbill-uk-buys-new-adult-star.html-0http://tammarawebber.blogspot.com/2012/09/penguinrazorbill-uk-announcement.html and Penguin/Berkley US Publishing in September 2012 and October 2012, respectively. As of today, Easy has a total of 23 separate foreign translation contracts. She is also the author of the US/Canada self-published young adult romance series Between the Lineshttp://www.goodreads.com/author/show/4826501.Tammara_Webber which has also been republished by Penguin/Razorbill UK Publishing.
Webber is currently working on a new-adult fiction novel titled Breakable, a prequel to her best selling novel Easy, tentatively set to be published May 6th 2014.https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/17936925-breakable
