[[Williamstown Beach railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1889

Williamstown Beach railway station is located on the Williamstown line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the western Melbourne suburb of Williamstown opening on 7 August 1889 being renamed Williamstown Beach on 1 February 1915.North Williamstown Vicsig
