[[Arqin Bolagh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Arqin Bolagh ([tpl]lang-fa|ارقين بلاغ[/tpl], also Romanized as Arqīn Bolāgh and Ārqīn Bolāgh; also known as Ārām Bulāq and Arghīn Bolāgh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3053627|Arqin Bolagh[/tpl] is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 954, in 204 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
