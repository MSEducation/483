[[Musée Jean Moulin]]

CATEGORIES: Museums in Paris, World War II museums in France, Biographical museums in France

The Musée Jean Moulin is a museum located in the XIVe arrondissement at 23, Allée de la 2e DB, Jardin Atlantique, Paris, France. It is open daily except Mondays; [tpl]As of|2008|lc=y[/tpl] admission was free of charge.
The museum opened in 1994 to commemorate Jean Moulin, a major figure of the French Resistance, and the occupation of Paris during World War II. Its accompanying museum, the Mémorial du maréchal Leclerc de Hauteclocque et de la Libération de Paris, commemorates Maréchal Philippe Leclerc de Hauteclocque and the liberation of Paris.
