The Bradford West Gwillimbury Fire & Emergency Services is the fire department serving Bradford West Gwillimbury, Ontario, Canada.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.innisfilscope.com/news/2009-04-29/police/014.html|title=Batgirl clings to outlet mall tower |date=April 29, 2009|work=The Scope|accessdate=10 January 2010|location=Innisfil, Ontario[/tpl]

==Suppression==

Bradford West Gwillimbury Fire & Emergency Services consists of 8 full-time firefighters, 1 full-time Fire Prevention Officer, 2 full-time Chiefs, and 30 volunteer firefighters.
