[[Taking the Blame]]

CATEGORIES: 1935 films, American animated films, Betty Boop cartoons

Taking The Blame is a 1935 Fleischer Studios animated short film starring Betty Boop, and featuring Pudgy the Puppy.

==Synopsis==

Betty brings home a cat as a playmate for her pet puppy, Pudgy. The cat manages to get Pudgy blamed for all his misbehavior.
