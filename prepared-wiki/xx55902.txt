[[List of common carrier freight railroads in the United States]]

CATEGORIES: Railway companies of the United States, Lists of companies of the United States, United States railway-related lists

About 650 railroads operate common carrier freight service in the United States. There are about 150,000 miles (240,000 km) of railroad track in the United States, nearly all standard gauge.
Reporting marks are listed in parentheses.Railinc, Search MARKs, accessed February 2009
