[[Edwin Hazelton]]

CATEGORIES: 1861 births, 1916 deaths, People from Southampton, English cricketers, Hampshire cricketers

Edwin Hills Hazelton (16 December 1861 â€“ 25 July 1916) was an English cricketer.
Hazelton represented Hampshire in three first-class matches in 1883. Hazelton made his debut against Sussex, where he made a score of 50 exactly, thus registering his only first-class half century and his highest first-class score on debut. Hazelton played two further matches against Surrey and Somerset.
Hazelton died in Simla in the British Raj on July 25, 1916.
