Jamie Thomas King (born 9 July 1981) is an English actor.

==Personal life==

King is the son of director Christopher King and writer Laura Lamson. He attended the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art, graduating in 2002.

==Career==

King has appeared in a number of stage, film and TV productions, most notably in the film apaptation of Alice Hoffman's The River King opposite Edward Burns and in Tristan & Isolde.
