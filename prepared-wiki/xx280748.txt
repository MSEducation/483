[[Hajji Ahmad]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Hajji Ahmad ([tpl]lang-fa|حاجي احمد[/tpl], also Romanized as Ḩājjī Aḩmad)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3838177|Hajji Ahmad[/tpl] is a village in Bonab Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 24, in 11 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
