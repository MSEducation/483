[[Tazeh Kand, Ijrud]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Tazeh Kand ([tpl]lang-fa|تازه كند[/tpl], also Romanized as Tāzeh Kand; also known as Tazakend)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3087297|Tazeh Kand[/tpl] is a village in Golabar Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 49, in 10 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
