[[Council of Ministers of Cyprus]]

CATEGORIES: Government of Cyprus, Cypriot governments, National cabinets

The Council of Ministers is the executive branch of the Cypriot federal government, consisting of ministers. The council is chaired by the President of Cyprus and the ministers head executive departments of the government. The President and his ministers administer the government and the various public services.

==Cabinet==

The current council, Anastasiades Government, sworn in on 28 February 2013, consists of 13 ministers and 5 state secretaries formed by a coalition of DISY, DIKO and EVROKO.
