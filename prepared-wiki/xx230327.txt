[[Isopogon adenanthoides]]

CATEGORIES: Eudicots of Western Australia, Isopogon

Isopogon adenanthoides (Spider Coneflower) is a small shrub that is endemic to the south-west of Western Australia.
It is usually between 0.3 and 1 metre high and produces pink to purple flowers between June and October in the species native range.[tpl]FloraBase|name=Isopogon adenanthoides|id=2219[/tpl]
The species was first formally described by botanist Carl Meissner in Hooker's Journal of Botany and Kew Garden Miscellany in 1855.
