[[Post Oak Middle School]]

CATEGORIES: Educational institutions established in 2006, Public middle schools in Virginia, Spotsylvania County Public Schools

Post Oak Middle School is a middle school located near Post Oak, Virginia in Spotsylvania County in the U.S. state of Virginia. Spotsylvania High School is right near Post Oak Middle School. In 2006, the school was opened to the public.[tpl]Cite news| url=http://www.fredericksburg.com/News/FLS/2006/082006/08242006/216312| title=School begins in Spotsylvania; Post Oak Middle School opens| date=2006-08-24| first=Melissa| last=Nix| publisher=The Free Lance-Star[/tpl] 
Post Oak Middle School is the home of the Patriots. 
In 2008, Post Oak Middle School made AYP for the first time ever.
As of 2008, the theme is (Failure Is Not An Option) or FINAO.
Post Oak Middle School is named after the area Post Oak, Virginia, which is less than half a mile away.
