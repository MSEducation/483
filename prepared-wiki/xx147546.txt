[[RNA spike-in]]

CATEGORIES: RNA, Microarrays

A RNA spike-in is a RNA transcript used to calibrate measurements in a DNA microarray experiment. Each spike-in is designed to hybridize with a specific control probe on the target array.  Manufacturers of commercially available microarrays typically offer companion RNA spike-ins "kits".[tpl]cite journal |author=Yang IV |title=Use of external controls in microarray experiments. |journal=Methods Enzymol |volume=411 |issue= |pages=50–63 |year=2006 |pmid=16939785 |doi=10.1016/S0076-6879(06)11004-6[/tpl][tpl]cite journal |author=Fardin P, Moretti S, Biasotti B, Ricciardi A, Bonassi S, Varesio L |title=Normalization of low-density microarray using external spike-in controls: analysis of macrophage cell lines expression profile. |journal=BMC Genomics |volume=8 |issue= |pages=17 |year=2007 |pmid=17229315 | doi = 10.1186/1471-2164-8-17 |pmc=1797020[/tpl][tpl]cite journal |author=Wilkes T, Laux H, Foy CA |title=Microarray data quality - review of current developments. |journal=OMICS |volume=11 |issue=1 |pages=1–13 |year=2007 |pmid=17411392 |doi=10.1089/omi.2006.0001[/tpl][tpl]cite journal |author=Schuster EF, Blanc E, Partridge L, Thornton JM |title=Estimation and correction of non-specific binding in a large-scale spike-in experiment. |journal=Genome Biol |volume=8 |issue=6 |pages=R126 |year=2007 |pmid=17594493 |doi=10.1186/gb-2007-8-6-r126 |pmc=2394775[/tpl]
Known amounts of RNA spike-ins are mixed with the experiment sample during preparation. Subsequently the measured degree of hybridization between the spike-ins and the control probes is used to normalize the hybridization measurements of the sample RNA.
