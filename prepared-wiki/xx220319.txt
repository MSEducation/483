[[Nogawan]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Fatehgarh Sahib district

Nogawan is a small town on Bassi-Morinda road, about 6 km from Bassi Pathana. It is fast growing town and proudly houses a Senior Secondary School, Petrol Pump, Gurudwara, Flour Mill, Post Office, Railway station, Telephone Exchange, Bus Stand, Animal Husbandry and a Dispensary.
