[[KHKV]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Texas, Religious radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 1998

KHKV (91.1 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a religious radio format.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www1.arbitron.com/sip/sip.do#access |date=Summer 2009 |title=Station Information Profile |publisher=Arbitron |accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl] Licensed to Kerrville, Texas, USA, the station serves the Kerrville-Fredericksburg area.  The station is currently owned by Houston Christian Broadcasters. [tpl]cite web |url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KHKV |title=KHKV Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division |accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl]

==History==

The station was assigned the call letters KBBP on February 20, 1998. On April 6, 1998, the station changed its call sign to KKER, on February 1, 2002 to the current KHKV, [tpl]cite web |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=83433&Callsign=KHKV |title=KHKV Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division |accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl] On January 4, 2001 the station was sold to Houston Christian Broadcasters.[tpl]cite web|url= |title=FCC Application  |publisher=Federal Communications Commission |accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl]
