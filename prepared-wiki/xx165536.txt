[[Fathers and Sons (The Outer Limits)]]

CATEGORIES: The Outer Limits (1995 TV series) episodes, 1999 television episodes

"Fathers and Sons" is an episode of The Outer Limits television show. It first aired on 6 August 1999, during the fifth season.

==Introduction==

A young woman visits her grandmother, who appears as if she is suffering from Alzheimer's disease. As soon as the young woman leaves, the caretakers put the grandmother back into a container in a very large storage facility.

==Opening narration==

==Plot==

Later a man decides that his father, a famous blues musician, needs to move to an old folks home. Shortly after joining the home the man begins to suffer memory problems and complain about bad dreams. It turns out that he is having his memories taken out of his brain at the home and they're then being sold. When the grandson tries to rescue the grandfather from the home his father stops him and then destroys the memory stealing and transferring machines after removing the memories of the 3 staff members in that building.
