[[Rural Municipality of Shell River]]

CATEGORIES: Hudson's Bay Company trading posts, Rural municipalities in Manitoba

Shell River is a rural municipality in the province of Manitoba in Western Canada. On January 1, 2007, the area of Park (North), which was previously a detached section of the rural municipality of Park, was added to Shell River.[tpl]Cite web |url=http://www.amm.mb.ca/PDF/Tools%20for%20Change/Shell%20River%20Park%20North.doc |title=Working Regionally With New Partners: Annexation.
