[[Shahram Mokri]]

CATEGORIES: Iranian film directors, Living people

Shahram Mokri ([tpl]lang-fa|شهرام مکری[/tpl]) is an Iranian Kurd from Kermashan writer and film director.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.sourehcinema.com/People/People.aspx?Id=138403160004 |title=Iranian Movie Database شهرام مكري : |publisher=Sourehcinema.com |date= |accessdate=2013-11-06[/tpl] His film Mahi Va Gorbeh (Fish & Cat) was shown at the 70th Venice Film Festival.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.labiennale.org/en/cinema/70th-festival/line-up/off-sel/orizzonti/gorbeh.html |title=La Biennale di Venezia - Mahi Va Gorbeh (Fish & Cat) |publisher=Venice Film Festival |date=2013-09-07 |accessdate=2013-11-06[/tpl] He was nominated for the 2013 Asia Pacific Screen Award for Achievement in Directing for Fish & Cat.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.asiapacificscreenacademy.com/the-awards/nominees-2013/|title=Winners & Nominees 2013|accessdate=2013-12-19|work=asiapacificscreenacademy.com[/tpl] For his short film The Dragonfly Storm, Mokri won Best Director at the 2007 Noor Iranian Film Festival.
