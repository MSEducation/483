Sergei Viktorovich Soin ([tpl]lang-ru|Серге́й Викторович Соин[/tpl]; born March 31, 1982 in Moscow, Soviet Union) is a Russian professional  ice hockey forward who is currently playing for Dynamo Moscow of the Kontinental Hockey League. He joined Dynamo on a two-year contract from the Severstal Cherepovets on May 16, 2011.[tpl]cite web| url = http://www.dynamo.ru/press-center/club_news/37578/ | title = Sergei Soin joins the fold in Dynamo | language = Russian | date = 2011-05-16 | accessdate = 2011-05-16 | publisher = UHC Dynamo Moscow[/tpl]
