[[Durmeshqan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Durmeshqan ([tpl]lang-fa|دورمشقان[/tpl], also Romanized as Dūrmeshqān and Dūrmoshqān; also known as Darmeshkān, Domoshqān, Dor Meshkān, Dormoshkān, and Durmushkha)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3061865|Durmeshqan[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Bala Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 46, in 8 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
