[[Wonder World Amusement Park]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 video games, Majesco Entertainment games, Party video games, Wii games, Wii-only games

Wonder World Amusement Park is a video game for seventh generation consoles developed by Majesco Games and published by Coyote Console.

==Gameplay==

Wonder World features a Carnival setting and an array of mini-games based on Carnival Games. Games include Ring Toss, Basketball and Knife Throwing. Players for the DS use the stylus to push or throw the necessary items, most common technique is via a simple flick upwards.
