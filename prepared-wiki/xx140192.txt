[[Stephen Douglas (journalist)]]

CATEGORIES: British journalists, Living people

Stephen Douglas is a British journalist, currently working for Sky News. He is the former North of England Correspondent for ITV News.[tpl]cite web |url=http://tvnewsroom.co.uk/people/stephen-douglas/ |title=Stephen Douglas: Biography and Images |deadurl=no |accessdate=1 February 2013[/tpl] 
Stephen is a BAFTAhttp://corporate.itn.co.uk/press.php?parent_id=13&content_id=1312 award winning journalist having been the first reporter on the scene of the Cumbria Shootings.
He used to report for the flagship programmes ITV News at 6:30 and the ITV News at Ten
Stephen has reported from across the UK and the world.
