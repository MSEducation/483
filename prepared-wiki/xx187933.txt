[[Lamont, Kansas]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Greenwood County, Kansas, Unincorporated communities in Kansas

Lamont (also La Monts Mill) is an unincorporated community in northeastern Greenwood County, Kansas, United States.  It lies along K-58 northeast of the city of Eureka, the county seat of Greenwood County.Rand McNally.  The Road Atlas '08. Chicago: Rand McNally, 2008, p. 41.  Its elevation is 1,125 feet (343 m), and it is located at [tpl]coord|38|6|45|N|96|1|36|W|region:US_type:city[/tpl] (38.1125234, -96.0266589).Geographic Names Information System Feature Detail Report, Geographic Names Information System, 1978-10-13.  Accessed 2008-04-28.  Although Lamont is unincorporated, it has a post office, with the ZIP code of 66855.Zip Code Lookup
