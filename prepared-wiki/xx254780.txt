[[Goin' Ape!]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 albums, Jackie Martling albums

Goin' Ape! is an album by American comedian, comedy writer and radio personality Jackie Martling.  The album was released in 1980 on the Off Hour Rockers label.

==Track listing==

==Background==

In 1979, Martling issued his debut LP, What Did You Expect?  He released two more albums, 1980's Goin' Ape! and 1981's Normal People Are People You Don't Know That Well.  Martling sent all three records to fledgling New York City disk jockey Howard Stern.[tpl]cite web|url=[tpl]Allmusic|class=artist|id=p201876/biography|pure_url=yes[/tpl]|title= Jackie Martling's Biography |last=Ankeny |first=Jason |accessdate=January 10, 2010 |publisher=AllMusic Guide [/tpl] By 1986, he was a full-time member of Stern's show, later becoming the program's head writer. Martling maintained a steady schedule of live dates while working with Stern, recording Sgt. Pecker, Hot Dogs + Donuts, and The Very Best of Jackie Martling's Talking Joke Book Cassettes, Vol. 1.
