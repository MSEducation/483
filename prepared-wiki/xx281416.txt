[[K14IS-D]]

CATEGORIES: Channel 14 digital TV stations in the United States, Television channels and stations established in 1984, Television stations in Montana, Television stations in Billings, Montana, Channel 14 virtual TV stations in the United States

K14IS-D is a Daystar affiliated television station licensed to Billings, Montana. The station began broadcasting digitally on October 19, 2009. It was once known as K05HS from 1984 to 1993, broadcasting on VHF channel 5. The station's transmitter is located on Coburn Hill southeast of downtown Billings.
