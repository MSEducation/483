[[Sarvis Creek Wilderness]]

CATEGORIES: Wilderness Areas of Colorado, Protected areas established in 1993, Protected areas of Grand County, Colorado, Protected areas of Routt County, Colorado, Routt National Forest

The Sarvis Creek Wilderness is a U.S. Wilderness Area located south of Steamboat Springs, Colorado in the Routt National Forest. The area is named after the Sarvis Timber Company, which once logged the area. The area contains no alpine tundra, in contrast to most wilderness areas in Colorado.
