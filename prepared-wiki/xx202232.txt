[[Koudougou Department]]

CATEGORIES: Departments of Burkina Faso, Boulkiemdé Province

Koudougou is a department or commune of Boulkiemdé Province in central Burkina Faso. As of 2005 it has a population of 131,394. Its capital lies at the town of Koudougou.Burkinabé government inforoute communale
