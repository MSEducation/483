[[Clube Atlético Monte Líbano (basketball)]]

CATEGORIES: Basketball teams in Brazil, Basketball teams in São Paulo (city)

Clube Atlético Monte Líbano, or C.A. Monte Líbano, is a Brazilian men's professional basketball club that is based in Brazil.

==History==

Monte Líbano won the Brazilian League championship 5 times, in the years 1982, 1984, 1985, 1986, and 1987. They also won the South American Club Championship twice, in the years 1985 and 1986.
