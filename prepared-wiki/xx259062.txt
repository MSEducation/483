Jacques Kallis is a South African cricketer who plays as an all-rounder. He has been described as "the greatest cricketer ever",[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/sport/cricket/international/england/6622640/South-Africa-all-rounder-Jacques-Kallis-ruled-out-of-ODI-against-England.html |title=South Africa all-rounder Jacques Kallis ruled out of ODI against England |work=The Daily Telegraph |publisher=Telegraph Media Group |location=London |last=Berry |first=Scyld |date=21 November 2009 |accessdate=2010-12-29[/tpl][tpl]cite news |url=http://sport.iafrica.com/columns/dan_world/694888.html |title=Kallis the greatest ever? |last=Nicholl |first=Dan |work=iafrica.com |publisher=Primedia |date=21 December 2010 |accessdate=2011-10-19[/tpl] and is considered one of the best all-rounders of all time, along with Sir Garfield Sobers.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.espncricinfo.com/allrounder/content/story/273619.html |title=Sharing space with Sir Garry |last=Manthorp |first=Neil |date=22 December 2006 |publisher=ESPNcricinfo |accessdate=2010-12-29[/tpl] He has scored 45 centuries (100 or more runs in a single innings) in Test cricket, the most by any South African,[tpl]cite web|url=http://stats.espncricinfo.com/ci/engine/records/batting/most_hundreds_career.html?class=1;id=3;type=team |title=Records / South Africa / Test matches / Most hundreds |publisher=ESPNcricinfo |accessdate=2010-12-29[/tpl] and 17 centuries in One Day International (ODI) matches.[tpl]cite web|url=http://stats.espncricinfo.com/ci/engine/records/batting/most_hundreds_career.html?class=2;id=3;type=team |title=Records / South Africa / One-Day Internationals / Most hundreds |publisher=ESPNcricinfo |accessdate=2010-12-29[/tpl] He leads all South African batsmen in runs in both Test and ODI cricket.[tpl]cite web|url=http://stats.espncricinfo.com/ci/engine/records/batting/most_runs_career.html?class=1;id=3;type=team |title=Records / South Africa / Test matches / Most runs |publisher=ESPNcricinfo |accessdate=2011-01-02[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://stats.espncricinfo.com/ci/engine/records/batting/most_runs_career.html?class=2;id=3;type=team |title=Records / South Africa / One-Day Internationals / Most runs |publisher=ESPNcricinfo |accessdate=2011-01-02[/tpl]
Kallis made his Test debut against England in December 1995.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/2/2231/Test_Matches.html |title=Test Matches played by Jacques Kallis |publisher=CricketArchive |accessdate=2010-12-29[/tpl] He scored his maiden Test century just over two years later, in his seventh Test match, making 101 against Australia.[tpl]cite web|url=http://stats.espncricinfo.com/ci/engine/player/45789.html?class=1;filter=advanced;orderby=start;runsmin1=100;runsval1=runs;template=results;type=batting;view=innings |title=Statistics / Statsguru / JH Kallis / Test matches |publisher=ESPNcricinfo |accessdate=2010-12-29[/tpl] In the 2003–04 series against the West Indies, Kallis scored a century in each of the four Test matches, becoming the first cricketer to pass 100 in every match of a series lasting more than three Tests.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.espncricinfo.com/ci/content/story/137625.html |title=Kallis breaks new ground as South Africa dominate |last=Auld |first=Freddie |date=17 January 2004 |publisher=ESPNcricinfo |accessdate=2010-12-29[/tpl] His feat also marked the first time a South African player scored centuries in four consecutive Test matches, a run he extended in his next match, scoring 150 not out against New Zealand, to become the second player, after Sir Donald Bradman, to pass 100 in five consecutive Tests.[tpl]cite web|url=http://stats.espncricinfo.com/ci/content/records/282976.html |title=Records / Test matches / Batting records / Hundreds in consecutive matches |publisher=ESPNcricinfo |accessdate=2010-12-29[/tpl] Kallis passed Gary Kirsten as South Africa's leading run-scorer in Test cricket when he reached his 22nd century during the 2004–05 tour of the West Indies.[tpl]cite book |editor1-first=Matthew |editor1-last=Engel |title=Wisden Cricketer's Almanack 2006 |edition=143 |year=2006 |publisher=John Wisden & Co. Ltd. |location=Alton, Hampshire |isbn=0-947766-98-7 |last=Vice |first=Telford |chapter=West Indies v South Africa, 2004–05 |pages=1114–1115 |url=http://www.espncricinfo.com/wisdenalmanack/content/story/289021.html[/tpl] His achievements during the 2004–05 and 2005 cricket seasons, during which time he scored six Test centuries, resulted in him being named as Test Player of the Year by the International Cricket Council (ICC).[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.espncricinfo.com/icc-media/content/story/221650.html |title=Jacques Kallis wins Test Player of the Year honours at ICC Awards |publisher=ESPNcricinfo |author=ICC Media Release |date=11 October 2005 |accessdate=2011-01-02[/tpl] In October 2007 he became the fourth South African to reach 100 in both innings of a Test match when he did so against Pakistan.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.espncricinfo.com/magazine/content/story/313686.html |title=Hope for left-arm spin, and Kallis' riposte |author=S Rajesh |publisher=ESPNcricinfo |date=5 October 2007 |accessdate=2011-01-02[/tpl] He scored a century in each of the following three Tests, tallying five centuries in four Test matches.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.espncricinfo.com/rsavnz/content/story/321138.html |title=The Steyn show |publisher=ESPNcricinfo |last=Varghese |first=Mathew |date=19 November 2007 |accessdate=2011-01-02[/tpl] In doing so, he joined Bradman, Matthew Hayden and Ken Barrington as one of just four players to have scored centuries in four consecutive Test matches on two occasions. Kallis scored his first double century in Test cricket in December 2010, scoring an unbeaten 201 in the first Test against India.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.espncricinfo.com/ci/content/story/493284.html |title=New feather in Kallis' cap |publisher=ESPNcricinfo |last=Moonda |first=Firdose |date=18 December 2010 |accessdate=2011-01-03[/tpl] Prior to his double century, Kallis had been the only player in the top 15 Test run-scorers not to have reached 200.[tpl]cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/cricket/9302028.stm |title=India in trouble after Jacques Kallis double century |publisher=BBC Sport |date=18 December 2010 |accessdate=2011-01-03[/tpl] Later during the same series, Kallis struck centuries in both innings of a match for the second time in his career, becoming the first South African to achieve the feat on two occasions.[tpl]cite web|url=http://stats.espncricinfo.com/ci/content/records/282951.html |title=Records / Test matches / Batting records / Hundred in each innings of a match |publisher=ESPNcricinfo |accessdate=2011-01-16[/tpl] He reached his second double century, and his highest score, in 2012 against Sri Lanka, making 224.
In ODI cricket, Kallis scored his maiden century in January 1998, totalling 111 against New Zealand.[tpl]cite web|url=http://stats.espncricinfo.com/ci/engine/player/45789.html?class=2;filter=advanced;orderby=start;runsmin1=100;runsval1=runs;template=results;type=batting;view=innings |title=Statistics / Statsguru / JH Kallis / One-Day Internationals |publisher=ESPNcricinfo |accessdate=2011-01-16[/tpl] He reached his highest total in ODIs against the West Indies in February 2004, scoring 139. He generally scores at slower than one run per delivery, but does score more quickly on occasions on which he reaches a century, averaging 90.24 runs per hundred balls, rather than 72.77 when he scores less than 100. His highest strike-rate when scoring a century was 117.43, achieved when he scored 128 not out against the Netherlands during the 2007 Cricket World Cup. He is the only South African to have scored over 10,000 runs in ODI cricket,[tpl]cite web|url=http://stats.espncricinfo.com/ci/engine/records/batting/most_runs_career.html?class=2;id=3;type=team |title=Records / South Africa / One-Day Internationals / Most runs |publisher=ESPNcricinfo |accessdate=2011-01-16[/tpl] while his 17 ODI centuries rank him second behind the 21 scored by Herschelle Gibbs among his countrymen.[tpl]cite web|url=http://stats.espncricinfo.com/ci/engine/records/batting/most_hundreds_career.html?class=2;id=3;type=team |title=Records / South Africa / One-Day Internationals / Most hundreds |publisher=ESPNcricinfo |accessdate=2011-01-16[/tpl]
