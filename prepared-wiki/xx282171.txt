[[Hershey Lodge]]

CATEGORIES: Buildings and structures in Dauphin County, Pennsylvania, Convention centers in Pennsylvania

The Hershey Lodge and Convention Center is a hotel and convention center complex located in Hershey, Pennsylvania. It has 665 guest rooms and 100,000 square feet of function space. Its meeting and exhibit space consists of the Great American and Confection Halls along with the Chocolate and Cocoa Ballrooms.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.hersheymeetings.com/hershey_properties/hershey_lodge/pdf/hersheyLodgeSpecs.pdf|title=Hershey Lodge Specs|accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl] It is located between and near Routes 322 and 422.
