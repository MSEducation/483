[[Strawberry Line (Miniature Railway)]]

CATEGORIES: British miniature railways, Heritage railways in Somerset

The Strawberry Line is a [tpl]convert|0.75|mi|km|sing=on[/tpl] length of [tpl]RailGauge|5in[/tpl] gauge railway inside Avon Valley Country Park (near Keynsham). It is Britain's only commercial ground-level [tpl]RailGauge|5in[/tpl] railway.[ref]
