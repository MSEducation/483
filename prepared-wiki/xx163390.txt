Mohamed Amine Chermiti (born 26 December 1987 in Sfax) is a Tunisian footballer who plays as a striker for FC Zürich.

==Club career==

Chermiti has been mentioned as one of the African players of the year due to his performance in the African Champions League, where he scored eight goals in the tournament. He is credited for Etoile's superb performance in the Champions League.
On 6 August 2008, he officially transferred to Hertha BSC[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.tagesspiegel.de/sport/hertha-bsc/amine-chermiti-der-kleine-kommt-zurueck/1370116.html | title = Amine Chermiti: Der Kleine kommt zurück | language = German | publisher = tagesspiegel.de | date = 12 November 2008 | accessdate = 5 March 2011[/tpl] and played his first match for them the following day in the first round of the German Cup. On 11 August 2009, Chermiti was loaned to Saudi Arabian top club Al-Ittihad for one year[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.berlin-aktuell.de/hertha-bsc-amine-chermiti-auf-leihbasis-nach-saudi-arabien_id1347.html | title = Hertha BSC: Amine Chermiti auf Leihbasis nach Saudi-Arabien | language = German | publisher = berlin-aktuell.de | date = 13 August 2009 | accessdate = 5 March 2011[/tpl] and returned on 30 June 2010.[tpl]cite web | url = http://bazonline.ch/sport/fussball/FCZ-pokert-um-Amine-Chermiti/story/28122137 | title = FCZ pokert um Amine Chermiti | language = German | publisher = Basler Zeitung | date = 15 June 2010 | accessdate = 5 March 2011[/tpl] 
On 5 July 2010, Chermiti signed a four-year contract with FC Zürich[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.fcz.ch/profis/news_detail.htm?id=885 | title = Amine Chermiti unterschreibt bis Sommer 2014 | language = German | publisher = FC Zürich | date = 5 July 2010 | accessdate = 5 March 2011[/tpl] until summer 2014.
