[[Abdullah Alam]]

CATEGORIES: Bangladeshi cricketers, Rajshahi Division cricketers, Living people, Year of birth missing (living people)

Abdullah Alam, also referred to as Abdulled Al Mamun or Mamun, is a former Bangladeshi cricketer. He played in 3 first-class cricket matches and 9 List A cricket matches for Rajshahi Division in the Bangladesh domestic league between the 2001-02 and 2003-04 seasons.
