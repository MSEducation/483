[[Lê Gia Tông]]

CATEGORIES: 1661 births, 1675 deaths, Lê Dynasty emperors, 17th-century monarchs in Asia

Lê Gia Tông (chữ Hán: 黎嘉宗; 1661-1675), also Lê Duy Cối (黎維禬, 黎維𥘺) was a child king of Vietnam, reigning from 1672 to 1675 as the 9th of the figurehead kings of the later Lê dynasty warlord period.Bruce M. Lockhart,William J. Duiker The A to Z of Vietnam 2010 p437 "Lê Gia Tông"
