[[Dyscord]]

CATEGORIES: Western Australian musical groups, Musical groups established in 2002, 2002 establishments in Australia

Dyscord are a Metal band from Perth, Western Australia, Australia. In 2006 they released their debut E.P. Arming Within through independent label Prime Cuts and toured this release extensively around Australia on runs with such International bands as God Forbid and The Black Dahlia Murder.
2008 seen the release of their debut full length album Dakota, recorded by West Australian Music Industry Award nominated producer Adam Sparks of Wing Command Studios and Birds of Tokyo. Dakota was well received to many positive reviews and was toured nationally both on an independent headlining tour and alongside Tennessee's Whitechapel and Tasmania's Psycroptic.
Through their short career they have supported the likes of onternational heavyweights Machine Head, Arch Enemy, Trivium, Killswitch Engage, Unearth, As I Lay Dying, The Haunted, Mudvayne, Ill Ni√±o, Static-X, Darkest Hour, God Forbid, The Black Dahlia Murder, Whitechapel and Australian bands such as Psycroptic, Parkway Drive, I Killed the Prom Queen, Double Dragon and Truth Corroded to name a few.
Since the 2010 release of their album 'Tirades' Dyscord have disbanded to focus on other projects.
