[[Madasafish]]

CATEGORIES: Internet service providers of the United Kingdom

Madasafish is an Internet Service Provider (ISP) based in Sheffield and a wholly owned subsidiary of the BT Group.

==History==

The company was created as part of the Brightview Group in April 2001 and bought by BT in July 2007.BT buys Madasafish operator Broadband Finder. 6 July 2007
Totalise (of Totalise plc v Motley Fool Ltd) became part of Madasafish in 2006.Totalise Broadband via broadbandwatchdog.co.uk, accessed 2010 5 31
