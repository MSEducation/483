[[Keeper of the Flame (song)]]

CATEGORIES: 1995 singles, Martin Page songs, Songs written by Martin Page

"Keeper of the Flame" is a single by British musician Martin Page. The single was released in 1995, and is the second single to Page's debut solo album, In the House of Stone and Light. The song peaked at 83 in the Billboard Hot 100, and 19 in Billboard Hot Adult Contemporary. 
The music video features Page, a woman, a drummer, and a boy all in separate rooms in a lantern than turns into a four-room house.
