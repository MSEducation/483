[[ŽNK Radomlje]]

CATEGORIES: Association football clubs established in 2012, Women's football clubs in Slovenia, 2012 establishments in Slovenia

Ženski Nogometni Klub Radomlje ([tpl]lang-en|Women's Football Club Radomlje[/tpl]), commonly referred to as ŽNK Radomlje or simply Radomlje, is a Slovenian women's football club from the city of Radomlje, currently playing in the SŽNL, the top division of Slovenian women's football.
