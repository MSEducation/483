[[Gomeshabad]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Gomeshabad ([tpl]lang-fa|گمش اباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Gomeshābād; also known as Gomīshābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795522|Gomeshabad[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 851, in 189 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
