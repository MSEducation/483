[[Clatsop Butte]]

CATEGORIES: Parks in Portland, Oregon, Geography of Portland, Oregon, Hills of Oregon, Buttes of the United States, Landforms of Multnomah County, Oregon, Protected areas established in 2000

Clatsop Butte is an upland butte lying directly south of Powell Butte in southeast Portland, Oregon, United States.  Clatsop Butte City Park, which occupies part of the butte, is at coordinates [tpl]coord|45.47456|-122.50676|display=inline,title[/tpl] at an elevation of [tpl]convert|571|ft|m[/tpl].[ref]
