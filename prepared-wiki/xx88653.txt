David James Ball (born 3 May 1959, Blackpool, Lancashire)[tpl]Citation  | last = Harrison  | first = Nigel  | title = Songwriters: A Biographical Dictionary with Discographies  | publisher = McFarland  | year = 1998  | isbn = 0-7864-0542-2  | page = 17[/tpl] is an English producer and electronic musician, who has played in bands such as Soft Cell and The Grid, and collaborated with other producers including Ingo Vauk and Chris Braide. He is usually referred to as Dave Ball on record sleeves.
