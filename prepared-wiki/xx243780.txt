[[The Best of Crush 40 – Super Sonic Songs]]

CATEGORIES: 2009 compilation albums, Crush 40 albums

The Best of Crush 40 – Super Sonic Songs is a greatest hits compilation album by Crush 40. The album contains songs from the Sonic the Hedgehog series, from their previous two albums, and some newly recorded songs. A few of the tracks have also been remastered into new mixes.
