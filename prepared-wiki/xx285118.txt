[[Lê Huyền Tông]]

CATEGORIES: 1654 births, 1671 deaths, Lê Dynasty emperors, 17th-century monarchs in Asia

Lê Huyền Tông (chữ Hán: 黎玄宗, 1654 – 1671), birth name Lê Duy Vũ (黎維禑, 黎維禧) was a child king of Vietnam; he reigned from 1662 to 1671 as the 8th figurehead king of the Later Lê dynasty warlord period.Bruce M. Lockhart,William J. Duiker The A to Z of Vietnam 2010 p437 "Lê Huyền Tông"
