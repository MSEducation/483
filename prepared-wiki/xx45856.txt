[[Estaimpuis]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Hainaut (province)

Estaimpuis (in Dutch Steenput) is a Walloon municipality of Belgium located in the province of Hainaut. It consists of the former municipalities Estaimpuis, Bailleul, Estaimbourg, Évregnies, Leers-Nord, Néchin, and Saint-Léger.
