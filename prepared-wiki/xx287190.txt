[[Maicel Uibo]]

CATEGORIES: 1992 births, Living people, Estonian decathletes, People from Põlva

Maicel Uibo (born 27 December 1992 in Põlva) is a Estonian decathlete. He finished 19th at the 2013 World Championships in Moscow, Russia.
