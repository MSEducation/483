[[French Kiss with Kylie]]

CATEGORIES: 2006 albums

French Kiss with Kylie is the debut English, chart-topping album by Romanian dance-pop group Akcent, which was released across Europe on August 23, 2006.  The album went gold and platinum in several countries. 
The album was re-released on July 7, 2007 which included the smash single "King of Disco" and a DVD that featured 4 music videos along with never before seen photos of the band.
