[[Hypocoena basistriga]]

CATEGORIES: Apameini, Moths of Canada

Hypocoena basistriga Poole, R. W. (1989). Lepidopterorum Catalogus (New Series) Fascicle 118, Noctuidae. CRC Press. ISBN 0-916846-45-8, ISBN 978-0-916846-45-9. is a species of moth of the family Noctuidae. It is found from Newfoundland and Labrador west to British Columbia and Yukon.
The wingspan is 25–27 mm. Adults are on wing from mid to late summer. There is one generation per year.
