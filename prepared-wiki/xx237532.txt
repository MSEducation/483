[[Alfred de Moges]]

CATEGORIES: 19th-century French diplomats, 1830 births, 1861 deaths

Alfred, Marquis de Moges (1830-1861 in Menton, Alpes-Maritimes) was a French diplomat of the 19th century. In 1858 he accompanied Charles de Chassiron and Jean-Baptiste Louis Gros to sign the Treaty of Amity and Commerce between France and Japan in Edo on October 9, 1858.Recollections of Baron Gros's embassy to China and Japan in 1857-58 by Moges (Alfred, marquis de)Medzini, p.16
