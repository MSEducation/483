[[Central District (Faridan County)]]

CATEGORIES: Faridan County, Districts of Isfahan Province

The Central District of Faridan County ([tpl]lang-fa|بخش مرکزی شهرستان فریدن[/tpl]) is a district (bakhsh) in Faridan County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 54,036, in 13,549 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|10[/tpl]  The District has two cities: Daran and Damaneh. The District has four rural districts (dehestan): Dalankuh Rural District, Varzaq Rural District, Varzaq-e Jonubi Rural District, and Zayandeh Rud-e Shomali Rural District.
