[[Christos Moustogiannis]]

CATEGORIES: 1983 births, Living people, Greek footballers, Egaleo F.C. players, Acharnaikos F.C. players, Kerkyra FC players, Thrasyvoulos F.C. players, Aiolikos F.C. players

 Christos Moustogiannis  ([tpl]lang-el|Χρήστος Μουτσόγιαννης[/tpl]; born 1 March 1983) is a Greek footballer who plays for Fostiras F.C. in the Football League (Greece), as Midfielder.

==Career==

Moustogiannis previously played for Egaleo in the Greek Super League[tpl]cite web|publisher=Guardian.co.uk|title=Stats Centre: Christos Moustogiannis Facts|url=http://guardian.touch-line.com/StatsCentre.asp?CTID=31&CPID=135&TEID=6391&PLID=180634&pStr=Player|accessdate=2010-02-23[/tpl] and Kerkyra in the Beta Ethniki.
