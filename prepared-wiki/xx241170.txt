[[Aohan Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Aohan Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠠᠤᠬᠠᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Auqan qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=敖汉旗 |t=敖漢旗 |p=Áohàn Qí[/tpl]) is a banner of southeastern Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China, bordering Liaoning province to the south. It is under the administration of Chifeng City, [tpl]convert|85|km[/tpl] to the west.
