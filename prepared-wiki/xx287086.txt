[[Taleh Jerd]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Taleh Jerd ([tpl]lang-fa|تله جرد[/tpl], also Romanized as Taleh Jerd; also known as Tahleh Cherd, Talaird, Talegerd, Taleh Gerd, and Tehla Sard)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3086891|Taleh Jerd[/tpl] is a village in Golabar Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 693, in 155 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
