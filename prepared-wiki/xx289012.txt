[[Oilers Octane]]

CATEGORIES: Edmonton Oilers, National Hockey League Ice Dancers squads

The Oilers Octane is the cheerleading team for the NHL team the Edmonton Oilers. The team was the first Canadian cheerteam in NHL history.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.closetcanuck.com/2010/12/oilers-octane-debut-in-edmonton-as.html |title=Oilers Octane debut as first Canadian NHL cheer team |date=December 15, 2010 |accessdate=August 13, 2013 |publisher=Closet Canuck[/tpl] The debut of the cheerleaders had a mixed reaction from the Edmontonian community, some signing petitions to keep cheerleading out of the sport of hockey in Canada while others supported the team.[tpl]cite web |url=http://sports.ca.msn.com/other/photos/top-cheerleaders-raise-a-cheer?page=1 |date=November 24, 2011 |accessdate=August 13, 2013 |title=Top cheerleaders: Raise a cheer! Oilers Octane |publisher=Fox Sports |author=Ian Harrison[/tpl] Despite this, the team performed for the first time on December 14, 2010 in a home game against the Toronto Maple Leafs.[tpl]cite web |url=http://sportsillustrated.cnn.com/2010/extramustard/hotclicks/12/16/oilers-cheerleader-nate-robinson-falls-while-celebrating/ |title=History Has Been Made |accessdate=August 13, 2013 |date=December 16, 2010 |publisher=Sports Illustrated |author=Jimmy Traina[/tpl][tpl]cite news |url=http://www.lfpress.com/sports/hockey/2010/12/15/16560126.html |author=Andrew Hanon |accessdate=August 13, 2013 |date=December 15, 2010 |title=Oiler cheerleaders heat things up |publisher=London Free Press[/tpl][tpl]cite news |url=http://www.edmontonsun.com/sports/myoilers/2010/12/15/16555356.html |publisher=Edmonton Sun |accessdate=August 13, 2013 |date=December 15, 2010 |title=Oilers unveil cheer team[/tpl] The first team had 19 cheerleaders, dressed in uniforms that resembled the original Edmonton Oilers jerseys.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.thestar.com/sports/hockey/2010/12/15/edmonton_oilers_introduce_cheerleaders.html |date=December 15, 2010 |accessdate=August 13, 2013 |title=Edmonton Oilers introduce cheerleaders |publisher=Toronto Star[/tpl]
