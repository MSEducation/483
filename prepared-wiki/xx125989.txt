[[Andrea Ferrell]]

CATEGORIES: American deaf actresses, Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, Actresses from Utah, People from Salt Lake City, Utah, American film actresses, American television actresses

Andrea Marie Ferrell is an American television and movie actress.  She was born in Salt Lake City, Utah.  She is deaf, and therefore often portrays deaf characters.
