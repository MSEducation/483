[[Čechy pod Kosířem]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Prostějov District, Prostějov District, Villages in the Czech Republic

Čechy pod Kosířem is a village and municipality (obec) in Prostějov District in the Olomouc Region of the Czech Republic. Its name means "Bohemians below the Kosíř". It was first mentioned in 1131.
The municipality covers an area of [tpl]convert|9.18|km2|sqmi[/tpl], and has a population of 1,025 (as at 31 December 2007).
Čechy pod Kosířem lies at the foot of the Velký Kosíř hill (442 m), approximately [tpl]convert|11|km|mi|0[/tpl] north-west of Prostějov, [tpl]convert|17|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] west of Olomouc, and [tpl]convert|197|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] east of Prague.
