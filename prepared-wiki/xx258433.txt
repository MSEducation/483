[[Jungle of the Midwest Sea]]

CATEGORIES: 2007 albums

Jungle of the Midwest Sea is the fourth studio album by the Chicago celtic punk band Flatfoot 56.[tpl]cite web | last = Monger | first = Christopher | url = http://www.allmusic.com/artist/p799814 | title = Flatfoot 56 | work=Allmusic | accessdate = 30 December 2010[/tpl] The album was released on May 15, 2007 by Flicker Records.[tpl]cite web|title= Jungle of the Midwest Sea|url=http://www.allmusic.com/album/r1059450|publisher=Allmusic|accessdate=1 January 2011[/tpl] The album received critical acclaim and the band embarked on several tours after the release.Flatfoot 56 to begin recording the follow-up to the band's 2007 effort Jungle of the Midwest Sea at Bridgeport West  The album was produced by The O.C. Supertones members, Ethan Luck & Daniel Spencer.
