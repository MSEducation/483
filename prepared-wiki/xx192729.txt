[[Gary Ilman]]

CATEGORIES: 1943 births, Living people, American male freestyle swimmers, Former world record holders in swimming, Long Beach State 49ers athletes, Olympic gold medalists for the United States, Olympic medalists in swimming, Olympic swimmers of the United States, Sportspeople from Glendale, California, Swimmers at the 1963 Pan American Games, Swimmers at the 1964 Summer Olympics

Gary Steven Ilman (born August 13, 1943) is an American former competition swimmer, Olympic champion, and former world record-holder.  He competed at the 1964 Summer Olympics in Tokyo, Japan, where he won gold medals in the men's 4x100-meter freestyle relay and men's 4x200-meter freestyle relay."1964 Summer Olympics – Tokyo, Japan – Swimming" – databaseOlympics.com (Retrieved on April 29, 2008)
