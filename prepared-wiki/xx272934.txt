[[Mucho, Mucho]]

CATEGORIES: 1960 albums, Albums produced by Esmond Edwards, Albums recorded at Van Gelder Studio, Prestige Records albums, Shirley Scott albums

Mucho, Mucho is a Latin-oriented jazz album by organist Shirley Scott recorded and released in 1960 on Prestige Records as PRLP 7182.
