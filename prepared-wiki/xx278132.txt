[[2013 Stu Sells Oakville Tankard]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 in Canadian curling, Oakville, Ontario

The 2013 StuSells Oakville Tankard was held from September 5 to 8 in Oakville, Ontario as part of the 2013–14 World Curling Tour. Both the men's and women's events were held in a round robin format. The purse for the men's event was CAD$34,000, of which the winner, Brad Jacobs, received CAD$8,000,[tpl]cite web|url=http://worldcurl.com/events.php?task=Event&eventid=3081&view=Contact|title=2013 StuSells Oakville Tankard – Men's event – Event/Contact information|publisher=World Curling Tour|accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl] while the purse for the women's event was CAD$24,000, of which the winner, Sherry Middaugh, received CAD$5,000.[tpl]cite web|url=http://worldcurl.com/events.php?task=Event&eventid=3082&view=Contact|title=2013 StuSells Oakville Tankard – Women's event – Event/Contact information|publisher=World Curling Tour|accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl]
Jacobs successfully defended his title by defeating Kevin Koe with a score of 4–3, while Middaugh defeated Cathy Auld with a score of 5–4 in an extra end.
