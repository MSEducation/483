[[Diablerets Glacier]]

CATEGORIES: Glaciers of Switzerland

The Diablerets Glacier ([tpl]lang-fr|Glacier des Diablerets[/tpl]) is a glacier situated on the summit of Les Diablerets in the Bernese Alps. It covers an area of approximatively 1 km2.
The Diablerets Glacier is often confused with the nearby much larger and popular Tsanfleuron Glacier.
