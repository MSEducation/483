[[Lucas Landa]]

CATEGORIES: 1986 births, Living people, People from Santa Fe Province, Argentine footballers, Association football defenders, Gimnasia y Esgrima de La Plata footballers, Barcelona Sporting Club footballers, Expatriate footballers in Ecuador, Argentine expatriate footballers

Lucas León Landa (born April 3, 1986 in Chañar Ladeado, Santa Fe Province), is an Argentine football defender playing for Colón in the Argentine Primera División.

==Career==

Landa started his career for Gimnasia y Esgrima de La Plata in 2006. He had a loan spell at Ecuadorian Barcelona SC for the first half of 2010, but returned to Gimnasia in July.[tpl]cite news|title=Lucas Landa regresó al Lobo tras su paso por Ecuador |newspaper=TyC Sports |date=2010-07-08 |url=http://www.tycsports.com/notas/56948-lucas-landa-regres-al-lobo-su-paso-ecuador |language=Spanish |accessdate=2010-07-09 [/tpl]
