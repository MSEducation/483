[[Voganj]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Syrmia, Populated places in Vojvodina

Voganj ([tpl]Lang-sr-Cyrl|Вогањ[/tpl]) is a village in Serbia. It is situated in the Ruma municipality, in the Srem District, Vojvodina province. The village has a Serb ethnic majority and its population numbering 1,614 people (2002 census).

==History==

In 1807, the village of Voganj was the center of the rebellion of the Syrmian peasants, which was known as the Tican's Rebellion.
