[[2006 Macau Grand Prix]]

CATEGORIES: 2006 in Formula Three, Macau Grand Prix, 2006 in Macau

The 2006 Macau Grand Prix Formula Three was the 53rd Macau Grand Prix race held on the streets of Macau on 19 November 2006. It was supported by the 2006 Guia Race of Macau.
