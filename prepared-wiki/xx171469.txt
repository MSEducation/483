[[A Few More Published Studies]]

CATEGORIES: 2006 albums

A Few More Published Studies is the first full length album by the indie rock group The XYZ Affair.
The music video for the album's first single, "All My Friends", was shot in Brooklyn, NY in 2007 featuring former Nickelodeon TV stars Marc Summers (Double Dare - Host), Danny Cooksey (Salute Your Shorts - Budnick), Michael Maronna (The Adventures of Pete & Pete - Big Pete), and Jason Zimbler (Clarissa Explains It All - Ferguson).[tpl]YouTube|IerHOrDQKW0|All My Friends[/tpl]
