[[Philip Vaughan]]

CATEGORIES: Welsh inventors, 18th-century Welsh people

Philip Vaughan was a Welsh inventor and ironmaster who patented the first design for a ball bearing in Carmarthen in 1794.Carlisle, p. 512. His design ran along a track in an axle assembly, known as a ball race, thus originating the modern ball bearing design.Rowland, p. 160.
