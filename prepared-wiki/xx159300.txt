[[Dhanori]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Navsari district

Dhanori is a small village in Navsari district in the state of Gujarat, India. It is about [tpl]convert|40|km|mi[/tpl] north of the city of Valsad and about [tpl]convert|25|km|mi[/tpl] south of the district city of Navsari.
Dhanori is well connected to Surat, Bilimora, Navsari, Gandevi by road, and it is in these places that most of the village population work. Their primary occupation is that of farming.
