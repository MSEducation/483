[[Aq Kand, Qareh Poshtelu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Aq Kand ([tpl]lang-fa|اق كند[/tpl], also Romanized as Āq Kand and Āqkand; also known as  Ak-Kend)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3053458|Aq Kand[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 188, in 50 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
