[[William Jonet]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing, Year of death missing, 14th-century English people, People from Hereford, Members of the Parliament of England (pre-1707)

William Jonet (fl. 1381-1388), of Hereford and 'Hulle' near Clifford, Herefordshire, was an English politician.
He was a Member (MP) of the Parliament of England for Hereford in 1381, February 1388 and September 1388.http://www.historyofparliamentonline.org/volume/1386-1421/member/jonet-william
