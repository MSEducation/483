Enes Ünal (born 10 May 1997) is a Turkish footballer who plays for Bursaspor.http://www.tff.org/Default.aspx?pageId=30&kisiId=980555 He made his Süper Lig debut on 25 August 2013 against Galatasaray.

==Club career==

Promoted from Bursaspor Youth Squad, he made his professional debut against FK Vojvodina in the first match of third qualifying round of 2013–14 UEFA Europa League.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.uefa.com/uefaeuropaleague/season=2014/matches/round=2000467/match=2012381/postmatch/lineups/index.html |title=Voijvodina 2-2 Bursaspor |publisher= UEFA | date=2011-08-01|accessdate=2011-08-11[/tpl] [tpl]tr icon[/tpl]

===Bursaspor===

On August 25, 2013, Enes Ünal came off the bench substituting Ferhat Kiraz in the 71 mintute and scoring his first league goal against Galatasaray in the 74th minute with the assist by Pablo Batalla. This goal made Enes Ünal the youngest player to score in Turkish Super League. On October 21, 2013, Enes Ünal scored his second Super League goal against Kayserispor with the assist by Şener Özbayraklı. On December 19, 2013, Enes Ünal came off the bench substituting Musa Çağıran in the 80th minute in the Turkish Cup game against İnegölspor and scoring a fabulous freekick goal in the 82nd minute making the second goal of the game for Bursaspor. In the 86th minute Enes Ünal made his second goal of the game and scoring the third goal for Bursapor against İnegölspor. Bursaspor wins the game 3-0 and advances to the Turkish Cup 2013-2014 group stages. On February 12, 2014, Enes Ünal scored the 3rd goal for Bursaspor against Akhisar Belediyespor in the Turkish Cup 2013-2014 group stages. Bursaspor finished first place in the Turkish Cup 2013-2014 group stage with 13 points and advances to semifinals vs. Galatasaray.

==International career==

After scoring a spectacular 25 goals in 24 games for the Turkey U16 side, he was quickly promoted to the U17s, and after scoring his first professional goal in 2013 was invited to join the Turkey U21 side, despite being just 16 years old. He scored his first U21 goal in 4-0 win against Malta.[ref]http://www.uefa.com/under21/season=2015/matches/round=2000412/match=2011512/postmatch/report/index.html
