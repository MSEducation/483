[[Tara Chandra Chaudhari]]

CATEGORIES: Members of the Nepalese Constituent Assembly, Living people, Nepal Loktantrik Samajbadi Dal politicians

Tara Chandra Chaudhari is a Nepalese politician, belonging to the Nepal Loktantrik Samajbadi Dal. After the 2008 Constituent Assembly election, in which NLSD won one Proportional Representation seat, Chaudhari was selected to represent the party in the Constituent Assembly. Chaudhari is a historian by profession.Nepalnews.com, news from Nepal as it happens
