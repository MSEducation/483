[[Brayan López]]

CATEGORIES: 1987 births, Colombian footballers, Independiente Medellín footballers, Atlético Bucaramanga footballers, Living people, Association football goalkeepers

Brayan Styven López Yepes (born June 25, 1987 in Medellín) is a Colombian footballer. He currently plays as a goalkeeper for Independiente Medellín in the Copa Mustang. He is agile and fast, with good one-on-one skills.
