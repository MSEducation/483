[[Bob McAneeley]]

CATEGORIES: 1950 births, Living people, Canadian ice hockey left wingers, Edmonton Oilers (WHA) players, Ice hockey people from British Columbia, People from Cranbrook, British Columbia

Robert William McAneeley (born November 7, 1950 in Cranbrook, British Columbia) is a former World Hockey Association player. He played 174 games for the Edmonton Oilers.
