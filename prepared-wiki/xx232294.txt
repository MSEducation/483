[[Javier Hernán García]]

CATEGORIES: Sportspeople from Buenos Aires, Argentine footballers, Primera División Argentina players, Boca Juniors footballers, Club Atlético Tigre footballers, Association football goalkeepers, Living people, 1987 births, Argentina international footballers

Javier Hernán García (born January 29, 1987, in Buenos Aires), is an Argentine football Goalkeeper who plays for Tigre in the Argentine Primera División.

==Club career==

García started his professional career playing for Boca Juniors in the Argentine Primera División. He made his debut on August 24, 2008 in a 2–1 victory against Lanús.[tpl]cite web|url=http://edant.ole.com.ar/notas/2008/08/25/futbollocal/01745515.html|title=Podría haber sido palo y afuera, ¿no?|date=25 August 2008|publisher=Diario Olé|language=Spanish|accessdate=1 May 2010[/tpl]
On July 2011, García was loaned out to Tigre for 1 year.[tpl]cite web|title=García es Matador|url=http://www.ole.com.ar/tigre/Garcia-Matador_0_515348516.html|publisher=Diario Olé|accessdate=11 July 2011[/tpl]

==International career==

In 2007 García was part of the Argentina U-20 squad that won the FIFA U-20 World Cup, but he did not feature in any of the matches.
