[[List of universities in Karachi]]

CATEGORIES: Universities in Karachi, Karachi-related lists, Lists of universities and colleges in Sindh

There are a number of higher education institutions in Karachi, Sindh, Pakistan. These include a number of universities supported by the federal government of Pakistan and the provincial government of Sindh. There are also several private universities supported by various bodies and societies. The Higher Education Commission of Pakistan maintains a list of recognized universities.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.hec.gov.pk/ourinstitutes/pages/default.aspx |title=HEC recognized Universities |publisher=Higher Education Commission of Pakistan |accessdate=27 January 2014[/tpl]
