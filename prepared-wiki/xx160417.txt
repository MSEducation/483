[[HMS C22]]

CATEGORIES: Barrow-built ships, British C-class submarines, Royal Navy ship names, 1908 ships

HMS ''C22'' was a British C class submarine built by Vickers, Barrow. She was laid down on 4 February 1908 and was commissioned on 5 May 1909.
C22 was sold on 2 February 1920.
