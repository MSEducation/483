[[St. John's Church Complex (Delhi, New York)]]

CATEGORIES: Episcopal churches in New York, Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, National Register of Historic Places in Delaware County, New York, Religious buildings completed in 1831, 19th-century Episcopal church buildings, Churches in Delaware County, New York, Romanesque Revival architecture in New York

St. John's Church Complex is a historic  Episcopal church complex at 136 Main Street in Delhi, Delaware County, New York. The complex consists of the church / chapel, parish house, rectory, rectory garage, connecting stairway, and site of the 1831 church. The centerpiece is the 1887-1888 Richardsonian Romanesque style Sheldon Memorial Chapel.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=1698|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: St. John's Church Complex|date=April 1995|accessdate=2010-02-05 |author=Mark Peckham|publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation[/tpl] See also: [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=1697|title=Accompanying 13 photos[/tpl]
It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1995.
