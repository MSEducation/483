[[Paul Hutcheon]]

CATEGORIES: Scottish journalists

Paul Hutcheon is a Scottish political journalist who currently serves as Investigations Editor for the Sunday Herald.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.heraldscotland.com/paul-hutcheon|title=Paul Hutcheon|accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl] In 2006, while serving as political editor for the Sunday Herald, he won both Journalist of the Year and Political Journalist of the Year at the Scottish Press Awards.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.pressgazette.co.uk/node/33881|title=Hutcheon leads winners at Scottish Press Awards|date=24 April 2006|accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl] He moved to the position of Investigations Editor in 2008.
