[[2013 PTT Thailand Open]]

CATEGORIES: PTT Thailand Open, 2013 ATP World Tour

The 2013 PTT Thailand Open was a tennis tournament played on indoor hard courts. It was the 11th edition of the Thailand Open, and part of the ATP World Tour 250 Series of the 2013 ATP World Tour. It took place at the Impact Arena in Bangkok, Thailand, from September 21 through September 29, 2013.
