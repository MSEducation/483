[[Farm to Market Road 464]]

CATEGORIES: Farm to Market Roads in Texas, Transportation in Guadalupe County, Texas

Farm to Market Road 464 (FM 464) is a [tpl]convert|1.770|mi|km|abbr=on[/tpl] farm-to-market road in Seguin, Texas.

==Route description==

The western end of FM 464 is at a dead end near the banks of the Guadalupe River, at the community of Nolte. The route travels east along the Seguin city limits, intersecting [tpl]roadlink|state=TX|FM|1620[/tpl] before crossing Interstate 10 at Exit #605. FM 464 ends at an intersection with US 90 and US 90 Alternate.[tpl]Texas Mapbook|434|accessdate=2011-01-06[/tpl]

==History==

FM 464 was designated along its current routing in 1945.
