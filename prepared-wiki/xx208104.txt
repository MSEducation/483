[[Peder Paulsen Anzjøn]]

Peder Paulsen Anzjøn (born 1806) was a Norwegian politician.
He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament in 1842, representing the rural constituency of Søndre Trondhjems Amt (today named Sør-Trøndelag). He worked as a farmer and bailiff.Peder Paulsen Anzjøn — Norwegian Social Science Data Services (NSD) He sat through one term.
