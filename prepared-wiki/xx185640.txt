[[Gmina Kietrz]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Opole Voivodeship, Głubczyce County

Gmina Kietrz is an urban-rural gmina (administrative district) in Głubczyce County, Opole Voivodeship, in south-western Poland, on the Czech border. Its seat is the town of Kietrz, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|19|km|mi|0[/tpl] south-east of Głubczyce and [tpl]convert|66|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south of the regional capital Opole.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|139.93|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 11,838 (of which the population of Kietrz is 6,365, and the population of the rural part of the gmina is 5,473).

==Location==

Gmina Kietrz is bordered by the gminas of Baborów, Branice, Głubczyce and Pietrowice Wielkie. It also borders the Czech Republic.
