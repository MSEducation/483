[[Bolquère]]

CATEGORIES: Communes of Pyrénées-Orientales

Bolquère ([tpl]lang-ca|Bolquera[/tpl]) is a commune in the Pyrénées-Orientales department in southern France.

==Population==

==Transportation==

Bolquère railway station is the highest SNCF station in France at an elevation of 1593 metres. It serves the Yellow Train exclusively.
