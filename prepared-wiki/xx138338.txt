[[Yoshidaguchi Station]]

CATEGORIES: Geibi Line, Railway stations in Hiroshima Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Yoshidaguchi Station|吉田口駅|Yoshidaguchi-eki[/tpl] is a JR West Geibi Line station located in Shimokobara, Kōta-chō, Akitakata, Hiroshima Prefecture, Japan. The station name is derived from being the "entrance" to the former Yoshida-chō.

==History==

==Station building and platforms==

Yoshidaguchi Station features one raised, island-style platform, capable of handling two lines simultaneously. The station is a multi-purpose meeting building named "Pratt House."
