[[Ideal Standard]]

CATEGORIES: Bathroom fixture companies, Private equity portfolio companies

Ideal Standard International is a privately held multinational plumbing fixture company based in Brussels, Belgium. Principally owned by Bain Capital Partners since 2007 for 1.75b USD.[tpl]cite web|last=Haffenden |first=Chris |url=http://www.ft.com/cms/s/2/174dabc0-1558-11de-b9a9-0000779fd2ac.html#axzz29IeIFsIW |title=Ideal Standard sponsor Bain hires financial adviser as 2007 LBO falters |publisher=FT.com |date=2009-03-20 |accessdate=2012-10-16[/tpl] It operates primarily in Europe, Middle East and North Africa. In North America, the formerly affiliated American Standard Brands still operates.
Ideal Standard International is a leading provider of innovative and design-driven bathroom solutions, producing sanitaryware and baths, as well as brass fittings, shower enclosures, valves and kits, bathroom furniture and vanity units.
With its long experience and history in bathroom design, Ideal Standard continues to invest in advanced technology to ensure that their innovative products work perfectly as well as look beautiful.
Ideal Standard has a long standing history of collaborations with top designers and architects to create bathrooms that span across top end, middle and mass markets 

==History==

The company was originally founded as the National Radiator Company in Kingston upon Hull in 1936.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.british-history.ac.uk/report.aspx?compid=66776 |title=Modern Hull | A History of the County of York East Riding: Volume 1 (pp. 215-286) |publisher=British-history.ac.uk |date= |accessdate=2012-10-16[/tpl]
