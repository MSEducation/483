[[Gowrie railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Premium Melbourne railway stations, Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1928

Gowrie railway station is located on the Upfield line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the northern Melbourne suburb of Fawkner opening on 16 October 1928 as Rail Motor Stopping Place 21. It closed on 5 May 1956, reopening on 17 May 1965 as Gowrie.Gowrie Vicsig It was upgraded to a Premium station in 1998.[tpl]cite journal|date=October 1997|title=Upgrading Eltham to a Premium Station|journal=Newsrail|publisher=Australian Railway Historical Society|page=page 310[/tpl]
North of the station, the double track becomes single for the journey to Upfield.
