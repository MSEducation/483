[[James Maina Boi]]

CATEGORIES: 1954 births, 2004 deaths, Athletes (track and field) at the 1982 Commonwealth Games, Kenyan middle-distance runners, Commonwealth Games silver medallists for Kenya

James Maina Boi (4 April 1954 â€“ 15 July 2004) was a Kenyan middle distance runner. He is best known for winning the 800 metres distance at the 1978 All-Africa Games and a silver medal at the 1982 Commonwealth Games.
He also competed in 4 x 400 metres relay at the 1983 World Championships with the Kenyan team that was eliminated in the semi finals.
Maina Boi died in 2004 following long-term illness. IAAF Newsletter

==Achievements==

Maina also won two gold medals at the East African Championships in 1977 and 1979.
