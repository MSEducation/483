[[Abdullahpur Mori]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Ghaziabad district, India

Abdullahpur Mori is a village in the Ghaziabad district of Uttar Pradesh, India.[tpl]Cite web |url=http://ghaziabad.nic.in/dso/Home%20Page/Home%20Page%20Files/I.V.R.S.%20CODE.pdf |title=I.V.R.S. CODE NUMBERS June 2007 |publisher=National Informatics Centre of India [/tpl] The village has a small population. The nearest town is Gulaothi, and nearby villages include Kurana, Akbarpur, Hirdaypur, and Murshadpur.
