[[Isabelle Carbonell]]

CATEGORIES: American documentary filmmakers, Living people, University of Michigan alumni, People from Washington, D.C., Year of birth missing (living people), Place of birth missing (living people), Documentary photographers

Isabelle Carbonell is an American documentary photographer and filmmaker based out of Washington D.C.  She is trilingual in English, French, and Spanish.[tpl]Citation needed|date=May 2010[/tpl] She graduated from the Residential College at the University of Michigan with degrees in Environmental and Social Science, Photography and Filmmaking.[tpl]Citation needed|date=May 2010[/tpl] Her documentary work has taken her to countries such as Cuba, Mexico, Vietnam, the Dominican Republic, India, Qatar, Uruguay, and Argentina.
