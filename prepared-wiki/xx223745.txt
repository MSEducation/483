[[McFarlane Strait]]

CATEGORIES: Bodies of water of Greenwich Island, Bodies of water of Livingston Island, Straits of the South Shetland Islands

McFarlane Strait is a strait lying between Greenwich Island and Livingston Island, in the South Shetland Islands off Antarctica. It is [tpl]Convert|24|km|nmi[/tpl] long and [tpl]Convert|3|km|nmi[/tpl] wide. The name appears on an 1822 chart by Captain George Powell, a British sealer, and is now well established in international usage.

==Location==

The strait is centred at ([tpl]Coord|62|32|S|59|55|W|source:GNIS|display=inline,title[/tpl]), and entered between Williams Point and Duff Point to the northwest and between Renier Point and Ephraim Bluff to the southeastMcFarlane Strait.
