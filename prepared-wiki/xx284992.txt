[[Kata Kata Kosong (TV game show)]]

CATEGORIES: Indonesian television series

Kata Kata Kosong is a television game show Indonesia adopted from Match Game under license from the FremantleMedia. The show runs from July 6, 2013, Saturday - Sunday 20:00 pm at Shine TV. Starting on August 24, 2013, the show was renamed to "Apa Benar Kata Gue?" (en: What I Really Said?).
