[[List of Speakers of the National Assembly of Hungary]]

CATEGORIES: Politics of Hungary, Speakers of the National Assembly of Hungary, Lists of legislative speakers

The Speaker of the National Assembly of Hungary is the presiding officer (or speaker) of the Hungarian National Assembly. The current Speaker is László Kövér.
Since Hungary has no vice president, the Speaker becomes acting President whenever the President resigns, dies or is unable to discharge his duties.

==Speakers of the National Assembly of Hungary==

In 1927 the National Assembly of Hungary became bicameral.
