[[1997 Toshiba Classic]]

CATEGORIES: 1997 WTA Tour, Southern California Open

The 1997 Toshiba Classic was a tennis tournament played on outdoor hard courts at the La Costa Resort and Spa in San Diego, California in the United States that was part of Tier II of the 1997 WTA Tour. The tournament was held from July 28 through August 3, 1997.
