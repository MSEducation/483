[[Majority minority]]

CATEGORIES: Demographics of the United States

A majority-minority or minority-majority area refers to a sub-national jurisdiction in which one or more racial and/or ethnic minorities (relative to the whole country's population) make up a majority of the population. This term was used in this context from at least 1978, if not earlier.http://news.google.com/newspapers?id=_w5ZAAAAIBAJ&sjid=Mm0DAAAAIBAJ&pg=2251,4222974&dq=majority-minority&hl=en
