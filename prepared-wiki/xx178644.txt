[[Batha, Lebanon]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Lebanon

Batha ([tpl]lang-ar|بطحا[/tpl]) is a village of Keserwan District in Mount Lebanon, situated on the east of Jounieh Bay with a special coastal view of Mediterranean sea, at an altitude from 500 to 600 meters. A crystal water spring flow in a wonderful pine and oak valley on the north of Batha watering a fruitful gardens before going down to the Mediterranean sea. Batha is the origine of Painter Michel Elmir (1930-1973).
