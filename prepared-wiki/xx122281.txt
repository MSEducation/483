[[Nemunėlis]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of Lithuania, Rivers of Latvia, International rivers of Europe, Latvia–Lithuania border, Lielupe river basin

Nemunėlis ([tpl]Lang-lv|Mēmele[/tpl]) is a river in northern Lithuania and southern Latvia. It originates 6 km south of Rokiškis. It is 191 kilometres long (75 km in Lithuania, 76 km on the Latvia–Lithuania border and 40 km in Latvia) before its confluence with the Mūša, near Bauska, forming the Lielupe.

The European Union gave 100,000 euros to establish tourist routes along the Nemunėlis.
