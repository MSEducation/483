[[Joseph Hallock House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Houses completed in 1855, Greek Revival architecture in New York, Italianate architecture in New York, Houses in Greene County, New York

The Joseph Hallock House is located at 241 West Main Street, Catskill, New York. The structure is architecturally significant as a highly intact representation of the mid-nineteenth-century architecture of the town.  Its style is transitional, with elements of both Greek Revival and Italianate styles.  The house was built circa 1855 and was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1995.[tpl]cite web|last=Bonafide|first=John A.|title=National Register of Historic Places Inventory/Nomination:Joseph Hallock House|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=2614|date=April 1995|accessdate=2008-04-29[/tpl] and Accompanying 5 photos, exterior and interior, from 1995
