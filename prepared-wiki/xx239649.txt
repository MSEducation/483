[[Illinois Fighting Illini women's basketball]]

CATEGORIES: Illinois Fighting Illini women's basketball, College women's basketball teams in the United States

The Illinois Fighting Illini women's basketball team is an NCAA Division I college basketball team competing in the Big Ten Conference. Home games are played at Assembly Hall, located on University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign's campus in Champaign.
