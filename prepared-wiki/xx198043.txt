[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Caetité]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1913, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Vitória da Conquista, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Caetité ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Caëtitensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Caetité in the Ecclesiastical province of Vitória da Conquista in Brazil.
