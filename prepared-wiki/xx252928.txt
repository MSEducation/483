[[Ryōsuke Hashiguchi]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Japanese film directors, LGBT people from Japan, People from Nagasaki Prefecture, LGBT directors

[tpl]Nihongo|Ryōsuke Hashiguchi|橋口亮輔|Hashiguchi Ryōsuke| 13 July 1962[/tpl] is a Japanese film director particularly known for projects concerning LGBT community issues. He won the award for Best Director at the 24th Yokohama Film Festival for Hush![tpl]Cite web |url=http://homepage3.nifty.com/yokohama-eigasai/24-2002/24_2002_shou.html |title=第24回ヨコハマ映画祭 日本映画個人賞|accessdate= 2010-01-10|language= Japanese |publisher= Yokohama Film Festival[/tpl] and at the 33rd Hochi Film Award for Gururi no koto.[tpl]Cite web |url=http://cinemahochi.yomiuri.co.jp/h_award/2008/ |title= 報知映画賞ヒストリー |accessdate= 2010-01-10|language= Japanese|publisher= Cinema Hochi[/tpl]
