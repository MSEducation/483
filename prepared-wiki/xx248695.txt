[[Rung Paisarn RPS-001]]

CATEGORIES: 5.56 mm firearms, Assault rifles, Trial and research firearms, Weapons of Thailand

The RPS-001 is an assault rifle manufactured by Rung Paisarn Heavy Industries of Thailand. It is an adaptation of the Czechoslovak Vz.58 with components from the M16A2. It was introduced during the Advanced Combat Rifle trials.
