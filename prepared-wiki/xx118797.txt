[[Gibbonsdown]]

CATEGORIES: Barry, Vale of Glamorgan

Gibbonsdown, colloquially known as 'Gibbo', is a housing estate situated in the north east area of Barry in the Vale of Glamorgan, Wales. Gibbonsdown borders Merthyr Dyfan to the northwest and Cadoxton to the southeast.

==Crime==

In 2009, Gibbonsdown was the location of the fatal stabbing of Paul Jones.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.barryanddistrictnews.co.uk/news/latestnews/4555068.Barry_man_denies_murder_charge/|title=Barry man denies murder charge|publisher=Barry and District News|date=August 20, 2009|accessdate=June 7, 2010[/tpl] John Chivers was arrested that night and later sentenced to a minimum of 18 years in prison,[tpl]cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/wales/8562041.stm|title=John Chivers' life term for pal's Barry stabbing |date=11 March 2010|accessdate=16 July 2013|publisher=bbc.co.uk[/tpl] After the murder, Gibbonsdown Residents' Board chairwoman Molly Conway made complaints about the police for a lack of manpower and patrols on an estate well known as having problems with crime and anti-social behaviour.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.walesonline.co.uk/news/cardiff-news/2009/08/08/murder-accused-is-set-to-deny-charge-91466-24347273/|title=Murder Accused is Set to Deny Charge|publisher=Wales Online|date=August 8, 2009|accessdate=June 7, 2010[/tpl]

==Landmarks==

Gibbonsdown is home to the Holm View Community Leisure Centre, the Gibbonsdown Children Centre, and the Oakfield Primary School (known as the Gibbonsdown Primary School before its reconstruction). It also has the Ysgol Maes Dyfan, a primary school with 96 pupils as of 2005, teaching special needs children.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.estyn.gov.uk/inspection_reports/YsgolMaesDyfanEnglishReport.pdf|title=Ysgol Maes Dyfan English Report|publisher=Estyn|accessdate=June 7, 2010[/tpl] It is also home to Pencoedtre park and the Master Mariner pub.

==Transport==

Notable roads include Treharne Road (known to locals as "The Treharne"), Caradoc Avenue, Robins Lane, and Skomer Road, which connects the area to Wenvoe.
