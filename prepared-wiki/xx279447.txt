[[Brasschaat Airfield]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in Belgium, Antwerp (province)

Brasschaat Airfield ([tpl]lang-nl|Vliegveld Brasschaat[/tpl], [tpl]Airport codes||EBBT|p=n[/tpl]) is an airfield located [tpl]convert|4|km|0[/tpl] north of Brasschaat, Antwerp, Belgium. Formerly the base of the Belgian Army Flying Service, it is today home to recreational flying, including gliders and microlights.
