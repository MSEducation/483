[[Pitze]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of Tyrol (state), Tributaries of the Inn (river)

 
The Pitze (also: Pitzbach) is a right tributary of the river Inn in the Imst (district) and flows through the Pitztal valley. It has a length of about [tpl]convert|39|km[/tpl]. It merges with the Inn [tpl]convert|3|km[/tpl] east of the city of Imst at an elevation of [tpl]convert|700|m[/tpl].
