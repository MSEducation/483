[[Tengushevsky District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Mordovia

Tengushevsky District ([tpl]lang-ru|Теньгу́шевский райо́н[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-mdf|Теньгжелень район[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-myv|Теньгушбуе[/tpl]) is an administrativeConstitution of the Republic of Mordovia and municipalLaw #123-Z district (raion), one of the twenty-two in the Republic of Mordovia, Russia. It is located in the northwest of the republic. The area of the district is [tpl]convert|845.94|km2|sp=us[/tpl].[tpl]citation needed|date=October 2012[/tpl] Its administrative center is the rural locality (a selo) of Tengushevo.Law #7-Z Population: [tpl]ru-census2010|12,340|;[/tpl] 14,286 (2002 Census);[tpl]ru-pop-ref|2002Census[/tpl] [tpl]su-census1989|15,897|.[/tpl] The population of Tengushevo accounts for 34.3% of the district's total population.
