[[Pat Hodgson]]

CATEGORIES: 1944 births, Living people, People from Columbus, Georgia, American football wide receivers, Georgia Bulldogs football players, Washington Redskins players, Pittsburgh Steelers coaches

Patrick Shannon Hodgson (born January 30, 1944 in Columbus, Georgia) is a former American football end in the National Football League for the Washington Redskins.  He played college football at the University of Georgia. After his career, he would serve as a WR and TE coach for multiple NFL teams.
