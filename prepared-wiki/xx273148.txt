[[The Crusader (1932 film)]]

CATEGORIES: 1932 films, 1930s drama films, American films, American drama films, Black-and-white films, Films directed by Frank R. Strayer

The Crusader is a 1932 American drama film directed by Frank R. Strayer and starring Evelyn Brent.[tpl]cite web |url=http://movies.nytimes.com/movie/88300/The-Crusader/overview |title=The Crusader |accessdate=2011-09-25 |work=NY Times[/tpl]

==Plot==

A pushy newspaper reporter Eddie Crane (Ned Sparks) schemes to get rid of crusading District Attorney Phillip Brandon (H. B. Warner). Complicating matters is the sordid past of Brandon's wife Tess (Evelyn Brent) as well as his sister Marcia's affair with a gangster.
