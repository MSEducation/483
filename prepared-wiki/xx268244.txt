[[Juliet Lundholm]]

CATEGORIES: Alumni of the Guildford School of Acting, English stage actresses, English film actresses, Living people

Juliet Lundholm is a professional actress.

==Career==

Juliet Lundholm is a classically trained Anglo-Swedish actress based in the UK. She studied at Middlesex University, where she has also taught, and at the Guildford School of Acting.
Her agent is Robert Kelly Associates.
