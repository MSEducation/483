[[The Banner of Freedom]]

CATEGORIES: National anthems, Samoan music, National symbols of Samoa

The Banner of Freedom ([tpl]lang-sm|O Le Fu'a o Le Sa'olotoga o Samoa[/tpl]) is the national anthem of Samoa.  Both the words (which honour the country's flag) and the music were composed by Sauni Iiga Kuresa.  Samoa adopted The Banner of Freedom as its national anthem upon gaining its independence from New Zealand in 1962.
