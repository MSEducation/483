[[WLBT Tower]]

CATEGORIES: Towers completed in 1966, Towers in Mississippi, Radio masts and towers, Former radio masts and towers

The WLBT Tower was a [tpl]convert|610|m|ft[/tpl]-high guy-wired aerial mast for the transmission of FM radio and TV-programs from Raymond, Mississippi, United States for the Jackson metro area.  The mast, constructed in 1966. On October 23, 1997 the mast, which was property of Cosmos Broadcasting (WLBT a local NBC affiliate) collapsed during the renovation of its guy wires.  Three workers were killed.  The tower has since been rebuilt.[tpl]when|date=March 2013[/tpl]
