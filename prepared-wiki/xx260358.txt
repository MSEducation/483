[[Biawak]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Sarawak

Biawak (also known as Kampung Biawak) is a settlement in Sarawak, Malaysia. It lies approximately [tpl]convert|70.6|km|mi|0[/tpl] west of the state capital Kuching, very close to the border with Indonesian Kalimantan. 
The name biawak is the Malay word for monitor lizard.
