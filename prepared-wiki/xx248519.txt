[[The Party Ain't Over Yet (song)]]

CATEGORIES: Status Quo (band) songs, Patty Loveless songs, 1997 songs, 2005 singles

"The Party Ain't Over Yet" is a song written by John David and recorded by country singer Patty Loveless for her 1997 album, Long Stretch of Lonesome. The song was later covered and released as a single by the British Rock band Status Quo in 2005.
