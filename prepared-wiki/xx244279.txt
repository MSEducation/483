[[Pushpavalli]]

CATEGORIES: Actresses in Tamil cinema, Actresses in Telugu cinema, Indian film actresses, 20th-century Indian actresses

Pentapadu Pushpavalli better known as Pushpavalli is a Telugu and Tamil   film actress.http://www.ghantasala.info/tfs/cdata1028.html She was the first romantic interest of Gemini Ganesan. She had two daughters with him, of which the eldest was Rekha.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.hindu.com/cp/2008/07/25/stories/2008072550391600.htm |title=Miss Malini 1947 |publisher=The Hindu |date=2008-07-25 |accessdate=2009-10-20[/tpl] She starred in many films with Ganesan, mostly as his heroine.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.screenindia.com/old/20001215/renews.htm|title=Gemini Ganesan celebrates 80th birthday|date=December 15, 2000|publisher=Screen India|accessdate=2009-10-20[/tpl] Her film work includes title roles in Miss Malini (1947), an adaptation of R.K. Narayan's novel Mr. Sampath, and the Telugu film Satyabhama.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.screenindia.com/old/print.php?content_id=3722|title=Trendsetter - YV Rao|date=October 21, 2009|publisher=Screen India|accessdate=2009-10-20[/tpl] Pushpavalli died in 1991.
