[[Kamayut Township]]

CATEGORIES: Townships of Yangon

Kamayut Township ([tpl]lang-my|ကမာရွတ် မြို့နယ်[/tpl], [tpl]IPA-my|kəmàjʊʔ mjo̰nɛ̀|pron[/tpl]; also spelt Kamaryut Township) is located in the north central part of Yangon. The township comprises ten wards, and shares borders with Hlaing township in the north, Hlaing township and Kyimyindaing township in the west, the Inya Lake, Bahan township and Mayangon township in the east, and Sanchaung township in the south. The name Kamayut roughly means Khmer Region, which may be an indicator of the possibility of the existence of a Mon settlement earlier.[tpl]citation needed|date=November 2010[/tpl]
One of the most prosperous areas in Yangon (an prime upmarket area),[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.mmtimes.com/feature/Construction/con02.htm|title=Suburbs for the status conscious|author=Zaw Win Than|date=3 September 2007|work=Myanmar Times|accessdate=5 March 2012[/tpl] Kamayut is also the "college town" of the Yangon. Pyay Road which cuts across the township is lined with many education and media related institutions such as Myanmar Radio and Television headquarters. Yangon University, the University of Medicine 1, Yangon, the Yangon Institute of Economics, the Yangon Institute of Education and affiliated TTC and University of Distance Education, Yangon are all located in the township. The township has twelve primary schools, two middle schools and five high schools.

==Economy==

Sinhmalike Market, formerly Kunchan Market, built in 1962, is the township's primary market. In 2010, the Yangon administration announced it would upgrade the market by building an eight-storey [tpl]convert|69720|ft2|m2[/tpl] shopping center.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.mmtimes.com/2010/news/506/n50606.html|title=YCDC to renovate Sinhmalike Market|author=Myo Myo|date=18 January 2010|work=Myanmar Times|accessdate=5 March 2012[/tpl]
Kamayut Township is also home to Junction Square Centre, a 4-storey [tpl]convert|300000|ft2|m2[/tpl] shopping centre on the corner of Pyay and Kyuntaw Roads and opened in March 2012.[tpl]cite news|url=http://mmtimes.com/2012/business/617/biz61704.html|title=New retail outlet coming on March 16|author=Htar Htar Khin|date=5 March 2012|work=Myanmar Times|accessdate=5 March 2012[/tpl] The Blazon Department Store is also located in the township.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.mmtimes.com/feature/Shopping/sh012.htm|title=High-end market brings work home|author=Htar Htar Khin|date=17 September 2007|work=Myanmar Times|accessdate=5 March 2012[/tpl]
The M&A Office Tower, a 17-storey office building, is expected to be completed in 2013.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.mmtimes.com/2012/business/610/biz61008.html|title=Energy firm plans Pyay Road office tower|author=Htar Htar Khin|date=16 January 2012|work=Myanmar Times|accessdate=5 March 2012[/tpl]
