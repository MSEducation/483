[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Nnewi]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic Church in Nigeria, Roman Catholic dioceses in Nigeria, Religious organizations established in 2001, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 21st century, 2001 establishments in Nigeria

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Nnewi ([tpl]lang-la|Nnevien(sis)[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Nnewi in the Ecclesiastical province of Onitsha in Nigeria.
