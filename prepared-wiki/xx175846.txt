[[WSGP]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Kentucky

WSGP is a Christian radio station licensed to Somerset, Kentucky, broadcasting on 88.3 MHz FM.  The station is owned by Somerset Educational Broadcasting Foundation.WSGP fcc.gov. Accessed August 9, 2012
WSGP's programming includes Christian talk and teaching shows such as Back to the Bible, Focus on the Family, Love Worth Finding with Adrian Rogers, In Touch with Charles Stanley, Tony Evans, and Unshackled!.  WSGP also airs a variety of Christian music.King of Kings Radio Programming www.kingofkingsradio.com. Accessed August 21, 2012
