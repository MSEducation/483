[[Takayama, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Takayama|高山村|Takayama-mura[/tpl] is a village located in Kamitakai District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the village has an estimated population of 7,756 and a density of 78.74 persons per km². The total area is 98.50 km².
