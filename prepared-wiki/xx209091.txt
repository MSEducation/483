[[Holden Township, Adams County, North Dakota]]

CATEGORIES: Defunct townships in North Dakota, Defunct townships in Adams County, North Dakota

Holden Township is a defunct civil township in Adams County, North Dakota, USA. The 1990 census recorded a population of 44.[tpl]cite web | url = http://factfinder.census.gov/servlet/DTTable?_bm=y&-context=dt&-ds_name=DEC_1990_STF1_&-mt_name=DEC_1990_STF1_P001&-CONTEXT=dt&-tree_id=100&-all_geo_types=N&-currentselections=DEC_1990_STF1_P001&-geo_id=06000US38001560&-search_results=01000US&-format=&-_lang=en | title = P001. PERSONS - Universe: Persons, Census 1990 Summary Tape File 1 | work= American Factfinder.  | publisher = United States Census Bureau | date = 2000 | accessdate = 2008-07-20[/tpl]
The township dissolved after the 1990 Census,[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.census.gov/prod/cen2000/phc-3-36.pdf | title =  2000 Census of Population and Housing: North Dakota | format = PDF | publisher = United States Census Bureau | date = 2003 | pages = III-3 | accessdate = 2008-07-20[/tpl] and is now designated by the United States Census Bureau as Holden Unorganized Territory. The 2000 census population of the area was 32.[tpl]cite American Factfinder3|UT|3800138420|general|Holden Unorganized Territory[/tpl]
