[[Zhenglan (Xulun Hoh) Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Zhenglan (Xulun Hoh) Banner, Inner Mongolia, also known as Plain Blue Banner or Shuluun Huh Banner, (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠰᠢᠯᠤᠭᠤᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠥᠬᠡ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Siluɣun Köke qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=正蓝旗|t=正藍旗|p=Zhènglán Qí[/tpl]) is a banner of Inner Mongolia, China. It is under the administration of Xilin Gol League. Xanadu was located here.
