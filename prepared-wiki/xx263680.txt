[[Bochner–Martinelli formula]]

CATEGORIES: Complex analysis, Several complex variables

In mathematics, the Bochner–Martinelli formula is a generalization of the Cauchy integral formula to functions of several complex variables, introduced by [tpl]harvs|txt|first=Enzo|last=Martinelli| authorlink=Enzo Martinelli|year=1938[/tpl] and [tpl]harvs|txt|first=Salomon|last=Bochner|authorlink=Salomon Bochner|year=1943[/tpl].
