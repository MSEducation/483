[[John Robertson Aikman]]

CATEGORIES: British curlers, Olympic curlers of Great Britain, Curlers at the 1924 Winter Olympics, Olympic gold medalists for Great Britain, Olympic medalists in curling

John Robertson Aikman was a British curler. John Robertson Aikman participated in the 1924 Winter Olympics edition held in Chamonix (France), and managed to win a gold medal in the British team with fellow Laurence Jackson, Robin Welsh, William K. Jackson, Thomas Murray, John McLeod, William Brown and D. G. Astley.Sweden and France at the 1924 Winter Olympics won the Silver and Bronze medals respectively.
