[[Helvetia (spider)]]

CATEGORIES: Salticidae

Helvetia is a Neotropical genus of the spider family Salticidae (jumping spiders). The genus name is derived from Helvetia.

==Systematics==

Helvetia is a small genus belonging to the subfamily Heliophaninae. Currently, it comprises eleven valid species.Ruiz & Brescovit 2008
