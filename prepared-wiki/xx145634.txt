[[All That I Need]]

CATEGORIES: Boyzone songs, 1998 singles, UK Singles Chart number-one singles, Irish Singles Chart number-one singles, Songs written by Carl Sturken and Evan Rogers, Pop ballads

"All That I Need" is the third single from Irish Boyband Boyzone's third studio album, Where We Belong. The song reached No. 1 in the UK Singles Chart in May 1998, selling 220,000 copies in the UK. The song was written and produced by Carl Sturken and Evan Rogers.
