[[Jeff Killed John (EP)]]

CATEGORIES: Bullet for My Valentine albums

 
Jeff Killed John is the last EP by Welsh metalcore band Jeff Killed John, before changing their name to Bullet for My Valentine. This EP also marks the last recorded material with bassist Nick Crandle, before Jason James replaced him.http://jeffkilledjohn.blogspot.com/2011/02/jeff-killed-john-full-discography.html Track numbers 4 is a demo of the song later released on Bullet for My Valentine's debut album, "The Poison".http://jeffkilledjohn.blogspot.com/2011/02/jeff-killed-john-full-discography.html Track 2 contains riff elements that would later become Tears Don't Fall with lyrical content that appears on 10 Years Today. http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zwuPzTyOHLU http://m.metrolyrics.com/routine-unhappiness-lyrics-jeff-killed-john.html
