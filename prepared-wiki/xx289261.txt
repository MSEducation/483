[[Nariman Qeshlaq]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Nariman Qeshlaq ([tpl]lang-fa|نريمان قشلاق[/tpl], also Romanized as Narīmān Qeshlāq; also known as Nareiman Gheshlagh, Nāremān Qishlāq, Narīmān, and Narīmān Qeshlāqī)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3076532|Nariman Qeshlaq[/tpl] is a village in Shivanat Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 329, in 76 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
