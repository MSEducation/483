[[Ethnikos Filippiada F.C.]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Greece, Football clubs in Epirus, Association football clubs established in 1935

[tpl]Refimprove|date=May 2008[/tpl]

Ethnikos Football Club is a Greek football club, based in Filippiada.
The association was founded in 1948. In 2008, they were promoted to Football League 2.
