[[Central Ishikari Mountains]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of Hokkaido, Mountain ranges of Japan

The [tpl]nihongo|Central Ishikari Mountains|石狩中央山地|Ishikari Chūō Sanchi[/tpl], or more commonly referred to simply as the [tpl]nihongo|Ishikari Mountains|石狩山地|Ishikari Sanchi[/tpl], are a group of mountains in central Hokkaidō, Japan.

==List of peaks==

==Geography==

These mountains border Tokachi-Mitsumata Caldera to the south and Nipesotsu-Maruyama Volcanic Group to the southwest.
