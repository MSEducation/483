[[Taqi Kandi, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Taqi Kandi ([tpl]lang-fa|تقي كندي[/tpl], also Romanized as Taqī Kandī)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3796090|Taqi Kandi[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 163, in 40 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
