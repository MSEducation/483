[[Municipal Auditorium (Columbus, Georgia)]]

CATEGORIES: Defunct sports venues in Georgia (U.S. state), Indoor arenas in the United States, Buildings and structures in Columbus, Georgia, Sports venues in Columbus, Georgia

The Municipal Auditorium was a 3,916-seat multi-purpose arena in Columbus, Georgia, USA. It was opened in 1955 and closed in 1996, when the Columbus Civic Center opened. It hosted local sporting events and concerts.
