[[Providence Newberg Medical Center Heliport]]

CATEGORIES: Hospital heliports in Oregon, Transportation in Yamhill County, Oregon, Buildings and structures in Newberg, Oregon

Providence Newberg Medical Center Heliport [tpl]Airport codes|||OG55[/tpl] is a private heliport located in Newberg in Yamhill County, Oregon, United States. It was built in 2006 as a part of the Providence Newberg Medical Center. The heliport at the former Newberg Community Hospital, Newberg Community Hospital Heliport also moved when the hospital moved to its new location.
