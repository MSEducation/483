[[Black Thursday (film)]]

CATEGORIES: French films, French-language films, 1974 films, 1970s drama films, Films directed by Michel Mitrani

Black Thursday ([tpl]lang-fr|Les Guichets du Louvre[/tpl]) is a French film from 1974 directed by Michel Mitrani. It was entered into the 24th Berlin International Film Festival.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0192099/awards |title=IMDB.com: Awards for Black Thursday |accessdate=4 July 2010 |work=imdb.com[/tpl]

==Synopsis==

On 16 July 1942, in Paris. A young student, Paul, tries to save some of the Jews being rounded up by Nazis to ship off to concentration camps.
