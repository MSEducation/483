[[Adriane Lenox]]

CATEGORIES: 1956 births, Actresses from Memphis, Tennessee, American film actresses, American stage actresses, American television actresses, Drama Desk Award winners, Living people, Tony Award winners, African-American actresses, 20th-century American actresses, 21st-century American actresses

Adriane Lenox (born September 11, 1956) is an American stage and film actress whose performance in the play Doubt: A Parable garnered her the Tony Award for Best Featured Actress in a Play in 2005.
