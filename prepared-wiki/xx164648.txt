[[WASP-1]]

CATEGORIES: Andromeda (constellation), F-type main-sequence stars, Planetary transit variables, Planetary systems

WASP-1 is a metal-rich magnitude 12 star located about [tpl]formatnum:1240[/tpl] light-years away in the Andromeda constellation.

==Planetary system==

In 2006, an extrasolar planet was discovered by transit method of the SuperWASP.
