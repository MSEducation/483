Prem is an Indian film actor who appears in Tamil and Kannada films. He rose to fame starring in television serials, while he was also the winner of the reality dance show Jodi Number One during season 1.

==Career==

Prem made his acting debut starring in a television serial produced by K. Balachander and directed by Samuthirakani. He was recommended by M. Karunanidhi, who had seen him perform in the TV serial Annamalai, to feature in the film version of Karunanidhi's novel Kannamma.http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2010-11-18/news-interviews/28221398_1_prem-tamil-films-vaada The film released in 2005 to average reviews.http://movies.bizhat.com/review_kannamma.php
He participated in Jodi Number One with Pooja, his co-star in Annamalai. He went on to win season 1 and announced a move away from television serials and into films.http://www.hindu.com/mp/2007/12/01/stories/2007120150640400.htm He has since appeared in films including Nepali (2008), Unnaipol Oruvan (2009) and Vallakottai (2010). His brother-in-law is the singer Harish Raghavendra.
