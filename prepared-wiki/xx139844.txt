[[A Thousand Songs]]

CATEGORIES: 2000 debut albums, Jim Guthrie albums

A Thousand Songs is the debut solo album by Jim Guthrie, released in 2000 on Three Gut Records. It was also the first album ever released by that label. The song “Who Needs What” was covered by the indiepop band Tullycraft on their 2002 album Beat Surf Fun.
