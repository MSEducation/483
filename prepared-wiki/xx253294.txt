[[Dnata Travel Services]]

CATEGORIES: Travel agencies, Companies based in Dubai, Travel and holiday companies of the United Arab Emirates

dnata Travel is the biggest provider of travel services and products for retail and business customers for the Middle Eastern market. The company is based in Dubai. It is a business segment of Dnata, and as such, part of The Emirates Group.  It has an annual turnover rate of $500 million.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.theemiratesgroup.com/english/our-brands/travel/dnata-travel-services.aspx |title=Dnata Travel Services info page |publisher=Theemiratesgroup.com |date= |accessdate=2010-01-18[/tpl]  
On June 27, 2008 Dnata Travel Services bought a 20% stake in United Kingdom-based Hogg Robinson Group [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ttnworldwide.com/articles.aspx?id=1090&artid=8043 |title=Dnata Buys Hogg Robinson Stake |publisher=TTN Worldwide |date= |accessdate=2010-01-18[/tpl] making it the largest shareholder of that company.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.btnonline.com/businesstravelnews/headlines/article_display.jsp?vnu_content_id=1003821877 |title=HRG Partner Dnata To Become Single Largest Shareholder |publisher=Btnonline.com |date=2008-06-27 |accessdate=2010-01-18[/tpl]

==Locations==

dnata Travel has its headquarters located in dnata Airline Center in the Sheikh Zayed Road, Dubai, UAE.  As the retail arm of dnata offering travel services and products, dnata Travel Services has 44 retail outlets scattered within Dubai.
In January 2009, dnata Travel Services and HRG launched a satellite in Kabul, Afghanistan.,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.arabianbusiness.com/press_releases/detail/37005 |title=Arabian Business News: Dnata Travel and HRG bring internationally recognised travel brands to Afghanistan |publisher=Arabianbusiness.com |date=2009-01-28 |accessdate=2010-01-18[/tpl] expanding the company's reach further outward in the Middle East region.

==Awards and achievements==

In 2009, Dnata Travel Services won two awards in the World Travel Awards, a prestigious international awarding body.  For the third consecutive year, Dnata Travel Services won the Middle East's Leading Travel Management Company.
