Diego Vicente Aguirre Camblor (born 13 September 1965 in Montevideohttp://www.pagineviola.com/index.php?a=scheda_articolo&id=24) is a Uruguayan former soccer player, known in his playing career for scoring the 1-0 goal in the 120th minute of the 1987 Copa Libertadores final. 

==Career==

Diego Aguirre played in the 1987 Peñarol team which won the Libertadores Cup.  Diego became notoriously famous for scoring the victory goal in the last seconds of the final minute of the third final match.http://southamerican-futbol.blogspot.com/2010/10/copa-libertadores.html
