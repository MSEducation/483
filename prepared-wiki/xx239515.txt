[[Sitanda]]

CATEGORIES: 2000s drama films, English-language films, Best Film Africa Movie Academy Award winners, Films directed by Izu Ojukwu

Sitanda is a Nigerian adventure / drama film directed by African Movie Academy Award winner Izu Ojukwu, and written by Fidel Akpom.
