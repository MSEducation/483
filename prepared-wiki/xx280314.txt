[[Bakhshi Stadium]]

CATEGORIES: Football venues in India

Bakhshi Stadium is a stadium  in Srinagar, India. It is mainly  used  for football matches.  The capacity of the stadium is 30,000 people. Association football club JK XI play home games at the stadium. One of them was a match against Mohun Bagan, in front of a capacity crowd of 30,000.
