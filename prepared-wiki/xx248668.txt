[[That's the Way I've Always Heard It Should Be]]

CATEGORIES: 1971 singles, Carly Simon songs, Songs written by Carly Simon, 1970s ballads, Songs with lyrics by Jacob Brackman, 1971 songs, Elektra Records singles

"That's the Way I've Always Heard It Should Be" is a 1971 single written by Carly Simon and Jacob Brackman and performed by Simon, released from her self-titled debut album.
