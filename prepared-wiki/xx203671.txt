[[Renzo Marangon]]

CATEGORIES: Venetian politicians, Forza Italia politicians, Living people, 1955 births

Renzo Marangon (born 29 July 1955 in Porto Tolle) is an Italian politician from Veneto.
A member of Christian Democracy since 1974, he entered the Regional Council of Veneto in 1993, in replacement of Giulio Veronese. In 1993 he was also briefly Mayor of Rovigo. In 1994 he joined the United Christian Democrats and was elected to the Council the year later. Re-elected in 2000 for Forza Italia (party which he had joined in 1998), he was floor leader of the party for five years.
