[[NHL Breakaway 99]]

CATEGORIES: 1998 video games, Acclaim Entertainment games, National Hockey League video games, Nintendo 64 games, Video games developed in the United States, Ice hockey video games

NHL Breakaway '99 is an ice hockey game for the Nintendo 64 and is a sequel to NHL Breakaway '98. It was released in 1998, The cover art features Steve Yzerman of the Detroit Red Wings.

==Features==

The game features the same features as '98 did: a season mode, practice mode, playoff mode and an exhibition mode. It features all the NHL teams.
