[[Balai Ringin]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Sarawak

Balai Ringin  is a town and constituency in Sarawak, Malaysia, about one hour's drive from the town of Serian along the Pan Borneo Highway. It lies approximately [tpl]convert|75.3|km|mi|0[/tpl] southeast of the state capital Kuching. The area is predominantly inhabited by Iban tribespeople, though there are a few Malay villages.
