[[George Davenport (American football)]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing, Year of death missing, Tennessee State Tigers football coaches

George Davenport was the third head football coach for the Tennessee State University Tigers located in Nashville, Tennessee and he held that position for two seasons, from 1925 until 1926.  His career coaching record at Tennessee State was 5 wins, 3 losses, and 3 ties.  As of the conclusion of the 2007 season, this ranks him 13th at Tennessee State in total wins and tenth at Tennessee State in winning percentage (.591).Tennessee State University coaching records
