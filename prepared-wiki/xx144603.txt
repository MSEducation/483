[[Lucy Payton]]

CATEGORIES: 1877 births, 1969 deaths, Actresses from Kansas, American silent film actresses, Burials at Forest Lawn Memorial Park (Glendale), 20th-century American actresses

Lucy Payton (12 October 1877, Kansas  - 15 January 1969, Louisiana, Missouri) was an American silent film actress.
Lucy married another actor, Claude Payton.
