CATEGORIES: Swimming at the 1984 Summer Olympics

The final of the Women's 400 metres Individual Medley event at the 1984 Summer Olympics was held in the McDonald's Olympic Swim Stadium in Los Angeles, California, on July 29, 1984. The first eight qualified for the final, the next eight for the B-final.
