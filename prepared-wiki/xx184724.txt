[[Al Milch]]

CATEGORIES: Sul Ross State Lobos football coaches, Texas–Arlington Mavericks football coaches, 1919 births, 2010 deaths

Alfred Wesley Milch (November 30, 1919 – November 3, 2010) was the seventh head college football coach for the University of Texas at Arlington Mavericks located in Arlington, Texas and he held that position for the 1951 season.http://www.brownwoodnews.com/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=3608%3Aalfred-al-wesley-milch&Itemid=91  His career coaching record at UT Arlington was 3 wins, 4 losses, and 1 ties.
