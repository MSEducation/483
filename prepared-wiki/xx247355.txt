[[Carnane]]

CATEGORIES: Townlands of County Limerick

Carnane is a townland in the parish of Fedamore, in County Limerick, Ireland.
Neighbouring townlands include Fanningstown to the west, Arywee and Williamstown to the east, Boherlode to the north, and Enaghroe and Poultaloon to the south.Google Maps
It has an area of approximately 184 hectares (455 acres).IreAtlas Townland Database It is located [tpl]convert|13.6|km|mi|abbr=on[/tpl] distance by road from Limerick city centre, in a South South East direction.Google Earth - earth.google.com
The name is an anglicisation from the Irish language "An Carnán", meaning "small cairn" (or heap of stones).Placenames Database of Ireland - www.logainm.ie
Residents of Carnane entitled to vote may do so for candidates in the Adare Local Electoral Area for local elections, the Limerick (Dáil Éireann constituency) for General elections, South (European Parliament constituency) for European Parliament elections, and in referenda to change the Irish Constitution. The polling station for these elections is in Fedamore National School.Limerick County Council - Register of Electors - www.lcc.ie/ereg
Carnane National School currently has a mixed enrolment of 40 boys and 34 girls. It is under the sponsorship of the Catholic Church.SchoolDays.ie
