[[Let's Live for Today (album)]]

CATEGORIES: 1967 albums, The Grass Roots albums, English-language albums, Dunhill Records albums

Let's Live for Today is the second studio album by The Grass Roots. Released in 1967, it features their first top-ten hit by the same name, "Let's Live for Today".[tpl]cite web|url=http://the-grassroots.com/html/discography.html|title=Discography|publisher=The Grass Roots Official site|accessdate=21 January 2010[/tpl]

==Track listing==

All songs written by P.F. Sloan & Steve Barri except as noted.
