[[List of diplomatic missions of Somalia]]

CATEGORIES: Foreign relations of Somalia, Lists of diplomatic missions by sending country, Somalia-related lists

This is a list of diplomatic missions of Somalia, excluding honorary consulates. Foreign relations of Somalia are handled primarily by the President as the head of state, the Prime Minister as the head of government, and the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Federal Government.[tpl]cite web|title=The Federal Republic of Somalia - Provisional Constitution|url=http://www.somaliweyn.com/pages/news/Aug_12/Somalia_Constitution_English_FOR_WEB.pdf|accessdate=13 March 2013[/tpl]
According to Article 54 of the national constitution, the allocation of powers and resources between the Federal Government and the Federal Republic of Somalia's constituent Federal Member States shall be negotiated and agreed upon by the Federal Government and the Federal Member States, except in matters pertaining to foreign affairs, national defense, citizenship and immigration, and monetary policy. Article 53 also stipulates that the Federal Government shall consult the Federal Member States on major issues related to international agreements, including negotiations vis-a-vis foreign trade, finance and treaties.
Somaliland, a self-declared sovereign state that is internationally recognised as an autonomous region of Somalia, maintains consulate-level informal relations with some foreign governments. However, its self-proclaimed independence remains unrecognised by any country or international organisation.[tpl]cite web|title=The UK Prime Minister's Office Reply To The "Somaliland E-Petition"|url=http://www.somalilandtimes.net/sl/2006/270/3.shtml|publisher=Somaliland Times|accessdate=4 September 2013[/tpl][tpl]Cite news |title = The Signs Say Somaliland, but the World Says Somalia |publisher = New York Times|date=5 June 2006|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2006/06/05/world/africa/05somaliland.html|accessdate=2 February 2010|first=Marc |last=Lacey[/tpl] The autonomous Puntland region in northeastern Somalia also maintains consulate-level relations with some foreign governments.[tpl]cite news|title=SOMALIA: Ethiopia opens consulate in Puntland|url=http://horseedmedia.net/2010/05/30/somalia-ethiopia-opens-consulate-in-puntland/|accessdate=4 September 2013|newspaper=Horseed Media|date=30 May 2010[/tpl]
