[[Beau Archibald]]

CATEGORIES: American basketball coaches, 1976 births, Living people

Beau Archibald (born 1976). Is an American basketball coach currently coaching internationally with El Jaish in Doha, Qatar. He previously coached theZhejiang Guangsha Lions of the Chinese Basketball Association and the Shanxi Zhongyu Brave Dragons and Foshan Dralions. Before coaching Internationally Archibald coached under Hall of Fame coach Jim Calhounhttp://www.jimcalhouncamp.com/coacharchibald.php at his alma mater the University of Connecticuthttp://www.uconnhuskies.com/datadump/MBasketball/Coaching/Archibald_bio.html where he was a part of the Huskies Final Four run in 2009. Before Connecticut Archibald worked at Florida International, Texas San Antonio, Jacksonville University,http://www.judolphins.com/baseball/roster/68/472-print/ Florida Atlantic University http://www.fausports.com/sports/m-baskbl/mtt/archibald_beau00.html and started his career as a Volunteer at Texas Tech with Bobby Knight. Throughout his coaching career Archibald has worked with some of basketballs best coaches including Jim Calhoun, and Bobby Knight. Archibald has also worked with Hugh Durham,http://www.cstv.com/sports/m-baskbl/stories/081904aac.html Wang Fei who was the former China men's national basketball team) coach.http://english.people.com.cn/english/200103/15/eng20010315_65120.html
