[[Rafał Andraszak]]

CATEGORIES: 1978 births, Living people, Polish footballers, Amica Wronki players, Górnik Zabrze players, Association football forwards, People from Słupsk, Sportspeople from Pomeranian Voivodeship

Rafał Andraszak (born 5 February 1978 in Słupsk) is a Polish footballer who currently plays for Flota Świnoujście in the Polish First League.
