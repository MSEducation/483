[[I'm Good, I'm Gone]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 singles, Atlantic Records singles, Lykke Li songs, Songs from films

"I'm Good, I'm Gone" is a song by Swedish recording artist Lykke Li from her debut album, Youth Novels. Produced by Björn Yttling of Peter Bjorn and John and co-produced by Lasse Mårtén, it was released in Sweden on 14 January 2008 as the album's second single.
