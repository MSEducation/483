Sagi Strauss ([tpl]lang-he|שגיא שטראוס[/tpl]; born 29 June 1976) is a retired Israeli international football player. His brother in law is Roberto Colautti. Strauss han been transferred or loaned to a new team before almost every season of his professional career.
