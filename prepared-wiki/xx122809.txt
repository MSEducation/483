[[Korthio]]

Korthio ([tpl]lang-el|Κόρθιο[/tpl]) is a former municipality on the island of Andros, in the Cyclades, Greece. Since the 2011 local government reform it is part of the municipality Andros, of which it is a municipal unit.Kallikratis law Greece Ministry of Interior [tpl]el icon[/tpl] Its population was 2,547 inhabitants at the 2001 census, and its land area is 81.918 km². It shares the island of Andros with the municipal unit of Andros and Ydrousa.
