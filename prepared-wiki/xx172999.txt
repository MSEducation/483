[[Palau-de-Cerdagne]]

CATEGORIES: Communes of Pyrénées-Orientales

Palau-de-Cerdagne ([tpl]lang-ca|Palau de Cerdanya[/tpl]) is a commune in the eastern Pyrenees (Pyrénées-Orientales department in southern France), bordering with the Spanish Catalonia.
The people of the town celebrate the Xicolatada, every year, on the 16th of August.
