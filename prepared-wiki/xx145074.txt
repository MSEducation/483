[[Venus in Love]]

CATEGORIES: 1999 manga, Shōjo manga, Romance anime and manga, CMX titles

[tpl]Nihongo|Venus in Love|ビーナスは片想い|Venus wa Kataomoi[/tpl], also known as Love for Venus, is a shōjo manga by [tpl]Nihongo|Yuki Nakaji|なかじ 有紀|Nakaji Yūki[/tpl]. The series revolves around the lives of a group of students at Koutou University in Hyōgo Prefecture and primarily around the relationships of the main characters, Suzuna Ashihara and Eichi Uozumi. The manga is published in English in Singapore by Chuang Yi as Love For Venus[tpl]Citation needed|date=April 2010[/tpl] and in North America by CMX Manga as Venus in Love.
