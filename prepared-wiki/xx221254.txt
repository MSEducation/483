[[Between Love and Hate (1993 film)]]

CATEGORIES: 1993 television films, 1990s crime drama films, American crime drama films, American television films, English-language films, Films directed by Rod Hardy, True crime films

Between Love and Hate is a 1993 American television movie directed by Ron Hardy.

==Plot==

The film is based on actual events regarding a young swimming coach having an affair with an older woman until her husband is forced to end it. This resulted in a chain of reactions from photocopying of love letters leading up to her murder.
