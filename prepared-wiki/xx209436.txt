[[Henry St Aubyn Murray]]

CATEGORIES: 1886 births, 1943 deaths, New Zealand hurdlers, Olympic athletes of New Zealand, Athletes (track and field) at the 1908 Summer Olympics

Henry St Aubyn Murray (14 January 1886 - 12 April 1943) was a New Zealand athlete, who competed for Australasia in the 1908 Summer Olympics in London, England.[tpl]cite web|title=Henry Murray|url=http://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/mu/henry-murray-1.html|publisher=Sports Reference, LLC|accessdate=16 December 2010[/tpl]
Murray competed in two athletic events. In the 110 metre hurdles he was 2nd in the heat (1-4.58) but did not qualify. In the 400 metre hurdles he was 2nd in the heat (59.8) but did not qualify.
