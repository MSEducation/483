[[Athletics at the 2011 Pan American Games – Men's 4 × 400 metres relay]]

CATEGORIES: Athletics at the 2011 Pan American Games, Relay foot races at the Pan American Games

The men's 4 x 400 metres relay competition of the athletics events at the 2011 Pan American Games took place on the 27 and 28 October at the Telmex Athletics Stadium.[tpl]cite web|title=Guadalajara 2011 Sessions|url=http://guadalajara2011.org.mx/documentos/boletaje/lista-de-sesiones.pdf|publisher=Guadalajara 2011|accessdate=29 October 2011[/tpl]  The defending Pan American Games champion were Andrae Williams, Avard Moncur, Michael Matheau and Chris Brown of Bahamas.
