[[1951 Cannes Film Festival]]

CATEGORIES: 1951 film festivals, 1951 in France, Cannes Film Festival

The 4th Cannes Film Festival was held on 3-20 April 1951. The festival was not held in 1950. This year, the festival took place in April instead of September.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ukhotmovies.com/film-festivals/cannes-film-festival/|title=Cannes Film Festival|accessdate=November 14, 2013[/tpl]
The Grand Prize of the Festival went to the three different films, Miss Julie by Alf Sjöberg, Miracle in Milan by Vittorio De Sica and Spiegel van Holland by Bert Haanstra.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.festival-cannes.fr/en/archives/1951/allAward.html| title=Awards 1951 : All Awards|accessdate=10 December 2013[/tpl] The festival honored Michèle Morgan, Jean Marais and Jean Cocteau with Victoire du cinéma français award.[tpl]cite news|url=http://fresques.ina.fr/festival-de-cannes-en/fiche-media/Cannes00010/awarding-of-the-victoire-du-cinema-francais-awards-at-the-opening-of-the-1951-festival.html| title=Awarding of the "Victoire du cinéma français" awards at the opening of the 1951 Festival|accessdate=10 December 2013[/tpl]
