[[Shinee World (concert)]]

CATEGORIES: Shinee concert tours, 2010 concert tours, 2011 concert tours

Shinee World (promoted as SHINee THE 1ST CONCERT "SHINee WORLD") is the first independent concert tour by South Korean boy group, Shinee. The tour kicked off in Tokyo on December 26, 2010 and ended in Osaka on November 25, 2011, with a total of 10 concerts in 7 cities.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.koreastardaily.com/tc/news/5126|title=SHINee will hold a special concert at the end of concert between Korea and Japan.  |date=November 25, 2010|accessdate=January 19, 2011|publisher=Korea Star Daily[/tpl]
The photobook of this concert, Shinee The 1st Concert "Shinee World" Photobook released on December 26, 2010SHINee The First Concert 'SHINee World' Photobook ~ Daily K Pop News and a live dvd, Shinee The 1st Concert in Japan "Shinee World" was filmed from their concert in Tokyo and Nagoya released on January 1, 2012, followed a live album, Shinee The 1st Concert Album "Shinee World", of the concert held in Seoul released on February 1, 2012.

===Tokyo===

The concert was originally scheduled for the December 26, 2010 at 6PM, but due to over 100,000 crowded fans that joined the month-long ticket event, they had to put out another concert at 1PM.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.allkpop.com/2011/01/shinees-japanese-website-crashes-moments-after-opening|title=SHINee's Japanese website crashes moments after opening|date=January 3, 2011|publisher=allkpop.com|accessdate=January 4, 2011[/tpl] SM Entertainment put out an official statement saying, 
The concert took place at Yoyogi National Gymnasium, with both concerts sold out, the total audience of both the performances together was approximately 24,000, they sang over sixty songs.[tpl]cite web|url=http://ent.gz163.cn/star/hrxw/1072886.shtml|title=SHINee concert a big success in Japan next year, will officially enter the Japanese music scene|publisher=gz163.cn|date=December 27, 2010|accessdate=January 19, 2011[/tpl] They also revealed the music video for their next ballad single, "Quasimodo", from their second album, Lucifer.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.allkpop.com/2010/12/shinee-reveals-quasimodo-mv-at-tokyo-concert|title=SHINee reveals ‘Quasimodo’ MV at Tokyo concert!|date=December 27, 2010|publisher=allkpop.com|accessdate=December 2, 2011[/tpl]
The group also announced that they will make their official debut in Japan in March 2011 under the label of EMI Music Japan. It was later revealed on their official Japanese website that the concert will air on Fuji TV on February 27, 2011, from 7:00 pm to 9:00 pm.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.allkpop.com/2010/12/shinees-the-1st-concert-in-tokyo-shinee-world-to-air-in-february|title=SHINee’s ‘THE 1st CONCERT in TOKYO’ "SHINee World" to air in February|date=December 27, 2010|publisher=allkpop.com|accessdate=December 2, 2011[/tpl]

===Seoul===

On January 1, 2011, on the start of the new year, the group displayed the same set of performances in the concert at Olympic Gymnastics Arena in Seoul, South Korea.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.koreatimes.co.kr/www/news/art/2011/01/143_79076.html|title=SHINee sparkles at first concert|date=January 3, 2011|accessdate=January 19, 2011|publisher=Korea Times[/tpl] The concert, previously scheduled to be held at the KBS 88 Gymnasium on December 18 and 19, 2010. But On November 19, 2010, it was reported recently that their solo concert have a new date and location. SM Entertainment revealed,
On January 2, 2011, before the second running of the concert in Seoul, Shinee held a press conference. The group answered many questions related to their future plans and also reflected on their success in 2010.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.allkpop.com/2011/01/shinee-holds-press-conference-before-second-running-of-shinee-world|title=SHINee holds press conference before second running of "SHINee World"|date=January 2, 2011|publisher=allkpop.com|accessdate=December 2, 2011[/tpl]

===Taipei===

On May 26, 2011, it was confirmed that Shinee will be holding a concert in Taipei, Taiwan. 10,000 tickets were made available on June 4, 2011, and were sold out within an hour.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.koreaboo.com/index.html/_/music/beast-and-shinee-sell-out-their-events-in-taiwan-r6720|title=BEAST and SHINee sell out their events in Taiwan|date=June 5, 2011|accessdate=July 18, 2011|publisher=koreaboo.com[/tpl] On July 16, 2011, the boy band held their first concert in Taipei at Taipei Arena. R&B singer and fellow labelmate Zhang Liyin appeared as a special guest and sang  a duet with Jonghyun.

===Nanjing===

On August 20, 2011, Shinee also performed in a concert in Nanjing, China at Nanjing Olympic Sports Center Gymnasium. On September 23, 2011, SM Entertainment released a video highlighting the concert in Nanjing.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HNDzlvExEoo&feature=youtu.be|title=SHINee 샤이니 'SHINee World' in Nanjing, China Highlights|date=September 23, 2011|work=SM Entertainment|publisher=YouTube|accessdate=September 23, 2011[/tpl]

===Singapore===

It was revealed in July 12, 2011, that the group will visit hold its next concert in Singapore at Singapore Indoor Stadium on September 10, 2011. The production cost for the concert was revealed to be around $1.7 million. Online ticket reservations began on July 31, 2011 and within 45 minutes, all tickets were sold out.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.allkpop.com/2011/09/shinee-successfully-completes-their-solo-concert-in-singapore|title=SHINee successfully completes their solo concert in Singapore|date=September 11, 2011|publisher=allkpop.com|accessdate=September 11, 2011[/tpl]

===Nagoya===

Shinee also performed in a concert at Nippon Gaishi Hall in Nagoya, Japan on October 27, 2011.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.koreaherald.com/entertainment/Detail.jsp?newsMLId=20111031000616|title=Shinee fans crash U.K. ticket site|date=October 31, 2011|accessdate=November 9, 2011|publisher=The Korea Herald|work=koreaherald.com[/tpl] Live recordings of performances from the concert were released as a live album, Shinee The 1st Concert In Japan "Shinee World", on January 12, 2011.

===Osaka===

Sankei Sports reported that over 18,000 people attended the concerts held on the November 24 and 25, 2011 in Osaka.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.allkpop.com/2011/11/shinee-wraps-up-a-successful-concert-in-japan|title=SHINee wraps up a successful concert in Japan|date=November 26, 2011|publisher=allkpop.com|accessdate=November 26, 2011[/tpl]
