[[Santa Isabel, La Pampa]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in La Pampa Province, Populated places established in 1904

Santa Isabel is a town in the province of La Pampa, Argentina. It has 2,493 inhabitants as of the [tpl]census-ar|2001[/tpl], and is the head town of the Chalileo Department. It lies on the west of the province, 240 km from the provincial capital Santa Rosa, by National Route 143, near the junction of the borders of the provinces of La Pampa, San Luis and Mendoza, in the area of the Atuel River marshlands.
