[[Attica Prefecture]]

CATEGORIES: 1899 establishments in Greece, Prefectures of Greece, Geography of Attica

Attica Prefecture ([tpl]lang-el|Νομὸς Ἀττικῆς[/tpl]) was a prefecture of Greece, established from the Attica and Boeotia Prefecture in 1899. From 1971, it consisted of the following prefectural-level administrations (nomarchies):
