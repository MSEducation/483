[[Nowruzabad, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Nowruzabad ([tpl]lang-fa|نوروزاباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Nowrūzābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795763|Nowruzabad[/tpl] is a village in Chaypareh-ye Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 72, in 16 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
