[[Owrachi]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Owrachi ([tpl]lang-fa|اوراچي[/tpl], also Romanized as Owrāchī and Ūrāchī; also known as Orāchī and Orchi)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3087950|Owrachi[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 40, in 12 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
