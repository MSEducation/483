[[Doische]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Belgium, Municipalities of Namur (province)

Doische ([tpl]IPA-fr|dwaʃ[/tpl]) is a Walloon municipality located in Belgium in the province of Namur. On 1 January 2006 the municipality had 2,846 inhabitants. The total area is 84.02 km², giving a population density of 34 inhabitants per km².
