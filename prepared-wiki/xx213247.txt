[[Miaoli Hills]]

CATEGORIES: Landforms of Taiwan, Hill lands, Geography of Miaoli County

Miaoli Hills ([tpl]zh|c=苗栗丘陵[/tpl]) is an area of hills stretching across the Miaoli County of central-northern Taiwan. The hills is lies on the south of Hsinchu Hills and on the north of Taichung. It is extended from the Hsuehshan Mountain Range as a part of foothills of the range, and adjoins the northwestern seacoast of Taiwan island. The Hakka people in this region are majority.
