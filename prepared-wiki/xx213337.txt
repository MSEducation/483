[[Bagua Plateau]]

CATEGORIES: Plateaus of Taiwan, Geography of Changhua County, Geography of Nantou County

Pakua Plateau or Bagua Plateau ([tpl]zh|c=八卦台地[/tpl]), also known as the Pakua Mountain Range (八卦山脈), is located at the central-western Taiwan. It is stretching across the Changhua County and Nantou County. The plateau faces the Changhua Plain on the west and Taichung Basin on the east. It is long and narrow, has a length of about 32 km, and a width of about 4 to 7 km. The highest peak of the plateau is the Mt.
