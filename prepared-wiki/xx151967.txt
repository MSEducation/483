[[Richard Bleiler]]

CATEGORIES: American bibliographers, American librarians, 1959 births, Living people, English-language writers

Richard James Bleiler (born 1959) is an American bibliographer in science fiction, fantasy, horror, crime, and adventure fiction.The Encyclopedia of Science Fiction, page 135: "Bleiler, Richard" He is the son of Everett F. Bleiler and was a nominee for the Bram Stoker Award for Best Non-Fiction in 2002. Bleiler is currently the Humanities Reference Librarian at the Homer Babbidge Library at the University of Connecticut.
