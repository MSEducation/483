[[Rasheeduddin Khan]]

CATEGORIES: Nominated members of the Rajya Sabha, 1924 births, 1996 deaths

Rasheeduddin Khan (1924-1996) was an Indian author and parliamentarian. He was nominated to the Rajya Sabha in 1970 and served for two terms till 1982. He wrote a number of papers and studies on socio-economic problems and culture.
