[[Chie Tsuji]]

CATEGORIES: 1969 births, Living people, Japanese volleyball players, Volleyball players at the 2004 Summer Olympics, Olympic volleyball players of Japan

[tpl]Nihongo|Chie Tsuji|辻 知恵||born August 9, 1969[/tpl] is a volleyball player from Japan. Her maiden name was [tpl]nihongo|Chie Natori|名取 知恵|Natori Chie[/tpl].[tpl]Citation needed|date=October 2012[/tpl]
She competed at the 2004 Summer Olympics in Athens, Greece, wearing the Number 2 jersey. She and the Japan women's national team took fifth place. Tsuji played as a setter.
