[[Bocșa]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Caraș-Severin County, Towns in Romania, Mining communities in Romania

Bocșa ([tpl]IPA-ro|ˈbokʃa|-|Ro-Bocșa.ogg[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-hu|Boksánbánya[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-de|Deutsch-Bokschan, Neuwerk[/tpl]) is a town in Caraș-Severin County, in southwestern Romania, with a population of 15,842 in 2011.
