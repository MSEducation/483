Tony Rigby (born 10 August 1972) is an English former professional footballer who played as a midfielder, making over 150 career appearances.

==Career==

Born in Ormskirk, Rigby played for Crewe Alexandra, Lancaster City, Burscough, Barrow, Bury, Scarborough and Shrewsbury Town.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.neilbrown.newcastlefans.com/player8/tonyrigby.html|title=Profile|accessdate=15 February 2014|publisher=Post War English & Scottish Football League A - Z Player's Database[/tpl]
