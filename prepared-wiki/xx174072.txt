[[Game (KHM album)]]

CATEGORIES: Kool Keith albums, 2002 debut albums

Game is the debut album by KHM, a rap group consisting of Kool Keith, H-Bomb (aka Jacky Jasper), and Marc Live.  The album was released on November 19, 2002 for Number 6 Records and was produced by all three members of the group.  The album gained some positive reviews but  was a commercial failure and did not make it to the Billboard charts or spawn any hit singles.[tpl]Fact|date=October 2008[/tpl]  English trip hop artist Tricky makes a guest appearance on the track "Run Dem Red".
