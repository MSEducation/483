[[Swimming at the 1984 Summer Olympics – Men's 4 × 200 metre freestyle relay]]

CATEGORIES: Swimming at the 1984 Summer Olympics

The final of the Men's 4x200 metres Freestyle Relay event at the 1984 Summer Olympics was held in Los Angeles, California, on July 30, 1984. The first eight teams qualified for the final.
