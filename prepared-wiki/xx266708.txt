[[Eugène Pierre Nicolas Fournier]]

CATEGORIES: 1834 births, 1884 deaths, French botanists, Botanists with author abbreviations

Eugène Pierre Nicolas Fournier (15 February 1834, Paris — 10 June 1884) was a French botanist.Catalogue des thèses de sciences: soutenues en France de 1810 à 1890 by Albert Maire
He was particularly interested in ferns. He was a member of the Société Royale de Botanique de Belgique.
The genus Fourniera (family Cyatheaceae) is named in his honor.Google Books Etymological Dictionary of Grasses
