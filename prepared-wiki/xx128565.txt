[[Torgu Parish]]

CATEGORIES: Torgu Parish

Torgu Parish  is a rural municipality at the tip of the Sõrve Peninsula on the island of Saaremaa in western Estonia. It is a part of Saare County.
This parish consists of 22 villages. The municipality has a population of 375 (as of 1 January 2006) and covers an area of 126.44 km².
