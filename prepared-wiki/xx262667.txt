[[Taiko no Tatsujin (Clay Anime)]]

CATEGORIES: Clay animation television series and films

[tpl]nihongo|Taiko no Tatsujin Clay Anime|太鼓の達人粘土アニメ|Taiko no Tatsujin Nendo Anime|lit. Taiko Drum Master Clay Anime[/tpl] is a clay animation series of 5-minute long episodes that were produced by Namco of the Taiko no Tatsujin series and were all released only in Japan. The series were released in 2006. There is currently a total 26 episodes in the series.
