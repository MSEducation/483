[[Enrico Cardoso Nazaré]]

Enrico Cardoso Nazaré (born 4 May 1984 in Belo Horizonte), commonly known as Enrico, is a Brazilian footballer who plays as a attacking midfielder, under contract with Brazilian club Vasco da Gama until 30 May 2014.

==Career==
