[[Magnus Häggström]]

Magnus Häggström (born November 4, 1986 in Örnsköldsvik, Sweden) is a Swedish professional ice hockey player. He is currently playing for Malmö Redhawks of the HockeyAllsvenskan (Swe-1).[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.malmoredhawks.com/Nyheter/120127-Magnus-Haggstrom/ |title=Forward förstärker Malmö Redhawks |publisher=Malmö Redhawks |last=Martin |first=Nathalie |language=Swedish |date=2012-01-27 |accessdate=2012-01-28[/tpl]
Häggström played in the Elitserien (SEL) with Modo Hockey during the 2009–10 and 2010–11 Elitserien seasons.
