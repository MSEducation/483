[[1948–49 Egyptian Premier League]]

CATEGORIES: Egyptian Premier League, 1948 in Egypt, 1949 in Egypt, 1948–49 domestic association football leagues

The 1948–49 Egyptian Premier League started on 22 October 1948. Al-Ahly were crowned champions for the first time in total.
