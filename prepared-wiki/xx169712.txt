[[Kentucky Route 40]]

CATEGORIES: State highways in Kentucky, Transportation in Magoffin County, Kentucky, Transportation in Johnson County, Kentucky, Transportation in Martin County, Kentucky

Kentucky Route 40 (KY 40) is a state highway in the U.S. state of Kentucky. The road begins at a junction with U.S. Route 460 in Salyersville in Magoffin County, then continues eastward through Paintsville in Johnson County. KY 40 ends in Martin County at an intersection with KY Route 292 and an access bridge to U.S. 52 at the West Virginia border. The road was featured during the 1980s in a song by Ricky Skaggs "Highway 40 Blues".
