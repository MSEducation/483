[[Odhanavaneswarar Temple, Tiruchotruturai]]

CATEGORIES: Padal Petra Stalam, Hindu temples in Thanjavur district

Odhanavaneswarar Temple or Oppillaselvar Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva located near Tiruvaiyaru, Tamilnadu, India. The temple is incarnated by the hymns of Appar and is classified as Paadal Petra Sthalam.

==Legend==

The temple is revered by the hymns of 7th century Tamil saint poet, Appar.  The temple is built in the early Chola style of 9th century made of graniteConcise classified dictionary of Hinduism .P.53.Kodayanallur Vanamamalai Soundara Rajan.
