[[Old Statehouse Historic District]]

CATEGORIES: Federal architecture in Kentucky, National Register of Historic Places in Franklin County, Kentucky, Geography of Franklin County, Kentucky, National Register of Historic Places in Kentucky, Historic districts in Kentucky, African-American history of Kentucky

Old Statehouse Historic District is an area in downtown Frankfort, Kentucky near the old State Capitol and the Old Governors Mansion. The area is bounded by Broadway Street, Blanton Street, St. Clair Street, Ann Street and High Street and contains 74 historic buildings. The historic district was added to the United States National Register of Historic Places in 1980.
