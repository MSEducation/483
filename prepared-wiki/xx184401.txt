[[Gołkowice, Silesian Voivodeship]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Wodzisław County

Gołkowice [tpl]IPAc-pl|g|o|U|k|o|'|w|i|c|e[/tpl] is a village in Gmina Godów, Wodzisław County, Silesian Voivodeship, southern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It has a population of 3,345 and lies on the border with the Czech Republic.
There is a wooden Saint Anne Church from 1878 in the village.
