[[Black Diamond Bay (UK)]]

CATEGORIES: English electronic music groups

Black Diamond Bay (UK) is a UK independent electronic band formed in 2008, based in Leeds and London. The band consists of Agne Motie, Jesse O'Mahoney, Tom Sidebottom, Ben Ziapour, Ben Wilson and Colin Sutton. They produce and play live in several electronic dance music styles including Slo-Mo House and electronic music.

==History==

Black Diamond Bay was formed by Jesse O'Mahoney and Tom Sidebottom and was realised fully as a live band in 2006 when they recruited four other band members. In 2010 they signed a recording deal with Exceptional Records[tpl]cite news | url = http://exceptionalrecords.co.uk/artists_detail.asp?id=59[/tpl] with whom they have released an album - Come The Desert - in March 2011,[tpl]cite news | url = http://itunes.apple.com/gb/artist/black-diamond-bay/id302089083[/tpl] along with several singles and an EP. The band have a strong Lithuanian following in London[tpl]cite news | url = http://www.b-uk.lt/?s=black+diamond+bay&lang=lt[/tpl] (Agne is Lithuanian) and have received strong support from Alan Raw via the Leeds based BBC Introducing show,[tpl]cite news | url = http://www.bbc.co.uk/music/festivals/readingandleeds/2011/artists/blackdiamondbay.mp[/tpl] which led to them playing at both Reading and Leeds Festivals[tpl]cite news | url = http://www.leedsfestival.com/2011/lineup/artist/black_diamond_bay[/tpl] in 2011, and being added to the BBC Radio 1 playlist[tpl]cite news | url = http://www.bbc.co.uk/radio1/playlist/[/tpl] rotation.
