[[Uttampur]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Aligarh district

Uttampur  is a village in Aligarh district and located 30 km from Aligarh. This village comes in block - Gonda, tehsil - Iglas, district - Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh.
The population of this village is around 1500. The main profession of the village people is potato, wheat farming and rice.
This village also is a Gram panchayat of five villages.
