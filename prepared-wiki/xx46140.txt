[[Héron]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Liège (province)

Héron is a Walloon municipality located in the Belgian province of Liège and the Huy-Ware district.

==Geography==

Héron is located on the river Mehaigne.

==Demography==

On January 1, 2006 Héron had a total population of 4,534. The total area is 38.32 km2 which gives a population density of 118 inhabitants per km2.

==Facilities==

Heron is home to Couthuin Airport.
