[[Norman Robinson Stakes]]

CATEGORIES: Horse races in Australia, Flat horse races for three-year-olds, Caulfield Racecourse

The Norman Robinson Stakes is a Melbourne Racing Club Group 3 Australian Thoroughbred horse race held under open handicap conditions, for horses aged three-years-old, over a distance of 2000 metres. It is held at Caulfield Racecourse in Melbourne on Caulfied Cup day in October, and serves as a lead-up to the VRC Derby.
