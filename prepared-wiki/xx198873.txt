[[Priscilla (album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2003 albums, Priscilla (French singer) albums

Priscilla is the name given to the second album of the French singer Priscilla Betti. It was released in December 2002 and was supported by the two hit singles "Regarde-moi (teste-moi, déteste-moi)" and  "Tchouk tchouk musik" which reached the top ten in France. 
All songs are composed by Philippe Osman (music, arrangements) and Bertrand Châtenet (lyrics, mixing).
On the French Albums Chart, although the album was unable to enter the top ten, it was charted for 45 weeks, which remains to date Priscilla's longest chart trajectory. It eventually achieved Gold status.
