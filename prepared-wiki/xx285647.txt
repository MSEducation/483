[[Huitang, Ningxiang]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Ningxiang County

Huitang Town ([tpl]zh|s=灰汤镇|t=灰湯鎮|p=Huītāng Zhèn[/tpl]) is an urban town in Ningxiang County, Changsha City, Hunan Province, People's Republic of China. It's surrounded by Fengmuqiao Township on the northwest, Xieleqiao Town on the northeast, and Jinsou Township on the south. As of the 2000 census it had a population of 20,559 and an area of 43 square kilometers.

==Cityscape==

The Town is divided into six villages and one community, the following areas: Tangquan Community (汤泉社区), Quanjiang Village (泉江村), Huaguo Village (花果村), Ningnan Village (宁南村), Xinlian Village (新联村), Shuangjian Village (双建村), Huitang Village (灰汤村).
