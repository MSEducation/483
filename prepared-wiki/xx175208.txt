[[KADV]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in California, Religious radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 1989, Stanislaus County, California, 1989 establishments in California

KADV (90.5 FM) is a non-commercial educational radio station licensed to serve Modesto, California, USA. The station is owned by the Central Valley Christian Academy and the broadcast license is held by the Modesto Adventist Academy.
KADV broadcasts a religious radio format.[tpl]cite web |work=Arbitron |url=http://www.arbitron.com/radio_stations/station_information.htm |title=Station Information Profile |accessdate=June 25, 2009[/tpl]

==History==

This station received its original construction permit from the Federal Communications Commission on July 6, 1987.[tpl]cite web |publisher=FCC Media Bureau |title=Application Search Details (BPED-19840221AN) |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/app_det.pl?Application_id=66676 |date=July 6, 1987[/tpl] The new station was assigned the KADV call sign by the FCC on August 26, 1987.[tpl]cite web |title=Call Sign History |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=43333&Callsign=KADV |publisher=FCC Media Bureau CDBS Public Access Database |accessdate=June 25, 2009[/tpl] KADV received its license to cover from the FCC on June 6, 1989.[tpl]cite web |publisher=FCC Media Bureau |title=Application Search Details (BLED-19890105KA) |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/app_det.pl?Application_id=122311 |date=June 6, 1989[/tpl]
