[[Van Buren, Tennessee]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Hardeman County, Tennessee, Unincorporated communities in Tennessee

Van Buren is an unincorporated community in west-central Hardeman County, Tennessee, United States. Although it is unincorporated, it shares a ZIP code of 38042 with Hickory Valley.http://zipinfo.com/cgi-local/zipsrch.exe?zip=38042&Go=Go Van Buren's exact location is at the intersection of Van Buren Road and Lake Hardeman Road in Hardeman County.
