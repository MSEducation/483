[[HMS Sandringham (M2791)]]

CATEGORIES: Ham-class minesweepers, 1957 ships, Clyde-built ships, 1957 in Scotland, History of Renfrewshire, Economy of Renfrewshire

HMS ''Sandringham'' was one of 93 ships of the [tpl]sclass|Ham|minesweeper|0[/tpl] of inshore minesweepers.
Their names were all chosen from villages ending in -ham. The minesweeper was named after Sandringham in Norfolk.
