[[Utica Public Library]]

CATEGORIES: Library buildings completed in 1903, Utica, New York, Libraries on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Neoclassical architecture in New York, Buildings and structures in Utica, New York

Utica Public Library is a historic library building located in Utica in Oneida County, New York. It is a rectangular five story Neoclassical style structure, constructed of New Haven brick on a limestone foundation.  It features a central pedimented pavilion with Corinthian order columns.
