Harry Steel (April 18, 1899 – October 8, 1971) was an American wrestler and Olympic champion. He competed at the 1924 Olympic Games in Paris, where he won a gold medal in freestyle heavyweight.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/st/harry-steel-1.html |title=Harry Steel Olympic Results |accessdate=2013-09-15 |work=sports-reference.com[/tpl] He did not make the US team at first, but the man who was supposed to go couldn't.
