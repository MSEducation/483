Inza ([tpl]lang-ru|И́нза[/tpl]) is a town and the administrative center of Inzensky District of Ulyanovsk Oblast, Russia, located on the Syuksyumka River (Sura's basin) [tpl]convert|167|km|sp=us[/tpl] southwest of Ulyanovsk. Population: [tpl]ru-census|p2010=18,803|p2002=20,288|p1989=23,509[/tpl]

==History==

It was founded in 1897 as a settlement around a railway station. Urban-type settlement status was granted to it in 1938; town status was granted in 1946.
