[[Temnikovsky District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Mordovia

Temnikovsky District ([tpl]lang-ru|Те́мниковский райо́н[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-mdf|Темникавонь район[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-myv|Чополтбуе[/tpl]) is an administrativeConstitution of the Republic of Mordovia and municipalLaw #124-Z district (raion), one of the twenty-two in the Republic of Mordovia, Russia. It is located in the northwest of the republic. The area of the district is [tpl]convert|1936.8|km2|sp=us[/tpl].[tpl]citation needed|date=October 2012[/tpl] Its administrative center is the town of Temnikov.Law #7-Z Population: [tpl]ru-census2010|17,261|;[/tpl] 21,172 (2002 Census);[tpl]ru-pop-ref|2002Census[/tpl] [tpl]su-census1989|26,782|.[/tpl] The population of Temnikov accounts for 42.0% of the district's total population.
