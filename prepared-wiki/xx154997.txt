[[Mirganj, Uttar Pradesh]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Bareilly district

Mirganj is a town and a nagar panchayat in Bareilly district in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh.

==Geography==

Mirganj is located at [tpl]Coord|28.541|N|79.208|E|[/tpl]. It has an average elevation of 170 metres (557 feet).

==Demographics==

[tpl]As of|2001[/tpl] India census,[tpl]GR|India[/tpl] Mirganj had a population of 13,336. Males constitute 53% of the population and females 47%. Mirganj has an average literacy rate of 44%, lower than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 52%, and female literacy is 35%. In Mirganj, 16% of the population is under 6 years of age.
