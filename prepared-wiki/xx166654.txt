[[Danny Sullivan (ice hockey)]]

CATEGORIES: 1947 births, Canadian ice hockey goaltenders, Ice hockey people from British Columbia, Philadelphia Blazers players, Vancouver Blazers players, Living people

Danny Sullivan (born May 31, 1947 in Kimberley, British Columbia) is a former ice hockey goaltender who played in the World Hockey Association for the Philadelphia Blazers and the Vancouver Blazers.
In his WHA career, Sullivan appeared in only two games. He won his Philadelphia start, defeating the Los Angeles Sharks 4-3 on November 11, 1972. On March 21, 1974 he lost his only start with the Vancouver Blazers 7-1 to the New England Whalers.
