[[Isuzu Journey-K]]

CATEGORIES: Isuzu vehicles, Buses

The Isuzu Erga Journey-K (kana:いすゞ・ジャーニーK) is a midibus built by Isuzu. The range was primarily available as city bus and tourist coach.
