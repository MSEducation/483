[[Price channels]]

CATEGORIES: Chart overlays

A price channel is  a pair of parallel trend lines that form a chart pattern for a stock or commodity.Murphy, pages 80-85  Channels may be horizontal, ascending or descending.  When prices pass through and stay through a trendline representing support or resistance, the trend is said to be broken and there is a "breakout".Murphy, pages 400-401
