[[Alexander Falls]]

CATEGORIES: Waterfalls of British Columbia, Sea-to-Sky Corridor

Alexander Falls is a waterfall on Madeley Creek, a tributary of Callaghan Creek in the Callaghan Valley area of the Sea to Sky Country of southwestern British Columbia, Canada.  The falls are located just below a bridge on the access road to Callaghan Lake Provincial Park, at the head of the valley, which lies to the west of the resort town of Whistler.[tpl]cite bcgnis|id=34794|title=Alexander Falls[/tpl]Waterfalls of the Pacific Northwest: Alexander Falls
The falls consist of three drops that total up to [tpl]convert|141|ft|m|0[/tpl] in total.  The falls are about [tpl]convert|40|ft|m|0[/tpl] wide.
