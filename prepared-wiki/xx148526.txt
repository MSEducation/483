[[Gold Hill, North Carolina]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Rowan County, North Carolina, Unincorporated communities in North Carolina

Gold Hill is a small unincorporated community in southeastern Rowan County, North Carolina near the Cabarrus County line. It is situated near the Yadkin River and is served by U.S. Highway 52 and Old Beatty Ford Road. Gold was found in this small town outside of Salisbury in the 19th century.google
