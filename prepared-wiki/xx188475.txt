[[Ica District]]

Ica District is one of fourteen districts of the province of Ica in region of Ica within Peru.[tpl]es icon[/tpl] Instituto Nacional de Estadística e Informática. Banco de Información Distrital. Retrieved April 11, 2008.
The Province capital city of Ica is located in this district. Ica is also the capital of the entire Region of Ica.  Also, close by is the tourist oasis village of Huacachina.
