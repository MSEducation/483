[[River Clydach (Neath)]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of Neath Port Talbot

The River Clydach is a short river in the county borough of Neath Port Talbot, South Wales. It runs runs generally southwards from the western slopes of Mynydd Marchywel for about 9km and past Fforest Goch, Bryn Coch and Neath Abbey to join the River Neath near Neath.Ordnance Survey 1:25,000 scale Explorer map 165 Swansea
