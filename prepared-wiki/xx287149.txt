[[Ahmad Kandi]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Ahmad Kandi ([tpl]lang-fa|احمدكندي[/tpl], also Romanized as Aḩmad Kandī; also known as Akhmadkandi)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3052049|Ahmad Kandi[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Bala Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 894, in 209 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
