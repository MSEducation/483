[[WFST]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Maine, Christian radio stations in the United States, Moody Radio affiliate stations, Caribou, Maine, Radio stations established in 1956, 1956 establishments in Maine

WFST (600 AM) is a radio station licensed to Caribou, Maine, USA.  The station is currently owned by Northern Broadcast Ministries.
