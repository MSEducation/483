[[Suvarte Soedarmadji]]

CATEGORIES: 1915 births, 1979 deaths, Indonesian footballers, Indonesia international footballers, Association football forwards, 1938 FIFA World Cup players

Suvarte Soedarmadji (6 December 1915 – 1979) was an Indonesian football forward who played for the Dutch East Indies in the 1938 FIFA World Cup.Copa do Mundo da FIFA França 1938 He also played for HBS Soerabaja.
