[[Arthur Dock Johnson]]

CATEGORIES: 1982 births, Living people, Basketball players from New Jersey, American basketball players, New Jersey Nets players

Arthur Dock Johnson (born December 20, 1982 in Detroit, Michigan) is an American basketball player.
A graduate of Pershing High School in Detroit, Johnson played four seasons at the University of Missouri before declaring for the 2004 NBA Draft. He was not selected, but did sign with the New Jersey Nets at the start of the 2005-06 NBA season, appearing in three games before being released.
