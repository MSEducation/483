[[Gmina Ciepielów]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Lipsko County

Gmina Ciepielów is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Lipsko County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Ciepielów, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|12|km|mi|0[/tpl] north-west of Lipsko and [tpl]convert|115|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|135.3|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 5,789.

==Villages==

Gmina Ciepielów contains the villages and settlements of Antoniów, Anusin, Bąkowa, Bielany, Borowiec, Chotyze, Ciepielów, Ciepielów-Kolonia, Czarnolas, Czerwona, Dąbrowa, Drezno, Gardzienice-Kolonia, Kałków, Kochanów, Kunegundów, Łaziska, Marianki, Nowy Kawęczyn, Pasieki, Pcin, Podgórze, Podolany, Ranachów B, Rekówka, Sajdy, Stare Gardzienice, Stary Ciepielów, Świesielice, Wielgie and Wólka Dąbrowska.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Ciepielów is bordered by the gminas of Chotcza, Iłża, Kazanów, Lipsko, Rzeczniów, Sienno and Zwoleń.
