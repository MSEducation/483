[[1995 Thriftway ATP Championships]]

CATEGORIES: 1995 Thriftway ATP Championships, 1995 ATP Tour, Cincinnati Masters, 1995 in American sports

The 1995 Cincinnati Open, known by the corporate title of the Thriftway ATP Championships was a tennis tournament played on outdoor clay courts that was part of the Super 9 of the 1995 ATP Tour. It took place in Cincinnati, Ohio, USA, from August 7 through August 14 1995.
