[[Ibaraichi Station]]

CATEGORIES: Geibi Line, Stations of West Japan Railway Company in Hiroshima city, Railway stations opened in 1915

[tpl]nihongo|Ibaraichi Station|井原市駅|Ibaraichi-eki[/tpl] is a JR West Geibi Line station located in Ibara, Shiraki-chō, Asakita-ku, Hiroshima, Hiroshima Prefecture, Japan.  This is the final of fifteen Geibi Line station within the boundaries of the city of Hiroshima.

==History==

==Station building and platforms==

Ibaraichi Station features one raised, station-side platform, capable of handling one line.  Due to previously handling large amounts of freight traffice, the train yard is very large.  The station building is an unusual shape due to countless renovations and additions since being opened.  It is an older-style wooden building with a tile roof. Ibaraichi Station is currently an unmanned station.
