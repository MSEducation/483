[[Susan Musgrave]]

CATEGORIES: 1951 births, Living people, Canadian children's writers, Canadian women novelists, Canadian women poets

Susan Musgrave (born March 12, 1951) is a Canadian poet and children's writer. She was born in Santa Cruz, California to Canadian parents, and currently lives in British Columbia, dividing her time between Sidney and Haida Gwaii.
Musgrave is married to Stephen Reid, a writer, convicted bank robber and former member of the infamous band of thieves known as the Stopwatch Gang. Their relationship was chronicled in 1999 in the CBC series Life and Times.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cbc.ca/lifeandtimes/musgrave_reid.htm|title=The Poet and the Bandit: The Life and Times of Susan Musgrave and Stephen Reid|publisher=CBC|accessdate=2009-12-30[/tpl]

She currently teaches creative writing in the University of British Columbia's Optional Residency Master of Fine Arts Program.
