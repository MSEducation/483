[[Roberto Polo]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 births, Living people, People from Barranquilla, Colombian footballers, Colombia international footballers, La Equidad footballers, Expatriate footballers in Ecuador, Expatriate footballers in Costa Rica, Llaneros F.C. players

Roberto Polo (born December 21, 1980 in Barranquilla) is a Colombian football striker. He currently is free agent. 
After coming out of almost nowhere and having a great season with Equidad has recently got him a call up for a friendly game against Venezuela on April 30 of 2008. He played the whole game and scored in only 10 minutes into his debut.
