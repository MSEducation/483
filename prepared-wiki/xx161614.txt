[[Ben James-Ellis]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, British male stage actors, People from Scarborough, North Yorkshire, 1988 births

Benjamin James Ellis (born 22 December 1988 in Scarborough, North Yorkshire) is an English stage and script actor who starred in the role of Link Larkin in the West End production of the musical Hairspray from its opening in October 2007 until July 2009. He is currently working aboard Cunard's RMS Queen Mary 2. He is also starring in Fireball XL5, along with Scotland's Rachel Wyper, Italy's Santino Marella, Japan's Yoshi Tatsu and Ghana's Kofi Kingston [ref]
