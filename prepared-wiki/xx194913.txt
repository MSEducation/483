[[Galmihorn]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of the Alps, Alpine three-thousanders, Mountains of Switzerland, Mountains of Valais

The Galmihorn is a mountain of the Bernese Alps, overlooking the Fiescher Glacier in the canton of Valais. The main summit is distinguished by the name Vorderes Galmihorn and has an elevation of 3,505 metres. A secondary summit named Hinteres Galmihorn has an elevation of 3,486 metres.
The closest locality is Reckingen on the eastern side.
