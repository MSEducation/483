Johnny Ballantyne  was an early twentieth century Scottish football (soccer)  inside forward who played professionally in Scotland, England and the United States.
Ballantyne began his career with Partick Thistle in 1921.  In 1924, after signing a one season contract with Thistle including a signing bonus, he moved to the United States where he signed with the Boston Soccer Club of the American Soccer League.The Year in American Soccer – 1925  This nearly led to the suspension of the United States Football Association, an action prevented by stronger agreements between FIFA member associations on player transfer rules.
