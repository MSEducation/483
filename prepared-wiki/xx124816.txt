[[Dunstan High School]]

CATEGORIES: Secondary schools in Otago, Educational institutions established in 1962

Dunstan High School is a state secondary school located in Alexandra, in the Central Otago district in the South Island of New Zealand.
Dunstan High School also runs the Tititea Outdoor Education Centre, which is located in the old homestead in the East Matukituki Valley on the outskirts of the Mount Aspiring National Park. 
In 2009, the school installed an $800,000 clean-burning heating system, replacing a coal-fuelled system,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.odt.co.nz/your-town/alexandra/45773/alexandra-school-switches-green-energy|title=Alexandra school switches to green energy|last=Van Kempen|first=Lynda |date=3 March 2009|work=Otago Daily Times|accessdate=2009-03-02[/tpl] and constructed a new $2.3 million gymnasium.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.odt.co.nz/your-town/alexandra/70863/gymnasium-progress|title=Gymnasium progress|last=Manins|first=Rosie|date=22 August 2009|publisher=Otago Daily Times|accessdate=2009-08-21[/tpl]
