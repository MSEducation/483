[[St. Luke's Episcopal Church (Brockport, New York)]]

CATEGORIES: Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Episcopal churches in New York, Gothic Revival architecture in New York, Churches in Monroe County, New York

St. Luke's Episcopal Church is a historic Episcopal church complex located at Brockport in Monroe County, New York.  The complex consists of an 1855 Gothic Revival style church of Medina sandstone and 1903 Romanesque style parish hall.  The eastern chancel window features a tripartate composition executed in favrile glass by the Tiffany studios of New York.
