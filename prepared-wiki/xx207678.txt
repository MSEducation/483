[[Ribeira Grande, Cape Verde]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Cape Verde, Santo Antão, Cape Verde

[tpl]Other uses|Ribeira Grande (disambiguation)[tpl]![/tpl]Ribeira Grande[/tpl]

Ribeira Grande is the largest town of the Ribeira Grande Municipality on the island of Santo Antão, Cape Verde. It is situated near the outflow of the river Ribeira Grande into the Atlantic Ocean, in the northeastern part of the island.
