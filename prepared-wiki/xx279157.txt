[[Baghlujeh-ye Sardar]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Baghlujeh-ye Sardar ([tpl]lang-fa|باغلوجه سردار[/tpl], also Romanized as Bāghlūjeh-ye Sardār and Bāghlūjeh Sardār; also known as Bāghlūjah and Bagludzhakh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3054701|Baghlujeh-ye Sardar[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 293, in 73 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
