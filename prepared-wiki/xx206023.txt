[[Last of the Summer Wine (series 4)]]

CATEGORIES: Last of the Summer Wine series, 1977 television seasons, 1978 television seasons

Last of the Summer Wine's fourth series originally aired on BBC1 between 29 November 1979 and 4 January 1980.Bright and Ross (2000), pp. 172–174  All episodes from this series were written by Roy Clarke and produced and directed by Sydney Lotterby.Bright and Ross (2000), p. 164
The fourth series was released on DVD in region 2 as a combined box set with series 3 on 26 July 2004. A box set featuring just series 4 was released for region 1 on 9 September 2008.
