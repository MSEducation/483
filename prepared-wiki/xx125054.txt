[[Gomen Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Kōchi Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Gomen Station|後免駅|Gomen-eki[/tpl] is a crailway station located in Nankoku, Kōchi Prefecture, Japan. The station is on the Dosan Line of JR Shikoku and the Gomen Nahari Line (Asa Line) of Tosa Kuroshio Railway.
