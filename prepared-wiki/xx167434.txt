[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Awka]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic Church in Nigeria, Roman Catholic dioceses in Nigeria, Religious organizations established in 1977, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century, 1977 establishments in Nigeria

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Awka ([tpl]lang-la|Avkaën(sis)[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Awka, Anambra State in the Ecclesiastical province of Onitsha in Nigeria.
