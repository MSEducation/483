David James Stevens (born March 4, 1970 in Fullerton, California) is an American former Major League Baseball player.  A pitcher, Stevens played for the Minnesota Twins ([tpl]by|1994[/tpl]-[tpl]by|1997|97[/tpl]), Chicago Cubs ([tpl]by|1998[/tpl]), Cleveland Indians ([tpl]by|1999[/tpl]), and Atlanta Braves ([tpl]by|2000[/tpl]).
