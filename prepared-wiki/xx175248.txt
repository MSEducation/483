[[KSUB]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Utah, News and talk radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 1937, Talk radio stations in the United States, Cedar City, Utah

KSUB (590 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a News Talk Information format. Licensed to Cedar City, Utah, USA, the station is currently owned by Cherry Creek Radio. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=KSUB |title=KSUB Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl] They are also an affiliate of the BYU Cougars Sports Network.
