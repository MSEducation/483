[[DBL Defensive Player of the Year]]

CATEGORIES: European basketball awards, Dutch Basketball League awards

The Dutch Basketball League Best Defender of the Year is an award that is yearly given to the best defensive player in the DBL, the highest professional basketball league in the Netherlands. The award is handed out after the regular season since 2013.[tpl]Cite web | url = http://j-dus.com/beste-verdediger/ | title = Best Defender of the Year award | work = j-dus.com (history of Dutch basketball)[/tpl] The award is handed out by the FEB (Federatie Eredivisie Basketbal).
