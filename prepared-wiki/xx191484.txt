[[Koupéla Department]]

CATEGORIES: Departments of Burkina Faso, Kouritenga Province

Koupéla is a department or commune of Kouritenga Province in eastern Burkina Faso. Its capital lies at the town of Koupéla. According to the 1996 census the department has a total population of 50,785.Burkinabé government inforoute communale
