[[Nabil Baalbaki]]

CATEGORIES: Lebanese footballers, Lebanese expatriate footballers, Al-Ansar (Lebanon) players, 1978 births, Living people, Expatriate footballers in Syria

Nabil Baalbaki (born on May 27, 1978) is a Lebanese footballer who plays for Al-Akhaa Al-Ahli Aley in Lebanon.
== Biography == 
Baalabaki started his career with Tadamon sour and moved to Olympic Beirut in 2002 before joining Al-Ansar.
He is a midfielder wears shirt number 7.  He formerly represented the Lebanon national football team at international level.
