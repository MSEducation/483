CATEGORIES: Orders of magnitude (volume)

To help compare different orders of magnitude  this page lists volumes between 10−18 cubic meters and 10−17 cubic meters (1 femtolitre and 10 femtolitres). a litre is 10−3 cubic metres, so 10−18cubic metres is −15 litres, or one femtometre. See also volumes or capacities of other orders of magnitude.
