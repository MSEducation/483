[[Hylarana mortenseni]]

CATEGORIES: Hylarana, Amphibians of Cambodia, Amphibians of Thailand, Animals described in 1903

Hylarana mortenseni is a species of true frog in the genus Hylarana. It is native to Cambodia and Thailand.[ref]
