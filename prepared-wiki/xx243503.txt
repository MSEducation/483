[[John Wilkie (canoeist)]]

CATEGORIES: 1977 births, Australian canoeists, Canoeists at the 2000 Summer Olympics, Living people, Olympic canoeists of Australia

John Wilkie (born 24 March 1977 in Murwillumbah) is an Australian slalom canoer who competed from the mid-1990s to the mid-2000s. He finished in 21st place in the K-1 event at the 2000 Summer Olympics in Sydney after being eliminated in the qualifying round.
