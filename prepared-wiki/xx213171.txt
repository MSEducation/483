[[Wilkołaz]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Kraśnik County

Wilkołaz [tpl]IPAc-pl|w|i|l|'|k|o|ł|a|s[/tpl] is a village in Kraśnik County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It is the seat of the gmina (administrative district) called Gmina Wilkołaz. It lies approximately [tpl]convert|13|km|mi|0[/tpl] north-east of Kraśnik and [tpl]convert|32|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-west of the regional capital Lublin.
