[[Grizzly College of Education]]

CATEGORIES: Education schools in India, Universities and colleges in Jharkhand, Education in Jharkhand

Grizzly College of Education is a co-educational college for Bachelors of Education, located in Jhumri Telaiya, Jharkhand, India. It was founded in 1999 by Grizzly Charitable Trust. The College is affiliated with Vinoba Bhave University, Hazaribagh, and is recognized by NCTE, Bhubaneshwar.

==History==

Grizzly Vidyalaya was founded in 2009.

==General information==

The Grizzly College of Education, Telaiya Dam, has a sprawling campus of five acres. Telaiya Dam, fourteen kilometers  from Jhumri Teliaya.
