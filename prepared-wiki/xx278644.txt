[[Golchin, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Golchin ([tpl]lang-fa|گلچين[/tpl], also Romanized as Golchīn; also known as Galjīn, Gil’dzhin, Giljin, Goljīn, and Jeljīn)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3064642|Golchin[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 185, in 44 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
