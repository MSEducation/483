[[Sugar Creek (film)]]

CATEGORIES: 2007 films, American films, English-language films, 2000s thriller films, 2000s Western (genre) films, American Western (genre) films

Sugar Creek is a 2007 film. It is a supernatural western thriller, set in 1889 and filmed in Arkansas.

==Plot summary==

Sugar Creek follows Adam Stanton as he awakes, alone in a field and without his shoes. Setting out in search of help he finds the local townspeople to be afraid of him.
