[[This Is Gospel]]

CATEGORIES: Panic! at the Disco songs, 2013 singles

"This Is Gospel" is a song by the American rock band Panic! at the Disco. It was released as the second single from their fourth studio album Too Weird to Live, Too Rare to Die! on August 12, 2013. A music video for the song, directed by Daniel "Cloud" Campos, was also released on the same day. It peaked at number 86 on the Billboard Hot 100 singles chart and later impacted US alternative radio on February 4, 2014.
