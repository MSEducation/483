[[Teufel's Farm Strip]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in Washington County, Oregon, Transportation in Hillsboro, Oregon, Defunct airports in Oregon

Teufel's Farm Strip [tpl]Airport codes|||OR80[/tpl] was a private airport located southeast of Hillsboro in Washington County, Oregon, USA. In 2002 the property containing Teufel's Farm was rezoned to make way for the Arbor Homes Oregon Roses neighborhood.City of Hillsboro City Council Agenda March 5, 2002
