[[Metro Obrera]]

CATEGORIES: Mexico City metro stations, Railway stations opened in 1994

Metro Obrera is a station along Line 8 of the metro of Mexico City.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.metro.df.gob.mx/red/estacion.html?id=104|title=Obrera|language=Spanish|accessdate=31 July 2011[/tpl][tpl]Cite web|last=Archambault|first=Richard|url=http://mexicometro.org/metro/line8/obrera/|title=Obrera »  Mexico City Metro System|accessdate=31 July 2011[/tpl] The station is situated on Eje Central Lázaro Cárdenas. The station's logo is a construction worker's helmet framed with two gears. The name obrera comes from the Colonia Obrera neighbourhood where the station is located.
