[[Lineboro, Maryland]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Carroll County, Maryland, Unincorporated communities in Maryland

Lineboro is an unincorporated community located in northeastern Carroll County, Maryland, in the United States. The community was named for its location on the Mason-Dixon Line.

==History==

Much of the town was added to the National Register of Historic Places as the Lineboro Historic District in 1996.[tpl]NRISref|2008a[/tpl]
