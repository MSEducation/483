[[Hoseynabad, Zanjanrud]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Hoseynabad ([tpl]lang-fa|حسين اباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Ḩoseynābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795569|Hoseynabad[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 136, in 35 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
