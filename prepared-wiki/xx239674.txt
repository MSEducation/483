[[Magic Lore]]

CATEGORIES: 1972 racehorse births, 1975 racehorse deaths, Thoroughbred racehorses

Magic Lore (1972-1975) was an American thoroughbred racehorse best known for winning a lifetime earnings of $19,000 and suffering a fatal heart attack in a $40,000 Belmont Park race, where Ruffian and Foolish Pleasure's usual jockey Jacinto rode him in his last race.
