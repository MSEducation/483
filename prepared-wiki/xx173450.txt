[[Keshavarz F.C.]]

CATEGORIES: Defunct football clubs in Iran, Sport in Tehran

Keshavarz Football Club ([tpl]lang-fa|باشگاه فوتبال کشاورز[/tpl]) is a defunct Iranian football club that was based in Tehran, Iran. It was owned by the Ministry of Agriculture.
