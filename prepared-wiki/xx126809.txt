[[Ryan Thomas]]

CATEGORIES: 1984 births, Living people, Male actors from Manchester

Ryan James Thomas (born 10 June 1984 in Manchester) is an English actor best known for playing the character Jason Grimshaw on ITV's Coronation Street.

==Career==

Ryan has been nominated for various "Sexiest Male"-type awards. He also won the award for Rear of the Year in 2008.[tpl]cite web|author=April 30, 2005 |url=http://www.hecklerspray.com/british-soap-awards-sexiest-male-nominees/2005355.php |title=British Soap Awards - Sexiest Male Nominees |publisher=Hecklerspray.com |date=2005-04-30 |accessdate=2013-03-20[/tpl]
Besides playing Jason Grimshaw in Coronation Street, Ryan has also taken part in many programmes related to the soap opera.  In 2012, Ryan took part in a documentary called "Corrie goes to Kenya," alongside Brooke Vincent who plays Sophie Webster in the soap, Sue Cleaver, who plays his on-screen mother Eileen Grimshaw and Ben Price who plays Nick Tilsley. ==Personal life==

Ryan has two brothers who are twins. One of them is Adam Thomas who played Donte Charles in the BBC drama Waterloo Road and now plays Adam Barton in the ITV soap Emmerdale. Ryan was in a relationship with actress Tina O' Brien from 2003 until 2009. Their daughter Scarlett was born in 2008. In April 2014, it was revealed that he was dating British-Indian Actress Amy Jackson.[tpl]cite web|title=Amy Jackson reportedly having Love affair with Ryan Thomas|url=http://news.biharprabha.com/2014/04/amy-jackson-reportedly-having-affair-with-ryan-thomas/|work=IANS|publisher=news.biharprabha.com|accessdate=20 April 2014[/tpl]
