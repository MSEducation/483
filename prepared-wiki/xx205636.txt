[[Mistletoe School]]

CATEGORIES: Schools in Shasta County, California, Public elementary schools in California, Public middle schools in California

Mistletoe School is a public elementary school, built in 1961, with 584 students in grades K-8 located at 1225 Mistletoe Ln., Redding, California 96002 in Shasta County and is part of the Enterprise Elementary School District.Great Schools Page for School The school mascot is a bulldog.
