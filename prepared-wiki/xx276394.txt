[[Plácido Galindo]]

CATEGORIES: 1906 births, 1988 deaths, Peruvian footballers, Peru international footballers, Association football midfielders, Universitario de Deportes footballers, 1930 FIFA World Cup players

Plácido Reynaldo Galindo Pando http://www.footballzz.com/jogador.php?id=20909&epoca_id=59(born 9 March 1906 - death 22 October 1988) was a Peruvian football midfielder who played for Peru in the 1930 FIFA World Cup, where he also became the first ever player sent off in a World Cup Finals.1930 FIFA World Cup Uruguay He also played for Universitario de Deportes.
