[[Geoff Smith (athlete)]]

CATEGORIES: 1953 births, Athletes (track and field) at the 1980 Summer Olympics, Athletes (track and field) at the 1984 Summer Olympics, Olympic athletes of Great Britain, Living people, Providence College alumni, Boston Marathon winners

Geoffrey ("Geoff") Smith (born 24 October 1953) is a British long-distance runner who won the Boston Marathon in both 1984 and 1985.
