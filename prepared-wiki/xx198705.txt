[[George E. Edwards]]

CATEGORIES: Year of death missing, Kentucky State Thorobreds football coaches, 1918 births

George E. Edwards was the 11th head college football coach for the Kentucky State University Thorobreds located in Frankfort, Kentucky and he held that position for six seasons, from 1951 until 1956.  His career coaching record at Kentucky State was 27 wins, 27 losses, and 2 ties.  As of the conclusion of the 2007 season, this ranks him fourth at Kentucky State in total wins and eighth at Kentucky State in winning percentage (.500).Kentucky State University coaching records
