[[Horqin Right Middle Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Horqin Right Middle Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠷᠴᠢᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠪᠠᠷᠠᠭᠤᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠭᠠᠷᠤᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠳᠤᠮᠳᠠᠳᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Qorčin Baraɣun Ɣarun Dumdadu qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=科尔沁右翼中旗|t=科爾沁右翼中旗|p=kē'ěrqìn Yòuyì Zhōng Qí[/tpl]) is a county of Inner Mongolia, China. It is under the administration of Hinggan League. The local Mongolian dialect is Khorchin Mongolian.
