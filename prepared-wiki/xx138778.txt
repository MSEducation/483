[[Tony Skuse]]

CATEGORIES: Welsh darts players, Living people, 1962 births, People from Dolgellau, British Darts Organisation players

Tony Skuse is a retired Welsh darts player who competed in the 1980s. 
A winner of the 1981 British Gold Cup, Skuse reached the quarter finals of the 1982 British Professional, beating Tim Gould and Les Capewell before losing 5-0 to Eric Bristow who eventually won the tournament. He then competed in the 1983 BDO World Darts Championship but was defeated by Paul Lim in the first round.
