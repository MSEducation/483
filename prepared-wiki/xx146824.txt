Dr Saavedra was born to a Mexican-American mother in the border town of Naco, Sonora. He has two masters degrees from the Harvard School of Public Health; one in public health and the other in health policy management. In 2000, he founded the first Ambulatory Care AIDS Clinic in Mexico City, which was to become Mexico's largest care center for HIV positive people.
