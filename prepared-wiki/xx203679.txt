[[Transtage]]

CATEGORIES: Rocket stages

Transtage was an American upper stage used on Titan III rockets.[tpl]cite web|url=http://space.skyrocket.de/doc_stage/transtage.htm|title=Transtage|last=Krebs|first=Gunter|publisher=Gunter's Space Page|accessdate=2008-05-23[/tpl] Forty were launched, of which three failed.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.astronautix.com/stages/titstage.htm|title=Titan Transtage|last=Wade|first=Mark|publisher=Encyclopedia Astronautica|accessdate=2008-05-23[/tpl]
The first launch, boosted by a Titan IIIA carrier rocket, occurred on 1 September 1964. The Transtage failed to pressurise, resulting in premature engine cutoff, and a failure to reach orbit.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.astronautix.com/lvs/titan3a.htm|title=Titan 3A|last=Wade|first=Mark|publisher=Encyclopedia Astronautica|accessdate=2008-05-23[/tpl] The second launch, on 10 December, was successful. The last launch of a transtage occurred on 4 September 1989, boosted by a Titan 34D rocket.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.astronautix.com/lvs/titan34d.htm|title=Titan 34D|last=Wade|first=Mark|publisher=Encyclopedia Astronautica|accessdate=2008-05-23[/tpl]
