[[Gmina Ozimek]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Opole Voivodeship, Opole County

Gmina Ozimek is an urban-rural gmina (administrative district) in Opole County, Opole Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Its seat is the town of Ozimek, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|20|km|mi|0[/tpl] east of the regional capital Opole.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|126.5|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 20,989 (out of which the population of Ozimek amounts to 9,944, and the population of the rural part of the gmina is 11,045).

==Villages==

Apart from the town of Ozimek, Gmina Ozimek contains the villages and settlements of Antoniów, Biestrzynnik, Chobie, Dylaki, Grodziec, Jedlice, Krasiejów, Krzyżowa Dolina, Mnichus, Nowa Schodnia, Pustków, Schodnia and Szczedrzyk.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Ozimek is bordered by the gminas of Chrząstowice, Dobrodzień, Izbicko, Kolonowskie, Strzelce Opolskie, Turawa and Zębowice.
