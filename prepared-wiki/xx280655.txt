[[Ardin, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Ardin ([tpl]lang-fa|اردين[/tpl], also Romanized as Ardīn)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3053602|Ardin[/tpl] is a village in Bonab Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 154, in 33 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
