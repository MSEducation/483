[[Mikhail Krichman]]

CATEGORIES: 1967 births, Russian cinematographers, Living people

Mikhail Krichman (Михаил Владимирович Кричман; born 1967) is a Russian cinematographer who received a Golden Osella award at the 67th Venice Film Festival for Silent Souls.http://www.labiennale.org/en/cinema/archive/67th-festival/awards/ He photographed all of Andrey Zvyagintsev's films, including The Return (2003), The Banishment (2007), and Elena (2010).http://russiancinema.ru/template.php?dept_id=3&e_dept_id=1&e_person_id=5788 Zvyagintsev claims that Krichman (an engineer by profession) learned his craft by reading American Cinematographer.
