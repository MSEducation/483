[[Paul Taylor (musician)]]

CATEGORIES: American jazz saxophonists, Smooth jazz saxophonists, Las Vegas musicians, Living people, 1960 births

[tpl]BLP sources|date=May 2011[/tpl]Paul Taylor (born April 30, 1960) is an American smooth jazz saxophonist who has released eight albums since his debut On the Horn in 1995. He is signed to Peak Records. Although originally from Denver, Colorado, he is based in Las Vegas, Nevada.[tpl]cite news| last =Ollison| first =Rashod| title =Paul Taylor gives smooth jazz a good name | page =19T| publisher =Baltimore Sun| date =2005-03-17[/tpl] He also played as a special guest with the Rippingtons for a short time in 1999 and 2000, after Jeff Kashiwa left the group and before Eric Marienthal joined them.
He collaborated with many R&B vocalists such as LaToya London, Regina Belle, and Terry Dexter.
