[[1997 FINA World Junior Synchronised Swimming Championships]]

CATEGORIES: Synchronized swimming, 1997 in synchronized swimming

The 5th FINA World Junior Synchronised Swimming Championships was held June 29-July 3, 1997 in Moscow, Russia. The synchronised swimmers are aged between 15 and 18 years old from 27 nations, swimming in three events: Solo, Duet and Team.[tpl]cite book | url=http://books.google.fr/books?id=o-dD3p8eTYUC&lpg=PA227&hl=fr&pg=PA230#v=onepage&q&f=false | title=Synchronized Swimming: An American History | publisher=McFarland | author=Bean, Dawn Pawson | year=2005 | pages=312[/tpl]
