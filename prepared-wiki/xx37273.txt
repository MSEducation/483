[[Businessperson]]

CATEGORIES: Business and financial operations occupations, Businesspeople

A businessperson (plural businesspeople) is a business professional; especially one with a senior position (such as a corporate executive).[tpl]Cite web |url=http://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/businessperson |title=businessperson |website=m-w.com |publisher=Merriam-Webster |accessdate=20 January 2014[/tpl]
Gender-specific names for a businessperson are businessman and businesswoman. The word businessman was first attested in 1826;[tpl]Cite web |url=http://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/businessman |title=businessman |website=m-w.com |publisher=Merriam-Webster |accessdate=20 January 2014[/tpl] businesswoman in 1844;[tpl]Cite web |url=http://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/businesswoman |title=businesswoman |website=m-w.com |publisher=Merriam-Webster |accessdate=20 January 2014[/tpl] and businessperson in 1974.
An extraordinarily wealthy or powerful businessperson is called a business magnate or tycoon.[tpl]Cite web |url=http://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/tycoon |title=tycoon |website=m-w.com |publisher=Merriam-Webster |accessdate=20 January 2014[/tpl]
