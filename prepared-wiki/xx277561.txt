[[Cash For Clothes Xchange]]

CATEGORIES: Recycling, Reuse

Cash For Clothes Xchange is a privately owned, family-operated cash for clothes retailer that buys and resells used clothing. The company has over 20 stores [tpl]cite web|last=Cash For Clothes Xchange|title=Cash For Clothes Xchange Locations|url=http://www.clothes-xchange.co.uk/stores-collecting-areas[/tpl] across the UK.
