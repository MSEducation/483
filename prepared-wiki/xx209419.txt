[[Cedar Butte Township, Adams County, North Dakota]]

CATEGORIES: Defunct townships in North Dakota, Defunct townships in Adams County, North Dakota

Cedar Butte Township is a defunct civil township in Adams County, North Dakota, USA. The 1960 census recorded a population of 75.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www2.census.gov/prod2/decennial/documents/37749301v.1p36ch2.pdf | title =  Census of Population: 1960, North Dakota | format = PDF | publisher = United States Census Bureau | date = 1961 | accessdate = 2008-07-20[/tpl]
The township dissolved prior to the 1980 Census,[tpl]cite web | url = http://www2.census.gov/prod2/decennial/documents/1980a_ndABCD-01.pdf | title =  1980 Census of Population: North Dakota | format = PDF | publisher = United States Census Bureau | date = 1981 | accessdate = 2008-07-20[/tpl] when it was combined with Dakota, Jordan, Kansas City and Spring Butte Townships to form the Census-designated East Adams Unorganized Territory. As of the 1990 Census, the combined area had a population of 146.[tpl]cite web | url = http://factfinder.census.gov/servlet/DTTable?_bm=y&-context=dt&-ds_name=DEC_1990_STF1_&-CONTEXT=dt&-mt_name=DEC_1990_STF1_P001&-tree_id=100&-redoLog=true&-all_geo_types=N&-_caller=geoselect&-currentselections=DEC_1990_STF1_P001&-geo_id=06000US38001547&-search_results=01000US&-format=&-_lang=en | title = P001.
