[[Pir Marzban]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Pir Marzban ([tpl]lang-fa|پيرمرزبان[/tpl], also Romanized as Pīr Marzbān; also known as Pīrmazabān and Pīr Mehzābān)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3078341|Pir Marzban[/tpl] is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 776, in 174 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
