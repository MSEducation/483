[[Charles Luylier]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 births, Living people, French radio journalists

Charles Luylier is a French broadcast journalist, born in Angers on 5 January 1989.
He first came to public attention participating in televised debates, for example on Le Nouveau Journal on Direct 8. He once anchored Voyage au Bout de la Nuit on that network.https://twitter.com/CharlesLuylier
He then joined the team at Radio laser in Guichen (35).http://www.radiolaser.fr/Nicolas-Hulot-est-L-heritier-des-Pharaons_a6247.htmlhttp://www.radiolaser.fr/Skip-The-Use-est-dans-les-Loges-_a6230.html A year after that, in 2012, he joined RCF Email Limousin, where he presents the regional news every morning at 8.02.http://www.limoges-catholique.fr/index.php?page=article_liste&id=145&type=article
