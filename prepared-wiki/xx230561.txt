[[Hassan II of the Maldives]]

CATEGORIES: Sultans of the Maldives

Sultan Hassan II Sri dhanmaru bavana mahaa radun was the fifth Sultan to ascend the throne of Maldives from the Hilaaly Dynasty. He was the son of HH Maayin Ranna Badeyri.
