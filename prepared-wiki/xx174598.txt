[[KOLT-FM]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Wyoming

KOLT-FM (92.9 FM) The Boss is a radio station broadcasting a Hot Adult Contemporary format. Licensed to F. E. Warren Air Force Base, Wyoming, USA, the station serves the Cheyenne area. The station is currently owned by Tracy Broadcasting Corporation. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KOLT-FM |title=KOLT-FM Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==History==

The station was assigned the call letters KOLT-FM on 2007-02-07. It was known previously as a Regional Mexican and prior to that a Country Music station.
