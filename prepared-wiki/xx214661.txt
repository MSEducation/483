[[Gilbert Turner]]

CATEGORIES: 1888 births, 1968 deaths, Bishops of St Helena, Alumni of St Augustine's College, Canterbury

Gilbert Price Lloyd Turner was born on 8 May 1888 and died  in 1968. He was a Bishop in the Anglican church.

==Biography==

The son of Major G. H. Turner, he was educated at the Collegiate School, Victoria in British Columbia and at St Augustine's College, Canterbury. In 1912, he was ordained as a priest. He was a curate at St. Paul's Church, Cape Town from 1920 to 1922, and again from 1925 to 1927, then rector. In 1939, he was appointed Bishop of St. Helena in 1939.
