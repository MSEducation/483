[[Anders Göthberg]]

CATEGORIES: 1975 births, 2008 deaths, Musicians who committed suicide, Swedish rock guitarists, Suicides by jumping in Sweden

Anders Göthberg (9 October 1975 – 30 March 2008) was a Swedish guitarist.
Göthberg played guitar for Broder Daniel and Honey Is Cool. He lived his latter life in Stockholm with his girlfriend, artist Paola Bruna.
