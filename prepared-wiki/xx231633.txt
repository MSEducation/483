[[Anni Rossi]]

CATEGORIES: American female singers, Living people, American violists, Musicians from Minnesota, 4AD artists

Anni Rossi is an American singer,  violist and keyboardist from Minnesota. She graduated for the Perpich Center for Arts Education in Golden Valley. She is notable for her unique style of playing the viola and singing, often at the same time, although her solo records also incorporate elements of electronic music.
Her debut EP Afton, a six track affair consisting of previously released tracks, including 7" single "Wheelpusher", initially released via Too Pure[tpl]cite web|url=http://hmv.com/hmvweb/displayProductDetails.do?ctx=282;2;-1;-1&sku=759060 |title=live it |publisher=hmv |date=2005-12-15 |accessdate=2012-04-04[/tpl] and MySpace previews, was released in October 2008 through 4AD Records.[tpl]dead link|date=April 2012[/tpl] The majority of Afton was recorded at Bomb Shelter Studios, in Los Angeles by Ben Tierney in February, 2006.
Rossi recorded her mini-album, entitled Rockwell with Steve Albini at Electrical Audio in Chicago. The album was released in March 2009 through 4AD Records. An appearance at the South by Southwest (SXSW) music festival and a west coast tour followed in late winter/early spring of 2009.
