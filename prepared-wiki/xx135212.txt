[[George Sawaya]]

George Sawaya (August 14, 1923 â€“ September 17, 2003) was an American stuntman and actor.  He acted in almost one hundred different films and television episodes, but always as minor characters.  His career was relatively consistent from the 1950s to the 1980s.
Sawaya acted in the original Star Trek several times, first as Chief Humboldt in "The Menagerie, Part I" and then as Klingons in "Errand of Mercy" and "Day of the Dove".
