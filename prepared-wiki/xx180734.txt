[[Isaac Baron]]

CATEGORIES: American poker players, 1987 births, Living people, People from Menlo Park, California

Isaac Baron (born July 10, 1987) an American professional poker player from Menlo Park, California. He was the winner of the first Card Player Magazine Online Poker Player of the Year Award in 2007.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cardplayer.com/online-poker/players/839 |title=Isaac "westmenloAA" Baron |accessdate=2008-05-08 |work=CardPlayer.com [/tpl]

==Poker==

===Online poker===

Playing under the screen names of "westmenloAA" on PokerStars and "Isaac Baron" on Full Tilt Poker, Baron's online winnings exceeded $1,000,000 in 2007, including a win in the PokerStars weekly Sunday Million tournament in January for $254,000. He has also been able to translate his online success to live games. At a time when he was unable to play tournaments in the United States because of his age, he cashed four times in World Poker Tour and two European Poker Tour events held outside of the U.S.

====Full Tilt Online Poker Series (FTOPS)====

Baron under the screen name "the guru 11," won the first event of FTOPS VIII on May 8, 2008, earning $158,852.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cardplayer.com/poker-news/article/12006/isaac-the-guru-11-baron-wins-first-event-in-ftops-viii |title=Isaac 'the guru 11' Baron Wins First Event in FTOPS VIII |accessdate=2008-05-08 |last=Green |first=Shawn Patrick |date=2008-05-08 |work=CardPlayer.com [/tpl]
