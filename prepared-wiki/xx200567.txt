[[1994–95 A PFG]]

CATEGORIES: Bulgarian A Professional Football Group seasons, 1994–95 domestic association football leagues, 1994–95 in Bulgarian football

Statistics of Bulgarian A Professional Football Group in the 1994/1995 season.

==Overview==

It was contested by 16 teams, and PFC Levski Sofia won the championship. LEX Lovech, winners of the Bulgarian Second Division in the previous season, played in A PFG for the first time in their history.
