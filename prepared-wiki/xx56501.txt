[[Fully Completely]]

CATEGORIES: 1992 albums, The Tragically Hip albums, Albums produced by Chris Tsangarides, MCA Records albums

Fully Completely is the third full-length album by Canadian rock band The Tragically Hip. It reached #1 on the RPM Top 100 albums chart, their second album to do so.Top Albums/CDs - Volume 56, No. 19, November 07 1992 The album is listed at #5 on The Top 100 Canadian Albums by Bob Mersereau and #9 on The Top 102 Modern Rock Albums of All Time by 102.1 The Edge. The album was certified Diamond in Canada in January 2007.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.cria.ca/gold/0107_g.php|title=Canadian Recording Industry Association|year=2010|accessdate=2010-05-10[/tpl]
The album was released in October 1992 and produced by Chris Tsangarides. The cover art was designed by Dutch artist Lieve Prins.
Despite the Tragically Hip's great domestic success, this was the only album of theirs to be released in Japan. It was released there on April 21, 1993 with the catalog number of MVCM-360.
