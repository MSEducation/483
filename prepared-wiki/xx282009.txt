[[Bosnia and Herzegovina at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics, 2013 in Bosnia and Herzegovina, Athletics in Bosnia and Herzegovina

Bosnia and Herzegovina is scheduled to compete at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics in Moscow, Russia, from 10–18 August 2013.
A team of 2 athletes was announced to represent the country in the event.
