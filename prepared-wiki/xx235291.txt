Personal Profile : Peter Kronschnabl is the president of BMW India and has been
based in New Delhi since 2006. After completing an apprenticeship in commerce
he studied international business management at the Pforzheim University
of Applied Sciences. He started his career at BMW AG in Munich in 1995 in
the international management trainee program in sales and marketing. Having
completed the trainee program he worked as product and price manager for the
region Africa and the Caribbean between 1996 and 1998, was promoted to junior
area manager in 1998 and worked as area manager for the same region from 1999
till 2002. Before joining BMW India he was General Manager Market Development
for the region Asia, Pacific, Africa, Central and Eastern Europe.

==Success==

Under Peter's leadership, BMW India increased its market share in the luxury segment of the Indian automotive market to over 40% in 2009 (from 9% at the end of 2006).  With 3619 cars delivered to customers in the calendar year 2009, BMW India achieved the “highest sales by a manufacturer in the luxury car segment in a year” in the country till date.

==Personal life==

Peter Kronschnabl is unmarried. His interests are world history and in his leisure time he
enjoys jogging, golf, mountain biking and snowboarding.
