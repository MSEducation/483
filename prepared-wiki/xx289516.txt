[[Qias Kandi]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Qias Kandi ([tpl]lang-fa|قياسكندي[/tpl], also Romanized as Qīās Kandī)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3773310|Qias Kandi[/tpl] is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 418, in 78 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
