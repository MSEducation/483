[[Church of Iulia Constantia Zilil]]

CATEGORIES: Churches in Morocco, 3rd-century church buildings

The church of Iulia Constantia was an early Christian church in Morocco, located  in the Roman colony of Iulia Constantia Zilil. It had three naves, was equipped with a baptistery and had various appendices, close to the western door.
