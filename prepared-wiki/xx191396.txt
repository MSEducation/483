[[Ouargaye Department]]

CATEGORIES: Departments of Burkina Faso, Koulpélogo Province

Ouargaye  is a department or commune of Koulpélogo Province in eastern Burkina Faso. Its capital lies at the town of Ouargaye. According to the 1996 census the department has a total population of 28,603.Burkinabé government inforoute communale
