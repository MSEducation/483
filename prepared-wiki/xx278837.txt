[[Kabrik]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Kabrik ([tpl]lang-fa|كبريك[/tpl], also Romanized as Kabrīk; also known as Kaberak, Kavarik, and Kyavaryk)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3068236|Kabrik[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Pain Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 16, in 4 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
