[[Jami'a Nooriyya Arabic College]]

CATEGORIES: Islamic universities and colleges in India, Islam in India

Jamia Nooriyya Arabic College is a religious higher educational institute of Sunni Muslims of Kerala, India, founded by Ahamed Mohiyudheen Noorishah Jeelani.Samastha kerala jam-iyyathul ulama institutions It is situated at Pattikkad, near Perinthalmanna in Malappuram district of Kerala. The institute is running under Samastha Kerala Jamiyyathul Ulama. It celebrates its anniversary in February/March of every year.The Hindu news report of 43rd anniversary celebrationhttp://annoormagazine.yolasite.com
