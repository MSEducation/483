[[English cricket team in Zimbabwe in 1996–97]]

CATEGORIES: 1996 in Zimbabwean sport, 1997 in Zimbabwean sport, International cricket competitions from 1994–95 to 1997, English cricket tours of Zimbabwe, Zimbabwean cricket seasons from 1980–81 to 1999–2000, 1996 in English cricket, 1997 in English cricket

The English cricket team toured Zimbabwe for a two-match Test series and a three-match One Day International (ODI) series between 15 December 1996 and 3 January 1997.
