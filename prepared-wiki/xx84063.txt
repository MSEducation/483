[[Mochizuki, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Dissolved municipalities of Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Mochizuki|望月町|Mochizuki-machi[/tpl] was a town located in Kitasaku District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the town had an estimated population of 10,440 and a density of 81.16 persons per km². The total area was 128.64 km².
On April 1, 2005 Mochizuki, along with the town of Usuda (from Minamisaku District), and the village of Asashina (also from Kitasaku District), was merged into the expanded city of Saku.
