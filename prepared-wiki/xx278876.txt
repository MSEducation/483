[[Rashtabad, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Rashtabad ([tpl]lang-fa|رشت اباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Rashtābād and Reshtabad)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3196516|Rashtabad[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Pain Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 128, in 23 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
