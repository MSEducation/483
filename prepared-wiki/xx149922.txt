[[Estonia at the 1992 Winter Olympics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 1992 Winter Olympics, Estonia at the Olympics, 1992 in Estonian sport

Estonia competed at the 1992 Winter Olympics in Albertville, France.
It was the first time since 1936 that the nation had competed as an independent nation at the Winter Olympic Games.  Estonian athletes competed for the Soviet Union from 1956 to 1988.Estonian National Olympic Representative was Tiit Nuudi and Estonian Olympic Team attach√© was Ene Balder.
