[[Chataz, Ijrud]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Chataz ([tpl]lang-fa|چتز[/tpl], also Romanized as Chetez; also known as Chhātaz)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3058193|Chataz[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Bala Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 741, in 167 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
