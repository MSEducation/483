[[Caloosahatchee Manuscripts]]

CATEGORIES: Sculptures by Jim Sanborn, Bronze sculptures in Florida, 2001 sculptures

Caloosahatchee Manuscripts is the title of a sculpture designed by American artist Jim Sanborn.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.elonka.com/kryptos/sanborn.html|title=Jim Sanborn: Sculptor, Photographer, Artist|publisher=Elonka|accessdate=2009-10-18[/tpl]It is currently located at the Old Post Office Building in Fort Myers, Florida.

==Medium==

The sculpture is bronze with text cut from a water jet cutter, and a pinpoint light source.
