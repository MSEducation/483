[[3603 Gajdušek]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Asteroids named for people, Astronomical objects discovered in 1981

3603 Gajdušek is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1505.3160235 days (4.12 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=3603 |  title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-04-28 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on September 5, 1981.
Named in memory of Vilém Gajdušek, Czech optician and telescope maker.
