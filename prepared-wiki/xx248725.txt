[[Clash of the Titans (video game)]]

CATEGORIES: 2010 video games, Game Republic games, Namco Bandai games, PlayStation 3 games, Video games based on films, Xbox 360 games

Clash of the Titans is a 2010 video game for the PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360 based on the film of the same name.

==Gameplay==

The game features hack and slash combat. Some of the fights end with quick time sequences. The player can use points to upgrade their weapons. In some sections of the game the player has their weapons removed and has to fight by hand. The gameplay features frequent loading times.[tpl]cite news|author=Nicky Woolf |url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/technology/gamesblog/2010/may/28/clash-of-the-titans-review |title=Clash of The Titans for Xbox 360 and PS3 | Game review | Technology | guardian.co.uk |publisher=Guardian |date= 28 May 2010|accessdate=2 September 2010 | location=London[/tpl] The player can perform both light and heavy attacks and can hold buttons to release special attacks. The player can wield swords, bones, bows, and scorpion tails. The enemies have health bars with colors to show how much health they have left.[tpl]cite web|url=http://uk.gamespot.com/ps3/action/clashofthetitans/review.html?om_act=convert&om_clk=gssummary&tag=summary%3Bread-review |title=Clash of the Titans Review for PlayStation 3 - GameSpot |publisher=Uk.gamespot.com |date=28 May 2010 |accessdate=2 September 2010[/tpl]

==Development==

The game was developed by Game Republic and published by Namco Bandai Games. The game was originally going to be published by Brash Entertainment,[tpl]cite web|url=http://uk.gamespot.com/news/6200743.htm |title=Video Game News - Video Game Articles - Video Game Information |publisher=Uk.gamespot.com |date= |accessdate=2 September 2010[/tpl] before it was picked up by Namco.[tpl]cite web|last=Sinclair |first=Brendan |url=http://uk.gamespot.com/ps3/action/clashofthetitans/news.html?sid=6232576&mode=news |title=Clash of the Titans descending next spring - PlayStation 3 News at GameSpot |publisher=Uk.gamespot.com |date=28 May 2010 |accessdate=2 September 2010[/tpl]
