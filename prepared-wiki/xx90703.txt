[[Pasco Region]]

CATEGORIES: Pasco Region, Regions of Peru

Pasco is a region in central Peru. Its capital is Cerro de Pasco.

==Political division==

The region is divided into 3 provinces ([tpl]lang-es|provincias[/tpl], singular: [tpl]lang|es|provincia[/tpl]), which are composed of 28 districts (distritos, singular: distrito).
