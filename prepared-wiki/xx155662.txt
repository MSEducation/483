[[Parnassius nordmanni]]

CATEGORIES: Parnassius

Parnassius nordmanni is a high altitude butterfly which is found the Caucasus.It is a member of the Snow Apollo genus Parnassius of the Swallowtail (Papilionidae) family. 
The larva feeds on Corydalis C. alpestris, C. conorhiza, C. emanueli
