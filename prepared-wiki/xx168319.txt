[[Graphic Arts Monthly]]

CATEGORIES: American business magazines, Professional and trade magazines, Monthly magazines, Publications established in 1929

Graphic Arts Monthly (ISSN-1047-9325) was a trade publication and web site owned by Reed Business Information serving the information needs of the printing industry, including printers and trade plants.
The editor-in-chief was Bill Esler, with the editorial offices located in Oak Brook, Illinois, USA.
As the name implies, Graphic Arts Monthly had been published monthly since its establishment in 1929. Several common articles includd Best Track, Ink Etc., Mail Call, and Sales Call. 
In September, Graphic Arts Monthly published The Official Show Daily, which was produced on behalf of the Graphic Arts Show Company for the annual Graph Expo & PackPrint. 
As of June 2008, total BPA audited circulation was 70,100 subscribers.
