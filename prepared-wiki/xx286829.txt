[[Garneh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Garneh ([tpl]lang-fa|گرنه[/tpl]; also known as Karnaj, and Karneh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3063645|Garneh[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 71, in 21 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
