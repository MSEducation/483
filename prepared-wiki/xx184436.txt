[[Krostoszowice]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Wodzisław County

Krostoszowice [tpl]IPAc-pl|k|r|o|s|t|o|sz|o|'|w|i|c|e[/tpl] is a village in Gmina Godów, Wodzisław County, Silesian Voivodeship, southern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) – TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It has a population of 988 and lies near the border with the Czech Republic. It is approximately [tpl]convert|4|km|mi|0[/tpl] north of Godów, [tpl]convert|5|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south of Wodzisław Śląski, and [tpl]convert|50|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-west of the regional capital Katowice.
