[[KZHR]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Washington (state), Radio stations established in 1982

KZHR (92.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Regional Mexican format. Licensed to Dayton, Washington, USA, the station serves the Tri-Cities and Walla Walla areas.  The station is owned by Ccr-Tri Cities Iv, LLC.
