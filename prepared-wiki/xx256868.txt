[[Tiradentes (São Paulo Metro)]]

CATEGORIES: São Paulo Metro stations

Tiradentes is a station on Line 1 (Blue) of the São Paulo Metro. It's placed nearby ESCOLA TÉCNICA ESTADUAL DE SÃO PAULO, or ETESP (São Paulo's State Technical School), near access to the ROTA headquarters and for the Tiradentes Avenue.  It has two accesses toward it.Information about Tiradentes on the São Paulo Metro website (Portuguese)
