[[Got Any Gum?]]

CATEGORIES: Joe Walsh albums, 1987 albums, Albums produced by Terry Manning

Got Any Gum? is the eighth studio album by Joe Walsh. the album was released in 1987, on Full Moon. This album features the song, "In My Car", which was co-written with Ringo Starr (and was originally released on Starr's solo album Old Wave, in 1983).

==Critical reception==

Writing for AllMusic, critic James Chrispell wrote of the album "There's nothing sadder than seeing a talented artist come up with a real bummer, but that's just what this record is."
