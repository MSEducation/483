[[Roshnai Gate]]

CATEGORIES: Gates of Lahore, Mughal architecture, Islamic architecture

The Roshnai Gate ([tpl]lang-ur|روشنائی دروازہ[/tpl]) is one of the thirteen gates within the Walled City of Lahore in Lahore, Punjab, Pakistan. Its extended height and width is testament to its use by emperors' caravans of elephants.
