[[Mohammed El Moustaoui]]

CATEGORIES: Berber poets, Moroccan writers, Moroccan songwriters, Living people, People from Souss-Massa-Drâa

Mohamed El Moustaoui is a Moroccan, Berber poet and songwriter. He was one of the first modern writers to write in Tamazight.Aziz Kich, La littérature amazighe: oralité et écriture, spécificités et perspectives : actes du colloque international, ed. Institut royal de la culture amazighe, 2004, p. 217
He was born in 1943 in Mekzert, a village in Souss. He studied in Taroudant and Marrakech.
