[[Tetraselmis]]

CATEGORIES: Green algae, Algae genera

Tetraselmis is a genus of phytoplankton. Tetraselmis has a very high lipid level; their amino acids stimulate feeding in marine organisms.[tpl]Citation needed|date=July 2013[/tpl] Tetraselmis is green, motile, and usually grows 10 µm long x 14 µm wide.
The species T. convolutae is a symbiont of the acoelomate animal Symsagittifera roscoffensis (and can also live freely in the ocean).[tpl]citation | pmc = 2562460 | doi = 10.1186/1742-9994-5-14 |date=Sep 2008 | author = Semmler, H; Bailly, X; Wanninger, A | title = Myogenesis in the basal bilaterian Symsagittifera roscoffensis (Acoela) | volume = 5 | issue =  | pages = 14 | pmid = 18803837 | journal = Frontiers in zoology | url = http://www.frontiersinzoology.com/content/5//14 | format = Free full text [/tpl]
