[[U Know My "P"]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 albums, Naeto C albums

U Know My "P" is the first studio album by Lagos rapper Naeto C, released in 2008. Featured guests include Ikechukwu, Wande Coal and Saro Wiwa.  It was one of the most popular albums in Nigeria in 2008 selling over 1 million copies.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.mynaijanews.com/content/view/2094/246/|title=The Big Deal about Naeto C|accessdate=2009-10-18[/tpl]
