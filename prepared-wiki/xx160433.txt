[[Kanaltürk]]

CATEGORIES: Secularism in Turkey, Television stations in Turkey, Turkish nationalism

Kanaltürk is a TV channel in Turkey.
Founded by Tuncay Özkan in 2004, it was sold by Özkan in May 2008 to Koza İpek Holding. The company had been in financial difficulties, with tax debts of as much as $10m, and many employees not paid over the preceding seven months.Hurriyet Daily News, 14 May 2008, Deal props gov't boosters
