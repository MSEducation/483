William M. Leete, Jr. (born c. 1944) is a former American football and baseball player, coach, and college athletics administrator.  He was the head coach of Hofstra University's football team from 1975 to 1980.[tpl]cite news|url=http://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=2519&dat=19741129&id=w_FdAAAAIBAJ&sjid=Q18NAAAAIBAJ&pg=6025,5852948|title=Leete is named Hofstra coach|date=November 29, 1974|publisher=Observer Reporter|accessdate=November 22, 2011[/tpl] He compiled a 30–23–1 overall record.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.gohofstra.com/fls/22200/pdf/HofstraFootballB.pdf|title=Hofstra Football Archive Book|year=2009|publisher=Office of Athletic Communications, Hofstra University|accessdate=November 22, 2011[/tpl] After leaving Hofstra, Leete went on to become the athletic director at the University of New Haven.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.nytimes.com/1981/02/24/sports/kwiatkowski-is-named-hofstra-s-football-coach.html|title=Kwiatkowski Is Named Hofstra's Football Coach|date=February 24, 1981|newspaper=The New York Times|accessdate=November 22, 2011[/tpl]
