Ljubo Baranin ([tpl]Lang-sr-cyr|Љубо Баранин[/tpl], born 25 August 1986 in Belgrade) is a Serbian footballer and former Serbian U21 international who plays for FK Bezanija. 

==Career==

Baranin began his career in the youth team of Železnik, before spells in the senior squads of Dorćol and Beograd. He made his name with Bežanija, breaking into the Serbian under-21 team during his time with the club. In August 2009 Baranin signed a one-year contract with Gabala FC.[tpl]cite web|title=«Габала» избрала метод оптовой закупки футболистов|url=http://extratime.echo.az/article.php?aid=7541|publisher=extratime|accessdate=16 April 2014|language=Russian[/tpl] Baranin went on to spend another 18months at Gabala before a short spell with Kapaz in 2012.
