[[Peter Danielson]]

CATEGORIES: Collective pseudonyms

Peter Danielson is the pen name used by the authors of a series of 19 books published by Bantam Books between 1984 and 1995.  The series, called Children of the Lion, is loosely based on Old Testament Biblical events.
The original "Peter Danielson" and creator of the series was George Warren. He wrote volumes #1 through #14. #15 was by Franklin King; Hugh Zachary took over the series with the next book and wrote #16, #17, and #18.
