[[Janet Wanja]]

CATEGORIES: 1984 births, Living people, Kenyan volleyball players, Volleyball players at the 2004 Summer Olympics, Olympic volleyball players of Kenya, People from Nairobi

Janet Wanja (born February 24, 1984 in Nairobi) is a female volleyball player from Kenya, who competed for her native country at the 2004 Summer Olympics in Athens, Greece, wearing the number #7 jersey. There she ended up in eleventh place with the Kenya women's national team.
Wanja went to Mukumu Girls high School.http://www.fivb.org/EN/volleyball/competitions/olympics/2004/women/Teams/VB_Player.asp?No=14310 She has played for Kenya Commercial Bank and Kenya Pipeline.
