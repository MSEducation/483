[[José María Lavalle]]

CATEGORIES: 1902 births, 1984 deaths, Peruvian footballers, Peru international footballers, Association football forwards, Alianza Lima footballers, 1930 FIFA World Cup players

José María Lavalle (born 21 April 1902 - death 7 July 1984) was a Peruvian football forward who played for Peru in the 1930 FIFA World Cup.1930 FIFA World Cup Uruguay He also played for Alianza Lima.
