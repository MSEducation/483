[[Harald Kolbeinson Guddal]]

CATEGORIES: 1798 births, 1887 deaths, Members of the Parliament of Norway, Sogn og Fjordane politicians

Harald Kolbeinson Guddal (1798–1887) was a Norwegian politician.
He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament in 1842, representing the rural constituency of Nordre Bergenhus Amt (today named Sogn og Fjordane). He worked as a farmer.Harald Kolbeinson Guddal — Norwegian Social Science Data Services (NSD) He served only one term.
He hailed from Oppstryn, and was deputy mayor in Stryn municipality from 1837.NRK Sogn og Fjordane County Encyclopedia
