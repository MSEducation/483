[[Brandon Miree]]

Brandon Miree (Born April 14, 1981 in Cincinnati, Ohio) is a former American football fullback in the National Football League. He was drafted by the Denver Broncos in the seventh round of the 2004 NFL Draft. He played college football at Pittsburgh.
Miree also played for the Green Bay Packers.

==College career==

Miree started his college football career at Alabama, but then transferred to the Pittsburgh.

==Professional career==

Miree was drafted in the seventh-round of the 2004 NFL Draft by the Denver Broncos. He signed with the Green Bay Packers before the 2006 season. On September 1, 2007 the Packers released him.
