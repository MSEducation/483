[[KJLT-FM]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Nebraska

KJLT-FM is a Christian radio station licensed to North Platte, Nebraska, broadcasting on 94.9 MHz FM.  The station is owned by Tri-State Broadcasting Association. KJLT fcc.gov. Accessed August 21, 2012
KJLT-FM plays a variety of Christian Music, as well as Christian Talk and Teaching programming including; Focus on the Family, Insight for Living with Chuck Swindoll, Breakpoint with Chuck Colson, Love Worth Finding with Adrian Rogers, and Joni and Friends. KJLT FM 94.9 KJLT. Accessed August 21, 2012

==Translators==

KJLT is also heard on translators throughout Nebraska and North-East Colorado.KJLT Translators KJLT. Accessed August 21, 2012
