[[Qarasuran]]

CATEGORIES: Law enforcement in Iran

Qarasuran,(Turkic: قره سوران/قراسوران) was the gendarmerie corps and  provincial security administration of Safavid dynasty and Qajar dynasty era of Iran. Majority of qarasuran members involved in protection and safety of the main roads.Ann K. S. Lambton; Landlord and peasant in Persia: a study of land tenure and land revenue, pg117Reza Ra'iss Tousi; The Persian Army, 1880, 1907: 'Qarasuran' or police for the protection of main routes
