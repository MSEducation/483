[[Joe Dawkins]]

CATEGORIES: 1948 births, Living people, Sportspeople from Los Angeles, California, American football running backs, Wisconsin Badgers football players, Houston Oilers players, Denver Broncos players, New York Giants players

Joseph Dawkins, III born January 27, 1948 in Los Angeles, California is a former American football running back in the National Football League for the Houston Oilers, Denver Broncos, and the New York Giants.  He played college football at the University of Wisconsin and was drafted in the tenth round of the 1970 NFL Draft.
