[[Texas Park Road 68]]

CATEGORIES: Park roads in Texas, Transportation in El Paso County, Texas

Texas Park Road 68 (Hueco Tanks State Park Road) is a state park road in Hueco Tanks State Historic Site in El Paso County, Texas.[tpl]Texas Mapbook|74|accessdate=2011-09-22[/tpl]

==Route description==

The southern terminus of Park Road 68 is at [tpl]Roadlink|state=TX|RM|2775[/tpl] at the southern boundary of Hueco Tanks State Historic Site.  It proceeds 2.0 miles northward from there, entirely within the state park.

==History==

Park Road 68 was established along its current route in 1974.
