[[Ind Coope Ground]]

CATEGORIES: Cricket grounds in Staffordshire

The Ind Coope Ground is a cricket ground in Burton, England where Derbyshire CCC played between 1938 and 1980. In most years, the ground would host at least one County Championship match. Staffordshire CCC used the ground between 1987 and 1991 for NatWest Trophy and Minor Counties Championship matches.
The ground hosted 38 first-class matches and 7 List A matches.
The ground is named after the former brewing firm Ind Coope.
