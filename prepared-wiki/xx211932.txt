[[Pet shipping]]

CATEGORIES: Pets

Pet shipping is an industry that involves transporting pets, often by plane. This service is commonly used when the animal's owner is moving house (in which case, in the United States, it is tax deductible.http://www.irs.gov/newsroom/article/0,,id=173193,00.html[tpl]dead link|date=November 2012[/tpl]http://www.irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/p521.pdf However, it can also be used when transporting animals for other reasons, to dog shows, for instance.
