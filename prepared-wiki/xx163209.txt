[[Michael Woodford, Jr.]]

CATEGORIES: 1981 births, American ice hockey forwards, Michigan Wolverines men's ice hockey players, Long Beach Ice Dogs (ECHL) players, Florida Panthers draft picks, Living people, People from Middlesex County, Massachusetts, Ice hockey people from Massachusetts

Michael Dean Woodford Jr. (born October 4, 1981 in Westford, Massachusetts) is an American professional ice hockey forward.

==Playing career==

He played college hockey at the University of Michigan for the Michigan Wolverines. After turning professional, he has played for the Long Beach Ice Dogs in the ECHL.
