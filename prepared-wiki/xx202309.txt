[[John Simpson (MP)]]

CATEGORIES: 1763 births, 1850 deaths, Members of the Parliament of Great Britain for English constituencies, Bridgeman family

John Simpson (13 May 1763 â€“ 5 June 1850) of Babworth Hall, Nottinghamshire was born John Bridgeman, a younger son of Sir Henry Bridgeman, later 1st Baron Bridgeman.  He assumed the surname and arms of Simpson by Act of Parliament.
He served as Member of Parliament for Wenlock from 1784 to 1785 (resigned) and again 1794-1820.
He married on June 3, 1784 Henrietta, daughter of Sir Thomas Worsley. After she died in 1791, he married Grace, daughter of Samuel Estwicke on November 27, 1793.Catalogue Note for the portrait by George Romney
