Esko Tapani Rechardt (born May 9, 1958 in Helsinki) is a Finnish sailor and Olympic champion. He won a gold medal in the Finn Class at the 1980 Summer Olympics in Moscow.1980 Summer Olympics – Moscow, Soviet Union – Sailing – databaseOlympics.com (Retrieved on May 21, 2008) 

==Biography==

Rechardt has a Master's degree in engineering, and after his sporting career has designed bridges, such as the bridge connecting the Korkeasaari zoo to Helsinki mainland.http://www.mtv3.fi/olympialaiset/olympialegendat.shtml
