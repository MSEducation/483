[[Durall B. Evans]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing, Year of death missing, Kentucky State Thorobreds football coaches

Durral B. Evans was the first head football coach for the Kentucky State University Thorobreds located in Frankfort, Kentucky and he held that position for three seasons, from 1921 until 1923.  His career coaching record at Kentucky State was 5 wins, 7 losses, and 0 ties.  As of the conclusion of the 2007 season, this ranks him 14th at Kentucky State in total wins and 14th at Kentucky State in winning percentage (.417).Kentucky State University coaching records
