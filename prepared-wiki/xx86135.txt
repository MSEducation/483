[[John Williams (Pitt County, North Carolina)]]

CATEGORIES: Members of the North Carolina House of Representatives, American farmers, 1735 births, Year of death missing, American planters

John Williams (born c.1735, date of death unknown) was a planter and an American Revolutionary from Pitt County, North Carolina. He was the son of a Welsh immigrant, and served on the county Committee of Correspondence. He represented his county in the North Carolina provisional Assembly of 1777 as well as the state's House of Commons in 1778 and 1779.
