[[Kurt Gimmi]]

CATEGORIES: Swiss male cyclists, Swiss Tour de France stage winners, 1936 births, 2003 deaths, Sportspeople from Zürich, Tour de Suisse stage winners

Kurt Gimmi (January 13, 1936 – March 29, 2003) was a Swiss professional road bicycle racer. He was born and died in Zürich and was a professional from 1958 until 1964. He won the eleventh stage of the 1960 Tour de France and the 1959 Tour de Romandie.
