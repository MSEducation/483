[[Sant Andreu de la Barca]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities in the Province of Barcelona, Baix Llobregat

Sant Andreu de la Barca ([tpl]IPA-ca|ˈsant ənˈdɾew ðə ɫə ˈβarkə[/tpl]) is a municipality in the comarca of the Baix Llobregat in Catalonia, Spain. It is situated on the right bank of the Llobregat river, on the main N-II road 
The main rail lines of the Llobregat corridor pass through the town, and all the 
FGC suburban services stop at the station.
