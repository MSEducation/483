[[DBL Statistical Player of the Year]]

CATEGORIES: Dutch Basketball League awards

The Dutch Basketball League Statistical Player of the Year is an award given to the player who had the best statistical season in the Dutch highest professional basketball league. The player with the highest Player efficiency rating receives the award from the FEB. The award is given after the regular season and was first handed out in 2003 to Travis Reed. No Dutch player has won the award so far, all winners have been players from the United States thus far.
