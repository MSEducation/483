[[Georges-Kévin N'Koudou]]

CATEGORIES: French footballers, Cameroonian footballers, French people of Cameroonian descent, People from Versailles, 1995 births, Living people, Ligue 1 players, FC Nantes players

Georges-Kévin Nkoudou Mbida (born 13 February 1995 in Versailles, France) is a French-Cameroonian footballer who plays for French side FC Nantes. He's a winger. He made his Ligue 1 debut on the opening game of the 2013/14 season at 11 August 2013 against SC Bastia.
