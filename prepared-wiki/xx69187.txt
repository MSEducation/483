[[Ophidiocephalus]]

CATEGORIES: Legless lizards, Reptiles of Australia, Monotypic reptile genera

Ophidiocephalus is a monotypic genus of lizards in the family Pygopodidae from Australia. The sole species is Ophidiocephalus taeniatus.[tpl]NRDB genus|genus=Ophidiocephalus|accessdate=24 December 2013[/tpl][tpl]ITIS|id=818062 |taxon=Ophidiocephalus Lucas and Frost, 1897[/tpl]
