[[Kingswood Elementary School (Nova Scotia)]]

CATEGORIES: Elementary schools in Nova Scotia, Educational institutions established in 2005, Schools in the Halifax Regional Municipality

Kingswood Elementary School located in the Kingswood subdivision of Hammonds Plains,  Nova Scotia is a non-denominational English and French Immersion institution. 
The school offers Primary to Grade 6 instruction and was arguably the first elementary school in the province designed for growth. It also has lunch supervisors.[tpl]citation needed|date=September 2012[/tpl]
The school has 27 classrooms, 10 for French immersion, 16 for the English program and one for core French language instruction.
Enrollment as of 2006 was 591 students.
Kingswood Elementary School is part of the Halifax Regional School Board.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.hrsb.ns.ca/tools/schoolfinder/findSchool.aspx?code=KWE | title=School Finder - Kingsway Elementary | publisher=Halifax Regional School Board | accessdate=September 27, 2012[/tpl]
