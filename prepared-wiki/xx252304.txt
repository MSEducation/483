Lena Kumar, or Lena Abhilash, known professionally as Lena,[tpl]cn|date=January 2014[/tpl] is an Indian film actress. She debuted in Jayaraj's Sneham which was followed by Karunam, Oru Cheru Punchiri, Varna Kazhchakal. Her breakthrough came through the critically acclaimed Traffic (2011) after which she acted in films such in Snehaveedu, Ee Adutha Kaalathu, and Spirit.She is no cry baby The Hindu Entertainment, Thiruvananthapuram Lena is a post-graduate in clinical psychology, and has worked as a clinical psychologist in Mumbai, before she quit her job to enter full-time acting.
