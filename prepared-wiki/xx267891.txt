[[Elachista amseli]]

CATEGORIES: Elachistidae, Animals described in 1833, Moths of Asia, Moths of Europe

Elachista amseli is a moth of the Elachistidae family. It is found in Croatia,[tpl]cite web|url= http://www.landesmuseum.at/pdf_frei_remote/ZOEV_18_0081-0082.pdf|title=Zwei neue Microlepidopteren aus Dalmatien|volume=18|issue=11-12|date=December 15, 1933|format=PDF[/tpl] Afghanistan, southern Tajikistan and Turkmenistan.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ekoi.lt/info/azl/1998/AZL%208%283%29_38-48.pdf|title=Contribution to the knowledge of Central Asiatic Microlepidoptera with Description of New Species|year=1998|volume=8|issue=3|issn=1392-1657|format=PDF[/tpl]
The wingspan is [tpl]convert|6.5|-|7|mm[/tpl]. The forewings are grey-brown to blackish brown, slightly mottled by a whitish base of scales. The hindwings are greyish brown. Adults have been recorded from late May to mid August.
