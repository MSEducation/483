[[Reyhan, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Reyhan ([tpl]lang-fa|ريحان[/tpl], also Romanized as Reyḩān and Rihān; also known as Rikhan)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3081056|Reyhan[/tpl] is a village in Mojezat Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 167, in 40 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
