[[Christopher Simonsen Fougner]]

CATEGORIES: 1795 births, 1869 deaths, People from Hole, Norway, Norwegian farmers, Members of the Parliament of Norway, Mayors of places in Buskerud

Christopher Simonsen Fougner (1795–1869) was a Norwegian politician.
He was elected to the Parliament of Norway in 1836, 1839, 1842, 1845 and 1851, representing the rural constituency Buskeruds Amt).[tpl]cite web|url=http://samfunnsveven.no/TT/U21.cfm?Utside=&Uttaksnummer=21&person=10843 |title=Christopher Simonsen Fougner|publisher=Norwegian Social Science Data Services (NSD)|language=Norwegian|accessdate=16 August 2011[/tpl] He was the mayor of Hole from 1840 to 1848.[tpl]cite web|url=http://domino.hole.kommune.no/hole/OSKSYS-B.NSF/fa3b0e93b5938e4ac1255fab0073ef89/f7a23b39f6301eaf412564ab003dbd4d/$FILE/Ordf%C3%B8rere%201837-2006.doc|title=Ordførere i Hole 1837–2006|publisher=Hole Municipality|language=Norwegian|accessdate=16 August 2011[/tpl] He owned the farm Stein at Ringerike, and worked as a farmer in Hole.
