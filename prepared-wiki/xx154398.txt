[[2000 Colorado Rockies season]]

CATEGORIES: Colorado Rockies seasons, 2000 Major League Baseball season

The Colorado Rockies' 2000 season was the 8th for the Rockies. They competed in the National League West. Buddy Bell was their manager. They played home games at Coors Field. They finished with a record of 82-80, 4th in the NL West.
