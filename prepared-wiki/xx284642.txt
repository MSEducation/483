[[Sandwell Academy]]

CATEGORIES: West Bromwich, Secondary schools in Sandwell, Academies in Sandwell, Educational institutions established in 2006, 2006 establishments in England

Sandwell Academy is a secondary school and sixth form with academy status located in West Bromwich in the West Midlands, England.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.sandwellacademy.com/ |title=Home |publisher=Sandwell Academy |date= |accessdate=2013-08-11[/tpl] The school opened in 2006, and also offers an early years nursery provision for children up to the age of 5.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.sandwellacademy.com/page/Sandwell-Academy-Nursery.aspx |title=Nursery |publisher=Sandwell Academy |date= |accessdate=2013-08-11[/tpl]
The school is sponsored by the Worshipful Company of Mercers, Thomas Telford School, HSBC Education Trust, Tarmac Group and West Bromwich Albion F.C.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.sandwellacademy.com/page/sponsors.aspx |title=About The Sponsors |publisher=Sandwell Academy |date=1968-05-18 |accessdate=2013-08-11[/tpl] The school has specialisms in Sport and Business Enterprise, and has additional facilities to support the specialisms.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.sandwellacademy.com/page/Specialism-and-Vision.aspx |title=Specialism and Vision |publisher=Sandwell Academy |date= |accessdate=2013-08-11[/tpl]
Sandwell Academy operates a wide catchment area which includes all of West Bromwich and the entirety of Sandwell. The school achieved an 'Outstanding' rating in its most recent Ofsted inspection (2011).[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.sandwellacademy.com/page/Message-Head.aspx |title=Message from the Head |publisher=Sandwell Academy |date= |accessdate=2013-08-11[/tpl]
