[[Helmut Lennartz]]

CATEGORIES: Luftwaffe pilots, German World War II flying aces, Possibly living people

Helmut Lennartz was a former German Luftwaffe fighter ace during World War II. He is credited with 13 aerial victories, including eight victories while flying the Messerschmitt Me-262 jet fighter.For a list of Luftwaffe Jet aces see List of German World War II jet aces He is noted for achieving the first aerial victory over a B-17 Flying Fortress on 15 August 1944 while flying the Messerschmitt Me 262 jet fighter.Morgan & Weal 1998, p. 17.
