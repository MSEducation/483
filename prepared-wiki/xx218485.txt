[[Bad Man's River]]

CATEGORIES: 1971 films, Spanish films, Spanish-language films, 1970s comedy films, 1970s Western (genre) films, Western (genre) comedy films, Spaghetti Western films

Bad Man's River (E continuavano a fregarsi il milione di dollari) is a 1971 European Comedy Western film directed by Eugenio Martín and starring Lee Van Cleef, James Mason, Gina Lollobrigida and Simón Andreu.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.unifrance.org/film/6973/les-quatre-mercenaires-d-el-paso|title=Les Quatre mercenaires d'El Paso|publisher=unifrance.org|accessdate=2014-04-19[/tpl]

==Plot==

Roy King's gang robs a bank and flees to Mexico on a train. Roy meets a beautiful woman, Alicia, and marries her, only to have her run off with all of the money.
An offer comes his way to rob the arsenal of a Mexican army. A daring plan gets the job done, only to have Roy double-crossed once more, unable to get his money.
