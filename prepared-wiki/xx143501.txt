[[Belgium at the 1932 Summer Olympics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 1932 Summer Olympics, Belgium at the Olympics, 1932 in Belgian sport

Belgium competed at the 1932 Summer Olympics in Los Angeles, United States. 36 competitors, 29 men and 7 women, took part in 10 events in 2 sports.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/countries/BEL/summer/1932/ |title=Belgium at the 1932 Los Angeles Summer Games |accessdate=2010-05-02 |work=sports-reference.com[/tpl]
