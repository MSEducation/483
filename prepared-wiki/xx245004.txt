[[Ellen Contini-Morava]]

CATEGORIES: American linguists, Living people, University of Virginia faculty, Year of birth missing (living people), Place of birth missing (living people)

Ellen Contini-Morava is an anthropological linguist at the University of Virginia, interested in the meanings of linguistic forms, discourse analysis, functional linguistics and (noun) classification http://www3.iath.virginia.edu/swahili/; in particular, in the relationship between lexicon and grammar. She received her PhD from Columbia University in 1983 under William Diver and Erica Garcia; she specializes in Bantu languages in general, and Swahili in particular.
