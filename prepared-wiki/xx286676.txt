[[Juqin, Iran]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Juqin ([tpl]lang-fa|جوقين[/tpl], also Romanized as Jūqīn; also known as Chugun, Joqin, and Khūqīn)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3068202|Juqin[/tpl] is a village in Golabar Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 2,557, in 568 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
