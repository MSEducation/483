[[The Bald Bandit]]

CATEGORIES: American children's novels, 1997 novels

The Bald Bandit is a children's mystery novel by Ron Roy written in 1997. It is the second in a series of mystery books called A to Z Mysteries, following the adventures of three junior detectives, Dink, Josh, and Ruth Rose.
When the Green Lawn Bank is robbed and when a Detective Ready knocks on Dink's door asking for help, the trio is on the case immediately with only one clue: a red-haired teen who filmed the incident. Dink, Josh, and Ruth Rose's adventures continue in The Canary Caper
