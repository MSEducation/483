[[Cavalleria rusticana (1953 film)]]

CATEGORIES: 1953 films, Italian films, Black-and-white films, Italian-language films, 1950s drama films, 1950s 3D films, Films directed by Carmine Gallone, Films based on operas

Cavalleria rusticana (1953) is a 3-D Italian drama film directed by Carmine Gallone. The film is based on the opera Cavalleria rusticana and stars May Britt, Anthony Quinn, and Ettore Manni. Quinn plays Alfio, miming to the voice of Tito Gobbi.
