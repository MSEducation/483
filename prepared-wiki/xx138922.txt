[[After All This Time (Simon Webbe song)]]

CATEGORIES: 2006 singles, Simon Webbe songs, Songs written by Simon Webbe, Songs written by Matt Prime, Songs written by Tim Woodcock

"After All This Time" was the third and final single released from Blue band-member Simon Webbe's debut solo album, Sanctuary. The song failed to become Webbe's third top-five single, peaking at #16 on the UK Singles Chart. The song later featured in a historic montage in an episode of BBC2's Top Gear.
