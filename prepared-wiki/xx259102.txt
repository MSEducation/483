[[Sterling Winfield]]

CATEGORIES: Record producers, 1970 births, Living people

Sterling Winfield (born January 14, 1970) is a music producer. He has worked with Pantera on several albums, including Reinventing the Steel (2000). He also co-produced the post-Pantera band Damageplan's 2004 debut album, New Found Power.
