[[Brachionostylum]]

CATEGORIES: Asteraceae genera, Flora of New Guinea, Monotypic plant genera

Brachionostylum is a monotypic genus of flowering plants in the aster family, Asteraceae, containing the single species Brachionostylum pullei.Brachionostylum. The Plant List. It is endemic to New Guinea.Kadereit, J. J. W. and C. Jeffrey. Flowering Plants. Eudicots: Asterales. The Families and Genera of Vascular Plants, Volume 8. Springer. 2007. pg 225.
