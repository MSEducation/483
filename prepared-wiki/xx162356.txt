[[Kévin Monnet-Paquet]]

CATEGORIES: 1988 births, Living people, People from Bourgoin-Jallieu, French people of Rwandan descent, French footballers, Ligue 1 players, Ligue 2 players, RC Lens players, FC Lorient players, France under-21 international footballers, France youth international footballers

Kévin Monnet-Paquet (born 19 August 1988 in Bourgoin-Jallieu) is a French football player. His mother is of Rwandan descent and his father is French. He currently plays for FC Lorient, primarily as an out and out striker. Monnet-Paquet, who is nicknamed KMP, has represented France at under-18 and under-19 level. He has also appeared for Lens five times in the UEFA Cup.
