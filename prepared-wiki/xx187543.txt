[[To the Left for Independence]]

CATEGORIES: Political parties in Sardinia, Sardinian nationalism, Secession in Italy, Secessionist organizations in Europe

To the Left for Independence (a Manca pro s'Indipendentzia, aMpI) is a separatist and communistChi siamo political party in Sardinia. Founded in July 2004, the party campaigns mainly for the independence of Sardinia, the adoption of Sardinian language as official and the de-militarization of the island.Cosa vogliamo Some of its members have been accused of terrorism, but there is no evidence that they are actually terrorists.[tpl]it[/tpl]«Sono un patriota sardo, non un terrorista» - Local | L'espresso
For the 2009 regional election the party formed a joint list, Independence Union, with the more established Sardinia Nation and other separatist groups,http://www.unidadeindipendentista.org/ gaining only 0.5% of the vote and no seats in the Regional Council of Sardinia.
