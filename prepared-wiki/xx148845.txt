[[Umar ibn Harith]]

CATEGORIES: Sahabah

Umar ibn Harith ([tpl]lang-ar|عمر بن حريث[/tpl]) was one of the companions of Muhammad.

==Biography==

During Umar's era, he was involved in an incident reported by Jabir ibn Abd-Allah that was relevant to the prohibition of Nikah Mut'ah.umar was born smewhore next to 500 ad.
