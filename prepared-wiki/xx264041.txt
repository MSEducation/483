[[Deshbandhu College for Girls]]

CATEGORIES: Academic institutions currently affiliated with the University of Calcutta, Educational institutions established in 1955, Women's universities and colleges in West Bengal, 1955 establishments in India

Deshbandhu College for Girls is a women's college located in Kolkata. It was established in 1955 with the affiliation of the University of Calcutta.History of Deshbandhu College for Girls

==Academics==

The college offers B.A./B.Sc. (3 years degree Course) degrees in Bengali, English, Geography, History, Economics, Philosophy, Sanskrit and Education.http://yellowpages.sulekha.com The college has introduced BBA program from the session 2003-2004.http://www.deshbandhucollegeforgirls.com/bba.html
