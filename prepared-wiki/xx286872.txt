[[Kahriz Siyah Mansur]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Kahriz Siyah Mansur ([tpl]lang-fa|كهريزسياه منصور[/tpl], also Romanized as Kahrīz Sīyāh Mansūr and Kahrīz Sīāh Mansūr; also known as Kahrīz and Nahrīz)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3068578|Kahriz Siyah Mansur[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 150, in 37 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
