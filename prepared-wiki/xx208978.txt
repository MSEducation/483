[[Thesprotia subhyalina]]

CATEGORIES: Thesprotia (genus), Insects of South America, Fauna of Bolivia, Fauna of Brazil

Thesprotia subhyalina, common name Grass Mantis, is a species of praying mantis found in Brazil. It was originally identified as Oligonyx subhyalina.http://insects.tamu.edu/research/collection/hallan/Arthropoda/Insects/Mantodea/Family/Thespidae.txt Texas A&M University
