[[Yelnikovsky District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Mordovia

Yelnikovsky District ([tpl]lang-ru|Е́льниковский райо́н[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-mdf|Ельниконь район[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-myv|Кузбуе[/tpl]) is an administrativeConstitution of the Republic of Mordovia and municipalLaw #97-Z district (raion), one of the twenty-two in the Republic of Mordovia, Russia. It is located in the north of the republic. The area of the district is [tpl]convert|1056|km2|sp=us[/tpl].[tpl]citation needed|date=October 2012[/tpl] Its administrative center is the rural locality (a selo) of Yelniki.Law #7-Z Population: [tpl]ru-census2010|11,995|;[/tpl] 13,359 (2002 Census);[tpl]ru-pop-ref|2002Census[/tpl] [tpl]su-census1989|15,842|.[/tpl] The population of Yelniki accounts for 49.2% of the district's total population.
