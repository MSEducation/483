[[DJ Sun]]

CATEGORIES: American DJs, Club DJs, Musicians from Houston, Texas, Living people

DJ Sun is an American Music Producer/DJ.

==Biography==

===Early life===

He was born January 19,[tpl]cite web|last=Ignited|first=Lauren|title=DJ SUN:100|url=http://laurenignited.com/2013/01/10/dj-sun-100/|work=Lauren Ingnited|publisher=Lauren Ignited[/tpl]  in Rotterdam, the Netherlands. He grew up there and in Suriname before settling in Houston, Texas (USA) in his teens.[tpl]cite web|url=[tpl]Allmusic|class=artist|id=p467276/biography|pure_url=yes[/tpl]|title=Biography: DJ Sun|last=Rowland|first=Hobart|publisher=Allmusic|accessdate=5 May 2010[/tpl][tpl]cite web|last=Lipczyk|first=Jacob|title=DJ Sun- One Hundred|url=http://www.theaquarian.com/2013/03/04/dj-sun-one-hundred/|work=The Aquarian Weekly|publisher=Diane Casazza, Chris Farinas|accessdate=4 March 2013[/tpl]

===Career===

DJ Sun started a radio show in 1995, Soular Grooves on KPFT 90.1 FM, Pacifica Network. The music played on Soular Grooves has the aesthetics of soul and jazz, irrespective of bpm or tempo.  Soular Grooves was initially known as an acid jazz show.[tpl]cite web|last=Rowland|first=Hobart|title=Here Comes The Sun|url=http://www.houstonpress.com/1997-08-21/music/here-comes-the-sun/|work=The Houston Press|publisher=Voice Media Group|accessdate=21 August 1997[/tpl][tpl]cite web|last=Lipczyk|first=Jacob|title=DJ Sun- One Hundred|url=http://www.theaquarian.com/2013/03/04/dj-sun-one-hundred/|work=The Aquarian Weekly|publisher=Diane Casazza, Chris Farinas|accessdate=4 March 2013[/tpl]    DJ Sun started his DJ career in 1993 and has maintained a career during which he has earned Houston Press DJ of The Year awards seven times between 1999 and 2009 (the last being in 2009).[tpl]cite web|last=Alberto|first=Valerie|title=2007 Houston Press Music Awards Showcase|url=http://www.houstonpress.com/2007-07-26/music/2007-houston-press-music-awards-showcase/full/|work=The Houston Press|publisher=Voice Media Group|accessdate=26 July 2007[/tpl][tpl]cite web|last=Cobb|first=David|title=Houston Press Music Award Winners|url=http://houstoncalling.net/2009/07/31/houston-press-music-awards-winners-2/|work=Houston Calling|publisher=David Cobb|accessdate=31 July 2009[/tpl]
DJ Sun’s style has been described by music writers like Shea Sherrano of The Houston Press weekly[tpl]cite web|last=Serrano|first=Shea|title=Monday Night Hero|url=http://www.houstonpress.com/2010-12-23/music/monday-night-hero/|work=Houston Press|publisher=Village Voice Media Holdiings|accessdate=28 March 2011[/tpl] as varied with a distinct signature for loungey, groove-oriented eclectic music shaped by his exposure to music while living abroad.
In 2007, DJ Sun became a music producer with the release of Monday Drive (Alternate Take Records), an EP reflective of his varied background, blending styles and nostalgic aesthetics into a down to midtempo paced EP.
DJ Sun released Para, a 12” EP which included remixes from DJ Sabo (Sol Selectas), J Boogie (OM) and Genre Jazz (Presto and Mhax Montes), released in November 2008.
