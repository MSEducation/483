[[1989 Supercoppa Italiana]]

CATEGORIES: Supercoppa Italiana, Inter Milan matches, U.C. Sampdoria matches, 1989–90 in Italian football

The 1989 Supercoppa Italiana was a match played by the 1988–89 Serie A winners Internazionale and 1988–89 Coppa Italia winners Sampdoria.  It took place on 29 September 1989 at the San Siro in Milan, Italy.  Inter won the match 2–0, to earn their first Supercoppa.
