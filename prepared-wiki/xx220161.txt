[[Brezje, Radovljica]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Municipality of Radovljica

Brezje ([tpl]IPA-sl|ˈbɾeːzjɛ|pron[/tpl]) is a settlement in the Municipality of Radovljica in the Upper Carniola region of Slovenia.
It is best known for the pilgrimage church at Brezje, the largest pilgrimage church in Slovenia.
