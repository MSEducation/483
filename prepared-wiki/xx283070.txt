[[Marys River Peak]]

CATEGORIES: Landforms of Elko County, Nevada, Mountains of Nevada

Marys River Peak is the highest mountain in the Marys River Range of northern Elko County, Nevada. It is located within the Jarbidge Wilderness, which is administered by the Jarbidge Ranger District of the Humboldt-Toiyabe National Forest. The peak's name is derived from the Marys River, a tributary of the Humboldt.
