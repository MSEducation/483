[[Mgarr United F.C.]]

CATEGORIES: Association football clubs established in 1967, Football clubs in Malta, 1967 establishments in Malta

Mgarr United F.C. is a Maltese Football Club, currently playing in the Maltese Third Division. The club was founded in 1967 following the dissolution of Mgarr Eagles F.C. The clubs first success came in 2006-2007 when they won the BOV Premier League 3rd Division.
