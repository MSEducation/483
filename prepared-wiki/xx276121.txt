[[(27784) 1992 OE]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1992, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(27784) 1992 OE is a main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on July 27, 1992.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
