[[Boronia railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Premium Melbourne railway stations, Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1920

Boronia railway station is located on the Belgrave line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the eastern Melbourne suburb of Boronia opening on 19 June 1920.Boronia Vicsig
The station has been rebuilt a number of times due to traffic congestion and level crossing accidents on Boronia Road.Met Lines Vol.1 No. 8 1985. It was upgraded to a Premium station on 2 July 1996.[tpl]cite journal|date=October 1997|title=Upgrading Eltham to a Premium Station|journal=Newsrail|publisher=Australian Railway Historical Society|page=page 310[/tpl]
As part of a grade separation project, a new below ground station opened on 5 November 1998.

==Platforms & services==
