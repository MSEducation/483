[[Tony Jantjies]]

CATEGORIES: South African rugby union players, Living people, 1992 births, Sportspeople from Cape Town, Blue Bulls players

Tony Jantjies (born 19 April 1992 in Cape Town) is a South African rugby union player, currently playing with the [tpl]Rut Blue Bulls[/tpl].[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/playerprofile.aspx?id=45463 | title=SARU Player Profile Tony Jantjies | work=South African Rugby Union | accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl] His regular position is fly-half.

==Career==

===Youth===

He represented the [tpl]Rut Golden Lions[/tpl] at the 2008 Under-16 Grant Khomo Week tournament, which lead to his inclusion in the Under-16 Elite Squad.
He then joined near neighbours the [tpl]Rut Blue Bulls[/tpl] from the 2009 season. He played for them at the 2009 and 2010 Under-18 Craven Week competitions, earning a spot in the S.A. Schools side in 2009. He then played for the [tpl]Rut Blue Bulls U19[/tpl] team in the 2010 and 2011 Under-19 Currie Cup competitions and for the [tpl]Rut Blue Bulls U21[/tpl] team in 2012 and 2013.
He was included in the S.A. Under-20 squad for the 2012 IRB Junior World Championship competition. He started the first game of the competition, kicking four penalties and a conversion in South Africa's 19–23 defeat to Ireland,[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/matchbreakdown.aspx?id=109211&category=&leagueid=1171&homeid=13091&awayid=13031&fullview=true | title=Match Breakdown: South Africa vs Ireland | work=South African Rugby Union | date=4 June 2012 | accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl] Handré Pollard was preferred to him for the remainder of the competition, but he did play in matches against Italy[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/matchbreakdown.aspx?id=109261&category=&leagueid=1171&homeid=13091&awayid=13041&fullview=true | title=Match Breakdown: South Africa vs Italy | work=South African Rugby Union | date=8 June 2012 | accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl] and Argentina,[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/matchbreakdown.aspx?id=109331&category=&leagueid=1171&homeid=13071&awayid=13091&fullview=true | title=Match Breakdown: Argentina vs South Africa | work=South African Rugby Union | date=17 June 2012 | accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl] and was an unused substitute in the final against New Zealand.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/matchbreakdown.aspx?id=109391&category=&leagueid=1171&homeid=12981&awayid=13091&fullview=true | title=Match Breakdown: New Zealand vs South Africa | work=South African Rugby Union | date=22 June 2012 | accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl]

===Blue Bulls===

He made his first class debut for the [tpl]Rut Blue Bulls[/tpl] in their opening match of the 2012 Vodacom Cup competition, coming on as a second half substitute against [tpl]Rut Griquas[/tpl].[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/matchbreakdown.aspx?id=103591&category=&leagueid=1211&homeid=11341&awayid=11351&fullview=true | title=Match Breakdown: Vodacom Blue Bulls vs GWK Griquas | work=South African Rugby Union | date=10 March 2012 | accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl] He made his first start in the same competition two weeks later, against former side the [tpl]Rut Golden Lions[/tpl].[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/matchbreakdown.aspx?id=103751&category=&leagueid=1211&homeid=11341&awayid=11391&fullview=true | title=Match Breakdown: Vodacom Blue Bulls vs MTN Golden Lions | work=South African Rugby Union | date=24 March 2012 | accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl] He also scored his first points during that game, scoring a try and kicking five conversions and three penalties to get a personal haul of 24 points in the Blue Bulls' 49–10 victory. He made five appearances in total in that competition, scoring 43 points.
He made a further five appearances for the Blue Bulls in the 2013 Vodacom Cup competition, scoring 79 points to finish as the fifth top points scorer in that competition.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/scorers.aspx?categoryid=sarugby/vodacomcup&leagueid=1164&fullview=true | title=2013 Vodacom Cup Top Scorers | work=South African Rugby Union | accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl]
His Currie Cup debut came later in the same year, when he was included in the Blue Bulls starting team to face [tpl]Rut Western Province[/tpl] in the opening match of the 2013 Currie Cup Premier Division season.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.supersport.com/Rugby/currie-cup/news/130807/Bulls_now_take_their_orders_in_English | title=Bulls now take their orders in English | work=SuperSport | date=7 August 2013 | accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl]
