[[Asrangue Souleymane]]

CATEGORIES: 1982 births, Living people, Central African basketball players, New Orleans Privateers men's basketball players, Cincinnati Bearcats men's basketball players, People from Bangui

Asrangue Souleymane (born 1982 in Bangui) is a former college basketball power forward with the University of New Orleans Privateers.Biography at UNOprivateers.com Souleymane began his career with the Cincinnati Bearcats. He is from the Central African Republic and played with his national team at the FIBA Africa Championship 2005 and 2007.
