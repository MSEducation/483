[[Shuroy]]

CATEGORIES: Twentieth Dynasty of Egypt, Ancient Egyptians

The Ancient Egyptian Shuroy lived during the 20th Dynasty.  He was buried in a tomb in the necropolis of Dra' Abu el-Naga' on the west bank of the Nile, opposite Thebes.[tpl]cite book|title=Topographical Bibliography of Ancient Egyptian Hieroglyphic Texts, Reliefs and Paintings: The Theban Necropolis v. 1 - Part 1, Private Tombs|author=Porter, Bertha & Moss, Rosalind L.B.|publisher=Griffith Institute|year=1994[/tpl] His titles included Head of Brazier-bearers of Amun.
