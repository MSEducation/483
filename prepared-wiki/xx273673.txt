[[Fly Together]]

CATEGORIES: 2011 singles, Rick Ross songs, Ryan Leslie songs, Song recordings produced by Ryan Leslie, Songs written by Ryan Leslie, Music videos directed by Colin Tilley

"Fly Together" is a song by American hip hop recording artist Red Café, released as the lead single off his upcoming debut studio album ShakeDown. The song features rapper Rick Ross along with vocals and production from Ryan Leslie.

==Remix==

On December 9th, 2011 a remix was released, featuring Trey Songz, Wale & J. Trey Songz, Wale & J.Cole |publisher=HipHopDX.com |date=2011-12-09 |accessdate=2011-12-21[/tpl] The remix is included on Red Café's third mixtape Hell's Kitchen, which was released on January 13th, 2012.

==Music video==

On October 7, 2011 a trailer for the music video was released through Café's official YouTube page."Fly Together" (OFFICIAL MUSIC VIDEO TRAILER) YouTube.com The video directed by Colin Tilley, was released October 14, 2011.

==Chart performance==

"Fly Together" first charted on the U.S. Billboard R&B/Hip-Hop Songs on the week of September 12, 2011 at number sixty-six.http://creativedisc.com/top-charts/billboard-hot-100-singles/billboard-hot-rb-hip-hop-songs-12-sep/ CreativeDisk.com It has since peaked at number forty-four The song is Red Café's highest charting single to date.
