[[Rude Awakening (Prong album)]]

CATEGORIES: Prong albums, 1996 albums, Albums produced by Terry Date, Albums produced by Tommy Victor, Epic Records albums

Rude Awakening is Prong's fifth album. Rude Awakening is an enhanced CD. It was also released as a special limited edition on 12" red vinyl. The album was reissued in 2008 as a digipack version, featuring four remixes of the "Rude Awakening" single and a new booklet.
Victor appears on the Argyle Park album Misguided on the track "Doomsayer". The song shares some of the same guitar riffs as the song "Controller".
