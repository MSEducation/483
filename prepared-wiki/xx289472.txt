==History==

International Capital Markets Pty Ltd was founded in 2007 by Andrew Budzinski who was responsible for the founding DMA CFD provider First Prudential Markets (FP Markets) and also involved with CMC Markets in Australia. In 2010, the company launched its retail foreign exchange division. The company has offices in China, Vietnam and Australia.

==Products and Services==

IC Markets offers ECN forex trading using the Metatrader 4 platform. 77 currency pairs and CFDs are offered."IC Markets Review – The Best In Australia" Tech Marking. Retrieved 13 August 2013.
In 2012, IC Markets partnered with Spotware to launch the cTrader platform in the region, making it the first Australian broker and only regulated broker in the Asia Pacific region to offer the platform."IC Markets Rolls Out cTrader" Forex Magnates. Retrieved 13 August 2013.
In October 2013, IC Markets announced a partnership with Tradable to offer their app based platform, the first Australian based broker to offer the platform.Markets to Offer tradable Platform, as Prospects of App-Powered Trading Continues to Evolve" Forex Magnates. Retrieved 24 October 2013.
