[[Karanga language]]

CATEGORIES: Maban languages, Languages of Chad

Karanga is a Maban language language of Chad spoken by four peoples, each with their own dialect: the Karanga (Kurunga), Kashmere (Kachmere), Bakha (Baxa, Bakhat) [tpl]sc|aka[/tpl] Fala (Faala), and Koniéré (Konyare, Kognere) [tpl]sc|aka[/tpl] Moyo (Mooyo).
