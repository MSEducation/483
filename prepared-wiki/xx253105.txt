[[Atyuryevsky District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Mordovia

Atyuryevsky District ([tpl]lang-ru|Атю́рьевский райо́н[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-mdf|Атерень район[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-myv|Атюрьбуе[/tpl]) is an administrativeConstitution of the Republic of Mordovia and municipalLaw #116-Z district (raion), one of the twenty-two in the Republic of Mordovia, Russia. It is located in the west of the republic. The area of the district is [tpl]convert|826.1|km2|sp=us[/tpl].[tpl]citation needed|date=October 2012[/tpl] Its administrative center is the rural locality (a selo) of Atyuryevo.Law #7-Z Population: [tpl]ru-census2010|10,952|;[/tpl] 12,151 (2002 Census);[tpl]ru-pop-ref|2002Census[/tpl] [tpl]su-census1989|15,112|.[/tpl] The population of Atyuryevo accounts for 40.4% of the district's total population.
