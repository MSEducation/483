[[Bald Knob, West Virginia]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Boone County, West Virginia, Unincorporated communities in West Virginia

Bald Knob is an unincorporated community in Boone County, West Virginia, United States. Bald Knob is located on West Virginia Route 85 [tpl]convert|17|mi|km[/tpl] southeast of Madison. Bald Knob had a post office, which closed on October 1, 2005.[tpl]cite web|title=Postmaster Finder - Post Offices by ZIP Code|url=http://webpmt.usps.gov/pmt008.cfm|publisher=United States Postal Service|accessdate=September 26, 2011[/tpl]
