[[Matt, Switzerland]]

CATEGORIES: Former municipalities of the canton of Glarus

Matt is a former municipality in the canton of Glarus in Switzerland. It is located in Sernftal (Sernf valley). Effective from 1 January 2011, Matt is part of the municipality of Glarus SÃ¼d.

==History==

Matt is first mentioned in 1273 as Mattun.

==Geography==

Matt has an area, [tpl]as of|2006|lc=on[/tpl], of [tpl]convert|41.3|km2|sqmi|abbr=on[/tpl].  Of this area, 39.6% is used for agricultural purposes, while 32.4% is forested.  Of the rest of the land, 0.8% is settled (buildings or roads) and the remainder (27.2%) is non-productive (rivers, glaciers or mountains).
Matt is located in the middle Sernf valley at the entrance to the Krauch Valley.  It consists of the village of Matt and the mountain hamlet of Weissenberge.
