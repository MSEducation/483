[[Adam Davies (cricketer)]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 births, Living people, Sportspeople from Cardiff, Welsh cricketers, Wales Minor Counties cricketers

Adam James Davies (born 26 October 1980) is a Welsh cricketer.  Davies is a right-handed batsman who bowls right-arm medium-fast.  He was born in Cardiff, Glamorgan.
Davies played a single Minor Counties Championship match for Wales Minor Counties in 1999 against Cornwall.Minor Counties Championship Matches played by Adam Davies
From 1999 to 2003, Davies played a number of Second XI matches for the Glamorgan Second XI,Teams Adam Davies played for before playing his only List A match, which came for Wales Minor Counties against Nottinghamshire in the 2005 Cheltenham & Gloucester Trophy.List A Matches played by Adam Davies  In the match he scored 17 runsList A Batting and Fielding For Each Team by Adam Davies and took 2 wickets for the cost of 24 runs from 6 overs.List A Bowling For Each Team by Adam Davies
His father, Hugh, played first-class cricket for Glamorgan from 1955 to 1960.
