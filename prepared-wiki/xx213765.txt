[[2005–06 2. Bundesliga (women)]]

CATEGORIES: 2. Bundesliga (women) seasons, 2005–06 domestic women's association football leagues, 2005–06 in German women's football, 2005–06 in German football leagues

The 2nd Fußball-Bundesliga (women) 2005–06 was the 2nd season of the 2. Fußball-Bundesliga (women), Germany's second football league. It began on 14 August 2005 and ended on 5 June 2006.[tpl]cite web | title = 2.
