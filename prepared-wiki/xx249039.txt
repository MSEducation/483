[[Mega Melon]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 albums, Melon Kinenbi albums

Mega Melon is a "greatest-hits" album by Japanese girl group Melon Kinenbi. It was released on December 10th 2008, little under a year after the release of their last album, Melon Juice. It reached a peak position of #122 on the Oricon weekly chart. As part of the tenth anniversary of Hello! Project "Mega Best Series", it was released as a 2-disc CD + DVD package. It is the second "single best" compilation, including the more recent singles since the first, Fruity Killer Tune, was released in 2006.
