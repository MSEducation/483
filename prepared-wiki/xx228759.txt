[[C. S. Golden House]]

CATEGORIES: National Register of Historic Places in Marengo County, Alabama, Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Alabama, Queen Anne architecture in Alabama, Houses completed in 1898, Houses in Marengo County, Alabama

The C.S. Golden House, also known as the Leonard and Ellie Crain House, is a historic house located in Thomaston, Alabama. It was built in 1898 in the Queen Anne style.[tpl]cite web|last=Embree|first=Joan|title=Golden, C.S., House|url=http://pdfhost.focus.nps.gov/docs/nrhp/text/00001029.pdf|work=National Register of Historic Places Registration Form|publisher=National Park Service|accessdate=March 11, 2014|date=April 25, 2000|archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/6O0eiyJh0 |archivedate=March 11, 2014|deadurl=no[/tpl] See also: [tpl]cite web|title=Accompanying photos|url=http://pdfhost.focus.nps.gov/docs/NRHP/Photos/00001029.pdf|accessdate=March 11, 2014|archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/6O0ejgTeQ |archivedate=March 11, 2014|deadurl=no[/tpl]  The house was added to the National Register of Historic Places on August 31, 2000 due to its architectural significance.
