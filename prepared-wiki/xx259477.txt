[[Rodolfo da Ponte]]

CATEGORIES: 1938 births, Living people, Paraguayan fencers, Olympic fencers of Paraguay, Fencers at the 1968 Summer Olympics

Rodolfo da Ponte (born November 26, 1938) is a Paraguayan fencer. He competed in the individual foil event at the 1968 Summer Olympics. He became the first person to represent Paraguay at the Olympic Games.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/da/rodolfo-da-ponte-1.html |title=Rodolfo da Ponte Olympic Results |accessdate=2010-12-30 |work=sports-reference.com[/tpl] He is the father of fencer Enzo da Ponte.
