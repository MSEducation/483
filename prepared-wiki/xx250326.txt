[[Melon Tea]]

CATEGORIES: 2009 singles

[tpl]nihongo|"Melon Tea"|メロンティー[/tpl] is the fifth indie single by Japanese girl group Melon Kinenbi, in a collaboration with Going Under Ground. It was released as limited distribution on 30 December 2009. People who purchased the single from the Tower Records online store received a free original computer wallpaper.
