Ziv Caveda ([tpl]lang-he|זיו כבדה[/tpl]; born December 10, 1978) is an Israeli professional football (soccer) player. After not seeing much time at Hapoel Tel Aviv, Caveda transferred to Tzafririm Holon where he was an instant starter.
