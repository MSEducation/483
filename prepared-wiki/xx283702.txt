[[Saudi Arabia at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics, 2013 in Saudi Arabian sport, Athletics in Saudi Arabia

Saudi Arabia is competing at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics in Moscow, Russia, from 10–18 August 2013.
A team of 11 athletes was announced to represent the country in the event.
