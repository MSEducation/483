[[Kijewo Królewskie]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Chełmno County

Kijewo Królewskie [tpl]IPAc-pl|k|i|'|j|e|w|o|-|k|r|u|'|l|e|f|s|k|J|e[/tpl] ([tpl]lang-de|Königlich Kiewo[/tpl]) is a village in Chełmno County, Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-central Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It is the seat of the gmina (administrative district) called Gmina Kijewo Królewskie. It lies [tpl]convert|11|km|mi|0[/tpl] south of Chełmno, [tpl]convert|30|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north-west of Toruń, and [tpl]convert|35|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north-east of Bydgoszcz.
The name of the village means "Royal Kijewo".
