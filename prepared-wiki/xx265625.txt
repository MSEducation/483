[[Wholesale fashion distribution]]

CATEGORIES: Commercial item transport and distribution, Distribution, retailing, and wholesaling, Supply chain management

Wholesale Fashion Distribution represents the global market of clothing sales, in which producers, wholesalers and sellers are involved in a commercial, business-to-business process.

==Procedure==

Most of the wholesalers get their fashion stocks from the producers that commercialize the unsold last collections in bulk, at discounts.[tpl]cite web|url=http://taxhistory.tax.org/Civilization/Documents/Sales/hst6646/6646-1.htm|title=Tax History [/tpl] Their clients are the resellers that purchase those stocks
and sell it then after to the final consumers.

==Wholesale Businesses==

We are talking about a wholesale business when a fashion distributor sells large quantities of clothing stocks to a reseller.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.entrepreneur.com/article/190460 |title=Entrepreneur[/tpl] Nowadays, many websites offer this opportunity.

==Wholesale Clothing Brands==

More and More famous fashion brands from all around the world are selling wholesale clothing at discount prices. That happens because all fashion producers sell on bulk their unsold collections, in order to remove the capital loss problem or getting their money blocked in stocks.

===Risks===

Dealing with business always involves a risk, especially on a market in which the clothing producers and the final consumers are the ones that decide how things will go.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.referenceforbusiness.com/small/Di-Eq/Distribution-Channels.html|title=Distribution channels [/tpl] Producers are the ones that set
up the apparel stocks starting prices and consumers are the ones that choose to buy or not a product sold at a certain price.
[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.italianbrandsdistribution.com|title=Brands Distribution [/tpl]

==Related concepts==

A related concept is Drop Shipping, a commercial process used in the Wholesale Fashion Distribution.
Wholesale Marketing is a very important aspect of this business area.[tpl]cite web|url=http://retail.about.com/popular.htm|title=Retailing [/tpl] It sets up the marketing
instruments which are used to power the Wholesale Market.
