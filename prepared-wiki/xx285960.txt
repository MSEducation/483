[[Lynne Kelly]]

CATEGORIES: American children's writers, 1969 births, Living people, People from Galesburg, Illinois, Stephen F. Austin State University alumni, Writers from Texas, People from Houston, Texas

Lynne Kelly (born March 1, 1969) is an American author of books for children and young adults. Her first novel, Chained, was published in May 2012 by Farrar, Straus, & Giroux/Margaret Ferguson Books.[tpl]cite web|title=Chained Macmillan Page|url=http://us.macmillan.com/chained/LynneKelly|accessdate=August 12, 2013[/tpl]

==Biography==

Lynne Kelly is an author of middle grade and young adult fiction. She was born in Galesburg, Illinois and grew up in Houston, Texas. While studying for a psychology degree from Stephen F. Austin State University in Nacogdoches, she started working as a sign language interpreter and continued that career after graduation.[tpl]cite web|title=Lynne Kelly LinkedIn Page|url=http://www.linkedin.com/pub/lynne-kelly/a/173/b58|accessdate=August 12, 2013[/tpl] Kelly became certified as a special education teacher in 2002, and started working on her first novel, Chained, while teaching in Spring ISD near Houston.[tpl]cite news|last=Nandakumar|first=Sowmya|title=Chained Redefines Connections and Breaking Free of Shackles|url=http://www.indoamerican-news.com/?p=6641|accessdate=August 12, 2013|newspaper=Indo American News|date=April 26, 2012[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Lynne Kelly LinkedIn Page|url=http://www.linkedin.com/pub/lynne-kelly/a/173/b58|accessdate=August 12, 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite news|last=Buron|first=Melissa|title=Interview with Lynne Kelly|url=http://melissaburon.wordpress.com/2010/07/01/interview-with-lynne-kelly/|accessdate=August 12, 2013|newspaper=The Houston Banner|date=July 2010[/tpl]
Kelly currently resides in the Houston, Texas area.[tpl]cite news|last=Nandakumar|first=Sowmya|title=Chained Redefines Connections and Breaking Free of Shackles|url=http://www.indoamerican-news.com/?p=6641|accessdate=August 12, 2013|newspaper=Indo American News|date=April 26, 2012[/tpl]
