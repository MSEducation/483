CATEGORIES: Hindu goddesses

thumb|right|Brahmani representation during the Bhairab Naach festival. In Hinduism, Brahmani (also known as Brahmi and Brahmayi) is a kind and benevolent aspect of Devi, the Divine mother. Brahmani is associated with the Hindu creator god, Brahma as His  Shakti (power).
