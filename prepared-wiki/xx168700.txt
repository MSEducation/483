[[Sunflower revolution]]

CATEGORIES: Health-related fundraisers, Charity fundraisers

Sunflower Revolution is a 3-day event held every fall in Cincinnati, Ohio, that raises funds and awareness about Parkinson's Disease. The event is sponsored by the James J. and Joan A.
