[[Willie Williamson]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, Kentucky State Thorobreds football coaches

Willie Williamson was the 19th head football coach for the Kentucky State University Thorobreds located in Frankfort, Kentucky and he held that position for the 1984 season.  His coaching record at Kentucky State was 2 wins, 9 losses, and 0 ties.  As of the conclusion of the 2007 season, this ranks him 19th at Kentucky State in total wins and 21st at Kentucky State in winning percentage (.182).Kentucky State University coaching records  Some records show that he shared the head coaching duties with Theo Lemon.
