[tpl]brazil fc|Paraná[/tpl] declared as the Campeonato Brasileiro Série B champions by aggregate score of 3-1.

===Promotion===

The three best placed teams in each one of the four groups in the first stage, which are [tpl]brazil fc|Santa Cruz[/tpl], [tpl]brazil fc|Fortaleza[/tpl], Ceará, [tpl]brazil fc|Remo[/tpl], [tpl]brazil fc|Vitória[/tpl], Desportiva, [tpl]brazil fc|Criciúma[/tpl], [tpl]brazil fc|Coritiba[/tpl], União São João, América-MG, [tpl]brazil fc|Paraná[/tpl] and [tpl]brazil fc|Grêmio[/tpl], were promoted to the following year's first level.

==Campeonato Brasileiro Série C==

Final

Tuna Luso declared as the Campeonato Brasileiro Série C champions by aggregate score of 3-3.

===Promotion===

No club was promoted to the following year's second level.

==Copa do Brasil==

The Copa do Brasil final was played between [tpl]brazil fc|Internacional[/tpl] and [tpl]brazil fc|Fluminense[/tpl].

[tpl]brazil fc|Internacional[/tpl] declared as the cup champions on the away goal rule by aggregate score of 2-2.

==State championship champions==

 (1)Four clubs, which are Fortaleza, Ceará, Tiradentes-CE and Icasa, shared the 1992 Ceará State Championship title.
