[[Glaðsheimr]]

CATEGORIES: Locations in Norse mythology

In Norse mythology, Glaðsheimr (Old Norse "bright home"Orchard (1997:57).) is a realm in Asgard where Odin's hall of Valhalla is located according to Grímnismál.
Snorri states in Gylfaginning that Glaðsheimr is a meeting hall containing thirteen high seatshttp://en.wikisource.org/wiki/Prose_Edda/Gylfaginning where the male Asgardians hold council, located in Iðavöllr in Asgard, near the hall of Vingólf where the Asgardian goddesses gathered.
