[[Kindaruma Power Station]]

CATEGORIES: Articles created via the Article Wizard, Hydroelectric power stations in Kenya

Kindaruma Power Station is Kenya's first post-independence hydro power plant having an initial electric capacity of 44 MW from two turbines.[tpl]cite web|title=Hydropower Stations in Kenya|url=https://energypedia.info/wiki/Hydropower_Stations_in_Kenya[/tpl]
Commissioned in 1968 as part of the  seven forks scheme it is located on the Tana River in eastern Mbeere District of Kenya and supplies power to Nairobi and Kamburu.[tpl]cite web|title=Kindaruma Dam|url=http://wikimapia.org/7269789/Kindaruma-Dam[/tpl]
As of 2012, the addition of a third turbine will increase capacity to 72 MW.[tpl]cite web|title=KINDARUMA POWER STATION|url=http://www.kengen.co.ke/[/tpl]
