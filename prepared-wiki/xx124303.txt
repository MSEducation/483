[[José Ángel Llamas]]

CATEGORIES: 1966 births, Living people, Mexican male telenovela actors, Male actors from Mexico City, Mexican expatriates in the United States

José Ángel Llamas Olmos (born October 13, 1966) is a Mexican actor best known for his roles of Rudolfo in Amor descarado. His striking good looks make him quite the heart throb among his many fans.(Spanish) People en Espanol
He has been married to Mara Croatto since 29 August 2004 and has one son, Rafael. He is half brother to the famous Mexican actress María Eugenia Llamas and the late María Victoria Llamas who was a well-known author and telejournalist in Mexico City. It was Maru who gave him his start in television.Bio of Jose Angel Llamas
