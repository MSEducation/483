[[Doon Theological Journal]]

CATEGORIES: Religious studies journals, Publications established in 2004, Biannual journals, English-language journals

The Doon Theological Journal ([tpl]ISSN|0973-4678[/tpl]; [tpl]OCLC|56191586[/tpl]) is a biannual journal of theological reflection published by the Luther W. New Jr. Theological College of Dehradun, India. It was established in 2004 in partnership with the Presbyterian Theological Seminary, Dehradun, India.[tpl]cite journal | title = SAMUEL,SIMON (ed.), Doon Theological Journal,  Vol. 1.1 (Dehra Dun: New Theological College, 2004), pp. 98. Brooke | journal = Journal for the Study of the Old Testament | volume = 29 | issue = 5 | year = 2005 | pages = 17–8 [/tpl][tpl]cite journal | title = Ecumenical resources | journal = Journal of Ecumenical Studies | volume = 41 | issue = 3-4; Summer-Fall |date=June 2004 | pages = 476–509 | url = http://findarticles.com/p/articles/mi_hb3236/is_3-4_41/ai_n29153516/pg_11/ [/tpl] The editor is Simon Samuel. The journal aims to promote Christian theological learning and thoughtful conversation between various Christian traditions in India. It is open to creative engagement with contemporary religious, political, social, economic, and cultural issues.
