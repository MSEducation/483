[[Kutan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Sardasht County

Kutan ([tpl]lang-fa|كوتان[/tpl], also Romanized as Kūtān)[tpl]GEOnet3|10077923|Kutan[/tpl] is a village in Gavork-e Sardasht Rural District, in the Central District of Sardasht County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.[tpl]IranCensus2006|04[/tpl]
