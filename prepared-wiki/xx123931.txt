[[St. Andrew's School (Sevierville, Tennessee)]]

CATEGORIES: Private high schools in Tennessee, Boarding schools in Tennessee, Schools in Sevier County, Tennessee, Private middle schools in Tennessee, Private elementary schools in Tennessee

St. Andrew's School in Sevierville, Tennessee is a co-educational, private Christian boarding school which enrolls fewer than fifty students in kindergarten through grade twelve. 
It is part of Wears Valley Ranch, a Christian children's home for youth from crisis family situations. The academic program is modeled after homeschooling. 
The school is a member of Association of Christian Schools International.
