[[Michael Kinzer House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Virginia, Houses completed in 1845, Houses in Montgomery County, Virginia, Blacksburg, Virginia, National Register of Historic Places in Montgomery County, Virginia

Michael Kinzer House is a historic home located at Blacksburg, Montgomery County, Virginia.  It was built about 1845, and is a two-story, four-bay brick two-room-plan house. It features a decorative brick cornice.  Also on the property is the contributing site of a brick kiln.[tpl]cite web |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=NJa_64aH1iMC&pg=PA323&lpg=PA323&dq=Michael+Kinzer+House&source=bl&ots=PD1Eiy3caM&sig=vof8ruMovtxoUzmWuK8T3UnsGyQ&hl=en&sa=X&ei=IfAHUtL8Maj8yAGXhIGIBQ&ved=0CFkQ6AEwCg#v=onepage&q=Michael%20Kinzer%20House&f=false|title=Google Books: The Virginia Landmarks Register, p. 323 |author=ed. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1989.
