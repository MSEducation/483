[[Qelich Qayeh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Qelich Qayeh ([tpl]lang-fa|قليچ قيه[/tpl], also Romanized as Qelīch Qayeh; also known as Qalajqiyeh, Qelesh Qayah, Qelesh Qayeh, and Qelīj Qayeh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3080077|Qelich Qayeh[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 116, in 22 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
