[[Carel Greeff]]

Carel Greeff (born 20 May 1990) is a South African rugby union player, currently playing with [tpl]Rut Griquas[/tpl].[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/playerprofile.aspx?id=40275 | title=SARU Player Profile Carel Greeff | work=South African Rugby Union | accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl] His regular position is loose-forward.

==Career==

===Youth===

He represented the [tpl]Rut Leopards[/tpl] at the 2006 Under-16 Grant Khomo Week, 2007 Under-18 Academy Week and 2008 Under-18 Craven Week competitions. His performances in the latter also resulted in his inclusion in an Under-18 South African Elite squad in 2008.
The then joined the [tpl]Rut Natal Sharks[/tpl] and was included in their U19 squad for the 2009 Under-19 Currie Cup competition.

===Golden Lions===

In 2011, he made his first class debut for the [tpl]Rut Golden Lions[/tpl] in a compulsory friendly match prior to the 2011 Currie Cup Premier Division season, starting the match against the [tpl]Rut Falcons[/tpl].[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/matchbreakdown.aspx?id=20754&homeid=8795&awayid=8796&fullview=true | title=Match breakdown: Valke vs MTN Golden Lions XV | work=South African Rugby Union | date=1 July 2011 | accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl] He never appeared in the competition proper, instead representing the [tpl]Rut Golden Lions U21[/tpl] team in the 2011 Under-21 Currie Cup competition, making twelve appearances.

===Varsity Rugby===

He played in the 2013 Varsity Cup competition for [tpl]Rut Wits[/tpl], scoring five tries in seven appearances,[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/tryscorers.aspx?categoryid=sarugby/amateur&leagueid=1084&fullview=true | title=2013 Varsity Cup Try Scorers | work=South African Rugby Union | accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl] making him the joint second top try scorer in the competition.
