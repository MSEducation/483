[[Seia]]

CATEGORIES: Cities in Portugal, Populated places in Guarda District

Seia is a city in Seia Municipality, Portugal.
Characterization of the Municipality: 
Situated on the western slope of the Serra da Estrela, the county Seia consists of 21 parishes (115 locations), occupies an area of 436 km2, administratively belongs to the district of Guarda and, according to the 2011 census, has a population of 24,641 inhabitants . 
Elevated to city July 3, 1986, Seia is now a booming city, staking a strong cultural promotion and tourism in the region.
