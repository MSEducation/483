[[KIOX-FM]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Texas, Classic country radio stations in the United States

KIOX-FM (96.1 FM, is a radio station licensed to serve Edna, Texas.  The station is owned by Globecom Media. It airs a classic country music format.
The station was assigned the KIOX call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on January 4, 2008.[tpl]cite web |title=Call Sign History |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=27226&Callsign=KIOX-FM |work=FCC Media Bureau CDBS Public Access Database[/tpl]
