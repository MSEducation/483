[[Wallidan F.C.]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in the Gambia, Banjul

Wallidan Football Club is a Gambian football club based in Banjul. They play in the top division in Gambian football, the Gambian Championnat National D1, and are the most decorated team in the country's football history. They play at the Independence Stadium (Bakau) which has a capacity of 20,000.
