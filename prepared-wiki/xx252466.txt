[[Akethorpe]]

CATEGORIES: Lowestoft

Akethorpe was an English village thought to have been located in what is now part of the Suffolk town of Lowestoft.Akethorpe, Domesday Book Online. Retrieved 2011-04-21.Akethorpe, Domesday Map. Retrieved 2011-04-21. The small village was described in the Domesday book as being home to 4 households in the Hundred of Lothingland. It formed part of the King's holding in 1086, having been held by Aelmer the Priest in 1066.
A road in modern Lowestoft is named Akethorpe Way.
