[[Plácido]]

CATEGORIES: 1961 films, Spanish films, Spanish Christmas films, 1960s comedy films, Spanish-language films, Black-and-white films, Films directed by Luis García Berlanga

Plácido is a 1961 Spanish black comedy film directed by Luis García Berlanga. It was nominated to the Academy Award as Best Foreign Language Film.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/legacy/ceremony/34th-winners.html |title=The 34th Academy Awards (1962) Nominees and Winners |accessdate=2011-10-29|work=oscars.org[/tpl] It was also entered into the 1962 Cannes Film Festival.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.festival-cannes.com/en/archives/ficheFilm/id/3163/year/1962.html |title=Festival de Cannes: Plácido |accessdate=2009-02-24|work=festival-cannes.com[/tpl]
