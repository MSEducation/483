[[Arkuyen]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Arkuyen ([tpl]lang-fa|ارکوئين[/tpl], also Romanized as Arkūyen and Arkowyen; also known as Arkaun, Arkavin, Arkovīn, Arkūten, and Darkovīn)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3053665|Arkuyen[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 217, in 75 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
