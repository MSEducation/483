[[Nechayevka, Voronezh Oblast]]

CATEGORIES: Rural localities in Novousmansky District

Nechayevka ([tpl]lang-ru|Нечаевка[/tpl]) is a rural locality (a selo) in Usmanskoye 2-ye Rural Settlement of Novousmansky District of Voronezh Oblast, Russia,Law #87-OZ located [tpl]convert|3.9|km|sp=us[/tpl] west from the district's administrative center Novaya Usman, near the [tpl]Highway M (Russia)|4[/tpl] ([tpl]BAB-E|115[/tpl]) road. Population: 187 (2005 est.).Список сельских населенных пунктов и численности населения по данным статистического учета Новоусманского муниципального района
By the Law of Voronezh Oblast #93-OZ of October 27, 2008, the status of Nechayevka was changed from a settlement to a selo.Law #93
