[[3827 Zdeněkhorský]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Asteroids named for people, Astronomical objects discovered in 1986

3827 Zdeněkhorský is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1659.2550203 days (4.54 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=3827 | title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-05-24 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on November 3, 1986.
