[[Josef Schmitt]]

CATEGORIES: 1908 births, 1980 deaths, German footballers, Germany international footballers, 1. FC Nürnberg players, Olympic footballers of Germany, Footballers at the 1928 Summer Olympics

Josef Schmitt (21 March 1908 – 16 April 1980) was a German international footballer who played for 1. FC Nürnberg.
In 1928, he appeared twice for the German national football team, scoring one goal, and participated in the 1928 Summer Olympics.
