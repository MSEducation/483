Derick Chuka Ogbu (born 19 March 1990)Voetbal International profile commonly known as Chuka, is a Nigerian professional footballer who plays as a forward for Romanian Liga I club CFR Cluj. 
Ogbu was on trial at PSV Eindhoven during the 2011/2012 pre-season on the recommendation of then Umm Salal manager Henk ten Cate. However he did not sign for them but Fred Rutten, the coach of PSV, described him as "very fast and interesting player, with good character and a hard shot".
