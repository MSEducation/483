[[Mithkal]]

CATEGORIES: Arabic words and phrases

The mithkal or sextula (in Persian and Arabic: مثقال) is an Arabic unit of weight used in Iran, mostly for weighing gold and Saffron. It is equivalent to a little over 4.6 grams.
The word mithkal is sometimes also used to mean a very small amount, due to its use in the Quran in this way.[tpl]citation needed|date=December 2010[/tpl]
