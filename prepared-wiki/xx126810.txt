[[Vasiliy Bubka]]

CATEGORIES: 1960 births, Living people, People from Luhansk, Ukrainian pole vaulters, Soviet pole vaulters, Athletes (track and field) at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Olympic athletes of Ukraine, European Athletics Championships medalists, Male pole vaulters

Vasiliy Bubka (Ukraine: Василь Бубка; born 26 November 1960 in Voroshilovgrad) is a retired pole vaulter who represented the USSR and later Ukraine. His personal best was 5.86 metres, achieved in July 1988 in Chelyabinsk.
Bubka is the older brother of pole vaulting world record holder Sergey Bubka and father of pole vaulter Oleksandr Bubka, born 1986.
