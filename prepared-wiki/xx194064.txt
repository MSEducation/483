He has previously done radio play-by-play for the Florida Atlantic Owls, Miami Hurricanes and Miami Heat.
He announced the Liberty Bowl on December 31, 2011 with Ray Bentley doing the color commentating and Quint Kessenich doing the sidelines on ABC between the Cincinnati Bearcats and the Vanderbilt Commordores at the Liberty Bowl in Memphis, Tennessee.
He is currently doing games on ESPN/ESPN2/ABC with Kelly Stouffer.
