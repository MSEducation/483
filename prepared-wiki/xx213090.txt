[[Trzydnik Duży]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Kraśnik County

Trzydnik Duży [tpl]IPAc-pl|'|t|sz|y|d|n|i|k|-|'|d|u|ż|y[/tpl] is a village in Kraśnik County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It is the seat of the gmina (administrative district) called Gmina Trzydnik Duży. It lies approximately [tpl]convert|9|km|mi|0[/tpl] south-west of Kraśnik and [tpl]convert|53|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-west of the regional capital Lublin.
