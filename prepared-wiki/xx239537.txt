[[Aliquandostipite]]

CATEGORIES: Dothideomycetes

Aliquandostipite is a genus of fungi in the Aliquandostipitaceae family.[tpl]cite journal |last=Lumbsch TH, Huhndorf SM. |date=December 2007 |title=Outline of Ascomycota – 2007 |journal=Myconet |volume=13 |issue= |pages=1–58 |id= |url=http://www.fieldmuseum.org/myconet/outline.asp |accessdate= |publisher=The Field Museum, Department of Botany, Chicago, USA [/tpl] Species in the genus are saprobic, and grow on decaying wood in aquatic or moist environments. The type genus of the Aliquandostipitaceae, Aliquandostipite was first described by Patrik Inderbitzin and colleagues in 2001.[tpl]cite journal |author= Inderbitzin P, Landvik S, Abdel-Wahab, MA, Berbee ML.|year=2001 |title=Aliquandostipitaceae, a new family for two new tropical ascomycetes with unusually wide hyphae and dimorphic ascomata |journal=American Journal of Botany |volume=88 |issue=1 |pages=52–61|url=http://www.amjbot.org/cgi/reprint/88/1/52.pdf|format=PDF |doi= 10.2307/2657126 |pmid= 11159126[/tpl]
