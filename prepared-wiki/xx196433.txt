[[Iris Cahn]]

CATEGORIES: Living people

Iris Cahn is a Full Professor and Chair Emerita of the Film Conservatory at SUNY Purchase College.

==Biography==

Cahn earned her B.F.A. from SUNY Purchase College and her M.A. from New York University.  She edits feature films and documentaries, and has directed short films.
