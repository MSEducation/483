[[Teopisca]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Chiapas, Municipalities of Chiapas

 
Teopisca is a town and one of the 119 Municipalities of Chiapas, in southern Mexico.
As of 2010, the municipality had a total population of 37,607,[tpl]cite web|title=Teopisca|url=http://www.microrregiones.gob.mx/catloc/LocdeMun.aspx?tipo=clave&campo=loc&ent=07&mun=094|work=Catálogo de Localidades|publisher=Secretaría de Desarrollo Social (SEDESOL)|accessdate=24 April 2014[/tpl] up from 26,996 as of 2005. [tpl]cite web|title=Teopisca|url=http://www.e-local.gob.mx/work/templates/enciclo/chiapas/municipios/07094a.htm|work=Enciclopedia de los Municipios de México|publisher=Instituto Nacional para el Federalismo y el Desarrollo Municipal Gobierno del Estado de Chiapas |accessdate=July 27, 2008[/tpl] It covers an area of 174 km².  
As of 2010, the town of Teopisca had a population of 16,240. Other than the town of Teopisca, the municipality had 123 localities, the largest of which (with 2010 populations in parentheses) were Nuevo León (2,782) and Betania (2,274).
