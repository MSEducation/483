[[Pietrari, Dâmbovița]]

CATEGORIES: Communes in Dâmboviţa County

Pietrari is a commune in Romania.  It is located in Dâmboviţa County. It is composed of five villages: Aluniş, După Deal, Pietrari, Şipot and Valea. These were part of Bărbulețu Commune until 2004, when they were split off.
