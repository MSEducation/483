[[Petr Jelínek]]

CATEGORIES: 1984 births, Living people, HC Slavia Praha players, Czech ice hockey players

Petr Jelínek (born 19 June 1984) is a Czech professional ice hockey forward who currently plays for HC Slavia Praha of the Czech Extraliga.Slavia Praha HC's 2010-11 Roster
Jelínek played previously also for Moose Jaw Warriors, Prince George Cougars, HC Slovan Ústečtí Lvi, HC Rebel Havlíčkův Brod and MšHK Žilina.Profile at HC Slavia Praha official website
