[[7 (Zap Mama album)]]

CATEGORIES: 1997 albums, Virgin Records albums

"Seven", or "7" is the fourth album by Zap Mama, released in 1997 on Luaka Bop Records, a sub-label of Virgin, started by David Byrne of Talking Heads.
