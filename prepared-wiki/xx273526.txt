[[Barrett Trotter]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 births, Living people, Sportspeople from Birmingham, Alabama, American football quarterbacks, Auburn Tigers football players

Barrett Trotter (born February 10, 1989) is a former American football quarterback. He previously served as backup quarterback to Heisman Trophy winner Cam Newton during the 2010 Auburn Tigers national championship team. In 2011, he started seven games as the quarterback for the Auburn Tigers football team.
In 2010, Trotter completed 6-of-9 passes for 64 yards, rushed for 68 yards and a touchdown. His sole touchdown came on a 18-yard run against Louisiana-Monroe. In the SEC Championship Game against South Carolina, Trotter rushed twice for 37 yards and connected on his only pass attempt for 16 yards.Auburn Tigers bio
Trotter is currently a football operations assistant with the St. Louis Rams.
