[[Sandstad Church]]

CATEGORIES: Hitra, Churches in Sør-Trøndelag

Sandstad church ([tpl]lang-no|Sandstad kirke[/tpl]) is a parish church in the municipality of Hitra in Sør-Trøndelag county, Norway.  The church is located in the village of Sandstad on the southeastern coast of the island of Hitra.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.orkdalsmenighetene.no/sandstad/index.htm|title=Sandstad kirke|language=Norwegian|publisher=Orkdals prosti|accessdate=2010-12-30[/tpl]
