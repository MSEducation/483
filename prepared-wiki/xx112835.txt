[[Sitticus pubescens]]

CATEGORIES: Salticidae, Animals described in 1775, Jumping spiders of Europe, Spiders of Russia, Spiders of North America

Sitticus pubescens is a jumping spider.

==Description and habitat==

The species have an angular head, and could be found in stone or inside houses.Nick's Spiders

==Distribution==

S. pubescens lives in Europe, Russia and the USA.
