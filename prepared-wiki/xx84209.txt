[[Kamiminochi District, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Districts in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Kamiminochi|上水内郡|Kamiminochi-gun[/tpl] is a district located in Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2011, the district has an estimated population of 23,774. As a result of mergers the total area of the district is now 282.65 km².
