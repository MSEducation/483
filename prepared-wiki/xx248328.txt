Dirk Caspers (born 31 May 1980 in Duisburg) is a German former footballer.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.kicker.de/news/fussball/2bundesliga/vereine/2-bundesliga/2002-03/alemannia-aachen-110/29398/spieler_dirk-caspers.html | title = Caspers, Dirk | language = German | publisher = kicker.de | accessdate = 29 September 2013[/tpl]

==Career==

Caspers played for several clubs in North-Rhine Westphalia, including many reserve teams, and made 10 appearances in the 2. Bundesliga for Alemannia Aachen.
