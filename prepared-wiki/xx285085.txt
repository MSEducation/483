[[Daisy grubber]]

CATEGORIES: Gardening tools

A daisy grubber is a garden tool that is used to pull out roots.[tpl]cite web|title=grubber|url=http://books.google.ca/books?id=anecAQAAQBAJ&pg=PA776&dq=Daisy+Grubber&hl=en&sa=X&ei=o0yXUr2-B4fyqQGVo4GwDw&redir_esc=y#v=onepage&q=Daisy%20Grubber&f=false|work=Oxford Dictionary of English|publisher=Oxford University Press|accessdate=28 November 2013[/tpl]  It is effective because it can pull out deep roots yet cause little or no disturbance to the surrounding soil.
