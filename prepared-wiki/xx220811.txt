[[Platyptilia eberti]]

CATEGORIES: Pterophoridae, Endemic fauna of the Philippines

Platyptilia eberti is a moth of the family Pterophoroidea. It is found on LuzonReview of the Pterophoridae (Lepidoptera) from the Philippines and was recently discovered in Vietnam, although this record might represent a related undescribed species.On The Plume Moths (Lepidoptera, Pterophoridae) Of Vietnam
The wingspan is ca. 17 mm. Adults are on wing in November.

==Etymology==

The species is called after Mr W. Ebert, one of the collectors.
