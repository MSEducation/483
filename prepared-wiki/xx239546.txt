[[Dr. Who & the Daleks (soundtrack)]]

CATEGORIES: Music based on Doctor Who, 2009 soundtracks

Dr. Who & the Daleks is a soundtrack album of music from the two Dalek films based on the BBC television series Doctor Who.  It includes music from Dr. Who and the Daleks by Malcolm Lockyer, music from Daleks' Invasion Earth: 2150 A.D. by Bill McGuffie with electronic effects by Barry Gray.
