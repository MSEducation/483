[[James F. Fairman]]

CATEGORIES: IEEE Edison Medal recipients, 1896 births, 1967 deaths

James Ferdinand Fairman was an American electrical engineer who received the IEEE Edison Medal in 1959 for "outstanding performance in improving the design of large electric power systems; for far-sighted leadership in atomic power development; and for unremitting efforts to improve the engineering profession".[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.ieeeghn.org/wiki/index.php/James_F._Fairman |title=James F.
