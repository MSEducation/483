[[The Girl in the Taxi (1937 film)]]

CATEGORIES: 1937 films, 1930s musical comedy films, British musical comedy films, English-language films, Films directed by André Berthomieu, Films based on plays, British films

The Girl in the Taxi is a 1937 British musical comedy film directed by André Berthomieu and starring Frances Day, Henri Garat and Lawrence Grossmith.http://ftvdb.bfi.org.uk/sift/title/34693 It was based on the stage musical The Girl in the Taxi.
