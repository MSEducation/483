[[Crataerina]]

CATEGORIES: Parasitic flies, Parasites of birds, Diptera of Europe, Diptera of Asia, Hippoboscidae

Crataerina is a genus of  Louse flies in the family Hippoboscidae. All are parasites of birds, feeding on the blood of various species of Apodidae (swifts) and Hirundinidae (swallows and martins). The genus is sometimes spelled Craterina.Evenhuis, N.L. 2010: Type designations of Diptera (Insecta) in the Encyclopaedia Metropolitana. Zootaxa, 2653: 37-50. Preview
