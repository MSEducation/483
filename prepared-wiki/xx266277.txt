[[Cathedral of the Sacred Heart (San Angelo, Texas)]]

CATEGORIES: Religious organizations established in 1884, Roman Catholic cathedrals in the United States, Roman Catholic churches in Texas, Modernist architecture in Texas, Buildings and structures in San Angelo, Texas, Churches in Tom Green County, Texas

The Cathedral of the Sacred Heart is a cathedral church located in San Angelo, Texas, United States.   It is the seat of the Catholic Diocese of San Angelo.   Sacred Heart Parish was founded in 1884,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.gcatholic.org/churches/northamerica/2454.htm|title=Sacred Heart Cathedral|publisher= GCatholic.org|accessdate=2011-09-22|last=|first=[/tpl] and it was named a cathedral when the Diocese of San Angelo was established in 1961.[tpl]cite web|url= http://www.catholic-hierarchy.org/diocese/dsang.html|title=Diocese of San Angelo|publisher=Catholic-Hierarchy|accessdate=2011-09-22|last=|first=[/tpl]  The present church was built in the Modern architectural style.
