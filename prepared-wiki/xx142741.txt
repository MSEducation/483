[[Schooled (magazine)]]

CATEGORIES: Media in Salt Lake City, Utah, Monthly magazines, Local interest magazines

Schooled magazine is a monthly magazine based in Provo, Utah and has been in operation since September 2003. It has a readership of 40,000+ students in Utah Valley.  It is owned and managed by Russ Taylor. Schooled magazine is written for and by students and focuses on college student life.
