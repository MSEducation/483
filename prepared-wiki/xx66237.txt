[[Shirlee Matheson]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Canadian children's writers, Athabasca University alumni, Writers from Alberta, Canadian women writers, 1943 births

Shirlee Smith Matheson is a Canadian children's writer.
She was born in Winnipeg, Manitoba, on October 16, 1943.
A graduate of Athabasca University, she has lived in Winnipeg, Saskatchewan, Alberta, and British Columbia. She now lives in Calgary, Alberta.
