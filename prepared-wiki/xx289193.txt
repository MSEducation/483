[[Dini Beyk]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Dini Beyk ([tpl]lang-fa|ديني بيك[/tpl], also Romanized as Dīnī Beyk; also known as Dīn Beyg and Dīnī Bak)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3773148|Dini Beyk[/tpl] is a village in Shivanat Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 114, in 23 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
