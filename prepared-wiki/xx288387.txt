[[George Klicka]]

CATEGORIES: 1934 births, Living people, People from Milwaukee, Wisconsin, People from Wauwatosa, Wisconsin, Marquette University alumni, Businesspeople from Wisconsin, Wisconsin Republicans, Members of the Wisconsin State Assembly

George H. Klicka (b. December 23, 1934) is an American businessman and former politician.
Born in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, Klicka served in the United States Army and Wisconsin National Guard during the Berlin Crisis. Klicka went to Marquette University and was a print ink salesman. Klicka served in the Wisconsin State Assembly 1967-1991 as a Republican and lived in Wauwatosa, Wisconsin.Wisconsin Historical Society-George H. Klicka'Wisconsin Blue Book 1981-1982,' Biographical Sketch of George H. Klicka, pg. 37
Klicka was the father of Chris Klicka who was a lawyer in support of home schooling.The Home School Court Report
