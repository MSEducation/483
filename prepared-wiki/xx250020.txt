[[First Presbyterian Church (Plattsburgh, New York)]]

CATEGORIES: Presbyterian churches in New York, Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Gothic Revival architecture in New York, Churches completed in 1868, 19th-century Presbyterian church buildings, Churches in Clinton County, New York

First Presbyterian Church is a historic church at 34 Brinkerhoff Street in Plattsburgh, New York.
It was built in 1868 and added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1982.
