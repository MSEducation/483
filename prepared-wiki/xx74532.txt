==History==

The Konoe claim descent from Fujiwara Iezane (1179–1242).  The family was founded by Konoe Motozane.[tpl]fact|date=August 2013[/tpl]
The Konoe was one of the five Fujiwara families from which the Sesshō and Kampaku were chosen.  The Konoe is the original (main) branch of the Fujiwara clan.[tpl]fact|date=August 2013[/tpl]
Tadateru Konoe is the 50th Head of the family which goes back to Kamatari Fujiwara (614-669), Michinaga Fujiwara (966-1027) the Patron and the model of Hikaru no Kimi of "Tale of Genji". And have been the main member of the Royal Family of for over 1000 years.Sandler, Stanley. (2012). "Konoe Fumimaro (1891-1945)," World War II in the Pacific: An Encyclopedia, p. 671.
Prime Minister Fumimaro Konoe was a member of this family.Nussbaum,  "Konoe Fumimaro" in Japan Encyclopedia, p. 558-559. His brother Prince Hidemaro Konoye was a prominent conductor who founded the New Symphony Orchestra of Tokyo, now the NHK Symphony Orchestra.[tpl]fact|date=August 2013[/tpl]
