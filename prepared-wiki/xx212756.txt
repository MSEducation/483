[[Krzywe, Lublin Voivodeship]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Krasnystaw County

Krzywe [tpl]IPAc-pl|'|k|sz|y|w|e[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Łopiennik Górny, within Krasnystaw County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) – TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|5|km|mi|0[/tpl] south-west of Łopiennik Górny, [tpl]convert|13|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] west of Krasnystaw, and [tpl]convert|40|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-east of the regional capital Lublin.
