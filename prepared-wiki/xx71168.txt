Reinhard Höppner (born 1948 in Haldensleben) is a German politician (SPD).[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.randomhouse.com/author/results.pperl?authorid=13501|title=Reinhard Hoppner|publisher=Random House, Inc|accessdate=23 January 2010[/tpl]
He was Minister-President of Saxony-Anhalt from July 1994 until May 16, 2002, when he was succeeded by Wolfgang Böhmer.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.wolfgang-boehmer.de/lebenslauf.html|title=wolfgang böhmer|author=Wolfgang Böhmer|accessdate=23 January 2010[/tpl]
