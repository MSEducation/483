[[Matosinhos]]

CATEGORIES: Cities in Portugal, Port cities and towns in Portugal, Populated places in Porto District

Matosinhos is a city in Matosinhos Municipality in northern Portugal. The city has a population of 45,703.UMA POPULAÇÃO QUE SE URBANIZA, Uma avaliação recente – Cidades, 2004 Nuno Pires Soares, Instituto Geográfico Português (Geographic Institute of Portugal)
The city was the birthplace of the architect Siza Vieira.
She Changes, a monumental sculpture by American artist Janet Echelman, sits on the waterfront of Porto and Matosinhos. She Changes Janet Echelman's website
