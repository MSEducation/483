[[Rashtrottahana Parishat]]

CATEGORIES: Organisations based in Karnataka

Rashtrottahana Parishat is a social service organisation in Karnataka, India, founded in Bangalore in 1965. It is an initiative of Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh for social reforms in Karnataka.[tpl]citation needed|date=August 2013[/tpl]
Rashtrottahana Parishat runs various projects across Karnataka.
Rashtrotthana Rakta Nidhi (Blood Bank) is one among those. It was established in Chamarajpet, Bangalore in 1993. It is an ISO certified blood bank,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.hindu.com/2007/03/01/stories/2007030119630400.htm |title=Engagements / Bangalore : In Bangalore Today |publisher=The Hindu |date=2007-03-01 |accessdate=2013-08-12[/tpl] provides blood for the needy 24 hours each day.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.karunadu.gov.in/Emergency/Pages/BloodBanks.aspx |title=Emergency Contact Numbers - Blood Banks |publisher=Karunadu.gov.in |date=2013-07-23 |accessdate=2013-08-12[/tpl]
In 2011-2012, the blood bank collected 48,647 units of blood through various blood donation camps, and is reported to be the largest blood bank in the state.[tpl]cite web|url=http://samvada.org/2012/news/rashtrotthana-blood-bank-tops-in-blood-collection-transfusion-in-karnataka/ |title=Rashtrotthana Blood Bank tops in blood collection-transfusion in Karnataka | Vishwa Samvada Kendra |publisher=Samvada.org |date=2012-06-22 |accessdate=2013-08-12[/tpl]
