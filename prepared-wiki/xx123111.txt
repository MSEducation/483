[[Mountain Falls, Virginia]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Frederick County, Virginia, Unincorporated communities in Virginia

Mountain Falls is an unincorporated community in Frederick County, Virginia, USA. Mountain Falls is situated near the confluence of Richards Run and Fall Run, from which it takes its name. Mountain Falls is also the location of the crossroads of Wardensville Grade, Back Mountain Road, and Mountain Falls Road.
