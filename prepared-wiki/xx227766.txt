[[Nam Hyun-Seong]]

Nam Hyun-Seong ([tpl]Ko-hhrm|남현성[/tpl]; born 6 May 1985) is a South Korean football midfielder.

==Club career==

Nam joined Daegu FC from his youth club Sungkyunkwan University as a draftee for the 2008 season, and would make just four appearances in the senior side that season.  He gained more matchplay in the 2009 season, playing in eight K-League matches up until the mid-season break.  On 1 July 2009, Nam transferred to Czech Republic club FC Banik Ostrava,http://nzl.worldfootball.net/spieler_profil/hyun-seong-nam/ who play in the Gambrinus liga, the Czech Republic's premier league.  
Nam made his first start for his new club on 8 August 2009, coming on as a 90th minute substitute in a match against Viktoria Plzen.http://www.football-lineups.com/match/59117  However, this proved to be his only match in his one year stint with FC Banik.  During the 2010 K-League mid-season break, Nam returned to Daegu FC.
