[[KVAN (AM)]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Washington (state), News and talk radio stations in the United States

KVAN (1560 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a News/Talk format. Located near Burbank, Washington, USA, the station serves the Tri-Cities area.  The station is owned by Compadres, LLC.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=KVAN |title=KVAN Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==History==
