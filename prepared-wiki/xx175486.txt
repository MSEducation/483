[[KTLX]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Nebraska, Christian radio stations in the United States

KTLX (91.3 FM) is a Christian radio station licensed to Columbus, Nebraska.  The station is owned by TLC Educational Corporation.
KTGW fcc.gov. Accessed August 22, 2012

==Construction permit==

On October 12, 2011, KTLX was granted an FCC construction permit to move to 91.3 MHz, increase ERP to 250 watts from 100 watts and decrease HAAT from 16 metres to 10. A license to cover was issued on June 18, 2013.
