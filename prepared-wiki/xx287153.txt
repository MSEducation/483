[[Ala Chaman]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Ala Chaman ([tpl]lang-fa|الاچمن[/tpl], also Romanized as A‘lā Chaman and Ālā Chaman)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3766490|Ala Chaman[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Bala Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,048, in 233 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
