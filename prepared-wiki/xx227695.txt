[[Philomycus togatus]]

CATEGORIES: Philomycidae

Philomycus togatus, also known as the toga mantleslug, is a species of land slug, a terrestrial pulmonate gastropod mollusk in the family Philomycidae.

==Anatomy==

These slugs create and use love darts as part of their mating behavior.Ken Hotopp: Land Snails of Pennsylvania: Philomycus togatus (Gould, 1841), published online 2 January 2006.
