[[Shinshūshinmachi, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Dissolved municipalities of Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Shinshūshinmachi|信州新町|Shinshūshin-machi[/tpl] was a town located in Kamiminochi District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the town had an estimated population of 5,762 and a density of 81.46 persons per km². The total area was 70.73 km².
On January 1, 2010, Shinshūshinmachi, along with the village of Nakajō (also from Kamiminochi District), was merged into the expanded city of Nagano.[tpl]cite web|title=長野市・信州新町・中条村合併後調査報告について|url=http://www.city.nagano.nagano.jp/pcp_portal/PortalServlet?DISPLAY_ID=DIRECT&NEXT_DISPLAY_ID=U000004&CONTENTS_ID=13354|work=Nagano official website|publisher=Nagano City|accessdate=18 September 2011|language=Japanese[/tpl]
