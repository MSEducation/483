[[2345 Fučik]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Asteroids named for people, Astronomical objects discovered in 1974

2345 Fučik is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1913.2713054 days (5.24 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=2345 | title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-04-28 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on July 25, 1974.
