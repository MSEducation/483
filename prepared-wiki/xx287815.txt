[[Elham Aminzadeh]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Alumni of the University of Glasgow, Iranian academics, University of Tehran faculty, Iranian Majlis Representatives, Iranian women in politics, Female Iranian Vice Presidents

Elham Aminzadeh is an Iranian academic, lawmaker and vice president of Iran in the cabinet of Hassan Rouhani.

==Education==

Aminzadeh holds a PhD in law from the University of Glasgow in 1997. The title of her PhD thesis is "the United Nations and international peace and security: a legal and practical analysis".[tpl]cite web|title=The United Nations and international peace and security: A legal and practical analysis|url=http://theses.gla.ac.uk/734/|work=University of Glasgow|accessdate=13 August 2013[/tpl]

==Career==

Aminzadeh worked as assistant professor of law at the University of Tehran and her speciality is in the fields of international public law, energy law and human rights.[tpl]cite web|title=Faculty Members|url=http://www.ut.ac.ir/en/contents/FacultyMembers-law/Faculty.Members.html|work=University of Tehran|accessdate=13 August 2013[/tpl] She also taught at the University of the Foreign Affairs Ministry, Allameh Tabatabaei University[tpl]cite news|last=Nasseri|first=Ladane|title=Rohani Picks Female Vice-President After Vow to Back Women|url=http://www.bloomberg.com/news/2013-08-13/iran-s-rohani-picks-female-vp-after-pledge-to-back-iranian-women.html|accessdate=13 August 2013|newspaper=Bloomberg|date=13 August 2013[/tpl] and Imam Sadegh University. She served at the seventh term of the Majlis as a lawmaker from 2004 to 2008.[tpl]cite news|title=New Vice Presidents Appointed In Iran|url=http://en.haberler.com/new-vice-presidents-appointed-in-iran-291180/|accessdate=13 August 2013|newspaper=Haberler (News)|date=12 August 2013[/tpl] She was the deputy head of the Majlis's national security and foreign policy committee.[tpl]cite news|last=Esfandiari|first=Esfandiari|title=Perhaps Bowing To Pressure, Rohani Appoints Woman To Iranian Cabinet|url=http://www.rferl.org/content/rohani-woman-cabinet-iran/25074111.html|accessdate=13 August 2013|newspaper=Radio Free Europe|date=13 August 2013[/tpl]
