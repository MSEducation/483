[[Oberwinkleria]]

CATEGORIES: Ustilaginomycotina, Monotypic Basidiomycota genera

Oberwinkleria is a genus of fungi in the Tilletiaceae family. This is a monotypic genus, containing the single species Oberwinkleria anulata.[tpl]cite journal |author=Vánky K, Bauer R. |year=1995 |title=Studies in Heterobasidiomycetes .99.
