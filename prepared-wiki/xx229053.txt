[[Madanpur, Bagmati]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Nuwakot District

 
Madanpur, Nepal is a Village Development Committee in Nuwakot District in the Bagmati Zone of central Nepal. At the time of the 1991 Nepal census it had a population of 7995 people residing in 1334 individual households.[tpl]citation| url=http://www.digitalhimalaya.com/collections/nepalcensus/form.php?selection=1|title=Nepal Census 2001|journal=Nepal's Village Development Committees|publisher=Digital Himalaya|accessdate= 6 October 2008 [/tpl]
