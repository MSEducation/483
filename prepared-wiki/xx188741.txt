[[Chacapampa District]]

Chacapampa or Chakapampa (Aymara and Quechua chaka bridge, pampa plain, "bridge plain") is one of twenty-eight districts of the province Huancayo in Peru.[tpl]es icon[/tpl] Instituto Nacional de Estadística e Informática. Banco de Información Distrital. Retrieved April 11, 2008.
