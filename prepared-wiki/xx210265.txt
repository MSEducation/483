[[Goolwa Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in South Australia

Goolwa Airport [tpl]airport codes||YGWA[/tpl] is an airport located [tpl]Convert|2.2|NM|lk=in[/tpl] northwest of Goolwa, South Australia.
