[[Gözce]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Mersin Province, Mediterranean Region, Turkey, Villages in Turkey, Populated places in Bozyazı District, Populated coastal places in Turkey

 
Gözce is a village in Bozyazı district of Mersin Province, Turkey. It is situated at the west bank of a creek and about [tpl]convert|1|km[/tpl] north of the Mediterranean Sea coast. The state highway [tpl]TUR-D|400[/tpl] is at the south of the village. The distance to Bozyazı is [tpl]convert|20|km[/tpl] and to Mersin is [tpl]convert|200|km[/tpl]. The population of the village is 847.[tpl]cite web|url=  http://rapor.tuik.gov.tr/reports/rwservlet?adnksdb2&ENVID=adnksdb2Env&report=wa_idari_yapi_10sonrasi.RDF&p_il1=33&p_yil=2011&p_dil=2&desformat=html |title=Turkstat|publisher=Turkish Statistical Institute|accessdate=2012[/tpl] as of 2011.
