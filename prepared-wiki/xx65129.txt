[[Paraćin]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Pomoravlje District, Municipalities and cities of Šumadija and Western Serbia

Paraćin ([tpl]lang-sr-Cyrl|Параћин[/tpl], [tpl]IPA-sh|pâratɕin|pron[/tpl]) is a town and municipality in Serbia, located in the valley of the Velika Morava river, north of Kruševac and southeast of Kragujevac. In 2011 the town has total population of 24,573 and it has a civil airport, also known as Davidovac Airport.
