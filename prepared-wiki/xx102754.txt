[[Pojana Maggiore]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Veneto

Pojana Maggiore (Poiana Maggiore) is a town and comune in the province of Vicenza, Veneto, Italy. It is the site of Villa Pojana, designed by the Renaissance architect Andrea Palladio. The town has reputation for its agriculture.  It is bounded by the other communes of Noventa Vicentina and Montagnana.
