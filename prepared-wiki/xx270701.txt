[[Carla Porta Musa]]

CATEGORIES: 1902 births, Italian supercentenarians, People from Como, 2012 deaths

Carla Porta Musa (15 March 1902[tpl]spaced ndash[/tpl]10 October 2012) was an Italian essayist and poet.  Musa was born in Como, Italy, to Maria Casella and Enrico Musa, a well known engineer in Milan. Her passion for books started on her sixth birthday when her father gave her a small library which she shared with her three siblings. She studied in Lausanne, Switzerland, Bushey, United Kingdom, and Paris, France.[tpl]Cite news|last = Novaga|first = Michele|title = Carla Porta Musa, un secolo di scrittura|newspaper = swissinfo|language = Italian|publisher = SRG SSR|date = 2010-05-17|url = http://www.swissinfo.ch/ita/cultura/Carla_Porta_Musa,_un_secolo_di_scrittura.html?cid=8819288|accessdate = 2011-09-25[/tpl]
She died on 10 October 2012 at Valduce Hospital in Como from pneumonia complications.[tpl]cite web|title=Morta Carla Porta Musa|url=http://www.laprovinciadicomo.it/stories/Cronaca/319827_ricoverata_carla_porta_musa_e_in_gravi_condizioni/|publisher=La Provincia di Como|accessdate=10 October 2012[/tpl]
