The Brother Kite is an American alternative rock band, formed in Providence, Rhode Island in 2002.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.allmusic.com/album/isolation-mw0002027513|title=Review: The Brother Kite |work=Allmusic|date=September 2010|first=Ben|last=Peterson|accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl] The band consists of Patrick Boutwell on guitar and lead vocals; Jon Downs on guitar and vocals; Andrea Downs on bass guitar; Mark Howard on guitar, keyboard, and vocals; and Matt Rozzero on drums and sampler.[tpl]cite news|url=http://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/9643-waiting-for-the-time-to-be-right/|title=The Brother Kite: Waiting for the Time to Be Right |work=Pitchfork Media|date=27 November 2006|first=Joe|last=Tangari|accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite news|url=http://thephoenix.com/boston/music/109122-brother-kites-isolation-was-worth-the-wait/|title=The Brother Kite's Isolation Was Worth The Wait|work=The Phoenix|date=29 September 2010|first=Chris|last=Conti|accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite news|url=http://bostonist.com/2009/06/03/live_review_the_brother_kite_middle.php/|title=Live Review: The Brother Kite @ Middle East|work=Bostonist|date=3 June 2009|first=Jill|last=D'Urso|accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl]
In 2008, their song "The Coat of Arms" from the album Waiting for the Time to be Right was featured in the British television series Skins.
