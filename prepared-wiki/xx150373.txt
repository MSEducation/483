[[Papín]]

CATEGORIES: Villages and municipalities in Humenné District

Papín is a village and municipality in Humenné District in the Prešov Region of north-east Slovakia.[tpl]Citation needed|date=August 2007[/tpl]

==History==

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1451.[tpl]Citation needed|date=August 2007[/tpl]

==Geography==

The municipality lies at an altitude of 280 metres and covers an area of 25.878 km².[tpl]Citation needed|date=August 2007[/tpl] It has a population of about 1090 people.[tpl]Citation needed|date=August 2007[/tpl]
