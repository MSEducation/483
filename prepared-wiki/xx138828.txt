[[Nepal at the 1988 Summer Olympics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 1988 Summer Olympics, Nepal at the Olympics, 1988 in Nepal

Nepal competed at the 1988 Summer Olympics in Seoul, Korea.  Bidhan Lama, won a bronze medal in  taekwondo which was then an exhibition sport.
