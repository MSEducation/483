[[Maankulam]]

CATEGORIES: Mullaitivu District, Populated places in Northern Province, Sri Lanka

Maankulam or Mankulam is located in northern Sri Lanka, about 3.5 hours drive south of City of Jaffna. 

==Transport==

Major road A9 and a railway line connecting North and South of Sri Lanka goes through Maankulam.  Maankulam is also located few hours drive south of Kilinochchi and few hours drive north of Vavuniya.  Highway A34 connects Maankulam to eastern coastal City of Mullaitivu.
