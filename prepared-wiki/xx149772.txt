[[Aspidosperma populifolium]]

CATEGORIES: Aspidosperma, Trees of Brazil, Honey plants

Aspidosperma populifolium is a tree native to Brazil, which is typical of Caatinga vegetation. In addition, it is useful for beekeeping. It was first described by Alphonse Pyrame de Candolle in 1844.[tpl]IPNI|id=77434-1|accessdate=14 November 2010[/tpl]
