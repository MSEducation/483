[[Andy May (footballer born 1964)]]

Andrew Michael Peter "Andy" May (born 26 February 1964 in Collyhurst) is a former professional footballer, who played for Manchester City, Huddersfield Town, Bolton Wanderers, Bristol City, Millwall and Welling United. In October 1995 he also joined Larne F.C. in the Irish Football League for a loan spell.Marshall Gillespie, The Northern Ireland Football Yearbook 1996/97, p. 200
