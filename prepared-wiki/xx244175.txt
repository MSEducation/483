[[Radio Vox T]]

CATEGORIES: Media in Iaşi, Romanian-language radio stations, Radio stations in Romania

Vox T was an FM radio station in Iaşi, Romania. The station signed on in March 1990 as the first independent non-public radio station outside Bucharest, and the third in Romania after the change from Socialist system.Un deceniu de presa in Romania[tpl]ro icon[/tpl] It operated out of T17 students residence hall, part of the Technical University Tudor Vladimirescu campus area. During the first years of its broadcasting, Vox T enjoyed wide public interest with its phone-in talk-shows and music programmes.
Now Radio Vox T is no longer in operation.
