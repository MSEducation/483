[[Viborg Municipality]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Central Denmark Region, Municipalities of Denmark, Viborg Municipality

Viborg municipality is a municipality (Danish, kommune) in Region Midtjylland on the Jutland peninsula in northern Denmark.  The municipality covers an area of 1,390 km², and has a total population of 94,218 (2012).  Its mayor is Søren Pape Poulsen, a member of the Conservative People's Party (Denmark)
The main city and the site of its municipal council is the city of Viborg.
On 1 January 2007 Viborg municipality was, as the result of Kommunalreformen ("The Municipal Reform" of 2007), merged with Bjerringbro, Fjends, Karup, Møldrup, and Tjele municipalities  to form an enlarged Viborg municipality.

==Twin cities==

Viborg has a twin city in each of the Nordic countries, as well as in other world regions.
