[[Florida Law Related Education Association]]

CATEGORIES: Legal organizations based in the United States, Non-profit organizations based in Florida, Legal education in the United States, Educational organizations based in the United States, 1984 establishments in Florida, Organizations established in 1984

The Florida Law Related Education Association, Inc. (FLREA) is a private corporation classified as a non-profit organization under the 501(c)(3) section of the United States Internal Revenue Code. The main offices are located at 2874 Remington Green Circle, Suite A, Tallahassee, Florida, 32308, United States.[tpl]cite web| last = | first = | authorlink = | coauthors = | title = The Florida Law Related Education Association, Inc. | work = | publisher = Idealist:Action Without Borders| date = 2007-01-25| url = http://www.idealist.org/en/org/159073-214| format = | doi = | accessdate = 2008-05-20 [/tpl]

==History==

FLREA was established in 1984 “to improve justice and expand education for democracy through the development and implementation of law related education programs for Florida youth”[tpl]cite web| last = | first = | authorlink = | coauthors = | title = About FLREA| work = FLREA| publisher = Florida Law Related Education Association, Inc.| year = 2005| url = http://www.flrea.org/about.html| doi = | accessdate = 2008-05-20[/tpl] The organization has grown into one of the premiere and most respected law related education associations in the country.
The current executive director is Annette Boyd Pitts[tpl]cite web| last = | first = | authorlink = | coauthors = | title = Civics in Florida| work = | publisher = Florida Law Related Education Association, Inc.| date = | url = http://www.flrea.org/reports/FLREAPowerpoint.ppt| format = PowerPoint Presentation| doi = | accessdate = 2008-05-20[/tpl]

==Awards==

FLREA has received the National Improvements in Justice Award and the Isidore Starr Award for excellence in law related education.[tpl]cite web| last = | first = | authorlink = | coauthors = | title = Welcome to FLREA!| work = | publisher = Florida Law Related Education Association, Inc.| year = 2005| url = http://www.flrea.org/index.html| doi = | accessdate = 2008-05-20[/tpl] In addition, participants in the FLREA Mock Trial competition have gone on to place in the top ten at the National High School Mock Trial Championship.
