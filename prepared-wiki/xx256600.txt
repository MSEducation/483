[[Jackpot (Irish TV series)]]

CATEGORIES: 1962 television series debuts, 1965 television series endings, 1960s in Irish television, Irish quiz shows, RTÉ television programmes

Jackpot was an Irish general knowledge quiz show produced by Telefís Éireann between 6 January 1962 and 9 June 1965."Television and radio programmes", The Irish Times, 6 January 1962  Presented firstly by Gay Byrne and later by Terry Wogan, the show remained one of the most popular programmes in the first years of the television station.  Jackpot was similar in format to the ITV quiz show Criss Cross Quiz."The Wheel of Fortune", The Irish Times, 29 March 1962
