[[Pyigyidagun Township]]

CATEGORIES: Townships of Mandalay, Townships of Mandalay Region, Mandalay

Pyigyidagun Township (also spelled Pyigyitagun Township; [tpl]lang-my|ပြည်ကြီးတံခွန် မြို့နယ်[/tpl], [tpl]IPA-my|pjìdʑí dəɡʊ̀ɴ mjo̰nɛ̀|pron[/tpl]) is located in the southern part of Mandalay, Myanmar. The township is bounded by Chanmyathazi township in the north and the west, and Amarapura in the south.[tpl]Cite web| url=http://www.dpsmap.com/mandalay | title=Map of Mandalay | publisher=DPS | accessdate=2009-04-04[/tpl]
