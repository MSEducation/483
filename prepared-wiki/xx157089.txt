[[Rock the World (Five Star album)]]

CATEGORIES: Five Star albums, 1988 albums, Albums produced by Leon Sylvers III

Rock The World is the fourth album by the British pop group Five Star. It was released in 1988 and reached #17 on the UK charts.
Two singles from the album were UK Top 30 hits: "Another Weekend" and "Rock My World". The latter was the group's final chart hit. Two further singles from the album, "There's A Brand New World" and "Let Me Be Yours", failed to reached the Top 40. Following its release, the album was awarded a Silver disc for selling more than 60,000 copies in the UK.
The group adopted a new image for the release of the album, mimicking the leather-clad look from Michael Jackson's "Bad", released the previous year. 
An expanded version of Rock the World was released by Cherry Pop Records in April 2012.
