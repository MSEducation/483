Kim Yi-Yong ([tpl]lang-ko|김이용[/tpl]; born 20 September 1973 in Gangwon-do) is a retired South Korean long-distance runner who specialized in the marathon. He was the 2004 Asian Marathon Championship winner.Asian Championships Marathon. Association of Road Racing Statisticians (2013-03-02). Retrieved on 2013-09-28.
His personal best for the marathon is 2:07:49 hours, set in 1999.World men's all-time best marathon (last updated 2001)
