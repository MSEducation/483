[[List of Casshern Sins episodes]]

CATEGORIES: Lists of anime episodes

This is a list of episodes for Casshern Sins, a remake of the original Casshern anime. Casshern Sins was first announced at the Tokyo International Anime Fair 2008 with Madhouse being in charge of animation.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/news/2008-06-19/new-casshern-anime-tentatively-titled-casshern-sins|title=New Casshern Anime Tentatively Titled Casshern Sins|accessdate=2009-01-18|publisher=Anime News Network[/tpl] Soon after, an official trailer was posted online on its official Japanese website.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/news/2008-08-08/casshern-sins-trailer-posted-online|title=Casshern Sins Trailer Posted Online|accessdate=2009-01-18|publisher=Anime News Network[/tpl] The 2008 show was directed by Shiyasu Yamauchi.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.casshern-sins.jp/onair.html|title=Casshern Sins's On Air Page|accessdate=2009-10-31|language=Japanese[/tpl]
Casshern Sins centers on Casshern, who is originally a subordinate of Braiking Boss instead of being a hero to humanity. He was sent to assassinate a girl named Luna due to Boss' fears that she would liberate humanity from his grip. Her death resulted in a war that has humanity on the losing end with robots being on the verge of death due to the radical change in the environment; a legend was made known to the robots that Casshern's death can save them all.
The show had previously aired on Japan's Chiba TV, TV Aichi, MBS, TV Kanagawa & TV Saitama stations. The show aired in Japan from October 1, 2008 to March 18, 2009.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.allcinema.net/prog/show_c.php?num_c=331525#1|title=キャシャーンSins(2008)|accessdate=2009-10-31|language=Japanese|publisher=All Cinema[/tpl] Casshern Sins had also aired on Singapore's Arts Central channel.[tpl]cite web|url=http://artscentral.mediacorptv.com/WhatShowing/Wednesday/index.htm|title=Arts Central's Casshern Sins Page|accessdate=2009-10-31[/tpl] The series aired in North American on December 14, 2010, on the Funimation Channel.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.funimationchannel.com/schedule/2_e01214.htm|title=Funimation Channel Schedule|publisher=Funimation Channel|date=2010-12-14[/tpl] Cartoon Network and Adult Swim will broadcast the anime on their revived Toonami, premiering May 27, 2012.[tpl]cite web|title=Adult Swim's Toonami Block to Broadcast Deadman Wonderland, Casshern Sins|url=http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/news/2012-05-21/adult-swim-toonami-block-to-air-casshern-sins-deadman-wonderland|publisher=Anime News Network|accessdate=22 May 2012[/tpl]
