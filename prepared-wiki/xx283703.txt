[[National electoral calendar 2016]]

CATEGORIES: 2016 elections

This national electoral calendar for the year 2016 lists the national/federal direct elections to be held in 2016 in all sovereign states and their dependent territories. By-elections are excluded, though national referendums are included. Specific dates are given where they have been known, but most have not yet been scheduled.
