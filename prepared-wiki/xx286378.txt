[[Esfajin]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Esfajin ([tpl]lang-fa|اسفجين[/tpl], also Romanized as Esfajīn and Esfejīn; also known as Isbadzhin and Isbājin)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3062147|Esfajin[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Bala Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 835, in 201 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
