[[Xin'an County]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Luoyang

Xin'an County (Chinese: 新安县; Pinyin: Xīn'ān Xiàn，Hakka Dialect:Sin On Yen) is a part of Luoyang City, Henan Province, People's Republic of China.

==History==

In ancient China, Xin'an was a county located to the west of Luoyang, former capital of the Zhou Dynasty (1049-256 BCE).
