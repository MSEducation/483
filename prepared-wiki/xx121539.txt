[[Ivelet]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in North Yorkshire, Swaledale

Ivelet is a hamlet in the Yorkshire Dales, North Yorkshire, England about a mile west of Gunnerside in Swaledale.
Ivelet bridge crosses the River Swale near Ivelet.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.gunnerside.info/Ivelet/ivelet_bridge.html|title=Ivelet Bridge|work=Gunnerside.info|accessdate=2007-04-09[/tpl] A coffin stone is set into the verge on the north side of the bridge for pall bearers to rest with a coffin on their way from Muker to the church at Grinton. The bridge is featured in the British television series All Creatures Great and Small, in the episode "Call of the Wild".
