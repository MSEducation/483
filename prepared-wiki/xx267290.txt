[[(13060) 1991 EJ]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1991, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Jupiter Trojans (Greek camp)

(13060) 1991 EJ is a Jupiter Trojan minor planet, located in the L4 Lagrangian point.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on March 10, 1991.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
