Jason V Brock (born March 1, 1970) is an American author, artist, editor and director. He is the CEO and co-founder (with his wife, Sunni) of JaSunni Productions, LLC, whose documentary films include the controversial Charles Beaumont: The Short Life of Twilight Zone’s Magic Man,[tpl]cite web|last=Elliot|first=Tom|title=Charles Beaumont: Twilight Zone's Magic Man|url=http://tznet.co.uk/charles-beaumont-twilight-zones-magic-man-jason-sunni-brock-interview/|work=The Twilight Zone Podcast|publisher=The Twilight Zone Network|accessdate=17 August 2011[/tpl] The AckerMonster Chronicles!, and Image, Reflection, Shadow: Artists of the Fantastic. He is also the author of Totems and Taboos, a compilation of his poetry and artwork, and an editor, along with William F.
