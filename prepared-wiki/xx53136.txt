==Reception==

===Box office===

Bound grossed $3,802,260 in the United States. In its opening weekend, showing at 261 theaters, it earned $900,902, which was 23.7% of its total gross. According to Box Office Mojo, it ranked at 161 for all films released in the United States in 1996, and at 74 for R-rated films released that year. As of July 2012, its all-time ranking for LGBT-related films is 59.

===Critical response===

The review aggregator website Rotten Tomatoes gave it a "fresh" rating of 91% based on 35 reviews, while Metacritic gave it a score of 61/100 based on 19 reviews.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/bound/ |title=Bound |accessdate=July 8, 2012 |publisher=Rotten Tomatoes[/tpl][tpl]cite web |url=http://www.metacritic.com/movie/bound|title=Bound |accessdate=May 9, 2007 |publisher=MetaCritic[/tpl] The Wachowskis were widely acclaimed by critics for their debut which was described as clever, sophisticated and stylish.[ref]
