[[Changur]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Changur ([tpl]lang-fa|چنگور[/tpl], also Romanized as Changūr; also known as Chanqūr and Jankūr)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3058048|Changur[/tpl] is a village in Sojas Rud Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 468, in 102 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
