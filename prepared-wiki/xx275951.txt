[[Nahún Solís]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 births, Living people, People from Santa Bárbara Department, Association football defenders, Honduran footballers, Platense F.C. players, Liga Nacional de Fútbol de Honduras footballers

Nahún Alberto Solís Peña (born 18 January 1989 in San Marcos, Santa Bárbara, Honduras) is a Honduran footballer, currently playing as a central defender for Platense.

==Club career==

===Platense===

On 31 January 2010, Solís played his first the Liga Nacional de Honduras, in a 1-0 away win match against Marathón. He scored his first goal on 7 August 2011, in 2-1 home win match against Victoria.Platense comenzó con “victoria”.

==International career==

Solís was called up by the Honduran under-20 team for the 2009 FIFA U-20 World Cup in Egypt.Roster - FIFA
