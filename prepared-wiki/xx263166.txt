[[Paul Jenkins (cricketer)]]

CATEGORIES: 1972 births, Living people, People from Gloucester, English cricketers, Wales Minor Counties cricketers

Paul Edward Jenkins (born 8 February 1972) is an English cricketer.  Jenkins is a right-handed batsman who bowls slow left-arm orthodox.  He was born in Gloucester, Gloucestershire.
Jenkins made his Minor Counties Championship debut for Wales Minor Counties in 1998 against Devon.
