[[Watrous, New Mexico]]

CATEGORIES: Census-designated places in New Mexico, Census-designated places in Mora County, New Mexico

Watrous is a census-designated place  in Mora County, New Mexico, United States. Its population was 135 as of the 2010 census. Watrous has a post office with ZIP code 87753, which opened on April 14, 1868.[tpl]GR|7[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Postmaster Finder - Post Offices by ZIP Code|url=http://webpmt.usps.gov/pmt008.cfm|publisher=United States Postal Service|accessdate=August 7, 2013[/tpl] The community is located along Interstate 25.
