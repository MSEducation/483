[[Makira flying fox]]

CATEGORIES: Pteropus, Animals described in 1908

The Makira flying fox (Pteropus cognatus) is a species of megabat in the Pteropus genus, found in the Solomon Islands.D.E. Wilson & D.M. Reeder, 2005: Mammal Species of the World: A Taxonomic and Geographic Reference. Third Edition. The Johns Hopkins University Press, Baltimore  The species is currently decreasing and is endangered due to threats from logging and hunting.[tpl]IUCN|id=136397|taxon=Pteropus cognatus | assessors = James, R., Hamilton, S. & Helgen, K. | assessment_year= 2008 | version = 3.1 | accessdate= 23 December 2013[/tpl]
