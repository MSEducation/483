[[Fawler]]

CATEGORIES: Hamlets in Oxfordshire, Civil parishes in Oxfordshire, West Oxfordshire

Fawler is a hamlet and civil parish in the valley of the River Evenlode, [tpl]convert|1.5|mi|km[/tpl] southeast of Charlbury in Oxfordshire, England.
There are traces of a Roman villa at Oatlands Farm.Sherwood & Pevsner, 1974, page 605 The manor house was built in 1660.
Finstock railway station on the Cotswold Line is actually closer to Fawler.
