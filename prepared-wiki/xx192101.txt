[[The Human Goddess]]

CATEGORIES: 1972 films, Hong Kong films, 1970s comedy films, Mandarin-language films, Fantasy-comedy films

The Human Goddess (Chinese: 仙女下凡, Sin Lui Ha Fan) is a 1972 Hong Kong fantasy film comedy directed by Ho Heng Hua.HKcinemamagic.com
Lee Ching stars as an alluring fairy goddess who is irresistible to the opposite sex. She is able to manipulate men to achieve her goals, and finds a man who in is charge of an orphanage.
