[[13531 Weizsäcker]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Asteroids named for people, Astronomical objects discovered in 1991

13531 Weizsäcker is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1943.3637932 days (5.32 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=13531 | title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-05-23 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on September 13, 1991. It is named after German physicist and philosopher Carl Friedrich von Weizsäcker.
