[[98th Indian Infantry Brigade]]

CATEGORIES: British Indian Army brigades

The 98th Indian Infantry Brigade was an Infantry formation of the Indian Army during World War II. It was formed in April 1941, at Bareilly. The brigade served with three different divisions in the Burma Campaign. The 34th Indian Infantry Division between October 1941 and January 1942. The 25th Indian Infantry Division between January and March 1942 and the 19th Indian Infantry Division from March 1942, until the end of the war.[tpl]cite web|accessdate=2009-10-23|publisher=Order of Battle|title=98 Indian Brigade|url=http://www.ordersofbattle.com/UnitData.aspx?UniX=6204&Tab=Sup[/tpl]
