[[Kokonoe, Ōita]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Ōita Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Kokonoe|九重町|Kokonoe-machi[/tpl] is a small town located in Kusu District, Ōita Prefecture, Japan. It promotes its nine onsen, which are occasionally closed due to flooding; it is also the summer headquarters of Labo Camp Kujū, part of the LABO language program.
Kokonoe consists mainly of farms, hot springs, and countryside tourist attractions.
As of 2003, the town has an estimated population of 11,289 and the density of 41.59 persons per km². The total area is 271.41 km².
