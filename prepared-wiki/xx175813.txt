[[WMTC-FM]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Kentucky, Breathitt County, Kentucky, Moody Radio affiliate stations

WMTC (99.9 FM) is a Christian radio station, licensed to Vancleve, Kentucky, United States. The station is currently owned by the Kentucky Mountain Holiness Association and features programming from Salem Communications. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=WMTC |title=WMTC Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]  WMTC's format consists of Southern Gospel music, as well as Christian talk and teaching programs such as; Revive our Hearts with Nancy Leigh DeMoss, Joni & Friends, Focus on the Family, and Unshackled!, as well as children's programming such as Adventures in Odyssey and Jonathan Park.
