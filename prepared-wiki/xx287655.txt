[[Montanha]]

CATEGORIES: Villages and settlements in Santiago, Cape Verde, Important Bird Areas of Cape Verde

Montanha is a settlement in the central part of the island of Santiago, Cape Verde. It is situated 8 km southwest of Pedra Badejo. The area is intensively farmed; mainly maize, beans and sugar-cane. 

==Mahogany trees==
