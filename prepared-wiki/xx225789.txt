Bruce Barth, born September 7, 1958 in Pasadena, California, is a jazz pianist.[tpl]cite web| last = Palm   | first = Kristin   | title = Barth Biography   | publisher = Net Industries   | year = 2008   | url = http://www.musicianguide.com/biographies/1608004203/Bruce-Barth.html   | accessdate = 2008-10-25[/tpl][tpl]cite web| title = Barth at All About Jazz   | publisher = All About Jazz   | date = 2007-05-31   | url = http://www.allaboutjazz.com/php/musician.php?id=11847   | accessdate =2008-10-25 [/tpl] He has played the piano since the age of five. Barth's career had included major work with ensembles, as well as solo work. Throughout his career, he has played on the Enja, Double-Time, Fresh Sound, and MAXJAZZ labels.
