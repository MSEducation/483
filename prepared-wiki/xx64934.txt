[[Whiting Writers' Award]]

CATEGORIES: American literary awards, Awards established in 1985, Fiction awards, Non-fiction literary awards, American poetry awards

The Whiting Writers' Award is an American award presented annually to ten emerging writers in fiction, nonfiction, poetry and plays. The award is sponsored by the Mrs. Giles Whiting Foundation and has been presented since 1985. As of 2007, winners receive US $50,000.Mrs. Giles Whiting Foundationhttp://latimesblogs.latimes.com/jacketcopy/2009/10/whiting-awards-presented-to-10-upandcoming-writers-.html
