Kevin Baldemar Moreno (Born May 23, 1984 in Usulután, El Salvador) is a  Salvadoran footballer who last played for Vista Hermosa.

==Club career==

Moreno started his career at Salvadoran second division side Jocoro, then played for two years at Atlético Chaparratique.
