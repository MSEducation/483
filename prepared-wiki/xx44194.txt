[[Toledo Settlement]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Toledo District

Toledo Settlement is a town in Belize's Toledo District.
Toledo Settlement is located at [tpl]Coord|16|8|0|N|88|49|0|W|type:city|display=inline,title[/tpl], at an elevation of 37 meters above sea level. It was originally settled by African American refugees from the United States who had fled during the American Civil War.
