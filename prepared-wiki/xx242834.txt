[[Smith Westerns]]

CATEGORIES: Garage rock groups, Glam rock groups, American indie rock groups, Musical groups from Chicago, Illinois, Fat Possum Records artists

Smith Westerns are an American indie rock band from Chicago, Illinois.[tpl]cite web|last=Mehr |first=Bob |url=http://www.chicagoreader.com/chicago/big-kids/Content?oid=1187816 |title=Big Kids | Music Column |publisher=Chicago Reader |date= |accessdate=2009-10-20[/tpl] Their musical influences include David Bowie, T. Rex[tpl]cite web|url=http://pitchfork.com/news/36019-rising-the-smith-westerns/ |title=Rising: The Smith Westerns |publisher=Pitchfork Media|date= |accessdate=2009-10-20[/tpl] and Oasis.[tpl]cite web|url=http://pitchfork.com/news/40315-smith-westerns-talk-britpop-influenced-new-lp/ |title=Smith Westerns Talk Their New Brit Pop Influenced LP |publisher=Pitchfork Media|date= | accessdate=2010-10-08[/tpl]
Their self-titled debut album was released on HoZac Records on June 5, 2009.[tpl]cite web|url=http://blogs.myspace.com/index.cfm?fuseaction=blog.view&friendId=144202417&blogId=493135982 |title=Smith Westerns LP Out Now on HoZac - MySpace-blog | van The Smith Westerns |publisher=Blogs.myspace.com |date=2009-10-16 |accessdate=2009-10-20[/tpl] Most of the album was recorded throughout the winter and early spring in Max Kakacek's basement.
They released a new single, "Weekend", on November 4, 2010, from their album Dye It Blonde, which was released on January 18, 2011.[tpl]cite web|url=http://pitchfork.com/reviews/tracks/12032-weekend/ |title=Pitchfork: The Playlist: Smith Westerns - "Weekend" |publisher=Pitchfork Media|date=  |accessdate=2010-11-08[/tpl]
The band signed to Mom + Pop Music on March 5, 2013,http://www.momandpopmusic.com/2013/03/smith-westerns-sign-to-mom-and-pop/ and released Soft Will on June 25, 2013, following up from their 2011 LP, Dye It Blonde. The first single off the album, "Varsity," was released March 6, 2013.[tpl]cite news|last=Minsker|first=Evan|title=Smith Westerns Announce New Album Soft Will, Share New Track "Varsity"|url=http://pitchfork.com/news/49781-smith-westerns-announce-new-album-soft-will-share-new-track-varsity/|accessdate=6 March 2013|newspaper=Pitchfork Media|date=6 March 2013[/tpl] The album has received mostly positive reviews upon release.[tpl]Citation needed|date=July 2013[/tpl]
