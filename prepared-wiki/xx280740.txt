[[Gow Ali]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Gow Ali ([tpl]lang-fa|گوالي[/tpl], also Romanized as Gow Ālī, Gūālī, Gavaali, Gavālī; also known as Gawatali)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3063741|Gow Ali[/tpl] is a village in Bonab Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 117, in 31 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
