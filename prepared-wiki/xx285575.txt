[[Doosukeltha]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 films, Indian films, Telugu-language films

Doosukeltha is a 2013 Telugu film directed by Veeru Potla starring Vishnu Manchu.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.indiaglitz.com/channels/telugu/article/94410.html |title=Manchu Vishnu's 'Doosukeltha' shooting in Hyderabad - Telugu Movie News |publisher=Indiaglitz.com |date=2013-06-08 |accessdate=2014-01-23[/tpl] Lavanya Tripathi, who made her debut with Andala Rakshasi, is the leading lady in the movie and Mani Sharma composed the music.[tpl]dead link|date=January 2014[/tpl] Dr. Mohan Babu produced the film under the banner of 24 Frames Factory. Doosukeltha is touted to be a romantic entertainer.[tpl]cite web|author=TNN Jun 3, 2013, 12.52PM IST |url=http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2013-06-03/news-interviews/39713625_1_vishnu-manchu-film-progressing-dhenikaina |title=Vishnu Manchu's film progressing briskly - Times Of India |publisher=Articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com |date=2013-06-03 |accessdate=2014-01-23[/tpl] The film received a U/A Certificate.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.25cineframes.com/doosukeltha-gets-a-ua-certificate.html |title=Doosukeltha gets a U/A Certificate. |publisher=25cineframes.com |accessdate=October 7, 2013[/tpl] It was released on October 17, 2013. The film received positive reviews from critics and was a hit at the box office.
