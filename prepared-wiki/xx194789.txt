[[All Star Records]]

CATEGORIES: Don Omar, Puerto Rican record labels, Reggaeton record labels, Hip hop record labels, Record labels established in 2004

All Star Records Now Orfanato Music Group is Don Omar's record label. He released the album Los Bandoleros under this label in 2005. Don Omar is currently the label's biggest attraction. It operates as a subsidiary of, and is distributed through, Universal Music Group's Machete Records.

==History==

After the debut of the album The Last Don, Don Omar was granted his own label. Since 2004, his label became successful for not only himself, but his appointed label artists.
