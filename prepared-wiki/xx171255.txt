[[Langton Hill]]

CATEGORIES: Hills of Lincolnshire

Langton Hill is a small hill in the town of Horncastle in the English county of Lincolnshire. The hill forming part of the western River Bain Valley. It is one of the first larger hills that form the hilly area that becomes the Lincolnshire Wolds on the other side[tpl]clarify|date=April 2011[/tpl] of Horncastle. The B1191 road, Woodhall Road, runs along the eastern side from Horncastle and is also called 'Langton Hill' but then turns off south where a smaller road goes to the summit. On the summit is Langton Hill Farm and a small communications mast.
