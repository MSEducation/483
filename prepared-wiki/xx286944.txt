[[Bidgineh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Bidgineh ([tpl]lang-fa|بيدگينه[/tpl], also Romanized as Bīdgīneh and Bīd Gīneh; also known as Bichneh, Bīd Geneh, Bīdkaneh, and Bitkinya)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3056300|Bidgineh[/tpl] is a village in Golabar Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 880, in 220 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
