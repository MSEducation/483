==Career==

Nitesh Pandey began doing theatre in 1990.In 1995, Nitesh got his first acting break in a show called Tejas in which he played a detective. Unfortunately,after a few episodes BITV ran into trouble because the satellite was lost.http://www.hindu.com/2010/11/22/stories/2010112251200200.htmhttp://www.indianexpress.com/news/talking-point-with-nitesh-pandey/714150/2 Nitesh Pandey has worked in serials like Manzilein Apani Apani,Astitva...Ek Prem Kahani, Saaya, Justajoo and Durgesh Nandini and in films like Om Shanti Om, Khosla Ka Ghosla. Today he also runs an independent production house - 'Dream Castle Productions' which produce Radio shows.Nitesh did theatre  shows like Aastha and Misal Pav with Sudha Chandran.http://in.news.yahoo.com/talking-point-nitesh-pandey.html His performance was noticed in Khosla ka Ghosla.
