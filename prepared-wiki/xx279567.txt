[[Mizoram College Teachers' Association]]

CATEGORIES: Mizoram, Non-profit organizations, Associations, Education in Mizoram

The Mizoram College Teachers' Association (MCTA) is a governmental, non-political and non-profit organisation for college teachers under the Government of Mizoram, India. It was established by a conference of college teachers on 7 September 1979 at Aizawl, which became its headquarters. The organisation is recognised by the Government of Mizoram, and consists of 21 government colleges and 1 grant-in-aid college.[tpl]cite web| author=IndCareer |title= Mizoram College Teachers Association (MCTA), Aizawl|date=2007â€“2013 | url= http://www.indcareer.com/mizoram-college-teachers-association-mcta-aizawl |work= indcareer.com |publisher=IndCareer.com |accessdate= 9 August 2013[/tpl]

==Membership==

MCTA opens membership to teachers of higher education under the Government of Mizoram, but only those appointed according to the norms of UGC. Enrolment requires a fee of INR 100, and annual fee of INR 50. Membership ceases om death, retirement, transfer to other post, and failure to pay fee. Every member is also imposed to contribute a welfare fund of INR 100 and condolence fund of INR 300 annually.[tpl]cite web| author= MCTA|title= Constitution of the Mizoram College Teachers' Association (MCTA) (Third Edition)|year=2010 | url= http://www.mcta.org.in/download/file/28-mcta-constitution-2010-edition |work=mcta.org.in |publisher= Mizoram College Teachers' Association |accessdate= 9 August 2013[/tpl]
