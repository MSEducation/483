[[Năpradea]]

CATEGORIES: Communes in Sălaj County

Năpradea ([tpl]lang-hu|Náprád[/tpl]) is a commune located in Sălaj County, Romania. It is composed of five villages: Cheud (Köd), Năpradea, Someş-Guruslău (Nagygoroszló), Traniş (Kisgoroszló) and Vădurele (Szamosdebrecen).
