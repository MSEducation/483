[[Jarud Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Jarud Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠵᠠᠷᠤᠳ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Jarud qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=扎鲁特旗 |t=扎魯特旗 |p=Zālǔtè Qí[/tpl]) is a banner of eastern Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China. It is under the administration of Tongliao City, [tpl]convert|150|km[/tpl], and the China National Highway 304 passes through the area.

==Climate==

Jarud Banner has a monsoon-influenced, continental semi-arid climate (Köppen BSk), with very cold and dry winters, hot, somewhat humid summers, and strong winds, especially in spring. The monthly 24-hour average temperature ranges from [tpl]convert|−12.6|°C|1[/tpl] in January to [tpl]convert|23.9|°C|1[/tpl] in July, with the annual mean at [tpl]convert|6.58|°C|1[/tpl]. The annual precipitation is [tpl]convert|383|mm|in|1[/tpl], with more than half of it falling in July and August alone. There are 2,883 hours of bright sunshine annually, with each of the winter months having over two-thirds of the possible total, and this percentage falling to 54 in July.
