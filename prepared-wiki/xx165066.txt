[[J. Levine]]

CATEGORIES: Landmarks in Manhattan, Jewish American literature, Jewish organizations based in the United States, Jews and Judaism in Manhattan, Companies established in 1905

J. Levine Books and Judaica is a family-owned Judaica store located in Midtown Manhattan. J. Levine is famous for being a 5th generation family business, and for being one of the oldest standing Judaica stores in America.

==History==

J. Levine started as a pushcart on the Lower East Side where it only sold synagogue vestments, and has expanded to its current location in Midtown Manhattan which offers the whole gamut of Judaic books and products. The store has also gained renown for its constant recurrence in the media, including The New York Times, The Wall Street Journal, CNN and Sex And The City.
