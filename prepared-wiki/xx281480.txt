[[Zhu De's Former Residence]]

CATEGORIES: Buildings and structures in Nanchong

The Zhu De's Former Residence([tpl]zh|s=朱德故居|t=朱德故居|p=Zhū Dé Gùjū[/tpl]) built in the late Qing Dynasty, it is located in Linlang Village, Ma'an Town, Yilong County, Nanchong City, Sichuan Province, People's Republic of China (仪陇县马鞍镇琳琅村).It has  a building area of about 2560 square meters, embodies buildings such as the old houses (故居老屋), the Zhu De Memorial Hall ([tpl]lang|zh-hans|朱德纪念馆[/tpl]), the statue of Zhu De ([tpl]lang|zh-hans|朱德汉白玉雕像[/tpl]), the Cultural relics Exhibition Hall ([tpl]lang|zh-hans|文物展览馆[/tpl]), and Lancao Garden ([tpl]lang|zh-hans|兰草园[/tpl]).[tpl]cite web |url=http://sc.sina.com.cn/travel/line/2013-05-29/000091438.html |title= 朱德故里 红色之旅仪陇县琳琅山|accessdate=2013 |publisher= Sina|work= |language=Simplified Chinese [/tpl][tpl]cite web |url=http://news.xinhuanet.com/yzyd/travel/20130618/c_116192713.htm |title=四川朱德故里红色旅游热度不减 3天游客破3万人次 |accessdate= 2013|publisher=Xinhuanet |work= |language=Simplified Chinese [/tpl]

==History==

On 11 December 1886, Zhu De was born in here, he lived in here about 13 years.
In July,1980, it was listed as a provincial culture and relics site ([tpl]lang|zh-hans|省级文物保护单位[/tpl]).
In 1988, it was listed as a China's most important culture and relics site (全国重点文物保护单位) by the State Council of China.
On 1 August 1982,Deng Xiaoping ([tpl]lang|zh-hans|邓小平[/tpl]) wrote "Zhu De's Former Residence" on the horizontal tablet.
On 11 December 1996, the statue of Zhu De was built. At the same year, it was listed as a National Patriotic Education Base ([tpl]lang|zh-hans|全国爱国主义教育基地[/tpl]).
