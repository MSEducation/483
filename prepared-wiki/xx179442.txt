[[Youssef Darbaki]]

CATEGORIES: 1964 births, Living people, Moroccan footballers, CA Marbella footballers, Sporting Clube de Portugal footballers, People from Casablanca, American people of Moroccan descent, American soccer players

Youssef Darbaki, (born February 15, 1964 in Casablanca, Morocco) was a Moroccan American soccer midfielder. He was a participant in the 1982 World Cup Qualifiers.
He is the Head Coach/Manager of the Minnesota Twin Stars, and MSHSL runners up, Prairie Seeds Academy, in the USA.

==Coaching career==

He is fluent in Spanish, French, Arabic, English, Portuguese, and Italian.
