[[KJCY]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Iowa

KJCY is a Christian radio station licensed to St. Ansgar, Iowa, broadcasting on 95.5 MHz FM. KJCY serves the areas of Mason City, Iowa and Austin, Minnesota. The station is owned by Minn-Iowa Christian Broadcasting, Inc. KJCY fcc.gov. Accessed July 25, 2012

==Translators==

KJCY is also heard locally in Mason City, Iowa through a translator on 92.5 FM, and in Blooming Prairie, Minnesota through a translator on 98.3 FM.
