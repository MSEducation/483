[[Dillingham Census Area, Alaska]]

CATEGORIES: Dillingham Census Area, Alaska, Alaska census areas, Counties of the United States with Native American majority populations

Dillingham Census Area is a census area located in the state of Alaska, United States.  As of the 2010 census, the population was 4,847.  It is part of the unorganized borough and therefore has no borough seat.  Its largest community by far is the city of Dillingham, on a small arm of Bristol Bay on the Bering Sea.
