[[Pacifique (album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2000 albums, Deep Forest albums

Pacifique is the fourth studio album and soundtrack by the French duo Deep Forest released on December 12, 2000 by Sony Music / St George label.Deep Forest: Pacifique It mixes Pacific and island beats with electronic music. The music was written for the French film Le Prince du Pacifique, directed by Alain Corneau. The album was less successful than were its predecessors.
