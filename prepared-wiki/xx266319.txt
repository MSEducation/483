[[Mount Herbert / Te Ahu Patiki]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of the Canterbury Region, Banks Peninsula

Mount Herbert is, at [tpl]convert|920|m|ft[/tpl], the highest peak on Banks Peninsula, New Zealand.[tpl]cite web|title=Mount Herbert / Te Ahu Patiki|url=http://tramper.co.nz/?180|publisher=New Zealand Tramper|accessdate=22 September 2011[/tpl]  It is located south of Lyttelton Harbour and has the township of Diamond Harbour located at its northern foot.
