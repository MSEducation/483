[[Taşobası]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Mersin Province, Mediterranean Region, Turkey, Villages in Turkey, Tarsus District

 
Taşobası is a village in Tarsus district of Mersin Province, Turkey. It is on Turkish state higheay [tpl]TUR-D|750[/tpl]. At [tpl]coord|37|05|N|34|56|E[/tpl] it is [tpl]convert|15|km[/tpl] to Tarsus and [tpl]convert|42|km[/tpl] to Mersin. The population of village is 1266 Statistical Institute as of 2011.
