[[Closer (Slinkee Minx song)]]

CATEGORIES: 2004 singles, Slinkee Minx songs

"Closer" is a 2004 dance song written by M. Palmer, A. Failla, B. Tartaglia and performed by Slinkee Minx. It reached number 39 on the Australian ARIA Singles Chart.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.australian-charts.com/search.asp|title=Australian charts archive|publisher=Hung Medien|accessdate=2010-12-03[/tpl]
