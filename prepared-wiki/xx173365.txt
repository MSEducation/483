[[WCAM]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in South Carolina

WCAM (1590 AM) is a radio station broadcasting an Adult Standards format.Licensed to Camden, South Carolina, USA.  The station is currently owned by Kershaw Radio Corp. and features programing from CNN Radio.
