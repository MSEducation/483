[[David Reader]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, Ambassadors of the United Kingdom to Cambodia

David Reader retired from the Foreign and Commonwealth Office in 2008. He was latterly British Ambassador to Cambodia. Reader was High Commissioner to the Kingdom of Swaziland before his appointment to Cambodia.Ambassador David Reader
He has also held a number of positions at the Foreign & Commonwealth Office. Married with two children.
