[[Matt Crane]]

CATEGORIES: American male television actors, American male soap opera actors, 1967 births, Living people

Matthew Crane (born May 4, 1967) is an American actor, usually credited as Matt Crane.

==Private life==

He was born in Kimberton, Pennsylvania.  He is the brother of Anthony Crane, former J. Crew model. Crane attended the University of Honolulu and the American Academy of Dramatic Arts.
Crane has worked as a model and has appeared in the mini series War and Remembrance and guest starred on the series Tour of Duty and Gidget.
Crane’s hobbies include water skiing, swimming and surfing.
He married former co-star Robin Christopher in 2000. They have two children together a son August Christopher (b. July 14, 2004) and a daughter Gianna Maria (b. July 10, 2006). His daughter Gianna used to play Skye's daughter, Lila Alcazar, on General Hospital, the daughter of Skye (Robin's character) and Lorenzo Alcazar before Robin and Gianna left the show in 2008.
They currently live in Pennsylvania.

==Career==

His most notable soap opera credit is playing Matthew Cory on Another World (1988–1997, 1998–1999).  He left Another World in 1997 to pursue his passion of sculpting, and returned in 1999.
Crane also appeared on General Hospital in 2004, as Ross Duncan.
Crane was also a formula two motorcycle racer in the Northeast region; New Hampshire in particular.
