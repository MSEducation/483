[[Paweł Stok]]

CATEGORIES: 1913 births, 1993 deaths, People from Ternopil, Polish basketball players, Olympic basketball players of Poland, Basketball players at the 1936 Summer Olympics

[tpl]MedalCountry | [tpl]POL[/tpl] [/tpl]
Paweł Stanisław Stok (22 March 1913, Tarnopol — 18 August 1993, Kraków) was a Polish basketball player who competed in the 1936 Summer Olympics.
He was part of the Polish basketball team that finished fourth in the Olympic tournament.
