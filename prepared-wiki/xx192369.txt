[[2003 Atlanta Falcons season]]

CATEGORIES: 2003 National Football League season by team, Atlanta Falcons seasons

The [tpl]NFL year|2003[/tpl] Atlanta Falcons season was the franchise's 38th season in the National Football League (NFL). It is best remembered for the third preseason game, in which quarterback Michael Vick broke his leg and was done for most of the season. Atlanta had two other quarterbacks take over for a combined 2–10 record (Doug Johnson and Kurt Kittner). Vick returned in week 14 and ended the season with a 3–1 record.
