[[Saint-Génis-des-Fontaines]]

CATEGORIES: Saint-Génis-des-Fontaines, Communes of Pyrénées-Orientales

Saint-Génis-des-Fontaines ([tpl]lang-ca|Sant Genís de Fontanes[/tpl]) is a commune in the Pyrénées-Orientales department in southern France.
It is home to a Benedictine abbey, founded in the late 8th or early 9th century.
