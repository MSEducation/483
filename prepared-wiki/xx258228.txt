[[Nor-West Jets AFC]]

CATEGORIES: Australian rules football clubs in Sydney, Australian rules football clubs in New South Wales

Nor-West Jets Australian Football Club are an Australian rules football club based in Sydney, Australia. The club colours are blue and white. The Jets have teams in the Second and Fourth Divisions of the Sydney AFL league.
The Jets home ground is Bensons Lane located in the north-western Sydney suburb of Richmond.

==History==

Premierships: 2005 and 2006 (Second Division)
