[[Latvian Floorball League]]

CATEGORIES: Floorball in Latvia

The Latvian Men's Elite Floorball League ([tpl]lang-lv|Latvijas vīriešu florbola virslīga[/tpl]) is the top men's floorball league in the country of Latvia. There are 12 teams participating in the league, till 2010 there was one team from the country of Estonia.
