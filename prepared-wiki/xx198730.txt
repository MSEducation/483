[[William K. Head]]

CATEGORIES: 1947 births, Living people, Fayetteville State Broncos football coaches, Kentucky State Thorobreds football coaches

William K. Head (born November 15, 1947) is a former head football coach for the Kentucky State University Thorobreds; he held that position for three seasons, from 1987 until 1989.  His career coaching record at Kentucky State was 9 wins, 23 losses, and 1 tie.  As of the conclusion of the 2007 season, this ranks him tenth at Kentucky State in total wins and 16th at Kentucky State in winning percentage (.288).Kentucky State University coaching records
