[[Giorgi Shengelaia]]

CATEGORIES: 1937 births, Living people, Writers from Moscow, Mingrelians, Film directors from Georgia (country), Soviet film directors, Soviet screenwriters, Silver Bear for Best Director recipients

Giorgi Shengelaia ([tpl]lang-ka|გიორგი შენგელაია[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-ru|Гео́ргий Никола́евич Шенгела́я[/tpl]; born 11 May 1937) is a Georgian and Soviet film director. He has directed 14 films since 1961. His 1985 film The Journey of a Young Composer was entered into the 36th Berlin International Film Festival where he won the Silver Bear for Best Director.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.berlinale.de/en/archiv/jahresarchive/1986/03_preistr_ger_1986/03_Preistraeger_1986.html |title=Berlinale: 1986 Prize Winners |accessdate=2011-01-16 |work=berlinale.de[/tpl]
