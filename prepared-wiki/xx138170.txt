[[Oh Oh]]

CATEGORIES: 2006 singles, Guy Sebastian songs, Songs written by Guy Sebastian, Songs written by Jarrad Rogers

"Oh Oh" is a song written by Guy Sebastian and Jarrad Rogers and is the third and final single from Sebastian's second studio album, Beautiful Life. It was released on 6 March 2005. The single peaked at #11 on the ARIA Charts.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://australian-charts.com/showitem.asp?interpret=Guy+Sebastian&titel=Oh+Oh&cat=s|title=Australian charts archive|publisher=Hung Medien|accessdate=6 March 2010[/tpl]
