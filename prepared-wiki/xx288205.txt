[[Shahrak, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Shahrak ([tpl]lang-fa|شهرك[/tpl]; also known as Shāhrek and Sharak)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3084141|Shahrak[/tpl] is a village in Saidabad Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,179, in 340 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
