[[Reverie Sound Revue (EP)]]

CATEGORIES: 2003 EPs, Reverie Sound Revue albums, Debut EPs

Reverie Sound Revue is the title of Reverie Sound Revue's first EP, and their debut release.  The EP was released independently in 2003. It has had positive reviews in the local music scene. This EP has been released three times. The first release included only the first 6 tracks, the re-release with a new cover, and 8 total tracks. The most recent re-release was distributed by Boompa, digitally, and excluded the track "Cascade." Physically, the EP is now out of print.
