[[11948 Justinehénin]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Astronomical objects discovered in 1993

11948 Justinehénin is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 2092.9300474 days (5.73 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=11948 |  title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-04-28 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on August 18, 1993. It is named after famous Belgian tennis player Justine Henin. The Minor Planet Center seems to have erroneously added an accent to the name, which is now official.
