[[It's My Life (film)]]

CATEGORIES: 2014 films, Hindi-language films, Indian films, Indian film remakes

It's My Life is an upcoming Hindi film directed by Anees Bazmee. It stars Harman Baweja and Genelia D'Souza[tpl]cite web|title=It’s my life Movie star cast and movie stills|url=http://bollywoodinside.com/bollywoodmovies/its-my-life-movie-star-cast-movie-stills/|publisher= Bollywood Inside[/tpl]  in lead roles[tpl]cite web|title=It’s my life complete cast and crew details|url=http://www.bollywoodhungama.com/movies/cast/13714/index.html/|publisher= Bollywood Hungama[/tpl] The film is a remake of the successful Telugu film Bommarillu.

==Plot==

Rohit (Harman Baweja) has an over-protective businessman father. Every decision in Rohit's life is taken by his father. This causes Rohit frustration. He half-heartedly agrees to become engaged to a rich man's daughter, but then falls for a middle-class young woman called Natalie (Genelia D'souza). The film focuses on Rohit's realization that he has to come out of his father's protective shell and try to live by his terms. Rohit also finally decides to speak to his father that, for once, he would like to do what his heart desires.
