[[Svartfjelljøkulen]]

CATEGORIES: Glaciers of Norway, Geography of Finnmark, Loppa

Svartfjelljøkulen is a glacier in the municipality of Loppa in Finnmark, Norway. The glacier has an extension of about six square kilometers.
