[[Aner]]

CATEGORIES: Hebrew Bible places, Torah people

Aner [tpl]IPAc-en|ˈ|eɪ|n|ər[/tpl] (1.) One of three Amorite confederates of Abram in the Hebron area, who joined his forces with those of Abraham in pursuit of Chedorlaomer (Gen. 14:13,24).
(2.) A city of Manasseh given to the Levites of Kohath's
family (1 Chr. 6:55).
