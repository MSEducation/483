[[National College of Music]]

CATEGORIES: 1894 establishments in the United Kingdom, Organizations established in 1894, Music organisations based in the United Kingdom

The National College of Music, London is a music examination organisation based in the United Kingdom, offering external grade examinations in music, drama and speech both in the UK and overseas.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.roamproject.org/VocationalSchoolMusic.htm|title=Afghani Music Project - Reviving and Rejuvenating Music in Afghanistan|publisher=Friends of Roam|accessdate=20 November 2010[/tpl]

==History==
