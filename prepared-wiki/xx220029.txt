[[Gornje Vreme]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Municipality of Divača

Gornje Vreme ([tpl]lang-it|Auremo di Sopra[/tpl]) is a village on the banks of the Reka River in the Municipality  of Divača in the Littoral region of Slovenia.
It is the birthplace of the writer Bogomir Magajna.
