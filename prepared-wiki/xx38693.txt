[[Association des Bibliophiles Universels]]

CATEGORIES: Organizations based in France, Language-oriented digital libraries

The Association des Bibliophiles Universels (ABU; in English "The Association of Universal Booklovers") is a French language organization dedicated to producing e-text versions of public domain French texts. It was founded in April 1993, and has members from Belgium, Canada, France, Portugal, Switzerland and the USA.
