[[Kangping County]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Liaoning

Kangping ([tpl]zh|c=康平 |p=Kāngpíng[/tpl]) is a county in northern Liaoning province, People's Republic of China, bordering Inner Mongolia to the northwest, and is under the administration of the provincial capital, Shenyang, the downtown of which is [tpl]convert|104|km|abbr=on[/tpl] to the south. [tpl]asof|2010[/tpl], it has a population of 353,061 residing in an area of [tpl]convert|2173|km2|abbr=on[/tpl]. It lies just off of G25 Changchun–Shenzhen Expressway.

==Administrative divisions==

The county includes 7 towns, 4 townships, and 4 ethnic townships.[ref]
