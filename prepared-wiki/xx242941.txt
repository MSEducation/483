[[Praya, Lombok]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in West Nusa Tenggara, Regency capitals of Indonesia, Lombok

Praya (Indonesian: Praya or Kecamatan Praya) is the capital town of Central Lombok Regency (Indonesian: Kabupaten Lombok Tengah) on Lombok Island in the province of West Nusa Tenggara, Indonesia.  It has an area of [tpl]convert|31.12|km2|sqmi[/tpl] and 55,041 people.  It is a predominantly Muslim town, with minorities of Balinese inhabitants following Hinduism and Chinese inhabitants following Buddhism or Christianity.
It is located a short distance to the north of the Bandara Internasional Lombok airport [tpl]airport codes|LOP|WADL[/tpl], which opened on 1 October 2011. The airport is the island's only operational airport and provides services for both international and domestic operations. It is also the only international airport serving the province of West Nusa Tenggara.
