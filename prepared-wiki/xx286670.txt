[[Harris Academy Greenwich]]

CATEGORIES: Secondary schools in Greenwich, Academies in Greenwich, Harris Federation

Harris Academy Greenwich (formerly Eltham Foundation School) is a coeducational secondary school and sixth form with academy status, located in the Eltham area of the Royal Borough of Greenwich in London, England.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.harrisgreenwich.org.uk/ |title=Welcome from the Principal - Harris Academy Greenwich |publisher=Harrisgreenwich.org.uk |date=2013-05-02 |accessdate=2013-08-12[/tpl]
Eltham Foundation School converted to academy status in September 2012 under the sponsorship of the Harris Federation and was renamed Harris Academy Greenwich.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.newsshopper.co.uk/news/10495362.Pupil_Malika_Sawyer_found_hanged_at_Eltham_s_Harris_Academy_Greenwich/ |title=Pupil Malika Sawyer found hanged at Eltham's Harris Academy Greenwich (From News Shopper) |publisher=Newsshopper.co.uk |date=2013-06-19 |accessdate=2013-08-12[/tpl] Previously it had been a foundation school controlled by Greenwich London Borough Council. The school continues to coordinate with Greenwich London Borough Council for admissions.
Harris Academy Greenwich offers GCSEs and BTECs as programmes of study for pupils.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.harrisgreenwich.org.uk/95/curriculum |title=Curriculum - Harris Academy Greenwich |publisher=Harrisgreenwich.org.uk |date= |accessdate=2013-08-12[/tpl] Students in the sixth form have the option of studying from a range of A Levels as well as further BTECs. Some sixth form courses are offered in conjunction with Harris Academy Falconwood.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.harrisgreenwich.org.uk/97/our-sixth-form |title=Our Sixth Form - Harris Academy Greenwich |publisher=Harrisgreenwich.org.uk |date= |accessdate=2013-08-12[/tpl] The school has specialisms in sport, enterprise and communication, and has additional resources for the specialisms.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.harrisgreenwich.org.uk/96/our-specialisms |title=Our Specialisms - Harris Academy Greenwich |publisher=Harrisgreenwich.org.uk |date= |accessdate=2013-08-12[/tpl]
