[[Działyński Palace in Poznań]]

CATEGORIES: Buildings and structures in Poznań, Libraries in Poland, Palaces in Poland, Baroque architecture in Poland, Houses completed in 1776, Visitor attractions in Poznań

Działyński Palace in Poznań, Poland is a Baroque palace built in  1773 - 1776. The building features a rich stucco-decorated interior of its "Red Hall" and a classicist façade decorated with sculptures. The palace serves as one of the locations of Kórnik Library today.[tpl]cite web|title=Walk in Poznan:Market Square and around|accessdate=2012-12-10|url=http://poland.pl/poland/56,125610,11739663,Dzialynski_Palace,,3.html[/tpl]
