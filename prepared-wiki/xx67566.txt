[[Consort clan]]

CATEGORIES: Chinese nobility, Kinship and descent

The consort clan ([tpl]zh|c=外戚|p=wàiqī[/tpl]) is the family, clan of or group related to an empress dowager or a spouse of a Chinese dynastic ruler or a warlord. The leading figure of the clan was either a sibling, cousin, or parent of the empress or consort.
