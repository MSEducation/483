[[John Wych]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing, Year of death missing, 14th-century English people, People from Hereford, Members of the Parliament of England (pre-1707)

John Wych (fl. 1384-1393) of Hereford, was an English politician.

==Family==

He was married and had two sons.

==Career==

He was a Member (MP) of the Parliament of England for Hereford in November 1384, 1385, 1386, January 1390 and 1393.http://www.historyofparliamentonline.org/volume/1386-1421/member/wych-john
