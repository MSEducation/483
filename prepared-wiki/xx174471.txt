[[KGOS]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Wyoming, Country radio stations in the United States

KGOS (1490 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Country music format. It is licensed to Torrington, Wyoming, USA.  The station is currently owned by Kath Broadcasting, and features programing from ABC Radio .[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=KGOS |title=KGOS Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
