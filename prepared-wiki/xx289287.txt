[[M104 railway (Croatia)]]

CATEGORIES: Railway lines in Croatia

|}
The Zagreb Glavni kolodvor–Sisak–Novska railway, officially designated the M104 railway is a [tpl]convert|116.757|km|adj=on[/tpl] railway line in Croatia connecting Zagreb to Sisak and Novska.[tpl]cite map|publisher=OpenStreetMap|url=http://www.openstreetmap.org/?relation=1628033|title=Overview map of the Zagreb-Novska railway|accessdate=October 7, 2013[/tpl] It is a part of the Pan-European Corridor X. It was once a part of Orient Express line.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Government of Croatia|url=http://cadial.hidra.hr/searchdoc.php?query=&lang=hr&bid=nJbXVZkld1Kh4b1smwO6jA%3D%3D|title=Odluka o razvrstavanju željezničkih pruga|language=Croatian|trans_title=Decision on Classification of Railways|work=Narodne novine|issue=81|year=2006[/tpl][tpl]cite web|publisher=Institut IGH|url=http://www.igh.hr/upload/tbl_projekti/pps-zagreba-sazetak_143339.pdf|format=PDF|page=11|language=Croatian|title=Prostorno-prometna studija cestovno-željezničkog sustava šireg područja grada Zagreba|trans_title=Spatial and Transport Study of Greater Zagreb Area Road and Railway System|date=May 2009[/tpl]
The western terminus of the railway is located at Zagreb Glavni kolodvor, where it links to the M101, M102 and M202 lines, as well as a number of other railway within the Zagreb railway node. In Sisak, the railway forms a junction to the L217. Further to the southeast, the line links to the R102 and R203 at Sunja and Krivaja wye respectively. At the eastern terminus, in Novska, the railway links to the M103 (to Zagreb via Dugo Selo) and the M105 to Vinkovci and Belgrade.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Croatian Railways|url=http://www.hzinfra.hr/karta-pruga|title=Karta pruga|trans_title=Railway map|language=Croatian[/tpl]
The M104 railway is electrified using an 25kV 50Hz AC overhead line system and it is single-tracked.[tpl]cite web|publisher=HŽ Infrastruktura|url=http://www.hzinfra.hr/network-statement02|format=PDF|title=Network Statement 2014|chapter=Annex 3.3. Types of Lines|page=1[/tpl][tpl]cite web|publisher=HŽ Infrastruktura|url=http://www.hzinfra.hr/network-statement02|format=PDF|title=Network Statement 2014|chapter=Annex 3.6. Electrification System|page=1[/tpl] The maximum permitted speed along the M104 varies considerably. In Zagreb Glavni kolodvor–Zagreb Klara section the maximum speed is limited to [tpl]convert|90|km/h|abbr=off[/tpl]. Between Zagreb Klara and Velika Gorica, the speed is limited to [tpl]convert|120|km/h|abbr=off[/tpl]. In Velika Gorica–Turopolje section, the limit is set at [tpl]convert|100|km/h|abbr=off[/tpl], and in Turopolje–Greda section it is increased to [tpl]convert|140|km/h|abbr=off[/tpl]. In Greda–Sunja and Hrvatska Dubica–Novska sections, the maximum speed is limited to just [tpl]convert|60|km/h|abbr=off[/tpl], while the limit of [tpl]convert|80|km/h|abbr=off[/tpl] applies between Sunja and Hrvatska Dubica.[tpl]cite web|publisher=HŽ Infrastruktura|url=http://www.hzinfra.hr/network-statement02|format=PDF|title=Network Statement 2014|chapter=Annex 3.14. Distance Between Establishments and Maximum Permitted Line Speed|pages=4-5[/tpl]
