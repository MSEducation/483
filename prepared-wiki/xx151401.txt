[[Tommy Steenberg]]

CATEGORIES: American female single skaters, People from Honolulu, Hawaii, Living people, 1988 births

Tommy Steenberg (born November 23, 1988 in Honolulu, Hawaii) is an American figure skater. He is the 2004 U.S. Junior pewter medalist, 2003 U.S. Novice bronze medalist, and 2002 U.S. Intermediate silver medalist. 
He graduated from Woodson High School in 2007 and graduated from George Mason University in 2013 with a B.A. in dance and a B.S. in business.
