Eric Barroso Sánchez (born 4 October 1990), commonly known as Eric Barroso, is a Spanish professional footballer who plays for FC Nitra mainly as a left back.

==Club career==

He came to FC Nitra from CD San Fernando in summer 2013. He made his debut for FC Nitra against MŠK Žilina on 11 August 2013.ŽILINA VS. NITRA  4 - 4 11.08.2013, soccerway.com
