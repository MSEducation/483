[[Man Descending (album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 albums, Justin Rutledge albums, Six Shooter Records albums

Man Descending is the third album by Canadian singer-songwriter Justin Rutledge, released April 8, 2008 on Six Shooter Records.
Rutledge has stated to CBC Radio 3 that the album is titled for, and to an extent thematically inspired by, Guy Vanderhaeghe's short story collection Man Descending."For the Record: Justin Rutledge's Man Descending", CBC Radio 3, April 16, 2008.
