[[Kawaii Kare]]

CATEGORIES: 2003 singles, Zetima Records singles

[tpl]nihongo|"Kawaii Kare"|かわいい彼|He's Cute[/tpl] is the eleventh single by Japanese girl group Melon Kinenbi. It was released on December 3rd 2003, and its highest position on the Oricon weekly chart was #14.
