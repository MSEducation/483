[[Religious Studies Review]]

CATEGORIES: Religious studies journals, Rice University, Wiley-Blackwell academic journals

Religious Studies Review (RSR) is the journal of the Council of Societies for the Study of Religion (CSSR), which is based at Rice University. The journal is published quarterly by Wiley-Blackwell. RSR reviews over 1,000 titles annually in review essays and critical notes.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.researchgate.net/journal/0319-485X_Religious_Studies_Review|title=Religious Studies Review|work=ResearchGate|accessdate=28 April 2014[/tpl]

==History==

RSR was wholly produced and distributed by the Council till 2005 when they contracted Wiley-Blackwell to manage those. In 2009, Rice University purchased RSR, while Wiley-Blackwell continued the publishing duties.[tpl]cite web|url=http://humanities.rice.edu/Content.aspx?id=427|title=Religious Studies Review|publisher=Rice University School of Humanities|accessdate=29 April 2014[/tpl]
