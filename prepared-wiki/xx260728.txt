[[Lunazul]]

CATEGORIES: Tequila, Brand name alcohol products

Lunazul Tequila is a 100% blue agave tequila produced by the Tierra de Agaves company.

==History==

Francisco Beckmann, a former co-owner of the tequila brand José Cuervo, founded the Tierra de Agaves company in 2002. Lunazul tequila is grown, distilled, and bottled on a single estate in Tequila, Jalisco, Mexico.Lunazul website
