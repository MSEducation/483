[[Randy Ciarlante]]

CATEGORIES: The Band members, Living people, Musicians from New York, American percussionists, American drummers, American rock drummers, American rock singer-songwriters

Randy Ciarlante is a musician who frequently played with The Band. He joined The Band in 1990, singing harmony and playing drums. Randy Ciarlante has also been a mainstay of the Woodstock NY music scene since 1981 and has played with many of that area's leading musicians over the years.
