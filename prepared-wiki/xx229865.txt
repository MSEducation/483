[[Gucheng District]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Lijiang City

Gucheng District (古城区; pinyin: Gǔchéng Qū) is a county-level district located in Lijiang City Prefecture, Yunnan, China.
Its county seat is the large community Dayan (大研镇), otherwise known as the old town of Lijiang.
