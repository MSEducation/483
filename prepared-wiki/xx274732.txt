[[Nartaki (film)]]

CATEGORIES: 1940 films, Hindi-language films, Indian films, Indian dance films

Nartaki  starring Sunil Dutt and Nanda in key roles with good music is a 1963 Indian Bollywood film. It was the fifth grossing Indian film of 1963. The song 'Zindagi ke safar mein akele the hum' is very good.
