[[Operation Mandrel]]

CATEGORIES: Nevada Test Site nuclear explosive tests, 1969 in the United States

Operation Mandrel was a series of 52 nuclear tests conducted by the United States in 1969-1970 at the Nevada Test Site. These tests followed the Operation Bowline series and preceded the Operation Emery series.
The United States test series summary table is here: United States' nuclear testing series.
