[[Mytilaster lineatus]]

CATEGORIES: Mytilaster, Molluscs of the Mediterranean Sea, Molluscs of the Black Sea, Molluscs of Ukraine, Invertebrates of Greece, Fauna of Turkey, Marine molluscs of Asia, Marine molluscs of Africa, Marine molluscs of Europe

Mytilaster lineatus is marine mussels, distributed in European waters. Common in Greece, Russia, Ukraine (Mediterranean and Black Seas). Mentioned as introduced species in the Caspian Sea.
