[[African Swimming Confederation]]

CATEGORIES: National members of FINA, Sports governing bodies in Africa

CANA is the African Swimming Confederation: ''Confédération Africaine de Natation''. It is the Continental Association charged with overseeing swimming for Africa.
CANA was founded in 1970, with 7 members. As of 2008, the Confederation now boast 43 members.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.canaswim.com/news.asp?id=8 |title=Message from the President  |accessdate=2008-10-07[/tpl] Note: The 43 number is what is listed on the CANA website; however, FINA has 45 national federations in Africa.
Mustapha Larfaoui of Algeria is the current CANA President.
