[[Mickey Boardman]]

CATEGORIES: Magazine editors, Hanover Park, Illinois

Mickey Boardman is the editorial directorWall Street Journal: "'J.Lo of India' Draws a Crowd Downtown" By Adrienne Gaffney January 18, 2014 and advice columnist for PAPER Magazine.   

==Biography==

Boardman was raised in Hanover Park, IllinoisNew York Times: "The Natural History Museum of Mickey" By JACOB BERNSTEIN December 13, 2012 and graduated from Purdue University in 1989 with a degree in Spanish.New York Times: "Mickey Boardman: Keeping Up With His Followers" By MARY BILLARD October 23, 2013 After school, he spent a year in Madrid, Spain teaching before moving to New York City to study fashion design at The Parsons School of Design.Buzz Feed: "From Parsons To Paper Mag: How Mickey Boardman Made It In Fashion" by Amy Odell  July 15, 2013 Since 1993, he has written the advice column “Ask Mr. Mickey” in PAPER Magazine.[tpl]cite news|title=Ask Mr. Mickey|url=http://www.papermag.com/mr_mickey/|accessdate=22 September 2011|newspaper=Paper Magazine[/tpl]  His writing has appeared in the New York Times Magazine, Out and German Vogue.[tpl]cite book|last=Doonan, Simon and Roxanne Lowit|title=Eccentric Glamour: Creating an Insanely More Fabulous You|year=2008|publisher=Simon and Schuster|pages=87-92|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=H6m7C_HLx7IC&pg=PA87&dq=mickey+boardman+paper+magazine&hl=en&ei=5H57Tv-YOqfW0QHJuMSpAg&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=1&ved=0CDAQ6AEwAA#v=onepage&q=mickey%20boardman%20paper%20magazine&f=false[/tpl] Boardman is an active commentator on the New York social/fashion scene and appears as a cultural commentator, lifestyle expert and fashion guru for networks like VH-1, A & E, CNN, Style Channel, E! and Fox News.[tpl]cite book|last=Hastreiter, Kim, David Hershkovits and John Waters|title=From AbFab to zen: PAPER's guide to pop culture|year=1999|publisher=Paper Pub. Co.|location=New York, New York|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=xywqAAAAYAAJ&q=Mickey+Boardman&dq=Mickey+Boardman&hl=en&ei=vX17Tuj3E4T10gHx4czJAg&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=3&ved=0CDsQ6AEwAg[/tpl] 
Boardman was recognized as one of New York magazine’s “Most Photographed Faces in New York” and voted by Fashion Week Daily as one of the most-invited people.[tpl]cite web|title=The Most Powerful People in New York- The Most Photographed Faces of 2010|url=http://nymag.com/news/features/establishments/68495/|publisher=New York Magazine|accessdate=22 September 2011[/tpl] Boardman has been featured in books like Smile i-D: Fashion & Style, 20 years of I-D Magazine, Simon Doonan’s Eccentric Glamour and Timothy Greenfield-Sanders, Look: Portraits Backstage at Olympus Fashion Week. Also a philanthropist, Boardman has been active in many charitable efforts including Mr. Mickey’s Sidewalk Sale, Doctors Without Borders, Coalition for the Homeless and Red Cross earthquake relief for Haiti and Chile.  Boardman is a dedicated supporter of Citta, a charity that builds schools, clinics and women's cooperatives in India and Nepal.
