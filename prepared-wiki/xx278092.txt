[[Leonid Akulinin]]

CATEGORIES: 1993 births, Living people, Ukrainian footballers, Association football forwards, FC Shakhtar Donetsk players, Sportspeople from Donetsk

Leonid Akulinin ([tpl]lang-uk|Леонід Ігорович Акулінін[/tpl]; born 7 March 1993 in Donetsk, Ukraine) is a Ukrainian footballer currently playing as a striker for FC Shakhtar Donetsk. He is also a member of the Ukraine national under-21 football team, where he was called-up first time by Serhiy Kovalets for Valeriy Lobanovskyi Memorial Tournament in 2013.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.champion.com.ua/football/2013/05/21/533684/|title=Ковалець назвав склад "молодіжки" на турнір пам'яті Лобановського |publisher=champion.com.ua|accessdate=8 August 2013|date=21 May 2013 2011 | language = Ukrainian[/tpl]
He was chosen best player of the 2014 Commonwealth of Independent States Cup.http://www.sports.ru/football/157404802.html[/ref]
