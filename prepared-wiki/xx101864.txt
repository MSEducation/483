[[Fain-lès-Moutiers]]

CATEGORIES: Communes of Côte-d'Or

Fain-lès-Moutiers is a commune in the Côte-d'Or department in Burgundy in eastern France.

==Geography==

Fain-lès-Moutiers is located [tpl]convert|80|km|mi|abbr=on[/tpl] from Dijon and [tpl]convert|15|km|mi|abbr=on[/tpl] from Montbard.

==Population==

==Personalities==

It was the birthplace of Catherine Labouré (1806–1876), Catholic saint.
