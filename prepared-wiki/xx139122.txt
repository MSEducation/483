[[Real Love (Lee Ryan song)]]

CATEGORIES: 2006 singles, Lee Ryan songs, Songs written by Lee Ryan, Songs written by Ash Howes, Songs written by Martin Harrington, 2005 songs, Sony BMG singles

"Real Love" is the fourth and final single released from Blue member Lee Ryan's debut solo album, Lee Ryan. The song was not released in the UK, due to the lack of sales for Ryan's previous singles. The track received attention for its use as the lead single for the film Ice Age: The Meltdown. The song peaked at #57 in Italy and #72 in Switzerland.http://www.chartstats.com/artistinfo.php?id=702 The song was later covered by Katharine McPhee and Elliott Yamin for Randy Jackson's debut album Randy Jackson's Music Club, Vol. 1. The track was released on April 21, 2008 and served as the second single from the album. According to USA Today, the song has sold 8,000 copies as of June 2008.Idol Chatter 07-02-2008
