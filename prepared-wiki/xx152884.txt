[[The Empire at Broadway]]

CATEGORIES: Skyscrapers in Providence, Rhode Island, Proposed skyscrapers in the United States, Office buildings in Providence, Rhode Island

The Empire at Broadway is a planned high-rise office tower in Providence, Rhode Island being developed by The Procaccianti Group.
The 22 story office tower will occupy the land currently occupied by the disused fire and police station.
