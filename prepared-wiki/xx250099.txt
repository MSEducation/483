[[1989 DHL Open]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 WTA Tour, WTA Singapore Open, 1989 in Singapore

The 1989 DHL Open was a tennis tournament played on outdoor hard courts at the National Stadium in Singapore and was part of Tier V of the 1989 WTA Tour. The tournament took place from April 10 through April 16, 1989.
