[[RWS/Sunday Times Watercolour Competition]]

CATEGORIES: Competitions

The RWS/Sunday Times Watercolour Competition is nationwide competition promoting the art of painting in water-based media.
It was launched in 1988 as the Kaupthing Singer and Friedlander / Sunday Times Watercolour Competition, through sponsorship by Kaupthing Bank and The Sunday Times.
