[[Botoșani County]]

CATEGORIES: Botoşani County, Counties of Romania

Botoșani ([tpl]IPA-ro|botoˈʃanʲ|-|Ro-Botoșani.ogg[/tpl]) is a county (județ) of Romania, in Moldavia (few villages in Bukovina), with the capital city at Botoșani.

==Demographics==

In October 31, 2011, it had a population of 412,626 and the population density was 83/km2. Tab8. Populația stabilă după etnie – județe, municipii, orașe, comune
