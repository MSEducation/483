[[Petit Combin]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of the Alps, Alpine three-thousanders, Mountains of Switzerland, Mountains of Valais

The Petit Combin is a mountain of the Swiss Pennine Alps, located between the valleys of Entremont and Bagnes in the canton of Valais. It belong to the Grand Combin massif.
The Petit Combin is a popular destination for heliskiing and snow landing.
