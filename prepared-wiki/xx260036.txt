[[Immaculate Conception Church (Bangall, New York)]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic churches in New York, Churches in Dutchess County, New York

The Church of the Immaculate Conception is a Roman Catholic parish church under the authority of the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of New York, located in 623 Hunns Lake Road, Bangall, Dutchess County, New York City.
It holds masses Saturdays at 5pm, Sundays at 10 and 11:30am, and confessions on Saturdays from 3:30 to 4:30pm.
