[[Aafat]]

CATEGORIES: 1977 films, Hindi-language films, Indian films

Aafat ([tpl]lang-hi|आफ़त[/tpl]; translation: Hardship) is 1977 Bollywood film directed by Atma Ram and starring Navin Nischol, Leena Chandavarkar, Amjad Khan, Mehmood,Nazir Hussain.

==Plot==

Inspector Amar and Inspector Chhaya are after criminal Hardayal. Amar and Chhaya fall in love. Amar goes beyond call of his duty to catch Hardayal and hence suspended. Amar is caught by Hardayal and imprisoned. Now Chhaya is supposed find criminal Hardayal.
