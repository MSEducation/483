[[Suurupi]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Harju County

Suurupi is a village in Harku Parish, Harju County in northern Estonia. It has a population of 756 (as of 1 June 2010).[tpl]cite web|title=Üldinfo|url=http://www.harku.ee/index.php|publisher=Harku vald[/tpl]
