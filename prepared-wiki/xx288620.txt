[[United States House of Representatives election in North Dakota, 2014]]

CATEGORIES: United States House of Representatives elections, 2014, United States House of Representatives elections in North Dakota, North Dakota elections, 2014

The 2014 United States House of Representatives election in North Dakota will be held on Tuesday, November 4, 2014 to elect the U.S. Representative from North Dakota's at-large congressional district, who will represent the state of North Dakota in the 114th United States Congress.[tpl]cite web|title=2014 North Dakota Election Calendar|url=https://vip.sos.nd.gov/pdfs/Portals/electioncalendar.pdf|work=nd.gov|publisher=North Dakota Secretary of State|accessdate=October 24, 2013[/tpl] The election will coincide with all other states' House of Representatives elections. Incumbent Republican Representative Kevin Cramer, who has served in the seat since 2013, is running for re-election to a second two-year term in office.
