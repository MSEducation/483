[[Edward H. Griffith]]

CATEGORIES: 1894 births, 1975 deaths, American film directors

Edward H. Griffith (August 23, 1894 â€“ March 3, 1975) was an American motion picture director, screenwriter and producer. He directed 61 films from 1917 to 1946. He was born in Lynchburg, Virginia and began his career in motion pictures as a screenwriter in 1916, and advanced to the position of a director of two-reelers. He directed actress Madeleine Carroll in several films including Honeymoon in Bali (1939). Griffith died on March 3, 1975 at the age of 80.
