[[KMFR]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in San Antonio, Texas

KMFR (1280 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Spanish Variety format. Licensed to Pearsall, Texas, USA, the station serves the San Antonio area. The station is currently owned by Pearsall Radioworks, Ltd. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KMFR |title=KMFR Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==History==

The station was assigned the call letters KSAH on 2004-05-10. On 2004-05-20, the station changed its call sign to KTFM, on 2005-01-19 to KILM, and on 2005-01-26 to KVWG, and on 2011-09-21, to the current KMFR.[tpl]cite web |title=KMFR Call Sign History |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=52048&Callsign=KMFR |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division[/tpl]
