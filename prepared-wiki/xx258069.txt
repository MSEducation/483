[[Youssoufou Bamba]]

CATEGORIES: Permanent Representatives of Ivory Coast to the United Nations, Ivorian diplomats, Living people

Youssoufou Bamba is an Ivorian diplomat.  He has served as the Permanent Representative of Côte d'Ivoire to the United Nations since 2010; he was appointed to the position by Alassane Ouattara after the latter claimed victory in the disputed presidential election of that year.http://www.unmultimedia.org/radio/english/detail/110232.html New Côte d'Ivoire ambassador at the UN
