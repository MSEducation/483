[[Jason Podollan]]

CATEGORIES: 1976 births, Canadian ice hockey right wingers, Ice hockey people from British Columbia, People from Vernon, British Columbia, Florida Panthers draft picks, Florida Panthers players, Toronto Maple Leafs players, Los Angeles Kings players, New York Islanders players, Manitoba Moose (IHL) players, Living people

Jason Podollan (born February 18, 1976) is a Canadian former ice hockey player. 
Podollan was drafted 31st overall in the 1994 NHL Entry Draft by the Florida Panthers from the Western Hockey League's Spokane Chiefs.  He played in 41 games in the National Hockey League with the Florida Panthers, Toronto Maple Leafs, Los Angeles Kings, and New York Islanders.  In 2002 he moved to the Deutsche Eishockey Liga in Germany for Adler Mannheim where he spent three seasons before retiring in 2005.
