[[Right ascension]]

CATEGORIES: Celestial coordinate system, Angle, Technical factors of astrology

Right ascension (abbreviated RA; symbol Î±) is the angular distance measured eastward along the celestial equator from the vernal equinox to the hour circle of the point in question.[ref]

</ref> 
When combined with declination, these astronomical coordinates specify the direction of a point on the celestial sphere in the equatorial coordinate system.
