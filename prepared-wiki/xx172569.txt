[[Silver chimaera]]

CATEGORIES: Chimaera, Animals described in 1900

The silver chimaera (Chimaera phantasma) is a species of fish in the Chimaeridae family found near Australia, China, Japan, North Korea, South Korea, New Caledonia, the Philippines, Taiwan, and Vietnam.Dagit, D.D. 2005.  Chimaera phantasma.   2006 IUCN Red List of Threatened Species.   Downloaded on 3 August 2007. Its natural habitat is open seas. It is threatened by habitat loss.
