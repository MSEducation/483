[[KMMG]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Washington (state)

KMMG (96.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Spanish adult contemporary format. Licensed to Benton City, Washington, USA, the station serves the Tri-Cities area.  The station is currently owned by Adelante Media Group of Eastern Washington License, LLC. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KMMG |title=KMMG Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==History==

The station went on the air as KREW-FM on January 26, 1981.  On November 18, 1996, the station changed its call sign to KZTB. On 2006-03-14 to the current KMMG. [tpl]cite web|url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=4758&Callsign=KMMG |title=KMMG Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl] 
Bustos Media used to own the station. In September 2010, Bustos transferred most of its licenses to Adelante Media Group as part of a settlement with its lenders.
