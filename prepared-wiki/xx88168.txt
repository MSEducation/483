[[Stony Point railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1889

Stony Point railway station is located on the Stony Point line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the town of Stony Point opening on 17 December 1889.Point Stony Point Vicsig A run-around loop exists east of the platform but has not been regularly used since locomotive hauled services ceased in April 2008.

==Platforms & services==

Stony Point has one platform. It is serviced by Metro Trains' Stony Point line services.Stony Point Line timetable Public Transport Victoria

==Transport links==

A jetty is located near the station, where ferry services operate to Tankerton on French Island and Cowes on Phillip Island.Phillip Island Inter Island Feries
