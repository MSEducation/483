[[Qarah Tappeh, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Qarah Tappeh ([tpl]lang-fa|قره تپه[/tpl], also Romanized as Qareh Tappeh; also known as Kara-Tappekh and Qaratepe)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3079773|Qarah Tappeh[/tpl] is a village in Mojezat Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 359, in 94 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
