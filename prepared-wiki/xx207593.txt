Oksana Aleksandrovna Verevka ([tpl]lang-ru|Оксана Александровна Веревка[/tpl]; born 22 November 1977 in Chernihiv, Ukraine) is a retired medley swimmer from Russia. She twice competed at the Summer Olympics (2000 and 2004), and is best known for winning the gold medal in the women's 200 m individual medley at the 1997 European Championships (LC).
