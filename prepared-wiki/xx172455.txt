[[Ron Beagle]]

CATEGORIES: 1934 births, Living people, All-American college football players, Sportspeople from Hartford, Connecticut, Players of American football from Connecticut, American football wide receivers, Navy Midshipmen football players, College Football Hall of Fame inductees

Ron Beagle (born February 7, 1934 in Hartford, Connecticut) is a former American football end.  He was elected to the College Football Hall of Fame in 1986.
