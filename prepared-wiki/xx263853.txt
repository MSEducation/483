[[Seetharampoor]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Warangal district

Seetharampoor also called Madharam (according to Grampanchayat) is one of the villages of Parkal Mandal of Warangal District, Andhra Pradesh, India.
It is 2 km far from Parkal and 40 km far from Warangal.
People of this village are having different cultures. They all do agriculture.
The largest community here is Munnuru Kepu (Telangana),
and the 2nd largest community here is Yadava (Golla).
Literacy percentage is increasing day by day here.
