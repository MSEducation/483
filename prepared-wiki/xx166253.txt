Sebastien E. Boucher (born October 19, 1981 in Hull, Quebec) is a minor league baseball outfielder, who currently plays for the Quebec Capitales of the Can Am League. He was originally drafted by the Seattle Mariners in the 7th Round (213th Overall) in the [tpl]By|2004[/tpl] Amateur entry-draft from Bethune-Cookman College. On August 9, 2007, Boucher was traded to the Baltimore Orioles for LHP John Parrish.
He also played for the Canadian national team at the 2006 World Baseball Classic.
