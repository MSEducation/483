[[Giuseppe Giovinco]]

CATEGORIES: Italian footballers, Juventus F.C. players, Carrarese Calcio players, F.C. Esperia Viareggio players, Spezia Calcio players, A.C. Pisa 1909 players, Association football midfielders, Sportspeople from Turin, 1990 births, Living people

Giuseppe Giovinco (born 26 September 1990) is an Italian footballer who plays for Italian third division club Pisa.

==Biography==

Born in Turin, Piedmont, Giuseppe followed the footsteps of his elder brother Sebastian to start his career at Juventus, one of the two largest team in Piedmont as well the team which won the Serie A most. Giuseppe was a player of under-20 reserve team from 2008 until 2010, where he won 2010 Torneo di Viareggio with the "white-black"; Giuseppe was a member of the under-18 team in 2007–08 season, for the Berretti reserve League.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.juventus.com/it/news/detail.aspx?lml_language_id=0&trs_id=1202000&ID=12326|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071008061414/http://www.juventus.com/it/news/detail.aspx?lml_language_id=0&trs_id=1202000&ID=12326|title= Berretti 2007/2008|archivedate=8 October 2007|date=24 September 2007|accessdate=10 August 2013|publisher=Juventus FC[/tpl] In summer 2010 Giuseppe left for Italian fourth division club Carrarese in co-ownership deal,[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.tuttolegapro.com/calciomercato/carrarese-presi-giovinco-jr-e-orlandi-11448|title=Carrarese, presi Giovinco Jr e Orlandi|date=5 August 2010|accessdate=10 August 2013|work=Tutto Lega Pro|publisher=Tutto Mercato Web Network|language=Italian[/tpl] where he won the promotion playoffs. In June 2011 the co-ownership was renewed; Carrarese also signed another attacking midfielder Luca Belcastro in the same window from Juventus.
Giuseppe made 16 starts and 9 substitutes for Carrarese in 2011–12 Lega Pro Prima Divisione. In June 2012 Juventus gave up the co-ownership deal of Giuseppe to Cararese.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.carraresecalcio1908.it/press-area/comunicati-stampa/94-lesito-delle-compartecipazioni.html|title=L'esito delle compartecipazioni|date=22 June 2012|accessdate=10 August 2013|publisher=Carrarese Calcio|language=Italian[/tpl] That window Carrarese also borrowed more players from Turin, such as forward Francesco Margiotta. On 31 August 2012 Juventus also transferred the co-ownership of Belcastro to Carrarese; on the same day Giuseppe left for fellow third division club Viareggio, another "white-black".[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.carraresecalcio1908.it/press-area/comunicati-stampa/89-operazioni-di-mercato-al-31082012.html|title=Operazioni di Mercato al 31/08/2012|date=31 August 2012|accessdate=10 August 2013|publisher=Carrarese Calcio|language=Italian[/tpl] Before he left the club, Giuseppe played twice for Carrarese in 2012–13 Coppa Italia, scored once in the first match, as starting wing forward.[tpl]cite news|url=http://archiviostorico.gazzetta.it/2012/agosto/06/Seredova_uno_show_perla_Venitucci_ga_10_120806108.shtml|title=C' è la Seredova ed è uno show: perla Venitucci|date=6 August 2012|accessdate=10 August 2013|work=La Gazzetta dello Sport|language=Italian[/tpl] Belcastro, Giovinco and Margiotta were the substitutes in the second match.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.ascittadella.it/primoPianoMain.asp?id_new=984|title= TIM CUP: IL CITTA ACCEDE AL 3° TURNO|date=12 August 2012|accessdate=10 August 2013|publisher=AS Cittadella|language=Italian[/tpl]
Giuseppe were the team joint-top-scorer in the league with 7 goals, for Viareggio, along with Simone Magnaghi. Among the whole Group B of the third division, they were joint-16th, along with player such as Belcastro. Giuseppe also played 4 times (4 goals and two caution) for the runner-up of 2012–13 Coppa Italia Lega Pro.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.tritium1908.it/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=297:eviareggio-tritium-1-1-211112&catid=50:coppa-italia&Itemid=120|title= E.Viareggio-Tritium 1-1 21/11/12 |date=21 November 2012|accessdate=10 August 2013|publisher=Tritium Calcio 1908|language=Italian[/tpl][tpl]cite news|url=http://www.pisachannel.tv/2013/01/coppa-italia-lega-pro-viareggio-pisa-1-3/|title=Coppa Italia Lega Pro: Viareggio-Pisa 1-3|date=28 January 2013|accessdate=10 August 2013|work=Pisa Channel|publisher=AC Pisa 1909|language=Italian[/tpl][tpl]cite news|url=http://www.pisachannel.tv/2013/02/pisa-viareggio-0-3/|title=Harakiri Pisa, finale addio.
