[[Dream-A-Lot's Theme (I Will Live for Love)]]

CATEGORIES: Donna Summer songs, 2003 songs, Songs written by Donna Summer, Song recordings produced by Giorgio Moroder

"Dream-A-Lot's Theme (I Will Live for Love)" is a song recorded by Donna Summer in 2003.
Despite the fact that the song wasn't given a commercial release as a single, but included as one of three new tracks on Universal's greatest hits package The Journey: The Very Best of Donna Summer and only released as a 12" promo single to club DJs, "Dream-A-Lot's Theme" became a sizeable dance hit, reaching #20 on the US Club Play Chart in 2004.
In 2005 the track was included on a second Universal compilation, Gold.
