[[Pella Department]]

CATEGORIES: Departments of Burkina Faso, Boulkiemdé Province

Pella is a department or commune of Boulkiemdé Province in central Burkina Faso. As of 2005 it has a population of 19,563. Its capital lies at the town of Pella.Burkinabé government inforoute communale
