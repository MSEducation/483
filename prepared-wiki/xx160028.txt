[[Communist Party of Switzerland/Marxist–Leninists]]

CATEGORIES: Defunct political parties in Switzerland, Communist parties in Switzerland, Maoist organizations, Political parties established in 1969, 1969 establishments in Switzerland, Political parties disestablished in 1987

Communist Party of Switzerland/Marxist–Leninists ([tpl]lang-de|Kommunistische Partei der Schweiz/Marxisten-Leninisten[/tpl], [tpl]lang-fr|Parti Communiste Suisse/Marxistes-Léninistes[/tpl], [tpl]lang-it|Partito Comunista della Svizzera/Marxista-Leninista[/tpl], KPS/ML) was a Maoist political party in Switzerland. It was founded in 1969 by now exiled intellectual Nils Andersson.http://archives.tsr.ch/player/personnalite-andersson Its fore-runner, the Lenin Centre, had been founded in Lausanne in 1964.http://www.sozialarchiv.ch/Bestaende/Archive/Ar201_207/Ar201_207frameset.htm
Lenin Centre/KPS/ML published Octubre (in French) 1964-1987, Oktober (in German) 1966-1987 and Ottobre (in Italian) 1971-1986.
In 1987 the party was dissolved, and substituted by the Libertarian Socialist Party.
