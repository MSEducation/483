[[Lites]]

CATEGORIES: Berkeley Software Distribution, Mach, Microkernel-based operating systems

Lites was a Unix-like operating system, based on 4.4BSD and the Mach microkernel. 
Specifically, Lites was a multi-threaded server and emulation library that provided unix functionality to a Mach based system. At the time of its release Lites provided binary compatibility with 4.4BSD, NetBSD, FreeBSD, 386BSD, UX (4.3BSD) and Linux.http://web.archive.org/web/19970716001117/www.cs.hut.fi/~jvh/lites-1.0-announcement.html 
Lites was originally written by Johannes Helander at Helsinki University of
Technology, and was further developed by The Flux Research Group at the University of Utah.
