[[European Union of Gymnastics]]

CATEGORIES: Gymnastics organizations, Gymnastics in Switzerland, Sports governing bodies in Europe, International sports bodies based in Switzerland, Organisations based in Lausanne

The European Union of Gymnastics ([tpl]lang fr|Union Européenne de Gymnastique[/tpl]) is one of four continental unions that represents the interests of Europe in the Fédération Internationale de Gymnastique. It was formed in 1982.
