[[Fannie Ellsworth Newberry]]

Newberry was born in Monroe, Michigan on 7 May 1848, the daughter of Hiram and Sophia Stone.
When Newberry was five, she moved to Chicago. She attended the Young Ladies Seminary of Monroe, Michigan. At age 17 she graduated from a school in Brookline, Massachusetts.[tpl]Cite book | last = Burke | first = Bridget Ellen | title = Literature and Art Books (Book 5) | publisher = Educational Publishing Company | year = 1909 | location = Boston | pages = 15 | url = http://books.google.com/books?id=ecIAAAAAYAAJ&pg=PA15&lpg=PA15&dq=literature+and+art+books+bridget+burke+fannie+newberry&source=bl&ots=SH-9MsiwIk&sig=wfQmFvk2TP41ZLuReyuO5EiT14w&hl=en&ei=fzsdTb_UJI6CsQPSjdnoAQ&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=1&ved=0CB4Q6AEwAA#v=onepage&q&f=false | isbn = [/tpl]
