[[Kardlu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Kardlu ([tpl]lang-fa|كردلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Kardlū and Kordlū; also known as Gardlū, Kurdulu, and Kūridlu)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3072086|Kardlu[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 96, in 20 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
