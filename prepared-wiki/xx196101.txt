[[Gettin' Together (Art Pepper album)]]

CATEGORIES: 1960 albums, Art Pepper albums, Contemporary Records albums, Original Jazz Classics albums

Gettin' Together is a 1960 jazz album by saxophonist Art Pepper playing with Conte Candoli, Wynton Kelly, Paul Chambers and Jimmy Cobb.
The sleeve notes (by Martin Williams) describe the album as 'a sort of sequel to the earlier Art Pepper Meets the Rhythm Section' which also featured Miles Davis rhythm section of its time.
