Rob Lalonde is an American Ice Hockey player. Lalonde has played in the Hockey East, ECHL, and AHL.

==Playing career==

A native of Williamsville, New York, Lalonde attended Merrimack College. In the 2004–05 season he scored the second highest amount of goals of any player on Merrimack's defense, after Bryan Schmidt.[tpl]cite web|title=2004-2005 Team Statistics|url=http://www.uscho.com/stats/team/merrimack/mens-hockey/2004-2005/|work=Merrimack Men's Hockey History|publisher=USCHO.com|accessdate=5 March 2011[/tpl] After graduating, he played four seasons with the Reading Royals. He has played twenty eight games in the AHL with five different teams.
