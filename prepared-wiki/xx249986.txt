[[St. John's Lutheran Church (Ancram, New York)]]

CATEGORIES: Religious buildings completed in 1847, 19th-century Lutheran church buildings, Lutheran churches in New York, Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Churches in Columbia County, New York

St. John's Lutheran Church is a historic Lutheran church complex located at Ancram in Columbia County, New York.  The complex includes the church, parsonage (ca. 1853), 19th century horse sheds, and church hall (ca. 1910).  The church was built in 1847 and is a rectangular, heavy timber frame meeting house that received major modifications in 1854, 1886, and 1906.  It is sparsely decorated in Greek Revival style.
