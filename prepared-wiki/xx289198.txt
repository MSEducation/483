[[Gugarchinak]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Gugarchinak ([tpl]lang-fa|گوگر چينک[/tpl], also Romanized as Gūgarchīnak, Googar Chīnak, and Govarchīnak; also known as Gohār Chānak, Gowgarchīnīk, and Gūrchīnak)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3065073|Gugarchinak[/tpl] is a village in Shivanat Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 738, in 168 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
