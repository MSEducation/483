[[Lars Monrad-Krohn]]

CATEGORIES: 1933 births, Living people, Norwegian electronics engineers, Norwegian businesspeople, University of Oslo faculty, Norwegian Institute of Technology alumni, Norsk Data people, Members of the Norwegian Academy of Technological Sciences

Lars Monrad-Krohn (born July 14, 1933) is a Norwegian engineer and entrepreneur.http://www.snl.no/Lars_Monrad-Krohnhttp://www.snl.no/.nbl_biografi/Lars_Monrad-Krohn/utdypning
He graduated from the Norwegian Institute of Technology, Institute for Radio Technology, (NTH, Institutt for Radioteknikk) in 1959. His master thesis addressed construction of computer core memory and was the first computer-oriented thesis handed in at NTH. As an entrepreneur, Monrad-Krohn established Norsk Data AS in 1967 (CEO 1967-1972), established A/S Mycron in 1975 (CEO 1975-1982), and established Tiki-Data in 1984 (CEO 1984-1996). He [tpl]As of|2005|alt=currently[/tpl] teaches entrepreneurship at the Department of Computer Science, University of Oslo.
He is a fellow of the Norwegian Academy of Technological Sciences.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ntva.no/index.php?option=com_club&view=member&id=309%3Amonrad-krohn-lars&Itemid=60|title=Medlemmer: MONRAD-KROHN, Lars|publisher=Norwegian Academy of Technological Sciences|language=Norwegian|accessdate=11 May 2013[/tpl]
