[[KRVL]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Texas, Classic rock radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 1982

KRVL (94.3 FM) is a radio station licensed to Kerrville, Texas, USA. It broadcasts a classic rock format as "Rev FM". The station is currently owned by Foster Charitable Foundation. [tpl]cite web |url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KRVL |title=KRVL Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division |accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl]

==History==

The station was assigned the call letters KERV-FM on June 14, 1982.  On December 1, 1984, the station changed its call sign to the current KRVL.[tpl]cite web |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=25380&Callsign=KRVL  |title=KRVL Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division |accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl] On June 12, 2007 the station was sold to Foster Charitable Foundation.
