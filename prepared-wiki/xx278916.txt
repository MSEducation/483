[[Churuk-e Olya]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Churuk-e Olya ([tpl]lang-fa|چوروك عليا[/tpl], also Romanized as Chūrūk-e ‘Olyā; also known as Chorak, Churak, Chūrūk-e Bālā, and Chūzak-e ‘Olyā)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3058918|Churuk-e Olya[/tpl] is a village in Chaypareh-ye Bala Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 293, in 63 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
