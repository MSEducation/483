[[Keith Regier]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Members of the Montana House of Representatives, Montana Republicans, University of Nebraska alumni, People from Kalispell, Montana

Keith Regier is  Republican member of the Montana Legislature.  He was elected to House District 5 which represents the Kalispell area.[tpl]cite web|last=Montana Legislature|title=Keith Regier|url=http://leg.mt.gov/css/Sessions/62nd/leg_info.asp?HouseID=0&SessionID=94&LAWSID=6479|accessdate=24 September 2011[/tpl]  He was selected as the majority whip in the 2011 legislative session.
