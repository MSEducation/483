[[Harm Kamerlingh Onnes]]

CATEGORIES: 1893 births, 1985 deaths, Dutch painters, People from Zoeterwoude

Harm Henrick Kamerlingh Onnes (15 February 1893, Zoeterwoude - 20 May 1985, Leiden) was a Dutch painter, the nephew of physicist Heike Kamerlingh Onnes. (Heike's father was also named Harm Kamerlingh Onnes.)
