[[Shebelinka gas field]]

CATEGORIES: Natural gas fields in Ukraine

The Shebelinka gas field is a Ukrainian natural gas field that was discovered in 1956. It began production in 1958 and produces natural gas and condensates. The total proven reserves of the Shebelinka gas field are around 18.4 trillion cubic feet (520×109m³).[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fox-davies.com/media/1263/fdc_ukraine_report.pdf|title=Ukraine oil and gas report|date=December 2005|publisher=Fox Davies|accessdate=30 December 2010[/tpl]
