[[Tina and Lolo]]

CATEGORIES: Hindi-language films, Indian action films, 2014 films, Indian films

Tina and Lolo is an upcoming Hindi Action movie directed by Devang Dholakia and starring Taaha Shah,Sunny Leone and Karishma Tanna. Deepak Tijori is also playing a role.
