[[Culpa (1993 film)]]

CATEGORIES: 1993 films, Cuban films, Spanish-language films

Culpa ([tpl]Lang-es|guilt[/tpl]), sometimes referred to as Molina's Culpa, is a 1993 film directed by Cuban film director Jorge Molina.  It is a black-and-white film that pioneered a postmodern use of Noir, blood, gore and pornography genres in Cuba.
