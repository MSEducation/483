[[Trogglodynamite]]

CATEGORIES: 1967 albums, The Troggs albums, English-language albums

Trogglodynamite is the second album by the English rock band The Troggs, released in 1967. The album was re-released in 2003 with eight bonus tracks by Repertoire Records.
