[[Sankata Boys Sports Club]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Nepal, 1954 establishments in Nepal

Sankata Boys Sports Club (known as the Sankata Kathmandu Mall Club for sponsorship reasons) is a Nepali football club, which plays in the town of Katmandu. They play in the Nepali first division, the Martyr's Memorial A-Division League.

==2010==

Due to poor performance just winning 2 games out of 22 in A-Division league 2010  the club was relegated to B division after 38 years.
