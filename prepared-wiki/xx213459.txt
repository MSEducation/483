[[Jan Nevens]]

CATEGORIES: Belgian cyclists, 1958 births, Living people, Belgian Tour de France stage winners, Olympic cyclists of Belgium, Cyclists at the 1980 Summer Olympics, People from Ninove, Tour de Suisse stage winners

Jan Nevens (Ninove, 26 August 1958) was a Belgian professional road bicycle racer. Nevens won the 8th stage of the 1992 Tour de France.
