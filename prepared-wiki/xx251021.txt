[[Flaherty, Kentucky]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Meade County, Kentucky, Louisville metropolitan area, Unincorporated communities in Kentucky

Flaherty is a rural unincorporated community in Meade County, Kentucky, United States.  It is a small unincorporated community that lies a few miles south of Brandenburg on KY 144, at its intersection with KY 1600 and KY 1816.
The National Register of Historic Places registered Clarkson House resides in Flaherty.
