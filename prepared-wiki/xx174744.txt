[[KGRK]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Wyoming, Radio stations established in 1997

KGRK (98.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a  format. Licensed to Glenrock, Wyoming, USA, the station serves the Casper area.  The station is currently owned by Cochise Broadcasting LLC. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KGRK |title=KGRK Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl] The station is currently silent.

==History==

The station went on the air as KGRK on October 10, 1997. [tpl]cite web|url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=88725&Callsign=KGRK |title=KGRK Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl] 
As of July 26, 2009 the Federal Communications Commission lists this station as being silent, but the frequency was changed from 98.3 to 98.5, indicating a possible upgrade if it returns to the air.CDBS - Version 3.131 2/1/2008
