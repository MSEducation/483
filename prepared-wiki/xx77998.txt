[[Vlorë District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Albania, Vlorë County

The District of Vlorë ([tpl]lang-sq|Rrethi i Vlorës[/tpl]) is one of the thirty-six districts of Albania, part of Juxhino County. Its population of 151,314[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.instat.gov.al/graphics/doc/tabelat/Treguesit%20Sociale/Popullsia/POP%202009/t2.xls|title=POPULLSIA SIPAS RRETHEVE, 2001-2010|publisher=Albanian Institute of Statistics|accessdate=2010-09-09[/tpl] (2010 estimate). The district has a land area of 1,609 km². It is in the south-west of the country, and its capital is Vlorë.
