[[Zangolabad]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Zangolabad ([tpl]lang-fa|زنگل اباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Zangolābād and Zangalabad; also known as Rangolābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3088967|Zangolabad[/tpl] is a village in Mojezat Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 200, in 50 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
