[[Bibi Fatima]]

CATEGORIES: Iranian people

Bibi Fatima was the daughter of the mystic Taj Al-Din Ebrahim ibn Rushan Amir Al-Kurdi Al-Sanjani or Sheikh Zahed Gilani, who was the Grandmaster (Murshid Kamil) of the famed [tpl]fact|date=April 2012[/tpl] Zahediyeh Sufi Order at Lahijan, and the mother of Sheikh Sadr al-Dīn Mūsā.
