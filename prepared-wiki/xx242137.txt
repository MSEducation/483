[[The Foundation (TV series)]]

CATEGORIES: Canadian television sitcoms, 2009 Canadian television series debuts, Showcase network shows

The Foundation is a Canadian television sitcom, airing on Showcase in the 2009-10 television season."Giving 'til it hurts". cbc.ca, September 11, 2009.
Created and produced by Michael Dowse, the show stars Mike Wilmot as Michael Valmont-Selkirk, the crooked and corrupt director of a philanthropic foundation. The cast also includes Martin Sims, Rebecca Northan, Yvan Ponton, Paul Spence, Michael Sinelnikoff and Martha Burns.
