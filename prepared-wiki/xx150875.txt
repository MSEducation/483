[[Lone Hand Wilson]]

CATEGORIES: American films, American silent feature films, 1920 films, Black-and-white films, 1920s Western (genre) films, American Western (genre) films

Lone Hand Wilson is a 1920 Western silent film. It stars Lester Cuneo in the title role. His romance with Madge Walker (Grace Gordon) is complicated when he is falsely accused of murdering her father (Phil Gastrock). Other actors include Annette DeFoe (playing Lolita Hansen) and Thomas Randall.http://www.tcmdb.com/title/title.jsp?stid=562665
