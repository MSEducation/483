[[The Gathering (Infected Mushroom album)]]

CATEGORIES: 1999 debut albums, Infected Mushroom albums

The Gathering is the debut studio album by the Israeli psychedelic trance duo Infected Mushroom. The album forms dark and organic atmospheres that are usual in every Goa or psychedelic trance album. The cover art was done by Israeli artist Eyal B. The album was released on 28 April 1999 worldwide
