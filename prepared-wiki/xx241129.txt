[[Tumed Left Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Tumed Left Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠲᠦᠮᠡᠳ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠵᠡᠭᠦᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Tümed Jegün qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|c=土默特左旗|p=Tǔmòtè Zuǒqí[/tpl]) is a banner of west-central Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China. It is under the administration the regional capital of Hohhot, which is some distance to the east-northeast.
