[[Navitaire]]

CATEGORIES: Accenture, Companies based in Minneapolis, Minnesota, Information technology companies of the United States

Navitaire LLC is a subsidiary of Accenture, offering technology solutions and services for airlines, high-speed rail and bus companies. Navitaire offers products in the areas of reservations, travel commerce ancillary sales, distribution, loyalty, revenue management, revenue accounting and business intelligence. The company has offices in North America, Europe, Asia and Australia.
