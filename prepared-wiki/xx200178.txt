[[Dan Sutton]]

CATEGORIES: South Dakota State Senators, Members of the South Dakota House of Representatives, 1970 births, Living people

Daniel D. "Dan" Sutton is a Democratic member of the South Dakota Senate, representing the 8th district since 2001. He is currently Minority Whip. Earlier he was a member of the South Dakota House of Representatives from 1999 through 2000.
