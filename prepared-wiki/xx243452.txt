[[Deirdre O'Shaughnessy]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Irish editors

Deirdre O'Shaughnessy is editor of the Cork Independent,Cork Independent's "Our Team" page Cork's largest circulating free weekly newspaper. She took up the position in July 2009, coming from the Galway Independent, where she was Deputy Editor. She has also been a contributor to the Limerick Independent and been editor of the University of Limerick student newspaper, An Focal. Her work has been published in the Irish Times, Irish Examiner, Irish Independent, Irish Daily Mail, Irish Sun, Irish Mirror, Irish Daily Star, and she is a regular radio contributor.
