Entertainment artists, South Korean dance music groups, Musical groups established in 2008, 2008 establishments in South Korea, Musical quintets, Synthpop groups, South Korean boy bands, Virgin Records artists, Universal Music Japan artists, Korean-language singers, Japanese-language singers, K-pop music groups, SM Town, Musical groups from Seoul

Shinee ([tpl]IPAc-en|ˈ|ʃ|aɪ|n|iː[/tpl] [tpl]respell|SHY|nee[/tpl]; Korean: 샤이니; Japanese: シャイニー; more commonly known and stylized as SHINee) is a South Korean contemporary R&B boy group under the management of SM Entertainment.[tpl]ko icon[/tpl] [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.newsen.com/news_view.php?uid=200805190845361002|title=SM's new 5-member group - SHINee|publisher=Kim Hyeong Woo|work=Newsen|accessdate=May 21, 2008|date=May 19, 2008[/tpl] Formed in 2008, the group consists of Onew, Jonghyun, Key, Minho, and Taemin. They made their debut on May 25, 2008 on SBS's Inkigayo with their single, "Replay".
Since their debut, Shinee has released three full-length albums (in Korean language; with one being a separate double-album release), five mini-albums, one live album and various singles. They have also won many awards, held three concert tours and starred in their own reality shows. Shinee is considered to be a fashion icon having started the "Shinee Trend" and are known for their highly synchronized and complex dancing.
They made their Japanese debut on June 22, 2011 by releasing a Japanese version of their single, Replay, and gained the title, "The artist who managed to get in top 3 for 3 continuous releases since debut," for the foreign artist category. The group answered many questions related to their future plans; announcing that in March 2011 they will be debuting in Japan.
