[[List of members of Croatian Parliament, 1992–95]]

CATEGORIES: Lists of Croatian parliamentarians, 1990s in Croatia

The second assembly of the Croatian Parliament ([tpl]lang-hr|Drugi saziv Hrvatskog sabora[/tpl]) was constituted on September 7, 1992 with mandates divived to 138 representatives after the August 2, 1992 elections.

==Parliamentary officials==

The presidents of the parliament (often also called the speaker in English) were Stjepan Mesić (HDZ) from September 7, 1992 to May 24, 1994 and Nedjeljko Mihanović (HDZ) from May 24, 1994 to November 28, 1995.
