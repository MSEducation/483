[[Kuyeda (settlement)]]

CATEGORIES: Rural localities in Perm Krai

Kyeda ([tpl]lang-ru|Куеда[/tpl]) is a rural locality (a settlement) and the administrative center of Kuyedinsky District of Perm Krai, Russia. Population: [tpl]ru-census|p2010=9,551|p2002=9,809|p1989=9,613[/tpl]

==History==

The settlement started in 1915 as a railway station during the construction of the Kazan–Yekaterinburg line.Encyclopedia of Perm Krai. Entry on Kuyeda [tpl]ru icon[/tpl] It was named after a nearby village which, in turn, got its name from the Kuyeda River.
