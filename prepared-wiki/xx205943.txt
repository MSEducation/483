[[Elisabeth Tarras-Wahlberg]]

CATEGORIES: 1950 births, Living people, People from Kristianstad Municipality, Swedish public relations people, Recipients of the Order of the White Star, 2nd Class

Astrid Birgitta ''Elisabeth'' Tarras-Wahlberg born von Engelhardt on [tpl]Birth date and age|1950|9|15|df=yes[/tpl] in Kristianstad, Skåne County. She was Press secretary to the Royal Court of Sweden from 1976 to 1995 and Director of the Press and Information Department at the Royal Court from 1995 to 2008.[tpl]cite news | first=Ulrika | last=Lundberg | title=”Han är mest allmänbildad i Sverige | url=http://www.aftonbladet.se/nyheter/article372720.ab | date=28 April 2006 | newspaper=Aftonbladet | language=Swedish | accessdate=18 June 2010[/tpl]
In 2008 she quit her services to the Royal Swedish family after 32 years of service. Her new job is to work as an international relations advisor to Emir Hamad bin Khalifa Al Thani of Qatar.[tpl]cite news | first=Alexandra | last=Hernadi | title=”Det blir inga avslöjanden” | url=http://www.svd.se/nyheter/inrikes/det-blir-inga-avslojanden_1281201.svd | date=23 May 2008 | newspaper=Svenska Dagbladet | language=Swedish | accessdate=18 June 2010[/tpl]
She has since 2009 returned to Sweden.
