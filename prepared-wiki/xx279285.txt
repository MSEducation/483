[[Arabcheh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Arabcheh ([tpl]lang-fa|عربچه[/tpl], also Romanized as ‘Arabcheh; also known as ‘Arabchāh, Arabchakh, ‘Arafsheh, and ‘Aruqsheh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3053518|Arabcheh[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 59, in 13 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
