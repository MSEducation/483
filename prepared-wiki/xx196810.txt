[[Channing Wilroy]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, American male film actors, 1940 births

Channing Wilroy (born November 8, 1940)[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0932902/|title=Channing Wilroy on IMDb|publisher=IMDb|accessdate=2008-06-15[/tpl]
is an American film actor who has appeared in seven films by John Waters. His first film role was the character Channing, the manservant in the film  Pink Flamingos. Because of his work with Waters, Wilroy is considered one of the Dreamlanders, Waters' ensemble of regular cast and crew members.
Prior to appearing in the films of John Waters, he was a regular on The Buddy Deane Show for 3 years.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.geraldpeary.com/interviews/wxyz/waters-p-town.html|title= John Waters and his Dreamlanders in P-town|publisher=GeraldPeary|accessdate=2008-07-01[/tpl]
He currently lives in Provincetown, Massachusetts and runs an inn.
