[[Vladimír Růžička (ice hockey b. 1989)]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, HC Slavia Praha players, Phoenix Coyotes draft picks, 1989 births

Vladimír Růžička (born 17 February 1989) is a Czech professional ice hockey forward who currently plays for HC Slavia Praha of the Czech Extraliga.Slavia Praha HC's 2010-11 roster He was selected by the Phoenix Coyotes in the 4th round (103rd overall) of the 2007 NHL Entry Draft.
