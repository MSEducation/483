[[Pogradec District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Albania, Korçë County

The District of Pogradec ([tpl]lang-sq|Rrethi i Pogradecit[/tpl]) is one of the thirty-six districts of Albania, part of Korçë County. It has a population of 70,575[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.instat.gov.al/graphics/doc/tabelat/Treguesit%20Sociale/Popullsia/POP%202009/t2.xls|title=POPULLSIA SIPAS RRETHEVE, 2001-2010|publisher=Albanian Institute of Statistics|accessdate=2010-09-09[/tpl] (2010 estimate), and an area of 725 km².
