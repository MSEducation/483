[[Pádraig Ághas]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing, 1966 deaths, Independent politicians in Ireland, Irish schoolteachers, Members of the 7th Seanad

Pádraig Ághas (died 22 December 1966) was an Irish independent politician and schoolteacher. He was a member of Seanad Éireann from 1951 to 1954.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oireachtas.ie/members-hist/default.asp?housetype=1&H1&HouseNum=7&MemberID=1845&ConstID=206|title=Mr. Pádraig Ághas|work=Oireachtas Members Database|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] He was elected to the 7th Seanad in 1951 by the Labour Panel.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.electionsireland.org/candidate.cfm?ID=9264|title=Pádraig Ághas|work=ElectionsIreland.org|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] He did not contest the 1954 Seanad election.
