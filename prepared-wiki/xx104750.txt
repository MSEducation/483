[[Norfolk Collegiate School]]

CATEGORIES: Private elementary schools in Virginia, Private middle schools in Virginia, Private high schools in Virginia, Schools in Norfolk, Virginia, Educational institutions established in 1948, 1948 establishments in Virginia

Norfolk Collegiate School is an independent K-12, private day school in Norfolk, Virginia.
Norfolk Collegiate School is a member of the TCIS Conference, which includes 10 Private High Schools around the Hampton Roads area of Virginia
