[[Zarnan, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Zarnan ([tpl]lang-fa|زرنان[/tpl], also Romanized as Zarnān; also known as Gol Bāgh, Zaran, and Zarlān)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3089164|Zarnan[/tpl] is a village in Bonab Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 321, in 72 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
