Coleen Sommer (born June 6, 1960) is a retired high jumper from the United States, who was born as Coleen Rienstra. She represented her native country at the 1988 Summer Olympics in Seoul, South Korea.Olympic results She set her personal best at 2.00 metres indoors on 1982-02-14 in Ottawa. http://www.alltime-athletics.com/whighok.htm
