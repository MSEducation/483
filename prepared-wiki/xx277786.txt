[[Sizo Maseko]]

CATEGORIES: South African rugby union players, Living people, 1991 births, People from Ermelo, Mpumalanga, Natal Sharks players

Sizo Maseko (born 3 March 1991) is a South African rugby union player, currently playing with the [tpl]Rut Natal Sharks[/tpl].[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/playerprofile.aspx?id=45597 | title=SARU Player Profile Sizo Maseko | work=South African Rugby Union | accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl] His regular position is winger.

==Career==

===Youth===

Maseko represented the [tpl]Rut Pumas[/tpl] at the 2008 and 2009 Under-18 Craven Week competitions, resulting in a call-up to the Under-18 Elite squad in 2008 and the S.A. Schools team in 2009. He also represented the [tpl]Rut Pumas U19[/tpl] team in the 2008 and 2009 Under-19 Currie Cup competition.
He joined [tpl]Rut Western Province[/tpl] in 2010 and played for their Under-19 team in the Under-19 Currie Cup competition. He was named in their squad for the 2011 Vodacom Cup, but did not make an appearance. He did play for [tpl]Rut Western Province[/tpl] in the 2011 Under-21 Currie Cup. He was named in the [tpl]Rut Maties[/tpl] squad for the 2012 Varsity Cup competition, but also failed to make an appearance for them.
He then joined Durban-based side [tpl]Rut Natal Sharks[/tpl] and represented their U21 side in the 2012 Under-21 Currie Cup.

===Sharks===

His first team debut came in the 2013 Vodacom Cup. He was named in the [tpl]Rut Sharks XV[/tpl] starting line-up in the first game of the season against the [tpl]Rut Border Bulldogs[/tpl] and scored two tries early in the second half to help them to a 72–6 victory.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/matchbreakdown.aspx?id=103804&category=&leagueid=1164&homeid=11714&awayid=11614&fullview=true | title=Match Breakdown: Sharks XV vs Border Bulldogs | work=South African Rugby Union | date=8 March 2013 | accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl] He started all eight matches in the competition and weighed in with a further three tries.
His Currie Cup debut came later in the same year, when he was included in the Natal Sharks starting team to face [tpl]Rut Griquas[/tpl] in the opening match of the 2013 Currie Cup Premier Division season.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.bdlive.co.za/sport/rugby/2013/08/08/sharks-maseko-selected-for-griquas-clash | title=Sharks’ Maseko selected for Griquas clash | work=Business Day | date=8 August 2013 | accessdate=8 August 2013 | author=Mjikeliso, Sbu[/tpl]
