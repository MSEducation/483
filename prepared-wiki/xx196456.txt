[[Chandbibi]]

CATEGORIES: Culture of West Bengal, Hinduism in Bangladesh, Islam in Bangladesh, Regional Hindu gods, Hindu folk deities

Chandbibi is a Hindu goddess and folk deity in Bengal, worshipped in conjunction with the Goddesses Oladevi (the Goddess of Cholera), Ajgaibibi, Jholabibi, Bahadabibi, Jhetunebibi and Asanbibi. Experts and researches believe that these seven deities are transmogrifications of Vedic deities.Oladevi - Banglapedia Their collective worship is evidenced in prehistoric times by a terracotta relic found at Mohenjodaro, a major city of the Indus Valley Civilisation located in Sindh, which shows the image of seven women standing together.
