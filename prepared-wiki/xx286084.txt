[[Chayerlu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Chayerlu ([tpl]lang-fa|چايرلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Chāyerlū and Chāy Yerlū; also known as Chehārlu)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3058216|Chayerlu[/tpl] is a village in Qoltuq Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 742, in 152 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
