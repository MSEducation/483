[[WAPP-LP]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in New York, Low-power FM radio stations in New York, Media in Suffolk County, New York

WAPP-LP (100.3 FM) is a low-power FM radio station licensed to Westhampton, New York.  The station was silent and its license was set to expire on July 2, 2013 if they did not resume operations.[tpl]cite web |title=Imported Letter |url=http://licensing.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/prod/cdbs/forms/prod/getimportletter_exh.cgi?import_letter_id=35600 |work=CDBS Public Access Database |publisher=FCC Media Bureau |date=August 23, 2012|accessdate=April 30, 2013[/tpl]  It resumed operations on June 27, 2013.[tpl]cite web |title=Informal Filing |url=http://licensing.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/forms/prod/prefill_and_display.pl?Application_id=1560711&Form_id=910&Facility_id=132494 |work=CDBS Public Access Database |publisher=FCC Media Bureau |date=Jun 27, 2013 |accessdate=July 15, 2013[/tpl]
