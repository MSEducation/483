[[Diego Olate]]

CATEGORIES: 1987 births, Living people, People from Rancagua, Chilean footballers, Chilean Primera División players, Club Deportivo O'Higgins players, CSD Colo-Colo players, Ñublense footballers

Diego Alejandro Olate Jeria (born January 11, 1987 in Rancagua, Chile) is a Chilean footballer, who currently plays for Husqvarna FF.
