[[Maravilla Tenejapa]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Chiapas, Municipalities of Chiapas

 
Maravilla Tenejapa is a town and one of the 119 Municipalities of Chiapas, in southern Mexico.
As of 2005, the municipality had a total population of 10,526. [tpl]cite web|title=Maravilla Tenejapa|url=http://www.e-local.gob.mx/work/templates/enciclo/chiapas/municipios/07115a.htm|work=Enciclopedia de los Municipios de México|publisher=Instituto Nacional para el Federalismo y el Desarrollo Municipal Gobierno del Estado de Chiapas |accessdate=July 27, 2008[/tpl] It covers an area of 411.32 km².
