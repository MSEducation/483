[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Cachoeiro de Itapemirim]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1958, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Vitória, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Cachoeiro de Itapemirim ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Cachoëirensis de Itapemirim[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Cachoeiro de Itapemirim in the Ecclesiastical province of Vitória in Brazil.
