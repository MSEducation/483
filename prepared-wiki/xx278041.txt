[[Grade II* listed buildings in Kent]]

CATEGORIES: Lists of listed buildings in England

The county of Kent is divided into 13 districts. The districts of Kent are Sevenoaks, Dartford, Gravesham, Tonbridge and Malling, Medway, Maidstone, Tunbridge Wells, Swale, Ashford, Canterbury, Shepway, Thanet, and Dover.
As there are 971 Grade II* listed buildings in the county they have been split into separate lists for each district.
