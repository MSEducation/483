[[Marti Maraden]]

CATEGORIES: 1945 births, Living people, Canadian male film actors, People from El Centro, California, American emigrants to Canada

Marti Maraden (born June 22, 1945 in El Centro, California) is a Canadian actor and director.
She emigrated to Canada in 1968, and became a leading actor at the Stratford Festival in the 1970s.Canadian Theatre Encyclopedia
She was Artistic Director of the National Arts Centre English Theatre in Ottawa from 1997 to 2006.National Arts Centre - Centre national des Arts While in this position, and arising from her activities in that role, the National Arts Centre co-founded the Magnetic North Theatre Festival.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.canadiantheatre.com/dict.pl?term=Magnetic%20North%20Theatre%20Festival |title=Magnetic North Theatre Festival |date= |publisher= Canadian Theatre Encyclopedia |accessdate=2011-06-29[/tpl][tpl]cite book |last=Jennings |first=Sarah |year=2009 | title=Art and Politics: The History of the National Arts Centre | pages= 327â€“330| location=Toronto | publisher= Dundurn Press | isbn=978-1-55002-886-7[/tpl]
In 2006 Maraden was appointed to succeed Richard Monette in 2007 as an Artistic Director of the Stratford Shakespeare Theatre in Stratford, Ontario. Unlike previous Artistic Directors at Stratford, Maraden was a member of a three person Artistic Team which includes co-directors Des McAnuff and Don Shipley, until creative differences forced the three to end their working relationship.http://www.stratfordfestival.ca/about/pdf/NR28A2006.pdf
