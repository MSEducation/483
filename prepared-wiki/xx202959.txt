[[Crișeni]]

CATEGORIES: Communes in Sălaj County

Crişeni ([tpl]lang-hu|Cigányi[/tpl]) is a commune in Sălaj County, Romania. It consists of three villages: Crişeni, Cristur-Crişeni (Szilágyfőkeresztúr) and Gârceiu (Szilágygörcsön).
Crişeni village was founded in 1387. The local economy consists of crafts, services, agriculture, trade and tourism.
