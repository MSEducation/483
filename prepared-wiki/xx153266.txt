[[Hilliard Gates Sports Center]]

CATEGORIES: Sports venues in Indiana, College basketball venues in the United States, Indoor arenas in the United States, Sports venues in Fort Wayne, Indiana, IPFW Mastodons men's basketball

Hilliard Gates Sports Center is a 2,700-seat multi-purpose arena in Fort Wayne, Indiana.  It opened in 1981 and contains [tpl]convert|68106|sqft|m2[/tpl] of space.  It is home to the Indiana University-Purdue University Fort Wayne Mastodons volleyball and men's and women's basketball teams.Facilities at gomastodons.com, URL accessed January 1, 2010. Archived 1/1/10
The venue is named for former area sportscaster Hilliard Gates.
