[[Barik Ab, Ijrud]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Barik Ab ([tpl]lang-fa|باريك اب[/tpl], also Romanized as Bārīk Āb; also known as Barkāb and Barkoud)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3055564|Barik Ab[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Bala Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 715, in 181 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
