[[Goodbye Summer]]

CATEGORIES: F(x) songs, S.M. Entertainment singles, 2013 songs, Korean-language songs

"Goodbye Summer" (Korean 굿바이섬머) is a K-Pop song performed by Luna, Krystal Jung and Amber Liu, members of the South Korean artist girl group f(x). The song also features an appearance by D.O. of fellow label-mate boy group Exo-K as the guest vocalist. The song was included as a track in the f(x)'s second Korean studio album Pink Tape which was released on July 29, 2013 under the record label of S.M. Entertainment. "Goodbye Summer" entered the Gaon Chart debuting at spot 7 for the first week of its sales. The f(x)'s sub-group, titled officially as f(Amber+Luna+Krystal), had also performed "Beautiful Stranger" from their 2012 EP Electric Shock.

==Composition and background==

According to the album description on the South Korean music portal site Naver Music and K-Pop news site Seoulbeats, the lyrics of "Goodbye Summer" talk about the unexpressed feelings of girl who falls in love with her best friend and the memories from the two months of Summer holidays she spent with him.[tpl]ko icon[/tpl] [tpl]cite web|url=http://music.naver.com/album/index.nhn?albumId=388594|title=The 2nd Album: Pink Tape|date=July 29, 2013|publisher=Naver|accessdate=August 8, 2013[/tpl] The song also happens to mark the first time the member Amber Liu has self-composed a track and also the first time the veteran music producer team NoizeBank has collaborated with f(x) in composition. Gen Neo of NoizeBank, who also had produced songs for Henry Lau and Super Junior-M,[tpl]ko icon[/tpl] [tpl]cite web|url=http://music.naver.com/artist/home.nhn?artistId=256523|title=Neo Gen|publisher=Naver|accessdate=August 8, 2013[/tpl] handled the arrangement of the piece using mostly acoustic instruments to produce its background music. Amber also raps while the vocalists Luna, Krytsal and D.O. take on the chorus, verses and belting in the song. The lyrics were penned by Kim Young-hoo, who also worked with other SM Town artists on hits like Girls' Generation's "Oh!" and Shinee's debut single "Replay". He also wrote f(x)'s song "ME+U" from their first EP Nu ABO in 2010.[tpl]ko icon[/tpl] [tpl]cite web|url=http://music.naver.com/artist/home.nhn?artistId=89005|title=김영후|publisher=Naver|accessdate=August 8, 2013[/tpl]

==Live performances==

The song was included in the set-list of f(x) and Exo's special winter festival joint concert SM Town Week: "Christmas Wonderland", on December 23 and 24, 2013 at KINTEX in Goyang.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.allkpop.com/article/2013/12/fx-and-exo-light-up-their-christmas-wonderland-at-joint-concert-during-smtown-week|title=f(x) and EXO light up their 'Christmas Wonderland' at joint concert during 'SMTOWN WEEK'|date=December 24, 2013|accessdate=December 25, 2013|publisher=Allkpop[/tpl]

==Critical reception==

"Goodbye Summer" gained generally favorable reviews from various K-Pop news sites and critics. Popular news site Allkpop praised the song citing the showcasing of "a lot of very pretty harmonies between Luna and guest vocalist D.O." as the reason.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.allkpop.com/review/2013/08/album-review-fx-pink-tape|title=Review f(x) 'Pink Tape'|date=August 7, 2013|publisher=|accessdate=August 8, 2013[/tpl] Another news site Seoulbeats commented that the track was enjoyable and is a little more sweetly mellow and acoustic than an average RnB love song is expected to be.[tpl]cite web|url=http://seoulbeats.com/2013/08/popping-in-fxs-pink-tape/|title=Popping In F(X)'S "Pink Tape"|date=August 1, 2013|publisher=Seoulbeats|accessdate=August 8, 2013[/tpl]
