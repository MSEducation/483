[[Jangan Ketawa]]

CATEGORIES: Malaysian television series, 2003 Malaysian television series debuts, 2005 Malaysian television series endings, 1990s in Malaysian television

Jangan Ketawa was a Malaysian sketch comedy which aired in the early 1990s.[tpl]cite news|url=http://thestar.com.my/news/story.asp?file=/2005/11/9/nation/12542631&sec=nation|title=Jangan Ketawa actor dies of a stroke|date=9 November 2005|work=Malaysia Star|accessdate=9 September 2010[/tpl] The show was produced by Filem Karya Nusa and broadcast on TV3 in 1991 and was directed by Louisa Cheong and Harith Iskandar. Reruns of the programme aired on Astro Prima from 2003 until 2005. The programme was also aired on Astro Warna in 2009.
