[[Gilbert Bougnol]]

CATEGORIES: French fencers, Olympic silver medalists for France, Olympic fencers of France, Fencers at the 1900 Summer Olympics, 1866 births, 1947 deaths, Place of birth missing, Olympic medalists in fencing, Medalists at the 1900 Summer Olympics

Gilbert Bougnol (31 August 1866 – 20 October 1947) was a French professional fencer who competed in the late 19th century and early 20th century.
