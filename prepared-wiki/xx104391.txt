[[NWA World Tag Team Championship (Texas version)]]

CATEGORIES: Tag team wrestling championships, National Wrestling Alliance championships, World Class Championship Wrestling championships

The Texas version of the NWA World Tag Team Championship existed from 1957 to 1968, and from 1981 to 1982. This title was recognized by World Class Championship Wrestling and The Funk Dynasty's N.W.A.
