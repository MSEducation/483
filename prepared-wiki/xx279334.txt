[[2006 FINA Synchronised Swimming World Trophy]]

CATEGORIES: Synchronized swimming, 2006 in synchronized swimming

The 1st FINA Synchronized Swimming World Trophy was held 2006 in Moscou, Russia. It featured swimmers from 8 nations, swimming in three events: Duet, Team and Free Combination.HistoFINA, volume 9 published by FINA on 2009-07-01; retrieved 2013-08-09.
