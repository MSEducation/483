Shiria Khatun (born 26 August 1972) is a British Labour Party politician, councillor in East India and Lansbury ward, Cabinet member for childrenâ€™s services and one of the short listed mayoral candidates in Tower Hamlets.

==Early life==

Khatun was born in Birmingham, West Midlands, United Kingdom. She was raised and educated in Tower Hamlets, she attended Central Foundation Girls' School. Her parents, Haji Mohammed Moklis and Joytera Khatun came to United Kingdom in late 1950s. Both her parents died when she was a teenager. Khatun and her younger brother were raised by their eldest sister, Jamila, and brother-in-law, Mohammed.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.bbwhoswho.co.uk/images/who%27%20who%20website%202010.pdf|title=Shiria Khatun|work=British Bangladeshi Who's Who 2010|location= |publisher=British Bangla Media Group|date=October 2010|page=74|accessdate=25 September 2011[/tpl]
