[[Garrison, West Virginia]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Boone County, West Virginia, Unincorporated communities in West Virginia, Coal towns in West Virginia

Garrison is an unincorporated community and coal town in Boone County, West Virginia, United States. Garrison is [tpl]convert|3|mi|km[/tpl] east-southeast of Sylvester. Garrison had a post office, which closed on March 30, 1989.[tpl]cite web|title=Postmaster Finder - Post Offices by ZIP Code|url=http://webpmt.usps.gov/pmt008.cfm|publisher=United States Postal Service|accessdate=September 26, 2011[/tpl]
