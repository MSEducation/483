[[Bolivian legislative election, 1958]]

CATEGORIES: Elections in Bolivia, 1958 elections in South America, 1958 in Bolivia

Parliamentary elections were held in Bolivia on 20 June 1958.Nohlen, D (2005) Elections in the Americas: A data handbook, Volume II, p133 ISBN 978-0-19-928358-3 The Revolutionary Nationalist Movement (MNR) received 85% of the vote,Nohlen, p141 and retained its large majorities in both houses of Congress.
