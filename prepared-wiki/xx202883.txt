[[The Killbillies]]

CATEGORIES: Australian films, 2002 horror films, Exploitation films, 2000s comedy horror films, Australian horror films, 2002 films

The Killbillies is a 2002 Australian comedy horror film directed by Duke Hendrix and distributed by Crypt Keeper Films.

==Plot==

In this movie, two somewhat insane hicks are sent forth by their father to resolve the feud between two families in as bloody a manner as possible.
