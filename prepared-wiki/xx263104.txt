[[Trachilos]]

CATEGORIES: Lasithi, Uninhabited islands of Greece

Trachilos ([tpl]lang-el|Τράχηλος[/tpl], "neck"), also known as Trachila, is an uninhabited Greek islet, located south of cape Goudero on the coast of Lasithi, eastern Crete, in the Libyan Sea. It forms a close group of islands with Koufonisi, Makroulo, Marmaro, and Strongyli.
