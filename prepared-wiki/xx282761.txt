[[Joint European Resources for Micro to Medium Enterprises]]

CATEGORIES: Economy of the European Union

Joint European Resources for Micro to Medium Enterprises or JEREMIE is an initiative of the European Commission [tpl]cite web|title=JEREMIE: Joint European Resources for Micro to medium Enterprises|url=http://ec.europa.eu/regional_policy/thefunds/instruments/jeremie_en.cfm#1|work=Regional Policy|publisher=European Commission|accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl] in cooperation with the European Investment Fund[tpl]cite web|title=JEREMIE - Joint European Resources for Micro to Medium Enterprises|url=http://www.eif.org/what_we_do/jeremie/index.htm|work=What we Do|publisher=European Investment Fund|accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl] to support private businesses in select member states.
