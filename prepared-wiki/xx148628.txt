[[Ore no Ryouri]]

CATEGORIES: 1999 video games, Japan-exclusive video games, PlayStation games, PlayStation Network games, Cooking video games

Ore no Ryouri (俺の料理 Ore no Ryouri, "My Cooking") was released by Sony for the original PlayStation. Players must successfully manage different types of restaurants, utilizing the DualShock's analog sticks to perform various tasks. 
It was published in Japan on September 9, 1999. Due to its success, it was re-released under the PlayStation's 'The Best' label.  A 1-level demo was made available in the US via PlayStation Underground. 
A semi-sequel party game, Gacharoku, was released on the PlayStation 2.
The game inspired a fan-made remake for English-speaking audiences released in 2004 called Ore No Ryomi, which ultimately spawned a commercial sequel titled  Cook, Serve, Delicious!, independently released for PC and Mac through Steam in 2012.
