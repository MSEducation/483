[[Wendy Neuss]]

Wendy Neuss, (born October 24, 1954) is an American film producer.

==Biography==

Neuss graduated from the University of Pennsylvania in 1976 with a bachelor's degree in psychology.
Neuss was the executive producer of several TV films starring her then-husband Patrick Stewart,[tpl]cite news | url=http://www.nytimes.com/2013/03/19/fashion/weddings/ian-mckellen-to-lead-wedding-for-patrick-stewart.html | title=Ian McKellen to Lead Wedding for Patrick Stewart | work=New York Times | accessdate=20 March 2013|date=19 March 2013|first=Bob|last=Woletz[/tpl] including A Christmas Carol, The Lion in Winter and King of Texas. She produced these films as the president of Flying Freehold Productions, a company she co-founded with Stewart. She was also a producer for the series Star Trek: Voyager[tpl]cite book|last=Poe|first=Stephen Edward|title=A Vision of the Future|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=MiJTlrdSkpAC&pg=PA284|accessdate=May 19, 2011|date=April 1, 1998|publisher=Simon and Schuster|isbn=978-0-671-53481-3|pages=284â€“[/tpl] and co-producer on Star Trek: The Next Generation. She has also produced the Motown series on the Showtime channel.
