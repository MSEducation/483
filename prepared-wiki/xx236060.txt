[[Blainvillea]]

CATEGORIES: Asteraceae genera

Blainvillea is a genus of flowering plants in the aster family, Asteraceae. They are native to the tropics.Blainvillea. Flora of China.
They are annual or perennial herbs. The flower heads contain small ray florets with toothed tips, usually yellow, or occasionally white. There are about 10 species.
