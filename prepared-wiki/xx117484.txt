[[Xenocytaea zabkai]]

CATEGORIES: Salticidae

Xenocytaea zabkai is a jumping spider.

==Name==

The species has been named after Marek Zabka, a polish Salticid specialist.

==Appearance==

X. zabkai females are about 4mm long. Males are not yet known.

==Distribution==

X. zabkai is only known from Viti Levu, Fiji.
