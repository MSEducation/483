[[Run to the Light]]

CATEGORIES: Trouble albums, 1987 albums, Metal Blade Records albums

Run to the Light was the third album by the American doom metal band Trouble, released on July 15, 1987. It was the band's last release on Metal Blade before signing to Def American for their follow-up, the 1990 self-titled release.

==Track listing==

All songs by Eric Wagner, Bruce Franklin and Rick Wartell.
