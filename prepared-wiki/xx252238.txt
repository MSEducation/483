[[Jugendbund Neudeutschland]]

CATEGORIES: Historical youth organisations based in Germany, Organizations established in 1919, 1933 disestablishments in Germany, Historical youth wings of political parties in Germany, 1919 establishments in Germany

The Jugendbund Neudeutschland (Youth Federation New Germany) was a German Catholic organisation, founded in 1919 by Felix von Hartmann, the Archbishop of Cologne.[tpl]BBKL|h/hartmann_fe|band=2|autor=Friedrich Wilhelm Bautz|artikel=Hartmann, Felix von|spalte=577[/tpl] It was closely affiliated with the Catholic Centre Party. The organisation opposed Nazism and was banned by the Nazi regime in 1933.
