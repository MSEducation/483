[[Cape Codder (boat)]]

CATEGORIES: Ship types

The Cape Codder is a type of boat. Built for commercial fishermen, it is just [tpl]convert|19|ft[/tpl], but with the ride of a bigger boat.  It was originally designed and built by Alex "Sandy" Urquhart in Marion MA. They were made later made in in Chatham, Massachusetts.  The molds changed hands a few times over the years and the boat is now being built in Peabody MA.
