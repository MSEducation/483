[[Yengiabad, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Yengiabad ([tpl]lang-fa|ينگي اباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Yengīābād; also known as Yengābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3773435|Yengiabad[/tpl] is a village in Shivanat Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 117, in 26 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
