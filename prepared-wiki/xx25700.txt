[[Amos Kollek]]

CATEGORIES: 1947 births, Living people, People from Jerusalem, Israeli Jews, Israeli film directors, Israeli male film actors

Amos Kollek (born 1947) is an Israeli film director, writer and actor.

==Biography==

Amos Kollek was born in Jerusalem. He is the son of Teddy Kollek, the long-time mayor of Jerusalem. Kollek studied psychology and philosophy at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem.New York Times bio He became interested in film after working as a writer. He produced his first film, Goodbye, New York, in 1985. His film Fast Food Fast Women was entered into the 2000 Cannes Film Festival.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.festival-cannes.com/en/archives/ficheFilm/id/5168/year/2000.html |title=Festival de Cannes: Fast Food Fast Women |accessdate=11 October 2009|work=festival-cannes.com[/tpl]
