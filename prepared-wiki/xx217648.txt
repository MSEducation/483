[[Gal, Azerbaijan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Julfa District

Gal (also, Qal, Gyal, Gagh) is a village and the least populous municipality in the Julfa Rayon of Nakhchivan, Azerbaijan.  It has a population of 128. The village had a large, 19th century, Armenian church that was standing derelict in the 1980s Argam Aivazian, Nakhijevan: Book of Monuments, Yerevan, 1990 p178-179. but which is now destroyed.Switzerland-Armenia Parliamentary Group (ed.) "The destruction of Jugha", Bern, 2006. p75.
