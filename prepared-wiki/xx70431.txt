[[Basmath]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Hingoli district, Talukas in Maharashtra

Basmath or Vasmat is a city and a municipal council in Hingoli district  in the state of Maharashtra, India.

==Demographics==

[tpl]As of|2001[/tpl] India census,[tpl]GR|India[/tpl] Basmath had a population of 57,360. Males constitute 52% of the population and females 48%. Basmath has an average literacy rate of 65%, higher than the national average of 59.5%; with 58% of the males and 42% of females literate. 16% of the population is under 6 years of age.
There are 3 degree colleges – Bahirji Smarak Mahavidylaya Vasmath and Yoganand Mahavidylaya , and Raosaheb Patange arts college.
