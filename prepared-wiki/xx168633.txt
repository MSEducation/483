[[Itombwe Flycatcher]]

CATEGORIES: Muscicapa

Itombwe Flycatcher (Muscicapa itombwensis) is a bird species in the Old World flycatcher family (Muscicapidae). It is found in Democratic Republic of the Congo. It was formerly considered a subspecies of Chapin's Flycatcher.
Its natural habitat is subtropical or tropical moist montane forests. It is threatened by habitat loss.
