[[Titinius]]

CATEGORIES: Ancient Roman soldiers

Titinius was a nomen of ancient Rome.
He was a friend of Cassius and one of the conspirators in Caesar's death. Later at the battle of Phillipi, he took his own life because Cassius killed himself (Cassius thought Titinius had died). 
Titinius also appears as a character in the play Julius Caesar by William Shakespeare.
