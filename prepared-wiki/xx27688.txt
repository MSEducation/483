[[Adiel]]

CATEGORIES: Hebrew Bible people

Adiel ([tpl]lang-he|עדיאל[/tpl]) means "ornament of God." It could also be used to refer to any of the following: 
(1.)The father of Azmaveth, who was treasurer
under David and Solomon (1 Chr. 27:25) 
(2.) A family head of
the tribe of Simeon (1 Chr. 4:36)
(3.) A priest (1 Chr. 9:12)
