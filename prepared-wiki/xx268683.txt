[[Choi Yung-keun]]

CATEGORIES: 1923 births, South Korean footballers, South Korea international footballers, Association football forwards, 1954 FIFA World Cup players, 1994 deaths

Choi Yung-Keun (Hangul: 최영근, Hanja: 崔榮根, 8 February 1923 - 20 July 1994) is a South Korean football forward who played for the South Korea in the 1954 FIFA World Cup.1954 FIFA World Cup Switzerland He also played for Seoul Army Club.
