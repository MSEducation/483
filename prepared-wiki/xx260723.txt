[[Jack Seaward]]

CATEGORIES: 1924 births, 2010 deaths, American writers, American translators, People from Houston, Texas

Jack Seaward was a WWII veteran who was assigned to Military Intelligence because he knew some Japanese in 1941 at a time very few American did.  After some time in US service in the military and later CIA, he worked with a number of companies and became a prolific writer, some of his 45 books in Japanese and English are used today.http://southjnews.com/japan-expert-linguist-writer-jack-seward-passes-away-at-p212-1.htm Japanese Community Newspaper Obituary
