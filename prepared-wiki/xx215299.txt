[[Pruska Mała]]

Pruska Mała [tpl]IPAc-pl|'|p|r|u|s|k|a|-|'|m|a|ł|a[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Augustów, within Augustów County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) – TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|15|km|mi|0[/tpl] north-west of Augustów and [tpl]convert|93|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of the regional capital Białystok.
