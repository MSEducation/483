Santa Marta (also known as [tpl]Lang-es|Estación Santa Martha[/tpl]) is a station along Line A of the Mexico City Metro.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.metro.df.gob.mx/red/estacion.html?id=129|title=Santa Marta|language=Spanish|accessdate=6 August 2011[/tpl][tpl]Cite web|last=Archambault|first=Richard|url=http://mexicometro.org/metro/linea/santa-marta/|title=Santa Marta »  Mexico City Metro System|accessdate=6 August 2011[/tpl]  It is located in the Colonia Ermita Zaragoza  neighborhood of the Iztapalapa borough in Mexico City.
The logo of the station depicts a silhouette of Saint Martha with a pitcher in her hands.
