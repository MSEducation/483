[[Grenada at the Commonwealth Games]]

CATEGORIES: Grenada at the Commonwealth Games

Grenada have competed in seven Commonwealth Games. The first Games for the nation was in 1970. They attneded the next three Games, but did not appear between 1982 and 1998. The only Commonwealth medal to date for Grenada came in 2006, a silver in the men's 400 metres from Alleyne Francique.
