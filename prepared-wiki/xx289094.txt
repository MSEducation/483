[[Gandab, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Gandab ([tpl]lang-fa|گنداب[/tpl], also Romanized as Gandāb)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3773188|Gandab[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 260, in 45 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
