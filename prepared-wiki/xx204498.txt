[[Larry Noble (actor)]]

CATEGORIES: English male stage actors, 1993 deaths, Year of birth missing, English male comedians

Larry Noble was a celebrated stage comedian and actor best known for starring in the Whitehall farces with Brian Rix. He starred in the original production of Reluctant Heroes and as the chirpy French jockey in Dry Rot. On television, he made guest appearances on Last of the Summer Wine in 1975 and Blake's 7 in 1981. He died in 1993.[ref]
