[[The Temporal Void]]

CATEGORIES: 2009 novels, 2000s science fiction novels, British science fiction novels, Novels by Peter F. Hamilton

The Temporal Void is a science fiction novel by British writer Peter F. Hamilton,  the second in his  The Void Trilogy, which was released on 3 October 2008.The Temporal Void[tpl]Dead link|date=April 2013[/tpl] The events in the book follow on from those in The Dreaming Void. It is followed by The Evolutionary Void.
