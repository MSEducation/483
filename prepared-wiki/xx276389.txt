[[David Duncan (minister)]]

CATEGORIES: 1789 births, 1829 deaths, People from Perthshire, Scottish Presbyterian ministers, Presbyterian ministers, Deaths from infectious disease, Scottish clergy, Alumni of the University of Edinburgh

David Duncan (28 Feb 1789 - 27 Aug 1829) was a Scottish Presbyterian minister. Born in Forgandenny, Perthshire, Scotland, fourth son of Gilbert and Lilias Duncan. He was educated at Edinburgh University, studied Latin, Greek, logic, mathematics, moral and natural philosophy. In 1817 he was admitted a student into the Divinity Hall, under the inspection of the General Associate Synod of the Presbyterian Secession Church: the Rev. George Paxton, of Edinburgh, was professor.
