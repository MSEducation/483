[[National Taiwan Symphony Orchestra]]

CATEGORIES: Taiwanese orchestras, Musical groups established in 1945, 1945 establishments in Taiwan

The National Taiwan Symphony Orchestra (NTSO; [tpl]zh|t=國立臺灣交響樂團|t=Guólì Táiwān Jiāoxiǎng Yuètuán[/tpl]), founded in 1945, is the oldest symphony orchestra in Taiwan. It is based in Wufeng, Taichung.
