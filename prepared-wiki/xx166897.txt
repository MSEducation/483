[[ABC Central Victoria]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Victoria

91.1 ABC Central Victoria (call sign: 3ABCRR) is an ABC Local Radio station based in Bendigo, Victoria.[tpl]cite web |title=About ABC Central Victoria |url=http://www.abc.net.au/centralvic/about.htm |date=2007-03-05 |accessdate=2007-12-04 |archiveurl = http://web.archive.org/web/20071012173212/http://abc.net.au/centralvic/about.htm  |archivedate = 2007-10-12[/tpl] The station opened in January 1993 and covers Bendigo, Echuca, St Arnaud and Kyneton.
The station's local programming includes a breakfast show presented by Jonathan Ridnell on weekdays and Simon Rogers on Saturdays, and a weekday morning program hosted by Fiona Parker (also heard on ABC Western Victoria).
