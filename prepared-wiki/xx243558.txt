[[Honorary Architect of Georgia]]

CATEGORIES: Architecture awards

Honorary Architect of Georgia is an award issued annually to the most distinguished architects in Georgia. The award is mentioned in the Constitution of Georgia, in the chapter On Engineering Matters (IV-11),საქმიანობის შესახებ while the administration of the selective procedures and awarding ceremonies is undertaken by the Ministry of Economic Development of Georgia.
