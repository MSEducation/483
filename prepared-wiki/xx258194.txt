Johan Björk (born August 28, 1984) is a Swedish professional ice hockey defenceman who currently plays for Malmö Redhawks of the Swedish second-tier league HockeyAllsvenskan (Swe-2).[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.malmoredhawks.com/Nyheter/120627-Bjork-och-Hetta/ |title=Björk och Hetta klara för Malmö Redhawks |publisher=Malmö Redhawks |last=Martin |first=Nathalie |language=Swedish |date=2012-06-27 |accessdate=2012-07-01[/tpl] He was selected by the Ottawa Senators in the 4th round (125th overall) of the 2002 NHL Entry Draft.
Björk plays pretty secure defense, but can contribute offensively as well. He is a rather tough defenseman and plays a good physical game. Björk, who is quite agile and mobile, has also a good release and he passes the puck well.Eliteprospects.com - Johan Björk
