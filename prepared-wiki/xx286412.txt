[[Hajji Kandi, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Hajji Kandi ([tpl]lang-fa|حاجي كندي[/tpl], also Romanized as Ḩājjī Kandī and Ḩājī Kandī; also known as Khadzhi-Kendi)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3065600|Hajji Kandi[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Bala Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 122, in 34 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
