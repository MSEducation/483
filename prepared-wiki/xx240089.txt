[[Mr Hudson discography]]

CATEGORIES: Discographies of British artists, Rhythm and blues discographies, Pop music discographies

The discography of Mr Hudson, a British R&B/pop artist. Hudson is currently signed to GOOD Music and has released two studio album, most recently and most successful, the release of his second studio album, Straight No Chaser (2009), which included his first United Kingdom top five hit, "Supernova" which featured Kanye West which charted at number two in the United Kingdom, and charted at number one-hundred and four in the United States Billboard Singles Charts.
Hudson released a second and final single from his breakthrough album entitled "White Lies" which charted at number twenty in the United Kingdom. As for the album, Straight No Chaser went onto chart at number twenty-five on the UK Album Charts.
