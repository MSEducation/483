[[Aung Kham Hti]]

CATEGORIES: Burmese politicians, Living people

U Aung Kham Hti is a former monk and a Burmese politician. He was the Chairman of the Pa-O National Organization (PNO), which he re-organized in 1976. Its military wing, known as the Pa-O National Army was also headed by him. On 11 April 1991, the PNO, under his leadership, agreed a ceasefire with the ruling State Law and Order Restoration Council.
