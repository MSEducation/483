[[Royal Park Hospital]]

CATEGORIES: History of Melbourne, Psychiatric hospitals in Australia, Defunct hospitals in Australia, Hospitals established in 1907, 1999 disestablishments, 1907 establishments in Australia

Royal Park Psychiatric Hospital, commonly known as Royal Park is a former Receiving House and Psychiatric Hospital located in Parkville.   Operating for over 90 years, Royal Park Hospital was the first psychiatric hospital established in Victoria after the Lunacy Act of 1903, and was intended for patients with curable disorders.[ref]
