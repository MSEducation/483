Daniel Freire Mendes, ([tpl]IPA-pt|ˈmẽdʒis[/tpl], born 18 January 1981 in São Paulo) is a Brazilian football player who plays for Kalmar FF in the highest Swedish league Allsvenskan.  He is currently on loan with Minnesota United FC.

==Career==

He has previously played for AIK and Degerfors IF.
In 2006, Mendes scored most goals per minute played for AIK. All of his goals were scored as a substitute, earning him a reputation as the squad's "super sub".http://www.esporteclubebahia.com.br/noticias/2347.html
