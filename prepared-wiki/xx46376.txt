[[Bütgenbach]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Liège (province), Municipalities of the German-speaking Community

Bütgenbach ([tpl]lang-fr|Butgenbach[/tpl]) is a municipality located in the Belgian province of Liège. On January 1, 2006 Bütgenbach had a total population of 5,574. The total area is 97.31 km² which gives a population density of 57 inhabitants per km². The official language in this municipality is German.
