[[Pravda.ru]]

CATEGORIES: Russian websites

Pravda.ru (formerly Pravda On-line) is a Russian internet news website established in 1999 and owned by pro-Kremlin Pravda.ru Holding (headed by Vadim Gorshenin).
After the collapse of the Soviet Union, the oldest Soviet paper founded in 1903, Pravda, split into three different papers. Significant members of the main editorial staff (Viktor Afanasiev, Gennady Seleznev, Yuri Zhukov, Vera Tkachenko and Vadim Gorshenin) left Pravda to form the on-line version Pravda.ru.[tpl]cite web |url=http://english.pravda.ru/opinion/columnists/16-09-2013/125664-pravda_mccain-0/ |title=There is no Pravda. There is Pravda.Ru |last1=Gorshenin |first1=Vadim |date=September 16, 2013 |work=english.pravda.ru |publisher= |accessdate=[/tpl]
Pravda.ru has an online readership of about 258,000.
According to politologist Stanislav Belkovsky, Pravda.ru is controlled by Konstantin Kostin and his wife Olga Kostina.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.gazeta.ru/politics/2011/03/16_a_3556453.shtml |title=Называем Ходорковского серийным убийцей |last1= |first1= |last2= |first2= |date=March 16, 2011 |work=gazeta.ru |language=Russian |accessdate=[/tpl]
