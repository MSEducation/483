[[Evan Kaufmann]]

CATEGORIES: 1984 births, Living people, DEG Metro Stars players, Minnesota Golden Gophers men's ice hockey players, River City Lancers players, Thomas Sabo Ice Tigers players, Jewish ice hockey players, Jewish American sportspeople, Ice hockey people from Minnesota, American expatriate sportspeople in Germany, Naturalized citizens of Germany, American people of German-Jewish descent, People from Plymouth, Minnesota, Sportspeople from the Minneapolis–Saint Paul metropolitan area

Evan Kaufmann (born October 31, 1984) is a German-American professional ice hockey forward who currently plays for Thomas Sabo Ice Tigers of the Deutsche Eishockey Liga (DEL).

==Playing career==

Kaufmann, who is Jewish,[tpl]cite journal|title=Professional Hockey Review: 2010–11; European Hockey League|journal=Jewish Sports Review|date=September/October 2011, accessed September 15, 2011|volume=8|issue=87|pages=8–9|accessdate=September 15, 2011[/tpl] was born in Plymouth, Minnesota and graduated from Robbinsdale Armstrong High School at Plymouth in 2004.[tpl]cite web|title=Hockey Profile: Evan Kaufmann|url=http://www.gophersports.com/genrel/122006aab.html|publisher=Minnesota Golden Gophers|accessdate=February 18, 2012[/tpl]  His great-grandparents were murdered during the Holocaust.[tpl]cite news|last=Longman|first=Jere|title=A Jewish Hockey Player at History’s Indelible Crossroad|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2012/02/19/sports/a-jewish-american-hockey-player-at-historys-indelible-crossroad.html?pagewanted=all|newspaper=The New York Times|date=February 19, 2012[/tpl] At the University of Minnesota, Kaufmann played on the Golden Gophers hockey team and studied accounting at the Carlson School of Management. Kaufmann was a Western Collegiate Hockey Association scholar-athlete pick all four years at Minnesota.[tpl]cite web|last=Augustovitz|first=Roman|title=Gophers teammates reach fork in the road|url=http://www.startribune.com/sports/gophers/16366751.html|work=Minneapolis Star-Tribune|archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20081010224118/http://www.startribune.com/sports/gophers/16366751.html|archivedate=October 10, 2008|date=March 6, 2008[/tpl] 
Kaufmann moved to Germany in 2008 and received German citizenship in order to play for the national team, DEG Metro Stars of Deutsche Eishockey Liga.[tpl]cite web| url = http://www.deg-metro-stars.de/servlet/PB/menu/1016372/index.html | title = Roach and Kaufmann extend with Metro Stars | publisher = DEG Metro Stars | date = March 14, 2011 | accessdate = April 17, 2011 | language = German[/tpl] He is among the top scorers in the league. Kaufmann admits that his teammates are very curious about him being Jewish and often ask him questions. Kaufmann told the UK's Daily Mail, "I didn't have to think hard about it. It is a great honour but it will also be a very emotional moment for me when I hear the national anthem played."[tpl]cite web|author=Allan Hall |url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2098217/Ice-hockey-star-set-handful-post-war-Jews-represent-Germany-international-level.html#ixzz1ltPpW2 yd |title=Ice hockey star set to become one of handful of post-war Jews to represent Germany at international level |publisher=Dailymail.co.uk |date=February 8, 2012 |accessdate=January 14, 2013[/tpl]
On February 16, 2012, Kaufmann agreed to leave DEG at seasons end and sign a two-year contract with fellow DEL club, Nürnberg Ice Tigers.[tpl]cite web| url = http://www.icetigers.de/ICETIGERS/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=1066:ice-tigers-holen-evan-kaufmann&catid=1:aktuelle-nachrichten&Itemid=76 | title = Kaufmann agrees to contract with Ice Tigers | publisher = Nürnberg Ice Tigers | date = February 16, 2012 | accessdate = February 16, 2012 | language = German[/tpl]
