[[Bucknall, Staffordshire]]

CATEGORIES: Areas of Stoke-on-Trent

Bucknall is a suburb of Stoke-on-Trent in the county of Staffordshire.
It was recorded in the Domesday Book as having a taxable value of 0.3 geld and consisting of three ploughlands.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.domesdaymap.co.uk/place/SJ9047/bucknall/|title=Place: Bucknall|work=domesdaymap.co.uk|accessdate=25 June 2011[/tpl]
Mitchell High School is a comprehensive school located in Bucknall that educates pupils of ages 11-16."Mitchell High School", BBC News, 11 January 2007 There are around 570 students on roll drawn from a
community that has high levels of social deprivation."Mitchell High School", Ofsted, 19 January 2004
Nearby Berry Hill High School and Sports College, located on the Eaton Park/Berryhill estate is often described as within Bucknall, sharing as it does the ST2 postcode.
