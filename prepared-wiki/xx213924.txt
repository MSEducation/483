[[Vi er allesammen tossede]]

CATEGORIES: 1959 films, 1950s comedy films, Danish films, Danish comedy films, Danish-language films, Films directed by Sven Methling

Vi er allesammen tossede ([tpl]lang-en|We are Altogether Crazy[/tpl]) is a 1959 Danish comedy directed by Sven Methling and starring Kjeld Petersen, Buster Larsen, Birgitte Reimer and Dirch Passer. The film relates the story of a confused driver who is mistakenly committed to an insane asylum after he insists to police that his car was damaged in an accident with an elephant. The film received the Bodil Award for Best Danish Film in 1960 and is listed on the Danish Film Institute's Top 100 Danish Films.[tpl]cite web |url=http://dnfx.dfi.dk/pls/dnf/pwt.page_setup?p_pagename=dnffuldvis&p_parmlist=filmid=8771 |title=Vi er allesammen tossede - Danmarks Nationalfilmografi |format= |work= |accessdate=[/tpl]
