==Biography==

Haber has competed in three Paralympics, winning at least one medal at each.  His first games were in 1992 where he won three track golds in 100m, 200m and 400m and a silver in the long jump as well as being part of the German 4x100m relay team.  In 1996 he won three more silvers in the 10om, 200m and long jump.  This was followed in 2000 where he won a silver in the 100m and missed out in the relay and 200m.profile on paralympic.org
