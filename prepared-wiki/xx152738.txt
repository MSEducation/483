==Career==

She was born in Maharashtra, India. She started her career with Vikram Bhatt's Inteha (2003) which failed at the box office. After a string of unsuccessful movies and several advertisements, she acted in  Chak De India in the year 2007 where she plays the role of a goalkeeper and captain of the Indian women's national hockey team. Her most recent movie was 1920: Evil Returns.
