[[Belgian general election, 1971]]

CATEGORIES: 1971 elections in Europe, Elections in Belgium, 1971 in Belgium

General elections were held in Belgium on 7 November 1971.Nohlen, D & Stöver, P (2010) Elections in Europe: A data handbook, p289 ISBN 978-3-8329-5609-7 The result was a victory for the Christian People's Party, which won 40 of the 212 seats in the Chamber of Representatives and 34 of the 106 seats in the Senate. Voter turnout was 91.5%.Nohlen & Stöver, p291
