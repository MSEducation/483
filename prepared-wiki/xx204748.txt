[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Itapipoca]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1971, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Fortaleza, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Itapipoca ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Itapipocanus[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Itapipoca in the Ecclesiastical province of Fortaleza in Brazil.
