[[Talkhab, Zanjanrud]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Talkhab ([tpl]lang-fa|تلخاب[/tpl], also Romanized as Talkhāb)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3766530|Talkhab[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 461, in 110 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
