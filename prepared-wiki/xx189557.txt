[[8685 Fauré]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Asteroids named for people, Astronomical objects discovered in 1992, Gabriel Fauré

8685 Fauré is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1197.7873275 days (3.28 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=8685 | title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-04-28 | publisher = NASA[/tpl]
The asteroid was discovered on April 4, 1992, and named after the composer Gabriel Fauré.
