[[Mark Rasch]]

CATEGORIES: American lawyers, People associated with computer security, Living people, 1958 births

Mark D. Rasch is an attorney and author, working in the areas of corporate and government cybersecurity, privacy and incident response. He is currently the Vice President, Deputy General Counsel, and Chief Privacy and Data Security Officer for SAIC.
