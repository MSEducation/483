[[2002 Kremlin Cup]]

CATEGORIES: 2002 ATP Tour, 2002 WTA Tour, Kremlin Cup, 2002 in Russia

The 2002 Kremlin Cup was a tennis tournament played on indoor carpet courts at the Olympic Stadium in Moscow in Russia that was part of the International Series of the 2002 ATP Tour and of Tier I of the 2002 WTA Tour. The tournament ran from September 30 through October 6, 2002.
