[[Mark Leggett]]

CATEGORIES: American composers, Living people

Mark Leggett is a Los Angeles-based, twice Emmy nominated composer who has composed music for hundreds of television and documentary productions in the U.S.
Credits include television series My Name Is Earl, theme music for The Pretender (Emmy nominated), Young Americans, Red Handed. 
Documentary credits include "Conquistador : A Day In Their Lives" (Emmy nominated ), "Nazi America: A Secret History",  "Wage Slaves: Not Getting By in America", and the "Unsolved History" series for the Discovery Channel as well as many other broad-ranging documentary series and programs.
Leggett has also written music for and performed by The Band for the PBS series "Great Drives" as well as writing music performed by Richard Thompson for the PBS series "The Royal Mile".
