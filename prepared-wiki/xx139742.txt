[[Malcov]]

CATEGORIES: Villages and municipalities in Bardejov District

Malcov is a village and municipality in Bardejov District in the Prešov Region of north-east Slovakia.

==History==

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1338

==Geography==

The municipality lies at an altitude of 410 metres and covers an area of 18.876 km².
It has a population of about 1460 people.
