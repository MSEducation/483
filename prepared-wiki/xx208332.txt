[[Ditte Jensen]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 births, Living people, Danish swimmers, Olympic swimmers of Denmark, Swimmers at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Freestyle swimmers, European Aquatics Championships medalists in swimming

Ditte Christiane Jensen (born October 25, 1980 in Birkerød, Hovedstaden) is a former freestyle swimmer from Denmark, who represented her native country at the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta, Georgia. 
A member of swimming club Idrætsforeningen Skjold she is best known for winning the bronze medal at the 1997 European Championships (LC) in the women's 4×200 m freestyle, alongside Britt Raaby, Berit Puggaard, and Mette Jacobsen. She was the youngest member (15 years, 271 days) of the Danish delegation at the 1996 Summer Olympics.
