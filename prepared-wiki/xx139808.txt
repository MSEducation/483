[[Tristan Wilds]]

Tristan Paul Mack Wilds (born July 15, 1989) is an American actor and Grammy-nominated recording artist. He is best known for his roles as Michael Lee on the HBO original drama series The Wire and as Dixon Wilson on the CW drama series 90210.

==Early life==

Wilds was born in Staten Island, New York, to Monique Moncion and Paul Wilds.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.silive.com/entertainment/tvfilm/index.ssf/2008/01/profile_tristain_wilds.html|title=PROFILE: Tristan Wilds|author=Platt, Tevan|publisher=Staten Island Advance|date=2008-01-06|accessdate=2009-03-17[/tpl][tpl]cite web|author= |url=http://www.silive.com/news/index.ssf/2008/01/star_of_hbos_the_wire_is_still.html |title=Star of HBO's 'The Wire' is still an Islander at heart |publisher=SILive.com |date= |accessdate=2013-03-28[/tpl] He is of African American (father) and Irish, and Dominican  (mother) descent.[tpl]cite web|url=http://en.terra.com/lifestyle/articles/html/hof2537.htm|title=EXCLUSIVE: '90210' Stars Talk To Terra|publisher=Terra Networks|author=Pastorizo, Dennis|accessdate=2009-03-17[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/latino/2008/09/03/2008-09-03_tristan_wilds_a_beverly_hills_dominican.html|title=Tristan Wilds: a Beverly Hills Dominican|author=Hernandez, Lee|publisher=Daily News|date=2008-09-04|accessdate=2009-03-17[/tpl] He attended Michael J. Petrides School. He started acting at age seven, following in the foot steps of his older brother.

==Acting career==

In 2005 Wilds made his acting debut in The N Drama Mini-Series Miracle's Boys. Wilds has since guest appeared on television shows such as Cold Case and Law & Order. The following year Wilds made his film debut in a bit-role in the Independent Drama film Half Nelson alongside Academy Award Nominee Ryan Gosling.http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0468489/ Wilds has dominantly appeared in a number of music videos for Jay-Z, Lil Flip, Wale, Wynter Gordon and Alicia Keys.http://www.imdb.com/name/nm2080933/otherworks Most recently he made a cameo in the music video for How To Love by American Hip-Hop Rapper Lil Wayne.[tpl]cite web|url=http://thequeenscastle.net/official-visual-lil-waynes-how-to-love-feat-tristan-wilds/ |title=Official Visual :: Lil' Wayne's "How to Love" w/Cameo by Tristan Wilds - The Queen's Castle |publisher=Thequeenscastle.net |date=2011-08-24 |accessdate=2013-03-28[/tpl] In 2008 Wilds appeared alongside Jessica Alba and Hayden Panettiere in a promotional-clip titled The Muzzler encouraging young people to vote.[tpl]cite web|url=http://thesuperficial.com/2008/10/jessica_alba_muzzling_hayden_p.php |title=Jessica Alba ‘muzzling’ Hayden Panettiere not at all like I imagined (No pudding, jet packs.) - The Superficial - Because You're Ugly |publisher=The Superficial |date=2008-10-03 |accessdate=2013-03-28[/tpl]
In 2006 Wilds joined the HBO Drama television series The Wire in a major recurring role. Wilds portrayed Michael Lee, a troubled middle school student who is living in poverty and caring for his younger brother. The Wire cast mate Jermaine Crawford is his second cousin.[tpl]cite web|last=Arnold|first=Paul W.|title=Jermaine Crawford: Youngest In Charge|url=http://www.hiphopdx.com/index/interviews/id.993/title.jermaine-crawford-youngest-in-charge|publisher=hiphopdx|accessdate=2012-07-14[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.hbo.com/the-wire/cast-and-crew |title=The Wire: Cast & Crew |publisher=HBO |date= |accessdate=2013-03-28[/tpl] Wilds appeared over the course of 23-episodes.http://www.imdb.com/name/nm2080933/filmoseries#tt0306414 Shortly after "The Wire" ended Wilds attended an acting workshop at the Roundabout Theatre Company, in which he worked with Actress Phylicia Rashād and played the role of Brice in a stage production of Stockholm Brooklyn at the Cherry Lane Theatre.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.starpulse.com/Actors/Wilds,_Tristan/Biography/ |title=Tristan Wilds Biography |publisher=Starpulse.com |date=1989-07-15 |accessdate=2013-03-28[/tpl] In 2007 Wilds was cast in the Fox Searchlight Pictures Drama film The Secret Life of Bees an adaptation of the novel of the same name.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprah.com/omagazine/On-the-Set-of-The-Secret-Life-of-Bees |title=On the Set of The Secret Life of Bees |publisher=Oprah.com |date=2008-09-29 |accessdate=2013-03-28[/tpl] Production began in January 2008 in Lumberton, North Carolina with Queen Latifah, Alicia Keys and Dakota Fanning confirmed to star.[tpl]cite web|author= |url=http://www.myentertainmentworld.com/mew/film_production_newlisting.html |title=Feature Films: New Listing |publisher=Myentertainmentworld.com |date= |accessdate=2013-03-28[/tpl] Wilds portrays Zach Taylor, the romantic interest to  Fanning's character. In July 2008 Wilds was cast in The CW Teen Drama television series 90210 a spin-off to Beverly Hills, 90210.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.zap2it.com/tv/zap-tristanwilds90210,0,2451104.story |title='The Wire' Guy Joins '90210' Spinoff |publisher=Zap2it |date=2008-07-13 |accessdate=2013-03-28[/tpl] Wilds portrays Dixon Wilson a teenage boy who is adopted and moves with his family to Beverly Hills.Tristan Wilds makes move to "90210", Reuters, May 4, 2008. In July 2011 Wilds announced he had joined the Independent Drama film Indelible.[tpl]cite web|url=http://90210-media.org/?p=9263 |title=Tristan Wilds will film ‘Indelible’ in Schenectady, NY this fall | 90210 Media • Your #1 fansite for the CW's new 90210! |publisher=|date= |accessdate=2013-03-28[/tpl] The film was planned to begin filming in Upstate New York sometime in October that same year but plans never materialized.[tpl]cite web|author=Christine |url=http://www.onlocationvacations.com/2011/07/05/tristan-wilds-will-film-indelible-in-schenectady-ny-this-fall/ |title=Tristan Wilds will film ‘Indelible’ in Schenectady, NY this fall |publisher=Onlocationvacations.com |date=2011-07-05 |accessdate=2013-03-28[/tpl] In April 2009 a trailer was filmed to promote awareness of the yet-to-be filmed film.
