[[Hijack Stories]]

CATEGORIES: 2000 films, South African films, English-language films, 2000s crime films, Films directed by Oliver Schmitz

Hijack Stories is a 2000 South African crime film directed by Oliver Schmitz. It was screened in the Un Certain Regard section at the 2001 Cannes Film Festival.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.festival-cannes.com/en/archives/ficheFilm/id/2000125/year/2001.html |title=Festival de Cannes: Hijack Stories |accessdate=2009-10-19|work=festival-cannes.com[/tpl]
