[[Slovakia at the 2008 Summer Paralympics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 2008 Summer Paralympics, Slovakia at the Paralympics, 2008 in Slovak sport

Slovakia competed at the 2008 Summer Paralympics in Beijing.
Slovak competitors took part in table tennis,"9 Nations preparing for the Paralympics in Lasko", official website of the International Paralympic Table Tennis Federation among other sports.
