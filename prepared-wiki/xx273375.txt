[[Bangkok Jazz Festival]]

CATEGORIES: Jazz festivals in Thailand, Recurring events established in 2003, 2003 establishments in Thailand, Bangkok culture

Bangkok Jazz Festival is a jazz festival in Bangkok, Thailand. The festival was established in 2003 in commemoration of the King of Thailand who is said to have a love of jazz.[tpl]cite book|last1=Burke|first1=Andrew|last2=Bush|first2=Austin (CON)|title=Bangkok|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=xS5qv9mi1jgC&pg=PA22|accessdate=28 September 2011|date=15 September 2010|publisher=Lonely Planet|isbn=978-1-74179-587-5|page=22[/tpl] It takes place at Sanam Suea Pa at Dusit. Usually held for three nights in December,[tpl]cite book|last1=Cummings|first1=Joe|last2=Williams|first2=China|title=Bangkok|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=nnW6AAAAIAAJ|accessdate=28 September 2011|date=15 September 2006|publisher=Lonely Planet|isbn=978-1-74059-858-3[/tpl] a number of internationally known artists perform at the festival. The festival attracts around 30,000 people annually.[tpl]cite book|last=Williams|first=Paul|title=Bangkok A-Z...on the cheap|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=Xx1YoWSIRYUC&pg=PA37|accessdate=28 September 2011|publisher=Bangkokbooks|isbn=978-616-222-008-1|page=37[/tpl]
