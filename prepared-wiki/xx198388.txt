[[Odwa Ndungane]]

CATEGORIES: 1981 births, Living people, People from Mthatha, Xhosa people, South African rugby union players, South Africa international rugby union players, Sharks (rugby union) players, Natal Sharks players, Bulls (rugby union) players, Blue Bulls players, Border Bulldogs players, Rugby union wings

Odwa Ndungane (born 20 February 1981 in Mthatha, South Africa) is a rugby union player for the Sharks in the Super Rugby competition. He plays on the wing.
He made his Test debut against Italy in a 26-0 victory in Cape Town in 2008 ahead of, amongst others, his twin brother Akona Ndungane.
