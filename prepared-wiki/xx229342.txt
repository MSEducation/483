[[Seema Bhargav]]

CATEGORIES: Living people

Seema is an Indian soap opera actress.[tpl]cite news|title=The soap that gripped the nation|url=http://www.telegraphindia.com/1080706/jsp/7days/story_9510833.jsp|accessdate=16 November 2010|newspaper=The Telegraph (India)|date=6 July 2008[/tpl]  She played Maasi, or Aunt on Kasamh Se, a popular Zee TV soap opera by Ekta Kapoor.  The character is very kind and loving towards her nephew and his wife Bani Walia, the protagonist.
