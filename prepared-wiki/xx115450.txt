One Voice is the first album from country music singer Billy Gilman. The album was released on June 20, 2000 via Epic Records Nashville. It reached #2 on the Billboard Top Country Albums chart, and was certified 2Ã— Platinum. The highest charting single from the album was the lead-off, which was also the title track. "One Voice" is also Gilman's highest chart hit, reached #20.
