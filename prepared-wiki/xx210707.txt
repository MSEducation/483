[[Woodbridge High School (Bridgeville, Delaware)]]

CATEGORIES: High schools in Sussex County, Delaware, Public high schools in Delaware

Woodbridge High School is a public high school in Bridgeville, Delaware. It is part of the Woodbridge School District and located in Sussex County. It has about 500 students who go by the name of the "Blue Raiders". The school colors are blue, grey, and white.
