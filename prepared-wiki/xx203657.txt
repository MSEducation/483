[[Raffaele Zanon]]

CATEGORIES: Venetian politicians, Venetist politicians, Living people, 1957 births, National Alliance (Italy) politicians

Raffaele Zanon (Verona, 26 September 1957) is an Italian politician.
He joined the Italian Social Movement in the 1970s and later National Alliance. Three times elected to the Regional Council of Veneto (1995, 2000, 2005), he was regional minister of Social Affairs in Galan I Government (1995–2000) and minister of Security and Immigration in Galan II Government (2000–2005).[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.raffaelezanon.it/chisono |title=Chi Sono | Raffaele Zanon - Fratelli Italia |publisher=Raffaelezanon.it |date=2013-01-18 |accessdate=2013-06-17[/tpl]
In 2008, along with National Alliance, Zanon joined The People of Freedom (PdL), but failed to be re-elected to the Council in 2010.[tpl]cite web|url=http://elezionistorico.interno.it/candidati.php?tpel=R&dtel=28/03/2010&tpa=I&tpe=P&lev0=0&levsut0=0&lev1=5&levsut1=1&lev2=54&levsut2=2&ne1=5&ne2=54&es0=S&es1=S&es2=S&ms=S&ne=54&nlg=8&ccp=3424&ts=C |title=::: Ministero dell'Interno ::: Archivio Storico delle Elezioni - Regionali del 28 Marzo 2010 |publisher=Elezionistorico.interno.it |date= |accessdate=2013-06-17[/tpl] He is also the leader of Venetian Right, a regional faction of the PdL composed by former Missini who are both Italian and Venetian nationalists.[tpl]dead link|date=June 2013[/tpl]
