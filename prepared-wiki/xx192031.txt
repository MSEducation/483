[[Minerva Clark Gets a Clue]]

CATEGORIES: American children's novels, 2005 novels, American mystery novels, Bloomsbury Publishing books

Minerva Clark Gets a Clue (ISBN 978-1-58234-677-9) is the first book in the Minerva Clark series of mystery novels. Minerva Clark Gets a Clue was written by Karen Karbo, and was published by Bloomsbury Publishing in 2005.
