[[Andrzej Pluciński]]

CATEGORIES: 1915 births, 1963 deaths, Olympic basketball players of Poland, Basketball players at the 1936 Summer Olympics, Sportspeople from Kraków

Andrzej Pluciński (November 13, 1915 in Kraków – August 27, 1963) was a Polish basketball player who competed in the 1936 Summer Olympics.
He was part of the Polish basketball team, which finished fourth in the Olympic tournament. He played all six matches.
