Daniel Raymond McClintock (born April 19, 1977) is an American professional basketball player at the center position. He is 7'0" and weighs 260 lb.
He attended Northern Arizona University before being selected with the 53rd overall pick in the 2000 Draft by the National Basketball Association's Denver Nuggets. During the 2000–01 NBA season, he played six games while averaging 3.0 points and 2.8 rebounds per contest.
