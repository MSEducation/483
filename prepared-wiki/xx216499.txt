[[2008 Individual Speedway Junior Polish Championship]]

CATEGORIES: Individual Speedway Junior Polish Championship, 2008 in Polish speedway

The 2008 Individual Speedway Junior Polish Championship ([tpl]lang-pl|Młodzieżowe Indywidualne Mistrzostwa Polski[/tpl], MIMP) is the 2008 version of Individual Speedway Junior Polish Championship organized by the Polish Motor Union (PZM).
The Final will take place on August 19, 2008 in Rybnik. Last time Final was took in Leszno in 2003 season when Łukasz Romanek beat Adrian Miedziński and Zbigniew Czerwiński.
Defending Under-21 Polish Champion is Paweł Hlib from Caelum Stal Gorzów Wlkp. who won in 2007 Final in Rzeszów, but he is 22 old and can not started in this competition.
