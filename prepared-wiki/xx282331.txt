Jack Alexander Price (born 19 December 1992) is an English footballer who plays as a midfielder for Wolverhampton Wanderers.

==Career==

Price progressed from the academy of Wolverhampton Wanderers to sign a professional contract in 2011.[tpl]cite news| title=Wolves offer pro contracts to three|publisher=Express & Star| url=http://www.expressandstar.com/sport/wolverhampton-wanderers-fc/2011/03/11/wolves-offer-pro-contracts-to-three/| date=11 March 2011|accessdate=[/tpl] The midfielder made his senior debut on 10 August 2013, coming on as a substitute in a 4–0 win against Gillingham.[tpl]cite news| title=Wolves 4–0 Gillingham|publisher=BBC Sport| url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/23558680| date=10 August 2013|accessdate=[/tpl]
