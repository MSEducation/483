[[Manticora (band)]]

CATEGORIES: Danish progressive metal musical groups, Danish power metal musical groups, Musical groups established in 1996

Manticora is a heavy metal band from Hvidovre, Denmark formed in 1996 by Lars and Kristian Larsen."Manticora, Encyclopaedia Metallum. Web, Sep. 11, 2010. Their current label is Locomotive Music. Their lyrical content typically consists of literature, fantasy, and science fiction. Although they are considered to be a progressive power metal band, they do have an essence of a speed metal influence.
