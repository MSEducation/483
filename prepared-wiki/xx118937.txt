[[Blues Matters!]]

CATEGORIES: British music magazines, Blues music magazines

Blues Matters! is a British blues magazine.

==History==

Blues Matters! was founded in 1999.
The Blues Foundation, in Memphis, Tennessee, awarded the magazine the "Keeping the Blues Alive" (KBA) Award for 2007, in the Print Media category. This is only the second time a British publication has received the award, and the first time for a Welsh production. Previous recipients have included major titles, such as Rolling Stone.
