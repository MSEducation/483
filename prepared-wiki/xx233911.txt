[[Christine Chinkin]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Academics of the London School of Economics, University of Michigan Law School faculty, International law scholars

Christine Chinkin is a Professor of International Law at the London School of Economics and Political Science[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/profile/christine-chinkin|title=Christine Chinkin|publisher=The Guardian|accessdate=17 April 2011|location=London|date=14 April 2011[/tpl] and the 
William W. Cook Global Law Professor at the University of Michigan Law School. She was a member of the four-person United Nations Fact Finding Mission on the Gaza Conflict created by the United Nations Human Rights Council.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.un.org/apps/news/story.asp?NewsID=32526&Cr=palestin&Cr1=|publisher=UN News Centre|title=Human Rights Council to discuss recent UN probe into Gaza conflict|date=13 October 2009|accessdate=16 October 2009[/tpl]
From January 2010, she is a member of the Human Rights Advisory Panel[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.unmikonline.org/hrap/Eng/Pages/default.aspx|title=The Human Rights Advisory Panel|publisher=UNMIK DPI|accessdate=21 January 2013[/tpl] of the United Nations Mission in Kosovo.
