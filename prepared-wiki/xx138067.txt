[[Australians in the United Kingdom]]

CATEGORIES: British people of Australian descent, Immigration to the United Kingdom by country of origin, Australian diaspora

Australians in the United Kingdom include Australians who have become residents or citizens of the United Kingdom, as well as the numerous students and temporary workers from Australia who live in the UK.
In the United Kingdom there are 400,000 Australians.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.smh.com.au/news/world/poms-plead-with-aussies-not-to-leave/2008/11/26/1227491577546.html |title=British paper pleads with Aussies not to go home - World |publisher=Smh.com.au |date=2008-11-26 |accessdate=2013-11-18[/tpl] The 2001 UK Census recorded 107,871 Australian-born people.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oecd.org/dataoecd/18/23/34792376.xls|title=Country-of-birth database|publisher=Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development|accessdate=2008-12-07[/tpl] Estimates published by the Office for National Statistics suggest that, in 2009, this figure stood at 118,000.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.statistics.gov.uk/downloads/theme_population/Population-by-country-of-birth-and-nationality-Oct08-Sep09.zip | title=Estimated population resident in the United Kingdom, by foreign country of birth (Table 1.3) | publisher=Office for National Statistics | date=September 2009 | accessdate=8 July 2010[/tpl] The highest concentration of Australians in the UK is in south-west London with sizeable communities in Earl's Court, Kensington, Hammersmith, Fulham, Shepherds Bush and Putney.[tpl]cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/shared/spl/hi/uk/05/born_abroad/countries/html/australia.stm|title=Born abroad: Australia|publisher=BBC News|accessdate=2009-10-05 | date=2005-09-07[/tpl] In London, there are 200,000 Australians.[tpl]cite web|last=Daley |first=Gemma |url=http://www.bloomberg.com/apps/news%3Fpid%3Dnewsarchive%26sid%3Da.PII8jBuvyU&sa=U&ei=_ffSUO6OI834sgbJ9YH4Cg&ved=0CCkQFjAIOAo&sig2=mn6QNT9S-JN3Ea1Y5RFp3Q&usg=AFQjCNEcbkaofOtu6DTc0k8COyi233UuxQ |title=Australia's Voters Choose Between Howard, Rudd (Update1) |publisher=Bloomberg |date=2007-11-23 |accessdate=2013-11-18[/tpl]
The late-2000s recession has resulted in increasing numbers of Australians returning home from the UK, with 2,700 Australians leaving each month in late 2008, compared to 1,750 a month in 2005.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.timesonline.co.uk/tol/news/world/article5225956.ece|title=London exodus as Australians return home for jobs and sun|last=Hamilton|first=Fiona|date=2008-11-25|work=The Times|accessdate=2009-10-05[/tpl] The number of Australians applying for working holiday visas has also fallen.
