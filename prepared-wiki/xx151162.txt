[[W. Gregory Stewart]]

CATEGORIES: Canadian science fiction writers, Canadian poets, Canadian emigrants to Australia, Living people

W. Gregory Stewart (born in Toronto) is a poet most associated with speculative fiction. He has won the Rhysling Award four times.Locus Index of SF Awards 
He was born in Canada, has lived in Australia, and currently resides in Los Angeles. Aside from poetry he also writes short fiction and was a past nominee for the Nebula Award for Best Short Story. Stewart conducts a writer's workshop for youth, works for a public utility, and grows heirloom tomatoes from seed. He is married to illustrator Helen Shoenfeld and has a son, Jesse.Fables.org
