[[Dev discography]]

CATEGORIES: Discographies of American artists, Pop music discographies

The discography for American pop artist Dev consists of one studio album, three singles, eight featured singles, and four music videos. Her debut album The Night the Sun Came Up, has spawned four singles, "Bass Down Low" featuring The Cataracs, "In the Dark", "Naked" with Enrique Iglesias and "In My Trunk".
