[[Rudolf Gramlich]]

CATEGORIES: 1908 births, 1988 deaths, Sportspeople from Frankfurt, German footballers, 1934 FIFA World Cup players, Footballers at the 1936 Summer Olympics, Olympic footballers of Germany, Germany international footballers, Eintracht Frankfurt players, Eintracht Frankfurt presidents

Rudolf "Rudi" Gramlich (June 6, 1908 â€“ March 14, 1988 in Frankfurt) was a German football player and chairman. 
As an active player he performed for Eintracht Frankfurt. Gramlich made 22 international appearances for Germany between 1931 and 1936, achieving third place at the 1934 World Cup in Italy. He was the captain of the German team at the 1936 Olympic Games in Berlin.
From 1939 to 1942 and between 1955 and 1970 he was the chairman of Eintracht Frankfurt.
