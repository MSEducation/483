[[Xinjiekou Station (Nanjing)]]

CATEGORIES: Xinjiekou Station (Nanjing), Nanjing Metro, Nanjing Metro stations, Railway stations opened in 2005

Xinjiekou Station ([tpl]Zh|s=新街口站|t=新街口站[/tpl]) is an interchange station between Line 1 and Line 2 of the Nanjing Metro. It is located in Xinjiekou, the central business and commercial district of Nanjing.
Line 1 and Line 2 have another interchange station: Yuantong Station.

==Opening dates==

Xinjiekou first opened on August 12, 2005, with the opening of Line 1. On May 28, 2010, Xinjiekou became an interchange when Line 2 entered into operation.
