[[Autistic art]]

CATEGORIES: Art genres, Autism in arts

Autistic art is art created by autistic artists or art which captures or conveys a variety of autistic experiences or demeanor.

==Autistic art==

Art by autistic artists has long been shown in separate venues from artists without disabilities.AutismConnect - Museums thrust autistic artists into the mainstream and the art of those with autism has often been considered part of Outsider Art The works of some autistic artists have featured in art publications, documentaries and been exhibited in mainstream galleries.
Stephen Wiltshire is one of the most famous autistic artists.  His works are largely architectural or of cityscapes, monuments and buildings.
