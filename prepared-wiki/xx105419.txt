[[Demetrio Camarda]]

CATEGORIES: Linguists from Italy, Italian people of Arbëresh descent, 1821 births, 1882 deaths, People from Piana degli Albanesi

Demetrio Camarda (Arbërisht: Dhimitër Kamarda; Piana degli Albanesi, 1821 – Livorno, 1882) was an Arbëreshë linguist, patriot of the arbëreshë, and publisher of Albanian folklore, with scientific knowledge also in the field of Indo-European linguistics. He was the follower of the work already performed by Jeronim De Rada.Encyclopaedia britannica: a dictionary of arts, sciences, literature and ..., by Hugh Chisholm, page 486, eleventh edition, New York 1911 (http://books.google.com/books?id=0PgtAAAAIAAJ&pg=PA486&dq=Demetrio+Camarda&cd=8#v=onepage&q=Demetrio%20Camarda&f=false). His main work, Test of Comparative Grammar on Albanian Language is the first scientific work of comparative historic study on the topic.Saggio di grammatologia comparata sulla lingua albanese, by Demetrio Camarda (http://books.google.com/books?id=OfEGAAAAQAAJ&printsec=frontcover&dq=Demetrio+Camarda&cd=1#v=onepage&q=&f=false) and Appendice al saggio di grammatologia comparata sulla lingua albanese, by Demetrio Camarda (http://books.google.com/books?id=syYTAAAAYAAJ&printsec=frontcover&dq=Demetrio+Camarda&cd=2#v=onepage&q=&f=false). He made an important contribution with the publication of The Albanian general alphabet in 1869.
