[[André Miguel Valente Silva]]

CATEGORIES: 1995 births, Living people, Portuguese footballers, Association football forwards, Segunda Liga players, F.C. Porto players, F.C. Porto B players

André Miguel Valente da Silva (born 6 November 1995 in Baguim do Monte, Gondomar) is a Portuguese footballer who plays for F.C. Porto B as a forward.

==Football career==
