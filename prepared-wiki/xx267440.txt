[[Neil Pinner]]

Neil Douglas Pinner (born 28 September 1990) is an English cricketer.  Pinner is a right-handed batsman who bowls right-arm off break.  He was born in Wordsley, Worcestershire and educated at the Royal Grammar School Worcester.[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/224/224455/224455.html|title=Player profile: Neil Pinner|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=23 September 2011[/tpl]
Having played for the Worcestershire Second XI since 2008,[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/224/224455/all_teams.html|title=Teams Neil Pinner played for|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=23 September 2011[/tpl] Pinner made his full debut for Worcestershire in a List A match against Middlesex in the 2011 Clydesdale Bank 40.  He made eight further appearances in that competition,[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/224/224455/List_A_Matches.html|title=List A Matches played by Neil Pinner|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=23 September 2011[/tpl] scoring a total of 122 runs at an average of 13.55, with a high score of 37.[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/224/224455/a_Batting_by_Team.html|title=List A Batting and Fielding For Each Team by Neil Pinner|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=23 September 2011[/tpl]  He also made a single first-class appearance in the 2011 County Championship against Nottinghamshire.[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/224/224455/First-Class_Matches.html|title=First-Class Matches played by Neil Pinner|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=23 September 2011[/tpl]  He batted once in the match and was dismissed for a duck by Charlie Shreck.[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Scorecards/316/316098.html|title=Worcestershire v Nottinghamshire, 2011 County Championship|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=23 September 2011[/tpl]
