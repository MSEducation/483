[[Twenty (Boyz II Men album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2011 albums, Boyz II Men albums, Albums produced by Tim & Bob, Avex Group albums

Twenty is the tenth studio album by American R&B group Boyz II Men. The album was released in the United States on October 25, 2011.  The first single was "More Than You'll Ever Know" featuring Charlie Wilson. As of September 2011, the single was the most added song to radio sets on urban adult contemporary radio stations.[tpl]cite web|title= Most Added at Urban AC Radio| url=http://www.boyziimen.com/news/45561 |date=1 September 2011 |accessdate=25 September 2011[/tpl] The album featured 13 new material songs and nine rerecorded Boyz II Men classics. It was produced Tim & Bob, Babyface, Jimmy Jam and Terry Lewis, and Teddy Riley.[tpl]cite web|url=http://blogs.centrictv.com/music/soulsessions/new-music-boyz-ii-men-twenty-tracklist/ |title=NEW MUSIC// Boyz II Men “Twenty” Tracklist | Soul Sessions |publisher=Blogs.centrictv.com |date= |accessdate=2012-08-15[/tpl] The album debuted at number 20 on the Billboard 200, selling 18,400 copies in its first week.[tpl]cite web|last=Langhorne |first=Cyrus |url=http://www.sohh.com/2011/11/jay-z_kanye_west_go_platinum_boyz_ii_men.html |title=Jay-Z & Kanye West Go Platinum, Boyz II Men's "20" Hits No. 20, Jedi Mind Tricks Invade The Chart |publisher=Sohh.Com |date=2011-11-02 |accessdate=2012-08-15[/tpl]
