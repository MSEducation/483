[[Tom Barrington]]

CATEGORIES: 1944 births, People from Lima, Ohio, American football running backs, Ohio State Buckeyes football players, Washington Redskins players, New Orleans Saints players, Living people

George Thomas Barrington (born January 29, 1944 in Lima, Ohio) is a former American football running back in the National Football League for the Washington Redskins and New Orleans Saints.  He played college football at The Ohio State University and was drafted in the third round of the 1966 NFL Draft.  Barrington was also selected in the sixteenth round of the 1966 AFL Draft by the Kansas City Chiefs.
