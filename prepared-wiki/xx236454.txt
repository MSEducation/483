[[Apalochlamys]]

CATEGORIES: Asteraceae genera, Flora of New South Wales, Flora of South Australia, Flora of Tasmania, Flora of Victoria (Australia), Monotypic plant genera

Apalochlamys is a genus of flowering plants in the daisy family. The sole species in the genus is Apalochlamys spectabilis, commonly known as showy cassinia or fireweed.[tpl]cite web |url=http://plantnet.rbgsyd.nsw.gov.au/cgi-bin/NSWfl.pl?page=nswfl&lvl=sp&name=Apalochlamys~spectabilis|title=Apalochlamys spectabilis (Labill.)
