[[Giant stargazer]]

CATEGORIES: Uranoscopidae, Endemic marine fish of New Zealand

The giant stargazer, Kathetostoma giganteum, is a stargazer  of the family Uranoscopidae, found on the continental shelf around New Zealand and endemic to that area.[tpl]cite web|title=Kathetostoma giganteum  Haast, 1873|url=http://www.fishbase.org/summary/Kathetostoma-giganteum.html|publisher=fishbase.org[/tpl] 
It is commonly called monkfish, but this should not be confused with the Northern Hemisphere monkfish which is an entirely different species in the order Lophiiformes.[tpl]cite web|title=New Zealand Monkfish|url=http://www.fooduniversity.com/foodu/seafood_c/resources/ocean%20fin/Cold%20Water/Monkfish/NewZealandMonk.htm[/tpl]
