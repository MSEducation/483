Johan Arneng (born 14 June 1979 in Uddevalla) is a Swedish football midfielder for IK Sirius.[tpl]cite web |url=http://guardian.touch-line.com/StatsCentre.asp?Lang=0&CTID=45&CPID=93&pStr=Player&PLID=275&TEID=5932 |title=Johan Arneng Facts |work=guardian.touch-line.com |accessdate=2010-01-23[/tpl]
Previously he has represented many clubs, including Svane IF, IFK Sunne, Degerfors IF, Empoli FC,[tpl]cite news |title=Johan Arneng till Djurgården |author=Berndt Rosqvist |newspaper=Dagens Nyheter |date=2003-02-11 |url=http://www.dn.se/sport/fotboll/johan-arneng-till-djurgarden-1.154961 |language=Swedish |accessdate=2010-01-23 [/tpl] Raufoss IL, Vålerenga, Aalesund, and Syrianska FC. He won the championship title and the Swedish Cup title with Djurgården in both 2003 and 2005,[tpl]cite book |last=Gänger |first=Hasse |title=Djurgårdens IF Fotboll 1899-2006 |origyear=2007 |edition= |page=79 |publisher=Djurgårdens IF Fotboll |location=Stockholm |language=Swedish |isbn=978-91-633-0992-2 [/tpl] and also the Norwegian Cup with Aalesund in 2009. He has been capped twice for the Sweden national football team.
