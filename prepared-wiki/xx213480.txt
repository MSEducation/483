[[Elitzur Kiryat Ata]]

CATEGORIES: Basketball teams in Israel, Former Israeli Basketball Super League teams

Elitzur Kiryat Ata ([tpl]lang-he|אליצור קריית אתא[/tpl]) is a basketball club based in Kiryat Ata in Israel. The club play in Ligat HaAl, the top division of Israeli basketball, having been promoted from Liga Leumit at the end of the 2007-08 season.
