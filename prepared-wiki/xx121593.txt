[[Spare wheel cover]]

CATEGORIES: Automotive accessories

A spare wheel cover or spare tire cover is an accessory that covers the rear mounted spare wheel on 4x4 vehicles. It is often printed with a dealer's name or something more fun, such as the infamous two rhinos on the Suzuki ATVs in the late 1980s made famous by Fred Cutler Manufacturing.[tpl]Citation needed|date=February 2007[/tpl]
In areas such as Australian Outback, they are an essential as they protect the tire rubber from the destructive properties of the sun's rays and harsh climate.[tpl]cn|date=February 2014[/tpl] Spare wheel covers need to be removed once in a while and cleaned.[tpl]cn|date=February 2014[/tpl]
