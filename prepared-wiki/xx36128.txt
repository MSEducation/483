[[Chet (month)]]

CATEGORIES: Months of the Nanakshahi calendar, Sikh terms

Chet ([tpl]lang-pa|ਚੇਤ[/tpl]) is a first month of the Nanakshahi calendar, which govern the activities within Sikhism. This month coincides with March and April in the Gregorian and Julian calendars and is 31 days long. Also during this month, the second son of Guru Gobind Singh, Sahibzada Jujhar Singh was born on 9 April 1691.
