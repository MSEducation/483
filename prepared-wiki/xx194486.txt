[[Calca District]]

Calca District is one of eight districts of the province Calca in Peru.[tpl]es icon[/tpl] Instituto Nacional de Estadística e Informática. Banco de Información Distrital. Retrieved April 11, 2008.
Some of the highest mountains of the Urupampa mountain range like Ch'iqun, Q'irayuq, Sawasiray and Siriwani lie in the northwest of the Calca District.
