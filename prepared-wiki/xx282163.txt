[[1990 UCI Road World Championships – Women's team time trial]]

CATEGORIES: 1990 UCI Road World Championships, UCI Road World Championships – Women's team time trial

The women's team time trial of the 1990 UCI Road World Championships cycling event took place on 29 August 1990 in Utsunomiya, Japan. The course was 49 km long and went from Utsunomiya to Nikkō and back to Utsunomiya.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.memoire-du-cyclisme.eu/feminines/mondial_clmpe_1990.php|work=memoire-du-cyclisme.eu|accessdate=10 August 2013|title=Championnat du Monde féminin CLM par équipes 1990[/tpl] [tpl]fr icon[/tpl]
