[[Nazlati Mohamed Andhumdine]]

CATEGORIES: 1997 births, Living people, Comorian swimmers, Freestyle swimmers

Nazlati Mohamed Andhumdine (born 20 December 1997)[tpl]cite web|title=Profile of Nazlati Mohamed Andhumdine|url=http://rs.arabgames2011.qa/ENG/ZB/ZBB101A_SW@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@ENG_number=21247.htm|publisher=Doha 2011|date=December 2011|accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl] is a Comorian swimmer specializing in freestyle.
She made her international debut at the 2011 Pan Arab Games, competing in the 50 m freestyle on her 13th birthday where unfortunately she was disqualified.[tpl]cite web|title=Results of Heat 1 of the 2011 Pan Arab Games - Women's 50 m freestyle|url=http://rs.arabgames2011.qa/ENG/SW/SWR173A_SWW01001010000001ENG.htm|publisher=Doha 2011|date=20 December 2011|accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl] She was due to compete at the 2012 FINA World Swimming Championships (25 m) in Istanbul in the 50 m[tpl]cite web|title=Heat results of the 2012 FINA World Swimming Championships (25 m) – Women's 50 metre freestyle|url=http://omegatiming.com/File/Download?id=00010C010D01001400FFFFFFFFFFFF02|publisher=Omega Timing|format=pdf|page=3|date=15 December 2012|accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl] and 100 m freestyle[tpl]cite web|title=Heat results of the 2012 FINA World Swimming Championships (25 m) – Women's 100 metre freestyle|url=http://omegatiming.com/File/Download?id=00010C010D01001500FFFFFFFFFFFF02|publisher=Omega Timing|format=pdf|page=3|date=13 December 2012|accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl] and the 50 m breaststroke[tpl]cite web|title=Heat results of the 2012 FINA World Swimming Championships (25 m) – Women's 50 metre breaststroke|url=http://omegatiming.com/File/Download?id=00010C010D01021D00FFFFFFFFFFFF02|publisher=Omega Timing|format=pdf|page=2|date=12 December 2012|accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl] but withdrew. At the 2013 World Aquatics Championships she swan the 50 m freestyle in 38.45 s finishing 82nd[tpl]cite web|title=Heat results of the 2013 World Aquatics Championships – Women's 50 metre freestyle|url=http://www.omegatiming.com/File/Download?id=00010D020101001400FFFFFFFFFFFF02|publisher=Omega Timing|format=pdf|page=3|date=3 August 2013|accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl] and placed 73rd in the 100 m freestyle covering the distance in 1:33.88 s.[tpl]cite web|title=Heat results of the 2013 World Aquatics Championships – Women's 100 metre freestyle|url=http://www.omegatiming.com/File/Download?id=00010D020101001500FFFFFFFFFFFF02|publisher=Omega Timing|format=pdf|page=3|date=1 August 2013|accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl]
