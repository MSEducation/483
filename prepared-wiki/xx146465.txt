[[BPM (band)]]

CATEGORIES: American jazz ensembles, Supergroups (music), Musical groups established in 2002, 2002 establishments in Alabama

BPM is a jazz-fusion band formed by Alex Machacek, Terry Bozzio and Gerald Preinfalk in 2002. They released the album, Delete and Roll, in 2002, which has received heavy critical acclaim.[tpl]Citation needed|date=June 2010[/tpl]
They are from Montgomery, Alabama.
