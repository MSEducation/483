[[Jaroš Griemiller]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing, Year of death missing, Alchemists, Czech writers, 16th-century writers

Jaroš Griemiller of Třebsko was a Czech alchemist, remembered for his illuminated manuscript Rosarium philosophorum.  He worked under Wilhelm von Rosenberg in the 1570s, and dedicated the Rosarium to him.  He completed work on the manuscript in 1578 while he was working in Český Krumlov.[tpl]cite book|author=Rudolf Werner Soukup|title=Chemie in Österreich: von den Anfängen bis zum Ende des 18 Jahrhunderts|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=MZq6r1G_NScC&pg=PA337|accessdate=13 September 2013|year=2007|publisher=Böhlau Verlag Wien|language=German|isbn=978-3-205-77567-6|pages=337–[/tpl]
