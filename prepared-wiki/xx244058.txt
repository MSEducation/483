[[Karamel Club]]

CATEGORIES: Theatres in Haringey, Arts centres in London

The Karamel Club is a small multi-purpose theatre/arts venue in Wood Green, London.
It is part of the Chocolate Factory art centre, and hosts amateur, student and emerging theatrical, musical and film and comedy performances, as well as being a receiving house.
It regularly features amateur and student performances of the Mountview Academy of Theatre Arts.
