==Demographics==

[tpl]As of|2001[/tpl] India census,[tpl]GR|India[/tpl] Ayyampettai had a population of 2,14,317. Males constitute 48% of the population and females 52%. Ayyampettai has an average literacy rate of 73%, higher than the national average of 59.5%; with 51% of the males and 49% of females literate. 11% of the population is under 6 years of age.
