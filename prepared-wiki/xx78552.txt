[[Kawaminami, Miyazaki]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Miyazaki Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Kawaminami|川南町|Kawaminami-chō[/tpl] is a town located in Koyu District, Miyazaki Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the town has an estimated population of 17,385 and the density of 192.59 persons per km². The total area is 90.27 km².
