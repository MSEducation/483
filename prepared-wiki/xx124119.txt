[[1975 Wimbledon Championships – Men's Singles]]

CATEGORIES: Wimbledon Championship by year – Gentlemen's Singles, 1975 Wimbledon Championships

Arthur Ashe defeated Jimmy Connors 6–1, 6–1, 5–7, 6–4 in the final to win the Gentlemen's Singles tennis title at the 1975 Wimbledon Championships.
