[[12318 Kästner]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Astronomical objects discovered in 1992

12318 Kästner is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1901.0514346 days (5.20 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=12318 |  title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-04-28 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on April 30, 1992. It is named after the German writer Erich Kästner.
