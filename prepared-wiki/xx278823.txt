[[Jazvan, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Jazvan ([tpl]lang-fa|جزوان[/tpl], also Romanized as Jazvān; also known as Jīzvān)[tpl]GEOnet3|10081954|Jazvan[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Pain Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 37, in 8 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
