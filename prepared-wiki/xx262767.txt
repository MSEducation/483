[[Camilo Miettinen]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Espoo Blues players, Finnish ice hockey players, Finnish people of Colombian descent, 1986 births, TPS players

Camilo Miettinen (born March 1, 1986) is a Colombian-born Finnish professional ice hockey forward who currently plays for HC TPS of the Finnish Liiga.
Miettinen made his SM-liiga debut playing with Espoo Blues during the 2006–07 season.Eliteprospects.com - 2006–07 TPS player statistics
