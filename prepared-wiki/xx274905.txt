[[Akarca, Mersin]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Mersin Province, Mediterranean Region, Turkey, Villages in Turkey, Populated places in Mezitli District

 
Akarca is a village in Mersin Province, Turkey. It is a part of Mezitli district which is an intracity district of Greater Mersin. It is situated in the Taurus Mountains. Its distance to Mersin is [tpl]convert|36|km[/tpl]. In the vicinity of the village is a chromium mining site.
