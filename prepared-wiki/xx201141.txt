[[WQXD-LP]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Alabama, Low-power FM radio stations in Alabama, Christian radio stations in the United States, Country radio stations in the United States, Limestone County, Alabama

WQXD-LP (97.9 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve Athens, Alabama.  The station is owned by Emmanuel Full Gospel Ministries. It airs a Christian Country music format.[tpl]cite web |work=Alabama Broadcast Media Page |title=Alabama LPFM & Translators |url=http://bellsouthpwp2.net/c/r/crackedlcd/almediapage/lpfm.html |accessdate=2008-05-21[/tpl][tpl]cite web |title=Station Information Profile |work=Arbitron |url=http://www.arbitron.com/[/tpl]
The station was assigned the WQXD-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on February 6, 2006.[tpl]cite web |title=Call Sign History |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=135547&Callsign=WQXD-LP |work=FCC Media Bureau CDBS Public Access Database[/tpl]
