[[Play Witcha Mama]]

CATEGORIES: 1994 albums, Willie D albums, G-funk albums

Play Witcha Mama is the third album by rapper Willie D. It was released in 1994 through Willie D's independent Wrap Records label. Ice Cube, appearing in the title track, is the album's only featured artist from outside of Willie D's protégés.http://www.discogs.com/Willie-D-Play-Witcha-Mama/release/395997
