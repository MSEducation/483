[[Quşqara]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Goygol District

Quşqara is a village and municipality in the Goygol Rayon of Azerbaijan.  It has a population of 1895.  The municipality consists of the villages of Quşqara, Bəhrəmbağ, and Səmədli.Belediyye Informasiya Sistemi [tpl]dead link|date=January 2013[/tpl]
