[[Cautionary Tales for the Brave]]

CATEGORIES: 2005 albums, Pure Reason Revolution albums

Cautionary Tales for the Brave is a 2005 mini-album by the British New prog band Pure Reason Revolution. Singer Chloe Alper described the album as "a kind of warm up to The Dark Third".Progressive Newsletter Most of the tracks appear on their debut album The Dark Third, with the exception of "In Aurelia", with Jon Courtney saying that they were initially written to be released on that album before Sony BMG decided to release a mini-album, prompting the band to write more songs.ProgSheet - Jon Courtney Interview
