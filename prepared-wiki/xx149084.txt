[[Markus Johannesson]]

CATEGORIES: 1975 births, Living people, Djurgårdens IF Fotboll players, Örgryte IS players, Swedish footballers, Sweden international footballers, Association football defenders, IK Oddevold players

Markus Johannesson (born May 29, 1975) is a retired Swedish football defender.
Johannesson started his career at Rölanda IF,[tpl]cite web |url=http://svt.se/2.21511/1.470496/djurgardens_guldtrupp |title=Djurgårdens guldtrupp |date=2005-10-17 |work=svt.se |publisher=Sveriges Television |language=Swedish |accessdate=2010-01-24[/tpl] but moved on to Eds FF[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.dif.se/sidor/nyheter/nyheter.asp?action=view&id=9390&typ=1 |title=16 Markus Johannesson |author=Jonas Riedel |date=2009-03-23 |work=dif.se |publisher=Djurgårdens IF Fotboll |language=Swedish |accessdate=2010-01-24[/tpl] and IK Oddevold before he was signed by Örgryte IS in 1997. In 2004, Johannesson left Örgryte IS for Djurgårdens IF.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.dif.se/sidor/nyheter/nyheter.asp?action=view&id=5218&typ=1 |title=Markus Johannesson klar för DIF |author=Jonas Riedel |date=2004-07-05 |work=dif.se |publisher=Djurgårdens IF Fotboll |language=Swedish |accessdate=2010-01-24[/tpl] He won The Double with Djurgården in 2005 and was also the captain of the team. He has been capped five times and scored one goal for the Sweden national football team. He was later on the 2009 season on the bench for Kebba Ceesay. But later on he took back his place but then as a defensive midfielder where he used to play with Örgryte IS. Johannesson scored his first league goal for Djurgården in his last game against Kalmar FF, helping Djurgården to reach relegation round against Assyriska FF.[tpl]cite news |title=Johannesson förde Dif till kval |author=Linus Schröder |newspaper=Svenska Dagbladet |date=2009-11-01 |url=http://www.svd.se/sportspel/nyheter/johannesson-forde-dif-till-kval_3738873.svd |language=Swedish |accessdate=2010-01-24[/tpl] Djurgården lost the first game away 0-2 but won at home in extra time, 3-0.
Johannesson retired from professional football after the 2009 season.[tpl]cite news |title=Djurgårdskaptenens perfekta slut |author=Lisa Edwinsson |newspaper=Dagens Nyheter |date=2009-11-08 |url=http://www.dn.se/sport/fotboll/djurgardskaptenens-perfekta-slut-1.990233 |language=Swedish |accessdate=2010-01-23 [/tpl]
