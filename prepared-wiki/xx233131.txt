[[Mohammed Al-Kharashy]]

CATEGORIES: Saudi Arabian football managers, 1998 FIFA World Cup managers, Living people, Saudi Arabia national football team managers

Mohammed Al-Kharashy is a football manager from Saudi Arabia.
