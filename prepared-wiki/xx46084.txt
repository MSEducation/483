[[Fosses-la-Ville]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Namur (province), Populated places in Belgium

Fosses-la-Ville ([tpl]lang-wa|Fosse-li-Veye[/tpl]) is a Walloon municipality located in the Belgian province of Namur. On January 1, 2006 Fosses-la-Ville had a total population of 9,311. The total area is 63.24 km² which gives a population density of 147 inhabitants per km².
