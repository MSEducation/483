[[Krasnoslobodsky District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Mordovia

Krasnoslobodsky District ([tpl]lang-ru|Краснослобо́дский райо́н[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-mdf|Ошень район[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-myv|Якстерекуро буе[/tpl]) is an administrativeConstitution of the Republic of Mordovia and municipalLaw #125-Z district (raion), one of the twenty-two in the Republic of Mordovia, Russia. It is located in the center of the republic. The area of the district is [tpl]convert|1379.4|km2|sp=us[/tpl].[tpl]citation needed|date=October 2012[/tpl] Its administrative center is the town of Krasnoslobodsk.Law #7-Z Population: [tpl]ru-census2010|26,406|;[/tpl] 28,703 (2002 Census);[tpl]ru-pop-ref|2002Census[/tpl] [tpl]su-census1989|33,669|.[/tpl] The population of Krasnoslobodsk accounts for 38.4% of the district's total population.
