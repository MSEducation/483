[[Platykampos]]

Platykampos ([tpl]lang-el|Πλατύκαμπος[/tpl]) is a village and a former municipality in the Larissa regional unit, Thessaly, Greece. Since the 2011 local government reform it is part of the municipality Kileler, of which it is a municipal unit.Kallikratis law Greece Ministry of Interior [tpl]el icon[/tpl] Population 8,292 (2001).
