[[Royal Alexandra Hospital, Edmonton]]

CATEGORIES: Hospitals in Edmonton, Hospitals established in 1899, Heliports in Canada, Certified airports in Alberta

The Royal Alexandra Hospital (RAH) is a large and long serving hospital in the Canadian province of Alberta. Operated by Alberta Health Services and located in the Edmonton's city centre, the Royal Alex serves a diverse community stretching from downtown Edmonton to western and northern Canada.Royal Alexandra Hospital
They operate 678 beds, and care for 450,000 patients annually. The RAH is home to the Women's Health Centre, the Regional Eye Centre and the Aboriginal Health program, in addition to a wide range of child, adult and geriatric programs and services.

==Etymology==

The Royal Alexandra Hospital is named after Queen Alexandra (1844-1925), consort of King Edward VII of the United Kingdom from 1901-1910.
