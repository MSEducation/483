[[Du Xi]]

CATEGORIES: Generals under Cao Cao, Officials under Cao Cao, Politicians from Pingdingshan, Liu Biao and associates, Cao Wei politicians, Cao Wei generals, Generals from Henan, Han dynasty politicians from Henan

Du Xi, courtesy name Zixu (子绪), was a military general serving under the warlord Cao Cao in the late Eastern Han Dynasty. He was a subordinate of Cao's general Xiahou Yuan. Du proposed to the troops that Zhang He take command after Xiahou Yuan was killed at the Battle of Mount Dingjun.
