[[Newfoundland, Kentucky]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Elliott County, Kentucky, Unincorporated communities in Kentucky

Newfoundland (also Crackers Neck) is an unincorporated community in Elliott County, Kentucky, United States.  It lies along Routes 7 and 32 north of the city of Sandy Hook, the county seat of Elliott County.Rand McNally.  The Road Atlas '06. Chicago: Rand McNally, 2006, p. 43.  Its elevation is 643 feet (196 m).[tpl]gnis|508699[/tpl], Geographic Names Information System, 1979-09-20.  Accessed 2008-01-03.
