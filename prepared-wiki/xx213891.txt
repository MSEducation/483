[[Przypisówka]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Lubartów County

Przypisówka [tpl]IPAc-pl|p|sz|y|p|i|'|s|u|f|k|a[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Firlej, within Lubartów County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) – TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|5|km|mi|0[/tpl] east of Firlej, [tpl]convert|10|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of Lubartów, and [tpl]convert|34|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of the regional capital Lublin.
The village has an approximate population of 530.
