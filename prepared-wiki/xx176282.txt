[[WGTN (AM)]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in South Carolina, News and talk radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 1949, 1949 establishments in South Carolina

WGTN (1400 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Talk radio format.Licensed to Georgetown, South Carolina, USA.  The station is currently owned by R.J. Stalvey.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=WGTN |title=WGTN Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl] It features programming from America's Radio News Network and CBS News. It airs Fox Sports Radio on weekends.
The previous format was oldies.
Programming includes Dennis Miller in afternoon drive time.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.myrtlebeachonline.com/2014/02/15/4030286/myrtle-beach-area-radio-stations.html|title=Myrtle Beach-area radio stations change up some shows|last=Palisin|first=Steve|work=The Sun News|date=February 15, 2014|accessdate=February 19, 2014[/tpl]
