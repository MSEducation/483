[[XMO]]

CATEGORIES: Gay pornographic television channels, Television channels in the Netherlands, LGBT-related media in the Netherlands, Television channels and stations established in 2008, Media in Utrecht (city), Gay (male) television channels

XMO is a Dutch language premium digital cable specialty channel from the Netherlands. XMO offers all gay male pornography films. XMO broadcasts 24 hours a day and on the UPC network daily between 23:00 and 11:00 hours. The remaining part of the schedule, 11:00 and 23:00, is shared with sister network OUTTV, offering general lifestyle and entertainment programming directed at gay male audiences.
