[[Thuès-Entre-Valls]]

CATEGORIES: Communes of Pyrénées-Orientales

Thuès-Entre-Valls ([tpl]lang-ca|Toès i Entrevalls[/tpl]) is a commune in the Pyrénées-Orientales department in southern France. Le Petit Train Jaune de la Cerdagne passes high over the Gorges de la Carança.
