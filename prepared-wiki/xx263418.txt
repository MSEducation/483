[[Makarye, Voronezh Oblast]]

CATEGORIES: Rural localities in Novousmansky District

Makarye ([tpl]lang-ru|Макарье[/tpl]) is a rural locality (a selo) in Novousmansky District of Voronezh Oblast, Russia,Law #87-OZ located on the Makariy River [tpl]convert|23.8|km|sp=us[/tpl] northeast from the district's administrative center of Novaya Usman and connected to Orlovo with a [tpl]convert|5|km|adj=on|sp=us[/tpl] road.
