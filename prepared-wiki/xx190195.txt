[[Academy of the Canyons]]

CATEGORIES: Educational institutions established in 2000, High schools in Los Angeles County, California, Public high schools in California, Education in Santa Clarita, California

Academy of the Canyons (AOC) is a public middle college high school in Santa Clarita, California, USA. The school, which enrolls students from the 9th to the 12th grade, is part of the William S. Hart High School District.  It is located at College of the Canyons campus where the students of AOC are concurrently enrolled. Those who are enrolled take high school classes on the College of the Canyons campus and they also take college career based courses at COC. During grades 9-12 students could earn a maximum of 74 college units, slightly more than enough to get an associates degree. A bonus is that high school students will also receive a College of the Canyons I.D. card and a 25 Score Card. 
The school's motto/slogan is "Your Future Today".  The symbol or mascot of the school is the Golden Oak Tree.
