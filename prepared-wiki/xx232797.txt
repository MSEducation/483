[[Grillenbanner]]

CATEGORIES: Waltzes by Johann Strauss II, 1861 compositions

Grillenbanner (Banisher of Gloom), opus 247, is a waltz composed by Johann Strauss II. Strauss himself conducted its premiere at a ball in the Sofienbad-Saal in February 1861.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.naxosdirect.com/title/8.223214 |accessdate=2008-10-08  |title=STRAUSS II, J.: Edition - Vol. 14 CD|publisher=NaxosDirect[/tpl] The work was dedicated to Prince Leopold of Saxe-Coburg-Gotha.
