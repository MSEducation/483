[[Bespoke Music]]

CATEGORIES: Jingle companies, Advertising, Audio branding, Media companies of the United Kingdom, Companies based in Cornwall

Bespoke Music is a music production company which specialise in radio imaging and branding. It is based in Penryn, Cornwall and run by composer and director Tim James.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.audiocourses.com/article914.html | title = Interview with Tim James about jingles | accessdate = 2010-08-01 [/tpl]
