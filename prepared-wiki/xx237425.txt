[[Cairns Tropical Zoo]]

CATEGORIES: Zoos in Australia, 1980 introductions, 1980 establishments in Australia, Parks in Queensland, Buildings and structures in Queensland, Cairns

Cairns Tropical Zoo is a [tpl]Convert|6|ha|sing=on[/tpl] tropical zoo in Cairns, Queensland, Australia. It has a small collection of native and introduced species, and is a member of the Zoo and Aquarium Association (ZAA).
