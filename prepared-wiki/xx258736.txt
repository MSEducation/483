[[Anthony J. Battaglia]]

Anthony Joseph Battaglia (born 1949) was a United States magistrate judge in California and is now a District Judge for the United States District Court for the Southern District of California.  On February 4, 2011, Battaglia, was for the second time unanimously approved by the Senate Judiciary Committee.http://www.thestatecolumn.com/state_politics/california/sen-barbara-boxer-senate-judiciary-committee-approves-judge-anthony-battaglia/, The State Column (February 4, 2011). On March 7, 2011 the Senate confirmed his nomination by a vote of 89 ayes to 0 nayshttp://judiciary.senate.gov/nominations/112thCongress.cfm and he received his commission on March 9, 2011.Biographical Directory of Federal Judges

==Early life and education==

Battaglia was born in 1949, in San Diego, California.Senate Judiciary Committee Questionnaire: Anthony Joseph Battaglia, (May 18, 2010).  He graduated from United States International University in 1971 with a Bachelor of Arts.President Obama Names Three to United States District Court, whitehouse.gov (May 20, 2010).  Battaglia then earned his Juris Doctor from California Western School of Law in 1974.Hon. Anthony J. Battaglia '74  Nominated to Seat on U.S. District Court, California Western School of Law News (May 21, 2010).

==Judicial service==

In 1993, Battaglia was selected to serve as a magistrate judge for the United States District Court for the Southern District of California.Greg Moran, Obama nominates federal judge in San Diego, The San Diego Union-Tribune (May 20, 2010).
On May 20, 2010, President Barack Obama nominated Battaglia for a district court judgeship on the Southern District of California. to fill the seat vacated by Judge James Lorenz. He was renominated at the beginning of the 112th Congress. On March 7, 2011 the Senate confirmed his nomination by a vote of 89 ayes to 0 nays and he received his commission on March 9, 2011.
