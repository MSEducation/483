[[Don Bandy]]

Donald Stewart Bandy (born July 1, 1945) is a former American football offensive lineman in the National Football League. He played professionally for the Washington Redskins.

==Early life==

Bandy was born in South Gate, California and played prep football at Lynwood High School in Lynwood, California.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.databasefootball.com/players/playerpage.htm?ilkid=BANDYDON01| title = Don Bandy| publisher= databaseFootball.com |accessdate= November 26, 2012[/tpl] He played college football at Compton Community College and the University of Tulsa.[tpl]cite web|url= http://www.profootballarchives.com/band00600.html| title=  Don Bandy|publisher=The Pro Football Archives |accessdate= November 26, 2012[/tpl]

==Professional career==

Bandy was drafted by the Washington Redskins in the sixth round of the 1967 NFL Draft. He played in the 1967 NFL season and the 1968 NFL season.

==Life after the NFL==

After leaving the NFL, Bandy served as head coach for Yucca Valley High School in Yucca Valley, California.[tpl]cite web|url= http://www.fanbase.com/Don-Bandy| title=  Don Bandy|publisher=Fan Base |accessdate= November 26, 2012[/tpl] He is the offensive line coach for Taft College in Taft, California,[tpl]cite web|url= http://www.taftcollege.edu/athletics/news/index.shtml| title=  Cougar Sports Center Renamed in Honor of a Legend|publisher=Taft College |accessdate= November 26, 2012[/tpl] and teaches history and geography at Taft.[tpl]cite web|url= http://www.fanbase.com/Don-Bandy| title=  Don Bandy|publisher=Fan Base |accessdate= November 26, 2012[/tpl]
