[[Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Tamale]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Ghana

The Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Tamale ([tpl]lang-la|Tamalen(sis)[/tpl]) is the Metropolitan See for the Ecclesiastical province of Tamale in Ghana.

==Special churches==

The seat of the archbishop is Our Lady of Annunciation Cathedral in Tamale.
