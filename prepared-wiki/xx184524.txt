[[Patrick Zurek]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1948 births, American people of Czech descent, American Roman Catholic bishops

Patrick James Zurek (born 17 August 1948) is the current Bishop of Amarillo since his installation on 22 February 2008. Zurek trained in Rome as a moral theologian who was a chaplain at the Bambino Gesu Children's Hospital during his seminary days. 
Zurek was ordained a priest in Rome on 29 June 1975 by Pope Paul VI along with 359 others for the Holy Year of 1975.

Zurek served 21 years as a priest of the Diocese of Austin, Texas, including 10 years as the original pastor of St. Thomas Aquinas Catholic Church in College Station.
