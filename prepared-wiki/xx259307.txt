[[Oleh Leshchynskyi]]

CATEGORIES: 1965 births, Living people, Soviet footballers, FC Desna Chernihiv players, FC Veres Rivne players, Association football midfielders, Sportspeople from Kiev, Ukrainian football managers, Ukrainian Premier League football managers, FC Sevastopol managers, FC Tytan Armyansk managers

Oleh Leschynskyi ([tpl]lang-uk|Олег Станіславович Лещинський[/tpl]; born 31 December 1965 in Kiev, Ukrainian SSR) is a former Soviet professional football midfielder and current coach. 
After a poor start to the 2012-13 Ukrainian First League season by Tytan Armyansk their coach Oleksandr Haydash was replaced by Leshchynskyi.[tpl]ru icon[/tpl] [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ua-football.com/ukrainian/first/5049efe5.html|title=Олег Лещинский назначен главным тренером "Титана"|trans_title=Oleh Leshchynskyi named new head coach of Tytan|publisher=ua-football.com|work=Tytan official webpage|language=Russian|accessdate=7 September 2012|date=7 September 2012[/tpl]
Since March 2014, Leschynskyi works as a manager of Russian side FC Tosno.[tpl]cite web|publisher=FC Tosno|url=http://fctosno.ru/news.php?readmore=136|title=Пополнение в тренерском штабе ФК "Тосно"|trans_title=Addition to FC Tosno coaching staff|date=4 March 2014|language=ru[/tpl]
