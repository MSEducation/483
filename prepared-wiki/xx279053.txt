[[Cherlanqush]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Cherlanqush ([tpl]lang-fa|چر لانقوش[/tpl], also Romanized as Cherlānqūsh; also known as Cherlān Qūch)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795422|Cherlanqush[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 200, in 44 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
