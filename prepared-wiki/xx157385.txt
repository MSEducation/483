[[José Carlos Moreno]]

CATEGORIES: Mexican voice actors, 1967 births, 2002 deaths, Barney & Friends

José Carlos Moreno Sánchez (1968 - México, May 2, 2002) was a Latin American voice actor. He was born in 1968 and killed in May 2, 2002 in a car accident.
Among other roles he was the voice of Barney in the Spanish dub of Barney & Friends. After he died, Ruben Cerda started doing Barney's voice, in place of Moreno.
