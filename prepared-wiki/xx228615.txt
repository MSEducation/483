[[1970–71 DFB-Pokal]]

CATEGORIES: DFB-Pokal seasons, 1970–71 in German football cups

The DFB-Pokal 1970–71 was the 28th season of the competition. It began on 12 December 1970 and ended on 19 June 1971. 32 teams competed in the tournament of five rounds. In the final Bayern Munich defeated FC Köln 2–1 after extra time.[tpl]cite web | title = DFB-Pokal 1970-71 | publisher = fussballdaten.de | accessdate = 2008-10-06 | year = 2008 | url = http://www.fussballdaten.de/dfb/1971/ | language = German| archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20080930214724/http://www.fussballdaten.de/dfb/1971/| archivedate= 30 September 2008 | deadurl= no[/tpl] It was Bayern's fifth triumph in the cup while it was Cologne's second consecutive loss in the final.

==Mode==

The tournament consisted of five single elimination rounds. In case a game ended with a draw 30 minutes of extra time were played. If the score was still level the game was replayed with 30 minutes of extra time in case of another draw. For the first time a penalty shootout was held if no winner could be determined after the replay.
