[[Dash Bolagh, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Dash Bolagh ([tpl]lang-fa|داش بلاغ[/tpl], also Romanized as Dāsh Bolāgh and Dāshbolāgh; also known as Dash Bulag, Dāshbūlāgh, and Dāshbulāq)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3059942|Dash Bolagh[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Pain Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 103, in 24 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
