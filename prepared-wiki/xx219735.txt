[[Asadli]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Bojnord County

Asadli ([tpl]lang-fa|اسدلي[/tpl], also Romanized as Asadlī)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3053807|Asadli[/tpl] is a village in Aladagh Rural District, in the Central District of Bojnord County, North Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 328, in 76 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|28[/tpl]
