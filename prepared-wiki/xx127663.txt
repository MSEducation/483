[[Armstrong House (Citra, Florida)]]

CATEGORIES: Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Florida, National Register of Historic Places in Marion County, Florida, Houses in Marion County, Florida

The Armstrong House (also known as the Melton-Shands House) is lisetd on the U.S. National Register of Historic Places (designated as such on June 9, 2000) located in Citra, Florida, at 18050 North US 301.
