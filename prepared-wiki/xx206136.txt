[[Dominggus Lim-Duan]]

CATEGORIES: 1983 births, Living people, Dutch footballers, Dutch people of Moluccan descent, PEC Zwolle players, SBV Excelsior players, FC Eindhoven players, People from Zwolle, Eerste Divisie players

Dominggus Lim-Duan (born 15 May 1983) is a Dutch football player who plays as a midfielder for Zwolle.

==Career==

Born in Zwolle, Lim-Duan began his career with his hometown club in 2001. After leaving in 2005, short spells at both Excelsior and Eindhoven followed, before Lim-Duan returned to Zwolle in 2008.
