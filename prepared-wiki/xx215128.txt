[[David Woobay]]

CATEGORIES: Mayors of places in Sierra Leone, Sierra Leone People's Party politicians, Living people, Mende people

David Woobay (born in Moyamba, Moyamba District) is a Sierra Leonean politician from the opposition Sierra Leone People's Party (SLPP), who is currently serving as mayor and Council Chairman Moymaba Town. He was re-elected as Mayor and council chairman of Moyamba with about 84% of the vote in the 2008 Sierra Leone local elections. David Woobay is a member of the Mende ethnic group.
