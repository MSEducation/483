[[Gun Shy, Trigger Happy]]

CATEGORIES: 1997 albums, Jen Trynin albums, Warner Bros. Records albums

Gun Shy, Trigger Happy is the second album by Boston, Massachusetts musician Jen Trynin.  It was released in 1997 on Warner Bros. Records. It was listed as Entertainment Weekly's album of the year for 1997 and topped many other critic's lists.

==Track listing==

All songs written by Jen Trynin.
