[[Ta-Tung]]

CATEGORIES: Food companies of Spain, Asian Food companies of Spain

Ta-Tung is a Catalan food company founded in 1980 dedicated to the production of precooked dishes inspired by oriental cuisine. Its headquarters are in the town of L'Hospitalet de Llobregat province of Barcelona (Spain).
The company, founded and directed by the Cambodian-born chef Ly Kav, started with a family structure to become one of the leading Spanish companies in the food sector of Asian food production, especially the rice and pasta base made.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.alimarket.es/noticia/120579//Tes|title=Comida étnica: La fiesta se traslada a casa|date=2013-04-23|author=|editor=Alimarket (España)|language=spanish[/tpl]
