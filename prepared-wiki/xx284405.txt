[[Ahmedabad Mumbai Central Double Decker Express]]

CATEGORIES: Rail transport in Gujarat, Double-decker trains of India, Railway services introduced in 2012, Named passenger trains of India

The 12931/12932  Ahmedabad Mumbai Central Double Decker Express  is a super fast express train belonging to Indian Railways that runs between Ahmedabad Junction and Mumbai Central in India. It is a 6 days a week service. It operates as train number 12932 from Ahmedabad Junction to Mumbai Central and as train number 12931 in the reverse direction.

==Coaches==

The Double Decker Express presently has 12 AC Chair Car & 2 EOG cars. As with most train services in India, Coach composition may be amended at the discretion of Indian Railways depending on demand.

==Service==

The Ahmedabad Mumbai Central Double Decker Express was first introduced on 19 September 2012. Presently it is a 6 days a week service (except Sunday).
It covers the distance of 491 kilometres in 7 hours as 12932 Ahmedabad Mumbai Central Double Decker Express (70.14 km/hr) & 7 hours 20 mins as 12931 Ahmedabad Mumbai Central Double Decker Express (66.95 km/hr).

==Traction==

As Western Railways switched over to AC system in February 2012, it is hauled by a WAP 5 engine from the Vadodara shed.

==Timetable==

12932 Ahmedabad Mumbai Central Double Decker Express leaves Ahmedabad Junction every day except Sunday at 06:00 hrs IST and reaches the Mumbai Central at 13:00 hrs IST. On return, the 12931 Mumbai Central Ahmedabad Double Decker Express leaves Mumbai Central every day except Sunday at 14:20 hrs IST and reaches Ahmedabad Junction at 21:40 hrs IST.
