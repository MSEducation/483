[[Marc Forné Molné]]

CATEGORIES: 1946 births, Living people, People from Andorra la Vella, Heads of Government of Andorra, Liberal Party of Andorra politicians

Marc Forné i Molné (born 30 December 1946) was the Prime Minister of Andorra[tpl]cite news|title='Centrality' of UN keynote theme on third day of General Assembly debate|url=http://www.un.org/apps/news/story.asp?newsid=8358&cr=&cr1=|accessdate=18 January 2011|newspaper=United Nations|date=25 September 2003[/tpl]  from 7 December 1994 to 27 May 2005. After 2 full terms, he was succeeded by Albert Pintat after he won the April 2005 election. He is a lawyer by profession, and was president of the Liberal Party of Andorra (Partit Liberal d'Andorra). 
The policy led by Marc Forné is inspired by liberalism and aims to maintain a low taxation level and avoid any statist drift.[tpl]cn|date=June 2012[/tpl]
