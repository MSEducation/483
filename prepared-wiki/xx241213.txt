[[Old Barag Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Old Barag Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠠᠭᠤᠴᠢᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠪᠠᠷᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Qaɣučin Barɣu qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=陈巴尔虎旗 |t=陳巴爾虎旗 |p=Chén Bā'ěrhǔ Qí[/tpl]) is a banner of northeastern Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China. It is located [tpl]convert|28|km[/tpl] from Hailar District, the administrative centre of Hulunbuir City, which administers this banner.
