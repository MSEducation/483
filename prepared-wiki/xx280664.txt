[[Badamestan, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Badamestan ([tpl]lang-fa|بادامستان[/tpl], also Romanized as Bādāmestān; also known as Badamistan)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3054383|Badamestan[/tpl] is a village in Bonab Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 97, in 21 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
