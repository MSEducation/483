[[Tzatziki]]

CATEGORIES: Appetizers, Dips (food), Sauces, Greek cuisine, Cypriot cuisine

Tzatziki (Anglicised: [tpl]IPAc-en|z|ɑː|d|ˈ|z|iː|k|i[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-el|τζατζίκι[/tpl] [tpl]IPA-el|dzaˈdzici|[/tpl] or [tpl]IPA-el|dʒaˈdʒici|[/tpl]) is a Greek sauce served with grilled meats. Tzatziki is made of strained yogurt (usually from sheep or goat milk) mixed with cucumbers, garlic, salt, olive oil, and sometimes lemon juice, and dill or mint or parsley.Classic Greek Tzatziki Sauce Tzatziki is always served cold.

==Etymology==

The name comes from the comparable Turkish dish cacık.
