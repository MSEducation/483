[[Catherine Corsini]]

CATEGORIES: 1956 births, Living people, French film directors, French screenwriters, People from Dreux, French women film directors, Women screenwriters

Catherine Corsini (born 18 May 1956) is a French film director and screenwriter. She has directed 16 films since 1982. Her film La répétition was entered into the 2001 Cannes Film Festival.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.festival-cannes.com/en/archives/ficheFilm/id/1100083/year/2001.html |title=Festival de Cannes: Replay |accessdate=2009-10-17|work=festival-cannes.com[/tpl] Her latest film Three Worlds competed in the Un Certain Regard section at the 2012 Cannes Film Festival.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.festival-cannes.fr/en/article/58878.html |title=2012 Official Selection |accessdate=2012-05-20 |work=Cannes[/tpl][tpl]cite web |url=http://www.boston.com/ae/movies/articles/2012/05/16/cannes_female_free_directors_line_up_criticized/ |title=Cannes' female-free directors line-up criticized |accessdate=2012-05-20 |work=Boston Globe[/tpl]
