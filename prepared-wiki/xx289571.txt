[[Serin, Iran]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Savadkuh County

Serin ([tpl]lang-fa|سرين[/tpl], also Romanized as Serīn)[tpl]GEOnet3|45742|Serin[/tpl] is a village in Valupey Rural District, in the Central District of Savadkuh County, Mazandaran Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 126, in 32 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|02[/tpl]
