CATEGORIES: 2004 manga, 2009 Japanese television series debuts, 2009 Japanese television series endings, Japanese television dramas based on manga, Manga adapted into films

[tpl]Nihongo|Saru Lock|猿ロック|Saru Rokku|trans. Monkey Lock[/tpl] is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Naoki Serizawa. It was adapted into a television drama in 2009 and a live action film in 2010.[tpl]Cite web |url=http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/news/2009-06-03/saru-lock-action-comedy-manga-to-get-tv-drama-film |title=Saru Lock Action Comedy Manga to Get TV Drama, Film|date=June 3, 2009|accessdate= September 23, 2011 |publisher= Anime News Network[/tpl]

==Plot==

Yataro Sarumaru, nickname "Saru" (Hayato Ichihara) is an average high school boy who daydreams about idols but otherwise has no luck with girls. While working with his father, a locksmith in Asakusa, Tokyo, he has gained exceptional skills to pick just about any lock. Using his exceptional skills, Yataro then finds himself solving various mysterious cases, while also trying his luck with the girls.
