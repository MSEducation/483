[[Bashir Raghe Shiiraar]]

CATEGORIES: 1965 births, Living people, Somalian businesspeople, Somali warlords

Bashir Rage Shiiraar (born 1965) is a Somali secular warlord and businessman who  hails from the Warsangali clan (an Abgaal subclan).http://www.globalsecurity.org/military/library/news/2006/03/mil-060322-irin04.htm
He became a member of the US-backed Alliance for the Restoration of Peace and Counter-Terrorism.
He was the head of the export department of the El-Ma An port which served as Mogadishu's port following the closure of the city's main harbour in 1995.http://www.iol.co.za/index.php?click_id=87&art_id=qw1085926140206B254&set_id=1
