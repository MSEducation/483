[[The Crucifix]]

CATEGORIES: 1934 films, British films, English-language films, Black-and-white films, Paramount Pictures films

The Crucifix is a 1934 black-and-white film directed by G.B. Samuelson.

==Plot==

An elderly woman hires a young aide to care for her and terrorizes her with her unreasonable demands.
