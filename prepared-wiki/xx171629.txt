[[John Andrews (footballer born 1950)]]

CATEGORIES: 1950 births, Living people, People from York, English footballers, Association football goalkeepers, York City F.C. players, The Football League players, English football referees, Footballers from North Yorkshire

John Edward Andrews (born 3 February 1950) is an English former footballer who played as a goalkeeper. He played for York City in the Football League and he later worked as a referee.

==Career==

Andrews was born in York, North Yorkshire and played for Moor Lane Youth Club before joining hometown club York City in August 1968 as an amateur.[ref]
