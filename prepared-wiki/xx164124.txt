[[Kiln Creek]]

CATEGORIES: Neighborhoods in Newport News, Virginia

Kiln Creek is a residential community located in Newport News and Yorktown, Virginia. It contains over 2,000 homes in 31 "villages", an elementary school, a golf course, and a recreational center.[tpl]cite web|title=Kiln Creek Owners Association|url=http://www.kilncreek.org/|publisher=Kiln Creek Home Owners Association|accessdate=17 September 2011[/tpl] The community has a board of directors, an architectural review board, and several other committees.[tpl]cite web|title=Board Members and Committees|url=http://www.kilncreek.org/management/bod.html|work=Kiln Creek Owners Association|publisher=Kiln Creek Home Owners Association|accessdate=17 September 2011[/tpl] 
The community's motto is "A Great Place To Be".[tpl]cite web|title=Kiln Creek Owners Association|url=http://www.kilncreek.org/|publisher=Kiln Creek Home Owners Association|accessdate=17 September 2011[/tpl]
