[[United States v. Schooner Peggy]]

CATEGORIES: United States Supreme Court cases, 1801 in United States case law

United States v. Schooner Peggy, [tpl]scite|5|103|1801[/tpl], was a United States Supreme Court case. It was one of a series of cases resolving disputes over ships captured during the undeclared war between the United States and France from 1798 to 1800.  The vessel was ordered returned to France.

==Background of the case==

From 1798 to 1800 the United States and France were engaged in many hostile Naval engagements.[ref]
