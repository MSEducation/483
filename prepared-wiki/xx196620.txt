[[United States House of Representatives elections in Pennsylvania, 2002]]

CATEGORIES: Pennsylvania elections, 2002, United States House of Representatives elections in Pennsylvania

The 2002 United States House elections in Pennsylvania was an election for Pennsylvania's delegation to the United States House of Representatives, which occurred as part of the general election of the House of Representatives on November 5, 2002.[tpl]cite web| title = Representative in Congress, 2002 General Election| work = Commonwealth of PA - Elections Information| publisher = Pennsylvania Department of State| year = 2004| url = http://www.electionreturns.state.pa.us/ElectionsInformation.aspx?FunctionID=13&ElectionID=7&OfficeID=11| accessdate = 2008-05-21[/tpl]
