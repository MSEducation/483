[[Sadri Khiari]]

CATEGORIES: 1958 births, Tunisian activists, Living people, People from Tunis

Sadri Khiari ([tpl]lang-ar|صدري الخياري[/tpl]), was born on 26 February 1958 in Tunis, and is a Tunisian activist. He was an exile in France from 2003. He is active in Indigènes de la République (Movement of the Indigenous of the Republic (MIR)Monthly Review accessed 23 September 2011  and has written texts with the MIR's spokesperson Houria Bouteldja.http://www.decolonialtranslation.com/english/
