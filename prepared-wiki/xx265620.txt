[[Ryoo Chang-kil]]

CATEGORIES: 1940 births, North Korean footballers, North Korea international footballers, Association football defenders, 1966 FIFA World Cup players, Living people

Ryoo Chang-Kil (born 5 November 1940) is a North Korean football defender who played for North Korea in the 1966 FIFA World Cup.Copa do Mundo da FIFA Inglaterra 1966 He also played for Kikwancha Pyongyang.
