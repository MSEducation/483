[[Gramenca]]

CATEGORIES: Salticidae

Gramenca is a genus of the spider family Salticidae (jumping spiders). Its single described species is found in Guinea.

==Name==

The genus name is derived from Latin gramen "grass", which is the habitat of G. prima. prima means first, for this is the first described Gramenca species.

==Appearance==

The female spider is about 3mm long. The male is not yet known.
