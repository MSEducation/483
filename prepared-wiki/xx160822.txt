Peter Valentine (born 16 April 1963 in Huddersfield) is an English former professional footballer who made nearly 500 appearances in the Football League playing as a defender for Huddersfield Town, Bolton Wanderers, Bury, Carlisle United and Rochdale.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.soccerbase.com/players_details.sd?playerid=8106 |title=Peter Valentine |work=Soccerbase |publisher=Centurycomm |accessdate=16 November 2009[/tpl]
He has a son called Liam, who was born on 19 August 1994.
