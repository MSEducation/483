[[Hiji, Ōita]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Ōita Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Hiji|日出町|Hiji-machi[/tpl] is a town located in Hayami District, Ōita Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the town has an estimated population of 26,872 and the density of 366.95 persons per km². The total area is 73.23 km².
The town has a trappist monastery.
It is also the home of Sanrio's Harmonyland theme park.
