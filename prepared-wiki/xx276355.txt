[[Juan Imhoff]]

CATEGORIES: 1988 births, Living people, Argentine rugby union players, Rugby union wings, Argentina international rugby union players, Racing Métro 92 players, Pampas XV players

Juan José Imhoff (born 11 May 1988) is an Argentine rugby union footballer who plays as a wing and fullback. He was part of the Pampas XV at the 2011 Vodacom Cup in South Africa and was also part of the Argentine squad at the 2011 Rugby World Cup.
