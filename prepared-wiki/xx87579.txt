[[Berwick railway station, Melbourne]]

CATEGORIES: Premium Melbourne railway stations, Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1877

Berwick railway station is located on the Pakenham line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the south-eastern Melbourne suburb of Berwick opening on 8 October 1887.Berwick Vicsig It was upgraded to a Premium station in 1998.[tpl]cite journal|date=October 1997|title=Upgrading Eltham to a Premium Station|journal=Newsrail|publisher=Australian Railway Historical Society|pages=page 310[/tpl]

==Platforms & services==
