[[Tenpyō-jingo]]

CATEGORIES: Japanese eras

[tpl]nihongo|Tenpyō-jingo|天平神護[/tpl] was a [tpl]nihongo|Japanese era name|年号,|nengō,|lit. "year name"[/tpl] after Tenpyō-hōji and before Jingo-keiun.  This period spanned the years from January 765 through August 767.Nussbaum, Louis-Frédéric. (2005). "Tenpyō-jingo" in [tpl]Google books|p2QnPijAEmEC|Japan Encyclopedia, p. 957|page=957[/tpl]; n.b., Louis-Frédéric is pseudonym of Louis-Frédéric Nussbaum, see Deutsche Nationalbibliothek Authority File.   The reigning empress was [tpl]nihongo|Empress Shōtoku|称徳天皇[/tpl].  This was the same woman who had reigned previously as [tpl]nihongo|Empress Kōken|孝謙天皇[/tpl].Titsingh, Isaac. (1834). Annales des empereurs du Japon, pp. 78-81; Brown, Delmer et al. (1979). Gukanshō, pp. 274-276; Varley, H. Paul. (1980). Jinnō Shōtōki. p. 143-147.
