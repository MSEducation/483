[[Telecommunications in Malawi]]

CATEGORIES: Communications in Malawi, Telecommunications in Malawi

Telecommunications in Malawi includes the country's telephone, television, radio and internet services.

==Telephone==

Telephones - main lines in use: 160,100 (as of 2010) up from 85,000 ([tpl]as of|2003[/tpl])
Telephones - mobile cellular: 3 million (as of 2010) up from 1.781 Million (as of 2008) up from 135,100 ([tpl]as of|2003[/tpl])
Telephone system:
domestic: fair system of open-wire lines, microwave radio relay links, and radiotelephone communications stations
international: satellite earth stations - 2 Intelsat (1 Indian Ocean and 1 Atlantic Ocean)

==Radio==

Radio broadcast stations: AM 9, FM 5 (plus 15 repeater stations), shortwave 2 (plus a third station held in standby status)) ([tpl]as of|2001[/tpl])
Radios: 2.6 million ([tpl]as of|1997[/tpl])
