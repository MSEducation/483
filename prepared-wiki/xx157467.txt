[[Transparent in Your Presence]]

CATEGORIES: Byron Cage albums, 1996 live albums

Transparent in Your Presence is the second and final gospel CD recorded by Byron Cage & Purpose released in the summer of 1996. Recorded at New Birth Missionary Bapstist Church in Atlanta, GA(Bishop Eddie L. Long, Pastor).  This album peaked at #2 on the U.S. Gospel charts.
