[[Kong Fei]]

CATEGORIES: 1911 births, 1993 deaths, Members of the National People's Congress, People's Republic of China politicians from Inner Mongolia, People from Tongliao, Chinese people of Mongolian descent, People's Liberation Army generals from Inner Mongolia, Political office-holders in Inner Mongolia, Communist Party of China politicians from Inner Mongolia

Kong Fei (Chinese: 孔飞; November 1911–January 23, 1993) was the Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region in China from 1978 to 1982.
