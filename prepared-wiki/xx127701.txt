[[Guatire]]

CATEGORIES: Cities in Miranda (state)

 
Guatire is a city in Miranda, Venezuela.  In 2006, its population has been estimated at 200,417.  Today, Guatire has virtually merged with its neighbor, Guarenas.
Located in Miranda State, and becoming part of "La gran Caracas" (Greater Caracas) because it is near Caracas (Venezuela's Capital).
