[[Irfan Bakti Abu Salim]]

CATEGORIES: Malaysian footballers, Malaysia international footballers, Malaysian Malay people, Malaysian football managers, Perlis FA managers, Living people

Irfan Bakti Abu Salim is a Malaysian football coach. A former football player, he was in the Malaysia squad for the 1976 AFC Asian Cup in Tehran, Iran.http://newspapers.nl.sg/Digitised/Article/beritaharian19760602-1.2.50.8.aspx
He spent most of his early coaching days as a coach with the Football Association of Malaysia in Wisma FAM. He first worked as a state coach with the Negri Sembilan team, and later joined Penang FA and TMFC respectively. In 2007 he suddenly resigned from TMFC to coach an Indonesian team, Persipura Jayapura. He returned to Malaysian football in 2008, joining Perlis FA. He later left Perlis and joined Terengganu FA for the 2010/11 season.[tpl]cite web|url=http://besonline.rtm.gov.my/details.php?id=2409&pageno=25&field=sp_Title&order=ASC&title=Sports|title=Irfan Bakti to coach Terengganu next season|date=20 November 2009|publisher=besonline|accessdate=24 October 2010[/tpl] [tpl]cite web|url=http://findarticles.com/p/news-articles/bernama-malaysian-national-news-agency/mi_8082/is_20091125/irfan-bakti-hopes-terengganu/ai_n51641230/|title=Irfan Bakti Has High Hopes For Terengganu|date=November 25, 2009|publisher=BNet Source: Bernama; Malaysian National News Agency|accessdate=24 October 2010[/tpl]
