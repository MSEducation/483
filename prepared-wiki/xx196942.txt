[[George R. Minot House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses completed in 1929, National Historic Landmarks in Massachusetts, Houses in Brookline, Massachusetts, Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Massachusetts

The George R. Minot House is a National Historic Landmark at 71 Sears Road in Brookline, Massachusetts.
The Colonial Revival house was built in 1929 and was the home of George R. Minot, a Nobel Prize winner in Medicine and Physiology who discovered a cure for pernicious anemia.  The house was added to the National Historic Register in 1976.NPS NHL Listing info
