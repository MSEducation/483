[[First Church of Jamaica Plain]]

CATEGORIES: Churches in Boston, Massachusetts, Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in Massachusetts, Stone churches in Massachusetts

First Church of Jamaica Plain is a historic church at 6 Eliot Street in Jamaica Plain, Massachusetts, a neighborhood of Boston.
The church was designed in 1854 by the well known Boston architect, Nathaniel J. Bradlee, and added to the National Historic Register in 1988.
