[[Iwaya Dam]]

CATEGORIES: Dams in Gifu Prefecture, Dams completed in 1976

The [tpl]nihongo|Iwaya Dam|岩屋ダム|Iwaya Damu[/tpl] is a dam in Gifu Prefecture, Japan. It supports a 288 MW hydroelectric power station.[tpl]cite web|title=Hydropower & Dams in South and East Asia|url=http://www.hydropower-dams.com/d/d_S&East_Asia_Map.pdf|publisher=Hydropower and Dams|accessdate=3 October 2010|page=9[/tpl]
