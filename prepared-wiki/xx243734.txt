==Biography==

He was born on May 24, 1884 in Bayonne, New Jersey.Alton Milford Young in the World War I draft registration of 1918 By 1918 he was the minister for the North Baptist Church in Jersey City, New Jersey. He received his Doctor of Divinity degree from Alma White College in 1927.[tpl]cite news |first= |last= |authorlink= |coauthors= |title=Zarepath Colony Institution in New Jersey Confers High Honors for First Time. |url=http://select.nytimes.com/gst/abstract.html?res=F40A1FFB385B157A93CBA8178DD85F438285F9&scp=1&sq=alma%20white%20college%20king%20kleagle%20young&st=cse |quote= For the first time in its history Alma College, at Zarepath, near Bound Brook, N.J., conferred the degree of Doctor of Divinity during its commencement exercises, which took place last week. ... Dr. A. M. Young, recipient of a degree at the  commencement, was at one time King Kleagle of the New Jersey Klan. |work=New York Times |date=June 19, 1927 |accessdate=2009-07-06 [/tpl]  In 1940 he testified before the Dies Committee.[tpl]cite book |last= |first= |authorlink= |coauthors= |title=Investigation of Un-American Propaganda Activities in the United States |year=1944 |publisher=United States Government Printing Office |quote=I am former grand kaliff of the State, which is vice Grand Dragon. ... I was made grand kaliff the day Mr. Bell was made grand dragon and I don't quite ... | url=http://books.google.com/books?id=uzQWAAAAIAAJ&q=%22Grand+Kaliff%22&dq=%22Grand+Kaliff%22&ei=DT7eSq6wI42UyATz0N2OBw&client=firefox-a |isbn= [/tpl] Young and Arthur H. Bell were ousted from the Klan following the joint meeting on 18 August 1940 of the Klan and the German-American Bund at the Bund's Camp Nordlund, near Andover, New Jersey.
In May 1948, Young converted to Roman Catholicism and began using his baptismal name, Joseph. He died July 16, 1950, at his home in Jersey City, New Jersey, 121 Garrison Avenue, "of a heart ailment."
