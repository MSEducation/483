[[Bin Laden (disambiguation)]]

CATEGORIES: Arabic-language surnames

bin Laden ([tpl]lang-ar|بن لادن[/tpl]) is an Arabic language surname synonymous with Osama bin Laden (1957–2011), it may also pertain to the Saudi Binladin Group, a holding company for the assets of the bin Laden family, and other notable members of Osama's family.
