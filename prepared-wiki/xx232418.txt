[[Luliang County]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Qujing City

Luliang County ([tpl]zh| s=陆良县| t=陸良縣| p=Lùliáng Xiàn[/tpl]) is a county under the administration of the prefecture-level city of Qujing, in Yunnan province of southwestern China.

==Flora and fauna==

The type locality of Sinocyclocheilus lateristriatus, a cyprinid fish endemic to China, is in Luliang County.[tpl]cite web |url=http://researcharchive.calacademy.org/research/Ichthyology/catalog/fishcatmain.asp |title=Catalog of Fishes |author=Eschmeyer, W. N. |date=5 August 2013 |publisher=California Academy of Sciences |accessdate=6 September 2013[/tpl] Similarly, Schuchertella luliangensis, a Permian brachiopod, has been described from the Kuangshan Formation in Luliang County (and named after the county).[tpl]cite web |url=http://paleodb.org/?a=basicTaxonInfo&taxon_no=245130 |title=†Schuchertella luliangensis Jin and Fang 1985 (lamp shell) |author= |year= |work=Paleobiology Database |publisher= |accessdate=5 September 2013[/tpl]
