[[Mukai-Noshiro Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Akita Prefecture

[tpl]Nihongo|Mukai-Noshiro Station|向能代駅|Mukai-Noshiro-eki[/tpl] is a railway station on the Gonō Line in Noshiro, Akita, Japan, operated by the East Japan Railway Company (JR East).[tpl]cite web |url= http://www.jreast.co.jp/estation/station/info.aspx?StationCd=1522|title= 各駅情報（向能代駅）|trans_title=Station Information (Mukai-Noshiro Station) |publisher= East Japan Railway Company|location= Japan|language= Japanese|archiveurl= |archivedate= |accessdate= 6 February 2012[/tpl]

==Lines==

Mukai-Noshiro Station is served by the Gonō Line from [tpl]Ja-stalink|Higashi-Noshiro[/tpl] and [tpl]Ja-stalink|Kawabe||Aomori[/tpl], and is located 6.1 km from Higashi-Noshiro.

==Station layout==

The station consists of one side platform serving a single bidirectional line.

==History==

The station opened on 25 January 1952.
