[[Roberto Lago]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 births, Living people, Sportspeople from Vigo, Spanish footballers, Galician footballers, Association football defenders, La Liga footballers, Segunda División footballers, Segunda División B footballers, Celta de Vigo footballers, Getafe CF footballers

Roberto Lago Soto (born 30 August 1985 in Vigo, Galicia) is a Spanish professional footballer who plays for Getafe CF as a left defender.

==Football career==

===Celta===

A product of hometown side Celta de Vigo's youth system, Lago made his debuts with the first team in the 2007–08 season, playing 33 matches for the Segunda División side which eventually finished 17th one year after being relegated. In the following years he was an undisputed starter when available, contributing with 35 games and one goal[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.marca.com/2012/01/13/futbol/2adivision/1326491737.html|title=El Celta se hace fuerte ante un Nàstic sin gol|trans_title=Celta gains strength against goalless Nàstic|publisher=Marca|language=Spanish|date=13 January 2012|accessdate=9 January 2014[/tpl] in the 2011–12 campaign as the Galicians returned to La Liga.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.lavozdegalicia.es/noticia/deportes/2012/02/15/roberto-lago-cree-celta-debe-aspirar-campeon-segunda/00031329329288395633570.htm|title=Roberto Lago cree que el Celta debe aspirar a ser campeón en Segunda|trans_title=Roberto Lago thinks Celta must aim to Segunda championship|publisher=La Voz de Galicia|language=Spanish|date=15 February 2012|accessdate=9 January 2014[/tpl]
Lago's maiden appearance in the top flight was on 18 August 2012, starting in a 0–1 home loss against Málaga CF.[tpl]cite web|url=http://soccernet.espn.go.com/report/_/id/348500?cc=5739|title=Olinga makes history|publisher=ESPN FC|date=18 August 2012|accessdate=9 January 2014[/tpl] He only missed four contests during the season, as Celta eventually stayed up at the expense of neighbouring Deportivo de La Coruña.

===Getafe===

Lago joined fellow top-divisioner Getafe CF on 12 June 2013, signing a four-year contract with the Madrid oustkirts club.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.goal.com/es/news/21/fichajes/2013/06/12/4043692/el-getafe-ficha-a-roberto-lago|title=El Getafe ficha a Roberto Lago|trans_title=Getafe signs Roberto Lago|publisher=Goal.com|language=Spanish|date=12 June 2013|accessdate=9 January 2014[/tpl]
