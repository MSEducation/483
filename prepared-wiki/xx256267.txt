Braian Damián Rodríguez Carballo (born 14 August 1986) is a Uruguayan footballer who plays for Real Betis as a striker.

==Club career==

Born in Salto, Rodríguez made his senior debuts with local Cerro in Primera División, but later moved to Rentistas. In the 2007 summer he joined neighbouring Tacuarembó, and a year later he signed with local giants Peñarol.Braian Rodríguez pasó a Peñarol (Braian Rodríguez gone to Peñarol); Ovación, 3 February 2009 [tpl]es icon[/tpl]
In January 2010 Rodríguez moved abroad for the first time of his career, joining Argentine Primera División side Tigre;Braian Rodríguez ya es de Tigre (Braian Rodríguez is already a Tigre player); Ovación, 9 January 2010 [tpl]es icon[/tpl] six months later he moved to Peru, signing with Universidad San Martín.Braian Rodríguez, el jale de la San Martín (Braian Rodríguez, San Martín's lift); Perú 21, 15 July 2010 [tpl]es icon[/tpl] However, after being sparingly used in the campaign, he moved teams and countries again, joining Unión La Calera.
In January 2012 Rodríguez joined Huachipato, and netted 17 goals in 37 appearances in his first season for the club, being also crowned champions of Clausura tournament.
On 25 July 2013 Rodríguez joined La Liga side Real Betis, penning a four-year deal.Braian Rodríguez y Dídac, nuevos jugadores del Betis (Braian Rodríguez and Dídac, new Betis players); Marca, 25 July 2013 [tpl]es icon[/tpl]
