[[Everything but the Girl discography]]

CATEGORIES: Discographies of British artists, Pop music group discographies, Rock music group discographies, Electronic music discographies

The discography of Everything but the Girl (EBTG), consists of eleven studio albums, seven compilation albums, four extended plays, thirtyone singles, and one video album. The band formed in Hull during 1982, consisting of lead singer and occasional guitarist Tracey Thorn and guitarist, keyboardist, and singer Ben Watt.[tpl]cite web |first= |last= |url=[tpl]Allmusic|class=artist|id=p4209/biography|pure_url=yes[/tpl] |title=Biography by Jason Ankeny |publisher=Allmusic.com |accessdate=17 July 2009[/tpl]
