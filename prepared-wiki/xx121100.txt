[[Tadashi Sasaki (musician)]]

CATEGORIES: Japanese classical guitarists, Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, Cologne University of Music alumni

Tadashi Sasaki is a classical guitarist born in Tokyo, Japan. Today he lives in Germany, where he is a professor at "Hochschule für Musik Köln - Standort Aachen" in Aachen.
In 1968, Tadashi Sasaki was the first Japanese to win the international guitar competition in Paris. He served as a juror in the Heinsberg International Guitar Festival and Competition in 2005.
