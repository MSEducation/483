[[Dukedoms of Spain]]

CATEGORIES: Dukedoms of Spain, Dukes of Spain, Lists of Spanish nobility

This is a list of dukedoms in Spain. 
All hold the court rank of grandee, i.e. grandee of the realm, which has precedence over all other feudatories.[tpl]Citation needed|date=November 2009[/tpl] 
Very often a single inheritance includes a whole list of ducal and other titles.
