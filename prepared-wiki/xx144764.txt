[[Lycaenopsis minima]]

CATEGORIES: Lycaenopsis, Butterflies of India

The Tiny Hedge Blue  Lycaenopsis minima (Evans, 1932).Evans,W.H.(1932) The Identification of Indian Butterflies, ser no H21.8, pp 221-226Card for  minima in LepIndex. Accessed 14 October 2006. is a small butterfly found in India that belongs to the Lycaenids or Blues family.

==Range==

It is found from Manipur in India to the Dawna Hills of Myanmar.
