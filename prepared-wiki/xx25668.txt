[[Car Wars]]

CATEGORIES: Board games introduced in 1980, Charles S. Roberts Award winners, Origins Award winners, Science fiction board games, Steve Jackson Games games

Car Wars is a vehicle combat simulation game developed by Steve Jackson Games. It was first published in 1980.http://www.sjgames.com/ourgames/board.html Players control armed vehicles in a post-apocalyptic future.

==Game play==

In Car Wars, players assume control of one or more cars or other powered vehicle, from motorcycles to semi trucks.[ref]
