[[Kamitakai District, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Districts in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Kamitakai|上高井郡|Kamitakai-gun[/tpl] is a district located in Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the district has an estimated population of 19,073 and a density of 162.23 persons per km². The total area is 117.57 km².
