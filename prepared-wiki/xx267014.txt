[[Zhang Meng]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Film actresses from Henan, Television actresses from Henan, People from Zhengzhou, Chinese stage actresses, 1988 births

Zhang Meng (born 28 December 1988) is a Chinese actress from Zhengzhou, Henan. She is the director of the Actors Working Committee of the China Television Artists Association.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ctaa.org.cn/ |title=中国电视艺术家协会 |publisher=Ctaa.org.cn |date= |accessdate=2011-10-08[/tpl]
Her role as Biyao, the female lead character in the online video game Jade Dynasty, was her breakthrough.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.yuncheng.com/chapters.aspx?rpid=10&bookid=2019&rcid=11015 |title=诛仙最新章节_免费阅读_作者萧鼎_云中书城 |publisher=Yuncheng.com |date=2011-08-04 |accessdate=2011-10-08[/tpl]
Taking "My Sassy Girl" as an example, she needed to change her soft image and played into a strong woman, and started a new cooperation with metrosexual man Peng Yuyan.[tpl]cite web|url=http://data.yule.sohu.com/star/data/21/21977/ |title=彭于晏_明星资料库_搜狐 |publisher=Data.yule.sohu.com |date= |accessdate=2011-10-08[/tpl]
