[[Menno Simons College]]

CATEGORIES: Universities and colleges in Manitoba, Universities and colleges affiliated with the Mennonite Church, Education in Winnipeg, Mennonitism in Canada

Menno Simons College is a Mennonite college in Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada. It is a college of Canadian Mennonite University (CMU) and is one of CMU's three founding colleges. Menno Simons College, located in downtown Winnipeg, is affiliated with the University of Winnipeg. 

==History==

thumb|left|Menno Simons The college is named after Menno Simons, anabaptist leader and founder of the Mennonites. It received a provincial charter to grant degrees in 1982.[tpl]cite web |url=http://io.uwinnipeg.ca/~msc/about_msc_03.html |title=History and Roots |accessdate=2008-08-16[/tpl]

Through The University of Winnipeg, MSC offers 3- and 4-year programs in International Development Studies and Conflict Resolution Studies.[tpl]cite web |url=http://io.uwinnipeg.ca/~msc/acad_01.htm |title=Academics |accessdate=2008-08-16[/tpl]
