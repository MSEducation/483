[[Wait for Me (Kings of Leon song)]]

CATEGORIES: Kings of Leon songs, 2013 singles, 2013 songs, RCA Records singles

"Wait for Me" is a song by American rock band Kings of Leon. The song was released in the United Kingdom in August 2013 as the second single from their sixth studio album Mechanical Bull (2013). On Sunday 11 August, it debuted at number 31 in the UK Singles Chart, as announced on BBC Radio 1's Chart Show.
