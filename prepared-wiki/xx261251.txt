[[Great Henny]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Essex, Braintree (district), Civil parishes in Essex

Great Henny is a village and a civil parish near Sudbury, in the Braintree district, in the county of Essex, England. Other nearby villages include Little Henny, Twinstead and Henny Street.

==St Mary, Great Henny==

St Mary was originally built by the Normans. Most of the rest of the church is 14th century.St. Mary's church, Great Henny, Essex:: OS grid TL8637 :: Geograph Britain and Ireland
