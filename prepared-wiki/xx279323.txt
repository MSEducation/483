[[Sarcham-e Sofla]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Sarcham-e Sofla ([tpl]lang-fa|سرچم سفلي[/tpl], also Romanized as Sarcham-e Soflá; also known as Sar Cham, Sarcham-e Pā’īn, and Sarcham Pa’īn)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3082605|Sarcham-e Sofla[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 248, in 49 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
