[[KTXD-LP]]

CATEGORIES: Television stations in Texas, Television channels and stations disestablished in 2011, Defunct terrestrial television stations in the United States, Television channels and stations established in 2009

KTXD-LP was a low power affiliate of Azteca America in the Amarillo, Texas DMA, broadcast on UHF channel 43. It was owned by Una Vez Mas Holdings, LLC. 
Around late 2009, KTXD-LP ceased operations due to technical difficulties. A silent STA was requested until the licensee can resolve the problems and resume operation. However, the station never resumed operation, and its license was cancelled by the FCC on May 28, 2011.FCC Broadcast Actions, April 1, 2011.
