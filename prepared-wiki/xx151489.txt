[[Vespo]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Bonaire, Football clubs in the Netherlands Antilles, 1959 establishments in Bonaire

S.V. Vespo is a football team, from the town of Rincon on Bonaire in the Caribbean Netherlands, playing at the top level. Vespo was founded on 9 April 1959 and plays in the Bonaire League.RSSSF
