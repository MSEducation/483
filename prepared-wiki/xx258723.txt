[[Nevis independence referendum, 1998]]

CATEGORIES: 1998 referendums, 1998 in Saint Kitts and Nevis, Referendums in Saint Kitts and Nevis, Secession in Saint Kitts and Nevis, Independence referendums

An independence referendum was held in Nevis on 10 August 1998. Although it was approved by 62% of voters, a two-thirds majority was necessary for the referendum to succeed. If it had passed, Nevis would have seceded from the Federation of Saint Kitts and Nevis.1998 Referendum Political Database of the AmericaElection Profile IFES
