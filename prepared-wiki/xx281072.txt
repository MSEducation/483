[[Soulasen Phommasen]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1992 births, Laotian swimmers

Soulasen Phommasen (born 1992) is a Laotian swimmer.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bcn2013.com/en/swimming/detail/member/soulasen-phommasen |title=Swimming - Member detail |publisher=Bcn2013.com |date= |accessdate=2013-08-10[/tpl] He competed in the 50 m freestyle event at the 2010 FINA World Swimming Championships (25 m) and in the 50 m and 100 m freestyle events at the 2013 World Aquatics Championships.
