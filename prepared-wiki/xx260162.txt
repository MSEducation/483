[[Jasmin Šćuk]]

CATEGORIES: 1990 births, Living people, Bosnia and Herzegovina footballers, Bosnia and Herzegovina expatriate footballers, Expatriate footballers in the Czech Republic, Gambrinus liga players, FK Mladá Boleslav players

Jasmin Šćuk (born 14 July 1990 in Jablanica) is a Bosnian football midfielder. He currently plays for FK Mlada Boleslav in the Czech Gambrinus Liga.http://sportsport.ba/ino_fudbalp-49272.htmhttp://sportsport.ba/ino_fudbal/scuk-debitovao-za-mlada-boleslav/56502http://sportin.ba/nogomet/ostalo/1632-jasmin-scuk-reprezentacija-je-moj-san
