[[Solygeia]]

Solygeia ([tpl]lang-el|Σολυγεία[/tpl]) is a former municipality in Corinthia, Peloponnese, Greece. Since the 2011 local government reform it is part of the municipality Corinth, of which it is a municipal unit.Kallikratis law Greece Ministry of Interior [tpl]el icon[/tpl] Population 2,723 (2011).
