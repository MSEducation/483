[[Ruloe House]]

CATEGORIES: Grade II listed buildings in Cheshire, Grade II listed houses, Country houses in Cheshire, John Douglas buildings

Ruloe House is a country house located [tpl]convert|1.75|mi|km|1[/tpl] to the east of Norley, Cheshire, England.  It was built in about 1873 for the Wilbraham estate, and designed by the Chester architect John Douglas.
