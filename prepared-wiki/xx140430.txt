[[Siak Regency]]

CATEGORIES: Regencies of Riau

Siak is a regency (kabupaten) of Riau Province, on the island of Sumatra, Indonesia. It has an area of 8,216.06 kmÂ² and population of 377,232 at the 2010 Census.   The northern part of this regency is covered by the Bukit Batu Biosphere Reserve.
The regency is divided into 13 subdistricts (kecamatan). The seat of the regency is located at Siak Sri Indrapura.
