[[Bergen Mekaniske Verksted]]

CATEGORIES: Shipyards of Norway, 1855 establishments in Norway, Companies established in 1855, Companies disestablished in 1991, Companies based in Bergen

Bergen Mekaniske Verksted, later Bergens Mekaniske Verksteder, was a shipyard in Solheimsviken in Bergen, Norway. Established in 1855, it later also built a drydock in Laksevåg. The company folded in 1991, but the yard in Laksevåg is still in use.[tpl]cite web |url=http://snl.no/A%252FS_Bergens_Mekaniske_Verksteder |title=A/S Bergens Mekaniske Verksteder |work=Store Norske Leksikon |accessdate=22 September 2011[/tpl]
