[[Towhidlu, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Towhidlu ([tpl]lang-fa|توحيدلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Towḩīdlū)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3773419|Towhidlu[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 160, in 43 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
