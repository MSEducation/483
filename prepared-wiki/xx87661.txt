[[Ginifer railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1982

Ginifer railway station is located on the Sunbury line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the western Melbourne suburb of St Albans opening on 31 October 1982.Ginifer Vicsig
Originally to be named Furlong, when opened it was named Ginifer, after Jack Ginifer a recently deceased local member of State Parliament. Boom barriers were provided at the Furlong Road level crossing in 1984.VR History by Andrew Waugh

==Platforms & services==

Ginifer has two side platforms. It is serviced by Metro Trains' Sunbury line services.Sunbury Line timetable Public Transport Victoria
