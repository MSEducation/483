[[World Report (RTÉ)]]

CATEGORIES: RTÉ News and Current Affairs, RTÉ Radio 1 programmes

World Report is an Irish radio programme broadcast on RTÉ Radio 1. The programme provides a series of reports about international events that happened in the week of broadcast. Various members of staff from RTÉ News present and provide content for the show which airs every Sunday morning after 08:00 for under half an hour.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.rte.ie/news/2009/1018/worldreport.html|title=http://www.rte.ie/news/worldreport/|title=World Report|work=RTÉ News|accessdate=7 March 2010[/tpl] [tpl]Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot[/tpl]
The latest episode of the show can be listened to online where there is also a number of previous editions available to download.
