[[Thma Puok (commune)]]

CATEGORIES: Communes of Banteay Meanchey Province, Thma Puok District

Thma Puok is a khum (commune) of Thma Puok District in Banteay Meanchey Province in north-western Cambodia.[tpl]cite web|url= http://www.cambodia.gov.kh/unisql1/egov/english/province/banteay_meanchey.html|title= Banteay Meanchey|publisher = cambodia.gov.kh[/tpl]
It is the capital of Thma Puok District.
