[[Gabby West]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 births, American film actresses, American television actresses, Living people, Participants in American reality television series, Reality casting show winners

Gabrielle D. "Gabby" West (born March 5, 1985)http://www.imdb.com/name/nm3982833/bio is an American actress. She won a role in Saw 3D in a competition on the reality show Scream Queens.

==Life and career==

West was born in Los Angeles, California; she graduated from Century High School in 2003.[tpl]Cite journal|last=Bauer|first=Tonya|title=CHS grad wins 'Scream Queen' title|url=http://www.bismarcktribune.com/news/local/article_43b87924-cb4b-11df-a82d-001cc4c03286.html|journal=The Bismarck Tribune|place=North Dakota|pages=B1–B4|date=September 28, 2010[/tpl]  She attended one semester at North Dakota State University, majoring in English Literature, and a year at a university in Bozeman, Montana. In 2005 she was a production assistant on the TV mini-series Into The West. In 2006, she played Suzy in the short film New Beginnings. In 2007, she moved to Los Angeles, California to pursue an acting career.[tpl]cite web|last=Kemmet|first=Kay|title=A N.D. 'Scream Queen': Century High grad on VH1 reality program|url=http://www.bismarcktribune.com/news/local/article_fa025b18-9df2-11df-aa76-001cc4c03286.html|work=The Bismarck Tribune|location=North Dakota|date=August 2, 2010|accessdate=October 1, 2010[/tpl] That same year she also appeared in the short film Linda, as in Beautiful and was a production assistant for the film 3:10 to Yuma.
In 2008, she guest starred on Tim and Eric Nite Live while being a production assistant for both the TV mini-series Comanche Moon and the film Appaloosa. In 2009, she appeared in Brüno as a German[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.horrorsociety.com/2011/02/13/interview-with-saw-3ds-gabby-west/ |title=Interview with Saw 3D’s Gabby West |publisher=Horror Society |date=2011-02-13 |accessdate=2012-01-10[/tpl] and then was cast out of 36,000 applicants[tpl]cite web|url=http://actionflickchick.com/superaction/interview-vh1s-scream-queens-2-the-winner|title=Interview: VH1’s Scream Queens 2 – The Winner!|work=Action Flick Chick|date=August 30, 2010|accessdate=2010-08-30[/tpl] on VH1's reality series Scream Queens and later won a role in the 2010 horror film Saw 3D.[tpl]cite news | url=http://blog.vh1.com/2010-09-27/the-celebreality-interview-gabby/#more-145026 | publisher=VH1|title=The Celebreality Interview: Gabby| date=September 27, 2010 | accessdate=September 27, 2010[/tpl]  West was originally cast in the 2011 remake of Fright Night but she dropped the role, telling DreadCentral, "I was cast in Fright Night (the remake), but unfortunately the role changed, and what they wanted me to do, I wasn't willing to do".[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/41009/exclusive-set-visit-chillerama-tim-sullivans-i-was-teenage-werebear-segment?page=0%2C3|title=Exclusive Set Visit: Chillerama - Tim Sullivan's I Was a Teenage Werebear Segment|last=Decker|first=Sean|work=Dread Central|publisher=CraveOnline|page=4|date=November 18, 2010|accessdate=January 6, 2010[/tpl]  West was cast in the Tim Sullivan segment of  I was a Teenage Werebear, part of the upcoming anthology series Chillerama scheduled to be released 2011. She plays Peggy Lou a "60′s era, Sandra Dee character who goes crazy".[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.killerfilm.com/interviews/read/exclusive-gabby-west-is-more-than-just-a-scream-queen-49894|title=Exclusive – Gabby West is more than just a Scream Queen|last=Bene|first=Jason|publisher=Killer Film|date=October 29, 2010|accessdate=November 3, 2010[/tpl]
