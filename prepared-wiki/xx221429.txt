[[Kungliga tennishallen]]

CATEGORIES: Tennis venues in Sweden, Sport in Stockholm, Sports venues completed in 1943

Kungliga tennishallen is a tennis venue in Stockholm, Sweden. The stadium was built in 1943 and has a capacity of 5,000.
Kungliga tennishallen, now a hard-court surface, remains the venue for the Stockholm Open tournament. In 1975 the venue was the host of the year end Masters tennis tournament.
