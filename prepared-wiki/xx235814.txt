[[Visor, svarta och röda]]

CATEGORIES: Cornelis Vreeswijk albums, 1972 albums, Swedish folk music

Visor, svarta och röda (English: Songs, black and red) is studio album by the Swedish-Dutch folk singer-songwriter Cornelis Vreeswijk. On this album, Vreeswijk sings songs with lyrics of Swedish writer Lars Forsell.Album info on www.cornelis.se
