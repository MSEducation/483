[[Vocha]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Corinthia

Vocha ([tpl]lang-el|Βόχα[/tpl]) is a former municipality in Corinthia, Peloponnese, Greece. Since the 2011 local government reform it is part of the municipality Velo-Vocha, of which it is a municipal unit.Kallikratis law Greece Ministry of Interior [tpl]el icon[/tpl] Population 10,112 (2001). The seat of the municipality was in Zevgolateio.
