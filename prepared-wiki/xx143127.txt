[[Certificate of medical necessity]]

CATEGORIES: Medicare and Medicaid (United States)

Generally, a Certificate of Medical Necessity is a piece of paper required by Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services to substantiate the medical necessity of an item of durable medical equipment furnished to a Medicare beneficiary.
