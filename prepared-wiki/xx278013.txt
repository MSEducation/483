[[A Return to Modesty]]

CATEGORIES: 1999 books, American non-fiction books, Debut books, 1990s books, English-language books

Return to Modesty: Discovering the Lost Virtue is a 1999 non-fiction debut book by Wendy Shalit.[tpl]cite web|work=The New York Times|date=March 7, 1999|title=Maids of Honor|first=Emily|last=Eakin|url=http://www.nytimes.com/books/99/03/07/reviews/990307.07eakint.html[/tpl]The New York TimesThe New York TimesPlantinga.ca

==Overview==

A non-fiction book that states the power to heal women's ills lies in reinstatement of sexual modesty, resurrection of romantic ideals, and basic good manners ala the era of Jane Austen.
