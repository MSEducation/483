[[Orgosorok]]

CATEGORIES: Ngorongoro District, Wards of Tanzania, Populated places in the Arusha Region

Orgosorok is an administrative ward in the Ngorongoro District of the Arusha Region of Tanzania. According to the 2002 census, the ward has a total population of 16,064.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.tanzania.go.tz/census/census/districts/ngorongoro.htm|title=2002 Population and Housing Census General Report|publisher=Government of Tanzania|language=en|accessdate=2008-08-19[/tpl] [tpl]Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot[/tpl]
