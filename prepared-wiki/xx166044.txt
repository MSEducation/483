[[Craig Endean]]

Craig Thomas Endean (born April 13, 1968 in Kamloops, British Columbia and raised in Prince George, British Columbia) is a retired National Hockey League left winger. In the 1986–87 NHL season, he played two games for the Winnipeg Jets, and registered one assist.
