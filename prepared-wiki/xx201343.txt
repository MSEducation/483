[[Daniel Pighín]]

CATEGORIES: 1962 births, Living people, People from Santa Fe Province, Argentine footballers, Association football midfielders, Gimnasia y Esgrima de La Plata footballers, Estudiantes de La Plata footballers, Colón de Santa Fe footballers, Rosario Central footballers, Club Atlas footballers, Expatriate footballers in Mexico

 Daniel Pighín  (born 4 September 1962) is a retired Argentine football midfielder who played for several clubs in Argentina and Mexico, including Club Gimnasia y Esgrima La Plata, Estudiantes de La Plata and Club Atlas.

==Career==

Born in Margarita, Santa Fe Province, Pighín played 14 seasons of top-flight club football.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Opus Dei|title=Impresiones de Daniel Pighín: 14 años como jugador de primera división|trans_title=Impressions of Daniel Pighín: a player for 14 years in the first division|url=http://www.opusdei.org.ar/art.php?p=40636|language=Spanish|date=1 October 2010[/tpl]
