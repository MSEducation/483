[[Buk]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Greater Poland Voivodeship, Poznań County

Buk [tpl]IPAc-pl|b|u|k[/tpl] is a town in central Poland, situated in the Greater Poland Voivodeship (since 1999), previously in Poznań Voivodeship (1975-1998).
The town's name means "Beech" in Polish, and the flag of the town shows a branch of beech, and three beech leaves.
