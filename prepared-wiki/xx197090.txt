[[Camerón]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Tierra del Fuego, Ports and harbours of Chile, Hamlets in Chile, Populated places in Tierra del Fuego Province, Chile

Villa Cameron is a hamlet ([tpl]lang-es|aldea [/tpl]) in southern Tierra del Fuego Island, and is the head of Timaukel commune, in Magallanes Region.http://www.municipalidadtimaukel.cl Website of the Municipalty Timaukel Villa Cameron is located in the southern shore of Inútil Bay.
