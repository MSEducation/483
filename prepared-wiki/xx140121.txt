[[Muraste]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Harju County

Muraste is a village in Harku Parish, Harju County in northern Estonia. It has a population of 1,435 (as of 1 June 2010).[tpl]cite web|title=Üldinfo|url=http://www.harku.ee/index.php|publisher=Harku vald[/tpl]
Writer Erni Krusten (1900–1984) was born in Muraste.
