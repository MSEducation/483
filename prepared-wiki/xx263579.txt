[[Cyril Blažo]]

CATEGORIES: Contemporary artists, Slovak artists, 1970 births, Living people

Cyril Blažo (born 1970 in Bratislava) is a Slovakian artist who lives in works in Bratislava in the Slovak Republic.

==Education==

Blažo was educated in Bratislava, attending supš from 1984 to 1988, and the Academy of Fine Arts and Design (VŠVU) from 1988 to 1994.
