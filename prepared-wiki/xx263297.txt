[[Myeloma Canada]]

CATEGORIES: Non-profit organizations based in Canada, Organizations established in 2004, Cancer research, Cancer organizations

Myeloma Canada is a non-profit organization which serves the myeloma community of Canada. The organization helps myeloma patients and raises awareness about this rare form of cancer.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.myelomacanada.ca/en/myelomacanadadesc.htm |title=Myeloma Canada : myeloma canada |publisher=Myelomacanada.ca |date= |accessdate=2011-01-02[/tpl] It was founded in 2004 by Aldo del Col and John F. Lemieux, both of whom are multiple myeloma survivors.http://www.thesuburbannews.ca/content/en/5554
The organization works with its Scientific Review Board composed of hematologists who are recognized myeloma specialists in Canada.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.canadahelps.org/CharityProfilePage.aspx?CharityID=s93139&Language=en |title=Charity Profile: MYELOMA CANADA |publisher=CanadaHelps |date= |accessdate=2011-01-02[/tpl] It is an affiliate of the International Myeloma Foundation.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.myelomabeacon.com/links/2008/10/15/myeloma-canada/ |title=Myeloma Canada |publisher=The Myeloma Beacon |date=2008-10-15 |accessdate=2011-01-02[/tpl]

==Mission==

The organization aims to "Make Myeloma Matter" by increasing awareness of the disease, providing support and educational tools to patients and their families, and promoting better treatment options and resources.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.newswire.ca/en/releases/archive/February2009/25/c3465.html |title=MYELOMA CANADA | Canadians lack confidence in access to cancer medications |publisher=Newswire.ca |date= |accessdate=2011-01-02[/tpl]
To further this awareness, the organization has organized an annual Multiple Myeloma March every October.http://www.newswire.ca/en/releases/archive/October2010/07/c8841.html
