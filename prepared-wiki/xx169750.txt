[[Paul Weller (politician)]]

CATEGORIES: Victoria (Australia) National politicians, 1959 births, Living people, Members of the Victorian Legislative Assembly

Paul Weller (born 3 April 1959) is an Australian politician and the Nationals member for Rodney in the Victorian Legislative Assembly since 2006.

Weller won pre-selection for Rodney after the retirement of Noel Maughan.  Despite unfavourable preferences from the Labor Party, Weller won 40% of the primary vote and 54% of the two party preferred vote against the Liberal candidate.
