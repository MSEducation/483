[[The True Story of Frank Zappa's 200 Motels]]

CATEGORIES: 1988 films, Direct-to-video documentary films, Films directed by Frank Zappa

The True Story of Frank Zappa's 200 Motels is a documentary film released in 1988 by Frank Zappa, detailing the making of Zappa's 1971 film 200 Motels.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0096313/|title=The True Story of Frank Zappa's 200 Motels|publisher=IMDb|accessdate=2008-12-31[/tpl] As of December 2013, the video has not yet been released on DVD.
