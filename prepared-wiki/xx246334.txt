[[Personal Reminiscences of Henry Irving]]

CATEGORIES: 1906 books, Works by Bram Stoker, Heinemann (publisher) books

Personal Reminiscences of Henry Irving is the third book of nonfiction by Bram Stoker (the author of Dracula), published in 1906.http://www.fantasticfiction.co.uk/s/bram-stoker/ Stoker's Bibliography at Fantastic Fiction.  It is a biography about the English actor Henry Irving.
