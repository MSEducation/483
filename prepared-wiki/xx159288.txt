[[Mein Baby war beim Frisör]]

CATEGORIES: 1996 singles, Die Ärzte songs, Songs written by Farin Urlaub

"Mein Baby war beim Frisör" (My baby's been to the hairdresser's) is a punk song by Die Ärzte. It's the second track on and the second single from their 1996 album Le Frisur.

==Track listing==

==B-sides==

"Zusamm'fassung" actually consists of 13 different tracks. On the CD and the 7" single, they're all in one track; on the maxi single they are separate, although the back cover lists them as one track. "Zusammenfassung" (Summary), the track of origin of these short tracks, is found on "Le Frisur".
