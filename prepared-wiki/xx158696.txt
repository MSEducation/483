[[Marc Boutte]]

CATEGORIES: 1969 births, Living people, American football defensive tackles, Washington Redskins players, Los Angeles Rams players, LSU Tigers football players

Marc Anthony Boutte (born July 25, 1969 in Lake Charles, Louisiana) is a former American football defensive tackle in the National Football League for the Washington Redskins and the Los Angeles Rams. He played college football at Louisiana State University and was drafted in the third round of the 1992 NFL Draft.
