[[Cervecería Polar]]

CATEGORIES: Beer and breweries in Venezuela, Companies of Venezuela, Companies established in 1941

Cervecería Polar, C.A. is one of the most important subsidiaries of the Empresas Polar, C.A. conglomerate. The enterprise makes beers and malt. For its distribution, it relies on "Red de Franquicias de Distribución" to carry out the rest of the job.
