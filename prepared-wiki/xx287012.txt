[[Yengi Kand-e Seyyedlar]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Yengi Kand-e Seyyedlar ([tpl]lang-fa|ينگي كندسيدلر[/tpl], also Romanized as Yengī Kand-e Seyyedlar, Yengī Kand Seyyedlar, and Yangī Kand-e Seyyedlar; also known as Yangikand, Yangikend, and Yengī Kand)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3088654|Yengi Kand-e Seyyedlar[/tpl] is a village in Golabar Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,284, in 376 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
