[[Valley Centre, Saskatchewan]]

CATEGORIES: Marriott No. 317, Saskatchewan, Unincorporated communities in Saskatchewan

[tpl]Other uses|Valley Center (disambiguation)[tpl]![/tpl]Valley Center[/tpl]
Valley Centre is a hamlet in Marriott Rural Municipality 317, Saskatchewan, Canada. The hamlet is located southeast of the Town of Biggar and northeast of the Town of Rosetown along Highway 768.
