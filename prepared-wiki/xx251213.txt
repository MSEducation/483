[[Ava Town Hall]]

CATEGORIES: Government buildings on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Government buildings completed in 1897, Buildings and structures in Oneida County, New York

Ava Town Hall is a historic town hall building located at Ava in Oneida County, New York. It was built in 1897 and is a two story, balloon frame structure with a "T" shaped floor plan, stone foundation, and intersecting gable roofs.  Also on the property is a small frame privy.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=6113|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: Ava Town Hall|date=February 1992|accessdate=2010-01-08 |author=Mark Peckham|publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation[/tpl] See also: [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=6114|title=Accompanying five photos[/tpl]
It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1992.
