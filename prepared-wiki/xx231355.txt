[[B. David]]

CATEGORIES: Sri Lankan cricketers, Kalutara Physical Culture Centre cricketers, Living people, Year of birth unknown, Place of birth missing (living people)

B. David (first name and birthdate unknown) was a Sri Lankan cricketer who played for Kalutara Physical Culture Centre.
David made two first-class appearances for the team, during the Saravanamuttu Trophy competition of 1991-92. David bowled in the tailend, scoring all 27 runs of his first-class career in the final innings in which he batted.
David bowled seven overs during his first-class career, though he failed to take a wicket in either match.
