[[Jack Cameron (baseball)]]

CATEGORIES: 1884 births, 1963 deaths, Major League Baseball outfielders, Boston Beaneaters players, Major League Baseball players from Canada, Baseball people from Nova Scotia, Lowell Tigers players, Fall River Indians players, Lawrence Colts players, Worcester Busters players

John Stanley "Happy Jack" Cameron (born September 22, 1884 - July 12, 1963) is a former Major League Baseball outfielder. He batted .180 with 4 RBIs in 18 games for the Boston Beaneaters in [tpl]By|1906[/tpl].
