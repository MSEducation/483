[[Galena Creek Schoolhouse]]

CATEGORIES: School buildings on the National Register of Historic Places in Nevada, Storey County, Nevada, Schools in Nevada

The Galena Creek Schoolhouse in Storey County, Nevada, near Reno, is a historic schoolhouse that was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2011.
