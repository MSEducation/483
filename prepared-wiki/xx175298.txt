[[KHYM]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Kansas, Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 1997

KHYM is a Christian radio station broadcasting on 103.9 FM, licensed to Copeland, Kansas and serving the areas of Dodge City, Kansas, Garden City, Kansas, and Liberal, Kansas. 
The station's format consists primarily of Christian music with blocks of Christian talk and teaching weekdays from Noon to 2:00 PM and 8:30 PM to 10:30 PM, Saturdays from 12:30 PM to 2:00 PM, and Sundays from 7:30 AM to 8:00 AM and from 9:00 PM to 10:00 PM.  Christian talk and teaching programs heard on KHYM include; Back to the Bible, Leading The Way with Michael Youssef, Turning Point with David Jeremiah, Insight For Living with Chuck Swindoll, Truth for Life with Alistair Begg, Focus on the Family, and Unshackled!.
