[[Ilta-Sanomat]]

CATEGORIES: 1932 establishments in Finland, Publications established in 1932, Newspapers published in Finland, Finnish-language newspapers, Media in Helsinki

Ilta-Sanomat (Finnish for the evening news) is one of Finland's two prominent tabloid size evening dailys and the second largest newspaper in the country. Its counterpart and biggest rival is Iltalehti. In the period of 1995-96 Ilta Sanomat had a circulation of 213,600 copies.[tpl]cite book|title=Media Policy: Convergence, Concentration & Commerce|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=k6HU9WdjwgkC&pg=PA7|accessdate=3 February 2014|date=24 September 1998|publisher=SAGE Publications|isbn=978-1-4462-6524-6|page=10[/tpl]
