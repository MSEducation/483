[[Harper, Oregon]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Malheur County, Oregon, Census-designated places in Oregon, Unincorporated communities in Oregon

Harper is an unincorporated community in Malheur County, Oregon, United States. Although it is unincorporated, it has a post office with a ZIP code of 97906.Zip Code Lookup  Harper lies off U.S. Route 20 southwest of Vale.

==Transportation==
