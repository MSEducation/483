[[Josef Hrabal]]

Josef Hrabal (born August 17, 1985 in Přerov, Czechoslovakia) is a Czech ice hockey player currently plays for Modo Hockey.

==Career==

He has played for HC Vsetin in Czech Extraliga and for the Edmonton Oilers in the NHL.
