[[Happy Couples Never Last]]

CATEGORIES: American independent record labels

Happy Couples Never Last, is an independent record label.  The label has gone on to produce nearly 40 albums under their title.  A majority of the records released have been artist splits.
HCNL has one compilation titled Relics of Ordinary Life under their title featuring various screamo acts, such as Usurp Synapse, Hassan I Sabbah, the Suicide Note and more.

==Current bands==

HCNL is currently inactive, although Clark Giles plans to issue one more release to bring the count to an even 50.
