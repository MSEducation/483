[[Kaohsiung County]]

CATEGORIES: Kaohsiung, Former counties of Taiwan, 2010 disestablishments in Taiwan

Kaohsiung County was a county in southern Taiwan. The county capital was Fongshan City.

==History==

Kaohsiung County was established on 6 December 1945 after the handover of Taiwan from Japan to the Republic of China in October 1945. On 25 December 2010, the county merged with Kaohsiung City to form a single special municipality.http://taiwanjournal.nat.gov.tw/ct.asp?xitem=53774&ctnode=413&mp=9
