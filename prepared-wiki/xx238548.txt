[[Get a Life (film)]]

CATEGORIES: 2001 films, Portuguese films, Portuguese-language films, 2000s drama films, Films directed by João Canijo

Get a Life ([tpl]lang-pt|Ganhar a Vida[/tpl]) is a 2001 Portuguese drama film directed by João Canijo. It was screened in the Un Certain Regard section at the 2001 Cannes Film Festival.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.festival-cannes.com/en/archives/ficheFilm/id/2001146/year/2001.html |title=Festival de Cannes: Get a Life |accessdate=2009-10-18|work=festival-cannes.com[/tpl]
