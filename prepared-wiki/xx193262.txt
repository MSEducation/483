[[Shelli VanDenburgh]]

CATEGORIES: Members of the Indiana House of Representatives, Living people, Indiana Democrats, Women state legislators in Indiana, 1969 births

Rochelle "Shelli" VanDenburgh (born October 21, 1969) is a Democratic member of the Indiana House of Representatives, representing the 19th District since 2007. She is Vice Chair of the Family, Children and Human Affairs Committee, and is a member of the Small Business and Economic Development, Elections and Apportionment, and Education committees.
VanDenburgh was Director of the Child Support Division in the Lake County Clerk's Office from 1995 until her resignation in May 2008.
