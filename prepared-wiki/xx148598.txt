Charles Cameron "Trusty" Tallman (September 18, 1900 – November 16, 1973) was an American football player and, coach of football and basketball, and law enforcement officer.  He served as the head football coach at Marshall University from 1925 to 1928 and at West Virginia University from 1934 to 1936, compiling a career college football record of 37–21–9.  Tallman was also the head basketball coach at Marshall during the 1925–26 season, tallying a mark of 10–7.
