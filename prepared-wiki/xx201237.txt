[[Karkarpko Island]]

CATEGORIES: Islands of the Chukchi Sea, Islands of Chukotka Autonomous Okrug

Karkarpko Island (Ostrov Karkarpko) is a small island in the Chukchi Sea. It is close to the coast, being only [tpl]convert|3|km|mi|abbr=on[/tpl] away to the east from the landtip north of Cape Vankarem in the Chukotka Peninsula. 
This island is only [tpl]convert|0.6|km|mi|abbr=on[/tpl] in length.  
Karkarpko Island has a Stationary Observation Site for environmental observations, like the monitoring of species.http://www.wwf.ru/data/pub/species/wwf_pb_monitoring_chuk_e.pdf
