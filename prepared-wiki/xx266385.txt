[[Hanes Middle School]]

CATEGORIES: Magnet schools in North Carolina, Public middle schools in North Carolina, Education in Winston-Salem, North Carolina, Schools in Forsyth County, North Carolina

Hanes Middle School school is a magnet school located in Winston-Salem, North Carolina known primarily for its HAG (Highly Academically Gifted) and AG programs. It is also known for its Project Lead the Way Program funded by the million dollar technology grant they received in 2008. It frequently generates the counties top scores, and has been named a National Magnet School of Distinction. Almost every year, they have the most students in the county that make All County Band. Multiple times they have had a student be in the Regional, State, and National Spelling Bee. Hanes has also had big success in the science fair.
