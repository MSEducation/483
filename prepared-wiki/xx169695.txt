[[Niagara SkyWheel]]

CATEGORIES: Niagara SkyWheel, Ferris wheels, Buildings and structures in Niagara Falls, Ontario, Visitor attractions in Niagara Falls, Ontario

Niagara SkyWheel is a [tpl]Convert|175|ft|m|sigfig=3|adj=on[/tpl] tall Ferris wheel in the middle of Clifton Hill, Niagara Falls, Ontario, Canada. 
Niagara SkyWheel is a Ronald Bussink Professional Rides designed R60 Giant Wheel, manufactured by Chance Rides and supplied by Chance Morgan.Breathtaking views of Niagara Falls Aboard the First Chance Morgan R60 Giant Wheel It opened on 17 June 2006, at a cost of $10 million.[tpl]Cite web|title=One of a Kind ‘Giant Wheel’ Debuts in Niagara Falls, Canada|url=http://www.prweb.com/releases/niagara/skywheel/prweb403069.htm|publisher=prweb.com|accessdate=2010-05-22[/tpl]
Its 42 Swiss-manufactured fully enclosed passenger cars can each carry eight people and are heated in the winter and air conditioned in the warmer months.
The ride is approximately 12 to 15 minutes long, giving passengers views of the Niagara River, and the Horseshoe Falls and American Falls, and is open all year, from 9:00 am to 1:00 am.
