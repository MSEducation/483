Jeremy Podeswa (born 1962) is a Canadian/American film and television director. He is best known for directing the films The Five Senses (1999) and Fugitive Pieces (2007). He has also worked as Director on the television shows Six Feet Under,[tpl]Cite web |author=HBO |title = Six Feet Under cast and crew |url = http://www.hbo.com/six-feet-under/cast-and-crew/index.html |accessdate = 12 May 2010[/tpl] Nip/Tuck, The Tudors, Queer as Folk, and the HBO World War II miniseries The Pacific.[tpl]Cite web |author=HBO |title = Jeremy Podeswa on The Pacific |url = http://www.hbo.com/the-pacific/cast-and-crew/jeremy-podeswa/index.html |accessdate = 12 May 2010[/tpl] He has also written several films.
