[[Agnes Dennis]]

CATEGORIES: People from the Halifax Regional Municipality, 1859 births, 1947 deaths, Canadian newspaper publishers (people), Canadian nurses, People from Colchester County, Nova Scotia

Agnes Dennis (11 Apr 1859-21 Apr 1947) was one of the Nova Scotia 5 who was active in the Local Council of Women of Halifax.  She was also president of the Victorian Order of Nurses and the Red Cross.  She also ran the Halifax Herald after the death of her husband.  She had 10 children.
