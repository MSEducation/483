[[Plombières]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Liège (province)

Plombières ([tpl]lang-de|Bleyberg[/tpl] or Bleiberg, [tpl]lang-nl|Blieberg[/tpl]) is a Walloon municipality located in the Belgian province of Liège. On January 1, 2006, Plombières had a total population of 9,672. The total area is 53.17 km² which gives a population density of 182 inhabitants per km². The municipality Plombières consists of the villages Gemmenich, Hombourg, Montzen, Moresnet and Sippenaeken.
The local language is Low Dietsch, a bridge dialect between East Limburgish and Ripuarian.
