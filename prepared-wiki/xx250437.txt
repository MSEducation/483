[[1990 Athens Trophy]]

CATEGORIES: 1990 WTA Tour, Athens Trophy, 1990 in Greek sport

The 1990 Athens Trophy was a women's tennis tournament played on outdoor clay courts in Athens in Greece that was part of Tier V of the 1990 WTA Tour. It was the fifth edition of the tournament and was held from September 10 through September 16, 1990.
