[[Amai Anata no Aji]]

CATEGORIES: 2000 singles, Zetima Records singles, Song recordings produced by Tsunku

[tpl]nihongo|"Amai Anata no Aji"|甘いあなたの味|Your Sweet Taste[/tpl] is the debut  single of Japanese girl group Melon Kinenbi. It was used as the ending theme of the TBS show "Majutsu-shi Ōfen Revenge". Its highest position on the Oricon weekly chart was #60.
