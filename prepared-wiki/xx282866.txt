[[Dirngulbai Misech]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1998 births, Palauan swimmers

Dirngulbai Misech (born 1998) is a Palauan swimmer.http://www.bcn2013.com/en/swimming/detail/member/dirngulbai-moreyna-misech She competed in the 50 m freestyle, 100 m freestyle and 50 m butterfly events at the 2012 FINA World Swimming Championships (25 m). Misech also competed in the 50 m and 100 m freestyle events at the 2013 World Aquatics Championships.
