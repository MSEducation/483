[[Stephen O'Donnell (footballer born 1992)]]

CATEGORIES: 1992 births, Living people, Scottish footballers, Scottish Football League players, Aberdeen F.C. players, Celtic F.C. players, Partick Thistle F.C. players, Association football utility players, Scottish Professional Football League players, Scotland under-21 international footballers

Stephen O'Donnell (born 11 May 1992) is a Scottish football defender who currently plays for Partick Thistle in the Scottish Premiership.

==Career==

O'Donnell began his career as a youth player with Wishaw Wycombe Wanderers and captained Aberdeen's Under 17's[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.wishawpress.co.uk/wishaw-sport/2008/10/01/school-pals-captain-celtic-and-aberdeen-u-17s-76495-21933356/ | title=School pals captain Celtic and Aberdeen U-17s | publisher=Wishaw Press | date=1 October 2008 | accessdate=25 September 2011[/tpl] before joining Celtic[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.football.co.uk/hibernian/hibs_offer_trial_to_freed_celtic_youngster_stephen_o_donnell_rss1655372.shtml | title=Hibs offer trial to freed Celtic youngster Stephen O'Donnell | publisher=Football.co.uk | date=3 May 2011 | accessdate=25 September 2011[/tpl] where he was part of the team that won the Scottish FA Youth Cup[tpl]cite web | url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/13218209.stm | title=Celtic U19 2-1 Rangers U19 (aet) | publisher=British Broadcasting Corporation | work=BBC Sport | date=27 April 2011 | accessdate=25 September 2011[/tpl] and under 19 League title  during season 2010-11 but was released at the end of the season.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.eveningtimes.co.uk/mobile/sport/editor-s-picks/stephen-will-go-through-hoops-for-hero-jackie-1.1123629 | title=Stephen will go through Hoops for hero Jackie | publisher=Evening Times | date=14 September 2011 | accessdate=25 September 2011[/tpl]

===Partick Thistle===
