[[KLMO-FM]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in San Antonio, Texas

KLMO-FM (98.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Spanish Religious format. Licensed to Dilley, Texas, USA, the station serves the San Antonio area.  The station is currently owned by Dilley Broadcasters.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KLMO-FM |title=KLMO-FM Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division[/tpl]

==History==

The station was assigned the call letters KMOA on 2000-06-21.  On 2000-10-27, the station changed its call sign to the current KLMO.[tpl]cite web |title=KLMO Call Sign History |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=16931&Callsign=KLMO |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division[/tpl] 

==Translators==

In addition to the main station, KLMO-FM is relayed by an additional translator to widen its broadcast area.
