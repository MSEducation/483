[[Kozue Amano]]

CATEGORIES: Manga artists, 1974 births, Living people, Female comics writers, Female comics artists, People from Saitama, Saitama

[tpl]Nihongo|Kozue Amano|天野 こずえ|Amano Kozue|born 26 May 1974[/tpl] is a female Japanese manga artist. She is widely known as the creator of Aria, which proved to be a best-selling hit,[tpl]cite web | url=http://archives.tcj.com/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=513&Itemid=70 | title=ADV's Abandoned Manga | author=Dirk Deppey | publisher=The Comics Journal | date= 24 January 2007 | accessdate= 22 January 2010[/tpl][tpl]cite web | url=http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/news/2007-07-05/production-i.g-announces-mag-garden-merger | title=Production I.G Announces Mag Garden Merger | publisher=Anime News Network | date= 5 July 2007 | accessdate= 19 January 2009[/tpl] and was adapted into an anime television series consisting of 3 seasons and an OVA.
As of autumn 2010, Amano's work releases have been shifted from a monthly schedule to a seasonal one (every 3 months) owing to pregnancy and subsequent childcare.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/news/2010-08-30/kozue-amano-amanchu-goes-quarterly-due-to-pregnancy | title=Kozue Amano's Amanchu! Goes Quarterly Due to Pregnancy | publisher=Anime News Network | date= 30 August 2010 | accessdate= 28 January 2011[/tpl]
