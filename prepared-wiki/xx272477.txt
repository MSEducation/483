[[Colmar, Illinois]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in McDonough County, Illinois, Unincorporated communities in Illinois

Colmar is an unincorporated community in Lamoine Township, McDonough County, Illinois, United States. Colmar is located on Illinois Route 61 [tpl]convert|4|mi|km[/tpl] north-northeast of Plymouth.[tpl]cite map |publisher=Illinois Department of Transportation |title=McDonough County General Highway Map |url=http://www.dot.state.il.us/maps/county/mcdonough.pdf |format= |edition= |year=2011 |cartography= |scale= |series= |page= |section= |inset= |accessdate=September 25, 2011 |isbn= |id= |ref= [/tpl] Colmar had a post office, which closed on July 18, 1992.[tpl]cite web|title=Postmaster Finder - Post Offices by ZIP Code|url=http://webpmt.usps.gov/pmt008.cfm|publisher=United States Postal Service|accessdate=September 25, 2011[/tpl]
