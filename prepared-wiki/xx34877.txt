==Fauna==

Many migratory birds use the coastal, estuarine and freshwater habitats of Eurobodalla National Park including the Far Eastern Curlew, Whimbrel, Greenshank, Turnstone and Bar-tailed Godwit. Endangered species in the park include the Long-nosed potoroo, White-footed dunnart, Little Tern and Hooded Plover.NSW National Parks and Wildlife Service. (2007).
