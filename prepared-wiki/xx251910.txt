[[Eclipse (K. A. Bedford novel)]]

CATEGORIES: 2005 novels, 2000s science fiction novels, Aurealis Award winning works

Eclipse is a 2005 science fiction novel by K. A. Bedford. It follows the story of James Dunne, an officer of the Royal Interstellar Service Academy whose first assignment becomes a nightmare when he is drafted into the First Contact Team.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.edgewebsite.com/books/eclipse/ec-catalog.html | title = ECLIPSE | publisher = Edge Science Fiction and Fantasy Publishing | date = | accessdate = 2010-01-10[/tpl]
