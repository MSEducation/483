[[Étalle, Belgium]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Luxembourg (Belgium)

[tpl]Other uses|Étalle (disambiguation)[tpl]![/tpl]Étalle[/tpl]

Étalle is a Walloon municipality of Belgium located in the province of Luxembourg. 
On 1 January 2007 the municipality, which covers 78.1 km², had 5,389 inhabitants, giving a population density of 69 inhabitants per km².
