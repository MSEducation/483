[[Zaghehlu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Zaghehlu ([tpl]lang-fa|زاغه لو[/tpl], also Romanized as Zāghehlū)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3773438|Zaghehlu[/tpl] is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 342, in 78 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
