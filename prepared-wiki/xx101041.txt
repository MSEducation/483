[[Free Haven]]

CATEGORIES: Anonymity networks

From their webpage:
Free Haven hosts the Tor "onion routing" software which can make SSL transactions such as web browsing anonymous as well as the Mixminion Type III anonymous remailer. Tor has been supported both by a US Navy grant http://www.wired.com/news/privacy/0,1848,64464,00.html and by the Electronic Frontier Foundation.
