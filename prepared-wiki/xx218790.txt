Michael Paul Mason (November 21, 1958 in Faribault, Minnesota), is a retired Major League Baseball player who played pitcher from [tpl]By|1982[/tpl]-[tpl]By|1988[/tpl] for the Texas Rangers, Chicago Cubs, and Minnesota Twins.  He is currently the pitching coach for the Iowa Cubs. His best season came as a member of the 1984 Texas Rangers.
