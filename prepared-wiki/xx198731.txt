[[George James, Jr.]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Alabama State Hornets football coaches, Kentucky State Thorobreds football coaches

George James, Jr. was the 23rd head college football coach
for the Kentucky State University Thorobreds located in Frankfort, Kentucky
and he held that position for two seasons, from 1990 until 1991.
His career coaching record at Kentucky State was 4 wins, 18 losses, and 0 ties.  As of the conclusion of the 2007 season, this ranks him
16th at Kentucky State in total wins and 21st at Kentucky State in winning percentage (0.182).
