[[Minamiaiki, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Minamiaiki|南相木村|Minamiaiki-mura[/tpl] is a village located in Minamisaku District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of April 1, 2012, the village has an estimated population of 1,108, with 533 men, and 575 women, and a density of 16.7 persons per km². The total area is 66.03 km².

==History==

Minamiaiki shares a similar history with Kitaaiki, its neighboring village.
