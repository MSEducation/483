[[Shur Ab, Ijrud]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Shur Ab ([tpl]lang-fa|شوراب[/tpl], also Romanized as Shūr Āb and Shūrāb; also known as Sarāb and Shirou)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3085342|Shur Ab[/tpl] is a village in Saidabad Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 81, in 33 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
