[[Lights Low]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 singles

Lights Low is the first single by rapper 2 Pistols for his second album Arrogant. It features singer Young Joe. 2 Pistols' part was recorded over an original instrumental produced by David James, and the track was re-produced by Cool & Dre who added the rapper C-Ride. This is the first single from his deal with Cash Money Records. It reached #93 on the Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs, and #13 on the Hot Rap Tracks.
