[[Pray Lake]]

CATEGORIES: Lakes of Glacier National Park (U.S.), Lakes of Glacier County, Montana

Pray Lake is located in Glacier National Park, in the U. S. state of Montana. The lake is approximately [tpl]convert|100|yd|m[/tpl] northeast of Two Medicine Lake and is only [tpl]convert|1|ft|m[/tpl] lower in altitude with a short stream connecting the two. A vehicular access National Park Service campground is adjacent to the lake.[tpl]cite web| title =Dancing Lady Mountain, MT|publisher =TopoQuest| url =http://www.topoquest.com/map.php?lat=48.49080&lon=-113.36565&datum=nad83&zoom=4| format =Map (USGS Quad)| accessdate = 2010-12-31[/tpl]
