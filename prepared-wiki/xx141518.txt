[[Dubu kimchi]]

CATEGORIES: Kimchi, Tofu dishes

Dubu kimchi is a Korean dish consisting of sauteed kimchi served with tofu.[tpl]cite web|title=Tofu and Sauteed Kimchi (Dubu Kimchi) Recipe|url=http://koreanfood.about.com/od/snacksanddesserts/r/Tofu-And-Sauteed-Kimchi-Dubu-Kimchi-Recipe.htm|publisher=About.com|accessdate=16 April 2013[/tpl]  Boiled or pan-fried sliced tofu and the kimchi are often prepared with sliced pork. Despite the name of the dish, the tofu is not fermented into kimchi.
The dish is a popular anju (a side dish consumed with alcohol).[tpl]cite web|title=Dubu kimchi, (tofu with stir-fried kimchi and pork)|url=http://m.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=20130308000726&ntn=0|publisher=The Korea Herald|accessdate=16 April 2013|date=2013-03-08[/tpl]
