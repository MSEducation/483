[[José Antonio Galán]]

CATEGORIES: 1749 births, 1782 deaths, History of Colombia, 18th-century Colombian people

José Antonio Galán (ca. 1749, Charalá, Santander (Colombia) – February 2, 1782, Santafé de Bogotá (Id.) was a Colombian historical figure of the 18th century. He was the leader of the Comuneros insurrection in 1781, sentenced and executed by the Spanish.
