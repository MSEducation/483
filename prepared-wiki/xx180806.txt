[[KORD-FM]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Washington (state)

KORD-FM (102.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Country music format.Licensed to Richland, Washington, USA, the station serves the Tri-Cities area.  The station is currently owned by Townsquare Media and features programing from Jones Radio Network. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KORD-FM |title=KORD-FM Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==History==

The station went on the air in 1965.  On October 11, 1989 the station changed its call sign from KZZK, using the on-air moniker KZ103 to the current KORD. [tpl]cite web|url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=16726&Callsign=KORD |title=KORD Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
As KZZK 102.7 this station operated a CHR/AC leaning format in the 1980s. Towards the end of that decade, it ceded the CHR market to then-powerhouse KIOK (OK95). It became a country station and has remained that to this day.
