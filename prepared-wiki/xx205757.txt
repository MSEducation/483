[[New York State Division of Human Rights]]

CATEGORIES: Human rights organizations based in the United States, State law enforcement agencies of New York

The New York State Division of Human Rights is a New York State agency created to enforce the state's Human Rights Law.  The law prohibits discrimination in the provision of housing, employment, credit, and access to certain public places  based on specified protected characteristics, which include age, race, gender, sexual orientation, and disability.New York State Division of Human Rights Mission Statement  The Division is responsible for investigating, prosecuting, and adjudicating complaints of discrimination brought under New York's Human Rights Law.
