[[Liaoyang County]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Liaoning, Liaoyang

Liaoyang County ([tpl]zh|s=辽阳县 |t=遼陽縣 |p=Liáoyáng Xiàn[/tpl]) is a county in east-central Liaoning province of Northeast China. It is under the administration of Liaoyang City, located in between the urban area of Liaoyang City and Anshan City.

There are 14 towns, 1 township, and 3 townships in the county.
