[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Jundiaí]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1966, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Sorocaba, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Jundiaí ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Iundiaiensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Jundiaí in the Ecclesiastical province of Sorocaba in Brazil.
