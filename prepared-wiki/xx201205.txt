[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Teófilo Otoni]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1960, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Diamantina, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century, 1960 establishments in Brazil

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Teófilo Otoni ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Otonipolitanus[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Teófilo Otoni in the Ecclesiastical province of Diamantina in Brazil.
