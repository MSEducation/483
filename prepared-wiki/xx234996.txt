[[Vladislav Ivanov (physicist)]]

CATEGORIES: Russian inventors

Vladislav Ivanov (Russian: Владислав Иванов) is a Soviet  physicist who in 1960 filed a document with the USSR State Committee for Inventions and Discovery at Leningrad for a Magnetic Resonance Imaging device, although this was not approved until the 1970s.[ref]
