[[1000 Second Avenue]]

CATEGORIES: Skyscrapers in Seattle, Washington, Skyscrapers between 150 and 199 meters, Buildings and structures completed in 1987, Office buildings in Seattle, Washington

1000 Second Avenue is a [tpl]convert|150|m|abbr=on[/tpl] skyscraper in Seattle, Washington. It was completed in 1987 and has 43 floors. Originally known as Key Tower, it is the fifteenth tallest building in Seattle [tpl]as of|2012|lc=y[/tpl].
