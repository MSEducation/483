[[Dae Kwang]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Seon Buddhists, Kwan Um School of Zen, Zen Buddhist monks and priests, American Zen Buddhists, Zen Buddhist teachers, 1944 births

Dae Kwang is a Soen Sa Nim and is the current guiding teacher of the Providence Zen Center. He was ordained as a monk in 1987 and received Dharma transmission from Seung Sahn in 1996.
