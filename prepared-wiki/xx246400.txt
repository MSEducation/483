[[Station Inn]]

CATEGORIES: Culture of Nashville, Tennessee, Music venues in Tennessee, Visitor attractions in Nashville, Tennessee, Buildings and structures in Nashville, Tennessee

The Station Inn is a concert venue in Nashville, Tennessee that hosts bluegrass acts. Frommers wrote that it is "widely regarded as one of the best bluegrass venues around".[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.frommers.com/destinations/nashville/N24122.html|title=Station Inn Review|work=Frommers|accessdate=8 January 2010[/tpl]  It has a reputation for being a simple building where the Nashville Bluegrass community convenes.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.baltimoresun.com/travel/sc-trav-0330-nashville-music-strip-20100330-29,0,4111450.story|title=Nashville offers a music bonanza|last=Hageman|first=William|date=April 4, 2010|work=The Baltimore Sun|accessdate=4 April 2010[/tpl] 
Notable artists such as Gillian Welch, Ralph Stanley, Dolly Parton, Randy Travis, and Reba McEntire have performed at The Station Inn.  Mel Gibson and U2 have visited the club as well.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cmt.com/news/articles/1558602/20070502/krauss_alison.jhtml|title=Station Inn Sets the Stage for Bluegrass in Nashville|last=Shelburne|first=Craig|date=2 May 2007|work=CMT.com|accessdate=8 January 2010[/tpl]
