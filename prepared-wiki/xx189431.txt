[[Carlos Grisales]]

CATEGORIES: 1966 births, Living people, Colombian long-distance runners, Athletes (track and field) at the 1992 Summer Olympics, Athletes (track and field) at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Olympic athletes of Colombia

Carlos Mario Grisales (born 24 August 1966) is a retired Colombian long-distance runner who specialized in the marathon. He is a two-time Olympian.
