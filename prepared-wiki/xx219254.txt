[[Exposed (2003 film)]]

CATEGORIES: 2003 films, 2000s comedy films, American films, English-language films

Exposed is a 2003 American independent comedy film starring Brenda Strong, Lumi Cavazos, Gia Carides, Tate Donovan and Missi Pyle.http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0299859/
Bob Smith (Donovan), the host of the scandalous news show "Probe", launches an investigation to dig up dirt on three popular television celebrities who are up for a "Woman of Distinction Award".
