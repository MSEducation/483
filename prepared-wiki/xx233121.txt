[[Backroads (album)]]

CATEGORIES: 1991 albums, Columbia Records albums, Ricky Van Shelton albums, Albums produced by Steve Buckingham

Backroads is the fifth Studio Album by Country Music artist Ricky Van Shelton. The first three singles released from the album, "Rockin' Years" (a duet with Dolly Parton), "I Am a Simple Man", and "Keep It Between the Lines" all were number 1 hits. "After the Lights Go Out" and "Backroads" charted at #13 and #2, respectively. The album was certified platinum by the RIAA on December 6, 1991.[tpl]cite AV media notes |title=Greatest Hits Plus |others=Ricky Van Shelton |year=1992 |type=CD |publisher=Columbia Records |id=52753[/tpl]
