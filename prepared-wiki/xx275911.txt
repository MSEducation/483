[[Tu (surname)]]

CATEGORIES: Chinese-language surnames

Tu (Chinese: 屠; pinyin: Tú) is a Chinese family name, and the 279th family name in Hundred Family Surnames (百家姓).

==Origin==

From one of the characters in the name of the ancient city of Zoutu. The legendary emperor Huang Di (2697–2595 bc) used this city as a military base. 

==People with the surname Tu (屠)==

Tu Youyou (屠呦呦), a Chinese female medical scientist awarded the 2011 Lasker Award in Clinical Medicine.
