Steven Robb (born 8 March 1982 in Perth, Scotland) is a Scottish footballer who currently plays as a left winger and left back for Brechin City.

==Career==

Robb started his career in 2001 as a youth team player with Dundee, going on to make over 80 league appearances with a short loan spell with Raith Rovers. At the end of the 2004–2005 season in which Dundee were relegated to the Scottish First Division, Robb decided to stay with Dundee but after failure to gain promotion Robb left the Dees for Dundee's rivals Dundee United in June 2006 on a free transfer.
