[[Chad Bassen]]

Chad Bassen (born April 25, 1983) is a Canadian professional ice hockey forward who is currently playing with Grizzly Adams Wolfsburg of the Deutsche Eishockey Liga (DEL). He previously played for the Hamburg Freezers of the Deutsche Eishockey Liga (DEL) during the 2010–11 season.[tpl]cite web| url = http://www.hamburg-freezers.de/index.php?link=http://www.hamburg-freezers.de/html/news_05090e5a-efb7-47ef-afb8-78aa608f73df.html | title = Personnel decisions continue, Traverse re-signed | publisher = Hamburg Freezers | date = 2011-03-20 | accessdate = 2011-03-29 | language = German[/tpl] On April 6, 2011, Bassen signed with Augsburger Panther to a one-year contract.[tpl]cite web| url = http://www.aev-panther.de/news_news,-Naechster-Neuzugang-fuer-die-Panther-–-Chad-Bassen-kommt-aus-Hamburg-_naid,680.html | title = Next newcomer for the Panthers: Chad Bassen from Hamburg | publisher = Augsburger Panther | date = 2011-04-06 | accessdate = 2011-04-06[/tpl] After the conclusion of his second season with Augsburger in 2012–13, Bassen was released as a free agent and signed for his fifth DEL club, EHC Wolfsburg on July 15, 2013.[tpl]Citeweb| url = http://www.ehc-wolfsburg.de/beitrag1349.html | title = Chad Bassen changes to EHC Wolfsburg | publisher = EHC Wolfsburg | date = 2013-07-15 | accessdate = 2013-07-15 | language = German[/tpl]
