[[Thomas Boberg]]

CATEGORIES: Danish poets, 1960 births, Living people

Thomas Boberg (born 5 May 1960 in Roskilde)[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.danishliterature.info/index.php?id=2092&no_cache=1&tx_lfforfatter_pi2%5Bstage%5D=8&tx_lfforfatter_pi2%5Buid%5D=60&tx_lfforfatter_pi2%5Blang%5D=_eng|title=Biography about Thomas Boberg|publisher=Danish Arts Agency|accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl] [tpl]Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot[/tpl] is a Danish poet and travel writer. Since his debut in 1984 with Hvæsende på mit øjekast (The Hissing on my Glance), Boberg has written numerous books and collections of poems.[tpl]cite web|url=http://snl.no/Thomas_Boberg|title=Thomas Boberg|publisher=Store norske leksikon|language=Norwegian|accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl] In 2000 he received the Otto Gelsted Prize.
Boberg lived in Peru for several years. Many of his works stem from his travels in South and Central America, often focusing on the exploited and poor.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.danishliterature.info/index.php?id=2092&no_cache=1&tx_lfforfatter_pi1%5Buid%5D=60&tx_lfforfatter_pi2%5Blang%5D=_eng|title=Author Profile|last=Conrad|first=Neal Ashley|year=1999|publisher=Danish Arts Agency|accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl] [tpl]Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot[/tpl] Boberg has been nominated twice for the Nordic Council's Literature Prize, in 1999 for Americas,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.nrk.no/nyheter/kultur/litteratur/1.3830644|title=På reise med Thomas Boberg|last=Thiis-Evensen|first=Charlotte|date=2007-10-23|publisher=NRK|language=Norwegian|accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl] and in 2006 for Livsstil (Life Style).[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.nordic-literature.org/2006/english/articles/104.htm|title=Chewing on the devil's throat|last=Stølan|first=Arne Hugo|publisher=Nordic Literature and Library Committee|accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl]
