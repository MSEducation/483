[[Cheragh Hesari]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Cheragh Hesari ([tpl]lang-fa|چراغ حصاري[/tpl], also Romanized as Cherāgh Ḩeşārī; also known as Chirāgh Hisari and Chirag-Khissari)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3058481|Cheragh Hesari[/tpl] is a village in Aq Bolagh Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 142, in 38 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
