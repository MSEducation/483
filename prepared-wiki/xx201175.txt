[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Araçuaí]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1913, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Diamantina, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Araçuaí ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Arassuahyensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Araçuaí in the Ecclesiastical province of Diamantina in Brazil.
According to biographical information contained in the Vatican Press Office's News.va and Vatican Information Service's (VIS) daily bulletins (in the section regarding episcopal appointments) for Wednesday, June 13, 2012, Pope Benedict XVI appointed the Reverend Father Marcello Romano, a member of the clergy of, and currently the Diocesan Administrator of, the Roman Catholic Diocese of Guanhaes, in Guanhaes, Brazil, as the ninth Bishop of Araçuaí.
