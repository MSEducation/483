[[New Barag Left Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
New Barag Left Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠰᠢᠨ᠎ᠡ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠪᠠᠷᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠵᠡᠭᠦᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Sin-e Barɣu Jegün qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=新巴尔虎左旗 |t=新巴爾虎左旗 |p=Xīn Bā'ěrhǔ Zuǒqí[/tpl]) is a banner of Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China, bordering Mongolia to the south. It is under the administration of Hulunbuir City.
