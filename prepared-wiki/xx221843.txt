[[Candelaria Hills]]

CATEGORIES: Mountain ranges of Nevada, Mountain ranges of the Great Basin, Landforms of Mineral County, Nevada

The Candelaria Hills are a mountain range in Mineral County, Nevada.  The highest peak is Miller Mountain which was the location of "Borax" Smith's board-and-batten cabin where he lived when he discovered a rich borax deposit at nearby Teel's Marsh.Hildebrand, GH. (1982) Borax Pioneer: Francis Marion Smith. San Diego: Howell-North Books. p 17 ISBN 0-8310-7148-6
