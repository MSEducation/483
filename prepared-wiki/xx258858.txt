[[Randy Winkler]]

CATEGORIES: People from Temple, Texas, Detroit Lions players, Atlanta Falcons players, Green Bay Packers players, American football offensive tackles, Tarleton State University alumni, 1943 births, Living people

Randy Winkler is a former offensive tackle in the National Football League. He was drafted in the twelfth round of the 1966 NFL Draft by the Detroit Lions and later played with the team during the 1967 NFL season. The following season he would play with the Atlanta Falcons. After two years away from the NFL, he played with the Green Bay Packers during the 1971 NFL season.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/W/WinkRa20.htm|title=Randy Winkler|publisher=Pro-Football-Reference.com|accessdate=2010-12-31[/tpl]
