[[H. D. Premaratne]]

CATEGORIES: 1944 births, 2005 deaths, Sri Lankan film directors

H.D. Premaratne was educated at Gurukula Vidyalaya in Kelaniya. He started his film career as a clapper boy in Daru Duka in 1967.[tpl]cite web|last=Uvais|first=Ramesh|title=H.D.Premaratne, the humble human being|url=http://archives.dailymirror.lk/2006/01/02/life/02.asp|publisher=Daily Mirror|accessdate=21 December 2012[/tpl]  While working at the old Times of Ceylon group, he was an Assistant Director for Pujithayo before embarking on his maiden directorial venture Sikuruliya starring Vijaya Kumaranatunga[tpl]cite web|title=Artists|url=http://www.nfc.gov.lk/artist/vijaya-kumaranatunga-134/|publisher=National Film Corporation|accessdate=21 December 2012[/tpl]  and Swineetha Weerasinghe in 1975.
A majority of his work focused on women and women's issues.[tpl]cite web|last=Munasinghe|first=Geesha|title=Film-maker who occupied the middle ground|url=http://www.dailynews.lk/2006/01/04/arts06.htm|publisher=Daily News|accessdate=22 December 2012[/tpl]  
Along with cinema, Premaratne produced the teledramas Sandungira Ginigani (1993),[tpl]cite web|title=Sandungira Ginigani|url=http://paradisaya.tripod.com/teledrama/sandungiragini.html|accessdate=22 December 2012[/tpl] Sihina Danauwa (1996),[tpl]cite web|title=Sihina Danauwa|url=http://paradisaya.tripod.com/teledrama/sihinadanauwa.html|accessdate=22 December 2012[/tpl] and Dulari (1997)[tpl]cite web|title=Dulari|url=http://paradisaya.tripod.com/teledrama/dulaari.html|accessdate=22 December 2012[/tpl]  and also the stage drama Yakage Kammala.
He worked at Swarnavahini as a Consultant to the Board of Directors, and functioned as the president of the Sri Lanka Cinema Bala Mandalaya.[tpl]cite web|last=Munasinghe|first=Geesha|title=Film-maker who occupied the middle ground|url=http://www.dailynews.lk/2006/01/04/arts06.htm|publisher=Daily News|accessdate=22 December 2012[/tpl]
