[[Turkish minorities in the former Ottoman Empire]]

CATEGORIES: Ethnic Turkish people, Ethnic minorities

The Turkish minorities ([tpl]lang-tr|Türk azınlıklar[/tpl]) refers to ethnic Turks who live in independent states which were formerly part of the Ottoman Empire. In the 19th century these states obtained independence from Ottoman rule but still contain relict Turkish communities.
