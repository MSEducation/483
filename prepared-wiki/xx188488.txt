CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1952, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Botucatu, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century, Marília

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Marília ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Mariliensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Marília in the Ecclesiastical province of Botucatu in Brazil.
