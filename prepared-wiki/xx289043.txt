[[Bash Qeshlaq, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Bash Qeshlaq ([tpl]lang-fa|باش قشلاق[/tpl], also Romanized as Bāsh Qeshlāq, and Bāsh Qishlāq; also known as Bashkishlak)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3055722|Bash Qeshlaq[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 185, in 45 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
