[[3rd Indian Infantry Brigade]]

CATEGORIES: British Indian Army brigades

The 3rd Indian Infantry Brigade was an Infantry formation of the Indian Army during World War II.
It was formed at Jhelum in India in September 1939. In August 1943, it was renamed the Frontier Reserve Brigade[tpl]cite web|accessdate=2009-10-19|publisher=Order of Battle|title=3 Brigade|url=http://www.ordersofbattle.com/UnitData.aspx?UniX=6157&Tab=Uhi[/tpl]
