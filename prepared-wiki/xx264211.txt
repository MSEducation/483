[[Russ Blailock]]

CATEGORIES: Players of American football from Texas, Milwaukee Badgers players, Akron Pros players, Baylor Bears football players, 1902 births, 1972 deaths

William Russell Blailock, Jr. was am American football player in the National Football League. He first played with the Milwaukee Badgers during the 1923 NFL season.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/B/BlaiRu20.htm|title=Russ Blailock|publisher=Pro-Football-Reference.com|accessdate=2011-01-02[/tpl] After a year away from the NFL, he played with the Akron Pros during the 1925 NFL season. He died of a heart attack in 1972.https://familysearch.org/pal:/MM9.3.1/TH-266-11544-136998-85?cc=1320964&wc=13012181
