[[Wolf Prize in Arts]]

CATEGORIES: Architecture awards, Art awards, Israeli music awards, Wolf Prizes, Lists of Israeli award winners, Awards established in 1981, Arts awards and contests in Israel, 1981 establishments in Israel

The Wolf Prize in Arts is awarded once a year by the Wolf Foundation in Israel. It is one of the six Wolf Prizes established by the Foundation, and has been awarded since 1981; the others are in Agriculture, Chemistry, Mathematics, Medicine and Physics, awarded since 1978. The Prize rotates annually among painting, music, architecture and sculpture.
