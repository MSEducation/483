[[David B. Snow, Jr.]]

CATEGORIES: Bates College alumni, Duke University alumni, Living people

David B. Snow, Jr. is the Chairman and CEO of Medco Health Solutions.http://investing.businessweek.com/research/stocks/people/person.asp?personId=3615024&ticker=MHS:US

==Education==

Snow obtained a bachelor of science degree from Bates College in 1976.  He also earned a master's degree in health care administration from Duke University in 1978.http://phx.corporate-ir.net/phoenix.zhtml?c=131268&p=irol-govBioCorpGov&ID=143491

==Career==

From 1993 to 1998, Snow served as an Executive Vice President of Oxford Health Plans.  He became Executive Vice President and COO of WellChoice from 1999 to 2001.  Snow then served as President and COO of WellChoice from 2001 to 2003.  Snow joined Medco as President and CEO in March 2003.  He became Chairman and CEO in June 2003.http://people.forbes.com/profile/david-b-snow/52258
