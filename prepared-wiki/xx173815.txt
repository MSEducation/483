Bryan Andrei Still (born June 3, 1974) is a retired American football wide receiver in the National Football League.[tpl]cite web |url= http://www.nfl.com/players/bryanstill/profile?id=STI376761|title=Bryan Still player page |accessdate=2008-05-23 |format= |work=nfl.com [/tpl] He was the MVP of the 1995 Sugar Bowl.

==College career==

Still played college football for the Virginia Tech Hokies. He finished his career at Virginia Tech with 74 catches for 1,458 yards and 11 touchdowns, and was named MVP of the 1995 Sugar Bowl, in which Virginia Tech defeated Texas 28-10 on December 31, 1995.  In this game, Still had 6 catches for 119 yards and 1 touchdown, and also returned a punt 60 yards for another touchdown.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.hokiesports.com/football/bowls/1995Sugar.html |title=1995 Sugar Bowl  |accessdate=2008-05-23 |work=hokiesports.com [/tpl]
