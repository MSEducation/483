[[Kyle Prater]]

CATEGORIES: 1992 births, Living people, Players of American football from Illinois, American football wide receivers, U.S. Army All-American Bowl football players, USC Trojans football players, Northwestern Wildcats football players, Sportspeople from Cook County, Illinois

Kyle Prater (born June 21, 1992) is an American football wide receiver who currently plays for Northwestern University. Prior to this, Prater attended Proviso West High School of Hillside, Illinois and played for the University of Southern California.

==High school career==

Prater graduated early from Proviso West High School. Rivals.com rated Prater as the #3 overall prospect in the class of 2010[tpl]cite web|url=http://rivals100.rivals.com/viewrank.asp?SID=880&Year=2010&ra_key=2303 |title=Rivals Class of 2010 football rankings |publisher=Rivals100.rivals.com |date= |accessdate=2012-01-10[/tpl] and Scout.com rated him as the #4 overall prospect.[tpl]cite web|url=http://recruiting.scout.com/a.z?s=73&p=9&c=4&pid=88&yr=2010 |title=Scout.com Class of 2010 football rankings |publisher=Recruiting.scout.com |date= |accessdate=2012-01-10[/tpl] In his junior season he had 60 catches for 948 yards and 9 touchdowns and in his senior year he had 64 receptions for 1151 yards and 13 touchdowns, virtually single-handedly leading the Proviso West Panthers to their first ever post-season appearance and first ever IHSA playoff win.[tpl]cite web|url=http://recruiting.scout.com/a.z?s=73&p=8&c=1&nid=4114790 |title=Season Stats from Scout.com |publisher=Recruiting.scout.com |date= |accessdate=2012-01-10[/tpl] He played in the 2010 U.S. Army All-American Bowl in San Antonio, Texas for his final high school game where he had 3 receptions for 32 yards.Army Bowl Stats[tpl]dead link|date=January 2012[/tpl]

==College career==

In September 2009, Prater committed to USC. He graduated early from high school and intended to be an early enrollee at USC for the spring term,Prater info on USC[tpl]dead link|date=January 2012[/tpl] but decided to wait on enrolling after he found out that Pete Carroll was going to become the Seattle Seahawks new head coach. Prater decided to go through with his plans to enroll early at USC when Lane Kiffin was named USC's new head coach.[tpl]cite web|url=http://sportsillustrated.cnn.com/2010/writers/andy_staples/01/13/kiffin.usc.tennessee/index.html?eref=sihp |title=Kiffin's departure already affecting USC and Vols recruiting classes |publisher=Sportsillustrated.cnn.com |date=2010-01-13 |accessdate=2012-01-10[/tpl] He red shirted his freshman year due to injury.[tpl]cite web|url=http://myespn.go.com/s/conversations/show/story/5514320?eref=sihp |title=Conversations: Touted WR Kyle Prater likely to redshirt |publisher=Myespn.go.com |date=2010-08-30 |accessdate=2012-01-10[/tpl] On January 9, 2012 Prater announced that he planned to transfer from USC to an undetermined school. He stated that he wanted to be closer to home and his release specified that he could not transfer to any other Pac-12 schools or Notre Dame, thus making a school in the Big Ten his likely destination.  Prater has since decided that he will be transferring to Northwestern.[tpl]cite web|url=http://espn.go.com/chicago/ncf/story/_/id/7448330/three-big-ten-schools-usc-trojans-transfer-kyle-prater-list |title=Kyle Prater considers Big Ten schools[/tpl]
