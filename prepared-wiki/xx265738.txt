[[Traveller (tomato)]]

CATEGORIES: Tomato cultivars

The Arkansas Traveler tomato is an open-pollinated heirloom variety that was bred by the University of Arkansas in 1968. The plant is indeterminate with round red fruits weighing approximately 6 ounces.[tpl]cite web|url=http://njaes.rutgers.edu/tomato-varieties/variety.asp?Arkansas+Traveler|title=Arkansas Traveler: Tomato|publisher=Rutgers New Jersey Agriculture Experiment Station|accessdate=4 September 2012[/tpl]
