[[St. Michael's Church (34th Street, Manhattan)]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic churches in New York, Churches in Manhattan, Hell's Kitchen, Manhattan

The Church of St. Michael is a Roman Catholic parish church in the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of New York, located at 424 West 34th Street, Manhattan, New York City.
In 1892, the address was listed at 408 W 32nd Street.The World Almanac 1892 and Book of Facts (New York: Press Publishing, 1892), p.390.
