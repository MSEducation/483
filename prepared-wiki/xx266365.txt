[[Henrik Bergh]]

CATEGORIES: 1879 births, 1952 deaths, People from Oslo, Norwegian lawyers, Conservative Party (Norway) politicians

Henrik Arnold Thaulow Bergh (1879–1952) was a  Norwegian lawyer and politician, born in Oslo, a barrister at the Supreme Court of Norway.
