[[Information Technology Lokam]]

CATEGORIES: Indian computer magazines, Monthly magazine, Magazines established in 2003

thumb|right|Official logo of IT LokamInformation Technology Lokam (or IT Lokam) is a computer magazine in Malayalam. This monthly officially started in January 2003 by Infofriend Publications, Kozhikode. 
In September 2005, IT Lokam started its CD edition, like its competition, Info Kairali. IT Lokam CD mainly contains free software programs and games, useful articles, demo and shareware programs, wallpapers, drivers, skins, etc.
