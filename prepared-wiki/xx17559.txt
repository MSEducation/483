[[Life After Life (book)]]

CATEGORIES: 1975 books, Afterlife, Death, Near-death experiences, Supernatural books

Life After Life is a 1975 book written by psychiatrist Raymond Moody. It is a report on a qualitative study in which Moody interviewed 150 people who had undergone near-death experiences (NDEs).
