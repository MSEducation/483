[[Seebach (Zürich)]]

CATEGORIES: District 11 of Zürich, Former municipalities of the canton of Zürich

Seebach is a quarter in the district 11 of Zürich, located in the Glatt Valley (German: Glattal).
It was formerly a municipality of its own, having been incorporated into Zürich in 1934.
The quarter has a population of 19,879 distributed on an area of 4.72 km².

==Transportation==

Zürich Seebach railway station is a stop of the Zürich S-Bahn on the line S6.
