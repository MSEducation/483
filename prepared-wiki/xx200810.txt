[[Kraljevi ulice]]

CATEGORIES: Croatian Eurovision Song Contest entrants, Eurovision Song Contest entrants of 2008

Kraljevi ulice is a Croatian band founded by Miran Hadži Veljković and Zlatko Petrović Pajo. The band's name translates as "Kings of the Street". They were chosen to represent Croatia at the Eurovision Song Contest 2008 in Belgrade, Serbia. They sang "Romanca" alongside 75 cents, and came in 21st.
75 Cents was the stage name of Croatian singer Ladislav Demeterffy aka Laci (29 January 1933 – 19 November 2010) who performed with Kraljevi ulice in the Eurovision Song Contest 2008.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.eurovision.tv/page/news/belgrade-2008?id=502|title=Kraljevi Ulice & 75 Cents win in Croatia|accessdate=2008-03-21|work=Eurovision Song Contest[/tpl] He was dubbed "75 Cents", a reference to his age at the time (75).
