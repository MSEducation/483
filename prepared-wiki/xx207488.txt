[[Bowers, Pennsylvania]]

CATEGORIES: Census-designated places in Berks County, Pennsylvania, Census-designated places in Pennsylvania

Bowers is a census-designated placehttp://www.census.gov/2010census/ in Maxatawny Township, Berks County, Pennsylvania, United States.  It is located near the borough of Lyons and is on Sacony Creek, a tributary of the Maiden Creek.  As of the 2010 census, the population was 326 residents.http://www.census.gov/# Although Bowers has its own post office with the zip code of 19511, some residents are served by the Mertztown post office with the zip code of 19539. http://pennsylvania.hometownlocator.com/zip-codes/data,zipcode,19511.cfm
