[[Utricularia sect. Polypompholyx]]

CATEGORIES: Utricularia, Plant sections

Utricularia'' sect. ''Polypompholyx is a section in the genus Utricularia that was considered to be its own genus in the family Lentibulariaceae but was reduce to sectional rank by Peter Taylor.Taylor, Peter. (1989). The genus Utricularia - a taxonomic monograph. Kew Bulletin Additional Series XIV: London.
