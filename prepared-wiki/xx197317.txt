[[The Adventures of Loki]]

CATEGORIES: Musical groups established in 2003, Musical groups disestablished in 2010, English musical groups

The Adventures of Loki were a 3 piece punk rock band from Lancaster, United Kingdom.
The band was formed from ex-Angelica members; Brigit Colton (Bass) and Rachel Parsons (Drums), who then teamed up with ex-Fifty Heads Wide guitarist and vocalist Steve Wade.
The band has been featured on BBC, and have played at the NXNE festival in Toronto, Canada.
