Rajko Brežančić (Serbian Cyrillic: Рајко Брежанчић; born 21 August 1989) is a Serbian footballer who plays for Čukarički in the Serbian SuperLiga.

==Club career==

Brežančić signed his first professional contract with Partizan on 1 June 2007, on a five-year deal.[tpl]cite news | url=http://www.partizan.co.rs/arhiva/vest.php?Jezik=sr&IDV=317&akcija=show&prikaz1=2007&prikaz2=6&sec=2 | title=Četiri prinove u crno-belom taboru | language=Serbian | publisher=partizan.rs | date=1 June 2007 | accessdate=11 February 2011[/tpl] After that, he played for Teleoptik and Bežanija at loan. In January 2009, he returned to Partizan and made seven league appearances until the end of the 2008–09 season. He recorded three more during the first part of the 2009–10 season, before moving to Teleoptik once again in January 2010. Brežančić signed for Metalac Gornji Milanovac at the start of the 2010–11 season.

==International career==

Brežančić was a member of the Serbian national under-21 team at the 2009 UEFA Under-21 Championship in Sweden, but did not play. He made his first appearance for the Under-21 side in a friendly match against Israel U21 on 27 August 2009. His second and last appearance for the team came on 26 May 2010, in another friendly match against Romania U21.[tpl]cite news | url=http://www.prosport.ro/fotbal-intern/tineret/avem-sperante-romania-u21-serbia-u21-3-1-6179048 | title=Avem speranţe!
