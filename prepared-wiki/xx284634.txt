[[Foolad Mahan Isfahan BC]]

CATEGORIES: Basketball teams in Iran, Basketball clubs established in 2007

Foolad Mahan Isfahan Basketball Club ([tpl]lang-fa|باشگاه بسکتبال فولاد ماهان سپاهان اصفهان[/tpl]) is an Iranian professional basketball club based in Esfahan, Iran. They compete in the Iranian Basketball Super League.
