[[Microfinance Insights]]

CATEGORIES: World magazines, Quarterly magazines, Publications established in 2006

Microfinance Insights is an international print magazine, published quarterly. The magazine features in-depth analyses and commentary on the Microfinance sector, updates on the latest trends, and profiles of global sector players. Each issue focuses on a theme from the microfinance sector.
The first issue of Microfinance Insights was released in October 2006. Till date, there have been 6 issues released. The magazine also has presence and media partnerships at various Microfinance related conferences around the world.
Microfinance Insights is a publication of Intellecap, a social business advisory firm.
