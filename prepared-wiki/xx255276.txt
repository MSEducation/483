[[Julie Hyzy]]

CATEGORIES: American mystery novelists, 21st-century American novelists, American women writers, Living people, Anthony Award winners, Barry Award winners

Julie Hyzy is an author of mystery fiction. She writes a series of books with sleuth 'Alex St James', news researcher, which combines mystery and adventure, and another about Olivia Paras, assistant chef at the White house, which sits in the sub genre 'culinary mysteries'.
