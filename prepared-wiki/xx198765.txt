[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Itapetininga]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1998, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Sorocaba, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Itapetininga ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Itapetiningensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Itapetininga in the Ecclesiastical province of Sorocaba in Brazil.
