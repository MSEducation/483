[[Joseph Lamb (politician)]]

CATEGORIES: 1873 births, 1949 deaths, Knights Bachelor, Conservative Party (UK) MPs, Members of the United Kingdom Parliament for English constituencies, UK MPs 1918–22, UK MPs 1922–23, UK MPs 1923–24, UK MPs 1924–29, UK MPs 1929–31, UK MPs 1931–35, UK MPs 1935–45

Sir Joseph Quinton Lamb (2 May 1873 – 20 November 1949) was a Conservative Party politician in the United Kingdom.   He was elected at the 1922 general election as Member of Parliament (MP) for Stone in Staffordshire, and held the seat until he stood down at the 1945 general election.
He was knighted in 1929.London Gazette Issue 33517 published on the 16 July 1929.
