[[Eslamiyeh]]

CATEGORIES: Cities in Iran, Cities in South Khorasan Province

Eslamiyeh ([tpl]lang-fa|اسلاميه[/tpl], also Romanized as Eslāmīyeh; formerly Bāghestān-e Ferdows and Beheshtābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3789880[/tpl] is a city in the Central District of Ferdows County, in South Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 5,167, in 1,515 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|29[/tpl] Islamiyeh is located in about [tpl]convert|3|km|mi[/tpl] northeast of Ferdows.
