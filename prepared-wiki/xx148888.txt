[[Nižná Sitnica]]

CATEGORIES: Villages and municipalities in Humenné District

Nižná Sitnica is a village and municipality in Humenné District in the Prešov Region of north-east Slovakia.

==History==

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1408.

==Geography==

The municipality lies at an altitude of 187 metres and covers an area of 9.172 km².
It has a population of about 370 people.
