[[Ken Mason (footballer)]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, New Zealand association footballers, New Zealand international footballers

Ken Mason is a former association football player who represented New Zealand at international level.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ultimatenzsoccer.com/NZRepSoccer/id1708.htm|title=A-International Appearances - Overall|author=|publisher=The Ultimate New Zealand Soccer Website|date=|accessdate=17 Oct 2009[/tpl]
Mason scored on his full All Whites debut in a 5-6 loss to South Africa on 28 June 1947[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ultimatenzsoccer.com/NZRepSoccer/id1559.htm|title=A-International Lineups|author=|publisher=The Ultimate New Zealand Soccer Website|date=|accessdate=17 Oct 2009[/tpl] and ended his international playing career with four A-international caps and one goal to his credit,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ultimatenzsoccer.com/NZRepSoccer/id1842.htm|title=A-International Scorers - Overall|author=|publisher=The Ultimate New Zealand Soccer Website|date=|accessdate=17 Oct 2009[/tpl] his final cap an appearance in a 1-4 loss to South Africa on 17 July 1947.
