Harold Beardmore, OBE, AKC  (13 November 1898 – 17 November 1968) was a Bishop in the Anglican church.

==Education==

He was educated at Berkhamsted School and King's College London.“Who was Who” 1897-2007 London, A & C Black, 1991  ISBN 978-0-19-954087-7

==Career==

He was a chaplain to the Royal Navy from 1927 to 1947.HMS Hood Association He was appointed Dean of Port of Spain, Trinidad in 1947, and Archdeacon of Basutoland in 1952.Crockford's Clerical Directory 1975-76 London: Oxford University Press, 1976 ISBN 0-19-200008-X He was appointed Bishop of St. Helena in 1960.Ascension Island Heritage Society
