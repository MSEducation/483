[[Gloucester Loop Line]]

CATEGORIES: Midland Railway, Rail transport in Gloucestershire, Rail transport in Worcestershire, Rail transport in Warwickshire, Railway lines in South West England

The Gloucester Loop Line was a railway line, operated by the Midland Railway which ran from [tpl]rws|Ashchurch[/tpl] to [tpl]rws|Barnt Green[/tpl] via [tpl]rws|Redditch[/tpl] and [tpl]rws|Evesham[/tpl], avoiding the main line via Bromsgrove. The line has been entirely dismantled, apart from the section from Redditch to Barnt Green, which forms the southern part of the Cross-City Line. The line was opened in 1866, services were withdrawn in 1962, the line was officially closed in 1964 and the track lifted in 1965.
