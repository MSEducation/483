==Origin==

Evans is of Welsh origin. In its anglicised form the name means "son of Evan".  Regarding its Welsh roots, it is a derivative of the name Ifan, a cognate of John.About.com; "Genealogy: Name Meaning and Origin"; http://genealogy.about.com/library/surnames/e/bl_name-EVANS.htm; retrieved August 9, 2006 In the Welsh language, the f produces the v sound; Ifan (Ivan) became Evan.
In the Welsh language the patronymic "ab Evan" resulted in the anglicized surname "Bevan", which is also common in Wales.
