[[Dezső Kolossváry]]

CATEGORIES: 1854 births, 1919 deaths, People from Veszprém, Hungarian politicians, Hungarian soldiers, Defence ministers of Hungary, Recipients of the Order of the Iron Crown, 2nd class

Dezső Kolossváry (1 May 1854 – 5 April 1919) was a Hungarian military officer and politician, who served as Minister of Defence in 1903.
