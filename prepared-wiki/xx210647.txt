[[Santiago Benítez]]

CATEGORIES: 1903 births, 1930 FIFA World Cup players, Paraguayan footballers, Club Olimpia footballers, Paraguay international footballers, 1997 deaths

Santiago Benítez (born 1903, death in 1997) was a Paraguayan footballer that played as a midfielder.

==Career==

Benítez was part of the Paraguay national football team that participated in the 1930 FIFA World Cup. FIFA profile During most of his career he played for Olimpia Asunción and was a key member of the three consecutive national championships obtained by Olimpia in 1927, 1928 and 1929.La Decada del 20
