[[Genome Compiler]]

CATEGORIES: Biotechnology, Synthetic biology

Genome Compiler is an advanced genetic design software produced by Genome Compiler Corporation,http://www.genomecompiler.com/ headed by CEO Omri Amirav-Drory and co-founders Nir Ben Moshe, Yogev Debbi and Roy Nevo.http://www.genomecompiler.com/about-us/management/ The software was prevalent in mainstream media in May 2013 due to its association with a Kickstarter initiative to create glowing plantshttp://www.kickstarter.com/projects/antonyevans/glowing-plants-natural-lighting-with-no-electricit with synthetic biology.
