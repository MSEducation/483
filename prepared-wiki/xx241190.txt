[[Hure Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Hure Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠦᠷᠢᠶ᠎ᠡ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Küriy-e qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=库伦旗 |t=庫倫旗 |p=Kùlún Qí[/tpl]) is a banner of southeastern Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China, bordering Liaoning province to the south. It is under the administration of Tongliao City, [tpl]convert|106|km[/tpl] to the north-northeast.
