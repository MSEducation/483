[[Le Prix du Danger]]

CATEGORIES: 1983 films, French science fiction films, 1980s science fiction films, French films, Yugoslav films, Films shot in Serbia, Films about television, Adaptations of works by Robert Sheckley

Le Prix du Danger (The price of the Danger ) is a 1983 French-Yugoslav science fiction movie, directed by Yves Boisset.  It is based on Robert Sheckley's The Prize of Peril, published in 1958.

==Story==

The most popular television game show is one where everyday men or women volunteer to be hunted. They are hunted down and killed by professional killers. If they survive they will get an enormous cash prize. But no one has managed to survive... so far.
Our hero, a family man, needs the money, so he takes part in the game. He proves out to be very good, but things are not what they seem.
