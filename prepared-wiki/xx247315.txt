[[Social Democratic Labour Youth Union]]

CATEGORIES: Youth wings of political parties in Romania, Youth wings of Social Democratic parties

The Social Democratic Labour Youth Union ([tpl]lang-ro|Uniunea tineretului muncitor socialdemocrat[/tpl]) was a youth organization in interbellum Romania and the youth of the Social Democratic Party of Romania.Kowalski, Werner. Geschichte der sozialistischen arbeiter-internationale: 1923 - 19. Berlin: Dt. Verl. d. Wissenschaften, 1985. p. 320 As of 1947, the organization had nearly 100,000 members.http://www.epa.oszk.hu/00400/00458/00062/2003honap2cikk38.htm
