[[Shi Tianzhe]]

CATEGORIES: Yuan dynasty politicians, Yuan dynasty generals

Shi Tianzhe （[tpl]lang-zh|史天澤[/tpl] 1202- 5 March 1275） is a famous general in the early period of Yuan dynasty of China. Later, he was promoted to the post of deputy prime minister and became the first Han minister of Yuan dynasty which was dominated by Mongols. He played a key role in early Yuan politics.
