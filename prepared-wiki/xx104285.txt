[[Antti Lange]]

CATEGORIES: 1941 births, Living people, People from Helsinki, 20th-century mathematicians, Finnish mathematicians, Finnish statisticians

Antti Lange (born December 11, 1941 in Helsinki, Finland) is a Finnish mathematician and 
statistician of the Finnish Meteorological Institute.  Previous employers included ECMWF, WMO, Helsinki University and the University of Jyväskylä.
