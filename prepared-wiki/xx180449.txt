[[Evgeny Pchelov]]

CATEGORIES: Historians of Russia, 1971 births, Living people, Russian genealogists

Eugeny Vladimirovich Pchelov ([tpl]lang-ru|Евге́ний Влади́мирович Пчело́в[/tpl]) (born September 1, 1971, Moscow) is a Russian specialist in history, heraldry and genealogy. He has published several monographs on the history, such as Rurikides. History of dynasty (2001), Romanov. History of dynasty (2001), Old Russian princely genealogy (2001), State symbols of Russia: Coat of Arms, Flag, Anthem (2007) and others.
