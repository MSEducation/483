[[B. M. Higginson, Jr.]]

CATEGORIES: American sport shooters, Trap and double trap shooters

B. M. Higginson, Jr. was the national clay pigeon shooting champion on 1912. He was a Harvard University graduate and a member of the New York Athletic Club. Coming in second in 1912 was the 1905 champion John H. Hendrickson.[tpl]cite news |author= |coauthors= |title=Higginson Wins at Amateur Shooting. New York A.C. Higginson, Jr., a former Harvard gunner, wearing the colors of the New York A.C., won the seventh annual National championship at clay birds over the Travers Island traps of the New York A.C. yesterday.
