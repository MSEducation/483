[[Melchior Schjelderup Olsson Fuhr]]

CATEGORIES: 1790 births, 1869 deaths, Members of the Parliament of Norway, Mayors of places in Sogn og Fjordane

Melchior Schjelderup Olsson Fuhr (1790–1869) was a Norwegian politician.
He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament in 1836 and 1842, representing the rural constituency of Nordre Bergenhus Amt (today named Sogn og Fjordane). He worked as a farmer.Melchior Schjelderup Olsen Fuhr — Norwegian Social Science Data Services (NSD) He was also a deputy representative in 1839.
He hailed from Luster, and became the first mayor of Luster in 1837.NRK Sogn og Fjordane County Encyclopedia
