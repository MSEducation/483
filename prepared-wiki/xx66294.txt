[[Joaquín Abarca]]

CATEGORIES: 1780 births, 1844 deaths, People from Huesca, Bishops of León, Carlists, Spanish politicians, 19th-century Spanish people, 19th-century Roman Catholic bishops

Joaquin Abarca (1780, Huesca - 1844) Spanish prelate. Born in Aragon in 1780 he went on to become Bishop of León in 1824.
During the 1833 civil war he was one of the leaders of the Carlist party.
