[[Slovenia at the 2008 Summer Paralympics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 2008 Summer Paralympics, Slovenia at the Paralympics, 2008 in Slovenian sport

Slovenia competed at the 2008 Summer Paralympics in Beijing. The country was represented by thirty athletes competing in seven sports. The delegation was Slovenia's largest in its history as an independent country."Paralympics Team Departs for Beijing", Slovenian government website, August 29, 2008
