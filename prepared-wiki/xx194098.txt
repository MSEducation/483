[[WHET]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Illinois, Country radio stations in the United States

WHET (97.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a classic country format. Licensed to West Frankfort, Illinois, USA, the station serves the Marion-Carbondale (IL) area. The station is currently owned by Withers Broadcasting and the broadcast license held by Withers Broadcasting of Southern Illinois, LLC. WHET features programming from Citadel Media and Westwood One. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=WHET |title=WHET Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==History==

The station was assigned call sign WFRX-FM on July 24, 1979. On October 1, 1996, the station changed its call sign to WQUL and on August 18, 2009, to the current WHET. [tpl]cite web|url=http://licensing.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=53978&Callsign=WHET |title=WHET Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
On March 9, 2011, WHET changed their format to classic country, branded as "US 97.7".
