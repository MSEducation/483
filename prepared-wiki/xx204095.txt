[[WWFC-LP]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Alabama, Low-power FM radio stations in Alabama, Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 2004, Jackson County, Alabama, 3ABN radio stations

WWFC-LP (99.9 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve Bryant, Alabama.  The station is owned by Floral Crest Broadcasting. It airs a Christian radio format.[tpl]cite web |work=Alabama Broadcast Media Page |title=Alabama LPFM & Translators |url=http://bellsouthpwp2.net/c/r/crackedlcd/almediapage/lpfm.html |accessdate=2008-05-21[/tpl][tpl]cite web |title=Station Information Profile |work=Arbitron |url=http://www.arbitron.com/[/tpl] The station derives a portion of its programming as an affiliate of the Three Angels Broadcasting Network.[tpl]cite web |work=3ABN Radio Network |title=3ABN Radio Stations |url=http://www.3abnradio.org/radio_stations.cfm[/tpl]
The station was assigned the WWFC-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on December 20, 2002.[tpl]cite web |title=Call Sign History |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=134944&Callsign=WWFC-LP |work=FCC Media Bureau CDBS Public Access Database[/tpl]
