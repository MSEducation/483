The XFL was a professional American football league, founded by World Wrestling Federation owner Vince McMahon. The XFL was intended to be a major professional sports league complement to the offseason of the National Football League, but was unable to find an audience and ceased operation after its debut season in 2001.http://www.sportinglife360.com/index.php/why-the-xfl-failed-19101/http://www.forbes.com/2001/05/11/0511topnews.html
