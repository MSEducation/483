[[St. Edmund High School (Eunice, Louisiana)]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic secondary schools in Louisiana, Schools in Acadia Parish, Louisiana, Educational institutions established in 1911, Schools in St. Landry Parish, Louisiana

St. Edmund High School is a private, Roman Catholic high school in Eunice, Louisiana.  It is located in the Roman Catholic Diocese of Lafayette.

==Background==

St.Edmund High School,also known as St.Eds, was established in 1911. It is home of the St.Eds Blue Jays. Grades 9-12.
