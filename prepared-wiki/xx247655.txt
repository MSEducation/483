[[Anthurium andraeanum]]

CATEGORIES: Anthurium

Anthurium andraeanum is a species of flowering plant in the Araceae family. It is one of the plants listed in the NASA Clean Air StudyPlants "Clean" Air Inside Our Homes (kilde NASA)[tpl]cite web|url=http://ntrs.nasa.gov/archive/nasa/casi.ntrs.nasa.gov/19860066312_1986066312.pdf|title=Foliage Plants for Removing Indoor Air Pollutants from Energy-efficient Homes|author=B. C. Wolverton, Rebecca C. McDonald, and E. A. Watkins, Jr|accessdate=2009-12-10[/tpl], http://www.sjodinweb.com/house/foliage_air.pdf (alternate link for 'Foliage Plants for Removing Indoor Air Pollutants from Energy-efficient Homes') as effective in removing formaldehyde, xylene, toluene, and ammonia from the air.
