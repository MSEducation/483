[[Corsicana Lemonade]]

CATEGORIES: Albums produced by Jeff Tweedy, 2013 albums, White Denim albums

Corsicana Lemonade is the sixth full-length studio album by Austin, Texas band White Denim.  The album was released on October 29, 2013 by Downtown Records.

==Release==

===Singles===

==Critical reception==

The album received mostly positive reviews.

==Commercial performance==

The album debuted at number four on the Billboard Heatseekers Albums chart. The album sold more than 3,000 copies in its first week. The album also charted at #147 on Billboard 200.
