[[Sakaigawa Dam]]

CATEGORIES: Dams in Gifu Prefecture, Dams completed in 1993, Dams on the Shō River, Hydroelectric power stations in Japan

[tpl]nihongo|Sakaigawa Dam|境川ダム|Sakaigawa damu[/tpl] is a dam in Nanto, Toyama Prefecture and Shirakawa, Gifu Prefecture, Japan.[tpl]cite web|title=Hydropower & Dams in South and East Asia|url=http://www.hydropower-dams.com/d/d_S&East_Asia_Map.pdf|publisher=Hydropower and Dams|accessdate=3 October 2010|page=9[/tpl] It is named for the Sakaigawa River (a tributary of the Shō River), upon which it is built. The dam has an associated 27 MW hydroelectric power station about [tpl]Convert|2.9|km|mi|abbr=on[/tpl] downstream. It was commissioned in June 1993.[tpl]cite web|title=Kansai Electric Power Sakaigawa power plant|url=http://www.suiryoku.com/gallery/toyama/sakaigaw/sakaigaw.html|publisher=Suiryoku|accessdate=15 July 2013|language=Japanese[/tpl]
