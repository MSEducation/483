[[Paul Straney]]

CATEGORIES: Association footballers from Northern Ireland, Glentoran F.C. players, Liverpool F.C. players, Stoke City F.C. players, People from Downpatrick, Living people, 1975 births

Paul Straney (born 7 October 1975) is a goalkeeper with Irish League side Cliftonville F.C..[tpl]cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport2/hi/football/irish/4751376.stm|title=Cliftonville 1-0 Loughgall|date=25 February 2006|publisher=BBC News|accessdate=12 October 2010[/tpl] He joined the Reds in June 2001, making his debut in the Intertoto Cup clash with Moldovan side Tiligul Tiraspol.
Born in Downpatrick, Straney also played for Newry City F.C., Glenavon F.C., Glentoran F.C., and English sides Telford United F.C., Stoke City, Liverpool FC. Straney was a regular in the Cliftonville side which won the 2003 CIS Cup.
