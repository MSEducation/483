[[Buciumi, Sălaj]]

CATEGORIES: Communes in Sălaj County

Buciumi ([tpl]lang-hu|Vármező[/tpl]) is a commune located in Sălaj County, Romania. It is composed of six villages: Bodia (Szilágybogya), Bogdana (Kásapatak), Buciumi, Huta (Csákyújfalu), Răstolţ (Nagyrajtolc) and Sângeorgiu de Meseş (Meszesszentgyörgy).
