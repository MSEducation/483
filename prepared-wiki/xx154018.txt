[[Gustaf Blomgren]]

Gustaf Blomgren (December 24, 1887 â€“ July 25, 1956) was a Swedish diver who competed in the 1912 Summer Olympics and in the 1920 Summer Olympics.
In 1912 he won the bronze medal in the 10 metre platform competition.
Eight years later at the Antwerp Games he finished fourth in the 3 metre springboard event as well as in the 10 metre platform competition.
