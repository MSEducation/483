[[John Quigley (author)]]

CATEGORIES: Scottish writers, 1925 births, Living people

John Quigley (born 1925)http://catalogue.nla.gov.au/Record/864106 is a Scottish author. His novel King's Royal (1975) uses his extensive experience in the liquor trade to produce a historical about the invention of blended whisky in mid-nineteenth century Glasgow. A 1983 BBC television series was based on the novel.http://www.answers.com/topic/king-s-royal-1977-tv-series, http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1278853/ Queen's Royal is a sequel.
