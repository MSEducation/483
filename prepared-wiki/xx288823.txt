[[Hirabad, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Hirabad ([tpl]lang-fa|هيراباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Hīrābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3066621|Hirabad[/tpl] is a village in Khararud Rural District, in the Central District of Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 323, in 59 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
