Antoniu Buci (born January 21, 1990 in Cluj-Napoca, Cluj) is a Romanian weightlifter.
At the 2006 World Championships he ranked 20th in the 56 kg category, with a total of 235 kg.[tpl]cite web|title=BUCI Antoniu|publisher=International Weightlifting Federation|url=http://www.iwf.net/results/results_athlete_e.php?compid=863[/tpl]
At the 2007 World Championships he ranked 22nd in the 62 kg category, with a total of 272 kg.[tpl]cite web|title=BUCI Victor Antoniu|publisher=International Weightlifting Federation|url=http://www.iwf.net/results/results_athlete_e.php?compid=1654[/tpl]
He competed in Weightlifting at the 2008 Summer Olympics in the 62 kg division finishing fourth, with 295 kg,[tpl]cite web|title=BUCI Antoniu|publisher=International Weightlifting Federation|url=http://www.iwf.net/results/results_athlete_e.php?compid=2575[/tpl] beating his previous personal best by 22 kg.
Buci recently won the silver medal at the 2011 European Weightlifting Championships held in Kazan, Russia.http://www.ironmind.com/ironmind/opencms/Articles/2011/Apr/European_Weightlifting_Championships-Turkish_Gold.html
He is 5 ft 3 inches tall and weighs 137 lb.
