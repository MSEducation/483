[[KJCX-LP]]

CATEGORIES: Television stations in Montana, Channel 40 low-power TV stations in the United States, Trinity Broadcasting network affiliates, Television channels and stations established in 2004

KJCX-LP UHF analog channel 40 and KHJT-LP analog channel 54 are low-power television stations licensed to Bozeman, Montana. They are repeaters that broadcasts programming from the Trinity Broadcasting Network, via satellite. KHJT-LP has a construction permit to move to channel 30 for digital service.
