[[Vranji Vrh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Municipality of Šentilj

Vranji Vrh ([tpl]IPA-sl|ˈwraːnji ˈvərx|pron[/tpl]) is a dispersed settlement in the Slovene Hills ([tpl]lang-sl|Slovenske gorice[/tpl]) southwest of Sladki Vrh in the Municipality of Šentilj in northeastern Slovenia.Šentilj municipal site
