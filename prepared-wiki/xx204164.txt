[[Pseudogynoxys cummingii]]

CATEGORIES: Pseudogynoxys

Pseudogynoxys cummingii is a species of the genus Pseudogynoxys and family Asteraceae.    According to the photographer of this photograph, it is also one of three species of Pseudogynoxys that are known in cultivation.[ref]
</ref>
However, Missouri Botanical Gardens claims it is a synonym of Pseudogynoxys benthamii,  which Spanish Wikipedia claims is on the IUCN Red List but a search there does not support this.[ref]
