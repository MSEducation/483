[[Nordbotn Church]]

CATEGORIES: Hitra, Churches in Sør-Trøndelag

Nordbotn church ([tpl]lang-no|Nordbotn kirke[/tpl]) is a church in the municipality of Hitra in Sør-Trøndelag county, Norway.  The church is located in the village of Nordbotn on the island of Fjellværsøya.  The church was built in 1900.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.orkdalsmenighetene.no/hitra/norbotn_kirke.htm|title=Nordbotn kirke|language=Norwegian|publisher=Orkdals prosti|accessdate=2010-12-30[/tpl]
