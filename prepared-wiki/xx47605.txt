Many MiG-15s are on display throughout the world. In addition, they are becoming increasingly common as private sport planes and warbirds. According to the FAA, there were 43 privately owned MiG-15s in the US in 2011, including Chinese and Polish derivatives. The first of which is owned by aviator and aerobatic flyer, Paul T.
