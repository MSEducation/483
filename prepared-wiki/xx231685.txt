[[Huta Pass]]

CATEGORIES: Mountain passes of Romania

Huta Pass (el. 587 m.) is a pass in the northern region of Romania. The pass connects the districts of Maramureş and Satu Mare. 
There is a restaurant on the pass.[tpl]citation needed|date=November 2011[/tpl]
