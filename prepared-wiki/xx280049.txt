[[Matúš Bero]]

CATEGORIES: 1995 births, Living people, Slovak footballers, Association football defenders, FK AS Trenčín players, Slovak First League players

Matúš Bero (born 6 September 1995) is a Slovak football defender who currently plays for the Corgoň Liga club AS Trenčín.

==Club career==

He made his professional debut for AS Trenčín senior side on 21 July 2013 in the Corgoň Liga match against FC Spartak Trnava.SPARTAK TRNAVA VS. TRENČÍN  2 - 1 21.07.2013, soccerway.com
