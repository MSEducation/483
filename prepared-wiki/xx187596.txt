[[Alf Bentley (footballer born 1931)]]

CATEGORIES: 1931 births, 1996 deaths, English footballers, Association football goalkeepers, Gillingham F.C. players, Coventry City F.C. players, Margate F.C. players, Tunbridge Wells F.C. players

Alfred "Alf" Bentley (28 October 1931 – 15 October 1996) was an English professional association football goalkeeper.
He started his career with Snowdown Colliery Welfare before moving into the Football League with Coventry City in 1955–56.  After a spell with Margate he returned to league football in 1958 with Gillingham, making 42 appearances in the Football League.  In 1962 he joined Tunbridge Wells United before going on to Hastings United, finishing his playing career back at Snowdown in season 1966/67[tpl]cite book |   first=Roger|last= Triggs |   title=The Men Who Made Gillingham Football Club|   publisher=Tempus Publishing Ltd |   year=2001|   isbn=0-7524-2243-X|pages=57–58[/tpl]
After retiring from playing Bentley was manager of Canterbury City for a number of years.
