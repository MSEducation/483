[[Alex DeLaszlo]]

CATEGORIES: American harmonica players, American saxophonists, American male singers, Living people, King Missile members, Place of birth missing (living people), Year of birth missing (living people)

Alex DeLaszlo is an American musician. He contributed bass harmonica, harmonica, saxophone, and backing vocals to King Missile (Dog Fly Religion), the first incarnation of avant-garde band King Missile.
