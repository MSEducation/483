Martin Nikolai Røymark (born November 10, 1986) is a Norwegian professional ice hockey winger, currently playing for Färjestad BK in the Swedish Elitserien.

==Playing career==

In the 2009–10 Elitserien season, he played 55 matches for Frölunda, scoring five goals and having four assists. On April 12th, Frölunda's General Manager, Kent Norberg, stated that they needed to reduce the number of forwards in the club.
