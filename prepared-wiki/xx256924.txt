[[Henry Pahl House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses completed in 1880, Greek Revival architecture in Iowa, Houses in Davenport, Iowa, Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Iowa, National Register of Historic Places in Scott County, Iowa

The Henry Pahl House is located in the West End of Davenport, Iowa, United States.  The Greek Revival style residence has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since 1983.
