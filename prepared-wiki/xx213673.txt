[[Hemistola chrysoprasaria]]

CATEGORIES: Hemitheini, Moths of Europe, Insects of Germany, Insects of Estonia, Insects of Sweden, Insects of Romania, Insects of Poland, Insects of Latvia, Insects of Albania, Insects of Andorra, Insects of Belgium, Insects of Belarus, Insects of Bosnia and Herzegovina, Insects of Hungary, Insects of Greece, Insects of Bulgaria, Insects of Turkey, Insects of Italy

The Small Emerald (Hemistola chrysoprasaria) is a moth of the family Geometridae. The species can be found in Europe.
The wingspan is 28–32 mm. The moths fly in one generation from June to August. [tpl]ref|flight_season[/tpl].
The larva feed on Clematis vitalba.
