[[Kowakidani Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Kanagawa Prefecture, Hakone, Kanagawa, 1919 establishments in Japan

[tpl]nihongo|Kowakidani Station|小涌谷駅|Kowakidani-eki[/tpl] is a railway station on the Hakone Tozan Line located in Hakone, Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan. It is 13.4 rail kilometers from the line’s terminus at Odawara Station.

==History==

Kowakidani Station was opened on June 1, 1919.
