Professor Andrew William Goudie CB is a Visiting Professor at the University of Strathclyde.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.strath.ac.uk/sippi/ourteam/andrewgoudie/|title=Andrew Goudie|publisher=University of Strathclyde|accessdate=15 April 2014[/tpl]
