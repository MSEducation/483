[[Jenny Hutchinson]]

CATEGORIES: British television actresses, Living people, Year of birth missing (living people)

Jenny Hutchinson was the original actress to play Fizz in the children's show Tweenies. Among her other roles, she has played Meedea in The Baaas, the joker in Series 1 of Megamaths and Florrie in Fimbles. In addition to acting, she also operates the Second Skin Agency which represents puppeteers, actors/actresses and voicers.
