[[Epeigeus]]

CATEGORIES: Characters in the Iliad, People of the Trojan War, Myrmidons

Epeigeus is a Greek hero in the Iliad. He was a Myrmidon and son of Agacles, who, having killed his father, was obliged to flee from Budeion. He took refuge in the house of Peleus who sent him with Achilles to Troy, where he was killed by Hector.Hom. Il. xvi. 570. (cited by Schmitz)
A Trojan asteroid, 5259 Epeigeus, has been named after him.
