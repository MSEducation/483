[[Alcohol licensing laws of Ireland]]

CATEGORIES: Alcohol law by country

The alcohol licensing laws of Ireland regulate the sale and consumption of alcohol. The legislation controlling licensing regulations is the Intoxicating Liquor Act 2008, Intoxicating Liquor Act 2003, Intoxicating Liquor Act 2000, the Licensing Act, 1872 and the Criminal Justice (Public Order) Act 1994.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/justice/criminal_law/criminal_offences/alcohol_and_the_law.html|title=Alcohol and the law|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl]
