[[Doug Barrault]]

CATEGORIES: 1970 births, Canadian ice hockey right wingers, Florida Panthers players, Ice hockey people from British Columbia, Living people, Minnesota North Stars draft picks, Minnesota North Stars players, Seattle Thunderbirds players, People from the Columbia-Shuswap Regional District

Douglas Barrault (born April 21, 1970 in Golden, British Columbia) is a retired ice hockey right winger. He played in four National Hockey League games with the Minnesota North Stars and Florida Panthers.
