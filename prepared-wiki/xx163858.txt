Loïc Lumbilla–Kandja (born 12 May 1987) is a French footballer. Lumbilla is a versatile player who can play on both left and right wings and as striker.

==Career==

Lumbilla-Kandja started his career at Paris FC. In July 2004, along with Tijani Belaid, they were signed for Internazionale.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.inter.it/aas/news/reader?L=en&N=14540|title= TWO YOUNGSTERS SIGN FOR INTER |date=4 July 2004|accessdate=9 January 2010|publisher=inter.it[/tpl] 
In the next season he played for Inter Primavera team and played numbers of friendlies for the first team.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.inter.it/aas/news/reader?L=en&N=19042|title= CRUZ GOAL DECIDES SUZZARA FRIENDLY |date=7 June 2005|accessdate=9 January 2010|publisher=inter.it[/tpl][tpl]cite news|url=http://www.inter.it/aas/news/reader?L=en&N=19488|title= INTER BEAT CAGLIARI ON PENALTIES |date=27 July 2005|accessdate=9 January 2010|publisher=inter.it[/tpl][tpl]cite news|url=http://www.inter.it/aas/news/reader?L=en&N=19516|title= INTER BEATEN 4–1 BY TREVISO |date=29 July 2005|accessdate=9 January 2010|publisher=inter.it[/tpl][tpl]cite news|url=http://www.inter.it/aas/news/reader?L=en&N=20436|title= VINOTINTO BEAT INTER 1–0 |date=17 October 2005|accessdate=9 January 2010|publisher=inter.it[/tpl]
In the 2006–07 season, he left on loan to Legnano of Serie C2, where he won the championshio.
He then left for FC Locarno of Swiss Challenge League,[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.inter.it/aas/news/reader?L=en&N=26943|title= New clubs for three academy players |date=30 July 2007|accessdate=9 January 2010|publisher=inter.it[/tpl] but he just played 9 matches.
In February 2009, he signed a contract with Écija Balompié of Spanish Segunda División B.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.ecijabalompie.com/index1.php?id=1143&num=2|title=Loic Lumbilla presentado con el Ecija Balompié SAD.|date=3 February 2009|accessdate=9 January 2010|publisher=Écija Balompié|language=Spanish[/tpl]
In August 2009, he signed a two-year contract with VfL Wolfsburg, rejoining former team-mate Obafemi Martins.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.vfl-wolfsburg.de/55012+M5bc7d01f639.html|title=U23: Zwei Neuzugänge|date=14 August 2009|accessdate=9 January 2010|publisher=VfL Wolfsburg|language=German[/tpl]
In January 2012 he signed a contract with Hamrun Spartans in Malta where he made his debut scoring a hat-trick.
