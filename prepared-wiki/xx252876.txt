[[Pontian shemaya]]

CATEGORIES: Alburnus, Animals described in 2007, Freshwater fish, Freshwater fish of Europe, Fish of Ukraine

Pontian shemaya (Alburnus sarmaticus) is a species of ray-finned fish in the genus Alburnus. Widspread in European rivers: Southern Bug, Dnieper, Danube (in Romania, Ukraine and Bulgaria); River Kolpa, an upper tributary to the River Sava in Croatia and Slovenia.Freyhof, J. and M. Kottelat, 2007. Review of the Alburnus mento species group with description of two new species (Teleostei: Cyprinidae). Ichthyol. Explor. Freshwat. 18(3):213-225. Almost extirpated in Danube, to be probably survived only in River Kolpa.Kottelat, M. and J. Freyhof, 2007. Handbook of European freshwater fishes. Publications Kottelat, Cornol, Switzerland. 646 p.
