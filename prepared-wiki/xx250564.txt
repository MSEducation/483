[[Almost but Not Quite There]]

CATEGORIES: Status Quo (band) songs, 1967 singles, Songs written by Francis Rossi, Song recordings produced by John Schroeder (musician), 1967 songs

"Almost But Not Quite There" is a single released by the British Rock band The Traffic Jam in 1967.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.statusquo.co.uk/records/singles1.htm |title=Status Quo discography |publisher=statusquo.co.uk |accessdate=2010-01-04[/tpl] It was the only single released under this band name between using the names The Spectres, The Traffic and The Status Quo.
The BBC banned the song from radio due to the lyrical content being about a woman's sexual fulfillment.
