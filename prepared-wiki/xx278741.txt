[[Chaetodiadema africanum]]

CATEGORIES: Echinoidea, Animals described in 1924

Chaetodiadema africanum is a species of sea urchins of the Family Diadematidae. Their armour is covered with spines. Chaetodiadema africanum was first scientifically described in 1924 by Hubert Lyman Clark.Kroh, A. (2010). Chaetodiadema africanum (Clark, 1924). In: Kroh, A. & Mooi, R. (2010) World Echinoidea Database. at the World Register of Marine Species.
