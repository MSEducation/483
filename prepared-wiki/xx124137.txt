[[Def Jux Presents 3]]

CATEGORIES: Albums produced by Aesop Rock, 2004 compilation albums, Definitive Jux compilation albums, Hip hop compilation albums, Record label compilation albums, English-language compilation albums, Sequel albums, Albums produced by El-P, East Coast hip hop compilation albums

Def Jux Presents 3 is a compilation album for the Definitive Jux independent record label. For fans, it was a chance for the already-familiar Def Jukies to show off their material. For new listeners, it was a great introduction to the Def Jux label roster.
