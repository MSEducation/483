[[2006 Kremlin Cup]]

CATEGORIES: Kremlin Cup, 2006 ATP Tour, 2006 WTA Tour, 2006 in Russia

The 2006 Kremlin Cup was a tennis tournament played on indoor hard courts. It was the 17th edition of the Kremlin Cup, and was part of the International Series of the 2006 ATP Tour, and of the Tier I Series of the 2006 WTA Tour. It took place at the Olympic Stadium in Moscow, Russia from October 9 through October 15.
