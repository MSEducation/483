Jeff Seymour is an American-Canadian actor, best known for his roles in television series such as Show Me Yours, Jeff Ltd., The Eleventh Hour and Being Erica.
Born in Washington, D.C. and raised in Virginia,Jeff Seymour at Northern Stars. Seymour had minor roles in television series such as Knots Landing, Hill Street Blues and Bosom Buddies in the early 1980s. He later moved to Vancouver, British Columbia, where he made guest appearances in numerous Canadian-produced series such as First Wave, Level 9, Da Vinci's Inquest, The Immortal, Smallville, Witchblade, Mutant X and Sue Thomas F.B.Eye, before landing his first major recurring role as Kamil Azizi in The Eleventh Hour.
He was a Gemini Award nominee for Best Performance by an Actor in a Continuing Leading Dramatic Role for all three of his seasons on The Eleventh Hour, winning the award at the 2003 Gemini Awards.
