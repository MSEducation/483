[[3NE]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Victoria

3NE is a radio station based in Wangaratta, Victoria, Australia. 3NE officially opened on 27 March 1954.[tpl]cite news |url=http://nla.gov.au/nla.news-article65497930 |title=New Radio Station 3NE Officiall? Opened at Wangaratta. |newspaper=Benalla Ensign (Vic. : 1938 - 1954) |location=Vic. |date=1 April 1954 |accessdate=14 April 2014 |page=6 |publisher=National Library of Australia[/tpl] 3NE broadcasts on the medium wave radio band at a frequency of 1566 kHz, to the north-eastern area of Victoria, and southern New South Wales.[tpl]cite book|title=Radio and television broadcasting stations|date=April 2013|publisher=Australian Communications and Media Authority|page=8|url=http://data.gov.au/storage/f/2013-05-12T174050/tmp5kSbV2stations_book_electronic_edition.pdf[/tpl]
