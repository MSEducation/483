[[1995–96 Watford F.C. season]]

CATEGORIES: Watford F.C. seasons, English football clubs 1995–96 season

During the 1995–96 English football season, Watford F.C. competed in the Football League First Division.

==Season summary==

In Glenn Roeder's third season as manager, Watford struggled and Roeder was sacked in February with the club bottom of the table.[tpl]cite web|title=Glenn Roeder factfile|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/football/2003/apr/29/newsstory.sport9|publisher=The Guardian|accessdate=23 September 2011[/tpl] Graham Taylor returned to Watford as Director of Football in February 1996, with former player Kenny Jackett as head coach, but was unable to stop the club from sliding into Division Two.
