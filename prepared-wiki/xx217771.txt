[[Qıvraq]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Kangarli District

Qıvraq (also, Givrakh, Kivrag, Kivrakh, Kyvrak, and Kyvrakh) is a village and municipality in, and is the administrative center of, the Kangarli Rayon of Nakhchivan, Azerbaijan.[tpl]citation needed|date=October 2010[/tpl]  It has a population of 4,444.
