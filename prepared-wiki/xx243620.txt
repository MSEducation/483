[[Alireza Koushk Jalali]]

CATEGORIES: 1958 births, Living people, Iranian theatre directors, Iranian dramatists and playwrights

Alireza Koushk Jalali (March 21, 1958, Tehran, Iran)
([tpl]lang-fa|علیرضا کوشک جلالی[/tpl]) is an Iranian theatre director and playwright.
Currently he resides in Cologne, Germany, where he also made his first German production in 1986.
