[[Salgótarjáni BTC]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Hungary, Association football clubs established in 1920, 1920 establishments in Hungary

Salgótarjáni Barátok Torna Club is a Hungarian football club based in Salgótarján and founded in 1920.
Their best performance in the Hungarian football championship was a third place during the 1971-1972 season, earning them a participation to the 1972-73 UEFA Cup where they were eliminated in the first round to AEK Athens.
