[[District 11 (Zurich)]]

CATEGORIES: District 11 of Zürich, Districts of Zürich

District 11 is the most northern and the most populous district in the Swiss city of Zurich.
The district comprises the quarters Affoltern, Oerlikon and Seebach. All entities were formerly municipalities of their own, but were incorporated into Zurich in 1934.
