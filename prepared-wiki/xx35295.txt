[[Murray River National Park]]

CATEGORIES: National parks of South Australia, Protected areas established in 1991, 1991 establishments in Australia, Important Bird Areas of South Australia

Murray River is a national park in South Australia (Australia), 186 km east of Adelaide.  It includes part of the 12,200 km2 Riverland Mallee Important Bird Area, identified as such by BirdLife International for its importance in the conservation of mallee birds and their habitats.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.birdata.com.au/iba.vm |title=IBA: Riverland Mallee |accessdate=2011-09-29 |work= Birdata |first= |last= |publisher=Birds Australia |date= [/tpl]
