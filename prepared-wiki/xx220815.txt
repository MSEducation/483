[[Thomas Bredahl]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 births, Living people, Danish rock singers, Danish rock guitarists, Volbeat members, English-language singers of Denmark

Thomas Bredahl (born 2 November 1980) is a Danish guitarist. He is not related to the Danish boxer Johnny Bredahl.  He is best known as the former lead guitarist of Volbeat and is currently the guitarist of Gob Squad. Bredahl joined Volbeat in early 2007, and the band announced his departure on 28 November 2011. He was the successor of Franz Gottschalk, who was fired by Volbeat after the recording of their second album Rock the Rebel / Metal the Devil was completed. Bredahl uses a Gibson Les Paul guitar, an ESP Eclipse guitar and, from 2011, a Fender Blacktop Telecaster
