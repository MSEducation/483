[[Julius Christopher Hammer]]

CATEGORIES: 1798 births, 1877 deaths, Members of the Parliament of Norway, Hordaland politicians, Norwegian priests

Julius Christopher Hammer (14 May 1798 - 31 December 1877) was a Norwegian priest and politician.
He was born in Bergen. In April 1831 he married Caroline Agnete Neumann.Genealogy
He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament in 1842, representing the constituency of Søndre Bergenhus Amt. He only served one term.Julius Christopher Hammer — Norwegian Social Science Data Services (NSD) At that time he worked as a vicar in Haus.
