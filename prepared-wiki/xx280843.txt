[[Black Sea (film)]]

CATEGORIES: Upcoming films, English-language films, Films directed by Kevin Macdonald, 2010s adventure films, 2010s thriller films, Treasure hunt films, Film4 Productions films, Focus Features films, British films

Black Sea is an upcoming British adventure thriller film directed by Kevin Macdonald and written by Dennis Kelly. The film stars Jude Law, Scoot McNairy, Karl Davies and Konstantin Khabensky.

==Production==

On February 14, 2012 news confirmed that director Kevin Macdonald is working with writer Dennis Kelly on his new film project Black Sea.[tpl]cite news|title=Kevin Macdonald travels to Black Sea and Eastern Europe|url=http://www.screendaily.com/news/production/kevin-macdonald-travels-to-black-sea-and-eastern-europe/5038031.article|accessdate=10 August 2013|newspaper=screendaily.com|date=14 February 2012[/tpl] On April 25, 2013 Focus Features acquired the international distribution rights to the film for Cannes Film Festival.[tpl]cite news|title=Focus Acquires Hot Cannes Title ‘Black Sea’ With Kevin Macdonald Directing Jude Law|url=http://www.deadline.com/2013/04/focus-acquires-hot-cannes-title-black-sea-with-kevin-macdonald-directing-jude-law/|accessdate=10 August 2013|newspaper=deadline.com|date=25 April 2013[/tpl]

===Casting===

On April 25, 2013 Jude Law joined the cast of Black Sea as a lead actor, he will play the role of an unemployed submarine captain hired by some shady folks to find a sunken treasure.[tpl]cite news|title=Jude Law Joins Kevin Macdonald's Submarine Thriller 'Black Sea'|url=http://blogs.indiewire.com/theplaylist/jude-law-joins-kevin-macdonalds-submarine-thriller-black-sea-20130425|accessdate=10 August 2013|newspaper=indiewire.com|date=25 April 2013[/tpl] Scoot McNairy also joined the cast of the film with Law on June 17, 2013, he'll play an American who convinces the captain to undertake a mission.[tpl]cite news|title=Scoot McNairy Shores Up ‘Black Sea’ Role|url=http://www.deadline.com/2013/06/scoot-mcnairy-shores-up-black-sea-role/|accessdate=10 August 2013|newspaper=deadline.com|date=17 June 2013[/tpl]
