[[KBLD]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Washington (state), Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 1994

KBLD (91.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Contemporary Christian format.  Licensed to Kennewick, Washington, USA, the station serves the Tri-Cities area.  The station is currently owned by CSN International. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KBLD |title=KBLD Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==History==

The station was assigned the call sign KAIS on 1994-09-30.  On 1994-11-14, the station changed its call sign to the current KBLD.
