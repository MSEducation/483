[[Hinduism in Brunei]]

CATEGORIES: Hinduism by country, Hinduism in Asia, Religion in Brunei

Almost the entire Hindu community in Brunei is made up of people of Indian origin. The approximate size is a few thousands. There are two Hindu temples in Brunei. According to the 2001 census, 124 of the Hindus are citizens and another 91 are permanent residents. The rest are non-citizens. IRF 2006 Report
