[[Eleutherius of Rocca d'Arce]]

CATEGORIES: Christian saints in unknown century, English saints, Year of death missing, Year of birth unknown

Saint Eleutherius of Rocca d'Arce, was, according to tradition, an English pilgrim who died at Rocca d'Arce and was afterwards venerated as a saint. Tradition also makes him a brother of Saints Grimwald[tpl]dn|date=July 2013[/tpl] and Fulk.[tpl]dn|date=July 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web|work=Saints Index|url=http://saints.sqpn.com/sainte4k.htm|title=Saint Eleutherius of Rocca d'Arce[/tpl]
