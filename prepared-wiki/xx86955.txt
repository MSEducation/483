[[Antoaneta Stefanova]]

CATEGORIES: 1979 births, Living people, Chess grandmasters, Chess woman grandmasters, Women's World Chess Champions, World Youth Chess Champions, Bulgarian chess players, Sportspeople from Sofia, European Chess Champions

Antoaneta Stefanova ([tpl]lang-bg| Антоанета Стефанова[/tpl]; born April 19, 1979) is a Bulgarian chess grandmaster, and a former Women's World Chess Champion. She became the tenth holder of that title in 2004 in a 64-player knockout tournament held in Elista, Kalmykia under the auspices of FIDE. 
Stefanova was born in Sofia, Bulgaria. Her passion for chess was first inspired when she was just four years old, receiving her first lessons from her father, Andon Stefanov, a designing artist.
