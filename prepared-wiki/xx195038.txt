[[Abdón Saavedra]]

CATEGORIES: 1872 births, 1942 deaths, Vice Presidents of Bolivia

Abdón Saavedra Mallea (1872, La Paz – 1942) served as Vice President of Bolivia from 1926 to 1930, during the presidency of Hernando Siles Reyes.Vicepresidency of Bolivia He was brother of president Bautista Saavedra.
