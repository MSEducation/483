[[Vayssierea cinnabarea]]

CATEGORIES: Okadaiidae

Vayssierea cinnabarea is a species of minute sea slug, a dorid nudibranch in the gastropod family Okadadiidae.Rosenberg, G. (2012). Vayssierea cinnabarea. Accessed through: World Register of Marine Species at http://www.marinespecies.org/aphia.php?p=taxdetails&id=599228 on 2012-06-03
This species feeds on Spirorbis tube worms. http://www.seaslug.info/factsheet.cfm?base=vaysfeli

==Distribution==

This species occurs in New Zealand.
