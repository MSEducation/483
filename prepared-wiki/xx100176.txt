[[Melissa, Calabria]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Calabria

Melissa (meaning "bee" in Greek) is a comune in the province of Crotone, in Calabria. The village is in a mountainous setting.

==History==

Melissa is mentioned for the first time in 13th century documents. It was  a fief of the de Micheli family (1463-1466), venitian nobles based in Calabria, of the Campitelli  family (1485-1688) and, later, to the Pignatelli (1688-1806).
In 1949 it was the location of a massacre, when the police shoot against the population, killing three people.
