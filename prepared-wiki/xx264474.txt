[[Lee Aaron (album)]]

CATEGORIES: Lee Aaron albums, 1987 albums, Attic Records albums

Lee Aaron is the self-titled fourth studio album by singer Lee Aaron, released on February 17, 1987 through Attic Records; a remastered edition was reissued in 2002 through Unidisc Music."Lee Aaron 1987". Lee Aaron European Fan Page. Retrieved 2013-01-02. The album reached No. 39 on the Canadian Albums Chart"Top Albums/CDs - Volume 46, No. 5, May 09 1987". RPM. Retrieved 2013-01-02. and remained on that chart for seventeen weeks, with two singles reaching the Canadian Singles Chart: "Only Human" at No. 44"Top Singles - Volume 46, No. 7, May 23 1987". RPM. Retrieved 2013-01-02. and "Goin' Off the Deep End" at No. 93."Top Singles - Volume 46, No. 13, July 04 1987". RPM. Retrieved 2013-01-02.
