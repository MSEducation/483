[[Dulak]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Dulak ([tpl]lang-fa|دولك[/tpl], also Romanized as Dūlak)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3061815|Dulak[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 68, in 18 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
