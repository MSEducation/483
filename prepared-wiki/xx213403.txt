[[Staffordshire Cups]]

CATEGORIES: Rugby union cup competitions in England, Rugby union in Staffordshire

The Staffordshire Cups are knockout competitions played by men's rugby union teams.
There are currently three cups, the Junior cup known as the Owen Cup, the Intermediate Cup known as The Lewis Taylor Cup
and the Staffordshire Senior Cup.
The teams competing in each cup is determined by the level or league rugby at which they play.
