[[Mr. and Mrs.]]

CATEGORIES: 1960s British television series, 1970s British television series, 1980s British television series, 1990s British television series, 1968 British television programme debuts, 1999 British television programme endings, British game shows, ITV game shows, Television series by ITV Studios

Mr. and Mrs. was a United Kingdom television show that aired on the ITV network with Alan Taylor hosting the HTV Wales version and Norman Vaughan first hosing the Border version and then Derek Batey afterwards. It was then aired on UK Living hosted by Nino Firetto entitled The All-New Mr. and Mrs. Show and then back on the ITV network in 1999 hosted by Julian Clary. The show was revived in 2008 as a celebrity game show entitled All Star Mr & Mrs, hosted by Phillip Schofield, originally with Fern Britton.

==International versions==

The show actually began in Canada in 1963. The Canadian version was produced at CFTO-TV in Toronto and ran on the CTV network for 780 episodes, and was hosted by Roy Ward Dickson, who was also the show's creator.Roy Ward Dickson: "Mr. and Mrs."
The British version actually began in Wales in 1964 as a Welsh language only programme in the same format entitled Sion a Sian ("John and Jane") where it continued until 2003 presented by  Ieuan Rhys . It was first broadcast on Teledu Cymru, then HTV Cymru and finally S4C. The series was revived in 2012, presented by Stifyn Parri and Heledd Cynwal.
