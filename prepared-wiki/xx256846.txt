[[Portuguesa-Tietê (São Paulo Metro)]]

CATEGORIES: São Paulo Metro stations

Portuguesa-Tietê is a station on Line 1 (Blue) of the São Paulo Metro.Information about Portuguesa-Tietê on the São Paulo Metro website (Portuguese)
The station provides access to the Tietê Bus Terminal, the largest such terminal in Latin America, and the second largest in the world.
