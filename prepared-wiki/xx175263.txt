[[WKZI]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the United States, Moody Radio affiliate stations, Radio stations in Illinois

WKZI is a Christian radio station licensed to Casey, Illinois, broadcasting on 800 kHz AM.  The station is owned by Word Power, Inc.WKZI fcc.gov. Accessed August 6, 2012 WKZI adopted its current Christian format in 1993. Word Power Inc. wordpower.us. Accessed August 6, 2012
