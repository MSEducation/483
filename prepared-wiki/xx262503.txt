[[Glaronisi]]

CATEGORIES: Heraklion (regional unit), Uninhabited islands of Greece, Islands of Crete

Glaronisi ([tpl]lang-el|Γλαρονήσι[/tpl], "seagull island", also known as Petalidi ([tpl]lang-el|Πεταλίδι[/tpl], "limpet"), is an islet off the northwest coast of the island Dia, north of the Greek island of Crete. Glaronisi is administered from Gouves in Heraklion regional unit.
