[[Cragie tube]]

CATEGORIES: Microbiology

The Cragie tube is a method used in microbiologyTextbook of Microbiology by Prof. C P Baveja, ISBN 81-7855-266-3Textbook of Microbiology by Ananthanarayan and Panikar, ISBN 81-250-2808-0 for determining bacterial motility.

==Technique==

A hollow tube with some culture medium is placed in semi-solid agar inside a bottle. Now a sample of the bacterium to be tested is inoculated into the medium in the hollow tube and the setup is incubated at 37Â°C overnight.
