[[Oreste Silvestri]]

CATEGORIES: 1858 births, 1936 deaths, 19th-century Italian painters, 20th-century Italian painters, Milanese painters

Oreste Silvestri (Pollone in Vercelli, September 5, 1858 - Milan, November 24, 1936short biography) was an Italian painter.

==Biography==

He presented his works at many National Expositions. In 1889 at Turin, he displayed: Una giornata di Sole. In 1883 at the Fine Arts Exposition in Rome, he displayed Alla fontana. In 1884 at the Mostra of Fine Arts at Turin: Impressione d'Estate.Dizionario degli Artisti Italiani Viventi: pittori, scultori, e Architetti., by Angelo de Gubernatis. Tipe dei Successori Le Monnier, 1889, page 477. He also participated in many prominent restorations including in 1924 of the The Last Supper by Da Vinci.RAI interview of Pietro Marani.voce ‘Restauro’ dell’Enciclopedia Italiana Treccani (1936) di PietroToesca, cfr. http://www.treccani.it/enciclopedia/restauro_res-3c1c79f6-8bb6-11dc-8e9d-0016357eee51_(Enciclopedia-Italiana)/ He was also a formidable art collector and merchant. He was likely related to the portrait artist Carlo Silvestri (1821-1883), whose self-portrait he donated (1937) to the Galleria d'Arte Moderna in Milan.
