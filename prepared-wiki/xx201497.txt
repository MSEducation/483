[[Surbiton F.C.]]

CATEGORIES: Association football clubs established in 1863, Defunct Football League clubs, Football clubs in London, 1863 establishments in England

Surbiton Football Club was an English association football club based in Surbiton, a local government district of Surrey but now in the Royal Borough of Kingston-upon-Thames, which was founded in 1863 and was a founder-member of the Football Association. It is now defunct.
