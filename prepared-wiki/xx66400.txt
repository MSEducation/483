[[Otopeni]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Romania, Populated places in Ilfov County

Otopeni ([tpl]IPA-ro|otoˈpenʲ[/tpl]) is a town in Ilfov County, Romania, some [tpl]convert|15|km|mi|abbr=on[/tpl] north of Bucharest along the road to Ploieşti. It has 10,215 inhabitants, of which 99.0% are Romanians. One village, Odăile, is administered by the town.
Henri Coandă International Airport is located inside Otopeni. ==History==

Originally an agricultural village, Otopeni was transformed into a city as part of Nicolae Ceaușescu's Systematization plan. It replaced the semidetached houses with four-story high apartment buildings.Darrick Danta, "Ceausescu's Bucharest", The Geographical Review 83, no. 2 (1993)
