[[Mike Toth]]

CATEGORIES: Major League Baseball announcers, 1963 births, Living people, Canadian television sportscasters, Canadian radio sportscasters, People from Moose Jaw, People from Newell County, Alberta, Canadian people of Hungarian descent

Mike Toth (born September 27, 1963 in Moose Jaw, Saskatchewan, Canada) is a Canadian sports anchor, formerly on Rogers Sportsnet Sportsnet Connected and with the Fan 590 in Toronto. He grew up in Bassano, Alberta and then spent much of his early career in Calgary, Alberta with CICT-TV as a sports anchor and co-host of the station's "Sports @ 11" nightly sports broadcast.  He then worked as a sports anchor for TSN's SportsCentre.http://www.theglobeandmail.com/servlet/story/LAC.20070221.TRUTH21/TPStory/TPSports/Television/ After leaving TSN, Toth hosted the Hockey Central program, as well as guest hosted Prime Time Sports on The Fan 590. Toth was also a co-host on the Fan 590's "The Bullpen" with Mike Hogan from 10:00am to noon.http://www.thestar.com/Sports/article/276927
As of December 2009, Toth has joined Newstalk 1010 as their morning sports announcer.http://thestar.blogs.com/sportsmedia/2009/12/change-at-cfrb-veteran-sports-announcer-rick-hodge-who-joined-cfrb-last-year-is-out-in-whats-been-described-as-a-parting.htmlhttp://www.youtube.com/watch?v=i3m1aMrfW9M
