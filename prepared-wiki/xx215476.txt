[[Ateny, Podlaskie Voivodeship]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Augustów County

Ateny [tpl]IPAc-pl|a|'|t|e|n|y[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Nowinka, within Augustów County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|4|km|mi|0[/tpl] north-east of Nowinka, [tpl]convert|12|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of Augustów, and [tpl]convert|93|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of the regional capital Białystok.
The village has a population of 40.
