[[In Shining Violence]]

CATEGORIES: 2007 EPs, Chromatics (band) EPs

"In Shining Violence" is an EP by Chromatics, released in 2007 on the Italians Do It Better record label.  It includes a cover of Bruce Springsteen's "I'm on Fire".  The label reissued an analogue remaster of the single, along with a handful of unreleased tracks, as In the City in 2010.http://www.troublemanunlimited.com/store/idib/
