[[Jan Stæchmann]]

CATEGORIES: 1966 births, Living people, Danish speedway riders, Wolverhampton Wolves riders, Stoke Potters riders, Hull Vikings riders, Long Eaton Invaders riders

Jan Stæchmann (born 5 June 1966 in Kolding, Denmark)Oakes, P.(2004). British Speedway Who's Who.  ISBN 0-948882-81-6 is a professional motorcycle speedway rider and the manager of the Danish national team. He took over as manager of Swindon Robins in July. The Robins, rock bottom of the league whilst Staechmann took over, has set his sights on the play-offs.
He led Denmark to the 2006 Speedway World Cup.
