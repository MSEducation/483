[[Miss Teen USA state rankings]]

CATEGORIES: Miss Teen USA

The following is similar to an Olympic medal table, that includes every state and territory that has placed in the Miss Teen USA pageant, based on all results from the first edition in 1983 to the most recent competition in 2013. Note that, from 1990 to 2000, there was only a [tpl]nowrap|Top 3[/tpl] with the third and fourth runners-up positions eliminated.
