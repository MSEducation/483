[[Płaska]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Augustów County

Płaska [tpl]IPAc-pl|'|p|ł|a|s|k|a[/tpl], ([tpl]lang-lt|Plaskai[/tpl]), is a village in Augustów County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland, close to the border with Belarus.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It is the seat of the gmina (administrative district) called Gmina Płaska. It lies approximately [tpl]convert|20|km|mi|0[/tpl] east of Augustów and [tpl]convert|88|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of the regional capital Białystok.
The village has a population of 370.
