[[Said Kandi, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Said Kandi ([tpl]lang-fa|سعيدكندي[/tpl], also Romanized as Sa‘īd Kandī; also known as Saiyid Kandi, Seytkendi, and Seyyed Kandī)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3081932|Said Kandi[/tpl] is a village in Bughda Kandi Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. In the 2006 census, its population was 205, in 43 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
