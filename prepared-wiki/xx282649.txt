Thomas Camp (fl. 1420), of Litlington, Cambridgeshire, was an English politician.

==Family==

Camp was the son of the MP, John Camp.

==Career==

He was a Member (MP) of the Parliament of England for Cambridgeshire in 1420.http://www.historyofparliamentonline.org/volume/1386-1421/member/camp-thomas
