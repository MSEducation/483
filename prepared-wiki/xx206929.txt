Isabel Cristina Ceballos Rojas (born February 3, 1979) is a female, Olympic breaststroke swimmer from Colombia.Ceballos' entry from sports-reference.com She swam for Colombia at the 1996 and 2000 Olympics. 
At the 2000 Olympics, she set the Colombian Records in the 100 and 200 Breaststrokes (1:11.90 and  2:34.09).
