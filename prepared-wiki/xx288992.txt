[[Sojas Rud Rural District]]

CATEGORIES: Rural Districts of Zanjan Province, Khodabandeh County

Sojas Rud Rural District ([tpl]lang-fa|دهستان سجاس رود[/tpl]) is a rural district (dehestan) in Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 15,771, in 3,530 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl] The rural district has 29 villages.
