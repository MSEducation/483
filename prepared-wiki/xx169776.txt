[[The Last Page]]

CATEGORIES: 1952 films, Black-and-white films, British films, British crime films, British drama films, Films directed by Terence Fisher, English-language films, 1950s drama films

The Last Page, released in the United States as Man Bait, is a 1952 British film noir starring George Brent, Marguerite Chapman, and Diana Dors. The movie, largely set in a bookstore, was directed by Terence Fisher.
