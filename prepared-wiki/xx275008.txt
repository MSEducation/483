[[Tshinabu Wa Munda]]

Martin Tshinabu Wa-Munda (born 8 May 1946) is a Congolese football midfielder who played for Zaire in the 1974 FIFA World Cup.1974 FIFA World Cup Germany He also played for TP Mazembe.
