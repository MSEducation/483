[[Epipogium]]

CATEGORIES: Epipogiinae, Orchid genera, Myco-heterotrophic orchids

Epipogium is a genus of terrestrial achlorophyllous myco-heterotrophic orchids. The genus is sympodial with no root. Leaves are scale-like. Flowers are non-resupinate, arranged in terminal racemes. Stigmas are 2-lobed and the rostellum is ventrally situated and not prominent. Species include:
