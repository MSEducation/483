[[Perm Krai Police]]

CATEGORIES: Perm Krai

Directorate of the Ministry for Internal Affairs in Perm Krai (ГУ МВД России по Пермскому краю) or the Police of Perm (Полиция Перми) is the main law enforcement agency of the Government of Perm Krai,Russia. It is answerable to the regional MVD and the Governor of Perm Krai.
Current chief of police in Perm Krai is Yuri Valyaev, the police is responsible for law enforcement in the region, including that of Perm City. Chief of Perm City Police (Управление МВД России по г. Перми) is currently Vladimir Alexandrovich Vlasov.
