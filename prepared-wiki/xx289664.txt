[[Aqa Jari, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Aqa Jari ([tpl]lang-fa|اقاجري[/tpl], also Romanized as Āqā Jarī; also known as Agach Ayry and Aga Jari)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3053351|Aqa Jari[/tpl] is a village in Sojas Rud Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 536, in 108 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
