[[Janet Zand]]

CATEGORIES: Living people

Janet Zand is a practitioner of Oriental medicine, naturopathy, herbalism, and acupuncture. Zand is also a lecturer and author.Janet Zand - Healthworld Online - HealthWorld Online
Zand has published several books including A Parent's Guide to Medical Emergencies, Smart Medicine, and Smart Medicine for a Healthier Childhttp://books.google.com/books?id=DK0ft9VYUJYC&printsec=frontcover&source=gbs_summary_r&cad=0#PPP1,M1 and has a line of herbal supplements. From 1978 to 1998, she was part of a private practice in Santa Monica, California specializing in traditional Chinese medicine and nutrition.  She has studied homeopathy, medicine, nutrition, and acupuncture throughout the U.S. and Asia.  From 1978-2002 Zand was the Chairman of the Board, co-founder, and formulator for Zand Herbal Formulas.
She lectures throughout the country to physicians, acupuncturists, naturopaths, chiropractors, nurses and other health-care professionals on natural medicine including traditional Chinese medicine, nutrition and homeopathy.  She still practices acupuncture and has contributed as a presenter at the Pacific Symposium.http://www.pacificcollege.edu/symposium/PDFs/PS_Brochure2005.pdf
Zand is now a contributing doctor, along with Nathan Bryan, for Neogenis Labs.
