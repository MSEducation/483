[[Telecommunications in Djibouti]]

CATEGORIES: Communications in Djibouti

Information about telecommunications in Djibouti.

==Communications==

===Telephones===

====Telephone system====

General assessment: Telephone facilities in the city of Djibouti are defined by CIA World Factbook as "adequate as are the microwave radio relay connections to outlying areas of the country."Djibouti, from CIA - The World Factbook. Accessed February 2009.

===Radio===

Radio broadcast stations: AM 1, FM 2, shortwave 0 (2001)

===Television===

====Broadcast media====

Maintains restrictions on the licensing and operation of broadcast media; state-owned Radiodiffusion-Television de Djibouti (RTD) operates the sole terrestrial TV station as well as the only 2 domestic radio networks; no private TV or radio stations; transmissions of several international broadcasters are available (2007)

===Internet===

Internet hosts: 215 (2012)  

====Internet users====

61,320 (2011)Africa Internet Facebook Usage and Population Statistics - internetworldstats.com
