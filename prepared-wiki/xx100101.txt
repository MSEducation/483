[[Michael Tuck Medal]]

CATEGORIES: Australian Football League awards, Awards established in 1992, 1992 establishments in Australia

Since 1992, the Michael Tuck Medal has been awarded to the best-and-fairest player in the AFL Pre-season Cup Final. It is named after Michael Tuck, the current AFL games record holder who played 426 senior matches for the Hawthorn Hawks between 1972 and 1991.[tpl]cite book | title=AFL Record Season Guide 2010 | editor=Lovett, Michael | year=2010 | page=901 | isbn=978-0-9806274-5-9[/tpl]
