[[1924 Uruguayan Primera División of AUF]]

CATEGORIES: Uruguayan Primera División seasons, 1924 in South American football leagues, 1924 in Uruguay

The Uruguayan Championship 1924 of AUF was the 24th season of Uruguay's top-flight football league.

==Overview==

The tournament consisted of a two-wheel championship of all against all. It involved twelve teams, and the champion was Nacional (third consecutively).
