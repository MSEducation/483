[[Hangzhou Botanical Garden]]

CATEGORIES: Geography of Hangzhou, Botanical gardens in China, Visitor attractions in Hangzhou

Hangzhou Botanical Garden ([tpl]zh|t=杭州植物園|s=杭州植物园[/tpl]) is a large public botanical garden located in Hangzhou City, Zhejiang Province, People's Republic of China. 

==Introduction==

The garden was founded in 1956. It is located in Taoyuanling, Xihu District, Hangzhou. It has an area of [tpl]convert|248.46|acre|km2[/tpl]. The soil belongs to the red soil and yellow soil, with pH value ranging from 4.9 to 6.5. It is affiliated to Hangzhou Gardens and Relics Management Bureau. It's also a main botanical study and research base for Zhejiang University.
