[[Florida State Road 326]]

CATEGORIES: State highways in Florida, County roads in Levy County, Florida, County roads in Marion County, Florida, State Roads in Marion County, Florida

State Road 326 (SR 326) is an eastâ€“west route in Marion County, Florida, in and around Ocala. The western terminus is at I-75 exit 358, and its eastern end is at State Road 40 east of Silver Springs. The highway acts as a bypass around Ocala, taking traffic to SR 40 on the edge of the Ocala National Forest.
West of I-75, the road continues as County Road 326 in Marion and Levy Counties to the Waccasassa River near Gulf Hammock.
