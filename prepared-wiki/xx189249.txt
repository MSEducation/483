[[Jason Rivers (American football)]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 births, Living people, American football wide receivers, Hawaii Rainbow Warriors football players, Sportspeople from Honolulu, Hawaii, Players of American football from Hawaii, Players of American football from California

Jason Rivers (born May 31, 1985) is currently a wide receiver for the UFAL Champion SoCal Coyotes, a AAA professional football team in Palm Springs, California. The Coyotes are a record-setting Run 'n' Shoot team under head coach J David Miller, a Run 'n' Shoot disciple of Mouse Davis and June Jones, who coached Rivers at Hawaii. Rivers is the all-time leading receiver college football for Hawaii Hawaii Warriors, and was a favorite target of former Heisman-candidate quarterback Colt Brennan. He was not selected in the 2008 NFL Draft but was signed by the Titans as a free agent but was subsequently released.
