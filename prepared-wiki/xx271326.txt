[[Cirkusový kôň]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 singles, Marika Gombitová songs, Songs written by Marika Gombitová, Songs written by Kamil Peteraj

"Cirkusový kôň" ([tpl]lang-en|Circus Horse[/tpl]) is a song by Marika Gombitová, released on single by OPUS in 1980.For Marika Gombitová's discography, see [tpl]harvnb|Lehotský|2008a|pp=54–56.[/tpl].
The composition was written by Ján Lauko and Kamil Peteraj, while released next year on album Môj malý príbeh (1981). B-side of the single featured "Deň letí", which was composed by Gombitová herself.[tpl]harvnb|Graclík|Nekvapil|2008|p=417.[/tpl]
