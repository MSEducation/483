[[Untitled (film)]]

CATEGORIES: Independent films, 2011 films, British films, British horror films, 2011 horror films, Found footage films

Untitled is a 2011 British drama-horror film directed by Shaun Troke.  Presented in the style of found footage, the film stars Troke alongside Nikki Harrup, Leonora Moore and Danny Goldberg.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.28dayslateranalysis.com/2010/12/shaun-troke-developing-supernatural.html|title=Shaun Troke Developing Supernatural Horror Film in Wales|work=28 Days Later Analysis|date=29 December 2010|accessdate=22 September 2011[/tpl]

==Plot==

A filmmaker and his crew spend a weekend making a documentary in a cottage in Wales.  Their film is never completed due to sinister forces in the building.

==Cast==

==Production==

Alongside writer Steven Jarrett, Troke explored the background mythology of the film and had created a script by early 2009.  Troke had originally intended to shoot the film by the end of 2009, but, after he was hired to direct the horror film Sparrow, production on Untitled halted.  After working with both Moore and Harrup on Sparrow, Troke ultimately decided to cast both of them in the lead female roles in Untitled.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.scifiworld.es/noticias.php?&id_noticia=8997&categoria&seccion=1|title=Untitled Lo Nuevo De Shaun Troke|work=SciFiWorld.es|language=Spanish|accessdate=22 September 2011[/tpl]

==Accolades==

The film won 'Best Narrative Feature' at the American International Film Festival in September 2011.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.aiff2010.com/Winners.htm|title=Winners: September 2011|work=American International Film Festival|accessdate=22 September 2011[/tpl] Untitled will go on a limited cinematic release in 2012.

Prior to destival release, Untitled used a viral marketing campaign by promoting various clips of the film via the film's official YouTube channel.[tpl]cite  web|url=http://www.youtube.com/user/UntitledProject2010|title=UntitledProject2010|work=YouTube|accessdate=22 September 2011[/tpl]  Similar to many mockumentary films, this footage was presented as "genuine".[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bloody-disgusting.com/news/24070|title=First Trailer for Found Footage 'Untitled'|work=Bloody Disgusting|date=5 April 2011|accessdate=22 September 2011[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/43625/new-trailer-and-tv-spots-more-untitled-terror|title=New Trailer and TV Spots - More Untitled Terror|work=Dread Central|date=4 April 2011|accessdate=22 September 2011[/tpl] Untitled premiered at the American International Film Festival in September 2011.
