[[Clear Light (album)]]

CATEGORIES: 1967 debut albums, Albums produced by Paul A. Rothchild, Elektra Records albums, Clear Light albums, English-language albums

Clear Light was the only album released by the 1960s American psychedelic rock band Clear Light.  It was released in September 1967 and peaked at number 126 on the Billboard pop albums chart. It combined elements of folk, rock, psychedelic, and classical music.  It is an AMG music pick,[tpl]cite web |title=Clear Light |url=[tpl]Allmusic|class=album|id=r60854|pure_url=yes[/tpl] |publisher=Allmusic |accessdate=28 July 2009[/tpl] although the album was not a success at the time.

==Track listing==

A later reissue of the album included a bonus track, "She's Ready to Be Free", which was the B-side of the group's first single.
