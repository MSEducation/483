[tpl]nihongo|Kokomi Sakura|桜ここみ|Sakura Kokomi[/tpl] is a Japanese adult video (AV) actress who is also known under the name Cocomi Sakura.

==Life and career==

Sakura was born in Shimane Prefecture, Japan on December 18, 1988.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.s1s1s1.com/actress/-/detail/=/id=27643/|title=桜ここみ（さくらここみ）|publisher=www.s1s1s1.com|language=Japanese[/tpl]
At the 2009 AV Grand Prix her softcore video [tpl]nihongo|AV Impossible - Kokomi Sakura|『AV無理』 桜ここみ[/tpl], directed by Yūki Iwasawa and released in November 2008, won one of the Special Awards for Best Fetish Video.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.av-gp.com/news/avgp_result/bottom.html|title=AVGP 2009 Result announcement|publisher=www.av-gp.com|accessdate=2009-03-02|language=Japanese[/tpl] A month later, a second softcore video starring Sakura appeared, [tpl]nihongo|AV Impossible 2 - Kokomi Sakura|『AV無理2』 桜ここみ[/tpl].[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.dmm.co.jp/digital/videoa/-/detail/=/cid=mmnd038/|title=『AV無理2』 桜ここみ|publisher=DMM|accessdate=2011-10-01|language=Japanese[/tpl] Sakura made her adult video (AV) debut for the S1 No. 1 Style studio on April 19, 2009 with Consent x New Face x Risky Mosaic Perfect Body.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.s1s1s1.com/works/-/detail/=/cid=soe206/|title=了解×新人×ギリモザ パーフェクトボディ|publisher=www.s1s1s1.com|accessdate=2011-10-10|language=Japanese[/tpl] Since that time she has appeared in about one video a month for S1. In January 2010, Sakura was one of the S1 actresses who took part in a collaborative event with All Japan Pro Wrestling to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the S1 studio.[tpl]cite web|url=http://allabout.co.jp/gm/gc/202135/|title=Ｓ１と全日本プロレスがコラボ興業開催！|publisher=AllAbout|accessdate=2011-10-11|language=Japanese|last=Otsubo|first=Kemuta|date=December 18, 2009[/tpl]
Sakura played the part of a nurse in Episode 11 of the Tokyo Broadcasting System comic detective TV series Unubore Deka ([tpl]nihongo2|うぬぼれ刑事[/tpl]) which aired on September 17, 2010.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.allcinema.net/prog/show_c.php?num_c=337267|title=うぬぼれ刑事（でか）(2010)|publisher=AllCinema|accessdate=2011-10-11|language=Japanese[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1731097/|title=Unubore deka (TV series 2010) Episode #1.11 (#1.11)|accessdate=2011-10-11|publisher=IMDb[/tpl] In another venture outside the AV field, she starred in the erotic thriller V-Cinema release [tpl]nihongo|Jiken wana - Ni hamaru onna tachi|事件　罠にはまる女たち[/tpl] which was released by GP Museum in February 2011.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.allcinema.net/prog/show_c.php?num_c=338933|title=事件　罠にはまる女たち(2011)|publisher=AllCinema|accessdate=2011-10-11|language=Japanese[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.dmm.com/mono/dvd/-/detail/=/cid=n_914dkis8946/|title=事件 ～罠にはまる女たち～|publisher=DMM|accessdate=2011-10-11|language=Japanese[/tpl]
Sakura's DVDs have been among the best selling at the large AV retailer DMM (part of the Hokuto Corporation) since her debut. She ranked #16 in the DMM top 100 actresses for the last half of 2009[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.dmm.co.jp/mono/dvd/-/ranking/=/term=last_2009/mode=actress/|title=2009年下半期AV女優ランキング ベスト100|publisher=DMM|accessdate=2011-09-27|language=Japanese[/tpl] and #19 for the full year.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.dmm.co.jp/mono/dvd/-/ranking/=/term=year_2009/mode=actress/|title=2009年年間AV女優ランキング ベスト100|publisher=DMM|accessdate=2011-09-27|language=Japanese[/tpl] In 2010 she ranked at #22[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.dmm.co.jp/mono/dvd/-/ranking/=/term=year_2010/mode=actress/rank=21_40/|title=2010年年間AV女優ランキング ベスト100|publisher=DMM|accessdate=2011-09-27|language=Japanese[/tpl] and was listed at #43 for the first half of 2011.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.dmm.co.jp/mono/dvd/-/ranking/=/term=first_2011/mode=actress/rank=41_60/|title=2011年上半期AV女優ランキング ベスト100|publisher=DMM|accessdate=2011-09-27|language=Japanese[/tpl]
