[[Mount Beshtor]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of Uzbekistan

Mount Beshtor ([tpl]lang-ru|гора Бештор[/tpl], also Беш-Тёр) rises to 4,299 m in Pskem Range in the north-eastern tip of Uzbekistan's Tashkent Province. Located on the southern border with Kyrgyzstan, a short distance to the south-west from another towering peak in Pskem Range, Mount Adelung (Adelunga Toghi, 4,301 m). Beshtor is the second highest peak in both Tashkent Province and the Pskem Range.
