[[Gary Figueroa]]

Gary Lee Figueroa (born September 28, 1956 in Phoenix, Arizona) is a former water polo player from the United States, who won the silver medal with Team USA at the 1984 Summer Olympics in Los Angeles, California.
He currently coaches women's water polo at California State University, Monterey Bay.
