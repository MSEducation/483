[[I Need a Girl (Part One)]]

CATEGORIES: 2002 singles, Sean Combs songs, Usher (entertainer) songs, Bad Boy Records singles, Songs written by Sean Combs, Rhythm and blues ballads, Billboard Rap Songs number-one singles, Songs written by Adonis Shropshire, Songs written by Mario Winans, Music videos directed by Benny Boom

"I Need a Girl (Part One)" is a single by American rapper P. Diddy with the collaboration of Usher and Loon from the album We Invented the Remix. Along with "I Need a Girl (Part Two)", P. Diddy achieved a rare occurrence by having two parts of a song become chart hits. Part one peaked at #2 on the US Billboard Hot 100 and #1 on the Billboard Hot Rap Tracks chart.
