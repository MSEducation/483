[[Chris Distefano]]

CATEGORIES: American comedians, 1985 births, Living people

Chris Distefano (born August 26, 1984) is an American comedian described as a "high energetic comic." He stars in MTV and MTV2's TV shows Guy Code and Girl Code. He also co-hosts the MSG TV show The Bracket.

==Personal life==

Distefano was awarded a bachelor's degree in Psychology from St. Joseph's College and a doctorate in Physical Therapy.[tpl]citation needed|date=August 2013[/tpl]

==Career==

Distefano has worked at all of the top comedy clubs across New York City including Gotham Comedy Club, Carolines on Broadway, and The Comic Strip. From that he hosted the 2010 Fencing Masters on the SNY network & Elite 8 of the 2011 and 2012 March Madness Comedy Competition at the Carolines. selected to headline Carolines' Breakout Artist Series and was selected as a finalist in the 2011 NY Comedy Festival. Chris has been heard on Sirius XM radio and his left elbow was featured on a National Subway Commercial.
