[[Corrado dal Fabbro]]

CATEGORIES: 1945 births, Bobsledders at the 1972 Winter Olympics, Italian bobsledders, Living people, Olympic bobsledders of Italy, Olympic silver medalists for Italy, Olympic medalists in bobsleigh

Corrado dal Fabbro (sometimes shown as Corrado Dal Fabbro or Corado dal Fabro, born August 4, 1945) is an Italian bobsledder who competed in the late 1960s and early 1970s. He won the silver medal in the four-man event at the 1972 Winter Olympics in Sapporo.
dal Fabbro also won a silver medal in the two-man event at the 1971 FIBT World Championships in Cervinia.
As of 2007, he is vice president of Sport for the FIBT
