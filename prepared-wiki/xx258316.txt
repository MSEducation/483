[[Anna Lang (harpsichordist)]]

CATEGORIES: Harpsichordists, 1874 births, 1920 deaths, Swedish women artists

Anna Lang, née Nordqvist (Stockholm 5 July 1874 – 12 December 1920) was a Swedish Harpsichordist.
Lang was the daughter of the conductor of the Kungliga Hovkapellet, Conrad Nordqvist. She received the von Beskows scholarship in 1883, became a student at Royal College of Music, Stockholm 1887, and was a Harpsichordist at Hovkapellet from 1890 until her death.
She married her teacher Josef Lang in 1895; their daughter Ingrid was also a harpsichordist in Hovkapellet.
