[[Niels Fleuren]]

CATEGORIES: 1986 births, Living people, People from Boxmeer, Dutch footballers, Eredivisie players, Eerste Divisie players, VVV-Venlo players

Niels Fleuren ([tpl]IPA-nl|ˈniɫs ˈflʏː.rə(n)[/tpl], born 1 November 1986) is a Dutch professional footballer who currently plays as a left back for VVV-Venlo. Born in Boxmeer, North Brabant, he made his professional debut in the 2003–04 season.
