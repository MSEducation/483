Gosford Park is a 2001 mystery comedy-drama film directed by Robert Altman and written by Julian Fellowes. It premiered on November 7, 2001 at the London Film Festival.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/film/2001/nov/08/londonfilmfestival2001.londonfilmfestival|title=High Hopes For London Film Festival 2001|date=November 8, 2001|work=The Guardian|publisher=Guardian Media Group|accessdate=July 17, 2010[/tpl] The film then received a limited release across theaters in the United States on December 26, 2001, before being widely released in January 2002 by USA Films.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.ew.com/ew/allabout/credits/0,,20000730,00.html|title=All About Gosford Park|work=Entertainment Weekly|publisher=Time Inc.|accessdate=July 17, 2010[/tpl] It was released on February 1, 2002 in the United Kingdom.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/films/2002/01/18/gosford_park_2002_review.shtml|title=Gosford Park (2002)|last=Smith|first=Neil|date=January 31, 2002|work=BBC Films|publisher=BBC|accessdate=July 17, 2010[/tpl] Gosford Park has earned over $87 million in its combined total gross at the box office.[tpl]cite web|url=http://boxofficemojo.com/movies/?id=gosfordpark.htm|title=Gosford Park|work=Box Office Mojo|publisher=Internet Movie Database|accessdate=July 17, 2010[/tpl]
Gosford Park garnered various awards and nominations following its release, with nominations ranging from recognition of the screenplay and its direction to the cast's acting performance, particularly Helen Mirren and Maggie Smith. The film received seven Academy Awards nominations; the ceremony saw Fellowes win for Best Original Screenplay.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/film/2002/feb/12/awardsandprizes.news|title=Full List Of Oscar Winners And Nominees|date=February 12, 2002|work=The Guardian|publisher=Guardian Media Group|accessdate=July 23, 2010[/tpl] At the 55th British Academy Film Awards, Gosford Park came away with two awards from nine nominations. Three of the film's actresses earned nominations for Best European Actress at the European Film Awards. The film received five nominations at the 59th Golden Globe Awards and Altman won the award for Best Director. Gosford Park won all six of the awards that it was nominated for at the National Society of Film Critics and the New York Film Critics Circle.
The film won two awards at the 8th Screen Actors Guild Awards for Outstanding Performance by a Female Actor in a Supporting Role and Outstanding Performance by a Cast in a Motion Picture. The film went on to win four more Best Cast awards from the Broadcast Film Critics Association, Florida Film Critics Circle, and Online Film Critics Society. Fellowes received recognition for the film's screenplay from the Writers Guild of America, where he won the Best Original Screenplay award. He subsequently received three more awards and three nominations. Fellowes was also nominated for Best Newcomer at the British Academy Film Awards. The film's score composer, Patrick Doyle received two nominations for his work. Doyle was nominated for Composer of the Year from the American Film Institute and he won the award for Soundtrack Composer of the Year from the World Soundtrack Awards. The film's costume, hair and make-up also earned three nominations between them.
