[[John David Burns]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Oregon State Senators, People from Gilliam County, Oregon

John David Burns, born in Condon, Oregon, was a member of the Oregon State Senate from 1967 to 1975. He served as Senate President from 1971 to 1973."Demos Select John Burns as Comm. Candidate," Times-Journal OR, vol. 120m no. 34 (Aug. 24, 2006), pg. 1.
