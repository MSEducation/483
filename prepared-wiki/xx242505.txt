[[Cannonball in Europe!]]

CATEGORIES: 1962 live albums, Cannonball Adderley live albums, Capitol Records live albums

Cannonball in Europe! is a live album by jazz saxophonist Cannonball Adderley recorded at the Comblain-la-Tour in Belgium and released on the Capitol label featuring performances by Adderley with Nat Adderley, Yusef Lateef, Joe Zawinul, Sam Jones and Louis Hayes.Cannonball Adderley discography accessed 20 October 2009

==Reception==

The Allmusic review by Al Campbell awarded the album 4 stars and states "Cannonball Adderley is in excellent form on this live date".Campbell, A. Allmusic Review accessed 20 October 2009
