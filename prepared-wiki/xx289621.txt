[[Owch Bolagh, Khodabandeh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Owch Bolagh ([tpl]lang-fa|اوچ بلاغ[/tpl], also Romanized as Owch Bolāgh, Aūch Bulāq, and Ūch Bolāgh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3087918|Owch Bolagh[/tpl] is a village in Aq Bolagh Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 161, in 32 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
