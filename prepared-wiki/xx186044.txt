Gmina Wilków is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Namysłów County, Opole Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Its seat is the village of Wilków, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|6|km|mi|0[/tpl] north-west of Namysłów and [tpl]convert|54|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of the regional capital Opole.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|100.57|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 4,678.
There is a small group of individuals, currently (2010) investigating the history of Wilkow.  The village is littered with ruins of old country-houses belonging to mostly German gentry that lived in the area prior to the second world war. The website is dedicated to the history of the village.   

==Villages==

Gmina Wilków contains the villages and settlements of Bukowie, Dębnik, Idzikowice, Jakubowice, Krzyków, Lubska, Młokicie, Pągów, Pielgrzymowice, Pszeniczna, Wilków and Wojciechów.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Wilków is bordered by the gminas of Bierutów, Dziadowa Kłoda and Namysłów.
