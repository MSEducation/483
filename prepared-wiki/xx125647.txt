[[WYMX]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Mississippi

WYMX (99.1 FM) is a classic hits formatted radio station licensed to Greenwood, Mississippi, USA.  The station is currently owned by Telesouth Communications. ==Programming==

The station has "Charlie in the Morning" during the day. The station also covers Ole Miss and Pillow Academy football.

==History==

It originally used the brand "Max 99.1", which it had used for decades, but rebranded as "Bob FM" on July 31, 2006.  Prior to the Bob FM format, the station operated under a hot AC format. The station recently re-branded itself again as Max 99 and bringing back their former hot AC format.
