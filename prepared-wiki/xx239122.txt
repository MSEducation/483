[[Roos House (Natchez, Mississippi)]]

CATEGORIES: Colonial Revival architecture in Mississippi, Houses completed in 1905, Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Mississippi, Houses in Natchez, Mississippi, Art Nouveau architecture in the United States

The Roos House in Natchez, Mississippi was built in 1905.  Its design includes Colonial Revival and Art Nouveau elements.  It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1979.
