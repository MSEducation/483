[[Central District (Kashan County)]]

CATEGORIES: Kashan County, Districts of Isfahan Province

The Central District of Kashan County ([tpl]lang-fa|بخش مرکزی شهرستان کاشان[/tpl]) is a district (bakhsh) in Kashan County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 266,921, in 72,469 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|10[/tpl]  The District has two cities: Kashan and Meshkat. The District has three rural districts (dehestan): Khorram Dasht Rural District, Kuhpayeh Rural District, and Miyandasht Rural District.
