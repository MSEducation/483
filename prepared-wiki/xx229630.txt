[[Rudolf Kramer]]

CATEGORIES: 1886 births, Year of death missing, Austrian male cyclists, Olympic cyclists of Austria, Cyclists at the 1912 Summer Olympics, Sportspeople from Vienna

Rudolf Kramer (born 17 January 1886, date of death unknown) was an Austrian road racing cyclist who competed in the 1912 Summer Olympics.
He was born in Vienna.
In 1912, he was a member of the Austrian cycling team which finished seventh in the team time trial event.
