[[Urs Burkart]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Swiss ice hockey players, 1963 births, Ice hockey players at the 1988 Winter Olympics, Olympic ice hockey players of Switzerland, EV Zug players, Lausanne HC players

Urs Burkart (born January 9, 1963) is a former Swiss professional ice hockey defenceman who last played for Lausanne HC in Switzerland's National League B.
Burkart has participated as a member of the Swiss national team at the 1988 Winter Olympics.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.eliteprospects.com/player.php?player=30094|title=Eliteprospects.com - Urs Burkart|publisher=EliteProspects.com|accessdate=2013-08-12[/tpl]
He is currently F&ACE coordinator.
