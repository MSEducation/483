[[Sardehat-e Sheykh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Sardehat-e Sheykh ([tpl]lang-fa|سر دهات شيخ[/tpl], also Romanized as Sardehāt-e Sheykh; also known as Sardahāt-e Bālā, Sardehāt, Sardehāt-e Sheykhlar, Sardehāt-e Sheykhlū, Sardehāt Sheykhestānlū, and Sardekhat)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3082694|Sardehat-e Sheykh[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 219, in 60 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
