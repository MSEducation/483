[[Indiavibes]]

CATEGORIES: Internet television channels

Indiavibes is a web television channel managed and owned by Vibes Visual & Media Pvt Ltd, a visual production company based in Kochi, Kerala, India. The creators claim it to be India's first on demand Web TV.[tpl]cite news|url=http://economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/news-by-industry/cons-products/electronics/indias-first-web-tv-to-be-launched-on-new-year/articleshow/7178548.cms |title=India's first web TV to be launched on New Year | The economic times|publisher=economictimes.indiatimes.com |date= December 28, 2010|accessdate= January 2, 2011 [/tpl] It was launched on January 1, 2011. Indiavibes produces and broadcasts a number of web television shows of various categories like fashion, music, films, business, technology, blogging, lifestyle and current affairs.
