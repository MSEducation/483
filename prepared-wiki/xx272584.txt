[[Maxime Gingras (skier)]]

CATEGORIES: 1984 births, Canadian freestyle skiers, Freestyle skiers at the 2010 Winter Olympics, Living people, Olympic freestyle skiers of Canada, Sportspeople from Montreal

Maxime Gingras (born December 17, 1984 in Montreal, Quebec) is a retired Canadian freestyle skier. He competed for Canada at the 2010 Winter Olympics.
