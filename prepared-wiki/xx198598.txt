[[Xavier Pentecôte]]

CATEGORIES: 1986 births, Living people, People from Saint-Dié-des-Vosges, French footballers, Association football forwards, Toulouse FC players, SC Bastia players, OGC Nice players, Ligue 1 players, Ligue 2 players

Xavier Pentecôte (born 13 August 1986) is a French footballer who plays for Ligue 1 club Nice as a striker, having previously played for Toulouse and Bastia.

==Career==

Born in Saint-Dié, Pentecôte joined Toulouse in 2001 and turned professional in 2004. He spent the 2007-2008 season on loan at Bastia, and rejoined the Corsican club in January 2010 for a second loan spell. in August 2011, he completed a move to Ligue 1 rivals Nice, signing a four-year contract.
