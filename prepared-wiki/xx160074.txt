[[Belgian general election, 1961]]

CATEGORIES: 1961 elections in Europe, Elections in Belgium, 1961 in Belgium

General elections were held in Belgium on 26 March 1961.Nohlen, D & Stöver, P (2010) Elections in Europe: A data handbook, p289 ISBN 978-3-8329-5609-7 The result was a victory for the Christian Social Party, which won 96 of the 212 seats in the Chamber of Representatives and 47 of the 106 seats in the Senate.Nohlen & Stöver, pp309-311 Voter turnout was 92.3%.Nohlen & Stöver, p291
