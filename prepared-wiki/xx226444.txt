[[Ibtisam Lutfi]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Saudi Arabian female singers, Blind musicians

Ibtisam Lutfi ([tpl]lang-ar|ابتسام لطفي[/tpl]) is a blind Saudi singer. She was born Khayriyyah Qurban ([tpl]lang-ar|خيرية قربان[/tpl]) in Saudi Arabia, and is one of the first female singers[tpl]cite news|url=http://archive.arabnews.com/?page=1&section=0&article=102185&d=8&m=10&y=2007&pix=kingdom.jpg&category=Kingdom|title=Book Celebrates Achievements of Saudi Women|last=Mubarak|first=Ebtihal|date=8 October 2007|work=Arab News|accessdate=22 April 2011[/tpl] of that country. She began her career singing at wedding feasts in the city of Jiddah in the 1960s and over the next two decades she became famous in her native country as well as in Egypt. She retired from singing in the mid-1980s due to her mother's death. In a phone call with al arabya channel in November 17, 2013 she confirmed that she will be returning to her singing career.
