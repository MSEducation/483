Edin Pehlić (born January 13, 1984 in Stolac, SR Bosnia and Herzegovina, SFR Yugoslavia) is a Bosnian footballer, playing for Velež Mostar.

==Career==

Pehlić has played for FK Sarajevo in the Premier League of Bosnia and Herzegovina and FBK Kaunas in the Lithuanian First Division.Edin Pehlič (g. 1984 m.) - Futbolinis He joined in the spring of 2008 on loan to FC MTZ-RIPO of Belarus and returned in Winter 2009 to FBK Kaunas. On 6 January 2011 signed with his former club FK Sarajevo. After one year with Sarajevo, signed on 22 Januar 2012 for League rival Velez Mostar.http://www.info-market.ba/bs/sport/24746--edin-pehli-prelazi-u-vele-
