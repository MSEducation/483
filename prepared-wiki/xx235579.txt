[[State Opera Stara Zagora]]

CATEGORIES: Opera houses in Bulgaria, Performing groups established in 1925, Stara Zagora, Ballet venues, Ballet companies, Bulgarian opera companies, Buildings and structures in Stara Zagora Province, Visitor attractions in Stara Zagora Province

The State Opera Stara Zagora ([tpl]lang-bg|Държавна опера Стара Загора[/tpl], Darzhavna opera Stara Zagora), founded in 1925, is the second opera theatre in Bulgaria, after the National Opera and Ballet in Sofia. It has the first purpose-built opera house building in Bulgaria.
