[[Hulland]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Derbyshire, Civil parishes in Derbyshire

Hulland is a village and civil parish in the Derbyshire Dales district of Derbyshire, England, adjoining the A517 road. It had a population of 215 according to the 2011 census.[tpl]cite web|title=Hulland (Parish): Key Figures for 2011 Cencus: Key Statistics|url=http://www.neighbourhood.statistics.gov.uk/dissemination/LeadKeyFigures.do?a=7&b=11124878&c=Hulland&d=16&e=62&g=6413695&i=1001x1003x1032x1004&m=0&r=1&s=1391593453369&enc=1|work=Neighbourhood Stastics|publisher=Office for National Statistics|accessdate=5 February 2014[/tpl]

==Population==

The population of Hulland has not changed much over the years, in 1881 the population was 202[tpl]cite web|title=Vision of Britain|url=http://www.visionofbritain.org.uk/unit/10127695/cube/TOT_POP|accessdate=19 March 2014[/tpl] and in 2011 the population was recorded at 215.[tpl]cite web|title=Hulland (parish)|url=http://www.neighbourhood.statistics.gov.uk/dissemination/LeadKeyFigures.do?a=7&b=11124878&c=Hulland&d=16&e=62&g=6413695&i=1001x1003x1032x1004&m=0&r=1&s=1395237706681&enc=1|publisher=Office for National Statistics|accessdate=19 March 2014[/tpl] The biggest change in population was between 1951 and 1961. Between these years the population dropped by 25. In 2011 The mean age of people in Hulland was 41.2 years of age and the median was 45 years of age.[tpl]cite web|last=Hulland (parish)|title=2011 Cencus data|url=http://www.neighbourhood.statistics.gov.uk/dissemination/LeadTableView.do?a=7&b=11124878&c=hulland&d=16&e=62&g=6413695&i=1001x1003x1032x1004&m=0&r=1&s=1395689043012&enc=1&dsFamilyId=2474|publisher=Office for national statistics|accessdate=24 March 2014[/tpl] 28.8 percent of the population was aged 45 to 59 in 2011 This is much higher than the Derbyshire district average (23.2 percent), the east midlands average (20 percent)and the national average (19.4 percent). Also in 2011 15.8 percent was aged 30 to 44.[tpl]cite web|last=Hulland (parish)|title=2011 Cencus data|url=http://www.neighbourhood.statistics.gov.uk/dissemination/LeadTableView.do?a=7&b=11124878&c=hulland&d=16&e=62&g=6413695&i=1001x1003x1032x1004&m=0&r=1&s=1395689043012&enc=1&dsFamilyId=2474|publisher=Office for national statistics|accessdate=24 March 2014[/tpl]
