[[Marc Gomez]]

CATEGORIES: French male cyclists, French Vuelta a España stage winners, 1954 births, Living people, People from Rennes

Marc Gomez (born September 19, 1954 in Rennes) is a French former professional road bicycle racer. He has Spanish heritage, as his parents were born in Torrelavega (Spain). He was a professional from 1982 until 1989.
