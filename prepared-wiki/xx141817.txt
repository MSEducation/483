[[Arthur Solinap]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 births, Living people, Filipino actors, People from Manila, Filipino people of Spanish descent, People from Iloilo

Arthur Gonzales Solinap, known as Arthur Solinap, (born in Manila on January 19, 1980) is a Filipino actor and model.
Solinap's career began as a member of Abztract Dancers together with his cousin Dingdong Dantes. He made his first TV appearance in 2005 portraying Muros in Encantadia. Solinap and Dantes work together in GMA primetime shows like MariMar, Atlantika and Encantadia. Solinap played Diego on 2007-2008 series of MariMar.
