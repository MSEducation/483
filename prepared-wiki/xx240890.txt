[[Guangling County]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Shanxi

Guangling County(Chinese: 广灵县) is in the northeast of Shanxi province, China. It is under the administration of Datong city. Guangling is a basin surrounded by Taihang Mountains. The temperature ranges from -34℃ to 38.2℃, average 7℃. Guangling has nine township level divisions. Famous tourist attractions include: Temple of Water God(水神堂), Temple of Holy Spring(圣泉寺), Zhao Great Wall(赵长城). Local products include: papercut, millet, sunflower seeds, dry Tofu etc.
