[[Theodor Emandi]]

CATEGORIES: Romanian diplomats, 1868 births, 1942 births, 1942 deaths

Theodor Emandi (1868–1942) was a Romanian diplomat.  He was son of Gheorghieş Emandi (1822–1904) prefect of Brăila and Covurlui counties and of his second wife Catinca Tuduri. His wife, Elena was daughter of the writer Nicolae Gane.
Theodor Emandi was Romanian minister plenipotentiary to Belgrade and Prague.
