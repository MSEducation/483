[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Bauchi]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic Church in Nigeria, Roman Catholic dioceses in Nigeria, Religious organizations established in 1996, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Bauchi ([tpl]lang-la|Bauchian(us)[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Bauchi in Bauchi State in the Ecclesiastical province of Jos in Nigeria.
