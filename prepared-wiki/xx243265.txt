[[Hypocoena rufostrigata]]

CATEGORIES: Apameini, Moths of Canada

Hypocoena rufostrigata Poole, R. W. (1989). Lepidopterorum Catalogus (New Series) Fascicle 118, Noctuidae. CRC Press. ISBN 0-916846-45-8, ISBN 978-0-916846-45-9. is a species of moth of the Noctuidae family. It is found along the Atlantic coast of North America north to the North West Territories and Alaska, south in the west to California and Utah.
The wingspan is 26–30 mm. Adults are on wing from June to September. There is one generation per year, although there may be a partial second brood.
