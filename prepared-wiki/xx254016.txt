[[International Socialist Congress, Amsterdam 1904]]

CATEGORIES: History of socialism, Second International

The International Socialist Congress, Amsterdam 1904 was the Sixth Congress of the Second International It was held from 14 to 18 August 1904.Flashlights of the Amsterdam Congress, by Daniel De Leon, New York Labour News Company, New York, 1904
The Congress was held in the Gebow, Amsterdam.
