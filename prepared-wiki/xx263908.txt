[[TSS Galtee More (1898)]]

CATEGORIES: 1898 ships, Passenger ships of the United Kingdom, Steamships, Clyde-built ships, Ships of the London and North Western Railway

TSS ''Galtee More'' was a twin screw steamer passenger vessel operated by the London and North Western Railway from 1898 to 1923.Railway and Other Steamers, Duckworth. 1962

==History==

She was built by William Denny and Brothers of Dumbarton for the London and North Western Railway in 1898. She was named after Galtymore, the highest of the Galtee Mountains in Ireland.
