[[Common Man]]

CATEGORIES: 1981 singles, 1983 singles, Billboard Hot Country Songs number-one singles, John Conlee songs, Sammy Johns songs, RPM Country Tracks number-one singles, Elektra Records singles, MCA Records singles, Songs written by Sammy Johns, Song recordings produced by James Stroud

"Common Man" is a song written by Sammy Johns and originally recorded by him in 1981 via Elektra Records. Johns's version charted at number 50 on Hot Country Songs in 1981.[tpl]cite book|last=Whitburn|first=Joel|title=Hot Country Songs 1944 to 2008|publisher=Record Research, Inc|date=2008|page=211|isbn=0-89820-177-2[/tpl] It had "Easy to Be with You" on the B-side, and was produced by James Stroud and Tom Long.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.45cat.com/record/47189|title='Common Man'|work=45cat.com|accessdate=20 March 2014[/tpl]
It was later recorded by American country music artist John Conlee.  It was released in February 1983 as the fourth single from the album Busted.  "Common Man" was John Conlee's third number one on the country chart.  It became his first number one since 'Backside of Thirty" in 1979.  The single stayed at number one for one week.Whitburn, p. 102[/ref]
