[[Fozzy Whittaker]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 births, Living people, American football running backs, Texas Longhorns football players, Undrafted National Football League players, Arizona Cardinals players, San Diego Chargers players, Cleveland Browns players

Foswhitt "Fozzy" Whittaker (born February 2, 1989)[tpl]cite web|url=http://sportsillustrated.cnn.com/football/ncaa/players/63899/index.html|title=Foswhitt Whittaker|date=Time Warner|work=Sports illustrated|accessdate=10 January 2010[/tpl] is an American football running back who plays for the Cleveland Browns of the National Football League. He was the halfback for the Texas Longhorns during the 2011 season.[tpl]cite news|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081122152921/http://www.dallasnews.com/sharedcontent/dws/spt/colleges/texas/stories/090808dnspoutwhittaker.13c097a.html|title=Texas Longhorns' Foswhitt Whittaker sparkles in debut|last=GEORGE|first=BRANDON|date=September 10, 2008|work=The Dallas Morning News|publisher=The Dallas Morning News, Inc|accessdate=10 January 2010|location=Dallas, Texas[/tpl] He is from Pearland, Texas.

==Professional career==

===Arizona Cardinals===

On December 18, 2012, Whittaker signed with the Arizona Cardinals to join their practice squad.[tpl]cite web | title= Cardinals sign RB Whittaker to practice squad  | work= arizonasports.com | url=http://arizonasports.com/?sid=1596418&nid=40 |accessdate= December 18, 2012[/tpl]
On March 11, 2013, he was released by the Cardinals.[tpl]cite web | last=Urban |first=Darren |title=Beanie Wells Released |url=http://www.azcardinals.com/news-and-events/article-2/Beanie-Wells-Released/bcff2176-67a1-4c7d-87c6-aa2cf9de62f7 |publisher=azcardinals.com |date=March 11, 2013 |accessdate=2013-03-11[/tpl]

===San Diego Chargers===

On March 12, 2013, Whittaker was claimed off waivers by the San Diego Chargers.
On September 28, 2013, he was released by the Chargers.

===Cleveland Browns===
