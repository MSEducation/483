[[Alien Crush Returns]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 video games, Crush Pinball, Hudson Soft games, WiiWare games, Wii-only games, Wii Wi-Fi games, Wii games

Alien Crush Returns is a WiiWare pinball video game developed by Tamsoft and published by Hudson Soft. It is a sequel/remake to 1988's Alien Crush, and is the fourth installment in the Crush Pinball series. It was released in Japan on August 26, 2008, in North America on November 3, 2008,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.wiiware-world.com/news/2008/11/usa_wiiware_update_alien_crush_returns_and_home_sweet_home |title=USA WiiWare Update: Alien Crush Returns and Home Sweet Home |publisher=Wiiware-world.com |date=2008-11-03 |accessdate=2010-05-21[/tpl] and in Europe on November 7, 2008.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.wiiware-world.com/news/2008/11/eu_wiiware_update_alien_crush_returns_brain_challenge_and_space_invaders_get_even |title=EU WiiWare Update: Alien Crush Returns, Brain Challenge and Space Invaders Get Even |publisher=Wiiware-world.com |date=2008-11-06 |accessdate=2010-05-21[/tpl]

==Overview==

In comparison to the original game, Alien Crush Returns features 3D graphics and controls using the Wii Remote and Nunchuk that allow functions such as tilting the table using motion sensing. In addition, the game introduces "Action Balls", pinballs with special attributes that can be used repeatedly during gameplay. Downloadable content in the form of extra stages and additional Action Balls will also be made available.[tpl]cite web|author= Spencer|url=http://www.siliconera.com/2008/08/04/tilt-the-table-in-alien-crush-returns/ |title=Tilt the table in Alien Crush Returns |publisher=Siliconera.com|date= 2008-08-04 |accessdate=2010-05-21[/tpl]
The game features a high score-based arcade mode where players can select from three tables, a six-stage story mode with gameplay goals and boss battles,[tpl]cite web|url=http://blog.wired.com/games/2008/08/impressions-ali.html |title=Impressions: Alien Crush Returns, Hooray |publisher= Blog.wired.com |date=2008-08-26 |accessdate=2010-05-21[/tpl] as well as online versus multiplayer and leaderboards.

==Reception==

IGN scored Alien Crush Returns a 7.8/10, calling the visual presentation impressive but noting some slight ball physics issues and that overall the game was short, with only three tables out of the box.[tpl]cite web|last=Bozon |first=Mark |url=http://wii.ign.com/articles/926/926638p1.html |title=IGN Alien Crush Returns Review |publisher=IGN|date=2008-11-03 |accessdate=2010-05-21| archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20100428225955/http://wii.ign.com/articles/926/926638p1.html| archivedate= 28 April 2010 | deadurl= no[/tpl]
