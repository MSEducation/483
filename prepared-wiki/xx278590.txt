[[Owch Bolagh, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Owch Bolagh ([tpl]lang-fa|اوچ بلاغ[/tpl], also Romanized as Owch Bolāgh and Ūch Bolāgh; also known as Uch-Bulag, Ūchbūlāgh, and Uchbulāq)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3087919|Owch Bolagh[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 223, in 54 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
