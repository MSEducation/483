[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Rondonópolis]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1940, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Cuiabá, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Rondonópolis ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Rondonopolitanus[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Rondonópolis in the Ecclesiastical province of Cuiabá in Brazil.
