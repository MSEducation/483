[[Buin va Miandasht District]]

CATEGORIES: Buin va Miandasht County, Faridan County, Former districts of Iran

Buin va Miandasht District ([tpl]lang-fa|بخش بوئین و میاندشت[/tpl], meaning Buin and Miandasht) was a district (bakhsh) in Faridan County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 27,586, in 6,666 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|10[/tpl]  The District had two cities: Buin va Miandasht and Afus. The district was promoted to county status and superseded by Buin va Miandasht County.
