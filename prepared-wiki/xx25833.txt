[[Antigone (Sophocles)]]

CATEGORIES: Plays by Sophocles, Theban mythology, Death customs, Books in political philosophy, Political philosophy in ancient Greece, Civil disobedience

Antigone ([tpl]IPAc-en|æ|n|ˈ|t|ɪ|ɡ|ə|n|iː[/tpl] [tpl]respell|an|TIG|ə-nee[/tpl], [tpl]lang-grc|Ἀντιγόνη[/tpl]) is a tragedy by Sophocles written in or before 441 BC. Chronologically, it is the third of the three Theban plays but was written first.[ref]
