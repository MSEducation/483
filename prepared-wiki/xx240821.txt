[[Zhenxing District]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Liaoning

Zhenxing District ([tpl]zh|s=振兴区 |t=振興區 |p=Zhènxīng Qū[/tpl]) is a district of the city of Dandong, Liaoning, People's Republic of China.

==Administrative Divisions丹东市-行政区划网==

There are 9 subdistricts and 1 town in the district.

The only town is Tangchi (汤池镇)
