[[Asahi Firearms]]

CATEGORIES: Airsoft, Companies of Japan

Asahi Firearms was a model company from Japan who operated in the 1980s and 90s. It should not to be confused with the Asahi company that makes cameras. Asahi firearms was one of the early pioneers of the Airsoft hobby. They created  guns during the Classic Airsoft period.[tpl]cite book|title=Air Soft Guns|pages=Homepage|url=http://www.airsoftatlanta.com/[/tpl]
