[[(35198) 1994 PM1]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1994, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(35198) 1994 PM1 is an inner main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on August 9, 1994.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
