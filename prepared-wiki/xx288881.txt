[[Qui, Iran]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Qui ([tpl]lang-fa|قوئي[/tpl], also Romanized as Qū’ī; also known as Ghoo’i and Qūyu)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3080472|Qui[/tpl] is a village in Shivanat Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 949, in 177 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
