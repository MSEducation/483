[[William Lamb Picknell]]

CATEGORIES: 1853 births, 1897 deaths, People from Hinesburg, Vermont, 19th-century American painters, Vermont culture, People from Marblehead, Massachusetts, American alumni of the École des Beaux-Arts, Pont-Aven painters, National Academy of Design associates

William Lamb Picknell (October 23, 1853 – August 8, 1897) was a United States painter of landscapes, coastal views, and figure genres, known for his rapid painting style. He was born in Hinesburg, Vermont and died in Marblehead, Massachusetts.
