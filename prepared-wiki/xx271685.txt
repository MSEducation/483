[[Lawton, Wisconsin]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Pierce County, Wisconsin, Unincorporated communities in Wisconsin

Lawton is an unincorporated community located in the towns of Ellsworth and Martell, Pierce County, Wisconsin, United States.
