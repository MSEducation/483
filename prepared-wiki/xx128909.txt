[[A Vintage Year]]

CATEGORIES: Mel Tormé live albums, George Shearing live albums, 1987 live albums, Concord Records live albums, Vocal–instrumental duet albums

 A Vintage Year is a 1987 (see 1987 in music) live album by the American jazz singer Mel Tormé, accompanied by George Shearing.
