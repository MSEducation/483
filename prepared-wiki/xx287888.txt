[[Oliver Møystad]]

CATEGORIES: 1892 births, 1956 deaths, Norwegian farmers, Norwegian engineers, Norwegian landowners, Norwegian Army personnel of World War II, Members of Nasjonal Samling, People convicted of treason against Norway

 Oliver Møystad (1892–1956) was a Norwegian engineer, farmer and forest owner. During the Norwegian Campaign in April 1940 he commanded Norwegian troops at the Battle of Midtskogen.
