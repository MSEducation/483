[[Ville Lajunen]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Espoo Blues players, 1988 births, Färjestad BK players

Ville Lajunen (born March 8, 1988) is a Finnish professional ice hockey defenceman who currently plays for Metallurg Magnitogorsk in the Kontinental Hockey League.
While playing for the Espoo Blues, he led the SM-liiga in assists (32) by a defenseman in the 2009–10 season.
On November 18, 2011, Lajunen left Espoo during the final year of his contract to join Metallurg Magnitogorsk in the KHL.http://www.metallurg.ru/site/news/read/68607/
