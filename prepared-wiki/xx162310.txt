Riznice Sjecanja is an unplugged album by the Bosnian pop band Crvena Jabuka. This album was released in 1999. It includes many of the band's best hit singles in unplugged format and was recorded on 13 March and 14 March of that same year in Zagreb's OTV auditorium.[tpl]citation needed|date=March 2012[/tpl]
