[[Project 6 Cyclemind]]

CATEGORIES: 6cyclemind albums, 2009 albums

Project 6 Cyclemind  is the 5th album of the Filipino  band, 6cyclemind. Having 12 tracks, it was released by Sony Music  in 2009. It was the last album to Feature Ney Dimaculangan  as Lead Vocalist. This album gained applause and kudos from radio stations and music channels as all singles released became chart toppers.

==Track listing and duration==

The last single,Kung Wala Na Nga earned kudos from Radio Stations and became 2010 Song Of The Year for OPM on Myx Philippines
