[[Cigarroa Middle School]]

CATEGORIES: Laredo Independent School District, Educational institutions with year of establishment missing, Public middle schools in Texas, Schools in Laredo, Texas

Cigarroa Middle School is a middle school that belongs to the Laredo Independent School District.
It serves students from 6th through 8th grade.
This middle school carries students from South Laredo.
Cigarroa middle school colors are red and yellow and their mascot is a bulldog.
