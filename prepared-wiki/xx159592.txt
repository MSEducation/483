[[Aylands School, Enfield]]

CATEGORIES: Educational institutions established in 1969, Special schools in Enfield, 1969 establishments in England, Community schools in Enfield

Aylands School is a coeducational special school for children who have emotional or behavioural difficulties.http://www.aylands.demon.co.uk/ It was founded in 1969, and provides for up to 40 pupils in year groups 3-16. The present number of children on the roll is 35.
