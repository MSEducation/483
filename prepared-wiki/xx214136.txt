[[Alexander of Cotiaeum]]

CATEGORIES: Ancient Greek grammarians, 2nd-century Greek people, Ancient Greeks in Rome, Roman Anatolia, Year of birth unknown, Year of death unknown

Alexander (Gr. [tpl]lang|grc|Ἀλέξανδρος[/tpl]) of Cotiaeum was a Greek grammarian, who is mentioned among the instructors of the Roman emperor Marcus Aurelius.Capitolinus, M.
