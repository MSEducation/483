[[Autumn (Subtle EP)]]

CATEGORIES: 2002 EPs, Subtle albums, Hip hop EPs

Autumn is the second EP by American alternative hip hop sextet Subtle. It was released in 2002 on the A Purple 100 label.[tpl]cite web|title=Subtle - Summer|publisher=discogs|url=http://www.discogs.com/Subtle-Autumn/release/182326[/tpl] It is now out of print. 
The tracks "Arsenic Chic" and "Earthsick" also appear on Earthsick, a compilation of material from the group's Season EPs.
