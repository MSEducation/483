Boston ([tpl]IPAc-en|audio=en-uk-Boston.ogg|ˈ|b|ɒ|s|t|ən[/tpl]) is a town and small port in Lincolnshire, on the east coast of England. It is the largest town of the wider Borough of Boston local government district. The borough had a total population of 64,637 at the 2011 census,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ons.gov.uk/ons/rel/census/2011-census/population-and-household-estimates-for-england-and-wales/rft-p04.xls|title=2011 Census: Population and household estimates for England and Wales|date=16 July 2012|publisher=Office for National Statistics|format=Excel|accessdate=16 July 2012[/tpl] whilst the town itself had a population of 35,124 at the 2001 census.Census 2001 Key Statistics, Urban areas in England and Wales - ONS It is due north of Greenwich on the Prime Meridian.
