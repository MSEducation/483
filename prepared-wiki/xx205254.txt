Nick Deacy (born 19 July 1953) is a Welsh former footballer who played as a striker.
He used to play for PSV Eindhoven, and won the UEFA Cup with them in 1978, coming on as a substitute in the second leg of the final.[tpl]cite web|url=http://home.wxs.nl/~rapo/psv/europacup/uefacup.html|title=Season 1977-1978|last=Raymond Posthouwer|work=UEFA Cup|publisher=PSV Eindhoven Wedstrijdhistorie|language=Dutch|accessdate=15 May 2012[/tpl]
After a spell with Vitesse he signed for Hull City in 1980 and made 87 Football League appearances for them.Football League Career Stats at Neil Brown – Hull players
He was capped on 12 occasions by the Wales national football team, and he scored on his debut for them against European champions CzechoslovakiaWalesonline.co.uk, Time to Czech out the chances of qualifying – Ian Carbis 01/09/06 in March 1977.
