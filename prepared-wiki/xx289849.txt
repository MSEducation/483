[[Mirza, Kamrup]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Kamrup district, Kamrup district, Western Assam

Mirza is a town in Kamrup rural district of Assam, situated in south bank of river Brahmaputra.[tpl]cite web|url=http://pincode.net.in/ASSAM/KAMRUP/B/Mirza#.Uchvndht2aY |title=Mirza |publisher=pincode.net.in |date= |accessdate=2013-08-14[/tpl] 

==Transport==

The town is near National Highway 37, and connected to nearby towns and cities with regular buses and other modes of transportation. Mirza has Railway station, and nearest airport is Guwahati airport.
