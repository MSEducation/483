[[Pik BAM]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of Russia, Chita Oblast, Geography of Zabaykalsky Krai

Pik BAM is a mountain in Zabaykalsky Krai, Russia. It reaches [tpl]convert|3072|m|ft|0[/tpl] above sea level. It was named after the Baikal Amur Mainline railway, which passes south of it. It is the highest summit of the Kodar Range. The nearest town is Novaya Chara.
