[[Ultimate Jeopardy]]

CATEGORIES: Extreme Championship Wrestling shows

ECW Ultimate Jeopardy was a professional wrestling supercard event held by Extreme Championship Wrestling (ECW) from 1994 through 1997.

==Results==

===1994===

The 1994 event was held on March 26, 1994 in Valley Forge, Pennsylvania.

===1995===

The 1995 event was scheduled for November 23, but was cancelled.[tpl]cite web|title= ECW 1995 results|url=http://www.thehistoryofwwe.com/ecw95.htm|publisher=The History of WWE[/tpl] The planned main event for that show was re-booked for the December 9 show, with some slight alterations to the lineup, becoming December to Dismember: Ultimate Jeopardy 1995. It was held in the ECW Arena, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

===1996===

The 1996 event was held on October 5, 1996 in the ECW Arena, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

===1997===

Ultimate Jeopardy 1997 was held on November 8, 1997 in the ECW Arena, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.
