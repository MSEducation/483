[[Citharomantis]]

CATEGORIES: Hymenopodidae, Insects of Indonesia, Insects of Asia, Monotypic insect genera

Citharomantis falcata is a species of praying mantis native to Sumatra and Borneo.  It is the sole member of its genus and is related to the genus Acromantis (Saussure, 1870).[tpl]cite web|title= Records of the genus Citharomantis.
