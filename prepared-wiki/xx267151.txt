[[Lu Hsi-chuen]]

CATEGORIES: Taiwanese golfers, Japan Golf Tour golfers, Year of birth missing (living people), Living people

Lu Hsi-chuen is a Taiwanese professional golfer.
Lu won several national opens throughout southeast Asia. Lu also played on the Japan Golf Tour, winning twice.
Lu is a golf instructor at Sunrise Golf Academy in Yangmei, Taiwan.Sunrise - Golf - Faculty
