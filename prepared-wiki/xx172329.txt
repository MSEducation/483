[[Vladykov's lamprey]]

CATEGORIES: Eudontomyzon, Fish of Austria, Fish of Germany, Fish of the Czech Republic, Fish of Bulgaria, Fish of Romania, Fish of Serbia, Fish of Montenegro

Vladykov's lamprey (Eudontomyzon vladykovi) is a species of fish in the Petromyzontidae family. It is found in Austria, Germany, The Czech Republic, Bulgaria, Romania, Serbia, and Montenegro.
