[[Laurel, Washington]]

CATEGORIES: Lynden, Washington, Unincorporated communities in Washington (state), Unincorporated communities in Whatcom County, Washington

Laurel is an unincorporated community in Whatcom County, Washington, United States.Laurel statistics It lies between the cities of Bellingham and Lynden. Today Laurel is included in the Lynden metropolitan statistical area. Laurel is landlocked.
