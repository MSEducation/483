[[Wedel station]]

CATEGORIES: Hamburg S-Bahn stations, Railway stations in Schleswig-Holstein

Wedel station is a railway station on the Altona-Blankenese line, serviced by the rapid transit trains of the Hamburg S-Bahn, located in Wedel, Germany.Name, station code and category: Liste Bahnhofskategorie 2008, DB Station&Service AG, Köthener Straße 2, 10963 Berlin (2008) [tpl]de icon[/tpl]
It is a terminus of the line S1.
