[[Pierre Johnsson]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Luleå HF players, 1984 births, Calgary Flames draft picks, Frölunda HC players

Pierre Johnsson (born February 7, 1984) is a Swedish former professional ice hockey defenceman, who last played for Frölunda HC of the Elitserien. He was selected by the Calgary Flames in the 7th round (207th overall) of the 2002 NHL Entry Draft.
Johnsson played the 2010-11 season with Luleå HF of the Elitserien.Lulea HF's 2010-11 Roster In November 2013, Johnsson officially announced his retirement.
