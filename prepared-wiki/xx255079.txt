[[Masanobu Matsunami]]

CATEGORIES: 1974 births, Living people, People from Gifu Prefecture, Japanese footballers, J. League Division 1 players, Gamba Osaka players, Japanese football managers, Gamba Osaka managers, Gainare Tottori managers

[tpl]nihongo|Masanobu Matsunami|松波 正信|Matsunami Masanobu[/tpl] (born 21 November 1974 in Gifu) is a Japanese former professional football player who spent his entire senior career at Gamba Osaka. On 20 November 1993 he became the youngest player to score a hat trick in J. League Division 1 at age 18 years, 364 days. 
On 26 March 2012 Matsunami was appointed as the head coach of Gamba Osaka.
