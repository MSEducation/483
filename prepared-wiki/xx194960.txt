[[New English High School]]

CATEGORIES: High schools in India, Schools in Maharashtra

New English High School is a high school in Kulgaon, Badlapur, India. The school is the first school in Badlapur to offer education till Standard Tenth in English medium.
Late Shri S. K. Shetkar, popularly known as Bhai Shetkar, was founder of the school, and the family trust used to run the school. Now the school has been taken over by Mr.Kisan Kathore, MLA of Maharashtra.
The school used to be located near Vivekanand Temple; it was later shifted to Vivekanand Nagar close to Adarsh Vidyamandir
