[[Enda Condron]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, Ballylinan Gaelic footballers, Laois inter-county Gaelic footballers

Enda Condron is a former Gaelic football player from County Laois. 
He played for many years on the Laois senior football team in the forwards and was widely regarded as one of their outstanding players of the 1970s.
In 1967, he was part of the Laois team that won the Leinster Minor Football Championship and in 1975 he became the first Laoisman to win an All-Ireland Senior Club Football Championship medal when he appeared as a substitute for UCD in their final win.
He assisted his home club Ballylinan to victory in the Laois Intermediate Football Championship in 1973.
