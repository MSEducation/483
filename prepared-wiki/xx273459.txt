[[Garey Mathurin]]

CATEGORIES: Windward Islands cricketers, Saint Lucian cricketers, West Indies Twenty20 International cricketers, St Lucia Zouks cricketers, 1983 births, Living people

Garey Earl Mathurin (born 23 September 1983 in Mon Repos, Saint Lucia) is a cricketer who plays for the West Indies at international level and for the Windward Islands domestically.
He made his Twenty20 International debut for the West Indies on 25 September 2011 against England. He finished with bowling figures of 3/9 and was named as the player of the match.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.espncricinfo.com/england-v-west-indies-2011/engine/current/match/525817.html|title=West Indies in England T20I Series - 2nd T20I| publisher= ESPN Cricinfo| accessdate= 25 September 2011[/tpl] He is just the third cricketer from Saint Lucia to represent the West Indies in international cricket after Johnson Charles (who made his debut just two days before Mathurin) and Darren Sammy, who was captain in Mathurin's first match.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.espncricinfo.com/england-v-west-indies-2011/content/story/532940.html|title=Mathurin replaces Nurse in T20 squad| publisher= ESPN Cricinfo| accessdate= 10 August 2012[/tpl]
