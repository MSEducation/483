[[List of national parks of France]]

CATEGORIES: Lists of national parks, National parks of France, France geography-related lists

The national parks of France is a system of ten national parks throughout metropolitan France and its overseas departments, coordinated by the government agency Parcs Nationaux de France. The French national parks protect a total area of [tpl]convert|3,710|km2|mi2[/tpl] in core area and [tpl]convert|9,162|km2|mi2[/tpl] in buffer zones in metropolitan France. This puts over 2% of the total area of metropolitan France under some level of protection. French national parks draw over seven million visitors every year.
