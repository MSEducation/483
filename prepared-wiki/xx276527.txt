[[Qamsar District]]

CATEGORIES: Kashan County, Districts of Isfahan Province

Qamsar District ([tpl]lang-fa|بخش قمصر[/tpl]) is a district (bakhsh) in Kashan County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 10,812, in 3,409 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|10[/tpl]  The District has two cities: Qamsar and Jowshaqan va Kamu. The District has two rural districts (dehestan): Jowshaqan-e Qali Rural District and Ghohrud Rural District.
