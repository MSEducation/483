[[St. Patrick's Catholic Church (Atchison, Kansas)]]

CATEGORIES: Gothic Revival architecture in Kansas, Roman Catholic churches completed in 1865, 19th-century Roman Catholic church buildings in the United States, Roman Catholic churches in Kansas, Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in Kansas, Churches in Atchison, Kansas

St. Patrick's Catholic Church is a historic Roman Catholic church in Atchison, Kansas. The stone church was built in 1866 and listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1998.
