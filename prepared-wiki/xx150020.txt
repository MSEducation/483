[[Anderton, Cornwall]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Cornwall, Populated coastal places in Cornwall

Anderton is a settlement in south-east Cornwall, England, United Kingdom, located at [tpl]gbmapping|SW 431 519[/tpl].
Anderton is part of Millbrook, a village on the Rame Peninsula. It is situated beside the tidal portion of Millbrook Lake on the Tamar estuary opposite Plymouth.Ordnance Survey: Landranger map sheet 201 Plymouth & Launceston ISBN 978-0-319-23146-3
Captain Charles Bampfield Yule RN, explorer and author of the Admiralty Australia Directory, died in Anderton in 1878.
