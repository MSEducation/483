[[Sibley Fleming]]

CATEGORIES: Centenary College of Louisiana alumni, American women writers, Living people

Sibley Fleming is an American journalist and writer of children's literature and adult non-fiction. She was named Outstanding Writer of 2000 by the National League of American Pen Women and the Georgia Writer of the Year by Georgia Writers.[tpl]cite book |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=NJwtfrKBjN8C&pg=PA59&dq=sibley+fleming&hl=en#v=onepage&q=sibley%20fleming&f=false |page=59 |title=Get Between the Covers: Leave a legacy by writing a book |last1=Shulman |first1=Neil |last2=Spencer |first2=Eric |publisher=Morgan James Publishing|year=2008 |isbn=1-60037-315-1[/tpl]

==Biography==

Fleming was born in Atlanta, Georgia, and attended Centenary College in Shreveport, Louisiana, on a music scholarship. She has worked as a feature writer and freelance weekly columnist for the Atlanta Journal-Constitution and managing editor for National Real Estate Investor magazine (2005â€“2010). She is now the national editor for real estate publications at Bisnow.[tpl]cite web |url= http://www.bisnow.com/team.php |title=The Team |publisher=Bisnow |accessdate=August 10, 2011[/tpl]
Fleming has written and edited seven books, including How to Rock Your Baby, for which she received the 1997 Family Channel Seal of Quality.[tpl]cite web |url= http://www.medicalofficetoday.com/page.asp?page=1 |title=About MOT |publisher=Medical Office Today |year=2009 |accessdate=August 10, 2011[/tpl] In 2000, she was named Outstanding Writer by the National League of American Pen Women and was named Georgia Author of the Year by Georgia Writers for her book, Celestine Sibley: A Granddaughter's Reminiscence, about her grandmother, a well-known Atlanta reporter and newspaper columnist for more than three decades.
Fleming and her husband, a professional geologist and environmental consultant, reside in Avondale Estates, Georgia, where she serves on the vestry of St. Dunstan's Episcopal Church. They have two sons.
