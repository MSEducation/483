[[Phyllota squarrosa]]

CATEGORIES: Mirbelieae, Fabales of Australia, Flora of New South Wales, Plants described in 1837

  
Phyllota squarrosa, commonly known as the dense phyllota, is a species of flowering plant in the pea family (Fabaceae) from eastern Australia. It was given its current name by George Bentham in 1837.[tpl]APNI | name = Phyllota squarrosa (DC.) Benth.  | id = 43261[/tpl]
