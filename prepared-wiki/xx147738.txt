[[Cook Islands Round Cup]]

CATEGORIES: Football competitions in the Cook Islands, National association football premier leagues

Cook Islands Round Cup is the top division of the Cook Islands Football Association in Cook Islands. 2,000 capacity National Stadium (Cook Islands) is one of the venues.
