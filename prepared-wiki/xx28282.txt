[[Ovos Moles de Aveiro]]

CATEGORIES: Aveiro District, Portuguese desserts, Egg dishes, Portuguese products with protected designation of origin, Products with protected designation of origin

Ovos Moles de Aveiro ("soft eggs from Aveiro", literally) is a local delicacy from Aveiro District, Portugal, made of egg yolks and sugar.
This is, since 2006, a product with Protected Geographical Indication.[tpl]cite web|title=Commission Regulation (EC) N.ยบ 510/2006|url=http://eur-lex.europa.eu/LexUriServ/LexUriServ.do?uri=OJ:C:2008:184:0042:0045:EN:PDF|author=European Commission|accessdate=14 December 2012|date=22 July 2008|publisher=Official Journal of the European Union|location=Brussels, Belgium[/tpl]
