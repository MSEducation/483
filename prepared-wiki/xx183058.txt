[[Santa Clara, Coimbra]]

CATEGORIES: Parishes of Coimbra Municipality

Santa Clara is a Portuguese parish in the municipality of Coimbra. It has  a population of 9637 (2001) and an area of 9.77 km². It is an urban parish, in the city Coimbra, and on the south (left bank) of the Mondego river.
