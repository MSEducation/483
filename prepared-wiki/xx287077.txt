[[Sarab, Ijrud]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Sarab ([tpl]lang-fa|سراب[/tpl], also Romanized as Sarāb and Serab)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3082401|Sarab[/tpl] is a village in Golabar Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 54, in 13 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
