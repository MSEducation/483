[[Andabad-e Olya]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Andabad-e Olya ([tpl]lang-fa|اندابادعليا[/tpl], also Romanized as Andābād-e ‘Olyā; also known as And Ābād, Andābād-e Bālā, Andābād Oliab, and Andābād ‘Ulīāb)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3053178|Andabad-e Olya[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 531, in 146 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
