[[Agatówka, Kraśnik County]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Kraśnik County

Agatówka [tpl]IPAc-pl|a|g|a|'|t|u|f|k|a[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Trzydnik Duży, within Kraśnik County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl]
The village has a population of 150.
