[[Gooch House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses completed in 1895, Houses in Frankfort, Kentucky, Queen Anne architecture in Kentucky, National Register of Historic Places in Franklin County, Kentucky, Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Kentucky

The Gooch House is a Queen Anne style house located in Frankfort, Kentucky. Currently, the building houses the Frankfort Visitor's Center and the offices of the Frankfort/Franklin County Tourist & Convention Commission, the Frankfort Area Chamber of Commerce, and the Downtown Frankfort, Inc.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.visitfrankfort.com/WhattodoinFrankfortduringtheFEIGames/tabid/1624/Default.aspx|title=Destination Frankfort|publisher=www.visitfrankfort.com|accessdate=2008-08-19[/tpl] The property was added to the U.S. National Register of Historic Places in 1980.
