Vinko Jelovac (born September 18, 1948 in Osijek, Croatiahttp://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/je/vinko-jelovac-1.html), is a former Slovenian basketball player and coach. 
A long time KK Olimpijahttp://www.adriaticbasket.com/finalfour2004/olimpia_legends_si.shtml player, Jelovac competed at 1972 and 1976 Summer Olympics with Yugoslav basketball team. He also competed at World basketball Championships. Jelovac was named Slovenian Sportsman of the year in 1973 and 1974.
