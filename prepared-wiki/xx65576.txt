Phyllis Fay Gotlieb, née Bloom (May 25, 1926 – July 14, 2009)Phyllis Gotlieb Service Details BA, MA was a Canadian science fiction novelist and poet.
Born of Jewish heritageBiography in Toronto, Gotlieb graduated from the University of Toronto with degrees in literature in 1948 (BA) and 1950 (MA).
The Sunburst Award is named for her first novel, Sunburst.The Sunburst Award  Three years before Sunburst was published, Gotlieb published the pamphlet Who Knows One, a collection of poems.Phyllis Fay Gotlieb at The Canadian Encyclopedia Gotlieb won the Prix Aurora Award for Best Novel in 1982 for her novel A Judgement of Dragons.
Her widower is Calvin Gotlieb, a computer science professor; they lived in Toronto, Ontario.
