[[Marc-André Craig]]

CATEGORIES: 1982 births, Canadian male single skaters, French Quebecers, Living people, People from Sherbrooke

Marc-André Craig (born October 21, 1982 in Sherbrooke, Quebec) is a Canadian figure skater. He placed fourth at the 2006 Four Continents Championships. It his first time competing there. He is also a coach.
