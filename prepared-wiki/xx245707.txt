[[Herbert Hoover High School (Fresno, California)]]

CATEGORIES: High schools in Fresno County, California, Public high schools in California

Herbert Hoover High School is a public secondary school in the Fresno Unified School District serving Fresno, California, USA, in northern Fresno County. It is named for Herbert Hoover, the 31st President of the United States. Its mascot is The Patriot.

==Performance==
