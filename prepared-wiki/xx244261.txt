[[De Cock Late Night]]

CATEGORIES: Belgian radio programmes, 2009 radio programme debuts, Talk radio programs

De Cock Late Night, also being called DCLN, is a radio show on the Flemish radio MNM presented by Tom De Cock.  
The show is airing from Monday till Thursday between 21h - 24h. The first show aired started at January 5, 2009.
Listeners can choose their own subjects during the show.
During the program there is a lot of improvising going on but there are some recurrent sections like Geen Nieuws om 23 uur met Tom De Cock and Sh*t On Tha Radio.
