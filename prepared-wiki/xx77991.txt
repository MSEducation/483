[[Shkodër District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Albania, Shkodër County, Shkodër District

The District of Shkodër ([tpl]Lang-sq|Rrethi i Shkodrës[/tpl], short name: Shkodër, variants: Rrethi Shkodër and Shkodra) is one of the thirty-six districts of Albania, part of Shkodër County. It has a population of 185,646[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.instat.gov.al/graphics/doc/tabelat/Treguesit%20Sociale/Popullsia/POP%202009/t2.xls|title=POPULLSIA SIPAS RRETHEVE, 2001-2010|publisher=Albanian Institute of Statistics|accessdate=2010-09-09[/tpl] (2010 estimate), and an area of 1,973 km². It is in the north of the country, and its capital is Shkodër. The district consists of the following municipalities:
The area includes significant tourist attractions, such as Rozafa Castle (fortress of Shkodra), one of the biggest and most famous castles in Albania, with the 13-14th century St. Stephen's Church in its court, later converted to a mosque, the Ottoman era Historical Museum building (1815) and the National Photo Gallery.
