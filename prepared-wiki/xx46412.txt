[[Oreye]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Liège (province)

Oreye is a Walloon municipality located in the Belgian province of Liège. On January 1, 2006 Oreye had a total population of 3,464. The total area is 19.64 km² which gives a population density of 176 inhabitants per km². It includes the former communes of Lens-sur-Geer, Grandville, Bergilers, and Otrange.
