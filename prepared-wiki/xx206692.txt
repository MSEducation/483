Studienzentrum Weikersheim (Weikersheim Think Tank) is a conservative and christian democratic German political think tank, that was founded in 1979 by Hans Filbinger, Helmut Metzner and others on Schloss Weikersheim in Germany. http://www.studienzentrum-weikersheim.de
