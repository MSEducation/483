[[Jewel Geyser]]

CATEGORIES: Geothermal features of Yellowstone National Park, Geysers of Wyoming, Geothermal features of Teton County, Wyoming

Jewel Geyser is a fountain geyser in the Upper Geyser Basin of Yellowstone National Park in the United States. It is in the Biscuit Basin complex that includes Shell Geyser, Avoca Spring, and Mustard Spring.[tpl]cite web |title=Biscuit Basin |publisher=National Park Service |url=http://www.nps.gov/archive/yell/tours/oldfaithful/saphires.htm[/tpl]
