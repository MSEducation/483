[[Idgah railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Agra, Railway junction stations in India, Agra railway division

Idgah railway station is in Idgah colony in the south west of the city of Agra in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh.

==Overview==

Agra, the 16-17th century capital of the Mughals, is home to monuments such as the Taj Mahal and Agra Fort.[tpl]cite web| url = http://www.makemytrip.com/railways/agra.html |title = Agra Railway Station| publisher= Make my trip| accessdate = 2 July 2013[/tpl] The Taj Mahal attracts 2-4 million tourists annually. About 200,000 foreign tourists visit it.[tpl]cite web| url = http://tajmahal.gov.in/taj_visitors.html |title = Taj Visitors| publisher= Department of Tourism, Govt. of Uttar Pradesh| accessdate = 2 July 2013[/tpl]

==History==

The [tpl]RailGauge|1000mm[/tpl] wide metre gauge Delhi-Bandikui and Bandikui-Agra lines of Rajputana State Railway were opened in 1874.[tpl]cite web| url = http://www.irfca.org/faq/faq-history2.html|title = IR History:Early Days II (1870-1899)| publisher= IRFCA| accessdate =23 June 2013 [/tpl] The Agra-Jaipur line was converted to [tpl]RailGauge|1676mm[/tpl] wide broad gauge in 2005.[tpl]cite web| url = http://www.hindu.com/2005/05/06/stories/2005050613070500.htm |title = Boost for Jaipur-Agra rail link | publisher= The Hindu, 6 May 2005| accessdate =2 July 2013 [/tpl]
The broad gauge Agra-Delhi chord was opened in 1904.[tpl]cite web| url = http://www.irfca.org/faq/faq-history3.html|title = IR History: Part III (1900-1947)| publisher= IRFCA| accessdate =2 July 2013 [/tpl]

==Electrification==

The Faridabad-Mathura-Agra section was electrified in 1982-85, Tundla-Yamuna Bridge in 1998-99 and Yamuna Bridge-Agra in 1990-91.[tpl]cite web| url = http://irfca.org/docs/electrification-history.html|title = History of Electrification| publisher= IRFCA| accessdate = 2 July 2013[/tpl]

==Passengers==

Idgah railway station serves around 75,000 passengers everyday.[tpl]cite web| url = http://railenquiry.in/stationinfo/IDH/Idgah |title = Idgah Agra Jn| publisher= Indian Rail Enquiry| accessdate = 2 July 2013[/tpl]
