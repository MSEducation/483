[[Habik'a B.C.]]

CATEGORIES: Basketball teams in Israel, Maccabi basketball clubs

Habik'a Basketball Club ([tpl]lang-he|מועדון כדורסל הבקעה[/tpl]) is a basketball club originally from Giv'at Shmuel in Israel.http://www.haaretz.com/print-edition/sports/basketball-onward-and-upward-1.362411 The club plays in Ligat HaAl, the First division of Israeli basketball.
The club were promoted to Ligat HaAl in 1998, remaining there until 2007 when they were relegated back to Liga Leumit. The home hall of the club is: "Leader Arena" (1400 Seats) in Ganei Tikva.
In June 2012, the team was merged with Hapoel Eilat B.C..
