[[Khalifehlu, Khodabandeh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Khalifehlu ([tpl]lang-fa|خليفه لو[/tpl], also Romanized as Khalīfehlū; also known as Khalīfeh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3849031|Khalifehlu[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 189, in 38 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
