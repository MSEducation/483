[[Hlynsko-Rozbyshevske oil field]]

CATEGORIES: Oil fields in Ukraine

The Hlynsko-Rozbyshevske oil field is a Ukrainian oil field that was discovered in 1959. It began production in 1960 and produces oil. The total proven reserves of the Hlynsko-Rozbyshevsky oil field are around [tpl]convert|225|e6oilbbl[/tpl], and production is centered around [tpl]convert|2000|oilbbl/d|m3/d[/tpl].[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ukrnafta.com/eng/select.shtml?203|title=Reserves|date=2010|publisher=Ukrnafta|accessdate=30 December 2010[/tpl]
