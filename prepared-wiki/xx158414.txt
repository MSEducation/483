[[André Barrais]]

CATEGORIES: 1920 births, 2004 deaths, French basketball players, Olympic basketball players of France, Basketball players at the 1948 Summer Olympics, Olympic silver medalists for France, Olympic medalists in basketball

André Barrais (February 22, 1920 – January 15, 2004) was a French basketball player. He competed in the 1948 Summer Olympics.
In 1948, Barrais was part of the French basketball team, which won the silver medal. He died in Brest in 2004.
