[[Gaochun Old Street]]

CATEGORIES: Visitor attractions in Nanjing, Geography of Nanjing, AAA-rated tourist attractions

Gaochun Old Street ([tpl]lang-zh|高淳老街[/tpl]) is located in Gaochun, Nanjing, in China. It is a national AAA level[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.gcu.gov.cn/default.php?mod=article&fid=10783 |title=高淳旅游局 |publisher=Gcu.gov.cn |date= |accessdate=2012-05-29[/tpl] tourist scenic zone. It is  the most well-preserved old street from the Chinese Ming and Qing Dynasty in Jiangsu Province.
It has been published as a provincial heritage unit[tpl]cite web|url=http://q.sohu.com/topic/47722181 |title=【海峡之鹰】【广场精华】高淳,淳溪老街 - 海峡之鹰 - 户外旅游 - 搜狐圈子 |publisher=Q.sohu.com |date= |accessdate=2012-05-29[/tpl] several years ago. It is called Gaochun Chunxi Old Street（Chinese:高淳淳溪老街） as well. Gaochun has recently been named International Slow-rhythm City[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.njgc.gov.cn/rdzt/gjmc/index.html |title=国际慢城 |publisher=Njgc.gov.cn |date=2010-10-06 |accessdate=2012-05-29[/tpl] which means people living there lead a relatively slow and peaceful life.
Gaochun Old Street is more than [tpl]convert|345|m|ft[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://news.sina.com.cn/c/2002-04-19/1629552247.html |title=走进南京高淳千年老街 |publisher=News.sina.com.cn |date=2002-04-19 |accessdate=2012-05-29[/tpl] long, and [tpl]convert|3.5|m|ft[/tpl] wide now. It was [tpl]convert|800|m|ft[/tpl]long originally. The street is lined on both sides with old-styled shops selling goods and services of interest to tourists.  There are also other tourist attractions such as the Qianlong Old Well（Chinese:乾隆老井）and The Guan Wang Temple(Chinese:关王庙）.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.china.com.cn/culture/minsu/2010-09/10/content_20904507_2.htm |title=名街魅力:苏南皖南经济命脉 高淳老街依市而成 |publisher=China.com.cn |date=2010-09-10 |accessdate=2012-05-29[/tpl]
