[[Walvis Bay Urban]]

CATEGORIES: Constituencies of Erongo Region, Walvis Bay

Walvis Bay Urban constituency is a constituency in the Erongo Region of Namibia. The constituency had a population of 40,849 in 2004.Constituencies of Namibia, 2004 The district capital is the city of Walvis Bay.

==Politics==

In the 2010 regional elections, SWAPO's Hafeni Ndemula won the constituency with 4,254 votes. His only challenger was Sippora Ndeshihala Kamati of the Rally for Democracy and Progress, who received 789 votes.Results from the Electoral Commission of Namibia
