Chris Heid (born. March 14, 1983) is a dual Canadian-German professional ice hockey defenceman who is currently an Unrestricted Free Agent. He most recently played for ERC Ingolstadt of the Deutsche Eishockey Liga.
Heid was drafted 74th overall in the 2001 NHL Entry Draft by the Minnesota Wild.
