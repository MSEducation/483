[[Sepnet]]

CATEGORIES: Higher education in the United Kingdom

The South-East Physics Network, or SEPnet, is an association of physics departments at universities in the South-East of England.
In 2008 it received a grant of  £12.5 million from the Higher Education Funding Council for England.[tpl]Cite news|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/education/2008/apr/07/highereducation.uk1|title= Hefce gives £12.5m to physics initiative|last=Lipsett|first=Anthea |date=  7 April 2008 |work=Education Guardian|accessdate=8 January 2010[/tpl]
