[[Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District]]

CATEGORIES: Rural Districts of Zanjan Province, Khodabandeh County

Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District ([tpl]lang-fa|دهستان قشلاقات افشار[/tpl]) is a rural district (dehestan) in Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 4,067, in 854 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl] The rural district has 35 villages.
