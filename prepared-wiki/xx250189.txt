[[Rhynocoris albopunctatus]]

CATEGORIES: Reduviidae

Rhynocoris albopunctatus is a species of assassin bug family (Reduviidae), in the subfamily Harpactorinae. It is common in cotton plantations, especially near pastures where Stylosanthes gracilis is incorporated.[tpl]cite web | author = Nyiira, Z.
