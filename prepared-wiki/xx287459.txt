[[Mtatsminda Park]]

CATEGORIES: Amusement parks in Georgia (country), Buildings and structures in Tbilisi, Visitor attractions in Tbilisi

Mtatsminda Park is a family amusement facility located atop Mount Mtatsminda overlooking the Georgian capital Tbilisi. It offers carrousels, water slides, a roller-coaster, and a big Ferris Wheel at the edge of the mountain, offering a splendid view over the city.

==Ownership and management==

Mtatsminda Park is owned by the Municipality of Tbilisi.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.tbilisi.gov.ge/index.php?lang_id=ENG&sec_id=1255 |title=Tbilisi Municipal Portal - Mtatsminda Park |publisher=Municipality of Tbilisi |date= |accessdate=15 August 2013[/tpl] Until 2007, it was managed under a lease contract by a company owned by Badri Patarkatsishvili, when it was forced to cease operations. In July 2008, Rakeen Development Georgia LLC, a subsidiary of Rakeen Development based in the United Arab Emirates, confirmed that it had been tasked by the municipality with the management for a 49-year term.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.civil.ge/eng/article.php?id=18675 |title=Rakeen Takes Over Disputed Amusement Park |publisher=Civil.Ge |date=7 November 2007 |accessdate=15 August 2013[/tpl] The family of Patarkatsishvili who had disputed the decisions of the municipality confirmed in July 2011 that a compromise deal had been reached.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.civil.ge/eng_old/article.php?id=23709 |title=Patarkatsishvili Family, Gov't Say 'Dispute Settled' |publisher=Civil.Ge |date= |accessdate=15 August 2013[/tpl]
