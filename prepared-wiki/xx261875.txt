[[Stadtwerke München]]

CATEGORIES: Companies based in Munich, Public services, Transport in Munich

Stadtwerke München GmbH (Munich City Utilities) or SWM is a German communal company, owned by the city of Munich, which offers public services for the city and the region of Munich. The company supplies electricity for more than 95% of Munich's 750.000 households[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.sueddeutsche.de/muenchen/oekostrom-fuer-muenchen-sonne-wind-und-wasser-1.41611|title=Sonne, Wind und Wasser|last=Tibudd|first=Michael|date=11 January 2012|publisher=Süddeutsche Zeitung|language=German|accessdate=26 October 2012[/tpl] as well as natural gas, drinking water and, through its stake in the M-net Telekommunikations GmbH, telecommunications services.
Through its subsidiary, the Münchner Verkehrsgesellschaft mbH (MVG) (founded in 2001[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.sueddeutsche.de/muenchen/busse-und-bahnen-eine-gmbh-uebernimmt-den-nahverkehr-1.679037|title=Eine GmbH übernimmt den Nahverkehr|last=Hutter|first=Dominik|date=29 March 2001|publisher=Süddeutsche Zeitung|language=German|accessdate=26 October 2012[/tpl]), the SWM also runs most of the inner city public transportation network by maintaining the Munich U-Bahn as well as the Munich Tramway and bus services.
