[[505 Madison]]

CATEGORIES: Skyscrapers in Seattle, Washington, Office buildings in Seattle, Washington

Madison Centre, formerly known as M5 Commerce Centre, is a [tpl]convert|550|ft|m|adj=on[/tpl] tall skyscraper planned for the Downtown district in Seattle, Washington. It is scheduled to be completed in 2015 and have 36 floors. Upon its completion, it would be one of the tallest buildings in Seattle. 
Schnitzer West bought the existing property at the site and hoped to begin construction of a skyscraper as early as 2008.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.seattlepi.com/business/article/More-office-space-at-College-Club-site-1231159.php |title=More office space at College Club site |first=Andrea |last=James |date=March 14, 2007 |newspaper=Seattle Post-Intelligencer[/tpl] In the meantime, however, the late-2000s recession struck the economy and consequently the fate of many commercial real estate projects came under doubt, including 505 Madison.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2008/10/22/business/22seattle.html?pagewanted=all |title=Even in Resilient Seattle, Office Vacancy Rate Is Rising |first=Kristina |last=Shevory |date=October 21, 2008 |newspaper=New York Times[/tpl] Further development of the project hinged on securing a sufficient number of preleases by 2009,[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.djc.com/news/re/11206571.htm |title=Seattle commercial real estate players: 'We're not immune' |first=Lynn |last=Porter |newspaper=Seattle Daily Journal of Commerce |date=November 10, 2008[/tpl] which did not occur.[tpl]cite news |url=http://slog.thestranger.com/slog/archives/2009/04/07/re-boom-forever |title=Re: Boom Forever |newspaper=SLOG |first=Dominic |last=Holden |date=April 7, 2009[/tpl]
[tpl]As of|2012[/tpl], the project is being revived and the developer is seeking permits to start construction.[tpl]cite news |url=http://seattletimes.nwsource.com/html/businesstechnology/2018071350_mfive26.html |title=Schnitzer West revives plan for 36-story office tower downtown |date=April 25, 2012 |author=Eric Pryne |newspaper=The Seattle Times[/tpl]
