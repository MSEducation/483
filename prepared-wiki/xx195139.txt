[[KSYM-FM]]

CATEGORIES: College radio stations in Texas, Radio stations in Texas, Radio stations established in 1966

KSYM-FM (90.1 FM) is a radio station  broadcasting a Variety format. Licensed to San Antonio, Texas, USA, the station serves the San Antonio area.  The station is currently owned by San Antonio College.
