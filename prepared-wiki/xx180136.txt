[[Otto Ulseth]]

CATEGORIES: 1957 births, Living people, Norwegian football managers, Norwegian sports journalists, Tromsø IL managers

Otto Ulseth (born 29 July 1957) is a Norwegian football coach and journalist. Ulseth has worked as sports journalist in Adresseavisen, Dagbladet, and the Norwegian Broadcasting Corporation. As a football coach he has worked as manager of Tromsø IL in 2005 and assistant coach of Iraq's national team from 2007 to 2008.[tpl]cite book |title=Hvem er hvem? |last= |first= |authorlink= |year=2008 |editor=Knut Olav Åmås |publisher=Aschehoug |location=Oslo |isbn=978-82-03-23561-0 |pages=16 [/tpl] During the 2010 season he was assistant coach of Ranheim Fotball in Norwegian First Division, in a coaching team including Per Joar Hansen and Christer Basma.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.nettavisen.no/sport/fotball/article2767421.ece|publisher=Nettavisen|title=Basma til Ranheim|date=27 November 2009|language=Norwegian[/tpl]
