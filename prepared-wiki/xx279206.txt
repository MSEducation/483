[[Hammamlu-e Bala]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Hammamlu-e Bala ([tpl]lang-fa|حماملو بالا[/tpl], also Romanized as Ḩammāmlū-e Bālā)[tpl]IranNCSGN[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 37, in 8 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
