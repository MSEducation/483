[[Cascadia Marine Trail]]

CATEGORIES: Water trails, Puget Sound

The Cascadia Marine Trail is a water trail on Puget Sound.
Created in 1993,"Cascadia Marine Trail", Joel Roger, greatoutdoors.com it is designated as one of the 16 National Millennium Trails and suitable for day or multi-day trips. It has over 50 campsites to visit."Cascadia Marine Trail", Washington Water Trails Assoc. People can boat to the campsites from many public and private launch sites or shoreline trailheads.
