Ambyr C. Childers (born July 18, 1988) is an American actress.

==Life and career==

Childers was born in Cottonwood, Arizona and raised in Murrieta, California. After appearing in the 2003 film Dickie Roberts: Former Child Star, Childers became established for her role on the daytime soap opera All My Children as Colby Chandler, whom she portrayed from 2006 to 2008. She also played Elizabeth "E" Dodd in The Master.
