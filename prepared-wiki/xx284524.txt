===Releases with Michael and Jackie Castro===

Jason Castro has released covers on iTunes with his two younger siblings, both of whom are also singer-songwriters. He has released covers of other popular songs with them and they have been released as singles; with his younger brother, Michael, he has released three and as a family trio named Castro (Jason, Michael and younger sister Jackie), they have released four covers as singles.
