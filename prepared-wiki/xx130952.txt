Ronny Nouwen (born 21 July 1982 in Rotterdam, Netherlands) is a football midfielder who currently plays in the Netherlands for top amateur side SC Feyenoord.Feyenoordav.nl - Ronnie Nouwen naar SC Feyenoord

==Club career==

Nouwen played professional football with Excelsior Rotterdam before joining the amateur ranks with DOTO in 2005. In summer 2009 he joined RVVH from Ridderkerk.RVVH heeft de selectie rond - RVVH

==International career==

Nouwen made his debut for Aruba in a March 2004 World Cup qualification match against Surinam. He has played five games for Aruba.
