[[Kandy Town Hall]]

CATEGORIES: Buildings and structures in Kandy, Landmarks in Kandy, City and town halls in Sri Lanka, Local authorities in Central Province, Sri Lanka, Houses in Sri Lanka

The Town Hall of Kandy is the headquarters of the Kandy Municipal Council and the office of the Mayor of Kandy. The mansion known as the Dunuwille Walawwe was taken up by the Municipal Council in 1870 as the city's Town Hall. Today it houses the administrative staff of the municipal council.
