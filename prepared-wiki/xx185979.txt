[[H. Frank Lawrence]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing, Year of death missing, Northwest Missouri State Bearcats football coaches, Northwest Missouri State Bearcats men's basketball coaches

H. Frank Lawrence was the seventh head of the football coach for the Northwest Missouri State University Bearcats located in Maryville, Missouri and he held that position for three seasons, from 1924 until 1926.  His career coaching record at Northwest Missouri State was 19 wins, 3 losses, and 2 ties.  This ranks him ninth at Northwest Missouri State in total wins and first at Northwest Missouri State in winning percentage.http://www.nwmissouri.edu/sports/football/mediaguide/2007/07fbmg14.pdf
