[[Some Girls Do (album)]]

CATEGORIES: Lee Aaron albums, 1991 albums, Attic Records albums

Some Girls Do is the sixth studio album by singer Lee Aaron, released on September 23, 1991 through Attic Records. It reached #38 on the RPM Canadian Albums Chart on November 30, 1991 and held that position for a week."Top Albums/CDs - Volume 54, No. 26, November 30 1991". RPM. Retrieved 2012-01-24. The only single, "Sex with Love", reached #55 on the RPM Canadian Singles Chart on October 26, 1991 and held that position for three weeks."Top Singles - Volume 54, No. 21, October 26 1991". RPM. Retrieved 2012-01-24. The album would go on to receive a Juno Award nomination for Rock Album of the Year in 1992."Aaron, Lee". Jam!. Canadian Online Explorer. Retrieved 2012-01-24.
