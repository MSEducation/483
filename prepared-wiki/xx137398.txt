[[The Resolve]]

CATEGORIES: 1915 films, 1910s drama films, American drama films, American silent short films, Black-and-white films, Mutual Film films, Films directed by Henry Otto

The Resolve is a 1915 American silent short drama film directed by Henry Otto starring Ed Coxen, Lizette Thorne, and Winifred Greenwood.
