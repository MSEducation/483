[[Lower Lake Schoolhouse Museum]]

CATEGORIES: Museums in Lake County, California, History museums in California

The Lower Lake Schoolhouse Museum is a local history museum located in Lower Lake, California.

==History==

Built in 1877,http://www.co.lake.ca.us/Government/Directory/Museums/LowerLake.htm the building served as a schoolhouse until 1935 when it was sold to the local Masonic lodge.http://www.lcrt.org/public_html/schoolhouse.htm In 1993, the building was restored and the first floor was opened as a museum.http://www.lakeportchamber.com/community/lower-lake.asp
