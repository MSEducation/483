[[Bolzano Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in Italy, Buildings and structures in Bolzano, Transport in South Tyrol

Bolzano Airport ([tpl]lang-it|Aeroporto di Bolzano — Dolomiti[/tpl], [tpl]lang-de|Flughafen Bozen — Dolomiten[/tpl]) [tpl]airport codes|BZO|LIPB[/tpl] is a small regional airport near Bolzano, South Tyrol in northern Italy.

==Airlines and destinations==

The nearest bigger international airport is Innsbruck Airport in Austria approx. 120 km to the north.
