CATEGORIES: Swimming at the 1984 Summer Olympics

The final of the Women's 200 metres Freestyle event at the 1984 Summer Olympics was held in the McDonald's Olympic Swim Stadium in Los Angeles, California, on July 30, 1984. The fastest eight qualified for the final, the next eight for the B-final.
