Durga Kumari B.K. is a Nepalese politician, belonging to the Communist Party of Nepal (Maoist). In the 2008 Constituent Assembly election she was elected from the Kaski-4 constituency, winning 14866 votes.Election Commission of Nepal At the age of 26, she was the youngest female Assembly member elected through the First Past the Post system.Wrapping up FPTP !
