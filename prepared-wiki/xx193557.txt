[[Mario Schiano]]

CATEGORIES: 1933 births, 2008 deaths, People from Naples, Italian jazz saxophonists, Jazz alto saxophonists, Jazz soprano saxophonists, Free jazz saxophonists, Avant-garde jazz saxophonists

Mario Schiano (July 20, 1933 Naples – May 10, 2008 Rome) was an Italian alto saxophonist and soprano saxophonist associated with avant-garde/free jazz. 
He was born in Naples. A member of the Italian Instabile Orchestra, Schiano recorded with musicians such as Famoudou Don Moye, Eugenio Colombo, Ernst Reijseger, Paul Rutherford, Gianluigi Trovesi and Joëlle Léandre, among others.[tpl]cite web| last =Martinelli| first =Francesco| title =Mario Schiano Biography| publisher =Allmusic| url= [tpl]Allmusic|class=artist|id=p25907|pure_url=yes[/tpl][/tpl]
He died in Rome in 2008.
