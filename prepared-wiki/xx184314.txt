[[Enge (Zürich)]]

CATEGORIES: District 2 of Zürich, Former municipalities of the canton of Zürich

Enge is a quarter in the district 2 in Zürich.
It was formerly a municipality of its own, having been incorporated into Zürich in 1893.
The quarter has a population of 8,375 distributed on an area of 2.4 km².

==Transportation==

Zürich Enge railway station is a stop of the Zürich S-Bahn on the lines S2, S8, S21 and S24.
