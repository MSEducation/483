[[Ross Kenseth]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1993 births, Sportspeople from Appleton, Wisconsin, Racing drivers from Wisconsin, ARCA Midwest Tour drivers, Clemson University alumni

Ross Kenseth (born May 25, 1993) is an American stock car racing driver. Son of 2003 NASCAR Winston Cup Series champion Matt Kenseth, he currently competes part-time in the ARCA Midwest Tour, CRA Super Series and ARCA Racing Series.
