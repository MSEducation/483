[[WAYR-FM]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Georgia (U.S. state), Radio stations established in 1998, Radio stations in Brunswick, Georgia

 
WAYR is a radio station airing a Christian Contemporary Music format, licensed to Brunswick, Georgia, broadcasting on 90.7 MHz FM. The station is owned by Good Tidings Trust, Inc. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=WAYR |title=WAYR Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division[/tpl] Accessed August 8, 2012 WAYR began broadcasting in November of 1998, and increased its power to 14,000 watts in 2003.
