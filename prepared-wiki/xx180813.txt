[[It Doesn't Matter (September song)]]

CATEGORIES: 2005 singles, 2006 singles, September (singer) songs

"It Doesn't Matter" is the fourth single to be released from September's second album, In Orbit.  This single was not released in her homeland of Sweden, but was a top 20 hit in Poland and a minor hit in Romania and Russia. The song gained popularity in Poland during her tour there in 2006, and during her summer concert tour in 2007 getting her ready to perform at Sopot Festival 2007.
