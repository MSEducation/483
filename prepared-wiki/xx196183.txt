[[Australian Vintage]]

CATEGORIES: Wineries of Australia, Australian wine

Australian Vintage Limited (AVL) is an Australian wine company. It was known as McGuigan Simeon Wines until February 2008, when shareholders voted to change the name.[tpl]Cite news|url=http://www.abc.net.au/news/stories/2008/02/05/2154506.htm|work= ABC News|title=McGuigan rejects grower view on new name|date= 5 February 2008|accessdate=28 May 2011[/tpl] The company produces and exports bulk and bottled wine, and is the second largest vineyard owner and manager in Australia.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.mswl.com.au/category.php?category_id=2|title= McGuigan Simeon Wines Limited homepage|accessdate=28 May 2011[/tpl] As of 2007, Australian Vintage had 567 employees and over $286 million in sales[tpl]Citation needed|date=May 2011[/tpl].
