Nicholas Caldecote (died 1443), of Meldreth, Cambridgeshire, was an English politician.

==Family==

Caldecote had thee sons by his wife, Joan.

==Career==

He was a Member (MP) of the Parliament of England for Cambridgeshire in 1420, 1426, and 1431.http://www.historyofparliamentonline.org/volume/1386-1421/member/caldecote-nicholas-1443
