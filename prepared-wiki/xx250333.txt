[[Carrothers Secondary]]

CATEGORIES: Rail infrastructure in Ohio, Rail infrastructure in Michigan

This is a railine currently owned by CSX. It was originally PRR's Toledo–Mansfield–Detroit line. Today the line operates from Toledo to Woodville.  The original length was probably about 50 to [tpl]convert|70|mi|km[/tpl] long. The line is about [tpl]convert|20|mi|km[/tpl] long right now. Woodville to Tiffin which is [tpl]convert|21|mi|km[/tpl] long is run by Northern Ohio and Western railway. This was one of the Conrail lines given to CSX. Conrail abandoned Tiffin to Carrothers in 1984. PRR abandoned Carrothers to Mansfield in 1959.
