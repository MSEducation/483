[[Güney Qışlaq]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Shakhbuz District

Güney Qışlaq (until 2003,Azerbaijan Parliament - renaming law Yuxarı Remeşin, Yukhari-Rameshin, and Yukhary Remeshin) is a village and municipality in the Shakhbuz Rayon of Nakhchivan, Azerbaijan.  It has a population of 545.
