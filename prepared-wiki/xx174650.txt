[[Gmina Budzów]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Lesser Poland Voivodeship, Sucha County

Gmina Budzów is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Sucha County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. Its seat is the village of Budzów, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|8|km|mi|0[/tpl] north-east of Sucha Beskidzka and [tpl]convert|37|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-west of the regional capital Kraków.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|73.41|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 8,311.

==Villages==

Gmina Budzów contains the villages and settlements of Baczyn, Bieńkówka, Budzów, Jachówka, Palcza and Zachełmna.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Budzów is bordered by the gminas of Lanckorona, Maków Podhalański, Pcim, Stryszów, Sułkowice, Tokarnia and Zembrzyce.
