[[Kord Kandi, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Kord Kandi ([tpl]lang-fa|كردكندي[/tpl], also Romanized as Kord Kandī and Kurd-Kandi)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3072076|Kord Kandi[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 322, in 76 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
