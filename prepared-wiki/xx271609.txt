[[Jani Kaçi]]

CATEGORIES: 1952 births, Living people, People from Korçë, Albanian footballers, Albanian Superliga players, Association football goalkeepers, Albania international footballers

Jani Kaçi (born 1952 in Korçë) is a retired Albanian professional footballer. He played in goal for Skënderbeu Korçë in the 70s and 80s as well as the Albania national team, earning himself 6 full senior caps. He is widely considered to be one of Skënderbeu Korçë's best ever goalkeepers.
He has also managed Skënderbeu Korçë and won the 2006-07 Albanian First Division with the club.[tpl]cite web|url=http://api.shqiperia.com/lajme/artikull/iden/83610/titulli/Jani-Kaci-trajneri-i-ri-i-Skenderbeut|title=Jani Kaçi trajneri i ri i Skënderbeut|author=|language=Albania|publisher=Info Arkiv|accessdate=2011-09-25|date=2006-08-02[/tpl] In February 2010 he was named as assistant manager under Mirel Josa at Skënderbeu,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.albaniasoccer.com/lajme/shqiperi/superliga/1252-jani-kaci-ndihmesi-i-joses-tek-skenderbeu.html|title=Jani Kaçi, ndihmesi i Joses tek Skenderbeu!|author=Kristo Zhidro|language=Albania|publisher=Albania Soccer|accessdate=2011-09-23|date=2010-02-10[/tpl] a position which he filled until the manager's departure at the end of the 2009-10 season. He has since returned to his post as the goalkeeping coach of Skënderbeu. In recent years he has also been goalkeeping coach of the Albania U-21 national team.
