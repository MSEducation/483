Mattia Altobelli (born 17 August 1983) is a professional Italian footballer who plays for Montichiari.

==Football career==

Son of the striker Alessandro Altobelli, he started his career at Internazionale. Where he won league title with Primavera Reserve team in 2002. He made his Inter first team debut against A.S. Bari in the Coppa Italia on 4 December 2002.
On summer 2003, due to too old for the youth team (U-20), he was loaned to sister club Spezia at Serie C1 along with Mathieu Moreau.
He then moved to SPAL of Serie C1 in August 2004,[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.inter.it/aas/news/reader?L=en&N=15100|title= TWO YOUTH SECTOR PLAYERS ON LOAN |date=6 August 2004|accessdate=8 February 2010|publisher=inter.it[/tpl] and then loaned to newly promoted Serie B team Avellino in July 2005.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.inter.it/aas/news/reader?L=en&N=19456|title= STOIKOV ON LOAN TO LA LOUVRIERE |date=26 July 2005|accessdate=8 February 2010|publisher=inter.it[/tpl]
After spent on loan at Sassari Torres at the second half of 2005/06 season, he was transferred to Chiasso of Swiss Challenge League,[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.inter.it/aas/news/reader?L=en&N=23817|title= DEALS DONE FOR EIGHT YOUTH PLAYERS |date=31 August 2006|accessdate=8 February 2010|publisher=inter.it[/tpl] where he rejoined former youth team-mate Wellington.
In summer 2007, Altobelli transferred to newly promoted Serie C1 team Lecco.
In January 2009, he was loaned to Pro Vercelli.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.aclecco.it/news.php?id=568|title=IL LECCO CEDE ALTOBELLI E PRENDE ANTONIONI|date=30 January 2009|accessdate=22 August 2009|publisher=Calcio Lecco 1912|language=Italian[/tpl] and in January 2010 left for Rodengo Saiano.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.tuttomercatoweb.com/?action=read&id=186896|title=UFFICIALE: Rodengo Saiano, colpo Altobelli|date=7 January 2010|accessdate=8 February 2010|publisher=tuttomercatoweb.com|language=Italian[/tpl]
