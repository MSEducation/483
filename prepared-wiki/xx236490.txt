[[Shawnee Indians (baseball)]]

CATEGORIES: Sports clubs established in 1923, Defunct minor league baseball teams, Professional baseball teams in Oklahoma

The Shawnee Indians were an Oklahoma State League baseball team based in Shawnee, Oklahoma, USA that played from 1923 to 1924.Shawnee, OK page Major league baseball pitcher Walt Tauscher played for them. In 1923, they were managed by Clyde Wren,1923 Shawnee Indians and in 1924 they were managed by Larry McLean.1924 Shawnee Indians
