[[Nantgaredig]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Carmarthenshire

Nantgaredig is a village in Carmarthenshire, Wales.
The village is most notable for being the home of the actor Julian Lewis Jones. It is also the home of the Welsh rugby union footballer Mefin Davies who plays as a hooker in the Wales national rugby union team and the Ospreys.
The winner of the 1990 Cheltenham Gold Cup, Norton's Coin, was trained by Sirrel Griffiths at his farm at Nantgaredig.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.thisissouthwales.co.uk/news/Norton-s-Coin/article-275552-detail/article.html | title=Norton's Coin | work =thisissouthwales.co.uk | author= L Sullivan | date=18 August 2008[/tpl]
