[[Michio Igasa]]

CATEGORIES: Japanese composers, Year of birth missing (living people), Living people

[tpl]Nihongo|Michio Igasa|衣笠 道雄|Igasa Michio[/tpl] is a female Japanese music composer. She has composed the opening theme songs for Utawarerumono and Asatte no Houkou which were both sung by Suara.
