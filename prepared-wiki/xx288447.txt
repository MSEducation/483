[[Zindagi Jalebi]]

CATEGORIES: Indian films, Indian comedy films, 2013 films

 Zindgi Jalebihttp://www.bollywoodhungama.com/moviemicro/cast/id/664019(Hindi: जिंदगी जलेबी) is an upcoming Bollywood comedy Film directed by Karan Kashyyap.

==Plot==

Zindgi Jalebi  is a comedy based on idle minds is devils’ workshop. It also symbolises an innate need and tendency of every human to do a heroic deed and a desire to be remembered for it for times to come. If their ordinary lives and circumstances do not have a place for a heroic deed, then how they want to manipulate and create one. The comedy is that those who set out for the task end up creating a hero out of somebody else and somehow come out of the series of events unscathed.[tpl]cite web | title = Karan Kashyyap talks about Zindagi Jalebi!| url = http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2012-12-29/news-interviews/36050738_1_film-festivals-samarth-chaturvedi-guinness-book|date=Dec 29, 2012| accessdate = 2013-08-16| publisher = The Times of India [/tpl] Zindagi jalebi is a story where everyone’s life is jumbling up in a mess and then how they come out of this mess.

==Cast==

Films stars Avinash Bhargava, Leena, Samarth Chaturvedi, Suresh Dubey and Urvashi Solanki  as Gunjan bai (Item Number/Dance)

==Crew==

The film is by Written and Directed by Karan Kashyap while it is produced by Kanti Chheda of Moksh Films Production.  The movie is set to all over India on 6 September 2013.
