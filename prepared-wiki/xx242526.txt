[[Canon EOS-1D Mark IV]]

CATEGORIES: Canon EOS DSLR cameras, Live-preview digital cameras

The EOS-1D Mark IV is a professional 16.1 effective megapixels digital single lens reflex camera (DSLR) camera body produced by Canon.
