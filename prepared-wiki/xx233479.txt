[[Jonathan Phillippe]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1988 births, People from Buenos Aires Province, Argentine footballers, Association football forwards, Boca Juniors footballers, San Martín de San Juan footballers, Primera División Argentina players

Jonathan Phillippe (born 24 January 1988 in Navarro) is an Argentine Striker who currently plays for America de Cali.http://www.soccerway.com/players/jonatan-philippe/59278/
He was signed in 2008 from the youth divisions of Boca Juniors and made his debut on August 31, 2008 in a 3-0 win over Huracán.
