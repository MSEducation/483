[[Songs for a Dying Planet]]

CATEGORIES: Joe Walsh albums, 1992 albums, Albums produced by Bill Szymczyk

Songs for a Dying Planet is the tenth studio album by Joe Walsh, released in 1992 (see 1992 in music). It would be 20 years before he released another solo album, 2012's Analog Man.

==Reception==

Writing for Allmusic, critic Vincent Jeffries wrote the album "fulfills those expectations to a degree, but the songwriter's weakened comedic instincts and extreme sincerity make Songs for a Dying Planet a difficult recommendation."
