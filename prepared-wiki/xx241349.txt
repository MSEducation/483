[[Mramorac belts]]

CATEGORIES: Collections of museums in Serbia

The Mramorac belts were Iron Age Mramorac type luxurious silver and gold belts used in ceremonial and religious aspects by the prominents of Triballi,http://www.sgi.co.rs/html/003/00314.html a Thracian tribe then inhabiting the Pcinja District of southern Serbia and western Bulgaria. The belts are characterized by special patterns, Anthropomorphic, Zoomorphic and Swastika symbolism.
The type is named after Mramorac, a place in Serbia.

==Serbia==

In 2004, the Serbian National Museum acquisited two silver belts.
