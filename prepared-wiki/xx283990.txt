[[Ride (2014 film)]]

CATEGORIES: American films, 2014 films, 2010s drama films, American drama films, Surfing films, Films shot in Los Angeles, California, Films directed by Helen Hunt

Ride is an upcoming American drama film directed and written by Helen Hunt. The film stars Helen Hunt, Luke Wilson, Brenton Thwaites and Leonor Varela.

==Plot==

A mother travels cross-country to California to be with her son after he decides to drop out of school and become a surfer.
