[[Gmina Świerczów]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Opole Voivodeship, Namysłów County

Gmina Świerczów is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Namysłów County, Opole Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Its seat is the village of Świerczów, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|14|km|mi|0[/tpl] south of Namysłów and [tpl]convert|34|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of the regional capital Opole.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|110.32|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 3,666.
The gmina contains part of the protected area called Stobrawa Landscape Park.

==Villages==

Gmina Świerczów contains the villages and settlements of Bąkowice, Bielice, Biestrzykowice, Dąbrowa, Gola, Grodziec, Kuźnica Dąbrowska, Miejsce, Miodary, Pieczyska, Starościn, Świerczów, Wężowice and Zbica.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Świerczów is bordered by the gminas of Domaszowice, Lubsza, Namysłów, Pokój and Popielów.
