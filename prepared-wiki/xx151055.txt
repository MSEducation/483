[[ASC Le Geldar]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in French Guiana, Association football clubs established in 1957, 1957 establishments in French Guiana

Association Sportive et Culturelle Le Geldar is a football club of French Guyana, playing in the town of Kourou. They play in the French Guyanese first division, the French Guyana Championnat National.
