[[Max Ippen]]

CATEGORIES: Bobsledders at the 1948 Winter Olympics, Czechoslovak bobsledders, Year of death missing, 1906 births

Max Ippen (born May 16, 1906, date of death unknown) was a Czechoslovak bobsledder who competed in the late 1940s. At the 1948 Winter Olympics in St. Moritz, he finished 14th in both the two-man and four-man events.
