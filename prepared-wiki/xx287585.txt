[[Kenmare Municipal Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in North Dakota, Buildings and structures in Ward County, North Dakota, Transportation in Ward County, North Dakota

Kenmare Municipal Airport [tpl]airport codes|||7K5[/tpl] is a public use airport located one nautical mile (2 km) southeast of the central business district of Kenmare, a city in Ward County, North Dakota, United States. Owned by the Kenmare Airport Authority, it is included in the National Plan of Integrated Airport Systems for 2011–2015, which categorized it as a general aviation facility.

==Facilities and aircraft==

Kenmare Municipal Airport covers an area of 158 acres (64 ha) at an elevation of 1,962 feet (598 m) above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 8/26 with an asphalt surface measuring 3,700 by 60 feet (1,128 x 18 m).
For the 12-month period ending August 14, 2012, the airport had 3,175 aircraft operations, an average of 264 per month: 91% general aviation and 9% air taxi. At that time there were 21 aircraft based at this airport: 95% single-engine and 5% helicopter.
