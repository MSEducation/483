[[WPIO]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Florida, Moody Radio affiliate stations

WPIO is a Christian radio station licensed to Titusville, Florida, broadcasting on 89.3 MHz FM.  The station is owned by Florida Public Radio, Inc. WPIO fcc.gov. Accessed August 9, 2012
WPIO's programming includes Christian talk and teaching shows such as Focus on the Family, Revive our Hearts with Nancy Leigh DeMoss, Adrian Rogers, Joyce Meyer, Chuck Swindoll, and Unshackled!.  WPIO also airs a variety of Christian music.WPIO Schedule Accessed August 9, 2012 WPIO's programming is also heard  in the Palm Bay, Melbourne area on 90.3 WEJF, and in the areas of Dade City, Florida and Bushnell, Florida on 89.3 WKFA.
