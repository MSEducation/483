[[Pilštanj]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Municipality of Kozje

Pilštanj is a village in the Municipality of Kozje in eastern Slovenia. The area is part of the historical Lower Styria region. The municipality is included in the Savinja statistical region.Kozje municipal site
The parish church in the settlement is dedicated to Saint Michael and belongs to the Roman Catholic Diocese of Celje. It was first mentioned in written documents dating to 1167. The current church was built in the 15th century and redone in Baroque style in the 18th century.Slovenian Ministry of Culture register of national heritage reference number ešd 3242
There are ruins of two castles near the settlement. Pilštanj Castle (Germ. Peilenstein) on a steep hill south of the settlement is mentioned in a number of documents dating to the 11th century. It was abandoned by the late 17th century. Hartenštajn Castle (Germ. Hartenstein), of which very few ruins remain, was a 13th-century castle northwest of the settlement.[ref]Slovenian Ministry of Culture register of national heritage reference numbers ešd 4626 
& 4627[/ref]
