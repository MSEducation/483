[[ARMA (series)]]

CATEGORIES: Bohemia Interactive games, First-person shooters, Open world video games, Tactical shooter video games, Video game sequels, Video games set in Greece, Video games set in the 2030s, Windows games, Video game franchises, Articles which contain graphical timelines

ARMA (stylized as A[tpl]small|RM[/tpl]A) is a series of military simulation games after a split with Codemasters which was once Operation Flashpoint. Since 2006 to 2013 all ARMA games are developed by Bohemia Interactive or their subsidiaries.
