[[Plaster Hounds]]

CATEGORIES: 2004 albums, Chromatics (band) albums

Plaster Hounds is the second studio album by Chromatics, released in 2004 on the Gold Standard Laboratories record label.
==Overview==  
It contains a cover version of "Program" by the Silver Apples.

==Track listing==

==Personnel==

Maximillion Ronald Avila:  Drum programming, percussion, and vocals 
Adam Miller:  Bass, drum programming, guitar, percussion, and vocals 
Nate Preston: Saxophone 
Nat Sahlstrom: Bass, guitar, and vocals 
Aleesha Whitley: Percussion
Recorded in July 2003 at the Type Foundry
