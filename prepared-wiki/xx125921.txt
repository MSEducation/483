[[Cass & Slide]]

CATEGORIES: British electronic music groups

Cass & Slide is an electronic music British production and remix duo of Cass Cutbush and Peter Martin.  Cass founded the record label Fire with Lee Burridge in 2000.  Cass & Slide released several singles on Fire and its sublabel, Fire 999.  These included remixes of songs from the films Gladiator and The Italian Job.  They also released original singles on labels such as Additive Records and EQ Grey.  Since the demise of Fire in 2003, Cass opened the new record label Sabotage Systems.
