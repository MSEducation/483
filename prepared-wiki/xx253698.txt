[[Yuri Vovk]]

CATEGORIES: Ukrainian chess players, Chess grandmasters, Living people, 1988 births, Sportspeople from Lviv

Yuri Vovk ([tpl]lang-uk|Юрій Вовк[/tpl]) (born 11 November 1988) is a Ukrainian chess player. He was trained by Vladimir Grabinsky, coach of the Ukrainian youth team. He became a Grandmaster in 2008, at age 20. 
His major success has been the victory In February 2009 of the colossal 610-player Cappelle-la-Grande Open in France, above 106 Grandmasters and 76 International Masters, with 7.5 points out of 9.Chessbase: Yuri Vovk wins 25th Cappelle-la-Grande   
Other tournament results:
Yuri achieved his maximum Elo rating in April 2013, with 2608 points.
He is the older brother of Andrey Vovk.
