[[Fame Cinemas]]

CATEGORIES: Companies based in Mumbai, Cinema chains in India, Companies established in 1999

INOX Leisure  is a company that owns and runs the INOX  chain of multiplexes headquartered in Mumbai.http://www.fame.co.in/FameIndia.htm It was established in 1999 as Shringar Cinemas Private Ltd.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fame.co.in/FameIndia.html |title=Fame |publisher=Fame |date= |accessdate=2010-12-31[/tpl] In 2004, upon conversion of Private Limited to a Public Limited Company, the name changed from Shringar Cinemas Private Limited to Shringar Cinemas Limited. In 2008, the name changed from Shringar Cinemas Limited to Fame India Limited.[tpl]cite web|url=http://economictimes.indiatimes.com/fame-india-ltd/infocompanyhistory/companyid-16471.cms |title=FAMEINDIA History | Fame India Ltd Company History & Profile |publisher=Economictimes.indiatimes.com |date= |accessdate=2010-12-31[/tpl] The Company went public in 2005 to raise money from equity market through an initial public offering. This helped opening up multiplexes across India. In 2011,Fame was taken over by INOX Leisure Limited  and in sep 2013 Fame Cinemas was rebranded as INOX(INOX Leisure Limited)
