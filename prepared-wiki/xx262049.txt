[[1999 Copa América Final]]

CATEGORIES: 1999 Copa América, Brazil national football team matches, Uruguay national football team matches, 1999 in Brazilian football, Copa América finals

The 1999 Copa América Final was the final match of the 1999 Copa América. It was held on July 18, 1999 in Asunción. Brazil won the match against Uruguay by a 3-0 score.This was the sixth Copa América obtained by Brazil.
