[[Conxita Julià]]

CATEGORIES: 1920 births, Catalan poets, Living people, People from Barcelona

Conxita Julià (born Barcelona, 1920), is a Catalan poet and excursionist. She exchanged letters with Lluís Companys i Jover, president of the Generalitat de Catalunya in the years 1934-1940, and her figure appears in a sculpture honouring Companys inaugurated in 1997 http://aeicaria.blogspot.com/2007/02/conxita-juli-i-farrs.html.
