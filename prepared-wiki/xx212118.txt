[[Twin Lakes Brewing Company]]

CATEGORIES: Beer brewing companies based in Delaware, Companies based in New Castle County, Delaware, Companies established in 2006, 2006 establishments in Delaware

Twin Lakes Brewing Company is an American brewing company. Its brewery and taproom are on Twin Lakes Farm in Greenville, Delaware, near the DuPont Historic Corridor.

==History==

The Twin Lakes Brewing Company was founded in Greenville, Delaware in April, 2006.[ref]
</ref>

==Products==

The Twin Lakes Brewing Company produces approximately 10,000 barrels of beer per year.[ref]
