[[Streets of Glory]]

CATEGORIES: Comics by Garth Ennis

Streets of Glory is a 6-issue western comic book mini-series written by Garth Ennis and illustrated by Mike Wolfer.  It was published by Avatar Press, and the first issue was released in September 2007.Ennis' "Streets of Glory" Run Red With Blood, Comic Book Resources, July 20, 2007Ennis' Wild West: Talking Streets of Glory, Newsarama, September 13, 2007
