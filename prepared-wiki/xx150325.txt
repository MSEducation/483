[[David Lonsdale]]

CATEGORIES: 1963 births, Living people, English male television actors, People educated at King George V College, English radio personalities, English male film actors, Male actors from Lancashire, People from Southport, 20th-century English male actors, 21st-century English male actors

David Lonsdale (born 21 May 1963) is an English actor. Until 2010, Lonsdale played David Stockwell in the long-running television series Heartbeat. He also played Peter Barlow in Coronation Street in 1986, and appeared in a minor role as a repo man in the 1997 feature film The Full Monty. In 2011, he appeared again in Coronation Street in a minor role working for a solicitor. In November 2011, Lonsdale appeared in Casualty (TV series). He returned to Coronation Street in May 2012 as Edwin Soames.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.digitalspy.co.uk/soaps/s3/coronation-street/scoop/a382366/coronation-street-update-on-kirstys-parents.html|title='Coronation Street': Update on Kirsty's parents|last=Kilkelly|first=Daniel|date=18 May 2012|work=Digital Spy|publisher=Hearst Magazines UK|accessdate=18 May 2012[/tpl] He now presents a Sunday morning show, The Southport Arts Show, on community radio station Mighty FM. He has been married since 1993 to his wife, Diane.
