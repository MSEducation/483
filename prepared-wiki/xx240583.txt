[[Chōkoku-no-Mori Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Kanagawa Prefecture, Hakone, Kanagawa, 1919 establishments in Japan

[tpl]nihongo|Chōkoku-no-mori Station|彫刻の森駅|Chōkokunomori-eki[/tpl] is a railway station on the Hakone Tozan Line located in Hakone, Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan. It is 14.3 rail kilometers from the line’s terminus at Odawara Station, named after Hakone Open-Air Museum.

==History==

Chōkoku-no-mori Station was opened on June 1, 1919 as [tpl]nihongo|Ni-no-Taira Station|二ノ平駅|Ninotaira-eki[/tpl]. It was renamed to its present name on March 15, 1972 on the opening of the nearby Hakone Open-Air Museum of modern sculpture.
