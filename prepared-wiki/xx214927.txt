[[Dakoda Motor Co.]]

CATEGORIES: American Christian rock groups, Alternative rock groups from California, Musical groups established in 1991

Dakoda Motor Co. (also Dakoda Motor Company) is an American Christian rock band.

==History==

Dakoda Motor Co. formed in the early 1990s; among its members was Peter King, a professional surfboarder and host of the MTV show Sandblast.John Bush, Dakoda Motor Co. at Allmusic They signed with Myrrh Records in 1993 and released two albums before moving to Atlantic Records in 1996.
