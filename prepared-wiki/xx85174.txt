[[Breeding Death]]

CATEGORIES: 2000 EPs, Bloodbath albums, Debut EPs

Breeding Death is the debut EP by Bloodbath. It was released on February 8, 2000, on Century Media Records.

==Release==

Breeding Death was initially released on February 8, 2000, on Century Media Records.[tpl]Allmusic|class=album|id=r465526|pure_url=yes[/tpl] It was re-released on CD in 2006 with two bonus demo tracks.[tpl]Allmusic|class=album|id=r1537004|pure_url=yes[/tpl] Breeding Death was released on vinyl in January 2010 on Animate Records. The first 200 copies were pressed on gold wax. The LP includes the 2 bonus demo tracks. Side B features laser engraving.[tpl]cite web| author=Blakkheim| title=‘BREEDING DEATH’ TO BE RE-RELEASED ON VINYL| url=http://bloodbath.biz/?p=219| publisher=Bloodbath| date=December 20, 2009| accessdate= August 14, 2012[/tpl]
