[[Trader Horn (1973 film)]]

CATEGORIES: American films, 1973 films, Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer films

Trader Horn is a 1973 film starring Rod Taylor as the African adventurer Trader Horn, whose life had been previously dramatised in a 1931 film.
The film was shot on the MGM backlot in Los Angeles, using stock footage from King Solomon's Mines (1950).Stephen Vagg, Rod Taylor: An Aussie in Hollywood, Bear Manor Media 2010 p 177.
