[[A Kick Up the Eighties]]

CATEGORIES: British television sketch shows, Tracey Ullman, 1980s Scottish television series, 1981 British television programme debuts, 1984 British television programme endings

A Kick Up the Eighties was a 1981-1984 BBC Scotland sketch show starring Robbie Coltrane, Tracey Ullman, Richard Stilgoe, Miriam Margolyes, Rik Mayall, Ron Bain and Roger Sloman.
The Series was created by Colin Gilbert, who went on to create Naked Video. The show was notable for introducing the Rik Mayall character Kevin Turvey.
