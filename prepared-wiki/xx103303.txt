[[Jilly Johnson]]

CATEGORIES: 1953 births, British female singers, Living people, Page 3 girls, People from Woking

Jilly Johnson (née Gosden) (born 17 November 1953) is a former Page 3 girl. In 1975 she became the first model to appear topless in the Daily Mirror.[tpl]cite book |title=Tabloid nation: the birth of the Daily Mirror to the death of the tabloid |authorlink=Chris Horrie |last=Horrie |first=Chris |page=148 |year=2003 |publisher=André Deutsch |isbn=978-0-233-00012-1 [/tpl] 
As a child she attended The Bishop David Brown School, Woking, Surrey.  In the 1980s, together with Nina Carter, she was in the girl group Blonde on Blonde.  She has been married twice and has one daughter by her first marriage. She has written a novel called Double Exposure.[tpl]cite news |title=Angel is a centrefold |work=The Independent on Sunday |date=26 November 2006 [/tpl]
