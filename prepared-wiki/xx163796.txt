[[List of diplomatic missions in Malta]]

CATEGORIES: Foreign relations of Malta, Lists of diplomatic missions by receiving country, Malta-related lists

This page lists embassies and consulates posted in Malta. There are currently 22 embassies/high commissions in Malta. Some other 75 countries have non resident embassies.
