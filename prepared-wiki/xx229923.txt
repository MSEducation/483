Oleg Vyacheslavovich Elyshev ([tpl]lang-ru|Олег Вячеславович Елышев[/tpl]; born May 30, 1971) is a retired Russian professional football player who played for several clubs in Europe and Asia, including Baltika Kaliningrad and Fakel Voronezh in the Soviet First League, Dynamo Moscow, Tekstilshchik Kamyshin and Lokomotiv Moscow in the Russian Premier League and FC Seoul of the South Korean K-League, then known as LG Cheetahs. and Hapoel Haifa in the Israeli Premier League.
