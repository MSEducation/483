==Function==

The protein encoded by this gene an endoplasmic reticulum transmembrane protein involved in processing of wingless proteins such as WNT7A.

==Disease linkage==

Mutations in this gene are associated with focal dermal hypoplasia.[tpl]cite journal |author=Wang X, Reid Sutton V, Omar Peraza-Llanes J, et al. |title=Mutations in X-linked PORCN, a putative regulator of Wnt signaling, cause focal dermal hypoplasia |journal=Nat.
