[[High Wire (song)]]

CATEGORIES: 1983 singles, Men at Work songs, Songs written by Colin Hay

"High Wire" is a song by the Australian group Men at Work.[tpl]cite encyclopedia | last = McFarlane | first = Ian | authorlink = Ian McFarlane | encyclopedia = Encyclopedia of Australian Rock and Pop | title = Encyclopedia entry for 'Men at Work' | url = http://web.archive.org/web/20030513171104/www.whammo.com.au/encyclopedia.asp?articleid=482 | accessdate = 29 March 2010 | year = 1999 | publisher = Allen & Unwin | location = St Leonards, NSW | isbn = 1-86508-072-1 [/tpl] The song was written by Men at Work singer/guitarist Colin Hay[tpl]cite web | publisher = Australasian Performing Right Association (APRA) | title = "High Wire" at APRA search engine | url = http://www.apra-amcos.com.au/worksearch.axd?q=High%20Wire | accessdate = 29 March 2010 [/tpl] and the recording was produced by Peter McIan. It was released in late 1983 as the fourth and final single from their album  Cargo.
