[[Choeradodis columbica]]

CATEGORIES: Mantidae, Insects of Colombia

Choeradodis columbica, common name Columbian Shield Mantis, is a species of praying mantis native to Colombia.  http://insects.tamu.edu/research/collection/hallan/Arthropoda/Insects/Mantodea/Family/Mantidae.txt Texas A&M University  It was formerly categorized as Choeradodis columbica (Beier 1931), as Choeradodis peruviana (Serville 1839), and as Mantis rhombicollis (Latreille 1833).  http://www.biolib.cz/en/taxon/id97391/ Choeradodis columbica Taxon Profile
