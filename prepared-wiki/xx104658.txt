[[National Board of Fisheries (Sweden)]]

CATEGORIES: Government agencies of Sweden, Economy of Sweden

Swedish National Board of Fisheries (Fiskeriverket) is a Swedish government agency within the Ministry of Rural Affairs. The agency is located in Gothenburg. It is the central government authority for fisheries in Sweden. It was established in 1948. It ceases to exict on July the 1st 2011.
