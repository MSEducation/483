[[Kate Doust]]

Catherine Esther (Kate) Doust (born 27 May 1962) is an Australian politician. She has been a Labor member of the Western Australian Legislative Council since 2001, representing the region of South Metropolitan.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.parliament.wa.gov.au/parliament%5Cmemblist.nsf/WAllMembersFlat/Doust,+Catherine+%28Kate%29+Esther|title=Hon. Catherine (Kate) Esther Doust MLC |publisher=Parliament of Western Australia|year=2010|accessdate=8 Feb 2010[/tpl]
Kate Doust was born in Kalgoorlie, Western Australia. She graduated with a Bachelor of Arts from the University of Western Australia.
Kate Doust is the Shadow Minister for Industrial Relations; Commerce; Small Business; Trade; Electoral Affairs and is Deputy Leader of the Opposition in the Legislative Council.
