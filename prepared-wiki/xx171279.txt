[[Marcin Jędrzejewski]]

CATEGORIES: Polish speedway riders, 1987 births, Living people, Polonia Bydgoszcz riders

Marcin Jędrzejewski (born April 10, 1987 in Tuchola, Poland) is a Polish speedway rider and has ridden for the Polish national junior team. He has been riding since 2003.
