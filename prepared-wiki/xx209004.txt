[[Junodia congicus]]

CATEGORIES: Junodia, Mantodea of Africa, Animals described in 1915

[tpl]Italic title[/tpl]

Junodia congicus is a species of praying mantis found in the Congo River region. It was first described in 1915 by Ermanno Giglio-Tos. Hymenopodidae listing by Texas A&M University
