[[Hartest]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Suffolk, Babergh

Hartest is a small village in the Babergh district of the English county  of Suffolk. It is located halfway between Bury St. Edmunds and Sudbury on the B1066 road in the Glem valley. Brockley is two miles north. 
The village is centred on the large village green, fringed by an array of brightly coloured cottages, the village hall or institute, the medieval All Saints church and the Crown public house, formerly Hartest Hall the local landowner's seat. Running east of the village centre is Hartest Hill, the steepest in Suffolk.[tpl]Citation needed|date=May 2008[/tpl]
