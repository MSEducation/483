[[Fujiwara Dam]]

CATEGORIES: Dams in Gunma Prefecture, Hydroelectric power stations in Japan, Gravity dams, Dams completed in 1957

[tpl]Nihongo|Fujiwara Dam|藤原ダム[/tpl] is a gravity dam on a Tone River tributary in Gunma Prefecture of Japan. It is located [tpl]Convert|14|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of Minakami. It was constructed between 1951 and 1957. The dam itself supports a single Francis turbine hydroelectric generator with a 23 MW capacity which was commissioned in 1957. The reservoir created by the dam serves as the lower reservoir for the 1,200 Tamahara Pumped Storage Power Station which was commissioned in 1986. The dam is [tpl]Convert|95|m|ft|0|abbr=on[/tpl] high and withholds a reservoir with a [tpl]Convert|52490000|m3|acre.ft|0|abbr=on[/tpl] storage capacity.[tpl]cite web|title=Tokyo Electric Power Co. power plant Fujiwara|url=http://www.suiryoku.com/gallery/gunma/fujiwara/fujiwara.html|publisher=Suiryoku|accessdate=17 February 2012|language=Japanese[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Tokyo Electric Power Co. power plant Tamahara|url=http://www.suiryoku.com/gallery/gunma/tanbara/tanbara.html|publisher=Suiryoku|accessdate=17 February 2012|language=Japanese[/tpl]
