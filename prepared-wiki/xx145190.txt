[[Cyberposium]]

CATEGORIES: Harvard University, Technology conferences

Cyberposium is the largest MBA technology and media conference in the world.  Held at Harvard Business School for the past 19 years, the conference draws some 1,000 attendees from the tech/media industries as well as the VC and startup communities.  Held on the campus of HBS, Cyberposium is the primary campus event of Harvard Business School's TechMedia Club.
The conference will be held on Saturday, November 2, 2013, on the school's campus in Boston.  Please see the Official Cyberposium website for more information.
