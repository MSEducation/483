[[Robinsón Pitalúa]]

CATEGORIES: 1964 births, 1985 deaths, Bantamweight boxers, Colombian boxers, Olympic boxers of Colombia, Boxers at the 1983 Pan American Games, Boxers at the 1984 Summer Olympics

Robinson Pitalúa Támara (September 3, 1964 – September 22, 1985) was a Colombian boxer, who represented his native country at the 1984 Summer Olympics in Los Angeles in the Men's Bantamweight division.East Side Boxing - Remembering Robinsón Pitalúa. accessed Nov 2012
Born in Montería, Córdoba Pitalúa won the bronze medal in the same weight category a year earlier at the 1983 Pan American Games.Sports Reference.com. accessed Nov 2012 He died in a drowning accident 1985 in Miami, Florida, USA.East Side Boxing. accessed Nov 2012
