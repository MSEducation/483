[[Gebeleizis]]

CATEGORIES: Dacian gods, Thracian gods, Thunder gods

Gebeleizis (or Gebeleixis, Nebeleizis) was a god worshiped by the Getae, probably related to the Thracian god of storm and lightning, Zibelthiurdos.Tomashek, Die Alten Thraker, II, page 62 He was represented as a handsome man, sometimes wearing a beard. The lightning and thunder were his manifestations. According to Herodotus, some Getae equated Gebeleizis with Zalmoxis as the same god.
