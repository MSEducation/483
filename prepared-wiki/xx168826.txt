[[El Gallito Airstrip]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in Baja California Sur

El Gallito Airstrip [tpl]Airport codes|N/A[/tpl] is a privately owned public-use dirt airstrip at Hotel Serenidad, located 2 miles south of Mulegé, Municipality of Mulegé, Baja California Sur, Mexico, just on the Gulf of California coast. The airstrip is used solely for general aviation purposes.
