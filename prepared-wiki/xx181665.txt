[[Sandy McCubbin]]

CATEGORIES: 1886 births, Year of death missing, Scottish footballers, People from Greenock, Association football forwards, The Football League players, Scottish Football League players, Greenock Morton F.C. players, Bristol Rovers F.C. players, Huddersfield Town F.C. players, Lincoln City F.C. players

Alexander C. "Sandy" McCubbin (born 1886 in Greenock, Scotland) was a professional footballer, who played for Greenock Morton, Bristol Rovers, Huddersfield Town and Lincoln City.
