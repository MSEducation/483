[[Vain Stakes]]

CATEGORIES: Horse races in Australia, Caulfield Racecourse

The Vain Stakes is a Group 3 Australian Thoroughbred horse race for three year old colts and geldings held under set weights with penalties conditions, over a distance of 1100 metres. It is held at Caulfield Racecourse annually in August. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.progroupracing.com.au/group-races/melbourne-racing-club/vain-stakes|title=Winners and Past Results for the Vain Stakes |work = Progroupracing.com (Aus)|year=2012|accessdate=13 August 2013[/tpl] Total prizemoney for the race is A$135,000.

==Race history==

===Race name===

The race was first held in 1992 and is named in honour of the former champion racehorse and sire Vain.
