Radivoje Manić ([tpl]lang-sr-Cyrl|Радивоје Манић[/tpl]; born 16 January 1972 in Pirot) is a Serbian football player who played as a forward. In 2005, he naturalized as a Korean citizen.However, he soon renounced his citizenship after leaving Korea.
