[[Fatimo Isaak Bihi]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, Ambassadors of Somalia to Switzerland, Permanent Representatives of Somalia to the United Nations, Female diplomats, Somalian women in politics, Ethnic Somali people, Somalian diplomats

Fatimo Isaak Bihi is a former Somali diplomat. She was the first ever female ambassador (Ambassador to Switzerland)[tpl]Citation needed|date=August 2010[/tpl]. Bihi is also the former Director of the African Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, as well as being a former Permanent Representative for Somalia at the United Nations.
