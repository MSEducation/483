[[Fineco]]

CATEGORIES: UniCredit Group, Banks of Italy, Banks established in 1999, Companies based in Milan

Fineco is an Italian online bank and brokerage.[tpl]cite web|url=http://online.fineco.it/public/areastampa/profiloEn.asp|title=Fineco - Who We Are|accessdate=2008-10-07[/tpl]

It is the largest online brokerage firm in Italy with over 800,000 customer accounts.
It was founded in 1999 and then integrated with Capitalia.[tpl]cite web|url=http://online.fineco.it/public/areastampa/storiaEn.asp|title=Fineco - History|accessdate=2008-10-07[/tpl]

It is now part of the Unicredit Group after its acquisition of Capitalia in 2007.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ft.com/cms/s/0/9bbdefba-06e0-11dc-93e1-000b5df10621.html?nclick_check=1|title=UniCredit buys Capitalia for â‚¬22bn|last=Michaels|first=Adrian|date=2007-05-20|publisher=Financial Times|language=English|accessdate=2008-10-07[/tpl]
