[[Valsø Holm]]

CATEGORIES: 1906 births, 1987 deaths, Danish male film actors, People from Copenhagen

Valsø Holm (31 December 1906 – 19 December 1987) was a Danish film actor. He appeared in 80 films between 1937 and 1982.
He was born in Copenhagen, Denmark and died in Denmark.
