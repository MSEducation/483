[[Harry Lange (ice hockey)]]

CATEGORIES: 1983 births, Austrian ice hockey players, Graz 99ers players, Klagenfurter AC (ice hockey) players, Living people

Harry Lange (born December 5, 1983 in Klagenfurt) is an Austrian ice hockey forward currently playing for Graz 99ers in the Erste Bank Eishockey League.
Lange began his career with his hometown team EC KAC where he spent four seasons.  In 2004 he moved to his current club Graz.
