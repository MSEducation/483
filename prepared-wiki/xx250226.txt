[[1986 Athens Trophy]]

CATEGORIES: 1986 Virginia Slims World Championship Series, Athens Trophy, 1986 in Greek sport

The 1986 Athens Trophy was a tennis tournament played on outdoor clay courts in Athens in Greece that was part of the 1986 Virginia Slims World Championship Series. It was held from September 15 through September 21, 1986.
