[[2011 Tour de Langkawi]]

CATEGORIES: Tour de Langkawi, 2011 in road cycling, 2011 in Malaysian sport

The 2011 Tour de Langkawi was the 16th edition of the Tour de Langkawi, a cycling stage race that took place in Malaysia. It began on 23 January in Dataran Lang, Langkawi and ended on 1 February in Dataran Merdeka, Kuala Lumpur. The race was sanctioned by the International Cycling Union (UCI) as a 2.HC (hors category) race on the 2010–11 UCI Asia Tour calendar.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cyclingnews.com/races/le-tour-de-langkawi-2-hc|title=2011 Tour de Langkawi at cyclingnews.com|publisher=cyclingnews.com|accessdate=8 March 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cyclingarchives.com/voorloopfiche.php?wedstrijdvoorloopid=12049|title=2011 Tour de Langkawi — Stages|publisher=cyclingarchives.com |accessdate=8 March 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ltdl.com.my/tdl_archive/result/2011/index.asp|title=2011 Tour de Langkawi Results|publisher=ltdl.com.my |accessdate=8 March 2013[/tpl]
Venezuela's Jonathan Monsalve won the race followed by Colombian cyclist, Libardo Niño second and Italian cyclist Emanuele Sella third. Italian cyclist, Andrea Guardini won the points classification and Jonathan Monsalve became the King of the Mountains of the race. [tpl]ct|TPT|2011[/tpl] led the teams classification of the race.

==Teams==

23 teams accepted invitations to participate in the 2011 Tour de Langkawi.
