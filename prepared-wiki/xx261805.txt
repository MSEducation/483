[[Lodewijk de Kruif]]

CATEGORIES: 1969 births, Living people, Dutch footballers, Dutch football managers, FC Oss players, Eerste Divisie players, Dutch expatriates in Nigeria, Dutch expatriates in Bangladesh, Bangladesh national football team managers, People from Ede, Netherlands

Lodewijk de Kruif (born 7 October 1969) is a Dutch football coach and former professional player who is currently the manager of the Bangladesh national football team.

==Career==

===Playing career===

De Kruif made 19 league appearances for TOP Oss in the 1993-94 season.[tpl]nl icon[/tpl] Voetbal International profile

===Coaching career===

After Samson Siasia left Nigerian club Heartland to manage the Nigerian national team, De Kruif was one of four European coaches named on the shortlist to replace him.[tpl]cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/africa/9328477.stm|title=Heartland move to replace Siasia|accessdate=1 January 2011|date=30 December 2010|author=Oluwashina Okeleji|publisher=BBC Sport[/tpl] On 2 May 2012, De Kruif has his contract with Heartland mutually terminated.http://allafrica.com/stories/201205030816.html In January 2013, De Kruif was appointed manager of the Bangladesh national football team.[ref]
