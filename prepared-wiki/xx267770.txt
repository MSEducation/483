[[Dardağan, Adıyaman]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Adıyaman Province, Adıyaman District, Villages in Turkey

 
Dardağan is a village in the District of Adıyaman, Adıyaman Province, Turkey. As of 2011, it had a population of 343 people.[tpl]cite web|url=http://rapor.tuik.gov.tr/reports/rwservlet?adnksdb2&ENVID=adnksdb2Env&report=wa_idari_yapi_10sonrasi.RDF&p_il1=02&p_yil=2011&p_dil=2&desformat=html|title=Population of city, towns and villages - 2011|publisher=Turkish Statistical Institute|accessdate=22 July 2012[/tpl] It is located to the northwest of Adıyaman, on the road to Kuşakkaya.
Its economy is based on agriculture and animal husbandry. The village has a primary school and a drinking water system, but as of 2011 there is no sewage system or post office.[tpl]Citation needed|date=May 2013[/tpl] There is no health center. Asphalt road also provides access to the village and the village has electricity and fixed telephone.
