[[Antoine Gélinas-Beaulieu]]

CATEGORIES: 1992 births, Canadian short track speed skaters, French Quebecers, Living people, People from Sherbrooke

Antoine Gélinas-Beaulieu (born May 5, 1992 in Sherbrooke, Quebec) is a Canadian short track speed skater from Sherbrooke, Quebec currently residing in Montreal. He is currently a member of the national junior short track team for Canada.
At the 2009 World Junior Short Track Championships were held in his hometown of Sherbrooke. Antoine won a silver medal in the 1000 m and a bronze in the 1500 m race, this aided his 4th place finish overall as a 16 year old. Gélinas-Beaulieu also received two bronze medals at the 2010 World Junior Speed Skating Championships in long track.
