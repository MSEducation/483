[[List of Preserve America Communities]]

CATEGORIES: Cultural heritage, Government of the United States, Historic preservation in the United States, Lists of places in the United States

This is a list of United States municipalities, counties, neighborhoods, and tribal communities that have been designated as "Preserve America Communities" under the federal government's Preserve America program. As of 2011, more than 800 communities, representing all 50 states and two U.S. territories, had been so designated.Overview, Preserve America website, accessed July 21, 2009Preserve America Communities directory, Preserve America website, accessed March 17, 2011
