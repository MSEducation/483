[[Le Tech]]

CATEGORIES: Communes of Pyrénées-Orientales

Le Tech ([tpl]lang-ca|El Tec[/tpl]) is a commune in the Pyrénées-Orientales department in southern France.
It is part of the historical Vallespir comarca.
