[[Bundesamt für Eich- und Vermessungswesen]]

CATEGORIES: Geodesy organizations, Cartography organizations, Politics of Austria, Geography of Austria, Measurement, Toponymy

The [tpl]lang|de|Bundesamt für Eich- und Vermessungswesen[/tpl] or BEV (German for Federal Office of Metrology and Surveying) is the body responsible for official surveying, geo-information and weights and measures (metrology) in Austria. It belongs to the [tpl]lang|de|Bundesministerium für Wirtschaft, Familie und Jugend[/tpl] (Federal Ministry of the Economy, Family and Youth). Its headquarters is in Vienna and it has 67 branches spread across all the Austrian federal states.Über das BEV at www.bev.gv.at. Accessed on 30 Dec 10.
