[[Bulqizë District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Albania, Dibër County

The District of Bulqizë ([tpl]Lang-sq|Rrethi i Bulqizës[/tpl]) is one of the thirty-six districts of Albania, part of Dibër County. It has a population of 28,374,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.instat.gov.al/graphics/doc/tabelat/Treguesit%20Sociale/Popullsia/POP%202009/t2.xls|title=POPULLSIA SIPAS RRETHEVE, 2001-2010|publisher=Albanian Institute of Statistics|accessdate=2010-09-09[/tpl] and an area of 469 km². It is in the east of the country and its capital is Bulqizë.
