Raymond Victoria (born 10 October 1972 in Utrecht) is a retired football midfielder.

==Career==

Victoria played for Feyenoord, De Graafschap, Willem II and ADO Den Haag in the Netherlands.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Voetbal International|title=Raymond Victoria|url=http://www.vi.nl/Spelers/Speler/Raymond-Victoria.htm|language=Dutch|accessdate=2009-08-26[/tpl] After making more than 200 appearances in seven seasons with Willem II, Victoria signed for Cypriot side AEK Larnaca in June 2006.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Volkskrant.nl|title=Voetbal: Victoria naar Cyprus|url=http://www.volkskrant.nl/vk/nl/2698/Sport/article/detail/830723/2006/06/16/Voetbal-Victoria-naar-Cyprus.dhtml|language=Dutch|date=6 June 2006[/tpl]
