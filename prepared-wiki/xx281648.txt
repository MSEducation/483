[[Ryan Robinson (American football)]]

CATEGORIES: 1990 births, Living people, American football defensive ends, Oklahoma State Cowboys football players, Undrafted National Football League players, Oakland Raiders players

Ryan Robinson (born December 9, 1990) is an American football defensive end for the Oakland Raiders  of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football for the Oklahoma State.

==High School Career==

He played his high school football at Mill Creek High School, in Hoschton, Georgia.

==Professional Career==

===Oakland Raiders===

He was first signed by the Oakland Raiders as an undrafted free agent in 2013.
