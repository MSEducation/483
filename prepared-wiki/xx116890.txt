[[Vicious Delite (album)]]

CATEGORIES: 1995 albums

Vicious Delite is the one and only full-length album by Vicious Delite released in 1995. It was re-released in 2000. 

==Track listing==

The Japanese release (copyright 1995) has 10 songs:  the first 9 songs above followed by "Secret Agent Man."  The first song is listed as "The Screw," but is the same song.
