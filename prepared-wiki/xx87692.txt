[[Anstey railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1926

Anstey railway station is located on the Upfield line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the northern Melbourne suburb of Brunswick opening on 15 December 1926 as North Brunswick, being renamed Anstey on 1 December 1942 after former State and Federal Member of Parliament, Frank Anstey.Anstey Vicsig
