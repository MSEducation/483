[[KCOR]]

CATEGORIES: Hispanic and Latino American culture in San Antonio, Texas, Radio stations in San Antonio, Texas, Spanish-language radio stations in Texas, Univision Radio Network stations

KCOR (1350 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Spanish-language full service format. Licensed to San Antonio, Texas, USA, the station serves the San Antonio area.  The station is currently owned by Tichenor License Corporation and features programming from Univision America.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KCOR |title=KCOR Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division[/tpl] The station also broadcasts on KXTN's HD2 signal.  Its studios and transmitter are located (separately) in Northwest San Antonio.
