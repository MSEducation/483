[[The Broken Down Comforter Collection]]

CATEGORIES: 1999 albums, Grandaddy albums

The Broken Down Comforter Collection is an album by the band Grandaddy, released in June 1999 by Big Cat Records.Strong, Martin C. (2003) The Great Indie Discography, Canongate, ISBN 1-84195-335-0, p. 773 It is a combination of the tracks from the mini-album A Pretty Mess By This One Band and the EP Machines Are Not She.

==Reception==

The NME gave it 8/10 and called it "patchy" but stated "there are gems hidden on here iridescent enough to render the collection essential"."The Broken Down Comforter Collection", NME, May 28, 1999, retrieved 2011-08-07 Allmusic writer Ben Davies gave it a three-star review, stating that the "low points come around rarely, and are far outnumbered by the quality tracks".Davies, Ben "Broken Down Comforter Collection Review", Allmusic, retrieved 2011-08-07

==Track listing==

All tracks written by Jason Lytle.
