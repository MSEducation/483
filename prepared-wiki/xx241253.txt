[[89th Indian Infantry Brigade]]

CATEGORIES: British Indian Army brigades

The 89th Indian Infantry Brigade was an Infantry formation of the Indian Army during World War II. It was formed in October 1942, at Bhiar Kund in India. The brigade was assigned to the 7th Indian Infantry Division and fought in the Burma Campaign. Apart from between May to June 1944, when it was attached to the 5th Indian Infantry Division, the brigade remained with the 7th Division until the end of the war.[tpl]cite web|accessdate=2009-10-23|publisher=Order of Battle|title=89 Indian Brigade|url=http://www.ordersofbattle.com/UnitData.aspx?UniX=6198&Tab=Sup[/tpl]
