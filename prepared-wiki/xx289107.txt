[[Isa Beyglu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Isa Beyglu ([tpl]lang-fa|عيسي بيگلو[/tpl], also Romanized as ‘Īsá Beyglū; also known as ‘Aīsbaklu and Īsā Beglū)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3067281|Isa Beyglu[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 23, in 4 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
