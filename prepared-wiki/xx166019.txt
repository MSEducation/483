[[Sean DePaula]]

CATEGORIES: 1973 births, Akron Aeros players, Cleveland Indians players, Living people, Buffalo Bisons (minor league) players, Kinston Indians players, Louisville Bats players, Major League Baseball pitchers, Baseball players from Massachusetts

Sean Michael DePaula (born November 7, 1973 in Newton, Massachusetts) is an American former Major League Baseball player. A pitcher, DePaula played for the Cleveland Indians appearing in major league games in [tpl]By|1999[/tpl], [tpl]By|2000[/tpl], and [tpl]By|2002[/tpl]. In [tpl]By|2003[/tpl], DePaula played 10 games for the Cincinnati Reds Triple-A affiliate, the Louisville Bats.
