[[Darebin railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1922

Darebin railway station is located on the Hurstbridge line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the north-eastern Melbourne suburb of Ivanhoe opening on 8 May 1922.Darebin Vicsig
