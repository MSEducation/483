[[Thibault Tchicaya]]

Thibault Tchicaya (born 17 July 1983) is a Gabon international footballer who plays as a defender who plays for Missile FC.

==Career==

Born in Makokou, Tchicaya has played in his native Gabon for TP Akwembe, Delta Téléstar, Sogéa FC and Missile FC. At age 30, he signed with Mbabane Swallows F.C. of Swaziland on a six-month contract in January 2009.[tpl]cite web|publisher=The Swazi Observer|author=Ndzinisa, Sabelo|title="Birds" to sign highly-rated Gabonese defender|url=http://www.observer.org.sz/index.php?news=1201|date=27 January 2009[/tpl]
Tchicaya has made several appearances for the Gabon national football team. He played for the side the finished third at the 2005 CEMAC Cup.[tpl]cite web|publisher=RSSSF|author=De Bock, Christofhe and Batalha, José|title=Coupe de la CEMAC 2005|url=http://www.rsssf.com/tablesc/cemac05.html|date=5 March 2006[/tpl]
