[[Qarah Hesarlu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Qarah Hesarlu ([tpl]lang-fa|قره حصارلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Qarah Ḩeşārlū and Qareh Ḩeşārlū; also known as Ḩeşārlū)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3771554|Qarah Hesarlu[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Pain Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 93, in 21 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
