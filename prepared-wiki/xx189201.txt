[[Ludmila Savelyeva]]

CATEGORIES: Soviet actresses, 1942 births, Living people

Ludmila Savelyeva (Russian: Людмила Савельева) is a Russian film actress and ballerina. Born on 24 January 1942 in Leningrad, she achieved lasting fame in the role of Natasha Rostova in the 1966-68 film War and Peace, which was six years in the making. She won a Dipolma prize for this role at the 4th Moscow International Film Festival.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.moscowfilmfestival.ru/miff34/eng/archives/?year=1965 |title=4th Moscow International Film Festival (1965) |accessdate=2012-12-08 |work=MIFF[/tpl]
