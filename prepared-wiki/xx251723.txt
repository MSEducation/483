[[Laguna BelAir School]]

CATEGORIES: Elementary schools in the Philippines, Science high schools in the Philippines, Educational institutions in Santa Rosa, Laguna, Education in Laguna (province), Educational institutions established in 1996

Laguna BelAir School (LBAS, or BelAir) is a science and math oriented private school located in Santa Rosa City, Laguna, Philippines.[tpl]cite news |title=New Laguna BelAir School Building |newspaper=Philippine Daily Inquirer |date=2002-02-19 |url=http://news.google.com/newspapers?id=x1o1AAAAIBAJ&sjid=kiUMAAAAIBAJ&pg=758,8631540&dq=laguna-belair-school&hl=en |accessdate=2010-01-10[/tpl] The school's curriculum is patterned after that of the Philippine Science High School System. The school was founded on February 13, 1996, with an emphasis on science and mathematics.[tpl]cite news |title=Empire East increases stake in school |author=Cuevas-Miel, Likha C. |newspaper=The Manila Times |date=2008-01-04 |url=http://www.manilatimes.net/national/2008/jan/04/yehey/business/20080104bus7.html |accessdate=2008-01-04 |archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/5mfmvEAuY |archivedate=2010-01-10[/tpl]
