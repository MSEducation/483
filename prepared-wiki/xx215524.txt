[[Huntsville Metropolitan Area]]

CATEGORIES: Huntsville metropolitan area, Geography of Madison County, Alabama, Geography of Limestone County, Alabama, Geography of Huntsville, Alabama

The Huntsville Metropolitan Area is a metropolitan statistical area in northern Alabama. The Huntsville Metropolitan Area's population was estimated at 395,645.

==Geography==

===Counties===

The metro area comprises two counties: Limestone and Madison.
