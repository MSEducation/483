[[North Brunswick High School]]

CATEGORIES: Schools in Brunswick County, North Carolina, Public high schools in North Carolina

North Brunswick High School is a public school located in Leland, North Carolina which is part of Brunswick County Schools district.  As of the 2007-08 school years, North Brunswick has 892 students  in grades 9 through 12 currently enrolled.  

==Extracurricular activities==

===Sport===

Sports teams include American Football, Volleyball, Cheerleading, Soccer, Basketball, Cross Country, Wrestling, Baseball, Softball, Track and Field, Golf,  Archery,  and Tennis

===Societies===

The school has a wide range of clubs and societies including a Science Olympiad, Dance Team, French Honor Society, Show Choir, After School Chorus and a Drama Department.
