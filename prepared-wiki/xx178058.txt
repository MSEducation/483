[[Laura Bissell]]

CATEGORIES: 1983 births, Living people, People from Hitchin, People from Stevenage, English female cyclists

Laura Elizabeth Bissell (born 19 June 1983)[tpl]cite web| url=http://members.fortunecity.es/pedalear/patriseven/feminas.htm| title=Ciclismo Femenino - 2007[/tpl] is an English road and track racing cyclist.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.roadcyclinguk.com/news/article/mps/uan/667|title=Team Fat Birds UK Women's 2 Day|date=13 June 2005|publisher=roadcyclinguk.com[/tpl]
Bissell was born in Hitchin, England.  She has represented her country at international events on several occasions.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.sportstevenage.co.uk/?page=profiles|title=Profiles of Local Sports People|publisher=Stevenage Sport[/tpl] She was a member of the British Cycling Development Plan squad alongside Nicole Cooke, competing at the 2000 and 2001 junior road world championship and the 2001 junior track world championships in Trexlertown, United States.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.uksport.gov.uk/news/597/|title=British Cycling Names World Road Team|publisher=UK Sport|date=19 September 2001[/tpl] 
Bissell lives in Stevenage, Hertfordshire. Her brother, Peter Bissell, was also a successful cyclist.
