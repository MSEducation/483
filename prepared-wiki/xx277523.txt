Unione Sportiva Dro is an Italian football club based in Dro, Trentino-Alto Adige. It currently plays in Italy's Serie D.

==History==

===Serie D===

In the season 2012-13 the team was promoted for the first time, from Eccellenza Trentino-Alto Adige to Serie D.

==Colors and badge==

The team's color is blue.
