[[K. V. Johansen]]

CATEGORIES: 1968 births, Living people, 20th-century Canadian novelists, 21st-century Canadian novelists, Canadian children's writers, Canadian fantasy writers, Canadian science fiction writers, People from Kingston, Ontario, People from Sackville, New Brunswick, Canadian women novelists, Writers from New Brunswick, Women science fiction and fantasy writers

K.V. Johansen (born 1968) is a Canadian fantasy, science fiction, and children's author.
Born in Kingston, Ontario, Johansen holds Master's degrees in Medieval Studies and English literature.
She lives in Sackville, New Brunswick.
