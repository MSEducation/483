[[Dream, Tiresias!]]

CATEGORIES: 2009 albums, Project Pitchfork albums

Dream, Tiresias! is a 2009 album by the German Electro-industrial act Project Pitchfork. It is their 13th studio album and was released  in multiple formats, including a double-disc limited version.
