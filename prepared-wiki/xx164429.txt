[[Utricularia biloba]]

CATEGORIES: Carnivorous plants of Australia, Flora of Queensland, Flora of New South Wales, Utricularia, Lamiales of Australia

Utricularia biloba, the moth bladderwort,[tpl]NSW Flora Online|genus=Utricularia|species=biloba|author=R. Rowe & E. A. Brown[/tpl] is a perennial, terrestrial or aquatic carnivorous plant that belongs to the genus Utricularia (family Lentibulariaceae). It is endemic to Australia with a distribution along the coastal regions of New South Wales and Queensland.Taylor, Peter. (1989). The genus Utricularia - a taxonomic monograph. Kew Bulletin Additional Series XIV: London.
