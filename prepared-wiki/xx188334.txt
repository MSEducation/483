[[Carole Berry]]

CATEGORIES: 20th-century American novelists, American mystery writers, American women novelists, Living people, Writers from New York City

Carole Berry is an American mystery fiction writer who is best known for her amateur sleuth series featuring New York City office temp worker Bonnie Indermill. Berry also has one suspense novel to her credit. She resides in New York City and is married.
