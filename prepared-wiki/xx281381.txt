[[Sidney M. Cadwell]]

CATEGORIES: Polymer scientists and engineers, U.S. Synthetic Rubber Program

Sidney Marsh Cadwell (5 Mar 1893-24 Jun 1986) noted as discoverer of anti-oxidants for rubber.[tpl]cite web|title=Obit in Grosse Pointe News 1986|url=http://digitize.gp.lib.mi.us/digitize/newspapers/gpnews/1985-89/86/1986-07-03.pdf|accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl]   Cadwell published perhaps the first scientific study of the fatigue behavior of rubber.[tpl]cite journal|last=Cadwell|first=Sidney|coauthors=Merrill, Sloman, Yost|title=Dynamic fatigue life of rubber|journal=Rubber Chemistry and Technology|year=1940|volume=13|issue=2|page=304-315|doi=10.5254/1.3539515|accessdate=19 August 2013[/tpl]   
Cadwell served as Chairman of the Rubber Division of the American Chemical Society in 1935 and was named the 1956 Charles Goodyear Medalist.
