[[Dangermuffin]]

CATEGORIES: American folk musical groups

Dangermuffin is an American band from Folly Beach, South Carolina. Their musical styles include Americana, folk, and jam music that's organic and cool.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.mysanantonio.com/entertainment/article/Jim-Beal-Jr-Night-Lights-1799288.php |title=Music Type |publisher=mysanantonio.com |date=2011-08-10 |accessdate=2011-11-29[/tpl] The band is a trio whose instrumentation includes acoustic guitar, electric guitar, and drums. The group was founded in 2007 and has been touring coast-to-coast since.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.charlestonscene.com/news/2011/nov/17/17turkeyjam/ |title=Coast-to-coast |publisher=charlestonscene.com |date=2011-11-17 |accessdate=2011-11-29[/tpl] Dangermuffin just released their fourth album, Olly Oxen Free, in May 2012.

==Style==

Dangermuffin's music is known for the songwriting and vocals of Dan Lotti,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.headstash.com/magazine/artist-spotlight/1177-dangermuffin-and-jam |title=Songwriting |publisher=headstash.com |date=2011-09-27 |accessdate=2011-11-29[/tpl] winner of the Songwriters Hall of Fame's 2005 "New Writers Showcase." Their music receives heavy airplay on Sirius XM Radio and was featured in the June 2011 issue of Relix Magazine.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.relix.com/on-the-verge/2011/06/10/dangermuffin |title=On The Verge |publisher=relix.com |date=2011-06-10 |accessdate=2011-09-23[/tpl] Dangermuffin was voted the Best Jam/Groove/Reggae Band of the Year in the 2011 Charleston City Paper Music Awards.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.charlestoncitypaper.com/charleston/the-winners-of-the-2011-city-paper-music-awards/Content?oid=3629174 |title=Best Jam/Groove/Reggae Band of the Year |publisher=charlestoncitypaper.com |date=2011-10-19 |accessdate=2011-11-28[/tpl]

==Philanthropy==

Dangermuffin gave back to the community after donating a percentage of merchandise sales to the Ronald McDonald House Charity of Charleston, SC. They also hosted the Turkey Jam, a benefit for the Low country Food Bank that raised over $3,000.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.charlestoncitypaper.com/charleston/annual-turkey-jam-is-a-feast-for-the-senses/Content?oid=3648588 |title=Turkey Jam |publisher=charlestoncitypaper.com |date=2011-11-22 |accessdate=2011-11-29[/tpl]

==Discography==

==Live Performances==

Dangermuffin has performed at some of the largest attended music festivals in the United States, including Wanee Music Festival in Florida, moe.down in New York, Bristol Rhythm & Roots Reunion in Tennessee, Taos Mountain Music Festival in New Mexico, Summer Camp Music Festival in Illinois, FloydFest in Virginia, Jazz Aspen in Colorado, and All Good Music Festival in Masontown, West Virginia. Dangermuffin has also opened for bands like Hot Tuna with Jorma Kaukonen and O.A.R..

==Members==

Dan Lotti - lyrics and acoustic guitar  
Mike Sivilli - lyrics and electric guitar  
Steven Sandifer - lyrics and drum set (2008–Present)  
Jim Donnelly - drum set (2007–2008)
