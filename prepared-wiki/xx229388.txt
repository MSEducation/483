[[Animal!]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 albums, Epic Records albums, Margot and the Nuclear So and So's albums

Animal! is the second full-length album chosen and released by Margot & the Nuclear So and So's, the first on Epic Records. It is being co-released with the label's choice, Not Animal. It was recorded at Engine Studios in Chicago, IL. Brian Deck produced the album and it was engineered by Neil Strauch.
