Jerson Anes Ribeiro (born March 9, 1988 in Rotterdam, Netherlands), is a Cape Verdean international footballer who plays, as a attacking midfielder, for Capelle.

==Career==

He also played for FC Dordrecht in the Eerste Divisie (on loan from Feyenoord), and SBV Excelsior, with whom he spent an entire Eerste Divisie season (on loan from Feyenoord) plus the first half of the club's 2010–11 Eredivisie campaign.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.transfermarkt.co.uk/en/jerson-ribeiro/leistungsdaten-detail/spieler_31117.html |publisher=Transfermarkt |accessdate=3 February 2011 |title=Jerson Ribeiro [/tpl]
He joined Fortuna Sittard of Eerste Divisie on 31 January 2011.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.fortunasittard.nl/index.jsp?id=15582#15582 |publisher=Fortuna Sittard|accessdate=3 February 2011 |date=31 January 2011|title=Fortuna Sittard huurt Mossouai en Ribeiro van Excelsior [/tpl] He joined FC Etar 1924 Veliko Tarnovo of Bulgaria in 2013.
