[[Muhammad Isa Ahmad]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1998 births, Bruneian swimmers

Muhammad Isa Ahmad (born 1998) is a Bruneian swimmer.http://www.bcn2013.com/en/swimming/detail/member/muhammad-isa-ahmad He competed in the 50 m and 100 m breaststroke events at the 2012 FINA World Swimming Championships (25 m) and in the 50 m and 100 m breaststroke events, the former of which he currently hold the national record with a time of 31.29 seconds,http://www.bt.com.bn/sports-national/2013/07/29/bittersweet-day-muhammad-isa at the 2013 World Aquatics Championships.
