[[Corey Mystyshyn]]

CATEGORIES: Canadian television variety show hosts, Living people

Corey Mystyshyn was a Polish-born Canadian actor, comedian, theatre director, poet, playwright and political activist who is best known for hosting the Hamilton, Ontario TV show Big Top Talent in the early 1980s.
