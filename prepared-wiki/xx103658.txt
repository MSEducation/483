[[Yuan Ze University]]

CATEGORIES: Educational institutions established in 1989, Universities and colleges in Taiwan, Zhongli, Buildings and structures in Taoyuan County, Taiwan, Education in Taoyuan County, Taiwan

Yuan Ze University (YZU; [tpl]zh|t=元智大學[/tpl]) is a private university located in Taoyuan, Taiwan.[tpl]cite web|url=http://web2.yzu.edu.tw/eng_2003/aboutYZU|title=Introduction of Yuan Ze University|work=Yuan Ze University|year=2012|accessdate=2012-10-13[/tpl] Established in 1989, it has campuses across Zhongli Taipai and Bade City.
This university provides scholarship to students for Masters degree.
In 2012, Yuan Ze University ranked 70th in Times Higher Education World University Rankings's top 100 universities under 50 years old category, and ranked within 350-400th place in its 2011-2012 World University Ranking category.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.timeshighereducation.co.uk/world-university-rankings/2012/one-hundred-under-fifty/institution/yuan-ze-university|title=100 UNDER 50 2012: Yuan Ze University|work=Times Higher Education World University Rankings|year=2012|accessdate=2012-10-13[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.timeshighereducation.co.uk/world-university-rankings/2011-12/world-ranking/institution/yuan-ze-university|title=World Rank 2011-2012: Yuan Ze University|work=Times Higher Education World University Rankings|year=2012|accessdate=2012-10-13[/tpl] Yuan Ze University is the only private education institute from Taiwan listed in Times Higher Education's top 400's ranking.
