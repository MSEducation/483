[[Éric Rochant]]

CATEGORIES: 1961 births, Living people, French film directors

Éric Rochant (born February 24, 1961, Paris), is a French film director and a screenwriter. He is a former student of the IDHEC (FEMIS) from the generation of Arnaud Desplechin and Noémie Lvovsky.
