CATEGORIES: Airports in Nepal, Dang district

Dang Airport [tpl]airport codes|DNP|VNDG[/tpl], also known as Tulsipur Airport or Tarigaun Airport, is an airport serving the Dang Deukhuri District in the Rapti Zone of the Mid-Western Region in Nepal.  The airport is [tpl]convert|2|km|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south of the town of Tulsipur and [tpl]convert|23|km|abbr=on[/tpl] west of Tribhuvannagar (also known as Ghorahi), the main city of the Dang Valley.

==Facilities==

The airport resides at an elevation of [tpl]convert|2100|ft|0[/tpl] above mean sea level. It has one runway which is [tpl]convert|832|m|0[/tpl] in length.
