[[Tetsuo Suda]]

CATEGORIES: 1948 births, Living people, FNN personalities, Japanese television personalities

[tpl]Nihongo|Tetsuo Suda|須田 哲夫|Suda Tetsuo|extra=born 16 January 1948, Shinagawa, Tokyo[/tpl] is a Japanese TV presenter and news anchor.
He graduated from Keio University, and joined Fuji TV in 1971.
Since April 2000, he has worked on Fuji Television's Super News evening news programme alongside Yūko Andō.
