[[Into the Stars]]

CATEGORIES: 2002 singles, Olivia Lufkin songs

"Into the Stars" is Olivia's 8th solo single. It was released on September 4, 2002. The title track was used in a commercial for "Hairmake Earth". The single reached #99 on the Oricon charts, and charted for one week.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.oricon.co.jp/prof/artist/18547/products/release/|title=Oliver's Oricon chart history|publisher=Oricon|accessdate=2010-12-19[/tpl]
