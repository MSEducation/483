[[Zalha]]

CATEGORIES: Communes in Sălaj County

Zalha ([tpl]lang-hu|Zálha[/tpl]) is a commune located in Sălaj County, Romania. It is composed of seven villages: Ceaca (Almáscsáka), Ciureni (Csurenypuszta), Valea Ciurenilor (Csurenyvölgy), Valea Hranei (Tormapataka), Valea Lungă (Gorbómező), Vârteşca (Virtyeskatelep) and Zalha.
