[[Eric Schumann]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, American football defensive backs, Alabama Crimson Tide football players, UAB Blazers football coaches, New Mexico Lobos football coaches, SMU Mustangs football coaches, Tulane Green Wave football coaches

Eric Schumann is an American football coach . His most recent job was as defensive coordinator at UAB from 2007-2010.
He served the previous five years as defensive coordinator of Tulane, and has coached defense at SMU, New Mexico, and Troy State. He was a defensive back at Alabama from 1973 to 1978.
