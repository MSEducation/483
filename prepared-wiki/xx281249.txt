[[2013 China Open (tennis)]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 ATP World Tour, 2013 WTA Tour, China Open (tennis), 2013 in Chinese sport

The 2013 China Open was a tennis tournament played on outdoor hard courts. It was the 15th edition of the China Open, and part of the ATP 500 Series of the 2013 ATP World Tour, and of the Premier Series of the 2013 WTA Tour. Both the men's and the women's events were held at the National Tennis Center in Beijing, China, from September 30 till October 6, 2013.
