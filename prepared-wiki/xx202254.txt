[[Sabou Department]]

CATEGORIES: Departments of Burkina Faso, Boulkiemdé Province

Sabou is a department or commune of Boulkiemdé Province in central Burkina Faso. As of 2005 it has a population of 45,378. Its capital lies at the town of Sabou.Burkinabé government inforoute communale
