[[Huntingdale railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1927

Huntingdale railway station is located on the Cranbourne and Pakenham lines in Victoria, Australia. It serves the south-eastern Melbourne suburb of Huntingdale opening on 25 June 1927 as East Oakleigh, being renamed Huntingdale on 1 October 1954.Huntingdale Vicsig

==Platforms & services==

Huntingdale has one island platform with two faces. It is serviced by Metro Trains' Cranbourne and Pakenham line services.Cranbourne Line timetable Public Transport VictoriaPakenham Line timetable Public Transport Victoria
