[[Ben Reeves]]

CATEGORIES: 1991 births, Living people, People from Dorset, English footballers, Southampton F.C. players, The Football League players, Association football midfielders, Premier League players, Southend United F.C. players, Dagenham & Redbridge F.C. players, Milton Keynes Dons F.C. players

Benjamin Neil Reeves (born 19 November 1991) is an English-born Northern Irish professional footballer who plays for Milton Keynes Dons as a midfielder.

==Club career==

Having started out in the youth team as a left back, he has since been converted into an attacking midfielder. On 5 April 2011, Reeves signed his first professional contract on a two year deal.[tpl]cite news|title=Southampton sign Plymouth Argyle defender Jack Stephens|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/s/southampton/9447524.stm|publisher=BBC Sport|date=5 April 2011|accessdate=3 July 2013[/tpl]
On 21 September 2011, he made his debut for Southampton, playing the full 90 minutes in the League Cup third round match against Preston North End. He set up the first goal in a 2–1 win.[tpl]cite web| title=Southampton 2 – 1 Preston|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/14914992.stm| publisher=BBC Sport| date=21 September 2011| accessdate=22 September 2011[/tpl]
He joined Dagenham & Redbridge on a month's loan on 23 February 2012,[tpl]cite web|title=Dagenham & Redbridge sign Jonathan Bond and Ben Reeves|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/17140531|publisher=BBC Sport|accessdate=18 February 2013 |date=23 February 2012[/tpl] making five appearances.[tpl]cite web|title=Reeves Set For Southend Spell |url=http://www.saintsfc.co.uk/news/article/reeves-set-for-southend-spell-631201.aspx|publisher=Southampton FC|accessdate=1 February 2013 |date=31 January 2013[/tpl]
He scored his first goal for Southampton against Stevenage in the League Cup on 28 August 2012.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/19340365 |title=Stevenage 1 – 4 Southampton |publisher= BBC Sport |date=28 August 2012 |accessdate=28 August 2012[/tpl] He made his Premier League debut as a substitute in a 3–1 defeat against Everton at Goodison Park on 29 September.[tpl]cite web |last=Johnston|first=Neil|title=Everton 3 – 1 Southampton|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/19684126|publisher=BBC Sport |accessdate=18 February 2013|date=29 September 2012[/tpl]
After six first-team appearances for the Saints in 2012–13, on 31 January 2013 Reeves joined Southend United on loan for a month.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.saintsfc.co.uk/news/article/reeves-set-for-southend-spell-631201.aspx |title=Reeves Set for Southend Spell|publisher=Southampton F.C. |date=31 January 2013 |accessdate=31 January 2013 [/tpl][tpl]cite news|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/21286041 |title=Southampton: Ben Reeves loaned to Southend United |publisher=BBC Sport|date=31 January 2013 |accessdate=31 January 2013 [/tpl] He scored his first goal for the club on 12 February, in a 2–1 defeat at home to Cheltenham Town.[tpl]cite web|title=Southend 1 – 2 Cheltenham |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/21332978|publisher=BBC Sport|accessdate=18 February 2013|date=12 February 2013[/tpl] Eight days later, Reeves scored his second goal for the club, in the last minutes of the Football League Trophy Southern Area Final, as Southend beat Leyton Orient 3–2 on aggregate, to send the club to the final.[tpl]cite news|title=Southend 2–2 L Orient (agg 3–2)|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/21449291|publisher=BBC Sport|date=20 February 2013|accessdate=3 July 2013[/tpl]
On 15 March 2013, it announced that Reeves had re-joined Southend United on loan, until the end of the season, in his second spell at the club.[tpl]cite news|title=Ben Reeves: Southend United re-sign JPT hero on loan|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/21808135|publisher= BBC Sport|date=15 March 2013|accessdate=3 July 2013[/tpl] Reeves played his first match in his second loan spell, providing an assist for Freddy Eastwood, in a 1–1 draw against Torquay United.[tpl]cite news|title=Southend United 1, Torquay United 1|url=http://www.echo-news.co.uk/sport/10294824.Southend_United_1__Torquay_United_1/|publisher=Daily Echo|date=16 March 2013|accessdate=3 July 2013[/tpl] Reeves played in the final of the Football League Trophy against Crewe, where he came on as a substitute for Bilel Mohsni in the 57th minutes, as Southend lost 2–0.
On 4 June 2013, he was released by Southampton.Saints 2012/13 release list After his release, Reeves was linked with a possible return to Southend United.[tpl]cite news|title=Southend United manager Phil Brown confirms interest in Ben Reeves and Matthew Lund|url=http://www.echo-news.co.uk/sport/10469018.Southend_United_manager_Phil_Brown_confirms_interest_in_Ben_Reeves_and_Matthew_Lund/|publisher=Daily Echo|date=7 June 2013|accessdate=3 July 2013[/tpl]
On 13 July 2013, Reeves  signed for Milton Keynes Dons on a one year contract.http://www.mkdons.com/news/article/dons-capture-reeves-911899.aspx#S7XxIeyb0dTHZjJl.99
