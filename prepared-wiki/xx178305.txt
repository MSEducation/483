[[Gmina Lelis]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Ostrołęka County

Gmina Lelis is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Ostrołęka County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Lelis, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|13|km|mi|0[/tpl] north of Ostrołęka and [tpl]convert|114|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|197|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 8,364.

==Villages==

Gmina Lelis contains the villages and settlements of Białobiel, Dąbrówka, Długi Kąt, Durlasy, Gąski, Gibałka, Gnaty, Kurpiewskie, Łęg Przedmiejski, Łęg Starościński, Lelis, Łodziska, Nasiadki, Obierwia, Olszewka, Płoszyce, Siemnocha, Szafarczyska, Szafarnia, Szkwa, Szwendrowy Most and Walery.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Lelis is bordered by the gminas of Baranowo, Kadzidło, Miastkowo, Olszewo-Borki, Rzekuń and Zbójna.
