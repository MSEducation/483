[[Minnah Karlsson (album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2010 albums, Covers albums

Minnah Karlsson is a cover album by Minnah Karlsson, Sweden. On the album are songs that she has made during the Swedish Idol 2010. The album was released on 19 December 2010 for digital download and 20 December 2010 in stores. Adam Lambert has been celebrating Minnah for her cover of his song Whataya Want From Me who is featured on the album.
