[[Tokyo Daigaku Monogatari]]

CATEGORIES: 1992 manga, 1994 Japanese television series debuts, 2004 anime OVAs, 2006 films, Japanese films, Films based on manga, Japanese television dramas, Japanese-language films, Shogakukan manga, Tatsuya Egawa, TV Asahi shows

[tpl]nihongo|Tōkyō Daigaku Monogatari|東京大学物語||lit. "Tokyo University Story"[/tpl] is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Tatsuya Egawa.
In the story, high schooler [tpl]nihongo|Naoki Murakami|村上 直樹|Murakami Naoki[/tpl] asks [tpl]nihongo|Haruka Mizuno|水野 遥|Mizuno Haruka[/tpl] to date him. They date while both worry about their upcoming university entrance examinations.
A live action film of the same name directed by Tatsuya Egawa and based on his manga was released in February 2006. The screenplay was written by Kotoe Nagata, and the film, produced by the Soft On Demand studio, starred Yoko Mitsuya, Kei Tanaka, Kazuki Namioka, Sasa Handa, Fujiko, Naoko Watanabe and Takeshi Masu.[tpl]cite web|url=http://twitchfilm.com/news/2006/01/trailer-for-tatsuya-egawas-tokyo-university-story-tky-daigaku-monogatari-st.php|title=Trailer for Tatsuya Egawa's TOKYO UNIVERSITY STORY|publisher=twitchfilm.com|accessdate=2011-02-28[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0781085/|title=Tôkyô Daigaku monogatari|publisher=IMDb|accessdate=2011-02-28[/tpl]
Also a two-part OVA series was produced.
