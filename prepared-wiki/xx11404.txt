CATEGORIES: Vehicles

A vehicle (from [tpl]lang-la|vehiculum[/tpl][tpl]Citation |chapter=vehicle, n. |title= OED Online |date=November 2010 |publisher=Oxford University Press [/tpl]) is a mobile machine that transports passengers or cargo. Most often, vehicles are manufactured, such as wagons, bicycles, Motor vehicles (motorcycles, cars, trucks, buses,  trains), watercraft (ships, boats), spacecraft and aircraft.Halsey, William D. (Editorial Director): MacMillan Contemporary Dictionary, page 1106. MacMillan Publishing, 1979. ISBN 0-02-080780-5
Land vehicles are classified broadly by what is used to apply steering and drive forces against the ground: wheeled, tracked, railed or skied. ISO 3833-1977 is the standard, also internationally used in legislation, for road vehicles types, terms and definitions.ISO 3833:1977 Road vehicles – Types – Terms and definitions Webstore.anis.org
