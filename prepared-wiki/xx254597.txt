[[Miles in Tokyo]]

CATEGORIES: Albums produced by Michael Cuscuna, Albums produced by Bob Belden, Miles Davis live albums, 1964 live albums, Columbia Records live albums, Sony Music Entertainment live albums

Miles in Tokyo is a live album recorded on July 14, 1964,[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.plosin.com/milesAhead/Sessions.aspx?s=640714 |title=Miles Ahead Session Details |accessdate=2009-01-10 |last=Losin |first=Peter |work=Miles Ahead |publisher=Peter Losin [/tpl] by the Miles Davis Quintet at the Kohseinenkin Hall, Shinjuku, Tokyo, Japan.  It was released in the United States on CD in 2005 and is the first recording of Davis in Japan. It is the only album[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.allaboutjazz.com/php/article.php?id=18526|title =Miles in Tokyo - AllAboutJazz Review[/tpl][tpl]cite web | url = http://www.allmusic.com/album/miles-in-tokyo-mw0002223441|title =Miles in Tokyo - AllMusic Review[/tpl] to showcase an early incarnation of his Second Great Quintet featuring Sam Rivers on tenor saxophone, following George Coleman's departure; after this, Wayne Shorter's appointment would complete the classic lineup which would go on to record such albums as ESP, Nefertiti and Miles Smiles, through to Miles in the Sky.
