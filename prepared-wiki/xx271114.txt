[[(18393) 1992 QB]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1992, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(18393) 1992 QB is a main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on August 19, 1992.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
