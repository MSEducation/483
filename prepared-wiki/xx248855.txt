[[Chance of Love]]

CATEGORIES: 2003 singles, Zetima Records singles, Song recordings produced by Tsunku

[tpl]nihongo|"Chance of Love"|チャンス of LOVE[/tpl] is the ninth single by Japanese girl group Melon Kinenbi. It was released on May 8th 2003, and its highest position on the Oricon weekly chart was #10.
