[[Qamcheqay, Ijrud]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Qamcheqay ([tpl]lang-fa|قمچقاي[/tpl], also Romanized as Qamcheqāy and Qamchaqai; also known as Kamchakay, Qamchāy, and Qamchīqāh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3079448|Qamcheqay[/tpl] is a village in Golabar Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 434, in 115 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
