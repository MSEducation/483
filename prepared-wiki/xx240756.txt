[[Yinzhou District, Tieling]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Liaoning, Tieling

Yinzhou District ([tpl]zh|s=银州区 |t=銀州區 |p=Yínzhōu Qū |l=Silver State[/tpl]) is a district of Tieling, Liaoning province, People's Republic of China.

==Administrative Divisions铁岭市-行政区划网==

There are 7 subdistricts and 1 township within the district.
Subdistricts:
The only township is Longshan Township (龙山乡)
Tieling Economic Development Zone (铁岭经济开发区)
