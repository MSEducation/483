[[Rockford Aviators]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 establishments in Illinois, Frontier League teams, Professional baseball teams in Illinois, Sports in Rockford, Illinois, Sports clubs established in 2013

The Rockford Aviators are a professional baseball team based in the Rockford, Illinois, suburb of Loves Park, in the United States. Currently, the Aviators are a member of the Frontier League. The franchise operated from 1993-2008 as the Chillicothe Paints, an original member of the Frontier League. After not playing in the 2009 and 2010 seasons, the franchise was moved to Loves Park and operated as the Rockford RiverHawks in 2011 and 2012. The team was renamed the Aviators on Jan. 27, 2013.[ref]
