[[Hindawi Publishing Corporation]]

CATEGORIES: Academic publishing companies, Publishing companies of Egypt, Publishing companies established in 1997, 1997 establishments in Egypt, Open content publishing companies, Open access (publishing)

Hindawi Publishing Corporation  is a commercial publisher of scientific, technical, and medical (STM) literature. Founded in 1997, Hindawi currently publishes more than 500 peer-reviewed scientific journals as well as, more recently, a number of scholarly monographs. [tpl]As of|2013|12[/tpl], 42 of the journals have a listed impact factor.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.hindawi.com/ai/science.citation.index.expanded/ |title=Hindawi Publishing Corporation - Science Citation Index Expanded (SCIE) |year=2013 |accessdate=2013-12-17[/tpl] The company has its headquarters in Cairo and an office in New York. The journals are published under the Creative Commons Attribution License (CC-BY).[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.hindawi.com/memberships/|title=Hindawi Publishing Corporation - Open Access Memberships|year=2013|accessdate=2013-02-01[/tpl] Hindawi was originally included on a list of publishers that "may show some characteristics of 'predatory open access publishers'",[tpl]cite web |url=http://carbon.ucdenver.edu/~jbeall/Beall's%20List%20of%20Predatory,%20Open-Access%20Publishers%202012.pdf |format=PDF |title=Beall's List of Predatory, Open-Access Publishers |year=2012 |author=Beall Jeffrey |accessdate=2012-09-18[/tpl] but has since been moved to the watch section of the list.[tpl]cite journal |doi=10.1038/495433a |title=Investigating journals: The dark side of publishing |year=2013 |last1=Butler |first1=Declan |journal=Nature |volume=495 |issue=7442 |pages=433–5 |pmid=23538810[/tpl]

==Founding==

Hindawi Publishing Corporation was founded in 1997 in Cairo by physics graduate Ahmed Hindawi and Nagwa Abdel-Mottaleb.[tpl]Cite journal |last=Shaw |first=Shelli |title=Hindawi Publishing: Catering to Open Access |journal=Information Today |date=Sep 2006 |url=http://www.hindawi.com/infotoday.pdf |accessdate=2010-11-02 |archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/5tw7p50JS |archivedate=2010-11-02 |deadurl=no[/tpl] The company's first journal was the International Journal of Mathematics and Mathematical Sciences. By 2006 Hindawi had about 220 employees, including 15 software developers, and it published journals in the physical sciences and in medical research.

==Open access==

By mid-2007, all of Hindawi's journals were open access journals.[tpl]cite web| last =Harris| first =Siân | authorlink =| coauthors =| title = Encouraging innovation| work =| publisher =Research information| date =April 2007| url =http://www.researchinformation.info/features/feature.php?feature_id=132 |format =| doi =| accessdate =2010-11-02 |archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/5twASREzB |archivedate=2010-11-02 |deadurl=no[/tpl] Hindawi was one of the first publishers to use this model exclusively. Print versions are also available, using a customary subscription model.[tpl]cite web|title=Subscription Information|url=http://www.hindawi.com/subs/|work=Hindawi Publishing Corporation|accessdate=17 December 2013|quote=All journals are open access and subscribers shall receive the print edition.[/tpl]
