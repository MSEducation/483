[[Trypophloeus populi]]

CATEGORIES: Curculionidae, Animals described in 1915

Tyrpophloeus populi is a species of bark beetle implicated in sudden aspen decline. [tpl]cite web | url = http://www.smithsonianmag.com/science-nature/Phenomena-Rocky-Aspens-200812.html?c=y&page=1 | author = Michelle Nijhuis | date = December 2008 | title = What's Killing the Aspen? | publisher = Smithsonian magazine[/tpl]
This beetle is tiny; adults measure about 2 millimeters.
