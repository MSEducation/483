[[Bolbena hottentotta]]

CATEGORIES: Bolbena, Mantodea of Africa, Animals described in 1908

[tpl]italic title[/tpl]

Bolbena hottentotta, common name hottentot mantis, is a species of praying mantis found in Angola, Kenya, and Namibia.http://insects.tamu.edu/research/collection/hallan/Arthropoda/Insects/Mantodea/Family/Iridopterygidae.txt Texas A&M University It is a tiny mantis species in which nymphs are only about [tpl]convert|2.5|mm|1[/tpl] long and adult females grow to only [tpl]convert|15|mm|1|abbr=on[/tpl] long. This species has recently been introduced to cultivation but are still very rare and are raised by only a handful of breeders.http://mantidforum.net/forums/index.php?showtopic=10331 Bolbena hottentotta the smallest species in captivityhttp://photo.net/photodb/photo?photo_id=7308796 Solygia sulcatifrons (20 cm) and Bolbena hottentotta (12 mm)
