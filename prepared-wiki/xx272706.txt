[[Mairéad Graham]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Antrim camogie players, Year of birth missing (living people)

Mairéad Graham is a camogie player, winner of a Soaring Star award in 2010 and three All Ireland Intermediate championship medals in 2001, 2003 and 2011.

==Other awards==

Four Ulster Championship, two All Ireland Purcell Cups, two Purcell All Stars, three Minor Antrim Championships and Leagues.[tpl]cite book | last = Moran | first = Mary | title = A Game of Our Own: The History of Camogie | publisher = Cumann Camógaíochta | year = 2011 | location = Dublin, Ireland | pages = 460| authorlink = Mary Moran[/tpl]
