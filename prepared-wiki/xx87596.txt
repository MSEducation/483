[[North Brighton railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1859

North Brighton railway station is located on the Sandringham line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the south-eastern Melbourne suburb of Brighton opening as Bay Street on 19 September 1859 being renamed North Brighton on 1 January 1867, Brighton on 1 December 1908 and back to North Brighton on 1 January 1920.North Brighton  Vicsig

==Platforms & services==
