[[Gmina Paczków]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Opole Voivodeship, Nysa County

Gmina Paczków is an urban-rural gmina (administrative district) in Nysa County, Opole Voivodeship, in south-western Poland, on the Czech border. Its seat is the town of Paczków, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|24|km|mi|0[/tpl] west of Nysa and [tpl]convert|71|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] west of the regional capital Opole.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|79.7|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 13,743 (out of which the population of Paczków amounts to 8,147, and the population of the rural part of the gmina is 5,596).

==Villages==

Apart from the town of Paczków, Gmina Paczków contains the villages and settlements of Dziewiętlice, Frydrychów, Gościce, Kamienica, Kozielno, Lisie Kąty, Ścibórz, Stary Paczków, Trzeboszowice, Ujeździec, Unikowice and Wilamowa.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Paczków is bordered by the gminas of Kamieniec Ząbkowicki, Otmuchów, Ziębice and Złoty Stok. It also borders the Czech Republic.
