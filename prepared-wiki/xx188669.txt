[[John Tanner House]]

CATEGORIES: National Register of Historic Places in Boone County, Kentucky, Houses in Boone County, Kentucky, Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Kentucky

The John Tanner House is a stone house located in Petersburg, Kentucky, United States, built around 1810.  It may be the oldest stone house existing in Boone County.[tpl]Citation needed|date=May 2008[/tpl]  It is located on Route 20 East.
