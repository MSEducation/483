[[Yoann de Boer]]

CATEGORIES: 1982 births, Living people, French footballers, FC Eindhoven players, FC Den Bosch players, Fortuna Sittard players, Eerste Divisie players, French expatriate footballers, Expatriate footballers in the Netherlands, French expatriates in the Netherlands, Sportspeople from Marseille

Yoann de Boer (born 27 January 1982) is a French professional footballer who plays for Dutch club Fortuna Sittard, as a midfielder. He formerly played for FC Eindhoven and FC Den Bosch.  His nickname is Chabal, which comes from the French rugby player Sebastien Chabal.
