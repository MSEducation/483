[[The Rise of Jennie Cushing]]

CATEGORIES: 1917 films, American silent feature films, Famous Players-Lasky films, Films based on plays, Films directed by Maurice Tourneur, Lost films, 1910s drama films

The Rise of Jennie Cushing is a 1917 silent film directed by Maurice Tourneur, produced by Famous Players-Lasky and distributed by Artcraft Pictures, an affiliate of Paramount Pictures. The story is from a novel, The Rise of Jennie Cushing, by Mary Watts and stars Broadway's Elsie Ferguson. The film marked Ferguson's second motion picture.
