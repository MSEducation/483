[[I've Got Two Legs]]

CATEGORIES: Monty Python songs, Year of song missing

"I've Got Two Legs" is a song by the British comedy troupe Monty Python that was composed by Terry Gilliam.Monty Python Sings CD booklet. 1989 Virgin Records It most prominently appears in the concert film Monty Python Live at the Hollywood Bowl, the concert LP Monty Python Live at Drury Lane and the album Monty Python Sings.Monty Python Sings - Monty Python : Songs, Reviews, Credits, Awards : AllMusic
At the Hollywood Bowl and Drury Lane, the song followed the Argument Sketch. Gilliam descended from the ceiling with a mandolin to perform the song.
Invariably, as he tries to begin the second verse, he is shot by another character.  On Monty Python Sings, the voice who introduces the song ("And now Mr Terry Gilliam will sing for you "I've Got Two Legs.") can be heard after the shooting saying "Thank you."
