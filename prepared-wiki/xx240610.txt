[[Yanda Nossiter]]

CATEGORIES: 1976 births, Australian female canoeists, Canoeists at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Canoeists at the 2000 Summer Olympics, Living people, Olympic canoeists of Australia

Yanda Nossiter (born 24 October 1976) is an Australian sprint canoer who competed from the mid-1990s to the early 2000s (decade). Competing in two Summer Olympics, she earned her best finish of eighth in the K-4 500 m event at Atlanta in 1996.
