[[Never Summer Wilderness]]

CATEGORIES: Wilderness Areas of Colorado, Protected areas established in 1980, Protected areas of Grand County, Colorado, Protected areas of Jackson County, Colorado, Arapaho National Forest, Routt National Forest

The Never Summer Wilderness is a U.S. Wilderness Area located immediately west of Rocky Mountain National Park in the Never Summer Mountains of Arapaho National Forest in northern Colorado. The wilderness has seven mountains over [tpl]convert|12000|ft|m[/tpl] in elevation, with the highest being Howard Mountain at [tpl]convert|12810|ft|m[/tpl][tpl]cite web |title=Never Summer Wilderness |publisher=Wilderness.net |url=http://www.wilderness.net/index.cfm?fuse=NWPS&sec=wildView&WID=406 |accessdate=August 12, 2012[/tpl][tpl]cite web |title=Never Summer Wilderness Area |publisher=Colorado Wilderness |url=http://www.coloradowilderness.com/wildpages/never.html |accessdate=August 12, 2012[/tpl]
