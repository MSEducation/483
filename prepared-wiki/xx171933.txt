[[Simone Cadamuro]]

CATEGORIES: 1976 births, Living people, People from San Donà di Piave, Italian male cyclists, Sportspeople from Veneto

Simone Cadamuro (born 28 June 1976) is an Italian professional road racing cyclist who rides for the Amica Chips-Knauf cycling team.
He is a native of San Donà di Piave.
