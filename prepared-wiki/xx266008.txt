[[Óscar Ayala]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 births, Living people, People from Mariano Roque Alonso, Paraguayan footballers, Paraguay international footballers

Óscar Ayala (born 3 April 1985) is an Paraguayan international footballer who plays for Boca Unidos, as a defender.

==Career==

Ayala has played for General Díaz, Rubio Ñu and Boca Unidos.[tpl]NFT player|pid=43372[/tpl]
He made his international debut for Paraguay in 2011.
