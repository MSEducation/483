[[Roosevelt, Lubbock County, Texas]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Lubbock County, Texas, Lubbock metropolitan area, Unincorporated communities in Texas

Roosevelt is an unincorporated community in Lubbock County, Texas, United States. It sits at an elevation of 3136 feet (956 m). The community is part of the Lubbock Metropolitan Statistical Area.
