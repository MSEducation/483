[[India at the 1976 Summer Olympics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 1976 Summer Olympics, India at the Olympics

India competed at the 1976 Summer Olympics in Montreal, Canada. This is the first Olympics since 1924 in which the Indian Men's hockey team did not win an Olympic Medal.
