[[Birmingham City Council election, 2008]]

CATEGORIES: Birmingham City Council elections, English local elections, 2008

Elections to Birmingham City Council in England were held on 1 May 2008. One third of the council was up for election and the council stayed under no overall control as it had been since 2003.[tpl]Cite web| url = http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/england/west_midlands/7379036.stm | title = Labour suffers Wolverhampton loss | accessdate = 2008-10-25 | date = 2 May 2008 | publisher = BBC Online[/tpl]
230 candidates stood in the election for the 40 seats that were contested. Five parties contested every ward in Birmingham, the Conservatives, Labour, Liberal Democrats, British National Party and the Greens.[tpl]Cite web| url = http://www.birminghampost.net/news/politics-news/2008/04/07/birmingham-city-council-election-candidates-revealed-65233-20731028/ | title = Birmingham City Council election candidates revealed | accessdate = 2008-10-25 | date = 7 April 2008 | publisher = Birmingham Post[/tpl] Overall turnout across the city was 31.2%
The results saw the Conservative party gain significantly, winning 6 seats from the Labour party and Respect gain an independent seat. The Liberal Democrats were pleased to hold all their seats.[tpl]Cite web| url = http://www.politics.co.uk/news/domestic-policy/london/mayoral-election-2008/tory-lib-dem-alliance-holds-firm-in-birmingham-$1221169.htm | title = Tory-Lib Dem alliance holds firm in Birmingham | accessdate = 2008-10-25 | date = 2 May 2008 | publisher = politics.co.uk[/tpl] As a result the Conservative and Liberal Democrat ruling coalition increased their majority to hold 75% of the seats on the council. Both Conservatives and Liberal Democrats attributed their success to below inflation council tax rises and the national unpopularity of the Labour government.[tpl]Cite web| url = http://www.birminghammail.net/news/local-elections-2008/latest-news/2008/05/02/conservatives-come-out-on-top-in-birmingham-97319-20851691/ | title = Conservatives come out on top in Birmingham | accessdate = 2008-10-25 | date = 2 May 2008 | publisher = Birmingham Mail[/tpl]
