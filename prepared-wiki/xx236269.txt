Antonio Marino (born August 9, 1988 in Mazara del Vallo) is an Italian association football defender currently playing for the Serie B club Cittadella.

==Career==

Marino started his career with amateur Eccellenza club Folgore Selinunte from Castelvetrano, until he was noticed by Udinese scout and former Italy international footballer Andrea Carnevale. He successively agreed to join the Primavera under-20 team of Udinese, also serving as team captain throughout the 2007–08 season.[tpl]cite news|publisher=Resport.it|language=Italian |accessdate=2009-10-17 |date=2009-02-12 |title=Juve Stabia, Marino l´uomo delle rimonte. Carnevale: "Ecco come l´ho scoperto" |url=http://www.resport.it/leggiarticolo2.asp?idarticolo=28762[/tpl] He then spent the 2008–09 season on loan to Lega Pro Prima Divisione club Juve Stabia, where he collected 15 first team appearances.
On July 2009 Marino was loaned out again, this time to Serie B outfit Ascoli.[tpl]cite news|publisher=TuttoMercatoWeb|language=Italian |accessdate=2009-10-17 |date=2009-07-10 |title=UFFICIALE: Blaiotta, Marino e Mattila all'Ascoli |url=http://www.tuttomercatoweb.com/?action=read&id=160408[/tpl] After making fourteen appearances in his first full season at Serie B level, his  loan deal was then extended also for the following 2010–11 season.
