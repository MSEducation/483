[[Flávio Alex Valêncio]]

CATEGORIES: 1983 births, Living people, Brazilian footballers, PFC Neftchi players

Flávio Alex Valêncio (better known just as Flavinho; born 27 July 1983) is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays for Neftchi Baku PFK in the Azerbaijan Premier League.
