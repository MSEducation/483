Mukhsin Mukhamadiev (born 21 October 1966) is a football manager and former player who currently manages the Tajikistan national team.

==Career==

Born in Dushanbe, he played club football for Pomir Dushanbe, Pakhtakor,Lokomotiv Moscow, Ankaragücü, Spartak Moscow, Lokomotiv Nizhny Novgorod, Austria Wien, Torpedo Moscow, Shinnik Yaroslavl, Buxoro, Dinamo Samarqand, Arsenal Tula and Vityaz Podolsk.[tpl]NFT player|pid=19846|accessdate=28 November 2013[/tpl] He also represented both Tajikistan and Russia at international level.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.rsssf.com/miscellaneous/double-caps.html|title=Players Appearing for Two or More Countries|accessdate=28 November 2013|publisher=RSSSF[/tpl]
