[[Billy Lynn]]

CATEGORIES: 1947 births, Living people, English footballers, People from Newcastle upon Tyne, Association football midfielders, The Football League players, Huddersfield Town F.C. players, Rotherham United F.C. players

William "Billy" Lynn (born 20 January 1947) is a former professional footballer, who played for Huddersfield Town and Rotherham United. He was born in Newcastle upon Tyne.
