Mourad Delhoum (born October, 1987 in Sétif) is an Algerian footballer who plays as central midfielder for Saudi Professional League club Al Nassr FC, on loan from ES Sétif.La Fiche de Mourad DELHOUM - Football algérien
Delhoum is the cousin of former Algerian international forward Rafik Saïfi.
