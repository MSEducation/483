[[Golojeh, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Golojeh ([tpl]lang-fa|گلجه[/tpl], also Romanized as Goljeh; also known as Golūjeh, Gūljeh, Guludzhakh, Gulujāh, and Koljeh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3064640|Golojeh[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 374, in 84 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
