[[Benghazi Zoo]]

CATEGORIES: Zoos in Libya, Buildings and structures in Benghazi

Benghazi Zoo or Al Fuwayhat  is a zoo and tourist park in the city of Benghazi, Libya. In 1991, Italian firm Cairtieri Trieste signed a $2 million contract to supply a low draught boat named Al-Berka to give tourist excursions on the lagoon inside the park.
