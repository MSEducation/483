[[Caina deletella]]

CATEGORIES: Animals described in 1893, Phycitinae

Caina deletella is a species of snout moth. It was described by Ragonot, in 1893. It is found in southern Iraq in the Persian Gulf region, Pune in India, Malta as well as the Canary Islands.[tpl]cite web|url=http://redalyc.uaemex.mx/redalyc/pdf/455/45515028002.pdf |title=New data for Pyraloidea from Fuerteventura (Canary Islands, Spain) including a species new to Science (Lepidoptera: Pyraloidea) |format=PDF |date= |accessdate=2011-09-29[/tpl]
