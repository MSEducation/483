[[Oelandia]]

CATEGORIES: Cambrian animals

Oelandia is an extinct genus of basal molluscs of the family helcionellid from the Middle Cambrian of Sweden, China and North America.[tpl]Peel 1991[/tpl]  It has a small, upwards-pointing anterior snorkel with a closed end. It bears pronounced concentric ribbing around its strongly coiled exogastric shell.
