[[Asia-Pacific Institute of Creativity]]

CATEGORIES: Universities and colleges in Taiwan

Asia-Pacific Institute of Creativity (APIC; [tpl]zh|t=亞太創意技術學院|p=Yàtài Chuàngyì Jìshù Xuéyuàn[/tpl]) is an educational college in Toufen Township, Miaoli County, Taiwan.

==History==

Founded as the Chin Min Junior College of Technology and Commerce ([tpl]zh|t=親民技術學院[/tpl]) by Jou Benchi and Chen Kuan-hua in 1988, it took its current name in 1993.
[tpl]As of|2004[/tpl] it has approximately 6,800 students and 250 administrative and teaching staff.
