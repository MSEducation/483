[[Puerto Rico Department of Corrections and Rehabilitation]]

CATEGORIES: Executive departments of the government of Puerto Rico, State corrections departments of the United States

The Department of Correction and Rehabilitation of Puerto Rico ([tpl]lang-es|Departamento de Corrección y Rehabilitación de Puerto Rico[/tpl]) is the executive department of the government of Puerto Rico responsible for structuring, developing, and coordinating the public policies of Puerto Rico over its correctional system and the rehabilitation of its adult and young population."Contáctenos."
