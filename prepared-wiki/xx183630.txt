[[Senad Kreso]]

CATEGORIES: 1955 births, Living people, Bosnia and Herzegovina footballers, Football managers in Bahrain

Senad Kreso (born 1955 in Bosnia and Herzegovina) is a Bosnian football manager and the current manager of Majees FC in the Omani League. He was a coach of FK Sarajevo (BiH), FK Olimpik Sarajevo (BiH), FK Unis (BiH), Bosnian National Team U/20, Al-Qadsia (Kuwait), Al-Merreikh (Sudan), Al-Raed (Saudi Arabia), Al-Fateh Sports Club (Saudi Arabia), Al-Ahli Club, Muharraq, Al Najma Club, Bahrain National team and Olympic team and Dhofar. 
In 2009 he signed with Omani club, Dhofar and finished the season with the club with second in the Sultan Qaboos Cup and second in the Omani League.  For the 2009-10 he was declared best coach in Omani League.  After disputes with the club management and financial delays by the club in December 2010 leaving the Dhofar Club. Season 2011-2012 ERiffa Bahrain.
