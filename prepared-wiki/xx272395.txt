[[Damlama]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Mersin Province, Mediterranean Region, Turkey, Villages in Turkey, Tarsus District

 
Damlama is a village in Tarsus district of Mersin Province, Turkey. It is on Turkish state highway [tpl]TUR-D|750[/tpl]. It is [tpl]convert|30|km[/tpl] to Tarsus and [tpl]convert|57|km[/tpl] to Mersin. The population of village is 393 as of 2011.Statistical Institute
