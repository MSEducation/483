[[Geneva National Golf Club]]

CATEGORIES: Golf clubs and courses in Wisconsin, Buildings and structures in Walworth County, Wisconsin

Geneva National Golf Club is a golf course resort in Lake Geneva, Wisconsin. It opened in 1991.http://www.golf.com/golf/courses_travel/coursefinder/course/0,28290,1280652,00.html
From 2006 to 2008, it hosted the Aurora Health Care Championship.
It has three courses, designed by Arnold Palmer, Gary Player and Lee Trevino respectively.[tpl]cite book|last=Wollenberg|first=Wendy|title=Night+Day Chicago|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=0VFQ_iBW47MC&pg=PA192|year=2007|publisher=ASDavis Media Group|isbn=978-0-9766013-6-4|page=192[/tpl]
