[[Chin Kuai-Ti]]

CATEGORIES: 1915 births, Year of death missing, Middleweight boxers, Olympic boxers of China, Boxers at the 1936 Summer Olympics, Taiwanese boxers

Chin Kuai-Ti ([tpl]zh|c=靳貴第[/tpl], born June 6, 1915, date of death unknown) was a Taiwanese boxer who competed for the Republic of China in the 1936 Summer Olympics.
In 1936 he was eliminated in the first round of the middleweight class after losing his fight to Richard Shrimpton.
