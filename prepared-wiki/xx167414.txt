[[Rudolf Marti]]

CATEGORIES: 1950 births, Bobsledders at the 1976 Winter Olympics, Bobsledders at the 1980 Winter Olympics, Living people, Swiss bobsledders, Olympic bobsledders of Switzerland, Olympic silver medalists for Switzerland, Olympic medalists in bobsleigh

Rudolf Marti (born April 7, 1950) is a Swiss bobsledder who competed from the mid-1970s to the early 1980s. Competing in two Winter Olympics, he won two silver medals  in the four-man event (1976, 1980).
Marti also won two silver medals in the four-man event at the FIBT World Championships, earning them in 1977 and 1978.
