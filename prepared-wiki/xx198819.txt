[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Itapeva]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1968, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Sorocaba, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Itapeva ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Itapevensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Itapeva in the Ecclesiastical province of Sorocaba in Brazil.
