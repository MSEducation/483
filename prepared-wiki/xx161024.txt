[[Edwards Wildman Palmer]]

CATEGORIES: Law firms established in 2005, Foreign law firms with offices in Hong Kong, Law firms based in Boston, Massachusetts

Edwards Wildman is an AmLaw 100 law firm. It was formed from the 2011 merger of Edwards Angell Palmer & Dodge and Wildman, Harrold, Allen & Dixon. Edwards Angell Palmer & Dodge had been formed by the 2005 merger of Edwards & Angell LLP and Palmer & Dodge LLP.Two Law Firms to Merge Into 520-Attorney Entity In 2008, Boston-based Edwards Angell Palmer & Dodge also merged with London-based Kendall Freeman, a 40-attorney firm with specialties in dispute resolution, litigation, and both contentious and regulatory insurance and reinsurance.   
Edwards Wildman has 15 offices in the U.S., Europe, and Asia.Latham, Wachtell, Covington, Edwards Wildman: Business of Law - Bloomberg
