[[Gilbert Car Company]]

CATEGORIES: Rolling stock manufacturers of the United States, Troy, New York

Gilbert Car Company was a railroad car builder based in Troy, New York.Mid-Continent Railway Museum. North Freedom, WI. "Gilbert, Bush & Company." Builders of Wooden Railway Cars. Accessed 2011-01-18. It began manufacturing streetcars in the late 1880s.
Founded by Orsamus Eaton (1792-1872) and Uri Gilbert (1809-1888), the company changed names several times as the partnership changed:
Following the Panic of 1893, the company stopped building rail cars in 1895.
