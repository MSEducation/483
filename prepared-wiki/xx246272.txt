[[Hector Cazenave]]

CATEGORIES: 1914 births, 1958 deaths, Uruguayan footballers, French footballers, France international footballers, Peñarol players, Defensor Sporting Club players, FC Sochaux-Montbéliard players, 1938 FIFA World Cup players

Héctor Cazenave (born 13 April 1914 in Montevideo; died 27 September 1958) was a Uruguayan- French footballer. He played for Peñarol, Defensor Sporting Club and FC Sochaux-Montbéliard, and earned 8 caps for the France national football team, and played in the 1938 FIFA World Cup.[tpl]FIFA player|47418[/tpl]
