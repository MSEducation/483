[[Jingdong Yi Autonomous County]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Pu'er City

Jingdong Yi Autonomous County (景东彝族自治县; pinyin: Jǐngdōng yízú Zìzhìxiàn) is an autonomous county under the jurisdiction of Pu'er Prefecture, Yunnan Province, China.

==Ethnic groups==

According to the Jingdong County Almanac (1994:519), ethnic Yao numbered 3,889 individuals in 1990, and lived mainly in Chaqing 岔箐http://www.ynszxc.gov.cn/villagePage/vIndex.aspx?departmentid=217095 and Dasongshu 大松树http://www.ynszxc.gov.cn/villagePage/vIndex.aspx?departmentid=217070 of Taizhong 太忠乡. Yao language speakers are found in Puya Village 普牙村, Chaqing Township 岔箐乡 (Jingdong County Ethnic Gazetteer 2012:144).http://www.ynszxc.gov.cn/villagePage/vIndex.aspx?departmentid=217633http://www.ynszxc.gov.cn/villagePage/vIndex.aspx?departmentid=217632http://www.ynszxc.gov.cn/villagePage/vIndex.aspx?departmentid=217630
The Hani of Jingdong (autonym: Kaduo 卡多) numbered 10,861 individuals as of 1990 and live primarily in Wenjing 文井, Zhehou 者后, and Huashan 花山 townships.
The Jingdong County Ethnic Gazetteer 景东县民族志 (2012:209) reports that were are about 200 ethnic Bulang in Manbeng Village 曼崩村, Dachaoshandong Town 大朝山东镇.http://www.ynszxc.gov.cn/villagePage/vIndex.aspx?departmentid=245506
