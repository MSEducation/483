[[Pune Pistons]]

CATEGORIES: Indian Badminton League team

Pune Pistons is a badminton franchise owned by Mohit Burman (Dabur Group) & Savan Daru for the Indian Badminton League. The team's home ground is Shree Shiv Chhatrapati Sports Complex in Pune. The team is captained by Ashwini Ponnappa.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.indianbadmintonleague.co/pune-pistons/ | title=Pune Pistons | publisher=IBL | accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl]
