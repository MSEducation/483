[[When You Love a Woman]]

CATEGORIES: 1996 singles, Journey (band) songs, Rock ballads, Billboard Adult Contemporary number-one singles, Songs written by Steve Perry (musician), Songs written by Jonathan Cain, Songs written by Neal Schon, Song recordings produced by Kevin Shirley, Columbia Records singles, Marin County, California, Music videos directed by Wayne Isham, 1996 songs

"When You Love a Woman" is a song by American rock band Journey. It is the third track from their 1996 album Trial by Fire and was released as the lead single from that album.
The song reached #1 on the Billboard Hot Adult Contemporary chart, where it remained for four weeks in December 1996.Hyatt, Wesley (1999). The Billboard Book of #1 Adult Contemporary Hits (Billboard Publications), page 404. It hit #12 on the U.S. Hot 100 and #57 on the Top Hot 100 Hits of 1997, as well as being a Top 10 hit on the Billboard Hot 100 Airplay chart, reaching #9. It is Journey's highest charting adult contemporary single.
"When You Love a Woman" was nominated for a Grammy Award in the category Best Pop Performance by a Duo or Group.
