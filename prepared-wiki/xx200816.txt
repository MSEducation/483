[[Heat illness]]

CATEGORIES: Emergency medicine, Effects of external causes

Heat illness or heat-related illness is a spectrum of disorders due to environmental heat exposure. It includes minor conditions such as heat cramps, heat syncope, and heat exhaustion as well as the more severe condition known as heat stroke.[tpl]cite journal |doi=10.1016/j.emc.2004.01.004 |title=Heat-related illness |year=2004 |last1=Lugo-Amador |first1=Nannette M |last2=Rothenhaus |first2=Todd |last3=Moyer |first3=Peter |journal=Emergency Medicine Clinics of North America |volume=22 |issue=2 |pages=315â€“27, viii |pmid=15163570[/tpl]
