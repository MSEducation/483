[[Bjarne Schrøen]]

CATEGORIES: 1918 births, 1991 deaths, Norwegian bobsledders, Olympic bobsledders of Norway, Bobsledders at the 1948 Winter Olympics, People from Trondheim

Bjarne Schrøen (born May 24, 1918 in Trondheim - died July 5, 1991 in Trondheimhttp://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/sc/bjarne-schroen-1.html), was a Norwegian bobsledder who competed in the late 1940s. At the 1948 Winter Olympics in St. Moritz, he finished tenth in the four-man event and 12th in the two-man event respectively.
