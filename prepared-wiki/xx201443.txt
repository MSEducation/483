[[Sérgio Luis Gardino da Silva]]

Sérgio Luis Gardino da Silva (born 9 December 1979 in Alvorada) commonly known as Serjão is a Brazilian footballer who currently plays as a striker for AEP Paphos F.C. in the Cypriot First Division.
The season 2008-2009 Serjão won the first goal scorer, the best forward and the best player awardss of Cypriot First Division with Doxa Katokopia. He was also voted in the 2008-2009 Cypriot first division championship best 11's.
Serjão was close to sign a contract with Anorthosis but at 19.05.2009 (and only in 3 hours) the chairman of AEL Limassol, Andreas Sofokleous, convinced him to come to Limassol instead.
