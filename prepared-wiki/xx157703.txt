[[Paradise/Freedom Fighters]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Belize, Toledo District

Paradise/Freedom Fighters  Football Club (previously known as Toledo Ambassadors FC) are a Belizean football team based in Toledo District, Punta Gorda. They are current member of the Premier League of Belize of the Football Federation of Belize.  
Their home stadium is Toledo Union Field, Punta Gorda.
