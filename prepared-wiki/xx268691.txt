[[Kim Ji-sung]]

CATEGORIES: 1924 births, South Korean footballers, South Korea international footballers, Association football midfielders, 1954 FIFA World Cup players, 1982 deaths, Asian Games medalists in football, Footballers at the 1954 Asian Games, Footballers at the 1958 Asian Games

Kim Ji-Sung (Hangul: 김지성, Hanja: 金智星, 7 November 1924 - 12 November 1982) was a South Korean football midfielder who played for the South Korea in the 1954 FIFA World Cup.1954 FIFA World Cup Switzerland He also played for Seoul Army Club.
