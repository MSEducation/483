[[Mayanga Maku]]

Adelard Mayanga Maku (born 31 October 1948) is a Congolese football forward who played for Zaire in the 1974 FIFA World Cup.1974 FIFA World Cup Germany He also played for AS Vita Club.
Mayanga scored three goals for Zaire at the 1972 African Cup of Nations finals.[tpl]cite web|publisher=RSSSF|author=Owsianski, Jaroslaw|title=African Nations Cup 1972 (Final Tournament) - Goal Scorers|url=http://www.rsssf.com/tables/72a-scor.html|date=16 December 2003[/tpl]
In 2006, he was selected by CAF as one of the best 200 African football players of the last 50 years.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Sport24.gr|title=Οι 200 κορυφαίοι Αφρικανοί|url=http://www.sport24.gr/football/article273152.ece?service=print|date=25 October 2006[/tpl]
