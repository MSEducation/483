[[Shettihalli]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Bangalore Urban district

Shettihalli is the Ward No. 12 of the 200 wards of BBMP Bangalore. This ward is part of Dasarahalli Assembly constituency. Shri S Muniraju (from BJP) is the MLA (2008-2013)
The village Shettihalli is part of the Ward-12. Incidentally Ward-12 is named Shettihalli Ward. The other villages of this ward are Mallasandra, Dasarahalli, Chikkasandra etc.
