[[David Redmond]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Wexford hurlers, 1987 births

David Redmond (born 1987 in Oulart, County Wexford) is an Irish sportsperson. He plays hurling with the Oulart the Ballagh club and is a member of the Wexford senior inter-county team. A versatile hurler, he has lined out in defence, midfield and attack.http://www.rte.ie/sport/gaa/championship/2009/1230/redmondd.html He was made captain of the Oulart club after the 2009 season.http://www.independent.ie/sport/hurling/new-generation-prepares-to-discard-oularts-enigma-tag-2516759.html
