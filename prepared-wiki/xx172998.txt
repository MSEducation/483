[[Planèzes]]

CATEGORIES: Planèzes, Communes of Pyrénées-Orientales

Planèzes ([tpl]lang-ca|Planeses[/tpl]) is a commune in the Pyrénées-Orientales department in southern France.

==Geography==

Planèzes is part of the Fenouillèdes.

==Toponymy==

In occitan, the name of the commune is Planeses.
