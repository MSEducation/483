[[Harand, Iran]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Isfahan County, Cities in Iran, Cities in Isfahan Province

Harand ([tpl]lang-fa|هرند[/tpl]; also known as Hājiābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|6013369[/tpl] is a city in and the capital of Jolgeh District, in Isfahan County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 6,613, in 1,856 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|10[/tpl]
