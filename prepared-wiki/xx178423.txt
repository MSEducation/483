Bertha Oliva Sánchez Rivera (born November 4, 1978) is a female long-distance runner from Colombia, who won several medals on continental level from the mid-1990s on. 

==Career==

She has won twice at the South American Cross Country Championships; first winning the long race in 2000, then winning the short race competition in 2002.Continental Cross Country Championships and Cups. GBR Athletics. Retrieved on 2010-03-02.
