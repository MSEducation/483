[[Erwin Library and Pratt House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Houses completed in 1875, Libraries in New York, Houses in Oneida County, New York

Erwin Library and Pratt House is a historic library building and house located in Boonville in Oneida County, New York. The library building was built in 1890 of rock-faced local limestone with a square tower at the entrance.  The Pratt House was constructed in 1875.  It is of brick with a foundation of smooth limestone and a mansard roof with a central tower.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=6085|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: Erwin Library and Pratt House|date=June 1973|accessdate=2010-01-08 |author=Doris Vandelipp Manley|publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation[/tpl] See also: [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=6084|title=Accompanying six photos[/tpl] 
It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1973.
