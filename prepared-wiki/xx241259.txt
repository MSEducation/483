[[Sonid Right Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Sonid Right Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠰᠥᠨᠡᠳ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠪᠠᠷᠠᠭᠤᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Söned Baraɣun qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=苏尼特右旗|t=蘇尼特右旗|p=Sūnítè Yòu Qí[/tpl]) is a banner of Inner Mongolia, China. It is under the administration of Xilin Gol League.Sunud Mongols live here.
