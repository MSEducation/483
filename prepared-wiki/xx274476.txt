[[Changzhou No.1 High School]]

CATEGORIES: Schools in Jiangsu

Changzhou No.1 High School (formerly Zhengheng Middle School) is a four-star-rated high school in the Jiangsu Province, established in 1925.http://www.czyz.com.cn/xscl/ The school is located in the center of Changzhou. The campus covers an area of 117 acres, over 40% of it wooded. There are about 2000 students, and 170 faculty and staff. Sports are a focus of the school's curriculum.
