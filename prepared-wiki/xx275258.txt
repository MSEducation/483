[[Ken Crawford (American football)]]

CATEGORIES: 1898 births, Players of American football from Ohio, Miami RedHawks football players, Akron Pros players, Cincinnati Celts players, Dayton Triangles players, Hammond Pros players, Year of death missing

Kenneth James Crawford was a professional American football player, who played in the early National Football League for the Akron Pros, Hammond Pros, Dayton Triangles and Cincinnati Celts. As a member of the 1920 Akron Pros, Crawford won the very first NFL Championship with the team. Prior to his professional career, he played at the college level at Miami University.
