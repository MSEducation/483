[[KDLT tower]]

CATEGORIES: Towers in South Dakota, Buildings and structures in Minnehaha County, South Dakota

The KDLT tower is a [tpl]Convert|609.2|m|ft[/tpl][tpl]ASR|key=104410|number=1042104[/tpl] high guy-wired aerial mast for the transmission of FM and TV programmes in Rowena, South Dakota, USA.[tpl]ASR|key=104410|number=1042104[/tpl] The KDLT tower was completed in 1998 and is owned by Red River Broadcast Co., LLC.[tpl]ASR|key=104410|number=1042104[/tpl]
Replacement of the antenna at the top of this tower was featured in an episode of World's Toughest Fixes.http://video.nationalgeographic.com/video/player/national-geographic-channel/full-episodes/worlds-toughest-fixes/ngc-2000-foot-tower.html
