[[José Carlos Chaves]]

CATEGORIES: 1958 births, Living people, Costa Rican footballers, Costa Rica international footballers, L.D. Alajuelense footballers, FK Inter Bratislava players, Expatriate footballers in Czechoslovakia, 1990 FIFA World Cup players, Costa Rican expatriates in Czechoslovakia

José Carlos Chávez Innecken (born 3 September 1958 in Atenas) is a former Costa Rican football player who played for LD Alajuelense.

==Club career==

Chaves played for Inter Bratislava in the Czechoslovak First League during the 1990-91 and 1991-92 seasons.[tpl]cite web|publisher=JFK-Fotbal|title=Počty startů hráčů v I. ==International career==

He was one of the eldest members of the national team squad, that played in the 1990 FIFA World Cup held in Italy.[tpl]FIFA player|174466|José Carlos Chaves[/tpl] The left-sided defender played his last international on 27 March 1994 against Norway.
Also, he featured in the Costa Rica squad in the 1992 FIFA Futsal World Championship.
