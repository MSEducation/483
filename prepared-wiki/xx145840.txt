[[Nellayi]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Thrissur district

Nellayi is a village situated 20 km towards south of Trichur in Kerala state, India. Its geographical coordinates are 10° 24' 0" North, 76° 18' 0" East. National Highway 47 (NH47) passes through Nellayi.
Nellayi also has a railway station and the name of the station is Pongotra. Nellayi is famous for its Church renovated in 2005.Nellayi is also famous for vyloor shiva temple,vyloor nellayi.
