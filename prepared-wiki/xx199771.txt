[[Lada Land VM-01]]

CATEGORIES: Russian civil utility aircraft 2000–2009, Russian helicopters 2000–2009, Aircraft manufactured in Russia

The VM-01 is a light helicopter from the Lada Land company in Togliatti, Russia. Design was created privately by Lada Land, but production was assisted by OKB Mil and Kamov. The aircraft contains a rotary-piston engine, four-bladed main rotor, two-bladed tail rotor, skid undercarriage, titanium alloys in the construction of the load system, and composite materials in the fuselage construction. First flight was May, 2001.
