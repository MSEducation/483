[[Construction Industry Institute]]

CATEGORIES: Non-profit organizations based in Texas

The Construction Industry Institute (CII), based at The University of Texas at Austin, is a non-profit consortium of more than 100 owner, engineering-contractor, and supplier firms from both the public and private arenas.member list The group aims to enhance the business effectiveness and sustainability of the capital facility life cycle[tpl]clarify|date=April 2013[/tpl] through research, related initiatives and industry alliances.
