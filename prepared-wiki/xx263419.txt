 '''Google Ranking'''is a link measuring algorithm, also said as Ranking in Google is the act of a website that is ranking in Google for search results.
 Google Ranking is also known as Page Rank; Page rank named after Larry Page and put to use in Google's Search Engine to determine a websites worth compared to other websites, not in currency but rank in Search Results.
 Google ranking is known as Page Rank it is know as this because in a Google Ranking makes up most of the internet search results or Page Rank. Page Rank was developed by Google founders Larry Page and Sergey Brin while students at Stanford University. In their researches Page and Brin came to the conclusions that if a search engine determines the most relevant pages according to relationships between web sites then would be more effective then other search engines of that time.
