[[Sandra Goldbacher]]

CATEGORIES: 1960 births, Living people, British Jews, Alumni of the University of Sussex, British women film directors

Sandra Goldbacher is a British film director and producer.Sandra GoldbacherSandra Goldbacher
She directed commercials for The Observer, Philips, Evian, Wella, Johnny Walker and Baileys. She also directed documentaries for the BBC series Building Sights and two documentaries on boxing for Channel Four.
Her first feature film, The Governess, starring Minnie Driver, which she also wrote,  was  nominated for a BAFTA award in 1999 for best newcomer. Her second film was Me Without You (2001), starring Anna Friel and Michelle Williams.
Both films feature Jewish characters and themes.
