Arnold Origi Otieno (born 15 November 1983) is a Kenyan football goalkeeper who currently plays for Lillestrøm in Norway.

==Career==

Origi played for Mathare United and Tusker in his home country before joining Moss, where fellow Kenyan John Muiruri was already played.
