[[Accelerationen]]

CATEGORIES: 1860 compositions, Waltzes by Johann Strauss II

Accellerationen (Accelerations), op. 234, is a waltz composed by Johann Strauss II in 1860 for the Engineering Students' Ball at the Sofienbad-Saal in Vienna.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.naxosdirect.com/title/8.550338 |title=STRAUSS II, J.: Waltzes, Polkas, Marches and Overtures |accessdate=2008-10-05|publisher=NaxosDirect[/tpl] It is one of his best-known waltzes, famous especially for its rapidly accelerating opening waltz theme.
Accelerations is featured in Erich Wolfgang Korngold's The Tales of Strauss, Op. 21 as well as many of Strauss's other well-known waltzes.
