[[Qezel Tappeh-ye Bayat]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Qezel Tappeh-ye Bayat ([tpl]lang-fa|قزل تپه بيات[/tpl], also Romanized as Qezel Tappeh-ye Bayāt and Qezel Tappeh Bayāt)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3080268|Qezel Tappeh-ye Bayat[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Bala Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 299, in 67 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
