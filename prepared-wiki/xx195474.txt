[[Le Cock Sportif]]

CATEGORIES: 2000 debut albums, Albums produced by David Kahne, Albums produced by Jason Slater

Le Cock Sportif was the first album by the rap rock/nu metal group Brougham. The cover art featured porn star Raylene.
A music video for "Murked Out" was directed by Chris Cuffaro.
