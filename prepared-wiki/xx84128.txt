[[Tenryū, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Tenryū|天龍村|Tenryū-mura[/tpl] is a village located in Shimoina District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2006, the village has an estimated population of 1,987 and a density of 18.14 persons per km². The total area is 109.53 km².
