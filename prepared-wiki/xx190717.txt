[[Uli Beckerhoff]]

CATEGORIES: German jazz composers, German jazz trumpeters, 1947 births, Living people

Ulrich (Uli) Beckerhoff (born December 6, 1947 in Münster) is a German jazz composer, trumpeter, and academic. 
He led the Uli Beckerhoff Trio with Jasper van 't Hof and John Stanley Marshall as the other two members.
