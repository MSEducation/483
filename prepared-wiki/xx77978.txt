[[Malësi e Madhe District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Albania, Shkodër County, Malësi e Madhe District

The Malësi e Madhe District ([tpl]Lang-sq|Rrethi i Malësisë së Madhe[/tpl]) is one of the thirty-six districts of Albania, part of Shkodër County. It has a population of 36,091[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.instat.gov.al/graphics/doc/tabelat/Treguesit%20Sociale/Popullsia/POP%202009/t2.xls|title=POPULLSIA SIPAS RRETHEVE, 2001-2010|publisher=Albanian Institute of Statistics|accessdate=2010-09-09[/tpl] (2010 estimate), and an area of 555 km². It is in the north of the country, and its capital is Koplik.
