==History==

The airline was established in 2002 by a group of investors and began operations in June of the same year.http://www.aeromaan.com.mx/nosotros.htm[tpl]Dead link|date=June 2011[/tpl] Aeromaan was under the direction of 2695731 Canada Inc., a Canadian group of general aviation, until March 2008, when the group went out of business due to the economic state. Aeromaan investments was transferred to K7 Investments SA de CV.
