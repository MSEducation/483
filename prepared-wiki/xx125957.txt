Robert Ray Wisdom (born September 14, 1953) is an American actor.

==Life and career==

Wisdom was born in Washington, D.C. to Jamaican parents. Wisdom is a graduate of Columbia University.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.tvguide.com/celebrities/robert-wisdom/bio/172447 |title=Robert Wisdom Biography |publisher=Tvguide.com |date=1953-09-14 |accessdate=2012-09-14[/tpl] He appeared in four of the five seasons (primarily seasons three and four) of HBO program The Wire as Howard "Bunny" Colvin. He has also starred in the 2004 films Barbershop 2: Back in Business and Ray, and the 2007 film Freedom Writers. He landed a regular role on season 3 of Prison Break playing the role of a Panamanian drug kingpin named Lechero.[ref]
Wisdom was once a producer for NPR's All Things Considered
[/ref] He also had a recurring roles in television series Supernatural, Happy Town, and Burn Notice."Robert Wisdom Cast on Burn Notice." March 18, 2010
From 2012 to 2013, Wisdom was series regular in season one of ABC drama series Nashville, as Coleman Carlisle.[tpl]cite web|author=Michael Ausiello |url=http://tvline.com/2013/09/16/nashville-season-2-powers-boothe-lamar-leaving-as-regular/ |title=‘Nashville’ Season 2: Powers Boothe Leaving as a Series Regular |publisher=TVLine |date=2013-09-16 |accessdate=2013-12-08[/tpl]
