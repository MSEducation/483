[[Ramón de la Fuente Leal]]

CATEGORIES: 1907 births, 1973 deaths, Basque footballers, Spanish footballers, Spain international footballers, 1934 FIFA World Cup players, Barakaldo CF footballers, Athletic Bilbao footballers, Atlético Madrid footballers, La Liga footballers, Spanish football managers, Deportivo de La Coruña managers

Ramón de Lafuente Leal, or de la fuente, nicknamed Lafuente (31 December 1907 – 15 September 1973)http://www.athletic-club.net/web/main.asp?a=1&b=1&c=1&d=0&jokalaria=267&idi=1 was a Spanish association footballer. He was born in Bilbao. During his career he played for Barakaldo CF (1923–1926), Athletic Bilbao (1926–1934), and Atlético de Madrid (1934–1936), and earned 8 caps for the Spain national football team, and participated in the 1934 FIFA World Cup.
He later coached Deportivo de La Coruñahttp://www.canaldeportivo.com/club/historia/entrenadores.html and died in Madrid.
