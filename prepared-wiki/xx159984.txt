[[Yvan Quénin]]

CATEGORIES: 1920 births, 2009 deaths, French basketball players, Olympic basketball players of France, Basketball players at the 1948 Summer Olympics, Olympic silver medalists for France, Olympic medalists in basketball, Chevaliers of the Ordre national du Mérite

Yvan Quénin (March 1, 1920 – July 2009) was a French basketball player who competed in the 1948 Summer Olympics.
He was part of the French basketball team, which won the silver medal.
