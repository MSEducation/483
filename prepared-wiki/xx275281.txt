Spokoyny (also known as Kutina)[tpl]cite web|title= Global Volcanism Program|url= http://www.volcano.si.edu/world/volcano.cfm?vnum=1000-671[/tpl] is a stratovolcano in the northern part of the Sredinny Range in Russia's Kamchatka Peninsula. It forms part of the Volcanoes of Kamchatka Unesco world heritage site.
