[[Poland at the Hopman Cup]]

CATEGORIES: Hopman Cup teams, Tennis in Poland, National sports teams of Poland

Poland is a nation that has competed at the Hopman Cup tournament on one occasion, in 2014. The nation reached the final in its inaugural appearance, but lost to France by two rubbers to one.France wins Hopman Cup title for the first time, beating Poland 2-1 in the final, ABC Grandstand Sport (Australian Broadcasting Corporation), 5 January 2014
