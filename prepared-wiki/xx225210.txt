Mejo Joseph is a music director from Malayalam Cinema. He made his debut in the blockbuster Malayalam movie Notebook.[tpl]cite news |title=Note Book - A definite chart topper |url=http://www.indiaglitz.com/channels/malayalam/article/32992.html |work=IndiaGlitz |date=13 January 2007 |accessdate=2008-10-08 [/tpl]

==Personal life==

Mejo is born to Joseph & Nancy on 28 January 1981 at Thrissur. Mejo is married to Merin on 19 July 2011.The couple is blessed with a son Abdiel on 31 August 2012.https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=201069029944750&set=a.169767903074863.52245.100001249631125&type=1&theater Noted director Rosshan Andrrews is his brother in law.

==Career==

Mejo made his debut in 2006 Malayalam movie Notebook which was directed by his brother-in-law Roshan Andrews. The music won him immediate attention. He also made a cameo appearance in the film as 'Firoz' a music enthusiast. Later he worked with Johny Antony for the super hit movie Cycle.[tpl]cite news |title=Mejo is back |url=http://www.indiaglitz.com/channels/malayalam/article/32992.html |work=IndiaGlitz |date=13 August 2007 |accessdate=2008-10-08 [/tpl] The music of Cycle also became superhit and Mejo got high acclaims. Mejo's latest work is the musical film Kanmazha Peyyum Munpe.
