[[Scarabeefilms]]

CATEGORIES: Companies of the Netherlands, Film companies, Dutch documentary filmmakers

Scarabeefilms B.V. is a Dutch corporation specialized in the production of creative documentaries, short films and feature films about all kind of arts, cultural and social subjects for the cinema and public broadcasting.

==Establishment==

Scarabeefilms was founded by Hetty Naaijkens-Retel Helmrich in 1989. The main office is located in Rotterdam, Netherlands. Scarabee has a second office in Orlando, Florida, USA.
