Derrick Watkins (born 15 March 1983 in Mount Isa, Queensland) is an Australian professional rugby league player for the Redcliffe Dolphins in the Queensland Rugby League. He previously played for the Brisbane Broncos and North Queensland Cowboys in the National Rugby League. 
He is eligible to play for Wales and Australia.http://www.qrl.com.au/competitions/redcliffe_player_profiles.php
