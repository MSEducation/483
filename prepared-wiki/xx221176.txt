[[Malgorzata Nowacka]]

CATEGORIES: 1979 births, Living people, Canadian choreographers

Malgorzata Nowacka (born 1979) is a Canadian contemporary choreographer. She has developed a distinct choreographic language within her Toronto based company The Chimera Project. Her work has been performed in Canada in some of the most prestigious contexts (Canada Dance Festival, Dance Victoria).
