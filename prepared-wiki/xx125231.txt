[[Striped trumpeter]]

CATEGORIES: Latridae, Animals described in 1872

Latris lineata, the Striped trumpeter, is a species of trumpeter native to the temperate oceans of the southern hemisphere.  They inhabit rocky reefs at depths of from [tpl]convert|50|to|400|m|ft[/tpl].
