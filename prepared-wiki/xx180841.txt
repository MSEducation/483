[[KFLD]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Washington (state)

KFLD (870 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a News Talk Information format.Licensed to Pasco, Washington, USA, the station serves the Tri-Cities area.  The station is currently owned by Townsquare Media and features programing from Premiere Radio Networks, Fox News Radio and Fox Sports Radio.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=KFLD |title=KFLD Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
