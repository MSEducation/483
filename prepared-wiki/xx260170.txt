[[Paul Winslow (American football)]]

CATEGORIES: People from Pasquotank County, North Carolina, Green Bay Packers players, American football running backs, North Carolina Central Eagles football players, 1938 births, Living people

Paul Winslow is a former defensive back in the National Football League. He was drafted by the Green Bay Packers in the thirteenth round of the 1960 NFL Draft and played that season with the team.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/W/WinsPa20.htm|title=Paul Winslow|publisher=Pro-Football-Reference.com|accessdate=2010-12-31[/tpl]
In the 1961 NFL Expansion Draft Paul Winslow was Selected, for the Newly created NFL frnachise, the  Minnesota Vikings .
