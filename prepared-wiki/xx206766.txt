[[James Beeston]]

CATEGORIES: 1778 births, English cricketers, English cricketers of 1787 to 1825, Middlesex cricketers, Year of death unknown

James (''aka'' John) Beeston His first name is uncertain with one source calling him James and another calling him John (born 17 September 1778 in London; date of death unknown) was an English professional cricketer who made 24 known appearances in major cricket matches from 1794 to 1808.
