[[Kimi ga Yobu, Megido no Oka de]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 video games, Bishōjo games, Role-playing video games, Eroge, Japan-exclusive video games, Windows games

[tpl]nihongo|Voices From Har Megiddo|君が呼ぶ、メギドの丘で|Kimi ga Yobu, Megido no Oka de[/tpl] is a 3D role-playing video game developed by Leaf and published by Aquaplus for the PC. It was released in Japan on December 26, 2008.
