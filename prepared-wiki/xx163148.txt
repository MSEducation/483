[[Not in Love (Enrique Iglesias song)]]

CATEGORIES: 2003 songs, 2004 singles, Billboard Hot Dance Club Songs number-one singles, Enrique Iglesias songs, Kelis songs, Music videos directed by Jake Nava, Songs written by Sheppard Solomon, Songs written by Enrique Iglesias, Songs written by Mark Taylor (producer), Songs written by Fernando Garibay, Songs written by Paul Barry (songwriter), Songs written by Victoria Horn, Song recordings produced by Mark Taylor (producer)

"Not in Love" is a pop song performed by Enrique Iglesias and Kelis. The song was written by Enrique Iglesias, Paul Barry, Mark Taylor and Fernando Garibay for Iglesias' seventh studio album, 7 (2003). The album version features Iglesias's vocals only, with the radio/single mix featuring an extra verse and chorus by Kelis. It was released as the album's second and final single in early 2004, peaking at number five in the United Kingdom.

==Music video==

The music video was directed by Jake Nava and filmed in Los Angeles, California. The video opens with Iglesias and Kelis outside a busy nightclub and Iglesias enters the club first while Kelis waits on a motorbike. Once inside, Iglesias meets up with his gang at the bar and soon makes his way to the stage where he performs the song backed by a live band. Meanwhile Kelis appears in sequences by herself or tantalizing Iglesias around various areas of the club.
