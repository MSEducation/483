[[Claude Wilkinson]]

CATEGORIES: American poets, People from Memphis, Tennessee, University of Mississippi alumni, University of Memphis alumni, Living people

Claude Wilkinson (born Memphis, Tennessee) is an American poet, and artist.

==Life==

He graduated from the University of Mississippi and University of Memphis. He was writer-in-residence at the University of Mississippi.http://www.olemiss.edu/depts/south/register/summer01/fifteen.htmhttp://www.thackermountain.com/schedule/show.php?date=1109221200
His work has appeared in Atlanta Review, Blue Mesa Review, California State Poetry Quarterly, Chattahoochee Review, The Oxford American, Poem, South Dakota Review, The Southern Review and Xavier Review. 
His paintings have been exhibited at University of Mississippi.http://olemiss.edu/depts/south/register/winter01/five.htm
He lives in Nesbit, Mississippi.http://www.pw.org/content/claude_wilkinson
