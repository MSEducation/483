[[Pigmentiphaga kullae]]

CATEGORIES: Burkholderiales

Pigmentiphaga kullae is a gram-negative, oxidase and catalase-positive, non-spore-forming, rod-shaped bacterium from the genus of Pigmentiphaga which has the ability to decolorize the azo dyes-compound 1-(4'-carboxyphenylazo)-4-naphthol aerobically and uses it as a sole source of carbon and energy.International Journal of Systematic and Evolutionary Microbiology http://ijs.sgmjournals.org/content/51/5/1867.full.pdfPubMedUniProt
