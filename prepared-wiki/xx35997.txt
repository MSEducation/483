[[Lewis V. Baldwin]]

CATEGORIES: American historians, Historians of the United States, Vanderbilt University faculty, Living people, Year of birth missing (living people)

Dr. Lewis V. Baldwin is an historian, author, and professor specializing in the history of the black churches in the United States.  He is the acknowledged expert on the Spencer Churches, the oldest black denominations in the country.  He currently teaches at Vanderbilt University.
