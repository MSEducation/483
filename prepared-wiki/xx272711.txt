[[Ploshcha Peremohy (Kiev Light Rail)]]

CATEGORIES: Kiev Light Rail stations

Ploshcha Peremohy ([tpl]lang-uk|Площа Перемоги[/tpl]) is a station on the Kiev Light Rail. Originally opened in 1978, during the reconstruction of the tramways in 2010 the station was moved to the middle Zhilyanskry Street and the previous track loops were removed.
