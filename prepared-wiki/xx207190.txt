[[Horní Hoštice (Javorník)]]

CATEGORIES: Neighbourhoods in the Czech Republic, Jeseník District

Horní Hoštice ([tpl]lang-de|Ober Gostitz[/tpl]) is a small village located in the foothills of Rychlebské Hory (Reichensteiner Gebirges in German) in the Olomouc Region in Czech Republic. Today, the village is a part of town  Javorník.

==History==

According to Statistik des Deutschen Reichs, Band 450; Teil II - Sudetendeutsche Gebiete und Memelland Horní Hoštice had 394 inhabitants in 1930 and 375 in 1939.
