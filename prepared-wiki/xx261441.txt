[[Nick Swetman]]

CATEGORIES: 1984 births, Living people, People from Penarth, People from Glamorgan, Welsh cricketers, Wales Minor Counties cricketers

Nicholas 'Nick' Taylor Swetman (born 27 September 1984) is a Welsh cricketer.  Swetman is a right-handed batsman who bowls right-arm fast-medium.  He was born in Penarth, Glamorgan.
Swetman made his debut for Wales Minor Counties in the 2002 MCCA Knockout Trophy against the Gloucestershire Cricket Board.  From 2002 to 2004, he represented the team in 4 Trophy matches, the last of which came against Berkshire.Minor Counties Trophy Matches played by Nick Swetman  His Minor Counties Championship debut came in 2003 against Cheshire.  From 2003 to 2004, he represented the team in 7 Championship matches, the last of which came against Oxfordshire.Minor Counties Championship Matches played by Nick Swetman  His only List A appearance for the team came in the 2nd round of the 2004 Cheltenham & Gloucester Trophy against Middlesex.List A Matches played by Nick Swetman  In the match he scored 4 runsList A Batting and Fielding For Each Team by Nick Swetman and took a single wicket for the cost of 41 runs from 10 overs.List A Bowling For Each Team by Nick Swetman
