[[Alain Portmann]]

CATEGORIES: 1981 births, Living people, Swiss footballers, SR Delémont players, Yverdon-Sport FC players, FC Thun players, FC Solothurn players, FC Concordia Basel players, Swiss Super League players, Association football goalkeepers

Alain Portmann (born 14 February 1981) is a Swiss football goalkeeper, who currently plays for FC Breitenrain (as of 2009/10 season).
He won promotion to Swiss Super League with SR Delémont in 2002.
