[[Walcourt]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Belgium, Municipalities of Namur (province)

Walcourt ([tpl]IPA-fr|walkuʁ[/tpl]) is a Walloon municipality and town located in Belgium in the province of Namur.
On 1 January 2006 the municipality had 17,516 inhabitants. The total area is 123.18 km², giving a population density of 142 inhabitants per km².
