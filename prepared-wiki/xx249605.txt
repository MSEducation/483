[[Solera Holdings]]

CATEGORIES: Companies based in Texas, Companies established in 1966, Companies listed on the New York Stock Exchange, 1966 establishments in the United States

Solera Holdings is an auto insurance software service provider.  It is a component of the S&P 400.
Its holdings include Audatex, the name under which it was founded in 1966, and Hollander Yard Management System.
Competitors include Mitchell International, Inc. and FICO.
