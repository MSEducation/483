[[Qameshlu, Afshar]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Qameshlu ([tpl]lang-fa|قمشلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Qameshlū; also known as Ghamesloo and Kāmeshlu)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3079455|Qameshlu[/tpl] is a village in Shivanat Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 133, in 33 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
