[[1961 Provincial Speedway League]]

CATEGORIES: Speedway Provincial League

The 1961 Provincial Speedway League was the second season of the Provincial League.[tpl]cite web | title =Championship History | work = | publisher = Poole Speedway | year = 2008 | url = http://www.poolespeedway.net/08-website/lgerecord.php | format = | doi = | accessdate = 2008-05-18 | archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20080516063211/http://www.poolespeedway.net/08-website/lgerecord.php| archivedate= 16 May 2008 | deadurl= no[/tpl] Eleven speedway teams took part. Bradford were unable to take their place in the league after the construction of their new track was delayed. Exeter, Plymouth, Wolverhampton, Middlesbrough and Newcastle all re-opened their tracks and joined the league for the start of the season. Poole were champions, finishing seven points ahead of runners up Plymouth.
