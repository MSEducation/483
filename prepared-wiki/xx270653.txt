[[Kashmir white granite]]

CATEGORIES: Granitic rocks, Building stone, Mining in Tamil Nadu

Kashmir White Granite is a white base granite with brown and black dots. These dots are called granuels and present in ever single piece of Kashmir White Granite. 
This natural stone is not a hundred percent granite technically, but in commercial use it is called Granite.
