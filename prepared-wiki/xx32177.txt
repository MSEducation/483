[[Perodicticinae]]

CATEGORIES: Lorises and galagos

Perodicticinae is the subfamily of Lorisidae that includes the four species of African primates as shown below. They have a vestigial tail and index finger. The snout is pointed and the ears and eyes are large. The coat is dense, brown and woolly.[tpl]Citation needed|date=August 2009[/tpl]
