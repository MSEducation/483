[[John E. Gallagher]]

CATEGORIES: American film directors, American television directors, Living people, Place of birth missing (living people), Year of birth missing (living people)

John E. Gallagher (sometimes credited as John Gallagher and John E. Gallagher III) is an American television director. His credits include Third Watch, ER, The Good Wife and Criminal Minds. Prior to working in television, he worked as an assistant director and production assistant on the films Friday the 13th Part VIII: Jason Takes Manhattan, Born on the Fourth of July, The Hard Way, Out for Justice, Stine Cold, Passenger 57 and other films.John E. Gallagher Movies & TV, The New York Times
