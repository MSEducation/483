[[Château de Salignac]]

CATEGORIES: Castles in Aquitaine, Official historical monuments of  France, Châteaux in Dordogne

The Château de Salignac is the former castle of the Salignac-Fénelon family in the commune of Salignac-Eyvigues  in the Dordogne département of France. It dates from the 12th-16th centuries.
It has been classified since 1969 as a monument historique by the French Ministry of Culture.Ministry of Culture listing
