[[Bathyporeia elegans]]

CATEGORIES: Gammaridea, Crustaceans of the Atlantic Ocean, Animals described in 1938

[tpl]italic title[/tpl]

Bathyporeia elegans is a species of amphipod crustacean in the genus Bathyporeia. It is unpigmented, and grows up to [tpl]convert|6|mm[/tpl] long.[tpl]cite web |author=Mark Costello & Denise Bellan-Santini |year=2011 |title=Bathyporeia elegans Watkin, 1938 |editor=J.
