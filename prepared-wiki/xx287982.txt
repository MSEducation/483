[[Mario Rottaris]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Swiss ice hockey players, 1968 births, Ice hockey players at the 1992 Winter Olympics, Olympic ice hockey players of Switzerland, HC Fribourg-Gottéron players

Mario Rottaris (born February 8, 1968) is a former Swiss professional ice hockey centre who last played for HC Fribourg-Gottéron in Switzerland's National League A.
Rottaris has participated as a member of the Swiss national team in numerous international tournaments, including the 1992 Winter Olympics.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.eliteprospects.com/player.php?player=27700|title=Eliteprospects.com - Mario Rottaris|publisher=EliteProspects.com|accessdate=2013-08-12[/tpl]
