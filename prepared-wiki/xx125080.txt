[[Paul Miller (actor)]]

CATEGORIES: 1960 births, Living people, Canadian male television actors, Male actors from Montreal

[tpl]Use dmy dates|date=October 2012[/tpl]
Paul Miller (born 30 October 1960) is a Canadian actor, best known for playing Conner Doyle on the TV series Psi Factor.
Miller graduated from Montreal's National Theatre School in 1987.
He is a lover of animals and the outdoors and plays several instruments, including the acoustic guitar. He has two sisters.
