[[The Diamond Fleece]]

CATEGORIES: Canadian television films, 1992 television films

The Diamond Fleece is a 1992 Canadian made-for-television film directed by Al Waxman and starring Ben Cross, Kate Nelligan and Brian Dennehy. The film earned Kate Nelligan the 1993 Gemini Award for Best Performance by an Actress in a Leading Role in a Dramatic Program or Mini-Series.Human Cargo, CBC.ca. Accessed April 29, 2008.
