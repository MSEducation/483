[[Chariklu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Chariklu ([tpl]lang-fa|چريكلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Charīklū; also known as Chirīklu)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3058133|Chariklu[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 85, in 21 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
