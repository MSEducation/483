[[1959 Governor General's Awards]]

CATEGORIES: Governor General's Awards, 1959 in Canada, 1959 awards

In Canada, the 1959 Governor General's Awards for Literary Merit saw a major change from previous awards.  Instead of five categories in English the awards were now presented in two categories in English and two in French.  The management of the awards was also transferred to the Canada Council.
