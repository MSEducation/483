[[Didymoplexis]]

CATEGORIES: Orchid genera, Gastrodiinae, Myco-heterotrophic orchids

Didymoplexis is a genus of 17 species of leafless, achlorophyllous orchids. Their rhizomes have aerial roots. Flowers are solitary, resupinate, and arranged into terminal or subterminal raceme. In particular, one dorsal sepal is fused with the petals forming a hoodlike structure.
