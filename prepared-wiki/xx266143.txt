[[Jaime Astrain]]

CATEGORIES: 1988 births, Living people, Sportspeople from Madrid, Spanish footballers, Madrilenian footballers, Association football defenders, Segunda División B footballers, Tercera División footballers, Córdoba CF footballers, Écija Balompié footballers, FC Cartagena footballers

Jaime Astrain Aguado (born 5 February 1988 in Madrid) is a Spanish footballer who plays for FC Cartagena on loan from Córdoba CF, as a central defender.
