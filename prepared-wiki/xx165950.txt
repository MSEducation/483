[[Live at Cobo Hall]]

CATEGORIES: 1969 live albums, Hank Williams, Jr. live albums, MGM Records live albums, English-language live albums, Polydor Records live albums

Live at Cobo Hall is an album by American country music singer and songwriter Hank Williams, Jr. The Full Title is Hank Williams, Jr Live at Cobo Hall Detroit. The Album was issued by MGM Records as number SE 4644 and later re-issued by Polydor Records as 811 902-1.
