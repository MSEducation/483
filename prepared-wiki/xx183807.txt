[[Codophila varia]]

CATEGORIES: Pentatomidae, Animals described in 1787

Codophila varia is a species of 'shield bugs' belonging to the Pentatomidae family Pentatomidae subfamily.
It is present around most of Europe.
The adults reach a body length of [tpl]convert|9|-|14|mm[/tpl] and can mainly be encountered  from June through August feeding on Asteraceae (especially on Echinops spinosus) and Apiaceae species.
