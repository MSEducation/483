[[Matanzas Beach, Illinois]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Mason County, Illinois, Unincorporated communities in Illinois

Matanzas Beach is an unincorporated community in Bath and Havana Townships, Mason County, Illinois, United States. Matanzas Beach is located on Illinois Route 78 along the southeast shore of Matanzas Lake, [tpl]convert|4|mi|km[/tpl] south-southeast of Havana.[tpl]cite map |publisher=Illinois Department of Transportation |title=Mason County General Highway Map |url=http://www.dot.state.il.us/maps/county/mason.pdf |format= |edition= |year=1984 |cartography= |scale= |series= |page= |section= |inset= |accessdate=2011-09-25 |isbn= |id= |ref= [/tpl]
