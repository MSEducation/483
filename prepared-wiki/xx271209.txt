[[Houmane Jarir]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1944 births, Moroccan footballers, Morocco international footballers, Association football forwards, Raja Casablanca players, 1970 FIFA World Cup players

Mohammed Houmane Jarir (born 30 November 1944) is a Moroccan former footballer who played as a forward. He represented the Moroccan national team in the 1970 FIFA World Cup, scoring in their opening match against West Germany.1970 FIFA World Cup Mexico He spent his club career with Raja CA Casablanca before a knee injury ended it.
