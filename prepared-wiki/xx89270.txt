[[Jesse Valenzuela]]

Jesse Valenzuela (born May 22, 1962) was the original vocalist of the alternative rock band Gin Blossoms when it was formed in 1987.[tpl]cite web|url=[tpl]Allmusic|class=artist|id=p57420/biography|pure_url=yes[/tpl]|title=Biography: Gin Blossoms|last=Erlewine|first=Stephen Thomas|publisher=AMG|accessdate=18 May 2010[/tpl] In 1988, he switched roles with the band's new guitarist, Robin Wilson. He continued to be a member until the band's breakup in 1997, and reunited with the rest of the group in 2002. In 2002, he released a solo album, "Tunes Young People Will Enjoy."  In 2004, Valenzuela collaborated with Canadian singer-songwriter Craig Northey on the album Northey Valenzuela, and they co-wrote and performed "Not A Lot Goin' On", the theme song to the Canadian sitcom ''Corner Gas.
