[[Eduard Müller (philologist)]]

CATEGORIES: 1804 births, 1875 deaths, German philologists, German writers, People from Brzeg

Eduard Müller (born in Brieg, 12 November 1804; died in Liegnitz 30 November 1875) was a teacher and philologist of Germany.

==Biography==

In 1853, he became director of the gymnasium of Liegnitz.
