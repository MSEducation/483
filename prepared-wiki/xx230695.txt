[[Henri Mbazumutima]]

CATEGORIES: 1988 births, Living people, Burundian footballers, Burundi international footballers, Association football midfielders, Burundian expatriates in Rwanda, Burundian expatriate footballers, Rayon Sport players, Vital'O F.C. players

Henri Mbazumutima (born 8 March 1988) is a Burundian midfielder who played with Vital'O F.C. in the Burundi Premier League.
