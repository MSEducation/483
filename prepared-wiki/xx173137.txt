[[WIBZ]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in South Carolina, Radio stations established in 1985

WIBZ (95.5 FM, "Z95") is a radio station broadcasting an adult hits format focusing on music from the 60's-2000's.  Licensed to Wedgefield, South Carolina, USA.  The station is currently owned by Miller Communications, Inc. .[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=WIBZ |title=WIBZ Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==History==

The station went on the air as WIBZ on 1985-02-05.[tpl]cite web|url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=55268&Callsign=WIBZ |title=WIBZ Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
In the 1970s, WIBZ was "99Z. The Rock of The Valley" delivering album and alternative rock music to the Parkersburg, West Virginia/Marietta, Ohio broadcast market.
