CATEGORIES: Constituencies of the Parliament of Ireland (pre-1801), Historic constituencies in County Kilkenny, 1541 establishments in Ireland, 1800 disestablishments in Ireland, Constituencies established in 1541, Constituencies disestablished in 1800

Thomastown was a constituency represented in the Irish House of Commons until 1800. Following the Act of Union 1800 the borough was disfranchised.
