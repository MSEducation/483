Eloy Edu Nkene (born 16 March 1985 in Ebebiyín), known as Eloy, is an Equatoguinean football defender, who plays for Villaverde in the Spanish Preferente of Madrid.

==International career==

Eloy was called for play with the Equatorial Guinea national team in the Africa Cup of Nations 2008 Qualifying match against Cameroon on 7 October 2006.http://www.lamecaderivas.com/El%20Club/NoticiasAficionado/convocatoriaeloy.html [tpl]es icon[/tpl] He was also part of the Equatoguinean team in the Mundialito de la Inmigración y la Solidaridad 2010 in Madrid, Spain.http://movistarenelmundialito.com/if_plantillas.asp
