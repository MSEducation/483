==Taxonomy==

Morrillia was named by Osborn (1924). It was assigned to Gomphotheriidae by Osborn (1924) and Shoshani and Tassy (1996).

==Fossil distribution==

Fossil distribution is reserved to Navajo County, Arizona and Briscoe County, Texas.
