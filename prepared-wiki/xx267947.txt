[[OpenCloud]]

CATEGORIES: Technology companies established in 2000, Companies based in Cambridge

OpenCloud was founded in 2000 in Wellington, New Zealand to create JAIN SLEE standards-based Java software products for the telecom service layer. OpenCloud is now incorporated in the United Kingdom as a privately held telecommunications software product company and is headquartered in Cambridge, England.
OpenCloud is a member of the Expert Group and the Specification Lead for JSR 22[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.jcp.org/en/jsr/detail?id=22 |title=JSR 22 |date=2000–2004 [/tpl] JAIN SLEE 1.0 and 240[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.jcp.org/en/jsr/summary?id=240 |title=JSR 240 |date=2004–2008 [/tpl] JAIN SLEE 1.1 within the Java Community Process. OpenCloud implements these standards in its telecom service layer products to provide products that bridge the SS7 circuit-switched and IP packet-switched telecom networks.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.analysysmason.com/About-Us/News/Insight/Service-brokers-bridging-legacy-and-next-generation-networks/ |title=Service brokers: bridging legacy and next-generation networks |date=Aug 12, 2010 [/tpl]
OpenCloud is a founder member and steering committee member of the Service Broker Forum[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.servicebrokerforum.org/ |title=Service Broker Forum |year=2009 [/tpl]
