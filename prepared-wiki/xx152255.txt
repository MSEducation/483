[[Buenavista, Michoacán]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Michoacán

Buenavista is a municipality located in the Mexican state of Michoacán. It is located at 19º12 North en 102º35’ West.  There are about 38,036 inhabitants. According to the census of 2005,[tpl]Cite web | url=http://www.inegi.gob.mx/est/contenidos/espanol/sistemas/conteo2005/localidad/iter/ | title=2005 Census | work=INEGI: Instituto Nacional de Estadística, Geografía e Informática | accessdate=2007-10-09[/tpl] there were 68 people speaking the native language.
Buenavista, Michoacan, Mexico, is better known as Buenavista, Tomatlan, Michoacan. Tomatlan comes from Nahuatl and it means "place of tomatoes."
