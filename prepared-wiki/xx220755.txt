[[Stenoptilia pterodactyla]]

Stenoptilia pterodactyla is a moth of the family Pterophoroidea. It is found in Europe, North America, Anatolia, IranNotes on the tribes Platyptiliini and Exelastini from Iran (Lepidoptera: Pterophoridae) and China.New Recorded Species of Pterophoridae from China (Lepidoptera: Pterophoridae)
The wingspan is 20–28 mm.
The larvae feed on Veronica chamaedrys.
