[[TIMCO]]

CATEGORIES: Companies based in Greensboro, North Carolina, Companies based in North Carolina, Aerospace companies

TIMCO Aviation Services, Inc. (TIMCO) provides fully integrated aircraft MRO (Maintenance, Repair and Overhaul) and interiors services for commercial and government aircraft operators.[tpl]cite news| url=http://www.bizjournals.com/triad/stories/2002/10/14/daily29.html | title=Timco buys California aircraft seat manufacturer[/tpl] The company's MRO services include complete airframe MRO for Boeing and Airbus fleets at TIMCO’s corporate complex in Greensboro, North Carolina near Piedmont Triad International Airport (GSO) as well as at facilities in Macon, Georgia and Lake City, Florida. The company also provides regional jet MRO at its dedicated facility at the Cincinnati Northern Kentucky International Airport (CVG) in Cincinnati, Ohio.  
Through its TIMCO Aerosystems division, the company provides fully integrated aircraft interiors design and certification services and manufactures a variety of interior monuments including seats, galleys and lavatories. TIMCO's FeatherWeight-branded products are designed to save operators fuel and maintenance costs through lighter weight and lower part counts.[tpl]cite web|title=FeatherWeight products|url=http://www.timco.aero/aerosystems/content/passenger-seats-formerly-brice-seating.html|work=TIMCO Aviation Services|publisher=TIMCO|accessdate=9 April 2013[/tpl] Timco Aerosystems was the result of the acquisition of Brice Manufacturing by Timco Aviation Services in 2002[tpl]cite news| url=http://www.bizjournals.com/triad/stories/2002/10/14/daily29.html | title=Timco buys California aircraft seat manufacturer[/tpl]
TIMCO is one of the largest independent commercial jet MRO service providers in the world.Creating Aviation Opportunities - www.Jsfirm.com  The company supports the world‘s leading global airlines, leasing companies, and government with comprehensive, individually tailored aircraft care services.  TIMCO runs state-of-the-art structures and composites repair operations at its locations, capable of handling advanced composite and bonded aluminum honeycomb assemblies.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.timco.aero/content/manufacturing-and-shop-support-services.html|work=TIMCO Aviation Services|accessdate=9 April 2013[/tpl]
TIMCO's maintenance operations are also supported by an advanced engine center in Oscoda, Michigan, and the company provides line maintenance operations at many airports through its TIMCO LineCare network.Official Website
