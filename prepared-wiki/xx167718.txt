[[Zion McKinney]]

CATEGORIES: 1958 births, Living people, American football wide receivers, People from Pickens County, South Carolina, South Carolina Gamecocks football players, Washington Redskins players

Zion Bailus McKinney (born February 10, 1958 in Pickens, South Carolina) is a former American football wide receiver in the National Football League for the Washington Redskins.  He played college football at the University of South Carolina.
He is now a truancy officer for fairfax county public schools
