[[Qazoqluy-e Olya]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Qazoqluy-e Olya ([tpl]lang-fa|قازقلوي عليا[/tpl], also Romanized as Qāzoqlūy-e ‘Olyā; also known as Qārqolū, Qāzeqlū, Qāzlu, Qāzoghlū-ye ‘Olyā, Qāzoqlū, and Qāzoqlū-ye ‘Olyā)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3080044|Qazoqluy-e Olya[/tpl] is a village in Shivanat Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 86, in 17 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
