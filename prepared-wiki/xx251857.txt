[[Tōbu Kanasaki Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Tochigi Prefecture, Stations of Tobu Railway, Railway stations opened in 1929, 1929 establishments in Japan

[tpl]Nihongo|Tōbu-kanasaki Station|東武金崎駅|Tōbu Kanasaki-eki[/tpl] is a railway station on the Tobu Nikko Line in Tochigi, Tochigi, Japan, operated by the private railway operator Tobu Railway.

==Lines==

Tōbu-kanasaki Station is served by the Tobu Nikko Line, and is 56.6 km from the starting point of the line at [tpl]Ja-stalink|Tōbu-Dōbutsu-Kōen[/tpl].

==Passenger statistics==

In fiscal 2011, the station was used by an average of 600 passengers daily.
