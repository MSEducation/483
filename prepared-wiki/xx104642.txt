[[Wuhan Sports Center Stadium]]

CATEGORIES: Buildings and structures in Wuhan, Football venues in China, FIFA Women's World Cup stadiums, Sports venues in Hubei

Wuhan Sports Center Stadium ([tpl]zh|s= 武汉体育中心体育场)[/tpl] or Zhuankou Stadium ([tpl]zh|s= 沌口体育场)[/tpl]) is a multi-use stadium located in Wuhan, China. Completed in 2002, it has an all-seated capacity of 54,357. Local football team Wuhan Guanggu play some high attendance matches at the stadium. It was one of the venues for the 2007 FIFA Women's World Cup.
