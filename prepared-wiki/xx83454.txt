[[Don't Blow Your Top (album)]]

CATEGORIES: 1988 albums, KMFDM albums, TVT Records albums, Wax Trax! Records albums

This article is about the album. For the single see Don't Blow Your Top (single).
Don't Blow Your Top is industrial rock group KMFDM's third album, released in 1988.
The album was recorded in Hamburg, Germany, with Adrian Sherwood and released in Germany by Cash Beat Records (Hamburg), in the UK by SkySaw Records and in the US by Wax Trax.
A remastered reissue of Dont Blow Your Top was released September 12, 2006, featuring new liner notes and photos of the band.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.mtv.com/news/articles/1540586/50-cent-fights-with-fan-at-fashion-week-event.jhtml | title=For The Record | author=MTV News Staff | publisher=MTV. ==Reception==

Andy Hinds from Allmusic said that Don't Blow Your Top "sounds a little flimsy when compared to the band's pile-driving later work", and that it sounds "like a combination of early Meat Beat Manifesto, Twitch-era Ministry, and even...the more primitive stuff of Alien Sex Fiend."
