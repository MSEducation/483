[[Jar opener]]

CATEGORIES: Kitchenware

right|thumb|A Jar Opener A Jar Opener is a kitchen device which is used to open glass jars. Although the lids of these can be removed by hand, some people prefer to use a jar opener. A traditional one will have two handles, leading up to two concentric grooved rings which can be used to fit different jars. It also sometimes has a device to open a bottle on it.
