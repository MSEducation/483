The ML-63 is a submachine gun manufactured by the Buenos Aires based Halcon corporation.

==Overview==

The ML-63 is chambered in 9mm Parabellum and is fed from a 30 round magazine. The ML-63 also came with a fixed wooden stockhttp://www.flickr.com/photos/jonasdecarvalho/5507958093/in/set-72157626242408185 and also a silenced variant.http://www.flickr.com/photos/jonasdecarvalho/5508375850/in/set-72157626242408185 A number of ML-63 submachine guns were found by the British during the Falklands War in 1982.http://world.guns.ru/smg/smg111-e.htm
