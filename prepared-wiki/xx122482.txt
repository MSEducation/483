[[Haad Khuad]]

CATEGORIES: Landforms of Thailand

Haad Khuad ([tpl]lang-en|Bottle Beach[/tpl]) is a beach on the northern portion of Ko Pha Ngan, Thailand.  It was named Bottle Beach for its protected cove and due to the shape of the cove which can be seen from the rock, which resembles the shape of a bottle. The beach is known mostly for its riotous Full Moon Party.
