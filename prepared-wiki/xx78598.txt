[[Higashikunisaki District, Ōita]]

CATEGORIES: Districts in Ōita Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Higashikunisaki|東国東郡|Higashikunisaki-gun[/tpl] is a district located in Ōita Prefecture, Japan.
As of April 2006, the district has an estimated population of 2,898. The total area is now down to 6.75 km².
There is only one village left in the district.
Until March 30, 2006, the district additionally had four towns.
