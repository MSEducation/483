[[Janette Rallison]]

CATEGORIES: 1966 births, Living people, American young adult novelists, American romantic fiction novelists

Janette Rallison (born 1966) is an American writer best known for her light romance novels for young adults. She also writes young adult science fiction/fantasy as C. J. Hill and adult romantic novels as Sierra St. James.Janette Rallison at Fantastic Fiction Rallison grew up in Pullman, Washington and later moved to Chandler, Arizona. She is married with five children.
