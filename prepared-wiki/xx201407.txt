[[Vickilyn Reynolds]]

CATEGORIES: 1955 births, African-American actresses, African-American singers, Living people, Actresses from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania

Vickilyn Reynolds (born June 2, 1955) is an American film and television actress and singer.

==Biography==

Vickilyn Reynolds was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. She first appeared on television in 1987 where she appeared on Kate & Allie, the following year (1988) she had appeared in two films, Crossing Delancey and I'm Gonna Git You Sucka. She has recently appeared on American Dreams in the episode "Beyond the Wire". She last appeared on television in 2005 where she played a talk show host in the film When Do We Eat?. Reynolds has also appeared on 227, a television show.
(2003), Vickilyn Reynolds had appeared on a television commercial for the Saturn Corporation.
