[[Buln Buln, Victoria]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Victoria (Australia), Gippsland

Buln Buln is a small town in West Gippsland, approximately 8 kilometres north of Warragul. At the 2011 census, Buln Buln had a population of 567, overwhelmingly born in Australia.[tpl]Census 2011 AUS|id=SSC20207|name=Buln Buln (State Suburb) |accessdate=9 April 2013|quick=on[/tpl]

==History==

The Post Office opened as Brandy Creek on 1 April 1873 and became Buln Buln in 1874 (closing in 1973).[ref]
