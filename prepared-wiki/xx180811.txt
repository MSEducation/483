[[KONA-FM]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Washington (state), Radio stations established in 1971

KONA-FM (105.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting an Adult Contemporary format. Licensed to Kennewick, Washington, USA, the station serves the Tri-Cities area.  The station is currently owned by Ccr-Tri Cities Iv, LLC and features programing from ABC Radio  and Premiere Radio Networks.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KONA-FM |title=KONA-FM Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
