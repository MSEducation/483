[[Randi Brooks]]

CATEGORIES: 1956 births, Actresses from New York City, American television actresses, Living people, 20th-century American actresses

Randi Brooks (born November 8, 1956) is an American actress. In 1982, Brooks was in the Dr. Hook music video for the song "Baby Makes Her Blue Jeans Talk". Brooks played the role of the aspiring, beautiful enchantress Bethel, who by using any and all means, wanted to become Queen in the television series Wizards and Warriors. The series only had 8 episodes during the 1983 season. Brooks also starred on the short-lived 1986 NBC series The Last Precinct, playing Officer Mel Brubaker. She also guest starred in 38 TV shows including Knight Rider, Designing Women, and Who's The Boss, and had small but memorable roles in the 1983 Steve Martin comedy The Man with Two Brains and the 1988 James Woods police drama Cop.
Brooks married Joseph Brazen in 1985 and is a prominent realtor in Bellevue, Washington.
