[[Cycling at the 2011 Pan American Games – Men's sprint]]

CATEGORIES: Cycling at the 2011 Pan American Games

The women's sprint competition of the cycling events at the 2011 Pan American Games was held between October 18 and 19 at the Pan American Velodrome in Guadalajara.Guadalajara 2011 sessions The defending champion is Julio César Herrera of Cuba.

==Schedule==

All times are Central Standard Time (UTC-6).

===Qualification===

===Eighth finals===
