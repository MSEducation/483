[[David M. Rosen]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, University of Illinois at Urbana–Champaign alumni, American anthropologists, Fairleigh Dickinson University faculty, Pace University School of Law alumni, People from Teaneck, New Jersey, University of Illinois alumni

David M. Rosen is an American anthropologist.  Rosen holds a J.D. from  Pace University School of Law and a  Ph.D. in anthropology from the  University of Illinois.   He is Professor of Anthropology, at Fairleigh Dickinson University.David M. Rosen, J.D., Ph.D - Professor of Anthropology, Becton College of Arts and Sciences, Fairleigh Dickinson University.
