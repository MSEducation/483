[[Wokingham Council election, 1999]]

CATEGORIES: English local elections, 1999, Wokingham Council elections

The 1999 Wokingham Council election took place on 6 May 1999 to elect members of Wokingham Unitary Council in Berkshire, England. One third of the council was up for election and the Conservative party stayed in overall control of the council.[tpl]cite web| url = http://news.bbc.co.uk/hi/english/static/vote_99/locals_99/html/constituency/300.stm | title = Wokingham | accessdate = 9 January 2010 | work = BBC News Online[/tpl] Overall turnout was 29%.[tpl]cite web| url = http://www.wokingham.gov.uk/elections/factpack/efpresults.htm | title = Election Fact-Pack 4 | accessdate = 9 January 2010 | archiveurl = http://web.archive.org/web/20010827200950/www.wokingham.gov.uk/elections/factpack/efpresults.htm | archivedate = 2001-08-27   | publisher = Wokingham District Council[/tpl]
