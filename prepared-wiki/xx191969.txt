[[Delightful Forest]]

CATEGORIES: 1972 films, Hong Kong films, 1970s action films, Mandarin-language films, Films directed by Chang Cheh

Delightful Forest is a 1972 Hong Kong film directed by Chang Cheh.HKcinemamagic.com

==Plot==

The legendary fighter Wu Song (Ti Lung) is sent to prison in Meng province after murdering his sister-in-law and her lover. There he meets with the prison officer Shih En (Tien Ching), who saved Wu from the baton punishment required for new prisoners. Wu learns that Shih's restaurant named the “Delightful Forest” has been occupied by Chiang Chung (Chu Mu) and now he seeks his revenge.
