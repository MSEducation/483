[[Wilbert Walker]]

[tpl]MedalCountry | [tpl]JAM[/tpl] [/tpl]
Wilbert Walker (born 7 January 1985) is a Jamaican triple jumper. His personal best jump is 16.47 metres (indoor), achieved in March 2006 in Boston. He also has 8.13 metres in the long jump, achieved in May 2006 in Emporia.
He finished twelfth at the 2004 World Junior Championships, won the bronze medal at the 2006 Central American and Caribbean Games and finished eighth at the 2006 Commonwealth Games. He recently achieved a new mark of 16.76 in the mens triple jump at the Jamaic national trials in June of this year, he went on to the cac games where he finished fourth with a leap of 16.65 meters.
