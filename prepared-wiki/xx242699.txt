[[Château de Tancarville]]

CATEGORIES: Castles in Upper Normandy, Seine-Maritime, 11th-century establishments in France, Official historical monuments of France, Ruins in Upper Normandy

Château de Tancarville is an 11th-century castle  on a cliff overlooking the Seine. It is located near Tancarville in Seine-Maritime, Normandy.
It has been classified as a Monument historique since 1862French Ministry of Culture database entry for Château de Tancarville [tpl]fr icon[/tpl] by the French Ministry of Culture.
