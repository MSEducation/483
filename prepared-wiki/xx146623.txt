[[First Kiss (Aya Matsuura album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2001 debut albums, Aya Matsuura albums, Zetima albums

This article is about the Japanese pop album. For other uses, see First Kiss (disambiguation).

[tpl]nihongo|First Kiss|ファーストKiss[/tpl] is the first album of the J-pop singer Aya Matsuura, a Hello! Project solo artist, containing her first four singles. It was released on January 1, 2002 and sold 293,540 copies.

==Track listing==

All lyrics are written by Tsunku.
