[[(15235) 1988 DA5]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1988, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(15235) 1988 DA5 is a main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on February 25, 1988.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
