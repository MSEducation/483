[[Gjirokastër District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Albania, Gjirokastër County

The District of Gjirokastër ([tpl]sq|Rrethi i Gjirokastrës[/tpl]) is one of the thirty-six districts of Albania. It has a population of 56,720[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.instat.gov.al/graphics/doc/tabelat/Treguesit%20Sociale/Popullsia/POP%202009/t2.xls|title=POPULLSIA SIPAS RRETHEVE, 2001-2010|publisher=Albanian Institute of Statistics|accessdate=2010-09-09[/tpl] (2010 estimate) containing a large Greek minority,The Albanians, a scattered people, by Philippe Rekacewicz, Le Monde diplomatique, January 1999[tpl]cite web |url=http://countrystudies.us/albania/49.htm|title =Country Studies US: Greeks and Other Minorities|accessdate=September 6, 2006[/tpl] and an area of 1,137 km². It is in the south of the country, and its capital is Gjirokastër. Together with the Përmet District and the Tepelenë District, it forms the Gjirokastër County.
