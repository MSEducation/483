[[Wandt v. Hearst's Chicago American]]

CATEGORIES: United States intellectual property case law, Wisconsin state case law, 1906 in United States case law

Wandt v. Hearst's Chicago American (1906) 129 Wis. 419, 109 NW 70 
This case is authority that: where a person's picture is taken by a photographer, there is a breach of an implied contract or relationship of trust when the photographer makes additional pictures and uses them for commercial purposes.http://epublications.marquette.edu/cgi/viewcontent.cgi?article=4161&context=mulr
