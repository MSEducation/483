[[Metro Doctores]]

CATEGORIES: Mexico City metro stations, Railway stations opened in 1994

Metro Doctores is a station on Line 8 of the Mexico City Metro.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.metro.df.gob.mx/red/estacion.html?id=103|title=Doctores|language=Spanish|accessdate=31 July 2011[/tpl][tpl]Cite web|last=Archambault|first=Richard|url=http://mexicometro.org/metro/line8/doctores/|title=Doctores »  Mexico City Metro System|accessdate=31 July 2011[/tpl][tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.mexicocity.gob.mx/detalle.php?id_pat=6548|title=ESTACIÓN DOCTORES|language=Spanish|accessdate=31 July 2011[/tpl]
Its logo is the silhouette of two doctors. The station is named after Colonia Doctores, the neighborhood in which it is located, whose streets commemorate famous doctors of the era of La Reforma.
On 7 March 2007 a 30 year old woman committed suicide at the station by throwing herself onto the tracks.[tpl]Cite news|last=Alejandro Medellín|first=Jorge|url=http://www.eluniversal.com.mx/notas/410956.html|title=Se arroja mujer a vías del Metro en la estación Doctores|work=El Universal|language=Spanish|date=7 March 2007|accessdate=31 July 2011[/tpl]  Service on the line was suspended for 20 minutes.
