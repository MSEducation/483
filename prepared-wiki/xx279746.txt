S.S.D. Correggese Calcio 1948 is an Italian football club based in Correggio, Emilia-Romagna. Currently it plays in Italy's Serie D.

==History==

===Foundation===

The club was founded in 1948.http://www.correggese.it/

===Serie D===

In the 2012-13 season 2012 the team was promoted for the first time, from Eccellenza Emilia-Romagna/A to Serie D thanks to the repechage.

==Colors and badge==

The team's color is red.
