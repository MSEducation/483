[[Ron Herrell]]

CATEGORIES: Members of the Indiana House of Representatives, 1948 births, Living people, People from Kokomo, Indiana, Indiana Democrats

Ronald D. "Ron" Herrell (born December 1, 1948) was a Democratic member of the Indiana House of Representatives, representing the 30th District from 2006 through 2010. He previously served from 1998 through 2004.
