[[Guillaume de la Sudrie]]

CATEGORIES: French cardinals, Cardinal-bishops of Ostia, Bishops of Marseille, French Dominicans, 1373 deaths, Year of birth unknown

Guillaume de la Sudrie (la Sudré) (died 18 April 1373) was a French Dominican and Cardinal, born Laguenne, Corrèze. He was bishop of Marseille, from 1361.
He was made cardinal on 18 September 1366 by Pope Urban V. He was Bishop of Ostia from 1367 to 1373.
