[[Cossacks of the Kuban]]

CATEGORIES: 1949 films, Soviet films, Cossacks, Agriculture in the Soviet Union, Films directed by Ivan Pyryev

The Cossacks of the Kuban ([tpl]Lang-ru|Кубанские казаки[/tpl]) from Mosfilm (1949) is a color film, glorifying the life of the farmers in the kolkhoz of the Soviet Union's Kuban region, directed by Ivan Pyryev and starring Marina Ladynina, his wife at that time.Cossacks of the Kuban (Shown in Paris, 2009)
