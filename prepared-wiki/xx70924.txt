[[Jet Ski]]

CATEGORIES: 1972 introductions, Personal water craft

Jet Ski is the brand name of a personal watercraft manufactured by Kawasaki. It was the "first commercially successful" personal watercraft in America, having been released in 1972.[tpl]cite book|title=Motorized Obsessions: Life, Liberty, and the Small-Bore Engine | author=Josephson, Paul R. | year=2007| isbn=9780801886416 |publisher=JHU Press |page=150|  url=http://books.google.com/books?id=MAPeAx-ZjzoC&lpg=PA150&ots=9jNsViZ0B3&pg=PA150#v=onepage&q&f=false |accessdate=2012-08-24[/tpl] The term is sometimes used to refer to any type of personal watercraft.

==Common noun==

Though the proper noun "Jet Ski" is a registered trademark of Kawasaki, the common noun "jet ski" refers to small recreational watercraft.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/jet%20ski |work=Merriam Webster Dictionary  |chapter=jet ski| accessdate=2013-05-04[/tpl] The Oxford English Dictionary records the term in use in 1961 to describe the aquatic motorbike, and in 1948 to designate a jet-propelled aircraft equipped with skis.[tpl]cite web| url= http://www.oed.com/?authRejection=true&url=%2Fsearch%3FsearchType%3Ddictionary%26q%3Djet%2520ski |chapter=jet ski |work= Oxford English Dictionary|accessdate=2013-05-04[/tpl]
