Love At Risk (simplified Chinese: 爱情风险) is a Singaporean television drama series. It stars Tay Ping Hui, Joanne Peh, Kate Pang, Zhang Yao Dong, Xiang Yun and Shane Pow as the main characters in the story. The series tells the story of how two individuals fall for each other despite their belief that love is the worst gamble one can take in life. 
It was broadcast on MediaCorp Channel 8 from 5 August 2013 to 30 August 2013,[tpl]cite web |url=http://entertainment.xin.msn.com/zh/tv/channel-8/programmes/%E3%80%8A%E7%88%B1%E6%83%85%E9%A3%8E%E9%99%A9%E3%80%8Blove-at-risk |title=Love at Risk |date= |accessdate= 2013-08-13 |work=Xinmsn |language=Chinese[/tpl] and a total of 20 episodes were aired during this period.
