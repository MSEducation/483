[[KOLU]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Washington (state), Radio stations established in 1966

KOLU (90.1 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Christian radio format.Licensed to Pasco, Washington, USA.  The station is currently owned by Riverview Baptist Christian Schools.
