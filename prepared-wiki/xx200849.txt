[[WRNK-LP]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Alabama, Low-power FM radio stations in Alabama, Religious radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 2004, Chambers County, Alabama

WRNK-LP (96.3 FM, "96.3 Keeper FM") is a radio station licensed to serve Lanett, Alabama.  The station is owned by Contact Ministry Center. It airs a Religious radio format as a ministry extension of Contact Ministries Center and Pastor John Eldridge.[tpl]cite web |work=Alabama Broadcast Media Page |title=Alabama LPFM & Translators |url=http://bellsouthpwp2.net/c/r/crackedlcd/almediapage/lpfm.html |accessdate=2008-05-21[/tpl][tpl]cite web |title=Station Information Profile |work=Arbitron |url=http://www.arbitron.com/[/tpl]
The station was assigned the WRNK-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on December 9, 2002.[tpl]cite web |title=Call Sign History |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=133833&Callsign=WRNK-LP |work=FCC Media Bureau CDBS Public Access Database[/tpl]
WRNK-LP was a recipient of the John Allen Smith Outstanding Volunteer Award for 2005.[tpl]cite web |work=WRNK.org |title=WRNK Honors & Awards |url= http://wrnk.org/awards/[/tpl]
