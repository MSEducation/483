[[Turkish Basketball Championship]]

CATEGORIES: Basketball leagues in Turkey, Turkish Basketball League, 1946 establishments in Turkey, 1967 disestablishments in Turkey

The Turkish Basketball Championship (Turkish: Türkiye Basketbol Şampiyonası) is a defunct basketball league competition in Turkish basketball, ran by the Turkish Basketball Federation from 1946 to 1967.
