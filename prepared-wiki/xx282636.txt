[[Deer Chase]]

CATEGORIES: Plantations in Virginia, Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Virginia, Houses completed in 1750, Houses in Middlesex County, Virginia, National Register of Historic Places in Middlesex County, Virginia

Deer Chase is a historic plantation home located near Saluda, Middlesex County, Virginia.  It was constructed about 1750, and is a 1 1/2-story, three-bay, brick dwelling with a clipped gable roof.  The interior has a central hall plan.  Also on the property is a contributing three-bay frame school house.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.dhr.virginia.gov/registers/Counties/Middlesex/059-0017_Deer_Chase_1973_Final_Nomination.pdf|title=National Register of Historic Places Inventory/Nomination: Deer Chase |author=Virginia Historic Landmarks Commission staff| date=June 1973|publisher=Virginia Department of Historic Resources[/tpl] and Accompanying photo
It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1973.
