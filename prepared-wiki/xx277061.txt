[[DCCI Tower]]

CATEGORIES: Skyscrapers in Bangladesh, Buildings and structures in Dhaka

Dhaka Chamber of Commerce & Industries Building ( also known as the DCCI Tower) is a 36 story, [tpl]convert|133|m|ft|adj=on[/tpl] supertall skyscraper that began construction in the city of Dhaka, Bangladesh, in 2013.[tpl]cite web|title=Dhaka Chamber of Commerce and Industry|url=http://www.dcci.org.bd/standing_committee_2012/Import%20Policy,%20Import,%20Indenting,%20Tariff%20and%20Trade%20Facilitation-2013.pdf|accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=DCCI to create 2,000 new young entrepreneurs|url=http://www.newstoday.com.bd/index.php?option=details&news_id=2352511&date=2013-07-31|accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl]

==Planning==

Dhaka Chamber of Commerce & Industry first proposed the skyscrapper in 2012 and the project was approved by Rajuk(Capital Development Authority) in 2013.[tpl]cite web|title=DCCI aiming to groom young entrepreneurs|url=http://www.bangladeshinfo.com/education/dcci-aiming-tp-groom-young-entrepreneurs/|accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=DCCI offers opportunities for updating training courses on supply chain management|url=http://www.thefinancialexpress-bd.com/more.php?news_id=132994&date=2012-06-14|accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl]
