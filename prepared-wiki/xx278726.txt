[[Qarah Tappeh, Qareh Poshtelu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Qarah Tappeh ([tpl]lang-fa|قره تپه[/tpl], also Romanized as Qareh Tappeh; also known as Kara-Tapa, Qaratepe, and Qār Tappeh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3079775|Qarah Tappeh[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 648, in 136 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
