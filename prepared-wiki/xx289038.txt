[[Baghlujeh, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Baghlujeh ([tpl]lang-fa|باغلوجه[/tpl], also Romanized as Bāghlūjeh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3054697|Baghlujeh[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 125, in 25 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
