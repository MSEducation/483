[[Takanabe, Miyazaki]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Miyazaki Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Takanabe|高鍋町|Takanabe-chō[/tpl] is a town located in Koyu District, Miyazaki Prefecture, Japan.
In 2003, the town had an estimated population of 22,613 and a density of 514.87 persons per km². The total area is 43.92 km².
