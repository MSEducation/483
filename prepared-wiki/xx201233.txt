[[Jungfernstieg station]]

CATEGORIES: , Hamburg S-Bahn stations, Hamburg U-Bahn stations, Railway stations in Hamburg, Railway stations opened in 1931, Buildings and structures in Hamburg-Mitte

Jungfernstieg is an underground railway stationName, station code and category: Liste Bahnhofskategorie 2008, DB Station&Service AG, Köthener Straße 2, 10963 Berlin (2008) [tpl]de icon[/tpl] in the city centre of Hamburg, Germany, serviced by the underground railway (U-Bahn) and the City S-Bahn line of the suburban railway (S-Bahn). The station is located in the Hamburg-Mitte borough under the artificial lake Binnenalster and named after a street of the same name. 

==History==

On [tpl]dts|format=dmy|1931|3|25[/tpl], the underground railway line Kellinghusenstr–Jungfernstieg — now part of the U1 — was opened. In 1958, the underground platforms between the Circle Line's Rathaus station (now U3) and Jungfernstieg station were connected. In 1973, the diameter line U2 was completed between Gänsemarkt and Hauptbahnhof Nord, and on [tpl]dts|format=dmy|1975|6|1[/tpl], the Hamburg S-Bahn opened the first section of their so-called "City-S-Bahn" between Hauptbahnhof and Landungsbrücken.

==Layout==

It is an underground station with two island platforms and two side platforms on three different levels. The rapid transit trains of the Hamburg S-Bahn system serve one island platform, the other island platform is served by the Hamburg U-Bahn line U1, and the line U2 serves the two side platforms. The U3 Rathaus underground station is connected with a pedestrian underpass.[tpl]citation |title=Tunnelplan |url=http://www.nimmbus.de/software/TunnelUSJungfernstieg.pdf |language=German |accessdate=2009-03-26 |year=2007 |publisher=www.nimmbus.de [/tpl] Toilets and shops are located at the station, but the station is not accessible for handicapped persons.[tpl]citation |title=DB-Konzern Jungfernstieg |url=http://www.deutschebahn.com/site/bahn/de/reisen/service/bfssuchefilter,variant=details,recordId=3068.html?c252936=Jungfernstieg |language=German |accessdate=2009-03-26 |year=2009 |publisher=Deutsche Bahn[/tpl]

==Services==

No personnel is attending the station, but there are ticket machines, CCTV, and SOS / information telephones.

===Trains===

The lines S1, S2 and S3 of the Hamburg S-Bahn and the lines U1 and U2 of the Hamburg U-Bahn serve the Jungfernstieg station.[tpl]citation |title=Rapid Transit/Regional Rail |url=http://www.hvv.de/pdf/fahrplaene/usar/hvv_usar_regio.pdf |accessdate=2009-03-26 |publisher=Hamburger Verkehrsverbund |date=2008-12-14[/tpl]
It is planned to open the new U-Bahn line U4 to the HafenCity quarter in 2011. It will serve the platforms at the deepest level side by side with U2 line.

===Buses===

Several bus routes call at bus stops on the streets above the station.
