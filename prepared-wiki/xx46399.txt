[[Welkenraedt]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Liège (province)

Welkenraedt ([tpl]IPA-fr|wɛlkɛnʁat[/tpl]) is a Walloon municipality located in the province of Liège, Belgium. 
On January 1, 2006, Welkenraedt had a total population of 9,163. 
The total area is 24.47 km² which gives a population density of 374 inhabitants per km².
The town and former municipality of Henri-Chapelle contains the Henri-Chapelle American Cemetery and Memorial which contains the graves of 7,992 members of the American military who died in World War II.
