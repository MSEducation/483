[[Sophia Rossi]]

CATEGORIES: 1977 births, American female pornographic film actors, Living people, People from Las Vegas, Nevada, Pornographic film actors from Nevada

Sophia Rossi (born September 22, 1977) is an American pornographic actress, model and dancer.

==Life and career==

Rossi was born in Las Vegas, Nevada. She appeared in the October 2005 edition of Penthouse and was the "Hustler Honey" in the December 2005 edition of Hustler.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.avn.com/articles/240621.html|title=Rossi Turns Double Play|accessdate=2007-07-04|author=Wade Garrett|date=2005-09-19|publisher=AVN |archiveurl = http://web.archive.org/web/20071004201107/http://www.avn.com/articles/240621.html |archivedate = 2007-10-04[/tpl]
In 2006, it was reported that the Secret Service detail of former president Bill Clinton, who was golfing nearby, had interrupted a fireworks display of Rossi's then-five year old daughter Savanna. 
She appeared in mainstream movie Bachelor Party Vegas.
In May 2007, she was released by Club Jenna after only five movies had been released.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.avn.com/articles/287993.html|title=Four Starlets Exit Club Jenna|accessdate=2007-07-04|author=David Sullivan|date=2007-05-02|publisher=AVN |archiveurl = http://web.archive.org/web/20070603185725/http://www.avn.com/articles/287993.html  |archivedate = 2007-06-03[/tpl]
