[[Peter Morley]]

CATEGORIES: English football chairmen and investors, Crystal Palace F.C. directors and chairmen, Commanders of the Order of the British Empire, Year of birth missing, 2013 deaths, Place of birth missing, Alumni of the University of Cambridge

Peter Morley, CBE was the President of Crystal Palace Football Club, an English football team.
After Chairman Mark Goldberg fell into financial ruin in 1999, and the club into administration, Morley was appointed temporary chairman.[tpl]cite news|title=Football: Goldberg resigns at Palace|url=http://www.independent.co.uk/sport/football-goldberg-resigns-at-palace-1112688.html|accessdate=4 February 2011|newspaper=The Independent|date=14 August 1999|author=Tommy Stanniforth[/tpl]  He stayed in this role until the summer of 2000, when Simon Jordan took control of the club.
On 15 September 2013 Crystal Palace announced that Morley had died.http://www.cpfc.co.uk/news/article/peter-morley-cbe-1055700.aspx
