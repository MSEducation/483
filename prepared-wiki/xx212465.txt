Oleksandr Chyzhov ([tpl]lang-uk|Олександр Олександрович Чижов[/tpl]; born 10 August 1986 in Poltava, Ukrainian SSR) is a Ukrainian football defender for FC Illichivets Mariupol in the Ukrainian Premier League.
