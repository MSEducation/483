[[Andorran political reform referendum, 1977]]

CATEGORIES: 1977 referendums, 1977 in Andorra, Referendums in Andorra

A referendum on political reforms was held in Andorra on 28 October 1977.Andorra, 28 October 1977: Political reforms Direct Democracy [tpl]de icon[/tpl] Voters were presented with six options, but none received a majority and over 30% of votes cast were left blank. A second referendum on political reform was held the following year.
