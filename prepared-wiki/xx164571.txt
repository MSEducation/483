[[1946 Philadelphia Athletics season]]

CATEGORIES: Oakland Athletics seasons, 1946 Major League Baseball season

The 1946 Philadelphia Athletics season involved the A's finishing 8th in the American League with a record of 49 wins and 105 losses.

==Regular season==

Buddy Rosar led the American League in assists and set the record for errorless games by a catcher, posting a 1.000 fielding percentage in 117 games.Buddy Rosar at Baseball ReferenceBuddy Rosar at The Encyclopedia of Catchers
