[[Cobalt Lake]]

CATEGORIES: Lakes of Glacier National Park (U.S.), Lakes of Glacier County, Montana

Cobalt Lake is located in Glacier National Park, in the U. S. state of Montana. The lake is just east of the Continental Divide in the Two Medicine region of Glacier National Park.[tpl]cite web| title =Mount Rockwell, MT|publisher =TopoQuest| url =http://www.topoquest.com/map.php?lat=48.43441&lon=-113.42621&datum=nad83&zoom=4| format =Map (USGS Quad)| accessdate = 2010-12-31[/tpl]  Cobalt Lake is a [tpl]convert|5.7|mi|km[/tpl] hike from the Two Medicine Store.[tpl]Cite web| title = Day Hikes | work = Plan Your Visit | publisher = National Park Service | url = http://www.nps.gov/glac/planyourvisit/upload/dayhikes_inside_09.pdf | format = pdf | accessdate = 2011-01-22[/tpl]
