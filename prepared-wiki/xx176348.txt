[[Illinois City, Illinois]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Rock Island County, Illinois, Unincorporated communities in Illinois

Illinois City is an unincorporated community in the U.S. state of Illinois, across the Mississippi River from Muscatine, Iowa.[tpl]cite gnis|410764|Illinois City, Illinois[/tpl] It straddles Buffalo Prairie Township and Drury Township in Rock Island County, Illinois.[tpl]cite book | url = https://archive.org/stream/historicrockisla00rock#page/n125/mode/2up | title = Historic Rock Island County: history of the settlement of Rock Island County from the earliest known period to the present time | publisher = Kramer & Company | location = Rock Island, Illinois | page = 113 | year = 1908 | lccn = 20-16031 [/tpl]
[tpl]As of|2014[/tpl], a United States Post Office, ZIP Code 61259,[tpl]GR|7[/tpl] remains open here at 23828 124th Avenue West.
