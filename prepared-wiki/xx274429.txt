[[Juan Sheridan]]

CATEGORIES: 1925 births, 1969 deaths, Canadian players of Canadian football, Grey Cup champions, Montreal Alouettes players, Ontario Rugby Football Union players, St. Hyacinthe-Donnacona Navy football players, Toronto Balmy Beach Beachers players

Juan Sheridan (February 2, 1925 - October 7, 1969) was a Grey Cup champion Canadian Football League player.
Sheridan won his first Grey Cup with Montreal St. Hyacinthe-Donnacona Navy team in 1944 (when league play was suspended during World War II.) After three seasons in the Ontario Rugby Football Union with the Toronto Indians and Toronto Balmy Beach Beachers, he began a nine-year career with the Montreal Alouettes. Over the course of 105 regular season games he scored only 1 touchdown, but his football fame came as a member of the Als first Grey Cup winning team in 1949.http://www.cflapedia.com/Players/s/sheridan_juan.htm CFLAPEDIA entry - Juan Sheridan
