[[Working Women's Union]]

CATEGORIES: Women's wings of political parties, Organizations based in Romania

The Working Women's Union ([tpl]lang-ro|Uniunea femeilor muncitoare[/tpl], abbreviated UFM) was a women's organization in interbellum Romania, and the women's wing of the Social Democratic Party of Romania.Kowalski, Werner.
