[[Karel Petrů]]

CATEGORIES: 1891 births, 1949 deaths, Czech football managers, Czechoslovak football managers, 1934 FIFA World Cup managers, Czechoslovakia national football team managers

Karel Petrů (24 January 1891 in Březové Hory - 1949) was the coach of the Czechoslovakia national football team when they finished second in the 1934 FIFA World Cup.
