[[Saidabad Rural District (Zanjan Province)]]

CATEGORIES: Rural Districts of Zanjan Province, Ijrud County

Saidabad Rural District ([tpl]lang-fa|دهستان سعيدآباد[/tpl]) is a rural district (dehestan) in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 3,706, in 1,064 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl] The rural district has 7 villages.
