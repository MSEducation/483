[[Srdcia dievčat]]

CATEGORIES: 1982 singles, Marika Gombitová songs, Songs written by Kamil Peteraj

"Srdcia dievčat" ([tpl]lang-en|Girlie Hearts[/tpl]) is a song by the female singer Marika Gombitová released on OPUS in 1982.[tpl]harvnb|Graclík|Nekvapil|2008|p=393.[/tpl]
The composition was written by Ján Lauko and Kamil Peteraj, and issued as the pilot single of the singer's studio album Slnečný kalendár (1982).[tpl]harvnb|Graclík|Nekvapil|2008|p=395.[/tpl]
Most recently, the single was for the first released in digital format, on the singer's second album re-release entitled Môj malý príbeh: Komplet 2. B-side of the single featured track"Slnečný kalendár".For Marika Gombitová's discography, see [tpl]harvnb|Lehotský|2008a|pp=54–56.[/tpl].
