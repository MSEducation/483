[[1992 European Figure Skating Championships]]

CATEGORIES: 1992 in Swiss sport, 1992 in figure skating, European Figure Skating Championships by year, Figure skating in Switzerland

The 1992 European Figure Skating Championships were held in Lausanne, Switzerland. Senior-level figure skaters from European ISU member nations competed in the disciplines of men's singles, ladies' singles, pair skating, and ice dancing.
