[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Sobral]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1915, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Fortaleza, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Sobral ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Sobralensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Sobral in the Ecclesiastical province of Fortaleza in Brazil.
