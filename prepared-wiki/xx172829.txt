[[Stanhope Football Club]]

CATEGORIES: Kyabram & District Football League clubs, Australian rules football clubs in Victoria (Australia)

The Stanhope Football Club is based in the small Victorian town of Stanhope, near Kyabram. The Club are known as the 'Lions' wearing a maroon jumper with gold chevron. They are the reigning Kyabram & District Football League Premiers, after defeating Ardmona Football Club in the  2007 and 2008 grand finals.
