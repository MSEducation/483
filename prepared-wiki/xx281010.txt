[[One Chance (film)]]

CATEGORIES: 2010s comedy-drama films, 2013 films, British films, British comedy-drama films, British biographical films, English-language films, Biographical films about musicians, Films about opera, Paul Potts, Films set in Wales, Films set in Venice, Films shot in Venice, Films directed by David Frankel

One Chance is a 2013 British comedy film about Britain's Got Talent winner Paul Potts,[tpl]cite web |url=http://metro.co.uk/2013/07/12/james-corden-is-britains-got-talent-winner-paul-potts-in-first-image-from-one-chance-3880793/ |title=James Corden is Britain's Got Talent winner Paul Potts in first image from One Chance |accessdate=9 August 2013 |work=Metro[/tpl] directed by David Frankel and written by Justin Zackham. It was screened in the Special Presentation section at the 2013 Toronto International Film Festival.[tpl]cite web |url=http://tiff.net/filmsandschedules/festival/2013/onechance |title=One Chance |accessdate=9 August 2013 |work=TIFF[/tpl] The film was shot in Italy and the United Kingdom.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.screendaily.com/news/filming-wraps-on-david-frankels-one-chance/5049631.article |title=Filming wraps on David Frankel's One Chance |accessdate=2 September 2013 |work=Screen Daily[/tpl][tpl]cite web |url=http://www.digitalspy.co.uk/movies/s107/britains-got-talent/news/a442534/paul-potts-biopic-one-chance-filming-wraps.html |title=Paul Potts biopic 'One Chance' filming wraps |accessdate=2 September 2013 |work=Digital Spy[/tpl] The soundtrack is scored by Theodore Shapiro and was nominated for a Golden Globe Award for Best Original Song - motion picture. The reception is generally positive with a score of 68-70% (critics and audience) on the web site Rotten Tomatoes.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/one_chance/ Rotten Tomatoes |title=One Chance |accessdate=15 March 2014 |work=Rotten Tomatoes[/tpl]
