[[Suksun]]

CATEGORIES: Urban-type settlements in Perm Krai, Krasnoufimsky Uyezd

Suksun ([tpl]lang-ru|Суксун[/tpl]) is an urban locality (a work settlement) and the administrative center of Suksunsky District of Perm Krai, Russia, located on the left bank of the Sylva River (a tributary of the Chusovaya River), [tpl]convert|150|km|sp=us[/tpl] from the city of Perm. Population: [tpl]ru-census|p2010=8,022|p2002=8,495|p1989=8,882[/tpl]

==Etymology==

The name is of Turkic origin and means cold water.Encyclopedia of Perm Krai. Entry on Suksun [tpl]ru icon[/tpl]

==History==

Suksun grew around the copper smelting and iron works plant in 1727–1729. Urban-type settlement status was granted to it on June 20, 1933.
