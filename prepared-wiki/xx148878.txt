[[Nižná Jablonka]]

CATEGORIES: Villages and municipalities in Humenné District

Nižná Jablonka is a village and municipality in Humenné District in the Prešov Region of north-east Slovakia.

==History==

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1436.

==Geography==

The municipality lies at an altitude of 334 metres and covers an area of 11.888 km².
It has a population of about 180 people.
