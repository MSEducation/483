[[Bülend Özveren]]

CATEGORIES: 1943 births, Living people, People from Istanbul, Istanbul University Faculty of Law alumni, Galatasaray High School alumni, Turkish television presenters, Sports commentators

Bülend Özveren (born 1943, Istanbul) is a Turkish television presenter and sports commentator.
He completed his secondary education at Lycée Saint-Benoît and higher education at Galatasaray High School. He graduated from Istanbul University, faculty of Law.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.hemsoruhemcevap.com/biyografi-hayati/270-uzmantv-uzmanlari/6201-program-sunucusu-buelent-oezveren.html |publisher=Hem Soru Hem Cevap |title=Bülend Özveren |language=Turkish [/tpl]
Bülend Özveren started to work at TRT on 31 July 1965. For a long time, he worked as a television presenter before he became a sports commentator in 1972. Since 1973, he is also presenting Eurovision Song Contest uninterrupted for Turkey.
He speaks English and French.
