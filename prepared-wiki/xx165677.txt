[[Intec, Inc.]]

CATEGORIES: Video game companies of the United States

Intec is one of the leading video game accessory manufacturers worldwide.Intec About Us They manufacture accessories for video game consoles and handhelds. Their products include controllers, sound systems, carrying cases, memory cards, expansion packs, racing wheels, game screens and connectivity accessories for all the major gaming platforms. Intec products are sold at stores including Best Buy, GameStop, EB Games, Walmart, Toys R Us and Hollywood Video. Their highlights include releasing the first-ever wireless A/V selector.[tpl]Citation needed|date=April 2010[/tpl]
