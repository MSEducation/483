[[Poland at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics, 2013 in Polish sport, Athletics in Poland

Poland is scheduled to compete at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics in Moscow, Russia, from 10–18 August 2013.
A team of 55 athlete was announced to represent the country in the event.
