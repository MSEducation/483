[[Ureaplasma infection]]

CATEGORIES: Sexually transmitted diseases and infections

Ureaplasma infection is an infection by an organism in the genus Ureaplasma, usually Ureaplasma urealyticum.
It is a significant cause of non-gonococcal urethritis.[tpl]cite journal |author=Burstein GR, Zenilman JM |title=Nongonococcal urethritis—a new paradigm |journal=Clinical Infectious Diseases |volume=28 |issue=Suppl 1 |pages=S66–73 |date=January 1999 |pmid=10028111 |doi=10.1086/514728 [/tpl]
