[[Raillardiopsis]]

CATEGORIES: Asteraceae genera, Flora of California, Historically recognized angiosperm genera

Raillardiopsis is a genus of flowering plants in the daisy family. They are native to California. Some botanists treat the two species in this genus as members of other genera, making this genus obsolete.
Anisocarpus scabridus was formerly named Raillardiopsis scabridus.Flora of North America: Anisocarpus scabridus
Carlquistia muirii was formerly named Raillardiopsis muirii.Flora of North America: Carlquistia
