[[No Molestar!]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 debut albums

No Molestar! is the debut album by the Chilean actress and singer Kel.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.tercera.cl/contenido/30_26843_9.shtml|title=Kel: "El disco se llama No molestar, porque viene con hartos prejuicios"|work=La Tercera|publisher=La Tercera|date=2008-07-03|accessdate=2011-08-03|language=Spanish[/tpl] The album was released on July 3, 2008 in Chile via Feria Music Records.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.latercera.com/contenido/661_103938_9.shtml|title=El íntimo encuentro de Raquel Calderón y Paolo Meneguzzi|date=2009-02-23|accessdate=2011-08-06|language=Spanish[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://web.archive.org/web/20080709033231/http://www.feriadeldisco.cl/cgi-bin/wspd_cgi.sh/WService=wsPFDD/MIXartista.htm?pr_partID=000000402875|title=Feria del Disco - Kel - No Molestar album details|accessdate=2011-08-06|publisher=Feria del Disco|language=Spanish[/tpl]
The first and lead single of the album was "Tenerte Cerca" FeriaDelDisco.cl "No Molestar!" album details. Retrieved: October 18, 2008
The album was produced by the Uruguayan Gonzalo Yáñez, an ex-vocalist of No Me Acuerdo.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.latercera.cl/contenido/30_23315_9.shtml|title=Kel lanza su primer disco como solista|work=La Tercera|publisher=La Tercera|date=2008-06-20|accessdate=2011-08-06|language=Spanish[/tpl]
No Molestar! was announced during the Viña del Mar International Song Festival, but there were some setbacks that delayed the release.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.emol.com/noticias/magazine/2008/06/19/309336/por-fin-kel-lanza-su-anunciado-primer-disco-no-molestar.html|title=Por fin Kel lanza su anunciado primer disco: "No molestar!"|publisher=Empresa El Mercurio S.A.P.|date=2008-06-19|accessdate=2011-08-06|language=Spanish[/tpl]
