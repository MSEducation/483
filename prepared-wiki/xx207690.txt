[[Porto Novo, Cape Verde]]

CATEGORIES: Cities in Cape Verde

Porto Novo is a town in the island of Santo Antão, in Cape Verde. It constitutes the seat of the Porto Novo municipality. It is situated on the south coast of the island, opposite the island São Vicente. At the 2010 census, the town had 9,310 inhabitants, which makes it the most populous town of the island.  
The city was once called Carvoeiros, and it gradually became the largest urban settlement of the island, and has the main harbor of the island for commercial exchanges.
