[[Jimmy Higdon]]

James Cecil Higdon, Jr., known as Jimmy Higdon (born July 15, 1953), is a businessman from Lebanon, Kentucky, who has been a  Republican member of the Kentucky State Senate since 2009. He represents  District 14, which until August 23, 2013 included Marion, Mercer, Nelson, Taylor, and Washington counties in Central Kentucky.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.lrc.ky.gov/legislator/s014.htm|title=Kentucky Legislature: Senator Jimmy Higdon|publisher=lrc.ky.gov|accessdate=August 10, 2013[/tpl]
