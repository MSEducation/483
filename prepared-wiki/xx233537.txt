[[Scooby-Doo (soundtrack)]]

CATEGORIES: Film soundtracks, Hip hop soundtracks, 2002 soundtracks, Atlantic Records soundtracks, Scooby-Doo soundtracks

Scooby-Doo is the soundtrack to the 2002 film Scooby-Doo. It was released on June 4, 2002 on Atlantic Records on Audio CD and Compact Cassette and consisted of alternative rock, hip hop and reggae music. The soundtrack fared well on the Billboard charts, peaking at #24 on the Billboard 200, #49 on the Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums and #4 on the Top Soundtracks.
