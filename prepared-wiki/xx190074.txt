[[2008 Johan Cruijff Schaal]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 in association football, 2008 in Dutch sport, Johan Cruijff-schaal, Feyenoord matches, PSV Eindhoven matches

The match for Johan Cruijff-schaal XIII was held on 23 August 2008 in the Amsterdam ArenA. The match featured the Eredivisie 2007-08 champions PSV and KNVB Cup 2007-08 winners Feyenoord. The match started off the 2008-09 season in Dutch football.
