[[GameSalad (company)]]

CATEGORIES: Companies based in Austin, Texas, American companies established in 2007, Software companies based in Texas

GameSalad, Inc., formerly Gendai Games ([tpl]IPAc-en|ɡ|ɛ|n|ˈ|d|aɪ[/tpl] [tpl]respell|gen|DY|'[/tpl]), is an American computer software company that designs and develops authoring tools and web services for game creation. Their flagship product is GameSalad Creator, a game creation system for creative professionals that enables non-programmers to create 2D casual games for Apple's mobile iOS platforms, such as the iPhone and iPad, HTML 5 games, and games for Android devices. The company is headquartered in Austin, Texas, United States with an additional office in Los Angeles, California.[tpl]cite web |author=Menell, Bryan |url=http://www.austinstartup.com/2009/09/gamesalad-gears-up-for-iphone-publishing-system/ |title=GameSalad Gears Up for iPhone Publishing System |date=2009-09-02 |publisher=AustinStartup |accessdate=2010-01-11 |archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/5mhGxsvo5 |archivedate=2010-01-11 [/tpl]
Previously called Gendai Games, Inc., in March 2009 the company launched an open beta of its first product, GameSalad.[tpl]cite news |title=GameSalad engine launches membership plan, iPhone games |author=Holt, Chris |newspaper=Macworld |date=2009-09-25 |url=http://www.infoworld.com/d/applications/gamesalad-engine-launches-membership-plan-iphone-games-371 |accessdate=2010-01-11 |archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/5mhGzraSh |archivedate=2010-01-11 [/tpl] GameSalad is a visual, drag-and-drop based tool for game creation, aimed at people without programming knowledge for building iPhone, iPod, and iPad games.  GameSalad is geared to provide support for Apple's iOS platform and devices to take advantage of the latest technology in mobile computing. Users can exchange templates of games online, share their games to GameSalad.com so others can play them in a web browser, and share their games over social networks.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.texastechpulse.com/interview_with_michael_augustin_gendai_games/s-0020624.html |title=Interview with Michael Augustin, Gendai Games |date=2009-05-29 |publisher=Texas Tech Pulse |accessdate=2010-01-11 |archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/5mhH4JGsQ |archivedate=2010-01-11 [/tpl][tpl]cite news |title=Gendai Games Announces Macworld 2010 GameSalad Challenge |author=Cook, Brad |newspaper=The Mac Observer |date=2009-12-21 |url=http://www.macobserver.com/tmo/article/gendai_games_announces_macworld_2010_gamesalad_challenge/ |accessdate=2010-01-11 |archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/5mhH6I4NO |archivedate=2010-01-11 [/tpl]
