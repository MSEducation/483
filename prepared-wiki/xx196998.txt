[[William Perry Hay]]

CATEGORIES: American carcinologists, People from Woodford County, Illinois, 1872 births, 1947 deaths

William Perry Hay (born in Eureka, Illinois on December 8, 1871; died in 1947) was an American zoologist known for work on crayfish and reptiles. He was the son of Oliver Perry Hay.William Perry Hay papersScience obituary
