[[ARQ (journal)]]

CATEGORIES: Architecture journals, Architecture in Chile, Pontifical Catholic University of Chile, Publications established in 1980, 1980 establishments in Chile

ARQ is a scientific journal published by the School of Architecture of the Pontifical Catholic University of Chile. It publishes articles on a wide range of architecture-related topics, primarily on issues that are relevant to Chile and South America. Each issue is centered around one theme.
