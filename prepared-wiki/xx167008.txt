[[It Never Entered My Mind]]

CATEGORIES: Songs with music by Richard Rodgers, Songs with lyrics by Lorenz Hart, Ella Fitzgerald songs, 1940 songs, Songs from musicals

"It Never Entered My Mind" is a show tune from the 1940 Rodgers and Hart musical Higher and Higher (1940), where it was introduced by Shirley Ross.

==Notable recordings==

The *Miles Davis recording was used in the movies *The Runaway Bride (1999) and the Lenny Bruce biopic *Lenny (1974).
