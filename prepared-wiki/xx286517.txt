[[Vilius Aleliūnas]]

Vilius Aleliūnas (born on October 19, 1987 in Panevėžys) is a Lithuanian orienteering competitor. At the World Games in 2013 he won a bronze medal in the middle distance, behind Matthias Kyburz and Daniel Hubmann, and ahead of Rasmus Thrane Hansen.[tpl]cite web|title =Middle Distance – Results |url=http://wg2013.orienteering.org/result-middle.html |publisher =The World Games 2013, Cali |accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl]
