[[Langston Moore]]

CATEGORIES: 1981 births, Living people, People from Charleston, South Carolina, Players of American football from South Carolina, American football defensive tackles, South Carolina Gamecocks football players, Cincinnati Bengals players, Arizona Cardinals players, Detroit Lions players, Florida Tuskers players

Langston Moore (born July 17, 1981 in Charleston, South Carolina) is an American football defensive tackle for the Virginia Destroyers of the United Football League. He was drafted by the Bengals in the sixth round of the 2003 NFL Draft. He played college football at South Carolina.
Moore attended James Island Charter High School in Charleston, South Carolina where he lettered in football and was a standout in Region AAAA play.
Moore has also played for the Arizona Cardinals, Detroit Lions and Cincinnati Bengals.
His father was well known Ft. Wayne (WOWO Radio) and Charleston, South Carolina radio personality Ken Moore.
He has joined the Gamecock IMG Sports Network in 2012 to serve as the sideline reporter for football broadcasts.
