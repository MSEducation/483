[[Gmina Dębe Wielkie]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Mińsk County

Gmina Dębe Wielkie is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Mińsk County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Dębe Wielkie, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|9|km|mi|0[/tpl] west of Mińsk Mazowiecki and [tpl]convert|31|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] east of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|77.88|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 8,299.

==Villages==

Gmina Dębe Wielkie contains the villages and settlements of Aleksandrówka, Bykowizna, Celinów, Cezarów, Choszczak, Choszczówka Dębska, Choszczówka Rudzka, Choszczówka Stojecka, Chrośla, Cięciwa, Cyganka, Dębe Wielkie, Górki, Gorzanka, Jędrzejnik, Kąty Goździejewskie Drugie, Kąty Goździejewskie Pierwsze, Kobierne, Łaszczyzna, Olesin, Ostrów-Kania, Poręby, Ruda, Rysie, Teresław and Walercin.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Dębe Wielkie is bordered by the town of Zielonka and by the gminas of Halinów, Mińsk Mazowiecki, Stanisławów and Wiązowna.
