[[T. D. Upshaw]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing, Year of death missing, Tennessee State Tigers football coaches

T. D. Upshaw was the seventh head football coach for the Tennessee State University Tigers located in Nashville, Tennessee and he held that position for two seasons, from 1930 until 1931.  His career coaching record at Tennessee State was 3 wins, 4 losses, and 0 ties.  As of the conclusion of the 2007 season, this ranks him 15th at Tennessee State in total wins and 14th at Tennessee State in winning percentage (.429).Tennessee State University coaching records
