[[Muhammad Nasir Khan I]]

CATEGORIES: Khans of Kalat, 18th-century monarchs in Asia, Baloch people

Mir Muhammad Nasir Khan I was the Khan (ruler) of the princely state of Kalat in what is now the Balochistan province of Pakistan during the eighteenth century.Baluchistan - Imperial Gazetteer of India, v. 6, p. 277.
