[[Folarin Ogunsola]]

CATEGORIES: 1998 births, Living people, Gambian swimmers

Folarin L. Ogunsola (born 23 June 1997) is a Gambian swimmer.http://www.bcn2013.com/en/swimming/detail/member/folarin-l-ogunsola He competed in the 50 m freestyle and 50 m backstroke events (the latter of which he did not start) at the 2011 World Aquatics Championships and in the 50 m freestyle and 50 m butterfly events at the 2013 World Aquatics Championships.
