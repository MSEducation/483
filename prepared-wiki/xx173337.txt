[[Brian Mahoney (footballer)]]

CATEGORIES: 1952 births, Living people, English footballers, People from Tantobie, Association football midfielders, The Football League players, Huddersfield Town F.C. players, Barnsley F.C. players

Brian Mahoney (born May 12, 1952 in Tantobie) is a former professional footballer, who played for Huddersfield Town and Barnsley.
