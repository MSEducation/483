[[Capita International Times Square]]

CATEGORIES: Buildings and structures in Dhaka, Proposed buildings and structures, Visitor attractions in Dhaka, Shopping malls in Bangladesh, Shopping malls by country

Capita International Times Square is an under construction shopping mall in Dhaka, Bangladesh, Construction of the shopping mall started in 2012. At the end of its construction, this shopping mall will be the 3rd largest shopping mall in Bangladesh with a total area of 5.6 million sq ft and office area of 1.5 million sq ft.[tpl]cite web|title=Capita International Times Square|url=http://www.capitalandbd.net/details.php?id=18|accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Capita International Times Square Floor Plans|url=http://www.capitalandbd.net/project/Floor%20Plan%20CITS.jpg|accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Shop@ Large shopping mall with multiplex|url=http://bikroy.com/en/shop-for-sale-dhaka-35|accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl]
