[[Resta in ascolto (song)]]

CATEGORIES: 2004 singles, Laura Pausini songs, Italian-language songs, Spanish-language songs, Number-one singles in Italy, Songs written by Cheope, Songs written by Laura Pausini

"Resta in ascolto" (English: Keep listening) is a pop rock ballad written by Laura Pausini, Daniel and Cheope and recorded by Italian singer Laura Pausini. It was released on 10 September 2004 as the first single from Pausini's album Resta in ascolto.[tpl]Cite web | url = http://www.rockol.it/news-65306/Arriva-%27Resta-in-ascolto%27,-il-nuovo-singolo-di-Laura-Pausini | publisher = Rockol.it | accessdate = 25 September 2011 | title = Arriva 'Resta in ascolto', il nuovo singolo di Laura Pausini | date= 9 September 2004[/tpl] The single reached number one in Italian Singles Chart, and it was her first number one single since "La solitudine". Pausini also recorded a Spanish-language version of the song, adapted by J. Badia and titled "Escucha atento". This version of the song was released as a single in the Hispanic market and it was included in the Spanish-language edition of her album, Escucha.

==Music video==

The music video for the song was filmed in 35 mm[tpl]Cite web | url = http://www.adnkronos.com/Archivio/AdnAgenzia/2004/09/09/Spettacolo/Musica/MUSICA-LAURA-PAUSINI-TORNA-IN-PISTA-CON-IL-SINGOLO-RESTA-IN-ASCOLTO_105954.php | publisher = Adnkronos | language = Italian | date = 9 September 2004 | title = Musica: Laura Pausini torna in pista con il singolo Resta in ascolto[/tpl] and directed by Paolo Monico. Released on 27 September 2004, it was shot in Los Angeles, between Venice, Santa Monica and the Vazquez Rocks.[tpl]Cite web | url = http://www.mtv.it/news/news-detail.asp?idnews=14376 | publisher = MTV Italy | language = Italian | accessdate = 28 September 2011 | title = Resta In Ascolto - Da lunedì 27 il nuovo video di Laura Pausini su MTV! | date = 23 September 2004[/tpl] The cinematography is by Patrizio Patrizi, while Francesca Chiappetta was credited as the executive producer of the video.
In the video, Pausini walks through the roads of Los Angeles. After witnessing the arrest of a man, Pausini enters in a café, where she meets a boy handcuffed to his girlfriend, and she soon realizes that the barman is also handcuffed. Several other people are later shown with handcuffs on them, representing relationships which, for better or for worse, look to be unbreakable. Only in the second part of the video, some of the people met by Pausini are able to free themselves from their duties, while others will be trapped forever.[tpl]Cite web | url = http://www.radioitalia.it/web/news/news.php?idNews=575 | archiveurl = http://web.archive.org/web/20071030212251/http://www.radioitalia.it/web/news/news.php?idNews=575 | publisher= Radio Italia Solo Musica Italiana | archivedate = 30 October 2007 | language = Italian | accessdate = 18 August 2012 | title = Laura Pausini: il nuovo video di Resta in Ascolto | date =4 October 2004[/tpl]
Other scenes, filmed from a helicopter, show Pausini singing on top of a mountain, and they were shot without using any body double.[tpl]Cite web | url= http://archiviostorico.corriere.it/2004/settembre/23/per_Laura_Pausini_inno_alla_co_9_040923072.shtml| work = Corriere della Sera | language = Italian | date = 23 September 2004 | title = E per Laura Pausini inno alla pace scritto a quattro mani con Madonna | author = Andrea Laffranchi[/tpl] This part of the video wants to highlight Pausini's personal willing of change and independence.
