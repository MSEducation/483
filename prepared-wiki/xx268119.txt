[[Academia Samuel Reyes]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Chile, Association football clubs established in 2002, 2002 establishments in Chile

Club Deportivo Academia de Fútbol Samuel Reyes is a Chilean Football club, their home town is Curicó, Chile.
The club were founded on May 11, 2002 and participed for 2 years in Tercera División B.
