[[Cross Canada Barndance]]

CATEGORIES: Canadian music television series, Canadian variety television series, CTV Television Network shows, 1961 Canadian television series debuts, 1962 Canadian television series endings, Country music television series

Cross Canada Barndance was a Canadian television variety show, which aired on CTV during that network's inaugural season in 1961-62.
