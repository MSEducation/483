[[Arsen Tandelov]]

CATEGORIES: 1996 births, Living people, Russian footballers

Arsen Andreyevich Tandelov ([tpl]lang-ru|Арсен Андреевич Танделов[/tpl]; born February 18, 1996) is a Russian football midfielder, who currently playing for  FC Alania-d Vladikavkaz.
He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Alania-d Vladikavkaz on July 12, 2013 in a game against FC Gazprom transgaz Stavropol Ryzdvyany.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Russian Football Union|url=http://rfspro.ru/cgi-bin/player/script2.cgi?par1=119468|title=Career Summary|date=10 August 2013[/tpl]
