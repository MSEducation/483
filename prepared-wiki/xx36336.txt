[[List of Ambassadors of the United Kingdom to Kazakhstan]]

CATEGORIES: Lists of ambassadors of the United Kingdom, Ambassadors of the United Kingdom to Kazakhstan, Kazakhstan-related lists

The Ambassador of the United Kingdom to Kazakhstan is the United Kingdom's foremost diplomatic representative in the Republic of Kazakhstan, and in charge of the UK's diplomatic mission in Kazakhstan.  The official title is Her Britannic Majesty's Ambassador to the Republic of Kazakhstan.
Until 2012 the ambassador served concurrently as the non-resident Ambassador to Kyrgyzstan, an ambassadorial position in its own right.
