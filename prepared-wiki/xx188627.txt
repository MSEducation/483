[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Piracicaba]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Campinas, Religious organizations established in 1944, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Piracicaba ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Piracicabensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Piracicaba in the Ecclesiastical province of Campinas in Brazil.
