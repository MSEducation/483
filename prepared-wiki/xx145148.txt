Gregory S. "Greg" Fitzsimmons (born April 5, 1966) is an American stand-up comedian, television writer/producer and radio host.

==Life and career==

Greg was born in New York City, New York, to New York City radio personality Bob Fitzsimmons, and Patricia (n√©e McCarthy) Fitzsimmons.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.howardstern.com/rundown.hs?d=1155614400|title=Superstars visit sirius|publisher=Howardstern.com|date=2006-08-15|accessdate=2011-10-19[/tpl] He grew up in Tarrytown, New York.
He began his stand up comedy career while attending Boston University. Fitzsimmons has since appeared on such programs as The Tonight Show with Jay Leno, Late Show with David Letterman, Late Night with Conan O'Brien, Chelsea Lately and Comedy Central Presents.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.wickedinfo.com/comedians/interview-with-greg-fitzsimmons|title=Interview with Greg Fitzsimmons|publisher=Wickedinfo.com |date=2007-02-21 |accessdate=2011-10-19[/tpl] In 1996, Greg hosted the MTV game show Idiot Savants. He is also a regular commentator on Vh1's Best Week Ever and I Love The series.
