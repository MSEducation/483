Peñon Viejo is a station along Line A of the Mexico City Metro.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.metro.df.gob.mx/red/estacion.html?id=127|title=Peñon Viejo|language=Spanish|accessdate=6 August 2011[/tpl][tpl]Cite web|last=Archambault|first=Richard|url=http://mexicometro.org/metro/linea/penon-viejo/|title=Peñon Viejo »  Mexico City Metro System|accessdate=6 August 2011[/tpl]  It is located in the Colonia Santa Martha Acatitla Norte and Colonia Santa Martha Acatitla Sur neighborhoods of the Iztapalapa borough of Mexico City.
The logo is an Aztec based glyph showing a rock that represents the Peñon Viejo formation near the station. The station was opened on 12 August 1991.
