[[Border, San Diego]]

CATEGORIES: Neighborhoods in San Diego, California

Border is a small non-residential neighborhood in the southern section of San Diego. It neighbors San Ysidro to the north, and Tijuana to the south.City of San Diego neighborhoods map The San Ysidro Port of Entry is located in Border, as is the southernmost station of the San Diego Trolley.
