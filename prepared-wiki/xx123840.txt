Mike started singing at a young age in a close harmony group and later sang in a hip-hop/R&B group called Vigilante, which won "De Grote Prijs van Nederland" (The Big Price of Holland) in the year 2000.
Mike is currently working on his new album which contains R&B/Soul with pop and rock influences. This album will come out in 2009.
