[[Lulekabad]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Lulekabad ([tpl]lang-fa|لولك اباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Lūlekābād and Lūlakābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3073245|Lulekabad[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 198, in 55 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
