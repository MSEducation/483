[[Joaquim Rufino do Rêgo]]

CATEGORIES: 1926 births, 2013 deaths, Brazilian Roman Catholic bishops

Joaquim Rufino do Rêgo (January 14, 1926 – August 10, 2013) was a Roman Catholic bishop.
Ordained in 1952, Rufino do Rêgo was named bishop in 1971 and in 1986 became bishop of the Diocese of Parnaiba, Brazil. He retired in 2001.Joaquim Rufino do Rego
