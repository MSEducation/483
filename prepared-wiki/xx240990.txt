[[Jiaoqu, Changzhi]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Shanxi, Changzhi

Jiaoqu ([tpl]zh|s=郊区 |t=郊區 |p=Jiāoqū |l=suburban district[/tpl]), or Jiao District, is a district of Changzhi, Shanxi, China. It has an area of [tpl]convert|285|km2[/tpl] and a population of 290,000.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.jqzf.changzhi.gov.cn/more.aspx?pid=16&id=24|title=Profile of Jiaoqu, Changzhi|publisher=Official website of Jiaoqu District government|language=Chinese|date=|accessdate=April 17, 2011[/tpl]
