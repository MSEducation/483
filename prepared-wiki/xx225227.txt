[[1975 Sandown 250]]

CATEGORIES: Motorsport at Sandown, 1975 in Australian motorsport

The 1975 Sandown 250 was an endurance race for Group C Touring Cars. 
The event was held at the Sandown circuit in Victoria, Australia on 14 September 1975.
Race distance was 130 laps of the 3.11 km circuit, totalling [tpl]convert|403.8|km|mi|abbr=on[/tpl]
The field was divided into four classes according to engine displacement: The Melbourne Age, 15 September 1975
The race was Round 2 of the 1975 Australian Manufacturers' Championship.
