[[Panda cow]]

CATEGORIES: Cattle breeds

Panda cow is a breed of miniature cattle with markings that resemble those of a giant panda.http://www.thirdage.com/news/panda-cow-rare-breed-miniature-cow-born_1-2-2011 There are reportedly only 24 in the world, as of January 2011http://www.app.com/article/20110101/NEWS06/110101052/Rare-panda-cow-born-in-northern-Colorado
Also known as the miniature panda cow, it is the result of a mutation. its markings include a white belt around the animal's midsection, and a white panda-like face with black markings around the eyes.http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/worldnews/northamerica/usa/8236447/Rare-panda-cow-born.html
In December, 2010, a panda cow was born in Colorado that received a lot of press.http://www.kktv.com/home/headlines/Rare_Panda_Cow_Born_in_Colorado_112750019.html In 2013 there was a miniature Panda Cow born in Roy, WA.
