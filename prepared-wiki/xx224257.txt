[[Dortmund University of Applied Sciences and Arts]]

CATEGORIES: Universities and colleges in North Rhine-Westphalia, Educational institutions established in 1971

Dortmund University of Applied Sciences and Arts ([tpl]lang-de|Fachhochschule Dortmund[/tpl]) is a Fachhochschule in Dortmund, North Rhine-Westphalia, Germany with 7,300 students, and 446 staff. It is situated in the Ruhr area, the fourth largest urban area in Europe.
