[[Select Model Management]]

CATEGORIES: Modeling agencies, Companies based in London

Select Model Management is a modelling agency based in London. Founded and owned by Tandy Anderson and sisters Clare and Chrissie Castagnetti, they opened the agency in 1977.[tpl]cite news | url=http://www.thesundaytimes.co.uk/sto/style/fashion/trends/article103367.ece| title=Relative Values: Clare and Chrissie Castagnetti | newspaper=The Sunday Times |accessdate=10 Aug 2013[/tpl] It is a modelling agency in the UK with 38 top-ranked models as of 2013.[tpl]cite web |url=http://models.com/agency/Select-Model-Management/ranked |publisher=Models.com |title=Select Models - Ranked Models |accessdate=10 Aug 2013[/tpl][tpl]rs|date=August 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web |url=http://www.babusinesslife.com/People/What-Ive-Learnt/Chrissie-Castagnetti.html |publisher=British Airways Business Life |title=Meet Chrissie Castagnetti |accessdate=10 Aug 2013[/tpl]
In their early days, Select was one of the first modelling agencies to find models by "scouting" them on the street. They adopted that method of recruitment to quickly establish a client list and reputation.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.independent.co.uk/life-style/real-lives-im-gonna-make-you-a-star-1098423.html|title=I'm Gonna Make You a Star|newspaper=Independent|accessdate=10 Aug 2013|location=London|first=Ursula|last=Kenny|date=6 June 1999[/tpl] In the 1980s, competitor agencies also used the technique to grow their businesses.[tpl]cite news| url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/education/3351787/Be-prepared-to-be-scouted.html |title=Be Prepared to be Scouted |newspaper=Telegraph |accessdate=10 Aug 2013 |location=London |first=Oliver |last=Bennett |date=30 July 2005[/tpl] In recent years, the company has held "open calls" for prospective models.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/femail/article-2324415/Could-YOU-David-Gandy-Stella-Tennant-MailOnline-join-forces-Select-Model-Management-new-face-Enter-HERE-.html |title=Could YOU be the next David Gandy or Agyness Deyn? |newspaper=Daily Mail |accessdate=10 Aug 2013 |location=London |date=14 May 2013[/tpl]
