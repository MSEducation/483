[[Meskanaw, Saskatchewan]]

CATEGORIES: Designated places in Saskatchewan, Invergordon No. 430, Saskatchewan, Organized hamlets in Saskatchewan

Meskanaw is a small community south west of Melfort, Saskatchewan. Meskanaw is the Cree word for trail or road.
The community, an organized hamlet with the status of designated place, had a population of 18 in the Canada 2006 Census.Canada 2006 Census: Designated places in Saskatchewan
