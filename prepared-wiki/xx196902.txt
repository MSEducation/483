[[Christ Lutheran Church (Highland, Wisconsin)]]

CATEGORIES: Lutheran churches in Wisconsin, Churches in Iowa County, Wisconsin, Lutheran congregations established in the 19th century

Christ Lutheran Church is a congregation of the Lutheran Church - Missouri Synod located in the village of Highland, Wisconsin.  The church was organized in 1895 and the congregation originally met in Pine Knob, a small country place five miles northeast of Highland. In 1937, the congregation purchased and began services in a Methodist church building located in the village of Highland.  The building is the oldest standing church structure in Highland, erected in the late 1850s.History of Iowa County, Wisconsin, 1881, p. 805
