[[2013–14 UMKC Kangaroos men's basketball team]]

CATEGORIES: UMKC Kangaroos men's basketball seasons, 2013–14 Western Athletic Conference men's basketball season

The 2013–14 UMKC Kangaroos men's basketball team represented the University of Missouri–Kansas City during the 2013–14 NCAA Division I men's basketball season. The Kangaroos were led by new head coach Kareem Richardson.[tpl]cite web|url=http://espn.go.com/mens-college-basketball/story/_/id/9071013/kareem-richardson-hired-coach-umkc-kangaroos|title=Kareem Richardson to coach UMKC|publisher=ESPN|accessdate=2013-03-19[/tpl] They played most of their home games at the Municipal Auditorium, with a few games also being held at the Independence Events Center. This was their first year as a member Western Athletic Conference.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.kansascity.com/2013/02/07/4054339/umkc-to-join-western-athletic.html|title=UMKC will leave Summit League for Western Athletic Conference|publisher=Kansas City Star|accessdate=2013-02-07[/tpl] They finished the season 10–20, 7–9 in WAC play to finish in a tie for fifth place. They lost in the quarterfinals of the WAC Tournament to Idaho.
