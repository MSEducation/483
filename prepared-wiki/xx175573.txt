===Relegation===

The two worst placed teams in the repechage, which are [tpl]Brazil fc|Remo[/tpl] and [tpl]Brazil fc|Náutico[/tpl], were relegated to the following year's second level.

[tpl]Brazil fc|Goiás[/tpl] declared as the Campeonato Brasileiro Série B champions by aggregate score of 3-3.

===Promotion===

The champion and runner-up, which are [tpl]Brazil fc|Goiás[/tpl] and [tpl]Brazil fc|Juventude[/tpl], were promoted to the following year's first level.

===Relegation===

The two worst placed teams in all the four groups in the first stage, which are [tpl]Brazil fc|Fortaleza[/tpl] and Tiradentes-DF, were relegated to the following year's third level.

==Copa do Brasil==

The Copa do Brasil final was played between [tpl]Brazil fc|Grêmio[/tpl] and Ceará.

[tpl]Brazil fc|Grêmio[/tpl] declared as the cup champions by aggregate score of 1-0.
