[[Sam Houston Schoolhouse]]

CATEGORIES: School buildings completed in 1794, National Register of Historic Places in Tennessee, Buildings and structures in Blount County, Tennessee, Museums in Blount County, Tennessee, Education museums in the United States, Defunct schools in Tennessee, Tennessee State Historic Sites, History museums in Tennessee

Sam Houston Schoolhouse is a single-room log cabin-style schoolhouse in Maryville, Tennessee, built in 1794.  Sam Houston taught at the school as a young man, before the War of 1812.Tennessee Historical Commission: State Owned Historic Site: Sam Houston Schoolhouse 
The schoolhouse is a Tennessee state historic site operated under an agreement with the Tennessee Historical Commission. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1972.
