==Geography==

Sool has an area, [tpl]as of|2006|lc=on[/tpl], of [tpl]convert|13.3|km2|sqmi|abbr=on[/tpl].  Of this area, 23.6% is used for agricultural purposes, while 42% is forested.  Of the rest of the land, 1.2% is settled (buildings or roads) and the remainder (33.2%) is non-productive (rivers, glaciers or mountains).

In the 2007 federal election the most popular party was the SPS which received 80% of the vote.  Most of the rest of the votes went to the SVP with 14.4% of the vote.
The entire Swiss population is generally well educated.  In Sool about 72.9% of the population (between age 25-64) have completed either non-mandatory upper secondary education or additional higher education (either University or a Fachhochschule).
Sool has an unemployment rate of 1.5%.  [tpl]as of|2005[/tpl], there were 15 people employed in the primary economic sector and about 9 businesses involved in this sector.  8 people are employed in the secondary sector and there are 2 businesses in this sector.  10 people are employed in the tertiary sector, with 4 businesses in this sector.
