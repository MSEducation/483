[[Nora, la Emprendedora]]

CATEGORIES: Televen telenovelas, 2013 telenovelas, Venezuelan telenovelas

Nora is a Venezuelan telenovela produced and aired on Televen in co-production with Mexican channel Cadena Tres and distributed internationally by Telemundo. It is the third telenovela produced in high definition by Televen after Dulce Amargo and the second to be co-produced with Cadena Tres.
