[[No Matter What (Boyzone song)]]

CATEGORIES: 1998 singles, Boyzone songs, Meat Loaf songs, Pop ballads, Songs written by Jim Steinman, Irish Singles Chart number-one singles, Number-one singles in New Zealand, UK Singles Chart number-one singles, Songs with music by Andrew Lloyd Webber, Songs from musicals, Song recordings produced by Jim Steinman

"No Matter What" is a song from the 1996 musical Whistle Down the Wind, written by Andrew Lloyd Webber and Jim Steinman and popularised by the Irish group Boyzone. The song reached number-one on the UK Singles Chart, and was the only Boyzone hit to become popular in the US. Jewels & Stone did a remix for the song for dance clubs which was popular.
