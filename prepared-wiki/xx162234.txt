[[Cold & Wet]]

CATEGORIES: 2006 singles, Will Oldham songs

"Cold & Wet" is a Bonnie 'Prince' Billy single taken from the 2006 album The Letting Go. It also includes a version of "The Way", originally from Master and Everyone (2004), recorded live on the Pebbles and Ripples Tour in 2004 (the tour from which the live album Summer in the Southeast was taken), and "Buried Treasure", a Kenny Rogers cover (the original version was a hit for Rogers in 1983). The single comes in both 12" vinyl and an Enhanced CD, which includes the music video for the title track, directed by Einar Baldvin.
