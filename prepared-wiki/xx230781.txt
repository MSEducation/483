[[2013 Summer Deaflympics]]

CATEGORIES: Deaflympics, Sport in Sofia, 2013 in Bulgarian sport, International sports competitions hosted by Bulgaria, 2013 in multi-sport events, Multi-sport events in Bulgaria

The 2013 Summer Deaflympics, officially known as the 22nd Summer Deaflympics, was an international multi-sport event that took place in Sofia, Bulgaria from July 26 to August 4, 2013.[tpl]cite web|title=Sofia 2013 – 22nd Summer Deaflympics|url=http://www.deaflympics.com/games/index.asp?GamesID=39|publisher=International Committee of Sports for the Deaf|accessdate=6 April 2013[/tpl] The marathon had been held before in Füssen, Germany in 21 July.[tpl]cite web|title=Sofia 2013 22nd Deaflympics Marathon Details|url=http://www.deaflympics.com/news/enews/?PID=2&id=1635|publisher=CISS|accessdate=11 April 2013[/tpl]
 Sofia was hosting the biggest sports event for deaf athletes for a second time. In the past the Bulgarian capital hosted the 1993 Summer Deaflympics, thus becoming only the second city, together with Copenhagen, which will host two Summer Deaflympics.[tpl]cite web|title=22nd Summer Deaflympics 2013|url=http://www.h3.tv/event_details.php?evnt_id=53|publisher=H3 TV|accessdate=6 April 2013[/tpl]
