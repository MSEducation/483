[[All Things Real]]

CATEGORIES: 2006 debut albums

All Things Real is the debut album by English singer-songwriter Steve Adey. The album was released on March 6, 2006, through Grand Harmonium Records. The album comprises original compositions, plus covers of Bob Dylan's Shelter from the Storm and Will Oldham's I See a Darkness.
The song "Mississippi" was written about American singer Jeff Buckley,http://www.stylusmagazine.com/reviews/steve-adey/all-things-real.htm
while the song "Mary Margaret O'Hara" is a tribute to Canadian singer/actress Mary Margaret O'Hara.http://www.last.fm/music/Steve+Adey/_/Mary+Margaret+O%27Hara
Two singles from the album were released; Find The Way which included two new acoustic versions of Find The Way and Mississippi and Mississippi: Remixed, a download-only EP featuring Kramer, Sweet Billy Pilgrim and A Marble Calm. The A Marble Calm remix was also released on 12" vinyl through Tonefloat Records).tonefloat
