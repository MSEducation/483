[[KAAP]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Washington (state), Adult contemporary radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 1988

KAAP (99.5 FM, "Apple FM 99.5") is a radio station broadcasting an Adult Contemporary format. Licensed to Rock Island, Washington, USA, the station serves the Wenatchee  area.  The station is currently owned by Cherry Creek Radio and licensed to CCR-Wenatchee IV, LLC and features programing from Premiere Radio Networks.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KAAP |title=KAAP Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==History==

The station went on the air as KXAA on 1988-06-24.  On 2000-02-01, the station changed its call sign to the current KAAP.[tpl]cite web|url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=63882&Callsign=KAAP |title=KAAP Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==Ownership==

In June 2006, a deal was reached for KAAP to be acquired by Cherry Creek Radio from Fisher Radio Regional Group as part of a 24 station deal with a total reported sale price of $33.3 million.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.broadcastingcable.com/article/CA6344749.html |work=Broadcasting & Cable |title=Deals |date=2006-06-19[/tpl]
