[[Detroit Lakes High School]]

CATEGORIES: Schools in Becker County, Minnesota, Educational institutions with year of establishment missing, Public high schools in Minnesota

Detroit Lakes High School is a public high school located in  Detroit Lakes, Minnesota. It serves approximately 950 students and is a part of the Detroit Lakes Public Schools system. The school colors are red and white and the athletic teams are known as "The Lakers". It is the largest high school in Becker County.

==Notable alumni==

Andy Lia, DLHS Class of 1959, radio host and longtime "Voice of the Lakers"
Tanner Lane, DLHS Class of 2011, Fargo Force hockey player, drafted to NHL team Atlanta Thrashers
