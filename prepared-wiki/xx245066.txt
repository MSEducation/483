[[Limhamns IP]]

CATEGORIES: Football venues in Sweden, IF Limhamn Bunkeflo, Buildings and structures in Malmö

Limhamns IP is a football stadium in Malmö, Sweden. It is the current home stadium of IF Limhamn Bunkeflo and Limhamns FF. When game regulations require the use of electric lights Malmö IP is used instead.
