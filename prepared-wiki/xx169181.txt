[[Marshall Sperbeck]]

Marshall Sperbeck (born May 17, 1960) is an American college football coach and former player. He was the head football coach at California State University, Sacramento from 2007 to April 2014. 

==Coaching career==

Sperbeck was named the ninth head football coach at Sacramento State on January 13, 2007.
