[[Albert Durrant Watson]]

CATEGORIES: 1859 births, 1926 deaths, Canadian poets

Albert Durrant Watson (January 8, 1859 Dixie, Ontario - May 3, 1926 Toronto) was a Canadian poet, and physician.

==Life==

He graduated from Victoria University, and Edinburgh University.  He practiced medicine for more than thirty years in the city of Toronto.http://digital.library.upenn.edu/women/garvin/poets/watson.htmlhttp://www.islandnet.com/~sric/Watson_Albert_Durrant.pdf
He held a series of seances from 1918 to 1920 by medium Louis Benjamin."WATSON, ALBERT DURRANT", Dictionary of Canadian Biography Online
