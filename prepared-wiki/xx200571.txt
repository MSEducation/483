[[Dave Rick]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, American rock guitarists, King Missile members, Yo La Tengo members, Bongwater (band) members

David Matthew "Dave" Rick is an American guitarist and former member of underground rock bands B.A.L.L.,True Bongwater,Strong, p. 240  King Missile,Strong, p. 390 Phantom Tollbooth, When People Were Shorter and Lived Near the Water, Wide Right, Wonderama, and Yo La Tengo . He has since been a member of Atlantic Drone, Dew-Claw, The Martinets, Overcat and Stress Test.
