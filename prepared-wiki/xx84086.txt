[[Suwa District, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Districts in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]Nihongo|Suwa|諏訪郡|Suwa-gun,suwa no kōri[/tpl] is a district located in Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the district has an estimated population of 46,162 and a density of 181.18 persons per km². The total area is 254.79 km².
