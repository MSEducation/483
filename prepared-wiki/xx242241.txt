Kiernan Peter Hughes-Mason (born 22 October 1991) is an English professional footballer who plays as a striker for Welling United.
He has previously played for Milwall, Cheltenham Town, Tooting & Mitcham, Chelmsford City, Kettering Town and Grimsby Town.

==Career==
