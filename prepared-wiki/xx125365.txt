[[Café Bossa]]

CATEGORIES: 2006 debut albums, Bossa nova albums, Sitti Navarro albums

Café Bossa is the debut album by Filipino bossa nova singer Sitti. It was released on January 25, 2006 by Warner Music Philippines. The album spawned four successful singles—"Tattooed on My Mind", "Hey Look at the Sun", "I Didn't Know I Was Looking for Love" and "Para sa Akin". The album was certified double platinum by the Philippine Association of the Record Industry on September 17, 2006, denoting over 60,000 units sold in the country.[tpl]cite web|url=http://sittionline.multiply.com/reviews/item/1|title=Cafe Bossa|publisher=SittiOnline.Multiply..com|accessdate=2010-12-16[/tpl]
A limited edition of the album was released in February 2007, containing a bonus video CD and a different cover.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.titikpilipino.com/album/index.php?albumid=1247|title=Cafe Bossa (Limited Edition) - Sitti|publisher=Titik Pilipino|accessdate=2010-12-16[/tpl] The video CD includes her official music videos from the album and her MTV Sessions Live performance. It was also released on digital download through iTunes and Amazon.com.[tpl]cite web|url=http://itunes.apple.com/us/album/cafe-bossa/id211162667|title=Cafe Bossa = Sitti|publisher=iTunes|accessdate=2010-12-16[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.amazon.com/dp/B001ESZPMA|title=Cafe Bossa = Sitti|publisher=Amazon.com|accessdate=2010-12-16[/tpl]
