[[Giada at Home]]

CATEGORIES: American cooking television series, Food Network series, 2008 American television series debuts, 2000s American television series

Giada at Home is a television show hosted by Giada De Laurentiis. It first aired on October 18, 2008 on the Food Network.[tpl]cite news | url = http://www.nypost.com/pagesixmag/issues/20080928/Sizzler+Giada+De+Laurentiis | title = The Sizzler: Giada De Laurentiis | first = Amy | last = Spencer | publisher = New York Post | date = 2008-09-28 | accessdate = 2008-10-07[/tpl]
The show was nominated for two Daytime Emmys in 2009: for Outstanding Culinary Program and Outstanding Directing in a Lifestyle/Culinary Program,[tpl]cite web|url=http://a330.g.akamai.net/7/330/2540/20090514014748/www.variety.com/graphics/photos/_storypics/daytime-emmys-2009.pdf|title=The 36th Annual Daytime Entertainment Emmy Award Nominations|date=The National Academy of Television Arts & Sciences|date=2009-05-14|accessdate=2009-05-19[/tpl] and won for the latter award.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.emmyonline.tv/mediacenter/day_0809_creative_winners.swf|title=Winners for the 36th Annual Daytime Entertainment Creative Arts Emmy Awards|date=2009-08-29|publisher=National Academy of Television Arts & Sciences|accessdate=2 January 2010[/tpl]
The show was nominated for four Daytime Emmys in 2010 for Outstanding Multi-Camera Editing, Outstanding Directing in a Lifestyle/Culinary Program, Outstanding Lifestyle/Culinary Host (Giada De Laurentiis) and Outstanding Culinary Program.  It won in the categories of Outstanding Directing in a Lifestyle/Culinary Program (2nd year in a row) and Snickle Snackle Culinary Program.

==Production==

The show moves beyond Italian cooking, and instead focuses on Giada cooking for friends and family, as well as party planning.[tpl]cite news | url = http://www.tcpalm.com/news/2008/sep/17/giada-move-beyond-italian/ | title = Giada to move beyond Italian | first = Bill | last = DeYoung | publisher = TC Palm | date = 2008-09-17 | accessdate = 2008-10-07[/tpl] Contrary to the title, the show is not taped at Giada's house.
