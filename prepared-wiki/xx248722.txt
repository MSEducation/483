[[Peter Dell the Elder]]

CATEGORIES: 1490 births, German artists, German sculptors, 1552 deaths

Peter Dell the Elder, or Peter Dell der Ältere, (1490–1552), was a German sculptor. 
Dell was born in Würzburg, and is father to another sculptor Peter Dell the Younger. He specialized in religious and portrait sculptures working with limewood and pearwood. Dell also did a number of church monuments out of stone.
