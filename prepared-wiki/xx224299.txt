[[Estela Giménez]]

CATEGORIES: 1979 births, Living people, Spanish rhythmic gymnasts, Olympic gold medalists for Spain, Gymnasts at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Olympic gymnasts of Spain, Spanish television presenters, Olympic medalists in gymnastics

Estela Giménez Cid (born 29 March 1979 in Madrid, Spain) is a Spanish former rhythmic gymnast who won a gold medal at the 1996 Summer Olympics in the group all-around event.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/gi/estela-gimenez-1.html |title=Estela Giménez |publisher=Sports Reference LLC |work=SR/Olympic Sports |accessdate=6 July 2011[/tpl] The team was formed by Giménez, Marta Baldó, Nuria Cabanillas, Lorena Guréndez, Estíbaliz Martínez and Tania Lamarca.
She featured on the cover of the Your Shape game for Wii endorsing it. She was the host of Insert Coin on AXN Spain from 2008 to 2012.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.cineytele.com/noticia.php?nid=24811 |title=Estela Giménez in Insert Coin |publisher=cineytele.com |accessdate=6 July 2011[/tpl]
