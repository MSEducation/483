[[Two (Charlotte Church EP)]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 EPs, Charlotte Church albums

TWO is the second extended play by Welsh recording artist Charlotte Church. It is the second in a series of five EPs released by Church. Her second alternative rock material, it was released on 4 March 2013 and is preceded by two singles "Glitterbombed" and "Lasts, or Eschaton".[tpl]cite web|author=Jason Lipshutz |url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/1548402/charlotte-church-talks-first-us-album-in-a-decade-listen-to-new-song|title=Charlotte Church Talks First U.S. Album in a Decade: Listen to New Song 'Glitterbombed' |publisher=Billboard Magazine |date=2013-02-19 |accessdate=2013-09-27[/tpl]
