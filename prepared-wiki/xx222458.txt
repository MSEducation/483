[[Bullaring, Western Australia]]

CATEGORIES: Wheatbelt (Western Australia), Grain receival points of Western Australia

Bullaring is a small town located in the Wheatbelt region of Western Australia.
The townsite was gazetted in 1914 and is situated along the railway line between Corrigin and Wickepin.
The name is Aboriginal in origin and comes from the name of a spring that is found close to town, but the meaning of the word is unknown.[tpl]LandInfo WA|c|B|2008-10-03[/tpl] 
The main industry in town is wheat farming with the town being a Cooperative Bulk Handling receival site.[tpl]cite web|url=https://www.cbh.com.au/media/120302/cbh%20receival%20sites%20-%20contact%20details.pdf|title=CBH Receival Sites - Contact Details|year=2010|accessdate=2013-03-31[/tpl]
