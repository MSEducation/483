[[Acharya Maharajshree Ayodhyaprasad Pande]]

CATEGORIES: Swaminarayan sect of Hinduism, 1809 births, 1868 deaths

Sanatan Dharma Dhurandar Acharya Maharaj Shree 1008 Ayodyaprasadji Maharaj (25 May 1809 - 18 Feb 1868) was the first Acharya of the NarNarayan Dev Gadi of the Swaminarayan Sampraday. He was the son of Rampratapji, Swaminarayan's elder brother.http://www.swaminarayan-oldham.org/history_sampraday.html He was the 1st successor of Swaminarayan in the North Diocese (NarNarayan Dev Gadi, Ahmedabad).[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.lordswaminarayan.org.uk/Dharmkul.htm | title=Dharmakul[/tpl] He was enthroned as Acharya on 10 Nov 1826 and held the post till his death.
His 200th birth anniversary was celebrated as the Dharmakul Vandana Mahotsav in Ahmedabad from the 1 to 3 June 2009. The event was attended by an estimated 600,000 people.[tpl]cite news|title=Devotees throng Dharmakul fest |url=http://www.dnaindia.com/report.asp?newsid=1261150|publisher=Daily News and Analysis|date= 2 June 2009|accessdate=4 June 2009[/tpl]
