[[2008 Team Speedway Junior Polish Championship]]

CATEGORIES: Team Speedway Junior Polish Championship, 2008 in Polish speedway

The 2008 Team Speedway Junior Polish Championship ([tpl]lang-pl|Młodzieżowe Drużynowe Mistrzostwa Polski[/tpl], MDMP) is the 2008 version of Team Speedway Junior Polish Championship organized by the Polish Motor Union (PZM). The Final will take place on 4 September 2008 in Alfred Smoczyk Stadium in Leszno. Championship was won by Unia Leszno. They beat defending champions RKM Rybnik and Złomrex Włókniarz Częstochowa.
