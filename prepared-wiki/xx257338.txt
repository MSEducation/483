[[Moto Esporte Clube]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Brazil, Association football clubs established in 1952, Sport in Rondônia, 1952 establishments in Brazil

Moto Esporte Clube, commonly known as Moto Clube, is a Brazilian football club based in Porto Velho, Rondônia state. They won the Campeonato Rondoniense ten times and the Torneio de Integração da Amazônia twice.

==History==

The club was founded on May 13, 1952.[tpl]cite book|title=Escudos dos Times do Mundo Inteiro|publisher=Panda Books|author=Rodolfo Rodrigues|year=2009|page=100[/tpl] Moto Clube won the Campeonato Rondoniense in 1954, 1968, 1969, 1971, 1972, 1975, 1976, 1977, 1980 and in 1981, and won the Torneio de Integração da Amazônia in 1977 and in 1978.
