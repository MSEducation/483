[[Haruko Tachiiri]]

CATEGORIES: Manga artists, Manga artists from Tokyo, Female comics artists, Female comics writers, Living people, People from Tokyo, 1949 births

[tpl]Nihongo|Haruko Tachiiri|たちいり ハルコ|Tachīri Haruko[/tpl] (born 7 May 1949 in Meguro, Tokyo, Japan) is a Japanese manga artist who writes mostly manga for children. In 1979, she received an Excellence Prize from the Japanese Cartoonists' Association for Picola-picola,[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.hahnlibrary.net/comics/awards/japancartoonist.shtml | author=Joel Hahn | title=Japanese Cartoonists' Association Award | work=Comic Book Awards Almanac | accessdate=2008-01-01 |archiveurl = http://web.archive.org/web/20071205034613/http://www.hahnlibrary.net/comics/awards/japancartoonist.shtml  |archivedate = 2007-12-05[/tpl] and in 1984 she received the Shogakukan Manga Award for children's manga for Panku Ponk.[tpl]cite web | url=http://comics.shogakukan.co.jp/mangasho/rist.html | title=小学館漫画賞：歴代受賞者 | publisher=Shogakukan | language=Japanese | accessdate=2007-08-19[/tpl]
