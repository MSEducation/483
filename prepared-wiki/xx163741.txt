[[List of diplomatic missions in Greece]]

CATEGORIES: Diplomatic missions in Greece, Greece-related lists, Lists of diplomatic missions by receiving country

This page lists embassies and consulates posted in Greece (as of July 2007). There are currently 85 embassies in Athens. In addition, there are diplomatic liaison offices of the Palestinian Authority and of the Republic of Macedonia (recognized by Greece under the provisional designation Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia), which do not have the full embassy status. Many other countries have non resident embassies (71)  and/or honorary consulates.
