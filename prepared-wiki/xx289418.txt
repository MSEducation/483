[[Astarud]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Astarud ([tpl]lang-fa|استرود[/tpl], also Romanized as Astarūd, Asterūd, and Esterūd)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3062440|Astarud[/tpl] is a village in Bizineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 933, in 201 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
