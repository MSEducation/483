"Faking Love" is a song written by Matraca Berg and Bobby Braddock, and recorded by American country music artists T.G. Sheppard and Karen Brooks as a duet.  It was released in October 1982 as the second from the album Perfect Stranger.  "Faking Love" was the only single T.G. Sheppard and Karen Brooks released as a duo.
