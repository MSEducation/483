[[337th Infantry Division (Wehrmacht)]]

CATEGORIES: Infantry divisions of Germany during World War II, Military units and formations established in 1940

The 337th Infantry Division (German: 337. Infanterie-Division) was a German division in World War II.  It was formed on 16 November 1940  in Kempten. The division was destroyed on the Eastern front in July 1944 and formed the stab of Divisionsgruppe 337 on 7 August 1944 which later was the basis of the 337. Volks-Grenadier-Division.
