[[1967 Kansas State Wildcats football team]]

CATEGORIES: Kansas State Wildcats football seasons, 1967 Big Eight Conference football season

The 1967 Kansas State Wildcats football team represented Kansas State University in the college football season of 1967.  The team's head football coach was Vince Gibson.  The Wildcats played their home games in Memorial Stadium.  1967 saw the Wildcats finish with a record of 1-9, and a 0-7 record in Big 8 play.  1967 would be the last season that the team would play at Memorial Stadium.  In 1968 the team would move to KSU Stadium.
