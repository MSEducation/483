[[George Edward Wales]]

CATEGORIES: 1792 births, 1860 deaths, Members of the United States House of Representatives from Vermont, Vermont National Republicans, Vermont Whigs, Vermont state court judges, Vermont lawyers, People from Hartford, Vermont, Members of the Vermont House of Representatives, Speakers of the Vermont House of Representatives, Burials in Connecticut

George Edward Wales (May 13, 1792 - January 8, 1860) was an American politician from Vermont. He served as a U.S. Representative from Vermont.

==Biography==

Wales was born in Westminster, Vermont and attended the common schools in Westminster.[tpl]cite book|last=Dodge|first=Prentiss Cutler|title=Encyclopedia, Vermont Biography: A Series of Authentic Biographical Sketches of the Representative Men of Vermont and Sons of Vermont in Other States. 1912|year=1912|publisher=Ullery Publishing Company|pages=70|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=tt2_3hTQxFMC&pg=PA70&dq=george+edward+wales+married+Amanda+Lathrop+Wales&hl=en&sa=X&ei=2MKuUK7jL-KC2AXOooCoDw&sqi=2&ved=0CDYQ6AEwAw#v=onepage&q=george%20edward%20wales%20married%20Amanda%20Lathrop%20Wales&f=false[/tpl] He studied law with Stephen R. Bradley in Westminster and with Titus Hutchinson (who later served as Chief Justice of the Vermont Supreme Court in Woodstock and was admitted to the bar in 1812.[tpl]cite book|last=United States Government Printing Office|title=Congressional serial set|year=1918|publisher=United States Government Printing Office|pages=1083|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=aMlTAAAAIAAJ&pg=PA1083&lpg=PA1083&dq=George+Edward+Wales+born+in+Westminster,+Vermont&source=bl&ots=Bq0kASAhfu&sig=p2N3YiWs_lRWHAV6L4P3Bm4VxTQ&hl=en&sa=X&ei=WLiuUNPmKIPRyQGes4GQBA&ved=0CFEQ6AEwBg#v=onepage&q=George%20Edward%20Wales%20born%20in%20Westminster%2C%20Vermont&f=false[/tpl] He moved to Hartford, Vermont in 1813 and began the practice of law. 
He served as clerk and treasurer of the White River Bridge Company from 1818 until 1825.[tpl]cite book|last=Ellis|first=William Arba|title=Norwich University, 1819-1911; Her History, Her Graduates, Her Roll of Honor|year=1911|publisher=Capital City Press|pages=19|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=qFhMAAAAMAAJ&pg=PA19&lpg=PA19&dq=George+Edward+Wales+born+in+Westminster,+Vermont&source=bl&ots=dun_YmoiOF&sig=nIWcsIYxRAFRzg_UcKaUpF9AFXM&hl=en&sa=X&ei=WLiuUNPmKIPRyQGes4GQBA&ved=0CEEQ6AEwAw#v=onepage&q=George%20Edward%20Wales%20born%20in%20Westminster%2C%20Vermont&f=false[/tpl] He was a member of the Vermont House of Representatives from 1822 until 1824, and served as Speaker in 1822, 1823 and 1824.[tpl]cite book|title=Vermont History|year=1921|pages=154|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=ACw7AAAAIAAJ&pg=PA153&lpg=PA153&dq=George+Edward+Wales+born+in+Westminster,+Vermont&source=bl&ots=VFOSXqxX9f&sig=rvA_uOy4segyHjj8Z4NcsZZK44s&hl=en&sa=X&ei=WLiuUNPmKIPRyQGes4GQBA&ved=0CFMQ6AEwBw#v=onepage&q=George%20Edward%20Wales&f=false[/tpl] Wales earned honorary degrees from Dartmouth College in 1823 and the University of Vermont in 1825.[tpl]cite book|last=Tucker|first=William Howard|title=History of Hartford, Vermont, July 4, 1761-April 4, 1889: The First Town on the New Hampshire Grants Chartered After the Close of the French War|year=1889|publisher=Free Press Association|pages=384|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=qTklAAAAMAAJ&pg=PA384&lpg=PA384&dq=George+Edward+Wales+born+in+Westminster,+Vermont&source=bl&ots=grgrdxBler&sig=FI0IXsIBNJIoin28vD2N0s059z8&hl=en&sa=X&ei=WLiuUNPmKIPRyQGes4GQBA&ved=0CD8Q6AEwAg#v=onepage&q=George%20Edward%20Wales%20born%20in%20Westminster%2C%20Vermont&f=false[/tpl]   
Wales was elected to the United States House of Representatives as a National Republican and served in the Nineteenth and Twentieth Congresses, March 4, 1825 to March 3, 1829.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.govtrack.us/congress/members/george_wales/411222/| title= Rep. George Wales|publisher= Govtrack.us|accessdate= November 21, 2012[/tpl] He was an unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1828 to the Twenty-First Congress.
After leaving Congress he resumed the practice of law.
He later joined the Whig Party.  Wales was elected town clerk in Hartford in 1840, and served in that position until his death.[tpl]cite book|last=Vermont Historical Society|title=Proceedings of the Vermont Historical Society|year=1921|publisher=Vermont Historical Society|pages=154|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=UFgSAAAAYAAJ&pg=PA153&lpg=PA153&dq=George+Edward+Wales+born+in+Westminster,+Vermont&source=bl&ots=ZVNVbtCEg9&sig=-TL3AfnIb51fhrosbbwgJ2c9e8w&hl=en&sa=X&ei=WLiuUNPmKIPRyQGes4GQBA&ved=0CFUQ6AEwCA#v=onepage&q=George%20Edward%20Wales%20born%20in%20Westminster%2C%20Vermont&f=false[/tpl]  From 1847 to 1850 he was Judge of the Probate Court for the district which included Windsor County.
Wales was a trustee of Norwich University from 1845 until 1857.[tpl]cite book|last=Ellis|first=William Arba|title=Norwich University, 1819-1911; Her History, Her Graduates, Her Roll of Honor|year=1911|publisher=Capital City Press|pages=19|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=qFhMAAAAMAAJ&pg=PA19&lpg=PA19&dq=George+Edward+Wales+born+in+Westminster,+Vermont&source=bl&ots=dun_YmoiOF&sig=nIWcsIYxRAFRzg_UcKaUpF9AFXM&hl=en&sa=X&ei=WLiuUNPmKIPRyQGes4GQBA&ved=0CEEQ6AEwAw#v=onepage&q=George%20Edward%20Wales%20born%20in%20Westminster%2C%20Vermont&f=false[/tpl] Wales served as judge of probate for the Hartford district from 1847 until 1850.

==Personal life==

Wales married Amanda Lathrop Wales in 1813, and they had seven children.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.findagrave.com/cgi-bin/fg.cgi?page=gr&GRid=58053782/| title= Amanda Lathrop Wales|publisher= Find A Grave|accessdate= November 21, 2012[/tpl]
Wales joined the Free and Accepted Masons of the State of Vermont in 1812, and was the state Grand Master from 1825 to 1827.[tpl]cite book|last=Tucker|first=William Howard|title=History of Hartford, Vermont, July 4, 1761-April 4, 1889: The First Town on the New Hampshire Grants Chartered After the Close of the French War|year=1889|publisher=Free Press Association|pages=384|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=qTklAAAAMAAJ&pg=PA384&lpg=PA384&dq=George+Edward+Wales+born+in+Westminster,+Vermont&source=bl&ots=grgrdxBler&sig=FI0IXsIBNJIoin28vD2N0s059z8&hl=en&sa=X&ei=WLiuUNPmKIPRyQGes4GQBA&ved=0CD8Q6AEwAg#v=onepage&q=George%20Edward%20Wales%20born%20in%20Westminster%2C%20Vermont&f=false[/tpl]

==Death==

Wales died on January 8, 1860 in Hartford, and was interred in Hartford Point Cemetery.[tpl]cite web|url= http://www.findagrave.com/cgi-bin/fg.cgi?page=gr&GRid=19734381| title= George E. Wales|publisher= Find A Grave|accessdate= November 21, 2012[/tpl]
