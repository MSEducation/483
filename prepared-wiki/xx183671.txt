[[Mi Verdad]]

CATEGORIES: 1999 albums, Alejandro Fernández albums, Latin Grammy Award for Best Ranchero Album

Mi Verdad is the seventh album recorded by Mexican singer Alejandro Fernández. Produced by Pedro Ramírez, this album marks the return to the genre that opened the doors him for the success. Videos were created for the songs "Loco" and "Nadie Simplemente Nadie". The songs "Loco", "Nadie Simplemente Nadie" and "Si He Sabido Amor" were the most popular tracks.[tpl]Citation needed|date=July 2008[/tpl] It received a nomination for a Grammy Award for Best Mexican/Mexican-American Album.
