[[Ali Hussein Shihab]]

CATEGORIES: 1961 births, Iraqi footballers, Iraq international footballers, Association football midfielders, Talaba SC players, 1986 FIFA World Cup players, Living people

Ali Hussein Shihab (born 5 May 1961) is an Iraqi football midfielder who played for Iraq in the 1986 FIFA World Cup.1986 FIFA World Cup Mexico He also played for Talaba SC.
He played in all 3 games in the 1986 World Cup lining up against Paraguay, Belgium and hosts Mexico. The midfielder also played in all three games in the 1984 Olympics against Canada, Cameroon and Yugoslavia. 
Ali scored the second goal in the last game against the Yugoslavians to help Iraq to a 2-0 lead before the half-time interval, a win would have given Iraq a chance of qualifying for the second round but the Eastern Europeans produced a marvelous come back from the 2-0 deficit to score four goals to win the game. Ali made his international debut in 1984 in the 4-0 win over Guinea.
