[[Hingis–V. Williams rivalry]]

CATEGORIES: Tennis rivalries

The tennis players Venus Williams (United States) and Martina Hingis (Switzerland) met 21 times during their careers. Their overall head-to-head series is 11–10, in Hingis' favor.
