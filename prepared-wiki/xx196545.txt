[[1957 Rose Bowl]]

CATEGORIES: 1956–57 NCAA football bowl games, Rose Bowl, Iowa Hawkeyes football bowl games, Oregon State Beavers football bowl games

The 1957 Rose Bowl was a college football bowl game played on January 1, 1957.  In the game, the 43rd Rose Bowl, the Iowa Hawkeyes defeated the Oregon State Beavers by a score of 35–19. Hawkeyes quarterback Ken Ploen was named the Rose Bowl Player of the Game.

==Game summary==

Ken Ploen, the Iowa quarterback, completed nine of 10 passes and rushed for 59 yards, including a 49-yard run for the Hawkeyes' first score. Collins Hagler scored twice for the Hawkeyes, the second on a 66-yard run.
