[[Sliedrecht Sport]]

CATEGORIES: Sports clubs established in 1956, Dutch volleyball clubs, Sport in South Holland

Sliedrecht Sport is a volleyball club from Sliedrecht, located in Zuid-Holland, the Netherlands. It is one of the largest volleyball clubs in the Netherlands, which was started in 1956 and has over 500 members. 

The first female team competes in the A-league, the highest level in the Netherlands, the first male team competes in the B-League, the second highest level.
