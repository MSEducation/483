[[Kilchrist Castle]]

CATEGORIES: Castles in Argyll and Bute

Kilchrist Castle is a castle south west of Campbeltown, Argyll and Bute.[tpl]cite web |url=http://canmore.rcahms.gov.uk/en/site/145827/details/kilchrist+castle/ |title=Kilchrist Castle |publisher=Royal Commission on the Ancient and Historical Monuments of Scotland |work=CANMORE |accessdate=26 September 2011[/tpl] The castle was rebuilt in 1834 by Dugald McTavish, and is 3 storeys high with a corbelled parapet.[tpl]cite web |url=http://kilchristcastle.com/History.htm |title=Kilchrest Castle History|publisher= |work= |accessdate=26 September 2011[/tpl] The vaulted foundations of the castle are of unknown date.
