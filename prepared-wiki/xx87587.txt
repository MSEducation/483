[[Prahran railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1860

Prahran railway station is located on the Sandringham line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the south-eastern Melbourne suburb of Prahran opening on 22 December 1860 as Greville Street, being renamed Prahran on 1 January 1867.Prahran Vicsig

==Platforms & services==

Prahran has two side platforms. It is serviced by Metro Trains Sandringham line services travelling between Flinders Street and Sandringham.Sandringham Line timetable Public Transport Victoria
