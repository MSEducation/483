[[Jean Deysel]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 births, Living people, South African rugby union players, South Africa international rugby union players, Golden Lions players, Sharks (rugby union) players, Natal Sharks players, White South African people, Afrikaner people

Jean Deysel (born 5 March 1985 in Virginia, Free State, South Africa) is a rugby union player for the Sharks in the Super Rugby competition. He plays number 7. Deysel is known for his bruising running with ball in hand, often "bumping" the first defender.
Deysel signed a two-year contract to join Japanese side Toyota Verblitz at the conclusion of the 2014 Super Rugby season.[tpl]cite press release | url=http://www.sharksrugby.co.za/news#article2404184 | title=Deysel signs in Japan | publisher=Sharks | date=16 April 2014 | accessdate=16 April 2014[/tpl]
