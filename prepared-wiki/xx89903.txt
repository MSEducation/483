Dormammu is apparently stronger in the Dark Dimension, being empowered by the worship of his followers, and can draw upon its power. The character has been shown to have one significant weakness: being vulnerable in environments that cannot fuel his mystical Faltine flames.

==Other versions==

===Marvel Mangaverse===

Dormammu is summoned into Marvel Mangaverse by The Incredible Hulk (Nick Fury is revealed to be behind the attacks by the Hulk as well).Marvel Mangaverse: Avengers Assemble!! #1-4 (2002)

===Ultimate Marvel===

In the Ultimate Marvel universe, Dormammu first appears in Ultimatum introduces him as a powerful demon who succeeds in killing Doctor Strange (actually the son of the original).Ultimatum #1-5 (September 2009) The demon was then confronted by Susan Storm and Ben Grimm in front of the destroyed Sanctum Sanctorum. Dormammu was then defeated after Susan sealed his entire head with her force-field and being overwhelmed by Johnny Storm's power, turning him into a powerless human being.Ultimatum: Fantastic Four Requiem #1 (2009)
