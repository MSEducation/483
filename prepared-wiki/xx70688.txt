[[King Si of Zhou]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing, 441 BC deaths, Zhou Dynasty kings, 5th-century BC rulers

King Si of Zhou ([tpl]zh|c=周思王|p=Zhōu Sī Wáng[/tpl]) was the twenty-ninth king of the Chinese Zhou Dynasty and the seventeenth of Eastern Zhou.[tpl]cite web|title=The History of China|url=http://books.google.com.np/books?id=69EbKf6JrxYC&pg=PA663&lpg=PA663&dq=King+Si+of+Zhou&source=bl&ots=f6gBhKQ66h&sig=gyTrPbZ8_mufWoKBvYSj02TYQU8&hl=en&sa=X&ei=d5DtUNSwN9DwlAWqwoCQBw&redir_esc=y#v=onepage&q=King%20Si%20of%20Zhou&f=false|publisher=Google Books|accessdate=9 January 2013[/tpl] 
He gained the throne in 441 by killing his older brother King Ai of Zhou, but was himself killed by his younger brother King Kao of Zhou after only five months of rule.Family tree of Chinese kings
