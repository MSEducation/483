[[Gmina Wyśmierzyce]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Białobrzegi County

Gmina Wyśmierzyce is an urban-rural gmina (administrative district) in Białobrzegi County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the town of Wyśmierzyce, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|10|km|mi|0[/tpl] west of Białobrzegi and [tpl]convert|67|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|104.31|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 2,897 (out of which the population of Wyśmierzyce amounts to 889, and the population of the rural part of the gmina is 2,008).

==Villages==

Apart from the town of Wyśmierzyce, Gmina Wyśmierzyce contains the villages and settlements of Brodek, Górki, Grzmiąca, Jabłonna, Jeruzal, Kiedrzyn, Klamy, Korzeń, Kostrzyn, Kozłów, Kożuchów, Olszowe, Paprotno, Redlin, Romanów, Ulaski Grzmiąckie, Ulaski Stamirowskie, Witaszyn and Wólka Kożuchowska.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Wyśmierzyce is bordered by the gminas of Białobrzegi, Klwów, Mogielnica, Nowe Miasto nad Pilicą, Potworów, Promna and Radzanów.
