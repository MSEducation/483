[[Dorchester Academy]]

CATEGORIES: National Historic Landmarks in Georgia (U.S. state), Buildings and structures completed in 1935, African-American museums in Georgia (U.S. state), Museums in Liberty County, Georgia, Education museums in the United States, History museums in Georgia (U.S. state)

Dorchester Academy near Midway, Georgia is a historic site that is listed, in 2009, as one of the top 11 U.S. most endangered historic sites.11 sites make new list of 'endangered historic places', at CNN.com  The Dorchester Academy Boys' Dormitory on the site was built in 1935, was listed on the U.S. National Register of Historic Places in 1986, and was declared a U.S. National Historic Landmark in 2006.[tpl]citation|title=[tpl]PDFlink|National Historic Landmark Nomination: Dorchester Academy Boys' Dormitory / Dorchester Center|32Â KB[/tpl]|date=February 09, 2006 |author=Caridad de la Vega, Susan Cianci Salvatore |publisher=National Park Service[/tpl]
The dormitory historic site is significant for its association with the Southern Christian Leadership's Citizen Education Program.  The program worked toward attaining equality for blacks in the south by teaching them their rights and helping them acquire the knowledge necessary to become registered voters by passing the required test.
