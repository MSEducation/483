[[1962 St. Louis Cardinals season]]

CATEGORIES: St. Louis Cardinals seasons, 1962 Major League Baseball season

The 1962 St. Louis Cardinals season was the team's 81st season in St. Louis, Missouri and its 71st season in the National League. The Cardinals went 84-78 during the season and finished 6th in the NL, 17Â½ games behind the San Francisco Giants.
