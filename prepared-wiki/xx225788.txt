[[Motorola Marco]]

CATEGORIES: Motorola products, Personal digital assistants

The Motorola Marco was a Newton OS-based personal digital assistant from Motorola, launched in January 1995 at MacWorld.Original Marco Launch Press Release It was unique in that it included a compatible RadioMail and ARDIS network radio antenna which allowed users to check and send e-mail as well as receive and send text messages.
In 2007, Motorola dusted off the Marco moniker and used it as the name for a new cell phone line.Engadget Marco Brings Wifi to the Z3
