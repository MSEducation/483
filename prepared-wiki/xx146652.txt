[[Mindarie, South Australia]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in South Australia

Mindarie is a small town in the Murray Mallee of South Australia between Karoonda and Loxton. The town was gazetted in 1912. The name Mindarie is believed to originate from the Dieri Aboriginal language, and possibly means "festival to invoke peace".
Mindarie is home to the Murray Zircon heavy mineral sands mine.Mines & Developing Projects  /  Approved Mines  / Mindarie
It is also home to the East Murray Area School.http://www.sa.gov.au/school/0102
Together with nearby Halidon, it hosts the Mindarie-Halidon Races in September each year, which is known as the Melbourne Cup of the Mallee.Mindarie - Halidon
