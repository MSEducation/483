[[Dovogame]]

CATEGORIES: Video game companies of the United States

Dovogame is a publisher and developer of online browser games. dovogame.com is a gaming entertaining platform developed by it.Dovogame.
It currently holds more than 30,000,000 players from around the world. Their browser game Business Tycoon Online has achieved great success in US market.Dovogame extends its business by partnering with Co-publishers on BTO.
