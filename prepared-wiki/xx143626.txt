[[Mikulášová]]

CATEGORIES: Villages and municipalities in Bardejov District

Mikulášová is a village and municipality in Bardejov District in the Prešov Region of north-east Slovakia.

==History==

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1414

==Geography==

The municipality lies at an altitude of 350 metres and covers an area of 7.997 km².
It has a population of about 145 people.
