[[Second Lord of the Treasury]]

CATEGORIES: Current ministerial offices in the United Kingdom, Lists of government ministers of the United Kingdom

[tpl]PoliticsUK[/tpl]
The Second Lord of the Treasury is a member of the commission exercising the ancient office of Lord High Treasurer in the United Kingdom.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/cabinetpapers/cabinet-gov/great-offices-of-state.htm|title=The Cabinet Papers - Great Offices of State|work=National Archives|accessdate=21 December 2010[/tpl] Since 1827, the Chancellor of the Exchequer has always simultaneously held the office of Second Lord of the Treasury when he has not also been the Prime Minister. The official residence of the Second Lord of The Treasury is 11 Downing Street.
