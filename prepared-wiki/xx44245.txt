[[Patchacan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Corozal District

Patchacan is a town in Corozal District, in northern Belize.http://belize.mybelize.net/index.php?section=35http://www.belmopancityonline.com/belize-districts.aspx  According to the 2000 census, it had a population of 1,142 people.
