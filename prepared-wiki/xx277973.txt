[[2009 UEC European Track Championships – Women's under-23 scratch]]

CATEGORIES: European Track Championships, Cycle racing in Belarus, European Track Championships – U23 Women's scratch

The U23 Women's scratch was one of the 8 women's under-23 events at the 2009 European Track Championships, held in Minsk, Belarus. It took place on 19 July 2009. 22 participated in the race.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.uci.infostradasports.com/asp/lib/TheASP.asp?PageID=19006&SportID=305&CompetitionID=15755&EditionID=561620&SeasonID=477&ClassID=101&GenderID=2&EventID=9548&EventPhaseID=0&Phase1ID=0&Phase2ID=0&Phase3ID=0&PhaseClassificationID=-1&Detail=1&Ranking=0&All=0&TaalCode=2&StyleID=0&Cache=8|title=Women   Under 23   Championnats d'Europe Piste (BLR/CC)   Scratch|publisher=UCI|accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cyclingnews.com/races/u23-junior-european-track-championships-cc/u23-women/results|title=U23/Junior European Track Championships 2009|publisher=Cyclingnews.com|date=30 July 2009|accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl]
Ellen van Dijk, who won the European title in 2008 and was still under-23, did not defended her title.

==Competition format==

A scratch race is a race in which all riders start together and the object is simply to be first over the finish line after a certain number of laps. There are no intermediate points or sprints.
