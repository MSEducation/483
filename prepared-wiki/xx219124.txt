[[August Weber]]

CATEGORIES: 19th-century German painters, 1817 births, 1873 deaths

August Weber (1817–1873) was a German painter.  He is known for his forest scenes and is a product of the Dusseldorf school.Outlines of the History of Art, by Wilhelm Lübke and Russell Sturgis, published by Dodd, Mead & company, 1904
