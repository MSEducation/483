[[Chris Polian]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, Indianapolis Colts executives, National Football League general managers

Chris Polian is an American football executive. He was the vice president and general manager of the Indianapolis Colts of the National Football League from 2009 to 2011.
On November 27, 2009, he was promoted by Colts owner Jim Irsay to his position. He had previously served in other roles in the Colts football operations.
