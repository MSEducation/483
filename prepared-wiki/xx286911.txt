[[Qareh Dowrakhlu-ye Sofla]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Qareh Dowrakhlu-ye Sofla ([tpl]lang-fa|قره دوراخلوسفلي[/tpl], also Romanized as Qareh Dowrākhlū-ye Soflá; also known as Qara Dūlāglū, Qarah Dūlākhlū, Qareh Delākhlū, Qareh Dolākhlū, Qareh Dowrākhlū-ye Pā’īn, Qareh Dūlākhlū, Qareh Dūlākhlū-ye Pā’īn, and Qareh Dūrākhlū)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3079689|Qareh Dowrakhlu-ye Sofla[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 37, in 11 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
