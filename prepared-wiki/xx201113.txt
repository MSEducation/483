Fabinho Capixaba, realname Antonio Fábio Cavalcante (born November 27, 1983 in Vitória), is a Brazilian full back. He currently plays for Joinville.
He debuted for Avaí Futebol Clube on August 15, 2009 by substituting Emerson Santos da Silva in the 73rd minute.
Week later he scored a goal in a 3-0 crashing of famous Brazilian club Clube de Regatas do Flamengo.
He came on the field in 72 minute and scored five minutes later.
