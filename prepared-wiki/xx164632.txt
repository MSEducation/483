[[Takes Two to Tango (song)]]

CATEGORIES: 1952 songs, Songs written by Al Hoffman, Songs written by Dick Manning, Louis Armstrong songs

"Takes Two to Tango" is a popular song written by Al Hoffman and Dick Manning and published in 1952.
Two versions of the song, by Pearl Bailey and by Louis Armstrong, charted in 1952.
The recording by Pearl Bailey was released by Coral Records as catalog number 60817. It first reached the Billboard magazine Best Seller chart on September 19, 1952, and lasted 17 weeks on the chart, peaking at #7.Whitburn, Joel. (1973). Top Pop Records 1940-1955, p. __.
The recording by Louis Armstrong was released by Decca Records as catalog number 28394. It reached the Billboard magazine Best Seller chart on October 17, 1952, at #28; this was its only week on the chart. 
The recording by Lester Young was released by Verve Records. This was the only song he sang in and it is made up lyrics. It was featured as a bonus track on the album Lester Young with the Oscar Peterson Trio.
