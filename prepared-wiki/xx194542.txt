[[United Nations Security Council Resolution 354]]

CATEGORIES: 1974 United Nations Security Council resolutions, United Nations Security Council resolutions concerning Cyprus, Turkish invasion of Cyprus, United Nations Security Council resolutions concerning Turkey

United Nations Security Council Resolution 354, adopted unanimously on July 23, 1974, was a brief resolution, it reaffirmed the provisions of resolution 353 and demanded that all parties to the fighting in Cyprus immediately comply with 353 and cease firing.  The Resolution also requested all states to refrain from any action which might further aggravate the situation.
