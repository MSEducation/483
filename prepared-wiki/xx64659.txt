</ref> Such an event may cause a small incremental offset of the rudder trim, according to the aircraft manufacturer. The Air Accidents Investigations Board ([[Air Accidents Investigation Branch|AAIB]]) subsequently issued Safety Recommendation 2010-092, recommending that Airbus alert all operators of A320 series aircraft of the possibility that an electrical malfunction may not be clearly annunciated on the [[Electronic Centralised Aircraft Monitor]]ing system (ECAM) and that such an event may lead to an uncommanded input of rudder trim.<ref>
