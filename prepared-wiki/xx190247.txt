[[KXAZ]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Arizona, Classic hits radio stations in the United States, Media in Coconino County, Arizona, Radio stations established in 1981

KXAZ (93.3 FM, "Z-93.3 FM") is a radio station licensed to serve Page, Arizona, USA.  The station is owned by Lake Powell Communications. It airs a classic hits music format.[tpl]cite web |title=Station Information Profile |work=Arbitron |url=http://www.arbitron.com/[/tpl]
The station was assigned the KXAZ call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on November 2, 1981.[tpl]cite web |title=Call Sign History |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=36340&Callsign=KXAZ |work=FCC Media Bureau CDBS Public Access Database[/tpl]

==History==

On April 2, 2012 KXAZ changed their format from adult contemporary to classic hits.
