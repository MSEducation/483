[[Claus Biederstaedt]]

CATEGORIES: 1928 births, Living people, German male television actors, German male voice actors, People from the Province of Pomerania, People from Stargard Szczeciński, German male film actors

Claus Biederstaedt (born 28 June 1928 in Stargard (today Stargard Szczeciński, Poland)) is a German actor and voice actor.  He studied in Hamburg and began his career working with Joseph Offenbach.  Among the actors for whom he has dubbed have been Yves Montand, Peter Falk, Marlon Brando, Vittorio Gassman, and James Garner.
