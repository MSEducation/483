[[Jonti]]

CATEGORIES: Australian electronic musicians, Living people, Date of birth missing (living people)

Jonti is the stage name of Australian electronic musician and producer Jonti Danilewitz. He was born in Johannesburg, South Africa, but emigrated to Australia when he was 13.[tpl]cite web|last=Rachael|first=Angres|title=Interview: Jonti|url=http://www.heavemedia.com/2012/08/23/interview-jonti/|publisher=Heave Media|accessdate=9 August 2013|date=August 23, 2012[/tpl] He currently resides in Los Angeles, California, United States, and is signed with Los Angeles-based label Stones Throw Records.[tpl]cite web|last=Banuelos|first=Jon|title=Jonti|url=http://www.stonesthrow.com/jonti|publisher=Stones Throw Records|accessdate=9 August 2013|year=2012[/tpl]
