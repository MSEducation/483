[[Evgeni Yordanov (athlete)]]

CATEGORIES: 1940 births, Living people, Bulgarian high jumpers, Olympic athletes of Bulgaria, Athletes (track and field) at the 1964 Summer Olympics

Evgeni Yotov Yordanov ([tpl]lang-bg|Евгени Йотов Йорданов[/tpl], born May 5, 1940) is a Bulgarian track and field athlete who competed in the 1964 Summer Olympics.
In 1964 he finished twelfth in the high jump event.
