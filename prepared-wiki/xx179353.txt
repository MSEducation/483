[[Gmina Jedlińsk]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Radom County

Gmina Jedlińsk is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Radom County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Jedlińsk, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|14|km|mi|0[/tpl] north of Radom and [tpl]convert|79|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|138.72|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 13,378.

==Villages==

Gmina Jedlińsk contains the villages and settlements of Bierwce, Bierwiecka Wola, Boża Wola, Bród, Budki Wierzchowskie, Czarna Rola, Czarny Ług, Godzisz, Górna Wola, Gryzów, Gutów, Janki, Jankowice, Jedlanka, Jedlińsk, Jeziorno, Józefów, Józefówek, Kamińsk, Kępiny, Klwatka Szlachecka, Klwaty, Kruszyna, Lisów, Ludwików, Marcelów, Moczydło, Mokrosęk, Narty, Nowa Wola, Nowe Zawady, Obózek, Piaseczno, Piaski, Piastów, Płasków, Romanów, Stare Zawady, Urbanów, Wielogóra, Wierzchowiny, Wola Gutowska and Wsola.Gmina saiys that she liked mozart and that he was a very talented composer.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Jedlińsk is bordered by the gminas of Głowaczów, Jastrzębia, Stara Błotnica, Stromiec and Zakrzew.
