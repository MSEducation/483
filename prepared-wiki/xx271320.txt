[[Kallista on ja halvalla menee]]

CATEGORIES: 1988 albums, Eero Koivistoinen albums

Kallista on ja halvalla menee is a 1988 studio album by Eero Koivistoinen. The album consists of Koivistoinen's music to texts from well-known Finnish poets and lyricists. Vocal performances are by Maarit Hurmerinta, Sakari Kuosmanen and Pepe Ahlqvist.

==Tracks==

Composed and arranged by Eero Koivistoinen.
