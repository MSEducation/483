[[2013 NBT Cup (women)]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 domestic association football cups, 2013 in Tuvalu

The 2013 NBT Cup (women) (National Bank of Tuvalu Cup) is the first edition of national football knockout tournament, known as the NBT Cup. The tournament beginning in 27 July 2013 and finishes in 24 August 2013. Five clubs participated.
