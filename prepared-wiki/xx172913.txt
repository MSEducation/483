[[Lech Jęczmyk]]

CATEGORIES: 1936 births, Living people, Polish critics, Polish essayists, Polish translators, Recipients of the Silver Medal for Merit to Culture – Gloria Artis

Lech Jęczmyk (January 10, 1936) is a Polish publicist, essayist, writer and translator. Critic of science-fiction, chief editor of Nowa Fantastyka from 1990–1992, editor of several science-fiction series and anthologies.
