[[Salina, Pennsylvania]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Westmoreland County, Pennsylvania, Unincorporated communities in Pennsylvania

Salina is an unincorporated community in northern Westmoreland County, Pennsylvania, United States.  Located near Route 819, it lies near communities such as Tinsmill and Avonmore. Salina also is located near Kiski Area School District's Elementary School, Bell-Avon. Salina also hosted a salt mine, years a go.
