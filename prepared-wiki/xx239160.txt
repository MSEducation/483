[[Conor Maloney]]

CATEGORIES: 1974 births, Canoeists at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Irish canoeists, Living people, Olympic canoeists of Ireland

Conor Maloney (born February 8, 1974) is an Irish sprint canoer who has competed in the mid-1990s. At the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta, he was eliminated in the repechages of both the K-2 500 m and the K-2 1000 m event.
