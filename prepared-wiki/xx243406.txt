[[Cryptobasidiaceae]]

CATEGORIES: Ustilaginomycotina, Fungal plant pathogens and diseases, Fungi families

The Cryptobasidiaceae are a family of fungi in the Basidiomycota, Exobasidiales order. Species in the family have a widespread distribution, especially in neotropical areas. Members of the Cryptobasidiaceae are plant pathogens that grow parasitically on the leaves, stems and fruits of plants, especially those in the Lauraceae family.[tpl]cite book |author=Cannon PF, Kirk PM. |title=Fungal Families of the World |publisher=CABI|location=Wallingford |year=2007 |pages=92â€“93 |isbn=0-85199-827-5[/tpl]
