[[Stealing Chairs]]

CATEGORIES: 2002 albums, 28 Days albums, English-language albums

Stealing Chairs is the third album by 28 Days, released on September 17, 2002.
Stealing Chairs was named for a favourite pastime of their late drummer, Scott Murray.
