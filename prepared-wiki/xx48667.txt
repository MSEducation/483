[[Røa]]

CATEGORIES: Neighbourhoods of Oslo

Røa was a borough of the city of Oslo, Norway up to January 1, 2004, when it became part of the Vestre Aker district. 
Røa is also a suburb of Oslo, located approximately 7 kilometers away from downtown Oslo. It primarily consists of duplex houses and apartment blocks as well as single-family detached homes.  It also has a small shopping mall.
