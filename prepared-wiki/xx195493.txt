[[Ron Snell]]

CATEGORIES: 1948 births, Living people, People from Regina, Saskatchewan, Pittsburgh Penguins draft picks, Pittsburgh Penguins players, Baltimore Clippers players, Buffalo Norsemen players, Hershey Bears players, Rochester Americans players, Winnipeg Jets (WHA) players

Ronald Wayne Snell (born August 11, 1948) is a Canadian ice hockey player. He was drafted by the Pittsburgh Penguins in the second round, fourteenth overall, in the 1968 NHL Amateur Draft. He played seven games in the NHL, all for the Pittsburgh Penguins, during the 68-69 and 69-70 seasons.
