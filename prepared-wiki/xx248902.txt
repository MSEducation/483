[[Champagne no Koi]]

CATEGORIES: 2004 singles, Zetima Records singles, Song recordings produced by Tsunku

[tpl]nihongo|"Champagne no Koi"|シャンパンの恋|Champagne Love[/tpl] is the thirteenth single by Japanese girl group Melon Kinenbi. It was released on October 27th 2004, and its highest position on the Oricon weekly chart was #25.
