[[Matrak]]

CATEGORIES: Turkish games, Lawn games, Turkish words and phrases, Games of physical skill, Combat sports

Matrak is an Ottoman game invented by Bosniak Matrakçı Nasuh in the 16th century. It is played with wooden sticks covered with leather. The top of the sticks are rounded and slightly wider than the body resembling bowling pins. The game is a kind of combat simulation, and is played on a lawn. It was used by Ottoman soldiers practice melee combat.
In the television series Muhteşem Yüzyıl, it is shown as a combat-game.
