CATEGORIES: Swimming at the 1984 Summer Olympics

The final of the Men's 1500 metres Freestyle event at the 1984 Summer Olympics was held in the McDonald's Olympic Swim Stadium in Los Angeles, California, on August 4, 1984. The fastest eight qualified for the final.
