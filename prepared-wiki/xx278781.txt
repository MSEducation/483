[[Zarrik]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Zarrik ([tpl]lang-fa|زريك[/tpl], also Romanized as Zarrīk and Zarīk)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3089139|Zarrik[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 19, in 5 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
