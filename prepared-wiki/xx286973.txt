[[Golijeh, Ijrud]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Golijeh ([tpl]lang-fa|گليجه[/tpl], also Romanized as Golījeh and Gollījeh; also known as Goljeh, Gullejeh, Gulludzhekh, and Kollījeh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3064655|Golijeh[/tpl] is a village in Golabar Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 34, in 7 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
