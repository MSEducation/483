[[National road 4 (Poland)]]

CATEGORIES: National roads in Poland

National road 4 ([tpl]lang-pl|Droga krajowa nr 4[/tpl]) is a route belonging to the Polish national road network. The highway connects the southern regions of Poland. It runs from Zgorzelec at the German border to Korczowa at the Ukrainian border, and most of it is the A4 motorway, the contemporary route commences at Targowisko. National road 4 is a component of European highway E40.
With the completion of the A4 motorway scheduled for 2012, the rest of current route will be renumbered as national route 94.
