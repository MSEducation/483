[[Stickland]]

CATEGORIES: English-language surnames

The family surname of Stickland is West Saxon (Wessex) in origin and comes from the English county of Dorset.http://www.surnamedb.com/Surname/Stickland
The name appears to centre on the village of the same name in the Parish of Winterborne Stickland which is recorded in Saxon records
