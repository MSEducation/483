[[Plodder Lane railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Disused railway stations in Bolton, Former London and North Western Railway stations, Railway stations opened in 1875, Railway stations closed in 1954

Plodder Lane railway station served the southern part of Bolton and the western part of Farnworth; it was in the Highfield district of the latter town, on the southern side of a road named Plodder Lane, the present B6199. It was on the London and North Western Railway route between Bolton and Manchester.

Philip |title=British Railways Pre-Grouping Atlas and Gazetteer |edition=5th |date=January 1976 |publisher=Ian Allan |location=Shepperton |isbn=0-7110-0320-3 |id=EX/0176 |at=p. 45, section C2 |ref=harv [/tpl]
The station closed on 29 March 1954.
