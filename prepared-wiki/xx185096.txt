Sérgio Mota Mello (born November 16, 1989 in São José dos Campos), is a Brazilian attacking midfielder who currently plays for Botafogo-SP.
Made 1st team debut for São Paulo in a 0-2 away defeat to Juventude in the Campeonato Brasileiro on November 8, 2007.[tpl]cite web|url=http://noticias.uol.com.br/pelenet/revista/ult1334u1343.jhtm|title=Aspirante a craque, Sérgio Mota adota cautela contra euforia|publisher=noticias.uol.com.br|accessdate=2008-01-03|date=2007-10-03[/tpl]
