[[2003 U-18 Junior World Cup]]

CATEGORIES: 2003 in ice hockey, Ivan Hlinka Memorial Tournament

The 2003 U-18 Junior World Cup was an under-18 international ice hockey tournament held in Břeclav, Czech Republic and Piešťany, Slovakia from August 11–18, 2003.  The United States captured their first gold medal at the tournament, defeating Russia 3–2 in the gold medal game.  The Czech Republic earned the bronze medal with an 8–2 win over Team Canada.
