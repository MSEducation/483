[[TSS North Wall (1883)]]

CATEGORIES: 1883 ships, Steamships, Clyde-built ships, Ships of the London and North Western Railway

TSS ''North Wall'' was a twin screw steamer cargo vessel operated by the London and North Western Railway from 1893 to 1904.Railway and Other Steamers, Duckworth. 1962 

==History==

She was built by Robert Duncan and Company of Port Glasgow for the London and North Western Railway in 1883 as a cargo vessel. She is notable as the first screw propulsion vessel acquired by the London and North Western Railway. 
In 1904 she was scrapped.
