[[Síofra]]

CATEGORIES: Irish feminine given names, Irish words and phrases

Síofra ([tpl]IPA-ga|ˈʃiːfˠɾˠə[/tpl]) is an Irish-language girl's name. It is commonly accepted to mean changeling child. A changeling child was believed to be a faerie child that had been left in place of a human child stolen by the faeries.
