[[WFGN]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in South Carolina

WFGN (1180 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Religious format.Licensed to Gaffney, South Carolina, USA.  The station is currently owned by Hope Broadcasting, Inc..
