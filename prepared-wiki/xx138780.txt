[[Uncle Meat (film)]]

CATEGORIES: 1987 direct-to-video films, 1980s documentary films, Films directed by Frank Zappa, 1980s comedy films, American documentary films, 1980s science fiction films, American films, English-language films, 1980s musical films

Uncle Meat is a film written and directed by Frank Zappa, released directly to video in 1987. Principal photography having never been completed, the videocassette is actually a "making of" documentary showing rehearsals and background footage from 1968 and interviews with people involved with the uncompleted production. To this day, the video has not yet been released on DVD.
