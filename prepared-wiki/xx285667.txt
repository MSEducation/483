[[Lijingpu, Ningxiang]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Ningxiang County

Lijingpu Township ([tpl]zh|s=历经铺乡|t=歷經鋪鄉|p=Lìjīngpù Xiāng[/tpl]) is an rural township in Ningxiang County, Changsha City, Hunan Province, People's Republic of China. It's surrounded by Yutan Town, Chengjiao Township and Baimaqiao Township on the west, Shuangjiangkou Town on the north and Xiaduopu Town on the southeast. As of the 2000 census it had a population of 27,952 and an area of 35 square kilometers.

==Cityscape==

The township is divided into seven villages and one community, the following areas: Jinnan Community (金南社区), Ziyun Village (紫云村), Jingtuan Village (净土庵村), Qunxing Village (群星村), Dawanling Village (大湾岭村), Lijingpu Village (历经铺村), Nantaihu Village (南太湖村) and Xinbaota Village (新宝塔村).

==Economy==

The region abounds with iron.
Grape is important to the economy.

==Culture==

Huaguxi is the most influence local theater.

==Transportation==

The 319 National Highway continues into Yiyang City, link Lijingpu Township to Yutan Town, Chengjiao Township, Jinghuapu Township.
