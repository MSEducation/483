[[Acushnet Fort]]

CATEGORIES: History of Massachusetts, Forts in Massachusetts, New Bedford, Massachusetts

Acushnet Fort was a fort that existed from 1776 to around 1815 on Eldridge Point in New Bedford, Massachusetts. Rebuilt in 1808, it could accommodate 40 men and had six guns and a magazine. By the time Fort Rodman was built in the 1840s, the fort no longer existed.[tpl]cite web|title=Southeastern Massachusetts|url=http://www.northamericanforts.com/East/masouth.html#acushnet|publisher=American Forts Network|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl]
