[[Donggang, Liaoning]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Liaoning

Donggang ([tpl]zh|s=东港 |t=東港 |p=Dōnggǎng |l=Eastern Port[/tpl]) is a city in the southeast of Liaoning Province in Northeast China. Situated on the coast of the Yellow Sea at the mouth of the Yalu River, it is located near the maritime border with North Korea. Administratively, it is a county-level city of Dandong, which lies [tpl]convert|35|km[/tpl] to the northeast.
