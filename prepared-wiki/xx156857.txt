[[Sally Mortemore]]

CATEGORIES: English stage actresses, Living people, English television actresses, English film actresses, Year of birth missing (living people), 20th-century English actresses, 21st-century English actresses

Sally Mortemore is an English stage, film and television actress. She appeared in Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets, where she played Madame Pince, the librarian of Hogwarts.

==Career==

Mortemore first worked in theatre as an acting assistant stage manager at the Queen's Theatre in Hornchurch[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.scarylittlegirls.co.uk/node/80 |title=Sally Mortemore |publisher = Scary little Girls |location=London |accessdate=22 October 2011[/tpl] followed by seasons at the Haymarket Theatre in Basingstoke, Watford's Palace Theatre, Bolton's Octagon Theatre and the Churchill Theatre in Bromley, Kent. She also toured with the David Glass Ensemble,[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.scarylittlegirls.co.uk/node/80 |title=Sally Mortemore |publisher = Scary little Girls |location=London |accessdate=22 October 2011[/tpl] Red Shift,[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.filmbug.com/db/342925 |title=Sally Mortemore |publisher = filmbug |accessdate=22 October 2011[/tpl] Cleanbreak Theatre Company and Great Eastern Stage.[tpl]cite news | url=http://www.oobdoo.com/online/latestmovies/potterd.htm  |accessdate=23 October 2011[/tpl]
