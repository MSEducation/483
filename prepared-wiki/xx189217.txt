[[37939 Hašler]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Astronomical objects discovered in 1998

37939 Hašler is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1506.0555986 days (4.12 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=37939 |  title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-04-28 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] The asteroid is named after the Czech songwriter Karel Hašler.
The asteroid was discovered on April 16, 1998.
