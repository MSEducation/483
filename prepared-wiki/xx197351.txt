[[Verbascum nigrum]]

CATEGORIES: Verbascum, Flora of Europe, Plants described in 1753

[tpl]Italic title[/tpl]

Verbascum nigrum (dark mullein) is a species of biennial or short-lived perennial herbaceous plants in the genus Verbascum (mullein), native to dry open sites in Europe and the Mediterranean region. It grows to 0.5-1.5 m.
