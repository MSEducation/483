[[Keperveyem]]

CATEGORIES: Rural localities in Chukotka Autonomous Okrug, Populated places of Arctic Russia

Keperveyem ([tpl]lang-ru|Кепервеем[/tpl]) is a rural locality (a selo) in Bilibinsky District of Chukotka Autonomous Okrug, Russia.  Municipally, Keperveyem is subordinated to Bilibinsky Municipal District and is incorporated within Bilibino Urban Settlement. Population: [tpl]ru-census|p2010=336[/tpl]

==Geography==

Keperveyem is located just southwest of Bilibino on the banks of the Bolshoy Keperveyem River, near its confluence with the Maly Anyuy River.Strogoff, M. et al. (2006), p.104 The name Keperveyem comes from the Chukchi for Wolverine River.Information on Keperveyem Electoral Commission of Chukotka Autnomous Okrug (Избирательная комиссия Чукотского автономного округа). ===Culture===

A Young Reindeer Festival is held here every August on Native People's Day. There is a folklore ensemble in the village called Nutengrep ([tpl]lang-ru|Нутэнгрэп[/tpl]).

===Change in municipal status===

On November 20, 2010, a law was passed abolishing Keperveyem Rural Settlement. Keperveyem still exists as a selo, but is now municipally incorporated into Bilibino Urban Settlement. The local municipal administration of Keperveyem was abolished, and governmental power, along with municipal property, property rights and budgets were transferred to Bilibino municipal administration.

==Transport==

The village is 36 km from the district centre Bilibino and approximately 626 km from the okrug centre Anadyr. It is the site of Keperveyem Airport, which serves Bilibino, around which the village is situated. Keperveyem has a Continental Subarctic or Boreal (taiga) climate (Dfc).McKnight and Hess, pp.232-5
