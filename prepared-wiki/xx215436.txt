[[Jaczniki]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Augustów County

Jaczniki [tpl]IPAc-pl|j|a|cz|'|n|i|k|i[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Lipsk, within Augustów County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland, close to the border with Belarus.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|8|km|mi|0[/tpl] east of Lipsk, [tpl]convert|40|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] east of Augustów, and [tpl]convert|71|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of the regional capital Białystok.
