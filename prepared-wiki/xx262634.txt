[[Yara Naoum]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1987 births, Egyptian beauty pageant winners

Yara Naoum ([tpl]lang-ar|يارا نعوم[/tpl]) (born in 1987 in Cairo, Egypt) is an Egyptian beauty queen. She is married to the Egyptian footballer Emad Moteab.[tpl]Citation needed|date=August 2011[/tpl] She earned the title of Miss Egypt in 2008.
