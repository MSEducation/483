[[KRMB]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Arizona, Spanish-language radio stations in Arizona, Radio stations established in 1997

KRMB is a Spanish language Christian radio station licensed to Bisbee, Arizona, broadcasting on 90.1 MHz FM. KRMB serves the Sierra Vista, Arizona area, and is owned by World Radio Network, Inc.KRMB fcc.gov. Accessed October 14, 2012
