[[Ian Horrocks (RAF officer)]]

CATEGORIES: Royal Air Force officers, People of the Royal Observer Corps, Helicopter pilots

Air Commodore Ian Horrocks was a British Royal Air Force helicopter pilot, a senior Royal Air Force officer in the 1970s and 1980s and a Commandant Royal Observer Corps.http://www.rafweb.org/Grp09.htm  Horrocks was the Station Commander of RAF Shawbury from 1978 to 1980.http://www.rafweb.org/Stations/Station%20OCs-Wales.htm
Horrocks retired as an Air Commodore.
