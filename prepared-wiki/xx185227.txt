[[Gmina Wyszków]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Wyszków County

Gmina Wyszków is an urban-rural gmina (administrative district) in Wyszków County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the town of Wyszków, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|53|km|mi|0[/tpl] north-east of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|165.6|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl] and, in 2006, its total population was 37,872 (of which the population of Wyszków amounted to 27,010 and the population of the rural part of the gmina was 10,862).
