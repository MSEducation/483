[[Myall Lakes National Park]]

CATEGORIES: National parks of the Hunter Region, Protected areas established in 1972, Great Lakes Council, 1972 establishments in Australia

Myall Lakes National Park is a national park in New South Wales, Australia, [tpl]Convert|188|km|mi|0[/tpl] northeast of Sydney. It surrounds the scenic Myall Lakes, and includes Broughton Island.
