[[Bambuka language]]

CATEGORIES: Adamawa languages

Kyak (Nyakyak), [tpl]sc|aka[/tpl] Bambuka, is an Adamawa language of Nigeria.
Ethnologue 13 included Kanawa as a dialect, but later additions dropped it. Blench lists it as a possible separate language.http://www.rogerblench.info/Language/Africa/Nigeria/Atlas%20of%20Nigerian%20Languages-%20ed%20III.pdf
