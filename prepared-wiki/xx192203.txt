[[Beautiful Plains School Division]]

CATEGORIES: School districts in Manitoba, Hutterite communities

The Beautiful Plains School Division is a rural school Division in Manitoba, Canada, with two major centers in Neepawa and Carberry.  The division is located west of Winnipeg and east of Brandon and spans and area approximately [tpl]convert|50|mi|km[/tpl] long and [tpl]convert|20|mi|km[/tpl] wide. The division office is located in Neepawa. It is overseen by a board of 7 elected trustees.
Beautiful Plains School Division is responsible for several public schools as well as several Hutterite colony schools.
