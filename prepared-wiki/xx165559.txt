[[Amr Elnady]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, Egyptian male cyclists, Cyclists at the 2000 Summer Olympics, Olympic cyclists of Egypt, Place of birth missing (living people)

Amr Elnady is an Egyptian professional road cyclist. He held the overall title of Tour d'Egypte 2001.  He competed in the Men's Individual time trial at the 2000 Summer Olympics in Sydney where he finished 36th.
