[[KGCR]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Kansas, Christian contemporary radio stations, Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 1988

KGCR is a Christian radio station broadcasting on 107.7 FM, licensed to Goodland, Kansas, serving  Northwestern Kansas. 
The station's format consists primarily of Christian contemporary music with some Christian talk and teaching programs.  Christian talk and teaching programs heard on KGCR include; Back to the Bible, Revive Our Hearts with Nancy Leigh DeMoss, Focus on the Family, Insight for Living with Chuck Swindoll, and Joni and Friends. Program Guide: 10:00am-3:00pm KGCR Accessed August 12, 2012
KGCR also broadcasts "Classic KGCR" on a digital sub-carrier, which plays traditional Christian music, along with many of the Christian talk and teaching programs that air on "Tri-State Praise KGCR" Classic KGCR KGCR. Accessed August 12, 2012
