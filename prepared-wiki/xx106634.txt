[[Baltis]]

CATEGORIES: West Semitic goddesses, Stellar goddesses

Baltis was an ancient Arabian goddess. She was revered at Carrhae and identified with the planet Venus.[tpl]cite book|last=Lurker|first=Manfred|title=The Routledge Dictionary of Gods and Goddesses, Devils and Demons|publisher=Routledge|date=1987|pages=30|isbn=0-415-34018-7[/tpl]
