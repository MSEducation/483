[[Warszawska Street, Katowice]]

CATEGORIES: Streets in Katowice

Warszawska Street in Katowice (Warsaw Street) is a street in the centre of Katowice. The street was building in the 19th century. During World War II the street was called Friedrichstraße,Alle Straßen bzw. Straßennamen von Kattowitz Deutsch – Polnisch www.grytzka-genealogie.de 1926–1939 Marszałka Piłsudskiego (Piłsudski Street).
