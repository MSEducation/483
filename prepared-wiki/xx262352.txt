[[BMW HP2 Enduro]]

CATEGORIES: BMW motorcycles, Motorcycles introduced in 2005, Off-road motorcycles

The HP2 Enduro is a motorcycle manufactured by BMW Motorrad from 2005 to 2008.[tpl]citation|url=http://www.visordown.com/reviews/motorcycles/off-road/bmw-hp2/review/255.html|publisher=Visor Down|title=BMW HP2 Enduro (2005 - 2008)|accessdate=2011-01-01[/tpl] 
It is a "High Performance" (HP) dirt bike based on the engine and electronics from the R1200GS adventure-touring model. 
The use of a trellis tube frame, a conventional fork rather than Telelever, and other modifications, make it [tpl]convert|53|lb|kg|abbr=on[/tpl] lighter than the corresponding R1200GS.
The HP2 Enduro was discontinued in 2008.[tpl]citation|url=https://www.press.bmwgroup.com/pressclub/p/ca/pressDetail.html?outputChannelId=21&id=T0031619EN|publisher=BMW|title=BMW Motorrad Canada announces 2009 Product and Pricing|date=April 11, 2008|accessdate=2011-01-01[/tpl]
