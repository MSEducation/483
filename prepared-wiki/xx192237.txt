[[John Troy (hurler)]]

CATEGORIES: 1971 births, Living people, Lusmagh hurlers, Offaly hurlers, Winners of two All-Ireland medals (hurling)

 
John Troy (born 1971 in Lusmagh, County Offaly) is a former Irish sportsperson. He played senior hurling with his local club Lusmagh and with the Offaly senior inter-county team from the late 1980s until the early 2000s.
