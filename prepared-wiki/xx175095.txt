[[KYTI]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Wyoming, Country radio stations in the United States, Sheridan County, Wyoming

KYTI (93.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Country music format. Licensed to Sheridan, Wyoming, USA.  The station is currently owned by Lovcom, Inc. and features programing from Westwood One.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KYTI |title=KYTI Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==History==

The station was assigned the call sign KWYO-FM on 1993-06-11.  On 1997-07-14, the station changed its call sign to the current KYTI.[tpl]cite web|url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=12931&Callsign=KYTI |title=KYTI Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl] The HD-2 channel of KYTI simulcasts the ESPN Radio network.http://www.sheridanmedia.com/page/hd-radio-formats
