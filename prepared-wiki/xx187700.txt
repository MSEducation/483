[[Duraisamypuram]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Virudhunagar district

Duraisamypuram is a village in the Virudhunagar district of Tamil Nadu.  It has a population of approximately 3,000 and is located between the towns of Sivakasi and Srivilliputhur.

==Industry==

Duraisamypuram's industry includes agriculture, firecrackers (10 companies), and matchbox making.
