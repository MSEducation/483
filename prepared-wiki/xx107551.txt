[[Anbukkodi Makkal]]

CATEGORIES: Ayya Vaikundar

Anbukkodi Makkal is the term first used by Bala Prajapathi Adikalar, one of the chief Panividaiyalar of Swamithoppe pathi, to represent the devotees of Ayya Vaikundar. As of the year of 2000 there are roughly over a million Anbukkodi makkal throughout India[tpl]Citation needed|date=February 2007[/tpl].
