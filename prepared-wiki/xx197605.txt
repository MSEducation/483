[[Katie Waitman]]

CATEGORIES: Writers from Palo Alto, California, 1956 births, Living people

Katharine Lura Waitman (born October 24, 1956 in Palo Alto, California) is an American science fiction writer. She is best known for the Compton Crook Award winning The Merro Tree.[tpl]cite web |publisher=Baltimore Science Fiction Society |title=Compton Crook Award Winners |url=http://www.bsfs.org/bsfsccw.htm |accessdate=June 25, 2012[/tpl] Her second book was The Divided.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.locusmag.com/1999/Issues/10/Waitman.html |title=Katie Waitman: Outsider's World |publisher=Locus Online |date=October 1999 |accessdate=September 20, 2011[/tpl]
