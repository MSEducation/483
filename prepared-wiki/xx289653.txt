[[Sheykh Musa, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Sheykh Musa ([tpl]lang-fa|شيخ موسي[/tpl], also Romanized as Sheykh Mūsá; also known as Shaikh Museh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3084903|Sheykh Musa[/tpl] is a village in Aq Bolagh Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 317, in 65 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
