[[PhilSports Football and Athletics Stadium]]

CATEGORIES: Sports venues in Metro Manila, Athletics (track and field) venues in the Philippines, Football venues in the Philippines, Buildings and structures in Pasig

The Philippine Institute of Sports Football and Athletics Stadium, formerly known as the ULTRA Stadium (University of Life Training and Recreational Arena), is a stadium located inside the PhilSports Complex in Pasig, Philippines. It has a seating capacity of 20,000.
The stadium was the site of the PhilSports Stadium stampede on February 4, 2006.
