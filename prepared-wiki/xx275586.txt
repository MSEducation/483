[[Shirahige Jinja]]

CATEGORIES: Shinto shrines in Shiga Prefecture, Important Cultural Properties of Japan

[tpl]nihongo|Shirahige Jinja|白鬚神社[/tpl] is a Shinto shrine in Takashima in Shiga Prefecture, Japan. The shrine is dedicated to Sarutahiko Okami. It is the head shrine of the Shirahige Shrines around the country.[tpl]cite web|url=http://shirahigejinja.com/ |title=白鬚神社ＨＰ |publisher=Shirahigejinja.com |date= |accessdate=2011-10-17[/tpl]
