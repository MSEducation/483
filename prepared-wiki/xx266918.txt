[[Texas State Highway Spur 6]]

CATEGORIES: Transportation in El Paso County, Texas, State highway spurs in Texas

State Highway Spur 6 (Spur 6) is a [tpl]convert|1.506|mi|km|abbr=on[/tpl] spur route in El Paso County, Texas.

==Route description==

The western terminus of Spur 6 is at [tpl]Roadlink|state=TX|SH|20[/tpl] (Doniphan Rd.) in Anthony. The route travels east and then north along Wildcat Dr. before ending at [tpl]Roadlink|state=TX|FM|1905[/tpl] (Antonio St.).[tpl]Texas Mapbook|70|accessdate=2011-09-22[/tpl]

==History==

The previous Texas Spur/Loop designated number 6 existed from 1939 to 1972. It was a loop off of [tpl]Roadlink|state=TX|US|370[/tpl] through the town of Kirkland in Childress County.  The route was returned to city control and maintenance in 1972.
The current designation for Spur 6 was established in 1998, replacing [tpl]roadlink|state=TX|FM|3500[/tpl].[tpl]TxDOT|FM|3500|accessdate=2011-10-02[/tpl]Note that the route of FM 3500 would have been different, but the same minute order removed FM 3500 and designated Spur 6.
