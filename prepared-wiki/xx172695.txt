[[Cambridge Shakespeare Festival]]

CATEGORIES: Festivals in Cambridgeshire, Theatre festivals in the United Kingdom, Shakespeare festivals, Theatre in Cambridge, Culture of the University of Cambridge, Recurring events established in 1987, July events, August events

Cambridge Shakespeare Festival is a festival of the plays of William Shakespeare held annually in Cambridge, England. The festival was founded in 1987.
The productions are performed in full period costume with live Elizabethan music. The festival attracts upwards of 25,000 visitorsAbout Cambridge Shakespeare Festival. for the productions which take place over a period of eight weeks in July and August. Each performance is held outside in the private gardens of the colleges of the University of Cambridge, including Kings College Gardens, Robinson College Gardens, Girton College Gardens, St John's College Gardens, Trinity College Gardens, and Downing College Gardens.
