[[Lytten, Kentucky]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Elliott County, Kentucky, Unincorporated communities in Kentucky

Lytten is an unincorporated community in Elliott County, Kentucky, United States.  It lies along Route 173 west of the city of Sandy Hook, the county seat of Elliott County.Rand McNally.  The Road Atlas '06. Chicago: Rand McNally, 2006, p. 43.  Its elevation is 1,155 feet (352 m).[tpl]gnis|508519[/tpl], Geographic Names Information System, 1979-09-20.  Accessed 2008-01-03.
