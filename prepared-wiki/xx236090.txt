[[German United Evangelical Church Complex]]

CATEGORIES: Churches in Rochester, New York, Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Religious buildings completed in 1874, 19th-century church buildings

German United Evangelical Church Complex, also known as Salem Evangelical Church (1921), Salem Evangelical and Reformed Church (1943), and Salem United Church of Christ (1957), is a historic Evangelical and Reformed church complex located at Rochester in Monroe County, New York.  The complex includes the original church structure (1874) with attached wing (1895) and the later parish house and church school (1923).[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=4235|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: German United Evangelical Church Complex|date=September 1991|accessdate=2009-10-01 |author=Kathleen La Frank |publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation[/tpl] See also: [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=4236|title=Accompanying six photos[/tpl]
It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1992.
