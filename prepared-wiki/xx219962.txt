[[Taboo (Kumi Koda song)]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 singles, Kumi Koda songs, Oricon Weekly number-one singles, Songs written by Hiro (music producer), Songs written by Koda Kumi, Singles certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of Japan, NHK Kōhaku Uta Gassen songs

Taboo (stylized as TABOO) is the 41st single released in by Kumi Koda.
The single was released on October 8, 2008, in time for Halloween, and contained the title track, its remix, the B-side Always, and instrumentals for both tracks. The Playroom special edition of the CD contained an alternate front cover, and was specially released to fanclub members. The fanclub edition contained a notebook with photos from the single. 
"Taboo" became her 5th #1 single when it topped the Oricon Weekly Charts.Oricon Weekly Ranking 2014.01.13

==Music Video==

Taboo became Koda Kumi's first single to have two different versions of the music video on the same DVD. The "another edition" omitted the dinner scene and had longer bathroom scenes. 
The video was leaked online on September, 15 2008 at 3:30.
