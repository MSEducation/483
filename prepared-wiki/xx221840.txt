[[Virginia Hudson]]

CATEGORIES: 1983 births, Living people, American flautists, Arkansas Tech University alumni, University of Arkansas faculty

Virginia Jane Hudson (b. April 13, 1983) is an American flautist and teacher.  She teaches in the Fort Smith area and serves instructor of flute on the University of Arkansas at Fort Smith faculty.http://www.uafortsmith.edu/Music/GinnyHudson

==Education==

Hudson began her musical education at Dover High School in Dover, Arkansas.  She continued her training at Arkansas Tech University in Russellville, Arkansas, in 2001.  While at ATU, she studied flute with Karen Futterer.  Hudson graduated magna cum laude in 2005 with a Bachelor of Arts in music with a minor in English.  After graduating she spent an additional year at Arkansas Tech working on a graduate degree in English.  In 2006 she accepted a scholarship from the Longy School of Music in Cambridge, Massachusetts, where she studied with world-renowned flautist and teacher Robert Willoughby.[tpl]dead link|date=April 2014[/tpl]

==Teaching==

During the summer of 2008, she accepted an appointment to the University of Arkansas at Fort Smith performance faculty.http://www.uafortsmith.edu/Academy/GinnyHudson  Currently, her duties include teaching the flute studio and Music Appreciation courses.  She also teaches for the UAFS Preparatory Academy of the Arts and maintains a sizeable private flute studio.

==Performing==

Hudson is principal flautist of the River Valley Community Bandhttp://www.uafortsmith.edu/CE/RiverValleyCommunityBand#River_Valley_Community_Band and plays with the Fort Smith Symphony.[tpl]dead link|date=April 2014[/tpl]  She can often be heard with the Garrison Wind Trio and with the Fort Smith Chorale.  She served as principal flautist for the Russellville Festival Choralehttp://www.communityfestivalchorale.org/ for four years.  While at the Longy School of Music, she performed with the Lowell House Opera Orchestra at Harvard University in a production of Strauss's Der Rosenkavalier.[tpl]dead link|date=April 2014[/tpl]  Hudson plays on a Powell Custom Handmade flute with an Aurumite headjoint.
