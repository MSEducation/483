[[Sala Krau District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Cambodia, Districts of Pailin Province

Sala Krau District ([tpl]lang-km|សាលាក្រៅ[/tpl]) is one of two districts (Khan) located in Pailin Province, western Cambodia.
