[[Iriuda Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Kanagawa Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Iriuda Station|入生田駅|Iriuda-eki[/tpl] is a railway station on the Hakone Tozan Line located in Odawara, Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan. It is 4.2 rail kilometers from the line’s terminus at Odawara Station.

==History==

Iriuda station was opened on October 10, 1935, when the Hakone Tozan Railway (founded 1928) changed its Odawawa - Hakone-Yumoto tram line to a railway.
