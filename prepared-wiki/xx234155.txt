[[Ōhito Station]]

CATEGORIES: Izuhakone Sunzu Line, Railway stations in Shizuoka Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Ōhito Station|大仁駅|Ōhito-eki[/tpl] is a train station of the Izuhakone Railway’s Sunzu Line located in the city of Izunokuni, Shizuoka Prefecture, Japan. It is 16.6 rail kilometers from the line’s terminus at Mishima Station.

==History==

Ōhito Station was opened on July 17, 1899 as part of the extension of the Sunzu line from Nanjō Station (present-day Izu-Nagaoka. The line was further extended to its preset terminus at Shuzenji in 1924.Japanese Wikipedia entry

==Lines==

==Station layout==

Ōhito Station has an island platform and a side platform connected to the station building by a level crossing. The station building has both a manned service counter and automatic ticket machines.
