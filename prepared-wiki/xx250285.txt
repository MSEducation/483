[[The Phantom Carriage (1958 film)]]

CATEGORIES: 1958 films, 1950s fantasy films, 1950s horror films, Black-and-white films, Swedish films, Films based on works by Selma Lagerlöf, Films directed by Arne Mattsson, Sound film remakes of silent films, Swedish-language films, Swedish horror films

The Phantom Carriage ([tpl]lang-sv|Körkarlen[/tpl]) is a 1958 Swedish horror film directed by Arne Mattsson. It was entered into the 9th Berlin International Film Festival.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0051836/awards |title=IMDB.com: Awards for The Phantom Carriage |accessdate=2010-01-09 |work=imdb.com[/tpl] It is based on the Selma Lagerlöf novel Thy Soul Shall Bear Witness! and is a remake of the 1921 film.
