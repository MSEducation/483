[[Oland (Frisian island)]]

CATEGORIES: Halligen, Former municipalities in Schleswig-Holstein, Tidal islands of Germany

Oland ([tpl]lang-da|Øland[/tpl], North Frisian: Ualöönist) is a small hallig which is connected by a narrow gauge railway to the mainland and to hallig Langeneß.
Germany's smallest lighthouse is located here, being also the only one with a thatched roof.
