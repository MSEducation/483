[[Step Down to Terror]]

CATEGORIES: 1958 films, 1950s drama films, American films, English-language films, Black-and-white films

Step Down to Terror is a 1958 American film directed by Harry Keller. It is a remake of the 1943 Alfred Hitchcock film Shadow of a Doubt.Stephen Vagg, Rod Taylor: An Aussie in Hollywood, Bear Manor Media 2010 p. 58
