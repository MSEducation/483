[[National Register of Historic Monuments in Romania]]

CATEGORIES: Historical monuments in Romania, Romania-related lists, Historic preservation, Heritage registers in Romania

The National Register of Historic Monuments ([tpl]lang-ro|Lista Monumentelor Istorice (LMI)[/tpl]) is the official English name of the Romania government's list of National Heritage Sites known as Monumente istorice. In Romania, these include sites, buildings, structures, and objects considered worthy of preservation due to the importance of their Romanian cultural heritage. The list,The official list of Historical Monuments at the Romanian Institute for National Cultural Heritage created in 2004, contains places that have been designated by the Ministry of Culture and National Patrimony of Romania and are maintained by the Romanian National Institute of Historical Monuments, as being of national historic significance.The National Institute of Historical Monuments
