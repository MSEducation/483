[[Preutești]]

CATEGORIES: Communes in Suceava County

Preuteşti is a commune located in Suceava County, Romania. It is composed of six villages: Arghira, Basarabi, Bahna-Arin, Preuteşti, Huşi and Leucuşeşti. It also included Hiartop village until 2004, when it was split off to form a separate commune.
