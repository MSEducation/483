[[Azizlu, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Azizlu ([tpl]lang-fa|عزيزلو[/tpl], also Romanized as ‘Azīzlū; also known as ‘Azīzlī)[tpl]GEOnet3|10138384|Azizlu[/tpl] is a village in Chaypareh-ye Bala Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 25, in 6 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
