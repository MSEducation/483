[[1984 U.S. Clay Court Championships]]

CATEGORIES: 1984 Virginia Slims World Championship Series, U.S. Men's Clay Court Championships

The 1984 U.S. Clay Court Championships (also known as the 1984 U.S. Open Clay Courts) was a tennis tournament played on outdoor clay courts in Indianapolis, Indiana in the  United States that was part of the 1984 Virginia Slims World Championship Series. The tournament was held from August 6 through August 12, 1984.
