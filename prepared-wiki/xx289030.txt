[[Aqa Choqlu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Aqa Choqlu ([tpl]lang-fa|اقاچقلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Āqā Choqlū; also known as Āqā Joghlū and Āq Joqlū)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3766557|Aqa Choqlu[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 209, in 44 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
