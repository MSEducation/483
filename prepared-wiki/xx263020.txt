[[Beautiful (Candido Camero album)]]

CATEGORIES: Blue Note Records albums, Candido Camero albums, 1970 albums

Beautiful is an album by Cuban jazz percussionist Candido Camero recorded in 1970 and released on the Blue Note label.Blue Note Records discography accessed January 2, 2011

==Reception==

The Allmusic review by Matt Collar awarded the album 3 stars stating "Beautiful isn't the most essential of Candido's recordings, but should hold a fascination for funk-jazz enthusiasts".Collar, M. Allmusic Review accessed January 2, 2011
