[[Surrey 3]]

CATEGORIES: Rugby union leagues in England

Surrey 3 is a level 11 English Rugby Union League. It is made up of teams predominantly from Surrey and south-west London. The ten teams play home and away matches from September through to April. Champions are Old Caterhamians 13/14
Promoted teams move up to Surrey 2.
