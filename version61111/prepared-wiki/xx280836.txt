[[Golbolaghi]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Golbolaghi ([tpl]lang-fa|گل بلاغي[/tpl], also Romanized as Golbolāghī)[tpl]IranNCSGN[/tpl] is a village in Bughda Kandi Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 626, in 163 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
