[[St. Joseph's Catholic Church (Topeka, Kansas)]]

CATEGORIES: Churches in Topeka, Kansas, German-Russian culture in Kansas, Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in Kansas, Roman Catholic churches in Kansas, Volga German diaspora

St. Joseph's Catholic Church is a historic Roman Catholic church in Topeka, Kansas, United States. It was established to serve the needs of the growing population of Volga Germans, ethnic Germans from Russia. The church is listed on the National Register of Historic Places. "Spires for All Time: the History and Art of St. Joseph German Catholic Church" is a wonderful book about this church.  It was researched for over 11 years and extensively photographed.
