[[Aulopora]]

CATEGORIES: Tabulata, Prehistoric invertebrates

Aulopora is an extinct genus of tabulate coral characterized by a bifurcated budding pattern and conical corallites.[tpl]cite book|title = Fossil Invertebrates|year = 1987|last= Boardman|first=R.S.|publisher= Blackwell|pages = 714[/tpl] Colonies commonly encrust hard substrates such as rocks, shells and carbonate hardgrounds.[tpl]cite book|title = Fossils of Ohio|year = 1996|last1= Feldman|first1=R.M.|last2= Hackathorn|publisher= Ohio Division of Geological Survey Bulletin 70|pages = 577[/tpl]
