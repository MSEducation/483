[[Jenya Lano]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, Russian actresses, Russian emigrants to the United States

Jenya Lano ([tpl]lang-ru|Же́ня Ла́но[/tpl]) is a Russian-born actress and comedian, best known for her recurring role as Inspector Sheridan on the American television series Charmed (Ten episodes total over two seasons).

==Career==

Raised in Moscow and Rome, Italy, Lano is fluent in English, Russian and Italian.Jenya Lano
Lano made her debut in the 1997 film Shrieker. She also made appearances in Blade, S.W.A.T., Ghost Rock, Stealing Candy and Fashionably L.A., as well as the TV series The Shield, Xena: Warrior Princess and NCIS. In 2004 she voiced the character of Xenia Onatopp in the video game GoldenEye: Rogue Agent.
