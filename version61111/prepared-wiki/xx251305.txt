[[Pillerton Hersey]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Warwickshire, Civil parishes in Warwickshire

Pillerton Hersey is a village and civil parish about [tpl]convert|5.5|mi|km[/tpl] north of Shipston-on-Stour in Warwickshire, England.
The Church of England parish church of Saint Mary,[tpl]cite book |editor-last=Salzman |editor-first=Louis F. |editor-link=Louis Francis Salzman |coauthors= |series=Victoria County History |title=A History of the County of Warwick, Volume 5: Kington Hundred |year=1949 |publisher= |location= |pages=133â€“136 |url=http://www.british-history.ac.uk/report.aspx?compid=57058#s3[/tpl] serves both Pillerton Hersey and nearby Pillerton Priors whose own church burned down in the 17th century.
Apart from a BT phone box there are no other public amenities in the village.
