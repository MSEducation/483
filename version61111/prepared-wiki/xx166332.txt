[[Rattlesnake (song)]]

CATEGORIES: Live (band) songs, 1997 singles, Songs written by Ed Kowalczyk

"Rattlesnake" is a song by alternative rock group Live, which was released as the fourth and final single from their 1997 album, Secret Samadhi.

==Chart positions==

In the US, the song peaked at #18 on the Billboard Modern Rock Tracks chart and #15 on the Billboard Mainstream Rock Tracks charts."Secret Samadhi > Charts & Awards > Billboard Singles" Allmusic In Canada "Rattlesnake" reached #13 on the RPM Alternative chart."Rock/Alternative - Volume 66, No. 15, December 15 1997" RPM via Library and Archives Canada
