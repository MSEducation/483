[[Tatar Autonomous Soviet Socialist Republic]]

CATEGORIES: Autonomous republics of the Soviet Union, History of Tatarstan, States and territories established in 1920

Tatar Autonomous Soviet Socialist Republic (TASSR, [tpl]lang-tt|Татарстан Автономияле Совет Социалистик Республикасы[/tpl]; [tpl]Lang-ru|Татарская Автономная Советская Социалистическая республика (ТАССР)[/tpl]) (1920–1990) was part of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic. It was created on May 27, 1920.  Kazan served as its capital.
The territory of the TASSR was a part of Kazan, Simbirsk, and Ufa Governorates (guberniyas) of the Imperial Russia before the October Revolution of 1917.
