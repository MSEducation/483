[[Notre Dame High School (Salinas, California)]]

CATEGORIES: High schools in Monterey County, California, Roman Catholic secondary schools in California, Education in Salinas, California, Roman Catholic Diocese of Monterey in California, Educational institutions established in 1964, Sisters of Notre Dame de Namur schools, Buildings and structures in Salinas, California

Notre Dame High School is a private, All-Female, Roman Catholic high school in Salinas, California. It is located in the Roman Catholic Diocese of Monterey.

==Background==

Notre Dame was established in 1964 by the Diocese of Monterey under the direction of the Sisters of Notre Dame de Namur.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.notredamesalinas.org/index.cfm?fuseaction=menu&menu_id=1|title=About Notre Dame|accessdate=2007-05-11|author=NDHS |archiveurl = http://web.archive.org/web/20070204123203/http://www.notredamesalinas.org/index.cfm?fuseaction=menu&menu_id=1  |archivedate = 2007-02-04[/tpl]
