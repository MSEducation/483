[[FC Politehnica II Timișoara]]

CATEGORIES: FC Politehnica Timișoara, Association football clubs established in 2005, Association football clubs disestablished in 2012, Defunct football clubs in Romania, Liga II clubs, Liga III clubs, 2005 establishments in Romania, 2012 disestablishments in Romania

FC Politehnica II Timișoara was the reserve squad of FC Politehnica Timișoara. It was founded in 2005 and dissolved in 2012.
