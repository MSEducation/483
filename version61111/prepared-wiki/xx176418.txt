[[Stu Laird]]

CATEGORIES: 1960 births, Living people, Calgary Dinos football players, Calgary Stampeders players, Canadian football defensive linemen, Grey Cup champions, Players of Canadian football from Saskatchewan

Stuart Laird (born July 8, 1960, in Assiniboia, Saskatchewan) is a former professional Canadian football defensive tackle who played thirteen seasons for the Calgary Stampeders of the Canadian Football League. He is the former president of the Canadian Football League Players' Association.
