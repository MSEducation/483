[[Khoshkeh Rud, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Khoshkeh Rud ([tpl]lang-fa|خشكه رود[/tpl], also Romanized as Khoshkeh Rūd; also known as Khoshg Rūd, Khoshkrūd, Khūshārūd, and Khushkyary)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3071277|Khoshkeh Rud[/tpl] is a village in Taham Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 199, in 52 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
