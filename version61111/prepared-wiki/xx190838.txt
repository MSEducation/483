[[Neem Annapurna]]

CATEGORIES: 1979 films, Indian films, Bengali-language films, Films directed by Buddhadev Dasgupta, Films set in Kolkata, Films whose cinematographer won the Best Cinematography National Film Award

Neem Annapurna (English language:Bitter Morsel) is a 1979 Bengali film directed and written by Buddhadev Dasgupta and starring Sunil Mukherjee.
