[[Clay Mills]]

CATEGORIES: American country songwriters, Living people

Clayton Bernard "Clay" Mills is an American songwriter. His credits include the #1 hits "Beautiful Mess" by Diamond Rio and "Don't Think I Don't Think About It" by Darius Rucker,http://www.bmi.com/news/entry/233177 which won Mills an award from ASCAP.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cmt.com/news/country-music/1599178/ascap-honors-darius-rucker-clay-mills-for-dont-think-i-dont-think-about-it.jhtml|title=ASCAP Honors Darius Rucker, Clay Mills for "Don't Think I Don't Think About It"|last=Morris|first=Edward|date=2008-11-12|accessdate=2010-02-09[/tpl] Other country music artists who have charted with his singles include Andy Griggs and Trisha Yearwood. Clay received Grammy nominations for his singles "Heaven, Heartache and the Power of Love" and "Beautiful Mess."
