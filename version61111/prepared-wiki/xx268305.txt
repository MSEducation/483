[[Ágnel Flores]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 births, Living people, Venezuelan footballers, Venezuela international footballers

Ágnel Flores (born 29 May 1989) is an Venezuelan international footballer who currently plays for Deportivo Táchira, as a midfielder.

==Career==

Flores has played for Mineros de Guayana and AC Minervén.[tpl]NFT player|pid=35842[/tpl]
For the new season 2012-13, Deportivo Táchira signed Flores on a 3-year contract.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.deportivotachira.com/index.php?subaction=showfull&id=1338758588&archive=&start_from=&ucat=1,3&seccion=noticias_escritas|title=Ágnel Flores se sube al Carrusel Aurinegro|date=3 June 2012|accessdate=7 July 2012|work=http://www.deportivotachira.com|language=Spanish[/tpl]
He made his international debut for Venezuela in 2010, and played at the 2009 FIFA U-20 World Cup.[tpl]FIFA player|312156[/tpl]
