[[HMS Stedham (M2730)]]

CATEGORIES: Ham-class minesweepers, Ships built in the United Kingdom, 1955 ships, Cold War minesweepers of the United Kingdom, Royal Navy ship names, Ham-class minesweepers of the French Navy

HMS ''Stedham'' was a [tpl]sclass2|Ham|minesweeper||ship[/tpl] of the Royal Navy.
Their names were all chosen from villages ending in -ham. The minesweeper was named after Stedham in West Sussex.
