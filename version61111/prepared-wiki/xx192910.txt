[[1947 Iso-Heikkilä]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Astronomical objects discovered in 1935

1947 Iso-Heikkilä is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 2045.9444096 days (5.60 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=1947  |  title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-04-30 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on March 4, 1935 by Yrjö Väisälä.
