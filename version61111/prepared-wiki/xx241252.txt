[[Duolun County]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

Duolun County (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠳᠣᠯᠣᠨᠨᠤᠤᠷ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠰᠢᠶᠠᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Dolonnuur siyan[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=多伦县 |t=多倫縣 |p=Duōlún Xiàn[/tpl]) is a county of Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China. It is under the administration of Xilin Gol League.

==Climate==

Duolun County has a dry, monsoon-influenced humid continental climate (Köppen Dwb), with bitterly cold and very dry winters, warm, humid summers, and strong winds, especially in spring. The monthly 24-hour average temperature ranges from [tpl]convert|−17.1|°C|1[/tpl] in January to [tpl]convert|19.0|°C|1[/tpl] in July, with the annual mean at [tpl]convert|2.36|°C|1[/tpl]. The annual precipitation is [tpl]convert|387|mm|in|1[/tpl], with more than half of it falling in July and August alone. With monthly percent possible sunshine ranging from 56% in July to 77% in January and February, sunshine is abundant year-round, with 3,036 hours of bright sunshine annually.
