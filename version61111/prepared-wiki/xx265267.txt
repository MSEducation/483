[[Hudson Valley Kingz]]

CATEGORIES: Atlantic Coast Professional Basketball League teams, Basketball teams in New York City, Hudson Valley, Sports clubs established in 2010

The Hudson Valley Kingz are a charter team of the Atlantic Coast Professional Basketball League that began play in the 2010-11 season. Based in the Hudson Valley of New York, the Kingz were playing all their games on the road for their inaugural season, with hopes of finding a home for 2011-12.
