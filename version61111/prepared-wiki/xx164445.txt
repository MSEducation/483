[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Jalingo]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic Church in Nigeria, Roman Catholic dioceses in Nigeria, Religious organizations established in 1995, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Jalingo ([tpl]lang-la|Ialingoën(sis)[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Jalingo, which is in Taraba State and in the Ecclesiastical province of Jos in Nigeria.
