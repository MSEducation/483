[[Monica Saili]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1997 births, Samoan swimmers

Monica Saili (born 18th September 1997) is a Samoan swimmer who currently lives in New Zealand.http://www.bcn2013.com/en/swimming/detail/member/monica-saili [tpl]dead link|date=April 2014[/tpl] She competed in the 50 m, 100 m, 200 m, 400m, 800 m freestyle, 50 m, 100 m breaststroke, 50 m, 100 m backstroke, 100 m butterfly and 100 m individual medley events at the 2012 FINA World Swimming Championships (25 m). Saili also competed in the 800 m and 400 m freestyle events at the 2013 World Aquatics Championships.
She is currently at Queen Margaret College, Wellington, New Zealand and is taking the International Baccalaureate course offered there.
