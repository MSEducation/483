[[Abrial A-13 Buse]]

CATEGORIES: Glider aircraft, Abrial aircraft, Tailless aircraft, High-wing aircraft

The  Abrial A-13 Buse was a tailless glider prototype that was designed in 1954. It was the last in a series of glider aircraft designed by Georges Abrial.[tpl]cite web|title=Charels Fauvel|url=http://www.nurflugel.com/Nurflugel/Fauvel/e_abrial.htm|accessdate=2 Jan 2011[/tpl]

==Design==

The glider featured a short fuselage with a single, non-tapered strut-braced straight wing. [tpl]cite web|title=A.13|url=http://www.nuricom.de/Models/KN_1950_Flywing_Draw_Mini_Abrial%20A13%20Buse.jpg|accessdate=2 Jan 2011[/tpl]
