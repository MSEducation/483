[[Epactaeus]]

CATEGORIES: Greek mythology

In Greek mythology, Epactaeus, Epactius, Epaktaios or Epaktios – that is, the god worshipped on the coast – was used as a surname of Poseidon in Samos,Hesych. s. v. (cited by Schmitz) and of Apollo.Orph. Argon. 1296; Apollon. Rhod. i. 404. (cited by Schmitz)
