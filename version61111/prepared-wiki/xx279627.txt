[[Al-Rastan Dam]]

CATEGORIES: Dams in Syria, Dams completed in 1960

Al-Rastan Dam is an embankment dam on the Orontes River in the city of Al-Rastan, Homs Governorate, Syria. It was completed in 1960 with the primary purpose of irrigation.[tpl]cite web|title=Middle East Dams|url=http://www.fao.org/nr/water/aquastat/dams/middle_east_dams.xls|publisher=UN FAO (via Aquasat)|accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl] It was constructed by the Bulgarian firm Hydrostroy along with the Mouhardeh Dam, downstream and also on the Orontes.[tpl]cite web|title=Hydrostroy|url=http://www.velgraf.biz/hydro.html|publisher=Velgraf|accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl]
