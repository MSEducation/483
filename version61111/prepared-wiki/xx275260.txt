[[Christoph Bertschy]]

CATEGORIES: 1994 births, Living people, Minnesota Wild draft picks, People from the canton of Fribourg, SC Bern players, Swiss ice hockey players

Christoph Bertschy (born April 5, 1994) is a Swiss professional ice hockey player. He is currently playing for the SC Bern of Switzerland's National League A.Eliteprospects.com - Bern He was drafted 158th overall by the Minnesota Wild in the 2012 NHL Entry Draft.
