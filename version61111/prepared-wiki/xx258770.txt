[[Chewed Up]]

CATEGORIES: Louis C.K. albums, 2008 live albums, English-language live albums, Image Entertainment live albums, Image Entertainment video albums

Chewed Up is the second album released by comedian Louis C.K., containing the audio of his second one-hour special filmed at the Berklee Performance Center in Boston. Released to CD & DVD by Image Entertainment on December 16, 2008.Amazon, Louis C.K.: Chewed Up.
George Carlin, (who had coincidentally filmed his final one-hour special on the same night of March 1, 2008) died of heart failure on June 22. The special, recorded on March 1, was dedicated to Carlin.
