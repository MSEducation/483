[[Shahurain Abu Samah]]

CATEGORIES: 1986 births, Living people, Malaysian footballers, Malaysia international footballers, Negeri Sembilan FA players

Shahurain Abu Samah (born on December 23, 1986 in Kajang, Selangor) is a Malaysian footballer who is currently a midfielder/right winger for PKNS F.C.. He started with Selangor President's Cup Team .  He help Negeri Sembilan FA to win the Malaysia Cup 2009 by scoring the first goal in a 3-1 win against Kelantan FA. He is also a member of the Malaysian national team.
