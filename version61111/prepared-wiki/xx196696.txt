[[Es Pontàs]]

CATEGORIES: Climbing routes, Landforms of Spain, Natural arches

Es Pontàs is a natural arch in the southeastern part of the island of Mallorca. The arch is located on the coastline between the Cala Santanyí and Cala Llombards in the municipality of Santanyí.
Es Pontàs is the recurring theme of the film King Lines, featuring Chris Sharma's first ascent of the hardest deep water solo (9b) climb in the world.http://www.rockandice.com/latest-new/chris-sharma-versus-the-arch?A=SearchResult&SearchID=1819481&ObjectID=4000920&ObjectType=35
