[[Las Vegas (Amtrak station)]]

CATEGORIES: Amtrak stations in New Mexico, Atchison, Topeka and Santa Fe Railway stations, Railway stations opened in 1899, Atchison, Topeka and Santa Fe Railway hotels, Buildings and structures in San Miguel County, New Mexico, Transportation in San Miguel County, New Mexico, Las Vegas, New Mexico

The Las Vegas station is a train station located at Railroad Street & Lincoln Avenue in Las Vegas, New Mexico. 
The station is near the Hotel Castañeda, a former hotel built by Fred Harvey for the Atchison, Topeka and Santa Fe Railroad.La Castaneda Hotel – Las Vegas Citizens' Committee for Historic Preservation  The hotel's architects were Frederick Roehrig and A. Reinsch.
