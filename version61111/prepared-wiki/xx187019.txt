[[Paul Platz]]

CATEGORIES: Ice hockey people from Manitoba, Canadian ice hockey left wingers, 1920 births, Living people, Winnipeg Monarchs players

Paul Platz  (born July 28, 1920 in Winnipeg, Manitoba) was a Canadian ice hockey left winger who played on 3 provincial championships with the Winnipeg Monarchs.
