[[Eltham Hill School]]

CATEGORIES: Secondary schools in Greenwich, Girls' schools in London, Community schools in Greenwich

Eltham Hill School is a secondary school and sixth form for girls, located in the Eltham area of the Royal Borough of Greenwich in London, England.[tpl]cite web|url=http://elthamhill-greenwich.com/ |title=Eltham Hill School |publisher=Elthamhill-greenwich.com |date=2011-01-01 |accessdate=2013-08-12[/tpl] It is a community school controlled by Greenwich London Borough Council. 
The school was originally founded as Eltham Hill Grammar School for Girls before a comprhensive education system was adopted in Greenwich Borough. The school went on to become a specialist Technology College for a time and was renamed Eltham Hill Technology College, before the Eltham Hill School name was readopted.
Eltham Hill School offers GCSEs and BTECs as programmes of study for pupils.[tpl]cite web|url=http://elthamhill-greenwich.com/curriculum/ |title=Curriculum | |publisher=Elthamhill-greenwich.com |date= |accessdate=2013-08-12[/tpl] Students in the sixth form have the option of studying from a range of A Levels as well as further BTECs. The school is also planning to offer the International Baccalaureate in the future.[tpl]cite web|url=http://elthamhill-greenwich.com/post-16/ |title=Post 16 | |publisher=Elthamhill-greenwich.com |date= |accessdate=2013-08-12[/tpl]
