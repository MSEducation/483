[[Avalon (Los Angeles Metro station)]]

CATEGORIES: Los Angeles County Metro Rail stations, Railway stations opened in 1995

Avalon Station is a Los Angeles County Metro Rail station on the Green Line.  It is located in the center median of Century Freeway above Avalon Boulevard in South Los Angeles.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.metro.net/around/rail/green-line/|title=Green Line station information|accessdate=2010-09-25| archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20101021184707/http://www.metro.net/around/rail/green-line/| archivedate= 21 October 2010 | deadurl= no[/tpl] The original name for this station was Avalon/I-105 and it may still be used in some places.
