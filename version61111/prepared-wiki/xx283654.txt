[[Tamara Poliakova]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Soviet female cyclists, Ukrainian female cyclists, UCI World Champions, 1960 births, Place of birth missing (living people)

Tamara Poliakova also written as Tamara Poljakowa or Tamara Polyakova (born 27 August 1960) is a retired Soviet Union and later Ukrainian female road and track cyclist. At the 1983 Summer Universiade she won the road race. Poliakova won a silver medal in the individual pursuit at the 1981 UCI Track Cycling World Championships and became world champion on the road in the women's team time trial in 1987 and 1989.[tpl]cite book|title=Tamara Poliakova|url=http://www.cyclingarchives.com/coureurfiche.php?coureurid=29430|publisher=cyclingarchives.com|accessdate=26 July 2013[/tpl]
