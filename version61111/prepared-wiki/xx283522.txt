==Career==

Mahima Makwana started her career by enacting various short roles as child artist in Balika Vadhu, Jhansi ki Rani, C.I.D., Aahat, Mile Jab Hum Tum, Mohe Rang De, and was a part of two advertisement telefilms Rin Ad, MasalaAd. She got her first break as a popular character in Sawaare Sabke Sapne - Preeto as Preeto's youngest sister.
