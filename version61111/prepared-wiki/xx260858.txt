[[Circe in popular culture]]

CATEGORIES: Greco-Roman mythology in popular culture

The Greek mythology character Circe has appeared many times in popular culture, either under her own name or as a sorceress with similar powers under others. Post-Classical amplifications and reinterpretations of that story and others involving her are dealt with in the article on Circe in the arts. The instances mentioned here are more recent allusions and adaptations.
