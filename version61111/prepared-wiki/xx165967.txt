[[Tenpyō-shōhō]]

CATEGORIES: Japanese eras

[tpl]nihongo|Tenpyō-shōhō|天平勝宝[/tpl] was a [tpl]nihongo|Japanese era name|年号,|nengō,|lit. "year name"[/tpl] after Tenpyō-kanpō and before Tenpyō-hōji.  This period spanned the years from July 749 through August 757.Nussbaum, Louis-Frédéric. (2005). "Tenpyō-shōhō" in [tpl]Google books|p2QnPijAEmEC|Japan Encyclopedia, p. 957|page=957[/tpl]; n.b., Louis-Frédéric is pseudonym of Louis-Frédéric Nussbaum, see Deutsche Nationalbibliothek Authority File.  The reigning emperor was [tpl]nihongo|Kōken-tennō|孝謙天皇[/tpl].Titsingh, Isaac. (1834). Annales des empereurs du Japon, pp. 73-75; Varley, Paul. (1980). Jinnō Shōtōki, pp. 143-144.
