[[Leaving Home EP]]

CATEGORIES: 2011 EPs, T. Mills EPs, Columbia Records EPs

Leaving Home EP is the major label debut EP by American rapper T. Mills. The EP was released for retail sale on January 13, 2012 by Columbia Records. Record production was handled by Omen, AudioBlack, Colin Munroe, and The Stereotypes among others. Leaving Home EP was supported by the lead single "Vans On" and a promotional single "The Boom".

==Background==

T. Mills signed with Columbia Records in early 2011 and begun working on his debut album. During a seven month process of creating it he recorded around 140 songs, which he narrowed down to 10. Then he decided rather than release an album, he would release a free project to the fans, which would be titled Leaving Home.Mixtape Daily Fire Starter: T. Mills | News Video | MTV
