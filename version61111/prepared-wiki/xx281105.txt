[[Pavel Balakhnichev]]

CATEGORIES: 1993 births, Living people, Russian footballers

Pavel Andreyevich Balakhnichev ([tpl]lang-ru|Павел Андреевич Балахничев[/tpl]; born 26 February 1993) is a Russian football forward, who currently playing for FC Podolye Podolsky district.
He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Podolye Podolsky district on 16 July 2013 in a game against FC Fakel Voronezh.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Russian Football Union|url=http://rfspro.ru/cgi-bin/player/script2.cgi?par1=119683|title=Career Summary|date=9 August 2013[/tpl]
