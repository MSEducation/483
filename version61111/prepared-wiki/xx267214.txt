[[(10736) 1988 DD3]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1988, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(10736) 1988 DD3 is a main-belt minor planet.JPL Small-Body Database Browser  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on February 22, 1988.Discovery Circumstances: Numbered Minor Planets (10001)-(15000)
