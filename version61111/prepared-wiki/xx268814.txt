[[Snežana Maleševič]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 births, Living people, Slovenian footballers, Slovenian women's footballers, FA Women's Premier League players, Millwall Lionesses L.F.C. players

Snežana Maleševič (born 10 October 1985) is a Slovenian international footballer. She plays for English FA Women's Premier League club Millwall Lionesses.
