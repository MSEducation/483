[[Nanae Chrono]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 births, Manga artists, Living people, People from Utsunomiya, Tochigi, Female comics artists, Female comics writers, Nanae Chrono

[tpl]nihongo|Nanae Chrono|黒乃 奈々絵|Kurono Nanae|born  June 18, 1980 in Tochigi, Japan[/tpl] is a female Japanese manga artist.  She is best known as the creator of the manga series Peacemaker Kurogane, Senki Senki Momotama, and Vassalord .
