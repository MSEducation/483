[[Taherlu, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Taherlu ([tpl]lang-fa|طاهرلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Ţāherlū; also known as Dzhakhanshakhlu, Jahānshāhlū, and Jehānshāhlu)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3086637|Taherlu[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 45, in 10 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
