CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 2000, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Florianópolis, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century, Blumenau

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Blumenau ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Florumpratensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Blumenau in the Ecclesiastical province of Florianópolis in Brazil.
