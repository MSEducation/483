[[Beau Falloon]]

CATEGORIES: 1987 births, Australian rugby league players, South Sydney Rabbitohs players, Gold Coast Titans players, Burleigh Bears players, North Sydney Bears players, Rugby league hookers, Living people

Beau Falloon (born 21 May 1987 in Sydney, New South Wales, Australia) is a rugby league player for the Gold Coast Titans in the National Rugby League. He plays as a hooker.
He will join the Gold Coast Titans in 2012.
