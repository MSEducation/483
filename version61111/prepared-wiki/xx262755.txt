[[Jarkko Immonen (ice hockey, born 1984)]]

CATEGORIES: 1984 births, Living people, Espoo Blues players, Pelicans players, SaiPa players

Jarkko Antero Immonen (born January 18, 1984) is a Finnish professional ice hockey forward who currently plays for Lahti Pelicans of the SM-liiga. He was selected by the Dallas Stars in the 4th round (110th overall) of the   2002 NHL Entry Draft.
Midway through the 2010–11 season, on January 28, 2011, Immonen transferred from his first SM-lliga club, the Espoo Blues, to the Pelicans and signed an addition one-year contract.[tpl]citeweb| url =  http://www.pelicans.fi/jarkko-immonen-pelicansiin  | title = Jarkko Immonen to the Pelicans | publisher = Pelicans | date = 2011-01-28 | accessdate = 2011-01-28 | language = Finnish[/tpl] In the following 2011–12 season, Immonen enjoyed his most successful season win the SM-liiga since 2008, scoring 32 points in 59 games to help the Pelicans reach the finals. 
On May 3, 2012, he was re-signed to a two-year extension with the Pelicans.[tpl]citeweb| url = http://www.pelicans.fi/p%C3%A4rssinen-korpikari-ja-myllyniemi-pelicansin-paitaan | title = Pärssinen, Kari Korpi and Myllyniemi Pelicans shirt | publisher = Lahti Pelicans | date = 2012-05-03 | accessdate = 2012-05-03 | language = Finnish[/tpl]
