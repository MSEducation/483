[[Bel Powley]]

CATEGORIES: British child actresses, British television actresses, People educated at Holland Park School, Living people, 1992 births

Isobel Dorothy "Bel" Powley (born 7 March 1992) is a British actress best known for playing Daisy Millar in the CBBC television series M.I. High. An alumnus of Holland Park School, she is the daughter of British actor Mark Powley.[tpl]cite web|author=Arcadia - Broadway |url=http://www.broadway.com/buzz/155655/arcadias-bel-powley-on-coming-to-broadway-as-a-brainy-teen/ |title=Arcadia's Bel Powley on Coming to Broadway as a Brainy Teen | Broadway Buzz |publisher=Broadway.com |date=2011-03-17 |accessdate=2014-01-16[/tpl]

==Career==

From 2007 until 2008 Powley was one of the lead characters in M.I.High, starring in 26 episodes alongside Rachel Petladwala and Moustafa Chousein-Oglou. In addition to M.I.High, Bel Powley has also appeared numerous other productions such as Murderland (3 episodes, 2009), Little Dorrit (1 episode, 2008), The Bill (1 episode, 2008) and The Whistleblowers (1 episode, 2007).
In 2013 it was announced that Powley was joining the cast of ITV series Benidorm (TV series) for series 6.[tpl]cite web|last=Lazarus |first=Susanna |url=http://www.radiotimes.com/news/2013-04-12/benidorm-series-6-confirmed |title=Benidorm series 6 confirmed |publisher=Radio Times |date=2013-04-12 |accessdate=2014-01-16[/tpl]

==Personal life==

In an interview in 2011, Powley said she had a spot reserved to read history at the University of Manchester. Powley joined the protests against university fee rises in 2010.[tpl]cite web|author=Susannah Butter |url=http://www.standard.co.uk/lifestyle/london-life/generation-next-meet-londons-bright-young-things-8276739.html |title=Generation next... meet London's bright young things - London Life - Life & Style - London Evening Standard |publisher=Standard.co.uk |date=2012-11-02 |accessdate=2014-01-16[/tpl] Nash Grier is her cousin.
