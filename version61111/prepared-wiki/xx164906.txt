[[Lakepoint State Park]]

CATEGORIES: State parks of Alabama, Protected areas of Barbour County, Alabama

Lakepoint State Park is an Alabama state park located on [tpl]convert|45000|acre|adj=on[/tpl] Lake Eufala (Walter F. George Lake) on the north side of the city of Eufaula. Park facilities include a convention center, 18-hole golf course, 101-room lodge, 192-site campground, 29 cabins, 10 lakeside cottages, marina, seven hiking trails totaling five miles, and picnicking areas.[tpl]cite web |title=Lakepoint Resort State Park |work=Alabama State Parks |publisher=Alabama Department of Conservation and Natural Resources |url=http://www.alapark.com/LakePointResort/ |accessdate=2012-11-12[/tpl]
