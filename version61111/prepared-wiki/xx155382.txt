[[Hamare Tumhare]]

CATEGORIES: Compositions by Rahul Dev Burman, 1979 films, Indian films, Hindi-language films

Hamare Tumhare is a 1979 Hindi movie produced by F. C. Mehra and directed by Umesh Mehra. The film stars Sanjeev Kumar, Raakhee, Amjad Khan, Prem Krishen, David, Mehmood, Asrani, Anil Kapoor, Lucky Ali, and Amrish Puri. The films music is by R. D. Burman.
