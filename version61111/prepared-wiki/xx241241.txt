[[Tuquan County]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Tuquan County (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠲᠦᠴᠢᠤᠸᠠᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠰᠢᠶᠠᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Tüčiuvan siyan[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=突泉县 |t=突泉縣 |p=Tūquán Xiàn[/tpl]) is a county of Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China, facing Jilin province to the east. It is under the administration of Hinggan League, bordering Horqin Right Middle Banner to the south and Horqin Right Front Banner to the north.
