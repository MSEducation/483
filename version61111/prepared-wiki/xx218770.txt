[[Orders Are Orders]]

CATEGORIES: 1954 films, 1950s comedy films, English-language films, British films, British comedy films

Orders Are Orders is a 1954 British comedy film directed by David Paltenghi, and featuring Peter Sellers, Sid James, Tony Hancock, Raymond Huntley, Donald Pleasence and Eric Sykes.[tpl]cite web|url=http://explore.bfi.org.uk/4ce2b6b248329 |title=Orders Are Orders | BFI | BFI |publisher=Explore.bfi.org.uk |date= |accessdate=2014-04-08[/tpl] It was a remake of the 1933 film Orders Is Orders.[tpl]IMDb title|id=0047310|title=Orders Are Orders[/tpl]

==Synopsis==

A film production company decides to make a new science fiction film in an army barracks, using the soldiers as extras. This does not go down well with the commanding officer, who attempts to make life as difficult as possible for the film crew.
