[[Rhagodia (arachnid)]]

CATEGORIES: Solifugae

[tpl]Italic title[/tpl]

Rhagodia is a genus in the arachnid order Solifugae, comprising four species. R. abessinica is found in Ethiopia, while the other three species are found in the Near East, from Turkey to Pakistan.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.solpugid.com/Genus%20Rhagodia.htm |title=Genus Rhagodia Roewer 1933 |work=The Arachnid Order Solifugae |date=2005â€“2006 |accessdate=May 16, 2011[/tpl]
