[[Merry Blues]]

CATEGORIES: 2001 songs, Manu Chao songs

"Merry Blues" is the first single and the opening track from Manu Chao's second album, Próxima Estación: Esperanza. Manu Chao re-recorded the song on the album True Love with reggae band Toots & the Maytals.
