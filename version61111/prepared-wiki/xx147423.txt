Austín Gerardo Berry Moya (born 5 April 1971) is a retired Costa Rican soccer player who is the assistant coach of CS Herediano. 
As a player, Berry played for L.D. Alajuelense and SC Freiburg.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.fussballdaten.de/spieler/berryaustin/1994/ | title = Austin Berry | language = German | publisher = fussballdaten.de | accessdate = 8 July 2013[/tpl] He made his debut for the national team in 1991 and collected a total of 65 caps, scoring five goals.[tpl]cite web|publisher=RSSSF| last = Passo Alpuin | first = Luis Fernando | title =  |url=http://www.rsssf.com/miscellaneous/cos-recintlp.html | accessdate = 8 July 2013 | date = 25 April 2013[/tpl] He played his last international in 2002 against Morocco but missed out on the 2002 FIFA World Cup squad.
He retired from football in 2006.
