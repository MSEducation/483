[[Hirslanden]]

CATEGORIES: District 7 of Zürich, Former municipalities of the canton of Zürich

Hirslanden is a quarter in the district 7 in Zurich.
It was formerly a municipality of its own, having been incorporated into Zurich in 1893.
The quarter has a population of 6,859 distributed on an area of 2.2 km².
Hirslanden is located on the western side of the Adlisberg.
