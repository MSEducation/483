[[Tolombeh-ye Khanehi Dehlu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Tolombeh-ye Khanehi Dehlu ([tpl]lang-fa|تلمبه خانه ايده لو[/tpl], also Romanized as Tolombeh-ye Khāneh'ī Dehlū) is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 39, in 11 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
