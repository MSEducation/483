[[Karen Foo Kune]]

CATEGORIES: Mauritian badminton players, Mauritian people of Chinese descent, 1982 births, Living people

[tpl]MedalCountry|[tpl]MRI[/tpl][/tpl]
Eileen Karen Lee Chin Foo Kune, born 29 May 1982 is a Mauritian amateur Badminton player, two times Mauritian Sportswoman of the Year (2004, 2009), ranked first in the African continent on several occasions. She once participated at the Olympic Games (2008) and made the Commonwealth games more often (2002, 2006, 2010).Karen Foo Kune Biography and Olympic Results Olympics at Sports-Reference.com
