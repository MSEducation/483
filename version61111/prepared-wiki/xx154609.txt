[[Maria Rita Lorenzetti]]

CATEGORIES: 1953 births, Living people, Presidents of Umbria, Democratic Party (Italy) politicians, Italian women in politics, People from Foligno

Maria Rita Lorenzetti is an Italian politician and a former President of the Italian region of Umbria.Amanda Knox film causes political storm in Italy In 2000 she was elected governor of Umbria becoming the first woman to serve as governor of Umbria, the fifth woman to serve as governor in Italy, and the first woman directly elected as governor of an Italian region.
