[[Gmina Pałecznica]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Lesser Poland Voivodeship, Proszowice County

Gmina Pałecznica is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Proszowice County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. Its seat is the village of Pałecznica, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|12|km|mi|0[/tpl] north of Proszowice and [tpl]convert|38|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north-east of the regional capital Kraków.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|47.95|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 3,730.

==Villages==

Gmina Pałecznica contains the villages and settlements of Bolów, Czuszów, Gruszów, Ibramowice, Łaszów, Lelowice-Kolonia, Nadzów, Niezwojowice, Pałecznica, Pamięcice, Pieczonogi, Solcza, Sudołek and Winiary.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Pałecznica is bordered by the gminas of Kazimierza Wielka, Proszowice, Racławice, Radziemice and Skalbmierz.
