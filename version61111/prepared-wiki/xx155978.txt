[[Hussain Industries]]

CATEGORIES: Clothing companies of Pakistan, Companies based in Multan, Manufacturing companies of Pakistan

Hussain Industries (est. 1994) is one of the largest textile manufacturers in Pakistan.  Located in Pakistan’s largest cotton-growing area of Multan, this co-proprietorship firm (partnership) is owned equally by two parties − CEO Ghulam Hussain and a group consisting of Muhammad Ajmal Hameed, Shahid Hameed, Tajamal Hameed, and Muhammad Amir.
Hussain Industries mainly manufactures articles with intense color tones, including dyed yarns, printed kitchen towels, napkins, tea towels, table cloths, terry towels, surgical towels, industrial towels (with and without logo, tile motifs, check variants, micro checks, graduated checks and linear embroidery), herringbone, mono checks, dishcloths, shower curtains, hospital bed sheets, thermal blankets, bedspreads, draw sheets, upholstery, handkerchiefs, aprons, place mats, pot holders, gloves, and beach throws.  These are manufactured using dobby looms, jacquard looms, and shuttle looms, which allow for state of the art floral motifs, subtle shadows, and colour gradients. They hold ISO 9001-2000 certification by SGS.
Hussain Industries is a regular exhibitor at the world's largest home textile fair, Heimtextil, organized by the Messe Frankfurt GmbH, Frankfurt, Germany.http://www.messefrankfurt.com/
