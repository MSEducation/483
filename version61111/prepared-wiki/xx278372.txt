[[K. Ramiah]]

CATEGORIES: Nominated members of the Rajya Sabha, Recipients of the Padma Shri, Recipients of the Padma Bhushan, 1892 births, 1974 deaths, Indian writers

Dr. K. Ramiah (1892-1974) was an Indian parliamentarian. He wrote many books on rice and allied matters. He was awarded the Padma Shri in 1956 and the Padma Bhushan in 1970. He was a nominated member of the Rajya Sabha from 1968 to 1974.
