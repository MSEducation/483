[[Rosanna railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1927

Rosanna railway station is located on the Hurstbridge line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the north-eastern Melbourne suburb of Rosanna opening on 1 October 1927.Rosanna Vicsig New station buildings were built in 1975.[tpl]cite book|author=Vincent Adams Winter|title=VR and VicRail: 1962-1983|year=1990|isbn=0-9592069-3-0|page=106[/tpl]
South of Rosanna the double line becomes single to pass through a short tunnel.
