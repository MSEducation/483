[[Sari Kand-e Dadash Beyk]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Sari Kand-e Dadash Beyk ([tpl]lang-fa|ساري كندداداش بيك[/tpl], also Romanized as Sārī Kand-e Dādāsh Beyk) is a village in Chaypareh-ye Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 116, in 23 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
