[[Esfarayen]]

CATEGORIES: Cities in Iran, Cities in North Khorasan Province, Populated places in Esfarayen County, Nishapur Quarter

Esfarayen ([tpl]lang-fa|اسفراین[/tpl], also Romanized as Esfarāyen; formerly, Meyanābād, Mīānābād, and Mīyānābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3062173[/tpl] is a city in and the capital of Esfarayen County, North Khorasan Province in Iran.  At the 2006 census its population was 51,321, in 13,376 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|28[/tpl]
Esfarayen is one of the focal points for residence of Aryan tribes after entering into Iran.  

==History==

After the Islamic conquest of Persia, Esfarayen flourished greatly; after the fall of the Abbasid caliphate, in spite of attacks and destruction, was reconstructed.  
The town was completely ruined at the time of ruler Ebrahim Afshar.

==Sport==

Esfarayen is home to  a wrestling tournament called Bachokheh or Koshti Bachokheh. This tournament is held in the open air.
