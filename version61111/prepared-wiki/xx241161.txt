[[Ar Horqin Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Ar Horqin Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠠᠷᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠷᠴᠢᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Aru Qorčin qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=阿鲁科尔沁旗 |t=阿魯科爾沁旗 |p=Ālǔ Kē'ěrqìn Qí[/tpl]) is a banner of eastern Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China. It is under the administration of Chifeng City, about [tpl]convert|200|km[/tpl] to the south-southwest. The banner lies on China National Highway 303, running from Ji'an, Jilin to Xilinhot, Inner Mongolia. The Mongolian dialect spoken in Ar Khorchin is not Khorchin, as the name suggests, but Baarin.
