[[2013 Super Coppa Sammarinese]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 in association football, 2013 in San Marino

The 2013 Super Coppa Sammarinese is the second Super Coppa Sammarinese football match. It will be contested by Tre Penne, the winner of the 2012–13 Campionato Sammarinese di Calcio, and La Fiorita the winner of the 2012–13 Coppa Titano. The match will be held on 31 August 2013 at the Campo di Fiorentino.
