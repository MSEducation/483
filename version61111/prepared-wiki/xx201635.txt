[[Nunapitchuk Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in the Bethel Census Area, Alaska

Nunapitchuk Airport [tpl]airport codes|NUP||16A[/tpl] is a state-owned public-use airport located in Nunapitchuk, a city in the Bethel Census Area of the U.S. state of Alaska.

==Facilities==

Nunapitchuk Airport has one runway designated 18/36 with a 2,040 x 60 ft (622 x 18 m) gravel surface. It also has one seaplane landing area designated NE/SW which measures 3,000 x 300 ft (914 x 91 m).
