[[Remington Model 121 Fieldmaster]]

CATEGORIES: Pump-action rifles, Remington Arms Company firearms, .22 LR firearms

The Fieldmaster 121 series is a .22 caliber, slide action, tubular magazine-fed rifle manufactured by Remington Arms between 1936 and 1954. 
The Sportmaster 121 has a 25" barrel, a one piece hardwood stock, and a blued metal finish. It was replaced by the Model 572 Fieldmaster in 1955.[tpl]cite web|title=Model 572 Fieldmaster Pump Action|url=http://www.remington.com/products/archived/rimfire/pump-action/model-572-fieldmaster.aspx|publisher=Remington Arms|accessdate=26 December 2012[/tpl]
