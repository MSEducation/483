[[The Best of Echobelly]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 greatest hits albums, Echobelly albums

The Best of Echobelly is the second greatest hits collection from Britpop band Echobelly, released in 2008. The songs on the album all come from the band's first three albums, Everyone's Got One, On and Lustra.
