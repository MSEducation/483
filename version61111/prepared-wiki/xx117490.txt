[[Xenocytaea daviesae]]

CATEGORIES: Salticidae

Xenocytaea daviesae is a jumping spider.

==Name==

Named in honor of Valerie Todd Davies, a Salticid specialist from Queensland Museum, Australia.

==Appearance==

X. daviesae males are about 3mm long, with females slightly more than  4mm.

==Distribution==

X. daviesae is only known from Viti Levu, Fiji.
