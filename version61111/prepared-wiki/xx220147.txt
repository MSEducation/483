[[Pitești Sud Power Station]]

CATEGORIES: Natural gas-fired power stations in Romania, Coal-fired power stations in Romania

The Piteşti Sud Power Station is a large thermal power plant located in Piteşti, having 2 generation groups of 50 MW each and 3 groups of 12 MW having a total electricity generation capacity of 136 MW.Presentare CET Piteşti [tpl]ro[/tpl]
