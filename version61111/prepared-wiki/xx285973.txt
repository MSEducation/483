[[Urban Boyz]]

CATEGORIES: Rwandan music

Urban Boyz is a Rwandan R&B Afropop group composed of members James Manzi (Humble Jizzo), Safi Niyibikora (Safi Lee) and Muhammed Nshimiyimana (Nizzo). A reviewer for the Rwandan newspaper New Times described them in 2013 as "the country's leading music group."[tpl]cite news | last = Kagire | first =  Linda M. | title = Rwanda: Urban Boys Release New Single 'Kelele' | newspaper = New Times | location = Rwanda | date = 23 April 2013 | url = http://allafrica.com/stories/201304240353.html | accessdate = 12 August 2013 [/tpl] 

==History==

Urban Boyz released their debut album, Icyicaro Today, in 2008 and have since released more than 70 singles and three studio albums. They have collaborated with numerous regional artists, including Rwandans Riderman and King James and Ugandans Jackie Chandiru, Rabadaba and Goodlyfe crew of Radio and Weasel.[tpl]cite news | last = Mugoya | first =  Grace | title = Rwanda: We Shall Not Have a Manager Soon — Urban Boys | newspaper = New Times | location = Rwanda | date = 4 June 2013 | url = http://allafrica.com/stories/201306050094.html | accessdate = 12 August 2013 [/tpl] The group performs songs written in English, Kiswahili and Kinyarwanda.

==Awards==

Urban Boyz took second place in the annual Primus Guma Guma Super Star talent competition, held at Amahoro Stadium in August 2013.[tpl]cite news | last = Ndushabandi  | first = Charles  | title = Riderman Wins Rwanda Talent Search | newspaper = New Times | location = Rwanda | date = 11 August 2013 | url = http://chimpreports.com/index.php/entertainment/12109-riderman-wins-rwanda-talent-search.html | accessdate = 12 August 2013 [/tpl] Their single, Bibaye, was voted song of the year. In March 2013, Urban Boyz won four Salax Awards in the categories Artiste of the Year, Best Male Artiste, Best Group and Best Video.[tpl]cite news | last = Asiimwe  | first = Peninah  | title = Rwanda: Urban Boys Shine At Salax Awards | newspaper = New Times | location = Rwanda | date = 11 March 2013 | url = http://allafrica.com/stories/201303120083.html | accessdate = 12 August 2013 [/tpl]
