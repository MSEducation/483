Pablo Herrera Barrantes (born 14 February 1987, in Alajuela) is a Costa Rican professional footballer. He currently plays for Cartaginés.

==Club career==

After playing for Alajuelense, Herrera joined Norwegian club Aalesund in August 2009.[tpl]cite news |url=http://fotball.smp.no/eliteserien/article147138.ece |language=Norwegian |title=Klubbene enige om Herrera |publisher=Sunnmørsposten |date=8 August 2009 |accessdate=9 September 2012[/tpl] After the 2011-season, Aalesund and Herrera decided to terminate the contract, as Herrera never managed to recover from an injury.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.nrk.no/nyheter/distrikt/more_og_romsdal/1.7971166 |language=Norwegian |title=Herrera ferdig i AaFK |publisher=NRK |date=27 January 2012 |accessdate=9 September 2012[/tpl]

==International career==

Herrera is part of the Costa Rica national football team, his debut coming in a friendly against Peru on 22 August 2007.[tpl]cite web|publisher=RSSSF|title=International Friendly Matches 2007|url=http://www.rsssf.com/tablesf/friend-intres2007.html|accessdate=2008-10-17[/tpl] He scored his first goal in a 2010 FIFA World Cup qualifying match against the United States on 3 June 2009. He played also in Liga Deportiva Alajuelense and scores many goals including one to Saprissa 
Herrera also played at the 2007 FIFA U-20 World Cup in Canada.[tpl]FIFA player|268828|Pablo Herrera Barrantes[/tpl]
