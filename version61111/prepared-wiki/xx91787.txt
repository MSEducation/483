[[Gilles Fauconnier]]

CATEGORIES: 1944 births, Living people, Cognitive scientists, French science writers, University of California, San Diego faculty, Linguists from France, Metaphor theorists

Gilles Fauconnier ([tpl]IPA-fr|ʒil fokɔˈnje[/tpl]) (born 19 August 1944) is a French linguist, researcher in cognitive science, and author, currently working in the U.S.  He is a professor at the University of California, San Diego, in the Department of Cognitive Science.
His work with Mark Turner founded the theory of conceptual blending.
