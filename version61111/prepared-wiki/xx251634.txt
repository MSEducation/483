[[Rockdale Community Church]]

CATEGORIES: Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Churches completed in 1860, Churches in Chenango County, New York

Rockdale Community Church is a historic church on New York State Route 8 in the hamlet of Rockdale, Chenango County, New York. It was built in 1860 and is a one story rectangular wood frame building, three bays long and three bays wide.  It sits on a stone foundation and is surmounted by a gable roof.  It features a two stage tower topped by a small cross.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=101257|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: Rockdale Community Church|date=December 2004|accessdate=2010-02-20 |author=Kathleen LaFrank|publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation[/tpl] See also: [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=101259|title=Accompanying six photos[/tpl] 
It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 2005.
