[[Arges]]

CATEGORIES: Cyclopes

Arges ([tpl]lang-gr|Ἄργης[/tpl]) was one of the Cyclopes in Greek mythology.  He was elsewhere called AcmonidesOvid, Fasti iv. 288 or Pyraemon.Virgil, Aeneid viii. 425 His name means "bright" and represents the brightness from lightning. He is one of Gaia's children by Uranus. In fear, Uranus is said to have locked Arges, along with his brothers in Tartarus. They were later freed to fashion lightning bolts for Zeus during his attempt to overthrow Cronus
