Yeh Shaam Mastani is a reality musical show featuring seven singers weaving 'saat suro ka magic'.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.indiantelevision.com/headlines/y2k8/apr/apr369.php|title=Sony to launch ‘Yeh Shaam Mastani’ on 3 May|work=Indiantelevision.com|date=April 29, 2008[/tpl] The show is hosted by Mini Mathur, who is known for her work as a host in Indian Idol television series. The show will air on the weekends on Sony TV, starting May 3, 2008. 

==Concept==

The show will have the country’s seven top singers who will regale audiences by singing their all-time favorites. Each episode will showcase the musical prowess of one artist who will perform live for the studio audience. These singers will take the stage to perform some of their greatest (solo or duet) hits and get up-close and personal with the viewers. Besides that the singers will also take requests for songs from their fans.
