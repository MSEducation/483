[[Serdinya]]

CATEGORIES: Serdinya, Communes of Pyrénées-Orientales

Serdinya ([tpl]lang-ca|Serdinyà[/tpl]) is a commune in the Pyrénées-Orientales department in southern France. 

==Transportation==

Route nationale 116 (RN 116) connecting Perpignan and Bourg-Madame (near the Spanish and Andorran borders) passes through Serdinya.
