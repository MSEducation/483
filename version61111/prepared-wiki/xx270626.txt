[[Adrián Čermák]]

CATEGORIES: 1993 births, Living people, Slovak footballers, Association football midfielders, ŠK Slovan Bratislava players, FC Nitra players, Slovak First League players, Sportspeople from Malacky

Adrián Čermák (born 1 July 1993 in Malacky) is a Slovak football midfielder who currently plays for the Corgoň Liga club FC Nitra, on loan from ŠK Slovan Bratislava.

==ŠK Slovan Bratislava==

Čermák made his debut for Slovan Bratislava against PŠC Pezinok in the 2nd leg of the 2011–12 Slovak Cup.
