[[Falcone (TV series)]]

CATEGORIES: 2000s American television series, 2000 American television series debuts, 2000 American television series endings, American drama television series, CBS network shows, English-language television programming, Television series by CBS Paramount Television, Television series by Sony Pictures Television, Television series about organized crime, Films based on non-fiction books about organized crime

Falcone is an American TV series on CBS which lasted only one season. The plot follows Joseph D. Pistone (Jason Gedrick), who goes undercover to bring down the mob. This was based on a true story, also having been dramatized in the movie Donnie Brasco. also Based on the book Donnie Brasco-My Undercover Life in the Mafia by FBI agent Joseph D.
