[[3 (Alejandro Sanz album)]]

CATEGORIES: 1995 albums, Alejandro Sanz albums, Warner Music Latina albums

3 is the third studio album recorded by Spanish singer-songwriter Alejandro Sanz. This album reaffirmed the success obtained with his previous albums and made him popular in Europe and Latin America. The album was also recorded in Portuguese and Italian. All songs were written by Alejandro Sanz except "Quiero Morir en Tu Veneno". The official singles of the album were "La Fuerza del Corazón", "Mi Soledad y Yo", "¿Lo Ves?" and "Quiero Morir en Tu Veneno", and he shot a video for each single.
