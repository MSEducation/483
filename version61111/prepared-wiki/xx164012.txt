[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Port Harcourt]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic Church in Nigeria, Roman Catholic dioceses in Nigeria, Religious organizations established in 1961, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century, Port Harcourt, 1961 establishments in Nigeria

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Port Hartcourt ([tpl]lang-la|Portus Harcurten(sis)[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Port Harcourt, Rivers State in the Ecclesiastical province of Calabar in Nigeria.
