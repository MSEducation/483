[[McKinnon railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1884

McKinnon railway station is located on the Frankston line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the south-eastern Melbourne suburb of McKinnon opening on 1 September 1884 as McKinnon Road, being renamed McKinnon on 14 December 1885.McKinnon Vicsig
In the morning peak hour, Frankston bound services utilise Platform 3 with Flinders Street bound trains utilising Platforms 1 and 2. At other times Frankston bound trains call at Platform 2.
