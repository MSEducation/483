Mauro Ramiro Milano (born 18 January 1984 in Buenos Aires) is an Argentinean football midfielder and striker he plays for Club Atlético Huracán in the Primera B Nacional.

==Career==

Milano made his debut in Argentina's Primera División as a player for Club Atlético Huracán. He was later transferred to Asteras Tripolis of Super League Greece. After two years in Tripoli, he accepted an offer from Iraklis Thessaloniki F.C..
