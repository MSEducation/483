[[Brennen Carvalho]]

Brennen Michael-Kapono Carvalho (born December 31, 1985 in Kapa'a, Hawaii) is an American football center for the Philadelphia Soul of the Arena Football League (AFL). He was signed by the Green Bay Packers as an undrafted free agent in 2008. He played college football at Portland State.

==Professional career==

===Pre-draft===

Prior to the 2008 NFL Draft, Barvalho was projected to be a 7th round to undrafted by NFLDraftScout.com. ===Green Bay Packers===

After going undrafted, Carvalho spent the 2008 season on the practice squad of the Green Bay Packers.

===Arizona Rattlers===

In 2010 and 2011, Carvalho played for the Arizona Rattlers of the Arena Football League.

===Philadelphia Soul===

Carvalho was assigned to the Philadelphia Soul for the 2012 season, where he anchored a line that lost ArenaBowl XXV.
