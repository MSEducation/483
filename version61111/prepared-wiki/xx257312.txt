[[Bolivian general election, 1966]]

CATEGORIES: Elections in Bolivia, 1966 elections in South America, 1966 in Bolivia

General elections were held in Bolivia on 3 July 1966.Nohlen, D (2005) Elections in the Americas: A data handbook, Volume II, p133 ISBN 978-0-19-928358-3 René Barrientos of the Front of the Bolivian Revolution (FSB) was elected President with 67% of the vote,Nohlen, p150 whilst the FSB won a majority in both houses of Congress.

==Background==

Following the 1964 elections, Barrientos had led a military coup to remove Víctor Paz Estenssoro from power.
