[[Continuum (topology)]]

CATEGORIES: Continuum theory

In the mathematical field of point-set topology, a continuum (pl continua) is a nonempty compact connected metric space, or less frequently, a compact connected Hausdorff space. Continuum theory is the branch of topology devoted to the study of continua.

==Properties==

There are two fundamental techniques for constructing continua, by means of nested intersections and inverse limits.
A finite or countable product of continua is a continuum.
