[[Ebrahimabad, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Ebrahimabad ([tpl]lang-fa|ابراهيم اباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Ebrāhīmābād; also known as Abramabad, Ibrāhīmābād, and Ibrakhimabad)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3061956|Ebrahimabad[/tpl] is a village in Bonab Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 166, in 34 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
