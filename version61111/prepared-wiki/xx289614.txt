[[Arqin]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Arqin ([tpl]lang-fa|ارقين[/tpl], also Romanized as Arqīn; also known as Arghīn and Arkin)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3053626|Arqin[/tpl] is a village in Aq Bolagh Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 936, in 209 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
