[[Mister Know-It-All]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 American television series debuts, 2013 web series debuts, 2010s American television series, American web series, Comedy web series, Wired (magazine), YouTube series

Mister Know-It-All (or Mr. Know-It-All) is an American animated web series distributed by technology news magazine Wired, based on the popular advice column.

==Background==

After Wired joined Condé Nast's Digital Video Network, five original web series were announced for Wired's video channel including Mister Know-It-All and Codefellas.[tpl]cite web|author=Erik Hayden|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/conde-nast-entertainment-launches-wired-523288|title=Conde Nast Entertainment Launches 'Wired' Video Channel - The Hollywood Reporter|publisher=The Hollywood Reporter|date=15 May 2013|accessdate=2013-08-11[/tpl]
