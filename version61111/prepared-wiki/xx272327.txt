[[Gravesham Council election, 2003]]

CATEGORIES: English local elections, 2003, Gravesham Council elections

The 2003 Gravesham Council election took place on 1 May 2003 to elect members of Gravesham Borough Council in Kent, England. The whole council was up for election with boundary changes since the last election in 1999.[tpl]cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/shared/bsp/hi/vote2003/locals/html/120.stm|title=Local elections|publisher=BBC News Online|accessdate=25 September 2011[/tpl] The Labour party stayed in overall control of the council.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/uknews/1429056/Local-council-election-results.html|title=Local council election results|date=3 May 2003|work=The Daily Telegraph|accessdate=25 September 2011[/tpl]
