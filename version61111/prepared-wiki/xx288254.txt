[[Sonali Majumdar]]

CATEGORIES: 2005 births, Living people, People from North 24 Parganas district, Reality show winners

Sonali Majumdar (born; 2005) is an Indian professional salsa dancer. She was born in Sholoardari village located in North 24 Parganas district of West Bengal state near India-Bangladesh border.http://www.filmyfolks.com/kmh/sonali-majumdar.shtml
She is the winner of Colors TV reality show India's Got Talent (season 4).http://indiatoday.intoday.in/story/indias-got-talent-salsa-dancers-maraju-sumanth-sonali-majumdar-india-today/1/234614.html

==Career==

Sonali started her dancing career with India's Got Talent as contestant on Colors TV, now she will be seen in dancing show Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa 6 on Colors TV.
