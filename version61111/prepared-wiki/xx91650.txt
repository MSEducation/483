[[Qahtanite]]

CATEGORIES: Semitic peoples, Descendants of Eber, Qahtanites, Tribes of Arabia, History of Yemen, Yemeni tribes

The terms Qahtanite and Qahtani ([tpl]lang-ar|قَحْطَانِي from قَحْطَان[/tpl]; transliterated: Qahtani or Qaḥṭānī or Kahtani) refer to one of the main groups of Arab peoples either originating in, or claiming genealogical descent from the southern extent of the Arabian Peninsula, especially from Yemen.[ref]
