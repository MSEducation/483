[[Unnamed EP]]

CATEGORIES: 2010 EPs, Fuck the Facts albums

Unnamed EP is the fourth EP by Canadian grindcore band Fuck the Facts. ==Recording==

The drums were recorded on November 15, 2009 at Raven Street Studios by Dave Sarazin, while the band recorded the rest at Super Pro Studio in Gatineau between November 16 and 28. Craig Boychuk mixed the album at CB Audio, and Alan Douches mastered the album at West West Side in December.[tpl]cite web |url=http://blogs.myspace.com/index.cfm?fuseaction=blog.view&friendId=3028679&blogId=525004584 |title=Unnamed EP (Studio Video) |last=Das |first=Topon |date=January 7, 2010 |work=Myspace.com/FuckTheFacts |publisher=Fuck the Facts |accessdate=January 7, 2010[/tpl]
