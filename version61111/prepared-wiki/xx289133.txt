[[Mohammadlu, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Mohammadlu ([tpl]lang-fa|محمدلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Moḩammadlū)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3773286|Mohammadlu[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 147, in 33 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
