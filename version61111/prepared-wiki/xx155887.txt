[[İzmir Institute of Technology]]

CATEGORIES: Technical universities and colleges, Engineering universities and colleges in Turkey, Institutes of Technology in Turkey, Educational institutions established in 1992, İzmir Institute of Technology, 1992 establishments in Turkey, Universities and colleges in Izmir

İzmir Institute of Technology ([tpl]lang-tr|İzmir Yüksek Teknoloji Enstitüsü[/tpl], commonly referred to as İYTE) is a public research universityİzmir Institute Of Technology in İzmir, Turkey. İYTE maintains a strong emphasis on the natural sciences and engineering. 
The medium of instruction in all the departments of İYTE is English.
