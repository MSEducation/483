[[Belize International Film Festival]]

CATEGORIES: Latin American cinema, Belizean culture, Recurring events established in 2003, Cinema of Belize

The Belize International Film Festival is an international film festival held annually in Belize City, Belize since 2003. It focuses on Central American and Caribbean films. In 2005 a presentation was given on Nicaraguan cinema.  In 2012, for the first time in its history a Belizean feature film (2012: Kurse a di Xtabai) was presented as the opening night movie.
