[[Peter J. Paulsen House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses completed in 1895, Queen Anne architecture in Iowa, Houses in Davenport, Iowa, Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Iowa, National Register of Historic Places in Scott County, Iowa

The Peter J. Paulsen House is located on the hill above downtown Davenport, Iowa, United States.  The Queen Anne style residence has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since 1983.
