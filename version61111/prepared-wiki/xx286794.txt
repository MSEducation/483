[[Chulcheh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Chulcheh ([tpl]lang-fa|چولچه[/tpl], also Romanized as Chūlcheh; also known as Choldzhekh and Chūleh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3058884|Chulcheh[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 90, in 23 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
