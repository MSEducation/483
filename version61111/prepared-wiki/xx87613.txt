[[Syndal railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1930

Syndal railway station is located on the Glen Waverley line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the eastern Melbourne suburb of Syndal opening on 5 May 1930.Syndal Vicsig

==Platforms & services==

Syndal has one island platform with two sides. It is serviced by Metro Trains' Glen Waverley line services.Glen Waverley Line timetable Public Transport Victoria
