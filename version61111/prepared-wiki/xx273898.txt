[[State's Attorney (film)]]

CATEGORIES: 1932 films, American drama films, RKO Pictures films, Films made before the MPAA Production Code, Films directed by George Archainbaud, 1930s drama films

State's Attorney is a 1932 talking drama film made at RKO and starring John Barrymore. George Archainbaud directed and the film could more or less be considered a warm up for Barrymore when he later went to Universal to film the similar Counsellor at Law. Remade in 1937 and 1951 both titled Criminal Lawyer.The American Film Institute Catalog Feature Films: 1931-40 by The American Film Institute, c.1993
