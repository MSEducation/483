[[Belgian general election, 1965]]

CATEGORIES: 1965 elections in Europe, Elections in Belgium, 1965 in Belgium

General elections were held in Belgium on 23 May 1965.Nohlen, D & Stöver, P (2010) Elections in Europe: A data handbook, p289 ISBN 978-3-8329-5609-7 The result was a victory for the Christian Social Party, which won 77 of the 212 seats in the Chamber of Representatives and 44 of the 106 seats in the Senate.Nohlen & Stöver, pp309-311 Voter turnout was 91.6%.Nohlen & Stöver, p291
