[[Sükhbaatar Province]]

CATEGORIES: Sükhbaatar Province, Provinces of Mongolia

Sükhbaatar ([tpl]lang-mn|Сүхбаатар[/tpl]) is one of the 21 aimags of Mongolia, located in the east of the country. Its capital is Baruun-Urt. It is named after Damdin Sükhbaatar.

==Population==

==Transportation==

The Baruun-Urt Airport (UUN/ZMBU) has one unpaved runway and is served by regular flights to Ulaanbaatar.

==Administrative Subdivision==

The Aimag capital Baruun-Urt is geographically located wihin the Sühbaatar sum, but administrated independently.
