[[Makroulo]]

CATEGORIES: Lasithi, Uninhabited islands of Greece

Makroulo ([tpl]lang-el|Μακρουλό[/tpl], "long"), is an uninhabited Greek islet, located south of cape Goudero on the coast of Lasithi, eastern Crete, in the Libyan Sea. It forms a close group of islands with Koufonisi, Marmaro, Strongyli, and Trachilos.
