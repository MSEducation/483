Radoslav Mitrevski ([tpl]lang-bg|Радослав Митревски[/tpl]; born 22 April 1981) is a Bulgarian footballer, currently playing for Septemvri Simitli as a defender. 
Mitrevski previously played for CSKA Sofia, Marek Dupnitsa and Pirin Blagoevgrad.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Guardian.co.uk|title=Stats Centre: Radoslav Mitrevski Facts|url=http://guardian.touch-line.com/StatsCentre.asp?CTID=7&CPID=11&TEID=339&PLID=174989&pStr=Player|accessdate=2010-01-12[/tpl]
