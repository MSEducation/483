[[Working Class Millionaire]]

CATEGORIES: Richard Digance albums, 2007 compilation albums, Folk compilation albums

Working Class Millionaire - The Transatlantic Anthology is a 2007 album by the English musician and entertainer Richard Digance. It was released on double-disc CD and contains a total of 35 tracks recorded during Richard's many years of performing.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/tracks/B000F6ZFFO |title="Working Class Millionaire" track listing and details |publisher=Amazon[/tpl]
