[[Almere Poort railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations on the Flevolijn, Railway stations in Flevoland, Almere

Almere Poort is a railway station in the city of Almere in the Dutch province of Flevoland. It is located on the Flevolijn and is expected to replace Almere Strand railway station as the city's sixth railway station.
The opening of the station was first planned for 2007, but it was finally opened on 9 December 2012.ProRail The station serves the new borough Almere Poort which is also under development since 2007 and will have 11,000 dwellings when it is finished.[tpl]Citation needed|date=June 2011[/tpl]
