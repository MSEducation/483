CATEGORIES: Glossop North End A.F.C., English football club seasons

Glossop North End A.F.C. is an English football club based in Glossop, Derbyshire.  Former members of the Football League, the club's first team currently play in the North West Counties League Premier Division. They play their home matches at Surrey Street, which has a capacity of 2,374 (209 seated, 2,165 standing). The team play in blue, and are known as the "Hillmen". Between 1899 and 1992 the club was known simply as Glossop.
