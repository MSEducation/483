[[Maracaibo wood turtle]]

CATEGORIES: Rhinoclemmys, Reptiles of Colombia, Reptiles of Venezuela

The Maracaibo wood turtle (Rhinoclemmys diademata or Rhinoclemmys dademata) is a species of turtles belonging to the Rhinoclemmys genus of the family Geoemydida found in  Colombia and Venezuela.[tpl]sfn|Fritz|2007|p=242[/tpl]
