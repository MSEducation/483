[[Telmac TMC-600]]

CATEGORIES: Early microcomputers

The Telmac TMC-600 was a Finnish microcomputer produced during the early 1980s starting in 1982. It was introduced in 1982 by Telercas, the Finnish importer of RCA microchips. Only 600 units were produced, making it very rare today. The TMC-600 was the only commercially available microcomputer designed and manufactured in Finland.tietokonemuseo.net - Telmac TMC 600, 2006-08-03
