[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Foz do Iguaçu]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Cascavel, Religious organizations established in 1926, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century, Foz do Iguaçu

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Foz do Iguaçu ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Iguassuensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Foz do Iguaçu in the Ecclesiastical province of Cascavel in Brazil.
