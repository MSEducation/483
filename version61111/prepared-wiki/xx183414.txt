[[Lunar Eclipse (album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2000 albums, David Bryan albums

Lunar Eclipse is the second solo instrumental album by David Bryan from the band Bon Jovi. Released in 2000, it features all the songs from On a Full Moon (his first album) except for "Awakening" and "Midnight Voodoo", instead of those two tracks it features two new tracks: "Second Chance" and "I Can Love". Also, his version of the Bon Jovi song In These Arms has David on vocals for this album, previously only a instrumental version.
