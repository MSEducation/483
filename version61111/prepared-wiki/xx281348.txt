[[Stuart Reid (Scottish historical writer)]]

CATEGORIES: 1954 births, Living people, Date of birth missing (living people), People from Aberdeen, Scottish writers, Scottish historians, British military officers

Stuart Reid (born 1954, Aberdeen, Scotland) is a writer, librarian and retired British military officer. His writing focuses on military history, particularly from the 18th and 19th centuries. Reid's own ancestors served in the British Army and the East India Company, who fought at, among other places and sites, Culloden, Bunker Hill, and the Texas Revolution.
