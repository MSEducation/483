[[Höga Kusten Flyg]]

CATEGORIES: Airlines of Sweden, Airlines established in 2007, Swedish companies established in 2007

Höga Kusten Flyg is an airline based in Örnsköldsvik, Sweden. It operates scheduled services in Sweden.

==History==

The airline was established in 2007 to operate domestic flights between Stockholm, Sweden and Örnsköldsvik, Sweden.
