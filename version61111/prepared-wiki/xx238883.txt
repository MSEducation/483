[[Storm World Tour]]

CATEGORIES: Tarja Turunen concert tours, 2007 concert tours, 2008 concert tours, 2009 concert tours

Storm World Tour (or simply Storm Tour) was the first world tour by Finnish singer Tarja Turunen to support her second studio album, My Winter Storm, released on November 19, 2007, by Universal Music; it was Tarja's first world tour since her dismissal from Nightwish in 2005, playing for the first time in several countries like Luxembourg, Serbia, Israel, Ukraine, Bulgaria and Belarus.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.tarjaturunen.com/?cat=11|title=Tarja: Past Events|publisher=Tarja's Official Website|accessdate=2011-03-18[/tpl]
The tour was divided in seven legs between 2007 and 2009, with ten warm up concerts in Europe between November and December.[tpl]cite web|url=http://winterstormbrasil.com/ttbr/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=960&Itemid=134|title=Tarja: Events in 2008|publisher=Winter Storm Brazil|accessdate=2011-03-18[/tpl] In 2008, she played in South America and finished the year again in Europe; in 2009, Tarja played in North America, with dates in the United States and Canada, and then she hosted new concerts in South America, debuting in Venezuela.[tpl]cite web|url=http://winterstormbrasil.com/ttbr/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=989&Itemid=156|title=Tarja: Events in 2009|publisher=Winter Storm Brazil|accessdate=2011-03-18[/tpl] 
During the last leg, Tarja played for the first time in Turkey and Croatia, and finished the tour in London, England, on October 19, 2009, after 95 concerts.
