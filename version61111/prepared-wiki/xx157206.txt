Juan Carlos Mariño Márquez (born 2 January 1982 in Lima) is a Peruvian footballer who plays primarily as an attacking midfielder. He currently plays for Mexican club Delfines in the Ascenso MX.

==International career==

He has made 22 appearances for the Peru national football team.[tpl]cite web|publisher=RSSSF|author=Pierrend, José Luis|title=Peru - Record International Players|url=http://www.rsssf.com/miscellaneous/peru-recintlp.html|date=2009-05-28[/tpl]
Mariño made his debut as a substitute during his first Peru cap in the opening match vs Uruguay in the first round of the Copa America 2007, scoring a goal. Mariño scored his second international goal against Colombia on June 14, 2008.
He also played for Hércules in Spain.
