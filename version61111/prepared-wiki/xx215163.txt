[[Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Host for a Reality or Reality-Competition Program]]

CATEGORIES: Primetime Emmy Awards

Outstanding Host for a Reality or Reality-Competition Program was an award created in 2008.  On July 27, 2008, it was announced that this category's 5 nominees would all co-host the 2008 Primetime Emmy Awards telecast.
