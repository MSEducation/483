[[King Ai of Zhou]]

CATEGORIES: 441 BC deaths, Zhou Dynasty kings, 5th-century BC rulers, Year of birth unknown

King Ai of Zhou ([tpl]zh|c=周哀王|p=Zhōu Āi Wáng[/tpl]) was the twenty-eighth king of the Chinese Zhou Dynasty and the sixteenth of Eastern Zhou.  He was the eldest son of King Zhending of Zhou.Sima Qian: Records of the Grand Historian
He succeeded his father in 441, but was killed by his younger brother after only three months on the throne.Family tree of Chinese kings
