[[Hamois]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Belgium, Municipalities of Namur (province)

Hamois ([tpl]IPA-fr|am.mwa[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-wa|Hamwè[/tpl]) is a Walloon municipality and village located in the Belgian province of Namur. The village is around 25 km south-east of the city of Namur.
On 1 January 2006 the municipality had 6,640 inhabitants. The total area is 76.42 km², giving a population density of 87 inhabitants per km².
