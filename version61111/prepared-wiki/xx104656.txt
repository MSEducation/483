[[Board of Supervision of Estate Agents (Sweden)]]

CATEGORIES: Government agencies of Sweden, Real estate in Sweden

Swedish Board of Supervision of Estate Agents (Fastighetsmäklarnämnden) is a Swedish government agency that answers to the Ministry of Agriculture, Food and Consumer Affairs. The agency is located in Stockholm.
It handles tasks related to the state supervision of real estate agents.
