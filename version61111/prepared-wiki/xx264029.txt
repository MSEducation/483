[[TSS Scotia (1902)]]

CATEGORIES: 1902 ships, Passenger ships of the United Kingdom, Steamships, Clyde-built ships, Ships of the London and North Western Railway, Ferries of the Isle of Man, Armed boarding steamers of the Royal Navy

TSS ''Scotia'' was a twin screw steamer passenger vessel operated by the London and North Western Railway from 1902 to 1923.Railway and Other Steamers, Duckworth. 1962

==History==

She was built by William Denny and Brothers of Dumbarton for the London and North Western Railway in 1902.
She was requisitioned by the Admiralty as an Armed boarding steamer in 1914.
She was renamed TSS ''Menevia'' in 1920. In 1928 she was sold to the Isle of Man Steam Packet Company and scrapped later that year by T. W. Ward.
