Philip Stanley George (born 16 September 1978) is a former Welsh cricketer.  George was a right-handed batsman who bowled right-arm medium pace.

==Biography==

Philip  George was born in India on 16 September 1978.
George made his debut for Wales Minor Counties in the 1997 MCCA Knockout Trophy Devon.
