[[Caseolus calculus]]

CATEGORIES: Caseolus

Caseolus calculus (Common name : Madeiran land snail) is a species of small air-breathing land snails, terrestrial pulmonate gastropod mollusks in the family Hygromiidae, the hairy snails and their allies. 

==Distribution and conservation status==

This species lives in Europe. It is mentioned in annexes II and IV of Habitats Directive.
