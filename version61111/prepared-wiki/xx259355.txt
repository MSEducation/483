[[Tinatin Chulukhadze]]

CATEGORIES: 1991 births, Living people, Female singers from Georgia (country)

Tinatin Chulukhadze, ( in Georgian: თინათინ ჭულუხაძე ) born in 1991, is a Georgian Pop and Folk singer. She's been popular since 2006 when she won Geostar - Georgian franchise of the Idol series. She is also known to people as Tiko Chulukhadze (short version of her first name.)
She also participated in musical TV Show Bravo in 2010.
