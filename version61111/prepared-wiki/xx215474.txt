[[Christos Naidos]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1979 births, Greek footballers, Panetolikos players, Aris Thessaloniki F.C. players

Christos Naidos ([tpl]lang-el|χρηστος ναιντος[/tpl]; born 24 December 1979) is a professional football defender currently playing for Apollon Kalamarias F.C. in the Greek third division.

==Career==

Born in Thessaloniki, Naidos began playing football with Poseidon Michaniona. He joined Greek Superleague side Aris Thessaloniki F.C. from Agrotikos Asteras F.C. in January 2004.[tpl]cite web|publisher=RSSSF|author=Mastrogiannopoulos, Alexander|title=Greece 2003/04 (First and Second Level Details)|url=http://www.rsssf.com/tablesg/grk04det.html|date=19 September 2004[/tpl]
