[[Rotax 462]]

CATEGORIES: Air-cooled aircraft piston engines, Rotax engines, Two-stroke aircraft piston engines

The Rotax 462 is a [tpl]convert|51|hp|kW|0|abbr=on[/tpl], two-cylinder, two-stroke aircraft engine, derived from a snowmobile engine. It was built by Rotax of Austria for use in ultralight aircraft.
