[[Sue Scott (sociologist)]]

CATEGORIES: British  Sociologist, Living people, Women sociologists, Presidents of the British Sociological Association

Sue Scott is a British sociologist and feminist whose research has focused primarily on sexuality, gender and risk. She is an Honorary Professor of Sociology and Women's Studies at the University of York, an Honorary Professorial Fellow in Sociology at the University of Edinburgh and a Visiting Professor at the University of  Helsinki. She is Managing Editor (with Professor John Holmwood) of 'Discover Society' an online magazine for sociological and social policy research.
From 2009-2012 she was Pro Vice-Chancellor at Glasgow Caledonian University. She served as President of the British Sociological Association from 2007 to 2009. She has previously served as Dean of the Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences at Keele University And Post-Graduate Dean at Durham University.She has been a member of the ESRC Research Grants Board and iwas co chair of the steering group for the ESRC’s International Benchmarking of Sociology.http://www.gcu.ac.uk/theuniversity/principalvice-chancellorsoffice/theuniversityexecutive/professorsuescott/
