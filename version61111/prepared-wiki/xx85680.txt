[[Flag of the Republic of Tatarstan]]

CATEGORIES: Tatarstan, Flags of the federal subjects of Russia, 1991 introductions

The Flag of Tatarstan was adopted November 29, 1991 and was designed by Täwil Ğiniät ulı Xaziäxmätev (pronounced [tpl]IPA-tt|tæˈwil ɣiniɪˈæt uˈlɯ xæziəxˈmætəf|[/tpl]).[tpl]cn|date=October 2013[/tpl] The green, the colour of Islam, represents the Tatars; the red stands for the Russians.[tpl]cn|date=October 2013[/tpl] The white stripe symbolizes peace between the Tatar majority and the Russian minority.[tpl]cn|date=October 2013[/tpl]
