Edward B. Stratton, often known as E.B. Stratton, was an American architect based in Boston.  Several of his works are listed on the U.S. National Register of Historic Places.[tpl]NRISref|version=2010a[/tpl]
He was born in Chelsea, Boston Chelsea, Massachusetts and went to Chelsea and Boston schools.  He studied at least briefly at MIT and "at an atelier in Paris".[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.brooklinema.gov/index.php?option=com_docman&task=doc_download&gid=7087 |title=Brookline Preservation Commission Demolition Application Report: 198 Dean Road |publisher=Brookline[/tpl]
