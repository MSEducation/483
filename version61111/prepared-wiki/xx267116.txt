[[Lorenzo Scattorin]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Male actors from Milan, Italian male voice actors, 1971 births

 Lorenzo Scattorin (born September 22, 1971 in Milan) is an Italian voice actor. He contributes to voicing characters in anime, cartoons, movies, and other content.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.antoniogenna.net/doppiaggio/voci/vocilsca.htm |title=Lorenzo Scattorin's dubbing contributions|publisher=Antoniogenna.net |date= |accessdate=2011-09-23[/tpl]
He is well known for providing the voice of the character Sanji in the Italian-language version of the long-running anime series One Piece. Scattorin also voiced Seto Kaiba in the Italian-language version of the anime series Yu-Gi-Oh!.
He is also the son of the actor Maurizio Scattorin. He works at Merak Film, Studio Asci, Studio P.V.,and other dubbing studios in Italy.
