[[Bandhan (1940 film)]]

CATEGORIES: 1940 films, Hindi-language films, Indian films

Bandhan  is a 1940 Indian Bollywood film directed by N.R. Acharya. It stars Leela Chitnis, Ashok Kumar and Suresh. It was the second highest grossing Indian film of 1940.[tpl]cite web|title=Top Earners 1940|url=http://www.boxofficeindia.com/showProd.php?itemCat=145&catName=MTk0MA==|publisher=Box Office India|accessdate=26 September 2011[/tpl] It was produced by Bombay Talkies.
