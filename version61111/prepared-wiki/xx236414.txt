[[Further Down the Old Plank Road]]

CATEGORIES: 2003 albums, The Chieftains albums, RCA Victor albums

Further Down the Old Plank Road is a 2003 album by The Chieftains.  It is a collaboration between the Irish band and many top country music musicians including Rosanne Cash, Chet Atkins, The Nitty Gritty Dirt Band, Ricky Skaggs, and Patty Loveless.Further Down the Old Plank Road,Retrieved 12 April 2014.
