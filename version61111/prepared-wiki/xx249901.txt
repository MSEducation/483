[[James Robert McLean]]

CATEGORIES: 1823 births, 1870 deaths, Confederate States of America political leaders, People from Halifax County, North Carolina

James Robert McLean (September 21, 1823 - April 15, 1870) was a prominent Confederate politician. He was born in Halifax County, North Carolina and later represented the state in the First Confederate Congress from 1862 to 1864. He also served in the Confederate Army.http://politicalgraveyard.com/bio/mclean.html
