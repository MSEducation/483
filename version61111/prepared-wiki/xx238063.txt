[[Terminal Range]]

CATEGORIES: Mountain ranges of British Columbia, Liard Country, Ranges of the Canadian Rockies

The Terminal Range is the northernmost mountain range of the Canadian Rockies, so-named for its position at the northern terminus of the Rockies.[tpl]BCGNIS|15061|Terminal Range[/tpl]  Lying west of Muncho Lake and the Trout River, its northern permeter is the Liard River.  The Sentinel Range lies to its east.[tpl]BCGNIS|18860|Sentinel Range[/tpl]
