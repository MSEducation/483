[[Drepanostachyum]]

CATEGORIES: Bamboo genera

Drepanostachyum is a genus of medium-sized mountain clumping bamboos.

==Taxonomy==

The differences between this genus and Himalayacalamus are subtle: Drepanostachyum species have many equal branches while those of  Himalayacalamus have one dominant branch. Several species precedently comprised here have been moved to Himalayacalamus.
