[[Abraham Cohen Pimentel]]

CATEGORIES: Early Acharonim, 17th-century rabbis, Year of birth unknown, 1697 deaths

Abraham Cohen Pimentel (died March 21, 1697) was a rabbi of Amsterdam.  He was a student of Saul Levi Morteira, and he also served as hakham of the synagogue in Hamburgbibliopolis.com and was initially a signator to a letter of approbation for Sabbatai Zevi.The Sabbatean Movement in Amsterdam  He was the author of the Minchat Kohen, published in 1668.
