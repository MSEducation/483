[[Giovanni Battista Zaist]]

CATEGORIES: 1700 births, 1757 deaths, People from Cremona, 18th-century Italian painters, Cremonese painters, Italian Baroque painters, Italian art historians

Giovanni Battista Zaist (Cremona, June 14, 1700 – September 29, 1757) was an Italian painter and art historian.
He was a pupil of Giuseppe Natali. Zaist painted for the church of Santi Egidio ed Omobono and the oratory of San Girolamo.
