[[Little Braxted]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Essex, Maldon (district), Civil parishes in Essex

Little Braxted is a village and a civil parish located near the town of Witham, in the Maldon district, in the county of Essex, England. Little Braxted has a church called St Nicholas.http://www.achurchnearyou.com/little-braxted-st-nicholas/
