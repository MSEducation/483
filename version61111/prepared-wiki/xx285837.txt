[[Jawahir Ahmed]]

CATEGORIES: 1991 births, Living people, Ethnic Somali people, American female models, American people of Somali descent, People from Utah, Somalian female models

Jawahir Ahmed ([tpl]lang-so|Jawaahir Axmed[/tpl], [tpl]lang-ar|جواهر احمد[/tpl]) is a Somali-American model.

==Personal life==

Ahmed was born in 1991 in Logan, Utah to Somali parents. Her family moved to the United States earlier in the year, as the civil war in Somalia began.[tpl]cite web|title=Miss Somalia|url=http://gkfolks.com/public_html/competition-info/contestants/mau-2013-contestants/item/61-miss-somalia|publisher=GK Folks Productions|accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl]
For her post-secondary education, Ahmed is studying at the Utah State University on a scholarship. Majoring in Health Education with a specialization in Health Sciences, she hopes to eventually serve as a health adviser for Engineers Without Borders.[tpl]cite web|title=Jawahir Ahmed|url=http://www.thenationofpoets.com/2013/07/jawahir-ahmed.html|publisher=The Nation of Poets|accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl] Ahmed is also concurrently working for AmeriCorps and the CPASS college preparation program.

==Career==

Professionally, Ahmed is signed to the Stars Talent Group modeling agency.[tpl]cite web|title=Female models - Jawahir Ahmed|url=http://www.starstalentgroup.com/FEMALE-MODELS.html|publisher=Stars Talent Group|accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl]
In 2013, she represented Somalia in the Miss Africa Utah pageant, which Ahmed wound up winning. She also again served as Miss Somalia in the 2013 Miss United Nations USA pageant.[tpl]cite web|title=Miss United Nations USA|url=http://www.missunpageant.com/|publisher=Miss United Nations|accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl]
