[[Baron Horder]]

CATEGORIES: Baronies in the Peerage of the United Kingdom, Extinct baronies in the Peerage of the United Kingdom

Baron Horder, of Ashford in the County of Southampton[tpl]London Gazette |issue=33905 |date=24 January 1933 |startpage=521[/tpl] was a title in the Peerage of the United Kingdom. It was created on 23 January 1933 for the leading physician Sir Thomas Horder, 1st Baronet. He had already been created a baronet, of Shaston, in 1923.[tpl]London Gazette |issue=32849 |date=31 July 1923 |startpage=5238[/tpl] The titles became extinct on the death of his son, the second Baron, on 30 June 1997.
