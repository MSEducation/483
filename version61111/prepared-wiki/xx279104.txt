[[Bent, New Mexico]]

CATEGORIES: Census-designated places in New Mexico, Census-designated places in Otero County, New Mexico

Bent is a census-designated place in Otero County, New Mexico, United States. Its population was 119 as of the 2010 census. Bent has a post office with ZIP code 88314.[tpl]GR|7[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Postmaster Finder - Post Offices by ZIP Code|url=http://webpmt.usps.gov/pmt008.cfm|publisher=United States Postal Service|accessdate=August 8, 2013[/tpl] U.S. Route 70 passes through the community.
