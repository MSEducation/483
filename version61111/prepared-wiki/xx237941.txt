[[Sentinel Range (Canada)]]

CATEGORIES: Mountain ranges of British Columbia, Ranges of the Canadian Rockies, Liard Country

The Sentinel Range is one of the northernmost sub-ranges of the Canadian Rockies, lying between Muncho Lake (SW) and the Liard River (N).[tpl]BCGNIS|18860|Sentinel Range[/tpl]  The northernmost is the Terminal Range, so named for its position at the terminus of the Rockies, and lies to its west.[tpl]BCGNIS|15061|Terminal Range[/tpl]
