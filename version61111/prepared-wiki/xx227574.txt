[[Bherulal Balani]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, Pakistani politicians, Pakistani Hindus, People from Tharparkar District

Bherulal Balani is a politician from Tharparkar, Sindh, Pakistan.
Bherulal Balani was elected as a MPA in Provincial Assembly of Sindh in the 1997 elections, and was appointed Parliamentary Secretary for Power and Irrigation Department.[tpl]Citation needed|date=January 2011[/tpl] He served as Chairman of the Khidmat Committee of the District of Tharparkar, and played a leading role in Scheduled Caste Federation of Pakistan.Recently, he has joined PML N led by former Prime Minister Mohammad Nawaz Sharif.
