[[Huangcai, Ningxiang]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Ningxiang County

Huangcai Town ([tpl]zh|s=黄材镇|t=黃材鎮|p=Huángcaí Zhèn[/tpl]) is an urban town in Ningxiang County, Changsha City, Hunan Province, People's Republic of China. It's surrounded by Xiangzikou Town, Shatian Township and Weishan Township on the west, Songmutang Town on the north, Hengshi Town on the east, and Laoliangcang Town and Liushahe Town on the south. As of the 2000 census it had a population of 55,412 and an area of 220 square kilometers. It's famous for the bronze culture of the Shang culture as Laoliangcang Town.

==Cityscape==

The Town is divided into twenty four villages and one community, the following areas: Xinjie Community (新街社区), Huangcai Village (黄材村), Duanxi Village (段溪村), Yueshan Village (月山村), Longquan Village (龙泉村), Longxi Village (龙溪村), Shaping Village (沙坪村), Hetang Village (鹤塘村), Haoxi Village (蒿溪村), Ningfeng Village (宁峰村), Jingchong Village (井冲村), Lishan Village (栗山村), Shishi Village (石狮村), Songhua Village (松华村), Juanshui Village (涓水村), Xinqiao Village (新桥村), Shishan Village (石山村), Qingyang Village (清洋村), Shengxi Village (胜溪村), Shilongdong Village (石龙洞村), Yanxi Village (烟溪村), Cuiping Village (崔坪村), Tongxi Village (通溪村) and Xiatang Village (峡塘村).

==Geography==

Huangcai Reservoir is the largest reservoir in Ningxiang County, it's located in the town.
Wei River is known as "Mother River", a tributary of the Xiang River, it flows through the town.[tpl]cite book |author=黄海潮，姜宏照 |title=《宁乡史地》|year=2002-9 |publisher=南方出版社 |location=Hainan |pages= |isbn=7-80660-538-X |language=zh-cn[/tpl]

==Economy==

The region abounds with coal, manganese and uranium.
Capsicum annuum is important to the economy.

==Education==

There are one senior high school located with the town limits:Ningxiang County Third Senior High School (宁乡三中).

==Culture==

Huaguxi is the most influence local theater.
