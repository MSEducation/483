[[Stay (Elisa song)]]

CATEGORIES: 2007 singles, Elisa (Italian singer) songs, Songs written by Elisa (Italian singer), English-language Italian songs

"Stay""Stay" Video on YouTube  is the first track from Elisa's album Soundtrack '96-'06, it was the third single from the album and the first single from the international album Caterpillar, released in Italy, Switzerland and the Netherlands. Stay was released only download in Italy.

==Track listing==

==Release history==

==Music video==

The music video was filmed by Marco Salom and Elisa on March 2007 in California exclusively for the Italian market. On June 2007 it was re-shot by Joe Tunmer in London for the international version of the video.
