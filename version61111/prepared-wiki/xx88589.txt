[[List of hospitals in Switzerland]]

CATEGORIES: Lists of hospitals by country, Hospitals in Switzerland, Health in Switzerland, Lists of buildings and structures in Switzerland

This is a list of notable hospitals in Switzerland.
As of 2009, Switzerland had 313 hospitals – 129 general hospitals, 59 psychiatric hospitals, 53 rehabilitation clinics and 72 other specialty clinics. These hospitals had a total capacity of 39,539 patients and generated operating costs of CHF 20,5 billion per year.[tpl]cite web|last=Federal Statistical Office|title=Krankenhäuser (je-d-14.03.02.01)|url=http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/themen/14/03/01/key/01.html|accessdate=11 December 2010[/tpl]
