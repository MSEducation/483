[[Phera]]

CATEGORIES: 1988 films, Indian films, Bengali-language films, 1980s drama films, Films based on short fiction

 Phera (English language:The Return) is a 1988 Bengali drama film directed and written by Buddhadeb Dasgupta. It was entered into the 38th Berlin International Film Festival, competition section.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.berlinale.de/en/archiv/jahresarchive/1988/02_programm_1988/02_Programm_1988.html |title=Berlinale: 1988 Programme |accessdate=2011-03-06 |work=berlinale.de[/tpl]
