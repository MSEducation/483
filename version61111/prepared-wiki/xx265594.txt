[[Kim Bong-hwan]]

CATEGORIES: 1939 births, North Korean footballers, North Korea international footballers, Association football forwards, 1966 FIFA World Cup players, Living people

Kim Bong-Hwan (born 4 July 1939) is a North Korean football forward who played for North Korea in the 1966 FIFA World Cup.Copa do Mundo da FIFA Inglaterra 1966 He also played for Kikwancha Pyongyang.
