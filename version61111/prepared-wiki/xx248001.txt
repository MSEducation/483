[[Vámosgyörk]]

Vámosgyörk is a village in Heves County, Hungary. The population in 2009 was 2019.[ref]

==Location==

Vámosgyörk lies within Heves County, just north of neighboring Jász-Nagykun-Szolnok County. The village is on the Great Hungarian Plain about [tpl]convert|12|km|mi[/tpl] south of Gyöngyös, between the Rédei-Nagy Brook and Gyöngyös Brook ([tpl]lang-hu|Rédei-Nagy-patak, Gyöngyös-patak[/tpl]). Vámosgyörk is about [tpl]convert|90|km|mi[/tpl] from Budapest; other neighboring villages include Jászárokszállás (about [tpl]convert|7|km|mi[/tpl]), Adács (about [tpl]convert|5|km|mi[/tpl]), and Atkár (about [tpl]convert|5|km|mi[/tpl])
