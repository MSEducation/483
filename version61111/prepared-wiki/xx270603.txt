[[Hylarana elberti]]

CATEGORIES: Hylarana, Fauna of East Timor, Amphibians of Indonesia, Animals described in 1911

Hylarana elberti is a species of true frog in the genus Hylarana. It is native to Indonesia and Timor-Leste.Djoko Iskandar, Mumpuni 2004. Hylarana elberti. In: IUCN 2011. IUCN Red List of Threatened Species. Version 2011.1.
