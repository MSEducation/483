[[1 Girl Nation]]

CATEGORIES: Musical groups established in 2012, Musical groups from Nashville, Tennessee, Reunion Records artists, American girl groups

1 Girl Nation also known as 1GN is a Christian Contemporary-Power pop-Pop rock band from Nashville, Tennessee, United States. They are on the Reunion Records label, and released their first album entitled 1 Girl Nation on August 20, 2013. The album has had chart successes.

==Background==

In 2012, 1 Girl Nation was formed in Nashville, Tennessee, and the band members are Lauryn Taylor Bach from Birmingham, Alabama, Lindsey Ciresi from Jacksonville, Florida, Carmen Justice from Nashville, Tennessee, Kayli Robinson from Orlando, Florida, and Kelsey Sowards from Chicago, Illinois.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.jesusfreakhideout.com/news/2013/06/14.1%20GIRL%20NATION%20(1GN)%20JOINS%20REUNION%20RECORDS%20AND%20ANNOUNCES%20DEBUT,%20SELF-TITLED%20RELEASE%20DUE%20AUGUST%2020.asp|title=1 Girl Nation (1GN) Joins Reunion Records and Announces Debut, Self-Titled Release Due August 20|author=Provident Label Group|date=June 14, 2013|publisher=Jesus Freak Hideout|accessdate=August 30, 2013[/tpl] On May 1, 2014 The band announced that Kelsey was leaving the band.[tpl]Citation needed|date=May 2014[/tpl]

==Music==

In June 2013, the band was signed to Reunion Records, a major Christian music label in the United States.

===1 Girl Nation===

On August 20, 2013, 1 Girl Nation released their self-titled debut album 1 Girl Nation. 1 Girl Nation charted at No. 11 and No. 9 on the Top Christian Albums and the Top Heatseekers Albums charts respectively, for the Billboard charting week of September 7, 2013.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/artist/5694400/1-girl-nation/chart?f=310|title=1 Girl Nation - Chart history|author=Billboard|publisher=Prometheus Global Media|accessdate=October 24, 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/artist/5694400/1-girl-nation/chart?f=324|title=1 Girl Nation - Chart history|author=Billboard|publisher=Prometheus Global Media|accessdate=October 24, 2013[/tpl] On December 14, 2013, Billboard charts the album was the No. 2 Heatseekers Albums.
