[[Jorge Mautner]]

CATEGORIES: 1941 births, Living people, Brazilian composers, Brazilian Jews, Brazilian poets, Brazilian male singers, Latin Grammy Award winners, People from Rio de Janeiro (city)

Jorge Mautner (born January 17, 1941 in Rio de Janeiro) is a Brazilian writer and singer. He is the creator of the Moviment of Kaos.
