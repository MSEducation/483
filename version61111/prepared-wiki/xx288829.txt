[[Carol Meyers]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Old Testament scholars, Translators of the Bible into English, Feminist studies scholars, Wellesley College alumni, Brandeis University alumni, Duke University alumni, Bible commentators, American biblical scholars, American archaeologists

Carol L. Meyers is a feminist biblical scholar. She is the Mary Grace Wilson Professor of Religion at Duke University.[tpl]cite web|title=Carol L Meyers|url=http://religiondepartment.duke.edu/people?Gurl=&Uil=1699&subpage=profile|publisher=Duke University|accessdate=13 August 2013[/tpl] 
Meyers studied at Wellesley College and Brandeis University, and has taught at Duke since 1977. She writes and teaches in the areas of biblical studies, archaeology, and the study of women in the biblical world. She has been described as "one of today's leading historians and field archeologists".[tpl]cite book|last=Perdue|first=Leo G.|author-link=Leo G. Perdue|title=Reconstructing Old Testament Theology: After the Collapse of History|year=2005|publisher=Fortress Press|page=136|url=http://books.google.com.au/books?id=5o5nwRONDpIC&pg=PA136[/tpl] Her 1988 book, Discovering Eve: Ancient Israelite Women in Context, was the "first comprehensive effort to present a female-centred view of the Bible using historical rather than literary criticism".[tpl]cite book|last=Fuchs|first=Esther|title=The Hebrew Bible: New Insights and Scholarship|chapter=Feminist Approaches to the Hebrew Bible|year=2008|publisher=New York University Press|page=85|url=http://books.google.com.au/books?id=inRKaf_To5sC&pg=PT145[/tpl] Meyers has also written commentaries on Exodus, Haggai, and Zechariah.
Meyers is president-elect of the Society of Biblical Literature. She also served as part of the revision team for the 2010 New American Bible.Collaborators on the Old Testament of the New American Bible 2010
She is married to fellow biblical scholar and Duke professor Eric M. Meyers.[tpl]cite web|title=Biographies|url=http://www.pbs.org/wgbh/pages/frontline/shows/religion/etc/bios.html|publisher=PBS|accessdate=14 August 2013[/tpl]
