[[Hugo Kloppers]]

CATEGORIES: South African rugby union players, Living people, 1988 births, People from Worcester, Western Cape, Golden Lions players, Western Province (rugby team) players, Rugby union locks

Hugo Kloppers (born 14 October 1988) is a South African rugby union player, currently playing with the [tpl]Rut Golden Lions[/tpl].[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/playerprofile.aspx?id=35148 | title=SARU Player Profile Hugo Kloppers | work=South African Rugby Union | accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl] His regular position is lock.

==Career==

===Youth===

He represented [tpl]Rut Boland Cavaliers[/tpl] at the Under-16 Grant Khomo Week in 2004 and at the Under-18 Craven Week in 2006.
He then joined [tpl]Rut Western Province[/tpl] and played for their Under-19 team in 2007 and their Under-21 team in 2008 and 2009.

===Western Province===

He was included in the [tpl]Rut Western Province[/tpl] squad for the 2010 Vodacom Cup competition and made his first class debut in their 28–23 victory over [tpl]Rut Eastern Province[/tpl].[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/matchbreakdown.aspx?id=18851&category=&leagueid=765&homeid=7982&awayid=7979&fullview=true | title=Match breakdown: Eastern Province vs Western Province | work=South African Rugby Union | date=13 March 2010 | accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl] His first start came a month later when he was selected as the starting lock in their match against [tpl]Rut SWD Eagles[/tpl].[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/matchbreakdown.aspx?id=18646&category=&leagueid=765&homeid=7979&awayid=7976&fullview=true | title=Match breakdown: Western Province vs SWD | work=South African Rugby Union | date=10 April 2010 | accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl]

===Lions===

He joined the [tpl]Rut Golden Lions[/tpl] side for 2013.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.rugby15.co.za/2013/01/2013-lions-squad-confirmed/ | title=2013 Lions squad confirmed | publisher=Rugby15 | date=15 January 2013 | accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl] He made four appearances for them in the 2013 Vodacom Cup competition, starting in the final against the [tpl]Rut Pumas[/tpl], which the Lions won 42–28.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/matchbreakdown.aspx?id=107764&category=&leagueid=1164&homeid=11644&awayid=11684&fullview=true | title=Match breakdown: Steval Pumas vs MTN Golden Lions | work=South African Rugby Union | date=17 May 2013 | accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl] He was included in their squad for the 2013 Currie Cup Premier Division[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/teams.aspx?id=12634&category=sarugby/curriecup&leagueid=1294 | title=2013 Golden Lions Currie Cup season | work=South African Rugby Union | accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl] and made his Currie Cup debut in their first match of the season, a narrow 29–30 loss to the [tpl]Rut Free State Cheetahs[/tpl].[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/matchbreakdown.aspx?id=106944&category=sarugby/curriecup&leagueid=1294&homeid=12634&awayid=12654&fullview=true | title=Match breakdown: MTN Golden Lions vs Toyota Free State Cheetahs | work=South African Rugby Union | date=10 August 2013 | accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl]

===Varsity Cup===

He was a regular for the [tpl]Rut Maties[/tpl] Varsity Cup team, playing for them in their title-winning seasons in 2009 and 2010, as well as in 2011 and 2012.
