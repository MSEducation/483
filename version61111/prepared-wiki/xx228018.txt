[[Rosalie Filleul]]

CATEGORIES: 1753 births, 1794 deaths, 18th-century French painters, People from Île-de-France executed by guillotine during the French Revolution, Artists from Paris

Rosalie Filleul (1753 – June 24, 1794), was a French pastellist and painter.  She was born in Paris, and was concierge of the château de la Muette.  She was guillotined in 1794.
