[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Bom Jesus da Lapa]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1962, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Vitória da Conquista, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century, 1962 establishments in Brazil

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Bom Jesus da Lapa ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Spelaeopolitanus a Bono Iesu[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Bom Jesus da Lapa in the Ecclesiastical province of Vitória da Conquista in Brazil.
