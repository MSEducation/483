[[Hadi Kazemi]]

CATEGORIES: Iranian television actors, Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, People from Meyaneh

Hadi Kazemi is an Iranian actor mostly recognised for his role as Nezam Du Barareh in Mehran Modiri's comedy series Shabhaye Barareh, an Iranian satire television series broadcast by the IRIB network.
