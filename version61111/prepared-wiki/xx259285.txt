[[Bonifaci Ferrer]]

CATEGORIES: 1417 deaths, Carthusians, Latin–Catalan translators, Translators of the Bible into Catalan, Medieval Catalan-language writers, 1350 births

Boniface FerrerThere are variations to Ferrer's first name – in Valencian it is "Bonifaci", while in Spanish it is "Bonifacio") (1350–1417) was a Carthusian monk, brother of Saint Vincent Ferrer, and translator of the first Bible into Valencian.
His translation was the first translation into a Romance language since Peter Waldo's translation into Provençal.Rebeca Sanmartín Bastida, Rosa Vidal Doval Las Metamorfosis de la alegoría - 2005 p363
