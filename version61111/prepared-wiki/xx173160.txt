[[Lahav 433]]

CATEGORIES: Israel Police

Lahav 433 is a new Israeli crime-fighting umbrella organization within the Israel Police. Known as the "Israeli FBI", the unit is the merger of five law enforcement offices into one. It is tasked with investigating national crimes and corruption.'Israeli FBI' inaugurated in opening ceremony - Israel News, Ynetnews
The number four is for the four region departments and number 33 is for The Gideonites, a Mista'arvim unit.
