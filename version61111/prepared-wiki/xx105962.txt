[[Nzérékoré Region]]

CATEGORIES: Regions of Guinea, Nzérékoré Region

The Nzérékoré Region is a region in the southern part of Guinea. Its capital and largest city is Nzérékoré.   It is one of the eight regions of Guinea. It is bordered by the countries of Sierra Leone, Liberia, and Côte d'Ivoire and the Guinean regions of Kankan and Faranah.
