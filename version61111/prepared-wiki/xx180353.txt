[[Shallop Cove]]

CATEGORIES: Coves of South Georgia

Shallop Cove ([tpl]Coord|54|14|S|37|20|W||display=inline,title[/tpl]) is a cove forming the head of Queen Maud Bay on the south side of South Georgia. Surveyed by the SGS in the period 1951-57, and so named because the remains of a shallop were found here by the SGS in 1956.
