[[Jogger's Park]]

CATEGORIES: Parks in Mumbai, Bandra

Joggers' Park is a seaside jogging track where joggers of Bandra, Mumbai congregate. The little park is right next to the Otters Club, another recreation place for Bandra denizens, was where Mumbai's first laughing club was launched. Joggers Park project & initiative, was a gift of love from the late Sir. Oliver Andrade.
Joggers Park is nice place for jogging and exercising. It has a jogging track of length 400m. Its one of the best places to jog in Mumbai. Its located next to Carter Road and Otters Club. One can also reach there by taking the Bus 220 from Bandra West Station.
