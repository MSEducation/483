[[Azadkənd, Nakhchivan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ordubad District

Azadkənd (until 2003,Azerbaijan Parliament - renaming law Aşağı Aza and Ashagy Aza "Lower Aza") is a village and municipality in the Ordubad Rayon of Nakhchivan, Azerbaijan.  It has a population of 1,096.
