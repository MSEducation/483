[[Tamworth Public School]]

CATEGORIES: Buildings and structures in Tamworth, New South Wales, Primary schools in New South Wales, Public schools in New South Wales, Educational institutions established in 1855

Tamworth Public School is situated in East Tamworth, Tamworth, New South Wales, Australia. Over 600 students attend the primary school and it is the oldest school in Tamworth, being established in 1855. The school's mottos are Aim High and Care, Courtesy and Consideration.
