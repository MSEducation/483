[[Lahargin]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Lahargin ([tpl]lang-fa|لهرگين[/tpl], also Romanized as Lahargīn and Lahrgīn; also known as Laharjīn and Lakhargin)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3072741|Lahargin[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Pain Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 62, in 13 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
