[[Data Dynamics]]

CATEGORIES: Software companies based in Ohio, Companies based in Ohio

Data Dynamics, Ltd. a software development company that creates software components for application developers using Microsoft Visual Studio and the .NET platform. The company's primary product focus is on data analysis and information reporting. The company was founded in 1996 in Columbus Ohio [tpl]cite web|url=http://web.archive.org/web/20051215100758/http://www.datadynamics.com/AboutUs.aspx |title=Data Dynamics |publisher=Web.archive.org |date=2005-12-15 |accessdate=2013-06-14[/tpl] by American immigrants.[tpl]dead link|date=June 2013[/tpl]

==Products==

Released in 1996, DynamiCube was the first ActiveX control that allowed OLAP multidimensional analysis capabilities for client/server and browser-based Internet applications.[tpl]cite web|url=http://web.archive.org/web/19970629123716/www.datadynamics.com/press/press1.htm |title=Data Dynamics, Ltd. - For The Press |publisher=Web.archive.org |date= |accessdate=2013-06-14[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://web.archive.org/web/19970629123705/www.datadynamics.com/press/mspress.htm |title=Data Dynamics Press Release |publisher=Web.archive.org |date= |accessdate=2013-06-14[/tpl]
ActiveBar was the first ActiveX control providing developers with a tool to create detachable menus and toolbars similar to those in the Microsoft Office applications.[tpl]cite web|url=http://web.archive.org/web/19970629123502/www.datadynamics.com/press/abmarch97.htm |title=New Product Announcement |publisher=Web.archive.org |date= |accessdate=2013-06-14[/tpl] ActiveReports was the first ActiveX Designer report writer offering developers full report writing capabilities as well as extensive export formats. ActiveReports was also the first ActiveX Designer released outside of Microsoft.ActiveReports for Visual Basic ActiveReports for .NET was the first third party reporting tool for Visual Studio .NET that was entirely written in managed code.

===Acquisition===

ActiveReports was localized and sold in Japan by a strategic partner GrapeCity which localized ActiveReports for Japanese programmers. In addition to localizing the user interface and documentation, GrapeCity performed quality testing for Japanese usage of the product and requested new features. After a long association with the company, in October, 2008, GrapeCity announced their acquisition of Data Dynamics, in Columbus, Ohio.GrapeCity Acquires Data Dynamics Shortly after the acquisition Tim Moffatt, a founder and President of the company, died.Thanks, Tim In June, 2012, GrapeCity announced  their acquisition of ComponentOne. As a result, GrapeCity plans to sell ActiveReports under the brand of ComponentOne outside of Japan.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.sdtimes.com/link/36693 |title=GrapeCity acquires ComponentOne - SD Times: Software Development News |publisher=SD Times |date= |accessdate=2013-06-14[/tpl] To the future of ComponentOne Reports, another reporting tool sold by ComponentOne, no statement was given.
