[[15804 Yenisei]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Astronomical objects discovered in 1994

15804 Yenisei is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1654.8677660 days (4.53 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=15804 | title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-05-23 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on March 9, 1994.
