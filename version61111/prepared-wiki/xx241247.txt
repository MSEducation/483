[[100th Indian Infantry Brigade]]

CATEGORIES: British Indian Army brigades, Military units and formations established in 1941, Military units and formations disestablished in 1945

The 100th Indian Infantry Brigade was an Infantry formation of the Indian Army during World War II. It was formed in April 1941, at Jhansi. The brigade was assigned to the 34th Indian Infantry Division until June 1943, when it was transferred to the 20th Indian Infantry Division until the end of the war.[tpl]cite web|accessdate=2009-10-23|publisher=Order of Battle|title=100 Indian Brigade|url=http://www.ordersofbattle.com/UnitData.aspx?UniX=6174&Tab=Sup[/tpl]
