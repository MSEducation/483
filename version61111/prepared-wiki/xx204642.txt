[[Tutta la vita davanti]]

CATEGORIES: Films directed by Paolo Virzì, Italian films, Italian-language films, 2008 films

Tutta la vita davanti (English title: Your Whole Life Ahead of You) is an Italian movie directed by Paolo Virzì and written by Virzì and Francesco Bruni.

==Plot==

The events of a freshly graduated young woman in the universe of precarious work.
