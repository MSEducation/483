[[1963 Inter-Cities Fairs Cup Final]]

CATEGORIES: 1962–63 in European football, International club association football competitions hosted by Yugoslavia, GNK Dinamo Zagreb matches, Valencia CF matches, Inter-Cities Fairs Cup Finals, International club association football competitions hosted by Spain, 1962–63 in Spanish football

The 1963 Inter-Cities Fairs Cup Final was the final of the fifth Inter-Cities Fairs Cup. It was played on 12 June and 26 June 1963 between NK Dinamo Zagreb of SFR Yugoslavia and Valencia CF of Spain. Valencia won the tie 4–1 on aggregate.
