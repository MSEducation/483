[[Gilbertese dancing]]

CATEGORIES: Honiara, Oceanian culture, Polynesian culture

The Gilbertese dancing, which is accompanied by singing, is a characteristic dance style of the Melanesians and the Polynesians. It is a fusion of folk and ballad dance forms.
