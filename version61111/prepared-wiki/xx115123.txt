[[Nannenus]]

CATEGORIES: Salticidae

Nannenus is a spider genus of the Salticidae family (jumping spiders). Its two described species are endemic to Singapore.
One or two more species have been collected but not described.Murphy & Murphy 2000: 286

==Description==

Both sexes are about four mm long. The cephalus is high, the eye region blackish with the area between the rear eyes lighter. The rest of the thorax is brown with a whitish lateral band on each side. The small abdomen is light yellowish with a vague white pattern.
