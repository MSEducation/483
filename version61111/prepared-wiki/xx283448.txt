Semyon Ivanovich Perelygin ([tpl]lang-ru|Семён Иванович Перелыгин[/tpl]; born June 30, 1994) is a Russian football midfielder, who currently playing for  FC Chernomorets Novorossiysk.
He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Chernomorets Novorossiysk on July 12, 2013 in a game against FC Krasnodar-2.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Russian Football Union|url=http://rfspro.ru/cgi-bin/player/script2.cgi?par1=119569|title=Career Summary|date=11 August 2013[/tpl]
