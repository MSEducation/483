[[Agdistis]]

CATEGORIES: Greek mythology, Roman mythology, Phrygian goddesses, Deities of the Greco-Roman world, Cybele, LGBT history prior to the 19th century, Transgender topics and religion, Intersex in religion and mythology

Agdistis ([tpl]lang-grc|Ἄγδιστις[/tpl]) was a deity of Greek, Roman and Anatolian mythology, possessing both male and female sexual organs, connected with the Phrygian worship of Attis and Cybele.[ref]
