[[Bellida]]

CATEGORIES: Asteraceae genera, Flora of Western Australia, Monotypic plant genera

Bellida is a monotypic genus of flowering plants in the aster family, Asteraceae, containing the single species Bellida graminea.Bellida. The Plant List. It is native to Western Australia. Its common name is rosy bellida.Bellida graminea. FloraBase. Western Australian Herbarium.
