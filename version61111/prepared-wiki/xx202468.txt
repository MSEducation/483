[[Tikaré, Burkina Faso]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Centre-Nord Region

Tikaré  (also, Tinkari and Tinari) is a town in Bam Province, Burkina Faso.  It is the capital of the Tikare Department and had a population of 5,344.Burkinabé government inforoute communale
