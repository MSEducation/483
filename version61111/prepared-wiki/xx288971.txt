[[Shivanat Rural District]]

CATEGORIES: Rural Districts of Zanjan Province, Khodabandeh County

Shivanat Rural District ([tpl]lang-fa|دهستان شيوانات[/tpl]) is a rural district (dehestan) in Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 8,963, in 1,919 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl] The rural district has 29 villages.
