[[Greater Reading Expo Center]]

CATEGORIES: Buildings and structures in Berks County, Pennsylvania, Convention centers in Pennsylvania

The Greater Reading Expo Center  is an exhibition center located just north  of Reading, Pennsylvania and access from the Warren Street Bypass (Route 12.) It has 30 meeting rooms, two food courts, and a total area of over 270,000 square feet on one floor.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.greaterreadingexpocenter.com/the-facility/|title=The Facility Greater Reading Expo Center|accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl]
