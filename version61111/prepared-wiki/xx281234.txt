[[Orange Walk Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in Belize, Orange Walk District

Orange Walk Airport [tpl]airport codes|ORZ[/tpl] is a public use airport located [tpl]convert|1|NM[/tpl] east of Orange Walk Town, Orange Walk District, Belize. The airport is indeed the Orange Walk Bypass highway, on which the road traffic is interrupted while aircraft operate on it.
