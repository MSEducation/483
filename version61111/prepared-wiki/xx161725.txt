[[Eighth Army (France)]]

CATEGORIES: Field armies of France, Field armies of France in World War I, Military units and formations of France in World War II

The Eighth Army ([tpl]lang-fr|VIIIe Arm√©e[/tpl]) was a Field army of the French Army during World War I and World War II. 
After the armistice at the end of World War I, it was part of the occupation of the Rhineland. On 21 October 1919 it was combined with the Tenth Army to form the French Army of the Rhine.
It was reformed in the Second World War.
