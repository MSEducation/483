[[Abdelrahim Mohamed Abdelrahim]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1994 births, Sudanese swimmers

Abdelrahim Mohamed Abdelrahim, or Abdel Mohamed Abdelrahim' (born 1994) is a Sudanese swimmer.http://www.bcn2013.com/en/swimming/detail/member/abdelrahim-mohamed-abdelrahim He competed in the 100 m freestyle, 50 m backstroke and 50 m butterfly events at the 2012 FINA World Swimming Championships (25 m) and in the 50 m freestyle event at the 2013 Asian Indoor and Martial Arts Games. Abdelrahim also took part in the 50 m freestyle and 50 m butterfly events at the 2013 World Aquatics Championships.
