[[Ahmadabad, Zanjanrud]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Ahmadabad ([tpl]lang-fa|احمداباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Aḩmadābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795307|Ahmadabad[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
