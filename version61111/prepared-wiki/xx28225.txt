[[Edward, Count of Savoy]]

CATEGORIES: 1284 births, 1329 deaths, People from Maine-et-Loire, Counts of Savoy

Edward (1284–1329), surnamed the Liberal, was the Count of Savoy from 1323 to 1329. He was the son of Amadeus V, Count of Savoy, and his first wife Sybille of Bâgé.
Edward was born at Baugé.  He was married to Blanche (Bianca) of Burgundy, daughter of Robert II, Duke of Burgundy and Agnes of France, Duchess of Burgundy. They had a daughter, Joan (Giovanna), who married to John III the Good, duke of Brittany, but no son.
