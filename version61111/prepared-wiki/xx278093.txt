Park Joon-Gang ([tpl]Ko-hhrm|박준강[/tpl]; born 6 June 1991) is a South Korean footballer who plays as defender  for Busan IPark in K League Classic.[tpl]Soccerway|jun-gang-park/286380[/tpl]

==Career==

Park was selected by Busan IPark in the K League draft.[tpl]cite news |url=http://news20.busan.com/controller/newsController.jsp?newsId=20121211000061 |title=부산 아이파크 '로컬 보이'들 2013년을 부탁해! |publisher=Busan Ilbo |date=2012-12-11[/tpl] [tpl]ko icon[/tpl] He scored his first goal for the club against Gyeongnam FC on 3 August. Park established himself as IPark's first choice right back in the 2013 season.
