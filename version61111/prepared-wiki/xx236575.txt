[[William Alexander (Glasgow MP)]]

CATEGORIES: 1874 births, 1954 deaths, Unionist Party (Scotland) MPs, UK MPs 1923–24, UK MPs 1924–29, UK MPs 1929–31, UK MPs 1931–35, UK MPs 1935–45

Sir William Alexander (1874–1954) was Unionist Party of Scotland Member of Parliament (MP) for Glasgow Central (UK Parliament constituency) between the general elections of 1923 to 1945, when he stood down.
He was also a Brigadier-General, winning the DSO and a knighthood.
