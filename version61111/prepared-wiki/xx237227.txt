[[Kathy Pippy]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Pennsylvania political consultants, University of Pittsburgh alumni, United States Military Academy alumni, Pennsylvania Republicans

Kathy Pippy is a prominent political consultant in Pennsylvania. She is known for her political work in Western Pennsylvania, where she has had a hand in "most successful campaigns."
She was named to the PoliticsPA list of "Pennsylvania's Smartest Staffers and Operatives," where she was described as "one of the smartest operatives in Western Pennsylvania."[tpl]cite web| title =Pennsylvania's Smartest Staffers and Operatives|work = PoliticsPA| publisher =The Publius Group| date = | url = http://www./politicspa.com/FEATURES/smarteststaff.htm|archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20040405121357/politicspa.com/FEATURES/smarteststaff.htm|archivedate=2004-04-05[/tpl]
In 2003, she was named PoliticsPA's "2003 Operative of the Year" for her efforts work in electing her husband John Pippy to the Pennsylvania Senate, while he was deployed to Iraq.[tpl]cite web| title =2003 Operative of the Year| work = PoliticsPA| publisher =The Publius Group| year = 2001| url = http://www.politicspa.com/FEATURES/winner_of_the_year03operative.htm|archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20040405123307/politicspa.com/FEATURES/winner_of_the_year03operative.htm|archivedate=2004-04-05[/tpl]  During that election, she stood in for him at every event. She also managed his district office after his election and prior to his return.
