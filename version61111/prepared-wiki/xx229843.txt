[[1993 Australian Sports Sedan Championship]]

CATEGORIES: 1993 in Australian motorsport, Australian Sports Sedan Championship

[tpl]Cleanup|date=October 2008[/tpl]The 1993 Australian Sports Sedan Championship was a CAMS sanctioned national motor racing title Australian Titles Retrieved from CAMS Online Manual of Motor Sport on 7/10/2008 for drivers of Group 2D Sports Sedans. 
The championship was contested over an eight round series:Australian Motor Racing Year, 1993/94, pages 218-224 & 303
Championship points were awarded on a 20-15-12-10-8-6-4-3-2-1 basis to the first ten finishers in each round.
