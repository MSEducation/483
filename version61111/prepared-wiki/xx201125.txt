[[Roman Catholic Diocese of São José dos Pinhais]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Curitiba, Religious organizations established in 2006, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 21st century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of São José dos Pinhais ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Sancti Iosephi Pinealensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of São José dos Pinhais in the Ecclesiastical province of Curitiba in Brazil.
