[[Vrgorac]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Croatia, Populated places in Split-Dalmatia County

Vrgorac ([tpl]IPA-hr|ʋř̩ɡorats|pron[/tpl], [tpl]lang-it|Vergoraz[/tpl]) is a town in Croatia in the Split-Dalmatia County.
The total population of Vrgorac is 6,572 (census 2011), in the following settlements:[tpl]Croatian Census 2011|S|17|5118[/tpl]
In the 2011 census, 99% of the population were Croats.[tpl]Croatian Census 2011|E|17[/tpl]
