[[Against All Authority]]

CATEGORIES: American punk rock groups, Third-wave ska groups, Musical groups from Miami, Florida, Anarcho-punk groups, Musical groups established in 1992, Musical quartets, American ska punk musical groups

Against All Authority (often abbreviated -AAA-) WAS an American punk band, formed in Florida in 1992. Playing in the ska punk style,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.starpulse.com/Music/Against_All_Authority/Discography/album/P279226/R832130/ |title=Restoration of Chaos & Order Album Review, Songs, Ratings |work=Starpulse.com |date= |accessdate=2012-11-11[/tpl] their business practice follows a stringent DIY ethic.

==History==

Against All Authority is founded in Cutler Ridge, Florida in 1992,[tpl]cite web | title=Against All Authority|work=Allmusic| url=[tpl]Allmusic|class=artist|id=p279226|pure_url=yes[/tpl]| accessdate=June 11, 2007 [/tpl]  driven by willingness to promote their messageâ€”"questioning our economic differences and promoting our human similarities", as stated by their now defunct official website. A strong engagement in political and social issues is clearly evident in their lyrics. The band maintain a fierce DIY stance influenced by the approach of the Dead Kennedys and Subhumans.[tpl]cite web |title=A Punk Band That Most Certainly Does Not Suck |work=Nerve Magazine |archivedate=September 28, 2007 |archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20070928192630/http://www.thenervemagazine.com/2006/07/article_template.php?id=1 |url=http://www.thenervemagazine.com/2006/07/article_template.php?id=1 |accessdate=June 11, 2007[/tpl] Early in their career the band decided to book concerts, make recordings and even produce band t-shirts on their own. Their first release "Above The Law" was released on Far Out Records. Later they signed on independent label Hopeless Records, becoming one of their major bands. In addition to touring and recording, the band is frequently involved in demonstrations and social projects. They have also influenced a number of other bands. Guitar player Joe Koontz has formed a new band called Nobody's Hero, with members of The Vandals and Guajiro. Trombonist Fin Leavell  is now fronting the band Nightswim. Drummer Macbeth Proenza also formed a new band called Hit, Play!, with former members of Polly Esther.
