[[Overpelt]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Limburg (Belgium)

Overpelt is a municipality located in the Belgian province of Limburg. On January 1, 2006 Overpelt had a total population of 13,435. The total area is 40.85 km² which gives a population density of 329 inhabitants per km².
The current mayor is Jaak Fransen.
