Manuel de Blas Muñoz (April 12, 1941) is a Spanish film actor.

==Biography==

Manuel de Blas was born in Badajoz on 1941. He spent his childhood in Córdoba and subsequently moved to Madrid to study political science. While attending college he enrolled in an acting school, making his debut in films in 1961 with a minor role in the film Rosa de Lima.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0207458/bio?ref_=nm_ov_bio_sm |title= Manuel de Blas - Biography |publisher=IMDb |accessdate=11 February 2014 [/tpl]
In 1967 on the set of Cita en Navarra, he knows American film actress Patty Shepard, whom he married in the same year. They will continue to act in several films together and remain linked to the death of the actress in January, 2013.[tpl]cite news|first=Lee|last=Moran|title=Patty Shepard, American star of Spanish silver screen, dies at 68 |url=http://www.nydailynews.com/entertainment/tv-movies/patty-shepard-star-spanish-silver-screen-dies-68-article-1.1236346 |work=New York Daily News  |publisher= |date=2013-01-09 |accessdate=11 February 2014[/tpl]
During his career, he has starred in many films. In 2012 he obtained a role in the movie Paranormal Xperience 3D.
He is brother-in-law of  American soap opera actress Judith Chapman.
