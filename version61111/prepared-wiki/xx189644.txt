[[Eran Kolirin]]

CATEGORIES: European Film Awards winners (people), Israeli film directors, Ophir Award winners: Directors, Ophir Award winners: Screenwriters, Living people

Eran Kolirin (Hebrew: ערן קולירין) is an Israeli screenwriter and film director. 
His directorial debut, The Band's Visit (2007), was a critical success, winning eight Awards of the Israeli Film Academy and prizes at several international film festivals. Kolirin himself won the Israeli Film Academy's awards for best director and best screenplay.http://www.nypress.com/21/6/film/kohn1.cfm
