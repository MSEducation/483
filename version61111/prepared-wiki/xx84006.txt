[[Live & Rare (Rage Against the Machine album)]]

CATEGORIES: Rage Against the Machine albums, 1998 live albums, Epic Records live albums

Live & Rare is a compilation of live and rare material by Rage Against the Machine, released only in Japan in 1998 and only available overseas as an import. It comprises "official bootlegs" previously available on other singles as well as a pair of tracks from the band's 1991 demo.
