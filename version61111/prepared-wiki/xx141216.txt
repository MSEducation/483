[[Tanno Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Hokkaido Prefecture

[tpl]Nihongo|Tanno Station|端野駅|Tanno-eki[/tpl] is a railway station located in the town of Tanno in Kitami city in Hokkaidō, Japan, and services the Sekihoku Main Line operated by JR Hokkaido.

==Station structure==

The station is above ground and has two side platforms alongside two railway tracks. It is not permanently staffed.
