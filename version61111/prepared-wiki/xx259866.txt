[[Aurelio Caicedo Ayerbe]]

CATEGORIES: 1921 births, 1998 deaths, People from Cauca Department, University of Cauca alumni, Colombian lawyers, Colombian Ministers of Labour, Colombian Ministers of National Education, Members of the Chamber of Representatives of Colombia, Members of the Senate of Colombia, Permanent Representatives of Colombia to the United Nations, Ambassadors of Colombia to the Holy See

Aurelio Caicedo Ayerbe (4 May 1921 — August 1998) was a Colombian lawyer and diplomat. He served as the 14th Permanent Representative of Colombia to the United Nations and as Ambassador of Colombia to the Holy See.[tpl]cite web |url=http://unesdoc.unesco.org/images/0015/001531/153145eo.pdf |title=Election of members of the Executive Board: Curriculum Vitae, Mr Aurelio Caicedo Ayerbe (Colombia) |year=1985 |location=Sofia |format=Curriculum vitae |publisher=UNESCO [/tpl]

==Personal life==

In 1955, while serving as Minister of National Education, Caicedo married the former Miss Colombia 1953, Luz Marina Cruz Lozada, in the city of Cali. Together they had three children, Pablo, Andrés, and Santiago.[tpl]cite journal |journal=El Tiempo |accessdate=2011-01-09 |language=Spanish |title=Doble Luto en Popayán |trans_title=Double Mourning in Popayán |date=1998-08-26 |title=http://www.eltiempo.com/archivo/documento/MAM-774115 [/tpl]
