[[Nishiusuki District, Miyazaki]]

CATEGORIES: Districts in Miyazaki Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Nishiusuki|西臼杵郡|Nishiusuki-gun[/tpl] is a district located in Miyazaki Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the district has an estimated population of 25,479 and the density of 37.10 persons per km². The total area is 686.77 km².
