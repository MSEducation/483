[[Horqin Right Front Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Horqin Right Front Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠷᠴᠢᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠪᠠᠷᠠᠭᠤᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠭᠠᠷᠤᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠡᠮᠦᠨᠡᠳᠦ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Qorčin Baraɣun Ɣarun Emünedü qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=科尔沁右翼前旗|t=科爾沁右翼前旗|p=Kē'ěrqìn Yòuyì Qián Qi[/tpl]) is a county of Inner Mongolia, China. It is under the administration of Hinggan League. The local Mongolian dialect is Khorchin Mongolian.
