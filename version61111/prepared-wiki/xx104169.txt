[[Gjern]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Central Denmark Region, Ski areas and resorts in Denmark

Gjern is a town in central Denmark with a population of 1,449 (1 January 2013),"BEF44: Population 1st January, by urban areas" database from Statistics Denmark located in Silkeborg municipality in Region Midtjylland in Jutland in Denmark.
The town is the site of Jysk Automobilmuseum a large museum of automobiles.
