[[Diego Stocco]]

CATEGORIES: 1976 births, Inventors of musical instruments, Italian composers, Living people, Sound designers

Diego Stocco (born 1976) is an Italian sound designer and composer for movies, television and video games. He also constructs musical instruments and has used both a tree[tpl]cite web|last=Goodman|first=William|title=WHOAH: Sound designer/composer Diego Stocco makes music from a tree|url=http://www.cbsnews.com/8301-504784_162-20043948-10391705.html|work=The Feed Blog|publisher=CBS News|accessdate=15 December 2011|date=16 March 2011[/tpl] and a burning piano in his pieces.[tpl]cite web|last=Rosenthal|first=Emerson|title=Diego Stocco Can Make Music Out of Anything, Including A Burning Piano|url=http://thecreatorsproject.com/blog/diego-stocco-can-make-music-out-of-anything-including-a-burning-piano|publisher=The Creators Project|accessdate=15 December 2011|date=3 October 2011[/tpl]
Stocco was born in Rovigo, Italy.[tpl]cite web|title=Bio|url=http://diegostocco.com/|publisher=diegostocco.com|accessdate=15 December 2011|format=PDF[/tpl]

==Movies==

His most notable works in cinema are Takers, Sherlock Holmes, Crank and Into The Blue.

==TV==

Diego served as music sound designer for TV shows The Tudors, Moonlight and Sleeper Cell.

==Gaming==

He has done sound design and performed for video games such as Assassin's Creed, Far Cry Instincts and The Conduit. 

==Virtual instruments==

He also designed and programmed sound for Korg Z1, Spectrasonics Atmosphere, Omnisphere and Trillian, Bob Moog Tribute Library. He has released some of his albums on Bandcamp.[tpl]cite web|last=Resneck|first=Jacob|title=Interview with Sound Artist Diego Stocco|url=http://www.coolhunting.com/culture/diego-stocco-1.php|publisher=Cool Hunting|accessdate=15 December 2011|date=21 May 2009[/tpl]
