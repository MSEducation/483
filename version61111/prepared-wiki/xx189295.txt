[[1946–47 Segunda División]]

CATEGORIES: Segunda División seasons, 1946–47 in Spanish football leagues

The 1946–47 Segunda División season saw 14 teams participate in the second flight Spanish league. Alcoyano, Gimnàstic and Real Sociedad were promoted to the Primera División. Betis, Zaragoza and Racing were relegated to the Tercera División.
