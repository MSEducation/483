[[Diepenbeek]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Limburg (Belgium)

Diepenbeek is a municipality located in the Belgian province of Limburg near Hasselt. On January 1, 2012 Diepenbeek had a total population of 18,337. Its total area is 41.19 km² which gives a population density of 430 inhabitants per km².
It is home to Hasselt University, slowly expanding towards Hasselt itself. The Limburg Science Park is located on the university campus.
