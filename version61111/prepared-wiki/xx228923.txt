Dr. A. D. Williams (November 21, 1933) Originally from St.Louis, MO, Mr. Williams, a former Marine and educator resides in Waukegan, IL where he retired as the Deputy to the Director of Naval Education and Training. He has four children and five grandchildren and one great grand son.
