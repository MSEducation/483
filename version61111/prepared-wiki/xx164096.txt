[[Vojka (Stara Pazova)]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Syrmia, Populated places in Vojvodina

Vojka ([tpl]Lang-sr-Cyrl|Војка[/tpl]) is a village in Vojvodina, Serbia, in the municipality of Stara Pazova. 

==Population==

According to the 2002 census, the village had 5,012 residents.

==Origins==

In ancient times, the city of Idiminium (IDIMINIVM) served as a Roman city and military base.
