[[Marcona District]]

Marcona District is one of five districts of the province Nazca in Peru.[tpl]es icon[/tpl] Instituto Nacional de Estadística e Informática. Banco de Información Distrital. Retrieved April 11, 2008. The district capital is San Juan de Marcona a port on the Pacific coast.

==Marcona Mine==

The major industry in the Marcona District is the Marcona Mine, an open-pit iron mine. The mine was acquired in 1992 by Shougang Corporation a state owned Chinese corporation which does business locally as Shougang Hiero Peru."Tensions Over Chinese Mining Venture in Peru" article by Simon Romero in The New York Times August 14, 2010, accessed August 14, 2010Info portal at InfoMine.Com

==Economy==

In addition to mining, there is trade and commercial fishing.
