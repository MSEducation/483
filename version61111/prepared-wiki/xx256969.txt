[[Dominique-Augustin Dufêtre]]

CATEGORIES: 1796 births, 1860 deaths, Bishops of Nevers, French Roman Catholic bishops

Dominique-Augustin Dufêtre (Lyon, 17 April 1796–Nevers, 6 November 1860)[tpl]cite web|title=Bishop Dominique-Augustin Dufêtre †|url=http://www.catholic-hierarchy.org/bishop/bdufetre.html|publisher=Catholic-Hierarchy.org|accessdate=30 December 2010[/tpl] was the bishop of the Roman Catholic Diocese of Nevers[tpl]cite book|last=Crosnier|first=Augustin-Joseph|title=Hagiologie nivernaise: ou, Vies des saints et autres pieux personnages qui ont édifié le diocese de Nevers par leurs vertus|year=1858|publisher=I.-M. Fay|pages=352–61|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=LWuyI7FDP-cC&pg=RA4-PA352[/tpl] from 1842 to 1860.
Born in Lyon, Dufêtre entered the seminary of Saint Irene and then entered the monastery of the Carthusians in Lyon, after which he preached throughout France at missions and ecclesiastical retreats.[tpl]cite news|title=Chronique religieuse du mois|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=lwZBAAAAcAAJ&pg=PA327|accessdate=30 December 2010|newspaper=L'Observateur du dimanche|year=1861|pages=322–27[/tpl] He was vicar general of Tours[tpl]cite book|last=Soultrait (comte de)|first=Jacques Hyacinthe Georges Richard|title=Armorial historique et archéologique du Nivernais, Volume 1|year=1879|publisher=Michot|pages=57|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=nrIOKANhl-QC&pg=PA57[/tpl] when he was appointed on 13 September 1842 as bishop of Nevers; he was ordained on 21 March 1843. His patron saint was Saint Dominic.
