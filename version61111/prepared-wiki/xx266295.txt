[[Avios]]

CATEGORIES: International Airlines Group

Avios is the name of the rebranded Air Miles business owned by International Airlines Group in the United Kingdom and Spain. The new brand launched on 16 November 2011.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.creativereview.co.uk/cr-blog/2011/september/interbrand-renames-airmiles-avios |title=AirMiles says adios, returns as Avios |author=Patrick Burgoyne |date=2 September 2011 |publisher=Creative Review[/tpl] Avios points are the frequent flyer currency of British Airways and Iberia and can also be used for travel within the Oneworld alliance.
