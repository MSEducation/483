[[Coleothrix crassitibiella]]

CATEGORIES: Animals described in 1888, Phycitinae

Coleothrix crassitibiella is a species of snout moths in the genus Coleothrix. It was described by Ragonot in 1888. It is found in China (Hainan), Borneo and Sumatra.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bioone.org/doi/pdf/10.3157/0002-8320%282007%29133%5B143:AROAGC%5D2.0.CO%3B2 |title=A Review on Addyme-Calguia-Coleothrix Genera Complex (Lepidoptera: Pyralidae: Phycitinae), with One New Species from China |publisher=Bioone.org |date=1970-01-01 |accessdate=2011-09-29[/tpl]
