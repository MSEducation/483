[[Hylarana igorota]]

CATEGORIES: Hylarana, Amphibians of the Philippines, Endemic fauna of the Philippines

Hylarana igorota is a species of true frog in the genus Hylarana. It is endemic to Cordillera Central of the island of Luzon, Philippines. Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical dry forests, subtropical or tropical moist lowland forests, subtropical or tropical moist montane forests, rivers, and intermittent rivers. It is threatened by habitat loss.
