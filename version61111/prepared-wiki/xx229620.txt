[[Robert Rammer]]

CATEGORIES: 1890 births, Year of death missing, Austrian male cyclists, Olympic cyclists of Austria, Cyclists at the 1912 Summer Olympics, Sportspeople from Vienna

Robert Rammer (born 14 April 1890, date of death unknown) was an Austrian road racing cyclist who competed in the 1912 Summer Olympics.
He was born in Vienna.
In 1912 he was a member of the Austrian cycling team which finished seventh in the team time trial event.
