[[Simon Danner]]

CATEGORIES: 1986 births, Living people, Augsburger Panther players, DEG Metro Stars players, Frankfurt Lions players, EHC Wolfsburg players

Simon Danner (born December 25, 1986) is a German professional ice hockey forward who currently plays for EHC Wolfsburg of the Deutsche Eishockey Liga (DEL). Danner previously played with the DEG Metro Stars after he joined them from the Frankfurt Lions on July 13, 2010.[tpl]cite web| url = http://www.deg-metro-stars.de/servlet/PB/menu/1015679/index.html | title = Stars sign Duo, Beechey and Danner | publisher = DEG Metro Stars | date = 2010-07-13 | accessdate = 2011-03-31 | language = German[/tpl] After two seasons with the Metro Stars, Danner signed a one-year contract with Grizzly Adams Wolfsburg on April 2, 2012.[tpl]citeweb| url = http://www.ehc-wolfsburg.de/beitrag1039.html | title = EHC commit to trio of forwards | publisher = EHC Wolfsburg | date = 2012-04-02 | accessdate = 2012-04-02 | language = German[/tpl]
