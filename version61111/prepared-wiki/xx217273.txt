[[In Control (Kary Ng album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2007 albums, Kary Ng albums

In Control is Kary Ng's second solo album released on June 8, 2007 in CD+DVD and CD formats. It is the champion of the album award of the four channels in Hong Kong ([tpl]zh|t=四台聯頒音樂大獎的大碟獎[/tpl]) in 2007.
