CATEGORIES: Claymore (manga), Lists of manga volumes and chapters

The chapters of the Claymore manga series are written and drawn by Norihiro Yagi. They began serialized by Shueisha, first in Monthly Shōnen Jump and are currently serialized in Jump Square.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/news/2007-04-06/shueisha-to-launch-new-monthly-magazine |title=Shueisha to Launch New Monthly Magazine |publisher=Anime News Network |date=2007-04-06 |accessdate=2009-04-06 [/tpl] The series follows the adventures of Clare, a Claymore, or half-human, half-yoma hybrid, and her comrades as they fight for survival in a world filled with yoma, or shapeshifting demons.
The first chapter was released in 2001 in the Monthly Shōnen Jump, but afterwards the chapters were released in the Weekly Shōnen Jump on a monthly basis due to the discontinuation of the prior magazine. The manga is currently serialized in the Jump Square magazine, Shueisha's replacement for the Monthly Shōnen Jump.[tpl]cite web|url=http://mj.shueisha.co.jp/suspend.html|title=月刊少年ジャンプ休刊のお知らせ|publisher=Shueisha|date=2007-06-30 |language=Japanese|accessdate=2007-10-10[/tpl] As of November 2013, 144 chapters have been released in Japan. An anime adaptation of the manga was announced in the October 2006 edition of the Monthly Shōnen Jump.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/news/2006-11-01/claymore-anime|title=Claymore Anime|publisher=Anime News Network|date=2006-11-01|accessdate=2007-10-10[/tpl] The first episode of the anime aired on April 3, 2007 on Nippon Television, with the last one shown on September 25, 2007.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.ntv.co.jp/claymore/story/01/index.html |title=Story.01 |publisher=Nippon Television |accessdate=2009-04-06 [/tpl][tpl]cite web |url=http://www.ntv.co.jp/claymore/story/index.html |title=Story.26 |publisher=Nippon Television |accessdate=2009-04-06 [/tpl]
Viz Media announced the serialization of the Claymore manga in North America on July 18, 2006 at the San Diego Comic-Con International.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/news/2005-07-18/new-viz-manga|title=New Viz Manga|publisher=Anime News Network|date=2005-07-18|accessdate=2007-10-10[/tpl]
The chapters have been compiled into 24 tankōbon in Japan by Shueisha. The first volume was released on January 5, 2002, with the 24th released on June 4, 2013. All of the volumes contain six chapters of the original manga, with the exception of the first, which contained only four chapters, and the second, incorporating the next five chapters.[tpl]cite web|url=http://mj.shueisha.co.jp/claymore/main_c.html|title=CLAYMORE -月ジャンネット-|publisher=Shueisha|accessdate=2007-10-09[/tpl] [tpl]ja icon[/tpl] 23 volumes of the English adaptation of the manga have been released by Viz Media,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.viz.com/products/products.php?series_id=450|title=VIZ Media . products|publisher=Viz Media|accessdate=2007-10-10[/tpl] the latest released on October 1, 2013.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.viz.com/manga/print/claymore-volume-23/10743|title=Claymore, Volume 23|work=Viz.com|publisher=Viz Media|accessdate=November 22, 2013[/tpl]
