[[The Parsonage (Natick, Massachusetts)]]

CATEGORIES: Houses completed in 1866, National Historic Landmarks in Massachusetts, Houses in Middlesex County, Massachusetts, Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Massachusetts, Clergy houses in the United States, Natick, Massachusetts

The Parsonage (also known as the Horatio Alger House) is a National Historic Landmark at 16 Pleasant Street in Natick, Massachusetts.
The house was built in 1866 and used as a church parsonage and as a summer home to Horatio Alger. It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1971.NHL Listing info
