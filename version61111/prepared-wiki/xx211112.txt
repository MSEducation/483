[[Mark Dickson (tennis)]]

CATEGORIES: 1959 births, Living people, American male tennis players, Clemson Tigers tennis players, People from Sarasota, Florida, Sportspeople from Tampa, Florida, Tennis people from Florida, Place of birth missing (living people)

Mark Dickson is a former professional tennis player. 
Playing college tennis at Clemson University, Dickson was selected as the 1982 ITCA Senior Player of the Year. Additionally, he was named All-American in singles in 1980, 1981 and 1982. In 1981 he was also named All-American in doubles. 
As a tour pro, Dickson is best known for being a quarter-finalist in the 1983 US Open.http://clemsontigers.cstv.com/sports/m-tennis/mtt/dickson_mark00.html College biography of Mark Dickson  He captured two grand prix tour titles, at Houston and Toulouse.  
Born in Tampa, Florida, Dickson graduated from Jesuit High School in 1978 and currently resided in Sarasota, Florida while on tour.
