[[Nanki]]

CATEGORIES: Named passenger trains of Japan, Central Japan Railway Company, Railway services introduced in 1978

The [tpl]nihongo|Nanki|南紀[/tpl] is a limited express train service in Japan operated by Central Japan Railway Company (JR Central), which runs from Nagoya to Shingū and Kii-Katsuura.
