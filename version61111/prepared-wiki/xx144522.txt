[[Deming (Amtrak station)]]

CATEGORIES: Amtrak stations in New Mexico, Buildings and structures in Luna County, New Mexico, Transportation in Luna County, New Mexico, Railway stations opened in 1881, Southern Pacific Railroad stations, Atchison, Topeka and Santa Fe Railway stations

The Deming, New Mexico (Amtrak Station) (DEM) is located at 400 East Railroad Boulevard in Deming, New Mexico. It was built in 1881, and is situated between the Union Pacific and BNSF railroads.
