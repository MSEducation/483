[[Gug Tappeh, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Gug Tappeh ([tpl]lang-fa|گوگ تپه[/tpl], also Romanized as Gūg Tappeh; also known as Gorg Tappeh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3803868|Gug Tappeh[/tpl] is a village in Chaypareh-ye Bala Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 413, in 84 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
