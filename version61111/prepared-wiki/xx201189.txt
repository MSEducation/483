[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Guanhães]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1985, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Diamantina, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Guanhães ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Guanhanensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Guanhães in the Ecclesiastical province of Diamantina in Brazil.
