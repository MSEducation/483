[[Monastery of the Holy Trinity, Kikinda]]

CATEGORIES: Serbian Orthodox monasteries, Monasteries in Vojvodina, Christian monasteries in Serbia, Banat, Kikinda

The Holy Trinity Monastery (Serbian: Манастир Свете Тројице / Manastir Svete Trojice) is a Serb Orthodox monastery located in the Banat region, in the northern Serbian province of Vojvodina. The monastery is situated in the town of Kikinda. It was built in 1885-87 as a foundation of Melanija Nikolić-Gajčić.
