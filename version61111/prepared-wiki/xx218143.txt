David William Telgheder (November 11, 1966 in Middletown, New York), is a retired Major League Baseball player who pitched from [tpl]By|1993[/tpl]-[tpl]By|1998[/tpl]. He played for the  New York Mets and the Oakland Athletics. In 1999 he pitched for the Buffalo Bisons in the Cleveland Indians organization.
