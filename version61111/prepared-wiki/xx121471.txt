[[Kamvounia]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Kozani (regional unit), Former municipalities in West Macedonia

Kamvounia ([tpl]lang-el|Καμβούνια[/tpl]) is a former municipality in Kozani regional unit, West Macedonia, Greece. Since the 2011 local government reform it is part of the municipality Servia-Velventos, of which it is a municipal unit.Kallikratis law Greece Ministry of Interior [tpl]el icon[/tpl] The 2011 census recorded 1,539 residents in the municipal unit. The seat of the municipality was in Tranovalto.
