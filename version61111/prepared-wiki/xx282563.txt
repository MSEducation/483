[[Alla Jakovleva]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Soviet female cyclists, UCI World Champions, Olympic cyclists of the Soviet Union, Cyclists at the 1988 Summer Olympics, 1963 births

Alla Jakovleva (also written as Alla Jakowlewa and Alla Yakovleva) is a retired Soviet Union female road cyclist. After finishing third in the women's road race at the 1986 UCI Road World Championships she became world champion in the women's team time trial in 1987 and finished second in the women's team time trial in 1988.[tpl]cite book|title=Alla Jakovleva|url=http://www.cyclingarchives.com/coureurfiche.php?coureurid=62492|publisher=cyclingarchives.com|accessdate=26 July 2013[/tpl] Jakovleva competed at the 1988 Summer Olympics in the women's road race and finished 34th.Official Report women's road race 1988 Olympic-museum.de
