[[Marcelo Marmelo da Silva]]

CATEGORIES: 1972 births, Living people, Brazilian footballers, Brazilian expatriates in China, Guangzhou Evergrande F.C. players, Expatriate footballers in China

Marcelo Marmelo da Silva (born August 1, 1972) is former Brazilian footballer who played as a Striker. He is one of the most famous player in Chinese former top league Jia A.
Marmelo scored a superb solo goal which was scored one of the greatest goal in Chinese professional league history, in a game against Shanghai Shenhua.
Marmelo is living in Chengdu after he retired. His wife is a Russian and has a child.http://news.eastday.com/epublish/gb/paper148/20030829/class014800013/hwz1003665.htm
