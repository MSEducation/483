[[Sedition (album)]]

CATEGORIES: Heavy metal albums by New Zealand artists, 2005 albums

Sedition is the second album released by New Zealand death metal band Dawn of Azazel. It was released in October, 2005 in New Zealand, on Extreme Imprints. It was also released in USA and Europe in 2006 on Ibex Moon Records. It was the first studio album to feature Martin Cavanagh on drums.
