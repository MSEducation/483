[[Colville Island]]

CATEGORIES: San Juan Islands

Colville Island is an island in the San Juan Islands of the U.S. state of Washington. It lies near Point Colville, on the southeastern tip of Lopez Island.
The island and the point were named for Andrew Colville, the governor of Hudson's Bay Company, 1852-56.[tpl]cite book |last= Phillips |first= James W. |title= Washington State Place Names |year= 1971 |publisher= University of Washington Press |isbn= 0-295-95158-3[/tpl]
