[[Elena Donazzan]]

Elena Donazzan (Bassano del Grappa, 22 June 1972) is an Italian politician from Veneto.
A member of National Alliance (and, prior to that, of the Italian Social Movement), she was elected to the Regional Council of Veneto in the 2000 regional election.
