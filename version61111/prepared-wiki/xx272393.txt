[[Sanlıca]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Mersin Province, Mediterranean Region, Turkey, Villages in Turkey, Tarsus District

 
Sanlıca is a small village in Tarsus district of Mersin Province, Turkey. It is situated in Toros Mountains and extreme east of the province.  At [tpl]coord|37|14|N|34|51|E[/tpl] it is about [tpl]convert|45|km[/tpl] to Tarsus and [tpl]convert|72|km[/tpl] to Mersin. The population of village is 109 Statistical Institute as of 2011.
