[[Toussaint (album)]]

CATEGORIES: Allen Toussaint albums, 1971 albums, Albums produced by Allen Toussaint, Albums produced by Charles Greene

Toussaint is a 1971 solo funk, jazz and soul album by Allen Toussaint, his second solo album and his first since the 1950s.

==Track listing==

All songs written by Allen Toussaint unless otherwise indicated.
