[[Suzanne Pharr]]

CATEGORIES: American political consultants, Living people

Suzanne Pharr is an American organizer, political strategist, and author who has worked to build a broad-based social justice movement in the United States. Pharr is the founder of the Women's Project (based in Arkansas) and the author of the book Homophobia: A Weapon of Sexism (published in 1988 by Chardon Press).[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.nytimes.com/1991/04/28/us/3000-lesbians-meet-in-atlanta-to-set-own-agenda.html|title=3,000 Lesbians Meet in Atlanta to Set Own Agenda|last=Smothers|first=Ronald|date=April 28, 1991|work=The New York Times|accessdate=2009-08-29[/tpl]
