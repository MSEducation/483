[[Parag Madkaikar]]

CATEGORIES: Indian cricketers, Railways cricketers, 1986 births, Living people

Parag Mohan Madkaikar (born 22 April 1986 in Bombay) is an all-round Indian cricketer. Parag has represented Mumbai, and played in the winning MCA President's XI vs the touring Sri Lankans on 22 October 2005, at the Wankhede Stadium in Mumbai.http://www.cricketfundas.com/slinind05oct22warmupmum.html
Parag now plays for Hawkinge Cricket Club in the Kent Cricket Feeder League 1D, based near Folkestone, Kent, and is on course to be leading run-scorer in the division in 2008.
