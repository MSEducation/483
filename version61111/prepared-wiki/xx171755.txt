[[Åke Grönberg]]

CATEGORIES: 1914 births, 1969 deaths, Male actors from Stockholm, Deaths from myocardial infarction, Swedish actors, 20th-century Swedish male actors

Karl Åke Edvard Grönberg (26 March 1914 – 15 September 1969) was a Swedish film actor who appeared in nearly 100 films.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.sfi.se/en-GB/Swedish-film-database/Item/?type=PERSON&itemid=60294 |title=Åke Grönberg |accessdate=2013-02-06|work=Swedish Film Database[/tpl] Gronberg was a versatile stage personality of his day, performing as a singer, variety artist, actor, in musical shows and in dramatic productions.
He was born in Stockholm, Sweden and died of a heart attack in 1969.
