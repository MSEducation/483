[[Martin Sauer]]

CATEGORIES: 1982 births, Living people, Coxes, Rowers at the 2012 Summer Olympics, Olympic rowers of Germany, Olympic medalists in rowing, Olympic gold medalists for Germany, Medalists at the 2012 Summer Olympics

[tpl]MedalCountry|[tpl]GER[/tpl][/tpl]
Martin Sauer (born 17 December 1982 in Wriezen) is a German rowing cox. He was part of the team that won the gold medal in the Men's eight competition at the 2012 Summer Olympics in London.
