[[Stephen Au]]

CATEGORIES: 1964 births, TVB veteran actors, Hong Kong actors, Living people

Stephen Au Kam-tong (born 26 April 1964) is a Hong Kong actor for TVB (2000–present). He was formerly an actor for ATV (1990–2000).
He played David Mo (武紀勇) in TVB sitcom Best Selling Secrets (2007–2008).
