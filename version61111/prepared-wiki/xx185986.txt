Paul Turner (born c. 1924) was the tenth head football coach for the Northwest Missouri State University Bearcats located in Maryville, Missouri and he held that position for two seasons, from 1958 until [959.  His career coaching record at Northwest Missouri State was 3 wins, 11 losses, and 2 ties.  As of 2007, this ranks him 12th at Northwest Missouri State in total wins and 14th at Northwest Missouri State in winning percentage.Northwest Missouri State Football Media Guide
