[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Lokoja]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic Church in Nigeria, Roman Catholic dioceses in Nigeria, Religious organizations established in 1955, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Lokoja ([tpl]lang-la|Lokoian(us)[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Lokoja, Kogi State in the Ecclesiastical province of Abuja in Nigeria.
