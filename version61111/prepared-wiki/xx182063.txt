[[Angels Running]]

CATEGORIES: Patty Larkin albums, 1993 albums

Angels Running is the fifth album by singer-songwriter Patty Larkin. Produced by Ben Wisch in 1993 and distributed by High Street Records, it contained the following songs:Patty Larkin, Angels Running. Retrieved January 2, 2008.

==Track listing==

All songs were written by Patty Larkin except Banish Misfortune/Open Hand (traditional).
The song "Good Thing" was covered by Cher, titled simply "Angels Running", on her album "It's a Man's World"
