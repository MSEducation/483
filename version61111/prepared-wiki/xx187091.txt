[[Sarduri II]]

CATEGORIES: Urartian kings, History of Armenia, Anatolia, 8th-century BC rulers

Sarduri II (ruled 764-735 BCE) was a King of Urartu (modern-day Turkey and Armenia). The Urartian Kingdom was at its peak during his reign. He succeeded his father Argishti I to the throne.
