[[Abhirami Suresh]]

CATEGORIES: Malayalam cinema, Indian film actresses, Living people, 21st-century Indian actresses, Date of birth missing (living people)

Abhirami Suresh is an Indian film actress best known for her work in Malayalam cinema. She was born to PR Swresh and Laila Suresh.  She made her debut in television by imitating Sarath and Renjini Haridas.
