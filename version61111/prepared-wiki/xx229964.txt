[[C. J. Carter-Stephenson]]

CATEGORIES: 1977 births, Living people, English horror writers, English short story writers, English fantasy writers, English science fiction writers, English poets, English dramatists and playwrights, Alumni of De Montfort University

Christian James Carter-Stephenson is an English writer of fiction, poetry and drama. He writes primarily in the genres of horror, science fiction and fantasy. He was born in the county of Essex on 31 January 1977 and attended King Edward VI Grammar School from the ages of 11 to 18. In 1998, he graduated from De Montfort University in Leicester with a BA (Hons) in English and Performing Arts, and in 1999, obtained a postgraduate diploma in Acting from the Academy of Live & Recorded Arts in London. He currently lives on the Isle of Wight with his common-law wife.
