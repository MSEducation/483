[[Costești]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Argeș County, Towns in Romania, Monotowns in Romania

Costeşti ([tpl]IPA-ro|kosˈteʃtʲ[/tpl]) is a town in Argeş county, Wallachia, Romania. The population [tpl]as of|2002[/tpl] was 10,868.
The town administers six villages: Broşteni, Lăceni, Pârvu Roşu, Podu Broşteni, Smei and Stârci.
