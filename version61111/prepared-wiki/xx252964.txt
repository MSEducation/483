[[Safar Sardar]]

CATEGORIES: Indian footballers, 1989 births, Living people, I-League players, East Bengal F.C. players

Safar Sardar (born 17 May 1989 in Kolkata, West Bengal) is an Indian footballer who plays as a defender for East Bengal in the I-League.

==Career==

===Early career===

Sardar started his career at Chirag United in 2009 and stayed till 2010.[tpl]cite web|title=Chirag on cloud nine as EB leak goals|url=http://www.telegraphindia.com/1090906/jsp/sports/story_11456407.jsp|work=Telegraph India|accessdate=6 August 2012[/tpl] He then joined Mohammedan for two years.

===East Bengal===

Sardar joined East Bengal FC for the 2012–13 I-League season on 15 May 2012.http://www.footballdatabase.eu/football.joueurs.safar.sardar.168652.en.html

===United Sikkim (loan)===

On 25 August 2012, East Bengal agreed to loan Sardar to United Sikkim on a season-long deal, where he was given the number 32 shirt.
Sardar made his debut for United Sikkim F.C. on 2 December 2012 during an I-League match against Churchill Brothers S.C. at the Paljor Stadium in Gangtok, Sikkim in which he was in Starting 11; United Sikkim drew the match 0–0.[tpl]cite web|title=United Sikkim 0-0 Churchill Brothers|url=http://int.soccerway.com/matches/2012/12/02/india/i-league/united-sikkim-sc/churchill-brothers-sc/1394200/|work=Soccerway|accessdate=26 May 2013[/tpl]
