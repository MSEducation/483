[[Rogojevac (Stanovo)]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Šumadija District

Rogojevac ([tpl]Lang-sr-Cyrl|Рогојевац[/tpl]) is a village in the Stanovo municipality in the Šumadija district, Serbia. According to the 2002 census there were 404 inhabitants (1991: 464).
Bronze Age graves of south Russian steppe nomads was found in the village.http://www.rastko.rs/arheologija/srejovic/dsrejovic-eneolit.html
