[[André Pichot]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, French academics, French scientists

André Pichot is a researcher in Epistemology and History of Science, based at CNRS in Strasbourg. He was a pupil of Georges Canguilhem. He is known in France for his critical writings on issues related to genetics, in particular the influence modern biology has had on ideologies supporting eugenics.
