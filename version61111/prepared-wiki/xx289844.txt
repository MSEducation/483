[[Mahisagar district]]

CATEGORIES: Mahisagar district, 2013 establishments in India

Mahisagar district – also Mahisagar – ([tpl]lang-gu|મહીસાગર જિલ્લો[/tpl]) is a district in the state of Gujarat in India that came into being on 26 January 2013, becoming the 28th district of the state. The district has been carved out of the Panchmahal district and Kheda district.[tpl]cite news|title=Mahisagar now a district in Gujarat|url=http://www.dnaindia.com/ahmedabad/1874372/report-seven-new-districts-as-gujarat-s-i-day-gift|accessdate=14 August 2013|newspaper=DNA|date=14 August 2013[/tpl] & Lunawada a princely state is capital of District Mahisagar, started its operaration in full-fledged from 15 August 2013, by opening Collector Office on Godhra Road
Mahisagar district consist of 6 talukas:

1. Khanpur

2. Kadana

3. Virpur

4. Santrampur

5. Lunawada

6. Balasinor
