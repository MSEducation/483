[[Chad Premier League]]

CATEGORIES: Football competitions in Chad, National association football premier leagues

Chad Premier League is the top division of the Fédération Tchadienne de Football in Chad. It consists of 10 clubs and they are all from capital city N'Djamena.

==Clubs 2013==

==Format==

Today the national championship is played by all regional champions over a short time, usually within a week and held allongside the national cup. The leagues, as the most prominent Ligue de N'Djaména (the capital's one) run over a whole season.
