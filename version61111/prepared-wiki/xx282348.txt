[[Brake Lights (mixtape)]]

CATEGORIES: Game (rapper) albums, 2010 mixtape albums, Albums produced by DJ Toomp, Albums produced by Cool & Dre, Albums produced by Lex Luger, Gangsta rap albums

Brake Lights is a mixtape by West Coast rapper Game released as a free online download on August 3, 2010. The mixtape is hosted by DJ Skee."XXL Presents Game’s ‘Brake Lights’ Mixtape", XXL, August 3, 2010.
