[[English Evangelical Church of the Reformation and Parish House]]

CATEGORIES: Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Religious buildings completed in 1902, 20th-century Evangelical church buildings, Churches in Rochester, New York

English Evangelical Church of the Reformation and Parish House is a historic church complex located at Rochester in Monroe County, New York.
It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1992.
