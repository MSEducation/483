[[Sultanah Haminah Hamidun]]

CATEGORIES: 1953 births, Living people, Royal Consorts of Kedah, Consorts of Malaysia, Members of the Royal Family Order of Kedah, Members of the Halimi Family Order of Kedah, Recipients of the Order of the Crown of the Realm, Grand Cordons of the Order of the Precious Crown

Tuanku Sultanah Hajah Haminah binti Hamidun (born 15 July 1953) is the current Raja Permaisuri Agong, or Queen, of Malaysia and the current Sultanah of Kedah. Born a commoner from Bagan Serai, Perak, she married Sultan Abdul Halim of Kedah on 25 December 1975, and as the second wife of the Sultan was titled Che Puan Kedah. She was elevated to the rank of Sultanah on 9 January 2004 following the death of Tuanku Bahiyah, the previous Sultanah and first wife of Sultan Abdul Halim.

==Early life==

Haminah binti Hamidun was born on 15 July 1953 in Bagan Serai, Perak. She is the fourth of seven siblings born to father Hamidun Taib and mother Isma Mohamad. Haminah was educated at SK Matang Jelutong and later SM Inggeris Bagan Serai.[tpl]cite news |title=New Sultanah of Kedah crowned |newspaper=New Straits Times  |page=4 |author=Alina Simon |date=10 January 2004[/tpl]

==Royalty==

On 25 December 1975, at age 22, Haminah married the Sultan of Kedah, Sultan Abdul Halim, and became his second wife. She received the title Che Puan Kedah. Sultan Abdul Halim's first wife, Tuanku Bahiyah, died on 27 August 2003. On 21 November, Che Puan Haminah was proclaimed Sultanah. Her coronation ceremony took place in the royal town of Anak Bukit on 9 January 2004.
Upon the installation of Sultan Abdul Halim as Yang di-Pertuan Agong on 13 December 2011, Tuanku Haminah became Raja Permaisuri Agong.[tpl]cite web |title=Grand send-off for Sultan of Kedah |url=http://www.nst.com.my/top-news/grand-send-off-for-sultan-of-kedah-1.18728 |work=New Straits Times |date=13 December 2011 |author=Adib Povera |accessdate=13 December 2011[/tpl] Tuanku Haminah is the fourth commoner who become a Raja Permaisuri Agong after Tuanku Bainun binti Mohamad Ali of Perak, Tuanku Permaisuri Siti Aishah of Selangor and Tuanku Nur Zahirah of Terengganu.

==Interests==

Tuanku Haminah is known to enjoy golf, tennis, badminton and music.[tpl]cite web |title=Che Puan Haminah dimasyhur Sultanah Kedah |url=http://ww2.utusan.com.my/utusan/special.asp?pr=PR11&y=2004&dt=0110&pub=Utusan_Malaysia&sec=Muka_Hadapan&pg=mh_02.htm |work=Utusan Malaysia |date=10 January 2004 |language=Malay |archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/63uAO74vN |archivedate=13 December 2011[/tpl] She has participated in various golf tournaments in Kedah and initiated amateur women's golf tournaments such as the Kedah International Red Tee Invitational in Langkawi.[tpl]cite news |title=Queen of the rakyat's heart |author=Embun Majid |newspaper=The Star |date=2011-12-13 |at=Daulat Tuanku, p. 4[/tpl]
