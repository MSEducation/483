[[Uncle Jack]]

CATEGORIES: BBC children's television programmes, Environmental television, 1990 British television programme debuts, 1993 British television programme endings

Uncle Jack was a children's TV show which aired on BBC1 in the early 1990s.  The show's hero, Jack Green, and his family are on a mission to save the planet.  Jack Green's arch nemesis was a woman who was only known as The Vixen who would be planning on overtaking the world.
