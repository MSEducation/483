[[Khararud Rural District (Zanjan Province)]]

CATEGORIES: Rural Districts of Zanjan Province, Khodabandeh County

Khararud Rural District ([tpl]lang-fa|دهستان خرارود[/tpl]) is a rural district (dehestan) in the Central District of Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 22,070, in 4,754 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl] The rural district has 30 villages.
