[[Paralympic powerlifting]]

CATEGORIES: Paralympic sports, Powerlifting

Paralympic powerlifting is an adaptation of the sport of powerlifting for athletes with disabilities. The only discipline in Paralympic powerlifting is the bench press.
The sport is governed by the International Paralympic Committee and is open to anyone with a minimum level of disability who can extend their arms within 20Â° of full extension during a lift. Powerlifting has been competed at the Summer Paralympics since 1984.[tpl]cite web |title=About the sport |year=2009 |publisher=International Paralympic Committee |url=http://www.paralympic.org/release/Summer_Sports/Powerlifting/About_the_sport/ |accessdate=2009-01-08[/tpl]

==World records==

World records in Paralympic powerlifting are ratified by the International Paralympic Committee.
