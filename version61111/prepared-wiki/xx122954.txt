All About the Andersons is a situation comedy that originally aired on The WB from September 12, 2003 to February 12, 2004. It lasted one season before being canceled.

==Premise==

Anthony Anderson is a single father and struggling actor who moves back into his parents' house only to find that his room has been rented out to a nursing student.
