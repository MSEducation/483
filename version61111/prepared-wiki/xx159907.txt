Nilton Pacheco de Oliveira (July 26, 1920 – June 26, 2013)[tpl]cite web|title=Ex-Flu, medalhista olímpico pela seleção morre aos 92 anos, no Rio|url=http://globoesporte.globo.com/basquete/noticia/2013/06/ex-flu-medalhista-olimpico-pela-selecao-morre-aos-92-anos-no-rio.html|trans_title=Former 'Flu', National team olympic medalist dies at 92 in Rio|publisher=GloboEsporte.com|accessdate=28 June 2013|date=27 June 2013|language=Portuguese[/tpl] was a Brazilian basketball player who competed in the 1948 Summer Olympics in London, United Kingdom. There he won the bronze medal with the men's national basketball team under the guidance of head coach Moacyr Daiuto. He was born in Salvador, Bahia.
