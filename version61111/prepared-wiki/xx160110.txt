[[Belgian general election, 1968]]

CATEGORIES: 1968 elections in Europe, Elections in Belgium, 1968 in Belgium

General elections were held in Belgium on 31 March 1968.Nohlen, D & Stöver, P (2010) Elections in Europe: A data handbook, p289 ISBN 978-3-8329-5609-7 Although the Belgian Socialist Party received the most votes in the Chamber of Representatives elections, the Christian Social Party won the most seats. The Christian Social Party also emerged as the largest party in the Senate.Nohlen & Stöver, pp309-311 Voter turnout was 90.0%.Nohlen & Stöver, p291
