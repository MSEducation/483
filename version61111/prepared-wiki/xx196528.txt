[[2002 Men's World Floorball Championships]]

CATEGORIES: 2002 in sports, 2002 in floorball, International sports competitions hosted by Finland, Floorball World Championships

The 2002 Men's World Floorball Championships were the fourth world
championships in men's floorball. It was held from 18 May 2002 to 25 May 2002 in Finland. Sweden won the tournament for a fourth time after defeating Finland in the final.
