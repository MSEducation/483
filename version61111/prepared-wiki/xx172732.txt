[[Thulsa Doom (album)]]

CATEGORIES: Reverend Bizarre albums, 2006 EPs

 
Thulsa Doom is an EP by Finnish doom metal band Reverend Bizarre. It was recorded between March 2003 and April 2005 and was released in May 2006.
The recording is dedicated to actor James Earl Jones, who played the character Thulsa Doom in the 1982 film Conan the Barbarian.
