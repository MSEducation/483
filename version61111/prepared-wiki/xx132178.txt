[[Skaneateles High School]]

CATEGORIES: Public high schools in New York, Schools in Onondaga County, New York

Skaneateles Senior High School is a public high school in Onondaga County, New York, United States and is the highest level of the Skaneateles Central School District. It is Nationally Recognized for Excellence.[tpl]Citation needed|date=January 2009[/tpl]

==Skaneateles Central School District==

Belle H. Waterman Elementary School serves students from grades Kindergarten through second grade. State Street Elementary School serves third grade through fifth grade students. Skaneateles Middle School, which is adjacent to the high school, serves grades sixth through eighth, and Skaneateles High School serves ninth through twelfth.

==Curriculum==

Skaneateles High School claims to offer more than 130 different courses in English, Social Studies, mathematics, science, foreign language, art, business, health, home economics, technology education, music, and physical education.  Additional vocational training opportunities are available to Skaneateles students through the Cayuga-Onondaga BOCES.

==Administration==

Skaneateles High School is currently run by an administration consisting of one principal, one assistant principal, one humanities curriculum coordinator, and one math and science curriculum coordinator.

==Drama program==

The school is known for its drama productions in the fall and spring.  In the fall, the production is typically a musical, and in the spring, a straight play.
