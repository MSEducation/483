Daniel Paul Lewis (born 18 June 1982) is an English footballer who plays for Kidderminster Harriers as a goalkeeper.

==Career==

Born in Redditch, Worcestershire, Lewis played for the youth team of Alvechurch and went on to play for Bromsgrove Sunday team Garringtons, where he conceded 40 goals in 37 games. He left in 2002 for Studley and forced his way into the team, but had a trial at Kidderminster Harriers and joined them in May 2004. He went on to make 15 appearances in the Conference National the following season, after which he joined Moor Green in the Conference North. After one season with Moor Green, he joined hometown club Redditch United in July 2007. He re-signed for Kidderminster on a one-year contract on 10 May 2010.
