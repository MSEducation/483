[[Gordy Combs]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Dayton Flyers football players, Johns Hopkins Blue Jays football coaches, Players of American football from Maryland, Sportspeople from Baltimore, Maryland, Towson Tigers football coaches, Towson Tigers football players

Gordy Combs was the head coach of the Towson Tigers football team from 1992 through 2008. He had spent 19 seasons as an assistant coach to Phil Albert at Towson before taking over when Albert retired. Combs was replaced in 2009 by Rob Ambrose.
