[[Melissa James Gibson]]

CATEGORIES: American dramatists and playwrights, Living people, Columbia University alumni, Yale School of Drama alumni, Obie Award recipients, Guggenheim Fellows

Melissa James Gibson is a Canadian-born playwright based in New York.

==Life==

The child of former BC Liberal MLA Gordon Gibson and his journalist wife Valerie, Gibson grew up in North Vancouver.[tpl]dead link|date=May 2013[/tpl] She graduated from Columbia University and from the Yale School of Drama with an M.F.A. in Playwriting. She is working on commissions for the La Jolla Playhouse and The Adirondack Theatre Festival. She has received fellowships from the Jerome Foundation and the MacDowell Colony.[tpl]cite web|url=http://newdramatists.org/melissa_james_gibson.htm# |title=Alumni Playwrights |publisher=New Dramatists |date= |accessdate=2013-05-22[/tpl]
The New York Times theatre critic Charles Isherwood wrote that, with her play This, Gibson “graduates into the theatrical big leagues with this beautifully conceived, confidently executed and wholly accessible work.”
