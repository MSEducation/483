[[Calathea ornata]]

CATEGORIES: Calathea, House plants, Plants described in 1848, Flora of Colombia, Flora of Venezuela

Calathea ornata (also called variously Striped, Pin-stripe, or Pin-stripe calathea) is a species of perennial plant in the family known as the prayer plants. It is native to South America (Colombia, Venezuela), and is cultivated in temperate countries as a houseplant. It is affected by spider mites.
