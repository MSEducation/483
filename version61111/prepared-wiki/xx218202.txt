[[Mike Mimbs]]

CATEGORIES: Major League Baseball pitchers, Philadelphia Phillies players, Great Falls Dodgers players, Mercer Bears baseball players, Yakima Bears players, Vero Beach Dodgers players, San Antonio Missions players, St. Paul Saints players, Harrisburg Senators players, Scranton/Wilkes-Barre Red Barons players, Vancouver Canadians players, Columbus Clippers players, Baseball players from Georgia (U.S. state), 1969 births, Living people

Michael Randall Mimbs (born February 13, 1969) is a retired professional baseball pitcher. He played all or part of three seasons in Major League Baseball from 1995 to 1997 for the Philadelphia Phillies.
