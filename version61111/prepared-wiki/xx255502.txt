[[Indore–Surat Express]]

CATEGORIES: Rail transport in Madhya Pradesh, Train services in India, Transport in Indore, Indian Railways trains, Transport in Surat, Rail transport in Gujarat

The Indore–Surat Express (Hindi:इन्दौर –  एक्सप्रेस, Marathi: इंदूर –  एक्सप्रेस, Gujarati:, Urdu:‏ ایکسپریس – اندو), is a holiday special express service offered by Western Railway. It runs between Indore Junction railway station of Indore, the largest city and commercial hub of Central Indian state, Madhya Pradesh and Surat the economic capiltal of Gujarat and city of diamond and silk in India.
The service is tri-weekly; mainly from September to January.

==Coach composite==

1 AC II TIER, 3 AC III TIER, 12 SLEEPER, 4 GENERAL are available along with 1 Luggage cum break van.

==Route==

Train goes via Ratlam
