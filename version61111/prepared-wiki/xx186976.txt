[[Rome News-Tribune]]

CATEGORIES: Rome, Georgia, Newspapers published in Georgia (U.S. state), Publications established in 1843

Rome News-Tribune is the local daily newspaper of Rome, Georgia. Begun originally as a weekly newspaper, the paper has survived several merges with other newspapers.
