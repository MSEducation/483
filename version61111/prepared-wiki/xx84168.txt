[[Yamagata, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Yamagata|山形村|Yamagata-mura[/tpl] is a village located in Higashichikuma District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the village has an estimated population of 8,094 and a density of 324.54 persons per km². The total area is 24.94 km².
