[[Horrie Seden]]

CATEGORIES: Australian darts players, Living people, Year of birth missing (living people), British Darts Organisation players

Horrie Seden is an Australian former darts player.
Seden won the 1984 Australian Grand Masters and played in the 1984 Winmau World Masters, losing in the first round to Ceri Morgan.  Seden then played in the 1988 BDO World Darts Championship but lost in the first round 3-2 in sets to Bob Sinnaeve.
