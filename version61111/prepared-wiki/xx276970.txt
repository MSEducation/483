[[Enslow Publishers]]

Enslow Publishers is an American publisher of books[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.enslow.com/pages/Company-Information/3 |title=Company Information | Enslow Publishers, Inc |publisher=Enslow.com |date= |accessdate=2013-08-08[/tpl] and eBooks founded by Ridley M. Enslow, Jr. in 1976.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.pr.com/press-release/485744 |title=Enslow Publishers Announces Release of eBooks |publisher=PR.com |date=2013-04-17 |accessdate=2013-08-08[/tpl] Enslow Publishers publishes educational nonfiction, fiction, historical fiction, and trade books for children and young adults.  Their books are intended to be sold to school and public libraries. 
Enslow Publishers, Inc., is based in New Jersey and currently under the direction of Ridley (Mark) Enslow III, who serves as president, and Brian Enslow, who acts as vice president and publisher. 
Its current imprints include Enslow Elementary, Speeding Star and Chasing Roses. Enslow Publishers, Inc., uses 3rd party authors to write the manuscripts, and uses in-house editorial and production staff to create their final products.  Marketing, warehousing, and shipping operations are conducted at their headquarters in Berkeley Heights location.

==Notable books==

Enslow Publishers has published titles that have won many awards. Most recently is the title Jesse Owens: "I Always Loved Running" written by Jeff Burlingame won a NAACP Image Award for Outstanding Literary Work, Youth/Teens.
