[[Wuda District]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Wuda District (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠤᠳᠠ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠲᠣᠭᠣᠷᠢᠭ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Uda toɣoriɣ[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=乌达区 |t=烏達區 |p=Wūdá Qū[/tpl]) is a district of the city of Wuhai, Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China, located on the west (left) bank of the Yellow River.
