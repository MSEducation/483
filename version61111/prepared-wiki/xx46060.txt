[[Houyet]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Belgium, Municipalities of Namur (province)

Houyet ([tpl]IPA-fr|u.jɛ[/tpl]) is a Walloon municipality located in the Belgian province of Namur. On 1 January 2006 the municipality had 4,485 inhabitants. The total area is 122.31 km², giving a population density of 37 inhabitants per km².
The town of Houyet lies on the river Lesse, some 20 km to the southeast of Dinant.
 
Hour is one of the major areas of the Houyet municipality.
