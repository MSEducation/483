[[Syrian International Academy for Training and Development]]

CATEGORIES: Education in Damascus, Buildings and structures in Damascus

The Syrian International Academy for Training and Development ([tpl]lang-ar|الأكاديمية السورية الدولية للتدريب والتطوير[/tpl]) is an independent institution established jointly by the Syrian PR Association, International PR Association and Al Jazeera Media Training and Development Center. Offers a diploma in PR, short courses and workshops.
