[[Sharmi Albrechtsen]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Danish bloggers, Temple University alumni

Sharmi Albrechtsen is a Canadian born American-Indian journalist and author known for her blog Happy Danes on the official site, Blogging Denmark.[tpl]cite web| last=Oh| first=Yeonho| title=A Second Divorce Helped Her Find Real Happines |url=http://www.ohmynews.com/NWS_Web/View/at_pg.aspx?CNTN_CD=A0001876844| date=June 12, 2013 |publisher=OhMyNews| accessdate=August 12, 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web| last=Rajiv| first=Sarita| title=The Balancing Act Downloading the Happiness App| url=http://cphpost.dk/commentary/cph-post-voices/balancing-act-downloading-happiness-app| date=May 26, 2013| publisher=The Copenhagen Post| accessdate=August 12, 2013[/tpl] She has written a book, A Piece of Danish Happiness, which explores why the Danish people are considered the happiest in the world, according to some sources, including the 2012 Gallup Poll taken by the United Nations.[tpl]cite web|url=http://cphpost.dk/inout/book-review-piece-danish-happiness|title=Book Review : A Piece of Danish Happiness|author=Jessica Hanley|date=19 September 2013|accessdate=20 September 2013|publisher=The Copenhagen Post[/tpl][tpl]cite web| url=http://www.thebookbag.co.uk/reviews/index.php?title=A_Piece_of_Danish_Happiness_by_Sharmi_Albrechtsen| title=A Piece of Danish Happiness by Sharmi Albrechtsen| publisher = The Bookbag| accessdate=22 October 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web| last=van Gilder Cooke| first=Sonia| title=Where are the World’s Happiest Countries| url=http://newsfeed.time.com/2012/04/06/where-are-the-worlds-happiest-countries/| date=April 6, 2012| publisher=Time| accessdate=August 12, 2013[/tpl] Albrechtsen attributes this happiness to the cultural Laws of Jante, a state of “Hygge” meaning being together comfortably, Denmark’s welfare system, and factors intrinsic to being happy. Her writing infuses her personal story, and in 2012, she told her story to Oprah Winfrey during a televised Lifeclass in Toronto.[tpl]cite web| last=| first=| title=Oprah’s Lifeclass Mastering the Art of Gratitude|url=http://www.oprah.com/oprahs-lifeclass/First-Look-Oprahs-Lifeclass-Mastering-the-Art-of-Gratitude-Video| publisher=Oprah| accessdate=August 12, 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web| last=| first=| title=Sharmi and Oprah| url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=L28l3bnIYks| date=| publisher=Oprah| accessdate=August 12, 2013[/tpl]

==Personal life==

Albrechtsen is married to Jesper Nielsen and lives in Charlottenlund, Denmark.
Albrechtsen has received her undergraduate degree from Temple University and an MBA from Copenhagen Business School.
