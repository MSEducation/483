[[Haplochrois ochraceella]]

CATEGORIES: Animals described in 1903, Elachistidae, Moths of Europe, Insects of Germany, Insects of Romania, Insects of Albania, Insects of Hungary, Insects of Greece, Insects of Italy

Haplochrois ochraceella is a species of moth of the Elachistidae family. It is found in southern and central Europe.
The wingspan is 10–15 mm. Adults have been recorded from the beginning of June to the beginning of August.
The foodplant is unknown.
