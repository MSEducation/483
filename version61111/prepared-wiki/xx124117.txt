[[Hibana velox]]

CATEGORIES: Anyphaenidae, Spiders of North America

Hibana velox (formerly also known as Aysha velox) is a common spider of North America, for example Texas and Alabama. It can be found in foliage and in houses. It is useful in preying on insects like the citrus leafminer, Phyllocnistis citrella (Gracillariidae).
