[[Live in Concert (Natalie Merchant album)]]

CATEGORIES: Natalie Merchant albums, 1999 live albums, Live video albums, 1999 video albums, Elektra Records video albums, Elektra Records live albums, English-language video albums, English-language live albums

Live in Concert is a 1999 live album and DVD by Natalie Merchant.  The album and DVD were recorded at the Neil Simon Theatre in New York City. The setlist includes a rare cover of David Bowie's "Space Oddity".  The U.S. release is an HDCD hybrid.
