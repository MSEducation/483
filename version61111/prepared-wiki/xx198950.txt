[[Francis Bruguière]]

CATEGORIES: 1879 births, 1945 deaths, American photographers

Francis Joseph Bruguière (1879-1945) was an American-born photographer. Friends with Alfred Stieglitz, Bruguière worked in San Francisco (his hometown), New York, and London.
Frances Bruguière was a grandson of Peder Sather and a brother to Peder Sather Bruguière (1874-1967), a painter and physician.
His partner, from 1920 and for the rest of his life, was the actress Rosalinde Fuller (1892-1982).
