[[North Shore Art Association]]

CATEGORIES: Art in Massachusetts

[tpl]primarysources|date=November 2008[/tpl]The North Shore Art Association of East Gloucester, Massachusetts is one of the oldest art associations in the United States. Founded in 1922, it was the gathering place of some of the great  American Artists of the 20th century. 
Childe Hassam, Emile Gruppe, Paul Strisik, Fredrick Mulhaupt, Winslow Homer and many others were early members of the NSAA.
