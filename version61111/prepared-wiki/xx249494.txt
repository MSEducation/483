Comlanvi Akakpo (born 30 January 1985 in Lomé) is a Beninese football player who currently plays in Benin for Tonnerre d'Abomey FC.

==Career==

He began his career in his native Togo for Etoile Filante de Lomé and signed in January 2007 for Tonnerre d'Abomey FC.Ligue 1 AKAKPOVI, GUERRIER AFFAME - Blog de beninfootball
