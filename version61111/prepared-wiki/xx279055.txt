[[Degerman Daresi]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Degerman Daresi ([tpl]lang-fa|دگرمان درسي[/tpl], also Romanized as Degermān Daresī; also known as Degermān Darrehsī)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795462|Degerman Daresi[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 331, in 79 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
