[[Hylarana celebensis]]

CATEGORIES: Hylarana, Endemic fauna of Indonesia, Amphibians of Indonesia, Fauna of Sulawesi

Hylarana celebensis is a species of true frog in the genus Hylarana.[tpl]cite web |url=http://research.amnh.org/vz/herpetology/amphibia/?action=names&taxon=Hylarana+celebensis |title=Hylarana celebensis (Peters, 1872) |author=Frost, Darrel R. |date=2013 |work=Amphibian Species of the World 5.6, an Online Reference |publisher=American Museum of Natural History |accessdate=1 June 2013[/tpl] It is native to Sulawesi (Celebes), Indonesia. It is a lowland forest species, also occurring disturbed habitats.
