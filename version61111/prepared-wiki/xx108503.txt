[[Banu Taym]]

CATEGORIES: Quraish sub-clans

Banu Taym or Banu Taim or Banu Tahim  is a sub-clan to the Quraish tribe. 
They come from  Taym ibn Murrah ibn Ka'ab ibn Lu'ai ibn Ghalib ibn Fihr ibn Malik ibn Alnadhr.
