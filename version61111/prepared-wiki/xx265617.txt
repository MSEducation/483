[[Kim Yung-kil]]

Kim Yung-kil (born 29 January 1944) is a North Korean football defender who played for North Korea in the 1966 FIFA World Cup.Copa do Mundo da FIFA Inglaterra 1966 He also played for Rodongja Sports Team.
