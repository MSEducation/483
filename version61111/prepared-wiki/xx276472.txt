[[(48462) 1991 RT6]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1991, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(48462) 1991 RT6 is a main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on September 3, 1991.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
