[[Rufus Crawford]]

CATEGORIES: 1955 births, Living people, American players of Canadian football, American football return specialists, Canadian football running backs, Seattle Seahawks players, Hamilton Tiger-Cats players, Virginia State Trojans football players, People from Gastonia, North Carolina

Rufus Crawford  was a Canadian Football League running back who played for the Hamilton Tiger-Cats from 1979 through 1985. He broke the Canadian Football League yardage record which had stood for 28 years. He started his career in the NFL with the Seattle Seahawks.
