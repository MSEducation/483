[[A Fantasy Love Affair]]

CATEGORIES: 1978 debut albums, Boogie albums, Disco albums, Electronica albums, Peter Brown (singer) albums

A Fantasy Love Affair is the debut album by Peter Brown.[tpl]cite web|url=[tpl]Allmusic|class=album|id=r66278|pure_url=yes[/tpl]|title=A Fantasy Love Affair|publisher=Allmusic.com|accessdate=12 April 2011[/tpl] The album was recorded in 1976-77 and released in 1978. It charted #9 on Billboard's R&B charts and #11 on the Pop chart.  It was successful on the disco charts as well and spawned four singles, three of which were hits, including Do Ya Wanna Get Funky With Me and Dance With Me.
In the UK the album was titled "Do You Wanna Get Funky With Me" and had a backlit outline of a naked lady standing in a window as its sleeve cover.
The album was later reissued on Collectables Records.
