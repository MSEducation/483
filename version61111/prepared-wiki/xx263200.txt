[[Long Apu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Sarawak

Long Apu is a longhouse in the mountainous interior of the Marudi division of Sarawak, Malaysia. It lies approximately [tpl]convert|527.5|km|mi|0[/tpl] east-north-east of the state capital Kuching. 
The village is located on the Baram River and is known for white water rafting events. The people are from the Kenyah and Kayan tribes. If the Baram Dam hydroelectric project goes ahead, Long Apu will be one of the villages affected by the flooding of 389,000Â hectares of jungle.
