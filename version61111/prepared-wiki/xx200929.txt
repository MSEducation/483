[[Jindallae hwachae]]

CATEGORIES: Hwachae, Non-alcoholic beverages

Jindallae hwachae ([tpl]IPA-ko|tɕindallɛ hwatɕʰɛ[/tpl]) is a variety of hwachae, or Korean traditional fruit punch, made with azalea (Rhododendron mucronulatum)[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.encyber.com/plant/theme/gardening/detail.php?masterno=796092 |title=진달래, Korean rosebay, Rhododendron mucronulatum|publisher=Doosan Encyclopedia |accessdate=2008-05-21|language=Korean [/tpl]Rhododendron mucronulatum petals and mung bean starch. It is prepared for Samjinnal (삼짇날, a Korean traditional holiday which falls on every March 3 in the lunar calendar).[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.encyber.com/search_w/ctdetail.php?masterno=143428&contentno=143428 |title=Jindallae hwachae (진달래화채) |publisher=Doosan Encyclopedia |accessdate=2008-05-21|language=Korean [/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.foodinkorea.org/eng_food/korfood/korfood9_2.jsp |title=Kind of Eumcheongryu |work=Hwachae (Honeyed juice mixed with fruits) |accessdate=2008-05-21|publisher=Korea Agro-Fisheries Trade Corporation |language=English[/tpl]
