[[UTE]]

CATEGORIES: Power companies of Uruguay, Government-owned companies of Uruguay, Electric power companies, 1912 establishments in Uruguay, Companies established in 1912

UTE is the acronym of the National Administration of Power Plants and Electrical Transmissions ([tpl]lang-es|Administración Nacional de Usinas y Trasmisiones Eléctricas[/tpl]), Uruguay's government-owned power company. It was established in 1912.History of UTE [tpl]es[/tpl]
In 1912, the law 4273 created this public company as the unique distributor of electricity was approved.[tpl]cite book|url=http://www.ccee.edu.uy/ensenian/catderpu/material/entidades.PDF|title=State-owned companies|accessdate=3 Apr 2013|page=18[/tpl] [tpl]es[/tpl] Then, in 1931, the monopoly of communications was also granted to this company until the founding of ANTEL in 1974. Later, in 1980 an organic law was passed.Ley 15031 [tpl]es[/tpl]
