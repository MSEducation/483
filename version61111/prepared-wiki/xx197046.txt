[[Uma Dasgupta]]

CATEGORIES: Indian film actresses, Living people, Jogamaya Devi College alumni, University of Calcutta alumni, Actors in Bengali cinema, Satyajit Ray, People from Kolkata, 1930s births

Uma Dasgupta is an Indian actor who played the young Durga in Satyajit Ray's Pather Panchali (1955). 

==Pather Panchali==

At the time, she was 12 years old and studying in std 8 in Kolkata. Her name was recommended by the assistant headmaster of her school to Ashish Burman, who was a common friend with Ray.[tpl]cite web| title =Golden memories of 50 years ago|publisher= The Telegraph| url = http://www.telegraphindia.com/1050315/asp/calcutta/story_4492664.asp|date= March 15, 2005| accessdate = 2013-12-16[/tpl] Like Subir Banerjee, who played the lead of Apu Roy, this was to be the only film she would act in. 

==Later life==

She graduated from the Jogamaya Devi College, an affiliated undergraduate women's college of the historic University of Calcutta, in Kolkata.History of the College
