[[Philippe Ramos]]

CATEGORIES: 1964 births, Living people, French film directors

Philippe Ramos is a French film director. He was born in France in 1966. [tpl]Citation needed|date=August 2007[/tpl] He directed a very unusual adaptation of Moby-Dick: Capitaine Achab with Valérie Crunchant and Frédéric Bonpart in 2004.
Philippe Ramos is one of the French fllm directors in the new “New Wave” of French cinema such as Yves Caumon, Jean-Paul Civeyrac, and François Ozon.[tpl]Citation needed|date=August 2007[/tpl]. He directed the 2011 historical film The Silence of Joan.
