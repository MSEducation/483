[[A.E. Walsby]]

CATEGORIES: English biologists, Living people, Fellows of the Royal Society

Anthony Edward Walsby, BSc(Birm), PhD(Lond), FRS, is the Emeritus Professor of Microbiology at the School of Biological Sciences, University of Bristol.
He is a researcher in the fields of Algae, Cyanobacteria, lake ecology, gas vesicles/vacuoles and genetics, covering the European lakes and Baltic Sea."Details of Professor A. WALSBY" Ocean Expert, 2002-03-02 He is noted for his discovery of Haloquadratum walsbyi in brine ponds on the Sinai Peninsula in 1980.Nature  He was elected Fellow of the Royal Society, 11 March 1993.[tpl]cite news| url=http://www.independent.co.uk/news/people/royal-society-1497302.html| title=Royal Society| date=13 March 1993| work=The Independent| accessdate=July 28, 2010[/tpl]
