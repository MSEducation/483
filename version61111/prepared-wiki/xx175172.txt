[[WFBH-LP]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Alabama, Low-power FM radio stations in Alabama, Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 2002, Marion County, Alabama, Moody Radio affiliate stations, 2002 establishments in Alabama

WFBH-LP (96.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve Hamilton, Alabama.  The station is owned by First Baptist Church Hamilton. It airs a Christian radio format.[tpl]cite web |work=Alabama Broadcast Media Page |title=Alabama LPFM & Translators |url=http://bellsouthpwp2.net/c/r/crackedlcd/almediapage/lpfm.html |accessdate=2008-05-21[/tpl][tpl]cite web |title=Station Information Profile |work=Arbitron |url=http://www.arbitron.com/[/tpl] While largely locally originated, a portion of the station's programming is derived from the Moody Broadcasting Network.[tpl]cite web |work=Christian Community Broadcasters |url=http://www.ccbroadcasters.com/successful.htm |title=Successful Stations[/tpl]

==History==

This station was granted its original construction permit by the Federal Communications Commission on November 18, 2001.[tpl]cite web |title=Application Search Details (BNPL-20010613ADQ) |work=FCC Media Bureau |date=2002-11-18 |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/app_det.pl?Application_id=618212[/tpl] The new station was assigned the WFBH-LP call letters by the FCC on December 3, 2002.[tpl]cite web |title=Call Sign History |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=133868&Callsign=WFBH-LP |work=FCC Media Bureau CDBS Public Access Database[/tpl] WFBH-LP began regular broadcast operations on December 10, 2002, and received its license to cover on April 10, 2003.[tpl]cite web |title=Application Search Details (BLL-20021210ACG) |work=FCC Media Bureau |date=2003-04-10 |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/app_det.pl?Application_id=620420[/tpl]
Originally licensed to broadcast at 103.5 MHz, the station petitioned the FCC in August 2005 to change frequencies as its signal suffered interference from WKGL in Russellville, Alabama, and WMXV in Littleville, Alabama.[tpl]cite web |title=Application Search Details (BPL-20050816AAB) |work=FCC Media Bureau |date=2005-10-19 |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/app_det.pl?Application_id=1079967[/tpl] The FCC authorized the change in October 2005 and WFBH-LP began broadcasting at the current 96.7 MHZ in July 2006.[tpl]cite web |title=Application Search Details (BLL-20060724AAG) |work=FCC Media Bureau |date=2006-07-27 |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/app_det.pl?Application_id=1141798[/tpl]
