[[Wolf Prize in Agriculture]]

CATEGORIES: Agriculture awards, Agriculture in society, Wolf Prizes, Lists of Israeli award winners, Awards established in 1978, Israeli science and engineering awards, 1978 establishments in Israel

The Wolf Prize in Agriculture is awarded once a year by the Wolf Foundation in Israel. It is one of the six Wolf Prizes established by the Foundation and awarded since 1978; the others are in Chemistry, Mathematics, Medicine, Physics and the Arts.
