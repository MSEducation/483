[[2005 British motorcycle Grand Prix]]

CATEGORIES: 2005 in Grand Prix motorcycle racing, 2005 in British motorsport, MotoGP race reports, British motorcycle Grand Prix

The 2005 British motorcycle Grand Prix was the ninth round of the 2005 MotoGP Championship. It took place on the weekend of 22-24 July 2005 at the Donington Park circuit.
