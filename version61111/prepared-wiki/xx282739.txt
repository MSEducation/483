[[2013 Chick-fil-A Kickoff Game]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 NCAA Division I FBS football season, Chick-fil-A Kickoff Game, Alabama Crimson Tide football, Virginia Tech Hokies football

The 2013 Chick-fil-A Kickoff Game will feature the Alabama Crimson Tide, of the SEC and the Virginia Tech Hokies, of the ACC on August 31, 2013, at the Georgia Dome in Atlanta.
On July 7, 2011, officials from both Alabama and Virginia Tech announced the Crimson Tide and the Hokies would meet to open the 2013 season in the Chick-fil-A Kickoff Game.[tpl]cite news |title=Tide–Hokies in '13 Chick-fil-A Kickoff |agency=Associated Press |publisher=ESPN.com |url=http://sports.espn.go.com/ncf/news/story?id=6744536 |date=July 7, 2011 |accessdate=August 10, 2013[/tpl] The two teams met previously in the 2009 Chick-fil-A Kickoff Game game with Alabama winning 34–24. The match-up marked Alabama's third appearance in the game and Virginia Tech's second.
