[[Sfia Bouarfa]]

CATEGORIES: 1950 births, Living people, Socialist Party (francophone Belgium) politicians, Members of the Senate (Belgium), Belgian people of Moroccan descent, Members of the Parliament of the Brussels-Capital Region, Members of the Parliament of the French Community

Sfia Bouarfa ([tpl]lang-ar|صفية بوعرفة[/tpl]) born 14 October 1950 in Jerada, Morocco) is a Belgian politician and a member of the PS. Since 1995 she is a Member of the Parliament of the Brussels-Capital Region and a Member of the Parliament of the French Community. She was also a Community Senator appointed by the Parliament of the French Community since 16 May 2001.[tpl]cite web |title=Belgische Senaat |url=http://www.senate.be/www/?MIval=/index_senate&MENUID=11200&LANG=nl |publisher=Belgian Senate |language=Dutch |accessdate=10 June 2009[/tpl]
