[[Jacob Roll (born 1794)]]

CATEGORIES: 1794 births, 1857 deaths, Members of the Parliament of Norway, Østfold politicians

Jacob Roll (10 January 1794 – 23 November 1857) was a Norwegian politician.
He was elected to the Parliament of Norway in 1839, 1842, 1845, 1848 and 1851, representing the rural constituency of Smaalenenes Amt. He was also a deputy representative in 1836.
