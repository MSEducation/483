[[1997 Internazionali Femminili di Palermo]]

CATEGORIES: 1997 WTA Tour, Internazionali Femminili di Palermo

The 1997 Internazionali Femminili di Palermo was a women's tennis tournament played on outdoor clay courts in Palermo in Italy that was part of Tier IV of the 1997 WTA Tour. It was the tenth edition of the tournament and was held from July 14 through July 20, 1997. Sandrine Testud won the singles title.
