Marysa Baradji-Duchêne (born 17 October 1982) is a French épée fencer.[tpl]cite web|title=Marysa BARADJI-DUCHENE|url=http://www.fie.ch/Competitions/FencerDetail.aspx?param=1D96413473579E9FCC3E5AD4691FD407|publisher=Federation Internationale D'Escrime|accessdate=6 January 2011[/tpl]
Baradji-Duchêne won the silver medal in the épée team event at the 2006 World Fencing Championships after losing to China in the final. She accomplished this with her team mates Hajnalka Kiraly Picot, Maureen Nisima and Laura Flessel-Colovic.
