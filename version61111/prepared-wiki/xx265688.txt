[[Aceto (album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2010 albums

Aceto is the third studio album from Italian-born Canadian tenor, Micheal Castaldo. It was released on June 15, 2010. The album, includes 14 classic and 2 original Italian songs.http://www.michealcastaldo.com/music.htmlhttp://www.amazon.com/Aceto-Micheal-Castaldo/dp/B003QI95U4/ref=sr_1_3?ie=UTF8&s=music&qid=1276651547&sr=1-3 He recorded an Italian version of Hallelujah on album. A video of the song was filmed in Toronto, Canada in December 2010 by filmmaker Greg Riccio Jr. supporting the Free Hugs Campaign to promote random acts of kindness.http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=L7UK4PIfZV0
