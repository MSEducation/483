[[Foto Showdown]]

CATEGORIES: 2009 video games, Action role-playing video games, Nintendo DS games, Nintendo DS-only games, Nintendo DSi games, Konami games, Turn-based strategy video games

Foto Showdown, known in Japan as [tpl]nihongo|Monster Finder|モンスターファインダ[/tpl],[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.siliconera.com/2009/11/15/monster-finder-renamed-foto-showdown-for-north-america/|title=Monster Finder Renamed Foto Showdown For North America|first=Ishaan|date=November 15, 2009|accessdate=11 January 2010[/tpl] is a turn-based action role-playing video game made exclusively for the Nintendo DSi. It was released in Japan on November 19, 2009 and in North America on March 9, 2010.[tpl]cite web|url=http://ds.ign.com/objects/053/053162.html|title=IGN: Foto Showdown|publisher=IGN|accessdate=11 January 2010[/tpl] The game revolves around players using their monsters to battle against rivals. By winning tournaments and battles, players can advance through the game through upgrades and unlocking more powerful items. The game allows player to take advantage of the DSi's cameras by allowing them to spawn certain monsters for use in battle based on the dominant colors in the photo.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.pocketgamer.co.uk/r/DSi/Foto+Showdown/news.asp?c=17346|title=Konami preps creature-battling DSi RPG Foto Showdown|last=Jordon|first=Jon|publisher=Pocket Gamer|accessdate=11 January 2010[/tpl]
