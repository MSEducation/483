[[Tech Town (Dayton)]]

CATEGORIES: Geography of Dayton, Ohio, Buildings and structures in Dayton, Ohio

Tech Town is a [tpl]convert|40|acre|sing=on[/tpl] district developed in downtown Dayton, Ohio, that is located near Fifth Third Field. Tech Town was one of ten "brownfields" in the Dayton area, and is designed to attract technology-based firms to Dayton and revitalize the downtown area. Tech Town's first major lab was opened in September 2009. Tech Town will be a mixed-use development to promote the creation of a mixed use neighborhood (single use campus) that supports/leverages the functions of daily life: employment, recreation, retail, civic and educational opportunities. Tech Town will encompass approximately [tpl]convert|400000|sqft|m2[/tpl] of office and research space and accommodate up to 2,500 jobs once completed.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.daytontechtown.com/|title= Tech Town Introduction Page|accessdate=2009-12-03[/tpl]
