[[Kangarlu, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Kangarlu ([tpl]lang-fa|كنگرلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Kangarlū)[tpl]IranNCSGN[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 198, in 38 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
