[[Michelle Font]]

CATEGORIES: 1982 births, Living people, Miss USA 2008 delegates, People from Renton, Washington, American people of Cuban descent, American people of Puerto Rican descent, University of Washington alumni

Michelle Font (born in Renton, Washington) is a beauty queen who won Miss Washington USA and competed in the Miss USA pageant on April 11, 2008 at Planet Hollywood in Las Vegas, Nevada.  She did not make the top 15.
Michelle is of Cuban and Puerto Rican descent.  She attended and graduated from Kentridge High School in Kent, Washington where she was a part of band and the school dance team while enrolled in AP Calculus. She later graduated from the University of Washington and is currently employed as a research engineer for The Boeing Company in Everett, WA.

==Pageant History==

Michelle won the Miss Washington USA title on November 11, 2007.  This was her second attempt, previously earning a 3rd Runner-Up ranking for the state title.  Michelle has also held the titles of International Queen of the Pacific 2004, as well as Miss Washington Belleza Latina 2005.
