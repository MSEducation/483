[[Lord Lieutenant of Cromarty]]

CATEGORIES: Ross and Cromarty, Lord Lieutenancies of Scotland, Lord-Lieutenants of Cromarty, Lists of Scottish people

This is a list of Lord Lieutenants of Cromartyshire, Scotland.
The office was replaced by the Lord Lieutenant of Ross and Cromarty in 1891 through the operation of the Local Government (Scotland) Act 1889.
