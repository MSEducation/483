Alexander Yunkov ([tpl]lang-ru|Александр Юньков[/tpl]; born November 21, 1982) is a Russian ice hockey forward who is currently playing for Amur Khabarovsk team.

==Privates==

He is the brother of Mikhail Yunkov, who is also a professional ice hockey player, for HC Spartak Moscow.
