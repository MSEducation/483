[[Bizin]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Bizin ([tpl]lang-fa|بيزين[/tpl], also Romanized as Bīzīn) is a village in Golabar Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
