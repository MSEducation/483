William Etchu Tabi (born November 13, 1982)The African Champions League 2000 is a Cameroonian footballer, who is a Player-coach for CSL club Burlington SC.Burlington's new CSL team still waiting to play first game

==Career==

He started his career in Sable FC, then he made a  transfer to San Lorenzo. After one season in Turkey, in Samsunspor, he came to Bosnia and Herzegovina.
