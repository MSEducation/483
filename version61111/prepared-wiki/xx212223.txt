[[Baguio City Federation of the Sangguniang Kabataan]]

CATEGORIES: Baguio, Youth councils, Katipunan ng Kabataan, Local government in the Philippines

Please note that elections of new officers has been suspended until February 2015.
Philippine President Benigno S. Aquino III signed Republic Act 10632 deferring Sangguniang Kabataan Elections.
Sangguniang Kabataan Baguio City Federation is a special youth legislature that inititates programs, policies, and projects for youth development. It is composed of 124 members as SK Chairmen of their respective barangays.

==Official Website Launch==

The official portal of Sangguniang Kabataan Baguio Federation was launched on February 2010 in time with Panagbenga Festival opening. The web address is http://www.skbaguio.com
