[[Diana Janošťáková]]

CATEGORIES: Czech female ice dancers, 1984 births, Living people, Sportspeople from Bratislava

Diana Janošťáková (born 29 September 1984) is a former ice dancer. She represented the Czech Republic with Jiří Procházka, with whom she is the 2005 Czech champion.
