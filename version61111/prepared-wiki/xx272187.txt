[[Oleg Mityaev]]

CATEGORIES: Russian bards, 1956 births, Living people, People's Artists of Russia, People from Chelyabinsk

Oleg Mityaev ([tpl]Lang-ru|Олег Григорьевич Митяев[/tpl], 19 February 1956) is a Russian bard, musician and actor.
People's Artist of Russia (2009).[tpl]cite web |url=http://document.kremlin.ru/doc.asp?ID=53059&Page=2 |title=УКАЗ Президента РФ от 22.06.2009 N 698 |trans_title=Decree of the President of the Russian Federation No. 698 |date=22 June 2009 |author=Dmitry Medvedev |publisher=Presidential Administration of Russia |accessdate=17 October 2011 [/tpl] Member of Russian Writers Union.
An author and first performer of popular bard song "Как здорово, что все мы здесь сегодня собрались" (1978).
