[[Nymphaea odorata subsp. tuberosa]]

CATEGORIES: Nymphaea, Plant subspecies, Flora of North America

Nymphaea odorata'' subsp. ''tuberosa (syn. Castalia tuberosa (Paine) Greene, Nymphaea tuberosa Paine) is a subspecies of Nymphaea odorata that is found in the eastern United States. It has a round leaf.[tpl]cite web|title=Nymphaea odorata|url=http://wisplants.uwsp.edu/scripts/detail.asp?SpCode=NYMODOsTUB|work=Robert W.
