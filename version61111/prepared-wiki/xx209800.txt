[[World Heroes Anthology]]

CATEGORIES: 2007 video games, SNK games, Versus fighting games, Neo-Geo games, SNK Playmore games, PlayStation 2-only games, PlayStation 2 games, Video game compilations

World Heroes Anthology, known in Japan as [tpl]nihongo|World Heroes Gorgeous|ワールドヒーローズ ゴージャス[/tpl], is a game compilation, which includes all four games from ADK's World Heroes series, which were a part of the early Neo Geo titles from SNK. The four games presented here World Heroes, World Heroes 2, World Heroes 2 Jet, and World Heroes Perfect. The compilation was released for the PlayStation 2 on March 11, 2008. Official website of ''World Heroes Anthology.

==Story==

The main storyline is based around Dr. Brown who has created a time machine able to bring together fighters from various times in history, with the goal of determining the greatest fighter of all time. As the games focus on different time frames throughout history, most of its characters are based on real people.
