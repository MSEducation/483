[[Alona Regional Council]]

CATEGORIES: Alona Regional Council, Regional councils in Israel, Regional councils in Haifa District

Alona Regional Council ([tpl]lang-he|מועצה אזורית אלונה[/tpl], Mo'atza Azorit Alona) is a regional council in northern Israel. It is part of Haifa District and covers three moshavim, Amikam, Aviel and Givat Nili. The council's headquarters are located in Amikam.
