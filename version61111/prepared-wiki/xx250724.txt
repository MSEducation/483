[[Jeremy Rossiter]]

CATEGORIES: 1950 births, Living people, British Touring Car Championship drivers, English racing drivers, 24 Hours of Le Mans drivers, Place of birth missing (living people), World Sportscar Championship drivers

Jeremy Rossiter (born 18 June 1950) is a British auto racing driver. He has competed in various forms of motorsports including single-seater, sports cars and saloon cars. In 1989 he drove for the works Vauxhall team in the British Touring Car Championship.Official 1989 BTCC standings
His eldest son James is a racing driver who has been a Formula One test driver.
