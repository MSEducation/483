Carlos Francisco Jovel Navas  (Born January 6, 1982 in Usulután, El Salvador) is a Salvadoran professional football player who currently plays for Luis Ángel Firpo in the Salvadoran Premier Division.
He is the son of former World Cup player José Francisco Jovel.

==Club career==

Jovel came through the reserves at hometown club Firpo, but made his senior debut with Atlético Chaparratique in the Second division in 2001. He then had a spell at Dragón before joining premier division side Vista Hermosa in 2005.Profile El Gráfico [tpl]es icon[/tpl] He returned to Firpo in 2010.
