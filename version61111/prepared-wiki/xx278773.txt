[[Vananaq]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Vananaq ([tpl]lang-fa|وننق[/tpl]; also known as Vanana and Vanehnīq)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3088094|Vananaq[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 781, in 179 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
