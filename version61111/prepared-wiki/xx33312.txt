Hanga ([tpl]lang-mr|हंगा[/tpl]), is a village in Parner taluka in Ahmednagar district of state of Maharashtra, it is popular for temple of lord Shiva namely as Hangeshwar. It is situated at the bank of Hanga river. In the August Hindu month Shravan every Monday in hangeshwar temple round shape pindi of rice are become. for details contact to Devendra Kulkarni India.Villages in Parner taluka-Glorious India

==Religion==

The majority of the population in the village is Hindu.

==Economy==

The majority of the population has farming as their primary occupation.
