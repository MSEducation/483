[[Adam Marjan]]

Adam Ahmed Marjan (born 23 September 1957) is a Kuwaiti football goalkeeper who played for Kuwait in the 1982 FIFA World Cup.1982 FIFA World Cup Spain He also played for Kazma Sporting Club.
