[[A Woman in the Web]]

CATEGORIES: 1918 films, American films, American silent serial films, 1910s drama films, Lost films, Vitagraph Studios film serials, Black-and-white films, Films directed by David Smith (director)

A Woman in the Web is a 1918 American drama film serial directed by Paul Hurst and David Smith.  It was the 9th of 17 serials released by The Vitagraph Company of America.  This World War I period serial about a Russian princess and the overthrow of the Tsar introduced the concept of the Red Menace to serials.[ref]
