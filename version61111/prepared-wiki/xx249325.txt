[[Could You Be the One? (Stereophonics song)]]

CATEGORIES: 2010 singles, Stereophonics songs, Songs written by Kelly Jones

"Could You Be the One?" is a single by British rock band Stereophonics which was released on 15th February 2010. It is the second single taken from their seventh studio album, Keep Calm and Carry On. It failed to chart in the UK, their first single to do so.
Talking about the track, singer Kelly Jones says: "I knew there was something in the melody. I was trying to keep it beautiful rather than piling in for a big chorus.".[tpl]cite web | author= | title=Stereophonics To Release 'Could You Be The One'| work=Ultimate-Guitar.Com| url=http://www.ultimate-guitar.com/news/upcoming_releases/stereophonics_to_release_could_you_be_the_one.html| accessdate=24 January 2010[/tpl]
