[[Kalhatti Falls, Ooty]]

CATEGORIES: Visitor attractions in Nilgiris district

Kalhatti Falls is a tourist spot near Ooty, The Nilgiris, Tamil Nadu. These falls are about 13 km from Ooty, on the Ooty to Mysore Road or Sigur Ghat Road.
