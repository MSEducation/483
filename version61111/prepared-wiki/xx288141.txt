[[Khakriz, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Khakriz ([tpl]lang-fa|خاكريز[/tpl], also Romanized as Khākrīz and Khāk Rīz; also known as Khakir and Khākraz)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3070369|Khakriz[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Bala Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 162, in 41 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
