[[Rajesh Shah]]

CATEGORIES: Indian homeopaths, 1963 births, Living people

Rajesh Shah is a practicing homeopath in Mumbai, India. He graduated from CMPH Medical College, Mumbai, in 1984 and is mentioned in Limca Book of Records, where he holds the world record for having patients in the most countries.
He has been known as a promoter of homeopathy. Using the internet as a medium, he has launched over twenty web sites which talk about the role, scope and limitation of homeopathic treatment for various diseases.Homeopathic treatment just a click away, Economic Times, 2008
He is involved in conducting clinical trials using conventional research methods, including one for Hep C and HIV.
http://www.business-standard.com/india/storypage.php?autono=378486 Homeopathic institution conducts HIV study, Business Standards, December, 2009 
He has researched on and introduced a new Hepatitis C nosode.http://www.homeopathyjournal.net/article/S1475-4916(13)00007-6/abstract Hepatitis C nosode.  He also conducted a Homeopathic Pathogenetic trial of Hepatitis C nosode on healthy human volunteers. He conducted a homeopathic pathogenetic trial of Hydroquinone.http://www.ijrh.org/article.asp?issn=0974-7168;year=2013;volume=7;issue=2;spage=47;epage=61;aulast=Shah Hydroquinone.
Dr Shah has made a critical review on the current myths and facts about homeopathy in an article called 'What homeopathy is not', published in Homeopathic Links, where some points related to misinterpretation about various facets of homeopathy, by the homeopaths, are raised and discussed.
