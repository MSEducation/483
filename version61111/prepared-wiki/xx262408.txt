Paul John "P. J." Fenton (born August 26, 1985) is an American professional ice hockey forward who currently plays for the Rochester Americans of the American Hockey League (AHL). Drafted by the San Jose Sharks, 162nd overall in the 2005 NHL Entry Draft, Fenton initially joined the DEG Metro Stars of the Deutsche Eishockey Liga (DEL) to start the 2010-11 season on April 14, 2010.[tpl]cite web| url = http://www.deg-metro-stars.de/servlet/PB/menu/1015590/index.html | title = DEG Metro Stars undertake P.J. Fenton | publisher = DEG Metro Stars | date = 2010-04-14 | accessdate = 2011-03-13[/tpl] After posting 4 points in 19 games with the Stars, Fenton was released and opted to return to the AHL with the Rochester Americans on November 15, 2010.[tpl]cite web| url = http://amerks.com/news/press/index.html?article_id=876 | title = Panthers sign Fenton to AHL SPC | publisher = Rochester Americans | date = 2010-11-15 | accessdate = 2011-03-13[/tpl]
