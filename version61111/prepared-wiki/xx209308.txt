[[Argonne Township, Adams County, North Dakota]]

CATEGORIES: Defunct townships in North Dakota, Defunct townships in Adams County, North Dakota

Argonne Township is a defunct civil township in Adams County, North Dakota, USA. The 1960 census recorded a population of 90.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www2.census.gov/prod2/decennial/documents/37749301v.1p36ch2.pdf | title =  Census of Population: 1960, North Dakota | format = PDF | publisher = United States Census Bureau | date = 1961 | accessdate = 2008-07-20[/tpl]
The township dissolved prior to the 1980 Census,[tpl]cite web | url = http://www2.census.gov/prod2/decennial/documents/1980a_ndABCD-01.pdf | title =  1980 Census of Population: North Dakota | format = PDF | publisher = United States Census Bureau | date = 1981 | accessdate = 2008-07-20[/tpl] when it was combined with Holt Township to form the Census-designated Central Adams Unorganized Territory. As of the 2000 Census, the combined area had a population of 64.[tpl]cite American Factfinder3|UT|3800113270|general|Central Adams Unorganized Territory[/tpl]
