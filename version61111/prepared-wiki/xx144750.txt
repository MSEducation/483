[[Popovača]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Croatia, Populated places in Sisak-Moslavina County

Popovača is a town in Croatia in the Moslavina geographical region. Administratively it is part of the Sisak-Moslavina County. The town has a population of 11,905 (2011 census), 96% of which are ethnic Croats.
