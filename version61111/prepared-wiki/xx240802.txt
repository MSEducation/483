CATEGORIES: 1988 births, Living people, French footballers, SC Bastia players, Vannes OC players, Ligue 2 players

Christophe Gaffory (born May 10, 1988 in Bastia) is a French professional football player. He currently plays for Vannes OC.

==International  Career==

He represented Corsica in a game against Congo.
