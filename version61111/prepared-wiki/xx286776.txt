[[Bahman, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Bahman ([tpl]lang-fa|بهمن[/tpl]; also known as Bakhman and Behmān)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3054769|Bahman[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 128, in 33 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
