[[Ed Lindsey]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Members of the Georgia House of Representatives, Georgia (U.S. state) Republicans, Davidson College alumni, University of Georgia School of Law alumni

Edward "Ed" Lindsey is an American politician from the state of Georgia. He is a member of Georgia House of Representatives, representing the 54th district, and the Republican Party. Lindsey serves as the House Majority Whip.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cedartownstd.com/view/full_story/10739918/article-Ga--GOP-wants-to-limit-property-tax-increases?instance=home_news_lead_story |title=The Fish Wrap - Ga GOP wants to limit property tax increases |publisher=Cedartownstd.com |date=December 21, 2010 |accessdate=October 8, 2013[/tpl] Lindsey is running for the United States House of Representatives in [tpl]ushr|GA|11[/tpl], as Phil Gingrey is not running for reelection.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.mdjonline.com/view/full_story/23120891/article-Around-Town--Georgia-s-elections--July-Fourth-parades-to-have-new-look-in--14?instance=special_coverage_bullets_right_column |title=The Marietta Daily Journal - Around Town Georgia s elections July Fourth parades to have new look in 14 |publisher=Mdjonline.com |date= |accessdate=October 8, 2013[/tpl]
Lindsey is a native Georgian. He graduated from Northside High School in Warner Robins, Georgia. He earned a degree in History from Davidson College, and a Juris Doctorate from the University of Georgia School of Law. Lindsey and three partners started their own firm, Goodman McGuffey Lindsey & Johnson, LLP, in 1990.[tpl]cite web|url=http://mdjonline.com/view/full_story/22214755/article-Ga--Majority-Whip-to-face-Barr-for-U-S--House-seat?instance=special%20_coverage_right_column |title=The Marietta Daily Journal - Ga Majority Whip to face Barr for U S House seat |publisher=Mdjonline.com |date= |accessdate=October 8, 2013[/tpl]
