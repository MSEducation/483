[[The Rising Anger]]

CATEGORIES: 2006 albums

The Rising Anger is an album by Katagory V, released in 2006.Allmusic link

==Track listing==

All songs written by Dustin Mitchell and Curtis Morrell, except where noted.

==Line-up==

==Technical information==

The album was recorded and mixed at Streamline Sound Studios, in Salt Lake City. It was produced by Mike Foweks and the band itself, Curtis Morell also mixed the album. The Artwork was done by Rainer Kalwitz.
