[[Sandro Iashvili]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 births, Living people, Footballers from Georgia (country), Georgian expatriate footballers, Georgia (country) international footballers, FC Dinamo Tbilisi players, FC Thun players, FC Anzhi Makhachkala players, Expatriate footballers in Switzerland, Expatriate footballers in Russia

Sandro Iashvili ([tpl]lang-ka|სანდრო იაშვილი[/tpl]) (born 3 January 1985) is a Georgian football striker. As of March 2012, he plays for FC Chikhura Sachkhere.
He is the brother of Giga Iashvili, also a footballer.
