[[Bayswater railway station, Melbourne]]

CATEGORIES: Premium Melbourne railway stations, Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1889

Bayswater railway station is located on the Belgrave line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the eastern Melbourne suburb of Bayswater opening on 4 December 1889.Bayswater Vicsig
The current island platform opened on 19 December 1982, as part of the duplication of the line between Bayswater and Ringwood stations.VR History by Andrew Waugh It was once the destination for freight trains conveying cement, the last train running on 24 June 1987 when the traffic was relocated to Lyndhurst station, near Dandenong.[tpl]cite journal|date=September 1987|title=Traffic|journal=Newsrail|publisher=Australian Railway Historical Society|pages=page 280[/tpl] Bayswater was upgraded to a Premium station in 2001.
In 1998 a train maintenance and stabling facilities opened as part of the replacement of Jolimont Yard. The buildings are approximately 2,850 mÂ² in size, and permit bogie repair and replacement, under carriage and overhead work.
