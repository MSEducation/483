[[Volcanic glass]]

CATEGORIES: Volcanology, Glass in nature, Volcanic rocks

Volcanic glass is the amorphous (uncrystallized) product of rapidly cooling magma.  Like all types of glass, it is a state of matter intermediate between the close-packed, highly ordered array of a crystal and the highly disordered array of gas.Bates and Jackson, 1984, Dictionary of Geological Terms, 3rd ed., Prepared by the American Geological Institute Volcanic glass can refer to the interstitial, or matrix material in an aphanitic (fine grained) volcanic rock or can refer to any of several types of vitreous igneous rocks.  Most commonly, it refers to obsidian, a rhyolitic glass with high silica content.
