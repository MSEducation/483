[[Bierbeek]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Belgium, Municipalities of Flemish Brabant

thumb|left|200pxBierbeek ([tpl]IPA-nl|ˈbiːrbeːk[/tpl]) is a municipality located in the Belgian province of Flemish Brabant. The municipality comprises the towns of Bierbeek proper, Korbeek-Lo, Lovenjoel and Opvelp. On January 1, 2006 Bierbeek had a total population of 9,147. The total area is 39.73 km² which gives a population density of 230 inhabitants per km².
