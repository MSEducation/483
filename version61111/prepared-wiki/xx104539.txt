[[Silver (Moist album)]]

CATEGORIES: 1994 debut albums, Moist albums

Silver is the debut studio album album from Canadian alternative rock band Moist, released in 1994. It included the hit singles "Push", "Silver" and "Believe Me".
"Push", the band's first single, was supported by a music video that rapidly climbed the MuchMusic charts.
