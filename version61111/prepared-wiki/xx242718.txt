[[2006 Houston Cougars football team]]

CATEGORIES: 2006 Conference USA football season, Houston Cougars football seasons, Conference USA football champion seasons

The 2006 Houston Cougars football team, also known as the Houston Cougars, Houston, or UH represented the University of Houston in the 2006 NCAA Division I FBS football season.  It was the 61st year of season play for Houston. The team was coached by fourth year head football coach, Art Briles.  The team played its home games at Robertson Stadium, a 32,000-person capacity stadium on-campus in Houston.
