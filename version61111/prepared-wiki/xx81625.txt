[[Kabardin Autonomous Soviet Socialist Republic]]

CATEGORIES: Autonomous republics of the Soviet Union, Kabardino-Balkaria, States and territories established in 1944

Kabardin Autonomous Soviet Socialist Republic (Kabardin ASSR), ([tpl]lang-ru|Кабардинская Автономная Советская Социалистическая республика[/tpl]) was the name given to Kabardino-Balkar ASSR after deportation of the Balkars. In 1957 the old name was restored.
