[[Vladislav Bykov]]

CATEGORIES: 1994 births, Living people, Russian footballers

Vladislav Yuryevich Bykov ([tpl]lang-ru|Владислав Юрьевич Быков[/tpl]; born 17 March 1994) is a Russian football defender, who currently playing for FC Biolog-Novokubansk Progress.
He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Biolog-Novokubansk Progress on 12 July 2013 in a game against FC Olimpia Volgograd.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Russian Football Union|url=http://rfspro.ru/cgi-bin/player/script2.cgi?par1=119120|title=Career Summary|date=10 August 2013[/tpl]
