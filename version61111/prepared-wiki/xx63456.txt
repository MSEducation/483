Portia Lee James DeGeneres (born Amanda Lee Rogers; 31 January 1973), known professionally as Portia de Rossi [tpl]IPAc-en|ˈ|p|ɔər|ʃ|ə|_|d|ə|_|ˈ|r|ɒ|s|i[/tpl], is an Australian-American actress, model and philanthropist, known for her roles as lawyer Nelle Porter on the television series Ally McBeal and Lindsay Fünke on the sitcom Arrested Development.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.usmagazine.com/celebrity-bios/portia-de-rossi|accessdate=28 January 2009|title=Celebrity Bios: Portia de Rossi|publisher=Us Magazine[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Geelong's Portia Marries Ellen|url=http://www.geelongadvertiser.com.au/article/2008/08/18/17323_news.html|accessdate=28 January 2009|publisher=Geelong Advertiser|date=18 August 2008[/tpl] She also portrayed Veronica Palmer on the ABC sitcom Better Off Ted and Olivia Lord on Nip/Tuck. She is married to comedian Ellen DeGeneres.

==Early life==

De Rossi, born Amanda Lee Rogers in Horsham, Victoria, Australia,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.media-courses.com/blog/australian-actress-portia-de-rossi|title=Australian Actress: Portia de Rossi|author=London Academy of Media and Film TV|accessdate=24 July 2012[/tpl] is the daughter of Margaret, a medical receptionist, and Barry Rogers.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.filmreference.com/film/91/Portia-de-Rossi.html|title=Portia de Rossi profile|publisher=Filmreference.com|accessdate=12 July 2010[/tpl] Her father died when she was nine.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CTw1MoscAk4&feature=related|title=Portia de Rossi on Ellen|work=Youtube|accessdate=11 December 2011[/tpl] She grew up in Grovedale, a suburb of Geelong, Victoria, and modelled for print and TV commercials as a child. In 1988, at the age of 15, Rogers adopted the name Portia de Rossi, by which she remains best known; in 2005, she explained that she had intended to reinvent herself, using the given name of Portia, a character from William Shakespeare's The Merchant of Venice, and an Italian last name.[tpl]cite web|url=http://replay.waybackmachine.org/20090223013952/http://www.advocate.com/issue_story_ektid20037.asp|title=Portia heart and soul|author=Michele Kort|date=13 September 2005|publisher=The Advocate[/tpl] She has admitted, in an interview she gave to the London Daily Mail, to having struggled with anorexia throughout her teens and young adulthood, from which she credits the love of Ellen DeGeneres for having saved her.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/tvshowbiz/article-1325082/Portia-Rossis-Oprah-talking-shocking-details-anorexia-Ellens-love-saved-her.html |title=Portia de Rossi's Oprah cry talking about the shocking details of her anorexia and how Ellen's love saved her | Mail Online |publisher=Dailymail.co.uk |date=2010-10-31 |accessdate=2014-02-08 |location=London[/tpl]

==Career==

Her first significant role was playing a young and impressionable model in the Australian 1994 film Sirens. Soon afterwards she moved to Los Angeles, California, United States and had guest roles on several TV shows, and a permanent role in Nick Freno: Licensed Teacher, before landing a role in the film Scream 2. During this time in the United States, de Rossi worked diligently to replace her native Australian accent with her current General American one.[tpl]Cite news|url=http://www.theage.com.au/articles/2003/05/07/1052280317160.html|title=The Very Private Portia|work=The Age |location=Melbourne|date=10 May 2010[/tpl]
She attracted international attention when she joined the main cast of Ally McBeal in 1998, playing lawyer Nelle Porter. She remained with the show until its end in 2002. In 2001, she starred in Who Is Cletis Tout? with Christian Slater.
From 2003–2006, de Rossi starred as Lindsay Bluth Fünke on Fox Television's critically acclaimed, Emmy-winning series Arrested Development.
She also portrayed John F. Kennedy, Jr.'s wife, Carolyn Bessette-Kennedy, in the made for TV movie America's Prince: The John F. Kennedy Jr. Story in 2003. In 2005 she portrayed a fortune-teller named Zela in the Wes Craven thriller Cursed. From 2007–2008, de Rossi appeared in Nip/Tuck's fifth season as Julia McNamara's girlfriend Olivia Lord.
In 2009 and 2010, de Rossi played the high-strung and controlling Veronica Palmer on the ABC show Better Off Ted. In 2011 she appeared in Better Off Ted cast member Malcolm Barrett's music video for "Revenge of the Nerds", spoofing her character Veronica in a cameo (alongside other cast members of the cancelled series).[tpl]cite web| title =Better Off Ted cast reunites for Malcolm Barrett's hip-hop video| publisher =Zap2It.com| url =http://blog.zap2it.com/frominsidethebox/2011/07/better-off-ted-cast-reunites-for-malcolm-barretts-hip-hop-video.html| accessdate =16 November 2011[/tpl]
She ranked 69th in Stuff's 100 Sexiest Women, 31 in Femme Fatales' Sexiest Women of 2003 list, 24 in Maxim's 100 Sexiest Women List in 2004, and in late 2006, the magazine Blender listed her as one of the hottest women of film and TV.[tpl]cite web|title=Hottest Women of...Film and TV!|date=02/01/2007|first=Mike|last=Errico|url=http://www.blender.com/guide/articles.aspx?ID=2464&src=blender_ed[/tpl] In May 2007, she was featured as one of 100 Most Beautiful in a People magazine special edition. TV Guide included her and Ellen DeGeneres in their Power A-List couples in 2007.
In February 2012, it was announced that ABC had ordered a pilot for a new drama series The Smart One, executive produced by Ellen DeGeneres, and featured de Rossi in a leading role. The actress was to star as a "brilliant and successful woman who begrudgingly goes to work for her less-brainy but more popular sister – a former beauty queen, weather girl and now big-city Mayor."[tpl]cite web|url=http://spoilerville.unrealitytv.com/abc-to-air-ellen-degeneres-and-portia-de-rossis-the-smart-one/ |title=ABC to air Ellen DeGeneres and Portia de Rossi's "The Smart One" |publisher=Unreality TV |date=6 February 2012 |accessdate=6 February 2012[/tpl] The show was not picked up to series for the 2012-2013 season.
Six years after the series was canceled by Fox, filming for a revived fourth season of Arrested Development began on 7 August 2012 and de Rossi reprised her role as Lindsay Bluth Fünke. The season consists of 15 new episodes which debuted at the same time on Netflix on 26 May 2013. Each episode focuses on one particular character, with de Rossi's Lindsay, featured in episode 3, "Indian Takers" and episode 8, "Red Hairing", and also appearing in several other episodes of the season.

==Personal life==

De Rossi was married to documentary film-maker Mel Metcalfe from 1996 to 1999, initially part of a plan to get a green card, but she did not go through with it. She said that "it just obviously wasn't right for me". In a 2010 interview on Good Morning America, she explained that as a young actress, she was fearful of being exposed as a lesbian.[tpl]cite web|url=http://abcnews.go.com/Entertainment/portia-de-rossi-reveals-depths-eating-disorders-book/story?id=12033594|title=Portia de Rossi: 'I Would Starve Myself Daily'|date=3 November 2010|publisher=ABC News|author=Olivia Katrandjian|accessdate=3 November 2010[/tpl]
From 2000 to 2004, de Rossi dated singer Francesca Gregorini, the daughter of Barbara Bach and the stepdaughter of Ringo Starr. She said that most of her family and Ally McBeal castmates did not know she was a lesbian until tabloid pictures of the couple were published. She declined to publicly discuss the relationship or her sexual orientation at the time.
De Rossi and Gregorini broke up in late 2004 because de Rossi began dating Ellen DeGeneres, whom she met backstage at an awards show.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.vogue.com|title= Disappearing Act: How did Portia de Rossi withstand the pressures of Hollywood.|accessdate=22 March 2009|last=Van Meter|first=Jonathan|publisher=Vogue|archiveurl=http://www.accessmylibrary.com/coms2/summary_0286-15043182_ITM|archivedate= 1 April 2006[/tpl] In 2005, she opened up publicly about her sexual orientation in interviews with Details and The Advocate. She and DeGeneres became engaged when DeGeneres proposed with a three-carat pink diamond ring.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.usmagazine.com/celebrity-news/news/ellen-degeneres-and-portia-de-rossis-road-to-romance-2012261 |title=Ellen DeGeneres and Portia de Rossi's Road to Romance |date=26 January 2012 |first=Zach |last=Johnson |work=Us Weekly[/tpl] They were married at their Beverly Hills home on 16 August 2008, witnessed by their mothers and 17 other guests.[tpl]Cite news|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/newstopics/celebritynews/2580530/Television-presenter-Ellen-DeGeneres-marries-lesbian-lover-Portia-de-Rossi.html|work=The Daily Telegraph|location=London|title=Television presenter Ellen DeGeneres marries lesbian lover Portia de Rossi|first=Anita|last=Singh|date=18 August 2008|accessdate=22 May 2010[/tpl] On 6 August 2010, Portia filed a petition to legally change her name to Portia Lee James DeGeneres.[tpl]cite web|last=TMZ Staff|title=Portia to Ellen: I Want to Be a DeGeneres!|url=http://www.tmz.com/2010/08/09/ellen-degeneres-portia-de-rossi-name-change-petition-married-gay-marriage|work=TMZ|accessdate=11 August 2010[/tpl] The petition was granted on 23 September 2010.[tpl]Cite news|title=Portia de Rossi takes wife Ellen DeGeneres' name|url=http://www.usatoday.com/life/people/2010-09-23-derossi-name-change_N.htm | work=USA Today | date=23 September 2010[/tpl] She became a US citizen in September 2011.[tpl]cite web|author= |url=http://ellen.warnerbros.com/2011/09/portia_becomes_a_us_citizen_0915.php |title=Portia Becomes a U.S. Citizen! | EllenTV.com |publisher=Ellen.warnerbros.com |date=2011-09-20 |accessdate=2014-02-08[/tpl]
In 2010, de Rossi published the autobiography Unbearable Lightness which talks about the turmoil that she has experienced in her life, including suffering from anorexia nervosa and bulimia and being misdiagnosed with lupus.[tpl]cite book|last=de Rossi|first=Portia|title=Unbearable Lightness: A Story of Loss and Gain|publisher=Atria|date=November 2010|isbn=1-4391-7778-3[/tpl][tpl]cite news|url=http://content.usatoday.com/communities/entertainment/post/2010/11/portia-de-rossi-i-would-starve-myself-daily-on-300-calorie-diet/1|title=Portia de Rossi: 'I would starve myself daily' on 300 calorie diet|date=3 November 2010|work=USA Today|first=Ann|last=Oldenburg[/tpl][tpl]dead link|date=February 2014[/tpl]

==Philanthropy==

De Rossi supports a variety of charitable organizations, including Locks of Love, a group that provides human hair wigs (made from donated ponytails) either free of charge or on a sliding scale based on their own guidelines[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.locksoflove.org/faq.html#Ac5|title=Locks of Love|publisher=Locks of Love|accessdate=12 July 2010[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.locksoflove.org/faq.html#Aa8|title=Locks of Love|publisher=Locks of Love|accessdate=12 July 2010[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://charityreports.give.org/Public/Report.aspx?CharityID=1839|title=Charity Review of Locks of Love|publisher=Charityreports.give.org|accessdate=12 July 2010[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bbb.org/charity-reviews/national/children-and-youth/locks-of-love-in-lake-worth-fl-1839|title=BBB review of Locks of Love|publisher=Bbb.org|accessdate=12 July 2010[/tpl] for children with alopecia and other medical conditions that cause hair loss.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.locksoflove.org/faq.html#Ac2|title=Locks of Love FAQ|publisher=Locksoflove.org|accessdate=12 July 2010[/tpl] She has also supported fundraising efforts for FXB International,[tpl]cite web|url=http://celebrityhalo.blogspot.com/2006/07/portia-de-rossi-helps-fxb-to-rebuild.html|title=Portia de Rossi Helps FXB to Rebuild Uganda|work=Celebrity Halo|date=July 2006|accessdate=29 December 2008[/tpl] an African AIDS relief organisation, and The Art of Elysium,[tpl]cite web|url=http://celebrityhalo.blogspot.com/2008/01/portia-de-rossi-supports-art-of-elysium.html|title=Portia de Rossi supports The Art of Elysium|work=Celebrity Halo|date=January 2008|accessdate=29 December 2008[/tpl] an art foundation for terminally ill children. An avid animal lover, de Rossi also supports Alley Cat Allies,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.alleycat.org/NetCommunity/Page.aspx?pid=739|title=I'm an Alley Cat Ally – Portia de Rossi|accessdate=29 September 2010[/tpl] an organisation dedicated to protecting and improving the lives of cats.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.alleycat.org/NetCommunity/Page.aspx?pid=616|title=Alley Cat Allies – About Us|accessdate=29 September 2010[/tpl] De Rossi and wife Ellen DeGeneres have also been vegan since 2008, and are strong supporters of the Gentle Barn,[tpl]cite web|url=http://gentlebarn.org/|title=The Gentle Barn|publisher=gentlebarn.org|accessdate=8 March 2012[/tpl] a California-based sanctuary for abused animals.
