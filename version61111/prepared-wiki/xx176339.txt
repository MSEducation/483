[[WMBJ]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in South Carolina, Christian contemporary radio stations, Georgetown County, South Carolina, Radio stations established in 1999

WMBJ (88.3 FM, "HIS Radio") is a radio station licensed to serve Murrells Inlet, South Carolina.  The station is owned by Radio Training Network, Inc. It airs a Contemporary Christian music format.[tpl]cite web |title=Station Information Profile |work=Arbitron |url=http://www.arbitron.com/[/tpl] In addition to simulcast and syndicated programming, WMBJ airs programming from local churches.[tpl]cite web |work=His Radio |url=http://www.hisradio.com/common/contenthottop.asp?PAGE=363 |title=About Us/Mission[/tpl]
The station was assigned the WMBJ call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on November 18, 1999.[tpl]cite web |title=Call Sign History |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=76588&Callsign=WMBJ |work=FCC Media Bureau CDBS Public Access Database[/tpl]
