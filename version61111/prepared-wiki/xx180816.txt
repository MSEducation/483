[[2007–08 Albanian Superliga]]

CATEGORIES: Albanian Superliga seasons, 2007–08 domestic association football leagues, 2007–08 in Albanian football

The 2007–08 Kategoria superiore season was the 72nd season of top-tier in Albania, since its establishment. The first matches of the season were played on 25 August 2007. KF Tirana were the defending champions, having won their twenty-third title the previous season.
