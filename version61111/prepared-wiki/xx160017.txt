[[Belgian general election, 1949]]

CATEGORIES: 1949 elections in Europe, Elections in Belgium, 1949 in Belgium

General elections were held in Belgium on 26 June 1949.Nohlen, D & Stöver, P (2010) Elections in Europe: A data handbook, p289 ISBN 978-3-8329-5609-7 The result was a victory for the Christian Social Party, which won 105 of the 212 seats in the Chamber of Representatives and 54 of the 106 seats in the Senate.Nohlen & Stöver, pp309-311 Voter turnout was 94.4%.Nohlen & Stöver, p290 This election was the first after the introduction of universal women's suffrage in Belgium.
