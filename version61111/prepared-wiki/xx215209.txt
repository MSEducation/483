[[Eddyville, Oregon]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Lincoln County, Oregon, Unincorporated communities in Oregon

Eddyville is an unincorporated community in Lincoln County, Oregon, United States. Eddyville's estimated population as of 2007 was 962 residents.Eddyville, Oregon from Citydata.com. Accessed February 2010.
The community has a post office with a ZIP code of 97343.Zip Code Lookup  Eddyville lies at the intersection of U.S. Route 20 and Oregon Route 180 east of Chitwood.
