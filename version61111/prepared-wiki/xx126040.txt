[[OC Khouribga]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Morocco, Association football clubs established in 1923, 1923 establishments in Morocco, Sports clubs in Morocco

Olympique Club of Khouribga, also called OCK, is a Moroccan football club based in Khouribga.

==History==

The club was founded in 1923, and first arrived in the country's first division in the 1982-1983 season.
