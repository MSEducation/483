[[Miyazaki Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Miyazaki Prefecture, Stations of Kyushu Railway Company

[tpl]nihongo|Miyazaki Station|宮崎駅|Miyazaki-eki[/tpl] is a JR Kyushu railway station located in Miyazaki, Miyazaki Prefecture, Japan.
The central station of the prefectural capital is served by the Nippō Main Line connecting Fukuoka Prefecture and Kagoshima Prefecture. The station opened on December 15, 1913.
