[[Richlands railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Brisbane, Railway stations opened in 2011

Richlands is a railway station in the suburb of Richlands on the Springfield line of the Queensland Rail Citytrain network. The rail extension to Richlands is the first step in the progressive construction of the Springfield line to Springfield.
The station multi-storey car park has 650 car parking spaces for vehicles.[tpl]Cite news |url=http://www.brisbanetimes.com.au/queensland/bikeway-axed-for-centenary-motorway-widening-20121010-27d4n.html |title=Bikeway axed for Centenary Motorway widening |author=Tony Moore |accessdate=11 October 2012 |date=11 October 2012 |work=Brisbane Times |publisher=Fairfax Media [/tpl]  According to local councillor David Morrison, these are filled as early as 8:30 AM.

==Construction and opening==

Construction started on the station on [tpl]nowrap|[tpl]start date|2008|07|01|df=yes[/tpl][/tpl] with a cost of $40 million.[tpl]cite news |title=The train station with no trains |first=Daniel |last=Hurst |url=http://www.brisbanetimes.com.au/queensland/the-train-station-with-no-trains-20110105-19g54.html |publisher=Brisbane Times  |date=6 January 2011[/tpl] The planning included about 650 car parks, a bus interchange, bicycle lockers and a drop-off area. The station is expected to be completed by 2011, alongside the first stage of the Springfield line.[tpl]cite press release |title=$800 million project offers transport choices |publisher=Queensland Government |date=1 July 2008 |url=http://statements.cabinet.qld.gov.au/mms/StatementDisplaySingle.aspx?id=58934[/tpl]
The station was due to open to the public on [tpl]nowrap|[tpl]start date|2011|01|23|df=yes[/tpl][/tpl] with no services due to scheduled maintenance works on the Ipswich line, but due to the Queensland floods the opening was brought forward to [tpl]nowrap|[tpl]start date|2011|01|17|df=yes[/tpl][/tpl] in order to assist with the recovery efforts.[tpl]cite news |title=New train station to open early |url=http://www.brisbanetimes.com.au/queensland/new-train-station-to-open-early-20110116-19s6g.html |publisher=Brisbane Times |date=16 January 2011[/tpl]
