[[Mezzo]]

CATEGORIES: Italian words and phrases

mezzo is the Italian word for "half", "middle" or "medium." mezza is the feminine equivalent.  It is pronounced [tpl]IPA|/ˈmɛtso/[/tpl] in English, but [tpl]IPA|/ˈmɛddzo/[/tpl] in Italian.
In music, mezzo is the beginning of various Italian musical terms
