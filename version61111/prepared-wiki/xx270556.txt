[[Arfak Mountains frog]]

CATEGORIES: Hylarana, Amphibians of Indonesia, Amphibians of Papua New Guinea, Amphibians of New Guinea

The Arfak Mountains frog (Hylarana arfaki) is a species of true frog in the genus Hylarana. It is native to Indonesia and Papua New Guinea. Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical moist lowland forests and rivers.Stephen Richards, Fred Parker 2004. Hylarana arfaki. In: IUCN 2011. IUCN Red List of Threatened Species. Version 2011.1.
