[[Upper Two Medicine Lake]]

CATEGORIES: Lakes of Glacier National Park (U.S.), Lakes of Glacier County, Montana

Upper Two Medicine Lake is located in Glacier National Park, in the U. S. state of Montana. The lake is just east of the Continental Divide in the Two Medicine region of Glacier National Park.
