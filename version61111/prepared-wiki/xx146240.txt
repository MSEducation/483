[[Assunta Legnante]]

CATEGORIES: 1978 births, Living people, Italian shot putters, Athletes (track and field) at the 2008 Summer Olympics, Olympic athletes of Italy, Sportspeople from Naples, Paralympic athletes of Italy, Paralympic gold medalists for Italy, Athletes (track and field) at the 2012 Summer Paralympics, World record holders in Paralympic athletics

Assunta Legnante (born May 14, 1978 in Naples) is a visually impaired Italian shot putter. She has competed in both the Olympics and the Paralympics, one of the few athletes to do so. [tpl]As of|February 2013[/tpl], she is the current F11 shot put world record holder.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.paralympic.org/sdms/web/at/record.pdf.at.php?xml=0&rct=RCTWR&spec=out&gen=W&evt=|title=IPC Athletics World Records|publisher=International Paralympic Committee|accessdate=3 February 2013[/tpl]

==Non-disabled athletics career==

She won the silver medal at the 2002 and the gold medal at the 2007 European Indoor Athletics Championships, where she captained the Italian team. She finished fifth at the 2006 European Athletics Championships. She competed at the 2008 Summer Olympics in Beijing, having missed the 2004 Olympics due to dangerously high intraocular pressure. 
Her personal best throw is 19.04 metres, achieved in September 2006 in Busto Arsizio. Her best indoor result is 19.20 metres, achieved in February 2002 in Genova.IAAF Athlete Profile: Assunta Legnante

==Blindness and Paralympics==

Legnante was born with congenital glaucoma in both eyes.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.aipsmedia.com/index.php?page=news&cod=7917&tp=n|title=Beijing Olympic shot-putter Assunta Legnante now blind, seeks Paralympic title|publisher=Association Internationale De La Presse Sportive|accessdate=3 February 2013[/tpl] In 2009, she suddenly lost the sight in her better, right eye. Her left retina then deteriorated. She had a cataract removed in March 2012, but that did not improve her sight. She now has light perception in her left eye, but nothing more. 
The 2007 European indoor champion did not lose heart and started a new career as a Paralympic athlete, training for the forthcoming 2012 Summer Paralympics.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.sportlive.it/altri-sport/assunta-legnante-cieca-perdita-vista.html|title=Atletica: Legnante shock "Non ci vedo piu"|publisher=sportlive.it|language=italian|accessdate=11 May 2012[/tpl]
At the 2012 Italian Paralympic Athletics Championships in Turin she set a new F11 shot put world record with a throw of 13.27 metres.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fispes.it/showquestion.php?faq=1&fldAuto=348|title=Assunta Legnante nuovo record del mondo, tanti ottimi risultati.|publisher=Fispes.it|language=italian|accessdate=12 May 2012[/tpl] On 8 June, at Memorial Primo Nebiolo in Turin, she improve the world record, three times, to 15.22 m.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fidal.it/content/Torino-per-il-record-bis-di-Abate-13-28/50480|title=Torino per il record bis di Abate|publisher=fidal.it|language=italian|accessdate=9 June 2012[/tpl] At the London Paralympics, she broke her own world with a throw of 16.74 metres.
