[[1982 ATP Challenger Series]]

CATEGORIES: ATP Challenger Tour

The ATP Challenger Series is the second tier tour for professional tennis organised by the Association of Tennis Professionals (ATP). The 1982 ATP Challenger Series calendar comprises 41 tournaments, with prize money ranging from $25,000 up to $50,000.[tpl]cite web|work=atpworldtour.com|publisher=ATP Tour, Inc.|title=About the Challenger Circuit| url=http://www.atpworldtour.com/Tournaments/Challenger/ABOUT.aspx|accessdate=2009-11-01|archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/5ls0LVddl|archivedate=2009-08-05|deadurl=no[/tpl]
