[[Beauvechain]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Belgium, Municipalities of Walloon Brabant

Beauvechain (Walloon: Bôvètché, [tpl]lang-nl|Bevekom[/tpl]) is a Walloon municipality located in the Belgian province of Walloon Brabant. On 1 January 2006 the municipality had 6,529 inhabitants. The total area is 38.58 km², giving a population density of 169 inhabitants per km².
Beauvechain has several protected heritage sites; see this list. It also hosts a Belgian military airfield which formerly flew F-16 Fighting Falcon jets.

==SC Beauvechain==

Beauvechain is the home of SC Beauvechain football club. Since the 2011-2012 season the club has benefited from investment allowing the creation of new pitches (one synthetic and one grass)as well as a new club house and changing rooms. Both of the club's senior side's are on the rise and the future looks bright.http://www.footbeauvechain.com/
