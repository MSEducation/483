[[Nikutai wa Shōjiki na Eros]]

CATEGORIES: 2005 singles, Zetima Records singles, Song recordings produced by Tsunku

[tpl]nihongo|"Nikutai wa Shōjiki na Eros"|肉体は正直なEROS|The Body of a True Eros[/tpl] is the fourteenth single by Japanese girl group Melon Kinenbi. It was released on February 9, 2005, and its highest position on the Oricon weekly chart was #21. This was the last of Melon Kinenbi's singles to be produced by Tsunku, before fellow Sharam Q member Taisei took over.
