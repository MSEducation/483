[[Brave (Kelis song)]]

CATEGORIES: 2011 singles, Kelis songs, Songs written by Kelis, Songs written by Jean Baptiste (record producer), Songs written by will.i.am

"Brave" is a song performed by American recording artist Kelis, taken from her fifth studio album, Flesh Tone. The up-tempo electro-dance song was written by Kelis, will.i.am, Jean Baptiste and James Fauntleroy, and produced by Benny Benassi and Alle Benassi. It was released on 6 January 2011, as the album's fourth single by will.i.am music and Interscope Records. Kelis has also re-recorded the track in 'simlish' for video game The Sims 3: Late Night.[tpl]cite web|author=Published Wednesday, Nov 24 2010, 13:36 GMT |url=http://www.digitalspy.co.uk/music/news/a289602/kelis-records-new-single-in-simlish.html |title=Kelis records new single in Simlish - Music News |publisher=Digital Spy |date=2010-11-24 |accessdate=2012-02-08[/tpl]
