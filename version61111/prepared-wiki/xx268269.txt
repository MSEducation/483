[[Green Lights]]

CATEGORIES: 2011 singles, Aloe Blacc songs

"Green Lights" is a song by American singer and rapper Aloe Blacc, with music and lyrics by Jeffrey Scott Silverman, Leon Marcus Michels, Nicholas Anthony Movshon, E. Nathaniel Dawkins and produced by Leon Michels and Jeff Dynamite. It was released on 9 September 2011 as a Digital download in the United Kingdom. It has peaked to number 130 on the UK Singles Chart.

==Music video==

A music video to accompany the release of "Green Lights" was first released onto YouTube on 12 August 2011 at a total length of three minutes and twelve seconds.Aloe Blacc - Green Lights YouTube
