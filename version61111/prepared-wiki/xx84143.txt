[[Agematsu, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Agematsu|上松町|Agematsu-machi[/tpl] is a town located in Kiso District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the town has an estimated population of 6,034 and a density of 35.82 persons per km². The total area is 168.47 km².
