[[Pro Street]]

CATEGORIES: Vehicle modification, DIY culture

Pro Street is a style of street-legal custom car Popular in the 1980s, usually built to imitate a Pro-street class race car.  Pro Street cars should appear to be more at home on the drag strip than the street, while remaining street legal and not Gutted like a Race Car or Bracket Race car. Typically called a Backhalf car.
