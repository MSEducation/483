[[North Plains Gliderport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in Washington County, Oregon, Gliderports in the United States

North Plains Gliderport [tpl]airport codes|||1OR4[/tpl] is a private use glider airport located two nautical miles (4Â km) west of the central business district of North Plains, a city in Washington County, Oregon, United States. It is privately owned by the Willamette Valley Soaring Club.

==Facilities and aircraft==

North Plains Gliderport has one runway designated 9/27 with a 2,000 by 200 foot (610 x 61 m) turf surface. There are 12 gliders and 2 single-engine aircraft based at this airport.
