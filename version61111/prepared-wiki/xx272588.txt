[[Hylarana melanomenta]]

CATEGORIES: Hylarana, Amphibians of the Philippines, Endemic fauna of the Philippines

Hylarana melanomenta is a species of true frog in the genus Hylarana. It is native to the Sulu Archipelago of the Philippines. It lives in streams and rivers in lower montane and lowland forests.[ref]
