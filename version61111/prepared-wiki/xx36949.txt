[[Løgumkloster]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Region of Southern Denmark

Løgumkloster ([tpl]lang-de|Lügumkloster[/tpl]; both mean 'Løgum monastery'), is a town in Tønder municipality in Region of Southern Denmark on the Jutland peninsula in south Denmark with a population of 3,629 (1 January 2013)."BEF44: Population 1st January, by urban areas" database from Statistics Denmark Its name testifies that the town was once the site of the Cistercian Løgum Abbey, in the then Roman Catholic diocese of Ribe.
Logumkloster was the municipal seat of the now abolished Løgumkloster Municipality.
