[[Photedes enervata]]

CATEGORIES: Hadeninae, Moths of Canada

The Many-Lined Cordgrass Moth (Photedes enervata, until recently Hypocoena enervata)Poole, R. W. (1989). Lepidopterorum Catalogus (New Series) Fascicle 118, Noctuidae. CRC Press. ISBN 0-916846-45-8, ISBN 978-0-916846-45-9. is a species of moth of the family Noctuidae. It is found in marshes along the Atlantic Coast of North America, with scattered inland wetlands records from Nova Scotia and New Brunswick south to Florida.
The larvae feed on Spartina alterniflora.
