[[Kolokythas]]

CATEGORIES: Lasithi, Islands of Crete

Kolokythas ([tpl]lang-el|Κολοκύθας[/tpl] "pumpkin", also known as Κολοκυθιά, Kolokythia and Βρυονήσι, Vryonisi, "moss island") is a Greek islet with a hotel. It is part of municipality Agios Nikolaos of the regional unit Lasithi, eastern Crete. It is located some 850 m east of the Spinalonga peninsula and forms a bay with it.Beaches in north Crete: Kolokitha or Kolokytha beach in Elounda
