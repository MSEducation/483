[[Hammamlu-e Pain]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Hammamlu-e Pain ([tpl]lang-fa|حماملو پايين[/tpl], also Romanized as Ḩammāmlū-e Pā’īn and Ḩammāmlū-e Pāeen)[tpl]IranNCSGN[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 96, in 21 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
