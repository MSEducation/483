[[Kato Olympos]]

Kato Olympos ([tpl]lang-el|Κάτω Όλυμπος[/tpl], meaning "Lower Mount Olympus") is a former municipality in the Larissa regional unit, Thessaly, Greece. Since the 2011 local government reform it is part of the municipality Tempi, of which it is a municipal unit.Kallikratis law Greece Ministry of Interior [tpl]el icon[/tpl] Population 4,375 (2001). The seat of the municipality was in Pyrgetos.
