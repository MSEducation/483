==2013 Velika Ivanča shooting==

Velika Ivanča was the site of one of the deadliest spree shootings in modern-day Serbia. The shootings occurred in the early hours of 9 April 2013, when a gunman killed thirteen people, including six men, six women and a two-year-old boy, before he tried to kill himself and his wife. They were taken to hospital in critical conditions.
