[[Star Radio North East]]

CATEGORIES: The Local Radio Company, Radio stations in Yorkshire, Radio stations in North East England

Star Radio North East is a north-east based local radio station serving Darlington, Durham, Northallerton and surrounding areas.

==History==

Following UKRD take over of The Local Radio Company, Star Radio North East was launched on 2 November 2009 formed when the new owners merged Alpha 103.2, Minster Northallerton and Durham FM into one station.[tpl]cite news | url = http://www.guardian.co.uk/media/2009/may/14/ukrd-local-radio-company-hallwood | title = Merger/take over details | accessdate = 2010-01-26 | work=The Guardian | location=London | first=Mark | last=Sweney | date=14 May 2009[/tpl] Originally, the Alpha name was to be used making the stations Alpha Northallerton and Alpha Durham.[tpl]cite web | url = http://radiotoday.co.uk/news.php?extend.4991 | title = Alpha name change details | accessdate = 2010-01-26 [/tpl]
