[[Qamcheqay, Khodabandeh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Qamcheqay ([tpl]lang-fa|قمچقاي[/tpl], also Romanized as Qamcheqāy and Qamcheqā’ī; also known as Ghamchaghay, Kabajgāi, and Qamchāy)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3079446|Qamcheqay[/tpl] is a village in Shivanat Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 404, in 80 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
