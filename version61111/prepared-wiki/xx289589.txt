[[Sereshbar]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Sereshbar ([tpl]lang-fa|سرشبار[/tpl], also Romanized as Sereshbār and Sarashbar; also known as Chahārāshpar, Serīshbār, and Serīsh Bār)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3083528|Sereshbar[/tpl] is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 273, in 49 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
