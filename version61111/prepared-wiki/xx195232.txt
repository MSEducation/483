Charles David Clough (born 4 September 1990) is an English footballer who plays as a defender for Sutton United.
Originally from Taunton in Somerset, Clough was admitted into the Bristol Rovers youth set-up, based at the Bristol Academy of Sport, and was promoted to the first-team squad in the summer of 2007. His football league debut, aged 17, came in a Football League One match against Brighton & Hove Albion at the Memorial Stadium on 26 April 2008 as a substitute in the 68th minute.

In January 2011, Clough joined struggling Southern Premier League club Weymouth on a month-long loan.
