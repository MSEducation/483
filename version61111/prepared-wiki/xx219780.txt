[tpl]Nihongo|Chiaki Kusuhara|楠原千秋|Kusuhara Chiaki|born 1 November 1975 in Matsuyama, Ehime[/tpl] is a Japanese Olympic beach volleyballer. She competed at the 2008 Summer Olympics, and is partnered with Mika Teru Saiki.
