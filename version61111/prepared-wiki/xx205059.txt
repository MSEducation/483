[[A Tear for the Ghetto]]

CATEGORIES: 1999 albums, Group Home albums, Albums produced by DJ Premier, Albums produced by The Alchemist

A Tear for the Ghetto is the second album released by the hip-hop duo Group Home. Released June 1, 1999, on Replay Records, the album featured one hit single, "Make It In Life," which made to #16 on the Hot Rap Singles and production by The Alchemist, Agallah, Guru, DJ Premier and group member Lil' Dap.
