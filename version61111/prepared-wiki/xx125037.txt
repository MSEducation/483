[[Hitori (song)]]

CATEGORIES: 2005 singles, Mika Nakashima songs

[tpl]incomplete|date=March 2014[/tpl][tpl]Unreferenced|date=December 2009[/tpl]

[tpl]Nihongo|"Hitori"|ひとり[/tpl] is Mika Nakashima's 15th single. It is a re-cut single, originally from her Music album, and was used as the theme song for the PlayStation 2 game Drakengard 2. "Hitori (single ver.)" is the theme song for the game while "Hitori (Endroll ver.)" is the version heard during the credits. It was released on  a week ahead of the game. Overall, it sold 26,275 copies according to the Oricon charts.
