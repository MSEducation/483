[[1997 Heineken Trophy]]

CATEGORIES: 1997 ATP Tour, 1997 WTA Tour, Rosmalen Grass Court Championships, 1997 in Dutch sport

The 1997 Heineken Trophy was a tennis tournament played on grass courts in Rosmalen in the Netherlands that was part of the World Series of the 1996 ATP Tour and of Tier III of the 1997 WTA Tour. The tournament was held from June 16 through June 22, 1997.
