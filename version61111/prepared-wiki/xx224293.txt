Krystian Brzozowski (born February 20, 1982 in Namysłów) is a male freestyle wrestler from Poland. He participated in Men's freestyle 74 kg at 2008 Summer Olympics. He was eliminated in the 1/16 of final losing with Arsen Gitinov.
Brzozowski participated in Men's freestyle 74 kg at 2004 Summer Olympics as well. He was ranked on 4th place.
He won a bronze medal at 2006 European Wrestling Championships.
