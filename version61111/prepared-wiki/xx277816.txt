[[Nikos Marinakis]]

CATEGORIES: 1993 births, Living people, Greek footballers, Panathinaikos F.C. players, Superleague Greece players

Nikos Marinakis (Greek: Νίκος Μαρινάκης), is a Greek professional Footballer. He currently plays for Panathinaikos as a right back. 

==Career==

He is a product of Panathinaikos's youth ranks.
