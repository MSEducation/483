[[Jean-Pierre Carniaux]]

CATEGORIES: 1951 births, Living people

==Qualifications==

- Université de Paris: Master in Mathematics (1972) 
- Massachusetts Institute of Technology: Bachelor of Science in Art and Design (1974) 
- Master of Architecture (1976)
He joined Ricardo Bofill Taller de Arquitectura in 1976. He opened and lead New York’s office of the practice from 1986 to 1990. He is presently Senior Partner at Ricardo Bofill Taller de Arquitectura.

==Key data==

Jean-Pierre Carniaux’s design work in the last fifteen years has ranged from the design of the National Theater of Catalonia, a 240.000 m2 development for a district of Montpellier in Antigone, including housing, offices and retail areas, to the design of perfume bottles for Christian Dior and the Shiseido Headquarter in Tokyo. He has participated in the projects of the New Terminal 1 at Barcelona Airport, a 50 floors skyscraper in Chicago, and a Congress Centre in Madrid.
