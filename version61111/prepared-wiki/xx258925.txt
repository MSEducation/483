[[Cameron Brown (video game director)]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, Video game directors, Missing middle or first names

Cameron G. Brown is a video game director.  He has worked at developers Melbourne House and Pandemic Studios, working on such titles as Mercenaries: Playground of Destruction, Destroy All Humans, and Mercenaries 2.  He is currently co-founder of Means of Production, a start-up video game company in Los Angeles.http://www.mobygames.com/developer/sheet/view/developerId,63710/
