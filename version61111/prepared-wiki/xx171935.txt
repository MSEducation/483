[[Sven Lindberg]]

Sven Lindberg (20 November 1918 – 25 December 2006) was a Swedish film actor and director.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.sfi.se/en-GB/Swedish-film-database/Item/?type=PERSON&itemid=61421 |title=Sven Lindberg |accessdate=2013-02-06|work=Swedish Film Database[/tpl] He was born and died in Stockholm, Sweden. At the 29th Guldbagge Awards he won the award for Best Actor for the film Spring of Joy.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.sfi.se/en-GB/Swedish-film-database/Item/?type=MOVIE&itemid=17942&iv=Awards |title=Glädjekällan (1993) |accessdate=31 March 2014 |work=The Swedish Film Database[/tpl]
