[[Shozo Ishihara]]

CATEGORIES: 1910 births, 1993 deaths, Japanese speed skaters, Olympic speed skaters of Japan, Speed skaters at the 1932 Winter Olympics, Speed skaters at the 1936 Winter Olympics, Waseda University alumni

Shozo Ishihara ([tpl]lang-ja|石原省三[/tpl]; August 10, 1910 – July 19, 1993) was a Japanese speed skater who competed in the 1932 Winter Olympics and in the 1936 Winter Olympics.
He was born in the Tochigi Prefecture.
In 1932 he participated in the 500 metres competition, in the 1500 metres event, in the 5000 metres competition, and in the 10000 metres event but was eliminated in the heats in all four contests.
Four years later he finished fourth in the 500 metres competition and 19th in the 1500 metres event.
