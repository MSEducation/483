[[KJIA]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Iowa

KJIA is a Christian radio station licensed to Spirit Lake, Iowa, broadcasting on 88.9 MHz FM.  KJIA serves Northwest Iowa and Southwest Minnesota.  The station is owned by Minn-Iowa Christian Broadcasting, Inc. KJIA fcc.gov. Accessed July 25, 2012
