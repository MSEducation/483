[[Alfredo da Motta]]

Alfredo Rodrigues da Motta (born January 12, 1921) was a Brazilian basketball player, who competed in the 1948 Summer Olympics in London, United Kingdom. There he won the bronze medal with the men's national team. He was born in Rio de Janeiro.
