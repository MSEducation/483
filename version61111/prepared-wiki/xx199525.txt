[[Arvid Grundekjøn]]

Arvid Grundekjøn (born 26 June 1955)[tpl]cite news|title=50 år 26. juni. Styreformann Arvid Grundekjøn|date=20 June 2005|agency=Norwegian News Agency|language=Norwegian[/tpl] is a Norwegian businessperson and conservative politician.
He was chairman of Statkraft and awarded in 2009 Chair of the Year for his work, making Statkraft the largest producer of renewable energy in Europe.[tpl]cite news|last=Lerø|first=Magne|title=Giskes meningsregulering |url=http://www.ukeavisenledelse.no/meninger/redaktorens_mening/article59303.zrm|accessdate=30 June 2010|newspaper=Ukeavisen|date=14 June 2010| archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20100621001721/http://www.ukeavisenledelse.no/meninger/redaktorens_mening/article59303.zrm| archivedate= 21 June 2010 | deadurl= no[/tpl] and Anders Wilhelmsen Group,[tpl]cite news|last=Barstad|first=Stine|title=Utfordrer Carnival|url=http://www.dn.no/arkiv/article21510.ece|accessdate=30 June 2010|newspaper=Dagens Næringsliv|date=20 November 2001|author2=Stine Barstad,Lasse Bergesen[/tpl] and is a board member of Royal Caribbean Cruises Ltd.[tpl]cite news|title=RCCL avviser emisjonsrykter |url=http://www.nrk.no/nyheter/okonomi/2087381.html|accessdate=30 June 2010|newspaper=Norwegian Broadcasting Corporation|date=27 August 2002[/tpl][tpl]cite news|last=Fadnes|first=Ole-Morten|title=RCCL på vei ut av Norge|url=http://www.dn.no/forsiden/naringsliv/article209972.ece|accessdate=30 June 2010|newspaper=Dagens Næringsliv|date=31 March 2004[/tpl] and Dampskibsselskabet Norden.[tpl]cite web|title=Denmark D/S Norden to convene annual general meeting |url=http://www.bymnews.com/news/newsDetails.php?id=52814|accessdate=30 June 2010[/tpl]
Grundekjøn is Honorary Consul for Latvia in Norway, and has been awarded with the Order of the Three Stars.
Grundekjøn is currently (2014) the mayor of Kristiansand, representing the Conservative Party (Norway) from 2011.
