[[Johannes Bluyssen]]

CATEGORIES: 1926 births, 2013 deaths, People from Nijmegen, Dutch Roman Catholic bishops

Johannes Willem Maria Bluyssen or Bluijssen (10 April 1926 − 8 August 2013) was a Dutch Roman Catholic bishop.
Ordained to the priesthood in 1950, he was appointed titular bishop of Aëtus and auxiliary bishop of the Roman Catholic Diocese of 's-Hertogenbosch, Netherlands in 1961 and was named bishop of the diocese in 1966 and resigned in 1984 due to health reasons following an open heart surgery.Johannes Willem Maria Bluyssen He died after a short illness on 8 August 2013, aged 87, in 's-Hertogenbosch.Bisschop Bluyssen overleden [tpl]nl icon[/tpl]
