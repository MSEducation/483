[[Kim Alsop]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, American strength and conditioning coaches, Louisiana Tech Bulldogs football coaches, Samford Bulldogs football coaches

Kim Alsop is a former college head football coach. In 1984, he was hired to restart the football progran at Samford, which had been dormant since the end of the 1973 season.[tpl]cite news|url=http://news.google.ca/newspapers?id=5A8iAAAAIBAJ&sjid=DMgEAAAAIBAJ&pg=3634,6491760&dq=kim-alsop&hl=en|title=Kim Alsop named new Samford coach |date=April 27, 1984|publisher=Times Daily|accessdate=January 8, 2010[/tpl] In his three season with the team, Samford compiled a 6-21 record.  He was dismissed after his third season and replaced by Terry Bowden.[tpl]cite news|url=http://news.google.ca/newspapers?id=Hq0RAAAAIBAJ&sjid=4ukDAAAAIBAJ&pg=3998,2773297&dq=kim-alsop+terry+bowden&hl=en|title=Samford hires a second Bowden as coach|date=January 11, 1987|publisher=The Gainesville Sun|accessdate=January 8, 2010[/tpl] Following Samford, Alsop would accept a position as Strength and Conditioning Coach with Louisiana Tech under head coach Carl Torbush.  
Alsop would later resurface as a football coach in Mississippi at Jackson Academy, an independent school in Jackson, Mississippi.  After a 1993 season when JA missed the playoffs Alsop left coaching. He was replaced by Sherard Shaw.
