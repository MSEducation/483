[[Yamaga, Ōita]]

CATEGORIES: Dissolved municipalities of Ōita Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Yamaga|山香町|Yamaga-machi[/tpl] was a town located in Hayami District, Ōita Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the town had an estimated population of 8,590 and the density of 59.77 persons per km². The total area was 143.71 km².
On October 1, 2005, Yamaga, along with the village of Ōta (from Nishikunisaki District), was merged into the expanded city of Kitsuki.
