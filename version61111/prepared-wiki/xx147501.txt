[[Estádio Municipal Sérgio Conceição]]

CATEGORIES: Buildings and structures in Coimbra, Sport in Coimbra, Football venues in Portugal

Estádio Municipal Sérgio Conceição (Sérgio Conceição Municipal Stadium) is a football stadium in Taveiro, in the city of Coimbra, Portugal. Built in 2002, it has 2500 seats and was named after Sérgio Conceição, a football player born in Coimbra.
The stadium belongs to the municipality of Coimbra and is the home ground for the football teams of two local sports clubs: Clube de Futebol União de Coimbra and União Desportiva Taveirense.
