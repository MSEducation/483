[[List of WNBA franchise post-season droughts]]

CATEGORIES: Women's National Basketball Association Finals

This is a list of Women's National Basketball Association (WNBA) franchise post-season appearance droughts. This list includes the all-time and the active consecutive non-playoffs appearance up to and including the 2011 season. Aside from the WNBA Playoff appearance droughts, this list also includes the WNBA Finals appearance drought and the WNBA championships win drought. The teams which have never made a WNBA Playoffs appearance, a WNBA Finals appearance and have never won a WNBA championship are listed from the year they entered the league.
