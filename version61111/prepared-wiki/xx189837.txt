Anthony Russo (born September 10, 1984 in Leominster, Massachusetts) is a professional American and Canadian football wide receiver free agent. He was signed by the Seattle Seahawks as an undrafted free agent in 2008. He played college football for the Washington Huskies. Russo has also been a member of the BC Lions.LIONS AGREE TO TERMS ON CONTRACTS WITH TWO WIDE RECEIVERS

==College career==

Russo played college football at the University of Washington.
Finishing 5th all-time in receiving yards and receptions.

==Professional career==

===Seattle Seahawks===

Russo signed a free agent contract with the National Football League's Seattle Seahawks on April 28, 2008. He was released on June 4, 2008 due to injury.

===BC Lions===

Russo signed with the BC Lions on May 13, 2009. He was released on June 25, 2009 due to injury.

===Painter/Artist===

Anthony Russo has been focusing his talents on the arts as a local Seattle painter. Using expressionism and surrealism. Russo also goes to his roots in sketching and drafting.
