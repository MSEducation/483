[[Hesar-e Sofla, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Hesar-e Sofla ([tpl]lang-fa|حصارسفلي[/tpl], also Romanized as Ḩeşār-e Soflá and Hesar Sofla; also known as Hesār, Ḩeşār-e Pā’īn, and Hīsār Pāīn)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3066461|Hesar-e Sofla[/tpl] is a village in Shivanat Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 234, in 58 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
