[[(48438) 1989 WJ2]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1989, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Jupiter Trojans (Trojan camp)

(48438) 1989 WJ2 is a Jupiter Trojan minor planet, located in the L5 Lagrangian point.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on November 21, 1989.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
