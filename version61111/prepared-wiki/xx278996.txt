[[Qarah Aghaj-e Sofla]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Qarah Aghaj-e Sofla ([tpl]lang-fa|قره اغاج سفلي[/tpl], also Romanized as Qarah Āghāj-e Soflá; also known as Qarah Āqāj-e Soflá and Qareh Āqāj-e Soflá)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795844|Qarah Aghaj-e Sofla[/tpl] is a village in Chaypareh-ye Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 356, in 64 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
