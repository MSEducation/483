[[Tangia Tongiia]]

Tangia Tongiia is a Cook Islander rugby league footballer of the 1990s and 2000s, playing at representative level for Cook Islands, and at club level for Papanui Tigers, and Canterbury Bulls, as a [tpl]rlp|WG[/tpl].

==Playing career==
