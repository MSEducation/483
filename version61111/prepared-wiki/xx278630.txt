[[Bahram Beyk]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Bahram Beyk ([tpl]lang-fa|بهرامبيك[/tpl], also Romanized as Bahrām Beyk; also known as Bahrām Beyg, Bairambai, Bayrambey, and Bayrām Beyk)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3054818|Bahram Beyk[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 469, in 102 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
