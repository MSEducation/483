[[Eddie Larsson]]

CATEGORIES: 1991 births, Living people, Modo Hockey players, Swedish ice hockey players, Växjö Lakers players

Eddie Larsson (born February 15, 1991) is a Swedish ice hockey defenceman. He is currently playing with Växjö Lakers of the Swedish Hockey League (SHL). 
Larsson has previously played for Modo Hockey, and also for BIK Karlskoga of the Hockeyallsvenskan.Modo Hockey Ornskoldsvik's 2010-11 Roster
