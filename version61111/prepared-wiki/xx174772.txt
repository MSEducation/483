[[HMS Hampshire (1653)]]

CATEGORIES: Ships of the line of the Royal Navy, 1650s ships

HMS ''Hampshire'' was a 38-gun fourth-rate frigate of the English Royal Navy, originally built for the navy of the Commonwealth of England by Phineas Pett II at Deptford, and launched in 1653. By 1677 her armament had been increased to 46 guns.
In 1686 Hampshire was rebuilt at Deptford Dockyard as a 46-gun fourth-rate ship of the line. She was sunk in action on 26 August 1697 during the Battle of Hudson's Bay.
