[[Ollie russian]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, British rock musicians

Ollie Russian is a British rock musician, who was the bass player in the bands Meanwhile, back in Communist Russia... and Lux Luther.
After leaving Meanwhile, back in Communist Russia... in late 2004, Russian became a writer for the UK music magazine Drowned In Sound. As a radio plugger for Alan James PR, Russian has represented both Klaxons and The Pipettes. He previously founded and promoted Club Seal with G-Man at the Islington Bar Academy.
He regularly DJs with G-Man at Frog, Koko, Barfly, and is the official DJ for the Mystery Jets.
