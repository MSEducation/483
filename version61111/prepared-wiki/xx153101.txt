[[Muhammad ibn Khwand]]

CATEGORIES: 1433 births, 1498 deaths

Muhammad ibn Khwand ([tpl]lang-ar|محمد بن خواند[/tpl]) was an Iranian historian and Islamic scholar.Salaam Knowledge His full name was Muhammad ibn Khwand Shah ibn Mahmud.Imamate: The Vicegerency of the Prophet s by Sayyid Saeed Akhtar Rizvi — read online on Al-islam.org http://www.al-islam.org/imamate/biblio.htm
He had Ali Shir Navai, the celebrated minister of the last Timuried Sultan, Husayn Bayqarah as his patron, and he spent most of his life in the laters court. He wrote Rawdatu 's-safa on the Sultans  request
