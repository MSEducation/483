[[Yersiniops newboldi]]

CATEGORIES: Mantidae, Natural history of Baja California, Mantodea of North America

Yersiniops newboldii is a species of praying mantis found in Baja California.  The original literature for this species was published in Transactions of the American Entomological Society  in 1931.http://clade.ansp.org/entomology/index.php?mode=details&catalognumber=5495 The Academy of Natural Sciences
