[[Lassoes 'N Spurs]]

CATEGORIES: 1991 compilation albums, Family Brown albums, RCA Records compilation albums

Lassoes 'N Spurs is the fourth compilation album by Canadian country music group Family Brown. It was released by RCA Records in 1991. The album contains nine previously released songs and a newly recorded version of the group's first single, "R.R. #2."
