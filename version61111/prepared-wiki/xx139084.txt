[[El Segundo (Los Angeles Metro station)]]

CATEGORIES: Los Angeles County Metro Rail stations, Railway stations opened in 1995

El Segundo is a Los Angeles County Metro Rail station on the Green Line.  It is located on Nash Street and El Segundo Boulevard in El Segundo, California.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.metro.net/around/rail/green-line/|title=Green Line station information|accessdate=2010-09-25[/tpl] The original name for this station was El Segundo/Nash and this name may still be used in some places.
When the Crenshaw Corridor project is completed (expected in 2016 or 2018), this station will also be served by the Crenshaw Line.
