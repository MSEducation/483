[[WEJF]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Florida, Palm Bay, Florida

WEJF is a Christian radio station licensed to Palm Bay, Florida, broadcasting on 90.3 MHz FM.  The station serves the Palm Bay-Melbourne area, and is owned by Florida Public Radio, Inc. WEJF fcc.gov. Accessed August 15, 2012
WEJF's programming includes Christian talk and teaching shows such as Focus on the Family, Revive our Hearts with Nancy Leigh DeMoss, Adrian Rogers, Joyce Meyer, Chuck Swindoll, and Unshackled!.  WEJF also airs a variety of Christian music.Monday thru Friday common schedule Accessed August 15, 2012  WEJF's programming is also heard in the Titusville, Florida area on 89.3 WPIO and in the areas of Dade City, Florida and Bushnell, Florida on 89.3 WKFA.
