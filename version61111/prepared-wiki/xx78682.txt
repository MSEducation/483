[[Shimoge District, Ōita]]

CATEGORIES: Former districts of Ōita Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Shimoge|下毛郡|Shimoge-gun[/tpl] was a district located in Ōita Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the district had an estimated population of 17,827 with a density of 40.98 persons per km². The total area was 435.03 km².
