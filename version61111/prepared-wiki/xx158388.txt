[[Va savoir]]

CATEGORIES: 2001 films, 2000s comedy-drama films, 2000s romantic comedy films, Films based on plays, Films based on works by Luigi Pirandello, French films, French-language films, German films, Italian films, Italian-language films, Films directed by Jacques Rivette

Va savoir ([tpl]lang-en|Who Knows?[/tpl]) is a 2001 French romantic comedy-drama film directed by Jacques Rivette. It stars Jeanne Balibar, Marianne Basler, Hélène de Fougerolles, and Catherine Rouvel. In the normal version, Va Savoir is 154 minutes. It was entered into the 2001 Cannes Film Festival.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.festival-cannes.com/en/archives/ficheFilm/id/1100076/year/2001.html |title=Festival de Cannes: Va savoir |accessdate=2009-10-18|work=festival-cannes.com[/tpl]

==Plot==

The actress Camille is on tour with her theatre ensemble and her partner for life, the Italian director Ugo. On her return to her hometown Paris she learns that her former lover Pierre lives now with Sonia, a ballet dancer. Ugo meanwhile gets to know the female arts student Dominique who seems to know something about a legendary lost object.
