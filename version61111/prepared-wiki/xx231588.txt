[[Jeanne Darville]]

CATEGORIES: 1923 births, 1995 deaths, Danish film actresses, People from Copenhagen, 20th-century Danish actresses

Jeanne Darville (18 August 1923 – 9 May 1995) was a Danish film actress. She appeared in 30 films between 1939 and 1978.
She was born in Copenhagen, Denmark and died in Denmark. She was in the film series "min søsters børn", playing the mother of her real life daughter, Pusle Helmuth.
