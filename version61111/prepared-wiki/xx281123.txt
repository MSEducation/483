[[Renat Rakhmatulin]]

CATEGORIES: 1996 births, Living people, Russian footballers, FC Sokol Saratov players

Renat Ildarovich Rakhmatulin ([tpl]lang-ru|Ренат Ильдарович Рахматулин[/tpl]; born April 30, 1996) is a Russian football midfielder, who currently playing for  FC Sokol Saratov.
He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Sokol Saratov on July 16, 2013 in a game against FC Kaluga.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Russian Football Union|url=http://rfspro.ru/cgi-bin/player/script2.cgi?par1=119553|title=Career Summary|date=9 August 2013[/tpl]
