[[St. Anthony's Church (Pine Plains, New York)]]

CATEGORIES: Religious organizations established in 1913, Roman Catholic churches in New York, Churches in Dutchess County, New York

The Church of St. Anthony is a Roman Catholic parish church under the authority of the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of New York, located in Pine Plains, Dutchess County, New York. It was originally established as mission of Immaculate Conception Parish in Amenia in 1913, and was elevated to parish status in 1958.
The church was built in 1912.Cole, Helen, Historic Highlights (1937)
