[[Zia Atabay]]

CATEGORIES: 1942 births, American Bahá'ís, American people of Iranian descent, Iranian Bahá'ís, Iranian Azerbaijanis, Iranian secularists, Iranian democracy activists, Iranian emigrants to the United States, Iranian expatriates in the United States, Living people

Zia Atabay (Persian: ضیا آتابای; born March 30, 1942) is an Iranian-American singer, TV host and human rights activist.
