[[Lars Bastian Ridder Stabell]]

CATEGORIES: 1798 births, 1860 deaths, Members of the Parliament of Norway, County Governors of Norway, Nordland politicians

Lars Bastian Ridder Stabell (1798–1860) was a Norwegian politician.
From 1834 to 18487 he was County Governor of Nordlands Amt (today named Nordland).
He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament in 1842, representing Nordlands Amt. He sat only one term.Lars Bastian Ridder Stabell — Norwegian Social Science Data Services (NSD)
