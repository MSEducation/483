[[Here Come the Miracles]]

CATEGORIES: 2001 albums

Here Come the Miracles is an double album by Steve Wynn, released in 2001 on Blue Rose Records. 
The album was recorded in Tucson with his friends Linda Pitmon (drums), Chris Brokaw (guitar), Chris Cacavas (keyboards).[tpl]cite web|last=Sullivan |first=Denise |url=http://www.allmusic.com/album/r525636 |title=Here Come the Miracles - Steve Wynn |publisher=AllMusic |date=2001-04-24 |accessdate=2012-01-10[/tpl]
The album won the category for best "Rock Alternative" album of 2001 at the annual Association for Independent Music Indie Awards.[tpl]citation needed|date=December 2010[/tpl]
