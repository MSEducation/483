[[2008 Malaysia Super Series]]

CATEGORIES: Malaysia Open (badminton), Sport in Kuala Lumpur, 2008 in Malaysian sport, 2008 BWF Super Series

The 2008 Malaysia Super Series is the inaugural tournament of the 2008 BWF Super Series in badminton. It was held in Kuala Lumpur, the capital of Malaysia, from January 15 to January 20, 2008.

==Absence==
