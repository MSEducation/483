[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Cornélio Procópio]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1973, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Londrina, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Cornélio Procópio ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Procopiensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Cornélio Procópio in the Ecclesiastical province of Londrina in Brazil.
