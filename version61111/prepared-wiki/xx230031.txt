[[Stockbridge Indian Cemetery]]

CATEGORIES: Protected areas of Calumet County, Wisconsin, Cemeteries on the National Register of Historic Places in Wisconsin

The Stockbridge Indian Cemetery is a cemetery north of Stockbridge, Wisconsin. It is located on Moore Road just west of Wisconsin Highway 55. The cemetery was established in 1834 after the Stockbridge Indians moved to the community.[tpl]cite web|title=Stockbridge Indian Museum|url=http://www.travelcalumet.com/tourism/activities/?category15=Museums%2B%2526%2BMarkers|publisher=Calumet County, Wisconsin Tourism|accessdate=November 19, 2013[/tpl]  It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1980.
