[[Showalter]]

Showalter is a surname common in the United States, and is possibly an anglicized variant of the German Schulter ("shoulder").http://www.surnamedb.com/Surname/Showalter 
A more likely source is from the German words schön wald, ("pretty forest"), referring to one who lived in such a place:  a Schönwalder.
