[[Atwater, Illinois]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Macoupin County, Illinois, Unincorporated communities in Illinois

Atwater is an unincorporated community in Shaws Point Township, Macoupin County, Illinois, United States. Atwater is [tpl]convert|3.5|mi|km[/tpl] east-southeast of Standard City. Atwater had a post office, which closed on October 12, 2002.[tpl]cite web|title=Postmaster Finder - Post Offices by ZIP Code|url=http://webpmt.usps.gov/pmt008.cfm|publisher=United States Postal Service|accessdate=September 25, 2011[/tpl]
