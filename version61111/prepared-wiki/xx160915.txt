[[Bishop Garcia Diego High School]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic secondary schools in California, Buildings and structures in Santa Barbara, California, Educational institutions established in 1959, High schools in Santa Barbara County, California, 1959 establishments in California

Bishop Garcia Diego High School is a private, Roman Catholic high school in Santa Barbara, California.  It is located in the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Los Angeles.

==Background==

Bishop García Diego High School was established in 1959.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bishopdiego.org/about/history.php|title=About Bishop García Diego High School - History|accessdate=2007-05-11|author=BCDHS[/tpl] The school is named for Bishop Francisco García Diego y Moreno [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bishopdiego.org/about/history.php|title=About Bishop García Diego High School - History|accessdate=2010-03-17|author=BCDHS[/tpl] who was the first bishop of the Diocese of the Two Californias. Notable Alumni include the illustrious Jeff Restivo.
