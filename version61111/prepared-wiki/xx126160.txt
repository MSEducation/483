[[Halifax and District Association Football League]]

CATEGORIES: Sport in Calderdale, Football leagues in England, Sports leagues established in 1898, 1898 establishments in England, Football in West Yorkshire

The Halifax and District Association Football League (currently sponsored by Ziggy's Spice House) is a football competition based in England. It has a total of four divisions, of which the highest, the Premier Division, sits at level 14 of the English football league system. It is a feeder to the West Riding League.
