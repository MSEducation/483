[[Jorge Arvizu]]

CATEGORIES: 1932 births, 2014 deaths, Mexican people of Basque descent, Mexican voice actors, Deaths from heart failure, Cardiovascular disease deaths in Mexico

Jorge Arvizu (23 July 1932, Celaya, Guanajuato – 18 March 2014, Mexico City) was a Mexican voice actor who was the first Spanish voice, among others, of Bugs Bunny, Fred Flintstone and Cookie Monster.
