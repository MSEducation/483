Borówek [tpl]IPAc-pl|b|o|'|r|u|w|e|k[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Żółkiewka, within Krasnystaw County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|7|km|mi|0[/tpl] north-east of Żółkiewka, [tpl]convert|20|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-west of Krasnystaw, and [tpl]convert|43|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-east of the regional capital Lublin.
