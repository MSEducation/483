[[Another Story (Stanley Turrentine album)]]

CATEGORIES: 1970 albums, Stanley Turrentine albums, Blue Note Records albums, Albums recorded at Van Gelder Studio

Another Story is an album by jazz saxophonist Stanley Turrentine recorded for the Blue Note label in 1969 and performed by Turrentine with Thad Jones, Cedar Walton, Buster Williams, and Mickey Roker.Stanley Turrentine discography accessed January 8, 2010.

==Reception==

The Allmusic review awarded the album 4 stars.Allmusic Review accessed January 8, 2010.
