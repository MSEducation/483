[[Marco Constante]]

CATEGORIES: 1967 births, Living people, People from Saquisilí, Ecuadorian footballers, Ecuador international footballers, 1997 Copa América players, El Nacional footballers, Deportivo Quito footballers

Marco Vinicius Constante (born October 4, 1967 in Saquisilí) is a former Ecuadorian football midfielder. He played during the majority of his career for El Nacional.
