[[Radovo]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Bosilovo Municipality

Radovo ([tpl]lang-mk|Радово[/tpl]) is a village in Republic of Macedonia near Strumica. It is in the Bosilovo municipality.  According to the 2002 census, the total population of the village was 851 inhabitants.Macedonian census, language and religion Almost all of the inhabitants of the village are Macedonian Catholics of Eastern Rite.
