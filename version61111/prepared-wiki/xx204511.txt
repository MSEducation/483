[[Selnik, Macedonia]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in the Republic of Macedonia

Selnik ([tpl]lang-mk|Селник[/tpl]) is a village in the Republic of Macedonia. It belongs to the Delčevo municipality and is situated on one of the mountains next to the town of Delčevo. Parts of the village are named after Sezotove's old family clans who settled there long time ago, for example Lesovci (named after the family name Lesovski).
