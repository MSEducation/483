[[Mid-Herts Football League]]

CATEGORIES: Defunct football leagues in England

The Mid-Herts Football League was a football competition based in England. It had two divisions including the Mid-Hertfordshire League Premier Division, which sat at level 13 of the English football league system. It was a feeder to the Hertfordshire Senior County League.
In the summer of 2006 the league merged with the North Hertfordshire League to form the new North & Mid-Herts Football League. http://football.mitoo.co.uk/ShowNews.cfm?LeagueCode=NMHL2006&SeasonName=2006-2007%20Season&LeagueName=North%20%26%20Mid%20Herts%20Football%20League
