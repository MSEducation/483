[[Yalama]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khachmaz District

Yalama is a village and municipality in the Khachmaz Rayon of Azerbaijan.  It has a population of 4,762.  The municipality consists of the villages of Yalama, Düztahiroba, Səlimoba, Ortaoba, Ukuroba, Yaquboba, Zeyxuroba, and Zuxuloba.Belediyye Informasiya Sistemi [tpl]dead link|date=January 2013[/tpl]
