[[Telavi Open]]

CATEGORIES: ITF Women's Circuit, Clay court tennis tournaments, Tennis tournaments in Georgia (country), Telavi Open

The Telavi Open is a tournament for professional female tennis players played on outdoor clay courts. The event is classified as a $50,000 ITF Women's Circuit tournament. It has been held in Telavi, Georgia, since 2007. From 2007 until 2010 the tournament was classified as a $25,000 event on the ITF calendar.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.tennisgeorgia.ge/en/index.html|title=Official|work=Tennis Georgia|accessdate=[/tpl]
