[[David Stephenson (born 1972)]]

CATEGORIES: 1972 births, English rugby league players, Hull Kingston Rovers players, Living people, Oldham Roughyeds players, Rugby league players from Leeds, Rochdale Hornets players, Rugby league hookers, Rugby league locks, Rugby league props, Rugby league second-rows

David Stephenson (born 6 October 1972 in Leeds) is an English rugby league footballer of the 1990s and 2000s, playing at club level for Oldham Roughyeds, Hull Sharks, and Rochdale Hornets, as a [tpl]rlp|PR|HK|SR[/tpl], or [tpl]rlp|LF-LK[/tpl].
