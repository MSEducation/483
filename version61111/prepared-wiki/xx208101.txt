[[Lars Johannesen Aga]]

CATEGORIES: 1800 births, Year of death missing, Members of the Parliament of Norway, Hordaland politicians

Lars Johannesen Aga (1800 - ??) was a Norwegian politician.
He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament in 1842 and 1845, representing the rural constituency of Søndre Bergenhus Amt (today named Hordaland). He worked as a farmer and bailiff.Lars Johannesen Aga — Norwegian Social Science Data Services (NSD)
