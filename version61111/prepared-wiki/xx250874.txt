Michael Pressman (born July 1, 1950 in New York City, New York, U.S.) is an American director and producer of film and television.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.offoffoff.com/film/2004/pressmanchess.php|work=OffOffOff.com|date=June 14, 2004|title=Frankie, my dear|author=Andrea Gronvall[/tpl] 
Some of the films he has directed are The Great Texas Dynamite Chase (1976), Doctor Detroit (1983), Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles II: The Secret of the Ooze (1991), To Gillian on Her 37th Birthday (1996).[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.filmreference.com/film/20/Michael-Pressman.html |title=Michael Pressman Biography (1950â€“) |publisher=Film Reference|accessdate=April 27, 2011[/tpl]
For television, he has directed episodes of Picket Fences, The Practice, The Guardian, Boston Legal, The Closer, Law & Order, Weeds, Grey's Anatomy and other series.
He is an alumnus of Camp Rising Sun and studied filmmaking at the California Institute of the Arts.
