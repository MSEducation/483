[[Alcobaça Municipality]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Portugal, Populated places in Leiria District

Alcobaça Municipality contains the city of Alcobaça. It is located in Oeste Subregion, region Centro in Portugal, formerly included in the Estremadura Province
The municipality has a total population of 55,269 inhabitants and a total area of 408.1 km². The city itself has a population of 15,800 inhabitants. Mail post reference of Portugal
