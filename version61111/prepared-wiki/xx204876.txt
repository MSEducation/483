[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Jataí]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1929, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Goiânia, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Jataí ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Iataiensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Jataí in the Ecclesiastical province of Goiânia in Brazil.

==History==

On 21 June 1929 Pope Pius XI established the Territorial Prelature of Jataí from the Diocese of Goiás.  Pope Pius XII elevated the prelature to the Diocese of Jataí on 26 March 1956.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.catholic-hierarchy.org/diocese/djata.html|title=Diocese of Jataí|publisher=Catholic-Hierarchy|accessdate=2014-02-16|last=|first=[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.gcatholic.org/dioceses/diocese/jata0.htm|title=Diocese of Jataí|publisher=Giga-Catholic|accessdate=2014-02-16|last=|first=[/tpl]
