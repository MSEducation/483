[[Lordville Presbyterian Church]]

CATEGORIES: Presbyterian churches in New York, Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, National Register of Historic Places in Delaware County, New York, Religious buildings completed in 1896, 19th-century Presbyterian church buildings, Churches in Delaware County, New York

Lordville Presbyterian Church is a historic Presbyterian church located on Lordville Road in Lordville in Delaware County, New York. It is a single story frame building built in 1896.  It features a steep gable roof and open, square shaped belfry.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=1623|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: Lordville Presbyterian Church|date=October 1999|accessdate=2010-02-05 |author=Mary E. Curtis|publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation[/tpl] See also: [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=1621|title=Accompanying two photos[/tpl]
It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 2000.
