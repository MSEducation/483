[[Chavarzaq, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Chavarzaq ([tpl]lang-fa|چورزق[/tpl]; also known as Chauriz and Chavarza)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3058204|Chavarzaq[/tpl] is a village in Mojezat Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,538, in 363 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
