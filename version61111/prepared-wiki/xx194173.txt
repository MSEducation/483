[[Light+building]]

CATEGORIES: Trade fairs in Germany, Culture in Frankfurt, Economy of Frankfurt, Architectural lighting design

Light+Building is a biennial architecture and technology trade fair mainly focused on the fields of building design, lighting, electro-technology and building automation held at the Messe Frankfurt in Frankfurt am Main, Germany.
As of 2008, it is the world's biggest trade fair for lighting, counting with more than 165,000 visitors in its 2008 edition.[tpl]cite web | last = | first = | authorlink = | title = light+building, International Trade Fair for Architecture and Building Technology 2008 | work = | publisher = | date = 2008-04-11 | url = http://light-building.messefrankfurt.com/servlet/com.c4u.eis.core.servlets.Media?lang=en&cguid=c4u_media_doc_media&dguid=mf_ddsweb53_3900&pc=1&app=1&mf_download-file=Download+Final+report+Light+Building+2008.pdf | format=PDF | doi = | accessdate = 2008-04-30 [/tpl] [tpl]Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot[/tpl]
In 2010, there were 2,300 exhibitors and 196,000 visitors, while half of them are foreign. Top visitors out of Germany are coming from Netherlands, Italy, Austria, France, Switzerland, Great Britain, China, Spain, Belgium and Sweden.
The next one should be from 30 March to 4 April 2014
