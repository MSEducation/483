[[Elachista contaminatella]]

CATEGORIES: Elachistidae, Animals described in 1847, Butterflies and moths of Europe, Moths of Europe, Insects of Hungary, Insects of Bulgaria, Insects of Turkey, Insects of Italy

Elachista contaminatella is a moth of the Elachistidae family that is found from France, Austria and Slovakia to the Iberian Peninsula, Sardinia and Sicily. It is also found in Bulgaria and Russia.
The wingspan is about [tpl]convert|8|mm[/tpl].
The larvae feed on Carex distans. They mine the leaves of their host plant.[tpl]cite web|url= http://www.bladmineerders.nl/minersf/lepidopteramin/elachista/contaminatella/contaminatella.htm|title=Elachista contaminatella Zeller, 1847|publisher= Bladmineerders.nl|accessdate=September 23, 2011[/tpl]
