[[Scotura annulata]]

CATEGORIES: Notodontidae

 
Scotura annulata is a moth of the Notodontidae family. It is found from Texas, south through Central America (including Panama and Costa Rica) to Argentina. 
The larvae have been recorded on Hybanthus prunifolius in Panama. Other recorded host plants include Acalypha diversifolia, Chamaedorea, Randia and Meliosma species.
