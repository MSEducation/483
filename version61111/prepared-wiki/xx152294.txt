[[5 cm KwK 38]]

CATEGORIES: Tank guns of Germany, World War II artillery of Germany, 50 mm artillery

The 5 cm KwK 38 L/42 (5 cm Kampfwagenkanone 38 L/42) was a German 5 cm cannon used as the main armament of variants of the German SdKfz.141 Panzerkampfwagen III medium tank. It was used in vehicles that saw action in the Second World War.
