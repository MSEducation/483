[[Hans Blackwood, 3rd Baron Dufferin and Claneboye]]

Hans Blackwood, 3rd Baron Dufferin and Claneboye (1758 – 18 November 1839), styled The Honourable Hans Blackwood between 1800 and 1836, was an Irish peer and politician.

==Background==

Blackwood was the fourth son of Sir John Blackwood, 2nd Baronet and Dorcas Blackwood, 1st Baroness Dufferin and Claneboye.thepeerage.com Hans Blackwood, 3rd Baron Dufferin and Claneboye

==Public life==

Blackwood represented Killyleagh in the Irish House of Commons between 1799 and 1800.leighrayment.com Irish House of Commons 1692-1800 From 1813 to 1832 he was a Commissioner of Audit of Ireland. He succeeded his elder brother James in the barony in 1836. This was an Irish peerage and did not entitle him to an automatic seat in the House of Lords.
