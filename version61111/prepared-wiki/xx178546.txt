[[Mohammed Faisal Rahman]]

CATEGORIES: Members of the Senate of Trinidad and Tobago, Living people, United National Congress politicians

Mohammed Faisal Rahman ([tpl]lang-ur|[tpl]nastaliq|محمد فیصل رحمان[/tpl][/tpl] [tpl]lang-hi|मोहम्मद फ़ैसल रहमान[/tpl]), is a writer and has written several books on economics, business and religion.
In 1995 he founded the National Transformation Movement.
Appointed Senator as of December 17, 2007, by the United National Congress.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ttparliament.org/members.php?mid=26&pid=5&id=MFR|title=Mohammed Faisal Rahman > 1Member of the 9th Republican Parliament (17 Dec 2007 – 08 Apr 2010): Parliament of Trinidad and Tobago|work=Parliament of Trinidad and Tobago|archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/5vmbsNGNs|archivedate=16 January 2011[/tpl]
His term end 8 April 2010.
He died at the age of 72 on 17 September 2012.[tpl]fact|date=October 2012[/tpl]
