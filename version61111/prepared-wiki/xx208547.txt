[[Rastriya Praja Party]]

CATEGORIES: Political parties in Nepal, Political parties established in 1952

Rastriya Praja Party was a political party in Nepal, founded in 1952 when Matrika Prasad Koirala broke away from the Nepali Congress. As the leader of the party, M.P. Koiralawas appointed prime minister by the King. The party did however, not develop any major support base of its own. A splinter-group of the party did later join the Praja Parishad of Tanka Prasad Acharya.Levi, Werner. Politics in Nepal, published in Far Eastern Survey, Vol. 25, No. 3, (Mar., 1956), pp. 39-46
