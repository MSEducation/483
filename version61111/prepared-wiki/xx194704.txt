[[Ali Gane]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Senegal

Ali Gane is a small town in western-central Senegal.Maplandia world gazetteer It is located in the Kaolack Region.
Nearby towns and villages include Gama (2.2 nm), Moukhoume (2.2 nm), Ndiayene Bagana (1.0 nm), Guissam (1.4 nm), Kebe Keur Babou (1.4 nm), Medina Diognik (1.4 nm) and Loumene (1.4 nm).Fallingrain world gazetteer
