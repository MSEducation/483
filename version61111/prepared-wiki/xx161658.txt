Lucian Goian (born 10 February 1983) is a Romanian footballer who plays for Beijing Baxy in the China League One, as a central defender.

==Career==

Born in Suceava, Romania, Goian is the younger brother of Rangers defender Dorin Goian. He started his career beside his brother, at Foresta Suceava. He followed in his brother's footsteps, joining Ceahlăul Piatra Neamţ, but then Dorin arrived in 2005 at Steaua Bucureşti, and Lucian joined their greatest rivals, Dinamo. He signed for Dinamo, after scoring against Steaua, when he played for Ceahlăul. He played for five seasons with Dinamo, with a break for a year when he returned at Ceahlăul. He also played 6 matches in the UEFA Cup for Dinamo.
