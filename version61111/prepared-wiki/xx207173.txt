Cameron "Cammy" Bell (born 18 September 1986) is a Scottish professional football player. Bell is a goalkeeper and currently plays for Scottish League One club Rangers. He has represented Scotland once, in 2010.

==Club career==

===Early years===

Bell was born in Dumfries and was on the books of local club Queen of the South as a youngster and was also part of the youth set up at Annan Athletic. Bell moved from Queens to Kilmarnock in 2002.[tpl]cite news |title=Keeper Bell makes Queens return |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/q/queen_of_the_south/7526867.stm |work=BBC Sport |accessdate=15 May 2012 |date=26 July 2008 |quote=The Dumfries-born 21-year-old began his career with Queens before moving to Rugby Park in 2002.[/tpl]  In 2006, he extended his contract with Kilmarnock although he had not yet appeared for the first team.[tpl]cite news|title=Bell extends contract with Killie|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/k/kilmarnock/4645436.stm|work=BBC Sport|accessdate=15 May 2012|date=24 January 2006[/tpl]
