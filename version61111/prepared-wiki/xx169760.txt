Timothy Andrew "Tim" Drew (born August 31, 1978) is a former American Major League Baseball player.

==Baseball career==

Drew and Stephen Drew, Tim was a pitcher who played for the Cleveland Indians ([tpl]By|2000[/tpl]-[tpl]By|2001[/tpl]), Montreal Expos ([tpl]By|2002[/tpl]-[tpl]By|2003[/tpl]), and Atlanta Braves ([tpl]By|2004[/tpl]).[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.baseball-reference.com/d/drewti01.shtml|title= Baseball Reference-Tim Drew Statistics|accessdate=2008-06-08[/tpl]
