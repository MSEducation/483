[[Project (album)]]

CATEGORIES: Greg Howe albums, Richie Kotzen albums, 1997 albums, Shrapnel Records albums, Collaborative albums

Project is the second collaborative studio album by guitarists Greg Howe and Richie Kotzen, released on November 11, 1997 through Shrapnel Records. A previous collaboration between Howe and Kotzen, Tilt (1995), had sold well and thus resulted in a second album.Malusardi, Guglielmo (October 2006). "Interview: Greg Howe". Guitar Nine Records.
