[[Abdullah Mayouf]]

CATEGORIES: 1953 births, Kuwaiti footballers, Kuwait international footballers, Association football defenders, 1982 FIFA World Cup players, Living people

Abdullah Mayouf (born 3 December 1953) is a Kuwaiti football defender who played for Kuwait in the 1982 FIFA World Cup.1982 FIFA World Cup Spain He also played for Kazma Sporting Club.
