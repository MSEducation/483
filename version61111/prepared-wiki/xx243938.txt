[[Dmytro Pryma]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 births, Living people, FC Kremin Kremenchuk players, Ukrainian footballers

Dmytro Anatoliovych Pryma ([tpl]lang-uk|Дмитро Анатолійович Прима[/tpl]; born 13 March 1985 in Kiev, in the Ukrainian SSR of the Soviet Union) is a Ukrainian football striker currently playing for Ukrainian Second League club Stal Dniprodzerzhynsk.

==Club history==

Dmytro Pryma began his football career in Dynamo Youth in Kiev.
