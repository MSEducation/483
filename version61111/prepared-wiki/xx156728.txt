[[Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Lomé]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Togo, Archbishops of Lomé, Religious organizations established in 1892, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 19th century

The Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Lomé ([tpl]lang-la|Lomen(sis)[/tpl]) is the Metropolitan See for the Ecclesiastical province of Lomé in Togo.

==Special churches==
