[[Taishin Financial Holdings]]

CATEGORIES: Banks of Taiwan, Banks established in 2002, 2002 establishments in Taiwan

Founded in 2002 Taishin Financial Holdings (or Taishin Financial Holding Co., Ltd.) ([tpl]TSE|2887[/tpl]) is headquartered in Taipei, Taiwan. Taishin Financial Holdings consists of subsidiaries in the sectors of banking, securities, bills finance, assets management, and venture capital.
