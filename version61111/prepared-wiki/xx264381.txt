[[Alebtong]]

CATEGORIES: Alebtong District, Populated places in Uganda, Cities in the Great Rift Valley, Northern Region, Uganda

Alebtong, sometimes spelled Alebtongo, is a town in Northern Uganda. It is the chief municipal, administrative and commercial centre of Alebtong District. The district is named after the town.

==Location==

Alebtong lies on the main road between the city of Lira, in Lira District to the west, and the town of Omoro in Alebtong District to the east.
