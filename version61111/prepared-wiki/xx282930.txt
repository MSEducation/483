[[Continental Divide, New Mexico]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in McKinley County, New Mexico, Unincorporated communities in New Mexico

Continental Divide is an unincorporated community in McKinley County, New Mexico, United States. Continental Divide is located along Interstate 40 [tpl]convert|25.1|mi|km[/tpl] east-southeast of Gallup.
