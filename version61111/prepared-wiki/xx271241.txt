[[Chakrayapalem]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Guntur district

Chakrayapalem is a village and gram panchayat in Kollipara mandal, Guntur district, Andhra Pradesh, India.[tpl]cite web |url=http://offerings.nic.in/directory/adminreps/viewGPmapcvills.asp?gpcode=203056&rlbtype=V |title=List of Census Villages mapped for :CHAKRAYAPALEM Gram Panchayat,KOLLIPARA,GUNTUR,ANDHRA PRADESH |work=National Panchayat Directory |publisher=Ministry of Panchayati Raj |accessdate=26 September 2011 [/tpl]
Chakrayapalem is 34.3 km distance from its District Main City Guntur. And 270 km distance from its State Main City Hyderabad.
Other villages in Kollipara Mandal are Annavaram, Annavarapu Lanke, Athalurivari Palem, Bommuvani Palem, Chemudupadu ....etc.
Near by towns are Vemuru(12.1 k.m.), Tenali(7 k.m.), Kollur(12.4 k.m.), Duggirala(13.6 k.m.).
