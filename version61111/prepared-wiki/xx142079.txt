[[38 Counts of Battery]]

CATEGORIES: Pig Destroyer albums, 2001 compilation albums

38 Counts of Battery is a compilation album by the deathgrind band Pig Destroyer. The album is made up of the entire "Explosions in Ward 6" album, parts of the split "Orchid / Pig Destroyer", and parts of the band's 1997 "Demo". 38 Counts also comes in edited version, but it is hard to come by.
