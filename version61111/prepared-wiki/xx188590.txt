[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Amparo]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Campinas, Religious organizations established in 1997, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Amparo ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Amparensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Amparo in the Ecclesiastical province of Campinas in Brazil.
