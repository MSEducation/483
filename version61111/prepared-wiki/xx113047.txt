[[HMS Psyche (1814)]]

CATEGORIES: Frigates of the Royal Navy

HMS ''Psyche'' was to have been a 54-gun fourth rate frigate of the Royal Navy. She was built at the Kingston Royal Naval Dockyard in Kingston, Ontario during the War of 1812, using frames shipped from Britain and assembled in Canada. The ship was not completed until after the end of the war. Under the Rushâ€“Bagot Treaty, the frigate was disarmed and laid up in Kingston. The hulk was sold in 1837.
