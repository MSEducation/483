[[Gol og Hemsedal]]

CATEGORIES: Former municipalities of Norway

Gol og Hemsedal is a former municipality in Buskerud county, Norway.[ref]

The municipality of Goel og Hemsedal was created in 1837 formannskapsdistrikt. According to the 1835 census the municipality had a population of 3,645.[ref]

On 1 January 1897 Gol og Hemsedal was split to create two municipalities: Gol with a population of 2,384 and Hemsedal with a population of 1,499.[ref]
