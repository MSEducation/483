[[Heyington railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1890

Heyington railway station is located on the Glen Waverley line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the eastern Melbourne suburb of Toorak opening on 24 March 1890.Heyington Vicsig

==Platforms & services==

Heyington has two side platforms. It is serviced by Metro Trains' Glen Waverley line services.Glen Waverley Line timetable Public Transport Victoria
