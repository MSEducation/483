[[The Golden Harvest]]

CATEGORIES: 1940s novel stubs, 1944 novels, Modernist novels, Novels by Jorge Amado, Brazilian novels, Portuguese-language novels, Novels set in Bahia

The Golden Harvest (Portuguese: São Jorge dos Ilhéus) is a Brazilian Modernist novel. It was written by Jorge Amado in 1944 and published in English in 1992.
