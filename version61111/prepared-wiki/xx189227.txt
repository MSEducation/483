[[Saxifraga granulata]]

CATEGORIES: Saxifraga, Plants described in 1753

[tpl]italic title[/tpl]

Saxifraga granulata, commonly called meadow saxifrage, is a species of flowering plant of the genus Saxifraga in the family Saxifragaceae.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.gbif.org/species/7339311|title=Saxifraga granulata Hook.f. & Thomson|work=GBIF Backbone Taxonomy|publisher=Global Biodiversity Information Facility|accessdate=9 January 2014|archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/6MVAEyZCK|archivedate=9 January 2014[/tpl]

==Taxonomy==

Saxifraga granulata was first formally described by the famous British botanist Sir Joseph Dalton Hooker and his friend Thomas Thomson in 1753 (Sp. pl. 1:403.)
