[[Hughesdale railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1925

Hughesdale railway station is located on the Cranbourne and Pakenham lines in Victoria, Australia. It serves the south-eastern Melbourne suburb of Hughesdale opening on 28 February 1925.Hughesdale Vicsig
The station and suburb were named in honour of James Vincent Hughes, mayor of Oakleigh from 1924 to 1925 who, with the Poath Road Railway Station League, lobbied heavily for a new station for the area. Hughesdale was once the junction for the Outer Circle and Rosstown railway lines.
