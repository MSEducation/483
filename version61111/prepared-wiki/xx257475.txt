[[1981 Winnipeg Blue Bombers season]]

CATEGORIES: Winnipeg Blue Bombers seasons

The 1981 Winnipeg Blue Bombers finished in 2nd place in the West Division with a 11–5 record. They appeared in the West Semi-Final but lost 15–11 to the BC Lions.
