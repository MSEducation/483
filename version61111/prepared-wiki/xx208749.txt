[[Wedding in Malinovka]]

CATEGORIES: 1967 films, 1967 in the Soviet Union, Lenfilm films, Soviet films, 1960s musical comedy films, Russian-language films

Wedding in Malinovka ([tpl]lang-ru|Свадьба в Малиновке[/tpl], Svadba v Malinovke) is a 1967 Soviet musical comedy film directed by Andrei Tutyshkin based on an operetta by Boris Aleksandrov adapted by Leonid Yukhvid. Produced by Semyon Malkin (Lenfilm). Runtime - 95 min.
Cinematography by Vyacheslav Fastovich. Editing by Mariya Pen. Music by Boris Aleksandrov performed by A. Fedotov (conductor) and S. Orlansky (musical director).
