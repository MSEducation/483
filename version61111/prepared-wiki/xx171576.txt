[[Donald Little]]

CATEGORIES: Judges in Manitoba, University of Manitoba alumni, People from Brandon, Manitoba, Living people

Donald Mark Little, of Brandon, was appointed a judge of the Family Division of the Court of Queen's Bench of Manitoba on February 12, 1998. He replaced Madam Justice C. Davidson, who had died. 
Mr. Justice Little graduated in law from the University of Manitoba in 1981 and was called to the Bar of Manitoba in 1982. He had been practising law with the firm of Roy, Johnston & Company, where he had been a partner since 1983. He practiced primarily civil litigation, corporate and commercial law, as well as family law. 
Justice Little was president of the Law Society of Manitoba, and is a past president of the Federation of Law Societies of Canada and a Life Bencher of the Law Society.
