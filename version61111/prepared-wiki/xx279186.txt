[[Dulanab]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Dulanab ([tpl]lang-fa|دولاناب[/tpl], also Romanized as Dūlānāb; also known as Dolanab and Dūlehnāb)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3061816|Dulanab[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 689, in 156 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
