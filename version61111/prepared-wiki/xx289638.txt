[[Mamaqan, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Mamaqan ([tpl]lang-fa|ممقان[/tpl], also Romanized as Māmāqān; also known as Ḩamaqān, Madzha-Khan, and Mamaqāh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3073846|Mamaqan[/tpl] is a village in Aq Bolagh Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
