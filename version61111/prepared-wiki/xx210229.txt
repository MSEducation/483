[[Peng'an County]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Sichuan

Peng'an County (Chinese: 蓬安县; Pinyin: Péng'ān Xiàn) is a county in Nanchong, Sichuan, China.
Peng'an has an area of 1,334 square kilometers and a population of 680,000 as of 2004.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.xzqh.org/QUHUA/51sc/1323pa.htm|title=Profile of Peng'an County|publisher=|date=|accessdate=2008-07-27|language=Chinese[/tpl]
