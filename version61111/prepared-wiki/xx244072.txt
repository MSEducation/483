[[Peter Haynes (priest)]]

CATEGORIES: 1925 births, Living people, Alumni of Selwyn College, Cambridge, Archdeacons of Wells, Church of England deans, Deans of Hereford

Peter Haynes was an Anglican priest in the late 20th century.
He was born on 24 April 1925,”Who's Who 1992 “(London, A & C Black ISBN 0-7136-3514-2) educated at Selwyn College, Cambridge ”Debrett's People of Today 1992” p914(London, Debrett's) ISBN 1-870520-09-2) and ordained in 1953.Crockford's Clerical Directory 1975-76 London: Oxford University Press, 1976 ISBN 0-19-200008-X He held curacies at Stokesley and Hessle after which he was Vicar of Drypool. From 1963 to 1970 he was Bishop’s Chaplain for Youth in the Diocese of Bath and Wells then Vicar of Glastonbury. In 1974 he became Archdeacon of Wells and a Canon Residentiary at the Cathedral. In 1982 he was appointed Dean of Hereford,Hereford Cathedral: a history” Aylmer, G/Tiller,J: London, Hambledon, 2000 ISBN 1-85285-194-5 a post he held for a decade.
