[[Johan Ernst Berg]]

CATEGORIES: 1768 births, 1828 deaths, Members of the Parliament of Norway, County Governors of Norway, Nordland politicians

Johan Ernst Berg (1768–1828) was a Norwegian politician.
From 1815 to 1828 he was County Governor of Nordlands Amt (today named Nordland).
He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament in 1815 and 1826, representing Nordlands Amt.Johan Ernst Berg — Norwegian Social Science Data Services (NSD)
