[[Farfantepenaeus]]

CATEGORIES: Dendrobranchiata

[tpl]Italic title[/tpl]

Farfantepenaeus is a genus of prawns in the family Penaeidae. Its eight species[tpl]cite journal |journal = Raffles Bulletin of Zoology |year=2009 |volume = Suppl. 21 |pages=1–109 |title = A classification of living and fossil genera of decapod crustaceans |author=Sammy De Grave, N. Dean Pentcheff, Shane T. Ahyong et al. |url=http://rmbr.nus.edu.sg/rbz/biblio/s21/s21rbz1-109.pdf |format=PDF[/tpl] were formerly included in the genus Penaeus.[tpl]cite journal |author=Yu Shanshan, Kong Xiaoyu, Li Yulong & Xu Hui |title=The complete sequence of mitochondrial COII gene of Fenneropenaeus chinensis and its applicability as a marker for phylogenetic analysis |journal=Journal of Ocean University of China (English Edition) |volume=6 |issue=2 |year=2007 |doi=10.1007/s11802-007-0187-2 |pages=187–192[/tpl] It was first published as a genus name in 1972 by Rudolf N. Burukovsky, but without the necessary designation of a type species. That situation was corrected by the same author in 1997.[tpl]cite journal |author=Rudolf N. Burukovsky |year=1997 |title=Selection of a type species for Farfantepenaeus Burukovsky (Crustacea: Decapoda: Penaeidae) |journal=Proceedings of the Biological Society of Washington |volume=110 |issue=1 |page=154 |doi= |url=http://biostor.org/reference/81136[/tpl] The name Farfantepenaeus commemorates the Cuban carcinologist Isabel Pérez Farfante.
