[[Gmina Pokój]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Opole Voivodeship, Namysłów County

Gmina Pokój is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Namysłów County, Opole Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Its seat is the village of Pokój, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|20|km|mi|0[/tpl] south-east of Namysłów and [tpl]convert|29|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of the regional capital Opole.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|132.97|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 5,573.
The gmina contains part of the protected area called Stobrawa Landscape Park.

==Villages==

Gmina Pokój contains the villages and settlements of Dąbrówka Dolna, Domaradz, Domaradzka Kuźnia, Fałkowice, Jagienna, Kopalina, Kozuby, Krogulna, Krzywa Góra, Ładza, Lubnów, Paryż, Pokój, Siedlice, Świercowskie, Żabiniec, Zawiść and Zieleniec.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Pokój is bordered by the gminas of Dobrzeń Wielki, Domaszowice, Murów, Popielów, Świerczów and Wołczyn.
