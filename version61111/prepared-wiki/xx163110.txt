June Alexandra Croft (born 17 June 1963) is a retired freestyle swimmer from the United Kingdom, who represented her native country at three consecutive Summer Olympics, starting in 1980. At her Olympic debut in Moscow she won the silver medal in the women's 4×100 m medley relay, followed by the bronze in the women's 400 m freestyle four years later in Los Angeles, California.
