[[Cathal Sheridan]]

CATEGORIES: Meath inter-county Gaelic footballers, Meath inter-county hurlers, Moynalvey Gaelic footballers, Moynalvey hurlers, Kildare inter-county hurlers, Kilcock Gaelic footballers, Living people, Year of birth missing (living people)

Cathal Sheridan is a former dual player from County Meath, Ireland. He played both Gaelic football and hurling at all levels with Meath and hurling with Kildare.[tpl]cite web|url=http://hoganstand.com/meath/ArticleForm.aspx?ID=21707|title=Sheridan is a Kildare hotshot|publisher=Hoganstand.com|date=2001-04-24|accessdate=2011-01-04[/tpl] He won All Ireland football medals at Minor, Under 21 and Senior with Meath in the 1990s as well as a Junior medal in 2003.  He played his club football and hurling with Moynalvy[tpl]cite web|url=http://hoganstand.com/meath/ArticleForm.aspx?ID=21520|title=Sheridan stuck in limbo|publisher=Hoganstand.com|date=2002-03-27|accessdate=2011-01-04[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://hoganstand.com/meath/ArticleForm.aspx?ID=59486|title=Sheridan hits late Moynalvey winner|publisher=Hoganstand.com|date=2006-04-09|accessdate=2011-01-04[/tpl] early in his career, transferring to Kilcock, when his job as a Garda required him to move to Kildare.
