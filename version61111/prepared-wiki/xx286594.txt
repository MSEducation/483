[[BAYADA Home Health Care]]

CATEGORIES: Medical and health organizations based in the United States

BAYADA Home Health Care is an international, privately held home health care company. The organization has previously been known as RN Homecare and Bayada Nurses. While the headquarters is based in Moorestown Township, New Jersey, the company employs over 18,000 nursing support staff in 250 offices throughout the United States and India. The organization provides in-home nursing services, which encompass physical, occupational and speech therapy. Additional services include medical social work, home health aides and hospice support. Since 2004, the organization has co-sponsored the "Bayada Award for Technological Innovation in Nursing Education and Practice", which recognizes individuals in the nursing field who work to introduce advancements in technology in support of nursing education and the nursing industry overall.

==International expansion==

In 2012, BAYADA opened their first international office, in Hyderabad, India.  Faced with the challenge of succeeding as a standalone US company in the Indian healthcare market, in 2013 the company bought a 26% stake in Chennai, India-based India Home Health Care (IHHC). IHCC has operations in Chennai and Bangalore with a network of 300 nurses.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.thehindu.com/news/cities/chennai/doorstep-healthcare-service-picks-up-in-chennai/article5726740.ece |title=Doorstep healthcare service picks up in Chennai |publisher=The Hindu |date=2014-02-26 |accessdate=2014-02-26[/tpl] Bayada has also launched IHHC’s NRI services through its offices in the US.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bayada.com/india/india_home.asp |title=Bayada NRI Service |publisher=BAYADA Home Health Care |date=2014-02-24 |accessdate=2014-02-24[/tpl] Bayada and India Home Health Care have announced an investment of $10 million to create a pan india presence by 2016 with a team size of 3000 and revenue of $12 million.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.mydigitalfc.com/news/us%E2%80%99-bayada-invest-10m-pan-india-expansion-987 |title=US’ Bayada to invest $10m for pan-India expansion |publisher=Financial Chronicle |date=2014-02-26 |accessdate=2014-02-26[/tpl]

==Philanthropy==

In 2004, BAYADA, in conjunction with Drexel University's College of Nursing and Health Professions, created the BAYADA Award for Technological Innovation in Nursing Education and Practice. The award honors nursing educators and practicing nurses whose innovation leads to improved patient care or improved nursing education.[tpl]cite web|title=About BAYADA Awards|url=http://www.drexel.edu/cnhp/newsEvents/events/BayadaAwards/|publisher=drexel.edu|accessdate=26 August 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://news.nurse.com/article/20120827/PA02/308270019 |title=Drexel announces winners of Bayada Award For Technological Innovation | Nurse.com News |publisher=News.nurse.com |date=2012-08-27 |accessdate=2013-09-05[/tpl]
BAYADA is a Gold Team National Sponsor for the ALS Association, and is also working with local families to offer care at the local level, in addition to developing training programs to help caregivers better serve ALS patients.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.alsa.org/news/archive/bayada-home-health-care.html|title=BAYADA Home Health Care Partners with The ALS Association to Help Families with Lou Gehrig’s Disease and Support the Walk to Defeat ALS |publisher=ALSA.org |date=2013-05-06|accessdate=2014-01-31[/tpl]
