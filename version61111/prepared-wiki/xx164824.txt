[[Holger Löwenadler]]

CATEGORIES: 1904 births, 1977 deaths, Swedish actors, People from Jönköping, Eugene O'Neill Award winners, 20th-century Swedish male actors

Holger Löwenadler (1 April 1904 – 18 June 1977) was a Swedish film actor.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.sfi.se/en-GB/Swedish-film-database/Item/?type=PERSON&itemid=59637 |title=Holger Löwenadler |accessdate=2013-02-06|work=Swedish Film Database[/tpl]  He starred in Ingmar Bergman's 1947 A Ship to India.  He appeared in the 1951 Divorced, which was written by Bergman. Other appearances include Lacombe Lucien (1974).
