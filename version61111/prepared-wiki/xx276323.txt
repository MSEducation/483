[[Chris Piesley]]

CATEGORIES: 1992 births, Living people, People from Chatham, Kent, English cricketers, Kent cricketers

Christopher Damien Piesley (born 12 March 1992) is an English cricketer.  Piesley is a left-handed batsman who bowls right-arm off break.  He was born in Chatham, Kent and educated at Fulston Manor School in Sittingbourne.[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/126/126154/126154.html|title=Player profile: Chris Piesley|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=26 September 2011[/tpl]
Piesley made his first-class debut for Kent against the touring Pakistanis, being dismissed for a duck in Kent's first-innings by Mohammad Amir, but he followed this up by making 43 runs in Kent's second-innings, before being dismissed by Shoaib Malik.[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Scorecards/257/257219.html|title=Kent v Pakistanis, 2010|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=26 September 2011[/tpl]  He made two further first-class appearances in the 2011 season, against Loughborough MCCU and Gloucestershire in the County Championship.[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/126/126154/First-Class_Matches.html|title=First-Class Matches played by Chris Piesley|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=26 September 2011[/tpl]  To date he has scored 69 runs at an average of 13.80.[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/126/126154/f_Batting_by_Team.html|title=First-class Batting and Fielding For Each Team by Chris Piesley|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=26 September 2011[/tpl]  Piesley also made his List A debut during the 2011 season, which came against the Netherlands in the Clydesdale Bank 40.[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/126/126154/List_A_Matches.html|title=List A Matches played by Chris Piesley|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=26 September 2011[/tpl]  In what was his only appearance that season in that format, he scored 4 runs before being dismissed by Shane Mott.[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Scorecards/316/316282.html|title=Netherlands v Kent, 2011 Clydesdale Bank 40|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=26 September 2011[/tpl]
