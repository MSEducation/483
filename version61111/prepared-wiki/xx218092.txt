[[Umurbek Bazarbayev]]

Umurbek Bazarbayev (born September 17, 1981  in Ilyaly, Dasoguz Province, Turkmenistan) is a Turkmenistani weightlifter. His personal best is 303 kg, achieved at the 2011 World Championships.
He ranked sixth in the 62 kg category at the 2004 Summer Olympics, with a total of 287.5 kg.[tpl]cite web|title=BAZAARBAYEV Umurbek|publisher=International Weightlifting Federation|url=http://www.iwf.net/results/results_athlete_e.php?compid=464[/tpl]
At the 2008 Asian Championships he won the overall bronze medal, with a total of 296 kg.[tpl]cite web|title=BAZARBAYEV Umurbek|publisher=International Weightlifting Federation|url=http://www.iwf.net/results/results_athlete_e.php?compid=1110[/tpl]
He competed in Weightlifting at the 2008 Summer Olympics in the 62 kg division but did not finish.
He is 5 ft 4 inches tall and weighs 143 lb.
