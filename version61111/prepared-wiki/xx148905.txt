[[South Lakeland Council election, 2000]]

CATEGORIES: Council elections in South Lakeland, English local elections, 2000

The 2000 South Lakeland Council election took place on 4 May 2000 to elect members of South Lakeland District Council in Cumbria, England. One third of the council was up for election and the council stayed under no overall control.[tpl]cite web| url = http://news.bbc.co.uk/hi/english/static/uk_politics/vote2000/locals/112.stm | title = South Lakeland | accessdate = 2009-11-10 | publisher = BBC News Online[/tpl]
