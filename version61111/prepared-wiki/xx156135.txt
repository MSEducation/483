[[Hammersmith & Chiswick railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Disused railway stations in Hounslow, Railway stations opened in 1858

[tpl]Other uses|Hammersmith station (disambiguation)[tpl]![/tpl]Hammersmith station|Chiswick railway station[/tpl]

Hammersmith & Chiswick railway station was in west London. Originally named "Hammersmith" it became "Hammersmith & Chiswick" in 1880.[ref]
</ref>

==History==

The station was opened on 8 April 1858 by the North & South Western Junction Railway (N&SWJR) on the site of a goods yard which had opened on 1 May 1857 on Chiswick High Road in what was then a rural area.[ref]
