[[Minamimaki, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Minamimaki|南牧村|Minamimaki-mura[/tpl] is a village located in Minamisaku District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the village has an estimated population of 3,490 and a density of 26.22 persons per km². The total area is 133.10 km².
