[[1976 French Open – Men's Singles]]

CATEGORIES: 1976 French Open, French Open by year – Men's Singles, 1976 Grand Prix (tennis)

Adriano Panatta defeated Harold Solomon 6–1, 6–4, 4–6, 7–6 in the final to win the Men's Singles tennis title at the 1976 French Open.
