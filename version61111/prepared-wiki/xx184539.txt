[[Basic Education High School No. 1 Kyaukse]]

CATEGORIES: High schools in Mandalay Region

Basic Education High School No. 1 Kyaukse ([tpl]lang-my|အခြေခံ ပညာ အထက်တန်း ကျောင်း အမှတ် (၁) ကျောက်ဆည်; abbreviated to အ.ထ.က. (၁) ကျောက်ဆည်[/tpl]) is a public high school located in Kyaukse, Mandalay Region, Myanmar. The school offers classes from kindergarten to Tenth Standard.
