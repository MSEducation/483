[[Mohammed El Filali]]

Mohammed El Filali ([tpl]lang-ar|محمد الفيلالي[/tpl]) (born 9 July 1945) is a Moroccan football forward who played for the Morocco in the 1970 FIFA World Cup.1970 FIFA World Cup Mexico He also played for MC Oujda. Also, El Filali was a firefighter.
