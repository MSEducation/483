[[Spas Genov]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Bulgarian boxers

[tpl]MedalCountry | [tpl]BUL[/tpl] [/tpl]
Spas Genov is a Bulgarian boxer best known to win Silver at welterweight at the Euro 2006.
He beat Kakhaber Zhvania but lost the final to Andrey Balanov.

==Pro==

He turned pro in 2008 and won his first bout.
