[[Rinan Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Taichung

Rinan Station ([tpl]zh|t=日南車站|hp=Rìnán Chēzhàn|tp=Rìhnán Chejhàn[/tpl]) is a railway station on the Taiwan Railway Administration (TRA) Western Line (Coast Line) located in Dajia District, Taichung, Taiwan.

==Structure==

One of five surviving examples of wooden structured stations on the Coast Line in Taiwan.[tpl]clarify|date=January 2011[/tpl]
