[[Bagh Bahadur]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 films, Indian films, Bengali-language films, 1980s drama films, Films directed by Buddhadev Dasgupta, Best Feature Film National Film Award winners

Bagh Bahadur ([tpl]lang-bn|বাঘ বাহাদুর[/tpl], translation: The Tiger Dancer) is a 1989 Bengali drama film directed and written by Buddhadev Dasgupta. The film is about a man who paints himself as a tiger and dances in a village in Bengal. The movie  conveys a strong message as it illustrates the hardships of rural village life in Bengal.
