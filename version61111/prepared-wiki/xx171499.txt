[[Peter Wear]]

CATEGORIES: Australian journalists, Australian columnists, Australian satirists, 1949 births, Living people

Peter Wear is a Brisbane-based writer whose columns appear regularly in The Courier-Mail. Wear ran a long-running satire on Queensland politics with the major role played by "President for Life Mbeattie" - a reference to Premier Peter Beattie's longevity in office.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.smh.com.au/news/national/bulletproof-beattie-cruises-to-fourth-victory-in-a-row/2006/09/09/1157222376380.html|title=Bulletproof Beattie cruises to fourth victory in a row|date=10 September 2006|publisher=Sydney Morning Herald[/tpl]
Wear also wrote "The Madding of Daniel O'Hooligan" Published by University of Queensland Press in 1991.
