[[Massimo Marchiori]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1970 births

Massimo Marchiori (Padua, 1970) is an Italian mathematician and computer scientist.

==Biography==

In July, 2004, he was awarded the TR35 prize by Technology Review (the best 35 researchers in the world under the age of 35).[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.technologyreview.com/tr35/profile.aspx?TRID=174 |title=TR35: Massimo Marchiori, 34 |publisher=Technology Review |date= |accessdate=2012-07-06[/tpl]
He is Professor in Computer Science at the University of Padua, and Research Scientist at MIT’s Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (CSAIL) in the World Wide Web Consortium.[tpl]Citation needed|date=June 2012[/tpl]
He was the creator of HyperSearch, a search engine where the results were based not only on single page ranks, but on the relationship between single pages and the rest of the Web. Afterwards, Google co-founders Larry Page and Sergey Brin cited HyperSearch when they introduced PageRank.[tpl]cite web|author=Page, Lawrence and Brin, Sergey and Motwani, Rajeev and Winograd, Terry|title=The PageRank Citation Ranking: Bringing Order to the Web.|url=http://ilpubs.stanford.edu:8090/422/1/1999-66.pdf|format=PDF|date=January 29, 1998|publisher=Stanford University|accessdate=May 12, 2011[/tpl]
In April 2010 he became the Chief Technology Officer of Atomium Culture.[tpl]cite web|url=http://atomiumculture.eu/node/34 |title=About AC | Atomium Culture |publisher=Atomiumculture.eu |date= |accessdate=2012-07-06[/tpl]
He was the creator of Volunia, launched on February 2012.[tpl]cite web|author=Giovanni Caprara |url=http://www.corriere.it/english/12_febbraio_07/italy-answer-google_836e4e04-5187-11e1-bb26-b734ef1e73a5.shtml |title=Volunia, Italy’s Answer to Google - Corriere della Sera |publisher=Corriere.it |date=2009-12-24 |accessdate=2012-07-06[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://searchengineland.com/volunia-a-social-search-engine-says-the-web-has-come-alive-110462 |title=Volunia, A Social Search Engine, Invites Public To Kick The Tires |publisher=Searchengineland.com |date=2012-02-07 |accessdate=2012-07-06[/tpl] On the 8th of June 2012 Marchiori announced, with an open letter,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.math.unipd.it/~massimo/2012/left-volunia.html |title=Perché lascio Volunia |publisher=Math.unipd.it |date= |accessdate=2012-07-06[/tpl][tpl]cite web|author=Di Alessia Manfredi |url=http://www.repubblica.it/tecnologia/2012/06/08/news/marchiori_lascia_volunia-36804600/ |title=L'amaro addio di Marchiori a Volunia "Qualcuno vuole decidere senza di me" |publisher=Repubblica.it |date= |accessdate=2012-07-06[/tpl][tpl]dead link|date=July 2012[/tpl] that he had been excluded from the CTO position in the company "because someone else wants to do it instead of me. This person wants to decide everything, without me. And so, he put himself into my shoes, commanding me to step aside".
