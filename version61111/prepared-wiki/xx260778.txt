[[Buholmråsa Lighthouse]]

CATEGORIES: Lighthouses completed in 1917, Towers completed in 1917, Osen, Lighthouses in Sør-Trøndelag

The Buholmråsa lighthouse ([tpl]lang-no|Buholmråsa fyr[/tpl]) is a lighthouse in the municipality of Osen in Sør-Trøndelag county, Norway.  The lighthouse is located in the Svesfjorden on the small island Sønnaholmen about [tpl]convert|2|km[/tpl] northwest of Seter.  
The [tpl]convert|23.5|m|adj=on[/tpl] tall lighthouse is red with one white stripe and it can be seen for [tpl]convert|17.1|nmi|km mi[/tpl].  It was built in 1917 and automated in 1994.  The Kya lighthouse lies on a small island in the ocean [tpl]convert|13.5|km[/tpl] to the northwest of Buholmråsa lighthouse.[tpl]cite book|year=2008|url=http://www.kystverket.no/arch/_img/9719183.pdf|format=PDF|author=Kystverket|authorlink=Norwegian Coastal Administration|language=Norwegian|title=Norske Fyrliste 2008|isbn=978-82-450-0628-5[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.snl.no/Buholmr%C3%A5sa_fyr|title=Buholmråsa fyr|author=Store norske leksikon|authorlink=Store norske leksikon|language=Norwegian|accessdate=2010-12-31[/tpl]
