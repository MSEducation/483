[[Qaziabad, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Qaziabad ([tpl]lang-fa|قاضي اباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Qāẕīābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3766545|Qaziabad[/tpl] is a village in Mojezat Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 70, in 19 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
