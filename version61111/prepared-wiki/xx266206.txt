[[Funastrum]]

CATEGORIES: Asclepiadoideae

Funastrum is a genus of flowering plant in the dogbane family, Apocynaceae. The name is derived from the Latin word funis, meaning "rope", and astrum, alluding to the twining stems.[tpl]cite book|author=Daniel F. Austin|title=Baboquivari Mountain Plants: Identification, Ecology, and Ethnobotany|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=nnFVFdp0dWIC&pg=PA49|accessdate=30 August 2012|date=15 May 2010|publisher=University of Arizona Press|isbn=978-0-8165-2837-0|page=49[/tpl] Members of the genus are commonly known as twinevines.[tpl]ITIS |id=564853 |taxon=Funastrum |accessdate=2011-09-22[/tpl]
