[[Adnan Mohamed Muthasim]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1997 births, Maldivian swimmers

Mohamed Muthasim Adnan, (born 1997) is a Maldivian swimmer.http://www.bcn2013.com/en/swimming/detail/member/mohamed-muthasim-adnan He competed in the 50 m, 100 m butterfly and 50 m freestyle at the 2012 FINA World Swimming Championships (25 m) and in the 50m freestyle, 50 m butterfly and 100 m individual medley events at the 2013 Asian Indoor and Martial Arts Games. Mohamed Muthasim Adnan also took part in the 50 m freestyle and 50 m butterfly events at the 2013 World Aquatics Championships.
