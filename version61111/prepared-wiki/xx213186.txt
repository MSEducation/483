[[Ministry of Industrial Provisioning]]

CATEGORIES: Government ministries of Norway, 1920 disestablishments, Government agencies established in 1917

The Royal Norwegian Ministry of Industrial Provisioning ([tpl]lang-no|Industriforsyningsdepartementet[/tpl]) was a Norwegian ministry that existed from 1917 to 1920.
It was established on 30 April 1917, during the first World War. It ceased to exist on 30 April 1920. Its tasks were transferred to the Ministries of Defence, Finance, Trade and Provisioning. Ministries since 1814 - Government.no
The heads of the Ministry of Industrial Provisioning were: Torolf Prytz (1917-1918), Nils Ihlen (1918) and Haakon Hauan (1918-1920). Ministry of Industrial Provisioning, Councillor of State - Government.no
