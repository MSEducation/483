[[Nasher clan]]

CATEGORIES: Ghilzai Pashtun tribes

Nasher is a noble Afghan family of the Kharoti (Ghilzai) tribe, living in the North Konduz and South Ghazni of Afghanistan, in Konduz and Takhar.
The city of Shere Khan Bandar was named after Shere Khan Nasher, the town of Qal`eh-ye Nasher was named after the family.
