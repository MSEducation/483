[[Hampton railway station, Melbourne]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1887

Hampton railway station is located on the Sandringham line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the south-eastern Melbourne suburb of Hampton opening on 2 September 1887. It was renamed Retreat on 1 October 1887 and Hampton on 10 September 1889.Hampton Vicsig
The station building was reconstructed in 1975.[tpl]cite book|author=Vincent Adams Winter|title=VR and VicRail: 1962 - 1983|year=1990|isbn=0-9592069-3-0 |page=106[/tpl]

==Platforms & services==
