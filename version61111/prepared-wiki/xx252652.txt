[[Line 11 (Stockholm metro)]]

CATEGORIES: Stockholm metro

[tpl]unreferenced|date=January 2014[/tpl]Line 11 (T11) is one of the two blue lines of the Stockholm Metro connecting Kungsträdgården and Akalla. It is a 15.6 km (9.7 mi) long line with a 22 min traval time and 12 stations.
