[[Hwange National Park Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Hwange, Airports in Zimbabwe

Hwange National Park Airport [tpl]Airport codes|HWN|FVWN[/tpl] is an airport in Zimbabwe that serves the Hwange National Park and the town of Hwange. Because of the ongoing economic situation in Zimbabwe, and the 2012 collapse of Air Zimbabwe services are somewhat limited, but charter and a 3 times a week scheduled rotation to Victoria Falls is currently operated (April 2011) by Solenta Aviation http://www.solenta.co.zw using a 12 seat Cessna Grand Caravan aircraft.
Air Zimbabwe used to include Hwange on its daily internal service of Harare - Victoria Falls - Hwange - Kariba - Harare.
