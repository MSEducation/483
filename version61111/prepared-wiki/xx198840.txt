[[Daniel Henshall]]

CATEGORIES: 21st-century Australian male actors, Australian male film actors, Australian male television actors, Living people

Daniel Henshall is an Australian stage, film and television actor best known for his portrayal as serial killer John Bunting in the film Snowtown and as Caleb Brewster in the AMC series Turn.
