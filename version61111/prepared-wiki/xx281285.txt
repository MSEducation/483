==Racing career==

Fix began racing in amateur competitions in Sports Car Club of America (SCCA) Solo II events in the late 1980s and subsequently started entering series sanctioned by the SVRA and the Historic Sportscar Racing Ltd (HSR). He has won several races in both series with many podium finishes. Fix started his professional racing career in 2000, participating in SCCA Pro Racing Trans-Am Series at Road America, in Elkhart Lake, Wisconsin.[tpl]Cite web |url=http://www.motorsport.com/transam/news/paul-fix-ii-interview/ |title=Paul Fix II interview |date=18 February 2002 |publisher=Motorsports.com |accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl] He has competed in several other racing series since, including the American LeMans Series (ALMS).[tpl]Cite web |url=http://www.motorsport.com/kart/news/paul-fix-iii-takes-championship/ |title=Paul Fix III takes championship |date=12 December 2000 |accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl] In his rookie Trans Am season he totaled one top 10 finish and was third place overall in rookie points. He was also ranked 19th in Driver's Championship points and 6th in Owner's points.[tpl]Cite web |url=http://www.darkhorseracing.net/trans-am_fixx.htm |title=Paul Fix's Jaguar Races With A 'Budda Boom Budda Bing' |year=2002 |accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl]
In addition to the Trans-Am Series, Fix has also been participating in Vintage and Historic competitions since 1992 and GT Racing since 1999. He also participated in the Canadian GT Challenge Cup sanctioned by the ASN Canada FIA. Some career highlights include the 2005 Champ Car Trans-Am series Molson Indy Montreal at Circuit Gilles Villeneuve. Fix started 7th overall, finishing first in the GT category.[tpl]Cite web |url=http://theracesite.com/index.cfm?template=magazine&mag_id=10383 |title=Montreal Race Notes: Paul Fix |accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl] He raced in his number 77 Stopflex Mustang sponsored by Computer Systems Centre and Drive! Magazine. Paul Fix participated in many other major events throughout his career including pole position for the 2005 Taylor Woodrow Grand Prix of San Jose Cytomax 100.[tpl]Cite web |url=http://www.motorsport.com/transam/news/san-jose-paul-fix-race-notes/ |title=San Jose: Paul Fix race notes |date=1 August 2005 |accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl]
After a short break Paul Fix returned to professional racing in  the SCCA Pro Trans-Am Road Racing Series 2013 season as a member of the Tony Ave Racing team, managed by fellow racecar driver Tony Ave.[tpl]Cite web |url=http://www.gotransam.com/latest-news/2013/5/23/paul-fix-returns-to-trans-am-in-the-4-corvette-for-lime-rock.html |date=23 May 2013 |title=Paul Fix returns to Trans Am in the #4 Corvette for Lime Rock and Watkins Glen |accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl] With this team Fix won the 2013 Trans-Am Championship Series Round 3 in the TA class at Lime Rock in Connecticut.[tpl]Cite web |url=http://www.gotransam.com/storage/Final%20Race%20LRP%20'13.PDF |title= Final Race Result: Trans-Am Championship Series Round 3 |date=25 May 2013 |accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl][tpl]Cite web | url=http://www.laurenfix.com/Lauren-Fix-Articles-Paul-Fix-First-Place-Win-at-Trans-Am-2013|title=Paul Fix First Place Win at Trans-Am 2013|date=25 May 2013|accessdate=16 December 2013[/tpl] In June 2013 it was announced that Paul was to race at his "home" racetrack, Watkins Glen International in Watkins Glen, New York for the fourth stop on the 2013 Trans-Am Series schedule.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.gotransam.com/latest-news/2013/6/7/paul-fix-set-to-race-at-home-track.html | title = Paul Fix to Race at Home Track | date=6 June 2013 | accessdate=16 December 2013[/tpl] Paul subsequently finished second in the race, behind team mate Doug Peterson.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.gotransam.com/latest-news/2013/6/12/paul-fix-podiums-at-watkins-glen.html | title=Paul Fix podiums at Watkins Glen | date=11 June 2013 | accessdate=16 December 2013[/tpl]

In 1989 Fix had also founded Fix Motorsports which prepares race cars for customers competing in series sanctioned by the SCCA, the Historic Sportscar Racing Ltd (HSR), SVRA and other series. The company also restores and resells vintage vehicles.
