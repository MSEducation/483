[[Pothumarru]]

CATEGORIES: Krishna district, Villages in Krishna district

Pothumarru (Telugu:పోతుమర్రు) is a village which comes under Kalidindi mandal in Krishna district in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. Once upon a time it was one of the richest villages in the country.
Pothumarru is located at 16°31'46"N 81°18'46"E. It is 4.2 km far from its Mandal main town Kalidindi. This village is famous for aquaculture.
