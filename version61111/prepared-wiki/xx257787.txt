[[Dennis Conley]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 births, Living people, American football offensive guards, American football offensive linemen, Hampton Pirates football players, Chicago Bears players, New York Jets players, Jacksonville Sharks players, People from Suffolk, Virginia, Players of American football from Virginia

Dennis Conley (born July 15, 1985) is an American football player who plays for the Jacksonville Sharks. He played as an offensive lineman for Hampton University. He was signed as an undrafted free agent by the Chicago Bears in 2009.
