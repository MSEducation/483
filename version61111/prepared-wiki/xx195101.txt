Jimmy Roberto Blandón Quiñónez (born January 1, 1969 in Esmeraldas) is a former Ecuadorian football midfielder. He played in 30 matches for Ecuador between 1997 and 2000.rsssf: Ecuador record international footballers He was a strong defensive midfielder with great movility and tireless physical condition.
Blandón has played club football in  Ecuador for Barcelona SC, Emelec and Deportivo Cuenca, in Colombia for Millonarios, as well as for Bolivian side Blooming.
