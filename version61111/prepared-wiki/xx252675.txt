[[Line 13 (Stockholm metro)]]

CATEGORIES: Stockholm metro

[tpl]unreferenced|date=January 2014[/tpl]Line 13 (T13) is one of the two red lines of the Stockholm Metro connecting Norsborg and Ropsten. It is a 26.6 km (16.5 mi) long line with a 44 min travel time and 25 stations, making it the second longest line in the Stockholm Metro after line 19.
