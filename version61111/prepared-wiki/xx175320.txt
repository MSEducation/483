[[KBHW]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Minnesota, Christian radio stations in the United States, Moody Radio affiliate stations, Radio stations established in 1982

KBHW (99.5 FM, "Psalm FM") is a radio station broadcasting a Christian format.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www1.arbitron.com/sip/sip.do#access |date=Summer 2009 |title=Station Information Profile |publisher=Arbitron |accessdate=July 31, 2009[/tpl] Licensed to International Falls, Minnesota, USA, the station is currently owned by Heartland Christian Broadcasters, Inc.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KBHW |title=KBHW Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division |accessdate=July 7, 2009[/tpl]

==History==

The Federal Communications Commission issued a construction permit for the station to Minnesota Christian Broadcasters Inc. on June 30, 1982.[tpl]cite web |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/app_det.pl?Application_id=35209 |title=Application Search Details |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division |accessdate=July 31, 2009[/tpl] The station was issued the KBHW call sign on August 16, 1982,[tpl]cite web |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=42902&Callsign=KBHW |title=Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division |accessdate=July 31, 2009[/tpl] and received its license to cover on November 30, 1983.[tpl]cite web |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/app_det.pl?Application_id=51128 |title=Application Search Details |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division |accessdate=July 31, 2009[/tpl] On July 23, 1999, Minnesota Christian assigned the license to the current owners, Heartland Christian Broadcasters, Inc.[tpl]cite web |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/app_det.pl?Application_id=285845 |title=Application Search Details |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division |accessdate=July 31, 2009[/tpl]

==Translators==

In addition to the main station, KBHW is relayed by an additional 12 translators to widen its broadcast area.
