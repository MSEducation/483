[[(17503) 1992 FK1]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1992, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(17503) Celestechild 1992 FK1 is a main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on March 26, 1992.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
