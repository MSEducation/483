[[Varmazyar-e Sofla]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Varmazyar-e Sofla ([tpl]lang-fa|ورمزيارسفلي[/tpl], also Romanized as Varmazyār-e Soflá; also known as Varmaziar and Varmazyār)[tpl]GEOnet3|10081940|Varmazyar-e Sofla[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Pain Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 97, in 23 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
