[[Erquelinnes]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Hainaut (province)

Erquelinnes is a Walloon municipality located in the Belgian province of Hainaut. On January 1, 2006 Erquelinnes had a total population of 9,549. The total area is [tpl]convert|44.23|km²|2|abbr=on[/tpl] which gives a population density of 216 inhabitants per km².
