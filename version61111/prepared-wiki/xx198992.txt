[tpl]nihongo|Kanako Omura|大村加奈子|Ōmura Kanako|born December 15, 1976, in Nakagyō-ku, Kyoto[/tpl] is a volleyball player from Japan.
She competed at the 2004 and  2008 Olympic Games for the Japanese team, finishing fifth on both occasions.Olympic results
