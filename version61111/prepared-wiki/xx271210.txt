[[(19261) 1995 MB]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1995, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(19261) 1995 MB is a main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on June 21, 1995.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
