[[Tangipahoa Parish School Board]]

CATEGORIES: School districts in Louisiana, Education in Tangipahoa Parish, Louisiana

Tangipahoa Parish School Board is a school district headquartered in Amite City, Louisiana, Tangipahoa Parish, Louisiana, United States.
The district serves Tangipahoa Parish.
Robert L. Frye (1927-2011), the Republican nominee for state education superintendent in 1972, was a former member of the Tangipahoa Parish School Board.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.legacy.com/obituaries/theadvocate/obituary.aspx?n=robert-l-frye&pid=148361868|title=Robert L. Frye|publisher=Baton Rouge Morning Advocate, February 6, 2011|accessdate=February 6, 2011[/tpl]

==School uniforms==

All students are required to wear school uniforms."Dress Code and Uniform Regulations." Tangipahoa Parish School Board.
