[[Lakewood, Shelby County, Illinois]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Shelby County, Illinois, Unincorporated communities in Illinois, Populated places established in 1872, 1872 establishments in Illinois

Lakewood is an unincorporated community in Lakewood Township, Shelby County, Illinois, United States. Lakewood is located on County Highway 12 [tpl]convert|8|mi|km[/tpl] southwest of Shelbyville.[tpl]cite map |publisher=Illinois Department of Transportation |title=Shelby County General Highway Map |url=http://www.dot.state.il.us/maps/county/shelby.pdf |format= |edition= |year=2010 |cartography= |scale= |series= |page= |section= |inset= |accessdate=September 25, 2011 |isbn= |id= |ref= [/tpl] Lakewood had a post office, which opened on July 26, 1872, and closed on November 2, 2002.[tpl]cite web|title=Postmaster Finder - Post Offices by ZIP Code|url=http://webpmt.usps.gov/pmt008.cfm|publisher=United States Postal Service|accessdate=September 25, 2011[/tpl]
