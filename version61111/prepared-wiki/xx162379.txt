[[Tom Beasley]]

CATEGORIES: 1954 births, Living people, American football defensive linemen, Virginia Tech Hokies football players, Washington Redskins players, Pittsburgh Steelers players, People from Bluefield, West Virginia, Super Bowl champions

Thomas Lynn Beasley (born August 11, 1954 in Bluefield, West Virginia) is a former American football defensive lineman in the National Football League for the Washington Redskins and Pittsburgh Steelers.  He played college football at Virginia Tech.
