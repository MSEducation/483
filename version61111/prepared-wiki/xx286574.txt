[[1997 in UFC]]

CATEGORIES: Ultimate Fighting Championship events, 1997 in mixed martial arts

The year 1997 is the 5th year in the history of the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC), a mixed martial arts promotion based in the United States. In 1997 the UFC held 5 events beginning with, UFC 12: Judgement Day.
