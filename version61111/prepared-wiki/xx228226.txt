[[Erik Willoch]]

CATEGORIES: 1922 births, 1991 deaths, Norwegian jurists, Directors of government agencies of Norway, Avinor people, People from Oslo, University of Oslo faculty

Erik Willoch (1922 – 1991) was a Norwegian jurist and civil servant.
Born in Oslo as a brother of Kåre Willoch, he graduated as cand.jur. in 1948. He worked at the University of Oslo from 1950 to 1956, and then in the Office of the Attorney General of Norway from 1957.
