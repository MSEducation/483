[[Columbus Capitals (indoor soccer)]]

CATEGORIES: Soccer clubs in Columbus, Ohio, Ohio soccer clubs, American Indoor Soccer Association teams, Defunct indoor soccer clubs in the United States

The Columbus Capitals were an indoor soccer club based in Columbus, Ohio that competed in the American Indoor Soccer Association. The club played its home games at the Ohio Expo Center Coliseum from 1984-1986, then known as the Fairgrounds Coliseum.
