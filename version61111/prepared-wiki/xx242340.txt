[[1962 Campeonato Ecuatoriano de Fútbol]]

CATEGORIES: Ecuadorian Serie A seasons, 1962 in Ecuador, 1962 in South American football leagues

The 1962 Campeonato Ecuatoriano de Fútbol ([tpl]lang-en|Ecuadorian Football Championship[/tpl]) was the 4th national championship for football teams in Ecuador. 
Guayaquilean club Everest won their first national title, and earned a berth in the 1963 Copa de Campeones. To date, this is Everest's only national title.
