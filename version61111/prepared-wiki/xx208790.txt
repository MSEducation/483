[[Old-Timey Concert]]

CATEGORIES: 1967 live albums, Doc Watson live albums, Vanguard Records live albums, English-language live albums

Old-Timey Concert is the title of a live recording by American folk music artist Doc Watson, Clint Howard, and Fred Price. Originally a "Double LP", now one CD with four tracks omitted: Tracks 8, 15, 16 and 19.
