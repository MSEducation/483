[[I Know You Know]]

CATEGORIES: 2000s drama films, 2009 films

I Know You Know is a 2009 feature film written and directed by Justin Kerrigan, his first major work since his debut with Human Traffic in 1999.
The film is about a young boy who becomes involved with his father's espionage work, his father wants to finish one more job before they can both move to a better life in America.. however things do not go to plan. This movie was filmed in Wales at many other locations within the United Kingdom. 
Reviews say that the acting in this film is superb and the drama is well developed and original.
