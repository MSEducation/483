[[Zizeeria karsandra]]

CATEGORIES: Animals described in 1865, Polyommatini, Insects of Greece

Dark Grass Blue (Zizeeria karsandra) is a small butterfly found in the Southern Mediterranean, in a broad band to India, Sri Lanka, the Andaman and Nicobar Islands, Burma, Thailand, Malaysia,[tpl]aut|Fleming, W.A.[/tpl], 1975  Butterflies of West Malaysia & Singapore ISBN 0-900848-71-5 Indonesia, the Philippines, New Guinea and northern and eastern Australia.[tpl]aut|Parsons, M.[/tpl], 1999. The Butterflies of Papua New Guinea Academic Press, ISBN 0-12-55555-0 It belongs to the Lycaenids or Blues family, and the tribe Polyommatini[tpl]aut|Eliot, J.N.[/tpl] 1973: The higher classification of the Lycaenidae (Lepidoptera): a tentative arrangement. Bulletin of the British Museum (Natural History), entomology, 28: 371-505.
