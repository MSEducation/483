[[Battle of Rajgród (1794)]]

CATEGORIES: Battles of the Kościuszko Uprising, Battles involving Prussia, Conflicts in 1794

The Battle of Rajgród [tpl]lang-pl|Bitwa pod Rajgrodem[/tpl] was a battle of Kościuszko Uprising between Polish-Lithuanian commonwealth rebel army and the Kingdom of Prussia which took place in Rajgród on 1794 July 10.
