[[Jody Jenneker]]

CATEGORIES: 1984 births, Living people, South African rugby union players, Sharks (rugby union) players, Natal Sharks players, Western Province (rugby team) players, Falcons (rugby team) players, Rugby union hookers

Jody Jenneker born 10 April 1984 in Durban, South Africa is a rugby union player for the Sharks in the Super Rugby competition. He plays as a hooker.
