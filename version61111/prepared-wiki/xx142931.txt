[[HMS Shipham (M2726)]]

CATEGORIES: Ham-class minesweepers, Royal Navy ship names, 1955 ships

HMS ''Shipham'' was one of 93 ships of the [tpl]sclass|Ham|minesweeper|0[/tpl] of inshore minesweepers. 
Their names were all chosen from villages ending in -ham. The minesweeper was named after Shipham in Somerset.
