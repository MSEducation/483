[[Drago Grubelnik]]

CATEGORIES: Slovenian male alpine skiers, Living people, 1976 births, Alpine skiers at the 2006 Winter Olympics, Alpine skiers at the 2002 Winter Olympics, Olympic alpine skiers of Slovenia

Drago Grubelnik (born 15 January 1976)http://www.fis-ski.com/uk/604/613.html?type=biog&competitorid=21922&sector=AL is a former Slovenian alpine skier. 
Grubelnik, a slalom specialist, won one podium in Alpine Skiing World Cup, slalom in Wengen, Switzerland (January 2000). He competed at 2002 and 2006 Winter Olympics.
