[[Yengi Kand-e Jame ol Sara]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Yengi Kand-e Jame ol Sara ([tpl]lang-fa|ينگي كندجامع السرا[/tpl], also Romanized as Yengī Kand-e Jāme‘ ol Sarā and Yengī Kand-e Jāme‘ os Sarā; also known as Yangi Kand, Yengī Gand-e Jāmeh, Yengī Kand, Yengīkand-e Jāme‘eh Sarā, Yengī Kand-e Jāmeh Sarā, Yengī Kand-e Jameh Sarā, and Yengī Kand-e Jāme‘ Sarā)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3766401|Yengi Kand-e Jame ol Sara[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 523, in 119 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
