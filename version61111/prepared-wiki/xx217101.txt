Jonathan Fabbro (born 16 January 1982 in Buenos Aires) is an Argentine-born naturalized-Paraguayan football midfielder who currently plays for River Plate in the Primera División Argentina and the Paraguay national football team. Fabbro played in the 2004 Intercontinental Cup. In 2012, Fabbro was named as the Paraguayan Footballer of the Year. He is the brother of former footballer Darío Fabbro.
