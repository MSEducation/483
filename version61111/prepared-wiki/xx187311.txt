[[Everes lacturnus]]

CATEGORIES: Cupido (butterfly), Butterflies of Singapore

Cupido lacturnus or Orange-tipped Pea-blue is a small butterfly found in Australasia and Indomalaya that belongs to the Lycaenids or Blues family.
