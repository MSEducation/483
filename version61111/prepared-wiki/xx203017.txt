[[Șamșud]]

CATEGORIES: Communes in Sălaj County

Şamşud ([tpl]lang-hu|Szilágysámson[/tpl]) is a commune located in Sălaj County, Romania. It is composed of two villages, Şamşud and Valea Pomilor (Mocsolya).

==Demographics==

In 2002 it had a population of 1766, of which 1582 were Hungarian (89.58%), 155 Roma (8.78%), 33 Romanian (1.87%) and 1 German (0.06%).
