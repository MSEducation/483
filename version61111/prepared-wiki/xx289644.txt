[[Qarah Gol, Khodabandeh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Qarah Gol ([tpl]lang-fa|قره گل[/tpl], also Romanized as Qareh Gol; also known as Karakul and Qara Kūl)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3079693|Qarah Gol[/tpl] is a village in Aq Bolagh Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 184, in 31 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
