[[Gmina Sochocin]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Płońsk County

Gmina Sochocin is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Płońsk County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Sochocin, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|8|km|mi|0[/tpl] north-east of Płońsk and [tpl]convert|64|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north-west of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|119.67|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 5,713.

==Villages==

Gmina Sochocin contains the villages and settlements of Baraki, Biele, Bolęcin, Budy Gutarzewskie, Ciemniewo, Drożdżyn, Gromadzyn, Gutarzewo, Idzikowice, Jędrzejewo, Kępa, Koliszewo, Kolonia Sochocin, Kołoząb, Kondrajec, Kuchary Królewskie, Kuchary Żydowskie, Milewo, Niewikla, Podsmardzewo, Rzy, Ślepowrony, Smardzewo, Sochocin, Wierzbówiec, Wycinki and Żelechy.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Sochocin is bordered by the gminas of Baboszewo, Glinojeck, Joniec, Nowe Miasto, Ojrzeń, Płońsk and Sońsk.
