[[Worldways Canada]]

CATEGORIES: Defunct airlines of Canada, Airlines established in 1973, Airlines disestablished in 1990

Worldways Canada was a Canadian charter airline that started in operations in 1973, ceased its operations on 11 October 1990 and went out of business in 1991.[tpl]cite web |title=List of ceased airlines in our photo album | work= Commercial Jetliner Photography | url=http://www.homestead.com/mmaviation/Ceasedoperators.html | accessdate=2006-10-20[/tpl] 

==Operations and fleet==

Their fleet initially consisted of Douglas DC-4s, but soon added jet airliners including  McDonnell Douglas DC-8-63, Boeing 707, Boeing 727, Convair 640 and Lockheed L-1011-385-1TriStar.[tpl]cite web |title=Aviation Photos : Worldways Canada | work=Airliners.net | url=http://www.airliners.net/search/photo.search?airlinesearch=Worldways%20Canada&distinct_entry=true | accessdate=2006-10-20[/tpl][tpl]cite web |title=Widebody Aircraft Parade | work=The Widebody Aircraft Parade | url=http://widebodyaircraft.nl/l1011-60.htm | accessdate=2006-10-20[/tpl]
It was created and run by Roy Moore.
