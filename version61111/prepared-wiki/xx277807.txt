[[Sakai Tadatoshi]]

CATEGORIES: Daimyo, Rōjū, Sakai clan, 1562 births, 1627 deaths

[tpl]nihongo|Sakai Tadatoshi| 酒井 忠利||1562–1627[/tpl] was a Japanese samurai daimyō of the Edo period. He was head of a cadet branch of the Sakai clan.Papinot, Jacques Edmond Joseph. (1906). Dictionnaire d’histoire et de géographie du Japon;  Papinot, (2003). "Sakai" at Nobiliare du Japon, p. 51; retrieved 2013-8-8.

==Career==

In 1601, Tadatoshi was made head of Tanaka Domain (10,000 koku) in Suruga Province. His holdings were transferred in 1609 to Kawagoe Domain (30,000
koku) in Musashi Province.
