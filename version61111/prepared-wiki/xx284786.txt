[[New Laguna, New Mexico]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Cibola County, New Mexico, Unincorporated communities in New Mexico

New Laguna is an unincorporated community in Cibola County, New Mexico, United States. New Laguna is located along New Mexico State Road 124 [tpl]convert|2.25|mi|km[/tpl] west of Laguna. New Laguna has a post office with ZIP code 87038.[tpl]GR|7[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Postmaster Finder - Post Offices by ZIP Code|url=http://webpmt.usps.gov/pmt008.cfm|publisher=United States Postal Service|accessdate=August 10, 2013[/tpl]
