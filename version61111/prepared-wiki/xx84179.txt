[[Ikeda, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Ikeda|池田町|Ikeda-machi[/tpl] is a town located in Kitaazumi District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the town has an estimated population of 10,647 and a density of 264.98 persons per km². The total area is 40.18 km².
