Albert Malam (born 20 January 1913 in Liverpool - February 1992) [tpl]cite web|last=Malam|first=Albert|title=England & Wales, Death Index, 1916-2007|publisher=ancestry.com|accessdate=1 November 2013[/tpl] is a former professional footballer, who played for Everton, Colwyn Bay, Chesterfield, Huddersfield Town, Wrexham and Doncaster Rovers.During the Second World War he played for Manchester City, Crewe and New Brighton. Became player-manager of Runcorn in the Cheshire League after leaving Wrexham. Father of Colin Malam, former Football Correspondent of the Sunday Telegraph.
