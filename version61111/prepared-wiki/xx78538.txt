[[Takaharu, Miyazaki]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Miyazaki Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Takaharu|高原町|Takaharu-chō[/tpl] is a town located in Nishimorokata District, Miyazaki Prefecture, Japan.
As of September 1, 2011, the town has an estimated population of 9,887 and the population density of 116 persons per km². The total area is 85.38 km².
