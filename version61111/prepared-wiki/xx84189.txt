[[Sakaki, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Sakaki|坂城町|Sakaki-machi[/tpl] is a town located in Hanishina District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of December 1, 2005, the town has an estimated population of 16,742. The total area is 53.64 km².
The town has strong influence with the city of Ueda rather than Nagano or Chikuma thus the town is in the Hokushin Region.

==Geography==

===Surrounding municipalities===

The town of Sakaki is completely surrounded by the cities of Ueda and Chikuma.
