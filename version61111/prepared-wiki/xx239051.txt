[[You Name It]]

CATEGORIES: 1976 albums, The Dynamic Superiors albums, English-language albums, Albums produced by Hal Davis, Motown albums

You Name It is the third album by The Dynamic Superiors released on Motown Records in 1976. The album was arranged by Arthur Wright, Gene Page and Mel Bolton.
In 2012, this album was released on CD for the first time, not by Motown, by Universal Records' Soulmusic.com imprint.http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GQ8Axvojars
