[[Marsimik La]]

CATEGORIES: Mountain passes of India, Mountain passes of the Himalayas

Marsimik La or Marsimek La, elevation 5,681.5meters(18,640 ft), is a high mountain pass in the Chang-Chemno Range in northern India about [tpl]convert|100|km|abbr=on[/tpl] east of Leh, further by road.
Located [tpl]convert|20|km|abbr=on|sigfig=1[/tpl] northeast of the northwest tip of Pangong Lake, the pass is on the shortest route from there to the contested Kongka Pass area some [tpl]convert|42|km|abbr=on[/tpl] further northeast on the China-India line of actual control.  Marsimik La is [tpl]convert|4|km[/tpl] west of the sharp [tpl]convert|6000|to|6500|m|abbr=on|sigfig=3[/tpl] ridgeline forming the line of actual control.
