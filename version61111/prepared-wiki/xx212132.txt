William Alexander Johnson (born December 4, 1964) was a Canadian Football League defensive lineman who played nine seasons in the CFL,  mainly for the Calgary Stampeders. Johnson was a five-time All Star and won a Grey Cup with Calgary in 1992. He started his career in the NFL, playing for the Chicago Bears in 1987, where he was a linebacker.
