[[Issru]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Ludhiana district

Issru is a village situated near Khanna, Ludhiana, Punjab, India. Issru is a historical village.
Shaheed Karnail Singh Benipal, who sacrificed his life for the Goa Liberation Movement, belonged to this village.  This village is inhabited mainly by people having 'Benipal' as their surname.
