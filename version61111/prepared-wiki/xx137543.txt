[[Hawthorne/Lennox (Los Angeles Metro station)]]

CATEGORIES: Los Angeles County Metro Rail stations, Railway stations opened in 1995, Hawthorne, California

Hawthorne/Lennox is a Los Angeles County Metro Rail station on the Green Line.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.metro.net/around/rail/green-line/|title=Green Line station information|accessdate=2010-09-25[/tpl] It is located at Hawthorne Boulevard in the median of the Century Freeway in Hawthorne, California.  Due to its proximity, the station also serves Inglewood, California and the unincorporated area of Lennox, California. The original name for this station was Hawthorne/I-105 and it may still be used in some places.
