[[Wish Upon the Pleiades]]

CATEGORIES: 2011 anime OVAs, Gainax, Subaru, Magical girl anime and manga, Anime ONAs, 2010s anime films, Japanese films

Wish Upon the Pleiades, also known under the Japanese title [tpl]nihongo|Hōkago no Pleiades|放課後のプレアデス|Hōkago no Pureadesu|lit. "After School Pleiades"[/tpl], is an original net animation series developed jointly by Gainax and Japanese automaker  Subaru.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/news/2010-11-01/subaru-x-gainax-negima-dragon-crisis-promos-streamed|title=News: Subaru x Gainax, Negima, Dragon Crisis Promos Streamed|publisher=Anime News Network|date=November 1, 2010|accessdate=December 30, 2010[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/news/2010-12-28/pleiades-st-seiya-air-gear-black-lagoon-promos-streamed|title=News: Pleiades, St. Seiya, Air Gear, Black Lagoon Promos Streamed|publisher=Anime News Network|date=December 28, 2010|accessdate=December 30, 2010[/tpl] The series was released on YouTube in four parts on February 1, 2011 with the English-subtitled version appearing six days later. It will be adapted into an anime film and a television anime series.[tpl]cite web |url= http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/news/2013-03-21/gainax-and-subaru-hokago-no-pleiades-anime-gets-film|title=  Gainax & Subaru's Hōkago no Pleiades Anime Gets Film|date=2013-03-21|accessdate= 2013-03-26|work= Anime News Network[/tpl]
