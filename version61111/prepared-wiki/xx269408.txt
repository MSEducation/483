[[Bradypophila]]

CATEGORIES: Chrysauginae, Monotypic lepidoptera genera

Bradypophila is a genus of snout moths. It was described by Ihering, in 1914, and contains the species B. garbei. It is found in Brazil.[tpl]cite web|url=http://acta.inpa.gov.br/fasciculos/12-3/PDF/v12n3a17.pdf |title=Two new species of moths (Lepidoptera, Pyralidae, Chrysauginae) |format=PDF |date= |accessdate=2011-09-29[/tpl]
The forewings are unicolorous dark ochreous.
The larvae are associated with tree sloths.
