[[Medusa (Trapeze album)]]

CATEGORIES: Trapeze albums, 1970 albums, English-language albums, Universal Records albums, Threshold Records albums

Medusa is a 1970 album by the rock band Trapeze.  This was their second album, following their eponymous debut from the same year.  Trapeze also trimmed down to a trio following the departure of lead singer John Jones and guitarist/keyboard player Terry Rowley.
