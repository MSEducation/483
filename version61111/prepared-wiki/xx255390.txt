</ref> It has an area of [tpl]convert|6037|km2|abbr=on[/tpl] and, [tpl]asof|2010|lc=y[/tpl], a population of 2665 of which 1560 (54.4%) are ethnic [[Evenks]].<ref>[tpl]cite web | language = zh-cn | publisher = 内蒙古自治区民族宗教网 | title =陈巴尔虎旗鄂温克苏木 | url =http://www.nmgmzw.gov.cn/Article.aspx?Id=20802| date = 2010-05-01 | accessdate = 2013-01-19 [/tpl]</ref>
[tpl]Asof|2010[/tpl], it is the only "ethnic sumu" in China; the sumu is a type of administrative unit at the township (fourth) level only found in Inner Mongolia, equivalent to ethnic townships in other parts of China.
It is located on grassland [tpl]convert|68|km[/tpl] north-northeast of the urban area of Hulunbuir.
