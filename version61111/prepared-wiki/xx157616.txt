[[Ham-Mihan]]

CATEGORIES: Persian-language newspapers, Newspapers published in Iran, Publications with year of establishment missing

Ham-Mihan (هم میهن in Persian) is a popular reformist newspaper in Iran (Persia). Its chief editor is Mohammad Ghouchani since April 2007. Ham-Mihan is published under supervision of Mohammad Atrianfar.
It was temporarily closed in August 2007 by a court in Tehran.
