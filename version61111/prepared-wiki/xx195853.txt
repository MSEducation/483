[[Ellen Swallow Richards House]]

CATEGORIES: 1890s architecture in the United States, Houses in Boston, Massachusetts, National Historic Landmarks in Massachusetts, Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Massachusetts

The Ellen H. Swallow Richards House is a National Historic Landmark house at 32 Eliot Street in Jamaica Plain, a neighborhood of Boston, Massachusetts.
Ellen Swallow Richards and Robert Hallowell Richards moved into the Italianate house in 1876. The house was added to the National Register of Historic Places and declared a National Historic Landmark in 1992.National Historic Landmark profile, National Park Service.  Accessed 2009-11-24.
She is considered a pioneer in environmental engineering and many of her methods are still used today.
