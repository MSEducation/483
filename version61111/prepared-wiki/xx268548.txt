[[Central District (Semirom County)]]

CATEGORIES: Semirom County, Districts of Isfahan Province

The Central District of Semirom County ([tpl]lang-fa|بخش مرکزی شهرستان سمیرم[/tpl]) is a district (bakhsh) in Semirom County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 47,535, in 11,653 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|10[/tpl]  The District has three cities: Semirom, Vanak, and Hana. The District has three rural districts (dehestan): Hana Rural District, Vanak Rural District, and Vardasht Rural District.
