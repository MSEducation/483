[[McCready]]

McCready, a variant of McCreadie, is an Irish and Scottish surname. It is the Anglicized form of Gaelic Mac Riada "son of Riada", a personal name meaning "trained" and "expert".[tpl]cite web |url=http://genealogy.familyeducation.com/surname-origin/mccready |title=McCready |publisher=familyeducation.com |accessdate=2007-01-07[/tpl]
