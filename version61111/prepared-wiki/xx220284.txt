[[Bloomfield Schools]]

CATEGORIES: School districts in New Mexico, Education in San Juan County, New Mexico

Bloomfield Schools (also known as the Bloomfield School District) is a public school district based in Bloomfield, New Mexico, United States. The district covers a [tpl]convert|1508|sqmi|km2|adj=on[/tpl] area[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.ped.state.nm.us/IT/fs/18/district.square.miles.pdf | title = District Size in Square Miles| format = PDF | publisher = Accountability and Information Services, New Mexico Public Education Department | accessdate = 2008-08-20[/tpl] in eastern/southeastern San Juan County.  In addition to Bloomfield, the district also serves the communities of Huerfano and Nageezi.

==Demographics==

There were a total of 3,187 students enrolled in Bloomfield Schools during the 2007-2008 school year. The gender makeup of the district was 48.23% female and 51.77% male.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.ped.state.nm.us/IT/fs/15/07.08.gender.pdf | title = Total Enrollment by Gender for School Year 2007-2008| format = PDF | publisher = Information Technology Division, New Mexico Public Education Department  | accessdate = 2008-08-18[/tpl] The racial makeup of the district was 33.57% Hispanic, 33.04% White, 32.48% Native American, 0.56% African American, and 0.35% Asian/Pacific Islander.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.ped.state.nm.us/IT/fs/13/07.08.ethnic.pdf | title = Total Enrollment by Ethnicity for School Year 2007-2008| format = PDF | publisher = Information Technology Division, New Mexico Public Education Department  | accessdate = 2008-08-18[/tpl]
