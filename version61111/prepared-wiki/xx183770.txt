[[Troilus luridus]]

CATEGORIES: Pentatomidae

Troilus luridus is a species of shield bug.
==Description== 
Troilus luridus is a large predatory shieldbug in the family Pentatomidae. It measures about 10 to 12 mm in length.http://www.britishbugs.org.uk/heteroptera/Pentatomidae/troilus_luridus.html
The shieldbug has an orange band on the penultimate antennal segment. The legs are brown and the scutellum lacks an orange tip.[tpl]cite web |title= (Pentatomidae) Troilus luridus |url=  http://www.britishbugs.org.uk/heteroptera/Pentatomidae/troilus_luridus.html |accessdate=2009-02-06[/tpl]
It is one of four carnivorous species found in Britain of the predominantly phytophagous Pentatomidae insect family.www.earthlife.net
