[[Sini Latvala]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 births, Living people, People from Kankaanpää, Finnish hammer throwers, Athletes (track and field) at the 2000 Summer Olympics, Athletes (track and field) at the 2004 Summer Olympics, Olympic athletes of Finland

Sini Tuuli Susanne Latvala (née Pöyry, born 3 February 1980 in Kankaanpää) is a retired female hammer thrower from Finland. Her personal best throw is 69.16 metres, achieved in July 2004 in Kaustinen. She was nicknamed "Sintsa".
