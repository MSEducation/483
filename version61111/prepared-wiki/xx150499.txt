[[Tomas Bolme]]

CATEGORIES: 1945 births, Living people, Male actors from Stockholm, Swedish male stage actors, Swedish male voice actors, Swedish communists, Best Actor Guldbagge Award winners

Tomas Robert Olof Bolme (born 21 April 1945 in Högalid, Stockholm) is a Swedish actor. He is famous for being the Swedish voice of Tintin, and he is also the voice of the anime hero Cobra. He studied at Teaterhögskolan in Stockholm during the years 1966 to 1969. Bolme has performed at Fria Proteatern, Royal Dramatic Theatre, Stockholm City Theatre and Riksteatern. He has also recorded several audio books and one of them is Jan Guillou's Coq Rouge.
Bolme is a former member of the political party Communist League Marxists-Leninists ([tpl]lang-sv|Kommunistiska förbundet marxist-leninisterna[/tpl], KFML).[tpl]cite book |title=Medlöparna |last=Häggman |first=Bertil |authorlink=Bertil Häggman |year=1991 |publisher=Contra |location=Stockholm |isbn=91-86092-22-7 |language=Swedish[/tpl]
He is the brother of television host Agneta Bolme Börjefors. At the 24th Guldbagge Awards he won the award for Best Actor for his role in Creditors.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.sfi.se/en-GB/Swedish-film-database/Item/?type=MOVIE&itemid=16859&iv=Awards |title=Fordringsägare (1988) |accessdate=15 March 2014 |work=The Swedish Film Database[/tpl] Along with Kim Anderzon, he co-hosted the 27th Guldbagge Awards.[tpl]cite web|title=Värdar, tid och plats för alla Guldbaggegalor|url=http://www.sfi.se/sv/svensk-filmdatabas/guldbaggelistan/Historik1/Vardar-samt-tid-och-plats-for-alla-galor/|publisher=The Swedish Film Database|accessdate=19 February 2014|language=Swedish[/tpl]
