[[Mehdiabad, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Mehdiabad ([tpl]lang-fa|مهدي اباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Mehdīābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|10080905|Mehdiabad[/tpl] is a village in Aq Bolagh Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 414, in 79 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
