[[Kasagi Dam]]

CATEGORIES: Dams in Gifu Prefecture, Dams completed in 1936

[tpl]nihongo|Kasagi Dam|笠置ダム|Kasagi damu[/tpl] is a dam in the Gifu Prefecture of Japan. Construction of the dam was completed by 1936. Its height is 40.8m and its maximum power output (from the power plant) is 41,700 kW.
