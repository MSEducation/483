[[Tsugaru-Onoe Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Aomori Prefecture, Konan Railway, Hirakawa, Aomori

[tpl]nihongo| Tsugaru-Onoe Station|津軽尾上駅|Tsugaru-Onoe-eki[/tpl] is a railway station on the Kōnan Railway Kōnan Line located in the city of Hirakawa, Aomori Prefecture, Japan. It is 11.1 kilometers from the northern terminus of the line at Hirosaki Station.

==History==

Tsugaru-Onoe Station was opened on September 1, 1927. Freight operations were discontinued in 1979. A new station building was completed in September 1979. The station has been operated as a kan'i itaku station since October 1980. On June 12, 2007, an accident occurred at Hiraka Station when a Kuroishi-bound train derailed. The station has been unattended since April 2006.

==Lines==

==Station layout==

Tsugaru-Onoe Station has a single island platform serving two tracks. The station building is attended only during normal working hours.
