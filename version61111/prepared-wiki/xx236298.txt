[[New Moston]]

CATEGORIES: Areas of Manchester

New Moston is a district within the City of Manchester, in North West England.
Historically in Lancashire, it is around five miles from Manchester city centre.  
Originating as a residential hamlet, it occupies an area on the eastern border of the township of Moston, bordering the Whitegate area of Chadderton and Failsworth in the Metropolitan Borough of Oldham. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.british-history.ac.uk/report.aspx?compid=41415|title='Townships: Moston', A History of the County of Lancaster: Volume 4 (1911), pp. 264-270  |publisher=British-history.ac.uk |date=2003-06-22 |accessdate=2013-07-04[/tpl]
The area is home to New Moston Primary School, St Margaret Mary's Primary school, New Moston Library and New Moston Athletic football club, who play in the Chapel Road 6-a-side League.
In New Moston over half of the homes are privately owned.[tpl]fact|date=February 2013[/tpl]
