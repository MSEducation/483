[[Djibouti (anthem)]]

CATEGORIES: National anthems, Djiboutian music, National symbols of Djibouti

Djibouti ([tpl]lang-so|Jabuuti[/tpl], [tpl]lang-aa|Gabuuti[/tpl]) is the national anthem of the Republic of Djibouti.

==History==

The Djibouti national anthem was adopted upon independence in 1977. The words are in Somali, and were written by Aden Elmi. The melody was composed by Abdi Robleh.
