[[Noteć Łabiszyn]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Poland, Association football clubs established in 1974, 1974 establishments in Poland, Żnin County, Sport in Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship

Miesjki Ludowy Klub Sportowy Noteć Łabiszyn is a football club from Łabiszyn, Poland. It was found in 1974. They are currently playing in Polish Fourth League (V level).
