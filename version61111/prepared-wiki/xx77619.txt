[[Christopher B. Reeves]]

CATEGORIES: Emmy Award winners, 1958 births, Living people

Christopher Bennett Reeves is a television dialogue editor living in the Los Angeles area.  He has been awarded two Emmy Awards for his work on The X-Files and has two (1992 and 2004) Golden Reel Awards from the MPSE.  His wife, Gabrielle Reeves, is also a sound editor.
