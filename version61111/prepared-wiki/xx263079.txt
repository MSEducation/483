[[Marmaro]]

CATEGORIES: Lasithi, Uninhabited islands of Greece, Islands of Crete

Marmaro ([tpl]lang-el|Μάρμαρο[/tpl], "marble"), is an uninhabited Greek islet, located south of cape Goudero on the coast of Lasithi, eastern Crete, in the Libyan Sea. It forms a close group of islands with Koufonisi, Makroulo, Strongyli, and Trachilos.
