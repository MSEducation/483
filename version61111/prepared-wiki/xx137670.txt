[[Bittenfeld]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Baden-Württemberg

Bittenfeld is an incorporated (1975) town located in the northernmost quarter of Waiblingen in the Rems-Murr-Kreis in Baden-Württemberg.[tpl]cite book|author=Gerhard Köbler|title=Historisches Lexikon der Deutschen Länder: die deutschen Territorien vom Mittelalter bis zur Gegenwart|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=Fc9r8BbvIsUC&pg=PA272|accessdate=30 July 2013|year=2007|publisher=C.H.Beck|language=German|isbn=978-3-406-54986-1|pages=272–[/tpl] Its population is approximately 4,300 inhabitants.
