[[Fish River (Northern Territory)]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of the Northern Territory

The Fish River is a river in the Northern Territory of Australia. It is a tributary of the Daly River which ultimately flows into the Joseph Bonaparte Gulf which is part of the Timor Sea.
Its catchment covers an area of 1,748.15 kmÂ².[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.nt.gov.au/nreta/natres/daly/catchment/8.html |title=Fish River - Daly River Catchment |publisher=Northern Territory Government |accessdate=18 October 2011 [/tpl] None of the land within the river's watershed, which is covered by woodlands and melaleuca forest, has been cleared of its vegetation.
