[[Poem, ballader och lite blues]]

CATEGORIES: Cornelis Vreeswijk albums, 1970 albums, Swedish-language albums

Poem, ballader och lite blues (English: Poems, ballads and a little blues) is the sixth studio album by the Swedish-Dutch folk singer-songwriter Cornelis Vreeswijk.
All songs from this album were newly recorded in 2007 by other artists, as a homage to Vreeswijk.Album info on www.cornelis.se

==Track listing==

All songs by Cornelis Vreeswijk, unless otherwise noted.
