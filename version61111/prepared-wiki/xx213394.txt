[[Save Austria]]

CATEGORIES: Political parties in Austria

Independent Citizens' Initiative Save Austria ([tpl]lang-de|Unabhängige Bürgerinitiative Rettet Österreich[/tpl]) or Save Austria ([tpl]lang-de|Rettet Österreich[/tpl]) is an anti-EU movement in Austria which was founded by former presidential candidate and pro-neutrality activist Karl Walter Nowak to fight against the European Constitution and the Treaty of Lisbon. In the Austrian legislative election, 2008 they received 0.73% of the vote and no seats. Their largest vote - 0.9% - was in Upper Austria.
