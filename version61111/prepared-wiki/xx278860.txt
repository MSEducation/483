[[Project Orange]]

CATEGORIES: Architecture firms based in London, Companies established in 1997, Companies based in Islington

Project Orange is an award-winning architectural practice based in Clerkenwell, London, UK. It was formally established in 1997 by Christopher Ash (born 1966[tpl]cn|date=December 2013[/tpl]) and James Soane (born 1966[tpl]cn|date=December 2013[/tpl]), evolving from a loose grouping of architects.Building Design 
In 2005, the duo - who met at Cambridge University - were chosen for inclusion in the Architects Journal's list of 40 Under 40[tpl]cite web|author=22 October, 2007 |url=http://www.architectsjournal.co.uk/home/project-orange/186120.article |title=Project Orange | Archive |publisher=Architects Journal |date=2007-10-22 |accessdate=2013-12-28[/tpl] for being among the most significant architects working in the UK under the age of 40. Project Orange's work has included residential projects, hotel interiors and schools in locations such as London, Mumbai and Sheffield. As of 2013, the firm has 10 employees.
Soane is also a writer whose first book, New Homes (ISBN 9781840912838), was published in 2003.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.octopusbooks.co.uk/books/interiors-and-design/9781840912838/new-home/ |title=New Home |publisher=Octopusbooks.co.uk |date= |accessdate=2013-12-28[/tpl]
