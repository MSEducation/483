[[Mehdi Namli]]

CATEGORIES: 1987 births, Living people, Moroccan footballers, Clermont Foot players, Ligue 2 players, People from Safi, Morocco

Mehdi Namli (born 23 June 1987) is a Moroccan footballer who plays for MAT Tetouan. He usually plays as forward.
Namli previously played for Moroccan side Olympique Safi.
