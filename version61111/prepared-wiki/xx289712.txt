[[Providence Friars women's basketball]]

CATEGORIES: Providence Friars women's basketball, College women's basketball teams in the United States

The Providence Friars women's basketball team represents Providence College in Providence, Rhode Island, United States. The school's team currently competes in the Big East where it has competed since the 1982–83 season.Providence Women's Basketball All-Time Results, retrieved 2013-Aug-14.  Under coach Kay McDonald, the women’s basketball team began competing in the EAIAW in 1974-75, obtaining a 9-4 record and its first winning season.  The Friars are currently coached by Susan Robinson Fruchtl.
