[[Salim (film)]]

CATEGORIES: 2014 films, Tamil-language films, Indian films, Indian action films, Tamil films of 2014

Salim  is an upcoming Indian Tamil action thriller film directed by N V Nirmal Kumar, who was an associate to Bharathi Raja.[tpl]cite web|url=http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2013-04-11/news-interviews/38462770_1_naan-debut-flick-film|title=First look of Salim|publisher=Times of India  |date=11 April 2013 |accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl]  It stars Vijay Antony and Aksha Pardasany in the lead roles. Produced by Studio 9, Sri Green Productions and Vijay Antony Film Corporation, the film is named after the character Salim that Vijay Antony enacted in his debut movie Naan.
