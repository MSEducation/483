[[Picromerus bidens]]

CATEGORIES: Pentatomidae, Hemiptera of Europe, Hemiptera of Asia, Insects of Africa, Animals described in 1758

Picromerus bidens is a carnivorous species of shield bug in the family Pentatomidae. A large (12 to 13.5 mm long) and distinctive predatory shieldbug, which has unmistakable thorn-like projections on the front of the pronotum.http://www.britishbugs.org.uk/heteroptera/Pentatomidae/picromerus_bidens.html
It has a wide distribution in the Palaearctic, from 64°N to North Africa and from the British Isles to China.
