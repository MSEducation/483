[[Kaarma Parish]]

CATEGORIES: Kaarma Parish

Kaarma Parish is situated in Estonia, Saare County, next to Kuressaare.
The municipality has a population of 4,608 (as of 1 January 2012)[tpl]cite web|title=Asulad|url=http://www.kaarma.ee/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=162&Itemid=814|publisher=Kaarma vald|accessdate=3 April 2012|language=Estonian[/tpl] and covers an area of 400 km². It has 87 km of land border and 98 km of waterline.[tpl]cite web|title=Üldinfo|url=http://www.kaarma.ee|publisher=Kaarma vald|accessdate=3 April 2012|language=Estonian[/tpl]
Islands in Kaarma Parish: Abruka, Kasselaid, Linnusitamaa, Vahase, Väike-Tulpe.

==Settlements==

Kaarma Parish contains 70 settlements: 3 small boroughs and 67 villages.
