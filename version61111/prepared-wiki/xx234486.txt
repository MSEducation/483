[[Robert Vikestad]]

CATEGORIES: Norwegian footballers, Ranheim Fotball players, FK Bodø/Glimt players, 1983 births, Living people

Robert Vikestad (born 15 December 1983) was a Norwegian footballer who played on the wing for Bodø/Glimt. He was signed for Glimt from Ranheim in the 2008 season by his former coach Kåre Ingebrigtsen. Vikestad played a few games for Glimt over the season, but mostly played for Bodø/Glimt 2, and was a regular fixture in the second division for the second team.
In the 2009 season, Vikestad quit Glimt due to lack of motivation. He is now player-coach at third division side Charlottenlund SK.
