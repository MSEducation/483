[[Minamiminowa, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Minamiminowa|南箕輪村|Minamiminowa-mura[/tpl] is a village located in Kamiina District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the village has an estimated population of 13,559 and a density of 331.52 persons per km². The total area is 40.90 km².
