[[Hasan Abdali]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Hasan Abdali ([tpl]lang-fa|حسن ابدالي[/tpl], also Romanized as Ḩasan Abdālī; also known as Hasanabdāl, Ḩasan ‘Abdāl, and Khasanabdal)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3066119|Hasan Abdali[/tpl] is a village in Mojezat Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 276, in 71 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
