[[R.530]]

CATEGORIES: Air-to-air missiles of France

The Matra R.530 is a French medium to short range air-to-air missile.
It was available in IR (passive infrared) and SARH as the main armament of the Mirage III which was able to carry a single missile in the centerline, the Mirage F1, which carried 2 (1 of each type) under the wings, and the F-8 Crusader in the French Navy service.
