[[Jochen Babock]]

CATEGORIES: Bobsledders at the 1976 Winter Olympics, German bobsledders, Living people, Olympic bobsledders of East Germany, Olympic gold medalists for East Germany, 1953 births, Sportspeople from Erfurt, Olympic medalists in bobsleigh

[tpl]MedalCountry|[tpl]flagcountry|GDR[/tpl][/tpl]
Jochen Babock (born 26 August 1953 in Erfurt) is an East German bobsledder who competed in the mid-1970s. He won the gold medal in the four-man event at the 1976 Winter Olympics in Innsbruck.
