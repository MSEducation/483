[[Lacey Baker]]

Lacey Baker (born November 24, 1991) is an American professional skateboarder from Covina, California, who primarily focuses on "goofy-footed" street tricks.

==Skateboarding==

Lacey Baker is a part of a small group of professional female skaters.  She began skateboarding at the age of 5, but she only started focusing on it at the age of 11.[tpl]dead link|date=May 2012[/tpl]  Baker lists her proudest moment as the first time she landed a kickflip.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.elementskateboards.com/team/lacey-baker/ |title=Element Europe | Skateboards, Shoes, Clothes, Events, Videos, Online Store Mens & Women's Apparel, Skateboards & Footwear |publisher=Elementskateboards.com |date= |accessdate=2012-05-23[/tpl] Baker is said to be one of the best female skaters in the world.[tpl]citation needed|date=February 2009[/tpl]
In the March 2007 edition of The Skateboard Mag, Baker is featured in 2 photo sequences showing her perform a 360 flip and a hardflip down 8 stairs.  This was part of the magazine's article "Killer Kids."[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.theskateboardindustry.com/tsi/skater.aspx?ID=1391 |title=Welcome to SPoT Skate Shop at Skatepark of Tampa |publisher=Theskateboardindustry.com |date= |accessdate=2012-05-23[/tpl]
