[[2007–08 Primera Divisió]]

CATEGORIES: Primera Divisió seasons, 2007–08 domestic association football leagues, 2007 in Andorra, 2008 in Andorra

The 2007–08 Primera Divisió season is the 13th since its establishment in 1995. The first matches of the season were played on 23 September 2007. FC Rànger's are the defending champions, having won their 2nd title the previous season.
