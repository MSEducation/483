[[Viktor Svedberg]]

CATEGORIES: 1991 births, Living people, Frölunda HC players

Viktor Svedberg (born May 24, 1991) is a Swedish professional ice hockey defenceman. He is currently playing with  the Rockford IceHogs of the American Hockey League (AHL). 
He played with Frölunda HC of the Elitserien from 2010-11 to 2012–13Vastra Frolunda HC's 2010-11 Roster scoring his first Elitserien goal, a game-winning goal, on September 15 at the Fjällräven Center against Modo Hockey's Mikael Tellqvist.
On October 19, 2013, after playing four AHL games with the Rockford IceHogs, Svedberg was signed by the Chicago Blackhawks of the National Hockey League to a two-year, entry level contract.http://blackhawks.nhl.com/club/news.htm?id=687471
