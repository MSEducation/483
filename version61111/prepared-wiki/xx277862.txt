[[Krishna Kriplani]]

CATEGORIES: Nominated members of the Rajya Sabha, Recipients of the Padma Bhushan, 1907 births, 1992 deaths, Indian writers

Krishna Kripalani (1907-1992) was an Indian author and parliamentarian. He was nominated to the Rajya Sabha in 1974 and served  till 1980. He wrote a number of books on Rabindranath Tagore, Mahatma Gandhi and on Indian literature. He was also a recipient of the Padma Bhushan.
