[[Benfica F.C.]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Jamaica

Benfica F.C. is a football team based in Drax Hall, Jamaica.
The team competes in the National Premier League, the highest competitive league in Jamaica.

==History==

Benfica returned to the top flight in 2010 for the first time since the mid-1980s and are the first St Ann representative since Black Stars in 1996, Their coach, Harold Thomas, was dismissed early in the season after a bad start of the season.Lessons to be learnt from Waterhouse and Benfica - Jamaica Observer
