The Fox Broadcasting Company, commonly referred to as the Fox network or simply Fox, is an American commercial broadcasting television network owned by the Fox Entertainment Group.[tpl]cite news |first=Jane |last=Martinson |url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/media/2006/oct/27/broadcasting.business |title=Billionaire dropout still creates by the seat of his pants |work=The Guardian |publisher=Guardian Media Group |date=October 26, 2006 |accessdate=September 22, 2012[/tpl] Though it was officially launched on October 9, 1986,[tpl]cite web |first=Lanford |last=Beard |url=http://popwatch.ew.com/2012/04/21/fox-25th-anniversary/ |title=Fox 25 years later: How the network changed the world |work=Entertainment Weekly |publisher=Time Inc |date=April 21, 2012 |accessdate=September 21, 2012[/tpl] Fox began its official primetime setup on April 5, 1987, with the series Married... with Children and The Tracey Ullman Show airing that night.[tpl]cite web |first=Alan |last=Sepinwall |url=http://www.hitfix.com/blogs/whats-alan-watching/posts/fox-at-25-a-quarter-century-of-risk-taking |title=FOX at 25: A quarter-century of risk-taking |publisher=HitFix |date=April 18, 2012 |accessdate=September 25, 2012[/tpl]
As of October 2012, Fox maintains 19.5 hours of programming per week.[tpl]cite news |first=Leigh |last=Pressley |url=http://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=1454&dat=19920719&id=74dOAAAAIBAJ&sjid=oxQEAAAAIBAJ&pg=1940,1439958 |title=Fox digs in |work=Star-News |publisher=Halifax Media Group |date=July 19, 1992 |accessdate=September 21, 2012[/tpl] The animated comedy series The Simpsons is one of Fox's most popular shows, becoming the network's first series to rank among the top 30 highest-rated shows of a television season after its original debut,[tpl]cite news |title=Nielsen's top 50 shows|work=USA Today|publisher=Gannett Company|page=3D|date=April 18, 1990[/tpl] and is the longest running sitcom, as well as animated series, of all time, contributing to the channel's success. According to Lanford Beard of Entertainment Weekly, "The Simpsons turned Fox from the little network that could into the little network that could not be ignored." The science fiction television series The X-Files also contributed to the network's success, which led to two spin-offs Millennium and The Lone Gunmen.[tpl]cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/entertainment/337699.stm |title=Chris Carter's Millennium a flop |date=May 7, 1999 |accessdate=September 29, 2012 |work=BBC News |publisher=BBC[/tpl][tpl]cite web |url=http://www.tvguide.com/tvshows/the-lone-gunmen/100271 |title=The Lone Gunmen on FOX |work=TV Guide |accessdate=September 29, 2012[/tpl] Fox began airing in high-definition on September 12, 2004, with a series of National Football League (NFL) American football games. Fox had a programming block for children titled Fox Kids, which ran from September 8, 1990 to September 7, 2002.[tpl]cite news |first1=Melissa |last1=Grego |first2=Michael |last2=Schneider |url=http://www.variety.com/article/VR1117852436 |title=Fox Kids net adopted by Fox TV Ent. |work=Variety |publisher=Reed Business Information |date=September 9, 2001 |accessdate=September 29, 2012[/tpl]
Unlike the "three larger networks", which aired primetime programming from 8 to 11 pm (EST) Mondays to Saturdays and 7 to 11 pm (EST) Sundays, Fox has traditionally avoided programming in the 10 pm (EST) time interval, leaving that hour to affiliates to program locally.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.fox.com/schedule/ |title=FOX Broadcasting Company  – FOX Network Schedule |work=Fox Broadcasting Company |publisher=Fox Entertainment Group |accessdate=September 29, 2012[/tpl] On April 21, 2012, Fox celebrated its 25th anniversary, with a two-hour television special, featuring numerous people related to Fox and its shows. It presented Fox's programs 24, American Idol, Cops, Family Guy, Married... with Children, The Simpsons, The X-Files, among numerous other programs. The network's adult cartoons are listed under the Animation Domination banner, which is a Sunday night programming block.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.fox.com/animationdomination/ |title=Animation Domination – Sundays on FOX |work=Fox Broadcasting Company |publisher=Fox Entertainment Group |accessdate=September 22, 2012[/tpl][tpl]cite news |first=David |last=Hinckley |url=http://articles.nydailynews.com/2012-05-14/news/31703985_1_entertainment-chairman-peter-rice-fall-schedule-glee |title=Fox announces 2012–13 fall schedule |work=New York Daily News |publisher=Daily News, L.P |date=May 14, 2012 |accessdate=September 22, 2012[/tpl] Fox is a full member of the North American Broadcasters Association (NABA) and the National Association of Broadcasters (NAB).[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.nabanet.com/nabaweb/about/about.asp |title=About NABA |publisher=North American Broadcasters Association |accessdate=September 22, 2012[/tpl]
