Tainan County was a county of the Republic of China in southern Taiwan. The county capital was Xinying City.

==History==

Tainan County was established in Taiwan Province on 7 January 1946 after the handover of Taiwan from Japan to the Republic of China in October 1945. It merged with the city of Tainan on 25 December 2010 to form a single special municipality.[tpl]cite news | url=http://taiwantoday.tw/ct.asp?xItem=122869&CtNode=124 | title=A Bigger South | last=Gao | first=Pat | work=Taiwan Today | date=2010-11-01 | accessdate=2012-01-10[/tpl]

==Administration==

At the time it merged with city of Tainan, Tainan County administered two cities, seven urban townships and 22 rural townships. All of these became districts of the city of Tainan.
