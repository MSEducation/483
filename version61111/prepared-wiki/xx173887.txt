[[Windward Performance]]

CATEGORIES: Aircraft manufacturers of the United States, Companies based in Bend, Oregon

Windward Performance is a sailplane manufacturer located in  Bend, Oregon. They designed and developed the SparrowHawk ultralight sailplane and the DuckHawk high strength sailplane.Bayerl, Robby; Martin Berkemeier; et al: World Directory of Leisure Aviation 2011-12, page 138. WDLA UK, Lancaster UK, 2011. ISSN 1368-485X
The company also designed and is building the Windward Performance Perlan II.The Perlan II project continues to develop, 24-25. FreeFlight, the Journal of the Soaring Association of Canada, Autumn, 2011.
