[[Electoral results for the district of Upper Hunter]]

CATEGORIES: New South Wales state electoral results by district

This is a list of electoral results for the Electoral district of Upper Hunter in New South Wales state elections. The district had four incarnations, the first from 1856 to 1880, the second from 1880 to 1894, the third from 1904 to 1920, and the fourth from 1927 to the present.
