[[Barry House]]

CATEGORIES: 1949 births, Living people, Members of the Western Australian Legislative Council, Western Australian Liberal politicians, People from Busselton, Western Australia, Presidents of the Western Australian Legislative Council

Barry John House (born 27 November 1949) is an Australian politician. He has been a Liberal member of the Western Australian Legislative Council since 1987, representing the region of South West.
House was born in Busselton, Western Australia. He was elected to parliament at a by-election on 24 October 1987 to fill the seat vacated by Vic Ferry.
House is Parliamentary Secretary to the Treasurer, Parliamentary Secretary to the Minister for Commerce; Science and Innovation; Housing and Works and Deputy Chairman of Committees. He is also a current member of the Standing Committee on Public Administration.
In May 2009, House was elected President of the Legislative Council.
