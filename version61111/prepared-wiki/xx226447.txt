[[Another Left]]

CATEGORIES: Francophone political parties in Belgium, Communist parties in Belgium, Political parties established in 2006, 2006 establishments in Belgium

Another Left (Une Autre Gauche) was an electoral initiative in Wallonia, Belgium.  It was founded in 2006 as a result of proposed cooperation between the Revolutionary Communist LeagueBelgium - Left electoral alliance projects new left party and other left groups.
Its Flemish counterpart is the Committee for Another Policy, with whom it participated in the 2007 federal elections.  It is an observer member of the European Left.
