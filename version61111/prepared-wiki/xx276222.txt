[[(31044) 1996 NY]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1996, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(31044) 1996 NY is an asteroid-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on July 11, 1996.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
