[[Sarieha Alsawaf]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, American female singers, American actresses

Sarieha Alsawaf is an American singer, actress, and model.

==Biography==

At 16, Alsawaf was casted in the role of Princess Jasmine for a show at Walt Disney World. Alsawaf has stated that she has been inspired by actresses such as Angelina Jolie and Catherine Zeta-Jones.Sloan, Paul. "Star Walker Online Interview: Actress/Model Sarieha Alsawaf." Web. 14 Aug. 2013.
