[[Sri Siva Vishnu Temple]]

CATEGORIES: Asian-American culture in Maryland, Hindu temples in Maryland, Places of worship in Prince George's County, Maryland, Religious buildings completed in 2002, 2002 establishments in Maryland

Sri Siva Vishnu Temple (SSVT) is a Hindu temple located in Lanham, Maryland, about [tpl]convert|12|mi|km[/tpl] from Washington D.C. It is one of the largest temples in the United States. The actual temple construction began in 1988 and the last deity consecration took place in 2002.

==Location==

The Temple is located on Cipriano Road one mile away from the NASA Goddard Space Flight Center. There are two churches located on the same road. The Murugan Temple of North America is located in the vicinity.

==Deities==
