[[94.1 FM Creston, British Columbia]]

CATEGORIES: Adult hits radio stations in Canada, Hot adult contemporary radio stations in Canada, Radio stations in British Columbia, Proposed radio stations

94.1 FM is a new radio station which will broadcast an English language adult hits/hot adult contemporary format on the frequency of 94.1 MHz/FM in the Southern Interior town of Creston, British Columbia, Canada. The new station, to be owned by the Vista Broadcast Group, received approval by the Canadian Radio-television and Telecommunications Commission (CRTC) on August 9, 2013.Broadcasting Decision CRTC 2013-397, English-language commercial FM radio station in Creston, CRTC, August 9, 2013
