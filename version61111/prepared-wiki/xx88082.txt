[[8th millennium]]

CATEGORIES: Millennia, Centuries in the future, Years in the future, Future timelines

The eighth millennium is a period of time that will begin on January 1, 7001, and will end on December 31, 8000.

==Astronomical events==

All these dates are in a uniform time scale such as Terrestrial Time. When converted to our ordinary solar time or Universal Time, which is decidedly non-uniform, via Î”T, the dates would be about one day earlier.
