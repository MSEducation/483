[[WXBC (FM)]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Kentucky

WXBC (104.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Classic Country format. Licensed to Hardinsburg, Kentucky, USA.  The station is currently owned by Breckinridge Broadcasting Co., Inc. and features programing from Fox News Radio.
