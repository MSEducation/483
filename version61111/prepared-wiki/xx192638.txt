==Technical information==

The album was recorded and mixed at Streamline Sound Studios, between February 2007 and April 2007 in Salt Lake City. It was produced by Mike Foweks and the band itself, Curtis Morell mixed the album. mastering for the USA version by Lance King, mastering for European version by Michael Alexiou. The artwork and layout was done by Mattias Noren of ProgArt Media.
