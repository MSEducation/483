[[Marxist Philosophy Forum]]

CATEGORIES: Political parties established in 1986, Communist parties in the United Kingdom, Workers Revolutionary Party (UK), 1986 establishments in the United Kingdom

The Communist Forum was formed in 1986 by Michael Banda as a breakaway from the Workers' Revolutionary Party (UK). It later became the Marxist Philosophy Forum in 1987, and died out shortly afterwards.Encyclopedia of British and Irish political organizations parties, groups By Peter Barberis, John McHugh, Mike Tyldesley, p.154, p.170
