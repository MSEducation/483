[[Dimitrios Ypsilantis (municipality)]]

CATEGORIES: Former municipalities in West Macedonia

Dimitrios Ypsilantis ([tpl]lang-el|Δημήτριος Υψηλάντης[/tpl]) is a former municipality in Kozani regional unit, West Macedonia, Greece. Since the 2011 local government reform it is part of the municipality Kozani, of which it is a municipal unit.Kallikratis law Greece Ministry of Interior [tpl]el icon[/tpl] It was named after Demetrius Ypsilanti, a 19th-century leader of the Greek struggle for independence. Population 3,018 (2001). The seat of the municipality was in Mavrodendri.
