[[Salamanca AC]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Spain, Salamanca AC, Association football clubs established in 2013, 2013 establishments in Spain

Salamanca Athletic Club, S.A.D. is a Spanish football team based in Salamanca, in the autonomous community of Castile and León. Founded on 28 June 2013, it does not play any competition.
Club colours are white shirts and black shorts.Oficial. Así será la equipación del Salamanca Athletic (Official. This will be Salamanca Athletic's kits); Sport Salamanca, 9 August 2013 [tpl]es icon[/tpl]

==History==

Salamanca AC was founded on 28 June 2013, as a replacement for UD Salamanca which was dissolved due to financial issues. Initially assigned to Tercera División,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.rfef.es/GetDoc?UniqueKeyValue=73484&ShowPath=false&Download=true|title=Circular 1 2013–2014|trans_title=Note 1 2013–14|publisher=Royal Spanish Football Federation|language=Spanish|date=|accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl] the Royal Spanish Football Federation admitted the club in Segunda División B shortly after, but finally it did not register to play in any competition.
