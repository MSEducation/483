[[Siddartha of Kundgraam]]

CATEGORIES: Indian monarchs, Jain monarchs

King Siddartha(also known as Siddhartharaja) was the father of Mahavira, the 24th and last Tirthankara. He was  the ruler of the kingdom of Kundagrama in what is present-day Bihar, India. He was an important figure in Jain history. His wife was Trishala.Encyclopedia Brittanica
