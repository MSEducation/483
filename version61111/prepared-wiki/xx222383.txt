[[Entrance Island (Tasmania)]]

CATEGORIES: Islands of Tasmania, Macquarie Harbour, Coastline of Tasmania

Entrance Island is a low island with an area of 6.1 ha in south-eastern Australia.  It lies at the entrance to Macquarie Harbour in Western Tasmania. ==Fauna==

Recorded breeding seabird species are the Little Penguin (100 pairs) and Short-tailed Shearwater (1900 pairs).  The Metallic Skink is present.
