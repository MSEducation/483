[[Zhan Silu]]

CATEGORIES: 1961 births, Living people, Bishops of the Chinese Patriotic Catholic Association, Chinese bishops, Chinese Catholics

Zhan Silu (born c. 1961) is the Chinese Patriotic Catholic Association-sponsored Bishop of the archdiocese of Mindong, Fujian, China. He was consecrated as bishop in 2000 but had been serving as a deputy to another who died in 2005.
