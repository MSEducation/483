[[Qarkhotlu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Qarkhotlu ([tpl]lang-fa|قارختلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Qārkhotlū; also known as Karkhurta, Qarakhurtali, Qārkhowdlū, Qārkhūdlū, Qārkhūtlī, Qārkhūtlū, and Qārkhvodlū)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3079810|Qarkhotlu[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Bala Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 555, in 136 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
