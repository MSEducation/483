[[Emam Kandi, Khodabandeh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Emam Kandi ([tpl]lang-fa|امام كندي[/tpl], also Romanized as Emām Kandī)[tpl]GEOnet3|10153463|Emam Kandi[/tpl] is a village in Sojas Rud Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 318, in 72 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
