[[Erald Turdiu]]

CATEGORIES: 1984 births, Living people, Albanian Superliga players, KS Kastrioti Krujë players, KF Tirana players, KS Shkumbini Peqin players

Erald 'Tushe' Turdiu (born July 15, 1984 in Tirana) is an Albanian footballer playing for Kastrioti Krujë in the Albanian Superliga for the third time in his career. He has played for KF Tirana and Shkumbini Peqin.

==Career==

==Personal life==

Turdiu was assaulted by police in 2008 whilst he was a Shkumbini Peqin player, following a brawl to which he was witness. It is claimed that Turdiu had insulted the on-duty police officer and they reacted violently, beating Turdiu in the centre of the capital, Tirana.[tpl]cite web|url=http://lajme.shqiperia.com/lajme/artikull/iden/126164/titulli/Rrihet-ne-polici-futbollisti-i-Peqinit-Erald-Turdiu|title=Rrihet në polici futbollisti i Peqinit, Erald Turdiu|author=Gerti Xhaja|language=Albania|publisher=Info Arkiv|accessdate=2011-09-23|date=2008-11-11[/tpl]
In December 2010, Turdiu brutally assaulted Albanian Superliga referee Laver Alla, outside a bar near Tirana International Airport. He faced a lifetime ban by the FSHF as the player had a string of offences and bans, including an 8 match ban following a mass brawl during the Albanian Superliga 2010 Playoff finals.[tpl]cite web|url=http://news-al.com/sport/dhunoi-gjyqtarin-turdiu-rrezikon-pezullimin-perjete.html|title=Dhunoi gjyqtarin, Turdiu rrezikon pezullimin përjetë|author=|language=Albania|publisher=News-Al|accessdate=2011-09-23|date=2010-12-26[/tpl] He received a 4-year ban by the FSHF but following an appeal by the player and his club Kastrioti Krujë, the ban was lifted after just 8 months meaning Turdiu was eligible to play at the start of the 2011-12 campaign.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.sportekspres.com/contenti.aspx?id=521&idd=562&iddd=614&idjadok=8021|title=Disiplina fal Erald Turdiun|author=|language=Albania|publisher=Sport Ekspres|accessdate=2011-09-23|date=2011-07-13[/tpl]
