[[Sunday in August]]

CATEGORIES: 1950 films, Italian films, Black-and-white films, Italian-language films, 1950s comedy films, Films directed by Luciano Emmer

Sunday in August ([tpl]lang-it|Domenica d'agosto[/tpl]) is a 1950 Italian comedy film directed by Luciano Emmer. The film was nominated for a BAFTA.
