[[Naval Station Bremerton]]

CATEGORIES: Buildings and structures in Kitsap County, Washington, United States Navy facilities, Military facilities in Washington (state)

Naval Station Bremerton is a former station of the United States Navy that was merged with Naval Submarine Base Bangor into Naval Base Kitsap in 2004.[tpl]cite web|title=Naval Base Kitsap|url=http://www.cnic.navy.mil/regions/cnrnw/installations/navbase_kitsap.html|publisher=United States Navy|accessdate=23 January 2014[/tpl] It is home to Naval Hospital Bremerton.
