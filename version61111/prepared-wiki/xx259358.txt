[[Sonny Shepherd]]

CATEGORIES: Cook Islands national rugby league team players, Cook Island rugby league players, Place of birth missing (living people), Year of birth missing (living people), Living people

Sonny Shepherd is a Cook Islander rugby league footballer of the 1990s and 2000s, playing at representative level for Cook Islands, and at club level for Ngatangiia/Matavera Sea Eagles.
