[[Roman Catholic Diocese of São Luís de Montes Belos]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1961, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Goiânia, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century, 1961 establishments in Brazil

The Roman Catholic Diocese of São Luís de Montes Belos ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Sancti Aloisii de Montes Belos[/tpl]) is a diocese centred on the city of São Luís de Montes Belos and part of the ecclesiastical province of Goiânia in Brazil.

==History==

It was established as the Territorial Prelature of São Luís de Montes Belos on 25 November 1961 with territory taken from the Metropolitan Archdiocese of Goiânia, the Diocese of Goiás and the Diocese of Jataí, and was entrusted to the Dutch members of the Passionist religious institute.História da Diocese
It became the Diocese of São Luís de Montes Belos on 4 August 1981.
