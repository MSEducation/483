Andreas Amundsen (born 1980 in Senja, Norway) is a Norwegian jazz musician (upright bass).[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.jazzorakel.no/o/Person:Andreas_Amundsen |title=Andreas Amundsen Biography |publisher=JazzOrakel.no |accessdate=2013-08-08[/tpl]

==Career==

Amundsen attended the Jazz program at Trondheim Musikkonservatorium (2004–06), but did not graduate.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.ntnu.no/jazzlinja25/studentene.htm |title=Jazzlinja |publisher=NTNU.no |accessdate=2013-08-08[/tpl] He is distinguished for an European style in his musical expression.
He has performed with «North» the quartet of Håvard Stubø, with Tore Johansen Quartet, in a quartet with Ed Thigpen, Espen Reinertsen's «Platoon», and at the Trondheim Jazz Festival, 2005, with John Pål Inderberg and Erling Aksdal. 
In the summer of 2013 he played at the club «Herr Nilsen» together with Einar "Pastor'n" Iversen (piano), Staffan William-Olsson (guitar) and Andreas Bye (drums).[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.herrnilsen.no/H111210e.html |title=Einar Iversen/Staffan William-Olsson kvartett (OJ) |publisher=HerrNilsen.no |accessdate=2013-08-08[/tpl]
