[[Foxcroft School]]

CATEGORIES: Boarding schools in Virginia, Private high schools in Virginia, Independent School League, Schools in Loudoun County, Virginia, Educational institutions established in 1914, Girls' schools in Virginia

Foxcroft School, founded in 1914 by Ms. Charlotte Haxall Noland, is a college-preparatory boarding and day school for girls in grades 9-12, located near Middleburg, Virginia, United States.[tpl]cite web | title = Foxcroft's Accolade | publisher = Time Inc. | date = December 9, 1929 | url = http://www.time.com/time/magazine/article/0,9171,738262-1,00.html | accessdate = 2007-05-16[/tpl] It is accredited by the Virginia Association of Independent Schools Virginia Association of Independent Schools and the National Association of Independent School,National Association of Independent School and is a founding member of the National Coalition of Girls' Schools.National Coalition of Girls' School
The tuition for the 2013-2014 school year is $49,400 for boarders and $41,580 for day students.
