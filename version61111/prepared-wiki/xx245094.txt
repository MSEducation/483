[[Gunnison Gorge Wilderness]]

CATEGORIES: Wilderness Areas of Colorado, Protected areas of Montrose County, Colorado, Protected areas of Delta County, Colorado, Protected areas established in 1999, Bureau of Land Management areas in Colorado

The Gunnison Gorge Wilderness is a U.S. Wilderness Area located immediately northwest of the Black Canyon of the Gunnison National Park.  The [tpl]convert|17784|acre|km2|adj=on[/tpl] wilderness area established in 1999 includes [tpl]convert|14|mi|km[/tpl] of the Gunnison River inside the Gunnison Gorge National Conservation Area.[tpl]cite web |title=Gunnison Gorge Wilderness |publisher=Bureau of Land Management |url=http://www.blm.gov/co/st/en/nca/ggnca/gorge.html |accessdate=August 10, 2012[/tpl][tpl]cite web |title=Gunnison Gorge Wilderness |publisher=Wilderness.net |url=http://www.wilderness.net/index.cfm?fuse=NWPS&sec=wildView&WID=227 |accessdate=August 10, 2012[/tpl]
