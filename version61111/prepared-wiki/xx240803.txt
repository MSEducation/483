[[KCTY (AM)]]

CATEGORIES: Country radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Nebraska

KCTY (1590 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a classic country music format.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www1.arbitron.com/sip/sip.do#access |date=Summer 2009 |title=Station Information Profile |publisher=Arbitron |accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl] Licensed to Wayne, Nebraska, USA, the station is currently owned by Wayne Radio Works LLC. [tpl]cite web |url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=KCTY |title=KCTY Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division |accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl]

==History==

On January 13, 2003, the station was sold to WMMP. On December 28, 2005, the station was sold to Nrg Media. On February 19, 2008, the station was sold to Red Beacon Communications LLC, and on June 4, 2008, the station was sold to Wayne Radio Works LLC.[tpl]cite web|url= |title=FCC Application  |publisher=Federal Communications Commission |accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl]
