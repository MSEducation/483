[[Desnúdate Mujer]]

CATEGORIES: David Bisbal songs, 2004 singles

Desnúdate Mujer is the 2004 song released by popular Spanish singer David Bisbal. The song is a passionate and sexual ballad, pleading a woman to literally "get naked" and be unfaithful to her partner with the singer.
The song peaked at #6 on the Billboard Hot Latin Songs Chart.
