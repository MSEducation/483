==Family Background==

Evelyn is daughter of Dan Quigley, Wexford's All-Ireland winning Senior hurling captain of 1968. She spent some time in London where she lined out with the Fr. Murphy's club, and also with the county team in the All Ireland Junior championship.[tpl]cite book | last = Moran | first = Mary | title = A Game of Our Own: The History of Camogie | publisher = Cumann Camógaíochta | year = 2011 | location = Dublin, Ireland | pages = 460[/tpl][tpl]cite book | last = Goodison | first = Dean | title = I Gotta Feeling: Wexford 2010 All-Ireland Champions| publisher = Dean Goodison | year = 2010 | location = Wexford, Ireland | pages = 208[/tpl]
