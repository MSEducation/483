[[Irstead]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Norfolk, North Norfolk

Irstead is a village in The Broads National Park in the English county of Norfolk, England. The village is situated at Irstead Shoals, on the River Ant just south of Barton Broad, the second largest of the Norfolk Broads. The village forms part of the civil parish of Barton Turf[tpl]Citation needed|date=June 2009|doubt=Neatishead is closer[/tpl], and the district of North Norfolk.Ordnance Survey (2005). OS Explorer Map OL40 - The Broads. ISBN 0-319-23769-9.
Several of the village's buildings are reed-thatched, including in particular the very attractive St Michael's Church.
