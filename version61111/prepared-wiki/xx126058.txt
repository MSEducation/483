[[Association Salé]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Morocco, Sport in Salé, Association football clubs established in 1928, 1928 establishments in Morocco, Sports clubs in Morocco

AS Salé is a Moroccan football club based in Salé.
The club was founded in 1928.  They used to play their home games at Stade Marche Verte. The new field is located in Bettana. In 2004 AS Salé became vice-champion.
