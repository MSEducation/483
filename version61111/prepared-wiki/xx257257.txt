[[Enrico Borla]]

CATEGORIES: 1959 births, Living people, Psychodynamics, Psychology writers, People from Turin

Enrico Borla is an Italian writer, psychiatrist and psychotherapist, founder and editor of the magazine "Radure" Notebooks of psychic material, published in Italy by Moretti&Vitali.
Enrico Borla is the author of several publications and essays.
