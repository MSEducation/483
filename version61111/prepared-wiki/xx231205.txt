[[Pseudoliparis amblystomopsis]]

CATEGORIES: Liparidae

The hadal snailfish, Pseudoliparis amblystomopsis is a species of snailfish, described by Anatoly Petrovich Andriashev in 1955.[tpl]FishBase species|genus=Pseudoliparis|species=amblystomopsis|month=March|year=2009[/tpl]
In October 2008, a team of researchers from the University of Aberdeen's Oceanlab and the University of Tokyo's Ocean Research Institute discovered a shoal of P. amblystomopsis at a depth of [tpl]Convert|7700|m|ft|-2[/tpl] in the Japan Trench. These are, to date, the deepest living fish ever recorded.http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/sci/tech/7655358.stm
