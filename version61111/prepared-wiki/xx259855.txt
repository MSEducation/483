Steven Andrew Slocum[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.onlineworldofwrestling.com/profiles/j/jackson-andrews.html|title=Jackson Andrews|accessdate=December 31, 2010|publisher=Online World of Wrestling[/tpl] (born November 5) is an American professional wrestler, better known by his time in WWE under the ring name Jackson Andrews, and wrestled in Florida Championship Wrestling, a WWE developmental promotion. He is best known for being the enforcer of Tyson Kidd while on the Raw brand.

==Professional wrestling career==

Slocum wrestled as 'Cyrus' for the Pro Wrestling Alliance (PWA) in Texas, where he was managed by Lady Poison. He made his PWA debut at Clash of Champions on July 21, 2007 in Pasadena, Texas, where he defeated Devin in a squash match.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.onlineworldofwrestling.com/results/pwa-bookert/|title=Pro Wrestling Alliance (Booker T)|accessdate=December 31, 2010|publisher=Online World of Wrestling[/tpl] He also appeared at PWA's next show, Midsummer Madness, where he defeated Santana in a squash. Cyrus went on to defeat Nicolas, Samson, and Robbie Gilmore throughout late 2007 and early 2008.

===World Wrestling Entertainment / WWE===

In September 2009, it was reported that Slocum had signed a contract with World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE), and had been assigned to Florida Championship Wrestling (FCW), a WWE developmental territory under the ring name 'Jackson Andrews'.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.wrestleview.com/viewnews.php?id=1253647637|title=WWE signs Booker T wrestling student|date=September 22, 2009|last=Martin|first=Adam|accessdate=December 31, 2010|publisher=WrestleView[/tpl] He made his in-ring debut in late 2009.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.wrestleview.com/viewnews.php?id=1257140722|title=News and notes on WWE's FCW|date=November 2, 2009|last=Martin|first=Adam|accessdate=December 31, 2010|publisher=WrestleView[/tpl]
In early 2010, Andrews formed an alliance and tag team with Curt Hawkins, with the pair debuting as a tag team on February 18 by defeating The South Beach Boys (Darren Young and Percy Watson).[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.onlineworldofwrestling.com/results/fcw/_2010/|title=Florida Championship Wrestling (2010)|accessdate=December 31, 2010|publisher=Online World of Wrestling[/tpl] The two began feuding with Hawkins' former stablemates The Dude Busters (Caylen Croft and Trent Barreta), who Hawkins had abandoned to ally with Andrews. Andrews went on to defeat both Croft and Barreta in singles matches in March. Over the next few months, Andrews and Hawkins went on to face The Rotundo Brothers (Bo and Duke), and the team of Eli Cottonwood and Wes Brisco.
