[[Gmina Przesmyki]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Siedlce County

Gmina Przesmyki is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Siedlce County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Przesmyki, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|25|km|mi|0[/tpl] north-east of Siedlce and [tpl]convert|109|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] east of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|117.13|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 3,694.

==Villages==

Gmina Przesmyki contains the villages and settlements of Cierpigórz, Dąbrowa, Głuchówek, Górki, Kaliski, Kamianki Lackie, Kamianki-Czabaje, Kamianki-Nicki, Kamianki-Wańki, Kolonia Łysowa, Kukawki, Lipiny, Łysów, Pniewiski, Podraczynie, Przesmyki, Raczyny, Stare Rzewuski, Tarków, Tarkówek, Wólka Łysowska, Zaborów, Zalesie and Zawady.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Przesmyki is bordered by the gminas of Korczew, Łosice, Mordy, Paprotnia and Platerów.
