[[Arundhati Pantawane]]

CATEGORIES: Indian badminton players, Living people, 1989 births, Badminton players at the 2010 Asian Games, Sport in Nagpur, People from Nagpur

Arundhati Pantawane (born 2 September 1989) is an Indian badminton player who plays singles. She is currently the third highest ranking Indian in the women's singles badminton. Also as per the National circuit  she tops the Women's Singles rankings in India.Women's Singles She was born in Nagpur to Avinash Pantawane and Chitra Pantawane. She has a sister Abhilasha Pantawane who plays cricket.Ranking Worlds
