[[Mount Kerkeslin]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of Alberta

Mount Kerkeslin is a peak in the Canadian Rockies in Alberta, Canada. It is the highest peak of the Maligne Range.
It is located in the nsouth parts of the Maligne Range, east of the Icefield Parkway and is visible from the Athabasca Falls lookout.
