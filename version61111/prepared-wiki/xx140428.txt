[[Jeff Chadwick]]

CATEGORIES: 1960 births, American football wide receivers, Detroit Lions players, Grand Valley State Lakers football players, Living people, Los Angeles Rams players, Sportspeople from Detroit, Michigan, Seattle Seahawks players

Jeffrey Allan Chadwick (born December 16, 1960 in Detroit, Michigan), is a former professional American football player. A 6'3", 185-lb. wide receiver from Grand Valley State University, Chadwick was never drafted by a National Football League team, but did play in 10 NFL seasons from 1983 to 1992 for the Detroit Lions, the Seattle Seahawks, and the Los Angeles Rams.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/C/ChadJe00.htm|title=Jeff Chadwick|publisher=Profootball.com|accessdate=28 February 2010[/tpl] He is currently the Receiver/Tight End Coach at Adrenaline Athletic Training in Riverside, CA.
