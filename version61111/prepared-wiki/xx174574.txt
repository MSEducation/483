[[KIMX]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Wyoming, Radio stations established in 1999, Contemporary hit radio stations in the United States

KIMX (96.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Top 40 (CHR) format. The station is licensed to Laramie, Wyoming, USA. The station is currently owned by Appaloosa Broadcasting Company, Inc. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KIMX |title=KIMX Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==History==

The station went on the air as KBPV on 1999-10-18.  On 1999-11-01, the station changed its call sign to KKRR. On 2001-11-07 to KHAT. On 2003-11-18 to the current KIMX.
