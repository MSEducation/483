[[Anthony Rosano]]

CATEGORIES: 1977 births, Living people, American male pornographic film actors, People from Hialeah, Florida, Pornographic film actors from Florida

Anthony Rosano (Born Michael McKay) is an American pornographic actor. He was awarded with the Best Male Newcomer award in the 2009 AVN Awards ceremony. He is often known for playing comedic characters or buff characters in the movies. He is also the lead singer of Till All Is One.
