[[Michel Nielsen Grendahl]]

CATEGORIES: 1785 births, Year of death missing, Members of the Parliament of Norway, Sør-Trøndelag politicians

Michel Nielsen Grendahl (1785 - ??) was a Norwegian politician.
He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament in 1830, 1833, 1836 and 1842, representing the rural constituency of Søndre Trondhjems Amt (today named Sør-Trøndelag). He worked as a farmer.Michel Nielsen Grendahl — Norwegian Social Science Data Services (NSD)
