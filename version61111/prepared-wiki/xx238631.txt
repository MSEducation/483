[[Ophiostomataceae]]

CATEGORIES: Sordariomycetes, Fungi families

The Ophiostomataceae are a family of fungi in the Ascomycota, class Sordariomycetes.[tpl]cite journal |last=Lumbsch TH, Huhndorf SM. |date=December 2007 |title=Outline of Ascomycota – 2007 |journal=Myconet |volume=13 |issue= |pages=1–58 |id= |url=http://www.fieldmuseum.org/myconet/outline.asp |accessdate= |publisher=The Field Museum, Department of Botany, Chicago, USA [/tpl] The family was named by J.A. Nannfeldt in 1932.[tpl]cite journal |author=Nannfeldt, J. A. | authorlink= John Axel Nannfeldt |year=1932 |title=Studien über die morphologie und systematik der nichtlichenisierten inoperculaten Discomyceten |journal=Nova Acta Regiae Societatis Scientiarum upsaliensis |volume=8, series IV |issue=2 |page=30 |language=German [/tpl] Species in the family have a widespread distribution, and are typically found in temperate regions, as pathogens of both coniferous and deciduous trees.[tpl]cite book |author=Cannon PF, Kirk PM. |title=Fungal Families of the World |publisher=CABI|location=Wallingford |year=2007 |pages=249–50 |isbn=0-85199-827-5[/tpl]
