[[Stadice]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in the Czech Republic, Neighbourhoods in the Czech Republic, Populated places in Ústí nad Labem District

Stadice is a small village near Teplice in the northern part of the Czech Republic. It was the home of the legendary ruler Přemysl, the Ploughman. It is a part of the village of Řehlovice.

==Points of interest==

Stadice has a ruined 14th-century Gothic Castle http://www.mag-ul.cz/en/tours.asp, as well as nearby Royal field with the monument to Přemysl, the Ploughman.
