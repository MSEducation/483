[[Hit or Miss (song)]]

CATEGORIES: 1999 singles, 2000 singles, New Found Glory songs, Songs written by Steve Klein (musician), Songs written by Chad Gilbert, Song recordings produced by Chad Gilbert

"Hit or Miss" is the first single from A New Found Glory's debut studio album, Nothing Gold Can Stay.[tpl]Citation needed|date=November 2011[/tpl]
In 2000, "Hit or Miss" was re-recorded for the band's second studio album New Found Glory. The new version charted at number 15 on the Billboard Modern Rock Tracks chart in April 2001 and number 58 on the UK Singles Chart in June 2001.
