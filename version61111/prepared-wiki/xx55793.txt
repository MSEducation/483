[[New Best Friend]]

CATEGORIES: 2002 films, American films, English-language films, TriStar Pictures films, 2000s thriller films, Teen films, Psychological thriller films, Films shot in North Carolina, Films shot in South Carolina

New Best Friend is a 2002 American film based on a story by author James Edwards. The film was originally owned by MGM, which eventually let this film go.  Since then, TriStar Pictures acquired the rights to distribute this film in the United States and some other territories, primarily for home video market;[tpl]cite web|title=Columbia, Winchester take over New Best Friend|url=http://www.screendaily.com/columbia-winchester-take-over-new-best-friend/407380.article|work=Screen Daily|accessdate=4 January 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Crash registers at B.O.|url=http://www.variety.com/article/VR1117865366/?refCatId=1082|work=Variety|accessdate=4 January 2013[/tpl] still, TriStar Pictures gave this film a limited theatrical release in the United States on April 12, 2002.[tpl]cite web|title=New Best Friend|url=http://boxofficemojo.com/movies/?id=newbestfriend.htm|work=Box Office Mojo|accessdate=4 January 2013[/tpl]

==Summary==

A North Carolina sheriff (Taye Diggs) investigates the near-fatal drug overdose of a working class college girl (Mia Kirshner) and discovers many sordid details of her life before and during her descent into drugs and debauchery.http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0191074/
