[[Lázaro Ruiz]]

CATEGORIES: Cuban weightlifters, 1984 births, Living people, Weightlifters at the 2008 Summer Olympics, Olympic weightlifters of Cuba

Lázaro Maikel Ruiz (born October 12, 1984) is a Cuban weightlifter. 
At the 2008 Pan American Weightlifting Championships he won bronze in the 62 kg category, lifting a total of 284 kg.[tpl]cite web|title=RUIZ Lazaro M.|publisher=International Weightlifting Federation|url=http://www.iwf.net/results/results_athlete_e.php?compid=1562[/tpl]
He competed in Weightlifting at the 2008 Summer Olympics in the 62 kg division finishing sixth, with 294 kg,[tpl]cite web|title=RUIZ Lazaro|publisher=International Weightlifting Federation|url=http://www.iwf.net/results/results_athlete_e.php?compid=2576[/tpl] beating his previous personal best by 10 kg.
