[[Howmeh Rural District (Khodabandeh County)]]

CATEGORIES: Rural Districts of Zanjan Province, Khodabandeh County

Howmeh Rural District ([tpl]lang-fa|دهستان حومه[/tpl]) is a rural district (dehestan) in the Central District of Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 19,186, in 4,073 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl] The rural district has 21 villages.
