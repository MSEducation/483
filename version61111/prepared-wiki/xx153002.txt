[[Aiko Miyake]]

CATEGORIES: 1978 births, Living people, Freestyle swimmers, Swimmers at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Olympic swimmers of Japan

[tpl]Nihongo|Aiko Miyake|三宅 愛子|Miyake Aiko[/tpl] (born July 6, 1978 in Tokushima) is a former freestyle swimmer from Japan. She competed for her native country at the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta, Georgia. There, she finished in fourth place in the 4x20 0m freestyle relay, alongside Eri Yamanoi, Naoko Imoto, and Suzu Chiba. On her sole individual start, in the 80 0m freestyle, she ended up in 21st place, clocking 8:55.77 in the preliminary heats.
