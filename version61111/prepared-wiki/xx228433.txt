Countess Maria CarolinaOr in Polish, Karolina Zamoyska[tpl]cite web | url=http://thepeerage.com/p11217.htm#i112170| title=Marie Karoline Zamoyski, Countess Saryusz von Zamoysc-Zamoyska| date=10 May 2003 | accessdate=2008-10-06 | publisher=thePeerage.com | author=Darryl Lundy[/tpl][tpl]cite web | url=http://www.angelfire.com/realm/gotha/gotha/2sicilies.html| title=TWO SICILIES | date= | accessdate=2008-10-06 | publisher=Paul Theroff's Royal Genealogy Site | author=Paul Theroff[/tpl] [tpl]Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot[/tpl] (22 September 1896, Kraków, Poland – 9 May 1968, Marseille, France) was the wife of Prince Ranieri, Duke of Castro, claimant to the headship of the House of Bourbon-Two Sicilies. Carolina was the sixth child and youngest daughter of Polish nobleman Andrzej Przemysław Zamoyski, Count Zamoyski and his wife Princess Maria Carolina of Bourbon-Two Sicilies. Carolina's maternal grandparents were Prince Francis of Bourbon-Two Sicilies, Count of Trapani and his wife Archduchess Maria Isabella of Austria, Princess of Tuscany.
