[[NCAA Division I FBS football win-loss records]]

CATEGORIES: Lists of college football team records

The following data is current as of the end of the 2013 season, which ended after the 2014 BCS National Championship Game. The following list reflects the records according to the NCAA. This list takes into account results modified later due to NCAA action, such as vacated victories and forfeits.  This list includes teams that are transitioning from FCS to FBS (Appalachian State and Georgia Southern). Percentages are figured to 3 decimal places. In the event of a tie, the team with the most wins is listed first.
