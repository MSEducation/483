Iceland has [tpl]convert|12869|km|mi[/tpl] of publicly administered roads, [tpl]convert|5040|km|mi[/tpl] of which are paved.[tpl]cite web | title=Samgönguáætlun 2009-2012 (National transport plan 2009-2012) | publisher=Alþingi (Icelandic parliament) | accessdate=2010-04-25 | url=http://www.althingi.is/altext/138/s/pdf/0973.pdf[/tpl] Organized road building began about 1900 and has greatly expanded since 1980. Vegagerðin (Icelandic Roads Administration) is the legal owner and constructor of the roads, and oversees and maintains them as well.
