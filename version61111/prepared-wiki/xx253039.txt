[[Anabarilius grahami]]

CATEGORIES: Anabarilius, Endemic fauna of Yunnan, China, Fish of China

Anabarilius grahami is a species of cyprinid fish. It is a pelagic species endemic to Fuxian Lake in Yunnan, southern China.[tpl]cite doi|10.1007/s10641-009-9529-9[/tpl] However, the species may now be in the process of extinction because of the introduced noodlefish Neosalanx taihuensis, with which it is competing for food.[tpl]cite doi|10.1080/02705060.2007.9664165[/tpl]
