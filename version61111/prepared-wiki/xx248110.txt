[[Ismail Mohamed Said]]

CATEGORIES: Malaysian politicians, Living people, Members of the Dewan Rakyat, United Malays National Organisation politicians

Datuk Ismail Mohamed Said is a Malaysian politician and the Member of the Parliament of Malaysia for the Kuala Krau constituency in Pahang. He is a member of the United Malay National Organisation (UMNO) party in the governing Barisan Nasional coalition.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.parlimen.gov.my/index.php?modload=ahlidewan&uweb=dr&action=details&id=75|title=Ismail bin Haji Mohamed Said, Y.B. Dato' Haji|publisher=Parliament of Malaysia|language=Malay|accessdate=12 July 2010[/tpl]
Ismail was elected to Parliament in the 2004 election, winning the UMNO-held seat of Kuala Krau, and was re-elected in 2008.[ref]
