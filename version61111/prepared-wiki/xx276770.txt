[[Acyperas aurantiacella]]

CATEGORIES: Animals described in 1901, Tirathabini

Acyperas aurantiacella is a species of snout moths in the genus Acyperas. It was described by Hampson, in 1901, and is known from Papua New Guinea and the D'Entrecasteaux Islands.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.nic.funet.fi/pub/sci/bio/life/insecta/lepidoptera/ditrysia/pyraloidea/pyralidae/galleriinae/acyperas/index.html |title=Acyperas at funet |publisher=Nic.funet.fi |date= |accessdate=2011-09-29[/tpl]
