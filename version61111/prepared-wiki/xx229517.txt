[[Tegano]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Solomon Islands, Rennell and Bellona Islands

Tegano is a village in the Solomon Islands, on Rennell Island in the Rennell and Bellona province. This village has a guest house in European style and is very popular.

==Location==

The village is in on the Lake Tegano to the East of Nuipani, with access by boat or a 45 minute walk from Nuipani.  

==Population==

120 people approx
