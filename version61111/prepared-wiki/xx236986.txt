[[Invisible Republic (album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2009 albums, Young Galaxy albums

Invisible Republic is the second album by the Canadian indie rock band Young Galaxy, released August 25, 2009 on Fontana North."Exclusive: Young Galaxy Speak Out About Split from Arts & Crafts". Exclaim!, August 20, 2009.
The album is a longlisted nominee for the 2010 Polaris Music Prize."Blue Rodeo, BSS on Polaris long list". The Globe and Mail, June 17, 2010.

==Track listing==

All songs feature Liam O'Neil on drums, Max Henry on keyboards, Stephen Kamp on bass, Catherine McCandless on vocals and Stephen Ramsay on guitars and vocals.
