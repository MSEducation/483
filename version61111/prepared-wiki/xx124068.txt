[[Impossible Dream]]

CATEGORIES: 2004 albums, Patty Griffin albums

Impossible Dream is Patty Griffin’s fifth commercially released album, and fourth studio album.  It was released on April 20, 2004.  The album features an unlisted song – Griffin’s mother and father singing “The Impossible Dream” – at the end of “Top of the World.”
Impossible Dream reached a peak of number 67 on the Billboard 200 chart,griffin|chart=all}} Artist chart history for Patty Griffin, Billboard.com. selling 16,000 copies in the United States in its first week.Caulfield, Keith. “Ask Billboard – Griffin’s ‘Impossible Dream’”. Billboard. May 5, 2004.
