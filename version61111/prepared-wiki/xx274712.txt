[[Philippines at the 2011 World Amateur Boxing Championships]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 2011 World Amateur Boxing Championships, Boxing in the Philippines

Philippines competed at the 2011 World Amateur Boxing Championships at Baku, Azerbaijan in the hopes of sending boxers at the upcoming 2012 Summer Olympics. The country sent six athletes to the prestigious event.
