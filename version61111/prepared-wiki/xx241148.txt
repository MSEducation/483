[[Hainan District]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Hainan District (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠠᠶᠢᠨᠠᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠲᠣᠭᠣᠷᠢᠭ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Qayinan toɣoriɣ[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=海南区 |t=海南區 |p=Hǎinán Qū[/tpl]) is a district of the city of Wuhai, Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China, bordering Ningxia to the southwest.
