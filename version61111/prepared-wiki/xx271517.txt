[[United Arab Republic Cup]]

CATEGORIES: United Arab Republic

The United Arab Republic Cup was held one time in 1961 during the era of the United Arab Republic due to the fall of the Republic.

==Regulations==

The Competition included 4 teams the winners and the Runner-ups of the Egyptian Cup and the Syrian Cup

The Egyptian Cup Champion takes on the Syrian Cup Runner-up in Cairo. While, The Syrian Cup Champions takes on the Egyptian Cup Runner-up in Damascus.

==1960-1961 final==

The championship was only held once where Al-Ahly S.C. faced Al-Majd Damascus (then known as Al-Ahly Damascus) at Cairo International Stadium. The match ended with a decisive 4-1 win for Al-Ahly S.C. who became the only Champion in this competition.
