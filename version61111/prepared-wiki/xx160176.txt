[[Haedo, Buenos Aires Province]]

CATEGORIES: Morón Partido, Populated places in Buenos Aires Province, Populated places established in 1886, Cities in Argentina

Haedo is a city located in Morón Partido, Buenos Aires Province, Argentina. It forms part of the Greater Buenos Aires urban conurbation.
With a surface of 6.11 km², it holds 38,068 inhabitants ([tpl]census-ar|2001[/tpl]), a 13% less than the figures of the 1991 census. it is also the fourth most populated unit of the Morón Partido, with a 12% of the total.

==Transport==
