[[Majineh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Majineh ([tpl]lang-fa|مجينه[/tpl], also Romanized as Majīneh; also known as Madzhainak and Majāinah)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3073623|Majineh[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Bala Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 366, in 88 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
