[[Heather Linstad]]

Heather Linstad was the women's ice hockey coach at the University of Connecticut, coaching the Connecticut Huskies. She previously coached at Northeastern. Actively, she is in her 12th season as the Huskies head coach, after coaching the other Huskies for nine.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.uconnhuskies.com/sports/w-hockey/mtt/linstad_heather00.html|title=Heather Linstad – Connecticut Huskies|accessdate=24 September 2011[/tpl] She also was a player at Providence. On February 13, 2010, Linstad obtained her 300th win as a head coach, with a 4–1 over the alma matter Friars.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.uconnhuskies.com/sports/w-hockey/spec-rel/022810aaa.html|title=Heather Linstad Acknowledged for 300th Win|accessdate=24 September 2011[/tpl]
In June 2011, Linstad was named the head coach of the United States U-18 women's ice hockey team. She will hold this position at least through the 2011–12 year.[tpl]cite web|title=Linstad Named U.S. Women's|url=http://www.uconnhuskies.com/sports/w-hockey/spec-rel/060911aaa.html|accessdate=24 September 2011[/tpl] She has also won the ECAC Coach of the Year.
Linstad abruptly resigned from her coaching position on March 13, 2013.
