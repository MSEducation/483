[[Ronaldo Bôscoli]]

CATEGORIES: Brazilian songwriters, Brazilian composers, 1928 births, 1994 deaths, People from Rio de Janeiro (city), Bossa nova singers, Brazilian journalists, Brazilian record producers

Ronaldo Fernando Esquerdo Bôscoli, usually known as Ronaldo Bôscoli, or just Bôscoli  (Rio de Janeiro, October 28, 1928 — November 18, 1994) was a  Brazilian composer, songwriter, record producer and journalist.
He was very active and important to the creation and definition of the Bossa Nova style in Brazil.
