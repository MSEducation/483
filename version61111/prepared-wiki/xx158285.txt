[[KORE]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Oregon, Springfield, Oregon, Christian radio stations in the United States, 1927 establishments in Oregon

KORE (1050 AM) is a radio station in Springfield, Oregon broadcasting a Christian radio format. Licensed to Springfield and Eugene.  The station is currently owned by Support Christian Broadcasting. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=KORE |title=KORE Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
KORE is Eugene's oldest radio station.  It originally signed on from Portland in 1927, but moved to Springfield a few months later.  Originally a full-service radio station, it switched to country in 1970 and adopted its current Christian format in 1973.
