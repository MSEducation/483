[[Lee Menning]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Game show models, American television actresses, 1954 births

Lee Menning (born November 25, 1954) is an American actress who appeared as a card dealer on NBC game show Las Vegas Gambit, and then as the co-host of NBC's Sale of the Century from 1983 to 1984. 
After leaving Sale, Menning took time off to have a baby, and returned the next year as the co-host of Your Number's Up, which aired for 13 weeks near the end of 1985.
