[[Bear Island (South Carolina)]]

CATEGORIES: Protected areas of Colleton County, South Carolina, Islands of South Carolina, Protected areas of South Carolina

Bear Island is an undeveloped [tpl]convert|12021|acre|km2|adj=on[/tpl] Sea Island in Colleton County, South Carolina.  It is part of the ACE Basin estuarine reserve area and is a Wildlife Management Area that is managed by the South Carolina Department of Natural Resources (SCDNR).  The area is open to the public from early February to late October and is a popular spot for hiking, biking, birding, fishing, and hunting.  A wide variety of waterfowl species inhabit the area.  The area shuts down periodically for special hunts.
