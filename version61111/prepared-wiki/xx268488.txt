[[Ezhiyeh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Isfahan County, Cities in Iran, Cities in Isfahan Province

Ezhiyeh ([tpl]lang-fa|اژيه[/tpl], also Romanized as Ezhīyeh, Azhyeh, and Ezhyeh)[tpl]GEOnet3|6013417[/tpl] is a city in Jolgeh District, Isfahan County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 3,309, in 948 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|10[/tpl]
