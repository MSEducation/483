[[Renshaw (surname)]]

CATEGORIES: English-language surnames

Renshaw is an Olde English locational surname for a village in the area of Prestbury, Cheshire that disappeared before the 17th Century.  The suffix -shaw means "wood". The earliest variant spelling Renshae is dated 1561.  Other variants include Ravenshaw and Rainshaw.
