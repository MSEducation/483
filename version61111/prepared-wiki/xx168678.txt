[[Michel Monnin]]

CATEGORIES: Judges in Manitoba, University of Manitoba alumni, Living people

Michel A. Monnin was appointed a judge of the Manitoba Court of Appeal on July 27, 1995.  His appointment became effective on August 3, 1995.  He replaced Mr. Justice Alan R. Philp, who chose to become a supernumerary judge. 
Mr. Justice Monnin graduated from the University of Manitoba Law School in 1969, and was called to the Manitoba Bar in 1970. From 1972 until 1984, he practised law with the Winnipeg law firm of Teffaine, Monnin, Hogue, Teillet & Sharp. In 1984, Mr. Justice Monnin was appointed to the Manitoba Court of Queen's Bench in Winnipeg. 
His father, Alfred Monnin, also served as a member of the Manitoba Court of Appeal.  His brother, Marc Monnin, also serves on the Manitoba Court of Appeal.
