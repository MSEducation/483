[[Nate Kelley]]

CATEGORIES: Coheed and Cambria members, American rock drummers, Living people, 1978 births

Nate Kelley (born February 15, 1978 in Rockford, Illinois) was the original drummer and backing vocalist for the progressive rock band Shabutie (now called Coheed and Cambria).[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.trht.net/artist/Nate+Kelley |title=Nate Kelley - The Rock Hard Times |publisher=Trht.net |date= |accessdate=2011-10-20[/tpl] Kelley, along with Claudio Sanchez, Travis Stever and Jon Carleo formed the band Beautiful Loser in 1995, which later became Shabutie after Stever left.[tpl]Citation needed|date=April 2009[/tpl] Kelley left Shabutie in February 2000. He currently performs with the band Pontius Pilate Sales Pitch.
