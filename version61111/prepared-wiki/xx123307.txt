[[Vault Beach]]

CATEGORIES: Beaches of Cornwall

Vault Beach is situated on the south coast of Cornwall near Mevagissey. The beach consists mainly of shingle but does have some sand at low tide. Vault Beach faces southeast onto the English Channel and lies between Cadythew Rocks in the north and Dodman Point at the southern end. 
Car parking is available in Gorran Haven and Lamledra which are both some distance from the beach.   Toilet facilities are available at Gorran Haven.  The southern end of the beach tends to attract naturists.
