[[Yana Khuchilla]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of Peru, Mountains of Puno Region

Yana Khuchilla (Quechua, hispanicized spelling Yana Cuchilla) is a mountain in the La Raya mountain range in the Andes of Peru, about [tpl]convert|5472|m|ft|0[/tpl] high. It is situated in the Puno Region, Melgar Province, Santa Rosa District.escale.minedu.gob.pe - UGEL map of the Melgar Province (Puno Region) Yana Khuchilla lies northeast of the La Raya Pass, near the mountain Chimpulla.
