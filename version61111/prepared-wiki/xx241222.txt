[[Hanggin Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Hanggin (or Hangjin) Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠠᠩᠭᠢᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Qaŋɣin qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=杭锦旗 |t=杭錦旗 |p=Hángjǐn Qí[/tpl]) is a banner in the southwest of Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China, bordering Dalad Banner to the east, Otog Banner to the southwest, and Bayan Nur to the north. It is under the administration of Ordos City.
