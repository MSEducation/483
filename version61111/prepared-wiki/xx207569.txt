Denis Istomin ([tpl]lang-ru|Денис Истомин[/tpl]; born 12 January 1987) is a Russian professional ice hockey forward. Drafted 117th overall in the 2005 NHL Entry Draft by the Chicago Blackhawks NHL hockey club.
