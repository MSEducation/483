[[Chesb]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Chesb ([tpl]lang-fa|چسب[/tpl], also Romanized as Chasb; also known as Chāsp and Chisp)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3058502|Chesb[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 508, in 137 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
