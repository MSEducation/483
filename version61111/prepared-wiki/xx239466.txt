[[Inter-paradigm debate in international relations theory]]

CATEGORIES: International relations theory, Debates

The Inter paradigm debate in international relations theory refers to the academic debate between proponents of realism, liberalism and Marxist approaches to international relations theory. This academic debate was topical during the 1970s and 1980s.http://www.oup.com/uk/orc/bin/9780199298334/01student/guide/ch01/
