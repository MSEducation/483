[[Kami-Imaichi Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Tochigi Prefecture, Stations of Tobu Railway, Railway stations opened in 1929, 1929 establishments in Japan

[tpl]Nihongo|Kami-Imaichi Station|上今市駅|Kami-Imaichi-eki[/tpl] is a railway station on the Tobu Nikko Line in Nikkō, Tochigi, Japan, operated by the private railway operator Tobu Railway.

==Lines==

Kami-Imaichi Station is served by the 94.5 km Tobu Nikko Line, and is 88.4 km from the starting point of the line at [tpl]Ja-stalink|Tōbu-Dōbutsu-Kōen[/tpl].

==Station layout==

The station is unstaffed and consists of two side platforms serving two tracks.

==Passenger statistics==

In fiscal 2011, the station was used by an average of 139 passengers daily.
