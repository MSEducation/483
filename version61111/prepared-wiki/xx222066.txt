[[Andrew Nowacki]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 births, Living people, Players of Canadian football from Ontario, Canadian football wide receivers, Murray State Racers football players, Edmonton Eskimos players

Andrew Nowacki (born October 10, 1980, in Stoney Creek, Ontario) is a professional Canadian football wide receiver who is currently a free agent. He most recently played for the Edmonton Eskimos of the Canadian Football League. He was drafted by the Eskimos in the third round of the 2004 CFL Draft. He played college football for the Murray State Racers.

==College career==

Nowacki began his collegiate career at Scottsdale Community College before transferring after two seasons to Murray State University.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.esks.com/roster/show/id/378|title=#88 Andrew Nowacki|work=Edmonton Eskimos|accessdate=2009-07-10[/tpl] From 2001–2003, Nowacki played in 24 games recording 53 receptions for 608 receiving yards. He led the Racers in receiving as a redshirt senior with 43 catches for 477 yards.
