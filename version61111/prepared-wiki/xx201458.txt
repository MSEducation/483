Steven Robert Sykes (born 5 August 1984 in Middelburg, South Africa) is a rugby union player, currently playing for the [tpl]Rut Eastern Province Kings[/tpl] in the Currie Cup and the [tpl]Rut Kings[/tpl] in Super Rugby. His usual position is lock.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.sarugby.net/playerprofile.aspx?id=24557 | title=SA Rugby profile | publisher=SARU | accessdate=7 Sep 2012[/tpl]

==Playing career==

Sykes began his career with the [tpl]Rut Natal Sharks[/tpl] in 2005 and also played for the [tpl]Rut Sharks[/tpl] Super Rugby team from 2007.
Starting in the 2011/12 season, he signed a three year deal to play with Leinster in the RaboDirect Pro12 and the Heineken Cup. However, he was released from the deal after only 4 appearances and returned on a one year contract with Sharks.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www1.skysports.com/rugby-union/news/15186/7451691/Sykes-makes-Sharks-return | title=Sykes makes Sharks return | publisher=Sky Sports | date=24 Jan 2012 | accessdate=7 Sep 2012[/tpl]
He joined the [tpl]Rut Kings[/tpl] for the 2013 Super Rugby season on a two-year deal.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.worldartists.co.za/page/clients/638638-Steven-Sykes | title=worldartists.co.za - Steven Sykes profile | publisher=worldartists.co.za | accessdate=12 Sep 2012[/tpl]
