Alpha Phi Omega in the USA organizes the country into 11 geographical regions numbered generally East to West. These regions are designated with Roman Numerals. Each of these Regions elects a Regional Director who represents them on the National Board of Directors. Each of there Regions are further divided into between 3 and 9 sections.
