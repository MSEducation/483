[[Tony Wright (artist)]]

CATEGORIES: 1949 births, Living people, British artists

Tony Wright  (born 23 October 1949 London) is an artist who created album covers such as Bob Marley's Natty Dread and Traffic's The Low Spark of High Heeled Boys[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.prwebdirect.com/index.php|title=Web Press ReleaseNewswire|date=2005-07-24|publisher=www.prwebdirect.com|date=2009-07-06|accessdate=2009-06-27[/tpl] [tpl]Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot[/tpl] and others including Bob Dylan's Saved[tpl]cite web|url=http://pqasb.pqarchiver.com/washingtonpost/access/74745691.html?dids=74745691:74745691&FMT=ABS&FMTS=ABS:FT&date=OCT+23%2C+1991&|author=Richard Harrington|title=The Art of the Album Cover|publisher=pqarchiver.com/The Washington Post|date=1991-10-23|accessdate=2009-06-27[/tpl]
His art work for Traffic's 1971 album The Low Spark of High Heeled Boys and Steve Winwood's 1980 Arc of a Diver, were listed amongst Rolling Stone's 100 Greatest Album Covers.[tpl]cite web|url=http://rateyourmusic.com/list/fedderedder/rolling_stones_100_greatest_album_covers|title=100 Greatest Album Covers| publisher=rateyourmusic.com|date=1991-11-14|accessdate=2009-06-27[/tpl]
