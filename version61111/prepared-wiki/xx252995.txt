[[2010 Swedish Touring Car Championship season]]

CATEGORIES: Swedish Touring Car Championship seasons, 2010 in motorsport, 2010 in Swedish sport

The 2010 Swedish Touring Car Championship season will be the 15th and last Swedish Touring Car Championship (STCC) season. Four of the race weekends will be held together with the Danish Touring Car Championship and the results from these races will also count towards the Scandinavian Touring Car Cup. From 2011, the new Scandinavian Touring Car Championship will replace both the Swedish and the Danish Touring Car Championships.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.touringcartimes.com/article.php?id=4181|title=STCC + DTC = Scandinavian Championship|work=TouringCarTimes|publisher=Mediaempire Stockholm AB|date=3 November 2009|accessdate=6 November 2010|first=Johan|last=Meissner[/tpl]

Joint STCC and DTC races.

==Results and standings==

The points system used for both the main championship and Semcon Cup is the new FIA system of 25-18-15-12-10-8-6-4-2-1, awarded to the top ten finishers of each race. In case of ties in points, the championship positions are determined by the classification in the latest race.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stcc.se/fileadmin/user_upload/PDF/STCC/STCC_2010/Sporting_regulations_STCC_2010_English_version.pdf|title=Sporting regulations STCC 2010|publisher=STCC|accessdate=2010-04-25[/tpl]
