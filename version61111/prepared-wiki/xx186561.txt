[[Gmina Komprachcice]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Opole Voivodeship, Opole County

Gmina Komprachcice, German: Gemeinde Comprachtschütz is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Opole County, Opole Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Its seat is the village of Komprachcice (Comprachtschütz), which lies approximately [tpl]convert|10|km|mi|0[/tpl] south-west of the regional capital Opole.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|55.87|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 11,249. Since 2009 the commune, like much of the area, has been bilingual in German and Polish, a substantial German minority remaining behind after the area was given to Poland after the Second World War.
