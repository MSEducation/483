[[What Price Paradise]]

CATEGORIES: 1986 albums, China Crisis albums, Albums produced by Clive Langer, Albums produced by Alan Winstanley, Virgin Records albums

What Price Paradise is the fourth studio album by English new wave group China Crisis. It was released on CD, LP and Cassette in 1986. The CD version featured one bonus track: "Trading in Gold", originally released on the B-side of the "Arizona Sky" single.

==Track listing==

All tracks written by Gary Daly, Gazza Johnson, Eddie Lundon, Brian McNeill, Kevin Wilkinson, except where stated.
