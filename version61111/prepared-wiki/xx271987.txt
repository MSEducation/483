[[Ronni Kern]]

CATEGORIES: American screenwriters, American television writers, American television producers, American film producers, Living people, Year of birth missing (living people)

Ronni Kern is an American film and television writer and producer.[tpl]cite web|url=http://movies.nytimes.com/person/97244/Ronni-Kern|title=Ronni Kern|work=The New York Times[/tpl]
Kern is best known for writing such films as American Pop[tpl]cite news|url=http://movies.nytimes.com/movie/review?res=9A06E3D81038F930A25751C0A967948260|author=Canby, Vincent|authorlink=Vincent Canby |title='American Pop' Grown-Up Animation |publisher=The New York Times|date=February 13, 1981[/tpl] and A Change of Seasons as well as miniseries and television movies such as Helen of Troy and Jesse Stone: Sea Change.
Kern has also produced such films as All I Wanna Do.
