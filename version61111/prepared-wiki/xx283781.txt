[[Martin Luther Cathedral, Daugavpils]]

CATEGORIES: Cathedrals in Latvia, Daugavpils

The Evangelical Lutheran Church of Martin Luther ([tpl]lang-lv|Mārtiņa Lutera evaņģēliski luteriskā baznīca[/tpl]) or Martin Luther Cathedral is a Evangelical Lutheran church in Daugavpils, Latvia. It is the seat of the Lutheran Bishop of Daugavpils in the Evangelical Lutheran Church of Latvia.
