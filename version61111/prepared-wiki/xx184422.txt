[[Gaston Gibéryen]]

Gaston Gibéryen (born 29 June 1950 in Born) is a Luxembourgish politician for the Alternative Democratic Reform Party (ADR) and trade unionist.[tpl]fr icon[/tpl] [tpl]cite web |url=http://www.chd.lu/fr/organisation/membres/membres.jsp?ID=81 |title=Gaston Gibéryen |accessdate=2008-01-03 |publisher=Chamber of Deputies [/tpl] [tpl]Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot[/tpl]  He is one of the ADR's four members of the national legislature, the Chamber of Deputies, representing the Sud constituency since he was first elected in the 1989 election.  He is the leader of the ADR deputation in the Chamber.
Gibéryen was mayor of Frisange from 1982 until 2005, having previously been an échevin in the commune (1976–1981).
