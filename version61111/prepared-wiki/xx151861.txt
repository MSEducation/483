[[Clear Springs High School]]

CATEGORIES: High schools in Galveston County, Texas, Galveston Bay Area, Greater Houston, Public high schools in Texas

Clear Springs High School is located in League City, Texas (USA) on Palomino Lane, next to Creekside Intermediate School.  It is a part of the Clear Creek Independent School District (CCISD). The school opened in 2007. It has an active Advanced Placement program.

==Academics==

Clear Springs High School was opened in August 2007 for ninth and tenth grade students. More than a third of the school's students are enrolled in Advanced Placement courses. In 2013, U.S. News & World Report ranked the school 128th in Texas based on college readiness and state exam scores.[tpl]cite web|title=Clear Springs High School|url=http://www.usnews.com/education/best-high-schools/texas/districts/clear-creek-independent-school-district/clear-springs-high-school-18819|publisher=U.S. News & World Report|accessdate=January 22, 2014[/tpl]
