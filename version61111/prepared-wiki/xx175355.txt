[[Gmina Regimin]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Ciechanów County

Gmina Regimin is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Ciechanów County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Regimin, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|9|km|mi|0[/tpl] north-west of Ciechanów and [tpl]convert|85|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|111.29|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 4,955.

==Villages==

Gmina Regimin contains the villages and settlements of Grzybowo, Jarluty Duże, Jarluty Małe, Kalisz, Karniewo, Kątki, Klice, Kliczki, Kozdroje, Koziczyn, Lekówiec, Lekowo, Lipa, Mościce, Pawłówko, Pawłowo, Pniewo Wielkie, Pniewo-Czeruchy, Przybyszewo, Radomka, Regimin, Szulmierz, Targonie, Trzcianka, Włosty and Zeńbok.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Regimin is bordered by the gminas of Ciechanów, Czernice Borowe, Grudusk, Opinogóra Górna, Strzegowo and Stupsk.
