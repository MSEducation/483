[[Seikijuku]]

CATEGORIES: Terrorism in Japan

[tpl]nihongo|Seikijuku|正氣塾||literally Sane Thinkers School[/tpl] is a right-wing Japanese imperialist group based in Nagasaki Prefecture, founded in 1981. The group was responsible for a number of violent incidents, including the 1990 near-fatal shooting of the mayor of Nagasaki Hitoshi Motoshima who stated that Emperor Hirohito was responsible for the war.正氣塾表題
