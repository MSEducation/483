[[Minami-Kurihashi Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Saitama Prefecture, Stations of Tobu Railway, Railway stations opened in 1986, 1986 establishments in Japan

[tpl]Nihongo|Minami-Kurihashi Station|南栗橋駅|Minami-kurihashi-eki[/tpl] is a railway station on the Tobu Nikko Line in Kuki, Saitama, Japan, operated by the private railway operator Tobu Railway.

==Lines==

Minami-Kurihashi Station is served by the Tobu Nikko Line, and is 10.4 km from the starting point of the Nikko Line at [tpl]Ja-stalink|Tōbu-Dōbutsu-Kōen[/tpl].

==Station layout==

The station consists of two island platforms serving four tracks.
