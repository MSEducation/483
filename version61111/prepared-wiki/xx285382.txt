[[Carry Me On]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 singles, 2013 songs

Carry Me On is a song by Brookes Brothers featuring Chrom3. It debuted on the UK Singles Chart at number 71 on 11 August 2013.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.officialcharts.com/singles-chart/|title=Official UK Singles Top 100|publisher=The Official Charts Company|accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl]
On 23 May 2013, the song was selected as Zane Lowe's Hottest Record in the World.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/p019gjv0|title=Zane Lowe, Zane Lowe Vs Wretch 32, Brookes Brothers - Carry Me On Ft. Chrom3|accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl]
