[[Redneck Girl]]

CATEGORIES: 1982 singles, The Bellamy Brothers songs, Blake Shelton songs, Song recordings produced by Jimmy Bowen, Billboard Hot Country Songs number-one singles, Elektra Records singles, Curb Records singles, Songs written by David Bellamy (singer)

"Redneck Girl" is the title of a song written by David Bellamy, and recorded by American country music duo The Bellamy Brothers.  It was released in September 1982 as the first single from the album Strong Weakness.  "Redneck Girl" was the sixth number one country hit for The Bellamy Brothers.  The single went to number one for one week and spent a total of twelve weeks on the country chart.[tpl]cite book |title= The Billboard Book Of Top 40 Country Hits: 1944-2006, Second edition|last=Whitburn |first=Joel |authorlink=Joel Whitburn |year=2004 |publisher=Record Research |page=43[/tpl]  Blake Shelton covered the song on the soundtrack to The Dukes of Hazzard: The Beginning.
