[[Han Chang-wha]]

CATEGORIES: 1922 births, South Korean footballers, South Korea international footballers, Association football defenders, 1954 FIFA World Cup players, 2006 deaths, Asian Games medalists in football, Footballers at the 1954 Asian Games

Han Chang-Wha (Hangul: 한창화, Hanja: 韓昌華, 3 November 1922 - 18 April 2006) is a South Korean football defender who played for South Korea in the 1954 FIFA World Cup.1954 FIFA World Cup Switzerland He also played for Seoul Army Club.
