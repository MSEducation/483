[[24754 Zellyfry]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Astronomical objects discovered in 1992

24754 Zellyfry (provisional designation: 1992 UE6) is a Main-Belt minor planet. It was discovered by Tsutomu Hioki and Shuji Hayakawa in Okutama, Tokyo, Japan, on October 31, 1992. It is named after a Japanese potato-and-okra dish similar to korokke.
