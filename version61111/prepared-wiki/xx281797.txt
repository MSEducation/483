[[Nightside Emanations]]

CATEGORIES: 2012 albums, Behexen albums

Nightside Emanations is the fourth studio album by Behexen. The album was released through Debemur Morti Productions on September 21, 2012. Nightside Emanations features changes in the band's musical sound, this time more death metal influenced, but still contains a strong black metal vibe and atmosphere. The track "We Burn With Serpent Fire" contains a guest guitar solo by former Behexen guitarist Gargantum.
