[[Ponchatoula High School]]

CATEGORIES: Public high schools in Louisiana, Schools in Tangipahoa Parish, Louisiana

Ponchatoula High School is a high school near Ponchatoula, Louisiana, United States. It is operated by the Tangipahoa Parish School System.
The sports teams are known as the Green Wave or Greenwave, after the eponymous athletic glory days of the Tulane Green Wave. Ponchatoula High School's colors are, however, green and white instead of Tulane's green and sky blue.
