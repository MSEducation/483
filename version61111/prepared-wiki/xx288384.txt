[[Dragan Bogdanovski]]

CATEGORIES: People from Kumanovo, Macedonian politicians, Internal Macedonian Revolutionary Organization – Democratic Party for Macedonian National Unity politicians, Kumanovo, 1929 births, 1998 deaths

Dragan Bogdanovski (1929 - 1998) was a Yugoslav Macedonian political emigrant, civil rights and anti-communist activist.Crna Lisica, Blaže Minevski, Matica makedonska, 2012, ISBN 9786081002423 He was a prominent Macedonian nationalist receiving large support from the Macedonian emigration around the world. The ideology and principles that he coined would later grow into the political fraction in Macedonia known as the Internal Macedonian Revolutionary Organization - Democratic Party for Macedonian National Unity that Bogdanovski co-founded.Macedonia: Warlords and Rebels in the Balkans, John Phillips, (illustrated), I.B.Tauris, 2004, ISBN 9781860648410 (page 43)Walking on the Edge: Consolidating Multiethnic Macedonia, 1989-2004, Židas Daskalovski, Globic Press, 2006 (page 46)
