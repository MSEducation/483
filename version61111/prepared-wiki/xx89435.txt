[[Necdet Mahfi Ayral]]

CATEGORIES: 1908 births, 2004 deaths, Turkish male stage actors, Turkish male film actors

Necdet Mahfi Ayral (August 6, 1908 in Istanbul – June 6, 2004 in Istanbul) was a well-known Turkish stage and cinema actor, as well as theatre director. While alive, he held the record for being the oldest actor who was still active. He is father of actress and well-known voice-over artist Jeyan Ayral Tözüm.
