[[Gongchangling District]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Liaoning, Liaoyang

Gongchangling District ([tpl]zh|s=弓长岭区 |t=弓長嶺區 |p=Gōngchánglǐng Qū[/tpl] is a district of Liaoyang City, Liaoning province, People's Republic of China.

==Administrative Divisions辽阳市-行政区划网==

There are 2 subdistricts, 1 town, and 1 township within the district.
Subdistricts:
The only town is Tanghe (汤河镇)
The only township is Anping Township (安平乡)
