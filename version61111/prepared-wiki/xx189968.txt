[[14181 Koromházi]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Asteroids named for people, Astronomical objects discovered in 1998

14181 Koromházi (provisional designation: 1998 WX6) is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1632.9926200 days (4.47 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=14181 |  title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-04-28 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on November 20, 1998 by Krisztián Sárneczky and László Kiss (astronomer). Beáta Koromházi is a Hungarian woman, Sárneczky's mother.
