[[Shivamurthy Shivacharya Mahaswamiji]]

CATEGORIES: People from Karnataka, Living people

Dr. Shivamurthy Shivacharya Mahaswamiji ([tpl]Lang-kn|ಡಾ. ಶಿವಮೂರ್ತಿ ಶಿವಾಚಾರ್ಯ ಮಹಾಸ್ವಾಮೀಜಿ [/tpl]) is the founder president of STJ education society and current seer of Taralabalu Jagadguru Brihanmath, Sirigere, ([tpl]Lang-kn|ತರಳಬಾಳು ಜಗದ್ಗುರು ಬೃಹನ್ಮಠ, ಸಿರಿಗೆರೆ [/tpl]) . He hails from Sugur village, Shimoga District.http://taralabalu.net/http://www.stjesociety.org/

==Education==

He obtained PhD from Banaras Hindu University. Later pursued post doctoral research from University of Vienna.

==Publications==

For more than four years, Dr. Shivamurthy Shivacharya Maha Swamiji has been writing a Weekly Column entitled “Bisilu Beladingalu”  http://www.taralabalu.org/Vijaya%20Karnataka/  on every Thursday in Vijaya Karnataka, a popular Kannada News Paper.

==Software==

He has authored Ganakastadhyayi and Ganaka Vachana Samputa.

Ganakastadhyayi - a unique software on Sanskrit grammar based on the Sutras of Panini.

Ganaka Vachana Samputa - a unique software on the Vachana Literature of 12th century AD.
