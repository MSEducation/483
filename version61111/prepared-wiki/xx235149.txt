[[K3 discography]]

CATEGORIES: Discographies of Belgian artists, Pop music group discographies

The discography of K3, a Flemish-Dutch pop group, consists of thirteen studio albums, nine compilation albums, three musical soundtrack albums and 43 singles. Their debut album Parels was released in 1999 and peaked at 2 in the Flemish album chart. Their next singles and albums became all big hits in The Netherlands and Belgium.
