[[Tobias Karlsson (figure skater)]]

CATEGORIES: Swedish male single skaters, Living people, Year of birth missing (living people), Skal vi danse? participants

Tobias Karlsson is a Swedish figure skater. He is the 1994 Swedish national champion. As a professional skater, he appeared on Dancing with the Stars and Let's Dance 2010.[tpl]fact|date=July 2013[/tpl] He has also worked as a coach.
