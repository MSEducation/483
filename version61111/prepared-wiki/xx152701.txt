[[Nepenthes × sarawakiensis]]

CATEGORIES: Carnivorous plants of Asia, Nepenthes natural hybrids

Nepenthes × sarawakiensis ([tpl]IPAc-en|n|ɨ|ˈ|p|ɛ|n|θ|iː|z|_|s|ə|ˌ|r|ɑː|w|ɑː|k|i|ˈ|ɛ|n|s|ɨ|s[/tpl], after Sarawak, Borneo) is a natural hybrid involving N. muluensis and N. tentaculata. It is quite a rare plant as one of its parent species, N. muluensis, is only known from several isolated mountains.
