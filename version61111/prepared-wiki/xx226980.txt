[[Xs on Your Eyes]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 albums, +/- (band) albums

Xs on Your Eyes is the fourth album by +/-, released in late October 2008.Tiny Mix Tapes: +/- (Plus/Minus) Announce New Album, Xs On Your Eyes An instrumental version of the song "You'll Catch Your Death" was used as menu loop background music in the band's 2007 Self Titled Debut Digital Video Disc DVD release. A music video for the song "Snowblind", directed by Nancy Mitchell, was released on 29 October 2008. The track "The Hours You Keep" appears as background music in Series 1 Episode 9 of Crash.
