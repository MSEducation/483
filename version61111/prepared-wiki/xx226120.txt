Darron Cox (born November 21, 1967 in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma) is a former Major League Baseball player. Cox played in fifteen games for the Montreal Expos in the [tpl]By|1999[/tpl] season. He had six hits in twenty-five at-bats, with one home run and two RBIs. 
He was drafted by the Cincinnati Reds in the fifth round round of the 1989 amateur draft.
