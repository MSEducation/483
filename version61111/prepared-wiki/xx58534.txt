[[Health Economics]]

CATEGORIES: Economics journals, Health economics, Public health journals, Publications established in 1992, John Wiley & Sons academic journals, English-language journals, Monthly journals

Health Economics is a monthly peer-reviewed academic journal published by John Wiley & Sons, covering the subject of health economics. It was established in 1992.
