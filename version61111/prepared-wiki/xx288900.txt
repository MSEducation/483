[[Kahla, Iran]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Kahla ([tpl]lang-fa|كهلا[/tpl], also Romanized as Kahlā; also known as Kāhīla)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3068443|Kahla[/tpl] is a village in Bizineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,661, in 406 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
