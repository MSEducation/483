[[Malalhue]]

CATEGORIES: Hot springs of Chile, Populated places in Chile, Populated places in Valdivia Province

Malahue is a town ([tpl]lang-es|pueblo[/tpl]) in the commune of Lanco located northwest of Panguipulli, Chile. The main economic activity of the zone is the manufacturing of oriented strand boards at Louisiana-Pacific's a manufacturing plant near the town. The town lies along the 203-CH route that connects Panguipulli with Lanco at the Chile Highway 5.
