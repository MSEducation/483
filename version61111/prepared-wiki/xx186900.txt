[[2909 Hoshi-no-ie]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Astronomical objects discovered in 1983

2909 Hoshi-no-ie is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1915.6794532 days (5.24 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=2909 |  title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-04-28 | publisher = NASA[/tpl]
