[[Orko (Basque)]]

CATEGORIES: Basque and Iberian deities, Thunder gods

Orko was a thunder god in ancient Basque mythology[tpl]cite news|last=Salyer|first=Kirsten|title=Weather Channel Names Winter Storms; Watch Out for Orko!|url=http://www.businessweek.com/articles/2012-10-02/weather-channel-names-winter-storms-watch-out-for-orko|accessdate=February 10, 2013|newspaper=Bloomberg Businessweek|date=October 2, 2012[/tpl] and other Iberian peoples. The name is derived from Orkeguna, the Basque word for Thursday.[tpl]cite book|last=Sykes|first=Egerton|title=Who's Who in Non-Classical Mythology|year=2002|publisher=Psychology Press|isbn=9780415260404|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=xlRaVT1DOW8C&pg=PA148|page=148[/tpl]
