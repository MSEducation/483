[[Eirik Sivertsen]]

CATEGORIES: Bodø politicians, 1971 births, Living people, Members of the Parliament of Norway

Eirik Sivertsen (born 1971) is a Norwegian politician who represents the Arbeiderpartiet. He has been the leader of Bodø Arbeiderparti since August 2006. At the  Norwegian parliamentary election, 2009, Sivertsen was the party's third candidate in Nordland.
Since the municipal elections in 2007, he has been a full-time politician, as leader of the Planning, Business and Environment committee in Bodø municipality. 
Sivertsen previously worked at the quality coordinator at Bodø University College.
