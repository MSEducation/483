[[Owzaj]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Owzaj ([tpl]lang-fa|اوزج[/tpl], also Romanized as Ūzaj)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3796117|Owzaj[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 4, in 4 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
