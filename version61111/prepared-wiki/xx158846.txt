CATEGORIES: Gymnastics at the 2004 Summer Olympics, Rhythmic gymnastics at the Summer Olympics

These are the results of the rhythmic group all-around competition, one of the two events of the rhythmic gymnastics discipline contested in the gymnastics at the 2004 Summer Olympics in Athens. The qualification and final rounds took place on August 26 and August 28 at the Galatsi Olympic Hall.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.olympic.org/rhythmic-gymnastics-group-competition-women |title=Rhythmic Gymnastics> All Events> group competition women |publisher=Olympic.org |accessdate=20 August 2011[/tpl]

==Results==

Ten national teams, each composed by six gymnasts, competed in the group all-around event in the rhythmic gymnastics qualification round on August 26.
The eight highest scoring teams advanced to the final on August 28.
