[[Mariampole]]

Mariampole [tpl]IPAc-pl|m|a|r|j|a|m|'|p|o|l|e[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Czerwonka, within Maków County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|5|km|mi|0[/tpl] east of Czerwonka, [tpl]convert|13|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] east of Maków Mazowiecki, and [tpl]convert|79|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of Warsaw.
