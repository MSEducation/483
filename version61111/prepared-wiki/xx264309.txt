[[Bishop's Green, Essex]]

CATEGORIES: Uttlesford, Hamlets in Essex

Bishop's Green is a hamlet located in between the villages of High Easter and Barnston, in the Uttlesford district, in the county of Essex, England. It is also near the hamlet of Wellstye Green.
Bishop's Green has a pub called the Spotted Dog.http://www.ukpubfinder.com/pub/23996
