[[Francis B. Austin House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Massachusetts, Houses in Boston, Massachusetts, Second Empire architecture in Massachusetts, Houses completed in 1832

Francis B. Austin House is a historic house at 58 High Street in Charlestown, Massachusetts, a neighborhood of Boston.
The Second Empire style house was built in 1832 and added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1988.
