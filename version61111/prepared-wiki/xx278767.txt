[[Michelle Pesce]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, American DJs, People from Youngstown, Ohio

Michelle Pesce (born in Youngstown, Ohio, USA) is an American DJ.[tpl]cite web|last=D'Astolfo|first=Guy|title=Liberty native Michelle Pesce finds fame as a DJ The life of the party|url=http://www.vindy.com/news/2013/may/13/liberty-native-michelle-pesce-finds-fame/|work=Vindy.com|publisher=Youngstown News|date=May 13, 2013[/tpl]
Pesce began her career working at Wolf-Kasteler, the PR firm, where she represented a wide variety of musicians and actors. In 1999 she attended Woodstock where she became inspired to become a DJ. Pesce soon bought her first set of turntables and after about a year she met DJ Spinderella of Salt-n-Pepa, who then became a mentor to her. Pesce then begin working in her time off as a DJ playing 80s and 90s Hip Hop, which she loves.  She decided to leave Wolf-Kasteler in 2004 to pursue her DJ career full time.
Pesce has now played at many events and clubs across the globe and is a staple of the Hollywood party scene. She is now a very sought after DJ on the event scene.[tpl]cite web|last=Dubin|first=Alesandra|title=The Hottest Event DJs to Book Right Now|url=http://www.bizbash.com/the-hottest-event-djs-to-book-right-now/new-york/story/26770?mkt_tok=3RkMMJWWfF9wsRojs6jLZKXonjHpfsX%2F6OspULHr08Yy0EZ5VunJEUWy3YUBRdQ%2FcOedCQkZHblFnVUBSK2xVK4NqaQJ#sthash.FYAtBHwS.uUFk51ag.dpbs|work=BizBash|date=August 1, 2013[/tpl] Pesce spins weekly at LA hot spot The Dime[tpl]cite web|title=DJ Michelle Pesce's Summer Playlist|url=http://promotions.wmagazine.com/dj-michelle-pesces-summer-playlist/|work=W Magazine (The Blog)|date=July 11, 2013|accessdate=8 August 2013[/tpl] and has also performed for VIP crowds at parties like the Academy Awards, Golden Globes, Coachella and the Grammy Awards Official After-Party.
