[[Copenhagen County]]

CATEGORIES: Former counties of Denmark (1970–2006), Capital Region of Denmark

Københavns Amt ([tpl]Lang-en|Copenhagen County[/tpl]) is a former county (Danish, amt) on the island of Zealand (Sjælland) in eastern Denmark.  It covered the municipalities in the metropolitan Copenhagen area, with the exception of Copenhagen and Frederiksberg. Effective January 1, 2007, the county was abolished and merged into Region Hovedstaden (i.e. Copenhagen Capital Region).
The county was seated in Glostrup.
