[[Mount Lister]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of Victoria Land, Scott Coast

Mount Lister is a massive mountain, [tpl]convert|4025|m|-2[/tpl] high, forming the highest point in the Royal Society Range of Victoria Land, Antarctica. It was discovered by the British National Antarctic Expedition (1901–04) which named it for Lord Joseph Lister, President of the Royal Society, 1895–1900.
