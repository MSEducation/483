[[Nojiri, Miyazaki]]

CATEGORIES: Dissolved municipalities of Miyazaki Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Nojiri|野尻町|Nojiri-chō[/tpl] was a town located in Nishimorokata District, Miyazaki Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the town had an estimated population of 8,705 and the density of 97.96 persons per km². The total area was 88.86 km².
On March 23, 2010, Nojiri was merged into the expanded city of Kobayashi and no longer exists as an independent municipality.http://www.kokudo.or.jp/new/cities/sub/kyusyu/45.htm
