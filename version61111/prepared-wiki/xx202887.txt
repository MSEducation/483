[[Véronique Delobel]]

CATEGORIES: 1978 births, Living people, People from Clermont-Ferrand, French female ice dancers, Twin people from France, Twin sportspeople

Véronique Delobel (born 17 June 1978) is a French former competitive ice dancer. With Olivier Chapuis, she is the 2000 Ondrej Nepela Memorial championISU and competed on the senior Grand Prix series. She later competed briefly with Michael Zenezini.Tracings: A figure skating resource
She is the twin sister of Isabelle Delobel.
