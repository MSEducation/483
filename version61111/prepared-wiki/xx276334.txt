[[Shaun Webb]]

CATEGORIES: 1981 births, Living people, Japanese rugby union players, Japan international rugby union players, New Zealand emigrants to Japan, New Zealand expatriate rugby union players, Expatriate rugby union players in Japan, New Zealand expatriates in Japan

Shaun Webb (born 30 December 1981) is a New Zealand born[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.rwc2011.irb.com/home/teams/team=49/player=34350/index.html|title=Profile|date=|work=2011 Rugby World Cup Official Site|accessdate=2012-12-13[/tpl] Japanese rugby union footballer who plays as a fullback. Webb was part of the Japan squad at the 2011 Rugby World Cup. He obtained Japanese citizenship in July 2011, a month before the 2011 Rugby World Cup.
