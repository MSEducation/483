[[2010 MEAC Men's Basketball Tournament]]

CATEGORIES: MEAC Men's Basketball Tournament, 2009–10 Mid-Eastern Athletic Conference men's basketball season

The 2010 Mid-Eastern Athletic Conference Men's Basketball Tournament took place on March 9–13, 2010 at the Lawrence Joel Veterans Memorial Coliseum in Winston-Salem, North Carolina.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.meachoops.com/home/default/?DB_OEM_ID=20800|title=MEACHOOPS.com| accessdate= 8 January 2010 [/tpl] The championship game was nationally televised on ESPN2 on Saturday, March 13, 2010 at 2:00 p.m.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.meacsports.com/ViewArticle.dbml?DB_OEM_ID=20800&ATCLID=204901372|title=MEAC Men’s Bracket Set for 2010 Tournament|work=MEACHoops.com|date=2010-03-05|accessdate=2010-03-05| archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20100309040807/http://www.meacsports.com/ViewArticle.dbml?DB_OEM_ID=20800&ATCLID=204901372| archivedate= 9 March 2010 | deadurl= no[/tpl] The tournament champion, Morgan State, received an automatic berth to the 2010 NCAA Men's Division I Basketball Tournament.  
The Morgan State Bears, as the 2009-10 conference regular season champions, earned the No. 1 seed and an early round bye for the third straight season.
