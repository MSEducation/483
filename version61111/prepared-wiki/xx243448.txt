[[Tyler Lawlor]]

CATEGORIES: 1972 births, Canadian canoeists, Canoeists at the 2000 Summer Olympics, Living people, Olympic canoeists of Canada

Tyler Lawlor (born January 11, 1972 in Kabul, Afghanistan) is a Canadian slalom canoer who competed in the late 1990s and the early 2000s. He finished ninth in the C-2 event at the 2000 Summer Olympics in Sydney.
