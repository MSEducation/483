[[Cheragh Mazraeh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Cheragh Mazraeh ([tpl]lang-fa|چراغ مزرعه[/tpl], also Romanized as Cherāgh Mazra‘eh; also known as Chirāgh Mazreh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3058484|Cheragh Mazraeh[/tpl] is a village in Aq Bolagh Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 109, in 23 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
