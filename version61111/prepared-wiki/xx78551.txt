[[Kijō, Miyazaki]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Miyazaki Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Kijō|木城町|Kijō-chō[/tpl] is a town located in Koyu District, Miyazaki Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the town had an estimated population of 5,686 and the density of 38.94 persons per km². The total area is 146.02 km².
