[[Mołowiste]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Augustów County

Mołowiste [tpl]IPAc-pl|m|o|ł|o|'|w|i|s|t|e[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Płaska, within Augustów County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland, close to the border with Belarus.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) – TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|3|km|mi|0[/tpl] north-west of Płaska, [tpl]convert|18|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north-east of Augustów, and [tpl]convert|89|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of the regional capital Białystok.
