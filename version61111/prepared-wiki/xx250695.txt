[[Elisabeth Neumann-Viertel]]

CATEGORIES: 1900 births, 1994 deaths, Austrian silent film actresses, Actresses from Vienna, People who emigrated to escape Nazism, Austrian television actresses, Austrian film actresses

Elisabeth Neumann-Viertel (born Vienna, April 5, 1900 - died Vienna, December 24, 1994) was an Austrian actress.
Under the Nazi regime she emigrated to the United States, but she later returned. She was the second wife of Berthold Viertel.
