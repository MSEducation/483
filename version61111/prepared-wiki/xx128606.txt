Ethel Gabison, also known by her stage name Ethel Booba, is a Filipino TV personality and comedienne. She has also worked in the recording industry.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.abs-cbnnews.com/entertainment/12/17/09/ethel-booba-marks-33rd-birday|title=Ethel Booba marks 33rd birthday|date=17 December 2009|work=ABS-CBN News|accessdate=20 December 2010[/tpl]
She is a former talent of GMA Network and was one of the hosts of Extra Challenge. She also guest judged for Kakaibang Idol, a special edition of Philippine Idol.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.mb.com.ph/node/143599|title=Ethel Boobaâ€™s career on the rise|date=17 October 2005|work=Manila Bulletin|accessdate=20 December 2010[/tpl]
Before becoming a star, she joined in the singing contest of ABC's, Sing Galing. She defended her title for four weeks and wore glamorous outfits each week. After she lost the title, she signed a contract under GMA Network. She also became oe of the housemates of Pinoy Big Brother Celebrity Edition 2
She currently makes several appearances on ABS-CBN.

==Biography==

She was born on December 17, 1976 in General Santos City, South Cotabato, Philippines. She is the sister of Emyrose "Boobita" Gabison. Her mother is Emy Gabison.
