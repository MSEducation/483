[[KOLW]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Washington (state), Rhythmic contemporary radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 1988

KOLW (97.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Rhythmic CHR format.  Licensed to Basin City, Washington, USA, the station serves the Tri-Cities area.  The station is currently owned by Townsquare Media. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KOLW |title=KOLW Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==History==

The station went on the air as KZLO-FM on May 7, 1988.  On April 14, 1989, the station changed its call sign to KZLN-FM. On January 14, 2004 to KHTO. On October 31, 2004 to the current KOLW. [tpl]cite web|url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=51128&Callsign=KOLW |title=KOLW Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl] 
On September 19th, 2013, at 3:00 pm local time, the station flipped from classic hits as 97.5 Kool FM to Rhythmic CHR as Hot 97.5. The first song as "Hot" was J. Cole's Crooked Smile.
