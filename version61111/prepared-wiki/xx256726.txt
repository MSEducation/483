[[Egyptian referendum, 1979]]

CATEGORIES: 1979 referendums, 1979 in Egypt, Referendums in Egypt

A double referendum was held in Egypt on 19 April 1979. The two subjects were the Egypt–Israel Peace Treaty and changes to the countries political system, including the reintroduction of multi-party politics and the change to a bicameral Parliament through the creation of the Shura Council.Egypt: The constitutional amendments and Article 88... And the Shura Council elections Arab Election Watch The Peace Treaty was approved by 99.9% of voters, whilst the political reforms were approved by 99.7%.Nohlen, D, Krennerich, M & Thibaut, B (1999) Elections in Africa: A data handbook, p341 ISBN 0-19-829645-2 Voter turnout was 90.2%.
