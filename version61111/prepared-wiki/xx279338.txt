[[Sufilar, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Sufilar ([tpl]lang-fa|صو في لر[/tpl], also Romanized as Şūfīlar)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3804055|Sufilar[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
