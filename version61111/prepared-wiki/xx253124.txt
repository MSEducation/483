[[Staroshaygovsky District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Mordovia

Staroshaygovsky District ([tpl]lang-ru|Старошайго́вский райо́н[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-mdf|Сире Шяйгавонь район[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-myv|Ташто Шайгабуе[/tpl]) is an administrativeConstitution of the Republic of Mordovia and municipalLaw #126-Z district (raion), one of the twenty-two in the Republic of Mordovia, Russia. It is located in the northern and central parts of the republic and borders with Nizhny Novgorod Oblast in the north, Lyambirsky District in the east, Ruzayevsky District in the south, Kadoshkinsky District in the southwest, and with Krasnoslobodsky District in the west. The area of the district is [tpl]convert|1419.4|km2|sp=us[/tpl].[tpl]citation needed|date=October 2012[/tpl] Its administrative center is the rural locality (a selo) of Staroye Shaygovo.Law #7-Z Population: [tpl]ru-census2010|14,071|;[/tpl] 15,870 (2002 Census);[tpl]ru-pop-ref|2002Census[/tpl] [tpl]su-census1989|18,422|.[/tpl] The population of Staroye Shaygovo accounts for 36.9% of the district's total population.
