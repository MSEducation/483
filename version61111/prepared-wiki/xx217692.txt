[[Eric Matthias]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1984 births, Athletes (track and field) at the 2008 Summer Olympics, British Virgin Islands discus throwers, Olympic athletes of the British Virgin Islands

Eric Matthias (born February 13, 1984) is an athlete from British Virgin Islands. He participated in men's discus throw at 2008 Summer Olympics. He was ranked 36 (53.11 m.).
