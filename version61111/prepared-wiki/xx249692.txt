[[Pivagabine]]

CATEGORIES: Antidepressants, Amides

Pivagabine (Tonerg) is an antidepressant and anxiolytic drug which was introduced in Italy in 1997 for the treatment of depressive and maladaptive syndromes. But it was discontinued in Italy (according to Martindale).
