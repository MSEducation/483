[[Half Machine Records]]

CATEGORIES: British independent record labels

Half Machine Records is an independent record label based in Canvey Island and London.
They have released several singles, EPs and albums, which includes Real Estate's debut UK release,http://pitchfork.com/reviews/tracks/11336-green-river/ an EP and single by Banjo or Freakout, one half of Kompakt's Walls Sam Willis's debut solo album 'Winterval',http://www.residentadvisor.net/news.aspx?id=17129 a reissue of the cult 1979 Roberto Cacciapaglia LP 'The Ann Steel Album',http://www.residentadvisor.net/news.aspx?id=14815 Woods' debut UK release,http://pitchfork.com/forkcast/13081-rain-on-radio/ Islands affiliates The Magic's debut LP 'Ragged Gold',http://www.musicomh.com/reviews/albums/the-magic-ragged-gold the debut single by Oklahoma's Stardeath & White Dwarfs, with 7" artwork designed by Wayne Coyne of The Flaming Lips,http://drownedinsound.com/releases/12513/reviews/2885635 and Holiday Shores' second album 'New Masses For Squaw Peakhttp://www.shout4music.com/live/holiday-shores-release-second-album-new-masses-for-squaw-peak-on-october-31st
MTV Award nominatedhttp://www.kickmag.net/2009/08/asher-roth-major-lazer-nominated-for-mtv-video-music-awards graphic artist Ferry Gouw designed Half Machine's logo
