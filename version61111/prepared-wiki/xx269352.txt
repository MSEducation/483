[[Songs of Love (Anita Ward album)]]

CATEGORIES: 1979 albums, Soul albums by American artists, Funk albums by American artists

Songs of Love is an album by Anita Ward released on T.K. Records. It includes the chart-topping hit single "Ring My Bell". "Make Believe Lovers" was also released as a single but failed to chart. Big Break Records will release this album on CD in March 2013.
