[[Thomas Hall House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Virginia, Houses completed in 1882, Houses in Montgomery County, Virginia, National Register of Historic Places in Montgomery County, Virginia

Thomas Hall House is a historic home located near Childress, Montgomery County, Virginia.  It was built about 1882, and is a two-story, three-bay, frame dwelling with board-and-batten siding and a slate covered cross-gable roof. It has a rear ell and features a two-story, gable-roofed front porch. Also on the property is a contributing frame outbuilding.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.dhr.virginia.gov/registers/Counties/Montgomery/060-0082_Hall,_Thomas,_House_1989_Final_Nomination.pdf|title=National Register of Historic Places Inventory/Nomination: Thomas Hall House|author=Gibson Worsham| date=January 1988|publisher=Virginia Department of Historic Resources[/tpl] and Accompanying photo
It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1989.
