[[Bolshebereznikovsky District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Mordovia

Bolshebereznikovsky District ([tpl]lang-ru|Большеберезнико́вский райо́н[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-myv|Покш Килейбуе[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-mdf|Оцю Березниконь район[/tpl]) is an administrativeConstitution of the Republic of Mordovia and municipalLaw #95-Z district (raion), one of the twenty-two in the Republic of Mordovia, Russia. It is located in the southeast of the republic. The area of the district is [tpl]convert|957.7|km2|sp=us[/tpl].[tpl]citation needed|date=October 2012[/tpl] Its administrative center is the rural locality (a selo) of Bolshiye Berezniki.Law #7-Z Population: [tpl]ru-census2010|14,072|;[/tpl] 15,584 (2002 Census);[tpl]ru-pop-ref|2002Census[/tpl] [tpl]su-census1989|19,113|.[/tpl] The population of Bolshiye Berezniki accounts for 45.4% of the district's total population.
