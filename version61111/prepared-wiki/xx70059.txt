[[Trend Records]]

CATEGORIES: Defunct record labels of the United States, Jazz record labels

Trend Records was a post-World War II United States jazz record label.
Trend's back catalogue was purchased by Albert Marx, the owner of Discovery Records, and much of its material was reissued in the 1980s. Among those who recorded for Trend are Van Alexander, Robert Conti, Shelly Manne, Clare Fischer, Warm Dust, Dick Berk, Matt Dennis and Ray Linn.
