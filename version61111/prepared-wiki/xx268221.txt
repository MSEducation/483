[[(13506) 1989 AF3]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1989, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(13506) 1989 AF3 is a main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on January 4, 1989.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
