[[Joël Vermin]]

Joël Vermin (born February 5, 1992) is a Swiss professional ice hockey player. He is currently playing for the SC Bern of Switzerland's National League A.Eliteprospects.com - Bern Vermin was selected by the Tampa Bay Lightning in the 7th round (186th overall) of the 2013 NHL Entry Draft.
Vermin made his National League A debut with SC Bern during the 2009-10 season.http://www.eliteprospects.com/team.php?team=108&year0=2010&status=stats
His father is from the Netherlands while his mother is from Hungary.
