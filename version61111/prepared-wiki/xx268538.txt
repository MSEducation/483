[[Padena District]]

CATEGORIES: Semirom County, Districts of Isfahan Province

Padena District ([tpl]lang-fa|بخش پادنا[/tpl]) is a district (bakhsh) in Semirom County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 23,200, in 5,419 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|10[/tpl]  The District has one city: Komeh. The District has three rural districts (dehestan): Padena-ye Olya Rural District, Padena-ye Sofla Rural District, and Padena-ye Vosta Rural District.
