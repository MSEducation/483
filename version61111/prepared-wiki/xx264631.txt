[[Joona Karevaara]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, HPK players, Jokipojat players

Joona Karevaara is a Finnish professional ice hockey forward who currently plays for Jokipojat of Mestis. He also used to play for HPK of the SM-liiga.HPK Hameenlinna's 2010-11 Roster
