[[Jan van Logteren]]

CATEGORIES: 1709 births, 1745 deaths, 18th-century sculptors, People from Amsterdam, Dutch sculptors

Jan van Logteren (1709 – 1745), was an 18th-century sculptor from the Northern Netherlands.

==Biography==

He was born in Amsterdam as the son of the successful sculptor Ignatius van Logteren.Jan van Logteren in the RKD He became his father's pupil and assistant who continued his father's workshop in statuary and decorative stucco reliefs for the wealthy mansion owners of Amsterdam. His most famous work today is the stucco work he made for the Christiaan Müller organ in the Grote Kerk, Haarlem.
He died in Amsterdam.
