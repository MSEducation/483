[[William J. Rotch Gothic Cottage]]

CATEGORIES: Houses completed in 1845, National Historic Landmarks in Massachusetts, Houses in Bristol County, Massachusetts, Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Massachusetts

The William J. Rotch Gothic Cottage is a National Historic Landmark on 19 Irving Street in New Bedford, Massachusetts.
Alexander Jackson Davis built Rotch Cottage in a Gothic Revival style in 1845 and the house was included in "The Architecture of Country Houses" (1850) bringing it early fame.NPS NHL Listing information The house was added to the National Historic Register in 2006.
