[[Aoki, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Aoki|青木村|Aoki-mura[/tpl] is a village located in Chiisagata District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the village has an estimated population of 4,874 and a density of 85.37 persons per km². The total area is 57.09 km².
