[[Live Session EP (iTunes Exclusive) (3OH!3)]]

CATEGORIES: 3OH!3 albums, Photo Finish Records albums, 2008 EPs, English-language live albums, Live EPs, 2008 live albums

Live Session EP is the third release by Colorado electronica duo 3OH!3. It is their second release on the record label  Photo Finish. This EP contains six live recordings, four of which are previously released tracks, with one unreleased track, "You Don't Love Me Girl" and one remix of Lykke Li's "I'm Good, I'm Gone."Live Session (iTunes Exclusive) - EP iTunes StoreLive iTunes Sessions Now Available!
