[[David Morisset (ice hockey)]]

CATEGORIES: 1981 births, Canadian ice hockey players, Ice hockey people from British Columbia, Florida Panthers players, Seattle Thunderbirds players, St. Louis Blues draft picks, Living people

David Morisset (born April 6, 1981 in Langley, British Columbia) is a former National Hockey League player for the Florida Panthers. He only played in 4 NHL games.
