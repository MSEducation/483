[[East Ujimqin Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
East Ujimqin Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠵᠡᠭᠦᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠤᠵᠤᠮᠤᠴᠢᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Jegün Ujumučin qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=东乌珠穆沁旗 |t=東烏珠穆沁旗 |p=Dōng Wūzhūmùqìn Qí[/tpl]) is a banner in the northeast of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, People's Republic of China. It is under the administration of Xilin Gol League.

==Geography and climate==

East Ujimqin features a cold semi-arid climate (Köppen BSk), marked by long, cold and very dry winters, hot, somewhat humid summers, and strong winds, especially in spring. The monthly daily mean temperature in January, the coldest month, is [tpl]convert|−20.8|°C|1[/tpl], and in July, the warmest month, [tpl]convert|21.1|°C|1[/tpl], with the annual mean at [tpl]convert|1.38|°C|1[/tpl]. The annual precipitation is approximately [tpl]convert|260|mm|in|1[/tpl], with more than half of it falling in July and August alone. Due to the aridity and elevation, diurnal temperature variation often exceeds [tpl]convert|15|C-change[/tpl] in spring, averaging [tpl]convert|13.7|C-change|1[/tpl] annually. With monthly percent possible sunshine ranging from 59% in July to 76% in February, sunshine is abundant year-round, with an annual total of 3,006 hours.
