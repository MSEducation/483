[[Over Kellet]]

CATEGORIES: Geography of Lancaster, Civil parishes in Lancashire, Villages in Lancashire

Over Kellet is a village and civil parish near Carnforth in the English county of Lancashire. The village was referred to as Chellet in the Domesday Book, and more recently has also been known as Lesser Kellet. It is approximately [tpl]convert|1|mi|km[/tpl] east of Carnforth, and [tpl]convert|0.6|mi|km[/tpl] east of junction 35 of the M6 motorway.
