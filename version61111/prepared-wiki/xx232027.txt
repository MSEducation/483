[[Mikal Angell Jacobus Landmark]]

CATEGORIES: 1867 births, 1939 deaths, Members of the Parliament of Norway, Liberal Party (Norway) politicians, Møre og Romsdal politicians

Mikal Angell Jacobus Landmark (27 July 1867 - 27 June 1939) was a Norwegian politician for the Liberal Party.
He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament in 1916, representing the constituency Nordre Nordmør. He served only one term.Michael Angell Jakobus Landmark — Norwegian Social Science Data Services (NSD)
Born in Ålesund, he was a great-grandson of a Jens Landmark (1772–1811), brother of Andreas (1769–1839) and Nils Landmark (1775–1859) and uncle of Jens Landmark (1811–1880).Genealogy
Mikal Landmark mainly worked as a local police sergeant, in Aure, following in his father's footsteps. He was a member of Aure municipal council for some time.
