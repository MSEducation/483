[[Dakoro Department, Burkina Faso]]

CATEGORIES: Departments of Burkina Faso, Léraba Province

Dakoro  is a department or commune of Léraba Province in south-western  Burkina Faso. Its capital lies at the town of Dakoro. According to the 1996 census the department has a total population of 12,864.Burkinabé government inforoute communale
