[[Cingino Dam]]

CATEGORIES: Dams in Italy, Gravity dams, Dams completed in 1930, Masonry dams

The Cingino Dam is a gravity masonry dam located [tpl]Convert|7|km|mi|0[/tpl] southwest of Antrona Schieranco, Province of Verbano-Cusio-Ossola in Piedmont, Italy. The dam creates Lago Cingino (or Cingino reservoir) which is fed by the Antigine and Troncone streams along with the waters of the Banella brook. The reservoir has a surface area of [tpl]Convert|10|km2|mi2|0[/tpl] and is [tpl]Convert|2262|m|ft|0[/tpl] above sea level. It is one of five reservoirs within a hydroelectric complex in the Valle Antrona and helps supply the Campliccioli Power Plant with water for power production.[tpl]cite web|title=Dams and Central Valley Antrona|url=http://translate.googleusercontent.com/translate_c?hl=en&sl=it&u=http://www.comune.antronaschieranco.vb.it/Dighe&prev=/search%3Fq%3Ddiga%2Bantrona%26hl%3Den%26rlz%3D1B3GGIC_en___US343%26biw%3D1280%26bih%3D604%26prmd%3Divns&rurl=translate.google.com&twu=1&usg=ALkJrhj3iWpaE69aIM2H7Rzllg_NWEdyiw|publisher=Commune de Antrona Schieranco|accessdate=2 January 2011[/tpl]
