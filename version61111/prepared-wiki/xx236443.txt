[[Anastraphia]]

CATEGORIES: Asteraceae genera

Anastraphia is a genus of flowering plants in the aster family, Asteraceae. It contains about 32 species native to the West Indies.Tellería, M. C., et al. (2013). Pollen morphology and its taxonomic significance in the tribe Gochnatieae (Compositae, Gochnatioideae). Plant Systematics and Evolution  299(5), 935-48.
Species include:Acevedo-Rodríguez, P. and M. T. Strong. Catalogue of Seed Plants of the West Indies. Smithsonian Contributions to Botany Number 98. 2012.
