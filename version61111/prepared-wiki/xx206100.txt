[[Louis Coolen]]

CATEGORIES: 1952 births, Living people, Dutch footballers, Dutch football managers, Helmond Sport managers, People from Nuenen, Gerwen en Nederwetten, FC Eindhoven managers

Louis Coolen (born 12 January 1952 in Nuenen) is a Dutch football manager and former player who leads a football academy in Tolyatti, Russia.

==Career==

===Playing career===

During his playing career he played for PSV, Helmond Sport and RKSV Nuenen.

===Coaching career===

After retiring as a player in 1982, Coolen has managed RKSV Nuenen, Helmondia '55, UDI '19, Helmond Sport and FC Eindhoven.
