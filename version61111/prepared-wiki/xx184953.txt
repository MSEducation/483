[[Mühlebach (Zürich)]]

CATEGORIES: District 8 of Zürich

Mühlebach is a quarter in the district 8 of Zürich.
It was formerly a part of Riesbach municipality, which was incorporated into Zürich in 1893.
The quarter has a population of 5,577 distributed on an area of 0.63 km².
