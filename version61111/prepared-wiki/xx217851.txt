[[Roland Nef]]

CATEGORIES: 1959 births, Living people, Swiss military officers

Lieutenant-General (Korpskommandant) Roland Nef (born 1 July 1959 in Frauenfeld) was the Chief of the Armed Forces (Chef der Armee) of Switzerland in 2008.
Nef, a jurist by profession, succeeded Lt-Gen. Christophe Keckeis in the position of Chief of the Armed Forces in January 2008. In July 2008, Nef resigned his post following media reports that his former partner had accused him of sexual harassment in a criminal complaint.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.swissinfo.org/eng/politics/internal_affairs/Army_chief_falls_but_defence_minister_to_stay.html?siteSect=1511&sid=9374295|title=Army chief falls but defence minister to stay|date=July 25, 2008|publisher=Swissinfo|accessdate=2008-08-19[/tpl] The Swiss Federal Council accepted Nef's resignation on August 20, 2008.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.swissinfo.org/eng/search/Result.html?siteSect=882&ty=nd&sid=9568052|title=Government accepts army chief's notice|date=August 20, 2008|publisher=Swissinfo|accessdate=2009-01-15[/tpl]
