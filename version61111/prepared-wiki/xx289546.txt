[[Qashqejeh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Qashqejeh ([tpl]lang-fa|قشقجه[/tpl]; also known as Ghashghajeh, Qeshqajeh, and Qāshqejeh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3080186|Qashqejeh[/tpl] is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,146, in 244 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
