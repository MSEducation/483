[[John J. Carty]]

CATEGORIES: 1861 births, 1932 deaths, People from Cambridge, Massachusetts, IEEE Edison Medal recipients, American electrical engineers, National Academy of Sciences laureates, Fellows of the American Academy of Arts and Sciences

John Joseph Carty was an American electrical engineer and a major contributor to the development of telephone wires and related technology. He was a recipient of the Edison Medal.  As Chief Engineer of AT&T, he was instrumental in the development of the first transcontinental telephone line.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.ieeeghn.org/wiki/index.php/John_J._Carty |title=John J. Carty |author= |date= |work=IEEE Global History Network |publisher=IEEE |accessdate=22 July 2011[/tpl] Carty was president of the American Institute of Electrical Engineers from 1915 to 1916.
