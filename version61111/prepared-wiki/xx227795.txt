[[PASCAL (database)]]

CATEGORIES: Bibliographic databases, French National Centre for Scientific Research

PASCAL is a scientific bibliographic database, which is maintained by INIST (CNRS). PASCAL covers the core scientific literature in science, technology and medicine with special emphasis on European literature.[tpl]cite web |url=http://ds.datastarweb.com/ds/products/datastar/sheets/pasc.htm |title=PASC - Pascal datasheet |work= |accessdate=2009-12-26[/tpl][tpl]cite journal|doi=10.1007/BF02019275|title=Scientific innovation in bibliographical databases: A comparative study of the science citation index and the pascal database|year=1991|last1=Jagodzinski-Sigogneau|first1=M.|last2=Bauin|first2=S.|last3=Courtial|first3=J. P.|last4=Feillet|first4=H.|journal=Scientometrics|volume=22|pages=65[/tpl]
[tpl]As of|2012[/tpl], PASCAL maintains a database of more than 17 million records, 90% of these are author abstracts. Its coverage is from 1973 to present. Its source documents are composed of journal articles at 88% (3,085 international titles), proceedings at 9%, and dissertations, books, patents, and reports account combined for 3%.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.inist.fr/spip.php?article11 |title=PASCAL - Inist-Cnrs |publisher=Inist.fr |date= |accessdate=2012-05-31[/tpl]
