[[Symmetrics (cycling team)]]

CATEGORIES: Cycling teams based in Canada, Defunct cycling teams, Sports clubs established in 2005, Sports clubs disestablished in 2007

Symmetrics Professional Cycling Team (UCI Team Code: SYM) was a Canadian UCI Continental cycling team, active until 2008, focusing on road bicycle racing and title sponsored by Symmetrics, a Canadian-based computer software company. The team is owned by brothers Kevin Cunningham and Mark Cunningham with assistance from directeur sportif Kevin Field. The Cunninghams are entrepreneurs, owners of multiple businesses and lead sponsors of the team with their respective companies Westlam and Symmetrics. The team won the 2007 UCI America Tour teams classification, while team member Svein Tuft won the UCI America Tour Individual Classification.
