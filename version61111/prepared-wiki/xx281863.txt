Sergio Carlos Strandberg (born 14 April 1996) is a Swedish footballer who plays for BK Häcken as a forward.[tpl]cite web|title=Carlos Strandberg|url=http://svenskfotboll.se/allsvenskan/person/?playerid=7903|publisher=Svenskfotboll|accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl]

==Career==

Strandberg, who has Mozambican heritage,[tpl]cite web|title=Rosengård, Mozambique, Angered – magisk mångfald i U17-laget|url=http://www.tipselit.se/Startsida/Turneringar/VM-bloggen/Rosengard-Mozambique-Angered--magisk-mangfald-i-U17-laget/|publisher=Tips Elit|last=Björkmanfirst=Roger|accessdate=8 April 2014[/tpl] grew up in Backa, Gothenburg where he joined the local lower league side Backa IF as a six year old.[tpl]cite web|title=Hallå där, Carlos Strandberg|url=http://blogg.gp.se/balkander/2013/06/26/11061/|publisher=Göteborgsposten|accessdate=3 October 2013[/tpl] In 2005 the club merged with Hisingstads IS to form Hisingsbacka FC[tpl]cite web|title=Om klubben|url=http://www.laget.se/HISINGSBACKAFC/About|publisher=Hisingsbackas FC|accessdate=3 October 2013[/tpl] which is where Strandberg stayed and eventually got to make his first team debut in the seventh tier of Swedish football at age 15.[tpl]cite web|title=Officiellt: Carlos Strandberg får A-lagskontrakt med Häcken|url=http://www.laget.se/HISINGSBACKAFC/News/3159634/Officiellt-Carlos-Strandberg-far-A-lagskontrakt-med-Hacken|publisher=Hisingsbackas FC|accessdate=3 October 2013[/tpl]
In 2012 he signed a youth contract with Gothenburg based Allsvenskan club BK Häcken. There he became the top goalscorer of the U17 league and was moved up to the U19 team at the end of the year. In 2013 he continued scoring goals for the U19 and U21 teams which in the summer resulted in Häcken signing him to a 4.5 year first team contract.[tpl]cite web|title=Carlos Strandberg gulsvart till 2017|url=http://bkhacken.se/2013/08/22662/|publisher=BK Häcken|accessdate=3 October 2013[/tpl]

==International career==

In September 2013 Strandberg was selected to the Sweden national under-17 football team that would compete in the 2013 FIFA U-17 World Cup.[tpl]cite web|title=Carlos uttagen till VM|url=http://bkhacken.se/2013/09/carlos-uttagen-till-vm/|publisher=BK Häcken|accessdate=3 October 2013[/tpl]
