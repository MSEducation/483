==Ethnic groups==

The people in the province are mainly indigenous citizens of Quechua descent. Quechua is the language which the majority of the population (70.54%) learnt to speak in childhood, 29.09% of the residents started speaking using the Spanish language and  0.23% using Aymara  (2007 Peru Census).inei.gob.pe INEI, Peru, Censos Nacionales 2007
