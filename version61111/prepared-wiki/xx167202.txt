[[Pingxiang County]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Hebei

Pingxiang County ([tpl]zh|s=平乡县|t=平鄉縣|p=Píngxiāng Xiàn[/tpl]) is a county of Xingtai City, in southern Hebei province, China, located about [tpl]convert|45|km|abbr=on[/tpl] from downtown Xingtai. It has a population of 280,000[tpl]when|date=July 2012[/tpl] residing in an area of [tpl]convert|406|km2|abbr=on[/tpl]. In the Han Dynasty, Pingxiang County was known as Julu County (钜鹿县), the birthplace of Zhang Jiao, Zhang Bao and Zhang Liang.
