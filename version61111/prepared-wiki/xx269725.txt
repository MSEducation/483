[[Nagla Roran]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Karnal district

Nagla Roran is a village in Karnal District, Haryana, India.
In this village Ror community large in strength But you will found there around 12 community. This village was famous as Nagla Gadian. There was Muslim in large amount but after partition of India. Ror and others community settled here.
