[[Martin Ittner]]

CATEGORIES: American chemists, 1870 births, Date of death missing, Year of death missing, People from Erie County, Ohio, Washington University in St. Louis alumni, Harvard University alumni, Colgate-Palmolive

[tpl]Refimprove|date=January 2011[/tpl]Martin Hill Ittner was primarily a chemist, who is best known for his contributions to applied chemistry such as the development of toothpaste and detergent while working for Colgate, now known as Colgate-Palmolive.  He was born in Berlin Heights, Ohio, on May 2, 1870.[tpl]cite news | url=http://resources.metapress.com/pdf-preview.axd?code=b182075935074768&size=largest | title=publication note | publisher=Oil & Soap | year=1941 | author=editor | accessdate=January 2, 2011[/tpl]
