[[Dalikdash]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Chaldoran County

Dalikdash ([tpl]lang-fa|دليكداش[/tpl], also Romanized as Dalīkdash; also known as Dalīkdāshī)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3826570|Dalikdash[/tpl] is a village in Chaldoran-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 223, in 50 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|04[/tpl]
