Christos Dimopoulos ([tpl]lang-el|Χρήστος Δημόπουλος[/tpl], born 10 June 1958) is a retired Greek footballer who played for PAOK and Panathinaikos. His nickname was "Fonias" (Φονιάς, means Killer) of Greek football.

==Career==

Dimopoulos was born in Gastouni. He started his professional career in Aias Gastounis and then Panetolikos. He played for PAOK from 1980 to 1985, scoring 47 league goals in 112 appearances and 5 UEFA Cup goals in 8 appearances with the club.
At the end of the 1985 season, Dimopoulos signed to Panathinaikos, finally closing his career in Athinaikos.
