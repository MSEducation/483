[[MacLaren (surname)]]

CATEGORIES: Anglicised Scottish Gaelic-language surnames, Clan MacLaren

MacLaren or Maclaren is a surname of Scottish and northern Irish origin. The name is an Anglicisation of the Gaelic Mac Labhrainn meaning "son of Labhrann". The Gaelic personal name Labhrann is a Gaelicised form of Lawrence.[ref]McLaren Name Meaning and History, citing
