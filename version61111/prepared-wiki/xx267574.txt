[[A. Sarkunam]]

CATEGORIES: Indian film directors, Tamil film directors, Living people

A. Sarkunam ([tpl]lang-ta|சற்குணம்[/tpl]) is an Indian film director, Working in the Tamil film industry.

==Career==

Sarkunam made his directorial debut with the romantic comedy Kalavani (2009). Featuring Vimal and Oviya in the lead roles, the film released to positive critical response and went on to become a sleeper hit.M Suganth, Sarkunam's movie succeed at box office.
