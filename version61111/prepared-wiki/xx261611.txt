[[SK Freidig]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Norway, Defunct athletics clubs in Norway, Sport in Trondheim, Association football clubs established in 1903, Ski jumping clubs in Norway, 1903 establishments in Norway

Sportsklubben Freidig is a Norwegian sports club from Trondheim.
Founded in 1903, the club has sections for association football, team handball, orienteering and Nordic skiing. In football, it won the League of Norway 1947–48, and, in women's handball, the league in 1971 and 1985. The club has also won several medals in athletics, orienteering and skiing.[tpl]cite encyclopedia|year=2007|title=Sportsklubben Freidig|encyclopedia=Store norske leksikon|editor=Henriksen, Petter|publisher=Kunnskapsforlaget|location=Oslo|url=http://www.snl.no/Sportsklubben_Freidig|language=Norwegian|accessdate=1 January 2011[/tpl] The senior football section  currently plays in the 2nd Group of Trøndelag Region in 5th division, the 6th level of Norwegian League.
