[[Jimmy Harry]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, Songwriters from California, Genie Award winners for Best Achievement in Music - Original Song

Jimmy Harry is an American Golden Globe winning songwriter, producer, and multi-instrumentalist who currently resides in Los Angeles, California.  Harry has worked with artists such as Madonna, Pink, Weezer, Kylie Minogue, Kelly Clarkson, Fisher Spooner, Oh Land, and Santana.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.jimmyharrymusic.com |title=Jimmy Harry |publisher=Jimmyharrymusic.com |date= |accessdate=2013-03-31[/tpl]  Recognized at the 2012 Golden Globes by the Hollywood Foreign Press Association, he won "Best Original Song" with Madonna and Julie Frost for the song "Masterpiece".[tpl]cite web|author= |url=http://www.goldenglobes.org/nominations/ |title=2013 Golden Globe Nominations Announcement - GOLDEN GLOBE AWARDS |publisher=Goldenglobes.org |date=2012-12-13 |accessdate=2013-03-31[/tpl] In 2010 his production of Pink's "Sober" was nominated for a Grammy for "Best Female Pop Vocal Performance",[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.grammy.com/nominees?year=2009&genre=24 |title=The Official Site of Music's Biggest Night |publisher=GRAMMY.com |date= |accessdate=2013-03-31[/tpl] and he also wrote and produced "Funhouse", the title track of her release nominated in the category "Best Pop Album". Jimmy received recognition from the Academy of Canadian Cinema & Television at the 2014 Canadian Screen Awards where he and Serena Ryder won "Best Original Song" for the song "It's No Mistake" from the film The Right Kind of Wrong.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.academy.ca/getmedia/e8ecd19b-f5ef-4178-81f2-0f8ebee6a970/2014-CSA-Winners-FILM.aspx |title=2014 CANADIAN SCREEN AWARDS Film Winners |publisher=academy.ca |date= |accessdate=2014-03-12[/tpl]
