[[St Andrews Links Trophy]]

CATEGORIES: Amateur golf tournaments in the United Kingdom, Golf tournaments in Scotland, 1989 establishments in Scotland, Recurring sporting events established in 1989

The St Andrews Links Trophy is an international amateur golf tournament contested on the St Andrews Links in Scotland. It has been played annually since 1989.
The format is 72 hole stroke play contested over 4 days. 144 players compete the first two rounds, which are split across the Old Course and New Course. After 36 holes the leading 40 competitors and ties play a further 36 holes over the Old Course on the final two days.
