[[1997 in M-1 Global]]

CATEGORIES: M-1 Global events, 1997 in mixed martial arts

The year 1997 is the 1st year in the history of M-1 Global, a mixed martial arts promotion based in Russia. In 1997 M-1 Global held 1 event , M-1 MFC: World Championship 1997.
