[[Mohanlal Saksena]]

CATEGORIES: Nominated members of the Rajya Sabha, 1st Lok Sabha members, Members of Constituent Assembly of India, 1896 births, 1965 deaths

Mohanlal Saksena (1896-1965) was an Indian author and politician from Uttar Pradesh. He served as the Union Minister of
Rehabilitation (1948-1950). He was a member of the United Provinces Legislative Council (1924-1926), Central Legislative Assembly (l935-1947), Constituent Assembly of India, Provisional Parliament (1950-1952) and the Lok Sabha (1952-1957). He was nominated as a member of Rajya Sabha from 1959 to 1964. Saksena was also the Secretary/ President of UP Congress Committee in 1929-1935 and 1937-1939.
