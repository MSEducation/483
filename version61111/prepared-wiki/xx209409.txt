On 28 August 2013, in a major breakthrough Yasin Bhatkal, co-founder of IM and another IM terrorist have been arrested by Indian Police and NIA near Indo-Nepal border. According to Gujarat police, the breakthrough in 2008 Ahmedabad serial blasts case came from five ‘switched-off’ mobile phone numbers.Five SIM cards gave us the vital clues Joint Commissioner of Police (crime) Ashish Bhatia said that the terrorists had procured five SIM cards of phones that were switched off on the day of the blasts — 26 July.
