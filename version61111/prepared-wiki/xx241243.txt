[[80th Indian Infantry Brigade]]

CATEGORIES: British Indian Army brigades

The 80th Indian Infantry Brigade was an Infantry formation of the Indian Army during World War II. It was formed in April 1942, in India and assigned to the 20th Indian Infantry Division until the end of the war.[tpl]cite web|accessdate=2009-10-23|publisher=Order of Battle|title=80 Indian Brigade|url=http://www.ordersofbattle.com/UnitData.aspx?UniX=6172&Tab=Sup[/tpl]
