Luiz Eduardo Azevedo Dantas  (born 24 May 1985 in Parelhas) is a Brazilian footballer, who currently plays as a forward for Adana Demirspor.

==Career==

===Brazil===

For four years in his country, Luiz Eduardo played for ABC Futebol Clube, Atlético Clube Coríntians, Esporte Clube Bahia, Ceará Sporting Club, Santa Cruz Futebol Clube, Social Futebol Clube and Londrina Esporte Clube.

===Montana===

In June 2009 Eduardo relocated to Bulgaria, signing a contract with PFC Montana. On August 22, 2009 he made his debut in A PFG in a match against Minyor Pernik. Eduardo scored goal for first time in Bulgaria on 17 October 2009 against Lokomotiv Plovdiv, scoring twice in a 2–3 loss. In January 2010 it was reported in the Bulgaria media that he was a target for Armenian side FC Banants. He left PFC Montana at the end of 2011/2012 season when his contract expired.
