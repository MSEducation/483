[[Xinbin, Liaoning]]

CATEGORIES: Township-level divisions of Liaoning

Xinbin ([tpl]zh|s=新宾镇 |t=新賓鎮 |p=Xīnbīn Zhèn[/tpl]) is a town in and the county seat of Xinbin Manchu Autonomous County, in eastern Liaoning province, China.[ref]
</ref> [tpl]asof|2011[/tpl], it has 5 residential communities (社区) and 31 villages under its administration.<ref>
</ref> The town has a population of 65,100[tpl]when|date=July 2012[/tpl] residing in an area of [tpl]convert|388.7|km2|abbr=on[/tpl].
