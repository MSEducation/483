[[Manuk Hergnyan]]

CATEGORIES: Armenian scientists, Consultants, 1975 births, Living people

Manuk Hergnyan ([tpl]lang-hy|Մանուկ Հերգնյան[/tpl], [tpl]lang-ru|Манук Ергнян[/tpl]) is managing partner at EV Consulting and Granatus Ventures, as well as the chairman of Economy and Values Research Center. He holds membership of The Competitiveness Institute, an international association of competitiveness professionals, as well as the Microeconomics of Competitiveness Network of over 80 universities worldwide chaired by Michael E. Porter.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.aiprg.net/UserFiles/File/2009_Yerevan/bios.htm#Manuk |title=Manuk Hergnyan |date=July 2009  |publisher=Armenian International Policy Research Group (AIPRG) |location= Annual Conference: Impact of the Global Crisis on Armenia: Short- and Long-run Perspectives |accessdate=2011-01-01 [/tpl] He is also a member of the Republic of Armenia Government Scientific and Technical Council,[tpl]cite news |title=Scientific-Technical Council Opens in Armenian Government |author= Haroutiun Khachatrian |url=http://www.hl.nt.am/newsday.php?p=0&c=0&t=0&r=0&year=2010&month=09&day=09&shownews=17882&LangID=1#17882 |newspaper= The Noyan Tapan Highlights Weekly |date=2010-09-09 |accessdate=2011-01-01[/tpl] Republic of Armenia Industrial Board Adjacent to the Prime Minister,[tpl]cite web | url=https://www.e-gov.am/u_files/file/decrees/varch/2012/02/11_0178.pdf | title=Appendix to the Decision of the Prime Minister of the Republic of Armenia N 178-A | publisher=The Government of the Republic of Armenia | date=February 29, 2012 | accessdate=June 1, 2012[/tpl] Coordinating Council of CANDLE Project,[tpl]Cite web | title = Republic of Armenia Prime Minister Decree N417Ա on Confirming the Members of Coordinating Council of CANDLE Project (in Armenian)  | date = 2010-06-08  | url = http://www.scs.am/up/voroshum417-a.pdf  | format =   | doi =   | accessdate = 2010-01-05[/tpl] and Ayb Club.[tpl]cite web|title=Ayb Club and Educational Foundation: Our People|url=http://ayb.am/index.php?page=people|accessdate=10 May 2013[/tpl]
Manuk Hergynan holds Moscow State University PhD degree in Economics and Oxford University Executive MBA.
