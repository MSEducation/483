[[Antrodoco]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Lazio

Antrodoco is a town and comune in the province of Rieti, in the Lazio region of central Italy.

==History==

Located in Italy.
The name derives from the Latin Interocrea (between the mountains). During 2010, the town was briefly affiliated with Libyan leader Muammar Gaddafi, who offered Libyan financing for urban regeneration.
