[[Kenny Mower]]

CATEGORIES: 1960s births, Living people, English footballers, Walsall F.C. players, Nuneaton Town F.C. players, Stafford Rangers F.C. players, The Football League players

Kenny Mower is an English former professional footballer who played as a full back, making over 400 career appearances.

==Career==

Mower joined Walsall in June 1976, turned professional in November 1978, and made his debut in the Football League in May 1979.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.saddlers.co.uk/page/PastPlayers/0,,10428~1053046,00.html|title=Profile|publisher=Walsall F.C.[/tpl] He made a total of 415 appearances in the League for Walsall,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.neilbrown.newcastlefans.com/walsall/walsall.html|title=WALSALL : 1946/47 - 2010/11|publisher=Post War English & Scottish Football League A - Z Player's Database[/tpl] before playing non-League football with Nuneaton Borough, Stafford Rangers and Blakenhall.
