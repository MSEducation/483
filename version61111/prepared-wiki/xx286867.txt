Lucas Evangelista Santana de Oliveira (born 6 May 1995) is a Brazilian footballer who plays for São Paulo FC as a central midfielder.

==Career==

===São Paulo===

Evangelista was professionalized for Tricolor paulista in May 2013, after the club had been eliminated in 2013 Copa Libertadores. The president of club, Juvenal Juvêncio, with along the coach of that time, Ney Franco, removed seven players, and, from the youth system, young stars were called to main staff.Lucão, Lucas Evangelista, Allan e Diego: conheça as novas apostas do São Paulo [tpl]pt icon[/tpl]
Protected even from interviews, Evangelista had more expectations than Lucas Moura, who was sold for PSG by São Paulo some months ago. In August 11, 2013, he scored his first goal playing by São Paulo FC: in lose by 1-2 against Portuguesa de Desportos, Evangelista lobbed his marking-man and shot to goal defended by Lauro. Even in a crisis of club, the young praised his goal, dedicating it for his father (in Brazil, that day was Father's Day), Jesus.Mesmo com crise, Lucas Evangelista exalta primeiro gol pelo Tricolor [tpl]pt icon[/tpl]
