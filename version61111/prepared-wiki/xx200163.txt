[[Routan Islands]]

CATEGORIES: Islands of the East Siberian Sea, Islands of Chukotka Autonomous Okrug

The Routan Islands are two islands in the eastern coast of the Chaunskaya Bay of the East Siberian Sea. They are located directly off Pevek, separated from the shore of the Pevek Peninsula by a 4.7 km wide sound. Ayon Island lies on the other side of the Chaunskaya Bay, 24 km to the west.
There is a small Arctic Station at Bol'shoy Routan Island.Arctic Bases
Administratively these islands belong to the Chukotka Autonomous Okrug of the Russian Federation.
