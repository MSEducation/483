[[Andrei Varantsou]]

CATEGORIES: 1975 births, Living people, Belarusian hammer throwers, Male hammer throwers, Belarusian sportspeople in doping cases, Doping cases in athletics

Andrei Varantsou (born 24 July 1975) is a male hammer thrower from Belarus. His personal best throw is 80.56 metres, achieved in June 2006 in Minsk.

==Doping==

In 2013 Varantsou failed doping test. He received a life ban.IAAF newsletter
