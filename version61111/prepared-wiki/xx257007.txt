[[Central American snapping turtle]]

CATEGORIES: Chelydra, Reptiles of Belize, Reptiles of Guatemala, Reptiles of Honduras, Reptiles of Mexico

The Central American snapping turtle (Chelydra rossignonii) is a species of turtle in the Chelydridae family. This species was previously considered a subspecies of Chelydra serpentina.

==Distribution==

It is found in Belize, Guatemala, Honduras, Mexico.

==Subspecies==

No subspecies are recognized.
