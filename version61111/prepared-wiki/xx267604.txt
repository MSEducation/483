==Career==

Yuvraj Walmiki could not be a part of the Indian team at the 2012 London Olympics after incurring an injury during the qualifying tournament.[tpl]cite news |title= Yuvraj Walmiki aims comeback |url= http://www.hockeyindia.co.in/2012/10/yuvraj-walmikis-aiming-comeback-in-team.html |publisher= Hockey India blog |date= 10 October 2012 |accessdate= 16 January 2013[/tpl]
He has been playing in the second division German league for the club TG Frankenthal since 2010, emerging as the top scorer during the 2011 and 2012 seasons.[tpl]cite news |title= Weight and watch for Walmiki |url= http://www.indianexpress.com/news/weight-and-watch-for-walmiki/1050776/0 |publisher= The Indian Express |date= 27 December 2012 |accessdate= 16 January 2013[/tpl]
