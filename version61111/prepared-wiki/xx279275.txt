[[Qarah Charyan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Qarah Charyan ([tpl]lang-fa|قره چريان[/tpl], also Romanized as Qarah Charyān, Qareh Charyān, and Qareh Cheryān; also known as Ghareh Chariyan, Karachar, Qarachar, Qarehchar, and Qareh Jeryān)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3079661|Qarah Charyan[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 417, in 97 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
