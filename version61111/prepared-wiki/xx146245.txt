[[1958 English cricket season]]

CATEGORIES: English cricket seasons from 1946 to 1968, 1958 in cricket, 1958 in English sport, 1958 in English cricket

The 1958 English cricket season saw Surrey captain Peter May top the batting averages for the third time and his team win the County Championship for a record seventh successive title.

==Honours==

==Test series==

England defeated New Zealand 4-0 with one match drawn. 

==Leading batsmen==

Peter May topped the averages with 2231 runs @ 63.74

==Leading bowlers==

Les Jackson topped the averages with 143 wickets @ 10.99
