[[Abdul Rahman Bakri (Selangor politician)]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Malaysian politicians, Members of the Dewan Rakyat, United Malays National Organisation politicians, People from Selangor

Datuk Abdul Rahman Bakri is a Malaysian politician and is currently the Member of the Parliament of Malaysia for the Sabak Bernam constituency in Selangor. He is a member of the United Malays National Organisation (UMNO) party in the governing Barisan Nasional coalition.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.parlimen.gov.my/index.php?modload=ahlidewan&uweb=dr&action=details&id=89|title=Abd. Rahman bin Bakri, Y.B. Datuk|publisher=Parliament of Malaysia|language=Malay|accessdate=17 July 2010[/tpl]
Abdul Rahman was elected to Parliament in the 2008 election for the UMNO-held seat of Sabak Bernam.[ref]
