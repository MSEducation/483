[[List of S&P 400 companies]]

CATEGORIES: Standard & Poor's, Lists of companies, S&P Dow Jones Indices

This is a list of companies having stocks that are included in the S&P 400 stock market index.  The index, maintained by S&P Dow Jones Indices, comprises the common stocks of 400 mid-cap, mostly American, companies. The list is current as of September 30, 2013.
