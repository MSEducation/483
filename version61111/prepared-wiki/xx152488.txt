[[Xtra Mile Recordings]]

CATEGORIES: British independent record labels, Record labels established in 2003, Rock record labels, Indie rock record labels, Alternative rock record labels

Xtra Mile Recordings are an independent record label based in London, United Kingdom. It was founded in 2003 by Charlie Caplowe and they release rock albums and singles. They signed the artists Reuben and Million Dead.
On 8 August 2011 Xtra Mile were badly affected by the 2011 England riots, when the Sony Music warehouse that stored their records was set on fire.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/music/2011/aug/09/independent-record-labels-stock-london-riots|title=Independent record labels fear ruinous stock loss in London Riots Fire|work=The Guardian |location=UK|author=Staff|date=9 August 2011|accessdate=9 August 2011[/tpl]
