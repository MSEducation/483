[[Lars Volden]]

Lars Volden (born July 26, 1992) is a Norwegian professional ice hockey goaltender who currently plays for Espoo Blues of the SM-liiga.Espoo Blues 2013-14 Roster
He was drafted 181st overall by Boston in the 2011 NHL Entry Draft. On January 7, 2014 Volden was named to Norway's national ice hockey team that competed in the 2014 Winter Olympics in Sochi.http://www.tsn.ca/nhl/story/?id=440590
