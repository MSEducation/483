[[Powerhouse (1999 band)]]

CATEGORIES: American house music groups, American dance music groups, Electronic music groups from Illinois

Powerhouse is a Chicago, Illinois house collaboration between producer and DJ Lenny Fontana and vocalist Duane Harden. "What You Need," a track on which they both shared production and writing credits, and which sampled Thelma Houston's "I'm Here Again," went to #1 on the Billboard Hot Dance Music/Club Play chart in 1999.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/charts/1999-07-17/dance-club-play-songs |title=Hot Dance Club Songs, Issue date July 17, 1999|accessdate=2013-04-19|work=Billboard|publisher=Nielsen Business Media, Inc[/tpl] It peaked at #13 in the UK Singles Chart. The track was remixed by Full Intention.
