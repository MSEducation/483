[[1992 Atlanta Falcons season]]

CATEGORIES: Atlanta Falcons seasons, 1992 National Football League season by team

The [tpl]NFL Year|1992[/tpl] Atlanta Falcons season was the team's 27th season in the National Football League. Atlanta played its first season in the Georgia Dome, after having played their first 26 seasons at Fulton County Stadium. Atlanta was statistically one of the worst defenses in the NFL in 1992. They were the league's worst team in points allowed (414), total yards allowed (5,549), yards per play (5.9), rushing yards allowed (2,294), and yards per rushing attempt (4.9).Pro-Football-Reference.com: 1992 NFL Opposition & Defensive Statistics
