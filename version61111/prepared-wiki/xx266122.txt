[[Braxton Miller]]

CATEGORIES: 1992 births, Living people, American football quarterbacks, Ohio State Buckeyes football players

Braxton Miller (born November 30, 1992) is an American football player who currently plays quarterback for the Ohio State Buckeyes. He will return to the Buckeyes for his senior season.

==High school career==

Miller moved from Springfield, Ohio to attend Wayne High School in Huber Heights, Ohio before his freshman year.http://www.freep.com/article/20110128/HSS/101280327/Best-Midwest-OSU-s-Braxton-Miller-next-Terrelle-Pryor- While there, he was named a Mr. Football Award Finalist, and also competed in the UnderArmour All-American Game. He was also a letterman in basketball.

Miller committed to Ohio State on June 3, 2010. Miller also had scholarship offers from Alabama, Cincinnati, Florida, Georgia, Illinois, Kentucky, Michigan, North Carolina, Notre Dame, Tennessee, Southern California, West Virginia, and Wisconsin.[tpl]cite web |title=Braxton Miller |url=http://rivals.yahoo.com/ohiostate/football/recruiting/player-Braxton-Miller-85520;_ylt=AhCnVeXmKXW_UatehplMUElztJB4 |publisher=Rivals.com |accessdate=2011-09-28[/tpl] It was rumored[tpl]by whom|date=January 2014[/tpl] that Miller's friendship with another 5-star recruit from Springfield, OH, Trey DePriest, would result in DePreist attending Ohio State as well. DePreist chose to attend Alabama.http://bleacherreport.com/articles/361320-ohio-state-recruiting-why-braxton-miller-is-a-must-get-for-jim-tressel

==College career==

===2011 season===

Miller began the 2011 season as the backup to Joe Bauserman,[tpl]cite web |title=Buckeyes go with experience at QB |url=http://www.journal-news.com/hamilton-sports/ohio-state-buckeyes/buckeyes-go-with-experience-at-qb-1242922.html |publisher=journal-news.com |accessdate=2011-09-28[/tpl] but saw action in the season opening game against Akron, where he completed 8 of 12 passes for 130 yards and a touchdown.[tpl]cite web |title=Akron Zips vs. Ohio State Buckeyes - Box Score |url=http://espn.go.com/ncf/boxscore?gameId=312460194 |publisher=ESPN.com |accessdate=2011-09-28[/tpl] Miller didn't play in the second game of the season, against Toledo, due to a coaching decision, Luke Fickell's decision.[tpl]cite web |title=Braxton Miller sits in OSU's victory against Toledo |url=http://www.thelantern.com/sports/football/braxton-miller-sits-in-osu-s-victory-against-toledo-1.2583684 |publisher=ESPN.com |accessdate=2011-09-28[/tpl] Miller played very little of third game of the season against the Miami. Miller was named the starting quarterback for the Buckeyes' week 4 game against the Colorado.[tpl]cite web |title=Freshman Braxton Miller to start for Ohio State Buckeyes vs. Colorado Buffalos |url=http://espn.go.com/college-football/story/_/id/7004410/freshman-braxton-miller-start-ohio-state-buckeyes-vs-colorado-buffaloes/ |publisher=ESPN.com |accessdate=2011-09-28[/tpl] His first career start turned out to be the best performance of his young career, as he tossed 2 touchdown passes, while going just 5 for 13 for 83 yards in the game. He was also able to run for 83 yards in the game.[tpl]cite web |title=Colorado Buffaloes vs. Ohio State Buckeyes - Box Score |url=http://espn.go.com/ncf/boxscore?gameId=312670194 |publisher=ESPN.com |accessdate=2011-10-03[/tpl] His second career start proved to not be so successful, as the Buckeyes lost 7-10 against Michigan State.[tpl]cite web |title=Michigan State's D toys with Ohio State, rides 4th-quarter FG to win |url=http://espn.go.com/ncf/recap?gameId=312740194 |publisher=ESPN.com |accessdate=2011-10-03[/tpl] For the game, Miller completed only 5 of 10 passes for 56 yards with an interception.
