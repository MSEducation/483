==Education==

Cimarron is in the Katy Independent School District. Residents are zoned to Cimarron Elementary School, West Memorial Junior High School, and Katy High School.

==Parks and recreation==

The subdivision maintains a recreation complex for its residents. The complex includes a clubhouse, a playground, a swimming pool, and tennis courts.
