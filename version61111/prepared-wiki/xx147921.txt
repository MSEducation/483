[[Maio Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in Cape Verde, Maio, Cape Verde

The Maio Airport (Portuguese Aeroporto do Maio)  [tpl]Airport codes|MMO|GVMA[/tpl] is an airport in Cape Verde located in the island of Maio, about 3 km north of the island capital Vila do Maio.  Its IATA code means the letter M and Maio, the last two letters of the ICAO code is Maio (the first two letters of the island name). The airport was inaugurated in the late-20th century.
