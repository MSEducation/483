[[Ilanlu, Hamadan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Kabudarahang County

Ilanlu ([tpl]lang-fa|ايلان لو[/tpl], also Romanized as Īlānlū; also known as Mārān)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3067208|Ilanlu[/tpl] is a village in Mehraban-e Sofla Rural District, Gol Tappeh District, Kabudarahang County, Hamadan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 410, in 78 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|13[/tpl]
