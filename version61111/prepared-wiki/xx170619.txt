[[Thirteen Masks]]

CATEGORIES: 1991 debut albums, Albums produced by J. G. Thirlwell, Jarboe albums, Post-rock albums

Thirteen Masks is the solo debut record of Jarboe, released in 1991 through Sky Records. The album featured Swans members Michael Gira and Norman Westberg and Tony Maimone of Pere Ubu.
