[[Sí (album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2003 albums, Julieta Venegas albums, 2005 video albums, Music video compilation albums, 2005 compilation albums, Albums produced by Cachorro López, Latin Grammy Award for Best Rock Solo Vocal Album

Sí ("Yes") is the third album by the Mexican singer-songwriter Julieta Venegas. She co-produced the album with Cachorro López and Coti Sorokin. After the success of this album, it was re-released in 2005 including 9 new tracks and a DVD.
The album won the Latin Grammy Award for Best Rock Solo Vocal Album and received a nomination for Grammy Award for Best Latin Rock/Alternative Album. Worldwide, the album has sold 2,5 million copies.
