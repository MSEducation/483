[[Erik Čikoš]]

Erik Čikoš (born 31 July 1988) is a Slovak footballer who currently plays as a right back for Scottish Premiership club Ross County on loan from Slovan Bratislava.

==Career==

===Club===

Čikoš first club was Inter Bratislava, where he played till 2008. In 2009 he was transferred to the club MFK Petržalka. In July 2010 Čikoš came to Wisła Kraków for medical tests and then he joined Wisła on one-year loan deal.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.wisla.krakow.pl/pl/aktualnosci/cikos_i_bunoza_oficjalnie_w_wisle/|title=Cikos i Bunoza oficjalnie w Wiśle|publisher=wisla.krakow.pl|accessdate=7 March 2011[/tpl]
