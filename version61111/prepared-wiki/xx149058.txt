Stephen Hill Spruiell (born November 20, 1979)[tpl]Citation needed|date=June 2009[/tpl] has worked as a writer and columnist for the National Review.NRO author page: http://www.nationalreview.com/author/14145

==Personal==

Spruiell is a native of Arlington, Texas.[tpl]Citation needed|date=June 2009[/tpl]

==Education==

Spruiell received his BA in journalism from the University of Oklahoma in 2002.[tpl]Citation needed|date=June 2009[/tpl]
Spruiell was a Koch Summer Fellow, Class of 2002, at the Institute for Humane Studies at George Mason University in Arlington, Virginia.[tpl]Citation needed|date=June 2009[/tpl]
Spruiell received his masters in public affairs from the LBJ School at the University of Texas, Austin in 2005.[tpl]Citation needed|date=June 2009[/tpl]
