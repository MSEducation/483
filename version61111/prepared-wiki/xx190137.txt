[[Marc Minardi]]

CATEGORIES: 1986 births, Canadian male television actors, Living people, People from Hamilton, Ontario, Male actors from Ontario

Marc Minardi (born December 18, 1986) is a Canadian actor. He plays Lucas Valieri on Degrassi: The Next Generation for the seventh season. He was on the television show Ace Lightning, where he played a lead role for 39 episodes.
Marc's current interests include cars and film.
Marc is currently in a band, and he enjoys writing his music. He also has many interests and passions in the sports field. While not pursuing his dreams in sports and acting, he spends his free time enjoying his other favorite hobbies: writing and directing.
Minardi was born in Hamilton, Ontario.
