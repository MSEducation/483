[[Nikita Dvurechensky]]

Nikita Dvurechensky ([tpl]lang-ru|Ники́та Двуре́ченский[/tpl]; born July 30, 1991 in Lipetsk) is a Russian professional ice hockey player who is currently playing for Vityaz Chekhov of the Kontinental Hockey League.
