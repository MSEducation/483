[[Nick Kennedy]]

Nick Kennedy (born 19 August 1981) is a rugby union player who plays Lock for Harlequins and England.
His uncle Duncan Kennedy is a BBC news correspondent.
Kennedy represented England Saxons at the 2006 Churchill Cup[tpl]cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/rugby_union/international/5039582.stm|title=England Saxons 7–13 Scotland A|publisher=BBC|date=2006-06-03|accessdate=2009-07-06[/tpl] and 2007 Churchill Cup.[tpl]cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/rugby_union/6662037.stm|title=England Saxons 51–3 USA |publisher=BBC|date=2007-05-18|accessdate=2009-07-06[/tpl]
He was called into the England Saxons side that defeated Ireland A on 1 February 2008.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.england-rugby.com/englandrugby/index.cfm?fuseaction=News.News_Detail&storyid=18739|title=England Saxons squad for Ireland A match|publisher=RFU|date=2008-01-23|accessdate=2008-02-03[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/rugby_union/english/7217616.stm|title=Varndell shines in Saxons victory|publisher=BBC|date=2008-02-02|accessdate=2008-02-03[/tpl]
Kennedy was selected for the 2008 summer tour of New Zealand.[tpl]cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/rugby_union/my_club/london_irish/7397834.stm|title=Tour call-ups please Exiles coach|publisher=BBC|date=2008-05-13|accessdate=2009-07-06[/tpl] He was later picked for Martin Johnson's Elite Player Squad on 1 June 2008 ahead of Ben Kay.[tpl]cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/rugby_union/english/7481130.stm|title=Tindall left out of England squad|publisher=BBC|date=2008-07-01|accessdate=2009-07-06[/tpl] He started his first game against the Pacific Islanders in which he scored a try.[tpl]cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/rugby_union/english/7714313.stm|title=England 39–13 Pacific Islanders|publisher=BBC|date=2008-11-08|accessdate=2009-07-06[/tpl]
Kennedy participated in every game of the 2009 Six Nations.[tpl]cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/rugby_union/7954709.stm|title=2009 Six Nations|publisher=BBC|date=2009-03-21|accessdate=2009-07-06[/tpl]
In 2012 Kennedy was signed for Toulon for the upcoming 2012/13 season [tpl]cite web|url=http://bbc.co.uk/sport/0/rugby-union/16947997|title=BBC UK Rugby Union article[/tpl] In May 2013 he started as Toulon won the 2013 Heineken Cup Final by 16–15 against Clermont Auvergne.[tpl]citeweb|url= http://www.espn.co.uk/heineken-cup-2012-13/rugby/story/182967.html|title= Toulon claim Heineken Cup glory|date=18 March 2013|work=ESPN|accessdate=20 May 2013 [/tpl] Following his request to be released from Toulon, he returned to England to sign for Harlequins for 2013/14 season.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/rugby-union/23257091|work=BBC Sport|title=Harlequins sign Nick Kennedy from Toulon|date=10 July 2013[/tpl]
