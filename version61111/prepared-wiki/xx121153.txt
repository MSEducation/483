[[Mirdha]]

CATEGORIES: Jat clans of Rajasthan, Indian family names

Mirdha is surname of Jats found in Jodhpur Marwar region of Rajasthan, India. It is based on title Mirdha which was awarded by Emperor of Delhi  in 1584 when Raja Uday Singh of Jodhpur, under the orders of Emperor Akbar, marched to Gujarat and defeated Sultan Muzzafar in battle.  Distribution of Mirdhas in Rajasthan is in Jaipur, Jodhpur and Nagaur districts.
