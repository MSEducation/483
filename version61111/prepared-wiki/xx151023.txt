[[US de la Comoé]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Burkina Faso, Association football clubs established in 1985, Cascades Region, 1985 establishments in Burkina Faso

US de la Comoé is a Burkinabé football club based in Banfora. They play their home games at the Stade Banfora. The club's colors are blue and yellow.
