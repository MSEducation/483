[[P'yŏngwŏn Line]]

CATEGORIES: Railway lines in North Korea

The Pyongwon Line was a railway line in Korea, opened in 1941 during the period of Japanese rule. The line connected Pyongyang to Kowon. It exists now North Korea and forms part of the following present day lines:Kokubu, Hayato, 将軍様の鉄道 (Shōgun-sama no Tetsudō), ISBN 978-4-10-303731-6
