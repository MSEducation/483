[[Ian Robins]]

CATEGORIES: 1952 births, Living people, People from Bury, English footballers, The Football League players, Association football forwards, Oldham Athletic A.F.C. players, Bury F.C. players, Huddersfield Town F.C. players

Ian Robins (born 22 February 1952 in Bury, Lancashire) was a professional footballer who played for Oldham Athletic, Bury and Huddersfield Town.Joined Oldham as an apprentice in August 1967.
