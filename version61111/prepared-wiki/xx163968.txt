[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Ikot Ekpene]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic Church in Nigeria, Roman Catholic dioceses in Nigeria, Religious organizations established in 1964, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Ikot Ekpene ([tpl]lang-la|Ikotekpenen(sis)[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Ikot Ekpene, Akwa Ibom State in the Ecclesiastical province of Calabar in Nigeria.

==History==

==Special churches==

The Cathedral is  Saint Anne Cathedral in Ikot Ekpene.  The Pro-Cathedral is Saint John Pro-Cathedral in Abak.
