[[Hollywood Trials]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 in Irish television, Irish reality television series, RTÉ television programmes

Hollywood Trials was an observational documentary series which ran on RTÉ Two Ireland, in 2008.Official Page Official Page
The series followed ten Irish actors trying to break into Hollywood. The participants lived together as a group for four weeks as they were groomed and coached by experts in the field, including audition coach Margie Haber, who was also part of the selection panel which included Declan Lowney and Ros Hubbard. The programme concluded with a few of the actors, including Green and Ateh receiving offers from Hollywood agents.I've Got a Good Chance
Hollywood Trials was produced for RTÉ by Red Pepper Productions.
