[[Georges Périnal]]

CATEGORIES: 1897 births, 1965 deaths, French cinematographers, People from Paris, Best Cinematographer Academy Award winners

Georges Périnal (1897 – 23 April 1965) was a French cinematographer.  
Périnal was born in Paris.  He worked with, amongst others: Jean Grémillon, René Clair, Jean Cocteau, Michael Powell, Charlie Chaplin, Otto Preminger. He died in London.
