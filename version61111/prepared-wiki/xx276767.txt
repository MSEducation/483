[[Acteniopsis robustus]]

CATEGORIES: Animals described in 2010, Pyralinae

Acteniopsis robustus is a species of snout moths in the genus Acteniopsis. It was described by Asselbergs in 2010, and is known from the United Arab Emirates.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.afromoths.net/species/show/39989 |title=Afro Moths |publisher=Afro Moths |date= |accessdate=2011-09-29[/tpl]
