[[Gmina Somianka]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Wyszków County

Gmina Somianka is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Wyszków County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Somianka, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|12|km|mi|0[/tpl] west of Wyszków and [tpl]convert|44|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north-east of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|116.38|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 5,491.
