[[Chewacla State Park]]

CATEGORIES: State parks of Alabama, Civilian Conservation Corps in Alabama, Protected areas of Lee County, Alabama, Waterfalls of Alabama

Chewacla State Park is a [tpl]convert|696|acre|adj=on[/tpl] state park in Auburn, Alabama. It is located just south of Interstate 85 in Lee County and offers picnicking, hiking, swimming, camping, and lodging.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.alapark.com/Chewacla/ |title=Chewacla State Park |work=Alabama State Parks |publisher=Alabama Department of Conservation and Natural Resources |accessdate=2012-11-05[/tpl]
