[[Atyashevsky District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Mordovia

Atyashevsky District ([tpl]lang-ru|Атя́шевский райо́н[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-myv|Отяжбуе[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-mdf|Атяшевань район[/tpl]) is an administrativeConstitution of the Republic of Mordovia and municipalLaw #117-Z district (raion), one of the twenty-two in the Republic of Mordovia, Russia. It is located in the east of the republic. The area of the district is [tpl]convert|1095.8|km2|sp=us[/tpl].[tpl]citation needed|date=October 2012[/tpl] Its administrative center is the urban locality (an urban-type settlement) of Atyashevo.Law #7-Z Population: [tpl]ru-census2010|20,161|;[/tpl] 22,889 (2002 Census);[tpl]ru-pop-ref|2002Census[/tpl] [tpl]su-census1989|27,220|.[/tpl] The population of Atyashevo accounts for 31.0% of the district's total population.
