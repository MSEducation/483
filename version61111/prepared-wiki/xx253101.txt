The diner was founded in 1951 by Elmer and Grace Paxton for their son Lee who was returning from military service.  They bought a Mountain View prefabricated stainless steel diner (serial # 301) which was fitted to their house, which contains the kitchen.[tpl]citation |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=N1ZaJ8543KsC&pg=PA142 |title=The Lincoln Highway |author=Brian Butko[/tpl][tpl]cite book | last = Butko|first=Brian | title =The Lincoln Highway:Pennsylvania Traveler's Guide | publisher = Stackpole Books | location = Mechanicsburg | year = 2002 | isbn = 0-8117-2497-2|page=142 [/tpl]
