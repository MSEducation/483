[[Society for Macedonian Studies]]

CATEGORIES: 1939 establishments in Greece, Thessaloniki, History of Macedonia (Greece), Greek folklore

==Foundation==

The Society for Macedonian Studies was founded at April 29, 1939 in Thessaloniki - Greece.

==Purpose==

The purpose of the S.M.S. is the research on History and Folklore of Macedonia and the help for the development of the Intellectual development of Macedonian life.
