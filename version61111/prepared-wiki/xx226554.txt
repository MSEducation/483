[[Khuvkhoitun]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of Russia, Volcanoes of the Kamchatka Peninsula, Stratovolcanoes

Khuvkhoitun ([tpl]lang-ru|Хувхойтун[/tpl]) sometimes referred to as Gora Khuvkhoitun is a stratovolcano located on Russia's Kamchatka Peninsula. The mountain reaches 2,616 m in elevation above sea level.
