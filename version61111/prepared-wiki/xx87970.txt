[[Regent railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1889

Regent railway station is located on the South Morang line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the north-eastern Melbourne suburb of Reservoir opening as Preston-Regent Street on 8 October 1889 being renamed Regent on 1 August 1905.Regent Vicsig

==Platforms & services==

Regent has two side platforms. It is serviced by Metro Trains' South Morang line services.South Morang Line timetable Public Transport Victoria
