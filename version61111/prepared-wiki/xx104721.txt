[[Yonkers Marathon]]

CATEGORIES: Sports in Yonkers, New York, Marathons in the United States

The Yonkers Marathon, held in Yonkers, New York, is the second oldest marathon in the United States, after the Boston Marathon. The course of the Yonkers Marathon is known as being tough and hilly.[tpl]cite book |last1=Corbitt |first1=Ted |authorlink1=Ted Corbitt |last2= |first2= |authorlink2= |editor1-first=Gail Waesche |editor1-last=Kislevitz |editor1-link= |others= |title=The Spirit of the Marathon: What to Expect in Your First Marathon and How to Run Them the Rest of Your Life |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=FADySOIwAOkC&printsec=frontcover&source=gbs_atb#v=onepage&q&f=false |archiveurl= |archivedate= |format= |accessdate= |type= |edition= |series= |volume= |date= |year=2003 |month= |origyear= |publisher=Breakaway Books |location=Halcottsville, New York |language= |isbn=978-1-891369-36-0 |oclc= |doi= |id= |page=234 |pages= |at= |trans_chapter= |chapter=Ted Corbitt: A Willingness to Suffer |chapterurl=http://books.google.com/books?id=FADySOIwAOkC&lpg=PP1&pg=PA234#v=onepage&q&f=false |quote= |ref= |bibcode= |laysummary= |laydate= |separator= |postscript= |lastauthoramp=[/tpl]
The Yonkers marathon (the standard distance, 42.195 km) is typically held on the third Sunday in September. It is a double loop course which also stages a half marathon (21.1 km). It was initially sponsored by the Mercury Athletic Club.[tpl]cite news |title=MARATHON VICTORY FOR HARRY JENSEN; Finishes First in Yonkers Event With a Half Mile Advantage. The first few kilometers (or miles) of the race are very scenic, overlooking the Hudson River. However, the majority of the race is in urban industrial areas that are less than aesthetically pleasing.
Every year approximately 200 runners participate in the Yonkers marathon.
