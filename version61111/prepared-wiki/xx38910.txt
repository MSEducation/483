[[Boortmeerbeek]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Flemish Brabant

Boortmeerbeek ([tpl]IPA-nl|ˌboːrt̪ˈmeːrbeːk[/tpl]) is a town in the Belgian province of Flemish Brabant. The municipality comprises the towns of Boortmeerbeek proper, Schiplaken and Hever. The total area is 18.64 km² which gives a population density of 620 inhabitants per km².
