[[Max Wärn]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, HIFK players, HPK players, Pelicans players, JYP players, 1988 births

Max Wärn (born June 10, 1988) is a Finnish professional ice hockey forward who currently plays for JYP of the SM-liiga.The Internet Hockey Database He was selected by the Dallas Stars in the 5th round (150th overall) of the 2006 NHL Entry Draft.
