[[Haematopota]]

CATEGORIES: Tabanidae

Haematopota is a genus of fly in the horse-fly family, Tabanidae. Among the horse-flies, they are most commonly known as clegs. Many species have colorful, patterned eyes in life, a character that fades after death.Chainey, J. E. (1993). Horse-flies, deer-flies and clegs (Tabanidae). In: Medical Insects and Arachnids. Springer Netherlands. Chapter 8: pp. 310-32.
