[[Qarah Owghlanlu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Qarah Owghlanlu ([tpl]lang-fa|قره اوغلانلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Qarah Owghlānlū and Qareh Ūghlānlū)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3803241|Qarah Owghlanlu[/tpl] is a village in Chaypareh-ye Bala Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 380, in 92 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
