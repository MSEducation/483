[[Breathing Is Irrelevant]]

CATEGORIES: 2003 albums, Ion Dissonance albums

Breathing Is Irrelevant is the debut studio album by the Canadian grindcore/mathcore band Ion Dissonance, released on September 2, 2003 through Willowtip Records.
The title of the track "The Death of One Man Is a Tragedy, the Death of 10,000 Is a Statistic" is a quotation commonly attributed to Joseph Stalin.
