[[Baruth/Mark]]

CATEGORIES: Localities in Teltow-Fläming, Teltow-Fläming

Baruth/Mark is a town in the Teltow-Fläming district of Brandenburg, Germany. It is situated 24 km east of Luckenwalde, and 53 km south of Berlin.
