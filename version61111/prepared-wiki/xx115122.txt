[[Nagaina]]

CATEGORIES: Salticidae

Nagaina is a spider genus of the Salticidae family (jumping spiders).

==Name==

The genus name is derived from Nagaina, a character from Rudyard Kipling's story Rikki-Tikki-Tavi. Other salticid genera with names of Kipling's characters are  Bagheera, Messua and Akela.
