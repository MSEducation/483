[[La Grange Highlands, Illinois]]

CATEGORIES: Chicago metropolitan area, Unincorporated communities in Cook County, Illinois, Unincorporated communities in Illinois

La Grange Highlands is an unincorporated community south of La Grange in Cook County, Illinois, United States.[tpl]cite web|title=About La Grange Highlands Civic Association |url=http://lhcivic.com/pages/about.html|publisher=La Grange Highlands Civic Association|accessdate=12 September 2012[/tpl] It is a wooded area with large residential properties, located close to I-294, I-55 and I-355.
