[[Yasuoka, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Yasuoka|泰阜村|Yasuoka-mura[/tpl] is a village located in Shimoina District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the village has an estimated population of 2,153 and a density of 33.36 persons per km². The total area is 64.54 km².
