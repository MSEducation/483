[[Andrei Zolotov]]

CATEGORIES: 1993 births, Living people, Russian footballers

 Andrei Valeryevich Zolotov ([tpl]lang-ru|Андрей Валерьевич Золотов[/tpl]; born September 26, 1993) is a Russian football defender, who last played for FC Biolog-Novokubansk Progress.
He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Biolog-Novokubansk Progress on July 22, 2013 in a game against FC Torpedo Armavir.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Russian Football Union|url=http://rfspro.ru/cgi-bin/player/script2.cgi?par1=119024|title=Career Summary|date=10 August 2013[/tpl]
