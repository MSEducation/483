[[Erebia euryale]]

CATEGORIES: Erebia, Insects of Germany, Insects of Romania, Insects of Poland, Insects of Finland, Insects of Albania, Insects of Andorra, Insects of Bosnia and Herzegovina, Insects of Hungary, Insects of Greece, Insects of Bulgaria, Insects of Italy

The Large Ringlet (Erebia euryale) is a member of the Nymphalidae family. It's a mid-altitude alpine species. It lives in spruce forest clearings and damp meadows within these. A separate lowland subspecies occurs in Russia.
