[[Neile Graham]]

CATEGORIES: 1958 births, Living people, 20th-century Canadian poets, 21st-century Canadian poets, People from Seattle, Washington, Canadian women poets, Clarion Workshop

Neile Graham (born October 8, 1958) is a Canadian poet and scholar. She was born in WinnipegWinnipeg, Manitoba, and currently lives in Seattle in the United States.  
Graham serves as the program administrator for both the Ph.D. in the Built Environment and the interdiscipliinary certificates in Urban Design and Historic Preservation in the College of Built Environments (formerly the College of Architecture and Urban Planning) at the University of Washington. She also administers the Clarion West Writers Workshop.
