[[ARMA Tactics]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 video games, Bohemia Interactive games, Simulation video games, Single-player-only video games, Windows games, Linux games, Tactical role-playing video games

Arma Tactics THD (also known as ARMA TACTICS) is a simulation video game for mobile devices that run iOS and Android, developed by Bohemia Interactive. The game was released on May 2013  for the Nvidia Shield. Unlike the previous Arma games, this is a turn-based tactical gamehttp://www.vg247.com/2013/01/30/arma-tactics-announced-for-nvidia-tegra-devices-coming-q2-2013/ where the player controls a squad of several so called "operatives".

==Gameplay==

This is a turn based strategy game play. Player has control of 4 operators with different abilities. There are two game modes, one is the regular campaign and the other user created missions. Score up on experience points to gain operator superiority and add up credits to buy gadgets & upgrades. 

==Development==

As of July 26, 2013 this game will support only Tegra3 and Tegra4 devices a full list is available here.http://forums.bistudio.com/showthread.php?159980-List-of-supported-devices
Although, there is a separate version for non-Tegra devices.
Currently, the game is supported on Windows, Mac OS X, and Linux in addition to various mobile platforms.
