[[No Worries (Simon Webbe song)]]

CATEGORIES: 2005 singles, Simon Webbe songs, Songs written by Simon Webbe, Songs written by Matt Prime, Songs written by Tim Woodcock

"No Worries" is the second single released from Blue band member Simon Webbe's debut solo album, Sanctuary. The song features backing vocals by Yvonne John Lewis. The single peaked at #4 on the UK Singles Chart, the same position as its predecessor, "Lay Your Hands". The song became a Top 20 hit in Italy, as well as reaching #3 in the Netherlands.
