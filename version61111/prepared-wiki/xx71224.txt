[[Belair, Leon County, Florida]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Leon County, Florida, Unincorporated communities in Florida

Belair is an unincorporated community in southern Leon County, Florida, United States, south of Tallahassee.  It is in the area bordered on the south by Capital Circle SE, on the north by Campbell Pond, on the west by Woodville Highway, and on the east by wooded property.
During the American Civil War, this area was home to Camp Leon, a Confederate army encampment.
