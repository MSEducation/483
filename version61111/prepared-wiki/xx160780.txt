[[Adil Ahmed]]

CATEGORIES: Bangladeshi cricketers, Dhaka Division cricketers, Barisal Division cricketers, Year of birth missing (living people), Living people

Syed Adil Ahmed is a Bangladeshi cricketer who has played three first-class and two List A games during his career. He made his debut in the 2001-02 season, playing for Barisal Division against Rajshahi Division. He played his final game in the 2004-05 cricket season, for Dhaka Division against his former club Barisal Division. He was primarily a batsman, but did not reach a half century in either form of the game.
