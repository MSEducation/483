[[Kodomonokuni Station (Kanagawa)]]

[tpl]nihongo|Kodomonokuni Station|こどもの国駅|Kodomonokuni-eki[/tpl] is the terminal railway station operated by Yokohama Minatomirai Railway's Kodomonokuni Line located in the Aoba-ku, Yokohama, Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan. It is located next to the Kodomonokuni Theme Park. 

==History==

Kodomonokuni Station was opened on April 18, 1967.

==Lines==

==Station layout==

Kodomonokuni Station has an elevated side platform serving one track for bi-directional traffic. The station is normally unattended.
