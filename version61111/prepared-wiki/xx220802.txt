[[Platyptilia nussi]]

CATEGORIES: Pterophoridae, Endemic fauna of the Philippines

Platyptilia nussi is a moth of the family Pterophoroidea. It is found on Luzon.Review of the Pterophoridae (Lepidoptera) from the Philippines
The wingspan is ca. 19 mm. Adults are on wing in November.

==Etymology==

The species is named after Dr M. Nuss, one of the collectors of the specimen and investigator of microlepidoptera.
