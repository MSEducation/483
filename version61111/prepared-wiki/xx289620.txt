Matthew Register (born August 14, 1989) is a Canadian professional ice hockey defenceman. He is currently playing with the Ontario Reign of the ECHL.
Register made his professional debut on January 25, 2012, playing defence for the Allen Americans of the Central Hockey League in a 2-0 home game shut-out over the Arizona Sundogs.
On February 22, 2013, the Manchester Monarchs of the American Hockey League (AHL) signed Register to a one-year AHL contract and then loaned him to the Ontario Reign of the ECHLMonarchs Sign Register where he skated in 17 regular season games and 10 playoff contests during the 2012–13 season.
On July 3, 2013, the Ontario Reign re-signed Register for the 2013–14 ECHL season.
