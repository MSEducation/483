[[Margaret Whiting Sings the Jerome Kern Songbook]]

CATEGORIES: 1960 albums, Margaret Whiting albums, Verve Records albums, Albums arranged by Russell Garcia, English-language albums, Albums produced by Norman Granz, Albums conducted by Russell Garcia

Margaret Whiting Sings the Jerome Kern Songbook is a 1960 studio album by Margaret Whiting, with an orchestra conducted and arranged by Russell Garcia, focusing on the songs of Jerome Kern. Originally released as a double-LP set by Verve Records in 1960, it was reissued on CD by Universal in Japan (1998, 2007) and the United States (2002).[tpl]Allmusic|class=album|id=r583676|label=Margaret Whiting Sings the Jerome Kern Songbook[/tpl]

==Track listing==

All music written by Jerome Kern with lyricists as indicated.
