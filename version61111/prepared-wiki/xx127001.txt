[[Livadi]]

CATEGORIES: Aromanian settlements in Greece

Livadi ([tpl]lang-el|Λιβάδι[/tpl]) is a town and a former municipality in the Larissa regional unit, Thessaly, Greece. Since the 2011 local government reform it is part of the municipality Elassona, of which it is a municipal unit.Kallikratis law Greece Ministry of Interior [tpl]el icon[/tpl] Before the 2011 local government reform it was an independent municipality.The 2011 census recorded 2,674 residents in the municipal unit and 2,244 residents in the community of Livadi. ==Geography==

Livadi borders Pieria regional unit to the northeast, and Kozani regional unit to the northwest.  Livadi is located west of Pythio and Katerini, NNW of Larissa, north of Elassona and southeast of Kozani.
