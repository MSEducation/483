[[Zeibekiko of Evdokia]]

CATEGORIES: Greek music, Instrumentals, Film music

Zeibekiko of Evdokia ([tpl]lang-el|Ζεϊμπέκικο της Ευδοκίας[/tpl]) is a popular instrumental zeibekiko song.
It was written by Manos Loizos, for the 1971 Greek drama film Evdokia, one of the most important works of Greek cinema.
Today it is danced invariably as a characteristic zeibekiko song, and as one the most popular of the kind.
