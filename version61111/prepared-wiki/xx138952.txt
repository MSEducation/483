[[London Senior Football Championship]]

CATEGORIES: London Senior Football Championship, Senior Gaelic football county championships

The London Senior Football Championship is a Gaelic Athletic Association cup competition between the top Gaelic football clubs in London, England. The London Football championship began in 1897 with Ireland United claiming the inaugural title.
