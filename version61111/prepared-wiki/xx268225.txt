[[Edwin Retamoso]]

CATEGORIES: 1982 births, Living people, Peruvian footballers, Peru international footballers, Atlético Minero footballers, Inti Gas Deportes footballers, Cienciano footballers, Torneo Descentralizado footballers, Association football midfielders

Edwin Retamoso (born 23 February 1982) is a Peruvian international footballer who plays for Real Garcilaso in the Torneo Descentralizado, as a defensive midfielder.

==Career==

Retamoso has played for Atlético Minero, Inti Gas Deportes and Cienciano.[tpl]NFT player|pid=44959[/tpl]
He made his international debut for Peru in 2011.
