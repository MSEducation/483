[[Andrew Verity]]

CATEGORIES: British business and financial journalists, BBC Radio 5 Live presenters, 1969 births, Living people

John Andrew Verity (born April 8, 1969, Leeds) is an English financial journalist, currently working for the BBC Radio Five Live.
After being raised in Barnet, he became a financial journalist in the mid-1990s. He presents the daily radio show Wake Up to Money with Mickey Clark.
In March 2012 he married a fellow BBC journalist.
