[[NK Šoštanj]]

CATEGORIES: Association football clubs established in 1920, Football clubs in Slovenia, 1920 establishments in Slovenia

Nogometni Klub Šoštanj ([tpl]lang-en|Šoštanj Football Club[/tpl]), commonly referred to as NK Šoštanj or simply Šoštanj, is a Slovenian football club from Šoštanj, which plays in the Regional League. It was also known as Partizan Šoštanj and Usnjar Šoštanj. The club was founded in 1920.
