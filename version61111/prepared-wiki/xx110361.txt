[[Esquivel]]

CATEGORIES: Basque-language surnames, Surnames

Esquivel is a surname of Basque origin as well as a place name.

==Origin==

The last name Esquivel (or Esquibel) has its origins in the village of Esquivel, located in the ayuntamiento of Mendoza, √Ålava, in Euskadi.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.buber.net/Basque/Surname/E/eskibel.html| title=Eskerrik asko Ezkibel| publisher=Blas Uberuaga| date=23 April 2009|accessdate=24 May 2009[/tpl]

===Meaning===

Usually surnames were originated in the Basque Country as name of houses, being families known by the name of the house they inhabited once. Esquivel was originally spelled Ezkibel and evolved from the Basque words Ezki, which means lime tree, and Gibel, which means behind. Therefore, Esquivel means the house behind the lime trees.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.kaixo.com/euskaletxea/index.php?aukera=ikusi&hizk=2&abizena=ESKIBEL| title=Significado del Apellido Esquivel| publisher=Portal de Euskadi| date=|accessdate=24 May 2009[/tpl]
