[[Darband, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Darband ([tpl]lang-fa|دربند[/tpl])[tpl]GEOnet3|-3773151|Darband[/tpl] is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 218, in 39 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
