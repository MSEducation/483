[[2008 FINA Synchronised Swimming World Trophy]]

CATEGORIES: Synchronized swimming, 2008 in synchronized swimming

The 3rd FINA Synchronized Swimming World Trophy was held December 5-7, 2008 in Madrid, Spain. It featured swimmers from 8 nations, swimming in four events: Duet Free, Duet Thematic, Team Free and Free Combination.HistoFINA, volume 9 published by FINA on 2009-07-01; retrieved 2013-08-09.
