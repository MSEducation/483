[[Kellyn Acosta]]

Kellyn Acosta (born July 24, 1995) is an American soccer player who currently plays for FC Dallas in Major League Soccer.

==Career==

Acosta played for the FC Dallas academy prior to becoming a homegrown signing in July 2012 (although he was not eligible for selection until the 2013 season).http://www.google.com/#bav=on.2,or.r_qf.&fp=53e3d0ede0d92e8a&q=kellyn+acosta+signed He did make three MLS Reserve League appearances. He was named U.S. Soccer Development Academy Central Conference Player of the Year in 2011-12.http://www.bigdsoccer.com/2013/7/11/4514088/kellyn-acosta-named-u-s-soccer-development-academy-central-conference
He made his first-team debut in a 3-0 loss at Seattle in August 2013.http://www.fcdallas.com/news/2013/08/acosta-pleased-first-mls-appearance-seattle

==International==

After having played in the 2011 FIFA U-17 World Cup, Acosta became the youngest member of the US squad for the US squad at the 2013 FIFA U-20 World Cup.http://soccerblog.dallasnews.com/2013/06/kellyn-acosta-named-to-us-u-20-world-cup-roster.html/
