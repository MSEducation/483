[[Abschieds-Rufe]]

CATEGORIES: Waltzes by Johann Strauss II

Abschieds-Rufe (Cries of Farewell), op, 179, is a waltz composed by Johann Strauss II.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.naxosdirect.com/title/8.223233 |title=Strauss II, J.: Edition - Vol. 33|accessdate=2008-10-05|publisher=NaxosDirect[/tpl] It premiered in January 1856 at a ball attended by some two thousand guests in the Sofienbad-Saal in Vienna. The waltz was very well received, and was repeated four times.
