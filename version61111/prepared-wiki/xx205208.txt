[[One Tampa City Center]]

CATEGORIES: Skyscrapers in Tampa, Florida, Skyscrapers between 150 and 199 meters

One Tampa City Center is a skyscraper in Tampa, Florida. It was the tallest building in the state for three years and the tallest building in the city until the completion of the Bank of America tower in 1986. Skyscraper Page At 39 stories tall. It is currently the third tallest structure in the city at 537 feet tall.http://skyscraperpage.com/cities/?buildingID=652
On November 13, 2012, it was announced that PNC Bank will replace Verizon as the corporate logo atop the building, beginning in May 2013.PNC Bank logo to top Tampa's third highest building
