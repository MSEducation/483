[[Orient Express (1954 film)]]

CATEGORIES: 1954 films, French films, Italian films, West German films, Italian-language films, 1950s drama films, Rail transport films

Orient Express is a 1954 Italian-language film starring Silvana Pampanini, Henri Vidal and Eva Bartok.http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0047312/ The plot revolves around a two-day stop at a village in the Alps by passengers on the Orient Express.
