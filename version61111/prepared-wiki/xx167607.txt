[[Gehendra Bahadur Rajbhandari]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Prime ministers of Nepal, 1923 births

Gehendra Bahadur Rajbhandari (born 1923) was the most senior Minister in Nepal between 1970 and 1971. In 1969, Rajbhandari was Minister for Palace Affairs and Foreign Affairs, and was the leader of the Nepalese delegation to the United Nations.Nepal Ministry of Foreign Affairs He also paid a friendly visit to the Soviet Union in 1969.Nepal Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Russia He currently works as a Section Officer in the U.N.
