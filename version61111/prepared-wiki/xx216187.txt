Andrew L. Tan ([tpl]zh|t=吳聰滿|s=吴聪满|first=t[/tpl])[tpl]cite web|url=http://baike.baidu.com/view/5991196.htm|title=Andrew Tan @ Baidu (Chinese)[/tpl] is a Filipino-Chinese billionaire with business interests in real estate, liquor and fast food. In 2011, Forbes Magazine rated him fourth on the list of the "Philippines 40 richest" with an estimated net worth of $2 billion.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.forbes.com/lists/2010/86/philippines-10_Andrew-Tan_M1TQ.html|title=The Philippines' 40 Richest : #5 Andrew Tan|date=July 7, 2010|publisher=forbes.com[/tpl]
Originally an immigrant from China, Tan was born in the Fujian province. He spent his childhood at an apartment in Hong Kong which was shared by other families. Later, he moved to Manila where he studied accounting at University of the East.
