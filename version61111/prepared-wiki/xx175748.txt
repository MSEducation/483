[[WFKJ]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Pennsylvania

WFKJ is a Christian radio station licensed to Cashtown, Pennsylvania, broadcasting on 890 kHz AM.  The station only operates during daytime hours serves Adams County, Pennsylvania and is owned by Jesus Is Lord Ministries International.WFKJ fcc.gov. Accessed August 22, 2012 WFKJ's programming consists of Christian Talk and Teaching and includes programming from the Oasis Network.Schedule WFKJ. Accessed August 22, 2012
