[[Tor Bremer]]

CATEGORIES: 1955 births, Living people, Labour Party (Norway) politicians

Tor Bremer (born 1955) is a farmer and politician from Luster, Norway in Sogn og Fjordane municipality. He was deputy mayor of the municipality from 2003-2007 for the Arbeiderpartiet. At the Norwegian parliamentary election, 2009, he was the second candidate for Ap in Sogn og Fjordane.
Tor Bremer is from Luster and was leader of Sogn og Fjordane Arbeiderparti for a year.
