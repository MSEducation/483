[[Gmina Orońsko]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Szydłowiec County

Gmina Orońsko is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Szydłowiec County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Orońsko, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|14|km|mi|0[/tpl] north-east of Szydłowiec and [tpl]convert|100|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|81.91|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 5,706.

==Villages==

Gmina Orońsko contains the villages and settlements of Bąków, Chałupki Łaziskie, Chronów, Chronów-Kolonia Dolna, Chronów-Kolonia Górna, Chronówek, Ciepła, Dobrut, Gozdków, Guzów, Guzów-Kolonia, Helenów, Krogulcza Mokra, Krogulcza Sucha, Łaziska, Orońsko, Śniadków, Tomaszów, Wałsnów and Zaborowie.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Orońsko is bordered by the gminas of Jastrząb, Kowala, Szydłowiec, Wieniawa, Wierzbica and Wolanów.
