[[Cusihuiriachi]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Chihuahua

 
Cusihuiriachi is a town in the Mexican state of Chihuahua. Although it is now practically a ghost town, it serves as the municipal seat for the surrounding municipality of the same name. "Cusihuiriachi" is a Tarahumara word meaning "erect pole".Cusihuiriachi Enciclopedia de los municipios de MÃ©xico
