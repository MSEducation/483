[[List of islands of the Faroe Islands]]

CATEGORIES: Lists of islands by group, Islands of the Faroe Islands, Faroe Islands-related lists

This is a list of islands of the Faroe Islands. There are 18 islands, of which Lítla Dímun is the only one uninhabited. Besides these 18 islands there are also several islets and skerries in the Faroes. thumb|left|255px|Map with islands labeled.
