[[Beeston Hockey Club]]

CATEGORIES: English field hockey clubs, Sport in Nottingham, 1907 establishments in England

Beeston Hockey Club is a field hockey club based in Beeston, England. The club was founded in 1907, and play their home games at Highfields Hockey Centre in Nottingham. Beeston are nicknamed the Bees.[tpl]cite web|title=Beeston Hockey Club â€“ a Brief History|url=http://www.beestonhockeyclub.com/about|publisher=Beeston Hockey Club|accessdate=26 September 2012[/tpl]
The men's 1st XI play in the English Hockey League Men's Premier Division, and the ladies 1st XI play in the Women's Hockey League Ladies Premier Division. The large club fields six men's sides, seven ladies sides and various junior sides.
The men's 1st XI won their first major trophy in 2008 defeating Bowdon 4-3 in the Men's National Cup.[tpl]cite web|author=|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/hockey/18037741 |title=Men's National Cup treble for Beeston Hockey Club |publisher=BBC Sport |date=2012-05-14 |accessdate=26 September 2012[/tpl] The men's team won their first National League Premier Division title in 2010/11, and went on to win it again in 2012/13 and 2013/14.[tpl]cite web|author=|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/hockey/13033475 |title=Beeston Hockey Club seal Premier League title |publisher=BBC Sport |date=2011-04-11 |accessdate=26 September 2012[/tpl]
