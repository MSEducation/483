[[Pradnya Gadre]]

CATEGORIES: Indian badminton players, Indian Badminton Champions, Living people, 1990 births

Pradnya Gadre (born 17 October 1990) is an Indian badminton player from Nashik,Maharashtra who currently plays doubles and mixed doubles.She partners Ashwini Ponnappa for women's doubles events.
The other partners she earlier paired up for women's doubles are Jyotshna P, Prajakta Sawant, Nitya Sosale.Doubles Parners
For mixed doubles Events she partners with Akshay Dewalkar, earlier it was Pranaav Jerry Chopra.Mixed Doubles Partners
