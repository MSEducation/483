[[Yerma (Villa-Lobos)]]

CATEGORIES: Operas by Heitor Villa-Lobos, 1971 operas, Operas, Operas set in Iberia, Operas based on plays

Yerma is an opera in three acts by Heitor Villa-Lobos based on the  tragedy of the same name by Federico García Lorca. Written in 1955, it was first performed by the Santa Fe Opera in Santa Fe, New Mexico on August 12, 1971.
