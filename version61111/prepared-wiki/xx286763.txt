[[Abu Janlu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Abu Janlu ([tpl]lang-fa|ابوجانلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Abū Jānlū; also known as Ābājānlū, Abidzhanglu, Abijanglu, Āb-i-Jānlu, and Ābūchānlū)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3051673|Abu Janlu[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 60, in 17 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
