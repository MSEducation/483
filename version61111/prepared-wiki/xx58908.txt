[[Phuket International Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in Thailand, Buildings and structures in Phuket Province

Phuket International Airport ([tpl]lang-th|ท่าอากาศยานภูเก็ต[/tpl]) [tpl]airport codes|HKT|VTSP[/tpl] is an airport serving Phuket Province of Thailand. It is located in the north of Phuket Island, [tpl]convert|32|km|NM|abbr=off|lk=out[/tpl] from the centre of Phuket City. The airport plays a major role in Thailand's tourism industry, as Phuket Island is a popular resort destination. It is the third busiest airport in Thailand in terms of passengers, after Suvarnabhumi Airport and Don Mueang Airport in the Bangkok metropolitan area. This International airport set a record 11.3 million arrivals and departures in 2013, up 18.8 per cent from 2012. 

==Facilities==

The airport is at an elevation of [tpl]convert|82|ft|0[/tpl] above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 09/27 with an asphalt surface 
measuring [tpl]convert|3000|x|45|m|0[/tpl].
The airport has two terminals: Terminal 1 is used for international flights, and Terminal 2 for domestic flights. Terminal X for charter flight opened in Feb 2014.

==Airport expansion==

Due to record-setting passenger numbers, the airport is currently undergoing a THB 5.8 billion (approx. USD $188 million) expansion and renovation which will last until 2015. The expansion plans to increase capacity to 12.5 million passenger a year calls for a new international terminal, refurbishment of the existing terminal for domestic use, and other airfield improvements. A new car park as well as air cargo office are also being planned.
