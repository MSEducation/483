[[Chenghua District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Chengdu, County-level divisions of Sichuan

Chenghua District ([tpl]zh|s=成华区 |t=成華區 |p=Chénghuá Qū |l=become brilliant[/tpl]) is one of the five districts at the heart of Chengdu, People's Republic of China, covering part of the eastern portion of the city. [tpl]asof|2010[/tpl] it had a population of 938,785 residing in an area of [tpl]convert|109|km2[/tpl].
