[[1997 Copa América Final]]

CATEGORIES: 1997 Copa América, 1997 in Brazilian football, Brazil national football team matches, Bolivia national football team matches, Copa América finals, 1997 in Bolivian football

The 1997 Copa América Final was the final match of the 1997 Copa América. It was held on June 29, 1997 in La Paz. Brazil won the match against Bolivia by a 3-1 score. This was the fifth Copa América obtained by the Brazilian squad and first one outside their home nation.
