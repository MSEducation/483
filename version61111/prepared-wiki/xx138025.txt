[[Nishi-Memambetsu Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Hokkaido Prefecture

[tpl]Nihongo|Nishi-Memambetsu Station|西女満別駅|Nishi-Memanbetsu-eki[/tpl] is a railway station in Ōzora, Hokkaidō, Japan, operated by the Hokkaido Railway Company (JR Hokkaido).[tpl]cite web |url= http://www.jrasahi.co.jp/contents/facilities/station/a66_nishi-memambetsu.html|title= Nishi-Memambetsu Station information|year= 2007|publisher= Hokkaido Railway Company Asahikawa Branch|location= Japan|language= Japanese|archiveurl= |archivedate= |accessdate= 10 November 2011[/tpl] It is the closest station to Memanbetsu Airport.

==Lines==

The Nishi-Memambetsu Station is served by the Sekihoku Main Line from [tpl]Ja-stalink|Asahikawa[/tpl] to [tpl]Ja-stalink|Abashiri[/tpl].

==Station layout==

The station is an above-ground station and consists of a single side platform serving a single bidirectional track. The station has no toilet facilities.

==History==

The station opened in 1947 as a temporary arrival and departure point for Asahino. From January 15, 1950, the station becomes a permanent passenger station and was renamed Nishi-Memambetsu. A freight platform was constructed in 1951.
The station became unstaffed from January 10, 1983.

==Surrounding area==

The station is a twenty-minute walk from Memanbetsu Airport.
