[[Esperin]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Esperin ([tpl]lang-fa|اسپرين[/tpl], also Romanized as Esperīn, Esprīn, and Asperin; also known as Esfrīn, Isbarin, and Isbirin)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3062388|Esperin[/tpl] is a village in Sojas Rud Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 258, in 52 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
