[[Calybitia]]

CATEGORIES: Epipaschiinae, Monotypic lepidoptera genera

Calybitia is a genus of snout moths. It was described by Schaus, in 1922, and contains the species C. adolescens, which is found in Guatemala.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.nic.funet.fi/pub/sci/bio/life/insecta/lepidoptera/ditrysia/pyraloidea/pyralidae/epipaschiinae/calybitia/index.html |title=Calybitia at funet |publisher=Nic.funet.fi |date= |accessdate=2011-09-29[/tpl]
