[[47th Indian Infantry Brigade]]

CATEGORIES: British Indian Army brigades

The 47th Indian Infantry Brigade was an Infantry formation of the Indian Army during World War II. The brigade was formed October 1942, at Secunderabad in India and assigned to the 19th Indian Infantry Division. In March 1942, it was transferred to the 14th Indian Infantry Division.[tpl]cite web|accessdate=2009-10-23|publisher=Order of Battle|title=47 Indian Brigade|url=http://www.ordersofbattle.com/UnitData.aspx?UniX=6164&Tab=Sup[/tpl]
