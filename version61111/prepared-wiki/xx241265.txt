[[Taibus Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Taibus Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠲᠠᠶᠢᠫᠤᠰᠧ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Tayipusė qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|c=太仆寺旗|p=Tàipúsì Qí[/tpl]) is a banner of Inner Mongolia, China. It is under the administration of Xilin Gol League.
