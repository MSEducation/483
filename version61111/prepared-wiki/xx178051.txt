[[Gmina Strzegowo]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Mława County

Gmina Strzegowo is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Mława County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Strzegowo, which lies approximately 25 kilometres (16 mi) south-west of Mława and 89 km (55 mi) north-west of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|214.23|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 7,902.

==Villages==

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Strzegowo is bordered by the gminas of Ciechanów, Glinojeck, Raciąż, Radzanów, Regimin, Stupsk, Szreńsk and Wiśniewo.
