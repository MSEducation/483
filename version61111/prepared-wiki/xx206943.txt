[[2008–09 Dinamo Riga season]]

CATEGORIES: Dinamo Riga seasons, 2008–09 KHL season

The 2008–09 Dinamo Riga season was the first season of the franchise in the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). This was also inaugural season for the league. Dinamo Riga played most of their home games in Arena Riga, however some matches were held in other locations.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.diena.lv/lat/sports/hokejs/hokeja-komandu-dinamo-atbalstis-nike-bauer |title=Biļetes uz Rīgas Dinamo spēlēm Arēnā Rīga maksās no 7 - 12 latus |publisher=diena.lv |language=Latvian |date=June 6, 2008 |accessdate=2008-07-26[/tpl] Dinamo Riga were playing in Kharlamov division along with Avangard Omsk, Lokomotiv Yaroslavl, HC Lada Togliatti, HC Sibir Novosibirsk and Amur Khabarovsk.

==Pre-season==

As it is the first season for the franchise, Dinamo Riga had to gather their roster from nothing. Most of the team players are members of Latvia national team. First players for the team were announced on April 25.[tpl]cite news| title=First Dinamo players are known| publisher= eSports| date=2008-04-25| language=Latvian| url=http://esports.lv/hokejs/25042008-zinami_pirmie_rigas_dinamo_speletaji |accessdate=2008-07-26| archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20080625072516/http://esports.lv/hokejs/25042008-zinami_pirmie_rigas_dinamo_speletaji?| archivedate= 25 June 2008 | deadurl= no[/tpl] First foreign player to join the team was Czech Filip Novák on June 26.[tpl]cite news| title=First foreigner in Dinamo| publisher= eSports| date=2008-06-26| language=Latvian| url=http://esports.lv/hokejs/26062008-rigas_dinamo_pirmais_legionars| accessdate=2008-07-26| archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20080705230934/http://esports.lv/hokejs/26062008-rigas_dinamo_pirmais_legionars| archivedate= 5 July 2008 | deadurl= no[/tpl] The team roster was completed, when the last two leggionares Duvie Westcott and Mark Hartigan joined the team on July 22.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.dinamoriga.lv/zinas?zinaId=27 |title=Rīgas Dinamo pievienojas divkārtējs Stenlija kausa ieguvējs |publisher=DinamoRiga.lv |language=Latvian |date=June 22, 2008 |accessdate=2008-07-26 |archiveurl = http://web.archive.org/web/20080727181310/http://www.dinamoriga.lv/zinas?zinaId=27  |archivedate = 2008-07-27[/tpl]
Dinamo Riga started their pre-season training camp on July 15. The camp continued till July 28 and took place in Liptovský Mikuláš, Slovakia.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.dinamoriga.lv/zinas?zinaId=17 |title=Rīgas Dinamo dodas uz Slovākiju |publisher=DinamoRiga.lv |language=Latvian |date=June 15, 2008 |accessdate=2008-07-26[/tpl] [tpl]Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot[/tpl] The first game in franchise history was held on July 25, when Dinamo Riga defeated MHk 32 Liptovský Mikuláš in a shootout after a 1-1 in regular time.
On August 2 Dinamo played their first home game, when they won against Amur Khabarovsk in Riga. Then at the "Inbox.lv hall" in Riga, Dinamo won the Inbox.lv tournament, in which they defeated Dinamo Minsk and Barys Astana. In August the team also participated in the Tampere Cup (Tampere) and the Governor's Cup (Mytishchi).
