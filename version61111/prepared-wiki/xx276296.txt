[[Yuri Kushnarev]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 births, Living people, Russian rugby union players, Sportspeople from Moscow

Yuri Kushnarev  ([tpl]lang-ru|Юрий Кушнарёв[/tpl]) (born 6 June 1985) is a rugby union footballer who plays as a fly-half.
