[[Wang Yunlan]]

CATEGORIES: 1913 births, Year of death missing, Light-heavyweight boxers, Olympic boxers of China, Boxers at the 1936 Summer Olympics, Taiwanese boxers

Wang Yunlan ([tpl]zh|c=王潤蘭[/tpl], born May 28, 1913, date of death unknown) was a Taiwanese boxer who competed for the Republic of China in the 1936 Summer Olympics.
In 1936 he was eliminated in the first round of the light heavyweight class after losing his fight to Wim Fock.
