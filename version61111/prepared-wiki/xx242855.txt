[[Rošpoh, Kungota]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Municipality of Kungota

Rošpoh is a dispersed settlement in the western Slovene Hills ([tpl]lang-sl|Slovenske gorice[/tpl]) in northeastern Slovenia. The hamlet of Mali Rošpoh in the northern part of the settlement lies in the Municipality of Kungota.Kungota municipal site The remaining part of the settlement belongs to the municipality of Maribor.
