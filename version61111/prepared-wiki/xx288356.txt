[[Kinfolk (magazine)]]

CATEGORIES: American lifestyle magazines, Magazines published in Oregon, Media in Portland, Oregon

Kinfolk Magazine is an independent quarterly entertainment and lifestyle magazine based out of Portland, Oregon, founded in 2011.

==The Magazine==

Kinfolk was created by Nathan Williams and a group of friends, the first edition being issued in July 2011.[tpl]cite web|author=Alexandra Zeigler |url=http://www.weldonowen.com/blog/kinfolk-magazines-creator-nathan-williams-inspiration-small-gatherings-and-creative-process |title=Kinfolk magazine's creator Nathan Williams on inspiration, small gatherings, and the creative process |publisher=Weldon Owen |date=2012-05-28 |accessdate=2013-09-22[/tpl] Kinfolk publishes an issue each season based on the themes of "cook, make and do." Primarily a lifestyle magazine, it focuses on food culture and redefining the modern art of entertaining through photo essays, recipes, interviews, profiles, personal stories and practical tips.[tpl]cite news|last=Simmons |first=Andrew |url=http://blogs.laweekly.com/squidink/2011/06/kinfolk_magazine_launches_july.php |title=Kinfolk Magazine Launches July 15th |work=LA Weekly (blog) |date=2011-06-28 |accessdate=2013-09-22[/tpl]
In addition to their print publication, Kinfolk is also known for their 'Community Gathering' event series that takes place globally each month, and also have begun producing international food-based workshops, cookbooks and a short film series.[tpl]cite web|last=Levitt |first=Jonathan |url=http://www.boston.com/lifestyle/food/2012/11/28/the-unstyled-look-hipster-food-magazines/iG7vKB5B8k1wsQvjfdk22O/story.html |title=The unstyled look of hipster food magazines |publisher=Boston.com |date=2012-11-27 |accessdate=2013-09-22[/tpl]
Released quarterly, Kinfolk published four 144-page, advertisement-free issues a year. Each issue is themed around a topic that is related to the season it is being published in, with all food, entertaining and lifestyle content geared towards that theme.[tpl]cite web|author=http://www.sltrib.com |url=http://www.sltrib.com/sltrib/entertainment/53685783-81/kinfolk-says-magazine-digital.html.csp?page=1 |title=Utah Local News - Salt Lake City News, Sports, Archive - The Salt Lake Tribune |publisher=Sltrib.com |date=2012-03-23 |accessdate=2013-09-22[/tpl] Sample articles include interviews with famous chefs, sample themed menus with accompanying recipes, illustrated guides to daily encounters and inspirational photo essays encouraging their readers to try new activities.[tpl]cite web|last=Velden |first=Dana |url=http://www.thekitchn.com/kinfolk-a-guide-for-small-gath-152318 |title=Kinfolk: A Guide for Small Gatherings |publisher=The Kitchn |date=2011-07-27 |accessdate=2013-09-22[/tpl][tpl]cite web|author=0:00:00 0:01:00 |url=http://monocle.com/radio/shows/the-stack/41/ |title=The Stack, episode 41, - Monocle 24 (Radio) / Shows |publisher=Monocle.com |date=2013-06-08 |accessdate=2013-09-22[/tpl]
The writers, photographers, designers and chefs that contribute to Kinfolk are drawn from a largely international pool of creatives, often numbering over 50 individual contributors an issue.[tpl]cite web|url=http://saydaily.com/2012/11/kinfolk-an-exquisite-guide-to-small-gatherings.html |title=Kinfolk: An Exquisite Guide to Small Gatherings / Say Daily |publisher=Saydaily.com |date=2012-11-14 |accessdate=2013-09-22[/tpl]
The magazine has been consistently praised for its clean design and photographic aesthetic.[tpl]cite web|author=Matthew McIntyre |url=http://desktopmag.com.au/blogs/kinfolk-magazine/#.Uht5RrxR2X1 |title=Kinfolk Magazine | Desktop |publisher=Desktopmag.com.au |date=2011-07-25 |accessdate=2013-09-22[/tpl][tpl]cite web|last=Bainbridge |first=Julia |url=http://www.bonappetit.com/recipes/article/french-fry-and-clam-recipes-from-the-kinfolk-magazine-cutting-room-floor |title=French Fry and Clam Recipes from the Kinfolk Magazine Cutting Room Floor - Bon Appétit |publisher=Bonappetit.com |date=2011-12-22 |accessdate=2013-09-22[/tpl]

===International ties===

In addition to their American readership, Kinfolk also has a growing international following, leading to the magazine being also translated and published into Japanese, Russian and Korean, and their digital realm also existing in an entirely Japanese portal.[tpl]cite web|url=http://minimal.jp/kinfolk-japan-edition-launched/ |title=KINFOLK Japan Edition Launched |publisher=Minimal.Jp |date= |accessdate=2013-09-22[/tpl]

==Community Gatherings==

Starting in 2012, Kinfolk began orchestrating different event series around the world. They have now grown to having up to 30 international events every month based on a seasonal theme, such as flower potlucks, butcher's block parties and campfire cooking.[tpl]cite web|url=http://the189.com/lifestyle/kinfolk-dinner-brooklyn-new-york-short-film/ |title=Kinfolk Dinner – Brooklyn, New York Short Film | OEN |publisher=The189.com |date=2012-03-05 |accessdate=2013-09-22[/tpl] These events take place concurrently on a chosen weekend each month and aim to unite a global community of Kinfolk readers while also offering practical advice and lessons.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.refinery29.com/kinfolk-dinner-san-francisco |title=Kinfolk Dinner Series - Kinfolk Dinner San Francisco |publisher=Refinery29.com |date=2012-04-06 |accessdate=2013-09-22[/tpl]
