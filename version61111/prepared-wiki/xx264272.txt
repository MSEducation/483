[[Radwinter]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Essex, Uttlesford, Civil parishes in Essex

Radwinter is a village and a civil parish on the B1053 road, in the Uttlesford district of the county of Essex, England. Radwinter has a church dedicated to St Mary the Virgin http://www.radwinterhistory.org.uk/radwinter/baptismspt4.html and a primary school.http://www.radwinter.essex.sch.uk/ The hamlet of Radwinter End is to the north.
