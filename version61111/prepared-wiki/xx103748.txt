[[Pogonishte]]

CATEGORIES: Albanian dances

Pogonishte (Pronunciation: Poh-goh-NEE-shteh) is a traditional dance from South Albania. 
The time signature is 2/4 and it is danced in an open and mixed circle of people. The style is proud. Dancers bounce twice gently on every count.[tpl]cite web|url=http://ifdvl.org/dances/Valle%20Pogonishte.pdf|title=Valle Pogonishte[/tpl]
