[[Jimmy Abegg]]

CATEGORIES: 1954 births, Living people, American male singers, American photographers, American singer-songwriters, Musicians from Nashville, Tennessee, People from Box Butte County, Nebraska

Jimmy Abegg, also known as Jimmy A, (December 29, 1954, Alliance, Nebraska) is an American guitarist, composer, director, photographer and artist, currently living in Nashville, Tennessee.
In the 1980s, he formed the pop-rock band Vector with Steve Griffith. In the 1990s, he played as a solo musician, then joined Rich Mullins' A Ragamuffin Band.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.crossrhythms.co.uk/artists/Jimmy_Abegg/8200/ |title=Jimmy Abegg Artist Profile |publisher=Crossrhythms.co.uk |date= |accessdate=2012-01-21[/tpl] He has created a variety of album artwork.
