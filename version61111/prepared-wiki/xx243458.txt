[[Now That's What I Call Music! 11 (South African series)]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 compilation albums, EMI Records compilation albums, Now That's What I Call Music! albums (South African series)

Now That's What I Call Music! 11 or Now 11 was released in 1989. The album is the 11th edition of the South African Now! series. 
There might be more tracks on the CD version of the album.
