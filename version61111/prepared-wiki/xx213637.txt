[[David McIntosh (Gladiators)]]

David McIntosh is a British television personality, Actor, fitness model and security operative. A former Royal Marines commando, he was a Gladiator on the Sky1 TV series Gladiators, under the name Tornado.

==Career==

Born to an English mother and Jamaican father, he grew up in Ashton-in-Makerfield, Greater Manchester. McIntosh fulfilled his childhood dream at the age of 16 of becoming a Royal Marines Commando. He has served in Afghanistan, Northern Ireland, Somalia, and the Persian Gulf. He was deployed to Afghanistan in October 2008 for six months.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.manchestereveningnews.co.uk/news/s/1045362_marine_dave_is_new_gladiator |title=Marine Dave is new gladiator|publisher=Manchester Evening News|accessdate=18 February 2014[/tpl] Upon leaving the Marines he landed a role of Gladiator "Tornado" on SKY 1 hit show "Gladiators" he became a British TV personality.
Until 2013 McIntosh worked as hired security operative in various hot spots around the world, working in roles concerned with terrorism and piracy in Africa. During his time working as a mercenary he was in the headlines when he was among former commandos who escaped from an African despot’s henchmen after a gun battle at sea.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.thesun.co.uk/sol/homepage/news/3407439/TV-Gladiator-Tornado-flees-Eritrean-despots-hell-hole.html |title=TV Gladiator flees despot’s hell hole|publisher=The Sun|date=12 February 2011|accessdate=18 February 2014[/tpl] In late 2013 McIntosh worked in security transportation on a Badger cull operation, and received a traffic violation resulting in a court fine.http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2579244/Kelly-Brooks-ex-Gladiator-boyfriend-pleads-guilty-crashing-van-dead-badgers-bus-stop.html
