[[Dobrogea Wind Farm]]

CATEGORIES: Proposed wind farms in Romania

EDP Dobrogea Wind Farm is a large wind power project in the Dobrogea region, Romania.  It will consist of 113 individual wind turbinesEDP face un parc eolian de 225 MW in circa doi ani [tpl]ro[/tpl] each with a nameplate capacity of around 2 MW.  Collective output will be up to 226 MW of power, enough to power over 148,030 homes, with a capital investment required of approximately US$ 300 million.
