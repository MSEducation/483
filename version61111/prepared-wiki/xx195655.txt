[[Ryan Khatam]]

CATEGORIES: American animators, 1984 births, Living people

Ryan Khatam (born c. 1984) is an American born animator and director. He has worked on Metalocalypse and MTV's Good Vibes. He has recently worked on the Kickstarter-funded feature film, Dick Figures: The Movie.

==History==

Ryan began animating at the age of 14. His first animation was very crude using a basic stick figure. He studied animation at Rancho Bernardo High School, Palomar College, Los Angeles Academy of Figurative Art, and The Animation Academy in 2005. His first freelance job was to animate deaths for the sequel to Gorillas. The second time, he was contacted by Conspiracy Games to create a sequel to his own Johnny Rocketfingers. .
