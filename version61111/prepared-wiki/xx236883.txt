[[Mount Baldy (Polk County, Oregon)]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of Oregon, Landforms of Polk County, Oregon

Mount Baldy is a [tpl]convert|414|foot|m|0[/tpl] hill located within the Baskett Slough National Wildlife Refuge in Polk County, Oregon.
