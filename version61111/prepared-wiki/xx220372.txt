[[Lift Me Up (Kate Voegele song)]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 singles

"Lift Me Up" is a promo single from singer/songwriter Kate Voegele's second studio album, A Fine Mess.http://katevoegele.com/discography/ This single, and the rest of her album, is produced by Mike Elizondo, who has worked with the likes of P!nk and Maroon 5.http://www.starpulse.com/news/index.php/2009/04/15/check_out_kate_voegele_s_new_single_99_t The song was included in 2008 on the Beijing Olympic Games soundtrack, sponsored by AT&T.
