[[Canada at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics, 2013 in Canadian sports, Track and field in Canada

Canada competed at the 2013 World Championships in Athletics in Moscow, Russia, from 10–18 August 2013.
A team of 45 athletes represented the country in the event.
Canada won a total of 5 medals at the event, the most ever surpassing the 1995 edition in which the country won 4 medals.Canada's men's relay team wins bronze after Great Britain disqualfies
