[[Sergey Kirmasov]]

CATEGORIES: 1970 births, Living people, People from Mtsensk, Russian hammer throwers, Soviet hammer throwers, Athletes (track and field) at the 2004 Summer Olympics, Olympic athletes of Russia

Sergey Stanislavovich Kirmasov ([tpl]lang|ru|Сергей Станиславович Кирмасов[/tpl]; born 25 March 1970 in Mtsensk, Oryol) is a retired male hammer thrower from Russia. His personal best is 82.62 metres, achieved in May 1998 in Bryansk.
