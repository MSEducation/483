[[Shallcross (Anchorage, Kentucky)]]

CATEGORIES: Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Kentucky, Queen Anne architecture in Kentucky, Houses completed in 1898, Houses in Jefferson County, Kentucky

Shallcross is a Queen Anne style house built in 1898 in Anchorage, Kentucky by William B. Wood.[tpl]cite web|url=http://pdfhost.focus.nps.gov/docs/NRHP/Text/80001567.pdf |title=National Register of Historic Places Registration:  Shallcross |author= |date= [/tpl] and accompanying photos
It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1980 for its architectural significance.
