Matthew "Matty" Poole is an English professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Lancaster City.

==Career==

Born in Lancaster, Poole made his professional debut for Morecambe on 11 August 2009 in a Football League Cup match against Preston North End.[tpl]soccerbase|id=51748[/tpl] Poole was released by Morecambe at the end of the 2009-10 season,[tpl]cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/m/morecambe/8703242.stm|title=Morecambe release forward Wayne Curtis|accessdate=28 November 2010|date=25 May 2010|publisher=BBC Sport[/tpl] and he later signed for non-league Lancaster City, making his debut for them in August 2010.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.pitchero.com/clubs/lancastercity/s/players-coaches-25604.html&player_id=220276&archive_id=|title=Player profile|accessdate=28 November 2010|publisher=PitchHero[/tpl]
