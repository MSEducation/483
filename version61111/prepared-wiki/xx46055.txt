[[Gedinne]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Belgium, Municipalities of Namur (province)

Gedinne ([tpl]IPA-fr|ʒə.din[/tpl]) is a Walloon municipality located in the Belgian province of Namur. On 1 January 2006 the municipality had 4,405 inhabitants. The total area is 151.56 km², giving a population density of 29 inhabitants per km².
It is situated in the Ardennes east of Meuse valley. The western limit of the Municipality is the French border.
