[[Gârbou]]

CATEGORIES: Communes in Sălaj County

Gârbou ([tpl]lang-hu|Csákigorbó[/tpl]) is a commune located in Sălaj County, Romania. It is composed of seven villages: Bezded (Bezdédtelek), Călacea (Kiskalocsa), Cernuc (Csernek), Fabrica (Cukorgyártelep), Gârbou, Popteleac (Paptelke) and Solomon (Gorbósalamon).
