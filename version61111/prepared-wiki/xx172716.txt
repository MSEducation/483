[[Gordon Low]]

CATEGORIES: 1940 births, Living people, Scottish footballers, Sportspeople from Aberdeen, Association football defenders, The Football League players, Huddersfield Town F.C. players, Bristol City F.C. players, Stockport County F.C. players, Crewe Alexandra F.C. players, Selby Town F.C. players

Gordon Alexander Low (born 11 July 1940 in Aberdeen) is a former professional footballer, who played for Huddersfield Town, Bristol City, Stockport County, Crewe Alexandra and Selby Town.
