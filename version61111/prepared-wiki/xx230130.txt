[[Alvai]]

CATEGORIES: Jaffna District, Populated places in Northern Province, Sri Lanka

Alvai or Alvaay ([tpl]lang-ta|அல்வாய்[/tpl] Alvāy) is a coastal village in Vadamaraadchi, northern Jaffna Peninsula, Sri Lanka. It is located [tpl]convert|30|km|mi|sp=us[/tpl] from the city of Jaffna or [tpl]convert|5|km|mi|sp=us[/tpl] from the town of Point Pedro.
Panai Munai (Point Palmyra), the northern most point of Sri Lanka, is in Alvai.
