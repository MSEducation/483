[[Ford Fiesta R5]]

CATEGORIES: R5 cars, Ford of Europe vehicles

The Ford Fiesta R5 is a R5 rally car built by M-Sport. It is based upon the Ford Fiesta road car. It is the first car homologated to R5 rally class.
