[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Kano]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic Church in Nigeria, Roman Catholic dioceses in Nigeria, Religious organizations established in 1960, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century, 1960 establishments in Nigeria

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Kano ([tpl]lang-la|Kanen(sis)[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Kano in the Ecclesiastical province of Kaduna in Nigeria.
