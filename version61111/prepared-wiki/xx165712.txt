[[Dakota Style]]

CATEGORIES: Snack companies of the United States, Companies based in South Dakota, Clark County, South Dakota, 1985 introductions

Dakota Style is the brand of snack foods produced by Dakota Style Chips, Inc. of Clark, South Dakota. Their flagship product remains the "Industrial Strength" potato chips, though Dakota Style also produces sunflower seeds, salad toppers, and trail corn (mixture of Regular, Caramel & Cheddar Cheese Popcorn).
