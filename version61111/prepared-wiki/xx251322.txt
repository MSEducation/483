[[Hydrangea kwangtungensis]]

CATEGORIES: Hydrangea, Flora of China

Hydrangea kwangtungensis is a species of Hydrangea, native to China.Encyclopedia of hydrangeas, by Cornelis Johannes Gelderen, D. M. van Gelderen. Timber Press, 2004. Page 21. ISBN 0-88192-622-1, ISBN 978-0-88192-622-4  It is most commonly blue, though there are also yellow, purple, and tangerine varieties.
