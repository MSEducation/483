[[Metro Atlalilco]]

CATEGORIES: Mexico City metro stations, Nahuatl words and phrases, Railway stations opened in 1994

Metro Atlalilco is a station along Line 8 and Line 12 of the metro of Mexico City.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.metro.df.gob.mx/red/estacion.html?id=111|title=Atlalilco|language=Spanish|accessdate=30 July 2011[/tpl][tpl]Cite web|last=Archambault|first=Richard|url=http://mexicometro.org/metro/line8/atlalilco/|title=Atlalilco »  Mexico City Metro System|accessdate=30 July 2011[/tpl] Atlalilco is a transfer station between the Line 12 and Line 8.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.metro.df.gob.mx/sabias/linea12b.html|title=Linea 12 - Linea Dorado|language=Spanish|accessdate=8 August 2011[/tpl] It is located on the Calzada Ermita Iztapalapa in the Colonia Santa Isabel Industrial neighbourhood.
The station's logo is a well of water. Atlalilco in Nahuatl means: "where water is kept". It opened for service along Line 8 on 20 July 1994.[tpl]Cite web|last=Monroy|first=Marc|editor-last=Schwandl|editor-first=Robert|url=http://www.urbanrail.net/am/mexi/mex-history.htm|title=Opening Dates for Mexico City's Subway|accessdate=2 August 2011[/tpl] The distance between transfer station in Line 8 and Line 12 measures about [tpl]convert|800|m|ft|0|abbr=on[/tpl] because Metro Axomulco station was cancelled.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.aldf.gob.mx/comsoc-solicitaran-diputados-auditar-proyecto-linea-12-metro-y-transparentar-su-ejecucion--5895.html|title=Solicitarán Diputados Auditar Proyecto de Línea 12 del Metro y Transparentar su Ejecución|language=Spanish|date=10 February 2010|publisher=Asamblea Legislativa del Distrito Federal|accessdate=22 May 2013[/tpl]
