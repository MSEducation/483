[[Lego Prince of Persia]]

CATEGORIES: Lego themes licensed from Disney, Prince of Persia

Lego ''Prince of Persia is a Lego theme based on the video game Prince of Persia: The Sands of Time'' and the film of the same name.[tpl]Cite web |url=http://club.lego.com/en-us/news/newsdetails.aspx?id=150828 |title=Q&A With "The Lego Book" Author Daniel Lipkowitz! |publisher=Lego |accessdate=2010-03-01[/tpl] The first sets were released in April 2010.

==Promotion and release==

In early 2009 Disney and Lego announced a multi-year plans to produce Lego sets based on Disney films. In a press release they announced plans to three Disney themes – Cars, Prince of Persia and Toy Story.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.wired.com/geekdad/2009/02/lego-and-disney/ | title = LEGO and Disney in Deal for Toy Story, Cars, Prince of Persia Kits |first = Ken |last = Denmead | publisher = Wired.com | date = 2009-02-15 | accessdate = 2010-06-19 |archiveurl= |archivedate= |deadurl=no[/tpl] To coincide with the release of the new movie, Prince of Persia: The Sands of Time, Lego released a short Lego-based animated film based on the events of the movie. It was narrated by Jake Gyllenhaal who played Prince Dastan in the movie.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.kotaku.com.au/2010/05/prince-of-persia-lego-movie-looks-like-a-game-to-me/ | title = Prince Of Persia: LEGO Movie Looks Like A Game To Me | publisher = Kotaku |first=Luke |last=Plunkett | date =2010-05-29 | accessdate = 2010-06-19 |archiveurl= |archivedate= |deadurl=no[/tpl]

==Set Information==

The first sets were released in were released in the United States on April 12, 2010 and in the United Kingdom on April 19. The Ostrich Race is a 169 piece set with an age rating of 6–12. The set features a platform, a set of gates, two ostriches and three minifigures – Dastan, Sheik Amar and an ostrich jockey.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.brickset.com/detail/?Set=7570-1 | title = 7570-1: The Ostrich Race | publisher = Brickset | date = | accessdate = 2010-06-19 |archiveurl= |archivedate= |deadurl=no[/tpl] The Fight for the Dagger is a 258 piece set with a recommended age rating of 7–12. The set features a building from the Alamut market, a market stall, a camel, and four minifigures – Dastan, Princess Tamina, Asoka, and an Alamut merchant.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.brickset.com/detail/?Set=7571-1 | title = 7571-1: The Fight for the Dagger | publisher = Brickset | date = | accessdate = 2010-06-19 |archiveurl= |archivedate= |deadurl=no[/tpl] Quest Against Time is a 506 piece set with an age rating of 7–12. It features a trap-filled passage, a rock setting with the Sands of Time, and four minifigures – Princess Tamina, Dastan, Nizam, and Zolm.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.brickset.com/detail/?Set=7572-1 | title = 7572-1: Quest Against Time | publisher = Brickset | date = | accessdate = 2010-06-19 |archiveurl= |archivedate= |deadurl=no[/tpl] Battle of Alamut is a 821 piece set with an age rating of 8–14. The set features the Alamut castle, a camel, and seven minifigures – Dastan, Nizam, Seso, two Alamat guards, Gool, and Tamah.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.brickset.com/detail/?Set=7573-1 | title = 7573-1: Battle of Alamut | publisher = Brickset | date = | accessdate = 2010-06-19 |archiveurl= |archivedate= |deadurl=no[/tpl] Desert Attack is a 67 piece set with a recommended age of 6–12. The set features a desert setting, a horse and four minifigures – Dastan, a hatchet hassansin, a claw hassansin, and a skeleton.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.brickset.com/detail/?Set=7569-1 | title = 7569-1: Desert Attack | publisher = Brickset | date = | accessdate = 2010-06-19 |archiveurl= |archivedate= |deadurl=no[/tpl]
A small set was released in 2010 as an exclusive, available with a subscription of Lego's Brickmaster Magazine.[tpl]cite web | url =http://www.brickset.com/detail/?Set=20017-1 | title = 20017-1: BrickMaster exclusive | publisher = Brickset | date = | accessdate = 2010-08-02 |archiveurl= |archivedate= |deadurl=no[/tpl][tpl]cite web | url = http://www.brickset.com/browse/themes/?theme=Prince%20of%20Persia | title = LEGO Prince of Persia | publisher = Brickset | date = | accessdate = 2010-06-19 |archiveurl= |archivedate= |deadurl=no[/tpl]
