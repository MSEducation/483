[[Shamil Mirzayev]]

CATEGORIES: 1992 births, Living people, Russian footballers

Shamil Sapibulayevich Mirzayev ([tpl]lang-ru|Шамиль Сапибулаевич Мирзаев[/tpl]; born November 28, 1992) is a Russian football midfielder, who currently playing for  FC Dagdizel Kaspiysk.
He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Dagdizel Kaspiysk on July 12, 2013 in a game against FC Taganrog.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Russian Football Union|url=http://rfspro.ru/cgi-bin/player/script2.cgi?par1=117310|title=Career Summary|date=11 August 2013[/tpl]
