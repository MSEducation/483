Acatitla is a station along Line A of the Mexico City Metro.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.metro.df.gob.mx/red/estacion.html?id=128|title=Acatitla|language=Spanish|accessdate=6 August 2011[/tpl][tpl]Cite web|last=Archambault|first=Richard|url=http://mexicometro.org/metro/linea/acatitla/|title=Acatitla »  Mexico City Metro System|accessdate=6 August 2011[/tpl] It is an aboveground station.
