[[WESZ-LP]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Alabama, Low-power FM radio stations in Alabama, Oldies radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 2004, Henry County, Alabama

WESZ-LP (98.7 FM, "Oldies 98.7") is a radio station licensed to serve Abbeville, Alabama.  The station is owned by Abbeville Broadcasting Inc. It airs an Oldies music format.[tpl]cite web |work=Alabama Broadcast Media Page |title=Alabama LPFM & Translators |url=http://bellsouthpwp2.net/c/r/crackedlcd/almediapage/lpfm.html |accessdate=2008-05-21[/tpl][tpl]cite web |title=Station Information Profile |work=Arbitron |url=http://www.arbitron.com/[/tpl]
The station was assigned the WESZ-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on December 27, 2002.[tpl]cite web |title=Call Sign History |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=132859&Callsign=WESZ-LP |work=FCC Media Bureau CDBS Public Access Database[/tpl]
