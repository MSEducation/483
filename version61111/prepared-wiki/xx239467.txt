[[Banco Chambers]]

CATEGORIES: Law firms of Australia, Barristers' chambers

Banco Chambers is a chamber of barristers in Sydney, Australia, specialising in commercial litigation and public law.  The chambers is located on the 5th floor of the Reserve Bank Building (65 Martin Place, Sydney) and the 9th floor of Chifley Tower (2 Chifley Square, Sydney).
The Head of Chambers is Robert Newlinds SC, who replaced Justin Gleeson SC upon his appointment as Solicitor-General of Australia in February 2013.Media Release by the Attorney-General of the Commonwealth of Australia

==Barristers==

The floor has 30 members of varying levels of seniority.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.banco.net.au/index.php?page=barristers|title=Barristers|publisher=Banco Chambers[/tpl] The members of the floor who are senior counsel are: Tom Blackburn SC, Peter Brereton SC, Andrew Coleman SC, Robert Dick SC, Richard McHugh SC, Cameron Moore SC and Robert Newlinds SC.
Many of the floor's junior barristers have extensive experience practising litigation in Australia and overseas prior to joining the bar.

==Locations==

Banco Chambers has two floors.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.banco.net.au/index.php?page=contact|title=Contact Us|publisher=Banco Chambers[/tpl] One is in the Reserve Bank Building on Martin Place. The other is in Chifley Tower on Chifley Square.

==Practice area==

Although Banco Chambers has core practice areas, its barristers area able to practise in any area of civil law.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.banco.net.au/index.php?page=core-practice-areas|title=Core Practice Areas|publisher=Banco Chambers[/tpl]
