[[WRGN]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Pennsylvania, Christian radio stations in the United States

WRGN (88.1 FM) is a Christian radio station licensed to Sweet Valley, Pennsylvania, serving Northeastern Pennsylvania.  The station is owned by Gospel Media Institute, Inc.
WRGN fcc.gov. Accessed August 22, 2012
WRGN's programming includes Christian Talk and Teaching and Christian music. Christian Talk and Teaching shows heard on WRGN include; Breakpoint with Chuck Colson, Joni and Friends,  Turning Point with David Jeremiah, Focus On The Family, The Alternative with Tony Evans, Insight For Living with Chuck Swindoll, In The Market with Janet Parshall, and Love Worth Finding with Adrian Rogers.
