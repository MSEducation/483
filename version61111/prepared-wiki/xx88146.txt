[[Tyabb railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1889

Tyabb railway station is located on the Stony Point line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the town of Tyabb opening on 10 September 1889.Tyabb Vicsig
Until the mid-1980s the station consisted of a waiting room, basic toilets and a ticket office. As part of a government rationalisation of public transport, these facilities were no longer deemed necessary and the buildings were demolished and replaced with the current shelters.

==Platforms & services==

Tyabb has one platform. It is serviced by Metro Trains' Stony Point line services.Stony Point Line timetable Public Transport Victoria
