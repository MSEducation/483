[[1989 UCI Road World Championships – Women's team time trial]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 UCI Road World Championships, UCI Road World Championships – Women's team time trial

The women's team time trial of the 1989 UCI Road World Championships cycling event took place on 23 August 1989 in Chambéry, France. The course was 50.9 km long and went from Chambéry to Le Touvet and back to Chambéry.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.memoire-du-cyclisme.eu/feminines/mondial_clmpe_1989.php|work=memoire-du-cyclisme.eu|accessdate=10 August 2013|title=Championnat du Monde féminin CLM par équipes 1989[/tpl] [tpl]fr icon[/tpl]
