[[Farm to Market Road 1337]]

CATEGORIES: Farm to Market Roads in Texas, Transportation in Cochran County, Texas

Farm to Market Road 1337 (FM 1337) is a [tpl]convert|4.642|mi|km|abbr=on[/tpl] farm-to-market road in Cochran County, Texas.

==Route description==

The southern terminus of FM 1337 is at an intersection with SH 114, northwest of Whiteface.[tpl]Texas Mapbook|164|accessdate=2011-01-06[/tpl] The northern terminus is at an intersection with [tpl]roadlink|state=TX|FM|1780[/tpl] east of Morton.[tpl]Texas Mapbook|163|accessdate=2011-01-06[/tpl]

==History==

The currently designated FM 1337 was established in 1979. A previous route with that designation existed in Comal County from 1949 to 1973; it is now a part of FM 2252.
