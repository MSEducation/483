[[Preston railway station, Melbourne]]

CATEGORIES: Premium Melbourne railway stations, Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1889

Preston railway station is located on the South Morang line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the north-eastern Melbourne suburb of Preston opening as Preston-Murray Road on 8 October 1889 being renamed Murray on 1 August 1905 and Preston on 1 December 1909.Preston Vicsig It was upgraded to a Premium station in 1998.[tpl]cite journal|date=October 1997|title=Upgrading Eltham to a Premium Station|journal=Newsrail|publisher=Australian Railway Historical Society|pages=page 310[/tpl]
