[[Toyooka, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Toyooka|豊丘村|Toyooka-mura[/tpl] is a village located in Shimoina District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the village has an estimated population of 7,108 and a density of 92.49 persons per km². The total area is 76.85 km².
