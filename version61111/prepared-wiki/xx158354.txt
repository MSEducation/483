[[Alena Sharp]]

CATEGORIES: Canadian female golfers, LPGA Tour golfers, Golfing people from Ontario, New Mexico State University alumni, People from Hamilton, Ontario, 1981 births, Living people

Alena Sharp (born March 7, 1981 in Hamilton, Ontario, Canada) is a professional golfer from Canada, currently playing on the U.S.-based LPGA Tour. A graduate of New Mexico State University, where she played on the golf team, Sharp turned professional in 2003, playing two seasons on the Futures Tour and on other minor tours before joining the LPGA Tour in 2005.
