[[Nizhneyansk]]

CATEGORIES: Settlements of urban type in the Sakha Republic, Populated places of Arctic Russia

Nizhneyansk ([tpl]lang-ru|Нижнеянск[/tpl]) is an urban locality (a settlement) in Ust-Yansky District of the Sakha Republic, Russia, located in the delta of the Yana River, [tpl]convert|602|km|sp=us[/tpl] north from Deputatsky, the administrative center of the district.Registry of the Administrative-Territorial Divisions of the Sakha Republic Population: [tpl]ru-census|p2010=391|p2002=701|p1989=2,502[/tpl]

==History==

It was founded in the 1940s and was granted urban-type settlement status in 1958.
