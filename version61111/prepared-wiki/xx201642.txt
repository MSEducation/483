[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Caçador]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1968, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Florianópolis, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Caçador ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Captatoropolitanus[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Caçador in the Ecclesiastical province of Florianópolis in Brazil.
