Aleksandr Mikhailovich Familtsev ([tpl]lang-ru|Александр Михайлович Фамильцев[/tpl]; born 3 August 1975 in Pavlodar) is a Kazakhstani football coach and a former player. He also has Russian citizenship. Currently, he is a manager of FC Tom Tomsk Reserves.
Familtsev made 34 appearances for the Kazakhstan national football team, scoring one goal.[tpl]cite web|publisher=RSSSF|title=Kazakhstan - Record International Players|url=http://www.rsssf.com/miscellaneous/kaz-recintlp.html|accessdate=2008-10-10| archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20081023172708/http://www.rsssf.com/miscellaneous/kaz-recintlp.html| archivedate= 23 October 2008 | deadurl= no[/tpl]
