[[CFVT-FM]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Vancouver, Tourist information radio stations in Canada

CFVT-FM, branded as Vancouver Tourist Radio, is a low powered tourist information radio station in the Metro Vancouver region of British Columbia. The station broadcasts on 89.3 FM in North Vancouver with an effective radiated power of 38 watts.
CFVT's content is produced by the Broadcast Performing Arts students of Columbia Academy, a private post-secondary broadcasting, recording, and video production and film production career college located in Vancouver, British Columbia.
