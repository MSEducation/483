[[Qalicheh Bolagh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Qalicheh Bolagh ([tpl]lang-fa|قاليچه بلاغ[/tpl], also Romanized as Qālīcheh Bolāgh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795798|Qalicheh Bolagh[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 32, in seven families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
