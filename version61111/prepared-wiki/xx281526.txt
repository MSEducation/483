[[Tabernaemontana rostrata]]

CATEGORIES: Tabernaemontana, Plants described in 1829, Flora of India, Flora of Thailand, Flora of Malaysia, Flora of Vietnam, Flora of Indonesia, Flora of the Philippines

Tabernaemontana rostrata grows as a shrub up to [tpl]Convert|2|m[/tpl] tall. Its flowers feature a white corolla. Habitat is scrub or forest, from sea-level to [tpl]Convert|1400|m[/tpl] altitude. T. rostrata is found in India, Thailand, Malaysia, Vietnam, Indonesia and the Philippines.
