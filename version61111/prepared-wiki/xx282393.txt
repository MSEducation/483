[[DBL All-Star Team]]

CATEGORIES: Dutch Basketball League awards

The Dutch Basketball League All-Star Team or All-DBL Team is a team constituted by the main players in a season of the Dutch Basketball League, the highest professional basketball league in the Netherlands. This team is based on players' performance throughout the regular season. After the end of the season, the best players of each position are chosen.[tpl]cite web | url = http://j-dus.com/all-star-team/ | title = All-Star Team''' | publisher = j-dus.com (History of Dutch Basketball | accessdate= 07-03-2014 [/tpl] It is not to be confused with the DBL All-Star Gala, an event where the best players in the league come together.
