[[Jacque Robinson]]

CATEGORIES: 1963 births, Living people, Sportspeople from San Jose, California, Buffalo Bills players, Washington Huskies football players, American football running backs

Jacque Robinson (born March 5, 1963) was a drafted running back by the Buffalo Bills.  During his college career at Washington, Robinson amassed 2,300 career yards. He also earned MVP honors in both the 1982 Rose Bowl and 1985 Orange Bowl.http://graphics.fansonly.com/photos/schools/wash/sports/m-footbl/auto_pdf/FB_Record_Book.pdf Robinson was the first freshman to win Player of the Game honor at the Rose Bowl.
