[[Harqin Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Harqin Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠠᠷᠠᠴᠢᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Qaračin qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|c=喀喇沁旗 |p=Kèlǎqìn Qí[/tpl]) is a banner of southeastern Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China. It is under the administration of Chifeng City, [tpl]convert|40|km[/tpl] to the north-northeast.
