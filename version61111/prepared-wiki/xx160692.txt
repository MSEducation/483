[[HD 170469]]

CATEGORIES: G-type subgiants, Henry Draper Catalogue objects, Hipparcos objects, Ophiuchus (constellation), Planetary systems

HD 170469 is a yellow subgiant star located approximately 212 light-years away in the constellation Ophiuchus. It has apparent magnitude +8.21 and it is 211.91 ly away.
In 2007, a planet was discovered by the N2K Consortium, led by principal investigators Debra Fischer and Gregory P. Laughlin.
