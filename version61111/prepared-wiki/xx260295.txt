[[Sanco, Texas]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Texas, Unincorporated communities in Coke County, Texas, Ghost towns in West Texas

Sanco, Texas is a near-abandoned unincorporated community in Coke County, Texas, sixteen miles northwest of Robert Lee east of Highway 208 on an unnamed county road.Sanco at TexasEscapes In 1990, the population was a mere thirty people.
