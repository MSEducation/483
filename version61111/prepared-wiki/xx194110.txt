[[KLEY (AM)]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Kansas, Radio stations established in 1983

KLEY (1130 AM) is a radio station  broadcasting a news/talk format to the Wellington, Kansas, USA area.  The station is owned by Johnson Enterprises, Inc.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=KLEY |title=KLEY Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==History==

KLEY was a family-owned station for years, run by Ed and Zola Belle Hundley.  In the 1980s it featured a variety of programming during a sunrise-to-sunset schedule, including a daily half-hour of polka music at 1:30 p.m.
