[[Joe Martin (American football)]]

CATEGORIES: 1983 births, Living people, People from Los Angeles County, California, Players of American football from California, American football linebackers, San Diego State Aztecs football players

Joe Martin (born October 25, 1983 in Palmdale, California) is an American football linebacker who is currently a free agent. He played college football at San Diego State University

==College career==

Martin was a starting outside linebacker both his junior and senior year at San Diego State. During his junior season he had 81 tackles. As a senior he recorded 109 tackles.

==Pro career==

Joe Martin signed with the Baltimore Ravens as a rookie free agent on May 4, 2007[tpl]cite web | title=Ravens Sign 16 Rookie Free Agents | url=http://www.baltimoreravens.com/News/Articles/2007/05/Ravens_Sign_16_Rookie_Free_Agents.aspx | work= | publisher=BaltimoreRavens.com | date=May 4, 2007 | accessdate=2009-05-21[/tpl] and was released on December 2, 2007.
He was drafted to UFL San Francisco in the UFL's inaugural draft.
