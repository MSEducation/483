[[Operation Tinderbox]]

CATEGORIES: Nevada Test Site nuclear explosive tests

Operation Tinderbox was a series of 14 nuclear tests conducted by the United States in 1979-1980 at the Nevada Test Site. These tests followed the Operation Quicksilver (1978) series and preceded the Operation Guardian series.
