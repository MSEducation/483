[[HMS C15]]

CATEGORIES: Barrow-built ships, British C-class submarines, Royal Navy ship names, 1908 ships

HMS ''C15'' was a British C class submarine built by Vickers, Barrow. She was laid down on 7 December 1906 and was commissioned on 1 April 1908.
C15 torpedoed [tpl]ship|SM|UC-65||2[/tpl] in the English Channel on 3 November 1917.
She was sold on 28 February 1922.
