[[Oskar Wahlström]]

CATEGORIES: 1976 births, Djurgårdens IF Fotboll players, Swedish footballers, Living people, Association football goalkeepers

Oskar Wahlström (born August 16, 1976) is a former Swedish football goalkeeper. He most recently played  for Djurgårdens IF after retiring after the 2009 season.[tpl]cite web|url=http://dif.se/2009/12/10/oskar-wahlstrom-slutar/|title=Oskar Wahlström slutar|last=Riedel|first=Jonas|date=10 December 2009|publisher=Djurgårdens IF Fotboll|language=Swedish|accessdate=16 January 2012[/tpl] Wahlström joined Djurgården in 2004, leaving Västerås SK. He made his Allsvenskan debut against Landskrona BoIS on May 2, 2004 as a substitute. Wahlström has also represented Katrineholms SK and Bie GoIF.
