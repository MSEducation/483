[[Cloud About Mercury]]

CATEGORIES: ECM Records albums, David Torn albums, Albums produced by Manfred Eicher, 1987 albums

Cloud About Mercury is the second album by guitarist David Torn.  It was recorded in 1986 and released on the ECM label.ECM discography accessed September 26, 2011  

==Reception==

The Allmusic review by Glenn Astarita awarded the album 5 stars stating "Simply put, Cloud About Mercury looms as one of the finest jazz fusion dates of the '80s, and should be deemed a mandatory purchase for aficionados of this genre".Astaria, G. Allmusic Review accessed September 26, 2011
