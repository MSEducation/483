[[Ronnie Coleman (American football)]]

CATEGORIES: 1951 births, Living people, People from Jasper, Alabama, American football running backs, Alabama A&M Bulldogs football players, Undrafted National Football League players, Houston Oilers players

Ronnie L. Coleman, born July 9, 1951 in Jasper, Alabama is a graduate of Alabama A&M University, played professional American football for the Houston Oilers from 1974 to 1981 in the National Football League.
He currently is a teacher at Spring Oaks Middle School in Houston, Texas.Spring Oaks Middle School Home of the Panthers Excellence Through Determination
