[[Kura (restaurant)]]

CATEGORIES: Restaurant chains in Japan

Kura is a sushi restaurant chain located in Japan.  It has 262 locations.  While it is a conveyor belt sushi chain, it relies on a high level of automation allowing the average location to function with fifteen to twenty staff members.[tpl]cite news | first = Tabuchi | last = Hiroko | title = For Sushi Chain, Conveyor Belts Carry Profit | date = 2010-12-30 | publisher = New York Times | url = http://www.nytimes.com/2010/12/31/business/global/31sushi.html | work = The New York Times | accessdate = 2010-12-31[/tpl]
