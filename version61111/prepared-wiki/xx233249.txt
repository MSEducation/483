[tpl]nihongo|Ikki Sawamura|沢村 一樹|Sawamura Ikki|born July 10, 1967[/tpl] is a Japanese model, film and television actor, and television presenter signed to Ken-On.

==Biography==

Ikki Sawamura was born in Kagoshima in 1967. After graduating from the local high school, he moved to Tokyo and started his career as a fashion model. In 1996, Sawamura debuted in a TV drama Matsuda no Drama as an actor.  Since then, he has appeared in many television dramas and commercials. Sawamura married a Japanese ex-model in 2000, and has two sons.[tpl]cite web|title = Kaiteki Kūkan Rakuen ni Sumau|publisher = Nikkei Trendy Net L-cruise|url = http://trendy.nikkeibp.co.jp/lc/rakuen/rakuen04/|language = Japanese|accessdate=October 8, 2008[/tpl]

====Asami Mitsuhiko series====

[tpl]Nihongo|Asami Mitsuhiko series|浅見光彦シリーズ[/tpl] is a mystery novel series created by Japanese author Yasuo Uchida. Many of the novels have been made into 2 hour one-episode drama by several different networks with the different casts. Sawamura played the protagonist Mitsuhiko Asami from part 14 in the series originally aired on TBS.
