[[Dash Tappeh, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Dash Tappeh ([tpl]lang-fa|داش تپه[/tpl], also Romanized as Dāsh Tappeh; also known as Dash-Tepe)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3059979|Dash Tappeh[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 263, in 56 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
