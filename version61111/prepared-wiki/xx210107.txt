[[Hari Bahadur Basnet]]

CATEGORIES: Government ministers of Nepal, Living people, Rastriya Prajatantra Party politicians, Members of the Nepal House of Representatives

Hari Bahadur Basnet is a Nepalese politician. He is the head of the Foreign Relations Department of the Rastriya Janashakti Party.http://www.kantipuronline.com/kolnews.php?&nid=154893 [tpl]dl|date=June 2010[/tpl] Basnet holds a M.Sc. in Engineering.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ndi.org/worldwide/asia/combatcorruption/participantbios.pdf|title=BIOGRAPHIES OF PARTICIPANTS|accessdate=8 June 2010|archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20040511100814/http://www.ndi.org/worldwide/asia/combatcorruption/participantbios.pdf|archivedate=May 11, 2004[/tpl]
Basnet entered politics in 1970. Three times he was elected to the Rastriya Panchayat. He joined the Rastriya Prajatantra Party when it was formed, and became a Central Committee member of the party. He also headed the International Relations department of RPP.
In 2003, he was appointed Minister of Law, Justice and Parliamentary Affairs, Education and Sports, Industry, Commerce, and Supplies in the cabinet of Surya Bahadur Thapa.Nepal (01/04), U.S. Department of Statehttp://www.bpw-international.org/word/library-international-newsletter-no3-2004-06-eng.doc [tpl]dl|date=June 2010[/tpl]
