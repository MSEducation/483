[[Provincial Archives of Newfoundland and Labrador]]

CATEGORIES: Newfoundland and Labrador government departments and agencies, Archives in Canada, Official document archives

Provincial Archives of Newfoundland and Labrador is the official government archive of the Canadian province of Newfoundland and Labrador. It is located at The Rooms in St. John's, Newfoundland and Labrador, Canada.
