[[Lenore Kasdorf]]

CATEGORIES: 1948 births, American soap opera actresses, American television actresses, Living people, People from Queens, New York, Actresses from New York City

Lenore Kasdorf (born July 23, 1948) is an American actress.

==Biography==

Kasdorf was born in New York City to an army colonel father.http://www.filmreference.com/film/18/Lenore-Kasdorf.html She is best known for her role as the alluring and promiscuous nurse Rita Stapleton Bauer, whom she played from 1975 to 1981 in the soap opera Guiding Light, and for her performance as Mrs Rico in Starship Troopers. 
She has since appeared on the soaps Santa Barbara and Days of our Lives, as well as a number of films (her most prominent film role costarring with Chuck Norris in 1984's Missing in Action). She also had a recurring role on the 1980s sitcom Coach. Her television credits also include guest-starring roles on The A-Team, Knight Rider, Murder She Wrote, Barnaby Jones, 21 Jump Street, The Six Million Dollar Man, NYPD Blue, Beverly Hills, 90210, Magnum P.I, Star Trek: The Next Generation, Streets of San Francisco, and Babylon 5.
