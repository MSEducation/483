[[Torch This Place]]

CATEGORIES: 1999 albums, The Atomic Fireballs albums, Albums produced by Bruce Fairbairn, Lava Records albums

Torch This Place is an album made in 1999 by The Atomic Fireballs. It is the band's only full-length studio release.
