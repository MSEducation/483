[[(308193) 2005 CB79]]

CATEGORIES: Haumea family, Classical Kuiper belt objects

[tpl]mp|(308193) 2005 CB|79[/tpl] is a trans-Neptunian object that is a member of the Haumea family.
As a member of the Haumea family, [tpl]mp|(308193) 2005 CB|79[/tpl] is suspected of being an icy mantle collisional fragment from dwarf planet Haumea.  With an absolute magnitude (H) of 4.7, and a Haumea-family albedo of 0.7, this object would have a diameter of 158Â km.
