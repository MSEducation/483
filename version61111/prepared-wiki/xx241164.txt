[[Bairin Left Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Baarin Left Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠪᠠᠭᠠᠷᠢᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠵᠡᠭᠦᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Baɣarin Jegün qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|c=巴林左旗 |p=Bālín Zuǒ Qí[/tpl]), or Bairin, is a banner of eastern Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China. It is under the administration of Chifeng City, [tpl]convert|195|km[/tpl] to the south-southwest.
Baarin Mongols live here.The distinct Mongolian dialect of this region is called Baarin. It is located at the intersection of two national highways: China National Highway 303 and 305. It was the Capital of Chinese Liao Dynasty, when the city is called Shangjing ([tpl]zh|c=上京[/tpl]) at that time.

==Climate==

Baarin Left Banner has a dry, monsoon-influenced humid continental climate (Köppen Dwa), falling just short of being semi-arid (Köppen BSk), with very cold and dry winters, hot, somewhat humid summers, and strong winds, especially in spring. The monthly 24-hour average temperature ranges from [tpl]convert|−13.5|°C|1[/tpl] in January to [tpl]convert|22.5|°C|1[/tpl] in July, with the annual mean at [tpl]convert|5.33|°C|1[/tpl]. The annual precipitation is approximately [tpl]convert|390|mm|in|1[/tpl], with more than half of it falling in July and August alone. Due to the aridity and elevation, diurnal temperature variation often exceeds [tpl]convert|15|C-change[/tpl] in spring, averaging [tpl]convert|14.2|C-change|1[/tpl] annually. There are 2,934 hours of bright sunshine annually, with each of the winter months having over 70% of the possible total, and this percentage falling to 52 in July.
