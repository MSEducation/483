[[The Law of the Range]]

CATEGORIES: 1928 films, American silent feature films, American films, English-language films, 1920s Western (genre) films, American Western (genre) films, Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer films

The Law of the Range is a 1928 American silent Western film starring Tim McCoy and Joan Crawford and Rex Lease.The Law of the Range at silentera.com

==Cast==

==Plot==

Betty Dallas (Crawford)is a passenger on a stagecoach that is held up by an outlaw named, The Solitaire Kid (Lease).  Ranger Jim Lockhart, (McCoy),
who is Betty's sweetheart, is in pursuit of The Solitaire Kid, and in the end, as the two men face one another, there is a mortal shoot-out.
