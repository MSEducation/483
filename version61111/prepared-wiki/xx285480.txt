[[Oreoluwa Cherebin]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1997 births, Grenadian swimmers

Oreoluwa Cherebin (born 24 December 1997) is a Grenadian swimmer.http://www.bcn2013.com/en/swimming/detail/member/oreoluwa-cherebin She competed in the 50 m and 100 m butterfly, 50 m and 100 m breststroke events at the 2012 FINA World Swimming Championships (25 m). Cherebin also competed in the 50 m breaststroke and 50 m butterfly events at the 2013 World Aquatics Championships.
