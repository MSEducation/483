[[(18514) 1996 TE11]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1996, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Mars-crossing asteroids

(18514) 1996 TE11 is a Mars-crossing minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on October 14, 1996.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
