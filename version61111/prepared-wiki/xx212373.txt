[[Raúl Antonio Coreas]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 births, Living people, People from San Miguel, El Salvador, Salvadoran footballers, C.D. Vista Hermosa footballers

Raúl Antonio Coreas Pérez (born February 7, 1989 in San Miguel, El Salvador) is a Salvadoran footballer who currently plays for Dragón in the Salvadoran  Second Division.
He is the younger brother of El Salvador national team member Salvador Coreas.

==Club career==

Coreas' professional career began in July 2007, when he signed a contract with C.D. Vista Hermosa. He officially made his debut on November 7, 2007, in a league match against San Salvador F.C..
