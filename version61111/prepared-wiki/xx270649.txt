[[Nicoline Artursson]]

Nicoline Artursson born in 1993 is a Swedish fashion model and Miss World Sweden titleholder.[tpl]cite web|author=Foto: Jashar Ghavampour |url=http://kvp.expressen.se/nyheter/1.2551902/soderns-brudar-snyggast-igen |title=Söderns brudar snyggast igen - KVP.se - Senaste nytt | Expressen - Nyheter Sport Ekonomi Nöje |publisher=Kvp.expressen.se |date=2011-09-06 |accessdate=2011-10-15[/tpl][tpl]cite web|author=Lennart Weman lennart.weman@hallandsposten.se |url=http://hallandsposten.se/folkfamilj/1.1302576-modelljobbet-blev-det-stora-lyftet-for-nicoline |title=Modelljobbet blev det stora lyftet för Nicoline - Folk & familj |language=[tpl]sv icon[/tpl] |publisher=www.hallandsposten.se |date= |accessdate=2011-10-15[/tpl] Artursson represented Sweden in Miss World 2011 on November 6 in London. Nicoline Artursson was born in the village of Villshärad outside Halmstad. Artursson speaks French after living in Paris, France for a year modelling for the clothing company Abercrombie & Fitch located on Champs-Élysées. Artursson was also offered to be the Bachelorette in the French version of the television reality series The Bachelorette, but declined as she had to finish her studies back in Sweden.
