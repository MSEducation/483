[[Lalawmpuia Pachuau]]

CATEGORIES: Indian footballers, 1989 births, Living people, I-League players, Footballers from Mizoram

Lalawmpuia Pachuau is an Indian football who is currently playing for South United in the I-League 2nd Division as a Forward. Lalawmpuia Pachuau is a product of the famous Tata Football Academy in Jamshedpur, India.

==Career==

===Mumbai===

After spending years at Tata Football Academy Puia signed a professional contract with Mumbai F.C. of the I-League.
Before joining Mumbai F.C. Lalawmpuia played for Mohun Bagan A.C.He is commonly known as Tuaitea among his fellow Mizo people.

===South United===

Lalawmpuia signed for South United for 2013 I-League 2nd Division season.
