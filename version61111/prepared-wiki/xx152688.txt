[[Sahara Bank]]

CATEGORIES: Banks of Libya, Banks established in 1964, BNP Paribas

The Sahara Bank ([tpl]lang-ar|مصرف الصحاري[/tpl]) was established in 1964,designed by Italian Vincenzo Maria Morici. 
On 22 December 1970, the Revolutionary Command issued another law "the Nationalization Decision" which demanded that all the foreign banks shares were nationalized and became completely owned by Libya.
Sahara Bank is now a part of the French-based BNP Paribas group.
