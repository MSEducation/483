Cine Club is a movie theater in Universidad Austral, Valdivia, Chile. The theater is located in Universidad Austral de Chile's Isla Teja campus. Cine Club was founded in 1982 and organizes the yearly Valdivia International Film Festival since 1992, when it was created on its 10th anniversary.
