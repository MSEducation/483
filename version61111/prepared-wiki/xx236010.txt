[[Linnéas fina visor]]

CATEGORIES: Cornelis Vreeswijk albums, 1973 albums, Swedish folk music

Linnéas fina visor (English: Linnea's fine songs) is studio album by the Swedish-Dutch folk singer-songwriter Cornelis Vreeswijk.Album info on www.cornelis.se
