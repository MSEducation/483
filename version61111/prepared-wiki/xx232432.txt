[[Gérard Bruchési]]

CATEGORIES: 1931 births, Members of the House of Commons of Canada from Quebec, People from Montreal, Progressive Conservative Party of Canada candidates in the 1962 Canadian federal election, Progressive Conservative Party of Canada MPs, Living people

Gérard Bruchési (born March 30, 1931 in Montreal, Quebec, Canada) is a former Canadian politician and insurance broker. He was elected to the Canadian House of Commons as a Member of the Progressive Conservative Party in the 1958 election to represent the riding of Beauharnois—Salaberry. He was defeated in the 1962 election.
