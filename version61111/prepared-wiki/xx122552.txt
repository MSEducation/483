[[Baron Ilkeston]]

CATEGORIES: Baronies in the Peerage of the United Kingdom, Extinct baronies in the Peerage of the United Kingdom

Baron Ilkeston, of Ilkeston in the County of Derby,[tpl]London Gazette |issue=28398 |date=22 July 1910 |startpage=5269[/tpl] was a title in the Peerage of the United Kingdom. It was created on 14 July 1910 for the physician and politician Sir Balthazar Foster. The title became extinct on the death of his youngest but only surviving son, the second Baron, on 4 January 1952.
