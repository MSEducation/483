[[Somerset County Gazette]]

CATEGORIES: Newspapers published in Somerset

The Somerset County Gazette is a weekly tabloid newspaper in Somerset, England.
It is published in a tabloid format on a weekly basis and has a circulation of circa 21,000.
It was founded in 1836,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.britishpapers.co.uk/england-sw/somerset-county-gazette/|title=Somerset County Gazette|publisher=British Newspapers Online|accessdate=10 January 2010[/tpl] and is now owned by Newsquest.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.londonfreelance.org/rates/titles/_nq.html|title=The Media Owners|publisher=londonfreelance.org|accessdate=10 January 2010[/tpl]
