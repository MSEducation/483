Babajide "Baba" Collins Babatunde (born 2 December 1988) is a Nigerian footballer striker who currently plays for Kazakhstan Premier League side FC Ordabasy.

==Career==

He began his career by FC Ebedei played there between 2006, joined then to Ikast FS. Collins played there in there reserve and was 2007 scouted from FC Midtjylland, on 15 January 2009 was loaned to Vejle Boldklub for 6 months also 30 June 2009.http://www.vejle-boldklub.dk/index.php?nyheder-generelle&newsId=2146
