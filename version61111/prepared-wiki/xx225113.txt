[[Sztandar Socjalizmu]]

CATEGORIES: Communist newspapers, Defunct newspapers of Poland, Publications established in 1918, Communist Party of Poland

Sztandar Socjalizmu ('Standard of Socialism') was a Polish newspaper, founded in December 1918 as the main press organ of the Communist Workers Party of Poland. The first editorial board of the paper was elected at the founding congress of the party in 1918.Dziewanowski, M. K.. The Foundation of the Communist Party of Poland, published in American Slavic and East European Review, Vol. 11, No. 2 (Apr., 1952), pp. 106-122
