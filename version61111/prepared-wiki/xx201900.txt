[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Tubarão]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1954, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Florianópolis, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Tubarão ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Tubaraoënsis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Tubarão in the Ecclesiastical province of Florianópolis in Brazil.
