[[Half Life (novel)]]

CATEGORIES: 2006 novels, American alternate history novels, Fictional conjoined twins, James Tiptree, Jr. Award winning works, Debut novels, HarperCollins books

Half Life is the 2006 debut novel of American writer and artist Shelley Jackson.  The novel presupposes an alternate history in which the atomic bomb resulted in a genetic preponderance of conjoined twins, who eventually become a minority subculture.  It tells the story of a disenchanted conjoined twin named Nora Olney who plots to have her twin murdered.
