Amadou «Diamou» Diamouténé (born November 3, 1985 in Adiopodoumé) is a Malian professional footballer. He currently plays for Djoliba AC.

==Personal==

Diamouténé was born in Adiopodoumé, Côte d'Ivoire to Malian parents. In 1999, he moved back to Mali.
