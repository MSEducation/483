[[Montenegro women's national volleyball team]]

CATEGORIES: Volleyball in Montenegro

The Montenegro women's national volleyball team is the national team of Montenegro. It is governed by the Volleyball Federation of Montenegro and takes part in international volleyball competitions.

==History==

First matches after the independence of Montenegro, women's national team played at 2008. In the April and May, Montenegro played their debut friendly match against Albania, and after that, national team played first leg of European Championship qualifications, against the national team of Georgia.
