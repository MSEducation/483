Ezequiel Scarione (born 14 July 1985 in José Clemente Paz) is an Argentine football midfielder, who currently plays for Kasimpasa in the Süper Lig. http://www.habermonitor.com/en/haber/detay/st-oscar-gallen-li-scarione-kasimpasa/138714/

==Career==

He began his career Boca Juniors and was 2002 promoted to the first team, before in 2004 transferred to Deportivo Cuenca. He left 2006 Deportivo Cuenca and moved to Switzerland based club FC Thun, he played by Thun 42 games and scored 7 goals, before on 8 January 2009 joined to FC Lucerne on loan. On July 2009 he came back to FC Thun to play in the 2009-2010 season in the Swiss Challenge League, winning the Championship and achieving immediate promotion to the Swiss Super League for the season 2010-2011.
