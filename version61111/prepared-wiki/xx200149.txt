[[Stuart Michael Thomas]]

CATEGORIES: 1970 births, Living people, American composers

==Biography==

Stuart Michael Thomas (born November 17, 1970 in Los Angeles) is an American music composer, producer and arranger for film and television. He has been a credited contributor to dozens of films including Michael Clayton, Snow White & the Huntsman, The Dark Knight and Iron Man 3 as well as TV films such as High Noon, Northern Lights, and Angels Fall.Stuart Michael ThomasAwards/Nominations He is briefly interviewed in Peter Jackson's King Kong Production Diaries.Kong is King.net | King Kong | Peter Jackson's Production Diary
