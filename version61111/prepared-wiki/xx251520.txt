[[Long Journey Home (Live)]]

CATEGORIES: Cowboy Junkies albums

Long Journey Home (Live) is an album by the Canadian rock band Cowboy Junkies, recorded in Liverpool, England in 2004 and released in 2007. The release includes a concert DVD along with a CD. Only 11 tracks of the 18 that are on the DVD appear on the CD.
