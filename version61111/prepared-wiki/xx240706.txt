[[KNAF (AM)]]

CATEGORIES: Country radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Texas, Radio stations established in 1983

KNAF (910 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a country music format.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www1.arbitron.com/sip/sip.do#access |date=Summer 2009 |title=Station Information Profile |publisher=Arbitron |accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl] Licensed to Fredericksburg, Texas, USA, the station serves the Kerrville-Fredericksburg area.  The station is currently owned by J. & J. Fritz Media, Ltd..
