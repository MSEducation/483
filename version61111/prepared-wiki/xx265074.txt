[[Bolivian legislative election, 1944]]

CATEGORIES: Elections in Bolivia, 1944 elections in South America, 1944 in Bolivia

Bolivia held a parliamentary election on 2 July 1944, electing a new Constituent Assembly, 1944–1946. 

==Congressional Elections resultsPolitical handbook of the world 1946. New York, 1946. P. 14.==

On 4 August, 1944 the Constituent Assembly confirmed President Gualberto Villarroel López, who had assumed the presidency on 20 December, 1943 as a result of a coup d'état. Political handbook of the world 1946. New York, 1946. P. 15.
