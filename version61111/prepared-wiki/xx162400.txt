[[Asperisporium caricae]]

CATEGORIES: Ascomycota incertae sedis, Plant pathogens and diseases, Fungi of North America, Fungi of South America, Fungi described in 1886

Asperisporium caricae is an ascomycete fungus that is a plant pathogen, found in North and South America. It is responsible for the black spot disease on Papaya Trees.
