[[Oscar Eklund]]

CATEGORIES: 1988 births, Living people, Djurgårdens IF Hockey players, HIFK players, Swedish ice hockey players

Oscar Eklund (born August 27, 1988) is a Swedish ice hockey player.[tpl]cite web | title=Oscar Eklund| publisher=Eurohockey.net | url=http://www.eurohockey.net/players/show_player.cgi?serial=85942| accessdate=April 28, 2008[/tpl] He is currently playing with HIFK in the Finnish Liiga. 
Eklund had previously played with the Oulun KärpätRuotsalaista puolustajataitoa joukkueeseen in the SM-Liiga.
