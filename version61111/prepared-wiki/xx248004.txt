[[Top Shelf Coffee]]

CATEGORIES: Companies established in 1994, Coffee companies, Warren, Ohio, Companies based in Ohio, Coffee brands

Top Shelf Coffee,Inc. is a US privately owned and operated coffee company. Top Shelf Coffee was founded in 1994 as Top Shelf Coffee and Foods. The company was a medium scale business, operating mostly within Ohio; serving local factories, restaurants and coffee shops both small and large.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.topshelfcoffee.com |title=Top Shelf Coffee |publisher=Top Shelf Coffee |date= |accessdate=2011-06-28[/tpl] The company's main supplier of coffee beans is Cafe Lobo.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cafelobo.com/ |title=Green Coffee from |publisher=Cafe Lobo |date= |accessdate=2011-06-28[/tpl]
