[[Public School No. 13]]

CATEGORIES: School buildings on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Neoclassical architecture in New York, School buildings completed in 1910, Buildings and structures in Yonkers, New York

Public School No. 13 is a historic school building located at Yonkers, Westchester County, New York. It was built in 1900, with additions in 1905 and 1910.  It is a 3Â½-story, asymmetrical, steel- and wood-frame building in a Neoclassical style. It has stone, brick, and structural clay tile walls.
