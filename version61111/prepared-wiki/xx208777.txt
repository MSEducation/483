[[Temein language]]

CATEGORIES: Eastern Sudanic languages

Temein, also known as Ron(g)e, is an Eastern Sudanic language spoken by the Temein people of the Nuba Hills in Sudan.
Ronge is an approximation of the endonym. Stevenson reports the people are [tpl]IPA|ɔ̀rɔ́ŋɡɔ̀ʔ[/tpl] and the language [tpl]IPA|lɔ́ŋɔ na rɔŋɛ[/tpl]; Dimmendaal has [tpl]IPA|ɔ́ràntɛ̀t[/tpl] for a person, [tpl]IPA|kààkɪ́nɪ́ ɔ́rɔ̀ŋɛ̀[/tpl] for the people, and [tpl]IPA|ŋɔ́nɔ́t ná ɔ́rɔ̀ŋɛ[/tpl] for the language.
