[[Adam Kraus]]

Adam Michael Kraus (born September 10, 1984) is a former American football guard.

==Collegiate career==

At Michigan, Kraus was a four-year letterman who appeared in 39 career games, making 35 starts along the offensive line. Adam started 26 straight games to finish his career, 21 at left guard and the final five contests at center. He is a two-time All-Big Ten first team selection in 2006 and 2007.Adam Kraus Bio

==Professional career==

Adam was signed by the Baltimore Ravens as a rookie free agent on May 9, 2008 and released on August 30, 2008.Ravens sign rookie free agent from Michigan
