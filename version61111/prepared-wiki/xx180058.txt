[[Winton, Cumbria]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Cumbria, Civil parishes in Cumbria

Winton is a village and civil parish in the Eden District of Cumbria, England. It is [tpl]convert|2.9|mi|km[/tpl] south of Brough, and [tpl]convert|1.6|mi|km[/tpl] north of Kirkby Stephen, and has a population of 213.Office for National Statistics : Census 2001 : Parish Headcounts : Eden Retrieved 26 October 2010
