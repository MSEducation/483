[[Barszczowa Góra]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Augustów County

Barszczowa Góra [tpl]IPAc-pl|b|a|r|sz|'|cz|o|w|a|-|'|g|u|r|a[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Nowinka, within Augustów County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|4|km|mi|0[/tpl] north of Nowinka, [tpl]convert|13|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of Augustów, and [tpl]convert|96|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of the regional capital Białystok.
The village has a population of 70.
