[[Fool for Your Love]]

CATEGORIES: 1983 singles, Mickey Gilley songs, Song recordings produced by Jim Ed Norman, Billboard Hot Country Songs number-one singles, RPM Country Tracks number-one singles, Epic Records singles

"Fool For Your Love" is a song written by Don Singleton, and recorded by American country music artist Mickey Gilley.  It was released in March 1983 as the first single and title track from the album Fool for Your Love.  The song was Mickey Gilley's sixteenth number one country single as a solo artist.  The single went to number one for one week and spent a total of twelve weeks on the country chart.[tpl]cite book |title= The Billboard Book Of Top 40 Country Hits: 1944-2006, Second edition|last=Whitburn |first=Joel |authorlink=Joel Whitburn |year=2004 |publisher=Record Research |page=137[/tpl]
