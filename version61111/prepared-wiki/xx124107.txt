[[1994 US Open – Men's Singles]]

CATEGORIES: 1994 US Open (tennis), US Open (tennis) by year

Andre Agassi defeated Michael Stich 6–1, 7–6(7–5), 7–5 in the final to win the Men's Singles tennis title at the 1994 US Open.This is wherer Tennis legend Ivan Lendl played his final Grand Slam.
