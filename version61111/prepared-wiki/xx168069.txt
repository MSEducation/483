[[Brilliantine]]

CATEGORIES: Hairdressing, 1900 introductions

Brilliantine is a hair-grooming product intended to soften men's hair, including beards and moustaches, and give it a glossy, well-groomed appearance.  It was created at the turn of the 20th century by French perfumer Edouard Pinaud (a.k.a. Ed. Pinaud).Musees de Grasse. In English-speaking markets Pinaud's name is associated with the Clubman tradename in men's toiletries.  He presented a product he called Brillantine (from the French [tpl]lang|fr|brillant[/tpl] meaning "brilliant") at the 1900 Exposition Universelle in Paris.  It consisted of a perfumed and colored oily liquid.Bumble and bumble Brilliance. Sephora.com. Accessed May 6, 2012.
