[[Lingyuan]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Liaoning, Chaoyang, Liaoning

Lingyuan ([tpl]zh|c=凌源 |p=Língyuán[/tpl]) is a city in the west of Liaoning province in Northeast China, bordering Hebei province and Inner Mongolia. It is under the administration of Chaoyang City, which lies [tpl]convert|94|km[/tpl] to the east-northeast.

==Administrative Divisions朝阳市-行政区划网==

There are 8 subdistricts, 11 towns, 10 townships, and 1 ethnic township under the administration of the city.
