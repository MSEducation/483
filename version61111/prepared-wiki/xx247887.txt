[[411 East Wisconsin Center]]

CATEGORIES: Skyscrapers in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, Office buildings completed in 1985, Skyscrapers between 100 and 149 meters

The 411 East Wisconsin Center is a high-rise located in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. It was built in 1985 on the former site of the Goldsmith Building. It is the fifth tallest building in Milwaukee, and it was the second tallest building in Milwaukee at the time of its completion, surpassed by the Milwaukee Center in 1988.
The building has been home to the Quarles & Brady law firm since 1986 and the von Briesen & Roper law firm since 1985.
