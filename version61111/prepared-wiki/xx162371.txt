[[Kampong Sungai Mau]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Brunei

Kampong Sungai Mau is a village located in the mukim of Bukit Sawat in the Belait district. Sungai Mau Recreational Park is located in this area near the Sungai Liang - Labi road. The village has a mosque named the Al-Mashhor Mosque, a multi-purpose hall and a health clinic.
