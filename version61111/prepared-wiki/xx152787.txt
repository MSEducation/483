Mlungisi Raphael "Professor" Ngubane (born 20 February 1956 in Durban, KwaZulu-Natal) is a former South African football (soccer) player and coach currently managing National First Division club Thanda Royal Zulu.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.goal.com/en/news/1659/south-africa/2009/04/27/1232073/thanda-royal-zulu-coach-mlungisi-ngubane-slams-false-reports|title=Thanda Royal Zulu Coach Mlungisi Ngubane Slams False Reports|date=27 April 2009|publisher=Goal.com[/tpl] He previously coached Durban Bush Bucks, Maritzburg United, Bush Bucks, Black Leopards and Namibia among others.[tpl]Citation needed|date=September 2011[/tpl]
