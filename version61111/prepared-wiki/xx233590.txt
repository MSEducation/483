[[United States Senate election in Nebraska, 1988]]

CATEGORIES: Nebraska elections, 1988, United States Senate elections, 1988, United States Senate elections in Nebraska

The 1988 United States Senate election in Nebraska was held on November 3, 1988 to select the U.S. Senator from the state. Republican U.S. Senator David Karnes decided to seek re-election for his first complete term after being appointed to the seat of the late Edward Zorinsky in March 1987, but was soundly defeated by former governor Bob Kerrey in the November general election. http://www.ourcampaigns.com/RaceDetail.html?RaceID=3436
