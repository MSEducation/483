[[European Female]]

CATEGORIES: 1983 singles, The Stranglers songs

"European Female" is a single by the British group The Stranglers, released in 1982. It is taken from the album Feline where it features under its full title "The European Female (In Celebration Of)". The track features bassist Jean-Jacques Burnel on lead vocals.
The single was the Stranglers first for Epic Records.
