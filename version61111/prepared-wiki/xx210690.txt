[[Bure Equity]]

CATEGORIES: Companies of Sweden

Bure Equity is an investment company involved in communications, textiles, training, and education.Corporate Information Snapshot It is largest private school operator in Sweden. This role gained it international attention in connection to the rise of independent schools in Sweden. Bure Equity owns Vittra, a company that runs schools in Sweden and other countries."Embracing private schools", The Washington Times, August 11, 2008.
On the 11th of February 2009 Altor Fund III and Bure Equity AB signed an agreement to acquire Carnegie Investment Bank AB, a leading independent Nordic investment bank, from the Swedish National Debt Office.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cisionwire.com/bure-equity/altor-and-bure-acquire-carnegie-from-the-national-debt-office |title=Bure - press release |publisher=Cision Wire |date= |accessdate=2009-02-11[/tpl]
