[[Baksan River]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of Kabardino-Balkaria

Baksan River, also known as Azau ([tpl]lang-ru|Баксан, Азау[/tpl]) is a river in Kabardino-Balkaria in Russia, a right tributary of the Malka River (Terek's basin). The length of the Baksan is 173 km, the area of its drainage basin is 6,800 km². The Baksan originates in the glaciers of the Elbrus. The towns of Tyrnyauz and Baksan are located on the Baksan River.
