[[Polydamantas]]

Polydamantas ([tpl]lang-el|Πολυδάμαντας[/tpl]) is a former municipality in the Larissa regional unit, Thessaly, Greece. Since the 2011 local government reform it is part of the municipality Farsala, of which it is a municipal unit.Kallikratis law Greece Ministry of Interior [tpl]el icon[/tpl] Population 6,412 (2001). The seat of the municipality was in Vamvakou.
