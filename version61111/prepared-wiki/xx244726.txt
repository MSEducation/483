[[Christian Church (East Delhi, New York)]]

CATEGORIES: Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, National Register of Historic Places in Delaware County, New York, Gothic Revival architecture in New York, Churches completed in 1861, Churches in Delaware County, New York

Christian Church, also known as Fitches Bridge Church, is a historic church on NY 10 at East Delhi in Delaware County, New York.  It was built in 1861 and is in the Greek Revival style with an overlay of Gothic Revival decoration.  It is a small rectangular structure of post and beam construction.  It features 12 narrow, engaged towers with pinnacles that mark the corners of the building.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=1638|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: Christian Church|date=October 2000|accessdate=2010-01-08 |author=Kathleen LaFrank|publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation[/tpl] See also: [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=1637|title=Accompanying three photos[/tpl]
It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 2001.
