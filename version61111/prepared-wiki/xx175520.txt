[[Gmina Jaktorów]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Grodzisk Mazowiecki County

Gmina Jaktorów is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Grodzisk Mazowiecki County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Jaktorów, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|8|km|mi|0[/tpl] west of Grodzisk Mazowiecki and [tpl]convert|37|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-west of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|55.24|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 10,090.

==Villages==

Gmina Jaktorów contains the villages and settlements of Bieganów, Budy Michałowskie, Budy Zosine, Budy-Grzybek, Chylice, Chylice-Kolonia, Chylice-Osada, Grabnik, Grądy, Henryszew, Jaktorów, Jaktorów-Kolonia, Jaktorów-Osada, Kołaczek, Mariampol, Maruna, Międzyborów, Sade Budy and Stare Budy.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Jaktorów is bordered by the town of Żyrardów and by the gminas of Baranów, Grodzisk Mazowiecki, Radziejowice and Wiskitki.
