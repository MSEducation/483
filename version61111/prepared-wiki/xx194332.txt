[[Central Europe Rally]]

CATEGORIES: Rally raids, Dakar Rally, 2008 in rallying, 2008 in European sport

The Central Europe Rally was a rally raid endurance race held in Romania and Hungary.Central Europe Rally 2008: 2008 Edition > The Route It was held in 2008 as a replacement for the canceled 2008 Dakar Rally.Central Europe Rally 2008: 2008 Edition > Sporting Aspects It was organized by the Amaury Sport Organisation.

==Route==

The event began in Budapest on April 19 and finished at Lake Balaton in Hungary on April 26, 2008.
