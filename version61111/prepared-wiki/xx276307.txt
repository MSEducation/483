[[Cynthia Falabella]]

Cynthia Lima Falabella (born January 19, 1972 in Belo Horizonte, Brazil) is a Brazilian actress. Daughter of actor Rogério Falabella and singer Maria Olympia, she is the sister of actress Débora Falabella.
