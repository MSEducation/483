[[Newfoundland general election, 1861]]

CATEGORIES: Elections in Newfoundland and Labrador, 1861 elections in North America, 1861 elections in Canada, Pre-Confederation Newfoundland, 1861 in Newfoundland

The 9th Newfoundland general election was held in 1861 to elect members of the 8th General Assembly of Newfoundland in the Dominion of Newfoundland. 14 Conservatives and 12 Liberals were elected. The results in Harbour Grace and Harbour Main electoral districts were set aside following violence at the polls; members were elected in subsequent by-elections.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.heritage.nf.ca/law/riots_1861.html |title=The Election Riots of 1861 |work=Newfoundland and Labrador Heritage |publisher=Memorial University |accessdate=2009-10-20[/tpl] The Liberal Party led by John Kent was defeated by the Conservatives led by Hugh Hoyles; Ambrose Shea replaced Kent as party leader.
