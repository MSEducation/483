[[Miss Venezuela World 2013]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 in Venezuela, 2013 beauty pageants

Miss Venezuela World 2013, the 5th edition of Miss Venezuela World beauty pageant and the 1st. Edition as an official separate pageant to choose Venezuela's representative to Miss World (the pageant was previously held in 2000, 2001, 2002 and 2006. The winner of 2013 edition was Karen Soto of Zulia.
The pageant was broadcast live on Venevisión from the Estudio 1, in Venevisión in Caracas, Venezuela. At the conclusion of the final night of competition, outgoing titleholder Gabriella Ferrari and Miss World 2011, Ivian Sarcos crowned Karen Soto of Zulia as the new Miss Venezuela World.[tpl]cite web|publisher=El Universal|title=Karen Soto va al Miss Mundo|url=http://www.eluniversal.com/que-hay/130811/karen-soto-va-al-miss-mundo|accessdate=2013-08-11[/tpl]
The show was hosted by Miss World 1984 Astrid Carolina Herrera, actor Luciano D'Alessandro and Miss World 2006 semifinalist and TV Host Federica Guzmán.
