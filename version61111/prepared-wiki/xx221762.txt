[[The 50th Anniversary Album]]

CATEGORIES: Cliff Richard compilation albums, 2008 compilation albums, EMI Records compilation albums

The 50th Anniversary Album is a compilation album by Cliff Richard.  The album was released by EMI on November 3, 2008 to mark Cliff's 50th Anniversary as a recording artist.[ref]
</ref>
[ref]
</ref>
It featured 50 tracks across two CD's including new single Thank You For A Lifetime which charted at number 3 in the UK Singles Chart in September 2008. Cliff Richard (real name Harry Webbe) is therefore one of most successful British male solo artists.
