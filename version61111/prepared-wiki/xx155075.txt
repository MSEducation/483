[[Eli Fuchs]]

CATEGORIES: 1924 births, 1992 deaths, Israeli Jews, Jewish footballers, Israeli footballers, Maccabi Tel Aviv F.C. players, Israel international footballers, Maccabi Haifa F.C. players, Israeli football managers, Maccabi Haifa F.C. managers, Maccabi Netanya F.C. managers, AC Omonia managers, Olympiakos Nicosia managers, Expatriate soccer managers in South Africa

Eli Fuchs ([tpl]lang-he|אלי פוקס[/tpl] ;1924–1992) was an Israeli international football player and manager of Maccabi Haifa. Aside from managing in Israel, he has managed clubs in South Africa and Cyprus. He died at 1992 as an Arkia employee.
