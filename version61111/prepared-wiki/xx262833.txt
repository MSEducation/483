[[1962 USAC Championship Car season]]

CATEGORIES: 1962 in motorsport, USAC Championship Car, 1962 in American motorsport

The 1962 USAC Championship Car season consisted of 13 races, beginning in Trenton, New Jersey on April 8 and concluding in Phoenix, Arizona on November 18. There was also one non-championship event at Pikes Peak, Colorado.  The USAC National Champion and Indianapolis 500 winner was Rodger Ward. In the Langhorne 100 at Langhorne Speedway death Hugh Randall, he was 29 years old.
