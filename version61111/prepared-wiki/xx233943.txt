 Her performances were so well received that she was engaged to sing eight more times beyond her original four performances. Her success led her mother to request an audience for Mignon with [[Pope Pius X]]. (Her mother had had an audience with [[Leo XIII]] ten years prior.)

"American Singers in European Opera-Houses" World Today 17: 832. and then made her way to England where she appeared as Ophelia in Thomas' Hamlet under Thomas Beecham at the opening of the 1910 winter season at Covent Garden. In 1917 she appeared as Desdemona in Verdi's Otello with Frank Mullings in the title role and Frederic Austin as Iago.
