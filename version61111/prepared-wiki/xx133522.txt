[[ASC Port Autonome]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Senegal, Sport in Dakar

Union Culturelle et Sportive des Travailleurs Port Autonome is a Senegalese football club based in Dakar. 

==History==

Their home stadium is Stade Port Autonome. 
The club was relegated in the 2010 season to the Senegal Ligue 2.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.rsssf.com/tabless/sene2010.html|publisher=rsssf.com|title=Senegal 2010[/tpl]
