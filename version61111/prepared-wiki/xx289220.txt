[[Hesar Shivan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Hesar Shivan ([tpl]lang-fa|حصارشيوان[/tpl], also Romanized as Ḩeşār Shīvān)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3773078|Hesar Shivan[/tpl] is a village in Shivanat Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 86, in 17 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
