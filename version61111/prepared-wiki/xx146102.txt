Olha Saladuha ([tpl]lang-uk|Ольга Саладуха[/tpl], born June 4, 1983) is an Ukrainian triple jumper.

==Biography==

She won the bronze medal at the 2012 Olympic Games in London and is reigning World and European champion at the women's triple jump.
Saladukha's personal best jump is 14.99 meters, achieved on June 29, 2012 in Helsinki at the European Championships. She also has a personal best of 6.37 metres in the long jump.
