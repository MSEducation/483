[[Rahim Beširović]]

CATEGORIES: 1971 births, Living people, People from Belgrade, Bosniaks of Serbia, Association football forwards, Serbian footballers, FK Novi Pazar players, FK Budućnost Podgorica players, FK Partizan players, UE Lleida footballers, Expatriate footballers in Spain, Busan IPark players, Serbian expatriates in South Korea, K League players, Expatriate footballers in South Korea

Rahim Beširović (Serbian Cyrillic: Paхим Бeшиpoвић ; born January 2, 1971 in Belgrade) is a Serbian-born Bosniak football player who has played as forward.
His previous club were FK Novi Pazar, FK Budućnost Podgorica, and most important, FK Partizan, UE Lleida in Spain and Busan Daewoo Royals in South Korea.
