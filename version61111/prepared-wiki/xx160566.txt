[[Amaranthus graecizans]]

CATEGORIES: Amaranthus, Tumbleweeds, Flora of New Jersey, Plants described in 1753

Amaranthus graecizans is an African[tpl]Citation needed|date=January 2009[/tpl] species in the botanical family Amaranthaceae.  It is naturalized in North America.
Common names include tumbleweed and pigweed.[ref]
