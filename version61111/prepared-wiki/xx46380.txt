[[Jalhay]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Liège (province)

Jalhay is a municipality of Belgium. It lies in the country's Walloon Region and Province of Liege. On January 1, 2006 Jalhay had a total population of 7,953. The total area is 107.75 km² which gives a population density of 74 inhabitants per km². The highest point of the municipality is the Baraque Michel, in the High Fens, at 674 m.
