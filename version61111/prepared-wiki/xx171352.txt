</ref> Ashuba was re-elected in 2007 both as Deputy and as Speaker, but suffered a first-round loss in the 2012 elections. When President [[Sergei Bagapsh]] died in 2011, and Vice President [[Alexander Ankvab]] participated in the [[Abkhazian presidential election, 2011|subsequent Presidential election]], Ashuba acted as President. ==Early life==

Nugzar Ashuba was born on March 2, 1952 in the village of Guada, Ochamchira District, and is married with two children.
