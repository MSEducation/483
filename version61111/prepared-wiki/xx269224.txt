[[Gordon Hendrick]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1949 births, Members of the Montana House of Representatives, Montana Republicans, People from Mineral County, Montana

Gordon Hendrick (February 16, 1949) is a former Republican member of the Montana Legislature.  He was elected to House District 14 which represents the Superior area.[tpl]cite web|last=Montana Legislature|title=Gordon Hendrick|url=http://leg.mt.gov/css/Sessions/61st/leg_info.asp?HouseID=1&SessionID=94&LAWSID=3841|accessdate=24 September 2011[/tpl] Due to Montana's term limits, he was ineligible to run for re-election in 2012. He was succeeded by Republican candidate Nicholas Schwaderer for the 2013 legislature cycle.
