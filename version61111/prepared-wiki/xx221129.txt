[[All That! Music and More Festival]]

CATEGORIES: 1999 concert tours, 2000 concert tours, All That

All That Music and More Festival was a summer festival promotional tour for All That, during June 1999 - August 1999. It was hosted by the season 6 cast of All That, and features musical guest, mostly the ones that were on All That. During every tour location, The cast have performed live sketchs on stage.
