[[Axlr8r]]

CATEGORIES: Venture capital firms

Axlr8r is a business startup program founded in 2009 by SOSventures. The programs take place across three continents with a goal to provide startup companies with immediate exposure and access to market knowledge, mentors and funding in a concentrated amount of time.  Haxlr8r, Selr8r, China Axlr8r and SynBio Axlr8r' focus on startup development in the areas of hardware, sales, software and biotechnology.

==HAXLR8R==

HAXLR8R[tpl]cite web|last=-|first=Admin|title=Chinaccelerator and the Chinese Startup Scene|url=http://www.popularindustry.com/china-accelerator-and-the-chinese-startup-secene/|publisher=Popular Industry|accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl] runs in China and the United States. It is a venture fund that focuses on entrepreneurs building hardware devices. It offers seed funding ($25,000), office space as well as a broad mentorship program. It gives an opportunity to 2 - 4 person start-ups to take an idea and make it a product. The HAXLR8R program selects start-ups to work with twice a year and has been widely received as the start of a new hardware revolution. ==SynBio Axlr8r==

SynBio Axlr8r runs in Cork and Dublin - SynBio axlr8r is a venture fund which focuses on entrepreneurs building technologies in or around the field of Synthetic Biology. It offers companies seed funding ($30,000), lab space as well as mentorship to help take an idea to a product.[tpl]cite web|title=Reasons to be in Ireland this October: Synbio AXLR8R, Grow Your Own|url=http://www.indiebiotech.com/?p=196|publisher=Indie Biotech|accessdate=14 August 2013[/tpl]
