[[Qareh Vali, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Qareh Vali ([tpl]lang-fa|قره ولي[/tpl], also Romanized as Qareh Valī and Qarah Valī; also known as Ghareh Vali, Qarāvalī, and Qarāwāli)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3079796|Qareh Vali[/tpl] is a village in Shivanat Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 390, in 83 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
