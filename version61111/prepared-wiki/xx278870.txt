[[British Chess Company]]

CATEGORIES: Chess equipment manufacturers, Manufacturing companies of the United Kingdom, 1891 establishments in England, Companies established in 1891

The British Chess Company (BCC) was founded by William Moffatt (1843-1918) and William Hughes and began manufacturing chess pieces in 1891.British Chess Company Sets

==Chess sets==

The BCC was an attempt to compete against Jaques of London.  The BCC developed new manufacturing processes with new types of materials, such as celluloid for their chess pieces.  In 1891, the BCC registered the design for two of their chess sets: (1) Royal Chessmen; (2) Imperial Staunton.
