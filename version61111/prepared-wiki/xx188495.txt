[[Parcona District]]

Parcona District is one of fourteen districts of the province Ica in Peru.  Parcona is the second smallest of the fourteen districts.  thumbnail|Parcona district within Ica Province of Ica Region Peru.[tpl]es icon[/tpl] Instituto Nacional de Estadística e Informática. Banco de Información Distrital. Retrieved April 11, 2008.
Parcona is an inland small area, 2.5 km from the Ica District and inhabits 8,000 people.
