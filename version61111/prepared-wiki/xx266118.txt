[[Costa Mesa Police Department]]

CATEGORIES: Costa Mesa, California, Municipal police departments of California, Emergency services in Orange County, California, Government in Orange County, California, 1953 establishments in California

The Costa Mesa Police Department (CMPD) is a police organization active in Costa Mesa, California.

==Organization==

The department is authorized 130 officers plus civilian support staff.  The sworn personnel are represented by the Costa Mesa Police Officer Association.http://costamesapoa.com/http://articles.ocregister.com/2011-06-14/news/29662767_1_reserve-officers-city-council-cuts-chief-steve-staveley The department's web site seems to indicate the agency operates a police dog unit, motorcycle patrols and a SWAT team. 
The department divides the city into Area 1 and Area 2 (subdivided into two patrol beats each), both areas being commanded by an officer in the rank of lieutenant.http://www.costamesapd.org
The Support Services includes the Detective Bureau, Helicopter Bureau, Traffic Safety Bureau and the Communications Division.http://www.costamesapd.org/cmpdPage.php?pg=support
The Detective Bureau has three details, Property Crimes, Economic Crimes and Crimes against Persons.
Technical Services include the jail, Property and Evidence Bureau, Records Bureau, a Training Bureau and an Recruiting Bureau.http://www.costamesapd.org/cmpdPage.php?pg=Training
The department also has an Animal Control Sectionhttp://costamesaca.gov/index.aspx?page=304

==Mid-air collision==

On March 10, 1987 a helicopter from the CMPD collided with another helicopter operated by the Newport Beach Police Department. The two crewmen in the CMPD aircraft were killed, while the other aircraft was able to land safely.http://www.odmp.org/officer/7507-police-officer-james-david-ketchum
