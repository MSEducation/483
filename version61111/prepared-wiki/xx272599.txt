[[Hylarana mocquardi]]

CATEGORIES: Hylarana, Amphibians of Indonesia, Fauna of Sulawesi, Endemic fauna of Indonesia

Hylarana mocquardi is a species of true frog in the genus Hylarana. It is known only from Sulawesi, Indonesia.[tpl]cite web |url=http://research.amnh.org/vz/herpetology/amphibia/?action=names&taxon=Hylarana+mocquardi |title=Hylarana mocquardi (Werner, 1901) |author=Frost, Darrel R. |date=2013 |work=Amphibian Species of the World 5.6, an Online Reference |publisher=American Museum of Natural History |accessdate=26 April 2013[/tpl] Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical moist lowland forests, rivers, intermittent rivers, freshwater marshes, intermittent freshwater marshes, rural gardens, and heavily degraded former forest.
