[[I'm Your Pusher (Scooter song)]]

CATEGORIES: Scooter (band) songs, 2000 singles, Songs written by H. P. Baxxter, Songs written by Rick J. Jordan, 2000 songs, Songs written by Jens Thele

I'm Your Pusher is a song by German Techno band Scooter based on the melody of "Flieger, Grüß' Mir Die Sonne" by Allan Gray and Walter Reisch. It was released in May 2000 as the lead single off the album Sheffield. It is the first single to be released from the sublabel of Kontor Records, Sheffield Tunes.
