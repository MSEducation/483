[[Noonday Creek Structure Number 16]]

CATEGORIES: Buildings and structures in Cobb County, Georgia, Dams in Georgia (U.S. state)

Noonday Creek Structure Number 16 is a category 2 (low hazard) earthen dam on Noonday Creek regulated by the Georgia Safe Dams Program,Georgia Safe Dams Program located a few yards north of Big Shanty Road in Kennesaw, GA. Construction was completed in 1956.

==Dimensions==

The dam is [tpl]convert|32|ft|m[/tpl] high, and [tpl]convert|912|ft|m[/tpl] long. The maximum discharge is [tpl]convert|2050|cuft/s|m3/s[/tpl].Findlakes
