[[Kita-Kōnosu Station]]

CATEGORIES: Takasaki Line, Stations of East Japan Railway Company, Railway stations in Saitama Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Kita-Kōnosu Station|北鴻巣駅|Kita-Kōnosu-eki[/tpl] is a railway station of  Takasaki Line, East Japan Railway Company in Akamidai, in the city of Kōnosu, Saitama Prefecture, Japan.

==Layout==

The station has an island platform.

==Statistics==

The daily average usage for 2006 was 8,191 people.
