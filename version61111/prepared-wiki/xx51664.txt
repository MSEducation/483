[[Cochoa]]

CATEGORIES: Cochoa, Bird genera

The cochoas (from cocho, Nepali for Cochoa purpurea)[tpl]Cite book| last = Jobling| first = James A.| title = A Dictionary of Scientific Bird Names| publisher = Oxford University Press| location = | year = 1991| page = 57| isbn = 0-19-854634-3 [/tpl] are medium-sized frugivorous, insectivorous and molluscivorous birds in the genus Cochoa.
