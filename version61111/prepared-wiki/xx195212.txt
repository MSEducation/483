[[Blue Plate Specials Live]]

CATEGORIES: 1999 live albums, The Specials live albums

Blue Plate Specials is a live album by The Specials, released in 1999 (see 1999 in music).  The material on the album is sourced from two serperate performances: a 1980 concert by The Specials, and an earlier show when they were still known as either Coventry Automatics or the Special A.K.A.
