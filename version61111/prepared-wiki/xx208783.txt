[[Josiah Dennis House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses completed in 1736, Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Massachusetts, Historic house museums in Massachusetts, Museums in Barnstable County, Massachusetts, Houses in Barnstable County, Massachusetts, Dennis, Massachusetts

Josiah Dennis House is a historic house at 16 Whig Street in Dennis, Massachusetts.
The house was built in 1736 and added to the National Historic Register in 1974.
The Dennis Historical Society owns and operates the house as the Josiah Dennis Manse Museum, an 18th-century historic house museum.  The house is open on Tuesdays and Thursdays in the summer.
