[[2011 Toray Pan Pacific Open – Doubles]]

CATEGORIES: Pan Pacific Open, 2011 WTA Tour

Iveta Benešová and Barbora Záhlavová-Strýcová were the defending champions, but lost to Gisela Dulko and Flavia Pennetta in the first round.
Dulko and Pennetta eventually lost the final against top seeded and winner of the tournament Liezel Huber and Lisa Raymond, 7–6(7–4), 0–6, 10–6.
