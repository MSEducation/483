==Early life==

Nancy was born and raised in Vancouver, British Columbia.  She studied drama during high school, and went on to attend the Breck Academy and The Vancouver Arts Club Theatre Program
[tpl]cite web|url = http://www.thehearttruth.ca/nancy_robertson/ |title = The Heart Truth: Nancy Robertson Bio |publisher = The Heart and Stroke Foundation |accessdate = 10 December 2011[/tpl]

==Career==

Robertson's film career began with the CBC sketch comedy series The 11th Hour.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.thecanadianencyclopedia.com/index.cfm?PgNm=TCE&Params=A1ARTA0012214 |title=Nancy Robertson |publisher=The Canadian Encyclopedia |accessdate=10 December 2011[/tpl]  She later played Harriet Sharpe, a temperamental parking attendant, in the mockumentary film The Delicate Art of Parking.  Harriet's fellow parking attendant, Grant Parker, is played by Fred Ewanuick,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/delicate_art_of_parking/ |title=The Delicate Art of Parking |publisher=Rotten Tomatoes |accessdate=10 December 2011[/tpl] who later co-starred with Robertson in Corner Gas. From 2004 to 2009, Robertson played Wanda Dollard, an intelligent and sarcastic gas station attendant, on the hit Canadian TV series Corner Gas.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cornergas.com/whoswho/?bio=7 |title=Who's Who |publisher=Corner Gas Online |accessdate=10 December 2011[/tpl]  In 2010, Nancy's husband and Corner Gas co-star, Brent Butt created a new TV series, Hiccups. The show's lead role, a children's author with anger management issues, was not initially written with Nancy in mind, but upon reading the script, she decided to take on the role.[tpl]cite web|url = http://www.cbc.ca/arts/tv/story/2008/12/03/brent-butt.html |title = Brent Butt to stay behind scenes in new comedy Hiccups |publisher = CBC News |date = 2008-12-03 |accessdate = 10 December 2011[/tpl]  The second and final season of Hiccups ended on August 28, 2011.  Robertson plays the role of Principal Moreno in the TV movie, Radio Rebel.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0731970/ |title=Nancy Robertson |publisher=IMDb |accessdate= 10 December 2011[/tpl]
Robertson is an alumnus of the Vancouver TheatreSports League improvisational comedy troupe.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.vtsl.com/company/alumni.php?from=Q&to=T |title=Vancouver TheatreSports League Alumni Q-T |publisher = Vancouver TheatreSports League |accessdate=10 December 2011[/tpl]

==Personal life==

On November 19, 2005, Nancy married her Corner Gas and Hiccups co-star Brent Butt.[tpl]cite web|url = http://www.lovetripper.com/newswire/11855-butt-robertson.html |title = Brent Butt and Nancy Robertson Wedding |publisher = lovetripper.com |accessdate = 10 December 2011[/tpl] They live in Vancouver.
