[[Now That's What I Call Dance Classics]]

CATEGORIES: 2009 compilation albums, Dance-pop compilation albums, Now That's What I Call Music! albums (American series)

Now That's What I Call Dance Classics is a compilation album in the U.S. Now! series released on November 3, 2009,Now That's What I Call Dance Classics, Various Artists: Dance/DJ, Walmart.com consisting of popular dance tracks released between 1978 and 1996. It peaked at number three on the Billboard Dance/Electronic Albums chart.artists|chart=all}} Now That's What I Call Dance Classics album info. Billboard.com. Retrieved 2009-11-13.
