[[Korea Squash Federation]]

CATEGORIES: Sports governing bodies in South Korea, National members of the World Squash Federation

Korea Squash Federation is the national organisation for Squash in Republic of Korea.
KSF was founded in May 1989, as the name of Korea Squashball Association, and changed its name as the current in November 1992. In January 1993, the organization joined World Squash Federation as a member country, and also joined Asian Squash Federation on June in the same year.
