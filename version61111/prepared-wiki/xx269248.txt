CATEGORIES: Cycling at the 2011 Pan American Games

The women's team sprint competition of the cycling events at the 2011 Pan American Games will be held on October 17 at the Pan American Velodrome in Guadalajara.Guadalajara 2011 sessions This event was not held at the 2007 Pan American Games, and therefore will make its debut at the games.

==Schedule==

All times are Central Standard Time (UTC-6).

==Results==

7 pairs of two competitors each competed. The top two pairs will race for gold, while third and fourth race for the bronze medals.Women's Team Sprint Qualifying
