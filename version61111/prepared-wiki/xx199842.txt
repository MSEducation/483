[[Wilde River]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of Bristol County, Massachusetts, Seekonk, Massachusetts, Pawtucket, Rhode Island, Rivers of Providence County, Rhode Island, Rivers of Massachusetts, Rivers of Rhode Island

Wilde Brook is a stream in Seekonk, Massachusetts and Pawtucket, Rhode Island. It begins at Bitersweet Pond in Seekonk and flows 5.2 miles to its confluence with the Ten Mile River in Pawtucket.

==Crossings==

==West Branch==

West Branch Wilde Brook is a small stream in Seekonk that begins in an unnamed pond and flows 2 miles to its junction with  Wilde Brook.
