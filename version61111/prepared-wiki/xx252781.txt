[[1978 Iowa State Cyclones football team]]

CATEGORIES: 1978 Big Eight Conference football season, Iowa State Cyclones football seasons, 1978 in Iowa

The 1978 Iowa State Cyclones football team represented the Iowa State University during the 1978 NCAA Division I-A football season as a member of the Big Eight Conference (Big 8). The team was led by head coach Earle Bruce, in his sixth year, and they played their home games at Cyclone Stadium in Ames, Iowa. They finished the season with a record of eight wins and four losses (8–4, 4–3 Big 8) and with a loss to [tpl]cfb link|year=1978|team=Texas A&M Aggies|title=Texas A&M[/tpl] in the [tpl]alternate links|1978 Hall of Fame Classic Bowl|All-American Bowl|title=Hall of Fame Classic[/tpl]. Of note, Iowa State's secondary coach was Pete Carroll.
