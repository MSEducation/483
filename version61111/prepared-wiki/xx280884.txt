[[Barut Aghaji, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Barut Aghaji ([tpl]lang-fa|باروت اغاجي[/tpl], also Romanized as Bārūt Āghājī; also known as Balūţ Āqājī, Baraalu, and Bārūţ Āqājī)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3055655|Barut Aghaji[/tpl] is a village in Mojezat Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 577, in 139 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
