[[Atinggola]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Gorontalo, Subdistricts of Gorontalo (province)

 
Atinggola is a small town and subdistrict in Gorontalo Regency in Gorontalo, northern Sulawesi, Indonesia. It is located on the Celebes Sea. As of 1981 it had a population of around 15,000 Bolango people.[tpl]cite book|last1=Grimes|first1=Barbara F.|last2=Grimes|first2=Joseph Evans|author3=Summer Institute of Linguistics|title=Ethnologue|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=b2liAAAAMAAJ|accessdate=30 December 2010|date=1 November 2000|publisher=SIL International|isbn=978-1-55671-103-9[/tpl] The people speak a local dialect of the Bolango language, called the Atinggola language.[tpl]cite book|last=Noorduyn|first=J.|title=A critical survey of studies on the languages of Sulawesi|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=0mIOAAAAYAAJ|accessdate=30 December 2010|year=1991|publisher=KITLV Press|isbn=978-90-6718-028-3|pages=64â€“68[/tpl][tpl]cite book|author1=Library of Congress|author2=Library of Congress.
