[[Guatay, California]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in San Diego County, California, Unincorporated communities in California, San Antonio-San Diego Mail Line

Guatay is an unincorporated community in the Mountain Empire area of southeastern San Diego County, California.  Its elevation is 3,999 feet (1,219 m).[tpl]cite gnis|id=1656536|name=Guatay, California[/tpl]  Although Guatay is unincorporated, it has a post office, with the ZIP code of 91931.Zip Code Lookup
Nearby Guatay Mountain is home to a grove of the rare Tecate Cypress.
