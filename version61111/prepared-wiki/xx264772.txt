[[Teemu Kesä]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Ilves players

Teemu Kesä (born June 7, 1981) is a Finnish professional ice hockey defenceman. He currently plays for Malmö Redhawks of the Swedish Allsvenskan. He was selected by the New Jersey Devils in the 4th round (100th overall) of the 1999 NHL Entry Draft.http://devils.nhl.com/club/player.htm?id=8467973
