[[Higashiusuki District, Miyazaki]]

CATEGORIES: Districts in Miyazaki Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Higashiusuki|東臼杵郡|Higashiusuki-gun[/tpl] is a district located in Miyazaki Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2006, the district has an estimated population of 31,269 and the density of 24.18 persons per km². The total area is 1,292.99 km².
