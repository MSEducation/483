[[Line 10 (Stockholm metro)]]

CATEGORIES: Stockholm metro

[tpl]unreferenced|date=January 2014[/tpl]Line 10 (T10) is one of the two blue lines of the Stockholm Metro connecting Kungsträdgården and  Hjulsta. It is a 15.1 km (9.4 mi) long line, with a 23 min travel time and 14 stations.
