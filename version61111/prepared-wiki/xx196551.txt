[[Li Weining]]

CATEGORIES: 1959 births, People's Republic of China politicians from Zhejiang, Living people, Communist Party of China politicians from Zhejiang, Political office-holders in Zhejiang, People from Taizhou, Zhejiang

Li Weining (Chinese: 李卫宁; pinyin: Lǐ Wèiníng; b. October 1959) is currently acting mayor of Jiaxing, Zhejiang Province of China.
He is a member of Communist Party of China, and became acting mayor since September 2007.
