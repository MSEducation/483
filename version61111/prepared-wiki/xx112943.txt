[[Shayna Rose]]

CATEGORIES: 1983 births, Living people, Actresses from Denver, Colorado, American soap opera actresses, American female singers, Musicians from Denver, Colorado

Shayna Rose (born Shayna Rose Mordue; November 3, 1983) is an American actress and singer.

==Private life==

Rose moved to Los Angeles in 2002 after declining a scholarship to the Berklee College of Music in Boston. She went between acting, singing, songwriting, auditions, and two jobs.

==Career==

Rose started performing and singing professionally when she was eight years old and appeared in a variety of regional musicals, including "Into the Woods" starring as Little Red Riding Hood at the Denver Civic Theater / Feast of Fools Theatre.
