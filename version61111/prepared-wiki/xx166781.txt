[[1958 Giro d'Italia]]

CATEGORIES: Giro d'Italia, 1958 in cycle racing, 1958 in Italian sport, 1958 in road cycling

The 1958 Giro d'Italia of cycling was held from 18 May to 8 June 1958, consisting of 20 stages.
