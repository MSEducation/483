[[2008–09 A1 Grand Prix of Nations, Italy]]

CATEGORIES: 2008–09 A1 Grand Prix season

The 2008–09 A1 Grand Prix of Nations, Italy was set to be an A1 Grand Prix race, to be held at Autodromo Internazionale di Mugello, Mugello, Italy. It was originally set to be the first race of the 2008-09 A1 Grand Prix season, but a delay in the build schedule of the new chassis forced it to be moved from the season opener.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.a1gp.com/News/NewsArticle.aspx?newsId=41882|title=A1GP delays start of Season Four |accessdate=2008-08-21|date=2008-08-21|publisher=a1gp.com[/tpl]
On September 9, 2008 it was announced that a replacement date for the race could not be found, and thus the race was removed from the schedule.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.a1gp.com/News/NewsArticle.aspx?newsId=41979|title=The battle of the Brands|accessdate=2008-09-09|date=2008-09-08|publisher=a1gp.com[/tpl]
