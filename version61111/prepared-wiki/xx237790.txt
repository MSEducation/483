[[Nils Håkansson]]

CATEGORIES: Swedish painters, Medieval painters, Year of death unknown, Year of birth unknown, 15th-century Swedish people

Nils Håkansson (Latinized as Nicolaus Haquini) was a late medieval Swedish painter from  Vadstena, known for his wall paintings in numerous churches in Scania, Sweden.[tpl]cite book|last=Hernfjäll|first=Viola|title=Medeltida målningar i gamla skara stift.|year=1993|pages=76–77, 195|url=http://www.svenskakyrkan.se/kinnekulle/Osterplana/Oplana%20gamla.htm|language=Swedish[/tpl] Håkansson was the leader of a group of painters known as Vittskövlegruppen or Vittskövlemästaren.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.formonline.se/kyrkor/Ysane/index.html|title=Kalkmålningar i Ysane kyrka av Nils Håkansson|last=Albertson|first=Lars-Olof|year=2007|publisher=Medeltida konst|accessdate=2009-10-18|language=Swedish[/tpl]
