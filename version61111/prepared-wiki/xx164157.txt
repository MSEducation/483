[[Upland Mutual Insurance Company]]

CATEGORIES: Insurance companies of the United States, Geary County, Kansas

The Upland Mutual Insurance Inc. is a small mutual insurance company in the United States that operates primarily in the state of Kansas.  Its roots go back to 1892 when a group of farmers came together for managing risk of fire and lighting.Kansas Insurance Commissioner
Originally based in unincorporated Upland, Kansas, the company location has moved to Junction City, Kansas.[ref]Upland Mutual Home Page
