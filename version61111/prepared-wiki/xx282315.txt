[[Desertiguana]]

CATEGORIES: Prehistoric reptiles of Asia, Cretaceous reptiles, Phrynosomatids

Desertiguana is an extinct genus of lizard in the family Phrynosomatidae. It is a monotypic genus represented by the type species Desertiguana gobiensis from the Late Cretaceous of Mongolia. Desertiguana gobiensis is known from a single left lower jaw.[tpl]cite doi|10.1134/S0031030113040023[/tpl]
