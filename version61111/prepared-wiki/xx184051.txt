[[Cambridge Zen Center]]

CATEGORIES: Zen centers, Kwan Um School of Zen, Buddhist temples in Massachusetts, Religious organizations established in 1973, Buildings and structures in Cambridge, Massachusetts

Cambridge Zen Center is an urban meditation center in Cambridge, Massachusetts close to Harvard University, part of the Kwan Um School of Zen.
