[[Wendlebury Halt railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Disused railway stations in Oxfordshire, Former London and North Western Railway stations, Railway stations opened in 1905, Railway stations closed in 1926

Wendlebury Halt was a railway station on the Varsity Line [tpl]convert|0.5|mi|m[/tpl] east of the village of Wendlebury. The London and North Western Railway opened the halt in 1905 and the London, Midland and Scottish Railway closed it in 1926.
