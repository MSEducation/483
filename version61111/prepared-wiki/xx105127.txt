[[Democratic Union (Ukraine)]]

CATEGORIES: Liberal parties in Ukraine

The Democratic Union ([tpl]Lang-uk|Демократиний союз[/tpl] Demokratyčnyj Sojuz) is a political party in Ukraine registered in June 1999.[tpl]uk icon[/tpl] Партія «Демократичний Союз», Database ASD
At the parliamentary elections on 30 March 2002, the party won together with the Democratic Party of Ukraine 0.87%% of the popular vote and 4 (single-mandate constituency) seats out of 450 seats. The party did not take part in further national elections.[tpl]uk icon[/tpl] Results of voting in single constituencies in 2012 & Nation-wide list, Central Election Commission of Ukraine
