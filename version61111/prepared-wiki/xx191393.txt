[[Lalgaye Department]]

CATEGORIES: Departments of Burkina Faso, Koulpélogo Province

Lalgaye  is a department or commune of Koulpélogo Province in eastern Burkina Faso. Its capital lies at the town of Lalgaye. According to the 1996 census the department has a total population of 18,691.Burkinabé government inforoute communale
