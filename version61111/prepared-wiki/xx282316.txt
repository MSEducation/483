[[2013 Speedway European Championship qualification]]

CATEGORIES: Individual Speedway European Championship

This page describes the qualifying procedure for the 2013 Speedway European Championship finals.
The 2013 Speedway European Championship qualification events was took place from April 20 to July 6, 2013.[tpl]cite web | title = Cztery imprezy rangi Mistrzostw Europy w Polsce | language = Polish | publisher = SportoweFakty.pl | date = 21 Nov 2012 | url = http://www.sportowefakty.pl/zuzel/324323/cztery-imprezy-rangi-mistrzostw-europy-w-polsce | accessdate = 10 Aug 2013 [/tpl] There was four final meetings with fifteen permanent riders and one wild cards and two track reserves. The permanent riders was determined in three SEC Semifinal and one SEC Challenge.

==Qualification system==

In three Semifinals was started 48 riders and to the Challenge was qualify top 5 from each meetings. This 15 riders and 1 riders from Challenge' host federations (Slovakia) was started in the final event. The top 9 riders was qualify for the final series.
