[[List of AAA World Heavyweight Champions]]

CATEGORIES: Asistencia Asesoría y Administración championships, World heavyweight wrestling championships, Professional wrestling champion lists

The AAA World Heavyweight Championship is a professional wrestling championship in Asistencia Asesoría y Administración (AAA). The Championship has been in existence since 2007 and competes with CMLL World Heavyweight Championship, the other top heavyweight championship in the country.
