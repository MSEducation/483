[[Spikko]]

CATEGORIES: VoIP companies

Spikko is a software based service that allows users to make voice calls over the Internet, PC to phone, phone to PC and  traditional phone to phone international calls. Initially it was a freeware, but starting from 2 August 2011, it is no longer free[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.spikko.com/anywhereNotice/new_anywhere_eng.html|title=Spikko Notice to new and existing users[/tpl] Existing users were displayed a notice upon login, and are required to pay to continue their services.
Nowadays Spikko offers various services related to international telephone services.
