[[Gary Langley]]

CATEGORIES: St Helens RLFC players, Living people, 1984 births

Gareth (Gary) "Gaz" Langley was a professional rugby league footballer who played for Salford City Reds and St. Helens in the Super League before moving on to Oldham Roughyeds. He was by a preference a wing and could kick goals. He made one first-grade appearance in St Helens' 26-22 defeat away to Catalans Dragons in 2006's Super League XI, a game in which Saints rested several of their regular first-team squad in preparation for their Challenge Cup Final with Huddersfield Giants. In this game, he scored one try and kicked three goals.
