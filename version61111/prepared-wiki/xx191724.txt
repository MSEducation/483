[[Ouéleni Department]]

CATEGORIES: Departments of Burkina Faso, Léraba Province

 Ouéleni   is a department or commune of Léraba Province in south-western  Burkina Faso. Its capital lies at the town of Ouéleni. According to the 1996 census the department has a total population of 10.020.Burkinabé government inforoute communale
