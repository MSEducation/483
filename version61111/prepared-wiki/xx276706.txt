[[Judith Brett]]

CATEGORIES: 1949 births, Living people, Australian political scientists, La Trobe University faculty, People from Melbourne

Judith Brett (born 1949, Melbourne) is a professor of politics at La Trobe University, Melbourne, Australia.
She was an editor of Meanjin, the Melbourne-based Australian literary quarterly in the 1980s, and was a co-editor of Arena Magazine in the 1990s.
