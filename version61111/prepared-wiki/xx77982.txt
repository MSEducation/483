[[Mirditë District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Albania, Lezhë County

The District of Mirditë ([tpl]Lang-sq|Rrethi i Mirditës[/tpl]) is one of the thirty-six districts of Albania, part of Lezhë County. It has a population of 26,668[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.instat.gov.al/graphics/doc/tabelat/Treguesit%20Sociale/Popullsia/POP%202009/t2.xls|title=POPULLSIA SIPAS RRETHEVE, 2001-2010|publisher=Albanian Institute of Statistics|accessdate=2010-09-09[/tpl] (2010 estimate), and an area of 867 km². It is in the north of the country, and its capital is Rrëshen. The district consists of the following municipalities:
During the time of Ottoman rule, Mirdita, along with the surrounding tribal regions, obtained a status of semi-independence from the Ottomans and managed to preserve their Roman Catholic faith.[tpl]citation needed|date=September 2010[/tpl]
