==History==

The band formed in 2007 and has released two singles, titled "And We're Off" and "Alone", in the fall of 2009. They recorded material for their singles and upcoming album in their home studio[tpl]cite web|url=http://blogs.myspace.com/index.cfm?fuseaction=blog.view&friendId=286677929&blogId=496626711 |title=New Stars Demo! van Power Pirate op Myspace |publisher=Blogs.myspace.com |date=June 24, 2009 |accessdate=October 21, 2011[/tpl] in the summer of 2009. The band has been noted as an example of positive press coverage.[tpl]cite web|author=December 27, 2009 |url=http://massey-media.com/2009/12/i-wanna-rock-the-power-of-the-press-in-the-entertainment-industry/ |title=I Wanna Rock: The Power of the Press in the Entertainment Industry |publisher=Massey Media |date=December 27, 2009 |accessdate=October 21, 2011[/tpl]
Emily began learning guitar in 2006. From there she learned basic chords from her mother, a classical guitar teacher, and then continued to teach herself on the electric guitar. Emily had been playing for roughly a year when Power Pirate was formed.[tpl]cite web|url=http://power-pirate.com/about.html |title=Press Kit |publisher=Power Pirate |accessdate=October 21, 2011[/tpl] Emily does much of the graphic design, booking, and management work for the band.[tpl]cite web|url=http://power-pirate.com/aboutthesite.html |title=About Our Site |publisher=Power-pirate.com |accessdate=October 21, 2011[/tpl]
Michael coded most of the band's website and Myspace page with Emily doing the designing, and additionally acts as the band's promotional photographer, sound technician and producer. Michael has studied music theory with the Levine School of Music in Kensington, MD, and Studio Recording at their Washington, D.C. location.
Their album Plane Ticket was released on April 10, 2010, at a release show at the U Street Music Hall in Washington DC.[tpl]cite web|author=October 20, 2011 |url=http://ownthepress.com/?p=781 |title=Orders » Blog Archive » Diet Weight Loss Supplements |publisher=Ownthepress.com |date=February 14, 1999 |accessdate=October 21, 2011[/tpl][tpl]cite web|last=Malitz |first=David |url=http://voices.washingtonpost.com/goingoutgurus/2010/04/nightlife_agenda_jellybean_ben.html#sat |title=Going Out Gurus – Nightlife agenda: Jellybean Benitez, Done and Done, Panda Head |publisher=Voices.washingtonpost.com |date=April 7, 2010 |accessdate=October 21, 2011[/tpl]
