[[2005 Macau Grand Prix]]

CATEGORIES: 2005 in Formula Three, Macau Grand Prix, 2005 in Macau

The 2005 Macau Grand Prix Formula Three was the 52nd Macau Grand Prix race held on the streets of Macau on 20 November 2005. It was supported by the 2005 Guia Race of Macau.
