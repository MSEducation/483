[[Khanqah, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Khanqah ([tpl]lang-fa|خانقاه[/tpl], also Romanized as Khānqāh and Khāneqāh; also known as Khanakakh and Khānegāh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3070593|Khanqah[/tpl] is a village in Golabar Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 311, in 83 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
