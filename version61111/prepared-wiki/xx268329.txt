Jaime Andrés Bustamante Suárez (born 21 April 1980) is a Colombian born – Venezuelan international footballer who plays for Brazilian club São Caetano, as a defender.

==Career==

Bustamante has played for Millonarios, Caracas FC and Zamora FC.[tpl]NFT player|pid=37191[/tpl] He signed for Brazilian club São Caetano in June 2012.[tpl]cite web|url=http://adsaocaetano.com.br/noticias/3268-azulao-oficializa-contratacao-de-xerife-da-selecao-venezuelana|title=AZULÃO OFICIALIZA CONTRATAÇÃO DE “XERIFE” DA SELEÇÃO VENEZUELANA|date=13 June 2012|language=Portuguese|publisher=Associação Desportiva São Caetano[/tpl]
He made his international debut for Venezuela in 2010.
