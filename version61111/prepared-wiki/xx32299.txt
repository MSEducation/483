[[Viðfinnr]]

CATEGORIES: People in Norse mythology and legends

In Norse mythology, Viðfinnr ("wood-Finn"Lindow (2001:315).) is the father of Hjúki and Bil, a brother and sister who, according to Gylfaginning, were taken up from the earth by Máni, the personified moon, as they were fetching water from the well Byrgir.
