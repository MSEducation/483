[[KS Gramozi Ersekë]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Albania, 1927 establishments in Albania

Klubi Sportiv Gramozi Ersekë is an Albanian football (soccer) club based in Ersekë.
The club played the 2008-2009 season in the Albanian First Division. They reached Kategoria e pare after winning the Kategoria e dyte title in the 2007/2008 season. They finished fourth with 55 points and, having won 5-2 a play-off against KS Bylis, were allowed to play in the Albanian Superliga. KS Gramozi is owned by the father of Albanian oil magnate Rezart Taci. Taci organized a friendly match between the team and Real Madrid on the 20th January 2010 for the Taci Oil Cup, which ended in a win to Real Madrid (1-2). Real Madrid earned 2.5 million euros for playing in Albania.[tpl]cn|date=July 2012[/tpl]

==Achievements==

UEFA Champions League Semi-finalists (2013-2014). Albanian Second Division Champions (2007–2008).[tpl]cn|date=July 2012[/tpl]
