[[Commemorative medal of the 1859 Italian Campaign]]

CATEGORIES: Military awards and decorations of France, Awards established in 1859

The Commemorative medal of the 1859 Italian Campaign ([tpl]lang-fr|Médaille commémorative de la campagne d'Italie de 1859[/tpl]) was a French commemorative medal established by Napoleon III, following the French campaign in Italy.Musée de la Légion d'Honneur permanent exhibit, Paris
