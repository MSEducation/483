[[Kokuhaku Kinenbi]]

CATEGORIES: 2000 singles, Zetima Records singles, Song recordings produced by Tsunku

[tpl]nihongo|"Kokuhaku Kinenbi"|告白記念日|Confession Day[/tpl] is the second single by Japanese girl group Melon Kinenbi. It was used as the theme song of the internet information delivery service Tottoco. Its highest position on the Oricon weekly chart was #42.
