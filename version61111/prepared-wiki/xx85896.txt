[[3018 Godiva]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Discoveries by Edward L. G. Bowell, Asteroids named for people, Astronomical objects discovered in 1982

3018 Godiva is a small main belt asteroid, which was discovered by Edward L. G. Bowell in 1982. It is named after Lady Godiva, the Saxon woman who, legend has it, rode naked through the city of Coventry.
