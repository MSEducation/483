James Irvine RDI (1958 – 18 February 2013) was a London-born designer working in Milan, where he moved in 1984.james irvine[tpl]cite web|last=Montgomery |first=Angus |url=http://www.designweek.co.uk/news/product-and-furniture-designer-james-irvine-dies-aged-54/3036035.article |title=Product and furniture designer James Irvine dies aged 54 | News |publisher=Design Week |date=2010-12-31 |accessdate=2013-02-18[/tpl] He obtained his Bachelor's degree from the Kingston Polytechnic (now University) Design school and his Master's from the Royal College of Art. In 1999 he specially designed a body for the Mercedes-Benz O530 Citaro bus for the city of Hannover, in preparation for Expo 2000.
