[[Tomáš Čížek]]

CATEGORIES: 1978 births, Living people, People from Děčín, Czech footballers, Czech expatriate footballers, FK Baumit Jablonec players, AC Sparta Prague players, FC Rubin Kazan players, FC Moscow players, FC Alania Vladikavkaz players, FC Sibir Novosibirsk players, Expatriate footballers in Russia, Gambrinus liga players, Russian Premier League players

Tomáš Cízek (born 27 November 1978 in Děčín) is a Czech footballer who currently plays for FK Baumit Jablonec.
Previously he played for Jablonec, Pelikán Děčín, and Sparta Prague in the Czech Republic, and for Rubin Kazan, FC Moscow, and Alania Vladikavkaz in Russia.
