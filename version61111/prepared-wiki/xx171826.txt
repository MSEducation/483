Andrej Nedorost (born April 30, 1980) is a Slovak professional ice hockey player who currently plays for Yertis Pavlodar of the Kazakhstan Hockey Championship.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.eliteprospects.com/team.php?team=3870 | title=Yertis Pavlodar current roster|publisher=Eliteprospects.com |date= |accessdate=November 22, 2013[/tpl]

==Career==

He has played 28 games in the NHL with the Columbus Blue Jackets. On 25 September 2008, he moved from Czech Republic team HC Slovan Ústí nad Labem to HC Dukla Trenčín in Slovakia.
