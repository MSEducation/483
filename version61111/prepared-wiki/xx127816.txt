[[T. W. Randall House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Florida, National Register of Historic Places in Marion County, Florida, Houses in Marion County, Florida

The T. W. Randall House (also known as the Randall-Freeman-Leslie House) is a historic home in Silver Springs, Florida, United States. It is located at 11685 Northeast SR 314. On April 6, 1995, it was added to the U.S. National Register of Historic Places.
