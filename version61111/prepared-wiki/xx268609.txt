[[Bhalchandra Vaman Kelkar]]

CATEGORIES: Marathi-language writers, 1920 births, 1987 deaths

Bhalchandra Vaman Kelkar ([tpl]lang-mr|भालचंद्र वामन केळकर[/tpl]) (September 23, 1920 – November 6, 1987) was a Marathi writer and actor, from Maharashtra, India.  He was one of the founders of Progressive Dramatic Association in Pune.
