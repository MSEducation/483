[[Istället för vykort]]

CATEGORIES: Cornelis Vreeswijk albums, 1973 albums, Swedish folk music

Istället för vykort (English: Instead of postcard) is studio album by the Swedish-Dutch folk singer-songwriter Cornelis Vreeswijk.The album is considered as the most political one by Vreeswijk.Album info on www.cornelis.se
