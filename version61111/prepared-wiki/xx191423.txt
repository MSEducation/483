[[List of television series screened on Showcase Australia]]

CATEGORIES: Lists of television series by network

This is a list of television series that are currently screening or have previously been screened on the Showcase channel in Australia, up to May 2008.

==Miniseries and serials==

A number of miniseries and serials (the UK term for a miniseries) have also been screened on Showcase. Some are screened at weekly intervals whilst others are screened with all parts played back to back.
