[[Gmina Zębowice]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Opole Voivodeship, Olesno County

Gmina Zębowice, German Gemeinde Zembowitz is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Olesno County, Opole Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Its seat is the village of Zębowice (Zembowitz), which lies approximately [tpl]convert|14|km|mi|0[/tpl] south of Olesno and [tpl]convert|32|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] east of the regional capital Opole.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|95.81|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 4,090. Since 2007 the commune, like much of the area, has been bilingual in German and Polish, a large German population remaining in the area after Silesia was partitioned to Poland.
