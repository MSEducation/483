[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Registro]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1974, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Sorocaba, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Registro ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Registrensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Registro in the Ecclesiastical province of Sorocaba in Brazil.
