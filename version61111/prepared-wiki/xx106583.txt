Henry Nicholas Gage, 8th Viscount Gage ("Nicholas") (born on 9 April 1934) is a British viscount. 
He was educated at Eton, and Oxford."The virile viscount is a dad at 75", The Daily Mail, 20 August 2009
He lives at the 15th-century stately home, Firle Place in East Sussex. http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-1207737/The-virile-viscount-dad-75.html#ixzz2Jq1vbewe  He has opened Firle Place for public tours and exhibited his paintings in 2010."LORD GAGE'S FIRST SALE AND PUBLIC EXHIBITION OF PAINTINGS - FIRLE PLACE, NR LEWES", Sussex Life
He offers discounted rents in Firle village for people who work in the village.
There was a controversy over a Sussex Bonfire Night.
