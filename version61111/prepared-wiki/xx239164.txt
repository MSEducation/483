[[Gary Mawer]]

CATEGORIES: 1969 births, Canoeists at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Canoeists at the 2000 Summer Olympics, Irish canoeists, Living people, Olympic canoeists of Ireland

Gary Mawer (born December 12, 1969) is an Irish sprint canoer who competed from the mid-1990s to the early 2000s (decade). At the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta, he was eliminated in the repechages of both the K-2 500 m and the K-2 1000 m event. Four years later, Mawer was eliminated in the heats of the K-1 500 m event and the semifinals of the K-1 1000 m event.
