[[Antonio Adipe]]

CATEGORIES: 1912 births, Possibly living people, Uruguayan boxers, Light-heavyweight boxers, Olympic boxers of Uruguay, Boxers at the 1936 Summer Olympics

Antonio R. Adipe Marrone (born April 24, 1912) is an Uruguayan boxer who competed in the 1936 Summer Olympics.
In 1936 he was eliminated in the second round of the light heavyweight class after losing his fight to Thomas Griffin.
