[[Kasumori Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Aichi Prefecture

[tpl]Nihongo|Kasumori Station|烏森駅|Kasumori-eki[/tpl] is a railway station located in Nakamura-ku, Nagoya, Japan and is on the Kintetsu Nagoya Line.
