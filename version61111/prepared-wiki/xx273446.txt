[[Olivier Charroin]]

CATEGORIES: 1982 births, Living people, French male tennis players

Olivier Charroin (born 10 March 1982) is a French professional tennis player. He competes on the ATP Challenger Tour and ITF Futures, mainly in doubles competition.
