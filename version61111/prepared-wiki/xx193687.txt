[[2005 Chinese motorcycle Grand Prix]]

CATEGORIES: 2005 in Grand Prix motorcycle racing, Chinese motorcycle Grand Prix, 2005 in Chinese sport

The 2005 Chinese motorcycle Grand Prix was the third round of the 2005 MotoGP Championship. It took place on the weekend of 29 April-1 May 2005 at the Shanghai International Circuit.
