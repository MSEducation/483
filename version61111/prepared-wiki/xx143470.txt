[[Sarah Edwards (American actress)]]

CATEGORIES: American television actresses, Living people, 1984 births

Sarah Edwards (born June 4, 1984) is an American actress, best known as having played Theresa (a cheerleader) on The WB's television show One Tree Hill. Sarah's other work include one of the leads, Millison Avery, in the short-lived TV series Palmetto Pointe.
