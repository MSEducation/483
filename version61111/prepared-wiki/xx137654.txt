[[Juan de la Fuente]]

Juan de la Fuente (born August 15, 1976 in Buenos Aires) is a sailor from Argentina who won a bronze medal at the 2000 Summer Olympics in Sydney, alongside Javier Conte. At the 2012 Summer Olympics in London he also won a bronze medal.
