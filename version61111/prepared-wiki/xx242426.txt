[[Che Yin Wong]]

CATEGORIES: 1959 births, Hong Kong entrepreneurs, Hong Kong billionaires, Hong Kong philanthropists, Living people

Che Yin Wong, (simplified Chinese: 黄志贤, born May 13, 1959) is a successful businessman and philanthropist from Hong Kong, China.黄志贤：乐此不疲为慈善  He is the founder and chairman of Kong Fung International Group, a property conglomerate with interests in properties, hotels, mining and constructions.品位生活

==Political career==

Wong is a member of the National People's Congress and the Chairman of the Hong Kong China Chamber of Commerce, besides many other political responsibilities. Wong started to be actively involved in Chinese politics in the early 2000s.

==Businesses==

Wong's real estate development company, Heng Long Property Development Ltd Co., is one of the largest property development companies in China. It currently has a land bank in mainland China of exceeding [tpl]convert|20000000|sqft|m2[/tpl] in developable gross floor area.
