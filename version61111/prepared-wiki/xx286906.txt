[[Qara Qayeh, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Qara Qayeh ([tpl]lang-fa|قراقيه[/tpl], also Romanized as Qarā Qayeh; also known as Qarah Qayeh and Qareh Qayeh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3079584|Qara Qayeh[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 104, in 23 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
