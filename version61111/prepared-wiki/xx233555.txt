[[Either You're In or You're In The Way]]

CATEGORIES: 2009 books, HarperCollins books, American memoirs

Either You're In or You're In The Way (2009) is a memoir written by Logan and Noah Millers.  It was a San Francisco Chronicle non-fiction weekly bestseller in 2009.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.sfgate.com/cgi-bin/article.cgi?f=/c/a/2009/06/14/RV7R184OSO.DTL |title=San Francisco Chronicle Best-Sellers June 14 |publisher=SFGate |date=2009-06-14 |accessdate=2014-03-12[/tpl] Logan and Noah Miller were featured on FORA.tv's Filmmaking on the Fly: Logan & Noah Miller.[tpl]cite web|url=http://fora.tv/2009/05/28/Filmmaking_on_the_Fly_Logan__Noah_Miller |title=Filmmaking on the Fly: Logan & Noah Miller |publisher=FORA.tv |date=2009-05-28 |accessdate=2014-03-12[/tpl]
