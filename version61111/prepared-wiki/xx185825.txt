[[Abbas Farzanegan]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Iranian governors, Iranian generals, Ambassadors of Iran to Saudi Arabia, Iranian expatriates in the United States

Abbas Farzanegan (need dates) served as Iran's ambassador to Saudi Arabia and 4 other countries during Mohammad-Rezā Shāh Pahlavi's reign.
