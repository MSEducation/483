[[Rolin Jones]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1970s births, American dramatists and playwrights, American television writers, Yale School of Drama alumni

Rolin Jones (born [tpl]circa[/tpl] 1972/1973)[tpl]cite web |last = Schillinger | first = Liesl | title = The Accidental Design of Rolin Jones's Career | url = http://www.nytimes.com/2005/09/18/theater/newsandfeatures/18schi.html?pagewanted=all | work = New York Times | date = September 18, 2005[/tpl] is a playwright and television writer. His plays include The Intelligent Design of Jenny Chow, for which he was a 2006 Pulitzer Prize finalist,[tpl]cite web | title = Drama: Past Winners and Finalists | url = http://www.pulitzer.org/bycat/Drama | publisher = The Pulitzer Prizes  accessdate = January 22, 2012[/tpl] and Sovereignty.Rolin Jones - complete guide to the Playwright and Plays His work in television include Showtime's Weeds and United States of Tara, NBC drama Friday Night Lights and HBO's Boardwalk Empire.[tpl]IMDb name|1289754[/tpl]
Jones joined the crew of Friday Night Lights as a writer and supervising producer for the series fourth season in 2009 and, at that sesion, wrote the episodes "The Son" and "Laboring". He was nominated for two Writers Guild of America Award for Best Drama Series for his work on the series[tpl]cite web | author=Gregg Mitchell & Sherry Goldman | title = 2010 Writers Guild Awards Television, Radio, News, Promotional Writing, and Graphic Animation Nominees Announced | url = http://www.wga.org/awards/awardssub.aspx?id=1516 | publisher = Writers Guild of America | year = 2009 | accessdate = April 30, 2010|archiveurl=http://archive.is/Q6v5|archivedate=2012-05-25[/tpl] and received a 2010 Emmy Award nomination for Outstanding Writing for a Drama Series for the episode "The Son".[tpl]cite web | last = Lowman | first = Rob | title = Playwright Rolin Jones finds inspiration in San Fernando Valley life | url = http://www.dailynews.com/arts/ci_19158779 | work = Los Angeles Daily News | date = October 20, 2011[/tpl]
Jones grew up in the Woodland Hills area of Los Angeles, California,
and graduated from El Camino Real High School in 1990. He studied filmmaking and English at Cal State Northridge[tpl]cite web | last = Boehm | first = Mike | title = A fanciful slice-of-life story | url = http://articles.latimes.com/2003/may/04/entertainment/ca-boehm4 | work = Los Angeles Times | date = May 4, 2003[/tpl] and graduated from the Yale School of Drama in 2004.[tpl]cite web | title = Yale Rep's next offering is a 'techno-comedy' by an alumnus | url = http://www.yale.edu/opa/arc-ybc/v33.n7/story7.html | work = Yale Bulletin & Calendar | publisher = Yale University | date = October 15, 2004[/tpl]
