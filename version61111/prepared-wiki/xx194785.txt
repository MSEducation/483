[[Luis Ossio]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1930 births, Vice Presidents of Bolivia, Christian Democratic Party (Bolivia) politicians

Luis Ossio Sanjinés (b. 1930 in Oruro) served as Vice President of Bolivia from 1989 to 1993, during the presidency of Jaime Paz Zamora.Vicepresidency of Bolivia He belonged to the Christian Democratic Party (in Spanish: Partido Demócrata Cristiano (PDC)) of Bolivia.
