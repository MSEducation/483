[[Middle Footscray railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1906

Middle Footscray railway station is located on the Sunbury line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the western Melbourne suburb of Footscray opening on 10 December 1906.Middle Footscray Vicsig
Two dual gauge tracks run north of the station forming the South Kensington - West Footscray freight line, linking the Port of Melbourne and other freight terminals to the rest of the state. The tracks also form part of the Melbourne - Sydney standard gauge line. The future Regional Rail Link lines run to the south of the station.Middle Footscray map Regional Rail Link
