[[Mount Smythe]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of Alberta, Winston Churchill Range

Mount Smythe is a mountain in Jasper National Park, Alberta, Canada. 
It is located in the Winston Churchill Range, [tpl]convert|2|km|mi|abbr=on[/tpl] southwest of Gong Peak and [tpl]convert|1|km|mi|abbr=on[/tpl] north of Mount Nelson. It reaches a height of [tpl]convert|3246|m|ft|abbr=on[/tpl]. 
The mountain was named after Francis Sydney Smythe, an international mountaineer who climbed in the Himalaya, Alps and the Canadian Rockies.
