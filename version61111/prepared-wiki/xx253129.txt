[[Torbeyevsky District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Mordovia

Torbeyevsky District ([tpl]lang-ru|Торбе́евский райо́н[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-mdf|Тарбеень район[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-myv|Торбейбуе[/tpl]) is an administrativeConstitution of the Republic of Mordovia and municipalLaw #127-Z district (raion), one of the twenty-two in the Republic of Mordovia, Russia. It is located in the southwest of the republic. The area of the district is [tpl]convert|1128.92|km2|sp=us[/tpl].[tpl]citation needed|date=October 2012[/tpl] Its administrative center is the urban locality (an urban-type settlement) of Torbeyevo.Law #7-Z Population: [tpl]ru-census2010|21,479|;[/tpl] 23,285 (2002 Census);[tpl]ru-pop-ref|2002Census[/tpl] [tpl]su-census1989|26,246|.[/tpl] The population of Torbeyevo accounts for 43.6% of the district's total population.
