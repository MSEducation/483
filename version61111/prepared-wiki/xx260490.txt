[[Kōsa ni Fukarete]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 singles, Shizuka Kudō songs, Oricon Weekly number-one singles, Songs written by Miyuki Nakajima, 1989 songs

"Kōsa ni Fukarete" is a song composed by the Japanese record producer/bassist Tsugutoshi Gotō. It was recorded by pop icon Shizuka Kudō and released as her eighth solo single, providing her with sixth chart-topper.[tpl]cite web| url=http://www7a.biglobe.ne.jp/~yamag/single/shizuka.html s-工藤静香| title= Yamachan Land (Japanese Chart Archives) - Singles Chart Daijiten - Shizuka Kudo|  publisher=Original Confidence| language=Japanese|date=December 30, 2007|accessdate=January 1, 2011[/tpl]
"Kōsa ni Fukarete" features lyrics written by Miyuki Nakajima, who has already contributed lyrics for Kudō's previous singles ("Fu-Ji-Tsu" and "Mugo,n...Iroppoi") and all songs of her second studio album Shizuka. It was featured on TV advertisement of audio cassette by Taiyo Yuden.
CD single was issued in September 1989. It debuted at the summit of the Oricon Weekly chart with sales of over 140,000 copies, becoming the 5th of Kudo's 8 consecutive #1 hit singles.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oricon.co.jp/search/result.php?kbn=js&types=rnk&sw=+&search_type=single&year=1989&month=9&week=3&web.x=43&web.y=6&web=Oricon+Search 1989年09月第3週の邦楽シングルランキング情報|title= Search results of the Japanese Oricon Weekly Singles Chart - 3rd week of September 1989|work=oricon.co.jp|language=Japanese|publisher=Oricon|accessdate=December 23, 2010[/tpl][tpl]cite web| url=http://www.oricon.co.jp/review/080820_01.html 音楽を語ろう特集| title=　音楽を語ろう特集『中島みゆきが詞・曲を提供！工藤静香の名曲を語ろう』 | language= Japanese|publisher= Oricon Style|work= oricon.co.jp| accessdate=December 23, 2010[/tpl]
