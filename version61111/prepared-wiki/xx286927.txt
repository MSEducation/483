[[Quriyeh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Quriyeh ([tpl]lang-fa|قوريه[/tpl], also Romanized as Qūrīyeh and Qowryeh; also known as Qoryeh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3080432|Quriyeh[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 124, in 40 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
