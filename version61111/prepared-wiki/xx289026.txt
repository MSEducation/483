[[Aji Kahriz]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Aji Kahriz ([tpl]lang-fa|اجي كهريز[/tpl], also Romanized as Ājī Kahrīz; also known as Hāji Kehīrīz and Ḩājjī Kahrīz)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3052124|Aji Kahriz[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 42, in 9 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
