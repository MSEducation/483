[[WVBN-LP]]

CATEGORIES: Channel 18 low-power TV stations in the United States, Television stations in Virginia, Television stations in the Hampton Roads market, Television channels and stations established in 2002, 2002 establishments in Virginia

WVBN-LP Channel 18 (Virginia Beach, Virginia) is a television station owned and operated by JBS Inc. It previously broadcast MTV2 (formerly The Box). WVBN-LP was on channel 19 until late 2002. It was owned by the Izzo Family Trust until JBS Inc took over in December 2002. Its signal is highly directional: all of its power is pointed towards the north and northwest to cover the northern half of Virginia Beach.
