[[Officer railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1881

Officer railway station is located on the Pakenham line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the south-eastern Melbourne suburb of Officer opening on 4 August 1881 as Officer's Siding being renamed Officer on 13 February 1899. On 13 May 1956 a new station opened east of the original.Officer Vicsig
In 2011/12, it was the 2nd least used station in Melbourne's metropolitan system, with 55,000 passenger movements.Station Patronage Research Public Transport Victoria

==Platforms & services==

Officer has two side platforms. It is serviced by Metro Trains' Pakenham line services.Pakenham Line timetable Public Transport Victoria
