[[Central European Gas Hub]]

CATEGORIES: Oil and gas companies of Austria, Natural gas trading hubs

Central European Gas Hub AG (CEGH, formerly known as Gas Hub Baumgarten) is a natural gas company in Austria.  It was founded in 2000.  CEGH is owned by  OMV Gas & Power GmbH (80%) and Wiener Börse AG (Vienna Stock Exchange) 20%[tpl]cite web|title=CEGH Shareholder Structure|url=http://www.cegh.at/index.php?id=34|publisher=Central European Gas Hub AG|accessdate=27 November 2011[/tpl]  It is operated as a subsidiary of OMV Gas International.
Central European Gas Hub provides a trading platform at the a gas hub in Baumgarten an der March, close to Slovakian and Hungarian borders. Approximately one third of all gas exports from Russia to Western Europe pass through the natural gas junction in Baumgarten. The Baumgarten physical gas hub is wholly owned by OMV.
In late 2008 a deal was going to make iCEGH's owners Gazprom (30%), OMV (30%), Centrex Europe Energy and Gas AG (20%) and Vienna Stock Exchange (20%).
