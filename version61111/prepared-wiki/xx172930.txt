[[Gyeongju Citizen FC]]

CATEGORIES: Challengers League clubs, Sport in North Gyeongsang Province, Gyeongju, 2007 establishments in South Korea, Association football clubs established in 2007

Gyeongju Citizen FC is a South Korean football club based in the city of Gyeongju. It is a member of the Challengers League, an amateur league and the third tier of league football in South Korea.
2008 will be its first season of participation in the Korean football league setup.
