[[Tamil Nadu Untouchability Eradication Front]]

CATEGORIES: Communist Party of India (Marxist), Political parties in Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu Untouchability Eradication Front (TNUEF) is the one of the mass front of Communist Party of India (Marxist). It works to eradicate the untouchability and other forms of caste oppression.
