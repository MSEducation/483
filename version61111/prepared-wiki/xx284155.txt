[[Ashthimki festival]]

CATEGORIES: Nepalese culture, Festivals

Ashthimki is a festival celebrated by the Tharu Community in the Western Part of Nepal.About Ashthimki Tharus prepare a picture on this day called as Ashtimki Chitra.
Tharu men and women take fast all day on this festival.Poisonous porridge claims three lives in Ashthimki
