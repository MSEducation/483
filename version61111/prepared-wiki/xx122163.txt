[[Preston-under-Scar]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in North Yorkshire, Civil parishes in North Yorkshire, Wensleydale

Preston-under-Scar is a village and civil parish in the Richmondshire district of North Yorkshire, England. It is situated approximately [tpl]convert|2|mi|km|0[/tpl] west from Leyburn. Village population is 120.
At the south of the village stands Bolton Hall, the seat of the Barons Bolton.
The Wensleydale Railway passes [tpl]convert|1|mi|km|1[/tpl] to the south. The railway station – Wensley station – is now in private hands.
There are remains of the old lead mining industry in the nearby Condenser Wood.
