[[Tai Po Methodist School]]

CATEGORIES: Primary schools in Hong Kong, Christian schools in Hong Kong

Tai Po Methodist School (Traditional Chinese: 大埔循道衛理小學; Jyutping: daai6 bou3 ceon4 dou6 wai6 lei5 siu2 hok6), which was founded in 1992, is the first Protestant, full-time primary school belonging to the Methodist Church, Hong Kong. There are 30 classes in total, and about 1,000 students and 47 teachers. Miss (In Traditional Chinese: 歐陽美嬋) is the current principal.
