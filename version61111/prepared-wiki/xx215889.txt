[[Ali Ahmed (archer)]]

CATEGORIES: 1973 births, Living people, Qatari archers, Archers at the 2008 Summer Olympics, Olympic archers of Qatar

Ali Ahmed Salem Al-Yazidi (born March 30, 1973) is an athlete from Qatar, who competes in archery.

==2008 Summer Olympics==

At the 2008 Summer Olympics in Beijing Salem finished his ranking round with a total of 627 points, which gave him the 57th seed for the final competition bracket in which he faced Im Dong-Hyun in the first round. Im won the match by 108-103 and Salem was eliminated. Im would lose in the third round against Vic Wunderle.Athlete biography: Ali Salem, beijing2008.cn, ret: Aug, 19 2008
