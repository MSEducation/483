[[Maurice Girardot]]

CATEGORIES: 1921 births, Possibly living people, French basketball players, Olympic basketball players of France, Basketball players at the 1948 Summer Olympics, Olympic silver medalists for France, Olympic medalists in basketball

Maurice Girardot (born December 22, 1921) was a French basketball player. He competed in the 1948 Summer Olympics.
Girardot was part of the French basketball team, which won the silver medal.
