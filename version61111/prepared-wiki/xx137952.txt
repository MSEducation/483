[[Governor Francisco Gabrielli International Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in Argentina, Mendoza, Argentina, Buildings and structures in Mendoza Province

Governor Francisco Gabrielli International Airport ([tpl]lang-es|Aeropuerto Internacional Gobernador Francisco Gabrielli[/tpl]) [tpl]airport codes|MDZ|SAME[/tpl], better known as El Plumerillo International Airport, is located [tpl]convert|8|km|mi[/tpl] northeast of the centre of Mendoza, a city in the Mendoza Province of Argentina. The airport covers an area of [tpl]convert|490|ha[/tpl] and is operated by Aeropuertos Argentina 2000 S.A.
The terminal building is [tpl]convert|12800|sqm[/tpl] and has two levels.  There are 161 parking spaces at the airport, one infrequently used arrival/departure lounge, and 4 gates.
