[[Pioneer, Tasmania]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Tasmania, North East Tasmania

Pioneer is a village in north-east Tasmania, Australia.  It is at the junction of Gladstone Road and Racecourse Road.  Tebrakunna Road heads out to the east.  Other hamlets in the area are Herrick and Winnaleah. At the 2006 census, Pioneer had a population of 144.[tpl]Census 2006 AUS|id=SSC65931|name=Pioneer (State Suburb)|accessdate=22 November 2011|quick=on[/tpl]
