[[Llanos del Caudillo]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities in the Province of Ciudad Real, Populated places in the Province of Ciudad Real

Llanos del Caudillo is a municipality in Ciudad Real, Castile-La Mancha, Spain. It has a population of 692.

==History==

Llanos del Caudillo was founded by colonist families in the 1950s,Decreto del Ministerio de la Gobernación del 6 de julio del año 1956 subsidized by the Instituto Nacional de Colonización of the Spanish government. It was first established as an Entidad Local (Minor Local Entity) carved out of Manzanares municipal term.
