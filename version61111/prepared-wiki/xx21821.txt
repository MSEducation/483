[[Badakhshan]]

CATEGORIES: Regions of Afghanistan, Geography of Tajikistan

Badakhshan (Pashto/[tpl]lang-fa| بدخشان[/tpl], [tpl]lang-tg|Бадахшон[/tpl], [tpl]Lang-ru|Бадахшан[/tpl], [tpl]zh|t=巴達克山|s=[/tpl], meaning "Badakh Mountains") is a historic region comprising parts of what is now northeastern Afghanistan and southeastern Tajikistan. The name is retained in  Badakhshan Province is one of the 34 provinces of Afghanistan, And is located in North-East Afghanistan. Much of historic Badakhshan lies within Tajikistan's Gorno-Badakhshan Autonomous Province located in the in south-eastern part of the country.  The music of Badakhshan is an important part of the region's cultural heritage.

==People==

Badakhshan has a diverse ethno-linguistic and religious community. Tajiks and Uzbeks are the majority while a minority of Kyrgyzs also live there. There are also groups of speakers of several Pamir languages of the Eastern Iranian language group.G. The Pamiri people were not officially recognized as a separate ethnic group in Tajikistan, but in Tajikistan Pamiri movements and associations have been formed.[ref]
