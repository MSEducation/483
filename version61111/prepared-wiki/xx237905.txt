[[Shizhong District, Jinan]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Shandong, Jinan

Shizhong District ([tpl]zh|c=[tpl]linktext|市|中|区[/tpl]|p=[tpl]linktext|Shì|zhōng| Qū[/tpl]|l=city center district[/tpl]) is a district of the Chinese province of Shandong. It is part of the urban center of the subprovincial town of Jinan. It is located to the southwest of the historical city center. It borders Lixia District to the east, Tianqiao District to the north, Huaiyin District to the west, and Licheng District to the south.
