[[Syrian Telecom]]

CATEGORIES: Telecommunications companies of Syria, Internet service providers of Syria, Government-owned companies of Syria, Companies based in Damascus

Syrian Telecom (officially: Syrian Telecommunications Establishment) ([tpl]lang-ar|الإتصالات السورية[/tpl]) is a telecommunications company in Syria.  The company is affiliated with the government of Syria. The twelve service providers in Syria run through two AS's, both of which are owned by Syrian Telecom (AS29256 and AS29386).http://www.eng.tau.ac.il/~noa/publications/SZ12_Arabian.pdf Baker Baker has served as Director General and CEO for Syria Telecom for several years.http://www.itu.int/ITU-D/membership/portal/index.asp?Name=880 Their partners include ST-Samsung of Korea, a technology solutions provider to Syria, Iraq, and Sudan.http://www.st-samsung.com/http://www.st-samsung.com/profile.pdf Since 2006, agreements for wireless systems such as the INTRACOM IAS-W have been acquired through international agreements with foreign hardware suppliers.http://www.intracom-telecom.com/en/news/press/press2006/2006_5_11.htm
