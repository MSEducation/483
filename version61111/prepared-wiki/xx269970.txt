Jeffrey Mallen Christiaens (born 17 May 1991) is  a Belgian–Filipino footballer who mainly plays as a winger for Filipino side Global FC.  He also represents the Philippines at international level.

==Career==

===Club===

Christiaens started his youth career at Ruiselede before moving on to Deinze and eventually Club Brugge.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.kmtorhout.be/index.php?option=com_joomsport&task=player&id=92&sid=2&Itemid=2 |title=Jeffrey Christiaens - Speler |work=KMTorhout.be |publisher=Torhout 1992 Koninklijke Maatschappij  |accessdate=2011-09-24 [/tpl]  Towards the end of the 2010–11 season, he was released by Club Brugge and signed for Belgian Third Division side Torhout.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.footgoal.net/suite.php?selection=66847 |title=Officiel : Christiaens à Torhout |publisher=Foot Goal |date=2011-04-07 |accessdate=2011-09-24 [/tpl]  He made his senior debut on 17 August 2011 in the 1–1 home draw against Géants Athois, coming on as a substitute in 80th minute.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.soccerway.com/matches/2011/08/17/belgium/third-division/km-torhout-1992/royal-gaants-athois/1146920/ |title=Torhout vs. Géants Athois |publisher=Soccer Way |accessdate=2011-09-24 [/tpl]  He made his first start and scored his first goal for the club in his second appearance in the 2–2 away draw to Royal Mouscron-Péruwelz.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.soccerway.com/matches/2011/08/24/belgium/third-division/rrc-peruwelz/km-torhout-1992/1146932/ |title=Mouscron-Péruwelz vs. Torhout |publisher=Soccer Way |accessdate=2011-09-24 [/tpl]
On 19 November 2011, he made an appearance for Filipino side Global FC in a round of 16 match in the 2011 United Football Cup against the Philippine Army.  They won 3–0 and progressed to the quarter-finals.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.interaksyon.com/interaktv/defending-ufl-cup-champs-global-smashes-army |title=Defending UFL Cup champs Global slashes Army |author=Olivares, Rick |publisher=Associated Broadcasting Company (TV5) |date=2011-11-20 |accessdate=2011-11-21 [/tpl]  However, there were no reports indicating that there was any deal in place which would see him play for Global FC.  It would be his only appearance for the club before returning to Torhout.  Upon his return, he would make a further four appearances and finishing the 2011–12 season with 13 appearances.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.soccerway.com/players/jeffrey-christiaens/203240/ |title=Jeffrey Christiaens profile |work=Soccer Way |publisher=Global Sports Media |accessdate=2012-06-01 [/tpl]
In the summer of 2012, he moved to the Philippines and signed for Global FC.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.gmanetwork.com/news/story/271015/sports/football/growth-of-phl-football-as-seen-at-the-grassroots-and-in-ufl |title=Growth of PHL football as seen at the grassroots and in UFL |author=Moore, Roy |work=GMA News |location=Philippines |date=2012-08-24 |accessdate=2012-09-10 |quote=Jeffrey Christiaens and Denis Wolf have already signed with Global, Stallion took on Jason De Jong and OJ Porteria, while loaning in Spanish duo Rufo and Joaco from Inter De Madrid. ===International===

In August 2011, the team manager of the Philippines national team traveled to Europe to scout for potential talent.  Three players that were tapped were Belgian–Filipinos which included Christiaens.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.manilastandardtoday.com/insideSports.htm?f=2011/september/8/sports2.isx&d=2011/september/8 |title=Fil-Spanish booter impresses coach Weiss |newspaper=Manila Standard Today |author=Atencio, Peter |date=2011-09-08 |accessdate=2011-09-24 [/tpl]  They traveled to the Philippines in early September to try out for the Philippines national under-23 team that will be competing at the 2011 Southeast Asian Games.  Christiaens in particular was able to impress sufficiently that he was also named in the senior national team's 20-man squad for the 2011 Long Teng Cup.[tpl]cite news |url=http://sports.inquirer.net/17327/new-azkals-team-join-long-teng-cup-tilt#_ |title=New Azkals team join Long Teng Cup tilt |author=Tupas, Cedelf P. |newspaper=Philippine Daily Inquirer |date=2011-09-21 |accessdate=2011-09-24 [/tpl]
