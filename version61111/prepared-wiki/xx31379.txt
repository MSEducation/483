[[Aello]]

CATEGORIES: Greek mythology, Legendary birds

Aello ([tpl]IPAc-en|ˈ|eɪ|ə|l|oʊ[/tpl]; Ancient Greek: Ἀελλώ, Aellō, "whirlwind") in Greek mythology was one of the Harpy sisters who would abduct people and torture them on their way to Tartarus. She is also referred to as:
It's claimed she is the mother of Achilles's immortal steeds Balius (Balios) and Xanthus (Xanthos) by Zephyrus, but some sources claim it was really her sister Celaeno. 
She is sometimes confused with Aella the Amazon.
