[[Platyla polita]]

CATEGORIES: Platyla, Animals described in 1840

Platyla polita is a species of very small land snail with an operculum, a terrestrialgastropod mollusk or micromollusk in the family Aciculidae.

==Distribution==

This species is found in the Czech Republic, Poland, the Netherlands, Slovakia, UkraineBalashov I. & Gural-Sverlova N. 2012. An annotated checklist of the terrestrial molluscs of Ukraine. Journal of Conchology. 41 (1): 91-109. and others.
