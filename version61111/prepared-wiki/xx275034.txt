==Aftershock==

A strong aftershock occurred on July 18, 1928 at 14:05 local time (19:05 UTC)."Historia de los Sismos mas Notables Occurridos en El Peru (1513 - 1974)" (PDF). (Spanish). Some houses which had already been damaged in the main shock collapsed.
