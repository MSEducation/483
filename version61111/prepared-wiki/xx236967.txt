Calhoun County High School is a public secondary school in St. Matthews, South Carolina, USA.

==Campus==

The campus has a pasture on the right, with the new K-8 school in front is it on Saints Avenue.

==Extracurricular activities==

The school's sports teams, known as the Saints, compete in the South Carolina High School League Class A, Region V. They field teams in baseball, basketball, football, softball, track, and volleyball.
