[[1984 Florida Federal Open]]

CATEGORIES: Eckerd Open, 1984 in Florida, 1984 Virginia Slims World Championship Series, 20th century in Tampa, Florida, Sports in Tampa, Florida

The 1984 Florida Federal Open  was a tennis tournament played on outdoor hard courts in Tampa, Florida in the United States that was part of the 1984 Virginia Slims World Championship Series. The tournament was held from October 8 through October 14, 1984.
