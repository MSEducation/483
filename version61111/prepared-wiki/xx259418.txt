[[Alyaksandr Krakushyn]]

CATEGORIES: 1991 births, Living people, Belarusian footballers, FC Torpedo-BelAZ Zhodino players

Alyaksandr Krakushyn ([tpl]lang-be|Аляксандр Кракушын[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-ru|Александр Крякушин[/tpl]; born 15 March 1991) is a retired Belarusian professional football player. He had to retire from professional football in late 2010 at the age of 19 due to health issues.
