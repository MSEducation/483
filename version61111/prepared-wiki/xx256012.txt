[[Lian Lunson]]

CATEGORIES: Australian actresses, Australian expatriate actresses in the United States, Australian filmmakers, Australian film directors, Living people

Lian Lunson is an Australian actress who became an award-winning documentary filmmaker. She has also written a novel.

==Biography==

She was born in Ned Kelly country in Australia.  
She moved to Los Angeles in 1987 after working as an actress in Australia.
Her production company is based in LA and is called Horse Pictures.
She made a documentary in 1997 about Willie Nelson for the TV channel PBS.
She also made a documentary about the poet and singer/songwriter Leonard Cohen, called Leonard Cohen: I'm Your Man.
In 2006, she was awarded the Women in Film Dorothy Arzner Directors Award for her work on this film.http://wif.org/past-recipients 
She has a feature film in pre-production based on her own novel. The film is called The Boom Boom Room.
