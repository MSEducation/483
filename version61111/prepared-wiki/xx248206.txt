[[Kokiborou]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Alibori Department, Arrondissements of Benin

Kokiborou is a town and arrondissement in the Alibori Department of northeastern Benin. It is an administrative division under the jurisdiction of the commune of Banikoara. According to the population census conducted by the Institut National de la Statistique Benin on February 15, 2002, the arrondissement had a total population of 3,790.Institut National de la Statistique Benin, accessed by Geohive, accessed January 8, 2010
