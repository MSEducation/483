[[Kalhin]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Kalhin ([tpl]lang-fa|كلهين[/tpl], also Romanized as Kalhīn)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3771562|Kalhin[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Pain Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 115, in 26 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
