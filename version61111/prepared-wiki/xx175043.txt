[[KGCC]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Wyoming

For the airport with ICAO code KGCC, see: Gillette-Campbell County Airport in Gillette, Wyoming, United States.

KGCC (99.9 FM) is a radio station licensed to Gillette, Wyoming, USA. The station is currently owned by Keyhole Broadcasting, LLC.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KGCC |title=KGCC Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==History==

The station was assigned the call letters KXXL on 2002-10-25. On 2008-06-11, the station changed its call sign to the current KGCC.[tpl]cite web|url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=88381&Callsign=KGCC |title=KGCC Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
