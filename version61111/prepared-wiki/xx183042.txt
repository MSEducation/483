[[Panther Lick]]

CATEGORIES: Geography of Marion County, West Virginia, Rivers of West Virginia

Panther Lick is a hollow located near Grant Town, West Virginia, United States.  Approximately [tpl]convert|2.5|mi[/tpl] in length, the hollow runs from the Paw Paw Creek Valley in Grant Town to the Little Paw Paw Creek valley near McCurdysville. The valley is drained by Panther Lick Run, emptying into Paw Paw Creek.
