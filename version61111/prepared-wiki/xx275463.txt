[[Liang Wengen]]

CATEGORIES: Chinese billionaires, Living people, People's Republic of China politicians from Hunan, Sany Group, Businesspeople from Hunan, People from Loudi, Communist Party of China politicians from Hunan

Liang Wengen ([tpl]zh|c= 梁稳根|p=Liáng Wěngēn|links=no[/tpl]) is a Chinese entrepreneur in the fields of heavy manufacturing, currently the founder and chairman of the Sany Heavy Industry.In 2011 he became the richest man of mainland China, and tapped to become member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, with a net worth of about US$ 8 billion. However he lost that position, and as of March 2013 he was the third richest person in China, with a net worth of about US$ 7.6 billion. http://www.forbes.com/profile/wengen-liang/, The profile of Liang Genwen on Forbes, March 2013.

==Biography==

He was born to a poor peasant family in the Hunan province in 1956.http://www.chinadaily.com.cn/china/2012cpc/2012-11/12/content_15919473.htm, China Daily, "Entrepreneurs' presence grows at CPC congress", updated 2012.http://www.baike.com/wiki/%E6%A2%81%E7%A8%B3%E6%A0%B9, profile of Liang Wengen on Baike, accessed on 2013.Liang used to work as a top manager at a state arms plant, before getting involved in the construction-equipment industry. http://www.forbes.com/profile/wengen-liang/, The profile of Liang Genwen on Forbes, March 2013.
He is the founder and main shareholder of Sany Group, a heavy industry manufacturer based in Changsha, Hunan Province.
His personal wealth is variously estimated at USD $8 billion, $9 billion, or $11 billion.
"My property, even my life, belongs to the (Chinese Communist) Party", he answered journalists' question about the conflict between the interests of the Party and himself as a businessman.Entrepreneurs' presence grows at CPC congress |Latest News |chinadaily.com.cn, China Daily

==Personal life==

Liang  has a Bachelor of Arts degree from Central South University. He is married and has one child. He resides in Changsha.
