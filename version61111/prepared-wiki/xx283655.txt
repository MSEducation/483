[[Shenley Hall]]

CATEGORIES: Country houses in Hertfordshire, Grade II listed buildings in Hertfordshire

Shenley Hall is a Grade II listed country house at Shenley in Hertfordshire.

==History==

Built in the 19th century, the hall benefited from additions by Sir Edwin Lutyens in 1914 just before World War I.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.britishlistedbuildings.co.uk/en-164334-shenley-hall-shenley-hertfordshire|title=Shenley Hall, Shenley|publisher=British Listed Buildings|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] The hall featured in several episodes of The Avengers in the 1960s.[tpl]cite web|url=http://theavengers.tv/forever/locations-10.htm|title=The Avengers for Ever|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] Now owned by a transport software business,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.roadtech.co.uk/|title=Road Tech|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] it is set in 16 acres of parkland[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.blueskyaircon.co.uk/case-studies/|title=Case study: Shenley Hall|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] and is Grade II listed.
