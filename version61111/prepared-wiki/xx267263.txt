[[Emas bond]]

CATEGORIES: Malay words and phrases

An Emas bond or sukuk (Islamic bond) is a non-Ringgit denominated bond or sukuk issued out of Malaysia.[tpl]cite web|url=http://biz.thestar.com.my/news/story.asp?file=/2009/8/11/business/4493291&sec=business|title=Central Bank: Right time for Govt to issue Emas bond|publisher=The Star|accessdate=23 September 2011[/tpl] "Emas" is the Malay word for gold which symbolizes universal value and security.

==References==

"Emas" is Malaysia's designation accorded to non-Ringgit bonds and sukuk issued in Malaysia in the global capital market. "Emas" is the Malay word for gold which symbolizes universal value and security.
