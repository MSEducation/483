[[Our First President's Quickstep]]

CATEGORIES: Songs of the American Civil War, 1861 songs

"Our First President's Quickstep" is a song written by P. Rivinac and published by Blackmar & Bros, a company based out of Augusta, Georgia. The song celebrates President of the Confederacy Jefferson Davis and was published the year he became President, 1861.Abel, pgs. 98-99Holzer et al., pg. 13
