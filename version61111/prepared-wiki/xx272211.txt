[[Earl, Wisconsin]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Washburn County, Wisconsin, Unincorporated communities in Wisconsin

Earl is an unincorporated community located in the town of Springbrook, Washburn County, Wisconsin, United States. Earl is located at the junction of U.S. Route 63 and County Highway E [tpl]convert|8.5|mi|km[/tpl] northeast of Spooner; the Wisconsin Great Northern Railroad runs through the community.[tpl]cite map |publisher=Wisconsin Department of Transportation |title=Washburn County |url=http://dot.wisconsin.gov/travel/maps/docs/counties/washburn.pdf |format= |edition= |year=2011 |cartography= |scale= |series= |page= |section= |inset= |accessdate=September 25, 2011 |isbn= |id= |ref= [/tpl] Earl had a post office, which opened on August 30, 1905, and closed on July 1, 1984.[tpl]cite web|title=Postmaster Finder - Post Offices by ZIP Code|url=http://webpmt.usps.gov/pmt008.cfm|publisher=United States Postal Service|accessdate=September 25, 2011[/tpl]
