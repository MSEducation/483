[[Urugi, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Urugi|売木村|Urugi-mura[/tpl] is a village located in Shimoina District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the village has an estimated population of 711 and a density of 16.33 persons per km². The total area is 43.55 km².
