[[Afropsipyla similis]]

CATEGORIES: Animals described in 1994, Phycitinae

Afropsipyla similis is a species of snout moths in the genus Afropsipyla. It was described by Balinsky in 1994, and is known from South Africa (it was described from Johannesburg).[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.afromoths.net/species/show/40395 |title=Afro Moths |publisher=Afro Moths |date= |accessdate=2011-09-29[/tpl]
