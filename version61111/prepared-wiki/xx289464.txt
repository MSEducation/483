[[Emam Kahriz]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Emam Kahriz ([tpl]lang-fa|امام كهريز[/tpl], also Romanized as Emām Kahrīz) is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 225, in 43 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
