[[Gmina Kłaj]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Lesser Poland Voivodeship, Wieliczka County

Gmina Kłaj is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Wieliczka County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. Its seat is the village of Kłaj, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|17|km|mi|0[/tpl] east of Wieliczka and [tpl]convert|28|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] east of the regional capital Kraków.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|83.1|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 9,832.

==Villages==

Gmina Kłaj contains the villages and settlements of Brzezie, Dąbrowa, Grodkowice, Gruszki, Kłaj, Łężkowice, Łysokanie, Szarów and Targowisko.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Kłaj is bordered by the gminas of Bochnia, Drwinia, Niepołomice and Gdów.
