[[Matthew Smith (Irish politician)]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing, 1955 deaths, Fianna Fáil politicians, Members of the 8th Seanad, People from County Dublin

Matthew Smith (died 3 November 1955) was an Irish Fianna Fáil politician. He was a member of Seanad Éireann from 1954 to 1957. He was elected to the 8th Seanad in 1954 by the Industrial and Commercial Panel.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oireachtas.ie/members-hist/default.asp?housetype=1&HouseNum=8&MemberID=1682&ConstID=207|title=Mr. Matthew Smith|work=Oireachtas Members Database|accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl] He died in office in 1955.
He had previously stood unsuccessfully for Dáil Éireann as a Fianna Fáil candidate for the Dún Laoghaire and Rathdown constituency at the 1951 general election.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.electionsireland.org/candidate.cfm?ID=2418|title=Matthew Smith|work=ElectionsIreland.org|accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl]
