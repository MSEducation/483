[[Alaa Abdul-Hussein]]

CATEGORIES: Iraqi footballers, People from Basra, Iraqi expatriate footballers, Living people, 1986 births

 Alaa Abdul-Hussein  ([tpl]lang-ar|علاء عبدالحسين[/tpl]) (born October 7, 1986 in Basra, Iraq) is an Iraqi football player who currently plays for Arbil FC in Iraq.
