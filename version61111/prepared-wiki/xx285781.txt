==Film adaptation==

Deadline reported on August 10, 2010 that actress Frances McDormand has bought the rights of the 2004 crime novel Every Secret Thing written by Laura Lippman, she'll produce the film with her partner Anthony Bregman.[tpl]cite news|title=Frances McDormand Playing Producer Role|url=http://www.deadline.com/2010/08/frances-mcdormand-playing-producer-role/|accessdate=12 August 2013|newspaper=deadline.com|date=10 August 2010[/tpl] On July 31, 2012 Variety posted the news that Nicole Holofcener has written the script for the film and Amy J. Berg is set to direct the film to make her directorial debut.[tpl]cite news|title=‘West of Memphis’ helmer to adapt ‘Secret Thing’|url=http://variety.com/2012/film/news/west-of-memphis-helmer-to-adapt-secret-thing-1118057277/|accessdate=12 August 2013|newspaper=variety.com|date=31 July 2012[/tpl]
