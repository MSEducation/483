[[Aylan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Aylan ([tpl]lang-fa|ايلن[/tpl], also Romanized as Āylan) is a village in Chaypareh-ye Bala Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 90, in 15 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
