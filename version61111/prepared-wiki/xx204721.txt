[[NATI (motorcycle)]]

CATEGORIES: Motorcycle manufacturers of the Soviet Union, Motorcycle manufacturers of Russia

NATI (НАТИ) Nauchnyj avtotraktornyj institut (Научный автотракторный институт) was a manufacturer of motorcycles in the Soviet Union from 1931 -1933. It built the NATI-A-750, designed by Soviet engineer Pyotr Mozharov, prior to the transfer of manufacturing to PMZ.
