William Danforth Mulliken (born August 27, 1939) is an American former competition swimmer and Olympic champion.  He competed at the 1960 Olympic Games in Rome, Italy, where he received a gold medal for winning the men's 200-meter breaststroke."1960 Summer Olympics – Rome, Italy– Swimming" – databaseOlympics.com (Retrieved on April 30, 2008)
