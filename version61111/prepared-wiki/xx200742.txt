[[WAUF-LP]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Alabama, Christian contemporary radio stations, Low-power FM radio stations in Alabama, Lee County, Alabama, Radio stations established in 2004

WAUF-LP (104.5 FM, "The Core 104") is a radio station licensed to serve Auburn, Alabama.  The station is owned by Core Radio Ministry, Inc. It airs a Christian Contemporary radio format targeted at teens, college students, and young adults.[tpl]cite web |work=Alabama Broadcast Media Page |title=Alabama LPFM & Translators |url=http://bellsouthpwp2.net/c/r/crackedlcd/almediapage/lpfm.html |accessdate=2008-05-21[/tpl][tpl]cite web |title=Station Information Profile |work=Arbitron |url=http://www.arbitron.com/[/tpl]
The station was assigned the WAUF-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on August 13, 2004.[tpl]cite web |title=Call Sign History |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=133684&Callsign=WAUF-LP |work=FCC Media Bureau CDBS Public Access Database[/tpl]
