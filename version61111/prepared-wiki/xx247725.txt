[[Power Move]]

CATEGORIES: Screaming Females albums, Don Giovanni Records albums, 2009 albums

Power Move is the third full-length album by the Screaming Females, however it was their first on Don Giovanni Records and their first not to be self-released.

==Track listing==

All songs written by Screaming Females (Jarrett Dougherty, King Mike, Marissa Paternoster).
