[[Mossie Lawlor]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 births, Living people, Irish rugby union players, Munster Rugby players, Rugby union wings, Rugby union centres, Shannon RFC players

Mossie Lawlor born 5 April 1980 is a rugby union player who currently plays for UL Bohemians R.F.C.. He previously played for Munster in the Magners League and Heineken Cup, and also played two games on a trial period for London Wasps during 2008, in the Guinness Premiership. He can play at 10, 11, 12, 13, 14 or 15.
