[[Lake Lorelei, Ohio]]

CATEGORIES: Lakes of Ohio, Census-designated places in Ohio, Census-designated places in Brown County, Ohio

Lake Lorelei is a gated community and census-designated place in Brown County, Ohio, United States, near the village of Fayetteville.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.lakeloreleiohio.com/|title=Lake Lorelei|work=Lake Lorelei|accessdate=2009-06-20[/tpl][tpl]gnis|2628918[/tpl] As of the 2010 census, it had a population of 1,170.[tpl]cite web| url=http://factfinder2.census.gov/bkmk/table/1.0/en/DEC/10_DP/G001/1600000US3941419| title=Geographic Identifiers: 2010 Demographic Profile Data (G001): Lake Lorelei CDP, Ohio| publisher=U.S. Census Bureau, American Factfinder| accessdate=February 21, 2014[/tpl]

==Geography==

Lake Lorelei is located in northern Brown County, in the western part of Perry Township. It consists of a housing development built around Lake Lorelei, a reservoir on Glady Run, a tributary of the Little Miami River. 
Ohio State Route 131 forms the southern border of the CDP, and U.S. Route 50 touches the community's southeast corner. Lake Lorelei is [tpl]convert|3|mi|0[/tpl] west of the village of Fayetteville and [tpl]convert|34|mi[/tpl] east of downtown Cincinnati.
