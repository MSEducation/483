[[HMS Sparham (M2731)]]

CATEGORIES: Ham-class minesweepers, Portsmouth-built ships, 1954 ships, Cold War minesweepers of the United Kingdom, Royal Navy ship names, Ham-class minesweepers of the French Navy

HMS ''Sparham'' was a [tpl]sclass2|Ham|minesweeper||ship[/tpl] of the Royal Navy.
Their names were all chosen from villages ending in -ham. The minesweeper was named after Sparham in Norfolk.
