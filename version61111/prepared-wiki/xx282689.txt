[[Alex Kenyon]]

CATEGORIES: 1992 births, Living people, English footballers, Association football midfielders, Chorley F.C. players, Lancaster City F.C. players, Stockport County F.C. players, Morecambe F.C. players, The Football League players, Football Conference players

Alex Kenyon (born 17 July 1992) is an English footballer who plays for Morecambe.

==Career==

Kenyon began his career with Everton before being released in the summer of 2008 and he then joined non-league Chorley.[tpl]cite web|title=Kenyon rewarded with Morecambe deal|url=http://www.chorley-guardian.co.uk/sport/football/kenyon-rewarded-with-morecambe-deal-1-5815406|work=Chorley Guardian|accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl] Kenyon then joined Lancaster City in the summer of 2009 before signing for Conference Premier side Stockport County in May 2012.[tpl]cite web|title=Lancaster’s Alex Kenyon joins Stockport County|url=http://www.lancasterguardian.co.uk/sport/lancaster-s-alex-kenyon-joins-stockport-county-1-4561759|work=Lancaster Guardian|accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl] After suffering relegation with County in 2012–13 he joined League Two side Morecambe on 27 June 2013.[tpl]cite web|title=Morecambe sign Alex Kenyon after Stockport's relegation|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/23081953|work=BBC Sport|accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl] He made his Football League debut on 10 August 2013 in a 1–1 draw with Torquay United.[tpl]cite web|title=Morecambe 1-1 Torquay|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/23558610|work=BBC Sport|accessdate=10 August 2013[/tpl] He signed a new contract in March 2014.[tpl]cite web|title=Morecambe midfielder Kenyon signs new deal|url=http://www.thevisitor.co.uk/sport/football/morecambe-fc/morecambe-midfielder-kenyon-signs-new-deal-1-6458421|work=The Visitor|accessdate=4 March 2014[/tpl]
