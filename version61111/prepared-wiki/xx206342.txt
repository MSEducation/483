[[Bennington Street Burying Ground]]

CATEGORIES: Cemeteries in Boston, Massachusetts, Cemeteries on the National Register of Historic Places in Massachusetts, East Boston, Massachusetts

Bennington Street Burying Ground is a historic cemetery on Bennington Street, between Swift St. and Harmony St., in East Boston, Massachusetts.
The cemetery was established in 1838.[tpl]cite book |last=Lambert |first=David Allen |authorlink= |title=A Guide to Massachusetts Cemeteries |url=http://www.books.google.com/books?isbn=0880822228 |year=2002 |publisher=New England Historic Genealogical Society |location= |isbn=0880821345 |page=27 [/tpl]  It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 2002.
The professional baseball player Red Woodhead was interred at the cemetery in 1881.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.baseball-reference.com/players/w/woodhre01.shtml |title=Red Woodhead Statistics and History |author= |date= |work= |publisher=Baseball Reference |accessdate=March 19, 2013[/tpl]
