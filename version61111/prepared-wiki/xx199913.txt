[[Pirlimycin]]

CATEGORIES: Lincosamide antibiotics, Veterinary drugs

Pirlimycin hydrochloride belongs to the lincosamide class of antimicrobials. Under the trade name Pirsue,Pirsue Sterile Solution it is used in the treatment of mastitis in cattle.

==Activity==

Pirlimycin is active against Gram-positive bacteria, specifically Staphylococcus aureus and coagulase negative species of Staphylococcus and Streptococcus. It has no activity against Gram-negative bacteria.USP: Pirlimycin

==Mechanism of action==

It is bacteriostatic and acts by inhibiting bacterial protein synthesis via binding with the 50S subunit of the ribosome.
