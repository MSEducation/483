[[Teo Nie Ching]]

CATEGORIES: Malaysian politicians, Living people, Members of the Dewan Rakyat, People from Batu Pahat, Democratic Action Party (Malaysia) politicians, 1981 births, People from Johor

Teo Nie Ching ([tpl]zh|c=张念群[/tpl]; born 1981) is a politician, currently the Member of Parliament for Kulai. She is also holding the position as the Assistant National Publicity Secretary of the Democratic Action Party (DAP), a democratic socialist opposition party in Malaysia.
Teo comes from a political family. Her father was the secretary of DAP Labis Parliamentary Constituency Campaign Committee, while her sister and brother-in-law are the current Chief and Deputy Chair of the Dapsy Johor State Council.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.wretch.cc/blog/teonieching/17800 |title=认识张念群 | date=2008-02-17|accessdate=2010-01-10[/tpl]
Teo completed her secondary school education in Chinese High School, Batu Pahat, Johor. She pursued her tertiary education under a twinning programme and obtained her law degree from the University of the West of England, in 2002. After graduating from the university, she spent one year to work as a librarian in UK and travel around Europe. In 2004, she received her Certificate in Legal Practice from the University of Malaya.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.wretch.cc/blog/teonieching/21272298 |title=Off The Edge - Boldly Going: Is young DAP set for uncharted territory? | date=2010-01-10|accessdate=2010-01-10[/tpl]
In the 2008 general election, Teo ran for the Parliamentary seat of Serdang on a DAP ticket.[tpl]cite news | title="沙登小辣椒"首度亮相‧張念群為捍衛民主參選 | url=http://www.sinchew-i.com/sciWWW/node/6799?tid=19|work=Sinchew|publisher=Sinchew Online| date=2008-02-18|accessdate=2010-01-10[/tpl] She won against the candidate from Barisan Nasional, Datuk Hoh Hee Lee with a majority of 21,025 votes. She obtained 47,444 votes comparing to her opposing candidate who only bagged 26,419 votes.[tpl]cite news | title=General Election 2008 Results | url=http://elections2.thestar.com.my/election/results/08/08p.html|work=TheStar|publisher=TheStar Online| date=2008-03-09|accessdate=2010-01-10[/tpl]
In the 2013 general election, Teo contests in BN's stronghold Kulai for the Parliamentary seat and won against the candidate from Barisan Nasional, Tay Chin Hein. She obtained 43,338 with a majority of 13,450.[tpl]cite web|title=PRU13 Keputusan Terkini|url=http://keputusan.spr.gov.my/#parlimen/1/P.163|accessdate=7 May 2013[/tpl]
