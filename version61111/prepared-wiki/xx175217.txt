[[KJYE]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Colorado, Christian radio stations in Colorado, Moody Radio affiliate stations, Radio stations established in 1986, 1986 establishments in Colorado

KJYE (1400 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Religious format.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www1.arbitron.com/sip/sip.do#access |date=Summer 2009 |title=Station Information Profile |publisher=Arbitron |accessdate=2009-10-13[/tpl] Licensed to Delta, Colorado, USA, KJYE relays the programming of KJOL and is currently owned by United Ministries. [tpl]cite web |url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=KJYE |title=KJYE Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division |accessdate=2009-10-13[/tpl]

==History==

The station was assigned the call letters KPLG on March 1, 1986. On January 15, 1988, the station changed its call sign to KDTA. On September 21, 2012, the station changed its call sign to the current KJYE.
