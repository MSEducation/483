[[HMCS Granby (J264)]]

CATEGORIES: Bangor-class minesweepers of the Royal Canadian Navy, Ships built in Canada, 1942 ships, World War II minesweepers of Canada

HMCS ''Granby'' (J264) was a [tpl]sclass|Bangor|minesweeper||ship[/tpl] that served in the Royal Canadian Navy during World War II. She was commissioned on 2 May 1942 and paid off on 31 July 1945.[tpl]cite web|title= Ship History | url=http://www.readyayeready.com/ships/shipview.php?id=1161&ship=GRANBY|work=Ready Aye Ready|accessdate=18 Sep 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title= Ship History 2 | url=http://www.hazegray.org/navhist/canada/ww2/bangor/|work=Haze Grey and Underway|accessdate=18 Sep 2013[/tpl] She was recommissioned from 1953â€“1966 as a diving support ship.
