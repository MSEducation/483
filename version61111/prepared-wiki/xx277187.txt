[[Captain Hurricane (comics)]]

CATEGORIES: British comics, Comics characters introduced in 1962, Fictional World War II veterans, Fictional special forces personnel, Fictional British Army officers, Fictional captains, Fictional characters with superhuman strength, Fictional super soldiers, Fleetway and IPC Comics titles

Captain Hurricane was a fictional comic book character in the Valiant in the 1960s and 1970s.
He was in the British Royal Marines Commandos, fighting against the German and Japanese armed forces in the 2nd World War. With his first mate and Batman, "Maggots" Malone, he battled the Axis powers with his super human strength that appeared as he was enraged.
