[[KGEN (AM)]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in California, Regional Mexican radio stations, Spanish-language radio stations in California

KGEN (1370 AM) is a radio station  broadcasting a Regional Mexican format. Licensed to Tulare, California, USA.  The station is currently owned by Azteca Broadcasting Corporation.
