[[Providence Hall Charter School]]

CATEGORIES: Public elementary schools in Utah, Schools in Salt Lake County, Utah, Charter schools in Utah

Providence Hall Charter School is an elementary school located in Herriman, Utah.
Providence Hall was originally planned to be location in neighboring city Bluffdale, but relocated to Herriman in late 2007.[tpl]cite news|url=http://deseretnews.com/article/1,5143,695221711,00.html|title=Bluffdale charter seeks friendlier area to build|date=25 October 2007[/tpl]
Providence Hall will initially provide grades K-6.
