[[Dimitris Roussis]]

CATEGORIES: 1988 births, Living people, Greek footballers, Panetolikos players

Dimitris Roussis ([tpl]lang-el|Δημήτρης Ρούσσης[/tpl]; born 6 May 1988) is a professional football defender. He currently plays for  AEL 1964 in Gamma Ethniki.

==Career==

Born in Agrinio, Roussis began playing football with his hometown club, Panetolikos F.C. in Gamma Ethniki.
