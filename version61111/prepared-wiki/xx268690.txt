[[Kang Chang-gi]]

CATEGORIES: 1928 births, South Korean footballers, South Korea international footballers, Association football midfielders, 1954 FIFA World Cup players, 2007 deaths, Asian Games medalists in football, Footballers at the 1954 Asian Games

Kang Chang-Gi (Hangul: 강창기, Hanja: 姜昌基; 28 August 1928 – 5 January 2007) was a South Korean football midfielder who played for the South Korea in the 1954 FIFA World Cup.1954 FIFA World Cup Switzerland He also played for Seoul Army Club.
