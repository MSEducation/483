[[Call of the Wild (Lee Aaron album)]]

CATEGORIES: Lee Aaron albums, 1985 albums, Attic Records albums, Albums produced by Bob Ezrin

Call of the Wild is the third studio album by singer Lee Aaron, released on June 27, 1985 through Attic Records; a remastered edition was reissued in 2002 through Unidisc Music."Call of the Wild". Lee Aaron European Fan Page. Retrieved 2013-01-01. The album reached #86 on the RPM Canadian Albums Chart on August 24, 1985"Top Albums/CDs - Volume 42, No. 24, August 24 1985". RPM. Retrieved 2013-01-01. and remained on the chart for twelve weeks.
