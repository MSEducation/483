[[2010 IIHF World U18 Championships]]

CATEGORIES: 2010 in ice hockey, IIHF World U18 Championships, 2010 in Belarus, 2010 in Turkish sport, Sport in Erzurum, Youth ice hockey in Turkey, International ice hockey competitions hosted by Turkey

The 2010 IIHF World U18 Championships was the 12th edition of the IIHF World U18 Championship.  The tournament was held in Minsk and Babruysk, Belarus between April 13 and April 23, 2010.[tpl]cite press release|title=World U18 Championship Div. I in Minsk|url=http://www.en.hockey.by/news-35.html|publisher=BIHA|date=April 6, 2009|accessdate=2009-10-20[/tpl] Tournament games were played at the Minsk Sports Palace in Minsk and the Babruysk Arena in Babruysk.  The United States won the gold medal for the second consecutive year with a 3-1 victory over Sweden in the championship game.[tpl]cite web|title=U18: Gold again for USA|url=http://www.iihf.com/channels0910/wm18/home.html|publisher=IIHF|accessdate=2011-06-29[/tpl]  The gold medal was the fifth for the United States since the tournament began in 1999; Sweden matched their best ever performance with a silver medal.
