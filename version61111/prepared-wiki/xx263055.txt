[[Joel González]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 births, Living people, People from Girona, Olympic gold medalists for Spain, Spanish taekwondo practitioners, Taekwondo practitioners at the 2012 Summer Olympics, Olympic medalists in taekwondo, Olympic taekwondo practitioners of Spain, Medalists at the 2012 Summer Olympics

Joel González Bonilla (born September 30, 1989 in Figueres, Spain) is a taekwondo practitioner from Spain. He won the gold medal at the 2012 Summer Olympics Men's 58 kg against Lee Dae-Hoon.
