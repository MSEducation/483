[[River Allen (Truro)]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of Cornwall

The River Allen ([tpl]lang-kw|Dowr Alen[/tpl], meaning shining river), or St Allen River, to the north of Truro is one of two watercourses in Cornwall which share this name.
The River Allen rises at Ventoneage ([tpl]lang-kw|Fentenyk[/tpl], meaning little spring) north of St Allen[tpl]coord|50.315|-5.058|display=inline|region:GB_scale:10000[/tpl] and flows southwards through the Idless Valley into Truro. Here it joins the River Kenwyn to form the Truro River.
