[[SSOT (satellite)]]

CATEGORIES: Earth observation satellites, Science and technology in Chile, Spacecraft launched in 2011

Sistema Satelital para Observación de la Tierra (SSOT), also known as FASat-Charlie, is a Chilean earth observation satellite launched on 16 December 2011 from Guiana Space Centre on board a Soyuz ST rocket.[tpl]cite web|last=Amos |first=Jonathan |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/science-environment-16223533 |title=BBC News - Soyuz launches sharp-eyed Pleiades satellite |publisher=Bbc.co.uk |date= |accessdate=2011-12-18[/tpl]
SSOT is a Miniaturized satellite built on the Myriade satellite bus by EADS Astrium. It was part of a six-satellite payload along with Pléiades-HR 1, ELISA 1, ELISA 2, ELISA 3 and ELISA 4.
