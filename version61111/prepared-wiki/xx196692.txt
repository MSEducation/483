[[Wanshan District]]

CATEGORIES: Geography of Guizhou

Wanshan District ([tpl]zh|s=万山区 |t=萬山區 |p=Wànshān Qū[/tpl]) is a district of the city of Tongren, Guizhou province, People's Republic of China. Wanshan was known as Wanshan Special District (万山特区) until November 2011, when it was renamed Wanshan District. The district has an area of [tpl]convert|338|km2[/tpl] and in 2002 had a population of 60,000.
Postal Code: 554200.
