[[Trudovaya]]

CATEGORIES: Rural localities in Moscow Oblast, Mytishchinsky District

Trudovaya ([tpl]lang-ru|Трудовая[/tpl]) is a rural locality (a settlement) in Mytishchinsky District of Moscow Oblast, Russia, located [tpl]convert|26|km|sp=us[/tpl] northwest from Mytishchi. Population: 62 (2005 est.).
