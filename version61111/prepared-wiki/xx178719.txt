[[Gmina Bulkowo]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Płock County

Gmina Bulkowo is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Płock County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Bulkowo, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|29|km|mi|0[/tpl] east of Płock and [tpl]convert|72|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north-west of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|117.11|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 5,905.

==Villages==

Gmina Bulkowo contains the villages and settlements of Blichowo, Bulkowo, Bulkowo-Kolonia, Chlebowo, Daniszewo, Dobra, Gniewkowo, Gocłowo, Krubice Stare, Krzykosy, Nadułki, Nowa Słupca, Nowe Krubice, Nowe Łubki, Nowy Podleck, Osiek, Pilichówko, Pilichowo, Rogowo, Słupca, Sochocino-Badurki, Sochocino-Czyżewo, Sochocino-Praga, Stare Łubki, Stary Podleck, Szasty, Włóki, Wołowa and Worowice.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Bulkowo is bordered by the gminas of Bodzanów, Dzierzążnia, Mała Wieś, Naruszewo, Radzanowo and Staroźreby.
