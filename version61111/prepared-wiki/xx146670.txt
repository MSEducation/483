[[T.W.O (Aya Matsuura album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2003 albums, Aya Matsuura albums, Albums certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of Japan

T.W.O （ティー・ダブリュ・オー "tee doubleyou oh"）is Aya Matsuura's second album, released on January 29, 2003.  It achieved platinum for sales of  over 250,000 copies.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.riaj.or.jp/data/others/gold/200307.html|title=RIAJ: Certifications 2003|language=Japanese|publisher=Recording Industry Association of Japan|accessdate=2011-11-05[/tpl]  It contains four of her previously released singles as well as a solo version of SHALL WE LOVE? that she had originally sung as part of Gomattou.
