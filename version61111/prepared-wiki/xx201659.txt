[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Chapecó]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1958, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Florianópolis, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Chapecó ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Xapecoënsis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Chapecó in the Ecclesiastical province of Florianópolis in Brazil.
