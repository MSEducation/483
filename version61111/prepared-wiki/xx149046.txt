[[Tijan Jaiteh]]

CATEGORIES: 1988 births, Living people, SK Brann players, Randers FC players, Sandefjord Fotball players, Gambian expatriate footballers, Expatriate footballers in Norway, Expatriate footballers in Denmark, The Gambia international footballers, Norwegian Premier League players, Danish Superliga players

Tijan Jaiteh (born 31 December 1988) is a Gambian footballer who currently plays for Norwegian club Sandnes Ulf.  He was captain of Gambia's international youth team for a number of years.[tpl]cite web|url=http://observer.gm/africa/gambia/article/2008/1/7/pride-of-the-gambia-tijan-jaiteh |title=Pride of The Gambia - Tijan Jaiteh - Daily Observer |publisher=Observer.gm |date= |accessdate=2008-12-19[/tpl]

==Club career==

In 2005, Jaiteh, along with three of his countrymen, came to Bergen on a trial for the Norwegian Premier League club SK Brann. The club secured exclusive rights to buy all four of the young Gambians. In October 2006 it became clear that only Jaiteh passed the bar, and he joined SK Brann in January 2007.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.vg.no/pub/vgart.hbs?artid=135576 |title=Brann henter gambier |publisher=Vg.no |date= |accessdate=2008-12-19[/tpl] He won the Norwegian Premier League with Brann the same year.
31 January 2011 he was loaned out to Randers until the end of 2011, but he had his loan cut short after disappearing from the club in August.[tpl]cite web|url=http://fotball.bt.no/eliteserien/article208121.ece |title=-Tijan Jaiteh har forsvunnet til Italia |publisher=Bergens Tidende |language=Norwegian |date=2011-08-25 |accessdate=2011-08-26[/tpl] 
On 18 March 2012 he signed a one year contract with Sandefjord.

==International==

Jaiteh competed in the 2007 FIFA U-20 World Cup, for the Gambian team that was knocked out by Austria in the Round of 16.[tpl]cite web|author= Roar Lyngøy |url=http://fotball.aftenbladet.no/uefa/article85734.ece |title=Døgnvill Jaiteh | Aftenbladet.no |publisher=Fotball.aftenbladet.no |date= |accessdate=2008-12-19[/tpl]
