[[Paston & Knapton railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Disused railway stations in Norfolk, Former Norfolk and Suffolk Joint Railway stations, Railway stations closed in 1953, Beeching closures in England

Paston and Knapton railway station was a station in North Norfolk on the Norfolk and Suffolk Joint Railway line between Cromer Beach and North Walsham.British Railways Atlas.1947. p.18 It served the settlements of Paston & Knapton, through it was nearer to the former. It closed on 5 October 1964 to passengers and to goods on 28 December 1964.
