[[Ahmed Adaweyah]]

CATEGORIES: Egyptian male singers, Living people, Year of birth missing (living people)

Ahmed Adaweyah ([tpl]lang-ar|احمد عدوية[/tpl]) is an Egyptian singer of sha'abi music. He has starred in 27 Egyptian films. Adaweyah started his career as a cafe waiter but in 1971, he began performing songs using the language of the streets of Cairo, full of working class slang and double entendres. Like many shaabi (meaning of the 'people', or working class) singers Adaweyah specializes in mawal (vocal improvisation).[tpl]cite web|url=[tpl]Allmusic|class=artist|id=p42767|pure_url=yes[/tpl]|title=Biography: Ahmed Adaweyah|last=Jackson|first=Leon |publisher=Allmusic|accessdate=10 April 2010[/tpl]
