[[Teymurlu, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Teymurlu ([tpl]lang-fa|تيمورلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Teymūrlū and Teimoor Loo; also known as Taimirili)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3087393|Teymurlu[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 92, in 23 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
