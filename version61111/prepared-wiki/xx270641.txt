[[Collins Gulch]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of British Columbia

Collins Gulch is a creek in the Similkameen region of British Columbia. Collins Gulch is a stream which flows into the Tulameen River from the south side.[tpl]cite bcgnis|10869|Collins Gulch[/tpl]  The creek is located approximately one and a half miles east of the village of Tulameen, British Columbia.  Collins Gulch was discovered in 1885.  It was mined by both Europeans and Chinese for gold.  Early mining efforts were successful although diggings were exhausted quite rapidly.
