[[Mojikō Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Fukuoka Prefecture, Buildings and structures in Kitakyushu, Railway stations opened in 1891, 1891 establishments in Japan

[tpl]nihongo|Mojikō Station|門司港駅|Mojikō-eki[/tpl] is a railway station on the Kagoshima Main Line in Moji-ku, Kitakyushu, Japan, operated by the Kyushu Railway Company (JR Kyushu).

==Lines==

Mojikō Station is served by the Kagoshima Main Line.

==Station layout==

The Neo-Renaissance style building was built in 1914.
The station has a "Midori no Madoguchi" staffed ticket counter..[tpl]cite web |url= http://www.jrkyushu.co.jp/EkiApp?LISTID=502&EKI=91101010|title= 駅情報 門司港駅 |trans_title= Station Information: Mojiko Station|publisher= Kyushu Railway Company |location= Japan|language= Japanese|archiveurl= |archivedate= |accessdate= 22 October 2012[/tpl]

==History==

The station opened on 1 April 1891.[tpl]cite book |title = 日本国有鉄道停車場一覧 |trans_title= JNR Station Directory | publisher = Japanese National Railways | year = 1985 | location = Japan | page = 158| isbn = 4-533-00503-9[/tpl]
From 29 September 2012, the 1914-vintage station building was temporarily closed for major renovations. It is scheduled to reopen in March 2018.[tpl]cite journal| title = 9/29, 門司港駅,「大正駅舎」の営業を終了|trans_title= 29 Sept: Mojiko Station "Taisho period building" closes| journal = Japan Railfan Magazine | volume = 52| issue = 620| page = p.162| publisher = Koyusha Co., Ltd.| location = Japan | date = December 2012[/tpl]

==Surrounding area==

Mojikō Station is the gateway to various tourist attractions and old buildings which have been preserved in the nearby streets under the name Mojikō Retro.
