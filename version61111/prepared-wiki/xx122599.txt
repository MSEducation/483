[[Shari McEwan]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Miss Universe 2006 contestants, Guyanese female models, 1987 births, Antigua and Barbuda beauty pageant winners, Antigua and Barbuda people of Guyanese descent

Shari McEwan (born c. 1987) is Miss Antigua and Barbuda Universe 2006. Her hobbies include dancing, modeling, traveling, music and reading. Born in Guyana, she stands at 5' 11" and admires Rosa Parks.
