[[Turk Hristiyan Haber]]

CATEGORIES: Turkish websites, Christian websites, European news websites

Turkhristiyanhaber is an online news portal founded in 2012. Turkhristiyanhaber is an independent Christian news agency which covers news from around the world from a Christian worldview. Turkhristiyanhaber focuses on persecuted Christians all over the world. The portal publishes in Turkish and also in English. Editor M.O. Karatay gathered a team from all over the world, aiming to connect Turkish Christians to other Christians. The editor and founder of the portal holds the degree of Master of Divinity and studies for his doctorate degree and founder one of theologian who has Turkish Citizenship.
