[[Yaskawa Electric Corporation]]

CATEGORIES: Companies of Japan, Electronics companies of Japan, Companies listed on the Tokyo Stock Exchange, Companies listed on the Fukuoka Stock Exchange, Companies based in Fukuoka Prefecture, Companies established in 1915, Kitakyushu

The [tpl]nihongo|Yaskawa Electric Corporation|株式会社安川電機|Kabushiki-gaisha Yasukawa Denki[/tpl] is a Japanese manufacturer of servos, motion controllers, AC motor drives, switches and industrial robots. Their Motoman robots are heavy duty industrial robots used for example in car manufacturing.
The company was founded in 1915, and its head office is located in Kitakyushu, Fukuoka Prefecture.
Yaskawa applied for a trademark on the term "Mechatronics" in 1969, it was approved in 1972.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.yaskawa.co.jp/en/company/outline08.html |title=Company History |accessdate=April 9, 2014[/tpl]
The head-office, in KitaKyushu, was designed by the American architect Antonin Raymond in 1954.Yaskawa introduction booklet, 2012
The company is listed on the Tokyo and Fukuoka Stock Exchange and is a constituent of the Nikkei 225 stock index.[tpl]cite web|url=http://indexes.nikkei.co.jp/en/nkave/index/component?idx=nk225#23 |title=Components:Nikkei Stock Average |publisher=Nikkei Inc. |accessdate=April 9, 2014[/tpl]
