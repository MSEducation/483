[[Hallo Berlin]]

CATEGORIES: Drinking establishments in Manhattan, Restaurants in Manhattan, Hell's Kitchen, Manhattan, Beer gardens

Hallo Berlin is a restaurant in the Hell's Kitchen neighborhood of New York City.  The chain consists of two beer garden restaurants and a street cart. The chain serves authentic German beer and cuisine like frankfurters, sauerkraut, potato pancakes, red cabbage, spätzle, wursts and other foods.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.gothamist.com/archives/2006/04/19/street_eats_hal.php|title=Street Eats: Hallo Berlin|publisher=Gothamist|date=April 19, 2006[/tpl][tpl]cite news|url=http://www.nypress.com/19/30/food/cheapeats.cfm|publisher=New York Press|author=Kari Milchman|title=Cheap Eats:Hallo Berlin|accessdate=November 15, 2006[/tpl] Hallo Berlin's motto is: "New York's wurst restaurant."[tpl]cite news|url=http://nymag.com/listings/restaurant/hallo_berlin/index.html|title=Hallo Berlin – Hell's Kitchen Restaurant Guide|accessdate=November 15, 2006|publisher=New York[/tpl]
Hallo Berlin's larger NYC location is the 'Beer Hall' at 10th Avenue at 44th Street. The smaller restaurant at 9th Avenue and 51st Street has no garden, but has outside seating.  There also has been a third branch, near Binghamton, New York.  The restaurants were operated by Rolf Babiel until his death in October 2009,New York Daily News Rolf Babiel death notice and his wife and sons.
Hallo Berlin's pushcart is located on 54th Street and 5th Avenue in Manhattan. In 2004, New York magazine named it one of four "best power lunches" in the city.[tpl]cite news|url=http://nymag.com/urban/guides/bestofny/food/04/powerlunch.htm|publisher=New York|date=March 22, 2004|title=Best Power Lunch – Best of New York 2004[/tpl] It was also awarded the Vendy Award in 2005, ranking first among New York City's street food vendors.[tpl]cite web|url=http://streetvendor.org/public_html/staticpages/index.php?page=20051021224336800|title=Street Vendor Project – Vendy Awards 2005 Finalists|publisher=Urban Justice|accessdate=November 15, 2006 |archiveurl = http://web.archive.org/web/20061010002208/http://streetvendor.org/public_html/staticpages/index.php?page=20051021224336800  |archivedate = October 10, 2006[/tpl]
