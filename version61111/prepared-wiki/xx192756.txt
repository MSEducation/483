William Norval Craig (born January 16, 1945) is an American former swimmer, Olympic champion, and world record-holder.  He competed at the 1964 Summer Olympics in Tokyo, Japan, where he won a gold medal as a member of the first-place U.S. team in the men's 4x100-meter medley relay."1964 Summer Olympics – Tokyo, Japan – Swimming" – databaseOlympics.com (Retrieved on April 29, 2008)
Craig attended the University of Southern California (USC), where he swam for the USC Trojans swimming and diving team.USC OLYMPIANS: 1904-2008, USC Trojans Athletic Department, Accessed August 26, 2008.
