[[Larry May]]

CATEGORIES: 1958 births, Living people, English footballers, Leicester City F.C. players, Barnsley F.C. players, Sheffield Wednesday F.C. players, Brighton & Hove Albion F.C. players, The Football League players

Larry May (born 26 December 1958) is an English former professional footballer who played as a central defender, making over 350 career appearances.

==Career==

Born in Sutton Coldfield, May played for Leicester City, Barnsley, Sheffield Wednesday and Brighton & Hove Albion.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.neilbrown.newcastlefans.com/player3/larrymay.html|title=Profile|publisher=Post War English & Scottish Football League A - Z Player's Database[/tpl]
