[[Toéghin Department]]

CATEGORIES: Departments of Burkina Faso, Kourwéogo Province

Toéghin  is a department or commune of Kourwéogo Province in central  Burkina Faso. Its capital lies at the town of Toéghin. According to the 1996 census the department has a total population of 17,276.Burkinabé government inforoute communale
