[[Air Force Falcons men's ice hockey]]

CATEGORIES: Air Force Falcons ice hockey, College men's ice hockey teams in the United States, Ice hockey teams in Colorado

The Air Force Falcons men's ice hockey team is a National Collegiate Athletic Association (NCAA) Division I college ice hockey program that represents the United States Air Force Academy. The Falcons are a member of Atlantic Hockey. They play at the Cadet Ice Arena in Colorado Springs, Colorado.[tpl]cite web|work=U.S. College Hockey Online|url=http://www.uscho.com/team/air-force/mens-hockey/|title=Air Force Falcons Men's Hockey|accessdate=April 8, 2011[/tpl]
The team picked up their first NCAA Division I tournament win in the 2009 NCAA Division I Men's Ice Hockey Tournament with a 2–0 upset over the first-seeded Michigan Wolverines.
