[[1989 Kentucky Wildcats football team]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 Southeastern Conference football season, 1989 in Kentucky, Kentucky Wildcats football seasons

The 1989 Kentucky Wildcats football team represented the University of Kentucky in the 1989 NCAA Division I-A football season. The Wildcats scored 212 points while allowing 220 points.[tpl]cite web|title=1989 Kentucky Wildcats results|url=http://www.jhowell.net/cf/scores/Kentucky.htm#1989|work=|archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/5kJByYIFZ|archivedate=2009-10-05|deadurl=no|accessdate=2009-10-03[/tpl]  This was Jerry Claiborne's eighth and final season as Kentucky's head coach.

==Season==

Kentucky opened with wins against Indiana and North Carolina, lost to Alabama and Auburn, beat Rutgers and LSU, lost at Georgia, beat Cincinnati and Vanderbilt, and lost to Florida and Tennessee.1997 Kentucky Wildcats Football Media Guide, p.168, 209
