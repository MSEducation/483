[[Bruno Tommaso]]

CATEGORIES: 1946 births, Free jazz double-bassists, Free improvisation, Italian jazz musicians, Living people, Avant-garde jazz double-bassists

Bruno Tommaso (born 1946) is an Italian jazz double-bass player and composer, the cousin of fellow double-bass player Giovanni Tommaso. The first president of the Italian Association of Jazz Musicians and a founding member of the Italian Instabile Orchestra, Tommaso has performed with such musicians as Enrico Rava, Mario Schiano, Franco d'Andrea, Eugenio Colombo and Enrico Pieranunzi, among others.[tpl]cite web| last =Martinelli| first =Francesco| title =Bruno Tommaso Biography| publisher =Allmusic| url= [tpl]Allmusic|class=artist|id=p42006|pure_url=yes[/tpl][/tpl]
