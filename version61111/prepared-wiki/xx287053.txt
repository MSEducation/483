[[Qareh Daraq, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Qareh Daraq ([tpl]lang-fa|قره درق[/tpl]; also known as Kara Darrekh, Qara Darreh, Qareh Darreh, and Qarrah Darreh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3079678|Qareh Daraq[/tpl] is a village in Golabar Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 120, in 34 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
