Mikel Erentxun (born 23 February 1965) is a Venezuelan-born Spanish rock musician.  Formerly with the group Duncan Dhu, he started his solo career in 1992.[tpl]cite web|url=[tpl]Allmusic|class=artist|id=p170827|pure_url=yes[/tpl]|title=Biography: Mikel Erentxun|last=Gutierrez|first=Evan C.|publisher=Allmusic|accessdate=4 June 2010[/tpl]  He has released six albums as a solo artist and has covered  The Smiths' song, "There Is a Light That Never Goes Out", as "Esta luz nunca se apagar√°", as well as Morrissey's "Everyday Is Like Sunday" as "Todo es Igual Siempre".  He has collaborated with Mark Gardener, Robert Quine, Pete Thomas, Lloyd Cole, Matthew Sweet and Fred Maher.  In 2005 the band the Lightning Seeds sued Erentxun for alleged plagiarism of their song "Pure", but it was finally dismissed.[tpl]cite news |title=Desestimada una demanda por plagio contra Mikel Erentxun |author= |url=http://www.efeeme.com/4783/desestimada-una-demanda-por-plagio-contra-mikel-erentxun/ |newspaper=Efe Eme |date=8 July 2009 |language=spanish |accessdate=8 January 2011[/tpl]
