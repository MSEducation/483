[[Raquel Monteza]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Filipino actresses, 1955 births

Raquel Monteza (born September 30, 1955) is an actress in the Philippines. 

==Career==

Monteza appeared in movies such as What Am I Living For (1970) starring Eddie Peregrina, Bakit Bughaw Ang Langit? (1981) starring Nora Aunor, and Zuma (1985) starring Max Laurel.
Monteza also appeared in TV series such as Ang Iibigin Ay Ikaw Pa Rin (2003) starring Christopher de Leon, Richard Gomez and Alice Dixson, aired in GMA Network, and Felina: Prinsesa ng mga Pusa (2012) starring Arci Munoz, Ahron Villena and Carla Humphries, aired in TV5.

==Personal life==

She is married to former actor Jun Soler. She is the mother of actors Ryan Soler, Symon Soler (also became a lead singer of Jeremiah band) and singer Maffy Soler.
