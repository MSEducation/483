[[Church of the Good Shepherd (Rhinebeck, New York)]]

CATEGORIES: Religious organizations established in 1901, Religious organizations established in 1975, Roman Catholic churches in New York, Churches in Dutchess County, New York, Gothic Revival architecture in New York, Shingle Style architecture in New York

The Church of the Good Shepherd is a Roman Catholic parish church under the authority of the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of New York, located in Rhinebeck, Dutchess County, New York City. The parish was established in 1975; it was formerly a mission of St. Joseph's Church (Rhinecliff, New York) from 1901 to 1975. The church address is Good Shepherd Church, 3 Mulberry Street, Rhinebeck,  New York 12572

==Buildings==

The double-height timber church is designed in the Gothic Revival style and Shingle Style. It has a three-stage bell tower.Official Website photos (accessed 5 February 2011)
