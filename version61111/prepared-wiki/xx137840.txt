[[Sketches of Frank Gehry]]

CATEGORIES: Documentary films about architecture, 2005 films, English-language films, Films shot in Super 16, Films shot in multiple formats, Sony Pictures Classics films, Films directed by Sydney Pollack, American Masters films

Sketches of Frank Gehry is a 2006 American documentary film directed by Sydney Pollack and produced by Ultan Guilfoyle, about the life and work of the Canadian-American architect Frank Gehry. The film was screened out of competition at the 2006 Cannes Film Festival.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.festival-cannes.com/en/archives/ficheFilm/id/4352168/year/2006.html |title=Festival de Cannes: Sketches of Frank Gehry |accessdate=2009-12-17|work=festival-cannes.com[/tpl] Pollack and Gehry had been friends and mutual admirers for years.[tpl]cite news | url=http://movies.nytimes.com/2006/05/12/movies/12gehr.html | title=In Sketches of Frank Gehry, a Design for Living Large | work=New York Times | author=A.O. Scott | date=12 May 2006 | accessdate=2008-12-27[/tpl] The film features footage of various Gehry-designed buildings, including a hockey arena for the Mighty Ducks of Anaheim, the Guggenheim Museum Bilbao, and the Walt Disney Concert Hall.
