[[Hans-Henrik Ørsted]]

CATEGORIES: Cyclists at the 1980 Summer Olympics, Danish male cyclists, Olympic bronze medalists for Denmark, Olympic cyclists of Denmark, Track cyclists, UCI World Champions, 1954 births, Living people, Olympic medalists in cycling, People from Norddjurs Municipality

Hans-Henrik Ørsted (born December 13, 1954 in Grenå) is a Danish former professional track cyclist and multi-medalist in the Olympics and World Championships at pursuit. 
He raced in many indoor Six-Day track races in Europe.
He turned professional following the 1980 Summer Olympics, where he won a bronze medal.Olympic results 
At the World Championships as a professional, he won 3 gold medals, 3 silver, and 2 bronze.
