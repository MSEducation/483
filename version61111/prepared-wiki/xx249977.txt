[[Old Parsonage]]

CATEGORIES: Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Religious buildings completed in 1867, Houses in Columbia County, New York, Clergy houses

Old Parsonage is a historic church parsonage on Buckwheat Bridge Road in Clermont, Columbia County, New York.  It was constructed in 1867 and is a two story, three bay frame residence with a jerkinhead metal roof.  It features a decorative sawn bargeboard in a picturesque cottage style.  Also on the property are a garage and small well house.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=1363|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: Old Parsonage|date=February 1979|accessdate=2010-06-26 |author=Jessica Kemm|publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation[/tpl] See also: [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=1365|title=Accompanying five photos[/tpl]
It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1983.
