[[McCord Junior High School]]

CATEGORIES: Education in Lucas County, Ohio, Buildings and structures in Lucas County, Ohio, Public middle schools in Ohio

McCord Junior High School is a school in the Sylvania Schools System district, located in Sylvania, Ohio.
The principal of McCord is Keith Limes, and the assistant principal is Amanda Ogren.
The school covers sixth to eighth grade. McCord Junior High is located in Sylvania, Ohio. It is a public school serving 641 students. The building was constructed in the 1950s.
