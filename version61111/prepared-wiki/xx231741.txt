Oleg Aleksandrovich Kuzmin ([tpl]lang-ru|Олег Александрович Кузьмин[/tpl], born May 9, 1981 in Moscow) is a Russian footballer currently playing for FC Rubin Kazan. He is a full back.

==International==

In October 2009, he was called up to the Russia national football team for the 2010 FIFA World Cup qualifier against Azerbaijan.
