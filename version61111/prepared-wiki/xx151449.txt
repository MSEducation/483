[[Union Deportivo Banda Abou]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Curaçao, Football clubs in the Netherlands Antilles, Association football clubs established in 1974, 1974 establishments in Curaçao

Union Deportivo Banda Abou Is a Football Club in Curaçao who represents the west side of the island Banda abou.  
This club is playing at the first division of the Curaçao Football Association "Federashon Futbòl Kòrsou".
