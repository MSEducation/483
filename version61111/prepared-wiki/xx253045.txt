[[Anabarilius maculatus]]

CATEGORIES: Anabarilius, Animals described in 1980, Endemic fauna of China, Fish of Asia

Anabarilius maculatus is a species of ray-finned fish in the genus Alburnus, that can be found in Nan Pan Jiang basin in south-eastern China. The species have not been studied well, therefore its ranked as Data Deficient.
