==Biography==

Begley was born in Ireland and enlisted at age 20 in the Army from Albany, New York in February 1864.Regimental roster Begley was killed in combat during the Second Battle of Ream's Station at Reams Station, Virginia on 25 August 1864, just two months after the actions that led to his Medal of Honor award.[tpl]Cite web |url=http://www.mohhsus.com/lost-to-history |title=Lost to History |publisher=Medal of Honor Historical Society of the United States |accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl]
