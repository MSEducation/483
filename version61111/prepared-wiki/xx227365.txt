[[Tokachi-Mitsumata Caldera]]

CATEGORIES: Volcanic calderas of Japan, Geography of Hokkaido

Tokachi-Mitsumata Caldera is an 8-km wide volcanic caldera in the Ishikari Mountains of Daisetsuzan National Park in Hokkaid≈ç, Japan.
The caldera is bounded to the north by the Ishikari Mountains and to the southwest by the Nipesotsu-Maruyama Volcanic Group.
