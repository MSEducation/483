[[Jordan's chimaera]]

CATEGORIES: Chimaera

Jordan's chimaera (Chimaera jordani) is a species of fish in the Chimaeridae family found near Japan, Madagascar, and Mozambique.Dagit, D.D. & Compagno, L.J.V. 2005.  Chimaera jordani.   2006 IUCN Red List of Threatened Species.   Downloaded on 3 August 2007. Its natural habitat is open seas, and it is threatened by habitat loss.
