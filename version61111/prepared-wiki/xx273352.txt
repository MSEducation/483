In this match, he scored 13 runs in Gloucestershire's only innings before being dismissed by Alfred Shaw.[tpl]cite web|url=http://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Scorecards/1/1656.html|title=Marylebone Cricket Club v Gloucestershire, 1870|publisher=CricketArchive|accessdate=25 September 2011[/tpl]
