[[Sinan County, Guizhou]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Guizhou

Sinan County (Chinese: 思南县; Pinyin: Sīnán Xiàn) is a county in Tongren Prefecture, Guizhou Province of China.
Area: 2231 km2. Population is 610,000 in 2002. 
Postal Code: 565100. 
The government is located in Sitang town.
