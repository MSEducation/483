[[Athabasca]]

Athabasca (also Athabaska) is an anglicized version of the Cree name for Lake Athabasca in Canada, āthap-āsk-ā-w (pronounced [tpl]IPA-alg|aːθapaːskaːw|[/tpl]), meaning “grass or reeds here and there”.
