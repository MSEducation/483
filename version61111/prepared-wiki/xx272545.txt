[[Hylarana jimiensis]]

CATEGORIES: Hylarana, Amphibians of Indonesia, Amphibians of Papua New Guinea, Amphibians of New Guinea

Hylarana jimiensis is a species of true frog in the genus Hylarana. It is endemic to the mountains of central and western New Guinea. It inhabits mountain streams and nearby areas, and can also be found in degraded habitats.
