[[Sălățig]]

CATEGORIES: Communes in Sălaj County

Sălăţig is a commune located in Sălaj County, Romania. It is composed of five villages: Bulgari (Nyírfalva), Deja (Désháza), Mineu (Menyő), Noţig (Nagyszeg) and Sălăţig.
