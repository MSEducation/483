[[Flober Peña]]

CATEGORIES: 1974 births, Living people, Colombian cyclists, Vuelta a Colombia stage winners, Tour de Guadeloupe winners, People from Boyacá Department

José Flober Peña Peña (born February 7, 1974 in Paipa, Boyacá) is a male professional road racing cyclist from Colombia. He was nicknamed "Donatelo" during his career.
