[[St Leonards Salt Lagoon]]

CATEGORIES: Lakes of Victoria (Australia), Nature reserves in Victoria (Australia), Port Phillip, Bellarine Peninsula

St Leonards Salt Lagoon is a former marine embayment, isolated by coastal deposition from Port Phillip Bay, at St Leonards, Victoria, Australia.  It now forms a shallow and hypersaline lake, of about [tpl]convert|50|ha[/tpl], which is often dry.  It is a Victorian Nature Reserve.Victorian Respurces Online: St Leonards Lagoon
