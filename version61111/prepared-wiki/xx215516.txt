[[Speedknot Mobstaz]]

CATEGORIES: American hip hop groups, Musical groups from Chicago, Illinois, Musical groups established in 1998, Midwest hip hop groups, American musical duos, Hip hop duos

Speedknot Mobstaz are a hip hop group affiliated with Twista. They have released albums Mobstability, and Mobstability II: Nation Business. In 1994, they appeared on Twista's 2nd album, Resurrection. They were not known yet.
