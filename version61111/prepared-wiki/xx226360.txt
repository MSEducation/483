[[Freuet Euch des Lebens]]

CATEGORIES: Waltzes by Johann Strauss II

Freuet Euch des Lebens (Enjoy Life), op. 340, is a waltz composed by Johann Strauss II. It was written for the Vienna Gesellschaft der Musikfreunde, and premiered at the new Musikverein building in Vienna in 1870.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.naxosdirect.ca/STRAUSS-II-J-Waltzes-Polkas-Marches-and-Overtures-Vol-5/title/8550340/ |title=STRAUSS II, J.:Waltzes, Polkas, Marches and Overtures |accessdate=2008-10-05|publisher=NaxosDirect[/tpl]
