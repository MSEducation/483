[[Dash Bolagh, Khodabandeh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Dash Bolagh ([tpl]lang-fa|داشبلاغ[/tpl], also Romanized as Dāsh Bolāgh; also known as Dāsh Būlāq)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3059939|Dash Bolagh[/tpl] is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 446, in 84 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
