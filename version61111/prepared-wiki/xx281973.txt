Tim Sanders (born 6 January 1986 in Wehl) is a footballer who plays as a defender[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.worldfootball.net/spieler_profil/tim-sanders/ | title = Tim Sanders | publisher = worldfootball.net | accessdate = 10 August 2013[/tpl] for Achilles '29 in the Dutch Eerste Divisie. He formerly played for the German SV Hönnepel-Niedermörmter. He is a youth product from SV Concordia-Wehl and De Graafschap.
