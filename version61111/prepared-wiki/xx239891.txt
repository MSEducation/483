[[Jack Tinkler]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Possibly living people, New Zealand association footballers, New Zealand international footballers

Jack Tinkler is a former football (soccer) player who represented New Zealand at international level.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ultimatenzsoccer.com/NZRepSoccer/id1708.htm|title=A-International Appearances - Overall|author=|publisher=The Ultimate New Zealand Soccer Website|date=|accessdate=18 Oct 2009[/tpl]
Tinkler played two official A-international matches for the All Whites in 1927, both against the touring Canadians, the first a 2-2 draw on 25 June 1927, the second a 1-2 loss on 2 July.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ultimatenzsoccer.com/NZRepSoccer/id1559.htm|title=A-International Lineups|author=|publisher=The Ultimate New Zealand Soccer Website|date=|accessdate=18 June 2009[/tpl]
