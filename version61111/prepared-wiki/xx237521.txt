[[Håkon Tysdal]]

CATEGORIES: Norwegian non-fiction writers, People from Enebakk, 1947 births, Living people

Håkon Tysdal (born 17 February 1947) is a Norwegian writer from Enebakk in Akershus. He lives in Rælingen. He was editor of det lokalhistoriske tidsskriftet IGN and has written many local history articles and books.
