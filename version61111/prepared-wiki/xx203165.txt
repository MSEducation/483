[[Richard Crawford (music historian)]]

CATEGORIES: American music historians, Living people, American historians, Guggenheim Fellows

Richard Crawford is an American music historian, formerly a professor of music at the University of Michigan.University of Michigan faculty directory His American Musical Landscape is one of the seminal works of American music history,University of Michigan news published in 2001. He has published a number of other books, and edited a series of books on American music. He was formerly president of the American Musicological Society and is the editor-in-chief of MUSA (Music of the United States of America).WW Norton
