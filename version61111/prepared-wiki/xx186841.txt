[[2582 Harimaya-Bashi]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Asteroids named for places, Astronomical objects discovered in 1981

2582 Harimaya-Bashi is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 2094.6735419 days (5.73 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=2582| title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-04-28 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on September 26, 1981.
