[[Qareh Aqajlu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Qareh Aqajlu ([tpl]lang-fa|قره آقاجلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Qareh Āghājlū, Qarah Āghājlū, and Qareh Āqājl’ū; also known as Kara-Agach, Qara Aghāch, Qareh Āqāch, and Qareh Āqāchlū)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3079619|Qareh Aqajlu[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 58, in 15 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
