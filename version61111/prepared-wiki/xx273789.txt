[[Hearts Are Magnets]]

CATEGORIES: 2006 EPs, English-language EPs, Jason Reeves (songwriter) EPs, Albums produced by Mikal Blue

Hearts Are Magnets is the first EP of singer/songwriter Jason Reeves and his first published work with producer Mikal Blue.  It was self-released in the US on February 14, 2006.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.amazon.com/Hearts-Are-Magnets-Jason-Reeves/dp/B000FTVXUW |title=Amazon CD listing |work=amazon.com |accessdate=24 September 2011 [/tpl]

==History==

Hearts Are Magnets is the fourth overall release of artist Jason Reeves. After briefly attending college, Reeves kept pursuing music in his native Iowa City, Iowa[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.chicagoflame.com/inferno/jason-reeves-and-the-realities-of-co-writing-1.1978843?pagereq=1 |title=Jason Reeves and the realities of co-writing |author=Erin Vogel |date=14 February 2011 |work=Chicago Flame |accessdate=25 September 2011[/tpl] and self-released three albums. Two of these albums, Makeshift Aircraft (2003) and The Nervous Mind Of Love (2004) were sold through online store cdbaby.com and at his shows. Record producer Mikal Blue discovered these albums through his manager, Anna Rambeau, whom herself found the CDs through cdbaby.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.songwriteruniverse.com/jasonreeves123.htm |title=Jason Reeves Co-Writes Two Hits With Colbie Caillat, Releases Solo Album On Warner Bros. Records |author=Jonathan Widran |work=Song Writer Universe |accessdate=25 September 2011[/tpl] Blue was interested in working with Reeves and invited him to California to record with him in his studio. Working in Blue's studio, Reeves, Blue and another producer, Curt Schneider, assembled what eventually became the EP Hearts Are Magnets.

==Track listing==

All songs written by Jason Reeves, except for "Pretty Eyes" which was written by Jason Reeves/Mikal Blue/Curt Schneider.
