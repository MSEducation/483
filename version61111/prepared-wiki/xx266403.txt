Ionuț Eugen Năstăsie (born 7 January 1992)[tpl]cite web|url=http://int.soccerway.com/players/ionu-nstsie/102708/|title=Ionuț Năstăsie profile|publisher=soccerway.com|accessdate=20 June 2013[/tpl] is a Romanian footballer who plays as a right winger for the Liga I club Viitorul Constanța on loan from Steaua București. 

==Club career==

===Steaua București===

He made his first team debut on 22 September 2011 against Sănătatea Cluj, coming on as a substitute.

==International career==

He played for Romania U-19 in the UEFA European Under-19 Football Championship group stage.
On 31 July 2011 at the age of 19 he was called up for Romania senior team.
