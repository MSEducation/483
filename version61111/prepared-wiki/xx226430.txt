Guy Alan Hoffman is a former Major League Baseball pitcher. He played all or part of six seasons in the major leagues from [tpl]By|1979[/tpl] to [tpl]By|1988[/tpl], chiefly as a reliever. After his major league career, he played three seasons for the Orix BlueWave in Japan from [tpl]By|1989[/tpl] until [tpl]By|1991[/tpl]
