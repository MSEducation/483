[[Liangcheng County]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia, Ulanqab

 
Liangcheng County (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠯᠢᠶᠠᠩᠴᠠᠩ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠰᠢᠶᠠᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Liyaŋčaŋ siyan[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=凉城县 |t=涼城縣 |p=Liángchéng Xiàn[/tpl]) is a county of south-central Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China, bounded by Shanxi province to the south. It is under the administration of Ulaan Chab city, and borders Fengzhen City to the east, Zhuozi County to the north, and the regional capital of Hohhot to the west.
