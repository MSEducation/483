[[Egyptian presidential confirmation referendum, 1987]]

CATEGORIES: Elections in Egypt, Referendums in Egypt, 1987 in Egypt, 1987 elections in Africa, 1987 referendums, Single-candidate elections

A referendum on the candidacy of Hosni Mubarak for the post of President was held in Egypt on 5 October 1987. Mubarak had been nominated by the two-thirds required in the People's Assembly on 5 July. His candidacy was approved by 97.1% of votes cast, with an 88.5% turnout.Nohlen, D, Krennerich, M & Thibaut, B (1999) Elections in Africa: A data handbook, p345 ISBN 0-19-829645-2
