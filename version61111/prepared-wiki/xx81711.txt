[[List of Billboard Hot 100 number-one singles of 1993]]

CATEGORIES: 1993 record charts, Lists of Billboard Hot 100 number-one songs

This is a list of the U.S. Billboard magazine Hot 100 number-ones of 1993. There were 11 singles that topped the chart this year. The first of these, "I Will Always Love You" by Whitney Houston, spent nine weeks at the top, concluding a 14-week run that had begun in November 1992. "I Will Always Love You" was the longest running number-one singles of 1993, logging nine weeks at number-one.
