[[Emilie M. Hafner-Burton]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, University of Wisconsin&ndash;Madison alumni

Emilie M. Hafner-Burton is a professor at the Graduate School of International Relations and Pacific Studies (IR/PS) and director of the School’s new Laboratory on International Law and Regulation.[tpl]cite web|url=http://ilar.ucsd.edu/index.htm |title=ILAR |publisher=Ilar.ucsd.edu |date= |accessdate=2013-09-21[/tpl] She is the author of the book "Making Human Rights a Reality." [tpl]cite web|url=http://press.princeton.edu/titles/10036.html |title=Hafner-Burton, E.M.: Making Human Rights a Reality |publisher=Press.princeton.edu |date=2013-09-09 |accessdate=2013-09-21[/tpl] 

==Education==

Hafner-Burton received her Ph.D. in political science from the University of Wisconsin—Madison in 2003, honorary M.A. from Oxford University, M.A. in political science from University of Wisconsin—Madison in 1999, and B.A. in political science and philosophy from Seattle University in 1995.
