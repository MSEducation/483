[[Balvbin]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Balvbin ([tpl]lang-fa|بلوبين[/tpl], also Romanized as Balvbīn and Balūbīn; also known as Balugan)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3055006|Balvbin[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 66, in 23 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
