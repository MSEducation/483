Tom Raftery (born 15 August 1933) is a former Irish Fine Gael politician and university professor. He was elected to the European Parliament at the 1984 European election for the Munster constituency. He lost his seat at the 1989 European election but was elected to the Seanad at the 1989 Seanad election for the Administrative Panel.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oireachtas.ie/members-hist/default.asp?housetype=1&HouseNum=19&MemberID=1646&ConstID=208|title=Mr. Tom Raftery|work=Oireachtas Members Database|accessdate=10 April 2009[/tpl] He failed to retain his Seanad seat at the 1992 election. He stood unsuccessfully in the Munster constituency at the 1994 European election.[tpl]cite web|url=http://electionsireland.org/candidate.cfm?ID=3845|title=Tom Raftery|work=ElectionsIreland.org|accessdate=10 April 2009[/tpl]
