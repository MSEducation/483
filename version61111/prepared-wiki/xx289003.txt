[[Eric M. Meyers]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Old Testament scholars, Brandeis University alumni, Duke University alumni, American biblical scholars, American archaeologists, Dartmouth College alumni, Harvard University alumni

Eric M. Meyers is a biblical scholar and archaeologist. He is the Bernice & Morton Lerner Professor at Duke University.[tpl]cite web|title=Eric M Meyers|url=http://religiondepartment.duke.edu/people?Gurl=&Uil=1690&subpage=profile|publisher=Duke University|accessdate=14 August 2013[/tpl] 
Meyers has degrees from Dartmouth College, Brandeis University and Harvard University. He has served as President of the American Schools of Oriental Research.
Meyers is married to fellow biblical scholar and Duke professor Carol Meyers.[tpl]cite web|title=Biographies|url=http://www.pbs.org/wgbh/pages/frontline/shows/religion/etc/bios.html|publisher=PBS|accessdate=14 August 2013[/tpl]
