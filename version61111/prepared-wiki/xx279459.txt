[[RY Sagittarii]]

CATEGORIES: Sagittarius (constellation), G-type supergiants, Objects named with variable star designations, Henry Draper Catalogue objects, HR objects

RY Sagittarii is a G type supergiant and a R Coronae Borealis type variable star in the constellation Sagittarius, 9049 light years away from us. The star is also a helium star.
