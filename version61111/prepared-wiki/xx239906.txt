[[Froleh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Municipality of Sveta Ana

Froleh ([tpl]IPA-sl|ˈfɾoːlɛx|pron[/tpl]) is a road-side settlement in the Slovene Hills in the Municipality of Sveta Ana in northeastern Slovenia.Sveta Ana municipal site
There is a small chapel in the settlement. It was built in the early 20th century by extending an existing older chapel and adding a small belfry.Slovenian Ministry of Culture register of national heritage reference number 23213
