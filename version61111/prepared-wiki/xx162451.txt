[[Neofusicoccum mangiferae]]

CATEGORIES: Ascomycota, Fungi described in 1916, Plant pathogens and diseases

Neofusicoccum mangiferae is an anamorphic species of fungus in Ascomycota. It is a plant pathogen. Originally named Dothiorella mangiferae by Hans and Paul Sydow in 1916, it  was given its current name in 2006 and was previously classified in the genus Nattrassia.
