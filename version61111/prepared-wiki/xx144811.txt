[[Pučišća]]

CATEGORIES: Brač, Split-Dalmatia County, Municipalities of Croatia, Populated coastal places in Croatia

Pučišća ([tpl]lang-it|Pucischie[/tpl]) is a municipality in Croatia on the island of Brač in the Split-Dalmatia county. It has a population of 2,224 (2001 census), 99% which are Croats. The municipality consists of three settlements: Gornji Humac, Pražnica and Pučišća.
