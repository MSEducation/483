[[Hamlin Garland House]]

Hamlin Garland House is a site associated with the life of author Hamlin Garland in West Salem, Wisconsin, United States.Homestead: The Hamlin Garland Collection The street it is on was renamed in honor of the author.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.westsalemwi.com/wshist.htm|title=West Salem History|publisher=Village of West Salem[/tpl]
