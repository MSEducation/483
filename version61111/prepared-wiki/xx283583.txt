[[Shabnam Sayed]]

CATEGORIES: Indian television actresses, Indian people

Shabnam Sayed(now known as Surabhi Vanzara) is an Indian television actress.  She is known for her role as Shireen Rashid Ahmed Khan in Qubool Hai.  Shabnam Sayed is also known for her roles in  Jassi Jaisi Koi Nahin, Miilee and Hitler Didi.
