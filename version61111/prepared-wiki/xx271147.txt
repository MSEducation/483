[[(18506) 1996 PY6]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1996, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(18506) 1996 PY6 is a main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught and Jack B. Child in Macquarie, Australian Capital Territory, Australia, on August 15, 1996.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
