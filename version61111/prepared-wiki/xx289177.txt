[[Aqcheh Gonbad]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Aqcheh Gonbad ([tpl]lang-fa|اقچه گنبد[/tpl], also Romanized as Āqcheh Gonbad; also known as Āghcheh Gonbad, Aghcheh Gonbad, Āqcheh Kand, Āqjakūmbez, Āq Jāqumbez, and Āqjeh Gonbad)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3053444|Aqcheh Gonbad[/tpl] is a village in Shivanat Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 449, in 100 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
