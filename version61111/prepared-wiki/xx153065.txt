[[Ganayogi Panchakshara Gawai]]

CATEGORIES: Blind musicians, Hindustani singers, People from Gadag, 1892 births, 1944 deaths, Blind people from India, People from Haveri district

Ganayogi Panchakshara Gawai (2 February 1892 - 11 June 1944) was a blind singer from Gadag, a town in southern state of Karnataka, India. He was born at Kadashettihalli in Hangal Taluk, Karnataka.[tpl]cite web|title=Shri Ganayogi Panchakshara Gawai|url=http://sangeetyogashram.org/panchgawai.shtml|accessdate=6 December 2010[/tpl] His school Veereshwara Punyashrama was a premier learning center for Hindustani Music. The Kannada film Ganayogi Panchakshari Gavayi is based on his real life incidents.
