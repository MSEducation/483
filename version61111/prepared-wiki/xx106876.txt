[[Frank Miller (singer)]]

CATEGORIES: 1918 births, Living people, American singer-songwriters

Frank Miller (born July 29, 1918) is a singer and songwriter. He was born in Brooklyn, New York.[tpl]Allmusic|class=artist|id=p12851|label=The Easy Riders[/tpl]
With Terry Gilkyson and Richard Dehr, he wrote the song "Marianne." As a group called The Easy Riders, Dehr and Miller accompanied Gilkyson on the best-selling recording of the song in 1956.
Subsequently, Frank Miller, Jerry Yester, and Doug Myres formed another Easy Riders group.
