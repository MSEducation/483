[[Spoon (album)]]

CATEGORIES: 1994 albums, Emilíana Torrini albums, Virgin Records albums

Spoon is the self-titled 1994 first album by Spoon, on which the Icelandic singer Emilíana Torrini contributed vocals for the first time. The album was only released in Iceland. Torrini sings on six of the tracks.
