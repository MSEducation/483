[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Iguatu]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1961, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Fortaleza, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century, 1961 establishments in Brazil

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Iguatu ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Iguatuvinus[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Iguatu in the Ecclesiastical province of Fortaleza in Brazil.
