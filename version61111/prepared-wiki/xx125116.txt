[[Scotland women's national field hockey team]]

CATEGORIES: Field hockey in Scotland, Women's national field hockey teams, Women's national sports teams of Scotland

The Scotland women's national field hockey team represents Scotland in international women's field hockey, with the exception of the Olympic Games when Scottish players are eligible to play for the Great Britain national women's field hockey team as selected.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.scottish-hockey.org.uk/ |title=Scottish Hockey |publisher=Scottish Hockey |year=2013 |accessdate=1 March 2013[/tpl]  Scotland recently participated in the inaugural season of the FIH Hockey World League, but were knocked out in round 2, failing to qualify for the 2014 Hockey World Cup in The Hague, Netherlands.
