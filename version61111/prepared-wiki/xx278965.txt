[[Anjoman-e Olya]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Anjoman-e Olya ([tpl]lang-fa|انجمن عليا[/tpl], also Romanized as Anjoman-e ‘Olyā)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3803681|Anjoman-e Olya[/tpl] is a village in Chaypareh-ye Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 382, in 66 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
