[[Gmina Rzepiennik Strzyżewski]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Lesser Poland Voivodeship, Tarnów County

Gmina Rzepiennik Strzyżewski is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Tarnów County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. Its seat is the village of Rzepiennik Strzyżewski, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|27|km|mi|0[/tpl] south of Tarnów and [tpl]convert|85|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] east of the regional capital Kraków.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|70.23|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 6,832.
The gmina contains part of the protected area called Ciężkowice-Rożnów Landscape Park.

==Villages==

Gmina Rzepiennik Strzyżewski contains the villages and settlements of Kołkówka, Olszyny, Rzepiennik Biskupi, Rzepiennik Strzyżewski, Rzepiennik Suchy and Turza.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Rzepiennik Strzyżewski is bordered by the gminas of Biecz, Ciężkowice, Gromnik, Moszczenica, Szerzyny and Tuchów.
