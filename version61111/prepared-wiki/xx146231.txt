[[Arkadak]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Saratov Oblast, Populated places established in 1721

Arkadak ([tpl]lang-ru|Аркада́к[/tpl]) is a town and the administrative center of Arkadaksky District of Saratov Oblast, Russia, located on the Bolshoy Arkadak River near its confluence with the Khopyor, [tpl]convert|248|km|sp=us[/tpl] west of Saratov. Population: [tpl]ru-census|p2010=12,845|p2002=14,438|p1989=14,244[/tpl]

==History==

It was founded in 1721.[tpl]cite book|title=Энциклопедия Города России|year=2003|publisher=Большая Российская Энциклопедия|location=Moscow|isbn=5-7107-7399-9|page=24[/tpl] Urban-type settlement status was granted to it in 1939; town status was granted in 1963.[tpl]citation needed|date=May 2010[/tpl]

==Culture==

Ardak museum is a feature of the town.
