[[Point de Gaze]]

CATEGORIES: Lace, Textile arts of Belgium

Point de Gaze (sometimes Point de Gauze) is a needle lace from Belgium named for the gauze-like appearance of the mesh ground.

==Etymology==

The word lace is from Middle English, from Old French las, noose, string, from Vulgar Latin *laceum, from Latin laqueus, noose; probably akin to lacere, to entice, ensnare.[tpl]cite web| url= http://www.thefreedictionary.com/lace |title=Show election|publisher=Lace|accessdate=2012-05-23[/tpl]
