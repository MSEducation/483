[[Devoll District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Albania, Korçë County

The District of Devoll ([tpl]lang-sq|Rrethi i Devollit[/tpl]) is one of the thirty-six districts of Albania, part of Korçë County and derives its name from the Devoll river flowing through the valley. It has a population of 33,785,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.instat.gov.al/graphics/doc/tabelat/Treguesit%20Sociale/Popullsia/POP%202009/t2.xls|title=POPULLSIA SIPAS RRETHEVE, 2001-2010|publisher=Albanian Institute of Statistics|accessdate=2010-09-09[/tpl] and an area of 429 km². It is in the southeastern corner of the country, and its capital is Bilisht.Albania Gobernment - Flags, Maps, Economy, Geography, Climate... Its busy border point Kapshticë/Krystallopigi connects the district with the Greek regional units of Florina and Kastoria to the east and southeast. Devoll borders the district of Kolonjë to the southwest and Korçë to the west and north.

==History==

The district is known in history for the Devol fortress, where the Treaty of Devol between Bohemond I of Antioch and Byzantine Emperor Alexios I Komnenos was held in 1108.
