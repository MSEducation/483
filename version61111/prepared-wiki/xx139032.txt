[[East Meets West (TV series)]]

CATEGORIES: American cooking television series, Food Network series, 1990s American television series, 2000s American television series, 1998 American television series debuts, 2003 American television series endings

[tpl]For|works with similar titles|East Meets West (disambiguation)[tpl]![/tpl]East Meets West[/tpl]

East Meets West is a popular cooking show on the Food Network hosted by the renowned Chinese American chef Ming Tsai. During the half-hour show, Tsai cooked Asian-European fusion cuisine. East Meets West aired from 1998 to 2003."East Meets West with Ming Tsai" (1998) In 1999, Tsai won the Daytime Emmy award in the category Outstanding Service Show Host for the show."East Meets West with Ming Tsai" (1998) - Awards
