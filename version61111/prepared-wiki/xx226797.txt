[[2005 Kremlin Cup]]

CATEGORIES: Kremlin Cup, 2005 ATP Tour, 2005 WTA Tour, 2005 in Russia

The 2006 Kremlin Cup was a tennis tournament played on indoor hard courts. It was the 16th edition of the Kremlin Cup, and was part of the International Series of the 2005 ATP Tour, and of the Tier I Series of the 2005 WTA Tour. It took place at the Olympic Stadium in Moscow, Russia, from October 10 through October 16, 2005.
