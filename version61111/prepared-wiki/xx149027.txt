[[Islamic sexual hygienical jurisprudence]]

CATEGORIES: Fiqh, Sexuality in Islam

Islamic sexual hygienical jurisprudence is a prominent topic in Islamic jurisprudence ([tpl]lang-ar|fiqh[/tpl]), due to its relevance to the issues prominent in everyday life.
