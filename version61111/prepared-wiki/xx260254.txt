[[Lee Taemin]]

CATEGORIES: Shinee members, Korean-language singers, South Korean idols, South Korean pop singers, South Korean Roman Catholics, South Korean male singers, 1993 births, Living people, South Korean dance musicians

Lee Tae-min (Hangul: [tpl]linktext|이|태|민[/tpl]; Hanja: [tpl]linktext|李|泰|民[/tpl]; born July 18, 1993) is a South Korean idol dancer, singer, actor and promotional model. He is a member of South Korean boy group Shinee under the label of SM Entertainment, as the maknae, main dancer, and vocalist.

==Biography==

Taemin studied Chinese in Beijing in 2007. In March 2011, he transferred to Hanlim Multi Arts High School from his previous school, Chung Dam High School to accommodate his hectic schedule for SHINee’s Japanese advancement.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.allkpop.com/2011/03/shinees-taemin-transfers-to-an-arts-high-school-due-to-his-busy-schedule|title=Taemin transfers to an arts high school due to his busy schedule|date=March 1, 2011[/tpl] He graduated in February 2012, but was unable to make it to the ceremony due to SHINee activities.

===Career===

Taemin was discovered at the 2005 S.M. Open Weekend Audition Casting. In 2008, he was chosen as a member of the group Shinee, the 5-member boy group.  The group debuted on May 25, 2008 on SBS's Inkigayo."[tpl]ko icon[/tpl] [tpl]cite web|url=http://shinee.iple.com/|title=Shinee's iple profile|accessdate=January 18, 2009[/tpl]
