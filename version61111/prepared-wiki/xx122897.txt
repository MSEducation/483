[[North River Mountain]]

CATEGORIES: Geography of Hampshire County, West Virginia, Mountains of West Virginia, Northwestern Turnpike

North River Mountain is a mountain ridge that runs southwest to northeast in Hampshire County, West Virginia, United States. Named for the North River that parallels its western flanks for its entire length, North River Mountain reaches its highest elevation of [tpl]convert|2149|ft|m|0[/tpl] near the community of Delray. North River Mountain extends from Staack's Gap at Rio to the Hiett Run gap at North River Mills. Geologically, Ice Mountain is the same mountain ridge, with the Hiett Run gap acting as the physical divide.
