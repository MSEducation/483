[[James Sloan (congressman)]]

CATEGORIES: 1811 deaths, Members of the United States House of Representatives from New Jersey, Year of birth unknown, New Jersey Democratic-Republicans

James Sloan (died September 7, 1811) was a U.S. Representative from New Jersey.
Born in that State, Sloan engaged in agricultural pursuits.
Assessor of Newton township for several years.
Held several other local offices.
Sloan was elected as a Democratic-Republican to the Eighth, Ninth, and Tenth Congresses (March 4, 1803 â€“ March 3, 1809).
He was not a candidate for renomination.
He died in Elmira, New York, September 7, 1831.
