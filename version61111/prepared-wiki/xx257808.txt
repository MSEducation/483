[[Cliff Loya]]

CATEGORIES: 1981 births, Living people, Chicago Blackhawks draft picks, Wheeling Nailers players, Ice hockey people from Pennsylvania

Cliff Loya (born May 8, 1981) is an American professional ice hockey player who is currently playing for the Wheeling Nailers in the ECHL. He was selected by the Chicago Blackhawks in the 7th round (207th overall) of the 2000 NHL Entry Draft.
Loya played NCAA Division I college ice hockey with the Maine Black Bears men's ice hockey team of the University of Maine.Mt. Lebonon's Loya Leads Maine into 'Frozen Four', Pittsburgh Post-Gazette - April 6, 2000
