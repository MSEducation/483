[[FC Belize]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Belize, 2005 establishments in Belize

FC Belize is a Belizean football team which currently competes in the Premier League of Belize (PLB) of the Football Federation of Belize.  
The team is based in Belize City.  Their home stadium is MCC Grounds.
