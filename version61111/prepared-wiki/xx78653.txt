[[Ōyama, Ōita]]

CATEGORIES: Dissolved municipalities of Ōita Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Ōyama|大山町|Ōyama-machi[/tpl] was a town located in Hita District, Ōita Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the town had an estimated population of 3,729 and the density of 81.56 persons per km². The total area was 45.72 km².
On March 22, 2005, Ōyama, along with the town of Amagase, and the villages of Kamitsue, Maetsue and Nakatsue (all from Hita District), was merged into the expanded city of Hita.
