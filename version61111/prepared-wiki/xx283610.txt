[[Walter Risse]]

CATEGORIES: 1893 births, 1969 deaths, Association football defenders, German footballers, Germany international footballers, Hamburger SV players, FC St. Pauli managers

Walter Risse (2 December 1893 – 16 June 1969) was a German international footballer who played for Düsseldorfer SC 99 and Hamburger SV.
