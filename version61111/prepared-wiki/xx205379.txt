[[Con-Ed Redevelopment]]

CATEGORIES: Skyscrapers in New York City, Proposed skyscrapers in the United States

The Con-Ed Redevelopment is a complex of residential skyscrapers proposed in New York City. 685 First Avenue will be [tpl]convert|718|ft|m[/tpl] tall and have 69 floors. 700 First Avenue Tower 3
will be [tpl]convert|689|ft|m[/tpl] tall and have 66 floors. 708 First Avenue will be [tpl]convert|666|ft|m[/tpl] tall and have 45 floors. It will also contain 700 First Avenue Tower 2, 700 First Avenue Tower 1, 616 First Avenue Tower 1, and 616 First Avenue Tower 2.
