[[London at Kirschenbaum]]

CATEGORIES: Television programmes on Channel 10 (Israel), Israeli news and current affairs television programmes

London et Kirshenbaum ([tpl]lang-he-n|לונדון את קירשנבאום[/tpl]; lit. London & Kirschenbaum) is an Israeli current affairs news show on Israeli Channel 10. It is hosted by two veterans Israeli journalists, Yaron London and Moti Kirschenbaum. 

==Overview==

The show interviews Israeli politicians, public figures, artists and scientists, and often ends with music performance. The show won an Israeli Academy Award for best current affairs news show in 2005.
