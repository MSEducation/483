José Eduardo de Araújo or simply Zé Eduardo (born 16 August 1991 in Uberaba, BrazilJogador Zé Eduardo do Cruzeiro – Central Brasileirão 2008), is a Brazilian footballer defensive midfielder, currently playing for OFI Crete on loan from Parma. He can also play at full-back or on either wing, but sees central midfield as his strongest position.http://www.fcparma.com/stagione/archivio-primo-piano/9187.html
