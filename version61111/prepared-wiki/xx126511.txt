[[L.A. Louver]]

CATEGORIES: 1976 establishments in California, Contemporary art galleries in the United States, Art galleries in Los Angeles, California, Art galleries established in 1976

L.A. Louver is an art gallery focusing on American and European contemporary art.  The gallery is located in Venice, Los Angeles, California, USA.Leah Ollman, "Art review: 'Kienholz Before LACMA' at L.A. Louver," The Los Angeles Times, February 2, 2012.

==Directors==

The gallery directors are Peter Goulds, Kimberly Davis, and Elizabeth East.  Lisa Jann is the Managing Director. The gallery is a member of the Art Dealers Association of America.Art Dealers Association of America Member Galleries
