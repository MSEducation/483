[[Robbie Harris]]

CATEGORIES: 1982 births, Living people, People from Durban, South African rugby union players, Natal Sharks players, Rugby union props, African people of British descent, Leicester Tigers players

Robbie Harris (born 30 March 1982 in Durban, South Africa) is a rugby union prop. He previously played for Leicester Tigers[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.leicestertigers.com/news/13678.php|title=Tigers announce 4 new signings|date=29 July 2009|publisher=Leicester Tigers|accessdate=2009-07-29[/tpl] in the Guinness Premiership and before that for the Natal Sharks in the Currie Cup competition and Nottingham RFC in National Division One.
