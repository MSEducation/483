[[Head on Collision]]

CATEGORIES: 2001 songs, 2002 singles, New Found Glory songs, Music videos directed by The Malloys, Songs written by Chad Gilbert

"Head on Collision" is the second single from New Found Glory's third studio album, Sticks and Stones. The single peaked at number 28 on the Billboard Modern Rock Tracks chart.allmusic ((( New Found Glory > Charts & Awards > Billboard Singles )))
