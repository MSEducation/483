[[WIBI]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Illinois, Macoupin County, Illinois, Christian contemporary radio stations, Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 1975

WIBI (91.1 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Contemporary Christian format, relaying the programming of 91.7 WBGL in Champaign, Illinois. Licensed to Carlinville, Illinois, USA, the station is currently owned by  the Illinois Bible Institute,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=WIBI |title=WIBI Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division[/tpl] Accessed October 25, 2013 the educational extension of the Illinois District Council, Assemblies of God (IDCAG) and is part of the New Life Media network of Christian Radio Stations.
WIBI began broadcasting in September 1975, and was the first of the Illinois Bible Institute's radio stations. WIBI began relaying the programming of fellow Illinois Bible Institute station, WBGL in February 2013.
