[[Gustavo Cristaldo]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 births, Living people, People from Caacupé, Paraguayan footballers, Paraguay international footballers, Club Libertad footballers, Club Rubio Ñu footballers, Club Sol de América footballers, Sportivo Luqueño footballers

Gustavo Cristaldo (born 31 May 1989) is an Paraguayan international footballer who plays for Rubio Ñu, as a midfielder.

==Career==

Cristaldo has played for Libertad, Sol de América and Rubio Ñu.[tpl]NFT player|pid=43378[/tpl]
He made his international debut for Paraguay in 2011, and participated at the 2009 FIFA U-20 World Cup.[tpl]FIFA player|305116[/tpl]
