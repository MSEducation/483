[[Aqcheh Pireh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Aqcheh Pireh ([tpl]lang-fa|اقچه پيره[/tpl], also Romanized as Āqcheh Pīreh; also known as Aghājapra and Āqjeh Pīreh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3053410|Aqcheh Pireh[/tpl] is a village in Mojezat Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 99, in 22 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
