[[Barnesmore Gap]]

CATEGORIES: Geography of County Donegal, Mountain passes of Ireland

Barnesmore Gap ([tpl]irish place name|An Bearnas MÃ³r|the big gap[/tpl]) is a mountain pass or gap situated in the Bluestack Mountains, County Donegal, Ireland.  The main Donegal to Ballybofey road, the N15, and former County Donegal Railway run through Barnesmore gap, acting as the main route between south and north Donegal. The name in Irish literally means The Big Gap.
