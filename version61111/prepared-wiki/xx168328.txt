[[The City of Metronome]]

CATEGORIES: Adventure games, Video games developed in Sweden, Upcoming video games

The City of Metronome, previously known as just Metronome is an upcoming adventure game from Swedish developers Tarsier Studios.
It was officially revealed during E3 2005 where it became an anticipated game for the seventh-generation of video game consoles. However, the status of this project is uncertain since Tarsier Studios was unable to find a publisher up to 2007.
On 13 July 2010, Tarsier Studios officially announced that the developer "has signed an exclusive first party deal with Sony Computer Entertainment Europe".http://www.tarsier.se/?pageid=105 It is currently unknown if the deal relates to The City of Metronome.
