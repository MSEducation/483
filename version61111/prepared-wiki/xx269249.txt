[[Cycling at the 2011 Pan American Games – Men's Keirin]]

CATEGORIES: Cycling at the 2011 Pan American Games

The men's Keirin competition of the cycling events at the 2011 Pan American Games was held on October 20 at the Pan American Velodrome in Guadalajara.Guadalajara 2011 sessions The defending champion is Leonardo Narváez of Colombia.
