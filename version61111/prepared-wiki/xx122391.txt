CATEGORIES: Populated places in Corinthia

Assos-Lechaio ([tpl]lang-el|Άσσος-Λέχαιο[/tpl]) is a former municipality in Corinthia, Peloponnese, Greece. Since the 2011 local government reform it is part of the municipality Corinth, of which it is a municipal unit.Kallikratis law Greece Ministry of Interior [tpl]el icon[/tpl] Population 6,993 (2011). The seat of the municipality was in Perigiali.
