Avivi Zohar ([tpl]lang-he|אביבי זוהר[/tpl]; born December 4, 1972) is an Israeli former footballer.[tpl]cite web |url=http://eng.football.org.il/Leagues/Pages/PlayerDetails.aspx?PLAYER_ID=28719 |title=Player Details: Zohar Avivi |publisher=The Israel Football Association |accessdate=December 27, 2010[/tpl] He spent a large portion of his career with Maccabi Jaffa in Israel. At international level, Zohar was capped for the Israel national under-21 football team.[tpl]cite web |url=http://eng.football.org.il/NationalTeam/Pages/NationalTeamPlayerDetails.aspx?PLAYER_ID=28719&NATIONAL_TEAM_ID=583 |title=National team player's details: Zohar Avivi |publisher=The Israel Football Association |accessdate=December 27, 2010[/tpl] He is the younger brother of Israel international player Itzik Zohar,[tpl]Citation needed|date=December 2010[/tpl] who also played for Jaffa, but left for neighbors Maccabi Tel Aviv.
