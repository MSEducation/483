[[Be There (Howie Day song)]]

CATEGORIES: 2009 singles, Howie Day songs

Be There is a single by Howie Day from the album Sound the Alarm.

==Charts==

"Be There" debuted on the Billboard Adult Pop Songs at 26 as of October 5, 2009. As of October 12, 2009 it was 24 on the same chart.
