[[Saginaw County H.W. Browne Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in Michigan, Transportation in Saginaw County, Michigan, Buildings and structures in Saginaw County, Michigan

Saginaw County H.W. Browne Airport [tpl]airport codes|HYX|KHYX|HYX[/tpl], is a county-owned, public-use airport located in Buena Vista Township, east of Saginaw, in Saginaw County, Michigan, United States.

==Facilities and aircraft==

H.W. Browne Airport covers an area of [tpl]convert|293|acre|ha|sigfig=3[/tpl] at an elevation of [tpl]convert|601|ft|m|sigfig=3[/tpl] above mean sea level. It has two asphalt paved runways: 9/27 is 5,000 by 100 feet (1,524 x 30 m) and 5/23 is 2,951 by 60 feet (899 x 18 m).
For the 12-month period ending December 31, 2010, the airport had 8,000 general aviation aircraft operations, an average of 22 per day. At that time there were 66 aircraft based at this airport: 88% single-engine, 2% multi-engine, 2% jet, and 9% helicopter.
