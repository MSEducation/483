[[American Film Theatre]]

CATEGORIES: American films, Films based on plays, Film series, 1970s drama films

The American Film Theatre was a limited run series of film adaptations of stage plays, produced by Ely Landau.Barrett, Michael (2008), “Canon Fodder: American Film Theatre” of The American Film Theater Complete 14 Film Collection DVD set at Popmatters.com. Two seasons were produced from 1973 to 1975. The 14 films were contracted to be exhibited in 500 theaters in 400 cities, admission based on subscription to the entire series.Katharine Hepburn interview with Dick Cavett. 
The films were released on DVD in 2003 by Kino International and again in 2008 as a boxed set.
