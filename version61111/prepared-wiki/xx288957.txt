[[Karasf Rural District]]

CATEGORIES: Rural Districts of Zanjan Province, Khodabandeh County

Karasf Rural District ([tpl]lang-fa|دهستان كرسف[/tpl]) is a rural district (dehestan) in the Central District of Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 11,263, in 2,655 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl] The rural district has 22 villages.
