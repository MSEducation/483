[[Lilla Träslövs FF]]

CATEGORIES: Sport in Halland, Football clubs in Sweden, 1931 establishments in Sweden

Lilla Träslövs FF is a Swedish football club located in Varberg.[tpl]cite web |url= http://www2.idrottonline.se/default.aspx?id=80168 |title= Lilla Träslövs FF – Fotboll Klubb |format= |work= |accessdate=2011-09-23[/tpl]

==Background==

Lilla Träslövs FF currently plays in Division 4 Halland which is the sixth tier of Swedish football.[tpl]cite web |url= http://www.everysport.com/sport/fotboll/team/lilla-trslvs-ff/12588#competition44605|title= Halland - Division 4 – Fotbollsserier 2011 – Fotboll – everysport.com |format= |work= |accessdate=2011-09-23[/tpl] They play their home matches at the Östervi in Varberg.[tpl]cite web |url= http://svenskfotboll.se/svensk-fotboll/om-svff/distrikten/?scr=table&ftid=32364|title= Tabell och resultat - Division 4 Herrar - Svenskfotboll.se |format= |work= |accessdate=2011-09-23[/tpl]
The club is affiliated to Hallands Fotbollförbund.[tpl]cite web |url= http://svenskfotboll.se/svensk-fotboll/om-svff/distrikten/?flid=30434 |title= Kontaktuppgifter och tävlingar – Lilla Träslövs FF - Svenskfotboll.se |format= |work= |accessdate=2011-09-23[/tpl] Lilla Träslövs FF played in the 2010 Svenska Cupen but lost 1-5 at home to Ramlösa Södra FF in the preliminary round.
