[[Thesprotia macilenta]]

CATEGORIES: Thesprotia (genus), Insects of South America, Insects of Bolivia, Insects of Brazil, Insects of Colombia, Insects of Costa Rica, Insects of Paraguay

Thesprotia macilenta, common name Bolivian Grass Mantis, is a  species of praying mantis found in Bolivia, Brazil, Costa Rica, Colombia, and Paraguay. http://insects.tamu.edu/research/collection/hallan/Arthropoda/Insects/Mantodea/Family/Thespidae.txt Texas A&M University
