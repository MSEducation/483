[[In the Gloaming (film)]]

CATEGORIES: American television films, English-language films, Films directed by Christopher Reeve, Directorial debut films, HBO Films films, 1997 television films, American LGBT-related films, HIV/AIDS in film, 1990s drama films

In the Gloaming is a 1997 HBO film directed by Christopher Reeve based on a story written by Alice Elliott Dark. Starring Glenn Close, Bridget Fonda, Whoopi Goldberg and Robert Sean Leonard.

==Plot summary==

Danny is dying of AIDS and returns home to spend his final months. He shares moments of closeness with his mother that tend to shut out his father and sister.
