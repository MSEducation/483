George Webber (George James Webber; born 1895, date of death unknown) was a British athlete who competed mainly in the 3000 metre team.
He competed for Great Britain in the 1924 Summer Olympics held in Paris, France in the 3000 metre team where he won the silver medal with his team mates Bertram Macdonald and Herbert Johnston.
