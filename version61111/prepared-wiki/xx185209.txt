[[Gmina Strachówka]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Wołomin County

Gmina Strachówka is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Wołomin County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Strachówka, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|30|km|mi|0[/tpl] east of Wołomin and [tpl]convert|51|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north-east of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|107.7|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 3,029.

==Villages==

Gmina Strachówka contains the villages and settlements of Annopol, Borucza, Grabszczyzna, Jadwisin, Józefów, Kąty Czernickie, Kąty-Miąski, Kąty-Wielgi, Krawcowizna, Księżyki, Marysin, Młynisko, Osęka, Piaski, Równe, Rozalin, Ruda-Czernik, Strachówka, Szamocin, Szlędaki, Wiktoria and Zofinin.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Strachówka is bordered by the gminas of Dobre, Jadów, Korytnica, Poświętne, Stanisławów and Tłuszcz.
