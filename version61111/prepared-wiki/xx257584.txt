[[DXRE]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in General Santos, Radio stations established in 1971, ACQ-Kingdom Broadcasting Network, News and talk radio stations in the Philippines

DXRE (837 kHz General Santos) Sonshine Radio General Santos is an AM radio station of Sonshine Media Network International in the Philippines. The station's studio is located in General Santos. The main format of the station is music, news and public service.[tpl]Citation needed|date=September 2008[/tpl]
