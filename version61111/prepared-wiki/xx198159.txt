[[SG Dynamo Zinnwald]]

CATEGORIES: Multi-sport clubs Germany, SV Dynamo, Sports clubs established in 1992, Sport in Altenberg, Saxony

SG Dynamo Zinnwald is a sector of SV Dynamo located in Altenberg, Saxony. Sometimes referred to as SSV Altenberg, it was founded in October 1992.

==Sports covered==

The main sports covered by the club are biathlon, bobsleigh, luge, mountain biking, and Skeleton (sport). This is due to the terrain surrounding Altenberg and the bobsleigh, luge, and skeleton track located in the area. Secondary sports  covered by the club are alpine skiing, chess, dance, cross-country skiing, football (soccer), nordic combined, and table tennis.
