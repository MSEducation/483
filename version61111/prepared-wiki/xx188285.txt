Founded in 2007, Amsterdam Airlines used to provide both charter and wet lease services. It ceased its operation on October 31, 2011http://luchtvaartnieuws.nl/nl-NL/Article.cms/Airlines/Amsterdam_Airlines_staakt_vliegoperaties_onmiddellijk and went bankrupt on November 22.Faillissement Amsterdam Airlines uitgesproken ED.nl, 22 november 2011

==Fleet==

At the time of closure, Amsterdam Airlines' fleet consisted of 3 aircraft:Amsterdam Airlines Fleet (as of October 31, 2011)

Amsterdam Airlines' first aircraft, an Airbus A320, arrived at Schiphol on 18 June 2008 from Mexico City via Gander, Canada. The aircraft (MSN 430), was on lease from ILFC, and is registered PH-AAX.Luchtvaartnieuws.nl – Eerste vliegtuig Amsterdam Airlines aangekomen op Schiphol (Dutch only), article retrieved June 21, 2008. Their second aircraft, registration PH-AAY (MSN 527), entered service mid-2009 and a third aircraft, registration PH-AAZ (MSN 299) was delivered on 18-04-2011. Two aircraft have been returned to their lessors. The flights are taken over by ML Tours Strategic Airlines but at the moment Corendon Nederland Airlines NLD operates these flights.
