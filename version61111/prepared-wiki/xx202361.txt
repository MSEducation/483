[[Rumbek Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in South Sudan, Lakes (state), Bahr el Ghazal

Rumbek Airport [tpl]airport codes|RBX|HSMK[/tpl] is an airport in South Sudan, near Rumbek the capital of Lakes state. The airport is served by several national airlines and by air charter service providers.

==Location==

Rumbek Airport is located in Rumbek Central County, Lakes State, in central South Sudan, near the town of Rumbek.
