[[Cailin]]

CATEGORIES: 1999 songs, Unwritten Law songs, Song recordings produced by Rick Parashar

"Cailin" is a song by the San Diego-based rock band Unwritten Law from their 1998 album Unwritten Law. The song is named after singer Scott Russo's daughter, about whom he penned the lyrics. Though it was not released as a single, it became the band's first song to chart, reaching #28 on Billboard's Modern Rock Tracks chart.[tpl]cite web| url=[tpl]BillboardURLbyName|artist=unwritten law|chart=Alternative Songs[/tpl] |title=Unwritten Law Album & Song Chart History: Alternative Songs |publisher=Billboard charts |accessdate=2009-10-06[/tpl]
