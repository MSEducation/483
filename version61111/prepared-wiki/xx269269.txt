[[2011 Lexus of Las Vegas Open]]

CATEGORIES: 2011 ITF Women's Circuit, Party Rock Open, 2011 in American sports

The 2011 Lexus of Las Vegas Open will be a professional tennis tournament played on hard courts. It will be the third edition of the tournament which is part of the 2011 ITF Women's Circuit. It will take place in Las Vegas, Nevada, United States between 26 September and 2 October 2011.
