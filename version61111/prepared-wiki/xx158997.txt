[[Pine Hill, North Carolina]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Surry County, North Carolina, Unincorporated communities in North Carolina

Pine Hill is a small unincorporated community in the Eldora Township of Surry County, North Carolina.  The community is centered around the intersection of Eldora Road/Quaker Church Road and North Carolina Highway 268.  Prominent landmarks in the community include the Pine Hill Friends Meeting and cemetery.
