[[Joel Kanning]]

CATEGORIES: Ambient musicians, Canadian electronic musicians, New Age musicians, Living people, 1973 births, Musicians from Alberta, People from Warner County, Alberta

Joel Kanning (born June 25, 1973 in Milk River, Alberta Canada) is an independent musician, composer and producer. His music, which blends elements of New Age, Chillout, and World Music, has been compared to that of Michael Cretu (Enigma)), and Eric Mouquet (Deep Forest).http://www.newagemusic.nu/?p=1715
