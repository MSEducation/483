[[Taulabé]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of the Comayagua Department, Populated places in Honduras

Taulabé is a municipality in the Honduran department of Comayagua.
It is a very small town that is rapidly growing. Location to the Caves of Taulabe where you can enjoy a guided tour of 400 meters through the caves. The caves have been explored up to 12 km, but still nobody knows exactly where they end at.
