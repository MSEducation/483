[[Pavel Dushkin]]

Pavel Vyacheslavovich Dushkin ([tpl]lang-ru|Павел Вячеславович Душкин[/tpl]; born January 27, 1995) is a Russian football midfielder, who currently playing for  FC Metallurg Lipetsk.
He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Metallurg Lipetsk on July 16, 2013 in a game against FC Vityaz Podolsk.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Russian Football Union|url=http://rfspro.ru/cgi-bin/player/script2.cgi?par1=119742|title=Career Summary|date=9 August 2013[/tpl]
