[[St. Michael the Archangel Catholic Church (Cary, North Carolina)]]

CATEGORIES: Religious organizations established in 1965, Buildings and structures in Cary, North Carolina, Roman Catholic Diocese of Raleigh, Roman Catholic churches in North Carolina, Roman Catholic elementary schools in the United States, Schools in Wake County, North Carolina, Blue Ribbon Schools Program, Churches in Wake County, North Carolina, Private middle schools in North Carolina, Private elementary schools in North Carolina

St. Michael the Archangel Catholic Church & School is a Roman Catholic church and lower school located in Cary, North Carolina in the Roman Catholic Diocese of Raleigh. It is the largest parish in the Diocese of Raleigh, and the largest Catholic parish in the state of North Carolina.

==Church==

St. Michael the Archangel Catholic Church is one of the largest churches within the diocese. Rev. Douglas Reed is the current parish priest.http://www.stmichaelcary.org/parishstaff
The church's mission statement is: "Saint Michael the Archangel Roman Catholic Church is a worshipping servant community dedicated to stewardship, social justice, and ministering to our members and to the greater community in the image given to us by Jesus. This ministry is centered around the sacraments and our acts of faith, hope, and charity."http://www.acswebnetworks.com/stmichaelcary/

==School==

St. Michael the Archangel Catholic School hosts grades preK through eight.  The school feeds into Cardinal Gibbons Catholic High School. The school is a member of the Triangle Catholic School Athletic Conference.http://school.stmcary.org/index.htmlhttp://www.acswebnetworks.com/stmichaelcary/faithformation/article153477.htm  St. Michael the Archangel was recently awarded the Blue Ribbon Program Award for outstanding student achievement.http://www2.ed.gov/programs/nclbbrs/awards.html

===Sports===

The school offers sports for students, and plays against other private schools in the area.http://school.stmcary.org/sports.html
