[[Bon Rud District]]

CATEGORIES: Isfahan County, Districts of Isfahan Province

Bon Rud District ([tpl]lang-fa|بخش بن‌رود[/tpl]) is a district (bakhsh) in Isfahan County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 26,285, in 6,934 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|10[/tpl]  The District has one city: Varzaneh. The District has two rural districts (dehestan): Gavkhuni Rural District and Rudasht-e Sharqi Rural District.
