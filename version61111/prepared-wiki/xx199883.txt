[[Asahi Station (Mie)]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Mie Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo| Asahi Station|朝日駅|Asahi-eki[/tpl] is a railway station in Asahi, Mie Prefecture, Japan, on the Kansai Main Line. It is located 28.5 rail kilometers from Nagoya Station, and is operated by Central Japan Railway Company (JR Central).

==Station history==

Asahi Station was opened on August 8, 1983 as a station on the Japan National Railways (JNR). The station was absorbed into the JR Central network upon the privatization of the JNR on April 1, 1987. 

==Lines==

==Layout==

Asahi Station has two opposed elevated side platforms. The station is unattended.
