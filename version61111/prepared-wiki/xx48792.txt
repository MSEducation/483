[[Technology Administration]]

CATEGORIES: United States Department of Commerce

rightThe Technology Administration (TA) was an agency in the United States Department of Commerce that worked with United States industries to promote economic competitiveness.  TA was most recently led by former Under Secretary of Commerce for Technology, Robert Cresanti.

As is stated on the TA website, TA was abolished under the America COMPETES Act of 2007. The website remains for archival purposes but the responsibilities of the administration were transferred to other agencies.
