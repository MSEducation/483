[[Panamanian presidential election, 1945]]

CATEGORIES: 1945 elections in North America, 1945 in Panama, Elections in Panama

The Panama held a presidential election on 15 June 1945, electing both a provisional President of the Republic.
On 15 June 1945 the Constitutional Assembly elected Enrique Adolfo Jiménez provisional President of the Republic.
