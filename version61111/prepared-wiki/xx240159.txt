[[Salvadoran Constitutional Assembly election, 1982]]

CATEGORIES: Elections in El Salvador, 1982 in El Salvador, 1982 elections in North America

Constitutional Assembly elections were held in El Salvador on 28 March 1982.Nohlen, D (2005) Elections in the Americas: A data handbook, Volume I, p276 ISBN 978-0-19-928357-6 The result was a victory for the Christian Democratic Party, which won 24 of the 60 seats. Voter turnout was 68.0%.Nohlen, p281
