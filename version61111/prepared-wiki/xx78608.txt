[[Hayami District, Ōita]]

CATEGORIES: Districts in Ōita Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Hayami|速見郡|Hayami-gun[/tpl] is a district located in Ōita Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the district has an estimated population of 35,462 and the density of 163.46 persons per km². The total area is 216.94 km².
