[[Aya, Miyazaki]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Miyazaki Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Aya|綾町|Aya-chō[/tpl] is a town located in Higashimorokata District, Miyazaki Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the town has an estimated population of 7,604 and the density of 79.87 persons per km². The total area is 95.21 km².
Aya's town constitution gives it one of the tightest recycling policies in Japan. 
Aya is also home to Aya Castle.

==Economy==

Aya is the most famous place in Miyazaki for wine making. Most people depend on the winery as a profession.

==Festivals and events that are important==

Aya has several festivals in the course of the year.
