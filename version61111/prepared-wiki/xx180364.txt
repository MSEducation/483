[[Shanklin Glacier]]

CATEGORIES: Glaciers of the Ross Dependency, Dufek Coast

Shanklin Glacier ([tpl]coord|84|37|S|176|40|E|type:glacier_region:AQ[/tpl]) is a glacier in the Hughes Range of Antarctica, flowing southeast from Mount Waterman to enter Muck Glacier at a point [tpl]convert|8|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] west of Ramsey Glacier.
The glacier was named by Advisory Committee on Antarctic Names (US-ACAN) for CWO David M. Shanklin of the U.S. Army Aviation Detachment which supported the Texas Tech Shackleton Glacier Expedition, 1964-65.
