[[Everything But the Girl (song)]]

CATEGORIES: 2007 singles, Darin songs, Dance-pop songs, Songs written by Fredrik Thomander

"Everything But the Girl" is a song recorded by Swedish singer Darin. It was released as the second physical single from Darin's third studio album Break the News in Sweden on January 31, 2007. It had poor success charting at only 38 on the Swedish Singles Chart being Darin's second lowest charting single to date after "What If" from his fourth album Flashback.
