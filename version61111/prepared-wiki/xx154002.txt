[[Sacred Heart Catholic Church (Omaha, Nebraska)]]

CATEGORIES: Churches in Omaha, Nebraska, Landmarks in North Omaha, Nebraska, History of North Omaha, Nebraska, National Register of Historic Places in Omaha, Nebraska, Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Omaha, African-American history in Omaha, Nebraska, Roman Catholic churches in Nebraska, Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in Nebraska

Sacred Heart Catholic Church is located at 2206 Binney Street in the Kountze Place neighborhood of North Omaha, Nebraska. Built in 1902 in Late Gothic Revival style, the City of Omaha declared it a landmark in 1979, and it was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1983.
The Church's land was donated by Omaha real estate investor and banker Herman Kountze.
