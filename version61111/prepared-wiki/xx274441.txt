[[Doomsday Prophecy]]

CATEGORIES: Disaster films, 2011 television films, 2010s science fiction films

Doomsday Prophecy is a 2011 sci-fi disaster television film by Jason Bourque starring Jewel Staite, Alan Dale and A.J. Buckley.

==Plot==

Events from the Doomsday Prophecy novel by Rupert Crane begin happening in real life. Eric and Brooke, following instructions left by Crane, work to trigger an anti-doomsday machine and prevent massive destruction.
