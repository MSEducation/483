[[Jason Christophe White]]

CATEGORIES: African-American dramatists and playwrights, African-American screenwriters, American dramatists and playwrights, American screenwriters, Living people

Jason Christophe White is an NAACP Theater Award-winning American playwright, his most notable work being The Dance: The History of American Minstrelsy, which he co-directed with Aaron White (no relation). Jason White is also the co-owner of In Tha Cut Productions.
He is a graduate of The California Institute of the Arts, where he received a BFA in Acting in 2004.http://www.seethedance.com/who-are-we/4515998366
