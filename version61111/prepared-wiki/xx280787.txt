[[Nimavar]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Nimavar ([tpl]lang-fa|نيماور[/tpl], also Romanized as Nīmāvar; also known as Nemāvar)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3077017|Nimavar[/tpl] is a village in Bonab Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,089, in 315 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
