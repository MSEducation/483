[[Katta Subba Rao]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Telugu film directors

Katta Subba Rao(03 Jan 1940 â€“ 12 July 1988),  is a film director known for directing family-oriented and social movies in the Telugu film industry. Almost all the movies which he directed during his career depict the Indian family relationships with respect to issues and romance between naughty husbands and wives, Indian marriages, sons-in-law, mothers-in-law and daughters-in-law, etc.
Many of the films directed by Katta Subbarao are hit movies, namely Srirasthu Subhamasthu, Mogudu Kaavali, Gadasari Atha-Sogasari Kodalu, Kontemogudu - Penki Pellam and others. He directed the movies with popular heroes of today including N.T.R, Chiranjeevi, Krishna (actor), Shoban Babu, Chandra Mohan, Murali Mohan.
He also directed Krishna and N.T.R to act togeather in Vayyari Bhamalu Vagalamari Bhartalu in 1982, which happened to be a silver jubilee hit movie in Telugu cinema.
