Carlos Adolfo "Lucho" Sosa (21 July 1919 – 2 March 2009http://www.clarin.com/diario/2009/03/03/deportes/d-01869356.htmhttp://www.urgente24.com/index.php?id=ver&tx_ttnewstt_news=118938&cHash=a9f7e650ed) was an Argentine football midfielder.

==Playing career==

Sosa started his career with Atlanta, before joining Boca Juniors where he played midfield with Ernesto Lazzatti and Natalio Pescia. At the end of the year 1951, he left for France to play with RC Paris and Red Star,http://www.allezredstar.com/fr_a2z.php where he ended his career in 1958.

==Managerial career==

He coached briefly Boca Juniors in 1960. He was also manager of San Telmo and Racing Club de Montevideo.
