[[Xavier University Preparatory School]]

CATEGORIES: Schools in New Orleans, Louisiana, Roman Catholic secondary schools in New Orleans, Louisiana, Educational institutions established in 1915, Private middle schools in Louisiana, 1915 establishments in Louisiana

Xavier University Preparatory School is a private, Roman Catholic high school in New Orleans, Louisiana.  It is located in the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of New Orleans.

==Background==

Xavier Prep was established in 1915 by Saint Katharine Drexel.
