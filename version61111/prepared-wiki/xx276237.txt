[[Konstantin Rachkov]]

CATEGORIES: 1978 births, Living people, Russian rugby union players

Konstantin Rachkov ([tpl]lang-ru|Константин Рачков[/tpl])(born 8 October 1978) is a rugby union footballer who plays as a fly-half.
