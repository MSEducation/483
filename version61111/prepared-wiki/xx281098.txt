[[Aleksei Nikishin]]

CATEGORIES: 1995 births, Living people, Russian footballers

Aleksei Alekseyevich Nikishin ([tpl]lang-ru|Алексей Алексеевич Никишин[/tpl]; born August 14, 1995) is a Russian football midfielder, who currently playing for  FC Podolye Podolsky district.
He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Podolye Podolsky district on July 16, 2013 in a game against FC Fakel Voronezh.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Russian Football Union|url=http://rfspro.ru/cgi-bin/player/script2.cgi?par1=119685|title=Career Summary|date=9 August 2013[/tpl]
