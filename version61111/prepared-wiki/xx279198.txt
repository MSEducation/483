[[Govalan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Govalan ([tpl]lang-fa|گوالان[/tpl], also Romanized as Govālān; also known as Gūgalān)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3803897|Govalan[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 336, in 78 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
