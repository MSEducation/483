[[Khvoin]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Khvoin ([tpl]lang-fa|خوئين[/tpl], also Romanized as Khvo’īn, Kho’īn, Khow’īn, Khoyun, and Khūyin)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3071544|Khvoin[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 132, in 57 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
