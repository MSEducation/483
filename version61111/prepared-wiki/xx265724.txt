[[Black Krim (tomato)]]

CATEGORIES: Tomato cultivars

The Black Krim is an heirloom tomato originating from Crimea, Ukraine. The plant is open-pollinated, indeterminate, bearing 8 ounce flattened globe fruits that are dark reddish-purple to black with green/brown shoulders.[tpl]cite web|url=http://njaes.rutgers.edu/tomato-varieties/variety.asp?Black+Krim|title=Black Krim|publisher=Rutgers New Jersey Agriculture Experiment Station|accessdate=4 September 2012[/tpl]
