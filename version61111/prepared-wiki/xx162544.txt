Mijat Marić (born 30 April 1984 in Minusio) is a Swiss footballer of Croatian descent, who currently plays for Belgian Pro League club Lokeren. He is a defender.

==Career==

Marić started his career at AC Lugano of Ticino, an Italian speaking region. After the bankrupt of the club, he joined FC Malcantone Agno. In summer 2003 he joined FC St. Gallen of Swiss Super League. He played 2 matches for the club before left on loan to FC Luzern until December 2007.
In March 2007, he signed a 3-year contract with Bari on free transfer, effective on 1 July 2008.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.asbari.it/societa/archivio-news/698-domani-sarresentato-maric.html |publisher=AS Bari |title=Domani sarà presentato Maric  |date=2008-03-31 |accessdate=2010-01-19 |language=Italian[/tpl]
