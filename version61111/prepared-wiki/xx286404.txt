[[Olena Voronina]]

CATEGORIES: 1990 births, Living people, Ukrainian fencers, People from Kharkiv, Women fencers

Olena Voronina ([tpl]lang-uk|Олена Вороніна[/tpl]; born 5 May 1990) is a Ukrainian gold medallist in sabre fencing. She won Gold during the Women's team sabre at the 2013 World Fencing Championships.
Voronina lives in Kharkiv, Ukraine and is a student at the Kharkiv Polytechnic Institute.
