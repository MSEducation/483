Nabil Maâloul ([tpl]lang-ar|نبيل معلول[/tpl]), born 25 July 1962, is a football coach and former footballer from Tunisia. He played with the Tunisian team and participated in the 1988 Summer Olympics in Seoul. He is currently the coach of Qatar Stars League outfit El Jaish.EL JAISH APPOINT NEW COACH 21 January 2014.
