[[Víctor Coto Ortega]]

CATEGORIES: 1990 births, Living people, Costa Rican footballers, Costa Rican expatriate footballers, Gimnasia y Esgrima de Jujuy footballers, Expatriate footballers in Argentina, Expatriate footballers in Malta

Víctor Coto Ortega (born September 29, 1990 in San José, Costa Rica) is a Costa Rican footballer currently playing for Gimnasia y Esgrima de Jujuy of the Primera B Nacional in Argentina.
