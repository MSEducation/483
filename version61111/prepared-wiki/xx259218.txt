[[Ümit Ergirdi]]

CATEGORIES: 1981 births, Living people, Sportspeople from Berlin, Turkish footballers, Association football midfielders, 3. Liga players, BFC Preussen players, Tennis Borussia Berlin players, SV Babelsberg 03 players, Free University of Berlin alumni

Ümit Ergirdi (born 5 November 1981 in Berlin, Germany) is a former professional Turkish footballer.
Ergirdi made 5 appearances in the 3. Fußball-Liga for SV Babelsberg 03 before deciding to end his professional career and to finish his law studies at the Free University of Berlin.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.maerkischeallgemeine.de/cms/beitrag/11969446/62449/Der-Mittelfeldspieler-verlaesst-den-SV-Babelsberg-um-sich.html|title=Ümit Ergirdi sagt tschüss – Der Mittelfeldspieler verlässt den SV Babelsberg, um sich seiner beruflichen Zukunft zu widmen|first=Jens|last=Trommer|work=Märkische Allgemeine|date=23 December 2010|accessdate=31 December 2010|language=German[/tpl]
