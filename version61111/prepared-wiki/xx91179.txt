[[La Albuera]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities in the Province of Badajoz, Populated places in the Province of Badajoz

La Albuera is a village southeast of Badajoz, Extremadura, Spain. It was scene of a battle (May 16, 1811) between the British troops under  William Carr Beresford and the French ones led by Marshal Soult, in the course of  the Peninsular war. [tpl]As of|2009[/tpl] it had a population of c. 2,000 inhabitants.
