[[César Hernández (outfielder)]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Cincinnati Reds players, Nashville Sounds players, Major League Baseball outfielders, 1966 births, Major League Baseball players from the Dominican Republic

César Dario (Pérez) Hernández (born September 28, 1966) is a former Major League Baseball player who played 61 games for the Cincinnati Reds in [tpl]By|1992[/tpl] and [tpl]By|1993[/tpl]. He was drafted by the Montreal Expos in the 1st round of the [tpl]By|1985[/tpl] amateur draft (Dominican Draft) and in [tpl]By|1991[/tpl] the Reds selected him off the waiver wire. He made his major league debut on July 19, 1992 against the St. Louis Cardinals.
