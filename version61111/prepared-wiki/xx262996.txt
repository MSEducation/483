[[Hwang Kwanghee]]

CATEGORIES: 1988 births, Living people, South Korean male singers, Running Man (TV series) contestants, South Korean idols

Hwang Kwang-hee (Hangul: 황광희; Hanja: [tpl]linktext|黃|光|熙|[/tpl]; born on August 25, 1988), also known mononymously as Kwanghee, is a member of the nine-member South Korean boyband ZE:A managed by Star Empire Entertainment. They debuted on January 7, 2010 with the single Mazeltov.[tpl]ko icon[/tpl] [tpl]cite news|url=http://news.joins.com/article/457/3953457.html?ctg=15|title=기대주 男아이돌 제아 티저 전격 공개 ‘막내 동준 맨몸 투혼’|date= January 5, 2010|publisher=Joins|accessdate=November 20, 2012[/tpl] Hwang is also well known for being a popular star in the variety show scene.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.allkpop.com/2010/11/who-were-the-top-variety-show-idol-stars-of-2010 |title=Who were the top variety show idol stars of 2010? |work=allkpop |date=24 November 2010 |accessdate=3 January 2010[/tpl]
On May 28, Hwang attended the "2011 Environment Day" event in Seoul, where he participated in a "clothes layering game" and shocked everyone by putting on 252 layers of t-shirts, earning him a Guinness World Record for the most T-shirts worn.http://news.nate.com/view/20110530n08990
He went on We Got Married with Secret's Sunhwa. The last episode was filmed on April 10, 2013.
