[[Moisés Candelario]]

CATEGORIES: 1978 births, Living people, Ecuadorian footballers, Ecuador international footballers, Emelec footballers, LDU Quito footballers, Barcelona Sporting Club footballers, 1999 Copa América players, People from Guayaquil

Moisés Antonio Candelario (born 10 October 1978 in Guayaquil) is an international football player from Ecuador. He plays as a defensive midfielder.
Candelario played most of his career for Emelec. Since 2006 he has played for a number of other teams in Ecuador.
