Located beneath Sugarloaf Mountain in the Rio de Janeiro neighborhood of Urca, this walking trail leads from the Praia Vermelha to the top of the first of Sugarloaf Mountain's two humps.  It is frequented by families, tourists and local runners from Urca's nearby military school.
