[[Claudia Galli]]

CATEGORIES: 1978 births, Living people, Swedish actresses, 21st-century Swedish actresses, Swedish people of Italian descent, People from Stockholm, People from Malmö

Claudia Josefina Galli Concha (born 3 August 1978) is a Swedish actress. Claudia was born in Stockholm but is now residing in Malmö, she started her career at age three in the segment "Film-tajm" in the television show Trazan & Banarne.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.svt.se/nyheter/regionalt/sydnytt/nybliven-malmobo-i-fjallbackamorden |title=Nybliven Malmöbo i huvudroll – Nyheter |publisher=SVT.se |date=27 December 2012[/tpl] Galli got her breakthrough as an actress in the TV4 soap opera Skilda världar where she played "Frida Lindberg.[tpl]cite web|author=Harry Amster |url=http://www.svd.se/kultur/tv/skilda-varldar-var-sapa-pa-riktigt_5949771.svd |title=Skilda världar var såpa på riktigt | SvD |language=[tpl]sv icon[/tpl] |work=Svenska Dagbladet |date=17 February 2011[/tpl]
She also played "Lina Svensson" for two seasons of the SVT comedy series Svensson, Svensson she also has been a panel member of the comedy show Parlamentet. Claudia Galli came in second place during Let's Dance 2010 being one of the celebrity dancers being partnered with Tobias Wallin.[tpl]cite web|author=Foto: Andreas Lundberg/TV4 |url=http://www.tv4.se/lets-dance/artiklar/claudia-galli-tobias-wallin-4fbf87ab04bf7251940028a4 |title=Claudia Galli & Tobias Wallin – Let's dance |publisher=tv4.se |date=2 December 2009[/tpl]
After her dance appearancces Galli played "Jonna Wetterström" in the SVT comedy series Starke man.[tpl]cite web|author={userName} |url=http://www.expressen.se/noje/tv/politisk-maktkamp-i-trakiga-svinarp---premiar-for-starke-man/ |title=Politisk maktkamp i tråkiga Svinarp – premiär för "Starke man" | Expressen Nöje | Nöjesnyheter Musiknyheter Filmnyheter |work=Expressen |date=26 September 2010[/tpl] And from 2012 and forward Galli will appear in twelve filmatizations of author Camilla Läckbergs "Fjällbackamorden" films.
Claudia Galli is since 2012 married to director Manuel Concha.[tpl]cite web|author=Martin Gustafsson |url=http://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/klick/article15428708.ab |title=Claudia Galli har gift sig | Klick! | Nöjesbladet | Aftonbladet |work=Aftonbladet |date=12 September 2012[/tpl] She is also the aunt of actress Josephine Bornebusch.[tpl]cite web|author={userName} |url=http://www.expressen.se/noje/extra/claudia-gallis-pappa-var-forsvunnen-i-atta-ar/ |title=Claudia Gallis pappa var försvunnen i åtta år | Kändisar Hänt i Veckan Skvaller |work=Expressen [/tpl]
