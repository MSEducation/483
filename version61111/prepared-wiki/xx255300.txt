[[Mathiura Union]]

CATEGORIES: Sylhet District, Unions of Bangladesh

Mathiura Union is a Union in Beanibazar Upazila, Sylhet District, Bangladesh.

==History==

The Union dates back to before the 12th century.[tpl]Citation needed|date=January 2010[/tpl] The religion followed by the majority is Islam,[tpl]Citation needed|date=January 2010[/tpl] which was first introduced in the area by the Great Saint Shah Jalal.[tpl]Citation needed|date=January 2010[/tpl]

==Geography==

Mathiura Union is located at [tpl]Coord|24.8250|N|92.1625|E|[/tpl]. It has 27,089 units of household and a total area of 253.22 km².

==Demographics==

As of the 1991 Bangladesh census, Mathiura Union had a population of around 30,547, 49.5% of whom are male and 50.5% are female.

==Administrative==

Mathiura Union has 3 Wards, Mauzas/Mahallas, and 15 Villages.
