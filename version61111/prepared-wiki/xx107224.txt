[[Juhan af Grann]]

CATEGORIES: Finnish film directors, 1948 births, Living people, People from Kuopio, Finnish Freemasons

Juhan af Grann (born 4 December 1948 in Kuopio, Finland as Heikki Juhani Grann) is a Finnish film director and producer known for his UFO documentaries. His most notable documentary is Mankind's Last Exodus, released in 1998 and sold in over 120 countries.
