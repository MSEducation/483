[[Hocus Pocus (Enon album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2003 albums, Touch and Go Records albums, Enon albums

Hocus Pocus is an album released by the band Enon. It was released September 9, 2003 on Touch and Go Records.
The album also contains a URL to a hidden website which can been seen by removing the black disc holder in the jewel case.
