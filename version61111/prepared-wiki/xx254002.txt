[[The Jungle (1967 film)]]

CATEGORIES: 1967 films, United States National Film Registry films, Short documentary films, Documentary films about African Americans, Documentary films about organized crime in the United States, Student films, Temple University, 1960s documentary films

The Jungle (1967) is a short film about gangs made by African American students under the direction of Temple University professor Harold Haskins. In 2009, it was added to the National Film Registry by the Library of Congress as being “culturally, historically or aesthetically” significant and will be preserved for all time."Thriller and 24 Other Films Named to National Film Registry", Associated Press via Yahoo News (December 30, 2009)Library of Congress press release on films named to National Film Registry in December 2009
