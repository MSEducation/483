[[Constantinos Laifis]]

CATEGORIES: 1993 births, Living people, Association football midfielders, Cypriot footballers, Cyprus under-21 international footballers, Enosis Neon Paralimni players, Nottingham Forest F.C. players, Anorthosis Famagusta players, Alki Larnaca FC players

Constantinos Laifis (Greek: Κωνσταντίνος Λαϊφης; born 19 May 1993) is a Cypriot footballer who plays for Cypriot First Division club Alki Larnaca and the Cyprus under-21 national team. He plays as a left winger or at left back.

==Early life and career==

Laifis was born in Paralimni. As a boy, he played tennis to a good standard, and played football for the junior teams of his local club, Enosis Neon Paralimni. ==Anorthosis Famagusta==

Laifis made his first-team debut for Anorthosis the next month, in their 2011–12 Cypriot Cup second round second-leg match against Second Division club APOP Kinyras, as a 61st-minute substitute for Vincent Laban.[tpl]cite web |url=http://uk.soccerway.com/matches/2012/02/08/cyprus/cup/anorthosis-famagusta-fc/apop-kinyras-peyias/1249172/ |title=Anorthosis vs. APOP Kinyras 3–0 |publisher=Global Sports Media |work=Soccerway |accessdate=18 August 2013[/tpl] That was his last competitive appearance until July 2013, when injuries to Valentinos Sielis and Andreas Avraam left a vacancy at left back for pre-season friendlies.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.24sports.com.cy/index.php?pageaction=kat&modid=1&artid=67888 |title=Αρπάζει την ευκαιρία ο Λαΐφης |language=Greek |trans_title=Laifis grabs his chance |publisher=24sports |date=6 July 2013 |accessdate=18 August 2013[/tpl] Laifis performed well enough to be given a place in the starting eleven for the second leg of the second qualifying round match against Allsvenskan club Gefle. He played the full 90 minutes as Anorthosis let slip a 3–0 lead from the first leg and were eliminated 4–3 on aggregate.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.uefa.com/uefaeuropaleague/season=2014/matches/round=2000466/match=2012352/postmatch/index.html |title=UEFA Europa League 2014: Gefle–Anorthosis |publisher=UEFA |accessdate=18 August 2013[/tpl]

==International career==

Laifis represented Cyprus internationally at all under-age levels.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.thisisnottingham.co.uk/Paul-Taylor-column-Forest-s-latest-investment-wisest/story-12172201-detail/story.html |title=Forest's latest investment could be their wisest yet |author=Taylor, Paul |newspaper=Nottingham Evening Post |date=31 March 2011 |accessdate=18 August 2013[/tpl] He helped the under-19 team reach the elite qualification round of the 2012 European championships,[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.uefa.com/under19/season=2012/teams/player=250014082/index.html |title=Kostas Laifis: Under-19 |publisher=UEFA |accessdate=18 August 2013[/tpl] and made his first appearance for the under-21s in March 2013, when he played the first half of a 2–0 defeat to Belgium in a qualifier for the 2015 European under-21 championships.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.uefa.com/under21/season=2015/matches/round=2000412/match=2011553/postmatch/report/index.html |title=Belgium up and running against Cyprus |author=Scholten, Berend |publisher=UEFA |date=25 March 2013 |accessdate=18 August 2013[/tpl] He played the whole of their next qualifier, a 3–0 win at home to Northern Ireland,[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.uefa.com/under21/season=2015/matches/round=2000412/match=2011555/postmatch/report/index.html |title=Cyprus ease past Northern Ireland |publisher=UEFA |date=30 May 2013 |accessdate=18 August 2013[/tpl] and retained his place for a friendly in Romania.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.romaniansoccer.ro/stiri/40444/amical-u21-romania--u21-cipru-0-2.htm |title=Amical: U21 Romania – U21 Cipru 0–2 |language=Romanian |publisher=Romanian Soccer |date=14 August 2013 |accessdate=18 August 2013[/tpl]
