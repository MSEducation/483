[[Burun, Iran]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Burun ([tpl]lang-fa|بورون[/tpl], also Romanized as Būrūn; also known as Bīrūn, Boroon, and Borūn)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3057156|Burun[/tpl] is a village in Shivanat Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 342, in 68 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
