[[John P. Mabry]]

CATEGORIES: 1969 births, Living people, People from Austin, Texas, Baylor University alumni, Texas Democrats, Members of the Texas House of Representatives

John Porter Mabry, Jr. (born May 7, 1969, in Austin, Texas) is a Texas politician who served as a Democrat in the Texas House of Representatives during the Seventy-eighth Texas Legislature. Mabry was a member of the Killer Ds, the Democratic representatives who prevented the House from achieving a quorum in May 2003, thus blocking regular session consideration of congressional redistricting.[tpl]cite news|url=http://news.google.com/newspapers?id=2V4vAAAAIBAJ&sjid=ytwFAAAAIBAJ&pg=2185,1700731&dq=john-mabry+quorum+texas&hl=en|title=Texas Democrats don't expect ant political fallout from revolt|last=Brown|first=Angela K.|date=18 May 2003|work=The Gadsden Times|page=3|accessdate=7 March 2011[/tpl]
He holds a law degree from Baylor University.
