[[Mazraeh-ye At Batan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Mazraeh-ye At Batan ([tpl]lang-fa|مزرعه آت باتان[/tpl], also Romanized as Mazra‘eh-ye Āt Bātān) is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
