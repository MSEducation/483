[[Jamesville-DeWitt Central School District]]

CATEGORIES: School districts in New York, Education in Onondaga County, New York

Jamesville-DeWitt Central School District is a public school district that serves Jamesville, New York and the town of DeWitt, New York. The school district consists of 2786 students in 5 schools (three K-4 elementary schools, one 5-8 middle school, and one 9-12 high school). The current superintendent is Dr. Alice Kendrick.
