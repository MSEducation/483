Robert Pearce (February 29, 1908 – March 15, 1996) was an American wrestler and olympic champion. He competed at the 1932 Olympic Games in Los Angeles, where he won a gold medal in freestyle bantamweight.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.databaseolympics.com/games/gamessport.htm?g=10&sp=WRE |title=1932 Summer Olympics – Los Angeles, United States – Wrestling |publisher=databaseOlympics.com |accessdate=28 April 2008[/tpl]
