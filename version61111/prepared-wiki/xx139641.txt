Hojat Chaharmahali ([tpl]lang-fa|حجت چهارمحالی[/tpl], born March 25, 1989 in Shoushtar, Iran) is an Iranian football player who last played for the Iran Pro League's club Esteghlal.Fars

==Club career==

Chaharmahali started his career at Foolad FC in 2005. Chaharmahali played for Foolad in the 2006 AFC Champions League group stage.[tpl]cite web|publisher=RSSSF|author=Saaid, Hamdan|title=Asian Champions League 2006 Details|url=http://www.rsssf.com/tablesa/ascup06-chldet.html|date=2006-03-30[/tpl]
On November 14, 2006 Radio Farda reported that Olympiacos were to sign Chaharmahali on a 5-year deal, although the deal did not go through.[tpl]Citation needed|date=April 2011[/tpl]
