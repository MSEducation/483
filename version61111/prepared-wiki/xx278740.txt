[[Saremsaqlu, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Saremsaqlu ([tpl]lang-fa|سارمساقلو/سارمساخلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Sāremsāqlū; also known as Sāremsākhlū, Sāram Sākhli, and Sarym-Sogly)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3082733|Saremsaqlu[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 542, in 145 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
