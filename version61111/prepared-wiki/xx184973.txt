[[Cassandra Craft]]

CATEGORIES: Comics characters introduced in 1973, DC Comics characters, Fictional businesspeople, Fictional blind characters, San Francisco, California in fiction, Fictional mediums

Cassandra Craft is a character in DC Comics.  She is a blind psychic who runs an occult bookstore in San Francisco, California.  She is friends with Zatanna and Phantom Stranger.  She first appeared in The Phantom Stranger vol. 2 #17 and plays an important role in Grant Morrison's Seven Soldiers of Victory crossover.  She was created by Len Wein and Jim Aparo.
