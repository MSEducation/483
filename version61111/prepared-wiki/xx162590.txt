[[Tight Shoes]]

CATEGORIES: Foghat albums, 1980 albums, Bearsville Records albums, Rhino Records albums

Tight Shoes is the ninth studio album by Foghat, released in 1980 on Bearsville Records. The band has commented on the album saying, "It was a tentative foray into New Wave." This was also the last release Rod Price participated on until 1994's Return of the Boogie Men.
