[[Francisco Raúl Villalobos Padilla]]

CATEGORIES: 20th-century Roman Catholic bishops, 1921 births, Mexican Roman Catholic bishops, Living people

Francisco Raúl Villalobos Padilla, (born February 1, 1921) is a Mexican prelate of the Roman Catholic Church.
Padilla was born in Guadalajara and ordained a priest on April 2, 1949. He was appointed auxiliary bishop of the Roman Catholic Diocese of Saltillo on May 4, 1971 as well as Titular bishop of Columnata and ordained bishop on August 4, 1971. He was appointed bishop of Saltillo on October 4, 1975, retiring from the post on December 30, 1999.
