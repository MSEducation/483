[[Across the Concrete Sky]]

CATEGORIES: 2003 albums, Air Supply albums

Across the Concrete Sky is the sixteenth studio album by Australian soft rock band Air Supply released in 2003. Single "Goodnight" got minor recognition.

==Track listing==

All songs written by Graham Russell; except "A Place Where We Belong" co-written by Alejandro Lerner.
