[[Union Bank of California Building]]

CATEGORIES: Office buildings completed in 1969, Skyscrapers in San Diego, California, Skyscrapers between 100 and 149 meters

The Union Bank of California Building is a 27 storey [tpl]convert|118.26|m|abbr=on[/tpl] skyscraper in San Diego, California, completed in 1969. For twenty years, the tower stood as the tallest building in the city, until the completion of the [tpl]convert|152|m|abbr=on[/tpl] Symphony Towers in 1989.
