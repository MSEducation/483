Nils Christensen Ringnæs (1792 - ??) was a Norwegian politician.
He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament in 1842 and 1845, representing the rural constituency of Hedemarkens Amt (today named Hedmark). He worked as a farmer and bailiff.Nils Christensen Ringnæs — Norwegian Social Science Data Services (NSD)
