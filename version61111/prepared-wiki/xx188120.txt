[[Stockhorn]]

CATEGORIES: Bernese Alps, Mountains of the Alps, Mountains of Switzerland, Cable cars in Switzerland, Mountains of the canton of Bern

The Stockhorn is a mountain of the Bernese Alps, overlooking the region of Lake Thun in the Bernese Oberland. It is located north of the town of Erlenbach im Simmental.
The Stockhorn is [tpl]convert|2190|m|ft|abbr=on[/tpl] high and is accessible via cable car from Erlenbach. It has a restaurant at the top and is a good starting point for many hikes. There is good fishing in the two lakes right below the Stockhorn, the Oberstockensee (around [tpl]convert|1650|m|ft|abbr=on|disp=x| |[/tpl] high) and the Hinterstockensee (around [tpl]convert|1550|m|ft|abbr=on|disp=x| |[/tpl] high). There is a subterranean water connection between the two lakes.
