[[Niles Car and Manufacturing Company]]

CATEGORIES: Companies established in 1901, Companies disestablished in 1917, Defunct companies based in Ohio, Rolling stock manufacturers of the United States, Tram manufacturers, Trumbull County, Ohio

The Niles Car and Manufacturing Company was a United States manufacturer of railroad equipment, including many streetcar and interurban cars.[tpl]Hilton Interurbans[/tpl]  It was founded in 1901 in Niles, Ohio, and ceased producing railroad cars in 1917; the plant and equipment were purchased by the Engel Aircraft Company to produce aircraft parts for the United States Army Signal Corps.[tpl]cite news|title=Baker's Brother got a Contract|publisher=The New York Times|date=February 1, 1918|pages=1, 6|url=http://query.nytimes.com/mem/archive-free/pdf?res=9E07E3DC103FE433A25752C0A9649C946996D6CF|format=PDF[/tpl][tpl]cite book|title=The Aircraft Year Book|publisher=Manufacturers Aircraft Association, Inc.|pages=149â€“153|date=February 1919|url=http://books.google.com/books/pdf/The_Aerospace_Year_Book.pdf?id=Shnyh-IXOJcC&output=pdf|author=Faurote, Fay L. (Ed.)|location=New York City, New York|accessdate=September 30, 2007[/tpl]
The company also produced equipment for the trucking industry, an industry reference citing 2 models of 1 and 2 tons respectively, costing $1500 to $2400, utilizing a worm drive and custom bodies to suit.[tpl]cite book|title=Story of the Automobile: Its History and Development from 1760 to 1917|author=Barber, H.L.|publisher=A.J. Munson & Co.|location=Chicago, Illinois|year=1917|url=http://books.google.com/books/pdf/Story_of_the_Automobile.pdf?id=0j2YAvD2Yc8C&output=pdf|accessdate=September 30, 2007|page = 238[/tpl]
Niles specialized in building wooden-bodied cars in the heyday of interurban building. Its cars had a reputation of being well-built and stylish; Niles advertising called them "The Electric Pullmans."[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ironhorse129.com/rollingstock/builders/niles.htm|title=Niles Car & Manufacturing Company|accessdate=September 30, 2007|archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20070211200053/http://www.ironhorse129.com/rollingstock/builders/niles.htm|archivedate=February 11, 2007[/tpl][tpl]cite book|title=American Street Railway Investments: Fifteenth Annual Volume: 1908|publisher=McGraw Publishing Company|year=1908|url=http://books.google.com/books/pdf/McGraw_Electric_Railway_Manual.pdf?id=j47IQgaWJM4C&output=pdf|accessdate=September 30, 2007|page=XXI[/tpl][tpl]dead link|date=January 2013[/tpl]
