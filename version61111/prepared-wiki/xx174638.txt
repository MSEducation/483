[[KOWB]]

CATEGORIES: News and talk radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Wyoming

KOWB (1290 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a news talk information format licensed to operate in Laramie, Wyoming, USA.  The station is currently owned by Townsquare Media and features programing from Fox News Radio, Fox Sports Radio and Premiere Radio Networks.[tpl]cite web|url=http://transition.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=KOWB |title=KOWB Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==History==

The station went on the air as KOWB.[tpl]cite web|url=http://licensing.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=24700&Callsign=KOWB |title=KOWB Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl] The station won the station of the year award from the Wyoming Association of Broadcasters in 2008.
