[[Árpád Házi]]

CATEGORIES: 1908 births, 1970 deaths, People from Hajdú-Bihar County, Members of the Hungarian Working People's Party, Members of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, Hungarian Interior Ministers, Hungarian Communist Party politicians

Árpád Házi (20 September 1908 – 13 November 1970) was a Hungarian communist politician, who served as Interior Minister between 1951 and 1952.
