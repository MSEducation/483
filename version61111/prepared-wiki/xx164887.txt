[[Chattahoochee State Park]]

CATEGORIES: State parks of Alabama, Protected areas of Houston County, Alabama

Chattahoochee State Park is a state park located in the extreme southeast corner of Alabama. It is an Alabama state park and owned by the state, but it is operated by Houston County.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.encyclopediaofalabama.org/face/Article.jsp?id=h-2919 |first=Thomas V.|last=Ress |date=August 10, 2010 |title=Chattahoochee State Park |work=The Encyclopedia of Alabama |publisher=Auburn University, The University of Alabama, Alabama State Department of Education[/tpl] 
The park's [tpl]convert|22|acre|adj=on[/tpl] lake offers opportunities for fishing, swimming, and boating. There are also picnic grounds, eight hiking trails, and both primitive and RV campsites.
