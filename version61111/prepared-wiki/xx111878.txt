[[Miari]]

CATEGORIES: Red-light districts in South Korea, Seoul

Miaris / Miari is a Greek surname (roots from Corfu).

==In other fields==

Mia-ri, is one of largest red-light district in Seoul, South Korea. It is located in the Wolgok-dong area at Gireum Station. Officially illegal, it was not allowed to exist by the government and policed by local law enforcement to keep out minors.
