[[2005 Australian Formula Ford Championship]]

CATEGORIES: Australian Formula Ford Championship seasons, 2005 in Australian motorsport

The 2005 Australian Formula Ford Championship was a CAMS sanctioned national motor racing title for drivers of Formula Ford racing cars.CAMS Manual of Motor Sport, 2005, Page 17-2

==Calendar==

The title was contested over an eight round series with three races per round.

==Points==

Championship points were awarded on a 20-16-14-12-10-8-6-4-2-1 basis to the top ten finishers in each race with an additional point awarded to the driver earning pole position for Race 1 at each round.[ref]
