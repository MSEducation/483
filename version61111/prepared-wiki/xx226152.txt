[[Naramata Dam]]

CATEGORIES: Dams in Gunma Prefecture, Dams completed in 1990

Naramata Dam is a dam near Minakami, in the Gunma Prefecture of Japan. It supports a 12.2 MW hydroelectric power station.[tpl]cite web|title=Hydroelectric Power Plants in Japan - Gunma|url=http://www.industcards.com/hydro-japan-gunma.htm|publisher=IndustCards|accessdate=3 October 2010[/tpl]
