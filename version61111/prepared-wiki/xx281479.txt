[[Thomas Walkom]]

CATEGORIES: Canadian political writers, Canadian columnists, University of Toronto alumni, Living people, Toronto Star people

Thomas Walkom is national affairs columnist for the Toronto Star.http://www.thestar.com/authors.walkom_thomas.html  
Prior to his current position, he was the Star's Queen's Park columnist covering Ontario politics for eight years, including the governments of Premiers Bob Rae and Mike Harris. Walkom wrote a book, Rae Days: the rise and follies of the NDP about the Rae government. 
In the 1980s, Walkom was a parliamentary reporter for the Globe and Mail based in Ottawa and then served as the newspaper's bureau chief in Tokyo.  
He has won two National Newspaper Awards for foreign reporting and column writing.
