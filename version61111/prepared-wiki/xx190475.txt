Lesley Jackson is a London-based design curator, historian and author specialising in twentieth century design. She has published at least eleven books to date, include Twentieth Century Pattern Design from Princeton Architectural Press http://www.papress.com/bookpage.tpl?isbn=1568987129&cart=110, The Sixties: Decade of Design Revolution from Phaidon, The New Look: Design in the Fifties http://www.jstor.org/pss/1315941, and Robin and Lucienne Day: Pioneers of Contemporary Design from Mitchell Beazley. 
She curated the 'From Atoms to Patterns' exhibition at the Wellcome Collection in 2008. http://www.designweek.co.uk/Articles/138305/Wellcome+launches+1950s+materials+show.html
