[[Oleksandr Polovkov]]

CATEGORIES: 1979 births, Living people, Ukrainian footballers, FC Shakhtar Sverdlovsk players, FC Stal Alchevsk players, FC Zorya Luhansk players, Navbahor Namangan players, Ukrainian expatriate footballers, Expatriate footballers in Uzbekistan

Oleksandr Polovkov ([tpl]lang-uk|Олександр Іванович Половков[/tpl]; born 4 October 1979, Sverdlovsk, Voroshylovhrad Oblast, Ukrainian SSR, Soviet Union) is a professional Ukrainian football defender who played for different clubs in the Ukrainian Premier League. He currently plays for FC Shakhtar Sverdlovsk.
