[[Mehrabad, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Mehrabad ([tpl]lang-fa|مهراباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Mehrābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3074660|Mehrabad[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 728, in 194 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
