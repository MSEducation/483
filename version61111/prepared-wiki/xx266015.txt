[[2010 ITU Duathlon World Championships]]

CATEGORIES: 2010 in triathlon, 2010 in Scottish sport

The 2010 ITU Duathlon World Championships was a duathlon competition held in Edinburgh, Scotland from 3-5 September 2010 and organized by the International Triathlon Union (ITU). The championship course included a 10k run, a 38.4k bike, and a 5k run around the areas of Holyrood Park, Arthur’s Seat and the Scottish Parliament Building.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.triathlon.org/news/article/morrison_aernouts_take_duathlon_world_titles/|title=Morrison, Aernouts Take Duathlon World Titles|last=Culp|first=Brad|date=5 September 2010|accessdate=22 September 2011|publisher=International Triathlon Union[/tpl][tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.triathlon.org/news/article/date_set_for_edinburgh_2010/|title=Date Set for Edinburgh 2010|last=Davies|first=Gareth|date=6 July 2009|accessdate=22 September 2011|publisher=International Triathlon Union[/tpl] General Electric was the title sponsor of the championship.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.triathlon.org/news/article/three_months_until_ge_edinburgh_itu_duathlon_world_champs/|title=Three Months Until GE Edinburgh ITU Duathlon World Champs|last=Culp|first=Brad|date=17 May 2010|accessdate=22 September 2011|publisher=International Triathlon Union[/tpl] Titles for amateur duathletes, elite paraduathletes, and elite duathletes were awarded during the three days of competition.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.triathlon.org/news/article/world_duathlon_champs_head_to_edinburgh/|title=World Duathlon Champs Head to Edinburgh|last=Culp|first=Brad|date=1 September 2010|accessdate=22 September 2011|publisher=International Triathlon Union[/tpl]

==Results==

Scotland native Catriona Morrison captured the overall women's elite title, her second following her 2006 win. Bart Aernouts took the men's elite title, leading the Belgium sweep of the podium spots.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://competitor.com/2010/09/other-sites/news/morrison-aernouts-take-duathlon-world-titles-in-scotland_6454|title=Morrison, Aernouts Take Duathlon World Titles In Scotland|date=5 September 2010|accessdate=22 September 2011|publisher=Competitor Group, Inc.[/tpl]
