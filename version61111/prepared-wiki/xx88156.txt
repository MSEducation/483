[[Bittern railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1889

Bittern railway station is located on the Stony Point line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the town of Bittern opening on 17 September 1889.Bittern Vicsig Between 1921 and 1953 it was the junction station for a branch line to Red Hill.Red Hill Line Vicsig

==Platforms & services==

Bittern has one platform. It is serviced by Metro Trains' Stony Point line services.Stony Point Line timetable Public Transport Victoria
