[[Qaderlu, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Qaderlu ([tpl]lang-fa|قادرلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Qāderlū; also known as Kadirlu and Qādirlu)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3078723|Qaderlu[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 24, in 5 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
