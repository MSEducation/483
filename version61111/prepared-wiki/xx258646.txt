[[Mattias Carlsson]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Djurgårdens IF Hockey players, Södertälje SK players, 1980 births

Mattias Carlsson is a Swedish professional ice hockey winger who currently plays for Djurgårdens IF of the Elitserien. Carlsson previously played for Elitserien rivals Linköpings HC and Södertälje SK.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.aftonbladet.se/sportbladet/hockey/sverige/elitserien/djurgarden/article12935405.ab|title=Djurgården värvar målkung från SSK|last=Grefve|first=Daniel|date=26 April 2011|work=Aftonbladet|language=Swedish|accessdate=29 April 2011[/tpl]
