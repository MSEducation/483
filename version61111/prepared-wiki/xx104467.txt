[[Grrl Power]]

CATEGORIES: 2004 anime OVAs, ADV Films, Animated short films, Anime with original screenplays, Comedy anime and manga

Grrl Power, known in Japan as [tpl]nihongo|Makasete Iruka!|まかせてイルか[/tpl] is an independently produced original video animation by Akitaro Daichi. The animated short film was released simultaneously in Japan and North America on June 10, 2004. This anime was licensed for North America by ADV Films.

==Plot==

Sora, Umi and Ao are three school-aged girls who will tackle any job: delivering lunches, to doing your homework and cleaning smokestacks, in order to amass enough money to buy a nearby island and make their own country. However, they have encountered their biggest challenge yet: to convince a boy, Rikku, to go back to school when they themselves do not go.
