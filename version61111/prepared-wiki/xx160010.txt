[[Belgian general election, 1946]]

CATEGORIES: 1946 elections in Europe, Elections in Belgium, 1946 in Belgium

General elections were held in Belgium on 17 February 1946.Nohlen, D & Stöver, P (2010) Elections in Europe: A data handbook, p289 ISBN 978-3-8329-5609-7 The result was a victory for the Christian Social Party, which won 92 of the 202 seats in the Chamber of Representatives and 51 of the 101 seats in the Senate.Nohlen & Stöver, pp309-311 Voter turnout was 90.3%.Nohlen & Stöver, p290
