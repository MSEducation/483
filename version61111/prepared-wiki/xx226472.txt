[[Whispered and Shouted]]

CATEGORIES: 2007 albums, Aaron Shust albums, English-language albums

Whispered and Shouted is the second studio album released by Contemporary Christian musician Aaron Shust. It was released on June 5, 2007 and debuted at #151 on the Billboard 200.[tpl]cite web |url=[tpl]BillboardURLbyName|artist=plymouth festival chorus & orchestra|chart=all[/tpl] |title=Aaron Shust – Whispered and Shouted |work=Billboard |accessdate=2008-10-05[/tpl] [tpl]Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot[/tpl]

==Background==

Aaron Shust said of the meaning behind the album's title; "I chose the title for the project as I considered how God reveals himself to us. He whispers in the wind and shouts in the waves that he loves us and hears us."[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.christianitytoday.com/music/news/2007/thescoop-0423.html |title=The Scoop (04/23/07) |work=Christianity Today |date=2007-04-23 |accessdate=2008-10-09 |author=Andree Farias[/tpl]

==Release==

Whispered and Shouted was released in the United States on June 5, 2007. It debuted at #151 on the Billboard 200 and #17 on Billboard's Top Independent Albums chart.[tpl]cite web |url=[tpl]BillboardURLbyName|artist=plymouth festival chorus & orchestra|chart=all[/tpl] |title=Artist Chart History – Aaron Shust |work=Billboard |accessdate=2008-10-09[/tpl] The first single released from the album was the song "Give Me Words to Speak".
