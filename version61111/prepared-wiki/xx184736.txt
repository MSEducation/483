[[Burley Bearden]]

CATEGORIES: 1917 births, 1997 deaths, Texas–Arlington Mavericks football coaches, People from Waxahachie, Texas, People from Mansfield, Texas

Burley L. Bearden (August 28, 1917 – January 18, 1997) was an American football coach.  He served as the head football coach at the University of Texas at Arlington from 1966 until 1970, compiling record of 27–24.  In 1967, he led his team to victory in the Pecan Bowl, beating North Dakota State 13–0.[tpl]cite web|url=http://cfreference.net/cfr/bowl.s?id=124&season=1967|first=Ian|last=Shafer|accessdate=April 22, 2012|publisher=College Football Reference|title=Pecan Bowl (Since 1964)[/tpl]  The school discontinued its football team after completion of the 1985 season.[tpl]cite web|url=http://sports.espn.go.com/dallas/ncf/columns/story?id=6770673|publisher=ESPN.com|first=Art|last=Garcia|title=Joining the WAC is first big step-UTA's move to higher profile conference would be enhanced with addition of football|accessdate=April 22, 2012|date=July 15, 2011[/tpl]
