[[34753 Zdeněkmatyáš]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Astronomical objects discovered in 2001

34753 Zdeněkmatyáš is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1179.5103328 days (3.23 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=34753 | title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-05-24 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on August 24, 2001.
