Richar Luis Mercado Corozo (born December 20, 1986 in Esmeraldas, Ecuador) is an Ecuadorian footballer currently playing as a striker for Montevideo Wanderers in the Primera División Uruguaya.

==Career==

Mercado started his professional career playing for U.T. de Cotopaxi in August 2004, playing in the Ecuadorian Segunda Categoría. After two years in such club, he was took a big step in his career, signing with Ecuadorian giants El Nacional.
