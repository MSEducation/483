[[18676 Zdeňkaplavcová]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Astronomical objects discovered in 1998

18676 Zdeňkaplavcová is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1543.5771113 days (4.23 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=18676 | title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-05-24 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on May 30, 1998.
