[[Cytaea nausori]]

CATEGORIES: Salticidae, Endemic fauna of Fiji, Animals described in 1998

Cytaea nausori is a species of jumping spider.

==Name==

The species is named after the area where the first specimen was collected.[tpl]Citation needed|date=April 2011[/tpl]

==Appearance==

C. nausori females are about 6 mm long, males slightly longer than 4 mm.

==Distribution==

C. nausori is only known from Viti Levu in Fiji.
