[[(39618) 1994 LT]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1994, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(39618) 1994 LT is an inner main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on June 12, 1994.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
