[[Hajj Qeshlaq]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Hajj Qeshlaq ([tpl]lang-fa|حاج قشلاق[/tpl], also Romanized as Ḩājj Qeshlāq; also known as Hāj, Ḩājī Qeshlāq, Ḩājjī Qeshlāq, and Hāz Qishlāq)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3065626|Hajj Qeshlaq[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 166, in 35 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
