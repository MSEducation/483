[[Elsinoë batatas]]

CATEGORIES: Fungi described in 1943, Elsinoë, Plant pathogens and diseases

Elsinoë batatas is a species of fungus in the Elsinoaceae family. It is a plant pathogen that affects members of the genus Ipomoea, including the sweet potato. The species was described in 1943 by Ahmés Pinto Viégas and Anna Eliza Jenkins.
