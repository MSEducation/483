David Crawford Lochary (August 21, 1944 in Baltimore, Maryland â€“ July 29, 1977 in New York City)[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0516634/|title=David Lochary|publisher=IMDb|accessdate=2008-08-23[/tpl] was one of the regular "Dreamlander" actors in early films of the controversial "trash" film director John Waters.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.cnn.com/SHOWBIZ/Movies/9810/14/john.waters/index.html|title=Come on in: John Waters is just fine|date=October 14, 1998|publisher=CNN|accessdate=April 8, 2010[/tpl] He starred in such films as Pink Flamingos, Female Trouble, and Multiple Maniacs, in which he typically played exotically-dressed, sophisticated perverts. Lochary met Divine at beauty school and used to style his wigs and makeup for parties. Divine later commented that he had "never even heard the word 'drag' before David."
