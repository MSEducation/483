[[Sain, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Sain ([tpl]lang-fa|صايين[/tpl], also Romanized as Şā’īn and Sā’īn)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3081934|Sain[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 247, in 83 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
