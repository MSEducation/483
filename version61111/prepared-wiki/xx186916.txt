[[3069 Heyrovský]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Asteroids named for people, Astronomical objects discovered in 1982

3069 Heyrovský is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1317.6460254 days (3.61 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=3069 |  title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-04-28 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on October 16, 1982. It is named by Jaroslav Heyrovský.
