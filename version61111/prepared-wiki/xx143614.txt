[[Lifting Me (song)]]

CATEGORIES: The Corrs songs, 1999 singles

[tpl]Unreferenced|date=May 2008[/tpl]"Lifting Me" is a single by Irish band The Corrs, released in 1999.  It was issued as part of a Pepsi promotion and has never been available on general release.  The song was not taken from an album, although it did later appear as a bonus track on at least one version of the Best of The Corrs album.
