[[Gmina Młodzieszyn]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Sochaczew County

Gmina Młodzieszyn is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Sochaczew County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Młodzieszyn, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|9|km|mi|0[/tpl] north of Sochaczew and [tpl]convert|56|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] west of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|117.07|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 5,541.

==Villages==

Gmina Młodzieszyn contains the villages and settlements of Adamowa Góra, Bibiampol, Bieliny, Helenka, Helenów, Janów, Januszew, Juliopol, Justynów, Kamion, Kamion Dolny, Kamion Mały, Kamion Podgórny, Leontynów, Marysin, Mistrzewice, Młodzieszyn, Młodzieszynek, Nowa Wieś, Nowe Mistrzewice, Nowy Kamion, Olszynki, Radziwiłka, Rokicina, Rokiejna, Ruszki, Skutki, Stare Budy and Witkowice.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Młodzieszyn is bordered by the town of Sochaczew and by the gminas of Brochów, Iłów, Rybno, Sochaczew and Wyszogród.
