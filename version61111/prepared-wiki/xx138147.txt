[[Patrick Roscoe]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Canadian male novelists, Canadian short story writers

Patrick Roscoe is a novelist, short story writer, and actor.[tpl]cite book|last=Shelley|first=Peter|title=Frances Farmer: The Life and Films of a Troubled Star|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=1TlLcS0y2dcC&pg=PA64|accessdate=19 April 2011|date=2010-11-05|publisher=McFarland|isbn=978-0-7864-4745-9|page=64[/tpl]
He spent his childhood in East Africa, was educated in England and Canada, and later lived in California and Mexico. He is the author of seven books of fiction that have been translated into nine languages.
Roscoe currently divides his time between Canada, Spain, and North Africa.
