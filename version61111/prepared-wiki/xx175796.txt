[[WLIH]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Pennsylvania

WLIH is a Christian radio station licensed to Whitneyville, Pennsylvania, broadcasting on 107.1 MHz FM.  The station is owned by Good Christian Radio Broadcasting, Inc. WLIH fcc.gov. Accessed August 9, 2012
WLIH's programming includes Christian talk and teaching shows such as Focus on the Family, In Touch with Charles Stanley, Truths That Transform with D. James Kennedy, Insight for Living with Chuck Swindoll, and Adrian Rogers.  WPIO also airs a variety of Christian music.Programming Accessed August 9, 2012
