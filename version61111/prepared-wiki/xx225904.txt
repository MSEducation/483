Sjur Hansen Halkjeldsvik (1787 - ??) was a Norwegian politician.
He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament in 1830, representing the constituency of Romsdals Amt. He was re-elected in 1848, having been a deputy representative in 1845.Sjur Hansen Halkjeldsvik — Norwegian Social Science Data Services (NSD) He worked as a farmer.
