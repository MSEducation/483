[[Zee TV]]

CATEGORIES: Foreign television channels broadcasting in the United Kingdom, Hindi-language television channels in India, Television channels and stations established in 1992, Television stations in Mumbai, Zee Group, Essel Group, Television stations in Fiji, Television stations in South Africa, 1992 establishments in India

Zee TV is an Indian cable and satellite television channel owned and operated by Zee Entertainment Enterprises, a media and entertainment company based in Mumbai.[tpl]cite web|author=zeetv.com |url=http://www.zeetv.com/contact-us.html |title=Contact Us |publisher=zeetv.com |date= |accessdate=2010-07-16[/tpl] It primarily airs programmes in Hindi and other regional languages of India. The channel is also available in various nations of Southeast Asia, Europe, the Middle East, Africa, Australasia and North America. A part of the Essel Group, it started broadcasting on 2 October 1992 as the first Hindi-language cable channel in India.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.britannica.com/EBchecked/topic/285248/India/46449/Media-and-publishing#ref975375 |title=India : Media and publishing - Britannica Online Encyclopedia |publisher=Britannica.com |date= |accessdate=2012-11-03[/tpl]  
The channel's programming includes Ek Mutthi Aasmaan (TV series), Sapne Suhane Ladakpan Ke, Jodha Akbar (TV series), Do Dil Bandhe Ek Dori Se, Pavitra Rishta, Kumkum Bhagya, Aur Pyaar Ho Gaya (TV series), Doli Armaano Ki, Fear Files: Darr Ki Sacchi Tasvirein, Dance India Dance Li'l Masters (season 3).

==History==

Zee TV was founded by Subhash Chandra and was launched in India on 1 October 1992 as a Hindi satellite channel. Zee formerly had a partnership with STAR TV. However, STAR TV ended their partnership with ZEE TV when Rupert Murdoch's News Corporation acquired STAR TV. Zee TV was launched in the UK in March 1995, which acquired[tpl]huh|date=April 2014[/tpl] TV Asia,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www2.tv-ark.org.uk/otherchannels/tvasia.html|title=TV Asia|publisher=TVARK|accessdate=2011-06-24[/tpl] the first Asian channel to be launched in the UK and Europe. Zee TV was launched in the USA on 15 July 1998. In the USA, some of programmes are subtitled in English and some commercials are in English. An HD feed first became available in India, Zee TV HD launched in the US on 5 September 2012 on Dish Network.  
In December 1994, Zee TV also launched a sister channel called El TV. It was an entertainment channel initially.  It was converted into a General Entertainment cum political News based channel called Zee India TV which in turn was converted into the 24-hour News channel (Zee News) around 1998-99.
