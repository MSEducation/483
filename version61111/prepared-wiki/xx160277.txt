[[Throwim Way Leg]]

CATEGORIES: Science books, Australian books, Books by Tim Flannery, Western New Guinea

Throwim' Way Leg: Tree-Kangaroos, Possums, and Penis Gourds is a book written by Australian zoologist and climate change activist Tim Flannery. It documents Flannery's experiences in the highlands of Papua New Guinea and Indonesian Western New Guinea. The book describes the flora and fauna of the island and the cultures of the various tribes. The title is an anglicised spelling of the New Guinean Pidgin "Toromwe Lek," to go on a journey.
It is a syllabus choice for the HSC English Advanced course in NSW, Australia.
