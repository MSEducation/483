[[Xander (Dutch singer)]]

CATEGORIES: Dutch male singers, Popstars contestants, 1985 births, Living people, People from Rotterdam

Xander Venema (born in Nieuw-Amsterdam, Netherlands on 27 August 1985)[tpl]cite web|url=http://xanderfan.hyves.nl/ |title=Xander fansite |publisher=Xanderfan.hyves.nl |date= |accessdate=2014-01-22[/tpl] performing under his mononym Xander is a Dutch singer, songwriter who was part of the Dutch boyband 4 AM. He took part in Dutch series 2 of Popstars finishing 11th. After Popstars, he has continued with a solo musical career.

==Beginnings==

Coming from a musical family, where almost every family member sing or play a musical instrument. At age six he started playing keyboards when he was eight he started singing and composing songs and learnt playing the piano. He also studied Media and Entertainment. Around the age of 16 he made a demo, signed a music deal with Willem van Kooten and Eelco van Cooten  and released an independent single.

==In boyband 4 AM==

In 2006, he auditioned for the Amsterdam-based Dutch boy band 4 AM[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.youtube.com/user/band4am |title=4 AM MySpace page |publisher=Youtube.com |date= |accessdate=2014-01-22[/tpl] and became a member. 4 AM was composed of Koen, Jeff and Daan besides Xander. Xander and 4 AM had a minor hit with the Las Vegas-themed "Wake Up Call"[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FdUBxHrHmCA |title=Music video for 4AM's "Wake Up Call" |publisher=Youtube.com |date= |accessdate=2014-01-22[/tpl] that reached #24 in the MegaTop 100http://www.madeindrenthe.nl/index.php?option=com_artistavenue&task=singleArtist&id=416&Itemid=0 followed by "Livin' on Love"[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=c4sw37GZHN8 |title=Music video for "Livin' on Love" |publisher=Youtube.com |date= |accessdate=2014-01-22[/tpl] The band also appeared on the Hummertime, a Dutch television reality show on Jetix television.

==Popstars Netherlands==

In 2008, he auditioned unsuccessfully in the Netherlands to the first season of the Dutch version of Popstars.
In 2009, he applied for a second time to season 2 (2009–2010) of the competition where he sang "Arms of s Woman" from Amos Lee.Muzieknu.nl Xander – Popstars 2009 (in Dutch) Known as Xander, he was picked as one of the Final 14. He sang "Built to Last" from Mêlée in week 1, "Patience" from Take That in week 2 and was eliminated from competition on 5 December 2009 finishing 11th overall, after singing "Patience" once again and "I Can't Stop Loving You" from Leo Sayer / Phil Collins in a sing-off with the contestants Joshua Newton and Dewi Pechler.

Xander has continued a solo career after Popstar. He performs usually at the Lef gay club in Rotterdam, Netherlands.Xander bij laatste 10[tpl]dead link|date=January 2014[/tpl] He sings in English, Dutch, Spanish and Italian.
