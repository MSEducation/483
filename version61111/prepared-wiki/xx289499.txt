[[Yasti Bulagh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Yasti Bulagh ([tpl]lang-fa|ياستي بولاغ[/tpl], also Romanized as Yāstī Būlāgh; also known as Yāstī Bolāgh and Yāsu Bulāq)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3088554|Yasti Bulagh[/tpl] is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 308, in 54 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
