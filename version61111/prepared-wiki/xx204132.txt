[[Russian Goal]]

CATEGORIES: Russian nationalist organizations, Politics of Russia, Fascist parties in Russia, Far-right politics in Russia

Russian Goal (Russian: Русская Цель) is a militant far-right organization in Russia, at one time led by Semyon Tokmakov. The group achieved international notoriety in 1999 when its members, led by Tokmakov, attacked William Jefferson, a black United States Marine embassy guard.  Tokmakov was arrested for the attack, but amnestied after receiving a short jail sentence.Russian Skinheads Mark Hitler's Birthday
