[[Cabramatta state by-election, 2008]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 elections in Australia, New South Wales state by-elections

A by-election was held for the New South Wales Legislative Assembly seat of Cabramatta on 18 October 2008 to coincide with the Lakemba, Port Macquarie and Ryde by-elections.  The Cabramatta by-election was triggered by the resignation of sitting member and former Health Minister Reba Meagher, after a spectacular series of events[tpl]Citation needed|date=November 2010[/tpl] that saw both the Deputy Premier and the Premier resign.
The seat was retained by the Labor Party at the by-election, but with a sharp decline in the party's vote.
