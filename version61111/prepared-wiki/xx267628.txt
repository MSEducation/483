[[Stockland Traralgon]]

CATEGORIES: Shopping centres in Victoria (Australia), Shopping malls established in 1985

Stockland Traralgon is a shopping centre located in the CBD of Traralgon, Victoria.
Opened in 1985 it was originally named Traralgon Centre Plaza, until acquisition by Stocklands during the 2000s. It is home to the largest Coles Supermarket in the Gippsland Area.
