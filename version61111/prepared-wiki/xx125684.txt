[[Kimball Medical Center]]

CATEGORIES: Hospitals established in 1913, Hospitals in New Jersey, Buildings and structures in Ocean County, New Jersey, Lakewood Township, New Jersey, U.S. Route 9

Kimball Medical Center, formerly Paul Kimball Hospital, is a hospital located in Lakewood Township, New Jersey, United States, that serves parts of northern Ocean County and southern Monmouth County. The hospital is affiliated with the Barnabas Health. Kimball Medical Center also operates two satellite facilities: a psychiatric hospital known as Kimball Behavioral Health (with 100 inpatient beds) in Toms River and The Center for Healthy Living in Lakewood.
Kimball originated as a small community hospital in 1913.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.barnabashealthnurses.org/affiliate/kimball.html |title=Our Affiliates - Kimball Medical Center - Barnabas Health Patient Care Services - Nursing, NJ |publisher=Barnabashealthnurses.org |date= |accessdate=2012-05-11[/tpl] Its first six operating rooms were built in 1969, with two more added in 1985.
