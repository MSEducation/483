Theodorus ("Theo") Jacob de Jong (born 11 August 1947FIFA profile in Leeuwarden) is a former Dutch footballer and now coach.
During his career he played for NEC Nijmegen and Feyenoord Rotterdam.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://voetbalstats.nl/ec/sp628.html|title=Europa Cup Cijfiers van: Theo de Jong[/tpl] He earned 15 caps and scored 3 goals for the Netherlands national football team, and played for them in the 1974 FIFA World Cup Final.
De Jong was a member of the Feyenoord team that won the UEFA Cup in 1974, beating Tottenham Hotspur in the then two-legged final.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.feyenoordgeschiedenis.net/wie-is-wie/i-j.htm#theo-de-jong|title=Feyenoord  Wie is Wie[/tpl] He later played 3 seasons in Hong Kong for Seiko.[tpl]NFT player|pid=19433|de Jong, Theo[/tpl]
Theo's son, Dave de Jong, is also a footballer.
