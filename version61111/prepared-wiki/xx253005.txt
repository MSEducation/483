[[Shankar Oraon]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 births, Living people, Indian footballers, Prayag United S.C. players, Mohun Bagan A.C. players, I-League players, People from Chennai, Footballers from Tamil Nadu

Shankar Oraon is an Indian footballer who is currently playing for Mohun Bagan A.C. in the I-League as a forward.

==Career==

===Prayag United===

On April 2, 2011 Oraon scored his first goal for Prayag United S.C. against Dempo, this was also Oraon's first professional game of his career. He then scored again in his second game of his career against Churchill Brothers

===Mohun Bagan===

After spending three years at Prayag United S.C.
