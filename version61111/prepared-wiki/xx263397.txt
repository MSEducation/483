[[Aljazeera International Documentary Film Festival]]

CATEGORIES: Al Jazeera

Al Jazeera International Documentary Film Festival is an international film festival showcasing documentaries and it is organized by Al Jazeera. It is directed by Abbas Arnaout. The first festival was held on April 18, 2005. Every year the festival has a different theme.
