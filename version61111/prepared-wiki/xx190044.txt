Lu Feng ([tpl]zh|s=陆峰[/tpl]; born 12 November 1981 in Luoyang) is a Chinese footballer who currently plays as a midfielder for Henan Jianye in the Chinese Super League.

==Club career==

Lu Feng started his professional football career with Henan Jianye in 2001, however he rose to prominace in 2003 when in appeared in 26 league games.[tpl]NFT player|pid=25895|Lù, Fēng|accessdate=2012-08-07[/tpl] This led to the transfer to Qingdao and a chance to play in the Chinese Super League. After two seasons with Qingdao he returned to Henan Jianye in their successful fight for promotion to the Chinese Super League after winning the 2006 China League One division with them.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.rsssf.com/tablesc/china06.html|title=China 2006|publisher=rsssf.com|date=8 Mar 2007|accessdate=2012-08-07[/tpl] He would be a vital member of the team as the club survived within the league and gradually saw them improve  their league standings until they reached third within the 2009 Chinese Super League season and qualified for the 2010 AFC Champions League for the first time.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.rsssf.com/tablesc/china09.html|title=China 2009|publisher=rsssf.com|date=23 Apr 2010|accessdate=2012-08-07[/tpl] Within the tournament he would go on to play in five games as Henan were knocked out within the group stages.

==International career==

Despite playing for a second tier club, Lu Feng was considered a promising young player and was given his debut cap against Jordan on the 9th of December 2002 in a 0-0 friendly.[tpl]cite web|url=http://teamchina.freehostia.com/en/match.php?date=2002-12-09|title=China PR 0-0 Jordan|publisher=teamchina.freehostia.com|date=2002-12-09|accessdate=2012-08-07[/tpl] An unimpressive performance saw Lu Feng having to wait six more years before he was given his second cap, against Mexico on April 16, 2008 in a 1-0 friendly defeat.[tpl]cite web|url=http://teamchina.freehostia.com/en/match.php?date=2008-04-16|title=China PR 0-1 Mexico|publisher=teamchina.freehostia.com|date=2008-04-16|accessdate=2012-08-07[/tpl]
