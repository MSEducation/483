[[Gu Yasha]]

CATEGORIES: 1990 births, Living people, Chinese women's footballers, Footballers at the 2008 Summer Olympics, Olympic footballers of China, People from Zhengzhou, Footballers from Henan

Gu Yasha ([tpl]zh|c=古雅沙|p=Gǔ Yǎshā[/tpl]; born November 28, 1990 in Zhengzhou, Henan) is a female Chinese football player who competed for the national team in the 2008 Summer Olympics. Her position is that of midfielder.
