[[Inna Tsymbalyuk]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1985 births, Miss Universe 2006 contestants, People from Chernivtsi, Ukrainian actresses, Ukrainian female models, Ukrainian beauty pageant winners

Inna Tsymbalyuk ([tpl]lang-ua|Инна Цимбалюк[/tpl]; born June 11, 1985) is a Ukrainian actress and model who was crowned Miss Ukraine Universe 2006.
