[[Single Cell Orchestra]]

CATEGORIES: American electronic musicians

Single Cell Orchestra is the performing name of Miguel Fierro (born Miguel Angelo Fierro), a San Francisco-born musician who has worked for many years in electronic music and techno.[tpl]cite web|last=Cooper|first=Sean|title=Single Cell Orchestra biography|url=http://allmusic.com/artist/single-cell-orchestra-p173435/biography|publisher=allmusic.com|accessdate=10 November 2011[/tpl]  His first successful single was "Transmit Liberation", an underground hit that proved influential in the development of trip hop.  His first full-length was Dead Vent 7 (1995), followed by Single Cell Orchestra (1996).[tpl]cite web|last=Ankeny|first=Jason|title=Single Cell Orchestra album review|url=http://allmusic.com/album/single-cell-orchestra-r241849|publisher=allmusic.com|accessdate=10 November 2011[/tpl]  Later that year, he collaborated with Daum Bentley (of Freaky Chakra) to produce an album entitled Freaky Chakra vs Single Cell Orchestra.
