[[Taiwan Stock Exchange]]

CATEGORIES: Economy of Taiwan, Stock exchanges in Asia, Companies listed on the Taiwan Stock Exchange

The Taiwan Stock Exchange Corporation (TSEC; [tpl]zh|t=臺灣證券交易所|p=Táiwān Zhèngquàn Jiāoyì Suǒ[/tpl]) is a financial institution, located in Taipei 101, in Taipei, Taiwan. The TSEC was established in 1961 and began operating as a stock exchange on 9 February 1962. It is regulated by the Financial Supervisory Commission.
As of 31 December 2013, the Taiwan Stock Exchange had 809 listed companies with a combined market capitalization of NT$ 24,519,622 million.http://www.twse.com.tw/ch/about/press_room/tsec_news_detail.php?id=13354
The exchange has normal trading sessions from 09:30am to 05:30pm and post-market sessions from 05:30pm to 06:00pm on all days of the week except Saturdays, Sundays and holidays declared by the Exchange in advance.Market Hours, Taiwan Stock Exchange via Wikinvest
The current chairman of the TSEC is Lee Sush-der,[tpl]cite web|title=Lee Sush-der appointed as Chairman of Taiwan Stock Exchange|url=http://www.twse.com.tw/en/about/press_room/tsec_news_detail.php?id=11501|publisher=Taiwan Stock Exchange|accessdate=2013-10-24|date=2013-03-01[/tpl] The President is Michael Lin.TWSE executives
