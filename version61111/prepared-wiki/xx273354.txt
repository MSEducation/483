[[Gega Diasamidze]]

CATEGORIES: 1992 births, Living people

Gega Diasamidze (born May 8, 1992) is a Georgian footballer who currently plays as a midfielder for Danish Superliga side Aarhus Gymnastikforening. 
Gega Diasamidze was invited to train with AGF's resv team in January 2011. And signed a contract with AGF Aarhus in the spring of 2011, then began to train with the A squad. 
Another AGF Georgian footballer Davit Devdariani recommended AGF Aarhus to get Davit Skhirtladze and Giorgi Diasamidze
