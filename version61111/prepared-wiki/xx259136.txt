[[MythBusters (2011 season)]]

CATEGORIES: Lists of MythBusters episodes

The cast of the television series MythBusters perform experiments to verify or debunk urban legends, old wives' tales, and the like. This is a list of the various myths tested on the show as well as the results of the experiments (the myth is Busted, Plausible, or Confirmed). On March 16, 2011, Discovery Channel announced that the 2011 season would commence airing on April 6, 2011.[tpl]cite press release|url=http://press.discovery.com/us/dsc/press-releases/2011/new-episodes-discovery-channels-mythbusters-r-1196|title=New Episodes of Discovery Channel's "Mythbusters" Return April 6 at 9PM ET/PT|publisher=Discovery Communications|date=March 16, 2011|accessdate=March 19, 2011[/tpl]
