[[Belgrave (Puffing Billy) railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Tourist railway stations in Melbourne

Belgrave (Puffing Billy) railway station is situated in Belgrave, a suburb of Melbourne in the Australian state of Victoria. It is the inner terminal of the famous Puffing Billy heritage steam railway.
