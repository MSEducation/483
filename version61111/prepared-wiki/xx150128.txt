[[Estonia at the 1928 Summer Olympics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 1928 Summer Olympics, Estonia at the Olympics, 1928 in Estonia

Estonia competed at the 1928 Summer Olympics in Amsterdam, Netherlands.

==Medalists==

==The 1928 Estonian Olympic Team==

Estonia sent 20 athletes and 3 representatives to those games. 
Representatives were Harald Tammer, Arnold Veiss and Johannes Villemson.
Estonians in other delegations were 1924 Summer Olympics bronze medalist Aleksander Klumberg, who was athletics coach for [tpl]flagIOCteam|POL|1928 Summer[/tpl] and Albert Vollrat massage therapist in Hungarian olympic team.
