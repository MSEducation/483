[[C.D. UDET]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in El Salvador, 1951 establishments in El Salvador, Association football clubs established in 1951

Club Deportivo UDET  is a Salvadoran professional football club based in El Tránsito, San Miguel  El Salvador.
The club currently plays in the Tercera Division de Fútbol Salvadoreño.

==History==

Founded by a local tailor in 1951, the team was exclusively made up of tailors in its first years. UDET has swapped divisions over the years but never made it to the Premier Division.UDET no está de tránsito - El Diario de Hoy [tpl]es icon[/tpl]
During the 2004 Clausura season, UDET only managed to include 10 players in the lineup against A.D. Municipal. Municipal was given the win and UDET was threatened with demotion to the Third Division.El UDET se va a Tercera - El Diario de Hoy [tpl]es icon[/tpl]
