[[(18413) 1993 LD1]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1993, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(18413) 1993 LD1 is a main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on June 13, 1993.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
In 2012, Asteroid 18413 was named in honour of Adam Spencer, an Australian broadcaster, mathematician and stand up comedian.
