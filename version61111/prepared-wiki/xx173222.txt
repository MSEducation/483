The 2007 Capital One Bowl was held on January 1, 2007 at the Citrus Bowl in Orlando, Florida.  The game featured the Badgers of The University of Wisconsin, who finished tied for 2nd in the Big Ten Conference, and the Razorbacks of The University of Arkansas, who finished 1st in the Southeastern Conference's West Division.
