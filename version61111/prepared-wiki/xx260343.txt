[[Gregory Manchess]]

CATEGORIES: American illustrators, Living people, American portrait painters, 20th-century American painters, 21st-century American painters, Contemporary painters, Minneapolis College of Art and Design alumni

Gregory Manchess is American illustrator from Kentucky. His illustrations have appeared in magazines, digital murals,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.artonagrandscale.com/manchess.html |title=Over 30 Award Winning Artists & Muralists for Commission | Large Format Architectural Murals in Public and Private Spaces | Artist Gregory Manchess |publisher=Art On A Grand Scale |date= |accessdate=2011-10-10[/tpl] illustrated movie posters, advertising campaigns and book covers  including sixty covers for Louis L’Amour. His work had appeared on Major League Baseball World Series Programs, Time, Newsweek, The Atlantic Monthly, Playboy, Smithsonian and The National Geographic.  His style includes broad brush strokes and excellent figure work.
He earned a BFA from the Minneapolis College of Art and Design in 1977.  He spent the next two years as a studio illustrator with Hellman Design Associates which was led by Gary Kelley.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.societyillustrators.org/At-the-Museum/2011/Greg-Manchess/Greg-Manchess.aspx|title=Society of Illustrators:Greg Manchess|accessdate=2011-02-04[/tpl]
He lectures frequently at universities and colleges nationwide and gives workshops on painting at the Norman Rockwell Museum in Stockbridge, MA. He leads an Illustration Master Class in Amherst, MA.
He is represented by Richard Solomon[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.linesandcolors.com/2006/09/01/gregory-manchess/ |title=a blog about drawing, painting, illustration, comics, concept art and other visual arts » Gregory Manchess |publisher=lines and colors |date= |accessdate=2011-10-10[/tpl]
