[[Lower Glenelg National Park]]

CATEGORIES: National parks of Victoria (Australia), Protected areas established in 1969, 1969 establishments in Australia

Lower Glenelg is a national park in Victoria, Australia, 323 km west of Melbourne. The major features are the Glenelg River gorge and the Princess Margaret Rose Cave.Parks Victoria, retrieved 25 January 2013
