Zakrzówek [tpl]IPAc-pl|z|a|'|k|sz|u|w|e|k[/tpl] is a village in Kraśnik County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It is the seat of the gmina (administrative district) called Gmina Zakrzówek. It lies approximately [tpl]convert|11|km|mi|0[/tpl] east of Kraśnik and [tpl]convert|38|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south of the regional capital Lublin.
