[[Sega Classics Collection]]

CATEGORIES: PlayStation 2 games, PlayStation 2-only games, Sega video games, Video game compilations, 2005 video games

Sega Classics Collection is a PlayStation 2 compilation that contains new 3D versions of classic Sega titles. The original games were released separately as part of the Sega Ages games in Japan. This game is not to be confused with the similarly titled Sega Genesis Collection, which contains 28 emulated classic games for the Sega Mega Drive/Genesis console in their original form includes three of the games in this collection (Bonanza Bros., Columns, Golden Axe).
