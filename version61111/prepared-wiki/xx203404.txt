[[Little Sheep Group]]

CATEGORIES: Little Sheep Group, Companies listed on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange, Companies based in Inner Mongolia, Privately held companies of China, Chinese restaurants, Restaurants in China, Companies established in 1999, Companies formerly listed on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange, 1999 establishments in China

 Little Sheep Group Limited is a private company founded in 1999 in Baotou, Inner Mongolia, China. It specializes in operating hot pot restaurants, condiment processing and meat processing.
Little Sheep currently has over 300 chain restaurants in China (including Hong Kong, Macau), Taiwan and also the United States, the United Kingdom, Japan, South Korea and Canada.
In 2007, it was awarded as the most popular Chinese hot pot restaurant by Chinese Restaurant Association. 
It was listed on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange in 2008.[tpl]cite web|url=http://financeasia.com/article.aspx?CIaNID=76287 |title=Hong Kong ship owner seeks to re-list |publisher=Financeasia.com |date= |accessdate=2011-12-21[/tpl]
Little Sheep posted ¥2 billion in revenues in 2010, or 2% of dining-out receipts in China, according to Pei Liang, secretary-general of the China Chain Store & Franchise Association.[tpl]cite web|last=Jin |first=Tony |url=http://www.thechinaperspective.com/articles/uncertaintysurf-8863/ |title=Uncertainty Surfaces Over Yum's Takeover of Little Sheep |publisher=Thechinaperspective.com |date=2011-10-27 |accessdate=2011-12-21[/tpl]
In 2011, Little Sheep was sold to Yum! Brands Inc, a U.S.-based fast food franchise conglomerate (headquartered in Louisville, Kentucky).  Yum agreed in May 2011 to acquire Little Sheep for $587 million, however the deal was delayed for four months as the Chinese Ministry of Commerce conducted an anti-trust investigation.[tpl]cite web|last=Guang |first=Yin |url=http://www.thechinaperspective.com/articles/chinacorporaten-8897/ |title=China Corporate News: Yum's Little Sheep Takeover Review Cleared |publisher=Thechinaperspective.com |date=2011-11-08 |accessdate=2011-12-21[/tpl]
