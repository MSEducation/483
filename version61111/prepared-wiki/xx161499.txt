The hamlet has a population of about 30,[tpl]citation needed|date=May 2012[/tpl] and is within the civil parish of Potterhanworth. It is adjacent to Potterhanworth Fen to the south-east. Potter Hanworth Wood, a Site of Special Scientific Interest, is [tpl]convert|700|yd|m[/tpl] to the south.
Potterhanworth Booths takes its name from the village of Potterhanworth, [tpl]convert|1.5|mi|km|1[/tpl] to the south-west, whose name refers to the early clay industries set up in the local area.[tpl]citation needed|date=May 2012[/tpl]
