[[Gabriela Duarte]]

Gabriela Duarte Franco Goldfuss (born April 15, 1974 in Campinas, São Paulo) is a Brazilian actress. She is also the daughter of actress Regina Duarte.

==Biography==

She is the daughter of actress Regina Duarte and businessman Marcos Flávio Cunha Franco. She worked in the film O Vestido, which won her the award for Best Actress at the Festival de Cinema Ibero-Americano de Huelva.[tpl]cite web|url=http://cinema.terra.com.br/noticias/0,,OI213045-EI1176,00-Festival+de+Huelva+consagra+Gabriela+Duarte.html|title=Festival de Huelva consagra Gabriela Duarte |work=Cinema.terra|date=17 November 2003|accessdate=4 November 2011[/tpl] Her first film was in 1983, O Cangaceiro Trapalhão.
