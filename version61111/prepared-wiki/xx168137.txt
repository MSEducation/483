[[Brihthelm (bishop of London)]]

CATEGORIES: Bishops of London, 10th-century bishops, 950s deaths, Year of birth unknown

Brihthelm or Beorhthelm (died between 957 and 959) was a medieval Bishop of London.
Brihthelm was consecrated between 951 and 953 and he died between 957 and 959.Fryde, et al. Handbook of British Chronology p. 220
