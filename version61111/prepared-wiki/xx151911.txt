[[Kage-ryū]]

CATEGORIES: Koryu bujutsu, Japanese swordsmanship

[tpl]nihongo|Kage-ryū|影流[/tpl]Kanji according to www.koryu.com – Kage-ryu (Kage = shade; shadow; other side). is a Japanese koryu martial art founded in the late Muromachi period ca 1550 by Yamamoto Hisaya Masakatsu.
The system teaches battojutsu using very long swords known as choken.
