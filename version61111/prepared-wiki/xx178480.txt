[[Fionnuala Ellwood]]

CATEGORIES: 1964 births, Living people, Irish television actresses, People from Dublin (city)

Fionnuala Ellwood (born 3 July 1964 in Dublin, Ireland)[tpl]IMDb name|id=0255339[/tpl] is an actress best known for portraying Lynn Whiteley in the ITV soap Emmerdale between 1989 and 1994. Other appearances include Doctors, Grange Hill, Holby City and Casualty.
Away from television, Ellwood is a qualified scuba diver.
