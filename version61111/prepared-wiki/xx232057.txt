[[2003 Kremlin Cup]]

CATEGORIES: 2003 ATP Tour, 2003 WTA Tour, Kremlin Cup, 2003 in Russia

The 2003 Kremlin Cup was a tennis tournament played on indoor carpet courts at the Olympic Stadium in Moscow in Russia that was part of the International Series of the 2003 ATP Tour and of Tier I of the 2003 WTA Tour. The tournament ran from September 29 through October 5, 2003.
