[[Serena Armstrong-Jones, Viscountess Linley]]

CATEGORIES: 1970 births, Living people, People from County Limerick, British socialites, British courtesy viscountesses, People educated at Heathfield School, Ascot, Stanhope family, Armstrong-Jones family

Serena Alleyne Armstrong-Jones, Viscountess Linley (née Stanhope; born 1 March 1970) is an Anglo-Irish aristocrat, and by marriage, a niece-in-law of Queen Elizabeth II and Prince Philip, Duke of Edinburgh.

==Early life==

Lady Linley was born in Limerick, Ireland. She is the daughter of Charles Stanhope, 12th Earl of Harrington, and the former Virginia Freeman Jackson.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.thepeerage.com/p10274.htm#i102734 |title=Person Page 10274 |publisher=Thepeerage.com |date= |accessdate=30 June 2012[/tpl] Her parents divorced when she was 13 years old. Through her father, Lady Linley descends from Charles II of England's illegitimate child, Henry FitzRoy, 1st Duke of Grafton.Charles Mosley, editor, Burke's Peerage, Baronetage & Knightage, 107th edition, 3 volumes (Wilmington, Delaware, U.S.A.: Burke's Peerage (Genealogical Books) Ltd, 2003), volume 2, page 1796. This makes her a distant cousin of Diana, Princess of Wales.
