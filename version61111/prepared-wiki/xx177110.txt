[[Patrick Baumann]]

Patrick Baumann (born 8 January 1982) is a former Swiss professional association football player.
In summer 2006, he signed a 1-year contract with FC Thun.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.fcthun.ch/index.php?id=64&L=0&tx_ttnewsbackPid=79&tx_ttnewspointer=14&tx_ttnewstt_news=102&cHash=892540c621|title=Patrick Baumann unterschreibt beim FC Thun|accessdate=2010-06-15|work=FC Thun|language=German[/tpl] But since the second half of the season was excluded in the squad. In 2007-08 season he joined 1. Liga side Grenchen, as Kurt Baumann the head coach of the team is his father.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.nachrichten.ch/detail/276298.htm|title=Fussball: News und Transfers|work=nachrichten.ch|date=2007-05-22|accessdate=2010-06-15|language=German[/tpl]
He was the member of Swiss U21 team at 2004 UEFA European Under-21 Football Championship.
