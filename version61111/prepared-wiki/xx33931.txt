[[Atto-]]

CATEGORIES: Metric prefixes

Atto- (symbol a) is a prefix in the metric system denoting a factor of 10−18 or [tpl]gaps|0.000|000|000|000|000|001[/tpl].
The unit multiple was adopted at the 12th Conférence Générale des Poids et Mesures (CGPM) in Resolution 8.[tpl]SIbrochure8th[/tpl] It is derived from the Danish word atten, meaning "eighteen".
