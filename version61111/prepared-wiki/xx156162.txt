[[Hatch Lane (ward)]]

CATEGORIES: Wards of Waltham Forest

Hatch Lane Ward is a political division of the London Borough of Waltham Forest, and is one of the six Waltham Forest wards of the Chingford and Woodford Green Constituency represented by Iain Duncan Smith MP.
The ward is represented at the council by three councillors with elections every four years. At the previous local election on 4 May 2006 Laurie Braham, Marion Fitzgerald, and Geoff Walker, all Conservative Party candidates, were returned.
