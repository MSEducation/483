[[1987 Copa América Final]]

CATEGORIES: 1987 Copa América, Uruguay national football team matches, Chile national football team matches, Copa América finals

The 1987 Copa América Final was the final match of the 1987 Copa América.It was held on July 12, 1987 in Buenos Aires.Uruguay won the match against Chile by a 1-0 score.This was the ninth cup for Uruguay and the second and last (to date) final for Chile.
