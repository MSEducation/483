Arthur Addison Studenroth (October 9, 1899 - March 14, 1992) was an American athlete who competed mainly in the Cross Country Team.
He competed for the United States in the 1924 Summer Olympics held in Paris, France in the Cross Country Team where he won the silver medal with his team mates Earl Johnson and August Fager.
