[[Pir Bakran District]]

CATEGORIES: Falavarjan County, Districts of Isfahan Province

Pir Bakran District ([tpl]lang-fa|بخش پیربکران[/tpl]) is a district (bakhsh) in Falavarjan County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 50,417, in 13,687 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|10[/tpl]  The District has two cities: Pir Bakran and Baharan Shahr.  The District has two rural districts (dehestan): Garkan-e Shomali Rural District and Sohr va Firuzan Rural District.
