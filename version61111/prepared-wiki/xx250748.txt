[[Pinch wa Chance – Baka ni Narō ze!]]

CATEGORIES: 2009 singles

[tpl]nihongo|"Pinchi wa Chansu - Baka ni Narō ze!"|ピンチはチャンス　バカになろうぜ！[/tpl] is the second indies single by Japanese girl group Melon Kinenbi, in a collaboration with [tpl]nihongo|New Rote'ka|ニューロティカ|"Neurotica"[/tpl]. It was released as limited distribution on July 22, 2009. People that purchased the single from the Tower Records online store received a free original computer wallpaper.
