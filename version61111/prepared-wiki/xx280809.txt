[[Sulichay]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Sulichay ([tpl]lang-fa|سولي چاي[/tpl], also Romanized as Sūlīchāy)[tpl]IranNCSGN[/tpl] is a village in Bonab Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 107, in 26 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
