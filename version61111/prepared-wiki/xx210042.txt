[[Neya River]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of Kostroma Oblast, Volga basin

Neya ([tpl]lang-ru|Не́я[/tpl]) is a river in Kostroma Oblast, Russia. It is a tributary of the Unzha River (in Volga's drainage basin). It is [tpl]convert|253|km|sp=us[/tpl] long, with a drainage basin of [tpl]convert|6060|km2|sp=us[/tpl].
The town of Neya is situated by the Neya River.
