[[2004 Forest Hills Tennis Classic]]

CATEGORIES: 2004 WTA Tour, Forest Hills Tennis Classic

The 2004 Forest Hills Tennis Classic was a women's tennis tournament played on outdoor hard courts. It was the 1st edition of the Forest Hills Tennis Classic, and was part of the Tier V Series of the 2004 WTA Tour. It took place in Forest Hills, New York City, United States from August 24 through August 28, 2004.
