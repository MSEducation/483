Leora Dana (April 1, 1923, New York City, New York â€“ December 13, 1983, New York City, New York) was an American film, stage and television actress.
She won the Tony Award for Best Performance by a Featured Actress in a Play in 1973 for The Last of Mrs. Lincoln.

==Personal life==

She was married to actor Kurt Kasznar from 1950 to 1958; the union ended in divorce. 

==Death==

Leora Dana died of cancer, aged 60, in 1983
