[[WGTN-FM]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in South Carolina, Easy listening radio stations

WGTN-FM (100.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting an easy listening format. It is licensed to Andrews, South Carolina, USA.  The station is owned by Coastline Communications of Carolina, Inc.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=WGTN-FM |title=WGTN-FM Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==History==

The station went on the air as WQSC on 12 April 1984.  On 30 March 1990, the station changed its call sign to the current WGTN.[tpl]cite web|url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=23898&Callsign=WGTN |title=WGTN Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl] At that time, the format was adult contemporary.
At one time[tpl]vague|date=February 2014[/tpl], this station aired the programming of WYNA, which was classic hits "Cool 104.9" and later Bob FM, "We play anything." Now it airs the easy listening music of WEZV.Steve Palisin, "Radio Format and Station Changes in Progress," The Sun News, September 7, 2008.
