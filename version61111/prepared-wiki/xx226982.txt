[[Franz Lemnitz]]

CATEGORIES: 1890 births, 1942 deaths, German male cyclists, Olympic cyclists of Germany, Cyclists at the 1912 Summer Olympics, People from Bad Dürrenberg, Sportspeople from Saxony-Anhalt

Franz Lemnitz (11 July 1890 – 1942) was a German road racing cyclist who competed in the 1912 Summer Olympics.
He was born in Tollwitz.
In 1912 he was a member of the German cycling team which finished sixth in the team time trial event.
