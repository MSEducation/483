[[Berardia]]

CATEGORIES: Cynareae, Asteraceae genera, Monotypic plant genera

Berardia is a monotypic genus of flowering plants in the aster family, Asteraceae, containing the single species Berardia subacaulis. It is an alpine plant native to the Alps.Garcia-Jacas, N., et al. (2002). Tribal and subtribal delimitation and phylogeny of the Cardueae (Asteraceae): a combined nuclear and chloroplast DNA analysis. Molecular Phylogenetics and Evolution 22(1), 51-64.
