[[Bana, Burkina Faso]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in the Boucle du Mouhoun Region, Balé Province

Bana is a town in the Bana Department of Balé Province in southern Burkina Faso. It is the capital of the Bana Department and the town has a total population of 2769.Burkinabé government inforoute communale
