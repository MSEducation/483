[[Mount Buggery]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of Victoria (Australia)

Mount Buggery is the name of two mountains in the Australian state of Victoria. The more well known peak lies on the end of a ridgeline known as the Crosscut Saw between Mount Speculation and Mount Howitt ([tpl]coord|37|08|S|146|38|E[/tpl][tpl]Gazetteer of Australia| name = Mount Buggery | id = 238562[/tpl]). The other, smaller peak is near Abbeyard on the Buffalo River ([tpl]coord|36|54|S|146|44|E[/tpl][tpl]Gazetteer of Australia| name = Mount Buggery | id = 238561[/tpl]).
