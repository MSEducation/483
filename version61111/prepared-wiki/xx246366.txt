[[Ilford, Manitoba railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Via Rail stations in Manitoba

Ilford Railway Station is a station stop in Ilford, Manitoba, Canada.  The nearest city to the station is York Landing, Manitoba (43 km).  The stop is served by Via Rail's Winnipeg – Churchill train.http://www.viarail.ca/en/stations/prairies-and-northern-manitoba/Ilfordhttp://www.viarail.ca/en/trains/prairies-and-northern-manitoba/winnipeg-churchill/schedules
