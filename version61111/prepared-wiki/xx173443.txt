The Solbergfoss Line ([tpl]lang-no|Solbergfossbanen[/tpl]) was a railway line between Askim and Solbergfoss in Norway. It was opened in 1918 as a branch line from the Eastern Østfold Line at Askim Station. Its tracks were 7.9 kilometers long and single track.
The line was later closed and currently lies in disuse, but its tracks have not be removed.
