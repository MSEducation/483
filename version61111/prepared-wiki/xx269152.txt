[[Natalia Popova]]

CATEGORIES: 1993 births, Living people, Ukrainian female single skaters, People from Simferopol, Figure skaters at the 2014 Winter Olympics, Olympic figure skaters of Ukraine

Natalia Popova ([tpl]lang-uk|Наталія Ігорівна Попова[/tpl]; born September 15, 1993) is a Ukrainian figure skater. She is a four-time Ukrainian national champion (2010, 2012–2014) and has won five senior international medals.
Popova trains in Hackensack, New Jersey, while her family lives in Ontario, Canada.
