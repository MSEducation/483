[[Passyunk Avenue Bridge]]

CATEGORIES: Bascule bridges, Bridges over the Schuylkill River, Bridges in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, Road bridges in Pennsylvania

The Passyunk Avenue Bridge is a double leaf bascule bridge spanning the Schuylkill River between the South Philadelphia and the Southwest Philadelphia sections of Philadelphia.[tpl]cite web | url = http://uchs.net/HistoricDistricts/uavebridge.html | title = UNIVERSITY AVENUE BRIDGE Paul Philippe Cret, architect | accessdate = 2010-07-10 | last = Steffe | first = Michael J. | date = 1994-04-26 | work = National Register of Historic Places | publisher = National Park Service | quote = The much more recent Passyunk Avenue Bridge, and the University Avenue Bridge remain the only two extant double leaf bascule type drawbridges on the Schuylkill River in Philadelphia.[/tpl]
The current bridge was completed in 1983. It was constructed on an alignment slightly to the north of its predecessor, a 2-lane double-leaf bascule bridge dating to 1911.
