[[Colcot]]

CATEGORIES: Barry, Vale of Glamorgan

Colcot is a northern district of Barry near Wenvoe in the Vale of Glamorgan 11 miles outside Cardiff in South Wales.

==History & Amenities==

Colcot Primary School was built in 1952. Nearby areas include Highlight Park and Brynhill Golf Course.
Sports facilities include the Colcot Sports Center and playing fields also the Colcot Sports Hall. Activities in these facilities include: Weight Training, Football (soccer), Basketball, Badminton, Hockey, Rugby union, Cricket and a new feature Rock Climbing with a climbing wall.
