[[Moothali]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Barmer district

Moothali is a village in the suburb of Siwana, Barmer district, India. Rajput, Purojit, Nai, Suthar, Jain, Meghwal (name of the castes) and many other castes live in this village. Moothali is one of the  12 villages of the Bhayal community. Previously Moothali was won by the Bhayals. Other than the Bhayals the other communities are Dhaadhal Rathore, Mahecha, Dhavecha, Chauhan, Parmara. The Thakur (Indian title) of this place is "Jogsingh ji". There is also a school up to the 12th grade.
