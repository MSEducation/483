[[Arbat, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Arbat ([tpl]lang-fa|اربط[/tpl], also Romanized as Arbaţ; also known as Arband)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3053555|Arbat[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 144, in 33 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
