[[KKLX]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Wyoming, Hot adult contemporary radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 1979, 1979 establishments in Wyoming

KKLX (96.1 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Hot Adult Contemporary format. Licensed to Worland, Wyoming, USA, the station is currently owned by the Big Horn Radio Network, a division of Legend Communications of Wyoming, LLC, and features programing from NBC News Radio, and One Global Networks.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KKLX |title=KKLX Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
KWOR KYTS and KKLX studios are located at 1340 Radio Drive, Worland. KKLX has its transmitter site on Rattlesnake Ridge Road, northeast of Worland.

==History==

The station was assigned the call sign KENB-FM on 1979-12-03. On 1987-08-20, the station changed its call sign to KWOR-FM. On 1989-10-16 the station became the current KKLX.[tpl]cite web|url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=35896&Callsign=KKLX |title=KKLX Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
