Skrzyszów [tpl]IPAc-pl|'|s|k|sz|y|sz|u|f[/tpl] is a village in Gmina Godów, Wodzisław County, Silesian Voivodeship, southern Poland. It has a population of 3,280 and lies close to the border with the Czech Republic.
