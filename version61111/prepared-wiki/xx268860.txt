[[William Seeley (neurologist)]]

CATEGORIES: 1971 births, University of California, San Francisco alumni, University of California, San Francisco faculty, MacArthur Fellows, American neuroscientists, Brown University alumni, Living people

William W. Seeley (born 1971) is an American neurologist and Associate Professor of Neurology at the University of California, San Francisco.http://memory.ucsf.edu/ourcenter/staff/wseeley
He is Principal Investigator for the Selective Vulnerability Research Lab.http://neurology.ucsf.edu/seeley/people.html
He is a 2011 MacArthur Fellow."William Seeley: Neurologist", John D. & Catherine T. MacArthur Foundation.
