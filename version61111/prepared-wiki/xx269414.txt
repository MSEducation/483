[[Eastern Upper Carniolan subdialect]]

CATEGORIES: Slovene dialects

The Eastern Upper Carniolan subdialect (vzhodnogorenjski govor,Smole, Vera. 1998. "Slovenska narečja." Enciklopedija Slovenije vol. 12, pp. 1–5. Ljubljana: Mladinska knjiga, p. 2. vzhodna gorenjščinaLogar, Tine. 1996. Dialektološke in jezikovnozgodovinske razprave. Ljubljana: SAZU, p. 45.) is a Slovene subdialect in the Upper Carniolan dialect group. It is spoken in the eastern part of Upper Carniola, east of a line running west of Špitalič, Trojane, and Kisovec, then east of Vače and Zgornji Hotič, and then south along the Sava to east of Dol pri Ljubljani.Toporišič, Jože. 1992. Enciklopedija slovenskega jezika. Ljubljana: Cankarjeva založba, pp. 361.

==Phonological and morphological characteristics==

The Eastern Upper Carniolan subdialect is a subdialect of the Upper Carniolan dialect. It has preserved pitch accent, except for a small area in Moravče.
