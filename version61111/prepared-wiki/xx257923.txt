[[Companies Act 1947]]

CATEGORIES: United Kingdom company law, United Kingdom Acts of Parliament 1947

The Companies Act 1947 was a United Kingdom Act of Parliament, that updated UK company law after the Companies Act 1929. 
It covered issues such as winding up and bankruptcy. UK Legislation http://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/Geo6/10-11/47/contents
It was soon recodified in the Companies Act 1948.
