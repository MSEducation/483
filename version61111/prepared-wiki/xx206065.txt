[[2705 Wu]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Asteroids named for people, Astronomical objects discovered in 1980

2705 Wu is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1183.5173900 days (3.24 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=2705 | title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-05-23 | publisher = NASA[/tpl]
