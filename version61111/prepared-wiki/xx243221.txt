[[KILL]]

CATEGORIES: Electric Six albums, 2009 albums

KILL is the sixth  album by Detroit rock band Electric Six.
In initial press releases, the band described the album as being a return to a sound more akin to their debut album, but this was later revealed by front-man Dick Valentine to be more gimmick than truth.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.qromag.com/interviews/interviews/dick_valentine_of_electric_six |title=QRO Magazine - Interviews >> Dick Valentine of Electric Six|accessdate=June 25, 2010[/tpl]
An explicit video was released for "Body Shot".

All songs written by Tyler Spencer.
