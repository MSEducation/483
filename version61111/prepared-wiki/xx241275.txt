[[36th Indian Infantry Brigade]]

CATEGORIES: British Indian Army brigades

The 36th Indian Infantry Brigade was an Infantry formation of the Indian Army during World War II. It was formed in June 1941, at Baleli in India and assigned to the 14th Indian Infantry Division. The brigade spent between March 1942  and April 1943, with the Eastern Army. It was then assigned to the 26th Indian Infantry Division until the end of the war.
