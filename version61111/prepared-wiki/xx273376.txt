[[Délice d'Argental]]

CATEGORIES: Articles created via the Article Wizard, French cheeses, Culture of Burgundy

Delice d'Argental  is a luxurious double cream French cheese from the Burgundy region. It is made by hand from cow's milk. The addition of crème fraiche to the curds during manufacture provides for extra richness - developing an exceptionally creamy texture.Mastering Cheese: Lessons for Connoisseurship from a Maître Fromager - Max McCalman 2009
This cheese has a soft bloomy rind which is yellow-white in colour. It is similar to Brie both in appearance and texture.
