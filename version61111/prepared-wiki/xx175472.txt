[[Independent Dream Machine]]

CATEGORIES: Film production companies of the United States, Companies based in Pennsylvania

Independent Dream Machine is an Allentown, Pennsylvania-based production company founded by film producer Zeke Zelker in 1997.  Past feature film include Affairs (film)(1997), A.K.A.: It's a Wiley World! (film)(2003), Fading (film)(2003), Loggerheads (film)(2005) and InSearchOf (2009).
