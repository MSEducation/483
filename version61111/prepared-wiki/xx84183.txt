[[Otari, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Otari|小谷村|Otari-mura[/tpl] is a village located in Kitaazumi District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the village has an estimated population of 4,122 and a density of 15.39 persons per km². The total area is 267.91 km².
