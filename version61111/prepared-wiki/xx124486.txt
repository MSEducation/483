[[Nethradhama Superspeciality Eye Hospital]]

CATEGORIES: Hospitals in Bangalore

Nethradhama Superspeciality Eye Hospital , located in Jayanagar near Ediyur Lake is  a hightec Eye Hospital offering comprehensive Eye Care facilities in Bangalore, India.  

==History==

Started as a Subspeciality Clinic in 1994 by Dr.Sri Ganesh an eminent Ophthalmologist, Nethradhama has grown into a Superspeciality eye hospital and a Post graduate Institute of Ophthalmology.
Within a few years of its inception, Nethradhama has become an icon of dynamic progress in the field of Ophthalmology. Located at Jayanagar, the hospital has the most sophisticated equipment and facilities offering state-of-the-art technology to treat various eye disorders. The main areas of focus are comprehensive eye care, education, community services and eye banking.

==Mission==

The Mission of Nethradhama Superspeciality Eye Hospital is to provide specialized eye care treatment as a tertiary care centre. It is also a social organization committed to the goal of eradicating preventable blindness in the developing world by focusing on comprehensive eye care services, education, training and community services with the best of technology and skills.
