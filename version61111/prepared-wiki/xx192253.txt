[[Steve Goulding]]

CATEGORIES: 1954 births, Living people, English drummers, People from South London

Steve Goulding (born 1954, South London, England) is an English drummer, who's played as a member of Graham Parker and The Rumour, The Associates, Poi Dog Pondering, The Waco Brothers and The Mekons. With the Associates he had a UK #9 hit single with "Party Fears Two". He also played the drums on the hit single "Let's Go to Bed" by The CureSteve at Waco Brothers and "Watching the Detectives" with Elvis Costello.
