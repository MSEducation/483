[[Pukhtoonkhwa Mazdoor Kissan Party]]

CATEGORIES: Nationalist parties, Political parties in Pakistan, 1979 establishments in Pakistan, Political parties established in 1979

The Pukhtoonkhwa Mazdoor Kissan Party was a Pushtun nationalist political party in Pakistan. It was formed by Sher Ali Bacha in 1979, as a split from the Maoist Mazdoor Kisan Party. In 1989 the party merged into the Pukhtunkhwa Milli Awami Party.
