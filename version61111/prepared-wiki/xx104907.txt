Ava Vincent (born September 29, 1975), formerly known as Jewel Valmont, is an American former pornographic actress.

==Early life==

Vincent graduated from San Joaquin Delta College with a theatre arts degree. She was a manager at an adult bookstore in Stockton, California prior to joining the adult industry.[tpl]cite book|last=Gerrie Lim|first=|title=In Lust We Trust: Adventures in Adult Cinema|year=2006|publisher=Monsoon Books|isbn=978-981-05-5302-9|page=92[/tpl]

==Career==

Vincent entered the adult film industry in 1998.
