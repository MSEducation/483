[[1989–90 Belgian Cup]]

CATEGORIES: Belgian Cup, 1989–90 in Belgian football

The 1989–90 Belgian Cup was the 35th season of the main knockout competition in Belgian association football, the Belgian Cup.

==Final rounds==

The final phase started when all clubs from the top two divisions in Belgian football entered the competition in the round of 64 (18 clubs from first division, 16 clubs from second division and 30 clubs from the qualifications).[tpl]cite book |author=Hereng, Jacques & De Veene, Carlos |title=Football |publisher=Artis-Historia |year=1990 |pages=44[/tpl] The first two rounds were played in one leg, while the next 3 rounds (round of 16, quarter-finals and semifinals) were played in two legs. The final game was played at the Heysel Stadium between FC Liège and Germinal Ekeren, FC Liège winning 2-1.
