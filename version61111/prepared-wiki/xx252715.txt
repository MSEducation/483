[[Line 18 (Stockholm metro)]]

CATEGORIES: Stockholm metro

[tpl]unreferenced|date=January 2014[/tpl]Line 18 (T18) is one of the three green lines of the Stockholm Metro connecting Alvik and Farsta strand. It is a 18.4 km (11.4 mi) long line with a 37 min travel time and 23 stations.
