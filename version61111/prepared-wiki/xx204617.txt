[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Crato]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1914, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Fortaleza, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Crato ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Cratensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Crato in the Ecclesiastical province of Fortaleza in Brazil.
