[[McKinney Acres, Texas]]

CATEGORIES: Census-designated places in Andrews County, Texas, Census-designated places in Texas

McKinney Acres is a census-designated place (CDP) in Andrews County, Texas, United States. The population was 815 at the 2010 census.[tpl]cite web| url=http://factfinder2.census.gov/bkmk/table/1.0/en/DEC/10_DP/G001/1600000US4845750| title=Geographic Identifiers: 2010 Demographic Profile Data (G001): McKinney Acres CDP, Texas| publisher=U.S. Census Bureau, American Factfinder| accessdate=August 9, 2013[/tpl]

==Geography==

McKinney Acres is located along the southern border of Andrews, the county seat. U.S. Route 385 runs along the western edge of the CDP, leading south [tpl]convert|32|mi[/tpl] to Odessa.
According to the United States Census Bureau, the CDP has a total area of [tpl]convert|4.3|sqkm|disp=flip[/tpl], all of it land.
