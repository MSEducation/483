[[Andabad-e Sofla]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Andabad-e Sofla ([tpl]lang-fa|اندابادسفلي[/tpl], also Romanized as Andābād-e Soflá)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795182|Andabad-e Sofla[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 831, in 216 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
