[[Kat Maconie]]

CATEGORIES: Shoe brands, Shoe companies of the United Kingdom

Kat Maconie is a London based shoe label set up by Kat Maconie in 2009.
After two years at Whistles, working on buying and product development, Kat Maconie moved into fashion recruitment and began creating her label part-time during her holidays.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.drapersonline.com/news/people/next-generation/30-under-30-kat-maconie/5044506.article|publisher=Drapers|title=30 under 30: Kat Maconie|date=12 January 2013|accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl] The shoes are now stocked in twenty countries and in leading department stores such as Harvey Nichols and Selfridges. Kat has also collaborated with high street brands like Dune and fashion designers Felder Felder in the past, creating shoes for their catwalk shows at London Fashion Week.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.vogue.co.uk/news/2011/05/20/kat-maconie-shoe-collaboration-with-felder-felder|title=Catwalk Kat|work=Vogue|date=20 May 2011|accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl] Her signature block heels with gold frames and city sandals have won her much acclaim in the industry and saw her win Drapers Shoe Designer of the Year award in 2013.Fashion Monitor
In 2013 the brand secured an investment from venture capital trust Pembroke that includes Next chief executive Lord Wolfson among its investors. Maconie plans to use the investment to expand her main collections, expand the sales and marketing team and relaunch the brands website.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.drapersonline.com/news/kat-maconie-earns-expansion-cash/5051469.article|publisher=Drapers|title=Kat Maconie earns expansion cash|date=26 July 2013|accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl]
