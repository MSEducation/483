[[Édouard Kargu]]

CATEGORIES: French footballers, France international footballers, Ligue 1 players, Ligue 2 players, 1925 births, 2010 deaths, French people of Polish descent, French-Polish footballers, FC Girondins de Bordeaux players

Édouard Kargulewicz (16 December 1925 – 13 March 2010), known simply as Édouard Kargu, was a Polish-born French former international footballer who played as a striker. Kargu played professionally for Bordeaux between 1947 and 1958, and was the Ligue 1 topscorer in the 1953–54 season scoring 27 goals.
