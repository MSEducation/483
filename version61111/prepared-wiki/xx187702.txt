[[Peugeot 108]]

CATEGORIES: Peugeot vehicles, Upcoming automobile models, City cars, Vehicles introduced in 2014

The Peugeot 108 is a city car, launched by the French manufacturer Peugeot in March 2014, at the Geneva Motor Show, with sales expected by summer 2014.[tpl]cite web|last=Holloway|first=Hilton|title=Peugeot 108 set for July launch|url=http://www.autocar.co.uk/car-news/geneva-motor-show/peugeot-108-set-july-launch|work=Autocar|accessdate=4 March 2014|date=4 March 2014[/tpl]
It is related to the Citroën C1 and Toyota Aygo, again sharing the same chassis, engines, transmissions and electricals, and are built in the current factory situated in the Czech Republic.[tpl]cite web|title=New Peugeot 108 to be built with Toyota|url=http://www.autoexpress.co.uk/peugeot/108/61511/new-peugeot-108-be-built-toyota|publisher=Auto Express|accessdate=17 February 2013|archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/query?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.autoexpress.co.uk%2Fpeugeot%2F108%2F61511%2Fnew-peugeot-108-be-built-toyota%2F&date=2013-02-17|archivedate=17 February 2013[/tpl]
The 108 is powered by a choice of two 3-cylinder petrol engines: a 1.0-litre VTi with 68 bhp, emitting up to 97g/km of [tpl]co2[/tpl], with an e-VTi producing 88g/km, and a larger 1.2-litre VTi has 82 bhp and emits 99g/km of [tpl]CO2[/tpl].
