[[Chocolate Crunchies]]

CATEGORIES: Food ingredients, Candy bars

Chocolate Crunchies, sometimes abbreviated Crunchies, are commonly used to make up a layer of ice cream cake. The layer of Chocolate Crunchies is generally sandwiched in between two layers of ice cream. Carvel, Friendly's and Baskin-Robbins, to name a few, all use a layer of Chocolate Crunchies in their ice cream cakes.
