[[Germany at the 1928 Summer Olympics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 1928 Summer Olympics, Germany at the Summer Olympics, 1928 in German sport

Germany competed at the 1928 Summer Olympics in Amsterdam, Netherlands. Germany returned to the Olympic Games after not being invited to both the 1920 and 1924 Games. Despite a total absence of 16 years since 1912, German athletes were ranked 2nd. 296 competitors, 261 men and 35 women, took part in 95 events in 16 sports.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/countries/GER/summer/1928/ |title=Germany at the 1928 Amsterdam Summer Games |accessdate=2010-04-25 |work=sports-reference.com[/tpl]
