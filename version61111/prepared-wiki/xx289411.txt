[[Abrek Mukov]]

CATEGORIES: 1995 births, Living people, Russian footballers

Abrek Shamsudinovich Mukov ([tpl]lang-ru|Абрек Шамсудинович Муков[/tpl]; born May 27, 1995) is a Russian football defender, who currently playing for FC Druzhba Maykop.
He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Druzhba Maykop on August 9, 2013 in a game against FC Chernomorets Novorossiysk.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Russian Football Union|url=http://rfspro.ru/cgi-bin/player/script2.cgi?par1=119597|title=Career Summary|date=14 August 2013[/tpl]
