[[Qandar Qalu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Qandar Qalu ([tpl]lang-fa|قندرقالو[/tpl], also Romanized as Qandar Qālū and Qanderqālū; also known as Kardinkali, Qandarqālī, and Qandirqāli)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3079514|Qandar Qalu[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Pain Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 258, in 52 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
