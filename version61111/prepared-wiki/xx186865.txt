Michael Timothy Byrne (born October 17, 1986 in Lancaster, Pennsylvania) is an American football offensive lineman who is currently a free agent. He was signed by the Miami Dolphins as an undrafted free agent in 2008. He played college football at Delaware.

==Professional career==

Byrne has also played in the Canadian Football League for the Calgary Stampeders and was a member of the Toronto Argonauts.
