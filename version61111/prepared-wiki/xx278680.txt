[[Legahi]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Legahi ([tpl]lang-fa|لگاهي[/tpl], also Romanized as Legāhī and Lagāhī; also known as Lyakyagi and Nakāhī)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3072729|Legahi[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 384, in 80 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
