Deane W. Kelley was an American football coach in the United States.

==Coaching career==

Deane Kelley was the head football coach for Michigan State Normal School (now Eastern Michigan University) football team located in Ypsilanti, Michigan.  He held that position for the 1892 season.[tpl]Cite web|last=Shafer|first=Ian|title=Eastern Michigan University (All seasons results)|publisher=College Football Reference|url=http://cfreference.net/cfr/school.s?m=viewAllSeasons&id=202|accessdate=December 30, 2010[/tpl] His coaching record at Eastern Michigan was 2 wins and 1 losses.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.sportsnetwork.com/merge/tsnform.aspx?c=sportsnetwork&page=cfoot/teams/direct495.htm|publisher=The Sports Network|accessdate=April 13, 2011|title=Football Bowl Subdivision: Eastern Michigan University Directory[/tpl]  As of the conclusion of the 2010 season, this ranks him #28 at Eastern Michigan in total wins and #4 at the school in winning percentage ([tpl]Winning percentage|2|1|0[/tpl]).
