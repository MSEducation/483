[[Gmina Tczów]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Zwoleń County

Gmina Tczów is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Zwoleń County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Tczów, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|9|km|mi|0[/tpl] west of Zwoleń and [tpl]convert|104|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|72.12|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 4,877.

==Villages==

Gmina Tczów contains the villages and settlements of Bartodzieje, Borki, Brzezinki Nowe, Brzezinki Stare, Janów, Józefów, Julianów, Kazimierzów, Lucin, Podzakrzówek, Rawica Nowa, Rawica Stara, Rawica-Józefatka, Rawica-Kolonia, Tczów, Tynica and Wincentów.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Tczów is bordered by the gminas of Gózd, Kazanów, Skaryszew and Zwoleń.
