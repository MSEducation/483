[[Tage]]

CATEGORIES: Scandinavian masculine given names

Tage, or Thage is a male given name with Danish origins.
Tage is one of the major clan of Apatani tribe residing in the Ziro plateau of Arunachal Pradesh one among North Eastern States of India. Categorically, there are two similar name of Tage clan in the Apatani tribe, namely Tajang-Tage residing in Tajang village and Mudang-Tage residing in Mudang-Tage village.
