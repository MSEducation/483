[[Nikolskaya Street]]

CATEGORIES: Streets in Moscow, Kitay-Gorod

Nikolskaya Street ([tpl]lang-ru|Никольская улица[/tpl]) is a street in the Kitay-Gorod district, Moscow, Russia.[tpl]cite web|url=http://travel.michelin.com/web/destination/Russia-Moscow/tourist_site-Nikolskaya_Ulitsa-Nikolskaia_oulitsa|title=Nikolskaya Ulitsa|work=Michelin World Guide|publisher=Michelin|accessdate=13 August 2013[/tpl]  It connects Red Square and Lubyanka Square.[tpl]cite news|url=http://themoscownews.com/local/20130404/191410038.html|title=Moscow to get another pedestrian street next to Kremlin|last=Budrys|first=Aleksandras|date=4 April 2013|work=The Moscow News|accessdate=13 August 2013[/tpl] Notable buildings located on Nikolskaya Street include the Monastery of the Holy Saviour and the former Holy Synod Printing Offices, Russia's first publishing house.
