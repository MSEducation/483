[[Coral (color)]]

CATEGORIES: Shades of orange, Shades of pink, Shades of red

The various tones of the colour coral are representations of the colors of those cnidarians known as corals.

==Coral==

The web colour coral is a pinkish orange colour.  It is displayed at right.
The first recorded use of coral as a colour name in English was in 1513.[tpl]cite book |title=A Dictionary of Colour |last= Maerz |first=Aloys John |coauthors= Paul, Morris Rea |year=1930 |publisher=McGraw-Hill Book Company |location=New York |isbn= |page=193 |postscript= [/tpl]; colour sample of coral: Page 27, Plate 2, Colour Sample J10.

==Variations of coral==

===Coral pink===

The colour coral pink is displayed at right, a pinkish colour.
The complementary colour of coral pink is teal. The first recorded use of coral pink as a colour name in English was in 1892.[tpl]cite book |title=A Dictionary of Colour |last= Maerz |first=Aloys John |coauthors= Paul, Morris Rea |year=1930 |publisher=McGraw-Hill Book Company |location=New York |isbn= |page=193 |postscript= [/tpl];  colour sample of coral pink: page 27, plate 2, colour sample F9.
