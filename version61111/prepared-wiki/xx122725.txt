[[In the Still of the Night (1937 song)]]

CATEGORIES: Songs written by Cole Porter, 1937 songs, Frank Sinatra songs

"In the Still of the Night" is a popular song written by Cole Porter  for the MGM film Rosalie sung by Nelson Eddy and published in 1937.
Two popular early recordings were by Tommy Dorsey and Leo Reisman. The Dorsey charted on October 16, 1937 and peaked at #3.  The Reisman charted on December 25, 1937 and peaked at #9. [tpl]Wayback |date=20091026114345 |url=http://geocities.com/porterguide/rosalie.html [/tpl]
