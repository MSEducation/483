[[2910 Yoshkar-Ola]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Asteroids named for people, Astronomical objects discovered in 1980

2910 Yoshkar-Ola is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1193.4884860 days (3.27 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=2910 | title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-05-23 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on October 11, 1980.
