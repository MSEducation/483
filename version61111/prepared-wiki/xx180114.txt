[[Cowgill, Cumbria]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Cumbria

Cowgill is a village in the South Lakeland district of Cumbria, England. Historically part of the West Riding of Yorkshire, it is located [tpl]convert|9|mi[/tpl] south east of Sedbergh. The village is served by Dent railway station on the Settle-Carlisle Line with services north towards Appleby-in-Westmorland and Carlisle and south towards Skipton and Leeds.
