[[Unisport Bafang]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Cameroon, 1959 establishments in French Cameroons, Sports clubs in Cameroon

Unisport de Bafang is a football club based in Bafang in Cameroon's West Province. They are a member of the Fédération Camerounaise de Football and play in the Cameroon Premiere Division. The club's colours are yellow and red.
