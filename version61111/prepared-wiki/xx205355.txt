[[Maurice Lafforgue]]

CATEGORIES: 1913 births, 1970 deaths, French alpine skiers, Olympic alpine skiers of France, Alpine skiers at the 1936 Winter Olympics

[tpl]MedalCountry|[tpl]FRA[/tpl][/tpl]
Maurice Lafforgue (March 26, 1913 – 1970) was a French alpine skier who competed in the 1936 Winter Olympics.
He was born in Bagnères-de-Luchon and was the father of Britt Lafforgue and Ingrid Lafforgue.
In 1936, he finished eleventh in the alpine skiing combined event.
