[[Boľavé námestie]]

CATEGORIES: 1977 singles, Marika Gombitová songs, Songs written by Ján Lehotský

"Boľavé námestie" ([tpl]lang-en|Sore Square[/tpl]) is the debut solo single by the female singer Marika Gombitová released on OPUS in 1977.[tpl]harvnb|Graclík|Nekvapil|2008|p=393.[/tpl]
The composition was written by Lehotský and Brezovská, being issued on the various artists compilation OPUS '77. B-side of the single featured "Ty vieš, mama". Both songs were on CD released in 2000 as bonus tracks of the singer's debut album re-release Dievča do dažďa.For Marika Gombitová's discography, see [tpl]harvnb|Lehotský|2008a|pp=54–56.[/tpl].
