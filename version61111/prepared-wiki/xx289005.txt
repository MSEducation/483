Mark Ellis (born 26 August 1990) is an Irish hurler who plays as a right corner-back for the Cork senior team.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.gaacork.ie/playerProfile/4353/mark_ellis|title=Player profile: Mark Ellis|publisher=Cork GAA|date=|accessdate=14 August 2013[/tpl]
Born in Millstreet, County Cork, Ellis first excelled at hurling whilst at school in Millstreet Community School. He arrived on the inter-county scene at the age of nineteen when he first linked up with the Cork under-21 team, before later lining out with the intermediate side. He made his senior debut in the 2013 National Hurling League.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.irishexaminer.com/sport/free-scoring-rebels-stay-grounded-220932.html|title=Free-scoring Rebels stay grounded|publisher=Irish Examiner|date=28 January 2013|accessdate=14 August 2013|first=Michael|last=Moynihan[/tpl] Ellis was later included on Cork's championship team as a substitute.
At club level Ellis plays with Millstreet.
