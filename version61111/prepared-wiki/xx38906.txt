[[Begijnendijk]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Flemish Brabant

Begijnendijk ([tpl]IPA-nl|bəˌɣ̟ɛɪ̯n̪ən̪ˈd̪ɛɪ̯k[/tpl]) is a municipality located in the Belgian province of Flemish Brabant. The municipality comprises the towns of Begijnendijk proper and Betekom. On January 1, 2006 Begijnendijk had a total population of 9,400. The total area is 17.62 km² which gives a population density of 534 inhabitants per km². The official spoken language is Dutch.
