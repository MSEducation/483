[[Elachista arnoldi]]

CATEGORIES: Elachistidae, Animals described in 1993, Moths of Europe

Elachista arnoldi is a moth of the Elachistidae family that can be found in the Netherlands and Germany.
The larvae probably feed on Carex. They mine the leaves of their host plant.[tpl]cite web|url= http://www.bladmineerders.nl/minersf/lepidopteramin/elachista/arnoldi/arnoldi.htm|title=Elachista arnoldi (Koster, 1993)|publisher=Bladmineerders.nl|accessdate=September 23, 2011[/tpl]
