[[Michael Cohen (pharmacist)]]

CATEGORIES: American pharmacists, Temple University alumni, Living people

Michael Cohen is an American pharmacist, and president of The Institute for Safe Medication Practices.http://www.ismp.org/about/staff.asp He was a 2005 MacArthur Fellow.

==Life==

He graduated from Temple University with a RPh, and MS. He wrote a column for the journal Hospital Pharmacy. He writes a column for the Philadelphia Inquirer.[tpl]cite news|title=Check Up|url=http://www.philly.com/philly/health/Check_Up_-_Cohen.html|accessdate=30 April 2013|newspaper=Philadelphia Inquirer[/tpl] 

==Family==

He is married to Hedy Cohen and has two children, Rachel Cohen and Jennifer Gold. http://www.philly.com/philly/health_and_science/97905324.html
