[[Mohammadabad, Ghazipur]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Ghazipur district

Mohammadabad ([tpl]lang-hi|मुहम्मदाबाद[/tpl], [tpl]lang-ur|[tpl]Nastaliq|محمّدآباد[/tpl][/tpl]) is a city and a municipal board in Ghazipur district in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh.

==Demographics==

[tpl]As of|2001[/tpl] India census,[tpl]GR|India[/tpl] Mohammadabad had a population of 30,234. Males constitute 52% of the population and females 48%. Mohammadabad has an average literacy rate of 47%, lower than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 53%, and female literacy is 40%. In Mohammadabad, 17% of the population is under 6 years of age.

==Overview==

Mohammadabad is situated on the Ghazipur–Patna National Highway 19. It is also located beside the Ghazipur–Ballia state highway. Many of the politicians representing Ghazipur area belong to this town.
