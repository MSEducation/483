[[6064 Holašovice]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Asteroids named for places, Astronomical objects discovered in 1987

6064 Holašovice is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1198.2890031 days (3.28 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=6064 |  title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-04-28 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on April 23, 1987 by Antonín Mrkos at Kleť in southern Bohemia and named after the nearby village of Holašovice.http://www.klet.org/jmena/view.php3?astnum=6064
