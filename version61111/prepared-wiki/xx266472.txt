[[Red Bank Armory]]

CATEGORIES: Red Bank, New Jersey, Armories in New Jersey, Sports venues in New Jersey, Buildings and structures in Monmouth County, New Jersey, Visitor attractions in Monmouth County, New Jersey, Ice hockey in New Jersey, Indoor ice hockey venues in the United States

The Red Bank Armory is a former armory located at 76 Chestnut Street in Red Bank, New Jersey that has been converted to ice rink.

In 1998 Armory was converted to an ice rink, and is home to the Red Bank Generals,  members of the New Jersey Youth Hockey League.
