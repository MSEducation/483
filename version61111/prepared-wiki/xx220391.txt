[[Fiji at the Commonwealth Games]]

CATEGORIES: Fiji at the Commonwealth Games, Fiji and the Commonwealth of Nations

Fiji have competed in all but two Commonwealth Games since 1938, missing only 1990 and 1994, as they were not members of the Commonwealth during this time. Fiji have won fourteen Commonwealth medals across six sports. 
The country is currently suspended from the Commonwealth, and was banned from taking part in the 2010 Games."Fiji to appeal Commonwealth Games ban", The Times of India, December 1, 2009
Fiji will be represented with a team at the 2014 Commonwealth Games in Glasgow,Scotland.
