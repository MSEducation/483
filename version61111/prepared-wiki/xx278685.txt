[[Mahmudabad, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Mahmudabad ([tpl]lang-fa|محموداباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Maḩmūdābād; also known as Maḩmūābād, Maḩmūdābād-e Tāzeh Kand, and Makhmudabad)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3073526|Mahmudabad[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 265, in 53 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
