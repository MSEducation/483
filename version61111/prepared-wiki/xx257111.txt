[[William G. Smith House (Davenport, Iowa)]]

CATEGORIES: Houses completed in 1894, Queen Anne architecture in Iowa, Houses in Davenport, Iowa, Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Iowa, National Register of Historic Places in Scott County, Iowa

The William G. Smith House is located on the east side of Davenport, Iowa, United States.  The Queen Anne style residence has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since 1984.
