[[Johannes Christoffel Jan Mastenbroek]]

CATEGORIES: 1902 births, 1978 deaths, Dutch football managers, 1938 FIFA World Cup managers, People from Dordrecht

Johannes Christoffel Jan Mastenbroek (5 July 1902 – 23 May 1978) was a Dutch football manager who coached the Dutch East Indies in the 1938 FIFA World Cup.Copa do Mundo da FIFA França 1938
Mastenbroek was born and raised in Dordrecht, son of Klaas Mastenbroek, a tailor, and Goverdina Rackwitsz. In 1924 he married Johanna van den Bovenkamp in Hengelo, at which time he was a school teacher.Mastenbroek's wedding at wiewaswie.nl
He was chairman of the Nederlandsch-Indische Voetbal Unie (Dutch East Indies football association) and vice-president of the Dutch East Indies Olympic Committee.
