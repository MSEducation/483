[[Kapilatirtham]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Chittoor district

Kapila Tirtham (also called Kapila Theertham) is located in Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh. It is famous for its Lord Shiva temple.  In the premises of temple, there is a waterfall and a big Koneru (or pond).  The waterfalls come from the Seven Hills (Tirumala).
