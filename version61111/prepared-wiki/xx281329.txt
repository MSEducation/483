[[92.5 FM Wells, British Columbia]]

CATEGORIES: Community radio stations in Canada, Radio stations in British Columbia, Proposed radio stations

92.5 is a new English language community radio station which will operate on the frequency of 92.5 MHz/FM in Wells, British Columbia, Canada. The Wells Community Radio Association, on behalf of a corporation to be incorporated, received approval for the new station by the Canadian Radio-television and Telecommunications Commission (CRTC) on August 9, 2013. Broadcasting Decision CRTC 2013-398, Developmental community radio station in Wells, CRTC, August 9, 2013
