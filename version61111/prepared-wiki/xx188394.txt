[[Highway Traffic Act (Ontario)]]

CATEGORIES: Ontario provincial legislation, 1923 in law, Rules of the road, Traffic law

The Highway Traffic Act (HTA) is an Ontario Act which regulates the licensing of vehicles, classification of traffic offenses, administration of loads, classification of vehicles and other transport related issues. First introduced in 1923 to deal with increasing accidents during the early years of motoring in Ontario,The Snow Plough - A History there have been amendments due to changes to driving conditions and new transportation trends.
