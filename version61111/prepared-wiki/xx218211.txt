[[Miss Universo Italia 2009]]

CATEGORIES: Miss Universo Italia, 2009 beauty pageants, 2009 in Italy

The Miss Universo Italia 2009 pageant was held on May 27, 2009.Final on May 27 The winner was Laura Valenti, who went on to represent Italy at Miss Universe 2009, which took place in the Bahamas on August 23, 2009. Valenti did not place in the semis.
