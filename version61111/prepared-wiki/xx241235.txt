[[Chahar Right Middle Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia, Ulanqab

 
Chahar Right Middle Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠴᠠᠬᠠᠷ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠪᠠᠷᠠᠭᠤᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠭᠠᠷᠤᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠳᠤᠮᠳᠠᠳᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Čaqar Baraɣun Ɣarun Dumdadu qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=察哈尔右翼中旗 |t=察哈爾右翼中旗 |p=Cháhā'ěr Yòuyì Zhōngqí[/tpl]) is a banner (county equivalent) of Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China, bordered by Chahar Right Back Banner to the east, Zhuozi County to the south, and Siziwang Banner to the northwest. It is under the administration of Ulaan Chab City.
