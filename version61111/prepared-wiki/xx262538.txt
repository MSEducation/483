[[Wilfried Louisy-Daniel]]

CATEGORIES: 1986 births, Living people, French footballers, UJA Alfortville players, AS Beauvais Oise players, SR Colmar players

Wilfried Louisy-Daniel (born 29 May 1986 in Versailles) is a French football player who currently plays for French club US Orléans in the Championnat National.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.espoirsdufootball.com/12006-wilfried-louisy-daniel-signe-a-beauvais.html | publisher = Espoirs du Football | title = Wilfried Louisy-Daniel signe à Beauvais |language = French |date = 30 May 2010 | accessdate = 1 January 2010[/tpl] Prior to signing with Beauvais, he had a prolific season with AS Poissy in the Championnat de France amateur 2, the fifth level of French football, scoring 23 goals in 20 appearances. In the 2005–06 season, Louisy-Daniel migrated to Germany after successfully trialing with Oberliga club DJK TuS Hordel. He spent one season at the club before returning to France.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.fff.fr/champ/national/actualite/535418.shtml | publisher = French Football Federation | title = Louisy-Daniel remonte la pente |language = French |date = 27 October 2010 | accessdate = 1 January 2010[/tpl]
