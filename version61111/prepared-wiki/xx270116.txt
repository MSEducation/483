[[T Brigade Group]]

CATEGORIES: 1946 in Indonesia, Brigades of the Netherlands

The T Brigade Group was a formation of the Royal Netherlands Army. At the end of the Second World War the formation consisted of the 2nd Battalion, 6th RIR, 1st Stootroepen Battalion and the 2/13th Light Infantry Battalion. The brigade group relieved the British 5th Parachute Brigade in Java on 26 April 1946.Mcmillan, p.101
