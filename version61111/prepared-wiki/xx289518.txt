[[Qarah Kahriz, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Qarah Kahriz ([tpl]lang-fa|قره كهريز[/tpl], also Romanized as Qarah Kahrīz and Qareh Kahrīz; also known as Ghareh Kahriz and Qara Kārīz)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3079703|Qarah Kahriz[/tpl] is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 617, in 114 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
