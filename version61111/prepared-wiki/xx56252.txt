Rosalind Chao ([tpl]IPAc-en|ˈ|r|ɒ|z|ə|l|ɪ|n|d|_|ˈ|tʃ|aʊ[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=赵家玲|t=趙家玲|p=Zhào Jiālíng[/tpl]; born [tpl]start-date|September 23, 1957[/tpl]) is an American actress. Chao's best-known roles have been as a star of CBS's AfterMASH portraying South Korean refugee Soon-Lee Klinger for both seasons, and the recurring character Keiko O'Brien with twenty-seven appearances on the syndicated science fiction series Star Trek: The Next Generation and Star Trek: Deep Space Nine.
