[[Galleria Department Store]]

CATEGORIES: Gangnam District, Hanwha subsidiaries, Department stores of South Korea

Galleria Department Store ([tpl]ko-hhrm|갤러리아백화점[/tpl]) is an upmarket South Korean department store franchise owned by Hanwha Group. It has 8 branches throughout Korea, notably the Luxury Hall West and Luxury Hall East, both in Apgujeong-dong, as upmarket luxury-brand fashion malls in Seoul.[tpl]cite web|url=http://english.visitkorea.or.kr/enu/SH/SH_EN_7_2.jsp?cid=273773|title=Galleria Department Store|work=Korea Tourism Organization|accessdate=11 April 2013[/tpl]
