[[Corentin Tolisso]]

CATEGORIES: French footballers, Olympique Lyonnais players, Ligue 1 players, 1994 births, Living people, France youth international footballers

Corentin Tolisso (born 3 August 1994 in Tarare) is a French footballer who currently plays for French side Olympique Lyon. He plays as right back or a midfielder. He made his Ligue 1 debut at 11 August 2013 against OGC Nice. He replaced Arnold Mvuemba in the extra-time.
