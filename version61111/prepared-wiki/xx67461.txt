[[James Tuchet, 6th Earl of Castlehaven]]

CATEGORIES: 1740 deaths, Earls in the Peerage of Ireland, Earls of Castlehaven, Barons Audley, Year of birth unknown

James Tuchet, 6th Earl of Castlehaven (died 12 October 1740) was the son of James Tuchet, 5th Earl of Castlehaven and his wife Anne Pelson.Cokayne, G. E. Complete Peerage 2000 Reprint Vol. 3, p. 88
He succeeded his father as Earl of Castlehaven and Baron Audley on 9 August 1700.
He married, on 14 May 1722, Elizabeth Arundell (1693–1743), daughter of Henry Arundell, 5th Baron Arundell of WardourCokayne, p. 88 and his wife, née Elizabeth Panton.
