[[Orba Co]]

CATEGORIES: Ngari Prefecture, Lakes of Tibet

Orba Co, also Wo Erba or Wo Erbacuo ([tpl]zh|c=窝尔巴错|p=Wōěrbā cuò[/tpl]; [tpl]bo|t=འོར་པ་མཚོ|w='or pa mtsho[/tpl]) is a lake in Rutog County in the Ngari Prefecture in the northwest of the Tibet Autonomous Region of China.  It lies at an elevation of [tpl]convert|5209|m|ft[/tpl], to the southeast of Longmu Lake. The lake has several islands which are the islands with the highest altitude on earth.
