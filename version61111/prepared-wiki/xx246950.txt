[[John Cassavetes filmography]]

CATEGORIES: John Cassavetes, Director filmographies

John Cassavetes began his career in film in 1953 and ended it in 1986, between which times he was involved in every aspect of the film, television, and stage arts, including acting, directing, scoring, shooting, editing, producing, and marketing.Raymond Carney, The films of John Cassavetes, New York and London: Cambridge University Press, 1994: p. 28.Ray Carney, Cassavetes on Cassavetes, Macmillan, 2001: p. 254.
