[[Qarah Buteh, Ijrud]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Qarah Buteh ([tpl]lang-fa|قره بوته[/tpl], also Romanized as Qarah Būteh and Qareh Būteh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795849|Qarah Buteh[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 157, in 40 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
