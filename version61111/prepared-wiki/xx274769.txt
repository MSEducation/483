[[Romania at the 2011 World Amateur Boxing Championships]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 2011 World Amateur Boxing Championships, Boxing in Romania

Romania competed at the 2011 World Amateur Boxing Championships at Baku, Azerbaijan in the hopes of sending boxers at the 2012 Summer Olympics.
The country sent eight athletes to the championships.
