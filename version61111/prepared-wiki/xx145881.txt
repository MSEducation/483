[[Coronet Books]]

CATEGORIES: Book publishing companies of the United Kingdom

Coronet Books was established in 1966 as the paperback an imprint of Hodder & Stoughton, and has a venerable history that includes P.G. Wodehouse and Ian Fleming. The imprint was closed in 2004, but then re-launched in 2010 publishing fiction and non-fiction in hardback and paperback, including works by Chris Ryan, Lorna Byrne, and Auberon Waugh.[tpl]cite news|last=Neilan|first=Catherine|title=Hodder relaunches Coronet imprint for Booth|url=http://www.thebookseller.com/news/hodder-relaunches-coronet-imprint-booth.html|accessdate=11 September 2012|newspaper=The Bookseller|date=9 March 2010[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Imprints|url=http://hodder.co.uk/AboutUs/Imprints.aspx|work=About Us|publisher=Hodder & Stoughton|accessdate=11 September 2012[/tpl]
