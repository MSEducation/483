[[Leiksandin]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Mandalay District, Amarapura Township

 
Leiksandin is a village in Amarapura Township, Mandalay District, in the Mandalay Region of central Burma.It is located 8 miles south of Amarapura and had a population of 310 in 1891 and was reported to have paid RS. 390 in thathameda tax.[tpl]cite book|author=John Percy Hardiman|title=Gazetteer of Upper Burma and the Shan States|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=UroMAAAAIAAJ|accessdate=2 January 2011|year=1901|publisher=Printed by the superintendent, Government printing, Burma|page=40[/tpl]
