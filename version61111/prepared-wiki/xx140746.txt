[[Thug Holiday]]

CATEGORIES: 2002 albums, Trick Daddy albums, Albums produced by Cool & Dre, Gangsta rap albums

Thug Holiday is the fifth studio album by rapper Trick Daddy,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.amazon.com/Thug-Holiday-Trick-Daddy/dp/B00006BTD5/ |title=Thug Holiday: Trick Daddy: Music |publisher=Amazon.com |date= |accessdate=2010-06-06[/tpl] released on August 6, 2002, on Slip-N-Slide Records. The album debuted at #6 on he Billboard 200 with 129,500 copies sold in the first week released.Page 81 A month later the album was certified gold by the RIAA for an excess of 500,000 copies.[tpl]cite web|url=http://riaa.com/goldandplatinumdata.php?table=SEARCH |title=Recording Industry Association of America |publisher=RIAA |date= |accessdate=2012-03-12[/tpl]
