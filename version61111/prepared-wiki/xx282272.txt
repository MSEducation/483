[[William Porter (died 1436)]]

CATEGORIES: 16th-century English people, People of the Tudor period, People from Cambridgeshire, Members of the Parliament of England (pre-1707), 1436 deaths, Year of birth unknown

William Porter (died 1436) of Wimpole, Cambridgeshire,was an English politician.

==Family==

Porter was the son of Reynold Porter of Rutland. By December 1411, Porter had married Agnes (c.1387-4 March 1461), daughter and coheiress of Sir Adam Francis, MP.

==Career==

He was a Member (MP) of the Parliament of England for Cambridgeshire in May 1413.http://www.historyofparliamentonline.org/volume/1386-1421/member/porter-william-ii-1436
