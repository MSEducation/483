[[Kossi Efoui]]

CATEGORIES: 1962 births, Living people, Togolese writers

Kossi Efoui (Anfoin, 1962) is a Togolese writer.[tpl]cite web|title=Kossi Efoui|url=http://www.lesfrancophonies.com/maison-des-auteurs/efoui-kossi|work=lesfrancophonies.com|accessdate=27 October 2010[/tpl]
He studied Philosophy in the University of Lomé, and he took part in Gnassingbé Eyadéma's non-conformist movement for which he lives nowadays in France. He is playwright, chronicler (specially for Jeune Afrique) and novelist.
