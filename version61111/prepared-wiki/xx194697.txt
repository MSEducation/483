[[2005 Valencian Community motorcycle Grand Prix]]

CATEGORIES: 2005 in Grand Prix motorcycle racing, Valencian Community motorcycle Grand Prix, 2005 in Spanish motorsport

The 2005 Valencian Community motorcycle Grand Prix was the last round of the 2005 MotoGP Championship. It took place on the weekend of 4-6 November 2005 at the Circuit de Valencia.
