His media nickname referred to the cartoon character Tom Terrific.
In 1991 Seaver was diagnosed with Lyme disease which caused Bell's palsy. After laying dormant in his system the disease reoccurred in 2012. Seaver suffered from loss of memory not even remembering long term acquaintances, "sleep disorder, nausea and a general overall feeling of chemical imbalance". In January 2013 Seaver was diagnosed with Stage 3 of the disease.
