[[Nephrosis]]

CATEGORIES: Kidney diseases

Nephrosis refers to a non-inflammatory nephropathy.[tpl]MeshName|Nephrosis[/tpl]
Also known as nephrotic syndrome, nephrosis is any degenerative disease of the renal tubules. Nephrosis can be caused by kidney disease, or it may be secondary to another disorder.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.emedicinehealth.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4534 |title=eMedicineHealth Definition of Nephrosis[/tpl]
It should not be confused with nephritis, where inflammation is implied. However, some sources equate nephrosis with nephropathy.[tpl]DorlandsDict|six/000070832|Nephrosis[/tpl]
It can also be used to indicate an emphasis on the renal tubule.[tpl]eMedicineDictionary|nephrosis[/tpl]
Examples include amyloid nephrosis and osmotic nephrosis.
