==Demography==

According to the 2002 Poland census, there were 315 people residing in Przeczów village, of whom 50.8% were male and 49.2% were female. In the village, the population was spread out with 25.7% under the age of 18, 36.2% from 18 to 44, 19% from 45 to 64, and 19% who were 65 years of age or older.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.stat.gov.pl/bdlen/app/strona.html?p_name=indeks | title = Local Data Bank (Bank Danych Lokalnych) – Layout by NTS nomenclature (Układ wg klasyfikacji NTS) | publisher = GUS | location = demografia.stat.gov.pl | date = 10 March 2011[/tpl]
