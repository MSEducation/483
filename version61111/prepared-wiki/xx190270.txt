[[Killer Show]]

CATEGORIES: Nightmare (Japanese band) albums, 2008 albums

Killer Show is Nightmare's fifth studio album.ナイトメア：Nightmare Official Site | DISCOGRAPHY It was released May 21, 2008 in three  different versions: one with just a CD; one with a CD+DVD; and a limited edition with a CD+DVD and a photo book.
