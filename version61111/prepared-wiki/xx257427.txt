[[Jeremy Miller (politician)]]

CATEGORIES: 1983 births, Living people, Minnesota State Senators, Minnesota Republicans, People from Winona, Minnesota, Businesspeople from Minnesota

Jeremy R. Miller (born February 9, 1983) is a Minnesota politician and member of the Minnesota Senate. A member of the Republican Party of Minnesota, he represents District 28, which comprises parts of Fillmore, Houston, and Winona counties in the southeastern part of the state.

==Education==

Miller graduated from Winona Senior High School in 2001, and earned his A.A.S. from Southeast Technical College in Winona in 2004.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.friendsformiller.com/biography.html |title=Jeremy Miller for State Senate |publisher=Friendsformiller.com |date= |accessdate=2012-12-08[/tpl]

==Minnesota Senate==

Miller was first elected to the Senate in 2010 and was re-elected in 2012.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.leg.state.mn.us/legdb/fulldetail.asp?ID=15335 |title=Legislator Record - Miller, Jeremy R |publisher=Minnesota Legislators Past & Present |date= |accessdate=2012-12-08[/tpl] His special legislative concerns include frac sand, jobs, fiscal responsibility, and education.

==Personal life==

Miller is a small business owner, and the chief financial officer of William Miller Scrap Iron and Metal Company in Winona, a family-owned and -operated recycling business that dates back to 1910. 
He is a member of the Board of Directors of the Winona Family YMCA, the Rock Solid Youth Center, and the Winona State University Warrior Club. He is active in the Minnesota State College - Southeast Technical Alumni Association and a member of the Young Professionals of the Winona Area Chamber of Commerce. He also serves as vice president of the Morrie Miller Athletic Foundation, an organization that has been instrumental in supporting and sustaining youth athletics.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.friendsformiller.com/biography.html |title=Jeremy Miller for State Senate | Winona, MN 55987 |publisher=Friendsformiller.com |date= |accessdate=2012-12-08[/tpl]
Miller is married to Janel Miller of Lewiston, Minnesota. The couple have a one-year-old son, Drew, and announced in February 2013 that they are expecting twins.
