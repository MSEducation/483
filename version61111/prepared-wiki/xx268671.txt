[[Park Jae-Seung]]

CATEGORIES: 1923 births, South Korean footballers, South Korea international footballers, Association football defenders, 1954 FIFA World Cup players, Living people

Park Jae-Seung (Korean: 박재승, Hanja: 朴在昇, born 1 April 1923) is a South Korean football defender who played for the South Korea in the 1954 FIFA World Cup.1954 FIFA World Cup Switzerland He also played for Seoul Army Club.
