[[Affonso Évora]]

Affonso de Azevedo Évora (August 29, 1918 – August 2, 2008) was a Brazilian basketball player who competed in the 1948 Summer Olympics. There he won the bronze medal with the national team under the guidance of head coach Moacyr Daiuto.
