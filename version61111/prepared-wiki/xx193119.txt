[[Blake Watson]]

CATEGORIES: Canadian ice hockey left wingers, Ice hockey people from Manitoba, Manitoba Bisons ice hockey players, People from Carman, Manitoba, 1903 births, 1998 deaths

Blake Watson (Born - October 18, 1903 in Carman, Manitoba, Died - May 19, 1998) was a Canadian ice hockey left winger who played for the University of Manitoba Grads team that won the World Hockey Championship.
