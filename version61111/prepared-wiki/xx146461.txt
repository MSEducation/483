[[Intria Items]]

CATEGORIES: Canadian Imperial Bank of Commerce

Intria Items is a Canadian leader in payment and information processing services. Processing more than two billion items each year, the company serves a wide range of clients in the financial, utility, and retail sectors. Intria Items was founded in 1996 by the Canadian Imperial Bank of Commerce to provide payment and information processing services for itself, but now offers one or more of these services to other financial institutions as well.
