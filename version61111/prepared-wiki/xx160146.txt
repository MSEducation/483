[[Belgian general election, 1974]]

CATEGORIES: 1974 elections in Europe, Elections in Belgium, 1974 in Belgium

General elections were held in Belgium on 10 March 1974.Nohlen, D & Stöver, P (2010) Elections in Europe: A data handbook, p289 ISBN 978-3-8329-5609-7 The Belgian Socialist Party emerged as the largest faction in the Chamber of Representatives with 59 of the 212 seats. Voter turnout was 90.3%.Nohlen & Stöver, p291
