[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Rio do Sul]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1968, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Florianópolis, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Rio do Sul ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Rivi Australis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Rio do Sul in the Ecclesiastical province of Florianópolis in Brazil.
