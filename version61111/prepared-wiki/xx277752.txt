[[Mga Awiting Pilipino]]

CATEGORIES: Nora Aunor albums, 1972 albums

Mga Awiting Pilipino is the first studio album by Filipino singer-actress Nora Aunor, that is in Filipino language. released in 1972 by Alpha Records Corporation in the Philippines in LP format[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.superstarnoraaunor.com/mgaawitingpilipino.html|title=Mga Awiting Pilipino album |accessdate=2013-08-08[/tpl]  The album contains some of the original Filipino compositions by National Artist for Music, Levi Celerio. 

==Background==

The album also contains 12 Filipino Folk songs and most of them are regional folk songs sang in local dialect like "Dandansoy" and "Walay Angay" in Hiligaynon, "Ay Kalisud" in Cebuano, "Sarung Bangi" in Bikol among others.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.himig.com.ph/search?category=album_name&query=Mga+Awiting+Pilipino|title=The Filipino Music Collection|accessdate=2013-08-08[/tpl]
