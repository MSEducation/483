[[Qebleh Bolaghi, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Qebleh Bolaghi ([tpl]lang-fa|قبله بلاغي[/tpl], also Romanized as Qebleh Bolāghī; also known as Kiblag-Bulag, Qebleh Būlāgh, and Qiblahbulāq)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3080067|Qebleh Bolaghi[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Pain Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 266, in 45 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
