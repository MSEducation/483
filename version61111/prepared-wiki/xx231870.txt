Vitali Igorevich Kaleshin ([tpl]lang-ru|Виталий Игоревич Калешин[/tpl]) (born October 3, 1980 in Krasnodar) is a Russian footballer currently playing for FC Krasnodar.

==Career==

On 20 February 2009 FC Rubin Kazan have signed right back/right winger, on loan until December 2009 from FC Moscow.
In December 2009 FC Rubin Kazan bought from FC Moscow's right to Kaleshin. He signed a contract with the club for 2 years. If Kaleshin will need a club in 2 years, it will sign a contract for a further 1 year.
On 28 May 2013 he signed a one-year contract with FC Krasnodar.http://www.fckrasnodar.ru/main/news/ct1/59601.html

==Personal==

Vitali is a son of the former FC Kuban Krasnodar player Igor Kaleshin and the younger brother of Yevgeni Kaleshin.
