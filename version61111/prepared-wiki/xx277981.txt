[[Jacqueline Newman]]

CATEGORIES: Benjamin N. Cardozo School of Law alumni, University of Delaware alumni

Jacqueline Newman is an American family and matrimonial law lawyer.

==Education and career==

Newman graduated from a BA in sociology from the University of Delaware in 1996 and a JD from Benjamin N. Cardozo School of Law in 1999.http://www.tenleaders.com/law/matrimonial-divorce-law/new-york/new-york-city-age-45-under/jacqueline-newman/4319/ While in law school she was recruited by Barry Berkman, who taught her in a matrimonial law class and Newman joined Berkman's firm in 1998. Newman |accessdate=July 4, 2013 |publisher=Super Lawyers[/tpl][tpl]cite news |url=http://nymag.com/nymag/rss/all/berkman-bottger-newman-rodd-llp/ |date=April 14, 2013 |date=July 4, 2013 |title=Berkman Bottger Newman & Rodd, LLP |publisher=New York Magazine[/tpl][tpl]cite web |url=http://www.tenleaders.com/law/matrimonial-divorce-law/new-york/new-york-city-age-45-under/jacqueline-newman/4319/ |title=Jacqueline Newman Divorce Law - New York, NY |accessdate=July 4, 2013 |publisher=Ten Leaders[/tpl] She has worked as the hiring manager for the firm[tpl]cite article |url=http://www.crainsnewyork.com/article/20121028/PROFESSIONAL_SERVICES/310289993 |title=So you've got a J.D.
