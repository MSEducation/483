[[Alphington railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1888

Alphington railway station is located on the Hurstbridge line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the north-eastern Melbourne suburb of Alphington opening on 8 May 1888.Alphington Vicsig
