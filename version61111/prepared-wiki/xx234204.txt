[[Kirkianella]]

CATEGORIES: Asteraceae genera

Kirkianella is a monotypic genus of flowering plants in the daisy family, with a single species, K. novae-zelandiae. DNA analysis suggests that it is very closely related to certain species of the genus Sonchus.Kim et al. (1996), Phylogenetic relationships among the genera of the subtribe Sonchinae (Asteraceae): evidence from ITS sequences, Systematic Botany vol. 21 (3) , 417-432.
