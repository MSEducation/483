[[Gholam Veys]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Gholam Veys ([tpl]lang-fa|غلام ويس[/tpl], also Romanized as Gholām Veys and Gholam veis; also known as Ghulām Raīs)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3064291|Gholam Veys[/tpl] is a village in Bizineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,735, in 343 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
