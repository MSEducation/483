[[Joerg Stadler]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, German male television actors, German male film actors

Joerg Stadler is a German actor.  His most noted roles include that of a captured German soldier, 'Steamboat Willie', in Saving Private Ryan, and opposite Brad Pitt in Spy Game as an East German spy left behind enemy lines.
