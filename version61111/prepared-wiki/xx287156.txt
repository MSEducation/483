[[Aq Bolagh, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Aq Bolagh ([tpl]lang-fa|آقبلاغ[/tpl], also Romanized as Āq Bolāgh; also known as Ak-Bulag and Āqbulāq)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3053391|Aq Bolagh[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Bala Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 674, in 157 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
