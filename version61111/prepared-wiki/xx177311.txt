[[Musangsa]]

CATEGORIES: Buddhist temples in South Korea, Kwan Um School of Zen, Seon temples

Musangsa is a Zen Buddhist temple near Daejeon, South Korea, an affiliate of the Kwan Um School of Zen. The temple holds various silent retreats throughout the year and is open to guests on Saturdays at the approval of the head abbot.
