[[Grand Rapids, Grand Haven and Muskegon Railway Depot]]

CATEGORIES: National Register of Historic Places in Michigan, Railway stations in Michigan, Railroad museums in Michigan, Towers in Michigan, Former railway stations in the United States, Museums in Ottawa County, Michigan

Grand Rapids, Grand Haven and Muskegon Railway Depot is a historic railway station for the Grand Rapids, Grand Haven and Muskegon Railway in Coopersville, Michigan.  
The Coopersville Area Historical Society and Museum is now housed in the station.
