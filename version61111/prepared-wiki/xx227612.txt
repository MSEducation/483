[[Tyrants of the Rising Sun]]

CATEGORIES: Arch Enemy albums, 2008 video albums, Live video albums, 2008 live albums, Century Media Records live albums, Century Media Records video albums

Tyrants of the Rising Sun  is a DVD+CD set of Arch Enemy, released in Europe on November 24, 2008 and in North America on November 25, 2008 via Century Media. The DVD contains a live show filmed in Tokyo in early 2008, an in-depth 40 minute road-movie feature, and all promotional videos that have been shot for the band's latest studio album, Rise of the Tyrant.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.roadrunnerrecords.com/blabbermouth.net/news.aspx?mode=Article&newsitemID=109070|title=Arch Enemy's 'Tyrants of the Rising Sun' DVD: 'Nemesis' Clip, Road Movie Preview Available|date=17 November 2008|work=Blabbermouth|publisher=Roadrunner Records|accessdate=21 November 2008[/tpl]
The DVD is available in the following versions: Deluxe edition DVD+2CDs, Standard DVD, Standard 2CDs, ltd EPs.
