[[Hesar-e Olya, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Hesar-e Olya ([tpl]lang-fa|حصارعليا[/tpl], also Romanized as Ḩeşār-e ‘Olyā and Hesar Olya; also known as Ḩeşār-e Bālā and Hisār Bāla)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3066449|Hesar-e Olya[/tpl] is a village in Shivanat Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 350, in 67 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
