[[2005–06 First Macedonian Football League]]

CATEGORIES: 2005–06 domestic association football leagues, 2005–06 in Republic of Macedonia football, First Macedonian Football League seasons

The 2005-06 First Macedonian Football League was the 14th since its establishment. The first matches of the season were played on 8 July 2005 and the last on 21 May 2006. FK Rabotnički defended their championship title, having won their second title in a row.
