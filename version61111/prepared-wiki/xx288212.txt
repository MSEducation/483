[[Pietro Cunti]]

CATEGORIES: 1962 births, Living people, Ice hockey players at the 1988 Winter Olympics, Olympic ice hockey players of Switzerland, SC Bern players, Swiss ice hockey players

Pietro Cunti (born September 3, 1962) is a former Swiss professional ice hockey centre who last played for SC Bern in Switzerland's National League A.
Cunti has participated as a member of the Swiss national team at the 1988 Winter Olympics.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.eliteprospects.com/player.php?player=28509|title=Eliteprospects.com - Pietro Cunti|publisher=EliteProspects.com|accessdate=2013-08-12[/tpl]
