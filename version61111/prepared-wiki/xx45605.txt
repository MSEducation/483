[[Sarah and Son]]

CATEGORIES: 1930 films, American films, English-language films, Black-and-white films, 1930s drama films, Films directed by Dorothy Arzner, Paramount Pictures films, Films based on novels

Sarah and Son is a 1930 American drama film which tells the story of a woman who searches for the son that her abusive husband sold to a wealthy family.  It stars Ruth Chatterton, Fredric March, Fuller Mellish Jr., Gilbert Emery and Doris Lloyd. It was filmed at Paramount Studios in Los Angeles and released by Paramount Pictures.
