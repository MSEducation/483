Michal Vondrka (born May 17, 1982) is a Czech professional ice hockey forward who currently plays for HC Slovan Bratislava in the Kontinental Hockey League. He was selected by the Buffalo Sabres in the 5th round (155th overall) of the 2001 NHL Entry Draft.
Vondrka played previously for HC Slavia Praha, HC České Budějovice, IHC Písek, and HC Karlovy Vary.Profile at HC Slavia Praha official website
