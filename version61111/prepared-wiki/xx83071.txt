[[Felicity Andersen]]

CATEGORIES: 1972 births, Living people, People from Geelong, Australian film actresses, Australian television actresses, Australian soap opera actresses

Felicity Andersen (born 21 October 1972) is an Australian actress.  Born in Geelong, she has appeared in such films as Trail of Passion,[tpl]IMDb title|0378905| Trail Of Passion[/tpl] Queen of the Damned and in the television series Neighbours and the miniseries My Brother Jack and Blonde.
