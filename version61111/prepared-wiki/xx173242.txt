[[WZIQ]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Georgia (U.S. state), Christian radio stations in the United States, Moody Radio affiliate stations, Radio stations established in 1992

WZIQ (106.5 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve Smithville, Georgia, USA.  The station is owned by Augusta Radio Fellowship Institute, Inc.WZIQ fcc.gov. Accessed September 8, 2012
It airs a Christian radio format and is an affiliate of the "Good News Network".[tpl]cite web |title=Station Information Profile |work=Arbitron |url=http://www.arbitron.com/[/tpl]
The station was assigned the call WXAA letters by the Federal Communications Commission on May 17, 1992.[tpl]cite web |title=Call Sign History |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=29130&Callsign=WZIQ |work=FCC Media Bureau CDBS Public Access Database[/tpl]  On May 15, 1993, the station changed its call sign to the current WZIQ.
