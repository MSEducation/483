[[Makoto Niwano]]

CATEGORIES: Manga artists, Living people

[tpl]nihongo|Makoto Niwano|にわの まこと|Niwano Makoto|born May 1, 1964 in Satsumasendai, Kagoshima[/tpl] is a manga artist. He is known for several works, including The Momotaroh, Bomber Girl and Jinnairyū Jyūjyutsu Butouden Majimakun Suttobasu!!."Bomber Girl Volume 1". Amazon.com. Accessed October 4, 2008. Niwano has also written a manga adaptation of Deltora Quest which was adapted into an anime series. He was mentor to Takeshi Obata of Hikaru no Go and Death Note fame.
