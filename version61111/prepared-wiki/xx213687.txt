[[Steve Brooks (statistician)]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, English statisticians, Alumni of the University of Bristol, Alumni of the University of Kent, Alumni of the University of Cambridge, Fellows of King's College, Cambridge, Fellows of Wolfson College, Cambridge, Winners of the Guy Medal in Bronze, Members of the Department of Pure Mathematics and Mathematical Statistics, 1970 births

Stephen Peter "Steve" Brooks is Executive Director of Select Statistical Services Ltd,[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.select-statistics.co.uk| title= Select Statistical Consulting Home Page[/tpl] a statistical research consultancy company based in Exeter, and former professor of statistics at the Statistical Laboratory of the University of Cambridge.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.admin.cam.ac.uk/reporter/1999-2000/weekly/5808/8.html|title=Cambridge University Reporter: Elections and Appointments, May 2000[/tpl]
He received a degree in mathematics from Bristol University in 1991, and a Master's degree in statistics from the University of Kent. He received his Ph.D. at Cambridge; his supervisor was Gareth Roberts. Post-graduation he then returned to Bristol as a lecturer in the Statistics Group and then Senior Lecturer at the University of Surrey. In 2000 Brooks returned to Cambridge first as a fellow of Kings College.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.admin.cam.ac.uk/reporter/2001-02/special/03/14.html|title=Fellows of Kings College|date=2001-10-01|accessdate=2010-12-20|publisher=Cambridge University|work=Cambridge University Reporter[/tpl] and then of Wolfson College.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.admin.cam.ac.uk/reporter/2008-09/special/03/31.html|title=Fellows of Wolfson College|date=2008-10-02|accessdate=2009-04-30|publisher=Cambridge University|work=Cambridge University Reporter[/tpl]
He is a specialist in Markov chain Monte Carlo and applied statistical methods.
He is one of the founding directors[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.ncse.org.uk/documents/reports/annualreports/NCSE_Interim_Review.pdf| title= NCSE Interim Review Report, 2007[/tpl] of the National Centre for Statistical Ecology[tpl]cite web| url= http://www.ncse.org.uk/ | title= National Centre for Statistical Ecology Home Page[/tpl] in 2005.
He left Cambridge in 2006 to become Director of Research for ATASS Sports and is now executive director of Select Statistical Services Ltd a statistical consultancy firm based in Exeter and the Director of the Exeter Initiative for Statistics and its Applications[tpl]cite web | url=http://emps.exeter.ac.uk/research/mathematics/eisa/ | title= ExIStA Home Page[/tpl]
