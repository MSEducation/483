[[Azuma, Gunma (Tone)]]

CATEGORIES: Dissolved municipalities of Gunma Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Azuma|東村|Azuma-mura[/tpl] was a village located in Tone District, northern Gunma Prefecture, Japan.

==Geography==

The Katashina River, a branch of Tone River, flows on the west side of the former village.
