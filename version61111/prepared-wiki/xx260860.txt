[[Glenn Davis (American actor)]]

CATEGORIES: Articles created via the Article Wizard, Living people, American male film actors, People from South Holland, Illinois, Year of birth missing (living people), American male stage actors, Male actors from Illinois

Glenn Davis is an American actor noted for roles  in "Jericho," (2008), "The Unit," (2006) and "24," (2009), according to the Internet Movie Database.
Davis previously appeared onstage in Rajiv Joseph's play "Bengal Tiger at the Baghdad Zoo," at the Kirk Douglas Theatre in Los Angeles. In 2011, Davis will reprise his role, which also stars award-winning actor Robin Williams in his Broadway debut in the spring. Davis plays Tom, a soldier whose hand was bitten off by an angry tiger, played by Williams.
Davis grew up in South Holland, Illinois but resides full-time in Los Angeles. In 2010, he starred as Legba/Marcus in Tarell Alvin McCraney's "Brother/Sister Trilogy" at the Steppenwolf Theatre in Chicago. He also appeared off-Broadway in the Vineyard Theatre production of Tarell McCraney's Wig Out! A play on the KIDZ.
