[[The Deeper You Dig...]]

CATEGORIES: 2006 albums, Butterfingers (Australian band) albums

The Deeper You Dig... is the second studio album released by Australian group, Butterfingers.  It was released 20 June 2006 on the band's independent label, Valley Trash Records.
The album received a lot of commercial success, with the album peaking at #14 on the Australian album chartsAustralian Charts and #2 on the ARIA Urban album chart.http://pandora.nla.gov.au/pan/56172/20090210-1003/www.aria.com.au/pages/documents/aria+report+sample.pdf The single "Figjam" was nominated for the Best Urban Release ARIA Award.Butterfingers at the ARIA Music Awards website Popular with Triple J listeners which gained attention to the tracks "Get Up Outta the Dirt", "Figjam" and "I Like Em When They're Trouble".
"Figjam" contains a sample taken from "77%", as performed by The Herd and "Variety" contains elements from "Ladies Man", performed by Bias B.
