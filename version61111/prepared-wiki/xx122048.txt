[[Michael Owen's WLS 2000]]

CATEGORIES: 2000 video games, Association football video games, Nintendo 64 games, Video games developed in the United Kingdom, Nintendo 64 sports games

Michael Owen's WLS (World League Soccer) 2000 is a soccer video game for the Nintendo 64. It was released in 2000. It features the English soccer star Michael Owen. It was re-released in North America as a women's soccer game under the name Mia Hamm 64 Soccerhttp://ign64.ign.com/articles/075/075999p1.html IGN News "Mia Hamm Soccer to Nintendo 64" about title changing for North American release. on November 9, 2000, starring American soccer star Mia Hamm. Both games use a graphics engine similar to the ISS series.
