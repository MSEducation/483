[[Ōta, Ōita]]

CATEGORIES: Dissolved municipalities of Ōita Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Ōta|大田村|Ōta-mura[/tpl] was a village located in Nishikunisaki District, Ōita Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the village had an estimated population of 1,829 and the density of 39.70 persons per km². The total area was 46.07 km².
On October 1, 2005, Ōta, along with the town of Yamaga (from Hayami District), was merged into the expanded city of Kitsuki.
