[[Ralph Granville]]

CATEGORIES: Scottish footballers, Clyde F.C. players, Nottingham Forest F.C. players, Gateshead F.C. players, Living people, 1931 births, Scottish Football League players, The Football League players, Association football wing halves, Sportspeople from Glasgow

Ralph Granville (born 23 April 1931 in Glasgow) is a former Scottish footballer.
He won the 1955 Scottish Cup with Clyde, before having spells with Nottingham Forest and Gateshead.
