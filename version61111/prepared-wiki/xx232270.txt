Larry L. Belcher (April 4, 1947 â€“ October 6, 2008) was an American educator, politician and a Democratic member of the Kentucky House of Representatives. He represented the 49th district which includes parts of Bullitt County.
Belcher was born in Campbellsville, Kentucky on April 4, 1947.
Belcher first served in the House of Representatives from 1999 to 2001. In 2002, he unsuccessfully ran against Gary Tapp for a State Senate Seat. He returned to the House in 2006 and ran for reelection in 2008.
On October 6, 2008, Belcher was killed in a traffic accident in Edmonson County, Kentucky when his vehicle ran into the back of a stopped tractor-trailer truck on Interstate 65. His wife Linda, a former elementary school principal, replaced Belcher on the 2008 ballot.
