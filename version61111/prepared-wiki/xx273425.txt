[[No. 321 Expeditionary Combat Support Squadron RAAF]]

CATEGORIES: RAAF ground support squadrons

No. 321 Expeditionary Combat Support Squadron (322ECSS) was a Royal Australian Air Force (RAAF) ground support squadron based at RAAF Base Darwin near Darwin, Northern Territory.[tpl]cite web|title=No. 396 Expeditionary Combat Support Wing|url=http://www.airforce.gov.au/Wings/396ecsw.aspx|publisher=Royal Australian Air Force|accessdate=25 September 2011[/tpl] 
Along with other northern ECSS it was formed after the disbanding of 386ECSS at RAAF Richmond in 2006.[tpl]cite news|title=396 spreads its wing|url=http://www.defence.gov.au/news/raafnews/editions/4901/topstories/story7.htm|accessdate=25 September 2011|newspaper=Air Force|date=8 February 2007[/tpl] 
On 1 July 2010 changes to the structure of the Combat Support Group resulted in the functions of 321ECSS being integrated with No. 13 (City of Darwin) Squadron at RAAF Darwin and 321ECSS being disbanded.[tpl]cite news|last=Stackpool|first=Andrew|title=Over To You Now|url=http://digital.realviewtechnologies.com/default.aspx?iid=38875&startpage=page0000006|accessdate=25 September 2011|newspaper=Air Force|date=5 August 2010|page=6[/tpl]
