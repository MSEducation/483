[[Days Like These (song)]]

CATEGORIES: 2004 singles, 2003 songs, The Cat Empire songs

"Days Like These" was the second single taken from The Cat Empire's 2003 debut album. It was their first CD single  released in their native Australia,Official Website where it peaked at #37 in the singles chart.[tpl]cite web |url=http://australian-charts.com/showitem.asp?interpret=The+Cat+Empire&titel=Days+Like+These&cat=s |title=australian-charts.com - The Cat Empire - Days Like These |accessdate=25 April 2009[/tpl]

==Promotional releases==

150px|thumb|left|Promo cover artThere was only one promotional release of Days Like These. The disc contains 1 track - the radio edit of Days Like These. They were given out at concerts and also to radio stations for airplay purposes. The cover is similar to the Hello 'black' promo, with the colours being inverted (mainly white) and the text is different. The disc is labeled CDRP694 and is produced by Virgin Records Australia.
