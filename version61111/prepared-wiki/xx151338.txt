[[Atléticos de Levittown FC]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Puerto Rico, Puerto Rico Soccer League 1st Division, 1989 establishments in Puerto Rico

Atlético de Levittown is a Puerto Rican football club which played in the now defunct Campeonato Nacional de Fútbol de Puerto Rico. Now the team plays in the Puerto Rico Soccer League first division. They now have a new stage[tpl]Clarify|date=October 2013|reason=Should 'stage' be 'field' or 'stadium'?[/tpl] in Levittown called Pista de Levittown where they played their first soccer game vs. Criollos, which they won 1-0.
