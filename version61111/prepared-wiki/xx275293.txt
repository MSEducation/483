[[Martin Höhener]]

CATEGORIES: Genève-Servette HC players, HC Ambri-Piotta players, Kloten Flyers players, Nashville Predators draft picks, SC Bern players, Swiss ice hockey players, ZSC Lions players, Sportspeople from Zürich, 1980 births, Living people

Martin Höhener (born June 23, 1980) is a Swiss professional ice hockey defenceman. He is currently playing for the SC Bern of Switzerland's National League A.Eliteprospects.com – Bern He was selected by the Nashville Predators in the 9th round (284th overall) of the 2000 NHL Entry Draft.
In 2002, he played for the Switzerland national ice hockey team in the Olympic Games in Salt Lake City, Utah.
