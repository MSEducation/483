[[Martin Andersson (footballer born 1982)]]

CATEGORIES: 1982 births, Living people, Swedish footballers, Association football midfielders, Enköpings SK players, Djurgårdens IF Fotboll players, Vasalunds IF players

Martin Andersson (born May 9, 1982) is a Swedish football player, currently a free agent.

==Career==

Andersson started his career at Enköpings SK. He joined Djurgårdens IF in the beginning of 2008 together with Sebastian Rajalakso, also from Enköpings SK.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.dif.se/sidor/nyheter/nyheter.asp?action=view&id=8101&typ=1 |title=Rajalakso och Andersson till DIF |first=Jonas |last=Riedel |accessdate=2008-08-21 |work=Dif.se |publisher=Djurgårdens IF Fotboll |date=2007-09-13 |language=Swedish[/tpl] He made his Allsvenskan debut against IFK Norrköping on March 30, 2008. Due to an injury in the groin, Andersson used painkillers during the spring 2008 to be able to play Djurgården's games. The injury eventually caused too much pain and Andersson did not play for much of the 2008 season.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.fotbolldirekt.se/martin-andersson-blir-borta-l%C3%A4nge |title=Martin Andersson blir borta länge |first=Daniel |last=Bogefors |accessdate=2008-08-21 |publisher=Fotbolldirekt.se |date=2008-06-27 |language=Swedish [/tpl] He left on loan to Vasalunds IF in 2009 and returned to Djurgården after the season.[tpl]cite web|last=Bogefors|first=Daniel|title=Klart - Martin Andersson lämnar Djurgården|url=http://www.fotbolldirekt.se/klart-martin-andersson-l%C3%A4mnar-djurg%C3%A5rden|work=fotbolldirekt.se|accessdate=2 January 2011|language=Swedish[/tpl] His contract with Djurgården expired in December 2010 and was not extended.[tpl]cite news|title=Sportbladet granskar – så är läget i ditt lag|url=http://www.aftonbladet.se/sportbladet/fotboll/sverige/allsvenskan/article8100054.ab|accessdate=2 January 2011|newspaper=Aftonbladet|date=10 November 2010|author=Kristoffer Bergström|author2=Markus Eriksson|author3=Michael Wagner |language=Swedish[/tpl]
