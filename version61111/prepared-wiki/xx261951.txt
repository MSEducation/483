[[Jabaquara (São Paulo Metro)]]

CATEGORIES: São Paulo Metro stations

Jabaquara is a station on Line 1 (Blue) of the São Paulo Metro and is the current terminus.Information about Jabaquara on the São Paulo Metro website (Portuguese) It will be integrated with the planned Line 17 (Gold).
The station integrates with the Jabaquara Intermunicipal Terminal, allowing passengers to transfer to SPTrans and EMTU bus lines with their Bilhete Único card.
