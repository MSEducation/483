[[The Charladies' Ball]]

CATEGORIES: Irish folk songs, Traditional ballads, Irish words and phrases

"The Charladies' Ball" is an Irish folk song written by Harry O'Donovan and popularised by his collaborator, music-hall singer and comedian Jimmy O'Dea.Harte, Frank, 'Daybreak and a Candle End' liner notes  The song concerns O'Dea's character Biddy Mulligan's attendance at a ball in Dublin.

==Recordings==

The song was recorded by Frank Harte on the album Daybreak and a Candle End.
Also by the Black Brothers on their album What a Time.
Also by Martyn Johnson on his album Jolly Boys 2008.
Also by Tara on its album Irish Music.
