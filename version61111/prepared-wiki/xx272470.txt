[[Fast Company (1929 film)]]

CATEGORIES: 1929 films, 1920s comedy films, American films, American comedy films, Black-and-white films, Films directed by A. Edward Sutherland

Fast Company is a 1929 American comedy film directed by A. Edward Sutherland and starring Jack Oakie and Evelyn Brent.[tpl]cite web |url=http://movies.nytimes.com/movie/91069/Fast-Company/overview |title=Fast Company |accessdate=2011-09-25 |work=NY Times[/tpl]

==Preservation status==

According to the Internet Movie Database, the UCLA Film and Television Archive has reels 1, 2, and 3 of this film, with reel 4 having disintegrated in 1990.
