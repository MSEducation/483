[[WBAW-FM]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in South Carolina

WBAW-FM (99.1 FM) is a silent radio station which most recently broadcast a gospel music format as "Praise 99.1". Licensed to Barnwell, South Carolina, USA, the station is currently owned by Bullie Broadcasting Corporation.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=WBAW-FM |title=WBAW-FM Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
