[[Jack Abbott (coach)]]

CATEGORIES: Dartmouth Big Green baseball players, Dartmouth Big Green football players, Maine Black Bears baseball coaches, Maine Black Bears football coaches

George Henry (Jack) Abbott (February 6, 1873 – June 22, 1918) was the head coach of the University of Maine's football team in 1896 and compiled a 1–3–2 record.  Also in 1896, Abbott was the head coach of the Maine Black Bears baseball team, which a 5-4 record that season.http://books.google.ca/books?ei=0x5rUqGxCqbD2AX3iYCwBw&id=SP8SAAAAIAAJ&dq=%22****Our+anticipations+soon+became+realizations%2C+for+our+coach%2C+%22Jack%22+Abbott%2C+of+Dartmouth%22&focus=searchwithinvolume&q=Abbott
Abbott was a native of Manchester, New Hampshire, where he was born in 1873,https://familysearch.org/pal:/MM9.1.1/FL26-VMP and resided during his years at Dartmouth.http://www.ebooksread.com/authors-eng/dartmouth-college/general-catalogue-of-dartmouth-college-and-the-associated-schools-1769-1910-inc-hci/page-45-general-catalogue-of-dartmouth-college-and-the-associated-schools-1769-1910-inc-hci.shtml He died at Concord, New Hampshire on June 22, 1918.[ref]https://familysearch.org/pal:/MM9.1.1/FSJC-K56
