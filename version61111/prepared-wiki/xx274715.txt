[[Olympic Sports Centre, Riga]]

CATEGORIES: Sports venues in Latvia

The Olympic Sport Centre is a multi-functional sports facility in Riga which was opened in 2005 for continuous and perfect athlete trainings and preparation for competitions. 
Olympic Sport Centre covers the area of approximately 24.000 square meters and can be visited by about 2.000 people per day. The Centre has all the equipment and prepared halls for football, basketball, volleyball, athletics, swimming, gymnastics and aerobic training. In 2009, Olympic Sport Centre was awarded the status of “European healthy stadium”.
