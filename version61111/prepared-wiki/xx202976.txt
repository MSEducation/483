[[Nușfalău]]

CATEGORIES: Communes in Sălaj County

Nușfalău ([tpl]lang-hu|Szilágynagyfalu[/tpl] or Nagyfalu) is a commune located in Sălaj County, Romania. It is composed of two villages, Bilghez (Bürgezd) and Nușfalău; Boghiș and Bozieș split off in 2005 to form Boghiș commune.
