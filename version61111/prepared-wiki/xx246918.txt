[[Floriskraal Dam]]

CATEGORIES: Dams in South Africa, Dams completed in 1957

Floriskraal Dam is a combined gravity and buttress type dam located on the Buffels River, near Laingsburg, Western Cape, South Africa. It was established in 1957.List of South African Dams from the Department of Water Affairs and Forestry (South Africa) The primary purpose of the dam is to serve for irrigation. Its hazard potential has been ranked high (3).
