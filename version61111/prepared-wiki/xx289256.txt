[[Mostafalu, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Mostafalu ([tpl]lang-fa|مصطفي لو[/tpl], also Romanized as Moşţafálū and Mostafa Loo; also known as Mustāfiu)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3075898|Mostafalu[/tpl] is a village in Shivanat Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 143, in 30 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
