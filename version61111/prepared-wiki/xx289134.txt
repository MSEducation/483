[[Mandaq]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Mandaq ([tpl]lang-fa|منداق[/tpl], also Romanized as Mandāq; also known as Mandak and Mindāq)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3073888|Mandaq[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 260, in 60 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
