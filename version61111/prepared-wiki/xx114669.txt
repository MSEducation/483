[[Attulus]]

CATEGORIES: Salticidae, Jumping spiders of Europe, Spiders of Europe

Attulus is a genus of jumping spiders. Its only species A. helveolus is found in Europe.

==Name==

The genus name is a diminutive form of the common ending for salticid genera -attus.

==Distribution==

A. helveolus can for example be found in the dunes of the Atlantic coast.Catalogue of French Salticidae
