[[Vadym Strashkevych]]

Vadym Strashkevych ([tpl]lang-ua|Вадим В′ячеславович Страшкевич[/tpl]; born 21 April 1994 in Lviv, Ukraine) is a professional Ukrainian football defender who currently plays for club FC Karpaty Lviv in Ukrainian Premier League.
Strashkevych is the product of the UFK Lviv School System. He made his debut for FC Karpaty entering as a second-half substitute against FC Illichivets Mariupol on 2 August 2013 in Ukrainian Premier League.[tpl]cite web|publisher=ua-football.com|title="Іллічівець" - "Карпати" - 1:1. He also played for Ukrainian under-17 national football team.
