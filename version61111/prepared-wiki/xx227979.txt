[[Institute of Electronics, Information and Communication Engineers]]

CATEGORIES: Electronic engineering, Organizations based in Japan, Telecommunications organizations

The [tpl]Nihongo|Institute of Electronics, Information and Communication Engineers|電子情報通信学会|Denshi Jōhō Tsūshin Gakkai|extra=IEICE[/tpl] is a Japanese institute specializing in the areas of electronic, information and communication engineering and associated fields. It was established in May, 1917. Its headquarters are located in Tokyo, Japan.
