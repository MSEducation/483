[[SRB Communications]]

CATEGORIES: Articles created via the Article Wizard, Marketing companies of the United States

SRB Communications, LLC is a multimedia communications agency that specializes in social marketing, multicultural advertising and broadcast production. SRB Communications develops strategic integrated advertising and marketing campaigns for corporations, government agencies, and non-profit organizations. 
The company assists its clients in the development of brand identity, positioning and strategies in advertising, marketing and public relations through cross-platform media formats in video production, print, and multimedia.

==History==

SRB Communications was founded in 1990 by current CEO Sheila Brooks. The media and communications agency has an impressive list of clients including Washington Gas, Disney Parks, National Association of Black Journalists, McCormick and Company, U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD), U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), National Center for Children and Families, U.S. Army, U.S. Navy, TV One, U.S. Postal Service and Pepco Holdings, Inc.
