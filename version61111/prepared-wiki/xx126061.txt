Mouloudia Club of Oujda ([tpl]lang-ar|مولودية وجدة[/tpl]) is a Moroccan football club based in Oujda.Mouloudia which closely means in Arabic Birth was named after the coincidence of the day of it creation: March 16, 1946 (12 second spring of 1365 Hjeria) with the anniversary of the birth of  Muhammad.
