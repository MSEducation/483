[[Josef Pürer]]

CATEGORIES: 1894 births, 1917 deaths, People from Brno, Austro-Hungarian World War I flying aces

Oberleutnant Josef Pürer was a World War I flying ace credited with six aerial victories. He was one of only four aces to win two awards of the Military Merit Cross.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.theaerodrome.com/aces/austrhun/purer.php|title=Retrieved 4 April 2011 |publisher=Theaerodrome.com |date= |accessdate=2013-06-16[/tpl]
