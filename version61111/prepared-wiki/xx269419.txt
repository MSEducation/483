[[(15227) 1986 VA]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1986, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(15227) 1986 VA is a main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on November 4, 1986.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
