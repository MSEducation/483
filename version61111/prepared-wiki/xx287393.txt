[[Yōko Aramaki]]

CATEGORIES: 1981 births, Japanese impressionists (entertainers), Japanese singers, Living people, People from Okayama, Musicians from Okayama Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Yōko Aramaki|荒牧陽子|Aramaki Yōko[/tpl] (January 14, 1981 - ) is a Japanese singer and impressionist connected to the K-Dash-associated office Peach. She is a graduate of Shujitsu High School. While she aimed to become a singer since childhood, she is also trained in piano and enka. In November 2012, she announced she would take a break from singing to recuperate.[tpl]cite web|title=荒牧陽子が一時休養！恐怖…体調不良|url=http://www.nikkansports.com/entertainment/news/f-et-tp0-20121109-1044676.html|work=Nikkan Sports|accessdate=16 August 2013[/tpl]
