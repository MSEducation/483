[[Luciano Rebay]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, Columbia University faculty, Guggenheim Fellows

Luciano Rebay, known especially for his work on the poet Giuseppe Ungaretti, is one of the leading post-war critics of Italian literature in America.  He has had a long affiliation with Columbia University, where he is Giuseppe Ungaretti Professor Emeritus of Italian.
