Sven Schultze (born July 11, 1978) is a German professional basketball power forward/center. He is 2.08 m (6'10") in height and he weighs 111 kg (245 pounds). He currently plays for Alba Berlin of the German League.

==German national team==

Schultze is also a member of the German national basketball team.
