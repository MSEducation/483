[[Beaufortia sparsa]]

CATEGORIES: Myrtaceae, Myrtales of Australia, Rosids of Western Australia

Beaufortia sparsa, commonly known as the Swamp Bottlebrush or the Gravel Bottlebrush, is a species of shrub that grows in the south west and great southern regions of Western Australia.
B. sparsa is an evergreen shrub that grows from between [tpl]convert|1|m|ft|0[/tpl] to [tpl]convert|3|m|ft|0[/tpl] tall[tpl]cite web|url=http://florabase.calm.wa.gov.au/browse/profile/5392|title=Florabase -Beaufortia sparsa|year=1995|accessdate=2008-04-28[/tpl] and a width of [tpl]convert|1|m|ft|0[/tpl] to [tpl]convert|2.5|m|ft|0[/tpl].[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.yarraranges.vic.gov.au/karwarra/calendar_march.htm|title=Karwarra - Flowering Calendar - March|year=2007|accessdate=2008-04-28| archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20080421130810/http://www.yarraranges.vic.gov.au/karwarra/calendar_march.htm| archivedate= 21 April 2008 | deadurl= no[/tpl]  The leaves of the plant are bright green and oval shaped with a length of approximately [tpl]convert|10|mm|in|0[/tpl].[tpl]cite web|url=http://asgap.org.au/b-spa.html|title=Association of Societies for Growing Australian Plants-Beaufortia sparsa|year=2007|accessdate=2008-04-28| archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20080510102123/http://asgap.org.au/b-spa.html| archivedate= 10 May 2008 | deadurl= no[/tpl]
The flowers of B. sparsa are a bright orange to red in colour and the shape of a bottlebrush flower cluster or inflorescence that tend to occur toward the end of the branch.[tpl]cite web|url=http://toptropicals.com/catalog/uid/Beaufortia_sparsa.htm|title=Top Tropicals Plant Catalogue|year=2008|accessdate=2008-04-28[/tpl] Flowers are produced from January to April and September to November.
The plant grows naturally in damp areas and is not drought tolerant.[tpl]cite web|url=http://geographelandcarenursery.mysouthwest.com.au/Our%20Species%20List%20(fast)/Beaufortia%20sparsa|title=GEOGRAPHE COMMUNITY LANDCARE NURSERY|year=2005|accessdate=2008-04-28 |archiveurl = http://web.archive.org/web/20080424093159/http://geographelandcarenursery.mysouthwest.com.au/Our+Species+List+(fast)/Beaufortia+sparsa  |archivedate = 2008-04-24[/tpl]
