[[Benson High School (Minnesota)]]

CATEGORIES: Educational institutions established in 1890, Public high schools in Minnesota, Schools in Swift County, Minnesota

Benson High School is a public high school located in Benson, Minnesota. The school educates about 520 students in grades 7 to 12 in the Benson Public Schools District.
