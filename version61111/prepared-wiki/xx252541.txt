Mark Smith (born 10 October 1961) is a former English footballer who played, as a full-back, for West Ham United.

==Career==

Smith was a product of West Ham's youth system. He played for Newham, Essex and London Boys and was an England Youth trialist before signing for West Ham in October 1979. He captained the youth team before making his West Ham debut in October 1979 in a 5–1, League Cup victory against Southend United.
