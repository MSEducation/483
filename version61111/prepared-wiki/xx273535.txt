[[Brian Mitchell (American football coach)]]

CATEGORIES: 1968 births, Living people, People from Indianapolis, Indiana, Players of American football from Indiana, American football cornerbacks, BYU Cougars football players, Atlanta Falcons players, East Carolina Pirates football coaches

Brian Keith Mitchell (born December 13, 1968) is an American football coach and former professional cornerback who played three seasons for the Atlanta Falcons in the National Football League. He is currently the cornerbacks coach for the West Virginia Mountaineers college football team.

==Professional career==

Mitchell was drafted by the Atlanta Falcons in the seventh round (172nd overall) of the 1991 NFL Draft.
