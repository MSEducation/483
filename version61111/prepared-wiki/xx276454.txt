[[(46605) 1993 HQ1]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1993, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Main Belt asteroids

(46605) 1993 HQ1 is an inner main-belt minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on April 18, 1993.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
