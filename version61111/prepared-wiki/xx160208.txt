[[Parabanks Shopping Centre]]

CATEGORIES: Shopping centres in Adelaide

Parabanks Shopping Centre is a shopping centre located in the major shopping area of Salisbury, a suburb in the northern part of Metropolitan Adelaide, South Australia.

==History==

In April 2008, property group Stockland sold the shopping centre to the Angaet Group who also own Sefton Plaza and Aberfoyle Hub Shopping Centre.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.indaily.com.au/default.aspx?iid=7513&startpage=page0000027 | title=Dimauro buys Parabanks centre | publisher=InDaily | page=27 |date=2008-04-18 [/tpl]

==Redevelopment Plans==

Plans to upgrade the centre at a cost of A$100M were announced in February 2009 consisting of an additional supermarket, food court, up to 60 specialty shops plus the installation of escalators linking to the existing undercover car park.[tpl]cite web | url=http://news-review-messenger.whereilive.com.au/news/story/parabanks-plans-100m-expansion/ | title=Parabanks plans $100m expansion | publisher=Messenger Newspapers | first=Jane | last=Tonkin | date=2009-02-24 [/tpl]
However the upgrade may not go ahead should a nearby shopping centre plan for Salisbury East and Salisbury Heights residents be approved.[tpl]cite web | url=http://news-review-messenger.whereilive.com.au/news/story/plain-centre-may-stymie-parabanks/ | title=Parabanks threat | publisher=Messenger Newspapers | first=Kym | last=Morgan | date=2009-06-30 [/tpl]
