[[Dave Moore Award]]

CATEGORIES: Awards established in 1999, Sports writing awards, American literary awards

The baseball journal Elysian Fields Quarterly began awarding the Dave Moore Award in 1999.Dave Moore Award at Elysian Fields Quarterly, official website The honor is given to the “most important” baseball book of the year. It is named for the late Dave Moore, a well known Minnesota broadcaster, who loved sports and literature.
