[[Upstyledown]]

CATEGORIES: 2000 albums, 28 Days albums, English-language albums

Upstyledown is the second studio album by 28 Days, released in 2000. It debuted at No. 1 on the Australian ARIAnet Albums Chart and peaked at No. 33 on the New Zealand albums chart.[tpl]cite web|url=http://australian-charts.com/showitem.asp?interpret=28+Days&titel=Upstyledown&cat=a|title=Australian charts archive|accessdate=2012-01-10[/tpl]
