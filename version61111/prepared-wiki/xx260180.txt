[[Appetite for Destruction (Ruby Isle album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2010 albums, Mark Mallman albums

Appetite for Destruction is a 2010 album by Ruby Isle. It is a cover of Guns N' Roses' 1987 album of the same name. It was released through iTunes on December 2, 2010.Connecting to the iTunes Store It was scheduled for a CD release with a release party scheduled for January 29, 2011, but on January 11, 2011 it was announced that it was being pulled from all music stores and online music outlets.Kindercore: It was fun while it lasted
