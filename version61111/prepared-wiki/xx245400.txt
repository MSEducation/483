[[Roderich von Erckert]]

CATEGORIES: 1821 births, 1900 deaths, German ethnographers

Roderich von Erckert (1821–1900) was a German ethnographer and officer. His work on the Caucasian languages includes Der Kaukasus und seine Völker (The Caucasus and Its Peoples; 1887); Die Sprachen des kaukasischen Stammes (The Languages of the Caucasian Tribes; 1895); and Wanderungen und Siedelungen der germanischen Stämme in Mitteleuropa (Migration and Settlement of the Germanic Tribes in Central Europe; 1901).
