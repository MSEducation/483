==History==

The land that would become Westview Cemetery was the site of a portion of the Battle of Ezra Church, a Civil War battle."Battle of Ezra Church, Westview Cemetery"  The cemetery was established in 1884[tpl] Citation | title=The History of Westview | publisher=Westview Cemetery | url=http://www.westviewcemetery.com/history.php | accessdate=2009-01-28 [/tpl] on Atlanta's west side when Oakland Cemetery was mostly filled out.  Over [tpl]convert|582|acre|km2[/tpl] were purchased by a private corporation, headed by E. P. McBurney.[tpl]Citation needed|date=January 2009[/tpl] The main gate is one of the oldest standing structures in Atlanta.

==Location==

The cemetery is located at 1680 Westview Dr SW, Atlanta, GA 30310. Its telephone number is (404) 755-6611 The office is open Monday-Friday 9-5 and Saturday 9:30-2 and is closed on Sunday. Gates are open from 8 am until 5:30 pm every day except Christmas and Thanksgiving. The site is a fifteen-minute walk from the West Lake MARTA station
