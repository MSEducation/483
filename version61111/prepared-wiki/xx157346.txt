[[Lay & Love]]

CATEGORIES: 2007 singles, Will Oldham songs

"Lay & Love" is a Bonnie 'Prince' Billy single taken from the 2006 album The Letting Go. The single includes two Bob Dylan cover songs. It comes in both 12" vinyl and an Enhanced CD, which includes the music video for the title track, directed by Vernon Chatman and John Lee (of Wonder Showzen).
