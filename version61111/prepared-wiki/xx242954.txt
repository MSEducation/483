[[14th Indian Infantry Brigade]]

CATEGORIES: British Indian Army brigades

The 14th Indian Infantry Brigade was an Infantry formation of the Indian Army during World War II. The brigade was formed at Attock in October 1940, and assigned to the 7th Indian Infantry Division. In April 1942, the brigade was renumbered the 114th Indian Infantry Brigade. The brigade fought in the Burma Campaign with the 7th and 26th Indian Infantry Divisions.[tpl]cite web|accessdate=2009-10-19|publisher=Order of Battle|title=14 Brigade|url=http://www.ordersofbattle.com/UnitData.aspx?UniX=6156&Tab=Sup[/tpl]
