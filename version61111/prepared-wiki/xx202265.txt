[[Let Me Down (Jonas Brothers song)]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 songs, Jonas Brothers songs, Songs written by Nick Jonas, Songs written by Joe Jonas, Songs written by Kevin Jonas

"Let Me Down" is a song by American pop rock band the Jonas Brothers released on their third studio album, A Little Bit Longer in 2008. It was written by the band itself.

==Song description==

Nick told Twist Magazine that the song is about a girlfriend telling you you're not good enough. You've had enough of hearing that you're not doing enough for her.

==Chart and airplay information==

It hasn't been confirmed if this song will turn into one of the Jonas Brothers single.
