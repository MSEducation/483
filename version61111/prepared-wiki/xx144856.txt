[[Gradina, Virovitica-Podravina County]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Virovitica-Podravina County, Municipalities of Croatia

Gradina is a municipality in Croatia in the Virovitica–Podravina County. It has a population of 4,485 (2001), 85.6% which are Croats. The municipality contains eleven villages:
Gradina, Bačevac, Brezovica, Budakovac, Detkovac, Lipovac, Lug Gradinski, Novi Gradac, Rušani, Vladimirovac, and Žlebina.
