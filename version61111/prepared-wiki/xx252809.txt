[[Giuseppe Merisi]]

CATEGORIES: 1938 births, Living people, People from Treviglio, Italian bishops, Bishops of Lodi, 20th-century Roman Catholic priests, 20th-century Roman Catholic bishops

Giuseppe Merisi (born September 25, 1938 in Treviglio) is the current bishop of the Roman Catholic Diocese of Lodi.

==Biography==

He was ordained priest on February 22, 1971.
He was appointed auxiliary bishop of Milan and titular Bishop of Drusiliana on September 8, 1995.
Receiving his episcopal consecration on November 4, 1995 from cardinal Carlo Maria Martini.
He was appointed bishop of Lodi on November 14, 2005 and installed him in the diocese on December 17, 2005.
He replaces the previous bishop of Lodi Giacomo Capuzzi.
