[[Russ Hill]]

CATEGORIES: Hamlets in Surrey, Mole Valley

Russ Hill is a hamlet in the Mole Valley District, in the county of Surrey, England. Nearby settlements include the towns of Crawley, Horley, Reigate and Redhill and the village of Charlwood. For transport there is the A23 road, the A24 road, the A217 road and the M23 motorway nearby. There is also London Gatwick Airport nearby. Russ Hill is served by Southdown PSV route G4 to Charlwood, Gatwick Airport and Maidenbower
