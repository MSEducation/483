[[1976 Australian Sports Sedan Championship]]

CATEGORIES: Australian Sports Sedan Championship, 1976 in Australian motorsport

The 1976 Australian Sports Sedan Championship was a CAMS sanctioned motor racing title for drivers CAMS Online Manual of Motor Sport > About CAMS > Australian Titles Retrieved from www.camsmanual.com.au on 18 October 2009 of Group B Sports Sedans.Official Programme, Adelaide International Raceway, Sunday, 22 August 1976, page 3 It was the inaugural Australian Sports Sedan Championship.

==Calendar==

The title was contested over a seven round series with two heats per round.Jim Shepherd, A History of Australian Motor Sport, 1980, pages 157-158

==Points system==

Championship points were awarded on a 9-6-4-3-2-1 basis to the six best placed drivers at each round.
