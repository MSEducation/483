[[Soleyman Bolaghi]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Soleyman Bolaghi ([tpl]lang-fa|سليمان بلاغي[/tpl], also Romanized as Soleymān Bolāghī; also known as Soleymān Bolāgh, Soleymānbūlāghī, Sulaimanbulāqi, and Suleyman-Bulag)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3086026|Soleyman Bolaghi[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Pain Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 40, in 9 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
