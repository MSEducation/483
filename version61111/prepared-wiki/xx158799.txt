[[Ascochyta pisi]]

CATEGORIES: Plant pathogens and diseases, Ascochyta

Ascochyta pisi fungal is a plant pathogen that causes ascochyta blight on pea causing lesions of stems, leaves and pods. These same symptoms can also be caused by Ascochyta pinodes and the two fungi are not easily distinguishable.Pea Ascochyta Blight Symptoms
