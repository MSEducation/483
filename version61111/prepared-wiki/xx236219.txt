[[Sergei Gavrilov]]

CATEGORIES: 1987 births, Living people, Russian footballers, FC Tom Tomsk players, FC Fakel Voronezh players

Sergei Olegovich Gavrilov ([tpl]lang-ru|Серге́й Олегович Гаврилов[/tpl]; born January 14, 1987) is a Russian professional football player. Currently, he plays for FC Zvezda Ryazan.
He played for the main squad of FC Tom Tomsk in the Russian Cup.
