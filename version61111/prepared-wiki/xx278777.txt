[[Zangi Kuh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Zangi Kuh ([tpl]lang-fa|زنگي كوه[/tpl], also Romanized as Zangī Kūh; also known as Dāgh Zangī and Dag-Zangi)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3058992|Zangi Kuh[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 160, in 40 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
