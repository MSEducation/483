Vera Lindsay (née Poliakoff;Vera Lindsay Dictionary of Pseudonyms: 13,000 Assumed Names and Their Origins 1911–1992) was a British Shakespearean actress.

==Career==

Vera Lindsay performed at The Old Vic during the 1930s alongside Laurence Olivier, John Gielgud and under the direction of Michel Saint-Denis.
She also appeared in a number of early TV productions including Annajanska the Bolshevik Empress (1939),Annajanska the Bolshevik Empress in Internet Movie Database Katharine and Petruchio (1939),Katharine and Petruchio in Internet Movie Database The Tempest (1939),The Tempest in Internet Movie Database Twelfth Night (1939) Twelfth Nightin Internet Movie Database and Spellbound (1941)Spellbound in Internet Movie Database
