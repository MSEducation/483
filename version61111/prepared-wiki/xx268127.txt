[[Central District (Ardestan County)]]

CATEGORIES: Ardestan County, Districts of Isfahan Province

The Central District of Ardestan County ([tpl]lang-fa|بخش مرکزی شهرستان اردستان[/tpl]) is a district (bakhsh) in Ardestan County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 30,838, in 8,958 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|10[/tpl]  The District has two cities: Ardestan and Mahabad. The District has five rural districts (dehestan): Barzavand Rural District, Garmsir Rural District, Hombarat Rural District, Kachu Rural District, and Olya Rural District.
