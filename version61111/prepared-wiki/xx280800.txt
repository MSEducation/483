[[Qaleh, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Qaleh ([tpl]lang-fa|قلعه[/tpl], also Romanized as Qal‘eh; also known as Kalakh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3078876|Qaleh[/tpl] is a village in Bonab Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 283, in 55 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
