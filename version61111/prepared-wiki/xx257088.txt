[[South American snapping turtle]]

CATEGORIES: Chelydra, Reptiles of Colombia, Reptiles of Costa Rica, Reptiles of Ecuador, Reptiles of Honduras, Reptiles of Panama, Reptiles of Nicaragua

The South American snapping turtle (Chelydra acutirostris) is a species of turtle in the Chelydridae family. This species was previously considered a subspecies of Chelydra serpentina.

==Geographic range==

It is found in Colombia, Costa Rica, Ecuador, Honduras, Nicaragua, and Panama.

==Subspecies==

No subspecies are recognized.[tpl]Harnvb|Fritz|2007|pp=171[/tpl]
