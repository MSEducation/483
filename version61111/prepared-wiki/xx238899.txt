[[Backwaxed]]

CATEGORIES: Anvil (band) albums, 1983 compilation albums, Attic Records albums

Backwaxed is the fourth album by the Canadian heavy metal band Anvil. It is a compilation album of music released on previous albums with five previously unreleased tracks. This is Anvil's only official release that does not feature a three word title with the first and last words starting with the same letter.
