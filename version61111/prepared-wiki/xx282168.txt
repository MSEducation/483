[[Huang Yali]]

Huang Yali ([tpl]zh|s=黄雅莉 |t=黃雅莉|hp=Huáng Yǎlì[/tpl]) (born March 22, 1989) is a Chinese pop singer who earned sixth place in the 2006 Super Girl contest.

==Biography==

Huang was born and raised in Yutan Town, Ningxiang County, Changsha City, Hunan Province, China. During her childhood years, Huang developed an interest in singing and experimented with several different vocal techniques after listening to the songs of popular female singers such as Cai Yilin and Sun Yanzi.
While still a 16-year old student at the Number 4 High School in Ningxiang County, Huang participated in the 2005 Super Girl singing contest. She won the second of the qualifying competitions in Changsha, then finished sixth in the overall competition. In 2007, Huang went to Tokyo and Singapore to study music.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.yangtse.com/system/2013/08/09/018206597.shtml |title= 选秀十年揭历届超女现状:有人霸气上位有人落寞(组图)|accessdate=2013 |publisher=Yangtse |work= |language=Simplified Chinese[/tpl]
Her first single was "Hudie Quanbian"([tpl]lang|zh-hans|蝴蝶泉边[/tpl]), recorded during the time she was on Super Girl. She joined EE-Media in 2005. Her debut album, titled "Baby" ([tpl]lang|zh-hans|崽崽[/tpl]), was released on 19 December 2006. On July 11, 2008, she joined Linfair Records Ltd.福茂唱片/黃雅莉簡介 Her 2nd album, titled " I shall not fear" ([tpl]lang|zh-hans|雅莉不怕[/tpl]), was released on 19 June 2009. Her 3rd album, titled "Former girlfriend" ([tpl]lang|zh-hans|前任女友[/tpl]), was released on 20 December 2010. Her 4th album, titled "Annual ring" ([tpl]lang|zh-hans|年轮[/tpl]), was released on 26 October 2012.
In 2006, she appeared in the television series "Meili Fenbei" ([tpl]lang|zh-hans|美丽分贝[/tpl]). In 2007, Her film debut was in "Hard to Get Notre Dame Tickets" ([tpl]lang|zh-hans|一票难求[/tpl]). In that year, she appeared in the television series "The guy was gorgeous" ([tpl]lang|zh-hans|那小子真帅[/tpl]).
