[[Oreolyce quadriplaga]]

CATEGORIES: Polyommatinae, Butterflies of India

The Naga Hedge Blue (Oreolyce quadriplaga)Card for  quadriplaga in LepIndex. Accessed 14 October 2006. is a small butterfly found in IndiaEvans,W.H.(1932) The Identification of Indian Butterflies, ser no H21.4, pp 221-226 that belongs to the Lycaenids or Blues family.

==Taxonomy==

The butterfly was earlier known as Lycaenopsis quadriplaga (Tytler).

==Range==

It is found in Nagaland in India.
