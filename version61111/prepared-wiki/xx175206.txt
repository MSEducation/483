[[KAMB (FM)]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in California, Christian contemporary radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 1967

KAMB (101.5 FM) is an American radio station broadcasting a Contemporary Christian format. Licensed to Merced, California, United States, KAMB is an independent, commercial-free, listener-supported station.

==Translators==

In addition to the main station, KAMB is relayed by broadcast translator K296FO in Sonora, California.
