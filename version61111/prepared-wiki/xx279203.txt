[[Tazeh Kand, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Tazeh Kand ([tpl]lang-fa|تازه كند[/tpl], also Romanized as Tāzeh Kand)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3804293|Tazeh Kand[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 23, in 6 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
