[[Alternating Currents (collection)]]

CATEGORIES: 1956 short story collections, Short story collections by Frederik Pohl, Ballantine Books books

Alternating Currents is a collection of science fiction stories by Frederik Pohl first published by Ballantine Books in 1956 (ISBN #B000BH7ANM)

==Contents==

"The Children of Night" - Galaxy Science Fiction Oct. '64 - replaces "Happy Birthday, Dear Jesus" in UK editions. Penguin Books, (ISBN 0-14-002452-2)

==Reception==

Anthony Boucher found the stories to be "largely slight and hasty in view of Pohl's best work . . . but still reveal much wit and imagination.""Recommended Reading," F&SF, July 1956, p.94.
