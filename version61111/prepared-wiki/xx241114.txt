[[155th Indian Infantry Brigade]]

CATEGORIES: Brigades of India in World War II, British Indian Army brigades

The 115th Indian Infantry Brigade was an Infantry formation of the Indian Army during World War II.
It was formed by the conversion of the Risalpur Training Brigade in April 1944, and it provided training for units destined for the western theatres of war.[tpl]cite web|accessdate=2009-10-19|publisher=Order of Battle|title=115 Brigade|url=http://www.ordersofbattle.com/UnitData.aspx?UniX=63301&Tab=Uhi[/tpl]
