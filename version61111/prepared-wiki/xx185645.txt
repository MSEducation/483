[[Gmina Bierawa]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Opole Voivodeship, Kędzierzyn-Koźle County

Gmina Bierawa, German Gemeinde Birawa is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Kędzierzyn-Koźle County, Opole Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Its seat is the village of Bierawa, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|8|km|mi|0[/tpl] south-east of Kędzierzyn-Koźle and [tpl]convert|48|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-east of the regional capital Opole.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|119.24|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 8,046. Since 2007 the commune, like much of the area, is officially bilingual in German and Polish.
