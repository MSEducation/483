[[Peta Stephens]]

CATEGORIES: 1978 births, Living people, Australian netball players, Sportswomen from Brisbane, People educated at Brisbane State High School

Peta Stephens (born 15 April 1978 in Brisbane, Australia) is an Australian netball player. Stephens played for the Queensland Firebirds in the Commonwealth Bank Trophy (CBT) and in the ANZ Championship; in 2004, she played for the Melbourne Phoenix and in New Zealand for the Canterbury Flames.2008/09 Queensland Firebirds profile. Retrieved 15 July 2008.
