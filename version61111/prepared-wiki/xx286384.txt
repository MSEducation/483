[[Baghlujeh Bayat]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Baghlujeh Bayat ([tpl]lang-fa|باغلوجه بيات[/tpl], also Romanized as Bāghlūjeh Bayāt; also known as Bāghlūjeh, Bāghlūjeh-ye Mehtar, Bakildzhar, and Baqiljār)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3766572|Baghlujeh Bayat[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Bala Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 89, in 22 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
