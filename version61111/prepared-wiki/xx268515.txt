[[Frans Alfred Meeng]]

CATEGORIES: 1910 births, 1944 deaths, Indonesian footballers, Indonesia international footballers, Association football midfielders, 1938 FIFA World Cup players

Frans Alfred Meeng (18 January 1910 – 18 September 1944) was an Indonesian football midfielder who played for the Dutch East Indies in the 1938 FIFA World Cup.Copa do Mundo da FIFA França 1938 He also played for SVVB Batavia.
