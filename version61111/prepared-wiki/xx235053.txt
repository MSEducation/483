[[1996 Skoda Czech Open]]

CATEGORIES: 1996 ATP Tour, Prague Open (tennis), 1996 in Czech sport

The 1996 Skoda Czech Open was a men's tennis tournament played on outdoor clay courts at the I. Czech Lawn Tennis Club in Prague in the Czech Republic and was part of the World Series of the 1996 ATP Tour. It was the tenth edition of the tournament and ran from April 29 through May 5, 1996.
