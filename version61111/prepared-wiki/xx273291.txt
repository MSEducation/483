Jayson Musson (Jayson Scott Musson) is an artist, currently based in Brooklyn, NY.[tpl]cite web|title=Art In The Age of Mechanical Reproduction; Artists; Jayson Musson|url=http://www.artintheage.com/artists/jayson-musson/|work=http://www.artintheage.com|accessdate=25 September 2011[/tpl]  He was born in Bronx, NY.

==Education==

Musson earned a BFA in photography from the University of the Arts in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. In 2011, he completed an MFA in painting from the University of Pennsylvania.

==Music==

Musson is an original member of the Philadelphian rap group Plastic Little, formed during the summer of 2001. Along with Plastic Little, Musson performed with British producer Mark Ronson as well as collaborated with a number of notable Philadelphian acts including Spank Rock and Amanda Blank.[tpl]cite web|last=Pytlik|first=Mark|title=Interviews Diplo|url=http://pitchfork.com/features/interviews/6006-diplo/|work=http://pitchfork.com|accessdate=25 September 2011[/tpl]

==Video Performance==

In 2010, Musson created the character of Hennessy Youngman, which subsequently became an Internet phenomenon through the popularity of the "Art Thoughtz" video series.[tpl]cite web|last=Boucher|first=Brian|title=Ali G With an MFA: Q+A With Hennessy Youngman|url=http://www.artinamericamagazine.com/news-opinion/conversations/2011-03-24/hennessey-youngman-youtube/|work=http://www.artinamericamagazine.com|accessdate=25 September 2011[/tpl] Musson publicly divides his persona with the character's by referring to Youngman as his "cousin."[tpl]cite web|title=Tuesday Evenings at the Modern Fall 2011 Schedule; October 4; Jayson Musson|url=http://themodern.org/lectures.html|work=http://themodern.org/|accessdate=25 September 2011|year=2011[/tpl] Musson's "ART THOUGHTZ" series is distributed internationally by  Electronic Arts Intermix.[tpl]cite web|title=Jayson Scott Musson Biography on Electronic Arts Intermix; Jayson Musson|url=http://www.eai.org/artistTitles.htm?id=13116|work=http://www.eai.org/artistTitles.htm?id=13116|accessdate=13 July 2012|year=2012[/tpl]
