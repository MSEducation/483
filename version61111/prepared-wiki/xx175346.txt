[[KHCH]]

CATEGORIES: Asian-American culture in Texas, Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Texas, Spanish-language radio stations in Texas

KHCH is an Ethnic Christian radio station licensed to Huntsville, Texas, broadcasting on 1410 kHz AM. KHCH broadcasts in the languages of Spanish, Vietnamese, Cantonese, and Mandarin, and is owned by Houston Christian Broadcasters, Inc.KHCH fcc.gov. Accessed August 23, 2012
KHCH is also heard on 98.7 FM.
