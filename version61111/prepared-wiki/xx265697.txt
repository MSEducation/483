[[Yuhikaku Publishing]]

CATEGORIES: Publishing companies of Japan, Publishing companies established in 1877, Companies based in Tokyo, Media in Tokyo

[tpl]Nihongo|Yuhikaku Publishing Co., Ltd.|株式会社 有斐閣|Kabushiki-gaisha Yūhikaku[/tpl] is a Japanese publishing company known as a specialist legal publisher.[tpl]cite web|last=Ibusuki|first=Makoto|title=Japanese Law via the Internet|url=http://www.nyulawglobal.org/Globalex/Japan1.htm|work=Globalex|publisher=New York University School of Law|accessdate=22 September 2011|date=February 2009[/tpl] It has been run by the Egusa family since its foundation in 1877, headquartered in Kanda-Jinbōchō, Tokyo.[tpl]cite web|title=有斐閣略年譜|url=http://www.yuhikaku.co.jp/static_files/ayumi.pdf|publisher=Yuhikaku Publishing|accessdate=22 September 2011|language=Japanese[/tpl]
