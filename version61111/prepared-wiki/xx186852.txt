[[2734 Hašek]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Asteroids named for people, Astronomical objects discovered in 1976

2734 Hašek is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 2053.7166710 days (5.62 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=2734 |  title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-04-28 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] It is named after Jaroslav Hašek
The asteroid was discovered on April 1, 1976.
