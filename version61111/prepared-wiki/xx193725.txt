[[Resurs Bank]]

CATEGORIES: Banks of Sweden, Banks established in 2001, 2001 establishments in Sweden

Resurs Bank is a Swedish bank founded in October 2001. Resurs Bank originates from Resurs Finans that has been active in Sweden since 1984. Resurs Bank is specialized in consumer credits, unsecure loans and issues a Mastercard named Supreme Card.
The bank has 225 employees at the offices in Helsingborg and Stockholm. The company is also represented in Denmark, Finland and Norway.
