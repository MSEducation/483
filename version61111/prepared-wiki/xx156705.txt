[[Surxondaryo River]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of Uzbekistan

Surxondaryo River, also Surkhandarya River, Surxon River, Surkhan River, Surkhan Darya  (darya means "river") is a river in  Uzbekistan, a right tributary of the Amu Darya. Length: 175 km., basin: 13,500 sw. km. The Karatag River also flows into this river.
It gives the name to the Surxondaryo Province
