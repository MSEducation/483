[[Cobanus]]

CATEGORIES: Salticidae

Cobanus is a genus of the spider family Salticidae (jumping spiders).

==Distribution==

Most of the 16 described species are endemic to Panama, with others ranging from Mexico to Venezuela, and C. cambridgei found on Hispaniola.
Petrunkevitch described a species from Borneo in 1914 (C. beebei). As all other species are distributed in Central America, this placement seems unlikely.Murphy & Murphy 2000: 273
