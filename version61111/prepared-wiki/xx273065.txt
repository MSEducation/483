[[Daniel Padilla]]

CATEGORIES: 1995 births, Living people, People from Metro Manila, Filipino actors, Filipino male child actors, Filipino male singers, Star Magic, Padilla family

Daniel John Ford Padilla (born April 26, 1995), popularly known as Daniel Padilla, is a Filipino actor and recording artist/bassist of the band Parking 5. Padilla is a talent of ABS-CBN and Star Magic and was chosen as one of the Original Pilipino Music Junior Ambassadors in promoting local music to the Filipino youth. He is the nephew of action star Robin Padilla and the son of Rommel Padilla and Karla Estrada .

===2012–present: Debut as a primetime actor===

After two consecutive Sunday youth-oriented shows, Padilla finally landed a role in a primetime television series in Princess And I. He played Gerald Antonio "Gino" dela Rosa or Dasho Yuan Rinpoche, a character whom he describes as "a swaggering rich kid who will change and sacrifice a lot for the sake of love".[tpl]cite web|last=Santos|first=Rhea|title=Daniel Padilla denies that he and Kathryn Bernardo are already mag-'M.U.'|url=http://www.push.com.ph/features/8089/daniel-padilla-denies-that-he-and-kathryn-bernardo-are-already-mag-mu/|date=July 1, 2012[/tpl] The romantic-comedy royalty-themed series did well in the ratings, having reached the 40% mark and placed second behind Walang Hanggan.[tpl]cite web|last=Donida|first=Krissa|title=Khalil Ramos admits feeling more pressure because of Princess and I’s high ratings|url=http://www.push.com.ph/features/8098/khalil-ramos-admits-feeling-more-pressure-because-of-princess-and-is-high-ratings/|date=July 4, 2012[/tpl]

===2012-present: Music industry===

On May 28, 2012, Padilla released his debut self-titled Album, Daniel Padilla. The album has six tracks with the carrier single "Hinahanap-hanap Kita", which is also featured in his primetime show Princess And I. According to Star Records producer Jonathan Manalo, Padilla's debut album sold more than 10,000 copies in less than a week.[tpl]cite web|title=Daniel Padilla's Debut Album Turns Gold in Less Than A Week!|url=http://bidakapamilya.blogspot.com/2012/06/daniel-padillas-debut-album-turns-gold.html|date=June 18, 2012[/tpl] On June 17 of the same year, Padilla was given a Gold Record Award for the sales of his debut album and later on sold more than 30,000+ copies and reached Double Platinum.[tpl]cite web|title=Daniel Padilla's debut album reaches gold|url=http://www.abs-cbnnews.com/entertainment/06/20/12/daniel-padillas-debut-album-reaches-gold|publisher=ABS-CBNnews.com|date=June 20, 2012[/tpl] On April 14, 2014, Star Records will release Daniel Padilla's new album. I Heart You is one of the singles in the said album.
