[[Kantipur English High School]]

CATEGORIES: Schools in Nepal, Educational institutions established in 1983, 1983 establishments in Nepal

Kantipur English High School is an English-language high school in Nepal that was established in 1983. It is situated in the province of Kathmandu.
The enrollment is just below 1,000. The school motto is "Love, Learn & Serve".[tpl]Citation needed|date=November 2010[/tpl]
Kantipur School's principal is Mrs. Subadra Shresta. [tpl]Coord missing|Nepal[/tpl]
