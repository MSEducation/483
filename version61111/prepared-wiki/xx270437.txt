[[Trams in Ulm]]

CATEGORIES: Tram transport in Germany, Transport in Baden-Württemberg, Ulm

The Ulm tramway network ([tpl]lang-de|Straßenbahnnetz Ulm[/tpl]) is a network of tramways forming part of the public transport system in Ulm, a city in the federal state of Baden-Württemberg, Germany.
Opened in 1897, the network is currently operated by Stadtwerken Ulm/Neu-Ulm GmbH (SWU).
