Hudson Tonga'uiha born as Kosilio Tonga'uiha (born in Tofua, 16 November 1983)Hudson Tonga'uiha player profile Scrum.com is a Tongan rugby union footballer. Tonga'uiha plays at centre or fullback and currently plays for London Welsh having previously played for the Doncaster Knights.
In 2006 he represented the Tonga national rugby league team at the 2006 Pacific Cup.Pacific Cup Tongan National Rugby League
He was a member of Tonga squad at the 2007 Rugby World Cup, but he only played at the 20-36 loss to England.
