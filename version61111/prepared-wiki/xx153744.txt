[[Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Yaoundé]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Cameroon, Yaoundé, Religious organizations established in 1890, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 19th century

The Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Yaoundé ([tpl]lang-la|Yaunden(sis)[/tpl]) is the Metropolitan See for the Ecclesiastical province of Yaoundé in Cameroon.

==History==

==Special churches==

The seat of the archbishop is the Cathédrale Notre Dame des Victoires in Yaoundé.  There is also a Minor Basilica at Mary Queen of the Apostles Basilica in Yaoundé.
