Diego Hofland (born September 21, 1990 in Rotterdam, Netherlands) is a professional ice hockey forward. From 2009 through 2013 he played for Düsseldorfer EG, formerly known as DEG Metro Stars, of the Deutsche Eishockey Liga (German Ice Hockey League).[tpl]DEL profile|3560889|Diego Hofland[/tpl] He also played for EV Duisburg[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.ev-duisburg.de/die-fuechse/das-team/1531.html | title = Diego Hofland # 6 | publisher = EV Duisburg | accessdate = 2013-11-20[/tpl] and currently plays for the Iserlohn Roosters.
