[[MTV Pilipinas for Favorite Hip-Hop and R&B Video]]

CATEGORIES: MTV Pilipinas Music Awards

The following is a list of MTV Pilipinas winners for Favorite Hip-Hop and R&B Video. The said category is one of the seven new categories added in 2006 along with Favorite Pop Video, Favorite Rock Video, Best Animated Video, Best Cinematography in a Video, Best Editing in a Video and Best Production Design in a Video.
