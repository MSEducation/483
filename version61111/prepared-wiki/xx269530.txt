[[Osaka Municipal College of Design]]

CATEGORIES: Education in Osaka, Art schools in Japan, Universities and colleges in Osaka Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Osaka Municipal College of Design|大阪市立デザイン教育研究所|Ōsaka Shiritsu Dezain Kyōiku Kenkyūsho[/tpl] is a public art college run by the city of Osaka. It is called OMCD for short. OMCD was founded in 1988 as an institution of continuous vocational education and is a sister institution to the Osaka City Kogei High School.
