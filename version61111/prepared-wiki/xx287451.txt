[[Fort Bliss (film)]]

CATEGORIES: Upcoming films, Films shot in Los Angeles, California, Films shot in El Paso, Texas, Films shot in New Mexico, Films about Afghanistan, American films, 2010s drama films, American drama films

Fort Bliss is an upcoming American drama film directed and written by Claudia Myers, a military based set on Fort Bliss. The film stars Ron Livingston, Michelle Monaghan, Pablo Schreiber, Emmanuelle Chriqui and Dash Mihok.

==Cast==

==Production==

In October 2011 Michelle Monaghan was added to the cast to play lead in the upcoming drama Fort Bliss about the returning of troops from war in Afghanistan. Filming is scheduled to begin in June 2012 in New Mexico.[tpl]cite news|title=Monaghan Recruited By Fort Bliss|url=http://www.empireonline.com/news/story.asp?NID=32302|accessdate=13 August 2013|newspaper=empireonline.com|date=24 October 2011[/tpl] Later on April 5, 2012 Ron Livingston also signed in to join the cast of the film to star opposite to Monaghan.[tpl]cite news|title=Ron Livingston in indie ‘Bliss’|url=http://variety.com/2012/film/news/ron-livingston-in-indie-bliss-1118052353/|accessdate=13 August 2013|newspaper=variety.com|date=5 April 2012[/tpl] In August 2012 filming was scheduled to begin in September in Los Angeles, producer Cody Gallo said they will later move to Fort Bliss.[tpl]cite news|title=Film will feature Fort Bliss, Michelle Monaghan as medic|url=http://www.elpasotimes.com/news/ci_21407147/film-will-feature-fort-bliss-michelle-monaghan-medic|accessdate=13 August 2013|newspaper=elpasotimes.com|date=27 August 2012[/tpl]

===Filming===

The filming of the drama Fort Bliss began in September 2012 in Los Angeles, later filming moved at Fort Bliss in El Paso, Texas.[tpl]cite news|title=Michelle Monaghan Filming Movie at Fort Bliss|url=http://www.nbcdfw.com/entertainment/movies/Michelle-Monaghan-Filming-Movie-at-Fort-Bliss-173127131.html|accessdate=13 August 2013|newspaper=nbcdfw.com|date=9 October 2012[/tpl]
