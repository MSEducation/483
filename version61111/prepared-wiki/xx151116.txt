[[Zumbro Parkway Bridge]]

CATEGORIES: Arch bridges, Bridges completed in 1937, Bridges on the National Register of Historic Places in Minnesota, Buildings and structures in Wabasha County, Minnesota, Road bridges in Minnesota, Transportation in Wabasha County, Minnesota, Works Progress Administration in Minnesota, Zumbro River

The Zumbro Parkway Bridge is a steel arch bridge located in Zumbro Falls, Minnesota. The bridge was built in 1937 by the engineer J.M. Evans and is [tpl]convert|59|ft|m[/tpl] long and [tpl]convert|32.9|ft|m[/tpl] wide.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.mnhs.org/places/nationalregister/bridges/nrwbzpb/nrwbzum.html |title=Minnesota's Historic Bridges - Zumbro Parkway Bridge | work=Minnesota Historical Society website |accessdate=2007-05-13[/tpl] It allows County 68 to cross a portion of the Zumbro River. The bridge is on the National Register of Historic Places (#89001824), and has been listed since 1989.
