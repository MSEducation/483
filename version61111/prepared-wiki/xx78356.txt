[[Okonek]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Greater Poland Voivodeship, Złotów County

Okonek [tpl]IPAc-pl|o|'|k|o|n|e|k[/tpl] ([tpl]lang-de|Ratzebuhr[/tpl]) is a town in Poland, in Greater Poland Voivodeship, with approximately 4,200 inhabitants. Town and commune in Złotów County. Chartered as a town in 1754.
