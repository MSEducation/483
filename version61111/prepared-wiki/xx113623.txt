[[Vadavannur]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Palakkad district, Gram Panchayats in Palakkad district

Vadavannur is a village and gram panchayat in the Palakkad District of Kerala, India.[tpl]cite web |title=Reports of National Panchayat Directory |publisher=Ministry of Panchayati Raj |url=http://panchayatdirectory.gov.in/adminreps/viewGPmapcvills.asp?gpcode=224698&rlbtype=V |accessdate=31 December 2013[/tpl]

==Demographics==

[tpl]As of|2001[/tpl] India census, Vadavannur had a population of 16,378, comprising 8,021 males and 8,357 females.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.censusindia.gov.in/Census_Data_2001/Village_Directory/Population_data/Population_5000_and_Above.aspx|title=Census of India : Villages with population 5000 & above|first=Registrar General & Census Commissioner, India|accessdate=2008-12-10[/tpl]
