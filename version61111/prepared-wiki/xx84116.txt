[[Takamori, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Takamori|高森町|Takamori-machi[/tpl] is a town located in Shimoina District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the town has an estimated population of 12,838 and a density of 283.65 persons per km². The total area is 45.26 km².
