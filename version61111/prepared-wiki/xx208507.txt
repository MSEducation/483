[[Netani Suka]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Fijian rugby league players, Fiji national rugby league team players, Gateshead Thunder players, Wentworthville Magpies players

Netani Suka (born in Fiji) is a rugby league player who currently plays for the Wentworthville Magpies in the Bundaberg Red Cup. He plays in the second-row. He is a Fijian international.[tpl]Citation needed|date=August 2009[/tpl] He has previously played Premier League for the Wests Tigers and Penrith Panthers and for the Gateshead Thunder in National League Two..
He has been named in the Fiji training squad for the 2008 Rugby League World Cup.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fijitimes.com/story.aspx?id=96697|title=Bati pencil in new Newcastle pace ace Uate|publisher=Fiji Times Online|date=2008-08-01|accessdate=2008-08-08[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.rlwc08.com/news/news/archive/2008/08/06/fijian-preliminary-squad.aspx|title=Fijian preliminary squad|publisher=RLWC08.com|date=2008-08-06|accessdate=2008-08-02[/tpl]
