[[Bryan Foods]]

CATEGORIES: Companies established in 1936, Food production companies of the United States, Companies based in Mississippi, Companies based in Cincinnati, Ohio, Companies based in DuPage County, Illinois, Sara Lee Corporation, Downers Grove, Illinois, Clay County, Mississippi

Bryan Foods is an American meat company founded in 1936 by John H. Bryan, Sr and W.B. Bryan in West Point, Mississippi.  

==Information==

The company was acquired in 1968 by Sara Lee Corporation, now Hillshire Brands. Its headquarters was originally in West Point, and then Cincinnati, Ohio before moving to Downers Grove, Illinois. Bryan Foods products are primarily available for sale in the Southern United States.[tpl]cite web|title=Our Heritage|url=http://www.bryanfoods.com/heritage.php|publisher=Bryan Foods|accessdate=16 February 2013[/tpl]
