[[Aonbaw]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Naga Self-Administered Zone, Lahe Township

 
Aonbaw is a village in Lahe Township, Naga Self-Administered Zone, in the Sagaing Region of northwestern Burma. It is located in the Naga Hills, to the north of Lahe.[tpl]cite map|title=Google Maps|publisher=Google|accessdate=2 January 2010[/tpl][tpl]cite map|title=Bing Maps|publisher= Microsoft and Harris Corporation Earthstar Geographics LLC|accessdate=2 January 2010[/tpl]Human sacrifice and headhunting has been documented in the Lahe Township.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1G1-68273454.html|title=Human Sacrifice and Slavery in the "Unadministered" Areas of Upper Burma During the Colonial Era.|author=Menas, Gordon P.|publisher=Journal of Social Issues in Southeast Asia |date=October 1, 2000|accessdate=2 January 2010[/tpl]
