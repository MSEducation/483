[[June Kirby]]

CATEGORIES: American film actresses, Living people, 1928 births, American musical theatre actresses

June Kirby is an American actress and costume designer. She was a student at the Kingston Art School (now Kingston University).[tpl]cn|date=October 2012[/tpl] She was in at least four movies and did wardrobe for many more. She was in Famous Models Magazine dated June 1950, as well as many others.  She had a stint as a Goldwyn Girl and appeared uncredited as one of the Hot Box Girls in the film version of Guys and Dolls (1955).[tpl]cite book|last=Kurtti|first=Jeff|title=The Great Hollywood Musical Trivia Book|year=1996|publisher=Applause Books|location=New York|isbn=1-55783-222-6|page=41[/tpl] 
She also performed on Broadway in the musicals Gentlemen Prefer Blondes and As the Girls Go. She also went on to date men such as Howard Hughes and Mickey Rooney.[tpl]Citation needed|date=October 2012[/tpl]
Her whereabouts are currently unknown; however, she is said to be retired.
