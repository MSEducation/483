[[Story Seat]]

CATEGORIES: CBC network shows, 1962 Canadian television series debuts, 1962 Canadian television series endings, Canadian children's television series

Story Seat was a Canadian children's television series which aired on CBC Television in 1962.

==Premise==

Hosts Lillian Carlson and Norman Young recited and performed stories in this series geared towards young children.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.broadcasting-history.ca/programming/television/programming_popup.php?id=1009 | first=John | last=Corcelli | date=May 2005 | title=Story Seat | publisher=Canadian Communications Foundation | accessdate=7 May 2010 [/tpl]

==Scheduling==

This 15-minute series was broadcast Thursdays at 4:15 p.m. (Eastern time) from 11 January to 28 June 1962.
