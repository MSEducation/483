==Characteristics==

Xenon has atomic number 54; that is, its nucleus contains 54 protons. At standard temperature and pressure, pure xenon gas has a density of 5.761 kg/m3, about 4.5 times the surface density of the Earth's atmosphere, 1.217 kg/m3.[ref]
</ref> As a liquid, xenon has a density of up to 3.100&nbsp;g/mL, with the density maximum occurring at the triple point.<ref name="detectors">
