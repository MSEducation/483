[[Warwick Mountain]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of Alberta, Mountains of Jasper National Park

Warwick Mountain is a mountain in Alberta, Canada. 
It is located south of Warwick Creek in the Athabasca River Valley of Jasper National Park. It stands east of the Chaba Icefield and north of the Columbia Icefield.
The mountain was named in 1919 by Arthur O. Wheeler after Warwick Castle in England when he noted the castellated appearance of the mountain and it being situated just [tpl]convert|8|km|mi|abbr=on[/tpl] north of Mt. King Edward.
