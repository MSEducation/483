[[Jraberd]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Tartar District

Jraberd ([tpl]lang-hy|Ջրաբերդ[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-az|Çiləbörd[/tpl]) is a village in the Tartar Rayon of Azerbaijan.  The village is on the cease-fire line between the armed forces of the self-proclaimed Nagorno-Karabakh Republic and those of Azerbaijan. There have been allegations of cease fire violations in the village's vicinity.DeFacto Armenian News Agency
