[[Wang Chongwei]]

CATEGORIES: 1988 births, Living people, Chinese ice hockey players

Wang Chongwei ([tpl]zh|s=王崇炜|t=王崇煒|p=Wáng Chóngwěi[/tpl]; born December 10, 1988) is a Chinese professional ice hockey forward for the China Dragon of the Asia League Ice Hockey (AHIL).

==Playing career==

Wang Chongwei began his ice hockey career in the youth system of the Qiqihar ice hockey team which in 2007 merged with the Harbin ice hockey team to become the China Sharks. In the 2008-09 season he was called up to the Sharks and gave his professional debut in the Asia League Ice Hockey. In his firs 22 games he stayed pointless and missed the playoffs with his team. For the next season the Sharks were renamed China Dragon.
He represented his country both in the 2007 and 2008 World Junior Ice Hockey Championships as in the 2007 IIHF World U18 Championships.
