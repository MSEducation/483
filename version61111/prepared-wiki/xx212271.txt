Curtiss Patrick (born October 8, 1979) is a retired American professional ice hockey defenseman.

==Career==

Curtiss has appeared in 172 games in the ECHL for the Wheeling Nailers and 11 games in the AHL, ten for the Wilkes-Barre/Scranton Penguins and one for the Springfield Falcons, posting 8 goals, 19 assists, and 516 minutes in penalties. He formerly played in the Elite Ice Hockey League in the United Kingdom for the Basingstoke Bison, in November left the club and moved to Wichita Thunder.

==Family==

He is the great-grandson of Lester Patrick and the grandson of Lynn Patrick. His father Glenn Patrick and uncle Craig Patrick also played in the NHL.  His Great Great Uncle Is Frank Patrick and his Great Uncle is Muzz Patrick, both of whom played or coached in the NHL.
