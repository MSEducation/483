[[British and Irish Hardwoods Improvement Programme]]

CATEGORIES: Environmental organisations based in the United Kingdom, Environmental organisations based in Ireland, 1991 establishments in the United Kingdom, Organizations established in 1991, Forestry in the United Kingdom

The British and Irish Hardwoods Improvement Programme (BIHIP) was established in 1991 with the aim of improving and increasing the stock of hardwood trees in Britain and Ireland.http://www.futuretrees.org/index.php?option=com_k2&view=itemlist&layout=category&task=category&id=30&Itemid=28 It was renamed the Future Trees Trust in 2008.
