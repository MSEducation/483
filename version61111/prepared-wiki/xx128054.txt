==Early life==

Amri was born in Saint-Avold, France to Algerian parents. His family is originally from Laâchache, Tlemcen Province, Algeria.

==Football career==

After leaving FC Metz's junior ranks, he played briefly at amateur club ASC Lascasbas before joining the reserve team of 1. FC Saarbrücken. After one season with the reserve team, he was called up to the senior squad and scored six goals in 25 games. The next summer he signed a four-year contract with then Bundesliga side 1. FSV Mainz 05.
