[[Hylarana moellendorffi]]

CATEGORIES: Hylarana, Amphibians of the Philippines, Endemic fauna of the Philippines

Hylarana moellendorffi (Culion frog) is a species of true frog in the genus Hylarana.[tpl]cite web |url=http://research.amnh.org/vz/herpetology/amphibia/?action=names&taxon=Hylarana+moellendorffi |title= Hylarana moellendorffi (Boettger, 1893)|author=Frost, Darrel R. |date=2013 |work=Amphibian Species of the World 5.6, an Online Reference |publisher=American Museum of Natural History |accessdate=30 May 2013[/tpl] It is endemic to the Palawan islands of the Philippines. It inhabits streams and rivers in lower montane and lowland forests. It is threatened by habitat loss.
