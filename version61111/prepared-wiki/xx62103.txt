[[Steve Fisk]]

CATEGORIES: American rock musicians, American record producers, K Records artists, Sub Pop artists, Musicians from Washington (state), Living people, Pigeonhed members

Steve Fisk is a Washington-based audio engineer, record producer and musician.
Fisk joined the instrumental rock band Pell Mell in 1982.Strong, Martin C. The Great Indie Discography, 2nd Edition. Edinburgh: Canongate, 2003. With vocalist Shawn Smith, he formed Pigeonhed, which released its first album in 1993.
