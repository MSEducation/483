[[Jasazrin Jamaluddin]]

CATEGORIES: 1986 births, Living people, Malaysian footballers, Johor, Association football midfielders

Jasazrin Bin Jamaludin (born April 3, 1986), is a Malaysian footballer who plays as a midfielder for Johor Darul Takzim FC in the Malaysia Super League.He also study at English College Johore Bahru.
