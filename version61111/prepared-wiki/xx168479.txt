[[Obrad Belošević]]

CATEGORIES: 1928 births, 1986 deaths, People from Leskovac, FIBA Hall of Fame inductees, Basketball in Serbia

Obrad Belošević (born April 28, 1928 in Leskovac, Serbia; died January 20, 1986 in Belgrade, Serbia) was a Serbian basketball referee. He refereed over 300 games of the Yugoslav basketball league from 1951-1976. Other notable events he refereed in include 1968 Olympics, 1970 World Championship, 1974 World Championship, two European Champions Cup final games (in 1969 and 1970) and a Korać Cup final. He was enshrined in the FIBA Hall of Fame in 2007.
