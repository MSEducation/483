[[The Clown (Conway Twitty song)]]

CATEGORIES: 1982 singles, Conway Twitty songs, Billboard Hot Country Songs number-one singles, Songs written by Wayne Carson, Song recordings produced by Jimmy Bowen, Elektra Records singles

"The Clown" is a song written by Wayne Carson, Brenda Barnett, Charlie Chalmers and Sandra Rhodes, and recorded by American country music artist Conway Twitty.  It was released in December 1981 as the first single from the album Southern Comfort.  "The Clown" was Conway Twitty's 28th number one the country chart.
