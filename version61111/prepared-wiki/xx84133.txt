[[Ōshika, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Ōshika|大鹿村|Ōshika-mura[/tpl] is a village located in Shimoina District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the village has an estimated population of 1,432 and a density of 5.77 people per km². The total area is 248.35 km².
The rural Kabuki, Ōshika Kabuki, is popular in Japan.
