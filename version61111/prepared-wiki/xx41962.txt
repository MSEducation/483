[[1998 in film]]

CATEGORIES: Years in film, 1998 in film

The year 1998 in film involved many significant films including; Shakespeare in Love—which won the Academy Award for Best Picture—Saving Private Ryan, American History X, The Truman Show, Primary Colors, Rushmore, Rush Hour, There's Something About Mary, The Big Lebowski, and Terrence Malik's directorial return in The Thin Red Line. Animated films from this year include; A Bug's Life, Antz, Mulan, and The Prince of Egypt.
