[[Shoulder Lean]]

CATEGORIES: 2006 singles, Singles certified double platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America, Billboard Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs number-one singles, Billboard Rap Songs number-one singles, Debut singles, Grand Hustle Records singles, Number-one debut singles, Songs written by T.I., T.I. songs, Young Dro songs, Atlantic Records singles

"Shoulder Lean" is a song by American hip hop recording artist Young Dro, released July 4, 2006 as his debut single and the lead single from his debut studio album Best Thang Smokin'.  The song features fellow American rapper and his Grand Hustle label boss T.I. on the hook. The production was handled by Grand Hustle in-house producer Cordale "Lil C" Quinn. The song was a massive hit in the United States, reaching the Top 10 on the Billboard Hot 100 and #1 on the Hot R&B/Hip Hop Songs chart. This was Young Dro's first and only Top 10 single and T.I.'s fourth Top 10 single. The song received heavy airplay and was certified 2x Platinum by RIAA.
A freestyle over the song's production, entitled "Live from the 504" by Lil Wayne, is featured on his mixtape Da Drought 3 (2007).

==Music video==

The music video for "Shoulder Lean" makes references to the respective video of the Dr. Dre and Snoop Dogg song "Nuthin But A "G" Thang". The video includes cameo appearances from DJ Drama, DJ Khaled, Slim Thug, Big Gipp of Goodie Mob and Parlae of Dem Franchize Boyz.
