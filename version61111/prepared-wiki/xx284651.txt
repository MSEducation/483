[[Kamil Sylwestrzak]]

CATEGORIES: Polish footballers, Korona Kielce players, Chojniczanka Chojnice players, Chrobry Głogów players, 1988 births, Living people, Ekstraklasa players, Association football defenders

 Kamil Sylwestrzak (born 16 July 1988) is a Polish professional footballer who plays as a right-back for Korona Kielce.
