[[Edward Countryman]]

CATEGORIES: Manhattan College alumni, Cornell University alumni, Yale University faculty, University of Canterbury faculty, Southern Methodist University faculty, Academics of the University of Warwick, Academics of the University of Cambridge, Living people, American historians

Edward Countryman is an American historian.

==Life==

He graduated from Manhattan College, and from Cornell University with an MA, and a Ph.D. in 1971.
He taught at Yale University, University of Canterbury, University of Warwick, University of Cambridge.   
He teaches at Southern Methodist University.http://smu.edu/history/faculty/countryman.shtml
