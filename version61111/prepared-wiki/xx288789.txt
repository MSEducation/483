[[Parchin, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Parchin ([tpl]lang-fa|پرچين[/tpl], also Romanized as Parchīn; also known as Barchīn)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3077848|Parchin[/tpl] is a village in Howmeh Rural District, in the Central District of Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 834, in 158 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
