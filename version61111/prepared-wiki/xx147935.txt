[[Podlipki]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Sokółka County

Podlipki [tpl]IPAc-pl|p|o|d|'|l|i|p|k|i[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Krynki, within Sokółka County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland, close to the border with Belarus.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) – TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|11|km|mi|0[/tpl] south-west of Krynki, [tpl]convert|24|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-east of Sokółka, and [tpl]convert|35|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] east of the regional capital Białystok.
The village has a population of 40.
