[[John Wirth (television producer)]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, American television producers, American television writers, Date of birth missing (living people)

John Wirth is a television showrunner, producer, and writer. He is currently the showrunner and executive producer for the American Western series Hell on Wheels.
His previous producer and writer credits include such series as The Cape (2011), the V remake (2009–2011), Terminator: The Sarah Connor Chronicles (2008–2009), Love Monkey (2006), Ghost Whisperer (2005–2010), Nash Bridges (1996–2001), and Remington Steele (1982–1987).[tpl]cite web |last=Goldberg |first=Lesley |date=December 12, 2012 |url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/hell-wheels-season-3-john-wirth-401266 |title='Hell on Wheels' Season 3 a Go With New Showrunner John Wirth |work=The Hollywood Reporter |accessdate=August 12, 2013[/tpl]
