[[Utsab]]

CATEGORIES: 2000 films, Bengali-language films, Indian films, Indian art films, Films whose director won the Best Director National Film Award

Utsab (pronounced: Utshob) (The Festival) (2000) is an Indian Bengali feature film directed by Rituparno Ghosh.

==Plot==

This movie is a family drama which is portrayed on a background of Durgapuja, West Bengal's biggest "Utshob" (Festival). The story is about a cultured Bengali family, different members of which have gathered in native house on the occasion of Durgapuja. This movie remained instrumental in showing different common and complex problems being faced by a middle-class family.
