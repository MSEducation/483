Christian Larrivée (born August 25, 1982 in Gaspé, Quebec, Canada) is a professional Canadian ice hockey player.
He played for Storhamar Dragons in the GET-ligaen in Norway during the 2006-07 season where he was named Most Valuable Player in the playoffs. He scored a record 32 points in 17 playoff games.[ref]

 [tpl]Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot[/tpl]</ref>
The two following seasons he spent with Rødovre Mighty Bulls in Denmark.
He was drafted by the Montreal Canadiens of the NHL in the fourth round (114th overall) in 2000. He has played for the Canadiens' top affiliate the Hamilton Bulldogs of the AHL and has also played for the Long Beach Ice Dogs.
In 2009/10 he played for the HockeyAllsvenskan team Västerås IK or V.I.K. He signed a two-year contract with the Storhamar Dragons in July 2010. After finishing the contract he signed a four-year extension to stay with Storhamar.
Larrivèe is a huge fan favorite in Hamar, and is arguably the best player who as ever played for Storhamar. Larrivèe picks his game up for big games and has a huge heart for the game.[tpl]citation needed|date=October 2013[/tpl]
