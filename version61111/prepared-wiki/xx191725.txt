[[Virola rufula]]

CATEGORIES: Virola, Medicinal plants of South America

Virola rufula is a species of tree in the Myristicaceae family.
The tree contains alkaloids in its bark, leaves and roots. 5-methoxy-dimethyltryptamine makes up 95% of the alkaloids.www.tryptamines.com[tpl]Verify credibility|date=March 2012[/tpl] There is about 0.190% 5-MeO-DMT in bark, 0.135% 5-MeO-DMT in root, and 0.092% dimethyltryptamine in the leaves.
