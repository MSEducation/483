[[Terpsichore]]

CATEGORIES: Dance in Greek mythology, Muses, Offspring of Zeus

In Greek mythology, Terpsichore ([tpl]IPAc-en|t|ər|p|ˈ|s|ɪ|k|ər|iː[/tpl]; Τερψιχόρη) "delight in dancing" was one of the nine Muses and goddess of dance and chorus.Theoi Project, Greek Mythology, Muses http://www.theoi.com/Ouranios/Mousai.html, Retrieved April 29, 2014 She lends her name to the word "terpsichorean" which means "of or relating to dance". She is usually depicted sitting down, holding a lyre, accompanying the ballerinas' choirs with her music.  Her name comes from the Greek words τέρπω ("delight") and χoρός ("dance").
