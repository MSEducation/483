[[Cult Energy-Vital Water]]

CATEGORIES: UCI Continental Teams (Europe), Sports clubs established in 2000, Cycling teams based in Denmark

Cult Energy-Vital Water ([tpl]UCI code|CUL[/tpl]) is a professional road bicycle racing team licensed in Denmark, that compete in the UCI Continental Circuits. It was established in 2000 under the name of "Cycling Horsens".
