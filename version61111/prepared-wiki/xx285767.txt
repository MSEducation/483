[[Galaxy Supernova]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 singles, Girls' Generation songs, Japanese-language songs, Universal Music Japan singles, S.M. Entertainment singles, 2013 songs

"Galaxy Supernova" is the eighth Japanese single by South Korean girl group Girls' Generation. It was released on September 18, 2013 in Japan.[tpl]cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/jp/album/galaxy-supernova-single/id698387103|title=Galaxy Supernova by Girls' Generation|work=iTunes JP|publisher=Apple|language=Japanese|accessdate=November 6, 2013[/tpl] After being on top of the Tower Record's pre-order chart,[tpl]cite news|title=일본 데뷔 3주년 앞둔 소녀시대, 예약판매차트 1~3위 싹쓸이 건재 과시|url=http://sports.hankooki.com/lpage/music/201308/sp2013081407020495510.htm|accessdate=September 19, 2013|newspaper=Sports Hankooki|date=August 14, 2013|language=Korean[/tpl] the single landed fourth on the Japanese Oricon Daily Singles Chart a day prior to its official release day and topped the chart afterwards.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.allkpop.com/article/2013/09/girls-generations-galaxy-supernova-takes-over-first-on-oricon-singles-chart#axzz2fOLvtNp4 | title=Girl's Generation's 'Galaxy Supernova' takes over first on Oricon Singles Chart | publisher=6Theory Media | work=Allkpop | date=September 19, 2013 | accessdate=September 19, 2013[/tpl]

==Background==

It was announced on August 11, 2013, that Girls' Generation would be releasing their eighth Japanese single, "Galaxy Supernova", on September 18, 2013.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.allkpop.com/article/2013/08/girls-generation-to-release-new-japanese-single-galaxy-supernova-on-september-18th | title=Girls' Generation to release new Japanese single 'Galaxy Supernova' on September 18th | publisher=6Theory Media | work=Allkpop | date=August 11, 2013 | accessdate=August 13, 2013[/tpl] The single was released in two versions: a regular edition and a limited CD+DVD edition. The regular edition contains the title track "Galaxy Supernova" and its the B-Side, "Do the Catwalk". The DVD edition contains the full music video, dance version of the music video and a Behind the Scenes  video of "Galaxy Supernova".[tpl]citation needed|date=November 2013[/tpl]
Girls' Generation's Japanese website announced that "Galaxy Supernova" will be used in an advertisement campaign for Samantha Thavasa Jeans, the very first jeans brand from Samantha Thavasa, a Japanese fashion company. Girls' Generation will be featured in commercials for Samantha Thavasa with possible projects in the future related to the advertisement campaign.[tpl]cite news|title=少女時代・新曲MUSIC VIDEO解禁　サマンサタバサとタイアップ決定！|url=http://www.girls-generation.jp/gg/news/%E5%B0%91%E5%A5%B3%E6%99%82%E4%BB%A3%E3%83%BB%E6%96%B0%E6%9B%B2music-video%E8%A7%A3%E7%A6%81%E3%80%80%E3%82%B5%E3%83%9E%E3%83%B3%E3%82%B5%E3%82%BF%E3%83%90%E3%82%B5%E3%81%A8%E3%82%BF%E3%82%A4%E3%82%A2.html|accessdate=September 5, 2013|newspaper=Girls Generation Official Japanese Website|language=Japanese[/tpl][tpl]cite web | url=http://www.allkpop.com/article/2013/09/girls-generation-are-the-new-muses-for-samantha-thavasa-jeans | title=Girls' Generation are the new muses for 'Samantha Thavasa Jeans' | publisher=6Theory Media | work=Allkpop | date=September 5, 2013 | accessdate=September 5, 2013[/tpl]

==Music video==

The dance version music video for "Galaxy Supernova" was released on September 4, 2013 and the music video was released on September 5, 2013.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.allkpop.com/article/2013/09/girls-generations-galaxy-supernova-pv-released | title=Girls' Generation's 'Galaxy Supernova' PV released | publisher=6Theory Media | work=Allkpop | date=September 4, 2013 | accessdate=September 5, 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web | url=http://www.allkpop.com/article/2013/09/girls-generation-continue-to-treat-fans-with-the-normal-version-of-galaxy-supernova-pv | title=Girls' Generation continue to treat fans with the normal version of 'Galaxy Supernova' PV | publisher=6Theory Media | work=Allkpop | date=September 5, 2013 | accessdate=September 6, 2013[/tpl]
The video begins in silence with the girls lining up in colored denim, which is reminiscent of the music video for "Gee" music video, and also features the girls wearing metallic and futuristic-looking dresses while dancing in a box set with LED lights serving as the background. The group also reuses the "arrow dance" from previous video "Hoot" in the first chorus.[tpl]R|Galaxy[/tpl]
A behind the scenes video was released on September 20, 2013, featuring scenes from the photo shoot for the collection as well as the group practicing their choreography.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.allkpop.com/article/2013/09/girls-generation-bts-samantha-thavasa-jeans#axzz2faWa2jwq| title=Girls' Generation takes you behind the scenes for their Samantha Thavasa jeans endorsement | publisher=6Theory Media | work=Allkpop | date=September 20, 2013 | accessdate=September 22, 2013[/tpl]

==Reception==

Seoulbeats criticized SM's infamous walled setting in the music video, calling it boring. The article also noted the choreography as too tame for the group's standards. However, the review complimented the song's catchiness and cuteness as well as the effort to place extra focus on member Sooyoung.[tpl]cite web | url=http://seoulbeats.com/2013/09/snsd-hits-solar-system-galaxy-supernova/ | title=SNSD Hits Our Solar System With "Galaxy Supernova" | publisher=SeoulBeats | date=September 7, 2013 | accessdate=September 7, 2013[/tpl]

==Commercial performance==

Galaxy Supernova achieved the number one spot on Tower Record’s pre-order charts. The single took the number four spot on its debut in Oricon Daily Single Chart[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.oricon.co.jp/rank/js/d/2013-09-17/ | title=Oricon Daily Single Chart | date=September 17, 2013 | work=Oricon | accessdate=September 18, 2013[/tpl] but rose to the number one spot afterwards.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.oricon.co.jp/rank/js/d/2013-09-18/ | title=Oricon Daily Single Chart | date=September 18, 2013 | work=Oricon | accessdate=September 19, 2013[/tpl] The single managed to sell 14,564 physical copies on its second day achieving first place in the Oricon Daily Singles Chart.
Galaxy Supernova debuted on 38th spot in Japan Hot 100[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.billboard-japan.com/charts/detail?a=hot100 | title=Billboard Japan Hot 100 | publisher=Prometheus Global Media | work=Billboard | accessdate=September 21, 2013[/tpl] and 75th spot on Japan Adult Contemporary Airplay.[tpl]cite web|title=Billboard Japan Adult Contemporary Airplay|url=http://www.billboard-japan.com/charts/detail?a=adult_airplay|publisher=Billboard Japan|accessdate=September 21, 2013[/tpl] It debuted at 5th spot on Recochoku Chaku-Uta-Weekly  6th spot on Recochoku Digital Single-Weekly.[tpl]cite news|title=Recochoku's weekly download charts for 09/11~09/17|url=http://www.tokyohive.com/article/2013/09/recochokus-weekly-download-charts-for-09-1109-17|accessdate=September 24, 2013|newspaper=Tokyo Hive|date=September 19, 2013[/tpl] Galaxy Supernova managed to rank 3rd on the Recochoku Daily Single.[tpl]citation needed|date=November 2013[/tpl] The single sold 50,793 physical copies on its first week.[tpl]cite web|title=Oricon Weekly, 9/16-9/22|url=http://www.oricon.co.jp/rank/js/w/2013-09-30/[/tpl]
