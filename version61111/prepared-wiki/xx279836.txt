[[Atoka, New Mexico]]

CATEGORIES: Census-designated places in New Mexico, Census-designated places in Eddy County, New Mexico

Atoka is a census-designated place in Eddy County, New Mexico, United States. Its population was 1,077 as of the 2010 census.[tpl]GR|8[/tpl] U.S. Route 285 passes through the community.
