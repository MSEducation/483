[[Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Korhogo]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Ivory Coast

The Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Korhogo ([tpl]lang-la|Korhogoën(sis)[/tpl]) is the Metropolitan See for the Ecclesiastical province of Korhogo in Côte d'Ivoire.

==Special churches==
