[[WPFR-FM]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Indiana, Radio stations in Terre Haute, Indiana, Moody Radio affiliate stations

WPFR is a Christian radio station licensed to Clinton, Indiana, broadcasting on 93.7 MHz FM.  The station serves the Terre Haute, Indiana area, and is owned by Word Power, Inc.WKZI fcc.gov. Accessed August 6, 2012
