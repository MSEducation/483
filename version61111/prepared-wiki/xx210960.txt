[[Operation Have Horn]]

CATEGORIES: Military in Massachusetts, Sounding rockets of the United States, Truro, Massachusetts

Operation Have Horn was a project in 1969 by the United States Air Force that involved the launching of Nike Hydac sounding rockets. It occurred at North Truro Air Force Station, located in North Truro, Massachusetts. Upon completion of the operation in 1970, the used devices were returned to the White Sands Missile Range, in New Mexico.Have Horn was support and carried out by the  Missile and Drone Division of the Air Force Missile Development Center, Holloman Air Force Base, New Mexico.astronautix.com: North Truro
