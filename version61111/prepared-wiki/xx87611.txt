[[Jordanville railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1930

Jordanville railway station is located on the Glen Waverley line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the eastern Melbourne suburb of Mount Waverley opening on 5 May 1930.Jordanville Vicsig
At the time the station opened and up to the creation of the City of Monash in 1994, Jordanville was a suburb of Melbourne, before being split after redistribution, into Chadstone and Mount Waverley.
