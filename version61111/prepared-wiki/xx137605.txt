[[Press (song)]]

CATEGORIES: Paul McCartney songs, Songs written by Paul McCartney, 1986 singles, Music published by MPL Music Publishing

"Press" is a 1986 single by Paul McCartney also featured on the album Press to Play. "It's Not True" was featured as the B-side.

==Charts==

Though hitting the Top 30 in both the US (number 21)[tpl]cite web |url=[tpl]Allmusic|class=artist|id=p4865/charts-awards/billboard-singles|pure_url=yes[/tpl] |title=Paul McCartney > Charts & Awards  > Billboard Singles |publisher=allmusic |accessdate=2009-08-30[/tpl] and the UK (#25),[tpl]cite web|title=Official Charts: Paul McCartney|url=http://www.theofficialcharts.com/artist/_/paul%20mccartney/|publisher=The Official UK Charts Company|accessdate=2011-10-13[/tpl] "Press" marked the beginning of a downturn of McCartney's fortunes on the singles charts, as it was the first time since Back to the Egg in 1979 that a lead single from a McCartney album failed to hit the Top 20.

==Release==

Various issues in the UK include two 7" singles ("Press" and "Press-video edit"), a 10" single in a special fold-out sleeve and two 12" singles.  In the UK, a Hugh Padgham mix and edit of "Press" was released on the single, but was quickly withdrawn and replaced with the preferred Bevans/Forward mix.[tpl]cite web|title=Paul McCartney - Press at Discogs|url=http://www.discogs.com/Paul-McCartney-Press/master/141285|publisher=discogs.com|accessdate=25 April 2012[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Press (Hugh Padgham mix ) / It's Not True by Paul McCartney|url=http://rateyourmusic.com/release/single/paul_mccartney/press__hugh_padgham_mix_its_not_true/|publisher=rateyourmusic.com|accessdate=25 April 2012[/tpl]
