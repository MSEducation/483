[[Ron Mitchell (American football)]]

CATEGORIES: Alabama State Hornets football coaches, Kentucky State Thorobreds football coaches

Ron Mitchell was the 17th head college football coach for the Kentucky State University Thorobreds located in Frankfort, Kentucky and he held that position for the 1982 season. His career coaching record at Kentucky State was 2 wins, 9 losses, and 0 ties.  As of the conclusion of the 2007 season, this ranks him 19th at Kentucky State in total wins and 21st at Kentucky State in winning percentage (0.182).
Kentucky State University coaching records  The school's media guide lists a slightly different result, having him coach for merely half of the 1982 season, with a record of 0 wins, 5 losses, and 0 ties (0.000).Kentucky State University Football Media Guide, Coaching Records
