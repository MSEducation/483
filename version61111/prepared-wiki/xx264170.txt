[[Ranch to Market Road 474]]

CATEGORIES: Farm to Market Roads in Texas, Transportation in Kendall County, Texas

Ranch to Market Road 474 (RM 474) is a [tpl]convert|15.764|miles|km|abbr=on[/tpl] ranch to market road in Kendall County, Texas.

==Route description==

RM 474 begins in central Boerne at [tpl]roadlink|state=TX|US|87[/tpl]. The route travels east through Boerne along Blanco St. before curving north along Esser Rd. near Boerne High School.[tpl]Texas Mapbook|373|accessdate=2011-01-05[/tpl] After leaving Boerne, the route travels generally toward the north-northeast. After crossing the Guadalupe River, RM 474 ends at an intersection with RM 473 between Sisterdale and Kendalia.[tpl]Texas Mapbook|402|accessdate=2011-01-05[/tpl]

==History==

RM 474 was designated in 1945, and connected US 87 (the present-day business route) to the community of Kreutzberg near the Guadalupe River. The route was extended north in 1949 to a county road; that road would be designated part of FM (later RM) 473 in 1951.[tpl]TxDOT|RM|473|accessdate=2011-09-23[/tpl]
