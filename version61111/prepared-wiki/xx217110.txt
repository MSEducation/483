[[Əvçədulan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Yardymli District

Əvçədulan (also, Avchadulan) is a village and municipality in the Yardymli Rayon of Azerbaijan.  It has a population of 409.  The municipality consists of the villages of Əvçədulan and Keçələkəran.Belediyye Informasiya Sistemi [tpl]dead link|date=January 2013[/tpl]
