[[Mindil Beach]]

CATEGORIES: Visitor attractions in Darwin, Northern Territory, Beaches of Northern Territory

Mindil Beach is a beach located near the Darwin's central business district. Mindil Beach holds the Mindil Beach Sunset Markets which runs during the Dry season (from May until October) of every year.[tpl]cite web | title = Mindil Beach Sunset Markets | work = Tourism NT | publisher = Travel NT | url = http://www.travelnt.com/en/search/product-detail.aspx?product_id=9001524 | accessdate = 2008-07-27[/tpl]
