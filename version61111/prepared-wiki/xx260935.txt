[[Not Too Young]]

CATEGORIES: 2009 albums, Christian music albums

Not Too Young is the second album released by the Christian punk band, Mission Six.  The album was released on October 27, 2009 on iShine Records and Provident Music Group.Jesusfreakhideout.com: Mission Six, "Not Too Young" Review jesusfreakhideout.com.  Retrieved 03-29-2011.  Much like other iShine artists, the ten-track album contains five original tracks followed by the sing-along versions of those tracks.
