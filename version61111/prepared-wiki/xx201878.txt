[[Ilija Zavišić]]

CATEGORIES: 1952 births, Living people, Serbian footballers, Yugoslav footballers, Yugoslavia international footballers, FK Partizan players, Eintracht Braunschweig players, Bundesliga players, FK Rad players, FK Bor players, Association football midfielders

Ilija Zavišić (Serbian Cyrillic: Илиja Зaвишић ; born January 10, 1952 in Donji Milanovac) is a Serbian retired football player.

==Coaching career==

He is working in FK Partizan youth categories for many years. His son Bojan made through the same system, and eventually played for Partizan and many Superliga clubs.
