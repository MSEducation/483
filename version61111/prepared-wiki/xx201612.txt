[[Aggenus Urbicus]]

CATEGORIES: Latin-language writers, Ancient Roman writers, Ancient Roman surveyors, 4th-century Christians

Aggenus Urbicus was an ancient Roman technical writer on the science of the Agrimensores, that is, land surveying.Dict of Ant. p. 30  It is uncertain when he lived; but he appears to have been a Christian, and it is not improbable from some expressions which he uses, that he lived at the latter part of the 4th century.[ref]
