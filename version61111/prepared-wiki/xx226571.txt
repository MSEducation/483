Hans Laurits Olsen Hammerstad (1840 - ??) was a Norwegian politician.
He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament in 1877, representing the constituency of Kristians Amt. He only served one term.Hans Laurits Olsen Hammerstad — Norwegian Social Science Data Services (NSD)
He was also involved in local politics, and was the mayor of Østre Toten from 1876 to 1877. He was succeeded by Martin Adolf Andersen.Mayors of municipalities west of Lake Mjøsa
He worked as a farmer.
