Vaine Kino is a Cook Islander rugby footballer who has represented the Cook Islands in rugby league.

==Rugby league career==

Kino won a cap for Cook Islands in the 2000 Rugby League World Cup.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.rugbyleagueproject.org/players/Vaine-Kino/summary.html|title=Statistics at rugbyleagueproject.org|publisher=rugbyleagueproject.org|date=2009-12-31|accessdate=2010-01-01[/tpl]
Kino later played for the Sydney Bulls in the Metropolitan Cup, kicking a field goal to ensure the Bulls won their first ever title.History Sydney Bulls

==Rugby union career==
