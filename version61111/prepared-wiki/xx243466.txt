[[Tomáš Kobes]]

CATEGORIES: Czech canoeists, Olympic canoeists of the Czech Republic, Canoeists at the 2000 Summer Olympics, 1978 births, Living people, People from Český Krumlov

[tpl]MedalCountry| [tpl]CZE[/tpl][/tpl]
Tomáš Kobes (born May 14, 1978 in Český Krumlov) is a Czech slalom canoer who competed in the 1990s and 2000s.
He won a bronze medal in the K-1 team event at the 1999 ICF Canoe Slalom World Championships in La Seu d'Urgell. He also won a silver and a bronze in the same event at the European Championships.
Kobes also finished seventh in the K-1 event at the 2000 Summer Olympics in Sydney.
