==CapeNature==

CapeNature, the Western Cape provincial conservation authority, manages Driftsands Nature Reserve on the Cape Flats, and the eastern part of the Kogelberg Nature Reserve. A small area of the Hottentots-Holland Nature Reserve also falls within the city limits of Cape Town.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.capetown.gov.za/en/EnvironmentalResourceManagement/publications/Documents/CCT_Nature_Reserves_book_2010-02.pdf |title=City of Cape Town Nature Reserves |publisher=City of Cape Town |date=February 2010 |accessdate=4 May 2011[/tpl]
