[[Julie Khaner]]

CATEGORIES: 1957 births, Actresses from Montreal, Anglophone Quebec people, Canadian film actresses, Canadian television actresses, Living people

Julie Khaner (born December 5, 1957) is a Canadian television and film actress, best known for her roles in as Alana Robinovitch in Street Legal, Sidney Dernhoff in The Newsroom, Gen in Deepwater Black and Bridey Jones in Videodrome.  She also appeared in the 1995 Susan Dey vampire flick Deadly Love.
