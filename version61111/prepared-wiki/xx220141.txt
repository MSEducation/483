[[Arad Power Station]]

CATEGORIES: Natural gas-fired power stations in Romania, Coal-fired power stations in Romania

The Arad Power Station is a large thermal power plant located in Arad, having 2 generation groups of 50 MW each  and one generating unit of 12 MW having a total electricity generation capacity of 112 MW.Capacitati CET Arad [tpl]ro[/tpl]
The two chimneys of the power station are 201 metres tall.
