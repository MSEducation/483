Canal de San Juan is a station along Line A of the Mexico City Metro.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.metro.df.gob.mx/red/estacion.html?id=124|title=Canal de San Juan|language=Spanish|accessdate=6 August 2011[/tpl][tpl]Cite web|last=Archambault|first=Richard|url=http://mexicometro.org/metro/linea/canal-de-san-juan/|title=Canal de San Juan »  Mexico City Metro System|accessdate=6 August 2011[/tpl]
The logo for the station depicts the bow of an Aztec canoe travelling through a canal. The station was opened on 12 August 1991.
