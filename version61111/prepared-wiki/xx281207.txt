[[9 August 2013 Quetta shooting]]

CATEGORIES: Terrorist incidents in Pakistan in 2013, Quetta, Mass shootings, Mass murder in 2013

On 9 August 2013, four gunmen opened fire at a mosque in Quetta, Pakistan while people were exiting the mosque. As a result, at least ten people were killed and thirty more people were injured. It is suspected that the target in the shooting was former Pakistan Peoples Party minister Ali Madad Jatak. This shooting occurred during the time when a lot of Pakistan citizens were celebrating Eid al-Fitr.
