[[Sharapudin Shalbuzov]]

CATEGORIES: 1993 births, Living people, Russian footballers

Sharapudin Mavludinovich Shalbuzov ([tpl]lang-ru|Шарапудин Мавлудинович Шалбузов[/tpl]; born February 23, 1993) is a Russian football midfielder, who currently playing for  FC Dagdizel Kaspiysk.
He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Dagdizel Kaspiysk on August 9, 2013 in a game against FC Mashuk-KMV Pyatigorsk.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Russian Football Union|url=http://rfspro.ru/cgi-bin/player/script2.cgi?par1=119897|title=Career Summary|date=12 August 2013[/tpl]
