[[Jarosław Drozd]]

CATEGORIES: Polish diplomats, Living people, Politology

Jarosław Drozd is a Polish politologist and diplomat. Currently he is the Consul-General of Poland in Saint Petersburghttp://www.petersburgkg.polemb.net/.
Before this he was a professor of politology in Cardinal Stefan Wyszyński University in Warsaw http://nauka.opi.org.pl/raporty/opisy/osoby/18000/o18239.htm getting his Dr. Sci degree in 1999. His dissertation was devoted to the German-Polish military cooperation in 1989-1997 http://nauka.opi.org.pl/raporty/opisy/synaba/82000/sn82290.htm
