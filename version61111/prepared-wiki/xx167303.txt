[[Netherlands Leprosy Relief]]

CATEGORIES: Organizations established in 1967, Charities based in the Netherlands, Non-profit organisations based in the Netherlands, Non-governmental organizations based in Vietnam, Leprosy organizations, 1967 establishments in the Netherlands

Netherlands Leprosy Relief ([tpl]lang-nl|Leprastichting[/tpl]) is a non-governmental organization, established on 30 March 1967, which promotes and supports the eradication of leprosy and all its consequences in seven regions where the disease is endemic.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ilep.org.uk/ilep-members/nlr-the-netherlands/|title=Netherlands Leprosy Relief|work=ILEP|accessdate=December 4, 2012[/tpl] NLR was a founding member of the International Federation of Anti-Leprosy Associations (ILEP), and sponsors over 100 projects in about 20 countries.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ilep.org.uk/?mid=6&id=1|title=Welcome to ILEP |accessdate=December 4, 2012[/tpl] The association helped create (and maintain) a leprosy-information service (Infolep),[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.leprosy-information.org/|title=ILEP INFOLEP Leprosy Information Portal|accessdate=December 4, 2012[/tpl] which may be accessed from the ILEP website.
