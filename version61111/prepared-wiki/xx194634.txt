[[North Texas Soccer Association]]

CATEGORIES: Sports in Dallas, Texas, Non-profit organizations based in Texas, Soccer in Texas, Soccer governing bodies in the United States

North Texas Soccer Association, established in 1964, organises over 175,000 soccer players registered through over 123 member associations. It is based in Carrollton, Texas.
