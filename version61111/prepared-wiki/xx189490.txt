[[Stop (Franco De Vita album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2004 albums, Franco De Vita albums

[tpl]Refimprove|date=April 2008[/tpl]

Stop is a 2004 studio album by Franco De Vita. The major hit single was "Tú De Qué Vas", however, "Ay Dios" and "Si La Ves", a collaboration with Sin Bandera, were also released as singles. The album earned a Latin Grammy Award nomination for Best Male Pop Vocal Album.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.usatoday.com/life/music/news/2005-11-02-latin-grammy-list_x.htm|title=Complete list of 6th annual Latin Grammy nominations|work=USA Today|publisher=Gannett Company|accessdate=January 28, 2011|date=November 2, 2005[/tpl]
