[[Alf Handrahan]]

CATEGORIES: 1949 births, Canadian ice hockey right wingers, Ice hockey people from Prince Edward Island, Cincinnati Stingers players, Living people, People from Alberton, Prince Edward Island

Alf Handrahan (born December 27, 1949) is a former World Hockey Association player. He played fourteen games for the Cincinnati Stingers. He was born in Alberton, Prince Edward Island.
