[[Cyrtocara]]

CATEGORIES: Haplochromini, Fish of Africa, Monotypic fish genera

Cyrtocara moorii, the Hump-head, is a species of cichlid endemic to Lake Malawi in east Africa where they prefer areas with sandy substrates.  It can grow to a length of [tpl]convert|20|cm|in[/tpl] TL. The species is popular among aquarium keepers where it is known as the hump-head cichlid, blue dolphin cichlid, Malawi dolphin or simply as moorii.  It is currently the only known member of its genus.[tpl]FishBase species |genus= Cyrtocara|species= moorii| month = February | year = 2013[/tpl]

==Physical description==

They are varying shades of blue, ranging from turquoise to a silvery-blue, and grow a distinctive hump on their forehead.
