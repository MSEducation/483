[[45th meridian east]]

CATEGORIES: Lines of longitude

The meridian 45Â° east of Greenwich is a line of longitude that extends from the North Pole across the Arctic Ocean, Europe, Asia, Africa, the Indian Ocean, the Southern Ocean, and Antarctica to the South Pole.
The 45th meridian east forms a great circle with the 135th meridian west.
