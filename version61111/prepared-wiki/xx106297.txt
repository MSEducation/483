[[Milić od Mačve]]

CATEGORIES: 20th-century Serbian people, Serbian painters, Mačva, People from Šabac, Burials in Belgrade, 1934 births, 2000 deaths

Milić Stanković, known by his artistic name Milić of Mačva ([tpl]lang-sr|Милић од Мачве[/tpl], Milić od Mačve; 30 October 1934 – 8 December 2000), was a Serbian painter and artist.
Stanković was born in 1934 in Belotić, Kingdom of Yugoslavia. He graduated from the Belgrade Academy of Arts in 1959. He died in 2000 in Belgrade, Federal Republic of Yugoslavia.
