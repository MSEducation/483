[[Pirates (2014 film)]]

CATEGORIES: Upcoming films, South Korean adventure films, South Korean films, Films set in the Joseon Dynasty, Lotte Entertainment films

Pirates ([tpl]ko-hhrm|해적||Haejuk[/tpl]) is an upcoming South Korean period adventure film starring Kim Nam-gil and Son Ye-jin.

==Plot==

Set in the early Joseon Dynasty, a group led by a female pirate and another group led by a male bandit are on a mission to hunt down a whale that swallowed the royal seal bestowed on Joseon from China.[tpl]cite web|last=Lee|first=Eun-ah|title=Kim Nam-gil and Son Ye-jin Cast in New Adventure Pic|url=http://www.tenasia.com/kim-nam-gil-and-son-ye-jin-cast-in-new-adventure-pic/|work=TenAsia|accessdate=2013-08-14|date=3 July 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web|last=Tae|first=Sang-joon|title=Period Adventure Film PIRATES Set Sail|url=http://www.koreanfilm.or.kr/jsp/news/news.jsp?mode=VIEW&seq=2618|work=Korean Film Council|accessdate=2013-08-14|date=5 August 2013[/tpl]
