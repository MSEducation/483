Giorgos Skartados (born 7 April 1960) is a retired Greek international footballer.

==Career==

Born in Rhodes, Skartados started his career at local club Rodos F.C.. In the summer of 1982, he joined PAOK and played mainly as a midfielder for 256 league games and scored 84 goals, including the national championship in 1985. He was also a regular for the Greek national side.Euro '84 qualifiers, via RSSSF.com
