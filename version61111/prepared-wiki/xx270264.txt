[[Logarithmic Schrödinger equation]]

CATEGORIES: Theoretical physics, Quantum mechanics, Schrödinger equation

In theoretical physics, the logarithmic Schrödinger equation (sometimes abbreviated as LNSE or LogSE) is one of the nonlinear modifications of Schrödinger's equation.
