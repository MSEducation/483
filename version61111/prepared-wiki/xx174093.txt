[[Saeid Janfada]]

CATEGORIES: 1964 births, Living people, Iranian footballers, Iran international footballers, Iranian expatriate footballers, VVV-Venlo players, Esteghlal F.C. players

Saeed Janfada ([tpl]lang-fa|سعید جان‌فدا[/tpl], born 21 March 1964 in Tehran) is a retired Iranian football defender who played for Esteghlal, VVV-Venlo and the Iran national football team.
Janfada competed for Iran at the 1988 AFC Asian Cup.
