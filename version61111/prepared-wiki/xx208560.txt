[[Pyrgomantis nasuta]]

CATEGORIES: Pyrgomantis, Mantodea of Africa, Animals described in 1784

Pyrgomantis nasuta is a species of praying mantis found in Angola, Cape Province, Cameroon, Kenya, Natal, Namibia, Somalia, and Tanzania.
