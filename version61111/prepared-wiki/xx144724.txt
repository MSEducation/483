[[Zhou Zhuoru]]

Zhou Zhuoru ([tpl]zh|周卓如[/tpl]) born September 14, 1988 is a World Champion Chinese Gymnast from Fushun, Liaoning, Peoples Republic of China.[tpl]cite web |url=http://data.star.sports.cn/person_en.php?id=280 |title=Official Website of the Chinese Olympic Committee |author= |date= |work=CIS Chinese Athletes Database |publisher=Chinese Olympic Committee |accessdate=10 December 2010[/tpl]
Zhou Zhuoru was named alternate for the 2004 Chinese Olympic team. Following steady improvement and development, she was named to the Chinese team to compete at the 2006 World Gymnastics Championships in Aarhus, Denmark. The Chinese team won the Gold Medal in the team event, with Zhou Zhuoru contributing routines on Vault (score 14.450) and Uneven Bars (score 15.150).
She also participated in the All Around final, finishing a respectable 12th overall with a combined score of 58.575.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.gymnasticsresults.com/2006/w2006w.html |title=30th Women's Artistic Gymnastics World Championships Aarhus (DEN) |author= |date= |work= |publisher=GymnasticsResults.com |accessdate=10 December 2010[/tpl]
