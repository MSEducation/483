[[J. B. Hanauer]]

CATEGORIES: Stock brokerages and investment banks of Canada

J.B. Hanauer, a wealth management firm established in 1931 and based in Parsippany, New Jersey, was bought out by RBC Dain Rauscher in March 2007.Royal Bank of Canada: RBC Dain Rauscher to acquire J.B. Hanauer & Co. RBC Dain Rauscher changed its name to RBC Wealth Management in October 2009.
