[[(39561) 1992 QA]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1992, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Mars-crossing asteroids

(39561) 1992 QA is a Mars-crossing minor planet.  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on August 19, 1992.JPL Small-Body Database Browser
