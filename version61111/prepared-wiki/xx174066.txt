[[Qingyang District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Chengdu, County-level divisions of Sichuan

Qingyang District ([tpl]zh|s=青羊区|p=Qīngyáng Qū[/tpl]) is a district of Chengdu, the capital of Sichuan Province, China.
Qingyang has an area of 68 square kilometers and a population of 460,000.[tpl]zh icon[/tpl] Profile of Qingyang District, Official website of Qingyang District Government, visited on May 26, 2008.
