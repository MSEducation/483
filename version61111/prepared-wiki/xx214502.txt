[[HMS Resolution (1708)]]

CATEGORIES: Ships of the line of the Royal Navy, 1700s ships

HMS ''Resolution'' was a 70-gun third rate ship of the line of the Royal Navy, built by Sir Joseph Allin according to the 1706 Establishment at Deptford Dockyard, and launched on 25 March 1708.
Resolution was wrecked in 1711.
