[[Saenger Theatre (Pine Bluff, Arkansas)]]

CATEGORIES: Cinemas and movie theaters in Arkansas, Emile Weil buildings, Buildings and structures in Pine Bluff, Arkansas, Movie palaces, National Register of Historic Places in Arkansas, Theatres completed in 1924

The Saenger Theatre is an historic theater in Pine Bluff, Arkansas.  Located at West Second 
Ave. and Pine St. on the southeast corner, it was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1995.[tpl]cite web | title =Local Resources Listed on the National Register of Historic Places | work =cityofpinebluff.com | publisher = | date = | url =http://www.cityofpinebluff.com/historic/National%20Register%20of%20Historic%20Places%20-%20Pine%20Bluff%20Listings.pdf | format =PDF | doi = | accessdate =2011-09-24[/tpl] Built in 1924 it is one of only a handful of Saenger movie palaces that remain.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.saengeramusements.com/theatres/theatres.htm|title=The Saenger Theatres|publisher=Saenger Amusements| accessdate =2011-09-24[/tpl]
