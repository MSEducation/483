[[Scylla tranquebarica]]

CATEGORIES: Portunoidea, Indo-Pacific crustaceans, Animals described in 1798

[tpl]Italic title[/tpl]

Scylla tranquebarica, is a species of mangrove crab in the genus Scylla. Scylla tranquebarica, one of several crabs known as the mud crab, is found in mangrove areas from Pakistan and Taiwan to the Malay Archipelago and other Indo-Pacific regions.[tpl]cite web |url=http://species-identification.org/species.php?species_group=crabs_of_japan&id=1085 |title=Scylla tranquebarica |work=Crabs of Japan |publisher=Marine Species Identification Portal |accessdate=February 26, 2011[/tpl]
