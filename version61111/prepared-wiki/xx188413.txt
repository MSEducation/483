[[1999 U.S. Open (golf)]]

CATEGORIES: U.S. Open (golf), Golf in North Carolina, 1999 in golf, 1999 in American sports

The 1999 United States Open Championship was the 99th U.S. Open, held from June 17–20 at Pinehurst Resort Course No. 2 in Pinehurst, North Carolina. Payne Stewart won his second U.S. Open and third major championship, one stroke ahead of runner-up Phil Mickelson. The U.S. Open was the 11th and final PGA Tour win for Stewart.
