[[Łukaszówka]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Krasnystaw County

Łukaszówka [tpl]IPAc-pl|ł|u|k|a|'|sz|u|f|k|a[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kraśniczyn, within Krasnystaw County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) – TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|11|km|mi|0[/tpl] south-east of Krasnystaw and [tpl]convert|61|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-east of the regional capital Lublin.
