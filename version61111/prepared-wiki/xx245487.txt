[[Ljubavi (album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2009 albums, Željko Joksimović albums

Ljubavi[tpl]dead link|date=May 2012[/tpl]  (in [tpl]lang-sr|Љубави[/tpl]) is the fifth studio album by Serbian pop singer and songwriter Željko Joksimović. It was released in all the countries of former Yugoslavia on 2 November 2009.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.zeljkojoksimovic.com/ |title=Željko Joksimović |publisher=Zeljkojoksimovic.com |date= |accessdate=2012-02-29[/tpl]  The first single from the album shares the same name with the album.
