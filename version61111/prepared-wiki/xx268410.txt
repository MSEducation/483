[[The Tiger Warrior]]

CATEGORIES: 2005 novels, British adventure novels, British thriller novels

The Tiger Warrior is an archaeological adventure novel by David Gibbins. First published in 2009, it is the fourth book in Gibbins' Jack Howard series. It has been published in 18 languages and was a New York Times bestseller http://www.nytimes.com/best-sellers-books/2009-07-12/mass-market-paperback/list.html?scp=10&sq=David%20Gibbins&st=cse.

==International editions==

Among its foreign language editions, The Tiger Warrior is entitled Tigers de Guerre in France (Editions First).
