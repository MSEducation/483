[[Ningcheng County]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Ningcheng County (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠨᠢᠩᠴᠧᠩ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠰᠢᠶᠠᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Niŋčėŋ siyan[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=宁城县 |t=寧城縣 |p=Níngchéng Xiàn[/tpl]) is a county of southeastern Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China, bordering Liaoning province to the east. It is under the administration of Chifeng City.www.xzqh.org [tpl]zh icon[/tpl]
It was formerly known as Beijing under the 12th-century Jurchen Jin dynasty, who used it as their northern capital.Theobald, Ulrich. China Knowledge. "Chinese History - Jin Dynasty 金 (1115-1234): Map and Geography". Accessed 19 Oct 2012.
