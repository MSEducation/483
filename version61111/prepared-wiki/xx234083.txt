[[Disney Cruise Line Terminal]]

CATEGORIES: Buildings and structures in Brevard County, Florida, Transportation in Brevard County, Florida, Disney Cruise Line

The Disney Cruise Line Terminal or Port Canaveral's terminal 8, is located in Port Canaveral in Cape Canaveral, Florida, USA. It is used to transport passengers on and off of the Disney Cruise Line ships the Disney Magic, the Disney Wonder, the Disney Dream, and the Disney Fantasy.
The port, located in Port Canaveral, at cruise terminal A, is a large building, owned by Port Canaveral, and operated by the Walt Disney Company. At [tpl]convert|70000|sqft|m2[/tpl], this massive building includes is the base for Disney Cruise Line check-in, security, and boarding.
