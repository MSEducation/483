[[Dušan Vidojević]]

Dušan Vidojević (born 6 April 1979) is a footballer who last played as a midfielder for FK Sloga Kraljevo.

==Club career==

Born in Belgrade, Vidojević began playing football with FK Rad in the First League of Serbia and Montenegro.
Vidojević moved to Greece in July 2002, joining Greek first division side PAS Giannina F.C.,[tpl]cite web|publisher=RSSSF|author=Mastrogiannopoulos, Alexander|title=Foreign Players in Greece since 1959/60|url=http://www.rsssf.com/players/foreign-players-in-grk6080.html|date=11 May 2005[/tpl] making 21 top flight league appearances for the club.[tpl]cite web|publisher=RSSSF|author=Mastrogiannopoulos, Alexander|title=Greece 2002/03|url=http://www.rsssf.com/tablesg/grk03.html|date=21 March 2004[/tpl] He would also play for Kerkyra F.C. and Panachaiki F.C. in the Greek second division. He also had a brief spell with FC Braşov in the Romanian second division.
He returned to Serbia and played for FK Voždovac and FK Banat Zrenjanin in the Serbian Superliga.
