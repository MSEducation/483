[[Elachista klimeschiella]]

CATEGORIES: Elachistidae, Moths described in 2002, Butterflies and moths of Europe, Moths of Europe, Insects of Hungary, Insects of Italy

Elachista klimeschiella is a moth of the Elachistidae family. It is found in France, Switzerland, Austria, Italy, the Czech Republic and Hungary.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.faunaeur.org/full_results.php?id=435366|title=Elachista (Aphelosetia) klimeschiella Parenti, 2002|publisher=Fauna Europaea|version=2.6.2|date=August 29, 2013|accessdate=November 12, 2013[/tpl]
