[[Pennsylvania Route 392]]

CATEGORIES: State highways in Pennsylvania, Transportation in York County, Pennsylvania

Pennsylvania Route 392 (PA 392) is a [tpl]convert|5.16|mi|km|adj=mid|-long[/tpl] state highway located in York County, Pennsylvania.  The western terminus is at PA 177 near Lewisberry.  The eastern terminus is at PA 262 near Goldsboro.

==Route description==

PA 392 begins at an intersection with PA 177 in Newberry Township, heading east on two-lane undivided Potts Hill Road. The road winds east along a forested hill, passing a few homes. The route turns to the southeast and runs a short distance to the west of I-83. PA 392 turns north onto Old Trail Road and immediately interchanges with I-83 before heading north into a mix of woods and development. The route turns east onto Yocumtown Road and passes housing developments. Farther northeast, PA 392 runs through a mix of agriculture and homes with some woods before heading east into open farmland and ending at PA 262 in the community of Plainfield.[tpl]google maps|url=http://maps.google.com/maps?f=d&source=s_d&saddr=PA+177+and+PA+392&daddr=PA+262+and+PA+392&hl=en&geocode=FfypZAId435r-ymXbFC4O-vIiTHk5cjZlK7OsA%3BFR_xZAIdFats-ykjFsq7u5XIiTHGP_ahez1fFw&mra=ls&sll=40.00177,-76.765645&sspn=0.059174,0.169086&ie=UTF8&t=h&z=14|title=overview of Pennsylvania Route 392|accessdate=February 23, 2011[/tpl][tpl]cite map|publisher=PennDOT|title=York County, Pennsylvania Highway Map|url=ftp://ftp.dot.state.pa.us/public/pdf/BPR_pdf_files/Maps/GHS/Roadnames/york_GHSN.PDF|year=2011|accessdate=February 22, 2011[/tpl]
