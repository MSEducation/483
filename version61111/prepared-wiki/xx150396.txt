[[KVTX-LP]]

CATEGORIES: Channel 45 low-power TV stations in the United States

KVTX-LP is the Telemundo affiliate in Victoria, Texas.  The station is owned by Saga Communications, and operates on UHF channel 45 with no companion digital signal. KVTX-LP is carried as a digital subchannel on sister station KAVU-TV's digital signal, along with two other sister stations. http://myvictoriaonline.com/HDTV#subchannel
The station is part of a group of television stations in Victoria that are owned by Saga, based out of KAVU-TV.  It has operated since the early 2000s at the earliest.
