[[China Travel Service]]

CATEGORIES: Tourism in China, Government of China, Travel agencies

[tpl]Unreferenced|date=November 2013[/tpl]The China Travel Service ([tpl]zh|c=中国旅行社总社[/tpl]) is the tourism and travel agency of the government of the People's Republic of China. It was established on November 19, 1949 tasked with marketing China to the rest of the world and to promote and develop its tourism economy. It is a subordinate agency  to the National Tourism Administration.
