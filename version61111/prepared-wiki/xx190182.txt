[[Conchas Lake Seaplane Base]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in New Mexico, Seaplane bases in the United States, Transportation in San Miguel County, New Mexico, Buildings and structures in San Miguel County, New Mexico

Conchas Lake Seaplane Base [tpl]airport codes|||E61[/tpl] is a public use seaplane base located two nautical miles (4 km) southwest of the central business district of Conchas Dam, in San Miguel County, New Mexico, United States. It is owned by the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers. It has one seaplane landing area measuring 21,120 by 1,320 feet (6,437 by 402 m).
