CATEGORIES: Swimming at the 1984 Summer Olympics

The final of the Women's 200 metres backstroke event at the 1984 Summer Olympics was held in the McDonald's Olympic Swim Stadium in Los Angeles, California, on August 4, 1984. The fastest eight qualified for the final, the next eight for the B-final.
