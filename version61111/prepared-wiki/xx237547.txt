[[1997 Acura Classic]]

CATEGORIES: 1997 WTA Tour, LA Women's Tennis Championships, Sports events in Manhattan Beach, California

The 1997 Acura Classic was a tennis tournament played on outdoor hard courts at the Manhattan Country Club in Manhattan Beach, California in the United States that was part of Tier II of the 1997 WTA Tour. The tournament was held from August 4 through August 10, 1997.
