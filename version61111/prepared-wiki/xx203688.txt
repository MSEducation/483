[[1998–99 Prva HNL]]

CATEGORIES: Prva HNL seasons, 1998–99 domestic association football leagues, 1998–99 in Croatian football

The 1998–99 Prva HNL was the eighth season of the Croatian top-level football league since its establishment.

==Overview==

It was contested by 12 teams, and Croatia Zagreb won the championship.
In the first stage upper six teams advance to Championship Group, bottom six to Relegation Group, with 50% of points taken to the next phase of the competition.
