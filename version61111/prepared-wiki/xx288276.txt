[[Spain in the Eurovision Song Contest 1967]]

CATEGORIES: Countries in the Eurovision Song Contest 1967, Spain in the Eurovision Song Contest, 1967 in Spain

Spain was represented by Raphael at the Eurovision Song Contest 1967, held in Vienna, Austria. Raphael was selected internally for the second year in a row by Radiotelevisión Española (RTVE), the Spanish broadcaster, to represent the country with the song "Hablemos del amor".

==At Eurovision==

Raphael performed 12th in the night of the contest, following the United Kingdom and preceding Norway. At the close of voting Raphael had received 9 points, placing 6th in a field of 17.http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eurovision_Song_Contest_1967
