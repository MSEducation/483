[[Mita Hachiman Jinja]]

CATEGORIES: Minato, Tokyo, Shinto shrines in Tokyo

Mita Hachiman Jinja (御田八幡神社) is a Shinto shrine in Mita 3-7-16, Minato, Tokyo, Japan.
