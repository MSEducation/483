[[Eugenio Colombo]]

CATEGORIES: 1953 births, Avant-garde jazz flautists, Avant-garde jazz saxophonists, Musicians from Rome, Living people

Eugenio Colombo (born 1953) is an Italian saxophonist and flautist most associated with avant-garde jazz. A founding member of the Italian Instabile Orchestra, Colombo has worked with such musicians as Mario Schiano, Giorgio Gaslini, Steve Lacy, Bruno Tommaso, Maurizio Giammarco, Giancarlo Schiaffini and the band Area.[tpl]cite web| last =Martinelli| first =Francesco| title =Eugenio Colombo Biography| publisher =Allmusic| url= [tpl]Allmusic|class=artist|id=p236375|pure_url=yes[/tpl][/tpl]
