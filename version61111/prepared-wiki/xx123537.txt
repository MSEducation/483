[[Mexico City Blues]]

CATEGORIES: Poetry by Jack Kerouac, American poetry collections, 1959 poems

Mexico City Blues is a poem published by Jack Kerouac in 1959 composed of 242 "choruses" or stanzas.  In his own words, Kerouac wanted to be known as a jazz poet and with this book he sought to write in a way consistent with how a musician would play jazz.
