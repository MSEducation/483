[[Tell Me (Billie Myers song)]]

CATEGORIES: 1998 singles

"Tell Me" is a song by Billie Myers, released as the second single from her debut album. It was released in 1998. It reached #28 on the UK charts, which compared to her previous single was a commercial failure.
