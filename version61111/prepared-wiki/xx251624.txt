[[Estamos locos... ¿o qué?]]

CATEGORIES: 1987 albums, Hombres G albums

Estamos locos... ¿o qué? is the third studio album by Spanish rock band Hombres G, released in 1987. ==History==

After finishing filming their first movie, Sufre mamón, Hombres G began work on their third studio album. David Summers isolated himself for a week at a family house in Huelva and upon returning to the rest of the band, had composed 12 songs.  To avoid pressure, Hombres G left for Manchester, where they recorded Estamos locos... ¿o qué? with producer Carlos Narea.[tpl]Citation|title = HombresG.Net - Oficial - Archivos - Historia| url=http://hombresg.net/| accessdate = 8 June 2010 [/tpl][tpl]Citation|title = Historia Hombres G :: www.Hombres-G.com| url=http://hombres-g.com/historia.html| accessdate = 8 June 2010 [/tpl]
