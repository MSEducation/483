[[New World Order (video game)]]

CATEGORIES: 2003 video games, First-person shooters, Multiplayer online games, Video games developed in Sweden, Windows games

New World Order is an online team-based tactical first-person shooter video game, similar to Counter-Strike. The game was developed by Termite Games, a Swedish game studio and is powered by the DVA graphics engine. The game went on sale in 2003. The game was negatively received by critics, with GameSpot stating that it "fails in every imaginable way"http://uk.gamespot.com/pc/action/newworldorder/review.html and Eurogamer describing it as "horrendously bad" and "a total farce".http://www.eurogamer.net/articles/r_newworldorder_pc
