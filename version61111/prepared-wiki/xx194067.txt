[[Ministry of Culture (Pakistan)]]

CATEGORIES: Cabinet of Pakistan, Culture ministries, Government ministries of Pakistan, Tourism ministries

The Ministry of Culture, National Heritage and Integration, Urdu: وزارث ثقافت; reporting name: MoCul, is a Cabinet-level ministry of Government of Pakistan, charged with the promotion of culture (architecture, cinema (films), dance, folklore, literature (Mushaira), music, philosophy, textiles and theatre), implementing and enforcing the cultural policies and activities in the country.  The ministry is devolved into provisional governments of Pakistan, but its federal activities currently manages by the Ministry of Culture, National Heritage and Integration  which is currently headquartered in Cabinet Secretariat, Islamabad Capital Venue. Its executive and political figure is the Culture Minister, who is a public appointed official and must be to required to be a member of Parliament of Pakistan. The ministry coordinates its activities through its designated departments and also at the university levels.
