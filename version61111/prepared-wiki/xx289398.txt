[[Qameshlu, Sojas Rud]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Qameshlu ([tpl]lang-fa|قمشلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Qameshlū; also known as Gomeshlū, Gowmeshlū, Kūkūshlu, Kūshgūnlū, Kushkunu, Kūshlū, and Qīmishlu)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3079456|Qameshlu[/tpl] is a village in Aq Bolagh Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 77, in 12 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
