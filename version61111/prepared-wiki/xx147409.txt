[[Ikawa-Sakura Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Akita Prefecture, Ōu Main Line

[tpl]nihongo|Ikawa-Sakura Station|井川さくら駅|Ikawa-Sakura-eki[/tpl] is a railway station on the Ōu Main Line in Ikawa, Akita, Japan, operated by East Japan Railway Company (JR East). 

==Lines==

Ikawa-Sakura Station is served by the Ōu Main Line, and is located 323.6 rail kilometers from the terminus of the line at Fukushima Station. 

==Station layout==

Ikawa-Sakura Station consists of two opposed side platforms connected by an underpass. Ikawa-Sakura Station is a simple consignment station (Kan'i itaku station), administered by Hachirōgata Station, and operated by Ikawa municipal authority, with point-of-sales terminal installed.[tpl]Citation needed|date=September 2013[/tpl] The station building includes a community center, a reading room, and a cafe.[tpl]Citation needed|date=September 2013[/tpl]
