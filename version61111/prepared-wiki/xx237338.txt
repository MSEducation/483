[[Yao He]]

CATEGORIES: Tang dynasty poets

Yao He (Chinese:姚合) was a Chinese poet of middle Tang Dynasty. He was the son of grand nephew of Yao Chong. He was ofen called Yao Wugong (姚武功), and his poetry style was called "Wugong Style". Yao was very famous at his time. He knew Liu Yuxi, Li Shen, Zhang Ji, Wang Jian, Yang Juyuan, Ma Dai, Li Qunyu well, and was a close friend of Jia Dao. His style was very similar to Jia Dao, but was more tame and superficial.[tpl]zh icon[/tpl]Zhu Jincheng. Yao He, Encyclopedia of China (Chinese Literature Edition), 1st ed.
