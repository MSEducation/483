[[Čađavica, Croatia]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Croatia, Populated places in Virovitica-Podravina County

Čađavica is a village and municipality in Croatia in the Virovitica–Podravina County. It has a population of 2,394 (2001 census), of which 89% are Croats. It is situated 14 kilometers NE of Slatina.
Until 1920 it was an integral part of Hungary.
Trivia: The mother, Paula Mazaly, of the Hungarian Nobel-prize winner Georg von Békésy was born here.
