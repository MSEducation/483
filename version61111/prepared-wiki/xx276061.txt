[[Hitra–Frøya]]

CATEGORIES: Publications established in 1974, 1974 establishments in Norway, Newspapers published in Norway

Hitra–Frøya is a Norwegian newspaper, published in Hitra, Norway. The newspaper was founded in 1974 as Hitra-Nytt, and was called Hitra–Frøya from 1985.
