[[Baghlujeh-ye Aqa]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Baghlujeh-ye Aqa ([tpl]lang-fa|باغلوجه اقا[/tpl], also Romanized as Bāghlūjeh-ye Āqā; also known as Baghlūjah, Bāghlūjeh, and Bagludzhakh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3054699|Baghlujeh-ye Aqa[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 220, in 53 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
