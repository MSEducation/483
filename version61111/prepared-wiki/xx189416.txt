[[Charlotte Gregg]]

CATEGORIES: Australian television actresses, 1978 births, Living people

Charlotte Gregg (born December, 1978/January, 1979) is an Australian actress, who played the part of Charity Fernbrook in Home and Away, who was a member of the Cult 'The Believers' and prior to that was a midwife. The character was in the show originally as a cult member but was brought back by force by Mama Rose to induce the labour of Tasha.
Gregg played Tracy O'Donnell, wife of Jarrod O'Donnell in the BBC commissioned Australian drama Out of the Blue. She also played the lead character in the 2007 romantic comedy All My Friends Are Leaving Brisbane.
According to her MySpace page, she was Miss Teen New South Wales in 1993 and graduated in 1996 and is an alumna from John Paul College in Coffs Harbour, New South Wales (1991–1996) with the High School diploma.
