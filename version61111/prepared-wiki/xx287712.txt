[[America's Best Franchising]]

CATEGORIES: Companies based in Atlanta, Georgia, Hotel chains

America's Best Franchising, based in Atlanta, Georgia is an American lodging company. Its brands comprise America's Best Inn, Country Hearth Inns & Suites, Jameson Inn, 3 Palms Hotel, Parkside Hotels, and Budgetel.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.americasbestinn.com/about-us/ |title=About Us |publisher=Americasbestinn.com |date= |accessdate=2013-08-14[/tpl] Of the brands, America's Best Inn was founded in Marion, Illinois,[tpl]cite book |last1=Jakle |first1=John A. |last2=Sculle |first2=Keith A. |last3=Rogers |first3=Jefferson S. |title=The Motel in America |year=1996 |publisher=Johns Hopkins University Press |isbn=0-8018-5383-4 |pages=215-216[/tpl] The Budgetel name is a revival of a brand which was renamed Baymont Inn & Suites. Country Hearth opened its first location in 1983 as Homeplace Inn in Angleton, Texas.[tpl]cite web|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=LZH-0RR3z-oC&pg=PA113&dq=%22Country+hearth+inn%22&hl=en&sa=X&ei=RYQFUuLSG-ioyAG1goHQAQ&ved=0CC0Q6AEwAA#v=onepage&q=%22Country%20hearth%20inn%22&f=false |title=Historic Brazoria County: An Illustrated History - Margaret Swett Henson |publisher= |date= |accessdate=2013-08-14[/tpl]
Budgetel Franchise System  is an American-based hotel franchiser to United States and Canada.  The current Budgetel was established in Atlanta, Georgia in 2007 by brothers Mike and R. C. Patel who bought the brand from Blackstone Group.Budgetel Franchise System, LLC  In 2009, America's Best Franchising, Inc. began franchising the brand.
