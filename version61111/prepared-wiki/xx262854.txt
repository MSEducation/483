[[1968 USAC Championship Car season]]

CATEGORIES: 1968 in motorsport, USAC Championship Car, 1968 in American motorsport

The 1968 USAC Championship Car season consisted of 28 races, beginning in Hanford, California on March 17 and concluding in Riverside, California on December 1.  The USAC National Champion and Indianapolis 500 winner was Bobby Unser.
