Axel Otto Christian Hagemann (1856–1907) was a Norwegian politician for the Conservative Party.
He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament in 1895, representing the constituency of Finmarkens Amt. He only served one term.Axel Otto Kristian Hagemann — Norwegian Social Science Data Services (NSD)
