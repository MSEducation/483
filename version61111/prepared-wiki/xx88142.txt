[[Leawarra railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1959

Leawarra railway station is located on the Stony Point line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the south-eastern Melbourne suburb of Frankston opening in 1959 as Railway Stopping Place No 16, being renamed Lewarra in 1962.Leawarra Vicsig
Langwarrin, a former station on the Stony Point line, is located between Leawarra and Baxter stations.Langwarrin Vicsig

==Platforms & services==

Leawarra has one platform. It is serviced by Metro Trains' Stony Point line services.Stony Point Line timetable Public Transport Victoria At 44 metres, it is the shortest platform on the VicTrack network.
