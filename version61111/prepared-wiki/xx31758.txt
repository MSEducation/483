[[Fluorocarbon]]

CATEGORIES: Fluorocarbons, Greenhouse gases

Fluorocarbons, sometimes referred to as perfluorocarbons or PFCs, are, strictly speaking organofluorine compounds with the formula CxFy, i.e. they contain only carbon and fluorine.[tpl]GoldBookRef | title = fluorocarbons | file = F02459[/tpl]   The terminology is not strictly followed.[ref]
