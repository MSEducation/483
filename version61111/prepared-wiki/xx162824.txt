[[Ralph Arlyck]]

CATEGORIES: American documentary filmmakers, Colgate University alumni, Living people, Year of birth missing (living people), Guggenheim Fellows

Ralph Arlyck is an American documentary filmmaker. He has won many awards for his films and has been shown at film festivals including Sundance, New York, London, and Cannes.Ralph Arlyck biography at PBS.org
An alumnus of Colgate University, his first major film was Sean, a short film which features the story of four year old Sean Farrell growing up in the Haight-Ashbury section of San Francisco in 1969. It received wide acclaim in the United States and Europe for its frank portrayal of the 1960s counterculture.
