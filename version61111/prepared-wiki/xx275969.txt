[[Ellen Barry (attorney)]]

CATEGORIES: American lawyers, 1955 births, MacArthur Fellows, Swarthmore College alumni, New York University School of Law alumni, Living people, People from Somerville, Massachusetts, American women lawyers

Ellen M. Barry (born 1955) is an American attorney, and public interest lawyer.http://www.caldir.com/lawyers/California/San-Francisco/94102/1wr1/Ellen-M-Barry.html She was a 1998 MacArthur Fellow.http://www.macfound.org/site/c.lkLXJ8MQKrH/b.1139459/k.954D/Fellows_List__B.htm

==Life==

She grew up in Somerville, Massachusetts as the second of ten children. 
She graduated from Swarthmore College, and New York University Law School
She started Legal Services for Prisoners with Children, in 1978.http://www.prisonerswithchildren.org/about/board/
