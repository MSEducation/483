[[Meitanba, Ningxiang]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Ningxiang County

Meitanba Town([tpl]zh|s=煤炭坝镇|t=煤炭壩鎮|p=Meitanba Zhen[/tpl]), is an urban town in Ningxiang County, Changsha City, Hunan Province, People's Republic of China. It's surrounded by Yujia'ao Township on the west, Yuejiaqiao Town on the north, Jinghuapu Township on the east, and Dachengqiao Township and Huilongpu Town on the south. As of the 2000 census it had a population of 50,567 and an area of 73.4 square kilometers.

==Cityscape==

The Town is divided into fourteen villages and one community, the following areas: Meitaba Community (煤炭坝社区), Queshu Village (棤树村), Zhuantang Village (砖塘村), Nanzhushan Village (楠竹山村), Shixin Village (石新村), Hejiawan Village (贺家湾村), Dongshan Village (东山村), Heshiqiao Village (贺石桥村), Fujiachong Village (傅家冲村), Changsi Village (菖丝村), Zhangjiawan Village (张家湾村), Longshi Village (龙石村) and Shuanglong Village (双龙村).

==Transportation==

National Highway running through the territory of 319(国道319), with S206 Provincial Highway (省道S206) and 3 county rural roads and improve the formation transportation network. The 1824 Provincial Highway (1824省道) from Jinghuapu Township, running through Meitanba Town to Taojiang County.

==Attractions==

Baishi Temple (白石庵), was built in the period of the Jiajing Emperor, is a buddhism temple in the town, it was destroyed and rebuilt several times.
