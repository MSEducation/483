[[Ministry of Overseas Pakistanis and Human Resource Development]]

CATEGORIES: Government ministries of Pakistan, 2013 establishments in Pakistan, Pakistani diaspora, Diaspora ministries

The Ministry of Overseas Pakistanis and Human Resource Development is a ministry of the Government of Pakistan that oversees matters concerning Overseas Pakistanis and human resource development in Pakistan.
