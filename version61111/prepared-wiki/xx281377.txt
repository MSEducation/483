[[No Revolution]]

CATEGORIES: 2005 singles, 2004 songs, Virgin Records singles

"No Revolution" is a single by the band The Explosion, released as the second single from their 2004 album Black Tape on August 2, 2005. The song was originally recorded for the band's 2000 album Flash Flash Flash but was rerecorded for Black Tape. The music video features the band playing live. The song reached 107st[tpl]Clarify|date=October 2013|reason=Should this be '101st', '107th' or something else?[/tpl] in the UK single album list. The song has been featured in the soundtracks of the Tony Hawk's Underground in its original Flash Flash Flash version  and Midnight Club 3: DUB Edition in its Black Tape version.
