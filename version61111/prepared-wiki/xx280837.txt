[[Gol Tappeh, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Gol Tappeh ([tpl]lang-fa|گل تپه[/tpl]; also known as Gul Tepe)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3064707|Gol Tappeh[/tpl] is a village in Bughda Kandi Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 857, in 191 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
