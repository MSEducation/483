[[Methanogenium marinum]]

CATEGORIES: Archaea species

Methanogenium marinum is a psychrophilic, H2-using methanogen from Skan Bay, Alaska.[tpl]cite journal|last1=Chong|first1=Song C.|last2=Liu|first2=Yitai|last3=Cummins|first3=Michael|last4=Valentine|first4=David L.|last5=Boone|first5=David R.|journal=Antonie van Leeuwenhoek|volume=81|issue=1|year=2002|pages=263–270|issn=00036072|doi=10.1023/A:1020535222281[/tpl] Its cells are highly irregular, non-motile coccoids (diameter, 1 to 1.2 μm), occurring singly. AK-1 is its type strain.
