[[Little Belize]]

CATEGORIES: Mennonitism in Belize, Populated places in Corozal District

Little Belize is a colony of conservative Mennonites, known as "Old Colony Mennonites", in the Corozal District of the nation of Belize. Little Belize is located at 18N, 88W, at an elevation of 1 meter above sea level. The population is mostly Mennonite. According to the 2000 census, Little Belize had a population of 2,059 people.
