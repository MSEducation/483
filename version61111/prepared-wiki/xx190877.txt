[[Luigi Cecchini]]

CATEGORIES: 1944 births, Living people, Italian cycling coaches, Italian physicians, People from Ferrara

Luigi Cecchini (born 1944) is an Italian sports doctor that is active in road bicycle racing. He is well known as maker of training schemes that he writes for his clients as well as use of the SRM cycle computer. 
Cecchini is a former motor-racing pilot and the son of a millionaire shirt manufacturer, who had specialized as a sports scientist under Francesco Conconi.
