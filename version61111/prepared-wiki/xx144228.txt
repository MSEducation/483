[[Antonio Smith (defensive end)]]

CATEGORIES: 1981 births, Living people, Sportspeople from Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, Players of American football from Oklahoma, American football defensive ends, Oklahoma State Cowboys football players, Arizona Cardinals players, Hamburg Sea Devils players, Houston Texans players, Oakland Raiders players, American Conference Pro Bowl players

Antonio DeShonta Smith (born October 21, 1981), nicknamed the Ninja Assassin, is an American football defensive end for the Oakland Raiders of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football for Oklahoma State University, and was drafted by the Arizona Cardinals in the fifth round of the 2004 NFL Draft. He has also played for the Houston Texans. He graduated from John Marshall High School (Oklahoma). 

==College career==

He played college football at Oklahoma State. He was an honorable mention All-Big 12 in his junior season at Oklahoma State.[tpl]cite web | title=Oklahoma State Profile  | work= okstate.com | url=http://www.okstate.com/sports/m-footbl/spec-rel/070303aac.html |accessdate=[/tpl]

==Professional career==

===Arizona Cardinals===

Smith was drafted 135th overall in the 2004 NFL Draft and was the starting left defensive end.
During the 2008 NFL Season, Smith played in every game and had 2 forced fumbles and 3.5 sacks.
Despite a great performance during the course of the season, the Cardinals chose not to re-sign him and let him go into free agency.

===Houston Texans===

Smith was signed as an unrestricted free agent by the Houston Texans to replace the released Anthony Weaver.
In 2011, Smith went to his first career Pro Bowl, replacing the New England Patriots' Andre Carter who was unable to play due to injury.[tpl]cite web | title= Texans C Myers, DE Smith added to Pro Bowl roster | work= abclocal.go.com | url=http://abclocal.go.com/ktrk/story?section=news/sports/pro/football&id=8511838 |accessdate=January 19, 2012[/tpl]
In 2013, Smith was suspended for week one after an incident in the preseason in which he removed Miami Dolphins lineman Richie Incognito's helmet and swung it at him.[tpl]cite web|last=McIntyre|first=Brian|url=http://sports.yahoo.com/blogs/nfl-shutdown-corner/report-texans-antonio-smith-suspended-week-1-helmet-174519695.html|title=Report: Texans DE Antonio Smith to be suspended for Week 1 after helmet swinging incident|publisher=Yahoo! Sports|date=August 20, 2013|accessdate=August 21, 2013[/tpl] On November 29, 2013, he was fined $15,750 for hitting Jacksonville Jaguars' quarterback Chad Henne in the head and neck region.

===Oakland Raiders===

On March 14, 2014, Smith signed a two year, $9 million contract with the Oakland Raiders.[tpl]cite web|last=Schefter|first=Adam|title=Former Texans DE Antonio Smith just signed a 2-year, $9M deal with the Raiders.|url=https://twitter.com/AdamSchefter/status/444619349781798912|publisher=Twitter|accessdate=14 March 2014[/tpl]
