Clickatell is a privately held mobile communications company founded in 2000 in South Africa and headquartered in Redwood City, California, USA, with offices in Cape Town and Johannesburg, South Africa[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.crunchbase.com/company/clickatell-inc |title=Clickatell | CrunchBase Profile |publisher=Crunchbase.com |date=2011-04-08 |accessdate=2011-10-13[/tpl] and Lagos, Nigeria.[tpl]cite web|url=http://mobilemoneyafrica.com/interoperability-will-unlock-the-potentials-of-mobile-financial-services-in-africa-pieter-de-villiers/ |title=Interoperability will unlock the potentials of mobile financial services in Africa – Pieter de Villiers |publisher=Mobile Money Africa |date=2011-07-12 |accessdate=2011-10-13[/tpl] Clickatell delivers short message service (SMS) messages through its Clickatell Gateway[tpl]cite web|url=http://investing.businessweek.com/businessweek/research/stocks/private/snapshot.asp?privcapId=8200329 |title=Clickatell (Pty) Ltd.: Private Company Information - BusinessWeek |publisher=Investing.businessweek.com |date= |accessdate=2011-10-13[/tpl] to mobile phone users through more than 800 networks in more than 220 countries and territories.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.xconomy.com/san-francisco/2011/02/28/clickatell-garners-12000000-series-b-funding-round/ |title=Clickatell Garners $12,000,000 Series B Funding Round |publisher=Xconomy |date=2011-02-28 |accessdate=2011-10-13[/tpl] Clickatell’s customer base includes more than 10,000 small, medium and large enterprises in industries that need software as a service (SaaS) messaging services and bulk SMS gateway connectivity.

==History==

The company was founded to address a need in South Africa for an SMS provider that could enable the sending of multiple texts to different countries from a single website. It was founded by Pieter DE Villiers in Cape Town, South Africa. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.gomonews.com/apps-are-no-threat-to-sms-clickatell-on-why-mobile-text-will-never-die/ |title=Apps are no threat to SMS - Clickatell on why mobile text will never die |publisher=Gomonews.com |date=2010-09-20 |accessdate=2011-10-13[/tpl]
In 2006, the company acquired the assets of Multimode, Inc., an application service provider that offers global delivery of time-critical notifications to communication devices of all types,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.mobileafrica.net/clickatell-merges-with-multimode-inc-moves-headquarters-to-u-s-and-expands-the-reach-of-messaging-and-alerts-to-all-communication-devices-around-the-globe/ |title=Clickatell Merges with MultiMode, Inc.; Moves Headquarters to U.S. and Expands the Reach of Messaging and Alerts to All Communication Devices Around the Globe |publisher=Mobile Africa |date=2006-04-15 |accessdate=2011-10-13[/tpl] and established its headquarters in Redwood City, California. In 2008 Clickatell received venture capital funding from Sequoia Capital and became a USA private company backed by large private equity investors.
In 2009, Clickatell provided the U.S. Department of State (USDOS) with a mobile message service to enable it to give African and international/non-U.S. citizens around the world live speech highlights during a speech given by U.S. President Barack Obama from Accra, Ghana. In addition, enrolled participants were able to send comments via text message back to the USDOS, where selected responses were posted online.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.america.gov/ghana_comments.html |title=Comments to President Obama |publisher=America.gov |date= |accessdate=2011-10-13[/tpl][tpl]cite web|last=Butcher |first=Dan |url=http://www.mobilemarketer.com/cms/news/messaging/3674.html |title=Obama’s Ghana speech highlights delivered live via SMS - Mobile Marketer - Messaging |publisher=Mobile Marketer |date=2009-07-15 |accessdate=2011-10-13[/tpl] Clickatell’s involvement in this service resulted in a World Technology Award nomination from the World Technology Network in association with TIME magazine, Fortune Magazine, and Science Magazine for work of "the greatest likely long-term significance" in Communications and Technology Sector.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.wtn.net/summit2009/ |title=The World Technology Summit & Awards 2009 |publisher=Wtn.net |date= |accessdate=2011-10-13[/tpl]

==Technology==

Clickatell’s SMS gateway enables businesses, financial institutions, governments, and communities to use mobile messaging to aid their businesses.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.mobilevillage.com/yp/clickatell.htm |title=PDAs, Handhelds, Mobile Computing & Wireless Technology |publisher=MobileVillage |date= |accessdate=2011-10-13[/tpl]
Integration with front-end and legacy systems is carried out via application programming interface (API) connections. These offer developers a choice of connection options (HTTP/S, SMPP, SMTP, FTP, XML, SOAP).[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.programmableweb.com/api/clickatell |title=Clickatell API Profile |publisher=Programmableweb.com |date= |accessdate=2011-10-13[/tpl] Developer support includes developer forums, 24/7 phone and email support, and script libraries available for PHP, VB.Net, VBScript, Cold Fusion, and C#.[tpl]cite web|author=Nathan Eddy |url=http://www.eweek.com/c/a/Midmarket/Clickatell-ChamberofCommercecom-Offer-Connectivity-Platform-for-Businesses-293842/ |title=Clickatell, ChamberofCommerce.com Offer Connectivity Platform for Businesses |publisher=Eweek.com |date=2011-04-25 |accessdate=2013-03-22[/tpl]
