[[Zimbor]]

CATEGORIES: Communes in Sălaj County

Zimbor ([tpl]lang-hu|Magyarzsombor[/tpl]) is a commune located in Sălaj County, Romania. It is composed of five villages: Chendremal (Kendermál), Dolu (Almásdál), Sutoru (Zutor), Sâncraiu Almaşului (Topaszentkirály) and Zimbor.
