[[Topdalsfjorden]]

CATEGORIES: Fjords of Vest-Agder, Kristiansand

Topdalsfjorden is a fjord in the municipality of Kristiansand in Vest-Agder, Norway. The  river Tovdalselva mouths into the fjord at the eastern side.
