[[Castellgalí]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities in Bages

Castellgalí is a municipality in the comarca of the Bages in 
Catalonia, Spain. It is situated at the confluence of the Llobregat river and the Cardener river. 
The railway station serves both the FGC line R5 between Barcelona and 
Manresa and the RENFE line between Barcelona and Zaragoza.
The ruins of a Roman tomb (third century, known locally as la Torre del Breny or "The Devil's Tower") are visible.
