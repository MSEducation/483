[[Waste 'Em All]]

CATEGORIES: Municipal Waste (band) albums, 2003 debut albums, 2003 EPs, Debut EPs

Waste 'Em All is the debut album by thrash metal band Municipal Waste. This album caused the band to receive the attention of Earache Records, which the band signed to in May 2004.[tpl]cite book| last = Sharpe-Young| first = Garry| title = New Wave of American Heavy Metal| url = http://books.google.com/?id=uIIf03bGyAAC&pg=PA214| accessdate = July 10, 2009| date = 2005| publisher = Zonda Books Limited| isbn = 0-9582684-0-1| page = 214 [/tpl] The album's name pays homage to Metallica's debut album, Kill 'em All.
