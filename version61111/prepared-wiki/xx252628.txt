[[Brick Store Building]]

CATEGORIES: Commercial buildings on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Federal architecture in New York, Commercial buildings completed in 1807, Buildings and structures in Oneida County, New York

Brick Store Building is a historic commercial building located at Bridgewater in Oneida County, New York. It was built in 1807 and consists of a two story, gable roofed, rectangular brick main block with a two story, wood frame rear wing in a vernacular Federal style.  The entire building rests on a limestone foundation.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=6136|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: Brick Store Building|date=March 1996|accessdate=2010-01-08 |author=Raymond W. Smith|publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation[/tpl] See also: [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=6138|title=Accompanying three photos[/tpl] 
It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1996.
