Gmina Olszanka is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Brzeg County, Opole Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Its seat is the village of Olszanka, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|10|km|mi|0[/tpl] south of Brzeg and [tpl]convert|35|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] west of the regional capital Opole.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|92.61|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 4,943.

==Villages==

Gmina Olszanka contains the villages and settlements of Czeska Wieś, Gierszowice, Jankowice Wielkie, Janów, Krzyżowice, Michałów, Obórki, Olszanka, Pogorzela and Przylesie.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Olszanka is bordered by the gminas of Grodków, Lewin Brzeski, Niemodlin, Skarbimierz and Wiązów.
