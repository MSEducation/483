[[Roman Novotný]]

CATEGORIES: 1986 births, Living people, Czech long jumpers, Athletes (track and field) at the 2008 Summer Olympics, Athletes (track and field) at the 2012 Summer Olympics, Olympic athletes of the Czech Republic

Roman Novotný ([tpl]IPA-cs|ˈroman ˈnovotniː[/tpl]; born 5 January 1986) is a Czech long jumper.
He finished seventh at the 2003 World Youth Championships, won the silver medal at the 2007 Summer Universiade and finished eighth at the 2008 Olympic Games.
His personal best jump is 8.21 metres, achieved in July 2008 in Brno.
