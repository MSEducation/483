[[Noriah Kasnon]]

CATEGORIES: Malaysian politicians, Malaysian Malay people, Malaysian Muslims, Living people, Members of the Dewan Rakyat, United Malays National Organisation politicians, Year of birth missing (living people)

Noriah Kasnon is the Member of the Parliament of Malaysia for the Sungai Besar constituency in Selangor, Malaysia. She is a member of the United Malay National Organisation (UMNO) party and is a Deputy Minister in the Barisan Nasional government of Najib Tun Razak.
Noriah was elected to Parliament in the 2004 election for the newly created seat of Sungai Besar.[ref]
