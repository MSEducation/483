[[Shaw Mansion (Barton, Maryland)]]

CATEGORIES: Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Maryland, Italianate architecture in Maryland, Houses completed in 1872, Houses in Allegany County, Maryland

The Shaw Mansion is an Italianate style house in the George's Creek Valley of Allegany County, Maryland, built in 1872. The house is significant as an unusually large and well-preserved example of the style for its area, with stone trim, detailed brick bonding, cast-iron mantels and much of the original interior woodwork.[tpl]cite web|url=http://mht.maryland.gov/nr/NRDetail.aspx?HDID=886&COUNTY=Allegany&FROM=NRCountyList.aspx?COUNTY=Allegany|title=Maryland Historical Trust|date=2008-10-06|work= National Register of Historic Places: Properties in Allegany County|publisher=Maryland Historical Trust[/tpl]
It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1985.
