[[I'm Doin' Fine Now]]

CATEGORIES: 1973 singles, 1988 singles, 1973 songs, RCA Records singles

I'm Doin' Fine Now is a 1973 song by New York City. It made #14 on the R&B Singles,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/new-york-city-mn0000398716/awards|title=New York City [tpl]![/tpl] Awards|publisher=AllMusic|accessdate=Sunday, 11 August 2013[/tpl] #17 on the Billboard Hot 100, #20 on the UK Singles Chart[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.officialcharts.com/artist/_/new%20york%20city/|title=NEW YORK CITY|publisher=The Official Charts Company|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] and #46 on the Billboard Year-End Hot 100 singles of 1973.[tpl]cite web|url=http://longboredsurfer.com/charts/1973.php|title=Billboard Top 100 - 1973|accessdate=2011-01-02[/tpl]

==The Pasadenas cover==

In 1988, The Pasadenas did a cover of this song which, in the UK, out-peaked the original by 15 places, charting at #5[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.officialcharts.com/artist/_/pasadenas/|title=PASADENAS|publisher=The Official Charts Company|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] selling over 200,000 copies in the process.[tpl]cite web |first= |last= |url=[tpl]Allmusic|class=artist|id=p26834/biography|pure_url=yes[/tpl] |title=Biography by Michael Sutton |publisher=Allmusic.com |accessdate=26 March 2009[/tpl]
