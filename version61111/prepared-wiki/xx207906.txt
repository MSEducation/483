[[Jørgen Fredrik Spørck]]

CATEGORIES: 1787 births, 1866 deaths, Deputy members of the Parliament of Norway, Mayors of places in Sogn og Fjordane, Norwegian Army personnel

Jørgen Fredrik Spørck (1787–1866) was a Norwegian military officer and politician.
As a politician,  mayor in Stryn from 1842 to 1843. He was also a deputy representative to the Norwegian Parliament.NRK Sogn og Fjordane County Encyclopedia
