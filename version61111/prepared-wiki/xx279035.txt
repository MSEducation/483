[[Robin Biddulph]]

CATEGORIES: Australian swimmers, Swimmers at the 1938 British Empire Games

Robin Biddulph was an Australian swimmer who specialized in freestyle. He was the Australian national swimming champion and an Australian and New South Wales record holderhttp://trove.nla.gov.au/ndp/del/article/49345627?searchTerm=biddulph&searchLimits= and a bronze medalist in the British Empire Games in 1938.
