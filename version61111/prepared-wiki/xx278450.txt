[[General Blood]]

CATEGORIES: Medical and health organizations

General Blood is a FDA registered and AABB accredited distributor of human blood, platelets, and plasma for hospitals, non-transfusion facilities, and group-purchasing organizations.[tpl]cite web| url=http://csom.umn.edu/news/07/24/12/Expediting-the-Blood-Flow-.aspx|title=Expediting the Blood Flow|date=July 24, 2012|accessdate=August 5, 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web| url=http://deltaskymag.delta.com/Destinations/Minneapolis/Destination-Posts/David-Mitchell.aspx|title=David Mitchell on General Blood[/tpl][tpl]cite web| url=http://tcbmag.com/Industries/Health-Care/General-Blood-Aims-to-Standardize-Pricing-in-the-B|title=General Blood Aims to Standardize Pricing In The Blood Business|author=Ingrid Case|publisher=Twin Cities Business|date=March 27, 2013|accessdate=August 5, 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web| url=http://minnesota.publicradio.org/display/web/2012/07/03/daily-circuit-blood-supply-distribution-general-blood|title=Minn. Company rethinking blood distribution business|date=July 3, 2012|accessdate=August 5, 2013[/tpl] General Blood's process is designed to balance excesses and shortages of blood by improving supply chain efficiencies and logistical models within the blood distribution industry.[tpl]cite web| url=http://www.forbes.com/sites/erincarlyle/2012/06/27/blood-money-the-guys-who-trade-your-blood-for-profit/|title=The Guys Who Trade Your Blood For Profit|author=Erin Carlyle|publisher=Forbes|date=June 27, 2012|accessdate=August 5, 2013[/tpl] The company’s goal is to redistribute blood supplies, increase competition in the distribution market, and lower the cost of health care in the United States. General Blood was established by creating an online marketplace where medical centers are able to post excess quantities of blood for sale and place orders to replenish blood supplies.

==Overview==

General Blood was founded by David Mitchell and Ben Bowman, who both received MBA’s from the Carlson School of Management at the University of Minnesota. Bowman began researching the blood distribution industry in 2007 while still enrolled in the MBA program. General Blood LLC was created in 2010 and is headquartered in downtown Minneapolis, Minnesota on the Mississippi River. Ben Bowman is Chief Executive Officer at General Blood, and David Mitchell is the Executive Vice President.[tpl]cite web| url=https://gust.com/c/generalblood|title=General Blood, LLC[/tpl] In 2013, General Blood was an exhibitor for the Clinical Laboratory Management Association.[tpl]cite web| url=http://www.clma.org/?page=13Exhibit_List|title=2013 Exhibitor Listing[/tpl] In 2012, General Blood was a Minnesota Cup Semi-Finalist in the Bio-Science and Health IT Division.[tpl]cite web| url=http://breakthroughideas.org/2012-minnesota-cup-semi-finalists|title=Announcing the 2012 Minnesota Cup Semi-finalists[/tpl]
