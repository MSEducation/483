[[Cardston-Chief Mountain]]

CATEGORIES: Former Alberta provincial electoral districts

Cardston-Chief Mountain was a provincial electoral district in Alberta, Canada.
The riding was created in 1993 when the boundaries for Cardston were re-drawn after Pincher Creek-Crowsnest moved south.
The riding was merged with Taber-Warner in 1997 to form the riding Cardston-Taber-Warner.
