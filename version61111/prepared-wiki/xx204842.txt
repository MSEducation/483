[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Goiás]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1745, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Goiânia, Dioceses established in the 18th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Goiás ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Goiasensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Goiás in the Ecclesiastical province of Goiânia in Brazil.
