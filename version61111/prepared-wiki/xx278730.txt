[[Ceratophysa rosea]]

CATEGORIES: Echinoidea, Animals described in 1879

Ceratophysa rosea is a species of sea urchins of the Family Pourtalesiidae. Their armour is covered with spines. Ceratophysa rosea was first scientifically described in 1879 by Alexander Emanuel Agassiz.Kroh, A. (2010). Ceratophysa rosea (Alexander Emanuel Agassiz, 1879). In: Kroh, A. & Mooi, R. (2010) World Echinoidea Database. at the World Register of Marine Species.
