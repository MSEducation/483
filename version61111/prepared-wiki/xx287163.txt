[[Owch Tappeh, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Owch Tappeh ([tpl]lang-fa|اوچ تپه[/tpl], also Romanized as Uch-Tapa, Ūch Tappeh, and Uchtepe)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3087924|Owch Tappeh[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Bala Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 705, in 184 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
