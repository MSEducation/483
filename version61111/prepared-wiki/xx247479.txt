[[Gustaf Spetz]]

CATEGORIES: Swedish male singers, Swedish indie rock groups, Musical groups established in 2008

Gustaf Spetz is a Swedish singer. He started the band Eskju Divine along with two friends in Norrköping in 2002. The band went on to release two EP's and two albums, tour Japan and open for The Smashing Pumpkins on two dateshttp://www.nt.se/arkiv/2007/08/11/N%F6je/1688956/Eskju-f%F6rband-till-Smashing.aspx before calling it quits sometime around 2008.
His solo debut album Good Night Mr Spetz was officially released on January 28, 2009.http://othersidedmusic.wordpress.com/2009/01/27/gustaf-spetz/ 

Gustav Spetz describes his music as "A Ted Gärdestad on drugs that has listened too much to the Beach Boys and Beatles.” http://meadowmusic.se/en/?p=216

He toured with Jonathan Johansson in 2009,http://meadowmusic.se/en/?p=216 did a few gigs backing Serenades (Markus Krunegård's (of Laakso) project with Adam Olenius (of Shout out Louds)) in 2011, and is accompanying Tomas Andersson Wij for his acoustic tour in 2013.http://www.nt.se/nyheter/artikel.aspx?articleid=8384285
