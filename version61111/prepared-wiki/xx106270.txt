[[Richard Oakes (guitarist)]]

CATEGORIES: 1976 births, Living people, English songwriters, English rock guitarists, Suede (band) members, Britpop musicians, Alternative rock guitarists, Musicians from London, People from Poole

Richard Oakes (born Richard John Oakes 1 October 1976 in Perivale, London) is an English musician and songwriter, best known as the guitarist and occasional pianist and backing vocalist of the English band Suede.

==Early life==

Richard Oakes was born in Perivale, London, and grew up in the Parkstone area of Poole, Dorset, in the southwest of England.[ref]
