[[Kuznetsov]]

CATEGORIES: Occupational surnames, Russian-language surnames

Kuznetsov, Kuznyetsov, Kuznetsoff, or Kouznetsov (masculine, [tpl]lang-ru|Кузнецо́в[/tpl]) or Kuznetsova (feminine, [tpl]lang-ru|Кузнецо́ва[/tpl]) is the third most common Russian surname, an equivalent of the English "Smith" (derived from a Russian word kuznets that means blacksmith).
