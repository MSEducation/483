[[2002 UEFA Intertoto Cup]]

CATEGORIES: UEFA Intertoto Cup, 2002–03 in European football

The 2002 UEFA Intertoto Cup finals were won by Málaga, Fulham, and Stuttgart. All three teams advanced to the UEFA Cup.
