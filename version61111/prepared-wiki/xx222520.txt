[[Michael Newton (field hockey)]]

CATEGORIES: 1952 births, American field hockey players, Sportspeople from British Columbia, American people of Canadian descent, Canadian field hockey players, People from Penticton, Living people

Michael Douglas Newton (born May 10, 1952 in Penticton, British Columbia, Canada) was an American field hockey player, who competed at the 1984 Summer Olympics in Los Angeles. His brother Gary Newton was one of his Olympic teammates.
