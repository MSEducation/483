Gregg Burnett (born 24 May 1987) is a Scottish footballer who plays in midfield for junior side Armadale Thistle, having started his career with Dundee United.

==Career==

Burnett was born at Bangour, West Lothian and grew up in Livingston, where he attended Deans Community High School. He initially became attached to Dundee United at under-14 level.
