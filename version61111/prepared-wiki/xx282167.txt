Pioneer ([tpl]lang-no|Pionér[/tpl]) is a 2013 Norwegian thriller film directed by Erik Skjoldbjærg. It was released on 30 August, followed by a screening in the Special Presentation section at the 2013 Toronto International Film Festival.[tpl]cite web |url=http://tiff.net/filmsandschedules/festival/2013/pioneer |title=Pioneer |accessdate=2013-08-10 |work=TIFF[/tpl]
