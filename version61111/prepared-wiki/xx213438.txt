[[São Vicente, Cape Verde (municipality)]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Cape Verde

São Vicente is a concelho (municipality) of Cape Verde. It covers the whole island of São Vicente and the uninhabited island Santa Luzia. Its capital is the city Mindelo. ==History==

In older times it belonged to the older Municipality of Santo Antão, but by the beginning of the 20th century it was already a separated municipality. Later, the island of Santa Luzia that belonged to the older Municipality of São Nicolau, was integrated in the Municipality of São Vicente.[tpl]cn|date=March 2014[/tpl]
