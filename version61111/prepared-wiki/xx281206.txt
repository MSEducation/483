[[Marc Sorenson]]

CATEGORIES: Brigham Young University alumni, Living people

Dr. Marc Sorenson is a doctor of education with a background in health and fitness.  He graduated from Brigham Young University in 1973.  He and his wife, Vicki, founded and developed National Institute of Fitness in 1974.[tpl]cite web|url=http://articles.chicagotribune.com/1992-02-02/travel/9201100433_1_spa-nif-guests |title=No-frills Utah Spa Motivates Guests To Shed Pounds - Chicago Tribune |publisher=Articles.chicagotribune.com |date=1992-02-02 |accessdate=2013-09-02[/tpl] Dr. Sorenson and Vicki sold the resort to Franklin Quest (now Franklin Covey) corporation in 1994. They are currently directors of another health resort called National Institute of Health and Fitness (NIHF) in Midway, Utah.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.nihf.com/team/national-institute-of-health-and-fitness |title=Team | National Institute of Health & Fitness | Weight Loss Camp |publisher=Nihf.com |date= |accessdate=2013-09-02[/tpl]
Dr. Sorenson has published a scientific paper in the journal Dermatoendocrinology, on Vitamin D and erectile dysfunction (ED), theorizing that lack of vitamin D or sunlight could be a contributor to ED.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.landesbioscience.com/journals/dermatoendocrinology/article/20361/ |title=Dermato-Endocrinology: Review |doi=10.4161/derm.20361 |publisher=Landesbioscience.com |date= |accessdate=2013-09-02[/tpl] He was also co-author of a scientific paper on vitamin D and athletics,[tpl]cite web|author= |url=http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/19346976 |title=Athletic performance and vitamin D. Sci Sports Exerc. 2009 - PubMed - NCBI |publisher=Ncbi.nlm.nih.gov |date=2013-03-25 |accessdate=2013-09-02[/tpl] published in Science and Medicine in Exercise and Sports, and coauthored another paper in the Journal of Sexual Medicine regarding ED, chronic periodontitis and vitamin D.[tpl]cite web|author= |url=http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/23845094 |title=Vitamin D Deficiency May Contribute to the Explana... Sex Med. 2013 - PubMed - NCBI |publisher=Ncbi.nlm.nih.gov |date=2013-03-25 |accessdate=2013-09-02[/tpl]
His book Megahealth was a selection of the Literary Guild, Doubleday Book Club and Doubleday Health Book Club.[tpl]cite web|url=http://preventbc.org/?page_id=53 |title=Our Stories | bcnpf |publisher=Preventbc.org |date= |accessdate=2013-09-02[/tpl]  He has done meticulous and extensive research into hundreds of published papers on sunlight, vitamin D and health in order to write his current book, Vitamin D3 and Solar Power.[tpl]cite web|author= |url=http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/19346976 |title=Athletic performance and vitamin D.
