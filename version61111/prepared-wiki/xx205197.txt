[[Kathy Miles]]

CATEGORIES: Members of the South Dakota House of Representatives, Living people, Women state legislators in South Dakota, South Dakota State Senators

Kathleen "Kathy" Miles is an American politician who served in both houses of the South Dakota Legislature. A Democrat, she represented District 15 in the South Dakota House of Representatives from 2003 to 2009. She then served a single term in the South Dakota Senate (2009 to 2011) before being defeated for re-election in the Democratic primary held on June 8, 2010. In that contest, Miles received 276 votes while Angie Buhl received 398.
