[[Cockos]]

CATEGORIES: Companies based in San Francisco, California

Cockos, Inc is an American digital audio technology company, most notable for their digital audio workstation software REAPER.
The company's stated goal is "to develop software sustainably while preventing profit rationale from forcing engineering compromises. By doing so, we can keep our product visions intact, giving maximum benefit to our users." ==History==

Cockos was founded in 2004 by Justin Frankel after his departure from Nullsoft. The company name stems from mis-hearing a quote from the movie Office Space.[tpl]cite news| url=http://www.wired.com/politics/onlinerights/commentary/listeningpost/2006/10/71876 | work=Wired | title=Justin Frankel Rocks On | date=October 2, 2006[/tpl]
While the company also develops small software tools often released under an open source licensehttp://www.cockos.com/products.php its main focus is on music software. They released their first music software product, a programmable effects processing software called Jesusonic, on December 21, 2004.http://www.jesusonic.com/index.php Following up in 2005, they released the online music jam software NINJAM under the GPL,http://www.ninjam.com/index.php?skip=6 with the release of their flagship product REAPER following later that year.

==Staff==

Cockos currently has three programmers.http://www.cockos.com/team.php Justin Frankel is an American programmer best known for his work on the Winamp media player application. Christophe Thibault, who joined Cockos in 2005, is a French programmer and was the founder of the Kaillera and K-Meleon projects. John Schwartz joined in 2008; he is the author of many audio plug-ins, notably a virtual analogue synthesizer called Olga.
