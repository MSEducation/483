[[Mehrinabad]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Mehrinabad ([tpl]lang-fa|مهرين اباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Mehrīnābād; also known as Mehrānābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3074687|Mehrinabad[/tpl] is a village in Aq Bolagh Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 160, in 36 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
