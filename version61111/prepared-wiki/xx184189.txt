[[Withrotor Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in Lake County, Oregon

Withrotor Airport [tpl]Airport codes|||22OG[/tpl] is a private Airport located 6 miles northwest of Lakeview in Lake County, Oregon, USA. It is the principal operations base of Withrotor Aviation, Inc. - a utility helicopter company that specializes in precision lifting operations such as aerial firefighting, powerline and general construction, aircraft retrieval, aerial tree harvesting, aerial mapping, frost control and various helicopter applications.
