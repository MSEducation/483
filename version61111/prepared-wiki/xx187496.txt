[[Madison Browne]]

Madison Robinson (nee Browne) (born 5 January 1988) is an Australian international netball player. Browne was included in the Australian national squad in 2007 after the World Championships in November.[tpl]cite news |last=Green |first=Bradley |title=Sharelle McMahon talks up Madison Browne's netball future |publisher=Geelong Advertiser |url=http://www.geelongadvertiser.com.au/article/2008/03/06/11912_geelong_sports.html |date=2008-03-06 |accessdate=2008-06-18[/tpl] She debuted in the Commonwealth Bank Trophy in 2006, playing with the Melbourne Kestrels. In 2008, Browne signed with the Melbourne Vixens to play in the ANZ Championship.2008 Melbourne Vixens profile. Retrieved on 2008-06-18. For the 2009 season, Browne transferred to the West Coast Fever.2009 West Coast Fever profile. Retrieved on 2009-03-12. In 2011, Browne transferred back to the Melbourne Vixens.
In 2010, Browne was selected to be part of the Diamonds squad during the England tour, replacing Lauren Nourse due to commitments. In one of the matches, Browne was awarded player of the match. Browne was also part of the 15 player squad, but didn't make the final 12 player squad.
