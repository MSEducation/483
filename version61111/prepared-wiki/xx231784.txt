[[Alpiglen railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in the canton of Bern, Grindelwald

Alpiglen is a request stop railway station in the municipality of Grindelwald in the Swiss canton of Bern. The station is served by the Wengernalpbahn (WAB), whose trains operate from Grindelwald to Kleine Scheidegg.
