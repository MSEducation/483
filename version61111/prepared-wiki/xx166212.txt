[[Central City Value]]

CATEGORIES: Los Angeles Unified School District schools, Los Angeles Unified School District, High schools in Los Angeles, California, Public high schools in California, Charter schools in California

Central City Value High School or CCVHS is an alternative charter high school of the Los Angeles Unified School District located in Los Angeles, California with approximately 400 students. The school offers education to students in grades 9-12.  The school year ends June 12.
