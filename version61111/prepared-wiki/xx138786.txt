[[Kevin Mullaney]]

CATEGORIES: American darts players, Living people, Year of birth missing (living people), British Darts Organisation players

Kevin Mullaney is a retired American professional darts player who competed in the 1980s. He competed in the 1982 World Masters, losing in the first round to John Joe O'Shea. He also played in the 1983 BDO World Darts Championship, but was defeated in the first round by Swedish player Stefan Lord.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.dartnet.nl/toernooien/e1983.php |title=Embassy World Professional Darts Championship 1983 |work=Dartnet.nl |accessdate=2009-03-05 [/tpl]
