[[Valtteri Virkkunen]]

CATEGORIES: 1991 births, Living people, Espoo Blues players, People from Espoo

Valtteri Virkkunen (born February 18, 1991) is a Finnish professional ice hockey forward who currently plays for Espoo Blues of the SM-liiga.Espoo Blues 2013-14 Roster He was involved in the 2011 World Junior Ice Hockey Championships in Buffalo, New York, U.S.A..
