[[Platinum Collection (Željko Joksimović album)]]

CATEGORIES: Željko Joksimović albums, 2007 greatest hits albums

Platinum Collection is the second greatest hits album by Serbian singer, songwriter and producer Željko Joksimović. The album was released on April 18, 2007 and contains 16 platinum hits from Joksimović's career plus two new songs: "Nije Do Mene" and "Devojka". The single "Devojka" charted very well, and got on the first place in most countries from the former Yugoslavia where this album was actually released. In the album is included the song "Lane Moje" too. With this song Željko represented Serbia and Montenegro in the Eurovision Song Contest 2004 and finished on the 2nd place.
