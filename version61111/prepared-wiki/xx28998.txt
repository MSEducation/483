[[Fucanglong]]

CATEGORIES: Chinese mythology, Chinese dragons

In Chinese mythology the Fucanglong ([tpl]zh|s=伏藏龙|t=伏藏龍|p=Fúcánglóng[/tpl]) or Fu-ts'ang-Lung (Wade-Giles) is the Chinese underworld dragon which guard buried treasures, both natural and man-made. Volcanoes are said to be created when they burst out of the ground to report to heaven.
Futs-lung possesses a magic pearl which is his most treasured possession
