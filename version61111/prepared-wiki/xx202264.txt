Richard Soumah (born 6 October 1986) is a Guinean international footballer, who currently plays as a midfielder for Mons in the Belgian Pro League. Born in Créteil, he started his career at Rennes, before moving to Guingamp in 2004. He made his professional debut in the 2005–06 season.
