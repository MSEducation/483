[[(10737) 1988 DZ4]]

CATEGORIES: Astronomical objects discovered in 1988, Discoveries by Robert H. McNaught, Mars-crossing asteroids

(10737) 1988 DZ4 is a Mars-crossing minor planet.JPL Small-Body Database Browser  It was discovered by Robert H. McNaught at the Siding Spring Observatory in Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia, on February 25, 1988.Discovery Circumstances: Numbered Minor Planets (10001)-(15000)
