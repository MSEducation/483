[[Mount Monbetsu]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of Hokkaido

[tpl]nihongo|Mount Monbetsu|紋別岳|Mombetsu-dake[/tpl] is a mountain located in Shikotsu-Toya National Park in Hokkaidō, Japan. It sits on the shore of Lake Shikotsu, a caldera lake. It also hosts a radio relay station.Geographical Survey Institute> website, 25000:1 map 支笏湖温泉

==Climbing Route==

There is a road from Lake Shikotsu shore to the top of the mountain.Paul Hunt, Hiking in Japan: An Adventurer's Guide to the Mountain Trails, pg. 181
