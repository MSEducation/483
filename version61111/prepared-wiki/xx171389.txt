[[Gretzky (album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2006 debut albums, Wayne Gretzky, Heavy metal albums by Canadian artists

Gretzky is the debut full-length album by Canadian instrumental progressive metal band Electro Quarterstaff.  It is named after hockey legend Wayne Gretzky Mikkelson, Jill (December 2006). "Electro Quarterstaff: Gretzky", Exclaim!. Retrieved September 15, 2013. and follows the trend of naming their releases as the last name of a male celebrity, begun on their first EP, Swayze (named for Patrick Swayze) and continued with the release of their second LP Aykroyd.
