[[Nanyang, Shanghang County]]

CATEGORIES: Geography of Fujian

Nanyang ([tpl]zh|t=南陽|s=南阳[/tpl]) is a Town in Shanghang County, Longyan, Fujian.

==Administration==

The town executive, party subbranch and police substation (paichusuo, 派出所) are in Shaxiaba (沙下坝). The town oversees twenty official villages:http://baike.baidu.com/view/402170.htm#2
