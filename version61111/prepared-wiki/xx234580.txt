[[Frank H. Shaw]]

CATEGORIES: 1882 births, 1950 deaths, American engineers, Bridge engineers, People from Lancaster, Pennsylvania

Frank Harold Shaw (1882 – May 14, 1950) was an American civil engineer notable for designing bridges and water supply infrastructure in Lancaster County, Pennsylvania.“Frank H. Shaw Dies; Was Former Water Works Supt.” Obituary in Lancaster Intelligencer Journal, May 15, 1950. Two water towers that he designed, affectionately named "George and Martha" by local residents, were landmarks on the Lancaster skyline until their demolition in 1996.[tpl]cite web|author=Franklin and Marshall College Archives|title=History of Campus Buildings and Landmarks|url=http://library.fandm.edu/archives/collegearc/buildingslist.html#W|accessdate=2009-10-16[/tpl]
