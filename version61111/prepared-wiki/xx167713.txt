[[Margaret Slade]]

CATEGORIES: Comics characters introduced in 2006, Fictional shapeshifters, Marvel Comics mutants, Marvel Comics supervillains

Margaret Slade is a fictional character and mutant in the Marvel Comics universe. She makes her first appearance in X-Men: Apocalypse vs. Dracula #2.

==Fictional character biography==

==Powers and abilities==

Margaret like many of Apocalypse's descendents possess a degree of molecular rearrangement. She usually uses this ability to transform her hands into weapons.
