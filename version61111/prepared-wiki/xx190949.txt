[[Finland at the 2008 Summer Olympics]]

CATEGORIES: Finland at the 2008 Summer Olympics, Nations at the 2008 Summer Olympics, Finland at the Olympics, 2008 in Finnish sport

Finland competed at the 2008 Summer Olympics in Beijing, People's Republic of China. The athletes were named in four selections: first took place on December 19, 2007, 2nd on April 16, 3rd on May 20 and 4th on July 21, 2008.
The goal of the Finnish Olympic team in the Beijing 2008 Olympic Games was three medals with at least one gold medal.http://www.noc.fi/in_english/?x126=1035599
Shooter Juha Hirvi and dressage rider Kyra Kyrklund made Finnish olympic history by participating in their sixth Olympic Games. They shared the record with three winter Olympians: cross-country skiers Marja-Liisa Kirvesniemi and Harri Kirvesniemi and ice hockey player Raimo Helminen.http://www.noc.fi/in_english/?x126=1069450
