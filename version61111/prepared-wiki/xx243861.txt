[[César Salazar (cyclist)]]

CATEGORIES: 1972 births, Living people, Venezuelan male cyclists, Colombian cyclists, Vuelta a Colombia stage winners, Vuelta a Venezuela stage winners, People from Risaralda Department

César Augusto Salazar Herrera (born February 14, 1972 in Pereira, Risaralda) is a male road racing cyclist from Colombia, who also holds Venezuelan nationality.
