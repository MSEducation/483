[[Golden Nugget 64]]

CATEGORIES: 1998 video games, Nintendo 64 games, North America-exclusive video games, Video games set in the Las Vegas Valley, Casino video games, Westwood Studios games, Card game video games

Golden Nugget 64 is a multi-player virtual casino video game for the Nintendo 64. It was developed by Westwood Studios, published by Electronic Arts, and was released on December 1, 1998 in North America. Golden Nugget 64 is unique because it is the only gambling/casino game released in North America for the Nintendo 64.List of N64 games The game starts off by having the player create an account with $1000 which is saved on the controller pack. Players have the choice from one of ten different popular casino games. Each game has its own set of rules and a guide to learn how to play.
