[[Peter Munch Brager]]

CATEGORIES: 1806 births, Year of death missing, Members of the Parliament of Norway, Finnmark politicians, Norwegian priests, Lutheran priests

Peter Munch Brager (1806 - ??) was a Norwegian priest and politician.
He was elected to the Parliament of Norway in 1845, representing the constituency of Finmarkens Amt. He only served one term.Peter Munch Brager — Norwegian Social Science Data Services (NSD)
At that time he was a vicar in Lenvig; he was later appoined vicar in Øiestad. His son followed in his footsteps as a vicar.En Krønike om Kvinesdal, by Ludvig Ludvigsen Daae, 1894.
