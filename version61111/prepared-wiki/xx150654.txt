[[Taneichi Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Iwate Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Taneichi Station|種市駅|Taneichi-eki[/tpl] is a railway station on the JR East Hachinohe Line located in the town of Hirono, Kunohe District, Iwate Prefecture, Japan. It is 34.2 kilometers from the terminus of the Hachinohe Line at Hachinohe Station.

==Station layout==

Taneichi Station has a single side platform serving traffic in both directions. The station is unmanned. Taneichi Station is a simple consignment station, administered by Kuji Station, and operated by Jaster Co., Ltd., with point-of-sales terminal installed. Ordinary tickets, express tickets, and reserved-seat tickets for all JR lines are on sale.

==History==

Taneichi Station opened on November 10, 1924. Upon the privatization of the Japan National Railways on April 1, 1987 the station became part of JR East.
