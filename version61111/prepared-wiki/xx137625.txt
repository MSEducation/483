[[General Directorate for National Security]]

CATEGORIES: Law enforcement in Morocco

The La Direction Générale de la Sûreté Nationale ([tpl]lang-ar|المديرية العامة للأمن الوطني[/tpl]) is the authoritative and main State police body of Morocco. The Sûreté Nationale is tasked with upholding the law and public order. It works alongside the Gendarmerie Royale and the Forces Auxiliaires.
