[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Juína]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1997, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Cuiabá, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Juína ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Iuinensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Juína in the Ecclesiastical province of Cuiabá in Brazil.
