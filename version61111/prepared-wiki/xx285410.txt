[[Bryan Constant]]

CATEGORIES: French footballers, 1994 births, Living people, OGC Nice players, Association football midfielders, People from Fréjus, Ligue 1 players, France youth international footballers

Bryan Constant (born 27 March 1994 in Fréjus, France) is a French footballer of Guinean descent who currently plays for French side OGC Nice. He plays as an offensive midfielder. He made his Ligue 1 debut on the opening game of the 2013/14 season at 10 August 2013 against Olympique Lyon.
