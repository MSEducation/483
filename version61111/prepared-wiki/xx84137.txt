[[Kami, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Dissolved municipalities of Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Kami|上村|Kami-mura[/tpl] was a village located in Shimoina District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the village had an estimated population of 811 and a density of 6.41 persons per km². The total area was 126.51 km².
On October 1, 2005, Kami, along with the village of Minamishinano (also from Shimoina District), was merged into the city of Iida.
