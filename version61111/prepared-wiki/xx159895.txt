[[Alexandre Gemignani]]

[tpl]MedalCountry | [tpl]BRA[/tpl] [/tpl]
Alexandre Gemignani (June 26, 1925 in São Paulo — 1998[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.panathlonsp.org.br/documentos/panathlon-memoria.pdf | title = Atividades / História (07/08/1974 a 31/12/2006) | publisher = Panathlon Club São Paulo | accessdate = 23 January 2013[/tpl]) was a Brazilian basketball player who competed in the 1948 Summer Olympics. There he won the bronze medal with the national team under the guidance of head coach Moacyr Daiuto.
