[[Ballader och oförskämdheter]]

CATEGORIES: Cornelis Vreeswijk albums, 1964 debut albums, Swedish-language albums

Ballader och oförskämdheter (English: Ballads and impertinencies) is the debut album by the Swedish-Dutch folk singer-songwriter Cornelis Vreeswijk.Album info on www.cornelis.se Initially, Vreeswijk's songs were about to be sung by his friend Fred Åkerström, who introduced him to the label Metronom; it was however decided that Cornelis Vreeswijk should sing the songs on his own. The album was a success and a good start of his career.Bio incl. this album info on www.answers.com
