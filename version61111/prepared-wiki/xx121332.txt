[[Hrabovec]]

CATEGORIES: Villages and municipalities in Bardejov District, Shtetls

Hrabovec ([tpl]lang-hu|Rabóc[/tpl]) is a village and municipality in the Bardejov District, Prešov Region of north-east Slovakia. In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1353.

==Geography==

The municipality lies at an altitude of 460 metres and covers an area of 10.905 km².
It has a population of about 545 people.
