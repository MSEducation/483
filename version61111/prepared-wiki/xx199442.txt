[[Eternal Prisoner]]

CATEGORIES: Axel Rudi Pell albums, 1992 albums, SPV/Steamhammer albums

Eternal Prisoner is the third solo album from German guitar player Axel Rudi Pell. It was released 1992 on Steamhammer Records, and re-released in 2003. 
Also, in 1993 was edited a 2 tracks CD Single from the song Forever Young that comes with Your Life (Not Close Enough to Paradise) as B.
