[[Tanzi District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Taichung

Tanzi District ([tpl]zh|t=潭子區[/tpl]) is a suburban district with a population passes over 100,000 as of 2007, located north of Taichung, Taiwan. It has an area of 25.8497 km².

==Economy==

Taichung Tanzi Export Processing Zone is located in the township, providing a variety of employment opportunities.
