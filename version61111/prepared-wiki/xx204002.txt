İstisu (also, Istibulagh; [tpl]lang-hy|Ջերմաջուր[/tpl] Jermajur) is a small village in the Kelbajar district of Azerbaijan. The area has been under the control of forces from Nagorno-Karabakh since 1993.
The name İstisu means "hot water" in Azerbaijani language[tpl]ru icon[/tpl] Istisu. Resorts of the World
