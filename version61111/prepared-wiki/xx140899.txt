[[Generation EFX]]

CATEGORIES: Das EFX albums, 1998 albums, Elektra Records albums

Generation EFX is the fourth album by Brooklyn-based rap duo Das EFX, released on March 24, 1998. It was poorly received commercially and critically ignored.[tpl]Fact|date=January 2010[/tpl] The bulk of its production is done by 8-Off, PMD, and long time Das EFX collaborators Derek Lynch and Chris Charity. Unlike the first three Das EFX albums, it features a number of guest appearances, including Redman, M.O.P., EPMD, and Miss Jones.
