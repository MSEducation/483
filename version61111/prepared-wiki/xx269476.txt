[[Sharon Lubinski]]

CATEGORIES: Hamline University alumni, LGBT law enforcement workers, LGBT people from Minnesota, Living people, United States Marshals, University of Wisconsin–Madison alumni

Sharon Lubinski is the United States Marshal for the District of Minnesota. Lubinski is the first openly lesbian United States Marshal.[tpl]cite news| first=Ryan J. | last=Reilly | url=http://www.mainjustice.com/2010/06/21/justice-department-honors-work-of-lgbt-employees/ | title=Justice Department Honors Work of LGBT Employees | work=Main Justice | date=June 21, 2010 | accessdate=September 24, 2011[/tpl]

==Early life and education==

Lubinski earned an undergraduate degree at the University of Wisconsin–Madison and a master's degree from Hamline University.[tpl]cite web | author=The White House: Office of the Press Secretary | url=http://www.whitehouse.gov/the-press-office/president-obama-nominates-sharon-lubinski-us-marshal-district-minnesota | title=President Obama Nominates Sharon Lubinski for U.S. Marshal for the District of Minnesota | work=whitehouse.gov | date=October 13, 2009 | accessdate=September 24, 2011[/tpl]

==Career==

On October 13, 2009, President Barack Obama nominated Lubinski to serve as United States Marshal for the District of Minnesota. She was sworn into office in January 2010.[tpl]cite news| first=Joe | last=Kimball | url=http://www.minnpost.com/politicalagenda/2010/01/15/15001/new_federal_marshal_sharon_lubinski_sworn_in_today | title=New Federal Marshal Sharon Lubinski Sworn In Today | work=MinnPost.com | date=January 15, 2010 | accessdate=September 24, 2011[/tpl]
