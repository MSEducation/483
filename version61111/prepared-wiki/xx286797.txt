[[Chulcheh Qeshlaq]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Chulcheh Qeshlaq ([tpl]lang-fa|چولچه قشلاق[/tpl], also Romanized as Chūlcheh Qeshlāq; also known as Choljah Qeshlāq, Choljeh Qeshlāq, Chooljeh Gheslagh, Chowlcheh Qeshlāq, Chowljeh Qeshlāq, and Chūljeh Qeshlāq)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3058884|Chulcheh Qeshlaq[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 142, in 40 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
