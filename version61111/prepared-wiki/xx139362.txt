[[Fédération de l'informatique du Québec]]

CATEGORIES: Professional associations based in Canada

Fédération de l'informatique du Québec Inc. is an information technology professional association in Quebec, Canada.  Founded in 1977, FiQ's mission is to advance innovation in information technology and promote excellence in information technology people and practices.[tpl]cite web|url=https://ssl.req.gouv.qc.ca/igif-bin/isl08tr1/cidreq/**URL**?matricule=1142293670&banque=C&action=ETAT_CIDREQ&section1=O&section2=O&section3=O&section4=O|title=LE REGISTRAIRE DES ENTREPRISES SYSTÈME CIDREQ: FÉDÉRATION DE L'INFORMATIQUE DU QUÉBEC INC.|accessdate=2006-10-18[/tpl]  FiQ currently represents 2500 professional members from over 1110 organizations throughout Quebec.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fiq.qc.ca/FRANCAIS/a_propos/apropos.html|title=About FiQ|accessdate=2006-10-18[/tpl]
