[[Sector 42 Stadium]]

CATEGORIES: Field hockey venues in India, Buildings and structures in Chandigarh, Sport in Chandigarh

Sector 42 Stadium is a field hockey stadium at Chandigarh, Punjab, India. It was the home of the Chandigarh Dynamos of the Premier Hockey League.  It has a seating capacity of 30,000http://chandigarh.gov.in/dept_sports2.htm people.
Important tournaments held here includes Indira Gandhi International Gold Cup, Asian School Hockey, National School Games Twice, All India Women Festival, Indo-Uzabekistak 1977, Indo Pan American Hockey 1995, All India Hockey Tournaments and Punjab Gold Cup.
It presently serves as the home ground for World Series Hockey's local franchise, Chandigarh Comets.
