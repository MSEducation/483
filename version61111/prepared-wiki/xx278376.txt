[[DICE (DEA database)]]

CATEGORIES: Drug Enforcement Administration

DICE is a DEA database consists largely of phone log and Internet data gathered legally by the DEA through subpoenas, arrests and search warrants nationwide. DICE includes about 1 billion records, and they are kept for about a year and then purgedExclusive: IRS manual detailed DEA's use of hidden intel evidence, Reuters, John Shiffman and David Ingram, 8/8/2013
