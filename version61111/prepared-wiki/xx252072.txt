[[Robert Rhodin]]

CATEGORIES: 1972 births, Living people, People from Stalowa Wola, Polish film directors

Robert Rhodin is a digital media professional working with tv and films since 1992. He founded an animation studio, White Shark in 2007.
In 2008 Rhodin produced and directed the first CG animated feature film in Sweden.Rhodin's first film directing credit was Gnomes and Trolls - The Secret Chamber which screened May 2008 in Cannes, France. The picture was sold to over 80 countries entering the top ten box office in Sweden, Kuwait (UAE) and Turkey.
