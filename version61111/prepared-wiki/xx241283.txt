[[88th Indian Infantry Brigade]]

CATEGORIES: British Indian Army brigades

The 88th Indian Infantry Brigade was an Infantry formation of the Indian Army during World War II. It was formed in September 1942, at Chittagong to protect 14th Indian Infantry Division's Lines of Communications and disbanded on 8 May 1943.
