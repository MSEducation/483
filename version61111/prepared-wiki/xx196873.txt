[[Money (TV series)]]

CATEGORIES: Australian non-fiction television series, Nine Network shows, 1990s Australian television series, 2000s Australian television series, 1993 Australian television series debuts, 2002 Australian television series endings

Money is an Australian factual television program that was broadcast on the Nine Network as a regular weekly series from 1993 to 2002. It also appeared as occasional specials from 2002 to 2006. Money was a financial and investment program, hosted by Paul Clitheroe. 
The series spawned a successful magazine called Money, which is still published today.Money home - ninemsn
