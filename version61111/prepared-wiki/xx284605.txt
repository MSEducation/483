Bruno Ricardo Mendonça de Caires (born 2 April 1976 in Lisbon) is a retired Portuguese professional footballer who played as a defensive midfielder.

==Club career==

A product of Benfica youth system, Caires was iniatially loaned to Belenenses in his first year as senior, returning in 1995, appearing in 35 league games the following two seasons. In 1997, Celta de Vigo offered 3,5M€ for him, signing for the Spaniards.
