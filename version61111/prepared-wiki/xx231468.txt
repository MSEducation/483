[[Genaside II]]

CATEGORIES: British electronic music groups

Genaside II was a British electronic group active in the 1990s and early 2000s. Their music started as rave, developing into jungle, breakbeat and bigbeat. Its main member was Kris Ogden,http://www.discogs.com/artist/Genaside+II though some other members went on to form the band Archive. Their 1991 song Narra Mine provided a sample for the Prodigy track Firestarter.http://www.freeweb.hu/drf/geninf4.htm
