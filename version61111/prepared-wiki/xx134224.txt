[[Memphre]]

CATEGORIES: North American lake cryptids, Canadian lake cryptids, Canadian mythology

Memphre is a long-necked lake monster cyptid that allegedly lives in Lake Memphremagog, Quebec, Canada.

==History==

Memphre is often described as much like the Loch Ness Monster. While Memphre and most other lake monsters are disputed by many scientists, sightings persist and Memphre was last seen in 2005.[tpl]Citation needed|date=March 2008[/tpl] It has been described as a whale or a plesiosaur-like animal,[tpl]Citation needed|date=March 2008[/tpl] a description commonly brought up for other lake monsters.
