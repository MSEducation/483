[[Ole Christian Andersen Nøstvig]]

CATEGORIES: 1782 births, Year of death missing, Members of the Parliament of Norway, Nordland politicians

Ole Christian Andersen Nøstvig (1782 - ??) was a Norwegian politician.
He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament in 1830, 1833, 1836, 1842 and 1845, representing the rural constituency of Nordlands Amt (today named Nordland). He was also a deputy representative in 1827. He worked as a farmer.Ole Christian Andersen Nøstvig — Norwegian Social Science Data Services (NSD)
