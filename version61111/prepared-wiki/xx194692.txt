[[Csaba]]

CATEGORIES: Hungarian masculine given names

Csaba ([tpl]IPA-hu|ˈtʃɒbɒ[/tpl]) is a Hungarian given name for males. The name may originally mean either "shepherd" or "gift" in Hungarian.[tpl]citation needed|date=January 2011[/tpl] According to legend this was the name of the son of Attila the Hun.[tpl]citation needed|date=January 2011[/tpl]
