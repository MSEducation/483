[[Rupnagar, Guwahati]]

CATEGORIES: Neighbourhoods in Guwahati, Western Assam, Kamarupa

Rupnagar is a locality of Guwahati and a city in Northeast India in the state Assam. It is surrounded by the localities of Bhangagarh and Birubari.[tpl]cite web|url=http://pincode.net.in/ASSAM/KAMRUP/B/Rupnagar#.Uchvndht2aY |title=Rupnagar |publisher=pincode.net.in |date= |accessdate=2013-08-14[/tpl]

==Geography==

Rupnagar is located near the city center of Bhangagarh. It is well connected to the rest of the city by different means of transportation.

==See also==

There is also a Rupnagar in Punjab (India), which is currently called as Ropar.
