[[Słodków Drugi]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Kraśnik County

Słodków Drugi [tpl]IPAc-pl|'|s|ł|o|t|k|u|f|-|'|d|r|u|g|i[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kraśnik, within Kraśnik County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) – TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl]
In 2006 the village had a population of 676.
