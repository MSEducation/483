Sara Khoshjamal Fekri ([tpl]lang-fa|سارا خوش‌جمال فکری[/tpl], born 21 September 1988 in Rudbar) is an Iranian taekwondoka and the first Iranian female taekwondo Olympic qualifier.Iranian Taekwondo competitor Sara Khoshjamal She competed at the 2008 Summer Olympics, defeating Ghizlane Toudali of Morocco in the round of sixteen before losing to Yang Shu-Chun of Chinese Taipei in the quarter-finals.
