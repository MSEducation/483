==Early kingdoms==

In the first millennium CE, Malays became the dominant race on the peninsula. The small early states that were established were greatly influenced by Indian culture. Indian influence in the region dates back to at least the 3rd century BCE. South Indian culture was spread to Southeast Asia by the south Indian Pallava dynasty in the 4th and 5th century.History of Humanity: From the seventh century B.C. to the seventh century A.D.  by Sigfried J. de Laet p.395
