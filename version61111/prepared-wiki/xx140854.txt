[[Banksia Creek (Victoria)]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of Victoria (Australia), Wilsons Promontory

Banksia Creek is a watercourse in Victoria. It is located at [tpl]coord|39|07|S|146|22|E|display=inline,title|type:river_region:AU-VIC[/tpl]  at the southern tip of Wilsons Promontory. The name refers to the plant genus Banksia, which grows in the area.
