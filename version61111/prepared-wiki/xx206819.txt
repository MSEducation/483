[[Panopea smithae]]

CATEGORIES: Hiatellidae, Bivalves of New Zealand

Panopea smithae is a species of large marine bivalve mollusc in the family Hiatellidae, found in the waters surrounding New Zealand. Unlike its relative Panopea zelandica, it lives in deeper waters, ranging from deep harbours to the outer continental shelf.[tpl]cite book|last=Beu|first=A.G.|title=Revised descriptions of New Zealand Cenozoic Mollusca from Beu and Maxwell (1990).
