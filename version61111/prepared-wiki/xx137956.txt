[[Metro Iztapalapa]]

CATEGORIES: Mexico City metro stations, Railway stations opened in 1994

Metro Iztapalapa is a station along Line 8 of the metro of Mexico City.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.metro.df.gob.mx/red/estacion.html?id=112|title=Iztapalapa|language=Spanish|accessdate=30 July 2011[/tpl][tpl]Cite web|last=Archambault|first=Richard|url=http://mexicometro.org/metro/line8/iztapalapa/|title=Iztapalapa »  Mexico City Metro System|accessdate=30 July 2011[/tpl]Estación Iztapalapa is also the name of a firefighting station in the borough.  See: [tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.bomberos.df.gob.mx/wb/hcb/estacion_iztapalapa|title=Heroico Cuerpo de Bomberos del Distrito Federal|accessdate=30 July 2011[/tpl]
It is located on the Calzada Ermita Iztapalapa (also known as Eje 8 Sur) in the Colonia El Santuario neighborhood of Iztapalapa borough on the southeast side of the city.
The station's logo is a sun, representing the ceremony that is celebrated in Iztapalapa every year of the birth of the new sun.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.gobiernodigital.inah.gob.mx/ZonasArqueologicas/todas/htme/za00702.html|first=Méndez|last=Martínez|title=Enrique Cerro de la Estrella, INAH Official Web Page|date=|publisher=INAH|language=Spanish|accessdate=31 July 2011[/tpl]
The station was opened 20 July 1994.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.iztapalapa.gob.mx/htm/0430000000_2005.html|title=Transporte|publisher=Delegación Iztapalapa|language=Spanish|accessdate=30 July 2011[/tpl][tpl]Cite web|last=Monroy|first=Marco|editor-last=Schwandl|editor-first=Robert|url=http://www.urbanrail.net/am/mexi/mex-history.htm|title=Opening Dates for Mexico City's Subway|accessdate=20 August 2011[/tpl]
