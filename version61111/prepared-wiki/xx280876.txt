[[Azhdahatu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Azhdahatu ([tpl]lang-fa|اژدهاتو[/tpl], also Romanized as Azhdahātū and Azhdehātū; also known as Adzhdikhatu, Āzdehsū, and Azhdasu)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3054164|Azhdahatu[/tpl] is a village in Mojezat Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,276, in 364 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
