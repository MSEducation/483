[[Herald, Illinois]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in White County, Illinois, Unincorporated communities in Illinois, Populated places established in 1886

Herald is an unincorporated community in Heralds Prairie Township, White County, Illinois, United States. Herald is located on County Route 8 [tpl]convert|8.5|mi|km[/tpl] south of Carmi.[tpl]cite map |publisher=Illinois Department of Transportation |title=White County General Highway Map |url=http://www.dot.state.il.us/maps/county/white.pdf |format= |edition= |year=2009 |cartography= |scale= |series= |page= |section= |inset= |accessdate=September 25, 2011 |isbn= |id= |ref= [/tpl] Herald had a post office, which opened on September 13, 1886, and closed on April 10, 2004.[tpl]cite web|title=Postmaster Finder - Post Offices by ZIP Code|url=http://webpmt.usps.gov/pmt008.cfm|publisher=United States Postal Service|accessdate=September 25, 2011[/tpl]
