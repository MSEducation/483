[[Huarochirí District]]

Huarochirí District (in hispanicized spelling) or WaruchiriTeofilo Laime Acopa, Diccionario Bilingüe, Iskay simipi yuyay k'ancha, Quechua – Castellano, Castellano – Quechua: waruchiri - s.
