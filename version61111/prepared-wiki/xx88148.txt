[[Hastings railway station, Melbourne]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1889

Hastings railway station is located on the Stony Point line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the town of Hastings opening on 10 September 1889.Hastings Vicsig

==Platforms & services==

Hastings has one platform. It is serviced by Metro Trains' Stony Point line services.Stony Point Line timetable Public Transport Victoria
