[[77th Division (People's Republic of China)]]

CATEGORIES: Infantry divisions of the People's Volunteer Army, Infantry divisions of the People's Liberation Army

The 77th Division was a military formation of the People's Volunteer Army (aka Chinese People's Volunteers (CPV) or Chinese Communist Forces (CCF)) during the Korean War. 
The division was a component of the 26th Army, consisting of the 229th, 230th, and the 231st Regiments.[tpl]cite book| chapterurl = http://www.history.army.mil/books/korea/ebb/ch3.htm| chapter = Chapter III The Enemy| last =  Mossman| first = Billy C.|  title = Ebb and Flow November 1950-July 1951| page = 55| publisher = United States Army Center of Military History[/tpl]
