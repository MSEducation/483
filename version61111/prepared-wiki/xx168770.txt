[[Rosanna Roces]]

Jennifer Adriano-Molina (born May 25, 1972),http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0733726/bio better known by her stage names Rosanna Roces and Osang, is a Filipina actress, known in the early 1990s as one of the hottest actresses.  She was a contract star of Seiko films from 1994 to 1996, introduced initially as "Ana Maceda". After her years with Seiko, she starred in the critically acclaimed film Ligaya Ang Itawag Mo Sa Akin (Call Me Joy) by Reyna Films, which paved the way for her transition to "serious acting". [tpl]Citation needed|date=December 2009[/tpl]
In addition to doing films, Rosanna Roces ventured into hosting by serving as a replacement for Dawn Zulueta on GMA 7's Startalk. Roces hosted the show until 2004, after a misunderstanding between her and her manager-friend Lolit Solis. Leaving GMA 7 for ABS CBN, Rosanna Roces was replaced by Lorna Tolentino in the reformatting of Startalk. Roces also did acting on TV, appearing in shows such as 1 for 3 with Vic Sotto and Charlene Gonzales from 1997 to 2002 and Daboy en Da Girl with Rudy Fernandez from 2002 to 2003.
She was also part of ABS-CBN's Sineserye Presents: Natutulog Ba Ang Diyos? as Patria and on the soap opera Ysabella (as Rosario), and Maging Sino Ka Man: Ang Pagbabalik as Veronica Rubio. She also recently appeared once on the finale episode of Tayong Dalawa on September 25, 2009 on ABS-CBN 2. [tpl]Citation needed|date=December 2009[/tpl]
Her latest appearance is her portrayal in TV5 Mini Series Sa Ngalan ng Ina in 2011 with Nora Aunor.

==Personal life==

Rosanna Roces is of German-Jewish decent.  She was adopted by a loving father but an indifferent mother.  She gained showbiz fame in the 1990s.  She is a divorced mother of two, and a grandmother.
