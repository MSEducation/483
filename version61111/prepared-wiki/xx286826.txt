[[Gonbad, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Gonbad ([tpl]lang-fa|گنبد[/tpl]; also known as Gumbezler and Gunbad)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3064785|Gonbad[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 106, in 31 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
