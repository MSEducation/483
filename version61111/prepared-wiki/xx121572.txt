Retired judge K. Palakidnar of the Special Presidential Commission of inquiry appointed by the [[People's Alliance (Sri Lanka)|
People's Alliance]] government described the massacre in detail in his final report. According to the report of the commission of inquiry, 5 infants, 42 children under ten, 85 women and 28 old persons were among the 184 villagers who were murdered. The judge also identified three captains of the Sri Lankan Army: Warnakulasooriya, Herath and Wijenaike as the responsible parties.
