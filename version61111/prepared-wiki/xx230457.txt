Shin Byung-Ho (born April 26, 1977) is a South Korean former football player who played for Jeju United, Gyeongnam FC, Chunnam Dragons, Ulsan Hyundai Horang-i and Mito HollyHock in Japan.
On 2 November 2008, he announced his retirement. After retirement, he coached Jeju Middle School football team.
