[[Wani Books]]

CATEGORIES: Book publishing companies of Japan, Companies based in Tokyo, Magazine publishing companies of Japan, Manga distributors, Publishing companies established in 1979, 1979 establishments in Japan, Comic book publishing companies of Japan

[tpl]Nihongo|Wani Books Co., Ltd.|ワニブックス株式会社|Wani Bukkusu Kabushiki Gaisha[/tpl] is a Japanese publishing company focused on manga-related publication, including magazines and books. The company was established in November 1979.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.wani.co.jp/company.html|title=会社概要|publisher=Wani Books official website|language=Japanese|accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl] It publishes one manga magazine Comic Gum.http://comipedia.com/publisher/wani-books
