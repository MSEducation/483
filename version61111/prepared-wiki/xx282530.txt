[[Nicholas Morys]]

CATEGORIES: 14th-century births, 15th-century deaths, 15th-century English people, People from Cambridgeshire, Members of the Parliament of England (pre-1707)

Nicholas Morys (fl. 1414), of Trumpington, Cambridgeshire, was an ENglish politician.

==Family==

Morys married Margaret, and they had one son, the MP, John.

==Career==

He was a Member (MP) of the Parliament of England for Cambridgeshire in April 1414.http://www.historyofparliamentonline.org/volume/1386-1421/member/morys-nicholas
