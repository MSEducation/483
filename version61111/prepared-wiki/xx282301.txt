[[Club Deportivo San Andrés Basquet]]

CATEGORIES: Basketball teams in Argentina

Club Deportivo San Andrés Basquet, or C.D. San Andrés Basquet, is a professional basketball club that is based in Buenos Aires, Argentina. It is a part of the multi-sports Club Deportivo San Andrés. The club has competed in the Argentine League.

==History==

C.D. San Andrés made it to the final of the South American Club Championship in 1985. They also competed at the 1985 edition of the Intercontinental Cup.Linguasport.com II Club World Cup (Girona / Barcelona 1985).
