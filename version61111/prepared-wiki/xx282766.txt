Jeffrey Alan "Buddy" Boshers (born May 9, 1988) is a professional baseball pitcher for the Los Angeles Angels of Anaheim of Major League Baseball.

==Early life==

A native of Huntsville, Alabama, Boshers is a graduate of Lee High School.

==Career==

Boshers was drafted by the Los Angeles Angels of Anaheim in the fourth round of the 2008 Major League Baseball Draft.
Boshers was called up to the majors for the first time on August 10, 2013.
