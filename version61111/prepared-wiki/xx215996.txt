[[Hojjatollah Vaezi]]

CATEGORIES: 1977 births, Living people, Iranian archers, Archers at the 2008 Summer Olympics, Olympic archers of Iran

Hojjatollah Vaezi (born March 27, 1977) is an athlete from Iran, who competes in archery.

==2008 Summer Olympics==

At the 2008 Summer Olympics in Beijing Hojjatolah finished his ranking round with a total of 604 points, which gave him the 63rd seed for the final competition bracket in which he faced Mangal Singh Champia in the first round.
