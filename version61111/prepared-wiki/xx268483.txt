[[Thank You Lord]]

CATEGORIES: Don Moen (singer) live albums, 2004 live albums, Epic Records albums, Columbia Records albums, 2004 video albums, Live video albums

Thank You Lord is a Contemporary Christian worship album recorded by Don Moen. The album was released on April 6, 2004, by Integrity Music, Hosanna! Music, Christian Broadcasting Network, Epic Records, and Sony Music Entertainment.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.newreleasetuesday.com/albumdetail.php?album_id=3444 |title=Thank You Lord by Don Moen | CD Reviews And Information |publisher=NewReleaseTuesday.com |date= |accessdate=2011-09-27[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cmt.com/artists/az/moen_don/368622/album.jhtml |title=Don Moen : Thank You Lord |publisher=CMT.com |date=2004-04-06 |accessdate=2011-09-27[/tpl] On March 5, 2005, was recognized by Billboard Magazine, charting #15 on the Top Christian Albums and #22 on the Heatseekers.[tpl]cite web|url=[tpl]BillboardURLbyName|artist=don moen|chart=all[/tpl] |title=Christian Songs |publisher=Billboard.com |date= |accessdate=2011-09-27[/tpl]Billboard Magazine, Nielsen Business Media, March 5, 2005, Vol. 117, No. 10, page 65. ==Recording==

Thank You Lord was recorded on October 3, 2003, during a live worship service at Regent University, located in Virginia Beach, Virginia.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cduniverse.com/search/xx/music/pid/6709724/a/Thank+You+Lord.htm |title=Don Moen - Thank You Lord CD Album |publisher=Cduniverse.com |date= |accessdate=2011-09-27[/tpl] The school was founded by the American televangelist Pat Robertson, president and founder of the Christian Broadcasting Network.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.regent.edu/about_us |title=Regent University - About Us |publisher=Regent.edu |date= |accessdate=2011-09-27[/tpl] The recording featured worship leader, Don Moen, along with the Kingdom Choir of Tide Water and the Brentwood Baptist Church Worship Choir.
