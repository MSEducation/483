[[The Look (band)]]

CATEGORIES: English New Wave musical groups, English pop music groups

The Look are a UK pop band, who had a number six hit single in the UK Singles Chart in 1981, with "I Am The Beat".

==Career==

From Ely, Cambs. and originally named The Kreed, the band changed their name to The Look when they moved to London.Songfacts.com Their lead singer and frontman was Johnny Whetstone (born c. 1955). The group moved to Towerbell Records, an indie label.Allmusic.com - album overview The Look disbanded in 1983.
