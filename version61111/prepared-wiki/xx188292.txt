Mawarid Holding is a private Saudi investment company.- Mawarid Holding, Zawya. It was established in 1968.[tpl]cite news|title=Company Overview of The Mawarid Group of Companies|url=http://investing.businessweek.com/research/stocks/private/snapshot.asp?privcapId=22595361|accessdate=9 December 2013|newspaper=Bloomberg BusinessWeek[/tpl] Its headquarters is in Suleimania. It manages and controls several investments which have diversified businesses in financial services, telecommunications, satellite television and radio network, construction, general distribution and trading, catering and restaurants.
