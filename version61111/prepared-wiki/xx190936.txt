[[Charachar]]

CATEGORIES: 1993 films, Indian films, Bengali-language films, 1990s drama films, Films directed by Buddhadev Dasgupta, Best Feature Film National Film Award winners

Charachar (English language:Shelter of the Wings) is a 1993 Bengali drama film directed and written by Buddhadev Dasgupta based on a novel by Prafulla Roy.
