[[Harrogate and District Football League]]

CATEGORIES: Sport in Harrogate, Football leagues in England, Football in North Yorkshire

The Harrogate and District Football League is a football competition based in Yorkshire, England. It has a total of three divisions, the highest of which, the Premier Division, sits at level 14 of the English football league system. It is a feeder to the West Yorkshire League[tpl]Citation needed|date=May 2009[/tpl].
