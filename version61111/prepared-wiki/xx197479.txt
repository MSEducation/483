[[Antilhue]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Chile, Populated places in Valdivia Province

Antilhue is a village ([tpl]lang-es|aldea[/tpl]) in Chile, South America. It is located in the commune of Los Lagos on the shores of Calle-Calle River just east of Valdivia. Two  petroleum-fueled power plants, Antilhue I and Antilhue II, with a combined production capacity of 101.3 MW, are located near the village.
