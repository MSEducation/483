[[Moshampa]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Moshampa ([tpl]lang-fa|مشمپا[/tpl], also Romanized as Moshampā)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3075856|Moshampa[/tpl] is a village in Chaypareh-ye Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,195, in 288 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
