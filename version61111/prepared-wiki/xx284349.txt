[[Junkers EF 127]]

CATEGORIES: World War II aircraft of Germany

The Junkers EF 127 was a jet powered fighter aircraft designed by the Third Reich as a part of the Emergency Fighter Program in the closing year of WWII. The project was codenamed "Walli". The EF 127 was a heavily redesigned version of the Junkers EF 126. The propulsion for the aircraft was a Liquid-propellant rocket designed by the Walter company.
