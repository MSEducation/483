[[Qarah Bolagh, Ijrud]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Qarah Bolagh ([tpl]lang-fa|قره بلاغ[/tpl], also Romanized as Qarah Bolāgh and Qareh Bolāgh; also known as Karbala)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3079640|Qarah Bolagh[/tpl] is a village in Saidabad Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 4, in 4 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
