[[Kentucky Route 2629]]

CATEGORIES: State highways in Kentucky

Kentucky Route 2629 (KY 2629) is a south–to–north [tpl]convert|6.536|mi|km|adj=on[/tpl] state highway that runs through southeastern Warren County, Kentucky.

==Route description==

Known locally as Old Scottsville Rd., KY 2629 starts at a junction with KY 961 near US 231 in Alvaton, Kentucky. The two-lane road travels north-northwest for [tpl]convert|5.1|mi|km[/tpl] until it crosses Drake's Creek, a tributary of the Barren River. The route then travels about [tpl]convert|1.4|mi|km[/tpl] in a west direction until it ends at KY 2158, just to the east of I-65 inside the city limits of Bowling Green.
