[[Hippolyte Bis]]

CATEGORIES: French dramatists and playwrights, People from Lille, 1789 births, 1855 deaths, French opera librettists

Hippolyte Louis Florent Bis (29 August 1789 – 3 March 1855) was a French playwright and librettist.Phillips, Lawrence Barnett (1871). The Dictionary of Biographical Reference, p. 153. Sampson Low, Son & Marston He is most known for the libretto to Giachino Rossini's opera William Tell (premiered in 1829), which he wrote with Étienne de Jouy.[tpl]cite web|title=Libretto to Guillaume Tell|url=http://opera.stanford.edu/Rossini/GuillaumeTell/livret.html|publisher=Stanford OperaGlass[/tpl]
