[[Arena da Floresta]]

CATEGORIES: Football venues in Brazil, Sports venues in Acre (state), Event venues established in 2006

Arena da Floresta ([tpl]lang-en|Forest Arena[/tpl]) is a multi-use stadium in Rio Branco, Acre, Brazil.  It is currently used mostly for football matches.  The stadium holds 20,000.[tpl]cite web | title = Arena da Floresta | url=http://mavalem.sites.uol.com.br/ac/RioBranco2.htm | accessdate = 2007-06-29 | publisher=Templos do Futebol [/tpl] It was built in 2006.

==History==

The stadium, built in 2006, was inaugurated on December 17 of that year, when Rio Branco-AC beat Brazil national under-20 football team 2-1.[tpl]cite web | title = Brasil sub-20 perde amistoso no Acre | url=http://www.gazetaesportiva.net/ge_noticias/bin/noticia.php?chid=130&nwid=8556 | accessdate = 2007-06-29 | publisher=Gazeta Esportiva |archiveurl = http://web.archive.org/web/20070929083954/http://www.gazetaesportiva.net/ge_noticias/bin/noticia.php?chid=130&nwid=8556  |archivedate = 2007-09-29[/tpl] The stadium's attendance record currently stands at 23,000, set on that match.
