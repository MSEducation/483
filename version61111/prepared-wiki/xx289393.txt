[[Far Away from the Sun]]

CATEGORIES: Heavy metal albums by Swedish artists, 1996 albums

Far Away From The Sun is the debut album of the Swedish black/death metal band Sacramentum. It was released through Adipocere Records in 1996. The album features a cover artwork created by Kristian Wåhlin (aka Necrolord).
