[[Naked Eye (song)]]

CATEGORIES: 1996 singles, Song recordings produced by Daniel Lanois

"Naked Eye" is a 1996 song by American alternative rock band, Luscious Jackson, the first single from their second album entitled Fever In Fever Out also released in 1996. The song was a commercial success peaking at No.18 on the Billboard Modern Rock Tracks chart and No.36 on the Billboard Hot 100.[tpl]Allmusic|class=artist|id=p40141/charts-awards/billboard-singles|pure_url=yes[/tpl] It was released on CD and 12", both of which feature three remixes and an instrumental version of the song as well as two non-LP tracks.GR2 Records
The song was also released as downloadable content for Rock Band, Rock Band 2, and Rock Band 3.
