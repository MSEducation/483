[[Khinalug language]]

CATEGORIES: Northeast Caucasian languages, Languages of Azerbaijan, Endangered Caucasian languages, Khinalug

Khinalug (also spelled Khinalig, Xinalug(h)  or Khinalugh) is a Northeast Caucasian language spoken by about 1,500 people in the villages of Khinalug and Gülüstan[tpl]dn|date=June 2013[/tpl][tpl]Citation needed|date=June 2013[/tpl] in the mountains of Quba Rayon, northern Azerbaijan. It forms its own independent branch within the Northeast Caucasian language family.Family tree of Northeast Caucasian languages at Ethnologue
Khinalug is endangered,Published in: Encyclopedia of the world’s endangered languages. Edited by Christopher Moseley. London & New York: Routledge, 2007. 211–280. classified as "severely endangered" by UNESCO's Atlas of the World's Languages in Danger.UNESCO Interactive Atlas of the World’s Languages in Danger
