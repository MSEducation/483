[[Columbus Community Church]]

CATEGORIES: Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Religious buildings completed in 1844, 19th-century church buildings, Churches in Chenango County, New York

Columbus Community Church is a historic church on New York State Route 80 in Columbus, Chenango County, New York. It was built in 1844 and is a one story rectangular frame building, a low pitched gable roof, and a three stage bell tower and spire.  It is in the Greek Revival style, with some Gothic Revival features introduced with a remodeling in 1879.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=1151|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: Columbus Community Church|date=December 1985|accessdate=2010-02-20 |author=Mark L. Peckham|publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation[/tpl] See also: [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=1150|title=Accompanying six photos[/tpl] 
It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1986.
