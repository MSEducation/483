[[1945–46 Copa Mexico]]

CATEGORIES: Copa México, 1945–46 in Mexican football, 1945–46 domestic association football leagues

The Copa México 1945–46 was the 30th staging of the Copa México, the 3rd staging in the professional era.
The competition started on June 23, 1945 and concluded on July 21, 1946 with the final, in which Atlas lifted the trophy for the first time ever with a 5–4 victory over Atlante.
This edition was played by 16 teams, in a knock-out stage, in a single macth.
