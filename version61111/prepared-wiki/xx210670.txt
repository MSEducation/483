[[Love Me Again (album)]]

CATEGORIES: Rita Coolidge albums, 1978 albums, A&M Records albums

Love Me Again is a 1978 album by Rita Coolidge and was released on the A&M Records label. The title track "Love Me Again" was released as a singleTop country singles, 1944 to 2001 Joel Whitburn - 2002 "Love Me Again Rita Coolidge 80/86" and then covered and appeared as a single for Patti Austin in 1980.
