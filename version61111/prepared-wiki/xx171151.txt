[[KPKY]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Idaho, Classic rock radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 1975

KPKY (94.9 FM, "94-9 The Rock") is a commercial radio station located in Pocatello, Idaho.  KPKY airs a classic rock music format.

==Ownership==

In October 2007, a deal was reached for KPKY to be acquired by GAP Broadcasting II LLC (Samuel Weller, president) from Clear Channel Communications as part of a 57 station deal with a total reported sale price of $74.78 million.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.broadcastingcable.com/article/CA6494941.html |work=Broadcasting & Cable |title=Deals |date=2006-06-19[/tpl]  What eventually became GapWest Broadcasting was folded into Townsquare Media on August 13, 2010.[tpl]cite news|title=Townsquare Media completes roll-up of GAP|url=http://www.rbr.com/radio/radio_deals/26710.html|accessdate=August 15, 2010|newspaper=Radio Business Report|date=August 13, 2010[/tpl]
