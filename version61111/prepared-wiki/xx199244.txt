[[Freskanova]]

CATEGORIES: British independent record labels, Electronic music record labels, Hip hop record labels, Drum and bass record labels

Freskanova was the record label responsible for the Freestylers,Freestylers site - see Pressure Point & We Rock Hard Mad Doctor XDiscogs and many other UK breaks artists but shut down in April 2002.
