Jan Khan (born 25 April 1958 in Rotorua, New Zealand) is a lawn bowls competitor for New Zealand. 
At the 2002 Commonwealth Games she won a bronze medal in the women's fours event. At the 2006 Commonwealth Games she again won a bronze in the women's pairs event with her sister Marina Khan.Profile at the New Zealand Olympic Committee website They are daughters of lawn bowler Millie Khan.
