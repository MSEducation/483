[[Liberté]]

CATEGORIES: Guinean music, National symbols of Guinea, National anthems, Liberty symbols

"[tpl]Lang|fr|Liberté[/tpl]" (Liberty) has been the national anthem of Guinea since independence in 1958. It was arranged by Fodéba Keïta and was based on the melody of "Alfa yaya". The author of the lyrics is unknown.
