[[4-Pyrone]]

CATEGORIES: 4-Pyrones

4-Pyrone (Î³-pyrone or pyran-4-one) is an unsaturated cyclic chemical compound with the molecular formula C5H4O2.  It is isomeric with 2-pyrone.
4-Pyrone forms the central core of several natural chemical compounds including maltol and kojic acid and of the important class of the Flavones.
