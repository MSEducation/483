[[Omantel]]

CATEGORIES: Telecommunications companies of Oman, Internet service providers of Oman

Oman Telecommunications Company (Omantel) is the first telecommunications company in Oman and is the primary provider of internet services in the country. Omantel acquired a 65% share in WorldCall Pakistan in April 2008.[tpl]cite web | title = Omantel acquires 65% stake in WorldCall Pakistan | work = Inside Telephony | date = April 19, 2008 | url = http://www.insidetelephony.com/telecom-news/Omantel-acquires-stake-WorldCall-Pakistan-Telecom-News.php | accessdate = March 5, 2009[/tpl] The government of Oman owns a 70% share in Omantel.[tpl]cite news  | last = Cordahi | first = James | title = Emirates Telecom says eyeing Omantel stake | work = Reuters | publisher = Thomson Reuters | date = October 18, 2007 | url = http://www.reuters.com/article/innovationNews/idUSL1835491120071018 | accessdate = March 5, 2009[/tpl]
Omantel has estbalished it self as a major international hub, with currently 10 submarine cables landing in Oman, e.g. TWA, EIG, PLAG, Falcon, EPEG, SMW-3, Mena, POI, OMRAN, GBI and BBG.
