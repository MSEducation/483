[[Xbox Entertainment Studios]]

CATEGORIES: Xbox Live, Video game development companies, Video game companies of the United States, Entertainment companies of the United States, Film production companies of the United States, Companies based in Redmond, Washington, Companies established in 2012, Microsoft subsidiaries, First-party video game developers

Xbox Entertainment Studios is an American video game developer based in Redmond, Washington created internally by Microsoft Studios in 2012, in order to create "interactive television content" for Xbox Live.[tpl]cite web|last=Farber |first=Dan |url=http://news.cnet.com/8301-1023_3-57568826-93/microsofts-xbox-entertainment-studio-working-on-interactive-tv/ |title=Microsoft's Xbox Entertainment Studio working on interactive TV |publisher=CNET |date=2013-02-11 |accessdate=2013-12-20[/tpl]

==Video content==

Xbox Entertainment Studios is currently developing their first projects, a documentary about the video game crash of 1983http://news.xbox.com/2013/12/ent-xes-lightbox, a science-fiction drama titled Humans and a live action television series based on the Halo franchise in collaboration with film producer Steven Spielberg.[tpl]cite web|last=McWhertor |first=Michael |url=http://www.polygon.com/2013/5/22/4354018/halo-tv-series-xbox-one-343-industries-steven-spielberg |title=Halo TV series early in development, but Microsoft expects 'dramatic impact on how people think about TV' |publisher=Polygon |date=2013-05-22 |accessdate=2013-12-20[/tpl] The studio will also co-produce certain live events for Xbox Live, including future editions of the Call of Duty Championship, the Miss Teen USA beauty pageant and the VGX, among others.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.microsoft.com/en-us/news/features/2013/feb13/02-11Xbox.aspx |title=Xbox Execs Talk Momentum and the Future of TV |publisher=Microsoft |date=2013-02-11 |accessdate=2013-12-20[/tpl]
