[[Sanseirui]]

CATEGORIES: Gōjū-ryū, Uechi-ryū, Karate kata

Sanseirui (or Sanseiryu or Sandairyu) is a karate kata of Southern Chinese (Fujianese) origin. The name literally means thirty-six (steps, or positions of attack and defense) . 
Versions of it are found in several karate styles, including Goju-ryu, but it is most associated with Uechi-ryuEDS Dojo and the related system Shohei-ryu.
