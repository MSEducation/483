[[Ta Gegonota tis Achaias]]

CATEGORIES: Greek-language newspapers, Newspapers published in Patras

Ta Gegonota tis Achaias ([tpl]lang-el|Τα Γεγονότα της Αχαΐας[/tpl]) ([tpl]lang-en|The Events of Achaia[/tpl]) is a daily newspaper that was founded in 1987 in Patras, Greece.  It is owned by the company Patraikes ekdoseis monoprosopi EPE (Πατραϊκές εκδόσεις μονοπρόσωπη ΕΠΕ) and is written by Ekdotiki Patron A.E. (Εκδοτική Πατρών ΑΕ).
