[[Salarabad, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Salarabad ([tpl]lang-fa|سالاراباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Sālārābād; also known as Qārājālār)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3771555|Salarabad[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Pain Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 23, in 4 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
