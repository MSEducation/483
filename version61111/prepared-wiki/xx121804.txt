[[Egton]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in North Yorkshire, Civil parishes in North Yorkshire

Egton is a village and civil parish in the Scarborough district of North Yorkshire, England about [tpl]convert|5|mi|km[/tpl] west of Whitby, located within the North York Moors National Park. There is a nearby village called Egton Bridge which is home to  Egton railway station.  The village was included in the Survey of English Dialects.  Unlike the other sites, a full book was written on the local dialect by Hans Tidholm.[ref]

</ref>  Being a very isolated village, its ways of speech were very unusual in Britain.
