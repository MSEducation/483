[[Spotlight (CBC TV series)]]

CATEGORIES: CBC network shows, 1975 Canadian television series debuts, 1975 Canadian television series endings, Canadian news television series

Spotlight was a Canadian current affairs television series which aired on CBC Television in 1975.

==Premise==

This interview series featured guests such as separatist Quebec premier René Lévesque, politicians Bob Andras and Philip Givens, conservationist Roderick Haig-Brown and Auditor General Maxwell Henderson.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.broadcasting-history.ca/programming/television/programming_popup.php?id=1280 | first=John | last=Corcelli | date=May 2005 | title=Spotlight | publisher=Canadian Communications Foundation | accessdate=31 December 2010 [/tpl]

==Scheduling==

This half-hour series was broadcast Thursdays at 10:30 p.m. (Eastern time) from 17 July to 4 September 1975.
