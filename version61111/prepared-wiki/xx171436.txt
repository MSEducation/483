CATEGORIES: Swimming at the 1984 Summer Olympics, Men's 100 metre butterfly

The final of the Men's 100 metres Butterfly event at the 1984 Summer Olympics was held in the McDonald's Olympic Swim Stadium in Los Angeles, California, on July 30, 1984. The first eight qualified for the final, the next eight for the B-final.
