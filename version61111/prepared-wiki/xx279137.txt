[[Dundee Stock Exchange]]

CATEGORIES: 1879 establishments in Scotland, 1964 disestablishments in Scotland, Economy of Dundee, Organisations based in Dundee, Organizations established in 1879, Stock exchanges in Europe

The Dundee Stock Exchange was established in 1879.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.dundee.ac.uk/archives/ms069.htm|title=Archives: MS 69 (University of Dundee)|accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl] In 1964, it merged into the Scottish Stock Exchange along with the Glasgow Stock Exchange, Edinburgh Stock Exchange, and Aberdeen Stock Exchange. It continued to operate as a local branch until 1971,[tpl]cite news|work=The Herald|date=30 April 1963|url=http://news.google.com/newspapers?id=uGxAAAAAIBAJ&sjid=kaMMAAAAIBAJ&dq=scottish-stock-exchange&pg=4493%2C4607990|title=All Deals Marked[/tpl] when the local exchanges closed completely. By 1973, the Scottish Stock Exchange had merged into the London Stock Exchange.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.heraldscotland.com/news/home-news/calls-for-scotland-to-have-its-own-stock-exchange-1.1045699|title=Calls for Scotland to have its own stock exchange|date=4 August 2010|accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl]
