[[Ruby Ring]]

CATEGORIES: Korean Broadcasting System television dramas, 2013 South Korean television series debuts, 2014 South Korean television series endings, 2013 in South Korean television, Korean-language television programming, 2010s South Korean television series

Ruby Ring ([tpl]ko-hhrm|루비 반지||Rubi Banji[/tpl]) is a 2013 South Korean television daily drama series starring Lee So-yeon, Im Jung-eun, Kim Suk-hoon and Park Kwang-hyun.[tpl]cite web|last=Lee|first=Jun-hyeun|title=Lee So Yeon & Lim Jung Eun at press conference of Ruby Ring|url=http://en.starnnews.com/news/index.html?no=222478|work=StarN News|accessdate=2013-08-21[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Actress Lee So-yeon smiles during a press event for her new TV drama in Seoul on Tuesday.|url=http://english.chosun.com/svc/view.html?contid=2013081400388&catid=C|work=The Chosun Ilbo|accessdate=2013-08-20|date=14 August 2013[/tpl] It airs on KBS2 on Mondays to Fridays at 19:45 for 95 episodes beginning August 19, 2013.

==Plot==

Two sisters' faces and their fates are switched in a car accident.
