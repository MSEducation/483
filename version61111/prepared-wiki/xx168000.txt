[[Let's Get Physical]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 albums, Hip hop albums, Reggae albums, Albums produced by Swizz Beatz

Let's Get Physical is the debut album of Elephant Man on Bad Boy, released in 2008.
The first single is called "Five-O" and features Wyclef Jean.
The song "Willie Bounce" has appeared on several Mixtapes in early 2006. Elephant Man borrowed the first few bars from "I Will Survive" by Gloria Gaynor.
The second single "Jump" was released on November 6 and it features and was produced by Swizz Beatz.
