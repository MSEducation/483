Moulay Idriss Khanousi (born 1939) is a Moroccan football defender who played for the Morocco in the 1970 FIFA World Cup.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fifa.com/worldcup/archive/edition=32/teams/team=43872.html |title=1970 FIFA World Cup Mexico |publisher=Fifa.com |date= |accessdate=2012-07-08[/tpl] He also played for MAS Fez. Also, Khanousi was a nurse.
