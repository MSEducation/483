[[Vala Rud]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Vala Rud ([tpl]lang-fa|والارود[/tpl], also Romanized as Vālā Rūd; also known as Bārī (Persian: باري ), Baro, and Bārow)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3088034|Vala Rud[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Bala Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 482, in 127 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
