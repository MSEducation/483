[[Mark Johnsrud]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, People from Jefferson County, Wisconsin, People from La Crosse, Wisconsin, Mayors of La Crosse, Wisconsin, American people of Norwegian descent

Mark Johnsrud is the current Village Administrator for the Village of Johnson Creek, Wisconsin.  He was hired in April, 2009.http://www.wkbt.com/global/story.asp?s=10153284&ClientType=Printable

==Mayor of La Crosse==

Johnsrud was elected Mayor of La Crosse, Wisconsin in 2005 with 52.08 percent of all votes over Deke Swanson after two term mayor John Medinger retired.http://www.ourcampaigns.com/RaceDetail.html?RaceID=572628  After one term in office, the people decided to go a drastically different direction, voting in 24 year old Mathias Harter.  Johnsrud served as mayor from 2005-2009.
