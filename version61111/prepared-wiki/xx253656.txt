[[Key Waden]]

Key Waden is a small barrier island between Naples and Marco Island, Florida, United States.  
Located at the mouth of the Gulf of Mexico, this island offer a beautiful white sandy sandbar.  It can only be reached by boat, making it a very primitive and private location.  Tour boats offer special cruises to Key Waden and pontoon boats are also readily available for hire to get one there.
Popular among locals and tourist alike, people travel to the Key Waden to go shelling along the shore and to spend the day swimming at the beach.
