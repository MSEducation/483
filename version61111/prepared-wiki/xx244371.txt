[[Freddie Gibbs]]

CATEGORIES: 1982 births, American Muslims, African-American Muslims, African-American male rappers, Living people, Midwest hip hop musicians, Rappers from Indiana, Underground rappers

Fredrick Tipton (born June 28, 1982), better known by his stage name Freddie Gibbs, is an American rapper from Gary, Indiana. Active since 2003, he is also a member of the hip hop group, Str8 Slammin' Click.

==Early life==

Freddie Gibbs was born and raised in Gary, Indiana, and grew up at 17th and Virginia St. on the city's east side.[tpl]cite web|last=Weiss|first=Jeff|title=The Miseducation of Freddie Gibbs|url=http://www.laweekly.com/2009-12-03/music/the-miseducation-of-freddie-gibbs/|date=December 2, 2009[/tpl] Gibbs signed with Interscope Records in 2006, after which he moved to Los Angeles and recorded a debut album, but a year later the management of Interscope changed hands and the rapper was dropped.[tpl]cite web|last=Lipshutz|first=Jason|title=Freddie Gibb Revives Gangster Rap on 'Str8 Killa' EP|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/957079/freddie-gibbs-revives-gangster-rap-on-str8-killa-ep|date=August 2, 2010[/tpl]

==Career==

===2011–2012: Mixtapes===

On October 31, 2011, Gibbs released his ninth mixtape Cold Day In Hell, featuring guest appearances from Young Jeezy, Juicy J, Freeway and Dom Kennedy. Production comes from J.U.S.T.I.C.E. League, Big K.R.I.T., Cardo, DJ Burn One, Speakerbomb, Block Beattaz, Beatnick & K-Salaam and more. On February 18, 2011, Freddie Gibbs announced on his website that he has joined up with Cleveland's Chip tha Ripper and Chuck Inglish and Mikey Rocks of The Cool Kids to form the hip hop group, Pulled Over by the Cops (P.O.C.).[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.freddiegibbs.com/blog/2011/2/18/poc-pulled-over-by-the-cops-authority.html|title=Freddie Gibbs - BLOG - P.O.C. Pulled Over by the Cops "Authority"|publisher=freddiegibbs.com|author=Freddie Gibbs|accessdate=2012-02-06[/tpl] On September 25, 2012, Freddie Gibbs released his tenth mixtape Baby Face Killa featuring guest appearances from Young Jeezy, Krayzie Bone, SpaceGhostPurrp, Dom Kennedy, Jadakiss, Kirko Bangz, Jay Rock and Currensy among others.[tpl]cite web|url=http://pitchfork.com/news/47973-download-freddie-gibbs-baby-face-killa-mixtape/ |title=Download Freddie Gibbs' Baby Face Killa Mixtape | News |publisher=Pitchfork |date=2012-09-25 |accessdate=2013-06-07[/tpl] On December 11, 2012 Gibbs announced he was no longer a member of Young Jeezy's CTE label. He elaborated saying there is no beef between the two rappers.[tpl]cite web|last=Vasquez |first=Andres |url=http://www.hiphopdx.com/index/news/id.22170/title.freddie-gibbs-leaves-young-jeezys-label-says-they-move-in-different-ways |title=Freddie Gibbs Leaves Young Jeezy's Label, Says They "Move In Different Ways" |publisher=HipHop DX |date=2012-12-11 |accessdate=2013-06-07[/tpl]

===2013–present: ESGN===

Gibbs joined fellow hip-hop artists Brianna Perry and Ace Hood on the 2013 Forgiato Fest at the Miami Beach Convention Center, Florida, on April 6, 2013. On May 27, 2013, Freddie Gibbs announced he would be releasing his debut album ESGN on July 9, 2013.[tpl]cite web|title=Forgiato Fest 2013 > Forgiato Fest 2013 Line-up, Ticket Info|url=http://www.songkick.com/festivals/672569/id/16104889-forgiato-fest-2013?utm_source=8789&utm_medium=partner|work=Songkick|publisher=songkick.com|accessdate=March 22, 2013[/tpl] The album ended up being released on June 20, 2013, three weeks before the planned release date.http://www.hiphopdx.com/index/news/id.24082/title.freddie-gibbs-esgn-release-date-cover-art-tracklist- On January 17, 2014, Freddie Gibbs announced that he was 75% complete with his second studio album Eastside Slim, due for release in 2014.http://www.xxlmag.com/news/2014/01/freddie-gibbs-to-release-new-album-this-year/

==Recognition==

Critics have recognized Gibbs' technically proficient and stylistically diverse rapping ability. He was featured on the Max Payne 3 official soundtrack and the NBA 2K12 official soundtrack,[tpl]cite web|title=Str8 Killa EP Review|url=http://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/14499-str8-killa-ep/|publisher=Pitchfork Media[/tpl] and more recently on the Grand Theft Auto V soundtrack.

==Influences==

Freddie Gibbs lists as his influences artists such as Scarface, Tupac Shakur, Jay-Z, Nas, Bone Thugs-N-Harmony, The Geto Boys, UGK, Three 6 Mafia, OutKast, Raekwon, Eminem, Twista, Ice Cube, Noreaga, Ol' Dirty Bastard.[tpl]cite web|last=Burgess |first=Omar |url=http://www.hiphopdx.com/index/news/id.21787/title.freddie-gibbs-describes-his-partnerships-with-madlib-and-young-jeezy |title=Freddie Gibbs Describes His Partnerships With Madlib And Young Jeezy |publisher=HipHop DX |date=2012-11-08 |accessdate=2013-06-07[/tpl]
