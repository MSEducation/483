[[Atomic playboy]]

CATEGORIES: Pop culture language

"I am not an 'atomic playboy'" is a phrase uttered by Vice Admiral William H. P. Blandy while he was overseeing the Bikini Atoll atomic bomb tests.

Video of Blandy's speech was used as the opening to the video for the 1989 song "Atomic Playboy" by Steve Stevens.
