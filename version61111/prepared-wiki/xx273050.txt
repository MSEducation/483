[[RV MTA Selen]]

CATEGORIES: 2010 ships, Active research ships of Turkey

The RV ''MTA Selen'' is a Turkish research vessel owned by the General Directorate of Mineral Research and Exploration (MTA) in Ankara and operated by its division of Geophysical Directorate for subsea geophysical exploration in shallow waters.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.mta.gov.tr/v2.0/birimler/deniz/index.php?id=selen&m=2 |publisher=Maden Teknik ve Arama Genel Müdürlüğü |title=Araştırma Gemileri-R/V MTA Selen |language=Turkish |accessdate=2011-09-25 [/tpl]

==Characteristics==

MTA Selen is [tpl]convert|20|m|ftin|abbr=on[/tpl] long, with a beam of [tpl]convert|5.60|m|ftin|abbr=on[/tpl] and a max. draft of [tpl]convert|3.30|m|ftin|abbr=on[/tpl]. Assessed at [tpl]GT|91.08[/tpl] and 30.64 NRT, the boat has a speed of [tpl]convert|9|kn|km/h[/tpl] in service. She was built in 2010 and commissioned in January 2011.
