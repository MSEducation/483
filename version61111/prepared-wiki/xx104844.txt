[[Muscat Stadium]]

CATEGORIES: Baseball venues in Japan, Sports venues in Okayama Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Muscat Stadium|マスカットスタジアム|Masukatto Sutajiamu[/tpl] is a baseball stadium located in Kurashiki Sports Park, Kurashiki, Okayama Prefecture, Japan. It has a capacity of 30,670. It is an all-seater.
