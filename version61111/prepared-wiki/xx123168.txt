[[Brenda Schad]]

CATEGORIES: American female models, American people of Cherokee descent, American people of Choctaw descent, Living people, People from Texas, 1985 births

Brenda Schad (born 1971) is an American model.
She has appeared on the cover of Cosmopolitan UK, Vogue, ELLE and GQ UK magazines. Schad has also appeared in Wonderbra advertisements. She was approached to become a model when she was 14 years old while trying on a swimsuit at a store in Japan. Schad also appeared in the movies Head over Heels and D.R.E.A.M. Team.
Schad attended Sophia University in Japan, studying political science. Schad also advocated Native American causes, and she founded the Native American Children's Fund in Oklahoma. She is of Choctaw and Cherokee descent.[tpl]fact|date=April 2012[/tpl] She speaks English, French, Italian and Japanese.
Schad was adopted by military family as a child and was raised all over the United States. In addition to modeling she is also a general contractor and has worked on projects all over the world, including Paris, Monaco, Marbella, Los Angeles, and New York.  She has two children, Sterling and Aiden.
