[[West Delhi Presbyterian Church, Manse, and Cemetery]]

CATEGORIES: Presbyterian churches in New York, Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, National Register of Historic Places in Delaware County, New York, Religious buildings completed in 1892, 19th-century Presbyterian church buildings, Churches in Delaware County, New York, Protestant Reformed cemeteries, Cemeteries in Delaware County, New York

West Delhi Presbyterian Church, Manse, and Cemetery is a historic Presbyterian church complex and cemetery at 18 and 45 Sutherland Road in West Delhi, Delaware County, New York. The church is a one story, rectangular wood frame building constructed in 1892.  It is surmounted by a steep gable roof with overhanging eaves.  The manse was built about 1840 and is a large two story wood frame building with a cross gable plan.  The West Delhi Cemetery contains the graves of most settlement era families and features stones typical of their period and style.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=10514|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: West Delhi Presbyterian Church, Manse, and Cemetery|date=June 2002|accessdate=2010-02-05 |author=Kathleen LaFrank|publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation[/tpl] See also: [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=10509|title=Accompanying six photos[/tpl]
It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 2008.
