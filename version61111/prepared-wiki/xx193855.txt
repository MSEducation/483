[[Let's Push Things Forward]]

CATEGORIES: The Streets songs, 2002 songs

"Let's Push Things Forward" is a song released in April 2002 by UK rapper The Streets. It was the second single taken from his debut album Original Pirate Material, it peaked at #30 on the UK chart. The song is performed by Mike Skinner and Kevin Mark Trail.
