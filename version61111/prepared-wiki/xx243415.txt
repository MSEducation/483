[[Asser Mbai]]

CATEGORIES: 1950 births, Living people, Members of the National Assembly of Namibia, National Unity Democratic Organisation politicians, People from Otjiwarongo

Asser Mbai (born 1950 in Otjiwarongo) is a Namibian politician. A member of National Unity Democratic Organization (NUDO), Mbai has been a member of the National Assembly of Namibia since 2005. Mbai is the acting Vice President as well as NUDO Secretary General. He replaced Mburumba Kerina in the NA following Kerina's following out with the party over the use of government funds. Prior to election to the NA, he was a regional councillor for Okakarara.NUDO's foremost trio New Era, 6 October 2009
