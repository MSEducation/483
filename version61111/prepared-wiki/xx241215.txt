[[Morin Dawa Daur Autonomous Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia, Autonomous counties of the People's Republic of China

 
Morin Dawa Daur Autonomous Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠮᠣᠷᠢᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠳᠠᠪᠠᠭ᠎ᠠ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠳᠠᠭᠤᠷ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠥᠪᠡᠷᠲᠡᠭᠡᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠵᠠᠰᠠᠬᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Morin Dabaɣ-a Daɣur öbertegen jasaqu qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|s=莫力达瓦达斡尔族自治旗 |t=莫力達瓦達斡爾族自治旗 |p=Mòlì Dáwǎ Dáwò'ěrzú Zìzhìqí[/tpl]) is one of three autonomous banners in Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China, created for the Daur people. It lies on the Nen River, borders Heilongjiang province to the east, south, and southwest, and is under the administration of Hulunbuir City.
