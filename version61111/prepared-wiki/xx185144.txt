[[Polk Township, Adair County, Missouri]]

CATEGORIES: Townships in Adair County, Missouri, Kirksville, Missouri micropolitan area, Townships in Missouri

Polk Township is one of ten townships in Adair County, Missouri, United States. As of the 2010 census, its population was 639.[tpl]cite web| url=http://factfinder2.census.gov/bkmk/table/1.0/en/DEC/10_DP/G001/0600000US2900158682| title=Geographic Identifiers: 2010 Demographic Profile Data (G001): Polk township, Adair County, Missouri| publisher=U.S. Census Bureau, American Factfinder| accessdate=June 20, 2013[/tpl] It is named for US President James K. Polk.History of Adair County by E.M. Violette. Published 1911

==Geography==

Polk Township covers an area of [tpl]convert|77.6|km2|disp=flip[/tpl] and contains no incorporated settlements.
The stream of Buck Branch runs through this township.
