[[Sânmihaiu Almașului]]

CATEGORIES: Communes in Sălaj County

Sânmihaiu Almaşului ([tpl]lang-hu|Almásszentmihály[/tpl]) is a commune located in Sălaj County, Romania. It is composed of three villages: Bercea (Bercse), Sânmihaiu Almaşului and Sântă Măria (Almásszentmária).
