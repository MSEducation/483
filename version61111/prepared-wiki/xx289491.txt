[[Durakhlu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Durakhlu ([tpl]lang-fa|دوراخلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Dūrākhlū; also known as Darākhlū)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3773161|Durakhlu[/tpl] is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 70, in 13 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
