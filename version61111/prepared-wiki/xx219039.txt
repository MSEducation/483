[[Zhou Gaoping]]

CATEGORIES: 1986 births, Living people, Chinese women's footballers, Footballers at the 2008 Summer Olympics, Olympic footballers of China, People from Taizhou, Jiangsu, Footballers from Jiangsu

Zhou Gaoping (born October 20, 1986 in Taizhou, Jiangsu) is a female Chinese football player who competed for the national team in the 2008 Summer Olympics. Her position is that of defender.
