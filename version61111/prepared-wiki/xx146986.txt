[[Más (album)]]

CATEGORIES: 1997 albums, Alejandro Sanz albums, 1997 video albums, 1997 live albums, Live video albums, Music video compilation albums, 1997 compilation albums, Warner Music Latina albums

Más is the fourth studio album recorded by Spanish singer-songwriter Alejandro Sanz.

==Track listing==

==Re-release==

Más (Edición 2006) is the re-release of the album Más containing a CD and DVD. The CD contains 13 tracks and the DVD contains 15 videos.
