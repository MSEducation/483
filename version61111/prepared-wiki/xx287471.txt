[[Ararat Park Hyatt Moscow]]

CATEGORIES: Hotels in Moscow, Hotel buildings completed in 2002

Ararat Park Hyatt Moscow is a five-star hotel in central Moscow, built in 2002[tpl]cite web|title=ARARAT PARK HYATT MOSCOW|url=http://www.allrussianhotels.com/en/hotels/moscow/Ararat_park_hyatt_moscow/|publisher=All Russian Hotels|accessdate=26 August 2013[/tpl] by Murad Sargsyan, a Russian-Armenian businessman. Today it belongs to Samvel Sargsyan, who was the governor of Armenia's Vayots Dzor provnce from 2003 to 2007 and a member of the National Assembly of Armenia from 2007 to 2012 from the Republican Party of Armenia.[tpl]cite web|title=Samvel SARGSYAN|url=http://www.parliament.am/deputies.php?sel=details&ID=853&lang=eng|publisher=National Assembly of the Republic of Armenia|accessdate=26 August 2013[/tpl]
Ararat Park Hyatt Moscow was built on the location of an Armenian restaurant from the Soviet period.
