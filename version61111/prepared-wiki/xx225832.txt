[[Hans Olsen Hafsrød]]

CATEGORIES: 1783 births, 1835 deaths, Members of the Parliament of Norway, Vestfold politicians

Hans Olsen Hafsrød (15 August 1783 – 23 May 1835) was a Norwegian politician.
He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament in 1833, representing the constituency of Jarlsberg og Laurviks Amt. He worked as a farmer there. He only served one term.Hans Olsen Hafsrød — Norwegian Social Science Data Services (NSD)
