[[1922 Drake Bulldogs football team]]

CATEGORIES: 1922 NCAA football season, Drake Bulldogs football seasons, College football undefeated seasons, 1922 in Iowa

The team by many to be considered the greatest in Drake history is to date the only undefeated Bulldog team. Drake capped the historic season with a 48-6 triumph over Mississippi State on November 25, 1922.
