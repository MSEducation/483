[[Zubovo-Polyansky District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Mordovia

Zubovo-Polyansky District ([tpl]lang-ru|Зу́бово-Поля́нский райо́н[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-mdf|Зубунь район[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-myv|Пейкужо буе[/tpl]) is an administrativeConstitution of the Republic of Mordovia and municipalLaw #12-Z district (raion), one of the twenty-two in the Republic of Mordovia, Russia. It is located in the west of the republic. The area of the district is [tpl]convert|2710|km2|sp=us[/tpl].[tpl]citation needed|date=October 2012[/tpl] Its administrative center is the urban locality (an urban-type settlement) of Zubova Polyana.Law #7-Z Population: [tpl]ru-census2010|59,256|;[/tpl] 65,735 (2002 Census);[tpl]ru-pop-ref|2002Census[/tpl] [tpl]su-census1989|69,353|.[/tpl] The population of Zubova Polyana accounts for 17.5% of the district's total population.
