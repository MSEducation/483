[[Kamen-Rybolov]]

CATEGORIES: Rural localities in Primorsky Krai

Kamen-Rybolov ([tpl]lang-ru|Ка́мень-Рыболо́в[/tpl]) is a rural locality (a selo) and the administrative center of Khankaysky District of Primorsky Krai, Russia, located on the shores of Lake Khanka. Population: [tpl]ru-census|p2010=10,909|p2002=11,769|p1989=8,569[/tpl]

==History==

It was founded by peasants who migrated from the western regions of Russia and by the Cossacks in 1865.
