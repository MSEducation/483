[[Qezeljeh-ye Olya]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Qezeljeh-ye Olya ([tpl]lang-fa|قزلجه عليا[/tpl], also Romanized as Qezeljeh-ye ‘Olyā)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795919|Qezeljeh-ye Olya[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 243, in 46 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
