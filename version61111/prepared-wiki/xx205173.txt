[[İçerenköy]]

CATEGORIES: Neighbourhoods of Kadıköy

İçerenköy is a neighbourhood located in the borough of Ataşehir on the Asian side of Istanbul, Turkey.
İçerenköy is home to the Swiss Hospital ([tpl]lang-tr|İsviçre Hastanesi[/tpl]), the 5 star hotels Greenpark and Mariott , and the secondary school Hasan Leyli Secondary School ([tpl]lang-tr|Hasan Leyli İlköğretim Okulu[/tpl]), which is the largest of its kind in Ataşehir.
Its proximity to transport amenities and ease of access, as well as its recent modernisation make it a sought after neighbourhood.
