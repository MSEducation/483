[[Church of St. Simon Stock (Bronx, New York)]]

CATEGORIES: Modernist architecture in New York, Religious organizations established in 1919, Roman Catholic churches in New York, Churches in the Bronx

The Church of St. Simon Stock is a Roman Catholic parish church under the authority of the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of New York, located at 2191 Valentine Avenue, Bronx, New York City. It was established in 1919 and has been continuously staffed by the Carmelite Fathers since its founding.
