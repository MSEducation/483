[[Kalom]]

CATEGORIES: Newspapers published in Kolkata, Bengali-language newspapers published in India, Publications established in 1981, 1981 establishments in India

Kalom ([tpl]lang-bn|কলম[/tpl]) is an Indian Bengali language daily newspaper published in Kolkata by the Kalom Welfare Association. At present its editor is Ahmed Hassan Imran[tpl]cite web|title=Mamata govt. enlists 5 more newspapers including TOI for libraries|url=http://twocircles.net/node/282835|publisher=TwoCircles|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] who is presently a Rajya Sabha MP from West Bengal with support from Trinamool Congress, Congress and Communist Party of India (Marxist).[tpl]cite web|title=Editor of pro-TMC ‘Muslim’ Bengali daily ‘Kalom’ Ahmed Hassan Imran nominated to Rajya Sabha|url=http://twocircles.net/2014jan20/editor_protmc_%E2%80%98muslim%E2%80%99_bengali_daily_%E2%80%98kalom%E2%80%99_ahmed_hassan_imran_nominated_rajya_sabha.html?utm_source=feedburner&utm_medium=feed&utm_campaign=Feed%3A+Twocirclesnet-IndianMuslim+(TwoCircles.net+-+Indian+Muslim+News)|publisher=Two Cicles|accessdate=8 February 2014[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Trinamool Congress wins four Rajya Sabha seats in West Bengal; Congress bags two in Assam, three in Andhra Pradesh|url=http://www.ndtv.com/article/india/trinamool-congress-wins-four-rajya-sabha-seats-in-west-bengal-congress-bags-two-in-assam-three-in-an-480705|publisher=NDTV|accessdate=8 February 2014[/tpl]

==History==

Kalom was launched as a monthly magazine in 1981. It became a weekly in 1983. The Saradha Group bought it in 2011 and turned it into a daily newspaper with a circulation of up to 40,000.[tpl]cite web|title=Assault charge on former chief of Saradha Group newspaper|url=http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2013-07-03/kolkata/40350234_1_saradha-group-trinamool-congress-doctor|publisher=The Times of India|accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl]
In 2013 after the Saradha Group financial scandal it was again bought by Kalom Welfare Association.
