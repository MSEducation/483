[[James P. Shannon]]

CATEGORIES: 1921 births, 2003 deaths, People from Dakota County, Minnesota, American Roman Catholic bishops, Laicized Roman Catholic bishops, Writers from Minnesota, Participants in the Second Vatican Council, Roman Catholic Church in Minnesota

James Patrick Shannon (February 16, 1921 - August 28, 2003) was a Roman Catholic bishop. Born in South St. Paul, Minnesota, James Shannon was ordained to the Catholic priesthood on June 8, 1946 for the Archdiocese of Saint Paul and Minneapolis. On February 8, 1965, Pope Paul VI appointed him to be the auxiliary bishop for the Archdiocese and he was ordained a Bishop on March 31, 1965.
