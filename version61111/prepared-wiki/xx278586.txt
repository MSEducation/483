[[Eslamabad, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Eslamabad ([tpl]lang-fa|اسلام اباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Eslāmābād and Islamabad)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3062316|Eslamabad[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 71, in 15 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
