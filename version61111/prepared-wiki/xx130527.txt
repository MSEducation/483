[[Child of Glass (album)]]

CATEGORIES: 1999 albums, Blutengel albums

Child of Glass is the first album of Blutengel.

==Track listing==

==Info==

There is also an unreleased track called "Love", sung and written by Nina Bendigkeit, from the Child of Glass recording sessions released in 1998 on the compilation Awake The Machines Vol.2
Nina Bendigkeit left the band after the album release to become a photographer and was replaced by Gini Martin in 2001.
