[[Martín Cauteruccio]]

Martín Cauteruccio (born 14 April 1987) is a Uruguayan footballer playing for San Lorenzo.

==Career==

Born in Montevideo, Cauteruccio began his professional career with local side Nacional. He joined Argentine Primera División side Quilmes, where he would lead the club in goal-scoring despite the club's struggles near the bottom of the league table.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Ovación|title=A un paso del rojo|url=http://www.ovaciondigital.com.uy/130104/futinter-686240/futbolinternacional/A-un-paso-del-rojo/|language=Spanish|date=4 January 2013[/tpl]
