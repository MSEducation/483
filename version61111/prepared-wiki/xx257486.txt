[[1980 Winnipeg Blue Bombers season]]

CATEGORIES: Winnipeg Blue Bombers seasons

The 1980 Winnipeg Blue Bombers finished in 2nd place in the West Division with a 10–6 record. They appeared in the West Final but lost 15–11 to the Edmonton Eskimos.
