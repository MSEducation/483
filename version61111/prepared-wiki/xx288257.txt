[[The Golden Girls (season 2)]]

CATEGORIES: The Golden Girls, The Golden Girls seasons

The second season of The Golden Girls premiered on NBC on September 27, 1986, and concluded on May 16, 1987. The season consisted of 26 episodes.

==Broadcast history==

The season originally aired Saturdays at 9:00-9:30 pm (EST) on NBC from September 27, 1986 to May 16, 1987.TV Listings for September 27, 1986TV Listings for May 16, 1987

==DVD release==

The Region 1, 2 and 4 DVDs were respectively released on May 15, August 1 and September 21, 2005.
