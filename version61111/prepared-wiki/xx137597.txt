Marc Berdoll (born 6 April 1953 in Trélazé, Maine-et-Loire) is a former French association football striker,[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.worldfootball.net/spieler_profil/marc-berdoll/ | title = Marc Berdoll | publisher=worldfootball.net | accessdate =25 July 2010[/tpl] who earned sixteen caps (five goals) for the French national team.
During his career he played for Angers SCO, 1. FC Saarbrücken and Olympique de Marseille, Angers SCO again, Amiens SC, US Orléans and L'Hopital. He was part of the French team in the 1978 FIFA World Cup, as he scored in the first round match against Hungary.
