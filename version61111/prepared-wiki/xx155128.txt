[[Sally Perdue]]

Sally Perdue (also known as Sally Miller and Myra Belle Miller)[tpl]cite news |title=Whitewater runs deep |publisher=National Review |date=1994-03-21 |accessdate=2008-09-11[/tpl] is a former 1958 Miss Arkansas and Little Rock radio talk show host.[tpl]cite news |title=The Press and Whitewater: Shhhh |publisher=The Economist |date=1994-02-26 |accessdate=2008-08-23[/tpl][tpl]cite news |title=Woman in Clinton affair gets $90,000; The former official at West Chester Friends filed suit, charging bias over her firing in 2003 |work=Philadelphia Inquirer |date=2005-11-08 |accessdate=2008-09-11 |author=Benjamin Y. Fulfilling a childhood dream, Perdue traveled the length of The Great Wall of China on foot in 1990.[tpl]cite news |title=Former Ark. Beauty Queen Beats Snags to Run Great Wall of China | work=The Commercial Appeal |date=1990-11-09 |accessdate=2012-02-13 |author=Associated Press |page=A15[/tpl]
In 1994, Perdue  stated that she had had an affair with then-governor Bill Clinton of Arkansas in 1983. She also stated that she had been asked not to reveal the affair by a former Democratic party staffer in 1992 who told her that "they knew that I went jogging by myself and he couldn't guarantee what would happen to my pretty little legs".[tpl]cite news |title=The Trashing of Clinton's Women |publisher=The Village Voice |date=1999-01-19 |accessdate=2008-08-23 |author=Nat Hentoff[/tpl]
In 2005, Perdue settled a lawsuit over her dismissal from employment at the West Chester (PA) Friends School.

==Education==

Miller is a 1960 graduate of Lindenwood College, and was awarded the college's 1991 Alumni Merit Award for professional accomplishment.[tpl]cite news |title=Area Universities, Colleges Graduate 22,000 This Spring; Celebrities Speak |publisher=St. Louis Post-Dispatch |date=1991-05-12 |accessdate=2008-09-11[/tpl] She earned a Masters Degree in Education from Regent University, Washington DC Campus, in 2006.[tpl]cn|date=February 2012[/tpl]
