[[Women's Asia Cup in India in 2006-07]]

CATEGORIES: One Day International cricket, Cricket in India, One Day International cricket competitions, Cricket in Asia, Women's international cricket competitions, Asia Cup

The 2006-07 Women's One-Day Internationals Asia Cup is the Asian Cricket Council Women's One Day International cricket tournament. The three teams which took part in the tournament were India , Pakistan and Sri Lanka. It was held between 13 December 2006 and 21 December 2006, in India.[tpl]cite web |url= http://www.espncricinfo.com/women/content/story/272258.html|title= India target hat-trick of titles| publisher= espncricinfo.com| date= 12 December 2006| accessdate= 10 August 2013 [/tpl] All the matches of the tournament were played at the Sawai Mansingh Stadium , Jaipur .[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.espncricinfo.com/ci/content/series/271455.html?template=ground|title= Women's Asia Cup, 2006/07 / Grounds| publisher= espncricinfo.com| accessdate= 10 August 2013[/tpl] India Women won by 8 wickets against Sri Lanka in the final.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.espncricinfo.com/women/content/story/273585.html|title= India storm to Asia Cup success | publisher= espncricinfo.com| date=21 December 2006 | accessdate= 10 August 2013[/tpl]
