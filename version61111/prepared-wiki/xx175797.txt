[[WDKD]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in South Carolina, Adult hits radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 1949, 1949 establishments in South Carolina

WDKD is a radio station at 1310 AM and licensed to Kingstree, South Carolina, USA. Owned by Miller Communications Inc., it currently airs the programming of W246BX, or "97.1 Frank FM", the HD-2 channel of WSIM.

==History==

WDKD previously simulcast WSIM, which was an oldies station with the slogan "Good Time Rock and Roll"http://www.radio-locator.com/cgi-bin/info?call=WDKD&service=AM, Retrieved on 2009-08-25. and is now an adult contemporary station.
Then it simulcast 97.1 Frank FM, which was a WSIM HD-2 channel but moved to full-power WFRK.
