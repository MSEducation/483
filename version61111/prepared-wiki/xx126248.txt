[[Molaoi]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Laconia, Monemvasia

Molaoi ([tpl]lang-el|Μολάοι[/tpl]) is a town and a former municipality in Laconia, Peloponnese, Greece. Since the 2011 local government reform it is part of the municipality Monemvasia, of which it is the seat and a municipal unit.Kallikratis law Greece Ministry of Interior [tpl]el icon[/tpl] The population in 2001 was 5,597, of which 3,021 lived in the town itself.
