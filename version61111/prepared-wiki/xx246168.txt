[[Antonio Ramon Horta AG7]]

CATEGORIES: Schools of medicine in Cuba, Law enforcement in Cuba

Antonio Ramon Horta AG-7 is a medical school in Cuba that contains 315 Pakistani students.  The campus is located in Agramonte, which is a small town in Matanzas.  The campus has more than twenty professors.
Because of poor facilities in AG7, the students forced government to be shifted to medical university near to a hospital, now this school has been fully evacuated and the students shifted to Sancti Spiritus and a provincial hospital with more best facilities.
