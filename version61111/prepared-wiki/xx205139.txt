[[Delium]]

CATEGORIES: Ancient Greek cities, Greek colonies, Geography of ancient Boeotia, Former populated places in Greece

Delium (also called Delion Greek: Δήλιον Dêlion, Latin: Delium) was a Greek cityDe'lium in Dictionary of Greek and Roman Geography, Vol. I, Abaecenum-Hytanis, Sir William Smith, 1854, (Walton & Maberly, London) famous for its important temple dedicated to Apollo, similar to the one in Delos, thence its name. Founded by colonists of Tanagra, it was located approximately 2 km from Oropos and 8 km from Tanagra.
