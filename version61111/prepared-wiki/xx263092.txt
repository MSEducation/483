[[Common Sense on Mutual Funds]]

CATEGORIES: Finance books, 1999 books

Common Sense on Mutual Funds: New Imperatives for the Intelligent Investor is a book written by John Bogle.  Since its release, it has received high accolades in the investment community.   It has become a best seller and is considered a "classic."[tpl]cite news |title= John Bogle |url=http://www.pbs.org/now/politics/bogle.html|publisher= NOW on PBS |author=David Brancaccio|date= October 3, 2010|archiveurl = http://www.webcitation.org/5wCfTzkJD |archivedate = 2011-02-02|deadurl=no[/tpl][tpl]cite news |title=A visit with John Bogle |url=http://seattletimes.nwsource.com/html/businesstechnology/2011248667_burns14.html|publisher= Seattle Times |author=Scott Burns |date= March 13, 2010|archiveurl = http://www.webcitation.org/5wCfV8noI |archivedate = 2011-02-02|deadurl=no[/tpl]  ConsumerAffairs.com rated it on its "15 Business Books That Could Actually Help Make You Rich" list.[tpl]cite news |title=Here Are 15 Business Books That Could Actually Help Make You Rich|url=http://www.consumeraffairs.com/news04/2010/12/here-are-15-business-books-that-could-actually-help-make-you-rich.html |publisher= ConsumerAffairs.com |date= December 18, 2010| author =Yager, Fred|archiveurl = http://www.webcitation.org/5wCfWNcnA |archivedate = 2011-02-02|deadurl=no[/tpl]  Despite it being aimed at American audiences, the British newspaper The Independent stated "there is nothing in it that does not apply in some measure to the UK fund industry."[tpl]cite news |title=Investment: Simplicity and low costs are key to success  |url=http://www.independent.co.uk/news/business/investment-simplicity-and-low-costs-are-key-to-success-1094473.html |publisher= The Independent |date= May 19, 1999| author =Davis, Johnathan|archiveurl = http://www.webcitation.org/5wCfWt4x1 |archivedate = 2011-02-02|deadurl=no[/tpl]
The book gives advice to investors about mutual funds, the praise of index funds and the importance of having a long term strategy.  On the dust jacket cover, Jim Cramer wrote, "After a lifetime of picking stocks, I have to admit that (Vanguard Group founder John) Bogle's arguments in favor of the index fund have me thinking of joining him rather than trying to beat him."[tpl]cite news |title=Managing Your Money|url=http://www.usatoday.com/money/perfi/columnist/krantz/2006-04-03-jim-cramer_x.htm |publisher= USA Today |date= May 3, 2006| author =Krantz, Matt |archiveurl = http://www.webcitation.org/5wCfYboge |archivedate = 2011-02-02|deadurl=no[/tpl]
