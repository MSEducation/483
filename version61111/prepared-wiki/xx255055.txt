[[Loris Benito]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1992 births, Swiss footballers, Association football midfielders, FC Aarau players, FC Zürich players, Swiss Super League players

Loris Benito (born 7 January 1992) is a Swiss footballer who plays for FC Zürich in the Swiss Super League, as a defender and midfielder. On 10 March 2013, in an away match against FC Thun, Benito was bitten on the finger by a beech marten he had caught after it invaded the pitch."Marten runs wild on field, bites player during soccer match in Switzerland" National Post. Retrieved on 11 March 2013.
