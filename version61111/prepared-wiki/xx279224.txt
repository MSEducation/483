[[Mazraeh-ye Amini]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Mazraeh-ye Amini ([tpl]lang-fa|مزرعه اميني[/tpl], also Romanized as Mazra‘eh-ye Amīnī) is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
