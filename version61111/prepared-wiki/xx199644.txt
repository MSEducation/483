[[Run for Your Life (novel)]]

CATEGORIES: 2009 novels, Novels by James Patterson, Thriller novels

Run for Your Life, published in 2009, is the second novel in the Michael Bennett series by the American authors James Patterson and Michael Ledwidge. The novel debuted on the New York Times Best-Seller list at number 2 on February 20, 2009.[tpl]cite news |title=Inside the List |first=Jennifer |last=Schuessler |newspaper=New York Times |date=February 20, 2009 |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2009/02/22/books/review/InsideList-t.html |accessdate=October 21, 2010[/tpl]

==Critical reception==
