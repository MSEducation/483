[[Urs Salzmann]]

CATEGORIES: 1954 births, Bobsledders at the 1984 Winter Olympics, Living people, Swiss bobsledders, Olympic bobsledders of Switzerland, Olympic bronze medalists for Switzerland, Olympic medalists in bobsleigh, Medalists at the 1984 Winter Olympics

Urs Salzmann (born July 3, 1954) is a Swiss bobsledder who competed during the early to mid-1980s. He won a bronze medal in the four-man event at the 1984 Winter Olympics in Sarajevo.
Salzmann also won two medals in the four-man event at the FIBT World Championships with a gold in 1982 and a bronze in 1985.
