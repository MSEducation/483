[[Oli Rahman]]

CATEGORIES: 1975 births, Living people, Ghanaian footballers, Footballers at the 1992 Summer Olympics, Olympic footballers of Ghana, Olympic bronze medalists for Ghana, Place of birth missing (living people), Olympic medalists in football

Oli Rahman (born 7 July 1975) is a football player from Ghana, who was a member of the Men's National Team that won the bronze medal at the 1992 Summer Olympics in Barcelona, Spain.
