[[J. Jackson (Leicestershire cricketer)]]

CATEGORIES: English cricketers, English cricketers of 1787 to 1825, Leicestershire cricketers, Year of birth unknown, Year of death unknown

J Jackson (dates unknown) was an English professional cricketer who made 3 known appearances in major cricket matches from 1794 to 1800.

==Career==

He was mainly associated with Leicestershire but he also played in London, including one appearance for The Thursday Club.Arthur Haygarth, Scores & Biographies, Volume 1 (1744-1826), Lillywhite, 1862
