[[Susanna Coffey]]

CATEGORIES: American women painters, Living people, American portrait painters, People from New London County, Connecticut, Artists from New York City, 1949 births, Painters from Connecticut, Guggenheim Fellows

Susanna J. Coffey (born 1949) is an American artist who was born in New London, Connecticut.  She received a Bachelor of Fine Art degree Magna Cum Laude from the University of Connecticut at Storrs, Connecticut in 1977 and a Master of Fine Arts degree from the Yale School of Art in 1982.  She currently lives and works in New York City. She was elected into the National Academy of Design in 1999.Susanna J. Coffey in AskArt.com
