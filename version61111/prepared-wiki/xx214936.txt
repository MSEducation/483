Sergio Álvarez Boulet (born 11 October 1979) is a Cuban weightlifter.
He represented Cuba at the 2000 Summer Olympics, ranking 5th.sports-reference
He participated in the men's -56 kg class at the 2006 World Weightlifting Championships and won the silver medal, finishing behind Li Zheng. He snatched 123 kg and clean and jerked an additional 156 kg for a total of 279 kg, only 1 kg behind winner Li.75th MEN'S and 18th WOMEN'S WORLD CHAMPIONSHIPS -56 KG MEN, iwf.net
He twice won the gold medal at the Pan American Games in the 56 kg category.
