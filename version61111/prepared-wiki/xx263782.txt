[[Eero Kilpeläinen]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 births, Living people, Ässät players, Dallas Stars draft picks, Finnish ice hockey goaltenders, Jokerit players, KalPa players, Peterborough Petes (ice hockey) players

Eero Kilpeläinen (born May 7, 1985) is a Finnish professional ice hockey goaltender who currently plays for EV Zug of the National League A in Switzerland. He was selected by the Dallas Stars in the 5th round (144th overall) of the 2003 NHL Entry Draft.
