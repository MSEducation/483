[[Douna Department]]

CATEGORIES: Departments of Burkina Faso, Léraba Province

 Douna is a department or commune of Léraba Province in south-western  Burkina Faso. Its capital lies at the town of Douna. According to the 1996 census the department has a total population of 9,132.Burkinabé government inforoute communale
