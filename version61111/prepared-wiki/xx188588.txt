[[Beyond the Permafrost]]

CATEGORIES: 2007 albums, Skeletonwitch albums, Albums with cover art by John Dyer Baizley, Thrash metal albums

Beyond the Permafrost is the second album by American thrash/black metal band Skeletonwitch. The album blends thrash metal and influence from the NWOBHM movement with death/black metal vocals.CoC : Skeletonwitch - Beyond the Permafrost : Review The songs, while aggressive, also have melodic parts.Skeletonwitch - Beyond The Permafrost Review
