[[Liz Winfield]]

CATEGORIES: 1964 births, Living people, Australian poets

Liz Winfield (born 1964) is a contemporary Australian poet.
Liz Winfield lives in Hobart, Tasmania. In 1999 Liz instigated the Republic Readings in Hobart and has coordinated them ever since. She is a poetry editor for Famous Reporter and is young persons' liaison officer for the Fellowship of Australian Writers' (FAW) Tasmanian branch. Liz's collection Too Much Happens appeared from Cornford Press in 2003. Her second collection, Catalogue of Love—a chapbook from Walleah Press—was launched at the Tasmanian Poetry Festival in Launceston in October 2006.
