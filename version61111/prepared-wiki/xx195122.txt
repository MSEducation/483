[[MV Pool Fisher]]

CATEGORIES: Shipwrecks in the English Channel, Wreck diving sites in the United Kingdom, Maritime incidents in 1979

The MV Pool Fisher was a merchant vessel that sank off the Isle of Wight in November 1979 with the loss of most of her crew.
