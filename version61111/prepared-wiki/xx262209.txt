James Wilson (died January 1857) was an Irish bishop of the Church of Ireland.
A native of Dublin, The Times, Wednesday, Jan 07, 1857; pg. 10; Issue 22571; col A  Ireland he was educated at Trinity College, Dublin and was for 17 years Curate at  St. Audoen's Church, Dublin.
