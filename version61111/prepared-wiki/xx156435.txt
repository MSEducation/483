[[Kim Bauermeister]]

CATEGORIES: 1970 births, Living people, German long-distance runners, Athletes (track and field) at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Olympic athletes of Germany, Sportspeople from Stuttgart

Kim Bauermeister (born 20 November 1970 in Stuttgart) is a retired German runner who specialized in the 3000 metres steeplechase.
He won the bronze medal at the 1989 European Junior Championships and the gold medal in 3000 metres at the 1994 European Indoor Championships. He competed at the World Championships in 1993 and 19951993 World Championships Results - 3000 Metres Steeplechase - Men - Heats (IAAF)1995 World Championships Results - 3000 Metres Steeplechase - Men - Semi-Final (IAAF) as well as the 1996 Summer Olympics without reaching the finals.
His personal best time is 8:23.19 minutes, achieved in August 1994 in Berlin.
He won one national silver medal in steeplechase, in 1994.German championships, men's 3000 m steeplechase
