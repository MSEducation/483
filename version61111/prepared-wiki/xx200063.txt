[[Alex Davies (snooker player)]]

CATEGORIES: 1987 births, Living people, English snooker players, Sportspeople from Essex

Alex Davies (born 27 July 1987 in Essex, England) is a professional snooker player. He is the youngest person ever to win the English Amateur Championship, in 2003.[tpl]cite web |url= http://www.guardian.co.uk/sport/2003/may/12/snooker.cliveeverton|title= Ronnie sees red on yellow|accessdate=2008-05-21 |author= Clive Everton|date= 2003-05-12 |publisher=The Guardian[/tpl] Davies began his professional career by playing Challenge Tour in 2003, at the time the second-level professional tour.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.cuetracker.net/pages/players.php?ID=600&season=2003/2004 | title=Alex Davies - Season 2003/2004 | accessdate=26 May 2013[/tpl] Davies was on the WPBSA Main Tour for the 2007/2008 season, but dropped off.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.globalsnookercentre.co.uk/files/Players/Global_Europe/Global_England/eng_alex_davies.htm |title=ALEX DAVIES (England)|accessdate=2008-05-21 |publisher=Global Snooker Centre| archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20080630081511/http://www.globalsnookercentre.co.uk/files/Players/Global_Europe/Global_England/eng_alex_davies.htm| archivedate= 30 June 2008 | deadurl= no[/tpl] In May 2013, he returned to the main tour by winning five matches at the Q School event one concluding with a 4–1 win over Mitchell Travis to earn a place on the tour for the 2013/2014 and 2014/2015 season.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.snooker.org/res/index.asp?event=251 | title=Qualifying School - Event One | publisher=Snooker.org | accessdate=31 May 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web | url=http://www.worldsnooker.com/page/NewsArticles/0,,13165~3178842,00.html | title=Quartet Earn Tour Cards | publisher=World Snooker | accessdate=31 May 2013[/tpl]

==Career==

===2013/2014 season===

Davies made a dream start to the season as in his first match he defeated world number four Shaun Murphy 5–1 to qualify for the 2013 Wuxi Classic in China where he lost 5–2 to Joel Walker in the first round.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.worldsnooker.com/page/NewsArticles/0,,13165~3195338,00.html | title=Murphy Misses Out On Wuxi | publisher=World Snooker | accessdate=31 May 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite web | url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/snooker/22716590 | title=Shaun Murphy fails to qualify for season-opening major | publisher=BBC Sport | accessdate=2 June 2013[/tpl] He also qualified for the Indian Open, International Championship and World Open, but lost in the first round of each.[tpl]cite web|title=Alex Davies 2013/2014|url=http://www.snooker.org/res/index.asp?season=2013&player=144|publisher=Snooker.org|accessdate=11 April 2014[/tpl]
