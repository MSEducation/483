[[It's Heavy in Here]]

CATEGORIES: 1995 albums, Eric Matthews albums, Sub Pop albums

It's Heavy in Here is the debut solo studio album by American singer-songwriter Eric Matthews. It was released by Sub Pop Records on September 26, 1995.Sub Pop Records official website catalogue A track from the album, "Fanfare", was released as a single in the UK only, but failed to chart.
