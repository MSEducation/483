[[Dutch FilmWorks]]

CATEGORIES: Dutch film producers

Dutch FilmWorks is a Dutch film distributor founded in 1998, based in Utrecht, the Netherlands, focusing on Benelux rights[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/hr/content_display/world/news/e3i87155f066147ca398a2a6cd4f1dba025|title=Dutch Filmworks seals slew of Benelux deals|last=Kemp|first=Stuart|date=2008-11-18|publisher=The Hollywood Reporter|accessdate=22 July 2010|quote=Indie distributor Dutch Filmworks on Tuesday reported several high-profile acquisitions in the immediate aftermath of the American Film Market, snapping up Benelux rights to a slew of titles[/tpl] [tpl]Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot[/tpl] mainly to release films theatrically, on DVD, Blu-ray and VOD.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.screendaily.com/dutch-filmworks-acquires-diverse-dozen/4016377.article|title=Dutch FilmWorks acquires diverse dozen|last=Zagt|first=Ab|date=2003-12-08|publisher=Screendaily|accessdate=22 July 2010|location=main line of business in the Netherlands is releasing films on DVD and video.[/tpl] They are also a publisher and distributor of books and magazines.
They have recently released several TV series in the CBS library on DVD, under license from CBS Home Entertainment and Paramount Pictures.

==Background==

Dutch Filmworks purchases film licenses and releases these in the Benelux, and has a distribution library of over 2,500 titles. Large titles distributed by Dutch FilmWorks include Exorcist: The Prequel,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.variety.com/review/VE1117926588.html?categoryid=1263&cs=1|title=Exorcist: The Prequel|last=Felperin|first=Leslie|date=2005-03-18|publisher=Variety|accessdate=22 July 2010|quote=A Morgan Creek, DFW Dutch Filmworks presentation[/tpl] The Grudge, The Aviator, Unleashed, The Final Cut and Saw, as well as Dutch products such as the television series Westenwind and the film 06/05.
'The House of Knowledge' is a separate branch of the business that distributes documentaries, including those from Discovery Channel.
