==Early life and education==

Khomeini was born in Qom on 12 December 1930.[tpl]cite web|title=Biography and Struggles of Ayatollah Sayyid Mustafa Khomeini|url=http://en.imam-khomeini.ir/en/n3134/Imam_s_relatives/Sayyid_Mustafa_Khomeini/Biography_and_Struggles_of__Ayatollah_Sayyid_Mustafa_Khomeini|work=Imam Khomeini|accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl] He was the eldest son of Ayatollah Khomeini and Khadijeh Saqafi, daughter of a respected cleric, Hajj Mirza Tehrani.[tpl]cite book|last=Dabashi|first=H.|title=Theology of discont|year=1993|publisher=New York University Press|location=New York|url=http://online.sfsu.edu/mroozbeh/CLASS/H-606-pdfs/Khomeini-Dabashi.pdf[/tpl] 
He graduated from the Qom Theological Center.

==Activities==

Khomeini participated in his father's movement. He was arrested and imprisoned after the 1963 events and also, after his father's exile. On 3 January 1965, he joined his father in Bursa, Turkey, where he was in exile.[tpl]cite book|author=Baqer Moin|title=Khomeini: Life of the Ayatollah|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=b2OL9IEXaAgC&pg=PA136|accessdate=9 August 2013|year=1999|publisher=I.B.Tauris|isbn=978-1-85043-128-2|pages=136[/tpl]  Then he lived with his family in Najaf, Iraq. He and his brother Ahmad became part of Khomeini's underground movement in Najaf.[tpl]cite journal|last=Samii|first=Abbas William|title=The Shah's Lebanon policy: the role of SAVAK|journal=Middle Eastern Studies|year=1997|volume=33|issue=1|pages=66-91|url=http://dx.doi.org/10.1080/00263209708701142|accessdate=21 August 2013[/tpl] The group also included Mohammad Hussein Behesti and Morteza Motahhari.

==Death==

Khomeini died in Najaf on 23 October 1977.[tpl]cite book|author=Michael Axworthy|title=Revolutionary Iran: A History of the Islamic Republic|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=XYoeAAAAQBAJ&pg=PA99|accessdate=9 August 2013|date=1 September 2013|publisher=Oxford University Press|isbn=978-0-19-932226-8|pages=99[/tpl] He was buried in Najaf.[tpl]cite news|last=Zabeeh|first=Ismail|title=Mustafa Khomeini's tomb reopens|url=http://www.jafariyanews.com/2k7_news/jan/4Mustafa_Khomeini_tomb.htm|accessdate=9 August 2013|newspaper=Jafariya News|date=4 January 2007[/tpl] 
His death has been regarded as suspicious and the followers of Ayatollah Khomeini alleged SAVAK for his death. His father described Mostafa's death as one of hidden favors of God.[tpl]cite book|author=Abdar Rahman Koya|title=Imam Khomeini: Life, Thought and Legacy|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=HMd5C4KaOgkC&pg=PA35|accessdate=9 August 2013|year=2009|publisher=The Other Press|isbn=978-967-5062-25-4|pages=35[/tpl]
