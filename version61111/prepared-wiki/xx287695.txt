[[2006 South American Race Walking Championships]]

CATEGORIES: South American Race Walking Championships, 2006 in athletics (track and field), Sports competitions in Bolivia, Athletics in Bolivia, 2006 in Bolivia

The 2006 South American Race Walking Championships were held in Cochabamba, Bolivia, on April 8–9, 2006.   The track of the championship runs in the Paseo El Prado (Avenida Ballivián).
A detailed report on the event and an appraisal of the results was given by Eduardo Biscayart for the IAAF.
Complete results were published.  The junior events are doumented on the World Junior Athletics History webpages.
