Jeffery Renard Allen (born 1962 Chicago) is an American poet and novelist.[tpl]cite book| url=http://books.google.com/books?id=XG--8X3YDs4C&pg=PA32&dq=Jeffrey+Renard+Allen&cd=3#v=onepage&q=Jeffrey%20Renard%20Allen&f=false| title=The Columbia guide to contemporary African American fiction| author=Darryl Dickson-Carr|publisher=Columbia University Press| year= 2005| isbn= 978-0-231-12472-0 [/tpl]

==Life==

He graduated the University of Illinois at Chicago, with a Ph.D.   
He teaches at Queens College,http://queenscollegemfa.blogspot.com/2008_08_01_archive.html The New School for Social Research, and is a fellow at the New York Public Library's Center for Scholars and Writers.http://www.spectrumofpoeticfire.com/Reader%20Directory/Jeffrey_Renard_Allen.htm
His work appeared in The Antioch Review,http://books.google.com/books?id=wfkDAAAAYAAJ&q=Jeffrey+Renard+Allen&dq=Jeffrey+Renard+Allen&lr=&cd=17 Arkansas Review, African Voices, African American Review, Bomb,http://www.bombsite.com/issues/80/articles/2493 Black Renaissance Noire, Callaloo,http://muse.jhu.edu/login?uri=/journals/callaloo/v030/30.2allen.html The Chicago Tribune, Hambone, The Literary Review, Notre Dame Review, Other Voices, Poets & Writers, Ploughshares, StoryQuarterly, Triquarterly, and XCP: Cross Cultural Poetics. 
He lives in Far Rockaway, Queens.http://www.pw.org/content/jeffery_renard_allenhttp://www.educationupdate.com/archives/2007/FEB/html/cov-wecelebrate.html
