[[Pennypit Park]]

CATEGORIES: Football venues in Scotland, Rugby union stadiums in Scotland, Sports venues in East Lothian

Pennypit Park is a sports complex located in the town of Prestonpans in Scotland. It consists of two sports fields, one for association football and the other rugby union. It is the home of Scottish Lowland Football League club Preston Athletic F.C. and rugby union club Preston Lodge RFC.Details for Pennypit Park, Prestonpans, worldstadia.com. Retrieved 11 August 2013.
