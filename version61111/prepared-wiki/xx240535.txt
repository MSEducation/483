[[Kalbir Takher]]

CATEGORIES: 1968 births, Living people, English field hockey players, Olympic field hockey players of Great Britain, Field hockey players at the 1996 Summer Olympics, Sportspeople from Wolverhampton

Kalbir Singh Takher (born August 3, 1968 in Wolverhampton, United Kingdom) is an English field hockey player, who participated in the Summer Olympics for Great Britain: in 1996.
Kalbir, nicknamed Kali played club hockey for Cannock and continues to coach hockey for a number of clubs and ages.
