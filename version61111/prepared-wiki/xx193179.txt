[[Norm Yellowlees]]

CATEGORIES: Canadian ice hockey centres, Ice hockey people from Manitoba, Winnipeg Monarchs players, 1912 births, 1991 deaths

Norm Yellowlees (Born - March 17, 1912 in Winnipeg, Manitoba, Died - October 14, 1991) was a Canadian ice hockey centre who won the 1935 World Hockey championship with the Winnipeg Senior Monarchs in Davos, Switzerland.
