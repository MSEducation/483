[[Bazin, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Bazin ([tpl]lang-fa|بزين[/tpl], also Romanized as Bazīn, Bezīn, and Bozīn; also known as Barīn, Bīzīn, and Bīzīū)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3057001|Bazin[/tpl] is a village in Bizineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,754, in 433 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
