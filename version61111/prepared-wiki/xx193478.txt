[[Paolo Damiani]]

CATEGORIES: Italian jazz musicians, Post-bop double-bassists, Musicians from Rome, 1952 births, Living people, Avant-garde jazz cellists, Avant-garde jazz double-bassists

Paolo Damiani (born 1952) is an Italian jazz cellist and double-bassist. Performing primarily in the avant-garde jazz and post bop mediums, Damiani was born in Rome, Italy. He has performed over his career with a vast array of professional jazz musicians, including Gianluigi Trovesi, Kenny Wheeler, Tony Oxley, Paolo Fresu and John Taylor.
