[[Sergio Arredondo]]

CATEGORIES: Chilean Army personnel, Living people

Sergio Arredondo Gonzalez participated in the Caravan of Death in 1973 in Chile.http://www.memoriayjusticia.cl/english/en_focus-caravan.html He was the second in command to Sergio Arellano Stark who led the Caravan of Death. This death squad went out immediately following a successful coup and are said to be responsible for at least 75 deaths. The people who were executed were primarily non-hostile and already in custody, many were teachers, political organizers, or union leaders.The Judge and the General. Documentary 2008. After the Caravan tour of duty, he also was promoted, to the director of the Infantry School.
