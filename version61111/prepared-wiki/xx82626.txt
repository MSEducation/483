[[Gramophone Award]]

CATEGORIES: British music awards, Classical music awards

The 'Gramophone'' Awards''', launched in 1977, are one of the most significant honours bestowed on recordings in the classical record industry,http://entertainment.timesonline.co.uk/tol/arts_and_entertainment/music/article2260637.ece often referred to as the ''Oscars'' for classical music.http://www.dallassymphony.com/Press_Releases.aspx?aID=7http://www.prestoclassical.co.uk/gramophone.phphttp://www.classicfm.co.uk/music/music-news-and-features/classic-fm-gramophone-awards The winners are selected annually by critics for the Gramophone'''' magazine and various members of the industry, including retailers, broadcasters, arts administrators, and musicians. Awards are usually presented in September each year in London.
