[[Kristijan Polovanec]]

CATEGORIES: 1979 births, Living people, Croatian footballers, Sportspeople from Zagreb, GNK Dinamo Zagreb players, NK Varaždin players, NK Inter Zaprešić players, FC Libourne-Saint-Seurin players, Association football defenders, HNK Segesta players

Kristijan Polovanec (born 10 October 1979 in Zagreb) is a Croatian professional footballer who plays for NK Radnik Sesvete.

==Club career==

Polovanec previously played for NK Dinamo Zagreb, NK Istra, NK Varteks, NK Međimurje, Inter Zaprešić in the Croatian Prva HNL and with Slovenian club FC Koper.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Nogometni Magazin|title=Statistika: Kristijan Polovanec|url=http://www.1hnl.net/igrac.php?id=797|accessdate=2008-12-08[/tpl]
He played two seasons with FC Libourne-Saint-Seurin in the French Ligue 2.[tpl]Lfpfr|first=Kristijan|last=Polovanec[/tpl]
