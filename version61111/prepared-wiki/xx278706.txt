[[LeHi, Arkansas]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Crittenden County, Arkansas, Unincorporated communities in Arkansas

LeHi is an unincorporated community in Crittenden County, Arkansas, located at the intersection of I-49/US-79/US-63 and AR-147. http://arkansas.hometownlocator.com/ar/crittenden/lehi.cfm  It is perhaps best known as the location of a former NASCAR track, Memphis-Arkansas Speedway. http://fantasyracingcheatsheet.com/nascar/tracks/memphis-arkansas-speedway/29
