[[RADA Electronic Industries]]

CATEGORIES: Companies listed on NASDAQ, Information technology companies of Israel, Companies established in 1970, Defense companies of Israel

RADA Electronic Industries is an Israeli company specializing in defense electronics. It furnishes its clients with data recording and management systems, navigation systems, and radar sensors, among other products.[tpl]cite web|title=Rada Electronic Industries Ltd (RADA.OQ) Company Profile|url=http://www.reuters.com/finance/stocks/companyProfile?symbol=RADA.OQ|work=Reuters|accessdate=23 September 2011[/tpl] Its shares are traded on the NASDAQ Capital Market.
