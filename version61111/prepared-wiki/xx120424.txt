[[Cytaea levii]]

CATEGORIES: Salticidae, Animals described in 2002

Cytaea levii is a species of jumping spider.

==Appearance==

The species is allied to C. alburna.

==Name==

C. levii is named in honor of H.W. Levi, a mentor of one of the first describers at Harvard University.

==Distribution==

C. levii is only known from Taiwan.
