[[Starovokzalna (Kiev Light Rail)]]

CATEGORIES: Kiev Light Rail stations

Starovokzalna ([tpl]lang-uk|Старовокзальна[/tpl]) is a station on the Kiev Light Rail. It was originally opened in 1989 as a tram stop. On October 16, 2010 the station was reopened after reconstruction to service light rail trams.
