[[The Indie Rock Essentials]]

CATEGORIES: Jean Grae albums, Blue Sky Black Death albums, 2009 compilation albums, Babygrande Records compilation albums

The Indie Rock Essentials is a compilation album compiled from songs by Blue Sky Black Death, released on 1 September 2009. Artists featured on this album include Jean Grae, Yes Alexander, Holocaust, Hell Razah, and Ashley Wise; taking songs from the Blue Sky Black Death albums Late Night Cinema, The Evil Jeanius, The Holocaust, Slow Burning Lights, and Razah's Ladder.
