Tore Brovold (born June 12, 1970)  is a Norwegian sport shooter competing in Skeet. Brovold is one of the Norwegian athletes competing in the 2008 Summer Olympics in Beijing, where he won a silver medal.[ref]
 [tpl]Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot[/tpl][/ref] Later the same year, he won the 2008 ISSF World Cup Final.
Brovold started shooting in 1977, when he was only 7 years, and participated in his first competition two years later. He started with Skeet in 1996.
