[[Line 17 (Stockholm metro)]]

CATEGORIES: Stockholm metro

Line 17 (T17) is one of the three green lines of the Stockholm Metro connecting Åkeshov and Skarpnäck. It is a 19.6 km (12.2 mi) long line with a 43 min travel time and 24 stations. However, in the late evenings & early mornings, the trains on the T17 line only run as far as Odenplan. Use the T18 and T19 lines instead.
