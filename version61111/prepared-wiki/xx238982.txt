[[The Dynamic Superiors (album)]]

CATEGORIES: 1975 debut albums, The Dynamic Superiors albums, Albums produced by Ashford & Simpson, English-language albums, Motown albums

The Dynamic Superiors is the debut album for The Dynamic Superiors on Motown Records.  Released in 1975, it contains the hits "Shoe Shoe Shine" and "Leave It Alone". After a 35 year wait, in 2010,  this album was finally released on CD in a 2-for-1 set with The Superiors'  second Motown album, Pure Pleasure, not by Motown, but by Universal's Soul Music.com imprint. http://www.amazon.com/dp/B0040U8XTG Selected Tracks for the group's 2 album CD release.http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MBWFzuJ2Xh8
The album was arranged by Leon Pendarvis, Arthur Jenkins, Paul Riser and Richard Tee...and written and produced by the husband-and-wife team of Nick Ashford and Valerie Simpson .
