[[Qarah Kul]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Qarah Kul ([tpl]lang-fa|قره كول[/tpl], also Romanized as Qarah Kūl, Qareh Kowl, and Qareh Kūl; also known as Karakol, Qarakol, and Qareh Kol)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3079710|Qarah Kul[/tpl] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 423, in 104 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
