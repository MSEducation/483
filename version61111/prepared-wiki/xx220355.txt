[[Martin Schirenc]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1968 births, Death metal musicians

Martin Schirenc (born December 18, 1968 in Vienna) is an Austrian guitarist and vocalist, best known as a founder of death metal/grindcore band Pungent Stench[tpl]cite web|title=Personal Discography|url=http://blogs.myspace.com/index.cfm?fuseaction=blog.view&friendId=233101166&blogId=302193713|work=MySpace music|accessdate=9 November 2010[/tpl] and symphonic death metal band Hollenthon.
His former wife, Elena, is a member and main lyricist of Hollenthon.
