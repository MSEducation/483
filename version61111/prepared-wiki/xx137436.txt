[[Ablade Kumah]]

CATEGORIES: 1970 births, Living people, Ghanaian footballers, Footballers at the 1992 Summer Olympics, Olympic footballers of Ghana, Olympic bronze medalists for Ghana, 1996 African Cup of Nations players, Place of birth missing (living people), Olympic medalists in football

Samuel Ablade Kumah (born 26 June 1970) is a football player from Ghana, who was a member of the Men's National Team that won the bronze medal at the 1992 Summer Olympics in Barcelona, Spain.

==Club football==

Ablade Kumah played most of his club football in Ghana with Accra Hearts of Oak SC between 1987 and 1993.[tpl]cite web |url=http://phobiatalk.yuku.com/topic/899/t/Old-Players-Database.html |title=Old Players Database |accessdate=2010-08-24 |publisher=Phobia forum[/tpl] He played as a midfielder.

==International football==

Ablade Kumah was a member of the Ghana football team at the 1992 Barcelona Olympics where they won the bronze medal.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.fifa.com/worldfootball/statisticsandrecords/players/player=93142/index.html |title=Ablade KUMAH |accessdate=2010-08-24 |publisher=FIFA[/tpl]
