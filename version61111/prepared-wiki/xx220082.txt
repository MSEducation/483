[[Herbert McGregor]]

CATEGORIES: 1981 births, Living people, Jamaican long jumpers, Athletes (track and field) at the 2008 Summer Olympics, Athletes (track and field) at the 2011 Pan American Games, Olympic athletes of Jamaica

Herbert McGregor (born 13 September 1981) is a Jamaican long jumper. His personal best jump is 8.08 metres, achieved in May 2008 in Fort-de-France.
At the 2006 Central American and Caribbean Games he finished fourth in the long jump2006 Central American and Caribbean Games results, men's long jump final and won a bronze medal in the 4 x 100 metres relay. He also competed at the 2008 Olympic Games without reaching the final.
