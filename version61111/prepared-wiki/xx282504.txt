[[LD&ECR Class B]]

CATEGORIES: Lancashire, Derbyshire and East Coast Railway locomotives, 0-6-2T locomotives, Kitson locomotives, Railway locomotives introduced in 1895, Scrapped locomotives

The LD&ECR class B (LNER Class J60) was a class of 0-6-0T steam locomotives of the Lancashire, Derbyshire and East Coast Railway.
Four were built in 1897.[tpl]sfn|Dow|1965|p=374[/tpl] Three were normally based at Langwith Junction and the other at Tuxford. In later years they moved to Wrexham, with one transferring to Gorton as the works shunter.Class B: via LNER encyclopedia
