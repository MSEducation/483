[[Isham and Burton Latimer railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Disused railway stations in Northamptonshire, Former Midland Railway stations, Railway stations opened in 1857, Railway stations closed in 1950

Isham and Burton Latimer railway station was built by the Midland Railway in 1857 to serve the villages of Isham and Burton Latimer on its extension from Leicester to Bedford and Hitchin. It closed in 1950 though the buildings remain unused.Radford, B., (1983) Midland Line Memories: a Pictorial History of the Midland Railway Main Line Between London (St Pancras) & Derby London: Bloomsbury Books
Slightly north of it was a branch line which ran until 1959 to Thrapston and Huntingdon.
