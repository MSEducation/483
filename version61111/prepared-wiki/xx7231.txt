[[Macroeconomics]]

CATEGORIES: Macroeconomics

Macroeconomics (from the Greek prefix makro- meaning "large" and economics) is a branch of economics dealing with the performance, structure, behavior, and decision-making of an economy as a whole, rather than individual markets. This includes national, regional, and global economies.[tpl]Citation |author= Blaug, Mark |title=Economic theory in retrospect |publisher=Cambridge University Press |location=Cambridge, UK |year=1985 |isbn=0-521-31644-8[/tpl][ref]
