[[Kahab]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Kahab ([tpl]lang-fa|كهاب[/tpl], also Romanized as Kahāb; also known as Kakhab, Qahāb (Persian: قهاب), and Qohāb)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3078756|Kahab[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 219, in 53 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
