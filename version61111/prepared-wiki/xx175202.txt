[[KHAC]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in New Mexico, Moody Radio affiliate stations

KHAC (880 AM) is a radio station  broadcasting a Christian radio format. Licenced to Tse Bonito, New Mexico, USA.  The station is currently owned by Western Indian Ministries, Inc..
