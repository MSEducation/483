National Camogie League medals in 2009, 2010 and 2011; National League Div two 2009. Leinster Championship 2009, 2010, 2011. Three All-Ireland Féile na nGael 2000, 2001, 2002; Winner of Leinster and All-Ireland Senior medals in Colleges with [http://www.colaistebride.ie/ Coláiste Bríde\ 2003, 2004, 2005; All-Ireland Junior Colleges with Coláiste Bríde 2004; Club Senior 2003, 2004, 2005, 2006, 2007, 2009; Leinster Club Senior 2009; All Ireland club sevens 2006; Leinster Under-14 2002.
