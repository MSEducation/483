[[Battle Cry (album)]]

CATEGORIES: Omen (band) albums, 1984 debut albums, Metal Blade Records albums

Battle Cry is the debut album of the American heavy metal band Omen. It was originally released in 1984 by Metal Blade.
In 2005 Metal Blade re-released Battle Cry on Picture LP format, limited to 500 copies and including two bonus tracks.
