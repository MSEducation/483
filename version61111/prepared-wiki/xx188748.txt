[[Chongos Alto District]]

Chongos Alto District is one of twenty-eight districts of the province Huancayo in Peru.[tpl]es icon[/tpl] Instituto Nacional de Estadística e Informática. Banco de Información Distrital. Retrieved April 11, 2008. Is located in the Department of Junin, part of the Junín Region.
