[[Glenbervie railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1922

Glenbervie railway station is located on the Craigieburn line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the northern Melbourne suburb of Essendon opening on 11 September 1922.Glenbervie Vicsig
The railway past the site of Glenbervie station was opened in 1872 as part of the North East railway to Wodonga. The station itself opened on 11 September 1922, a year after the line was electrified. The station was initially provided with small timber station buildings  which were replaced by the current buildings in 1976.[tpl]cite book|last=Turton|first=Keith W|title=Six And A Half Inches From Destiny.
