[[West Kortright Presbyterian Church]]

CATEGORIES: Presbyterian churches in New York, Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, National Register of Historic Places in Delaware County, New York, Georgian architecture in New York, Churches completed in 1850, 19th-century Presbyterian church buildings, Churches in Delaware County, New York

West Kortright Presbyterian Church is a historic Presbyterian church located at 49 W. Kortright Church Road in West Kortright, Delaware County, New York. It is a wood frame building on a stone foundation surmounted by a broad gable roof.  It was originally constructed in 1850 and substantially renovated in 1890s.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=10514|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: West Kortright Presbyterian Church|date=June 2002|accessdate=2010-02-05 |author=Kathleen LaFrank|publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation[/tpl] See also: [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=10509|title=Accompanying six photos[/tpl]
It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 2002.
