Yukihiko Sato (佐藤 由紀彦, born May 11, 1976 in Fuji, Shizuoka, Japan) is a Japanese football player, currently playing for V-Varen Nagasaki in the J. League Division 2.
Sato previously played for FC Tokyo, Yokohama F. Marinos, Kashiwa Reysol and Vegalta Sendai in the J.
