[[Queer China]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 films, Chinese documentary films, Chinese LGBT-related films, Mandarin-language films, 2000s documentary films, Chinese films

Queer China, ‘Comrade’ China ([tpl]zh|c=誌同志 |p=zhi tong zhi[/tpl]), directed by Cui Zi’en, is a 2008 independent Chinese documentary about homosexuality in China.
