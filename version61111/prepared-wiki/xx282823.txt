[[Nélson Semedo]]

CATEGORIES: 1993 births, Living people, Portuguese footballers, Association football midfielders, S.L. Benfica footballers

Nélson Cabral Semedo (born 16 November 1993) aka Nelsinho is a Portuguese footballer who plays for Benfica B, as a midfielder.

==Football career==
