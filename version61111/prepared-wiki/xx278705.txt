[[Paltalu, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Paltalu ([tpl]lang-fa|پالتلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Pāltalū; also known as Bāletlū)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3766535|Paltalu[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 90, in 26 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
