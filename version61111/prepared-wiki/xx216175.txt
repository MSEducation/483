[[Ventsislav Yankov]]

CATEGORIES: Bulgarian classical pianists, People from Sofia, 1926 births, Living people, Long-Thibaud-Crespin Competition prize-winners, Bulgarian emigrants to France

Ventsislav Yankov (born in Sofia, 24 March 1926) is a Bulgarian pianist and pedagogue. Trained in Berlin, he settled in France in 1946; three years later he won ex-aequo with Aldo Ciccolini the III Concours Marguerite Long-Jacques Thibaud's Grand Prize.[ref]
