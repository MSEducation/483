[[Imasgho Department]]

CATEGORIES: Departments of Burkina Faso, Boulkiemdé Province

Imasgho is a department or commune of Boulkiemdé Province in central Burkina Faso. As of 2005, it had a population of 24,106. Its capital lies at the town of Imasgho.Burkinabé government inforoute communale
