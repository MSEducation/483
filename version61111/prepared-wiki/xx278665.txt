[[Jalilabad, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Jalilabad ([tpl]lang-fa|جليل اباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Jalīlābād; also known as Dzhalalabad and Jalālābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3067606|Jalilabad[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Bala Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 322, in 81 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
