[[C-Note (band)]]

CATEGORIES: American pop music groups, American boy bands, Musical groups established in 1997, Musical quartets, Musical quintets, Musical groups from Orlando, Florida

C-Note (acronym of Creating Nothing Other Than Excellence) is a boy band from Orlando, Florida. The group scored success in 1999 with their debut album, Different Kind of Love.

==History==

The group formed in 1997 in Orlando for the purposes of performing in a talent show. Three of the group's members had been in a previous vocal harmony group, but the five-member group lost two members, and a fourth was recruited, completing the group's later lineup.Biography, Allmusic.com Their performance at the competition resulted in attention from the backers of Backstreet Boys and 'N Sync, and soon after a record deal ensued. Incorporating Latin elements into their dance-pop sound, the group toured America in 1998 and released their debut album, Different Kind of Love, on Sony Records the next year. Different Kind of Love spawned a radio hit, "Wait Til I Get Home", which peaked at #33 on Billboard's Top 40 Mainstream charts in 1999.Billboard Singles, Allmusic.com On the strength of the single, Different Kind of Love peaked at #163 on the Billboard Top 200.Billboard, Allmusic.com
In 2008, C-Note's English language version of their song "Lo Siento" was issued as a single entitled "Forgive Me". The single returned the band to US Charts, with the song placing inside the Adult Top 40 Chart.
