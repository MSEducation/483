[[Jim Dobson (ice hockey)]]

CATEGORIES: 1960 births, Canadian ice hockey forwards, Colorado Rockies (NHL) players, Ice hockey people from Manitoba, Living people, Minnesota North Stars draft picks, Minnesota North Stars players, Quebec Nordiques players, Sportspeople from Winnipeg, New Westminster Bruins players, Portland Winterhawks players

James Herold Dobson (born February 29, 1960 in Winnipeg, Manitoba) is a retired NHL forward. He played in 12 NHL games with the Minnesota North Stars, Colorado Rockies, and Quebec Nordiques.
