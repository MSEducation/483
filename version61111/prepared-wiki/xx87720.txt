[[West Richmond railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1901

West Richmond railway station is located on the South Morang and Hurstbridge lines in Victoria, Australia. It serves the eastern Melbourne suburb of Richmond opening on 21 October 1901.West Richmond Vicsig

==Platforms & services==

West Richmond has two side platforms. It is serviced by Metro Trains' South Morang and Hurstbridge line services.South Morang Line timetable Public Transport VictoriaHurstbridge Line timetable Public Transport Victoria
