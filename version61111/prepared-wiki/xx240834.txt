[[Fengcheng, Liaoning]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Liaoning

Fengcheng ([tpl]zh|s=凤城 |t=鳳城 |p=Fèngchéng |l=Phoenix City[/tpl]) is a city in the southeast of Liaoning Province in Northeast China. Administratively, it is a county-level city under the administration of Dandong, which lies [tpl]convert|45|km[/tpl] southeast of the city.

==Administrative Divisions丹东市-行政区划网==
