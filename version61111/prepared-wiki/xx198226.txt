[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Colatina]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1990, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Vitória, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Colatina ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Colatinensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Colatina in the Ecclesiastical province of Vitória in Brazil.
