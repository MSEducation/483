[[West Baton Rouge Parish School Board]]

CATEGORIES: School districts in Louisiana, Education in West Baton Rouge Parish, Louisiana

West Baton Rouge Parish School Board is a school district headquartered in unincorporated West Baton Rouge Parish, Louisiana, United States. The district serves West Baton Rouge Parish.

==School uniforms==

On March 17, 1999 the school board established a new rule requiring all students to wear school uniforms, taking into effect in the 2000-2001 school year."Handbook." West Baton Rouge Parish School Board.
