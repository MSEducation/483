[[Kocayer]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Mersin Province, Mediterranean Region, Turkey, Villages in Turkey, Populated places in Mezitli District

 
Kocayer is a village in Mersin Province, Turkey. It is a part of Mezitli district which is an intracity district of Greater Mersin. It is situated in the Toros Mountains. At [tpl]coord|36|53|N|34|19|E[/tpl] the distance to Mersin is [tpl]convert|52|km[/tpl]. The population is 679[tpl]cite web|url= http://rapor.tuik.gov.tr/reports/rwservlet?adnksdb2&ENVID=adnksdb2Env&report=wa_idari_yapi_10sonrasi.RDF&p_il1=33&p_yil=2011&p_dil=2&desformat=html|title=Turkstat|publisher=Turkish Statistical Institute|accessdate=2012[/tpl] as of 2011.
