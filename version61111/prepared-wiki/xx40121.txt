===Guerrilla activities===

Following the delaying action down the central Luzon plain, Troop C was cut off from the rest of the Regiment, having been ordered into Northern Luzon in an attempt to defend Baguio by Major General Wainwright in late December 1941.  In January 1942, the unit, with assistance from 71st Infantry and elements of the 11th Infantry,[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.nhi.gov.ph/downloads/mp0110.pdf |title=Guillermo Nakar |author= |date= |work=National Historical Institute |publisher=Republic of the Philippines |accessdate=15 February 2011[/tpl] raided Tuguegarao Airfield, destroying several planes, and killing multiple Japanese soldiers.[ref]
