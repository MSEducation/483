[[WHGS]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in South Carolina

WHGS (1270 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Gospel format. Licensed to Hampton, South Carolina, USA.  The station is owned by Bocock Communications, LLC and features programing from CNN Radio.
