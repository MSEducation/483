[[WNNS]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Illinois

WNNS (98.7 FM) is a radio station  broadcasting an Adult Contemporary format. Licensed to Springfield, Illinois, USA, the station serves the Springfield IL area.  The station is currently owned by Long Nine, Inc.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=WNNS |title=WNNS Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www1.arbitron.com/sip/displaySip.do?surveyID=SU08&band=fm&callLetter=WNNS|title=WNNS Station Information Profile|publisher=Arbitron[/tpl]
They run American Top 40 The 80's with Casey Kasem show on Saturdays at 10 pm and again on Sundays at 8 am (CST/CDT).
