[[Dalny Las]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Augustów County

Dalny Las [tpl]IPAc-pl|'|d|a|l|n|y|-|'|l|a|s[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Płaska, within Augustów County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland, close to the border with Belarus.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl]
The village has a population of 187.
