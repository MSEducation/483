[[Self-Portrait in a Circle of Friends from Mantua]]

CATEGORIES: 1604 paintings, Rubens portraits

Self-portrait in a circle of friends from Mantua is an oil on canvas by Peter Paul Rubens, produced between 1602 and 1604. It measures 77.5 cm by 161 cm. It is now in the Wallraf-Richartz Museum in Cologne. From left to right it shows Frans Pourbus, Caspar Schoppe, William Richardot, the painter's brother Philip or Filippo Rubens, Rubens himself, and Justus Lipsius.
