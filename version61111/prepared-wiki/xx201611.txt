[[Blind Alleys]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 television specials

Blind Alleys was a 1985 television special produced by Metromedia.http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0343433/ It is the story of two people once linked by an interracial marriage setting up for their daughter's wedding. The film features Pat Morita, Cloris Leachman, and co-writer Frederic Kimball.
The film was directed by Bill Coswell and written by pre-eminent Asian American dramatist David Henry Hwang and Kimball.
The film is not available on DVD or videocassette.
