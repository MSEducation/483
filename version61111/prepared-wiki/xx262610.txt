[[The C Team]]

CATEGORIES: Australian radio programs, 2000s Australian radio programs

The C Team is an Australian radio show on Radio 2GB and syndicated to stations across Australia. The C Team was introduced as a back-up show to be broadcast when more prominent radio personalities were unavailable, but has become popular. 

==Format==

The C Team format is based on a format pioneered by Radio 2GB, merging talk-back, on-air talent and news reporting. During major events, the show includes live coverage from the scene.

==Presenters==

The show is hosted by Chris Bowen, who joined Radio 2GB in 1998; Trevor Long, a former producer and director who from 2007-8 hosted a technology show; and Mark Levy, a sports reporter.
