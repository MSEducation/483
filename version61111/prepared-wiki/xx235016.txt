[[Bulletproof (Reckless Kelly album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 albums, Reckless Kelly albums

Bulletproof is the fifth studio album by  Red Dirt artist Reckless Kelly. It was released on June 24, 2008. The album tracks critical of and reflecting on recent sociopolitical unfoldings, such as the Iraq War and Hurricane Katrina.[tpl]Allmusic|class=album|id=r1345404|pure_url=yes[/tpl]
