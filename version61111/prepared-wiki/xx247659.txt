Avatar is an American science fiction film written and directed by James Cameron that was released in 2009. The film was premiered by 20th Century Fox in London, England on December 10, and was released in the United States and Canada on December 18, grossing $27 million on its opening day and $77 million during its opening weekend in 3,461 theaters, ranking number one at the box office.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.boxofficemojo.com/movies/?page=main&id=avatar.htm|title=Avatar (2009) – Weekend Box Office Results |accessdate=February 2, 2010 |publisher=Box Office Mojo[/tpl] Avatar grossed $2.7 billion worldwide, to become the highest-grossing film of all time,[tpl]cite web|first=|last=|title=All Time Worldwide Box Office Grosses|publisher=Box Office Mojo|url=http://www.boxofficemojo.com/alltime/world/|accessdate=January 27, 2010[/tpl] as well as in the United States and Canada. It also became the first film to gross more than $2 billion worldwide.[tpl]cite web|url=http://abcnews.go.com/Entertainment/wireStory?id=9711561|title='Avatar' Wins Box Office, Nears Domestic Record|accessdate=2010-02-02|publisher=ABC News[/tpl] Avatar was also well criticized and accumulated an approval rating of 83% on the review aggregator Rotten Tomatoes.
Avatar won the 82nd Academy Awards for Best Art Direction, Best Cinematography and Best Visual Effects, and was nominated for a total of nine, including Best Picture and Best Director.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences|title=The 82nd Academy Awards (2010) Nominees and Winners|url=http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/legacy/ceremony/82nd-winners.html|date=|accessdate=August 22, 2012[/tpl] The film garnered four nominations at the 67th Golden Globe Awards ceremony, and received two awards for Best Film – Drama and Best Director.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.nbc.com/golden-globes/nominees/|title=The Golden Globe Awards 2010 Nominees |publisher=NBC|date= |accessdate=January 14, 2010[/tpl][tpl]dead link|date=March 2014[/tpl]  Avatar was nominated for eight British Academy Film Awards, winning Best Production Design and Best Special Visual Effects. The film's achievement in visual effects were praised by the Visual Effects Society, who honored it with six accolades during their annual awards ceremony. Avatar was also nominated for the Directors Guild of America Awards, the Producers Guild of America Awards, and the Writers Guild of America Awards. The film was nominated for ten Saturn Awards and it went on to win all ten at the 36th Saturn Awards ceremony. Zoe Saldana's win for the Saturn Award for Best Actress marked a rare occurrence for an all-CG character.[tpl]cite news|first=David S.|last=Cohen|url=http://www.variety.com/article/VR1118021047.html?categoryid=14&cs=1&ref=bd_tv|title=Saturn Awards open Pandora's box |publisher=Variety|date=June 24, 2010 |accessdate=June 24, 2010[/tpl]

In December 2009, the American Film Institute recognized the film and Cameron's advances in CGI effects with their yearly "AFI Moments of Significance" award claiming it "will have profound effects on the future of the art form".[tpl]cite web|title= AFI Moments Of Significance|date=December 2009|publisher=American Film Institute|url=http://www.afi.com/tvevents/afiawards/moments.aspx[/tpl][tpl]dead link|date=March 2014[/tpl] In January 2010, it was announced that the Southern Sky Column, a [tpl]convert|3544|ft|adj=on[/tpl] quartz-sandstone mountain in the Zhangjiajie National Forest Park in Zhangjiajie, Hunan, China, had been renamed "Avatar Hallelujah Mountain" (阿凡达-哈利路亚山) by the city government in honor of the film.[ref]
