[[Wuzhai County]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Shanxi, Xinzhou

Wuzhai ([tpl]zh|c=五寨 |p=Wǔzhài |l=five villages[/tpl]) is a county of northwestern Shanxi, China. It is under the administration of Xinzhou City.

==Climate==

Wuzhai has a monsoon-influenced, humid continental climate (Köppen Dwb), with cold and very dry winters, and warm, humid summers. The monthly 24-hour average temperature ranges from [tpl]convert|−12.8|°C|1[/tpl] in January to [tpl]convert|19.9|°C|1[/tpl] in July, and the annual mean is [tpl]convert|5.04|°C|1[/tpl]. June thru September accounts for nearly three-fourths of the [tpl]convert|454|mm[/tpl] of annual precipitation. Due to the high elevation and dry climate, the diurnal temperature variation averages [tpl]convert|15|C-change[/tpl] annually.
