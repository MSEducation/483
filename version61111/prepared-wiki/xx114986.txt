[[Lamottella]]

CATEGORIES: Salticidae

Lamottella is a spider genus of the Salticidae family (jumping spiders). Its single described species is found in Guinea.

==Name==

The genus is named in honor of Maxime Lamotte, an ecologist and frog specialist who initiated the early expeditions into the Nimba Mountains.
longipes means "long-legged".

==Appearance==

The male spider is about 5.5mm long. The female is not yet known.
