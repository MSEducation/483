[[Joe E. Tata]]

CATEGORIES: 1936 births, Living people, American male television actors, 20th-century American male actors, 21st-century American male actors, Male actors from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania

Joe E. Tata (born September 13, 1936) is an American television actor. He is known for his regular role (1990 to 2000) as Nat Busschio, the owner and operator of the Peach Pit diner, in the TV series Beverly Hills, 90210. He also reprised his role as Nat a few times in the spin-off 90210 in the very start of the first season.
