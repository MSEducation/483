[[Purbasthali II (community development block)]]

CATEGORIES: Community development blocks in Bardhaman district

Purbasthali II (community development block) ([tpl]lang-bn|পূ্র্বস্থলি II সমষ্টি উন্নয়ন ব্লক[/tpl]) is an administrative division in Kalna subdivision of Bardhaman district in the Indian state of West Bengal. Purbasthali police station serves this block. Headquarters of this block is at Patuli, a census town in this block.[tpl]cite web| url = http://wbprd.gov.in/html/asp/bdo_contact.asp?cd=DF |title = Contact details of Block Development Officers |work = Burdwan  district | publisher = West Bengal Government |accessdate = 2011-09-07 | [/tpl][tpl]cite web | url = http://web.cmc.net.in/wbcensus/DataTables/02/Table4_9.htm | title = Provisional population totals, West Bengal, Table 4, Barddhaman District | accessdate =  2011-09-07 | last = | first = | work = Census of India 2001 | publisher = Census Commission of India[/tpl]
==Geography== 
Patuli is located at [tpl]coord|23.55|N|88.25|E|format=dms|[/tpl].
Purbasthali II community development block has an area of 188.18  km2. 

===Gram panchayats===

Gram panchayats of Purbasthali II block/panchayat samiti are: Jhawdanga, Kalekhantola I, Kalekhantola II, Majida, Mertala, Mukshimpara, Nimdaha, Patuli, Pilla and Purbasthali.[tpl]cite web| url = http://wbprd.nic.in/html/asp/writereaddata/Notifications/01120055.doc | title = No. 229 (Sanction)-PN/P/II/1G-5/2005(Pt.II) dated 02.02.09 |work= Allotment No. 5 for five districts – Cooch Behar, Burdwan, Uttar Dinajpur, Hooghly and Bankura| publisher = Government of West Bengal - Department of Panchayats & Rural Development |accessdate = 2011-08-26 | [/tpl]

==Demographics==

As per 2001 census, Purbasthali II block had a total population of 188,149, out of which 97,024 were males and 91,125 were females. Purbasthali II block registered a population growth of 18.89 per cent during the 1991-2001 decade. Decadal growth for Bardhaman district was 14.36 per cent.Decadal growth in West Bengal was 17.84 per cent.[tpl]cite web | url = http://web.cmc.net.in/wbcensus/DataTables/02/FrameTable4_1.htm | title = Provisional Population Totals, West Bengal. Table 4 | accessdate =  2011-08-26 | last = | first = | work = Census of India 2001 | publisher = Census Commission of India [/tpl]
Scheduled castes at 55,528 formed around one-fourth the population. Scheduled tribes numbered 6,459.

==Literacy==

As per 2001 census, Purbasthali II block had a total literacy of 64.50 per cent for the 6+ age group. While male literacy was 72.38 per cent female literacy was 56.07 per cent. Bardhaman district had a total literacy of 70.18 per cent, male literacy being 78.63 per cent and female literacy being 60.95 per cent.[tpl]cite web | url = http://web.cmc.net.in/wbcensus/DataTables/02/Table5_9.htm | title = Provisional population totals, West Bengal, Table 5, Bardhaman District | accessdate =  2011-08-26 | last = | first = | work = Census of India 2001 | publisher = Census Commission of India[/tpl]

==Health==

Purbasthali I block is one of the areas of Bardhaman district which is affected by arsenic contamination of ground water.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.soesju.org/arsenic/wb7.htm | title = Groundwater Arsenic contamination in West Bengal-India (20 years study ) | accessdate =  2011-09-23 | last = | first = | work = Bardhaman district | publisher = SOES[/tpl]
