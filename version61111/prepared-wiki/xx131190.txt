[[Gyandu Park]]

CATEGORIES: Football venues in Ghana, Sekondi-Takoradi, Sports venues in Ghana

Gyandu Park is a multi-use stadium in Sekondi-Takoradi, Ghana. The stadium holds 15,000 spectators. It was renovated in 2008 and served as a training pitch for the African Cup teams in Group B.

==Stadium usage==

The stadium is used mostly for football matches and many other sporting events.

==Residence==

The stadium is the home stadium of Sekondi Hasaacas FC, and is sometimes used by Medeama SC of the Ghana Premier League.Rospak S.C begins campaign against Dolphins
