[[DePaul Blue Demons women's basketball]]

CATEGORIES: DePaul Blue Demons women's basketball, College women's basketball teams in the United States

The DePaul Blue Demons women's basketball team represents DePaul University in Chicago. The school is one of the seven core schools that left the original Big East Conference in 2013 to form the current Big East Conference.  Before joining the original Big East in 2005, the Blue Demons previously competed in Conference USA, the Great Midwest Conference, and the North Star Conference.DePaul Women's Basketball All-Time Results, retrieved 2013-Aug-11.  The women’s basketball team began competing in the IAIAW in 1974-1975 under coach Debbie Miller and had their first winning season two seasons later, obtaining a 11-10 record under current coach Doug Bruno.

==NCAA tournament results==

The Blue Demons will make their 19th appearance in the NCAA Tournament in 2014. Going into the 2014 tournament, their combined record is 10–18.
