[[À la carte]]

CATEGORIES: Restaurant menus

À la carte [tpl]IPAc-en|ɑː|l|ə|ˈ|k|ɑr|t[/tpl][tpl]OED|À la carte[/tpl] is a French language loan phrase meaning "according to the menu", and used  
The phrase was adopted into English in 1826, predating by a decade the common use of the French language loanword "menu".Richard Bailey, Eating Words, Michigan Today, 13 May 2008.Menu, The American Heritage Dictionary of the English Language, 4th edition, Houghton Mifflin
