[[Oirase Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Aomori Prefecture, Gonō Line

[tpl]nihongo| Oirase Station|追良瀬駅| Oirase-eki[/tpl] is a railway station on the JR East Gonō Line located in the town of Fukaura, Aomori Prefecture Japan. It is 72.9 rail kilometers from the southern terminus of the Gonō Line at Higashi-Noshiro Station.

==History==

Oirase Station was opened on December 13, 1934 as a station on the Japan National Railways (JNR). With the privatization of the JNR on April 1, 1987, it came under the operational control of JR East.

==Lines==

==Station layout==

Oirase Station has a single island platform, only one side of which is in use, making it effectively a side platform serving bidirectional traffic. The station is unattended and is administered from Fukaura Station. There is a small weather shelter on the platform but no station building.
