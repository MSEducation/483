[[Frank Bosch]]

CATEGORIES: 1945 births, Living people, American football defensive linemen, Washington Redskins players, Colorado Buffaloes football players

Frank William Bosch (born October 24, 1945 in Bremerton, Washington) is a former American football defensive lineman in the National Football League for the Washington Redskins.  He played college football at the University of Colorado and was drafted in the 17th round of the 1968 NFL Draft.
