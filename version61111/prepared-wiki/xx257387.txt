[[Sociedade Esportiva União Cacoalense]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Brazil, Association football clubs established in 1982, Sport in Rondônia, 1982 establishments in Brazil

Sociedade Esportiva União Cacoalense, commonly known as União Cacoalense, is a Brazilian football club based in Cacoal, Rondônia state. They won the Campeonato Rondoniense two twice.

==History==

The club was founded on January 1, 1982.[tpl]cite book|title=Escudos dos Times do Mundo Inteiro|publisher=Panda Books|author=Rodolfo Rodrigues|year=2009|page=81[/tpl] União Cacoalense won the Campeonato Rondoniense in 2003 and in 2004.

==Stadium==

Sociedade Esportiva União Cacoalense play their home games at Estádio Aglair Tonelli Nogueira. The stadium has a maximum capacity of 5,000 people.
