[[Qingshanqiao, Xiangtan]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Xiangtan County

Qingshanqiao Town ([tpl]zh|s=青山桥镇|t=青山橋鎮|p=Qīngshānqiáo Zhèn[/tpl]) is an urban town in Xiangtan County, Xiangtan City, Hunan Province, People's Republic of China.[tpl]cite web |url=http://news.hexun.com/2013-05-27/154558566.html |title=湘潭市青山桥乡土文化研究会成立 |accessdate= 2013|publisher=Hexun |work= |language=Chinese[/tpl] It is surrounded by Heye Town to the west, Fenshui Township to the north, Paitou Township and Longkou Township to the east, and Changqing Township to the south.
