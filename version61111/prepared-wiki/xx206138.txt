[[14877 Zauberflöte]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Astronomical objects discovered in 1990

14877 Zauberflöte is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1937.0758140 days (5.30 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=14877 | title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-05-24 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on November 19, 1990. It is named after Mozart's opera Die Zauberflöte.
