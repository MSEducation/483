[[Danowskie]]

Danowskie [tpl]IPAc-pl|d|a|'|n|o|f|s|k|J|e[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Nowinka, within Augustów County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|10|km|mi|0[/tpl] east of Nowinka, [tpl]convert|15|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north-east of Augustów, and [tpl]convert|93|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of the regional capital Białystok.
