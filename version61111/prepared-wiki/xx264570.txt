[[Bodyrock (album)]]

CATEGORIES: Lee Aaron albums, 1989 albums, Attic Records albums

Bodyrock is the fifth studio album by singer Lee Aaron, released on September 13, 1989 through Attic Records (Europe and North America) and Alfa Records (Japan).
Aaron's most successful and highest-charting album to date, Bodyrock reached #32 on the RPM Canadian Albums Chart on November 11, 1989 and held that position for a week."Top Albums/CDs - Volume 51, No. 2, November 11 1989". RPM. Retrieved 2012-01-24. Both singles also charted: "Whatcha Do to My Body" reached #25 on the RPM Canadian Singles Chart on December 2, 1989, "Top Singles - Volume 51, No. 5, December 02 1989". RPM. Retrieved 2012-01-24. and "Hands On" reached #38 on February 3, 1990; each holding their position for a week."Top Singles - Volume 51, No. 12, February 03 1990". RPM. Retrieved 2012-03-24.
The album received Juno Award nominations for Album of the Year and Rock Album of the Year in 1991, whilst the music video for "Whatcha Do to My Body" was nominated for Video of the Year in 1990."Aaron, Lee". Jam!. Canadian Online Explorer. Retrieved 2012-01-24. Furthermore, recording engineer Lenny DeRose received a nomination for Recording Engineer of the Year in 1990 for his work on the album.
