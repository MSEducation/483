[[1985 Torneo Godó]]

CATEGORIES: Barcelona Open (tennis), 1985 Grand Prix (tennis), 1985 in Spanish tennis

The 1985 Torneo Godó was a men's professional tennis tournament that took place on outdoor clay courts in Barcelona, Spain from September 23 to September 29, 1985.
