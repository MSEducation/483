[[Nick Salvatore]]

CATEGORIES: People from Brooklyn, Hunter College alumni, University of California, Berkeley alumni, College of the Holy Cross people, College of the Holy Cross faculty, Cornell University faculty, Living people, American historians

Nick Salvatore (born Brooklyn, New York) is an American historian, and Maurice and Hinda Neufeld Founders Professor of Industrial and Labor Relations and Professor of American Studies at Cornell University.http://www.ilr.cornell.edu/alumni/profiles/faculty/Nick-Salvatore.html

==Life==

He graduated from Hunter College in 1968, and from the University of California, Berkeley with an M.A. and Ph.D., where he studied with Leon F. Litwack. 
He has taught American history at the College of the Holy Cross, and at Cornell University.http://www.ilr.cornell.edu/directory/nas4/
He has two daughters, Gabriella and Nora, and a grandson, Joseph. He and his wife, Ann Sullivan, live in Ithaca, New York.
