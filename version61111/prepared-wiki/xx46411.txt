[[Lincent]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Liège (province)

Lincent ([tpl]lang-nl|Lijsem[/tpl]) is a municipality of Belgium. It lies in the country's Walloon Region and Province of Liege.  On January 1, 2006 Lincent had a total population of 2,998. The total area is 14.75 km² which gives a population density of 203 inhabitants per km².
