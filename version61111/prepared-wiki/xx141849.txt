[[Gunnel Linde]]

CATEGORIES: Swedish-language writers, 1924 births, Living people

Gunnel Linde (born 14 October 1924 in Stockholm) is a Swedish writer[tpl]cite encyclopedia | title=Gunnel Linde | encyclopedia=Nationalencyklopedin | language=Swedish | url=http://www.ne.se/gunnel-linde | accessdate=13 May 2010[/tpl] who has written over forty children's books, among them Den vita stenen. 
In 1971, she was one of the founders of BRIS ("Barnens rätt i samhället", in English Children's Rights in Society). 
She has received the Swedish Nils Holgersson Plaque in 1965 (for the book Den vita stenen) and the Astrid Lindgren award in 1978.
