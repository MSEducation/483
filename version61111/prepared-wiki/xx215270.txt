[[Czerkiesy, Podlaskie Voivodeship]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Augustów County

Czerkiesy [tpl]IPAc-pl|cz|e|r|'|k|J|e|s|y[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Augustów, within Augustów County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|12|km|mi|0[/tpl] west of Augustów and [tpl]convert|84|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of the regional capital Białystok.
The village has a population of 320.
