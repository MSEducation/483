[[Merve Aydın (basketball)]]

CATEGORIES: 1994 births, Sportspeople from Mersin, Living people, Turkish women's basketball players, Point guards, Mersin Büyükşehir Belediyesi women's basketball players

Merve Aydın (born March 10, 1994 in Mersin, Turkey) is a Turkish female professional basketball player. [tpl]convert|177|cm|ftin|abbr=on[/tpl] tall, she plays in the point guard position for Mersin Büyükşehir Belediyesi.[tpl]cite web |url=http://basketball.eurobasket.com/player/Merve_Aydin/Mersin_BSH_Bld/181988?Women=1 |publisher=EuroBasket |title=Merve Aydin Player Profile |accessdate=2013-08-11 [/tpl][tpl]cite news |url=http://www.basketfaul.com/makale/17524/mersini-merve-aydin-surukledi.html |newspaper=Basketfaul |title=Mersin'i Merve Aydın sürükledi |date=2012-10-13 |language=Turkish |accessdate=2013-08-11 [/tpl][tpl]cite web |url=http://u20women.fibaeurope.com/en/cid_FtjjjX0gGxw6o2JYq5YD90.teamID_376.compID_FBoLIZ9eGMw1MS4DX8h1D2.season_2012.roundID_8792.playerID_77055.html |publisher=U20 European Championship Women |title=Merve Aydın (Turkey) |accessdate=2013-08-11 [/tpl]
She began with basketball playing at the age of ten. Despite her height disadvantage, she became a successful player. In the 2008-09 season at the age of fourteen, Merve Aydın was admitted to the senior team of Mersin B.B. SK becoming the youngest ever player in the Turkish Women's Basketball League.[tpl]cite news |url=http://arama.hurriyet.com.tr/arsivnews.aspx?id=10402942 |newspaper=Hürriyet |title=Potaların en genç oyuncusu |date=2008-11-20 |language=Turkish |accessdate=2013-08-11 [/tpl] In the 2012-13 season, she was transferred back to Mersin B.B. after playing in the İstanbul Üniversitesi SK for some time.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.haberler.com/turkiye-kadinlar-basketbol-ligi-nde-transfer-3758551-haberi/ |newspaper=Haberler |title=Türkiye Kadınlar Basketbol Ligi'nde Transfer |date=2012-07-04 |language=Turkish |accessdate=2013-08-11 [/tpl]
Aydın was member of the national team at the 2011 FIBA Europe Under-18 Championship for Women, which ranked 7th.[tpl]cite web |url=http://u18women.fibaeurope.com/en/cid_Fl2zSEHHI4kx84gqwW40U3.teamID_376.compID_uHP8sHguJSYAvKyuMkarZ1.season_2011.roundID_8162.html |publisher=FIBA Europe |title=Turkey Take 7th Place Ahead Of Serbia |date=2011-08-14 |accessdate=2013-08-11 [/tpl] She won the bronze medal in 2012 and 2013 at FIBA Europe Under-20 Championship for Women with the national team.[tpl]cite web |url=http://jwsbasketball.org/2013/05/17/basarinin-adi-merve-aydin/ |publisher=Just Women's Basketball |title=Başarının Adı "MERVE AYDIN" |author=Yilmaz, Taner |date=2013-05-17 |language=Turkish |accessdate=2013-08-11 [/tpl]
