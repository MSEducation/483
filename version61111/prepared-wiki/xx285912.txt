[[Patrick Bergin (politician)]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing, 1991 deaths, Labour Party (Ireland) politicians, Members of the 8th Seanad, Irish trade unionists

Patrick Bergin (died 16 April 1991) was an Irish politician and trade union official. He was a Labour Party member of Seanad Éireann from 1954 to 1957. He was nominated by the Taoiseach to the 8th Seanad in 1954.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oireachtas.ie/members-hist/default.asp?housetype=1&HouseNum=8&MemberID=1139&ConstID=210|title=Mr. Patrick Bergin|work=Oireachtas Members Database|accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl] He did not contest the 1957 Seanad election. He stood unsuccessfully for Dáil Éireann as a Labour Party candidate for the Carlow–Kilkenny constituency at the 1948 general election.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.electionsireland.org/candidate.cfm?ID=2194|title=Patrick Bergin|work=ElectionsIreland.org|accessdate=12 August 2013[/tpl]
