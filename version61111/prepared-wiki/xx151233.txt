[[Geoffry Varner]]

CATEGORIES: American male single skaters, Living people, 1987 births

Geoffry Varner (born March 2, 1987 in Palo Alto, California) is an American figure skater. He was the 2006 U.S. Junior bronze medalist and placed third at the Junior Grand Prix final in 2005. In his first year as a senior, he was given two senior Grand Prix assignments.
In his first senior Grand Prix appearance at Skate Canada, Varner placed 11th.
Geoffry has two older brothers. Jeremy is a professional baseball player for the Chicago Cubs farm system.
