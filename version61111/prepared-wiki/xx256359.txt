[[Spot-legged turtle]]

CATEGORIES: Rhinoclemmys, Fauna of Brazil, Reptiles of French Guiana, Fauna of Guyana, Fauna of Suriname, Reptiles of Guyana, Fauna of Venezuela, Fauna of Trinidad and Tobago

The spot-legged turtle (Rhinoclemmys punctularia) is a species of turtle belonging to the Rhinoclemmys genus of the family Geoemydidae.

==Distribution==

The spot-legged turtle can be found in Brazil, French Guiana, Guyana, Suriname, Trinidad and Tobago, and Venezuela.
