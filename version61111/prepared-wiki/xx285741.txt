[[Zifu, Ningxiang]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Ningxiang County

Zifu Town ([tpl]zh|s=资福乡|t=資福鄉|p=Zīfú Xiāng[/tpl]) is an rural township in Ningxiang County, Changsha City, Hunan Province, People's Republic of China. It's surrounded by Shuangfupu Town and Xieleqiao Town on the west, Batang Town and Nantianping Township on the northeast, Dachengqiao Town on the north, and Jinshi Town on the south. As of the 2000 census it had a population of and an area of  square kilometers.

==Cityscape==

The township is divided into twelve villages, the following areas: Yaoli Village (窑里村), Hequan Village (和泉村), Shanhuchong Village (珊瑚冲村), Wanfu Village (万福村), Dahong Village (大红村), Jiangquan Village (江泉村), Qixing Village (七星村), Shuangliu Village (双流村), Longma Village (龙马村), Qingquanhe Village (清泉河村), Heqing Village (合星村) and Huabao Village (华宝村).
