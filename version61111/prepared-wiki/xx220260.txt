Kiril Stoychev Terziev ([tpl]lang-bg|Кирил Стойчев Терзиев[/tpl]; born 1 September 1983 in Petrich) is a Bulgarian freestyle wrestler. He won a bronze medal at the 2008 Summer Olympics in Beijing in his category (up to 74 kg).
