[[Underworld Beauty]]

CATEGORIES: 1958 films, Black-and-white films, 1950s crime films, Japanese films, Japanese-language films, Yakuza films, Films directed by Seijun Suzuki, Nikkatsu films

[tpl]nihongo|Underworld Beauty|暗黒街の美女|Ankokugai no bijo[/tpl] is a 1958 Japanese film directed by Seijun Suzuki. It marked Suzuki's first CinemaScope film and was also the first to be credited to his assumed name, Seijun Suzuki.
