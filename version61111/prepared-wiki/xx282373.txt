[[Giovanni Bonello]]

CATEGORIES: 1936 births, Judges of the European Court of Human Rights, Living people, People from Floriana, Italian jurists

Giovanni Bonello (born 11 June 1936 in Floriana) was a former European Court of Human Rights judge.Constitution Matters | Giovanni Bonello. Malta Today. 4 March 2012. Retrieved 10 August 2013
Bonello's term of office in the European Court of Human Rights in Strasbourg started on November 1, 1998 and ended on October 31, 2004. However, because the propsal list of the Maltese government got rejected repeatedly by the Parliamentary Assembly of the Council of Europe, Bonellos term of office according to Article 23 para. 1 3 CON ECHR eventually ended on September 19, 2010.Yearbook of the European Convention on Human Rights. 1959.
