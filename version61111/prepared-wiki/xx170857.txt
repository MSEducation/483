[[Karol Ząbik]]

CATEGORIES: 1986 births, Living people, Polish speedway riders, People from Toruń, Team Speedway Junior World Champions, Individual Speedway Junior European Champions, European Pairs Speedway Champions, Peterborough Panthers riders, Poole Pirates riders, Sportspeople from Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship

Karol Ząbik (born October 25, 1986 in Toruń, Poland) is a Polish speedway rider who has won World, European and Polish Under-21 titles and has ridden for the Polish national junior team.
His father, Jan Ząbik was also a speedway rider, and is now the team manager of Polish speedway club KS Toruń.
