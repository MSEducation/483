[[Chung Kuang-tien]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 births, Living people, Taiwanese footballers, Tatung F.C. players

Chung Kuang-tien ([tpl]zh|t=鍾光田[/tpl], born August 13, 1989) is a Taiwanese football goalkeeper who currently plays for National Pei Men Senior High School football team and Tatung F.C.. He made his first national team debut in 2010 AFC Challenge Cup qualifier against Brunei on April 8, 2009.[tpl]cite news|title=中華隊五球輕取汶萊隊|url=http://www.ctfa.com.tw/hot_news_details.php?newsid=490|date=April 10, 2009|publisher=Chinese Taipei Football Association|accessdate=April 12, 2009|language=Traditional Chinese[/tpl]
