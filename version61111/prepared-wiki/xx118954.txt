[[Donna Dewberry]]

CATEGORIES: 1953 births, American Latter Day Saints, American artists, Living people

Donna S. Dewberry (born 6 November 1953) is an American Mormon artist and author. She claims to have developed an "One Stroke" painting technique. 

==Personal==

Donna Dewberry is a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.Famous Mormon Artists

==Television==

She has a public television show, One Stroke Painting with Donna Dewberry. In 2007, One Stroke Painting was replaced with The Donna Dewberry Show. 

===One Stroke Technique===

The "One Stroke Technique" is a double loading technique that consists of loading a brush with two separate colors.  Dewberry claims that with her technique you are able to achieve the shading and highlighting in one stroke [tpl]Citation needed|date=May 2012[/tpl].

==Writing==

She is a columnist for magazines including Tole World, Painting magazine and Decorative Artists Workbook.
