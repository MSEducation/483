[[Aru flying fox]]

CATEGORIES: Pteropus, Animals described in 1867

The Aru flying fox (Pteropus aruensis) is a Critically Endangered species of megabat in the Pteropus genus found in the Aru Islands in Indonesia. It was formerly considered a subspecies of the black-bearded flying fox.D.E. Wilson & D.M. Reeder, 2005: Mammal Species of the World: A Taxonomic and Geographic Reference. Third Edition. The Johns Hopkins University Press, Baltimore  The species is poorly known, and has not been encountered since the 19th century.  It is classified as critically endangered by the IUCN and is listed on CITES appendix II.[tpl]IUCN|id = 136504 | taxon = Pteropus aruensis | assessors = Helgen, K. | assessment_year = 2008 | version = 2013.2 | accessdate = 24 December 2013[/tpl]
