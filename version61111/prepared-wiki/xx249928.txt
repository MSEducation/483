[[Thomas David Smith McDowell]]

CATEGORIES: Confederate States of America political leaders, 1823 births, 1898 deaths

Thomas David Smith McDowell (January 4, 1823 - May 1, 1898) was a prominent North Carolina politician.http://politicalgraveyard.com/bio/mcdowell.html He was born in Bladen County, North Carolina. He served in the state House from 1846 to 1850 and in the state senate from 1854 to 1858. He represented the state in the Provisional Confederate Congress and in the First Confederate Congress.
