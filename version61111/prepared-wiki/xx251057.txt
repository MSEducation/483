==Education==

Lansdowne was once home to Totara Drive and Lansdowne Schools, however these were closed by the Ministry of Education in 2004 due to expected roll drops.[tpl]cite web|title=Falling roll forces school closures|url=http://tvnz.co.nz/content/242550/4202557/article.html|publisher=TVNZ|accessdate=26 January 2012[/tpl][tpl]cite web|last=Crombie|first=Nathan|title=A final farewell to school|url=http://www.times-age.co.nz/news/a-final-farewell-to-school/1026804/|work=Wairarapa Times-Age|publisher=APN Holdings NZ Ltd|accessdate=26 January 2012[/tpl] The closest school to the suburb is now Lakeview School.[tpl]cite web|title=Contact Us|url=http://www.lakeviewschool.info/about-us|work=Lakeview School website|accessdate=26 January 2012[/tpl]

==Housing==

Lansdowne is more affluent in comparison to other Masterton suburbs and was the site of Masterton's most expensive house sale.[tpl]cite web|last=Stewart|first=Matt|title=Masterton house topples $1m mark|url=http://www.times-age.co.nz/news/masterton-house-topples-1m-mark/986027/|work=Wairarapa Times-Age|publisher=APN Holdings NZ Ltd|accessdate=26 January 2012[/tpl]  The property prices are helped by the suburb's views of the Tararua Ranges, the presence of Lansdowne Park lifestyle village[tpl]cite web|title=Welcome|url=http://www.lansdownepark.co.nz/|work=Lansdowne Park website|accessdate=26 January 2012[/tpl]  and two golf courses.[tpl]cite web|title=The Course on the Hill|url=http://www.mastertongolfclub.co.nz/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=74&Itemid=59|work=Masterton Golf Course website|accessdate=26 January 2012[/tpl][tpl]cite web|title=Location|url=http://mahungagolf.webatu.com/moodle/mod/resource/view.php?id=6|work=Mahunga Golf Club website|accessdate=26 January 2012[/tpl]
