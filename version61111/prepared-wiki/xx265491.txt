[[Li Dong-woon]]

CATEGORIES: 1945 births, North Korean footballers, North Korea international footballers, Association football forwards, 1966 FIFA World Cup players, Living people, Pyongyang City Sports Club players

Li Dong-Woon (born 4 July 1945) is a North Korean football forward who played for North Korea in the 1966 FIFA World Cup.Copa do Mundo da FIFA Inglaterra 1966 There he scored against Portugal in the Quarter-finals at Goodison Park. He also played for Rodongja Sports Team.
