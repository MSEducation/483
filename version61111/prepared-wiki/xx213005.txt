[[Salomin]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Kraśnik County

Salomin [tpl]IPAc-pl|s|a|'|l|o|m|i|n[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Gościeradów, within Kraśnik County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) – TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|17|km|mi|0[/tpl] south-west of Kraśnik and [tpl]convert|61|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-west of the regional capital Lublin.
