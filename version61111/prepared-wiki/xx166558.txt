[[Nebojša Joksimović (footballer)]]

CATEGORIES: 1981 births, Living people, Serbian footballers, FK Vojvodina players, Red Star Belgrade footballers, Swiss Super League players, Neuchâtel Xamax players, FK Čukarički players, Serbian SuperLiga players, Association football central defenders

Nebojša Joksimović (Serbian Cyrillic: Небојша Јоксимовић; born April 1, 1981 in Čačak) is a Serbian football player, currently playing in Serbian Superliga club Čukarički Stankom.
