[[Lars Thorstensen Tønsager]]

CATEGORIES: 1788 births, Year of death missing, Members of the Parliament of Norway, Mayors of places in Akershus

Lars Thorstensen Tønsager (1788 - ??) was a Norwegian politician.
He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament in 1830, 1836, 1842, 1845, 1848 and 1851, representing the rural constituency of Akershus Amt. He was also a deputy representative in 1833.
