[[2009 Special Olympics World Winter Games]]

CATEGORIES: Special Olympics, 2009 in multi-sport events, 2009 in American sports, International sports competitions hosted by the United States, 2009 in Idaho, Multi-sport events in the United States, Sports in Boise, Idaho

[tpl]Primary sources|date=May 2008[/tpl] The 2009 Special Olympics World Winter Games was held in the state of Idaho, USA from February 7 through February 13, 2009.
Nearly 2500 athletes from over 100 countries participated in the games.  Dignitaries included actors, musicians, athletes and politicians from around the United States and the world. Vice President Joe Biden presented awards to athletes, and met athletes and their families at other events on Thursday, February 12.
