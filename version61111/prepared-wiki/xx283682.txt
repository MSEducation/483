[[Trollsletta]]

CATEGORIES: Jan Mayen, Landforms of Norway

Trollsletta ("The Troll Plain") is a coastal plain of Jan Mayen. It has a length of about 1,400 meters, and is located on the southern side of the island, inside the southwestern part of the bay Rekvedbukta, between Båtvika and Helenesanden.[tpl]cite web |url=http://placenames.npolar.no/stadnamn/Trollsletta |title=Trollsletta (Jan Mayen) |publisher=Norwegian Polar Institute |accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite paper|title=The place-names of Jan Mayen |first=Anders K. |last=Orvin |authorlink=Anders K. Orvin |publisher= Norsk Polarinstitutt |year=1960 |series=Skrifter |volume=120 |page=60 [/tpl] The only settlement of Jan Mayen, Olonkinbyen, is located at Trollsletta.[tpl]cite web |url=http://placenames.npolar.no/stadnamn/Olonkinbyen |title=Olonkinbyen (Jan Mayen) |publisher=Norwegian Polar Institute |accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl] The coast consists of low and splintered cliffs.[tpl]cite book |title=Arctic Pilot: Sailing directions Svalbard–Jan Mayen |year=1988 | volume =7 |publisher=The Norwegian Hydrographic Service and The Norwegian Polar Institute |location=Stavanger |language= |isbn=82-90653-06-9 |page=404 [/tpl]
