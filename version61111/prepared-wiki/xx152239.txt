George McLean (born 26 May 1943) is a Scottish former  footballer who played at both professional and international levels as a centre forward.

==Career==

Born in Paisley, McLean played club football for Drumchapel Amateur, St. Mirren, Rangers, Dundee, Dunfermline Athletic, Ayr United and Hamilton Academical.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.neilbrown.newcastlefans.com/player/georgemclean1.html|title=Profile|publisher=Post War English & Scottish Football League A - Z Player's Database[/tpl]
