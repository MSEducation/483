[[KKTY-FM]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Wyoming

KKTY-FM (100.1 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Country format. Licensed to Glendo, Wyoming, USA, the station is locally owned and operated by Douglas Broadcasting, Inc and features programing from CBS Radio and Dial Global Networks. In addition to great country music and national and international news from CBS, KKTY-FM features news and sports from the Cowboy State News Network, weather from Don Day's DayWeather, and local news updates EVERY hour of every day. Ag news comes from the Northern Ag Network, and Bob Kingsley counts down the Country Top 40 Sunday afternoons at 1pm.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KKTY-FM |title=KKTY-FM Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
