[[Joseph Gerhard Zuccarini]]

CATEGORIES: Botanists with author abbreviations, German botanists, Botanists active in Japan, Botanists active in North America, 1797 births, 1848 deaths

Joseph Gerhard Zuccarini (10 August 1797 - 18 February 1848) was a German botanist, Professor of Botany at the University of Munich. He worked extensively with Philipp Franz von Siebold, assisting in describing his collections from Japan, but also described plants discovered in other areas, including Mexico. Siebold wrote his Flora Japonica in collaboration with Zuccarini. It first appeared in 1835, but the work was not completed until after his death, finished in 1870 by F. A. W.
