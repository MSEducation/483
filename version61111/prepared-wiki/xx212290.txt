Nikolay Padius (also spelled Nikolai Padius; born January 9, 1980 in Saint Petersburgh, Russia, Soviet Union) is a Russian professional basketball player. He is 1.97 m (6 ft 5 ¾ in) in height and he weighs 91 kg (200 pounds). He plays at the shooting guard position. He currently plays with the pro club BC Lokomotiv-Kuban Krasnodar.
