[[A Song of Autumn]]

CATEGORIES: Songs by Edward Elgar, 1892 songs

"A Song of Autumn" is a poem by Adam Lindsay Gordon set to music by Edward Elgar in 1892.
It was dedicated to 'Miss Marshall'.According to Percy Young (in Elgar O. M.) Mrs. Marshall and her daughter were friends of Lady Elgar
The song was first published by Orsborn & Tuckwood, then by Ascherberg in 1892.  It was re-published in 1907 as one of the Seven Lieder of Edward Elgar, with English and German words.

==Lyrics==
