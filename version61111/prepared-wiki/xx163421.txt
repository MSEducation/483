[[Discula leacockiana]]

CATEGORIES: Discula

Discula leacockiana is a species of small land snail, a terrestrial pulmonate gastropod mollusk in the family Hygromiidae, the hairy snails.
==Distribution== 
This species occurs in Europe.

==Conservation status==

This species of snail is mentioned in annexes II and IV of the Habitats Directive.
