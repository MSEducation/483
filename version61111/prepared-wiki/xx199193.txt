[[Sternschanze station]]

CATEGORIES: , Hamburg S-Bahn stations, Hamburg U-Bahn stations, Railway stations in Hamburg, Buildings and structures in Altona, Hamburg

Sternschanze station is a railway station in Hamburg, Germany,[tpl]Citation |url=http://www.db.de/site/shared/de/dateianhaenge/infomaterial/sonstige/bahnhofskategorieliste__2008.pdf |title= Liste Bahnhofskategorie 2008 (pdf) |author=Staff |publisher=DB Station&Service AG |accessdate=2008-08-08[/tpl] [tpl]de icon[/tpl] located in the quarter Sternschanze in the borough of Altona, is a station for the rapid transit trains of the line S11, S21 and S31 of the City Railway of the Hamburg S-Bahn and the line U3 of the underground railway.[tpl]Citation |author=Staff |publisher=Hamburger Verkehrsverbund |url=http://www.hvv.de/pdf/fahrplaene/usar/hvv_usar_regio.pdf |title=Rapid Transit/Regional Rail (Network plan, pdf) |accessdate=2009-01-24 |date=2008-12-14[/tpl]  North of the railway station is a bus stop for the terminating bus route 181.

==Station layout==

The tracks and the island platform for the city trains are elevated and separated from the tracks of the regional and long distance trains that are located south of it. Accessible for handicapped through a lift. The station is unstaffed, but there are emergency and information telephones, ticket machines and a shop.[tpl]Citation |url=http://www.db.de/site/bahn/de/reisen/service/bfssuchefilter,variant=details,recordId=2516.html?c252936=Sternschanze |title=DB-Konzern - Sternschanze |author=Staff |publisher=Deutsche Bahn AG |accessdate=2008-01-24 [/tpl] [tpl]de icon[/tpl]
The underground station for the U3 is situated at the east side of the railway station and has an island platform on underground level with one entrance only at its south. The underground station is not fully accessible for the disabled.
