[[Literary technique]]

CATEGORIES: Fiction, Literary techniques, Narratology, Poetic devices, Style (fiction)

A literary technique (also known as literary device) is any method an author uses to convey his or her message. This distinguishes them from literary elements, which exist inherently in literature.
