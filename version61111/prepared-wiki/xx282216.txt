[[2014–15 Primeira Liga]]

CATEGORIES: Primeira Liga, 2014–15 in Portuguese football, 2014–15 domestic association football leagues, Current association football seasons

The 2014–15 Primeira Liga season (also known as Liga ZON Sagres due to sponsorship reasons) will be the 81st season of the Primeira Liga, the top-tier football league in Portugal. It will begin in August 2014, and is scheduled to conclude in May 2015.[tpl]cite web|title=Calendário Liga ZON Sagres Época 2013-2014|url=http://www.ligaportugal.pt/media/5678/calendario-liga_zon_sagres-20132014.pdf|format=PDF|work=Liga Portugal.pt|publisher=Portuguese League for Professional Football (LPFP)|accessdate=15 September 2013[/tpl] Benfica will be the defending champions, and Moreirense will return following promotion from the Segunda Liga. The league will be scaled up to 18 teams, after the Court's rule to nullify Boavista's relegation from the Primeira Liga in the 2008-09 season.
