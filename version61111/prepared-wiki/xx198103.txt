[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Livramento de Nossa Senhora]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1967, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Vitória da Conquista, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Livramento de Nossa Senhora ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Liberationis Marianae[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Nossa Senhora do Livramento in the Ecclesiastical province of Vitória da Conquista in Brazil.
