[[European Society for the Study of Science and Theology]]

CATEGORIES: Religion and science

ESSSAT is a society of scholars who study the topic of Religion and Science. They meet every two years. They have 3 publications: Issues in Science and Theology (IST), Studies in Science and Theology (SSTh), and ESSSAT-News (a quarterly newsletter). It was founded in the mid-1980s.
