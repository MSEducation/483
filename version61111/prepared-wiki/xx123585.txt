[[College Ground, Loughborough]]

CATEGORIES: Cricket grounds in Leicestershire, Loughborough University, Defunct cricket grounds in England, Sport in Loughborough, Defunct sports venues in Leicestershire

The College Ground was a cricket stadium, based in the town of Loughborough, Leicestershire. It has, in the past, been used by Leicestershire as an outground and has held 2 first class games in total.  The first game took place in 1928 against Derbyshire and the last was a year later against Glamorgan.
The site of the ground is now occupied by Loughborough University Students' Union.College Ground, Loughborough from cricketarchive.com
