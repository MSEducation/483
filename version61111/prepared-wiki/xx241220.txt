[[Otog Front Banner]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Inner Mongolia

 
Otog Front Banner (Mongolian: [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠣᠲᠣᠭ ᠤᠨ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠡᠮᠦᠨᠡᠳᠦ[/tpl] [tpl]MongolUnicode|ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ[/tpl] [tpl]Unicode|Otoɣ-un Emünedü qosiɣu[/tpl]; [tpl]zh|c=鄂托克前旗 |p=Ètuōkè Qiánqí[/tpl]) is a banner of southwestern Inner Mongolia, People's Republic of China, bordering Ningxia to the southwest and Shaanxi province to the southeast. It is under the administration of Ordos City.
