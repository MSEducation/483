[[Murs (rapper)]]

CATEGORIES: Warner Bros. Records artists, Underground rappers, African-American male rappers, Rappers from Los Angeles, California, 1978 births, Living people, West Coast hip hop musicians, African-American songwriters, Songwriters from California

Nick Carter (born March 16, 1978), better known by his stage name Murs, is an American rapper. His name is an acronym (or backronym) that he himself has created multiple meanings for such as "Making the Universe Recognize and Submit" or "Making Underground Raw Shit."[tpl]cite web|url=http://laist.com/2008/10/07/laist_interview.php|title=LAist Interview: MURS|publisher=LAist|date=October 7, 2008[/tpl]
Murs is a member of hip hop groups such as Living Legends along with Luckyiam, Sunspot Jonz, The Grouch, Scarub, Eligh, Aesop, Bicasso and Arata,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.xlr8r.com/features/2008/04/living-legends-hip-hop-masterpie|title=The Living Legends' Hip-Hop Masterpiece|publisher=XLR8R|author=Eric K. Arnold|date=April 1, 2005[/tpl] 3 Melancholy Gypsys along with Scarub and Eligh,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2012/05/18/lacma-hip-hop-concerts-mu_n_1526438.html|title=LACMA Hip Hop Concerts: Murs Of 3MG Kicks Off Summer Series (VIDEO, PHOTOS)|publisher=The Huffington Post|author=Anna Almendrala|date=May 18, 2012[/tpl] Felt along with Slug,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.urb.com/2009/11/30/felt-felt-3-a-tribute-to-rosie-perez-review/|title=Felt - Felt 3: A Tribute to Rosie Perez|publisher=URB|author=Adam Figman|date=November 30, 2009[/tpl] and Melrose along with Terrace Martin.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ballerstatus.com/2011/01/05/murs-terrace-martin-on-melrose-music-video/|title=Murs & Terrace Martin: On Melrose (Music Video)|publisher=Baller Status|date=January 5, 2011[/tpl] He is also a lead vocalist of The Invincibles along with Whole Wheat Bread.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.okayplayer.com/news/murs-whole-wheat-bread-are-the-invincibles-free-ep-video.html|title=Murs & Whole Wheat Bread Are The Invincibles (Free EP + Video)|publisher=Okay Player|author=|date=July 6, 2012[/tpl] 
On June 11, 2013, Murs released an album as lead vocalist of a band called The White Mandingos. The album is titled The Ghetto is Tryna Kill Me.Frannie Kelley (August 13, 2013). "The White Mandingos: Music That Defies Definition". National Public Radio.

==Career==

Murs formed 3 Melancholy Gypsys with fellow Alexander Hamilton High School classmates Scarub and Eligh.[tpl]cite web|url=http://the5thelementmag.com/2012/05/31/lacma-through-the-mic-featuring-murs-and-3mg/|title=LACMA: Through The Mic featuring Murs and 3MG|publisher=The 5th Element Magazine|date=May 31, 2012[/tpl] The group became friends with Mystik Journeymen, and joined them in the Living Legends collective in 1996.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/murs-mn0000934439|title=MURS Biography|publisher=Allmusic|author=Bradley Torreano[/tpl] His solo debut album, F'Real, was released on Veritech in 1997.[tpl]cite web|url=http://exclaim.ca/Interviews/WebExclusive/murs|title=Murs - Interviews|publisher=Exclaim!|author=Luke Fox|date=November 2011[/tpl]
Murs has worked with Slug under the name Felt since 2002.[tpl]cite web|url=http://pitchfork.com/news/36391-aesop-rock-atmospheres-slug-and-murs-team-for-felt-3/|publisher=Pitchfork Media|title=Aesop Rock, Atmosphere's Slug, and Murs Team for Felt 3|author=Tom Breihanon|date=September 3, 2009[/tpl] The End of the Beginning, his debut album on Definitive Jux, was released in 2003.[tpl]cite web|url=http://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/5513-the-end-of-the-beginning/|publisher=Pitchfork Media|title=Murs: The End of the Beginning|author=Matt Kallman|date=April 23, 2003[/tpl] Murs released Murs 3:16: The 9th Edition, an album entirely produced by 9th Wonder, in 2004.[tpl]cite web|url=http://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/5514-murs-316-the-9th-edition/|publisher=Pitchfork Media|title=Murs: Murs 3:16 - The 9th Edition|author=Rollie Pemberton|date=March 15, 2004[/tpl] Taking a more thoughtful approach than gangster rappers, it prompted Andy Gill of The Independent to say "eschewing bogus glamour for emotional realism, Murs manages to say more about the corrosive cancer of hip-hop's gun culture than all the thousands of column inches lavished on 50 Cent's bullet wounds".[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/music/reviews/album-murs-559357.html|title=Album: Murs|publisher=The Independent|first=Andy|last=Gill|date=9 April 2004[/tpl] After another collaborative album with 9th Wonder, Murray's Revenge, in 2006,[tpl]cite web|url=http://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/5515-murrays-revenge/|publisher=Pitchfork Media|title=Murs: Murray's Revenge|author=Peter Macia|date=March 21, 2006[/tpl] Murs signed a contract with Warner Bros., his debut album for the label being Murs for President.[tpl]cite web|url=http://consequenceofsound.net/2008/10/album-review-murs-for-president/|title=Album Review: Murs – Murs For President|publisher=Consequence of Sound|author=Michael Denslow|date=October 15, 2008[/tpl] It was preceded by Sweet Lord which was given away free to fans.[tpl]cite web|url=http://exclaim.ca/Reviews/HipHop/murs_9th_wonder-sweet_lord|title=Murs & 9th Wonder - Sweet Lord|publisher=Exclaim!|author=Omar Mouallem|date=September 2008[/tpl]
Murs released Varsity Blues 2, a follow-up to his Varsity Blues EP, with production duties handled by Aesop Rock, Terrace Martin and Droop-E in 2011.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.hiphopdx.com/index/news/id.14723/title.murs-releases-sequel-varsity-blues-2-production-by-aesop-rock-droop-e|title=Murs Releases Sequel "Varsity Blues 2," Production By Aesop Rock, Droop-E|publisher=Hip Hop DX|author=Jake Paine|date=April 13, 2011[/tpl] He released This Generation, a collaborative album with Fashawn, in 2012.[tpl]cite web|url=http://exclaim.ca/Reviews/HipHop/murs_fashawn-this_generation|title=Murs & Fashawn - This Generation|publisher=Exclaim!|author=Luke Fox|date=September 24, 2012[/tpl]
Murs was invited by Sacha Jenkins, a hip hop journalist, to join the group The White Mandingos, a collaboration between Jenkins and Bad Brains bassist Daryl Jenifer.Jay Balfour (July 5, 2013). "The White Mandingos Analyze Adam Yauch's Role In Their Formation". Hip Hop DX. They released their first album, The Ghetto is Tryna Kill Me, on June 11, 2013 under the Fat Beats record label.Jake Crates (March 4, 2013). "Murs and New Group “The White Mandingos” Announce New Concept Album". All Hip Hop. Murs has stated that the recording is a loose concept album about a man from "the hood" who struggles to maintain his identity while recording as a rock artist. The band members have acknowledged that the album is partially based on their own experiences.
On February 7, 2014, it was announced via Tech N9ne's Facebook page that Murs was signed to his record label Strange Music.http://www.xxlmag.com/news/2014/02/murs-signs-tech-n9ne-strange-music/
