[[Rogmocrypta]]

CATEGORIES: Salticidae

Rogmocrypta is a spider genus of the Salticidae family (jumping spiders).

==Description==

The short carapace of R. puta is orange, with a black eye area. The oval abdomen is about as long, but not as wide as the carapace. The scutum of males is shiny dark orange. The legs are pale orange.Murphy & Murphy 2000: 287
