[[Addyme inductalis]]

CATEGORIES: Animals described in 1863, Phycitini

Addyme inductalis is a species of snout moths in the genus Addyme. It was described by Walker in 1863.  It is found in Taiwan, Japan, India, Sri Lanka, Borneo, Sumatra, Sulawesi and Australia.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bioone.org/doi/pdf/10.3157/0002-8320%282007%29133%5B143:AROAGC%5D2.0.CO%3B2 |title=A Review on Addyme-Calguia-Coleothrix Genera Complex (Lepidoptera: Pyralidae: Phycitinae), with One New Species from China |publisher=Bioone.org |date=1970-01-01 |accessdate=2011-09-29[/tpl]
