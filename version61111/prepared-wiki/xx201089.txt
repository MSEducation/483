[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Guarapuava]]

CATEGORIES: Guarapuava, Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1965, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Curitiba, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Guarapuava ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Guarapuavensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Guarapuava in the Ecclesiastical province of Curitiba in Brazil.
