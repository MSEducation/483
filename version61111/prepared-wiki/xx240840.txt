[[KMCX-FM]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Nebraska, Clear Channel radio stations

KMCX-FM (106.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Country format. Licensed to Ogallala, Nebraska, USA, the station is currently owned by Clear Channel Communications. [tpl]cite web |url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KMCX-FM |title=KMCX-FM Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division |accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl]

==History==

The station was assigned the call letters KMCX on May 22, 1980.  On March 30, 1982, the station changed its call sign to the current kmcx. [tpl]cite web |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=42075&Callsign=kmcx |title=kmcx Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division |accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl] 
on December 23, 2004 the station was sold to Capstar Tx Limited Partnership.[tpl]cite web|url= |title=FCC Application  |publisher=Federal Communications Commission |accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl]
