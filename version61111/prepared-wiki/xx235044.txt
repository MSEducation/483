[[1997 Skoda Czech Open]]

CATEGORIES: 1997 WTA Tour, Prague Open, 1997 in Czech sport

The 1997 Skoda Czech Open was a women's tennis tournament played on outdoor clay courts at the I. Czech Lawn Tennis Club in Prague in the Czech Republic that was part of Tier IV of the 1997 WTA Tour. It was the sixth edition of the tournament and was held from July 14 through July 20, 1997.
