[[Białobrzegi, Podlaskie Voivodeship]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Augustów County

Białobrzegi [tpl]IPAc-pl|b|J|a|ł|o|'|b|ż|e|g|J|i[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Augustów, within Augustów County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|6|km|mi|0[/tpl] south of Augustów and [tpl]convert|78|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of the regional capital Białystok.
The village has a population of 330.
