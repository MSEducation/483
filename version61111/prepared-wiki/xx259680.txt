[[Mark Derlago]]

CATEGORIES: 1986 births, Bakersfield Condors players, Brandon Wheat Kings players, Canadian ice hockey left wingers, Idaho Steelheads players, Living people, Manitoba Moose players, Providence Bruins players, Anyang Halla players

Mark Derlago (born January 17, 1986) is a Canadian professional ice hockey player who is currently playing for the Anyang Halla in the ALH.
Before joining Halla, Derlago played for the Texas Stars, Manitoba Moose, and the Providence Bruins of the AHL, and was a member of the Idaho Steelheads and Bakersfield Condors of the ECHL. Derlago also played for the Lausitzer Foxes in Germany prior to joining Halla.http://www.hockeydb.com/ihdb/stats/pdisplay.php?pid=73479
