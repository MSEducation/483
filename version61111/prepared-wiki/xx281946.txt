[[La Crosse Hotel]]

CATEGORIES: Hotel buildings on the National Register of Historic Places in Virginia, Colonial Revival architecture in Virginia, Hotel buildings completed in 1917, Buildings and structures in Mecklenburg County, Virginia, National Register of Historic Places in Mecklenburg County, Virginia

La Crosse Hotel is a historic hotel building located at La Crosse, Mecklenburg County, Virginia. It was built in 1917, and is a two-story, eight bay, brick building with a rear ell and a hipped roof covered with standing seam metal and pressed metal shingles.
