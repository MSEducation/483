[[Valimitika]]

CATEGORIES: Aigialeia, Aigio, Populated places in Achaea

Valimitika ([tpl]lang-el|Βαλιμίτικα[/tpl]) is a village in the municipal unit of Aigio, Achaea, Greece. It is situated at the outflow of the river Selinountas into the Gulf of Corinth, 5 km east of Aigio. The railway from Corinth to Patras passes through the village. In 2001 Valimitika had a population of 510.
