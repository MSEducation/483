[[13th Annual Grammy Awards]]

CATEGORIES: Grammy Awards ceremonies, 1971 in California, 1971 music awards, 20th century in Los Angeles, California

The 13th Annual Grammy Awards were held on 16 March 1971, and was the first time the ceremonies were broadcast on television by ABC. They recognized accomplishments by musicians from the year 1970.
