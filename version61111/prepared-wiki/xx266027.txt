[[Luis Cabral (footballer)]]

CATEGORIES: 1983 births, Living people, Paraguayan footballers, Paraguay international footballers

Luis Cabral (born 23 September 1983) is an Paraguayan international footballer who plays for Guaraní, as a defender.

==Career==

Cabral has played for Guaraní, Fernando de la Mora and Sol de América.[tpl]NFT player|pid=40690|Luis Cabral[/tpl]
He made his international debut for Paraguay in 2010.
