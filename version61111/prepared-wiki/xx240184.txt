[[Charly In-Albon]]

Charly In-Albon (born 23 June 1957 in Brig, Switzerland) is a retired football defender.
During his club career, In-Albon played for FC Sion and Grasshoppers Zürich. He also represented the Switzerland national football team, making 40 appearances and scoring one goal. He then became a manager.http://www.rsssf.com/players/trainers-zwit-clubs.html
