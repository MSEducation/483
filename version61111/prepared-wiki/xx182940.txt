[[Fred Sunnen]]

CATEGORIES: Mayors of Sanem, Members of the Chamber of Deputies of Luxembourg, Members of the Chamber of Deputies of Luxembourg from Sud, Councillors in Sanem, Christian Social People's Party politicians, 1939 births, 2014 deaths, People from Differdange

Fred Sunnen (11 April 1939 in Differdange – 22 February 2014) was a Luxembourgian politician for the Christian Social People's Party (CSV).  He was a member of the national legislature, the Chamber of Deputies, having been first elected in the 1999 election to represent the Sud constituency.[tpl]fr icon[/tpl] [tpl]cite web |url=http://www.chd.lu/fr/organisation/membres/membres.jsp?ID=143 |title=Fred Sunnen |accessdate=2008-01-03 |publisher=Chamber of Deputies |archiveurl = http://web.archive.org/web/20080504165551/http://www.chd.lu/fr/organisation/membres/membres.jsp?ID=143  |archivedate = 2008-05-04[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.wort.lu/de/view/ehemaliger-sassenheimer-buergermeister-fred-sunnen-verstorben-530b9f7fe4b0577c214a13d5 |title=Ehemaliger Sassenheimer Bürgermeister Fred Sunnen verstorben |publisher=Wort.lu |date=2014-02-15 |accessdate=2014-02-25[/tpl]
Sunnen was the Mayor of Sanem from 14 May 1997 until 30 November 2005, when he was replaced by Georges Engel of the Luxembourg Socialist Workers' Party.  He was a member of the communal council, in which the CSV forms the joint-largest contingent.  In the most recent elections, on 9 October 2005, Sunnen won the greatest number of votes of all candidates.[tpl]fr icon[/tpl] [tpl]cite web |url=http://www.sanem.lu/egocms/content_site_sanem_page_11.html |title=Le Conseil Communal |accessdate=2008-01-03 |publisher=Commune of Sanem |archiveurl = http://web.archive.org/web/20070722195744/http://www.sanem.lu/egocms/content_site_sanem_page_11.html  |archivedate = 2007-07-22[/tpl]
