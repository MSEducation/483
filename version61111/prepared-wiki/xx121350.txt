[[Agia Paraskevi, Kozani]]

Agia Paraskevi ([tpl]lang-el|Αγία Παρασκευή[/tpl]) is a former municipality in Kozani regional unit, West Macedonia, Greece. Since the 2011 local government reform it is part of the municipality Eordaia, of which it is a municipal unit.Kallikratis law Greece Ministry of Interior [tpl]el icon[/tpl] The population in 2001 was 1,977.
