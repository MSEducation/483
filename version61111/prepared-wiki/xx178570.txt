[[Gmina Osieck]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Otwock County

Gmina Osieck is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Otwock County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Osieck, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|20|km|mi|0[/tpl] south-east of Otwock and [tpl]convert|40|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-east of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|67.5|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 3,436.
The gmina contains part of the protected area called Masovian Landscape Park.

==Villages==

Gmina Osieck contains the villages and settlements of Augustówka, Czarnowiec, Górki, Grabianka, Kolonia Osieck, Kolonia Pogorzel, Lipiny, Natolin, Nowe Kościeliska, Osieck, Pogorzel, Rudnik, Sobieńki, Stare Kościeliska and Wójtowizna.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Osieck is bordered by the gminas of Celestynów, Garwolin, Kołbiel, Pilawa and Sobienie-Jeziory.
