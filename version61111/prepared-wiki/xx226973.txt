[[Foley Park Halt railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Disused railway stations in Worcestershire, Railway stations opened in 1905, Railway stations closed in 1970, 1905 establishments in England, 1970 disestablishments in England

Foley Park Halt was the first stop on the Kidderminster to Bridgnorth railway. It was located where the railway went under Stourport Road, Kidderminster.

==History==

Foley Park Halt opened in 1905 and was closed in 1970. When SVR services started operating, the station was not reopened.
