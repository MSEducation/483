[[Crash Craddock (1986 album)]]

CATEGORIES: Billy "Crash" Craddock albums, 1986 albums

Crash Craddock is an album by Billy "Crash" Craddock. It was released in 1986 by MCA/Dot Records. It was recorded in Muscle Shoals, Alabama and produced by Jimmy Johnson.Crash Craddock (Album notes). Billy Craddock. MCA/Dot Records. 1986.
