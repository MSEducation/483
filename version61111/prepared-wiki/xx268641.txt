[[Li Jong-kap]]

CATEGORIES: 1920 births, South Korean footballers, South Korea international footballers, Association football defenders, 1954 FIFA World Cup players, Living people, Asian Games medalists in football, Footballers at the 1954 Asian Games

Li Jong-Kap (Hangul: 이종갑, Hanja: 李鍾甲, born 18 March 1920) is a South Korean football defender who played for the South Korea in the 1954 FIFA World Cup.1954 FIFA World Cup Switzerland He also played for Wonjoo.
