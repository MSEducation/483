[[Unforgettable (Melon Kinenbi song)]]

CATEGORIES: 2007 singles, Zetima Records singles

[tpl]nihongo|"Unforgettable"|アンフォゲッタブル[/tpl] is the sixteenth single by Japanese girl group Melon Kinenbi. It was the first of Melon Kinenbi's singles to be produced by Sharam Q member Taisei. It was released on March 28th 2007, and its highest position on the Oricon weekly chart was #16.
