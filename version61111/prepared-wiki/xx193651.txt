[[Off Kilter (song)]]

CATEGORIES: 2005 singles, Australian rock songs

"Off Kilter" is the first song released from Birds of Tokyo's debut album Day One.  It was released on October 10, 2005 on independent label, Egg Records, through MGM Distribution.  It was produced by Forrester Savell (Helmut, Gavin Rossdale, Karnivool, Full Scale).
