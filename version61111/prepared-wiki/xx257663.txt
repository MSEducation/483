[[August 15 (2011 film)]]

CATEGORIES: 2010s Malayalam-language films, Indian films, 2011 films, Indian crime thriller films, 2010s crime films, 2010s thriller films

August 15 is a 2011 Malayalam crime thriller film directed by Shaji Kailas, written by S. N. Swamy, and starring Mammootty.NowRunning.com, August 15 Synopsis. The film is produced by M. Mani and was released on March 24, 2011.
The film is a sequel to the 1988 film August 1, which was directed by Sibi Malayil, and written by S. N. Swamy.
Following from August 1, Mammootty plays the character of the Crime Branch officer Perumal who investigates into an attempt of assassination on the chief minister of Kerala on the 15th of August, the Independence Day of India.
