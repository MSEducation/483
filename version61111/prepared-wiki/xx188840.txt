[[Yuri Loza]]

CATEGORIES: 1954 births, Living people, Russian composers, Russian poets, Russian male singers, Soviet male singers

Yuri Eduardovich Loza ([tpl]lang-ru|Ю́рий Эдуа́рдович Лоза́[/tpl]) (born February 1, 1954) is a Soviet and Russian singer, poet, and composer.Yuri Loza home page
In 1983, he moved to Moscow with the purpose to enter the Russian Academy of Theatre Arts, but failed the entry exams and joined the rock band, Zodchiye. Soon his songs became the majority of the band's repertoire."Zodchiye" [tpl]ru icon[/tpl]
Since 1987, Loza has been singing solo.
