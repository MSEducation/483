[[Andreas Valdix]]

CATEGORIES: 1984 births, Living people, IK Oskarshamn players, Malmö Redhawks players, Timrå IK players, Washington Capitals draft picks

Andreas Valdix (born December 6, 1984) is a Swedish professional ice hockey centre who currently plays for IK Oskarshamn of HockeyAllsvenskan. He was selected by the Washington Capitals in the 4th round (109th overall) of the 2003 NHL Entry Draft.
Valdix has played in the Elitserien, the highest-level ice hockey league in Sweden, with both the Malmö Redhawks and Timrå IK.Eliteprospects.com - Andreas Valdix
