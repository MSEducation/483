[[Advanced Bank of Asia]]

CATEGORIES: Banks of Cambodia, Phnom Penh

Advanced Bank Of Asia Ltd. (ABA) is a commercial bank of the Kingdom Of Cambodia.  It was founded in 1996 by bankers from Cambodia and Korea; its primary offices are located in Phnom Penh.Advanced Bank of Asia official site  The bank has several branches in Phnom Penh, and branches in Siem Reap and Battambang.ABA Bank in Cambodia Yellow Pages
