[[Tsuyoshi Yoneyama]]

CATEGORIES: Japanese golfers, Japan Golf Tour golfers, Asian Games medalists in golf, Golfers at the 1986 Asian Games, Sportspeople from Kanagawa Prefecture, 1965 births, Living people

Tsuyoshi Yoneyama (born 15 March 1965) is a Japanese professional golfer. 
Yoneyama was born in  Kanagawa Prefecture.
Yoneyama has won three tournaments on the Japan Golf Tour and featured in the top 100 of the Official World Golf Rankings. His most successful year was 1999 when he won three times and finished tied 15th in The Open Championship.
