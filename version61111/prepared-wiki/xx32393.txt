[[Bambusa]]

CATEGORIES: Bamboo genera, Garden plants of Asia

Bambusa is a large genus of about 130 species of clumping bamboos.[tpl]cite web|title=Bambusa|url=http://www.theplantlist.org/browse/A/Poaceae/Bambusa/|work=The Plant List, RBG Kew|accessdate=24 January 2012[/tpl] These species are usually giant ones, with numerous branches emerging from the nodes, and one or two much larger than the rest. The branches can be as long as 11 m (33 ft). They are found in tropical, subtropical areas of Asia, especially in the wet Tropics, in Western Africa as well as Southern Africa where they are non-indigenous and occur in managed bamboo plantations (see Bambusa balcooa).
