[[Victor Backman]]

Victor Backman is a Swedish professional ice hockey forward who is currently playing for IK Oskarshamn of the HockeyAllsvenskan.

==Playing career==

In 2007 Backman represented Göteborg 1 in TV-pucken, scoring two goals and six assist in eight games.
Backman scored his first Elitserien goal on December 26, 2011, against Joakim Lundström of Timrå IK.
