[[Connacht Under-21 Football Championship]]

CATEGORIES: Connacht GAA inter-county football competitions

The Connacht Under-21 Football Championship is the premier inter-county "knockout" competition for players aged under 21 years (on 1 January of competition year) played in the province of Connacht, in Ireland. The championship is organised by the Connacht council of the Gaelic Athletic Association and is played during the summer months. The winners represent Connacht in the All-Ireland Under-21 Football Championship semi-finals.
Roscommon are the current (2014) Connacht Under-21 Football Champions.
