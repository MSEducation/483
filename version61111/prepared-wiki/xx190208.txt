[[Conchas Lake Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in New Mexico, Transportation in San Miguel County, New Mexico, Buildings and structures in San Miguel County, New Mexico

Conchas Lake Airport [tpl]airport codes|||E89[/tpl] is a public use airport located one nautical mile (2 km) southwest of the central business district of Conchas Dam, in San Miguel County, New Mexico, United States. It is owned by the New Mexico Department of Transportation - Aviation Division.

==Facilities and aircraft==

Conchas Lake Airport covers an area of [tpl]convert|60|acre|ha[/tpl] at an elevation of 4,230 feet (1,289 m) above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 9/27 with a 4,790 x 60 ft (1,460 x 18 m) asphalt surface. For the 12-month period ending April 30, 2005, the airport had 1,000 general aviation aircraft operations, an average of 83 per month.
