[[Codex Guelferbytanus A]]

CATEGORIES: Greek New Testament uncials, 6th-century biblical manuscripts, Palimpsests, Herzog August Library

Codex Guelferbytanus A designated by Pe or 024 (in the Gregory-Aland numbering), ε 33 (von Soden), is a Greek uncial manuscript of the Gospels, dated palaeographically to the 6th century. The manuscript is very lacunose.

==Description==

The codex contains the text of the four Gospels in a very fragmentary condition on 44 leaves ([tpl]×|26.5|21.5|cm[/tpl]). Written in two columns per page, 24 lines per column.[ref]
