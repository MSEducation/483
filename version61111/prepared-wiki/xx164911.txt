[[Wind Creek State Park]]

CATEGORIES: State parks of Alabama, IUCN Category V, Protected areas of Tallapoosa County, Alabama

Wind Creek State Park is an Alabama state park located [tpl]convert|7|mi[/tpl] south of Alexander City. The park occupies [tpl]convert|1445|acre[/tpl] of shoreline along the northern portion of Lake Martin, a [tpl]convert|41000|acre|adj=on[/tpl] clear-water reservoir on the Tallapoosa River.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.alapark.com/WindCreek/ |title=Wind Creek State Park |work=Alabama State Parks |publisher=Alabama Department of Conservation and Natural Resources |accessdate=2012-11-06[/tpl] Wind Creek offers camping, swimming, boating, fishing, picnicking, playgrounds, and trails for hiking and equestrian use.
