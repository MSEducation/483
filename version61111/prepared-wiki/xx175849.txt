[[WTHL]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Kentucky, Radio stations established in 1987

WTHL is a Christian radio station licensed to Somerset, Kentucky, broadcasting on 90.5 MHz FM.  The station is owned by Somerset Educational Broadcasting Foundation.WTHL
WTHL's programming includes Christian talk and teaching shows such as Back to the Bible, Focus on the Family, Love Worth Finding with Adrian Rogers, In Touch with Charles Stanley, Tony Evans, and Unshackled!.  WTHL also airs a variety of Christian music.King of Kings Radio Programming
