[[Gmina Walce]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Opole Voivodeship, Krapkowice County

Gmina Walce, German Gemeinde Walzen is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Krapkowice County, Opole Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Its seat is the village of Walce (Walzen), which lies approximately [tpl]convert|11|km|mi|0[/tpl] south of Krapkowice and [tpl]convert|34|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south of the regional capital Opole.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|69.29|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 5,976. Since 2006 the commune, like much of the area, has been bilingual in German and Polish, a large German population remaining in the area after Silesia was partitioned to Poland.
