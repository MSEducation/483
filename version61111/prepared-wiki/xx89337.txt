[[Radio Academy]]

CATEGORIES: Professional associations based in the United Kingdom, Radio organisations in the United Kingdom, 1983 establishments in the United Kingdom, Organizations established in 1983, Charities based in the United Kingdom

The Radio Academy is a registered charity dedicated to 'the encouragement, recognition and promotion of excellence in UK broadcasting and audio production'.[tpl]cite web|title=The Radio Academy's official website|url=http://www.radioacademy.org/|publisher=The Radio Academy|accessdate=9 January 2013[/tpl] It was formed in 1983 and is run via a board of trustees, currently chaired by Ben Cooper, Controller of BBC Radio 1.[tpl]cite web|title=Trustees|url=http://www.radioacademy.org/about/trustees/|publisher=The Radio Academy[/tpl]
The Daily Telegraph has described The Radio Academy as "the industry's most powerful body".[tpl]cite news|last=Singh |first=Anita |url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/newstopics/celebritynews/3287593/Jonathan-Ross-and-Russell-Brand-row-Lesley-Douglas-profile.html |title=Jonathan Ross and Russell Brand row: Lesley Douglas profile |publisher=The Daily Telegraph |date=2008-10-30 |accessdate=2010-01-12 |location=London[/tpl]

==Events==

The Radio Academy runs a range of events throughout the year, including the annual Radio Festival[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.radioacademy.org/events/radio-festival-2012/ |title=Radio Festival |publisher=The Radio Academy |date= |accessdate=2013-01-08[/tpl] and the Radio Academy Awards.[tpl]cite web|title=Radio Academy Awards|url=http://www.radioacademyawards.org/|publisher=The Radio Academy|accessdate=8 January 2013[/tpl][tpl]cite news|title=Sony Radio Academy awards winners|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/media/2012/may/15/sony-radio-academy-awards-winners?INTCMP=SRCH|accessdate=9 January 2013|newspaper=The Guardian|date=15 May 2012 |location=London[/tpl]
The Academy also runs regular Masterclasses for young people who would like to work in radio.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.radioacademy.org/events/masterclasses/ |title=Masterclasses |publisher=The Radio Academy |date= |accessdate=2013-01-09[/tpl] In addition, the Academy's Branches regularly hold local events across the country.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.radioacademy.org/branches/ |title=Branches |publisher=The Radio Academy |accessdate=2013-01-08[/tpl]

==Honours==

The Radio Academy awards a number of honours each year, including induction into its UK Radio Hall of Fame,[tpl]cite web|title=The Radio Academy's Hall Of Fame|url=http://www.radioacademy.org/hall-of-fame/|publisher=The Radio Academy|accessdate=8 January 2013[/tpl] Fellowships, The John Peel Award for Outstanding Contribution to Music Radio, The PPL Music Icon Award, The Local and Regional Lifetime Achievement Award, The Most Played Artist on British Radio and the 30 Under 30.[tpl]cite web|title=Honours|url=http://www.radioacademy.org/about/honours/|publisher=The Radio Academy|accessdate=8 January 2013[/tpl]
