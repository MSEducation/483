[[Usa, Ōita]]

CATEGORIES: Cities in Ōita Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Usa|宇佐市|Usa-shi[/tpl] is a city located in Ōita Prefecture, Japan. The city was founded on April 1, 1967.
Usa is famous for being the location of the Usa Shrine, built in 725, the head shrine of all of Hachiman shrines in Japan. Nearby is the Ōita Prefectural Museum of History.
Usa is made up of three areas.
As of 2003, the city has an estimated population of 49,021 and a population density of 274.94 persons per km². The total area is 178.30 km².
On March 31, 2005, the towns of Ajimu and Innai (both from Usa District) were merged into Usa.

==Product labeling==

There have been false claims that products made in this town and exported to the US in the 1960s carried the label "MADE IN USA", for it to appear as if the product was made in the United States.
