[[Nosferattus discus]]

CATEGORIES: Salticidae

Nosferattus discus is a jumping spider.

==Etymology==

The epitheton is Latin for "disc" and refers to the shape of the cymbium.

N. discus is about 4mm long, with females slightly larger.
