[[Punkhorn Creek]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of New Jersey, Geography of Sussex County, New Jersey

[tpl]Coord|40|58|15|N|74|42|13|W|region:US-NJ_type:river_source:GNIS|display=title|notes=[tpl]Cite gnis|id=1802621|name=Punkhorn Creek|accessdate=November 22, 2010[/tpl][/tpl]
Punkhorn Creek is a small stream in the middle of Byram Township, Sussex County, New Jersey in the United States.
Punkhorn Creek flows southwest from Lake Bottom, on the north side of and parallel with Amity Road, to Roseville Pond.
