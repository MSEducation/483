[[Gilson Da Souza]]

CATEGORIES: 1967 births, Living people, Ecuadorian footballers, Ecuador international footballers, Brazilian emigrants to Ecuador, People from Duque de Caxias

Gilson de Souza (born 25 March 1967) is a Brazilian-born, Ecuadoran international footballer who played as a midfielder. He is best known for his spell at Barcelona SC where he was a fan favourite.
Gilson began his playing career in Brazil where he played for Atlético Paranaense and Vasco da Gama.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Globo Esporte|title=Futpedia: Gilson (Gilson Simões de Souza)|url=http://futpedia.globo.com/jogadores/gilson11|language=Portuguese|accessdate=2010-02-23[/tpl]
He made 17 appearances for the Ecuador national football team from 1996 to 1997.[tpl]cite web|publisher=RSSSF|author=Espinoza Añazco, Fernando|title=Ecuador - Record International Players|url=http://www.rsssf.com/miscellaneous/ecua-recintlp.html|date=2009-08-12[/tpl]
