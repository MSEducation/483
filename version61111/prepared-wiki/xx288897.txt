[[Hey, Iran]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Hey ([tpl]lang-fa|حي[/tpl], also Romanized as Ḩey; also known as Hay)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3066498|Hey[/tpl] is a village in Bizineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,355, in 311 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
