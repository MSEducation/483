[[I Want It All (Dangerous Muse EP)]]

CATEGORIES: 2009 EPs, 2009 remix albums, Remix EPs, Dangerous Muse albums

I Want It All is a song/single by American electropop duo Dangerous Muse, released on December 15, 2009. The music video was directed by Greko Sklavounos. This song is the first single taken off the band's debut album.
