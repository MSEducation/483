[[Drew Strojny]]

CATEGORIES: 1981 births, American football offensive tackles, Duke Blue Devils football players, Living people, St. Louis Rams players

Drew Strojny (the "j" is silent or pronounced as an i) (born June 30, 1981) is an American football player who currently is a web designer.http://thethemefoundry.com/blog/last-3-years/ He played for the St. Louis Rams. He attended Xaverian Brothers High School in Westwood, Ma.
