CATEGORIES: Speed skating at the 1936 Winter Olympics

The 500 metres speed skating event was part of the speed skating at the 1936 Winter Olympics programme. The competition was held on Tuesday, February 11, 1936.
Thirty-six speed skaters from 14 nations competed.

==Records==
