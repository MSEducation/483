[tpl]Portal|Anglicanism[/tpl] The Rt Rev Gay Lisle Griffith Mandeville  was Bishop of Barbados from 1951 until 1960.Crockford's Clerical Directory 1975-76. London: Oxford University Press, 1976. ISBN 0-19-200008-X He was born on 20 May 1894 and educated at Harrison College and Codrington College, Barbados.“Who was Who” 1897-1990 London, A & C Black, 1991 ISBN 0-7136-3457-X After graduation he was ordained in 1918 and began his ecclesiastical career with a curacy at St George, St Kitts followed by the post of Rector of Saba in what was then the Dutch West Indies and is now Netherlands Antilles. He then returned to his home country where he was successively  Vicar of St Bartholomew then St Stephen; Rector of St Philip; Vicar General then Dean and finally Bishop of Barbados.
