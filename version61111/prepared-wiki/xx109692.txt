[[Dries Buytaert]]

CATEGORIES: Free software programmers, Belgian computer programmers, 1978 births, Living people, People from Wilrijk, People from Antwerp

Dries Buytaert (born 19 November 1978 in Wilrijk, Antwerp, Belgium)Curriculum Vitae is an open-source software programmer notable as founder and lead developer of the Drupal CMS.[tpl]cite web|url=http://drupal.org/node/769 |title=History |publisher=Drupal.org |accessdate= 23 April 2012[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://drupal.org/node/21778 |title=The Drupal core |publisher=Drupal.org |accessdate= 23 April 2012[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://groups.drupal.org/node/5434 |title=Revamp of MAINTAINERS.txt |publisher=Groups.drupal.org |accessdate= 23 April 2012[/tpl]

==Career==

Buytaert defended his PhD dissertation in Computer Science on 27 January 2008[tpl]cite web|url=http://buytaert.net/dr-dre |title=Dr. Dre, Dries Buytaert |publisher=Buytaert.net |accessdate= 23 April 2012[/tpl] at Ghent University in Belgium.[tpl]cite web|url=http://conferences.oreillynet.com/cs/user/view/e_spkr/2368 |title=conferences.oreilly.com |publisher=Conferences.oreillynet.com |accessdate= 23 April 2012[/tpl]
From 1999-2000 he was the maintainer of the Linux-WLAN FAQ.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.linux-wlan.org/docs/linux-wlan-FAQ.html |title=Linux-Wlan Faq (Old) |publisher=Linux-wlan.org |date=6 March 2000 |accessdate= 23 April 2012[/tpl]
On 1 December 2007, Dries[tpl]cite web|url=http://buytaert.net/acquia-my-drupal-startup |title=Acquia, my Drupal startup, Dries Buytaert |publisher=Buytaert.net |accessdate= 23 April 2012[/tpl] announced, together with co-founder Jay Batson the launch of a start-up called Acquia.[tpl]cite web|url=http://acquia.com/ |title=Acquia |publisher=Acquia |accessdate= 23 April 2012[/tpl] Acquia is a commercial open-source software company providing products, services, and technical support for Drupal. Acquia wants to be to Drupal as what Red Hat has been to Linux.[tpl]cite web|url=http://venturebeat.com/2012/11/29/acquia-drupal-funding/ |title=With $30M in funding, Acquia edges closer to an IPO |publisher=Venturebeat.com |date=29 November 2012 |accessdate= 20 January 2013[/tpl] In 2009, Acquia helped re-launch Whitehouse.gov on Drupal.[tpl]cite web|url=http://techpresident.com/blog-entry/whitehousegov-goes-drupal |title=Whitehouse goes Drupal |publisher=Techpresident.com |date=24 October 2009 |accessdate= 23 April 2012[/tpl]
On 31 March 2008, Dries launched Mollom, a service dedicated to stopping website spam: "Mollom's purpose is to dramatically reduce the effort of keeping your site clean and the quality of your content high. Currently, Mollom is a spam-killing one-two punch combination of a state-of-the-art spam filter and CAPTCHA server." Over 59,000 websites are protected by the Mollom service,[tpl]cite web|title=Mollom Scorecard|url=http://mollom.com/scorecard|accessdate=27 January 2013[/tpl] including all of Netlog's messages.[tpl]cite web|url=http://buytaert.net/mollom-filtering-millions-of-messages-for-netlog |title=Mollom filtering millions of messages for Netlog |publisher=Buytaert.net |date=20 May 2009 |accessdate= 23 April 2012[/tpl]
In 2008, Buytaert was elected "Young Entrepreneurs of Tech" by BusinessWeek.[tpl]cite web|last=Kharif |first=Olga |url=http://www.businessweek.com/technology/content/apr2008/tc20080417_388737.htm |title=Young Entrepreneurs of Tech 2008 |publisher=Businessweek.com |date=17 April 2008 |accessdate= 23 April 2012[/tpl] He was also named to the MIT Technology Review TR35 as one of the top 35 innovators in the world under the age of 35.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.technologyreview.com/tr35/?year=2008 |title=2008 Young Innovators Under 35 |publisher=Technology Review | year=2008 | accessdate=15 August 2011[/tpl][tpl]cite web|author=Christopher Chang, 33 University of California, Berkeley |url=http://www.technologyreview.com/TR35/Profile.aspx?Cand=T&TRID=688 |title=TR35 2008 Young Innovator |publisher=Technologyreview.com |accessdate= 23 April 2012[/tpl]
