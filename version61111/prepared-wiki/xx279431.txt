[[Foul Play ni Kurari / Sakura Meikyuu]]

CATEGORIES: 2012 singles

"Foul Play ni Kurari / Sakura Meikyuu" (ファールプレーにくらり／サクラメイキュウ) is a double A-side single released by singer and cellist Kanon Wakeshima. It is Kanon's third solo single and fifth overall. Foul Play ni Kurari was used as the ending theme song of the anime To Love-Ru, and Sakura Meikyuu was used as the theme song for game Fate / Extra CCC.
