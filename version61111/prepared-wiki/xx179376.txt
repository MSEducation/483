[[Gmina Kowala]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Radom County

Gmina Kowala is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Radom County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Kowala, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|11|km|mi|0[/tpl] south-west of Radom and [tpl]convert|100|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|74.71|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 10,631.

==Villages==

Gmina Kowala contains the villages and settlements of Augustów, Bardzice, Dąbrówka Zabłotnia, Grabina, Huta Mazowszańska, Kończyce-Kolonia, Kosów,
Kotarwice, Kowala, Ludwinów, Maliszów, Mazowszany, Młodocin Mniejszy, Parznice, Romanów, Rożki, Ruda Mała and Trablice.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Kowala is bordered by the city of Radom and by the gminas of Orońsko, Skaryszew, Wierzbica and Wolanów.
