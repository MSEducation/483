[[Metro Aculco]]

CATEGORIES: Mexico City metro stations, Railway stations opened in 1994, Nahuatl words and phrases

Metro Aculco is a station along Line 8 of the metro of Mexico City.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.metro.df.gob.mx/red/estacion.html?id=109|title=Aculco|language=Spanish|publisher=Sistema de Transporte Colectivo|accessdate=30 July 2011[/tpl][tpl]Cite web|last=Archambault|first=Richard|url=http://mexicometro.org/metro/line8/aculco/|title=Aculco »  Mexico City Metro System|accessdate=30 July 2011[/tpl][tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.visitmexicocity.com.mx/detalle.php?id_pat=6552&idioma=ch|title=ESTACIÓN ACULCO|accessdate=30 July 2011[/tpl]  It is located under the bridge where the Trabajadoras Sociales (Eje 6 Sur) passes over the Eje 3 Ote in the Colonia Pueblo Aculco neighborhood if the Iztapalapa borough of Mexico City. The station's logo is a water wave in a canal. In Nahuatl it means "where the water twists". The name is also the name of the Aculco municipio in the State of Mexico.  The station was opened on 20 July 1994.[tpl]Cite web|last=Monroy|first=Marco|editor-last=Schwandl|editor-first=Robert|url=http://www.urbanrail.net/am/mexi/mex-history.htm|title=Opening Dates for Mexico City's Subway|accessdate=20 August 2011[/tpl]
Metro service at the station was interrupted on 16 December 2006 after an accident in which a passenger fell onto the tracks.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.terra.com/deportes/articulo/html/fox341228.htm|title=Cae una persona a vias en la estacion Aculco del Metro|accessdate=30 July 2011[/tpl][tpl]Cite news|url=http://www.eluniversal.com.mx/notas/394462.html|title=Cae persona a vías en la estación Aculco del Metro|work=El Universal|date=16 December 2006|language=Spanish|accessdate=30 July 2011[/tpl] On 24 July 2007 a boy was born to a passenger at the station.[tpl]Cite news|url=http://www.eluniversal.com.mx/notas/438799.html|title=Nace bebé en estación del Metro Aculco|work=El Universal|language=Spanish|accessdate=31 July 2011[/tpl] On 26 September 2010 a Line 8 train operator was arrested and fired for drunk driving after an incident in which he opened the doors on the opposite side of the train from the Aculco station platform.[tpl]Cite news|url=http://www2.esmas.com/noticierostelevisa/mexico/df/207388/detienen-conductor-del-metro-ebrio|title=Detienen a conductor del Metro por ebrio|publisher=Noticieros Televisa|language=Spanish|date=27 September 2010|accessdate=30 July 2011[/tpl][tpl]Cite web|url=http://wn.com/Conductor_del_Metro_Ebrio|title=Conductor del Metro Ebrio|accessdate=31 July 2011[/tpl]
