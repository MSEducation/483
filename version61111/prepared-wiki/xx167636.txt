[[Don Hover]]

CATEGORIES: 1954 births, Living people, People from Seattle, Washington, American football linebackers, Washington State Cougars football players, Washington Redskins players

Donald R. Hover (born December 13, 1954 in Seattle, Washington) is a former American football linebacker in the National Football League for the Washington Redskins.  He played college football at Washington State University and was drafted in the eighth round of the 1978 NFL Draft.
