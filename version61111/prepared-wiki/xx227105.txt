[[James H. Dodson Residence]]

CATEGORIES: Houses completed in 1881, Los Angeles Historic-Cultural Monuments, Houses in Los Angeles, California, Victorian architecture in California, San Pedro, Los Angeles

The James H. Dodson Residence is a Los Angeles Historic-Cultural Monument (HCM #147) located in the San Pedro section of Los Angeles, California, near the Port of Los Angeles.  
The Victorian architecture style house was built in 1881 by the Sepulveda family as a wedding present for their daughter Rudecinda and her husband, James Dodson. It was originally located at the corner of 7th and Beacon Streets.  It is a private residence and is not open to the public.[tpl]cite web|title=Dodson House|publisher=San Pedro.com | url=http://www.sanpedro.com/sp_point/dodsnhs.htm[/tpl]
