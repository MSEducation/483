[[CBVFI]]

CATEGORIES: Vietnamese stock market indices

CBVFI (CBV Foreign Investors Indexes) also as CBVI 15, includes one composite CBV Foreign Investors Index and 10 industry
sub-indexes based on Industry Classification Benchmark. Along with well-known criteria including liquidity
test, free-float market capital requirement and industry representatives, CBV Foreign Investors Indexes
(in short: CBVFI) introduce a new special selection for the top Vietnamese stocks which are currently
available for foreign investors with safety percentage weight of index components, and safety margin of P/E
ratio for each stock to protect foreign investors from non-investability, and too much aggressive investment
style.
