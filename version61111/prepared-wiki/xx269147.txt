[[Lila Walter Evans]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Year of birth missing (living people), Members of the Montana House of Representatives, Montana Republicans, People from Glacier County, Montana, Women state legislators in Montana

Lila Walter Evans is a former Republican member of the Montana House of Representatives, who represented District 16 from 2011 to 2013. She was defeated for re-election in 2012 by Lea Whitford, by a margin of 1,823 to 935.[tpl]cite web|title=2012 Unofficial General Election Results|url=http://ksenam.com/files/2012/11/Statewide-Unofficial-Results.pdf|publisher=KSEN AM 1150|accessdate=26 March 2014[/tpl]
