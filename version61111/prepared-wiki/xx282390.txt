[[Oh No! It's an Alien Invasion]]

CATEGORIES: 2013 Canadian television series debuts, Canadian animated television series, YTV shows

Oh No! It's An Alien Invasion! is an Canadian animated television series that is produced by Nelvana Limited in association with YTV. It premiered on YTV on August 3, 2013.[tpl]cite web|title=YTV Fast Forwards to Fall with Seven Side-Splitting New Series|url=http://www.corusent.com/home/Corporate/PressReleases/tabid/1697/Default.aspx?Id=2527[/tpl] The series was created by Philippe Ivanusic-Vallee and Peter Ricq. The first season consists of 26 episodes. In October 2013, YTV renewed the show for a second season of 14 episodes. The series is currently in reruns of the first 13 episodes, airing Saturdays at 7:00am.

==Cast==

==Plot==

Follow Nate and his rag-tag underground kid resistance group, Super Wicked Extreme Emergency Team (S.W.E.E.T), as they battle it out with aliens from outer space who have kidnapped all of the adults and taken over the planet.
