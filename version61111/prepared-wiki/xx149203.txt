[[Dnevni list]]

CATEGORIES: Newspapers published in Bosnia and Herzegovina, Croatian-language newspapers, Media in Mostar, Publications with year of establishment missing

Dnevni list is a popular daily newspaper in Bosnia and Herzegovina. Its headquarters is in Mostar.[tpl]cite book|author=Kadri Ackarbasic|title=International Journal of Rule of Law, Transitional Justice And Human Rights|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=_hp7nOXOBuIC&pg=PA90|accessdate=25 January 2014|publisher=Association Pravnik Sarajevo|page=90|id=GGKEY:B0XLC3UWS4H[/tpl] The paper is especially popular among the nation of the Croats  and Bosniaks.
