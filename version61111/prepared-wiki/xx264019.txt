[[Otto Preißecker]]

CATEGORIES: Austrian male single skaters, 1898 births, 1963 deaths, Sportspeople from Vienna, World Figure Skating Championships medalists, European Figure Skating Championships medalists

Otto Preißecker also Preissecker (August 3, 1898 – May 30, 1963) was an Austrian figure skater. He was the 1926-1928 national champion. He also skated in pairs with partner Gisela Hochhaltinger.
