[[Etcetera (Casuals Etcetera)]]

CATEGORIES: 1981 establishments in the United States, Clothing companies of the United States, Clothing brands, Companies based in New York City, Companies established in 1981

Etcetera is a women's contemporary clothing design company known for everyday wear sold by appointment only via private trunk shows across the country. Founded in 2000 by William Rondina, Etcetera is a company sells its clothes through a customized trunk show shopping experience.
Headquartered in New York City, Etcetera's national network of wardrobe representatives hold seasonal trunk showings of the company's four collections each year in over 660 cities and upscale suburbs across the U.S.
