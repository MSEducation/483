[[WKOC-LP]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Alabama, Low-power FM radio stations in Alabama, Christian radio stations in the United States, DeKalb County, Alabama, 3ABN radio stations, Radio stations disestablished in 2011, Defunct radio stations in the United States

WKOC-LP (103.1 FM) was a low power FM radio station formerly licensed to serve Ider, Alabama, USA. The station was owned by Ider Educational Broadcasting.
It aired a Christian radio format.[tpl]cite web |work=Alabama Broadcast Media Page |title=Alabama LPFM & Translators |url=http://bellsouthpwp2.net/c/r/crackedlcd/almediapage/lpfm.html |accessdate=2008-05-21[/tpl] The station received a portion of its programming as part of the Three Angels Broadcasting Network.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.3abnradio.org/radio_stations.cfm |work=3ABN Radio Network |title=3ABN Radio Stations[/tpl]

==History==

This station received its original construction permit from the Federal Communications Commission on June 18, 2003.[tpl]cite web |publisher=FCC Media Bureau |title=Application Search Details (BNPL-20010615AXF) |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/app_det.pl?Application_id=570497 |date=June 18, 2003[/tpl] The new station was assigned the call letters WKOC-LP by the FCC on September 17, 2004.[tpl]cite web |title=Call Sign History |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=135500&Callsign=WKOC-LP |publisher=FCC Media Bureau CDBS Public Access Database[/tpl] WKOC-LP received its license to cover from the FCC on July 19, 2005.[tpl]cite web |publisher=FCC Media Bureau |title=Application Search Details (BLL-20041217AVL) |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/app_det.pl?Application_id=1035729 |date=July 19, 2005[/tpl] On December 2, 2011, the station's license was cancelled and its call sign deleted from the FCC's database per the licensee's request.[tpl]cite web |publisher=FCC Media Bureau |title=Broadcast Actions |url=http://hraunfoss.fcc.gov/edocs_public/attachmatch/DOC-311354A1.pdf |date=December 7, 2011[/tpl]
