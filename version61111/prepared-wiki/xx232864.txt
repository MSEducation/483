[[KDNR]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Wyoming, Religious radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 2002, 2002 establishments in Wyoming

KDNR (88.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to South Greeley, Wyoming, USA. The station carries religious programming. The station is currently owned by  Western Inspirational Broadcasters. ==History==

The station was assigned the call letters KWRZ on 2002-06-19. On 2003-11-22, the station changed its call sign to KLCQ and on 2006-03-27 to KKGN. On 2007-03-09 the station became KEZF, and finally on 2008-05-16, the current KDNR. [tpl]cite web|url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=84104&Callsign=KDNR |title=KDNR Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl] 

==Translators==

In addition to the main station, KDNR is relayed by an additional two translators to widen its broadcast area.
