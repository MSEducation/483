[[Shire of Daylesford and Glenlyon]]

CATEGORIES: Former local government areas of Victoria (Australia)

The Shire of Daylesford and Glenlyon was a local government area about [tpl]convert|110|km|mi|0[/tpl] northwest of Melbourne, the state capital of Victoria, Australia. The shire covered an area of [tpl]convert|609.3|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and existed from 1860 until 1995.

==History==

What became the Shire of Daylesford and Glenlyon had its origins in several different local authorities:
