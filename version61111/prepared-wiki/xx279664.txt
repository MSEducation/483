[[Lubinski Racing]]

CATEGORIES: Defunct NASCAR teams

Lubinski Racing was a NASCAR team from 1971-1974.  Lubinski Racing failed to record a win, but did manage to earn a top 5, 9 top 10's, and 20 top 20's.  The team failed to finish the race 31 times.  Drivers who raced for Lubinski was J.D. Mcduffie, Frank Warren, Jim Hurtubise, and Ron Keselowski.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.driveraverages.com/nascar_stats/team.php?team_now=729 |title=NASCAR Statistics: Lubinski Racing |publisher=Driveraverages.com |date= |accessdate=2014-03-16[/tpl]
