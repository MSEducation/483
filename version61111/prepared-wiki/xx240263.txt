[[Lodewijk De Witte]]

CATEGORIES: 1954 births, Living people, People from West Flanders, Governors of Flemish Brabant, Katholieke Universiteit Leuven alumni

Lodewijk (Lode) De Witte (Zwevegem, 12 December 1954) is the governor of the Belgian province of Flemish Brabant.
After his studies at the law school of the Katholieke Universiteit Leuven, he remained active as a researcher and became specialised in issues concerning labour law (namely the formal position of foreign employees), which was assigned to him by the European Economic Community. Since 1981, he was involved with the Emile Vandervelde-Institute, where he became a public servant in 1988.
