[[Saudasjøen]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Rogaland, Sauda

Saudasjøen is a small village in Sauda municipality, Norway. It is located about 4 kilometres south of the city of Sauda, and for statistical purposes it is considered a part of the Sauda urban area. Saudasjøen is the former administrative centre in the municipality.
Historically, it is an important industrial location, especially in the field of metallurgy.
In the fall 2005, Saudasjøen finally got a football"binge"! This was built by parents and other inhabitants of Saudasjøen with funding collected from the people and businesses of Saudasjøen, with support of the local community.
