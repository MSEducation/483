[[Sam Anderson (Tamil actor)]]

CATEGORIES: Tamil actors, Indian actors, Living people, People from Erode district

Sam Anderson, born Anderson Samuel is a Tamil actor and internet celebrity. He played the lead in the Tamil movie Yaaruku Yaaro and became popular on the internet for his "feeble dance moves and feebler acting skills.".[tpl]cite web | url=http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2012-11-04/chennai/34908307_1_simple-superstar-wilbur-sargunaraj-social-media | title=Times Of India article | accessdate=November 24, 2012[/tpl]
He was born in Erode, Tamil Nadu and originally ran a courier delivery business, with an employee base of five[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.vikatan.com/anandavikatan/Cinema-News/8632--2.html | title=Ananda Vikatan interview | accessdate=November 24, 2012[/tpl] in L.A. Anderson was inspired by the Tamil film Chocolate, and decided to turn his life towards the cine industry.
Anderson played the lead role David, in the film Yaaruku Yaaro, produced by his uncle, and released in four  theaters in Tamil Nadu. For the sake of the film, Anderson Samuel changed his name to Sam Anderson, and practiced acting, story-writing and dancing in front of a mirror.
Yaaruku Yaaro ran for 25 days in the 4 theaters released. The film was released in parts on YouTube, and "went viral" on the Internet.  Anderson states  "What is the secret of my success? It could be because everything went wrong in my first attempt" .
