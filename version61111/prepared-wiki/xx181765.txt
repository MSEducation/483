[[Renaissance Tower (Sacramento)]]

CATEGORIES: Office buildings completed in 1989, Buildings and structures in Sacramento, California, Skyscrapers between 100 and 149 meters, Skyscrapers in Sacramento, California

Renaissance Tower is a [tpl]convert|113|m|abbr=on[/tpl] skyscraper in Sacramento, California,  completed in 1989. The 28 storey tower was the tallest in the city when completed, and is now the fifth.
