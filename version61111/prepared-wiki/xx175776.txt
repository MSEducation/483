[[WGLY-FM]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Vermont, Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 1995

WGLY-FM (91.5 FM, "The Light") is a radio station licensed to Bolton, Vermont, serving the Burlington, Vermont area. The station is owned by Christian Ministries, Inc.WGLY-FM fcc.gov. Accessed August 21, 2012
The Light Radio network's programming includes Christian Talk and Teaching and Christian music. Christian Talk and Teaching shows heard on The Light Radio network include; Focus On The Family, Turning Point with David Jeremiah, Truth for Life with Alistair Begg, Enjoying Everyday Life with Joyce Meyer, Grace to You with John MacArthur, Insight For Living with Chuck Swindoll, Revive Our Hearts with Nancy Leigh DeMoss, Joni and Friends, and In The Market with Janet Parshall.Weekday Programs The Light Radio. Accessed August 22, 2012 Heather Sheppard, Ric McClarry and Bob Pierce are radio hosts.
