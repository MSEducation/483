[[FK Bashkimi (2011)]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in the Republic of Macedonia, Sport in Kumanovo, Association football clubs established in 2011, 2011 establishments in the Republic of Macedonia

KF Bashkimi ([tpl]lang-mk|ФК Башкими[/tpl]) is a football club, which plays in the town of Kumanovo, Republic of Macedonia. They currently compete in the Macedonian Third League. Bashkimi was established in 2011, after the dissolvement of the club with same name, a club which folded before of the 2008–09 Macedonian First League season, due to a high financial debt.FK Bashkimi quits from Macedonian football  Legally, the two clubs' track records and honours are kept separate by the Football Federation of Macedonia.
The club's name means "unity" in Albanian.

==Supporters==

FK Bashkimi supporters were called Ilirët.
