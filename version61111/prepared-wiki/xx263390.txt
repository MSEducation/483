[[Thiruvananthapuram Golf Club]]

CATEGORIES: Sports in Thiruvananthapuram, Golf clubs and courses in India

Thiruvananthapuram Golf Club is a nine-hole golf course located at Thiruvananthapuram city in the Indian state of Kerala.[tpl]cite news|title=Go for golf|url=http://www.hinduonnet.com/thehindu/mp/2003/05/05/stories/2003050501480100.htm|accessdate=2 January 2011|newspaper=The Hindu|date=May 5, 2003[/tpl]

==Area==

The course is spread over 25.38 acres. It was started around 1850s during the days of the late Maharajah Sreemoolam Tirunal. The golf course here is one of the oldest in the country and is more than 150 years old.[tpl]cite news|title=Heritage golf club being restored|url=http://www.hinduonnet.com/2003/04/19/stories/2003041907760300.htm|accessdate=2 January 2011|newspaper=The Hindu|date=Apr 19, 2003[/tpl] It is also among the least expensive and less crowded places to play.
