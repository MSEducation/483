[[Miss You (Yuna Ito song)]]

CATEGORIES: 2008 singles, Yuna Ito songs

"Miss You" is the 11th single of Japanese artist Yuna Ito slated for a release on September 3, 2008.
Miss You is currently being used as the Ito En Vitamin Fruit CM song. Miss You was the inspiring song for the cell phone novel [tpl]nihongo|"Tenshi no Koi"|天使の恋|lit. The Angel's Love[/tpl]Tenshi no Koi Official Website song preview
