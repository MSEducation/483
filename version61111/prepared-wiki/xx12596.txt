[[Telecommunications in Serbia]]

CATEGORIES: Communications in Serbia

Telecommunication in Serbia have a developed and efficient telephone network, and has a number of radio and television broadcast stations.  The country code for Serbia is "SRB", the ccTLD is ".rs".

==Overviewhttp://www.ratel.rs/upload/documents/Pregled_trzista/Ratel_Pregled_trzista_2012.pdf==

Telephones - main lines in use: 2.990.100 (2012) 41% main lines penetration
Telephones - mobile cellular: 9.137.900 (2012) 125% mobile penetration
Telephone system:
Radio broadcast stations: AM 113, FM 194, shortwave 2 (1998)
Television broadcast stations: more than 771 (including 86 strong stations and 685 low-power stations, plus 20 repeaters in the principal networks;
also numerous local or private stations (1997)
Internet hosts: 22,046 (2005)
Internet users: 3.8 million (2011)
Internet service providers: 222 (2012)
Internet TLD: .rs, .срб (Cyrillic)
