==Behaviour==

Strap-toothed whales' diet consists primarily of squid.  Adult males have a gape half the size of females and juveniles, limiting their squid to those weighing [tpl]convert|100|g|oz[/tpl] and less.  Nothing is known about social organization of this species.

==Conservation==

This species has never been hunted or entangled in fishing gear. It is believed to be in a rather safe position compared with other mesoplodonts.
