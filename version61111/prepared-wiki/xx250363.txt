[[1989 Athens Trophy]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 WTA Tour, Athens Trophy, 1989 in Greek sport

The 1989 Athens Trophy was a tennis tournament played on outdoor clay courts in Athens in Greece that was part of Tier V of the 1989 WTA Tour. The tournament was held from September 11 through September 17, 1989.
