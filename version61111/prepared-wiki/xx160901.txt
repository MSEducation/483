[[Manitoba Soccer Association]]

CATEGORIES: Soccer in Manitoba, Soccer governing bodies in Canada, 1905 establishments in Canada, Sports governing bodies of Manitoba

The Manitoba Soccer Association (MSA) is the governing body for soccer in the Canadian province of Manitoba. The association was formed March 19, 1896 in Brandon.
The MSA is a member of the national governing body, the Canadian Soccer Association. [tpl]As of|2007[/tpl], its president is Sean Drain.
