[[Spigelia]]

CATEGORIES: Loganiaceae

Spigelia is a genus of flowering plants in the family Loganiaceae. It contains around 60 species, distributed over the warmer parts of the Americas, from the latitude of Buenos Aires to the Southern United States.[tpl]cite journal |author=Alex V.
