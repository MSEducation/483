[[Ian Beaurepaire]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, Mayors and Lord Mayors of Melbourne, Male mayors of places in Australia

Ian Francis Beaurepaire is a former Lord Mayor of Melbourne.
Beaurepaire was the son of Australian Olympic swimmer and prominent businessman, Sir Frank Beaurepaire. He was Lord Mayor of Melbourne from 1965 to 1967.
