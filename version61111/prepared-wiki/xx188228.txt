[[Football West State League Division One]]

CATEGORIES: Football West State League

The Football West State League Division One is the second highest state level soccer competition in Western Australia. The league is semi-professional and is conducted by Football West, the state's soccer governing body which is sanctioned by FIFA.

==Structure==

The league operates with a promotion and relegation system. The team that finishes top of the table after 22 games is considered champions and offered a place in the State League Premier Division. A new State League Second Division will be established in 2012, which might include teams that finish near the bottom of the First Division this season.
