[[Henry IV, Count of Bar]]

CATEGORIES: 1315 births, 1344 deaths, Counts of Bar

Henry IV of Bar (between 1315 and 1320â€“1344) was count of Bar from 1336 to 1344. His aunt, Joan of Bar, Countess of Surrey, governed Bar in his name during his minority.  He was the son of Edward I of Bar and his wife Marie of Burgundy.  He married Yolande of Dampierre (died 1395), a granddaughter of Robert III, Count of Flanders. Both his children (Edward II of Bar and Robert I of Bar) succeeded him as count.
