[[Gmina Szerzyny]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Lesser Poland Voivodeship, Tarnów County

Gmina Szerzyny is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Tarnów County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. Its seat is the village of Szerzyny, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|31|km|mi|0[/tpl] south-east of Tarnów and [tpl]convert|99|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] east of the regional capital Kraków.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|82.24|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 8,211.
The gmina contains part of the protected area called Pasmo Brzanki Landscape Park.

==Villages==

Gmina Szerzyny contains the villages and settlements of Czermna, Ołpiny, Swoszowa, Szerzyny and Żurowa.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Szerzyny is bordered by the gminas of Biecz, Brzyska, Jodłowa, Ryglice, Rzepiennik Strzyżewski, Skołyszyn and Tuchów.
