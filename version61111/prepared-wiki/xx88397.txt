[[Jan Miodek]]

Jan Miodek (born June 7, 1946, in Tarnowskie Góry, Silesian Voivodeship), Professor of Wrocław University, is a Polish linguist[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.catholicnews.com/data/stories/cns/0603042.htm|title=Poles praise pope for addressing them in Polish during papal trip|last=Luxmoore|first=Jonathan|date=25 May 2006|accessdate=8 March 2011[/tpl] in the normative tradition. For several years he has held the post of the head (director) of the Institute of Polish Filology at the Wrocław University.
