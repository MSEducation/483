[[New Japan Aviation]]

CATEGORIES: Airlines of Japan, 1969 establishments in Japan

[tpl]nihongo|New Japan Aviation|新日本航空|Shin Nihon Kōkū[/tpl] is a Japanese aviation services company that operates scheduled air service between Niigata and Sado Island, as well as flight training and other services from its headquarters at Kagoshima Airport.

==History==

NJA began scheduled service between Niigata and Sado in February 2011.[tpl]cite news|title=佐渡/新潟に新日本航空が就航へ-整備士の募集開始|url=http://flyteam.jp/airline/new-japan-aviation/news/article/902|accessdate=21 January 2014|newspaper=FlyTeam.jp|date=13 February 2011[/tpl] 
NJA suspended the route for eleven days in July 2012 after its Islander aircraft struck a car while taxiing at Niigata Airport.[tpl]cite news|title=新日本航空、新潟/佐渡線の運航を再開-7月14日から|url=http://flyteam.jp/airline/new-japan-aviation/news/article/12627|accessdate=21 January 2014|newspaper=FlyTeam.jp|date=15 July 2012[/tpl] NJA suspended the route again in February 2013 due to a faulty generator indicator on its Islander aircraft, which could only be fixed by importing special components.[tpl]cite news|title=新日本航空、メーターの故障で10月5日から新潟/佐渡線を運休|url=http://flyteam.jp/airline/new-japan-aviation/news/article/15494|accessdate=21 January 2014|newspaper=FlyTeam.jp|date=7 October 2012[/tpl] The service resumed in August 2013.[tpl]cite news|title=新日本航空、新潟/佐渡線の運航を再開 1ヶ月半は1往復運航|url=http://flyteam.jp/airline/new-japan-aviation/news/article/25859|accessdate=21 January 2014|newspaper=FlyTeam.jp|date=28 August 2013[/tpl] It is the only airline currently providing scheduled service to Sado Island, although other scheduled operators have served the airport in the past.[tpl]cite web|title=佐渡空港|url=http://www.pref.niigata.lg.jp/sado_seibi/1204045245988.html|publisher=Niigata Prefectural Government|accessdate=21 January 2014[/tpl]
