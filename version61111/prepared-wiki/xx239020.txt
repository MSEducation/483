[[Our Mother of Sorrows Roman Catholic Church Complex]]

CATEGORIES: Properties of religious function on the National Register of Historic Places in New York, Roman Catholic churches in New York, Religious buildings completed in 1878, 19th-century Roman Catholic church buildings, Churches in Monroe County, New York

Our Mother of Sorrows Roman Catholic Church Complex is a historic Roman Catholic church complex located at Greece in Monroe County, New York.  The complex consists of the Romanesue Revival style brick church (ca 1858-1878) and the adjacent community cemetery (1823-ca. 1900).  The church was converted for use as a public library.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=4249|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: Our Mother of Sorrows Roman Catholic Church Complex|date=September 1989|accessdate=2009-10-01 |author=Nancy L. Todd|publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation[/tpl] See also: [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=4250|title=Accompanying seven photos[/tpl]
It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1989.
