[[Majdan Sobolewski]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Lubartów County

Majdan Sobolewski [tpl]IPAc-pl|'|m|a|I|d|a|n|-|s|o|b|o|'|l|e|f|s|k|i[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Firlej, within Lubartów County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa|title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) – TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal)|date=2008-06-01|language=Polish[/tpl] It lies approximately [tpl]convert|6|km|mi|0[/tpl] south-west of Firlej, [tpl]convert|14|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north-west of Lubartów, and [tpl]convert|34|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of the regional capital Lublin.
The village has an approximate population of 160.
