[[Shahidabad, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Shahidabad ([tpl]lang-fa|شهيداباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Shahīdābād)[tpl]IranNCSGN[/tpl] is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 735, in 142 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
