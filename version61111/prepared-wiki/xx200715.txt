[[VL class]]

CATEGORIES: Diesel locomotives of Australia, Co-Co locomotives, Railway locomotives introduced in 2007

The VL class are a class of Australian diesel locomotives built by Avteq, Melbourne for Chicago Freight Car Leasing Australia between February 2007 and March 2009.VL Class Vicsig,netVL Class Locomotive Chicago Freight Car Leasing Australia
They have been hired to a variety of operators and operated in New South Wales, Victoria, South Australia, Western Australia and the Northern Territory. Some are named after notable Australian racehorses.
