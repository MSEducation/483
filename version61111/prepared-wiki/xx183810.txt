[[Miss Universe 1960]]

CATEGORIES: Miss Universe, 1960 in the United States, 1960 beauty pageants, Beauty pageants in the United States

Miss Universe 1960, the 9th Miss Universe pageant, was won by 18-year-old Linda Bement of USA. She was crowned by Akiko Kojima of Japan. It took place on 9 July 1960 at the Miami Beach Auditorium in Miami Beach, Florida, USA.
