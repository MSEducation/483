[[Barry Gillis]]

Barry Gillis ([tpl]lang-ga|Barra Mac Giolla Íosa[/tpl]; born 12 January 1980) is an Irish Gaelic footballer who plays for Derry, with whom he has won the National League twice. He plays his club football for Magherafelt O'Donovan Rossa.
Gillis plays full forward for Magherafelt, but plays as goalkeeper for Derry.[tpl]cite news |first=Alan |last=Rodgers |title=Kerry litmus test |url= |work=Gaelic Life |publisher= |date=29 February 2008 |accessdate=2008-06-08 [/tpl] He is in the unique position of winning a National League medal as an outfielder (2000), and another National League medal as goalkeeper (2008).[tpl]Cite web |url=http://www.hoganstand.com/ArticleForm.aspx?ID=105182 |title=Derry need to make a championship mark says Gillis |accessdate=3 January 2009 |work=Hogan Stand |publisher=Lynn Publications |date=2 January 2009 [/tpl]

==Playing career==

===Inter-county===

Gillis was originally called up to the Derry panel as an outfielder in the late 1990s and was on the panel for Derry's 2000 National League success.[tpl]cite news |url=http://www.independent.ie/sport/derry-record-intact-after-a-dour-struggle-392019.html |title=Derry record intact after a dour struggle|author= |work=Irish Independent|date= 29 November 1999|accessdate=2008-03-16[/tpl] Gillis was part of the panel when Derry reached that year's Ulster Senior Football Championship final, but were defeated by Armagh. He pulled out of the panel for a couple of seasons in the 2000s as he was working in Dublin, but returned again.
He was part of the Derry team that won the 2008 National League where Derry beat Kerry in the final.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.rte.ie/sport/gaa/2008/0427/derrykerry.html |title=Derry 2-13 Kerry 2-09 2008 National League Final report |accessdate=27 April 2008 |work=RTÉ Online | archiveurl= http://web.archive.org/web/20080430222703/http://www.rte.ie/sport/gaa/2008/0427/derrykerry.html| archivedate= 30 April 2008 | deadurl= no[/tpl][tpl]cite web |url=http://www.derrygaa.ie/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=630&Itemid=56 |title=Derry 2-13 Kerry 2-09 2008 National League Final report - Derry GAA website |accessdate=27 April 2008 |format= |work=Derry GAA website [/tpl]

===Club===

Gillis usually plays goalkepper for Magherafelt.
