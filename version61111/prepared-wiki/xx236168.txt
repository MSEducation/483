[[Calopappus]]

CATEGORIES: Asteraceae genera, Monotypic plant genera, Endemic flora of Chile

Calopappus is a monotypic genus of flowering plants in the aster family, Asteraceae, containing the single species Calopappus acerosus. It is endemic to central Chile, where it occurs in the Andes.Morrone, J. J., et al. (1997). A cladistic biogeographic analysis of Central Chile. J Comp Biol 2(1) 25-41.
