[[Victoria Hogg]]

CATEGORIES: British singer-songwriters, British pop singers, British female singers, People from Darley Dale, 1983 births, Living people

Victoria Hogg (born 15 June 1983) is a British singer-songwriter.

==Biography==

Victoria Hogg was born in Darley Dale, Derbyshire, England.
She was discovered at 15 years old after playing at a music seminar at Sheffield's Red Tape Studio[ref]
