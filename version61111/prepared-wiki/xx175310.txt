[[Gmina Grudusk]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Ciechanów County

Gmina Grudusk is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Ciechanów County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Grudusk, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|22|km|mi|0[/tpl] north of Ciechanów and [tpl]convert|97|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|96.69|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 3,892.

==Villages==

Gmina Grudusk contains the villages and settlements of Borzuchowo-Daćbogi, Dębowo, Grudusk, Grudusk-Brzozowo, Grudusk-Olszak, Humięcino, Humięcino-Andrychy, Humięcino-Klary, Humięcino-Koski, Humięcino-Retki, Kołaki Małe, Kołaki Wielkie, Leśniewo Dolne, Leśniewo Górne, Łysakowo, Mierzanowo, Mierzanowo-Kolonia, Nieborzyn, Polanka, Przywilcz, Pszczółki Górne, Pszczółki-Czubaki, Pszczółki-Szerszenie, Purzyce-Rozwory, Purzyce-Trojany, Rąbież Gruduski, Sokólnik, Sokołowo, Stryjewo Wielkie, Strzelnia, Wiksin, Wiśniewo, Zakrzewo Małe, Zakrzewo Wielkie and Żarnowo.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Grudusk is bordered by the gminas of Czernice Borowe, Dzierzgowo, Regimin, Stupsk and Szydłowo.
