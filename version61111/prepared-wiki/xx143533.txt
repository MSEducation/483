[[Róger Gómez]]

CATEGORIES: 1965 births, Living people, Costa Rican footballers, Costa Rica international footballers, 1990 FIFA World Cup players, C.S. Herediano footballers, C.S. Cartaginés players

Róger Gómez Tenorio (born February 7, 1965) is a former Costa Rican soccer player.
He played for C.S. Cartaginés and C.S. Herediano and scored 119 league goals during his career.[tpl]cite web|publisher=UNAFUT|title=Club de los 100|url=http://unafut.com/site/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=58:club-de-los-100&catid=5:comision-de-historia-y-estadistica&Itemid=34|language=Spanish|date=2009-08-24[/tpl]
He was part of the national team squad, that played in the 1990 FIFA World Cup held in Italy, and featured in three of their four games played.[tpl]FIFA player|174567[/tpl] Nicknamed 'El Policía', the midfielder collected over 20 caps for the "Ticos". He played his last international on February 16, 1993 against Nicaragua.
