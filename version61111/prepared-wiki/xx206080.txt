[[2846 Ylppö]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Asteroids named for people, Astronomical objects discovered in 1942

2846 Ylppö is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 2114.1382112 days (5.79 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=2846 | title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-05-23 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on February 12, 1942.
