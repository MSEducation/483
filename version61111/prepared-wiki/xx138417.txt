[[Hardwood Records]]

CATEGORIES: Canadian independent record labels, Vanity record labels, Indie rock record labels, Record labels established in 1994

Hardwood Records is a Canadian independent record label, owned and operated by singer-songwriter Hayden."Hayden Signs to Arts & Crafts for New Album". Exclaim!, November 8, 2012. Originally formed to distribute his own albums, the label has also released albums by Cuff the Duke, Basia Bulat and Lou Canon.
