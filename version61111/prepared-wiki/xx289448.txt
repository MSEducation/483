[[Mohammad Khalaj]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Mohammad Khalaj ([tpl]lang-fa|محمدخلج[/tpl], also Romanized as Moḩammad Khalaj; also known as Muhammad Khalach)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3075536|Mohammad Khalaj[/tpl] is a village in Bizineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,714, in 408 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
