[[Alberto Castagnetti]]

CATEGORIES: 1943 births, 2009 deaths, People from Verona, Italian swimmers, Italian swimming coaches, Olympic swimmers of Italy, Swimmers at the 1972 Summer Olympics, Deaths from surgical complications

Alberto Castagnetti (3 February 1943, Verona - 12 October 2009http://www.gazzetta.it/Sport_Vari/Altri_Sport/Nuoto/12-10-2009/-morto-alberto-castagnetti-501596194838.shtml) was an Italian swimming coach and freestyle swimmer. 
As a swimmer, Castagnetti won several Italian titles swimming in relays. He swam at the 1972 Summer Olympics in Munich and at the 1973 World Aquatics Championships in Belgrade.
He coached the Italian national swimming team from 1987 until his death from the consequences of a heart operation in October 2009.http://www.gazzetta.it/Sport_Vari/Altri_Sport/Nuoto/12-10-2009/-morto-alberto-castagnetti-501596194838.shtml Notable athletes he coached include Giorgio Lamberti, Domenico Fioravanti and Federica Pellegrini.
He has 2 daughters and 2 sons and he has 6 grandchildren. They all love swimming and have taken after him
