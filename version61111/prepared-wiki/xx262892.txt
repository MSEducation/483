[[Swakopmund Constituency]]

CATEGORIES: Constituencies of Erongo Region, Swakopmund

Swakopmund constituency is a constituency in the Erongo Region of Namibia. The constituency had a population of 25,442 in 2004.Constituencies of Namibia, 2004 The district capital is the city of Swakopmund.

==Politics==

In the 2010 regional elections, SWAPO's Germina Ndapua Shitaleni won the constituency with 5,132 votes. His only challenger was Esegiel Guiseb of the Rally for Democracy and Progress, who received 2,540 votes.Election results from Electoral Commission of Namibia
