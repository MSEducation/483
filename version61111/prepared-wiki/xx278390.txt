[[B. N. Banerjee]]

B. N. Banerjee (1916-2002) was a Bengali speaking  Indian lawyer and parliamentarian.Rajya Sabha members list, surname starting with the letter 'B'

==Education==

After graduating from the Scottish Church College,Some Alumni of Scottish Church College in 175th Year Commemoration Volume. Scottish Church College, April 2008. page 592 he studied law and earned his LL.B, and subsequently his LL.M degrees from the University of Calcutta.

==Career==

He served as the Secretary-General of the Rajya Sabha from 1963 to 1976. He was nominated as a member of the Rajya Sabha in 1976 where he served till 1982.
