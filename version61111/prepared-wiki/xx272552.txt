[[April 2006 Iran-Iraq cross-border raids]]

April 2006 Iran-Iraq cross-border raids were three military cross-border attacks on Iran-Iraqi Kurdistan border, in which PJAK claimed to had killed 24 members of Iranian security forces in early April, 2006.Tehran faces growing Kurdish opposition, James Brandon, The Washington Times, April 3, 2006 The raids were motivated as retaliation for the killing of 10 Kurds demonstrating in Maku by Iranian security forces.On April 10, 2006, seven PJAK members were arrested in Iran, on a suspicion that they had killed three Iranian security force personnel.[tpl]cn|date=September 2011[/tpl]
