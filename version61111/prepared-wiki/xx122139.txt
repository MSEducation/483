[[Anthology (Christie Front Drive album)]]

CATEGORIES: 1995 compilation albums, Christie Front Drive albums

Anthology is an album by American indie rock band Christie Front Drive.  The album was released in 1995 by Caulfield Records. It is a compilation of the band's first 12" and 7" releases up to 1995, however excluding the three songs from their split EP with Boys Life.
