[[My Radio Sure Sounds Good to Me]]

CATEGORIES: 1978 albums, Graham Central Station albums, Warner Bros. Records albums

My Radio Sure Sounds Good to Me is sixth album by Larry Graham & Graham Central Station.

==Reception==

Released in 1978, This record marked the first time that Graham called his band Larry Graham & Graham Central Station (as opposed to simply Graham Central Station). The album peaked at number eighteen on the Billboard Top Soul Albums chart.

==Track listing==

All songs written by Larry Graham.
