[[Crib Point railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1889

Crib Point railway station is located on the Stony Point line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the town of Crib Point and HMAS Cerberus opening on 17 December 1889.Point Crib Point Vicsig A former freight yard sits opposite of the station, while a branch line into HMAS Cerberus is situated at the northern end of the station, past the level crossing.

==Platforms & services==

Crib Point has one platform. It is serviced by Metro Trains' Stony Point line services.Stony Point Line timetable Public Transport Victoria
