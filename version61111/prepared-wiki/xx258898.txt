[[Ilham Chahine]]

CATEGORIES: 1962 births, Living people, Egyptian actresses

Ilham Chahine ([tpl]lang-ar| إلهام شاهين[/tpl]) (Various spellings are Elham Shahin, and Elham Shaheen) is an Egyptian actress.
She has appeared in many Egyptian films and television series and has won both Egyptian and international awards.[tpl]cite web |title=Elham Shahin|publisher=Fanoos Encyclopedia |url=http://www.fanoos.com/society/elham_shahin.html[/tpl]
