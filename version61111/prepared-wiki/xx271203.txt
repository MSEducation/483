[[Mohammed Hazzaz]]

CATEGORIES: 1945 births, Moroccan footballers, Morocco international footballers, Association football goalkeepers, MAS Fez players, 1970 FIFA World Cup players, Footballers at the 1972 Summer Olympics, Olympic footballers of Morocco, 1978 African Cup of Nations players, Living people

Mohammed Hazzaz (born 1945) is a Moroccan football goalkeeper who played for Morocco in the 1970 FIFA World Cup.1970 FIFA World Cup Mexico He also played for MAS Fez.
Hazzaz represented Morocco at the 1972 Summer Olympics in Munich.
