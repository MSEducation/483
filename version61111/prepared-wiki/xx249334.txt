[[Yokoiso Station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Aomori Prefecture, Gonō Line

[tpl]nihongo| Yokoiso Station|横磯駅|Yokoiso-eki[/tpl] is a railway station on the JR East Gonō Line located in the town of Fukaura, Aomori Prefecture Japan. It is 61.4 rail kilometers from the southern terminus of the Gonō Line at Higashi-Noshiro Station.

==History==

Yokoizo Station was opened on December 25, 1954 as a station on the Japan National Railways (JNR). With the privatization of the JNR on April 1, 1987, it came under the operational control of JR East.

==Lines==

==Station layout==

Yokoiso Station has a single side platform serving bidirectional traffic. The station is unattended, and has no station building, but only a small waiting room on the platform. The station is managed from Fukaura Station.
