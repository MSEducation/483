[[Zhaoqing Railway Station]]

CATEGORIES: China–Hong Kong border crossings, Railway stations in Guangdong

Zhaoqing railway station ([tpl]zh|t=肇慶站|s=肇庆站[/tpl]) serves the city of Zhaoqing, Guangdong province, China. Train services include those departing to Guangzhou, Kunming, Nanning and Hainan on mainland China, with once-daily Guangdong Through Train service to Hung Hom Station in Kowloon,  Hong Kong.
