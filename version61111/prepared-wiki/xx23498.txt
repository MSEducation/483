[[Giustina Pecori-Suárez]]

CATEGORIES: House of Bonaparte, 1811 births, 1903 deaths

Giustina Pecori-Suárez (November 27, 1811 in Florence Italy - January 30, 1903 in Florence), was the third wife of Jérôme Bonaparte, youngest brother of Emperor Napoleon I. She married on February 19, 1853 in Paris, France.
