[[Bruno da Silva Barbosa]]

CATEGORIES: 1988 births, Living people, People from Sorocaba, Brazilian footballers, Esporte Clube Vitória players, São Paulo FC players, Clube Atlético Sorocaba players, Gabala FC players, Expatriate footballers in Azerbaijan

Bruno da Silva Barbosa or simply Bruno (born 15 February 1988 in Sorocaba), is a Brazilian footballer who plays as a defensive midfielder for Marcílio Dias.
