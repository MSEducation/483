[[Divostin]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Šumadija District

Divostin ([tpl]Lang-sr-Cyrl|Дивостин[/tpl]) is a village in Stanovo in Šumadija district. According to the 2002 census there were 399 inhabitants (1991: 423).
Ceramics of Černa Voda III-culture dating to the Early and Middle Bronze Age have been found in Divostin.http://www.rastko.rs/arheologija/srejovic/dsrejovic-eneolit.html
