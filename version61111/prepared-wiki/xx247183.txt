[[Raggeds Wilderness]]

CATEGORIES: Wilderness Areas of Colorado, Protected areas established in 1980, Protected areas of Gunnison County, Colorado, White River National Forest, Gunnison National Forest

The Raggeds Wilderness is a U.S. Wilderness Area located northwest of Crested Butte, Colorado.
