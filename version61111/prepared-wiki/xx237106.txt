[[Ponganadu]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Thiruvananthapuram district

Ponganadu is a small town near Kilimanoor, Thiruvananthapuram district, Kerala, India. Kallambalam, Kilimanoor, Attingal, Varkala are the towns nearby to Ponganadu. Ponganadu is reachable from Varkala, Akathumuri, Kadakavur and Chirayinkil railway stations. It is the location of a government High School and government Ayurveda and Homeopathy hospitals are situated here.

==Location==

Ponganadu is situated in between National Highway 47 (India) and State Highway 1 (Kerala). It is near the Municipal towns of Varkala (17 KM)and Attingal (18 km). Nearest airport is Trivandrum (Thiruvananthapuram) International Airport. Nearest Railway station is Varkala Railway Station (Varkala Sivagiri Railway Station). It is around 42 km north of Thiruvananthapuram City and 37 km south of Kollam City.
