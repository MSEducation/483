[[2804 Yrjö]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Asteroids named for people, Astronomical objects discovered in 1941

2804 Yrjö is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1913.5995962 days (5.24 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=2804 | title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-05-23 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on April 19, 1941.
