[[PS Iverna (1895)]]

CATEGORIES: 1895 ships, Passenger ships of the United Kingdom, Steamships, Clyde-built ships, Ships of the London and North Western Railway, Ships of the Lancashire and Yorkshire Railway

PS Iverna was a paddle steamer passenger vessel operated by the Drogheda Steam Packet Company from 1895 to 1902 and the  Lancashire and Yorkshire Railway from 1902 to 1912.Railway and Other Steamers, Duckworth. 1962

==History==

She was built by A. & J. Inglis of Glasgow for the Drogheda Steam Packet Company and transferred to the Lancashire and Yorkshire Railway in 1902 when they took over the business of the Drogheda company.
In 1912 she was sold and scrapped by T.W. Ward.
