[[Ministry of Civil Affairs of the People's Republic of China]]

CATEGORIES: Government ministries of the People's Republic of China, Internal affairs ministries, Ministries established in 1978

The Ministry of Civil Affairs (MCA) of the Government of the People's Republic of China is a ministry under the jurisdiction of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, responsible for social and administrative affairs. It was founded in May 1978, and the current Minister is Li Xueju. Its precedent was the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the People's Republic of China.
