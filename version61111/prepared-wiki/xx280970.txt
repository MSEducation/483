[[Salmanlu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Salmanlu ([tpl]lang-fa|سلمانلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Salmānlū; also known as Salmālu, Salman, and Salmānū)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3082080|Salmanlu[/tpl] is a village in Mojezat Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 105, in 26 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
