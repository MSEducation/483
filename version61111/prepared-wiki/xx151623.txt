[[Chase Holbrook]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, American football quarterbacks, Southeastern Louisiana Lions football players, New Mexico State Aggies football players, McMurry War Hawks football coaches

Chase Andrew Holbrook was a starting quarterback for New Mexico State University from 2006 to 2008. He is currently the Offensive Coordinator for the McMurry University War Hawks football team. Holbrook played his freshman collegiate year at Southeastern Louisiana as a backup quarterback in 2004.  Holbrook attended L. D. Bell High School in Hurst, Texas.
During his collegiate career Holbrook played a total of 44 games, completing 1,093 of 1,575 attempts for a total of 11,921 yards with 85 touchdowns and 41 interceptions.  In his first year at NMSU he threw for 4,619 yards and had 4,541 yards of total offense, setting an NCAA single-season record.  That same season he scored 34 touchdowns with only 9 interceptions, breaking many of the school's single-season records in the process.
