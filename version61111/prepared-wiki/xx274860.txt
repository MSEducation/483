[[Do Bhai]]

CATEGORIES: 1947 films, Hindi-language films, Indian films

Do Bhai  is a 1947 Indian Bollywood film directed by Munshi Dil. Its starred Ulhas, Noor Jehan, Kamini Kaushal, Dipak Mukherjee, Ramayan Tiwari, Rajan Haksar and Paro in lead roles. It was the second highest grossing Indian film of 1947.[tpl]cite web|title=Top Earners 1947|url=http://www.boxofficeindia.com/showProd.php?itemCat=152&catName=MTk0Nw==|publisher=Box Office India|accessdate=26 September 2011[/tpl]
The features the song, "Mera Sundar Sapna Beet Gaya" and "Yaad Karoge", sung by Geeta Dutt, with music composed by Sachin Dev Burman, and lyrics by Raja Mehdi Ali Khan.
