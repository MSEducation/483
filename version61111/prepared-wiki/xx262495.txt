[[Jamie Douglas (footballer)]]

Jamie Douglas (born 4 July 1992) is a Northern Irish professional footballer who plays for Dungannon Swifts, as a striker.

==Career==

After signing his first professional contract at the end of the 2009-10 season,Preston North End F.C. profile Douglas made his debut for Preston North End on 1 January 2011, in a 1-2 loss against Derby County.[tpl]cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/eng_div_1/9322942.stm|title=Preston 1 - 2 Derby|accessdate=2 January 2011|date=1 January 2011|publisher=BBC Sport[/tpl] In May 2012, Douglas was released from the club after being told his contract would not be renewed.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.pnefc.net/page/NewsDetail/0,,10362~2766952,00.html|title=Summer Change Programme Underway|publisher=Preston North End F.C.|date=10 May 2012|accessdate=10 May 2012[/tpl] Later that month, he signed with Dungannon Swifts.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.tyronetimes.co.uk/sport/other-local-sport/gawley-and-douglas-for-stangmore-1-3867580|title=Gawley and Douglas for Stangmore|date=23 May 2012|publisher=Tyrone Times[/tpl]
