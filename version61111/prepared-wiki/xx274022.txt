[[Australia at the 1988 Winter Paralympics]]

CATEGORIES: Nations at the 1988 Winter Paralympics, Australia at the Paralympics, 1988 in Australian sport

Australia competed at the 1988 Winter Paralympics. Australia was represented by five male alpine skiers. They did not win a single medal.

==Administration==

Team officials were: 

Che de Mission - Ron Finneran
