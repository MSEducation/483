[[Sin-Eribam]]

CATEGORIES: Sumerian rulers, Amorite kings, 18th-century BC people

Sin-Eribam ruled the ancient Near East Amorite city-state
of Larsa for only two years, from ca. 1778 BC to 1776 BC.
