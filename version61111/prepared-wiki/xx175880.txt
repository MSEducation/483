[[WWOG]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Tennessee, Radio stations established in 1987

WWOG is a Christian radio station licensed to Cookeville, Tennessee, broadcasting on 90.9 MHz FM.  The station is owned by Somerset Educational Broadcasting Foundation.WWOG fcc.gov. Accessed August 9, 2012
WWOG's programming includes Christian talk and teaching shows such as Back to the Bible, Focus on the Family, Love Worth Finding with Adrian Rogers, In Touch with Charles Stanley, Tony Evans, and Unshackled!.  WWOG also airs a variety of Christian music.King of Kings Radio Programming www.kingofkingsradio.com. Accessed August 21, 2012
