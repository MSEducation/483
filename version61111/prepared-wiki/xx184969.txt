[[Friesenberg]]

CATEGORIES: District 3 of Zürich, Former municipalities of the canton of Zürich

Friesenberg is a quarter in the district 3 of Zurich.
It was formerly a part of Wiedikon municipality, which was incorporated into Zurich in 1893.
The quarter has a population of 10,360 distributed on an area of 5.15 km².

==Transportation==

The Friesenberg quarter is served by a number of railway stations on line S10 of the Zurich S-Bahn. These include Zürich Friesenberg, Zurich Schweighof and Zurich Triemli stations.
