[[Românași]]

CATEGORIES: Communes in Sălaj County

Românaşi ([tpl]lang-hu|Alsóegregy[/tpl]) is a commune located in Sălaj County, Romania. It is composed of six villages: Chichişa (Alsónyárló), Ciumărna (Csömörlő), Păuşa (Egregypósa), Poarta Sălajului (Vaskapu), Românaşi and Romita (Romlott).
