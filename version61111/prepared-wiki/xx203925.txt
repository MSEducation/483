[[Princess Aisha bint Al Faisal]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1997 births, House of Hashim, Twin people from Jordan, Jordanian princesses, People educated at Amman Baccalaureate School, People from Amman

 
 

Princess Aisha bint Al Faisal of Jordan (born  27 March 1997, Amman) is the daughter Prince Faisal bin Al Hussein and Princess Alia Tabba, and a niece of King Abdullah II of Jordan. She is the younger sister of Princess Ayah and Prince Omar, and also twin to Princess Sara. She attends Amman Baccalaureate School, a private school in Amman, Jordan.[tpl]Citation needed|date=February 2011[/tpl]
