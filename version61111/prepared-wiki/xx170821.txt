[[Mohammad-Reza Hedayati]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Iranian actors

Mohammad-Reza Hedayati (in Persian : محمد رضا هدایتی) is an Iranian actor .
He is known for his comedy roles in TV series directed by Mehran Modiri.
