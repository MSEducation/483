[[Steve Kuzmicich]]

CATEGORIES: Government Statisticians of New Zealand, 1931 births, Living people, New Zealand mathematicians, New Zealand statisticians

Steve Kuzmicich (born 2 November 1931) is a statistician from New Zealand and was Government Statistician of New Zealand from 1984 to 1992.New Zealand Statistical Association Newsletter 62, September 2005.
