[[Chengqu, Changzhi]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Shanxi, Changzhi

Chengqu or Cheng District ([tpl]zh|s=城区 |t=城區 |p=Chéngqū |l=Urban District[/tpl])is a district of Changzhi, Shanxi, China. It has an area of [tpl]convert|55.6|km2|abbr=on[/tpl] and a population of 416,000.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.chinaczcq.gov.cn/more.aspx?pid=16&&id=24|title=Profile of Chengqu, Changzhi|publisher=Official website of Chengqu Government|language=Chinese|date=August 29, 2008|accessdate=2011-04-17[/tpl]
