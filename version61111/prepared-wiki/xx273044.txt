[[Never Ending Tour 2004]]

CATEGORIES: Bob Dylan concert tours, 2004 concert tours

The Never Ending Tour is the popular name for Bob Dylan's endless touring schedule since June 7, 1988.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/music/2011/jun/12/bob-dylan-never-ending-tour |title=Bob Dylan begins his 'never-ending' tour |publisher=The Guardian |date=2011-06-12 |accessdate=2011-11-26[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.rollingstone.com/music/photos/the-evolution-of-bob-dylan-20110509/the-never-ending-tour-begins-1988-1996-0242530 |title=The Evolution of Bob Dylan |publisher=Rolling Stone Magazine |date= |accessdate=2011-11-26[/tpl]

==Tour==

The 2004 tour started with a spring tour of the United States.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bjorner.com/DSN25720%20-%202004%20US%20Spring%20Tour.htm |title=2004 US Spring Tour |publisher=Bjorner |date= |accessdate=2011-12-14[/tpl] Dylan performed several residencies during the tour. Including
Dylan’s European summer tour started on June 18 with a concert in Cardiff, Wales.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bobdylan.com/tour/2004-06-18-cardiff-international-centre-arena |title=Concert:June 18, 2004 - Cardiff, Wales, Cardiff International Centre Arena |publisher=Bob Dylan |date= |accessdate=2011-12-14[/tpl] Two days later Dylan performed in Finsbury Park, London, England as part of the Fleadh Festival.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.virtualfestivals.com/latest/news/970 |title=Dylan and Counting Crows for Fleadh |publisher=Virtual Festivals |date= |accessdate=2011-12-14[/tpl] Dylan only performed two dates in England, the second in Newcastle was performed two days later. He then went on to perform two shows in Glasgow, Scotland. One at the SE&CC and the second at Barrowland Ballroom.[tpl]cite web|url=http://jokerman.org.uk/lb/files/LBF-03677-text-barrowland-text.txt |title=Bob Dylan Glasgow Barrowland 24th June 2004 |publisher=Jokerman |date= |accessdate=2011-12-14[/tpl] This show was added due to the ticket demands at the previous night’s concert at the SE&CC. The tour finished 31 days after it started on July 18 in Caminha, Portugal.
Dylan went on to perform a Baseball Park tour of the Eastern United States. The concerts were a double-bill with Willie Nelson.[tpl]cite web|url=http://stillisstillmoving.com/willienelson/willie-nelson-and-bob-dylan-2004-minor-league-baseball-park-tour/ |title=Willie Nelson and Bob Dylan, 2004 Minor League Baseball Park Tour |publisher=Still Is Still Moving |date= |accessdate=2011-12-14[/tpl] Then on October 13 Dylan commenced the final leg of the tour performing in College sports venues across the United States. The tour started on October 13 in San Francisco, California and ended in Allston, Massachusetts, on November 21.
