[[The Peak Hill Express]]

CATEGORIES: Defunct newspapers of Australia, Newspapers published in Australia, Newspapers published in New South Wales, Newspapers published in Sydney

The Peak Hill Express was a weekly English language newspaper published in Peak Hill, New South Wales, Australia.[tpl]cite web|title=The Peak Hill Express (NSW : 1902 - 1952)|url=http://trove.nla.gov.au/ndp/del/title/514|work=Trove|publisher=National Library of Australia|accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl] 

==History==

The newspaper began publication on 18 July 1902 and ceased publication on 29 June 1956.[tpl]cite web|title=The Peak Hill Express microform|url=http://library.sl.nsw.gov.au/record=b1152887~S2|work=Catalogue|publisher=State Library of NSW|accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl]  Publishers of the newspaper included  A. Miles, Robert James Baker, John McIntyre, Rural Newspapers Ltd. and McLong Peak Hill Printing Co. Ltd. 

==Digitisation==

The paper has been digitised as part of the Australian Newspapers Digitisation Program (ANDP) project of the National Library of Australia.[tpl]cite web|title=Newspaper and magazine titles|url=http://trove.nla.gov.au/ndp/del/titles|work=Trove|publisher=National Library of Australia|accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl] [tpl]cite web|title=Newspaper Digitisation Program|url=http://www.nla.gov.au/content/newspaper-digitisation-program|publisher=National Library of Australia|accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl]
