[[Valea Brusturetului River]]

CATEGORIES: Rivers of Romania, Rivers of the Argeş subbasin, Rivers of Argeș County

The Valea Brusturetului River or Brusturetul River is a headwater of the Dâmbovicioara River in Romania. The upper reach of the river is sometimes known as the Valea Seacă a Pietrelor River
