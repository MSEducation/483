[[Tibbi]]

CATEGORIES: Cities and towns in Hanumangarh district, Hanumangarh district

Tibbi is one of the seven tehsil headquarters of Hanumangarh district of Rajasthan state in India. It is at the junction of Haryana, Punjab, and Rajasthan states.
It is also called as "Rice Belt of Rajasthan".[tpl]fact|date=September 2013[/tpl] Indira Gandhi Canal enters Rajasthan, from Punjab, at Masitawali Head village of Tibbi.
The first elected Sarpanch of Tibbi village, under the Panchayati Raj System after Independence, was Thakur Kesari Singh Gahlot. His son, Rajendra Singh Gahlot, was also elected to the same post in the succeeding years.
