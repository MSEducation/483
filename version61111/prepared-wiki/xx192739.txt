[[In Mourning (band)]]

CATEGORIES: Musical groups established in 2000, Swedish progressive metal musical groups, Swedish melodic death metal musical groups, Musical quintets

In Mourning is a progressive death metal band from Falun, Sweden. They are currently signed to Spinefarm Records. Their latest album The Weight Of Oceans was released on April 18, 2012.
