[[Theropithecus oswaldi]]

CATEGORIES: Cercopithecine monkeys, Pleistocene mammals, Fossil taxa described in 1916

Theropithecus oswaldi is an extinct species of gelada from the early to middle Pleistocene of Kenya, Ethiopia, Tanzania, South Africa, Spain, Morocco and Algeria.[tpl]cite web |url=http://paleodb.org/?a=checkTaxonInfo&taxon_no=133587&is_real_user=1 |title=The Paleobiology Database |accessdate=11 August 2013[/tpl]
