[[Ice Skating (album)]]

CATEGORIES: 2004 singles

Ice Skating is the debut album from Ultrafox, released in 2004 on the Loser Friendly Records label.  It was made in two weeks in Munich and London.[tpl]citation |url=http://profile.myspace.com/index.cfm?fuseaction=user.viewprofile&friendID=43071907[/tpl]
The track "Cloakroom Girl" was released as a double-pack-7" single before the album came out.  It was packaged with "I Get Lifted", "Music Is People Are" and "Horses".[tpl]citation |url=http://www.ultrafox.de/flash/start.html |format=[tpl]dead link|date=June 2010[/tpl][/tpl]  Select "Music" from the menu and cycle once to the left.
