[[Carolans]]

CATEGORIES: Irish liqueurs, South Tipperary, Cream liqueurs, Campari Group

Carolans Irish Cream (the registered trade mark omits the apostrophe) is a liqueur based on mead-wine (a mixture of whiskey and Irish spirits) and cream made in Clonmel, South Tipperary in Ireland.

==Contents==

It has a declared alcohol content of 17% or 14.5% alcohol by volume (ABV). The main components are cream (nearly 40%), mead-wine, and honey. The brand is owned by Gruppo Campari and was previously owned by William Grant and C&C Group.http://www.irishtimes.com/newspaper/breaking/2010/0916/breaking15.html
Commonly used description:
 "Deep milky brown hue. Forward, spirity, whiskey-accented lactic flavors. A rich, milky attack leads to a moderately full-bodied palate with a touch of sweetness."http://wineflair.net/products/liqueurs/liqueur_products/cream_carolans.htm
