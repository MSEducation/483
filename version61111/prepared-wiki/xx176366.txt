[[Bubba Hernandez]]

CATEGORIES: American male singers, Latin music musicians, Living people

Bubba Hernandez and Alex Meixner were nominated in the Best Polka album category in the 50th Annual Grammy Awards for their self-titled debut album, Polka Freak Out. 
He is a former member of the Denton, Texas based band, Brave Combo, but is best known as the bassist for The Wee-Beasties.[tpl]cite web|url=[tpl]Allmusic|class=artist|id=p3767/biography|pure_url=yes[/tpl]|title=Biography: Brave Combo|last=Bush|first=John|publisher=Allmusic|accessdate=16 July 2010[/tpl]
He is currently the front member of Los Super Vatos, also based out of Denton, where he primarily plays bass.
