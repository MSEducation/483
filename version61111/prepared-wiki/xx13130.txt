[[1800]]

CATEGORIES: 1800

Year 1800 (MDCCC) was an exceptional common year starting on Wednesday (link will display the full calendar) of the Gregorian calendar and a leap year starting on Sunday of the Julian calendar. The Gregorian calendar was 11 days ahead of the Julian calendar until Friday, [tpl]OldStyleDate|February 28|1800|February 17, 1800[/tpl], but 12 days ahead from Saturday, [tpl]OldStyleDate|March 1|1800|February 18, 1800[/tpl].
