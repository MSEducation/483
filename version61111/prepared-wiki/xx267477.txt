[[Elachista consortella]]

CATEGORIES: Elachistidae, Animals described in 1851, Moths of Europe

Elachista consortella is a moth of the Elachistidae family. It is found in most of Europe, except most of the Balkan Peninsula, Poland, Latvia and Finland.
The wingspan is [tpl]convert|7|-|8|mm[/tpl]. Adults are on wing from April to September.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.microlepidoptera.nl/soorten/species.php?speciescode=250350&p=1|title=Elachista consortella Stainton, 1851|publisher= Microlepidoptera.nl|accessdate=September 23, 2011[/tpl]
The larvae possibly feed on Cynodon dactylon, although other records state it feeds on Poa annua. They mine the leaves of their host plant.[tpl]cite web|url= http://www.bladmineerders.nl/minersf/lepidopteramin/elachista/consortella/consortella.htm|title=Elachista consortella Stainton, 1851|publisher=Bladmineerders.nl |accessdate=September 23, 2011[/tpl] Larvae can be found from April to August.
