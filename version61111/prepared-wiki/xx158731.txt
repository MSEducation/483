[[Keth Memorial Golf Course]]

CATEGORIES: Golf clubs and courses in Missouri

Keth Memorial Golf Course within Pertle Springs Park on the campus of the University of Central Missouri is an 18-hole grass greens course complete with a fully equipped pro shop.  Keth Memorial is under a $1.2 million renovation.  The renovation includes a new driving range, new practice putting and chipping greens, and a new pro shop.  Keth Memorial also is home for UCM golf and cross country competitions.
