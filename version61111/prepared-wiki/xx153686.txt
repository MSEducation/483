[[Opelousas Catholic School]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic secondary schools in Louisiana, Schools in St. Landry Parish, Louisiana, Educational institutions established in 1856, Private middle schools in Louisiana, Private elementary schools in Louisiana

Opelousas Catholic School is a private, Catholic school in Opelousas, Louisiana.  Located in the Roman Catholic Diocese of Lafayette, the school offers education from pre-kindergarten through 12th grade.
