[[Vladan Vasilijević]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Democratic Party (Serbia) politicians, Serbian Liberal Party politicians

Vladan Vasilijević was one of the most prominent Yugoslav specialists in criminal law in the 1980s.  Throughout the 1990s he was engaged as a human rights lawyer promoting a democratic civil society and the rule of law in Serbia.  In December 1989 he was one of the members of the Founding Committee of the Democratic Party. The Democratic Party was the first non-communist opposition party in Serbia. Later he left the Democratic Party to join the Serbian Liberal Party led by Kosta Čavoški and Nikola Milošević.
