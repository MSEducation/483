[[Fred Wallner]]

CATEGORIES: 1928 births, 1999 deaths, Players of American football from Massachusetts, American football offensive guards, Notre Dame Fighting Irish football players, Chicago Cardinals players, Houston Oilers (AFL) players, Eastern Conference Pro Bowl players, People from Greenfield, Massachusetts

Frederick William "Fred" Wallner (born April 28, 1928 in Greenfield, Massachusetts[tpl]spaced ndash[/tpl]November 4, 1999) was an American football guard who played five seasons in the National Football League and one season (1960) with the Houston Oilers of the American Football League.  He was a member of the Oilers' first AFL Championship team.
