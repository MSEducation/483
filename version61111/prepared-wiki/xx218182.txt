[[Ji Hun-Min]]

CATEGORIES: South Korean weightlifters, 1984 births, Living people, Weightlifters at the 2008 Summer Olympics, Weightlifters at the 2012 Summer Olympics, Olympic weightlifters of South Korea, Weightlifters at the 2006 Asian Games, Weightlifters at the 2010 Asian Games

Ji Hun-Min (born March 26, 1984) is a South Korean weightlifter.  He competed in the Men's Featherweight division at the 2008 Beijing Olympic Games.  After placing second after the snatch lift, he failed to successfully lift in the clean and jerk, and hence was disqualified.Olympic results
