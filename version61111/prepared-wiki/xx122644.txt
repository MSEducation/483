[[Ceyla Kirazlı]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Miss Universe 2006 contestants, 1986 births, Miss Turkey winners

Ceyla Kirazlı (born c. 1986) is Miss Turkey Universe 2006. Her interests include traditional dances, traveling, backgammon, swimming, music, and volleyball. Growing up in İzmir, she stands at 5' 10" and admires Atatürk, the founder of the modern Turkish Republic. Her ambition is to be a world-famous model.
