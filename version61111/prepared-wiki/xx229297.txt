[[Jasper Mills, Ohio]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Fayette County, Ohio, Unincorporated communities in Ohio

Jasper Mills is an unincorporated community in Jasper Township, Fayette County, Ohio, United States. It is located along U.S. Route 22, three miles west of Washington Court House.[tpl]cite book |last=Allen |first=Frank M. |title=History of Fayette County, Ohio: Her People, Industries and Institutions |publisher=B.F.
