[[Pterygopappus]]

CATEGORIES: Asteraceae genera, Flora of Australia, Flora of Tasmania, Endemic flora of Tasmania, Monotypic plant genera

Pterygopappus is a genus of flowering plants in the daisy family containing the single species Pterygopappus lawrencii (Sage Cushion Plant) which is endemic to alpine Tasmania where it forms thick, light blue/green mats with densely packed leaves. It is most common in the mountains of the islands northeast. It is a slow grower and prefers cool, moist environments.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.utas.edu.au/dicotkey/dicotkey/AST/gnaph/gPterygopappus.htm|title=Key to Tasmanian Dicots|author=University of Tasmania[/tpl]
