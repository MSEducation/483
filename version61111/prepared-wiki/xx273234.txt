[[Nassir Al-Ghanim]]

Nassir Ghanem AlEnezi (born 4 April 1961) is a Kuwaiti football midfielder who played for Kuwait in the 1982 FIFA World Cup.1982 FIFA World Cup Spain He also played for Kazma Sporting Club.
