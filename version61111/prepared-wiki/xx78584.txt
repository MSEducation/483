[[Gokase, Miyazaki]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Miyazaki Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Gokase|五ヶ瀬町|Gokase-chō[/tpl] is a town located in Nishiusuki District, Miyazaki Prefecture, Japan.
[tpl]As of|2003[/tpl], the town had an estimated population of 4,965 and density of 28.90 persons per km². The total area is 171.77 km². Much of the population work as farmers and fisherman, producing sansai and seema.[tpl]Fact|date=December 2007[/tpl]
