[[1924 Uruguayan Primera División of FUF]]

CATEGORIES: 1924 in association football, 1924 in Uruguay

The FUF Tournament 1924 was the second and last tournament organized by the Uruguayan Football Federation.

==Overview==

The tournament consisted of a two-wheel championship of all against all. It involved 17 teams, and the champion was Peñarol.
This was the last tournament to be played in this federation, having been suspended which would take place the following year. After the dissolution of the Federation, some of the clubs in the FUF returned to the Uruguayan Football Association and many others disappeared.
