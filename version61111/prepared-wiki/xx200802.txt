Loukima Tamoukini (born 6 September 1975 in Kinshasa) is a Congolese footballer who plays for Redondense Futebol Clube in Portugal, as a defensive midfielder.
He played professionally in Portugal for nearly 15 years, competing in eleven second division seasons, mainly with Penafiel.
