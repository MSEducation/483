[[NHL Blades of Steel '99]]

CATEGORIES: 1999 video games, Konami games, Nintendo 64 games, National Hockey League video games

NHL Blades of Steel '99 and NHL Pro 99 in Europe is an ice hockey game for the Nintendo 64, it was released in 1999. The game has Controller Pak and Rumble Pak support.

==Features==

==Reception==

IGN gave NHL Blades of Steel '99 a mediocre 5.1 out of 10 overall stating "Konami's NHL Blades of Steel '99 title was plagued by terrible gameplay and horrible control and few nice touches didn't compensate for a disappointing and generally dull game".IGN Reviews NHL Blades of Steel '99 (N64)
