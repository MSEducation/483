[[Bulamaji, Khodabandeh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Bulamaji ([tpl]lang-fa|بولاماجي[/tpl], also Romanized as Būlāmājī, Bulamāji, and Boolamaji; also known as Balā Māchī, Bolāmājī, and Bula-Madzhid)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3056561|Bulamaji[/tpl] is a village in Sojas Rud Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 755, in 197 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
