[[Roots & Herbs]]

CATEGORIES: 1970 albums, Art Blakey albums, Blue Note Records albums, Albums produced by Alfred Lion

Roots & Herbs is a jazz album by Art Blakey & the Jazz Messengers, recorded in 1961 but not released on the Blue Note label until 1970. The CD reissue features three alternate takes, two of which originally released in 1979 on Pisces.
