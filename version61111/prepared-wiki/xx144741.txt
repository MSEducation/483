[[Majur, Sisak-Moslavina County]]

CATEGORIES: Municipalities of Croatia

Majur is a municipality in Croatia in the Sisak-Moslavina County. It has a population of 1,185, 70.0% of which are Croats (2011 census).[tpl]Croatian Census 2011|E|03[/tpl]
By census 1991 Serbs were majority 1,381 (54.05%), then Croats 1,036 (40.54%), Yugoslavs 72 (2.81%) and others 66 (2.58%).[tpl]fact|date=July 2013[/tpl]
