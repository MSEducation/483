[[Waterfall (The Stone Roses song)]]

CATEGORIES: 1991 singles, The Stone Roses songs, 1989 songs, Song recordings produced by John Leckie

"Waterfall" is the ninth single from The Stone Roses. It was the fourth single taken from their debut album The Stone Roses. It was released on 30 December 1991 and reached number 27 in the UK.[tpl]cite web|title= Stone Roses |url= http://www.officialcharts.com/artist/_/stone%20roses/ |publisher= Official Charts Company |accessdate= 1 March 2013[/tpl]
