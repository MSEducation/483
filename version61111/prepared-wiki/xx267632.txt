[[Aarhus River]]

CATEGORIES: Aarhus, Aarhus Municipality, Skanderborg Municipality, Rivers of Denmark, Streams of Denmark

The Aarhus River is a 40 km long river or stream, that drains a basin of 324 km² in eastern part of Søhøjlandet, Denmark. The river originates 54 m above sea level in the swampy bogland of Astrup Mose close to Stilling-Solbjerg Lake, southwest of Aarhus[tpl]cite web|last=historien.dk|first=Danmarks|title=Aarhus|url=http://danmarkshistorien.dk/leksikon-og-kilder/vis/materiale/aarhus-1/?tx_historyview_pi1lang=1&cHash=42dfd94aaa459af3505372f8b2af029a|publisher=Aarhus University|accessdate=2 September 2012[/tpl] (56°6' northern latitude, 10°9' eastern longitude) and exits into Aarhus Harbour at 56°9' northern latitude, 10°13' eastern longitude.
