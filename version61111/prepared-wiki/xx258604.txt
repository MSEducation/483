[[Troelstralaan (Rotterdam Metro)]]

CATEGORIES: Rotterdam Metro

Troelstralaan is a metro station in Schiedam, situated on Rotterdam Metro line C. The station was opened on November 4, 2002, as part of the last major expansion of the Rotterdam metro. The main entrance, at the east end of the subway station, is located on the street named after P.J. Troelstralaan, and the island platform is accessible by entrances on both ends.
