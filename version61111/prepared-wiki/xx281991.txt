==Initial clashes==

A procession of Muslims after offering Eid prayers raised Anti-India slogans with Pakistani Flags which led to clashes[tpl]cite news|title=Kishtwar violence: Yasin Malik to lead peace march in Srinagar|url=http://www.aninews.in/videogallery2/14314-kishtwar-violence-yasin-malik-to-lead-peace-march-in-srinagar.html|accessdate=20 August 2013|date=12 August 2013|agency=ANI News[/tpl]http://www.indianexpress.com/news/kishtwar-a-tinderbox-region-under-curfew-after-clashes/1153860/2 Kishtwar a tinderbox, region under curfew after clashes which later spread to other parts of the district. People involved in the clashes initially started with stone pelting and later shops and houses were gutted.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.hindustantimes.com/India-news/NorthIndia/Kishtwar-clashes-govt-orders-inquiry-transfers-DC-SP/Article1-1106063.aspx |title=Kishtwar clashes: govt orders inquiry; transfers DC, SP |publisher=Hindustan Times |date=2013-07-19 |accessdate=2013-08-13[/tpl] It is reported that more than 80 shops, a petrol pump, an oil tanker, some vehicles of the state police and fire department were torched in addition to government buildings.http://www.firstpost.com/fwire/indefinite-curfew-continues-for-second-day-in-kishtwar-1023843.html Home minister of the state of Jammu and Kashmir Sajad Ahmed Kitchloo was suspected to be involved in the beginning of the clashes.http://www.firstpost.com/politics/kishtwar-riots-jk-minister-kitchloo-resigns-maintains-he-is-innocent-1028219.html

===Administrative Failure===

Administrative failure is being cited as the reason for Kishtwar clashes. Central Security establishment raised questions like:http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/india/Administrative-failure-behind-Kishtwar-riots-Centre/articleshow/21791817.cms Administrative failure behind Kishtwar riots: Centre
The home ministry, meanwhile, has issued an advisory asking J&K government to prevent violence from spreading further by deploying adequate forces in all parts of the state.
