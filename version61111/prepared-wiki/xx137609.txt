[[2006 FIA GT Adria 500km]]

CATEGORIES: 2006 in FIA GT, 2006 in Italian motorsport

The 2006 FIA GT Adria Supercar 500 was the ninth race for the 2006 FIA GT Championship season.  It took place on October 15, 2006.
