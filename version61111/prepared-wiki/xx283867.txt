[[The Malefice]]

CATEGORIES: Heavy metal albums by Chilean artists, 2013 albums

The Malefice is the debut full-length studio album by Chilean extreme metal band, Pentagram Chile. It is their first full-length album released in over 28 years after their formation. The album was released on September 6, 2013. 
The limited edition digipak includes a bonus album with re-recorded demos.
