==History==

The company was formed as Carter Medicine Company  which was incorporated in 1880 by John Samuel Carter of Erie, Pennsylvania. John Carter died in 1884 and his son, Samuel Carter took over. John Higgins Wallace, Jr., a research chemist from Princeton, New Jersey was hired and he formulated Arrid deodorant in 1935.[tpl]cite news |author= |coauthors= |title=John Wallace, 82, Chemist and Executive |url=http://www.nytimes.com/1989/03/24/obituaries/john-wallace-82-chemist-and-executive.html |quote= In 1934 he bought a laboratory where he had been a consulting chemist, and it became Wallace Laboratories. That organization merged with Carter Products to become Carter-Wallace Inc., a drug and cosmetics manufacturer based in Manhattan. ... |newspaper=New York Times |date=March 24, 1989 |accessdate=2011-09-27 [/tpl]
