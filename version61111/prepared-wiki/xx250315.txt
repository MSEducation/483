[[1988 Athens Trophy]]

CATEGORIES: 1988 WTA Tour, Athens Trophy, 1988 in Greek sport

The 1988 Athens Trophy was a tennis tournament played on outdoor clay courts in Athens in Greece and was part of Tier V of the 1988 WTA Tour. The tournament ran from August 1 through August 7, 1988.
