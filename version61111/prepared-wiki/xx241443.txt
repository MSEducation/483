[[Lane City, Texas]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Wharton County, Texas, Unincorporated communities in Texas

Lane City is an unincorporated community in Wharton County, Texas, United States. The community is situated at the intersection of Texas State Highway 60 and Farm to Market Road 442.

==Demographics==

According to the Handbook of Texas, the community had an estimated population of 111 in 2000.[tpl]cite web | url = http://www.tshaonline.org/handbook/online/articles/LL/hll15.html | title = Lane City, Texas | work = | publisher = The Handbook of Texas online | date =  | accessdate = 2009-10-18[/tpl]
Although it is unincorporated, Lane City has a post office, with the ZIP code of 77453.Zip Code Lookup

==Education==

Public education in the community of Lane City is provided by the Wharton Independent School District.
