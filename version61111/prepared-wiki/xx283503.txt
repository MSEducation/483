[[The Ultimate Addiction]]

CATEGORIES: TVB television programmes, Upcoming television series, Hong Kong drama television series, Television series set in the 2010s

The Ultimate Addiction (Chinese: 點金勝手) is an upcoming 30-episode Hong Kong television drama that is produced by Chong Wai-kin for TVB. The story centers on a group of individuals working in Hong Kong's financial world and stock market.A costume fitting was held on 21 August 2013 at Tseung Kwan O TVB City Studio Four at 12:30PM.[tpl]cite web|url=http://hyn5-hyn5.blogspot.sg/2013/08/midas-costume-fitting.html|title=《點金勝手》 "Midas" Costume Fitting[/tpl]

==Development and casting==

In late June 2013, TVB announced that the studios were preparing for a new drama that was "tailor-made" for Michael Miu, which Miu later confirmed.[tpl]cite news | work=Apple Daily | url=http://hk.apple.nextmedia.com/entertainment/art/20130629/18316020 | title=My stage: Michael Miu says having fun is the best | date=29 June 2013 | accessdate=11 August 2013 | language=Chinese[/tpl] The following week, Bosco Wong and Sharon Chan were confirmed to star alongside Miu in Midas. In July 2013, Nancy Wu joined the cast, followed by Kate Tsui, Ben Wong, and Elena Kong.[tpl]cite news | work=Kuangaitvb | url=http://e.weibo.com/1893802153/A3nOUexbr?ref=http%3A%2F%2Fhuati.weibo.com%2Fk%2F%25E7%258B%2582%25E5%25B0%258F%25E6%2596%2599%3Ffrom%3D510%26order%3Dtime | title=Kuangaitvb @ Weibo | date=5 August 2013 | accessdate=11 August 2013 | language=Chinese[/tpl]
On 6 August, Miu announced that he has withdrawn himself from the project due to scheduling conflicts.[tpl]cite news | work=IHKTV | url=http://www.ihktv.com/golden-kathrine-financial-sector.html | title=Michael Miu leaves new drama | date=6 August 2013 | accessdate=11 August 2013 | language=Chinese[/tpl] His role would not be replaced, and the story's script would be rewritten to accommodate a younger cast. On 10 August, Fred Cheng was confirmed to have joined the project,[tpl]cite news | work=Ming Pao via Weibo | url=http://e.weibo.com/1893802153/A43LvfC5G?ref=http%3A%2F%2Fe.weibo.com%2Fkuangaitvbcom | title=Kuangaitvb @ Weibo | date=10 August 2013 | accessdate=11 August 2013 | language=Chinese[/tpl] but Cheng left the cast in October 2013 due to his involvement in singing competition, Voice of Stars.[tpl]cite news | work=IHKTV | url=http://www.ihktv.com/voiceofstars-fred-moon-preparations.html | title=Fred Cheng leaves 'Midas' to prepare for 'Voice of Stars' | date=5 October 2013 | accessdate=6 October 2013 | language=Chinese[/tpl]
