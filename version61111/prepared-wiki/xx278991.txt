[[Qarah Aghaj-e Olya, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Qarah Aghaj-e Olya ([tpl]lang-fa|قره اغاج عليا[/tpl], also Romanized as Qarah Āghāj-e ‘Olyā; also known as Qarah Āqāj-e ‘Olyā and Qareh Āqāj-e ‘Olyā)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3795843|Qarah Aghaj-e Olya[/tpl] is a village in Chaypareh-ye Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 327, in 62 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
