[[Someș-Odorhei]]

CATEGORIES: Communes in Sălaj County

Someş-Odorhei ([tpl]lang-hu|Szamosudvarhely[/tpl]) is a commune located in Sălaj County, Romania. It is composed of five villages: Bârsa (Dabjonújfalu), Domnin (Dabjon), Inău (Inó), Someş-Odorhei and Şoimuş (Szilágysolymos).
