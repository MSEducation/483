[[Higashi-Tengachaya Station]]

[tpl]nihongo|Higashi-Tengachaya Station|東天下茶屋駅|Higashi-Tengachaya Eki|extra=station number: HN04[/tpl] is a tramway stop in Abeno-ku, Osaka, Japan on the Hankai Tramway Uemachi Line.

==Layout==

A side platform is located on each track, before passing the tramway crossing (Seimei-dōri).
