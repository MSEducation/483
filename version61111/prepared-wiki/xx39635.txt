[[I Feel It/Thousand]]

CATEGORIES: 1993 singles, Electronic songs, Moby songs, Songs written by Moby

"I Feel It" and "Thousand" are two songs by the American electronica musician Moby, released as a double A-side single from his first album, Moby.
"Thousand" has the Guinness world record for the fastest tempo in beats-per-minute (BPM) of any released single,[tpl]citation needed|date=January 2013[/tpl] although some Extratone songs have surpassed this mark.[tpl]citation needed|date=January 2013[/tpl] It clocks in at approximately 1,000 BPM, hence the title of the recording.[tpl]citation needed|date=January 2013[/tpl] The single peaked at number 38 on the UK Singles Chart.[tpl]citation needed|date=January 2013[/tpl]
