James Andrew Corrine,[tpl]cite web|url=[tpl]Allmusic|class=artist|id=p1038207/biography|pure_url=yes[/tpl]|title=DJ Felli Fel - Biography|last=Birchmeier|first=Jason|year=2008|publisher=allmusic|accessdate=2009-03-14[/tpl] better known by his stage name DJ Felli Fel is a club and radio DJ for L.A.'s Power 106, record producer, and a recording artist recently signed to So So Def/Island Urban Music. He is also a member of The Heavy Hitters DJs.

==Biography==

DJ Felli Fel was born in Rock Hill, South Carolina and grew up in Atlanta, Los Angeles, and Dallas. While a teenager in Dallas, he began spinning turntables for house parties. His radio career began on Dallas radio station K104 and Waco, Texas Spanish-language station KHCK. Later, he joined the Los Angeles hip-hop station Power 106.  At Power 106, he hosts the Felli Fel Show on Saturday evenings.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.power106.com/jocks/felli.aspx|title=Felli Fel|publisher=Power 106|accessdate=2009-03-14[/tpl]
He had a cameo role as himself in the 2003 film Malibu's Most Wanted.[tpl]cite web|url=[tpl]Allmusic|class=artist|id=p532901/biography|pure_url=yes[/tpl]|title=Felli Fel > Biography|last=Thomas|first=Vincent|publisher=allmusic|accessdate=2009-03-14[/tpl] In 2007, he signed with Island Def Jam/So So Def.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.hiphopdx.com/index/news/id.6089/title.dj-felli-fel-signs-with-so-so-def-def-jam|title=DJ Felli Fel Signs With So So Def/Def Jam|last=Paine|first=Jake|date=2007-12-11|work=HipHopDX|accessdate=2009-03-14[/tpl] His debut album for the label is tentatively titled Go DJ!.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.djbooth.net/index/interviews/entry/dj-felli-fel-interview-1219071/|title=DJ Felli Fel Interview|date=December 2007|publisher=DJ Booth|accessdate=2009-03-14[/tpl] Singles for the album included "Finer Things" with Jermaine Dupri, Kanye West, Fabolous, and Ne-Yo; "Get Buck in Here" with Diddy, Akon, Lil Jon, and Ludacris;[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.mtv.com/news/articles/1582587/20080229/jeezy_young.jhtml|title=Young Jeezy Fights To Get On Usher Track; DJ Felli Fel Wrangles Kanye West, Ne-Yo For Next Single: Mixtape Monday|last=Reid|first=Shaheem|coauthors=Rodriguez, Jayson|date=2008-03-03|publisher=MTV News|accessdate=2009-03-14[/tpl] and "Feel It" with Sean Paul, Pitbull, Flo Rida, and T-Pain. DJ Felli Fel was also featured on the soundtrack of the game Midnight Club: Los Angeles, with his song "Get Buck in Here". Recently he released the single "Boomerang" featuring Pitbull, Akon, and Jermaine Dupri. The track has been remixed by DJ Vice in 2011.
On April 22, he released his brand new single to iTunes titled "Reason To Hate" which features singer Ne-Yo and rappers Tyga and Wiz Khalifa. It was co-produced by Ned Cameron.
