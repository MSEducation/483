[[They Used Dark Forces]]

CATEGORIES: 1964 novels, Novels by Dennis Wheatley, World War II novels, Hutchinson (publisher) books

They Used Dark Forces is a World War II fictional novel by Dennis Wheatley. The hero, Gregory Sallust, finds himself in Nazi Germany and associated with a black magician who is advising Adolf Hitler.  They supposedly persuade him to commit suicide rather than to fight to the last, in the belief that he will be reincarnated among a population of Aryan warriors living on Mars.
