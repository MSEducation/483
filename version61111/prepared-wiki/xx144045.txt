[[Shirakiyama Station]]

CATEGORIES: Geibi Line, Stations of West Japan Railway Company in Hiroshima city

[tpl]nihongo|Shirakiyama Station|白木山駅|Shirakiyama-eki[/tpl] is a JR West Geibi Line station located in Mita, Shiraki-chō, Asakita-ku, Hiroshima, Hiroshima Prefecture, Japan. 

==History==

==Station building and platforms==

Shirakiyama Station features one station-side platform capable of handling one line. The station is unmanned, and is used as a convenient meeting place for the surrounding area.
