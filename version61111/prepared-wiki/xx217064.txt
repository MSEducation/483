[[Tingatinga (Tanzanian ward)]]

CATEGORIES: Monduli District, Wards of Tanzania

Tingatinga is an administrative ward in the Monduli district of the Arusha Region of Tanzania. According to the 2002 census, the ward has a total population of 4,811.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.tanzania.go.tz/census/census/districts/monduli.htm|title=2002 Population and Housing Census General Report|publisher=Government of Tanzania|language=en|accessdate=2008-08-19[/tpl]
