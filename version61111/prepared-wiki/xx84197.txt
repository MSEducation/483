[[Shimotakai District, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Districts in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Shimotakai|下高井郡|Shimotakai-gun[/tpl] is a district located in Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the district has an estimated population of 25,179 and a density of 59.50 persons per km². The total area is 423.19 km².
