[[Abd al-Haqq al-Badisi]]

CATEGORIES: 1322 deaths, Moroccan writers, Year of birth missing

Abd al-Haqq ibn Ismail al-Badisi (died after 1322) was a Moroccan biographer, author of Al-Maqsad al-sharif wa-al-manza al-latif fi tarif bi sulaha al-rifed. Said Arab (Rabat, 1982), The third section of this work is translated by G.S.
