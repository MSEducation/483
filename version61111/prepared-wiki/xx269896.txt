[[Estadio Parque Maracaná]]

CATEGORIES: Football venues in Uruguay, Sports venues completed in 2008

The Estadio Parque Maracaná is a football stadium in Casavalle, Montevideo, Uruguay. It has a capacity of 8,000 and is the home stadium of Club Sportivo Cerrito.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.soccerway.com/venues/uruguay/parque-maracana/|title=Parque Maracaná|publisher=Soccerway.com|accessdate=24 September 2011[/tpl] The stadium opened on Saturday 20 September 2008, with a match between Cerrito and Huracán Buceo in the Segunda División which Cerrito won 3 -0.
