[[Gene Kerrigan]]

CATEGORIES: Year of birth missing (living people), Living people, Irish columnists, Irish journalists, Irish novelists, Magill people, People from Dublin (city), Sunday Independent (Ireland) people

Gene Kerrigan is an Irish journalist and novelist who grew up in Cabra in Dublin. His works include political commentary on Ireland since the 1970s in such publications as Magill magazine and the Sunday Independent newspaper. He has also written about Ireland for International Socialism magazine.Ireland: A Special Survey International Socialism, October 1976 (with Des Derwin, John Goodwillie, & Brian Trench). He was chosen as World Journalist of the Year in 1985 and 1990, and has written books, including fiction and non-fiction.
