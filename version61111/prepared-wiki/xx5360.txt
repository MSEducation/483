==Portraits of Lovelock==

In March 2012 the National Portrait Gallery[tpl]NPG name | id=06519 |name=James Lovelock [/tpl] unveiled a new portrait of Lovelock by British artist Michael Gaskell (2011). The collection also has two photographic portraits by Nick Sinclair (1993) and Paul Tozer (1994). The archive of the Royal Society of Arts has a 2009 image taken by Anne-Katrin Purkiss.[tpl]cite web|author=By The RSA The RSA+ Add Contact |url=http://www.flickr.com/photos/the_rsa/3344496740/ |title=James Lovelock by Anne Purkiss | Flickr - Photo Sharing! |publisher=Flickr |date=2009-03-10 |accessdate=2012-09-01[/tpl] Lovelock agreed to sit for sculptor Jon Edgar in Devon during 2007, as part of The Environment Triptych (2008)[tpl]cite book|last=authors|first=various|title=Responses – Carvings and Claywork – Jon Edgar Sculpture 2003-2008|year=2008|publisher=Hesworth Press|location=UK|isbn=978-0-9558675-0-7[/tpl] along with heads of Mary Midgley and Richard Mabey.
