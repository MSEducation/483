[[Roman Catholic Diocese of Bonfim]]

CATEGORIES: Roman Catholic dioceses in Brazil, Religious organizations established in 1933, Roman Catholic Ecclesiastical Province of Feira de Santana, Roman Catholic dioceses and prelatures established in the 20th century, 1933 establishments in Brazil

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Bonfim ([tpl]lang-la|Dioecesis Bonfimensis[/tpl]) is a diocese located in the city of Senhor do Bonfim, in the Ecclesiastical province of Feira de Santana in Brazil.
