[[KBNR]]

CATEGORIES: Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Texas, Spanish-language radio stations in Texas

KBNR is a Spanish language Christian radio station licensed to Brownsville, Texas, broadcasting on 88.3 MHz FM. KBNR serves the Brownsville, Texas - Matamoros, Tamaulipas area, and is owned by World Radio Network, Inc.KBNR fcc.gov. Accessed August 22, 2012
