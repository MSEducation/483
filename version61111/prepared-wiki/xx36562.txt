[[Marrakesh Menara Airport]]

CATEGORIES: Airports in Morocco, Buildings and structures in Marrakesh, Airfields of the United States Army Air Forces Air Transport Command in North Africa

Marrakesh Menara Airport ([tpl]lang-fr|Aéroport Marrakech Ménara[/tpl], [tpl]lang-ar|مطار مراكش المنارة[/tpl], [tpl]airport codes|RAK|GMMX|p=n[/tpl]) is an international airport serving Marrakesh, the capital city of the Marrakesh-Tensift-El Haouz region in Morocco.  It is an international facility that receives several European flights as well as flights from Casablanca and some of the Arab world nations. The airport served over 3.3 million passengers in the year 2012.

==History==

During World War II, the airport was used by the United States Army Air Forces Air Transport Command as a hub for cargo, transiting aircraft and personnel.  It functioned as a stopover en route to Casablanca Airfield or to Agadir Airport on the North African Cairo-Dakar transport route for cargo, transiting aircraft and personnel.:File:Atcroutes-1sep1945.jpg

==Terminal==

Currently (2012) Menara Airport (RAK) has formally two passenger terminals, but these are more or less combined to one large terminal A third terminal is being built.ONDA website:Investment program 2011, visited 10 June 2012 The existing T1/T2 offer a space of 42.000 m2 and has a designed capacity of 4,5 million passengers/year. The separate freight-terminal has 340m2 covered spaceONDA Website: Presentation RAK, visited 10 Junes, 2012

==Facilities==

Aircraft parking space of [tpl]convert|125000|m²|0[/tpl] supports up to fourteen Boeing 737s and four Boeing 747s. The air terminals (1 and 2) are [tpl]convert|22000|m²|0|abbr=on[/tpl] and designed to handle [tpl]formatnum:2500000[/tpl] passengers per year. The cargo terminal is [tpl]convert|340|m²|0|abbr=on[/tpl] of covered space.
The paved runway is laid out in the direction 10/28 (almost East-West) is [tpl]convert|3100|x|45|m|0[/tpl]. It can receive all modern jetliners up to the Boeing 747 in size. The airport is equipped with an ILS Cat II landing system and offers the following radio navigation aids: VOR  – DME – NDB.

===VIP Service===

Menara is one of the six airports in Morocco where ONDA offers its special VIP service Salon Convives de Marque.ONDA website on the VIP service, visited 17 March 2012
