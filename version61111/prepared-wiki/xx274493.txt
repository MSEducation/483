[[The Gospel According to the Blues]]

CATEGORIES: Canadian television films, 2010 television films

The Gospel According to the Blues is a television drama. It originally aired on Vision TV in Canada. It is based on the stage play Gideon's Blues by George Elroy Boyd and adapted for screen by Thom Fitzgerald. The movie stars Jackie Richardson, Richard Chevolleau, Karen Robinson, and Cory Bowles.
