[[Intercap Esporte Clube]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in Brazil, Association football clubs established in 1972, Sport in Tocantins, 1972 establishments in Brazil

Intercap Esporte Clube, commonly known as Intercap, is a Brazilian football club based in Paraíso do Tocantins, Tocantins state. They competed in the Série C once.

==History==

The club was founded on July 10, 1972.[tpl]cite book|title=Escudos dos Times do Mundo Inteiro|publisher=Panda Books|author=Rodolfo Rodrigues|year=2009|page=95[/tpl] Intercap won the Campeonato Tocantinense in 1995, and competed in the Série C in the same year.

==Stadium==
