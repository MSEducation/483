Dmitri Vladimirovich Godunok ([tpl]lang-ru|Дмитрий Владимирович Годунок[/tpl]) (born January 4, 1976 in Volzhsky, Volgograd Oblast) is a Russian footballer who last played for FC Akademiya Togliatti.
