[[Shyam Veer Saini]]

CATEGORIES: Indian politicians

Shyam Veer Saini is a politician and Leader of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) and the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS). Saini was ex minister in the last Uttarakhand BJP government. Before joining politics he worked for IIT Roorkee.
