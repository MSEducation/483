[[KZEW]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Wyoming, Adult contemporary radio stations in the United States

KZEW (101.7 FM, "The Zoo") is a radio station broadcasting an adult contemporary music format. Licensed to Wheatland, Wyoming, USA, the station is currently owned by Smith Broadcasting, Incorporated, and features programming from NBC News Radio and Dial Global. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KZEW |title=KZEW Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==History==

The station went on the air as KYCN-FM on 1984-11-15.  On 1998-09-04, the station changed its call sign to the current KZEW. [tpl]cite web|url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=60645&Callsign=KZEW |title=KZEW Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl] Prior to 1998, the KZEW call sign was used by a classic rock format station in Dallas, Texas which from 1973-1989 also called itself "The Zoo" (now KBFB, 97.9 FM).
