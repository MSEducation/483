[[Death to Perfection]]

CATEGORIES: Torch albums, 2006 albums

"Death To Perfection" is the debut album by Torch. The album was released on March 27, 2006 and was recorded at Dsign Studio in Throndheim. It was produced and engineered by the band themselves and Edgar Lien.
