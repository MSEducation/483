CATEGORIES: Cycling at the 2011 Pan American Games

The women's BMX competition of the cycling events at the 2011 Pan American Games was held on October 21 at the CODE San Nicolás in Guadalajara.Guadalajara 2011 sessions The defending champion is Maria Gabriela Diaz of Argentina.
