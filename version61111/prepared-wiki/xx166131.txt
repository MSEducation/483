[[1936 Cincinnati Reds season]]

CATEGORIES: Cincinnati Reds seasons, 1936 Major League Baseball season

The 1936 Cincinnati Reds season was a season in American baseball. The team finished fifth in the National League with a record of 74-80, 18 games behind the New York Giants.

==Offseason==

The Reds held spring training at San Juan, Puerto Rico. The Reds opened camp at San Juan on February 10, 1936.[tpl]cite news |title=Majors' Spring Training Under Way Officially |author=Alan Gould |url=http://news.google.com/newspapers?id=b8FIAAAAIBAJ&sjid=fwENAAAAIBAJ&pg=1411,5278357&dq=reds+puerto+rico&hl=en |newspaper=The Meriden Daily Journal |date=February 11, 1936 |accessdate= March 6, 2011|page=4[/tpl]
