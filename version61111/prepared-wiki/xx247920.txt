[[Fancy Dance (manga)]]

CATEGORIES: 1984 manga, 1989 films, Films based on manga, Films directed by Masayuki Suo, Winners of the Shogakukan Manga Award for shōjo manga, Shōjo manga, Manga adapted into films, Shogakukan manga, Japanese films

[tpl]Nihongo|Fancy Dance|ファンシィダンス[/tpl] is a Japanese  shōjo manga series by Reiko Okano. It won the 34th Shogakukan Manga Award for shōjo.[tpl]cite web | url=http://comics.shogakukan.co.jp/mangasho/rist.html | title=小学館漫画賞: 歴代受賞者 | publisher=Shogakukan | language=Japanese | accessdate=2010-01-08[/tpl] It was adapted into a 1989 live action film directed by Masayuki Suo.
