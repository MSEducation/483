[[Otrera]]

CATEGORIES: Amazons (Greek mythology), Queens in Greek mythology, Offspring of Ares

In Greek mythology, Otrera (Greek: Οτρηρη Otrērē) was a Queen of the Amazons; the daughter of Eurus (the east wind), consort of Ares and mother of Hippolyta, Antiope, Melanippe, and Penthesilea.Bibliotheca, Epitome of Book IV, 5. 1Hyginus, Fabulae, 30; 112 
Otrera is sometimes considered the mythological founder of the Temple of Artemis in Ephesus,Hyginus, Fabulae 223, 225 which was closely connected with Amazons.Pausanias, Description of Greece 7. 2. 6 She is also sometimes considered the founder of the Amazon nation, though many myths place the first Amazons much earlier.
