[[Captatio benevolentiae]]

CATEGORIES: Latin literary phrases, Latin legal terms

Captatio benevolentiae (Latin for "winning of goodwill") is a rhetorical technique aimed to capture the goodwill of the audience at the beginning of a speech or appeal.
