[[Sterzh Cross]]

CATEGORIES: Novgorod Republic, Buildings and structures in Tver Oblast, Cross symbols, Old Russian inscriptions, 1133 works

Sterzh Cross is a stone cross formerly situated on the bank of the Sterzh Lake. It is now exhibited in the Tver History Museum. Its height is 167 cm.
The cross was erected by Ivanko Pavlovich, a posadnik from Novgorod, in 1133. The inscription on the cross reads: "On 14 July, 6641 we started to dig a channel, whereupon I, Ivanko Pavlovich, erected this cross".
