[[KDDI-LP]]

CATEGORIES: Low-power FM radio stations in Iowa, Radio stations in Iowa

KDDI-LP (102.9 FM) is a radio station licensed to Adair, Iowa, USA.  The station is currently owned by Iowa Department of Transportation.
