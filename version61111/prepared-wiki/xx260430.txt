[[Spectrum (1958 TV series)]]

CATEGORIES: CBC network shows, 1958 Canadian television series debuts, 1958 Canadian television series endings, Canadian drama television series

Spectrum is a Canadian drama anthology television miniseries which aired on CBC Television in 1958.

==Premise==

This Vancouver-produced series featured various teleplays.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.broadcasting-history.ca/programming/television/programming_popup.php?id=747 | first=John | last=Corcelli | date=February 2005 | title=Spectrum | publisher=Canadian Communications Foundation | accessdate=7 May 2010 [/tpl]
