[[Kiddie Coaster]]

CATEGORIES: Roller coasters introduced in 1997

Kiddie Coaster is the name of a steel roller coaster located at Lake Compounce in Bristol, Connecticut.  Manufactured by Molina & Son's and was installed in 1997. Children no taller than 56Â inches and no shorter than 36 inches can ride this kid sized coaster by themselves, adults may ride if accompanied by a child under 56 inches.
