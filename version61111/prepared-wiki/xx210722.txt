[[Bommersheim]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Hesse

Bommersheim is one of five town districts of Oberursel,  located southeast of the core city and immediately northwest of Frankfurt am Main in Germany.

==History==

The oldest known documented mention of Bommersheim is found in the Codex Laureshamensis, the so-called Lorscher Codex.
