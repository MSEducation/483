[[W18BL-D]]

CATEGORIES: Television stations in Tennessee, Television channels and stations established in 1994

W18BL-D Channel 18 TV is a Class A television station located in Adamsville, Tennessee.http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/tvq?list=0&facid=68911 The station is owned and operated by the Unity Broadcasting Network.http://ubntv.org/TV_34_ubntv/Locations.html In 2011 the station began broadcasting in digital, and changed its call sign from the previous W18BL on July 14, 2011.
