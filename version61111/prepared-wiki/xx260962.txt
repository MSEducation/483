[[Garden River, Alberta]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Alberta, Cree, First Nations in Alberta

Garden River, also known as Garden Creek, is an unincorporated community in northern Alberta, Canada within Wood Buffalo National Park. It is located on the north shore of the Peace River at the terminus of Garden River Road, an extension of Highway 58,[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.alberta.ca/acn/200905/2607187BB64F0-A471-0B63-01C4CB831E677FF8.html | title=Highway construction ramps up in northern Alberta | publisher=Alberta Transportation | date=2009-05-28 | accessdate=2010-12-31[/tpl] approximately [tpl]convert|190|km|mi|abbr=on[/tpl] east of the Town of High Level. It is a First Nations community of the Little Red River Cree Nation.[tpl]cite web | url=http://lrrcn.ab.ca/profile/community.html | title=LRRCN Profile â€“ Community | publisher=Little Red River Cree Nation | accessdate=2010-12-31[/tpl]
