[[Take On]]

CATEGORIES: Bohemia Interactive games, Open world video games, Video game sequels, Windows games, Video game franchises

Take On is a series about simulating one aspect and making a game out of it (e.g. Helicopters, Rovers, and Landers). Since 2011 to 2013, all Take On games are developed by Bohemia Interactive Studio or its subsidiaries.
