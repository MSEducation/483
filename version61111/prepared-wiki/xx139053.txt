[[Turn Your Car Around]]

CATEGORIES: 2005 singles, Lee Ryan songs, 2004 songs, Sony BMG singles

"Turn Your Car Around" is the second single released from Blue member Lee Ryan's debut solo album, Lee Ryan. The track peaked at #12 on the UK Singles Chart, also reaching the Top 20 in several other European countries, including number #2 in Italy. The song was originally written and recorded by a New Zealand musician, Ben Novak, in 2004.
