[[Blackwood Creek, Tasmania]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Tasmania

Blackwood Creek is a small community at the base of the Great Western Tiers, Australia.  At the 2006 census, Blackwood Creek and the surrounding area had a population of 117.
Blackwood Creek Post Office opened on 1 June 1884 and closed in 1974.[ref]
</ref>
Nearby towns include Poatina, Liffey, Bracknell, Cressy and Miena.
