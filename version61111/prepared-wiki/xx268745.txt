[[Judo at the 2011 Pan American Games – Men's 73 kg]]

CATEGORIES: Judo at the 2011 Pan American Games

The men's 73 kg competition of the judo events at the 2011 Pan American Games in Guadalajara, Mexico, was held on October 28 at the CODE II Gymanasium.[tpl]cite web|title=Judo technical manual|url=http://www.guadalajara2011.org.mx/documentos/manuales/eng/technical/tm_judo.pdf|publisher=Guadalajara 2011|accessdate=October 21, 2011[/tpl]  The defending champion was Ryan Reser of the United States.
