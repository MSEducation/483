[[Fuping County, Shaanxi]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Shaanxi

Fuping County ([tpl]zh|s=富平县|p=Fùpíng Xiàn[/tpl]) is a county in Weinan City, located in the center of Shaanxi Province of China.
Area: 1233 km2; Population: 750,000; Postal Code: 711700
It comprises 24 towns, 337 administrative villages.
