[[Irina Kulikova]]

CATEGORIES: 1991 births, Living people, Russian female models, People from Kirov Oblast

Irina Kulikova (born August 6, 1991) is a Russian fashion model. She was discovered in a Moscow restaurant by Ivan Bart of IMG Models and actress Liv Tyler.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.vogue.co.uk/news/daily/2007-08/070806-model-behaviour.aspx | title=Model Behaviour | publisher=Vogue | date=6 August 2007 | accessdate=2008-10-07[/tpl] She is now represented by IMG Models worldwide, and was named one of the top 10 models of 2007 by V.[tpl]cite web | url=http://www.vmagazine.com/2007/04/top-10-models-of-fall-2007-6-irina-kulikova/ | title=TOP 10 MODELS OF FALL 2007: #6 IRINA KULIKOVA | publisher=V | date=April 19, 2007 | accessdate=2008-10-07[/tpl]
During her first season in Fall/Winter 2007 she opened the shows of Prada and Nina Ricci, closed the shows of Yves Saint Laurent and Louis Vuitton, and was an exclusive for Calvin Klein in New York, among others. She has since opened shows for Dior, Donna Karan, BCBG Max Azria, Marc Jacobs, John Galliano, and Sonia Rykiel, and closed shows for Peter Som, Alberta Ferretti, Proenza Schouler, Donna Karan, Moschino, Dries Van Noten, John Galliano, Sonia Rykiel, Viktor & Rolf, and Vera Wang.[tpl]cite web | url=http://nymag.com/fashion/models/ikulikova/irinakulikova/ | title=Irina Kulikova | publisher=New York | accessdate=2008-10-07[/tpl] She also received her first crop of campaigns for Prada, Marc Jacobs' new fragrance "Daisy", Jil Sander, and Pringle of Scotland, while appearing regularly in editorials for W. She is known for being a favorite model of Marc Jacobs and John Galliano.[tpl]Citation needed|date=May 2012[/tpl]
