[[Agrij]]

CATEGORIES: Communes in Sălaj County

Agrij ([tpl]lang-hu|Felsőegregy[/tpl]) is a commune located in Sălaj County, Romania. It is composed of two villages, Agrij and Răstolţu Deşert (Pusztarajtolc). It was called Treznea-Agrij from 1988 to 1995, when Treznea and Bozna villages were split off to form Treznea Commune.
