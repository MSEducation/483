[[Shepherds of the Night]]

CATEGORIES: 1964 novels, Novels by Jorge Amado, Brazilian novels, Portuguese-language novels, Novels adapted into films

Shepherds of the Night (Portuguese: Os Pastores da Noite) is a Brazilian Modernist novel. It was written by Jorge Amado in 1964 and published in English in 1967. Marcel Camus adapted it into a 1975 film, Othalia de Bahia.
