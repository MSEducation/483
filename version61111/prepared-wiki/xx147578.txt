[[Sosny]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Belarus

Sosny ([tpl]lang-be|Сосны[/tpl]; English translation - Pines) is a city approximately 15 kilometers outside of Minsk, Belarus. Hidden in the tall Minsk pines, Sosny is considered by most a suburb of Minsk with a population of about 3,000. Until 1991, Sosny was home to a scientific nuclear reactor that was used for both military and civil purposes.[tpl]ref|wow[/tpl]
