[[Josep Raich]]

CATEGORIES: 1913 births, 1994 deaths, Catalan footballers, Spanish footballers, Spain international footballers, La Liga footballers, FC Barcelona footballers, FC Sète players, Ligue 1 players, Troyes AC players, Ligue 2 players, Expatriate footballers in France

Josep Raich Garriga (August 28, 1913 - 1988) is a former Spanish footballer of Catalan ethnicity who played for FC Barcelona in Spain and FC Sète and Troyes AC in France. He played once for Spain in 1941.
