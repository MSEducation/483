[[Jan Pahad Dargah]]

CATEGORIES: Islam in India, Indian Sufis, Nalgonda district, Dargahs in India, Islam in Bangladesh, Islam in Pakistan, Barelvi

Jan Pahad Saidula Dargah is a dargah situated in Neducherla in Nalgonda district in Andhra Pradesh, India.[tpl]cite news |title= Devotees throng dargah|author= |url= http://www.hindu.com/2009/01/24/stories/2009012450770200.htm|newspaper= The Hindu|date= |accessdate=18 February 2012[/tpl] People from different religious faiths visit the holy shirne on the occasion of the annual Urs festival.[tpl]cite news |title= Candidates seek divine intervention|author= |url= http://www.hindu.com/2009/03/25/stories/2009032551190200.htm|newspaper= The Hindu|date= |accessdate=18 February 2012[/tpl]
The dargah is built on the tomb of saint Mohiuddin alias Shaheed and Jan Pahad Saida. Qawwali, Sandal Sharif ceremonial procession and other special rituals mark the Urs festivities.
