[[The Fallen Man]]

CATEGORIES: 1996 novels, Novels by Tony Hillerman, HarperCollins books

The Fallen Man is a Tony Hillerman novel first published in 1996. The novel follows the investigation following the discovery of a corpse by a group of mountain climbers on Shiprock. 
The book features the characters Joe Leaphorn and Jim Chee. In this, Joe Leaphorn has recently retired, and Jim Chee is acting Lieutenant, who must learn the tricks of the trade from Leaphorn. 
The plot also involves the theft of cattle, in which they are being secretly lured onto trucks with bait.
