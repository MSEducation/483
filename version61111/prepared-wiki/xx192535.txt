[[Jon Gelius]]

CATEGORIES: 1964 births, Living people, People from Arendal, Norwegian journalists, Norwegian television news anchors, Norwegian Broadcasting Corporation people, Norwegian television reporters and correspondents, Norwegian expatriates in the United States

Jon Gelius (born May 26, 1964 in Arendal) is a Norwegian journalist.
Born in Arendal, he has worked as a reporter and presenter with both Dagsrevyen and Dagsnytt. In recent years he is best known as one of the Dagsrevyen main news anchors. He was appointed as television news editor in 2008.
Until 2010 he was the television news editor of NRK, and as such the editor of Dagsrevyen and other programmes. In 2010 he became their correspondent in Washington, DC.[tpl]cite news | url = http://www.nrk.no/nyheter/norge/1.6985031| author = NRK | page = | title = Gelius til Washington | work = NRK | date = February 9, 2010[/tpl] His tenure ended in 2013.[tpl]cite news|title=NRK ansetter fire nye korrespondenter|agency=Norwegian News Agency|date=13 February 2013|language=Norwegian[/tpl]
Gelius is the brother of the former priest and Labour politician Einar Gelius.
