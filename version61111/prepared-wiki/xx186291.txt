[[Gmina Pakosławice]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Opole Voivodeship, Nysa County

Gmina Pakosławice is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Nysa County, Opole Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Its seat is the village of Pakosławice, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|9|km|mi|0[/tpl] north of Nysa and [tpl]convert|43|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] west of the regional capital Opole.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|74.03|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 3,899.

==Villages==

Gmina Pakosławice contains the villages and settlements of Biechów, Bykowice, Frączków, Godkowice, Goszowice, Korzękwice, Naczków, Nowaki, Pakosławice, Prusinowice, Radowice, Reńska Wieś, Rzymiany, Słupice, Śmiłowice, Smolice, Spiny and Strobice.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Pakosławice is bordered by the gminas of Grodków, Kamiennik, Łambinowice, Nysa, Otmuchów and Skoroszyce.
