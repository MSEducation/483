[[Anton Herzog]]

CATEGORIES: Austrian educators, Austrian writers

A school teacher in Wiener Neustadt, Anton Herzog is most famous for his account of the commissioning of the requiem from Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart by Count Franz von Walsegg.www.angelfire.com/bc2/mozart/P13B.html.  "An interesting and comprehensive account..." The document was censored in Vienna in February 1839; the back of the booklet was marked "Not allowed Imperial and Royal Office Censor. Vienna, 8 Feb. 1839. Freyberger mpria."Landon, H. C. Robbins. Mozart's Last Year, p. 82. An edited version finally appeared in a Vienna newspaper in 1925.
