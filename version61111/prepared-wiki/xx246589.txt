[[José Muguerza]]

CATEGORIES: 1911 births, 1980 deaths, Basque footballers, Spanish footballers, Spain international footballers, 1934 FIFA World Cup players, Athletic Bilbao footballers, People from Eibar

José Muguerza Antinúa (born 15 September 1911 in Eibar;http://www.bdfutbol.com/en/j/j11310.html died 23 October 1984) was a Spanish association footballer. During his career he played for Athletic Bilbao (1928–1936), and earned 9 caps for the Spain national football team, and participated in the 1934 FIFA World Cup.
