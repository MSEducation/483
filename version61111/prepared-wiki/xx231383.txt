[[Jiangcheng Hani and Yi Autonomous County]]

CATEGORIES: County-level divisions of Pu'er City, Autonomous counties of the People's Republic of China, Hani people, Yi people

Jiangcheng Hani and Yi Autonomous County ([tpl]zh|s=江城哈尼族彝族自治县 |t=江城哈尼族彝族自治縣 |p=Jiāngchéng hānízú yízú Zìzhìxiàn[/tpl]) is an autonomous county under the jurisdiction of Pu'er City, in southern Yunnan, China, bordering Laos and Vietnam to the south, making it the only county in the province to border more than one country. By road, its seat, the town of Menglie ([tpl]lang|zh-hans|勐烈镇[/tpl]), is [tpl]convert|520|km|abbr=on[/tpl] from Kunming and [tpl]convert|145|km|abbr=on[/tpl] from Simao District, the municipal seat of Pu'er.

==Geography and climate==

Jiangcheng has latitude range of 22°20'−22°36' N and longitude range of 101°14'−102°19' E. Its seat, the town of Menglie ([tpl]lang|zh-hans|勐烈镇[/tpl]), has an elevation of [tpl]convert|1119|m|abbr=on[/tpl]. Located at an altitude of above [tpl]convert|1100|m|abbr=on[/tpl], Jiangcheng, as with much of southern Yunnan, has a warm humid subtropical climate (Köppen Cwa), with muddled distinction between the seasons and daytime temperatures remaining warm year-round. Highs peak in April and May before the core of the rainy season and reach a minimum in December; however, the warmest and coolest months are June and January, respectively at [tpl]convert|22.5|°C|1[/tpl] and [tpl]convert|12.6|°C|1[/tpl]; the annual mean is [tpl]convert|18.39|°C|1[/tpl]. June thru September accounts for nearly 70% of the annual rainfall of [tpl]convert|2240|mm|0|abbr=on[/tpl] and during this time, some rainfall occurs on most days.
