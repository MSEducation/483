[[Miss Universe 1968]]

CATEGORIES: Miss Universe, 1968 in the United States, 1968 beauty pageants, Beauty pageants in the United States

Miss Universe 1968, the 17th Miss Universe pageant, was won by 20-year-old Martha Vasconcellos of Brazil. She was crowned by Sylvia Hitchcock from USA. It took place on 13 July 1968 at the Miami Beach Auditorium in Miami Beach, Florida, USA.
