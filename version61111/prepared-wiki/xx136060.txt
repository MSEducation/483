[[Ari Boyland]]

CATEGORIES: New Zealand male film actors, New Zealand male television actors, New Zealand male stage actors, Living people, 1987 births, New Zealand male voice actors, People from Lower Hutt

Ari Boyland (born 10 August 1987) is a New Zealand stage and television actor.

==Career==

Best known for his role as KC in Cloud 9's The Tribe.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/dna/h2g2/A678332 |title='The Tribe' - the Television Series |accessdate= |author= |authorlink= |date=8 January 2002 |year= |month= |format= |work= |publisher=BBC.co.uk |pages= |language= |archiveurl= |archivedate= |quote= [/tpl] He was a cast member in Series 1-3, along with a brief appearance in Series 5, and whose character became somewhat of a protege of Lex (Caleb Ross).[tpl]cite web |url=http://213.86.54.16/tribeworld/guide/interviews/i32.asp |title=Head to Head - Ari Boyland and KC |accessdate= |author= |authorlink= |date= |year= |month= |format= |work=Guide: Interviews |publisher=TribeWorld.com |pages= |language= |archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20030820173327/http://213.86.54.16/tribeworld/guide/interviews/i32.asp |archivedate=2003-08-20 |quote= [/tpl] Boyland also starred in The Tribe music video and album Abe Messiah and the documentary The Making of The Tribe in 1999 as well as the 2001 British documentary A Date With the Tribe.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.worzel.force9.co.uk/ari-boyland.htm |title=Ari Boyland |accessdate= |author= |authorlink= |date= |year= |month= |work=Gummidge's Aussie & Kiwi Young Actors |publisher=Worzel.co.uk |pages= |language= |archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20040417105513/http://www.worzel.force9.co.uk/ari-boyland.htm |archivedate=2004-04-17 |quote= [/tpl]
While attending the New Zealand Children's Academy, he also had small roles in Cloud 9's Revelations - The Initial Journey and the Disney Channel Original Movie You Wish! as well as many theatrical and musical productions across New Zealand.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.tribeworld.com/guide/archive/who_is_who/whoiswho_kc.asp |title=Who's Who: K.C. |accessdate= |author=Cloud 9 Screen Entertainment Group |authorlink= |date= |year= |month= |format= |work= |publisher=TribeWorld.com |pages= |language= |archiveurl= |archivedate= |quote= [/tpl]
In August 2008, Boyland starred in the White Trash Omnibus, a play written and directed by Auckland University graduate Patrick Graham, with Kate Rylatt and Mike Ginn at the Maidment Theatre in Auckland, New Zealand.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.maidment.auckland.ac.nz/uoa/maidment/archives/2008/08/studio/white-trash-omnibus.cfm |title=White Trash Omnibus |accessdate= |author= |authorlink= |date= August 2008 |format= |work= |publisher=University of Auckland |pages= |language= |archiveurl= |archivedate= |quote= [/tpl][tpl]cite web |url=http://www.thehive.co.nz/2008/aug/auckland-central/white-trash-omnibus |title=White Trash Omnibus - Auckland Central - The Hive |accessdate= |author= |authorlink= |date= August 2008 |format= |work= |publisher=TheHive.co.nz |pages= |language= |archiveurl= |archivedate= |quote= [/tpl] He is also scheduled to appear in the upcoming South Pacific Pictures television series Go Girls based on another play written by Patrick Graham.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.theatreview.co.nz/reviews/production.php?id=836 |title=White Trash Omnibus |accessdate= |author=Robertson, Sian |authorlink= |date= August 2008 |format= |work=The New Zealand Performing Arts Review & Directory |publisher=TheatreView.co.nz |pages= |language= |archiveurl= |archivedate= |quote= [/tpl] In 2009, he played the character of Flynn McAllistair in the children's television series Power Rangers: RPM.
