[[Aaron Lim]]

CATEGORIES: 1985 births, Living people, Malaysian Chinese people, Malaysian Buddhists, Malaysian racing drivers, A1 Team Malaysia drivers, Formula BMW Asia drivers, Formula V6 Asia drivers, People from Selangor

Aaron Lim (born June 22, 1985 in Selangor) is a Malaysian racing driver. He has competed in such series as Formula V6 Asia and A1 Grand Prix for A1 Team Malaysia.
