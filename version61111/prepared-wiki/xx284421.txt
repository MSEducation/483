Ivan Mikhaylovich Gubar ([tpl]lang-ru|Иван Михайлович Губарь[/tpl]; born April 19, 1996) is a Russian football defender, who currently playing for FC Chernomorets Novorossiysk.
He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Chernomorets Novorossiysk on August 4, 2013 in a game against FC Olimpia Volgograd.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Russian Football Union|url=http://rfspro.ru/cgi-bin/player/script2.cgi?par1=119608|title=Career Summary|date=11 August 2013[/tpl]
