[[Little Alberta]]

CATEGORIES: Mountains of Alberta, Winston Churchill Range

Little Alberta is a mountain located east of Habel Creek in the Canadian Rockies of Jasper National Park. The mountain was named in 1984 after nearby Mount Alberta ([tpl]convert|3619|m|ft|abbr=on[/tpl]).
The Alpine Club of Canada (ACC) operates the Lloyd MacKay Hut on a rocky shoulder of Little Alberta.
