[[1040]]

CATEGORIES: 1040

Year 1040 (Roman numerals: MXL) was a leap year starting on Tuesday of the revised Julian calendar. It is the 40th year of the 11th century and of the 2nd millennium, and is the first year of the 1040s decade.
