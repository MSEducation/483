[[Real Sex]]

CATEGORIES: 1990s American television series, 1992 American television series debuts, 2000s American television series, 2010s American television series, Documentary films about sexuality, American documentary television series, Erotic television series, HBO network shows, English-language television programming

Real Sex is a documentary television series broadcast on and a production of HBO.http://www.hbo.com/#/schedule/series/REAL+SEX/PMRS161  As its name implies, Real Sex is a sexually explicit "magazine" which "explores sex '90s style."[tpl]cite web|author=TV.com |url=http://www.tv.com/shows/real-sex/ |title=Real Sex |publisher=TV.com |date=2012-12-19 |accessdate=2013-10-06[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.movieweb.com/tv/TV2BK775lM3B54 |title=HBO Real Sex |publisher=Movieweb.com |date= |accessdate=2013-10-06[/tpl]
Real Sex (dubbed the "60 Minutes of sex") explores human sexuality, Gary R. Edgerton and Jeffrey P. Jones described the fare in The Essential HBO Reader as "a peek into the diversity of sexual activities...with an emphasis that ranges from the unusual to the bizarre."  The show typically explores three to four topics each episode. Segments are separated by street interviews with random people, relating to the episode's topics.The Essential HBO Reader, Gary R. Edgerton and Jeffrey P. Jones, University Press of Kentucky, July 2013
The last Real Sex episode aired in 2009. Older episodes as well as "best-of" episodes are frequently re-aired during late nights on HBO.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/best_of_real_sex/ |title=Best of Real Sex |publisher=Rotten Tomatoes |date=1970-01-01 |accessdate=2013-10-06[/tpl]
It spawned a spin-off series called Pornucopia.
The series is being retitled, SEX // NOW, and returning in January 2014. http://vulture.com/news/real-sex
