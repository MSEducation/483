[[Bersvend Martinussen Røkkum]]

CATEGORIES: 1806 births, 1867 deaths, Members of the Parliament of Norway, Møre og Romsdal politicians

Bersvend Martinussen Røkkum (2 February 1806 - 14 August 1867) was a Norwegian politician.
He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament in 1842, representing the rural constituency of Romsdals Amt (today named Møre og Romsdal). He worked as a farmer.Bersvend Martinussen Røkkum — Norwegian Social Science Data Services (NSD) He served only one term.
He was born and died in Ålvundfjord.
