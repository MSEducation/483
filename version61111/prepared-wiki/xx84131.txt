[[Takagi, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Takagi|喬木村|Takagi-mura[/tpl] is a village located in Shimoina District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the village has an estimated population of 7,039 and a density of 105.66 persons per km². The total area is 66.62 km².
