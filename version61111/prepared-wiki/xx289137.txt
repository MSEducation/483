[[Peyghambarlu]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Peyghambarlu ([tpl]lang-fa|پيغمبرلو[/tpl], also Romanized as Peyghambarlū)[tpl]IranNCSGN[/tpl] is a village in Qeshlaqat-e Afshar Rural District, Afshar District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 40, in 10 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
