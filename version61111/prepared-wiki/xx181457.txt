Patrick Andrew "Paddy" McAlinney (9 November 1913 - 22 August 1990) was an Irish-born character actor who starred in many British dramas and sitcoms.[tpl]cite web|url=http://explore.bfi.org.uk/4ce2b9f56a496 |title=Patrick McAlinney | BFI | BFI |publisher=Explore.bfi.org.uk |date= |accessdate=2014-04-22[/tpl] His most memorable roles included a brother on the hit sitcom Oh, Brother!, which starred Derek Nimmo, Mr. O'Reilly in The Tomorrow People and Dr. Daley in Bless Me Father.
