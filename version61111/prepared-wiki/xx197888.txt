[[Levon Altonian]]

CATEGORIES: Armenian footballers, Lebanese footballers, Lebanese Armenians, Living people

Levon Altonian is a Lebanese former  international association football player. He was a striker. He used to play for Lebanese club Homenetmen Beirut.
He was awarded the top scorer of the Lebanese Premier League twice during the 61/62 and 64/65 seasons.[tpl]cite web|publisher=RSSSF|author=Mubarak, Hassanin|title=Lebanon - List of Topscorers|url=http://www.rsssf.com/tablesl/lebtops.html|accessdate=2009-07-09[/tpl]
Altonian played for the Lebanon national football team.[tpl]cite web|publisher=RSSSF|author=Mubarak, Hassanin|title=Lebanon - International Results - Early History|url=http://www.rsssf.com/tablesl/leb-intres-prehist.html|accessdate=2009-07-09[/tpl]
