[[Hilal Hemed Hilal]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1995 births, Tanzanian swimmers

Hilal Hemed Hilal, (born 1995) is a Tanzanian swimmer.http://www.bcn2013.com/en/swimming/detail/member/hilal-hemed-hilal He competed in the 50 m butterfly, 50 m, 100 m freestyle, 50 m, 100 m backstroke and 50 m breaststroke events at the 2012 FINA World Swimming Championships (25 m). Hilal also took part in the 50 m freestyle and 50 m butterfly events at the 2013 World Aquatics Championships.
