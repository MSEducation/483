[[White-crowned Cliff Chat]]

CATEGORIES: Thamnolaea

The White-crowned Cliff Chat (Thamnolaea coronata) is a species of bird in the Muscicapidae family.
It is found in west central Africa in Mali, Mauritania, Senegal, Ivory Coast, and Burkina Faso to western Sudan.
It is sometimes considered a subspecies of the Mocking Cliff Chat.
