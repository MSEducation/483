[[Vivi Bach]]

CATEGORIES: 1939 births, 2013 deaths, Cardiovascular disease deaths in Spain, Danish film actresses, People from Copenhagen, 20th-century Danish actresses

Vivi Bach (3 September 1939 – 22 April 2013)[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.bild.de/unterhaltung/leute/dietmar-schoenherr/fand-ex-frau-vivi-bach-tot-auf-dem-sofa-30108152.bild.html |title=Dietmar Schönherr fand sie tot auf dem Sofa |accessdate=2013-04-23 |work=bild.de[/tpl] was a Danish film actress. She appeared in 48 films between 1958 and 1974.
Bach was born in Copenhagen, Denmark and died on Ibiza, Spain where she lived with her husband, the Austrian film actor Dietmar Schönherr.
