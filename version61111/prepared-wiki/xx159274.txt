[[Han Ye-seul]]

CATEGORIES: 1982 births, Living people, South Korean female singers, South Korean film actors, South Korean female models, People who lost United States citizenship

Han Ye-seul (born 18 September 1981) is a Korean actress, singer and model.

==Biography==

Han was born as Kim Ye-Seul-Yi, while her English name is Leslie Kim.[tpl]cite web|url=http://english.tour2korea.com/02Culture/TVMiniseries/movie_03_view.asp?cpId=672&konum=2&kosm=m2_6|title=Korean Actor(Actress) Han Ye-Seul, Biography and Photos|publisher=Korea Tourism Organization|accessdate=2012-07-07|archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20071011135736/http://english.tour2korea.com/02Culture/TVMiniseries/movie_03_view.asp?cpId=672&konum=2&kosm=m2_6|archivedate=2007-10-11[/tpl] She was born and raised in Los Angeles, California, before moving to South Korea to pursue acting/modeling. She attended Cerritos High School and graduated from Cerritos College with an AA degree in computer graphics and is fluent in both English and Korean.[tpl]cite news|url=http://star.mt.co.kr/stview.php?no=2009112514424768938&ERV2|title=한예슬 나이관련 발언 와전돼 '오해'|work=Money Today|date=25 November 2009|accessdate=7 July 2012[/tpl]

===Naturalization===

Han renounced her U.S. citizenship in 2004 for South Korean citizenship to establish herself there as a Korean actress.[tpl]cite news|url=http://news.naver.com/main/read.nhn?mode=LSD&mid=sec&sid1=106&oid=073&aid=0000044390|title='왕싸가지' 한예슬, 미국서 더 큰 인기|work=Sports Seoul|date=9 November 2006|accessdate=2012-07-07[/tpl] [tpl]ko[/tpl][tpl]cite news|url=http://www.koreadaily.com/news/read.asp?page=4&branch=NEWS&source=DE&category=lifenleisure&art_id=536157|title=한예슬 '미국 국적 포기하겠다'|work=Korea Daily|date=17 March 2004|accessdate=7 July 2012[/tpl]

===Debut===

Han debuted as a model after winning a super model competition in 2001.[tpl]cite news|url=http://english.chosun.com/w21data/html/news/200610/200610280007.html|title=Han Ye Seul Wins Over Detractors as Amnesiac Heiress|work=Chosun Ilbo|date=28 October 2006|accessdate=12 November 2007[/tpl] 
She appeared in a variety of commercials in Korea ranging from health drinks to beauty products to Venus women's apparel to Pizza Hut TV spots, Samsung ads and commercials for Motorola phones. She is also the voice for the character Susan Murphy in the Korean-language version of the DreamWorks Animation film Monsters vs. Aliens (2009). She appeared in Rain's music video for "Love Song". [tpl]Citation needed|date=July 2012[/tpl]
On December 18, 2013, Han has signed an exclusive contract with her new management agency SB Entertainment, which is a subsidiary of its parent company Verdi Media.

===Personal life===

She was involved in a dispute with Korea Broadcasting System (KBS) for failing to show up to the filming of Myung Wol the Spy on 14 August 2011.[tpl]cite news|url=http://news.nate.com/view/20110815n04496|title=‘스파이명월’ 촬영거부 한예슬, 이젠 현명하게 처신할 때|date=15 August 2011|work=Nate|language=Korean|accessdate=15 August 2011[/tpl] Her agency cited conflicts with her PD over her working conditions, although it earlier explained that her absence was the result of poor health.[tpl]cite news|url=http://news.naver.com/main/read.nhn?mode=LSD&mid=sec&sid1=106&oid=382&aid=0000006576|title=드라마 촬영 거부 한예슬, PD 교체 요구…에릭은 날벼락|date=15 August 2011|work=Donga News|language=Korean|accessdate=15 August 2011[/tpl][tpl]cite news|url=http://news.nate.com/view/20110814n03504|title=한예슬 촬영거부 왜? She received an injection for fatigue. She unexpectedly left South Korea to return to Los Angeles on that date.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.fnnews.com/view?ra=Sent1301m_View&corp=fnnews&arcid=11081601082594&cDateYear=2011&cDateMonth=08&cDateDay=16|title='출국'한예슬, '스파이명월' 여주인공 교체 '누구?'|date=16 August 2011|work=저작권자|language=Korean|accessdate=15 August 2011[/tpl] At a meeting on the 15th, Kim agreed to return to filming on August 16.
She returned to Korea on the 18th, bringing her mother back with her for support. She was reported to have given a press conference at the airport apologizing on her sudden absence citing difficulties and hardships she had. She remarked that she firmly believes what she did was right despite criticism from the public and other media sources which pointed out her unexpected sudden absence from shooting.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.etoday.co.kr/news/section/newsview.php?TM=news&SM=5199&idxno=471208|title=한예슬 귀국|work=etoday.co.kr|accessdate=20 August 2011[/tpl]
