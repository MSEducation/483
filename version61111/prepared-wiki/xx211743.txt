[[Jenkinsville, South Carolina]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places established in 2008, Towns in South Carolina, Towns in Fairfield County, South Carolina, Columbia, South Carolina metropolitan area

Jenkinsville is an incorporated town in western Fairfield County, South Carolina, United States, between the Broad and the Little rivers. It is located east of Monticello Reservoirhttp://www.discoversouthcarolina.com/product.aspx?productID=25575 and is near the Virgil C. Summer Nuclear Generating Station. Also in the area is the Kincaid-Anderson Househttp://www.nationalregister.sc.gov/fairfield/S10817720019/index.htm and quarry. The town is South Carolina's newest, incorporating in 2008.http://www.masc.sc/municipalities/Pages/default.aspx Jenkinsville's population at incorporation was 50, also making it South Carolina's smallest town.
In addition to the Kincaid-Anderson House, the Ebenezer Associate Reformed Presbyterian Church, Dr. John Glenn House, High Point, Little River Baptist Church, and Mayfair are listed on the National Register of Historic Places.[tpl]NRISref|version=2010a[/tpl]
