[[Nineveh Township, Adair County, Missouri]]

CATEGORIES: German-American culture in Missouri, Townships in Adair County, Missouri, Kirksville, Missouri micropolitan area, Townships in Missouri

Nineveh Township is one of ten townships in Adair County, Missouri, United States. As of the 2010 census, its population was 1,289.[tpl]cite web| url=http://factfinder2.census.gov/bkmk/table/1.0/en/DEC/10_DP/G001/0600000US2900152526| title=Geographic Identifiers: 2010 Demographic Profile Data (G001): Nineveh township, Adair County, Missouri| publisher=U.S. Census Bureau, American Factfinder| accessdate=June 20, 2013[/tpl] The township is named for a German communistic colony that was established in the area in 1850.History of Adair County by E.M. Violette. Published 1911

==Geography==

Nineveh Township covers an area of [tpl]convert|54.41|sqmi|km2[/tpl] and contains one incorporated settlement, Novinger. It contains two cemeteries: Nineveh and Novinger.
The streams of Brush Creek, Davis Branch, Hazel Creek, Little Hazel Creek, Mulberry Creek, Rye Creek, Shuteye Creek and Spring Creek run through this township.
