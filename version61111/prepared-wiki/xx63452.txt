[[Chloral hydrate]]

CATEGORIES: Aldehydes, Hydrates, Hypnotics, Sedatives, Organochlorides, Prodrugs

Chloral hydrate is an organic compound with the formula C2H3Cl3O2.  It is a colourless solid.  It was once used as  sedative and hypnotic drug.  It remains a useful laboratory chemical reagent and precursor. It is derived from chloral (trichloroacetaldehyde) by the addition of one equivalent of water.
