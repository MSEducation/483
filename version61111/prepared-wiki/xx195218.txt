[[Martin, South Carolina]]

CATEGORIES: Unincorporated communities in Allendale County, South Carolina, Unincorporated communities in South Carolina

Martin is an unincorporated community in northwestern Allendale County, South Carolina, United States.  It lies along SC 125 northeast of the town of Allendale, the county seat of Allendale County.Rand McNally.  The Road Atlas '08. Chicago: Rand McNally, 2008, p. 92.  Its elevation is 92 feet (28 m).[tpl]gnis|1227943[/tpl], Geographic Names Information System, 1980-07-13.  Accessed 2008-04-30.  Although Martin is unincorporated, it has a post office, with the ZIP code of 29836.Zip Code Lookup
The Allendale Chert Quarries Archeological District was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1985.[tpl]NRISref|version=2010a[/tpl]
