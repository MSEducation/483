[[Celorico de Basto]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Portugal

Celorico de Basto  ([tpl]IPA-pt|-|lang|Pt-pt Celorico de Basto FF.ogg[/tpl]) has about 20,098 inhabitants and is located in Celorico de Basto Municipality, Braga District, Portugal. Celorico de Basto has been occupied since ancient times, as witness marks in the most ancient civilisations have left here.
