[[Skotniki, Piotrków County]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Piotrków County

Skotniki [tpl]IPAc-pl|s|k|o|t|'|n|i|k|i[/tpl] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Aleksandrów, within Piotrków County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately [tpl]convert|9|km|mi|0[/tpl] south-west of Aleksandrów, [tpl]convert|29|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-east of Piotrków Trybunalski, and [tpl]convert|73|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] south-east of the regional capital Łódź.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) – TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish[/tpl]
Skotniki lies on the Pilica River. It contains a church from the year 1528 and a manor house (16th century) with a park from the turn of the 16th and 17th centuries. Skotniki is a famous summer resort. The village is on the "Szlak wodny Pilicy" - a kayak route on the Pilica River. In the area are Diabla Góra Nature Reserve ([tpl]lang-pl|Rezerwat przyrody Diabla Góra[/tpl]) and a landscape park called Sulejów Landscape Park (Sulejowski Park Krajobrazowy).
