[[Arkadi Kuleshov]]

CATEGORIES: 1914 births, 1978 deaths, People from Kastsyukovichy Raion, People from Mogilev Governorate, Belarusian poets

Arkadź Kuliašoǔ, russified form Arkadi Kuleshov ([tpl]lang-be|Аркадзь Куляшоў[/tpl]; February 6, 1914 – February 4, 1978http://slounik.org/81124.html) was a poet and translator from Belarus.  He was best known for his poems, Brigade Flag and Cymbalon,  as well as his translations of poetry into Belarusian.
