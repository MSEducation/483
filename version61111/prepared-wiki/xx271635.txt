[[CAF U-23 Championship]]

CATEGORIES: CAF competitions for national teams, CAF U-23 Championship

The CAF U-23 Championship is the main international football competition for CAF nations, played by under 23 years old players. It is held every four years with the top three teams qualifying automatically to the Olympic Games and the fourth placed finisher playing in a play-off against a team from the Asian Football Confederation. The inaugural edition of the competition is held in Morocco from November 26 to December 10, 2011.Inaugural U-23 African Championship draw held in Cairo
