[[Véronique Augereau]]

CATEGORIES: French voice actresses, 1957 births, Living people

Véronique Augereau (born 25 May 1957) is a French actress who is notably active in dubbing. She is especially known for providing the voice of Marge Simpson in the French version of the animated series The Simpsons.[tpl]cite web|title=Entretien générique avec PHILIPPE PEYTHIEU et VERONIQUE AUGEREAU, les voix de Homer et Marge Simpson|url=http://www.cinemotions.com/interview/10392|work=CinEmotions.com|accessdate=13 August 2013[/tpl]
 
She is the wife of Philippe Peythieu, he does the voice of Homer Simpson, so also husband and wife in real life.
