[[Slide Show (album)]]

CATEGORIES: ECM Records albums, Ralph Towner albums, Gary Burton albums, 1986 albums, Albums produced by Manfred Eicher

Slide Show is an album by guitarist Ralph Towner and vibraphonist Gary Burton recorded in 1985 and released on the ECM label.ECM discography accessed September 26, 2011
