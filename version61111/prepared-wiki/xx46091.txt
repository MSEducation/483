[[Ohey]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Belgium, Municipalities of Namur (province)

Ohey ([tpl]lang-wa|Ohè[/tpl]) is a Walloon municipality and village in the Belgian province of Namur.
On 1 January 2006 the municipality had 4,283 inhabitants. The total area is 56.62 km², giving a population density of 76 inhabitants per km².
