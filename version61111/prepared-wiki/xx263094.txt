[[Strongyli (Crete)]]

CATEGORIES: Lasithi, Uninhabited islands of Greece

Strongyli ([tpl]lang-el|Στρογγυλή[/tpl], "round"), also known as Strongylo, is an uninhabited Greek islet, located south of cape Goudero on the coast of Lasithi, eastern Crete, in the Libyan Sea. It forms a close group of islands with Koufonisi, Makroulo, Marmaro, and Trachilos.
