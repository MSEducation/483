[[Svet stromov]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 singles, Marika Gombitová songs, Songs written by Ján Lehotský, Songs written by Kamil Peteraj

"Svet stromov" ([tpl]lang-en|The World of Trees[/tpl]) is a song by Marika Gombitová, released on single by OPUS in 1980.For Marika Gombitová's discography, see [tpl]harvnb|Lehotský|2008a|pp=54–56.[/tpl].
The composition was written by Janko Lehotský and Kamil Peteraj, being released with "Tajomstvo hier" (duet with Lehotský) on B-side .[tpl]harvnb|Graclík|Nekvapil|2008|p=417.[/tpl]
