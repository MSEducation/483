[[Torneio dos Campeões da CBD]]

CATEGORIES: 1969 in Brazilian football, Defunct football competitions in Brazil

Torneio dos Campeões da CBD was a Brazilian football competition held in 1969 with the objective of qualifying a team for the 1970 Copa Libertadores. Grêmio Maringá was declared as the champions after Botafogo forfeited the final after CBD decided not to have Brazilian teams competing in the 1970 Copa Libertadores.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.gazetadopovo.com.br/esportes/conteudo.phtml?tl=1id=1080488&tit=Gremio-Maringa-vai-entrar-na-Justica-para-ter-reconhecido-titulo-brasileiro|title=Grêmio Maringá vai entrar na Justiça para ter reconhecido título brasileiro|accessdate=December 31, 2010|publisher=Gazeta do Povo|language=Portuguese[/tpl]

==Games==

 (1)Santos forfeited to play the replay game thus Grêmio Maringá was awarded with a 1-0 win.
