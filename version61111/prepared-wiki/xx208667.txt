[[Bánh ướt]]

CATEGORIES: Vietnamese cuisine

Bánh ướt (literally "wet cakes"), is a Vietnamese thin pancake wrapperCharles Gordon Sinclair (1998) International Dictionary of Food & Cooking, Taylor & Francis, Page 48 ISBN 1-57958-057-2 consisting of rice noodle sheets, eaten with nước chấm, fried shallots, and a side of chả lụa (Vietnamese pork sausage).
