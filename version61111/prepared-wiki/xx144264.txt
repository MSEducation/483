Darryl Tyger BlackstockDarryl Blackstock NFL Football Statistics - Pro-Football-Reference.com (born May 30, 1983) is an American football linebacker who is currently a free agent] (NFL). He was drafted by the Arizona Cardinals in the third round of the 2005 NFL Draft. He played college football at Virginia.
He has also played for the Cincinnati Bengals, Oakland Raiders and Virginia Destroyers.

==College career==

Blackstock played college football at the University of Virginia. In 2004 Blackstock was selected to the Second-team All-ACC.

==Professional career==

===Arizona Cardinals===

Blackstock played three seasons in Arizona. From 2005-2007 Blackstock recorded 44 tackles, 4 sacks and 2 Forced fumbles.

===Cincinnati Bengals===

In 2008, Blackstock signed with the Bengals.

===Virginia Destroyers===

In 2010, Blackstock signed with Virginia Destroyers of the United Football League.

===Oakland Raiders===

Returned to the NFL and signed with the Raiders. Blackstock played 16 games and started one game and recorded 20 tackles.

===Baltimore Ravens===
