[[Sericomyiini]]

CATEGORIES: Hoverflies, Eristalinae, Sericomyiini

Sericomyiini is a tribe of hoverflies[tpl]cite book|author=Stubbs, Alan E. and Falk, Steven J.|year=1983 |title=British Hoverflies: An Illustrated Identification Guide |publisher=British Entomological & Natural History Society|isbn=0-9502891-4-0[/tpl] consisting mainly of large bumblebee or wasp mimics. As with the eristalini the larvae have a telescopic breathing tube which allows them to breathe while submerged in boggy areas.
