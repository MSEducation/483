[[Ljuvlig är sommarnatten]]

CATEGORIES: 1961 films, Swedish thriller films, Films directed by Arne Mattsson, Swedish films

Ljuvlig är sommarnatten ("The summer's night is delightful") is a Swedish film from 1961, directed by Arne Mattsson and starring Karl-Arne Holmsten, Christina Carlwind, Elisabeth Odén, Per Oscarsson, Folke Sundquist and Sif Ruud.  It is an adaptation of a detective novel by Dagmar Lange (Maria Lang), who wrote the script for the film herself.
