[[April Richardson]]

CATEGORIES: 1979 births, Living people, American stand-up comedians, People from Savannah, Georgia

April Marie Richardson (born May 1, 1979) is an American stand-up comedian. She is a round table regular on the television show Chelsea Lately. She is from Atlanta, Georgia and currently lives in Los Angeles, California.

==Early life==

Richardson was born in Savannah, Georgia. She spent several years in Manchester, England.
