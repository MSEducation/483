[[Kappelberg Tunnel]]

CATEGORIES: Tunnels in Germany

The Kappelberg Tunnel is a road tunnel on the Bundesstraße 14, located near the city of Stuttgart, Germany.
Following failings identified by the European Tunnel Assessment Programme, the tunnel underwent a €12 million refurbishment programme, which resulted in a rating of "very good" in 2006.[tpl]cite web|title=Europe's best tunnels on stage in Brussels|url=http://www.eurotestmobility.com/newslettershow.php?item=62|work=EuroTAP Newsletter|publisher=EuroTAP|accessdate=24 September 2011|date=30 January 2008[/tpl]
