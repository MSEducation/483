[[Tensobtenga Department]]

CATEGORIES: Departments of Burkina Faso, Kouritenga Province

Tensobtenga is a department or commune of Kouritenga Province in eastern Burkina Faso. Its capital lies at the town of Tensobtenga . According to the 1996 census the department has a total population of 18,746.Burkinabé government inforoute communale
