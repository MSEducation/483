[[Mireille]]

Mireille ([tpl]IPA-fr|miʁɛj[/tpl]) is a French given name, derived from the Provençal Occitan name Mirèio (or Mirèlha in the classical norm of Occitan, [tpl]IPA-oc|miˈɾɛjɔ, miˈɾɛʎɔ|pron[/tpl]). It could be related with the Occitan verb mirar "to look, to admire" or with the Occitan surnames Miriam "Myriam", Maria "Mary".
