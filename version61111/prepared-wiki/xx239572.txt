[[Nanyang, Shouning County]]

CATEGORIES: Ningde, Geography of Fujian

Nanyang (南阳镇) is a Town in Shouning County, in the municipal region of Ningde City, Fujian Province, China.

==Situation==

Nanyang borders the county-level city of Fu'an to the southeast; on all other sides it is bordered by towns and townships forming part of Shouning County: 
Xixi (犀溪乡) to the east; Da'an (大安乡) to the north; Qinyang (芹洋乡) to the northwest; Qingyuan (清源乡) to the west; Xietan (斜滩镇) to the southwest; and Zhuguanlong (竹管垅乡) to the south.
