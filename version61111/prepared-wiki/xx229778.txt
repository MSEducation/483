[[Martin Widdowson]]

CATEGORIES: Royal Air Force officers, People of the Royal Observer Corps, 1946 births, Living people

Air Commodore Martin Keith Widdowson (born 10 May 1946) was a British Royal Air Force pilot, a senior Royal Air Force officer in the 1980s and 1990s and the 24th and last Commandant Royal Observer Corps.

==Military career==

Widdowson was commissioned into the Royal Air Force on 25 March 1964, as an Acting Pilot Officer. A year later, on 25 March 1965, he was regraded as a Pilot Officer.[tpl]London Gazette |issue=43612 |date=26 March 1965 |startpage=3191 |supp=yes |accessdate=11 April 2010[/tpl] A year after that, on 25 March 1966, he was promoted to Flying Officer,[tpl]London Gazette |issue=43936 |date=25 March 1966 |startpage=3519 |endpage=3520 |supp=yes |accessdate=11 April 2010[/tpl] and he was appointed to a permanent commission on 1 October 1968.[tpl]London Gazette |issue=44850 |date=16 May 1969 |startpage=5261 |supp=yes |accessdate=11 April 2010[/tpl] He was promoted to Flight Lieutenant on 25 September 1969.[tpl]London Gazette |issue=44945 |date=26 September 1969 |startpage=9959 |supp=yes |accessdate=11 April 2010[/tpl] On 1 January 1974, he was promoted to Squadron Leader.[tpl]London Gazette |issue=46174 |date= 7 January 1974 |startpage=283 |endpage=284 |supp=yes |accessdate=11 April 2010[/tpl] He was promoted to Wing Commander on 1 July 1981.[tpl]London Gazette |issue=48677 |date=13 July 1981 |startpage=9312 |supp=yes |accessdate=11 April 2010[/tpl]
His final operational flying appointment was as the Officer Commanding No. 23 Squadron in 1982 and by the late 1980s, he was Station Commander of RAF Coningsby.http://www.rafweb.org/Stations/Station%20OCs-Lincs.htm He was promoted from Wing Commander to Group Captain on 1 January 1988.[tpl]London Gazette |issue=51188 |date=4 January 1988 |startpage=38 |supp=yes |accessdate=11 April 2010[/tpl] On 1 July 1992,[tpl]London Gazette |issue=52983 |date=6 July 1992 |startpage=11415 |supp=yes |accessdate=11 April 2010[/tpl] he was promoted to Air Commodorehttp://www.independent.co.uk/news/people/halfyearly-service-promotions-1542610.html and in 1995 he was appointed as Senior Air Staff Officer (SASO) of No. 11/18 Group RAF, simultaneously holding the appointment of 24th and last Commandant Royal Observer Corps until the ROC was Stood Down.http://www.rafweb.org/Grp09.htm  He retired from the RAF on 14 September 1996.[tpl]London Gazette |issue=54551 |date=14 October 1996 |startpage=13685 |supp=yes |accessdate=11 April 2010[/tpl]
