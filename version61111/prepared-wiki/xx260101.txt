[[Nu ABO (song)]]

CATEGORIES: F(x) songs, 2010 singles, Dance-pop songs, Korean-language songs, S.M. Entertainment singles, Songs written by Cutfather, Songs written by Yoo Young-jin, Songs written by Thomas Troelsen, Songs written by Engelina

Nu ABO (Hangul: [tpl]lang-ko|Nu 예삐오[/tpl]; Nu Yeppioh) is the lead single from the mini-album, Nu ABO, by a South Korean quintet pop girl band, f(x). It was released May 4, 2010 in South Korea and distributed by label agency, SM Entertainment. The first promotional performance of the song was made on May 7, 2010, on Music Bank.[tpl]cite web|url=http://community.livejournal.com/omonatheydidnt/3321612.html |title=omonatheydidnt: Music Core 080510 |publisher=Community.livejournal.com |date= |accessdate=2010-06-20[/tpl]

==Background==

===Etymology===

Nu ABO is a play on words meaning "New Blood Type": A, B and O are blood types, and if the letters are said in succession, it sounds like "ye-bbi-oh" ("예삐오"). The title is supposed to represent the fact that f(x) is a new group bringing their new talents, implying that they are the "new blood" to the music industry.[tpl]or|date=June 2010[/tpl]

===History===

The song was written by Thomas Troelsen, Cutfather, Mynority Jose Aguirre Lopez, and Engelina Larsen. The Korean lyrics were written by Yoo Young-jin. It was co-produced by Thomas Troelsen, Cutfather, and Mynority Jose Aguirre Lopez.[tpl]cite web|url=http://news.nate.com/view/20100430n03627 |title=네이트 뉴스 |publisher=News.nate.com |date= |accessdate=2010-06-20[/tpl] It was released May 4, 2010 in South Korea and distributed by label agency, SM Entertainment as well as Avex Asia. 

==Promotion==

The first promotional live performance of the song was made on May 7, 2010, on KBS's Music Bank. The music video was released on May 6, 2010.[tpl]cite web|url=http://community.livejournal.com/omonatheydidnt/3296498.html |title=omonatheydidnt: f(x) Nu ABO MV to be released on May 6th! |publisher=Community.livejournal.com |date= |accessdate=2010-06-20[/tpl]

==Reception==

It charted #1 online on Monkey3, Dosirak and Bugs, while it reached #2 on Melon.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.allkpop.com/2010/05/fxs-nu-abo-is-already-1-on-the-charts/comment-page-1 |title=f(x)’s Nu ABO is already #1 on the charts |publisher=Allkpop.com |date=2010-05-05 |accessdate=2010-06-20[/tpl] In the Philippines, it debuted on the #20 spot on the MYX International Top 20 chart and was the group's first single to debut at the said chart.
