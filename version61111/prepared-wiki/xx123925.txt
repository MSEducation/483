[[Mussidae]]

CATEGORIES: Mussidae

Mussidae is a family of stony coral that contains the genera Acanthastrea, Australomussa, Blastomussa, Cynarina, Isophyllastrea, Isophyllia, Lobophyllia, Mussa, Mussimilia, Mycetophyllia, Scolymia, and Symphyllia.
Members of this family are widely sought after for the reef aquarium trade.
