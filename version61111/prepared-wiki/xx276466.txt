[[Zazeran]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Falavarjan County, Cities in Iran, Cities in Isfahan Province

Zazeran ([tpl]lang-fa|زازران[/tpl], also Romanized as Zāzerān)[tpl]GEOnet3|6012979[/tpl]  is a city in the Central District of Falavarjan County, Isfahan Province, Iran.  At the 2006 census, its population was 7,670, in 2,003 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|10[/tpl]
