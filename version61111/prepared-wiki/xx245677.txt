[[Spanish Moon]]

CATEGORIES: 2004 racehorse births, Thoroughbred racehorses, Racehorses trained in the United Kingdom, Racehorses bred in Kentucky, Northern Dancer bloodline

Spanish Moon (foaled April 24, 2004) is an American-bred and British-trained Thoroughbred racehorse, owned by Khalid Abdullah. He became famous for running second in the Dubai Sheema Classic. His one group one win came in the Grand Prix de Saint-Cloud.
Due to unruly behavior at the starting stalls he was banned from racing in the United Kingdom for six months from May 2009.
