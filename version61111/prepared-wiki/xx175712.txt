[[Gmina Nieporęt]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Legionowo County

Gmina Nieporęt is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Legionowo County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Nieporęt, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|9|km|mi|0[/tpl] east of Legionowo and [tpl]convert|24|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|100.52|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 12,908.

==Villages==

Gmina Nieporęt contains the villages and settlements of Aleksandrów, Beniaminów, Białobrzegi, Izabelin, Józefów, Kąty Węgierskie, Michałów-Grabina, Nieporęt, Rembelszczyzna, Rynia, Stanisławów Drugi, Stanisławów Pierwszy, Wola Aleksandra, Wólka Radzymińska and Zegrze Południowe.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Nieporęt is bordered by Warsaw, by the towns of Legionowo and Marki, and by the gminas of Jabłonna, Radzymin, Serock and Wieliszew.
