[[Nomads Indians Saints]]

CATEGORIES: 1990 albums, Albums produced by Scott Litt, Epic Records albums, Indigo Girls albums, English-language albums, Albums certified gold by the Recording Industry Association of America

Nomads Indians Saints is the third studio album by Indigo Girls. Originally released in 1990, it was reissued and remastered in 2000 with three bonus tracks.
