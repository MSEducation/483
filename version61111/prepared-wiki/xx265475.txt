The Philippines Women's National Softball Team is the national team of Philippines. They were governed by the Amateur Softball Association of the Philippines (ASAPHIL). They won a bronze medal in 1970 ISF Women's World Championship in Osaka, Japan and its their first medal to win in World Championship.
The team competed at the 1990 ISF Women's World Championship in Normal, Illinois where they finished with 4 wins and 5 losses.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.isfsoftball.org/english/events/90_womens_wc.asp |title=1990 ISF Women's World Championship |publisher=International Softball Federation |date=2013 |accessdate=7 February 2014 |location=United States[/tpl]  The team competed at the 1998 ISF Women's World Championship in Fujinomiya City, Japan where they finished sixteenth.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.isfsoftball.org/english/events/98_women_wc.asp|title=1998 ISF Women's World Championship |publisher=International Softball Federation |date=2013 |accessdate=7 February 2014 |location=United States[/tpl]
