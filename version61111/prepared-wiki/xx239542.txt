[[Brad Bufanda]]

CATEGORIES: American male television actors, Living people, 1983 births, 20th-century American male actors, 21st-century American male actors

Brad Bufanda (born May 4, 1983) is an American actor. He is best known for the role of Larry Wilcox in the TV series Co-Ed Confidential.

==Biography==

===Personal life===

Brad was born in Upland, California. He attended El Toro High School and Mission Viejo High School in Orange County, California. Upon graduating he moved to Los Angeles, California. At the age of 23 he left Los Angeles in hopes of becoming a professional basketball player. In 2007 he squandered these plans after sustaining multiple injuries to his left knee and right ankle while playing at the college level. He currently lives in Orange County, California. Brad is rumored to have once been a rapper who went by the alias Bradley B.

===Career===

Brad began his acting career in 1994 with a Skechers Shoes commercial. Shortly after he appeared on the television series Roseanne and Hangin' with Mr. Cooper. At the age of 12 he starred in ABC's made for TV movie called The Secret of the Lizard Woman. Brad has appeared in a number of TV shows including Malcolm in the Middle, CSI: Miami, Even Stevens, and Days of our Lives. He played the character David in the film A Cinderella Story. Brad played the recurring character of Felix Toombs on the UPN series Veronica Mars. He starred in the Cinemax series Co-Ed Confidential from 2008-2010.
