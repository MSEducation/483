[[Richard E. Siferd]]

CATEGORIES: Living people

Richard Siferd, veteran,http://www.siferd.com/Attorneys/default.aspx was the 2006 Democratic candidate for Ohio's 4th congressional district. Siferd ran in a district that has not elected a Democrat since 1936. His opponent, Jim Jordan, won the seat 60% to 40%.
