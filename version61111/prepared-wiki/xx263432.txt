[[Destination Goa]]

CATEGORIES: Compilation album series, Electronic compilation albums, Psychedelic trance albums

Destination Goa is a series of compilation albums featuring goa and psychedelic trance. The albums were released between 1996 and 2002 in twelve parts, one of which was a best of album, by the Swedish record company Why Not Records. They featured music by artists such as Astral Projection, Hallucinogen, Miranda and Infected Mushroom.
