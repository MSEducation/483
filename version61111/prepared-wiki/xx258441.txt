[[Mbonambi Local Municipality]]

CATEGORIES: Local Municipalities of uThungulu

Mbonambi is an administrative area in the uThungulu District of KwaZulu-Natal in South Africa. The municipality is named after the Mbonambi Local Tribal Authority, which covers most of the municipal area.South African Languages - Place names
