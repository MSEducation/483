[[Wacky Waiters]]

CATEGORIES: 1982 video games, Commodore VIC-20 games, Platform games

Wacky Waiters is a [tpl]vgy|1982[/tpl] two-dimensional platform game for the Commodore VIC-20 home computer.

==Description==

Published by Imagine Software, Wacky Waiters was one of Imagine's more difficult games. Gameplay involved controlling a waiter from one side of the screen to the other and back again hopping in and out of lifts trying not to spill the drinks tray he was carrying to the beckoning customer on the opposite side of the screen.
