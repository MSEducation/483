[[Romodanovsky District]]

CATEGORIES: Districts of Mordovia

Romodanovsky District ([tpl]lang-ru|Ромода́новский райо́н[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-myv|Рамаданбуе[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-mdf|Ромодановань район[/tpl]) is an administrativeConstitution of the Republic of Mordovia and municipalLaw #99-Z district (raion), one of the twenty-two in the Republic of Mordovia, Russia. It is located in the eastern central part of the republic. The area of the district is [tpl]convert|820.8|km2|sp=us[/tpl].[tpl]citation needed|date=October 2012[/tpl] Its administrative center is the urban locality (an urban-type settlement) of Romodanovo.Law #7-Z Population: [tpl]ru-census2010|20,702|;[/tpl] 21,986 (2002 Census);[tpl]ru-pop-ref|2002Census[/tpl] [tpl]su-census1989|23,433|.[/tpl] The population of Romodanovo accounts for 45.5% of the district's total population.
