[[Evonne Hsu]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 births, Living people, American people of Taiwanese descent, Taiwanese female singers, Taiwanese Mandopop singers, People from Longview, Texas, Musicians from Texas

Evonne Hsu, also known as Hsu Hui-hsin ([tpl]zh|t=許慧欣[/tpl]) was born 5 December 1979, in Longview, Texas, United States. She is a Taiwanese American Mandopop singer. Hsu is managed by Music Nation Wingman Limited and signed by Universal Music Taiwan since her debut in 2002.

==Early life==

Evonne Hsu was born in Longview, Texas, United States.[tpl]zh icon[/tpl] Evonne Hsu's profile at baike.baidu.com Retrieved 2011-04-19  Hsu grew up with a love for singing and dancing but had chosen to major in psychology at the University of Texas at Austin when a musical arranger friend convinced her to make a demo tape of her singing.[tpl]fact|date=November 2012[/tpl]

==Career==

Hsu released her debut album To Be Happy in January 2002. To date, she has released eight albums and has also published a book.[tpl]Citation needed|date=June 2010[/tpl] On July 7, 2007 Hsu performed at the Chinese leg of Live Earth in Shanghai.[tpl]cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=xg4EAAAAMBAJ&pg=PA22&dq=%22Evonne+Hsu%22&as_pt=MAGAZINES&cd=2#v=onepage&q=%22Evonne%20Hsu%22&f=false|title=Planet Earth your guide to Live Earth's eight shows|date=7 July 2007|work=Billboard|page=22|accessdate=2 June 2010[/tpl]
