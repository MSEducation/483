[[Randwick City Saints AFC]]

CATEGORIES: Australian rules football clubs in Sydney, Australian rules football clubs in New South Wales, 2010 establishments in Australia

Randwick City Saints Australian Football Club (previously known as Saints AFC) is an Australian rules football club based in Sydney, Australia. The club colours are black, white and red. The Saints play in the third and fourth Divisions of the Sydney AFL league and also have a team in the Sydney Womens Division Two competition called the Angels and an over 35s side, the Maroubra Sinners
The Senior side has made the finals each year since joining the Sydney AFL comp and were runners up in 2013. The Reserve Grade side won the Division Five premiership in 2013 under captain/coach Peter Conduit
The Saints home ground is Pioneers Park located in the south-eastern Sydney suburb of Maroubra.
