[[Nechválova Polianka]]

CATEGORIES: Villages and municipalities in Humenné District

Nechválova Polianka is a village and municipality in Humenné District in the Prešov Region of north-east Slovakia.

==History==

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1547.

==Geography==

The municipality lies at an altitude of 320 metres and covers an area of 12.735 km².
It has a population of about 127 people.
