[[Marco Solari]]

CATEGORIES: Italian architects, Italian engineers, 1355 births, 1405 deaths

Marco Solari (c. 1355â€“1405) was an Italian architect and engineer.
He was born in Carona, in what today is the Swiss canton of Ticino, and was the father of the architect Giovanni Solari.
He worked at the  Duomo of Milan from 1387 and at the Certosa di Pavia from 1395. 
He was succeeded in Pavia by his son Giovanni Solari.
