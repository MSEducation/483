[[Estadio 10 de Diciembre]]

CATEGORIES: Football venues in Mexico

Estadio 10 de Diciembre is a multi-use stadium in the Mexican town of Jasso.  It is currently used mostly for football matches and is the home stadium of Cruz Azul Hidalgo and Cruz Azul Dulban .  The stadium holds 17,000 people and opened in 1963.http://www.worldstadiums.com/stadium_pictures/north_america/mexico/central_mexico/jasso_diciembre.shtml
