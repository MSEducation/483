==Biography==

Philip's mother is an Arab and his father an Iranian American. He spent most of his childhood in Chicago, Illinois. His family moved to Turlock, California in 1982. He spent his college years in Spokane, Washington before settling in Los Angeles, California in 1996.
Philip began his acting career in 2005 with a guest appearance on the critically acclaimed FX series, Over There. Since then, he has made guest appearances on shows such as The Unit, Californication, Grey's Anatomy, 24, and many more.
Philip is best known for his role as legendary Assassin, Altair, in Ubisoft's hit video game, Assassin's Creed.
