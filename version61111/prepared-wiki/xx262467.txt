[[Olovozavodskoy Bridge]]

CATEGORIES: Road bridges in Russia, Buildings and structures in Novosibirsk, Olovozavodskoy Bridge

The Olovozavodskoy Bridge ([tpl]lang-ru|Оловозаводско́й мост[/tpl]), also known as Bugrinsky Bridge ([tpl]lang-ru|Бугри́нский мост[/tpl]) is an automobile bridge over the Ob River which is currently being constructed in Novosibirsk, Russia. 
The construction of the bridge began in February 2010 and is expected to continue until 2014.Olovozavodskoy Bridge: A new hope news.ngs.ru [tpl]ru icon[/tpl] When completed, it will be the third bridge over the Ob River in the city of Novosibirsk.
