[[Zaghej]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Zaghej ([tpl]lang-fa|زاغج[/tpl], also Romanized as Zāghej; also known as Zāgheh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3088776|Zaghej[/tpl] is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, Bizineh Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 936, in 200 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
