[[Klistervatnet]]

CATEGORIES: Sør-Varanger, Lakes of Finnmark, Pechengsky District, Lakes of Murmansk Oblast, Norway–Russia border, International lakes of Europe

Klistervatnet is a lake that lies on the border between Norway and Russia. The [tpl]convert|17.2|km2|adj=on[/tpl] lake lies on the river Pasvikelva and it is about [tpl]convert|10|km[/tpl] long and [tpl]convert|2|km[/tpl] wide.
