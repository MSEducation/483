[[Bolivian legislative election, 1962]]

CATEGORIES: Elections in Bolivia, 1962 elections in South America, 1962 in Bolivia

Parliamentary elections were held in Bolivia on 4 June 1962.Nohlen, D (2005) Elections in the Americas: A data handbook, Volume II, p133 ISBN 978-0-19-928358-3 The Revolutionary Nationalist Movement (MNR) received 85% of the vote,Nohlen, p142 and retained its large majority in both houses of Congress.
