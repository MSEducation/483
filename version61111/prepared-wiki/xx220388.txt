[[Banana Support Programme]]

CATEGORIES: Economy of Jamaica, Banana production, European Union and agriculture

The European Union's Banana Support Programme is a financial support program for the Jamaican banana industry. Since 1999 the program has provided more than €40 million in aid to the industry, with the goals of supporting diversification where possible and improving the competitiveness of banana producers in an increasingly liberal market.[tpl]cite web|author=Jamaican Ministry of Agriculture|publisher=European Commission: External cooperation programmes|year=2007|accessdate=20 August 2008|title=Grant for Social and Economic Infrastructure in the Traditional Banana Growing Communities of Jamaica|url=http://ec.europa.eu/europeaid/tender/data/d53/AOF86753.doc|format=doc[/tpl]
