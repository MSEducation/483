[[Tebaieha Bible College]]

CATEGORIES: Schools in the Solomon Islands, Rennell and Bellona Islands

Tebaieha Bible College is a Bible college in the Solomon Islands, on Rennell Island in the Rennell and Bellona province.

==Location==

The college is approximately 50 km east (2¾ hours drive) from Tigoa and less than 1 km from Lake Tegno. It is about ¾ km west of Tebaitahe village.

==Religion==

Christian: South Sea Evangelical Church

==Police==

Generally policing is serviced by the Tigoa police station as well as a local area constable employed by the provincial government.
