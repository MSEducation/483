[[Gmina Załuski]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Masovian Voivodeship, Płońsk County

Gmina Załuski is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Płońsk County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Załuski, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|17|km|mi|0[/tpl] south-east of Płońsk and [tpl]convert|46|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] north-west of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|111.65|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 5,431.

==Villages==

Gmina Załuski contains the villages and settlements of Falbogi Wielkie, Gostolin, Kamienica, Kamienica-Wygoda, Karolinowo, Koryciska, Kroczewo, Michałówek, Naborowiec, Naborowo, Niepiekła, Nowe Olszyny, Nowe Wrońska, Przyborowice Dolne, Przyborowice Górne, Sadówiec, Słotwin, Smólska, Sobole, Stare Olszyny, Stare Wrońska, Stróżewo, Szczytniki, Szczytno, Wilamy, Wojny, Załuski, Zdunowo and Złotopolice.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Załuski is bordered by the gminas of Czerwińsk nad Wisłą, Joniec, Naruszewo, Płońsk and Zakroczym.
