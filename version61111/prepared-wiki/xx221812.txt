[[Anona (song)]]

CATEGORIES: 1903 songs

"Anona" is a popular song written by Vivian Grey (pseudonym for Mabel McKinley, niece of William McKinley[ref]Edison, Edison Amberola Monthly, p. 6: "... Vivian Grey (Miss Mabel McKinley, niece of the 
late President McKinley), the composer of 'Anona'."[/ref]) in 1903, both as an intermezzoGrey, "Anona" (Intermezzo-Two Step). and a vocal number. Popular in its day, it was recorded a number of times.
