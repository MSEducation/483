[[Spring mot Ulla, spring! Cornelis sjunger Bellman]]

CATEGORIES: Cornelis Vreeswijk albums, 1971 albums, Swedish folk music

Spring mot Ulla, spring! Cornelis sjunger Bellman (English: Run to Ulla, run! Cornelis sings Bellman) is a studio album by the Swedish-Dutch folk singer-songwriter Cornelis Vreeswijk. The album contains an unconventional presentation of Carl Michael Bellman songs and was a commercial success for Vreeswijk.Album info on www.cornelis.se

==Track listing==

All songs by Carl Michael Bellman
