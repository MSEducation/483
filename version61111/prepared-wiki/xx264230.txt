[[Chickney]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Essex, Uttlesford, Civil parishes in Essex

Chickney is a village and a civil parish on the B1051 road, near the village of Broxted, in the Uttlesford district, in the county of Essex, England. St Mary's Church in the village is grade I listed, and is a redundant church in the care of the Churches Conservation Trust.http://www.essexchurches.info/church.asp?p=Chickney
