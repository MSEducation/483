[[KVWG-FM]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in San Antonio, Texas, Univision Radio Network stations

KVWG-FM (95.3 FM) is a radio station  broadcasting a Classic Country format. Licensed to Dilley, Texas, USA, the station serves the San Antonio area. The station is currently owned by Univision Radio License Corporation. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KVWG-FM |title=KVWG-FM Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==History==

The station was assigned the call letters KVWG-FM on 1983-04-25.  On 2001-03-02, the station changed its call sign to KRNX, on 2001-03-09 to KMFR, then on 2001-04-17 to the current KVWG.[tpl]cite web |url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=52047&Callsign=KVWG |title=KVWG Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division[/tpl]
According to FCC filings, the station (then owned by Pearshall RadioWorks) ceased operations on May 31, 2006 due to "a number of equipment-related problems" and to improve the antenna.  In 2007, after the FCC approved the new antenna's operations, the station was sold to Univision, the current owner.
