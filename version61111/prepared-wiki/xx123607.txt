[[Winecoff House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Florida, National Register of Historic Places in Alachua County, Florida, Houses in Alachua County, Florida

The Winecoff House (also known as the Winecoff Hotel, Central Hotel or Carter Hall) is a U.S. historic building in Micanopy, Florida, at 102 Northeast Seminary Avenue. On February 14, 2002, it was added to the U.S. National Register of Historic Places.
