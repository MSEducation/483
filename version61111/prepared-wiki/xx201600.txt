Anton Ponkrashov (born April 23, 1986 in Leningrad, Soviet Union) is a Russian professional basketball player  for PBC Lokomotiv-Kuban of the Russian Professional Basketball League (PBL). He is 2.00 m (6 ft 6 ¾ in) in height and weighs 220 pounds. He can play as a swingman and as a point guard.

==Professional career==

Ponkrashov joined Pulkovo during the 2001-02 season. He moved to LenVo Saint Petersburg for the 2002-03 season. He then moved to Spartak St. Petersburg for the 2004-05 season. He then joined CSKA Moscow for the 2006-07 season. He then moved to Khimki Moscow Region for the 2007-08 season and he returned to CSKA Moscow before the 2009-10 season.

==Russian national team==

Ponkrashov is also a member of the senior Russian national basketball team. With the Russian national team he won the gold medal at the 2007 FIBA European Championship.
