[[Serena Amato]]

CATEGORIES: 1974 births, Living people, People from Vicente López Partido, Argentine yacht racers, Olympic sailors of Argentina, Sailors at the 1996 Summer Olympics – Europe, Sailors at the 2000 Summer Olympics – Europe, Sailors at the 2004 Summer Olympics – Europe, Olympic bronze medalists for Argentina, Olympic medalists in sailing

Serena Bibiana Amato (born September 10, 1974 in Olivos, Buenos Aires) is a sailor from Argentina, who won a bronze medal at the 2000 Summer Olympics in Sydney.
