[[Floreffe]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Belgium, Municipalities of Namur (province)

Floreffe ([tpl]IPA-fr|flɔ.ʁɛf[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-wa|Florefe[/tpl]) is a Walloon municipality located in the Belgian province of Namur. On 1 January 2012 the municipality had 7,883 inhabitants. The total area is 38.89 km², giving a population density of 203 inhabitants per km².
Floreffe is best known as the location of Floreffe Abbey, founded in the 12th century and suppressed during the French Revolution.
