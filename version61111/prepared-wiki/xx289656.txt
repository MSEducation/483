[[Sarin Darreh, Khodabandeh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Sarin Darreh ([tpl]lang-fa|سرين دره[/tpl], also Romanized as Sarīn Darreh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3082834|Sarin Darreh[/tpl] is a village in Aq Bolagh Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 243, in 36 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
