Vladislav Gennadyevich Yuzhakov ([tpl]lang-ru|Владисла́в Генна́дьевич Южако́в[/tpl]; born January 25, 1986 in Chusovoy, Perm Krai), or Vladislav Jurzhakov, is a Russian luger who has competed since 1995. His best Luge World Cup finish was 15th in men's doubles in 2007–08.
Yuzhakov qualified for the 2010 Winter Olympics where he finished tenth.
