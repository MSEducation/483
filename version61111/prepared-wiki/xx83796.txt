[[Ángel Serafín Seriche Dougan]]

CATEGORIES: 1946 births, Living people, Democratic Party of Equatorial Guinea politicians

Ángel Serafín Seriche Dougan (born 1946) was Prime Minister of Equatorial Guinea from April 1, 1996 to March 4, 2001.
Beginning in 2000, Dougan's government was subjected to serious criticism from the parliamentary majority with regard to allegations of corruption. This led to what was described as an "institutional crisis", and Dougan resigned on February 23, 2001. President Teodoro Obiang Nguema appointed Cándido Muatetema Rivas to succeed him on February 26."New Prime Minister appointed in Equatorial Guinea", afrol News, February 26, 2001.
