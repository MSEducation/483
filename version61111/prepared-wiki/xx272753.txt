[[Pascal Berger]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 births, Living people, SC Bern players, Swiss ice hockey players

Pascal Berger (born March 24, 1989) is a Swiss professional ice hockey player who is currently playing for the SC Bern in Switzerland's National League A. 

==Personal==

Pascal's brother Alain Berger plays hockey in North America with the Montreal Canadiens organization.Eliteprospects.com - Alain Berger
