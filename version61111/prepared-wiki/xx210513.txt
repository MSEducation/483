[[Dietrich Jells]]

CATEGORIES: 1972 births, Living people, People from Brooklyn, Pittsburgh Panthers football players, American football wide receivers, New England Patriots players, Philadelphia Eagles players, Players of American football from New York

Dietrich Jells (born April 11, 1972 in Erie, PA) is a retired American football wide receiver. Jells played in the NFL from 1996-1999. In his first two seasons, Jells was a member of the New England Patriots after being drafted out of the University of Pittsburgh by the Kansas City Chiefs in the 1996 NFL Draft. With the Patriots, Jells appeared in Super Bowl XXXI, a Patriots loss. In 1998-99, Jells played with the Philadelphia Eagles.Dietrich Jells pro-football-reference.com
