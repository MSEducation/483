[[Dennis railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1924

Dennis railway station is located on the Hurstbridge line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the north-eastern Melbourne suburb of Northcote opening on 4 February 1924.Dennis Vicsig

==Platforms & services==
