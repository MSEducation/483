[[Bjorn Ruytinx]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 births, Living people, Belgian footballers, Belgian Pro League players, Belgian Second Division footballers, Oud-Heverlee Leuven players

Bjorn 'El Toro' Ruytinx (born 18 August 1980) is a Belgian professional football player who currently plays for Oud-Heverlee Leuven as a forward.Voetbal International profile As of the 2014-15 season, Ruytinx will play for Oostende after signing a two-year contract in March 2014.
With OH Leuven, he enjoyed a first promotion to the Belgian Second Division in 2005 and a second promotion to the Belgian Pro League in 2011.[tpl]cite web|publisher=Stampmedia |title=I will prove I can handle the first division level |url=http://www.stampmedia.be/?p=16479 |language=Dutch |accessdate=2011-05-21[/tpl] After staying with the team for a decade (and becoming a club-icon in the process), Ruytinx finally moved to Oostende in 2014.
