[[Esfanaj]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Esfanaj ([tpl]lang-fa|اسفناج[/tpl], also Romanized as Esfanāj and Esfenāj; also known as Isfanaj and Ispanag)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3062150|Esfanaj[/tpl] is a village in Zanjanrud-e Pain Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 426, in 104 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
