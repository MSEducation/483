[[Nagiso, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Nagiso|南木曽町|Nagiso-machi[/tpl] is a town located in Kiso District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the town has an estimated population of 5,458 and a density of 25.27 persons per km². The total area is [tpl]Convert|215.96|km2|mi2|2|abbr=on[/tpl].
Nagiso is a local railway station on the Chūō Main Line, and is the nearest rail access to the restored Meiji-era town of Tsumago, Nagano Prefecture.
