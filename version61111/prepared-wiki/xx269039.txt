[[Bogert's coral snake]]

CATEGORIES: Elapidae, Micrurus, Animals described in 1967, Reptiles of Mexico, Endemic fauna of Mexico

Bogert's coral snake (Micrurus bogerti) is a species of elapid snake, native to the Pacific coast of Oaxaca in Mexico. There are no recognized subspecies.[tpl]ITIS |id=585941|taxon=Micrurus bogerti|accessdate=24 September 2011[/tpl]
