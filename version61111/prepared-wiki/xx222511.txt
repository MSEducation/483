[[Gary Newton]]

CATEGORIES: 1957 births, American field hockey players, Sportspeople from British Columbia, American people of Canadian descent, Canadian field hockey players, Living people

Gary Basil Newton (born December 12, 1957 in British Columbia, Canada) was an American field hockey player. He competed for the Americans at the 1984 Summer Olympics. His brother Michael was one of his teammates at the Olympics.
