[[Grimascher och telegram]]

CATEGORIES: Cornelis Vreeswijk albums, 1965 albums, Swedish-language albums

Grimascher och telegram (English: Grimaces and telegram) is the third studio album by the Swedish-Dutch folk singer-songwriter Cornelis Vreeswijk. It is a more jazz-oriented album.Album info on www.cornelis.se

==Track listing==

Music and lyrics by Cornelis Vreeswijk unless otherwise noted
