[[Jennifer Metcalfe]]

CATEGORIES: 1983 births, Living people, People from Bradford, Actresses from Yorkshire, English television actresses, English soap opera actresses

Jennifer Joanne Metcalfe (born 4 September 1983) is an English actress. She joined the cast of soap opera Hollyoaks in June 2006, playing Mercedes McQueen.

==Personal life==

Metcalfe was born and brought up in Holme Wood in Bradford, West Yorkshire,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.thetelegraphandargus.co.uk/news/2149079.make_me_the_mcqueen_of_soaps/|title='Make me the McQueen of soaps!'|last=Black|first=Michael|date=27 March 2008|work=Bradford Telegraph and Argus|accessdate=19 August 2010|archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/5s6Ka4IVZ|archivedate=19 August 2010[/tpl] and has been acting since age 12.[tpl]citation needed|date=February 2011[/tpl] She attended the Scala Kids drama school in her hometown. Metcalfe did a degree in Health and Fitness before proceeding with an acting career.[tpl]citation needed|date=February 2011[/tpl]
In between acting jobs, Metcalfe worked as a fitness instructor in Bradford. She appeared in the sixth series of ITV's Dancing on Ice, partnered with Sylvain Longchambon.
She co-owns the clothing rental boutique, The Closet, in Liverpool with Hollyoaks co-stars Claire Cooper and Leah Hackett,[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/tvshowbiz/article-1302097/Denise-Van-Outen-shops-Coleen-Rooneys-favourite-boutique.html?ito=feeds-newsxml|title=Denise Van Outen shops at Coleen Rooney's favourite boutique|date=3 August 2010|work=Mail Online|accessdate=19 August 2010|archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/5s6K7mrvC|archivedate=19 August 2010[/tpl] which opened in April 2010.
She dated ex-Geordie Shore star Greg Lake in 2013, and it was reported that they split, but they holidayed in Dubai in 2014.http://www.dailymail.co.uk/tvshowbiz/article-2594980/Jennifer-Metcalfe-shows-super-slim-waist-black-cut-bikini-holidays-Greg-Lake-Dubai.html

==Career==

Metcalfe has appeared in several television roles including children's television series My Parents Are Aliens and ITV dramas Where The Heart Is, At Home with the Braithwaites and soap opera Emmerdale.
