[[Hajj Siran]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Hajj Siran ([tpl]lang-fa|حاج سيران[/tpl], also Romanized as Ḩājj Sīrān; also known as Gol Sīrān, Ḩājjī Sīrān, Hajeiseran, Ḩājjsīrān, and Khadzheseyran)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3065630|Hajj Siran[/tpl] is a village in Qareh Poshtelu-e Pain Rural District, Qareh Poshtelu District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 383, in 83 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
