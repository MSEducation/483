[[John Burgoyne (MP for Cambridgeshire)]]

CATEGORIES: 16th-century English people, People of the Tudor period, People from Cambridgeshire, Members of the Parliament of England (pre-1707)

John Burgoyne (died c.1435), of Dry Drayton, Cambridgeshire, was an English politician.

==Family==

Burgoyne married and had one daughter and five sons, including the MP, Thomas Burgoyne.

==Career==

He was a Member (MP) of the Parliament of England for Cambridgeshire in May 1413, 1419, December 1421 and 1433.http://www.historyofparliamentonline.org/volume/1386-1421/member/burgoyne-john-1435
