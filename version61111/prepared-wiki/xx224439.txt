[[Dion Martinez]]

CATEGORIES: 1837 births, 1928 deaths, Cuban chess players, American chess players

Dion M. Martinez (26 June 1837 – 11 March 1928) was a Cuban–American chess master.http://www.sport-stat.ru/chess/players.php?id=83816
He was regarded as one of the strongest chess players in Philadelphia. There he played several matches against James Mason (4 : 5 in 1874 and 3 : 1 in 1875), Wilhelm Steinitz (0 : 7 in November 1882, 2.5 : 4.5 in December 1882, and 1 : 10 in 1883), and Johannes Zukertort (3.5 : 9.5 in 1884).http://www.chessmetrics.com
He took 9th at Philadelphia 1876 (Fourth American Chess Congress, Mason won),http://xoomer.alice.it/cserica/scacchi/storiascacchi/tornei/1851-99/1876philadelphia.htm and tied for 17-18th at New York 1889 (Sixth American CC, Max Weiss and Mikhail Chigorin won).http://xoomer.alice.it/cserica/scacchi/storiascacchi/tornei/1851-99/1889ny.htm His pen-portrait (jointly with other participants of the tournament) was published on page 8 of the New York Times, 16 June 1889, under the heading "The Chessboard Kings".http://www.chesshistory.com/winter/winter49.html
