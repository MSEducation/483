[[Kenny Burrell Volume 2]]

CATEGORIES: Blue Note Records albums, Kenny Burrell albums, 1956 albums, Albums recorded at Van Gelder Studio

Kenny Burrell Volume 2 is an album by American jazz guitarist Kenny Burrell recorded in 1956 and released on the Blue Note label.Blue Note Records discography accessed January 2, 2011 In 2000, it was released on the 2 CD-set Introducing Kenny Burrell: The First Blue Note Sessions along with Introducing Kenny Burrell, plus bonus tracks.

==Track listing==

Recorded on March 12 (tracks 3-7), May 29 (track 1) and May 30 (track 2), 1956.
