[[Libramont-Chevigny]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Belgium, Municipalities of Luxembourg (Belgium)

Libramont-Chevigny is a Walloon municipality of Belgium located in the province of Luxembourg. 
On 1 January 2007 the municipality, which covers 177.86 km², had 9,981 inhabitants, giving a population density of 56.1 inhabitants per km².
