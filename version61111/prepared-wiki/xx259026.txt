[[Mother (1999 film)]]

CATEGORIES: 1999 films, Indian films, Hindi-language films

Mother is an Indian comedy film released on 15 October 1999, based on the stage musical Mamma Mia!. The film stars Rekha as a woman who's unsure which of three former lovers (played by Rakesh Roshan, Jeetendra and Randhir Kapoor) is the father of her adult daughter (Sanober Kabir in her acting debut) and must deal with all three men coming to attend the daughter's wedding.
