[[Bart Exposito]]

CATEGORIES: 1970 births, Living people, 20th-century American painters, 21st-century American painters, People from Texas

Bart Exposito (born 1970, Texas, US) is an artist based in Los Angeles. He paints geometric images in a graphic design style.
Exposito’s work has appeared in a number of shows including “Game Over” at Galerie Grimm/Rosenfeld in Munich and “Wake Up & Apologise” at the Hayworth Gallery, Los Angeles.  He has exhibited internationally at galleries such as Studio 9, London and Galerie Fur Gegenwarstkunst, Bremen.  He is represented by Black Dragon Society in Los Angeles.
