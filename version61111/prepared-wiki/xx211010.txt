[[Patnam Rajender Reddy Memorial Engineering College]]

CATEGORIES: Engineering colleges in Andhra Pradesh

Patnam Rajender Reddy Memorial Engineering College (PRRM) is an engineering college in the village of Shabad Mandal, Rangareddy district, Andhra Pradesh, India. The college is [tpl]convert|55|km|mi[/tpl] from Mehidipatnam, Hyderabad, and lies between Chevella and Shadnagar on the national highway. 
The institute integrates classroom learning with work experience in the industry or in the concerned professional field.  During the first year, stress is laid on development of manual skills, communication skills, courses on general education and an introduction to engineering. The institute also encourages students to undergo practical training during the summer vacations.
Patnam Rajender Reddy Memorial Engineering College was founded in November 1998. The first graduates, in 2002, had a pass percentage of 98.6%.[tpl]Citation needed|date=August 2008[/tpl] The college is affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad.http://jntuhaac.com/admntemplate.php?code=61http://www.prrm.co.in/about.html PRRM Engineering College is sponsored by the Patnam Rajender Reddy Memorial Engineering College  Educational Society, registered under the societies act. The chairman is Dr. Mahender Reddy, an MLA from Tandur.
The college has been approved by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi (vide Lr no: 730-5-276/E/ET/98, dated 9 June 1998) and approved by the government of Andhra Pradesh (vide G.O. Ms no:286, dated 26 August 1998).
