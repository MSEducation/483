[[Jean-Christophe Ravier]]

CATEGORIES: 1979 births, Living people, French racing drivers, Formula Ford drivers, French Formula Three Championship drivers, French Formula Renault 2.0 drivers, Eurocup Formula Renault 2.0 drivers, American Le Mans Series drivers, People from Avignon

Jean-Christophe Ravier (born April 10, 1979 in Avignon) is a French racing driver. He has competed in such series as World Series by Nissan and Formula Renault 2000 Eurocup.
