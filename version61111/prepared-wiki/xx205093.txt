[[Kosa Dvukh Pilotov Island]]

CATEGORIES: Islands of the Chukchi Sea, Islands of Chukotka Autonomous Okrug, Spits of Russia

Kosa Dvukh Pilotov Island (коса Двух Пилотов, "Two Pilots' Sandspit") is a long and narrow island in the Chukchi Sea. It is located along the coast of the Chukotka Peninsula in the Chukotka Autonomous Okrug.
This island is [tpl]convert|52.5|km|mi|abbr=on[/tpl] in length but only [tpl]convert|1|km|mi|abbr=on[/tpl] wide on average. It is a long extended bar or spit between the Chukchi Sea and the lagoons of Amguema (Амгуэма) and Tynkergynpil'gyn (Тынкэргынпильгын), coastal lakes which are frozen most of the year.
The southeastern section of this island is also known as "Ostrov Dalstroy" after the Soviet organization Dalstroy[tpl]Verify credibility|date=September 2011[/tpl].
