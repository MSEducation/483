[[Alencheh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Khodabandeh County

Alencheh ([tpl]lang-fa|النچه[/tpl]; also known as Alenjeh, Alīnjeh, Alundzha, Alūnjeh, and Alunjeh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3052713|Alencheh[/tpl] is a village in Aq Bolagh Rural District, Sojas Rud District, Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 371, in 72 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
