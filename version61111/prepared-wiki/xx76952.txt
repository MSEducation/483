[[Hârlău]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Romania, Populated places in Iaşi County

Hârlău (also spelled Hîrlău, [tpl]IPA-ro|hɨrˈləw[/tpl]; [tpl]lang-he|חרלאו[/tpl], [tpl]lang-de|Harlau[/tpl], [tpl]lang-hu|Harló[/tpl]) is a town in Iaşi County, Moldavia, Romania. [tpl]As of|2004[/tpl] the estimated population is 11,271. It was the capital city of Moldova in the 15th century. One village, Pârcovaci, is administered by the town.

==Population==

The 2011 census counted 10,055 inhabitants, 93.85% Romanians and 6.06% Roma. A large Jewish community used to live in Hârlău.
