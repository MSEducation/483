[[Nilgiri Christian Guest Homes Association]]

CATEGORIES: Christian organisations based in India

The Nilgiri Christian Guest Homes Association is a Protestant trust of uncertain origins that was set up to run guest houses in the scenic Nilgiri Hills or blue mountains of Southern India.[tpl]cite book|title=The National Christian Council Review|volume=99|year=1979|publisher=Wesley Press|location=Mysore|author=National Christian Council of India|page=178[/tpl] It is a non-profit venture that provides places of rest and relaxation to Evangelical Protestant missionaries  on the field.
