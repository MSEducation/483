[[9711 Želetava]]

CATEGORIES: Main Belt asteroids, Astronomical objects discovered in 1972

9711 Želetava is a main belt asteroid with an orbital period of 1874.7159610 days (5.13 years).[tpl]cite web | url = http://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=9711 | title = JPL Small-Body Database Browser | accessdate = 2008-05-24 | publisher = NASA[/tpl] 
The asteroid was discovered on August 7, 1972.
