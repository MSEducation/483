[[Geumjeong Station]]

CATEGORIES: Seoul subway stations, Metro stations in Gyeonggi Province, Railway stations opened in 1988, Gunpo

Geumjeong Station is a ground-level metro station on lines 1 and 4 of the Seoul Subway network in South Korea.  The station is in Gunpo, a city approximately [tpl]convert|15|km|mi|abbr=on[/tpl] south of Seoul in Gyeonggi Province, between the cities of Anyang and Suwon.
Geumjeong is an important transfer point for passengers travelling to and from southern and western Gyeonggi Province to Seoul Grand Park and Seoul Racecourse Park. The tracks are aligned to allow passengers to transfer between lines 1 and 4 on the same platform.
