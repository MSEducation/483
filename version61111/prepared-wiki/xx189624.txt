Oleksiy Khramtsov ([tpl]lang-uk|Олексій Володимирович Храмцов[/tpl]) (born 8 November 1975, Simferopol, Ukrainian SSR, Soviet Union) is a professional Ukrainian football defender who played for different clubs in the Ukrainian Premier League. He is plays currently for the club of Navbahor Namangan in the Uzbek League as captain.
