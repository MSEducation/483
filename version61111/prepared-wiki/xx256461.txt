[[Camden Cats Senior Australian Football Club]]

CATEGORIES: Australian rules football clubs in Sydney, Australian rules football clubs in New South Wales

Camden Cats Senior Australian Football Club is an Australian rules football club based in the Sydney suburb of Harrington Park. The club colours are blue and white and they are nicknamed the Cats. Camden has teams in Divisions Two and Five of the Sydney AFL league and in 2013 will be fielding an U18 team .http://www.sportingpulse.com/assoc_page.cgi?client=1-1047-0-0-0&sID=95132&&news_task=DETAIL&articleID=14495549&sectionID=95132
