[[Aspidosperma subincanum]]

CATEGORIES: Aspidosperma, Trees of Brazil

Aspidosperma subincanum is a timber tree native to Brazil, which is typical of Cerrado vegetation. It was first described by Carl Friedrich Philipp von Martius.[tpl]IPNI|id=77465-1|accessdate=14 November 2010[/tpl]
