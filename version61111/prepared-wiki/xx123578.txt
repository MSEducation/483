[[Brush Ground]]

CATEGORIES: Cricket grounds in Leicestershire, Sport in Loughborough

The Brush Ground is a cricket stadium located in the town of Loughborough, Leicestershire. It has, in the past, been used by Leicestershire as an outground and has held 16 first class games in total.  The first game took place in 1953 against Hampshire and the last in 1965 against Middlesex.
