[[Hellbound (Nekromantix album)]]

CATEGORIES: Nekromantix albums, 1989 debut albums

Hellbound is the debut album by the Danish psychobilly band the Nekromantix, released in 1989 by Tombstone Records. After a few initial performances the band played a large festival in Hamburg, Germany and were offered the recording contract which resulted in this album.[ref]
</ref>

==Track listing==

All songs written by Gaarde/Sandorff
