[[Nottinghamshire Cricket Board Premier League]]

CATEGORIES: English domestic cricket competitions, Cricket in Nottinghamshire, ECB Premier Leagues

The Nottinghamshire Cricket Board Premier League is the top level of competition for recreational club cricket in Nottinghamshire, England, and is a designated ECB Premier League.List of ECB Premier Leagues
It has two feeder leagues serving the North and South of the county:
Since its inception, the league has been dominated by West Indian Cavaliers, who have been champions in nine of the fifteen seasons of competition.
The league attracts many top players, notable stars include Alex Tudor, Saqlain Mushtaq, Usman Afzaal and Bilal Shafayat.
The competing teams in 2014 are: Caythorpe, Clifton Village, Cuckney, Kimberley Institute, Mansfield Hosiery Mills, Nottinghamshire CCC Academy, Ordsall Bridon, Plumtree, Radcliffe on Trent, Rolls Royce Leisure, Welbeck Colliery, and West Indian Cavaliers.
