[[Thyou Department]]

CATEGORIES: Departments of Burkina Faso, Boulkiemdé Province

Thyrou is a department or commune of Boulkiemdé Province in central Burkina Faso. As of 2005 it has a population of 24,487. Its capital lies at the town of Thyrou.Burkinabé government inforoute communale
