[[Hussein Naeem]]

CATEGORIES: Lebanese footballers, 1987 births, 2007 deaths, Nejmeh SC players

Hussain Naeem (born on March 19, 1987 in Lebanon - died on June 13, 2007 in Beirut, Lebanon) was an association football player who died in a car bomb outside the Al Manara Stadium which killed Lebanese politician Walid Eido. He was always a Nejmeh player. He joined in 2001.

==Football career==

Naeem was a youth player with Nejmeh. He signed for them on October 26, 2001. He grew into a regular first-team player.[tpl]cn|date=April 2014[/tpl]

==Death==

Hussein Naeem died from the same car bomb which killed politician Walid Eido.Daily Star: March 14 MP Walid Eido assassinated in Beirut bombing, 2007-06-14 He died along with Nejmeh teammate Hussein Dokmak.
