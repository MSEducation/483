[[Little Sampford]]

CATEGORIES: Villages in Essex, Uttlesford, Civil parishes in Essex

Little Sampford is a village and a civil parish on the B1053 road, in the Uttlesford district, in the county of Essex, England. Until the 18th century the ecclesiastical Parish was known as St Mary the Virgin, Sampford Parva.[tpl]cn|date=September 2013[/tpl]
