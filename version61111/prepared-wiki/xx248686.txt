[[Henry Meaden]]

CATEGORIES: 1862 births, English cricketers, Hampshire cricketers, Year of death missing, People from Galle, English people of Sri Lankan descent

Henry J.B. Meaden (born in 1862 in Point de Galle, Ceylon; date of death unknown) was an English cricketer. 
Meaden represented Hampshire in three first-class match in 1881, making his debut against Sussex. Meaden represented the county in two more games, against Sussex again and lastly the Marylebone Cricket Club.
