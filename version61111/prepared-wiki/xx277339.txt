[[Eveland Christian College]]

CATEGORIES: Universities and colleges in the Philippines

Eveland Christian College is a religious college located at Barangay IV, San Mateo, 3318, Isabela, Philippines. It began as Eveland Memorial College in 1947, named after William Perry Eveland. It was renamed Eveland Junior College in the 1970s, and renamed again in 2000 to its current name.[tpl]cite web|url=http://ph.wowcity.com/isabela/sanmateo/gpid/109755657277388378198/eveland-christian-college.htm|work=Wow City|accessdate=26 September 2013[/tpl]
