[[Scottish Stock Exchange]]

CATEGORIES: Stock exchanges in Europe, 1964 establishments in Scotland, 1973 disestablishments in Scotland

The Scottish Stock Exchange was a stock exchange which started trading on 2 January 1964 following a merger of the four main stock exchanges in Scotland: the Edinburgh Stock Exchange, Glasgow Stock Exchange, Dundee Stock Exchange, and Aberdeen Stock Exchange.[tpl]cite news|work=The Herald|date=5 December 1963|url=http://news.google.com/newspapers?id=c1xAAAAAIBAJ&sjid=pKMMAAAAIBAJ&dq=scottish%20stock%20exchange&pg=2954%2C512884|title=Statement Soon on Stock Exchange[/tpl] The main secretariat of the exchange was in Glasgow, while the Edinburgh, Dundee, and Aberdeen exchanges operated as local branches[tpl]cite news|work=The Herald|date=30 April 1963|url=http://news.google.com/newspapers?id=uGxAAAAAIBAJ&sjid=kaMMAAAAIBAJ&dq=scottish-stock-exchange&pg=4493%2C4607990|title=All Deals Marked[/tpl] until 1971, when the local exchanges closed completely.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.dundee.ac.uk/archives/ms069.htm|title=Archives: MS 69 (University of Dundee)|accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl]
During the construction of the new Scottish Stock Exchange building in St George's Place, Glasgow, the exchange was housed in a converted warehouse on Ingram Street. The new building was opened after two years' work on 13 April 1971.[tpl]cite news|work=Evening Times|date=12 April 1971|url=http://news.google.com/newspapers?id=-exAAAAAIBAJ&sjid=UKcMAAAAIBAJ&dq=scottish-stock-exchange&pg=4084%2C5890718|title=Stock exchange latest-it's luxury![/tpl]
In 1973, the Scottish Stock Exchange merged into the London Stock Exchange.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.heraldscotland.com/news/home-news/calls-for-scotland-to-have-its-own-stock-exchange-1.1045699|title=Calls for Scotland to have its own stock exchange|date=4 August 2010|accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl]
Since then, there have been calls to re-establish an independent stock exchange in Scotland.[tpl]cite web|url=http://uk.reuters.com/article/2009/12/22/uk-markets-europe-scotland-analysis-idUKTRE5BL32K20091222|title=Limited buy-in for Scottish stock exchange|date=22 December 2009|accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl]
