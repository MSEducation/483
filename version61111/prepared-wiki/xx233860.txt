[[Banu Zayd]]

CATEGORIES: Tribes of Arabia

Banu Zayd or Bani Zaid or Bany Zaid (in Arabic  بني زيد  ) is a Nejdi tribe that traces its roots to Zayd of Quda'a who settled Shaqraa in Najd.Alaqd Alfareed Fe Nasab Al Haraqees Men Bani Zayd, Sheikh Abdullah Al mune'aBanu Zayd, al-qabilah al-Quda 'iyah fi hadirat Najd by Shuqayr, Abd al-Rahman ibn Abd Allah. Riyad : al-Mu'allif, 2002.
