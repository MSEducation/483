[[Benedict Jablonski]]

CATEGORIES: Science fiction fans, 1910s births, 2003 deaths

Benedict Jablonski, aka Ben Jason(c. 1917 â€“ May 15, 2003) was a longtime science fiction fan and booster who co-designed the Hugo Award based on a rocket-shaped hood ornament from an Oldsmobile 88.  He was also chairman of the 1966 World Science Fiction Convention in Cleveland, Ohio at which Gene Roddenberry previewed the two-hour pilot of his new series, "Star Trek", for science fiction fans.
He died May 15, 2003 in Ohio at the age of 86.
