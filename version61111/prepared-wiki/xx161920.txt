[[From the Screen to Your Stereo Part II]]

CATEGORIES: 2007 albums, New Found Glory albums, Covers albums, Drive-Thru Records albums, Sequel albums

From the Screen to Your Stereo Part II is a cover album by American band New Found Glory, and is the follow-up from the 2000 EP From the Screen to Your Stereo. All the tracks are covers of songs from motion picture soundtracks. The album was released September 18, 2007.
