[[Duipipal]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Nuwakot District

Duipipal is a village development committee in Nuwakot District in the Bagmati Zone of central Nepal. At the time of the 1991 Nepal census it had a population of 5788 living in 1052 individual households.[tpl]Citation| url=http://www.digitalhimalaya.com/collections/nepalcensus/form.php?selection=1|title=Nepal Census 2001|magazine=Nepal's Village Development Committees|publisher=Digital Himalaya|accessdate=15 November 2009| isbn=92-9115-782-1 [/tpl].
