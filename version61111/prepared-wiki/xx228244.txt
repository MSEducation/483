[[Man Kam To Control Point]]

CATEGORIES: North District, Hong Kong, China–Hong Kong border crossings, Closed Area

Man Kam To Control Point ([tpl]zh|t=文錦渡管制站[/tpl]) is a land immigration control point in Man Kam To, North District, New Territories, Hong Kong, within the Closed Area on the border with China. It is the first Hong Kong vehicular clearance point and it was taken over from the Hong Kong Immigration Department on June[tpl]nbsp[/tpl]25, 1981.Man Kam To Control Point[tpl]Dead link|date=May 2011[/tpl] Its counterpart across the border is the Wenjindu Port, located within the Luohu District of the city of Shenzhen, Guangdong Province.
