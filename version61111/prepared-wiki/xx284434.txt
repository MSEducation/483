Nathaniel Martin Jacquet (born September 2, 1975) is a former American football wide receiver in the National Football League. He was drafted by the Indianapolis Colts in the fifth round of the 1997 NFL Draft. He played college football at San Diego State.http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/J/JacqNa00.htm 
Jacquet also played for the Miami Dolphins, San Diego Chargers, Minnesota Vikings and Carolina Panthers.
