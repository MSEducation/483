[[Luis Villalobos]]

CATEGORIES: 1938 births, 2009 deaths, American engineers, Place of birth missing, American investors, Harvard Business School alumni, Massachusetts Institute of Technology alumni

Luis Villalobos (November 5, 1938 – October 1, 2009) was an engineer, entrepreneur and angel investor credited with
founding the angel investing organization Tech Coast Angels in 1997.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.gtamarketing.com/TheAngelJournal-Mar07FINAL.pdf|author=Thickins, Graeme|work=|publisher=The Angel Journal|title=Tech Coast Angels Founder: ‘Process’ Was Key to Success | date=March 2007[/tpl]
[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2007/05/18/your-money/18iht-mjoe19.1.5765593.html?_r=1|author=Flanigan, James |work=|publisher=New York Times|title=Entrepreneurs: In southern California, venture capitalists keep the faith | date=May 18, 2007 | accessdate=May 11, 2010[/tpl]
As an engineer and entrepreneur, Villalobos founded two companies and as an angel investor, invested in over 50 ventures.
[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.ocregister.com/ocregister/business/yourcounty/article_996125.php|author=Norman, Jan |work=|publisher=Orange County Register|title=O.C. investor helps 'angels' get their wings[/tpl]
In 2006 Luis was the recipient of the Hans Severiens Award
[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.angelcapitalassociation.org/dir_documents/2009%20Hans%20Severiens%20Award%20Application.pdf|author=Angel Capital Association  |work=|publisher=Angel Capital Association|title=Hans Severiens Award[/tpl]
for his accomplishments in the advancement of angel investing.
[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.bandangels.com/news/2007Award.pdf|author=Phillips, Thomas |work=|publisher=Band of Angels|title=Angel capital organizations name Luis Villalobos as recipient of Hans Severiens Award[/tpl]
The Paul Merage School of Business at the University of California, Irvine has established an endowed fellowship fund (the Luis Villalobos Endowed Fellowship in Entrepreneurship[tpl]cite web|url=http://merage.uci.edu/Resources/Documents/Villalobos%20flyer_5.pdf|author=Paul Merage School of Business |work=|publisher=University of California, Irvine|title=The Luis Villalobos Endowed Fellowship[/tpl]) in honor of Luis Villalobos' contributions to entrepreneurship.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.socaltech.com/new_fellowship_for_mba_students_at_uci_honors_luis_villalobos/s-0024988.html|author=|work=|publisher=socaltech.com|title=New Fellowship For MBA Students At UCI Honors Luis Villalobos[/tpl]
Villalobos was a graduate of MIT and the Harvard Business School.
