[[Chris Wood (footballer born 1987)]]

CATEGORIES: 1987 births, English footballers, Living people, The Football League players, People from Worksop, Mansfield Town F.C. players, Ilkeston Town F.C. players, Boston United F.C. players, Northern Premier League players, Worksop Town F.C. players

Chris Wood (born 24 January 1987) is an English professional footballer who plays for Worksop Town in the Northern Premier League. He can play either as a central defender or defensive midfielder.

==Career==

===Mansfield Town===

Born in Worksop, Wood came up through the Mansfield Town youth team where he was captain,[tpl]cite web |title= Chris Wood |work=Profiles |publisher=Mansfield Town FC Official site |url=http://www.mansfieldtown.premiumtv.co.uk/page/ProfilesDetail/0,,10325~32402,00.html |accessdate=2008-04-01[/tpl] before making his first-team debut aged 17 in an FA Cup tie against Colchester United in November 2004.[tpl]cite web |title= Games played by Chris Wood in 2004/2005 |work=Soccerbase |publisher=Racing Post |url=http://www.soccerbase.com/players_details.sd?playerid=40410&seasonid=134 |accessdate=2008-04-01[/tpl] He made his league debut in May 2005, when he came on as a substitute against Rochdale. In late March 2007, Wood was loaned to Ilkeston Town of the Northern Premier League to get first-team experience but was recalled in April to cover for injuries.[tpl]cite news|title=Wood recalled for injury cover |publisher=Evening Post |date=2007-04-19 |accessdate=2008-03-31[/tpl] After Mansfield Town were relegated to the Football Conference at the end of the 2007â€“08 season, he was out of contract but was offered new terms by the club.[tpl]cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/m/mansfield_town/7405428.stm|title=Seven players to leave Mansfield |date=2008-05-16|publisher=BBC Sport|accessdate=2008-05-17[/tpl]

===Boston United===

On 1 July 2009, Wood headed south and signed for Boston United. After being a regular in defence, he was released in May 2010 when the club achieved promotion to the Conference North.[tpl]cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/eng_conf/8671086.stm|title=Boston United release five after promotion season|date=9 May 2010|work=BBC Sport|accessdate=22 August 2010[/tpl] He made 47 appearances for the Pilgrims but failed to score a goal.

===Worksop Town===

In the summer of 2010, he joined his home town club Worksop Town on a free transfer. He made his debut in the away win at Nantwich Town and has been a key member of the first team ever since, regularly playing in the left back position.
