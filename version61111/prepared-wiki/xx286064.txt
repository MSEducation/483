[[Kitoko]]

CATEGORIES: Rwandan music

Kitoko, born Patrick Bibarwa, was a Rwandan Afrobeat and hip-hop artist. He released his debut album, Ifaranga, in January 2010. The album enjoyed immediate and wide success in Rwanda and Uganda.[tpl]cite news | last = Prince  | first =Emma | title = Kitoko to launch his debut album | newspaper = New Times | location = Rwanda | date = 28 November 2009 | url = http://chimpreports.com/index.php/entertainment/12109-riderman-wins-rwanda-talent-search.html | accessdate = 12 August 2013 [/tpl] In 2012 a reviewer for Ugandan newspaper The East African called him "the biggest Rwandan artiste at the moment".[tpl]cite news | last = Kagire | first = Edmund | title = Bibarwa to take on region’s artistes| newspaper = The East African | location = Uganda | date = 10 June 2012 | url = http://www.theeastafrican.co.ke/Rwanda/Lifestyle/Bibarwa-to-take-on-regions-artistes--/-/1433242/1424586/-/item/1/-/11hbsr1z/-/index.html | accessdate = 12 August 2013 [/tpl] Prior to launching his musical career, Bibarwa sang in a local church choir.[tpl]cite news | last = Mbabazi  | first =Linda| title = Kitoko recording songs in Uganda | newspaper = New Times | location = Rwanda | date = 18 January 2011| url = http://www.newtimes.co.rw/news/index.php?i=14509&a=37508 | accessdate = 12 August 2013 [/tpl]
In 2012, Kitoko was invited to give a private performance at the birthday of Ange Kagame, daughter of Rwandan president Paul Kagame.[tpl]cite news | title = Rwanda: Kitoko Performs at Ange Kagame’s Birthday Party | newspaper = RwandaShow | location = Rwanda | date = 25 November 2012| url = http://www.rwandashow.com/index.php/2012/11/rwanda-kitoko-performs-ange-kagames-birthday-party-2/ | accessdate = 12 August 2013 [/tpl] In February 2013, Kitoko performed at the Rwandan Cultural Festival in Jylland-Fyn, Denmark.[tpl]cite news | title = Kitoko thrills fans in Denmark | newspaper = New Times | location = Rwanda | date = 7 February 2013| url = http://www.newtimes.co.rw/news/index.php?i=15261&a=63620 | accessdate = 12 August 2013 [/tpl] He has also performed throughout the East African Community and in the United States, France and Belgium.[tpl]cite news | title = Kitoko thrills Rwandan community in Belgium | newspaper = RwandaShow | location = Rwanda | date = 4 January 2013| url = http://www.rwandashow.com/index.php/2013/01/kitoko-thrills-rwandan-community-in-belgium/ | accessdate = 12 August 2013 [/tpl] He was a winner twice at the Salax Awards, including in the category of Best Afrobeat Artiste.[tpl]cite news | title = Celebrity Central: Kitoko | newspaper = New Times | location = Rwanda | date = December 2009| url = http://www.newtimes.co.rw/news/views/article_print.php?i=14304&a=7181&icon=Print| accessdate = 12 August 2013 [/tpl]
