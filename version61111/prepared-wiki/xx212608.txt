[[Remedy Club Tour – Live]]

CATEGORIES: David Crowder Band albums, 2008 live albums, Sparrow Records live albums

Remedy Club Tour – Live is a second live album/DVD and eighth overall by American band David Crowder Band. The album was released on August 19, 2008.[tpl]dead link|date=March 2012[/tpl] It was recorded live on the band's club tour across America, which took place after the release of their album Remedy.
