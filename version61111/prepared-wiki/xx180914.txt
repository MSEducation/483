[[KJOX (AM)]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Washington (state), Sports radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 1945, 1945 establishments in Washington (state)

KJOX (1340 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a sports format. Located in Kennewick, Washington, USA, the station serves the Tri-Cities, Washington area. The station is owned by James Ingstad of Fargo, North Dakota and features programming from ESPN Radio and Westwood One.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=KJOX |title=KJOX Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
