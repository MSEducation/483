[[Jamil Majdalawi]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, Year of birth missing (living people), Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine members, People from the Gaza Strip, Members of the Palestinian Legislative Council

Jamil Muhammad Ismail al-Majdalawi is a leading member of the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine, and a member of the Palestinian Legislative Council.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.imemc.org/article/50587.html|title=Resident dies of wounds sustained after being attacked by gunmen|publisher=International Middle East Media Center|accessdate=29 April 2011[/tpl]
He is one of the PFLP's two representatives on the PLO CC, and the head of their Political Office based in Gaza.  He was one of their three deputies elected to the PLC in 2006.[tpl]cite web|url=http://archive.arabnews.com/?page=4&section=0&article=79493&d=20&m=3&y=2006|title=Hamas Gives Abbas List of Cabinet|publisher=Arab News|accessdate=29 April 2011[/tpl]
