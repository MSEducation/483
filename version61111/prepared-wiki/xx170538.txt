[[People's Movement for Justice and Welfare]]

CATEGORIES: Political movements, Political parties in Denmark

The People's Movement for Justice and Welfare ([tpl]Lang-da|Folkebevægelsen Ret- & Velfærd[/tpl]) is a Danish political movement, founded December 21, 2006 by former leading members of the Danish Progress Party.http://www.sj-nyheder.dk/apps/pbcs.dll/article?AID=/20070303/NY_SLAGELSE/103030597 [tpl]da icon[/tpl]
The movement is working for the constitutional rights of the Danish people, the strengthening of the individual's opportunities for development, and for the security of society and the individual Dane.Website for the People's Movement for Justice and Welfare - General rules [tpl]da icon[/tpl]
The movement publishes the magazine dagens Danmark (lit. Denmark Today).dagens Danmark [tpl]da icon[/tpl]
