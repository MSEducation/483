[[Saa! Koibito ni Narō]]

CATEGORIES: 2002 singles, Zetima Records singles, Song recordings produced by Tsunku, 2002 songs

[tpl]nihongo|"Saa! Koibito ni Narō"|さぁ!恋人になろう[/tpl] is the fifth single by Japanese girl group Melon Kinenbi. It was released on February 14, 2002, and its highest position on the Oricon weekly chart was #20.
