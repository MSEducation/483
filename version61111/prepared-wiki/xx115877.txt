[[Ed Krol]]

CATEGORIES: American technology writers, Internet pioneers, 1951 births, Living people

Ed Krol was the network manager at the National Center for Supercomputing Applications and the former assistant director of Campus Information Technologies and Educational Services (CITES) at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. He is also the author of The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Internet and The Whole Internet User's Guide and Catalog.

==Background==

Krol was born August 21, 1951,[tpl]where|date=May 2012[/tpl] and raised in Chicago, Illinois. He received his B.A. from the University of Illinois and spent his entire career there.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.oreillynet.com/cs/catalog/view/au/365?x-t=book.view |title=Biography |publisher=O'Reilly Media |accessdate=4 July 2006[/tpl]

==Career==

In 1985, he began working at the National Center for Supercomputing Applications. Indeed, "Ed Krol was the network manager for the NCSA when the contract was received to establish the NSFNet, and led the team in the network development".[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.livinginternet.com/i/ii_nsfnet.htm |title=NSFNET, National Science Foundation Network |accessdate=4 July 2006[/tpl] During this time, he published (through funding by the National Science Foundation), the 1987 popular user's guide Hitchhiker's Guide to the Internet. In 1989, he became the assistant director for Network Information Services, Computing and Communications Service Office, University of Illinois. 
In 1992, he published the popular user's guide to the Internet, The Whole Internet User's Guide and Catalog, which was noted by the New York Public Library as one of its Books of the Century in 1995.[tpl]cite news |url=http://articles.latimes.com/2008/oct/10/business/fi-oreilly10 |publisher=Los Angeles Times |title=Tech guru challenges next generation to get serious |first=Jessica |last=Guynn |accessdate=10 October 2008[/tpl][tpl]cite web |url=http://www.nypl.org/research/chss/events/booklist.html |title=The New York Public Library's Books of the Century |accessdate=29 November 2007 |editor-first=Elizabeth |editor-last=Diefendorf[/tpl]
In 2002 Krol retired from the University of Illinois after 29 years service.[tpl]cite news |first=Sharita |last=Forrest |url=http://news.illinois.edu/II/02/1107/krol.html |title=Krol takes time for self, travel and volunteering during retirement |newspaper=Inside Illinois |volume=22 |issue=9 |date=7 November 2002 |accessdate=4 July 2006[/tpl]
