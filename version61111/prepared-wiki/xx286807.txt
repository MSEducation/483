[[Daydar-e Olya]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Daydar-e Olya ([tpl]lang-fa|دايدارعليا[/tpl], also Romanized as Dāydār-e ‘Olyā and Daydar Olya; also known as Dāidār ‘Uliya, Daydar, Dāyedār, and Dāyedār-e Bālā)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3060185|Daydar-e Olya[/tpl] is a village in Ijrud-e Pain Rural District, Halab District, Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 142, in 43 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
