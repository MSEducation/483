[[Manitoba Agriculture, Food and Rural Initiatives]]

CATEGORIES: Manitoba government departments and agencies

Manitoba Agriculture, Food and Rural Initiatives is a department of the Government of Manitoba,[tpl]harvnb|MAFRI|2010|p=3[/tpl]. overseen by the Minister of Agriculture, Food and Rural Initiatives.[tpl]harvnb|MAFRI|2010[/tpl]. The current minister is Ron Kostyshyn.
The department regulates many aspects of agriculture, food and rural life in Manitoba, including food production, food safety, consumer rights and business development.
