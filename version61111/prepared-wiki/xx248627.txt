[[Jam Side Down]]

CATEGORIES: Status Quo (band) songs, 2002 singles, Songs written by Terry Britten, Songs written by Charlie Dore

"Jam Side Down" is a single released by the British Rock band Status Quo in August 2002. It was included on the album Heavy Traffic.[tpl]cite web |url=http://www.statusquo.co.uk/records/singles9.htm |title=Status Quo discography |publisher=statusquo.co.uk |accessdate=2010-01-04[/tpl]
Status Quo performed the song on Top of the Pops on 16 August 2002. It was also included in the video game Crazy Taxi 3: High Roller.
