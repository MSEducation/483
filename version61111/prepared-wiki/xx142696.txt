[[15th West Virginia Volunteer Infantry Regiment]]

CATEGORIES: West Virginia Civil War regiments

The 15th West Virginia Volunteer Infantry Regiment  was an infantry regiment that served in the Union Army during the American Civil War.

==Service==

The 15th West Virginia was organized at Wheeling in western Virginia between August and October 1862.
The regiment was mustered out on June 14, 1865.

==Casualties==

The 15th West Virginia Volunteer Infantry Regiment suffered 3 Officers and 50 enlisted men killed in battle or died from wounds, and 1 officer and  99 enlisted men dead from disease for a total of 153 fatalities.http://www.civilwararchive.com/Unreghst/unwvinf2.htm#15thinf
