[[Abdilwali Hersi Abdille Indhaguran]]

CATEGORIES: Government ministers of Somalia, 1969 births, Living people

Abdilwali Hersi Abdille Indhaguran (var. Abdiweli Hirsi Abdulle (Indhoguran), [tpl]Lang-so|Cabdiwali Xirsi Abdille Ina-indhaguran[/tpl]; born 1969) is a former minister of electricity and power generation of the Transitional National Government (TNG) of Somalia,[tpl]citation needed|date=January 2014[/tpl] and Minister of Labor, Youth and Sports for the Puntland region.[tpl]Cite news | title=Somalia: Puntland delegation on visit to Somali region of Ethiopia | url=http://www.garoweonline.com/artman2/publish/Wararka_19/Somalia_Puntland_delegation_on_visit_to_Somali_region_of_Ethiopia.shtml | website=Garowe Online | date=[tpl]Date|2013-08-25[/tpl] | accessdate=[tpl]Date|2014-01-24[/tpl] [/tpl]
From 1992–1997 he was minister of labour and social affairs of Somali region state of Ethiopia and head of social affairs sector. From 1994–1997 he was Head of Disaster Prevention and Preparedness committee of Somali regional state of Ethiopia and member of parliament.[tpl]citation needed|date=June 2012[/tpl]
