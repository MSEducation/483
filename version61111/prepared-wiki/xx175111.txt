[[Physa fontinalis]]

CATEGORIES: Physidae

Physa fontinalis, common name the common bladder snail, is a species of air-breathing freshwater snail, an aquatic gastropod mollusk in the family Physidae. The shells of this species are left-handed or sinistral.

==Distribution==

This species is found in the Czech Republic, Germany, Poland, the Netherlands, Slovakia, CroatiaBeran L. (2009). "The first record of Anisus vorticulus (Troschel, 1834) (Gastropoda: Planorbidae) in Croatia?". Malacologica Bohemoslovaca 8: 70. PDF. and others.
