[[Yar Kandi]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Ijrud County

Yar Kandi ([tpl]lang-fa|ياركندي[/tpl], also Romanized as Yār Kandī) is a village in Ijrud-e Bala Rural District, in the Central District of Ijrud County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 35, in 7 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
