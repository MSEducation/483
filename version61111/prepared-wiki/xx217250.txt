[[St. James' Episcopal School]]

CATEGORIES: Episcopal Church in California, Episcopal schools in the United States, Educational institutions established in 1968, Schools in Los Angeles, California, Private elementary schools in California

St. James' Episcopal School, a ministry of St. James' Episcopal Church, opened in Los Angeles in 1968.  It serves 344 students of varying economic, ethnic, racial and social backgrounds by providing a challenging academic program, Preschool through Grade Six, in a caring environment grounded in the Christian Faith.
