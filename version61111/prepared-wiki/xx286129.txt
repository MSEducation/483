[[Hajji Bachcheh]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Hajji Bachcheh ([tpl]lang-fa|حاجي بچه[/tpl], also Romanized as Ḩājjī Bachcheh and Ḩājī Bachcheh; also known as Hājibacheh)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3065570|Hajji Bachcheh[/tpl] is a village in Qoltuq Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 136, in 31 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
