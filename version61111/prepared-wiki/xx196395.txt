[[Khorgas]]

CATEGORIES: China–Kazakhstan border crossings

Khorgas ([tpl]zh|s=霍尔果斯|t=霍爾果斯|p=Huò'ěrguǒsī[/tpl], [tpl]lang-ug|قورعاس|Qorğas[/tpl]), also known as Huoerguosi, Huocheng, Khorgos, Chorgos and Gorgos, is a Chinese town near the border with Kazakhstan. It is located in Huocheng County in the Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture of Xinjiang Autonomous Region. The town on the Kazakh side of the border is also known as Khorgas ([tpl]lang-kk|Қорғас[/tpl], Qorğas, [tpl]lang-ru|Хоргос[/tpl], Khorgos; the train station there is Altynkol ([tpl]lang-ru|Алтынколь[/tpl]).

==Transportation==

The Jinghe-Yining-Khorgas Railway was completed in late 2009, and  now provides train service from Ürümqi and Yining to Khorgos.[tpl]Cite news|url=http://english.people.com.cn/90001/90776/90882/6760790.html|title=Xingjiang’s first electrified railway rails laid|date=2009-09-17|accessdate=2012-03-13|publisher=People's Daily Online[/tpl]
Passenger trains from Ürümqi  (started on July 1, 2010), however, initially only ran to Yining, and not all the way to Khorgos.[tpl]Cite news|url=http://www.aboutxinjiang.com/Photo_galley/content/2010-06/22/content_5054262.htm|title=Tickets of train from Urumqi to Yining put on sale|publisher=Tianshannet|date=2010-06-22|accessdate=2012-03-13|editor=Zhaoqian[/tpl][tpl]Cite news|url=http://en.chinaxinjiang.cn/01/01/201007/t20100707_36384.htm|title=Xinjiang's first electrified railway passenger train|date=2010-07-07|accessdate=2012-03-13|publisher=China Central Television[/tpl]
One of the daily Ürümqi-Yining passenger trains was extended to Khorgos in December 2013. The travel time from Khorgos to Yining is just over an hour.霍尔果斯往乌鲁木齐方向首趟客运列车正式开通 (The first Khorgos-Ürümqi passenger train started regular operations), 2013-12-26
In December 2011, a 293-km railway from the Khorgos border crossing to Zhetygen terminal (near Almaty) was completed; the tracks from the Chinese and Kazakhstan sides of the borders were connected on December 2, 2012.写在中国霍尔果斯至哈萨克斯坦阿腾科里铁路通车运营之际, 2012-12-23 For some months, the railway on the Kazakh side was still operating in a test mode.Хоргос-Жетыген: уже летом первые поезда соединят Китай с Европой (Already this summer first trains will connect China with Europe),  2012-03-16 [tpl]ru icon[/tpl] The railway border crossing (port of entry)  at Khorgos will become operational in the late 2012;新疆霍尔果斯火车站获批成立 年底有望通车, 中国新闻网, 2012-11-23) the first regular trains from the two countries crossed the border on  December 22, 2012.
The railway border crossing is expected to handle up to 15 million tons of freight per year initially, the volume rising to 30 million tons per year in the long run, opening up the second Europe-China rail link via Kazakhstan.Revival of the Silk Road
