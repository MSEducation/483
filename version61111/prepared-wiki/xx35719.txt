[[Salunga, Pennsylvania]]

CATEGORIES: Census-designated places in Lancaster County, Pennsylvania, Census-designated places in Pennsylvania

Salunga is a census-designated placehttp://www.census.gov/2010census/ located in Rapho and East Hempfield Townships in Lancaster County in the state of Pennsylvania.  It is located along Pennsylvania Route 283 just outside of the city of Lancaster.  The community was once part of the Salunga-Landisville CDP, before splitting into two separate CDPs for the 2010 cenus, the other being Landisville.  As of the 2010 censushttp://www.census.gov/# the population was 2,695 residents.
