[[25 Miles to Kissimmee]]

CATEGORIES: 2003 albums, Fools Garden albums

25 Miles to Kissimmee is the sixth album by German pop band Fool's Garden, released in 2003. It is also the last album featuring all of the original members of the band. The title track is about a girl who attempts to seduce her married passenger while she is driving them [tpl]convert|25|mi|km|0[/tpl] into a city for unspecified reasons.
