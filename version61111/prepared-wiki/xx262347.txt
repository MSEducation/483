[[Central chromatolysis]]

CATEGORIES: Pathology

Central chromatolysis is a histopathologic change seen in the cell body of a neuron, where the chromatin and cell nucleus is pushed to the cell periphery, in response to axonal injury.Neuropathology - Basic Reactions. This response is associated with increased protein synthesis to accommodate for axonal sprouting.
