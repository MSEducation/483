[[KNAF-FM]]

CATEGORIES: Country radio stations in the United States, Radio stations in Texas, Radio stations established in 1996

KNAF-FM (105.7 FM) is an American radio station broadcasting a country music format[tpl]cite web |url=http://www1.arbitron.com/sip/sip.do#access |date=Summer 2009 |title=Station Information Profile |publisher=Arbitron |accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl] [tpl]dead link|date=September 2012[/tpl] and licensed to the Fredericksburg, Texas area.  The station is owned by J. & J. Fritz Media, Ltd. [tpl]cite web |url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KNAF-FM |title=KNAF-FM Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division |accessdate=2009-10-19[/tpl]
The station was assigned the call letters KNAF-FM on August 26, 1996.  On December 10, 2003, the station changed its call sign to just KNAF.
