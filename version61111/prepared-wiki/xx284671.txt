[[Abdoulaye Touré (footballer born 1994)]]

CATEGORIES: French footballers, French people of Guinean descent, Sportspeople from Nantes, 1994 births, Living people

Abdoulaye Touré (born 3 March 1994 in Nantes, France) is a French footballer from Guinean descent who plays for French side FC Nantes. He's a defensive midfielder. He made his Ligue 1 debut on the opening game of the 2013/14 season at 11 August 2013 against SC Bastia. He replaced Yohann Eudeline after 62 minutes.
