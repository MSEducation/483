[[One Shot at Love]]

CATEGORIES: 1989 singles, LL Cool J songs, Songs written by LL Cool J, 1989 songs, Def Jam Recordings singles

"One Shot at Love" was the last single released from LL Cool J's third album, Walking with a Panther.  It was released in 1989 for Def Jam Recordings and was produced by Dwayne Simon and LL Cool J.  "One Shot at Love" was a commercial disappointment as it only peaked at #68 on the Hot R&B/Hip-Hop songs chart.[tpl]Cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/album/walking-with-a-panther-r28044/charts-awards/billboard-single |title=LL Cool J's Billboard singles history|publisher=Rovi Corporation / Billboard |accessdate=2011-11-08[/tpl]
