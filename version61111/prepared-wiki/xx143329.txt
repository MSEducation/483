[[Fuissé]]

CATEGORIES: Communes of Saône-et-Loire

Fuissé is a commune in the Saône-et-Loire department in the region of Burgundy in eastern France.

==Wine==

Vineyards of Fuissé are part of the appellation d'origine contrôlée Pouilly-Fuissé, which is used for white wines from Chardonnay grapes.
