[[Witikon]]

CATEGORIES: District 7 of Zürich, Former municipalities of the canton of Zürich

Witikon is a quarter in the district 7 in Zurich.
It was formerly a municipality of its own, having been incorporated into Zurich in 1893.
The quarter has a population of 9,864 distributed on an area of 4.93 km².
Witikon is on the eastern flank of the Adlisberg located.
