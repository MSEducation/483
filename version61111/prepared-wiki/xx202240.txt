[[Poa Department]]

CATEGORIES: Departments of Burkina Faso, Boulkiemdé Province

Poa is a department or commune of Boulkiemdé Province in central Burkina Faso. As of 2005 it has a population of 30,406. Its capital lies at the town of Poa.Burkinabé government inforoute communale
