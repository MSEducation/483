==Cricket career==

Born in New South Wales on 23 January 1885,  Penman first played for the New South Wales cricket team in December 1904 in a first-class match against Queensland. He played further matches for New South Wales against Tasmania and Queensland in the same season. The following season he played his last two first-class matches against Queensland and an Australian XI. He never played a match in the Sheffield Shield.First-class matches played by Percival Penman at CricketArchive
In the mid-1920s, Penman played three matches for the Federated Malay States, two against the Straits Settlements and one against Hong Kong.Other matches played by Percival Penman at CricketArchive He died in New South Wales on 11 September 1944.
