[[Aspendale railway station]]

CATEGORIES: Railway stations in Melbourne, Railway stations opened in 1891

Aspendale railway station is located on the Frankston line in Victoria, Australia. It serves the south-eastern Melbourne suburb of Aspendale opening in April 1891 as Aspendale Park Racecourse being renamed Aspendale on 1 August 1905.Aspendale Vicsig In 1981 the station buildings were rebuilt.[tpl]cite book|author=Vincent Adams Winter|title=VR and VicRail:1962-1983|year=1990|isbn=0-9592069-3-0 |page=107[/tpl]
