[[Beadle Middle School]]

CATEGORIES: Schools in Omaha, Nebraska, Public middle schools in Nebraska

George Beadle Middle School is one of the six public middle schools in the Millard Public Schools District, located in Millard, which is a part of Omaha, Nebraska. It was founded in 2001 and named after Nebraskan scientist George Beadle. In the current 2011-2012 school year, there are approximately 1,000 students enrolled.
Beadle almost exclusively feeds into Millard West High School.
