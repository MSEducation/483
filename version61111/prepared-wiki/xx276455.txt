[[Central District (Falavarjan County)]]

CATEGORIES: Falavarjan County, Districts of Isfahan Province

The Central District of Falvarjan County ([tpl]lang-fa|بخش مرکزی شهرستان فلاورجان[/tpl]) is a district (bakhsh) in Falavarjan County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 181,602, in 46,926 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|10[/tpl]  The District has six cities: Falavarjan, Kelishad va Sudarjan, Qahderijan, Abrisham, Zazeran, and Imanshahr. The District has four rural districts (dehestan): Abrisham Rural District, Golestan Rural District, Oshtorjan Rural District, and Zazeran Rural District.
