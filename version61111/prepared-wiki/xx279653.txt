[[Western Champion]]

CATEGORIES: Defunct newspapers of Australia, Newspapers published in Australia, Newspapers published in New South Wales

The Western Champion was a weekly English language newspaper published in Parkes, New South Wales, Australia.

==History==

The Western Champion began in 1893 and was published by M.J. Little. It ceased in 1934 and merged with the Champion Post to form the Parkes Post.[tpl]cite web|title=SLNSW Catalogue|url=http://library.sl.nsw.gov.au/record=b1184934~S2|publisher=State Library of New South Wales|accessdate=9 August 2013[/tpl] The paper consisted mainly of advertising and news columns once a month.

==Digitisation==

The various versions of the paper have been digitised as part of the Australian Newspapers Digitisation Program[tpl]cite web|title=Newspaper Digitisation Program|url=http://www.nla.gov.au/content/newspaper-digitisation-program|publisher=National Library of Australia|accessdate=22 August 2013[/tpl] project hosted by the National Library of Australia.[tpl]cite web|title=Trove Digitised Newspapers|url=http://www.nla.gov.au/content/newspaper-digitisation-program|publisher=National Library of Australia|accessdate=22 August 2013[/tpl]
