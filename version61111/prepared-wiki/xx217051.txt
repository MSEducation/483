[[Mto wa Mbu]]

CATEGORIES: Monduli District, Wards of Tanzania, Populated places in the Arusha Region

Mto wa Mbu, is an administrative ward in the Monduli district of the Arusha Region of Tanzania. According to the 2012 census, the ward has a total population of 11,405.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.nbs.go.tz/sensa/index.html|title=2012 Population and Housing Census|publisher=Government of Tanzania|language=en|accessdate=2013-06-16[/tpl]  The name Mto wa Mbu means "River of Mosquitoes". Mto wa mbu has an area of 3600square kilometers of which 1800 is for cultivation.
