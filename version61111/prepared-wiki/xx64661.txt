Lolly (born Anna Shantha Kumble, 27 June 1977, Sutton Coldfield, Birmingham[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.findmypast.co.uk/search/all/results?recordCount=-1&forenames=anna&includeForenamesVariants=true&_includeForenamesVariants=on&surname=kumble&includeSurnameVariants=true&_includeSurnameVariants=on&fromYear=&toYear=&region=&county=&mothersMaidenName=&_useMothersMaidenNameAsSurname=on&sortOrder=RK%3Atrue&_performExactSearch=on&event=B&recordType=ALL&route= |title=Search general register office (GRO)birth records 1761-2006 | Fully indexed birth records |publisher=Findmypast.co.uk |date= |accessdate=2012-12-15[/tpl][tpl]cite web|url=http://www.mtv.co.uk/artists/lolly |title=Lolly | MTV UK |publisher=Mtv.co.uk |date= |accessdate=2010-05-31[/tpl]) is a British pop star, TV presenter and actress.

==Career==

Anna Kumble worked as a model before going into a pop career.  Lolly arrived on the British pop music scene in 1999 with the release of her first single, "Viva La Radio". She released five singles and two albums over her two-year career in music. Her real name was released one letter at a time in Smash Hits magazine. After quitting her record label Polydor, Lolly reverted to her real name and began TV presenting, working for the BBC on programmes such as Xchange.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.femalefirst.co.uk/music/features/Whatever+happened+to+Lolly-6107.html |title=Whatever happened to... Lolly? |publisher=Femalefirst.co.uk |date= |accessdate=2010-01-20[/tpl] She also works for Nickelodeon. Kumble also acted as a stand-in for presenter Anna Williamson some Saturday and Sunday mornings on now-axed kids show Toonattik on CITV. Lolly has performed all around the UK in the past few years. She has had roles in West End productions of Starlight Express at the Apollo Victoria Theatre and played Priscilla Presley in Elvis at the Prince of Wales Theatre.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.cre8iveproductions.com/productionswhatweredoing.html |title=cre8ive productions |publisher=cre8ive productions |date= |accessdate=2010-01-20[/tpl] In 2003, she provided backing vocals on The Fast Food Rockers album It's Never Easy Being Cheesy.

===Pantomime===

Kumble has starred in a number of pantomimes. In 2002, she played Tinker Bell in Peter Pan at the Theatre Royal, Brighton.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.arts-archive.com/index.php?pg=12&action=performance&pid=L02066511037&arch=Y |title=Peter Pan (Theatre Royal, Brighton) on Arts Archive |publisher=Arts-archive.com |date= |accessdate=2010-01-20[/tpl] In 2003, she played the title role in Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs at the Pavilion Theatre in Bournemouth.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.paulholmanassociates.co.uk/showinfo.asp?ShowID=90&photos=true |title=Archived Shows > Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs |publisher=Paul Holman Associates & on 2006 Lolly launches in RTHK in Hong Kong |date= |accessdate=2010-01-19[/tpl] In 2006, Kumble starred as Alice Fitzwarren in Dick Whittington at The Playhouse in Weston-super-Mare.  In 2009, Kumble again starred in Dick Whittington as Fairy Bowbells, this time at the Stag theatre, Kent.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.thisiskent.co.uk/news/Dick-pulls-crowd/article-1637484-detail/article.html |title=Dick still pulls in a crowd |publisher=Thisiskent.co.uk |date= |accessdate=2010-01-19[/tpl] In 2010, Kumble returned to Peter Pan, this time playing the role of Tiger Lily at the Lichfield Garrick Theatre.[tpl]cite web|url=http://behindthearras.com/Reviewspr/reviewsprNovDec10/peterpanLG12-10.html |title=Peter Pan |publisher=Behindthearras.com |date= |accessdate=2011-12-31[/tpl] In December 2012, she will be appearing in Jack and the Beanstalk in Weymouth.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.magicbeansproductions.com/weymouthpanto/index.html |title=Weymouth Panto 2012/13 |publisher=Magicbeansproductions.com |date= |accessdate=2012-12-15[/tpl] and in 2013/2014 she starred in Robin Hood in the Stag, Sevenoaks.

==Personal==

Anna Kumble was born to Dr. Jaiker Kumble and Barbara in Sutton Coldfield. She has a brother Paul, and a sister Jodie. Kumble attended The Shrubbery School, Highclare School and Plantsbrook School in Sutton Coldfield.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.birminghammail.net/news/top-stories/2009/08/05/former-birmingham-pop-star-in-fight-to-become-face-of-chocolate-bar-97319-24314171/ |title=News - Top Stories - Former Birmingham pop star in fight to become face of chocolate bar |publisher=Birmingham Mail |date= |accessdate=2010-01-19[/tpl] Kumble is a graduate from the London Studio Centre.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.london-studio-centre.co.uk/graduate_update.html |title=Graduate Update |publisher=London Studio Centre |date= |accessdate=2011-12-31[/tpl] Her surname is pronounced "Koom-Blay".[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rEKaAzWrGK0 |title=Weymouth Panto 2012 Meet The Cast |publisher=YouTube |date=2012-06-18 |accessdate=2012-12-15[/tpl]
