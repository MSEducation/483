[[Lamy (Amtrak station)]]

CATEGORIES: Amtrak stations in New Mexico, Atchison, Topeka and Santa Fe Railway stations, Transportation in Santa Fe County, New Mexico, Railway stations opened in 1909, Buildings and structures in Santa Fe County, New Mexico

The Lamy Amtrak station is located at Santa Fe County Road 33, 152 Old Lamy Trail in Lamy, New Mexico, United States. The station also serves as the southern terminus for the Santa Fe Southern Railway.  The station was built in 1909 by the Atchison, Topeka and Santa Fe Railway and appears in the Bollywood film Kites starring Hrithik Roshan and Barbara Mori.
