[[Gugjeh Qeya]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Gugjeh Qeya ([tpl]lang-fa|گوگجه قيا[/tpl], also Romanized as Gūgjeh Qeyā and Gowgjeh Qīā; also known as Gholūcheh Qīā, Gogjeh Ghiya, Gowgcheh Qayeh, Gowgjeh Qayeh, Gucha-Kiya, Gūjeh Qayeh, Guoha Qiya, and Gūyjeh Qayah)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3065011|Gugjeh Qeya[/tpl] is a village in Qoltuq Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 919, in 229 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
