[[Jerry Scanlan]]

CATEGORIES: 1957 births, Living people, People from Honolulu, Hawaii, American football offensive tackles, Hawaii Rainbow Warriors football players, Washington Redskins players

Jerry Lafaele Scanlan (born January 4, 1957 in Honolulu, Hawaii), nicknamed "The Snake", is a former American football tight end in the National Football League for the Washington Redskins.  He played college football at the University of Hawaii.
