[[SunCommunications]]

CATEGORIES: Telecommunications in Moldova

SunCommunications is a Moldovan Internet and Digital/Cable TV service provider. The company was founded in 1993.

==Services==

SunCommunications offers Digital/Cable TV services under the brand SunTV. SunTV's services are offered in Chisinau, Balti and Cahul.
SunCommunications offers Internet access via Cable under the brand SunInternet. SunInternet offers its services in Chisinau.
Speeds range from 2Mbit/s to 300Mbit/s
