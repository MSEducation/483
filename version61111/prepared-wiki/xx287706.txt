[[Misr University for Science and Technology]]

CATEGORIES: 6th of October City, Educational institutions established in 1996, 1996 establishments in Egypt, Universities in Egypt, Education in Cairo

Misr University for Science and Technology is a university located in 6th October City, Egypt.

==Overview==

Misr University for Science and Technology   was established by presidential decree number 245 for the year 1996. All the degrees awarded by the university are accredited by the Supreme Council of Universities. It comprises 12 faculties.
