[[Metallic path facilities]]

CATEGORIES: Broadband, Networking hardware, Local loop

Metallic path facilities (MPF) are the unshielded twisted pair of copper wires that run from a main distribution frame (MDF) at a local telephone exchange to the customer.
Sharing Metallic Path Facility (SMPF) is a shared version of MPF often referred by broadband providers to differentiate whether the line will be shared with voice or not. In the case of SMPF the line is both a land line phone able connection and broadband line.
