[[KRSV (AM)]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Wyoming, Country radio stations in the United States, Lincoln County, Wyoming

KRSV (1210 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Country music format, and is licensed to Afton, Wyoming, USA.  The station is currently owned by Western Wyoming Radio, Inc. and features programing from ABC Radio .[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/amq?call=KRSV |title=KRSV Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]
