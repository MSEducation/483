[[Seamus Moore (singer)]]

CATEGORIES: Living people, 1947 births, Irish country singers, Musicians from County Kilkenny

Seamus Moore (born 18 June 1947) is a popular Irish performer. Moore entered a talent contest and won with his own composition "The JCB Song".  After two years, he was presented with a gold disc by his record company I&B Records to mark over 50,000 sales. 
He has been known as The JCB Man[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1G1-149951560.html|title=Seamus Moore|date=2006-08-26|work=The Mirror|publisher=MGN Ltd.|accessdate=2009-05-31[/tpl] ever since.  When touring he is also known as 'Moore the Hurr on Tour'.The Official Seamus Moore Website
Moore is currently running his own public house in Burnt Oak, Middlesex called Conways 3.[tpl]cite news|url=http://www.theirishworld.com/article.asp?SubSection_Id=11&Article_Id=8386|title=Great Music At Conways 3|date=2009-02-03|work=The Irish World|accessdate=2009-08-31[/tpl]
