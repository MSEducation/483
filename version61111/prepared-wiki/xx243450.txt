[[Kai Swoboda]]

CATEGORIES: 1971 births, Australian canoeists, Canoeists at the 2000 Summer Olympics, Living people, Olympic canoeists of Australia, Sportspeople from Canberra, Australian Institute of Sport canoeists

Kai Swoboda (born 4 August 1971 in Canberra) is an Australian slalom canoer who competed from the late 1990s to the mid-2000s. He finished 11th in the C-2 event at the 2000 Summer Olympics in Sydney. He was an Australian Institute of Sport scholarship holder in 1990-1992 and 2001-2002.[tpl]cite book|title=Excellence : the Australian Institute of Sport|year=2002|publisher=Australian Sports Commission|location=Canberra|isbn=1-74013-060-X[/tpl]
