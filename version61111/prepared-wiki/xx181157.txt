[[KZML]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Washington (state)

KZML (95.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Regional Mexican format. Licensed to Quincy, Washington, USA, the station serves the Wenatchee  area. The station is currently owned by Adelante Media Group of Eastern Washington License, LLC. [tpl]cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KZML |title=KZML Facility Record |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl]

==History==

The station went on the air as KTRQ on 1996-06-07. On 1998-05-01, the station changed its call sign to KYAK. On 1998-06-12 to KGER. On 2001-10-29 to the current KZML. [tpl]cite web|url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=15137&Callsign=KZML |title=KZML Call Sign History |work=United States Federal Communications Commission, audio division [/tpl] 
Bustos Media used to own the station. In September 2010, Bustos transferred most of its licenses to Adelante Media Group as part of a settlement with its lenders.
