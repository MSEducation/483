[[Minister for Development Cooperation of Denmark]]

CATEGORIES: Government ministerial offices of Denmark, Lists of government ministers of Denmark, Foreign relations of Denmark

Minister for Development Cooperation of Denmark ([tpl]lang-da|Udviklingsminister[/tpl]) is a Danish minister office. The office was introduced with the Cabinet of Poul Nyrup Rasmussen I on 25 January 1993. The minister works in the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Denmark. Current minister is Rasmus Helveg Petersen.
