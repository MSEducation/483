[[Frank Picklum House]]

CATEGORIES: Houses completed in 1881, Houses in Davenport, Iowa, Houses on the National Register of Historic Places in Iowa, National Register of Historic Places in Scott County, Iowa

The Frank Picklum House is located in the West End of Davenport, Iowa, United States.  The house has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since 1984.
