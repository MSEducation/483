[[Minister for States and Frontier Regions]]

CATEGORIES: Cabinet of Pakistan, Ministry of States and Frontier Regions, Pakistani politicians

The Minister for States and Frontier Regions Minister of Pakistan heads the Ministry of States and Frontier Regions. He serves in the cabinet of the Prime Minister. The Minister is required to be a member of Parliament.
