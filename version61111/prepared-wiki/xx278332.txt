[[Toronto Summer Music Festival]]

CATEGORIES: Classical music festivals in Canada, Music festivals in Ontario

The Toronto Summer Music Festival (TSMF) is a classical music festival and academy held annually in Toronto, Ontario, Canada.[tpl]cite news|last=Garcia|first=Guillaume|title=SUMMER MUSIC FESTIVAL: L’EFFERVESCENCE ARTISTIQUE DE LA BELLE ÉPOQUE|url=http://www.lexpress.to/archives/12544/|accessdate=1 August 2013|newspaper=L'Express|date=31 July 2013[/tpl]
Douglas McNabney, violist and Associate Professor (Chamber Music) at McGill at the Schulich School of Music, is the current Artistic Director of TSMF.[tpl]cite news|last=Veillette|first=Eric|title=Man behind Toronto Summer Music Festival inspired by La Belle Époque|url=http://www.thestar.com/life/2013/07/12/man_behind_toronto_summer_music_festival_inspired_by_la_belle_poque.html|accessdate=12 July 2013|newspaper=Toronto Star|date=12 July 2013[/tpl]
Musicians perform at concerts held at The Royal Conservatory of Music at Koerner Hall, and at the University of Toronto, Faculty of Music.[tpl]cite web|last=staff|title=Toronto Summer Music Festival 2013|url=http://www.classical963fm.com/events/toronto-summer-music-festival/|work=Classical FM 96.3|publisher=ZoomerMedia Limited|accessdate=1 August 2013[/tpl]
2013 marked the eighth season of TSMF.[tpl]cite web|last=Mann|first=Mark|title=Toronto Summer Music Festival Gets Nostalgic for La Belle Époque|url=http://ca.blouinartinfo.com/news/story/929987/toronto-summer-music-festival-gets-nostalgic-for-la-belle|work=BlouinArtinfo.com|accessdate=16 July 2013[/tpl]
