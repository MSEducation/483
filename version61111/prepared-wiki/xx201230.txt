[[Oestrus (genus)]]

CATEGORIES: Oestridae, Parasitic flies

Oestrus is a genus of bot flies, from the family Oestridae
The genus includes the sheep bot fly (Oestrus ovis Linnaeus, 1758) that is a major pest in the Australian sheep industry.
There are approximately 34 species in the genus.
