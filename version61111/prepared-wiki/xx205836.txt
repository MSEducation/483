[[Solomon Warriors F.C.]]

CATEGORIES: Football clubs in the Solomon Islands, Honiara, 1981 establishments in the Solomon Islands

Solomon Warriors FC are a Solomon Islands football club, formerly known as Uncles FC. They have also played in the S-League under the name Wantoks.
