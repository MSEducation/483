[[Miyota, Nagano]]

CATEGORIES: Towns in Nagano Prefecture

[tpl]nihongo|Miyota|御代田町|Miyota-machi[/tpl] is a town located in Kitasaku District, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
As of April 2007, the town has an estimated population of 14,231 and a density of 242.12 persons per km². The total area is 58.78 km².
