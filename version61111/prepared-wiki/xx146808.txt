[[Geovanny Jara]]

CATEGORIES: 1967 births, Living people, Costa Rican footballers, Costa Rica international footballers, 1990 FIFA World Cup players, C.S. Herediano footballers, A.D. Belén players

Geovanny Jara Granados (born July 20, 1969 in Puntarenas) is a former Costa Rican soccer player who played most of his career for C.S. Herediano and later with Ramonense, Puntarenas FC and A.D. Belén.
He was part of the national team squad, that played in the 1990 FIFA World Cup held in Italy. The defender collected 11 caps (no goals) for the "Ticos".
