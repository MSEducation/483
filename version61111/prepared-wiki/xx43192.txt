[[Restigouche County, New Brunswick]]

CATEGORIES: Restigouche County, New Brunswick, Counties of New Brunswick

Restigouche County (2011 population 32,594) is located in north-central New Brunswick, Canada.New Brunswick Provincial Archives - Restigouche County The county is named for the Restigouche River which flows through the county and is famous for its salmon pools, which have attracted wealthy American and Canadian tourists to the region's summer colonies for decades. Forestry dominates the local economy.
