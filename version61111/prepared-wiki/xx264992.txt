[[Jiří Vašíček]]

CATEGORIES: 1980 births, Living people, Czech ice hockey players, HC Slavia Praha players

Jiří Vašíček (born 31 December 1980) is a Czech professional ice hockey defenceman who currently plays for HC CSOB Pojistovna Pardubice of the Czech Extraliga.Slavia Praha HC's 2010-11 Roster
Vašíček previously played for KLH Vajgar Jindřichův Hradec and HC Rebel Havlíčkův Brod.Profile at HC Slavia Praha official website
