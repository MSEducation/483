[[Carolina Courage]]

CATEGORIES: Sports in Raleigh-Durham, Defunct soccer clubs in the United States, Women's soccer clubs in the United States, North Carolina soccer teams, Women's United Soccer Association teams

Carolina Courage was a professional soccer team that played in the Women's United Soccer Association. The team played at Fetzer Field on the UNC-Chapel Hill campus in 2001, and then at the soccer-specific SAS Stadium in Cary, North Carolina in 2002 and 2003.[tpl]cite web|title=Carolina Courage profile|url=http://www.soccertimes.com/directory/wusa/courage.htm|publisher=Soccer Times|accessdate=23 September 2012[/tpl]

==History==
