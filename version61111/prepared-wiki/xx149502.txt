[[46th Wisconsin Volunteer Infantry Regiment]]

CATEGORIES: Wisconsin Civil War regiments

The 46th Regiment Wisconsin Volunteer Infantry was an infantry regiment that served in the Union Army during the American Civil War.

==Service==

The 46th Wisconsin was organized at Madison, Wisconsin and mustered into Federal service on March 2, 1865. 
The regiment was mustered out on September 27, 1865.

==Casualties==

The 46th Wisconsin suffered 0 officers and 20 enlisted men who died of disease, for a total of 20 fatalities.http://www.civilwararchive.com/Unreghst/unwiinf2.htm#46th
