Edwin Linssen (born 28 August 1980) is a Dutch professional footballer who plays for Dutch side De Graafschap, as a midfielder.

==Career==

Born in Neeritter, Linssen played for VVV-Venlo, Helmond Sport, Roda JC, Fortuna Sittard and AEK Larnaca, before signing with De Graafschap.[tpl]cite web|url=http://www.vi.nl/Spelers/Speler/Edwin-Linssen.htm|title=Profile|language=Dutch|publisher=Voetbal International[/tpl]
He is the older brother of fellow professional footballer Bryan Linssen.[tpl]fact|date=November 2011[/tpl]
