[[Salvadoran legislative election, 1964]]

CATEGORIES: 1964 elections in North America, Elections in El Salvador, 1964 in El Salvador

Legislative elections were held in El Salvador on 8 March 1964.Nohlen, D (2005) Elections in the Americas: A data handbook, Volume I, p276 ISBN 978-0-19-928357-6 The result was a victory for the National Conciliation Party, which won 32 of the 52 seats.
