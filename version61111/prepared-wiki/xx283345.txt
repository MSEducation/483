[[Raj Kumar Sharma]]

CATEGORIES: Indian astrologers, 1962 births, Living people, People from Mumbai, Numerologist

Pandit Raj Kumar Sharma (born 14 August 1962) appears on various Indian television channels as an Astrologer. He is recognized for his predictions based on Hindu astrology, gesture readings, and numerology.
Pandit Raj Kumar Sharma inherited the science of astrology from his forefathers; his grand uncle Pandit Dharamchand Jhingan (Sharma) was a renowned astrologer to royal families in North India.[tpl]Citation needed|date=February 2014[/tpl]

==Astrological View==

Raj Kumar Sharma has been writing columns based on his Astrological views in the German Magazines Die Teleborse and DM Euro as well as in Indian Newspapers like Economic Times, Punjab Kesri, Times Of India, Mid-day, Jagwani, Samna and Nav-Bharat Times.[tpl]Citation needed|date=February 2014[/tpl] He has had his shows aired by the All India Radio, Doordarshan, Star Plus, Zee Television and Sahara Samay Mumbai, his programs are based on Astrology for television channels like German, Dutch, Holland and B.B.C. London. He appears weekly in a popular Astrological program on TV9 Channel.[tpl]Citation needed|date=February 2014[/tpl]
