[[Swiss Arms]]

CATEGORIES: Firearms manufacturers in Switzerland, Lüke & Ortmeier Gruppe

SAN Swiss Arms AG is a Swiss manufacturer of small arms. It was known as SIG Arms AG before 2000 when it was acquired by the German investors Michael Lüke and Thomas Ortmeier from parent company Schweizerische Industrie Gesellschaft (SIG).
