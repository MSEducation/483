[[Gmina Przeciszów]]

CATEGORIES: Gminas in Lesser Poland Voivodeship, Oświęcim County

Gmina Przeciszów is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Oświęcim County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. Its seat is the village of Przeciszów, which lies approximately [tpl]convert|12|km|mi|0[/tpl] east of Oświęcim and [tpl]convert|40|km|mi|0|abbr=on[/tpl] west of the regional capital Kraków.
The gmina covers an area of [tpl]convert|35.4|km2|sqmi|1[/tpl], and as of 2006 its total population is 6,679.

==Villages==

The gmina contains the villages of Piotrowice, Podlesie and Przeciszów.

==Neighbouring gminas==

Gmina Przeciszów is bordered by the gminas of Babice, Oświęcim, Polanka Wielka, Wieprz and Zator.
