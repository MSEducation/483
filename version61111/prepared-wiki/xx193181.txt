[[Triton (collection)]]

CATEGORIES: 1949 short story collections, Fantasy short story collections

Triton is a collection of fantasy short stories by author L. Ron Hubbard.  It was first published in 1949 by Fantasy Publishing Company, Inc. in an edition of 1,200 copies.
