[[WWIB]]

CATEGORIES: Radio stations in Wisconsin, Christian contemporary radio stations, Christian radio stations in the United States, Radio stations established in 1996

WWIB 103.7 FM is a radio station broadcasting a format of Adult Contemporary Christian music. Licensed to Hallie, Wisconsin, WWIB serves the Eau Claire - Chippewa Falls area.  WWIB is also heard locally in La Crosse, Wisconsin through a translator on 93.7 FM, in Marshfield, Wisconsin on 94.3 FM, and in Wausau, Wisconsin on 94.3 FM.
