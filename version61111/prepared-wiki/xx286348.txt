[[Taherabad, Zanjan]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Zanjan County

Taherabad ([tpl]lang-fa|طاهراباد[/tpl], also Romanized as Ţāherābād)[tpl]GEOnet3|-3766533|Taherabad[/tpl] is a village in Taham Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 83, in 26 families.[tpl]IranCensus2006|19[/tpl]
