[[1892–93 Western Football League]]

CATEGORIES: Western Football League seasons, 1892–93 in English association football leagues

The 1892–93 season was the first in the history of the Western Football League, which was known as the Bristol & District League until 1895. Nine clubs formed the new league, which consisted of a single division. Wells City joined the league from the Somerset Senior League, of which they had been champions twice. Warmley were the first champions of the new league.Robinson, Michael (ed.), Non-League Football Tables 1889–2006, Soccer Books, 2006
