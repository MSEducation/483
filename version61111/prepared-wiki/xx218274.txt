[[Yuxarı Yayci]]

CATEGORIES: Populated places in Sharur District

Yuxarı Yayci (also, Yuxarı Yaycı, Yuxarı Yəyci, Yaydzhi, and Yukhary-Yaydzhi) is a village and municipality in the Sharur Rayon of Nakhchivan, Azerbaijan.  It has a population of 906.
