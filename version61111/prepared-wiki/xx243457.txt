[[Matt Taylor (canoeist)]]

CATEGORIES: 1970 births, American canoeists, Canoeists at the 2000 Summer Olympics, Canoeists at the 2004 Summer Olympics, Living people, Olympic canoeists of the United States

Matthew McLeod "Matt" Taylor (born April 23, 1970 in Atlanta) is an American slalom canoer who competed from the mid-1990s to the mid-2000s. Competing in two Summer Olympics, he earned his best finish of eighth in the C-2 event in Athens in 2004.
