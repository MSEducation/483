[[Haplochrois buvati]]

CATEGORIES: Animals described in 1985, Elachistidae, Moths of Europe, Insects of Andorra

Haplochrois buvati is a species of moth of the Elachistidae family. It is found in south-western Europe.
The wingspan is 13–18 mm. Adults have been recorded from mid August to mid October.
The foodplant is unknown.
