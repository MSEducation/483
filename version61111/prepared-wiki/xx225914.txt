[[El Gato Negro (song)]]

CATEGORIES: 1987 singles, Spanish-language songs, Tejano music

"El Gato Negro" (The Black Cat) is a 1987 song performed by Tejano music singer Ruben Ramos and his band The Mexican Revolution. The hit single has been Ramos' trademark since then and has earned him the nickname El Gato Negro.Ruben Ramos biography The song tells the story of a young man named El Gato Negro and his bad luck with the law.
